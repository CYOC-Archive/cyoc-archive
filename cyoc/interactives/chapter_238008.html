    The next day Janice started writing notes about her observations in a journal.

    Date 14/11/2024
    4 Weeks Pregnant.
    James, now age 1 year 6 months old. I have taken him off the altered formula so I can observe his growth without it interfering. James can speak in short sentences and can make complex calculations in his head. The speed that his head is growing has slowed down to a above average speed. The plates covering his growing brain has stopped at his shoulder blades. Not only that but his body became leaner and more defined, almost like he was going through puberty early. (See Photo James'_Back241114A)
    I am currently 4 weeks pregnant with a baby girl, which I have directly injected with the adapted intelligence formula. I will be giving injections on a monthly basis.

    =========
    Date 12/12/2024
    2 Months Pregnant.
    The plates of James' head have fused together like a normal child, although the skull appears to be slightly thicker and denser than a regular human's. His muscles appear to be slightly more defined and toned than a child of similar age. He is learning faster than any child his age, and he has an exceptional memory. He is capable of reading and recalling books on advanced mathematics.
    The fetus inside me appears to be slightly larger than it should be for 2 months pregnant.

    =========
    Date 9/01/2025
    3 Months Pregnant.
    James continues to grow and learn at an astonishing rate. His memory is phenomenal, and he can recall large amounts of information with ease. He is now reading books at a university level. His physical development is also remarkable. At the age of 20 months, he looks and moves like a child twice his age. He is very active and athletic, and loves to run and play outside.
    It feels like the fetus' body is no longer developing normally, it's rapidly developing in a unnatural way. Personally I look as if I'm 5 months along.
    Note: I will continue giving the fetus injections until the start of the last month. The Institute has granted me an ultrasound machine and MRI machine to use in my lab.
    =========
    Date 6/03/2025
    5 Months Pregnant.
    James continues to grow and develop at an amazing rate. He is now able to read and understand books at a college level. His physical development is also amazing. At nearly 2 years old, he looks and moves like a child twice his age. His intelligence is far beyond anything I ever expected.
    I have checked the progression of the fetus using my portable Ultrasound machine and I was certanly surprised at what I saw. (Refer to Ultrasounds 250306F1 and 250306F4.) As you can see in the pictures the brain, it is twice as large as it should be for a normal fetus.
    Note: The Institute will be doing the checkups for me and have allowed me to work from home whenever I want.

    =========
    Date 1/05/2025
    7 Months Pregnant.
    I surprised James as much as he surprised me. Next week is his 2nd Birthday, and I thought it would be appropriate to give him a little present. So I took him to the toy store and let him choose anything he wanted. You can imagine my surprise when he came back with a stack of computer equipment. Turns out he is already proficient in computer programming. I have never seen a 2 year old program before, and he was very good at it. It makes me wonder what he would do when he's older. Note: I'm still deciding which Kindergarten he will be sent to next semester.

    =========
    Date 29/05/2025
    8 Months Pregnant.
    James' birthday party was a huge success, and he was the center of attention. He loved all the attention, and everyone was amazed by how smart he is. I couldn't be more proud of him.
    I will give the fetus its final injection tonight because it feels like she is trying to break free from my body, her movements are very violent and strong.
    Note: The fetus has grown so much, it looks like I have a 1 year old child in my stomach.
    =========
    Date 12/07/2025
    8.5 Months Pregnant.
    James has started kindergarden and has been pushed forward to finish grade 5 by next week. Also he has learnt 5 new languages: Arabic, Korean, Japanese, Russian and French.
    The fetus has been acting up lately. It's getting harder for me to walk around with this huge belly because of the cluster headaches I've been getting recently. The last time the fetus kicked, it left a large bruise on my belly. (Refer to Photo 070525F2)
    I have decided to perform an ultrasound on the fetus to check her progress. I was stunned at what I saw. (Refer to Ultrasound 070525F1.)
    As you can see, the fetus has grown so large that it has started displacing my organs. This may be the reason why I have been having such severe headaches.
    Note: I have booked myself to have a C-Section at the Institute in a fortnight as the baby will be too big to give birth naturally.
    =========
    Date 26/07/2025
    9 Months Pregnant.
    The fetus continues to grow at a astonishing rate. She appears to be the size of a 2 year old baby. I have checked her with an ultrasound and what I saw piqued my curiosity. (Refer to Ultrasound 260725F3.) The thickness of the fetus' skull has thickened and segmented itself considerably. This is truly a scientific breakthrough.
    Note: This is the day that I'm going to hospital.
