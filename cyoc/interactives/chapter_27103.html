    "What do you mean by that?" Mr. Bishlerp says, his hair had already become short and wavy and he was quickly losing height.
    "I'm just saying we arn't so different you and I, I'm sure you'll find some way of finding your way into my shoes" Kody says trying not to laugh at his own puns.

    "I don't care what you say, you humiliated me in front of the entire staff when I heard about you and my daughter!" His clothes suddenly shimmer off of him while he was still in the middle of his speech.
    "Well we all hate being confronted with bare truths" Kody says barely able to keep himself from bawling out in laughter watching his old principle completely unaware at what was happening.

    The door bell rang and the police knocked on the door

    "Just in time are you ready?" Mr. Bishlerp said as he stopped shrinking and clothes reappeared on his body.
    "Sure, here hold this!" Kody says tossing the principle his license and I.D. card. As his face rearranged itself looking exactly like the picture on the I.D. clothes and all.

    "Whats this for... Hay this is my drivers license! where did you get this?" Mr. Bishlerp's voice changed to exactly the same as Kody's making him now sound and look exactly like the original.

    Kody ducked behind the wall, it was important that Mr. Bishlerp not see him so he would think that he was in fact the one and only Kody Whitermen.
    "Hay where did you go?" Mr. Bishlerp said looking around for the person who had thrown him the I.D. "Strange..."

    The knocking on the door continued.

    "Just a minute" Mr. Bishlerp said pulling open the door
    "You Kody?" the police officer said looking at the the drivers license in the teens hands.
    "Posibly..."
    "Very funny I can see your drivers license, we got a call saying that you did some considerable damage to your old principle's office, house, and car"
    "Wait I can explain!" Mr. Bishlerp said backing up as the policeman escorted him outside to his car. (How did they find out so fast?) Mr. Bishlerp thought as he was dragged into the backseat.

    Kody locked the door "I wonder what hes going to say when he suddenly turns back into his old self not remembering anything tomorrow" He laughs at himself. (That went by so perfect I wonder what else I can do with it...) he thinks looking at his reflection in the mirror.
    "I never liked these curls... and I've always wanted to try highlights... You know I have been working out a lot with no result..." Kody changed his body little by little till he walked out of the bathroom looking like a Abercrombie & Fitch model with gelled spiked brown hair with blond highlights, perfectly toned skin, A almost hairless body, and a face that no girl could resist and no guy could help but envy.
