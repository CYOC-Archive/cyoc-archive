You said curious as to why he was asking. "I want to chase you like you really are prey." He told you in a predatory tone. "You won't hurt me will you?" You asked hoping. He shook his head no. You hopped up & yelled playfully "Then try & caught me wolf!" and you took off. You heard the wolf howl & yell "I'll get you rabbit!" And the chase game was on.
