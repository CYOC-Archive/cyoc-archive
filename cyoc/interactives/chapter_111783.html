    Meeting Your Son!?

    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago O

    When you fell into your dreams you saw Cal'kar again, but this time he was struggling to control his own demonic energy. You saw his energy lash out on random beings, and he eventually isolated himself after about 24 kills. Cal'kar then trained to control his energy privately in one of his temples, and eventually he was able to gain full control over his power. However it also showed that he killed many others while trying to do so accidentally, and the amount of dragons he killed in that regard was about 34 total, not counting the ones from before."Interesting, this shouldn't be much of a problem now of course, but could this be one of the reasons he was sealed away?" You thought as the dream faded, and you woke up.

    You woke up, and the others were up before you, and Topaz asked," What happened in that dream?" "I.....just saw a number of events in which I couldn't control my demonic energy, and because of that I accidentally roughly about 58 dragons trying to isolate myself to control it." You explained," I think that I may be scarred by the number of innocents that died though." You said as you felt yourself crying and continued,"I may not be able to redeem myself for those I'm sorry, but I can't stop thinking about them, their faces their fear, and my........" you said as you began crying. Kri'mal was shocked and thought that the others should give you some time alone to talk to you.

    "The deaths why, why did they happen? Why, damn it!" You cried as Kri'mal stepped towards you. "You really do feel guilty for those deaths huh." Kri'mal said sitting next to you. "Not all of them were adults either some hatchlings even, why did was I so stupid? Why didn't I isolate myself better?!" You roared at yourself. Kri'mal slapped you with his tail and roared,"Get a hold of yourself if you really couldn't control your power then you couldn't help it, you couldn't resist and needed to expell your energy it's okay!" Kri'mal said trying to get you back to reality. "But it's not when I originally performed that fucking god damn ritual even more died, and it's not like I can just end my life to stop feeling this way I immortal and can't die!" You roared. "Well then you made the right choice by sealing yourself away, but you can at least try to redeem yourself not to the world, but to your own damn son then FATHER!" Kri'mal roared and you looked at him shocked. "Father did you just call me father?" You looked at him shocked beyond comparison. "You really do have a different soul don't you dad, heh figures you don't remember at all do you." Kri'mal said to you and continued," You don't remember, because you ran away to protect me from your power, and when mother died she told me what might happen with your memories, and......and," Kri'mal sobbed and hugged you as tightly as he could, and you hesitated but did the same. "Your right I don't remember you or your mother at all, but I promise you that this time I won't leave you alone anymore I will stay." You said.

    After a few moments you stopped and you asked Kri'mal,"Are you okay? I'll be honest with you I'll have to get used to being called father from now on." "Yeah, you must have lots of questions about your life, and me, and for one I'm immortal like you which is why I've lived as long as I have, and like you I have demonic abilities, and my mark is under my right foreclaw, except I was able to train with them, while they were weaker so I could gain control over them." Kri'mal said your face still shocked. "Does anyone else know about this anyone at all?" You asked Kri'mal concerned. "Only Chaos does, and I wanted her to keep it a secret until I met you Dad." Kri'mal said trying to keep his cool. "Have you been mistaken for me before due to your abilities?" You asked your son. "Yes actually multiple times too over the last 3,893 years as a matter of fact." Kri'mal said. "What's your birthday I only ask this is because of my unfamiliarity with moder culture." You asked Kri'mal. "Of course your soul is different and so you don't know about tradition yet, my hatchday is March 6th, 4,103 years ago and the date is March 21st, 9,673 father." Kri'mal asked. "Finally who was your mother I don't have memories of her yet but I want to know what she was like, and how she died." You asked Kri'mal. "Mother.....mother was very nice to me when I was younger, and told me about your power and showed me your tomb when you sealed yourself away when I was 254 years old, and she died of natural causes when I was 789 years old." Kri'mal explained. "I see, and finally, and this may seem backwards considering I'm your dad, but can we train with our demonic magic you know so I can learn any new techniques that you know that I don't, because between my memories, and my sealing I don't know that many." You asked. "Yes, of course we can, we just need it to be a hidden location is all." Kri'mal said," also this may go without saying, but let's agree not to tell anyone about this conversation alright?" "Agreed." You said nodding still puzzled as to how your connection is with your son.
