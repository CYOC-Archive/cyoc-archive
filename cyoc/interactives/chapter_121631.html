    Well to be honest neither of you noticed her changes because the
    magical curse backlash that hurled both of you to opposite ends of the tent
    did a good job of scrambling your recollection of the event later on.

    You could hear her cloths tearing apart as she swiftly turned into a 400 pound assemblage of tail,four legs snout and wings.

    However the same thing was happening to you as well,since her curse was now your
    curse.

    Albeit a bit slower.

    The Strongman,who was named Drake and knew all about
    Princess Alisa's condition
    rushed in and started dragging out the transformed Princess
    by the spade of her tail.She hissed at that
    and when Drake heard a answering hiss from a darkened corner of the tent.

    He looked over in time to see your somewhat distorted human face
    scrunch and pushed outwards into a rather toothy
    dragons beak as the green scales finished sweeping over your skull
    and reshaping it.

    Fully transformed into a Revenge Dragon Beast you roared at the other
    female your challenge.

    Males are kinda rare you know.

    That meant Drake got a good look at your pointy sharp daggerlike teeth
    and quite sesibly he ran for his life.

    You and the other female charged at each another,rear up on your hind legs
    wings flailing,tails thrashing
    and trying to get the advantage over your rival.

    At that point the two of you perhaps were 5 feet off the ground
    and the hissing snarls and rors from two female
    Revenge Dragon Bests
    brought in the crowds.

    Neither of you could get a advantage over the other female so you
    both went back to instincts
    and soon both revealed
    your

    True Form

    You stretched and grew as you changed once again
    as you took on more of the appearance of
    a big black scales draft horse with feathery wings,toothy mares head
    and spiky dragons tail.

    The hissing and roaring didn't change however
    and the crowds watched in appreciation as
    two dragon mares reared up eight feet tall
    on their hind legs and tried to disable
    the other females reproductive powers

    Basically that vent between the rear legs and your
    swelling mammaries and teats.

    A clawed foreleg or hind leg
    at those sensitive areas would have been bad for
    either you or Princess Alissa,both as dragons and as
    your eventual return to human shape.

    Drake had returned by this time with a elephant tranquilizer
    gun and he shot the two of
    you up.

    Then he came in with the bowling pin
    and

    "whakkk"

    "whack"

    Two dragonesses slumped
    onto the grassy ground,stunned
    but still functional

    Drake soon had the two of you in a private tent,where he
    examined the two of you

    He already knew Princess Alissa's body,both human and dragon
    but was shocked to discover you seemed to be her identical twin as a dragoness.

    Which was only possible if Princess Alissa and you were
    the same person.

    Had she travelled through time?

    Were you a alternate dimension version of her?

    Drake needed to find out.

    First however,he had to see if he could restore
    some of your humanity.
