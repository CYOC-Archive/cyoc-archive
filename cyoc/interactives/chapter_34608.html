On the one hand, this does mean that the duck probably won't be retaliating for your earlier assault anytime soon. If you wake up tomorrow and it doesn't turn out that it's all been some fanciful dream brought on by an overactive imagination, you can figure it all out then. For now, you need some rest. Drooping the costume bill haphazardly on your night table, you tug the chain on your bedside lamp and close your eyes, drifting away with hopes that tomorrow is at least more sensible than tonight has been.
