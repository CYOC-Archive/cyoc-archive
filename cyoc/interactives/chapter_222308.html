She said, Antonio let out a sigh, his body lighting up as he did so "fine, thank you, there I said it" he grumbled as he continued walking, the demon then began walking along side him, keeping to the treeline almost as if she feared pulling the tigers tail and getting bit, which is what would happen if she kept pestering Antonio in his current mood.

    A long serpent body emerged from the cloak, it's body thick and muscular, covered in blood red scales, leading up to the torso of a woman, a leather belt and binding covering where her two halves connected, a strip of black cloth hanging from the front, her human half was very well tanned, her skin a beautiful shade of mocha, with a simple fabric binding over her breasts and a large bow strapped over her shoulder, she had an elongated neck and a slender snake like face, her eyes solid yellow with dagger like pupils, and a forked tongue that flickered in Antonio's direction.

    Instinctively Antonio let out a snarl, the snake woman, the creature rising up on its snake like body gaining significant hight and revealed a large hood that opened up along her neck and head, a plethora of colours bombarding Antonio's eyes, the moons light refraction off each scale dazzling and disorienting the hell cat
    "Easy you two, were all on the same side here" came Lilith's voice, spreading her wings out to keep the two at bay from eachother, the snake creature immediately backed down, her hood folding back into what looked like a hood on her outfit, Antonio reluctantly backed off as well, his flames dying down to a weak smoldering flame.

    "See we're all on the same side here, and ripping each other's throats out isn't going to solve anything" she sighed, folding her wings away, Antonio grumbled "true, but after the day I've had, it would sure as shit improve my mood" he sneered, raking his claws into the ground, all their bickering stopped however when the growl of thunder shook the earth, all of them looking to the distant sky, watching as black clouds rolled over the Forrest from the mountains, red lightning striking from above, but what captivated Antonio was the glowing symbol in the sky, an electrified red twisted crown with horns.
    "We need to go and we need to go now!" Yelled the snake as her eyes lit up green, thrusting her hands forward as a ring of green smoke appeared in front of her, swirling and spiraling, making the stones around it levitate around it
    "No I won't abandon my domain, I can still fight!" Antonio roared, letting loose another collom of fire into the clouds, when a large red beam of light shone down from the blackness.

    "Listen kitty if we don't leave RIGHT NOW, were all fucked!" Lilith yelled, but Antonio remained rooted where he stood, getting ready to let loose another roar, only to feel a large scaly presence wrap around his leg as he was flung through the air into the smoky ring, the other two following right behind him to god know where.
