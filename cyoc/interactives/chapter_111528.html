    Ken thought about what he wanted to do for a moment before he got an idea. He would turn humanity into a bunch of different tribal races. And he had a lot of ideas for the different tribes.
    Flipping the pages of the book until he reached the section talking about the human race Ken erased everything as he started writing into the new tribal races of earth.
    "Earth is inhabited by a dozen different tribal races, each with their own strengths and weaknesses, but all are limited in technology." Ken wrote as he started to write the different races in.
    "The first race is the Nekomata, a race of female human like people who have long cat tails, cat paws for feet, cat ears on their head, and a love of fish. Nekomata reproduce simply through affection, loving each other is enough for a Nekomata to become pregnant. Nekomata forgo having clothing, act very animalistic while hunting, are generally peaceful."
    "The second race is the Rabbits, a race of female human like people who have bunny ears on their head, small puffball tails, a love of carrots and great knowledge of archery. The rabbit people are genetically the greatest archers of all the races, and like the Nekomata forgo clothing. They reproduce in a similar manner to Nekomata."
    "The third race is the mermaids, who only live in water. They are all female humans from the waist up, from the waist down they have the tail of a fish and gills. These mermaids keep to themselves and choose to live at the bottom of oceans and lakes. Just because they are solitary should not let that fool anyone, they are the greatest fighters when in water, and can turn the sea against other races. like the other races they reproduce through love"
    "The fourth race is the Inu, a race of dog like people who are all female with dog ears, dog tails, and dog paws for feet. Unlike most other races the Inu wear clothing, usually robes. Despite being dog-like people they are the most peaceful race, the only race they have issues with being the Nekomata due to genetic traits. unlike the other races they reproduce through sex, which can get either one or both females pregnant."
    "The fifth race is the slimes, a race of slime people who can morph their bodies at will. They are the least civilized of all the races, acting more on instinct then knowledge, with notable exceptions. They reproduce by split parts of themselves off and letting those parts grow into new slimes."
    "The sixth race is the naga. They are a race of people who are human females from the waist up like mermaids. Below the waist they have the tails of snakes. Unlike the other races, while female from the waist up, Nagas can have either of colaca or hepitimes, which are vaginas and dicks receptively. These are only used for reproductive purposes. Naga are known to adorn themselves in fine robes, but are extremely good hunters, often suffocating their prey with their powerful tails."
    "The seventh race is the fairies. Tiny little people who are all female in appearance, but reproduce by pollination. They can fly and are known to either go naked or in clothing made of leaves or pedals. Also most have strange powers given to them from nature."
    "The eighth race is the harpies, a race of birdlike people who live in the mountains. They resemble human females in the torso and head, but they have wings in place of arms, feathered legs and three clawed feet. They also have feathers covering their breasts. They are slightly more civilized then the slimes, but are the most violent race bar none, constantly raiding the other races for food and water. While individuals are not the same, the race as a whole is very violent. They reproduce with large clutches of eggs, around six to eight at a time, hence why they require lots of food and hunting. They compete with the naga for title of best hunters."
    "The ninth race is the snow people. They are an all female race that lives atop snowy mountains and with harpies, and are the only race harpies don't attack. The snow people all look like female humans with completely white skin and white hair. They are immune to the cold and do not require food, but they will melt if removed from the cold."
    "The tenth race is the water elementals. The water elementals are what snow people become if they melt. They are a female race made entirely of water, and like slimes can morph their liquid forms to their desire. But they must remain close to a body of water in order to avoid evaporating. If they can return to the mountains and cold they can turn back into snow people."
    "The eleventh race is the fire elementals. Fire elementals are again a female only race. They live in volcanos and other hot places. Like the harpies they are an aggressive race, known for being very territorial. They control fire and appear as female humans with red skin, pointed ears and hair made of fire."
    "The twelfth, and rarest race, is the sylphs. Sylphs are only created when a fire elemental and a water elemental mate, which kills both elementals and mixes their remains to create a sylph child. Sylphs look completely like female humans, except for their green hair and pointed ears. Sylphs are known as the children of the wind, as they can control the weather, the wind and can fly. Sylphs are a peaceful race, but as they are rare, have no villages, and will join other races to live with, or will live alone. Their powers are considered a boon to the village they join, as they can help with harvests and other tasks."
    Ken smiled as he finished writing in the twelve races, now he just wanted to go one more thing before everything changed... what race was he going to be?
