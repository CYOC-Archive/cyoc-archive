    After the two 10-year-olds had disappeared up stairs and hearing the door to Riley’s bedroom close, Kyle leans back on the couch, his hands behind his head, “being a 16-year-old is so fun,” he says to himself, “for once I am the one in charge, NOT my stupid a-hole of a father. I don’t want to return to that hell… hmm… if only I could stay “Jeff” for a little while longer, but how? I’d need whatever “R. J.” used to transform me into his old self”. Kyle gets up from the couch and starts to search for “R. J.’s” phone, which he finds lying on the kitchen counter, grinning to himself, the former 11-year-old takes out his own phone, and soon he had copied the App over to his phone, creating an independent App with both his and Jeff’s file stored on it. “I’ll have some fun with those two boys,” he says to himself, “but first…,” he opens his file and changes the duration of the transformation from 24 hours to permanent, he then opens Jeff's file and changes Jeff's duration also to permanent, he then confirms the change and with a satisfied smile heads to bed himself.

    When “Jeff” awakes the next morning the teen grins, ‘thank god it was not a dream,’ he thinks to himself, ‘hehe… I’ll have some fuun with the boys, especially “R. J.”. Let’s see if the boys are awake”.

    After getting out of bed and dressed, “Jeff” heads over to Riley’s room and knocks on the door, “Riley? R. J.? Are you guys awake?”

    “Y-yeah, just getting up,” he hears one of the boys say.

    “May I come in?”

    “Sure”.

    When “Jeff” opens the door he smiles when he sees the two ten-year-olds lying next to each other, ‘they look so cute together,’ he thinks to himself.

    “Morning bro,” Riley says, smiling while “R. J.” has a look of concern on his face, “what’s up?”

    “Well, what would you two say if we’d visit the circus and carnival,” the fake Jeff asks.

    Riley’s eyes widen, “bro, that would be amazing and so much fun,” he squeals, turning to his “twin” he adds, “I’m surprised that you are still here, R. J.,” he says, “didn’t you say you’d go “poof” after sunday’s over?”

    “Um… y-yeah,” the other 10-year-old says, “I… I don’t uunderstand why I’m still here”.

    “Maybe you’ll go poof today at some point?”

    “M-Maybe”.

    “Then you HAVE to at least experience the circus and carnival,” Riley says.

    “I… I guess,” Jeff says.

    “Sweet! I’ll get myself ready first,” Riley says, before jumping out of bed, grabbing his clothes ad disappearing into the bathroom.

    “R. J.” looks at his clone, “okay Kyle,” he says, “wh-what are you planning!”

    Kyle grins, “you know Jeff,” he says, “being you is a lot of fun, so I’ve decided to continue to pretend to be you for a little while longer!”

    Jeff is horrified, “K-Kyle… you can’t,” he says, “I mean… my parents will be coming home tonight, there can’t be two Riley’s!”

    Kyle grins, “oh, I thought about that, so I have activated the reality alteration, only you, me and Riley know the truth,” this however is a flat out lie, but Kyle already has an idea on how to deal with the parents.

    “O-okay… I guess that’ll work,” Jeff says, “so… how… how long are you planning to stay as me?”

    ‘Permanently,’ Kyle thinks to himself, “a month at most,” he says instead.

    “A month?! What about school?”

    “Jeff” grins, “with the reality alteration set, you will be visiting the same school as Riley and I’ll be visiting your school!”

    Jeff groans, ‘why is it that every idea I have backfires on me,’ he asks himself, he then lets out a sigh, “okay… a month not a day longer!”

    “I promise,” Kyle/”Jeff” says, what Jeff is unaware of is that his “clone” has its fingers crossed behind its back.

    Jeff sighs, “fine, I guess I can’t blame you,” he says, “if I would live in such a hell like you… and someone would give me the opportunity to be someone else… I probably would do the same thing”.

    “Thanks for being so understanding… R. J.,” Kyle says.

    Jeff nods, they then hear the door to the bathroom open and Riley returns, “okay, R. J., all yours!”

    “R. J.” nods, he then grabs his own clothes, a blue jeans and white t-shirt and with one final glance at “Jeff” heads into the bathroom.
