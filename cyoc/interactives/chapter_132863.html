    The procession came to a halt in a courtyard, filled with students and a few teachers. Sid and Rick sat uncomfortably in the grass surrounded by Peters doing a multitude of things. Two pairs were wrestling, one reffing them. Another materialized a football and began throwing it around with another. The remaning sat with the two boys, neither of which could tell who the original Peter was.

    “You should really try this,” one of the Peters recommended.

    Sid shook his head. Rick spoke up, “I guess I can try.”

    Sid looked disgusted. All of the Peters, even those accomplishing other actions nodded in approval.
