    Meanwhile, Aiko Watanabe was getting the mail in her small suburban home when she noticed a bright pink envelope. It was addressed to her two daughters, Amy and Miko.

    They were both 20, each about to be a junior in college. Today was their final day as sophomores.

    Amy, however, wasn’t sure what to do - meanwhile, Miko was studying to be a doctor, and doing a great job at it. However, both girls had one problem - their scholarships had only been for two years, so today was their last day of tuition free school.

    The two walked in to find their mother going through the mail.

    “Hello girls! How was school? Oh, there’s a letter for both of you.” Aiko said.

    “Thanks mom. It was good!” Amy said.

    “Yeah, it was good! Where’s the letter?” Miko said.

    “Right here! It’s a bright pink for some reason.” Aiko said, handing it to Miko.

    “Hmmm. Maybe it’s for a wedding or something?” Miko said aloud.

    “Open it! Open it!” Amy said.

    “Okay, okay!” Miko said, ripping open the letter.

    Not of the girls noticed the microscopic dust that flew out when Miko opened it.

    Each girl suddenly sneezed.

    “Huh. That was weird.” Miko said, her head cloudy.

    “Yeah... what does it say?” Amy said, who wasn’t nearly as effected by the dust.

    “It’s... it’s a scholarship! For both of us!” Miko shouted out.

    “Oh, sweetie, that’s great! Oh, I’m so glad! Where’s it for?” Aiko said.

    “‘Lust School for Girls?’ I don’t think I’ve heard of it.” Amy said.

    “I don’t think I have either... but a full ride? I think you should do it!” Aiko said.

    “Yeah, I think that’s great!” Miko said.

    “I... I guess it’s alright. It says we can go as soon as we get the letter, so... maybe we should pack?” Amy said, slightly doubtful.

    “Yeah, lets do it!” Miko said.
