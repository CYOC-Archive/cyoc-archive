    The following morning, Ty and Dustin just got on the school bus on the way to their high school. Ty could have used his powers to teleport to school, but it was just more fun to ride the bus so he could be among the other students. He often got new ideas for changing his fellow classmates while riding the bus.

    He felt happy after having changed Reese the day before. Growing some boy’s muscles was just the coolest thing for him to do with his powers. It got him very aroused seeing an unsuspecting kid as his muscles inflated while the poor guy had no idea what was happening to him. Ty just loved that!

    Dustin nudged Ty, who was sitting next to him. Ty was just staring out the window when Dustin nudged him and pointed at the boy who was sitting across the aisle from him.

    “See that kid over there, on the other side?” Dustin whispered.

    “You mean that Asian kid?” Ty replied. “The one reading a book?”

    “Yeah. That’s Riku. He’s Japanese. Smart dude. But he gets pushed around in PE class all the time for being such a nerd!” Dustin whispered back.

    “Oh! I think he’ll be in my first period math class!” Ty grinned. “I’ll work on him during the day! He’s a cool guy. I’ve never talked to him before, but he’s never said anything bad to me, either. He seems really smart!”

    Riku was a little shorter than Ty and he was rather skinny. He wore glasses and he had a bit longer, straight, black hair which often covered his face. He was quiet and he didn’t talk much in class. He always got high grades and he was a good student. Rike was just wearing a plaid button shirt with shorts, white socks, and tennis shoes. Nothing unusual.

    Ty looked over and stared at the boy for a minute, wondering what to change first. He studied his physique and concluded that Riku was skinny and he had little muscle. He was just over 5 feet tall so he could also use some more height in addition to more muscle. So, Ty frowned and concentrated for a moment.

    Riku was suddenly overcome by a weird tingling in his chest and arms while he was reading his book. His pectorals pushed slightly outwards and sideways while his biceps inflated just a little bit, but enough to get noticed. He stopped reading and looked at his arms.

    “What’s going on?” he wondered quietly, as he noticed the hardened muscle on both arms. His plaid shirt felt a little tighter around his chest.

    “What did you do to him?” Dustin whispered to Ty.

    “I grew his muscles a bit. I’ll work on him some more during class!” Ty grinned, just as the bus pulled up to the main entrance of the high school. Ty couldn’t wait to work on the Japanese boy some more during the long and boring math class.
