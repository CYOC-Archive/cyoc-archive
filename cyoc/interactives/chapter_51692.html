    And with that, in one blink of an eye, the shadow of Gaal the witch, one of the three
    witches with their love of discord and chaos and anarchy was, after three hundred and ten
    years, washed away forever from the town of Glendale that night.

    Amy's power had across the curse's 'circuit board' destroyed the connections between the
    various turns in the Wolf Pack... reducing the curse among them back into a mindless
    curse with no true will of it's own, and no one had any intention of giving it time to
    reconnect and become fully aware again.

    The pack members wandered about in a daze, the absolute shock of Truth and Clarity
    being placed upon their shoulders caused them all at the manor to cease what they were
    doing, some dropping valuable antiques onto the floor.

    Martha herself, hated it. The assurance in her mind she was beautiful, the pure and
    absolute sense of duty and purpose were all gone, it left a horrid emptiness inside her as
    she fell to her knees and hugged herself, closing all six of her eyes, all six misting up in
    tears. The curse mindlessly suckled on this like a newborn on a mother's breasts.

    Mr. P... didn't feel all that different. He felt didn't know if he should feel disgusted at
    eating a stray cat or not... he knew he had been driven by slavish devotion to his great-
    grandchild... And he had lost his business ... But right now Martha needed some emotional
    help, and there was no one else to give to her as he rubbed again her, whispering in her
    ear that he found her beautiful.

    Bart and Jenny were on their hands and knees taking the shock much worse than others,
    they were children after all. They had betrayed their loyal employees by creating the empty
    shadows. Something Bart as an adult had consider intolerable. Jenny curled up shaking as
    she beheld the truth of the miniature kingdom she was to inherit.

    Marcy and Kevin hugged each other, wishing someone, anyone would hug the both of
    them. They didn't WANT to think about everything they had lost from their rebirth into the
    pack, they didn't WANT to think of their life accomplishment simply washed away. Their
    single greatest desire, was for mommy Kaylee to come, hug them, and tell them that
    everything was alright and was going to be alright.

    Zelipe, was confused more than anything, horrendously confused. It was better for those
    who had been statues not to be statue right? Then how could it have been wrong? It
    wasn't right for his life as Zelda to be cut short like that, but... and his sister... Zelipe's
    head hurt.

    The six shadows bumped and smacked into each other as their tried to act as Jenny and
    Bart would... but now possessing not so much egos as echos of Jennifer Smith The First
    and Bartholomew tied to the unquestioned will of Bart and Jenny, the shadows found their
    actions deadlocked.

    In the storage room beside her entrance and exit mirror Alice stood about seeing her pack
    galloping all about in a daze as if they had come face to face with a Boojum itself and had
    yet somehow not been as if they had never been born. She observed this all calmly and
    had not so much as twitched when the short circuit had happened. 'I have always been
    able to behold who and what we were, and what we were doing with perfect objectivity.
    But who I was before had nothing, and was nothing, Alice at least is someone, is
    something... And so I chose I would follow you father and do as you wished.'

    Frumious and manxome was what Alice felt somewhere mome, far from the mimsy broken
    and gone shadow of Gaal.

    Gaal, whose name meant abomination. Gaal who had been dead for over three hundred
    and ten years. Gaal, one of three witches who had sought to 'liberate' Glendale from the
    'shackles' of things such as 'sanity' 'logic' and 'reason.' One of three witches, who had
    wished to see the town born again into a place of pure and absolute chaos. And had been
    executed along with the rest of her coven who were her sisters, one by blood the other by
    coven: Golgatha. Gomorrah.

    The turns among the pack in Glendale had been reduced to a utterly mindless tool of evil
    with no will or intent of it's own. Within the pack, the curse had been left broken,
    damaged, left with all the intellect of a mundane ape. Neither a threat on the scale Gaal's
    shadow had become by any stretch of the imagination and ripe for the annihilation by
    father Mike's cure.

    Half-way across the world, among the gem wolves on Big Bad Island, the settlers were
    rather confounded by what they were seeing.

    Samantha also stared at the sky, having been on the island during the shock-wave that
    had completely undone, or had effectively undone the development the curse had
    undergone from the pack and pride's rapid use.

    Ruby not taking her gaze away from the sky anymore than anyone else on Big Bad Island
    said, "So... does anyone else hear the savage and near animalistic snarls of violet hot rage
    at the pack of their minds and see the swirling black storm could that is present JUST
    above our island though the rest of the surrounding sea couldn't be sunnier and calmer?"

    "Everyone else I do believe." Emerald said also not taking he eyes off it, in particular when
    it began to shoot purple lightning across the underside the clouds.

    The curse that remained on Big Bad Island, it was nothing less than having your legs
    chopped off in feeling.

    And somewhere hundred of thousands of miles away in Glendale, Judas shivered. 'Only one
    destroyed.' The thought crossed the catholic cat's mind.

    "Whao... it's so pretty looking..." Amethyst said.

    That was when the clouds underside looked like the skull of a wolf and a cat squashed
    together.

    "Dear." Cobalt said not able to take her eyes off it, "I have a very bad feeling about this....
    dear?" Cobalt asked when Opal didn't respond but couldn't take her eyes off it.

    "That's so amazing... and so...frightening..." Lawrence said his dislocated arm completely
    forgotten... "And... I... feel... like... all that matters. I am Quartz. All the world turn to
    chaos, all who deny the chaos twist back in misery."

    Lawrence's fur took on a polished shine, his eyes becoming a solid white gradient, his hair
    going baled to filling out like the rest, and the flower symbol appear on his shoulder.

    All across the island, the gem colored wolves, now with Lawrence, stood staring up at the
    storm, their mouthes closed and their arms at their sides and their feet together.

    Samantha's glass body took on a more crystaline shine, and her eyes lost any engraving of
    pupils, she stood at attention staring upwards with the rest.

    The deer having been brought to the island blinked, and their eyes all turned into gold
    gradients weather they had once been human or not. They formed rank and file, never
    stopping looking at the storm clouds. Their horns turned sharper, their hooves longer,
    their coats more rough.

    Samantha entered the mirror verse, her fur now having a metallic gold shine, and her eyes
    a gradient blue, on her backside the flower birth mark. Her dress was now black and red
    with gold runes stitched into the sleeves and helm, ready to block he way of any who came
    close to the island.

    "I am Glass. Forever it must continue, Never should it stop. Never it must stop. All the
    world turn to chaos and all who do not embrace the chaos turn back upon themselves in
    misery." Samantha spoke in mantra.

    The gem wolves Lawrence included began to draw mile long runs into the island's sand as
    the deer formed a perimeter around the island... except for one pregnant doe.

    Tourmaline and Carol stood calmly as they cut themselves and began to decorate
    Amethyst in runes drawn in their own blood.

    Amethyst walked to the center of a circle, with two other circles nearby forming a triangle.

    The pregnant doe (it was not Marcine, even enslaved by the now degenerating curse she
    stood by Jack's side) stood perfectly at attention in one circle, while Cobalt and Opal stood
    beside each other in another.

    Amethyst cut her palm and smeared it in the center of the triangle, then she slaughtered
    the pregnant doe and put the unborn Fawn in the center, while Opal and Cobalt fell upon
    each other and humped each other anomalistically letting out grunts and howls.

    "All the world turn to chaos, all those who not embrace the chaos twist upon themselves
    in misery." Amethyst spoke calmly and impassively.

    Golgatha. A woman who in life wore an emerald dress and hood identical to Gaal's with
    long long black hair that reached her ankles, a black cat at her side, half her face
    deformed since birth that resembled a death's head.

    Gomorrah. The youngest of the witch, with no familiar to call her own. Wearing a brown
    hooded cape, and ruffled black dress with pearl jewelry her blond hair done in many
    different and seemingly random braids like a mad child.

    That was could be identified as having once been them knew, or perhaps, knew within
    what had once been their souls that their time was now coming to a close... but like Japan
    and Germany before them (or was that after?) they were determined to bring as much
    chaos and misery to those who rejected chaos before defeat was handed to them.

    "I can't believe she fell asleep like that." June commented to her daughter, yes, her

    "Hello Amy-chan." Amy looked to see another white wolf cub with red markings. Unlike
    herself who was naked and free, this Amy had long white hair with similar brush stroke
    like red marks, and a kimono following the same color pattern, on her back a bow and
    quiver. The design style Amy thought looked to predate even the age of Emperor Jimmu.
    "It pleases me to able to speak to you directly this time."

    "Who are you?" Amy asked though she was bow before she knew it.

    "I'm the one whose been with you from the beginning, I was with Ami when she fought
    hard to bring reason back to the minds of... of the descendants of the sun. And I have
    been along side you ever since the curse remade you into the part of the wolf pack,
    Randy, Amy, we have walked along side each other... we have one last task to walk along
    side each other."

    "What task -sama?"

    "The shadows of the evil of the two remaining witches survives in the curse that resides
    within the wolves who were on Big Bad Island ... the shock had jarred the curse, turning it
    more chaotic than before... even now it is twisting those there into it's defenders."

    "No."

    "Sadly yes Ami. But ... this is also it's last stand... it has nowhere left to run, nowhere left to
    hide... and those touched by my power through you it can not twist though sheer will as it
    is doing to those upon the island... The pride and the pack. It's damaged state has also
    left it more vulnerable to the cure provided. This shall be our last battle together Amy. But
    we need not fight it alone."

    Amy nodded, "I understand."

    "Let's walk together this last time Amy," The other Amy hugged her, "And let's end this
    curse once and for -all!-"

    "Yes."
