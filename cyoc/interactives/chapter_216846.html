    Rei was not about to let her girlfriend deal with Kane alone, she used her coin and summoned everyone together at the Harris house. Connor and Spark arrived with Connor wearing his new armor. Miles and Miranda along with Amara were also summoned together, as were Sylvia and Tanya, and finally Lancelot.

    Ezekiel Kane nearly lashed out his gun towards Amara but his arm was grabbed by Lancelot who glared at him with familiarity.

    "You?!" Kane said in shock.

    "Yes, and this time, I am prepared, villain." Lancelot said as Kane smacked him away with garlic to the eyes as Lancelot looked to be wincing, and crying but then began to laugh.

    "What the fuck? That was my best batch of garlic." Kane said amazed.

    "Yeah, trade secret: Garlic doesn't actually kill vampires, it just bruises them a little." Lancelot smirked as he smacked.

    Sylvia and Tanya used magic to morph their clothes into witch's outfits. Kane flung open the door to head outside and try to run down the driveway, but he was suddenly blocked by a force field.

    "Think this will stop me, you ghost bitch?! The neighbors will probably call the cops on all your asses soon." Ezekiel Kane smirked.

    "Doubtful." Tanya said not fully telling Kane what she meant. Kane saw a neighbor putting out trash not far away and tried to waved to their attention.

    The Man taking out his garbage saw at the end of the street what looked like a family barbecue with music going on at night and smiled and waved politely to the man waving back before going inside.

    "Still think I don't have tricks up my sleeve?" Kane rhetorically asked as he lifted up his hands to use some stones before Connor in his fairy armor zipped over and lifted up Kane's hands making him drop his stones onto the concrete and watched as they broke before him.

    "This is for my friend, who you killed in the woods." Spark said using her fairy powers to leave a scar on the side of his face from his nose to his lips.

    "Just you wait, you fairy bitch, I'll make sure you get what's coming to you. I'll rip your wings off and...." Kane suddenly screamed as Connor began crushing his fingers.

    "Don't insult my girlfriend, don't threaten my girlfriend, and DON'T YOU FUCKING DARE HURT HER!!!" Connor said as he threw Kane down to the driveway pavement and began beating Kane's face in before Kane kicked him off.

    Kane rushed to the backyard where Sylvia and Tanya were still dressed in their witch's gear with war paint on their faces.

    "You two are the ghosts, right? So who were you two before you died?"

    "You killed my daughter and I." Sylvia said.

    Kane looked at them with a blank expression for a moment. "Do you have the slightest idea how little that narrows it down."
