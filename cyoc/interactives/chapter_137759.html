    "Roar"

    added by The 1 6 years ago A

    "Roar." The judge announced.

    ''Roar'? What are they up to?' James thought. Regardless, he needs the money for his family.

    James stepped forward.

    "Roar. R. O. A. R. Roar." James said.

    "ROOOAAAARRR!!!" James suddenly roared like a real animal. He put his feathered hands over his mouth in surprise.

    Instead of saying anything, the judge watched with a smile as James stood there awkwardly.

    Suddenly, James felt the skin on his legs tingle as his legs grew scales. James stumbled as he felt his feet stretch out, ripping his shoes to shreds in seconds. Within seconds, through painful muscle stretching, James finds himself standing on three toed T-Rex legs. Though his legs are a lot smaller then the real T-Rex's to match the proportions of his smaller body.

    The Judge's smile was almost eire.

    "Thank you James. You may proceed to the next round. Please take a seat and wait for your next turn."

    James nodded and awkwardly trotted back to his seat. Without any kind of tail, and with his feathered arms forced into chicken wings position, he has no way of balancing himself. He can't even stretch out his arms to balance himself.

    James ran into another problem. When he arrived to his chair, he released that his new legs couldn't bend like they used too. They weren't designed to sit in a chair.

    James was forced to stay standing while using his chair as a crutch.

    "Your turn Ashely." The judge asked politely.

    Ashely got up, walking awkwardly as she got used to her hoofed feet.

    But she didn’t get the chance too. When she got in front of the podium, in front of all the LIVE cameras, the judge spoke again.

    "Ashely, your word will be..."
