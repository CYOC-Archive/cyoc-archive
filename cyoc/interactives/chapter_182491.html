    There is a rumor that lately a new prison for women has been created whose objective is to try to reform the criminals who are sent there but using a somewhat particular method. This method consists of injecting them with a serum that will make the person begin to rejuvenate until some point in their early childhood. It can rejuvenate that person when they were 6, 4, 2, or even 1 year old. Despite the transformation, that person will still maintain their normal mind. After that, there is when the actual program begins. There is not much data about it, but for there they say that it is "as if you were in a normal day in a daycare". As they have commented, this program can last many years, it is even rumored that it can last up to 20 years. But even though a long time passes, the transformed person will not age a single day. Even if 100 years pass, that person will remain at the same age to which they were reduced.
    It is also said that if the person manages to complete the program, that person will be able to return to their original age but with the difference that now they will be a good person.
    But if that person decides not to cooperate in the program or if they do not make any progress in a long time, another serum will be injected which will cause that person to lose all their memory. After that, it is not known what happens to them afterwards. Some say that person is sent to a family where they can restart their lives but others say that they stay in prison forever.
    All these rumors arose after a large number of criminals began to disappear and they have not been heard from again for a long time. Although, it has been said that some have been seen again but that now they were just little girls with no memory of their past lives.
