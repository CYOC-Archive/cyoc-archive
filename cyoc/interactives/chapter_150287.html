    Jeff's mother Emily knew exactly what she could to have fun. With Jill slowly becoming more like a little girl and Megan just in awe at what her former brother had become. Emily had complete control over the Chronivac's power.

    Through a desire to experiment, she didn't want to do anymore with herself for the time being and wanted to try more with her two children. Getting up Jill's menu first, Emily wanted to make sure that no one could change her without her involvement so she made Jill unaware. With this now set, Jill would now act and think completely like a 8 year old and forget about the Chronivac.

    "Perfect, with this I won't have anyone get in my way of using the Chronivac. Now onto Megan." Was what Emily thought.

    Switching from Jill's menu to Megan's menu, Emily had begun to mess with her settings. While she made Jill younger, she wanted to make Megan older to see how things would change.

    Since Jill, before she ended up as an 8 year old girl, change Emily to be a 15 year old teen; someone would need to take up the mother role of the family. With this in mind, Emily altered some of Megan's setting to make the perfect mother to her and Jill.

    The first thing that would need to change is Megan's age. Bumping it up to 39 would be fitting as it meant she was old enough to be a mother but young enough to still be youthful. This also meant that some of the other changes she was planning for Megan weren't out of place on her.

    The next few changed Emily planned for Megan were with her physical appearance. Making her skin much more youthful with a very sexy body, she wanted to give her former daughter the beauty of maturity. Despite this, Emily didn't want to make Megan a complete bombshell so with some belly fat from supposedly being pregnant twice, Megan's new body was now a decently bust and mature woman of 39 years.

    The last thing that Emily wanted to change about Megan is her personality. Making sure she was unaware of all changes, she hit the unaware button so reality would change in accordance with all changes to Megan. Emily then began to alter Megan so that she was a overly caring but fun loving mother who felt extremely horny for men. While it would be cool to have someone like her old mother, Emily knew she was playing with the Chronivac so she atleast wanted to have a completely new mother, one she never had before.

    "So I'm going to have a boy crazy mother and a dumb 8 year old sister while being a 15 year old girl who is still going school. Time to see how things will happen now." Emily announced to herself as she hit the action button to activate all the changes.

    With the changes occurring, Emily went down to see how her new mother has changed.
