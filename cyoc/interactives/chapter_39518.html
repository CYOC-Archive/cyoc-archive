    This is the story of a drinking fountain, located near the entrance of a large parking lot, dividing three schools: one is an elementary school, the other a middle school, and the large school is the high school. The high school is located across the street from the other two schools. All kids walk across this parking lot, where the drinking fountain is located.

    Since the drinking fountain is closest to the middle school, most students who would use it are middle-schoolers. However, the kids from the other schools would also drink from this water fountain, of course. Since the baseball field and the football fields are down the street, student athletes would also make use of the fountain, especially if they have just come from practice, or after they have played a game.

    The strange water would cause any one of these kids to change, depending on the age and the size of the kid who would drink from the water fountain. What would happen to them? Would they grow bigger or taller? Would they become more muscular? Would they gain super strength? Would they gain weight? Would they become more attractive or handsome? Would they shrink or lose weight? Would the younger kids become older, taller and bigger while the older kids would regress back into their toddler years? Anything could happen to the kids, who would drink the water from this fountain!
