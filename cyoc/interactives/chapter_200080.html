    Amy's Pretty Pegasi

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago A BM TG O Mythological Magic Equine

    To say that Thomas was having an odd day would be the understatement of the century. Currently a pegasus, a FEMALE pegasus, she rode alongside a past version of herself, who was also a pegasus. They both left rainbow trails as they flew a carriage containing two versions of her sister Amy, Amy's past crush, and what used to be Thomas's girlfriend. That's not even mentioning Amy's goddess-level powers.

    But...she did her best to just endure until Amy got this out of her system. Thomas had never flown before, but the instinct just came natural to her as she flew onwards to the grandiose castle in the distance. It was bright white, and surrounded by a bustling whimsical village. Thomas saw other pegasi there as well, but unlike her simple brown and dark brown, they were all sorts of colors.

    Amy must have noticed her look, and smiled. "Oh, jealous of the pretty horses, Thomas? Don't worry, I'll help you fit in."

    Thomas sighed, a bright flash surrounding her and her other self as the two changed to much more vibrant colors. Thomas's past self getting a light green coat and a bright pink mane, while the original Thomas got a magenta coat and a white mane. Lilacs appeared in both of their manes, and their eyes grew larger, with their irises changing to a matching pink.

    "Aww, you're both so pretty now!" Amy chuckled. Oh, she was enjoying this, wasn't she?
