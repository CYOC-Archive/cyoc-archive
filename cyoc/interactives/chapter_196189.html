    A wide array of videogame characters.

    You find a section of the store dedicated exclusively to video game characters, some of them you recognize, and others are quite weird. Despite that, you decide to check the aisles for something that calls your attention.

    There's all kinds of costumes there. Some of them are just clothes items such as shirts or aprons, some are more elaborated like full cloaks with footwear, gloves and masks, and the more impressive ones are full bodysuits depicting all kinds of fictional characters and creatures. All of them look impressive, made from high quality materials, and any of those would easily win any cosplay contest with minimum effort.

    All of a sudden, you spot a particular costume you'd never thought would exist in real life. You don't remember the name or the videogame it comes from, all you know is that it's the perfect costume for you...

    What do you do now?
