    A week has passed since Steve has visited the Second Puberty support group.

    His body was now totally hairless, save of course for the gorgeous platinum blonde hair that was now down to his waist.

    His fingernails were now an inch long making certain tasks more difficult.

    He had also lost a significant amount of height and weight. Going from a solid 6’1 and 180lbs to just 5’6 and 105lbs.

    His breasts were now solid C-cups and very round and perky. They were also much more sensitive, causing a very pleasurable sensation when touched.

    His skin also has taken on a more tanned look like he was out in the sun all day, looking like a nice bronze.

    However the worst things so far for Steve had been trying to fight off the new mental changes...

    His memory was starting to get worse. He was struggling to remember a lot of his medical and school knowledge, but found himself remembering more about trivial things, like clothes.

    One day his former sister now brother Harry(Formally Harriet) came to visit as they went out to a cafe for lunch.

    “So...how are things?” Harry asked. He knew full well what Steve was going through. However his experience was far more typical. He had gone from a cute girl to a handsome man and kept his intellect. He had even used his new gender to his advantage to secure a promotion to residency director at his hospital.

    “How do you do think it’s going? I’m doing everything to hold onto my mind but it gets harder every day. I caught myself staring at high heels at a store the other day and I even had my first sexual fantasy yesterday when a cute guy walked past me..” Steve said almost sobbing.

    “You know it doesn’t matter what form you take you will always be my sibling. Whether it’s my little brother or sister I don’t care.” Harry said trying to comfort him.

    “What am I supposed to do? Our intelligence is what has always made our family special. I wanted to be a doctor like you and mom and dad. What am I supposed to do now? Start going by Stacy and start stripping?” Steve asked dishearteningly.

    “Hey, you don’t know how much this will affect your mind. There is still so much you can do. Plus new treatments are coming out everyday we will figure out something.” Harry said trying his best.

    Steve took a deep breath and sighed. “ I don’t have much longer...Maybe another couple weeks. Like...seriously...” Steve said as his eyes widened as he cupped his mouth realizing what he just said.

    Steve got up and left the cafe embarrassed. He had to try and figure out something before he lost everything that was important to him.
