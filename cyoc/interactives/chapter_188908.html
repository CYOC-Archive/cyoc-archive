    You walked into the Transcostume store & were met by an employee. "How can I help you sir?" The employee asked you. "I'm looking for a Transcostume to make me into a feral female bottlenose dolphin." You informed him. "Right this way sir." The employee told you & began leading you. You soon got to an aisle & he pulled the female bottlenose dolphin Transcostume off the rack. As you were walking back to the checkout you asked "I'll be able to have dolphin calves aka dolphin babies?" The employee answered not seeming surprised or anything "Yes. You'll be 100% dolphin cow; aka dolphin female. Dolphin bulls; aka male dolphins will be able to impregnate you with dolphin calves; dolphin babies. You'll be able to give birth to calves." "Good. Good." You said pleased. As you were paying for the Transcostume at the front desk he asked "You wanted to make sure you would be able to have calves hun?" You answered feeling a bit embarrassed "Yes." "Don't be embarrassed. I've been asked about animal mating & impregnating & birth before. And before you ask your calves will be 100% feral normal dolphins." He told you. "That's good." You replied. You finished paying for the costume & before you left he informed you "You won't have any hands to take off the costume. You have to have someone else return you to human. Also you'll know how to swim & everything dolphins know after the costume makes you a dolphin. That includes had to have sex & how to raise calves." You were pleased by that. You headed out to the zoo you worked at. You were met by your boss & he asked you "That is a female dolphin Transcostume, right?" "Of course." You answered nodding. You headed to the dolphin tank & you stripped naked. You put on the dolphin Transcostume; all but for the head. "Now as we agreed you'll let me be impregnated by bulls & have calves & be allowed to raise them. Also only you will know I'm a human transformed into a cow dolphin." You reminded him. As you got in the tank your boss asked it "How long should I leave you as a dolphin?" You looked & saw he had the Transcostume instructions in his hands. You thought for a bit & replied "5 years. That's good number." Your boss replied "Ok Daisy." You asked confused "Daisy?" Your boss answered "That's your dolphin name." Sounds good actually." You said liking it. You then flipped the costume head over your own. You instantly felt naked & dolphin information entered your brain. You were now a cow bottlenose dolphin.
