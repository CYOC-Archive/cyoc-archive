    Toddler Time for Tina

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Timetweaker 7 years ago AR BM

    "Okay Tina, I wish you wore baby clothes," she said, and the next thing they knew Tina stood there wearing training pants and a yellow sundress with my litttle pony on it and mary janes on her feet.
    "Oh my you look so cute like that," Janice informed her as she dragged her over to the mirror for the full effect.
    "Very funny dear, now please change me to something more appropriate for my age," Tina told her. "Though, I am amazed that I am an adult and that this clothing is big enough for me."
    "Maybe the magic has something to do with it."
    "That may be possible--so try something else."
    "Okay, I wish we had a million dollars," she said, and nothing seemed to happen.
    "Alright, so that did not work--wait, make another wish concerning my age."
    "Okay, I wish you were physically 3 years old."
    The next thing they knew Tina was regressing rapidly.
    "Okay--I will go along with this for now," the rapidly regressing woman replied as she passed 26.
    "The years are fading from you so fast," Janice laughed.
    "Ya think," Tina replied as she passed 20.
    "Oh absolutely, and no leaving the mirror, you have to watch all of this."
    Tina sighed. She had encouraged Janice to experiment, and now she was stuck--at least temporarily with the results.
    Janice smiled as she watched her friends breasts fade to nothing as she hit her preteen years.
    "Oh great, back to single digits," the nine year old said.
    "Hey, I do have coloring books and some dollies for you later."
    "Ya do? Wait, I am not going to get excited about this--unless we can go to McDonalds and I can play there in that place they have and me gets happy meal," the 6 year old told her.
    "Sure my dear, though we have more experimenting to do and I now have to take you and the baby. I wish I had car seats in my car for you and the baby--I hope that works."
    "Me too," added Tina as she hit 3, her hair now in pigtails and the little girl holding a teddy bear.
    "Oh, and I wish the car were loaded with all the things I need for a new baby and a 3 year old little girl."
    "You cawy me jan-is," Tina told her.
    Before long Janice had loaded the toddler and baby in the car and they were headed to McDonalds.
