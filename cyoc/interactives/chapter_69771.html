    The sobbing girl across the room turns out to be your only way out of this place but seeing her give up hope that easily puts your hopes down. Still, if she can be in a place like this. There must be some kind of food to live on. You sit next to her to ask her a few questions.

    "I wandered in here out of curiosity but ended up in this room. What is going on here?"
    The girl looks up to stare at the other side of the room. She seems angry.

    "This is the room where I will be staying for the rest of my life. You can leave anytime you want. There is a portal in the room where I ran out from. For me, I cannot leave. Not until the owner of this mansion decides to set me free."

    "Wait... portal. If I can leave, why can't you?"

    "It turns out my great, great, great, great grandmother was a stuck up bitch. I am a decedent of a princess that me or my family have never heard of. Apparently, a curse was placed on me to make sure my life was ruined.

    "What kind of curse?"

    "Never mind that. I should bring you home as soon as possible".

    Without a moments hesitation the girl was standing again and she made her way to the other room. You followed to see about this portal whether it exists or not.

    In your opinion, the other room looked like a school locker room. Rows of showers and a few benches. This image took you back to your school days. Although, far down at the back of the room there was no mistaking that the huge glowing orb was the portal the girl was talking abut. They are real after all.

    You step inside to get the real gist of the situation. To your left you find a huge stash of hair. piled to almost your height. There was a mix of blond and black hair all at an unbelievable length. You wonder who the blond hair belongs to. Then you look to your right and see a large empty troth like the ones pigs use. Next to that is a small pile of modern clothes probably belonging to the girl.

    The girl is standing next to the portal waiting for you to stop looking around. You eventually follow her.

    "As you can see, when you look at the portal, you can see your home right?"

    You loom in there and you can see a feint image of your front yard so you nod.

    "Good but when I stand in front of the portal the portal disappears."

    When the girl stands in front of the portal, The portal poofs like a puff of smoke but when the girls leaves the area you see the familiar portal again. The girl makes a small sigh.

    "Once you exit the portal, you automatically forget your time in the mansion so you can live your life again. OK in you go."

    "Wait I want to help you" you say to the girl. The girl then starts becoming a bit forceful.

    "You cannot help me now please leave".

    You look upon the naked girl which looks like to be living in harsh conditions. You suddenly look down at her tattoo. The tattoo has suddenly begun to glow. The girl collapses and moans in pain. You cannot go in the portal now. you need to help.

    "What's wrong. How come your tattoo is glowing." You see the tattoo has transformed it's shape into a pair of legs.

    "The... curse. I am... a... mermaid"
