    Ty grows another father and son together at the gym

    Just as he said that, another two guys walked in. Ty could tell right away that they were father and son. The younger one was the spitting image over the older one. Both of them looked kinda big, but Ty wanted to make them bigger. He looked over at Dustin and winked. Dustin started to laugh and watched eagerly.

    The two of them were standing at the counter, talking to the guy behind it. Neither Ty nor Dustin could really hear what was going on, but the father and son seemed to be getting angry. Ty was about to wish we could hear, when he heard the guy blurt out, "I'm sorry, but this membership card is only good for one person at a time. You can't both use it," then he turned and walked away. Ty and Dustin's eyes met right away and lit up. A evil grin spread across Ty's face, "I wish that father and son would grow... together!"

    Instantly, the father and son slammed together, side by side. Dustin watched as their bodies began to join. Their inner legs were absorbed into themselves, adding more muscle to their legs. Their heads were sitting side by side, on one body single body. Their body was bigger than their two separate ones, the mass from both had been added together, giving them more muscle and height.

    They looked at each other in awe, not knowing what to say, feeling their body with the two hands. They didn't even try to push away from each other, enjoying the feeling of the joining. The father patted his son on the shoulder then rang the bell again. The same guy came out and stood behind the counter. Even though they had retained their own heads, he didn't seem to recognize, or even notice they were now joined. He looked at their membership and smiled, "Have a good day!"

    Ty and Dustin watched carefully as the father and son walked passed, grinning and rubbing themselves, happy as can be that they were joined. "Wow dad, I never thought we'd be this close, ever!"

    "Me either... want to have a shower first and see just how close we are?" His son grinned and nodded eagerly.

    When they were out of ear shot Dustin turned to Ty, "Wow! I didn't know you could do that! That's awesome!" Ty grinned back and winked. "I wonder what it's like."
