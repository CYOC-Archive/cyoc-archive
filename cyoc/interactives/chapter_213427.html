    The giant python that is Jacob slithered out from under his covers. Jacob had no limbs anymore being a 9 foot long python. He slithered to the kitchen & at first saw no one. Soon Ann entered the kitchen naked. She looked sad at the snake & asked "You don't know who I am do you?" The snake slithered up to her tongue flickering in & out. The snake didn't remember her as Ann his sister. He didn't even remember his own name or that he was ever human. The snake bent his head down & Ann touched it asking sad "You barely know what I'm saying now don't you?" The python barely did. He'd nearly forgotten all of the human language. Ann looked into the look the python had been giving her for the past few days. She knew the python wasn't really her brother Jacob anymore. She knew the python was sizing her as prey. Ann wasn't afraid of that anymore. [I know what you think of me. You see me as only food now.] Ann thought. The python began to curl her up. So the python had completely curled up Ann. Ann didn't try to fight or flee. She was naked here to be devoured by the python. She had decided to let the snake end her life too. "If any of you is still Jacob please kill me quickly. I don't want to suffer." Ann begged sadly. Strange as it may seem the snake did give Ann some misery as he very quickly violently crushed her to death. Ann only felt a brief squeeze before she died. It was so quick she felt no pain at all. The python unhinged his jaws & took Ann's head into his mouth. The python slowly but surely swallowed Ann's whole body. The python then put his jaws back to normal & slithered off to find a warm place to sleep off his meal. Much later a few people from a wildlife reserve entered the house. They saw the large python curled up on the heating duct with a large bulge in it's body. They knew that was Ann Mester. She had called Dr. Crows to tell him to have someone pick up the large python that used to be Jacob at noon & take him to a wildlife reserve from any humans. She had also informed him that she was feeding herself to Jacob. The men sighed & darted the large python. It was clear to the men that Ann Mester decided to have her now snake brother eat her than rather live without him. The large python passed out fully.
