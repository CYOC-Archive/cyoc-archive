    "This is too weird," Nick said as he nervously played with the sleeve of the silvery pajama he was wearing.

    "Nothing about this place seems right," Mitchel added as he put back the figurine.

    "Hey guys," Jay greeted as he came back down. "There's nothing upstairs but an empty room. The halls were blocked off.

    "Maybe this place is under construction," Mitchel thought.

    "It doesn't explain why I was a statue. Or why there's another statue of me in here," Nick said as he pointed to the bronze statue in the middle of the room.

    "Maybe you got a stalker?"

    "Funny."

    Jay chuckled and walked over to the statue. "Ooh, so life like," he said in a mocking tone. Jay pushed the statue as a joke. However, much to the trio's surprise, the statue tipped over and shattered.

    "Dude!"

    Suddenly, an alarm went off. Confused, the boy's looked up to see lightbulbs flashing red. Then, the door slammed shut on them. Panic, they ran to the door trying to open it. It wouldn't budge.

    HHHHHHHHHHIIIIIISSSSSSSSSSSSSSS

    "What's that noise?" Mitchel asked as his ears picked up a high pitched hissing. The boys looked around. Nick was the one who saw it. Where the statue was a small pipe was releasing a flume of purple gas.

    "I-I feel...tired." Jay stated as he leaned against door.

    "I-I think...it's..." Mitchel started. His eyes drooped and he had to lean agaisnt the door as well.

    "Knockout gas," Nick concluded as he too fell under the chemicals invading his lungs.

    One by one the boys were knocked out. The gas filled the room and Jay, Nick, and Mitchel were knocked out in a pile. When they were good and out, the gas stopped and the ventilation system turned back on. however, it wasn't enough to wake the boys.

    The door opened and a tall figure stared down at their new prizes.
