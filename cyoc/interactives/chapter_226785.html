    Think Before You Wish

    As Damon's belongings were given back to his family, things looked bleak for his relatives. Damon's parents wondered desperately where he was and the exciting news of a new star in the sky, completely new and whose origins remained totally baffling. Damon's father had remarried and the new star had to stepbrothers, Joe and Sam. Sam, the youngest at fifteen, rummaged through Damon's belonging. The constant tears and cries of worry annoyed him and seeing the lamp, he thought he could sell it back. It looked made of gold and he hid it under his sweater, intent on cleaning it and then reselling. No one would notice it or art worst he'd claim he had never seen it. But as he rubbed a grubby cloth upon the object in his room, the smoke erupted and when Sam finished coughing, he could see a very attractive woman dressed in exotic veils. In his silly adolescent mind, he could only look at her curves and revealed skin and think of her sexiness and what he'd like to do to her. His lecherous stare was not lost on the genie and she swore to give him a good lesson with his wish. That idiot certainly deserved it.
    "You're... You're a genie?" he stammered.
    "Indeed," she said with a nod.
    She then proceeded to tell her new master the rules which she abided by. She also saw distinctly the geed in his eyes at the prospect of getting wishes galore. This one really was a moron, but hopefully she would get rid of him fast as he said the first rash wish on his list. And of course, he did, not bothering to ask questions, more details or even if she was linked to Damon's mysterious disappearance. His wariness really was below zero. And he did not disappoint on this point.
    "I wish to be admired and loved by women! The most adorable being they see!" he exclaims, rather foolishly.
    Oh, this was so easy to misinterpret. This boy was truly a young idiot to the core! The genie quickly came up with a way to twist his words and bring about a much-deserved mortification.
    "As you wish, master!" she said with a wide smile.
    She let loose her magic, laughing in advance at what was going to happen to this stupid teenager.
