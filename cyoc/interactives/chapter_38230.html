    From Superman to Superboy

    Kent and Ty looked on, as Peter flew across the sky in his Superman
    suit. He was looping through the air and ascending high above the
    ground, before making sharp dives towards his friends on the ground.
    Finally, he landed before them, with a wide grin on his face, with
    his long, red cape billowing behind him.

    “You know, I still can’t get used to Peter looking like a grownup…”
    Ty mumbled. “I’m not used to seeing Peter older than me like that…”

    “Oh, I see what you mean…” Ken replied. “I can still change him,
    right?”

    “Of course!” Ty said. “He’ll still have his powers and his costume!”

    Then all three boys heard a familiar voice coming from behind them.

    “Well, well… What have we here? Peter looks like Superman now?”

    Ty turned around and recognized the mighty wizard in the dark suit.

    “Prince!” he gasped.

    “Ty! Kent! And Peter… What a nice costume you’re wearing, Peter!”
    Prince grinned. “I like the cape… It fits you well… Whose idea was
    that?”

    “M-m-mine…” Kent stuttered. “I’ll change him back to normal…”

    “No, my dear Kent…” Prince smiled. “I like it! Very original
    transformation… Changing Peter into Superman? And you have him super
    powers, too?”

    “I did that, Master Prince!” Ty replied.

    Prince turned and stared at Peter for a moment, with a smile on his
    face. Then he extended his hand and allowed a bolt of energy to
    strike Peter’s body, engulfing him in a blue glow for a brief moment.

    “What did you do to him?” Kent wondered.

    “I made it permanent. I’m the only one who can remove the powers and
    his costume now. Furthermore, he has no weaknesses, either. So…”
    Prince said, as he turned to face Peter. “Enjoy life as a super hero,
    my friend! Ty and Kent can only change your size and your age, and
    your costume will change with you.”

    Then prince disappeared.

    “Oh wow! Sounds like you’re gonna be like this for awhile, cousin!”
    Ty chuckled, as he patted Peter on the back.

    “Yay! Prince really liked what I did to you!” Kent grinned. “Now, I
    think I’ll make you a few years younger, okay?”

    Slowly, Peter started to shrink. His face regained a more youthful
    appearance as his legs shrunk down a bit and his arms became a few
    inches shorter. He still remained just as muscular, but he lost a few
    inches in height. Within a few seconds, Peter regressed back to 15
    years old, but he still had his Superman costume and his long, red
    cape draped over his broad shoulders.

    “There! Now he looks like the old Peter again… except for the fact
    that he’s Superboy now!” Ty laughed.

    “Well, like Master Prince said, I guess it’s permanent…” the new
    Superboy shrugged. “I’d better start my first patrol and fly around
    the city…”

    “Have fun, cousin… See ya tonight for dinner!” Ty said, as he gave
    his super-cousin a high-five.

    “You look great! Well, catch some bad guys for me, okay?” Kent
    grinned.

    Then Ty and Kent walked home as Peter took off and began his first
    patrol over the city as the new Superboy.

    In the meantime, Kent and Ty began to wonder if they didn’t want to
    join Peter in his adventures as super heroes as well.
