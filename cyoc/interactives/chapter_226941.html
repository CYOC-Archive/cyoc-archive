    The Magic Shop

    Ann's sisters and girlfriend drop by

    added 15 days ago AP O Magic

    Ann was slowly waking up now, wanting to believe most of what had just happened to her had been nothing but a dream, but she knew her life was long past being a dream at all. Her girlfriend was a college age girl that would occasionally transform into an adult woman, her previously dead sister had been alive in another universe and was now in hers and now her parents could transform themselves into teenagers with anime like powers. Ann's eyes focused now to see her sister Lisa, and her sister Kellie in front of her looking concerned.

    "Oh my god! What happened? Are your kids okay? Did anything happen to the babies? Or mom and dad again? Where's Stephanie?!" Ann panicked. Lisa slapped her a little on the face to get her to calm down.

    "Calm down Ann, okay? First of all, everyone has gone out, Stephanie's gone with her friends, mom and dad have gone out to have a little time to themselves and....Stephanie told us that mom and dad are...superheroes now, I guess?" Lisa shrugged.

    "Yeah, as for us being here, mom suggested Stephanie call us to come over and see you cool down a little bit. Also the kids aren't here either or our husbands, it's just us sisters looking out for another like usual." Kellie reassured her sister.

    "Yeah, it usually is. But I've got a little bad news, mom knows about most of the crap we did when we were younger." Ann confessed.

    "My pot smoking?" Lisa asked as Ann nodded making Lisa groan. "Me coming home wasted?" Kellie asked remembering that moment too. Ann shrugged while nodding making Kellie cover her eyes.

    "And she never confronted us about this....because of dad, right?" Lisa asked. "Yep, dad is our hero that we didn't know about."

    "Great, so we sort of don't have anymore secrets between the three of us anymore." Kellie noted.

    "Well....actually, there is one thing that dad has kept quiet about that even he never told mom about. The wolf on the camping trip." Ann told them.

    "Oh man, I almost forgot about that wolf." Lisa remembered. "God, I still remember how that went. I was begging dad to teach us how to shoot for our own safety..." Kellie thought back. "Yeah, and stupid me, didn't keep her sight focused properly and accidentally missed the target and hit a wolf in the leg by mistake." Lisa recalled.

    "Yeah, just lucky dad knew enough medical training to heal that wolf. Oh my god, he learned it in his war training, that's what he meant by trade secret all those years ago." Ann remembered.

    "I'm actually surprised the wolf didn't harm dad or us at all. It was nice seeing that wolf reunite with her cubs, I'd hate to think what would have happened if that poor wolf actually had died." Kellie remembered.

    "You know, Stephanie told me that one of her friends has god parents that can turn into bears. I remember seeing a woman in the forest that day with beautiful blonde hair, but when I looked back, I saw a bear. I thought I imagined it, but could the bear and the woman have been the same?" Ann asked.

    "It's most likely, we have encountered some really unique people in our life so far." Lisa said.

    "Well, I'm just happy, that there's absolutely no secrets left between us." Ann said. After she said that the front door, opened and a lovely middle age woman came in with a few boxes of wrapped presents.

    "Angel! I'm sorry I came by a little earlier than I should have, but I finished getting gifts for you and Stephanie early, I just I'd come by and see your.....lovely face and you've got company, it's your sisters....who I've definitely never met before. Hi, I'm Jackie." Jackie said holding out her hand to Lisa and Kellie.

    "I'm guessing we've met you before." Kellie guessed. "Maybe." Jackie replied. "You and Ann are pretty close then?" Lisa asked. "We've....dated in the last couple of years." Jackie confessed. "Did you meet her through magic?" Kellie asked. Jackie and Ann both looked at each other and blushed bright red while nodding.

    "Okay, Kellie? Sister swear?" Lisa asked. "Sister swear." Kellie replied. "Our lips are sealed, your secret's with us here not there, and we promise not to tell mom and dad about this anywhere." Lisa and Kellie both said together.

    "Oh thank god, you still invoke the sister swear." Ann sighed with relief. "Yes, now for the final part of the secret swear. Tell us....the goods." Lisa said with Ann breathing in and out rapidly, but Jackie placed a gentle hand on her shoulder to calm her down.

    Ann loved her sisters, but they asked for a lot to keep their secret safe, especially from Marilyn and Martin.
