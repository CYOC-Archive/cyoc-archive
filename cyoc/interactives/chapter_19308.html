    "Of course you're interested." The sow grins. "You just don't know it yet."

    She delivers a short, smart slap to your ass. It stings more than it should and with it, she utters a strange, guttural word in a porcine drawl emphasized by her thick tongue. No sooner has she done so then there's a burning sensation where her greasy paw just slapped you.

    "What did you just do to me?" You ask, rubbing the spot on your backside where the sow slapped you.

    "Huh. I just gave you a little motivation.

    There's a small hitch of panic in your chest while you pull your pants partway down, just enough to have a look at the spot where she slapped you and you felt that burning sting. Sure enough, there's a brand. It looks a little strange, a circle in which is a glyph that looks almost as though it were finger-painted by a child. The sow admires her handiwork with a smile.

    "The Pig Queen has accepted your pledge of loyalty. She brands all her grunts like that."

    "Grunts?"

    "Oh yes.. you've been drafted into her army."

    "What does that mean?"

    "Full of questions, aren't you? Well, what that means is that you're going to change into more of a pig. Then you'll be suited up and sent out as a loyal grunt in the queen's armies. You'll love spreading lust, sloth and gluttony everywhere. It's great!"

    "Are you insane?"

    "Noooo. I'm Oozerune, a priestess of the great pig queen."

    Unsure of what you just wandered into, your head spins with the pig girl's information. It's not just that, however. It's also the brand. It feels like it's throbbing and pulsating as though it had a heartbeat all its own. Something in your mind stirs, awakening strange hungers and desires that weren't there before. You can't be sure what's happening but it must have something to do with whatever spell or curse that the pig girl must have put upon you. Before you know it, running away seems like less and less of an option as a wonderful, warm feeling of lethargy creeps into you along with another sensation. Hunger.

    Oozerune seems to recognize the dizzy look on your face. Her snout breaks out in a broad grin. "Wanna join me for dinner?"
