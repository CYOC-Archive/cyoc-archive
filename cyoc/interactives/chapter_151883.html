    They didn't get far as the Inn was in the process of being
    robbed by Thorny Oakenstaff's gang of dwarf outlaws.
    So Mika quickly cut loose from Audrey as he realized the Amazonian fool planned to tackle to cold blooded
    dwarf outlaws standing point just outside the Inn,where
    the rest of the gang were masked and robbing the patrons and
    cracking the Safe.
    From the safety of a nearby buildings corner Mika watched
    whilst Audrey dropped the sacks of archaeological goodies they'd
    just spent 3 months collecting and went for her sword.

    The dwarves on sentry duty ,three of them were surprised at
    first,but since they were unmasked at the moment their counter reaction to the screaming amazon barbarian woman rushing them was swift and final.

    All three pulled out their 45 Squeakmakers and pointed
    them at Audrey and fanned the hammers.

    With three roars of smoke and magical energy Audrey was struck by the propelled Squeakmakers
    which struck the surprised barbarian in the bosom and as Mika watched
    his companion began to squeak,sprout white fur and shrink.

    In a matter of three seconds where Audrey had once been now crawled a squeaking white rat out of its discarded human clothes.

    Mika realized that he might be next and frantically looked for a
    escape.Nearby was a doorway with the sign SHERIFF
    hamging above it.
    Mika rushed inside just as the three dwarves stepped carefully towards the white rat that had been Audrey once
    and examined the spilled contents of the sacks she had dropped.
    For Mika however there was a different fate.
    What he didn't know was that this was a tiny hamlet of anthro dragons,with a anthro dragon Sheriff in desperate need of a Deputy
    what with all the outlaw dwarves running around with
    Squeakmakers.
    So although Sheriff Nate Ignitious was no wizard he had propped
    a pail above the main door
    which contained a potion to turn all other sentient beings
    into a anthro dragon.

    And so,since Mika knew knew none of that and probably could
    have cared less at the moment
    the pail poured its contents over Mika's head and soon
    he was a anthro dragon lying on the floor sputtering away.
    He came to his senses just in time to hear the "KLIKKK"
    of the front door lock being set as Sheriff Nate carefully
    closed it,as to not draw the attention of the outlaw dwarves outsite the relative safety of his office.
