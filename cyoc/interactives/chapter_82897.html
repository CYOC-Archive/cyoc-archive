    Cal followed Will and caught up to him in an empty corridor near the halls were the 43rd Persephone was lodged.

    "Hey, Will,I want to speak to you privately.", announced Cal.

    "What's up, little nun?", joked Will as he saw Cal in her new form behind him.

    "Okay, I got it. Well, despite my former accusation on you I want to discuss some things with you about what happened and about....", Cal looked
    over her shoulder, confirming that the corridor was still empty. "About Logan."

    "What is with her. She is whiny cause she's a chick again. So what?", asked Will curiously.

    "Well, did you noticed that she always acted as if she was the head of ship's security and in command of all investigations?"

    "Yeah, and what's the problem? That's just her role."

    "No it isn't! Am I the only one who listened at the introduction?", hissed Cal angrily, "She is just responsible for the safety of some merchants on
    this ship, not the ship itself. She is taking control of the investigation but nobody seemed to care so far!"

    "Shit, you are right!", exclaimed Will, "Also she is the one who is last in standings, so she will do everything to win this round. But do you think
    she is the murderer?"

    "I am not sure, but perhaps."

    "Why are you even trusting me with this? It isn't like we were best friends.", asked Will.

    "Well, at first I am leading at the moment, so I can take some risk in trusting you, Rebecca or Gabe. I don't need this win as much as Logan. And,
    well Gabe is kind of difficult to read with his new obscure body and his affinity to this setting. And Rebecca is always kinda arrogant, especially in
    her new role as aristocrat. So I think me chances with you are the best. And I think I will notice if you try to betray me. But what is most
    important: We both are in control of the best armed forces on this ship and even if Gabe said our weapons were useless, I still thin that firepower
    will be important at some time of this drama."

    "Yeah, at least that is true. I just wanted to mobilize my squads and with your battle-sisters we can form a reasonable army."

    "Exactly, also Gabe said that someone took the eyes of the captain for the retina scanners at the escape pods. So I would suggest we use some of
    our troops to guard the pods. Just in case someone tries to escape. And using both our companions ensures that none of us can flee without
    notification of the other. So, do we have a plan?", asked Cal.

    "We have a plan. Let's Cal our troops and then ask Miss Security Chief a bit about her sudden promotion.", grinned Will and shook Cal's
    outstretched hand. "Sergeant Corbec, Colonel Musted speaking.", said Will using his personal intercom. "We have an emergency. Send Seargent
    Veers and his squad to guard the escape pods together with the sisters of the Bloody Rose. Nobody shall leave this ship! And then meet me with
    me command squad at the starboard hangar." To Cal he said: "I heard that the captain wanted to inspect a problem there yesterday evening.
    Perhaps he was even murdered there."

    "Okay, I will look at the medical station. The murderer must have used medical equipment to remove the eyes undamaged. I'll see if I find any
    clues. Perhaps we can somehow tie this round.", answered Cal. Then she turned around and went to the medical station at the larboard of the
    ship while speaking into her own intercom:"This Prioris Scarleth, Sister Sophia, Sister Cornelia and Sister Myra will go to the escape pods and
    guard them to together with the soldiers of the 43rd. None shall pass! The rest of the squad will meet me at the medical station. We have an
    heretic on board, who killed the captain and it is our duty to punish him."
