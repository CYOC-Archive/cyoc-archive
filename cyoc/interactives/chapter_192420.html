    It was quite disturbing seeing the flat slug that Patricia had turned into moving alongside Quincy. The Guess your weight machine was kinda like what you'd guess from the Entity. It had a habit of making the average person anorexic or obese, and Patricia was given a large layer of blubber. Then of course there was the limb Melter rollercoaster which made your limbs essentially boneless for awhile. Dr. Steinburg watched and saw that Patricia's eyes dimmed slightly less. Maybe she was getting some feeling that there was something off about her body... That said it didn't stop her from using her arms like the sticky hands you'd find at some arcade prize return. Stretch them out, crab onto something, and then pull herself in the direction she wanted to go.
    "Teach, Quincy wants Patricia back to Pete!" Quincy demanded
    Dr. Steinburg rolled her eyes and said "One more Entity and then I'll call Uncle Kaine."
    Claude asked "Why didn't you do that earlier to make Patricia into a man?"
    "Easy my little 5 wannabe. Uncle Kaine wants to get Patricia a resistance to all his Haze around here. So he'll want Patricia to change a lot from the entities here. So he'll get annoyed if I have to call him to reset Patricia each and every time. This'll be the last time Entity, and then we can get started on your debriefing." Claude wasn't that happy with that, but with Patricia's limbs starting to reform properly the group got to the hall of mirrors. Dr. Steinburg knew this was more akin to a mirror maze, but with it's entity twist.
    Patricia was breathing heavily and said "K Quincy, be in and out in a jiffy!" and waddled into the Hall of Mirrors. Quincy wanted to go with Patricia, but teach had convinced her to stay away from Entities for now. Her changes from the entities and serum had knocked off her Quim perfumed bodily fluids and a decent amount of her assets, and they would need to do some diagnostics on her.

    Dr. Steinburg thought "True love huh? Well if that actually exists then our Patricia should find her way back to our dimension... and I shouldn't have to tell some mind snooping 5 in training what happens if they leak my thoughts do I?". Steinburg glanced at Claude who was looking elsewhere.
    "Hey Quincy, how about we get some funnel cakes?" Claude asked trying to get Quincy's attention.
    Steinburg was lost in her thoughts again "Yep, the mirrors have transformatic powers... but more in a leaking other dimensions into our own sorta way. Touch or stand in front of a particular mirror for too long and your body will start to be traded with the version of you from a different universe. Stand even longer and your existence will be swapped as well and you'll find yourself stuck in that alternate dimension. The likelihood of touching a mirror? Extremely high. The likelihood you could find your way back? Incalculable, but presumably extremely high. If Fitzpatrick's hunch about True Love being Entity #1 is correct and his hunch about Pete and Quincy being influenced is also correct then their 5932-12 should find their way back... If not then with the Haze and right explanation no one should noticed anything wrong.

There was male chubby Patricia, male toddler chubby Patricia, doggy Patricia, Red-eyed Patricia, etc. Patricia had a sinking feeling she probably should keep moving, but also that she shouldn't touch any of the mirrors.
