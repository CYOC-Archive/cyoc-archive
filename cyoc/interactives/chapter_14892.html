You head toward him as the other pigboy has found his rabbit-man. As you turn to go from one hulking youth to the other. Horns are growing out of his skull as you race back across the arena. The other pigboy is attaching a peacock's tail to a boy with perfectly coifed hair, and a designer outfit.
