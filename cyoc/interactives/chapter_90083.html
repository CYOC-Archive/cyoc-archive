    "Katie," Mika said. "I need to talk to you real quick." Katie looked at her new friend with a puzzled look. "Okay Mika what's up?" She asked.

    Mika glanced around the garage before looking at Katie. "Did you make any more wishes on us since the beach?" She asked. Katie looked puzzled and paused, she was unsure what Mika was thinking but could see the look of concern on the young Asian girls face. "Of course not Mika," she exclaimed. "You've been here for all the changes I've made, why would you ask."

    Mika stared down at her shoes. She was feeling foolish for asking such a question. Why would Katie make a wish without telling her they were best friends, they had been for years. Mika stopped and shook her head. "No," she whispered. "That's wrong....what's happening to me."

    Mika looked up at Katie trying to focus. "Katie," she whispered. "Something's happening to me. I'm....I'm starting to think like a 12 year old girl. I don't know why but when you made me younger it started to affect me. I think....I think I'm starting to actually become kid."

    Katie looked at Mika with a look of concern. If the ring was still affecting the girls maybe she had something to do with it. "Mika I promise I haven't made any wishes," she said.

    "Then why do you think this is happening," Mika moaned. "I mean it's fun being young again but I don't want to actually relive my youth again."

    Katie placed her hand on the young girls shoulder. "We'll figure this out Mika I promise," she said. Mika looked up at Katie and gave her a weak smile. "Thanks," she sighed. "Maybe I'm just overreacting, it's not everyday you get to be a kid again. I hope it's just my imagination but just to be safe keep an eye on me and the others. Let me know if something seems off about us."

    Katie smiled and nodded. "That's the least I can do Mika," she said. "Now come on,there are five girls inside just waiting to have a fun sleepover."

    Mika smiled and paused. An idea was beginning to form in her head. "I think this will be fun," she said slyly. "But what if we made them even younger. Maybe be the babysitters. If you don't like that idea maybe we can all become a little younger experience a sleepover at seven or six or even five. Unless it's too much in which case let's just stay like this play with the ring later."

    Katie looked at Mika and smiled. The young girl was certainly mischievous and the idea was indeed tempting. Katie now thought about Mika's idea.
