    At your house Nic started shaking uncontrollably. "You all right dude?" Jack asks. Nic unusually replied, "Y-yeah j-j-just r-r-run far a-a-away from m-me." Jack just looked at you, bewildered.

    "I SAID RUN" Nic yelled. "Calm your tits Nic, this is my house? Why would I run away?" You ask calmly. "B-because I'm a monster" Jack looked at Nic. "You sure you don't need to go to the hospital dude? You look sick."

    "I'm sick because I don't want to hurt you but I will. I'm going to go on a rampage in five minutes, for five minutes. RUN AWAY" Nic screamed in an animalistic tone.

    Nic looked at you with animal eyes. "You will need it." The oven had five minutes left on the chicken.

    Just then, Nic doubled over in pain. He sobbed as another wave of pain centered in on his head. Cracking and popping sounds came from him as his body was breaking bones and re shaping them. Nic got bigger and muscular as a dark patch of fur expanded from the center of his chest. He fell on all fours as paws formed where his hands used to be. His teeth came into his jaw for a spit second and a row of razor sharp teeth replaced them. Finally, his head reshaped into a long snout and his ears became pointed. He looked exactly like a wolf at first glance, but if you looked closer it would appear he would be more muscular.

    Nic's words bounced around your head. "Loose my sanity for five minutes..." Jack screeched as Nic jumped at him and butted him into the nearest wall. You took a piece of pipe from the counter and made contact with Nic's spine. Nic howled in pain as he reared on you. "Fuck" was all you could say as he slammed you into the counter. Jack scooted over to you and sat against the wall. Nic but down hard on your leg. "Fuuuckk, help me!" Pain shot up and down your body and made you dizzy. Nic jumped over to Jack and bit down on his forearm. "Stop it Nic!"

I'm so sorry, please forgive me." He came over and licked up most of the blood that was coming from your leg.
