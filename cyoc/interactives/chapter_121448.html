    Your human companions had a somewhat different recollection of those first
    days of the New World Order.

    Scientists had discovered ancient technologies in the old city of
    Babylon that promised unlimited energy by altering reality.

    The theory was,and the scientists were quick to point out
    that this reality altering would be kept contained
    within magnetic hyperspace chambers and could never ever spill out into the real world.

    The first of these new power sources had been built at the Old West town of
    Tombstone.

    You and your friends had headed over to witness
    the big event and just
    before the lights went out
    permanently you had been babbling about
    Tia-Mats granddaughter,,the horrors if the new technology failed and actually had been praying to
    salvation to those ancient
    Babylonian dragon gods and goodesses.

    Then the switch had been thrown and your human companions had
    watched in horror
    and you turned into a young Babylonian dragon goddess yourself.

    Basically you had grown bigger and scaly and horned as they themselves began shrinking in size.

    When it was done the Babylon Old West was the New World Order.

    You yourself no longer remember ever being human.

    You were now a five year old granddaughter of rootin tootin
    "Ma TiaMat" who your grandaddy Sheriff Apsu had tamed eons before you came into existence.

    So your human companions had tried to avoid getting squashed by all those
    Victorian era boots all the "Western" dragon citizens
    of Tombstone were clomping about in.

    Luckily for your human companions,Sheriff Apsu
    noticed all those tiny bipedal creatures running about,and since as Sheriff he was
    sworn
    to defend all creatures in his town,he
    soon had them safe on a table where 5 year old dragoness you
    stared in wide eyed wonder at the talking little creatures who
    called themselves hu man sss

    Naturally those hu man sss
    said they were now the size of rats and mice

    which confused everyone in Tombstone
    because as everyone knows

    Baaly the Kid ( rat) Bat Mardukty ( mouse) and
    they're assorted rodent outlaws are not the size

    hu mon sss
    but just as big as dragons.
