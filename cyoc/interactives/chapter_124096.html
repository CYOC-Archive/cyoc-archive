    A group of friends decide to host a small party on the 6th of June 2006 (06/06/06). The group are made up of drunk college lasses looking for a little game. The little lasses are Jonas, James, Kirumi, Liu, steven, Amanda, Scarlet and Rose and each ones bio is below:

    Jonas is a cocky American rich kid from the California state. He sees everyone as below him and is often known to speak his mind. He fears nothing, not even a god if he met one. His appearance is slightly athletic and is quite a charmer at first appearance with nice ginger hair and blue eyes.

    James is a polar opposite of Jonas despite being identical twins. James got sprayed at birth and lived in a poor household. He is kind but extremely shy and often is scared of a lot. He met up with Jonas while they were at college and they learned of the connection they had with each other. His appearance is pretty much identical to Jonas bit not as athletic.

    Kirumi is a sweet Japanese girl. She loves art and is quite shy just like James. This in turn led to them going out and being boyfriend and girlfriend but they haven't had their first kiss yet due to being shy. She is quite intelligent also and her appearance is Japanese and athletic with green eyes and raven black hair.

    Liu is a Chinese relative to Kirumi. He is quite a laid back person and will often resort to things in a pacifistic way, thus leads him to hate Jonas for being violent. He is quite on the short side but not athletic with slight fat and is slightly tanned with black hair and brown eyes.

    Steven is an anti-social person. He suffered from aspdand is deemed as a sociopath. He wants to know how to be a normal person but doesn't know how. He sometimes is rude but is only trying to be good. His appearance is quite a both appearance. He has a lot of piercings and and he dyed his hair black. His eyes are quite a bright green which contrasts his main wear which also hides his chubby body.

    Amanda and Scarlett are pretty much identical personality wise. They both are quite bitchy since they came from rich backgrounds and are often have threesomes with Jonas since they can all stand each other. Amanda is quite busty while Scarlett is quite modest in appearance buf both are athletic and the two both dyed their hair blonde and both have blue eyes.

    Rose is the big sister type. She is easy going and quite the chatter but when it is necessary she will be serious. People talk to her about some very serious topics and she will help them get through the difficulties. She easily knows when something is amiss or when someone lies and will quickly point it out. She often supports Steven in trying to be a normal human and Steven actually has a crush on Rose but she knows but will wait until he says it himself. In her appearance she is quite athletic and quite busty. She does yoga which gives her high flexibility and also has a plump ass. Her hair is a messy brunette and reached down to her but and her eyes are slightly blue/grey colour.

    The 8 college students all sit around a table. On the table is a satanic circle and they want to summon the devil himself and perform a contract. Steven proposed the idea and had everything needed in the ritual which were the candles and sacrificial item.

    Steven placed the items onto the circle which were three things. An upside down cross, a lamb's heart and goat horns. By this point, the 8 lasses were chanting the ritual but in their drunken state, they mixed up the words. The chant should have been in Latin and should have gone:

    "Devil, oh devil. I come to make a pact.
    Devil, oh devil. I offer up no soul.
    I only wish to for a pact so please, oh please.
    Do not ruin my soul."

    It didn't go like that. Their drunken minds made them say:

    "Devil, oh devil. I come to make a pact.
    Devil, oh devil. I wish for transformation.
    I am wishing for a game so please, oh please.
    Make a transformation game."

    This made the 8 lasses sign a contract where the devil can decide on a game for them to play and the losers permanently get transformed.

    The next instance smoke pops out the circle and the devil appears. The devil speaks to them and mentions that he will perform 7 trials. Each trial will have 1 loser who gets transformed from his selection of cookies and the remaining people go on to the next round. The loser may pick from a plate of cookies with each doing a certain transformation based on the cookies appearance.

    They 8 lasses all look at each other wondering what to do. It comes down to what the devil wants and they all don't know how the devil will play.
