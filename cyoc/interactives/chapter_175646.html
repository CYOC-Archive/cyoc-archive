It was more like a vision being beamed into my brain. Make that two visions. I was simultaneously watching the bride and groom say their vows from a seat near the back of the church and ALSO watching the groom's face, inches in front of me.

The bride could see the man scratching his head and as he lowered his hand I could see the bride FROM the man, as she looked at me and he looked at her. It was the strangest experience ever, to be looking at myself from two different parts of a room!
