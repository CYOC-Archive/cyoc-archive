    Now Ty's brother Dustin didn't care a hoot about the mythical creature called a dragon but her did have a keen interest
    in shipwrecks and their place in modern history.

    So the next morning the two brothers headed out the house and passed
    the two 135 year old statues of Grant and Lee that stood at opposite sides of the park before the two brothers
    reached the high school.

    They shared a joint history class on modern history and the
    topic for the last while had been about Fascist Italy's role in
    World War Two.
    So Dustin was pleasantly surprised and Ty was bored
    when Miss Chipps announced that today she had two special guests
    a deep sea wreck diver
    and
    a researcher who specialized in modern naval history.

    The lights went off and the video played whilst the deep sea diver explained her side of the mystery.
    She'd been doing some multi beam scans of the seafloor
    off of the island of Bermuda and the sonar scans had
    picked up the image of a intact wreck one thousand feet
    down that looked like the wreck of a submarine,but one
    that didn't fit the configuration of any German U boat she'd even seen.
    The bow of the wreck was BIG like it had lots of torpedo tubes
    jockeying for a spot.
    So once she and her team had gotten into their deep sea pressure chamber they'd descended to the sea floor and confirmed that she was no German U boat but instead had
    the guns and other trimmings from the Fscist Italian Navy
    on her.
    She had 12 torpedo tubes up at her bow section and it was
    clear she was meant to unlease a massive torpedo attack on her
    targets,
    The divers had swum into her open forward hatch and discovered to their astonishment and excitemrnt that she still
    carried 24 Japanese Long Lance torpedos in her,the kind with
    a range of 105 miles and with a tonne of
    super high explosive Methulene in each one.
    The divers were getting even more excited as they realized that
    she was a super weapon,capable of delivering the
    equivalent of fifty tonnes of TNT in one horrific blast that could
    take out the mightiest of the Allies battleships or aircraft carriers.
    Clear Il'Duces answer to all the nift super weapons everyone
    else was building during WW2.
    From the looks of her hatches and crews wallets and spare clothes they found aboard her the divers learned she was
    the Dragon.
    Her crew had scuttled her to the briny deep at the end of the War
    rather then handing over her secrets to the victorious
    Allies.

    At which point the naval researcher took over the special lesson.
    Clearly everyone wanted to know what the Dragon had been up to during the War and it appeared generally not much of anything beyond ferrying Axis spies to Allied beaches.
    She didn't actually do what she had been built for,which was to
    be the Italian Navys equivalent of Germanys pocket battleship
    the Bismark.

    However the researcher had gone deeper into the Italian Navy
    archives and made a connection to the submarines whereabouts
    that could have changed world history bigtime.
    It seemed on the days the Allied leaders Roosevelt,Mackenzie
    King,Stalin and Churchill had all been gathered on one of
    the Allied ships dickering over the post War world
    the Dragon had nbeen a mere three miles away from them,her
    12 torpedo tubes loaded with Long Lances and ready to blow the four Allied chieftains into Lunar orbit
    but alas there'd been a fire control problem and the Dragon
    had missed her chance to alter history.

    The class began to discuss what their world would have been
    like in this alternative reality where Il'Duce got to lord it
    over everyone and found the New Roman Empire he always
    dreamed about.
    All but for a Fire Control short circuit
    the Empire was lost.

    So Dustin and Ty departed after school was over and Dustin kept
    talking about what it would be like if Il'Duce had gotten
    the big break the submarine had offered him.
    And that night,bored Ty relented and wished the Dragons
    Fire Control had actually worked properly that long ago day
    on the Atlantic when the four Allied leaders had been in the
    submarine Captains sights.
    And so it came to be.

    The world didn't change immediately or even overnight.
    It was some time in the early dawns rays that the impact
    of Tys changing history reached
    2019.
    At first things appeared normal enough for the two brothers
    but as they passed the park where the two American Civil Wr
    statues of Grant and Lee aught to be
    they both immediately noticed that
    in that park now stood two heroically
    posed statues of the Roman Emperors
    Claudius
    and
    Augustus....
