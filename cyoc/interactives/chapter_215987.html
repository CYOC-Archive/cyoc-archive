    “Well yeah, but-“ Shara started.

    Then she spotted a tiny little wooden stand selling oysters.
    Shara started thinking ‘Always wanted to try oysters… Colin probably won’t mind if I grab a couple….’

    She picked up a shell and sucked down the snotty clump of meat.
    “That’s… pretty good actually.” Shara said, dousing another in hot sauce before slurping it down, “Now we’re talking!”
    She was down to the last four oysters when a man pinned a sign to the stand’s window.
    The shell dropped out of her hand as her heart filled with dread.
    It was a Health Inspection Rating…

    A big.
    Bold.
    Red.
    F.
    Shara’s stomach violently gurgled as the weight of her decision sunk in with nothing but a simple
    “Uh oh……”

    “Got ghosted, huh?” A voice said as someone sat across from Colin.
    “Not exactly…” Colin sighed.
