You need to leave and get help: You're the only one who knows about this potential threat, and you need to alert EVERYONE before you end up caught!

    You back away from the wall, getting ready to sprint out of the house and call the Government, The Police, Anyone; That is, until you hear a wiff-ing noise and end up getting clonked on the head.
