    The baker stops laughing and says to you. "Well aren't you just adorable! I could not have asked for a better test subject! Someone who wants to be chocolate AND wants to be experimented on? This is just too good to be true! I have other things i can do to you and i know a bit of magic as well."

Everyone who turns eventually gets used to it, and I make sure they are not abused and well taken care of until they have expired."

    You reply with a snarky remark: "They don't know what they're missing; I've wanted to be made of chocolate since I was least twelve. I would envy cartoon and storybook characters who would turn. I kept a 50 lb box of chocolate by my bed encase I would get lucky and could make a mold of myself. I would spend hours on the internet looking up pictures of things made of chocolate; and for other stories of people who had been turned into chocolate. If you had eaten me only minutes after turning me, Those few minutes would have felt more real and significant than the 30 years bland normal years I was a human. If I had known you could have turned me into chocolate, you wouldn't have been fighting me to put me in the vat; you would have been fighting me to keep me out of the store!"

    He busts out in laughter, he cannot believe what he's hearing! He tries to regain his composure and just smiles at you and says: "I like you. I'm glad I didn't decide to mute you and overwrite your mind. Your the first one I've met who actually WANTS to be chocolate, so I will make you a deal. I'll change you to be whatever you want as long as you promise to not leave the store until all of your experiments are done. You can even play as my little assistant until then. What do you say?"

    "Can I leave after that?" You ask the baker.

    "Absolutely! That is, of course, unless you ask for something to be done to you makes you unable to leave." he replies with his rather cheerful voice.

    "What if I don't like the change?" You say with a slight bit of worry from his answer.

    "If you do not like it, I will change you into something else upon your request. Or, if something goes horribly wrong without for consent, I will restore you to as you are right now. If you ask for something like inanimacy, I'll change you back after a day to see if you still like it."

    "You promise?" You demand from the baker.
