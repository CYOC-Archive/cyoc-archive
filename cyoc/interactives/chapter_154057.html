    "Gloria, really? You know we don't have to pair miss Haskins up with anybody." Sasha said before looking at Gloria who was giving her a puppy eyed look.

    "God, Heather, I can't believe you're letting little Malcolm go to this woman's house again." The boy's aunt said her sister, Malcolm's mother.

    "Oh come on now, Sarah, I think it's cute that he's got a little crush." Heather, Malcolm's mother said to her sister, Sarah.

    As both women and the young boy were approaching the house, Gloria used her ring to transform the tree of them. Sarah and Heather regressed from their adult womanly bodies, but didn't become little kids like Gloria and the girls had, instead Gloria made the two 14 and it looked like it might be their last year trick-or-treating with Heather now dressed up as Kim Possible while Sarah was dressed up as Shego. Malcolm who was dressed up as a little He-Man grew to become a 44 year-old man in a He-Man outfit, which almost looked a little silly but the man clearly loved dressing up with his now daughters.

    Maddie Haskins opened the door to see the three of them.

    "Trick or Treat Miss Haskins." Heather and Sarah said to Miss Haskins.

    "Oh, hello girls. Last year trick or treating, huh?" Maddie asked them.

    "Yeah. Dad insisted, because he wanted to have one last fun memory with his two favorite girls." Sarah said pointing to their dad who Maddie hadn't noticed until Sarah pointed him out.

    "Hello Maddie." Malcolm greeted Maddie.

    Maddie almost didn't say anything immediately. She couldn't believe it, but she was love-struck when she saw Malcolm in his He-Man outfit with his muscular chest in clear view of her.

    "Uh, Miss Haskins? Miss Haskins?" Heather said waving a hand in front of her face.

    "What? Oh, yes. Here's your candy girls. Uh, hi there Malcolm, I....uh...I didn't know you'd dress up too. You know, I was always more of a fan of She-Ra growing up." Maddie said as she chuckled a little bit.

    "Well maybe another Halloween we could go to a costume party as He-Man and She-Ra." Malcolm suggested.

    "Oh, ye-yeah, sounds cool." Maddie said trying not to look nervous.

    "Okay Miss Haskins, well we've got to get going, see you later." Sarah said.

    "Right. I'll see you around the neighborhood." Maddie trying to act as cool as possible as Malcolm and his daughters left.

    Gloria and the girls had watched this whole exchange happen not too far away and were giggling a little at how much like a teenager Miss Haskins was acting around Malcolm.

    "Wow, I didn't think she'd be someone who gets a school girl crush like that." Sasha said.

    "I know, right? By the way, Gloria, how come you made the mom and aunt teenagers? Why didn't you make them kids?" Naomi asked.

    "I thought about it, but then I thought: it might be fun to have some new classmates and who knows? Maybe in the future, Adam and Nate will have some stepsisters?" Gloria explained as they all now continued the night.
