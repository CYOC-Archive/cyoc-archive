    Choosing new Royalty

    Ally decided against using magic, considering that 'Master' George may have someone sensitive to magic keeping an Eye on the Barn.
    It might not be a problem if she did it inside the Barn, because the Queens own enormous reserves of magical energy would most likely make her own power seem like a slight fluctuation at most, but doing it out in the open would pose a significant risk.
    The Guards may not even fall asleep for all she knows.
    Fighting them directly is a possibility she also quickly gave up on. She would most likely win the fight, but probably not before they could
    call reinforcements, wich Ally, despite her skill, would likely be unable to fight off.
    Ally Llaemadam had never been someone to give up easily and thought about many different possibilities, including but not limited to:
    telling the Guards that she was supposed to wash the Queen and the Bull, running to the Guards and telling them that something happened nearby and somehow quietly making a hole in either the Wall or Roof of the the Barn, that she could get in through.
    She scrapped most of those Ideas, before finally thinking of something that she knew would work, something that involved little risk.
    She remembered that her Target was to get the Queen, but she also remembered that the wording of the Contract she had made
    never specifically mentioned that she needed to get the Queen.
    Her Contract was for the 'recovery of someone capable of restoring the Monarchy', wich included any and all Decendants and Relatives of the Queen. No one ever mentioned that they needed to be of any specific Species.
    The Barn housing the Queen's Offspring was barely guarded, there were even times when they weren't watched at all.
    She retreated quickly before being discovered and completed the Farmwork that was expected of her for the rest of the Day.
    At night Ally snuck out of the Building she and the other Prisoners were being held in, avoided the Guards and finally arrived at the Barn housing all the Cows and Bulls that the Queen had given birth to.
    Many of the Queen's Offspring had been sold, but the Farmer kept the ones he deemed to be truly exeptional in some way.
    Ally quietly slipped into the Barn and was faced with an impressive number of Stalls, all of wich were housing Cattle.
    She had made it this far and all she had to do now was to choose one of the Cows or Bulls, that she would transform into an Elf with the Scroll she had prepared, but she hadn't expected there to be so many of them.
    Ally Llaemadam was faced with a very unique problem, that very few People throughout History ever had to deal with.
    Ally had to choose, at this moment, who she wanted to rule an entire Country.
    She was also faced with a Problem that no one in recorded history has ever faced.
    Ally Llaemadam had to choose a new King or Queen, from a Barn filled with Livestock.
