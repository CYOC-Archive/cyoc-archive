    After he had his fun Barry took off his costume and put his normal clothes back on again along with taking off his clown nose. This returned his skin back to its normal color. Popsy showed him over to his dressing corner this is where his costumes, all of his props and his clown nose would be kept. Hanging all of his stuff up, Tootsy and Alfie walked over next to Popsy as she said alright youre all set your normal start time will be to come over right after school ends so that you have sometime to get ready and rehearse for shows. Barry agreed, when all of a sudden he go walloped by three whipped cream pies in the face, chest and stomach this knocked him over whipping the cream from his eyes he saw the three clowns standing there laugh at him as they said welcome to the circus!!

    Leaving the circus and arriving at home Barry’s mom asked if he had found a job yet. He said yes I did over at the local carnival. His mom said oh?! Doing what?” Replying he told her, “yeah… um.. I’m one of the people who runs the games.” He didn’t want to tell her about being clown thinking that she wouldn’t approve. That night before going to bed Barry had called Popsy and told her about the story he told his mom. She told him that she would send of a game runners uniform over to his house right away so that he could put that on before he came to the circus to keep his cover story. Barry said thank you and then went to bed. The next morning Barry received a notification on his phone going down stairs on his porch there was a box address to him. Bringing it up to his room he found a white button up shirt. A matching set of bright red pants with white stars on it with a matching red vest. Along with a red bowler hat, a container for make up and a red clown nose. Reading the note in the box.
    “Dear Barry,
    Here is your games uniform, you will find a matching set of pants and vest and a set of make up. This is just a simple set of clown make up that we have all of the people running the games wear this make up and a normal clown nose. Just put on this uniform and put on your makeup before you leave school so that everyone will actually think that is his job.”

    From,
    Popsy

    Folding it up he put it into a separate bag went downstairs grabbed some breakfast from his mom and left to hop on to the bus. Arriving at school he met up with his friends Blake and Trevor he told them about his new job at the carnival as a game runner. They didn’t like much of it. And went along with their days. At lunch Barry was sitting there eating with his friends when two people he recognized but never really talked to, Allen and Tiffany they both sat down together with the 3 boys for lunch. Which confused them since they had never done that before. Once the final bell rang Barry walked over to the bath room and put on his game runners uniform and putting on his makeup and nose, making his way out to his bike his mom dropped off during the day. He peddled his way over to the carnival where he walked passed all of the carnival goers over into the back of the circus tent. Saying hi to Popsy, Barry turned around to see Allen and Tiffany there as well confused they said hi. He asked why they were here. They both giggled and put on there clown noses as Barry watched Allen and Tiffany turn into Alfie and Tootsy. Barry couldn’t believe he was so dumb. Moving passed that Barry walked over to his dressing room grabbing his clown nose and putting it on he watched as his whole body turned back into that of a clown’s. He grabbed one of his costumes. And put it on, waddling back out of the dressing room looking like a clown. He went over to Alfie and Tootsy and they showed him their routine for the night. Tootsy explained, one thing that our circus does that others don’t is perform magic that really get the audience involved. They can change other people into all kinds of stuff. Animals, robots other clowns, really anything we could come up with. Any changes that they would perform tonight would only be a active while a participant is in the circus tent if they leave they will go back to normal. Excited to learn Alfie explained and got ready for the show.
