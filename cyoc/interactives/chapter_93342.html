Perhaps this ring she had was the cause of it? There was one way for her to find out.

Briefly the idea of simply doing away with them and letting it out where she pleased entertained her mind before she knocked it away with a shake of her head. Not least because she didn’t yet know what, if any, limits there might be to this Ring. More testing would be needed to assess its capabilities, the question she asked herself being, just how would she test it?
