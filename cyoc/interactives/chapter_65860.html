    I made twenty devices so far. I then invited several friends to a rented meeting room at the library for the unveiling. I invited more than twenty
    people, both men and women, because I was sure that several would not want to play. I specifically gave Sean the wrong time intending for him
    to show up late.

    Several of my friends had a friendly rivalry going on. We often competed against each other in track even though we were on the same team.
    Our competition overflowed into other areas as well. We competed on the basketball court. We competed to see who could bench press the
    most weight. We competed to see who could squeeze through a hole that some dog had dug under a fence. We were always challenging each
    other but it was all in fun. None of us wanted to hurt each other but we did enjoy pwning each other.

    After I passed out the statuary rays and explained how they worked, I began to divide the group up into teams and explain the rules for a
    variation of freeze tag.

    "Rule number one: Don't zap anyone in such a way that could get them in serious trouble. For example, don't zap someone while he's at work
    and might get fired from his job and don't zap someone while he's driving a car. Once zapped, a person is nearly helpless so stick around and
    make sure there are no serious consequences."

    "Rule number two: Let's keep this game a secret. Don't zap anyone in public or where you can be caught by anyone who isn't already in on our
    secret game."

    "At the end of the month, we'll tally up who's been zapped and how often. The individual whose been zapped the most and the team that has
    been zapped the most will each have to pay a forfeit. Scoring will be based on frequency not duration so you won't get any extra points for
    leaving a fellow player frozen for longer than an hour. Also don't keep re-zapping the same person. Let's make it a rule that after one of us is
    able to move again, we have at least an hour before we can be zapped again. Any zaps during that hour don't count toward the final tally. Let's
    also set up this meeting room and the entire library as a neutral zone where we can meet under a truce if we wish to talk with the opposing
    team. But just like freeze tag, don't hover around. Give your intended victim a chance to get away before you zap him. The game begins now
    but it would make the game no fun if half of us are zapped the second we leave the library."

    Riley asked, "Once we've zapped someone, what are we allowed to do with them?"

    I told them, "Almost anything you want. Both Bobby and I can tell you that being zapped feels really good, better than having sex with your
    girlfriend. Because it feels so good, I'm sure none of you will mind a little mischief at your expense. Just use some common sense remembering
    that whatever you do to one of us when we are immobile and helpless can easily be done back to you when you are zapped and become
    immobile and helpless. I've arranged a little demonstration of just what kind of mischief this statuary ray makes possible. I told Sean the wrong
    time on purpose and according to my watch, he should be arriving soon. Then I'll zap him and we can have some fun."

    We waited in anticipation. There was some chatter but the room was mostly quiet like we were about to spring a birthday surprise on Sean
    although it wasn't his birthday and we were giving ourselves the present.

    The moment arrived. I stood up as I heard the door handle move. I prepared my aim. Just as I saw Sean walk through the door, I felt waves of
    electricity pass over my body. Bobby had zapped me.

    Bobby stood up and said, "Don't you think it was unfair to make a demonstration out of Sean without giving Sean any idea of what to expect?
    That's exactly what you did to me the first time you zapped me. I did enjoy the experience but I was terrified because I didn't know what to
    expect. So I owe you. Now let's strip you down so you can show everyone your hard on and show just how insanely pleasurable it is to be hit by
    a statuary ray. Then we can get down to having some more fun."

    Even though I was enjoying the physical sensations flowing over and through my body, I was embarrassed as my pants slid to the floor
    revealing my hard on to everyone in the room.
