His best friends had all gone out of town, and there was no way Dustin was going out with Ty and Peter, who were younger than him. I would like to have some fun, or go do something…” Dustin mumbled. Then he looked at Peter. “My birthday is coming up next week. I would like to do something different.” Peter looked at him feeling a little confused. But then he picked up on Dustin’s thoughts.

    “I know what you want for your birthday!” Peter said out loud. Dustin looked at him with a smile. “I wish I could borrow your powers for a couple of days!” Dustin said to him. Peter thought about it, and figured that he wanted to take a break from always having to think about what he would wish for, and he didn’t mind being a normal kid for just a few days.

With aloud bark Peter stood up on the bed, realizing that Dustin had now changed him into a dog!

Dustin winked at him and pointed at the little, black puppy dog. “What?? Peter turned himself into a dog?” Ty said, with complete surprise. “No, I did that!” Dustin responded.

    Then Ty felt power coming from Dustin! Dustin grinned, as Ty slowly transformed into a dog as well, but a brown cocker. In an instant, Ty had become a dog, and the two dogs began to bark at Dustin. “Alright you two, I’ll change you back!” Dustin said, as he wished for both boys to return to normal. Ty looked at Peter after he was done changing. “You did what? You gave Dustin your powers? But why?” Ty asked him, almost shouting at Peter. “It’s his birthday present!” Peter said, ‘It’s only for three days!” he continued.

Good… You know, I always wanted to have a little brother who was ten years old!” And immediately, Peter gasped as he started to grow younger and younger. Peter was now only five feet tall, and he was smaller and shorter than before! All the muscles were gone, for Ty had transformed him into a little ten year-old boy! “Whoa! I was an adult yesterday, and now I’m a little kid!” Peter said, with his high voice.

Dustin concentrated and little Peter was suddenly floating in the air, as he floated right next to Dustin, who was sitting in the recliner in front of the TV. “I need to add something to this ten year old boy as well, so he can play with the other boy down the street. Besides, I’ll get my powers back in two days, no matter what. So Dustin can do whatever he wants! He turned me into an adult last night and we went to the bar!”
