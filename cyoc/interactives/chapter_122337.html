    Now Mrs Petunia Foxtrot ,Mayor of the town,was out walking her twin poodles in the dog park later that evening.

    Preports had been coming in all day about a golden scaled dragon flying aimlessly above the
    town and there'd
    been some weird sighting of a half sheep woman seen now the university tower.

    So the Mayor wasn't too surprised when a young nude asian woman with glowing fiery slitted eyes stepped out
    of the bushes of the park and demanded her clothes.

    For a second Mrs Foxtrot thought she recognized the figure as one of the
    university students,a Joanna,who occassionaly had visited town hall
    but quickly she realized when you've got fiery slitted eyes,talons for finger and toenails,a hissing growly
    manner of speech and rather large fangs in those distended jaws of yours,theres
    only one thing you can be

    a golden scaled flamebreather recently seen winging above the town

    THE Dragon

    So quickly Mrs Foxtrot stripped down to her corset and bloomers and asked the monster if it
    wanted those too
    but the monster was happy with what it got.

    It put on the human clothing and asked Foxtrot if it looked passably human.

    Lying the Mayor said
    "Yes"
    and thought she was done with the creature
    when it grabbed one of the yapping poodles and ate it
    in nine mouthfuls.

    The horrified Mrs Foxtrot thought
    "Nope,this not Joanna"

    Sensing this was a never before seen event,Mrs Foxtrot asked if she could follow the creature for a while.

    The monster agreed,but told her to leave behind the other poodle,as it
    might get hungry again.

    Realizing she might be the dragons next meal if none other were around
    Mrs Foxtrot ignored the monsters advice and took along the
    sole yappy poodle.
