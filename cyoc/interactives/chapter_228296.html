    Just as you and your girlfriend were going to try out the machine. A tall guy walked right in front of you. He introduced himself to Dr.Terrific as Jack McCall. He was 6'3 210.

    He looked to be around 18 or 20. He had blonde hair, blue eyes and muscles. He was the perfect candidate to be a superhero. He handed the doctor his filled out forms. She looked it over then looked at him. She nodded with approval and escorted him into the machine. She strapped him down explaining what was about to happen. Once he was secure, Dr.Terrific started up the machine lights flickered and technical noise were heard. The out of the machine came ...

    Jack McCall

    The machine apparently didn't work. He wasn't in any flashy costumes and didn't have any powers he looked the same as he did when entered the machine.

    Dr. Terrific did every test she could think of to see if he had any powers and the results showed he had none. She apologized profusely to Jack about this and paid a small fortune for his inconvenience.

    Everyone left the convention disappointed.

    That night Jack McCall had taken his shower and went to bed. He was selected to be the next superhero and it didn't happen. As he closed his eyes and fell asleep a strange tingling was happening to his body. The muscles that Jack worked so hard to maintain in high school as a star football player, had vanished all that was left was a toned body with no definition. The eyes of Jack suddenly opened but it wasn't Jack who was appearing out of them.

    It was someone new. Someone who had a smile on his face as if he had finally been awake thanks to that machine. He lifted the body out of the bed. He felt waited down by the clothes that were on. So he removed them and stood there naked. He went into the bathroom and looked at the body and face he was working with

    The features of the chiseled face of Jack began to soften as this new being looked in the mirror. The lips began to will themselves into being fuller and more lush. His noses shrank as his eyes began to grow. His lashes also grew longer and fuller. His hair styled into a more pompadour away from the buzz cut of Jack Wore proudly.

    This new being licked his lips and smiled. He left the bathroom and went back to the bedroom and put on his superhero a clear mesh tank crop top with pink lining and a skimpy bright pink thong that hid very little. He also put a pink domino mask that just meld to his face.

    He opened the door and down to the streets for some crime fighting he didn't have to search long. He spotted a couple guys robbing a convenient store. He rushed over there. "Excuse me bois, but I hope you're paying for those." He said with a sultry voice the petty crooks looked up and to see who just said that. When they saw the figure in his superhero outfit. They just laughed. He then moved into action as he grabbed one of the robbers and with his vice-like grip was able to subdue him. He then turned his attention to the other robber who started running at him. The robber tripped into his hand with a slight kiss to the robber cheek, he too was immobilized.

    So began the new adventures of TwinkBoi
