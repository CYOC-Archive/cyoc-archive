    ... saw another great change opportunity. This time, it wasn't the shop she wanted to change, but the people standing outside of it. She was looking at the local toy store, where a father was desperately trying to control his daughter. She was practically screaming as she tried to move towards the, while her father stood firm.
    "Honey, for the last time, no!" He yelled, clearly embarrassed by his unruly child.

    Paula looked at both of them. The father was on the younger side, only in his early thirties at most. He had blonde scruffy hair that was beginning to thin. He had a bit of a beer gut starting to grow, as taking care of children was likely too hard of a job for him. The little girl was about five, and was wearing overalls with short hair and a purple shirt. She looked like a bit of a tomboy, so was probably wanting something not doll related inside.

    Paula took a picture, making sure to not change the whole store by only getting those two in shot. The photo came out and Paula was almost a little disappointed. The little girl had grown, looking to be about in her mid fifties. Her hair was longer but clearly starting to grey, and her features were that of a MILF. Beside her was an elegant white poodle, attempting to pee on a fire hydrant.
    "No!" The woman yelled. "No Fifi, bad girl!" She scolded.

    "That's not exactly what I had in mind." Paula thought annoyed. "Oh well, attempt number two." She said as she took another photo.
    She pulled out the photo, and as was much more pleased with the outcome. The woman, former daughter, was younger again, but this time she was around the father's original age.

    She was your standard woman with long brown hair, styled into a nice braid, and had some nice assets which Paula noticed as she walked in her clean business skirt. Beside her was an older woman. She had blonde hair cut in a bob with an even fancier business suit, who was holding a clipboard next to her, and appeared to be the 40 something year old superior. Paula looked up, and it seemed that the former father was now the former daughter's boss. The boss was blabbing about some nonsense as they stood in front of the toy store, blocking some kids.

    Paula liked the daughter's change, but felt that the father didn't need this much power. Adjusting her shot, she managed to get a picture of just the former father. This time, she was laughing at the change. Standing beside the former daughter was a little girl. She was adorable with her blonde hair tied in pigtails and she was dressed in a pink skirt.
    "Mommy?" The new little girl asked the former young girl. "Can I get a new dolly?"

    "Well, Mommy has to drop you off at daycare and then get to work, but I promise as soon as I'm done and if you're a good girl today we can get you something, ok?" She calmly explained.

    "Yay!" The little girl cheered as she held the new mothers hand and the two walked off.

    "There." Paula thought. "He seems like he'll enjoy growing up as a girl way more than a guy. Plus, that mother seems born to be a good role model.


    Paula walked down the street some more, seeing some opportunities for some fun, but not seeing anything fully grabbing her attention. That was at least, until she spotted a spot that really peaked her interest.

    Where does she go to change people?
