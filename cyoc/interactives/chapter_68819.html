    a weird feeling swept over my body. it was a strange numbing feeling as i watched my reflection raise its hand and began licking it with its sand paper tongue. it just felt right as i gave myself a cat bath. i wondered why i had done it any other way before. after i finished my arm i looked at my body. the clothes i was wearing wear in the way, but i had to wear clothes, i had a friend over. my clothes seemed baggy too, i think i was shrinking.

    "Micky, I found the amulet and-what are you doing?!" he said as I had moved onto my feet. i was blushing out of embaressemnet. I should have closed the door before I began my bath. I was defiantly shrinking as my friend who was normally about the same height as me was almost a foot taller when I stood up. I got on the balls of my feet to try and even the size, besides my feet felt better that way.

    "Meow" I said glancing at the mirror again. fur had began to grow over the sides of my cat face.

    "Mickey Come here!" Jake said calling me as he left. he looked worried, i wonder how to cheer him up. i followed clumsily on my two legs. I began to wonder why I walked on two legs anyway. Jake sat down on the couch, as i hopped on next to him. i was starting to get itchy, my fur under my inceasingly larger clothes growing. i was maybe three feet tall now.

    "Ok, Micky according to this note you'll be stuck like this for a day or so. now i don't think your parents will like seeing you like this so i think you'll need to come over to my house till you change back."

    "Meow" i responded. i didn't really mind. however when i agreed he scratched the back of my changing ears. it felt too good. I instinctively began to purr as he continued and my body finished its changes. i wondered what would happen next?
