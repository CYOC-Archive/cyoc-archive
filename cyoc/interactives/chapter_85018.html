An hour later he came back and started to pack. "What's going on?" Samantha asks.

    "Go pack, get the twins, Julie and Ryley to do the same." James said.

    "Why?"

    "We're moving." He looked at her. "We just inherited the zoo, under the condition we stay in the castle for a year." He kept packing some clothes and then started packing some necessities as he took the bag and took it to his car. He then went and to help his younger siblings while Samantha packed and helped the twins. After the packing was done they drove down to the zoo. There they found the castle, well they had parked in the lot and walked into the zoo, with their things in hand.

She had handed it to her brother. "Hmmm... Mammals, Reptiles, Amphibians, Birds, Insects, Aquatic, Jurassic.... myth.... and Ice Age? Um... okay."

    "Well, it says here there are three sections I know there shouldn't be. Jurassic usually refers to dinosaurs, myth is something that shouldn't exist, Ice age is... pretty self explanitory."
