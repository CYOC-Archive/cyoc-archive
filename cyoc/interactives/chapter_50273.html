    Getting off the bus Jen tried not to draw attention to herself. No sooner than the bus had left though she spotted her boyfriend. Her twin hearts beat rapidly as she tried to avoid her boyfriend seeing her in in this condition. Almost to the door and the percieved safety of the school she heard a gasp.
    "Oh my god Jen! Are you okay!" She heard Adam yell, turning to see her boyfriend running toward her. She blushed yellow with embarrassment, not knowing that her circulation had changed that much already. "Its nothing Adam, I was just stung by a bee and I swelled up." She stammered, trying to come up with a plausible excuse. "I wouldn't blame you if you thought I was hideous."
    "Nonsense, even with a hump you're still the prettiest girl in school. Here take my jacket." He replied, placing his jacket over her deformed shoulders and kissing her gently on the lips.
