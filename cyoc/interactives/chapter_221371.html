He could not afford to only become Samantha in the morning or else given the 12 hour wait, he would be flying home as a female. No, he would need to wait until after collecting the signed contract before using the medallion once more.

    “Hey Samantha, enough work for the day. I stopped at the bar downstairs for a bottle of champagne. Finish up and let’s have a celebratory drink”

    “I’ll just finish this code. I won’t be long”

    “Sam it’s 5.45 and we’ll need to start getting ready soon. Ease up on the work, you’ve been at it all day”

    She opened the bottle and poured two glasses. Handing one to Sam who was still typing away

    10 minutes later Sam finally shut down his laptop. “Done!”

    “About time. This is expensive champers! You shouldn’t let it go flat! I’m already finishing my second glass and you’ve barely had a sip”

    “So you think we have the contract?”

    “Almost certainly. We just have to behave civilly tonight, act like professional business women and I’m fairly certain we will be collecting a signed contract in the morning”

    “So where are we actually going?”

    “Some charity dinner. I don’t actually know where. Angus is going to send a car around to pick us up”

    “Hmmh”

    “His uncle the CEO will be there. He wants to meet us. I guess he wants to check that we are good girls that he would like to do business with”

    “Didn’t you say that the uncle is a confirmed batchelor and a lady’s man?”

    “How to inspire confidence in a man” Sam hoped his tone was sarcastic.

    “Just tease a little and let him think there is a chance down the track. Main thing is not to outright reject any advances he might make. That can wait until we have a signed contract”

    “You first turn me into a woman and now you want me to prostitute myself. No thanks. I’ll plead sickness. I’m sure you can represent the company well enough on your own”

    “Don’t be gutless Sam. Man up. This is the sort of situation women in business find themselves in all the time. You don’t need to actually do anything. Perhaps dance with him and giggle at his jokes. He’ll think the night a success if he gains your personal phone number before it is over”

    “If it’s so easy why don’t you do the flirting?”
