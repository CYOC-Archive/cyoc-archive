    “I wish there was a better way to travel home without waddling all the way there.” He then heard something from his phone as he took it out and saw an ad for a new riding service. Jeff clicked on the link, where he was greeted by a black and white screen.

    “Does it feel like it takes too long to get to where you’re going? Do you wish there were a better way to get to places?” The advertisements ask before it changes to comfortably bright colors and relaxing upbeat music. “Then try out Strollie, the first ever strolling service that makes riding fun. Strollie has you carried by a strollductor, where you are strapped into a comfy stroller where you get pampered to the fullest. Our strollers contain lots of cool stuff to have you occupied during your ride. Join right now and you can get your first ride 45% off.” The commercial then ended with an adorable sounding jingle before Jeff’s phone went back to the home screen.

    As he was intrigued by the advertisement, Jeff went to his phone’s app store where he downloaded the Strollie app. As he opened it up and created an account, he soon requested a ride towards his house. While he waited for his strollductor to come get him, he played with a couple sunflowers that he picked up from the grass nearby. It didn’t take the strollductor that long to find him from the GPS of the app. “You must be Jeffrey.” She said to him. “Yes, I am.” He replied. “Well, I bet you’re going to love being on a stroller again.” She said before Jeff giggled as he was excited to be in a stroller for the first time since what felt like a very long time.

    The strollductor later put Jeff onto the stroller where she buckled him up on the seatbelt, where he was then greeted with a couple of plushies and a tablet on top of it. As the tablet turned on, it showed a cartoon of brightly colored animals wearing cute shirts with diapers underneath named Binkytown. In it, they were having a picnic party with yummy foods such as sandwiches, ice cream, vegetables, fruits, and many others. Jeff enjoyed the cartoon as the characters were eating the food, which made him slightly hungrier because he had not eaten lunch before he went to the park.

    “Josie, these are some yummy sandwiches that you made.” A chubby little piggy said while he was washing his food down with a sippy cup full of apple juice. “Why, thank you. I made them with lots of love.” Josie, who was a yellow cat with brown stripes, said to her friend. “And this ice cream you have is the best thing I ever had.” Said Jelly Bean, who was a light blue elephant. “Same with this pie.” Louis, a blue and white emperor penguin chick, said to his friend as well. “Why thank you all for your compliments. After the picnic, we could play with my kites and run around the park.” Josie said while all her friends cheered.

    After the picnic, their bellies were full and started to play with Josie’s kites just like she proposed. It then showed the characters running around with the kites as they were giggling without a care in the world. This made Jeff very happy as he enjoyed the cartoon during his little stroll. “Man, they have it all good there.” He thought to himself as he watched the cartoon. Suddenly, Alex, who was the chubby little piggy, felt some cramps from his tummy and immediately bent his knees. “Schmoopy, doopy.” He grunted before a bulge formed under his diaper that was noticeable to the viewer.

    After a few moments, he felt relieved from his accident and proceeded to suck one of his feet. Before his friends could do anything, Jeff arrived at his house as the strollductor automatically turned off the tablet and got him off the stroller. “But I wanted to watch more of that.” Jeff demanded to her. “Sorry sweetie. The tablet is automatically turned off whenever you arrive at your destination. But you can watch that show in your house.” She said gently to the pouty toddler. “But I was just getting into the show. It looked so calm and cozy.” He complained. “I’ll give you a free pass for CutiePie+, which has all the episodes of Binkytown.” “You sure about that?” He asked. "Of course. I'll send it to you right now with your email." She said to him before he got a notification from his cellphone, which made him delighted. "Oh my god. Thank you so much." He squealed happily. "No, thank you. I'm sure you'll enjoy it. Also, thank you for choosing Strollie and have a nice day." She said to him as Jeff waddles to his front door and the strollductor walks away to get another customer.
