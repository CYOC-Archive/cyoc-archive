    K: Four days? Allan are you all right.

    Those serpents are proof. It's a minion boss, so he has probably been spawning his army, that much I can tell

    G: What? From merman to midget-king?

    The short part comes from being old, and you are the Candy King, another gender specific hero, be glad is
    male.

    G: Candy King? What I defeated enemies with the power of cavities?

    J: Hey! I'm the comedian!

    The Candy King is a magic user, you can create any candy... or candy based weaponry. You fiddle with your
    hands and produce a candy cane large and thick enough to actually use as a cane.

    G: I'll stick with the sword concealing cane from my explorer times, very thank you.
