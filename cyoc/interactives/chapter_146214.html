    Edwin was looking through all the packages the Chronivac had, finding it hard to pick the right one. Looking through his options, he noticed one transformation package that said, ‘Racial Change’.

    Staring at the package, Edwin rubbed his chin and said, “Hmm… Racial Changes? I wonder what that means?” Curious, he clicked on the link to the package, and a new page opened up. In that page, there was a message that talked about it. As he was reading the package, it stated…

    ‘Welcome to the Racial Change transformation package. Here in this page is an entire section where you can change your race into whatever you want. You can pick whatever race you want, whether it be African, Italian, Hispanic, French, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, British, German, etc.

    If you want to go beyond racial tendency, you can choose on being an alien (from outerspace) and decided on the features you want your alien to be. As an optional approach, you can change your gender just so you can experience what’s it like on the other side in another’s race.

    You can also change your reality, make a choice if you want to mentally transform, the duration of the change, or how long you would keep your change. Just remember that altering reality would ultimately change the world around you. Meaning you would be with a different family in a different place. So please be careful if you want to change your reality. Other then that, have fun with it.’

    As soon as Edwin finished reading this, he was quite surprised by that whole thing. He could actually change his ethnic race and probably change his reality to fit his new race as well. Let alone change both his race and gender. In his thought, he though it would be awesome to give that a try. He then looked at his options finding them to be a bit basic.

    The first was the name box, meaning one would have to give the program the name of the person they would be after the change. Thinking that was simple enough, Edwin moved onto the next option. The second option was a drop down list asking Edwin to select which race he wanted to be for the change. The third option below was if he wanted to change his gender or not. Then there were the small text boxes that wanted to know the duration of the transformation and how long he would stay as his changed self. This was followed by the yes or no option to change reality with his change.

    Looking through all of this, Edwin found himself thinking that this was a little too easy. But he knew that it would be cool to change his race and experience a life completely different from his. Looking through his options, he began to think about what race he wanted to be. After giving it some thought, he decided that he wanted to be…
