    Laura to the rescue

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago AR Toddler Baby

    After having slept for a couple of hours, Laura finally wakes up. She did need a good nap after all the events that have occurred these days. Then Laura begins to get out of bed and starts checking her cell phone to see what time it was. But when she was about to check the time, she noticed that a lot of messages had just come through from Kaitlin.

    At first, Laura believed that all the messages were about Kaitlin scolding her for running away from her home without her consent. But when she saw what those messages were really about, she was somewhat surprised. There were a lot of messages but they were all full of misspellings to the point that they were totally unintelligible. The only thing she really understood from those messages was where Kaitlin apparently asked Laura for help and that she needed her to come back.

    After reading all of Kaitlin's messages, Laura thought it was some kind of joke. But after seeing the large number of messages and the way in which Kaitlin sent them, Laura thought that it really was something important.

    Then, Laura proceeded again to take her bag where she had all the things that she had previously packed, such as her personal items as well as clothes and training pants. After leaving her house, still with the pacifier in her mouth, Laura proceeded to return to Kaitlin's house. It was a long road but she finally got there.

    When she was about to knock on the door, Laura noticed that the door did not have a lock. So she simply proceeded to open the door. But after Laura opens the door, she sees something that leaves her speechless.
