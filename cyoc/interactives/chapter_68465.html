    They all also had very large eyes, hair styles that defied gravity, and eye colors that always matched their hair colors that defied nature. Green?

    They were all smiling like they didn't have a care in the world and were all looking right at them.

    But the MOST insane part was how they were all dressed in brightly colored and frilly outfits in flat top shoes!

    "This is Moonshine. I am Warden Miracle Mary!" She struck a pose. "Now! Are you little girls gonna give us trouble as we remake you for society
    benefit, or are you gonna give us trouble? Oh PLEASE give us trouble! We love getting in the practice!"

    "THIS IS FRIGGIN' NUTS! NUTS WITH IT! NUTS!" Screamed Matt as he ran like a mad dog into the desert to who knows where.

    "LOVE LIGHTNING!" Shouted one guard in a pink outfit pointing her finger. Pink electricity jumped from her and hit Matt, he fell over
    unconscious a few seconds later twitching. The rabbit/cat gave him a kiss as two magical girls with the red cross on their outfits carried him
    into the prison on a stretcher.

    "Anyone else?" Miracle Mary asked grinning.

    "HI-YA! I AM NOT A SLAVE TO LITTLE GIRLS!" Lee Chin chop at the guard right next to him, knocking her cat themed magic wand out of her
    hand.

    She grabbed his hand in a blur with one hand, twisted it, and broke his wrist with her other had. She then threw him into the truck leaving an
    indentation. Samuel wondered where all those dented had come from.

    The rabbit gave him a kiss as he was again carried away on stretchers.

    "Got it all out of your system yet?" Politely asked the cat themed magical girl who had just thrown a man twice her size into a bus.

    Keith had SOMEHOW snuck a gun into the bus and drew it now and fired it at short blond MG armed with armed with a long ring staff. She
