    Haley saw how much fun Lisa was having and finally stepped in “Lisa stop, she’s had enough ok? She isn’t a threat to you or me or anyone” Haley pleads standing in front of Lisa. Lisa looked passed Haley to see the frightened Rachel on the ground crying like a child would, she looks at Haley “I’m…I’m sorry I don’t know what got into me…I just felt so…” what was this new feeling she had, it felt strong and powerful and seeing Rachel it wasn’t scary or wrong…it felt right…

    “Powerful…” she thought to herself watching Haley take care of the young Rachel “it’s ok Rach, here let’s get you dressed up ok?” Haley started to speak to Rachel like she was her older sister taking care of her scared and upset little sister. Rachel still wiping her tears Haley then looks at Lisa who is staring at her hands “Lisa!” Haley shouted a bit to get her attention which seemed to snap Lisa out of this trance she was in. “Oh yeah? What’s wrong…sorry I was somewhere else…” Lisa said looking at her hands once more holding the excitement back. “Can you turn her back to normal? You know back to her old 17 year old self?” Haley asked Lisa who then looked to Rachel who was hiding behind her friend scared for what may happen next.

    Lisa kneels down “I can change you back but you can’t tell anyone what happened is that clear?” Lisa warns the young Rachel who nods “I promise, just please…make me older” she sniffles. Lisa stands up and snaps her fingers and instantly the young girl was fully matured into her original 17 year old self, however she stood completely naked in front of Haley and Lisa. “Thank you…Lisa…I promise I won’t be that way ever again…” she says covering her body with her two hands “now please can I go out before coach and the other girls see me?” Rachel starts to run off with her clothes in hand her bare feet slapping the floor as she runs to change.

    Later

    “Lisa what the hell was that…you looked like you enjoyed torturing her like she did us…I mean come on you’ve never done that before this power” she confronts Lisa who stops and looks at her with a warning look “hey she picked on us for years! I wanted a little payback! Is that so wrong?” She shouts and sighs “these powers I’m given can change everything for us, don’t you want that?” She asks her friend Haley who looks off to her right “yeah but…to hurt people like that…is that really what you want?” Haley asks and Lisa smiles “I bet you want something…someone who is giving you a hard time to be treated just like I treated Rachel” Lisa says to her friend “who is it?” Lisa smiles. Haley stops and shakes her head “no I don’t! I promise…” Lisa raises an eyebrow with a smile “ok I do have someone who I want to hurt but…not like that” she says softly “I want you to change Mrs. Montoya” she blurts out and Lisa looks confused “our Spanish teacher who likes 70? Why?” Lisa says puzzled
