    Marveling at the realism of the 3D modeling, Celeste first clicked on the Gender toggle, and watched in fascination as the image of her morphed into a reasonably fit man, conservatively trimmed brown hair shot through with gray, wearing a gray business suit. Not bad.

    Next she went to the Race entry and changed Caucasian to Black, and saw the figure adjust accordingly: hair became a close cropped Afro with a little bit of white mixed in with black, skin darkened, her/his features changed slightly-- not as much as she had expected. The suit stayed the same She then entered Native American. The Afro went away, and her/his hair went all black and a bit longer than the Caucasian image. The skin lightened to a deep brown. Interestingly, her/his clothes changed to a tribal look-- apparently, in that incarnation, her (his?) catering business had gone with an ethnic emphasis.

    She glanced at the other settings, and made the figure short and fat, then thin, then muscular, then tall..., and then decided to play with the Race again.

    She entered Asian, and again watched as the now tall and heavily muscled figure changed again.... The eyes gained folds, the hair stayed black and straight, although it did lengthen, the height went just a bit shorter and the build a bit lighter. Looking at the figure she noted it looked like a Chinese basketball player. The clothes were back to a suit.

    She returned to playing with the height and weight, and set it so that the figure looked more like a gymnast than a basketball player, and then noticed the Age setting.

    'Well, let's see how old it will let me get away with,' Celeste thought, and typed in 150 years.

    The figure seemed to rapidly age, and deteriorate before her eyes. Skin wrinkled, hair thinned, facial hair appeared as a long, wispy white beard and matching mustache. Glasses appeared over yellowing, cataract plagued eyes. Muscle tone seemed to melt away, leaving sagging skin covered in liver spots. The back bent and height decreased. Teeth fled from the mouth. Soon she was looking at an image of a Chinese man who looked like he had just stepped out of a fresh grave. Celeste shivered with revulsion.

    She then entered 1 year, and the figured rapidly went in the other direction: hair appeared and darkened. Muscle tone returned, liver spots vanished, and the skin smoothed out. The back straightened. Health and youth appeared, and increased. Then youth intensified and took over. Height lessened and the figure slimmed down into a young gymnast in his mid, then early teens. The face rounded height vanished, and soon she found herself staring at a young boy, then toddler, baby, then infant. A one year old with only a bit of wispy black hair on its head.

    Hm, it seemed surprisingly realistic.

    She then typed in 24, her actual age with the digits reversed, and watched as the child became a young man, seemingly fresh from college with an MBA.

    She sat back, then looked at the clock. My goodness! She had wasted almost an hour playing with the site! She definitely had to contact its designers and see if they could do a work up for her business! Annoyingly, she had not even spent any of her time trying to find contact information.

    Bookmarking the page, Celeste leaned back. Playing with the figure had been a rather nice way to relax. She hit the Save button...

    ...and felt a strange wave of pleasure hit her as her clothes vanished. Barely having time to react (suppose someone walked into her office?!) she stared as her breasts shrank down, being replaced by a well chiseled and muscled male chest. Her shoulders broadened, more lean muscle growing into place. Celeste's hips narrowed, a penis and testicles emerging, body fat vanishing, and the leather of the chair sticking. Her height increased from 5'2" to 5'7" while her neck thickened with more muscle.

    "What--?!" Celeste asked, discovering her, now his, voice was now a tenor.

    Celeste looked into a mirror she/he kept on the desk, and saw a now young, male, oriental face with black hair staring back. The last of her body fat melted away, leaving lean and athletic muscle behind. Then clothing returned-- a charcoal business suit.

    Leaning back Celeste discovered her-- no, his-- name was now Kim Lee.

    Taking a deep breath, Kim Lee wondered what sort of mischief his young intern had been up to earlier in the day...
