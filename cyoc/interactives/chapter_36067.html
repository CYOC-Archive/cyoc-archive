    The colour went of the candle turned brown and the candle began to spread another scent through the room. Although Joey was in a closed closet hiding for his brother he could smell it very good. As a matter of fact he wondered where this smell came from.

    He didn’t notice how fur began to sprout on his skin. Long brown hairs and even longer white hairs around his neck. He began to sweat because it made him really warm so he decided to remove his shirt and trousers. Even now that he was only in his white underwear he failed to notice the changes.

    The white fur kept growing and curled until it became a mane around his neck. On that moment when he saw the long white hairs he knew something was wrong. The brown hairs were so dense now he couldn’t see the skin underneath it anymore. His nose began to change, something that was really weird to feel. Like someone was changing his nose as it was made of clay. A small muzzle was slowly forming. Joey himself was thrilled by the ongoing changes. He was turning into a pokémon and he figured out from the mane he had grown he would be an Eevee.

    His ears retracted in his skull to regrow on his head. The hair on his head changed into the same white as on his mane. His headhair even felt as fur. The ears were definitely of that of an Eevee.

    He remained calm even if he was transforming. He surely didn’t want to blow his hiding spot. Surely not as Steve entered it now. He looked at the candle and wondered how it could change colour so fast. Steve looked below the bed and behind the closet but didn’t look in the closet itself. Joey’s hiding place remained intact.


    For a moment, when Steve wanted to leave the room and search further he heard a load ripping sound. The sudden appearance of Joey’s brown tail with on the tip shredded his underwear. It was the final part of his transformation.

    Steve ran back to the closet and opened it, looking at a human shaped Eevee.
    “Pika!You’re a pokémon too?” he asked enthusiast.
    “Yeah, you found me” he said slightly annoyed because of that. He decided to see how he looked and watched himself in the mirror.

    One thing he noticed that he was now a head shorter than Steve. Before he had transformed he had been a head taller than the Pikachu Steve. Unlike Steve Joey felt a bit strange to be naked although he knew that being this way was perfectly normal.

    The tail felt so strange and yet it was a part of him he couldn’t think of not having a tail attached to his body. As it always had been like this.

    The candle’s colour changed again but this time it became a normal white that served no magical purpose. Steve wanted to examinate the candle but discovered no hidden secrets or anything special.

    However, when Steve turned around the candle fell over and the fire began to spread on Steve’s homework.
