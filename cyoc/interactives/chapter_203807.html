Her blouse became floral patterned as her tights became deep black.
    "Ay, honestly Señor, how could you forget me?" She asked.

    "Oh, well I uh-um, I..." He sputtered, completely baffled with himself. How could he forget Alejandra, their best Spanish teacher?! He has had a crush on her for over 5 years now! "I-I was just messing with you that's all! Ha!" He laughed awkwardly, causing Sarah, who had hidden behind Alejandra, to sigh in relief.

    "Whatever you say George." She chuckled as she wrapped her arm around Sarah's shoulder. "We'll be seeing you."

    George began to walk away flustered, forgetting what he had even stopped them for. He then slapped his balding head, wondering when he'd get the courage to just ask that angel out already.

    "Phew, that was a close one." Sarah stammered, chuckling awkwardly.

    "Hmmhmm." Alejandra mumbled.

    Sarah hung her head lower to the ground. Alejandra was like her at-school big sister/mentor. She showed her the ropes here, and alwasy had her back, even when she was goofing off.

    "You should really get those reports done mi amiga." Alejandra laughed.

    "Y-yes ma'am." Sarah chuckled awkwardly.

    "Oh, honestly honey..." She began, brushing her hand through her hand, unaware that on it, the ring was about to change reality again. Will any weird phrases Alejandra come up with screw things up even more?
