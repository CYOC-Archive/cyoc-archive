    Lilia says “But sir!”
    Her boss says “What’s wrong Lilia? Are you afraid that I find out about your secret?”
    Lilia gulped and said “Secret? I’m not hiding any secrets.”
    Her boss “That you are a werewolf.” Lilia jaw drops in surprise and he continues “That’s right, I knew it
    sense I first hired you, aren’t you forgetting that I’m an Equal Opportunity Employer and unless you already
    sensed it, I too am a werewolf.”
    Lilia says “But boss, why didn’t you tell me this before?”

    Hes boss says “Because I wasn’t instructed to reveal it yet by our Alpha, he arranged for you to become one of
    us and now he wants to see you tonight, come Lilia, all your questions will be answered when we get to the
    Alpha’s cave and we have a long trek ahead of us and not much time to make it there.”

    Lilia is soon taken through the woods and into a large clearing with a Cliffside with a large wooden door, her
    boss gives Lilia a wooden box “Quickly, it’s almost moonrise, strip and put your clothes in here.”
    Lilia quickly took off her clothes and folded them neatly into the box putting her shoes on the top to fill
    the box and just in time too, the full moon is seen overhead shining over her and her boss, she quickly felt
    her transformation start as she put down the box. Her changes started as she arched over in pain, Pitch black
    fur started growing from her body as she grew as well. Lilia's face shifted as her muzzle grew out while her
    ears shifted onto the top of her head, changing into wolf ears. Her feet, at the same, became digigrade. Her
    hands changed into claws and her tail grew out, completing her transformation. Her boss is slightly larger
    than Lilia except he has brown fur.

    “Still not used to the transformations yet eh?” he asked in a deeper voice. “Don’t worry, you’ll get used to
    the changes in time.” He lets out a long howl towards the door and another howl soon responds on the other
    side as the door opens. They go inside.
