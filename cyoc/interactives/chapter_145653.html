    Amy strolled in, turning her face away from a few passing gazes.
    A few guys gawked at her from behind but were met by a middle finger.
    She walked through the lunch line, trying to ignore how weighed down her back was as she ordered.
    She spotted the other girls who got the treatment and sat down across from them.
    Jamie stopped eating and Rachel wiped the oatmeal off her chin.
    Mona just looked at them with a curious glance while twirling some of her minty green hair.
    And Mei... seemed pretty jealous?
    "Hi girls." Amy smiled.
    The four of them just nodded at her.
    "Heyyy...Amy? You guys settling in alright?" Jamie asked.
    "I think they're settling in just fine." Rachel said before Amy could open her mouth.

    Amy sighed "She jumped up in my arms when I went to grab her laundry.... and now she won't let me set her down."
    Cailin nuzzled Amy's neck and slipped into her lap, grabbing a fork and a bite of pancake.
    "My dad wants to see you two after you eat." Mona said.
    "Why?"Amy asked as she took a sip of juice.
    "He wants to see how you two having sex affected your mind." Mona said nonchalantly.
    Mei dove in front her, taking a face-full of spat up orange juice.
    "W-W-WHAT?!" Amy shrilly asked after a coughing fit, her face completely flushed.
    "I could here Cailin moaning and shouting your name for two hours straight last night." Rachel answered.

    "But I don't remember..." Amy started.
    "Oh cut the crap, you guys are as lovey dovey as it gets, Jamie saw it for herself." Rachel said.
    "Sis!" Jamie snapped.
    "I seriously don't remember what happened!" Amy yelled.
    The rest of the cafeteria turned to face them.
    "Yeah, you don't remember eating her out and squashing her tits like bouncy balls while she goes 'OH AMY! YES BABY, GIVE TO ME, GIVE IT TO ME!'" Rachel yelled back.
    A few guys turned as red as Amy (one of them covered up his groin with a napkin).
    "I was asleep!" Amy yelled.
    "Yeah fucking right!" Rachel yelled back. "Mona and Jamie heard the same thing!"
    "And why were Mona and Jamie with you, hmmmm?" Cailin asked.
    "Oooooooooooh!" a couple of guys went as Rachel got flustered herself.
    "Also, why where you watching us?" Cailin added.
    "Uh.... well, I.... you guys woke me up, shut up!" Rachel yelled with a deep blush.
    "As if, perv!" Cailin laughed back.
    "YOU WANNA STAY IN THE INFIRMARY THIS TIME, SNOWBALL?!" Rachel yelled.
    "ENOUGH!!!" Jamie shouted.

    Dr. Grant and The Army Marshall just happened to walk in at that moment as everyone looked over at the girl's table.
    Mona sat there completely flustered as her father walked over.
    "Sweetheart, what's going on?" Dr. Grant asked, "Did you tell them about our appointment?"
    "That's what I told them about." Mona said.
    "Explains what we heard from the lunch line." the Marshall said with an unenthusiastic grin.
    The girls blushed heavily, Cailin hiding her face behind Amy's shoulder.
    "Jamie was in there with me because my father wanted to keep a close eye on her." Mona explained, " She was the only one in our group to receive the treatment as a male until now."
    The was still for a moment as everything calmed back down.
    "See you lovebirds after breakfast!" Dr Grant smiled.
    "We aren't lovebirds!" Amy called as the doctor walked off.

    "At least it can't get any more awkward..." Amy sighed.
    The other girls nodded and went back to eating.
