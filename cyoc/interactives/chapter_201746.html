    Back at home, Max enters his room and place the book down on the table. He will deal with later as he went back downstairs to make himself something to eat. After pondering for a bit, he decides to make a simple macaroni and cheese. He technically lives with his step brother here who comes by from time to time and they got a good relationship together.

    Sitting down at the dining table with his mac and cheese, Max wonders if Lance will be coming back tomorrow. He heads back up after finishing his dinner and saw the book laying on the desk just like he left it. He was torn to either sleep or answer his curiosity before settling on the latter. He sits down in the chair in front of the desk and opens up the book.

    "Huh." He didn't know what he expected but there is a simple line on the front page telling him to write who owns this book. He just shrugs and write down his name before feeling a sudden chill once he finished. He looks around a bit alarmed before calming down thinking it was just his air conditioner. Turning the page, he was greeted with a blank page ready to be filled. Max smiles then begins to write,

    'Dear Diary.

    Today have been pretty good and a bit odd to say the least. School was normal with the jocks showing off who's the biggest, the nerds discussing about the next game release or about what they did yesterday at a convention, and the teachers helping to guide their students towards a brighter future.

    Oh right! Earlier today in art class, Sir Terrador was teaching us about the details we needed to consider when drawing body structures. He even demonstrated it by getting naked and showing off his large sculpted muscles.'

    Max pauses at this wondering why he wrote that last bit. Sir Terrador was handsome and very nice to his students but he has the body that's thin despite being tall at 6'3. As he is thinking about, and image of said teacher appears in his mind but with the body of a ripped bodybuilder. He felt a slight throb down south and just let the sentence be without changing it.

    'School was done for the day since that was my last class so I heard home. My best friend Leon asked me if I wanted to watch a movie together sometime this week so I agreed. He's a great friend and honestly helped me in coming out to my parents in being gay. He's also very charming and I find myself often blushing when he jokingly flirted at me. I mean I wouldn't mind if we started dating but he needs to make the first move.

    Then finally, I received a text from my step brother Lance during lunch at school telling me that he's picking me up tomorrow. He says that he's taking me to the North Pole to visit my step father (uncle?) Nick. I was pretty surprised when my dad and Lance revealed to me that THE Santa Claus himself was Lance's father.

    All in all, I'm pretty excited to visit him again and maybe help around the workshop. The first time I met him, he threw the stereotype of Santa Claus out the window. He was huge with a height of 7 feet and muscles that rivals the biggest bodybuilder I've seen. He still has a belly but it was more of a power gut and he also have pure white hair and a long beard. Honestly, tomorrow is going to be excited so I better head to bed now.

    Oh almost forgot, the reindeers that are like super buff and walking on two legs. There doesn't seem to be any elves around so I just assumed that they are responsible for making the toys and pulling Santa's the sleigh.

    Goodnight Diary.'

    Max finished writing and blinked a bit when he realized what he had wrote in the end. He narrow his eyes before sighing and closing the book.

    "I must be so tired that I even include part of my fantasy there." He mutters to himself and brush his teeth first before stripping naked and get under the covers of his bed. Not soon after he fell asleep and unbeknownst to him the book had glowed faintly then return back to normal.
