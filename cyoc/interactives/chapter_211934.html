Yeah, shrink spurt. ... the shrink spurt caused Mike to be sent straight from being a teenager to a preteen. His adult shorts and boxers dropped to the floor, though his now stretched out shirt just barely bagged over himself to give him modesty, though it seemed that last wave was pleasurable enough to cause his Dad to cum all over his dropped shorts and boxers.

But the transformation wasn't finished yet, only this time instead of it being pleasurable, Mike seemed to giggle like the little boy he was becoming. This next shrink spurt dropped him back into the single digits of life as his adult shirt positively ballooned on him and now stretched down to his knees like a large shirt a kid might use as pajamas. The giggling intensified into outright fits of laughter as Mike's previously well kept hair grew out a bit as he left school age for being just too young for school, and he fell onto his back in laughter, causing him to slip easily out of his oversized shoes and socks and kick his now tiny boy feet in the air as he settled on what Jeff estimated was his destination of five years old.

Some other man was there in his place--a man who looked suspiciously like Uncle Eric--no... Dad, Jeff corrected himself. Uncle Eric was his Dad now. In the old reality Uncle Eric hadn't ever been married, instead choosing to be a life-long bachelor--or so his family had said. The adult Mike disappeared from photos before Mikey's new birth date, and in photos where he was alive, he now shifted into being the baby and little boy of the family.

    Without missing a beat, Mikey sat up with a big grin that was already missing a tooth in the front and declared "Yep Jeff! This is the best thing that ever happened to me!"

    And then Jeff nearly fell over as Mikey jumped up and ran over and hugged him saying "Thank you" over and over again.
