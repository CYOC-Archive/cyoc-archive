It took me a while, but I was able to finish it surprisingly enough that scientist was right.

Dr. Craud lead me to a lab of sorts and told me to remain calm while they took a blood sample. However the scales on my body caused the needle to break, and while they were debating how to do this I nudged Craud and showed him that the scales could be lifted up from behind, so they took the sample. They then took me to a room with nothing but open space so that they could test physical strength.

I looked at my claw in amazement with my jaw dropped to show them wondering how I did that so easily. They seemed surprised as well, but nonetheless they then told me to use my fire on another dummy to test my flames strength, and temperature. As thought the dummy wasn't destroyed after I tested my flame, however it left burn marks on it which seemed in some areas to melt. I then told them that they would know more about my body than me because I only remember being like this for about 3 days now.
