    she stood up on her toes as the transformation made it increasingly uncomfortable. finally, she could feel her head start tingle as her nose and mouth pushed out into a fully developed fox muzzle.

    she felt the fur with her fingers. it was just the way she wanted it to be. nice and thick while remaining smooth and silky.
    She turned to notice that Jill had followed her into the restroom, just standing in the doorway looking at Jennifer.

    Jill looked stunned for a few seconds but then told her about how Kyle was her cousin and she had promised to help him find out more about Jennifer and was even the original one who told him about transform or dare.

She heard every noise at all times and her sense of smell could even tell how someone was feeling at a given time.
