    A still naked Carrie, who was a giant compared to Todd, squatted down next to him.

    "Aww," she cooed. "Aren't you the cutest thing ever?"

    She reached down to pet him with a hand as big as he was now. Todd was embarrassed to admit it to himself, but he liked the feeling of her petting him gently.

    "Well, Todd, how do you like being a hamster?"
