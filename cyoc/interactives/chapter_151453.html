    Well as a young squire in training the former Elphin Knight had taken a Transformation Spells and Counter Measures 101
    course in the Elphin Technical College and Trades.

    So she had transformed a few times and countertransformed
    a few times as a young male elf the breeder dragoness had a
    few school lessons still bouncing around in that altered
    mind and brain of hers.

    Lesson No 1
    TF Spells have a lot of smoke and mirrors and time delay
    actions to them.
    As her long ago teacher had told the long ago class of elf
    squires "You become what you see and what you don't see is
    what you actually are"

    So the crooning breeder dragoness shook her head violently to
    clear some of the estrogen induced fog at of her own mind and
    concentrating
    tried to see what lay actually there,rather then what Beth's
    illusins made her see what Beth wanted her to see.
    Her own transformation was shocking enough.She definitely
    was a breeder dragoness,no doubt about it,but her breasts were
    slight lumps on her chest rather then the huge dripping things
    that the illusion had tricked her into seeing.
    And if her mammaries were smaller the rest of her was certainly
    bigger,longer and sometimes both.

    The breeder dragoness concentrated on remembering those
    long ago Counter Measure lessons,not to reverse her transformation back into her former self,clearly a lost cause
    now
    but
    to stop Beth's magic,still crackling along the surface of her green
    scales,from doing any more mucking about with her mind
    and body.

    That done the breeder dragoness took stock of the surroundings
    around her.
    They weren't who or where they aught to be.
    Rather then in the catacombs beside the dragon breeding pens
    like Beth's trickery had forced them to see they were actually
    all on the walks beside the outer walls of Garons castle.
    The side of the outer walls perched above the Endless Depths.
    Circling the castle in the skies were winged things that the
    breeder dragoness assumed were Beth's minions.waiting for the
    signal from their mistress to swoop down and invade
    Garons kingdom.
    Beth herself was actually a warty hunched over old crone
    whilst Garon,his sons and Queen Bella were still
    unchanged.
    The two elven women making love were no longer elphen females anymore.
    The breeder dragoness frantically looked for Queen Bella's daughter,the supposed small pet dragoness.
    Horrified she spotted the changing human,perched on the wall
    ready to leap into the air and stretch her wings.
    Not a good idea.
    Although the young Princess now sported a pink spiky and
    still growing dragons tail
    the rest of her was still her human self,albeit naked at the moment.

    If the Princess,who due to Beths deceiving illusion magic did leap off the wall and into the sky,it was a endless plunge into
    the Endless Depths for the Princess.
