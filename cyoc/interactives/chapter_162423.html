    "Wait, that would make me VERY small." she said. "Is that such a good idea?"

    "Of course it is! In fact, I have an idea to make it a great adventure for you!" he said as he pulled a few things out of his bag. "Firstly, drink a handful of this" he said as he set a large container (which was a bottle cap, she noted) of red liquid by her

    She shrugged and walked over and dipped her hands in and took a big gulp of it. "Oooh that's super sweet." she replied as she looked at her skin and watched it turn as white as the clowns. "Woah"

    "For this bit, it's best if you are a clown, which I am sure you don't mind" he noted as he watched her change. Her body stayed mostly the same, except her feet started to get thicker and larger, while her hair started curl up into a tight large (but tiny) afro. Her face got a new nose, a round orb, while her lips stretched wider and she felt them expand and inflate.

    "My wips!" she said as she touched them. "This is wacky, I kinda love it." she said looking at her own inflated feet. "Woazah"

    "Now then" he said hovering a large magic wand over her head. "This part is where the true adventure comes."

    "Not to give a whine, As a human you were fine,
    but now you'll be stellar as my minuscule shoe-dweller." he changted as the wand started glowing, and she found herself shrinking again, rapidly to a much smaller size. Just a few millimeters tall, she looked at a now skyscraper sized foot looming above her, which moved and a massive face looked down.

    "SO TINY" the loud voice boomed as the clown's finger tip came down and offered a place to stand. It took a bit of effort for Jenny to climb it at her new size, but she marveled at how big everything was.

    He then hovered her over a the hole of one of his large sneakers, the pair he'd been wearing when she met him. "Want to go inside?" he asked.

    Her mind shifted for a second, and what would seem like a terrifying prospect, going into his massive shoe and having his foot enter after, was now thrilling as could be and it's all she could think of doing. She squeaked her response, which he giggled about not being able to hear, but he knew she said yes. The spell would ensure that. He then tipped his finger over and the tiny little speck dropped for ages, it felt like until it hit the soft foam of the shoe soul.

    "WOAH!" she squeaked as she looked around and felt where his foot had indented it. "I remember when he did that to me." She noted.

    Then the shoe hit the ground with a thud, which made her fall over, but she then saw his toes entering,a nd again, something that should be scary was not, because she knew she would not be harmed. That's why she had been turned into a clown first. She was now inpervious to the crushing of his foot, and in fact, would be able to roam around quite well. Her adventure was beginning as Jenny the Shoe-Dweller.

    He chuckled as he tied his shoes and then sat back down. "That should satisfy her need for feet."
