    Getting The Shoes Off after Getting Off

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Lupic 5 years ago AR S O Sports

    The afterglow is so dizzying and amazing at the same time, you almost want to close your eyes for just a second. A quick push to your shoulders managed to pull you out of your daze. "Give me the paper towels, will ya?" Benji asks. You scan the desk in front of you but you only see some empty sport drinks and a small stack of unfinished homework. With a blank smile you ask "Uh ... were are they?" Benji's hand reaches out and grabs a roll from right in front of your nose. "Maybe you should try looking in front of you, bonehead." he grinned as he tore of a few towels and gave them to you. Your jizz had landed all over your stomach and even some on your shirt. Its glowing warmth was actually quite comfortable and relaxing after you were done. You wipe the sticky fluid off your belly trying your best not to smear it all over your clothes and ask Benji what to do next.

    Benji paced around the room while you were basking in the aftermath with your abs exposed. "We'll first have to fix your shoe problem. Abrasions are no good, you won't be able to skate for days." Indeed you almost forgot, you were dumb enough to glue your feet to the soles of your shoes. Alongside never being able to take them off again, it would also hurt like hell if your feet ever got caught on something. "What are we gonna do about that." you groan. "No worries my friend, luckily you used water soluble glue." You look at him with a puzzled expression and ask "What does that mean?" You both go to the bathroom and you put your feet in the bathtub while Benji opens the faucet. After a while you're able to wriggle your feet out of the shoes. "It worked!" you shout. Benji says "Yeah, now we can dry your shoes. And also my socks ... why did you put my socks on?" Your face turned red as you tried to think about why you had worn them, you stummer "I can't really remember?" as you stare at him with an innocent yet dumbfounded expression. Benji's face relaxed and he said "No worries, we're buds right, mi casa et su casa. But next time you'll have to tell me." He hung the dried clothes from a radiator and you went outside to do some more skating.

    The day had been off to a rough start. Your mind had felt heavy and slow, at least more than usual, but now the fog started to clear. It was fun, doing some more long boarding while also meeting some of Benji's other buds. They stayed for a while talking about a new game that had come out, Benji already had a copy of it and he promised you'd play it together tonight. He was such a great friend, not only that but he had also found some cool hidden cheat codes for infinite money and a jetpack. He couldn't imagine anyone else being his best friend, he was always looking out for him and they could do it all over again tomorrow since he stayed the weekend.

    Benji's Perspective

    You're curiosity had gotten the better of you. Cam was currently fast asleep in your brother's, his own, bed so you could do whatever you want. Turning yourself into someone else was really tempting so you wanted to try it. You sneaked to the bathroom and grabbed the dried white socks from the radiator. On the packaging of the socks - that you had stolen from your brother's room - it stated that if you put the shoes on by yourself you will remember the change and if it's forced you'll just completely adjust to the shoes. That was kind of creepy but still Cam was too dimwitted to pose any real threat so you decided to just try it out, but with which shoes?
