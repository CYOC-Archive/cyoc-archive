    By the time Bill’s heifer, Lilly, moved in, Sherry should have been visibly pregnant again. However, she was actually losing the weight she’d gained from her back-to-back pregnancies. Bill was a bit disappointed that neither the feral human nor he had impregnated Sherry. But when he met Lilly’s human male, Jeff, he was immediately pleased.

    “He looks like he comes from good breeding stock,” Bill said to Lilly, looking Jeff over.

    “Oh, he does,” Lilly said. “He comes from stud stock in addition to housekeeping stock, so he was basically bred to breed.”

    “Is he castrated?”

    “I couldn’t castrate a male human that came from such good stud stock.”

    “Hmm. Maybe he’d like to breed with Sherry. She comes from excellent breeding stock,” Bill remarked. Just then, Jeff spotted Sherry. He ran over to her and shoved her to the ground, mounting her. Bill and Lilly watched Jeff thrust forcefully into Sherry, grunting loudly. Sherry mooed with pleasure.

    “Oh, yeah, one thing about Jeff. He’s the horniest human you’ll ever meet,” Lilly said. “All male humans insist on claiming females, but he’s even worse than normal for that. We might have to keep them separate if we don’t want to be knee-deep in human calves.”

    “We’ll do that. But not yet. First, I want him to impregnate Sherry. She’s been moved to breeding responsibilities for a reason,” Bill said, watching Jeff and Sherry continue to mate.

    Lilly’s warning about Jeff wasn’t an exaggeration. Any time Jeff wasn’t actively doing work, he was mating with Sherry, shooting multiple thick loads into her. Each time, the semen dripped out of her from sheer volume. To no one’s surprise, Sherry began to gain weight shortly after Jeff and Lilly’s arrival.

    When the vet arrived for Sherry’s checkup, he had to forcibly separate Sherry and Jeff, who were mating yet again. The vet shook his head as Bill dragged Jeff away into a stall for the duration of Sherry’s checkup.

    “I’ve seen a lot of horny humans. He’s probably the horniest. You better be renting him out for stud.”

    “He’s Lilly’s human, so she’ll have to make that decision,” Bill replied. He watched the vet inspect Sherry. “So, she’s pregnant, right?”
