He had almost walked right by the new store front on his way home from work, but something about the sign had caught it eye. NuYu, nothing to explain what it was until you got closer. He was short, skinny, and even at twenty-two and being a college grad he could walk into a high school and pass for a student. He looked like a teen at best. The mop of blond hair that never cooperated, and big blue eyes didn't help. Well, unless he was cruising a gay bar, he was about as twinky as you could get and be legal.

Tucker felt himself blushing at the guy's attention and quickly put the bar of soap back on the shelf like he was a kid caught with his hand in a cookie jar. "We're running a grand opening special. You can pick any item in the store with a value of less than thirty dollars and take it home free. We think you'll be so impressed you'll be back." The man broke into a salesman smile. "So, tell me, what do you most want to change?"

    "Both?"

The clerk reached over onto one of the shelves and handed Tucker what looked like a shaving razor, like all the ones you buy in the store it only had one head, with others in a package next to it. Those were out of the thirty dollar range, but the single head and handle weren't.

    "Razors get rid of hair." Tucker said, looking over the package. "GorillaRazor.. more hair in less time.."

    "Haven't you heard the saying, when you shave it off it grows back in thicker. Well, this makes that true. Any time you want a little more hair anywhere, shave with this and in a few hours you'll have a thicker patch growing in. Good for tiny adjustments or when you're not sure how much you want." The clerk leaned on the shelf as he watched Tucker check out the item. "One razor is good for about ten shaves. Depending on how much you want.. it might take a few refills, but we have other stuff if you want a much faster result. Little pricier for the product but cheaper in the long run."

    "What have I got to loose?" Tucker said as he turned the package over in his hands once more.

    "Not much, if you over do it we have the Baby Smooth Razor that will let you get rid of your hair and start from scratch."

    "And if I don't have hair there?" Tucker asked, looking up.

    "Shave the spot and you will. Oh and, best results happen if you shave before going to sleep. Enjoy!" The clerk smiled. "I'm sure I'll see you in a few days."

    Tucker returned to his apartment with his prize, setting it on the table as he got dinner in the oven, just heating up a frozen pizza. Though his eyes kept going back to the package. "Best results from overnight, but he said it would work in a few hours... so maybe a test.." He took the razor into the bathroom and got it out of its package, browsing the little instruction pamphlet inside. Though it pretty much said it worked how the clerk said it would.

He pulled his shirt back on and went to enjoy a little after work pizza and TV.

He went into the bathroom to take his evening shower and wipe away the day's grime, taking off his clothes and tossing them in the hamper. It was as he stretched out his arm to turn on the shower he noticed it in the vanity mirror. There was a darker patch under his arms. Stunned, he lifted both arms up. It wasn't a lot more, but it went from a few wispy pale blond hairs to a patch of darker blond hair, thick enough you could see it under his arms without having to really look. He reached up and gave the hairs a little tug. Ouch.. okay.. bad idea. But they were his.
