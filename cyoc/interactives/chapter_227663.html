The blonde didn’t know what came over her, Hinata was her ally, she herself was part of the Normal, they fought against werewolves, kitsune, and wizards.     “I bet your stepdad is going to be pissed that you didn’t come home last night! Daddy’s probably pissed as well, but all I have to do is bat my eyes and give a cute pout and I’m off the hook.” Winery said.

    Felicity gave a smile but inside she was dreading how the Boss or Choon Hee would punish her for both not returning after school and disrupting Hinata’s mission. But a part of her was excited to have defied them, she was still unused to this teenage girl angst.
    Then Mia’s white cat jumped from one of the Sakura trees and landed in front of the two blondes.

    “What’s up cat? You know you can’t use the zen garden as a litter box.” Winery quipped.

    “Put a sock in it Fido, I left for a second and now Mia, Annie and that other chick aren’t in their room.” Rexie growled.

    “Get lost fleabag, we don’t know, we’ve been out here all morning. They probably went to get air or something.” Felicity said feeling defensive of the other blonde.

    “Tsk, don’t get lost In each other’s eyes or you won’t find your way out.” The cat said as it leapt onto the walls.

    Both girls blushed and started walking faster.

    “Why are we here again? I’m not really a flower girl anyway.” Felicity said awkwardly.

    “Yea, I’m not really a fan of pink either, it looks good on you though.” The werewolf said in reference to Felicity’s pink kimono.

    The girl blushed and walked slightly faster, her face was getting hot and she needed to get back to the Normal before she got into real trouble. The other blonde followed behind and they both returned to their room, running into the black kitsune from before.

    “Emberleaf-Sama, Monroe-Chan. If you’d like to return to the human realm your car has been moved to the front gates, a servant will open a portal to send you home.” Haikata said.

    Winery nodded and led Felicity through the imperial palace. Then the two bumped into a human boy, Winery gave an apologetic wave and Felicity’s eyes widened, it was the Catalyst: Darren. She didn’t Ino what to do, she couldn’t attack him or take him into Normal custody, she just stared at him as the two passed.

    “Do you know that boy or something?” The werewolf asked.

    “Uh—no, it’s just weird seeing another human here.” The blonde lied.

    “I guess, I’ve met him before, can’t remember his name though, he’s like some kind of special wizard or something.” The werewolf princess replied.

    They arrived at the front gates and got back on Winery’s truck, the kitsune out front then transported them back to the human world where they drove back to the school. Felicity got into her car and the two parted ways, once Winery was gone Felicity leaned against her steering wheel.

    “What the fuck am I going to tell the Boss?” The girl mumbled.

    “For one, you will no longer be driving yourself to and from school. Also you will be confined to your quarters when not at school for a month. Be thankful you are not sent to re-education, you will learn to be more obedient and follow your curfew.” the Kumiho stated, surprising the blonde from the car window.

    Felicity rolled her eyes and moved over to the passenger seat as the Asian woman took the wheel and activated the timeline-transport-engine to return them to their original timeline. From there they returned to the Normal HQ and the girl stomped to her quarters.
