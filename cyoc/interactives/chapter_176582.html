    To say that the quarantine has left many Animalians with a pent-up need for vacation would be a major understatement. As with everyone else, plans for last year had been disrupted by the enforced need for isolation. Even travel between the colonies was harder because of the efforts necessary to stem the spread of the coronavirus. Many Animalians had to delay or cancel their vacations.

    For instance, the two gay couples of Herman and Jason and Moxie and Arlene weren't able to go to Mieni Atoll like they had been planning for. They had such a lovely time there on their honeymoons, they wanted to go back. But because of the pandemic, they had to cancel their trip. Even though next year promised to be much better, the sea Animalians still felt very restless. Though when the holidays came around, they came up with a rather interesting idea. Since coronavirus cases were starting to drop since a vaccine was going out, they could go on vacation. Not to Mieni Atoll, but to Heiya Island. The temperate island ought to be beautiful with the snowfall. And they wouldn't be going just for themselves. The island still had a lot of those girls who had been kidnapped by that smuggling ring. The two couples figured the people on Heiya could use some extra help. What they didn't expect was how many girls there still were to help.
