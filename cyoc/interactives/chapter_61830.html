And he couldn't have that, after all, Mark was a huge success and Matthew wanted to monitor his progress.

    "Allison, I'm so sorry, but there's going to be a change in plans." said Matthew.
    "What do you mean?" she asked back, worried she'd never get to see Simon again. "Will I ever see him..?"
    "Well, kind of. You'll get the chance to have your dream come true." said Matthew. He was wording his words carefully. Allison had always wanted to date Simon, and technically, she was getting what she wanted.
    "Matthew, why are you being so vague?"
    "Well, I might tell you later if I'm feeling nice. But now, you need to sleep." said Matthew.
    Allison gasped, and one word came out of her mouth before she glazed over into hypnosis. The word was "Simon..."
    "Now Allison, look at Brandon. Take a big, long, look." said Matthew.
    Allison obeyed, and turned towards the former nerd, now the captain of the football team.
    "This is what you want in a man, and it is what you'll have soon enough. Say something to show you understand." instructed Matthew, starting to reprogram her brain.
    "Brandon is perfect." Allison said, kind of dreamily, but still in the monotone hypnotized voice. It was creepy.
    "But, you are not perfect for him." said Matthew. "Brandon is the captain of the football team, so he'd be happier dating a cheerleader. Do you want to be a cheerleader?"

    Matthew laughed a little bit, then proceeded to change Allison.
    "Now Allison, you're a cheerleader. You have long, blond hair."

    "You're kind of dumb, but you're nice to everyone."
    Allison let out a giggle.
    "You look your best everyday, and to you, that's flawless."
    Allison's zits and freckles disappeared, and her face was spotless.
    "You've always been proud of your body shape and big boobs."
    Allisons boos inflated to a DD, and she lost a lot of weight. Her body was now in a perfect hourglass shape.
