She now watched as Rick's family and Julia's family gathered around to proceed with the wedding. Nat remembered overhearing Julia and Claudia in the bathroom talk about a big fancy wedding instead of a courthouse wedding. She then had an idea as she whispered something to Rei and proceeded to use the ring and made an illusion of a grand, fancy, wedding in what looked like a church. Rei limited the illusion however to herself, Rei, Rick, Julia, and Claudia, everyone else was none the wiser.

The judge of the courthouse read from his book then asked if the bride and groom wanted to have words to each other. Rick nodded and went first.

    "Julia, I know you didn't plant exactly what your future would be, but I promise to be the best man for you in your life. Through the good days and the bad days, I'll be there for us." Rick said looking at his wife, who in Nat's illusion was wearing her dream wedding dress.

    "Rick...I didn't know what kind of a man I would marry. But since you came along, I know the kind of man I wanted to marry, it's you, Rick Moreland." Julia said with a warm smile.

    Claudia knew the real circumstances of the wedding was about, but still to see the illusion brought tears to her eyes trying to keep from crying.

    "So I now pronounce you....have you decided on a last name?" The judge asked them.

    "Moreland-Harris." Julia replied.

    "Mr. and Mrs. Moreland-Harris." The judge finished as Rick and Julia kissed prompting Nat to finish the illusion. Julia had been holding a small bouquet of flowers which she had brought into the court house and tossed aside, they were caught by Spark who looked towards Connor and both of them gave each other a bright red blush across the face.

    "That was really sweet of you." Rei whispered to Nat.
