    Transform or Dare?

    A whole new world...maybe?

    Jen opened her eyes. She stared. She closed her eyes and rubbed them. She opened and closed her eyes a few more times. Opening and closing her eyes looked the same. Pitch black.

    Jen decided to check out her surroundings. She seemed to be floating. Was she in space? No. In space there were stars, galaxies, things to see. Here there was nothing. She felt up and down her body. She felt like she might be smaller, though she had nothing to compare herself to. All her hair was gone. That was a pretty major difference. Her breasts had dissolved. She was defiantly chubbier. She wasn't fat, just a little chuby, like a baby. A baby...

    Jen, curious, slowly reached out in front of her. To her surprise, she hit a wall. It was very close to her, maybe 3.5 inches away. The wall was soft, squishy...warm...

    "Oh my god..." Thought Jen in realization. "I'm a baby INSIDE someone!" Jen felt around herself. The soft walls were everywhere! She needed to get out! Now!
    She felt left, right and up. Then she felt down. She found an opening. She swam towards it and began to push herself out. She could feel the person she inhabited huffing and contracting. Jen had made the mistake of going out feet first. She wanted to see who this woman was as soon as possible and the wait was almost unbearable.

    It took nearly 10 minutes but Jen made it. Someone warped her in a blanket of some sort. Her "mother" cradled her in her hands. Jen was surprised to see the face of the UPS girl. The girl wasn't really a girl anymore though. She looked like a 25 year old woman.

    "My little girl. I'll call you...Jennifer!" She said.

    Jen was even more surprised by the sight of her new surroundings. She and the UPS woman were in a cave. Glowing blue water dripped from the ceiling into a stream the cast a watery light on the dank cave. They seemed to be alone. Jen expected to be fed with milk but to her surprise the UPS woman just set Jen's tiny body on the ground. Jen found that, after a few tries, she could walk and run just fine. She felt like a baby goat.
    That's when she noticed something about the UPS womanl that she hadn't seen before. She had no ears. Wait...no she DID have ears. GOAT ears on the top of her head. She had two small horns growing from her head. Jen felt her own head. She had ears but no horns yet. She assumed she would grow them later. A flick behind her and she noticed a tiny goats tail.

    Before Jen could explore any further transformations, a shrill cry sounded from deep in the blackness of the cave, away from the glowing stream.

    "Oh no...they found us!" Whispered the woman, fear in her eyes. "We'll have to venture deeper into the caves, Jenny!"
    Jen ran to the woman. She knew she needed to trust this lady for a bit. She was currently mostly helpless except for the ability to walk. The UPS woman picked Jen up and ran alongside the glowing stream. Jen was facing in the opposite direction of where the woman was running. There was defiantly something chasing them. Jen could see the shadows behind them moving. Judging from the amount of howls, screams and other disturbing noises, there were a LOT of somethings following them.

    Just then, the woman tripped over a large glowing purple mushroom. The grouped was littered with mushrooms, 2 feet tall. Jen's tiny body fell out of her new mothers hands and bounced on a mushroom. She stood and ran to her mother. The mushrooms were double the height of Jen but she managed.

    "Jen..." Said the woman. "I think I've...twisted something...you go...I'll be fine..."
    Jen shook her head and pulled on the woman's outstretched hands. The shrieks grew louder.

    "Take this." Said Jen's mother. She handed Jen her small backpack. "It's got things in it...that will keep you...safe."
    Jen was about to protest when there was a howl. Their pursuers were less than 15 feet from them. She took one last look at her new mother and ran.
