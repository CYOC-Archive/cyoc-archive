    added A year ago AR Kid Toddler Baby

    Charlotte and Madison are two attractive young women in their 30s who are very fascinated by video games. It could be said that they are like their favorite hobbies of the two. The two live in the same apartment and both have a huge collection of videogames and have managed to complete the vast majority of them, from the easiest to the most difficult of any existing genre.
    The only bad thing is that as they always tend to finish them quickly, they always have to search for which games they haven't tried yet or which ones are available.
    And one day, they would come across something that would change their lives completely.

    It was a normal day like any other and Charlotte was searching on the Internet for video games they hadn't tried yet. And among all the websites that she came across, there was one that was something special.
    It was a site called "Goo Goo Gaming" which was not only dedicated to selling video game related products, but was also dedicated to selling rare and ancient relics.
    And not only that, all the video games that the site sold were completely new to her. There wasn't a single one that seemed familiar to her.
    Immediately, Charlotte proceeded to investigate what types of video games this strange site sold. They had all genres: From action, fighting, platforms, racing, sports, puzzle, etc.

    And although the vast majority of video games seemed attractive to Charlotte, there was something that caught her attention. In almost all video games they had a somewhat particular description.
    For example, in fighting games, they had a description like: "Reduce your opponent to a crybaby and become a winner."
    Another example was the action games, which had descriptions such as: "Avoid dying in this challenging adventure or you will see how little by little you will reduce yourself to a defenseless baby" or "Complete this epic journey and face The King of Shadows, or you will see as little by little you will be reduced in a scared toddler"
    And in sports, parties, and even racing games they had descriptions like: "Whoever accumulates the most points will be the winner, and the loser will be reduced to a crying and fearful infant" or "Become a champion and see how all your opponents are gradually reduced in whiny infants"

    Charlotte assumed the descriptions meant that video games are really difficult. And that is something that Charlotte and Madison look for in a video game: That they be challenging and difficult to complete.

    So, without a second thought, Charlotte proceeded to purchase several of the site's video games. Also, Charlotte wanted these games to be like surprise gifts for Madison.

    A few days passed, and the video games that Charlotte ordered finally arrived. Charlotte immediately proceeded to open the package and check if everything she ordered was there. And yes, all the video games she ordered were found.

    Charlotte had ordered 12 video games and each one was of a different genre: fighting, racing, party, action-adventure, puzzle, sports, RPG, sandbox, platformer, simulation and even a virtual reality game. Each one was in good condition and for some strange reason they all smelled like baby powder.
