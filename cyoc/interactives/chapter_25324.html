And then you see the same green scales forming across her whole body, just as it had when you had rubbed it into her back. But things also seemed a little different this time. And it's then that the real changes begin.

Then her legs started to merge right between her thighs, and it continued downward until she had what appeared to be a tail. But it didn't look like a mermaids tail like you might have expected. No, as her former feet, now fin, turned itself on it's side, give a more fish look than a mermaids.

    Next, her arms begin to shrink into her body, as her hands, now flippers, grew in size to match her tail. And then her body as a whole began to take on a whole new shape. Her breast receded into her chest, as her top half swelled and her lower half shrank. But the swelling did not stop at her top half, instead it started expanding her neck too. And it was at this point that several tears opened in what was her neck, only to reveil what could only be gills.

    At this point, you were to terrified to move an inch, watching the maddness of her transformation before you. And while watching, you now see that all the hair on her head has simply disolved away, leaving her bald. Her eyes closed, and opened to show a full black with only a ring of color. The eyes of the fish she was to become. But at this point her body's changes quickend, rapidy reshaping what few changes were left. Her body now thinned across it's length, as she finally took her final form.

    What was now before you was a giant green bass. And yet one thing still remained. As you look the giant fish over, you notice it getting smaller. Rapidly, the fish that was your girlfriend shirks down to the size of a normal lake bass, and begins to flop around on the bed.

    Still in shock, you sit for a moment, not believing what had just happened. But in a rare moment of sanity at this time, you realize that your girlfriend, now being a fish, needs water to breath or she might die. So you quickly scoop up the bass and run to your fish tank in the living room. Luckly you hadn't sold the fish tank after your fish died about a week ago from your neighboor not feeding them while you were on vacation. And you drop her into the water.

    Now sitting in front of the fish tank, you again go into shock with all that has happened. Your girlfriend had just turned completely into a fish. And now that fish was swimming around in your aquarium and showing no signs of human intelligence. Not sure of what to do, you run back into the bedroom to see if there was anything on the bottle that you had missed that would explain this.

    In the bedroom you find the bottle, but again it gives you no information. Then you search the bag that the shop owner had put the oil in, and you find a small piece of paper. The paper reads:
    Congradulations on your oil purchase, please follow the percentage guide for desired transformations.
    1 hand full, small transformation with little physical changes.
    2 hand fulls, medium transformation with moderate physical changes.
    3 hand fulls, large transformation with major physical changes.
    4 hand fulls, complete transformation.
    All transformation effects will reverse after 24 hours.

    WARNING: DO NOT USE WHOLE BOTTLE, PERMANENT EFFECTS COULD RESULT. If whole bottle is used by accident, wait 24 hours for changes. If no changes occur, changes may be permanent. However, if small changes do occur, return to store and ask for more information.
