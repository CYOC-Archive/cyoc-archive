    " You little shit ! Come back here, so that I can tear you apart for trying to take away my son ! "

    " Go to hell ! " is what she can process through your hissings, " I was only trying to solve the situation, and YOU worsened it ! "

    Meanwhile, her son is staring at the whole scene, processing the correlation between two dogs, a cat and some vehicule keys. It's only a matter of seconds before he understands what happened this whole time.
