    Ty's Power

    Frog Legs

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by AstonMartin 7 years ago O

    Ty always had a thing for frog legs. And these were gigantic!
    He had never seen frog legs of that size! He totally forgot about
    Jennifer and thought only of "Cuisses de Grenouille". Or how
    about "sautéed frogs' legs"? Ty had seen them served simply
    with garlic and lemon juice. He had always dreamed of dining
    on frogs' legs at the annual frog fair at Vittel in France, but
    he had never come around it. Well ... first there was the
    financial issue. He had never had the money to do it, but
    since he had gotten the powers to do as he pleased he could
    have afforded to go or at least to wish to be there. That would
    be enough. Just to wish to be at Vittel and have frog's legs
    as many as he wanted.

    The frog in his arm said "ribbit, ribbit" in disapproval, or
    so it seemed. "Anyway, where was I?" Ty exclaimed. "Oh,
    right!" With that he turned the frog upside down, grabbed
    it by its feet and smashed its head onto the ground. He then
    took out a knife and cut off the ´legs and left the rest for the
    birds. He then went home and on his way thought about what
    he'd do to those yummy frog legs. Should he fry them with
    garlic and butter? Or how about some pesto and garlic, simmed
    in a pan with some black pepper and Himalayan salt? He could
    put the frog legs into the oven, they were large enough for
    that. When they were done and ready he could use the basil-
    pesto with garlic as some kind of dip.

    Just thinking of it was mouthwatering. And as he had reached
    his home, he did so. He cooked the frog legs up and made himself
    the most delicious meal he had in ages. Despite all his powers,
    there was nothing that could beat home-cooked meals with
    fresh ingredients. Something, however, was nagging on him.
    There was something he should have thought about, something
    important, something he should not have forgotten. Ty was looking
    at his plate and at the two frog legs he was about to eat.
    "Oh, right!" he said. How could he forget? "French baguette!
    Whats a French meal without the baguette?" Ty snapped his
    fingers and the typical French white-bread appeared. There
    was no stopping now: Ty dug in and enjoyed his meal!
