    added by Raven Cross A year ago A BM S O Anthro Reptilian Body merge

    Andrew & Maxwell walked into their local Animalia Transformation Center. Both only wore a pair of shorts. They approached the employee & Maxwell told the human employee "We have come here to be transformed into an anthromorphic chimera." The employee asked "So who wants to go first?" Andrew said "You misunderstand. We are here to be transformed into an anthromorphic chimera. We want to be merged into one." Maxwell went on to explain "I want to have my lower body be goat & my upper body be lion. I want to still be able to speak & have paw-like hands. As for Andrew here he will be 100% feral snake. He'll still be able to speak but with a hissing lisp. He will be attached to me where I would have a tail. He's be my tail. He will be able to move himself freely & I will have no control over his snake body. He will be a green tree python about 4 foot long." The employee looked at the 2 & said "This should be possible. But you really sure you want this?" The 2 men said "Yes," in unison & took off their shorts making them naked. They entered the transformation chamber as instructed & did everything they were told.
    5 months later...
    Sarah was waiting for her friends Maxwell & Andrew to arrive. They told her to meet them at this table outside this restaurant. She hadn't seen in monthes. Then it walked up. It was an anthromorphic creature with the lower body of a goat, upper body of a lion & had a green snake for a tail; the tail moved as if it had a mind of it's own. It was also naked. "Hello Sarah." The chimera creature said to Sarah's shock with Maxwell's voice. The snake tail then spoke with Andrew's voice "Sssup." Sarah yelled "Maxwell?! Andrew?! That you?!" "It's us Sarah." Maxwell said sitting down at the table. The snake tail that was Andrew came into view. "I didn't know you could be merged together like this." Maxwell answered "You can. We proof of that." Andrew nodded with a "It'sss great." Sarah asked curious "So how's it work?" Maxwell answered "Well I can eat & drink or Andrew can to feed my body & his body. Our bodies feed each other." Andrew then jumped in "But I have no pissss or ssshit holessss. All my wasste goesss through Maxwell'sss body. I also have no sesex organs i.e.penisess or ballss." Sarah amazed asked "Does it matter where someone who is a snake is attached to the person's body? Or how many?" Maxwell answered "No. Not at all." Sarah quickly whipped out her cellphone & gestured for Maxwell to stand up. Sarah did a conference call between her other friends Lucy, Richard, Philip, Wendy, David & Abby. "Hey guys. You know how we all talked about Gorgons & snakes?" The reply was all yes in various ways. Sarah turned the phone to video chat & pointed the phone at Maxwell & Andrew the anthromorphic chimera. "Hi." Both Maxwell & Andrew said. There was a lot of shocked talk. Sarah said then "I want to become a naga type Gorgon. A Gorgon that has a snake tail instead of legs & her whole body is covered in scales with a slight snake head. And I want you guys to be my 6 snakes for hair." There was excited talk about that. All their answers were various ways to say yes. "How about tomorrow after at about 3 pm for us to be merged together into a Gorgon?" There were various excited yes answers. Richard was the first to ask "Maxwell? Andrew? Uh..." Maxwell just said "Call us, me chimera." Richard reasked "Ok chimera can you explain what it's like to be a merged creature?" Maxwell answered "Sure." Lucy interrupted "Should we be able to speak as snakes?" There was a quick discussion between Sarah, Lucy, Richard, Wendy, David, Abby & Phillip. It was decided that Richard, Lucy, Wendy, David, Abby & Phillip won't be able to speak as snakes; only hiss sounds & the like. Also they would be genderless. And they would all give up their names to be nameless snakes. Only Sarah would have a name. Now that was settled they all began asking Chimera about being a merged being. And Chimera answered all their questions the best to their ability. It got late & as Sarah was about to end the conference call & leave Maxwell asked curious "You going to be naked like us as a Gorgon?" Sarah answered "Yes. Can't see any sense in wearing clothes as a mostly snake creature." This seemed to prompt Abby to say "We should all go together naked to the Transformation Center." David said "Would make sense sense none of us will ever be wearing anything ever again." Wendy then said "Yeah. We won't have a mess of clothes to take off for our merged transformation." "I'll take you in my van then." Maxwell suggested. Everyone loved that idea; so that was the plan. "I'll call them right now & explain our transformation & pay for it by credit card right now." Sarah said. Again everyone loved that idea. Sarah hung up & did just that.
    The next day...
    Sarah, Lucy, Wendy, David, Abby, Richard & Phillip all got out of the van at the Transformation Center. They were all naked. "Soon we'll be a Gorgon Sarah." Richard said with a smile. "Soon we'll be your snake hair." Wendy said with a smile. Sarah said with a smile "I can't thank you guys enough for agreeing to this." David said with a smile "No thanks necessary. We all want this." The 7 headed inside & they soon saw an anthromorphic cat of some kind (naked of course). He said a bit surprised "You all did come naked as you said you would." "Only made sense to." Abby stated. The cat agreed. The cat lead them to the Transformation Chamber & Richard joked to Sarah "Soon we'll be your snake hair Medusa." Sarah looked at them & smiled "Medusa. I like it. I will be Medusa my snakes." They all entered the chamber together. The chamber kicked on & their transformation began.
    An hour later a creature with a snake's tail instead of legs, a slight snake head, scales covering it's body & 6 moving snakes on its head where hair should be came slithering out of the building; a Gorgon. The van side door opened & the Gorgon slithered into the van & closed the door. "It's us Chimera." She said. The snakes hissed angry. "Oh sorry. We agreed it to only be me Medusa; not us. Less confusing." Sarah now Medusa corrected. Chimera looked back & Maxwell said "You look amazing." Andrew then said "I don't mean to offend but you look awesssome together." Medusa & the snakes were happy to hear that. "I have to agree Snake." Medusa asked confused "Snake?" Andrew now Snake explained "We decided I ssshould go by Sssnake Tail or Sssnake for ssshort & Maxwell by Chimera." Medusa thought about that & said "Yeah. It's less confusing." Her snakes agreed too (though they couldn't say it). "Aslo Medusa is a cool name. It's so fitting to you now." Chimera stated. "Agreed." Snake Tail nodded in agreement. Medusa asked "You guys hungry?" Chimera & Snake Tail answered in unison "I could eat." Medusa commented "I want to try swallowing food whole. I had myself remodeled to be able to unhinge my jaws & swallow food whole like any other snake." Snake Tail told her "It's really cool Medussa." Chimera chimed in "Even to watch."
    The End of This Tale.
