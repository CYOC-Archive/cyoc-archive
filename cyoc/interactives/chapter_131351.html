    -Sei-

    Void. Sei felt it. This spiralling nothingness. She couldn’t escape from the water’s high gradient. She was slipping and falling into the void. Her ears were bleeding, her nose was bleeding, He eyes were bleeding. But then the gradient seemed to lessen, it came easier to climb. She wiped the blood from her face and battled against the odds and climbed away from the void.

    She awoke.

    She could hear ringing…but also something else…a name…it seemed to echo in her mind “Sei…Sei…SEI!!”. She was shot back to reality. She was hanging from the wall. She looked around, the room was falling apart, but she could see It was some sort of underground laboratory. Sei saw vials of strange compounds on the table, she tried to read them but her vision was blurry.

    She then remembered. She was absorbed by that piano. She thought she was going to die. But she was oka- Her thoughts were broken off, by pain which seemed to form in her chest. She reached for it for the shackles stopped her. She was confused because the pain then went away.

    But it strikes back. Sei screamed in pain, as her chest violently pushed outwards and then back in. She looked down at the epicentre of the attack and saw her chest was changing colour to a dark black. She tried pulling her hands from the shackles, but then to her surprise they broke off. She wondered if they broke off due to the oldness of the room or her changing body.

    After her hands were free she brought them to her chest. She coughed violently “Oh…god…what is happening to me” she said slowly. Her chest was now completely black, but the flesh seemed to change as well to something more wooden and hollow. Her organs transfigured and stretched out. She lay on the ground in torture. But then her arms started changing. They twitched, and she heard bones the bones crunch. Her arms were getting thinner and started changing to the same medium as her chest.

    Her hands outstretched, seemed to pop off her bones, growing longer and pointier. The nails turning to claws. She lifted herself off the ground. She wasn’t going to let this happen to her, She had to do something. She with her new claws grabbed hold of the table and pulled herself up. She looked for something to help her, but saw nothing. She couldn’t hold her change. But then she realised. She couldn’t stop what was happening to her…but she could save mike. She grabbed a post it notes on the table and one of the pens on the floor and started writing whatever she could to help Mike. Until suddenly her legs gave way and started changing two, fragments of her leg ripping through her clothes like thorns. Her feet shrinked but like her hands the toes outstretched and formed claws.

    Her entire lower body was this monster now. She lay on the ground, with tears in her eyes. She had hoped she wrote enough…enough for him to understand. She could feel her neck changing, growing smaller. She cried in pain but found her voice lower in pitch and more monstrous. Then it was her head, it felt like her skull was ripping itself apart, shrinking the cage around her brain, and then absorbing her ears and nose. Until hey eyes and mouth were the only ones left.

    She saw her vision change to scarlet green, like some sort of night vision and her mouth grow sharper teeth. But then suddenly she felt this change in perception her mind was changing. She could see herself falling, back into spiral of nothingness, climbing against the water’s high gradient, falling into the void. She was alone again.

Erick saw it as well, it was some sort of necklace, which glinted a purple light in the skylight above.

    In fact, the only light was from the skylight and for some strange reason Erick looked up, to see to his horror the cause of death. The skylight was fractured, the was a massive whole in the middle. Which explained the glass on the body? But how did this happen? There was no floor above this one, except for the attic but that no windows. No force on earth could of caused this. Which made Erick ever more eager to leave.

If that happened to Seth or Maisy he might have had the same opinion. But they weren’t here so Erick decided to tell him a lie. “I’m sorry….Mike…Sei’s gone”. He saw in front of him his friend crumble to the ground in denial “No….No”.

    Ryan nearly forget about his injury when he made to stand up, He felt pain in his lower chest but felt it was nearly gone. Like he had been healed suddenly. He looked down and to his surprise there was a bandage around his wound and even more surprising it was recently dressed meaning…someone had found him. Ryan hoped it was Jonathan perhaps he found him in the cellar and healed him. But Jonathan didn’t know anything about wounds, so it couldn’t have been him. That would also mean it wasn’t Mike or Erick because they know nothing about how to stich a wound.

    But there it was, nicely stitched and with a perfectly applied dressing. Ryan then had the theory it was Sei…perhaps she found him because she was training to be a nurse she could have found him. There was a delight of vials and glasses on the shelves and chemicals of many different colours. Ryan could see this as some sort of laboratory perhaps Jonathan was here. But that only brought up more questions. Why was he in a laboratory now. It didn’t make any sense.

    He walked around the room and to his surprise saw another door, maybe that led out the complex and to Jonathan. He walked through the door, which nicely slided open for him. Whatever Lab he was in…he was in high security one.

He looked at the codex map and found a lab 47, he followed the directions and made his way towards it.
