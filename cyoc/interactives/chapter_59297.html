    Ty's Mistake

    Ty sat and watched Dustin try to hide himself on the TV. Every time he had tried to
    hide or put clothes on Ty would will the concealment away. It was a miracle that no
    one had seen him yet and Ty was about to will someone there but then he saw a girl
    walking down the street. She hadn't noticed Dustin yet but Dustin had noticed her.
    Dustin was frozen in fear looking at the girl and Ty thought of a way to make Dustin's
    life even harder. He willed the girl's clothes away and waited for the inevitable scream.
    Instead the girl looked around with an angry look on her face and spotted Dustin. She
    went up to him saying, "You like making people's clothes disappear huh."

    "It wasn't me," Dustin said unfreezing and stepping back.

    A look of confusion spread across the girl's face and she asked in a calm tone, "Who
    did it?"

    "My foster brother Ty," Dustin said looking terrified. He had every right to be terrified
    since Ty was going to make him pay for mentioning his name.

    The girl sighed and held out her hand saying, "Take my hand and I'll see what I can
    do."

    Dustin slowly reached out and grabbed the girl's hand. Once she was touching Dustin
    she smiled and snapped her fingers. As soon as she snapped her fingers they were in
    the room. Ty jumped a bit and asked, "Who are you?"

    "You must be Ty," the girl said narrowing her eyes. "I'm Lisa and I'm here to find out
    why you transformed your foster brother."

    "He's picked on me since I came here," Ty said bitterly, "I wanted to punish him a bit."

    "Is this true ," Lisa asked looking at Dustin.

    "Well maybe I have a bit," Dustin admitted.

    "Well then I'm not going to interfere with your punishment," Lisa said shaking her head.
    "I've already done that by bringing you back here so I'll send you back."

    "Please do..." Dustin started to say but Lisa snapped her fingers and he was back to
    wherever he had been.

    Lisa looked at Ty with an angry expression on her face and said, "I don't mind being
    naked but I don't like it when someone removes my clothes." Ty willed a change to
    happen to Lisa but she quickly raised her hand and a ball of pulsing red light appeared
    in it. Lisa scowled and said, "I don't like being transformed either unless I'm
    transforming myself or the person has my permission to transform me. Now let's see
    what you were going to transform me into." She peered into the ball of light and said,
    "Ah you were going to transform me into a doll. Well by the surprised look on your
    face I'd say you only recently discovered your powers. This means that you don't know
    how to catch transformations so I could turn this back on you." She held the ball a little
    higher to clarify what she was talking about.

    Ty looked nervous and said, "I'm sorry. I won't try it again."

    Lisa's face softened and she closed her hand extinguishing the light. She sat down on
    the bed and said, "You need a mentor. Someone to teach you how to use your power
    and how to defend yourself. Since I'm the first one that has come across you I could
    be your mentor. However there is a rule that must be followed. The mentor and
    student must be the same sex. One of us will have to become the opposite sex and
    it's not going to be me since I will not go back to being a male. That leaves us with
    you becoming a girl if you want me to be your mentor. If you want to remain a boy I'll
    leave and hopefully another will come to be your mentor. What will you do?"
