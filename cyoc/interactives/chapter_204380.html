A man going on about a secret lab where they put people into new bodies being admitted to a ward? Stranger things had happened. You'd then get to live out the rest of your life in there. You had been black for all of 30 minutes and you were already treated differently because of your skin? You'd move to Los Angeles, because apparently they thought your kind of body would fit in well on the beaches there. These people were probably putting you into positions they'd put themselves in. That's the feeling you got at least. But you didn't want any of this. You wanted to be you.
