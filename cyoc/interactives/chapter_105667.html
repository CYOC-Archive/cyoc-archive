    The Lion and the Latex

    added by The Brilliant Dr. Garfield 7 years ago O

    Staring back at you in the mirror is an anthro latex lion. Your skin...er, latex, is a dark honey color. Parish hands that can be used like human ones run down the length of your muscular body, producing squeaks as the latex rubs against itself. Your long rubber tail flicks and sways behind you. Your face has pushed out into a short muzzle, and your eyes are golden and your pupils are slitted, with your ears at the top of your head. Your feet have lengthened and pulled, giving them a slightly feline appearance, but you still appear to be bipedal. You don't have a mane, so you appear to be a latex lioness. Despite your rubbery skin, you feel powerful. But you're still scared.

    "Hello?" you try to say. You can still talk, but your voice sounds growly and low. Still shaking, you are suddenly gripped by a massive headache. Yowling, you bring your paws up to your head as you collapse on the floor. Your golden eyes fill completely with black and your psyche is sucked down a long, dark hole. Your body lies motionless on the bathroom floor.

    "GREETINGS TRANSFORMED HUMAN." A loud booming voice echoes in this dark place. You open your eyes, but see nothing but blackness. You try to move around, but can't, as you appear to have no body. "I SUPPOSE YOU ARE WONDERING WHAT HAS JUST HAPPENED TO YOU." You open your mouth to speak, but you realize you don't have one. "NO MATTER." The voice booms. "I WILL START AT THE BEGINNING. EONS AGO, I LANDED ON THIS PLANET, BUT I HAD THE BAD LUCK TO LAND IN AN ABANDONED, DESOLATE CAVE ON A MOUNTAIN WHICH JUST SO HAPPENED TO BE NEAR WHERE YOUR TOWN WOULD BE BUILT THOUSANDS OF YEARS LATER."

    "I ONLY HAD ONE THING IN MIND: TO TRANSFORM. SINCE I COULD NOT MOVE MYSELF UNTIL I HAD NOURISHMENT, I SAT THERE IN A PUDDLE FOR THOUSANDS OF YEARS. THE ANIMALS THAT LIVED IN THE MOUNTAINS RECONGINZED ME FOR THE EVIL THING I WAS AND STAYED AWAY. I BEGAN TO THINK I WOULD NEVER GET TO DO MY JOB. THAT IS, UNTIL LAST AFTERNOON, WHEN AN UNFORTUNATE HIKER FOUND MY CAVE. HE SLIPPED AND FELL INTO ME. IT WAS GLORIUS. I SLID OVER HIS BODY, TWISTING, CONTORTING, AND TRANSFORMING IT INTO THE PURPLE WOLF YOU SAW LAST NIGHT. I COULD MOVE BY MYSELF NOW, I WANTED TO TRY MY NEW SOLDIER OUT, SO I FOLLOWED HIM TO THE STREETLIGHT WHERE HE ATTACKED THE MAN. THE MAN BECAME A BLUE CAT, AND THEY RETREATED BACK TO THE CAVE. THAT WOULD HAVE BEEN THE END OF IT, BUT YOU STUCK YOUR FOOT IN ME. ONCE I HAVE TASTED A HUMAN, I CANNOT REST UNTIL I HAVE TRANSFORMED THEM. IT APPEARS I HAVE COMPLETED MY JOB." The voice pauses, as if to take a breath, then continues.

    "NOW, YOU WILL GO BACK WITH ME TO THE CAVE ONCE YOU HAVE TRANSFORMED ANOTHER HUMAN. THEN, MY FOUR NEW SOLDIERS AND I WILL REUTRN TO THE CAVE AND THERE WE WILL SLUMBER FOR ANOTHER THOUSAND YEARS, UNTIL IT IS TIME TO FEAST AGAIN. DO NOT TRY TO RESIST, BECAUSE YOU ARE NOW PART OF ME AND I AM NOW PART OF YOU. I WILL JUST CHANGE YOU MENTALLY IF YOU TRY TO GET HELP OR RUN AWAY. BACK TO REALITY WITH YOU." And with that, you're sucked up back the long hole .

    Your golden eyes snap back open and you find yourself lying on your bathroom floor, tail between your legs, panting heavily. You get to your paws and look in the mirror at your lion face and body. You put your paws on the counter and take a few deep breaths. You've been transformed into an anthro latex lioness, and it has invaded your mind and wants you to transform another person before going back to a cave to sleep for a thousand years. That sounds like a science fiction move plot. And it's all happened to you.
