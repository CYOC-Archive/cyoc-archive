    The Magic Shop

    A Brilliant Idea!

    As Alex stared at the magical flashlight, someone approached his door. THUMP THUMP THUMP! "Gah!" Alex yelped, nearly dropping the flashlight. "Yo, open up!" shouted another familiar voice. Alex sighed again and went to open the door, revealing Cammy, Sammy's friend.

    Cammy was a tall darkskinned girl with rich brown eyes, cocoa colored skin, full lips, a poofy afro, and a shapely figure, which included a chest size of 32D. She and Sammy had met when they were in grade school and had been fast friends since then. In fact, Cammy and Sammy currently went to the same college. Cammy popping in and out of Sammy and his house was nothing abnormal.

    "Is Sammy here?" Cammy asked. Alex glanced back over his shoulder into the l living room and said, "Y-yeah..." "Oh, good!" Cammy said, pushing past Alex and heading straight towards the living room. Thinking quickly, Alex switched the flashlight's pause switch on and shined it at Cammy. Instantly, Cammy froze in place. Alex shut and locked the door behind himself and strolled towards Cammy's frozen form.

    "This is perfect!" Alex exclaimed happily. "I was gonna test this thing on Sammy, but this is way better!" Cammy stayed politely quiet. "Now, let's go somewhere more... private," Alex chuckled almost evilly. Alex then grabbed Cammy and dragged her upstairs to his room. Alex switched off the flashlight and looked at the switches. "Well, I already tried the middle one..." Alex murmured, gesturing towards Cammy's stiffened figure. "What about this one?" Alex then switched on the left switch, the one that had the image of a box in a box.

    When the flashlight burst to life, its light was thin and red, like a laser pointer. The light landed squarely on Cammy's nose. Immediately, Cammy's nose began to inflate like a balloon, complete with the hissing noise and everything. "What the heck?" Alex murmured, switching the switch off. Tentatively, Alex reached for Cammy's inflated nose. It still felt like skin, but the skin seemed to be stretched taught. "It feels like a balloon..." Alex noted, pointing the flashlight at Cammy's nose and switching the box switch on once more. Once the red light hit Cammy's nose, it began to shrink back to normal. "I guess the light undoes its own effects," Alex murmured.

    Alex shifted his attention to the final switch, the right switch that seemed to have an image of the copy-paste symbol on it. Alex switched it on and pointed the flashlight at Cammy. Almost instantly, the light went from its normal white to a dull gray, much like the color of Cammy's sweater. "Why did the light change color?" Alex murmured confusedly as he shifted the light to Cammy's face. At that moment, Cammy's face changed to a dull gray and the light picked up the cocoa color of her skin. Immediately, Alex flicked off the flashlight and touched Cammy's face. To his surprise, Cammy's face felt like the thick fabric of her sweater. Alex switched the copy-paste switch back on and shone the now cocoa-colored light on Cammy's face once more. Cammy's face returned to normal, as did the light's color.

    Alex switched off the flashlight and sat down on his bed. He couldn't help but smile. "The old man was giving away THIS?" Alex said, falling back on his bead and shaking his head. Alex sat up and smiled. There was a lot more that he wanted to experiment with. So for now, Alex would...
