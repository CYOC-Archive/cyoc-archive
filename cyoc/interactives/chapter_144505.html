    “Since I’m with Beatrice she’d probably like to see a romantic film.” Walter thought to himself. “Gimme two tickets to ‘Bird and the Bee’.”

    The usher looked a bit confused. “Are you sure about you wanna see that movie? It’s main demographic is young couples and you two are-“

    “Don’t give me sass, Buster Brown!” Walter hissed. “I’m going a date night with my wife and this is what we’re gonna watch.”

    “Fair enough sir, here are your tickets.” The usher said as he gave them the tickets. The elderly couple then made their way to the where the movie was screening.

    “Walter, this is the kind of movie I would’ve liked if I were a teenager.” Beatrice said as the previews played. “Not sure I’d get the same enjoyment out of it after being in a marriage for many decades.”

    “Dear none of the movies really appealed to us.” Walter replied. “Besides I’d probably hate this lovey dovey crap at any age.”

    As the movie began, so did the changes to the Elderly couple. It happened gradually as the film went on. Walter’s hair began to darken into its original black along with his beard which gradually started shrinking. Beatrice’s hair started to look more lively as it gradually regained its blonde color and started to get longer. Walter’s muscle tone began to return making his skinny body look more muscular. Beatrice’s body started to thin out as her breasts started to sag less. Their wrinkles and liver spots started to disappear as their once ancient faces started looking more full of life with Wallace looking like a handsome man in his mid 40s with a full head of black hair and a thick black beard while his wife looked like a beautiful woman in her early 40s with long blonde hair and little to no wrinkles covering her face. Beatrice’s once plump body was now an hourglass figure and Walter’s once bag of bones body was now the ideal male body. Walter’s muscles began to shrink and his beard was nonexistent. Beatrice’s figure also started to diminish. Their mature faces started to smooth out and become more youthful. Their hair also seemed to get longer and livelier. The two were now teens in age appropriate clothes on their first date together.

    “Thank you for picking this movie, Wally!” The rejuvenated elder whispered to her former husband. “I’ve always wanted to see it!”

    “Yeah, don’t mention it, Bee.” Wally responded, trying to hide the fact he absolutely hated this movie.
