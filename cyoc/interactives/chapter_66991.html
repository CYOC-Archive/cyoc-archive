    Jeff is still Richie's loyal obedient dog trapped in a human body

    Jeff looked like his old self. He even wore the same kind of clothing he used to always wear. Jeff seemed on the surface to even act just like his
    old self. On the surface, it didn't seem that anything had changed. That helped Richie feel better because he no longer had to worry about
    explaining what happened to Jeff. As far as anyone was concerned, nothing had happened to Jeff. Jeff was just secretly dating Brad instead of
    Beth. Beth knew but she had lost her attraction to Jeff anyway so she accepted that her former boyfriend was now her brother's boyfriend.

    But then Jeff pulled Richie aside for a private talk. Jeff scolded, "Why did you do this to me? Why did you use the Chronivac on me without
    talking to me first?"

    Richie said, "Didn't you want to experience life as a dog?"

    Jeff said, "Yes, but not this way. You took control of what kind of dog I became. Then you made me loyal to you. Then you made me gay and
    gave me to Bruiser. Now you've turned us both into human gay lovers. Shouldn't I have some say in all of this."

    Richie said, "I'm sorry. You're right. But weren't you about to change me into a dog without asking me?"

    Jeff asked, "Is that what you thought? I wouldn't have done that. The idea crossed my mind but I would have talked to you first."

    Richie asked, "Should I change you back."

    Jeff said, "No, you and Beth look happy together. I'm loyal to you now. I want to make you happy so I'll let Brad plow me if it will help you get
    closer to Beth. May I ask a favor though?"

    Richie said, "Sure, what is it?"

    Jeff said, "Since you changed me so that I want to be plowed and I'm loyal to you, it's only fitting that you should plow me sometimes too.
    Because of what you did with the Chronivac, it's really you I want inside of me, not Brad. Figure out someway for me to be your dog again."

    What do you do now?
