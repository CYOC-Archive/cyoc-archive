    The Madame Illusia

    A paternity test

    "Well I don't really have any kind of desire. At most I would like to be a father", Damon told Madame Illusia.

    "Oh, so you two don't have kids yet?", Madame Illusia asked him.

    "No. My girlfriend doesn't like children. Besides, she thinks I wouldn't be able to take care of a child on my own", Damon replied. At that moment, Madame Illusia had an idea.

    "In that case, could you ask your girlfriend to come on stage too?" Madame Illusia said to Damon. That's when Damon's girlfriend, Monica, came on stage.

    "Tell me young lady, would you like to have a child?", Madame Illusia asked her.

    "Not really. Or at least for now", Monica replied.

    "And why is that?"

    "Look. It's not that I hate children, it's that I just don't like the idea of having one. I think they can be very disobedient, mischivious or even annoying. And it's even worse when they're younger".

    "And if I managed to convince you that children aren't so bad, would you finally consider the idea of having one?", Madame Illusia asked Monica.

    "Maybe, but I don't think it would be that easy to convince me", Monica replied.

    "We'll see about that", Madame Illusia told Monica before turning back to Damon.

    "Damon, do you really want to be a father?"

    "Well...yeah", Damon replied.

    "But do you promise to take good care of your kid no matter the circumstances?"

    "I promise with all my heart," Damon told her. At that moment, Madame Illusia let out a big smile.

    "In that case... it's magic time!", Madame Illusia exclaimed just before casting a spell at Monica with her wand. As a result, a large amount of smoke began to surround Monica. But when it disappeared and everyone could see Monica again, everyone in the audience, including Damon, stood frozen open-mouthed.

    Monica watched as both Damon and the audience stared at her in surprise. At first she wasn't sure why until she noticed something strange. She saw that now her hands were quite small and chubby, almost like a baby's, and now her pretty green dress had been replaced by a green sleeper. And to make it even stranger, she saw that everything was now more gigantic than before.

    "What the hell is going on here?", Monica wondered.

    "Goo Goo BaBa Boo?" Monica said.

    "Wait, why did I say that? Wait a second. Everything now is gigantic, I have baby hands, I'm wearing a small sleeper, I talk like a baby, and I smell like baby powder... oh no."

    Monica finally knew what had happened to her. That crazy woman somehow turned her into a 1-year-old baby girl.

    "Let's give the young lady a big round of applause!", Madame Illusia exclaimed to the audience who immediately began to applaud for having witnessed such a transformation.

    Meanwhile, Damon didn't know how to react to what had just happened. This was so bizarre that he even felt like he was going to faint. But before he could do so, he heard Madame Illusia say something to him.

    "Now Damon, take good care of Monica and teach her that you can be a good father."

    "But what's going to happen to Monica? Is she going to stay like this forever?", Damon asked her.

    What did Madame Illusia answer?"
