    Leah Loves Her Diapers

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 4 days ago AR O Clothes Mental Baby

    Clearly, Leah likes using diapers. Even before Amber made her comfortable using them in front of people who know who she truly is, she was secretly going to daycare just so she could pee and poop herself. But Amber's goal is to make Leah enjoy using diapers even in front of, say, her crush at school, with said crush knowing exactly who she is. Luckily, there's a tape for adult babies that fits the bill there as well.

    Leah gets put down for her afternoon nap and Amber plays the video. She then leaves and makes a phone call. Once Leah wakes up, she's going to test whether it worked or not. Laxatives in her post-nap bottle will guarantee she goes, but Amber wants Leah to go in front of someone she would normally be terrified to fill a diaper in front of, and not just not care, but actively enjoy using that diaper.

    Leah wakes up two hours later, the video finished running. Her tummy rumbles. Leah grins. She squats in her crib and begins to push. A smile spreads across her face as she feels a log enter her diaper. She loves using her diaper so much. When she finishes pooping, she pees as well. Then, for the heck of it, sits on her mess and wiggles around, giggling.

    Amber enters.

    "You made a big stinky mess," she remarks. Leah giggles again. "Here. Drink up while I change you." Leah eagerly accepts the bottle and guzzles hungrily as Amber removes her diaper, wipes her down, and puts a fresh diaper on. To her surprise, Amber then dresses her in a cute pink sundress.

    "Why are you dressing me up?" she asks. She finishes her bottle and burps.

    "Because we're going somewhere." Amber brings Leah out of the nursery and then places her in a stroller by the front door. "We're going to see someone very special."

    "Who?"

    "You'll see," Amber says with a grin. She can't wait for Leah to embarrass herself just like she's about to do. They're going to go meet up with...
