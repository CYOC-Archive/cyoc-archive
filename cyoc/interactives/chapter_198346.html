    It had to be Luna, Selena decided. There was no contest. As much as Diana teased and roughhoused with Selena, Selena almost sort of liked her. The werewolf jock honestly seemed to think her antics were all in good fun. Even the girlfriend stealing appeared to be unintentional, a natural side effect of being the most ripped wolf in the pack.

    The same could not be said for Luna. Luna Lyall was the family ice queen: beautiful, distant, and often callous. She knew she was the most beautiful wolf and felt that put her above all her packmates aside from her alpha parents. On that note, Luna was all too aware that she was her father's heir apparent as alpha. Selena cringed at the thought of Luna as alpha. She was bad enough as she was! Selena hoped Diana would challenge their older sister for the position; with her strength, she'd make short work of the pack's prissy princess! In the meantime, Luna needed some serious payback for all the sneering glances, all the put-downs, and all the whispering about the pack's resident dud wolf with her friends. Selena had given up hope of that... until today.

    "That's actually a really great idea, Tats," Selena said at last. "Luna especially really has it fucking coming. I'm in!" she handed Morgana twenty dollars. "Here you go. Now please, please teach me the spell!" She was practically bouncing up and down with excitement.

    Morgana smiled at her client. "My, my! So much like an eager puppy! I can practically see your tail wagging now. Don't worry, you'll soon have it for real. The incantation goes as follows: 'Wolf thou art, but wolf no longer! Thy wolf is mine, to make me stronger!' You must clasp hands with the victim, or the incantation is useless. It will take about a month to gain full use of your wolfgirl form."

    "Aw, crap!" Selena moaned. "The full moon's tonight! I won't wolf out?"

    Morgana shrugged. "Not really. If you did the spell today, you'd get a bit hairier tonight, and your fingernails, toenails, and canine teeth might sharpen a bit. That's it. It'll slowly get more drastic over the next month. Meanwhile, Luna will transform a bit less dramatically every night until in a month's time she finds she can't shift from her human form at all."

    Selena hung her head in frustration. "Damn it."

    Tatiana put a comforting arm around Selena's shoulders. "Sucks."

    Morgana pondered a bit. "Well... there is a way to speed the transformation up. I... hadn't wanted to mention it, on account of Luna being your sister."

    Selena's head snapped up. "What is it?!"

    "Well," Morgana went on, "before you perform the chant, you... have sex with the target."

    "What?!" Selena choked.

    "It's the exchange of psychic energies during lovemaking, you see," Morgana explained. "That's how succubi and incubi feed, you know. That blast of energy from Luna's soul should instantly transfer all of her wolf essence to you. I completely understand if you don't want to do this with your sister, of course."

    "Damn..." Selena sighed in shock. "Sex with... my sister... I dunno if I can take it that far."

    "I don't really get the big deal," said Tatiana. "Vampires date their my family all the time. My sisters Liesel and Claudia are girlfriends."

    Selena rolled her eyes. "That isn't the same thing, Tats. You vampires join your clan by being turned. Carmilla and Mircalla didn't give birth to Liesel and Claudia; they came from different human mothers. That's not they way werewolf packs work. Your sister is your SISTER! It's just... gross! Besides, I freaking hate Luna. I wouldn't wanna have sex with her even if she weren't my sister."

    Tatiana smirked. "That's what you say, but I've seen you checking out that firm, toned ass of hers."

    Selena rolled her eyes again. "Sure, Tatiana. Believe whatever you want. Well, I'd better go and at least perform the normal version of the spell."

    Tatiana gave Selena a thumbs up and a bright, fanged grin. "Awesome, Lena! See ya tomorrow in class!"

    "It's a date," said Selena. "Bye, Tats! And thanks, Morgana!"
