    Ty, Peter and Allen had decided to hang out on that lazy Sunday afternoon, just to chill and have fun, before the start of a new week of classes in high school. The year had just started, and already they each had writing assignments, tests, and quizzes coming their way, so this was the last chance to hang out before they would be buried in their studies.

    It was still early when they left to go home. Luckily, all three of them just lived within a few blocks from each other, and they knew they could get together later that night. Peter was still living in Dustin’s house, along with Ty, so he and his cousin rode their bikes home together.

    After they had arrived, Peter suddenly started hearing Joce’s voice ringing inside of his head.

    “Hi there, honey… I’m happy to see you’re home. Want to come over and hang out with me?”

    “Sure…” Peter replied out loud, as he was standing in his bedroom. “Well, I had wanted to go and work out in the gym, first… I need to do arms today!”

    “That can be arranged…” Joce chuckled.

    Suddenly, Peter’s jeans and button shirt disappeared, and they were replaced with his tank top and his spandex shorts, along with his basketball high-tops on his feet!

    “Whoa!” Peter exclaimed, as he noticed how his girlfriend changed his clothes from a distance. He was constantly amazed by her powers.

    Then, Peter disappeared into thin air, instantly materializing at the entrance to Golden’s Gym!

    “Heh… Thanks for saving me the bike ride over here, Joce…” Peter whispered to himself, knowing that Joce could hear everything he said, thought or heard.

    “Now, go in and show them who you are and what you can do!” Joce chuckled.

    Peter just laughed as he strolled in the gym. He showed his ID card at the front desk, before he wandered to the locker room. He put away his soft bag in his locker, before he walked back out to the weight room.

    Peter was 6ft 3 inches tall, so he wasn’t a small guy. He was lean and muscular, and Joce liked him that way. Occasionally, Joce wanted to have some fun with him, and she deliberately kept him lean, until the right opportunity came to make him grow.

    As Peter walked over to the rack of dumbbells, he spotted Butch. Butch was 6 ft and 6 inches tall, and he was built like a tank. He had thick legs, massive arms and a huge chest which was sticking out. His bald head and huge mustache made him seem even more intimidating to Peter.

    As Peter was about to walk up to where he was standing, he suddenly froze, when he was overcome by that familiar tingling feeling.

    “I don’t want you to feel intimidated by that big guy” Joce said inside of Peter’s mind. “So, you’re going to match him in size! Here you go!”

    “Uh oh…” Peter whispered to himself.

    Indeed, his legs suddenly started to grow longer, while his quads and calves increased proportionally. He grew a few inches wider, while his torso and chest expanded as well. His arms filled out some more, making them long and beefy. His abdominals grew, so that they were like buttons on his stomach, forming a neat row of a six-pack. Joce added four inches to Peter’s height, suddenly making him stand 6 foot 7 inches tall, making him an inch taller than Butch! Peter wasn’t overly muscles, just bigger! He knew that she was holding out, so she could make him grow gradually over time, rather than in one shot!

    “Hey there Peter…” Butch said as he greeted Peter, who was standing next to him, while facing the large mirror which was before them. “You’ve grown a little bit, huh?”

    “Yeah…. It must be the healthy food that my girlfriend cooks for me…” he chuckled in reply. He knew he was lying, but he decided to be modest. He knew that Joce would have more tricks up her sleeve, as Peter was making another visit to the gym!

    Peter started his routine of bicep curls, while standing right next to big Butch. He knew that Butch was staring at him, watching his development. At the same time, Peter gulped, when he was overcome by that familiar tingling feeling in his arms.

    What would Joce do next?
