    Isaac Evan Wismon sprinted through the cloud forest. Just like when he ran through the forest to find Nat, Isaac allowed his mind to wander.

    Thanks to the cloud shoes and corrupt air he absorbed from the Sky King's air dragon, he is a lot lighter then he was then when he first arrived in the cloud plane. Though he's not as light nor as flexible as his formally human self, he is at least as light as Ilivir was when he was a spawn. Meaning Isaac no longer has to worry about sinking through the clouds when he's standing still. He's still a lot slower then most cloud people, but he can at least sprint at a reliable pace.

    Though he doesn't feel much more lighter then he was before his battle with the air dragon. Which is strange. Isaac feels as though he should be a little more lighter then he should. He did absorbed a small portion of the air dragon's essence... unless...

    Isaac looked down at the metal pouch covering his twin cocks and the baby air dragon.

    ...unless... most of the essence absorbed from the air dragon was used to spawn this little guy...

    But Isaac can worry about that later. There's more recent developments to worry about.

    Who is Alicia? If she can change her appearance, then what is her true form? Or did he already saw her true appearance?

    His suspicions were correct in thinking that the mansion was her property. But that brings up the question on how did she get here. Does she have a method to traveling to other worlds... or did she happen to step through a portal like he did?

    His two spiritual companions were strongly quiet since Isaac's second encounter with that witch. Why is that?

    'I've seen her before...' Ilivir said out of the blue.

    'W-what ever do you mean?' Isaac mentally inquired.

    'I don't understand how I know ... but I seen her during my time....' Ilivir explained.

    Dark Isaac seem to have taken an interest. 'May I ask the circumstance of such a encounter?'

    '...No... You may not.' Ilivir replied. Ilivir seems to be thinking to himself.

    Isaac decided to direct his attention to someone else.

    'So... Dark Isaac... I seems that she angers you very easily... is there a reason for that?' Isaac asked.

    'Of course! How dare she takes control away from you! Only I have the right to do that! I must prove that I'm THE REAL ISAAC! I MUST PROVE THAT I"M NOT AN ANOMALY WHO"S DOESN"T SOPPED TO EXSIS-' Dark Isaac cut himself off.

    Isaac found himself surprised by Dark Isaac's reaction. It seems that he wants to prove that his existence means something, to prove that he has a identity.

    Dark Isaac is seeming less and less like a evil persona and more like a shadow who's trying to prove that he exists.

    Dark Isaac noticed Isaac's amusement.

    'Alright, you had your fun. If you're so intersted in what we think, then it's only right that tell us about your self. listen... have you ever had that feeling were you want to recall a memory with all the answers but no mater what you do, you just can't?' Dark Isaac asked.

    Isaac was taken aback by Dark Isaac's question. Dark Isaac must be thinking about how he can't remember his origin.

    Regardless, Isaac thought hard about Dark Isaac's question and how it applies to him.

    He found himself thinking about one moment in his life that he can't seem to recall...

    He thought back to when he was ambushed by that snow-white incubus, a rotting ghoul, a tainted witch, and succubus who ganged up on him back when he was in the crypt. That was when he first discovered his ability to assimilate other creatures. But the way he did it was very unlike the method of using his enchanted equipment or dragging his victims into his chimeric body. That incident was before any of that, and he didn't had the means nor foresight to do any of that. Instead... he felt white hot rage... then he just blacked out. The next thing he knew, he somehow absorbed all four of his assaulters. But he can't seem to recall HOW he assimilated them. Did he drag them them into his body... or did something else happened?

    Dark Isaac, able to read Isaac's thoughts through their mysterious connection, decided to comment.

    'Perhaps it was your ring of purity that saved you? The ring was too small to channel a large amount of the deity of purity's power. Perhaps the deity temporarily used your body as a larger vessel to protect you?' Dark Isaac theorized. 'But take my crazy theories with a grain of salt. I don't even believe myself half the time.'

    Isaac took Dark Isaac's suggestion at face value. Perhaps... but if the deity of purity did possessed his body, then would it make more sense for the deity to just disintegrate the corrupted creatures? After all, Isaac doesn't think it's in the deity of purity's nature to absorb corrupt creatures... aak!

    Isaac grabbed his head in pain as a suppressed memory arisen from the deaths of his mind....

    'Hisssssss.'

    ...strange. That's all he could recall. But for some reason, Isaac knows for sure that memory came from when he was passed out. But were did that hissing sound came from?

    His thoughts were interrupted when he arrived at the front porch of the mansion. All the bags seem to be packed up but there seems to be no sign of Mary. However, the front door seems to be left ajar.

    That bad feeling Isaac had when the lightning storm returned didn't go away. Mary was against going into that mansion last night. Did Slar make her go in there?

    As a extra precaution, Isaac placed their bags and supplies in his body storage. Wouldn't want to leave their camp supplies behind. Especially the cloud nap sacks. The sleeping bags was one of the only things that kept Isaac from falling though the ground while he was sleeping...

    That actually gave him an interesting idea. If he's so afraid of absorbing living things, then maybe he can absorb Nonliving things? He did absorbed his cloud shoes by accident. Maybe he can absorb more cloud items? Perhaps he can even absorb his armor and metal halo too?

    But that can wait till later. He's got to get Mary out of that mansion first.

    "I'm coming Mary."

    Isaac held out the magic piece of paper that is Alicia's map. It's looks just like any old map from the material plane. Except that there was a couple of unnatural black dots of ink moving across the map representing Mary and this "Slar" character. Since both dots are both black, Isaac can't tell which dot is Mary an which one represents Slar. Good thing that their currently together. But why is this map only showing Isaac the first floor? That mansion looked more then one story tall.

    "Where's the second floor?" Inc said out load.

    To his surprise, the ink on his map shifted around until it was showing Isaac the mansion's second floor. It does't look like there is any moving dots on this floor... Though he does have to question why would one witch would need so many bedrooms...

    Interesting. So the map will show him whichever area he want's to see just by asking?

    "Show me the first floor."

    And jut as he commanded, the ink shifted around until it showed the layout of the first floor once again, compete with the two dots representing Slar and Mary.

    Just how much can he do with this map?

    Just on a whim, Isaac asked a question he didn't expect a answer for.

    "Show me the mansion's backyard." Isaac commanded.

    To Isaac's surprise, the map did indeed changed to show the landscape of the back yard. It appears to be some kind of grave yard. Interesting.

    But what are the rules to using this map? Can he ask for specifics? Can he asked to be shown secret passageways? Is the map sentient? Does it reflects the user's memories? Perhaps the map can show him some instructions to using it if he asked?

    But he wasted enough time already. While he was thinking to himself, Mary is out there wandering around Alicia's mansion. He can experiment with his equipment and inventory later.

    He commanded the map to show him the first floor again.

    Isaac entered the mansion with a face full of determination.

    "Yes. A long time ago. But I wasn't happy with my life. Some men became soldiers so their family can relish on the money they earn. But I had no one to support. It was just me and me alone. But what's the point of earning money when you spend your who life on duty, not knowing if tomorrow will be the day you die? I lived a empty life, were I could nothing but follow the missions given to me... and I wasn't happy with everything I done... I had no freedom, nor any free time to do what I really wanted. I wanted to be an writer and a adventurer. I just did my job and I was eventually promoted for my skill... but I didn't feel proud being such a high rank... I felt trapped. I wanted to quit, but plenty of soldiers who tried were killed on the spot... while others got out scot free... it was a coin flip. It depended on the mood of whoever was releasing you. I was a coward at the time... so I didn't risk it..." Slar explained.

The people constantly complained how ruthless and out of control the king's men are... how they are allowed to do whatever they wanted and no one would bat an eye... but that only applies to the lower ranking grunts. In reality, the higher ranking you are, the more restricted life becomes. Sure, you are provided with an upper class life style... but is it worth not being able to step away from what's expected of you? The royal family especially has it hard... or at least an unfortunate few...

The Boss became the first member and leader of the gang and I became a freelanced mercenary. My life as an solider sorta programed me into following missions. But now I'm also a free man! I can choose my own fate! I can choose what missions I want to take and I can write my adventure novels whenever I want! I can finally be what I dreamed of being! To be an adventurer and a writer!" Star proclaimed.

But even though I'll help the boss anyway I can, I'll do it more as an friend then as an underling. There are plenty times were I'll go on my own and work for anyone for the right price. I am a freelance mercenary after all!" Star proudly proclaimed. "But right now, I'm doing a few favors for the boss. To find a weapon to fight dragons... and something else... So I'll ask again. Are you that little girl I saw from the castle years ago?"

But then again, she doesn't who this "boss" is. Their leader might still be a potential ally depending on who that person is. But Mary doesn't like the idea of Slar knowing dangerous information.

Some of the items were of cloud plane origin and others were obviously not. The same can be said about the name tags. Some were of cloud touge and others were of a different language.
