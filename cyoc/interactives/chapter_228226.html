The sounds and smells are insane, my head is throbbing with all the information I’m receiving, I have a hard time to keep my instincts, or rather the instincts of my “wolf-side” under control’.

    As Dr. Huff continues down the path he suddenly stops when he smells something rather interesting, ‘this new smell…,’ he thinks to himself, ‘it smells really good and it kind of makes me feel… warm… excited and aroused. My “wolf-mind” tells me I am smelling a female in heat and my instincts are telling me to do what “nature” wants me to do: mate! BUT I am NOT about to degrade myself to a dumb animal, besides: I have a wife and kid at home! I am NOT about to ruin my life just because this body WANTS to listen to what the instincts tell it to. I AM still in control, thankfully, and that is going to stay that way!’

    Dr. Huff tries to ignore the scent of the female nearby and continues down the path, but the scent is really powerful and Dr. Huff finds himself becoming more and more curious and he can feel his instincts also increasing, causing the transformed human to strain to keep control. The former human stops again and turns his head in the direction of the scent, the “Wolf” lets out a growl, then it sighs, ‘I guess it won’t hurt to take a look at the female,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I can feel that I am in control. So, I doubt that I’ll pounce and mate the female once I spot her, my human-side is stronger than the animal-side! One quick peek, then I’ll head back to my truck,’ with that Dr. Huff turns and heads in the direction of the scent.

    What the former human is unaware of, is that the human and wolf parts of his mind have begun to merge together, this causes Dr. Huff to assume he, meaning his human-side, is in full control, unaware that his personality has become more animal than human.
