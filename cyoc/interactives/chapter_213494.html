    Ben Meets Noah

    As you drive to the address, you can't help but feel a mix of emotions. Excitement for the new chapter in your life, but also a bit of anxiety about living with a complete stranger. What if you don't get along? What if he's a neat freak and you're not? These thoughts race through your mind as you pull up to the house.

    You take a deep breath and step out of your car, making your way to the front door. You knock, and a moment later the door opens. A tall, lanky man stands before you, his eyes scanning you up and down. "You must be my new roommate," he says, a hint of a smile on his face. You introduce yourself and he leads you inside, showing you around the house. It's an older home, but it's well kept and cozy. He points out the room that will be yours, and you can't help but feel a sense of relief. It's perfect, just what you were looking for.

    Your new roommate, whose name is Noah, seems friendly enough. He shows you around the house, pointing out where everything is and telling you a bit about himself. He's a bit on the quiet side, but that's okay with you. You're not looking for a best friend, just a place to live but after you introduce yourself to Noah, you both hit it off pretty well. He tells you that he's a student athlete at the school and that he's pretty laid back. He also mentions that he's a heavy sleeper and that you shouldn't worry about making noise when you're home. You tell him that's great news, as you're not really a morning person and you like to stay up late sometimes.

    Noah tells you a little more about the house and You can tell that he's lived here for a while and he seems to have made the place feel like home. As you chat, you learn that Noah is a bit of a neat freak, but he tells you that he doesn't mind if you're not as tidy as he is. He just wants everyone to be comfortable.

    You head back to your room to finish unpacking. As you hang up your clothes and put your things away, you can't help but feel a sense of relief. You've found a great roommate in Noah and a great place to live. You're excited to start this new chapter of your life and you're looking forward to getting to know Noah better.

    You also think about how it's great to have a roommate that is an athlete, as you can learn from his routine, discipline and healthy lifestyle habits.

    With a smile on your face, you head back downstairs to join Noah in the living room and watch a movie together.
