    The evil invisible dragon is still alive

    Now the thing was the evil dragon who had created this chain of events in the castle wasn't quite dead.He was certainly critically
    injured and it remained to be seen if he was going to still
    survive the next 72 hours.

    However dragons are hard to kill and this evil dragon had managed to swing his neck quickly enough away when the
    prince >> dragoness had slashed at it with her new talons.
    So he had just lain there playing dead and observing through half closed eyes the interaction of what he had assumed
    to be a boring old human fart,the King,
    the newly minted dragoness and that annoying female ogre
    called the Queen.
    So when the boring old fart had shown moves that the evil
    dragon knew were moves he could only have learned at the
    Knights Savant Templar,the still living evil dragon had taken a
    serious interest in this old human geezer.

    For the Knights Savant Templar had NOT been a educational
    school of humans teaching other humans how to slay dragons
    BUT
    a sort of highly secretive Dragon Dirty Tricks Operation where
    dragons who had disguised themselves as human teachers
    teaching clueless human pupils how to damage,spindle,mutilate
    and yes occasionally slay dragons.The dragon politics involved
    had been pretty twisting and as far as the evil dragon recalled the human pupils had their studies 'terminated' under the highly
    secret orders of the Dragon High King three decades earlier.
    So it was a major surprise to the evil dragon as he lay invisible
    in the castles throne room to find that somehow the dragon
    assassins who had been sent out in both dragon and human form to kill off the human pupils had somehow missed one
    human pupil,this old human King who had just used a dragon invisibility spell on him.

    So the evil dragon lay there,invisible and very carefully used the
    equally invisible sword the human king had dropped nearby to
    do a little invisible first aid surgery on his wound.
    Still in mortal danger but at least with a hope,the invisible evil
    dragon considered his options.....
