    So now I was on my way home with this wish-granting ring. Funny, I thought to myself. I really couldn’t think of any wishes I wanted granted. I was finally accepting of myself, I was in love with a wonderful man, I had a good job and lived in a great house. What more could I want? I wondered.

That meant Craig would only be in one place in the house. While our mansion was big, we only needed a few people to work around the house. We had a butler and a maid who worked full-time. We also had a chef and a gardener who worked part-time. The butler and the maid both had the day off, so I could be a little reckless.

    There was one special part of the house that was off limits to everyone but Craig, me and a few select people. None of our employees were allowed there. In fact, they didn’t even know about it, since it was secret. That was where I was headed. Our garage opened into our laundry room. I took off my shoes and walked out of the laundry room into the kitchen. As I walked through the kitchen I took off my shirt and dropped it on the floor. There was also a shower, a Jacuzzi and what we jokingly called a party room. There was one concrete rule for entering this secret area: clothes were forbidden. Craig and I realized that we both enjoyed being free of clothes when we could. We couldn’t actually live as nudists, so once I moved in, we made this part of the house our nudist zone.     “Oh, yea?” he asked. His voice was disinterested since he was focused on his workout. He didn’t laugh at me or say he didn’t believe me, however.

    “Yea, I saw the newswoman on the street and I wished for her clothes to disappear. They did, she screamed, I wished them back on and then wished for her to forget it. All of that happened!” I explained.

    Craig stopped his workout. He looked at me and asked, “You’re not kidding?”

    “Nope, not at all,” I said. “In fact, I know how to prove it!”
