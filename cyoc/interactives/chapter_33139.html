    Anna clicked the option to make Hannah their sister.

    Hannah lost about half an inch in height and her breasts grew from b cups to c cups. Her eyes turned from brown to blue. Her hair changed from black to blond and went from shoulder length to waist length. Her nose shrunk just a bit.

    "Ok this is weird" Sarah said. "I now have memories of Hannah being my twin sister as well as my best friend."

    "Just focus on me as your twin sister" Hannah said. "That is the reality we are in now."

    "I have a question" Anna said. "You said that Jenny changed her mother into her sister. Since I was her mother in one reality, does that mean that I'll have a girl when I'm older?"

    "You will have a girl when you're older" Hannah said. "You may have another child before then though."

    Just then their mother walks into the room.

    "Why are you all gathered in here?" Diana asked.

    "Nothing Mom" Sarah said.

    "You're doing something in here" Diana said. At this point she saw the computer screen. "Where did you get that game?" she asked.

    No one said anything for a bit.

    "Well I'm waiting for an answer" Diana said.

    "It just showed up on the computer" Anna said.

    "I don't want any of you to play that game" Diana said.

    "Why?" Tim asked.

    "Because I said so" Diana said. Seeing the defiant looks on their faces she added "You don't know what that game does."

    "We do know what it does" Hannah said.

    "You've already played haven't you?" Diana asked.

    "We have" Jenny said.

    "What did you change?" Diana asked with a sigh.

    "You should know Mom" Hannah said, "your awareness is still on."

    "How did you know that?" Diana asked.

    "That's the way it was when the game disappeared from the computer 16 years ago" Hannah said.

    "Who are you?" Diana asked. "I have memories of you being my daughter but also memories of you being Sarah's best friend."

    "Alright I'll tell you" Hannah said. "I am your daughter in this reality. Before I was Sarah's best friend in another reality. Before that I was a 34 year old man. You see I work for an organization that monitors reality for changes. They placed me here as Sarah's best friend two days ago. They decided that I should become your daughter for some time. I don't know how long it will be but I'm going to
    guess indefinitely."

    "I knew you weren't my daughter" Diana said.

    "Do you remember giving birth to me?" Hannah asked.

    "Yes I do" Diana said.

    "Then I am your daughter" Hannah said. "What matters is the reality we are in now."

    "You're just trying to confuse me" Diana said.

    "I'm not trying to confuse you" Hannah said, "I'm trying to help you through your confusion. You see with the awareness on you remember a reality before you make changes. You also gain memories of a reality after you make changes. This can lead to confusion and insanity if you don't focus on the reality you are in. I've been through that and it's not pretty."

    "You've gone insane?" Diana asked with fear in her voice.

    "Yes I did" Hannah said. "It took the psychologist about a year and a half to get me sane again."

    "And this was because you tried to focus on all the realities?" Diana asked.

    "In my case there was another factor" Hannah said, "but I've seen others go insane because they didn't focus on their current reality."

    "Ok I think I understand" Diana said. "Now I'm curious. What was the other factor you mentioned?"

    "My bosses seem to have a twisted sense of humor" Hannah said. "I joined 34 years ago when I was a 34 year old man. When they sent me out they would always sent me as a female but would return me to a 34 year old man afterwords. I've always felt more comfortable being female and going back to being a man was difficult. After I went insane they decided to leave me in the field almost all the time as a female. They've kept me moving from one place to another and I never
    stayed as one person for more than a few days."

    "That sounds terrible" Diana said. "So how long will you be my daughter again?"

    "I don't know" Hannah said. "They had always told me how long I would be a person before. This time though they haven't told me."

    'That must be such an awful life' Anna thought. 'To be changing from one person to another and keeping the memories, it's amazing that she's sane.'

    "That life must be hard on you" Sarah said.

    "It is" Hannah said, "though it's not as bad as you might think. Before I went insane I had been 12 different girls in 31 years. At that time they left me as one person for a couple of years. After that they kept me moving from being one person to another. They've been doing that for a year now. They must have detected something during my last checkup. That was just before this assignment. They may have decided to leave me here to give me a little break."

    "That's nice of them" Sarah said.

    "I think that they just don't want to go through me going insane again" Hannah said.

    "I wouldn't want to have anyone working for me going insane" Diana said.

    There was a beep from Hannah's phone. She opened it and read what she had been sent.

    "What is it?" Sarah asked when Hannah had closed her phone.

    "It seems that I can use the game now too" Hannah said. "Also they said that I'll be here for at least a hundred years."

    "A hundred years!" Diana said. "How are you supposed to live that long?"

    "I'll be able to regress my age as need be" Hannah said.

    "Alright then" Diana said, "it's time for supper. I want you all to get ready and be down at the dinner table in 20 minutes."
