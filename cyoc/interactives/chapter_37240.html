    I got out of bed and checked myself in the mirror. I still had a young girly body and I was now wearing a pink skirt down to my knees and white tights and a pink Hello Kitty t-shirt. I managed to hold back any mind slips as I went downstairs to find out what was for dinner.

    "Here you go Ashley" my Mom said, handing me a Happy Meal, even worse, it was a girl's Happy Meal. I struggled to keep my mind from slipping as I quietly ate my dinner. After I had finished, I began to head for my room when my mom called to me.

    "Ashley, you forgot your toy!" she said handing me a 'My Little Pony' figure. It was too much, as I eagerly took the toy and ran to my room to play.

    After about half an hour of playing happily with my pony, I managed to regain control of myself and decided to talk to Katherine about my situation. I knocked on her door and entered after she replied: "Come in!".

    After closing the door, I turned around and was faced with the sheer feminicity of Katherine's room. I felt my mind beginning to slip again.

    "Katherine, ever since you began playing with my medallion, strange things have been happening to me" I explained.

    "Really, I haven't noticed anything strange!" she replied. The sight of her dress as well as her room was making the feeling stronger and was beginning to overpower me.

    "I think that...I'm becoming...more like...becoming more...more...like..." I tailed off in a dream-like state.

    "More like what?" Katherine asked, a slightly puzzled look on her face
