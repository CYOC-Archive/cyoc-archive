    When the clock struck 8, Jake jumped up off of the bed. He had made it. He resisted temptation, and had refrained from having sex with Gina. And now he could switch back.

    "Okay, it's time. We can switch back now. Where is the medallion? " said Jake.

    Gina sighed and sat up on the bed. She was disappointed that she hadn't gotten a chance to really enjoy Jake's body, but she thought that perhaps he might change his mind later. After all, tomorrow was Saturday. They would have all day to play around with amulet.


    "I left it over there, on top of the dresser. Away from any clothes to avoid any more accidents, " said Gina.

    "Good thinking, " said Jake, as he picked it up and held it in his hands. "C'mon, let's get this over with." He beckoned Gina to come over, when they heard a loud knock on the door.

    "Shit, my parents must be home, " said Gina, as she jumped up and ran into the closet to hide.

    "What are you doing? "said Jake angrily. "Just get over here and touch me, then it won't matter anymore."

    "No way, " said Gina. "Last time it took like 30 minutes to change. What would my parents think if they saw that? Besides, if they find a boy in my room, they will kill me. Just open the door, see what they want, and send them on their way. We can do this after they leave."

    Gina ducked back into her closet, leaving the door open just a crack so she could see out. Jake sighed, and opened the door. He was still used to being short, so he expected to look up and see Gina's parents. He was surprised when he opened the door and saw no one. Then he looked down and saw a short girl with dark black hair staring up at him. He had no idea who this person was, and the young girl could tell.

    Gina stepped out of the closet, and cleared up the confusion. "Oh, it's just my stupid little sister. What the hell do you want, shrimp?" said Gina, glaring at Brin.

    Brin was used to the abuse. She and Gina did not get along. Gina liked to pick on her, and make fun of her short stature. It had led to some pretty intense but one-sided brawls. But over the years, Brin had learned that even the biggest opponents have a weakness.

    Brin slammed the heel of her foot as hard as she could on Jake's big toe, placing all her tiny weight on it. Jake howled in pain, and dropped the amulet. Brin scooped it up, and tried to run away. But Jake caught her by the shoulder, and spun her around. They both grabbed the amulet, grappling for control. Jake felt a familiar shock, and let go of the medallion. Brin felt it, too, but kept holding on to it. She fell flat on her ass when Jake let go. They stared at each other for a while, then Brin jumped up and ran away, cradling the medallion in her small hands.

    "That little bitch!" said Gina, as she ran up to the door. "She took the medallion. He have to catch the runt."

    She ran past Jake and into the hallway. She looked around the hallway, but could find no sign of the little rat. "C'mon, we have to hurry."

    "Um Gina, I think we have a problem, " said Jake with a shaky voice.

    Gina turned back to him, angry at the delay. She was shocked to see that Jake looked smaller, and his clothes seemed baggy.

    "I don't think she is going to be that small for much longer, " said Jake with fear.
