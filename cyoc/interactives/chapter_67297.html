    It was not the pen that gave Elizabeth her new found power, but it did come from the wizard. While Dave was in
    his room using the accidentally stolen pen, the magical traveling salesman made his rounds throughout the
    office proper, and Elizabeth had bought an indulgence from him. An indulgence is a temporary waiver granting
    whoever buys it the ability to warp reality to whatever desires they like, and it expires after twenty-four
    hours. If the buyer manages to clean up their mess and restore reality to it's normal state before the
    indulgence expires, everything is all right. If they aren't capable of doing so, or if they abuse their power
    somehow, or misuse it, they are then put under probation by the Magical Ruling Body.

    After Elizabeth had purchased the indulgence(she was the only one to do so), she of course got a good natured
    ribbing from her co-workers for being so naive. Some of them pushed the joke a little too far, but no real
    harm was meant by it. Now though, after she had tested and confirmed that her powers over reality were in fact
    real, she wanted to push it as far as she could and see if she could live out her fantasy...

    "OK. Dana. Sandy. We're going to turn all the men in this office into hot lesbians. Won't that be fun!?"

    "It sure is, honey!" squealed the curvaceous redhead Dana with delight.

    Inside, Dave's personality was simultaneously screaming to be "let out" of his cage and also sexually aroused
    by the possibility of hooking up with Elizabeth. He didn't mind being a woman(not that he would have preferred
    it over having his penis) as long as he got to be with her. He just wanted to be able to speak his mind, and
    somehow, he knew that he was currently under the mind control of Elizabeth. Besides, he really needed to get
    back that pen.

    "I don't mind. Seeing some women in charge besides Jasmine should be interesting for a change" answered Sandy.

    "OK, good. Sandy, touch my hand."

    She did so.

    "It feels warm."

    "That it does, milady. You now have a fraction of my indulgence."

    "What does that mean?" asked Sandy, roughly pulling her hand away.

    "It means that you're going to help me pay it forward. You can now change reality, and I order you to change
    Britt Reid in the corner office into a lesbian woman. You too Dana. Grab my hand."

    On instinct, Dana grabbed her girlfriend's hand, which indeed felt warm, and that warmness soon spread
    throughout Dana's body. Her memories also changed. While Dave still recalled his male life, he also now had a
    female version of that life where he- or rather "Dana"-, was now Elizabeth's submissive little girlfriend.
    They were the office lesbians, and every guy masturbated hard at home at the image of Elizabeth and Dana being
    naked in bed and getting it on.

    "Dana, you're going to change James Kirk on the next floor into a lesbian woman. Meanwhile, I'll be changing
    our common enemy Al Sherman into a woman as well. When the two of you are done, meet me back at my cubicle."

    So then the three hot women(two of whom were actually men) went off in their different directions to make the
    office into a lesbian utopia.
