    As Chad walked back to his locker from the shower room, he focused on toweling his head dry. When he removed the towel from his face, he was startled to see the new Coach staring at him in his coaching uniform standing next to Chad's locker.

    "Hey, do you mind you are blocking my locker," Chad said.

    "So Sinclair and Bruno insist you should be on the swim team. Me, I want you to tryout first. So turnaround and head to the pool."

    "Oh, you will be on the swim team one way or the other. You will wear a Speedo if your tryout goes well, or you will be a Speedo on one of the swim team. Maybe Todd or Bruno?"
