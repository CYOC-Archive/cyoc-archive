    There are a couple of days at the edge of each month where the old sign is weak enough and the new sign just gaining enough strength that both signs influence the person born on those days. These were relatively rare but far from unheard of, and live between the domains of the main signs. That rarity was before Earth gradually shifted out of the alignment with the stars that originally determined the zodiac signs. Some people say the signs shifted with the stars rather than the dates; others say the seasons matter more so the dates still determine the sign. Both are right, but it depends on the individual. Some naturally gravitate more toward the sign of the stars above them, others toward the sign of their season, and some are equally influenced by both. Thus the presence and prevalence of cusp individuals has steadily risen.

    Aries+Taurus=Tarius (L~M~H) [f2M] Diurnal, Trade: Wool and Milk

They stop ageing in their mid-teens to early 20s until age 50, at which point they turn male and their wool turns white and they resume normal aging, still not all live to 50 so there is a hi female to male ratio. Every Tarius under 50 is a female and, despite being humanoid, aren’t yet capable of human speech or complex thought and are treated as livestock even by their own mates. However, they are especially precious livestock, too precious to let anyone eat, because they will develop higher brain functions with age and by 50 will become fully intelligent males with only fuzzy memory of their first 40 years of life, and yet all language and concepts they were exposed to absorbed into useful knowledge.
    The Tarius tribe are nomadic hunter gatherers who mostly set up camp near the roads between cities, traveling and living in relatively modern vehicular homes (this can vary depending who they trade will, Tarius who trade with low-techs will be a bit more old school, maybe just a step up from covered wagons, and futuristic if they trade with hi-techs). The Tarius females are bred as dairy cow wool sheep; the males need to ingest their milk daily to survive and tend to suffer up to full body baldness around age 90 and so need the wool to keep warm. The resulting society is something like a friendly, fiercely protective biker gang of strong elders (remember that at 50 they still have the strength of a 20 year old, so they can live strong for longer than humans, and have a lot of muscle by default) with a lot of happily ditsy girlfriends.
    Wild Tarius tend to stay away from big cities and any lost and found Tarius are kept until mature, returned to their elders if they can be positively identified. Visitors form earth will find they de-aged, those over 50 will become a 30 to 50 year younger male and those 20 or younger will find themselves a female the same age (maybe a year or two younger). Visitors between 20 and 50 will become a female between 20 and 1 year old at complete random.
