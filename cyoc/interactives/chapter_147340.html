It's a bit dingy, but can handle yourself in a street fight. You think. You've never been in one before but you keep yourself fit with weekly exercise. At the very least, you're confident in your running speed.

    "Hey, kid!"

    An old, gravelly voice catches your ear and you stop to note it's owner. An old man, dressed like a ragged hermit, emerges from a dark corner away from what sunset rays manage to enter this closed up path. You reckon he's a real living fossil from the grey of his hair and how wrinkled he is.

    With a tired smile, the old man speaks to you.

    "Do... do you have any food?"

    Well... there is the azuki bun you bought bur forgot to eat. Returning him a smile, you take it and hold it out to him. He takes it happily from your hand.

    "Thank you so much kiddo."

    He hastely opens the packaging and stuffs it in his mouth. Seeing as you've done a good deed, you make to be on your way, but he holds a hand up to stop you.

    "Actually, I've got something for you too."

    He moves closer and places something in your palm. You raise it to your eye and examine it.

    In your hand is a exquisite pendent, a sky blue gem as its centerpiece. You don't know what to say.

    "This... Can I really have this?"

    The old man smiles a toothy yellow grin.

    "Why yes indeed kiddo."

    "That which you are holding is magic. It'll give you powers you've never seen! Just wear it and grip the gem tightly, then think about anybody you wanna see."

    As he walks behind you, he pats your shoulder.

    "Have some fun with it, huh?"

    "What do you mea..."

    You trail off. The old man is gone, as if he'd just vanished in the wind. Shocked, you look all around, but he is truly gone.

    You hold the pendent up. If what that man said is true, then you should probably test it out. It would be easy anyway.
