As they spent more time as merfolk one of the things which fascinated him was that while all the food fell into 5 basic types, fish, crustaceans, sea algae, seaweed and corals everything tasted different. There was bread made from seaweed which tasted a lot like wheat bread, there was a type of fish which tasted like chicken and aged sea algae which tasted like cheese. That came to mind now as he ate a grilled fish and cheese sandwich however the fish tasted like chicken.

It would be worse for her mother and truth be told herself as well, she was best friend to a future Queen and her mother may fall into the role of mother of the Queen.

She and Rose had run into each other at the jewelry store, Rose was helping Sam pick a ring for her and she was helping Nate/Cain pick a ring out for Rose. Both men where hopeless when it came to jewelry so had entrusted their girlfriend’s best friend to aid them, they had not expected to run into each other, but it worked out in the end. “Nate told me what happened, Rose admitted it to him.”

Taylor was confused until she turned her head and saw her mother coming toward her with the help of that Doctor. She had to smile as she noticed how close her mother was hanging on to the Doctor’s arm, they did make a good-looking couple. Her mother had been in a funk after losing her husband 5 years ago but in the past year or so she had started dating again but while some of the guys seamed nice none lasted more than 3 dates. Then she noticed her mother let go of the doctor’s arm and she went a little wild on swimming. She wasn’t used to swimming with only a single limb. Still she made it over to them, she looked younger and had a few bronze, gold and brass color scales while her eyes had shifted to a golden color.

    “Well, you aren’t going to end up with a yellow tail.” joked Taylor as her mom sat beside her, her mom looked younger as well. In the back of her mind, she thought bronze and silver went very well together as the Doctor helped her sit down.

    “I must admit I am happy about that.” Said Karen as she looked up at the Doctor. “Thank you very much.”

    “You are welcomed, I would join you for a meal, but I must get back to my rounds.” Said the Doctor as he nodded his head and swam away.
