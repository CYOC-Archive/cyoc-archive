    He took a long drink of the youthenizer intending to regress all the way back to when he was a toddler. After a horrible night like tonight Jason wanted to forget all the troubles of being an adult and return to a sweeter and much simpler time in his life.

    It tasted alright though it was a little too sweet in his opinion and he could detect the faint taste of cinnamon. He set the bottle down on the counter and licked his lips.

    "So how was it?" Tina asked as she came back with a couple more bottles, setting them down on the counter.

    "Not bad actually" Jason then glanced around as saw that everyone in the bar was looking at him expectantly.

    "Oh don't mind them" she told him, "It's your first drink from the bar and they just want to see your reaction. Not many people who order the youhenizer drank so much so their just curious as how young you'll get."

    'Boy are they in for a surprise' he thought.

    Jason looked down at the mirror counter top in astonishment as his reflection began to change. His face became less mature as the years went away until he looked like he did in high school. His clothes started to feel loose as he began losing height. The scruff on his chin quickly vanished and he couldn't help but smile as he watched his body become younger much to the delight of his audience.

    Tina then glanced at the nearly empty bottle of youthenizer and let out a sigh, "I told you not to drink so much. Now you'll most likely end up a toddler."

    "That's what I want" he said while he was still able to talk.

    "Oh well then enjoy being a baby, and don't worry I'll take care of you when you finish regressing" Tina replied.

    "Thank you"

    Jason then went back to watching his regression and saw that he had lost a lot more height.and his clothes were much to big for him. He now looked like a young kid on the cusp of puberty, a tween to be more precise. His brown hair seemed a bit longer and stuck out in odd directions, and he looked as though he was wearing his older brother's clothes. However the changes didn't seem to slow as he left behind his teens and re-entered his childhood becoming ten years old. He lost even more height and his hair became short again, his legs no longer touched the floor and dangled in the air. His adult teeth started to vanish as they were replaced with his baby teeth until they were the only teeth in his mouth.

    "Wow, do you see how young he's getting?" he overheard one of the patrons say.

    "Yeah he really going all the way" the guys friend replied.

    As he became five Jason lost quite a few of his baby teeth leaving a noticeable gap between his two front teeth. By this time the people in the bar had started placing bets on how young he was going to get. This was made aware to him by the fact that some people were chanting "Baby!" and the other were chanting "Toddler".

    He felt the changes start to slow down as he lost two more years before his regression came to a stop, leaving him a naked three year old toddler sitting on top of a pile of adult sized clothes on top of a bar stool. Seeing it was over half the bar let out disappointed groans as they paid the winners of the bet. Meanwhile Tina picked Jason up and laid him down before reaching under the counter and pulling out a disposable diaper. A minute later he was dressed in nothing but a Pamper and had a pacifier in his mouth sitting on the bar stool as Tina put away his now oversized clothes.

    "I'll hold onto these for now. You sit there there and be good while call my friend to come get you" Tina said before disappearing into the back room.

    When she came back five minutes later Jason was being passed around by the bar's patrons. They complemented him on what a cute baby he was and some even pinched his cheeks or patted the back of his diaper. A few even pulled back his diaper to see if he needed changing which made him blush. While he enjoyed being the center of attention Jason was glad when Tina picked him up and returned him to the bar stool.

    "Sorry about that, sometimes the people here can get over excited about something as simple as a regression" she said.

    Suddenly he heard the door bell ding...
