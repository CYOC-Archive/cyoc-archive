    Taking A Gamble With A Genie

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover A year ago AR TG O Magic Mental Male to female

    "Well," the genie said. "You would become an infant."
    "Really? Why?" asked Kate, confused.
    "When I was imprisoned, ages and ages ago, my jailer decreeded that my form could only be beheld by the innocent, like children. In the case of my current master and owner of the lamp, it would be an infant, so as to reenforce the 'innocence' part."
    "That's a bit weird," mumbled Kate.
    "Yeah, well, the imprisonners of genies enjoy making silly rules, trust me on that. As a result, well, all of my masters decided to speak to me via the lamp and get their wishes without seeing me. Logical. Except one who ended up in diapers. There's a rumor that someone really pure of heart as an adult could see me without issues but frankly, I don't believe so. It's never happened, at any rate."
    "I see," muttered Kate. "So... What do you look like?"
    "I'm a being from a higher plane of existence than humans. As such, I am beautiful and no one from your mortal realm could take in all of my handsome nature, except those that cannot grasp the implications of seeing such beauty in your imperfect world, hence the innocent. I am too beautiful for human eyes, to sum up things."
    "Really? Like a male supermodel?" asked Kate, her curiosity piqued.
    "Indeed. But incredibly more beautiful."
    "Oooh! Now I want to see you!" clapped Kate, her brain thinking about handsome boys to whom she was now attracted.
    And yes, she was very curious too, as she had been as a boy.
    "No, frankly, It's not a good idea, not at all and..."
    "I order you! Now!" snapped Kate. "I'm a good person. I'm sure I am pure of heart anyway!"
    The genie sighed, hoping things would work out. He magicked himself out of the lamp and showed himself. Kate was mesmerized. It was perfection made male indeed. His longish hair was the night and stars combined. His eyes the sun. His skin devoid of any imperfections, even the slightest. It was... It was... Suddenly, she felt herself change. The world became bigger. Her boobs started to retreat into her chest. She was shrinking! No! She was getting younger! She had been wrong! She was not pure of heart! She was going to become a baby again! No! She'd be a helpless baby girl! What could be worse!?!?
    "Quick! Make your two last wishes now! Before you become too young to speak!" pressed the genie, trying to help the foolish human at any cost.
    "I... I wish that my family be the richest on the planet! And the most loving! And... And... And I want to be impossibly beautiful, the most beautiful, because eternally young and never ! And I want my parents to have the boy that want again!" exclaimed Kate in an increasingly childish voice, thinking fast and thus not very much.
    "As you wish," said the genie gravely.
    He let the magic loose and after a flash of pure magic's work, the little baby that Kate was gone. The genie was a bit startled but quickly understood what had occurred. The wishes had been so hastily said that they became beyond the human realm. As such, Kate had been propelled to another realm, a fantasy realm of magic, one reminiscent of fairy tales, in order for her wishes to be granted properly. The genie was left, with a lamp yet to be discovered by someone else. He had warned the lad turned into a girl, as much as he could after all. And because the genie possessed a kind heart in spite of everything, he hoped Kate would enjoy her new life in a realm of magic.
