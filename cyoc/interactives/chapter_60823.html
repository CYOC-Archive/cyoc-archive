    Janet put the magical pen to paper and started writing:

    "I wish that humans lived in communities similar, in a way, to those of bee hives. I wish that only one in every 10,000 of people was male and therefore males would become the 'Kings' just as there's the Queen in every bee hive. Each city would be a collection of females of different roles and one King. Even though all the females would have free will and their own free minds, the King's pheromones would make them loose control over their bodies whenever he wanted making them extremely submissive towards him, to the extend that they even could commit suicide if he wished so. The females usually neither enjoy it nor fell comfortable with it, nevertheless they never try to fight their own King's 'call' as they instinctively know that he is too valuable for the survival of the species to not be respected."

    Dave thought about all those hordes of women just for him. He smiled and kept looking at Janet's dainty hand writing, looking forward to see what else she can come up with

    "I wish there was four types of females in any city-hive:

    *The Workers - doing all the hard and dirty work (e.g. ironworks, cleaning severs, farming, building). An average worker would be 5'10'' tall and have a body of a strength similar to this of an average man nowadays.

    *The Warriors - doing all the fighting (e.g. police, King's guard or military in an unlikely event of hive wars). An average worker would be 7'2'' tall and have a body of an extremely muscled Amazon.

    *The Thinkers - doing all the intellectual work (e.g. teaching, art, science research). An average thinker would be only 5 feet tall and have a very tiny and fragile build. Each one of them, however, would be a savant in hers field allowing the hive to develop technologically and culturally.

    *The Multipliers - whose only purpose is to give birth to children and nurture them before they're passed on to The Thinkers at the age of 2. Each multiplier would be an exaggeration of female sexuality, in the most stereotypical way possible, so that they can serve their role effortlessly. Each time a multiplier gives birth to a new member of a hive, she doesn't suffer any loss in her looks; often the opposite happens. Also because of the high levels of female hormones The Multipliers are weak mentally and therefore submissive not only to the King but also to other females and to each other."

    "I wish that although all the females could get pregnant, The Multipliers have developed to become impregnated every single time any amount of semen is present in their vaginas. Also in order to keep the population's numbers stable, The Multipliers' pregnancy became much shorter, lasting only four days. A side effect of such a high fertility rate is the lack of menstruation cycle (they are always fertile) and constant breast lactation."

    'It's getting better and better!' - thought Dave.

    "I wish that the King's testicles were the size of tennis balls, so that he won't ever run out of semen. Also Kings are now to be capable of starting a new intercourse within one minute after they ejaculated."

    Janet hesitated for a moment, then erased all that Dave wrote previously leaving only her entry and continued writing:

    "I wish that although people would remember who they were before the Hive World, they still wouldn't be able to act in a way other than a life-long member of a hive would. Finally, for me to not enjoy this thing as much as I can I wish me, Janet Smith, to become the King for this city."

    At first Dave didn't fully get what that meant. Then when he suddenly realized the fact that what Janet wrote meant he's going to become one of the members of her female court he panicked. He tried to take the pen away from her and change something but it was too late. He felt tinging spreading through his body as the world started to slowly change...
