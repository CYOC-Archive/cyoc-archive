    I got up out of bed and noticed my roommate Brian reading a news article on his laptop.

    "Hey Brian."

    Hey turned around with this really weird look on his face, like he was trying not to laugh out loud or smirk

    "I have a headache for the first time..well ever" says Ben.

    "Sorry buddy. Ben, you missed something absolutely incredible. There was this hot girl here at the
    school...just look."

    He hands me his computer, and I read the article. Apparently there was a chemical fire caused by misuse of
    ammonium nitrate in one of the research labs not too far from here earlier yesterday. Some idiot trying to

    out the fire, a woman flying at around 60 MPH crashed into the top of the building.

    Oh my god thought Ben. Was the dream real? Was this me?

    Ben clicked on the video which accompanied the article.

    The video of the superheroine showed her jumping out of the building with the student in her arms after she

    video and pausing on her standing before the firefighters, Ben appraised her looks. She was had long golden
    blonde hair was very tall, around 6'3. A brief glimpse of her face showed piercing eyes and a gorgeous,
    flawless face. Her breasts were huge without looking fake on her amazonian body, and her clothing consisted of
    brass chest armor protecting a simple pink mid sleeve shirt, a pair of long pink and white striped tights that
    showed off her curvy ass, a belt with a sheath carrying a blade of some kind, and tan thigh high boots. And a
    gold tiara.

    Brian saw Ben's reaction and said "Pretty cool, huh?"
