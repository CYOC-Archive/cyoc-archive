    Acting as natural as she can

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago AR Toddler

    Hearing this, Selina knew that she couldn't be hiding here for so long. But she knew that for now she couldn't do anything, much less try to escape. If at this time she tried to do something very different from the rest of the children in the daycare, the workers would become suspicious of her and she would eventually be found out. So for now, her best option was to try to act as natural as possible.

    Eventually, some workers proceeded to feed all the toddlers and babies in the high chairs. And the person who was in charge of feeding Selina was Reina. Reina proceeded to remove Selina's pacifier and strapped a bib around her neck. After that she set a rubber spoon and jar of baby food in front of her.

    “Here comes the airplane!” Reina coos as she attempts to spoonfeed her. Selina doesn't want to deal with the humiliation of being spoonfed baby food, but she's very hungry, so she reluctantly eats what she is given. “Good girl!”

    To Selina's surprise, she actually did enjoy the taste. Soon, the jar was empty and Selina's tummy was full. After finishing the jar of mush, Reina takes a towel and gently wipes up the mess on Selina. Once Selina is clean, she is then fed a bottle of warm baby milk. Selina happily drinks her milk and when the bottle is empty, Selina is patted on the back and lets out some little burps.

    After Selina had already been fed, Reina took Selina out of the high chair and proceeded to take her to the playroom that she had previously seen. Right now, the older children were no longer occupying the room so now all the toddlers and babies could play in the main area.

    Soon, Reina proceeded to put Selina in the main area with the rest of the toddlers and babies. But before she left, Reina put her pacifier back in Selina's mouth.

    "Now be a good girl and have fun," Reina cooed.

    Soon Reina left the room and Selina began to look around the playroom. Right now there was no employee watching nearby so she could take advantage of this to escape.

    But Selina couldn't help but feel like a little desire to play when she saw all the toys and activities in the room. Maybe a little distraction wouldn't hurt her.
