    Just a Little Older...

    Katie had had enough with waiting. She needed to convince her sister to go to the mall. She quickly wished herself to be 12. Katie shot up another inch stopping at 5' 2". Two small mounds appeared on her chest, and her hair, before in a pony tail, was let loose and was down to mid back. She stayed relatively skinny, but slightly toned as well.

    Kellie looked at her 12-year old sister again. She knew that she should wait for their mom to be home before going to the mall with Katie, but her sister was about to rip out of clothes that was two years old. She needed help, and fast.

    "Okay, fine. Let's go to the mall sis. But we have to be careful what we buy."

    "Okay sis. That's cool." Although her mind was not twelve, she did have some twelve year old instincts.

    Katie was glad to finally be going to the mall. However, she ran into a similar problem as yesterday.
    At the department store, Kellie took Katie directly to the pre-teen department. They began looking through clothes, but Katie started to feel like some of Kellie's choices were simply too childish for her.

    "Come on sis, I don't wanna wear that," Katie said about one outfit in particular. "That outfit looks lame." She spied an outfit on a nearby mannequin. "I wanna wear something like that!"

    "No Katie," Kellie said. "That outfit is much too flashy for you to wear at this age. I told you before, we have to pick out something mom will like."

    "Well I'm going to try it on," Katie said, determined.

    "Fine, Katie you can try it on," Kellie said. "But that doesn't mean you can get it!"

    Katie bounded off to the dressing room. She was soon back wearing the outfit she desired so much.

    "Well sis how do I look?" she asked.

    "Well ..." Kellie said. "I don't know."

    "I wish I was 2 years older," Katie thought, hoping that becoming even closer in age would help. "I have to be careful not to get too much older, I don't want to loose my whole childhood!"

    In seconds, Katie was a 14-year-old, 5' 4" tall and skinny with lanky legs and arms, as well as A almost B cup breasts. Her figure was also flaring out as she took puberty head on.

    "Wow Kat, you do look good," Kellie said to her sister. "I guess you're right. This seems appropriate enough for a 14 year old. But if we are going to get you that outfit, we should go up a few sizes.That looks like it would have fit you better two years ago though."

    Katie slyly smiled to herself. She may have sacrificed four years of her life, but look at her now.
