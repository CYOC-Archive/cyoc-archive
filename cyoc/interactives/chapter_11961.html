A chuckle came out of Jeff as he felt appeased by these men becoming hogs. He remembered an old painting he'd seen in the Chicago Museum of Art last summer.     "Dad, I'm sorry! I was granted this terrible cursed power by a demon named Zepar who is trapped in the museum part of that barn. I never meant for you or the Sheriff to become Hogs, really!" said Jeff.

    "My power works only one way and I might be able to bargin with the Demon to change you back, I hope!" said Jeff as his own thoughts about the chances sounded unlikely to all.
