    Welcome to the Fortune Teller:

    This room is welcome to all users.

    — — —

    This room is a single user experience.

    Once joined, the user will be greeted by their Fortune Teller:

    ‘MADAME TRANDAFIR’

    — — —

    Once entered, the user will be asked to consent by pressing:

    CONSENT

    Once completed, the user must confirm their name. Once confirmed, the user may officially speak to Madame Trandafir.

    She is an old, wise looking woman. Older than any relative that you still know, but incredibly attractive for her age. She greets you with a smile, and you communicates via text.

    ‘Would you like me to read your fortune?’

    She may ask.
    If the user replies with a ‘yes’, they will have their fortune read, and it will most likely be positive. If the user replies with a ‘no’, they will not have their fortune read.
    If a reply is not satisfactory, such as a repeated ‘yes’ ‘no’ cycle, Madame Trandafir will get annoyed. If so, she may decide to read the users fortune anyway… and its very unlikely to be positive.

    Before a fortune is read, Madame Trandafir will ask how long the user would like the their fortune to last. There is no limit to her fortune-telling power, so there is limit to how long. However, the longer the asking time, the higher the risk.

    Once confirmed, Madame Trandafir will finally reveal the users fortune.

    The fortune may be small, simple, or easy. The fortune may be big, sophisticated, or difficult. There is no telling to how intense a fortune may be, but once it has been read, there is no turning back.

    Madame Trandafir may congratulate the user on their fortune. She may apologise for the fortune. But she will never, ever, back down from a fortune, as she is never wrong.

    She will bid the user a farewell, and may send good luck their way. Then she will simply walk back into her tent until the next time the user may choose to visit her.

    — — —

    Once a fortune is read, and Madame Trandafir has said her piece and returned to her tent, the user will be exited from the session.

    Depending on the length of time the user chose, or the intensity of the fortune, the user will find themselves altering.
    It will take place over the course of either a minute, an hour, or even a day. The slow transformation will never last longer than a day.

    Once the user has completed their transformation, they will remain completely altered until their timeframe is met.

    Madame Trandafir will not speak with a user until their timeframe is met, however she will make an exception if the user has spent at least 6 months in their altered form. Here, she may ask a few questions, and if she feels enough sympathy, she may return the user back to normal. But if the user upsets, annoys, or angers her, their fortune may alter.

    Once the timeframe is met, the user will gradually transition back to how they were before the altered. This could take a minute, an hour, or a day. However, it can take up to a week and will not depend on how quick/slow the original altering did. The change back may return parts of a user to normal before others, which may cause some altercations to the user.

    It is entirely possible that the fortune read may never completely go away. Perhaps a single, or multiple traits may disappear or reappear after some time. It can never be truly known, but is always a risk a user must understand when CONSENTing to Madame Trandafir’s Fortune Telling. And of course Madame Trandafir may help to return you back to normal.

    That is if she decides to see you again…

    — — —

    If a user is not satisfied with what happened in during or after their visit to the fortune teller, they must refer back to the risk’s. All users are CONSENTing to whatever their fortune may be. This is why we suggest understanding who Madame Trandafir is before you CONSENT to having your fortune read.

    — — —

    Please make sure to read the rules very carefully as to understand the Do’s, Don’t’s, and Risk’s that follow consenting.

    There is no guarantee that all changes will be reversed. Stick to the rules and suggestions, and it will be more likely.
