    his long lost son to the
    Magic User Industry,which actually
    involves less
    magic use
    and more days writing reports
    for Insurance Companies
    and Legal Actions dealing
    with spells gone wrong.

    Basically 5 percent spell casting
    95 percent typing away reports
    and mailing three hundred page documents
    to Magic Spell Liability Insurance
    Adjusters.

    Wiffle had made most of his money writing these reports
    for various human run companies
    aand figured Current Damon would do well in the human industry.

    As it turned out the prison wardens brother in law
    happened to be the head of Big Whig Magic Industry Insurance and so after the two had shook hands
    Wiffle suddenly lost his business
    with various human companies.

    Which meant besides not being able to pay the mortgage on the chicken farm and house there was no money to give to various prison folk and police agencies and politicians as the bribes needed to keep Current Damon out of jail.So desperate Wiffle made contact with a old friend in the government of the nation of Drakavania a dragon country somewhere north of Scandinavian.

    Wiffle told this granddaughter of the President for Life Damon the Dragon that he was desperate and wanted business with
    Drakavania.She asked for the name of that son of his and when Wiffle replied "Damon" the dragoness reminded him that her
    grandfather absolutely hated anyone named Damon including himself and regularly ate for breakfast robots called the DAMON XI series.

    However Wiffle was desperate,the police cars already coming down the driveway to question his son Damon and so the dragoness friend agreed to fly over ( quite literally) but on condition they do things her way and not interfere.Wiffle agreed and so the granddaughter to the President for Life looked at what Wiffle had available.Clearly Current Damon wouldn't last a second once her granddad laid eyes
    on him,Brendan was too honest,Wiffle too old and needed
    elsewhere which left wicked Hen
    Alexander.

    And so Wiffle turned Alexander back into a human and the terrified con man was hauled off by what he viewed at the time as a huge alien reptile.

    Mercifully he didn't know that
    the granddaughter planned to turn him
    into a younger version of the monster who was
    hauling him off.

    She flew him to a old Temple to Dragon Goddesses humans had built
    centuries earlier and laid him down in the bottom of the hollow at the center of the Temple and showed him a
    huge dragon doll and Dragon Child Picture Book and other
    stuff a tweenie girl dragon would have as playthings.

    Then the President for Lifes granddaughter loaded up Alexander with a small bit of dragons blood

    hard for him to drink since the human was such a tiny
    slender thing.
    However as Alexanders body slowly began to change and grow she
    stopped
    addressing him as

    awakened dragon Xandra began taking a interest in her Dragon Dolly Lucinda
