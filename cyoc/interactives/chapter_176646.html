Though Amanda also had one question for the doctor as they followed him through the hallways.

    "Doctor Kreia and I hold the same rank," Doctor Manius gave soft laugh, "and in some ways we have shifts. Make sure you're healthy and not having any trouble... while bringing you food or escorting you to the hospital's cafeteria and so on. There were attempts to try and stay awake, for their loved ones, but in staying up through the ride from the summer palace back to the island where they became merpeople was finally catching up with them. With that she soon fell asleep and slept through Karen's waking up, Sam's return, Taylor being woken up and Doctor Manius coming in to find Karen awake, explaining some things, and then Karen drifting off to sleep again.     "Instead you all became merpeople," Kat said slowly.

    "And liked it," Taylor answered, "Nate and Rose will be king and queen in the future... and have all sorts of royal duties they'll have to learn in addition to what they went to school for... and they might get to do a good bit of what they want. This place seems to be more progressive than most other places in the human world in an odd way."

    "And my parents can't oppress us," Amanda said to Kat, "they're actually quite accepting of gays and lesbians... even if the number of merpeople that are gay are at about the same proportion as there are on Earth... so... they're very welcoming here. We'll get a happily ever after."
