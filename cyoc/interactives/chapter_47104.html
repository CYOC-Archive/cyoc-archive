    your lab, Zodis!" Hector proclaimed.

    "Hector! I thought you fled?! H-how did you get here?" Zodis's jaw
    dropped.

    Gideon! You two try to escape. I will hold off Zodis!" Hector yelled

    Before the blast hit him, Zodis managed to summon a shield of earth
    around himself, to nullify the attack. "Apparently you can have
