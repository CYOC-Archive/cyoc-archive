    You turn and look to the Genie for his reaction. The inner sense suggests he is about to render you to the animal world. As much as this was not your original plan, the thought of being as an equine does not bother you.

    In fact it is causing you to become excited, aroused!

    "I bow to your power great Genie, Achmed! Be pleased to make of my body what would be of great use to your brillant wisdom. I stand ready to be a great Wizard even if I must then be alive within the form of an animal. An equine, a horse or small pony stallion would serve to make me humble. If it would please your thoughts of me, then let me be as what suits you just so that I might be able to enjoy my new life." you would ask of the Genie rather than condem him for his trechery.

    The Genie Achmed eyes you with a squinting look wondering if perchance you have a trick up your sleeve.

    "Flirting with danger is what you like, then be it time you test your own limits my young Wizard." sethed Achmed.

    A raise of your arms up and then drawing them in close as if one would make a prayer, you close your eyes and think of what you would like to become as Equine.

    "Be of great care young Wizard there are those out and about who would wish your life to be as a trip into Hell. Be wise, chose well less being too simple would leave you vaurnable to attacks. Take care less being to much an equine stands you out as something else, again worthy of attack!" Ashmed tosses out his suggestions.

    Timidly you decide and your choice is to shouted from the rafters.

    "Ha, your thinking too long young Wizard!" chides Ashmed.

    "I am a wizard, a strong and powerful, wise Wizard and I say my form shall be as, A ZEBRA !" you shout to the world.

    What do you do now?
