    “Well as it’s one of the mixed wings it combines details from two” the receptionist started “First for both unless you choose the very rare and based on talks I myself believe joke option, unless you leave in said form your minds and memories are retained and this relates to all wings. Second age is that you cannot either go over 100 years of age or under 3 months before birth, former has...lethal side effects and the second just won’t work. Third for animals is that contrary to its name it also includes options for mythological and extinct beings alongside plants and select objects though the later has limits to prevent lethal problems”. “Artful lot of lethal prevention methods” Mavis noted “Wish other resorts and hotels had that consideration to inhibit suicide”. “Thank you and fourth, owing to the mixed ability owing to how different beings age differently if you change your age first when undergoing an ‘animal’ change or vice-versa you’ll become the resulting animal equivalent age. For example you go for age forty to forty-five human then a dog you’ll be seven” the receptionist continued kindly “Now enjoy your stay. Oh and for reference to what I just spoke of and anything I missing each room carries an instruction manual book for guests to use”.
