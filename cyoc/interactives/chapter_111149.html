    You woke up the next day and just like before Violet came up with breakfast. You saw that it was 7:30 so you had some time to talk.
    "I saw you in the residential park yesterday answering questions are you going to do that again today?" Violet asked
    "Actually I was going to the local school as a guest for the children today, then I thought that I d wander around here the rest of the time." You said
    "I think that will be excellent for both you and the kids mistress." Violet said
    "I thought I asked you to just call me Jade if we were private?" You said to Violet
    "Sorry Jade, I need to get used to that." Violet said
    "You'll get it eventually." You said smiling,"besides I like you."
    Violet blushed and said,"Thank you Jade."
    You had a talk about why you were there at the park and Violet left later. You saw that it was 8 so you stepped out and let the maid clean the room. You walked downstairs to find Razor again standing there waiting for you.
    "I don't feel right letting you leave on your own just tell me why you were alone." He asked
    "Well three reasons first even though I'm female I remember before I arrived a voice that told everything I know now including how to defend myself against foes, second if I really needed help I could just run to a nearby guard, and finally don't think I didn't see you watching me from a distance there in the crowd I know you when I see you." You said grinning
    Razor was speechless and then he finally said," Well you would be the first female then who could fight, but even so you are important to the people of Greatclaw," "and I wish to be part of them too while I lead them that's why I was there so that they could get to know me as I get to know them, so I could build a trust with them." You interjected
    "I....understand you may go." Razor hesitated.
    You arrived at the school 20 minutes early before school, but it looked like an apartment.
    "This is a school?" You asked Ruby
    "Yes for Kindergarten students each apartment up along the park serves for different grades, here we have kindergarten students the next apartment we have 1st grade and so on." Ruby stated
    "I get it so what do I do to surprise them?" You asked
    "Just being here will be a surprise for them, do you want to sit in the desk of pull up a chair?" Ruby asked
    "I'll pull up a chair you are the teacher after all." You asked, "Is that a closet?"
    "For school yes why'd you ask?" Ruby said
    "I could hide in it and surprise them I'd be glad to do it."You said
    "Okay if it's on your terms that is."Ruby said opening the closet," Just wait for me to mention a guest is that okay mistress?"
    "Yes and I better get in because I think it's almost time for class," you pointed to the clock and stepped in the closet.
    You shut the door as Ruby brought in the kids and took attendance then you heard her say," Children today we have a very-very important guest today I think you'll all be surprised to see who it is." Ruby said
    You then opened the door and the entire class was shocked to see you.
    "Hello children," You said as you sat down,"I am Jade many of you may, and some of you may know me already, but as most of you know I am the new leader for the tribe and I am here just to see all of you."
    You nodded to Ruby to signal that you're done talking and Ruby said,"Yes Mistress Jade is here today to answer all of your questions so gather around and ask away."The kids gathered around you and asked all sorts of questions, and you enjoyed evey one of those questions as well as the kids. Before you knew it it was time to leave so Ruby had the kids say goodbye and thanked you for being there. After that you headed back up to the temple to explore.
