    "What do you think it is?" Milo asks as him and Ryan walk closer to the archway.

    "I'm not sure...but I feel like I've seen it before." Ryan says as he tries to figure out where he'd seen it before. As he is pondering about this, there a rumble and a slight shake as dust falls down from the ceiling of the cave. The crystals inside starts to glow, glowing brighter and brighter before an explosion of light blinded them both momentarily. Blinking their eyes open, they are both greeted with the sight of a yellow vortex filling the inside of the archway.

    "This looks like a portal!" Milo shouted alarmed and Ryan have to agree. Behind the portal where the crystals are, they seem to be back to normal and somehow glittering even more than before. "I think we should check it out." he says excitedly making you dumbfounded.

    "Are you serious!" Ryan was wide eyed as I stares at his best friend. "What if we die if go through!?"

    Milo sighs and rubs his neck gently. "Well you've always wanted to explore more right?" He asks frowning at Ryan. "I know you and this is a chance of a lifetime. What if we never get another chance? What if this is your only opportunity in fulfilling your desire?"

    "I..." Ryan couldn't respond because he knows that it is possibly true. He sighs and looks down then back up at the former portal. "I guess...I'm just scared."

    Milo chuckles and pat his friend on the back. "And I was scared on climbing cliffs during our first few hiking trips together. This time I'll be the one to support you." He pulls him into a hug. "Besides I will be there with you." Ryan nods and blush slightly at how close they are before pulling away.

    "Alright, let's go in then." Ryan takes a deep breath as they step closer. The portal doesn't make any sound aside from a slight humming. It smirks in a hypnotic pattern with a few distortions and it still struck him as familiar. "He goes." He said before they stepped through together. There was a flash and soon everything went black.

    As the two humans enter through the portal, a figure sitting high up on a tree branch smirks. Pointed ears stood atop its head with a beastial maw moving to let out a soft chuckle. Jumping down and landing onto soft paws as they walk up towards the still swirling portal.

    The figure then snapped their fingers disappear with a bolt of lightning. They entered the portal as another bolt of lightning struck the cave and sealing it close from any unwanted guests.

    "Welcome to Wulvaros."
