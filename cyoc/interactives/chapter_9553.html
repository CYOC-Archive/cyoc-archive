    In an instant the four stood in the Court of Zeus.

    “Ah, Father! I see you have found the wayward trio, good!” Zeus thundered.

    With that Rhea, entered the court from behind the throne of Zeus.

    Rhea said in a voice that chimed like crystals of the deep earth, “So it shall come to pass, as was charted by My Husband before there was Kosmos!”

In mere moments She grew so large it looked as though She would deliver Her child. She passed nine months of gestation in the space of nine minutes.

His eyes were golden and had rectangular horizontal slits as the horse and deer, the sheep and goat have because they are prey; but Pan not being completely a beast was not prey. He was Man, Beast and God all in one!
