    Your first taste in action

    added by Wanderer 7 years ago BM I O

    You and 78328 heard the voice of the hive, <There is a break in in the cargo section, any nearby security drones and contain the situation!> You then, in some parts of your will, run to the scene in a semi-robot like fashion and away from Steve in the conversion room.

    Inside the warehouses, there were masks and balaclava wearing thieves that were taking the living suits and other zentai-like products from the cargo boxes and loading them where the truck docks and several security team that protected the warehouse were down. You finally reached the warehouse and entered the area searching the huge warehouse for the thugs that are in the vicinity along with your other drone bretherens. All of you then went off without saying a word to each other knowing what you needed to do as a role to protect the facility and the other products within it. You then bagged one of the thugs and turned his jacket into a spandex straight jacket and search for others. You then hear fighting on your right and see a checkered drone, Unit 55327, locked in a fist fight, of course, you still have your will, and tried to help him, but you were then tackled by another thug wielding a knife-like stun baton as he tries to stab you with it. Though you can't die, or be wounded, a hit from that stun knife will render you unconscious and you managed to dodge it and kick him in the back as he falls forward on you and then push him to the side and did the same to the other thug as his clothes turn into a sleepsack with no openings (well, unlike the other thug which is a straight jacket). You then try to help Unit 55327, but looks like he already took care of it.

    You then hear a thud as a snake skin zentai drone, Unit 63571, was knocked unconscious by the stun knife to the neck and rushed to the scene with 55327 and punched the thug's lights out. The fight lasted for at least 14 minutes as the remaining black market thugs took whatever they had left in the van and fled the scene. You wanted to stop them, but a voice halt your process, <Do not worry Unit 84730, we have dispatched our special drones to find them and bring them back safely, I assure you. Bring in those criminals so they can receive punishment.> You then have confidence with the Hive and dragged the struggling criminals you bagged to the prisoner cells where they're going to be trialed in the next few days.

    You kind of like your job already, you got your first taste of action guarding and protecting the living products. Though, you are sad that you didn't get to save the others that were taken, but you still have confidence on the Hive that they will get them back as you placed them in their cells. They shook the unbreakable spandex bars trying to break free as you then leave and received new orders from the Hive which is to be with the other security drones replacing the ones that were unconscious as they were taken to the rehabilitation section of the facility.

    A few days later, the hunter drones that were on the tail of those in the black market dealing came back successfully and placed them back in their rightful cargo boxes as situations returned to normal. You were then relieved of your current order and did a normal patrol around the facility perimeters. You wonder about those thugs that raided the warehouse, but it was not of your concern which was told by the Hive and then just continued following orders for the next few years. Though, you do hope you can talk to Steve again once it's over and maintain some connections with some drones once your service is done.
