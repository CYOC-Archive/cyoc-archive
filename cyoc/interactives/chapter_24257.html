    "You did this to me!" Chris yelled. She grabbed the necklace, took it off of her neck and put it down on the counter. Somehow, she'd expected to change back when she removed it but had no such luck.

    "Ah yes, I remember now. You having an exciting time yet?"

    "Please, change me back!" Chris pleaded.

    "Back? Into what?" the old man asked.

    "Stop joking around! You know this thing turned me into a girl. I'm supposed to be a guy!"

    "There has to be something here that can change me back! Please, I don't want to be a girl!"

    The old man pushed Chris' hands away and sighed. "They all react this way at first. You'll get used to it in a while. Now please, I have business to attend to."
