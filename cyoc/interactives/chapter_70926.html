    As Damon spends some time, in the stables, the royal Magican comes down to see him. "There's my favorite Prince. Prince of the Horse's. How are you Damon?" said the royal Magican.

    Damon smile as he was happy to see the royal Magican. The royal magican was the one who had taught Damon, Brendan and Aaron about magic. He was a really good teacher and he was like family to him. One time Brendan accidently knock magic powder off a table and turn him, and his brothers, into ducks and given them duck minds. They were lucky that there father and his guards caught them before they flew south for the winter. The royal magican change them back after that. Aaron was mad at Brendan and didn't speak to him for a few weeks.

    "I am good, royal Magican, I am enjoying my new life as a Stalion and I don't mind living in the stables and eating food that Horse's eat. I always thought Aaron was your favorite prince.

    "No no. Aaron is too stuborn to rule this kingdom and let's face it. Brendan is not right in the mind to rule the kingdom. I was disappointed that you weren't the one to rule the kingdom" said the royal Magican.

    "Yeah I know. My father felt the same way. But I told him that I like being a Horse instead of a prince. But I'm still a prince. All the Horse's here likes me and follow's my every order. So at least I'm ruling something" said Damon.

    "Yeah a bunch of horse's. Listen Damon. I need a favor from you" said the royal magican. "What is it?" said Damon.

    "I have a friend that needs help. She's a gypsy and she's trying to help a poor farmer boy to become a man. So she ask me if there's a royal mount to have the young man ride on. What do you say Damon? Would you help her?" said the royal magican.

    "Sure I'll help. What's the boy's name?" said Damon. "If I remember correctly. She said his name was Tobar" said the royal Magican.

    "Alright but before I go. What's the gybsy's name and what about Lawrence when he hears that I will be gone for a while" Damon said.

    "Her name is Madame Lyubitshka and you don't have to worry about your rider. I'll tell him about what you are doing and I'm sure he'll understand" said the royal magican.

    A few hours later. Madame Lyubitshka came to the castle. Everyone, including Damon's two brothers, was outside to say there goodbyes to Damon.

    "Madame Lyubitshka it's good to see you agin" the royal magican said as he kisses Madame Lyubitshka's hand. Madame Lyubithshka then took her hand away.

    "It's good to see you too royal magican. Where is the royal mount that you promise me" said Madame Lyubitshka. "Right here Madame. This is Prince Damon. He use to be a human who got turn into a Stallion by a evil witch and now he want's to stay like this as he is now called Prince of the Horse's" said the royal magican.

    Madame Lyubitshka then takes a good look at Damon. "Hmm he sure is a handsome fellow and he does look very strong to pull his own weight. Very well magican. I shall take this horse with me on our quest" said Madame Lyubitshka.

    Tobar then comes to see the horse and bow's before Damon. "I have heard about you prince Damon. I promise to take goos care of you and make sure that your fed and get plenty of water. I will not treat you bad as well. You can trust me on this quest" Tobar said as he get's up.

    "I place my fath into you Tobar. I hope that you keep your promise Tobar" Damon said. "Now then shall we get going?" said Madame Lyubitshka. "Yes I'm ready madame" said Damon. Tobar then climbs into Damon. "You can trust me Damon. I will make sure nothing happens to you and that you return home safely" Tobar said.
