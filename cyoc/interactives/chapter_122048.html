    Sarah Palmer was short, about 5'1", though she still liked to brag that she was taller than her mother. Kimberly imagined that stools were a required piece of furniture in the Palmer house. She had short copper hair that reached down to her chin and a very kind if plain face. What stood out were the ice blue eyes that were behind her big 'hipstery' glasses. She had a compact figure that was not extremely well endowed in any particular area but was well proportioned. Once all the board games, food and clothes were loaded Kimberly brought Pandora's Box downstairs and put it in the back.

While Kimberly, Sarah and Jessica generally fell into the more hipster/nerd side of the interest spectrum, Skye was a party girl. She loved pop and dance music and could always be expected to be out at some bar or event on the weekends. She had been Jessica's roommate for the past year and the two had traded interests between each other quite well, with Jessica becoming more outgoing and Skye getting a small interest in nerdy stuff and quiet nights, though it was never long before her extroverted side pulled her back to the local clubs. Skye was about 5'7", the closest to Kimberly in height. She had mid-back length blonde hair that had some wild pink highlights in it. She had hazel eyes and a very attractive face. Next to her personal gear was a large and oddly shaped bag the clinked when Skye began loading it into the van.

    Endless alcohol no doubt, Kimberly thought to herself, I wonder if drunk transformations are more pleasurable.

    Once the girls had gotten all their gear in the van, Kimberly started driving towards her family's lodge. Over the next 10 hours the other girls chatted, sang along to their favorite songs and napped. Skye even passed a flask between the other girls, resulting in singing that was more enthusiastic but less in tune with the music.

    Kimberly barely payed attention to the singing or gossip about boys and girls. Her mind was on the road and on the magic box tucked neatly away in the back of the car. Tonight she decided, after they made dinner, she would reveal the box to them and get this adventure started. She doubted any but Sarah would believe her at first, but she knew that would quickly pass once they started changing.

    By the time they had reached their destination the sun had already began setting. The lodge was high up in the mountain forests and was remote. Kimberly's father was quite wealthy and loved to ski, but didn't find much time in the summer to use the house and so had allowed her and her friends to stay by themselves. It was quite cozy, with modern appliances and furniture and had more bedrooms and bathrooms than any of them would need. The grand structure loomed impressively in the windshield as they approached.

    "Wow" the other girls said in unison as Kimberly pulled the van up.

    "I didn't know your family was this rich." said Jessica.

    "Yeah from now on you're buying the drinks when we go out." said Skye

    The girls began to unpack all their gear and bring it inside. Kimberly managed to sneak the Box to her room without the others noticing. Once they were done, they set to making dinner. The chicken tenders and mac and cheese were perfect for people tired after a long trip. Eventually the fatigued and stuffed girls found their way to the main living area, where they flopped on the couches, gossiping and sipping some wine from Skye's stash.

Skye eyed her as she did she, her eyes lingering for a second on the swell of Kimberly's rear before saying sarcastically, "Off to bed already? And without even a goodnight kiss?" The other two turned from their conversation and looked at Kimberly.

    "Not yet", Kim replied over her shoulder as she walked away, " just going to grab the activity I brought for the trip."

    "Ha!" Skye exclaimed as Kimberly left, "You owe me five bucks Jess, I told you she'd find something."

    "I wonder what it is?" Jessica thought aloud.

    "Some kind of fancy box", said Sarah softly, "She was really secretive about it when I saw it this morning, wouldn't tell me a thing about it."

    "Well!" said Kimberly, making her grand return, ornate box in her hands, "You guys are about to find out."
