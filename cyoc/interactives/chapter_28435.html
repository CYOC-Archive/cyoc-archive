    "Say that's some potent stuff," Simon said nodding at the rucksack.

    "Yeah, but it's for one use only. Believe me you wouldn't want to overdose on that stuff. It's one thing to be a jock like us, it's another to be a jock--" the bell rang, "Shoot gotta run, catch you later, Sy!"

    Simon didn't think about what Curt had said. His eyes just locked on the backpack as it retreated from view. Then he sprinted after his quarry.

    Turned out Curt was headed to his wrestling class, and just as he entered the locker room, Simon caught up with him. Swiftly Simon knocked Curt down and pillaged the product out of his pack.

    Curt raised his hand and said, "NO! Don't!"

    But it was too late, Simon greedily emptied the entire bottle on to his head and rubbed the chemical all over his body as if he were bathing in the stuff.

    Curt just sat on the concrete floor shaking his head.
