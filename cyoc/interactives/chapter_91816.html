So I am going to be generous and let you decide what I get" she says with a fake smile.

    "Even the 14 years you took off me?" Luke asks, knowing she wouldn't agree to that.

    "Of course not, if you say anything that would not be to my advantage I will be forced to take all 4 of your physical years and let mum go through pregnancy again." She says With an intimidating look, she smiles then says, "that should be fun."

    "A second room would be nice, I could use it as a changing room", she thinks for a moment "but wouldn't that mean that you would have to share a room with Ben?"

    "Yeah I can live with that, I just done want you to take any more years of my life"

    "What about it?" Luke said confused.

    "Come on I know you know it. I've seen you steal his card several times." Jessica stated.

    "Thanks for informing me but i do hope you remember that I hold your future or should I say past in my hands" she replies threateningly.

    "Yes I know. How about this, we play another game and if you win then you get my room and the pin number. Also I wont try to get the game again." He says, sitting down on Jessica's bed.

    "Okay, but what about if you win?" She asks waving her hand up in the air.

    "Everything goes back to how it was." Luke answer's.

    "No" she says bluntly.

    "How about I get half the years you took back." He barter.

    "Okay I'll be 16 and you'll be 12 that makes me older than you still. Okay I can agree with that." She agrees.

    "Fine, what game are we going to play?" Luke says.

    "Connect four" she decides.

    "So the game we played when we started then?" He remindes her.

    "Yup, plus the fact that I know I can beat you." Jessica informs cockily.

    Jessica picks up the box and opens the lid, inside was the connect four that was used when they first played. After clicking the parts together and sorting the coins.
