    After settling affairs and contacting her family, Charley was sitting in the covered back of a military truck accompanied by Dr. Abigail Matsuoka and two military police officers, being transported to her new home for an undetermined amount of time. She thought it was not an entirely bad situation to be in, even though her giant debut blew up the internet in such a viral fashion. Which made explaining to her parents all more difficult as to why their daughter was streaking in public, ignoring that she had grown to nearly thirty feet in size. Looking back on it and the resulting transformation into her new, powerful and well-endowed figure, Charley only smiled. “I take it you’re pleased with the results?”

    Dr. Matsuoka sat across from Charley, who worked on her tablet during the trip. Given Charley’s towering size, the doctor had to sit to her left side or else she be between her legs. “To be honest, it’s more than I could have ever asked for,” Charley replied.

    “I wouldn’t count your blessings just yet,” said Matsuoka. “Even though you’ve bonded with the mutagen, you may still be prone to unstable reactions.”

    “That aside, I’m curious, Dr. Matsuoka,” said Charley as she leaned forward. “You did not seem to react when you first saw me while I changed into this outfit you provided. I take it this is par for the course with your project?”

    “In a manner of speaking, yes,” said the doctor. “And please, you can call me Abi.”

    “Charley,” said the amazon. As she continued to speak, Charley’s nose tingled for a moment. “What else can you tell me, or do we need to be somewhere away from eavesdroppers?” She glanced at the officers, a man and a woman, who failed to avert their eyes away from statuesque powerhouse.

    “I would prefer if we weren’t around less than professional servicemen,” Abi said coldly. “However, the facility we will be arriving at is staffed with those of a more relaxed caliber.”

    Charley turned toward the officers, who quickly looked away. “I don’t mind the stares, in fact, I—” A sneeze erupted from Charley before she could finish her sentence, with the force of an air-powered cannon that nearly punched a hole in the truck’s tarp. “Excuse me. We must be passing by an open field. The pollen is messing with my… What?”

    Both officers only stared at Charley with wide-eye expressions of disbelief. After a moment, she finally registered that she was now sitting several feet shorter than before. Looking around, Charley guessed she had shrunk to less than three feet in height. She went to stand up only to fall forward as her breasts, which did not shrink but grew to massive proportions, threw off her center of gravity. The elastic pants she wore slid off her legs whereas the top lived up to its properties by stretching to contain her breasts. Her enhanced strength has also left her, to which she called out to Abi. “Uh, doc? Is this to be expected?”

    Abi looked away from her tablet and veered her gaze down toward Charley. Putting her device aside, she knelt to closely examine the new shrunken woman resting on beachball-size breasts. “Yes and no,” she said. “While I did anticipate further mass alterations, I did not take an allergic catalyst into account.”

    “Well how long do you think this will—” Charley quickly turned her head away as another sneeze interrupted her. Looking back to Abi, she was taken aback by how much larger the doctor now appeared. Charley watched as Abi nonchalantly pulled a tape measure from her jacket pocket and proceeded to inspect this latest change. Fearful, the diminished woman cried out, “I know this is fascinating for your research, but I’m legitimately terrified I might sneeze myself into molecule!”

    “I wouldn’t worry,” said Abi nonchalantly. She muttered measurements in between speaking. The only number Charley caught on was twelve inches. “We’ll reach the facility in a moment.”
