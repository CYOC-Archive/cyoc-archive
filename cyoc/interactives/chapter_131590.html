It was finally Gloria who stepped forward, hoping to help smooth this situation over. “Um excuse,” she said meekly. “Mr. And Mrs. Tozawa my name is Gloria. I...I kind of may have helped give you your youth back.”

As Mika’s parents listened they slowly nodded in agreement, finding that Gloria had good intentions even if they were a bit over the top. “So all of this was to make us more accepting of our daughter,” Hideo said solemnly, to which Gloria nodded. Hideo sighed heavily as he looked towards his children. “Mika,” he said sadly. “I...I am so sorry that I was stuck in the past. I let my feelings of hurt cloud my judgement, I was so...stupid I put myself before you.”
