    As the now teenage Keiko helped Lisa search the house for the Reset Watch, she was considering something. Once they had the Reset Watch, any age changes weren't permanent. Keiko gave a glance at Lisa and thought it might be fun to be that age or younger for a time.

    "I've been thinking about when we find the Reset Watch." Lisa paused and looked at Keiko. The Asian Teen looked a bit hesitant. "If I call and say to my parents that you need me to babysit you this weekend, they would allow me to."

    "I'm not staying eight." Lisa pouted cutely.

    Keiko smiled. "Have you babysat before?"

    Lisa thought for a moment. "Sure, I've done it for a few years and . . .no way. You want to be my age."

    Keiko was already calling up her parents. "Or a bit younger maybe." The other line picked up and Keiko explained she would be babysitting for the weekend, which her parents agreed.

    "So, lets find that Reset Watch." Keiko marched off toward the master bedroom of Lisa's parents while holding the Regression Watch. Lisa was about to follow but had a thought.

    "The den, of course." Lisa took a few minutes and had found the package for her in the desk drawer. She opened and found the Reset Watch and a booklet. "So once a day use and if you press it again while aging, you will stop aging at that point." She aimed and pressed the Reset Watch. She smiled as the feeling of getting older was felt as she could feel the tips of her toes reach the floor.

    Keiko looked around the bedroom and was about to look under the bed when she heard someone coming toward the bedroom. The footsteps were too heavy to be Lisa's. They must be Lisa's Mother's and Keiko would be in big trouble.

    "The closet." A small closet as Keiko noticed immediately as she tried to squeeze into it. "I'll be noticed immediately unless I can." The footsteps seemed to be checking something outside the room so Keiko only had a few moments. She pointed the Regression Watch and turned it way up, watching the years melt away. Within moments she managed to fit into the closet and close the door as Puberty became something to look forward too. She found a blanket and a somewhat empty corner of the closet.

    She had gone past 10 before she squeezed into the corner and put the blanket over her. The small closet seemed to expand around her as the years fell off of her. Before she could panic, she slowed the rapid regression to a crawl, allowing the Asian girl to better conceal herself. She felt the blanket conceal her fully and pressed the stop button of the Regression Watch.

    Lisa felt really good as she stretched. Her age, height, and curves were back to where they should be. She stepped out of the den after making certain everything in the room wasn't mess up before heading down the hall toward the Master Bedroom. Lisa paused for a moment as the sensation of how small things were and how easy she could see things. She could see herself in the hallway mirror and smiled.

    Lisa then continued into the master bedroom and found nobody in there. "Okay Keiko where are you?"

    A faint sound came from the closet and Lisa went over to it and opened the door. Inside the small closet was a blanket covering something and she reached over and pulled it out.

    Keiko apparently had gotten very young. The former Asian teen had the features of a young child and maybe was a few inches over three feet. "Hey Keiko."

    The child looked way up at Lisa. "Lisa is that you?"

    "Back to my normal age." Lisa reached in and gently picked up the small girl after getting the Regression Watch out of her hands. The time said 4yrs and 2 months.
