    "Of course" Harry said. "They have fun during battles too. Who wouldn't?"

    "No one does" Harry said. "But magical girls don't get hurt."

Allison saw the look in Harry's eyes that said he was thinking that it had hurt and he was right. She waited while he got a good look then said "I got this by being stupid. We went up against demonic wolves that were way out of our league. I was bitten. I didn't even get the chance to move."

    "You're a magical girl though aren't you?" Harry asked seriously.

    "I am" Allison said.

    "And you got hurt?" Harry asked.

    "I did" Allison said. "Do you still want to be a magical girl?"

    Harry thought for a minute and then said "Yes."

    "Grab my necklace with me" Jen said. As soon as he did she said "This is you."

    There was a slight guilty look about Marry so Allison asked "Do your parents know?"

    "I snuck out" Marry said looking down.

    Jen wrapped Marry into a big hug and said "I missed you."

    Marry returned the hug and said "Me too."

    "Not to break this up but I need to train you girls" Shaela said. "There are four of you now and you need some training before you try to take on anything. Really, it was irresponsible of you to go after those demonic wolves. Hmm... we need more space to train." Her eyes glowed gold and they were all transported to someplace else. It looked like a large park. "Now then" Shaela said. "First thing you need to know is when and when not to fight. You were outclassed during your first fight. You should have been able to sense that. Did any of you sense that?"

    "Um I wasn't there" Harry said.

    "It's Harry" Harry said. "Though I should have a girl's name now."

    "Hmm..." Shaela said in thought. "How about Harriette?"

    "That sounds fine" Harry said.

    "Then it's settled" Shaela said. "I know that you weren't there Harriette. Now you three!" she barked looking at Jen, Allison, and Marry. "Did you sense that you were outclassed?"
