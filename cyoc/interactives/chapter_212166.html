    Transform or Dare?

    Jen tackles other girls forever

    Jen couldn't tell if she was the unluckiest or the luckiest person ever. For as much word vomit as Kyle put out, he stupidly made the vague stipulation that Jen would only merge with someone randomly. And she kept losing the coin flip. Over. And over. And over again.

    It started out with people thinking Jen was just being a bitch for no reason. She couldn't go 5 minutes at school without hitting another girl with her shoulder. After a while people got sick of her acting like she wasn't trying to be rude and making up some story about body merging or something like that, so eventually she got brought to the principal's office. Jen also tackled the principal, since she was a woman. After nearly getting thrown right back out of the principal's office, Jen somehow managed to explain what had happened with Kyle over the phone (it had been a few days by that point).

    The principal had actually experienced the Transform or Dare curse in the past (she was cursed to become at least 6 inches shorter whenever she made contact with water, a humiliating secret for the principal to tell Jen), so thankfully the principal actually believed Jen's story.

    Kyle was immediately thrown out of school and expelled once the details emerged about Jen and her potential merging partner being made naked and having a giant erogenous zone placed on their body. The whole thing reeked of sexual assault (which, if you somehow didn't know, is bad). He was then investigated by a juvenile court and placed in a detention center.

    Things never got much better for Jen. She ended up having to introduce herself to people while including the curse. She was terrified to tell people about the chance that they would merge bodies with her, so she only ever told them about her compulsion to shoulder check people. She had to quit cheerleading because whenever she would show up to a football game she would be forced to charge the stands and bump into every single woman with a pulse. Kyle also had to pay for the shoulder pads Jen now needed once her shoulders inevitably started bruising.

    In order to maintain anything resembling a social life, Jen had to use the Transform or Dare curse on all of her friends in order to give them the ability to turn their arms into jelly and then back again, that way she wouldn't injure them.

    Jen hated everything about her new life and secretly began to pray that her transformation would finally activate because sharing a body with someone would probably be better than losing control of her body just to assault people for no reason...

    Will this torment ever end for Jen?
