It was something that all of them could rally against for the moment.

Her temperament left her more inclined to see Megan and Jenny as victims of Cupple, but while they were carrying out Cupple's orders and commands, that really didn't matter. "Our hive is based around real love. Not lust born out of some wizard's greed."

    "You should already know that your master failed to control us!" Lucy growled at the door, "so you can take your victory in forcing us to all become Slug People. But we are NOT part of your hive, nor will we EVER be!"

    "You say that now," Megan answered, "but you haven't had sex as a Slug Person yet... and once that happens you'll want to be with us. As we are the only Slug People here. Particularly while you're defending your human brother..."

At first it was something that he couldn't resist or didn't fully notice until Whitney started transforming, but once that was recognized it was something that unnerved him. It was like Megan and Jenny solely wanted him for sex and nothing more than that and the fact that they also made statements on how he would do the same with his sisters and mother was also unnerving. Slug People may have had some magical protection against inbreeding, but that didn't mean he wanted that for himself or his sisters.

    That only made the situation ever more frustrating with Megan and Jenny still present. They'd tried all they could to get at him through the door, and even tried going around to come in through the windows when his mother and sisters went to get food. Ultimately Megan and Jenny were pushed out, but between either help from their wizard master or because the front or back door wasn't locked they'd been able to come back in and were now messing with them through the door... again. It left him feeling trapped and nervous, for with the main door shut and locked, there was no way any of them could see if Cupple was with Megan and Jenny and thus a great deal of uncertainty remained.

    "I may end up a Slug Woman," Jeff commented, "though either way... I am not yours and nor will I be. Those I'd want to be with romantically... start a family with... it should be someone I love, not just someone who is horny and available."

    "Your transformation hasn't completed yet," Jenny answered, "and we can tell by your scent that a lot of all this turns you on. You want to experience it. To complete your transformation."

    "I want to be able to defend my family and stop whatever plans your master has," Jeff spoke as he managed to stand up, and feeling some of the united confidence coming from his sisters, "and if that means becoming a Slug Person... I'll accept that. But keep in mind that your master is not invincible."

    "Maybe," Megan answered, "but then you can't defeat him as you are... or even after your transformation is completed. Nor will the churches in the area help you. They're all largely wedded to their own beliefs on how we're wicked Satanists."

    "One can never know for sure," Grace answered, "as they've also tolerated the existence of Slug People."

    "Too bad you'll never get to ask them..." Jenny countered, "for you'll be joining our hive."

    The end of the sentence lacked some of the confidence that the earlier taunts had, but Jeff also knew that they were all in pickle. His family had to stay together so the Cupple couldn't overpower the hive mind that linked his mother and sisters together and they couldn't leave him because of what Megan and Jenny would do without his family being present to protect him. And as he'd recently found... he couldn't leave the room if he remained human.
