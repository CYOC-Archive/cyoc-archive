    'What? I don't understand what you mean...' John said in confusion. 'What if I tell you Kyle can be seen again?' Matthew said that in a very
    calm tone, his hands were starting to get restless. John sat beside him on the bed and said, 'Kyle can never be brought back and
    everyone knows that, you should know that fact even better yourself.' 'Ah but if it's a person whom mummy thinks its Kyle?' John gave a
    puzzled look and then as if he know where the conversation was going, he asked a question, voice shaking, 'You are hiding something
    aren't you? Do tell me.' Immediately, Matthew took out a pendant which John found familiar, it was the one that John saw back in the car.
    'With this pendant as my birthday gift right before my parents died, I found out its special power, with it, I can turn into anyone I like but so
    far, I have only changed once before changing back to myself.' 'Am I supposed to believe all that?! Is this some kind of a joke?' John
    asked and grabbed Matthew's shoulders. 'I am dead serious, see this mirror on the pendant? All I need is some of Kyle's DNA to be on
    this mirror and when I wear it, I will turn into him and have his memories, once I turned into him, people's memories of him will also be
    altered which means that mummy will still think he's alive and will love me. However, this will only work when I have the pendant on, I can
    choose to never take it off by making it permanent and to make it permanent, I will just need to put this pendant on Kyle's body and
    transfer my soul to his body and after that happens, the pendant will bind together and will be on his neck forever but people's memories
    does not have him wearing such a pendant and might trigger their memories that he is dead hence I have to be careful to always hide it
    when I am wearing a shirt and things like that.' John thought for awhile and then said, 'Are you alright with being in his body forever? I
    want you to make it permanent.' 'Okay so when are we going to do it?' 'Now, let's go to the cemetery and dig out his body so that you can
    take over him.' Once they reached the cemetery,John quickly dug out the coffin and took Kyle's body out,Matthew immediately put the
    pendant on him and then, a surge of energy could be seen leaving Matthew's body and entering Kyle's body. When that happened, the
    coffin and Matthew's body vanished, like Matthew had never existed before and Kyle had not died at all in the first place. Matthew who is
    now Kyle, started coughing and John helped him up, the necklace had bonded itself and Kule made sure to slip it inside his shirt and hide
    it. When they reached home, Emily was seen preparing dinner as of nothing happened at all. 'Where have the two of you been? John, did
    you take Kyle somewhere again?' Kyle smiled, knowing that Emily had accepted him.
