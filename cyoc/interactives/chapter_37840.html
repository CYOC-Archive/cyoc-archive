    You and your sister have been riding on the hover car for awhile now, flying over several neighborhoods until you reach a small supermarket. You suspect something is wrong because with your sensitive hearing, you can hear people screaming, and a few babies crying. You are a bit curious, and remain curious right up to the point when Christy stops the hover car just outside the supermarket.

    “Are you ready for our first mission?” asked Christy.

    “What?” you question. “First mission? What do you mean?”

    She simply points to the see-through window of the supermarket. You look to see what’s wrong, and when you do, you gasp. There’s some kind of pink thing in there flying around the store and zapping at people. When it does, the people immediately shrink and regress into babies!

    “What is that?” you ask.

    “That is experiment 151, Baby-fier,” said Christy. “You know him as Alex.” And she winks.

    “Christy, you can’t be serious. Have you turned everyone else into experiments from the show too?”

    “Pretty much.”

    “Well that’s wrong.” You say, but she quickly brings up the remote, and you shrink back. “We’ll do it your way for now, but you have to promise to turn us all back to normal when this is over.”

    “Whatever, let’s just go.”

    She kicks you off the hover car and jumps to the ground as well, dusting herself off. Then you both run into the store, and right into the mayhem. There he was, Alex, probably not knowing what he was doing. He zapped and zapped, not caring who he hit.

    “You better stay back a little,” you say. “He might hit you.”

    “Don’t worry,” said Christy, “I’m almost invisible to him. You on the other hand…”

    “What?!”

    You have no time to react. Alex comes right in front of you and gets ready for a hit on you…

    What do you do now?
