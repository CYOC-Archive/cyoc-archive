    “Okay girls, as you know it’s a big game tonight, we need to bring our A-game to make sure our team is completely supported, I have supplied you all with the list of routines we are going to perform tonight. We are going to do a light practice to make sure no mistakes happen on the field, I don’t want anyone to get injured tonight because someone was unable to perform their part. Sound good?” Jen said looking over the crowd meeting the eyes of a few of the girls who Jen was most irritated with due to their uniforms fitting a bit more snug this week.

    “I thought about it but I don’t think we are completely ready to do during such an important game. I have noticed some things this week that I would like to discuss after the game with everyone. For now, let’s get warmed up and make sure we are ready for the game to start. Now, move it ladies!” Jen said with an air of seriousness about her.

    Suzy backed down not used to the cold attitude from Jen that she normally only showed when she was displeased with something.
    The girls started warming up and did a full run-through of the routine.
