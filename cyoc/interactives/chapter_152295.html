    Lina in pieces

    Author note:
    The events of this chapter occur at the same time as the previous two chapters.

    After separating from Amy, Lina, having gone to a distant aisle from where Amy was, she found something that caught her eye. At the end of that aisle there was a guillotine. She knew the guillotine was too dangerous and she should not approach, but her curiosity was stronger and she decided to see more closely.

    Lina, analyzing the level of security, put her hands above the blade and pulled her down with all her might. The blade did not move a millimeter at all. "The Blade is stuck, there's no chance any accident could happen." she thought. Then she began fiddling with the guillotine without fear.

    Oh she had an idea "How does the person with the head feel to be cut off." Believing she was safe, Lina put her neck below the blade. But she was taken aback, because as soon as her head was in the right position, the guillotine blade slammed down, slicing Lina's head off, falling on a pillow that was on the other side.

    Lina settled for her own death and closed her eyes hoping to lose consciousness. But a few minutes passed and she was still conscious and only then did she realize that something was wrong. She realized she could still feel her body. Then she opened her eyes and turned her head so that she could see better. She saw, through a small gap beneath the guillotine, her body lying on the floor, but wondered why there wasn't a pool of blood there. So she tried to move her arms. The arms on her body moved the way she wanted. "Crazy, I've been beheaded, but I'm alive and I can control my body from a distance."

    Then Lina controlled her body by getting it to its feet. When the body came close enough for her to look over, she saw that in the space between her shoulders had a smooth skin, with no sign of her neck having been there.

    "I don't know what's going on, but I'll have to ask for help to get back to normal." Lina thought. But she made one more mistake. When she went to get her head, she made her body lower through the guillotine, but she had not realized that the blade had returned to its original position and as soon as she caught the head the blade came down again, cutting her body in half, causing her torso to fall to the side of her head and the entire underside to stand in the hallway.

    As with the head, it had no blood, but the severed part was covered with skin and it kept control of all parts. But now she finds herself in a more complicated situation, she screamed for help as loud as she could.

    Who showed up to help her?
