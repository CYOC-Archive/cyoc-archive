    Juanita so hoped that the other Managers didn't get involved. There were far too many
    'quick fix schemes' that could back fire in a situation like this. At least since the other
    Managers had left her with the task of working directly with the park guests, she would
    have complete authority of the situation for now. She knew Hannah was ideal when Juanita
    met with the doe. Hannah had common sense, self control, self respect, enjoyed her new
    self, wasn't ashamed of it, and still wanted to do something with her life as a fellow
    member of civilization. Juanita honestly believed Hannah could be the one. Hannah's new

    However, when a thirty year old man, and a disfigured fifty year old man, came out as a
    anthro vixen and sage and a teenage couple to boot, this would get people's attention, it
    would be 'fountain of youth.' Truly, after figuring out how to change, rearranged, and
    alter DNA, the RNA wasn't that impossible.

    Many of the Management feared the 'age regression' option was a mistake, that it would
    muddy the waters, and people fearing an immortal class of the rich would push it back,
    and the non-age altering DNA secret along with it.

    Juanita also knew it would destroy the foxes to have made to go back into his disfigured
    and elderly form now. Juanita knew this wasn't just about the foxes, or the Managers own
    plan to have anthros be assimilated into society. She knew what happened soon between
    herself and the foxes, would set many precedents for all guests to come.

    What SHOULD be the price for agelessness, heck, for a second go at life? For a third go? Or
    a sixteenth go? Juanita felt a headache coming on.
