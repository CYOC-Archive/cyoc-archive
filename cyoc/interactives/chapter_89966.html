    lee look around the shop until he found a walking stick.

    "a walking stick?" lee asks to himself, "it'll make me look cool while i'm walking around town." so lee grabs it to have a closer look.

    it looks like any other walking stick but it's incrusted with diamonds and rubys and other expensive looking jewels. The handle's blue, so is the bit at the bottom, it's got little paintings at birds on it and it's about the right height for him.

    "oh, the amazing walking stick, nice choose." lee jumps as the shopkeeper sneaks up behind him. "don't scare me like that, you almost gave me a heart attack.” lee says to the shopkeeper a little angry, "sorry, i didn't mean to scare you." the shopkeeper said.

    "it's alright. wait, did you say the amazing walking stick?" lee asks. "yes, yes i did." "why do you call it that?"

    "because this is no ordinary walking stick, this cane can transform you into anything you want. from animal transformations to modifications to your, or somebody elses, body, from a gender change to a species change. if you can think of it, it will do it, but don't worry, you can change yourself, or another person, back to the way they were, or you can set a timer."

    lee stares at the man and then bursts into laughter. "aw man, you crack me up. how old do u think i am? 5? i'm 27, dude. now can you be serious, please?" lee says to the shopkeeper who doesn't look fazed.

    "oh, i'm being deadly serious, lee." lee stops laughing and look at the man surprised. "how do you know my name?" lee asks "i know your name because i'm a wizard." the shopkeeper said.

    lee, again, bursts into laughter. " stop making me laugh." lee stops laughing before asking his next questions. "so, say if this thing actually can transform people, how does it work and did you say something about changing them back"

    "ok what you've got to do is hold the walking stick in one hand and think about what the transformation is going to be, when you've thought of one, you can ever lift the cane up and slam it down or, if the other person far away but you can still see him/her, point the walking stick at him/her and the transformation will begin, and if you want to set a timer, think of a timer and it will appear on the cane somewhere (probably the handle). afterwards, turn the timer to the appropriate time, the times very from 10 minutes to 1 month. if you feel like you, or they, have had enough, do the same thing that transformed them to change them back. if you feel like you, or they, need a little less time but you still want them to be transformed, turn the timer counterclockwise. for more time, turn the timer clockwise. to have the transformation go on for more than 1 month, don't add a timer and you can change them back whenever you want. if you've transformed into a animal with no thumbs, don't worry, just think of yourself back to normal, touch the cane and you'll change back. did you get all of that?" explained the shopkeeper.

    "i think so." lee said. "but it's not going to work anywhere." "oh, believe me, it will." the shopkeeper said. "we'll see about that, how much?" "£10" "wow, that's cheap for a walking stick with jewels."

    so, lee went to pay for the walking stick and left the shop, but before he left, he whispered to himself. "a walking stick that can transform people, yeah right." lee got in his car and headed for home.

    when lee got home, he decided to watch some tv. after a few hours of that, he got bored and turned it off. he didn't know what to do afterwards.

    then he looked over to the walking stick he had put down by the tv. should i try it? he thought to himself. it probably won't work, but you never know.

    so he picked up the amazing walking stick, as the shopkeeper called it, and tried to think of something to transform into. "what should i choose" he asked himself. after about 10 minutes, he thought of something, pulled up the cane and slamed it down on the carpet.
