    The dinner finished well and that allowed everyone to move to the Kat's Klub for the actual dance part of the prom. It took things a bit differently from the way most schools handled prom, but then, WSA wasn't "most schools." With WSA and Lyre functioning as an integrated district, there were a sizeable number of Human students that were there with the Animalian students. These differences then also manifested in how the dances went, particularly when it came to Animalians that were uplifted. Many that had been pets before being uplifted could actually dance fairly well, though they generally only matched their former owner's ability. Those that didn't see their owner's dance all that much didn't have that, and had to improvise as such.

    That in turn lead to curiosity as JC and Pauline found out. They had just gotten some water to drink, largely due to the fact that with so many students packed into the club for the prom, even with the AC on, the room was fairly warm. The two Microraptors had decided to rest for a few moments and get a bottle of water. They had just sat down near the front window when a pair of male teens approached them. JC though there was a look of curiousness about them, but couldn't fully tell on what the likely reasoning for it, and with Sophie's lessons on politeness in mind let them approach.

    "Can I help you?" JC asked slowly as the two approached while Pauline took a sip from her water bottle.

    "Yeah... uh... with that dance you... you two were doing... um..." the first teen sort of stammered, his voice sounding rather nervous after seeing the two largely grey feathered Dinotopians with their tails being red, danced earlier to a brisk pop song that had been played on the club's speakers.

    "It was weird," the second teen cut in, sounding more authoritative, "no one I've seen before dances like that."

    "Probably because it was a "dance" that I observed before I was uplifted," Pauline answered, thinking back to before being bought by her previous owner. It was largely based around some of the courtship dances she's observed among other parrots at the pet store she had been in and then combined with some of the dance steps that Sophie and Roland tried to teach. They and Sophie's parents had also taught them some of the more slow dances, which would work with the present song being played, and if they weren't so well heated by the accumulated body heat they might still be dancing.

    "But all Dinotopians are converted Humans," the second teen pointed out, "there are no Dinosaurs to uplift."

    "There are no non-Avian Dinosaurs to uplift," JC corrected, "given that Birds evolved from Dinosaurs... one could say that Birds are Dinosaurs."

    "So you became Bird Animalians and then Dinotopians?" the first teen asked, making Pauline facepalm.

    "No, Pauline and I were normal African Grey Parrots," JC answered and then pointed out to the dance floor where Sophie and Roland were dancing and nuzzling their crests together, "and until Project Mesozoic's opening at the start of last summer, I was Sophie's pet." JC didn't include that he still saw Sophie as his mother. "Pauline's former owner passed away and the owner's family didn't have legal ability to care for her... I'm guessing where they lived didn't allow for pets."

    "So we were uplifted from Grey Parrots to Microraptor Dinotopians," Pauline answered.

    "So... you were both converted and uplifted," the second surmised.

    "If that's how you want to view it... then yes," JC nodded.

    "You're getting better," Sarah said to Travis on a different part of the dance floor as they moved along.

    "I try," Travis managed a weak smile.

    The Dalmatian Animalian had grown up in the Lyre Fire Station before the second outbreak in Lyre. In this, unlike Malice who'd seen Sarah dance plenty of times, Travis's experience was with grown up firemen who were often working to maintain their equipment and keep themselves trained for the case of a fire or being deployed if there was any wildfire. This meant that once he was uplifted, Travis wasn't as skilled at dancing as Sarah, Malice, or Simon. Malice had learned from Sarah, and Simon was a converted Human, like Sarah. He'd learned a few things from Sarah over time, be it with the Valentines Dances, be it at Lyre or WSA, but Travis still had the sense that he lagged behind his mate when it came to dancing.

    "And succeeding," Sarah smiled and licked Travis's cheek, "even if you don't think so."

    "Thanks," Travis manages and licked Sarah's cheek in return with a small smile. Though he also found her fur feeling rather warm. "You're not too hot, are you?"

    As a Dalmatian, Travis's fur was shorter and while it could do reasonably well in winter, it wasn't perfect for winter weather. Sarah and Malice, however, as Siberian Huskies, a breed intended to live and survive in very cold conditions, could hold onto their own body heat easily. And with so many in the club at the moment, a lot of that extra body heat built up in the room, even with the AC on.

    "I'm doing okay," Sarah said slowly, "but... I could rest for a bit."

As they reached the south edge of the dance floor and stepped onto one of the carpeted levels on that edge, they found Simon and Malice also coming off the dance floor, and likely for the same reason. Both looked happy, especially Malice, as she got to have Simon as her "plus one" for the dance, even if he wasn't a student at WSA. Though that of course had been more the result in the difference in age between Simon and Sarah, and technically Malice.     "Though with all the people here," Simon commented, "it's understandable. And with Humans and Animalians having fun... it could well be a major success."
