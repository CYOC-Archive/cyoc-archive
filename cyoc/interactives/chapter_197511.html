    Ty's Power

    Dustin didn’t learn shit

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago O Magic Reality alteration

    “Okay I wish you wished I had every ounce of your power.”

    Ty felt a shiver run down his spine as his body went into autopilot becoming his brother’s as his brain went slack jawed and he zoned out and his will became Dustin’s
    “I wish all my power was Dustin’s”

    Immediately Ty felt sick as a tidal wave of energy left Ty flowing directly into Dustin. Dustin smirked as he felt the energy flow into him. Ty on the other hand looked gaunt as he became weak in the knees as he felt like a chunk of his soul was ripped off his and given to his brother.

    Dustin immediately looked over his hands and glanced into the mirror making sure the wish worked like he wanted to seeing that his power didn’t change him physically he chuckled. Ty weak in his knees shambled into the bathroom.

    “ Dustin please give it back I feel sick and weak I need to feel normal again”

    Dustin worried about what his brother would do to him back and thinking quick made a wish.

    “I wished I knew what my powers can do.”
    Suddenly a piece of paper ty scribbled on flew into the bathroom and levitated in front of Dustin’s view reading it he learned his powers were limitless. Nervous of losing his power he wished again.
    “I wish I knew what powers Ty still has.”
    An echo in his head answers “ nothing he’s a regular person again”

    Dustin smirked “ oh Ty, I wish that my wishes can’t destroy people and they would revert to their former version when I do.”

    Dustin felt a serge of electricity echo through his body.

    “ I get to do whatever I want now and there’s nothing you can do to stop me!”

    Dustin fell to his weak knees groveling please Dustin, please give me the power back to me you don’t know what you are doing!”

    “Fuck you Ty I wished you called me master!”
