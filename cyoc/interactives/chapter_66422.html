    I read the ad and immediately applied. I've heard about how much
    people made at these things and I'll get loads. It was probably
    something boring like sugar pills or how exercise effects the body.
    Blah blah. Still I could use the money. A large black car pulled up to
    collect me. The windows were blacked out. Maybe this was bigger than I
    thought...

    In the car was several men in suits. They were quite intimidating.
    This WAS bigger than I thought. I was forced to sign a contract which
    was thicker than my arm. I hope this money is worth it. Eventually we
    arrived at a large building. I was ushered out the car and into a
    large room. There were two scientists and a row of men and boys.
    Apparently I was bit late as they were already way into their
    presentation.

    "As I was saying, we're running a special medical experiment for the
    military. This is completely confidential, and no further information
    will be revealed unless and until you are picked. Now if you'll follow
    me into the next room we will perform a physical examination."
    Physical? Oh no. I was terrible at any form of physical activity. I
    was a short skinny 14 year old. I was probably already out of luck.

    In the next room all the men has taken off their shirts. Was this it?
    I did the same and threw mine onto a pile in the corner. Then I joined
    the line. I was quite ashamed. Most of the men here were big and
    strong. I was easily the skinniest one here. I had stick arms and no
    body hair. God I hated my body.

    I got to the front of the line and was measured and weighed. Without
    much thought they nodded and sent me to the front. There was no one
    else there. Had I made it? The rest of line were sent out of a door
    and I was left with the scientists. One man, the boss by the looks of
    things, took me to a small room with looked like a bedroom.
    "Congratulations in is order I presume. You have been selected to take
    part in a military experiment. This will be extremely beneficial for
    the Army and it will benefit you too."
    "Erm, what is this experiment?" I asked nervously. The man chuckled.
    "It's simple really. But all in due time my friend. We need to see if
    you are ready and worthy of this test."
    "Worthy?"
    "Now get some rest. You've got a big day tomorrow!"

    The door closed and I heard it lock. Hmm, guess I was stuck here then.
    Oh well I might as well go to sleep. I kept wondering about what the
    experiment was, and why they needed the skinniest kid in the room for
    it. Eventually I fell asleep.
