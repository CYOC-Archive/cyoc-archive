    Joey said nothing, looking at the shell shocked 10 year old red head. "Can I get some clothes that fit," she asked meekly.

    Joey nodded, making a wish. Teens othersized tshirt and Jean's sheank down to childish proportions. Gwen watched as the cuffs of her Jean's retracted, exposing her tiny feet while her shirt shrank down to a more natural fit. When winced slightly as she felt her formerly useless bra vanish. As the girl stood, she could only sigh. She was a barefoot 10byear old once more.

    "You okay," Joey asked.

    Gwen glanced upwards, her eyes growing wide as Joey seemed to loom over her. "I'm okay," she said softly. "You just look so...big. How did this happen Joey?"

    "Im not quite sure," he said meekly. "For a second I wondered what you were like as a kid. Next thing I know, poof, your 10."

    "Well here I am," Gwen chirped, forcing a smile. "Just glad you didn't wonder what I was like as a baby."

    Joey forced a chuckle, apologizing to Gwen once more for his mistake. "Can you fix this," Gwen asked.

    "I should be able toi," Joey stated. Gwen let out a sigh of relief, a smile finally appearing on her face. "Thank goodness," she sighed. "I mean dont get me wrong I had a great childhood but being a kid again just feels kinda weird."

    "Weird how?"

    Gwen shrugged. "Well I'm shorter for one," she chuckled. "And well I just feel... super hyper again. It's weird feeling this hyper while still thinking like a grown up."

    "Probably all the sugar you ate," Joey smirked. Gwen stuck her tongue out playfully as she gazed up at Joey. "No chance," she giggled. "Now unless you want me jumping on the couch you better make me older again."

    Joey laughed, agreeing to the young redheads request. As he was a out to make his wish the two heard a knock at the door. "Are you expecting company Gwen?"

    "No," Gwen said. The two looked at one another as a second series of knocks rang out. "Well answer the door," Gwen exclaimed.

    "Me? Why?"

    "I cant answer the door like this," Gwen stated, motioning to her diminished body. "What if it's someone I know? They're going to wonder why theres a little girl here?"

    "So what should I say?"

    "You'll think of something," Gwen shrugged. "I mean you seem like a resourceful young man."

    Joey sighed, despite being referred to as a young man by a 10 year old girl, it made some sense. As Joey opened the door, he was shocked to see who was at the door. "Stacy! Melissa!"

    "Oh wow hey Mr," Stacy said cheerfully. "Wow do you have like two apartments?" Joey was simply dumbfounded, staring at the two girl scouts. Stacy was still a cheerful and blunt girl while a now 10 year old Melissa shrank back slightly. The raven haired girl letting her friend do the talking.

    "No I was just coming here to visit," Joey stated. "What are you two doing here?"

    "We decided to make a second sweep," Stacy said. "Their were a couple of apartments we missed the first time through." Stacy now looked past Joey, seeing Gwen watching the them. "Are you babysitting?"

    Joey paused, looking back towards Gwen. "Um yeah," he said, causing Gwen to frown. "This is Gwen, shes 10 too. Gwen this is Stacy and Melissa. They sold me some Girl Scout cookies earlier."

    Gwen gave a polite wave as the two Gurl Scouts entered the apartment. "I'm Stacy," the young blond said. "This is my friend Melissa. Did you just move here?"

    "No," Gwen said. "I've been here for awhile."

    "I dont remember seeing you at school," Melissa stated. "Do you go to a private school or something?"

    Gwen stammered, trying to come up with a response as Joey grinned. For Joey he couldnt help but find the situation a little humorous. The two women he had regressed were now meeting eachother. He wondered how the two would get along. Not wanting to make anymore errant wishes he now approached Gwen, placing a hand on the girls shoulder. "So Gwen," he smirked. "Have you ever thought of joining the Girl Scouts?"

    "What," Geen gaped. "Um no not really."

    "You totally should," Stacy gushed. "Its so much fun."

    As Stacy and Melissa began trying to convince Gwen to join the Girl Scouts, Joey could only chuckle softly. He could definitely have a bit of fun with this.
