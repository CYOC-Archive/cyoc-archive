    Holding up the underwear, noting that nobody is asking why you have a pair of girl's panties, you repeat the question as the coach comes into the changing area.

    Looking at your classmates and then you, he asks, "What seems to be the problem?"

    Holding up the underwear, you tell him, "Somebody has been going through my stuff and replaced my underwear with these," as you glare at the remaining students, who aren't even paying attention to your ranting.
