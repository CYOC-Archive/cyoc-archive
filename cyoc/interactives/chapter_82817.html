    added by Sora112 9 years ago A BM

    Seconds pass as you hold the kiss. For a
    brief moment, you think that she's about
    to refuse you. Just as you begin to move
    away, she returns the kiss. You both make
    out for what feels like an eternity. As
    you move to begin undressing her, you feel
    something brush up against your waist,
    before it wraps itself around your back.
    It felt soft, but strong. It couldn't be
    her arms, because they were around your
    neck. You stop dead. It feels like a...

    Having felt your hesitation, June pulled
    back and the extra limb went with her,
    brushing down your back and making you
    feel all tingly. Her eyes go wide when she
    sees that she had moved your headband, but
    the bunny ears stayed. Your human ears
    were closer to the front and back
    (respectively). You swallowed hard as you
    noticed that you had done the same to her
    and the same happened. She was as much a
    cat-girl as you were a bunny-girl. Her
    tail curled up in fear and she hugged it
    to her chest, before realising what she
    was doing and tried to throw it behind
    her. She flinched from pain, and it curled
    back up reflexively. Fear spread across
    her features. Your face mirrored her's
    when you heard footsteps outside the door.

    "I'll explain as much as I can later." You
    say softly, placing a comforting hand on
    her lap. "For now, fix your headband and
    try not to move your tail too much."

    There was a moment where it looked like
    she was going to argue, but, after a quick
    glance to the door, she nodded. John and
    Sarah came through the door, mid-
    conversion. They both looked straight at
    the two of you. With every fibre of your
    being, you forced your ears not to react
    to the overwhelming sense of dread.

    "Did we interrupt something?" John said
    with a nervous giggle.

    "If you wanted to hide it, you should have
    fixed your lipstick." Commented Sarah,
    matter-of-factly.

    "Lip...stick?" Glancing to June, who had
    just about frozen, you seen that her black
    lipstick had indeed mixed with the red of
    yours, as well as being smudged from the
    kissing. No doubt that you were the same.
    Heat rushed to your face as June brought
    her tail round to play with it (probably
    to prevent it from moving on it's own).

    "I've always been a little...curious...
    And she was so disorientated. I guessed
    from the way that was talking to girls
    earlier that she was... Y'know..."

    June continued to play with her tail as
    she told the little white lie. You wonder
    briefly if she how genuine that she was
    being, wringing your hands all the while.

    John and Sarah both raised an eyebrow and
    looked at each other.

    "To each her own, I suppose." Sarah had a
    thoughtful expression as did John.

    "Do you want some privacy?"

    You blush deeper and nod.

    "Can I watch?!"

    Sarah silenced that option before you
    could react with a sharp punch to his
    stomach before dragging him out of the
    room.

    "Don't have to much fun." Sarah remarked
    as she dragged a complaining John from the
    room. Just as your blush reached critical
    mass, the door clicked shut.

    "That...was close..." June sighed and let
    go of her tail. "Now. Explain."

    Her eyes narrowed and that feeling of
    dread returned as you try to explain the
    situation up to this point.

    What do you do now?
