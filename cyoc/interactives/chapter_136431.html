    You and his friend Eric are walking home from a night at the bars. As you guys walk from through campus, you notice that the campus is darker then usual. Something was weird.
    "For some reason, I feel odd about this walk home" Eric said.
    "Me too man, something is off about campus. It's not always this dark."
    You and your roommate try to walk quickly back to your dorm. Suddenly a Teenage girl appears in front of your path. She is wearing a white dress and doll like make-up. She is stand very still, without moving. She does not even blink.
    "Alright little girl, move out the way so we can get home." Eric said. The girl just responds with giggle.
    "I don't have time for games, I'm too drunk for this right now. I need to go to bed." Eric said as he walked up to the girl and pushed her out of the way of the path. She falls to ground. " Go home little girl" He say a he starts to walk off. The little girl just giggles.
    "You've been a bad boy, maybe being a girl will make you a better person." The little girl say. You watch in horror as the girl's eye glow green. Suddenly Eric stop walking, and he turns around in your direction.
    "Dave, I feel funny." Eric say.
    You watch in horror as Eric slowly transform into a girl. His brown hair grows to shoulder length, his boobs grow to a young but perky b-cup, his face transform into a cute face a 20 year old girly girl and his/her height shrinks to 5'2. His clothes don't change much they just became tighter to fit her new form.
    "What happened to me." Eric(a) said as she examine her new body.
    "I've turned you into a girl, but this is just the start. You must learn how to be a girl" The little girl in the white said. As she snaps hers finger. Eric(a) Looked at you and said, "Dave..Help" and suddenly she disappeared. In her place was a female joker card.
    "Where he go, what happen to him?" you asked.
    The little girl stands up and look at you. "Your friend has been sent to school where he will learn how to be a proper and nice girls. Not a rude boy. You will see her again in a week. She will be the prefect girl. I will let you go, but be a bad boy, and you'll join him." After those words the girl disappeared into the night. The Jokers had strike again. You stood their stun and unable to move. As you wonder what to do next?
