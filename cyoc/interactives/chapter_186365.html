    After they discussed what happened and things started to settle down, Jess noticed a black truck parked in front of her house with a logo she didn't know. She looked for her mother but couldn't find her, probably having gone elsewhere, finding only Fiona and Wendy, who were carrying Roxanne in their arms.

    "Hey guys, look over there. Someone entered my house." Jess told them, who looked at the house and also saw the black truck

    "We have to go there immediately, we'll be in trouble if they find out about Chronivac's existence." Roxanne said

    The foursran to the house and after entering they ran to Jess's room, hoping that whoever was in the house hadn't gone there, but they found a couple of mutants there, a female raptortaur and a six-armed hermaphrodite, who were examining the Chronivac.

    "Who do you think you are to go into other people's homes without permission?" Roxanne said to the strangers

    "You must be the dwellers of this house. I'm Jay and this is my colleague Kris. We work for TransDem, the company that developed Chronivac." the six-armed hernaphrodite introduced herself.

    "We detected the energy wave released from here and came to check what had happened, as there was no one in the house and the door was left open we just went inside." Kris said

    "We've confirmed that the Chronivac present in this house is one of the version 4.0 prototypes that we've sent out to some random people for testing. Turns out this equipment here is totally fried, there's nothing we can do about it." Jay said

    "So are we stuck in this form? I won't have a body again?" Roxanne said

    "It's possible you have. We were discussing whether we would use another device to alter reality so that no one remembers the changes and everything has always been the way it is. There are a lot of people who have been transformed and we probably won't be able to get them all back to normal, so the reality alteration will make it all easier." Kris said

    "You might be right, maybe it's for the best." Wendy said, believing it would be a good idea.

    "I am against this plan." Jess said

    "And who are you to want to say something about the procedure of the professionals." Kris said, thinking the average person couldn't come up with a better idea.

    "I'm Jeff, Jess thanks to altered reality, the Chronivac was delivered addressed to me. I have a doubt about this plan of yours to simply alter reality so that all the changes never happened and people were always what they became. Since you are part of the team that developed Chronivac, can you tell me how the process of reality alteration works?" she said

    "Of course I know how the reality shift works. The system analyzes the current reality and the new form of transformed people, changing the memories and everything related to it, including their past, their belongings and everything that is necessary to do everything so that the present changes as little as possible. . That's why it's important that... oh my god I had forgotten about this detail." Kris said

    "Forgot what detail?" Jay asked

    "I had forgotten that in order to change reality, the system needs to know who has been transformed and what transformation has been carried out in order to work. Not knowing who was affected or how we can't do anything." Kris said

    "It's true, the girl was right, the plan won't work." Jay said

    "We're not in trouble for what happened, are we?" Roxanne asked

    "No. You would be in trouble if you had purposely caused this mess, but the problem was the device itself. As I said before this is still a prototype it can have flaws, as far as I could see it seems that the cooling system failed and the Chronivac overheated, which fried the equipment releasing an uncontrolled transformative wave, which for not having a specific target and not a defined transformation affected everyone in the area with random changes." Jay said

    "That's for the best, I thought my cat hitting several random buttons after lying down on the keyboard might have caused the problem." Jess said

    "Something like that wouldn't solve problems of this level, but the problem in the refrigeration system is worrisome. We send prototypes for more people to test, if everyone has the same problem the situation can get much worse." Kris said
