    Escaping to a safe place

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 9 months ago AR Toddler

    At first, Selina was happy to see that Derek was in this place, but as she started to approach the desk, her happiness soon began to fade. Looking closer, she realized that this person wasn't Derek. Her brother had no mustache, no green eyes, let alone brown hair. So to her bad luck, she would still have to find a way to escape.

    Soon, the daycare workers proceeded to take all the children (including Selina) to the nap room. Inside the room, the older children were placed on cots while the younger ones were placed in cribs. In the case of Selina, she was placed in a cot since she was one of the oldest in the daycare. Selina simply proceeded to lie down and pretended to fall asleep. After making sure all the children were asleep, the worker proceeded to turn off the lights and leave the room.

    As the worker left the room, Selina proceeded to get up and quickly started heading towards the door. Unfortunately, the daycare worker had closed it completely, so Selina had to find another way out. And to her surprise, she found something that might help her. In the room there was a small vent that might be able to take her out of the daycare.

    Although there was a small problem. The vent was small so Selina would have to regress a few years if she wanted to go in there. Selina didn't like the idea of regressing even more to enter the vent but she really didn't want to get trapped in this place so she had no choice but to take another 2 pills to escape. By swallowing the pills, Selina began to regress to the point that now all that was left of her was a 2-year-old toddler girl.

    Although she was already the right size to fit into the vent, her clothes were now too big for her, so she started looking for something she could wear and something where she could store the pills and the flash drive. Luckily, near her was a toy chest containing a green romper, green socks, a diaper, and a stuffed rabbit attached to a pacifier.

    So Selina proceeded to remove all her old clothes, she took the diaper that was inside the toy chest and with some difficulty she was able to put it on. Then she proceeded to put on the green romper and socks. And finally she proceeded to place both the pills and the flash drive inside the stuffed animal. And although she didn't think of using the pacifier at first, she eventually proceeded to place the pacifier in her mouth so she wouldn't have to go around holding it in her hand.

    Now that she was finally ready, she proceeded to head towards the vent. She proceeded to climb inside the vent and began to crawl inside as she continued to suck on her pacifier.

    She kept on crawling for a long time until she finally reached the end of the vent. Coming out of that little duct, Selina realized that she was now outside the daycare. And from what she could see, there was no one from the hospital, so Selina quickly proceeded to escape from that place. When she managed to get out of the mall, Selina felt very happy although she didn't want to stop until she found a nearby place where she could rest a bit. And luckily for her, near the mall there was…..
