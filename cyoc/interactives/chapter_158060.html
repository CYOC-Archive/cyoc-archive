    "Where is it? Do you still have it?" Emily asked, trying to sound a bit calmer.

    "Nah sorry Em." The Asian replied. "I just picked it up before and put it down when we saw you." She glanced down at one of the greeters desks as the walked passed it before shrugging. "It's no there anymore though so that stinks. I really wanted to show it to you but wither the real owner found it or some teacher took it."

    "Or the janitor threw it out!" The blonde added. "That thing was old and grimy looking."

    "Shut up Alexa you''re such a bitch!" The Asian playfully teased as the two girls laughed.

    Emily, meanwhile, was starting to really freak out. Her mind started to wander towards more upsetting thoughts, wondering what would become of them if they didn't get the camera back. Her boyfriend would live at the rest of his life as her mother, and who even knew where her father was? As they walked by the cafeteria windows, Emily noticed that her face had changed again. Now she didn't even look that much like herself, as she almost looked more like a younger, female Rick than herself.

    "God, that's probably because I have a new dad now..." She thought, freaking out even further.

    Thankfully, her thoughts were interrupted as she felt a text, and almost instinctively brought it up to read it. It was from her "Mom" and read "I think Asian is Dad?"

    Emily looked back up shocked. The camera's reality chaining powers suddenly taught her the Asian's name, as she somehow knew she was called Paula. This Asian teen was her father?!
