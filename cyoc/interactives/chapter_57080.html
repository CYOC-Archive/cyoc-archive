    "Trust me I know. This becomes a curse if you didn't chose it. But there's always the

    If anyone else wants to play the game and win they're welcome to it, but I've had my fill of
    that constantly moving chaotic world, I'll stick with the still one."

    "Death is still too."

    "I'd be lying if I said that I wasn't scared of death, I've done some things that earn me a
    place in Hell. BUt that actually wasn't believe it or not my reason. And if this was death,
    you wouldn't have front row seat of The Moving doing their thing. Besides, it's not like
    we're a total non-factor in existence."

    "How do you figure?"

    "Ame-no-Uzume-no-mikoto."

    That was Ulysses! Sarah turned her perception (not wondering how she was able to turn
    her view when her body was frozen in place), and saw the teenage purple lupman lad
    before her... why was he so small? It took Sarah a moment to realize that she was the one
    who had gotten bigger, she had been so intrigued by Kira that she hadn't noticed she was
    now the the size of the room like the other statues. 'Other statues.' That choice of words
    rang through her head.

    Ulysses looked up at the giant wolf headed woman, a strange look of respect on his face.
    And something about his body language seemed different, but not in a bad way. "I just
    want to say thank you, you've always been an inspiration to me. I may not be part of Amy's
    religion, nor do I want to be. But your story has always inspired me. And I want to thank
    you or that." Ulysses said knowing he was talking to 'just a statue' and felt slightly silly for
    it, but it felt right.

    Ulysses figure both Amy and Anne would throw a fit over what he did next if they found
    out but he hugged the giant purple statue.

    Sarah had no idea what happened next, as she felt a tingling actually go through her entire
    body when Ulysses made this simple gesture. What was even more disturbing is how she
    was sure that she could feel him even though he was no longer touching her, she could
    feel where he was, what he was feeling, almost what he was thinking!

    "The goddess of Dawn and Revelry, who brought the sun goddess Amaterasu out of hiding
    and saving the world from freezing over... " Ulysses said smiling at the statue.

    He looked at Sunny and smiled and nodded too, "Not that the rest of you aren't beautiful
    too."

    He looked at Sarah/Ame-no-Uzume-no-mikoto again and left.

    But Sarah COULD STILL FEEL HIM, even as he went into the next room, she knew exactly
    where he was in that next room, it was like a shadow of her was walking along side him
    almost constantly.

    It didn't occur to Sarah once to simply leave the still world, she told herself it was to make
    sure Sydney didn't do anything foolish, but at the same time, she was intrigued by what
    just happened.

    'He called me beautiful.' Sydney thought, when was the last time anyone had called her
    beautiful? For the last couple decades she had felt like nothing more than a fixture at her
    job, now at least she was called beautiful!

    Sunny said, "By process of elimination, and by the platter you're holding, that makes you
    Uke Mochi... a food goddess who the god of the moon killed after the way she produced a
    feast from her body openings disgusted him. That avatar Kira mentioned said something

    Sydney heard and understood what Sunny said (except for the 'avatar' thing).

    The siblings Terri and Sandi walked through the entry way, barely giving the five statues
    any mind, after all, they had been commissioned since before the two of them were born.

    Sydney thought about this, and realized, 'They ignore me, and I didn't feel bad about it...'
    Sydney thought. 'Uke Mochi huh? A goddess who made food out of her rear and was
    murdered for it?... Alright then.' Sydney thought. She then 'looked at Luna' 'I forgive you.'

    'Huh?'

    'I forgive you for killing me.'

    'Uh, that wasn't me you know, and that wasn't you.'

    'All the same, I forgive you.'

    Luna wasn't sure what was wrong with her when she replied, 'I accept your forgiveness.'

    The Monkey Prince didn't know if Ame-no-Uzume-no-mikoto would chose to stay or not,
    but Uke Mochi it seemed was already coming around.

    Later, when Luna's mind nodded off, she had a dream, of walking on the moon. And

    scales matched her fur, a gargoyle carved to resemble the serpent like oriental dragon, a
