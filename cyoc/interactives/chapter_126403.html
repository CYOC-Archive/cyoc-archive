    Kellie's New Secret

    Kellie waved goodbye to Mary Elise after secretly hooking her up with Sebastian as new memories of him began to fill her mind. He was a bit of a science geek like Mary Elise and had sort of been eyeing her for some time at school. Kellie now thought about heading home and waiting for Katie and Lisa to arrive home so she could return her ring to her. As she was heading home, she realized that the gym was on the way and decided to stop in and see Mika and Claudia for a moment. When she peeked through the window, however, she couldn't spot her friends anywhere and began to walk past the gym when something caught the corner of her eye. She thought for a moment, she had just imagined it, but then when she peeked back to the alleyway, she was correct in what she saw: Mika and Claudia kissing each other.

    "Mika? Claudia?" Kellie asked surprised.

    "Oh shit." Mika said as she and Claudia pushed away quickly from each other.

    "Were you two just...?" Kellie almost asked before Claudia interrupted her.

    "NO, we weren't! Mika was just telling me about a new play she was going to be in and was acting out a scene with me." Claudia explained.

    "Really? What was the play?" Kellie asked them.

    "It's....it's....oh hell, who are we kidding, someone was bound to find out." Mika said taking a deep sigh.

    "Wait, find out? You mean this didn't just happen now?" Kellie asked.

    "Well....not really. See we weren't always best friends. Back before we met you and the other girls, we would be at each other's throats over the dumbest of things. It was only after school one day when we actually sat down and began to talk that we realized we had a lot in common. Then one day during a game of 'Truth or Dare', I dared Mika to kiss me and well.....we kind of kissed for a lot longer than we planned. We didn't really want anyone to know about it and we thought it was just a dumb little crush we got." Claudia explained.

    "So what happened after that?" Kellie asked.

    "We tried to move on dating other people, guys of course. We still liked the guys we dated, and we do still like guys, but in the back of our minds, we couldn't get the other one out of our head. Finally we met secretly with each other and decided not to tell anyone about this. It was our little secret...until now." Mika said crossing her arms.

    "What? You think I'll tell? I swear I won't tell anyone." Kellie said holding up her hand.

    Claudia and Mika looked at each other than at Kellie.

    "Not even Katie?" Claudia and Mika both asked.

    "No, of course not." Kellie said.

    "We can't just take your word. Kellie, swear to us that you won't tell Katie about this until we're ready to tell her." Claudia asked her friend.

    Kellie took a deep breath and thought about it. She really wanted to tell Katie the good news, but she really should wait until Mika and Claudia are ready to tell her and the rest of their friends.

    "I swear I won't tell Katie until you're ready. By the way, why have you guys been meeting in secret like this?" Kellie asked her friends.

    "Our parents." Mika and Claudia both answered.

    "Both of them, really?"

    "Yeah, they wouldn't be too thrilled to find out their kids are bisexual." Claudia said.

    "Yeah. I'm lucky to have such good parents." Kellie said as she was about to leave.

    "Remember, tell no one else until we're ready to tell them." Mika said to Kellie before she left.

    "Promise." Kellie said as she finally left and headed home.
