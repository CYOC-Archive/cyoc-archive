    MaRe went to the direction 60Nain sent her, for her surprise she arrived a giant coliseum and some people waiting to enter. Next to that was 60Nain in a kind of shop.

    "I thought you were going to be late" 60Nain told her.

    "Well, it was easy to come since this place is very recognisable and..."

    Before MaRe finished to talk everyone heard an explosion inside the coliseum, then a giant light shape that turned into a massive Godzilla with six breasts on their chest and bat wings in their back.

    "Wow! What's happening inside there?" MaRe asked surprised by the giant monster that was larger than the stadium.

    "It seems that someone transformed into that, it will be really hard for their opponent to defeat that beast!" 60Nain amazingly said.

    "So we can transform into that kinds of creatures? That's amazing!"

    "Yes! We're here to select our monster cards and register"

    "This seems interesting but is my first time here and don't know how this works"

    "There's a few rules but is easy to understand. The game consists in using the cards we're buying here which each one is a different monster, in the coliseum you'll enter with a deck of them and each turn you'll receive a random card of your deck, you can use the card to transform yourself into the monster the card represents or partially transform into it, like transforming just your arms, head, etc. The objective is to defeat your opponent transforming into the monsters you have, if you attack enough times your rival in their monster form they'll lose that card for the rest of the match, you can also use multiple cards to transform yourself into a fusion of your monsters like a spider lamia or a
    flying slime kraken, but if they defeat you in that form you'll lose all the monsters you used in that fusion. Each monster has their special abilities to make more interesting the fight"

    "Then the cards we are buying here are for our deck so we can fight?" MaRe asked.

    "Exactly! Here you can buy individual cards or buy an existing deck. Since you're a beginner I'll recommend you the mixed deck, which has many monsters from different species"

    "And what are the other decks?"

    "They are for the experienced ones, some people prefer using decks of monsters of the same type, like a deck of insect monsters or sea creatures. They make a synergy between them but it's more difficult to use, the ones who achieve to master them get titles based on that"

    "Sure, I'll go for the mixed deck then. By the way what type is your deck?"

    "It's surprise! You'll know it later hehe"

    After buying the cards, both girls registered in the tournament and entered the coliseum. After some minutes waiting for the rest of the fights to end it was MaRe's turn, she walked the center of the arena and looked around how many people were watching her, then in front of her appeared her opponent.

    Who was her opponent?
