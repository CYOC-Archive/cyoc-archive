    The cyber-kitsune shook her head as she thought over her options, looking over the prone demigod and her new form... Definitely a kitsune of some kind from the vulpine changes and the mention of multiple tails. Not to mention a hungry one at that too, with this sensation. Unlike Mono in that one chapter, B.A doesn't actually know how to gather power with these. Does she plug them into an outlet? Is she supposed to put them in fire or steam or something to gather heat energy? Can she bang these on the ground or have them wave in the wind to gather kinetic energy? Wait. No, it was said to be electrical, and unless if B.A was lucky, she can't just go outside and try to get a tail shocked.

    Well. Beyond all of that, there were these options for B.A to figure out. Frey@ is already inactive! That's good, great even! The centaur potentially will be made to rest for strange eons where even death could die! So B.A could just wander off with a free TFTG and live life from now on. But being in front of Frey@, hearing her voice go from being so tired to being so... Automated... The cyber-kitsune couldn't help but feel sad for Frey@. Probably part of what had inspired B.A's direction for the chapter, as she shook her head.

    Another option would be to gather energy as Frey@ desires, but if the new kitsune gives a millimeter... How many kilometers would Frey@ take to create her perfect world? What would Frey@ do to B.A to ensure that? Commit some mental changes to her? Punish her until she's been programmed to obey Frey@'s demands? Make B.A want nothing more than to constantly give Frey@ second chances or be 'willingly' pulled along to Frey@'s view?

    Plus, there are so many ways that even trying to pick option one to lead to Frey@ winning. Sleeper Processes, New Compulsions, this hunger; maybe she made it so that B.A's power is wirelessly transmitted to Frey@? There's no way for B.A to know... And that's very unpleasant. Especially as this ravenous thirst for energy implies that if B.A doesn't figure out how to wield these tails quickly, she might not have long in general.

    It took her a dozen minutes at most as she chased her small tails, feeling a lingering draw from the LCD. If B.A took the power from it though, then would she be able to return? And at least get some clothes back on? And if she went through, then would she be able to return?

    Choices... Choices... But not one for any readers to pick, as she detached and connected a tail into the monitor. Watching at it grew, and looking back at Frey@ with wariness, the cyber-kitsune beginning to feel that tail drain more and more electricity. More power to recharge herself with, as that hunger was mitigated for right now. The tail returning to B.A once the LCD and its portal turned off, and the Cyber-Kitsune wondered about charging Frey@ up. It took a lot out of Mono, so it was unlikely that just plugging this into... The Charging Port, would refuel her completely.

    But it seemed too late now for much else, and a part of her wanted to get an idea on where to gain more power (and some clothes), as B.A cautiously went behind the sleeping equine and plugged in her now charged up tail. Hoping not too much power would be taken out of herself, as the cyber-kitsune silently fulfilled her directive...

    "...Something something, drone, something something..." She muttered in a bid to break the silence.

    Will this be something for her to regret?
