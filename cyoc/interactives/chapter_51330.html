    that seemed to be asserting itself more and more strongly, and it was

    corrected itself, but his countenance was that of a complete nerd.

    another voice spoke softly in his mind: This is how I’ve always
    looked. I’ve always been a huge nerd. Mitch shook his head in
    frustration. “That’s not me!” he cried out loud. Nevertheless, he
    couldn’t get over the feeling that this was how he was supposed to
    look. But there were still things missing. On cue with that thought,
    he felt a pain rush through his mouth. He opened his mouth wide to
    see that his perfectly white straight teeth had grown crooked. His
    two front teeth stuck out as huge buck teeth, and ugly metal braces
    now covered both levels of his mouth. Metal wires pushed themselves
    out of the braces, and came together behind his head, growing larger
    into a large pad on the back of his neck, completing his new headgear
    set. You’ve got to be kidding me! He thought. His large buck teeth
    and the headgear made it impossible for him to close his mouth without

    “What is going on?” Mitch cried out in despair. He stopped though, at
    the sound of his voice. It sounded higher. “Test…test…” he said
    softly. There was no doubt about it. His voice had gotten
    significantly higher, and had a marked nasally sound to it. “Oh no!”
    he moaned in his new nasally voice. “I’m a complete dweeb!” His

    To complete the ensemble, a large pocket protector appeared in the
    pocket on his shirt, jammed full of pens and pencils. His nose
    started running, and the armpits of his shirt dampened with sweat.
    The feeling that he had always looked this way completely asserted
    itself over his original persona. New memories forced their way in of
    being made fun of at his high school, his late bloomer body, and his
    overall nerdiness. His mind filled with math, science, video game,
    and anime information, replacing all of his old interests.

    Mitch the fine business man was now replaced with Mitch the high
    school nerd.
