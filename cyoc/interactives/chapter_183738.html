    Looking over the map, Jayden spots a path leading to the dormitories of the school.

    “Huh, didn’t know this was a boarding school... don’t know much about it now that I think about it... kinda odd how little anyone can remember about such a massive place..” he remarks scratching his head as he feels briefly dizzy. Shrugging, he pushes his questions to the side for the time being and decides that heading to the dorms would be a pretty good place to start.

    With the mild onset of dizziness, he didn’t notice the small note stating the specific dorm building he got directions to were in fact the Female Dormitory.

    Jayden leaves the main building, using the torch shining from his phone to guide his way through the dark halls of the school. Smashes windows, torn down wallpapers, cobwebs and creaking floorboards. It all seems exactly like some of the R-rated horror movie which Adam’s dared him to watch when they were both younger.

    Jayden shakes his head and pushes forward, reminding himself of the mockery he’ll be sure to receive should he return so soon. Whatever, maybe one of the students left something interesting for him to bring back p, it’s not like they’ll miss it right?

    He exits the building and walks from the large compute before making his way to the nearest dorm building the map pointed to. With some effort, he forcibly pulls the old front door open. Stepping inside he shines his light across the endless hallways of doors, each presumably leading to another student’s home away from home. As he steps inside, a sudden gust of wind slams the door shut behind him.

    “Ah! Damn, that gave me a fright...”

    Looking around he suddnely notices a sign.

    “Girls Dormitory A-1... oh...” with a blush, Jayden turns around. While some guys might explode at a change to explore a girls dorm unimpressed, Jayden wasn’t a pervert. Besides... he doubts he’d be able to bring back anything from here which the guys won’t tease him about anyway.

    He moves to push the door back open, only for it to jam.

    “Dammit, open!” He yells as he continues to push the door, but to no anvil. After some minutes of efforts, Jayden resigns himself to exploring the girls dorm.

    Walking back up through the various halls of doors, Jayden decides to pick one at random and hope he can find a window to climb out of or something.
