    The Magic Shop

    New life as earrings

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LastChance12 7 years ago I S

    ...a pair of earrings. Though you don't remember, this is just like the story you wrote about.

    Slowly, two senses come back. You can now hear and feel touch again, but you're still completely blind, and can't smell or taste anything. Minutes pass. You realize you're going to keep seeing black forever.

    The woman knows very well what you became, but you don't. You feel yourself split in two, your mind located somewhere in the void, feeling touch from two places at the same time. You also feel incredibly tiny, and the slight vibrations of the floor feel like earthquakes to you.

    It's not as you expected it to be... but you've done it. You are now inanimate. Forever. Your deepest fantasy was granted, thanks to the miraculous woman who now owns you. Your owner... that'll get some time getting used to.

    You hear her voice again, now sounding profound and loud. "My, such a perfect pair of earrings." So you're earrings. That's why you feel touch from two places at once. You try to ask her how do you look like, but no words come out. Right...

    "If you're wondering, you're diamonds with a gold chain."

    You're so expensive! It makes you feel a tiny bit more important.

    "It's time for me to decide your fate. And for you, I decide... to not acknowledge you. I'll cast another spell. I'll forget this bunch of events, and to me you will be some earrings I transmuted from a rock or something. I will not know that you're alive. Welcome to your new life: the life of an item, in which for most people, you are perfectly disposable."

    You feel shaking for some minutes, until everything remains quiet. The sound of something closing scares you, and then, a distant "poof". That's it. You're in a box, and she doesn't even know she owns you.

    What do you do now?
