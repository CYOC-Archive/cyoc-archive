    As much fun as he was having Ty was getting board. Then he had an idea. All the
    changes he made to the guys in the gym was from his own fantasies. What if all the gym
    members were able to have their dreams come true on their own. Ty could never change
    everyone that belongs to the gym because he doesn't know who they all are. He would
    have to stay there 24/7 for at least a month to be able see all the members. He could not
    force himself to do that, he was a teenager wizard and he wanted share his "gift" with the
    rest of the world.

    "I just wish that I had a friend here that would take care of the members needs." Ty
    thought to himself.

    At that very moment there was sudden change in air that even Ty didn't notice.

    {All they have to do is make a request and I will see that it is done.}

    Ty looked around to see who had just spoken to him and saw no one looking in his
    direction. "Who said that?

    {I do not have a name.}

    "Where are you?" Ty continues to search the surrounding area for the person that was
    talking to him. "Everyone has a name, what do your friends call you?"

    {What are friends?}

    Ty was getting concerned that he might be loosing his mind. Here he is sitting on a
    stantionary bike at the gym talking to a disenbodied voice. "There must be a hidden
    speaker nearby with a hidden microphone." Ty thinks to himself.

    {There is no such device on that machine on which you are sitting.}

    "What you heard that?" Ty said to himself testing a theory. "Ok FRIEND what do you
    want from me?"

    {Is friend my name?}

    "No, Friend is what people call other people when they don't know their names." Ty says
    in his mind. "This is getting weireder by the minute. Here I am sitting in the gym talking to
    Mr. Nobody."

    {Than is that my name?}

    "What?"

    {My name is Mr. Nobody.}

    "Ok ... does Mr. Nobody have a first name?"

    {What is "a first name"?}

    "Here we go again. Ok try this, what would you like me to call you? Mr. Nobody sounds to
    formal between friends here at the gym."

    {Here at the gym.}

    "No, no, no. That is where we are right now "here at the gym"."

    {Yes that is what people have called me in the past."}

    Than as if someone had pulled back the curtain and let some light into a darkend room it
    hit Ty. "Wait I've been talking to a building! Did I just bring a building to life ...?"

    {Yes Father, I am the building and you have given me a name. My name is now Mister
    Here At The Gym Nobody.}

    "That is a rather big name. How about we shorten it down to Mr. Gym Nobody? Do you
    mind if I call you Gym?"

    {If that pleases you, yes you may call me Gym.}

    "Ok Gym let's see what you can do."
