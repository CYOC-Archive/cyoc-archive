    Enslave him

    added by Drakkenfyre 7 years ago A

    Bruno headed straight home with the bottle jammed in his front pocket. He headed for his Ant Farm. They were mostly brown ants, but there were a few other species. Bruno had learned that ants would either eat or enslave ants of other species. He figured if he made Chad a red ant, he would fight and get eaten, but being a smaller ant, he'd be enslaved. He figured after a few months of being conditioned to be a slave, Bruno could change Chad back, and he'd have his own jock slave boy. That is if Chad wasn't stupid, and managed not to get eaten.

    He held Chad's bottle up, and spoke.

    His voice sounded weird to Chad, but he was still able to make out what Bruno said.

    "See this glass case. It's my ant farm, I've been working on it and expanding it since I was seven. This is your new home, Chad!"

    Chad wandered over to the glass wall and looked in the direction that the blurry hand was pointing. He didn't know whether is new ant eyesight was bad or if the glass wall was distorting the image. He shrugged. He vaguely wondered if all the other ants used to be people too.

    Bruno tapped the bottle to make sure Chad was on the bottom. Then he opened the lid. Next he slid back a panel on the top of the ant farm, and tipped the bottle over the opening. A couple more taps, and Chad went tumbling into the sandy tunnel opening below.

    Chad balked. He smelled Danger all around. He knew that it was sand, but they were like golf ball size, if he was still human-size. He tried to crawl back out, but Bruno slid the panel shut. He turned and saw Bruno's giant eye staring at him through the glass wall of the ant farm. He heard footsteps approaching. A big blackish/brown soldier ant towered over him. He felt like he was seven again being picked on by his 14 year old neighbor. Chad bent his knees backward in an instinctively submissive posture. The ant sprayed Chad with pheromone. He was now a slave on the colony.

    The little pharaoh ant was strong, careful and obedient, and he soon worked his way up from carrying food, to working in the nursery moving ad rotating eggs.
