He was out in public wearing a dress, and he could feel his face turn beat red every time someone walked by. The worst was when an old work colleague of his named Nancy walked by and cooed at Paul. He couldn't feel any more degraded than that.

Finally, they reached the park, and out from her bag Alex pulled a big blanket for the family to sit on. However, when she pulled it out, the camera came out.

    "Yeah it's ours." Rebecca said sadly. "But it's not like anyone in our family is good with photography." She said, recalling the awful family shot they took before.
