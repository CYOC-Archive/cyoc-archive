    Joey awoke again, not really sure when he fell asleep, only that when he woke he didn't understood were it was. He appeared to be laying on dirt, the light was really dim, and he felt odd. There was a window pane on the ceiling distorting the sunlight and some structures on the horizon. He tried to get off the ground but there was this feeling, he felt so light... so light in fact that he was floating, slowly he came to the realization that he was underwater. He frantically swam to the surface trying to gasp for air. When he surfaced the visage he found was pretty weird, there were a vivid vegetation and trees as tall as buildings.

    "Someone has woken up, hey guys the newbie is here." someone called, Joey turned in the direction of the caller and there he saw a boy with a wirey frame and long flowing red hair, he would have mistaken him for a girl, if the tell-tale wasn't evident between his legs, what he fail to notice at first was the butterfly-like wings he had on his back and the tipped ears. "Need a little help pal?" the fairy-boy asked.

    Joey wasn't so sure, but he wanted to get off the water, the fairy help him to were he was standing, which turned out to be a giant lily. "The name is Brock." said the fairy, in a frillish voice.

    "Joey, ehm... Joe." Joey stammered hearing his own underdeveloped voice.

    "That witch got you good eh? Let's meet the others." said as he took flight pulling the lily to shore.

    From the vegetation others emerged, a young boy with bird wings on his back an a mop of curly blonde hair, another looked like a mouse thanks to his prominent teeth, the big ears and had a large tail ending in pom-pom of hair. All of them were naked and it didn't took long for Joey to learn that he was also an that had some new features of his own.

    "Nice fins." commented the rodent-like boy.

    "Fins?" Joey hadn't examined himself, but he soon when he touched his neck he felt a strange membrane around his neck, it hurt to pull it out, it was made of his flesh and in his arms and legs, little orange and green fins. "Where am I? What's this?"

    "Don't you remember? You must have had an encounter with the witch to end up with us." said the cupid.

    Joey made a mental note to remember, yes he was turned into a boy by his babysitter,"But where are we now?"

    "Her garden. She really has a twisted sense of humor, she transform us into somekind of ironic punishment." explained the rodent.

    "She told me I was way to macho, so she stripped me of the muscles I took years in the gym to forge and of my prized manhood and changed it for this useless knob and this girlish form." said Brock the fairy.

    "She called me a baby." Joey said.

    "And she turned you into a water baby." said the rodent.

    "A what?"

    "Think of them as fairies of the sea."
