    "Hmm... I could try this." Dillon looked into the ring. "I'll get bat wings on my arms. They'll be able to support my weight so I can fly with them." Once he was done, the ring glowed once again. Dillon noticed right away his fingers were growing longer and his skin in between was stretching. His middle fingers were disappearing into his hand as his skin stretched in between each of his long fingers. His thumbs and were the only things to stay separate but changed to tiny nubs. "Wait. The ring! Is it there?" To Dillon's surprise, he saw the ring had somehow moved from his ring finger to his thumb.

    Dillon's skin from his arm started stretching all the way down from his muscular arm to his waist. His skin stretched all the way across to his long pinkie fingers. The expanding skin finally stopped and Dillon spread his muscular arms out to get a better look at his wings. "This is too weird. Is this ring really capable of anything?" He asked folding his wing to look at the ring. Dillon didn't mind his wings, but he needed his fingers to stay. "I'll grow fingers on the tip of my wings so I can still hold things." After saying that, fingers at the tip of his wings grew. They were thin like his bat fingers, but they would work.

    Dillon flexed his arms and body, examining his wings closer. "Alright, let's try this than." Dillon started to flap his arms around as he levitated of the ground a few inches. "I'm flying... Holy shit, I'm flying!" Dillon stopped flapping and he face planted face first on the floor. "Ugh... Gotta work on landing..." He said pushing himself up. "Still pretty cool. How far can I go with this thing?"
