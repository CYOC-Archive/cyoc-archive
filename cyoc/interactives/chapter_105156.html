    Not trusting any of the weird-looking doors, you step across the hallway towards the large, rectangular-shaped window. Your shoes clatter on the stone floor, echoing through the empty (you hope) house. You reach the window and pull aside the thin green curtains that were covering it. You see a section of the roof and the porch far below you. Beyond that is the large, sweeping grounds and the river flowing on the right side of the property. You get bored with the view and decide to leave. You turn around.... and scream in shock.

    A young woman, with her hands on her hips, stands in the middle of the hallway of doors, wearing a pair of ripped jeans and a yellow-and-purple flannel rolled up to her elbows. "Wow! You nearly gave me a heart attack!" you laugh. She continues to stare at you, and your laughter dies away quickly. It just dawned on you that you were trespassing in someone's house. "Uh... well, funny story..." you say, putting a hand on the back of your neck and rubbing it. The girl puts a hand up. "No, no. Let me speak." She says. You obediently drop your arms to your sides.

    "I don't know who you are, or what you think you're doing here, but this is my aunt's house and only her house. I want to know how you got in and any damage you've done." She glares at you disapprovingly. "You are a very rude boy." She says, clicking her tongue. "You're going to have to be punished..." You gulp and take a step back. What does she mean by punished?

    She looks up and you and you notice that she's... smiling. Guiltily. "Yes, yes." She says, taking a fish bat from her pocket. "You'll have to be punished indeed..." she smiles at you and takes a step forward. "Hey... take it easy..." you mumble, taking a step back in response. Then, without warning, she rushes forward, raises the bat above her head, and strikes you on the left temple with it. You cry out in pain and grab your head, vision rapidly becoming blurry. "Try not to wake up too soon, it'll be more fun for me..." is the last thing you hear her say before everything fades to gray and you collapse to the stone floor, head swimming in the clouds.

    (TIME PASSES)

    You feel groggy. You open your eyes and your vision is muddy and splotchy. Your wrists and feet hurt, and you feel like you have something in your mouth. Your vision clears and you look down. The tugging of your hands indicates that it's the same story with your hands as well. You bite down in your mouth on something hard and realize the girl has affixed you with a ball gag.

    Something has been written on your chest in lipstick. It's upside down from your angle but you can decipher it anyway: "Rude Boy" it says. Your stomach, chest, and legs hurt, and you see huge red welts all over all three. A discarded whip sits on the floor beside the bed. You try to scream, but the sound is blocked by the gag. You pull on the ropes, but they don't seem to be budging.
