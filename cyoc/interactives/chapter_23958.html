    Miranda must have seen him blushing, because she got up and put her arm around his shoulders, after brushing a long lock of coppery hair out of his face.

    "You're overreacting you know." Sarah commented suddenly. She had the game's rule book in her hand. "It says right here that whoever gets to the end first get's a wish. All we have to do is finish up and one of us can wish you back to normal." She said factually.

    Alex felt some relief at that, but there was still some doubt at the back of his mind. The Old Man hadn't said anything about the game being magic, and he wasn't sure he entirely trusted it. Besides, the game hadn't transformed anyone else yet. Would they all be able to get back to normal?

    "You should just have fun with it!" said Melanie excitedly. "How many guys get to know what it's like to be one of the girls? Besides, it's kind of odd, having a guy here when all the rest of us are female." Sarah nodded enthusiastically at that.

    Alex didn't like that at all. He'd always liked girls, and gotten along well with them, but he didn't want to be one for God's sake! And here these two were, thinking it was funny!
