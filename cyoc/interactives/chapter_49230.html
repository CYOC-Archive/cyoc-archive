    Frank nor Daryl said anything as Kaylee held Daryl as a human shield, Frank had his
    weapon drawn but couldn't risk his partner. And with those claws and muscles Kaylee
    looked quite capable of breaking Daryl's neck with a twist.

    Neither Frank nor Daryl were natives of the twon of Glendale, and thus had no actual
    knowledge of the game Transform or Dare. Frank and Daryl had stupidly assumed Kaylee
    was wearing a costume, this idea was quickly melting away as they looked as her muscles
    move and her skin fold and her eyes blink and the smell she gave off.

    To be fair, Frank and Daryl when they had signed up for the police -were- supposed to
    attention a police briefing on some of the local legends including a particularly dangerous
    game started between some long forgotten spirit and a couple mortals. Both brushed it off
    as a waste of time and wondered why the police department would waste money on it, but
    had never bothered to actually ask.

    This went on for fifty nine more seconds seconds until at last Kaylee said, "Time! Now you
    two get both!"

    Kaylee thought, how small minded Kyle had been, in fact, how small minded everyone
    who had been struck by the curse had likely been. Kaylee remembered the girl who had
    asked him out. Of course Kyle had accepted a date from a hot chick. Then he found out
    that she considered cat to be better than dogs, rabbits, and in particular human beings
    and loved to say how great the musical 'Cats' was. And she had no taste in movies either.
    Eventually Kyle broke off with her.

    He saw her walk into a pet store by chance once, and she picked up a cat and she seemed
    to talk to it! Of course it couldn't talk back until she took it's muzzle off. The cat suddenly
    looked angry and Kyle heard the animal say --words-- 'transform or dare' that was
    enough for Kyle and he walked away very quickly away from the pet store.

    The next time he saw Kathy, he had cats ears and a tail he assumed were fake, as did
    everyone else, and she got in trouble for refusing to take them off. Kyle caught her as she
    left the principle's office crying to gloat. That was when she snarled at him, "TRANSFORM
    OR DARE?" And he had in a panic chosen 'dare.'

    He was suspended two weeks from school for walking around school with his lower body
    exposed doing the highland flying singing song from Westside story. During those two
    weeks, Kathy's family moved away no leaving a forwarding address.

    Finally being allowed back on school grounds the incident with Jennifer happened, and
    Kyle chose to use the power of the curse, setting in motion things that had occurred.

    Where before the two police officers had been at an impasse, now they seemed genuinely
    paralyzed somehow. Perhaps it was the double-double wammy of the curse? Kaylee didn't
    remember any of the myths saying it being able to work on two people at once, but it
    clearly was.

    Kaylee spoke, "First for your dare, you'll call from your squad car that the entire incident
    was just a couple harmless newly bought dogs getting a bit too excited. Nothing to think
    about. Then you'll return to the police station or whatever, turn in your badges so to
    speak, making up a decent excuse for quitting, and come right back here. Then you'll also
    call any family you have and tell them that you need to off and find yourself and may never
    see then ever again."

    "No~!" Groaned Frank who had a wife and two children.

    "Oh Yes!"

    "Y-yes." Stuttered Frank.

    "You will do all of this as efficiently and non-suspcion-rousing as possible. After all that
    is done, you will come back here, and not having revealed anything about me or my
    master, you two will become identical twin humanoid she wolves. You will act the same,
    look the same, think the same, feel the same, be the same. You will love, serve, and obey
    my master. You are totally submissive to all his needs and wants. When he's not available
    you will listen to me as his Alpha Female. Your loyalty and adoration of my master, your
    lord and master is unbreakable and unquestionable. You will use the Transform or Dare
    only to further the master' pack but will be unable to use it until you return here and only
    after your transformation is complete."

    The two police men instantly turned around and went back to their squad car as Kaylee let
    go of Daryl. The two police men did precise as they had been told reporting the incident
    was just some pets after all and was nothing and drove directly back to the police station.

    Kaylee went back inside, sniffing and looking around making sure none of the neighbors
    had seen her and went back inside to inform The Big Bad Wolf of these developments.

    "Good afternoon master Jensen." Said Kaylee perkily into the Big Bad Wolf's ear.

    "Jensen?" The Big Bad Wolf opened his eyes slowly at his smiling mate.

    "Well you renamed me master, I feel it's only fitting that I rename you... unless... of course,
    master doesn't LIKE the name Jensen?"

    The Big Bad Wolf thought about it for a moment, then shrugged. "Sounds like a good a
    name as any."

    "I have new master. Two police came after hearing our love making, but I was able to use
    the game to make them report it was nothing, and they'll return as two new she wolves for
    your pack."

    Jensen groaned, "Kaylee! Why didn't you wake me?!"

    "Because you ordered me to let you sleep master."

    Jensen groaned again, Kaylee could still be as dense as Kyle. "I wanted you to save the
    game for when your parents got home! Then we'd go back to my family's mansion and use
    the game on -my- parents and then tomorrow we'd use the curse on our family maid
    Martha."

    "Do not worry master! I told them to come back as quickly as they could after they quite
    their job and told their family that they were going on a long long trip likely forever."

    Jensen sighed, "You had better hope Kaylee that they get back before your parents do."
