    Cassie gets Ruby on the changing table and pops open her diaper to much disappointment. She did not need to poop this time around, and only flooded the diaper.

    She looks at Alex and Hoshi, "Well I guess we need to have some fun another way"

    "I'm going to put 3 small legos in three of the diapers in the pail. You're both going to go exploring the diapers one by one until you find each of them."

    "I'll drop Ruby's diaper with you two into the bottom of this clear storage crate, then pour all the diapers in the the pail on top."

    "When you find a piece, one of you must climb to the top and show me while the other remains in the diaper it was found in."

    "You have one hour, and if you succeed I'll take you out of the box and get you two cleaned up and ready for some more games with the toddlers and myself. If not I'll pour the crate back into the pail and you'll be stuck there until its emptied this evening."

    Alex protests the sudden turn of events while Hoshi is gleeing in antisipation for this new game.

    "Or I could just leave you in the pail and no games."

    Alex sighs and agrees, Cassie then tapes the wet diaper back on Ruby.

    "Just a moment while I set it up then."

    She grabs a few diapers out the pail and untapes them next to Ruby, using a wipe she pushes a small Lego piece into the mush of the first before balling it back up. She repeats this for two more before marking each one inconspicuously with a sharpie.

    She adds them back to the pail before removing Ruby's diaper and cleaning her up.

    Ruby's wet diaper is then loosely balled with the two before being thrown in the bottom of the empty crate. Cassie then grabs the pail and pours the other diapers over the two. The lid is then clicked in place trapping them in

    Alex and Hoshi emerge from the diaper to a mountain of mess covering them. They have no choice but to get started searching.

    They work together to pull open the tape of the nearest diaper and work their way in.
