    "Okay, I think we both know what's best... right?" Jake asked Mark.

    "Definitely." Mark agreed.

    "Mine." They both said simultaneously. "What?" They both repeated again.

    The other girls heard this, and al began to giggle.

    "Aww, that was too funny!" Kendra laughed.

    "Totally!" Taylor agreed.

    "Yeah, maybe you two are the twins." Mary added.

    The three girls laughed, leaving Mark and Jake to sit there angrily at both each other and the kids they were forced to be with. Jake felt his face blush slightly, even though he wouldn't have normally been embarrassed by this, his new body clearly was. He turned his head, expecting to see Mark equally annoyed, but was surprised to see the small Vietnamese girl that was now Mark smirking.

    "Sorry... I mean, that is so us huh? Always butting heads... even as little girls we're still stubborn guys." Mark quietly explained as he began to chuckle himself.

    The infectious laughter finally reached Jake, as he realized the humor in the situation. He was some adorable black girl talking and acting exactly like a teenaged white male. He started to laugh, a little feminine but at that point it didn't't matter. Jake felt relief, wondering if they truly needed to be as quick as possible. After all, if they messed up they could always come back right?

    Suddenly, Mark stopped. Jake followed his gaze and his heart sank as well. While they laughed, Mary's mother Sarah had walked over to the shelf and grabbed the camera from its place. She was smiling, already holding the camera as if ready to take a picture. All relief vanished as Jake remembered just how awful it was to be a doll, and just how screwed they would be without the camera.

    "You girls are too precious!" Sarah laughed, just as a bright FLASH erupted from the camera. Jake and Mark could only brace for impact, hoping their changes wouldn't be too horrible.
