    Mad Science

    Furry side effects from the injection

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 13 years ago A BM

    Suddenly, you feel very dizzy. "Relax.", said the scientist. "We are performing research, but it is genetic, not medicinal. We injected you with a DNA sample from a wolf. You will not be allowed to leave until our observations are complete. But even then, what good would leaving do you?" He grins maliciously as two men grab you by your arms, taking you somewhere. You try to break free, but there's no hope. The injection has made you too weak. They throw you into a room and shut the door.

    Looking around you, the room you are in has no doors or windows. A bed sits in the corner and a mirror sits on the wall, but besides that, there's nothing in the room. You slam your fist on the wall, frustrated. Why would they do this to you? Money doesn't matter now. They're holding you prisoner here. What did the doctor mean by "What good would leaving do you?"

    Suddenly, a strange feeling flows through your body. All your hair stands on end. Looking in the mirror, your hair takes on a grayish tint. Your arms are getting hairier, gaining the same gray color. Your arms are soon covered in hair, which spreads all over your body. You grab your head as pain shoots through your whole body. Your feet grow a bit larger, breaking through your shoes. Your feet are now covered in gray fur, with paw pads growing on the bottoms of your feet. Your toenails extend into claw-like forms. By now, fur is all over your body. Your hands grow claws like your feet, but don't gain the paw pads. Your legs and arms become more muscular. You feel the most excruciating pain as your rear end feels like it's being pierced by something. A tail bone somehow generated, creating a furry, wagging tail. You scream, but no one hears you. Finally, your face finishes you off; Your ears shift to the top of your head, becoming pointier. Your face extend into a snout, complete with sharp fangs. The pain causes you to pass out.

    You wake up some time later. The pain is still there, but much smaller. You stand up and look in the mirror. What seems like a cliche werewolf character stares back at you. "Well, well. It seems the injection worked better than expected." You look around for the voice, only to find an intercom speaker. "Amazing. Your body morphed into a completely different species. And only because of the presence of wolf DNA!" Without warning, you growl at the intercom. "Don't be mad, you signed up for the job. And you're a scientific breakthrough. We'll continue observing you, making other injections as needed..." The intercom turned off. You fall to the ground, sobbing. How could they have robbed you of your humanity like this?
