Looking down you realize that it has now become a sword and your crotch has sealed up to the same hairless leather as your legs.

    As the leatherisation reaches upwards it creates a red ring of leather forming into a belt, separating what now looks like a pair of trousers from the rest. Above it your skin turns azure as it turns into leather. Feeling around it you notice the same construction with metal plates as in your legs. On your front two rows of 5 golden buttons emerge from the leather which also spreads over to your arms. Some changes seem to happen on your back, shifting your weight considerably, but you are unable to make them out, except for a sleeveless coat emerging from it. As the leather reaches your hands, another colour change takes place changing them into bright red and causing a fold to appear making them look like gloves. Suddenly you find that it's actually easy to extend them and it seems like you could even start the engine, which might be a bad idea indoors. Despite seemingly wearing a tight set of leathers you are able to move rather well. You start pulling on what looks like your boot and it comes off. However instead of the usual foot there's nothing in it, in fact as you reach into the stump you realize that it is completely empty, yet you can feel your fingers from inside of your leg. You put on your boot again and try the same thing with one of your hands, it too comes off leaving nothing but a hollow stump behind.
