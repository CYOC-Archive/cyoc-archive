    What you saw was the jerky grainy image one of the Founders
    had shot on September 7 1970
    as the star dragons had made good on their promise
    to return to Earth to
    Sarpent Island.

    The Founders voice as he or she narrated the historical event did a pretty good job
    of jogging your somewhat dull reptillian brain.

    Basically the Founders had believed that on September 7 1970 the star dragons
    would arrive on Sarpent Island ( just off Scotland)
    aboard the Mother Ship and haul the Founders off to some Heavenly paradise
    whilst all other humans perished when the Earth was destroyed.

    For once the Prophecy was fulfilled and a fairly huge triple ringed like craft with Saturn V like rocket engine came down from the Heavens,lit up the area and landed on Sarpent Island.

    Out of this craft came hundreds of anthro dragons,both sexes
    and imparted the Knowledge imparted through beams of light swirling out of
    prisms atop those rockets
    to the Founders.

    Then the star dragons headed aboard their ship again and headed off back to
    space,without taking a single Founder
    with them.

    Not that the somewhat traumatized and dazed Founders would
    have noticed.

    However as the cult off 33 Sarpent Islanders got back to their feet
    the Knowledge told them
    a)how to build a triple ringed Saturn V like spaceship
    b)how to turn themselves into anthro star dragons,both sexes
    c)the reason the Earth would destroy itself

    Naturally those Founders hadn't acted on that Knowledge but when they had babies
    discovered the Knowledge had been passed into the kids.

    So the human population of Sarpent Island had grown,but not their
    ability to put the Knowledge to any practical use.

    Its one thing to how inner visions of the genetic triple helix of a star dragon in your
    brain,another thing to actually turn human DNA into star dragon TNA.

    Nor did building a spaceship out of concrete like material appeal to them.

    So they contented themselves breeding and multiplying,putting their dear departed parents into
    cryogenic tubes for the eventual space trip
    and doing sonar surveys around the island,which showed all
    manner of underwater triple ringed buildings
    under the calm waters of the North Sea.

    Then came 2025 and it all changed.

    A ring of fire surrounded the state of California and that land mass slipped into the interior of the Earths molten seas in a matter
    of hours.

    The Domino Effect caved in and in a matter of days all of North America had submerged in that glowing sea
    of superheated liquid rock.

    Suddenly the Knowledge didn't seem so useless after all and the Sarpent Islanders got busy
    building that concrete space ship.

    A smaller group got to work figuring out how to
    turn humans into anthro dragons.

    Most people on the island assumed they'd be travelling aboard
    <Star Dragon> as human colonists.

    The select few imagining they all be anthro star dragons were
    doing that work because the calculations were showing that
    when <Star Dragon> fired up her 33 engines
    their human bodies could never stand the forces they'd be experiencing.

    They all needed big stout reptillian skeletons and stuff if they were
    going to get off a dying Earth alive.

    In a few generations they might have perfected the TF process but when the Earth gave those final death rattles
    in September 7 2040 AD the research had only got to the point where a
    human could be turned into a rather huge
    feral western female dragon with a slightly less then desireable IQ.

    The Knowledge was going to go "POOF"
    as a result of the transformation.

    So all 500 Sarpent Islanders stampeded aboard the <Star Dragon> in those final days,
    attached the IV tubes as they were instructed,didn't
    protest when the flight attendants went about injected\people with what they
    were assured were
    "sedatives"

    All 500 humans (and their dear departed Founders in their cryotubes)
    were knocked out cold and began to get bigger,stouter,more muscular and unkilleable
    aboard the silent <Star Dragon>

    If the timing had been off and <Star Dragon> had still been on Sarpent Island
    when Earth caved in
    that would have been the end.

    However all 500 partially transformed feral dragonesses
    in their dream minds
    suddenly started dreaming a giant had dropped a boulder
    on their wings at the same time as they
    were trying to keep their eggs from sliding out of that
    vent between their rear legs.

    That of course was the moments when <Star Dragons> engines burst to like and lit up a dying night sky as 500 still
    transforming feral dragones were hurled into space.

    Thirty three minutes later,the rear view cameras recorded the last fiery glow of a dead Earth.

    No one had told you beforehand that when you woke up,you wouldn't be human anymore.

    You'd assumed you'd be a human guy,a technician tasked to keep those engines
    going.

    So as you fully awakened to the tiny bits of human uniform still draped here and there
    over your still growing and transforming scaly hide
    you were wondering...

    What do you do now?
