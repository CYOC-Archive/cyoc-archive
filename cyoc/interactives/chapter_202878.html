    Time to relax

    "I see that my little girl has just woken up"

    Marcia turned to look at who had said that and saw that it was a tall lady who proceeded to take her out of the crib. She also saw that the lady was wearing a colorful uniform and a name tag that said "Betty".

    Marcia tried to ask the lady where she was but all she could say was "Goo Gaa Gaa?". Oh no, she had gotten younger and now she couldn't speak anymore.

    "Aawww, You're too cute when you babble in your baby talk", the lady told Marcia as she began to carry her to another room.

    Soon the lady took Marcia to another room which seemed to be a playroom designed for babies and toddlers.

    The lady then proceeded to lower Marcia onto the soft, colorful floor of the room.

    "Have fun with all the toys in the room while your nanny prepares your food. And if you get bored, there's Rusty to keep you company."

    Soon, the lady proceeded to slowly close the door of the room.

    "Wait, my nanny?" Marcia asked herself. Suddenly, Marcia finally got it. She was now at what appeared to be the home of a nanny, who goes by the name "Betty".

    Why Christine thought it was a good idea to leave her with a nanny? Marcia could be more upset about this but at least Christine assured her that maybe her transformation could be only temporary so maybe she should just accept it and relax a bit.

    Marcia began to crawl all over the place and saw that the immense amount of toys and activities that were there. There was everything: blocks, stuffed animals, dolls, a rockin' horse, a baby walker, a baby jumper, etc.

    But there was a question that began to arise for Marcia: Where was Rusty?

    Betty mentioned him so Marcia assumed that he should also be in the room. But suddenly, something strange started to happen.

    While she was distracted, she began to feel that someone was starting to sniff her from behind. Turning around, she saw that it was nothing more and nothing less than a small dog.

    "Where did this little animal come from?" Marcia wondered.

    Taking a closer look she saw that he had a necklace that said "Rusty". Wait a second... Rusty? Was this canine her partner? But how did this happen?

    Suddenly, Marcia began to remember something. When they had just arrived at Christine's mansion, Rusty had gone to investigate the Men's Wings in which there was supposedly a Hellhound hanging around. Rusty may have been attacked and cursed by said creature. That might explain why Rusty was now a little dog.

    "Ga?" Marcia told the little animal as she pointed at him.

    Rusty in response barked at Marcia and proceeded to wag his tail a lot.

    Marcia happily began to giggle and clap her hands. She was happy that Rusty still recognized her.
