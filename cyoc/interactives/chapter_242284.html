    Damon looked around, a bit embarrassed. Actually, he was feeling rather sheepish. He knew that Madame Illusia could do magic, but did he dare tell her what he greatly desired? There were an awful lot of people here and these included Monica, his girlfriend. What would she think once he heard him?...
    "Well... I... Actually, I... I wanted... er..." he stammered.
    "Yes?..." said Madame Illusia.
    She was smiling widely, her eyes twinkling with happy expectations. That boy was so cute! Whatever he wanted, Madame Illusia swore to herself that she would give him a happy life, even if she had to alter his mind a little. Yet her motherly and kind demeanor gave off good vibes to Damon so that he started trusting her completely.
    "I... I want to be a mythological creature," he said.
    "How exciting!" exclaimed Madame Illusia. "And easily done! But what would you like to be?"
    Damon smiled in turn, overjoyed by Madame Illusia's reaction. She had understood him! Now he just had to tell her which creature he liked and wished to be transformed into.
