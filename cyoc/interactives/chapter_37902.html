Emma, Suzy, and Heather had come over looking for Eric and Jack. I showed them the tape I made of you guys turning into dogs. I thought that it was funny and that they'd get a laugh out of it. I didn't know how far Emma would take it. After she figured out how to use the Chronivac she insisted on

After Emma had zapped me I wanted to punish you guys and I was having fun. I swear that I didn't know Emma would do that! I'm so sorry! To think that you could have been stuck as dogs and an ass forever like Emma had planned. Or worse, being killed like Emma had evidently planned now. Tony, Lisa, I'm sorry that I forced you to have sex." She sat down on her bed and buried her head in her hands.

    "That wasn't you" Jeff said. "I need to know something. Did Emma also scan Heather and Suzy?"

    "No" Suzy said. "She said that I was the only one that needed to be protected. That I was ideally placed in case you did something."

    "Well it looks like you get three new dolls" Lisa said.

"Well they're dolls now" Jeff said. "Emma won't be giving us any more trouble. I've also made it so they are aware and their families don't remember them. Take good care of them Suzy."

    "I will" Suzy said. "Are you going to leave them like this?"
