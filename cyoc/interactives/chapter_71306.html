"Anyways, you aren't seriously going to get that, are you?" Lina smiled again, and nodded her freckled face. "Oh come on, it's some funny off brand stuff!"

    "I know, that's why. I love trying out weird things like this." Lina said enthusiastically. Her hand brushed over the plastic sealing the lid, noticing something seemingly wrapped under it. She shrugged, and turned. "Did you find anything?"

"You'll definitely enjoy this." He said as he bagged it, and handed it back. Lina was looking at the jar and bag the entire time, so failed to notice, that the entire time the old man appeared to be addressing Amy. She shivered a bit, hoping that maybe this old asian man had a lazy eye, or was blind, and not really looking her way with that gaze.     "Italian? He was totally Asian." Amy turned to look at Lina this time, stopping. Lina walked on for a few steps more, before halting and turning back.
