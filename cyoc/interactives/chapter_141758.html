    Masami was pleased with her transformation and having thoughts of pleasure.

    The second she thought of having herself dreaming of Greg and what he was going to turn into as a girl, a note slipped under the door. She went to the door and picked up the note.

    She opens the note up. The note reads, “You won’t be going far, because there is a second stage to the transformation. Look for the blue button that says “Plastic” and push it on the wall above the bench. The second stage will start but you must stand perfectly straight in order for it to work”

    Thoughts start to pop on Masami, “A button on the wall above the bench that says “plastic” ?”. She is wondering, “Is this place is trying to turn me into a plastic figure of a geisha?”

    She walked to the bench. On it there was a fan, a headdress, and and another note. It said, “The button that will start the second stage is on the other side of this wall. Press the red button to make the piece turn so you can press the blue button to begin stage 2.” Another note was to the left of the fan. It said, “For the fan and headdress, you are also going to need them.” Masami grabbed the fan and opened it up. It was the same color as the kimono with the white butterfly design on it. She put the fan down and picked up the headdress. The headdress was to tie up her long black hair and had blue flowers on it

    She pressed the red button and the wall turned. On the other side was a black circular platform with rectangles marking her sandals and her name in gold on the front. She grabbed the headdress and put it by the accessories bin so it can be put on during the transformation. She then grabbed the fan and placed it in her left arm.

    “Well, here goes nothing.” Masami pressed the blue button that said “plastic” on it, stepped on the platform so her sandals would be locked in place, held out her left arm to open her fan up, and closed her eyes. She heard a voice, “Commencing phase two of transformation in 10, 9, 8..” . As the countdown occurred, a blackplastic cylinder raised and covered Masami. “6, 5, 4..” Masami knew that what the voice was saying, the machine was going turn her into a figure of an actual geisha. The voice finished with, “3, 2, 1. Commence phase 2”. The machine started up and the second phase of the transformation started.

    Masami’s hair rolled back into huge buns as the headdress was sucked in and was placed on top of her hair. Then, the two pieces of fabric that were cut below the obi merged together and the skirt on Masami’s kimono grew longer till it reached the level of her sandals, covering her legs and the butterfly pattern on the sleeves grew on the longer skirt. An arm came down and used water and a facecloth to remove the blush and red mark on her lips from her face. The arm then opened up and a brush came out. With it on, the brush went and painted Masami’s face white. After that, the arm applied red blush on her cheeks and put lipstick on and make her lips blue to match the kimono. Masami opened her eyes and noticed the machine has turned her into a geisha. However that was just the first part.

    Part two involved turning Masami into a figure. The machine took everything off and put a plastic insta-film over her entire body so she would look realistic, then put everything back off. The film sunk into her skin so every part of her body would become stationary. Her legs, already locked due to the platform, became stationary, followed by her torso and her right arm. The next part was her left arm so the fan would stay in place. Lastly, her head became stationary like the rest of her body.

    “Phase two complete”, said the machine. The black cylinder dropped and it showed the result: Masami becoming a life-sized geisha figure. One Body Benders staff member walked by and saw the figure. She was amazed by it. “I think I’ll make this geisha figure a good addition to the front lobby”. She picked it up and brought it by the front desk. Masami knew that she would spend the rest of her life as a figure no matter where she would go.
