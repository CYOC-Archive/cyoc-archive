    Now General Arthurious had spent the next few years puzzling out how to return the evil
    Dannisigilnationous back to the less imposing and probably less good then before
    Danny of the Sigil.

    Things had gotten a little more complicated because Danny of the Sigil and the General's
    daughter Princess Marian had been once in love.

    Naturally the Dragon had kept kidnapping the <female hairless ant ape> named Marian
    and mysterious when the evil monster kept allowing her to escape back to her father.

    Whilst the Dragon gobbled up other hairless ant apes with no remorse,including the
    Generals only son,a foolish young knight,the former thief turned monster seemed to have a
    relatively soft spot for Marian.

    The General knew that King John kept the Dragon's Eye and Clarence's
    skull safely locked up under triple barred iron doors,with many a warding spell
    to keep the object secure from ever being used again.

    That had puzzled the General,and he'd consulted with various wizards,and discovered
    why King John probably kept the thing locked up.

    The Dragon Eye could only be used once,then a new dragons eye had to be pried
    loose from the skull of a dragon to replace it,a dragon that used to be the last user
    of the previous Dragon Eye.

    Hence King John faced the prospect that one day he would be transformed into a
    dragon,slain,and his altered huge reptillian eyeball cut out,to form the next
    Dragon Eye.

    So King John had taken to banning all dragon transformation technology
    from the kingdom,including the locking up of the now lifeless Dragon Eye.

    So when he'd seen Amanda and the other kid turn into white dragons,he'd
    naturally freaked out,since it meant they possessed a dragon tf power he was
    unaware of and had no control over.

    He'd shivered mightily at the transformation,but had played along,as he
    tried to forestall the Dragon Doom the use of the Dragon Eye on Danny of the
    Sigil would visit apon him soon...
