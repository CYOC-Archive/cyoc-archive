Just as planned it had made her quite the hit at parties. She was Carol’s best and longest friend (occasionally with benefits).

They could've just gone to the next best workshop to get Carol’s very first mods, but being an unmodded cyborg at her age had become a rarity in their circles and so her friends had demanded that they had to do it somewhere special. Thankfully the perfect opportunity was just around the corner. The Prosthetic-Modification-Convention, short ProMoCon, a big convention centered around the rapidly growing prosthetic modding community was being held in their town. What better place to get her very first mods than there. Especially because it wasn't far by train.

A man with several hydraulic pipes sticking out of his arms was easily carrying crates larger than himself. One woman was invoking the image of a harpy with her winged arms and bird legs, while another had her limbs encased in what seemed to be big paw-shaped.
