    "My lord I conjured in the floating swords and was controlling them using one of my abilities that I possess naturally which is telekinesis the movement of objects with my mind over multiple objects, as for the exploding swords I could give order to them with this ability and told them to combust that very moment." You explained
    "So they're abilities that only you possess the comand and manipulation of objects combined with magic am I right?" Lord Ash asked
    "Correct and it is likely for the other guardians to have abilities not necessarily similar but equally as unique." You predicted
    "Yes this is a problem now what is your other ability I know guardians have two." Lord Ash said
    "You other ability is that you can restore mana faster the more damage you take in while remaining conscious." The voice said
    "My other ability I refer to it as critical mana, because of it I can regenerate my mana faster when I withstand damage the more damage I can withstand without being knocked unconscious the faster my mana can regenerate." You said
    "I see both of your abilities seem equally as useful as one another, but completely unique as your the only one who possesses such powers." Lord Ash commented

    "No you are dismissed," Lord Ash said
    You stood up and were going to leave when you realized that you had no food in your house so you decided to ask around town, and most said that they hunted so you did the same. You went into the woods and hunted a deer. You had no idea what to do after that until a male helped you out and showed how to skin it, you thanked him and went home with a chunk of venison that you cooked later that evening. You then heard someone knock on your door and when you answered it was Flame.
