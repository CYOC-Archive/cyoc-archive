And they were even bigger than him even now. Though... Cody thought about it, he already looked really good. What would amplifying his musculature and size even more do to him? What if he ended up---

Nothing could be as bad as barely being able to lift the bar in the gym. He needed to further his advantage and really take this home. With that kind of mindset coming through to him, Cody fired up the Chronivac again.

Moving over to the weight, he took it up to 108.8 kg, which he guessed would put him at an even better weight. He would be bigger than even some of the Instagram guys and he would never have to look bad in front of girls or guys again. He made sure he was good to go and then engaged the Chronivac to start the transformation.

    The machine informed him that "to acquire desired change, additional changes will need to be applied. Do you consent?"

    Cody didn't even bother thinking about it and clicked through that "yes";

    He immediately felt a hot sensation come over him as his already bulky shoulders shredded the sides of his shirt, filling into huge boulder shoulders that required multiple XXL shirts and specialized uniforms besides if he went into any particular job. His back swelled and broadened, forming huge lats and rear delts, Cody's trapezius muscles swelling up the way a lot of those weightlifters had them. His arms continued bulking up, losing some slight tone but piling on the bulk and developing a huge 21 inch look, his triceps fighting and following the cue of his biceps.

    His chest outright ripped the front of his shirt as the pecs likewise demanded a specialized shirt design just to hold them in, and again it seemed he lost a slight amount of tone but gained substantial size. His abdomen filled in and his abs pushed out into a pronounced form, each abdominal looking thick and strong. His obliques almost looked etched in stone.

His glutes were huge, and pushed out the back enough to damage his already severely strained boxers. He chuckled...and noted his voice was deeper.

His face was more squared and macho, and he almost winced in recognition of a similar facial structure belonging to Jason, a high school senior who had been bullying him. So he looked kinda like Jason now...a high school senior. He was also taller - yeah, 6 foot 3 was incredibly fucking tall for the former wallflower.

Honestly, for Cody it was a side of Jason he'd never gotten to know. He frowned a little. He wondered what to do...
