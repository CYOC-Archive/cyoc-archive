    "How the....?" ask Sam bewildered by the result.

    A screen had come up with a list of animals and percentages next to them. At the top of the list was wolf. This puzzles you as well.

    "Wolf?" you read aloud

    "Hmm interesting..." Sam says leaning back raising her hand to her chin "I know the term 'Bitch' can refer to any canine of the female gender but rarely it is a wolf...."

    "Is that bad?" you ask worriedly

    "Huh? Oh no actually this works to our advantage" Sam replies "Being a wolf type you won't be as....lets say domesticated as dogs. This means that you actually managed to alter the path of your transformation as you only got a handful of A-105.Hmmm"

    Unable to wrap your head around how this is a good thing you stand up sighing.

    "So what happens now?" you ask rubbing your shoulder

    "Now?" Sam replies sitting up "We stop the transformations from progressing any further"

    "How?" you ask turning around to look at her

    "Oh it's quite simple really" She says smirking slightly "The pleasure island scientists made a cure for A-105. Precautions you see, didn't want an experiment to fail and turn the scientists into animals right?"

    "Cure?" tilting your head quizzically as you ask

    "Well not a cure per Se" Sam says getting up and opening a nearby safe "It is only a cure from up to 10-20% change rate, and even that is dependent on each individual. However anytime after that and the changes that have occurred stay while any future ones do not happen"

    She closes the safe and turns around. You see she is holding a vial with a glowing green liquid inside.She pick up a nearby syringe and places the green liquid inside it.

    "It has to be inject, unlike the powder, as it has to get rid of the chemicals inside your body to stop it from doing any further changes" she says as she ties your arm off again, swabbing the area "It will sting and after the injection you will feel slight burning sensations all over your body"

    "Ouch!" you exclaim as she injects the substance into you. Immediately your arm starts to get warm, from where she injected, and it spreads all over your arm then up your shoulder. Soon it encompasses your whole body and, while not painful,it is uncomfortable. It even seems to be burning the joints in your arms and legs. After a couple minutes the burning sensation starts to ebb and you relax as you feel the coolness of the metal table as you sit on it, you realise that you had been sweating during the process.

    "Eww I'm all sweaty now" you exclaim wiping your forehead

    "Yes the vaccine does have that effect" Sam replies shutting down her computer after typing something down "It makes you sweat out the chemicals. Don't worry though it won't be able to chan ge you or anyone else now, it's been converted to harmless sweat now"

    You smirk at her relieved that you will no longer have to worry about changing into an animal anymore.

    "So I suppose you would like a shower after the ordeal you've been through today wouldn't you?" ask Sam as she walks towards you

    "Aww a shower would be VERY welcoming right now" you say exuberantly, getting up from the table

    "Right then, follow me" Sam says leaving the room

    You follow her out into a corridor that branches the the right and the left. Now that you can see outside the lab you can see that the walls and the ceiling are made out of metal while the floor was some sort of stone. Heading down the right corridor you walk by doors that have plaques with names next to each room, basically naming the purpose of the room and who works in it. The room you came out of had a plaque that said:

    Lab 1
    DNA testing and vaccination.
    Dr. Samantha Darelle

    The rooms that followed where mostly labs with names of other Dr's or examination rooms. Reach another branch in the corridor Sam heads down the left one, which is wider than the last. You notice the walls are now cement ones and the floor has intricate designs. There are more rooms down this corridor but they are more like dorm rooms now, showing the dorm Number and the occupants. Reading a few you read one that says:

    Dorm #6
    Kat

    Looking through the opaque glass you conjecture that Kat didn't go back to her room. 'So where did she go?' you think to yourself. Running to catch up to Sam you quickly look at the other dorm names and see some outlines of shapes in the rooms but cannot make distinct objects or people from them, well until you get to room 12. You see, what seems like, a person pressed against the glass moving up and down it, you also hear what sounds like moaning from the other side....you hurry on blushing slightly as you become aware of what the occupants are doing.

    "Right here we are!" Sam declares startling you from your thoughts

    Sam is standing in front of two doors with a sign that says it big and bold letters: SHOWERS

    "The one on the left is the girls, the one on the right is for boys" She tells you "Since you are new I will only allow you to be inside there for now so not to...frighten you of the other people here. They can be a bit-"

    She is cut off from the far off sounds of two screaming in, what sounds like, ecstasy. You think that it may be the two you saw in room 12.

    "...I guess that explains it better than I could" Sam says shrugging "Anyway you will find all the things you will need inside. I will be back with some clean clothes for you."

    "Wait" you say to her "How do you know there is now one in there?"

    "The showers are usually locked at this time" Sam explains pulling a key out. She unlocks the door and lets you in "So not to allow 'misuse' of the facilities. Now I'll be back soon so hurry up and get washed. Afterwards I'm going to explain everything 'kay?"

    "mmhmm" you reply as you go in

    "Right then!" Sam says "I'll be back ASAP"
