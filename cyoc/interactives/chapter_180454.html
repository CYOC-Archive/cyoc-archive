    Another pamphlet wound up in the mail again for yet another game show. It seemed like there was no end to them, and they all seemed kinda sketchy. Supposedly they were reaching out to the public for anyone willing enough to participate in some incredibly strange sounding game shows that were being streamed online. No entry fees were listed, and all of them had absolutely outlandish premises.

    Some examples included:

    Roll Out
    Contestants are inflated into limbless balls that must escape an obstacle course filled with all kinds of inflation themed traps.

    The Big Question
    A trivia quiz show where right answers make you giant and wrong answers shrink you.

    American Beast King
    Contestants progressively transform into anthros as they complete athletic challenges.

    Price of Love
    A show about guessing the prices of various products to earn points towards making yourself sexier.

    Wheel of Metamorphosis
    Contestants must correctly guess various phrases letter by letter to earn all kinds of random transformations.

    There were a lot more but you couldn’t remember all of them off the top of your head. You decided that enough was enough and for once you should try one of these out. The pamphlets touted all kinds of cash prizes, so you figured you had everything to gain and nothing to lose if you at least tried one.

    Little did you realize that in the fine print of every pamphlet was a warning that any transformation you experience could potentially be permanent. You had no idea what you were in for.

    Now, what show are you gonna be the next contestant on?
