    You think for a moment looking over the list of VR titles till one catches your eye.

    “What’s ‘Godhood VR about?” You ask.

    “An excellent question.” The voice responds selecting the title for you.

    Soon the dark void was replaced with a field of clouds under a yellow sky where the game’s title hovered, now much more stylized.

    “God hood is the latest divine sand box game by GV studios.” The voice began. “In this game you play as a god of varying power depending on the difficulty.”

    A menu appeared showing the options.

    “Customize your appearance or appearances of your various avatars in the mortal world or worlds. Your appearance determines your followers.”

    Next option appeared.

    “Play online and compete for worshipers in MULTI PLAYER MODE. The more worshipers you have, the more your power grows. Join pantheons with other gods & goddesses, or declare crusades against your rivals. Chose your chosen people and see how many followers you can get before the end of time.”

    The last option appeared.

    “Or create your own world from scratch in CREATIVE MODE. Plot out the evolution of every creature or spawn them in. If that’s not your style bring in creations from other worlds made by other gods.”

    The menu zoomed away displaying all the options before you.

    “In Godhood VR the only limit is your own WILL.” The voice finished.

    With the presentation over you decided to hit enter.
