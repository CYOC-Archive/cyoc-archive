    Search for the Sorceress' Ring

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by loony28 14 years ago O

    Your life has been an ordinary one so far but that is about to change. On the day your
    life changes you find a ring and pick it up. The ring looked like a cheap one that young
    teen girls would wear. Ordinarily you wouldn't consider putting it on nut for some
    reason you couldn't resist doing just that. Once you put it on you realized it wasn't an
    ordinary cheap ring as you were transported into a house.

    "Welcome to my house," a feminine voice said from out of nowhere.

    You looked around trying to find who was talking but you did not see anyone and then
    you asked, "Who are you?"

    "For now you may call me Sorceress," the voice said.

    "Ok Sorceress," you said looking around again, "why am I here?"

    "Why you're here to receive almost limitless power," Sorceress said. "All you have to
    do is find my ring which is gold with the symbols of the planets on it and set with an
    emerald."

    "There has to be a catch," you said.

    "Finding my ring won't be easy," Sorceress said. "It is well hidden in my house which
    may look like a regular house to you at the moment but there are numerous rooms
    and they're not always rooms. You will be transformed while you're searching for my
    ring. If you are transformed into an object then you'll become a permanent resident in
    my home. You'll also become a permanent resident if you should happen to find me.
    You may also be killed during your search like this young girl was."

    The air rippled in front of you and you were watching a 13 year old girl being dragged
    into a clearing. The girl was struggling and yelling but you couldn't hear any sound.
    She was dragged to a block and the men that had been dragging her forced her head
    down onto it. One of the men pulled her long hair away from her neck as another man
    with an ax came into view. You knew what was coming and closed your eyes against
    the sight. It didn't work as you were still able to see the man raise the ax over his
    head. The girl struggled even more trying to get away with a look of terror in her eyes
    but it was no use. The man swung the ax down slicing through the girl's neck and the
    man who had moved her hair lifted her severed head up by her hair. You opened your
    eyes as the image faded and said, "I'll just be going now."

    "You can not leave until you have found my ring," Sorceress said. "The ring you wear
    now will not allow you to leave and will not come off until you find my ring, you
    transform into an object, you find me, or you die. If you try to leave you will be turned
    into an object."

    "I guess I have no choice then," you said sighing accepting the fact that you were
    stuck. "What can I be transformed into besides an object?"

    "You can be transformed into just about anything so long as it's not more powerful
    than I am," Sorceress said. "Now before you begin your search please tell me your
    name, sex, and age."

    "Shouldn't you already know that?" you asked.

    "I may be powerful but I'm not omnipotent," Sorceress said. "Now please tell me your
    name, sex, and age."

    What do you do now?
