    Re-education for the glory of the Supreme Authority

    added 5 years ago BM TG Mental

    For years the Supreme Authority has ruled the shattered land with an iron fist. When the end came for the civilisations of the old world, the Supreme Authority seized the ruins of failed cities and forged their new society. One of absolutely control and expansion, where the common people lived in slums as the never ending machine of control expanded.

    Rebellion struck, but the technological and military might of the Supreme Authority reduced them to hit and run tactics. Very few were brave enough to fight against this dystopian state, yet you were one of them. A common soldier in the rebellion you fought to free your home of the Supreme Authority and their mysterious Grand Council. And yet despite all that you failed. Your squad ambushed, all wiped out or fled accept you.


    Now you are marched through a Supreme Authority Re-education facility. This terrifying hollow and lifeless place is the dread of anyone who wishes to defy the Supreme Authority. No one knows what happens here, only that no captured rebel or dissent has ever shown their face again after being sent to it.

    And now you sit in a chair, strapped to who knows how many horrifying inhuman machines.

    A Supreme Authority scientist walks up to you “Ah you must be the captured rebel who was just brought in. Jack wasn’t it? I welcome you to my humble workplace.”

    You attempt to spit in his face, but don’t have the energy nor water necessary, instead you puff out “I... don’t care what you do... no matter what I will always be loyal to the Rebel Brotherhood... and we still free this land from you..”

    The scientist seems to find this amusing, chuckling he says “oh that final cry of hope never gets old. Now let’s see what our glorious leaders have chosen for you. You body and mind will be transformed to suit our great state’s needs.”

    You eyes widen, is that why no one is ever seen? That can’t be possible “What... what.. that isn’t possible.. no matter how fancy your tech is.”

    “Oh you will see soon enough. Now, let’s have a look to see what our glorious leaders have selected for you.” Replied to the scientist as he activates a computer. Despite everything you can’t help but watch to see what insane fate those tyrants seemingly intend for you.

    What do you do now?
