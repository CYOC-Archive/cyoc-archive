    Overlooking the town of Normalville, there sits a large lit-up marble tower named the "Hall of Heroes". Within that tower holds the base for the League of Supers, a team of superheroes tasked with protecting the city, country, and the globe from threats to our everyday life. This crime-fighting team has stopped wide ranges of threats throughout time, most of whom were villains set on controlling the city or taking over the country. The League of Supers contains almost a hundred super heroes across the globe, but the most famous eight belong to the A-Squad, a group of eight crime fighting Normalize residents.
