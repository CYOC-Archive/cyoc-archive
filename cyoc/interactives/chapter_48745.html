    Arachnid

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LuisJM 13 years ago A

    After much thinking, Gloria decided that she wants to be an Arachnid. The reason why she picked it is because of how they live. Arachnids, or spiders as they are called, have six to eight legs and could crawl around walls and ceilings without being detected. Some can also spew out webbing to create both housing for their kind and a trap for their prey. Not only that but they can also survive the hottest of enviroments, like deserts and jungles. There are many types of spiders and other arachnid species to pick from and each of them has such unique features. Looking at her body one more time, she wondered what she would look like if she had six or eight legs, crawling up walls, or spitting out webs from her mouth or butt. Putting those questions to rest, she decided to turn into an arachnid creature. But the problem for that is what type of animal would Gloria pick?
