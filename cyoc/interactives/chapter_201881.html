    Claire and Kevin arrived at the Age Wing, a little exhausted from their travel but very eager to enjoy their time together.

    The bellhop kindly assisted in unloading their luggage and showing them around their suite, which was nothing short of breathtaking with an enormous kitchen, living room and two bedrooms each with their own king-sized beds. All that and a view of the beach and adjacent gardens to boot left the couple in awe and already planning how they would spend their week.

    As he left, the bellhop handed Kevin a letter, telling them to make sure to read it before taking advantage of the Age Wing’s properties. Kevin thanked and tipped the man before turning back into the bedroom and sitting next to his wife.

    “What’s that?” Claire asked as she stretched out on the bed, her shirt peeling back over belly as she did.

    “Looks like a list of the do’s and don’ts.” Kevin said as he sat down next to her and read out the letter:

    'Dear Mr and Mrs. Milford

    First of all, allow me to welcome you to the Transformation Resort. I hope this trip is worth it. Now I’m pretty aware that you have chosen the Age Wing of the resort. To that end let me give you some rules and advice.

    As you already know, this resort can transform their guests into anything and anyone they want. The Age Wing in particular can age or regress the guest into whatever age they want. But of course, there are a couple of ways to do that. The room you’re in is one of them. It can help you change your age just by thinking about it.

    All you have to do is to picture the age you want to be and the room will do it for you. If you don’t want to change your age in your room, there are other facilities in the wing that can suit you better. All of our restaurants have a special ingredient on their food for appropriate ages, eat them and you will regress/progress to that age. Our shopping center has different ways in changing your age. We have a pool and a spa for regression and progression respectively. And lastly our theater’s live entertainment can allow you to enjoy changing your ages.

    Just a bit of a warning before you start. Age does have a limit from zero (newborn age) to a hundred. If you go past a hundred, you will instantly die from old age. If you both go below ten-years-old a babysitter or a substitute mother will be assigned to the room to take care of you. Don’t worry if you regressed past newborn, you would be instantly inside the woman we would assign you with. But if you regress past conception, you will not die, you will simply become an egg and will need to wait to be reconceived again. And for the clothing and other necessities in your luggage, don’t worry. When you change your age, your clothes will change to match that age.

    Lastly, some information in respect to Mrs. Milford’s condition. Should you wish to regress but are concerned for the safety of your child, do not worry your pregnancy will NOT be terminated. While your pregnancy will revert along with your physical age, your child’s genetic makeup will remain present with the fetus reverting back to an egg in your body. Should you progress back to your current age, your pregnancy will be restored as normal without having to reconceive your child. Because you entered the resort already pregnant, the room will remember your current condition as your default state of being. Be warned though that if you progress past your current age, your pregnancy will also progress. This could result in an early birth should you progress to the baby’s due date.

    That is all, if you have any questions don’t hesitate to call the front desk or our other services. Once again, thank you for choosing the Transformation Resort and I hope you have yourself a safe and wonderful stay.'

    “Well, that’s a relief,” said Claire rubbing her stomach, “Now I don’t have to worry about changing my age.”

    “Yep, yep. Baby’ll be safe and sound no matter what.” Kevin said as he kicked off his shoes and joined his wife on the bed. The two allowed themselves to rest for a little while, enjoying a little snuggle time with each other. After 30 minutes or so, Kevin sat up.

    “So what do you think babe? What should we do first?” he asked.
