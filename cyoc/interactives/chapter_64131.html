    Damon was just leaving school since it was over and goes to his locker and opens it with his key to the lock. Then all of a sudden. Damon's friend, Andrew, comes up to Damon and closes his locker. "Hello Damon how have you been?" said Andrew. "Hey Andrew I've been doing good" said Damon as he opens his locker door and get's his stuff and closes it again. "So listen man. Me and some of my friends are going to play a game at my house and we would like you to come" said Andrew. "I don't know Andrew. It's a school's night and I don't think my parents would let me go" said Damon. "But you must come. Roxanne is coming too" said Andrew. "She is?" said Damon. Roxanne was Damon's girlfriend and they've been dating for at least 2 years. "Yeah she said she wants to see you there as well Damon" said Andrew. "Well okay then I'll be there. But only because Roxanne will be there" said Damon. "Awesome. Be at my house at 8am. Don't be late dude" said Andrew. Later on that day. Damon was in his room working on some of his homework and saw that it was almost 8am. He was lucky that his parents was out and his brother was at a friends house. He then drove his car to Andrew's house and made it just in time. He knocks on the door and Andrew answer it. "Damon your just in time. Please come in. We'll playing the game in my room" said Andrew. Andrew then leads Damon to his room and they both enter. Damon then saw Roxanne and two other people he knew. The other boy he saw was a friend of his name Eric. The other was a girl who was in the same class as he was. Her name is Ashly. Damon sit's near Roxanne as Roxanne hugs him. "I'm glad you came Damon" said Roxanne. Andrew then sit's down and Damon see's that there were six people, including him, in the same room. "Alright the game were going to play is Transform or Dare" said Andrew. "Excuse me but what's Transform or Dare?" said Damon. "I'm glad you ask Damon and I'll tell you. Transform or dare is a game in which a person ask another person Transform or Dare" said Andrew. "So it's like truth or dare then?" said Damon. "Sort of. But this is how the game works. When someone ask Transform or Dare. They have a minuet to pick one. Or they can pick both. If the person says Transform then the person that was asking can change the person's body or mind into anything they wish. If someone picks dare. Then the person dares them to do anything they want to. You'll also have to say this is your transformation or this is your dare to activate the magic. Did you get all that Damon?" said Andrew. Damon nods his head. "Good so who wants to go first?" said Andrew. "I'll go first Andrew" said Roxanne as she raises her hand. "Very well then Roxanne who's the person that you want to ask?" said Andrew. "I choose Damon my boyfriend" said Roxanne. "Me? But I'm at a beginner at this game" said Damon. "Don't worry Damon. I'll try to make it easy on you. So what do you say Damon? Transform or Dare?" said Roxanne. What does Damon choose? Does he choose Transform or Dare?
