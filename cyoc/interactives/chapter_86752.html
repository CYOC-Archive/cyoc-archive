    "Okay, yeah," Jimmy said with a smirk. Limitless. He could make them into anything he wanted. Anything. They could be eagles, soaring in the sky. They could be orca, swimming freely in the sea. Monkeys, climbing trees with the utmost of ease. The whole of the animal kingdom was theirs to be - his to make possible. And he had the woman of his dreams on hand for it.

    "What are you thinking?" Petula asked, looking up to him. Jimmy was not so much someone to love as he was someone to latch onto. His power could make her by proxy powerful. She could have him eliminate her enemies, alter her own form at request, give her everything anyone could want. If she had to be a little bit fat to gain such control, so be it. It was worth the price.

    "I'm thinking rhinos," he said with a dark grin.

    "You really do like 'em fat, huh?" Petula asked with a grin matching his. Fat and powerful. She could get used to that.

    "You know it," he answered, as he closed his eyes and focused. Imagined himself as a rhinoceros. Massive. Almost invincible. Strong enough to shake the earth as he ran, big enough to...

    "*grrnk*," he heard from next to him! and his eyes opened wide. Petula... Her frame was thickening, yes, but not in the way he wanted. The corkscrew tail and wide, flat snout were evidence enough of that.

    "Hey, why'd you sto- damn it, Jimmy!" Petula frowned as she opened her eyes to see him turning as much into a pig as she was.

    "Gimme a break. I'm still figuring this out. I know I focused on rhino, so why didn't..."

    That was when they both heard a scream in the distance.
