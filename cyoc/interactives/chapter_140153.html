    Later on I continued my normal life by day, and practiced this power at night. By the end of my college years, I mastered this ability. Now it would have taken me longer if it weren't for a friend of mine that helped me along the way. He was trying to create some machine, and I was wanting to learn my ability. Somehow we were able to help each other. The second he became famous the fucker left me, my only friend abandoned me. Ever since then I kept my power a secret wishing no one would leave me again.
