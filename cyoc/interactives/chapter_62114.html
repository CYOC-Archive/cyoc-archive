    "Hey, hey buddy, yo man are you ok?" your eyes slowly open and you see 3 faces staring down at you. "Uh dude are you ok?" one of them asked. "Oh, uh yah, I’m ok." you say. Some one helps you up and after refusing an offer to be taken to the hospital, you start heading over to your car.
    Your car is a white and blue 2002 ford mustang. You had bought it used and it was starting to fall apart. Soooo, you decided to just fix that right now. "I wish that my mustang would transform into a red Ferrari F430 with a full tank of gas, and no one would notice the cars transformation. The change was really cool, blue lines appeared all over your vehicle resembling circuitry, the body changes it's shape and the interior changes as well to that of the luxurious vehicle it is becoming. In a few seconds you old car had become a shiny new red Ferrari. Suddenly you thought of something. Reaching into your pocket you found that your keys had changed as well. You clicked the doors open and hopped into your new ride.

    You decided to cruise down the Delaware express way before you went shopping. You turned your head a little and looked out at the river, you were wondering about that vision you had. The ring had told you that you were supposed to be some, great judge of humanity but, how exactly should you go about it. Besides just making a quick wish of course. And what about that Carrie girl you meet. She was in the vision as well but that could have just been the rings attempt to use a form you were familiar with. That and she was really hot. "alright." you say to your self. "Time to go shopping.'

    You spend the next few hours in multiple stores, teleporting things from online shops to you, and upgrading your apartment. At 8:30pm you have everything you have ever wanted. Now, lying in you new ultracomfort bed in your new apartment near Rittenhouse square. You start to plan what you will say to Carrie tomorrow. Your plan is to find out where she will be tomorrow, then meet her there and ask her if she will help you fulfill the rings purpose. Eventually you fall asleep. Your dreams shrouded in a fog of blackness.
