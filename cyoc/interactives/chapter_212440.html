    Dan decides there is enough time to have a little fun and try out some more options on the App, he thinks about who to use, ‘I could cause Greg to think of Jeff as a car,’ he thinks to himself, ‘or I could cause the parents to “persuade” Greg into accepting Jeff as a car. OR I could use the App on the sister, maybe give her monster tits… hehe… hmm. I think I know WHO I’ll use: I’ll use Ben, Greg’s 10-year-old brother, I have an idea which, I believe, will be really fun. Ben it is!’

    The 15-year-old, after sending Jeff back to bed, quietly enters Ben’s room, he grins down at the sleeping boy and opens the App. After he scans the boy, Dan opens the boy’s file, ‘now, let’s see,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I think I’ll alter his mind, I could plant some dog commands into his mind, if anyone says a command, Ben will act like a dog. Yeah, that’ll be fun!’

    So Dan selects a few commands, such as: “sit”, “speak”, “stay”, “fetch”, “beg”, “lie down”, “quiet”, “roll over”, “play dead”, “high five”, “heel” and “come”. Dan also increases the boy’s hearing and sense of smell to be like that of a dog, he also edits the boy, so that Ben would feel discomfort when wearing clothes. For a final change, Dan edits Ben so that, every time Ben feels happy or is praised, he would “wag” by moving his butt from side to side.

    Grinning evilly, Dan confirms the changes, ‘time to go,’ he thinks to himself, ‘can’t wait to witness Ben acting like a dog. And I am looking forward to see what happens with the “Jeep” when Greg gets annoyed from Jeff’s whining… hehe… this is going to be so much fun!’ With that, Dan silently exits the house and, using his phone, reactivates the security system. He takes one last look at the house, an evil smile on his face, ‘I’ll see you soon,’ and with that Dan walks off into the darkness.
