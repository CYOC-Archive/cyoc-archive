    The rest of the week passed without much change. You still had your classes with the same people and life hadn't changed much for most. Most imortantly, you stayed away from the mall. Jared was an unexpected accident that you wouldn't want to have happen all over again. At the end of the week you met up with Jennifer and Katherine at the park.

    They are once again wating for you. "Ok. Before we talk about anything else, who wants to stay this way till the end of the year?" No one raised their hands, so you got right down to business.
    "Jennifer- how could we pull this off?"
    "Well, we would have to know where the equipment is first. From there we would need a way to get there and get through any security there might be." You nodded and looked to Katherine.
    "What are the possible consequences?"
    "Breaking-and-entering and theft which would lay us no more than, like, 10 years in prison." You nodded again.
    "Now that we know what the cards are, who's up for it?" you asked. Katherine jumped up.
    "I am! I can't take guys this SHIT of guys hitting on me for the rest of the year!"
    "Me too! I'm ready, no matter what!" Jennifer added.
    "Well, I guess that settles it. We're in business!"

    5 minutes later the three of you were standing at the back entrance of the school. After Katherine picked the lock you and Jennifer made your way to the main office and situated yourselves at the computer desk. Jennifer began typing away as you kept watch. A while later Jennifer spoke up.
    "Alright. I got it."
    "Where are they?"
    "Well, I have bad news. Apparantly whoever made those things decided to parade around the country and 'experiment' in every high school near a state capitol."
    "What?! Are you fuckin' kidding me?!"
    "I wish I was. But, I know where the equipment is!"
    "Where?"
    "Uhh...the old warehouse downtown."
    "Great! Now let's get the hell out of here before we get caught!" After reuniting with Katherine you all decided to go to the park and draw up a new plan.
