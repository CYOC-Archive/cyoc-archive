    Antoine played rugby, but at only 5'8" and 155 pounds, his size was always a problem. He envied some of the big guys in his rugby club, and despite his time spent in the gym, it proved difficult to gain any weight or muscle mass.

    He was just a college student, studying psychology, and he had heard of TransDem labs one evening when he was staying late on campus. A woman had approached him and gave him a piece of paper "do you want some free software? Its for physical alterations and human models" she said sweetly. He reluctantly agreed, since it was free, and gave her his address.

    The software came one week later.

    He didn't know what to do with it, but he figured he would see what this "physical alterations" bit was about. It came with a small white box with a black end on it like a remote. As soon as it booted up the box beeped and much to his surprise his stats came up in great deal:

    "Sex: Male
    Age: 21
    Height: 1.727 m
    Weight: 69.86 kg
    Hair Color: Black
    Eye Color: dark brown

    ADDITIONAL INFORMATION (click here)"

    Antoine, being american, didn't know the metric system too well, so he wasn't sure about his weight under metric. Finding a converter online, he translated his weight back into pounds, and his height too, and was shocked to find that they were exactly accurate. "How the f..." he muttered.
