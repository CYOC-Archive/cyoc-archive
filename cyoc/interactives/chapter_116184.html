    I don't know the layout of the garden or checked the previous interactives about the garden's description (if there was one) so let's just say it's symmetrical and large.

    Rex was disgruntled, "Why do I have to be the one to babysit these two? And if I can remember, the last time me and Kayne split up to cover ground, we got caught and had to be scapegoats for that fucker!" he murmured in his breath as they walk in the garden. The statues were posed in different positions, some in innuendos, while the garden itself is also symmetrical and a bit large. What's different about the statues though is that they're made of rubber, and it was the possessed Kayne and Rex that continued the bizarre doings. And they're still alive in someway. The evidence is the quivers and moans, mix of pleasure, fear and/or suffering. Rex doesn't know how many he and Kayne while possessed turned them into these bizarre things. While walking around to find some sort of clue, whether it is some sort of box hiding in a bush, beneath the base of the rubber statues, or hollow ground that can or might lead to some sort of path, there has to be something here and hope there is a way to fix the problem.

    Rex eyes at Sei who is behind and on his left while Mike is on his right. He can tell that Sei is excited and getting aroused of looking at the tormented/pleasuring rubber statues. He doesn't mind them walking w/ him, the problem is the rubber statues around them. Their minds are still susceptible to being aroused and try to persuade him to join them along w/ Mike and Sei. Thus causing more problems. "Mike?" Rex calls him out. "Yes Master Rex?" he replies.
    "It's just Rex. Are you sure you and Sei still loyal to Johnny? I get the feeling the more we walk around the garden, you might turn against me. And Sei looks like he's is liking where he's at." Mike looks at Sei who seems to be like he's in rubbery heaven and smacks Sei's rubbber arm. "Ow! What was that for?!" Sei rubbed his rubbery arm. "We're here to help Master Johnny and his friends to find a clue! Not to indulge our fantasies.... Yet." he barks as they continue walking. "I know, but.... The statues... I wonder how it'd feel like to be them? Just... Posing there and enjoy being looked on..." Sei goes on and on about it as if trying to persuade one or two of them. Mike seems to be feeling that too, but tries to resist it for his master. "No, we will do that later once we achieve Master Johnny's goal." Mike makes his point. Sei groans in disappointment and tries again, "But Master Johnny will just return us to normal and Eric too! You know Eric likes this way!"

    Rex sighs after listening to the two of them argue or debating what they'll do after they get what Johnny and his new friends need. Walking from rows of rows of the rubbery garden statues, there was one thing that stood out for Rex as they stopped at an empty statue base on his left. There two things really. One, they remember turning the last victim into an anonymous statue of rubber, and two, there was a strange bush that wasn't apart of the symmetrical design of the garden. Both Mike, and Sei stopped arguing as they noticed Rex stopped walking, "What's the matter, master Rex?" Mike asks. Rex is trying to decide what to look at first; should he check out that suspicious bush? Or should he find the missing rubber statue? But another question to wonder about the first one, why would he care about the last victim that they turned?
