    Drusilla Farnaby's doppelganger escapes from her Victorian crib

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous 4 years ago AR TG O

    Jack Farnaby ,son of eccentric farm boy inventor
    Phineas Farnaby quickly solved her first problem
    having dreams about being a dragon.

    The nursery walls in which her baby crib was located
    had old Victorian looking wallpaper of a dragon menacing a
    young boy with a wooden sword and a wooden shield.
    Not that she was a expert on Victorian nursey rhymes but
    someone familiar with the stories of Alice in Wonderland could have told the befuddled baby girl she was looking at copperplates of a illustration where the Jabberwokky is menacing the Kid with the Vorpal Sword.

    However the baby girl figured out quickly enough her
    dreams must have been caused by her seeing the dragons
    on the Victorian wall shortly after her Telematter Transformation.

    Telematter Transformation ?

    The girl began to claw back into her mind for the answers.
    She was Jack Farnaby,son of Phineas Farnaby,who had just
    invented the Telematter Oscillator.
    Now was 2022.

    However as she looked again around her at the chamber pot
    the high backed Victorian chair,the old fashioned drawings on the walls she realized 'now' was more like 1867.
    And so the baby girl waddled to the wooden slats of the crib
    and began tugging at them till she had pulled three of them
    free,thinking all the way.
    The Telematter Oscillator allowed matter to be teleported
    a millimetre or two.
    Jack had been there in his dads laboratory in 2022 when it had
    occurred to him he could use the device to accelerate the
    mitochondria DNA from his maternal ancestors in his own
    cells.
    Basically Jack Farnaby was going to turn himself into his
    great great great great grandmother using the device.
    It it had,accept she was now the spitting clone of
    Grandmammy Drusilla as a baby,and apparently back in 1867,when Grandmammy Drusilla had been in a baby crib
    in the old brick Farnaby farmhouse that she and her dad
    would one day live in a century and a half in the future.

    Clearly the technology still had a few wrinkles in it.
    However the baby girl who was a doppelganger for Drusilla
    Farnaby slid through the space she'd created in the crib,slid to the floor and toddled out of her nursery room.
