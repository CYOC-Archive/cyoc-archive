    Nate decided he wanted the most dramatic transformation. "I'd definitely have to go with the full, four legged werewolf, with big sharp fangs, snout, the works."

    "Excellent. The spell has now been cast. Enjoy your transformation, sir and have a good night."

    "Thank you." Nate hung up the phone and set it down on the coffee table in front of him, still not believing it. "Turning into a werewolf, yeah, right."

    But just then, Nate began to feel incredibly warm as an itchy sensation passed over him. He suddenly felt a sharp pain in his mouth. He felt around with his tongue and it felt like a few of his teeth were loose. He jumped up and ran to the bathroom. He flicked on the light and checked himself out in the mirror just in time to see his teeth start to be pushed out as sharp fangs grew in their place. He stared in shock at the image before him as he felt the pain radiate through his body as the transformation continued to grow.

    "Oh god, what have I done to myself," whispered Nate as he felt a searing pain next in his right hand. He watched as i slowly stretched out into a paw and as his thin fingernails peeled and split away as thick, sharp claws grew in their place. He looked to his left hand and saw the same thing happen there. He looked on as grey and black hairs were growing in on his previously largely hairless arms, getting denser by the moment. He looked back up at the mirror and watched as his ears grew to lupine points. His hair seemed longer and shaggier as the same fur grew along his face and down his neck. He fell to his knees and cried out as his back muscles began to change and reform. causing his shirt the stretch to it's breaking point and split down the back. He pawed at his pants and underwear, rolling on his back to slide out of them. He kicked them aside and watched in awe as his chest began to barrel out. He watched as his feet stretched out into paws and his legs reformed. He rolled back onto his stomach as he felt his face push out into a lupine snout.

    Nate slowly stood on his newly formed four legs and walked out of the bathroom in shock of the dramatic transformation he had just undertaken. As he walked through his house in his new lupine form, it began to sink in that he was now a werewolf. The question was what to do now.
