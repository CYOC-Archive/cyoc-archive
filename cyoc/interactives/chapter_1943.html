It's the ability to make wishes to change people into things is what is important. I have to admit, changing my mom into a teenager and Ms. Barnes into a baby kind of gave me a thrill. It's time to see how much of a thrill it will give me to change people on a larger scale.

On line were five people; an old couple; a man in a hard hat and dirty jeans; a twenty-something in a black skirt and white blouse and a girl about sixteen or seventeen. The girl was dressed like Britney Spears, complete with belly shirt and flayed cuffed jeans. Very cute.

He yawned and streached his arms up and out. He was ready to call it a day. All the standing can wipe you out. Well, in a second, he wouldn't have that problem.

    I didn't do much. I closed my eyes and thought that I wanted them to become infants for fifteen minutes and have them put back on their clothes and forget what happened to them.

Then, the most amazing thing I have ever seen started to happen...they started to change. It was subtle at first. The old couple on line, their hair started to darken and their bodies seemed to rise up. Now this would be noticed by most people, under normal circumstances. But no one noticed because they were undergoing their own transformations.

The woman in the skirt and blouse also lost some years. Her skirt had already fallen to the floor. Her white blouse hung loosely off her flattening chest and began to creep to her feet. The Britney Spears wanna be was already crawling around, her clothes were left in a loose pile behind her.

    The last to become infants were the old couple at the head of the line. They shrunk into their clothes and two bald heads peeked out at me. They both giggled toothlessly at me as I walked by them towards the partition that was probably locked but that's not a problem.
