    Kidd and Klark adjust to their new roles

    The rest of the boat trip was quiet, for the most part... Captain Klark’s son, Kidd, actually turned out to be a (somewhat) decent captain. He didn’t bark orders at his crew much. Kidd wanted to avoid exposing his body swapped situation to the crew. There were still a couple of things he didn’t know about managing a crew but he thought it was best to keep a low profile and refused to ask his father for advice at all.

    Klark on the other hand was having crazy nights with his shipmates. The first night of their swap he announced that his cabin was “open for some stress relief!” to the entire crew.

    Kidd had to simply sit back and grind his teeth while his father made him the ship’s public slut. Nevertheless, he still found comfort in the way his crew now feared and respected him as their captain.

    After he had finished whoring. Klark used his new found free time to take a well needed mini-vacation. He was able to convince several of the crew members to go on a minor expedition with him once they meet land. He swore he wouldn’t squander his youth like his son.

    “So are we ever gong to swap them back?” John whispered to Tenoch.

    “I actually game them a wooden plank with the symbols to swap them back on it. “ Tenoch replied.

    “What’s stopping them from just looking at the symbols?” John asked

    “I wrote the symbols in some flammable liquid and put a flint in the fabric wrapping the board. If they try to take a peak at what’s written, it’ll just burst into flames and they’ll be stuck. I told them this so they hid it really well. They’ll be fine.”

    Tenoch then tried to shift the conversation. “So, do you have any idea who’s body you are going to allow me to use for our adventure?”

    John scratched his head. “Well I had some candidates in mind. I assume you want to be over 18 right?”

    “That would be preferred, though being a young golden one might be interesting. It’s just that a youthful body might limit what I can do.”

    John decided to pitch a potential host. “How about my father? He’s a retired adventurer like myself. But I gotta warn you, he’s built a bit like captain Klark in terms bodies. Not nearly as fat but he has quite the beer gut.”

    Tenoch looked towards Kidd, who was scratching his father’s (now his) massive beard with an irritated look on his face. Tenoch winced.

    “What about hair?” He asked John.

    “He’s hairy but doesn’t have a long messy beard.” John once again looked at Kidd in the distance and began to whisper something to Tenoch. “I’m pretty sure Captain Klark left food in his.” The two laughed.

    Once the ship arrived at the dock they gave their goodbyes to Kidd and Klark. “Thanks again for your magic little boy!” Kirk said giving John playfully a noogie.

    “Ow, no problem.”

    “You gotta tell me how being a little boy is treating ya sometime John.” Klark commented.

    “Well it’s- WAIT what!? How did you-“

    “HAHA I knew it! I thought you two were acting odd.” Klark laughed. “Seems me son and I weren’t the only ones swapped around! Like I said, just tell us all about it on your next trip across the sea, hopefully we’ll all be normal by then haha!”

    “Got it Klark, see ya!

    Tenoch and John then proceeded to head to home of John’s father.
