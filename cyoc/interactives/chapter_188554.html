    Cute little lamb.

    You notice a tiny lamb close by, you walk over to it and start to pet it. The lamb happily bleated and pushed into your hand, seemingly begging for more attention from you. "Aww, that's such a cute little guy!" Your girlfriend said while still petting the ewe. The two animals adored the attention you two were giving them. "They seem to really love you two!" A female zookeeper interrupted the petting. "Sheep are just the Cutest!" Your girlfriend Squeed. You sat there continuing to pet the little lamb. "Could I interst you two in a Special offer?" The Zookeeper asked. "What is it?" You asked back. "You two can go into a room and See a bunch more of these guys." "We'd love to!" Your Girlfriend answered for you, you didn't mind, you wanted to see more cute lambs anyways. She took you two to a special room, it had a bunch of Sheep within it, a lot of lambs too. "Aww!!" Your girlfriend Said as she ran towards the Sheep, all of them Bleating a Pleanty. You made your way towards the back, you noticed a bunch of lambs coming over to you, along with some adults too. "Our sheep have tags to help you know what gender they are. Male lambs numbers start with ones, our female lambs start with twos, male sheep are threes, and our ewes start with fours." The Zookeeper randomly stated, Surely enough when you Checked the tags they were numbered that way. #120 seemed to love on you the most, you were to distracted petting him to notice the footsteps approaching behind you. You felt a Sudden startling tag get forced into your ear. "Ah!" You heard your girlfriend jump as the Same thing happened to her. You two got dragged off to another room together, you were tossed in and the Two men slammed the door shut. "What just happened?!" Your girlfriend asked, despite knowing that you had no Idea either. You looked at the tag in her ear. "#459" you said to her in a Monitone voice. "What?" "Your tag, it says #459." You finally pieced together what was going on, they were going to turn you into Sheep! "Quick! What's the Number on my tag?" You ask.
