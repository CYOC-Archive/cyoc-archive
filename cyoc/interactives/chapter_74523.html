    "Dude!"

    "I mean it relaxes me. As if that's the way it suppose to be a horse and his ride. You know what I mean?" he

    "Ok, fine... Now what?" asked Carlos.

    "I want to gallop." said Mike.

    "Dude, are you mental! I mean, sure I 'll love to be mental right now, at least it would make sense, but come

    "No, I mean it. I'm getting anxious being in such a confine space. And we need to get out. Before sunrise and

    With money, anything is possible, even make a complete horse.

    "What are these?" asked Jason.

    "And say what we are going to a costume party?" asked sarcastically Jason.

    "THE STALLIONS!" cheered the three in realization.

    The boys did as plan with Carlos laying on top of Mike so he could use the four legs of the suit, and he
