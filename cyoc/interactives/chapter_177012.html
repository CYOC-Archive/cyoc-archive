    "Either way, this'll be my first test on other people swapping without me involved... so I'll need to be careful." He thought.

    While walking, Tim didn't even realize that he was heading straight for the edge of a door. Lost in thought, the sudden bonk to the head caught him by surprise before he fell back over onto his ass.
    "Gahh!" He called out upon hitting the hard ground.

    "Shit, you okay?" The person who had opened the door asked.

    "Y-yeah, thanks." Tim replied half sarcastically.

    "Oh, Nancy!" He said surprised. "I'm so sorry here let me help you up." He offered.

    Tim glanced up to see one of the new kids, Zack. He was on the shorter side, had longer blonde hair, and cute dimples... wait, why was Tim thinking like that? Tim thought for a moment, and remembered that Nancy and Zack had been chatting and flirting with each other for a while now.
    "Great, don't tell me I got her crushes as well as her role?" He thought.

    As Zack helped Tim up, they locked eyes. Tim was surprised as he had never really noticed them before... they were nice. They were a deep blue color, which stared at Tim intently causing him to blush. Tim brushed his long hair back, wondering why the hell he felt like this?

    "So uh... how's your day going?" Zack asked.

    "Oh, um, fine." Tim said, shaking the thoughts from his mind. "I was just heading to go and do something important for the boss, ya know?" Tim mumbled.

    "Ah, yeah sorry she works you around like that. Heard she can be a real bitch." Zack chuckled, unaware that it was the real girl he was into whom he was calling a bitch. "A-anyway, I was wondering if you'd like to get some coffee? I know your break is almost coming up and I'm off right now."

    Tim's heart started racing. Shit, was he actually excited this kid was asking him out? He was a grown ass man, towering over him, yet he felt like a teenaged girl just getting asked to prom. Tim even realized he was absentmindedly playing with his hair and glasses trying not to blush.
    "Okay, this is getting ridiculous..." Tim thought.
