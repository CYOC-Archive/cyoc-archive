    Jane and her mother experience bigger changes then Colin and his dad

    Now the norse water spriggan who'd given Colin the power
    back when he,Jane and the parents had crossed that tiny
    stream way back several chapters had in the meantime
    left her stream and headed to their house,to see what was
    going on.

    Being centuries ,if not millennia old the spriggan's notion of what a
    'dragon' was tended to follow the Scandinavian model.
    So she'd been somewhat taken aback at Colin's anthro furry dragon
    notion.
    However she'd sat invisible and silent on a kitchen chair whilst
    Colin and Jane had chatted,scratching out her spriggan notes
    in rune.

    She had been interested in Janes comment that Colin had gotten
    the genetics for flying and strength and had mused on the matter
    long after Colin left and sullen Jane silently ate her Wheaties,ticked
    off that Colin got the dragon DNA for flight and strength.

    The spriggan,feeling creative traced out on the notepad one possible
    extrapolation of Janes words.
    Colin and Janes parents were genetically dragons
    Therefore Colin and Jane were genetically dragons.
    Apparently male dragons were human sized anthro dragons like
    Colin.
    That meant of course Colins dad was a human sized anthro dragon.
    Feeling a little crazy today the spriggan fished out a old norse
    coin of some long dead Viking king named Ulric the Third
    Heads female dragons were human sized anthro dragons
    Tails and female dragons were big flame breathing feral Western dragon type girlfriends and wives and ex's.

    The spriggan flipped the ancient coin and it came up tails.
    Giggling silently to herself the spriggan stared at Jane and imagined
    the sullen girl as a big feral flamebreather.She'd have wings
    but unlike Colin would need to exercise for the things to be of
    any use.And unlike the guys,girl dragons would be born with a bit of a fat to muscle mass issue.

    And there'd be the issue of girl dragons who knew they
    really were guy dragons,guy dragons who knew they were really meant
    to be girl dragons.

    Only Odin knew how a human sized anthro dragon husband was going
    to consummate the marriage with his elephant sized wife.
    The spriggan knew they were going to have to add another
    + to that human term
    LGBTQ2S+

    The spriggan took a few more runic notes then
    waved her finger
    and the changes began.

    Unlike the changes Colin had made Jane was immediately aware
    of her transformation as her body grew in size
    her scales sprouted all over her hardening darkening skin ,her
    gasps turned to the ragged breathing as her innards swelled and altered and her clothes burst apart as her legs became as thick as
    tree stumps,her neck,torso and tail stretched out and her human face shifted and became the toothy countenance of a feral dragon.Out spread her
    wings and before she totally lost it and started screaming,roaring and spewing forth fire the spriggan wiggled her finger again and
    the light of sentience returned to Jane the daughter of dragon parents
    and a dragon brother.
