    "Um, I was wondering, have you heard of the town curse here? I'm doing a research paper on local myths." Jen lied, she felt that old tingle.

    "Yeah I've heard of it but I think it's all children's games." He said. "Matter of fact, I played with that a long time ago, kid said I would sound like I was from New York or something like that, I never noticed. Anyway how'd you hear about it?"

    "I'm... I was cursed." Jen said.

    "Really? With what pray tell might I ask?" He said

    "This guy, Kyle Perkins, cursed me so that every time I told a lie everything from below my waist would grow four inches in proportion."

    "Well, I don't see nothing weird about those stunning legs, why don't you show me?"

    Jen grumbled "Okay, I'll lie twice and that should prove it to you. My name is Bob and I'm five foot four inches." Jen felt the familiar spurt of growth and waited.

    "Well I don't see nothing different, tell you what, you T and D me, and I'll change you to normal." He said

    "Oh thank you, this has been a nightmare! Transform or Dare?"

    "Dare."

    "I dare you to change my curse." Jen said and waited.

    "Transform or dare?" He said, a smirk on his face.

    "Transform." Jen said, feeling like she just bought herself more trouble

    "Okay, for you transformation, from now on when you lie you'll gain one, no wait, three feet all around, and whenever you try and use this curse thing on anyone but this Kyle guy, you'll spit out four lies at once." He said, "Now show me your cursed."

    "I am going to Kill you." Jen yelled, and gained three more feet to her already massive lower half.

    "Oh now I see it!" He said and Jen stood up, lifted her mighty foot and stomped on him.

    "Idiot, now I really have to find Kyle." Jen grumbled as she made her way to the high school, now being able to see nearly three blocks away from her high vantage point, her colossal legs, warm in the sun. Jen made it to the school in a matter of a few minutes, which scared her a little since she was nearly four miles away when she talked to that man. Jen scanned the area looking for Kyle or anyone who would know where he was. Jen spotted his friend Tom, and sat down like before.

    "Tom, Hey! Tom!" Jen yelled Tom looked up and pointed at himself mouthing the words "Who me?"

    "Yes you! Come here!" Jen called to him.

    "Hey Jen. Um.. what do you want?" Tom nervously asked

    "Have you seen Kyle today? I wanted to apologize to him for last week." Jen lied and gained three more feet, but kept her composure so as not to throw Tom off.

    "Yeah, about that, he moved yesterday." Tom said

    "He move?" Jen went white as a sheet. "Did he say where?"

    "Yeah, he said his families going clear across the country to California." Tom said.

    "Oh, well I'll just send him a letter or something." Jen said "Thanks, I gotta go."

    Tom said goodbye, and Jen started to walk away, she new what she had to do, but all she could think about was the distance she would have to travel just to get there, and how she would have to rent a car and... her thoughts stopped and she looked down.

    "I could just walk there, I mean, these babies take me a long way fast." Jen said, she looked around and spotted the highway, and started toward it. Jen was about to walk all the way to California, and she was in Virginia. This was going to be a long and hard trip, Jen sighed and began her trek.
