    "Hello young lady, do you want to buy anything?" He said in a friendly tone, he looked like he didn't have all his teeth and his hair had a large bald spot.
    "Yeah, sure, I have 10 dollars, what can that get me?" She replied, keeping the conversation friendly.
    "Teens seem to like the technology items, so scan this code, ill give you a magic app, you'll feel compelled to keep using it." The man held out a slip of paper that took you to 'The Magic App' "I've never been good with names, so I just called it Magic App'."
    "Nice doing business with you I guess." Willow said, handed him the money, and left.

    The app was quick to download so she loaded it up near the booth.
    'Would you like Magic App to view your location?'
    "Yeah, sure I guess," Willow said, clicking yes.
    'Awareness on or off?'
    "What? Like the app is aware? Um.. I'm gonna say no to that one.." She slowly pressed the no button. This meant that she wouldn't notice any changes, no one else would either, and she would keep using the weird application.
    'Easy or Hard?'
    "Hard all the way, yeah" Willow eagerly pressed the Hard button, making it so a random word(s) would change each time with no human input.

    The man piped up and said that it will generate a sentence, change a word and it will come true.
    "Thanks I guess, sounds cool. Lets see what it has to say and start small.." She targeted herself, unaware that she would accidentally change herself.

    'Willow is a 5'6" feet tall woman who is looking at her phone.'

    "So I guess I just change something? I'll make myself really tall."

"So I guess the app doesn't do anything, so I can do whatever I want with no consequences? Nice. I'm gonna do something a bit weird now."
