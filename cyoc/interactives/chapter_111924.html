    Damien; 22. You had always thought to yourself that--while you have acted morally decent your whole life--you would probably turn into an evil villain the moment you had an opportunity to do something incredibly selfish and get away with it. Such an opportunity may have very well just fallen into your lap, and you intend to waste no time indulging yourself in your strange desires. You remember that there is an apartment complex from here, that could be reached on foot narrowly before the sun sets, and deciding where to go first becomes easy.

    ~~~~

    Your first obstacle is getting through the front door. You rudely bang on the glass until a staffer of the apartment nervously comes up to you and--not unlocking the door--asks you what you need.

    This hobo-like behavior is making you feel very self-conscious very quickly, but you didn't charge towards this building to faff around and waste time.
    "Could I... come in please?" You ask; your stomach churning violently as you break every rule of social conduct you're used to following.

    "Do you know somebody who lives here, sir?" The employee asks, taking a step away from the glass.

    You feel yourself sinking into trouble. You need to get the hang of this mind-control amulet right now. You remember that, when you got the guy from before to put his hand on his head, the way you spoke that command didn't feel the way words normally feel when they come out of your mouth. The mistake you made just now, was you asked to come into the apartment like it was a normal, everyday request. Maybe you need to channel some sort of deeper, more commanding presence.

    ~Let me into the building~ You instruct, eyes dead-locked on the employee.

    The worker's body language immediately switches from tension to excitement as she opens the door and waves you in.

    "Your hospitality is much appreciated!" You remark as you slide some loose hairs behind your ears. "Why did you let me in, by the way?"

    The lady looks confused and scared again all of a sudden, as if she instantly regrets what she just did.

    ~You are okay with letting me into this building~ you butt in before she can say anything, and her expression relaxes again.

    You're excited to see this sapphire can be relied upon to seemingly control someone's mental state in very specific ways.

    What do you do now?
