    Troy decided changing wasn’t even worth it at this point, honestly the Pink Bunny suit was one of the more “redeemable” outfits and everyone had already seen him in it. He wanted to avoid the gingerbread man and the snowman the most.

    He told his parents he was going to find the other Kids before bed and went off. He walked into the hangout room where the teenagers usually spent their time here. He found Eve and Ryan chatting, Ryan was also annoyed about something.

    “Oh there is Troy, I am sure he would agree with me. Ryan complained from Eve’s body”

    “What is going on Ryan?”

    “Eve here is telling me that she has a new diet and that I can’t eat meat, dairy or anything made of animals...”

    “Oh Eve I didn’t know you were vegan?”

    “Oh yeah, just this year.”

    “I wasn’t finished, she also said I can’t have any carbs, or ice cream or breads or any processed sugars.”

    “Oh…. What does that even leave.”

    “He can have fruits and veggies while in my body.” Eve said proudly, although Troy thinks this is part of her prank.

    “Yeah expect the good ones, no berries, or apples or bananas”

    “Yeah I have an allergy”

    “That leaves almost nothing!”

    Troy laughed a little, although if he was honest a restricted diet would be better than his situation.

    “Hey Ryan, at least you didn’t get Quinn” Troy gestured to his costume. “He only packed stuff like this.”

    Ryan and Eve smiled. “I see he raided Mom’s costumes from the theater, those are nice outfits.” Eve said.

    “I’m sure” Troy was getting hot in his costume and started to sweat.

    Eve, Troy and Ryan chatted for a while and listened to some music. He really liked hanging out with his cousins, there were some cool people.

    “Alright, it’s getting late. I’ll see you tomorrow.”

    “Yeah, I’m heading to bed too.”

    Eve and Ryan left, so Quinn went back to his room. He brushed his teeth and got in bed, he wasn’t sure what to wear tomorrow, but he figured he would change from the bunny outfit.
