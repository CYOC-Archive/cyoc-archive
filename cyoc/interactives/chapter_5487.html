    Inside the large mansion at 1313 Forandring Street Dr Veranderen sat in his large lab typing away at his laptop computer:

    The first variance of Formula 525 has been prepared and had been added to the list of formulas ready to be tested.



    Riley, a almost 21-year-old college student, was in need of some extra cash for her first trip Vegas and after seeing the ad she found herself outside the mansion’s gate pushing the buzzer.

    Seconds later a voice came over the intercom “Yes, how can I help you?”

    Riley looked up into the camera and spoke “Hopefully this is the right place, I’m replying to the ad in the paper.”

    “Ah yes, I will open the gate, please come into the door to the right of the main entrance. You will find a clipboard with some paperwork to fill out and sign, I will be with you shortly.” The doctor replied.

    Riley pulled on the gate as it freely opened for her. Entering she closed the gate behind her hearing a large snap as the magnetic lock reinitiated.

    Entering the vacant waiting room Riley picked up a clipboard and a pin and sat down filling in the needed info; Name, Age, Weight, Height, and so on…

    The next page outlined the compensatory portion of the test:

    Compensation varies based on study(ies) completed and amount of time the study requires. Payment is made in cash at completion of the study. Minimum compensatory payment is $5000.

    The next 15 pages of small font text had this header:

    The following contract is to protect the organizations research. This must be signed in order to continue further. Main Clause is a non-disclosure agreement; if any mention of these tests are made outside of this research facility a suit in the amount of $10 million will be filed against you for damages.

    Not really wanting to read the legal mumbo jumbo Riley flipped to the last page and signed her name. If only she knew she was giving the Doctor complete control of her body and mind, she might have known what was soon to come.

    Just as she finished signing the middle age Dutch doctor walked in. Taking the clipboard from Riley he introduced himself, “Hi, I am Dr. Veranderen, if you follow me I will take you to one of the testing rooms for you to wait in while I double check your data.”

    Riley followed the doctor down the hall and into a door labeled ‘Testing Room 1’.

    “I will be back in just a few minutes, make yourself at home.”

    Riley was in awe looking at the large room, it was just like a dream studio apartment aside from the medical table in one corner and the two-way mirrors covering 2 of the 4 walls. It had a kitchen, a king sized bed, a TV with gaming system, a couch and a bathroom; much better than her own apartment on campus.
