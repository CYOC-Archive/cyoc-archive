    It wasn't long after relocating to Australia that Kyle (previously Marco) met someone else, which happens commonly enough. Of course Sow (previously Adam) didn't know this, as she spent most of her time eating and trying to please her master, as Kyle had trained Sow to do for the past two years.

    But as time progressed, Kyle spent less and less time with Sow. To the point where even she started really noticing, and missing her master. She sat pining away lonely night after lonely night, until one day Kyle came in and used magic to force a transformation for Sow to return back to Adam's form, before all the madness began. But because Adam's transformations had been on a deeper level than just magic, out of a love for his master and desire to be all that then Marco had wanted Adam to be, it was tricky. The magic just wasn't taking hold very easily, but finally Kyle figured he got it good enough - Adam stood in his old jock body, though still looking older than he should, and not as energetic - in fact kind of depressed.

    "You're free now. Go," Kyle/Marco waved his hand at Adam dismissively. "Live your life, be happy, and all that."

    "I don't understand," Adam/Sow responded. "Have I displeased you, Master?"

    "No, Sow - I mean Adam. It's just time to move on now. I'm sending you back to Stonewood, to our dorm, and from there you can do and go wherever you like." With another wave of his hand, Adam was gone.

    It wasn't long after that the Dean from the magic school, Professor Elwood Dorbledub, paid Kyle a visit. "Kyle Bidne, you have been summoned to answer for your crimes!"

    Kyle jumped, and spun around to see the esteemed intruder with two of his associates.

    "Or should I call you Marco?" Dorbledub continued.

    "What the hell?" Marco wasn't expecting a visit from the Dean.

    "Indeed, that's what we were about to ask you." Dorbledub looked at Marco sternly. "You see, a certain student disappeared for some time after what we presumed to be magic mischief. Since he was a normal, and his family didn't complain we didn't give it much thought. But then he returned quite abruptly, and is... frankly, a mess. And we can't seem to fix him."

    "What? What does this have to do with me?"

    "Well, you see, my boy, you used magic to make him fall in love with you, and then you resumed to own him as a playtoy, and conditioned him for two years as such, only to abandon him."

    "I didn't abandon him, I sent him back and told him he could do whatever he likes - I gave him his freedom."

    The Dean raised his voice. "Do not presume to tell me what you did, silly boy. This norm nearly killed himself very publicly on school grounds because you were foolish enough to condition him to need you and then selfishly abandon him! Your irresponsible behavior and short-sightedness nearly cost a life, and our school is now under investigation by the magic authorities because of your blind selfishness!"

    "But I set him free, I tried to change him back with magic."

    "There is a deeper magic in the heart of even norms... and you transgressed, severely. Do you know that even our most gifted and powerful wizards couldn't transform him back to his original self - who he was before you started messing with him? The deeper magics of the heart and years of conditioning have made it so that he's resistant to even the most powerful magic - he keeps reverting back to that blasted sow body and pines away all day and night for you."

    "Wow," Marco said proudly.

    The Dean raised his wand threateningly. "Damn you, boy, this is SERIOUS. It will take us years to undo the damage you've done and rehabilitate him - if it's even possible, and the reputation of our school is tetering thanks to the likes of you!"

    "I'm sorry," Marco said quietly, for the first time thinking of Adam and feeling - could it be guilt?

    "That's all good and well, but you're to be brought back to the school immediately, where sentencing will be carried out."

    "Sentencing?" Marco asked with a gulp.

    "Yes. Since you behaved like a selfish pig with no regard for anyone but yourself, it has been decided you will be turned into the very thing you turned that poor norm into... an obese anthro sow bent on pleasing men sexually." Professor Dorbledub grimaced, looking at Marco with disgust. "You had such potential... but you couldn't get over yourself, and now that's all going to go to waste becuase of it."

    Marco's face drained of blood, and he felt like he was going to pass out. The next thing he knew, he was standing before the facalty, student body, and magic authorities.
