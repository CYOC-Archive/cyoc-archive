And of course, with no ID and a different voice, he couldn't very well just get in touch with the bank. He would just have to deal with this until he could get it straightened out. Reaching for his phone he found that it too had been rendered useless by the chamber. What else could go wrong?

    "Hello, Gene? This is Diane Sherman from next door."

    "Diane? Whose phone are you on? I've actually been trying to reach you all day."

    "It's a long story, but I need you to do me a favor. You still have my spare key, right? I need you to go over to my place and-"

    "I think we have a bad connection, you sound all scratchy or something. About your house though, that's why I've been trying to reach you. First of all, I want you to know your cats are fine, they're here with me..."

    Sherman didn't like where this was going.

    "There was a huge fire last night. Not really sure what the cause was seriously Diane, you were living in a death trap they tell me. Old paper insulation in the walls, space heaters in rooms with shag carpeting... you know this place was built around the same time, I should look into what my walls are filled with..."

    Of all the possible times! "OK thanks for the update but believe it or not this is more important. I need you to head over, and just... if you could find a toothbrush for me, or the hairbrush on my nightstand..."

    "A toothbrush? Diane! Your house is gone sweetie! There was a delay at the fire department over something. I had to go over there myself to get your cats out, it's a miracle they're OK. Everything else just kinda went up like kindling. I'm telling you, it's these old buildings! They look nice, but they just didn't know what they were doing back then. At least we have insurance though, right?"

    Sherman sighed. "Right... Would you mind taking care of the cats for a while? I have some things I need to get worked out here."

    "Sure thing. Is this your new number?"

    "No, no, I'll call you when I get that straightened out. Just... bear with me here."
