    "When I click my fingers, you will be a horse girl, but will still be my sister" Kyle clicked his fingers.
    Ally snapped out of it, shaking her head, slightly snorting making Kyle's smile a lot bigger.
    "What are you smiling at?" Ally asked. Kyle didn't say anything, he was still smiling.
    "Alright, I'm going to get something to eat. You can just sit there, smiling".
    Ally walked away from Kyle's room, while Kyle noticed her tail swinging back and forth as she left the room.
    Kyle didn't want to see this opportunity go to waste, so he followed Ally without her noticing.
    Ally looked through her pantry, looking for anything that she fancied having. "Naan bread, no. Choc chip waffles, no..."
    Kyle peered through the doorway, seeing this half-horse, half-human creature looking for something to eat. He felt like David Attenborough, looking at an animal in the wild, keeping a distance away so it doesn't notice.
    "...no. Oats? Oats! Perfect!"
    The sounds of horse's hooves on the floorboard made Kyle hide behind the door frame. As the sound went quieter, he looked back to see Ally pouring oats into a rather large bowl. She set aside the box of oats and sat down on one of the chairs by the dinner table, pushing her tail through any gaps. Kyle watched on.
    Ally wasn't sure what to do. Did she want to eat the oats with a spoon or did she want to dive in face first and eat the oats right there and then. She wanted to eat it normally so she wouldn't make a huge mess, but those oats were so entrancing to her, she had to eat them, she had to, she had to eat them now. Ally dived her face into the bowl of oats and started to chow down. Kyle watched on, trying to hide his laughter under his breath.
    "Yes!" Kyle thought "This is perfect! Ally finally got what she both wanted and deserved. I wonder what I could do next?"
