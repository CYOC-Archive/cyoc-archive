Jump off and then we all go our separate way as the train turns.”

    “Lets do this.” said Constance as everyone prepared, they waited until a load bump and then throw open the door to the next compartment. Without missing a beat, the she-bear rushed forward waking everyone up as screams filled the compartment, she ran right to her still human husband who looked horrified to see his wife over him in her new bear form. There was not time to do this the way she wanted so she grabbed him by the face and shoved him face first into her wet bear hood. The liquid began the change almost as once. There was no way to hide a giant 600 pound humanoid bear but that didn’t matter as the lions and the jaguar entered the compartment and began to either splash people with the samples or simple cut them with their claws.
    The humans where panicking as they screamed in terror, all of them backing away from the claws even as the liquid landed on skin. That started the changed at once as people started to grow fur, scales, feathers and the like as the virus took hold. Some of the samples had been sexual fluids and as such those souls who did get hit with that started to become intelligent creatures while those who just got hit with blood began to turn into the more animal like nearly mindless creature.
    The sounds of screams alerted the next compartment but they where not ready to deal with four lions and a jaguar spraying people with transformation agent, by the time the five of them had reached the 3rd train car the 1st had full transformed and the more animal like creatures where rushing forward toward the sound of humans. Those with intelligence began to either search for loved ones who may or may not have been transformed into the same creature or maybe had been one of the few unturned humans after the cats had passed. They were not lucky anymore.
    The made it to the fifth compartment before they ran out of samples, by now almost a quarter of the train was either transformed or being transformed. The sound of the train screeching filled the air as the conductor was alerted to the issue and worse of all for the humans they where passing a refugee center where people who had just escaped the city were being settled. The slowing train made it easier for the humans at the front of the train to get off, but it also made it easier for the infected.
    “Lets go my cousins is only an hour or so away.” Said Barbara as the four of them jumped off the train, followed seconds later by the jaguar women. The entire place was chaos as screams filled the air and moments later gunfire as the infected began to spread out over the countryside.
    “We did good.” Said Aaron as the five of them reached the wood line to see a bear family now with a papa bear running out the back of the slowing train followed by an alligator woman and a rhino teenager. The rhino charged toward the city infecting everyone he could while the Alligator women headed for a nearby creak. The nearby town was quickly falling and since there was a refugee camp that population of the town had been much larger then normal.
    “We sure did.” said the Jaguar as she looked at the four lions. “Mind if I stick with you all for a bit, we are big cats after all and better in numbers, name is Kim.”
