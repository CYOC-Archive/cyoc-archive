    Amy's response was not long in being heard.
    "Yeah, that sounds awful," she said with a scowl of disgust.
    Amy was honest in her disgust. To be changed into a diaper? Gross! How completely sick was that? Each option of this twisted proposition seemed absolutely vile! None was good, barely less disgusting than the other! The idea of being the diaper, especially of an old person, notably an old man and incontinent at that... Yuck! Even being the diaper of a baby just seemed like a perfect nightmare! You'd be covered in feces. Who would ever want that? What sick person thought it would be a fun thing to do?
    "I knew you would not probably want that," smiled Miss Luna.
    This was not surprising. The girl was quite young and it was clear she would not be in those very specific fantasies. She was too young to be unfazed by what people found interesting sexually. But she WAS a cute-looking teen and Miss Luna had contacts with the fay realm. A fay realm that enjoyed having humans as slaves, pets, or offspring. Certainly many a fay would like to buy her. Miss Luna could not live from her dream shop solely so to earn money and a decent living, she commerced with the fay realm. Looked like she could make sure to pay the rent with the teen in front of her. She apologized and poured her a cup of tea. Amy took it and just sipped it. However, it was enough. After sitting down on a chair, saying she felt a bit woozy, she was soon slumped on the ground, fast asleep. Miss Luna smiled and called over a troll to gather a new victim for the auction in the fay realm. She then pondered. Should she make the girl younger or keep her as she was? Which option would be the most profitable? Or appropriate?
