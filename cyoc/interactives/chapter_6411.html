    "Since we don't want you to spend the rest of your life in prison..." Sam whispered something into Kyle's ear.

    "What are you doing Sam?"
    "Trust me Jen. Call the police and act very distressed."

    After a few minutes the police came. San and Jen gave oscar-worthy performances as they explained that they came home from the movies to find their parents brutally murdered by Kyle Perkins, who sat naked in a pool of blood and body parts.
    Perkins admitted to killing them both. The policemen were shocked and awed. A captain put his hand on Sam's shoulder.
    "I'm sorry son. It's horrifying what these sickos will do."
    Another policeman scratched his head and wondered aloud.
    "Still, it's pretty weird. These murders look more like the work of an animal than a man."
    Suddenly Kyle put his head in his hands and cried out. His scrawny body began filling with muscle, and the hair. He went through convulsions as his body grew in mass and rapidly became less human. A muzzle shot forward from his face as he fully transformed into a giant two-legged man-wolf.
    The policeman looked on with horror as Kyle-wolf took a bite out of the captain and tore him to pieces. The other policemen whipped out their guns and began firing.
    The angry man-wolf swiped on and then charged out of the kitchen window, running off into the night, followed by policemen.
    Jen looked on in shock and whispered.
    "Sam, what did you tell him?"
    "I told him to transform into a bloodthirsty man-wolf when-ever he hears the word 'animal.'"
    "WHY?"
    "Because he'll take all the heat off of us. Whenever any news comes up involving werewolves committing crimes, it'll be blamed on him. Besides, it was my gift to him for bringing us together."
    "A gift?"
    "Sure." He looked at her with his deep brown eyes. A red flicker passed across them as he said, "What could be better than being a werewolf? Kyle's stuck like that."
    Jennifer shivered.
