    Deciding that you are done playing around with the light for now, you head home.

    When you get there your mother is in the kitchen getting dinner ready and your father is in the living room watching television. Your smiles at you as you walk by, "How was your day?"

    "Not that bad," you offer, "I hung out with Gene at the mall today."

    "That's nice," you mother says absentmindedly, already paying more attention to the carrots she is peeling, "He's such a nice boy, so well mannered."

    "Yeah," you laugh and make your way to your room, only to be stopped again, this time by your five year old little brother, Luke.

    Luke has a huge pile of Pokemon game cards in his hands and is holding them up to you, "Play Pokemon with me! I got a bunch of new cards today and made a new deck!"

    "Not right now," you try to get by him, but he jumps in front of you.

    "Come on, please? If you play I'll let you pick what cards you use, you can even use my legendaries. My shiny legendaries even!" he pouts.

    Your frustration gets the better of you and you take the magic light out and flick it on to a medium pink setting, figuring that a little sister will not pester you to play pokemon.

    A quick pass of the light over your brother and the dinosaurs on his shirt become butterflies and his hair grows into two long braids.

    "What's that?" your new little sister pouts, still holding a pile of pokemon cards up to you.

    "Nothing much", you shrug, "Just a flashlight I bought. I'll tell you what though, I'll play cards with you after dinner."

    "Awesome! I'll make a really good deck for you, I promise!" your new little sister exclaims excitedly, running off to her room, braids swaying behind her.

    The sight makes you start wondering what else you can do with your family, especially because of how powerful its reality altering ability is. You sort of want to find out more about that, so you decide to conduct a little experiment, figuring that you can change things back when you are done.

    In the living room, your father has fallen asleep in front of the television, perfect for what you have planned.

    Turning the light to its highest pink setting you sweep it over his body.

    In an instant he is totally changed into a woman beautiful enough to be a supermodel. Her hair is wavy and brown, reaching just past her shoulders, her skin is smooth and tanned, her fingernails painted dark red and perfectly manicured, and her body curved and shapely, the perfect example of womanhood.

    Even his clothing has changed, to match his new womanly form, his white shirt, the top three buttons undone, just as they had been before you used the light on him, has changed into a white blouse, and you can see the curve of his, now her, breasts, which by your guess have to be a d-cup. Your father's slacks have become a navy blue skirt, reaching down just past the knees, and you can see that she is wearing stockings.

    Your father, once a typical business man, has become a business woman.

    From the kitchen your mother calls out, "Dinner's ready hon."

    Your father, for you have no clue what else to think of this woman as, stretches and yawns, "I'll be right there sweetheart."

    Her voice is as feminine as the rest of her, sort and sweet.

    Seeing that you are in the room with her she tussles your hair, "Hey kiddo, how was your day?"

    "Great...uh...dad," you stammer, unsure of what else to say.

    Your father frowns, but her concern seems to be at your hesitance rather than at being called 'dad', "You weren't up to anything I wouldn't approve of, were you?"

    "No, not at all," you say quickly, "I was just at the mall shopping with Gene."

    This gets you a smile from her, "Well, I know Gene is a good kid, so if you were with him, I'm sure you weren't getting into anything."

    Satisfied, your father walks into the kitchen, hips swaying in a very womanly fashion while you linger in the living room a bit longer, looking at the family pictures hanging on the walls. They ate just like they always were, except for the fact that they show you have two female parents and a little sister rather than a little brother.

    Over dinner your parents talk about work, like they always do, your father's job seemingly unchanged by the change of gender, then the topic changes to how 'Lucy', which you realize must be your former brother's name, did in school and what you'll be doing when you go back to college next semester.

    After dinner your parents do the dishes together, while you sit in the living room and play pokemon with Lucy until it is time for her to go to bed.

    You then wait for your parents to go to bed, and once they are asleep you go to the hall closet and take out a number of old photo albums to look through.

    The one that interests you the most is the one containing your parents' wedding pictures and photos from the two years after that. It is amazing to see your two female parents standing next to each other, both wearing white wedding dresses and smiling for the camera. There is a picture of them kissing, which makes you blush, and a picture of them eating wedding cake together. Later on in the album, there are pictures of your mother pregnant with you, making you wonder how that happened. A picture of your mother holding you as a newborn, while your father has an arm around her shoulder only adds to your curiosity.

    Closing the album, you ponder what to do next.
