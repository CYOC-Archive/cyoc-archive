    Chronivac Version 4.0

    Gary's school needs a new coach

    added by Celtic Water 3 years ago AR AP BM

    Gary was in the weight room, blasting out a serious of bench presses, when Eli walked in and headed over the the free weights nearby. With the latest round of transformations completed for over a week, Gary had been watching Coach Eli closely. Gary had noticed how deep in thought the coach was after he had told Eli that he was going to college with a wife and triplets, so Gary figured that this might have gotten the coach to decide to return to college.

    “Hey Coach. How’re things?”

    “Going good. Well at least for me.” Eli replied.

    “What’s that mean?”

    “I just told the principle that after the school year ends that I will be leaving.” Eli said putting the dumbbells down. “I’ve decided that I’m going to go back to college.”

    “That’s great, man.” Gary said, happy for the slightly older man. After talking for about ten minutes, Gary had to head to class and left his coach to continue his exercise.

    Arriving home that night, the first thing that Gary did was get onto the Chronivac. The first thing that Gary did was bring up Eli’s profile. He was thrilled that Eli had decided to return to college, but Gary thought that Eli should be a little bit younger when he arrived back on campus. Lowering Eli’s age another two years and upping his height to 6 feet 7 inches, and his weight to 350 pounds, Eli set it that the changes would taken at least six months. This way Eli would still be growing until he left. The one major problem that Gary now saw was the fact that the school needed a new coach.

    Thinking about it, Gary went into the program and looked through the various guys at the school. Gary started by looking at Neil. Sure it would be poetic that Neil would be forced to grow into a mature guy, but Gary saw that more as a gift. Furthermore, Gary did not want to change any of the various players on the team. Finally, one kid came to mind that Gary thought would be perfect, Luke.

    Luke was an average student that shared many of the same classes as Gary. Unfortunately, he was also very short and thin as a rail. Pulling up Luke’s profile, Gary could only whistle at what he saw. Luke was 4 feet 11 inches and weight 90 pounds. Gary knew that Luke had been the subject of Neil wrath a few times, but Gary had not realized just how much weaker Luke had been.

    Fortunately, Luke was also in foster care system. This meant that Gary could raise Luke’s age and no one would miss him. For this reason, Gary first raised Luke’s age from 17 to 27. This way, Luke would still be young enough to connect with the players and have a little time for fun. Next, Gary went to Luke’s height and raised it to 6 feet 5 inches. Finally, Gary set the program to increase the amount of muscle Luke had by 1 pound a day. Setting the program so that Luke’s transformation would last for 200 days, Gary knew that the school would have the perfect guy for coaching next year.

    Finally, Gary went into the altering reality and mind settings. First Gary set it so by the time Luke’s transformation was done, Luke would have a college degree and everything else he would need to be the new coach. Then, Gary set it so the only people who would know about the changes would be himself and Luke. Gary did not want anything messing with his mind and he wanted Luke to be able to enjoy his transformation.
