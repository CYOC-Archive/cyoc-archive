    A Bit of Reality

    You decide it won't do you any harm and step out of your room. Lucas leads the way as you bend down and grab your shoes before throwing themback into your room for later. You both walk downstairs and begin watching the game. You don't know how much time passed before Lucas asks you "Hey wanna hang out in the pool?" You interject saying you didn't "bring any swimming trunks over". "Don't worry I think I have a spare" Lucas says getting up and heading to his room. Wait...

    What were you doing hanging out with your Cousin in the body of his older brother? You get up and head upstairs only for Lucas to throw swim trunk at your head. "Meet you in the pool" He says running downstairs. You thought he was driving to the public pool but he probably wanted to hang out in the lame pool in the backyard. You shrug and get in the bathroom. You look in the mirror and just see your normal reflection. You take off you're clothes and grab the swim trunks. You put them in you're mouth when you use you hands to take off you're shoes and socks.

    You don't look at your reflection again but if you had you would have probably noticedo your hair coloring changing back to the dark brown you used to have. Or than you had you're regular amount of body hair again (Which used to be none) and that the tattoo on your left shoulder had dissapeared. Instead you out on the swim trunks and headed to the pool.

    At the pool you and Lucas hang out and talk about school and soon you find yourself almost relaxed. Until Lucas asks you "Football practice sucks huh cuz" You move your head up in a jerk and fall off the inflatable pool float you were laying on. "Sorry cuz, probably should've woke you". This was extremely weird, Lucas was calling you cuz but he didn't know about the magic socks or did he? "Well let's go inside" Lucas says getting up and drying off. You float in the pool waiting before getting up and following suit.

    Lucas is nowhere to be seen inside so you head to your room and gasp at you're used to be room. Your room is now messy covered in football gear and other equipment. A bench press is in the corner of your room and you're old bunk bed had become a King Bed in fact you're room was even bigger tHan it used to be. You step out into the hallway and notice a family photo with your Dad, Mom, older brother Chris, and younger brother Benji. Except there seems to be a stranger where you used to be...you fall down when you realize it's you in your new body.

    Lucas walks out of the bathroom and sees you on the floor. "Bathroom's all yours cuz" He says before going back into the guest room. You get up from your behind and grab a towel before heading into the bathroom. This time you notice the changes. It was as you kept AJ's body but your old features stayed behind a bit. You sigh and take a quick shower. After you leave the bathroom you pass Benji in the hall, back from his swim meet. "Hey big bro" He says waving at you before going into his room. Thats odd you think.

    When you were you're normal self Benji ignored you in fact he seemed to be embarrassed of you. Looks like something more than a reality change had occured. You look for the wrapper you bought the magic socks with and see the back. It seemed that there were rules written on it.

    RULES:
    1.)You must wear another person's shoes to become a carbon copy of them
    2.) Reality
    -If in the body of another person for too long reality will adjust with your new body. To turn back you must find the shoes you put on to transform into the copy you're in.
    - If you wear the shoes of someone and they wear your shoes, reality won't adjust due to the fact that you both replaced each other in reality.
    3.) Time
    -When putting on the shoes on someone, you will transform into the exact copy of them including their age when they last worn them.
    Any Questions? Call 777-7***

    You stare dumbfounded and decide to look for your old shoes. But is that really what you want?
