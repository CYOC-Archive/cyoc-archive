    "You know, there's no real rush to get back home, is there?" asked Kellie.

    "Well, Cole can take care Tommy for a while," said Kumiko.

    "And Gloria will still be a while in getting back from her date," said Lisa.

    "So, why don't we just have a little teen fun?" asked Kellie, "Just a little something to do as normal teens?"

    "Well, I suppose it wouldn't be so bad," said Kumiko.

    "So long as it doesn't take too much time," said Lisa.

    "Great," said Kellie, "I know just the place."

    Kellie wasn't taking chances for extra teen drama (as well as possible magic attacks). The ice cream parlor was public enough to prevent that kind of stuff. Kumiko and Lisa quickly warmed up to the idea.

    "Oh my gosh, this tastes so good," said Lisa as she was eating her ice cream.

    "Yeah, it's great to eat this stuff and not have to worry about how much weight you put on," said Kumiko.

    "I don't think the Ring of Age can make you skinnier," said Kellie.

    "Well, I doubt the extra calories are going to stick after we change back," said Kumiko.

    "It's nice to hang with you two without any hassle," said Kellie, "The Lisa and Kumi of this world were more likely to snap at me for some perceived threat."

    "I know we were hard as teens," said Lisa, "But we did outgrow our problems. And I think they're going to turn over a new leaf soon."

    "Just keep your patience with them," said Kumiko, "It'll be worth it."

    Not long afterwards, it was time for them to return to their own reality. It was simple to restore them to their proper ages though Katie was needed to take them home.

    "You know, as weird as it was, it was fun," said Lisa.

    "I think I'll stick with my own reality's weirdness," said Kumiko.

    "I hope we get to meet again," said Kellie.

    "Next time, I'll bring your alternate self along," said Katie.

    "That sounds...intriguing," said Kellie. Soon after that, the adults left to their own dimension. The circumstances were odd, but it was a relatively normal teen day.
