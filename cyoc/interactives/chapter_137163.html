    ---------------TIME SKIP 6 MONTHS----------------

    The demon had left Zoe to her own devices and was almost forgotten about, Almost. It was there, lurking in the back of Zoe's mind, always present like someone watching over your shoulder. Zoe had finished her junior year of high school without incident. Greg was given a funeral and a small memorial in the school garden. Zoe's guilt over his death slowly ebbed away as the months ticked by and summer break was a great distraction. 'Hell, Greg was a jerk anyway,' Mary thought as he was brought up by Casey one day in August, 'I've got bigger things to worry about, like school and band practice.'

    Zoe had joined another band during summer vacation, as Greg's band had disbanded after his untimely demise. Zoe was lead guitarist and loved playing her Les Paul she had saved up for working part time during summer.

    As the school year drew on, from August through September and then October arrived, Zoe's new favorite month. A month she didn't feel as outcast as the other 11 months. Her taste in wardrobe had shifted over summer, and black clothes were more appealing to her. By July, Zoe was dressing up like a regular goth, complete with black colored lipstick.
    This suited her new band just fine as it fit with their theme. Zoe's parents felt it was her just coping with the death of Greg and let it be.

    Over the summer, Zoe had developed a crush on a new student; Justin. Justin was well built at 6' 2" with dark brown hair, a deep voice and a very handsome face. Zoe would look into his emerald green eyes and get lost in thought several times. However, no matter how many times she tried, he just would not notice her. This was becoming increasingly more frustrating as the weeks went by.

    "Gahhh! Why won't he give me a try?," vented Zoe.
    Zoe and Casey had decided to hang out in the park after school to today and chat.
    "Maybe you just aren't his type?," offered Casey, "I've seen him talk to that bimbo Nancy plenty of times."
    "Melon tits!? But they're clearly fake!," whined Zoe, "and she doesn't have two brain cells to rub together in that empty space she calls a head!"
    "Calm down, there is no reason to get worked up over," comforted Casey, "What about Mark? He's hot."
    "He's president of the anime club! The. Anime. Club. Casey."
    "So he has some odd tastes, at least he's open about it." Casey's eyes narrowed in thought. "What weird shit is Justin into? I'm going with bondage."
    "Don't ruin him for me Casey, I've got to find a way to get him to notice me, but how?"
    "Well if Nancy is anything to go by; Justin likes a woman that has jumbo sized knockers, a big ass and thick thighs. Oh and an IQ of a goldfish. Unless your parents are willing to pay for plastic surgery or you can spontaneously get a second puberty, you are S.O.L."

    Zoe felt a presence in her mind shift around and her eyes widened. "Casey, I've gotta go. I'll see you later ok?"
    "Ok see ya later, don't forget we've got to get together to help plan the Halloween dance tomorrow," called Casey as Zoe ran off towards her car.

    As Zoe drove down the road to get back home she felt the demon inside her, after nearly a year of silence speak: It spike in a taunting sing song voice.
    "Get bent!"

    Zoe pulled the car into her driveway and went inside to her room. Zoe's parents were out of town for the next few days and she had the run of the house.
    "No, my answer still hasn't changed," said Zoe as she stepped in front of her full length mirror and faced her demonic self.
