    Altered Fates

    Luke is becoming an actual Sumo Wrestler!

    added by Blue the Sumo Wolf A year ago O Magic Sumo Reality alteration


    “I know I didn’t just throw them randomly!”

    Luke frantically searching for his clothes. He may have accidentally dropped his phone while he was trying to get a picture of himself in a mawashi, in his defense he didn’t expect 'this' to happen!

    “Come on! Where are you- Huh? Wait what?!” He just rememberd he put his clothes on Dave’s bed… But now, They're gone! ‘But I'm pretty sure I lay them on the bed, and now it's gone. Where am I gonna find my clothes now? This isn't funny anymore!’ Luke is agitated now!

    Luke doesn't have time for this! He decided to go back, and retrance his steps to see where he put his clothes. As he kept looking around the house, from the front door, the hallway, Dave's room, the bathroom, and the shrine at the corner of the house where two ancient mawashi (the Black one 'The Ancestor', and the White one 'The Rival') hung up on the wall along with an assortment of other sumo accessories below them.

    Wait what?! There is two of them now!

    Luke knows he took the Black one off the wall, and wore it. Which he is still wearing it. So how come there are two of them on the wall now? It's like he never touch them. 'What the Hell is going on!?' he though.

    "Dave!" Luke is freaking out! "I thought you said, your family only held two of these!" Dave came in, and saw what he was refering to. And that made Dave's eyes wide. "We do! But those are the historial clothes, We also have freshly made mawashi that are worn today. Which goes beyond just Black or White colored mawashi."

    "So tell me then," Luke started. "Why is there two of the same Black mawashi here, on the wall, and around my waist?" Dave just looks at him sadly "This is the Medallion's Magic, It's changing the Reality."

    "What!?!"

    As Luke says that his black mawashi started vibrating, and it lasted a minute until it stopped. And Dave looks at it weirdly. "Dave? What just happened?"

    "Luke your... Your mawashi just change from black to blue."

    "Huh? No way! Your lying!" Luke dosen't want to believe this. "There's no way my clothes can suddenly change!"

    "Then see for yourself." Dave points to the body mirror. A skeptical Luke waddles to it, and... Yes! In fact his clothes did indeed changed!

    It went from an old Ancient Black mawashi, to a new (albit, as if he used it for 6 years) Shiny Royal Blue mawashi. This is not Dave's great-something-grandfather's belt. This is Luke's very own mawashi, his own sumo belt. He's officially becoming an actual Sumo Wrestler!

    Luke is so baffled at this. He's turns towards Dave with fear. "Dave! You gatta helpu me!" he desperatly say. Without knowing it yet, he was starting to lose his grasp on the English grammar, talking in a way that is not used to the language. "Why this happeni?!"

    "Luke, I'm sorry. I-... I don't think I can help you." Luke did not like to hear that. "Why no help?" Dave was about to speak, but then something materialized around Luke. It was what looked like a blue, and white ocean pattern bathrobe. ' A Yukata '.

    "Luke..." Dave begins. "It was written: Anyone who wears somebodys clothes, with the mindset of ridicule or mockery will live that person's life for 365 days... And the existence of the wearers, and memories of those who met him or her will temporarily fade away. Unless they made contact with the medallion." So in other words, Everyone except Luke, and Dave will never remember Luke at all.

    Luke's mind is going crazy! He is going to live as a Sumo Wrestler for a whole year!

    "Watashi ni nani ga okita no ka?!" Luke exclaim in fear.
    (What is happening to me?!)

    "Nani?" Luke just now realizes he just spoke another language. He shakes his head. "Davu-" he clears his throat, and speaks slowly "Dave... Help... Me... Clothes... Find..."

    Dave knew what he was trying to say but. "Luke, I don't think you'll ever get-..."

    And suddenly another change happen!

    What is it this time?
