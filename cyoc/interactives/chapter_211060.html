The sports bra and yoga pants set were the same, though now they were joined by another new article of clothing: similarly colored gym socks! The now trio of clothing sad idly by in the changing room as the day wore on. Nearing the store's closing time, the door to the changing room slowly opened...

    As a clothing store employee, it was Mike's job to keep the store clean and organized. He took pride in his work, and he was always on the lookout for things that needed to be tidied up. This time, as Mike was cleaning out the women's changing room, he came across a strange sight. In the corner of the room, there was a matching set of gym clothes, socks, pants, and a sports bra, all neatly folded and placed together. It was odd, because normally, customers would leave their clothes in a pile on the floor or on the bench. But these clothes were almost like they had been intentionally left out.

    Mike picked up the clothes and examined them. They looked like they were brand new, and they still had the tags attached. It was as if someone had tried them on, but had decided not to buy them. But that wasn't the only strange thing Mike found in the changing room. Next to the clothes, there was an old polaroid camera. It was a bit battered and scratched, but it still looked like it was in good working condition.

    Mike picked up the camera and turned it over in his hands. It felt heavy and solid, like a well-made piece of equipment. Mike wondered why someone would have left it in the changing room.
