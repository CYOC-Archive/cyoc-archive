    Skeesy & Bruce team up

    As they were walking from the jail 'Sam' (the former Janet) explained to 'Jordan' (the former Bruce), that her twin was waiting in the car. He explained how he had mistakenly removed the wrong woman from the scene of the fight the previous night. He explained to 'Jordan' that he thought their best chance of getting the medallion back was to work together.

    Whilst feigning sleep in the jail cell, Bruce had overheard her twin admit to being the original Jorda, therefore surmised correctly that the woman waiting in the car must be the original Marcia. She had detected how this Marcia was not particularly popular with the the other Jordan nor with the blonde or the black woman she shared a cell with. On the basis that 'The enemy of my enemy is my friend' she decided that she would give this woman a chance. She was unaware that Marcia was also a former man nor that Marcia and her husband had enjoyed sex multiple times in the last 24 hours.

    'Sam' made the introductions. Bruce and Skeesy shook hands and agreed to work together. Bruce then explained what she had overheard in the cell. "Well I have news on the blonde twins. Neither of them have the medallion and neither of them are blondes anymore. Samantha has swapped bodies with a black prostitute and the girl who calls herself Jordan now has a copy of this body" she gestured at herself. "However she has told the arresting officers that she is Marcia Skeesy. I guess she is trying to steal your identity now or at least give you a criminal record" she said this to Skeesy. "Those girls are no friends of yours"

    Skeesy replied "So I now realise. She's welcome to that name though. It's not my real name"

    "Oh! What is your real name?"

    "Mark.... I was a man before I encountered that medallion. I'd like to be a man again"

    "I fully understand" replied Bruce. "I was a man as well. Sam used to be my wife Janet. We are sisters together now I guess. Best we give you a different name though. Mark doesn't suit you any more.t"

    "I guess not... Any suggestions?"

    "How about Belinda? It was Sam's middle name" she turned and looked at her husband "Any objection?"

    "No. You are welcome . Sam I am" he turned to Skeesy. "I would be honoured if you were to call yourself Belinda"

    "Well Belinda Skeesy I am then. At least while in this body. We need to find that medallion"

    'Jordan' added "The black woman said she had the medallion at the bar but lost it during the fight. They think it is probably lying or the floor of the bar somewhere. We need to go there straight away and look for it"

    "We tried last night. There was no sign of it" 'Sam' replied. "We got kicked out again after the barman saw us. Your current body is not welcome at the bar anymore"

    "We have to find it before the twins do" 'Jordan' replied. "We should try and keep them in the jail"

    "From what you have said they are no longer the twins" said Belinda. "We shouldn't talk here though. I live nearby. How about we head back to my place and discuss strategies there. I could do with a change of clothes"
