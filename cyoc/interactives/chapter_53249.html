    What's up with this vial?

    Suzy looked back down to see the vial brown and popped the cap to drink. The color had changed without her knowing though.

    Unknown to her, the vial changed to purple. In a moment her perspective changed again as her height shot up another 6 inches, making her 6'1''. "Well, that's not what I was wanting but it could be worse," Suzy said to no one in particular. She closed the vial and the liquid filled back up again. The color was purple again! Suzy sat and waited to the colors to cycle. After 20 minutes though, the color had never changed. It was still just as purple as it was before.

    "What the fuck?" At the sound of a swear word, her children kicked violently inside her stomach.

    "Sorry sweeties!"

    Suzy waited another 5 minutes with no change at all. Suzy thought that maybe she had not drank the whole thing before so it was stuck on the color. Without any choice, she tilted it back and drank again. Quickly, Suzy gained another 6 inches. From her seated position, she couldn't really tell how tall she was but figured she was now 6'7''. Looking at the vial, the liquid reappeared and was now green. Suzy shuddered at the thought of making all the permanent. After a few minutes though, Suzy became restless and put her shoes back on. Suzy stood up and started walking down the sidewalk.

    After walking a bit, Suzy noticed that she dwarfed everyone. That wasn't the part she didn't get though. She was really dwarfing everyone. Even at 6'7'' there should be at least a couple of guys near her height! What was going on?

    Suzy ducked into a clothing shop for three reasons; One, she was curious about her height. Two, her feet were killing her again and three, she really had to pee!

    Her first stop was the bathroom, after relieving herself she asked the shop owner for help with her measurements. The shop owner's eyes were large as she got up on a stool.

    "Ma'am, it looks like you are a shade over 7 feet tall."

    "Seven feet! That can't be right! I should be 6'7''!!!"

    "Ma'am I'm sorry but that's what the tape says."

    "Thanks."

    Suzy took a seat and thought about it. Why did she grow a foot instead of six inches? Suzy looked at the vial again and now the color changes were coming fast and often. Thinking about it, Suzy decided to go home.

    30 minutes later Suzy finally made it. She had to stop twice because of her feet and also, her back. She had to lay down for awhile. Getting home, Suzy laid on her bed and fell fast asleep. She would figure this out tomorrow.
