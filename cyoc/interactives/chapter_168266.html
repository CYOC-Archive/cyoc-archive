    Sarah could not be found anywhere but what he could find was a little brown haired baby girl who was crawling towards him.

    The moment the baby was under Isaac, she began to pull on his pants as if she was trying to tell him something.
    At first Isaac thought it was just the baby of some member of the organization, but noticed that there were many large pieces of clothing lying around the hall. And not only that, a lot of those clothes looked like the ones Sarah was wearing just now.

    "Is this baby Sarah? How is that possible? The transformation was supposed to have already ended."

    Then Isaac proceeded to take the little baby and the rest of the clothes that were all over the place.

    "Well Sarah, I have no idea how you could regress into a baby even though the transformation was already over. But right now I'm going to try to find out what happened." Isaac told the now baby Sarah.

    Sarah was trying to say a word to Isaac but all that came out of her mouth was "Gah"

    "I see you are trying to tell me something. It must be very frustrating not being able to speak. Unfortunately, you will have to do this mission at this age. The machine was only programmed to regress you to the age you would have chosen."

    Hearing this, Sarah became very upset. How was she supposed to carry out the mission at this age? She was supposed to be a teenager, not a helpless baby wearing diapers.
