    Transform or Dare?

    Well Armed

    Previous chapter

    added by sneme A year ago A BM

    Jen felt 30 pairs of eyes staring at her. Despite her new, long bunny ears, no one flinched. Her teacher was still looked at her, expecting her essay, or a good excuse. Jen was nervous, but she tried to put up her arrogant persona despite being thrown off by her change.

    "Well, you should've watched harder, because it's in there," the head cheerleader said. Students gasped, and the teacher was pissed. Jen, however, felt something else start to tingle. Her arms.

    Stunned gasps and an angry tirade faded into the background as Jen stared at her arms. She watched in shock and fear as her arm hair darkened and thickened, becoming jet-black fur. The skin on her palm also darkened, becoming almost black. Her hands swelled, and her arms lengthened. She felt bones growing, thickening, twisting, muscles rearranging and swelling. Her arms and hands were growing huge and long, far larger than any human's, but they still looked ape-like. The sensations should have been painful, but she was too stunned to process what was going on. As soon as they started, the changes were over, and she snapped back into reality.

    Her teacher was angrily saying something about detention, her arrogant parents raising such a brat, and so on and so forth while her classmates looked on in amusement at the drama they were already being served up on a Monday morning.

    Jen, however, could pay them little mind. Her arms had turned into silverback gorilla arms! She held them out in front of her, marveling at their size. Each hand was the size of her head, and her palms were hard and leathery. She couldn't really sit comfortably in the desk with them, given their huge size. If she'd spread them out, her armspan would be almost 7 feet! She was also rudely reminded of her first change as her ears drooped down, no longer being held up from her shocked and defensive position earlier. She felt them fall against her shoulders, long and silky. She was overwhelmed by her changes.

    Her teacher swiveled around after giving Jen some sort of punishment, she assumed detention, and then started her lesson. Jen fumbled with her new gorilla arms for her notebook and pencil, finding it hard to scrounge in her backpack. Eventually she pulled them out, and her pencil was dwarfed by her new inhuman hands. At least she could still grip it, but it was hard. She had to contort herself uncomfortably to write on the desk, pushing her arms far out to her sides. Eventually she got the hang of it, but her handwriting was atrocious now.

    When the lesson ended and the bell rang, she stuffed her notebook and pencil back into her backpack. This broke the pencil easily, and she realized her arms were now much stronger than they had been. As she pushed herself out of her desk, she found her new arms were long enough to reach her calves without her bending over! It took all her strength not to cry as she walked to her next class, her gigantic gorilla arms swaying heavily at her sides. She looked like such a freak with these arms, not to mention the bunny ears atop her head. And she had no idea what had caused these changes, but at least no one else was freaking out.
