    Mad Science

    Mysterious Slime anthro in the snow.

    As you fall on the ground being bearded by the snow, your body freezing into a solid state... You noticed something moving in the snow...

    You could barely make it out... it was... a ... another slime anthro?

    You must be going crazy...

    It was moving... before you can ask if the blue slime vixen can see it... you turn and see her right leg snap and she falls face first into the snow not getting up.

    You wince yet turning back you see the third slime anthro moving unimpeded by the weather...

    With nothing to lose You cry out to them...

    They don’t notice...

    With the last of your strength you cry out a second time.. this time they noticed......

    ...The last thing you see before passing out is the unknown anthro slime slithering closer to you, then darkness...

    ...

    ...

    .....

    ...

    When you next wake up, instead of being in a fluffy bed you wake up on a hard rock in a dark cave with a fire.

    You look around and noticed the blue anthro fox scientist was next to you, still in frozen pieces, but thawing, you figured that was a good sign.

    You turn to the light source and notice the anthro slime that had saved both your lives sitting next to it keeping it lit with fire wood.

    You had never seen anything like them, granted the only anthro slimes you have ever seen were your self and the former scientist you turned your self, both of you were anthro foxes colored green and blue respectively.

    But this was a slime...

    What do you do now?
