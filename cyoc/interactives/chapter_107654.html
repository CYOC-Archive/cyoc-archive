    You take some time to look at your stats, marveling at how accurate the thing was. You decided to look online to see if anything about this program being a hoax or a virus showed up, but neither TransDem Labs or Chronivac produced any results. Remembering the description at the beginning, you look for a way to contact the company through the program. Under the tab labeled "Help" you found an option for a chat. Opening this, you are given a window with a generic chat interface. "Please type your question..." was written in the field.

    What is this program?

    "The Chronivac can make any change to your reality that you desire. As the Primary User, you can choose who may or may not use the program, and how. The Chronivac appears to you as the type of technology your reality is familiar with, but this may change as you alter your universe. The Chronivac has only one limitation: it will not allow you to alter any other reality.

    Please type your question..."

    Who is TransDem Labs?

    "TransDem Labs is a subsidiary of Multiverse Holdings, Ltd. TransDem Labs has randomly selected you as the sole recipient of the Chronivac 4.0. Your usage data will be collected to further our understanding of your specific dimension; you may not opt out of this data collection. By using this device, you agree to hold TransDem Labs and Multiverse Holdings, Ltd. harmless from any wrongdoing in the event of catastrophic or irreversible choices on your part. Should the Chronivac malfunction due to manufacturing causes, please type HELP into this field (if still possible) and a representative will walk you through troubleshooting.

    Please type your question..."

    After several hours of asking questions on how to use the Chronivac, you understood the machine and interface inside and out. If this thing could do what it claimed, you were basically a god now. The thought excited you, especially because it meant you could try out any and all of your kinks (even the impossible ones) without fear of repercussion. But to make sure you retained the power to benefit and recover from changes you might make, you needed to test something you had read about during your search.

    Accessing the options menu for the Chronivac itself, you made it so that the program would no longer be a device attached to your computer, but rather, to you. This would ensure you could not lose the Chronivac and the powers it entailed and gave you unrestricted access to its full potential. Making sure everything was correct, you clicked the Accept button and watched the changed excitedly.

    The Chronivac transformed from a outdated-looking cd-rom and handheld device to a ball of holographic data, swirling in a bright orb in front of you. This ethereal orb floated inside of you, phasing through your flesh and nesting itself within your brain. Now your own body was the computer through which the Chronivac operated, more efficient and practical than your old desktop.

    You could feel the power flowing through your body and it was intoxicating. You wanted to try out all of your wildest fantasies, but couldn't decide which to chose first. After some long thought, you decided to change...
