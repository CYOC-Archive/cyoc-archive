    Auguste Clown

    ...that your skin is now a peachy orange color, and you have white patches around your eyes and your mouth. Inside your muzzle the lower lip is blue black and stretches into a wide smile. The muzzle also stretched down to your chin which has a blue bare painted at the very bottom. Your cheeks are a lighter shade of red than your nose and inside each are three black freckles.

    The patches around your eyes stretch up to your forehead and are topped with wide black eyebrows. Inside your eyes are lined with black and shaded blue; your eyelashes look almost mascaraed.

    The most dramatic change, however, is your hair. Around the edges a thick mass of blue curls stretches from your ears to the back of your head, and spreads across your shoulders. Despite your young age the hair on top is gone, with a tiny red derby set at a jaunty angle on smooth bare pate.

    Your clothes have changed too. Now you are dressed in a loose fitting light blue collared shirt with the sleeves stretching down to your wrists, a red vest lined in yellow and a puffy yellow bow tie with blue polka dots. Your pants are a darker blue, matching your remaining hair, and baggy looking. They're a tad too short, however, revealing the red striped socks you are wearing.
    Your shoes are now size 17 red and blue wing tips with 10 inch wide toes. Somehow you know by feeling that your feet have stretched to fit them. On your hands are red fingerless gloves.

    Because of you baggy clothes, the last thing you notice is that you have gained at least 15 pounds, possibly twenty. You were skinny to start with but now you are rather plump and you face also shows the weight.

    After the initial shock feel your transformed face, then take off the hat and rub your scalp. It's as smooth as an egg; you are truly bald.

    You give your nose another squeeze. HONK! it sounds and the feeling of sensual giddiness returns, deeper this time.
    "I-I'm a clown, An auguste clown," you say in a deep voice with a goofy lilt. All of a sudden a full knowledge of clown routines and joke both practical and impractical, as well as an understanding of the clown hierarchy and where augustes like you fit in, enters your head and....

    What do you do now?
