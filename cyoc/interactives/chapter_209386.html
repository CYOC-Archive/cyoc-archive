He read through a piece of paper included, and his eyes widened as he did so. His thoughts being that this was either some sort of elaborate prank, or he'd gotten the most immersive photo editor he'd ever seen. In fact, the vaguely feminine voice that emitted as part of the Chronivac software was nothing like anything he'd heard before. It was cybernetic obviously, but there was something...off about it.

Lean, reasonably tall, red haired, blue eyed. He was represented in all accuracy on the computer screen. His initial thought was that somehow the machine had hacked into his CPU's camera feature and scanned him that way. He was after all sitting in his birthday suit right in front of it.

The coding on this must be something else if it can do all that... He'd need to figure that out later though. His mind was reeling - and he wanted to figure out for now what else it could do.

A-A really...really advanced photo editing service..." But he was increasingly thinking about it. Wondering, his curiosity burning in the front of his mind as he tried to ration out what was going on. He wanted to see for himself if it was real. If it actually could change him - or someone else - or do something big.
