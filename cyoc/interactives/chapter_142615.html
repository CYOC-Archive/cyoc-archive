    Noah finally made it to the local park and breathed a deep and satisfied sigh. He had never felt more alive as he looked at all the different people he could mess with. He strolled in and eventually found a nice bench that had a good view of most of the park. There was a pond he was staring directly at but to his right was a playground and to his left but further in the distance was a pathway with many walkers and joggers going about their day. Noah laughed as the park visitors were in for a change.

    He decided he'd start small as he saw a male jogger pass him by. The guy had a fully shaved head, massive biceps, and legs built for running. He was wearing a tank top with jogger shorts and sneakers. He continued to run as Noah wrote, "The guy who just passed me is a woman." Noah felt a similar wave of distortion hit him as the man began to change. The man kept going as his height drastically shrank. The former 6 something man was barely over 5 feet. His head also exploded as blond hair began flowing from his scalp. It draped across his shoulders before it all became magically tied in a tight ponytail.

    He stopped to take a breather and stretch on a tree, seemingly unaware of the changes. His body mass went down drastically but his muscles were still clearly toned. As he stretched, his but began to inflate like a ballon until it was a nice ladies ass surrounded by new yoga pants. His tank top altered as his pecs grew into decent pairs of breasts, barely kept at bay by a newly formed sports bra. Noah smiled as he watched the final change. While he leaned his leg up on the tree, Noah saw the bulge in his pants shrink until it could not be seen. The woman happily finished her stretches before she continued her jog, unaware she had ever been a man.

    "This is so awesome." Noah thought. "So I know I can change forms, but just how far can I go with bending reality?"

    Noah glanced at the playground and saw a group of parents all sitting around a table, while their children all happily played. Noah decided the next change should be big, and the playground gave him the perfect idea. He then wrote, "All adults have the roles of kids while all the kids have roles of adults." Another wave of distortion hit as changes began. This time there were no physical changes, but rather cosmetic ones.

    Suddenly there were no more kids on the jungle gym. All the adults that had previously been sitting around talking were now climbing all over the equipment. Noah watched as their casual short morphed into overall and their modest dresses became pink and poofy. The jungle gym, swings, and every other object on the playground also grew, seemingly doing so to accommodate its new users. On the tables nearby, there were now a bunch of calm children wearing adult oriented clothes, calmly talking about their adults.

    Noah looked down and saw his shirt now had some cartoon character he didn't recognize on it, while his shorts budged as a pull up appeared underneath it. Noah blushed a little. He knew that reality changed so no one cared but it was still embarrassing nonetheless. Noah still watched in awe as children walked around pushing older people in strollers and children using wallets to buy their parents ice cream. He had made a world where kids were the responsible overseers while adults were to be cared for.

    "Why hello there big boy!" A voice came from behind Noah. He turned to see a girl no older than 7 wearing a business blouse and skirt re sized to fit her. She was holding the hand of a woman wearing a tutu, and who was endlessly staring around. The girl spoke again, and Noah realized his mistake. "Where are your children young man?" She said worried.

    Noah realized that he was now considered a kid for all intents and purposes. He didn't know what to say so he decided to just avoid it all together. He took his pencil and erased the sentence. Suddenly reality shifted again and when Noah's mind cleared he saw that everything was back to normal. He turned to see the woman and girl walking away from him, each with their proper clothes on again. Noah just laughed at how easy it was to create any reality he wanted.

    He looked around the park and didn't know where to keep going, or even what change to do. Eventually, he found his next targets.
