    With the two weeks done and Gary fully on top form in the team again, the athlete knew that he should leave well enough alone in terms of the Chronivac. He had gotten all that he wanted out of it...right? But the more that day that he looked at it the more tempted he was to just go ahead and do something else. Maybe it was just the potential, maybe it was some kind of latent and only just recently unlocked appreciation for transformations, but he was itching for another.

    Maybe not something as unfortunate and poorly planned out as that time he put on like 70 lbs of fat, but definitely something to really see what this thing could do.

    Stretching and cupping his groin as he lightly thrust into his hand, Gary sat there in just a pair of boxers - indeed, probably some of the biggest boxers in the house, made for him specifically. Maybe, going forward, he could try and see about getting more clothes thru the device as well.

    But that was neither here nor there. It was time to think about what to do next. He loved watching as Pete and Ty muscled up like that, seeing them go from their pre-established roles and looks to now being muscular and adapting to their physiques. He remembered seeing Pete awkwardly dealing with his new popularity with the girls, and it seemed the increases in Tyler's libido were causing him to have to duck outta class mostly during fourth or fifth period to take care of himself.

    More than a few times, Gary stopped by just to hear Tyler groaning as he readily took care of his dick.

    So...Gary thought as he tugged himself out of his fantasies and erstwhile sexual memories, there were only so many ways to really enjoy this thing during the context of high school. Maybe it was time to really look into some of the other options. He didn't know how good he was with all the animal stuff on there, or anything like that. But he did see that his age was another thing that was just a mutable variable within the program of the Chronivac.

    Maybe that was a good place to get started while Gary thought up more ways to change his good friends Tyler and Pete...

    Looking in at the Coach as well, he noted it seemed Coach Eli was really taking to the new youth he had been given. Maybe, Gary thought, I could be some sort of new teacher or something, and then grow into more maturity and tenure over time at the school. Besides, if I don't like the new role I get, I can always just use the Chronivac to revert the transformations and go back to how I was, such was what the jock thought.

    But then thinking again about the Coach running around and doing drills like a student really finalized the idea Gary was having about what to do next in terms of transformations. Snapping his fingers, Gary smirked, "Ohh fuck me, I've got it! I love this plan already. Let's go with this...I'll change my age to...mm, I wanna see the changes in my stature, position and power at the school over two weeks. Okay..."

    Gary put in a two week long transformation to being full on 55, specifying that he would work at the school as the Football Coach. Looking at the build after he was done, Gary made sure that it would preserve his general physique even as he aged - granted, there would be some sensible and normal weight loss as the aging went on, but Gary was okay with that in the name of discovering how this transformation was gonna go.

    "Now for the other one..." Gary said with a wry smile on his face, "Let's see what I'm gonna do with you, Coach Bramden. You love indulging yourself in all that sexy time now...so let's get you younger. See how you handle this." Deciding to regress Eli Bramden to 15, which would put him at about Sophomore Year, Gary wondered what a young teen Eli would be like.

    Smiling, Gary started the transformation and then clicked through the "recommended reality changes".

    However he then remembered he had forgotten some friends, and decided to see about Pete and Tyler. Well, he didn't want to change up their whole worlds just yet. So he decided to see how much further they could go with muscling up before they started to get at all concerned. Probably not at all, Gary thought.

    So once again he put in transformations for the two of them to each gain 5 pounds of muscle a day for the next two weeks, just to see what ended up happening to them. Gary leaned back and started to think, wondering how things were gonna change even over the course of the next day with how things were set up on there...
