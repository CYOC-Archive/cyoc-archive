    Midnight surprise

    Lily was a thirty year old pregnant woman who was currently lying in the delivery room of a hospital seconds away from giving birth to her baby on New Year's Eve.

    While she was suffering through her labor a male doctor along with a female nurse prepared to deliver the baby.

    "That's it Lily just keep pushing, your almost there" the doctor said encouragingly then looked up at the clock which read 11:58.

    "Another two more minutes and your son is going to be born on the new year. Now wouldn't that be special" he said as Lily just gave him a small smile despite the pain she was currently in.

    The doctor saw the infant began to slide out and took the baby into his arms.

    "Congratulations Lily you just gave birth to a healthy baby boy, and just in time too cause it was almost midnight" he told her right as the minute hand hit the twelve.

    Suddenly there was a blinding flash of white light as the doctor and nurse along with her new son vanished right before Lily's eyes. At the same time the room appeared to grow bigger before she was quickly engulfed by her hospital night gown. Frightened Lily began to kick at the fabric with her legs until it fell off her and she could see again. Sitting up she was shocked to find that she was now a baby and wearing a diaper.

    "Wait how did this happen? Why am I a baby again...and speaking of babies where's mine?" she asked aloud.

    Crawling over to the edge of the hospital bed on her hands and legs Lily peeked over the side and saw two three year old toddlers, a boy and girl wearing nothing but diapers talking to one another excitedly.

    Looking closely at them Lily realized that it was the doctor and nurse and that they had been turned into babies as well.

    "I'm not sure how it happened either but for some reason I feel really happy to be a baby again" he said with a smile.

    "Me too" the nurse replied, "And I'm also glad I'm wearing a diaper."

    "Why's that?"

    "You'll see" she giggled as seconds later a piddling sound could be heard as the front of her diaper began to darken.

    She then let out a sigh as she finished, "Ahhh I've been holding that in for hours."

    Turning to look at the doctor the nurse saw he was making a weird face. Before she could ask what he was doing the toddler let out a muffled fart as the seat of his diaper bulged outward.

    Smelling what he did she waves her hands in front of her face, "Ewwww you made a stinky."

    "I did" the toddler doctor stated proudly, "You weren't the only one who was holding something in."

    The two of them then break out in a childish fit of giggles.

    Lily having seen enough then...
