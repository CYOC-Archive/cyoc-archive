    Pete wandered into the tent. Looking for his friend he had heard about this show and thought he would go in
    and see what is going on.

    Pete would have stood out anyway at 7' he made a big entrance even when he was trying to be subtle.

    Hello sir Madame Illusia said.

    Everyone looked around as did Pete.

    All eyes fell on Pete.

    Yes you big man.

    Pete was trying to disappear but there was no hiding from Madame Illusia

    I am looking for a friend

    I know Madame Illusia said Leroy

    No Timothy Porter

    He no longer goes by that name he is now Leroy

    Come closer

    Pete found himself following here requests and was soon on stage with her

    So you would like to know what happened to Leroy

    Yes please

    Well audience would anyone like to help Pete find out what happened to his friend

    Wait how did you know my name?

    I know all

    Maybe your a little to big to see the big picture one voice rang out in the audience

    I think you may be right Madame Illusia said

    with that someone in the middle of the audience shot up taller than the others. And at the same time Pete
    shrank to just a bit over 3'. He was swimming in his clothes.

    It looks like those are too big for you. Why don't you trade clothes with your friend over there. The man that
    had shot up so tall looked like he was about to burst out of his clothes. Now James had always been one of the
    little people but was taller than anyone now. When they changed clothes Pete found that he was about crotch
    level with most men.

    Now how about a new life for you just like your friend. Who has a new life for this boy.

    A brusk man stepped forward and said I have a good job for him. He can work in my bar. If it hadn't been apparent by the look the rainbow flag tatooed on his arm would have told everyone what sort of bar he had. You

    But I don't want to do that.

    Madame Illusia made a wave of her hand and all doubt fell from Pete's mind about his new life and he went off
