    "I'm fine, I'm just going to take us back to mother, she's probably worried about you." Higon said

    "After I put you down, I will be sure to talk to them." Higon said, and he carried you over to where you talked with Tiamat earlier and slumped down onto his stomach tired.

    "You are heavier than you look sis." Higon said exhausted
