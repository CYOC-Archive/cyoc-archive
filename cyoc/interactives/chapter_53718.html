    Madam Serenity stayed the entire day with Trevor's family of gray and black lion people.
    They were polite, friendly, open, and eternally grateful for looking after and returning their
    cub to them.

    them she saw human beings... That was when the lioness Marie, Trevor's mother, eyes the
    color of bright honey and gray and black fur like her son began to explain her family's
    history. And before Serenity's eyes, the pictures morphed into naked lion men and naked
    lion women as lioness Marie spoke of them. Muscle growing, height increasing hair
    vanishing from the women's heads, and manes appear around the heads of the men. Golds
    and browns seemed to be on Jacob's side, Trevor's father. While on lioness Marie's side
    black and grays seemed to be dominant, though this was harder to tell in the earlier
    photos.

    "And that was my great-great-great-uncle Ervin," Marie said without missing a beat as the
    overweight balding man in the photo wearing a stripped sweater became a only slightly
    round naked lion man with a black mane. "He was excommunicated from The Pride when
    his Prime Mate found out he had mated with multiple females without her consent. She met
    another Leoman a couple years later who become her new Prime Mate Leo -no his parents
    didn't have much imagination- though one of her daughters from her litter with Uncle
    Ervin had a litter with Leo as well since she had yet to find her Prime Mate yet."

    "YOU'RE JOKING!" Madam Serenity exclaimed.

    Marie shrugged, "Not everyone finds their Prime Mate at the same pace, I had nearly given
    up hope when I met Jacob-"

    "No, no no, I mean, she really had -children- with her own step-father?"

    "What?" Marie blinked confused, "It's not like they were related by blood."

    before." 'Has every new species created by this damn game have nothing but sex on their
    minds-?!'

    Jacob became my prime mate." Marie chose to leave out that she hoped nothing was wrong
    with her having had two litters of one in a row, but didn't want to bring that up.

    Now that Madam Serenity had a moment to think she thought, 'The wording of the
    transformation power of the pride said that only those who they loved and who loved them
    changed into lions when interacting with them. Yet this Uncle Ervin in spite of being
    unfaithful changed anyway... did he make up with his family eventually?' Madam Serenity
    however didn't want to ask more about family knowing it would just cause more relatives

    amazement into purrs and cub cries, the young voice on the other end saying how it was
    great to have their pack-mate back.

    'It's like a plague.' Madam Serenity thought, 'Love between friends?' What were the four
    types of love again? 'Love between couples, love between family, love for one's god, and
    love for one's friends. Or are there more? Or are there less?' If there was one thing Madam
