    “Transfer package hmmm…” Jeff clicked on it getting his attention, then another menu appeared explaining the rules of the package.

    TRANSFER PACKAGE
    In this modality you can affect any aspect of a person, animal or object as you desire by swapping their attributes.

    What does this mean?
    As the package says, it “transfers” characteristics, meaning you will need at least two targets. What will be transferred is up to the user’s imagination, limited only by their ability to think or say that they want to move a certain thing. The package can transfer concrete qualities like weight, height, age, color, the size of body parts, and so on. Or it can transfer more abstract essences, like humanity, sexuality, knowledge, and gender identity.

    How to use?
    This package counts with some modes that will be explained below.

    Normal transfer: select two or more targets and the attributes you want to swap between them. Example: selecting two targets and swapping the height or age between them. It can by applied to specific body parts instead of the whole person, like swapping a two different body parts between two targets, even swapping a person’s feet with a dog’s paws.
    Percentage transfer: select two or more targets and the attributes you want to swap between them. In this mode you can choose how much will be transferred. Example: selecting two targets and swap 60% of their muscles, or even swap the 80% of the intelligence between a person and an animal.
    Copy transfer: select two or more targets and the attributes you want to swap between them. In this mode the second target wouldn’t steal the asset of the second but rather copying it. Example: using copy transfer to transfer the feminity of a woman into a man, the man will get all of her feminity without sacrificing the woman’s one. This mode also allows percentages.
    Merge transfer: select two or more targets and the assets to merge between them, this only applies for physical assets like body parts or objects rather than other aspects like size, sexuality, intelligence, etc. Example: merging a person’s skin with a metal or even merging two whole people together. The process can be reverted.

    All of the transfers, as previously stated, are up to the imagination of the user, there are no limits of what can or can’t be transferred.

    Additional notes
    - The transfer could also be used to steal a person’s body part if only one target’s asset are selected, like transferring the arms of target 1 to target 2
    - If more than two targets are selected and the change between them wasn’t specified, the swap will be random between them.
    - More than one transfer can be done at once. Like using a normal transfer to swap two target’s size and masculinity while using a percentage transfer to swap their body hair.
    - There’s a reality alteration option check for every time the user does a transfer if they want to make everyone else think the transfer is something normal or not.
    - Trans Inc. is not responsible for the death, conception, or injury of the target or the changed target(s).
