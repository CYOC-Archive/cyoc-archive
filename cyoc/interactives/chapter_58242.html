    "Third floor, room 304," the voice replied and I heard a buzzing noise on the door. I opened it and walked inside. The next room was a much bigger common room that had a lot of artwork on the walls, as well as several couches, an immense television set and other amenities you expect to find. I didn't care too much about the decor and went straight to the staircase. I ascended to the third floor and quickly found room 304.

    "Excuse me?" I asked, taken aback.
