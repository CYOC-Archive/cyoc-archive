    HIS NEIGHBOUR Phil.
    -------------------------------------------------------

    After dinner, he went outside to talk to his neighbour, Phil Mandiun.

    Phil was tall, but not as tall as Harold, and thin, with fair skin, brown shaggy hair, which was hidden by a gray beanie hat, 5 o'clock shadow, and black beady eyes. He wore a dark green t- shirt, pale blue sweats, and had blue flip-flops on his feet.

    Harold told Phil all about the MonsterMe.com site.
    At first, Phil didn't believe a site that turned people into monsters existed, but when Harold turned himself back into his monster self, that otherwise convinced him.

    When Harold turned himself back to his normal human self, Phil asked him if he could make a monster form for him. OF COURSE, Harold didn't think twice.

    Once he entered Phil's name into the site, he saw a full body picture of his neighbour on the screen, with his name, details, and all other stuff like that.

    UP Came the options.

    First, he gave the picture a third black beady eye, like his own. then, he made the ears pointed, almost bat-like.
    Phil made a suggestion of his own. "Maybe you could give me tentacles in place of arms?" he asked.
    Harold agreed. he changed the pic of Phil's arms to tentacles, then, he elongated Phil's feet, after removing the picture's flip flops.
    then he changed the nails of his fingers into claws. then he did the same to his toes.

    Then he removed Phil's shirt and sweats, leaving him in only his y-front underpants.

    PHil was reluctant about that at first, but soon relaxed when Harold stated that he'd make the awareness settings be so only Harold and he could see themselves as monsters.

    finally, came the fun bit: the colours.

    Phil chose his skin to be cyan, his favourite colour, his hair to be amber, and his tentacles to be purple.

    Satisfied, Harold clicked preview and watched as his digital icon slowly transformed into the more monstrous self.

    A new message box popped up: Initiate Transformation now? Yes/No

    Harold clicked 'Yes', and almost a second after that, Phil transformed into the monster form designed on the site.

    Phil took a look at his reflection in the glass in the window to his living room of his house.

    "Holy cow!" Phil exclaimed. "I'm a monster!"
