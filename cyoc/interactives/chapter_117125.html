    Mad Science

    Braiden gets into a fight with the local surfers who take his stuff

    added by Drakkenfyre 6 years ago I S O

    Braiden was really enjoying finding the sweet spot on the wave and riding the curl in, before the wave broke, he hopped off and paddled back out.

    Another guy on a surfboard shouted, "Hey, that was my wave. Get out of my ocean!"

    Braiden ignored him, and swam away from the guy in order to catch the next wave.

    The guy on the other board paddled after Braiden.

    Braiden surfed for about another 45 minutes with the weird guy stalking him, and finally decided to head for the beach and home.

    On the beach there were about a dozen guys waiting when Braiden headed for the changing room. They blocked his way. The guy who had been stalking him in the water came up behind with two more surfers.

    The stalker said, "I told you to get out of my ocean!"

    "I'm out," was Braiden's curt reply. He thought about saying it's not your ocean or it's a big ocean, but sometimes deference is the better part of valor.

    The guy paused, because Braiden had done what he said, maybe not right away, but he was out of the water.

    "Okay, well, this beach is a locals only beach!"

    "I am local, I live about a mile that way," Braiden said pointing inland.

    "Well, we haven't seen you before," the guy said.

    "Well, I didn't have a wetsuit that fit until today," Braiden answered truthfully.

    There was some murmuring about the guys blocking the changing room entrance.

    During the lull while the brute was thinking, Braiden said, "Excuse me, I need to get showered and changed, I am expected for lunch in an hour."

    "Hm, okay, so you say you're a local, let's see your ID."

    "It's in my locker."

    "Let him in the changing room," the brute stalker said.

    "But boss-" started one of the other local surfer gang members.

    The boss raised his hand, and the surfers parted. Braiden sighed in relief as he ducked into the changing room.

    "We let him get out of public view, and get him while he's vulnerable," explained the boss with an evil sneer.

    "But he's local?"

    "He's not one of us," snapped the boss.

    "He's not a bad surfer, maybe we should let him join-"

    "I decide who joins and who doesn't."

    "Right-oh, boss."

    "Does that sound like the shower just turned on?" the boss asked.

    "Yep," said the guy closest to the changing room.

    "Show time, boys, let's show this guy who owns this beach and ocean. We'll send him packing sans board and wet suit."

    "Sands?"

    "Sans, it means without."

    "Then why didn't you just say, "Without"?"

    The sound of hand slapping the back of a head followed.

    They entered the changing rooms.

    "Hey, do you mind I'm in the showers, I'll show you my ID in a minute," Braiden said.

    Callum hung nearby after having been rinsed thoroughly to wash off the saltwater.

    "Nice suit," the boss said. "It should fit me," he added.

    Braiden said, "I doubt it, your shoulders are much wider."

    "Thanks, but rubber stretches. Lemme try it on."

    All the men were now crowded into the small shower room with Braiden now, so he was silent. There was no exit except through them.

    The boss guy peeled off his old tattered wet suit, which had been repaired with duct tape, and put on Callum.

    Callum didn't know what to do. Instinctively, he shifted to conform to the muscle stud in the red Speedo who had stepped inside him.

    "Damn, boss, that fits you like a glove," said one of his boys.

    "Told you rubber stretches,"

    "Hey, that's mine," Braiden said stepping forward in his wet underwear.

    "Not anymore. Boys, show him who's boss. Because you were rude, I'm taking your board too."

    "But boss you said we were taking the board and the suit?"

    "Shut up, Russell, and hit the interloper."

    "Huh?"

    "The guy in the wet undies!"

    "Oh, interloper means "guy in wet undies", check."

    The boss shook his head, and turned. He didn't see the first punch but he heard it. Fist hitting wet skin, followed by an "OOF!"

    Braiden was bloodied and sore lying on the shower floor sobbing. The water spraying on him had grown cold. Trails of red blood flowed toward the drain. Braiden said only one word, "Callum!" He would rescue his friend no matter what.
