    A New Pet

    The rabbit steps forward, her face shifting further and further towards malice with every step as she looks down at you. "You are rather handsome, aren't you? Yes, I think you'll make a perfect addition to the group."

    Addition to the group? That phrase alone is enough to set off blaring alarms in your head and you immediately wriggle about, tipping the chair over and falling to the floor. The impact is enough to shatter it and set you free, but even as you bring yourself to your feet the rabbit seems unconcerned. Reaching behind her back, she pulls out a large red ribbon, and with a single flick of the wrist she sends it spiraling out towards you. Before you can even react, you find it tied about your neck, leaving a massive bow behind you. Tugging at it does nothing - it's stuck in place, as though it were glued together, and while pulling it causes it to stretch it seems impossible to tear.

    The rabbit simply lets out a short laugh, looking at you as though all of this were regular business. "You seem like the sort who might run off on me," she says with a smile. "Such disobedient pets need to have collars and proper tags, you know. If they didn't, who KNOWS where they might end up running off to!" She laughs again, then leans close to you, giving the bow a gentle tug as she whispers, "this means I own you."

    Panic sets in, and you struggle harder to pull off the ribbon. Yanking on the loops of the comically large bow, pulling at the shorter strands hanging from it, nothing seems to work. While you struggle, the rabbit produces a mirror and faces it towards you so that you can get a better look at her handiwork. The bow is elegantly tied, each loop thick and broad and the whole of it red, shiny, and soft. The loops themselves seem to be thicker than the rest of the ribbon, and are almost pillowy because of it. But even with the clearly strange appearance of the bow itself, your eyes are soon drawn to the front of the ribbon where it wraps around your neck. There, you can see a small loop from with a single metal tag hangs, reading "PROPERTY OF ALICE".

    Some monstrous rabbit women having claimed you as being hers and your situation growing more terrifying by the second, you start scanning the room for any way to escape this nightmare. The sooner you can get out of this horrible place, the better!
