    You woke up again in your school room and instantly went into panic. Quickly glancing around you definitely knew that you weren't in the room you went to sleep in. Your room, back with your family and servants. At least you were in the nightgown you wore the night before with your small 3 year old toddler body. You started hearing voices and footsteps outside of the door and thought "This was that kidnapping thing Stanley warned me about! What are they going to do with me?!?" The voices didn't seem to be right next to the door, and the door didn't seem like it had a lock but you couldn't bring yourself to even leave the bed. Although you weren't tired you tried to go back asleep and when you woke up again you would find this would be a bad dream. More slightly louder whispering and then there was a knocking on the door. You attempted to bury yourself deeper into the blankets. Then someone just opened the door. The voice of an old lady said "Well young lady come on out! We don't have all day!" Whoever it was you knew it wasn't one of your servants and you would've been told about any new help. You just continued to work with the "all a bad dream" theory. The voice sighed and then the blankets were ripped away from you and you flinched as the cold air attack your body. You still tried to sleep but the voice yelled "Lady Elizabeth Get Up! This Is Not A Dream!"

    shivering a little bit you opened your eyes an then cried out "WITCH!!!" the old lady in front of you had a pointed hat and a robe on. Shaking you remembered all the stories about witches and other creatures preying on the pure humans.

    The witch sighed and said "I am madam Cyan, a teacher here at this school that your parents have enrolled you in." she held a letter and said "This was the letter your parents gave as proof." she handed you the letter which you opened and recognized your father's unique handwriting. You couldn't read it but you knew it was from him.

    still shaking a little bit you asked "So what are you going to be doing with me?"

    The witch explained "Well the others are waiting so we're going to go have a meal so the group can bond. Now come, i'm not going to eat you or anything."

    from her command you got out of bed and walked barefoot on the carpet and found the other girls who all seemed to be humanoid at least. A kid in a robe with a blank stone covering her face, a small naked cat girl lying next to the robbed girl, a shorter girl with red hair, a blond haired girl with pointy ears, an egg, and also a bat perched ontop of the egg. Cyan said "Well girls she's the last of the group. Now go ahead and eat!" she raised her hands and waggled her fingers a little and a feast appeared on the living room table. All sorts of meats and fruits appeared, and the cat girl nearly lept on top of the table to start eating at some fish but something held her back. You noticed some plates and silverware on a side and went to get some nervously when...
