    As I was walking back home, thinking of things I can do with this magical ring that I got. I saw what it appeared two little kids - a boy and a girl - playing together on the front lawn. I happen to know a little of them both, considering in the past I babysat for them before.

    The boy's name was Robert, or Robbie is what he goes by, he had messy brown hair, and matching brown eyes. His face always seems to have that sort of devious smile from time to time like most boy's his age.

    The girl's name was Katherine, or Katie as she is often called. She had straight bright golden blonde hair that when a little past her tiny shoulders, and had bright blue eyes. She had a really cute round face and a cute button nose.

    Both of these kids were about 6-years-old, and they often played together since they lived close to one another, and gender wasn't really an issue for their age.

    "Come on Robbie" whined Kathy, "you promised you'd play house with me." She said while tugging on Robbie's hand, apparently trying to drag him into one of those tiny plastic houses.

    "I know Katie" Robbie moaned back "but I don't think we can fit in there."

    "Just come in" Katie protested, finally dragging Robbie in there with him.

    I couldn't help but giggle a bit as I saw these two kids try to stuff themselves into such a tiny plastic box. But that's when it hit me, to test how powerful this ring actually was, and to grant Robbie a favor while I'm at it. With that I held the ring up as I said.

    "I wish that little plastic house would become a real house here in this neighborhood."

    With that the little plastic house began to tremble, as the ground around it began to shift. All in an instant that plastic house grew instantly in size, magically shifting in place, and pushing and pulling the neighborhood in such a way that in fit almost too perfectly. The house became more realistic as it began to turn into wood, and other material used to make a normal house. Wooden fences began to rise from the ground, surrounding the house. And then it was over, the house was completely, and look like it always belong.

    I couldn't believe my eyes as I behold what was crafted right before my eyes. With that I remember that there was two kids still inside, and began to worry about what could of happen to them. With that I rushed to a window, and taking a peek inside I saw them both sitting rather shock, on a couch on their new furnished house. Curious to what they might be thinking, I lift up my ring and made another wish.

    "I wish that I was inside the house, but they wouldn't notice me"

    With that I appeared right next to them, but they wouldn't be of the wiser.

    "What... just... happened?" Katie said in a low surprised voice.

    "I don't know, I wished it was bigger, and then it happen, this is so cool!" Robbie said with a giant smile on his face. "Maybe what I say come true!"

    "Really!" said Katie, her excitement was bursting.

    "I wish I had a race-car!" Shouted Robbie.

    "No fair, you already got a wish, I want to make a wish" with that Katie took a breath as she thought for a minute. "I know" she said "I wish we had a baby!"

    "Ewwww" groan Robbie, as Katie just frowned at his reaction.

    Hearing both of their wishes I didn't know which one to grant... but that's when I decided to...
