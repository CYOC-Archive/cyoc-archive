    A diaper photoshoot

    added 4 years ago AR Toddler

    (A thank you to whoever updated the last chapter. Also please do another if you can)

    While she wasn’t happy with the mess she saw Grace couldn’t help but snicker at the sight of her regressed friend with marker all over her hands and feet. It soon became too much to hold in and eventually burst out in laughter.

    “Oh this is just too rich” she laughed while holding her sides, “Well I guess this is what I get for leaving you alone huh? And here I thought you were a big girl Maggie.” Meanwhile Maggie who had frowned at Grace’s laughing at her expense immediately took offense to that last comment.

    “I am a big girl!” Maggie announced with a stomp of her foot and crossing her arms with a playful pout.

    “Uh huh, not from where I’m standing you are” Grace calmly replied before she pulling out her phone, “Oh you are simply just too cute like this, I just have to take a couple of pictures of you.” Before Maggie could react her friend was already clicking away and snapping photos of her and doing them from different angles. Noticing that the baby girl was still giving her an unhappy look Grace spoke up, “Ah come on Maggie don’t be like that, give mommy Grace a smile…pretty please?” Despite her baby talk Maggie continued to pout like the upset toddler she appeared to be. Realizing she needed to change her approach Grace changed tactics.

    “You know this would be a good chance to practice in case they ask to do a photo op with you” she mused aloud, her words perking the attention of the baby girl.

    Seeing she had her Grace decided to seal the deal, “Sure would be a shame if we lost out on the commercial deal just because we didn’t practice a simple photoshoot.”

    “No!” Maggie shouted terrified at the thought of losing her big break after all the hard work she put in already.

    “I’ll smile okay, see” she said before giving her a great big smile.

    ‘Hook…Line…and Sinker’ Grace thought to herself as she had known Maggie for years and knew just how to manipulate her friend into doing what she wanted. Turns out it was even easier now that she was a toddler, though if it was due to the physical regression affecting her mind she wasn’t sure. Judging by the fact she colored her nails with marker while she was gone meant it was possible.

    Returning her focus to the task at hand she took a picture of the smiling baby Maggie.

    “That’s a good girl. Now why don’t we do some different poses instead of just standing there hm?” Grace said before an idea struck her, “Ooo I know, since you apparently like diapers so much why don’t you show yours off to me?” she proposed with an excited grin.

    “Okay” Maggie eagerly complied.

    She then spent the next fifteen minutes proudly showing off the Huggies taped to her bottom to her friend in a variety of poses. One of her favorites was when she laid down on the floor on her back like she was getting a diaper change and Grace took some nice close ups of padded rear end. Maggie really enjoyed that part and made it known by kicking her legs in the air a little and giggling loudly as the other invaded her personal space. The other shots were mainly of her in various stages of locomotion, the first being the age-old crawling around on her hands and knees which she found quite appealing, as was the crinkling noises that came from her diaper whenever she moved. She ended up doing a lap around the living room before they moved onto walking, or rather in Maggie’s case toddling which she actually didn’t have to fake given how difficult walking was for her at her age. That being the case Grace thought it best not to have her try running and just keep it slow. Despite not being able to walk properly Maggie was just thankful in being able to walk at all, a part of her actually happy at the hinderance of being in between development milestones. Along with not being potty trained it was another thing that made he feel more like a baby as she confidently toddled about like she was taking her very first steps. And regarding her Huggies Little Movers diaper the brand really lived up to their name as the padding stretched to comfortably accommodate her every movement making her glad she was going to endorse them.

    After Grace was done taking pictures of her in motion she went back to having her do some single pose shots. Maggie enjoyed those too and she even managed to make the shots cuter by poking and patting her crinkly padding with her hands like any curious toddler would when inspecting their own garment. She was currently in one of the more sillier poses that she came up with where she bent down and put her head between her legs while gripping her ankles with her hands as she stuck out her diapered butt for her photographer to capture on film. Holding the pose for a moment longer Maggie then reached back and ran both of her hands over the seat of her Huggies, giving the padding a few squeezes as she giggled at her own childishness.
