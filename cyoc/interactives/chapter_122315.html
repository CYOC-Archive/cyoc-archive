    You slowly begin to open your eyes. The first thing you can see is the room you're in. It's very simplistic in design. A bed, some shelves, but the shelves are filled with anime memorabilia and games...more games than even you yourself own! Taking all this in you believed yourself to be inside this person's computer, but when you focus, you can feel your "body" and it feels very much like a human one.

    Just outside of Atlus's peripheral view, a slightly chubby and acme ridden male named chuck sits at his computer, watching as the updates on his newly acquired all purpose fembot download and install into her. He notes the name of every package installed and skims over them silently. 'Emotional package installed...Role play package installed, Atlus program installed...Huh...that must be a map program or something.' The boy thinks to himself.

    Who knew he'd be the lucky winner of the most expensive robot in the world. The ultra realistic, and sex ready fembot. It can clean, do laundry, cook meals, and even have sex with its owner...How lucky he is to have won that fan fiction contest. He watches as the screen changes to "Installation Complete" and unplugs his fembot from the computer's network.

    You realize you're in a robot, and were able to will your neck to move slightly downward, allowing you to get a nice view of the form you entered. A very realistic looking woman. the skin looks so real you would almost think swat was coming from it. Even the sensation of touch was real. Everything that happened to the robot, you could feel. Including the disconnection of your internet port. Wait...what!?

    "Fembot, activate." Chuck states excitedly. He sits on his bed and watches as the eyes on his prize blink a few times and looks around. Chuck grins at the sight. She moves around so life-like. "State your name..."

    You look at Chuck. Somehow you have to get reconnected to the web. While you think of a way to escape, your body involuntarily moves on its own and speaks in a feminine voice. "Atlus"

    Chuck rubs his chin in confusion. "Hmm..must be the name of the programming group then or something. I hate pre-registered names...Your name is now Sarah!" He commands.

    "Understood, my name is now Sarah. What shall i call you?" You ask. You once again had no control over your robot body. At least so far the robot hasn't done anything to your mentality. As soon as the boy goes to sleep you can re-connect to the internet...so for now you have to deal with whatever chores he'll probably give you. Or maybe you can reconnect yourself while he's thinking...

    Chuck mumbles to himself for a bit as you try to move your arm slightly. He takes notice of this in confusion and gives you another order. "Stay in standby mode!"

    You stop moving and find it very difficult to move any more than you already have. Fuck.

    Chuck thinks...what orders should he give his new robot? there are so many features...
