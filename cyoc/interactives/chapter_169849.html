    "Jake, as weird as you'll think this is... I think I'll just stay this way." Jake looked at his sluggish friend with a confused face. "Your telling me... you like being a slug?" Jake questioned. "For now, Jake. I'll we can turn on this alter option thingy you were talking about and I'll see how this whole ordeal works out." Jake either didn't change his expression or it was too hard to tell with his t-rex maw. "Don't judge. I'm just curious. Nothing wrong with that." At that point Jake couldn't argue if Leon was okay with it. "Alright. You can stay that way. Just let me change one thing before I set reality. " Jake went into Leon's profile and made some adjustments.

    "Okay, there we go." Jake pressed enter leaving Leon confused blinking his eyestalks. "Uh... what exactly did you change?" Leon asked. Jake pointed to the slime trail Leon left from the bathroom. "Just so we don't have to deal with sticky slime everywhere. No offense, but if you clean you'd just make another trail and god knows I'm not-" Leon stopped Jake mid sentence. "Alright, I get Your point. Just make sure you set that reality thing on. Otherwise, slug trail is gonna be the least of our problems."

    Jake closed his laptop and looked back at Leon. Jake was amazed to see how content he was about the whole thing. Than again, Jake did change himself to become part t-rex and Leon thought that was normal so maybe it's not much of a bother because of Jake. Either way, Jake still wanted to test more on the program to see if it even I'd any limits at all. He wondered what would happen next.
