    “I was thinking we could go see a movie,” Hiro says.

    “Sure, do you have one in mind,” the “girl” asks.

    Hiro nods, “do you like martial arts?”

    “Natsumi” nods, “I do!”

    “There is a new martial arts movie out,” Hiro says.

    “Oh, you mean “Shadow of a Ninja”?”

    Hiro nods, “yup,” he says.

    “Natsumi” smiles, “I’d love to see the movie with you, Hiro,” she says.

    Hiro beams, “then let’s go,” he says, the two then start heading out of the park and to a nearby bus stop to catch the bus into town.

    Half an hour later they arrive at the theater and are soon sitting side by side in the movie room.

    Two hours later the two exit the theater, “that was one awesome movie,” Hiro says.

    “Natsumi” nods, “I really enjoyed it myself,” she says, she then turns to Hiro, “do you, by any chance, do some kind of martial arts?”

    Hiro nods, “I do,” he says, “I do Wing Chun, Judo, Karate, Ninjutsu and Krav Maga”.

    “Natsumi’s” eyes widen, “whoa… hehe… I better not get on the wrong side of you or piss you off then,” she chuckles.

    Hiro chuckles himself, “nah, don’t worry,” he says, “I’d never hurt or harm you in any way”.

    “Natsumi” smiles, “I know, Hiro,” she says, “you are an amazing and such a kind guy,” she continues.

    Hiro smiles, “thanks, Natsumi,” he says.

    “Natsumi” smiles, “what now,” she then asks.

    “Let’s go grab something to eat,” Hiro says.

    “Natsumi” nods, “sounds good, I’m kind of starving,” she says.

    Hiro smiles, “I know a great Italian restaurant,” he says.

    “Natsumi” smiles, “Italian sounds great,” she says, “lead the way”.

    Hiro smiles, the boy then leads the “girl” to a well known Italian restaurant, “this place, I personally think, has the best food”.

    “Natsumi” nods, “I agree, I’ve been here a couple of times,” she says, “they really make great food for low prices”.

    Hiro nods, “I agree,” he says, “come, let’s find a table”.

    After the meal the two teens decide to head to the beach to enjoy the sunset, both are soon sitting under a palm tree, looking out at the ocean, “I really enjoyed the day with you, Hiro,” the “girl” says.

    Hiro smiles, “yeah… I enjoyed it myself, Natsumi,” he says.

    “Natsumi” looks over at Hiro, the boy turns his head, both smiling at each other, “let’s meet up again soon,” the “girl” says.

    Hiro smiles wide, “yeah, how about next weekend?”

    “It’s a date,” the girl says jokingly, chuckling.

    Hiro chuckles himself, “and I’ll do my best to make it unforgettable,” he says.

    “Oh? I doubt you’ll be able to surpass this day,” the “girl” grins, “I know that I will not forget this day”.

    Hiro smiles, “oh… I’ll think of something,” he says.

    Both smile at each other and as Jeff stares into Hiro’s eyes, the boy in disguise can’t help but feel a weird feeling raise in him, his stomach flutters and he feels his body feeling slightly warmer than before, however Jeff doesn’t think anything of it.
