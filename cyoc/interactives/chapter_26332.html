    It's Super Weasel!

    added by Rusty Raccoon 16 years ago A

    "Yes Dan but there's more," Kat said.

    "What?" Dan asked, nervous.

    "Tell me, do you feel any different?" Kat said.

    "Well DUH, I'm a weasel man!"

    "That's not what I meant," Kat said.

    "Then what?" Dan asked.

    "Pick up the couch," Kat requested.

    Dan stared at Kat. "Pick up the couch? Are you insane?"

    "Just do it. Trust me," Kat said.

    Thinking Kat had finally flipped, Dan walked into the living room. He noticed that he could still walk on two feet, something he was grateful for.

    When he was in front of the couch, he knelt down and much to his delight, was able to pick up the couch. It was incredible, the couch felt light, as though he were picking up nothing heavier then his wallet (which given his lack of employment was anything but heavy).

    "What the hell?" Dan asked.

    "Tee hee," Kat said.

    "You gave me super strength? But why?" Dan asked.

    "I was benevolent to you for a reason dear brother. You see, in exchange for me keeping you more or less humanoid, I need you to do some jobs for me," Kat said with an evil grin.

    Dan put the couch down and without really thinking, he leapt into the air and flew over to Kat before coming to a landing in front of her.

    "I can fly too?"

    "Perhaps I should have made you smarter. Of course you can fly dummy!" Kat said.

    Dan folded his arms. "I am not going to become a super villain!"

    "If that's the way you want it, I hope you enjoy eating squirrels because..."

    "This is blackmail!" Dan protested.

    "No shit!" Kat said.

    Dan looked at his right hand and clenched it. "I could just put my fist through your skull with my strength."

    Kat folded her arms. "Such violence. I'm glad to see you have at least some intelligence. By all means Dan, if you wish to splatter my brains all over that fine white coat of yours ... try."

    Dan hesitated. Being angry with his sister was one thing. She was asking for him to kill her!

    "Seriouslly Dan, try," Kat said.

    Dan knew Kat must have had something planned. Given her challenge, he wasn't about to stand down. He tried to punch her only for his hand to collide with an invisible wall in front of Kat's face. Laughing, Kat announced that Dan would be unable to harm her.

    "Kat, please, don't do this. I don't want to be a bad guy! I'd almost rather be a dumb animal then a smart one that hurts innocent people!" Dan said.

    Kat petted Dan's right shoulder. "I never said anything about harming people. I just need you to ... acquire a few things for me. They'll be looking for a weasel man so once you're done, I'll change you back and we'll be fine."

    Dan hung his head.

    "What do you want me to do?" he asked.
