    Raiel decided to find a way out of the sewer, before trying to figure out a solution for his situation.
    He looked up and noticed that the grate had been put back into place at some point.
    'How did they not notice me fighting that rat down here?!' Raiel sighed 'I guess i'll have to find another way out.'
    He decided to pick a direction and start walking, thinking it had to lead outside at some point.
    After walking for a while, Raiel noticed that none of the rats were attacking him.
    'I guess there is an advantage to being a sewage slime after all.'
    He was occupied with his discovery and almost didn't notice a group of sewage slimes nearby,
    that seemed to be observing him. He felt that they looked hesitant, but not hostile,
    so he decided to approach them, squatting down a few metres away from them,
    with one of his hands streched out towards them, curious as to how they would react.
    One of the slimes slowly moved towards him, carfuly toching his had with it's body.
    Raiel suddenly felt a pleasant tingeling spread throughout his body, while the slime started jiggeling,
    seemingly happy about something, before jumping onto Raiel's hand and moving up his arm.
    He was so distracted by the slime, that he didn't notice that the others started to approach after the first had started to jiggle.
    One of them had already jumped onto his body, by the time he noticed that they had gotten closer.
    Raiel was about to remove them, when he felt that tingeling feeling again, causing him to hesitate.
    The slimes seemed to take his hesitation as consent, as the rest of them also jumped at him, knocking him over.
    He wanted to push them away, when the feeling came back several times stronger than before,
    now that it came from all the slimes at once, causing him to loose focus.
    It felt like the slimes were sending vibrations to each other through his body.
    He thought that they might be trying to communicate with him, but he couldn't understand them if they were.
    Raiel was so distracted by the pleasant sensations that he didn't notice that his body was slowly starting to loose it's shape.
