    Life as Eric's shoe continues

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous 8 years ago I

    Eric walked into his parents bedroom. He knows he should not walk here with shoes on, but you are still brand new and your rubber sole part is still clean, so you guess your brother won't be in trouble.

    As he walks to the wardrobe he closes the door. You wonder why, but as the door closes a mirror appears. Your former brother looks happy when he sees himself in the mirror. He really likes the shoes his mom bought for him.

    You can see yourself too, and you can feel his feet press into the insole part of your new body.
    “I wish I paid more attention on the shoe size I would become. Then I could have prevented this”. Eric jumps a few times. You feel again like screaming, but you cannot. You are his shoe for a year, and there is nothing you can do about it. Shoes don't call shoe shops to say they want to go back.

    As Eric leaved the room to go to the kitchen, you feel the warmth of the foot, and the pressing down on your insole body with each step. New shoes always slowly form to foot wearing them. You realize that you will slowly form to your brothers foot. You would cry if you could.

    Eric arrived in the kitchen. His mom looked at him.

    “They do look good on you!”, she said and he smiled again.
    “Don't forget to write your name in them. You don't want to loose them at soccer practice like before”

    Eric sat down and started pulling your laces. Loose me, you think to yourself. You hated what you had become, but even more to be stolen!

    You feel the cool air flow inside of you as he takes out his foot. Holding you in one hand, he was walking around the kitchen looking for a marker. You then noticed his skateboard. You startle again.

    “A yeah of this? A full year? How do I survive?”. The thought of having to go through all of it, and how you will be in a year really scares you.

    “Here is one”, and his mom gave him a marker.

    Eric sat down again.

    What do you do now?
