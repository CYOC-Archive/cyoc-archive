    Within the Arcade, there is a strange cabinet within the arcade that has the most unique of gameplay. This cabinet is known as 'A Walk in Life'. This cabinet is a built in Treadmill with a vertical screen and an number counter that depicts years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. What this cabinet does is let the use walk through life, literally. For five dollars, the user chooses to age or regress themselves and would walk, run, skip, or (in an infant perspective) crawl through life be that early infanthood to extremely elderly and only the user could stop it by getting off the treadmill.

    The only game over this cabinet has is its limit. If the user chooses to regress and keep going to beyond newborn, the user's body would liquify and become a white puddle filled with sperm. If the user chooses to progress and keep going beyond a hundred or at a certain age, the user's body would deteriorate and become a pile of dust. It's a rare occurrence as a certain few decide to keep going until they reached their game over.

    But that's not all, the user's body will not only age or regress, but also their mind will change based on the age they decide on along with clothes alterations and reality change. And while everyone would think that you are the age the user became, only the user would be aware of the changes. It is the most entertaining and controversial arcade game since Polybius and would change the gamer's life forever. But who will be brave enough to play this unique game.
