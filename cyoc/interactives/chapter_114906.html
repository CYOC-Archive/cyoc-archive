    When Andrew awoke he found himself feeling very cold. He opened his eyes to see he was in a cage. It wasn't very big but it was large enough to fit a small dirty bed. The rest of his surrounding were dark so he assumed he was in a dark room. "What Happened?" Andrew asked before realizing that his voice was back to normal.

    He was 14 again! Andrew was so happy but when he looked down he was mighty shocked. He was wearing a collar along with a shirt and short that were polka-dotted. Andrew also felt his head he was wearing some dog ears. Other than that he seemed to not be wearing anything else.

    "How do you like your costume Andrew?" A familiar voice said as Andrew turned around to see the Magician walking to him. "Hey what's going on?!" Andrew exclaimed. "My,My, aren't you rowdy, well Andrew it's as I said you're to become my slave for a short period of time"

    "I don't want to work for you!" The Magician smiled before looking straight at Andrew's eyes. "You keep forgetting Andrew that you don't have a choice anymore and even if you did you wouldn't do anything after I tell you this"

    Andrew looked as the Magician filled the room with light. The room was incredibly dirty with disgusting dishes in piles, thrashing trash in bins, and every disgusting piece of filth you could imagine.

    "Andrew, I'll keep you as my slave here until you clean this entire room and if you disobey or retort I'll refill this room with more trash" Andrew looked confused. "So what? Like I care!" "But you should Andrew because thanks to my magic I can keep you here working forever or as long as it takes"

    Andrew gasped processing what the Magician said. "Also you'll have to cook a couple dishes and wash my laundry here and there". "But you have magic to do that!" Andrew complained.

    "But making you do it is more fun" said the Magician with a snicker. "Well can you at least give me better clothing" Andrew said. The Magician looked at Andrew before snapping his fingers.

    Andrew then felt his shirt disappear as well as his underwear. Suddenly the shorts he were wearing became much smaller and tighter. Andrew felt unsatisfied and uncomfortable being nearly naked with only some dog ears, a collar, and some very tight short shorts.

    "Like your new uniform?" "No! This isn't what I meant!!!" Andrew said tearshe running down his face. "Well too bad, I've wasted too much time here! Do you think you're the only kid that I make suffer" the Magician stated as he unlocked Andrew's cage.

    "Before I leave I better add more trash" He said just as more trash appeared. "Why'd you do that?" Andrew said wiping his tears. "If I remember you've been complaining nonstop since I got here The Magician said with a smile When I get back I expect for dinner to be cooked and my laundry to be ironed" And with a final wave the Magician left.
