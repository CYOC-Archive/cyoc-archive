    Going back out to the garage you see your girlfriend, Melissa walking around the guillotine checking it out.
    Walking up behind her you say, "Pretty cool huh?"
    "Ya, its awesome. Do you have anything we can slice?", she questions you.
    "Well i did just get this watermelon to slice you tell her."
    She runs over to you and grabs the watermelon and runs back to the guillotine to place it in the stocks. She tells you to pull the leaver to drop the blade which quickly comes hurtling down and slices the watermelon and stops with a thud. As you two were watching your girlfriend noticed her hand was on the other side of the blade, looking at the end of her arm where her hand used to be she lets out a blood curdling scream of horror. Coming over to her you ask what happened she only shows you her wrist but looking at it you see the strangest sight for a cut off hand, the end of her arm was not bleeding, there was just skin. You walk over to the other side of the stock to look at her hand which the cut end look like the end of her arm just skin no blood. You pick it up when it suddenly grabs your hand back startling you.
    Melissa then stopped crying and said, "Hey I can feel you holding my hand."
    "Really? How does it feel not having your hand on the end of you arm?" you question her.
    "Strange, very strange. Umm can i have my hand back please?", she says.
    "Oh right sorry, umm here you go.", you say giving Melissa her hand back.
    She grabbed her hand and tries to put it back onto the stub of her arm, which to her relief reattaches and seals up easily. She gives an evil smile and says, "Let's try a little experiment."
