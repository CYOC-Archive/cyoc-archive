    "Yeah, okay, let's try that. It's just a computer simulation anyway," replied Brad.

    Brad fell onto his newly transformed forepaws as his face pushed out into a lupine snout and his ears grew pointed and shifted upwards on his head. As quickly as it started it seemed to stop.

    "This is amazing..." said Steve.

    "Will Brad turn back?" asked Steve.

    "Yeah, he will. It should wear off in a few hours. That's what was pre-set here. Hey, look, there's an option to make him permanently a werewolf! Cool..." said Tom.

    "Well, if we can turn back, let's see what other options there are," said Steve.
