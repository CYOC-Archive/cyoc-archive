    ”Damn it, you stupid ring!” Kelly exclaimed, after hearing that it didn’t know the answer. She tried to yank it off her finger, but it held firm -- and then she noticed that the bracelets she’d taken off a moment ago were back on her wrist.

    ”What the hell?” she asked, looking at the simple rings of silver metal. “I wish I knew why those are back!”

    ”Your previous wish may have been altered in some manner,” came the reply in her head.

    ”I knew that,” she muttered, then taking a deep breath and saying, “I wish that what’s written on this piece of paper reflects all the things this ring has granted to me, with the changes explained.”

    Hips and buttocks have been enlarged in the same manner as breasts, to create what is commonly referred to as an “hourglass figure.”

    External and internal foot, ankle, and leg structures, as well as balance and other properties, are altered so that standing, walking, running, etc. are as comfortable in high-heeled shoes as barefoot prior to wishing. Attempting to go barefoot, or to wear shoes with less than a 4-inch heel, will cause intense physical pain.

    Women are able to mentally and emotionally control their fertility; rather than menstruating, they will release an egg at the right moment when they genuinely desire to become pregnant by their current sexual partner.

    All women will feel a genuine desire to become pregnant at least once within the 20 years following puberty. They will always want to carry the baby to term. Whenever necessary, the pelvis, cervix, and other structures will widen to completely accommodate an object passing through, quickly shrinking to normal afterward.
