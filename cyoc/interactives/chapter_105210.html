    Looking at his options, there is really not much to choose from and not much to consider...

    "This one!" he yells out to the alien as the pain increases.
    The alien looks at the card and places into a console and the pain ends and the human lays on the ground and shivers. The effects of constant electrical shock leaves the muscles in a inflamed response and burning. The three other humans come over and help the human over to a table to begin the game as the clock is now ticking.
    "Please give me a moment!" the human cries as his legs burn and the muscles in his chest ache.
    "There is no time...now select three cards and give each and everyone of us one of them!" one of the players plead as they look at the clock.
    Without thinking or looking, the human gives each player a card and there is a few moans, groans and sighs. The human finally snaps out of his funk and looks inquisitive at the other players. "What?"
    "Look at your cards!" on the the human females say as she picks her own card...a card that he gave her.
    Looking at his cards he reads:

    --Age Regression Cards
    *Patient Age Regression 31-11
    *Patient Age Regression 31-16
    *Patient Age Regression 31-21

    Each card shows his own body at age 31 and then a second image of when he was much younger. One image of when he was eleven, another at age sixteen and the other at age twenty-one. This one seems like a no brainer! 11 is far to young, 16 included the terrible teens and 21 was the time he had a job, a car and a six pack!
    But would there be consequences in selecting an age 21 while being at a children's hospital?
    Maybe age 16 is a wise pick? It is a qualifying age to be a patient at a children's hospital, but old enough to be kind-of an adult.
    The more he thought, the harder the choices got! Maybe 11 was the correct pick...he would be a child and it is taboo for terrible things to happen to a child! But then again, he wasn't on Earth anymore...and from what the aliens were hinting at...no longer to be considered human...

    What do you do now?
