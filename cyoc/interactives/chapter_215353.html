    Mia removed the spider silk with a swung of her right index finger from Jason's mouth. "So! Speak!", she asked dominantly.

    Jason gathered all his courage and started to introduce himself, "Um, hi, I'm Jason. You're Mia, right?", remembering her name when she had introduced herself back in the store.

    "Hi Jason, yes I'm Mia. Listen, I don't know what happened to me, but I somehow like it. I will put you down if you honestly tell me everything you know." answered Mia in a serious tone of voice.

    Jason was scared about what will happened if would tell her the truth, but he didn't really had a choice, "Okay, let me get down first, then I'll tell you everything I know".

    "Fair deal", answered Mia.

    Mia grabbed the thick silky chord, which locked Jason's feet to the pipes and lifted the trapped boy a bit up with surprisingly ease.

    "How much do you weight?" she asked.

    "Don't know, maybe 160lbs. Why do you ask?" he replied.

    "Never mind."

    Mia ripped the chord and Jason dropped a few inches down, before she caught his full weight. Still holding the loose end in one hand and Jason in her other hand, Mia detached her spider legs from the ceiling and began to lower her boy. She spun some silk out of her hand and slowly lowered them both on the floor. She took care that Jason won't hit his head on the floor. She felt a bit guilty that she had already attacked and kidnapped him.

    Slowly she laid him on the floor, starting with his upper body. Then her feet made contact with the floor and she released the silk which she used to abseil.

    With her sharp nails she cut through Jason bonds. He was finally free and at least he could move again. Getting up on his feet Jason tried to break the silence, but "Thanks" was all he could say.

    "I'm sorry" replied Mia "I was in fear and I didn't know what to do. So what happened to me?"

    "Um..." started Jason "you know, this might sound crazy, but I've downloaded this app and it looks like I can change reality with this."

    "It looks like? I've merged with a tarantula and all my muscles grew when I..." Mia stocked and looked at Jason as her cheeks turned red ashamed. "You haven't seen this, have you?" she asked him concerned.

    "Um maybe. I'm sorry. I didn't want to. But you know, you one of the prettiest girls I've ever met."

    "You could have at least drew some attention" she said a bit angry.

    "Yes, your right, but I also was a bit scared of you."

    "Why are you scared of me?"

    "Hey you knocked me out and glued me on the ceiling" he said not wanting to mention the fact, that she was not always the sexy spider girl.

    "Um yes. I'm sorry. But as said, I was also scared."

    "So we were both in fear and we both made some stupid moves... We're cool?"

    "Yes, we're cool. But back to your app. I think there is no way I could not believe you. But why did you do this to me? Or should I say to my reality?"

    "Um. Maybe I was a bit bored and wanted to try out the limitations of the app. I'm sorry that I haven't asked you."

    "Your lucky that I like what has happened to me."

    "Yes, I'm very lucky" he faked a giggle knowing that he had changed reality so she would like it.

    "And now?"

    "Now you show me what your app is capable of."
