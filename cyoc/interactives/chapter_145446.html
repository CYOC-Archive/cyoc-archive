    Mixed: Colin and Josephine become one.

    The two slimes were splitting at the middle, but were bound by a membrane of their own ooze.
    The splitting slime slapped back into a unified mass.
    [What's going on?!] Colin asked.
    [I can't pull away from you, we must've gotten mixed together!] Josephine yelled.
    [Going on... mixed together...] a third voice joined in.
    The goo mass began to form one solid shape.
    Big, broad "hips" that swayed to and fro.

    [Got to get goi-.... going....] the new voice said.
    [What do we..... what's happen....] Colin's voice said as it grew weaker.
    [How are.... no we can't.... I won't....] Josephine's voice defiantly said as it grew weaker, too.
    The mass traveled upwards, forming a facsimile of a human midriff that saw two enormous goo mounds pop out above it. (About G Cup sized.)
    A few fingers wriggled and the mass began assuming it's main form.
    [I have to get out of here.] the third voice said as it found a hold.
    [No.... never .....play...] Colin voice said as it faded away.
    [Colin.... don't.... I need....] Josephine's voice said as it faded.
    A head popped out as arms stretched forth.

    "What happened?" Joslin asked as she looked around.
    The goo girl looked around the room trying to figure out what happened.
    "Oh right, I've gotta leave!" the merged slime said as she followed the only thought still left behind from her components.
    The slime slid out the door and to the elevator, dissolving a few guards that got in the way.
    She slid in and pressed the up button.
    **************************************

    The guards were patrolling after reports of prisoners escaping got out around the building.
    Suddenly, a wall of green ooze popped out of the elevator and turned humanoid.
    "I got out!..... What was I supposed to do again?" Joslin asked.
    A guard whipped out his taser and fired it at the ooze, electrifying the green mass until it bubbled violently.
    "AHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH!" the goo girl screamed as she exploded all over the guards.
    In turn, they screamed as the heated slime burned their skin like acid (exposing a guards skull in one case), all while the slime fizzled and popped....
