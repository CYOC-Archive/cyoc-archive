    Bill & Ted were what you might consider "Bachelor's for life" They were best buds who lived in the same town for most of their lives, both got jobs at an autoshop, and they were both roommates scraping by. Of course it was the 4th of July and they were waiting for the events on a hill somewhat hidden under a tree. No they weren't going gay sex, they were no-homo... They were just binge drinking. Bill was chugging his 4th beer already scratching the stubble on his chin and said "So, apparently this pyrotechnic is some sorta wizard. I have my doubts though." Bill was a bit of a redneck. Not the sharpest tool in the shed, does pretty well with his tools in his shed, and while was very attracted to women wasn't very attractive to women with a bit of a beer gut and being a bit of a hairy bear.
    Ted looked into his picnic basket and brought out a steaming hot dog and burger and said "Well, it'll probably still be a good 4th of July. Will probably be nice to get some fireworks different from the old ones." Ted on the other hand was more lean and technical. He was good at tinkering and had designed their steam picnic basket, and often was the one telling Bill what the actual problems with the cars they worked on were. Bill of course was better working on the heavy machines, but Ted often made them a cool gadget or two... He also was the guy who brought the food, picnic blanket, and found the exact spot.

    The two sat down with their little 4th of July picnic away from others the two cracked open a beer and took a drink as the sun fully set as the canvas for nice fireworks were set. The park hosting the official event turned off all the lights to highlight the fireworks, and then finally the fireworks started popping.
