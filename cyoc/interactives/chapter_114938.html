She looked over her shoulder to see Megumi give her a smile and a thumbs up. She nodded back and then looked over to her father. He looked over to her as they walked along.

That made Melissa happy. Harold looked better than he ever had, and even if it meant her dad becoming a were, him being the old happy and at times even joking dad really mattered to her.
