    In a flash, there were three more concubines standing by Kimmi. One was a girl about Kimmi's age, apparently of mixed race, clad in a colorful patterned sundress -- her choker gave her name as Grace. The next, Priya, appeared to be in her early-to-mid-20s, her Indian ethnicity made more obvious by the skimpy version of a sari that she wore. The third was in her early-to-mid-30s, fair-skinned with relatively short hair that was blonde but dark at the roots, wearing a plum-colored cocktail dress that nicely hugged her curves. Her choker read "Katheryn." The other two newly minted concubines looked scared, but Katheryn looked pissed off.

    Bob had a big grin on his face. "Katy," he said. "It's me -- Bob Gillis."

    Her big blue eyes narrowed as she looked him up and down. "I see you've changed," she said icily. "Let me guess -- our new God Jason Henderson answers all your prayers."

    "Something like that," said Bob. "But I'm as surprised as you are. I just wished for three concubines, and you're one of the girls who appeared."

    "Gee, let me guess again -- every woman who ever turned Jason down is gonna be a concubine."

    Bob shrugged. "Probably. But you don't belong to me. You belong to Kevin over there."

    She turned to see Kevin and gasped, her knees briefly buckling.

    "Ah, you're immediately horny for him, right?" asked Bob. "You feel like you'd do anything he asks, and you're getting wet between the legs to help you do that -- that's what happened to my concubines."

    "Y-yes," she said in a trembling voice.

    "Then you should go home with Kevin, his wife, and the others," Bob said, waving toward the super-mansion across the street. "Kevin, I will see you later. Enjoy your girls -- this one in particular." He reached over and gave Katheryn a quick pat on the rear end, which caused her to let out a little moan.

    "So you know her?" Kevin asked.

    "Ah, see, I told you the average dad wouldn't recognize you with your hair like that." Bob grinned again and said to Kevin, "This is Katy Perry."

    Kevin turned around shaking his head. Katy grabbed one of his arms, and Jennifer quickly glommed onto his other side. The other three concubines followed closely behind as the newly organized group crossed the street toward the expanded Jones mansion.
