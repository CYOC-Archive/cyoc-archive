    One such story is of Anita a young mother of 35 with 3 kids, 2 girls Anna 12, Clair 17, and 1 boy Jeff 12 (Anna's twin) and a 4th on the way...
    Anita was going about her usual chores one day quite happy as she planned to have some fun tonight with the new drug she had obtained. It was supposed to swap attributes or something after making one feel a great high and she was dreaming about her plans to use it and go to a bar latter that day to get back some youthful energy. As she was dreaming of her plans she failed to notice the bottle containing the powdered drug get knocked into the washing machine as she was washing clothes. As she dumped the bleach in to clean the clothes for her family she never noticed the strange smell as she closed the lid adn turned on the clothes. The drug being mixed with the certain bleach caused a chemical reaction where it became a contact drug where as normaly it must be ingested, but that wasnt the only change the swaping properties no longer had a time limit anyone currently wearing the clothes it had soaked into would be able to swap anything with anyone simply by thinking it while touching the other person even if it was just a curious passing thought, it also no longer gave a euphoric high when first used, but made the person in contact feel as if they were longing for something this effect would differ from user to user but in the end it is pretty much just a nagging feeling they are missing something and in time could become an obssesive feeling of obtaining that something, coupled with the swapping properties of the drug this could become quite an issue. Anita however was none the wiser as she happily moved the laundry into the dryer and went about the rest of her chores.
    Latter that day as she was preparing to go out, she could not find the bottle at all, and coupled with the call that her husband would be "working late" again she suspected he might be cheating on her with the office secretary, she was not in the best of moods and decided to go without the drug for today. She headed out to have her fun but not before leaving a note to her eldest daughter "Out with the girls, there is $30 on the table for pizza and please unload the laundry when you get home from school as it contains alot of your brother and sister's sleepware and underware for the whole family that might be needed the next day or tonight. Back by 10pm Lova ya, Mom"
