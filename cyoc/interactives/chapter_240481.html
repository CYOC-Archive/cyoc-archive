    "Will You Marry Me?"

    added by LittleBoy A month ago O Race change

    As Jeff sits there, happy with what he did, a thought pops into his mind, ‘maybe I should change my DNA,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I don’t want my children to have some form of disability, which is a high possibility since I am related to dad’.

    Jeff thinks for a while, a smile then appears on his face, ‘I’ll change my DNA to Japanese,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I know dad is super attracted towards Japanese women,’ so Jeff reopens his file and changes his race from Caucasian to Japanese, after confirming the change, he feels his face tingle and walks over to his mirror, smiling as he watches his face shift into that of a 16-year-old Japanese boy, which gives his face a much softer and even a more feminine appearance, ‘much better, dad will love the new me… hehe!’

    As Jeff stands there, smiling at his reflection, there is a knock on his door, “Jeff… can… can I come in,” he hears the voice of his father ask, which has a nervous sound to it.

    “Yeah,”Jeff says, turning to the door.

    As the door opens, Mark walks in, a nervous expression on his face, the man closes the door behind him.

    “Oh… my… god,” Mark says, “y-you… you look absolutely stunning!”

    Jeff smiles, “thanks, dear,” he says, “and you look handsome as always”.

    There is a long silence, Mark continues to stare at his son, not seeming to notice anything different about him, thanks to Jeff’s alteration of the reality.

    “Hun, you look nervous,” Jeff then says, concern in his voice, “what’s the matter?”

    Mark rubs the back of his neck, “I… it’s been five years since… since your mother died,” he begins, “and I’m still in disbelief that I ended up starting a relationship with my own son… but… you… you are just so beautiful, stunning and gorgeous”.

    “Thanks… I guess,” Jeff says, unsure of where his dad is going with this.

    Mark takes a step closer to Jeff, “there… there is something I want to ask you,” he says, before suddenly dropping to one knee, pulling out a small box from his pocket.

    Jeff’s eyes widen, his hands fly to his mouth to cover it as he shakes his head, “n-no… y-you… you can’t be serious!”

    “Son… Jeff…,” Mark says, his voice shaking as does his hand holding the box, opening it to reveal a diamond ring, “baby… I love you with all my heart and soul. Will… will you marry me?”

    Jeff is shocked, he didn’t expect this to happen, but then he feels tears in his eyes, and finds himself saying, “yes… yes… I want to marry you!”

    Mark’s eyes, at hearing his son… his new wife… say this, sparkle with happy tears as he slides the ring onto Jeff’s finger, before both embrace and kiss deeply.
