    “I think we should change, the couple next door!” Suggested Max but Ricky shock his head “nah I think we should change that family, it’ll be more funny” said Ricky.

    The twin brothers than kept rattling off different people they could change at the hotel and how they’ll change them but neither of them could agree on anything.

    Ricky was feeling restless as the minutes ticked on with no progress being made “man this is pointless! Pass me that stupid thing and let me make a change” said the brash young teen, Max stood up “come on, it’s my device so it’s only fair if I get to use it”

    “Fair!” Ricky crossed his arms “it’s not fair you turned me into a kid and a girl” he objected.

    “Than you turned me into a girl and returned yourself back into a teenager so that sounds fair to me” responded the reasonable teen.

    “So what you wanna do rock, paper, scissors for it!” Ricky said semi sarcastically, a smirk appeared on Max’s face when he came up with an idea “I got it!” He bellowed “who ever stays as a girl the longest gets to use the Chronivac next!”

    A look of bewilderment was placed on Ricky’s face “huh be a girl even longer, that blows!” He groaned, Max’s eyes lit up “what are you chickening out?” He was trying to play into Ricky’s competitive spirit “never” said Ricky.
