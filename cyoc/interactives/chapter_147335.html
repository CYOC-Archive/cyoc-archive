    Maggie is looking at the phone when someone bumps her. She turns around and is about to say something when she sees Braiden's body, "Callum?"
    "Yeah," he replies, "Is Braiden in my body?"
    "He is," replies Maggie.
    "Good," sighs Callum in Braiden's body, "Because I don't know what we'd do otherwise."
    "Braiden?" Says a teen girls voice.
    "Shit. I gotta hide."
    Callum looks around and finds a dumpster full of clothes. He jumps into it and hides. Leaving Maggie and the others confused.

    A girl walks around the corner. She has bright blonde hair and light blue eyes, she's in perfect shape.
    "Dammit. I let you out once and you run," says the blonde girl, "When I find you, which I will. I'll make sure I never let you out again."

    Maggie looks at the girl and notices a necklace that looks like a miniature test tube. She thinks for a second, factoring in what the girl said. She then realises the 'test tube' is made to contain something, or someone. She notices Maggie and says, "Have you seen my property. I swear I saw it run this way."
    "What does it look like?" Asks Maggie trying to sound as ignorant as possible.
    "It looks a bit like a boy."
    "Nup. Sorry. The only boys I've seen are mine," replies Maggie gesturing to Braiden (In Callum's body) and Brodie.
    "Ugh," sighs the girl, "If you find it. Capture it and please give it back to me."
    "Will do," replies Maggie.

    Maggie waits until the girl has turned the next corner and out of hearing distance. She knocks on the dumpster and Callum hops out. He looks at Maggie terrified, "What do I do? She'll find me and I'll be trapped in her necklace forever."
    "Hold on Callum. We can fix this," replies Maggie.
    "Please do. But don't fuck it up even more. Apparently people can now be kept in necklaces in this reality."
