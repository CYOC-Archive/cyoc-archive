But the feature of this Arena forbid normal clothing and forced swim wear on every BeastMon who was summoned. At least Josh still had his swords with him.
    Also normally, you would have complained about the weakened defense. But, with half naked Josh right before your eyes, you didn´t complain.
    "Josh, Hornet Slice! Cut down that bush boy!"

She clearly enjoyed it, announcing your doom with glee. "Use Zephyr!" And the frigging frog sprouted wings.

    "Yes, I also see a few great butts."

    "Well, it seems so ..."
    "Are you even working on a new strategy?", you hissed at your Heatdraka, who was, in fact, a human trapped in a video game just like you. "Or is your Gamer-brain occupied with the view?"
    "Hmmm, maybe?"
    You could already foresaw this problem. Right before this Arena, your Brawls were between Beast-children. Now your Team was almost full with Beast-teenagers, and your foes were the same.
    "I really hope, by your next evolution you will be more mature about serious moments like this."
