    "Well, I suppose they can, but only if they call up their parents first and ask if they can spend the night." Aunt Lisa said.

    "Cool, I'll tell them." Katie said as she went to tell her friends.

    "I'll go ask my folks if it's alright if I sleepover too." Kellie said as she went to go talk with her own parents.

    It was lucky that the ring had not only made Mary Elise, Samantha, Mika, and Claudia teenagers now, but they also had parents they could call up to ask if they could sleepover at their Katie's house. Eventually they all called up their parents and they all allowed their daughters to stay the night at Katie's house. Soon, the girls and adults all were getting ready to leave, but there wasn't any room for Katie and Kellie to get into Aunt Lisa's car, so Lee and Maggie drove the girls back in their car.

    "Hey, did you have a good time at the beach?" Katie asked Kellie.

    "I did, thank you for today." Kellie said holding Katie's hand in the car and kissing Katie on the cheek.

    "Hey you two, keep it 'PG' back there." Lee joked looking in the rear-view mirror.

    "Daddy!" Kellie said blushing.

    "I'm just kidding sweetheart." Lee said as they got closer to their house. They just happened to arrive first, it was taking Aunt Lisa and the girls a while to catch up with them.

    A few houses over they heard a commotion outside.

    "Jason, go to the liquor store and buy me some beer you little faggot." A rather obese blonde woman said to her son who looked to be about 16.

    "Mom, I can't keep doing this, you've got to get up off your ass and look for a job already." Jason said to his mother who sat on a chair on the porch.

    "Just go get it, come on, it doesn't take that long." Jason's fat mother, Gloria, said to her son.

    Jason rolled his eyes and took the money from his mother and began walking to the liquor store.

    Lee, Maggie, Katie, and Kellie had all been listening in to the conversation but didn't want to talk to them.

    "Real shame about her, isn't it Lee?" Maggie asked her husband.

    "Sure is hon." Lee replied to his wife.

    "What do you mean mom?" Kellie asked her mother.

    "I knew Gloria back in high school and she used to have it all. Looks, brains, good grades, but then she met this bad boy and the two began dating. Eventually she got pregnant and dropped out to raise her kid. Then, her boyfriend got arrested and now he faces twenty years." Maggie explained to the girls as she and Lee went inside the house.

    "Are you thinking, what I'm thinking?" Katie asked Kellie.

    "Oh yeah. Those two definitely need some changes done to the both of them." Kellie said.

    "Do you think we should make miss Gloria a teenager again, so she can make her life better? Or maybe we should age up Jason to be the new loving, caring husband?" Katie asked.

    "Tough choice there." Kellie said.
