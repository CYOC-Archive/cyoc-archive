    "Hey!" The blonde called suddenly, giggling.

    "Uh, yeah." Ty nodded, trying to stay cool even though this was the first time a girl had ever talked to him. "Guess it's because I've been working out."

    "Yeah, I see that!" The blonde giggled. "Been using Dustin's gear, huh?"

    "Uh, yeah." Ty coughed awkwardly.

    "Hey, I won't tell." The blonde said cheerily. "Between you and me he's a real jerk, but good on you for putting up with it, you know?"

    "Uh, yeah." Ty smiled. "Thanks." Not sure if they were done, he turned away and began walking towards the street.

    "Hey, I never did get your name. We've only been neighbours for like years!" She called.

    "Ty." He said simply, turning and smiling. "Likewise, I guess."

    "Becca!" She said cheerfully. "Going to do anything fun now that you're a free man?"

He wished to know, and all at once he was flooded with the revelation that Becca's dad was an alcoholic and she actually really wanted to go with Ty. Well, better desperation than pity, he decided.

    "I don't know." The boy shrugged. "Just walking."

    "Cool!" The blonde said excitedly. "Let me get my coat and I'll come with! Uh, if you don't mind."

    "Not at all." Ty wished himself to be suave with his crush for three years straight. "I don't mind having you around." He said with a smooth little smirk.
