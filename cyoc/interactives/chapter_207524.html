    "Don't be stupid, Jenna, thats impossi... AAAAAAAAAGHH!!!"

    "Ohmygawd!" the head cheerleader screamed in terror. "What the fucking hell is that... unngggh... what the... hell is... happening... to... meeeee...?!"

"You all... shall be... the beginnings... of my... zombie horde!" she hissed as her victim's skin went pale grey and began to slowly rot like hers and her tits swelled.
