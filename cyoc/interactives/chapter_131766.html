    You look down to your hands to find that patches of gray fur are growing at a rapid pace across it. As it grows across your arms, a surge of heat and itchiness covers your body, making your current clothing particularly uncomfortable. You grit your sharpening teeth together, wincing at the sight of your nails- Or eventual claws- sharpening as you scratch at your own arms, trying to remove the itch your body is experiencing.

    The fur never ceases to stop growing. It covers your stomach, your face, and your feet quickly, leaving you a heated wreck. Sweat pours down your forehead as you hear a sudden-

    CRACK

    in your spine. The pain is sharp and quick, and leaves quite the impression as it forces you onto the ground. Your hair grays as the fur finally finishes growing on your face, leaving a couple of sensitive whiskers near your nose. Just before you were going to get up, the changes suddenly continue once more!

    Your claws sharpen once more as your fingers shrink into the front feet of a cat. Your hands become rougher and rougher, while your thumb disappears as if it was never there. Your feet stretch, as your legs shrink. Soon enough, you can't even fit them into your shoes anymore, so you kick them off!

    Another sickening crack turns your awareness to your spine once more. You were NOT looking forward to this part at all. Slowly, a tail pushed out of your spine. A strange sense it was, first moving it... But there was no real time to as you continued shrinking, to the point you were trapped under your clothes.

    You couldn't see the rest of the transformation, but you felt it. Struggling through the pile of clothes, you were groaning for help as your voice faded to weak "meows". Your face stretched out as your tongue grew those white sandpaper-like barbs. Your hearing annoyingly shifted from the sides of your head to the top of it- Hearing new sounds. Your eyes lost their color, forcing you to shut them. When you opened them again, you felt that you could see a lot better now- Even in the dark.

    Meowing as a way of crying out, you clawed your way out of your old clothes. You are a cat now... Sure, you bet you look adorable, but the circumstances here are a bit inconvenient for ordinary living. Now, how to get out of the maze again?
