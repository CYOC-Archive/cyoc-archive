    Edwin and Dawn go for the Age wing

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago O

    Both Edwin and Dawn looked over their sheets very carefully. Edwin thought that the animal and gender wings sounded fine, but he thought it would be a little more fun if he tried out the age wing.

    "I think I'm going to go for the age wing." Edwin finally said.

    "Hey, that's quite the coincidence, I decided to do that one too." Dawn admitted as she did her sheet too.

    "Absolutely. Not too many people who come in together here often pick the same rooms." The attendant said as she clapped her hands causing two people to come out, both of them looked to be in their twenties. The two bag carries grabbed Edwin and Dawn's bags as the attendant led them to their rooms.

    "It may interest you to know that your two bag carriers were just ten year-olds the other day. They were interested and some open jobs here and applied here in the age wing. Of course, it is hard to work when you're so young, but that's not a problem when you can change your age." The attendant explained as Edwin and Dawn looked at the two and they obviously couldn't tell the difference, the two employees looked only a few years older than them.

    "Now here are your rooms, right next to each other, enjoy!" The attendant said as she left with her same smile and the two employees handed Edwin and Dawn their belongings. As they gave each other a quick hug, they went into their respective rooms.

    Edwin was pretty tired from the day's experience of traveling and while Dawn had wanted to stay up longer, she too found that she couldn't quite stay awake longer than she thought she would. Neither of them noticed a survey by the door, asking them how they'd like to be transformed. With the survey blank, the speed and changes to the siblings would now be left up to the staff.
