    Ty's Power

    Use more muscle, grow more muscle

    Marco finally managed to escape the crowd in school and he quietly get on the bus. Even though he sat in the far back, he was surrounded by fans who wanted to feel his muscles, including Ty. So, Ty quietly made another wish for Marco’s muscles to keep on growing whenever Marco would use his muscles. Whatever muscle he would use would grow very easily.

    After he got home, he would sit at his desk and do his homework, writing his math problems in his notebook with his pencil. Little did he realize that his biceps and his forearms would slowly grow in muscle since he was using them!

    SNAP! Suddenly, his pencil broke in half!

    “Aww! I broke my pencil!”

    Marco then reached over and pulled another pencil out of his backpack. He continued to write along as his muscles slowly increased in size, even adding a vein or two.

    SNAP!

    “Not again?”

    Marco broke another pencil!

    This time, he got up and he walked to his bedroom door. As soon as he turned the knob, it broke off due to his insane strength!

    CRACK!

    “What the heck is going on?”

    When he looked over, he noticed how BIG his biceps have become. He was truly turning into a Hulk!

    “Maybe I should just go for a walk outside?”
    Marco put on his sneakers and he went outside through the back door, carefully opening the door so he wouldn’t break anything. Everything seemed fragile now, since he felt so incredibly strong! So, he started to walk around the block when his quads and calves started to tingle as well. Were they growing?

    As time passed it seemed like he was walking faster and faster. Finally, he looked down and his quads and calves were massive!

    “Ohmygosh, I better run home and call Ty!”

    He started to run and suddenly he was at the end of the street… Four blocks past his house!

    “Whaah! How did I run that distance to quickly?” he groaned. “I may as well try to leap to my house from here!”

    He took three steps and launched himself in the air! He was soaring through the air, flying over a hundred feet above the ground until he landed on the roof of his house!

    “Yeah! I made it!”

    He jumped off the roof and landed on the ground. From there, he ran inside and called Ty.

    Suddenly Ty appeared standing before him.

    “Wow… Look at YOU!” he grinned, glancing at the big, Hulking boy. “You look so awesome!”

    “I keep on getting bigger and bigger! What’s the deal?” Marco sighed.

    “You look SO GOOD with big muscles on you! It is totally you, Marco!” Ty said with a big grin on his face.

    “But… My muscles are still growing!!!” Marco sighed. “Plus I’m REALLY strong!”

    “So?” Ty shrugged. “Get used to it! In fact, I’ll make you grow even faster!”

    Ty made a wish and disappeared.

    “Ohh wowww…” Marco sighed, making a fist and flexing his arm. A bicep bigger than a basketball rose on his arm…
