    Wait? A human house?

    Drank the whole thing, the vial wasn’t big and one sip was more than enough to drink the whole thing.

    “Not bad” she said to herself before continuing her usual housekeeping. However, after awhile something felt incredibly off.

    She couldn’t point a finger on it but certainly something was off today. Maybe it was the fact somehow her ears were rounder and covered in short light brownish fur. As she passed a mirror you would expect she would scream in terror of seeing her new ears..but she did not. She adjusted her hair for a moment before deciding to put to start sweeping the house.

    Sweeping the house never had been so strange, around her eyes black fur had sprouted and made it look she had used a bit too much shading.

    After the sweeping she paused for a moment, just then a short tail had burst through her Jeans. She was startled, to say the least but when she looked at her tail she sighed. “Damn tail destroyed my pants” She remembered she normally neatly cut a hole for the tail but if she swishes too much the fabric becomes tired and shreds. She had to admit though she never had seen it this worse. They practically could see her furred tush now.

    She sat down and turned the television on that broadcasted the usual shit that happens in the world and that turns every happy person in a depressed chimpanzee with heavy suicidal thoughts. Switching the channel you came on a children’s channel were on that moment “The Lion King” Was playing. They just were on the Hakuna Matata part.

    Her pants fell from here body, for a moment she stared at them but then shrugged. “I don’t need them anyway. They’re waaay to big” Her legs had became shorter considerably; her feet slowly had changed into little pads.

    Her whole body was starting to change in a faster pace now, probably the last droplets that finally found their way in the right spot. Her frame became much smaller and thinner, also getting covered in that same fur. Her nose pushed out and became black and her mouth changed with it, becoming a nice healthy Meerkat muzzle. The fur wasn’t to stop now as it spread rapidly over her whole body.

    In a nick of time she was totally coated in fur.

    The last part that changed were her arms that became much shorter and her hands changed into paws. She looked at her clothes and wanted to say something, but it only came out in Meerkat noises. For a moment she was shocked but then she calmed down again: Hakuna Matata; have no worries.

    She jumped from the seat and looked around. What the heck was she doing in a human house? She didn’t belong here, she belonged with her family far away from her. She ran to the door and looked to it. The doorknob looked kinda big for her.
