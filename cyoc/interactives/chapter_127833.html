    Denise led Jen and little Kelly into her room and shut the door.

    "I think it'd be safer if we stayed in here," Denise said as she locked the door.

    Kelly looked around the room in amazement. She'd never seen it at this angle before.

    "Looks like my little cousin is having fun," Jen giggled.

    "This is soo weird. Everything is huge!" Kelly said in amazement.

    Jen noticed a couple of hair ties on Denise's nightstand and grabbed them. Swiftly, she put them in Kelly's hair and put them in pigtails.

    "There - now you look like my 2-year-old cousin! Hey, you should see her sometime. That'd be sooo cute!" Jen giggled.

    Kelly grabbed a hand mirror (which was huge to her now) and looked at herself.

    "Wow, I am a cutie!" Kelly giggled.

    She turned to the girls who were staring right down at her. Kelly looked up.

    "Wait, here's a bit off a finishing touch," Kelly said.

    The tiny blonde giggled and stuck her thumb in her mouth.

    "Aww!" Jen and Denise said together.

    Denise bent down and picked her up and set her on her hip like a toddler.

    "Haha, Kelly. You pull that off so well!" Jen giggled. "We're gonna have so much fun with this thing!"

    Denise looked at Kelly and then Jen.

    "You could probably use a dolly, huh, widdle Kelly?" Denise teased.

    Kelly just smiled and nodded, her thumb still in her mouth.

    "Oh yeah! My baby cousin is always carrying around this Barbie doll of hers. You have any around Denise?" Jen asked, walking over to Denise's closet.

    "Oh I think I have one," Denise said, picking up the device and aiming it at Jen.

    The blonde didn't even turn around as Denise fired it at her. Jen dwindled down smaller and smaller until she was no bigger than a Barbie doll. Jen turned around and looked up at a smiling Denise and Kelly. Kelly giggled as Denise set her down in front of Jen.

    "Hey, no fair, I wasn't ready!" Jen whined.

    Kelly pulled her thumb out of her mouth with a pop.

    "Oh, dolly!" Kelly cooed in a mock-baby voice.

    "Wait Kelly ... HEY!" Jen shouted as Kelly's hand wrapped around her midsection and lifted her into the air.

    "Wee! Dolly!" Kelly giggled as she flew Jen around in the air while holding onto her.

    Denise giggled uncontrollably. Kelly brought Jen up to her lips and gave her a big kiss, engulfing half her face in her lips and gave a big exaggerated MWAH! Kelly stuck her free thumb back into her mouth and looked down at Jen, who was trying to wipe a bit of saliva and lip gloss off her face.

    "Eww! Kelly!" she whined.

    "You two are sooo cute!!!" Denise giggled.
