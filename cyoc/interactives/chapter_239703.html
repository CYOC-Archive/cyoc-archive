    A Dog's Night On The Town Begins For The Two "Dogs"

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy Yesterday A Magic Mental Canine

    As the two “dogs” trot down the street side by side, the two human boys are semi-aware, they can tell that their bodies are moving, but that they are on all fours, however thanks to the costumes’ magic, the boys think they are dreaming some realistic dream, unaware that they are not in control of their bodies and actions.

    Inside the costume, both the boys’ bodies are still human, their heads and faces, only their hands, legs and feet have changed to make walking on all fours easier and more realistic. The costume’s heads are so tight around the boys’ heads, whenever the costumes open their mouths, the mouths of the human boys inside of them also open.

    By now the costumes have walked into the center of the city, sniffing the air, a delicious scent flowing into their nostrils, following the scent, they soon find themselves behind a restaurant, walking directly towards a large open dumpster, and with one strong jump, both “dogs” jump into the dumpster and begin to rummage around.

    The “female” soon finds a half eaten Pizza, she doesn’t hesitate and opens her jaws, which in turn forces Henry to open his own mouth and soon Henry is chewing on the Pizza as if it were the most delicious thing he had ever eaten.

    The male “dog” finds himself a day old steak, and like the female, he forces Lennon’s mouth to open and the 11-year-old begins to eat the steak, not seeming to care that it already sticks and that mold already started forming on the piece of meet.

    After having their humans eat the pizza, steak, a loaf of bread and licking out some old sauce glasses, the two “dogs” are satisfied, and jump out of the dumpster, ignoring the groan from the boys, which still think they are simply dreaming a weird and very realistic dream.

    With their “hunger” sated, the two “dogs” focus on a new scent and begin following it, until they reach a dark alleyway, walking into the alleyway they notice a group of six stray dogs, three male and three female:

    - a dalmation (female)
    - a german shepherd (male)
    - two labrador retriever (male and female)
    - a large rottweiler (male)
    - a australian shepherd (female)

    As the two “dogs” slowly approach the group, the large rottweiler notices them, immediately taking on an aggressive and threatening posture, his fur standing on end and letting out a growl, as if wanting to say: “THIS is MY pack! Who are you? What are you doing here?”

    The costumes, thinking that they were real dogs, immediately lie down on the ground, making themselves as small and non-threatening as possible, even turning onto their sides and exposing their bellies, as if wanting to answer: “We don’t mean any harm! We just want to make friends!”

    Knowing that they might smell weird to the dogs, since “their” bodies are human, the costumes use some of their magic to overshadow the scent of the boys’ scents, causing them to smell more like normal dogs.

    The rottweiler slowly approaches the two and looks down upon them, letting out a growl, as if wanting to say: “Don’t move while I investigate!”

    The two “dogs” stay still while the rottweiler sniffs at them, finally the large animal, who seems to be the alpha male, the pack leader, lets out a huff, before turning and heading back to the rest of the group. After taking a few steps, the rottweiler turns and lets out a bark, which doesn’t sound threatening, more like if he would say: “Well, what are you waiting for? Join us,” its tail wagging, signaling to the two “dogs” that they have been accepted into the group.

    The costumes wag their tails as they get to their feet, following the rottweiler to the group to meet the rest of the pack.
