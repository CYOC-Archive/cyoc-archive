    A snack for the witch

    added by Anonymous 2 days ago BM I O Magic Vore

    Kat looked at the two boys sitting on the couch, staring at them from behind with her arms crossed thinking what their fate would be. Suddenly her stomach growled, the witch grinned knowing what to do now.

    As the kids were hypnotized by the TV distracted by the cartoons, Kat grabbed her magic stick one more time and pointed at the two twins, shooting some kind of magenta light towards them after whispering a spell. Both boys felt their bodies weird, like ants crawling over all of them, one noticed how his skin turned browner and harder, shrinking and compacting himself while he looked at his brother horrified like him, him getting a greenish look and squishy skin, shrinking like his twin too.

    “Wh-what’s going on bro!?” The first twin said in fear noticing how all his body was turning brown with his head remaining, he couldn’t move and his body was getting harder like a chocolate while he looked everything around him was getting bigger, but was him the one turning smaller.

    “I-I don’t know! Kat! Help!” The other twin said watching how he was at the same size of his tiny brother, but instead of brown and hard he was green and squishy like a gummy.

    The twins froze looking at the giant silhouette of Kat in front of them, standing between the couch and the TV. “Perfect! You’ll cause less trouble like that” she grinned.

    “You did this to us!?” The chocolate twin had his neck covered in brown too, almost getting to his head.

    “Aren’t you planning to eat us… r-r-right?” The gummy twin sweated in the last bits of his humanity.

    “Why not?” Kay chuckled. “I’m hungry and you two are no longer boys but snacks!” She grabbed a twin with each hand, smaller than the size of her palms.

    “You MONSTER!” the twin finished his transformation into a mini chocolate boy.

    “It seems that this one is ready~” Kat said before throwing the kid in her mouth, chewing and tasting the sweet flavor of him.

    “NOOOOOO-” the other fully turned into a gummy boy yelled before losing the ability to speak too, watching in horror his brother being chewed by the mischievous witch.

    Kat enjoyed every second of her taste test, she even opened a mental link with the boys to hear their suffering, how they cursed her and hated her. She just ate her brother as a turkey, she couldn’t care less about a pair of spoiled brats, that’s what she was thinking at that moment. After completely chewing the chocolate she swallowed all her “snack” with a gulp, ready to open her mouth and swallow the other that went through the same horrendous fate, swallowed and digested by the young witch.

    Kat patted her stomach with proud, burping a little at the end. “That was enough, it seems like I was just craving for something sweet” she giggled, “but don’t worry boys, I’m not over~” She placed her hands in front of her mouth like she was about to puke, then burped two little orbs, pretty much like Dan was but they were smaller, “perfect” she said to herself grinning, leaving the house of the twins with no trace of them while holding the two orbs walking to her room.

    In her house Kat opened the secret wall that revealed the jar Dan was trapped in. “Look bro, I got you more company so you won’t feel alone” she laughed to then cast a spell that opened the jar without letting Dan scape, then she dropped the souls of the two boys she just devoured like two marbles and closed the jar again. It was right that the spell in the jar was untraceable for anyone even for stronger witches, but Kat could feel any energy emanating from it, the fear, hate and struggles of the souls trapped there.

    Kat couldn’t contain her smile. “I know you’re wondering why am I doing this… You see Dan, after me and MY family ate you I realized I could use your soul, even better, more souls. It is an illegal practice for a witch to manipulate other people’s souls but the benefits of doing it grants an immeasurable boost in magic that you can’t imagine! Now that I have you and those brats I can start my plan to become the strongest witch in the family… no, in the entire world!” The young witch evily laughed, “so you’re noting but my key to my ascension, and none will ever catch me!” She kept laughing like a villain, while Dan and the twins feared what she would do to them now.
