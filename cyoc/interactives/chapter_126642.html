    You are Marcus, a 21-year-old male who has just completed his junior year of college.

    Going onto this site was sort of a miracle, to say the least. Your school's internet filters usually make it impossible to find anything you can access to appease your more...wild, desires. However, as you were looking over Google search options, you stumbled onto transformme.com, which had a few hundred thousands hits but was somehow on the 26th page of your searching.

    Surprised, you clicked on the site to find yourself immediately taken to the New Account creation page.

    A bit forward, you think to yourself. But, you've been looking for a website like this for a long time. Checking over your shoulders to make sure that your roommates hadn't snuck in on you while you were investigating, you quickly enter in some information with a fake email you created for just this purpose and hit 'Enter.'

    Lights flashed on the screen for just a moment. That's odd. Usually your computer screen does just fine. Squinting, you waited another few seconds before your screen flashed again. After that flash, the screen was filled with a 3D model of a human male.

    As you looked more closely at the model, poised without any clothes, you realized it was a model of you. Standing at a decent 5 feet, 10 inches and 220 pounds of mixed muscle and fat (mostly fat, you admit), the Caucasian model stood out on the black background like a digitized figurine. The only thing that seemed off about it was that your brown hair was less wavy and a bit more spiky all around.

    "That screen flash must have actually been my computer camera taking a photo of me," you say to yourself. "Huh. Didn't realize it was so complex."

    But your computer wasn't really that complex. In fact, your computer couldn't even run a decent League of Legends game without crashing. A series of words appeared on your screen, and as you read them, you felt your cheeks warm up.

    "IF YOU ARE READING THIS, YOU HAVE JUST BEEN SCANNED TO OUR WEBSITE'S SERVERS. AT THIS POINT IN TIME, YOUR PHOTO IS BEING SENT OUT TO ONE OF OUR TRANSFOMERS. WHEN THEY RECEIVE YOUR PHOTO, YOU WILL BE NOTIFIED AND THE CHANGES WILL BEGIN SHORTLY AFTER."

    Transformers? You'd thought that this site was going to let you change yourself. And now random people you didn't know were going to set you up?

    You try to log off the site before you can receive any notifications, but before you can even move the mouse to the button, you see a small message pop up at the bottom of your screen.

    "NOTIFICATION: INCOMING TRANSFORMATION"

    Uh-oh, you think to yourself. Looks like I'm in this for the long haul.

    Nervous about what might happen, you wring your hands and wait for a few moments. A minute passes before you remember that you can click on the notification itself - that shock really got to you.

    You click on the notification and see that your transformer is someone by the name of Workout_Master. You scroll down the notification past some generic nonsense to see what things he or she has to say:

    "Marcus, you seem really cute and all, but I think you deserve a better look. Since you're a new user, I figured I start you out easy. Let's see how you look with 30 pounds off of ya, and some blue eyes instead of those boring brown ones."

    The second your eyes finish that last word, you feel an electric tingle pass through your chubby body. It sort of feels a bit like the sensation you get when you brush your arms against a soft towel, except more internal and less fluffy.

    You lean back in your chair and watch as your chest, slowly taking in and pushing out air, seems to deflate just a bit. Your stomach, ordinarily pushing against the fabric of the shirts you've had since you were in high school, seems to have more space to breathe. In fact, as you pick up the fabric covering over your stomach, you see that the paunch that usually sits comfortably on your waist has shrunken pretty drastically.

    If you had actually worked out more, you figured you'd have seen the nice showings of a six pack on your stomach. You didn't actually work out, though, so your figure was just neater.

    Remembering the other change that the notification listed, you got up out of your chair and ran to the mirror by your bathroom sink. For one thing, it was pretty obvious that you'd lost some weight, as your shirt now hung a bit too loosely from your frame, your shoulders even seeming to have shrunk just a bit with the loss of pounds.

    However, you pulled your glasses off your face and noticed very easily that your familiar lack of color in your eyes was replaced with a verdant shade of green. You didn't look too bad now.

    Going back over to your chair, you realize that this website might be a lot of fun. As you close the notification and look back at your model, you notice that the changes that Workout_Master made were already applied. Now there was an added blue button, which said "Reverse Changes."

    You were tempted to click on the button and log out, letting your life be as it already was. However, this change was really nice...and perhaps needed. Now you wouldn't have to hear from your jockish roommates constantly about being overweight.

    Putting your hands behind your head, you lean back and wait for the next changes to arrive...

    What do you do now?
