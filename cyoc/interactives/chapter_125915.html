    Nat followed the bouncy cat hat girl down the hall as Rei and Alyla followed close behind.
    "Omigosh, you're gonna love this sooo much, Nat Chan! There's snacks and drinks and all kinds of stuff!" Nikki chirped as she skipped down the hall.
    'Okay Nat, it's nerd fest time.' Nat said as she walked in and took her seat in the anime club room, one used for the school's Japanese course judging by the word listings on the wall.

    The anime club excitedly chatted as they were apparently excited about something.
    Nat just held her head low to the desk as the Teacher stepped in. She had rounded brown eyes, a small nose, a simple frame with modest curves, in a simple blouse and skirt with her hair pinned up via chopstick looking things.
    "Hello Anime Club, I an Mrs. Nanaimo. I say this because I see we have a new member with us. Young lady, please introduce yourself." the teacher annouced as she motioned for Nat to come up.
    "Hey.... I'm Nat." the tomboy said causally.
    "Hello, Nat Chan!" the other students replied.
    "It's just Nat." the tomboy said.
    "Oh no dear, "Chan" is a japanese term of endearment. We welcome you as a member of our club and we hope you have a great time." Mrs. Nanaimo retorted.
    Nat just smiled and took her seat.

    "And now, our Club President will play our featured material for today: the pilot episode of the original Dragonball." Mrs. Nanaimo announced.
    "YEAH! WOO!" Alyla shouted.
    'Okay Nat, we just gotta sit through this...' the tomboy thought as the opening began ot play.

    ******************************** 1 Lunch Period Later **************************

    Nat couldn't believe it: that show was great! It had action, good writing, the artstyle was amazing, and the best part was that the food was great! Nat had never eaten Sushi before, and now she was busy noshing on spicy tuna rolls.
    'Can't wait until next time.' Nat thought.

    Over the week, Nat's exuberance died down a little. She still enjoyed sushi during lunch and started unconsciously adding "Chan" to everyone's names when she talked to them, but she hadn't become a full blown anime fanatic. But that would all change when...
