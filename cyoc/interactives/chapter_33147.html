    Deciding on gender was easy, in fact not really a conscious decision at all. Maggie knew that her authority over Jeremy was based on being his mother, an authority that would be lost if she switched genders. Species was harder. She didn't know what species Jeremy had chosen, so she couldn't just go with that. She knew she didn't need to be big and powerful to impress Jeremy, but it might not be a bad idea to be dealing with these Transformation Park creeps from a position of strength. Big, powerful bodies were just flat-out more intimidating.

    OK, Maggie thought, might as well max out. She picked "grizzly bear" off the carnivore menu and "female" off the gender menu. She sucked in her breath, and hit ENTER.

    Her first sensation was an itching that covered her entire body except for her palms. She saw coarse, brown hairs erupt from her skin. *This actually works* she thought, with wonder.

    Her next sensation was a crawling and swelling beneath her skin, as immensely powerful muscles grew on her rapid expanding frame. *God, I'm huge* Maggie thought, as she felt herself at a much greater distance from the floor, and much closer to the walls of the small transformation room. She flexed her massive new arms.

    Then she felt bombarded by sensations from all over her rapidly changing body. Short, sharp claws emerged from her fingers and toes. Her face reshaped itself, with her nose and mouth extending outward into a muzzle as her tongue grew long and flexible. A short tail grew from her muscular rump. Her insides changed to those of an anthro-bear, different from a humans.

    The door opened, and Maggie the anthro she-bear shambled into the park. She felt ready for anything, and more than ready to get her son back.
