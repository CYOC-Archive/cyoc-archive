Of course, her wedding with their father mean David and Katie would have to adapt to living in a palace, and to participate in some of the traditional events, for which they wild have to wear the most cumbersome ceremonial clothes her peoples ever made: imperial kitsune clothing.

Wanting to do something to her step-children adapt and feel at home in their new environment, and so had asked the palace chiefs to prepare pizza according to her new family's preferences. Although, the fact that the private part of the palace had practically none of the fast one would expect, being more in line with Emika's wish for an ordinary life, also helped.

Sure, each time one of her husband, children or step-children died, it pained her profoundly, but like all kitsunes she had a perfect memory, meaning they would always be remembered, a small part of them living through her memories.

How would it affect the world? Would her country and species keep being phased in and out of existence, or would everything continue as if nothing had changed?
