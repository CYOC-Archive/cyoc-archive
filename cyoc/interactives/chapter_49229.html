    John felt the entire room shake almost, the inn keeper covered his ears.

    Frozen with fear, John merely nodded.

    The inn keeper considered not comply, but didn't want the inn torn apart by an unhappy or

    "Here you go madam, er sir, this should help even things out with your husband, mate,

    Carrie eagerly took the potion from the inn keeper's offer hand, it was so small in her
    large hands now.

    "By the way John dear, how about for now on you call me Carl? A much better than I think
    now. And I think the name Johanna will be better for you..."

    John inched away but Carrie's, Carl's, giant paw stomped down on him.

    "Now open wide my dearest love, or I'll slit your throat and poor it down the opening." Carl
    said this in a totally dark, loving, ominous, and sweet voice.

    John didn't even think of saying no, resisting, crying for help, or trying to reason with the
    giant wolf beast on top of him.

    The formula, (which tasted like chicken, go figure), poured down his throat.

    John under the full moonlight changed almost instantly, his body also increased in height
    and size, though not as much as Carrie's had. Claws replaced nails, a tail punched it's way
    out of his rear. But first and foremost, his body became 2-D from an outside view,
    becoming ink and paint with a black outline and his colored totally contrast.

    His eyes became the same glowing solid yellow as his mate, as his arms and legs' muscle

    "Oh consider it on the house. We welcome you fully and utterly to Halloween Town of Toon

    "Thank you." Said Carl. "Johanna?"

    "Let's go."
