    The Magic Shop

    Regained her youth, but a little too much youth

    added 4 years ago AR BM S O Magic Reality alteration

    Amanda stared at her phone in shock, as her unfinished change solidified as a 2 instead of a 25. She barely had time to mutter anything to Hailey before she suddenly found herself staring up slightly at her own desk. She was still sitting on her chair, but a quick glance at her hands proved that the app had changed her into a toddler.

    "uh nuh." She muttered, attempting to say "Oh no" through her newly young mouth.

    "That's right oh no!" She heard a much louder Hailey say from above.

    Amanda looked up and saw Hailey staring down at her, only now she seemed colossal in comparison to Amanda. Hailey had one hand on her hip while the other reached for Amanda's phone. Amanda tried her best to reach for it before Hailey swiped it but her tiny body could no longer reach where she had left it.

    "Where did you get this Mandy?" Hailey asked in a somewhat condescending tone.

    Amanda wanted to explain how it was really hers and she could prove it if she gave it back but her mouth failed her. All she was able to say was a faint "Mine!" Which didn't go over well.

    "No, not yours. This is a grownups, not a toy." Hailey sighed. She slid the phone into her jean back pocket, and then picked up Amanda.

    Amanda struggled to break free but could not. She began to wonder how reality had been changed. Was Hailey supposed to be her mother now? Even worse, was how she was going to change back. She needed to type in the app but how would she be able to convince Hailey to let a 2 year old have it? She was still in diapers! Worse still, would reality adjusting mean that Amanda could no longer read?

    Eventually, Amanda's mind was taken back from these harder questions upon passing by some of the salon mirrors as Hailey carried her towards the back.

    Amanda always was a cute kid, and she was an even cuter 2 year old. She had curly blond hair tied tight in a ponytail, an adorable dress that just managed to hide the diaper on her, and maryjanes to tie the look together. If Amanda had seen her walking down the block she would have definitely stopped just to pinch her cheeks.

    "Well, at least I've got cuteness as a strength." She thought, before Hailey finally finished carrying her to the back.

    She was set down, as Hailey was bringing her there to hopefully confront her mother... which is?
