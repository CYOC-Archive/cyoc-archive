    “Key players? Why not just kill the leader and be done with it?” Bong-cha asked.

    “Because stronger people have tried, he eludes death.” Misery said with a slight smirk.

    “Nobody is immune to death.” Setepenre said plainly.

    “Either way, we don’t have enough people to take on the boss. And I wouldn’t do so even if I did, we stand a better chance of dismantling their organization by taking out specific individuals in the Normal.
    There are some main targets whose death would greatly affect the Normal. They are: Choon Hee; the kumiho who served as your handler while you were captured.
    Remy; the last goblin and the Normal’s main source of tech.
    Hinata Ayasa; a four tailed kitsune despite being only 87, one of the top Normal agents, wilder of Moon Slayer, and adopted daughter of the late Captain Cassidy himself.
    Rick (I don’t know his last name, sorry); a young wizard and formidable agent.” She planned.
    “There are also individuals that I believe could aide us, like Jolie Deathflower; a powerful necromancer and sorceress. The only problem is that she’s currently being held by S.P.E.A.R; a new organization that is opposed to the Normal but probably won’t take too kindly to us taking her from them. There are others too but we can discuss them later.”

    “Also there are operations we can disrupt to weaken the Normal; my intel suggests that something called ‘Project Runway’ is developing some kind of new mind control method, we obviously can’t let them finish that.
    The targeting of Shiro and Jennifer Tozawa means the Normal are starting to eliminate individuals that have opposed them in the past, so we should keep track of people they might target as well.” She finished.

    “That’s a lot to do.” Bong-cha stated.

    “We’ll take it one step at a time, first we should focus on gathering more allies if we want to get this new resistance going, then we can begin our work.” The auburn haired woman explained.
