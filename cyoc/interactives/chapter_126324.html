    Ty's Power

    Ty helps a teen's muscles grow in the gym

    Ty strolled around the weight room of the large gym when he heard a tall, older boy make fun of his younger, teenage brother. Ty stopped a few feet short of where they were standing and used his powers to find out their names. The older boy was Sean, age 19, and the younger boy was Brodie, age 13. Brodie wanted to do some curls with dumbbells, but Sean was making fun of him because the dumbbells were too heavy for him.

    “Start with 10 pounds, dweeb! You’re not strong enough for the 25 pounders!” Sean grinned.

    Brodie just groaned and lifted with the lighter weights.

    Ty saw that and used his powers to increase Brodie’s biceps in size with each rep. In fact, he quietly wished-for Brodie’s muscles to grow SUPER FAST, no matter which exercise Brodie did from now on.

    As soon as Brodie started to do his curls, he saw his biceps inflate with every single rep! His biceps ballooned with every rep! Every time he raised up his arm, it seemed like his muscles just GREW! He continued until the dumbbells became too light for him!

    “Uhh…. Let me try the heavier weights now…” Brodie said as he put them down and tried the 25-pound weights for the second time. Sean was about to scold him when he noticed Brodie’s bigger muscles!

    “Whoa! How did your bis get so big?” he wondered.

    Brodie did curls with the 25-pounders and he easily did a few reps, seeing his biceps inflate even MORE!

    “Dang! Check these out!” Brodie smiled as he flexed his huge arms. “My biceps are bigger than YOURS!”

    Sean’s jaw dropped wide open when he saw that Brodie was right!

    Next, Brodie grabbed a bar with 25 pounds and he did some squats with the bar resting on his shoulders. As soon as he had completed his first rep, he felt his calves and his quads inflate with muscle. After he had done a dozen reps, his legs were BULGING with muscle!

    “Ohhh wow… I need to add more weights!” he smiled, adding 30 pounds to the bar before he tried again.

    Brodie did another dozen reps and his calves and quads grew by three inches in width on both sides!

    When he put the bar down, he was HUGE! His legs were like oak trees!

    “What the hell is going on?” Sean shouted as he witnessed his little brother bulk up dramatically.

    Meanwhile, Ty was watching from a distance. This was awesome!
