    “While you all work out what the plans for today, I think I will go for my morning run.” announced a fit young women in her early 20’s as she looked at her friends and boyfriend. They had all came together to rent out this beach house to enjoy spring break as a group.

    “Penny? Are you sure you don’t want a voice in deciding what we do?” inquired her boyfriend Jon as he looked up at his girlfriend who had stood up and stretched a bit.

    “I am good with whatever really.” Confirmed Penny as she pulled her long red hair back into a ponytail and double checked that her shoes were on good and tight.

    “Just don’t take too long, if we play volleyball, we need you to kick everyone’s but.” Stated another one of her friends.

    “Wouldn’t miss it.” Laughed Penny as she bent down and kissed Jon on the lips before she headed off the patio of the beach house and headed toward the ocean.

    She stretched a bit, she was a very experienced runner, having played soccer since she was a little girl and was the captain of her club team at their university. She had also run track and field since middle school and even had a couple state medals back home.

    She had a runner’s body, but yet she was a head turner. With long red hair, stunning green eyes, strong yet feminine core and while her breasts were not the largest they did work very well with her body. Her legs were tone and strong.

    The last thing she did before she took off running was strapping her phone to an arm band and made sure her earbuds where in place. She started her running playlist and with a wave to her friends and blowing a kiss to her boyfriend before she took off down the beach.

    She took off down the beach enjoying the wind in her face and the pounding of her feet in the sand. She passed by a couple of empty beach houses before suddenly a static filled her headphone.

    “What the?” inquired Penny as she stopped to deal with her headphones. She hadn’t gone all that far from the house but far enough to be out of sight from her friends who where still back at the house. She was all alone on the beach; the nearest person was just an outline in the distance.

    Penny shut down her headphones and tried to turn them back on to get a new connection to her phone. However before she could a strange yet beautiful song filled her head. It was so beautiful and alluring and it was without question the most beautiful song she had ever heard.
    Without her even noticing she turned toward the ocean, the source of that wonderful song and began to walk into the sea. The waves crashed into her body but she kept moving forward, she didn’t even take off her shoes or remove her phone, all that matters was getting to that beautiful song.

    She dove into the water and began to swim out toward the source of that song. She had to get to the source and kept swimming more and more out to sea to get closer to the source of that wonderful song.

    Her eyes widened as she came face to face with a beautiful woman with long pure white hair pulled back into a braid and impossible blue eyes. What was really stunning was the fact that the women had a mermaid tail instead of legs. That beautiful song was coming from this women.

    She kept singing as she reached up and cupped her chin before pulling her close and closer to her face. She stopped singing as she pulled Penny into a kiss, instantly Penny relaxed as both of them sank below the waves. Internal Penny had started to scream but her body wasn’t listening to her anymore, her face was smiling even as she was screaming inside. She was laxed and calm as she sank below the waves and to her amazement she was somehow breathing under the water. The mermaid kept kissing her until they reached the bottom, once her shoes touched the sandy bottom the mermaid broke the kiss.

    “Welcome human, I am Queen Aquaria, Goddess of the Sea and all Merfolk.” Announced the mermaid “You and your friends have the great honor of being the first of my new subjects, now let us talk. I want to know more about my soon to be subjects.”

    Aquaria floated before her waiting for a moment.

    “Sorry, my bad.” Stated Aquaria as she waved her hand, “You may speak now.”
