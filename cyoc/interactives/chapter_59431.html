    Ty’s Power 2011

    When Ty and Dustin walked into the gym, Dustin was quickly surrounded by two other friends from elementary school who were in the same class with him. Since Dustin was regressed back to the fifth grade as an eleven-year-old muscle boy, he was now hanging out with kids who were his age, rather than students from his old high school.

    Two boys, Jake and Mike, walked up to the younger muscular youth, and had asked him to work out with him right away. Neither of them was as big as Dustin, not by a longshot, but they both seemed rather fit for their ages. They got on the treadmills and all three boys ran for a few minutes before Dustin got bored with it, and decided to try out the bench press. He wanted to lift some weights!

    Jake decided to get on the stair incline machine while Mike stayed with Dustin. Mike was completely in awe of Dustin’s physique, and Ty could tell that the boy had become a bit envious of his muscular, younger brother. He was studying Dustin all over and he started asking question about how Dustin had gotten so big in such a short amount of time.

    Finally, Dustin and Mike walked up to Ty.

    “Ty… Can you do something for Mike and make him bigger, the same way you helped me a few days ago?” Dustin asked his bigger brother.

    “Sure, no problem!” Ty grinned. He realized that after he had made Dustin grow into such a big muscle boy that word would get around and other boys would come and ask him to do the same thing to them as well. It was almost to be expected.

    Ty glanced over at Mikey, who was a few inches shorter than Dustin and who had brown hair and blue eyes. The boy was skinny as a rail, but he was always very friendly and he had always been polite towards Ty and Dustin.

    So Ty quickly allowed Mikey’s muscles to inflate all over his body! His calves suddenly pushed out from the back of his legs, quickly becoming as hard and as round as soccer balls, filling out like rock-hard muscle. Then, his quads expanded on all sides, widening and growing thicker as multiple layers of outrageous muscle formed on his legs above the knees, making his legs as thick and as wide as redwood trees! His quads now had thick layers of muscle on them, from the knees to his waist, while they were covered with thick rope-like veins all over them!

    “Oh man! Ohhhhh!” Mike groaned, as he felt his muscles inflate all over his body.

    Next, Mikey’s torso stretched out sideways as his abdominals popped out from his flat stomach, filling out his flat tummy and forming rows and columns of muscle like a washboard of abs or a tiled wall in the kitchen. Then, his flat pectorals on his chest then filled out like two mighty balloons which had air being pumped into them by an air pump! The pectorals inflated and became bigger and bigger, until they were sticking out from his chest by a foot! He looked like he was bigger than the biggest junior bodybuilder around, since his chest looked like a drawer from a desk that was sticking out from his torso!

    Finally, his arms inflated and grew from skinny limbs to thick muscular pythons of power, with bulging biceps and powerful triceps filling out his arms and giving them the power and definition of amazing manly strength, even for a young boy!

    Mikey just gasped as the growth stopped and he was left standing in the gym, over one-hundred pounds heavier, and with all that added muscle on his frame! He had the incredible urge to make fists and to flex his arms right away, making his huge biceps rise up from his massive arms.

    “Dustin! Look! Look at me! I’m big now, just like you!” Mikey cheered.

    “Well, you’re a little smaller than me, but you’re still pretty big for your age!” Dustin grinned, looking at the boy who was the same age he was, but still amazingly muscular.

    Now, Jake came walking by, and he spotted the two muscle boys.

    “Man… What about me?” Jake said.

    “Okay, you’re next…” Ty sighed.

    Ty knew that one boy’s growth would lead to another, but he wouldn’t mind…
