    When the party was over, Ty and his friends said good night to their girlfriends. As they squished back into their truck, they drove back to Ty's house. Inside the house, the five boys sat down in the living room, thinking about everything that had happened to them today: Ty had a new power, the five of them and bonded in the most unique way thanks to Ty's power, and they had spent the night partying with their girlfriends. Not only that, but the guys and girls had also been merged together by Ty's power: each group now consisted of one wide body, six legs, ten arms, and five heads one wide pair of shoulders!

    This was the source of a thought that all the boys had: wouldn't they be lonely if they and their girlfriends were the only people on the planet with five heads? Fortunately, Ty had already anticipated this, and had secretly made a wish to ensure that they would never be lonely. However, Ty had not revealed this wish to his friends/body bros.

    "What did you wish for, Ty?" asked Peter.

    "Something that will surprise you," Ty answered.

    "Can you tell us what it is?" Steven asked.

    "You'll find out in the morning," said Ty.

    "But why not now?" Allen said, "Besides, our parents might not be happy or used to seeing the five of us as conjoined brothers."

    Ty responded, "I told you, every time we fused I made it so that our parents believe that we have always been like this. Besides, this is a wish that affects us and our...uh, girlfriend."

    "So, now that our girlfriends are fused together into one person with five heads, we'll be referring to them as one person?" Dustin asked.

    "If we want to," said Ty.

    After a few minutes, the guys lifted their body and left the living room. Their six legs were fully coordinated, so they had no trouble walking up the stairs. The guys walked into Ty's room, where they noticed that Ty's wish had changed the room: wider desk, wider chair, and wider bed. Inside the dresser, the guys found what appeared to be custom-made pajamas: ten sleeves, six legs, and five holes for their heads. After putting the pajamas on, the boys lay down in their new bed and drifted off to sleep, wondering what was in store for them in the morning.

    When the five guys woke up the next morning, they walked out of the room and down the stairs. When they got to the kitchen, they were shocked at what they saw. At first, there appeared to be two people in the room, but it did not take long for the guys to realize that it was really ten people. On the male body were the five heads of their fathers, including Ty and Dustin's father Mark--Ty had wished him and their mother June away out of anger, but had now brought them back to be part of the conjoined family. On the female body were the five heads of their mothers, including June. It was interesting to see their parents conjoined into brothers and sisters like this: six legs, ten arms, and five heads on each body.

    Breakfast was a bit unusual for the boys; they were not yet used to their parents existing as conjoined siblings. When they went out the door to walk to school, they saw the full extent of Ty's wish: not only were the guys and their parents born as conjoined siblings with multiple heads and limbs, but so was every human on the planet. Up and down the street, there were men, women, boys, and girls each with multiple heads, arms, and legs in varying numbers. Their neighbors, their friends, everyone they saw and passed now had these appearances; while it may seem uncomfortable and disconcerting for someone with two arms, two legs, and one head, this world was actually pleasing to the guys: now they weren't the only ones with extra appendages.

    It didn't take long for the boys to reach their high school, since it was only walking distance from their home. Inside, they recognized the heads of several of their classmates and acquaintances, and were impressed to see each of them sharing a body with other people. They even saw their girlfriends--Hannah, Tamara, Rachel, Shawna, and Selena--still conjoined in the same way that they were yesterday. Ty, Dustin, Peter, Allen, and Steven were intrigued about the world they had created.
