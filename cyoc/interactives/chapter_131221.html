    Jen wakes up, and feels that only her and Rose should have this amazing abilities. Rose is a brown haired girl, who is in the cheerleading squad with Jen. She picks up the to call Rose.

    "Hello?" Said Rose, in a low, tired mood.

    "Hey Rose, can you come to my house to walk by the countryside?" Said Jen.

    "Why? I know seeing the other horses excites you, but why bring me along?" said Rose.

    "Well, you get air and energy, so you won't feel sleepy or tired," said Jen.

    "True," said Rose, "Ok, I'll come to your house, and we'll start our walk."

    "See you later," said Jen, before ending the call. She sees Martha coming out, with a normal shirt and jeans. "Thanks, Martha, said Jen, "but you know that I have to pratic this entire week?"

    "Yes I know, but I got a call from the school, saying that you won't have practice the rest of the week," said Martha.

    "How come?" said Jen.

    "Well, 37 of the 50 cheerleaders got shot in the morning duing their practice," said Martha, "None of them died yet, but they did had fatal injuries, and the police are looking into the scene."

    "Oh, I feel sorry," said Jen, in a sadder voice.

    "Well, at least you won't have to train hard," said Martha.

    "Yeah, that can be a plus," said Jen. She takes a shower, before putting on her clothes, and brushing her teeth, hair, and tail, Then she goes downstairs to eat her wheat, barley, and oats for breakfast. She gets her backpack, and gets ready to walk. She sets outside until Rose comes 10 minutes later, wearing a shirt and a short skirt.

    "Hey, you heard?" said Rose, in a deep tone.

    "Yeah, but let's get that topic off on hand, and walk through the countryside, and talk about something else." said Jen.

    "Ok, but I want to ride you soon," said Rose.

    "Soon, but maybe when you will get to know how to horse race," said Jen, in a housing matter.

    "Don't worry, I'll ask your mom soon, and she'll teach me with one of her horses," said Rose.

    They walked and talked, as average, before reaching the horses. Jen grabs her hands violently, looking into her pupils deep, and saying, "Transform or Dare? You got 60 seconds."

    "Jen, that's a myth, there is no way you, out of anyone, are the one believing in this shit?" said Rose.

    "25 seconds," said Jen.

    "Ok, then, I'll chose both of them to see if this shit does exist or not," said Rose.

    "Yes! For your transformation, you'll have the same horse genes with me."

    "Wait, what!?"

    "Oh yes, as people were use to this, and only you and me will know the difference."

    Just then, Rose's ears were deteriorating back into her head, and froming on top of her head, as a brown horse's ears.

    "And for your dare, both you and me won't use this curse to change people into half animals, in anyway. In fact, when you're about to do, you'll always forget."

    Then Rose felt hair growing from her ass, growing out of her panties to show it beautiful brown color.

    "Now I'm done, now let the process go through your veins."

    Then Rose felt pain on her arms, before feeling a lot more on her legs, falling down. After she got up 10 minutes later, she opened her eyes to see Jen saying, "Are you OK?"

    "What the hell, no! You turned me into you!" said Rose, in rage.

    "And am I a bad thing?" said Jen.

    "Well, no, not exactly," Rose said feeling calmer, "So wait, your a horse-like being because of this game?"

    "Yes, before then I was a normal girl, but now I love my horse-like being self. I run faster, kick harder, and get to feel what a horse feels."

    "Well, I never got to see that way."

    "Let's talk about this later, we will be late if we don't run like our horse counterpart."

    And they ran as fast as they can, while Rose feels oddly excited.
