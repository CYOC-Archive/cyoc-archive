    Rob, after finishing his lunch and going into the study hour, made his way to the library. It was there that he spotted Amy along with her friends Theresa, Betty, and Carla. Amy spotted Rob and waved him over, which Rob walked over and joined the four girls at the table.

    "Hey," Amy said as Rob sat down, "We were just talking about you."

    "Oh?" Rob responded, "about what exactly?"

    "Well," Theresa answered, "we were making bets. About how long you'd be still normal."

    "What?" Rob exclaimed while trying to keep his voice down, "I mean, I don't intend to..."

    "It's an old story," Carla interrupted, "Boy falls in love with girl, boy has sex with girl, girl changes boy into a were. It's a common story here in Moon Lake. Much more in Moon Lake High than anywhere else."

    A wry grin appeared on Theresa's face, "And if what Amy says is true, it's not a matter if you change, but when."

    "Amy!" Rob said as he turned to Amy with his eyes wide.

    "What?" Amy said with her hands up, "I couldn't keep things secret from my girlfriends."

    Rob could feel his face flush red as the girls giggled. He then waved his hands as he said, "Right now, I've got no plans to be anything other than myself."

    "Even with the mandatory were law?" Betty asked.

    "Naw, I don't have to worry about that," Rob responded, "Dad got changed last night. Turned he and mom went out into the woods and when he got back, he was a werelion."

    "Oh yeah, I heard about that," Theresa said. "Turns out there was this normal couple who went out near a werelion event and the guy tripped right into a werelioness. Guy became a werelion, but I'm not sure what happened to the girl, but I think she ran away deeper into the woods and got attacked by a cougar or something,"

    "Wait, how did you hear about that?" Rob asked.

    "Well, I was at the werelion meeting...enjoying myself," Theresa responded with a bit of a wink. "There was a bit of commotion when that happened, and just about everyone at the meeting heard what happened."

    "Yeah, well, that was my dad and mom probably then," Rob said.

    "But it does mean that you don't have to worry about the law," Carla said.

    "Yeah, so if I change, I'm not sure if and when it'll happen. But it won't be any time soon," Rob said.

    "Shame," Theresa said, "I think you'd make a handsome werelion. I mean, you'd be bulkier, a bit more muscle I think." Theresa formed a frame with her hands as she continued, "Yeah, I can imagine you with this mane as you take me in your big lion arms as your big barbed co..."

    "Theresa!" Amy called out interrupting the train of thought. Amy's outburst earned her a glare from the librarian as she sheepishly sunk down and mouthed sorry.

    Theresa giggled and said, "Oh come on, Amy. Don't deny that you've been imagining Rob as a wereskunk making love to you."

    "Are all weres like this?" Rob asked.

    "Constantly horny with sex on the brain?" Carla responded, "Yeah, pretty much."

    "Oh, don't act like you're above it all, Carla," Betty said as she rolled her eyes. "I've seen you stare just a little too long at those boys. You've imagined it too, except you kept it to yourself. I mean, if you were at that Halloween dance last week, you'd be furry too."

    Carla's face turned bright red at the teasing, "I mean...I just...I don't think..."

    Amy giggled at the teasing and the reaction before adding, "It's okay, Carla. We'll take you for who you are."

    Theresa added, "And besides, whatever you decide, we'll support you."

    Carla said, "Thank you." At this point, the bell rang marking the end of the study hall hour. Rob and the girls gathered their things and made their way out of the library.

    Before Rob left the library, Amy grabbed his arm and pulled him close to her. She whispered into his ear, "See you later after school?"

    Rob smiled and nodded his head. Amy gave a bright smile as she saw his response before letting go. Rob watched with a silly grin on his face as Amy walked out of the door.
