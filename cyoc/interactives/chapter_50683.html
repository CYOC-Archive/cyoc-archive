    In the court of Quell, Jeffrey sweated profusely. Every set of eyes, every smiling face
    looked as if they saw right through his magical disguise as Prince Eodor of Quell. He had
    some of the prince's memories and information to get past any code words of the sort, but
    he worried that it was not enough.

    He was repentant for the debauchery he engaged in on Pleasure Island. He would never
    forget his time as a feral beast. He just wanted to go home, but that was not part of the
    bargain. Dana had told him that he had to continue as a body double for Prince Eodor until
    the Sorceress Igna was done working her magic on the prince.

    Suddenly, the massive court doors opened as a loud flapping noise came through.
    Members of the court held onto their hats and dresses as the powerful winds blasted
    through the door.

    Through the doors came a flying athletic woman, her reddish-brown skin bare of any
    clothing and her pair of feathered wings pounding air into the ground. She descended
    gracefully, grabbing one of her feathers out of midair and sending it like an arrow across
    the room. It landed gently in the hands of a beautiful bespectacled maid, and she took the
    angelic woman's gift and clutched it across her bosom, blushing shyly.

    The flying woman landed on her feet, kneeling on one leg and spreading her wings back.
    She looked tired and cold from a long flight, but was proud of every inch of her beautiful
    nimble body. She raised her head solemnly.

    "Are you an angel?" Asked a guardsman.

    "No," she said. "I am Miggs, simply a humble messenger of Prince Eodor."

    She looked up, directly into the eyes of Jeffrey, disguised as Prince Eodor. Jeffrey felt his
    stomach twist. This woman, Miggs, knew his real identity. She must've came from the real
    Prince Eodor. These people would know he was part of the

    However, before Miggs could say another word, a loud humming echoed throughout the
    court. A temporary gate formed in the center of the hall, buzzing with chaotic energy. Out
    of it leaped a young demon girl, winged, horned and clothed in a one-piece. The gate
    quickly closed behind her. She looked around at Jeffrey, and he recognized her as Dana,
    the girl who had saved his life.

    Dana looked relieved to see Jeffrey still alive, then quickly turned to the speechless crowd
    and Miggs, glaring and baring her teeth.

    "None of you will lay so much as a hand on him!" She roared.
