    In the dream realm, Veikh vividly repeated his fight against the corrupted beasts, swinging his sword from side to side. But as he killed more appeared, one after another, his fights became more and more violent, bloody and even ... savage, mercilessly killing the beasts that pounced on him.
    until his sword broke in half, then he continued to fight with his butts, either by his fury or his iron will prevented him from fainting, even renewed strength invaded him every time he believed that he was in his last breath. He struggles with everything, hands, feet, even his teeth and tail ... wait, tail?

    His mind was out of the frenzy of battle for a moment, he realized that something was wrong, very wrong with his body, looked down at his gaze to look at his hands or, should I say front legs with thumbs?
    They were covered in scales as black as obsidian and sported 4 strong ruthless claws on each paw.

    Veikh looked behind him, at his now quadruped body, they wore the same black scales all over his body but it was not the only surprise, long neck, membranous wings, a long armored tail of the same scales and two strong legs that were previously the legs. of a man and scales on his belly comparable to plates of iron armor ...

    Of course, you couldn't see your face but you knew you had a strong and long snout, a hint of exploration on your head also relieved horns that came out from behind the head.

    his scaly body was covered his body was covered in blood from the infinite wave of beasts that he had killed, when he looked around again, all of them were bowing their heads and avoiding making vicual contact, fear, terror, but above all .. . I respect!
