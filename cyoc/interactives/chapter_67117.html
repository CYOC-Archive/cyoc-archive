    The Big City Under the Sea

    "Alright then, I guess I should come." you say. Katherine was sleeping like a baby in Elizabeth's arm.

    The two of you jumped into the water again, Elizabeth holding Katherine in her eyes.

    "Follow me, we're pretty close to a City already." she said, and dived deep down. You follow her. You two swam
    down for at least 20 minutes, to the point where you were getting bored at how ridiculously dark it was. You
    and Elizabeth were holding hands to prevent getting separated. Finally, you saw a dim light far away, that
    Elizabeth was swimming towards. The light grew and grew until you were blinded for a couple seconds, when your
    eyes got accustomed to the light, you saw immense buildings made of different colored stone, iron, and glass.
    There were various colored corals adorning the streets with sea weeds and other sea life forming what would be
    equivalent to parks. Thousands of mermaids were swimming in an ordered manner, and in what could be called
    similar to streets, and they weren't just fish mermaids, but mermaids of all types of sea creatures, octopus,
    squid, dolphins, even creatures that have sea shells. There were some sorts of vehicles similar to submarines;
    what could be considered cars. It was an advance society similar to human cities, just underwater. "The amount
    of nude women absolutely everywhere would have made my old self's penis just explode", you thought.

    "Wow... this is amazing." you say.

    "Isn't it? I was surprised when I first came here how advanced the technology was. There's an even bigger city
    underground, connected to other cities, in case of emergency." she says.

    She leads you to building marked "NEW SISTERS". Inside there was a mermaid with the bottom half of a squid.

    "Hello, I'm here to drop off a new sister." she said.

    "What's her story?" she asks.

    Elizabeth tells the woman Katherine's story, and gives her to the woman. She takes Katherine and swims off
    into another room.

    "Alright, now I just need to update our records. Then we'll find her a new family and school to go to. Thank
    you for coming. That'll be all." she says.

    "Thanks! Come on honey, since we're here, we may as well see if we can buy some accessories for you!" she
    grabs you by the hand and swims out the door in a hurry, taking you through the city looking for a store.

    "Wait, that was it?" you ask.

    "Yup, not much to it. Oh, look over there!" she pointed to a store, "MARIANNE'S" in some sort of font similar
    to "Times New Roman", but not exactly. She dragged you in there, and bought a whole assortment of accessory
    making equipment. Beads, threads, etcetera. When she finished getting everything, you noticed there was no
    checkout counter.

    "Hey, where do we pay for this?" you ask.

    "What? Paying? We haven't done that in ages. Machines make and gather materials for everything now, no need to
    pay people for anything. The only thing we pay for now is art, but these are art supplies, so they're free.
    Oh! This color matches your tail perfectly!" she said.

    "Oh." you respond. You leave with a large bag of art supplies and head out back to the cove. There, the
    others, Florencia, Abigail, Mary and Veronica were busy eating a lobster.

    "Hey! What took you girls so long? Having some fun in the city? So, how was it? Was the spot any good?" Mary
    asked.

    "Yeah, there was even an abued girl we made into a mermaid. I wonder how she's doing, if they already found
    her a family."

    "Oh, well that's nice. Oh, Michelle, you look like you're tired, here, have some lobster." she said looking at
    you. You were tired, to tell the truth, this had been a long day, and you were pregnant. You took the lobster
    and ate it. Suddenly you feel a hand being rubbed on your bloated stomach.

    "Oh... it's so warm. You can feel her moving in there. I wonder what color her tail'll be. We'll need to start
    getting diapers now... Ugh. That has to be the only useful thing man has ever invented. You have no idea how
    disgusting it is to swim in water that smells like urine." Mary said.

    "So, what are you going to name the kids?" Veronica asked. You two hadn't thought of that yet.

    "Oh well, you'll think of a name eventually. Hey, are those things in the bag for making accessories? Yay! We
    can finally start making Michelle even more beautiful!"

    8 Months. Nearly a year until you could be with your wife. Until then, you were stuck with these girls. You
    were already starting to feel love for Elizabeth, so it's only a matter of time until you start feeling close
    to the other girls. Oh well.

    What do you do now?
