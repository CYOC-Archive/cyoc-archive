    This was possibly a first for the Dark Master, two people sat on their knees before him bound in chains.

    That wasn’t odd at all, that was so common that it was hardly worthy of note to someone like him. So many champions and heroes had attempted to come to his castle to kill him. All of them where now members of his harem and served him not only in the art of pleasure but other duties. They where all transformed into lovely women of different magical/mythical creatures.

    “My Lord, these two were caught attempting to come into the castle.” Said a rather busty orc who had once been a handsome knight who had tried to kill the man she now served.

    “Really…because I heard they turned themselves over to one of your guards the moment they walked in the gate which one of your fellow guards left unlocked?” inquired the Dark Master

    “That…that is true my Lord.” Said the Orc guard

    “As punishment the one who left the gate open will not be gifted with my presence for 2 months while you will also forgo my love for 2 weeks.” Said the Dark Master as the female Orc guard lowered her head. That was a semi-harsh punishment for to go without her master’s love was hard, but she had failed him and she would take the punishment.

    “Now…who are you two?” inquired the Dark Lord

    “I am Isaac.” Said the man…man was giving him maybe too much credit he looked only in his early to mid-20s. He was dirty, wore the cloths of a common worker and carried no weapons. That was extremely odd for someone attempting to kill him.

    “I am Mary.” Said the women, she was about the same age as the man and wore about the same level of clothing. She was beautiful in a common sort of way, nothing like the members of his harem but each of them where borderline goddess and if his plan went right each would mother children fathered by him to bring magic back to this world.

    “So Isaac and Mary why did you break into my castle?” inquired the Dark master as he sat down on his throne. As he did one of his butlers a rather beautiful catgirl came up and gave him a goblet of his favorite drink before bowing so low to show off her breasts.

    “Thank you, Nancy.” Said the Dark Lord as the catgirl blushed before moving back. “Now why are you here?”

    What started coming out of both of them was a semi predictable story. Both of them loved each other, but their families wouldn’t let them be together. Isaac was a Manson a skill which was something he could use and Mary was a weaver another useful skill both where 24 so he was right about their ages. They went into details about how the local priest had refused to marry them in secret and had informed both sets of parents.

    “They want us to marry people whom we don’t love.” Said Mary “So…we ask you Dark Lord, to turn us.”

    That got his attention.

    “Turn you?” inquired the Dark Lord

    “Yes, we may become what some would call crimes against God but we shall be together.” Explained Isaac

    That was something new, he had never had two people willing to become his servants.

    “We only ask that we stay together.” stated Mary “Nothing more.”

    “Nothing more?” inquired the Dark Lord “You know, I transform people to become members of my harem to pleasure me and all of them are women. My goal is to father a new line of magical races, you understand this do you not?’

    “We do.” Said Isaac “And while I admit I would rather stay a man, being with Mary is more important then anything else to me.”

    “And me.” Confirmed Mary

    “I will have to consider this.” Stated the Dark Lord
