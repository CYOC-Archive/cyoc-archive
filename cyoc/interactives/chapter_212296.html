    It should have landed in Rick or someone else's hands, but had somehow wound up in there. Now, the camera was just waiting for activation to get itself and its users into a more sensible situation. Not that it had a mind, but rather that this was the will of the chaos magic which flowed through it.

    Rick was about to transform not into a person, or even an object, but a location. And Emily would be found inside it, herself changed and very confused.
