    As Jeff tries to come up with an idea of what to try next, he hears the doorbell ring, ‘now, who could that be,’ he asks himself, ‘I’m not awaiting anyone and I am the only one home, my brother and sister are both at friends and ma and pa are both on business trips and won’t be back for two weeks’. The doorbell rings again, Jeff grumbles, ‘someone’s persistent,’ he thinks to himself, before letting out a sigh, “COMING,” he calls out, before getting to his feet and head down the stairs to open the door.

    When Jeff opens the door his eyes widen in surprise to see the brother of his mother standing on the porch, “heya Jeff,” the 40-year-old man says, a smile on his face.

    “Uncle Boris,” Jeff asks in surprise, “what brings you here?”

    “Oh, I got word that your parents are gone for two weeks,” Boris says, “since I’’m divorced and living by myself, and since I haven’t seen you for so long, I figured it would be nice to spend some time with you and your siblings. Are Kyle and Lucy home?”

    Jeff shakes his head, “they are both at friends,” he says, “but, it would be nice hanging out with you, and catch up on things, why don’t you come in?”

    Boris smiles, “thanks, Jeff,” he says as he enters the house.

    Jeff then leads his uncle into the living room, “so, what’s new,” he asks, “would you like a drink?”

    “I’d like a Coke, if you don’t mind,” Boris says, sitting on the couch, “oh, not really, apart from my partner and I finally being able to get our hands on Mister “Big”, you know, the boss of one of the largest crime families?”

    Jeff’s eyes widen, he knew his uncle, who is a police officer, and his partner being after Mister “Big” for months, “wow, how did you pull it off?”

    “That, I’m afraid, is classified,” Boris says, “but everything went as planned, no one got injured”.

    “I hope you’ll get a reward for capturing him,” Jeff says, heading into the kitchen to get the drinks, soon returning with two glasses of Coke.

    Boris chuckles, “oh, I’m not asking for a reward,” he says, “having finally got the most dangerous man in the city behind bars is more than I ask for”.

    Jeff smiles up at his uncle, who, like Jeff has black hair with some gray, brown eyes and an athletic body.

    “So, how’s school been going,” Boris asks.

    “Oh, schools going fine,” Jeff says, “I’m top of my class,” he adds proudly.

    Boris chuckles, “if you keep it up, I might just see you join our police force”.

    “I’d love that,” Jeff says, “you know that I wanna become a police officer like you! You are my idol!”

    “Aaaaw, you flatter me, Jeff,” Boris chuckles.

    “Hehe,” Jeff chuckles, “sorry uncle, but I need the bathroom! I’ll be back a few minutes!”

    Boris nods, “do you mind if I look around the house,” he asks, “I haven’t been here in a while”.

    “Sure, go ahead,” Jeff says as he hurries out to use the bathroom.

    While Jeff is gone, Boris heads upstairs and enters Jeff’s room, there he immediately notices the program on Jeff’s computer and being curious, he decides to check it out. After reading the manual a grin appears on his face, “these will be two interesting weeks,” he says to himself.
