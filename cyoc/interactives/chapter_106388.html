Fortunately for Frieda the quiet ride soon ends as her mother pulls into the garage; parking her car next to Benton ostentatious Lamborghini. Seconds later the emergency generator kicks in and the house is once again illuminated.
    "Wouldn't be surprised if the whole town lost power," Amanda remarks.
