    2034:


    2034 was the year everything changed.

    Humanity woke to the greatest surprise they never expected. In orbit around the planet were hundreds of alien space craft. The various space
    agencies had looked straight through the craft, despite each being several hundred km wide. Within minutes of their detection, the aliens on
    board had taken over every communication frequency band available. Where possible, video was projected onto every TV, smartphone,
    computer and billboard.

    The aliens envoy, clearly a female of particular beauty, delivered a stark message to the world, her alien dialect being translated real-time by a
    universal translation tool. Her voice was sultry.

    "Our species is dying. Our scientists have devised a solution, but to deploy it without testing could prove fatal to us. Your species has been
    selected from the many across the universe due to our genetic similarities. The experiment will begin shortly, do not worry."

    Clearly recorded in advance, the message repeated itself multiple time as panic broke out in the streets. Politicians pondered a pre-emptive
    strike, but without knowing what was going to happen, accepted responding could be equally suicidal.

    Then it happened - high powered beams blasted from the sky to earth from each of the ships, striking at various strategic points around the
    globe. Once established, gigantic pulses emanated down the beams. Upon reaching the earth, the pulses spread out like waves across the
    planet, sweeping across the continents.

    Once the waves had passed, part of the aliens plan was clear. The only members of the human race left alive were female. All that remained of
    the other sex was dust, as if their bodies had been vaporised. Young children were confused as to why some of their friends had disappeared in
    front of them and a number of pregnant women were at a loss to explain the sudden change in their situation.

    Remaining members of state from the varying countries were quick to contact each other, some governments in more disarray than others as a
    result of their gender policies and decisions.

    Then all the TV screens lit up again, the same woman appearing.

    "Thank you for participating in Phase One of our experiment. The males of your species have been disposed of. This is to create a control
    situation similar to our own.

    Our males were eradicated by a virus - we understand the concern that we undertook such a radical simulation of the event on your species.
    Our scientists have devised a solution for a species to reproduce within the remaining population. As previously disclosed, since this involves a
    genetic restructuring at the cellular level, your species has been chosen to test the results of our experiments.

    Do not be alarmed at the results. We understand you may feel this unfair, but we consider the survival of our race a higher cause. We will not
    contact you again until the experiment has finished, although our ships will remain in orbit. Your continued participation is gratefully
    appreciated."

    As the broadcast ended, the beams started up again, striking the same positions on the planet. The waves once again spread across the planets
    population, rendering everyone unconscious.


    2037:


    Humanity accepted the change as best it could. The aliens brutal application of their technology in the name of 'science' threatened to rip
    humanity to pieces. Every member of the female species was affected, although in differing ways.

    The surviving elite (comprising the politicians, the think tanks and the celebrated) and their scientist cabals divided humanity into groupings,
    branded as 'Types', consistent with the effects of the alien technology, dubbed 'the Xenowaves'. Society, as it does, quickly reoriented itself
    around these groups, although less bound by gender it became a less formal, more open society. On the surface, anyway.
