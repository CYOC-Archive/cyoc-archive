When Tyler watch Jane meeting up with other boys, he started to become more and more angrier and jealous, “Jane is mine,” he always told himself, “no other boy has the right to touch or look at her. No one will take her away from me, I’ll make sure of that.”

The teen wanted to get Jane by herself, to prevent any interference with his plan, so seeing her all by herself in the mall, the boy could not believe his luck. “This is my chance to get Jane,” the boy says to himself as he follows “Jane” through the mall, already his phone in hand, conflicted with if he should just scan her or go and say “hi”.

My name is Amber… Amber Jones” he says.

    Tyler is totally taken aback, “oh… sorry…,” he says, “you look exactly like my ex.” The teen stares at “Amber”, ‘I can’t believe that there is a nother girl out there who looks just like Jane…,’ he thinks to himself. Tyler then grins inwardly, as a thought pops into his mind, ‘if I can’t have the original… I’ll just take the next best thing.’

What is your name, if you don’t mind me asking?” Jeff is unaware that Tyler will assume that “Amber” has an interest in him, why else would she ask for his name? In truth though, Jeff/”Amber” is just being friendly, ‘I never saw him,’ Jeff thinks to himself as he waits for a reply, ‘Jane never introduced him to me.’

    Jeff smiles, Tyler feels himself melting in the “girl’s” smile, “what a nice name,” she says.

    Jeff raises an eyebrow, he then chuckles, which comes out like a giggle, “well, some of us girls,” she says, “are also interested in “boy stuff”. I personally enjoy soccer, basketball, American football and shooter.”

    Tyler is amazed, “wow…,” he says, “hehe… so you’re a real “tom boy”, hm?”

    “Hehe,” Jeff laughs, “you could say that.”

    “What kind of games do you have,” the boy asks, ‘man… I really made a lucky catch with this chick. She is even better than that bitch Jane! Amber is interested in stuff I enjoy, where Jane just scoffed at.’

    ‘Tyler seems like a cool guy,’ Jeff thinks to himself, “oh, almost every Call Of Duty, Doom, Wolfenstein, Halo… those are games I enjoy.”

    Tyler widens his eyes in surprise, then he chuckles, “and I always thought girls were soft,” he says, he then smiles at “Amber”, “looks like I was wrong, hu?”

    “Amber” grins, “hehe... seems like it,” she says, “let me guess, you also enjoy shooters?”

    Tyler nods, “I LOVE 'em,” he replies, “my most favorite is the new Call of Duty game.”

    “Really,” the “girl” asks, “I assume you are talking about the new Modern Warfare 2, right?”

    “I sure am,” Tyler says, “have you played it?”

    “Amber” shakes her head, “nah, not yet. I watched some gameplays on YouTube, and I thought the story looked amazing, especially the graphics. Sadly, I have not much money left, after all the clothes shopping.”

    This causes Tyler to laugh, “I guess you are a “typical” girl,” he says, “spending all your money on clothes and make up.”

    “Amber” pouts, “hey, a girl has to look her best,” she replies, she then grins, “even a “Tomboy” like myself.”

    Tyler smiles at the “girl”, 'she is amazing,' he thinks to himself, 'we'd make such a perfect couple. I need to play it smart, I hope she is single and in search of a boyfriend, if not... well, I got my little “helper” who'll sort that issue out for me.' He then looks at “Amber”, “listen, since you seem to want the game so badly,” he says, “I'd be happy to pay for it.”

    Jeff/”Amber” is taken aback, “thanks Tyler,” she says, “that is really sweet of you, but I rather buy it myself. I can wait another month or so,” she adds with a smile.

    Tyler nods, “well, think about it,” he says, smiling, “the offer continues to stand. What have you planned for the rest of the day?”

    “Nothing much,” “Amber” says, “I think I'll head home and get my shopping sorted.”

    Tyler is about to ask if she'd like him to help her carry her bags, but decides against it, 'I can wait until she invites me over sometime,' he thinks. “Okay,” he then says, “um... w-would you like to meet up again sometime?”

    Jeff/”Amber” thinks for a while, 'Tyler sure is a cool and kind guy. It would not hurt to hang out with him from time to time as “Amber”. He has not even made any “certain” moves and has not been to flirty with me.' The “girl” smiles, “sure, I'd like to get to know you more,” she says, “how about we meet at the park at around 2pm tomorrow?”

    Tyler beams, “it's a date,” he says, grinning.

    Jeff/”Amber” chuckles, assuming Tyler means it in a joking way, unaware that Tyler really sees the “meeting up again” as a actual date. “So, I guess I'll see you tomorrow,” the “girl” then says.

    “Yeah,” Tyler replies, “most defiantly!”

    Both say good bye and “Amber” starts to walk away, unaware that Tyler has made up his mind to make her HIS. The boy watches “Amber” leave, he then grins, 'soon... my princess,' he thinks to himself, 'very soon... you'll be MINE!”
