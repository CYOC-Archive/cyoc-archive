    Eventually, the drink becomes more or less like water to you, and you feel you could down gallons of it at
    once.

    One morning, as you get dressed for work, you notice that your pants don't cover your ankles anymor, and your
    shirt shows your abdomen. You shrug it off, thinking that they shrunk in the wash. You'll have to get a new
    pair soon.

    Over the next few days, you notice that it wasn't the wash, and that you keep getting taller. You had been
    5'10"
    before, now you were closer to 7'.

    You decide to ask the ringleader today at work. When you arrive, you are offered a larger drink than usual.
    You try to ask your questions, but the drink is too tempting and you start to guzzle it down. As you drink,
    you skin becomes a deep gray , as you grow to eight feet tall. The ringleader then gives you shifts that make
    it impossible to go home, forcing you to stay in a bunk near the elephants.

    Strangely, they all snicker behind your back while you feed them and clean, almost as if expecting something.
    You are offered increasing amounts of that drink everyday, and you guzzle it as much as you can. You start to
    gain some weight, slowly growing from some slight pudge to a large belly, but you also get stronger too, with
    noticeable muscles like a professional weightlifter would have.

    Eventually, the ringleader asks you into his quarters. When you arrive, an incredibly large bottle of the
    drink is waiting for you. You can't resist and you make a mess everywhere. As you guzzle, you feel your
    clothes getting tighter, as you grow even more with height and mass. Your clothes burst to reveal that your
    feet have grown larger. You drink more and more as your feet meld with your thickening legs and your toes
    shrink to large nails on the side. Your hands do the same, as you are forced onto all fours.

    You are not sure what is happening, but you can't stop drinking, Your hair recedes into your head as your skin
    thickens. Your ears grow to an immense size as a small tail grows from your large behind. You can't seem to
    get to the last little bit, but then your nose forms a piglike snout and starts to grow, and grow, and grow,
    until it becomes prehensile and you can drink that last bit. When you finish, your teeth become broader and
    your mouth gets bigger as two large tusks form from your bottom teeth.

    "It looks like we have a new member of the circus!" Says the ringleader
