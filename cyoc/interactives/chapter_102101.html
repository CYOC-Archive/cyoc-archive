    Leader of the pack

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 7 years ago O

    The alpha wolf snapped at Jeff, and she jumped back. The alpha wolf began to slowly circle her, growling angrily. Jeff may have been mostly wolf, but she still did have some human intelligence. She knew that she could not match the Alpha wolf's strength, but perhaps she could outsmart it. She looked around the terrain. Maybe there was a cliff or something. She could dodge, and the Alpha would fly over. It seemed far-fetched, but she didn't have much options. Then she spotted it. Hidden in the snow was her salvation.

    Jeff dashed towards it in the snow, and the Alpha wolf followed close behind, nipping at her heels. Jeff ran as close as possible to the snare, but passed by it easily. The Alpha wolf was not so lucky. The snare caught his rear leg, and he was brought to an abrupt halt as it pulled tight. The Alpha fell flat on his face, and Jeff considered using this opportunity to attack. But the Alpha quickly recovered, and began snapping at her. Jeff backed off, circling around the trapped Alpha, just out of range of the rope attached to its leg. Jeff would have smiled if it were possible.

    The Alpha soon realized its predicament, and began to get more concerned about its trapped leg than Jeff. It started biting its leg, trying to chew it off. Jeff didn't waste the opportunity and sprang to attack. Her powerful jaws latched onto the neck of the large Alpha. She felt warm blood splatter on her coat, and cascade down her throat. It tasted surprisingly good. The Alpha whimpered and tried to fight back. But his attempts were weak. Jeff must have hit an artery or something. Jeff tore away the tissue in her jaw, and lurched forward again, tearing away more of the tissue around the Alpha's neck. It whimpered weakly, and fell to the ground. The fight was over, and Jeff had won. She stood over her prize, and howled in victory.

    The other wolves slowly approached. The females bowed their heads, and some of the males rolled over, presenting their bellies in submission.

    I guess I am the new alpha, thought Jeff. It's good to be the Queen.
