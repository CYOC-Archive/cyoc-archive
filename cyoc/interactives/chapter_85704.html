    He couldn't know what kind of effects the pink mist he had inhaled so much of would have on his body, but the angrier he became about the whole mess, the closer he came to finding out!

    Rick ran upstairs to give Brendon a piece of his mind but was suddenly stopped by a bolt of electricity shooting through his body. He stood by the entrance of the house, at the base of the long stairs, when he froze. All of his body tensed and he felt like he needed to take a deep breath of fresh air, so he began to walk out of the house. Walking towards the front door he felt like he would faint. Everything felt so awkard and it wasn't until he bumped his head on the doorframe that he noticed that something had happened to him. The doorframe was tall so bumping his forehead on his way out meant he was standing at least at seven and a half feet!

    He looked down and the change in height was dizzying, but not as dizzying as the feeling of tightness. Like he was being hugged so tightly! The sleeves of his coat were up to his growing elbow, his pants tight around his knee and already beginning to tear from his thighs and growing rump. Rick groaned and looked at how huge his arms were growing, and his hands with them. His hands and feet (which tore through his sneakers!) were huge, thick and beginning to take a green color, his nails turning black. "Whats happening to me!?" Rick yelled, and his voice was a guttural roar. His skin thickened and began to break into scales!

    When Rick could see above the first floor of his house he knew that he was turning into some kind of monster. The last of his clothes tore off his enormous frame and it began to change more and more, never stopping his growth. His thighs had grown thicker than the trees around the house and his arms were capable of bringing the entire house down, and he was still growing. His hands became bigger as his nails stretched into thick claws. His toenails thickened more and more, also growing into two-feet long claws that kept stretching as Rick pumped himself higher and higher.

    There was a huge thud as an enormous tail stretched from behind his large rump, crushing two cars behind him. Slowly Rick began to notice how his face became numb and his vision changed! Suddenly he was looking at a massive lizard-like snout where his face was, and feeling it with his mighty claws he could tell his teeth had become long and incredibly sharp. Rick roared and looked down. He was passing the house, standing at over thirty feet high. Soon he was becoming so gigantic he could see the entire neighborhood, and the spread between his monstrous legs was covering the entire block. Things like trees and houses became jammed on his toes, which were encroaching on the entire neighborhood. He was standing at least at one hundred and fifty feet. He roared again and took one step, destroying the ground beneath his feet.

    Rick looked at the city in the distance, a distance a gigantic monster like him could cross in seconds, and felt an uncontrollable urge to destroy it. Who would stop him?
