    Transform or Dare?

    An odd one out

    Branch Full view
    added by Anon 4 years ago O

    Her hand landed on a manga she had won in a contest some time ago. On the front cover was a bear who looked cute on one half and evil on the other. It was also one that her sister recommended to her, for some odd reason. She had also mentioned to Jennifer that it was also a game and an anime. Aside from that, she didn't know much about it.
    Skimming through it, she couldn't tell much about it. She knew there were these super talented kids trapped in a school and something about despair. She also knew that there was some murder and that was about it. But as she looked through it more, she began to truly regret her decision. A trial, a brutal execution, no one was safe. Wait. She wasn't actually going to be transported INTO the world of the manga, right? She wasn't entirely sure, but she was going to have to pick sooner or later. All she could do was hope for the best. After some time, she closed her eyes and continued to shuffle through pages until her finger landed on one of the female characters.
