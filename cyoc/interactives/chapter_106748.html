    The reporter pulled into the driveway of the officer that had been suspended after being attacked by Lionel Ulysses. He hoped to find something that would provide answers to all the mysteries that currently surrounded the case.

    The reported walked up to the door and knocked on it, waiting for a response. After about a minute, the door opened and the officer the reporter was looking for was standing there.

    "Can I help you?," the officer asked the reporter.

    "Yes," the reporter said, "I heard you were attacked by Lionel Ulysses and you saw some strange things on that day. Could you tell me what you saw?"

    The officer stepped out of the way and lead the reporter into the living room, where the reporter sat down in a chair across from the officer.

    "So," the reporter said, "Tell me, what happened that day?"

    "Well," the officer said, "I was guarding one of the exits where Ulysses and his gang were presumed to exit. All of a sudden, there was an explosion, and I was knocked to the ground. A shower of gunshots began as both sides began shooting. As I began to get up, I saw Ulysses running away, wounded. The strangest thing about it was that I saw a tail starting to sprout from Ulysses. It looked so lifelike and real, as if it was actually the tail of a rat. I told the FBI agent that was there and the SPD reviewed the footage from my vest cam. Sure enough, there was a tail, but no one is sure if it's a real tail or just some costume part."

    "I see," the reporter said, "Tell me, what do you think about all this?"

    "I think that something strange is happening here," the officer said, "It seems like some people are trying to cover up what's happening."

    "My thoughts exactly," the reporter said, "Thank you for time."
