    Mike looked at the package that arrived... the chronivac... he'd been waiting weeks for this. Was it possible for this device to change a person in any way? He really hoped so. He'd wanted to be different for some time.

    Mike was a fairly average guy. About 165 pounds, 5'11". Somewhat handsome, the boy next door type. This boy next door wanted more though. More weight, that is. He had been trying for over 2 years to get fat, but he hadn't gained a pound. He didn't know why, but he wanted to be a fat boy. He reached into the box and pulled the item out. "allright, let's get this hooked up and give it a shot."

    ----------

    After about 20 minutes, Mike had the chronivac set up and ready to go. He booted up the program and waited. It asked him to use the machine to pick a target. He pointed it at himself and clicked ok. The Machine zapped, then processed, then a box popped up.

    Gender: Male
    Age: 20 years old
    Height : 5'11"
    Weight: 166
    Penis Size : 4 inches

    "Wow, this thing is thorough when it scans.... Now... let's see what we can change here...."

    He clicked around for a few minutes, and found a list of transformations. There were lots of choices. Age, gender, species, penis size, height... weight. He wondered what to do. "I guess I should test it out with a little change first. Maybe get a little taller? Maybe I should take a trip back to highschool, make myself younger?" he hovered over the choices, the weight button was right there..... he couldn't resist, and he clicked it. A box popped up. "Okay.... just a little change though. 15 pounds." He set the chronivac to add pounds of fat, not muscle. He went to type in the weight to add... but he paused.


    "Why only 15 pounds? I know I want to be fat. Why hold back?" He grinned, and did some math... He decided to go for broke, and add 234 pounds. That'd make him 400 pounds by the end. He clicked the begin button and waited.... and waited.... and waited. Nothing. He sighed and got up, dissappointed. He decided to go out, and forget about the disappointment. He walked out the door, never noticing the timer counting down on the screen. He should get a good surprise in 15 minutes.

    -----------

    He sat in McDonalds, trying to get fat the old fashioned way, downing milkshake after milkshake, when a friend came in. His name was Dutch. Mike envied Dutch. He was always a muscular guy, his peak weight was 210. But He had slacked off and grown a good gut. He was 250 pounds now, and all 40 of the new pounds had gone to his stomach.

    Dutch sat down and grinned. "You must be thirsty today, huh?". Mike chuckled "you could say that. He noticed that Dutch looked even fatter today. The lucky bastard put on weight without trying. Dutch spoke up again. "Hey, I got a new entertainment center today. Big screen TV. I was wondering if you wanna come over and help me christen it with a kung fu movie marathon.". Mike grinned "Sure, as long as we can watch Game of Death."

    Dutch agreed, and the two guys left the restaurant. Mike felt a bit bloated... little did he realize his belly was a little bigger... and it was about to get extreme.
