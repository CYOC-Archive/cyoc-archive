    Emily decided she wanted Rick to be aware again. Then, she would at least be together with someone else in this mess. She made sure she didn't mess it up. If she'd made Olivia or Paul aware too, that would be beyond awkward. There. Rick should be back.

    She looked at Rebecca. The busty redhead looked back at Emily, she then glanced back at her husband Paul, and then back again at Emily, then at Olivia. Her gaze jumped between the husband and wife duo. Then she laughed. It did seem like Rick was aware again. At least it didn't seem like he was angry at Emily for being left unaware for a while.

    "Honey, what's so funny?" Paul asked his wife.

    "Oh, nothing, nothing." she pretended like nothing was going on. "Just some joke I remembered. It's not really something I can explain."

    "M-hm," Paul said.

    Emily wanted to speak to Rick one on one, but with Olivia and Paul here, she didn't quite know how. She could use the camera on them, but something kept her. Part of it was that she didn't really mess with what used to be her parents. Another part wanted to keep Olivia as Eric's wife, his wife.

    "Uh, Rebecca, do you have a moment," Emily tried communicating with Rick without being too suspicious.

    "Do I?" she giggled. Then she teased in a much quieter voice, "At least I'm no longer a mom, but you're married to yours. How do you like it?" She then laughed again, attracting the attention of Olivia to them.

    "What's goin on," Olivia walked between them. She grabbed Eric's hand, and gave it a little affectionate squeeze.

    "Uh, Rebecca is just in a weird mood, laughing to herself.

    "Oh," Olivia mused. She looked at Eric. Years of love made emotions surge through him as he looked at this woman. Then he remembered this was her mom, and he looked away. "You're acting weird. Is there something going on? Something you haven't told me?"

    "No. I'm just thinking about work, about this uh, Camera," Emily excused himself.

    "Don't worry too hard about it okay? And, if you need to take your mind off it," Olivia put on a sly grin. "I can help with distracting you later," she flirted.

    "Yeah, that'd be great," Emily replied, imagining making love to Olivia. His body desired her so much, and he couldn't help but find her so sweet. The subtle mannerisms as she tilted her head slightly as she looked up at Eric, or how she played with her hair. As Olivia stepped away to check out something in a store front, he admired her behind as she moved.

    Interrupting his thoughts, Rebecca/Rick spoke, with the two of them finally being alone, "So Em, are you going to use the camera? Or," she giggled, "Do you want to fuck you mom first?"

    What was next? While Rick was acting very nonchalantly, was that just because he was chill about this, or was he just treating this as a joke? Would he react well if Emily decided they'd not use the camera for a while? Also, Emily was feeling conflicted about this too, about her feelings for Olivia.

    Emily makes her decision.
