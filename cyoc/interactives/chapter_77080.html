    Carl Gets Fooled Into Thinking He's Meeting His Hero

    Carl didn't give the application a second look after it had been put down in front of him, his full attention
    was his and Steve's favorite baseball player of all time. And that lucky bastard seemed to have already been
    hanging out with him! At least that's what Carl thought. Jon had noticed Carly's attention being drawn away
    and turned and got a good look at the towering baseball player making his way for the table. "Well well well,
    Johnny 'The Hammer' Helms, how the hell are you man? Have you left that agent of yours yet? You know he's
    bleeding you dry and I would happily settle for 8%."

    Steve not knowing who this guy was just laughed and played along, "Which I'm sure you'd work up to about 12%
    by the time we were done." Jon laughed a whole lot harder at the joke than he normally would have if it
    weren't for Johnny's celebrity. "You know me all too well buddy. So did you just come all the way over here to
    get my hopes up?"

    "Actually I'm here because Stephanie here wanted to visit her friends over here." Steve said motioning to the
    twins.

    "Really?" Jon said, "Small world I guess."

    'Stephanie' stepped away from the guys as she set down by Carl and Wendy and noticing the paperwork said, "So
    what are you two doing here? Don't tell me this guy's talked you into a timeshare or something."

    "Actually Jon here is a talent agent and he's going to get us on Big Brother!" Wendy said with a squeal. "Oh
    my God!" 'Stephanie' said and started talking with Wendy about how great she thought the show was. Huh, I knew
    Janet liked the show but didn't know Steve did too Carl thought as he separated himself from the table to get
    close to Johnny. Nervously he managed to work up the courage to say, "Hi there."

    Steve turned to look at his feminine best friend and decided to have some fun. "Well hello there," Steve said
    in as suave a voice as he could manage. "What can I do for you?"

    Carl was really feeling the butterflies in his stomach, Johnny Helms was here talking to him! "Oh nothing
    nothing. I just wanted to say I'm a huge huge fan. And I just love you." Carl was mortified as soon as he
    realized what he said in his nervousness, "I don't mean like that! I just mean that..."

    Steve was doing his best not to bust out laughing, "I know what you mean, I get it all the time," then Steve
    decided to really have some fun and motioned for Janet to join him, "but even if you did mean it like that I
    couldn't return the affection, I've found the gal for me in your friend right here." Carl just blinked in
    confusion while Jon interrupted their conversation. "So you mean to tell me the rumors are true? You really
    did marry some girl last night on a whim in Vegas."

    "Not just any girl. This girl I gotta say, is the most, how should I put it, the most magical girl I know."
    Steve said causing Janet to let out a little bit of a giggle. Carl was dumbfounded and turned to Wendy to make
    sure she was seeing this, but she was busy with filling out hers (and Carly's) applications.

    "Well let me be the first to tell you two congratulations, couldn't happen to a nicer guy." Jon said not
    letting the shock get in the way of his glad handing. "I'll send you a nice bottle of Champlain. What room are
    you in?"

    "Just send it up with Carly and Wendy, the four of us are going to meeting up later tonight." Janet said
    winking at Carl deciding it was time to let him in on the joke.

    "Really?" Jon said raising his eyebrows.

    "What?" Carl said clearly not getting what was going on. Steve decided to give another hint, "You'll see what
    she means tonight," and he bent down to whisper to Carl so that only here would hear, "Carl." Carl's eyes went
    wide as Steve stood back and winked at him. "Oh. OH! Okay, we'll definitly see you tonight then."

    "You have room for one more big guy?" Jon said and when he just got a weird look from 'Johnny' 'Stephanie' and
    'Carly' backtracked, "It was just a joke, you have a good time in Vegas Hammer."

    "Will do, let's go honey." Steve said and in a single swoop picked up his wife and carried her out of the
    dinning hall making a big show of it.

    "So you know that girl huh? What can you tell me about her?" Jon said to Carl looking for some more info on
    the mystery fem that stole Johnny's heart.

    "Just that...well. Like Johnny said, she's magical." Carl said but before Jon could ask a follow up question
    said, "Sorry, I've got to get back to that application."
