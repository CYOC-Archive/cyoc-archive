As they burned through the lust, the Chakats there didn't go rushing to spread the virus any further. Fee City fell, but it didn't radiate out, at least not quickly... and by the time those that might have wanted to spread the virus consciously got the idea to do so, they'd been vaccinated, unknown to them. Now that was beginning to hit the outbreak area in Paris and would slow its spread... at least by the Fee City method of spreading the virus.

    The other idea didn't sit right with Jess, as shi wasn't entirely sure how someone in London could unleash an outbreak in Paris. There were hundreds of miles between the capital of the United Kingdom and France, and the French city of Orleans was closer, but there was one good point that Flake had made. If the spy or puppet master was in London, he or she might have had full access to the DNA but wouldn't be caught up in the virus allowing that control to be maintained. It also created another problem... if the person wasn't in Paris, or even in France, and wasn't subject to the quarantines the French were putting up, the virus could easily be spread somewhere else. They would need get some word to London, but they still had matters that had to be finished in Paris, namely the fact that virus was now also an airborne virus and not just an STD.

    "We'll still have the airborne issue of the virus's spread," Jess said after a moment.

    "Yes, but your dad is the key to that, you said so yourself," Flake told hir.

    That made Jess smile. Hir Foxkat DNA couldn't create a vaccine that would keep people human, but hir father's human DNA could. Shi smiled and then looked back to the pen where the European Wolves (a subspecies of the Grey Wolf) were laying and looking around. Jess didn't say that there was a great deal of fear in the back of hir mind as she looked at them, but shi also assumed that wolves would hunt and kill Fennec Foxes if the two species lived in the same areas. After all, wolves reintroduced into parts of the US had been documented killing Coyotes, and Coyotes weren't anywhere near as small as Fennec Foxes.

    "We'll need to report to the others that the more violent acts might be more territorial than political or mindless," Jess said after a moment.

    Flake nodded, "It should make things easier in terms of dealing with them."

    Jess nodded, "Let's get going."

    With that the two Kats began to move back toward the Chateau.

Even if there is more than enough food for all of them."

    The French Kats conceded the point, and followed Maurice around. After all, they'd had no trouble so far. They continued their patrol quietly for awhile longer until they found themselves in a poorer section of the northern part of Paris. It wasn't slums, but the apartments and buildings were built and placed to house a lot of people... a lot more than what would be comfortable.The area was quiet, but they did hear a few sounds. As Maurice took a step forward, a commanding voice called out to hir and made hir stop.

    "Je t'aimerai toujours, la mère. Vous n'êtes pas un monstre. Un Kat, oui, un monstre, non," Alexis said to hir mother.

    Francine lifted her head and actually looked hopeful and asked, "Vous souhaitez devenir un Kat pour moi?"

Shi didn't want to transform hir father, and knew that Sally wouldn't want that either, but the adult French noblewoman, who like Nikole now looked much younger, looked emotionally broken by hir transformation into a Chakat. Jess knew hir father could make love to Francine with no fear of infection, but that knowledge also conflicted with a couple of things.
