    "Ah knew that shortcut through the mountains was a bad idea!" a voice grumbled.
    There was the figure of a rotund woman dusting herself off in the hallway.
    "What did ah hit this ti-... oh naw." the figure said as she backed into the room and the moonlight pouring into it.
    The woman turned to face the boys.
    She was pretty pudgy, in a dark grey dress fastened around her waist by a belt buckle. Her deep brown skin glistened like her messy black hair. She wore big boots with belt buckles across them, and a big, flowery hat.
    "... Heyyy there guys.... how's it goin'?" She asked with a twinkle in her hazel eyes.
    "Good?" Phillip said in bewilderment.

    She poofed the headmistress into glitter, cast a repair spell on the wall, and grabbed her broom.
    "Hold it right there!" a man yelled.
