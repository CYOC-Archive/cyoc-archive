When we've mixed additional animal DNA in the past, it was with mundane animal DNA. Perhaps the fact that you've already been uplifted has changed things in a way that we didn't initially expect... We'll need to do some testing."

He ultimately approached the tank and tapped on the glass with one finger. Inside the tank, Teela noticed and turned toward him. The scientist then pointed up with that hand toward the surface of the water. Kirana seemed to think Teela understood as she nodded and lifted her head and ultimately breached the surface of the water.

    "But don't humans have legs?" Teela asked as she found herself balancing on her tail and "standing" in the same way that Kirana did.

    "Yes... but for snake Animalians and you, my dear Teela, our first eel Animalian, legs aren't necessary as you can clearly stand without them," the scientist flashed her a warm and friendly smile, "and in the end, whether or not you have legs doesn't change your status as an Animalian.
