    Nice to meet you, Alexandra...

    added 4 years ago A TG O Anthro

    You and Janice, formerly Jake, you decided to just call him or her Janice until further notice, ran down the hallway as the battle between the Mysterious Stranger and Prince Karther continued.

    The battle was so loud that the two of you could still hear it even now while you had gained several rooms and hallways of distance.

    The two of you had no chance of catching any breath because a couple of kitsune guards had started chasing you two immediately on sight.

    Then the two of you bumped into a vixen.

    "Sorry." you said to her as Janice helped her up.

    "Why are you two running?" she asked.

    "A couple of guards are chasing us!" Janice answered her.

    "That is because you two are clearly run away concubines," a voice said from behind.

    You, Janice, and the other vixen whose name you hadn't learned yet all jumped with startled surprise. The guards had caught up!

    Thinking quickly, you said, "No we're not! We were just trying to get to safety from the battle between the Prince and that Mysterious Stranger when you two just started chasing us!"

    The guards frowned.

    Then one asked Janice, "Is that true?"

    "Yes!" Janice said with a nod, "It is!"

    "Sorry," they both said and then left.

    "Ugh!" the vixen you and Janice had bumped into groaned, "Those palace guards are so overzealous. Sorry you had to go through that, I've been there myself. What are your names."

    "I'm Tammy," you say.

    "I'm Janice," Janice says.

    The vixen grins and nods her head and says, "Nice to meet you two, Tammy and Janice, I'm Alexandra. Did you two hear that the Prince is going to choose his Queen once that intruder has been dealt with?"

    You both shake your heads.

    "Oh well." Alexandra replies.

    The three of you made some casual, yet cautioned on the part of you and Janice, small talk for a while before you and Janice decided to continue onward.

    "OK," Alexandra says, "It was very nice to meet you two girls! Oh, are either of you interested in a Vixen's Scornful Revenge Potion?"

    "What is a Vixen's Scornful Revenge Potion?" you ask.

    "It is a potion that if you drink it, permanently gives you the power to get revenge on any male that has wronged you by turning him into a female fox (even if he wasn't a fox to begin with) by kissing him. If you kiss him more than once, it will also put a female version of his personality in control of his body, while leaving his original male persona trapped inside while being fully aware of everything that the new female self sees, hears, tastes, feels, touches, and does from that point on. Only a vixen like you two girls can drink it. Oh, and if the male that wronged you is apologetic, or is going to give you a gift when you use it on him, then it will simply turn him into your lesbian lover instead."

    She pauses and waits for the two of you to process the information.

    "So, are you interested?"
