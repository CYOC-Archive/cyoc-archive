    I wanted to scream right there and then. But I was interrupted by a little knock at the door.

    “Leeeooooo?” came Jenny’s voice. “You okay in there?”

    “I’m not!” I barked. Louder than I should have. I was starting to panic. Can you blame me?

    “Can I help you?”

    “I… oh fuck I don’t know…” I said. I was whimpering. Shivering a little. I didn’t protest when Jenny opened the door and stepped in. That’s when I realized how much I had shrunk. She’s like 5’ tall and I was 6’ tall when we first met. Now we were almost level with each other.

    There was a long moment of silence, and I realized I was still shrinking slowly throughout. That was what the sinking, the weird feeling was. Just everything around me seeming to move away from me as I shrunk. By the time Jenny spoke we were exactly the same height.

    “You got smaller”, she said.

    “Yes. I did. Thanks”, I said weakly.

    “That’s really interesting. How did you do it?”

    “I didn’t! It just… happened!”

    She grabbed me by the hand and pulled me out of the bathroom. I didn’t fight back. My clothes were sagging on me like a tent. Jenny pressed her side to mine and giggled. “Look at that, you’re smaller than me now!”

    “C-cut it out Jenny!” I said, panic rising in my voice again. “Call a-an ambulance!”

    “And what, tell them you’re the incredible shrinking man?”

    “Not funny!”

    I struggled with my clothes, trying to keep them from embarrassing me any further. I was unarguably shorter than Jenny now, and there was no sign of it stopping.

    “You know, this is really interesting”, she purred, bending over a little and patting me on the head.

    “Not funny!”

    “This shirt isn’t doing you any favors, tiny. Mind if I get rid of it for you?”

    “Shirt?” I felt the blood rushing to my face. “I… I mean if you want…” Jenny grabbed onto my shirt and whisked it off with ease.
