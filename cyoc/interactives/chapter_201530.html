    Kade Gets Ready For School

    Fortunately for Kade, he didn't need to display his spraying capabilities, as his mother looked up at the clock, “Oh! Kade, the bus will be here soon! Kids, you better get ready for school!”. Kade sighed in relief, glad he didn't have to humiliate himself in-front of his family. His father nodded, “We better get going, son.” to which Kade nodded, quickly rising from his chair, quickly joined by his sis-err-'brother' now with a grunt, still tapping on his phone, sticky pancake syrup dribbling down from his protruding lower lip and onto his matted fur.

    “Aww, I wanted to see big bro make stinky!” Penny giggled with his twin, as their mother went behind the small skunk boys and picked them up, “You can wait until after he gets back home. For now, you two stinkers need to be practicing at kindergarten.” He kissed the two, who mewled affectionately, Penny letting out a small but pungent toot at the affection. Louie chuckled, but still chided them as he scooped the two out of their seats, “No more of that until we get your protective pants on. Warren, could you help me get Peter and Penn into their diapers before you go to work?” Warren wiped his maw with his furry arm, standing up with a warm smile, “Of course, honey!” He walked over to a nearby cabinet, pulling out an enormous package of diapers, which he set on the kitchen table. Kade realized that, as Louie started laying the squirming skunk toddler boys onto the counter, they probably changed them there too, and felt a bit more disgusted since they had just eaten off of it. Not wanting to get nauseous, he walked back into the family's living room and grabbed his bookbag he had left there the day before he woke up to this insanity.

    Walking out the front door, he was a bit relieved to be free from the stink inside the house, he hadn't realized how nasty it was in there until he took a deep breath of the outside fresh air, although as he continued smelling the faint undertones of that sickly skunk stench, he realized that at least part of it was emanating from himself. He wondered if he should return to check out if they had any deodorant or air fresheners or something, but considering how his family now was, he doubted that. Kade still turned to run back into the house and look before the bus arrived, but was stopped as he stumbled into his older brother Ellis, taking a deep whiff of his sibling's horrific body odor as he did, who huffed, “Watch where you're going, dweeb.” He pushed past him, waddling to the garage, Kade seeing how Ellis's short-shorts barely covered his massive rear, his cheeks hanging out and jiggling with each step. Ellis got to his car, the one their dad had bought for 'her' for her 16th birthday he imagined, but Lizzie drove a cute, hot pink bug. Ellis's car was a big, dirty pick-up truck that looked like it hadn't ever been washed since he bought it. Ellis was probably going to college, like Lizzie normally would.

    Kade watched him get inside, exhaling as a cloud of green haze and muffled sounds of flatulence erupted from him as he sat behind the wheel, before his father, mother, and freshly-diapered twin brothers soon exited from the house. Louie kissed his husband as Warren, still not wearing pants, readjusted his tie and waved at Kade, “Study hard, son! I'm looking forward to seeing your progress on your skunking!” Louie giggled effeminately, “Oh Warren, sometimes I think that's all you think about!” Warren grinned, beaming with pride, “I can't help being from such a proud family of stinkers!”. Kade watched them snuggle, still feeling strange at the sight, before Warren walked to his own sleek, black Sedan still in the garage. The family's SUV was still parked in the garage, though a bit grimier than before, which Louie made his way to with the twins. The bus would be here soon, Kade hoped, but he couldn't help but feel nervous. He was a skunk boy, was there anyone else like this besides his family? What if everyone else was disgusting skunk people? He shuddered, not knowing what to do. He couldn't go around blasting huge skunk farts like the rest of his family now did and embarrassing himself, could he?
