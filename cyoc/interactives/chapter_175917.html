    Jacob unpacked his things in the guest room provided to him. It was practically his second room since he’s too old to sleep in the same bed as Greg. The two of them were like inseparable brothers when they were younger. Now, they’re different.

    “Greg! Jacob! Come on let’s go!” Greg’s dad ordered. Greg and Jacob both ran down the stairs dressed up nice. Jacob knew they would eventually go somewhere nice, but not this quick.

    To the Andersons, Jacob would only be seen as a relative to Greg’s mother. He stood out compared to them.

    The Andersons were a mother and father who had one son. They all had white skin with brunette hair to go with it. Greg’s mother had brown eyes while his father and him had green. All of them were around 5’ 8”, with Greg’s father being 5’10”. His parents were the stereotypes of their roles as a caring mother and the strong fatherly figure with their business.

    The Andersons are two business owners, but of different things. Greg’s dad Jake owned a line of exercise tools like equipment or protein powder. He himself uses his equipment, making him a fit and buff father to Greg.

    Greg’s mother Dian owned many business that all branched out from her main business. She started it off with things for new or old mothers. Cooking, cleaning, and child care. All things for any mother to start off or continue with. She started it after realizing how tough it was to take care of her son while also babysitting another.

    With how well their businesses are, they had access to be considered good enough to be with other famous business owners. They had the money to be rich, but they choose not to overdo it.

    The four of them got in Greg’s father’s car and drove off.

    As they were driving to the party, Greg and Jacob texted each other to avoid being heard.

    G: So when are we going to change?

    J: Well not now! Do you wanna be changed into diapers in front of all those people?

    G: It’s instant remember. Besides my mom would never change a diaper in front of people. She’ll respect our privacy.

    J: Lets just take it after the party. That way they’ll think the party food made us this way.

    G: Good idea.

    J: Delete these texts so we won’t get caught!

    Jacob and Greg deleted their entire conversation from their phones. Jacob knew Greg’s parents would react to their sudden age change, and their phones would be a part of their investigation. He just hoped that he would be able to play the shy baby trope when they change.

    They arrived at the party and all went their own ways. Greg’s dad went to his gym business friends while his mom went to her business friends. Greg and Jacob stayed together and just roamed the party hall. They danced, ate, and even snuck in some alcohol shots thanks to Greg’s dad. They were 16 year olds who were about to turn 3, so Greg wanted to live. Jacob only did the same things he did because he was sure they could play it off on their regression.

    After the party, the four of them went home. Greg and Jacob went straight to bed. Before they got to their rooms, Greg handed Jacob a small ziplock bag containing one pill. “I’ll hide the pills under my dresser. They won’t find it unless they lift it.” Greg whispered.

    As the two were in bed, Greg had already taken the pill without Jacob knowing. He was hesitant, but taken it too.

    The two shrank into toddlers who looked like they were playing dress up. Jacob took off his overgrown shirt and pants, but left the underwear for the change to look somewhat real. Greg on the other hand, didn’t care. He slept right after changing, leaving him in his overgrown clothes.

    In the morning, Jacob woke up to a text from Greg.

    G: ok how are we going to do this?

    J: wym?

    G: like do we go “gaa gaa goo goo” or what?

    J: well idk

    G: cause it’ll be weird to be talking like we’re still teenagers.

    J: maybe it’ll be good to be able to talk. Your mother will know we are faking it.

    G: true. So what do you think we should do. I’ll go with whatever you want.
