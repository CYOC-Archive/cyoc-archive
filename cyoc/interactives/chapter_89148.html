    An hour pass and Kylie is sitting on a bench in the middle of the park reading a fashion magazine as she hear the sounds of footsteps approaching her, she puts down the magazine and adjusts her classes as she sees Jennifer standing in front of her.
    Oh my god, Kyle? Is that you? Kylie looks at her a bit confused Kyle? Excuse me? Its Kylie, Kylie stands up and brushes away some dust from her skirt and looks at Jennifer, she is now barely taller than her.
    Jennifer looks at her surprised and shocked, Oh my god, Kylie? Kylie smiles and nods at her, Yep, thanks for coming by the way, i was kinda thinking you might decide not to show up.
    Jennifer shakes her head, well i didn’t plan to ever talk to you again but when you called me back i just had to see this for myself, Kylie smiles don’t be jealous darling, im sure you’re going to grow up into a fine woman as well in a few years.
    Kylie giggles and Jennifer looks at Kylie, okay, that is just weird hearing that coming from you of all people, Jennifer sits down on the bench while kylie looks at her, Look Jennifer, i know i kinda asked you out here for just a talk but let’s get straight to the point here.
    She sits down next to Jennifer, im a rich successful woman who have climbed my way up through the ranks in a male dominated world, I’m proud of what i am and i would never throw that away, Jennifer just nods but looks sorta confused, why are you telling me this ? How does that even works? I mean you were Kyle just this morning.
    Kylie giggles and looks at her, you and me are the only ones who know that Jennifer, for everyone else i have always been Kylie summers.
    Jennifer nods slowly not sure if she totally gets it, i guess that makes sense, well, i should probably go home then Kylie....it was...uh nice talking to you? I guess? I mean i don’t really get all this but whatever right? She is about to leave as Kylie giggles and pats her shoulder, not so fast Jennifer darling, i have something for you.
    Jennifer turns around and looks at her, oh? Kylie nods and walks over to her and looks at her, Transform or Dare? Jennifer's face turns pale and white, W-what? What did you say?! Kylie giggles oh dear did you think i had forgotten that little part? Don’t worry though; it’s the last time it can be said for the next hundred years so don’t even think about it turning it around.
    Jennifer starts stammering and looks at her, she is about to speak but Kylie puts her finger in front of her mouth and stops her from talking, D-Dare! Dare! Kylie giggles aww, too bad it took too long so you have to get both.
    Aww, what a shame isn’t it? Jennifer looks at Kylie angrily, you bitch! Kylie takes a step back and fakes feeling hurt, look, i didn’t get to where i am by being nice all the time so let’s start with your dare, Kylie looks at Jennifer and snaps her fingers i know it.
    Jennifer i dare you to become my own private maid, you’re going to love working for me and love your job, second for your transformation i want you to...well, you’re kinda young so that would be kinda awkward so i want you to turn into a 30 year old..Chinese woman and lets just call you...Meiying from now on.

    Two weeks later.

    Kylie sighs as she walks through the door to her mansion and throws of her high heels and walks over to the living room and sits down as she can hear footsteps, Ah Welcome home miss Summers, how was work ? Kylie turns around and looks over at Meiying wearing a typical maid uniform. It was a total mess, everyone is getting sick and....she looks up at the celling i think im falling in love again.
    Meiying smiles happily, oh that’s wonderful news miss summers! Who is it? Kylie looks up again and sighs, Cynthia flowers....Meiying giggles, a woman miss? That’s not surprising at all, but have you thought about asking her out? Kylie nods, yes i am just waiting for the right time.
