    Everyone would admit that the Super Bowl had been tight, especially since this was the first overtime game with new rules. But nobody really thought of that since both Animalia's new Cheerleading Squad and the Animalia Halftime Show were already going mega-viral on the internet, as the saying is known.

    However, the Super Bowl was only part of the fun that was starting in February.

    ---

    Of course, not all the attention is focused on the game in and of itself. Sometimes, more attention is given to the commercials that play during this time. And one commercial in particular was generating a lot of attention since it featured Svetlana and her new mate as Animalians. While the commercial in general focused on advertising the new Joyous Bounty, more people were focused on Svetlana's massive muscles and/or curves. This was generating a lot of comedy fuel for both meme makers and stand-up comedians.

    ---

    Another thing that was generating a lot of attention was the bachelor/bachelorette auction. Part of this was the simple fact that it would be taking place at the Meat Market, and it wouldn't just be females up on the stage. In fact, one video, locked safely behind the premium section of the Animalia Website, was of a new tiger Animalian pole dancing to, and singing, Tiësto's The Business, even more so when Miles did an interview with Joanne about who he was.

    "What?!" gasped Miles in surprise, as did everyone else.

    "I said he's one of the CEO's that converted recently." said Joanne, again. "His advertising company was one of those that began working with Animalia from the very start of its existence. He had considered retirement around 2006, long before Animalia, but the global financial crisis, and then the Covid-19 recession, forced him to stay on for longer, and his double dose means he's probably going to keep his position for quite a while."

    "How old is he anyway?" asked Miles, reading one of the most common questions from the viewers.

    "I know he doesn't look it," said Joanne, smiling foxily (pun semi-intended), "But he's actually had his 87th Birthday last winter." At that, there were many digital expressions of shock. "And, before you ask, yup, him dancing at the meat market is how he's dealing with his frat boy syndrome." she confirmed, using the unofficial name for male RHS.
