They lost track of what was going on around them and didn't notice that they were making a lot of noise, just pulling each other's attention away when they heard a scream.

    When they looked, they saw that they had hit someone who was already transforming. They could not see who it was as the stricken woman was shrinking and was already so small that it was completely hidden inside her clothes. They soon recognized from the clothes that they had hit Mrs. Gibson, the school's principal. They watched for a while as Ms. Gibson shrank so much that it was only possible to see her clothes lying on the floor, but bread seemed to have nothing underneath, which worried the two of them and made them lose the will to continue fighting.

    "Did you just see what happened? If you hadn't tried to take the TF Gun from me wouldn't this have happened?" Carolina said as she and Melissa rummaged through the clothes on the floor, mainly because they didn't see any movement coming from below. Doing this they found a gun inside the clothes, but this one looked exactly the same as the tf gun, but Carolina still had the one they were fighting over in hands.

    Melissa took this second gun and looked at all angles, then curious she took aim at herself and pulled the trigger. "Did you hear this?" she asked as soon as her mouth formed, and Carolina confirmed

    "What happened to me? Why do I get my body so hard and I can't move?" they heard Ms. Gibson's voice asking, coming from the tf gun in Melissa's hand.

    "Ms. Gibson?" Melissa asked and pointed the gun at her face.

    "You are... If I remember correctly your name is Melissa isn't it? Where are your trunks? By the way, now I remembered that I saw you fighting with your friend and you had a gun in your hands, so I ran to try to avoid a tragedy, but then I started to shrink and then I don't remember anything else. I must have passed out." Ms. Gibson said

    Carolina realized that while they were fighting, she and Melissa only thought about the gun and with that they had turned Ms. Gibson into a tf gun, and judging by her clothes and speech, reality had not changed and she was aware of the change, despite that she doesn't know it yet. She tried to fix it by shooting Mrs. Gibson, imagining her as she remembered and wanting her not to remember what happened, but it had no effect. She tried a few more times, but still nothing happened.

    "Won't you tell me what's going on? I can't move my neck to look at my body." Ms. Gibson said

    "We are very sorry Ms. Gibson. Do you see that gun in Carolina's hand? A shot from it doesn't hurt anyone, but the person hit becomes whatever the person shooting imagines. Turns out we shot the lady by accident and turned you into another gun just like that one." Melissa said

    "Stop, that's not possible... Oh my god! I'm a gun now." Ms. Gibson said and changed the line as Carolina took a compact mirror from her bag to let the school's principal see her own reflection, later finding it strange that she can still see, hear and speak in this form. "Since you've turned me into this then you can make me go back to normal, can't you?" she asked

    "No, I have to get back to normal ASAP." Ms. Gibson said

    "We'll try to find out who created this gun right away." Melissa said
