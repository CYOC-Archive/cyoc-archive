    Her first day as a babysitter

    Erica Smith was an 18-year-old girl who was about to start her first day of work as a babysitter. And on her first day, she had to take care of 3 girls: Riley, a 3-year-old toddler girl; Kylie, a 2-year-old toddler girl; and Audrey, a 1-year-old baby girl.

    And although Erica at first thought that this would be very easy to do, she soon realized that her job was not easy at all. This is because the toddlers that Erica had to take care of were very hyperactive and she always had to prevent them from getting into trouble. She even had to prevent Kylie from eating some small objects (among those objects were some crayons and a somewhat strange coin).

    Audrey luckily was the easiest to take care of them, although she did make a mess with her baby food. But easily the part that Erica didn't like at all was when she had to change Audrey and Kylie's diapers.

    After a few hours of playing, the three girls began to feel tired. So at that moment, Erica proceeded to take them to their room so they could finally sleep. While Riley slept in a toddler bed, Kylie and Audrey slept in separate cribs.

    After Erica put them in their respective beds, she proceeded to sit on a nearby rocking chair and proceeded to rest a bit after so much work. But while she rested, Erica proceeded to take out of her pocket the coin that she had previously taken from Kylie and began to look at it. And soon, without realizing it, she proceeded to flip the coin and muttered:

    "I wish babysitting was easier," Erica said as she flipped the coin.

    Which side did the coin land on?
