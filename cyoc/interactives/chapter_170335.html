    Ruth ran in to see Jamie hiding in the corner of the bathroom. She didn't show her face, but the hands were not that far from her face, as if something was attached to it. "Jamie? Jamie, are you okay? ...W-We saw the mirror, it's pretty silly. We're s-"

    Jamie jumped up, worried, but her appearance didn't show that worry. "Did you look like clowns?!"

    Ruth was speechless. A smile was affixed on Jamie's face, but her eyebrows show a different story. Fear. Pure fear. Ruth covered her mouth in shock, and backed away a couple steps. Jamie was still awaiting a response. Ruth nodded.

    "Ruth, the clown versions of us, the reflection is.... is gone...."
