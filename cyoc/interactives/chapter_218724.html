    Mary saw a small switch hidden between the vials. flipping it revealed a secret compartment. glancing at the labels she sensed a theme. each of them was linked to were-animals. picking one up she saw it was the typical werewolf.

    She then noticed it had the male symbol on it. picking up the next one she saw it said werewolf as well. this time however it showed the female symbol. 'guess it depends on which you pick' she thought as she put both of them back. somehow the box seemed endless with this section.

    she picked up a male were-elephant vial and read its label. "were-elephant vial: will change user into a male were-elephant. day form human. night form elephant. type anthro".

    She kept examining them. She found she didn't even have to stay human. reading the were-kangaroo vial she found it read differently. "were-kangaroo vial: will change user into a shemale kangaroo. day form shemale kangaroo. night form anthro shemale kangaroo. type anthro."

    She just kept finding more and more vials. " were-great Dane vial: will change user into a female great Dane. day form great Dane. night form human."

    she could even be an animal in the daytime. she soon found there was nothing she could not become. "were-griffin vial: will change user into a male were-griffin. day form human. night form griffin. type anthro". she could even be another mythological creature in addition to being a were.

    "were-daisy vial: will turn the user into a daisy. day form human. night form daisy. type feral." she saw that she could even become a plant. "were-car vial: will turn the user into a male were-car. day form human. night form car anthro. type male" even inanimate things were not of the table.

    she looked through the vials and sighed. a god vial was not anywhere. 'guess that makes sense. who would need these vials if you could just become it yourself' she thought as she examined the multi-colored vials. reading more of the note she saw that if she was dissatisfied just use a different vial.

    additionally the unhidden vials could be used to alter her new form if she chooses. she then picked up the vial. her curiosity was peaked. would this really work? without a second thought, she downed its entire contents. she didn't feel any different. 'guess it only begins at night'. once the nighttime rolled around she felt immense pleasure flooding through her body. She knew it was true as her body reacted to the moonlight.
