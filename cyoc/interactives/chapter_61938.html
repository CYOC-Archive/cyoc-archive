    Somewhere in a certain city, a new night club has opened up called "Pan's Palace".

    The serve nothing but fruit and veggies.

    Drinks are grape wine, and for the kids, grape juice.

    The dances are fun.

    There's only 1 catch to this place: anyone who comes in will slowly turn into a 3-year-old happy, musical satyr.

    If they leave before the transformation is complete, then they'll be back to normal in the morning, but once the transformation is done, they'll stay that way forever.

    Halfway through it, they'll be given the choice to go home and stop it, but most of them find they want to keep going, and if that's the case, then the process speeds up.

    Apperencewise, the goat fur they have matches the hair on their head.

    Their faces have freckles, and they're always happy.

    Boys have curly hair on their heads, and girls have long curly hair that goes past their tails in the back, and speads out in the front to cover were their assests were, or might've been.

    If they came as a boy-girl couple, then they'll always want to be with eachother, and they'll always want to hold eachother's hands.

    If one transforms all the way, they'll encourage their partner to do the same, saying how wounderful it is, and not many can say no to those cute faces!

    They're very strong, and can lift a table effortlessly with one hand.

    They're highly inteligent, and use their inteligence to paint beautiful paintings and create other flawless artwork - something a normal 3-year-old can't do.

    They're also fast runners, and can jump great distances.

    If the ones that come in are already 3 or younger, then it won't affect their age.
