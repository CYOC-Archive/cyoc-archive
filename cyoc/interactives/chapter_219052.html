Hm…, why don’t I use this to fix everything? Or…,” a grin appears on his face, “or I could have some fun with master… like some kind of payback? But… what should I do? I could transform him into a dog! I could make him think he is a dog! I could transform him into an other animal! I could transform him into some object! So many possibilities… but, I’d need to scan him first”.
