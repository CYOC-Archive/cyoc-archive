    "Let's freshen up first," Rachel said. "Oh, and get dressed, of course."

    "But why bother?" I laughed.

    "Because," she explained. "We should let ourselves be affected by the no clothes wish even though we're
    behind it. It'll be more fun. And..." she said as she got off the couch and gathered her pajamas, "we should
    have some people come over to see the effects of the wishes on others, too."

    I moved myself to a sitting position. "OK, but who should that be?" I asked.

    "Didn't you say you have siblings?" she asked.

    "Yea," I said. "A brother and a sister."

    "How old are they?" she asked.

    "They're both younger than me," I answered. "My brother Scott is 26 and my sister Pam is 22."

    "So I'm the same age as Scott," said Rachel. "And you're..."

    "29," I replied.

    "OK, how about you invite them over after we've made the wishes," she said. "I'll call one of my girlfriends.
    Someone you don't know."

    "So long as they agree," I said.

    We took our time getting ready. We had all the time in the world, since I wasn't due at work and Rachel
    didn't have to work today either. Once we were both fully dressed we sat down on the couch. With great care
    and detail we made the wishes we had discussed. We wished that every man on the planet would change into a
    woman. We wished for the change to be gradual, painless, and to be complete by night of the next day. We
    wished that everyone, man or woman, would know the change would take place, but that everyone would be
    accepting of it and not be concerned with why it happened. We wished that over the same time period, everyone
    would gradually shed their clothing. We wished that to happen in such a way that at random times over the
    next two days, a piece of clothing would make a person uncomfortable so they would just take it off and never
    put it on again. We also wished for it to be random as to what piece of clothing would do this, so as to
    create some differences in the process. We decided to add the fact that no one would really be aware that
    this change took place, but that everyone just did it without a second thought. We figured it will feel
    somewhat more natural that way. However, once everyone was naked, we wished that everyone would understand
    and accept this as being normal. We wished that everyone would stop physically aging once at their prime
    physical condition, and for everyone currently older than that to de-age until they were in such a condition.
    We wished that people would still recognize family, friends and acquaintances, even after the changes
    completed. We also made the necessary wishes for people to not be affected by weather and to be able to walk
    bare foot without suffering pain from anything that might cause it. Since we decided to not make people
    overtly aware of removing their clothing until it was over, we decided this change wouldn't be evident until
    that time either. And as a final wish for now, we wished that someone would find a way to make sure humanity
    could survive in a world of nothing but women.

    "One more wish you need to make," Rachel advised. "Make sure everything starts now."

    "Oh, yea," I agreed. "No since waiting around for this one. I wish that all of the wishes I just made will
    begin to take effect immediately."

    Rachel and I looked at each other and smiled. Then I saw the expression on her face change. Suddenly her
    eyes rolled back into her head and she passed out. Before I had a chance to figure out what was wrong, I felt
    an intense but unseen force hit my entire body. My eyes rolled back into my head and then there was sudden
    darkness.

    The next thing I knew I felt a hand on my cheek and heard a voice frantically calling my name. I managed to
    open my eyes as my senses slowly returned to me. My eyes focused on the image of Rachel. She slapped me
    lightly on the cheek and said, "Joe! Joe! Joe, wake up!" She looked very scared. "Oh, thank heavens," she
    said as she spied my eyes opening. "What happened?"

    I shook my head a few times. "I don't know," I admitted. "Something force hit me and...that was it."

    "That's what I felt, too," she said. "Was that the effect of our wishes?"

    "I guess so," I said. "Never thought that would happen." I glanced at my watch. About five minutes had
    passed since we made all of the wishes. "At least it didn't last very long."

    "So how will we know everything worked?" she asked.

    I grinned. "Hopefully this way," I said. I picked up the remote control and turned on the TV to one of the
    news channels. What we got was an emergency test pattern. I switched to a few more channels and they were
    much the same. "I guess everyone got hit by that force," I said. I went back to the original channel and we
    waited for a minute. The broadcast returned and everything appeared to be normal.

    Normal, that was, except for the news story that was reported. The news anchor reported that humanity was
    about to undergo a massive change. With succinct precision, all of the changes we wished for were reported by
    the news anchor. He reported that every male would change into a female by the end of the next day. He also
    reported that people would stop aging physically and that older people would de-age. He made no mention of
    clothing being shed, or of humanity then being resistant to weather. So far, everything worked perfectly.
    The news anchor added that no one knew how or why these changes were about to happen. He also said that those
    things didn't seem important anyway. He did say that the big question remained how would humanity survive
    with an all female population. So apparently that news was still to come.

    Rachel bounded off the couch and jumped up and down in celebration. "Yay, it worked! It worked!" she
    exclaimed.

    "Looks like," I said with a smile.

    "Do you mind if I get a little kinky?" she asked. I shook my head. "I really hope you end up with some big
    tits."

    I shot her an odd look. "Are you sure you're not only into women?" I asked.

    "Hmm," she said as she put a finger up to her chin. "Did we just have sex and did I not enjoy the hell out of
    it? You're right, that was someone else." She walked over to me and playfully slapped me on the cheek.
    "Naughty boy. Soon to be naughty girl. So come on, call your brother and sister. I'll call my friend."
