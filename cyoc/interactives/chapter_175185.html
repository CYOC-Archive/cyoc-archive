    It all began at the college laboratory of Dr. Diane Ilene during a High School field trip. The scientist there was giving a tour to the Sophomore science club of Retropolis High School. In the tour was Dr. Ilene's niece, Dawn Ilene. Dawn was the nerdiest girl in the high school, so nerdy that the only friends she has are the janitor, and the lunch lady of the school.

    All that changed the day of the field trip, when Dawn's aunt arrived at one of the inventions there. "And this is my new latest experiment," Dr. Ilene said, telling the class. "It's called the "Power Shower 4000", and it'll change the world. Whoever steps into it, will become different, but let's not play with it. Come on, let's go to our next part of the tour." As the rest of the class followed Dr. Ilene, her niece Dawn got distracted, and wrote notes on her notepad. Dawn then accidentally leaned against the shower device, and that caused it to turn on.

    Green water drops poured out of the sprinkler, covering Dawn in it. Dawn screamed in fear, which causes them to alert Dr. Ilene. "Stay here, everyone," she said, and rushed back to the lab. Dr. Ilene then noticed her niece in pain, as the shower was on. "OH MY GOD, DAWN," Dr. Ilene screamed, and turned off the shower. Dawn's glasses fell off her face, and she tries to get up. "Dawn, are you okay?" Dr. Ilene asked. "I'm not sure," Dawn answered while struggling to get up. Dawn then noticed that she can see normally without the glasses. "Say, I can normally again," Dawn said. "Let's see," Dr. Ilene said. "How many fingers am I holding up?"

    "Three," Dawn explained. "Good," Dr. Ilene said. "My machine cured your blindness. After school come with my lab. I want to do a couple of tests with you." Hours later, Dawn returned to the lab, where her aunt has her conduct tests of what the shower did to her. As it turns out the green liquid drops that fell on her were contained with toxic waste, which of course gave her superpowers. Dawn now had the ability to fly, the ability to change her size, super strength, and can shoot fire and ice from her hands. With these powers, Dawn decided to help people who can't help themselves, so she created a superhero identity. Which were a black, and white no leg leotard, with an Atomic symbol on the front, a red belt, red gloves, red boots, a black eye mask, and a red cape.

    With this new look, and her superpowers, Dawn became Atomic-Girl. The greatest super heroine in Retropolis. Of course, years passed, meaning she was now old enough to defend herself, and even changed her look, now that her eyesight was fixed, and she was now the most popular girl in College.
