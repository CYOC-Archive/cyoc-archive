    “I am becoming a…Lamia.” answered Haley as Kim blinked, she had never heard of a Lamia before.

    “A Naga.” answered Sharon as Kim eyes widened as she looked at Haley, the only thing she really remembered about Haley had gotten multiple state metals in track and field. She had been a star in the hurdles and is Haley was becoming a Lamia or a Naga whichever they wanted to call it, then goodbye legs and goodbye running the hurdles or any other track and field events.

    “In a way, Lamias are more human but still snake from the waist down.” confirmed Haley as she looked at her legs with tears in her eyes. “I woke up every morning at 4 am to train to have a shot at the Olympics, I am in the top 25 in the nation for my age and now I am losing my legs to get become sort of snake woman.”

    “Sorry.” Apologized Kim, not sure why she was apologizing to Haley. Haley seamed slightly insulted at the apology but decided to take it.

    “Not your fault I guess.” admitted Haley with the tone of someone giving her a great gift but then the walls broke a little bit. “I guess I should apologize to you, while I don’t believe I did anything to you personally I did take part in Lisa and her work.”

    “You where one of the better ones.” Admitted Kim before shaking her head. “Truth be told I should thank you one of my plans for revenge was steal Lisa’s boyfriend but if he is as big of a dog as you say then that will get me nowhere.”

    “If you want to go that way, get yourself an STD and give it to him and wait for him to give it to her, with your body
    now you should get him in bed with ease.” Explained Haley as Sharon shifted at the idea.

    “Not really an option, part of being a Nymph is that I am immune to all known STDs.” Added Kim “My other idea was to attack her with my powers once they come in, but I will not know that until I am done turning in two days.”

    “Wait…my turning is 10 days what day are you on?” inquired Haley "You just left school, you couldn't have been here 8 days already."

    “My first day, Nymphs change over 3 days with all physical changes after the 2nd day.” Explained Kim “The last day, my hair and eye color change and I get control over an element.”

    “Yes, this is her improvement after a single day, tomorrow she will be able to get any modeling job she would want.” Stated Sharon “You should see her mentors."

    “Lucky SOB.” stated Haley as she folded her arms over her chest again. “Here I am learning about my future where I will have to use special bathrooms, eat raw meat apparently as a snake person I will die if I stay a vegetarian and cooked meat is not good either. My Mentor recommended live food, mice and the like. I will be eating live mice for the love of God, I don’t want to imagine what it is like to eat a living creature. And Sex…well you don’t want to know.”

    “Not really.” Admitted Kim now feeling a lot better about becoming a Nymph, the more she saw the more she realized she had lucked out.

    “So a Catwoman, a Snake woman and a Nymph all teamed up to bring down a single person.” stated Sharon before she chuckled “I almost feel sorry for her.”

    “Don’t.” answered Kim and Haley almost as one, the two shared a look before Kim looked at her watch.
    “Want to get something to eat?” inquired Kim

    “Sure…why not.” Confirmed Haley “I don’t know anyone else besides those in my group and I don’t want to see them at the moment.”

    “I am hungry.” Confirmed Sharon as the three of them headed off to get some lunch.
