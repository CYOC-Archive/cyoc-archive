    Sylvia and Bella had gone into the mall and were amazed at the stores they saw inside. They didn't care much before for the malls, but now in their teenage forms, they got an overwhelming sense of excitement.

    "O.M.G. this is like totally gonna be the bomb of all dates!" Sylvia said cupping her mouth in embarrassment. Bella looked at her and giggled.

    "Sorry, I think it's these teenage hormones in this body I'm occupying." Sylvia apologized.

    "Oh, don't be sorry, you know every girl goes through that wild teenage phase." Bella said.

    "Yeah? Try being a witch and hiding your powers from friends when you want to show them some cool spells you learned." Sylvia said as they browsed looking at stores.

    "Really? Try to not have every boy and girl ogle over your body when you reach puberty." Bella said.

    "I'm not ogling, but...." Sylvia overlooked Bella's appearance at the moment and felt her heart skip a beat a little. "I think you're beautiful even not in your...natural form." Sylvia said reaching for Bella's hand and slightly touching it before Bella pulled it away fast.

    "Uh...look there's a store where we can try on some outfits." Bella said as they headed towards the store meant for teenage girls.

    Sylvia and Bella entered the store and tried on several outfits, showing them off to each other, and complimenting on each other's looks. At one point, Sylvia got up and entered the dressing room while Bella was in the middle of changing.

    "Hey! Ever hear of privacy?"

    "Relax, it's only me, besides I...wanted to try something." Sylvia said looking in the mirror at herself and Bella. Sylvia quietly cast a spell that showed the two of them as a teenage girl in a witch's outfit and Bella in her succubus form. Bella gasped and looked away from the mirror and looked at herself to see she was still human.

    "What's wrong? It's you."

    "Yeah, that's the problem, me. You know how I was saying earlier that it's hard for others to not be attracted to...someone like me? Well it's probably been the most difficult part of my life. Why do you think most of 'my people' end up...at the place you found me? It's because we don't really build relationships, people come, people go. That's just how it is for us. You'll go soon enough." Bella sighed.

    Syliva looked in the mirror at herself and saw the girl she was possessing, then for a brief moment, she saw her real teenage self.

    "I never told my ex about my witch heritage. He bailed on me, the moment he found out I was pregnant. I never really tried to love anyone again since I was raising a daughter. But I tell you, our past doesn't shape who we are, and it's not the people who leave, it's about the people who stay in your hearts when you're young, and when you're old." Sylvia said as she gently held Bella hand and made the mirror show their adult selves.

    "That's what you looked like? I mean, what you really looked like? You're beautiful." Bella complimented as Sylvia blushed.

    "So do you want to...?" Sylvia began to say before Bella kissed her on the lips and wrapped her arms around shoulders.

    "Sorry, what was that?" Bella apologized after letting go of the kiss.

    "Let's go see a movie. It'll be dark." Sylvia said as they put their regular clothes back on and left the changing room.
