    added A year ago BM I

    "Wilson... I like that name" The football turned man said with big smile "Yes, I'm Wilson!"
    "Nice to meet you Wilson" Said Jeff "Hope we can get along well, okay?"
    "Oh, I'm sure I would love my new football" Said Wilson, still smiling, same tone but Jeff caught the danger, maybe by the instinct.
    "What did you just said?" Jeff confused.
    "I said I would love YOU as my NEW FOOTBALL" Said Wilson, louder, still smiling.
    "Wait... No... Nooo!!!" But before Jeff can do anything, his body starting to change, Jeff have a brief moment to let him noticed Wilson just pressed the button on Chronova and then, a lot of huge pain feeling are overwhelming Jeff and his vision went black, he blacked out...

    Transformation process can be a bit too painful to bear, Wilson thought as the computer showed a message that Jeff has lost consciousness but that's should be only for a moment, Wilson made sure Jeff will still have all the consciousness and senses even in his newly transformed form, if Jeff able to watch his own transformation, it almost like when the football turned into Wilson but in reverse, it took only few seconds and Jeff is gone, only a brand new football laying on the floor, the text on the ball said [Jeff Chronova Version 4.0] Wilson picks the ball and examines it, than said "Wow, You turned into such a high quality football! Perfect!"

    At that moment, the computer showing a message, Jeff now woke up, he still feels the pain all over the body but then, Jeff mind filled with the confusion, what happened!? He can't move!!! W...Wilson? When did he becomes this big, bigger than before, wait... He said he would love with new football... No!!!

    "Jeff! Good to see you again! You know, the pain when your body transformed cause you to blacked out, I was worried but now you're okay! Enjoy your new form?" Wilson said with big smile as always but this time, his tone changed, sounds like a school bully when teasing its target "A fair exchange, don't you think? Now I can feel how it feel to be a human and you can feel how it feel to be football!" Then Wilson laughed.

    No! This can't be! Why did you do this to me! I turned you into a human and this is how you repay me!? Damn you!!!

    "Then you should care about your ball more than before, you didn't treat me well, some month, you didn't even touch me! But don't you worry! I'll not treat you the same, I'll play with you everyday, you should be happy!" Said Wilson and then he throwed Jeff into the bed and then Wilson jumped right into the bed to grab Jeff once again.

    Jeff feels like he is about to be crush when Wilson's big body landed on him! And then he been lift up to facing with Wilson's face again "Like the feeling? I still have more to do and you better get used to that"

    No!!! Someone, help me!!!

    And then, the door burst open as Jeff's mom walking in, look angrily at Wilson.

    Yes! Mom!!! Help me!!! This evil football turned man just turned me into his new football!!! Do something!!! Kicked him out!!!

    "Wilson, I told you not to play with your ball in the room, I know it is a brand new and all and you're happy but please go play outside!" Said Jeff's mom, Jeff went dumbfounded.

    What...?

    "Dang it! Sorry mom! I just getting too excited! I'll do just that soon!" Said Wilson, Jeff's mom nodded and walked out.

    What is this!?

    "Oh sorry!" Said Wilson "I didn't tell you yet, didn't I? You see, I didn't only used your Chronova to make you a football, I also been rewrites my backgrounds, I replaced myself in your place! To your mom, I'm her son, no Jeff, just Wilson, not just her, your dad, your brothers and sisters if you have, your relatives, your friends, everyone! So don't worry, no one going to miss you, they are now only remembers me not you Jeff!" And then, Wilson laughed again, this time longer than before.

    No... It can't be... Please... Change me back to normal... We can play together, I will not even angry at you for this, just... change me back...

    Wilson didn't looking at Jeff but he now looking at the poster on the wall, it is a poster for local football championship tournament, Jeff got from the gym teacher and thought it look cool so he sticks it on the wall but didn't think beyond that such as enter the tournament.

    "It said if you have a whole team, anyone can enter, this can be where any people who dreams to be famous can start here..." Wilson said, way most like he's talking to himself "Yes, this is where I will start my dream and the world will remember me as greatest football player!"

    Can't you just use Chronova to make you famous? Wait, that not the point! Turn me back, please!!!

    "Are you stupid? That cheating! Even someone like me have my own rules, you know? If I aimed to be greatest, I'll do it with my own hands!"

    I get it!!! But you can do it while I'm not a football!!! Please!!! Change me back!!!

    "Alright!" Wilson said to himself, ignored Jeff "I still have time before the tournament, I'll have to train myself a bit more and I need to find team too!"

    Jeff don't like this one bit!
