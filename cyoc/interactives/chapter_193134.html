    Baby in the Belly

    added A year ago BM TG O

    Jeff spent the month as a dairy cow nervous about how her pregnancy would affect her eventual transformation back to a human. For all she knew, she wouldn’t be able to turn properly human until after giving birth, or even at all! And she was definitely pregnant. The bull made sure of that, visiting and breeding her over and over again.

    Finally, her month was up. Jeff fell asleep in the barn one night and awoke the next morning in her apartment again. Or rather, his apartment. After a quick check, he breathed a sigh of relief, glad to be back to being a man. Being himself had never been so great.

    The bovine pregnancy scare was enough to turn Jeff off the idea of using the Chronivac for a while. For a few months, he went about his regular day-to-day life. He even stepped up how frequently he went to the gym, because he found himself gaining weight unexpectedly. When exercise and dieting didn’t work to shed pounds, Jeff decided to turn to the Chronivac again. Surely there was a way he could use it to lose weight.

    However, when Jeff began to mess with the Chronivac and set it to alter him, he stumbled across a list of his statistics. Curious, he scanned it, wondering if he could adjust his weight. Halfway down the list, something caught his eye.

    “SEX: MALE”
    “GENDER: MALE”
    “NOTE: PLACENTA CONTAINING ONE (1) MALE FETUS CURRENTLY ATTACHED TO ABDOMEN”

    Jeff was horrified. It explained the weight gain and how awful he’d been feeling, but how was this possible? He was pregnant without being female. Without even having a uterus! Hurriedly, he tried to edit this, but an error message popped up on the screen.

    “UNABLE TO REMOVE PREGNANCY. SUBJECT CAN ONLY BE ALTERED IN WAYS THAT WILL NOT HARM THE FETUS.”

    Great. Just great. He was pregnant and couldn’t do anything about it! Or could he? Jeff re-read the error message. It looked like he could still change himself, but whatever change he did couldn’t affect his pregnancy.

    Luckily, that left him with plenty of wiggle room.
