You aren't sure how to take this new development, you want to leave even if you have to be a doll, Just to get away from this house which tormented you, but you also know the chance of that is probably non existent. For an instant you are cut off from your senses once again finding yourself in the room... As how it looked when you first got here. You are propped on the one of the Study tables near a map of the world witha book placed open in front of you to make it look like you are reading. The House still taunting you by having the same book that you were enjoying when the house decided to stop waiting. It is still blank except for the chapter title on the page that it is open to. It seems to that the house has placed doll sized reading glasses on you much to your irritation. Finally you hear the door to the study open and the footsteps approaching you, finally the person comes into your sight. It's a girl of maybe twelve or ten, dressed in a right orange dress and jacket. Her eyes a very Vivid Blue, and relatively short hair wearing a large gatzby cap. You Watch her go through the study examining some of the books and the few other dolls in the room. Muttering something each time she picks one up and shaking her head, she makes her way over to you removing the book in front of you before picking you up. The first warm touch in however long feels incredibly nice you simply enjoy it as you couldn't do anything else. Though to your surprise the girl smiles at you and says.
    "Finally found one."
    which confuses you as you know that you don't look very different from any of the other dolls. Maybe... This girl knows that you are different somehow...
