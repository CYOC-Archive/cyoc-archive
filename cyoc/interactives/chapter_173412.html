    Yuriko grunted in pain as Casseus last attack knocked her into a tree. Despite the teens impressive psychic abilities, she was far too injured to mount much of an offensive. Still she was hurting Casseus, the enhanced wizards eyes had blown all their blood vessels, while his nose was bleeding. "Get out of my head," he bellowed as another blast of magical energy rocketed at Yuriko.

    Yuriko grunted, erecting a barrier at the last second to protect herself. Still she was thrown back. "Please," she thought. "Please, if anyone is out there I need help."

Turning he could see a five tail kitsune spring from the tree tops. Casseus chuckled at Akiras clumsy attack. "Amatuer," he smiled as he released a devastating blast at Akira.

The blind seven tail kitsune had been pursuing Akira through the forest. As Akira now lept from his hiding place Isao had sent forth several blazing swords, hoping to end Akiras life once and for all.
