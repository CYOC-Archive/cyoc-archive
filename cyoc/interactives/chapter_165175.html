    Animalia Ambassadoria: Animalia Comic Convention Begins...

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by makutamon 3 years ago O

    "Hello everyone," said Sarah, starting the latest Episode of Animalia Ambassadoria, "and welcome to a special edition episode of Animalia Ambassadoria."

    "We're beginning our official coverage of Animalia's first dedicated Comic Book Convention." said Malice, "As well as where to find certain events at said convention."

    ---

    "While Animalia is unique for having two different kinds of costume contests," said Ashley Jenkins, "At Animalia's comic book convention, this is of particular importance because the more 'grown-up' version is being held at the Meat Market, while the other, more family-friendly one is at Kat's Klub."

    ---

    "One thing that is rather inevitable, especially if you're going to have a convention celebrating videogames," said Jen-Jen, bringing her camera over to a large number of videogame console stations, "is PvP videogame tournaments starring well-known videogame streamers. But as a reminder to always be a good sport as well as respectful toward others, here was something from the initial qualifying round of one well known game that's a good example of karma."

    The example was when one of said gamers gave the L-sign to his camera, much to the surprise of the commentators who were covering said game. But not too long after he gave the sign, he was killed by another gamer which made him end up looking kind of silly.

    ---

    "Thanks to the success of movies and TV shows like Lord of the Rings and Game of Thrones, interest in archery and the medieval days is at an all time high." said Courtney, who was at a pseudo-real 'archery tournament', bringing up a few clips. "But there are occasionally cases where a million-to-one chance is achieved, and the reaction is well-deserved, if confusing to those who aren't familiar with such reactions."

    One clip showed Honoka participating in archery, and achieving the near impossible feat of shooting an arrow through another arrow. Almost immediately everyone began screaming things like "No Way!", much to her surprise and confusion before it was explained to her.

    A similar thing happened during a demonstration of a torsion catapult, when one of the rocks used in the demo struck one of the foam torsos used in the simulation, specifically one used to represent guards on the rampart, in the head.

    ---

    "And that's all the time we have for this episode." said Sarah, after some more coverage of the beginning of the convention, "But the coverage is not over quite yet. Be sure to check on the website for different coverages of different things."

    "We'll also be having a special celebration of St. John's Eve, with a special performance of the Night on Bald Mountain. But that part is only available to those who have premium accounts on the website." Said Malice.

    "But for now, see you all next time in the wilds of Animalia." said Sarah, finishing the episode.
