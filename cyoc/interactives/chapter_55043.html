    Marie didn't answer right away.

    "Anyway, I came to see if you needed any help," Katherine told her.

    "Why?" Marie asked back.

    "Because that's what friends do," Katherine answered, "they're with each other through thick and thin. For richer or poorer, in sickness and in health..."

    "You're confusing friendship with marriage vows," Marie answered, "and we're BOTH female, and even if we weren't... we've both taken vows of chastity to serve the church."

    "The concept is the same," Katherine answered, "true friends are always there for one another. No matter what.     "Thank you for the advice, but I don't need any help," Marie answered.

    And with that Marie began walking again.

    "Come on, Marie," Katherine actually sniffled, "You need a friend."
