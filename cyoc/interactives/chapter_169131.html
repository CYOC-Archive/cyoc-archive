    The first stop for the two is the baby department to get supplies for the daycare. They look around for a short time before Tina gets frustrated.

    "Oh darn, they don't have any of the usual brands here. I can't get anything if I don't know whether it'll be good or not which usually means I need a little one to try it out. But I can't hold off on getting new supplies."

    Leah jokes, "I'm the right size for this stuff. I could try it out."

    The joke seems to go over Tina's head, "Really? Are you sure?"

    Leah sighs internally she shouldn't have made that joke, but now it would be rude to say no, "Yeah it'll be fine."

    That permission is all Tina seemed to need, as she immediately sets about using Leah as her test subject. Leah goes wide eyed at the first thing she's expected to test when Tina grabs trial size packs of diapers and changing supplies.

    "Sorry I need to know how these diaper fit around the legs and I need you to be in a diaper to try on clothes."

    Leah concedes that makes sense and volunteers to go change herself, but Tina points out Leah has no experience changing diapers. So the teen let's Tina lead her to the bathroom and strip her of her down while she tries a few powders and creams on her. With some feedback from Leah, Tina settles on the ones she likes and moves on to the diapers.

    After checking that the fit is good, Leah is led back to the baby section in only a diaper. There she models a few outfits before being left in a just a shirt leaving her diaper on full display. Tina then takes wipes meant to clean marker and paint of children and tests them on Leah's makeup.

    Next Leah's professionally styled hair is brushed out. Tina frowns for a second before pulling out a pair of scissors.

    "Tina what are you doing?"

    "Sorry if I'm going to try out these hair ties and ribbons properly you have to have your hair cut like a little kid."

    Leah is about to tell her no, but already the scissors are doing their work. When the woman is finished, Leah's hair is bound in a few colorful braids. By this point, Leah's just about run out of patience, but Tina's not paying much attention as she straps Leah into a couple strollers.

    Once she's decided on one, she leaves Leah buckled in and starts pushing it. Leah tries to get Tina to listen to her, but the woman simply hands her a box of toddler crackers.

    "Sorry just a few more things. Thank you for being so helpful."

    Leah grumbles but she is hungry, so she starts eating. She expects to be back in her old clothes in a minute, but in a twist of fate...
