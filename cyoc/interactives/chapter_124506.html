    Triple Threat and Double Trouble

    The local indoor arena was having a championship game that night, so it was obvious to everyone that the former wrestlers would be there to try to take revenge and take their championship belt. After parking the three cars--and picking up some costumes and wrestling gear--the two fathers and thirteen teens got to the arena and went inside.

    Posing as new wrestlers, they were able to get backstage. They kept their eyes open for the trio, but made sure that the trio would not be able to recognize them. When they were alone, and sure that nobody was around, the groups put on their costumes and makeup, after using their new power to merge into seven groups: Ethan and Scott, Mark and Max and Zack, Kevin and Dean, Leo and Raph, Donnie and Mikey, Brian and Nick, Luke and Allan.

    After a little more sneaking, the groups made it to the main arena. As they watched, the final event was underway: the final challenger against the champion. But, just as the bell rang, a group of seats was thrown at the ring--thankfully, there was nobody in the seats. All eyes turned in the direction of the throw, and saw a large figure running down and jumping into the ring; Luke and Max recognized them instantly from Luke's basement.

    "Hey, sports fans!" the blue head shouted. "I'm back!"

    Apparently they were referring to themselves as one being.

    "And we wanna put the hurt on someone!" the red head grinned.

    "Hur-ur-urt!" giggled the yellow head.

    The announcer explained the wrestlers' backstory, one that the gang had already heard, as the challenger and champion stepped back towards the ropes.

    "You've heard that two heads are better than one?" the blue head said to the audience.

    "Well, THREE heads are gonna snap your spines!" the red head shouted to the wrestlers.

    With that, the round began. The three-headed wrestler(s) took on both wrestlers at once, and of course it was not long before the two wrestlers were out--cold and in the stands.

    "I'm the greatest wrestler in the world!" the blue head boasted as the trio flexed their muscles.

    At this, the group saw an opportunity and rushed down to the ring.

    "Eh, they're good," Luke said.

    "But we've all seen better," Max added.

    The three heads turned to look at the group--fortunately, they did not recognize anyone in the group.

    "You've got something to say, punks?" the red head snapped. "Say it in here!"

    With that, the announcer declared a new championship for the belt: a competition of multi-headed wrestlers. However, due to it being an elimination competition, that meant the groups would have to face each other.

    The following line-up resulted: Ethan and Scott vs. Kevin and Dean, Leo and Raph vs. Donnie and Mikey, Luke and Allan vs. Mark, Max, and Zack, and Brian and Nick vs. Triple Threat.
