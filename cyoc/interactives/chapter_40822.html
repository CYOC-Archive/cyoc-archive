    Brad decided that while Jim was upstairs getting changed, he'd have a little fun with him.
    Looking through the settings for Jim's profile, he saw a whole area relating to his
    occupation. The program currently showed 'Delivery (Pizza)' as Jim's job but there were
    loads of others to choose from and he scrolled through the list to see what else he could
    have Jim do for a living.

    As his eyes scanned the list of jobs, he decided to try the next item down the list 'Delivery
    (UPS)' to see what would happen.

    Brad clicked on the box next to the job title and checked the box for 'adjust reality'. No
    sooner had he made the selection than he felt a subtle shift in the room and noticed that on
    the floor where a pile of laundry stood, there was now the distinctive brown of a UPS
    uniform among the jumble of clothes.

    Brad was astonished to realise that although he knew Jim had previously been a Pizza boy,
    he could distinctly remember him, dressed in his UPS uniform, turning up at his door with a
    package and that the pizza delivery had been made by someone completely different earlier
    on that evening.

    "This is incredible!" Brad said to himself as he continued to browse through the list of job
    wondering what to pick next. "I wonder what he'd be like as...".

    Hearing Jim coming down the stairs, Brad made another quick selection and hit save. As he
    entered the door, Jim, still wearing his brown UPS shirt and pants morphed subtly into...
