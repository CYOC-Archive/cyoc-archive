    Transformations For Two

    added by Wolfy 13 years ago A BM S


    Jeremy looked at Mike smiling, enjoying the sound that came from his long time friend who was now so much more. Jeremy knew that Mike was his friend, his companion and his pack brother. Jeremy knew that he could not ask for much more than this since he had it all within his finger-tips. Jeremy breathed in deeply through his nostrils, savoring the smell of the air and feeling he had from the light of the moon. Watching Mike intently, Jeremy could see all of Mike's guards and inhibitions being dropped slowly as the change was overtaking Mike's person.

    "So my brother, do you feel that? Do you feel the power the moon is bestowing upon us?"

    Hesitant, Mike didn't know what to say exactly. "Jeremy... I... I do, but... I... I don't know what to think."

    "Then don't think, just enjoy it because if you are feeling anything remotely to what I am, you will love what is coming!"

    Feeling a wave of energy sweeping over the both of them, Mike's mind changed abruptly. No more did he feel the bonds of humanity, nor the cares humanity inherently has attached to it. He felt clean, pure and most of all... powerful. Mike threw his head back and howled once more, howling to show his freedom. Jeremy chuckled, knowing that Mike was truly like him now. Jeremy joined in with Mike's next full howl, making a chorus of delight. They howled again together, but this time things were different.

    What neither Jeremy nor Mike noticed, Mike's body had become massive in muscles and body hair, looking about thirty-five or so. They were both changed now, completely inside and what was on the outside was just about to change as well.

    As the moon reached its peak intensity the two felt euphoric, howling like mad and enjoying every bit of it. With each howl, more of their bodies changed. First, their eyes had changed to a deep emerald green, fading into a pale green near their pupils. Next, their faces began pushing out into a snout, forcing their nostrils to flare and flatten into a more lupine form while their ears grew longer while coming to a point. Then their muscles bulked and flared again, gaining a bit more mass to them. Then their hands started shifting into paws: their fingers becoming gnarled; their fingernails lengthening to form claws; thick pads of skin formed on their palms for added cushion for when they went down on all fours for better speed and control. Then their feet shifted into hind paws, bursting through their once perfect sneakers, with their toes and claws sticking out of them. Then fur, massive amounts of thick, cherry brown fur blossomed all over their bodies, covering them completely from head to toe. With one final howl, they ripped their confining human clothes from their bodies, while their tails popped from their tailbone. The two looked at each other, naked and in their new forms, admiring one another while enjoying what they both had now.

    The two did not speak, fore they did not have to, as they could hear one another's thoughts within their minds. Both had new feelings and desires, but which one would they tend to first?
