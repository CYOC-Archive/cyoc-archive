    Monica Eliza Burnside,daughter of Jeremiah Burnside ( US Army Engineering Corp)
    had been born in Jan 1 1844 and thus had barely turned
    18 when the off worlders came to Washington DC

    America was in crisis after the Confederates had fired on
    Fort Sumnter months earlier
    signalling the beginning of the War.

    Nobody knew when the War would end,not even Monica with her
    witchy powers.

    When handsome slave owning Prince Damon
    had bought a Southern Plantation earlier
    it had conflicted Monica
    as she loathed slavery.

    However those Princely eyes of Damon's
    had melted the young Northern
    witches heart.

    The problem was there was a War on now
    which meant that
    Prince Damon was now
    technically the "Enemy"

    However since
    Monica' Burnsides dad was friends with the recently
    elected President Abraham Lincoln
    she often went to the White House to ask for advice
    from Honest Abe.as some called him.

    So this fine day Monica took Prince Damon
    (as a toad) to the railway station at
    Sutter Gulch
    and walked up to thee ticket booth
    to purchase a ticket
    one-way to
    the Nations capitol.

    The clerk behind the wicket cage looked at her
    suspiciously
    as he knew witches had a habit
    of turning their
    friends and family into toads
    so that they could all ride the
    expensive steam trains with only one passenger
    having to pay train fare.

    So the wise for his ways
    ticket clerk asked Monica to open
    up her carpet bag to prove there were no toads hiding in there.

    There weren't.

    The bottle was there but it was empty.

    Where had Prince Damon gotten off to?

    Well Monica wasn't going to get the chance to answer
    that question because
    the sound of the steam whistle announced
    the arrival of the

    4:15 from Sutters Gulch to Washington Track 6

    So Monica scooped up the bottle,picked up that carpetbag and dashed up onto the
    iron bridge that spanned the 10 track station.

    She ran along to
    the stairs for

    Track 4
    and soon was on the gravel that sat beside the tracks that the 4 15 was
    huffing and steaming on.

    The Conductor checked her ticket then led her to the inside of the passenger railway car,where
    Monica was seated opposite
    four mangy looking men wearing cap n ball Navy's.

    "All Aboard Last Call" shouted the Conductor before closing the
    doors and the 4 15 pulled away from the Sutters Gulch station

    a worried Monica wondering where
    Prince Damon was.

    Where toad Prince Damon had been was clinging to Monica's hair,hidden from sight but
    as soon as they had both entered the railway car\he had let go,fallen to the floor and hopped
    off to some private corner where
    he used the spell Monica
    had taught him before hand to turn back into
    handsome Prince Damon,mysterious Count from
    Translvannia.

    He got up,fully clothed in that
    coat,cloak and black trousers popular in
    Transylvannia
    picked up his "dogshead" cane
    ( the one with the secret weapon in it_
    and headed back to Monica.

    Monica was glad to see him again
    but those 4 mangy men wearing guns sized
    up Prince Damon with their eyes.

    "You one o dem undead fellows?"
    asked one

    obviously thinking Damon must be a vampire or possibly a werewolf
    but the Prince Damon
    assured them that
    neither would he turn into a
    big hairy beast under the Full Moon
    nor turn into a bat and suck blood

    And so the 4 15 got to the Nations capitol and both Moica Burnside and Prince Damon
    Damon dodged around what seemed endless
    parades of blue uniformed soldiers
    and buckboard wagons bringing in supplies
    to the War Munitions Department,

    The two went straight to the secret back door of the
    White House and
    Present Abraham Lincoln
    top hat and all
    let them in and eagerly
    shook hands
    with Prince Damon
    of whom he had heard so much

    Prince Damon,had after all,just freed all his slaves a month earlier
    and was a sensation in the
    newspapers for that early
    bit of emancipation.

    However Honest Abe wasn't here to be member of the Prince Damon fan club
    but to give him
    and Monica a secret mission of
    importance to the World.

    What do you do now?
