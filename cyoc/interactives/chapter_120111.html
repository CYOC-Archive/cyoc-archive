He quickly tossed the rest of his ragged clothes on the kitchen counter and hurried to throw away the dirty rag in another room out of sight. Following a quick knock, Valter let himself in and shut the door.

He was used to being taller than Raiijin, and he was quite faimiliar with his body as they often went to the gym together. Staring up at his friend now, however, he felt rather small. He glossed over the part where he had a massive orgasm just about where Valter now stood, but the bulge in his sweats may have hinted at that. It had not gone unnoticed, either. Valter may have been staring a little.

    "So anyway," Raijin concluded, "I need to go buy some new clothes as you can tell. Did you bring those shoes?"

    Raijin slipped on the pair of size 11 flip flops. He decided to wear socks with them to hopefully help hide the fact that his toes stuck out over the ends. With that done, they headed to the mall. They both cracked up a bit at how Raijin looked crammed into the driver's seat of his sedan. The first stop they made was to the shoe store where Raijin decided to splurge on a pair of size 14 basketball shoes that had a tiger stripe pattern on them. They cost a lot, but it seemed hard to pass them up with his new look. As they went around shopping at other stores, Raijin noticed he was getting a lot of extra attention. He tried to play it all off as lots of workouts, contacts, and an awesome hairstylist when he got the occasional "How'd you get like that?" Eventually, with some financial help from Valter, they hit up enough big and tall outlets, discount racks, and sports apparel stores to put together a small but decent wardrobe. Afterwards, they returned to Raijin's apartment.

    Before Valter left, Raijin put on some of his new athletic gear and led the way up to the building's roof. He wanted to try something, and he wanted Valter to be there in case something went wrong. He stretched some then ran a couple of sprints along the rooftop to warm up. Then he took a deep breath and took off at a dead run toward the edge of the building. As he got to the edge, he made a powerful leap and landed solidly several feet over onto the roof of the next building. Valter was in awe of his friend's agility as he performed a couple more freerunning tricks then leapt back to his own rooftop.

    "That was amazing, Rai!" Valter said as they returned to the apartment. "I can't believe how far you jumped!"

    "I always thought it would be fun to try, but I never felt like I was strong enough until now. Thanks for your help today! I'll pay you back as soon as I can."

    "No problem. I have to get ready for class, but if you need anything, call me."

    With that, Valter left, and Raijin wondered what he should do with the rest of the day.
