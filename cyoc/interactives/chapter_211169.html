    After the class had finished cleaning up the shop, the students started to spill out of the garage, Tyler and Greg are walking together, “hey, let's go grab some lunch and chill a little before the next class starts,” Tyler suggests and Greg agrees.

    Unaware to Greg, Evan, who is a tech-nerd and IT-wiz, stays behind and when everyone had left he walks up to Mr. Osborne. “Sir,” he asks, Mr. Osborne looks up at the teen, “I was wondering: would it be okay if I stay here?”

    Mr. Osborne raises an eyebrow, “why?”

    “I have no classes after lunch,” the 16-year-old says, “I have at least two hours free. So I thought I could use that time to check the Jeeps IT-system and see if everything runs smoothly. I also have written a security and computer program, which I'd love to upload into the Jeep.”

    “Shouldn't you ask Greg,” the teacher asks, “it is his Jeep.”

    “I normally would,” Evan says, “but, it is Greg's birthday tomorrow. I want to surprise him with upgrading his IT-system in his car.”

    Mr. Osborne thinks for a while, “that actually is a neat idea,” he says, “okay... you may do as you please. Greg left the keys in the key box by the Garage door, help yourself,” and with that the teacher simply leaves.

    Smiling, Evan walks over to the key box and soon finds the keys to the “Jeep”, he unlocks the car and sits in the driver's seat. He turns on the power, which causes Jeff to awaken, “hu? Why is this guy sitting in me,” he asks himself.

    Evan is a athletic boy, his body is slender but still muscular, he has short brown hair and a brown skin tone, he has green eyes and some freckles across his nose. Evan turns on the Jeep's on board computer and takes out his tablet.

    'What is he planning,' Jeff asks himself, a part of him says he should speak up and tell Evan that he is not really a Jeep, but instead he decides to just wait and see. He can speak up any time if he feels Evan is going to far.

    Evan turns on the blue tooth of the Jeep and his Tablet and syncs both devices, “now, let's see what we have,” the teen says to himself.

    For Jeff the syncing feels weird, he feels as if someone is tickling his brain with a feather, this causes Jeff to mentally giggle and also somehow relaxed. 'Hmmm... actually,' he thinks to himself, 'this kinda feels good. I don't mind at all what he is doing, I feel so relaxed, it feels like a soft massage.' What Jeff is unaware of, the reason he feels so relaxed is because Evan is downloading all of the Jeeps data, in other words: the IT-wiz is not only downloading the data that ALL normal Jeeps have, but also his human-part of data. What will Evan do, when he notices the “extra” data? Jeff continues to feel himself relax more and more, 'feels as if I am being put in a trance,' he thinks to himself, 'feels... so... *yawn*... goooood.' The teen-turned-jeep feels himself become really tired, he tries to fight the urge of falling asleep, but the feeling becomes stronger, 'he... it w-won't hurt... to... *yawn*... sleep for a while,' he thinks to himself, 'I'll be... f-fine.'
