It influences the dice in that way, either through indirect means or creating an intoxicating effect that the user can't get enough of."

    "Is that why we couldn't stop?"

Your stuck having to grow up all over again."

    Alice simply nodded, thankful she had come to her senses before that happened. What would've become of them if they were forced to be born again. As she was about to ask another question, she watched Lorelai rolled once more.

    Thirty years you have walked this Earth. Now this number is but Twenty four.

Lorelai's olive skin was flawless, her body slimming while as her stomach flattened while her hips flared and breasts rose. The now young gypsy lifting her floor length dress slightly.     "So why roll them?"

    "Because the kids look like they're having so much fun," Lorelai grinned, pointing to the excited 9 year olds. "And it's been quote sometime since I was that young." Alice gasped, trying to talk Lorelai out of rolling herself into childhood. Unfortunately, Lorelai seems determined, Alice watching as the young woman rolled once more.

    Twenty four years you have walked this Earth. Now this number is but Sixteen.

Looking over herself the olive skinned girl glanced at her tiny feet, shaking off her large sandals before running a hand through her thick ebony spiral curls. "Ah much better," she chirped, her voice high and childish. "Now if you'll excuse me, I'm going to have some fun before everything gets fixed!"
