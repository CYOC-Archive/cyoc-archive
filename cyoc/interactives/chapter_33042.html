    "Are you sure about this?"
    Mary nodded and Jess downed the first vial. Almost instantly her bunny body began to shift around, and her fur changed from white into a deep red/brown colour. She too was now a fox-girl, and admired her bushy tail. Looking at the second vial, she stared at the lable 'GROWTH'. The reaction was fast though, as Jess only just managed to squeeze through the door before she was too big to get back it. A full minute later, Jess covered most of Mary's back yard.
    "I can't believe how huge you can get from such a small amount. Imagine if I took it all!"

    Jess laughed, but not in a good way. Mary could sense that the size was getting to Jess. She grabbed one and slipped outside again, hoping Jess would feel differently if her plan went through.
