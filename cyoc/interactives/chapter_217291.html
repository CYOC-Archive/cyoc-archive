    Toddler Tyler

    So, Tyler just stood there as he watched the wheel spin around and around, slowing down with every pass, until it stopped with the big, red arrow pointing to the text which read "Toddler". At this sight, everyone in the public laughed, delighted at the fun they were about to see. Madame Illusia even more so. Not only she could teach that youth full of hot air and bravado, but regressing a subject into a young age meant that possibilities opened up greatly. She could make this youth into something a lot better, which the world could use, instead of a bully that annoyed and mocked everyone else. Oh, she would humiliate him thoroughly too in the process. Transforming mean people was so much fun! She could let herself be somewhat cruel to give horrible people their just deserves. And his thorough humiliation would give this little bully a taste of his own medicine.
    Tyler was engulfed into a dense cloud of smoke. When it finally cleared, he was surprised at how large the world had suddenly become. As if it had suddenly become populated by giants. And he was naked. He just had his boxers which... No. Not his boxers. A diaper! He was wearing a diaper! He was sitting on his butt, clad in only a diaper! From what he could see, he was barely one or two years old, and his muscles had utterly vanished. He had baby fat and a tummy! Ugh! And he could just move clumsily, not even get on his two legs! He uttered baby talk, which prompted all of the audience to cheer and coo at him. He tried to insult those awful jerks but his baby gurgles only prompted more laughter and applause from the public towards the talented magician.
    "How cuter he looks, don't you think?" Madame Illusia asked.
    All agreed and she continued.
    "Should he stay that way or should he be changed back to the rude youth he was? You decide!"
    Of course, all wanted Tyler to remain a toddler of barely more than a year, much to his terror. What was going to happen to him now? What had that awful witch in mind for him?
    "Very well! But now Tyler needs new parents, right?" Madame Illusia said with a mischievous gleam in her eyes.
    Tyler wanted to just cry.
