    "Well I'm kinda in the mood for a drink" you say to the family who agree.
    "Do you think they have something to eat?" James asks.
    "I'm sure they do"
    To get to the shack you have to go inside the main building where everything is and walk about halfway around the dome. Normally it would have been a long walk but a worker directs you to a few moving sidewalks. The shack is a pretty normal looking jungle-themed looking place, made of bamboo from floor to roof. As you enter the shop the first thing that hits you is the aroma, the sickly sweet and yet sour smell hurts your sinuses until you are seated. As the waitress directs you toward the table you take a look at the inside of the dome. It's massive, when you entered it looked like you were on the same level as the dome but actually somewhere along the line you must have risen higher. Through layers of trees you can see things like mountain resembling structures, waterfalls, and forested areas so dense that you can't see through them.
    "It was a good idea to come here" your wife Emily says gazing out the window.
    "I agree" you say as you put an arm around her.
    You look at the kids. James is busying himself with the kid's menu's coloring page and Lily is immersed in the brochure.
    "I've never seen the kids so relaxed here" You say.
    The waitress arrives with all of the menus.
    "That's funny" Emily says. "The drinks aren't labeled by what kind they are only by color"
    "Wonder what's in a white extract." Lily ponders.
    "I think the red one sounds good." Says James.
    "Well I think I'll have a daily special."
    The waitress comes back and takes the orders. What did you order?
