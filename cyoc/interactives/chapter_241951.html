    Madame Illusia smirked. Now this skank fool had annoyed her extremely. She'd get her revenge at once. That idiot deserved a good punishment and the witch would be delighted in being the one to offer it to her. She possibly COULD understand that young brainless idiot was angry - and she had revelled in the audience's mocking of her - but her attitude was just unforgivable. Sure, Madame Illusia had simply felt a smack and no mark would ever appear, but that stupid ditz had made her look like a show to watch and this she could not simply let go. Something had to follow and she had a good idea on how to do just that. She could think about the perfect revenge. She would provide her audience with a good show too.
    "Feeling wild, now, don't you?" she smirked at her foe. "Your basic female instincts are reacting, right? Defending your male? Shame you have to do it in such a degrading manner."
    Monica was more than seething at this point.
    "Who do you think you are?!!?" she screamed. "Give me my boyfriend back! You're such a stupid bitch!"
    "I will just give you a good punishment," retorted Madame Illusia. "Since you like being wild, let's see you as a wild animal! Then all will see you for who you really are! And you can then give in to your base impulses too."
    The witch blasted her spell at Monica and everyone waited with bated breath, impatient to see what Monica would be turned into. Madame Illusia smiled wildly. Soon the carnival or a zoo would have a new addition.
