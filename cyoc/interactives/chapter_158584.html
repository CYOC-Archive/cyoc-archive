    Welcome to Dragon Land

    added by Brightest Midnight 3 years ago A BM

    Dragon Land was a new amusement park that had sprung up seemingly overnight. The park had been built by a mysterious benefactor, Samuel Kain who had hired some of the best park designers in the world. The park had seemingly been created by magic without anyone knowing about it, and perhaps there was some truth to that.

    Samuel Kain is actually a powerful silver dragon named Raimus. He had built the park as part of his part of the plan, weaving powerful magic into the park itself. The Silver Dragon was seated in the Dragon's Den, a large facility at the center of the park that had many stores and restaurants. He was at the top of the building, watching parts of the park on screens.

    There were some interesting parts of the park that could cause different transformations for the guests. Raimus had set the park up as the perfect way to transform his victims, letting the magic do its thing without drawing attention to himself. The truth was that he had grown more lonely lately and his ultimate goal here was to find a mate. With all the humans that would be coming, he was sure that it would be easy enough to find one.

    He looked at the map, everything was up and running. He looked over every area of the map one by one. Each one would be perfect to attract guests.

    Main Thoroughfare: The main entrance of the park, the Main Thoroughfare was where the guests would arrive and lead into the Dragon's Den. It had several shops as well as some smaller rides for visitors to enjoy. The carousel was the main draw there

    Sea Serpent Bay: The water park section of the park, it had several water slides as well as the Sea Dragon, a large water coaster that snaked around the area. It also had the standard wave pool and lazy river, both of which would distract guests from what was happening to them until there was no turning back.

    Camelot: An area of the park modeled on Medieval England, Camelot was the largest part of the park and was dominated by the Firebreather, a large steel roller coaster themed on a red dragon. It had more thrill rides dotting it as well as the occasional smaller rollercoaster, it was where the bravest of guests would come to play.

    Hatchling Area: The area for the human children to play, it had several kiddie rides, but its magic was more intwined into the play area. It was powerful enough that the adults could even be de-aged, hatchlings would be much easier to have accept their new lives after all.

    Futureland: A more futuristic part of the park, it had many sci-fi themes but mixed them with dragons. It was to reflect the future they were building, and those who were turned here would become full dragons always unlike in other places. It was dominated by the largest roller coaster in the park, the Bahumat's Glory, where anyone who got on would stand no chance of coming back human.

    He smiled a little as his tongue tasted the air. Humans were such fools, it had been simple enough to build the park and get a staff, and now he would bring the world to his doorstep.

    "Sir, the park is about to open," a voice said from behind him and he turned to see a green female Dragonoid standing there. She was one of the many staff members he had hired, and they had no idea that their contracts had sealed their fates as Dragonoids. Now they served him and everyone outside thought they were in very realistic costumes.

    "Very good, Nama," he said as he turned into his human form. "Let's go welcome our guests then."

    He walked out onto the balcony and looked down the Main thoroughfare where everyone was. He picked up a microphone and began speaking into it. "Welcome everyone, as you know I am Samuel Kain and I want to welcome you to my park. This park was born from a dream and a love of Dragons, so let us celebrate these fine creatures once more. Come, enjoy your time in my park, I don't want to see any frowns, the park is now... open!"

    With that mechanical dragons that had been placed around the park breathed fire into the air. The guests cheered as they started to make their way around the park.

    Raimus returned to where he was and returned to his true form as he flicked his tail lightly. It was only a matter of time before there was nothing here but Dragons and Dragonoids, and the rest of the world would follow.

    So, who will you follow?
