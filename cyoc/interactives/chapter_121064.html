    I was about to attack him from behind, but I got hit. I collapsed to the floor. My sight was slowly fading as I saw Mr. B walk away with a ton of money. Right before I passed out, I initiated the emergency button every superhero has on their suit.

    Something happened between that period of time while I was passed out. I woke up back at the HQ I worked in with my team. I looked normal at first, but I remembered something. Mr. B hit me with one of his attacks. Something was wrong because I looked the same age.

    I went to the training room and activated it. I had to work on my reaction time, so I turned on the lasers. In order to have a fast reaction without using super speed, I had to work extra hard.

    About 50 turrets appeared and started to shoot lasers rapidly. I strafe left and right. As I attempted to do a backflip, but my arm got attacked with 3 lasers. I collapsed on the floor again and activated my shield. The lasers stopped and the turrets went back into the wall.

    "Try doing something other than showing off next time." someone said outside of the room. I looked over to see Chris. His super power is speed, and I secretly got mine from him.

    "I heard what happened with Mr. B. Must be hard on you for losing again." he said.

    "Don't even mention it, so how was that haunted forest job?" I asked as I slowly got up.

    "It went amazing. There was someone who had some abilities that he used to scare people away. Apparently he was hiding some huge diamond mine." he said holding out a small diamond nugget.

    I walked over to get a closer look at the diamond, but I collapsed again and instantly passed out.


    I woke up in the med bay, with a view of myself in bed with a screen of red dots scrambling around. It had to be more than a million on the screen.

    "Oh my god the effects are happening too soon!" Chris said as he rushed over to the screen with the red circles. He started to press keys on the keyboard lightning fast.

    He then pulled out a mirror and gave it to me. My face looked completely different, as if I was looking at an old photo.

    "Well, it seems your skin cells have reverted to when you were..."
