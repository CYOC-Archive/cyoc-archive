    Father's Day in Animalia and its colonies was, in several ways, similar to Mother's Day, including spa packages exclusively for fathers. In other ways, it was completely different.

    One of the biggest ways that Father's Day was different was the Bachelor Auction, which was often confusing to those who weren't familiar with it. Especially since most male Animalians during the auctions tended to wear male leather harnesses and "matching" leather speedos. For most though, this didn't really matter much due to all the money they were raising for charities.

    Some may argue that the best way to celebrate Father's Day is by becoming one, but that's tricky at best. However, that didn't mean that there was some boasting on the Animalia Website, as Abby's parents showed.
