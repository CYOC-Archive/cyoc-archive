    "Mayumi" And Raúl End Up Having More Time

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy A month ago O

    “Mayumi” awakens from her phone giving of a notification tone, the “girl” stretches and yawns, she then looks at her body, “I see I have returned to normal,” she says, she then looks over at Raúl and smiles, “and I see my boyfriend has also returned to normal.”

    The “girl” then takes her phone, “Jane sent a message,” she mumbles, “I wonder what she wants,” she opens the message and starts to read:

    “Hey sis,” Jane’s message starts, “I found your note, thanks for letting me know. I wanted to inform you, I’ll be heading home, so take your time and enjoy the beach. I’ll see you later.”

    “Mayumi” grins, she sends a message back, “okay, sis,” she writes, “I’ll be staying a little longer. I’ll meet you back at our house, tell Carolina that I really enjoyed myself and give her my thanks.”

    Her phone pings and a new message appears, “will do, see you later,” is Jane’s reply.

    “Mayumi” puts her phone down beside her and turns to face Raúl, who is still sleeping, the “girl” lovingly strokes the boy’s head, causing him to smile as he slowly opens his eyes, “hey beautiful,” he says.

    “Hey Tiger,” the “girl” replies, “I have just received a message from Jane, she is heading home, so I can stay a little longer on the beach.”

    Raúl smiles, “sweet,” he says, they then hear Raúl’s phone chime, “looks like my sis is texting, give me a second.”

    The boy opens and reads the message, he then types into his phone and lies it back next to him, “my sis wanted to know where I am,” he explains, “I told her that I am at a friends house. She knows that this means I won’t be home until some time in the evening,” he adds with a wink.

    “Mayumi” giggles, “great, that means we can spend more time together!”

    Raúl nods, “what would you think of us transforming into Merpeople and explore the Oceans?”

    “That would be an adventure,” the “girl” says, “and I know you’ll keep me safe, we’d just need to watch out for sharks. Maybe we’ll find some lost treasures?”

    Raúl chuckles at the “girl’s” excitement, “someone seems excited,” he says, “and yes: I’ll keep you safe and protect you!”

    “Mayumi” smiles, put then she gets a thoughtful expression on her face, “what about our stuff?”

    “Oh, don’t worry,” Raúl says, “I’ll use the App to make them invisible or disguise them as some rocks until we return.”

    “Okay, sounds like a plan,” the “girl” says.
