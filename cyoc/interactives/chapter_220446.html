    "Well, I'll take these two,” Anat, horsewoman of war, announced as she approached the twins. “Don't worry, I won't be so cruel to tear you two apart. I can easily take care of both of you.” Anat’s words did nothing to lessen the twins' fear, the horsewoman letting out a sigh. “Lilith, you did inform them what was about to happen, right?”
    “Sorry, but we wanted to be back as soon as possible so we could be done before dinner.”

    The sigh turned to a groan. “Wish Di was here, she's much better at this,” she muttered under her breath. Anat bent down to be on eye-level with the twins, or rather tried, she still towered a full head over them. “Look girls, eh… Mia and Nicole was it, right?” She didn't get a response. “I can understand that you probably just went through some very tough times. Monsters invading your home, being saved by a demon who claims to be your friend. I was once like you, well not exactly, but…a normal girl like you some ages ago. I caught the attention of my predecessor and he made me who you see now.

    “I want you to know that you don't have to be afraid. You're currently in one of the safest places in all realms. Nobody here intends to harm you, unless you try something stupid. Princess Carmilla not only saved you, she gave you the chance to become something far greater. We demons aren't really as bad as the first impression might suggest. I got the body of a badass amazon warrior,” –of course she flexed at that– “the job pays well and I got a loving wife, who’d probably squee at how adorable you are… her words, not mine. And just so you know, if you decline I’ll just have to find someone else to become my successor and you two, best case, you’ll become the princess’ two-headed pet hydra, worst case, back to earth. So, what do ya say, wanna become my daughters?”

    The twins still looked apprehensive, but then Nicole spoke up, head held low, “When they attacked… I could do nothing… I just took Mia’s hand… and ran away…”

    Mia started to slowly come out from behind her sister, tears already running down her face, “Mommy, Daddy, Benny, David… I just stood there and w-watched as they k-killed them… if… if Nica didn't drag me away…”

    Then both said at once, “Can you protect my sister?”

    Anat grinned, “I can do better and ensure that neither of you would ever be apart.”

    Anat put one hand on each of their shoulders and both girls felt her flaming aura flowing into her. Nicole accepted it without issue while Mia still had some reservations and looked in the direction of Carmilla. The princess gave her a smile and Mia saw in the moment the girl she had a crush on for years, letting the energy freely flow through her body. Anat smiled while she slowly pushed the twins into each other, their bodies melding together while their clothes burned away. Within moments, a two-headed blonde stood where in place of Mia and Nicole. The conjoined twins' body combined Nicole's athletic physique and well-endowned thighs with Mia's D-cup breasts, giving them the appearance of an Amazon as they are taller in stature. Nicole's hair also grew out to the same length as Mia's. The physical changes also came with a sense of euphoria as the twins kneeled in front of Anat. “Thank you for giving us this great gift, mother,” both heads said in unison.

    Anat approached her two-headed daughter, flinging a cloak over the twins' merged body. “Well done, you two. Now I still have some business here, so you should have enough time to show the princess how grateful you are,” she punctuated with a dirty grin. “After that we’ll see how many windows will survive Diana seeing you two. And by the way, if anyone asks I found you two while you were defending yourselves single-handedly against a squadron of demons, gotta keep my image.” Then she turned to the other horsemen. “And if anything that happened here ever leaves this room I’ll introduce that demon’s business end to the business end of a spear!” Deonna watched as Mia and Nicole walked steadily over to Carmilla and embraced the princess, Mia kissing Carmilla on the cheek lovingly.
