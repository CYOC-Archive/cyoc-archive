    "Alright, I've got an idea." Alice pulled her bag up onto the desk -
    knocking down the keyboard as she did so, and apologising - and pulled
    out a small plush toy. It was a Dewott, both Aaron and Alice's
    favourite Pokemon. "Seeing as you didn't get me a real gift for our
    anniversary," She began, causing Aaron to become slightly nervous,
    "I'm going to have to take my revenge." She did an evil, but fake
    laugh.

    "Well, okay..."

    Alice propped the Dewott plushie up on the desk, and picked up the
    keyboard from the floor after removing her bag. "Don't worry, nothing
    will go wrong at all!" She began to enter in different options and
    parameters into the Chronivac, all the while preventing Aaron from
    seeing what she was doing.

    Before he could protest, Alice had slammed down on the Enter key, and
    he felt himself unable to move. At first, everything was black, but
    his eyesight came back to him afteer only a few seconds. As it cleared
    even further, he realised he was looking directly at Alice, who seemed
    a lot bigger than usual.

    "How do you like it?" She asked, her voice somewhat muffled.

    Aaron attempted to reply with a confused "Like what?" but stopped
    short when he realised his mouth wasn't moving. In fact, his mouth
    felt as if it had been stitched up, and the insides were nothing but
    fluff.

    Much to his dismay, he found he couldn't move, still. Try as he might,
    his body would not respond at all, and that was when he became
    nervous.

    "I'm going to take the extended silence as a 'no'. See, all I did was
    place you inside the Dewott plushie. Pretty neat, huh?"

    "I'm a freaking plushie?!" Aaron tried to scream, but his mouth was
    stitched shut. He really began to panic now. The fact that he wasn't
    moving or speaking at all was scaring him half to death, let alone the
    fact that there was a device capable of actually transferring his soul
    into a plush toy.

    "I didn't really think this far, though," Alice thought aloud. "What
    am I going to do with you like that? And just how long should you stay
    in there? Decisions..."

    This was not looking good! Alice absolutely loved plush toys, and
    never seemed to give up any, even the ones she had amassed when she
    was a kid. This was of course, not good news for Aaron. He could very
    well be trapped inside the body of a Dewott plushie for a very long
    time...
