    Recycling the stuffed toys

    You pick up a few of the stuffed toys, wondering what on earth Jack would be doing with so many. There are bears, dogs, dragons; toys of all shapes and sizes. Most are covered with dust.
    As you squeeze one of the toys, you notice how soft and comfortable the material is. Your eyes wander to the sewing machine; it would be a shame to let this stuff go to waste. Obviously Jack won't mind; he's way too old for teddy bears.

    You set about ripping open the toys, removing the fluff and straightening the shapes, until you have a huge amount of soft, strong cotton-like material to use, as well as a huge pile of fluff.

    You measure up the material; there is enough here for an entire full-body suit.
    You decide to recycle some of it by making new clothes, and start measuring yourself against the material. It is so soft and comforting against your skin; you get an odd idea. Why not recycle it into one huge teddy costume? It sounds sort of weird...but also kind of fun. You can always say it's your Halloween costume.
    You begin sewing, quickly shaping the material and decorating it. You make a set of two boots in the shape of a bear's paws, and give them huge, soft squishy pads on the soles. You add plastic claws on each of the three toes, and also make a pair of gloves, huge and round, with three stubby fingers and a thumb. You make a huge, hollow round chest, the front soft and furry, and a small nub tail.
    Then, using the harder, tougher material, you make the head, a huge round teddy bear, with big round ears and a smiling muzzle, and huge glass eyes.

    You sigh, patting your hands at a job well done. The costume is laid out in from of you; a huge, man-sized multicolored teddy bear. It looks adorable; but it is flat and deflated. You are sorely tempted to try it on.

    Well, it can't hurt, you think. You'll just take it off before you go downstairs. You turn the costume around, and push your foot through the slit in the back. But it gets caught; you made the costume exactly to your measurements, and with all these clothes on, it won't fit!

    You bite your bottom lip. Getting naked and climbing into a teddy costume in your friend's attic is sort of weird. But you really want to try it on. Quickly, you strip down, tossing aside your shoes, shirt, jeans and, with some hesitation, underwear.
    Then you push your bare foot into the slit in the costume, pulling the soft fabric around you. It is even more comfortable than you imagined! Quickly, you put in your other leg, and pull up the costume over you, dropping the head onto your own. It is momentarily dark; you keep having to move the head around to see out of the glass eyes.

    The suit is warm, and comfortable, but it still doesn't feel right. The head is too big and it feels hollow. You eye the pile of stuffing hungrily. Well, if you're going to do this, may as well go all the way.

    You start grabbing handfuls of the stuffing, and pushing them through the slit in the back of the costume. First you begin packing it around your feet, pushing it in tightly until your legs are huge, squishy and cuddly. Next, with only a slight feeling of guilty pleasure, you begin stuffing it around your crotch. The feeling of the soft, cushiony fluff around you is embarrassingly arousing.
    Next is the belly. You pack in as much of the fluff as you can, giving you a big, round, huggable teddy belly, which you pat proudly. Once you pack out the head, you no longer need to keep adjusting it so often. Finally, you do the arms and hands, leaving just enough room that you can move the paws.

    It's fun, but silly. You pad around the room as a big, squishy bear for a while, but it quickly becomes rather too warm, and you are probably enjoying yourself too much. Good job you have the big slit in the back to take it off; you turn around, meaning to slip out, but you accidentally stand on a toy car on the floor and slip, falling backwards.
    You hit something hard and sharp, and hear your sewing machine begin clacking and whirring. The suit pulls around you, feeling tight and constricting, and you feel the extra fluff being forced around your hands, making it very hard to move them. You eventually manage to pull away from the machine, and try to wriggle out of the costume; but you can't! You're sealed in!
