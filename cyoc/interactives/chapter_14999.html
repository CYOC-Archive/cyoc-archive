    Jessica thought about letting her mother have a weekend without kids and on what she
    should be. She then smiled as she thought of her mother’s Barbie collection.
    She remembered daydreaming about being one of her mother’s dolls when younger
    and giggled as she said “I want to be one of your Barbie dolls then.” Right before her
    mother could say anything, she added “Not just any Barbie, but the most expensive Barbie
    that you know of so I could be the best doll in your collection.”
    Jessica’s mother gave a knowing smile to her daughter as she said “I know the perfect
    doll then. I had been wanting to add an ‘AMERICAN GIRL BARBIE’ to my collection
    but the quality of doll I have been looking for would cost me almost eight hundred
    dollars. I can use this program to change you into one of them and then you will be the
    perfect centerpiece for my doll collection.”
    Jessica smiled back as she threw her arms wide and said “I’m all yours then.”
    Jessica’s mother quickly turned to the computer and pulled up Jessica’s profile.
    She then opened the 'Transform To' dialog box and typed in “Never Removed From Box
    1965 AMERICAN GIRL BARBIE Doll.”
    After pressing enter, she turned and watched her amazed daughter quickly shrink as
    she morphed into said Barbie doll still in it’s box.
    She then picked the box up and gave it a quick kiss as she admired the mint doll within.
    She then mumbled “This is so perfect", as she almost skipped into her bedroom and set
    the doll box inside her display case that housed her doll collection. She admired the
    new addition for a few minutes before she typed out a marking label to lay in front of
    her new addition to her collection.
    *****************************
    AMERICAN GIRL BARBIE
    DATE OF ISSUE    :  1965
    PRICE:  NRFB, up to $800
    ****************************
    She then smiled at her handiwork as she relocked the display case. She then sighed
    as she whispered “I now have a comfortable new bra and a new doll for my collection
    until Monday. I guess I should take a few pictures of my new Barbie to show it off to
    my internet friends and to prove that I really did have one.” She then grinned as she
    exclaimed to herself “But first, I’m going to return my age to twenty one again and
    go out and party like I used to before having children. Who knows, I just might find
    somebody to replace John.” She softly continued “After all, he has been deceased
    for almost five years and everyone at the office keep telling me to stop moping and
    start living again.” As she returned to the Chronivac, she mentally added “I think I’ll
    also modernize myself as well as my wardrobe.”
