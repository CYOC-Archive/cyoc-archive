    Bryan came to a halt at the edge of a busy sidewalk. His vision was
    filled with pairs of massive shoes as people walked to and fro. There
    wasn't any way he was going to willingly go out there among all those
    giants stomping back and forth on the pavement.

    But, a nervous glance over his shoulder showed him the rat still
    skulking in the shadows of the alley. The huge rodent looked agitated,
    as if it was deciding whether or not to continue its pursuit of him.

    Suddenly, something smashed down onto the pavement only inches from
    him. Bryan jumped back in fright. He needed a second to recognize the
    object. It was a cell phone, but the device was bigger than his body.

    Suddenly, a huge hand reached for the phone. Of course! Bryan realized
    his stupid mistake. Someone had dropped the phone. He started to run,
    but he didn't get far.

    Five giant fingers curled around his body and lifted him. Despite his
    terror, Bryan managed to focus on his captor.

    The man, looking to be in his mid twenties, wore a dark business suit
    and dress leather shoes. He had stooped over to retrieve his cell
    phone, and remained kneeling after he snatched the inconceivably small
    teenager. He had dark hair and brown eyes. His eyes stared at the
    struggling teenager in his hand.

    Other people were passing by the man as he continued to kneel on the
    sidewalk. He straightened to his full height. He slipped his cell
    phone into a pocket in his suit jacket. The hand holding his tiny
    prisoner he slid into another pocket.

    Convinced no one had seen anything out of the ordinary, the man
    quickly left the alley's entrance.
