Shi answered the phone and smiled. "Hi baby."

    "No, why?" Shi said softly.

    "Go to dad. Cause I want you both to hear."

    "Okay, hold on hun." Sally walked into the house and sat beside Charlie, who, of all things to not grow out of, it was hir football. Shi reached over, noting it was a new kind with kats in it, but not needing to watch it now, turned off the tv.

    "Hey, Sally, I was watchin..."

    "Shush. Luna has somethin' to tell us." Shi put hir phone on speaker. "Go ahead dear."

    "Mom... Dad..." Shi said, they could tell that shi was nervous. "I'm pregnant."

    Sally looked down. "What?"

    Charlie in equal shock stood up and paced back and forth. "My mate and daughter are both pregnant at the same time?" Shi then looked. "Well, hun, we're proud of you. Once your mom gets over the shock, shi'll be able to tell you too." Shi smiled. "Who's the father?"

    "That's the other thing. You see, we've been keeping our mating a secret from you and mom because of what you'd think." Shi said.

    Sally looks. "Who?"

    "Lia." Shi replied.

    Charlie looked. "Why would it matter what we think, dear?" Shi said walking over taking the phone and turning it off speaker. "I don't care who the father is, even if it's joe schmoo down the road, me and your mother will love you until the end of time and beyond." Charlie smiled.

    "Thank you dad. I love y.... I got to go. A bunch of kats are trying to get... I'll call you once we're safe."

    At that the line went dead. Charlie looked. "Wow." Shi walked back over and hugged Sally. "Which reminds me, should we tell Janice and Pinkie they'll be getting another sibling as well as a niece?"

    Sally shook her head then nodded. "Yeah. Let's go get them."

    Charlie and Sally had gathered Janice and Pinkie together, Snowflake decided to come with, seems Janice and Snowflake have taken a liking to each other, which made both mom and dad smiled. "So love had no age limits, eh, Charlie?"

    "Nope." Charlie replied. Shi and Sally sat down and looked at them. "We've got to tell you something. Okay, and I want each of you to understand clearly."

    "What is it?" Janice and Pinkie spoke up at the same time.

    "Now your mom is pregnant." Charlie spoke up. "But it isn't mine, but I want you to listen carefully. I want you to treat hir like you do Luna and Midnight, okay. No different. And speaking of Luna, shi's going to be coming home pregnant too. So no rough housing with hir like you use too. Okay?"

    Janice smiled. "No, we won't. We love our family, no matter what." Shi walked up and gave Sally a hug, followed by Pinkie and even Snowflake hugged hir.

    Charlie smiled hugging hir mate.
