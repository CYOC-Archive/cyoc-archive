    Lilith Fruits - The Rat Creature

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Raven Cross A month ago A I Anthro Mental

    Not all effected by Lilith Fruits is good. Some are pretty terrible. Like this one. An unconscious 3 & half foot rat creature was dropped in a cement room by 2 men. They then left the room & closed the metal door which locked with a click. In the room besides the rat creature was a mattress, a metal bowl with water in it. And a purple & black about 1 & half tall anthomorphic plushie fox in the left corner of the room sitting on the floor up against the wall. After about 3 hours the rat creature finally woke up. The rat creature looked around & he then began to whimper quietly in sadness (well best a rat can actually). The plushie fox started to stir in confusion from of the noise & slowly looked up & at the rat creature. The purple & black plushie fox hadn't seen or really heard any living thing in nearly 14 years. It was tossed in this room about 14 years ago. Sense plushies (also known as stuffed animals) don't need to eat or drink or anything being an inanimate object. So of course no one ever checked on this plushie fox forgetting all about it. Actually after all this time no one really is aware of it now. After a time the plushie fox just went into an inactive state; in a sense just shutting down completely. The rat creature's whimpering reawoke the plushie fox. Weeks passed & 2 people would came to the rat creature cell & give the creature more or less dogfood about twice a day. Or tranquilize the rat creature to clean the cell. None of these people really spoke a word to the rat creature. Over this time the plushie fox watched & started teaching itself how to move it's limbs again. One day the rat creature noticed the plushie fox standing & trying to walk. Even in the rat creature's mostly primal rat brain it realized the truth about the plushie. The rat creature walked over to the plushie fox & got onto his hindquarters & reached out. The plushie fox stumbled into the rat's awaiting front legs that doubled as arms & the rat hugged the plushie. The rat creature with much difficulty got out "Fruit....hurt... you...too..." The plushie fox nodded. It indeed had. "Can...talk?" the rat creature asked the plushie fox curious. The plushie fox shook its head no. That was the only time the plushie fox ever heard the rat creature speak. The rat creature now had a very limited vocabulary. His little rat brain struggled immensely with human language. The rat creature communicated more in rap vocalizations & body language. So the plushie fox ended up being the rat creature's constant companion. Something the rat creature could cuddle while sleeping; or when distressed. The plushie fox felt really sorry for the rat creature. The plushie fox realized that the rat creature was mentally hurting. The rat creature had been through hell so far. It started when while at a festival a stranger used his Lilith Fruit power to turn the kid into the rat creature he is now. The rat creature's mother ended up chaining the rat creature up in the basement. Then eventually called this faculty to take the rat creature. This faculty is a place to put Lilith Fruit victims that can't be in normal society anymore (or at least believed that). It's really a prison for them. So the rat creature woke up hearing the door click. He looked around & didn't see the plushie fox. The rat creature picked up the faint scent of the plushie fox on the human on the other side of the door. The rat creature quickly became inraged because in his mind this human took the plushie fox away from him. That another thing had been taken away from him. So the rat creature did what any inraged animal usually does. The rat creature charged out of the cell, jumped the human & began clawing & biting the human. The rat creature quickly killed the human. The rat creature then picked up the familiar scent. He looked over & saw the plushie fox standing staring with hand/paws over it's snout in shocked horror. The rat creature realizing what he'd done became horrified by what he'd done. The rat creature tried screaming no no no but all that came out we're horrified screeches. The rat creature then looked around for a place to ran & a ventilation duct up near the ceiling. The rat creature climbed up the wall using his claws & ripped a hole in the ventilation duct with his teeth & squeezed his body into the duck system. The rat creature started running through the ventilation duct system looking for a way out of the building. Soon a few armed humans ran up on the scene. They had been alerted by the security guy in he security room because it was all caught on security camera.
    [Why did you kill him? He was bringing me back after my washing.] The purple anthromorphic purple & black plushie fox thought in confusion. The humans got on their radios reporting in. The plushie fox knew that the humans were going to kill the rat creature for this; that's what you do to an animal that kills a human. That brought the anthromorphic purple and black plushie fox get sorrow.
