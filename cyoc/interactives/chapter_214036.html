    “You should go first, Jeff,” Ken says.

    Jeff raises an eyebrow, “why me,” he asks, “shouldn't I be the first to transform you? It's MY Chronivac afterall.”

    “Because, I feel like you can't wait to be transformed,” Ken says, grinning, “I know it is your Chronivac, that is why I think you should get to experience the most fun part, which is being transformed, first.”

    Jeff thinks for a while, “makes sense,” he says, “okay, I'll go first.”

    Ken grins, “sweet,” he says, “okay, stand in the middle of the room so that I can scan you.”

    Jeff moves into position, Ken then activates the scanner, “don't move, Jeff,” he says.

    Jeff nods, he even holds his breath, suddenly a red light shoots out from the Laptop's built in camera and hits Jeff. The teen manages to stay put and the light slowly moves down his body and back up, then it disappears and a “ping” is heard coming from the Laptop.

    Ken looks and smiles, “the scan was a success,” he says.

    “Man,” Jeff says, “that was kinda scary.”

    Ken chuckles, “yeah, it looked like a laser,” he says, “now, let me get your transformation set. And Jeff... NO peaking!”

    “Yeah, yeah,” Jeff says.

    Ken does not waste any time as he starts to type away on the Laptop, as the minutes tick by, Jeff feels himself becoming more and more anxious.

    'I hope I won't regret this,' Jeff thinks to himself.

    After, what felt like an hour to Jeff, Ken turns around, a big smile on his face, “all set,” he says, “I just need to confirm it. Are you ready, or are you chickening out,” he asks with a grin.

    “Just... do it already,” Jeff says, scowling.

    Ken grins, “you'll like it,” he says, and clicks the confirm button.

    Jeff suddenly feels a pins and needles sensation run through his whole body, and he starts to feel weird, “I wonder what you have chosen,” he says, “to be honest, I am a little scared.”

    Ken smiles, “don't worry, Jeff,” he says, “I promise you'll enjoy it,” he adds. The teen grins inwardly, 'I know I WILL,' he thinks to himself.
