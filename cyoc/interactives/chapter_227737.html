    Abigail's New Mommy. Sort Of...

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover A year ago AR O Magic Kid Reality alteration

    Abigail looked around her new surroundings. She was in a room but it wasn't hers. It wasn't even the room of her childhood. It was completely different. A four-poster bed that looked humongous, a white dresser, toys put away in various places and upon many shelves, soft toys and figurines galore and many books as well. And everything was in pastel shades. A lot of pink, though. Abigail got rather confused. What did all this have to do with her wish. She rose up and noticed she was dressed in a pink dress reminiscent of that of a ballerina, just adapted to everyday wear. Her new body also caught her attention.Her shapes and curves were gone! She was a little kid! As she reached a full-length mirror with a height gauge, she got the confirmation that indeed her body was that a little kid, four at best. She was back to being four! But... Her hair was dark and her eyes deep blue. Her skin was also a lot paler than before. This wasn't she looked at at four! Her very body was different.
    "Saanp, why am I like this?" she muttered, appalled. "This is not what I asked for!"
    "You did," said the disembodied voice of the deity. "You just gave no indication as to what family you meant. You have a new one, so a new appearance. But do not worry, you shall still remain their favorite, rest assured."
    Abigail started crying but Saanp's voice did not burst forth out of nothingness again. It was gone for good, leaving her to her new existence. It would be much more pleasant to her. However, her cries did attract her new parent. One of them, anyway.
    "Peach Blossom! What's the matter? Are you hurt?" a voice erupted from the open door.
    Abigail started. She thought this would be her new mommy and the frilly pink apron might have given that idea but... It was a big horned beast with a deep voice that spoke. A creature that should exist only in fairy tales. That being said, Abigail, now Peach Blossom, was in the Fay Realm, so it made sense. She thought the beast was going to cook her -which would have been quite fitting with this ridiculous apron - but it snatch her and nuzzled her, asking her where her booboo was. This was beyond bizarre, even though it did seem indeed that Saanp had respected her wish.
