    Jamie sat down at the computer, her dress and petticoats rustling as she did it. "Go out to him," she said as she adjusted her skirt. Alex and Miranda nodded and waddled out to the garage, triping once or twice on the way there. When they came through the door, they squeezed through with comical effort into the garage. Tucker did a double take when he saw them but then quickly chuckled at their appearance. "Sorry. Jamie told me you’d both be in costume but I guess I forgot. You two are the best looking Auguste clowns I’ve ever seen.” Since his family owned a novelty costume shop, it wasn’t uncommon for party clowns to come around for supplies for their shows. "Aw shucks Tucker, youre too kind," Miranda said giggled and squeezed her nose.

    "Well now that I can see you're both ready for tomorrow, Let me show you how this prop works." Tucker said that as he guestured to the cabinet. "its called the cabinet of doom, and you guys can use it to do a disapearing act with yoursleves or the kids tomorrow." The two of the waddled over, and Tucker showed them how thee was the hidden space in the cabinet that could be opened by turning a know in the back clockwise and then opened by doing the same action in the reverse direction. "I see, thanks Tucker, that'll be perfect for tomorrow Alex said as he backed avay from Tucker, his blue fringe brushing against Tucker shoulder briefly,

    "Oh boy, Juggling balls!" Miranda squeaked with glee as she picked up a few colored rubber balls about the size of lacrosse balls and began to juggle them for Alex and Tucker. Tucker was impressed, it looked so natural for her, and he knew Miranda had never played a sport in her life. After a minute or so she stopped, "Ta Da!" She said as she did a little curtsy holding a ball in each hand. 'Wait, she was juggling three balls' Tucker thought quickly as he watched the last ball sail downwards and bonk Alex on his pate with a comical sounding bounce. It rolled forward and then Alex proceeded to step forward and then slip on it flying forwards and landing on his rump which made a whoopie cushion sound. Tucker was laughing pretty well at this great display of clown comedy, it seemed so natural for them and he could wait to see what they woudl do with a real performance.

    "You two really are clowns, aren't ya?"
    "Yup, we sure are." The two of them said nodding vigorously after Alex stood up and dusted himself off.
    "More than you realize Tuck-Tuck” said another voice behind him
    Tucker looked and saw a female whiteface clown step into the garage. He stared at her for several seconds. "Jamie? You're a clown too?" Jamie nodded and smiled with glee and excitement. "Why didn't you tell me?"
    "I joined the act just a few minutes ago," said Jamie as she held up a device that looked like a camera. "Just as you're about to."
    A rainbow beam shot out from the device, hitting Tucker square in the chest and then enveloped him in light.

    His entire physique changed rapidly, first his thin and somewhat muscular figure began to put on some wheight until he looked quite jolly and funny. Next his skin turned comepletely white from head to toe and makeup appeared on his face. He quickly had a red muzzle and rosy cheeks, along with green triangles around his eyes and his eyebrows sucked themselves back in to his forehead and were replaced with drawn on lines that were arches on his forehead and made him look comically suprised. Furthermore his hair which originally was quite short and black became bright green and very curly however, the top of his head was bald and smooth.

    Next his pants and shirt morped and grew until he was wearign a blue clown tux. The jacket and pants were blue, but the shirt was green with red polka dots and had a little bow tie with a reversed pattern. His socks became rainbow striped and his feet grew from size 11 to size 18 and they became incased in red clown shoes with yellow laces. A small blue bowler hat with red and green trim appeared on his pate and was slanted just to the side. the last change was his nose turning bright red and swelling up it became a clown nose with nostroils for breathing. Right then all the knowledge, skills, and other things that the other three clown possessed entered his mind.

    When the light subsided and the new clowned Tucker stood before them, he broke into a smile and honked his nose. "Well, well well!" He said in a jolly and goofy voice. "I don't think I've ever felt better in my life! Lets make sure to give those kids a show tomorrow they’ll never forget.”
