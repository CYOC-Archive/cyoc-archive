    Searching for a Wife

    After watching the two kids aging up to a married couple through a magical kiss, Katie wanted to have a significant other to call her own.
    But she felt like a boy isn't enough for her. For some reason, she felt like she has a stronger connection with women since she has four to five friends she grew up with the ring. I made her feel more comfortable around them then that of boys. So after much consideration, she felt like she wanted a wife instead of a husband and would use the ring to make it possible. Katie look to the playground and saw a bunch of girls playing around with the boys. All of them are between five to ten and they are in a variety of races. Looking at the group, she could see a good selection and licked her lips on who to be the perfect match for her. The problem is how she going to do it.

    She can't do it in public as people would suspect something weird is going on. Not to mention that there will be people who will see her taking a child as a kidnapping. She was lucky enough that the last two weren't noticed, but how long until the secret of her ring becomes exposed. She has to play it safe and make sure she isn't caught. Maybe if she pick one of the girls and take her to a secluded place to change her privately, then no one would notice that something is amiss. It's seems like the perfect plan. Now all she needs to do is figure out which girl would become her husband. So who will she pick?
