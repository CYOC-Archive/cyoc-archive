At the very least, you can get lost in your own eyes, a phenomenon which your sisters seem to also share, the trio taking up positions around you. Your thoughts, your questions, they're stilled. The only thing you can think about is how pretty you are. Without so much as a word, the four of you stand stock still, staring at your reflections in the mirror, content to gaze at yourselves in your perfect outfits until...
