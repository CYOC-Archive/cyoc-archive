    Mary shrugged and emptied two doses into a tumbler glass, toasting the glass to herself in the mirror of the bathroom “So long dumb me!”

    Drinking the glass Mary felt her head surging with knowledge as she began seeing double as she continued to shiver and tingle eventually fainting. Coming back in focus Mary adjusted her glasses. Glasses?! Looking in the mirror Mary wanted to scream her 225 IQ had cost her everything except her age and gender. Dressed in a sensible bland beige dress she was a stark contrast from the girl she was before her curves had diminished to petite tomboyish proportions, pointy b cups, adult braces, pimples, and short shoulder length greasy brown hair kept in a messy bun, her nose was more bigger one could even call it bulbous. Sniffling and tearing up feeling stubble on her legs she looked into her panties and grumbled about her new thicket years of primping and preening gone. Looking at her ungroomed eyebrows and her now more plain and gawky face she grabbed tweezers from a drawer and began plucking her almost unibrow shaping them like she used to do.

    Suddenly Mary’s stomach churned and she began shaking violently as years of conflicting emotions and memories entered her head new memories of being a Mensa member and bullied by her peers as a freak for being an advanced learner. A lucrative job as a science researcher on a top secret project math league and cheerleading intermingled. Mary shook it off and decided to grab another vial and get smarter but found they were gone!

    After debating about what to do Mary decided to go out for makeup with her new salary working as a scientist.
