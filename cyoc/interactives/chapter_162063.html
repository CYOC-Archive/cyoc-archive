    Maddy was carrying the trash from one of the cans to the dumpsters. She didn’t like her job but she did it all the same. After lifting the bag into the bin she suddenly felt a jolt of something. Looking around she didn’t notice anything odd. Though... why was she suddenly so itchy?

    As she walked back to the zoo proper she felt off. Her skin was... harrier!

    She quickly hid in the bushes and watched as black smooth fur sprouted from from her skin. It grew to about an inch till her whole body was covered in the stuff. Next her muscles increased, Her clothes felt tighter as her shoulders broadened making her hunch slightly. Finally she felt her jaw push out of her face. Finally she felt her shoes get tighter as her feet became less human. She had no idea what was happening to her. What had the power to... the translator!

    It had to be that. Nothing else made sense. She had to get to it before those apes turned her into an ape fully!
