    “Evan?” Maggie answered her phone, hoping her husband had some good news.

    “Hey, we’re in luck. My old Alpha’s nephew is in town and more than happy to help Spencer with her problem.” Evan said. While he wasn’t thrilled at the idea of setting up his daughter with some perfect stranger to have sex, at least this guy came highly recommended by his old Alpha.

    ”What’s his name?” Maggie asked.

    “Morgan Barr. His family lives in Moon Lake but homeschools their kids.” Evan said.

    “And he’s willing to help?”

    “Yes. I gave my old Alpha Spencer’s cellphone number so Morgan can call or text her. Is she still passed out?”

    “No she’s up.” Maggie looked at her daughter, who was keenly listening in on the entire conversation with her sharp ears. She was more than a little surprised her father so willingly sought out a partner for her, not to mention was ok with her seeing any boy. He always had been a little overbearing, sometimes downright controlling of her. But he meant well, most of the time. And Spencer was a bit of a wild child herself. She wondered what this Morgan was like, and more importantly what kind of were he was.

    “Probably a werewolf or weredog if his family have military connections.” Spencer thought. It was true, most were in the military tended to be canine whether that be wolves, dogs and even foxes.

    “Alright thanks for letting me know dear. When will you be home?” Maggie asked.

    “Soon hopefully. I just have some last minute things ti take care of here. Why? Is there something you needed?” Evan couldn’t help but sense some amount of urgency in his wife’s voice.

    “No not really.” Maggie said. She wasn’t about to tell her husband that she was feeling a little frisky too (though thankfully her heat had already passed weeks ago). At least not talk about that sort of thing in front of their daughter. As open about sex as weres were, Maggie knew Spencer would be cringing and making faces if she said anything. And Spencer was the one who REALLY needed her itch scratched. Maggie could suck it up and wait until her husband came home.

    “Ok. You two take care, make sure Spencer takes her birth control with her.” Evan said. The one absolute condition he had in regard to Spencer being active was that she make sure she was “safe”. He didn’t need grand-pups, cubs, kits, whatever for a long time.

    “I know don’t worry.” Maggie said before saying her goodbyes and hanging up. She looked at Spencer who had her cellphone in her paws. Anxiously waiting for an incoming text or call from Morgan so she could finally quell the aching feeling in her gut.

    “Just remember, be safe and try not to get too out of control.” Maggie said, patting her daughter’s furry head before leaving her alone. She could tell Spencer was restless and very ready to go meet Morgan.

    “I know thanks Mom.” Spencer said. Her tail twitched beside her, anticipation momentarily overpowering the feeling in her gut. As she waited Spencer’s thoughts began to drift back and forth to Eric.

    “He’s got a girlfriend and made it pretty clear he isn’t interested in me romanticslly. So why can’t I get him out if my head? It can’t just be the heat.” Spencer wondered.

    No offense to Eric but he wasn’t exactly drop dead gorgeous. He was cute, and as a weremer had a decent body. But it wasn’t as if Spencer was fawning all over him because he looked like a model. He was a little awkward and undoubtedly nerdy just from the way Spencer had seen him behaving with other people. So what was it about him that Spencer felt herself drawn to?

    She didn’t have much more time to wonder as her phone started vibrating in her paw. An unknown number to be, she knew it must be Morgan.

    “Time to finally scratch this damn itch.” she thought, answering the call.
