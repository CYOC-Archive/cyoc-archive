    "Good Job!" said Negi. "Let's try 3 rocks this time." Negi threw 3 rocks, each in a different direction. Jen was sitting in a clearing, blindfolded.
    "Rock # 1, 12 o'clock, 15 feet nya. Rock # 2, 8 o'clock, 11 feet nya. Rock # 3, 5 o'clock, 23 feet nya." said Jen.
    "Correct, now then, I see a flock of birds south of here, what kind of bird are they? How many are there? Where is the biggest one?" asked Negi.
    "Ravens, 36 of them nya, the biggest one is at 6.3 o'clock, 60 feet nya. Lady Tsunade, what's up nya?" said Jen.
    "Correct. Now then- what?" said Negi.
    "Sounds like somebody's a fast learner. And it's only been 2 days too." said a voice.
    "Lady Hokage." said Negi.
    "I came to check on her. What's changed?" asked Lady Tsunade.
    "Some of her features have grown more cat-like, while some have grown more bird-like. The DNA of a bird of prey has increased the power of her Byakugan in a way that it far exceeds my own. She has gained the ability to change her Sharingan to Byakugan and vice versa. Her range seems to be approxamately 5.73 miles. It has gotten to the point where I must resign as her sensei." said Negi.
    "That fast huh? Okay, Kakashi, It's your turn!" said Lady Tsunade.
    "How'd you know I was here, never mind. I have a message for you. First of all, Iruka and the others are going to make a full recovery. Second, the remaining Shinobi lands have appeared beyond the border. They are arranged as they were originally." said Kakashi.
    "Huh? Are you joking? I've got to go. Tell me how the training goes!" said Lady Tsunade as she ran off.
    "What are you doing up there Guy sensei nya, Lee?" asked Jen. Rock Lee and Guy sensei dropped down from the trees.
    "I apologize Jen. I was simply observing your training because it was impressive. For someone to have learned anything so quickly, you must be truly talented." said Lee.
    "Rock Lee nya, my talent for being a quick learner in no match for your determination nya. Guy sensei was right all along, in that way, you are a genius nya. Even so, I'd like to challenge that dojo of yours someday nya. Maybe, since I can't die, you can teach me those gates of yours nya. I'd love to learn nya." said Jen.
    "Guy sensei? What do you think, can she learn?" asked Lee.
    "Hmm, she certainly has the potential. We'll see how she turns out when Naruto's finished with her." said Guy sensei.
    "Until then Jen, I look forward to your challenge!" said Lee.
    "Okay then miss Jen," said Kakashi, "Your in my boot camp now. First of all, take off the blindfold! Now switch your eyes to Sharingan. We'll start with a simple exercise. I'll sit here and write something on this scroll. By watching the movement of my hands, you will copy what I write in my handwriting." said Kakashi. He sat down and wrote something. It seemed to go in slow motion. Jen was dizzy. She began breathing hard.
    "Ohhh..." Jen collapsed.
    "Huh, poor kid, she used all her chakra."
