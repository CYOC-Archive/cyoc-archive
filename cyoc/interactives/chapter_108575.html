    Frieda Knight and her father sat in the dining area of The Local Diner* eating their breakfast sandwiches, as she started to text Eric and Spencer as to when they would be picked up.
    Then she saw the little red 1 in the upper right hand corner of the envelope icon on her phone, indicating that she had received an e-mail.
    After reading it, she simply frowned and started replying to it.
    "Everything all right?" wondered Benton Knight, while staring at his daughter. He would not admit it unless absolutely necessary, but despite the simplicity of their meal, it wasn't bad.
    "Just some fan mail from some flounder," Frieda replied, before hitting send.
    "Is that good or bad?" wondered her father.
    "Depends upon how you feel about spam," was all Frieda would say, before going back to her breakfast.
    "Okay. Don't forget to take your were birth control pill," Benton reminded her.
    "As long as you take your pills," Frieda said in return.
    Benton looked at their drinks and realized they would need refills to do so, especially him, and called the waitress over for two more milks.

    What Benton was unaware of was that the next time Julie Stovall checked her e-mail, there would be a brief reply from Frieda that read:
    NICE TRY WITH THE FAKE E-MAILS, BUT WE'RE ON TO YOU!
    But what Julie wouldn't know until Sheriff Cooper came to her house later that day with another warrant to check ALL the computer equipment in the Stovall household was that Frieda had BCC'ed** the Sheriff on Julie's latest fake e-mail attack, before filling him in on Julie's computer activities.
