    It was just twenty years ago this month that the Brazilian Railway had a steam locomotive excursion. People came from the world around to see two old steamers pull up the mountain grades. So it was for a man in his forties.

    Kevin Rooste was on the tour and took the train on a Saturday. The next day was another run and he planned to view it from several interesting mountainside lookouts. It was one of these lookouts which brought Kevin face to face with his new life.

    A two hour drive into the high brush took him off the beaten pathway and a luck would have it into the realm of the Medallion. His off road trip was soon trampling down the tall grasses of a little used cart path. When at last the rim of stone brought back memories of the train and tracks far below he came into the opening only to meet Madame DeOgge.

    The Madame was one big black woman well noted in those parts to be in league with the spirit world. It was when Kevin's large vehicle burst out of the tall grassy path she was outraged by his welcomed visit.

    Without a doubt the ex-static man wanting only to photo the train far below met the woman who felt as if he was intruding.

    "Intruder's are not welcome here! Go now and be quick about it less you find your soul and body separated!" ordered the Madame'.

    "Oh dear Lady the view here is so wonderful that I only wish to take some photo's of the train so very far below!" begged Kevin.

    The Madame was not one to suggest and then counter offered as if she were willing to be bargained.

    Stepping close to Kevin she looked down at him. The tall but very large woman's gaze made Kevin look away. Nearby were several male bucks flirting with a dark brown and shaggy nanny goat.
