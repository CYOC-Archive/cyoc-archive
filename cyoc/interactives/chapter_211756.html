    Ric looked oddly at Josh, so Josh shook his head, took the book from his little brother's grasp and gently shoved him towards the door.

    "Now Ricardo, while we're living."

Suddenly Josh now remembered that whenever he needed to tell Ric that he was being serious and acting as his guardian, he had to use his full name, not just his nickname... navigating the tightrope that was being both brother and guardian was tough, but Josh was really getting a handle on it now.

Thankfully the sound of the front door slamming shut drew Rosie's attention.
