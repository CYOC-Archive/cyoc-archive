    Magical Recovery Home is a series of large Halfway houses and Battered Shelters across the nation.

    They are known for being a very welcoming place that would help anyone, be it a drug addict trying to get clean, to abused women and men along with any children they may have. They take in kids who were kicked out of their homes for being gay or lesbian, they help young families and individuals. They do not judge; they do not force a faith on anyone, and they provide excellent help.

    Their facilities range from simple homes or farms in small towns to large buildings in larger cities and have a range of excellent help. From classrooms to medical and mental care, from daycare to swimming pools.

    All this is provided free of charge by a well-trained staff.

    All saw the good they did in the community and while some asked who was paying for it an answer of a series of large endowments from billionaires caused most to stop asking questions.

    They had an excellent rate of recovery and had great success on getting people back on their feet, but nothing is 100% successful so when some people disappeared it was brushed aside people falling back into bad habits, not wanting help or simply running away. While this did happen, not all the disappearances where that.

    Unknown to public or most of the workers, Magical recovery was a front. They would go through all those who came into their doors and if a person had certain skills, fit curtain requirements, or pulled at the heart strings of the higher staff hard enough a private offer would be made.

    An offer to leave humanity behind, to travel to a magical world hidden from view where all the creatures and races considered myths by humans lived. There they would be given a chance at a new life. The cost would be they could never go back to their old lives, and they would be transformed into a magical creature.

    An offer is about to be made, who is it to and what is their story.
