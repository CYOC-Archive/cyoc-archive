    "Toys & Tots" is a company that is in charge of creating products for babies and toddlers. From toys, food, clothes and even music. Everything that is for the care of the little ones. But even though they have been doing quite well lately, there has been a severe shortage of new ideas for their new products. So with the help of a group of scientists from a special academy, the company had come up with a plan that could help them not only create new products, but also improve the company as such.

    Through different media, the company reported that they are currently looking for people who can work in the company for a special job, which guarantees a good payment. But what they don't know is that the company actually plans to rejuvenate them and then put them in a special nursery. The way they will rejuvenate them will be with a special serum, which can turn them into 1-year-old babies or 2 or 3-year-old toddlers. They will still keep their minds intact, but if they return at the age of 1 they will not be able to walk or speak. If they return at the age of 2 or 3, they will still be able to talk and walk but with great difficulty. The serum will also prevent them from growing. This means that even if 10 years pass, that person will still be the same age at which they were rejuvenated.

    All this in order to study them in order to create new products for the company. They also plan to test their products on the regressed employees to determine if that product needs any improvement or not.

    The company even has plans to create a huge daycare center in the future, which will be divided into two parts. One part will be used for ordinary children and the other will be used for the regressed ones. And both parts will be studied for interactions and interests. Then they will use that data to create even better products.

    But who will be the first person to accept that special job?
