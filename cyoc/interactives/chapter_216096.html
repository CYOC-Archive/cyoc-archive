    The Magic Shop

    Mythology Soap Bars

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 5 months ago O Mythological Magic

    You decide to browse the different aisles. The products seem to cater to fantasy lovers but certainly it has a certain appeal. It brings quite a bit fresh influences and products. All cater to people sensitive to imaginary creatures as mascots. All designs of the products are based with mermaids, unicorns, centaurs, fauns, fairies, various monsters and whatnot. How strange. But how original! You look around, curious and surprised. Why, even the aisle dedicated to bars of soap is worthy of attention, the aisle that normally does not attract many buyers, more interested in other more precise and sophisticated products. You peer closely at the different soaps, surprising yourself. You are more interested than you would think possible. And indeed the products for sale are quite odd yet enticing. Normally, you'd find more than one dollar for a single bar of soap an outrageous price, but here, two dollars at least and more seems quite reasonable. A bit expensive if you purchase a while basket but for what it is, it looks like a bargain. You peer at the different samples on sale, amidst the many rows, intent upon trying at least one. The packaging is delicate and dainty, as much as it is original, just like the other products in the store. Drawing, pretty ones, of different creatures grace the packaging and unlike what might be expected with these sort of items, the packaging, for this kind of price, does not hide an awful product. The smell that emanates from the bars of soap is simply divine and you decide to do an exception to your habits and buy one or several.
