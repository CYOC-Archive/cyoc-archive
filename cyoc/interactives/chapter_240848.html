Sitting down on the chair his mother began eagerly typing away.

    "Hold on, what about dad?" Jeff asked realizing he forgot about his father.

    "Don't worry I already thought of that. I going to make him a single parent for the weekend. I'm sure that man can last three whole days without me" she chuckled. "Just in case though I'm also giving him a higher paying job that way he can afford a babysitter for us."

After a while a thought came to mind which he voiced immediately. "Hey uh, since you already on it can you do me a favor and make me three months older. I kind of want to be your big brother" he admitted with a blush, acknowledging that she was to be his little sister. "Oh and make sure I don't lose my diapers" Jeff added.

After setting a sixty'second delay timer they both walked to his nursery and stood in the middle of the room, waiting for the changes to take effect. When the transformation did happen it proved to be just as quick as his own. Jeff watched his thirty-eight year old mother rapidly regress before his eyes, growing younger and younger back into a young woman, a teen, a child. Paying close attention to the final changes he saw her body regain lost baby fat and her face turn cherub-like as she became a two year old toddler. The newly regressed baby girl was dressed in a Moana themed t-shirt and a diaper and had a pacifier in her mouth. She stared back at him as she calmly sucked on it, her eyes wide and innocent just like a toddler's. His changes were less noticable being only a few months but Jeff felt them, only being growing a couple more inches in height and going up a diaper size as the disposable garment grew a little with his waist.

    Jeff nodded, smiling as he took in the adorable sight of his now-toddler mother. "Yeah I know, it can feel pretty strange." He then couldn't help but ask, "So how does that diaper of yours feel?"

The pink side was clearly themed for a girl with appropriate toys scattered on the floor with a crib against the wall. The blue side was the same having a crib and toys, though it was geared toward a boy. A fully stocked changing table shared the boundary between as both of them had equal need of the piece of furniture.

    Amazed by their shared nursery Jeff and his mother stared in wonder for a few more moments before facing each other.

    "So what do you want to do first?" Jeff asked with a shrug, leaving the decision up to the other toddler.
