    The Zoo

    Wolves!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago A AR S

    "I would like to see the wolves!" You exploded with glee. "Right away!" The man started to escort you to the wolf exhibit. You were excited, you've never been this close to wild animals before! The trip there, although quick, was quiet, quiet enough for you to ponder, was this safe? You were going to be up close to wolves! Like, real wolves! Sure they were probably used to people, but that doesn't mean that they'll immediately like you. You figured you may say something, but before you could, the man yelled. "We're here!"

    Suddenly all your thoughts dissipated, you were once again excited to meet wolves up close and personal! You entered the building with the man, "Where do we go to enter the wolf exhibit?" You ask. "Right here!" The man chuckled, you were about to say something before he pushed you in. "HEY!" You started to panic, the man laughed, then disappeared. You looked around panicking, suddenly there were a couple of wolves looking at you. You nearly pissed yourself like a baby at the Sight of the two canines.

    The Wolves approached you, "Please no!" You covered your face, not wanting to see what was going to happen. You felt licking, they were licking you, and it kind of tickled. You were confused, but it was a way better choice than the alternative. The licks from the wolves were quite nice, it felt relaxing...

    You didn't even notice that your clothes were falling off your body. You also didn't notice the hair growing on your body either. You did however notice your face changes, it's hard to ignore your mouth and nose stretching out into a muzzle. You began to panic, but the wolves calmed you quickly, you didn't know what exactly was happening, but it could've ended up so much worse.

    You finally noticed your body was much smaller than the wolves, you looked down to see what was happening, you were becoming a wolf! You actually felt... happy? Yeah, that was cool! Being a human was dumb, being a wolf however, that'd be the life!

    It soon became obvious that you weren't just becoming a wolf, you were becoming a wolf pup! You felt excited for this new life you were going to live. After an hour of licking, you tried to walk out of the clothes, but it was... difficult... you weren't used to being on all fours.

    "You'll get used to it." One of the wolves chuckled. "You can talk?" You questioned. "Of course we can! We're like you! Humans that became wolves, although we didn't think you'd become our pup." The second wolf chuckled, he sounded like the male of the two.

    You try again and succeed, you were really a little gray wolf pup!
