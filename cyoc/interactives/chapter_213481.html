    Blaze was laying down when he heard & then saw the zoo staff members enter the Kangaroo Enclosure. He saw they were in cleaning gear. Blaze got up knowing what was going on. The staff members began leading the kangaroos to the holding pen. "Very good Blaze." The staff member named Adam praised Blaze for his usual compliance. Another staff member named Ben looked at the man & said sighing "Why you praising an animal? He doesn't know what you're saying." Ben replied to that "He does know what I'm saying." So now all of the kangaroos (that included Blaze & Hopper) were in the holding pen. Now alone Hopper asked Blaze in Werespeak 'Why we get put in here sometimes daddy?' Blaze explained 'So they can clean our home.' 32 minutes later... The zoo staff member named Mary entered the holding pen. She walked right up to Blaze & said to him "You've mastered acting like a normal kangaroo Blaze." Blaze very surprised asked 'You a werecreature too?' Mary's face pushed out to a yellow beak & she answered 'Yes. And we non zoo animals werecreatures know stuff.' Her beak then went back to a human mouth & nose. 'So there are other werecreatures besides us zoo animals living here?' Blaze asked surprised & curious. 'Yes. You met the werecrow & the weresquirrel already. So of us even work here as staff.' Mary informed Blaze. "Mary where are you?!" They heard someone yell for her. 'I got to go now.' she said. Blaze requested 'Mary please come to the staff room tonight for our monthly werecreatures get together.' Mary smiled saying 'I'll see you tonight then. I'll be in my anthro Starling form & I'll be only in my feathers as per the rule for us werecreatures.' And with that she left. Blaze was happy he'd get to talk & hang out with Mary tonight. Till now he didn't even know she was a werecreature too. Hopper asked 'What's a Starling?' Blaze answered 'A bird.' It was clear to Blaze by the fact Mary talked of being in feathers. Hopper then asked 'What's a Starling look like?' Blaze replied that 'I don't know. We'll find out tonight Hopper.' Blaze then began to wonder how many of the non zoo animals in the zoo were wereanimals too. He knows of 2 werebirds & a weresquirrel so far. How many others are there?
