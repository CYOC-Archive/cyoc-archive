    Kylie spotted her next to victims quickly. A couple obviously on a date together stood near the front of the crowd. The girl was dressed as a witch. She was painted green and had a scraggly white haired wig below her crooked black hat. The boy was dressed as the typical cliche wizard. He wore a white beard, blue starry hat, and all. She motioned them on stage. Once in the spotlight the boy tried to take the girls hand but she swatted him away.
    "Already some tension between you too I see. Good! You're going to need it!" Kylie said happily. "I saw your costumes and just couldn't resist calling you both up here." She paced back in forth in front of them as she spoke. "We are going to have ourselves a little magical duel. Witch versus Wizard. You will each take a side of the stage and when I will grant you both a little magic of transformation. You will take turns. During your turn you will be able to make one alteration to the body of the other, back and forth in retaliation, until I get bored with you!" The girl took a step away from her boyfriend glaring at him angrily. "The person who makes the changes that entertain me most MIGHT get a little help from me afterward." Kylie pressed one hand against the boys chest and shoved him to one side of the stage, the girl walked a couple steps back on her own.
