    "I'm only 17!" Andrew stated. The young boy threw his arms out making sure Zach got a good look at his muscular frame, the perfectly messy hair if a Hollywood adolescent, and the well tailored jeans, t-shirt, and jacket. His black hair, and blue eyes accompanied by his dimples gave him a pretty boy charm that would have all the ladies swoon.

    Zach gave him a look. "Are you sure? I thought you were 18 or 19."

    Andrew smirked. "OH I know how old I look. I am exceptionally manly now. I know this just like I know that I'm 17, who my new parents are, and that I have to go to high-school again. I doubt American high-school is going to be much more fun than Russian. The Nu-Life Booth was kind enough to give me new memories so can I can actually live this new life. Though I'm afraid this new life doesn't have me dating a 30 year old man."

    Zach nodded. "Yeah, I can see that being a problem. It might only be for 3 weeks but I'd rather not go to jail for dating a minor. Even when you turn back, who knows if I would magically be released from prison."

    "Best not to risk it. Which is why the only safe option is for you to join me in my new high-school " Andrew had a huge grin on his face

    While Zach could see the logic, he hadn't been prepared for using the booth himself. "Really? I wasn't going to actually step in there myself.:

    Andrew glared. "No you just want to make yourself happy by changing me, but never considered that I might want the same from you."

    "That's not what I mean."

    Andrew took a step to the side and waved a hand towards the booth. "Then you have no problem stepping in and letting me have complete control of the settings like I did for you."

    Zach sighed and lowered his head, resigning himself to the booth. While Andrew was a new person the booth obviously didn't take away Anya's stubborn streak. He figured that be the first thing it changed.

    Andrew leaned over and messed with the controls. Zach read as the boy typed. 'The hot 15 year old girlfriend of Andrew.'

    "Girlfriend?" Zach asked.

    "Well I'm not gay." Andrew responded. "Last time I checked neither were you. Besides, why should I be the only one who sees how the other half lives."

    Most of that made sense except one part. "Why am I going to be 15 if you are 17?"

    Andrew shrugged. "I was five years young than you so I figured of we are doing a role reversal let's go all the way and make you younger this time. But I'm not dating a 12 year old. 2 years younger than 17 should be sociable acceptable right?"

    Zach thought about it. "More or less. Some parents may complain."

    Andrew smiled. "I'm sure your parents will love me. I'm quite charming now. All the girls I remember dating had parent's who adored me." Andrew kept typing for a moment. "I'm setting it so you can have your sweet 16 before we turn back. It's a special day for girls and I thought you should have one for when you turn back into a guy." Finished typing Andrew removed himself from the booth and put a hand on the curtain. "If you go back to a guy. See you in a second babe." With a quick wink he closed the curtain and the camera started flashing.

    Zach felt the changes begin, wondering what kind of girl he would become. The Nu-Life Booth could have anything from a mild to a severe twist in store for him
