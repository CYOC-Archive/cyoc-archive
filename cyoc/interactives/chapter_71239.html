    Now Becky thought she was over and done with with the killing of the werewolf,and was
    properly horrified when the ash seemed to blow back and stick onto its huge
    bones.

    Before her eyes the ash seemed to be congealing,solidifying,turning
    back into werewolf flesh.

    She could see veins start to snake about under the skeletons ribcage
    and soon realized the dead thing was regenerating itself.

    Luckily for you and her Clint and Elsie came out of the library at that time
    and laid a silver cross on the dead monster,which seemed to stop the
    regeneration process.

    Temporarily

    Elsie told the two of you that when the Full Moon arose in 30 days time the cursed body
    would fully regenerate itself into a howling she-beast once more

    unless

    its vampiric wolf mummy host was reunited with it in a common peril.

    Becky glowered at Elsie for passing on that bit of news,but you two girls dutifully
    followed the dragonman and the library as they left the town and went to the Caverns of the
    Werewolves

    It was in a gloomy valley and the Sun barely reached through the canopy.

    You all entered a cavern,where you saw a huge werewolf hunched over beside a
    closed coffin.

    Becky wanted to blast the thing but Elsie stopped her,saying
    "It'll upset the balance"
    and explained if the werewolf guardian was slain,the spirit within the coffin would come
    forward and seek a new flesh and blood host to change.

    Curious,Becky nosed open the coffin lid,to find herself staring at a mummified
    long dead wolf,a real wolf,lying curled up .The dead wolfs fangs were ridiculously
    long,and Elsie explained this was so that the undead wolf could scratch a human and
    turn them into a werewolf host body that carried out the wolfs will.

    You all continued deeper in the cavern,till you came apon a coffin without a
    werewolf beside it.

    However there was lots of fresh blood on the floor and a torn girls dress,like the
    human girl inside the dress had gotten bigger and furrier as she had burst out of her
    clothing last night.

    Elsie lifted the coffin lid,and inside you saw another mumified wolf,with fresh blood
    coating its overly long fangs

    "This is the one who turned the werewolf that you killed in the library" Elsie
    explained to Becky.

    All four of you carried that coffin out of the cavern and brought it back to
    the library,and laid the casket beside the semi-regenerated werewolf skeleton.

    Elsie popped open the coffin lid,and took the silver cross from the skeleton
    and laid it on the undead wolf mummy.

    So intent were you at the sight of the howling wolf mummy spoting out human blood
    you never saw the werewolf behind you turn back into the corpse of the human
    girl ,its victim,with two deep puncture marks on her neck where the wolf mummy had sucked her
    human blood out.And changed her.

    Then Clint brought out the sharp wide bladed axe and the kerosene and
    Becky protested as she realized what the two islanders had in mind for the
    wolf mummy and the human girls mortal remains.

    Clint and Elsie just shrugged and put the tools away.

    Around the same time as the wolf mummy was growing more feeble in its struggles
    in its coffin,you heard the sounds of stretching flesh and cracking changing bones
    coming from behind you,from the dead girl who was stretching and changing into a newly
    lifeless wolf,complete with extra long fangs.

    Then the new undead wolf got onto its four lupine legs shakily,and its eyes began to
    glow yellow as the new monster maker came back to life,stared at her
    fur,slid a long wolf tongue over her huge fangs.

    She began to whimper,and when she looked in a nearby hall mirror and saw that her
    reflection didn't show in it,she really howled in despair...
