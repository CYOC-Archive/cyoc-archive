    With high school over and college only a month away, Jackie Kirkman decided now would be the best time to relax. Once college began, Jackie realized she would be to focused on school. After fighting a timeline conquering titan, a government shadow group, and the vampires she needed a vacation.The only question was, where should she go?

    Jackie couldn't decide, eventually asking her girlfriend Ann O'Toole where she would want to go. "I have no idea," Ann shrugged as the two approached the Magic Shop.

    "Well," Jackie asked. "You have to have some idea. I mena you've traveled all over the world."

    "On business," Ann replied. "I don't usually get a chance to to take in the sights." As Jackie was about to reply, she paused. "When did this travel agency open up?" The two now opened the door to the agency, cautiously gazing around the interior.

    "Welcome to the Magical Realms Travel Agency!"

    Ann and Jackie paused, curiously gazing at the woman behind the counter. The woman appeared to be in her 20s, an exuberant smile on her face as she beamed at the couple.

    "Um hi," Ann stated. "Who are you and whats the deal with this place?"

    "My names Jade," she smiled, reaching under the counter to hand Ann and Jackie a pamphlet. "And this is the Magic Travel Agency. Here, take a look." Cautiously both women now grasped the pamphlet, their eyes growing wide as they perused what the agency had to offer.

    "Wait we can go to other magical worlds!"

    "Thats right " Jade grinned. "We offer travel arrangements to almost any of the realms of the magical world. Kitsune, fairies, tanuki, unicorns, even the vampires."

    "Yeah we might want to avoid the vampires after what happened," Ann mumbled, remembering how not too long ago, she and her nieces had stormed the Tepes family castle to rescue her toddler niece.

    "The vampires are under new leadership," Jade replied happily. "The current lord is trying to maintain a more 'human friendly' environment."

    "Yeah Ann," Jackie smirked. "Dont be such an adult fart. Where's your sense of adventure."

    "Sense of adventure," Ann exclaimed. "Jackie we blew up the castle a couple of months ago! And in case you forgot, I am old!"

    "Are you," Jackie asked with a smirk. Ann could only sigh, as she saw Jackie's ring begin to glow. The 46 year old woman inspecting herself as Jackie began to regress her. The changes to her body were noticeable after a moment, her breasys rising, her hips narrowing, and her skin regaining its youthful luster. Grabbing a pocket mirror out her purse, Ann watched as the fine lines on her face smoothed, her hair regaining its youthful sheen. Frowning a now 18 year old Ann glowered at her girlfriend.

    "Just couldn't help yourself huh?"

    Jackie snickered, offering Ann an apology as the two looked at the pamphlet. The couple wanted a vacation together. The only question was where.
