    Jennifer stared at the phone like it had just sprouted
    wings and tried to fly away. "you're kidding right?
    Everyone knows that curse isn't real. It's just to give
    nerds like you hope of revenge." she then heard Kyle
    laughing on the other end of the line.
    "oh? Not real you say? If only you knew before it was too
    late. And oh look at that, time just ran out which means
    you get both." Jennifer felt a weird kind of shift around
    her. Like something wasn't quite right. *no, it's just this
    idiot trying to mess with me* she thought.
    "well then, since I get both, go a head asshole. And then
    when it doesn't work I can have my boyfriend beat your ass
    into next week." her voice dripped with venom.
    "Jennifer, I'm not afraid of you or your boyfriend anymore,
    and here's why. You are going to transform into my
    unlimited wish granting infinite power having genie. Your
    new home will be this gem I am currently holding in my
    hand. Everyone will still remember who you are once you
    transform. that way you can be humiliated. Just as you
    humiliated me. And as for your dare... Well that will come
    later." Jennifer heard the line go dead as Kyle hung up on
    the other end.
    Again, there felt like there was a little shift, but she
    couldn't tell what it was. Putting the phone back Jennifer
    felt a bit off. And the feeling was only increasing with
    each step she took towards her room. Opening the door,
    Jennifer walked into her room and shut the door behind her.
    That was when everything seemed to happen at once. Door
    knob shocking her, she yelped and jumped back. Watching in
    fear as the door itself seemed to fade away till it was
    just a solid wall. Turning around, she saw the same thing
    happen to the window. "what the hell is happening?!"
    running to where the window had been only moments ago, she
    began pounding on the wall in a futile effort to escape.
    "no no no, the curse isn't real! It can't be real! I'm not
    a genie!" even as the words left her mouth, she felt a
    surge of power flow through her body. First knocking her on
    the ground, then from the ground she floated towards the
    center of the room. Arms and legs spread she felt bolt
    after bolt of energy hit her. Pain racked her body at the
    same time as empowering it. This continued till she felt
    like she would explode. Suddenly, it stopped. Jennifer fell
    to the ground and winced. Reaching back, she rubbed her ass
    with her left hand. "Ouch, what the hell just happened?"
    standing on shakey legs, she looked around. Her walls,
    floor, and ceiling were now a brilliant blue. It almost
    looked like glass. There was still no door or window
    though. Walking to her full length mirror she stared in
    awe. Her clothes were nearly burnt to shreds. This of
    course went on the back burner as she looked at her eyes.
    They had always been blue... But now they were
    perfection... And they were glowing! "oh when I see Kyle I
    am going to kill him. That is... If I can ever find a way
    out of my room." stripping out of her burnt clothes,
    Jennifer was amazed but not completely surprised to see
    that every scar and blemish that had been on her body was
    gone. Every ache she had, even the ones her chest caused
    her back was gone. She had never felt better. "I feel so...
    Strong." looking at her closet door, she made a fist and
    punched it. The door exploded into pieces, said pieces
    flying into the glass like door before falling to the
    ground. she hadn't felt any pain. It had felt like she had
    punched paper. "maybe this isn't too bad." remembering she
    was naked, she grabbed some clothes from her closet and got
    dressed. Just in time too, the moment her shirt was on, she
    felt a pulling sensation across her whole body.
