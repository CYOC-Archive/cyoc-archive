    Aldo was moving pretty quickly as he headed toward the food court and
    the exit beyond it. He felt a sudden pang, and paused between two
    potted trees at the entrance to the food court. He leaned on the trash
    bin that was next to them for support. He suddenly felt very heavy. In
    inhaled and his chest exploded. His t-shirt ripped as he grew from xs
    to large or maybe xl. He wasn't sure. He just new that his shirt was
    pulled up to his belly button, and that the fabric had torn several
    places giving him that shredded look.

    He adjusted himself on his legs, and started to enter the food court.
    He saw some girls eying him, and he reached up to the tear in his
    collar and just ripped his t-shirt off his chest exposing his bare
    now-ripped tan torso. He grinned as they stared. He caught a glimpse
    of his reflection in the mirrored glass of one of the shops, and
    nearly freaked. His size 20 shorts looked way too small with his buff
    upper half. As he watched he felt his legs grow stronger, and his
    shorts tighter. The seams ripped and he was standing there in only his
    skintight yellow jammers. He blushed and flexed for the crowd.

    "How did you do that Mike?" asked Claudia.

    "A magician never reveals his secrets," Aldo said. Then he winked and
    leaned in and whispered, "It's done with mirrors, don't tell."

    He sashayed pass the girls and toward the exit. He was almost out
    when a mall cop came up to him.

    "What's the matter, officer, I'm just leaving," Aldo said as the
    officer grabbed his arm.

    "No shirt, no shoes, no service," the guard grumbled.

    "Right. I said I was leaving."

    "You bet you are. Stripping in public, I'm calling the police."

    "I didn't strip. The cheap clothes I got at your mall just ripped.
    See?" he held up the newer shorts torn along the seam.

    The guard frowned, "You were wearing those? Aren't those a might too
    small?"

    "That's what I told the clerk, but she said it would show off my body.
    Well, it did that. I'm glad I've got my old clothes in my gym bag,"
    Aldo replied.

    "There's a restroom here. I suggest you change in there. I'll wait
    out here," said the guard.
