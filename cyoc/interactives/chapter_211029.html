    Dan The Baby Animal - In A Surprise Egg!

    added by Elflover 9 months ago BM S O Mythological Magic

    Kat was elated. Not only she had great talent in the art of witchcraft, but she could finally get her revenge on Dan. Her brother had abused her endlessly for years and years and now, she was in power. Oh, she was going to make him pay! All his life, as Dan was handsome and he knew it, he had been cocky to the extreme and used his looks and charisma to cheat his way through life, whether at home or at school. Kat's parents fawned over their son's popularity, especially the one he had with girls and somehow seemed to see his pseudo-mature behavior like a form of intelligence. His smoking and drinking and interest in girls were viewed by Kat's parents like signs of maturity. How early did that boy stop playing with toys and switch to video games and teenage activities! For Kat, the smell of cigarettes and the bragging attitude of her brother seemed much more worrisome but for some reason, her parents never saw it that way. They confused pseudo-mature behavior with mature behavior. If Dan had been intelligent AND mature, he would have been nicer to everyone at school and to her and prepared for college. Instead, his popularity just meant he saw it as a way to stop teachers from getting him the punishments he deserved and he saw the girls thinking him very handsome as being his normal due. He didn't work at all at school but used his charisma to get barely passing grades. However, few people thought like Kat that Dan's behavior was good enough for high school or at best the first years of college. But now, she need not wait that long! Dan was in an egg, at her complete mercy. She'd get her revenge. She already knew she'd shrink him to be a small animal but... What sort? She could spirit him to another real, a fantasy realm, from which he was sure to never come back from. But should she leave him with his memories? Or only partially? Should she give him a female sex? Or homosexual tendencies? She was not going to turn him into something big and strong, that was for sure. So many possibilities... She pondered the question with due consideration and then reached a final decision. Yes! That small animal (or creature) would be perfect for her braggart of a brother!
