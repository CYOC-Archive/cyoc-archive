    Allison looked to the diaper and instantly she knew what she wanted to do. A smile crossed her face as she thought her plan over. She loved diapers but anybody wearing them at her age would be looked at as some kind of weirdo. Naturally, Allison wanted to be made younger. She didn’t want to be too young, lose her current mind to the new form, nor want the change to be permanent. Thus, she needed to get the effect just right, so she thought for the most careful response possible.

    “Alright,” Allison thought, “I want the effects of this diaper to be that it regresses the wearer into a young child only in body. The diapers will shrink to fit the new size, and the user will still be the same age they were when they put it on. Finally, the power of the diaper will last as long as the user wants with the only way to reverse it being to have someone else take it off. Finally, almost anyone suspicious will have their mind changed to think that the wearer always was a child until the diaper is taken off.”

    She finished her thought as one of the diapers in the pack shined, a smirk hitting her face. Now, she could wear diapers as much as she wanted without anyone getting suspicious. In her haste, Allison immediately took her sweatpants and underwear off, and put on the very same diaper that had just shined. Instantly, the girl begun shrinking.

    She expected the world around her to be much larger than herself. It wasn’t. In fact, Allison felt like she only shrunk around a little less than a foot. She looked down at her shirt, which had morphed from a sweater to suit her new form. It was black with a little white cat towards the bottom. Below the shirt was a light pink diaper. The girl put her hand on it, and pressed it. It felt so nice. However, she noticed that it looked out of place on her. Allison hopped off the bed and looked at a full-body mirror positioned in the corner. Looking back was a roughly nine year-old girl wearing the same shirt and diaper.

    “Shit,” Allison thought, realizing that she wasn’t specific enough, “I wanted to be younger.”

    Instantly, she begun to try reversing the transformation by taking her diaper off. For some reason she couldn’t. Her fingers just slipped off the tapes with each attempt. After a good few attempts, the regressed girl just gave up.

    “Well, this isn’t ideal,” Allison sighed, “I’m still too old for these.”

    Then, she heard knocking at her room’s door.
