    Bad time to use the phone

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 27 days ago O

    To Jason's bad luck, he was not able to make any changes because one of the daycare workers noticed that he had a phone so she took it from him.

    “Sorry, but you can't have electric devices in here", the daycare worker told Jason. “Besides, it’s nap time now.”

    “But…” Jason started.

    “No buts. We'll give it back when your parents are finally here", the worker replied.

    It was then that Jason was taken along with the others to the cots for a nap. Jason was a little upset about his phone but like the others he ended up obeying and fell asleep. Meanwhile, the worker began to check what was on the phone. And soon she ended up finding out about the app. As she was figuring out how it worked, a new sentence was generated:

    "Trinity now has Jason's own phone"

    Trinity was surprised to see that the app not only mentioned her name, but also indicated something she was doing right now. It was then that her curiosity for the app began to grow and she began to use it.

    The first change she made was to change "Jason" to "her". By making that said change, reality was altered. Although she didn't notice the change, now the phone was hers and not Jason's. And therefore, Jason no longer had any memory of his phone or the app.

    Then, the next sentence that was generated was:

    "Trinity is an employee of the mall's daycare"

    Trinity wondered what would happen if she ended up changing "employee" to "attendee". So she tried to find out and made said change. And it was then that something happened that would end up leaving Trinity completely surprised.

    She was no longer a 26-year-old who worked in the mall's daycare, now she was an 1-year-old baby who attended said daycare. Her work attire was replaced by a pink coverall adorned with Easter eggs. Not to mention, she had a pacifier in her mouth and to her bad luck…..a diaper.

    Trinity froze at what happened as she didn't expect to be changed like this. But before it occurred to her to try to use the phone again, she heard that one of the workers was approaching.

    Knowing that they could take her phone from her, Trinity tried to find a place to hide it as quickly as possible. Luckily, near her there was a small bookshelf. Maybe she could hide it under it.

    At first she tried to walk but after taking a small step she ended up falling on her padded butt. Great, she couldn't walk anymore so she had no choice but to crawl over to the bookshelf. So Trinity tried to crawl as best she could to the bookshelf and proceeded to put the phone under it.

    She was glad to manage to hide it but her happiness didn't last long as one of the workers ended up finding her.

    "Hi, little one. What are you doing here? You know little kids like you can't be in the big kids' room," the worker told the now-baby Trinity in a goofy way. Trinity tried to answer but all she could say was "Goo Goo". Apparently, she was also unable to speak.

    "Awww. Babies are so cute when they babble in their baby talk", the worker told her as she proceeded to pick her up. "Come on baby girl, let's go take you to where you should be."

    Before long, the worker took Trinity to another room where the younger children were, who were basically children under the age of 3. After leaving her in the right room, the worker left.
