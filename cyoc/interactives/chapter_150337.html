    Long and rather boring weeks went by as Selicia set up her shop in the manor. Marina had been such a good girl and told Selicia everything she knew about this new age. Having become quite confident that noone would come around, at least soon, to stop her, she finished the decor to her consultation room. She stood appraising the arrangement with the strong feeling that there was something missing. As she pondered the gate bell rang, Guests, that's what was missing. Satisfied customer testimonials, if you would.

    "Marina, recieve our first customers, would you dear?" Selicia moved to settle into her plush chair facing the doorway. Marina left with excitement to fulfill the request given to her.

    A group of girls, six in total were settled into the sofas to either side of Selicia within minutes. They taking in the room or talking to each while Marina retrieved snacks from the kitchen. Selicia took this time to take stock of the girls while they waited.

    1. Natasha - The picture of youth and beauty, blonde hair reaching reaching her hips, ice blue eyes, full figured with a captivating pair of breasts.
    2. Eliza - An intellectual type, brown shoulder length hair, green eyes with silver framed glasses, and a modest figure.
    3. Naomi - A sun-kissed athlete, short boycut black hair, hazel eyes, and a slim and tanned athletic body.
    4. Eve - A tall motherly type, midback length red hair pulled into a braided pony tail, honey colored eyes, and a soft, well endowed figure.
    5. Marie - Baggy eyed gamer girl, spiky blue hair, grey eyes, and a slim figure.
    6. Lily - Hoodie wearing Shy girl, curly dirty blonde hair, Hazel eyes, a modestly covered figure.

    Selicia nodded to herself as the snacks came to rest on the table, this was a fine variety to start with. According to their application, most of them were room mates in college whereas the last two roomed together elsewhere. The group knew each other well and thought it would be a novelty to try out a wish. It seemed that the last girl was dragged along for the venture as she shied away from Selicia's gaze every time it would fall on her. With the treats out of the way, Selicia asked the all important question. "What is it that you wish for?"

    Natasha - "To be young and beautiful for the rest of my life." She said with a proud flip of her hair.
    Eliza - "I just want to be able to finally get rid of all this stress." She spoke in a spiritually exhausted manner.
    Naomi - "I want to stay fit no matter what I do." She grinned as she flexed her arm to show off.
    Eve - "I really want to make people's lives brighter." As she was trying to ease the nerves of Lily.
    Marie - "Playing video games, all day, without needing to go anywhere." Her speech was slow and drawn by exhaustion.
    Lily - "I hope I never have to lose my friends..." She held her hands in front of her face peering over her knuckles.

    Selicia gave her best and brightest smile as to not scare them off. "Easily done, in fact, all of your wishes will come true in the next few days! As for the price? We'll call this a free service as part of my grand opening."

    She watched as the girls spoke excitedly amongst themselves only Lily still seemed apprehensive. No matter, Selicia thought as she worked the magic into each girl covertly. The girls left a short while later having finished the treats and Selicia let her true grin loose. It would be free in a way, they'd just pay her later after all.
