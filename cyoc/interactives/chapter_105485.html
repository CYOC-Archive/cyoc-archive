Nevertheless, it wasn't an immediate concern, so he decided to act passively for the moment, since his death would soon be revealed to the pack. Cutting two small slits in the blanket, Merse donned it as a hooded cape, sticking the ears through the holes, and hoping the getup would give her enough time to withdraw the ears to her human ones were she spotted. The makeshift cape also served to hide her tail, as well as masking the oddness of her gait.
