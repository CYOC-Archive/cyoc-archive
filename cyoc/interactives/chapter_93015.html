    Cat eagerly strode into a nearby gym. There was a lady at a counter, a few guys with towels round their necks, and a women on an elliptical.

    The largest man of the group turned to greet the newcomer. "Hey, we-" He jumped when he found a rather large, beast-like woman at the door.

    "Hi," Cat said. "My name's Cathy, but you can call me Cat."

    "I think I'll use Cat," The man said, noting her feline features.

    Cat wandered around the gym, looking at all the weights and dumbbells and pulleys scattered about. Her eye caught a particularly large looking weight. From a few feet away, Cat could see a label reading 60 lbs. Her senses were improving, that was certain.

    Cat closed her hand around the weight. She took a deep breath, and pulled her arm towards her, bringing the weight with it.

    Cat was shocked at how quickly she had picked up the weight. Moving on to the next one, she found a 70 lbs. weight. She lifted it with ease. 80 lbs. No problem. 90 lbs. Without breaking a sweat.

    All the while, the men marveled at the creature that had entered their gym. She didn't act very harmful, but her strength, and that size!

    The weights stopped at 100. Cat looked for another challenge, and was answered by a bench press. She took 2 100 lbs. and 2 50 lbs. weights, setting one each on both sides.

    The men gathered around the table as she prepared. "Y'know," Said the man who had greeted her. "You should really warm up first."

    "I was warming up." She shot back at him. Another man, much lankier than the others, began asking questions.

    "So, uh, Cat, was it?"

    "That's me." She said, sliding under the bar.

    "Well, uh how long have you been, uh, this way?" He asked, pointing to her lion-esque features.

    "Just got this tonight," She said. "Wanted to test my strength."

    She grabbed the bar, with bot hands, setting them at equal points. Taking 3 deep breaths, she closed her eyes, and started to push.

    The crowd backed away as the bar rose steadily. Within only 10 seconds, Cat had benched 250.

    "Well, It's been nice," She said. "But i've got to go home now."

    As she left, the large man stopped her. "Hey, uh, saw what you did back there. Really like your style. So, uh, come back whenever you feel like it, all right?c" He shook her hands. "Name's Dave.

    She smiled. "Nice to meet you, Dave."
