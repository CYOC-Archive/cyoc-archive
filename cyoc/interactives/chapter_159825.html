    Jeff skimmed through his options, wondering what inanimate objects he wanted to be. Looking on, he noticed what appears to be a section that caught his eyes.

    “Gaming Console Section?” he asked when reading that, “Wonder what’s that mean?”

    Soon enough, he clicked on the option and a message appeared that states what this section is about. It says…

    ‘Are you a gamer? Do you like to play video games? Do you have a favorite console you love to play? Ever wonder what it would be like to become the very console you like? Well today is your lucky day. This is the Gaming Console Section. It is where you or someone else will be transformed into a console of both the past and present. Here’s how it goes.

    First: Select a name of the target you want to change, whether it be you or anyone you know.

    Second: Select the game console that you or your target would change into. It can either be old school like a Magnavox or something recent like an Xbox One. You can even decided to become an arcade cabinet or a pinball machine, based on the title of the two. There’s also an option to randomize your choice depending on if you want to take the risk. The choice is up to you.

    Third: “You need to make some extra options on how you can make the changes. Mental, Awareness, sexual activity, reality, timer for transformation, and duration are the options you can make. Just remember a few key things.

    Mental and awareness means that if you want to be yourself within a gaming console. But remember, if you or your target has the memories of your previous lives or be aware of your surroundings, you can’t do anything then just wait until returning to normal.

    For sexual activity, it means that if you are a console and if someone turns you on, it means that, if selected, you or your target can feel sexually aroused. Think of it as masturbating or having sex, only to be for long periods of time for being on.

    When you select to alter reality, the effects will be randomize, be that a place or a time. It could either be in a random house at a present time or a certain year the console was released, or you can be in a store waiting to be purchased, or be a relic in a museum. It depends on where the changes will take you. So be prepared to be teleported randomly if you choose to alter reality.

    And for duration, there is a timer on the dropdown list where you can select a time on how long you cane to remain changed, be that in seconds or years. But there is a selection under the drop down if you want the changes to be permanent, meaning you will remain as a gaming console for the rest of your life. However that’s the choice you make.

    There is also an extra option where you will type in any additional information to make the changes. You need to be creative in order to make the changes more unique.

    That is all for the rules and regulations. All that you can do now is have fun. Thank you for choosing this section and happy gaming.’

    Jeff read through the directions and was amazed by it. A section in the Inanimate Package of the Chronivac where you can turn yourself into a gaming console, arcade, or pinball machine, that is a dream come true. As an added bonus, his entire family play video games for the past couple of generations, depending on what they played. What it would be like to become a console they like. It would be quite an interesting experience. So feeling like this is perfect, he decided to try this section a try. Skimming down, he could see the choices and fill in boxes for this section…

    Name:
    Console:
    Mentality: Y / N
    Awareness: Y / N
    Sexual Activity: Y / N
    Reality Change: Y / N
    Transformation Timer:
    Duration Timer:
    Extra Options:

    Looking to all of this, it does seem simple enough. All he needs to do is select a console he would turn into and enjoy the changes. He could even turn on the sexual activity, even if he’s turned on in console form. The main problem is what console he would turn into? There are a large number of consoles in the past and present, but he doesn’t have a clue on where to start. Hell, the arcade and pinball machines are interesting to try, but he doesn’t know where to begin with that. He thought long and hard until he came up with the perfect choice.

    What do you do now?
