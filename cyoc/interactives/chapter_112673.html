And from it sprouts your long new fox ears.

Leaving a slimy, wet, naked, fiery haired fox boy on the floor. A gas is released which instantly evaporates the strange liquid that's left, drying you off.

The scientist guides you out of the tank and over to a full length mirror where you see yourself and gasp. You are no longer the prince. You don't even recognize yourself. You're not even human anymore! No one would ever believe that you were a prince.

    You look several years younger and absolutely adorable. Not the proud and strong prince you once were. Your muscly and tall phasic has been replaced by this short and slim boy.

    “Do you like your new body?” the scientist asks. You feel your blood boil.

    “What have you done to me!?” You lash out, but the scientist easily catches your hand. He doesn't even seem to have to strain himself to completely overpower you.

    “Oh, I've done a lot. Your appearance is only the tip of the iceberg.”
