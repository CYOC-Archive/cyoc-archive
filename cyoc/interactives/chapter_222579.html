    "Hey! What in the world was that!? You two have some explaining to do here! And why do you have tails!?" Chloe shouted, her friends were about to approach the couple but she stops them. "Hey, be cautious alright, I think that wave of energy came from that baby.." Ana is so confused of the situation while Tim tries to process how the baby just did that.

    Chloe slowly cautiously approached Damon and Monica while keeping her distance and having her eyes focused on the baby.

    "Well!? Come on just shout it out to us! Because I am not getting near you guys and that baby after what just happend!" Chloe said, still being cautious of the baby.
    "Ah.. Well.. Umm, we can explain this.." Damon said, Monica doesn't know how to explain the situation. "Can we though? I mean, we have no idea what other powers this baby has."
    "Well!? What!? Cause we are so confused at what just happend! And why are you merpeople!?" Tim yelled.
    "Well.. we found this merbaby who was alone on the beach and we suddenly transformed into merpeople.. Yeah.. Guess you can say that this baby used magic to transform us.." Damon explained.
    "So can you guys transform back or something?" Ana stated with concern.
    "We don't know, We were trying to get underwater to avoid being detected, I mean if anyone else knows about this, we might be turned into experimental lab rats in some sort of science lab.."
    "Maybe it wont be a bad thing, Doctors might try to help us with the situation, right?" Monica stated.
    "Yeah but they might just turn us into experiments which would be the worse outcome that would happen, and besides, if they find out that it was the baby that did this, it would be a horrible fate for the baby.." Damon said as he watch the baby filled with confusion.
    "If merpeople are real, since they are now that there is proof, maybe they can help you guys turn back?" Tim said as everyone looked at him.
    "Well that could help, but how will they find them? Remember, the ocean can be dangerous and filled with creatures we have no idea about, and going to the hospital wont be a good idea either." Ana said concerned for her friends.
    "Well, you guys cant find them on the beach, you'd have to take the risk and go to the sea."
    "We better get going before anyone else finds us here." Stated Damon as he took the baby and move forward to the sea, Monica soon joined him, Damon then tried if he can breath on water, he then finds out that he can and it feels so refreshing, Monica felt the same.
    "Hey! if you guys need to talk to us then just watch if we arrived on the beach! I have a date tonight with Sarah and I am not going to miss it it! Especially because I have been planning this for months!" Chloe said as she said goodbye to Monica and Damon, leaving Ana and Tim there.
    "We have to get going to, be careful you two and stay away from dangerous creatures!" Ana said as she left the beach with Tim.
    "So.. where do we start?" Damon said.
