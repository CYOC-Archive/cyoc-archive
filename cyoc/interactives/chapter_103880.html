    You decide to stop for a little to examine the remote, as you've only really used it a few times. "It's a shame this thing didn't come with a manual or something." you say looking at the remote inquisitively. You run your fingers along the side and feel a crease on the right side of the remote. "What the hell's this you say?" as you dig a finger nail into the groove.

    Just then a click is heard, as a side panel snaps out of the remote. "Well that's new." you say examine the new section of the remote. The panel has a screen that reads:

    Scan Input Required. Please scan intended object for further operation.

    Wondering what the remote means by scan you examine the new buttons on the remote, finding the one labeled "Scan". Deciding to try it out, you scan a nearby man on his cellphone. The screen now reads:

    Input Entered. Scan Complete. Object Information Below:

    Subject #1
    Species: Human
    Name: Gregory Hall
    Age: 27 Years Old
    Height: 5 Foot 10 Inches
    Weight: 164 Pounds
    Sexual Orientation: Primarily Heterosexual
    Ethnicity: 80% Caucasian, 20% Asian

    Is Further Input Required?

    "What the hell does it mean by is further input required?" you ask perplexed. Looking to find this out you decide to scan another person. Eyeing a young girl playing with her phone on another bench, you decide to scan her. The screen now reads:

    Subject#2
    Species: Human
    Name: Amanda Chang
    Age: 16
    Height: 5 Foot 4 Inches
    Weight: 118 Pounds
    Sexual Orientation: Primarily Heterosexual
    Ethnicity: 90% Asian 10% Caucasian

    Is Further Input Required? Or Is Scan Complete?

    "Scan complete? I guess that means I can swap them now?" you ask curiously. "But how does this one work? It looks like it's not just point and click like the other method." you say processing these new revelations.

    Examining the buttons you notice arrow keys that move along the screen. Pressing the enter button highlights the selected item and brings up the same item on the second subject. Playing around with the screen you swap around a few different items between both subjects, until the screen reads:

    Subject#1
    Species: Human
    Name: Gregory Nam
    Age: 21 Years Old
    Height: 5 Foot 6 Inches
    Weight: 144 Pounds
    Sexual Orientation: Primarily Heterosexual
    Ethnicity: 70% Asian, 30% Caucasian

    Subject#2
    Species: Human
    Name: Amanda Hall
    Age: 22
    Height: 5 Foot 8 Inches
    Weight: 138 Pounds
    Sexual Orientation: Primarily Heterosexual
    Ethnicity: 60% Caucasian, 40% Asian

    Input Correct?

    Reviewing the screen you press on enter on the remote and watch as the changes begin to occur. The man begins to regress in age, as his facial features begin to change to that of a young man of Korean descent. His body shifts as his he becomes shorter and more stocky, despite the weight loss.

    On the other side of the spectrum, the girl begins to age at a steady rate, passing through puberty in a matter of a few minutes. Her once black hair begins to lighten into a dirty blond, coinciding with her features becoming more akin to a person of European descent. Her body grows taller, as she begins to develop a healthy set of curves for a young woman of her age.

    Satisfied with the changes you continue watching as the now more age-aligned victims, swap numbers to meet up for a date later in the week.

    "Well looks like I just did them a favor" you laugh.

    Looking back at the remote you realize the potential it now carries, being able to swap characteristics as well as body parts. "I wonder what else this thing can do?" you ask yourself.
