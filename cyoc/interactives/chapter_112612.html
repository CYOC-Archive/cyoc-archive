Rob used that time to purchase a Ken doll - a Ken doll he intended to imitate and replace so he could scare his friend.

    Rob sighed as he looked at white candies in his hand. The candies that would 'take away' or reduce his height. He was a little nervous about shrinking down to under a foot, about the size of a Barbie doll. But he looked up at the clock and saw his time was running out. It was now or never.

    Rob chewed a handful of the candies.

    Nothing seemed to happen at first. He waited a few minutes and considered eating a few more. Then he felt his stomach lurch like he was in a fast moving elevator and the room around him seemed to expand rapidly. After the initial drop, the shrinking process slowed and Rob watched with fascination as the world around him slowly got larger and larger. Soon his head was level with his small kitchen table, then his couch. It didn't hurt. It just tingled a little and he felt strangely full of energy. When he was just below the coffee table, he stopped shrinking.

    He walked over to the Ken doll box and saw that he was still a little too big, about two feet tall. It was kind of fun and he thought he might like to try being this short a little later. But for now he had a job to do. He picked up a few more candies off the floor. They looked bigger now, and he almost had to use two hands to hold them. Nervously he nibbled at one, carefully gauging his shrinkage and stopping when he was just over 11 inches.

    "Perfect, " he squeaked, as he slid the remaining candy under the couch. He slipped on the discarded Ken doll clothes, now a perfect fit. He walked to the Ken doll box and tried to open the lid. He was surprised at how hard it was to open, and realized how much weaker and more vulnerable he was at this size. He began to have second thoughts again. Maybe this wasn't such a good idea.

    But just as he had the lid open he heard a knock on the door. There was no backing out now. He slid into the box and pulled the lid shut. He did his best to stay still as he watched his friend Mike enter the apartment through the plastic covering of his packaging.
