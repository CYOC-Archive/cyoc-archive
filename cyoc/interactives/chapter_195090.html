    After your transformation you stood there naked in the cold room waiting to be released.

    A nearby mirror showed you exactly what you looked like now. You appeared just as you would have had you been born female naturally.

    You had semi long brown hair that touched your shoulders, modest B-Cup breasts, you were about 5’6, with a petite figure. Rather pale skin. You would most certainly say you were cute, but by no means were you a sexy bombshell or anything. If you had to be female this was probably the best case scenario for you honestly.

    A few minutes passed as the large screen in front of you turned on as the image of an older woman appeared. You recognized her from your school. She was an older Asian woman, probably mid 40s she was a special counselor at your school. Helped recent transformation students readjust.

    “Hi there Conner, My name is Mrs. Cheng.” She said waving to you.

    You felt awkward with her staring at you naked but you did your best to smile and wave back.

    “Oh don’t be alarmed I see all sorts every day, no need to be shy. Now I know we have never spoken officially so let me introduce myself. My job is to assign new roles and body types to new transformed females. I help former males
    Like yourself adjust to their new bodies and we discuss your future moving forward.” Mrs. Cheng said.

    “Oh…ok…so do you know how long I am going to be stuck like this?” You asked.

    “Well…Conner, that’s why we need to talk. It seems I’m your case…well…there is an exception.” Mrs. Cheng said.

    You began to worry as you looked back to her…

    “Well our school is meant to have a strong variety and balance of all student types. From the high end, to the low end, and everything in between.” Mrs. Cheng began to explain.

    You didn’t like where this was going.

    “Right now we have had a severe lack of what you might call…well…sexually passionate females. More and more women are going to college and getting educated with good jobs.” She said.

    “So…isn’t that a good thing?” You asked confused.

    “Well…it is…for them. Not so much for many hard working and wealthy men who just want a happy and sexy housewife to raise their kids and family. It’s affecting our population numbers more and more every year.” Mrs. Cheng said.

    Things were starting to take a turn for the worse for you it seemed…

    “Well based on your test scores and all of our data on you. We think you would be a prime target for our sexual enhancement program.” Mrs. Cheng said.

    “Excuse me? What does that mean?” You asked in a nervous tone.

    “It means we will adjust the transformation on you. Enhancing all your sexual features. We will then direct your education in a more…well productive manner. You will focus on things like physical health and mental health. Maintaining a healthy mind and body. When you graduate many very men will want to meet you.” Mrs. Cheng said.

    “Wait…so you are just grooming me to be some…trophy wife?” You shouted back.

    “Don’t look at it like this. You will fulfill a very important role in society. Everyone does their part nowadays.” Mrs. Cheng said.

    “What if I refuse?” You shouted back.

    “Well…here is the thing…we already accepted several bids from wealthy investors so…that isn’t really an option. Trust me you are going to love your new life once we get you adjusted!” Mrs Cheng said.

    The screen suddenly went blank as the machine from earlier began to awaken again…
