We know who’s the bully over here!”

If I find out that you’re acting like a bully, then I will cast a spell and remove your muscles!” the Madame said in a stern voice. “I don’t give muscle and strength to another so they can bully people! Do you hear me? Plus, you need to go to the gym every day from now on to work out and to maintain those big muscles!”

    “I… I understand, Madame! I won’t become a bully! I promise!” Kaden promised her.

    Then, the Madame waved her hand and Jerry moved again.

    “Oh! Umm…” Jerry sighed as he adjusted his glasses. He looked around, feeling a bit confused. Then, he stopped and walked back to the bleachers. “I need to get home after the show and continue my medical research!”
