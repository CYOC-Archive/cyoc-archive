    Suddenly, the Imp split in half, and the two halves each became a different imp. Then the two imps split themselves again so there were four of them, then split again, somehow only resulting in seven imps.

    "Oh, this is gonna suck," Kiki said.

    "Wait, I must have a card or something," Natasha said.

    But as she pulled out her hand, three imps leapt at her, knocking her into the sand. The same happened to Kiki. The one imp who wasn't holding down one of the girls walked between them. It gave a mock salute to the other players and started to sink into the sand.

    All of the imps sank, and Natasha and Kiki went with them. The girls screamed, mostly obscenities from Natasha, but within seconds, the desert was silent again.

    "Well shit," Dixie said.

    "I am really REALLY fired," Sara said.

    ---------------------------------------------------
    "Natasha, you awake?" Kiki asked as she struggled to open her eyes.

    "Barely," Natasha replied.

    "I think we're in the catacombs," Kiki said, still effectively blind.

    "That's good," Natasha said.

    "Hey Natasha?" Kiki asked, trying to take a step forward.

    "Yeah?" Natasha asked.

    "Can you see me? Because I'm having some trouble seeing much at all. Or doing much at all," Sara said.

    "I can't... Oh. Now I can see you," Natasha said.

    "What? What's wrong?" Sara asked, still struggling to open her eyes.

    "You're tied down to something. Your face is in it, so I don't know how you're talking," Natasha said.

    Kiki wiggled for a moment, feeling the ropes rub against her wrists and ankles, "Are you tied up too?"

    "Yeah, but I can wriggle out. I grew up with brothers, so it isn't the hardest thing I've had to get out of," Natasha said.

    After a few minutes of nothing, Natasha broke the silence, "I'm free. I'll untie you."

    "Thanks," Kiki said. Moments later, Kiki was free. When she stood up to face Natasha, the Irish girl covered her mouth in shock.

    "What?" Kiki asked.

    Natasha glanced around for a moment before pointing to a large mirror. Kiki walked over to see what had happened. Written in large black letters across her cheeks were the words 'Free Service' with arrows pointing to her mouth.

    "Oh, I'm gonna kill them," Kiki said.

    "Sounds good to me. Let's get out of here," Natasha said, opening the door at the far side of the room. When she did, another brown card appeared, blocking the doorway, "Oh, come on."
