    After a few turns left and behind some big oak trees you reach a huge facotory like building. The grey haired zoo employee unlocks a small door with a chip card from his pocket.

    "Please come in and find out why this zoo is special. Actually VERY special".

    When the door silently closes behind you you suddenly feel as this whole thing is not a good idea at all. But your friends Lydia, Rachel and Philip do not seem to sense any danger.

    "No I am curious!" Rachel says. "What is the big secret?" Lydia adds with a giggle.

    "You´re about to find out" says the warden and stops smiling. In a quick motion he draws a pistollike gun. Only with a set of darts in it.

    "Jesus! You must be joking - right?" Rachels question is rather unsure.

    "Not at all my girl!" and wile speaking these words he quickly shoots Rachel, Lydia and Philip with these strange darts.

    When he turns to you you jump towards the warden and luckily hit him offguard. The older man looses the grip on the gun and falls. You bought yourselves some seconds.

    "Quick. Let´s get the hell out of here!" you manage to blurt out in your confusion. Your friends and yourself manage to get out of the building. But as you run away you hear the warden shouting after you: "You won´t get far. You will soon be back and be fed and milked by me" Even when you gained some distance and you are among other visitors you think that you still here a laughter like from a mad scientist.

    Finally you stop running and the four of you are gathering next to a souvenir shop of the zoo.

    "What was that?" asked the obviously confused Lydia while absently rubbing her heart-shaped but where she was hit with the dart.

    Philip produced his dart. Coming to his senses the usually calm student of economics feels rage. "I am going to sue this idiot! Let´s go to the police. Or even better. To the management and get him fired!"

    "I don´t want to spoil the party" Rachel stammers. "But Lydia, look at her ears!"

    You wonder what Rachel is talking about. But looking at Rachel it´s rather obvious: The ears of the pretty young girl are not in the shape they were only minutes ago. There where fine brownish hairs on the outside and they looked more like cows ears really...

    What do you do now?
