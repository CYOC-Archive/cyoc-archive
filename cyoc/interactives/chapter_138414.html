    Amy and Taylor skirted the row of shops slowly, stopping here and there to admire the new stock, before founding an interesting store, manned by a girl with long blonde hair and a pleasant smile, that ushered them inside.

    “So what does two lovely ladies seek out in this place? Are you here only as passing visitors or do you want to buy something?”

    “We’re here to buy of course, I was interested to try out some dresses along my friend here, but if you keep in stock pants and shirts they will do too”

    “Splendid! Now for starters can I suggest a pair of earrings for accessories? Fact is that today is the grand opening and I have free earrings for my clients until depletion, so if you want you can have a pair each”

    The changeling teenagers eagerly accepted the gifts, a pair of big gold colored rings each that found their new place on their fuzzy ears, prompting a new wave of changes to start anew. Their faces grew a soft layer of fur in moments, black around the deep black eyes and various shades of brown around the rest, while the mouths and noses merged and pushed out to form a herbivore muzzle.

    “As I thought, you both look amazing with them. May I suggest other wares that I think will accentuate your figures?”

    After the positive answers the clerk displayed her entire stock, handing new clothes and accessories to the young girls, hooked to the attendant that had far more malicious plans for the two. With every change of wardrobe more and more of their true selves slipped away, leaving a clear path for the emergence of a pair of gazelle, as every worn item was a step toward a life in the zoo. Shaved legs and arms made way for the creeping fur, followed by the upper body, now sporting a stark white pelt. The feet hardened and turned into black cloven hooves, and under the watchful gaze of the woman the first signs of discomfort started to show on them, the odd attempt to decrease the amount of fur covered by rolling up the sleeves or the occasional scratching of a particularly stifled zone of coat. They were almost ready.

    The attendant hid a smile behind one of her hands when the two animal girls found themselves with a pair of hooves and continued unperturbed their stroll, leaving behind the pairs of shoes that clattered uselessly on the floor forgotten, as the still worn socks muffled the sounds of their hoof beats.

    Convinced that the moment was right the woman smirked and pounced metaphorically on the defenceless preys before her with hidden glee, wrong-footing the two young girls easily.

    “Can I ask a question to you both? Why did you came here fully clothed? It’s not uncomfortable with all that fur?”

    “I’m sorry what?” The two girls blink in unison and turn to each other with a bemused expression “Well, yes, it’s pretty impractical to move around like this, we wear clothes only for informal occasions normally, any other ordinary day we spend it with only our pelt. Now that I think about it, why did we dress completely? I can’t seem to remember…”

    “You know, this is strange, I am sure that we had a good reason” Amy interjects with a frown “I’ve got it on the tip of my tongue… “

    All according to plan.

    The clerk gained an odd glint in her eyes as she proposes to the two friends “You know, the shops of the mall can keep the garments for both and then send it to your houses” Before the girls can object the woman continues “This is not the first time one of our furry costumers inadvertently visited us fully clothed and asked if it was possible to leave everything to a clerk for safekeeping, we even have an internal bank for that very reason, some costumers didn’t want to carry around pursues or wallets and so opened an account to their name. Strictly speaking it’s recommended for you, if you want to be completely unburdened”

    “Mh, I don’t know what to say” Taylor seems doubtful, but Amy readily agree to the proposal.

    “Sign me up please, I feared that I would have to wait until tonight to discard everything. This is a very thoughtful service”

    Taylor huffs but concedes to her friend “Fine, you win, this outfit is also too itchy for my taste”

    Unconcerned about decorum or the fact that they were in the middle of the shop the teenagers disrobed, not with a small amount of difficulty because of the large antlers, removing the last articles that still clung to their changing frames. Amy’s breasts wobbled as the girl removed her bra and skirt in one fell swoop, then helped Taylor with the shirt wedged in one of her horns. The two stunning anthro gazelle had very alluring bodies, and even the employee had a rosy blush after this sudden stripping show, and it didn’t help that the two friends were much more exotic in the new forms. The only remaining characteristics that separated them from their home in a specific enclosure were the still human hands, even if covered in fur, and the upright posture.

    Amy collected all the various garments and politely handed them over to the clerk while Taylor stretched her arms and legs, pleased about her freedom, before thanking the woman along Amy “I was skeptical up until now but my friend was right, the skirt and shirt felt… off, for lack of a better term”

    “I know right? Thank miss for the help”

    The attendant shrugged and accepted the bundle of clothes , before passing an already prepared waiver to the animal girls “We try to accommodate all our client’s needs, to provide the most pleasant tour of our premises. Sign here to agree and leave here the superfluous things, we will take good care of them for you”
