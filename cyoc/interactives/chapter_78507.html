    Foolish King into sort of dragon-lady thing

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 10 years ago A S TG O

    Now the scholars know that all those folktales going about amongst the peasantry
    nowadays ( lets say 1360AD )began about a century and a half earlier when some
    Dutch bard named Zatikhoeven got the tale
    of the Sovereignty of Eire and the heroes who tried to woo her when she was in dragon form
    jumbled up in his romance.

    In Zatikhoeven's version a princess named Elidia ,who used to make rather vulgar jokes
    about the size of a man's "wand" made one foul remark too many and got
    turned into a foul mouthed dragon as a result.

    I suppose that taught Elidia some manners ( or not )

    Anyways the Dragon confronting this foolish King just happened to be the Sovereignty of
    both the East and West Kingdoms.

    Normally she was a gentle looking blond haired maiden,perhaps looking like a 20 year old,but in actuality as old as you'd expect the
    Spirit of the Land ( Lady Liberty,Britannia,et al ) to be.

    And with all the trouble her nastier side had been coming out
    in the form of those horns,reptillian eyes,fire breath,etc

    If she got angrier,she'd be sprouting scales,getting bigger,going on all fours,etc

    Not a good thing for all those mere mortals near her if she got really mad.

    Now it just so happened that the Kings of the Kingdom that had
    been before it split into the East and West Kingdom's had made their
    union with the Lady of the Land by entering her cave,and hours later
    coming out in a happy daze and holding the Staff of Power.

    The Lady of the Land generally got a pregnancy out of the deal,and eventually a new hero.

    Not surprisingly the practice had been discontinued long ago and forgotten about.

    However the foolish King just happened to be holding a Staff of Power,what he thought
    was a mere magical wand.

    And the Dragon/Lady of the Land ,feeling her eminent transformation
    into her nastier reptillian self,could tranfer her tf to a King holding a Staff of Power.

    Using the human as a sort of heatsink to redirect the tf energies,if you will.

    So when the foolish King made another insulting remark about this Dragon,
    the Lady of the Land could fell the pressure in her face to push out into a snout,her horns
    began to creak and lengthen and she went from 7 feet tall to 7 feet 1 inch tall.

    So she rushed forward ,easily passed through his wards and seized the Staff of Power
    in one taloned hand,bit down on his neck with her lengthening teeth,held her
    other enlarging hand over the foolish Kings face

    The Lady of the Land felt the dragonish change gush out of her own body and into his,and
    when his face beganto push away into a small but growing snout.

    Being of mortal flesh,the formerly human King didn't transform as readily as a divine beings meat would,
    but the change was drastic enough.

    Where the foolish King used to be now squated a female creture about the size
    of a baby elephant,with a long neck,the beginnings of spikes down its lengthy backbone.a pair of budding wings,lots of pink hide and a head that looked roughly like a horse with sharp teeth.

    Around this emerging dragoness that used to be the foolish King lay the torn split garments from her former Kinghood.

    The Lady of the Land was her normal sweet damsel self again,and as she held the Staff
    of Power she had taken back,she addressed the stunned new dragon thing.

    She now carried within herself the same transformational headache the Lady of the Land and her
    kind carried> get mad and you turn into a dragon

    And since dragons naturally get angry,the reverse transformation wasn't too easy.

    Then the Dragon/Lady of the Land waved the Staff of Power and vanished.

    What do you do now?
