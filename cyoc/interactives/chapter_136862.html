    Your name is Wendy and you had heard a lot of things about the new zoo from your friends who had visited there before. You had come there mostly out of curiosity, you had heard that they had animals that most Zoos didn't have and exhibits all over that had some of your favorite animals.

    You couldn't wait to visit the Zoo, so on suggestion from your best friend you decided to make a day out of it. And once you were inside the Zoo, you picked up a map and looked over the options to decide where you should go first.

    The options:

    The African Savannah.
    The Primate House.
    The American Plains.
    The Reptile House.
    The Insectarium.
    Big Cat Country.
    The Pacific Northwest Forests.
    The Deep Jungle.
    The Aviary.
    The Petting Zoo.
    The Dog Kennel.
    The Stables.

    That was weird you thought to yourself, there were a few things you didn't think would be in a Zoo like the kennel or the stables. But you figured that it was probably for locals that kept dogs or horses and couldn't keep them here for some reason, maybe it would be interesting to at least check out the stables, you always liked horses.

    You decided to go to...
