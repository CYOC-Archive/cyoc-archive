"What did you do?" trying to yell, yet it only comes out as a soft, aristocratic whisper, loud enough to be heard in room, but not outside. "Why is my voice unlike what it was before?!" trying to yell, scream, say something that isn't proper as Rhianna looks on with some satisfaction.

    "What next~" sing-songing almost as she looks at the newly forming Goth. "Maybe add some color to your pale self? We do want you to be monochromatic so, I think that should be fun." Even Rhianna calling Janine in her direction doesn't seem like who she is and, as she realizes it, her eyes widen, looking at the cheerleader in terror. "Y-you c-can change how I t-think too?" whispering in some horror.
