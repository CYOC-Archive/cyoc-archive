    Planet? Peter isn't through yet.

    added by TheMM 17 years ago BM S

    Peter woke after his massive growth was over and saw Joce coming closer to his head whispering something.

    "I wish you were still asleep"said Joce.

    Peter fell back into a deep sleep and relaxed.

    "Hmm, well now what to do with him?" she thought outloud."First I want to be able to kill him, so I wish that Peter's head was a normal size."

    Peter's head as it had grown to be in proportion to his mighty body shrank smaller and smaller as it became normal, well at least for someone without all his size anyways.

    "I wish Peter was awake."Joce wished."And that also he couldn't move his body."

    Peter reawoke feeling much more powerful then before. He tried to flex his bicep so he could see it but he couldn't move his arm.

    "Well my planet sized man, how are we this morning?" said Joce who was suddenly in front of him.

    "I-I feel so strong." Peter stammered.

    "Well you oughta, you are the size of a planet!"Joce responded.

    "Pl-pl-planet????"Peter barely replied.

    (Authors Note:after this line and all the lines after, it wont say "Joce said, Peter said" I'm too lazy to do that but it goes in this order Joce (next line) then Peter, Joce, Peter, etc. this is to just tell you who's talking when.)

    "Well sure, you think I wasn't going to make you the biggest guy ON the planet were you? I made you THE planet!"Joce told him.

    "I'm the planet Earth?"

    "No silly, I just made you the size of a planet, don't you like being this huge?"

    "Well yes, but I can't move."

    "Oh sorry, I didn't want you to move so I could get closer to your head, but anyways I wish you could move your arms.Oh and first, could you bring one of your hands over here so I could sit on it?"

    "Sure...Wow Joce I feel... so powerful, I can't belive how large I am."

    "You're not through yet, you're my man so I'm not about to make you stop growing now.

    "Wha.."

    "I wish that you would be grow by absorbing anything you touched into yourself... except me."

    Peter felt strange, like he had to grow, like his life depended on it. He lifted Joce up on his mighty shoulder and tried to move over to the moon.

    "I, I can't move over there."

    "Oh, how about I just teleport us there."

    Joce waved her hand and both of them appeared next to the moon. Peter placed his giant hands on it and felt the moon slowly shrinking while his body kept getting larger and larger. Eventually the moon was no more, but Peter still wanted to grow larger. Peter noticed that some of the planets and such were slightly moving towards him, he was so large he had his own gravity!

    "What planet is next Joce?"

    "Hmm how about Earth?"

    "But that's where we used to live... But hey you want me to grow, I'm gonna grow."

    Joce teleportted the both of them next to earth and Peter placed his hands on the planet and felt all of it's power, much much more then the moon because Earth felt like it was alive. Eventually Peter got to the core and felt it's warmth which also became his body as well.
    Now Peter could truly start to see the planets moving towards him. He decided to let them come to him. After who knows how long Peter had absorbed Mars, the Asteroid Belt, and was beginning on Jupiter. All the mass Peter had gained was starting to even draw the Sun to him.

    "Well, time for the sun and then the serious growth begins, ok my muscle god?"

    "Yes Joce, to the sun!"

    Joce waved her hand and both appeared next to the sun, the giant ball of hydrogen gas. Peter instead of placing his hands on it he wrapped most of his body on it. The sun quickly was absorbed into his body.

    "Now, time for the major growth."
