    Vicky was having a real problem focusing today. She had a project to work on for her final, but the events of last night had left her even more confused and frustrated. She had no idea how the Lesbianizer had affected the cheerleaders, and she really wanted to know.

    "Excuse me, Vicky?"

    Vicky looked up to see a cute blonde, maybe 5' 5" or so, who looked to be about 16. She was wearing sunglasses and clothing that looked too big on her frame. Something about her struck her as very familiar...

    "Georgia?!"

    "Yeah...I know...something's happened to me. Look, I know you have no reason to help me, I've been a complete bitch to you, and I was way out of line at the party. But you're the smartest person I know...and I need help." The blonde hung her head dejectedly.

    Of course, Vicky knew what had happened (even if she was confused by the results), so she didn't immediately tell Georgia to go fuck herself. "What happened?"

    Georgia related her story, about how she'd found herself shrinking. "I know, people don't shrink, at least, not like this...but it's happening to me anyways!"

    "I...well there are some tests I can do, I guess, but wouldn't a doctor..."

    "No! I mean...I'm sorry. I just don't want Mother to find out."

    Vicky frowned. "Why is that a problem?"

    Georgia found a stool to sit on. "You don't understand my Mom. All my life, she wanted me to be a carbon copy of her. When I had a late growth spurt at 17, she totally freaked and said it was my fault somehow. That I'd never end up with a rich guy if I was tall." She shook her head. "Who knows, maybe she'd be happy...but I'm tired of her abuse and trying to control my life. Maybe it would be easier if I shrank away to nothing..."

    Georgia sounded really sad, and Vicky wasn't sure what to think. "Wait, you're still shrinking?"

    Georgia nodded. "It seems to happen in spurts, not gradually." As if on cue, she shrank another few inches, looking a tiny bit younger. Two pairs of socks or not, this caused her right shoe to fall off of her foot, hitting the floor with a thud. "See what I mean?"

    If Georgia really wanted to shrink away to nothing, could that happen? She was supposed to turn into her ideal female...but since Vicky hadn't tested the Lesbianizer, she had no idea what would happen. "Say, Georgia...have you been feeling different, I mean, otherwise?"

    Georgia blushed. "Yeah, I've been stupidly horny, to be honest."

    "Hm. Thinking about boys, huh?"

    "Uh..." Georgia didn't really want to admit this, but she was over a barrel. She'd gone to Vicky for help, after all. "No."

    "No?" Vicky pressed.

    "Ok, so...I mean if you tell everyone this...I guess it doesn't matter if you can't stop what's happening to me. But I've been thinking about girls."

    "I...see. I guess that's really strange for you."

    "Not as much as you'd think. Honestly, I've always been at least...somewhat bi. But I never told anyone. My Mother would freak out, and who knows what kind of reputation I'd get. Stupid, I know." The pretty blonde sighed.

    "No, not that stupid." Vicky didn't believe she was admitting this, but... "I'm gay too."

    "You are?! I guess that's not too surprising, I just assumed you weren't interested in relationships. Could get in the way of your plans to take over the world." Georgia smiled ruefully. "Since we're being open, I think a lot of the reason I've been horrible to you is that I've been jealous. I'm not all that smart, so the only thing I have is my looks. You're going to be world famous and successful."

    "You...jealous of me?" Vicky blinked. "You could have anyone you wanted!"

    "Sure, sure. Sex at the drop of a hat. I know you think I'm a total slut, and I probably deserve that. But when my looks fade, you'll still have your smarts." She shrank a tiny bit more, but didn't seem to notice.

    "You're jealous me...Georgia, you're not...attracted to me, are you?"

    Georgia covered her face with her hands. "Yes. Like, a lot. You're so darned adorable!"

    One the one hand, this was surprising. On the other...Vicky had similar feelings for Georgia. "Well...I'm not sure what to do with that."

    "I know. I'm so sorry for bullying you. I'm fucked up. I'm sure there's no way we could ever be friends now...but if you can just tell me what's going to happen to me, I promise, I'll do anything to make it up to you."

    "Anything?" Vicky asked.

    "Yes. No matter how humiliating." Georgia nodded.
