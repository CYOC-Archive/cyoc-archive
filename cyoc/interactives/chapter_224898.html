    Not A Child Spirit!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy 2 months ago O Mental Ghost

    After Mandy asked the spirit for its name, the planchet starts to move once again, first is moves to the letter “J”, then “O”, then “H” and finally comes to a stop on the letter “N”, spelling out the name “John”.

    “So, John,” Lizzie asks next, “how old are you?”

    Again the planchet starts to move after a while, first moving to the number “1” and comes to rest on the number “0”.

    “You are ten,” Ashley asks, the girls then gasp when the planchet moves to “yes”.

    The girls look at each other, “this sure is a creepy game,” Lizzie says.

    “Yeah,” Ashley agrees, “and I can’t shake off the feeling that we are being watched.”

    “I think this game is a lot of fun,” Mandy says, “and so far nothing bad has happened, so let’s continue asking question.”

    What the girls are unaware of is that they have conjured a demon, who is pretending to be an innocent child spirit, just to win their trust. The longer the girls interact with the board, the stronger the demon becomes, since the demon plans on staying in the human realm, the stronger it wants to become, to prevent anyone banning it back into hell. So the Demon continues to play along, answering all the questions like a child would do, feeling itself becoming stronger with every passing second, ‘I need to find a host soon,’ it thinks to itself, ‘that way, when they say “good-bye”, I will not be pulled back into my realm, and I’ll be able to hide in plain sight. I don’t want to be one of these girls, but I can sense that there is a young boy nearby. He’ll become my host, once I’ve taken over his body, I can do whatever I want and no one will be able to stop me!’

    The Demon heads to Rick’s room, finding the boy lying on his bed, when the Demon sees the boy it licks its lips, ‘what a nice young body,’ it thinks to itself, ‘soon it’ll be mine,’ the creature of darkness then moves closer to the boy, stopping right by Rick’s side.

    Rick suddenly feels a shiver run down his spine, the sense of being watched, the boy looks up from his phone, straight at the Demon, but unable to see the being.

    The Demon grins to itself, ‘time to possess him,’ it thinks to itself, the Demon turns to a invisible mist which starts to ooze itself into the boy’s nostrils.

    Rick feels a sudden pressure in his nose and instinctively inhales hard through his nose, unaware that he is helping the Demon enter him. The pressure soon subsides, but soon after Rick feels his head starting to hurt. The young boy clutches his head and groans, “what the hell,” he asks himself.

    “Hehe,” a creepy voice inside his head suddenly laughs, “hello… Rick! Thanks for your body, I promise I’ll take good care of it!”

    Before Rick is able to react he feels as if his spirit is being swallowed by a darkness, he feels himself loosing his grip on himself as something else starts to take over.

    Suddenly Rick’s mouth curls into an evil grin, “finally,” the boy says, “I am free of that prison. This body is truly perfect! Hehe… now, let’s see what my body was doing before I took over!”

    The Demon, who is now Rick, looks down at the phone in his hand, “hm… looks like I was about to do something with this App,” he mumbles to himself, he then grins, “I could have some fun with this App!”
