    The Greatest Home Gym on Earth

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 8 years ago I O

    Chris looked around his living room. He was looking for something to give him an idea of what to wish for next. A thousand thoughts were in his head and he found it hard to pick just one. Suddenly, Chris turned to the living room table where he noticed something in the newspaper. The ad section was opened. Looking at the paper Chris saw an ad for the opening of a new gym in town. Immediately, the answer came to him.

    “I wish my entire basement would become the greatest home gym ever made. My home gym will have a wide array of weights, machines and other gym equipment. The equipment will always be in perfect condition. A shower/locker room is next to the gym. In the lockers there will always be clean white tank tops, black shorts, white sneakers and white socks. I will never suffer any injuries when working out down there. The gym won’t cost me anything. Finally, I want my home gym to be better than a real gym.”

    “Granted Master,” Eden said as she clapped her hands.

    A bright light appeared from the other side of the basement door, and then disappeared. Curiously, Chris walked down the stairs to the door. As soon as he looked inside Chris’s eyes widen, and the biggest smile he ever gave appeared on his face.

    In front of him was the most amazing home gym he, or anyone for that matter, had ever seen. Its’ just as I imaged it, he thought. No! Its’ even better! He was right. His new home gym was simply incredible. The condition of all the weights, machines, beaches and other equipment were better than brand new. There was no hint of dust or dirt anywhere. The place smelled brand new. It was like the whole thing had just been set-up. This was true, at least from Chris’s perspective.

    Looking around Chris saw a rack of dumbbells on the left wall. The dumbbells went from 5-100 pounds; he then noticed mirrors on the wall above the rack. In the same area were barbells, weight places, benches, etc.

    Chris then realized that the room was split-in-half. The weight machines were on one side and the cardio machines on the other. A cable-cross machine was the last weight machine followed by a treadmill.

    Turning to his right, Chris found the entrance to the locker/shower room. Walking in he saw six rows of lockers and found the clothes he wished for in each one. The showers themselves were located at the right of the room.

    As Chris was examining his new home gym, Eden came downstairs to check on her new master. Spotting Chris in the locker room she walked in and said, “So Master, are you pleased with your wish?”

    “You bet Eden!” Chris answered back enthusiastically. “This is what I have always dreamt of having. Now I can work out whenever I want to, and in a gym better than any other gym.”

    “Yea, I can see why you would prefer your place over others,” Eden stated as she looked around. She too was admiring the place. “So what is your next wish, Master?”

    “Please Eden, call me Chris. And want to work out a little bit. You know, give my new gym a whirl. So, can I hold off making any more wishes for now?”

    Eden happily replied, “Of course you can Mas…Chris! It’s your decision. I will return to my lamp until you are ready.”

    Eden turned back into smoke and disappeared. Chris immediately opened up a locker and started putting on the clothes. A minute later, Chris walked out wearing a white tank top, black shorts, sneakers and white socks. Smiling, Chris started to stretch as he prepared himself for his work out. “Well, time to get started!” Chris exclaimed happily as he headed into his new home gym.
