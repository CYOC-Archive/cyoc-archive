    "Now then, for the final touches..."

    Said the heavy-built man, who waved his hands and fingers to sew up the clothing ribbon tag right at the back of the neck inside of the hoodie. With the size "?XL" in bold letters on the front and some extra info behind it. He is wearing only a purple robe (and maybe one of his own undergarments he made) since any store he goes to doesn't have the right size for his body type, or at least in a cloth material that he is comfortable with wearing. Silk being his favorite.

    Which is understandable, since his body is almost nothing but flab. If it weren't for the fact he still has muscles beneath it all, not to mention his own magic powers, both help him move and work to his heart's content.

    That's right.
    This fat man is in fact a wizard!

    明希 天願 is his name.
    [Aki Tengan is his name.]

    Of course, this wizard is just enjoying his lot in life. Making, sewing, and creating clothes for those who can't fit in clothes made for skinny folks. Or just can't feel comfortable in a skinny-dominated world.

    Then it all changed when he found out about Pride Month and, with it, a flag that shows pride for being fat! While elated by this, he also came to an epiphany. There are even skinny people who love being with or even want to become overweight.

    And like any happy, wish-fulfilling wizard that Aki is, he wants to make clothes for them.


    -I-

    "And, done!" Aki finishes the Fat Pride flag hoodie he was making.

    The Fat Pride flag resembles having five strips of colors across the flag. Both black borders on the top and the bottom, then strawberry pink second to the top, vanilla white at the middle, then chocolate brown just below the white and above the second black border, and finally, a red heart just at the middle of the flag. Completing the look. Basically, it's a Napoleon ice cream flag!

    Anyway, Aki went outside of his tent and placed the black hoodies with the Fat Pride flag on the front at the stand. Near the entrance to his shopping yard, next to the entrance to the tent, there is a table as tall as a counter, with a bell on it for the customer to ring for Aki. As well as a sign at the front that reads:

    "All items here have magical properties within, a magic charm that makes the user grow, as in, gain weight, or change one's sexual attraction. Unless requested, the charm won't activate nor permanently change the user's body.

    When stolen, the owner is within his right to punish the thief if the purpose is fatphobic or the person is spiteful towards the owner's beliefs herein.

    So be careful of what you buy, and DO NOT STEAL!

    And if you have a request that's not in this shop or part of the magic charm in the items. You can commission an item from the owner within reason to either readjust or make a personalized item for you. But also, be clear with what you want... Though the change may not be permanent if not requested as such, there is no taking back a commissioned item. So ask and buy responsibly!"

    Here on this patch of land, where the Pride Month celebration is happening. It is where he is selling all things Pride Month. From shirts, hoodies, undergarments or underwear, and of course flags as well.

    While Aki is mostly interested in fat men or men with sumo wrestler builds. One look at his store can tell you that he has a blue and green gay flag, a gay bear flag, and, of course, fat pride flags on sale.

    But he's not opposed to taking commissions outside of his interests, even furry-related ones.

    And how can he call himself a wizard if there's no magic, right?

    He always puts a charm on each and every one of his items he sells.

    Be it making the wearer gain as much weight until he makes himself feel happy and jolly like Santa, or having the wearer become more attracted to heavier people, or even making them an anthro animal. That last one, though, is only upon request, that is. Of course, whatever magical transformation one can think of, Aki the Wizard can definitely give it a whirl.

    Still though, changing one's weight or body part and mental attractions is one thing, but changing one's species or gender is another thing. But he can learn!

    After Aki placed his Fat Pride flag hoodie, he went back inside of his tent.


    -II-

    Inside of his tent, which also acts as his commission room, he checks his crystal ball on its stand at his private desk surrounded by a small circular tablecloth.

    Usually he would have something to see to pass the time, like watching a couple of fat and happy gay men having the time of their lives at this event. Or seeing shy young men open up to what they like or support.

    Or find and hate-watch some fatphobes telling everyone how bad being fat is, screeching about how unhealthy and unattractive they are. And typically Aki would've used his magic to either make them fat (provided that they were skinny or even a jock) or make them attracted to fat folks, fall in love with them even.

    But he is only limited to close interactions, and Aki wouldn't want to deal with them anyway; or if by some chance one of them got one of his merchandise, he can make an enchantment through the item, even from afar and looking through the ball.

    Right now, though, all he could see was his facial reflection. His Asian features were fuller because of his obese body, as well as his long black hair tied in a man bun. One glance would make anyone assume that he is a sumo wrestler in a wizard's robe. Speaking of...

    He also is wearing a shiny turquoise pendant of a fat dragon face the size of a medallion around his neck. Included on his purple robe are a hood, which he's not wearing right now, sandals that are covered by his robe like his own undergarment, and a thick chocolate brown cloth belt around his waist (like a mawashi).

    In retrospect, Aki looks more like a sumo wrestler in a purple yukata (kimono) instead of a wizard, which he actually is.

    As Aki was going to chant to the magic ball with his thick fingers, the ball started to sparkle. Blinking red light three times, as if it was... Giving him an alert.

    "Strange?" the wizard ponders. "It wouldn't do that unless..." The fat wizard went wide-eyed. Smiling.

    "Someone's in my shopping yard!" Being excited, he immediately rushed out of his tent to try to find this new visitor. But it's empty...

    Now his shopping yard that he set up isn't really all that big. Not very spacious. And yet, he couldn't see anyone here. Leaning on his counter glumly. With what he assumed to be a false alarm. 'Maybe that ball detected someone too early?' He hoped to himself.

    Depressed, he looks over his clothing merchandise one more time; just then, as he was about to reside himself back inside of his tent, he turns and finds that...

    Who shows up, or what happened?
