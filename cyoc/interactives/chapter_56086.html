    The sound of knocking, followed by a truck speeding off startled Leandro from his ridiculously long slumber. It was 4PM, and as always, he had nothing planned. Leandro, to put it shortly, was a lazy guy with all the time in the world. He had won a lawsuit due to getting injured in a store, one that set him for life. It also made him unable to workout and swim like he once did, leaving his once-amazing body (perfectly sculpted from swimming a lot) on the less than desirable side. Though he was still lean to some degree, despite the spinal injury and lack of working out.

    He slowly got up from his bed to see what all the ruckus was about, and found a small package waiting for him at his doorstep. He eagerly opened it, only to find some software that he didn't order.

    "This isn't my new game!" He complained, taking a closer look at the software. He then found the manual, and proceeded to set up the software just to see what it was. The instillation was almost instant, and it brought up a strange looking screen with a bunch of writing - but most importantly - an exact 3D replica of Leandro.

    "What the hell...? Oh! This is one of those avatar building things. I guess one of my friends sent it to me with an avatar made already." Leo weird... Why is all this information here?"

    He scanned over the basics to see-

    Name: Leandro Abel Braun (Nickname: Leo)
    Age: 21
    Gender: male
    Ethnicity: 75% German, 25% Hispanic
    Height: 5'8"
    Posture: terrible (injured spine)
    Weight: 132 pounds
    Build: average
    Skin color: light intermediate (Luschan's chromatic value: 18)
    Hair color: ash brown
    Hair length: very short
    Hair type: wavy
    Hair style: buzzed
    Eye color: hazel
    Facial hair: none (slow growth - full beard possible)
    Body hair: light (underarm, chest, stomach, pubic region)
    <-Scroll Down For More Information Regrading [Leandro Abel Braun]->


    Leo was taken aback by all the information, especially since it was extremely accurate. Though he quickly regained his composure. It wasn't long before Leo found that he could alter the text. He made the 3D Leandro's hair go from a buzz cut to a common metro-sexual style by changing hair length to "short-medium" and style to "metro". His amusement level went from 1 flag to 6, as a certain commercial would say.

    "Ah, I guess I type stuff in and it happens to the model. I guess that change button there is the save button. This thing must have a huge selection of key words and changes if it was able to decide what I actually wanted... It looks pretty nice, actually." Leo said aloud, still oblivious to what the program actually did since he didn't click the change button. Regardless, he still went on changing the model to his liking before finally clicking what he thought to be the save button.
