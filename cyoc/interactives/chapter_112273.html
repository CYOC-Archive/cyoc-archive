    *For those of you confused, here is horse terminology*
    Mare- Female horse more than 4
    Stallion- A male horse
    Colt- Young male horse
    Filly- Young female horse
    Foal- Horse under 1 year old
    Hh- (Hands high) Unit of measurement for horses. Used to describe height of horse at its withers. One hand is 4 in (10 cm)
    Withers- Top of a horse's shoulders
    Friesian- (FREE-zhen) Breed of military horse bred in Friesland
    Kiso- Breed of military horse bred in Japan
