    Entity # 7612
    Codename: Deal Maker

    Origin: Unknown
    7612-1 was discovered by a woman, formerly known as Jane, now contained within G.E.T., labelled 7612-3. It was left on her doorstep on a Summer evening. Her (former) husband recounts hearing his doorbell chime, followed by a quick knocking on the door. 7612-1 was found alone, likely planted by an individual (currently unknown) with intention.

    Description:
    7612-1 is a small, plain looking box. The box is only slightly larger than the average hand size. The box looks hand crafted from standard Oak. The wood has been sanded and carved, and finished with a traditional wood finish. The box is smooth, somewhat shiny, and coloured a dark pale brown. The box is able to be opened with ease, no latch is present, however the box will not simply fall open if turned upside down. It must be opened by an individual.
    A description of the contents inside the box offered by multiple 5’s. All stated that the box contained a small pile of golden sand that seemed to glow, even in a completely dark room. The glow does not illuminate surroundings or individuals. The box has been dropped on several occasions, however, it has never been broken, damaged, or otherwise. In fact, it never hit the ground. The box simply floats back to its table.
    After a few (roughly 5-9) seconds of the box being opened, the sand will accumulate and create an entity (7612-2). The box will remain open, and will not close until the entity has returned to the box. No individual has attempted to close the box before the entity has returned, so no G.E.T. personnel knows if possible.

    7612-2 is a entity which refers to himself as (The) Deal Maker. The entity’s visible ‘body’ is mostly made of the illuminated sand found within 7612-1, however, it has been noted by numerous 5’s that through the sand is a red/orange/yellow mist, also creating the same luminescent light as the sand. Each personnel that has been close enough to him has stated, ‘it is not fire, it is too powerful to be fire’ (all personnel stating the same description). Despite the entity’s lack of discernable sex, he refers to himself as male. The being’s appearance looks traditionally masculine, including a simple beard (goatee) and short, slicked back hair. He wears a stylish suit and shoes (all features being made of the sand, however the brightness of each grain is changed to appear darker or lighter). The appearance has been compared to many depictions of The Devil (Lucifer, Beelzebub, Satan, etc.), but has never claimed to be the biblical figure.
    7612-2 has a deep, soothing voice. He has a relatively classic, posh British accent. The entity has been tested with majority English speaking personnel, it has proven to speak over 5 non-English languages. When tested with a multilingual 4, the Entity chose to speak in English, which was the 4’s first, and most understood language.
    The Entity has only ever referred to himself by one name, ‘Deal Maker’. Personnel typically refer to him as ‘The’ Deal Maker, but the entity has never been recorded saying this title, but doesn’t seem offended or insulted by the extra word.

    Known Abilities:
    7612-1’s only ability is to contain 7612-2. The two entities cannot be separated, only able to move 5 metres apart at most. No method of containment has successfully separated them. It has only ever been tested 4 times. The connection between both entities has most notably resulted in the destruction of a very thick wall of titanium. Only one casualty was suffered, disconnected from to the 7612-1 or 7612-2’s power.
    7612-2 is a sentient individual. He refers to himself by a strange title, is formed of an unknown material, but is generally independent. When activated, his only task is to make a ‘deal’ with the individual who opened the box. The deal can be anything from winning the lottery, to becoming a being of pure light-energy (see Entity #7612-19). His power is (as far as we can see) endless, limited only by his own will.
    But there is always a catch. Deal Maker has an affinity for physical changes. With each deal, he will always find a way to twist the words of the subject in order to transform them in some way. When successful, he typically (not always) choses to transform the subject. It is theorised that like some humans, he has a ‘fetish’ for transformation.
    He is capable of not only transforming the subject, but also transforming anyone. There is no known limit as to the distance the transformation can travel across the globe. The only known limit of his power comes to the confines of a limited space, as his deal-making power will never destroy an external object/entity unless specifically specified. His other limit is the number of people. Though the limit unknown, it is known that global transformations are not within his power, and will not take such deal. On the other hand, mass transformation is possible (see Entity #7612-8). Any unwanted transformations can be bargained, but will result in an equal transformation, usually applied. The subject, if performed a mass transformation, is able to reverse the effects, but will guarantee a severe transformation upon oneself.
    The deal is made verbally, but was once agreed on paper by a deaf individual (note, 7612-2 only wrote on paper during this test, did not speak a word). The deal is never as initially planned. Deal Maker will typically ask many questions, influencing the subject to alter their wording, usually resulting in an unintended transformation. Deal’s are always sealed by a handshake. On one occasion, a 5, missing both her arms, was able to create a deal via 7612-2 shaking her right foot (refer to 7612-12).
    Once 7612 has created his deal, the sand that makes up his body will gradually glow a bright red for exactly 5 seconds (note, this glow does cause reflection, unlike the glow of the sand), before returning back to his regular hue.
    His final words will always be, “it was a pleasure doing business with you,” before his body disperses and collects back inside the box (7612-1).
    Once the deal is set, the box is not able to be open again for exactly 13 hours. Once the time limit is up, it will be able to open again.
    One individual is able to make a deal more than once, and will be remembered by Deal Maker.

    Containment:
    7612-1 is to be locked inside it’s disclosed room cube room. The box is only to be removed when authorised by level 2 or higher. The box is never to be opened outside it’s disclosed room, especially in an open, outdoor area. When being transported, the box is to restrained by two leather belts, locked together by a padlock.

    Other:
    7612-2 is capable of toppling governments, eliminating any threat to G.E.T. or otherwise, and is incredibly generally limitless in terms of power. However, the Entity is held back by 7612-2’s transformation fetish. This limits its true potential, and is not wanted desired by 1.
