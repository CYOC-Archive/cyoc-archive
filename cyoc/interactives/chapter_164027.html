    Bill is Sherry's Human Master

    A wave washed over Sherry. She blinked. Suddenly, she could read the Chronivac! Luckily, the awareness setting was on for her, so she knew what had happened. In fact, she remembered everything that had happened since cows and humans swapped places. She peered at the screen closely, curious as to what she had just changed.

    Before she could read anything, however, she felt a jolt of pain hit her. She let out a loud moan. Apparently, she was still pregnant with twins, and still nursing Megan. Unable to focus on the Chronivac, Sherry looked at herself to figure out what had changed. To her relief, she looked just as she had before cows and humans had swapped roles. She was back to looking fully human, with no tail and only two breasts. And, thankfully, she was fully clothed.

    Another contraction hit Sherry. She moaned again.

    “Sherry, get off the computer!” snapped a voice behind her. Sherry got up and turned around. It was Bill, back to being human. She stifled a sigh of relief that things seemed to be fixed. They were both human, and Bill was still her master, impregnating her because his wife was infertile.

    “Master, I’ve gone into labor,” she informed him shyly. Bill crossed his arms.

    “Then go deliver in the servant’s quarters. You know how this works,” he said firmly. Sherry bowed her head subserviently and left. She waddled downstairs and outside, to the barn that, to her, had been restored to its original role as the servant’s quarters. Her fellow servant, Lilly, glanced at her as she entered.

    “Are you in labor?” Lilly asked. Sherry opened her mouth to reply, but before she could, her water broke. “Well, that works as an answer,” Lilly said, looking at the mess on the floor. “Come on, I’ll help you deliver.” Lilly guided Sherry into her room. Sherry looked around, glad to be a surrogacy servant. Normal servants like Lilly didn’t get their own rooms. Lilly laid Sherry down on the bed. Since Sherry was wearing a dress like all servants, there was no need to remove anything to prepare for the birth. They only needed to wait.

    After a few hours, Sherry finally delivered her twins – a boy and a girl. The girl looked like Bill, while the boy looked like Sherry. Lilly sighed quietly.

    “Master won’t be happy that you gave him a son that doesn’t look like him,” she said. Sherry nodded, saddened to have failed her master in this way.

    “I’ll just have to bear another child for him, once I am able to.”

    “He might want to move on to another servant.”

    “No. He won’t. Surrogacy servants are expensive,” Sherry said firmly. Lilly shrugged. She took the twins away to be cleaned up. A few minutes later, Bill entered Sherry’s room.

    “You gave me a son, like you were told to,” Bill said. Sherry nodded. “However, he doesn’t look like me.”

    “Master, I’m sorry,” Sherry gushed. “I’ll do better next time!” Bill looked her over thoughtfully.
