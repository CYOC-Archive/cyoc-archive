    The Magic Shop

    You teleport to Hunter's house

    Your best friend from school, a nerdy dude named Hunter, is sitting at his desk, studying for finals. You and Hunter are high school freshmen, so you’re both 15 years old.

    You use your teleportation abilities to casually pop up in his bedroom, without using the front door or anything at all. You can now pop in and show up at his house anytime you want to.

    “Yo, Hunter, what’s up?” you say to him, as you’re standing in the middle of his room.

    “Hey… Jaxon! What the heck? How… How did you get in my room?” Hunter said, looking around. “My door’s closed and locked! How did you do that?”

    “I teleported!” you shrug, like it’s totally normal.

    “WHAT!!?? Are you kidding me?” Hunter gasped.

    You vanish from where you’re standing and appear again, standing next to his dresser, on the other side of his room.

    “I’m over here…”

    “WHOA!” Hunter shouted, realizing that you teleported again. “What… How…?”

    You disappear again, and pop up, standing next to his bed. You sit down on his bed this time.

    “Hey! Quit teleporting around my room!” Hunter shouts at you as he stands up to face you.

    “Now, I gotta show you some other magical tricks I have picked up!” you grin. “But, don’t worry, it’s all good!”

    “What?” Hunter wonders.

    You stare at your friend, who has always been short, skinny and weak. He was always bullied at school. So, now that you got these cool powers from the genie, you can use the ability to change anything and anyone just by thinking it. So, what are you going to do with Hunter, your short, scrawny, 15-year-old best friend from the freshman high school class in school?

    What do you do now?
