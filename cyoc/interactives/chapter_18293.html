    “Gosh, dude! You seem kinda small! Not only that, but you don’t weigh a thing!” Peter chuckled, as he turned his head sideways so he could catch a glimpse of his tiny friend, who was sitting on his right shoulder.

    “That’s because I can make you grow as big as I want to, whenever I want to!” Allen replied.

    “Let’s go to my house. I just wanna get out of this blistering heat!” Allen instructed his personal, giant bodyguard.

    “Aye aye, sir!” Peter nodded, as he began to walk towards Allen’s house.

Peter was so strong, that he was able to sprint to the house, while still carrying Allen on his shoulder. He reached his friend’s house in minutes, where he grabbed hold of Allen and gently set him on the pavement in front of the house.

    “Alright, big guy! It’s time to shrink back to normal!” Allen commanded.
