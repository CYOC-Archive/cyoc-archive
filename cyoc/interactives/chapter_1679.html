    Soon a loud sound of rumbling was heard all over the world. It appeared that the entire earth was experiencing an earthquake at the exact same time. The news came on broadcasting scientists that were theorizing that the explanation for the earthquakes was that the earth was deteriorating from its core to it's crust. Many people ran from the streets screaming as did Wolf girls that had recently been transformed in California, Floridia and Europe. In fact some were going through the agonizing transformations while the earth was quaking. Other recently transformed girls were just coming down from their animalistic lesbian sexual acts. Some scientists had other theories, some that suggested that the earth quaking was caused by the howling that was transforming all the women and some men into women. In fact this seemed the most logical explanation when tied to the theories of the core giving out. Soon the entire western hemisphere had began to crumble and millions of people died. As luck would have it, some extraterestrial beings that had been observing the earth for thousands of years, were aware of the earths apparent destruction and had interevened.

    To preserve the earth in it's current state, the aliens had decided to hover their crafts within the earths atmosphere, and transport thousands and thousands of humans, were-wolves and mutants as well as some of the cryptolozolical life forms such as the Loch Ness Monster, Sasquatch, El Chupacabra, etc. They aimed to bring them to their planet and allow an earth like habitat to be constructed where they would be able to prosper as they would have had the howls not caused earths destruction. In fact the transported beings had been induced into a deep sleep and then transported to the earth habitat on Xzandrak 12 without even disturbing their current concept of existence. The aliens induced a slight rumbling and shaking in the habitat so the earthlings would believe they were still on their own planet, and then shut it off making them suspect that the earth was saved. The aliens then erased certain aspects of their memories, such as loved ones that had died in the fray, as well as adventurous tendencies to prevent them from exploring beyond their parameters. In fact the entire remaining population of earth was being treated like Jim Carrey in the Truman Show.

    Though it would appear that the aliens were doing this for the benefit of mankind, in actuality they were doing it to maintain their current food source in which they had been feeding since the 1940's when their craft crashed in Roswell, New Mexico.

    One by one the humans were eaten, and no one ever noticed. Sure pictures were posted on milk cartons as they always had been of missing children, and even adults. But they were just figured to have been abducted, not devoured. So it would go for decades and decades. The alien world would be supplied with a never ending food source until its sun burned out in a couple billion years, but that was a long time from now. In fact with the new were wolf race, the aliens had a new delicacy on their hands, and in some cases they would create wolf girls in other places that hadn't been infected yet. Should the aliens get greedy they would be able to change the entire population of Pseudo-Earth into their favorite meal.
