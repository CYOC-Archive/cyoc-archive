    Zed Section in the Library Stacks

    added by D 13 years ago O

    Zoomorphology 1001 was the class you really are looking forward to
    this year. You are pleased to learn that your new dormitory has a
    portal into the University's library stacks. You go into the small
    closet with stain glass doors which most resembles an old fashioned
    telephone booth except for being made of glowing stain glass.

    You step inside and the doors close behind you. The room glows, and
    the silver mirror where the pay phone would have been glows with a
    luminous countenance. It looks nearly human except that it is only a
    distortion in the glass which otherwise is reflecting your image and
    the glowing stain glass cabinet that surrounds you.

    "Library Portal," says the image in the looking glass, "Which section
    of the library is your desire?" the voice has an oddly hollow sounding
    echo as it speaks.

    "Zed Section in the stacks," you say.

    The mirror glows and all the stain glass glows much brighter than
    before. You are blinded. When the lights dim and you can see again you
    are standing in a similar portal booth set into the wall in front of a
    black wrought iron catwalk deep in the bowels of the library stacks.
    It is a smaller section but it still takes up considerable space.
    Three or four story bookcases disappear into the shadows above and
    below you. You walk out of the dimming booth and on to the catwalk and
    over to the nearest shelf. Randomly, you grab a book. It's black and
    the name on the spine has long since faded away.

    You open the book and read, "Zaalaamic Chimeric Magickal
    Transmogrifications."

    You grin, you are definitely in the right place. Though you're not
    sure if these books are filed alphabetically or by topic. You wave
    your hand and use a simple illumination spell. The spines of the dull
    books light up, as the worn text glows so you can read them. As you
    start to read the glowing spines, the books start to move of their own
    accord. Your spell as triggered something it would seem. You wave
    your hand to reverse the spell. Many of the spines dim, but some
    continue to glow and move. A book leaps off the shelf. It flies open
    and you see a glowing image, and....
