    Being diapered at Rita's is one thing. Being diapered at home is another. Maybe Leah can potty train herself during the week. She's heard Tina talk about the different methods plenty of times. Of course, this means she's going to have to stay in the house, but she doesn't want to be out and about diapered anyways. And she clearly needs diapers right now.

    As Leah cleans up and puts a fresh diaper on, she mentally comes up with a plan. She'll set an alarm to go off every hour during the day, and use the toilet when it does. If she feels the need to go before the alarm goes off, she'll go to the bathroom. Even if the need to go is small. She can't wait until it gets too strong, or she'll lose control. Any time she falls asleep, the moment she wakes up she'll go to the bathroom.

    During the night, well, she'll deal with that later. Hopefully when her daytime potty training improves, her nighttime will as well.

    Leah goes back to bed.

    In the morning, Leah wakes up in a wet diaper. She rushes to the bathroom to use the toilet, but not much comes out. Since her diaper is still warm, she must have used it shortly before waking up.

    A diaper change later, Leah walks downstairs with a top on but nothing over her diaper. To her surprise, there's a note on the kitchen table. Apparently Rita was called into work, and will be there during the day the rest of the week.

    Perfect. Leah was worried how Rita would respond to her frequent potty breaks.

    Leah sets her alarms, has breakfast, and goes to check for a package at the front door that Rita's note said was for her. She brings the package in, closes the door, and opens the box. To her delight, it's something that will make her week of self potty training easier. It's a...
