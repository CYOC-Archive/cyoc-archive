    Sneaking Her Way To The Breeder

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A month ago A O

    After getting comfortable in her new form that day and thinking about being bred for puppies, her human trainer walked her back to her chalet. They both returned, and her human trainer took her to get washed. "So, one for yes, two for no. Did you like what you saw today?" the trainer asked. She responded with one bark. After getting washed and dried off, Jenna looked at herself in her new form as a German Shepherd and liked her new body. The trainer asked if Jenna enjoyed being a dog, and Jenna barked once, possibly not knowing what he was talking about. Jenna was perhaps still trying to imagine herself as a mother to a litter of puppies and how she'd like to join the breeding pack.

    The trainer said, "We will show you the breeding center tomorrow. Would you like that?" Jenna barked once to say yes. The Trainer said, "Very Well, see you tomorrow." As the trainer left, Jenna pressed the button for food and water with her front paw. Jenna began wondering how her life would be once she left her human past behind. Her College Debt would be nullified, and her lonely life would be filled with something better, knowing that she could enjoy the fantasies she had for a long time. As she finished her food and water, she fell asleep, and as she dreamed, Jenna saw her human form come in contact with the German Shepherd she had become. The German Shepherd she became licked Jenna, and the two started to merge.

    The morning came, and Jenna's human trainer put a leash on her, and the two went to the breeding center. The two saw the process from the start as the recently transformed human entered a chamber that physically erased all their human past. The chip recognized the change, erasing all the past of the human who became the dog from every database in the world. The humans who did know them would receive a message that reprograms every facet of their lives without that person in their lives. Ten minutes later, the males would be given a mate to breed with, and the females would be given a male to breed them. Jenna had seen enough to know that was what she wanted. When the chamber was cleared, Jenna used her strength to pull the leash off her trainer's hand and enter. Jenna began the final stages as the chip in her collar connected, erasing Jenna's human past from every database. The trainer standing there watched as the name "Jenna" was replaced with the name "Daisy." "Daisy's" transformation was completed; ten minutes later, a newly transformed German Shepherd male smelled Daisy's Heat. Daisy was now getting bred, and Jenna's last drops of her past cried away, leaving Daisy having just been bred into an entirely female German Shepherd.
