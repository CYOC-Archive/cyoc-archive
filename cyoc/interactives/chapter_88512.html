    As you jump onto you're bed when you get home, you feel strange. You're almost sure you feel like you're burning up, like you're sick or something, but you don't mind the burning sensation. In fact, you enjoy it greatly. After a few minutes, you stand up and decide to go get a snack. As you walk down the stairs, you feel your muscles burning. They're almost aching, but it feels so good, you don't want it to stop. As you reach the kitchen, you open the fridge to find a few items. You grab some turkey out of the meat section, deciding to make a sandwich. Suddenly, a wave of hunger overcomes you. You need to eat. It almost feels like you haven't eaten in forever. You quickly stuff the turkey in your mouth, along with the plastic, and start eating it. It tastes so good, and somehow you even manage to eat the plastic. You smile, and your open mouth reveals little bits of turkey and plastic. As you walk back upstairs, it feels like you're almost floating up. It's so easy, you decide to jump up two at a time to make it harder. When you get up the stairs, you celebrate, almost... Proud? Whatever, the feeling is fantastic. You rush to you're room, and get inside. When you lie down on your bed again, you suddenly look at your muscles. You quickly notice they have grown, and when you flex, you're muscles glisten brightly, sticking out almost two inches. Not to mention, the burning feeling hasn't stopped. Did the picture do this? Was he now growing muscle because of a picture? He looked in the mirror, and smiled to himself. This was amazing.
