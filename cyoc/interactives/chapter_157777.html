    "Bu-bu-" Tried to respond Elspeth.

    "So in accordance to the witches' laws, since you tried to steel my own propriety, everything that was yours is now mine. As you are, Elspeth. Alas, I don't have use of an old hag, let alone a powerless one. …Nor do I have use of you ugly decor, for that matter. But I'm sure once I redecorate your house it will turn out even nicer than my current one."
