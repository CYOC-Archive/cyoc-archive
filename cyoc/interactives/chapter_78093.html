    They choose the Animal Wing

    added by Bahamut626 10 years ago O

    "Let's go to the Animal Wing!" Jennifer excitedly decided.

    "Alright then! The next Trolly Hub for that should be here any minute now." Eric agreed as he picked up the
    luggage again, reaching the waiting point as an empty trolley arrived. Putting it in, he joined his newly-wed
    wife in the seats as it took off to take them to their destination.

    The journey was smooth and comfortable, and as they reached the wing, they were treated to quite the sight.

    A multitude of wildly different peoples could be seen in the main lobby, in an equally wide range of different
    animal forms. Complete animals being tended to, others with animal parts, and anthros. Some real, others
    extinct, and mythological creatures too.

    "I'm gonna like our time here." Jennifer whispered to Eric, to which he nodded his agreement.

    Using the lift, they reached their room having passed a lizard that was walking on two legs, that had nothing
    on. Room 3386. Jennifer used the key card to open it.

    The room itself resembled most other Hotel Rooms. Large bedroom, living area, kitchen, bathroom, closets, TV,
    entertainment, the works. And all of a high standard.

    "Odd. I was expecting more given the rest of the Hotel." Jennifer stated, a little disappointed.

    "Well let's see what this letter says." Eric replied, having set down the luggage and picked up a letterthat
    was addressed to them.


    Dear Eric and Jennifer Kinneas

    First off. Allow me to thank you for choosing the Transformation Resort, and I hope your trip is worth it.
    Now
    you have selected the Animal Wing, allow me to give you some rules and advice.

    As you may have already noticed, the Animal Wing is where people may transform themselves into animals of
    their
    own choosing. Whether it be a real living animal, one that is extinct, or even mythological and fictional
    animals. You may even choose by how much you want to change. You may choose to change only parts of your
    body,
    you may choose to be a hybrid of human and animal. Or you may choose to be a full animal in which case our
    assistants will be called in to take care of you until your stay is over.

    The room you stay in is also magical. Once you change yourself, the room will automatically change itself as
    so
    to make your stay more comfortable.

    You may also choose to change yourself outside of your room, since the stores, entertainment centres,
    restaurants and relaxation areas are all capable of changing you into your animal of choice.

    On account of being able to change into such a wide variety of different animals. The Animal Wing has a very
    lax policy with regards to clothing and hygiene, though this is reserved only for the Wing itself.

    Once more, thank you for choosing the Transformation Resort, an I hope you have a safe and pleasant stay.

    Yours Sincerely

    The Chair of the Transformation Resort


    Once Eric an Jennifer had read the letter over, it clicked. Not only could they change into any animal they
    desired, but they could change by any degree they wished too. And anywhere within the wing they wanted to as
    well, with their room adjusting itself for them.

    "So?" Eric asked Jennifer. "What will it be?"
