    "Hey, Bruno," he began. Then seeing his own naked body on the screen. "Sorry, I dont have anything to wear. I mean Rick always says clothes dont need clothes. This is not a joke. Rick is dangerous. He is responsible for my disappearance. I'm not exactly sure what will happen if I leave Rick's sauna, but you should be able to see in a moment. I spent the last few days as Rick's Speedo. Yeah, I know it sounds crazy. You wore me before you left your phone in the sauna. It was so hard not to communicate with you, but Rick said he just have to transform you into a butt plug if I did. Um, the way he said it, he still might anyway so be careful. Uh, um, I dont know how he does it/did it, but he does have this power to change people into objects that he wears. Remember Felix that scrawny guy who went missing years ago. He's been Rick's jockstrap the whole time. Oh, and he can listen to my thoughts when he wears me. Same with jocko - uh, that's what he calls Felix." Chad added sheepishly, "He calls me Speedo. Don't if he's a witch, a wizard or an alien with super high tech nanobots or whatnot, but he can do what he said. This is your phone not trick photography. Gawd, I'm afraid to do this.
