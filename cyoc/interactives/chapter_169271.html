    "Okay, first of all, girls, you're going to wash off that makeup," he said.

    "What?!" Annabelle was first to object. "But he gave it to us. It's a reward."

    "It doesn't matter whether or not it's a reward," Kevin snapped. "You're too young for it. Go wash it off."

    She shrugged. "Not with just water and soap, no. I'm gonna try makeup remover at home."

    "I won't get bored!" Emma claimed. "And I wish we didn't have school tomorrow."

    "Yeah, and I wish I didn't have to go to work, but --" Kevin stopped, because he suddenly knew that tomorrow was a holiday, and that the girls' schools and his office would be closed. And he could tell from the expression on the girls' faces that they had just gained the same knowledge.

    "Whoa," said Annabelle, putting a hand to her forehead. Kevin noticed for the first time her elongated, manicured fingernails.
