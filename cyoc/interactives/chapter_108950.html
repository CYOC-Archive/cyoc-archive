    Joining the club

    You find a strip club with a sign that says "Club bimbo." What a weird name you think to yourself. Before you leave a woman who looks like a typical blonde bimbo comes out of the strip club and approaches you. "Like hay, are you the new stripper?" she says in a high pitch voice. You are taken aback. Sure you are a cute girl with long black hair, and brown eyes, but you are certainly no stripper. "Sorry I'm not stripper material" You tell her. "Oh silly we can change that." She says grabbing you by the arm and pulling you towards the building.

    She pulls you into the strip club against your will. Inside the club there were only women in skimpy outfits. They all stop what they are doing and look at you. The bimbo who was dragging you stops and exclaims, "We have a new member! Who wants to help me get her ready before opening?" Two women raise their hands and approaches you. One was a African american bimbo and the other was an Asian bimbo.

    "Like ready to go?" The blonde bimbo asks the two and they both giggle and nod their heads. You continue to protest but they don't seem to here you. They take you to a door that is labeled "Trixe". The blonde bimbo opens the door and the four of you enter. "Welcome to my dressing room." Says the blonde bimbo who you think is Trixe. Inside there was a large bed, a dresser, a couch, and a vanity mirror with a built in sink.

    "For the last time, I don't want to join!" You plead to them. The three of them looked at each other and giggled. "You say that now, but once we're done you won't want to leave." Trixe responded. She walked to the door and locked it. You now know that escape is inevitable. "Okay, do what ever you want to me." You say excepting defeat.

    What do you do now?
