    As Jordan and Jason walked through the grassy field toward their dorm, someone jumped them out of nowhere. They were pinned faces down, unable to move let alone wiggle much. It was like hands of steel held them in place. "H-how... could you?" the person whispered.

    Jordan instantly recognized it. "Alexia?"

    "How could you?" Alexia screamed in a demonic tone. Jordan and Jason moaned in sync as she knocked their heads together. "You two betrayed ME!" Alexia yelled. Keeping a strong hold on them, she rolled them over so she could look at them directly. Alexia's eyes were pure, her fangs had grown to full size, and her face was contorted in rage. She looked like a true vampire.

    "Alexia..." Jordan tried to speak, but the words died in his throat. Their heart was pounding so hard, he thought for sure it would pound right out of their chest. "Alexia... we-we're sorry. We didn't mean to hurt you." Jordan pleaded. If only one of us could get to our wand Jordan thought.

    "You're... our friend, Alexia. We just don't...like you in the same way." Jason said, to Jordan's shock. That was probably the worst thing he could have said at the moment. "We didn't mean to hurt you." Jason repeated Jordan's words again.

    The words didn't even seem to phase her. She was in a blood lust, and a blast from their wand would be the only thing that could knock her out of it. But with her vampire strength, it was impossible to reach for it. Jordan and Jason were completely helpless.
