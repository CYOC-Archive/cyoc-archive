    Nora and Molly now found themselves racing from store to store. The two newly minted teens enjoying their renewed youth.

    What either girl seemed to notice was how their regression was affecting them. Molly's "driving" for lack of a better term, was wild and dangerous. The now inexperienced driver waving in and out of lanes before they arrived. Neither girl thought anything of it, chalking up the people honking at them as crazy.

    The teens fashion choices were also more youthful. Thankfully the two teens had found something more to their liking. Quickky discarding, Nora's vintage outfits the two teens now found themselves in fashionable cut Jean's, long sleeved shirts which hugged their slight curves and white sneakers to complete the look. Exiting the store with entirely new wardrobe the teens giggled about their shopping spree.

    "Can you believe all of this cost $600," Molly exclaimed. Nora shrugged as the two walked. "Good thing for my credit cards," she said. "And besides I have enough money to pay the bill easy."

    "So does that mean more shopping," Mollybasked hopefully. Nora giggled, it did sound intriguing but first she wanted to grab a bite to eat.

    Making their way towards the food court, Nora and Molly gazed at the various restaurants. Before the two would have easily passed on the greasy fast food stands, now the smells were making their mouthed water. "Oh my God," Molly exclaimed. "That smells SOOOO GOOD!"

    The two teens now grabbed burgers, fries and two large milkshakes, taking a seat as they now talked of what had happened. "So did you plan on reliving your teens Nora?"

    "Was hoping to end up in my 30s," Nora replied, crinkling her nose at the thought. Who would want to be 30? That was so old. "To be honest this was a last attempt at saving my life. The doctor said my cancer spread, I had maybe 6 months to live."

    "That sucks," Molly sighed. "But hey now you're cured."

    "Yeah and I get a second chance," Nora said excitedly. The 15 year old paused as she now looked at her 16 year old friend. "But what about you Molly? I mean you have family."

    "My husbands been dead for a couple of years," Molly replied. "And my kids and grandkids live out of state. You know what's crazy, some of my grandkids are actually older than I am right now!"

    "It could of been all of them if you hadnt hit the stop button," Nora giggled, causing Molly to stick out her tongue.

    "Well," Molly said. "That hit the spot. So what should we do now?"

    Nora shrugged, to be honest she wasnt sure. Right now they were both too young to buy alcohol but they could try sneaking it. Nora blushed at the idea, stealing alcohol like some rambunctious, rebellious teen. Still though their were things they could do.

    A makeover, a mani/pedi, maybe even see a movie? Nora felt herself shiver at the thought of her and Molly making out in a dark theater. All those ideas sounded great as she now felt Molly's foot rub her leg. Smirking she took her hand, giving her a sweet smile. If all else failed the two could go back to Nora's for some private time together. Squeezing Molly's hand Nora now said....
