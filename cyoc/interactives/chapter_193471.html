    Rachel in Wonderland: Beware the Fan

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago S O Shrinking

    The first thing that Rachel had to do was to move the furniture back into place before she did anything else. Most of it was pretty heavy, but with some effort Rachel had managed to move everything back in their original spots. After taking a small break, Rachel considers what to do next and then sees the folding fan still on the floor.
    "Hmm, I should pick that up and move it to a safe space. I don't want to end up shrinking myself by mistake." Rachel said.
    Rachel bends over and gently picks the white folding fan off the floor with her slik glove covered hands.
    "What an amazing day. First I get an authentic Alice in Wonderland Outfit, then I ate an actual Eat Me cupcake and literally grew too big for my room, and now I'm back to my regular size thanks these fan and gloves. I wonder what'll happen to me next?"
    Rachel continued to examine the fan when she was suddenly overcome with the feeling that something was not right about her. She was uncertain of why, but Rachel felt as if she wasn't her right size after all. As Rachel looked around her room, she couldn't help but feel as if she was still bigger than she was suppose to be. Rachel tried to deny it, but it was no use.
    "I'm too big. I don't know why, but I'm still too big."
    Rachel was convinced that she was not her proper size and the only way to return to normal was in her hands.
    "I have to use you again. It's the only way. I need to get smaller." Rachel said to the fan.
    Rachel began fanning herself and immediately felt better. Rachel watched as the world around her grew as she shrank a few inches. As this was happening, the fan and gloves started to glow and sparkle. Rachel suddenly realizes that she already past her original height and stops fanning.
    "WHAT THE HELL AM DOING! I DON'T WANT TO GET ANY SMALLER!"
    But the feeling of being too big still shrouded over Rachel's thoughts.
    "Whats wrong with me? Why do I feel this way?"
    Rachel looks down and sees the fan and gloves still glowing and sparkling.
    "The fan and gloves. Of course! I should have known."
    Suddenly Rachel's right hand started moving on it's own and began fanning her, shrinking Rachel down another inch.
    "OH NO YOU DON'T!"
    Rachel grabs her right arm with her lert hand and pulls it down away fom her. But the fan was still being fanned even when it was facing the floor. Suddenly sparkles of light leaped off the fan, landing on the floor and began circling around Rachel's feet. Soon the sparkles stated spinning faster glowing brighter, causing the Alice outfit to slowly glow and sparkle the same way up along Rachel's body.
    "Oh this is not good. What are you up to-"

    KAPOOF

    Suddenly the Alice outfit's skirt and petticoats instantly expands out like a parachute, causing Rachel to regain control and drop the fan.
    "Well, definitely wasn't expecting this. but at least I don't have the-"
    Before Rachel could catch her breath, the glowing fan levitates off the floor and flies around her. The fan rapidly fans Rachel as it circles around her, causing Rachel to shrink even more.
    "Oh no no, not again."
    Rachel tries to run, but get lifted into the air by her inflated dress from the wind blowing from the fan. Now the fan was moving so fast that it had become a blur as Rachel floated in place and continued to shrink, fulfilling her request to get even smaller.
