    The wheel slows down and lands on one of the many green stripes. The old lady smiles and hands you your prize, the small statue. You start feeling a strange sensation all over your face. You try to throw it, only to find it firmly anchors to you hand. Suddenly it starts sinking into your skin and despite your best efforts to claw at it, you cannot stop or remove it. "What's going on!?!?!" You ask the old lady. You open your mouth to reply but stop as she holds up a mirror to your face. You scream because your head has been replaced with a furry,black skunk head, the rest of your body remains unscathed though. "You're going to be staying here until the totem decides to free you...unless it never does, hee hee hee!!!! Somehow taking much longer than you anticipate, eerily longer. You keep running until you spot a familiar spot in the distance. The very same stall you ran away from! The statue is sending you in circles without any way out of the carnival! You're trapped in the carnival, with a small amount of cash left for food or a couple more games to pass time until you have to accept your fate as a member of the freakshow.
