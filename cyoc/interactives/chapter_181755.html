So what if he is now exactly her type, Sam is/was still her dog. But maybe they could at least fuck once, maybe twice, just to prove the point.

And on the other hand, she would get spayed, but then would be allowed to have sex with any dog she wants, but she also would be treated as just another dog for the rest of her life, with 0 chance of becoming human again.
