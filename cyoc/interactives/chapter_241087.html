    The Magic Shop

    Chase, Aaron, and their girlfriends encounter the Seraphim

    After their great night out together with friends, Chase and Aaron went on a date night together with Andromeda and Nyx. Andromeda and Nyx had gotten a little dressed up for their night out together. After a lovely little dinner, the four of them went out on a stroll together.

    "Dinner was great Chase, but you know, I think I could would like some dessert." Andromeda said with a smirk.

    "You mean Ice Cream or something like that?" Chase asked being a little oblivious as Andromeda giggled at her boyfriend.

    "I meant something like this." Andromeda said as she pulled Chase's face into hers and passionately kissed him on the lips.

    Nyx and Aaron shook their heads as they watched the pair. All four of them were definitely in love with one another, but sometimes it seemed like Chase and Andromeda's love for each other could be seen from space.

    "So did you want to go back to your house or did you want to go to the Moreland farm tonight? Aaron?" Nyx asked noticing her boyfriend seemed a little bit distracted.

    Nyx looked over to where Aaron was looking to see a two women laying bloody and beaten on the street.

    "Chase, look, I think those women might need some help." Aaron said as Chase looked up not wanting to miss a chance to help out those in need, Aaron and Chase raced over to see if the women needed any assistance at all.

    "Excuse me ladies, are either of you seriously hurt? We can call an ambulance for you or something like that." Chase asked the two women as Nyx and Andromeda felt something was a little off about these two women.

    "N-N No....we....we....just....needed....SOME CHUMPS!" One of the women said as she and the other woman shapeshifted into little goblins and jumped onto both Aaron and Chase's backs.

    "What the hell?! Get off of me!" Chase shouted trying to bat the goblin thing off of him.

    "Andromeda, they're Aufhockers!" Nyx suddenly realized shouting to her sister.

    "They're what?!" Aaron asked as he wrestled to get the creature off of him.

    "Shapeshifters, basically they're like very distant relatives of vampires but completely different. Lancelot taught us all about them." Andromeda explained as she tried to help get the creature off Chase's back.

    "Well, can you tell them who you are or something like that to get them off of us?" Aaron asked.

    "No good with that. These things aren't like normal vampires, they won't listen to a word we say. We could say we're both queens of the Earth and they wouldn't do a thing." Nyx explained, the dhamphir straining to pull the creature off Aaron's back as Andromeda did the same for Chase back. Neither creature budged, their weight becoming heavier and heavier on the boys backs.

    The sound of vehicles speeding towards them, drew the couples attention. Two large trucks screeched to a halt, the group looked up thinking that S.P.E.A.R. had come to help them. But as they got closer, Aaron noticed that these militia weren't S.P.E.A.R., instead sporting the emblem of the Jerusalem cross with a raised fist over it.

    "Vampires, get out of the way, now!" One of the militia men ordered Nyx and Andromeda as they got out of the way, aiming what looked like tasers at Aufhockers l. The Aufhockers screeched in pain as the taser deployed, unintentionally hurting both Aaron and Chase in the process.

    "Please stop it! You're hurting them!" Andromeda begged to the men as one of them pushed her away.

    Aaron had enough of this, transforming into his Kintaro form. Grabbing the Aufhocker off of his back and tossing it to the ground, before sprinting towards Chase, using his supernatural strength to rip the other one off of Chase's back.

    The Aufhockers now both shifted into tiger forms as the militia used their weapons to try and take the creatures down hard, but were having trouble as the creatures pounced on the militia men's backs.

    "Should we help them?" Chase asked unsure if the ones who had come to their rescue could be trusted at all.

    Before any of them could answer Chase, the four of them heard a noise spotting a sports motorcycle coming up behind them. Leaping out of the way, they now saw a tall figure get off the bike wearing what looked to be a combat suit with a seraphim symbol on the front of it.

    The figure now went over to one of the Aufhockers, using their strength to kick one of them off the militia's back. They then used a special looking gun to shoot the Aufhocker in the head, neutralizing it before it grabbed the creature by it's neck and twisted it. The other Aufhocker ran over to try and jump onto the figure's back, but it managed to quickly roll out of the way and grabbing the Aufhocker by its arms and tearing them off. Nyx, Andromeda, Chase, and Aaron were shocked by the strength of this figure as it then slammed the Aufhocker down onto the ground smashing its head into the ground where it lay dead.

    "Take this these two away and off the streets." The figure said to one of the militia.

    "What about them?" The militia figure asked looking over at the four.

    "Leave them to me." The figure replied as the militia soldier nodded and the super soldier went over to the four friends.

    "The four of you, leave now and forget what you saw here tonight." The soldier said to the group.

    "Forgetting that our boyfriends were attacked by a supernatural creature and that some S.P.E.A.R. knockoff saved our asses? Yeah, I don't think we will." Nyx said crossing her arms.

    "You would do well to respect my request, you do not want to make an enemy of me or our organization." The figure said.

    "What organization?" Andromeda asked.

    "That's classified. Leave now." The figure said as they were about to get back on their motorcycle.

    "Wait, can we trust you?" Chase now asked stepping in front of the motorcycle.

    "You all are starting to get on my nerves. I suggest you let me leave. Now." The figure said the four looked like they weren't going to let this person move.

    The figure now jumped off the bike and kicked Aaron down surprising him as Chase now fought against the figure and before Andromeda or Nyx could lay a hit on the soldier, what they all now recognized to be an actual S.P.E.A.R. truck came up to the four of them as the figure dropped a smoke bomb and got on the bike and drove away as Director Hailey Myers ran out of the vehicle and tried shooting the motorcycle.

    Myers looked at the four of them and shook her head. "Moreland, Harris, Princesses, I'd like to request that you all come with me back to S.P.E.A.R. now." Meyers said firmly.

    "With all due respect Director, I think the four of us would rather we...." Chase began to say.

    "GET THE FUCK IN MY VEHICLE.....NOW!!!!" Director Meyers shouted at them showing her vampire eyes. Something she only did when she was serious.
