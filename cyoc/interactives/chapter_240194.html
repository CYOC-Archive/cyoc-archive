    Ty anxiously awaited Lisa's decision. He felt bad about having gotten Lisa into this awkward position, but if he had not cancelled the wish, his subconscious would have gnawed away at him, making him miserable. Depending on her choice, he might still be, but at least it would be her choice, not his will supernaturally imposed upon her.
    He heard a knock at the open door, and looked up to Lisa. She paused for a moment, and said, "I'm angry with you, and I'm not in love with you."
    His heart sank, but he waited for her to tell him how she wanted to proceed. To his utter amazement, she continued, "The time we've shared was absolutely amazing, and I'm very torn, because I want more of it, but I just don't think I can continue with this anger. I might get over it eventually, and I _want_ to love you, but I just can't right now.
    "However, I think we both want a solution now, not in the indefinite future. After careful consideration, I've decided that I was happier when your wish for me to love you was in place, and I want you make that wish again."
    Ty was dumbfounded. "Wouldn't that just compell you to be a slave to my desires again?"
    "No," Lisa replied, "because this time I'll know that you used the wish to make me love you, and I'll remember that I _chose_ to ask for that. Also, I know that you didn't mean to hurt me, and I'm trusting you not to hurt me in the future. I believe that you're a good person, and that the wish just got away from you in the heat of the moment."
    "I'm not sure I believe in that distinction between loving someone, and only wanting to love them," Ty said. "However, if you're absolutely certain that this is what you want, and that it will make you happy, then I'll do it. I want you to be happy! I want you to remember that if you ever change your mind, all you have to do is tell me, and I can remove the wish again."
    Lisa said, "See? That reinforces my point. Even though I don't think what I feel for you right now is love, your concern for my well-being makes me _want_ to love you, and shows that you love me. Please go ahead with the wish!"
    Despite a little bit of lingering anxiety from his conscience, he decided that she really did want this, and that he should grant it. "I wish for Lisa to be in love with me again," he stated.
    Lisa shivered, and closed her eyes for a moment. "That kind of abrupt mental change takes some time to process," she said. "It is really amazing to consider that I'm solidly in love with you now, but only seconds ago, I was not. But I assure you that this really is what I wanted, and you should never again feel bad about it.
    "Right now, I just want you to hold me for a while. We've both been through a whirlwind today, first of transformations, and now of mental state. Let's just try to relax a bit. I know we'll both want to continue our play in a while."
    Ty said, "Sure, honey! That sounds great!" They sat together, with Ty's arm around her. They had all the time in the world.
