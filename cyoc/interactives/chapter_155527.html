    Changing the Family Gene Pool

    As Jeff looked at his options, he noticed something a little different about his reflection on the screen. He couldn't quite make it out. He stood up and went to the mirror in his closet. Looking back, he saw the same Jeff he has always seen. And yet something seemed different. Did his facial features change just the littlest bit? And was he a bit more fit than before. And yet Jeff could always remember being like this. He did recall Joe wanting him to be more active.

    That's when Jeff figured it out. Now that Joe was his father instead of Johnny, that affected him as well, He was having a little trouble recalling exactly what he looked like before, but he was pretty sure it wasn't that much different. If Joe didn't look so much like John did, Jeff would probably look like an entirely different person.

    Of course, that triggered a new idea for Jeff. Suppose that Joe looked a lot different, that would change Jeff as well. He could make really improve Joe and thus make him pass the genes down to him. Jeff went back to the Chronivac program and looked over Joe's profile. As Jeff considered what to change, he noticed the box labeled 'Race'. That piqued Jeff's interest. He wondered what it would be like to be biracial. He could certain reap some good ethnic looks from Joe if he changed his race.

    And why stop there? Why should his mother be stuck as plain old white? If she was another race, it could blend him into a really attractive guy. The girls would be throwing themselves at Jeff. Not to mention if he gained more of Joe's muscles, he could be really popular in sports.

    The only problem was Johnny. His former father had been his younger brother for only a little while and Jeff was already finding him annoying. He didn't have to a role model for Joe back when he was his brother. Joe clearly expected more from Jeff. Not to mention, John was continuing to root through Jeff's closet. Jeff soon decided having a little brother was a bit more trouble than it was worth. And he didn't think having an older brother would be fun long if Johnny starts to exert his authority. But a twin might a better option. Better yet, a twin sister. Jeff might as well see what Johnny would look like as a biracial daughter.

    Jeff worked over Joe, Cheryl, and Johnny's profiles. The biggest question is what race should he make his parents. He wanted good looks and muscles to inherit from Joe. Making Joe black would certainly make him bigger and stronger. But making Joe Hispanic would give Jeff plenty of charm. As for Cheryl, Jeff wanted an exotic blend to inherit from her. He thought making her Asian before deciding to go with Indian. That would certainly insure he'd have dark skin and increase his exotic good looks. On the other hand, making Cheryl Polynesean could potentially give him the breeding to be extra-large and very muscular. Whatever his choice, Johnny would become his 16 year old sister and Jeff was curious as to how she'd turn out. But what combination did he want to go with?
