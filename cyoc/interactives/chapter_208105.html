She had worked as a trapper and professional hunter in the region, much to the annoyance of the men who wanted her to "settle down" and let them handle things, but she'd found that they'd also been careless and unskilled. It was something that reminded Yashka of the days in the Soviet Union where enemies of the state just disappeared in the dark of the night. She tried to then make inquiries as to what had happened and got a very stern warning that she was already on a "watch list" over her lectures and arguments with other trappers and if she didn't stay quiet, she would be sent to a prison and Bystrouska would go to a zoo. He and his actions had cost her everything and was costing others just as much.
