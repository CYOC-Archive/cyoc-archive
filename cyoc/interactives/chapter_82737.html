    Richard went into her house and saw an immaculately cared for home. Everything in it's place.

    She looked at the pictures on the wall and saw her wedding to Paul. Two men standing up in front of a judge
    exchanging vows.

    They both looked quite hansom.

    Several years passed in the pictures and she sees her children appear in the pictures. They are two boys like
    they had name Simon and Roy just as her children were but they looked like neither of the two of them.

    A few pictures later and she sees the adoption papers perfectly framed.

    As the years roll on in the pictures she stops at the one of the four of them. She now had a full beard and
    Paul was quite a bit rounder.

    I just lost my figure when you decided to have a family. He said chuckling to her at his little quip.

    Well you didn't have to stay home and be the house husband I could have done that.

    Well honey you know you have the higher paying job and there is no way you would have ever stayed home.
    Besides I like cooking and cleaning and looking after the boys.

    Richard realized he was snapping back at Paul like it was nothing that they were a married gay couple.

    Simon being the older of the two was in kindergarten and Paul said he had to go pick him up. He picked Roy up
    out of the play pen and put him in a harness that kept him close to his chest. He felt it made the bond closer
    and he used this with Simon as he grew up and now he was so close to Paul.

    Now off to work baby Paul said to Richard giving him a deep kiss between the beards. Thanks for the nooner.

    She was off and this seemed the strangest as she had always stayed home with their kids and now she was the
    highest wage earner in the household.

    When she got home the two were playing in the living room while Richard was cooking. He was a good cook as his
    400 pound 5'6" body showed. There he was in a frilly apron laboring at the stove. He had taken off his shirt
    and she saw the fur peaking out of the sides of his frilly apron. He wasn't really nelly, the apron was sort
    of a joke Richard had remembered buying for him when they had decided on who would take care of the kids and
    he embraced the apron.

    As Richard came home from work he had noticed that it seemed as though almost every house on the block had a
    rainbow, bear or leather stripe flag hanging from the flag poles. And he thought holly crap he was living in a
    gay community.

    When he wrapped his arms around Paul, which wasn't easy to do he whispered that he loved him.

    Paul said not now I have to get ready. Carl and Jordan are coming over. You know every third Friday night we
    have them over. Now you just watch the kids and read your paper while I get ready.

    Paul went up stairs and showered and got ready and as he was bounding down the stairs, well bounding down as
    much as a 400 pound man can, the door bell rang and there were two men that Richard didn't recognize. They had
    a young male child with them.

    Well now this must be Dustin. Richard said. He must be about Simon's age. Come on in.

    Paul gave both Carl and Jordan a quick peck lasting a little too long on Jordan for Richards liking and he let
    out a rough cough.

    Oh my big bad jealous bubba bear. You know I love you. Just because Jordan and I had a thing before we met
    doesn't mean I want to get back with him.

    The two were football player types. Both had the big barrel chest and thick arms and legs.

    Richard gave them a quick pec and asked them to sit down while Richard excused himself to finish dinner.

    It wasn't more than a few moments when Richard announced dinner is served.

    Leading everyone into the dinning room the four of them sat at the big table while the kids sat at a little
    table in sight of Richard.

    They had a lovely evening and Richard began to loose his memories of being a woman as the evening went on and
    within months she had no more twinges of memory save a little feeling now and then.
