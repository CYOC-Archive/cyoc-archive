    Adam arrived at the store. He told the employee who he was & what he was there for. The employee got them. The employee then confirmed / explained "These costumes will turn the wearer into anthropomorphic goldfish. But it the wearer roughly 11 inches. So the wearer will be a roughly 11 inches anthropomorphic goldfish. Also as an anthromorphic goldfish you will only be able to be out of water for 6 minutes or so. After that your scales will start drying out. And you we'll begin to suffocate because your lungs will shut off while your gills activate. Of course heat can cut that time down." Adam smiled with a "Great. Just what we wanted. And we don't plan on being out of the water for any length of time." The employee replied with "Ok." Adam then left with the costumes. [Why would anyone want to be a 11 inches anthropomorphic goldfish?] The employee thought wondering.
    At the house...
    Adam walked in with the costumes in a bag. He saw Matt & Rebecca sitting on the couch. He also saw the large fish tank all filled & set up on a large table. "That them?" Matt asked excited getting up. "Yep." Adam nodded with a smile. Adam placed the costumes down & looked into the tank. It looked really nice. The fake weeds, the rocks, the little plastic castle, all of it. Adam was quite surprised when a 11 inches anthropomorphic goldfish was dropped into the tank. The goldfish poked his head out of the water saying "Hurry up Adam." "I just saw Matt naked. So it no big deal." Rebecca told him. Adam stripped naked & put on the other anthropomorphic goldfish costume. Almost instantly he felt naked & he shrank to 11 inches too. Rebecca wasted no time in picking Adam up & dropping him in the tank too. The water felt great on Adam's scales & breathing through gills was amazing. The now two 11 inches anthropomorphic goldfish looked at Rebecca through the fish tank glass. "Now for my part. I will feed you two & clean your tank for the next 2 months while you 11 inches anthropomorphic goldfish. I will be your caretaker." Rebecca told them. That was the agreement they had made. Rebecca then turned & began picking up their discarded clothes &:stuff. "But I will only do caretaker stuff for you. I will leave you alone & disregard you the rest of the time because you will just be fish to me." Rebecca further told them. She then left the room with their clothes. That was fine, infact great for the two 11 inches anthropomorphic male goldfish.
