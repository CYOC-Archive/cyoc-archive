    Appears to be a Piñata

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A month ago BM I O

    Dan walks to the living room to greet his parents and tell them what Kat has been saying. He was planning to even mention about her believing she had magic. He sees his parents walking in with last minute party decorations for his sister party.

    "Welcome home. Mom, Dad. You won't believe what Kat has been saying and doing." Dan says to them. But they continue to enter the house and put things down to sort later as if they dont hear him.

    Behind Dan, Kat enters the room amused as she did hear him and saw the supposed snubbing. She greets her parents.

    "Mom, Dad. I love you. I cant wait for tonight" She says as she goes to hug them. They laugh and smile as they return the hug. Even kissing her head fondly.

    Dan watches confused why their parents are ignoring him. "Mom, Dad. Did you not hear me?"

    They dont hear him. Instead, when they look in his direction. Mom says, "why is the pinata in middle of the doorway? Someone could trip"

    Kat was trying not to laugh at Dan horrified face as they both discover what their parents see instead of their son. Dan was apparently the pinata for Kat party.

    Dad sighs. "Sorry dear. I must have put it their when I was trying to organize. Ill make sure their is candy inside and hang it up in the backyard." Dad replies as he walks over to do just that. Dad picked him up with ease and carried him to the kitchen where he had placed the bags of candy and small toy prizes to go inside the pinata.

    Dan tried to protest, but he couldn't see to touch his dad or any other object. "Dad, no! Its me your son. Stop this"

    Kat said nothing, as she focused on helping her mom finish up other party set ups. She was smiling, but her mom just figured she was excited for her birthday.

    In the kitchen, Dan was placed on the counter. Dad grabbed the bags and opened them up to start stuffing them into the pinata.  Dan saw it, and continued to protest to deaf ears.

    Dan was freaking out as whatever Kat spell did, seemed to have worked. But he has no idea to what extent. Dad 'open' the hole to start shoving candy and toys into. His 'hole' was his ass. His dad had spread his ass open and shoved handfuls of candy inside. Small toys scrapped his insides as they were all stuffed inside him. All his dad saw was a piñata for his daughters party. Dan was crying each each new candy and toy, until his dad was satisfied with how packed he was.

    Then his dad carried him to the backyard and went to the tree he was planned to hang Piñata Dan. Dan tried to fight Dad, but could not budge. Finally Dad hooked him up with his arms and legs bound together and tied with another rope that was hanging off a sturdy tree branch. Now dan was midair, waiting for the party to start. His dad looked at his piñata son pleased, then went back inside to continue with the party preparations.

    What will happen to Dan next?
