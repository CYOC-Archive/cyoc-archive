In return he won't come to the Catalysts aid if my organization decides to move against him." The Boss now shook his head, a clucking sound coming from his mouth as he wagged his finger at Fukushu disapprovingly. "Shame, shame, Fukushu. Don't you know marriage is built on trust. Now why would you leave the wifey out of the loop?"

    "What do you mean she left," Abolith asked.

    "It means she's gone," the Boss answered. "Quit. Decided to go solo, try her luck on her own. Be master of her domain, a strong independent woman."

    "We get it," Fukushu replied angrily. "Where did she go?"

    "Your guess is as good as mine," the Boss shrugged. Fukushu and Abolith casting him a dubious look. "I'm being serious. Turns out she was able to destroy our counter measures as well as any tracking methods. We haven't seen her since Evans untimely passing."

    "Why should I believe you," Abolith asked.

    "I've got no reason to lie," the Boss said. "There's no advantage. If I did have Morgan, I'd show you. And you both know that." Fukushu and Abolith nodded, the Bosses arrogance was such that if Morgan was present, he would have her make a grand entrance.

    "Now" the Biss said taking a breath. "We have a bit of a situation here. You two are trespassing. Step away from the computer and comply with a search. I want to make sure you two aren't trying to take any of our trade secrets."

    "And if I refuse," Fukushu threatened, drawing his blade slowly into an attack position. The soldiers now leveled their rifles, several red dots from the laser scopes resting on Abolith and Fukushu. The Boss though was unmoved, taking a step forward before answering.

    "You willing to bet your sons life on that," he asked, his voice venomous. "Do you really think that we don't have our people keeping an eye on him? Our pact is keeping them from harming him but believe me. You break it and I give my word he'll suffer before we finish him off."

    Fukushu and Abolith tensed, their eyes going wide with anger. Soghing the two nodded, Fukushu sheathing his sword before glaring at the Boss. As soldiers now approached, he stopped them. "Search Fukushu I'll take care of Abolith."
