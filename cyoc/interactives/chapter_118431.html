    Clown Cuties, pt. 2

    added by The Man In Black 6 years ago BM

    Allie had never been more scared. She had just seen her sister become a clown, and she really wanted to run away. "Wow!! This is amazing Mom! I feel like I can do anything!" Stacy giggled at Billie's enthusiasm. "Well, it should be your turn now, sis." Billie said happily. "M-mom! Billie! Please! I don't wanna be a clown!" "Oh don't worry, sweetie," Stacy said kindly. "You'll learn to love it." Ty snapped his fingers.

    Allie watched in horror as her skin turned white, her long brown hair turned bubblegum pink, and her nose popped into a pink ball. Her lips grew and turned red, pink circles formed over her eyes, and red hearts appeared on her cheeks. A giant yellow and purple bow appeared on her head. "No, this can't be-" Allie started, but stopped when her clothes started changing. Her shirt and shorts formed together into a fancy blue and pink dress with plenty of lace, ribbons, and puffy shoulder pads. Purple opera gloves covered her arms, frilly purple petticoats and bloomers were visible under her dress. Yellow and purple tights covered her legs and feet, while her shoes turned into giant pink clown mary-janes.

    Allie was now very conflicted. She was now a scary clown, but she had always loved dressing up. Allie had never worn anything so pretty, she felt like a princess. She began to wonder, was it worth it to be a clown, if she could also be a princess? This was enough to satisfy Ty, though. Allie stood still a moment, and woke up a happy little clown girl. "Thanks so much, Billie!" She said. "I should have known it was gonna be fun!" "No problem, sis!" Billie said, giving her sister a hug. "Thanks mom!" They said together. "Anytime, my little jokers!" Stacy said, embracing her daughters. Ty felt like crying. This was something he had never had. A real family.
