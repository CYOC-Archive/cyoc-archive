    Healing Factor?

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 15 years ago BM S

    "REGEN is designed to simulate the ability of some creatures to regenerate lost limbs and
    regrow nerve tissue. I don't know if it's possible for you to grow back your eye, but it
    would be a perfect test, Sage. I should warn you, however, that there could be some side
    effects."

    Sage frowned slightly. "Like...what?"

    "We haven't seen anything yet, but humans aren't designed to grow back missing body
    parts. If there's a flaw in your DNA, the new tissue could be mutated or deformed."

    Sage shrugged. "So I could get ugly. Is that all."

    Marcy had serious doubts about letting Sage use the REGEN medication. Sure, it could
    heal her body, but the younger woman had serious psychological trauma. If there were
    negative side effects, it could send Sage over the edge. In the end, however, she passed
    Sage the bottle of REGEN pills.

    "Now remember, just one a day. And stick to the suggested diet, and get a lot of
    excercise, ok honey? I'll see you in a week."

    The first few days, Sage felt better, but it wasn't until the fourth day that she noticed her
    scars had almost completely vanished. She seemed to be gaining muscle mass as well, her
    figure was more trim, and she'd gotten some tone. She visited Skye that day, and even a
    confrontation with her lover's father didn't dim her good mood.

    The money she was earning would keep Skye alive...and who knows? This drug might
    eventually help her!

    The next day, Sage found her scars completely vanished, and she was the picture of
    health. Her hair had grown considerably, and now reached her shoulders. Plus, her right
    eye was itching like crazy. Could it actually come back?

    Day six finally came, and she'd gained more muscle, even though it was only noticeable
    when she flexed. Taking off her eyepatch, she'd found her eye had grown back, although
    it was cloudy, and she couldn't see very well out of it.

    Her clothing was a bit tighter on her, and measuring herself, she realized she'd actually
    grown two inches during the week!

    "Oh great, that's all I need, to get taller." She frowned, but only for a moment. She looked
    like less of a stick figure, and she had to admit, maybe she was just a little cute. Tying her
    hair (which now reached her back) into a ponytail, she went for a jog.

    She was seeing better out of her right eye all the time, and by that afternoon, her vision
    was better than ever; before she'd been a touch near sighted, but she could see clearly
    much farther.

    Her body was in a perfect state of health after six days, which made her wonder if she
    should take the last pill before her check-up.

    Unable to get in touch with Marcy, she decided nothing could go wrong, and took the last
    pill before she went to bed.
