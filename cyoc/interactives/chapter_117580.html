    Congratulations are in order! You, Robin, after a multitude of long and agonizing years, have finally completed college and earned that well deserved degree. With your newfound ability to become independent from your family, its about high time you moved out of your parent's basement and relocate to the big city, allowing your parents to retire from childrearing. Before this could actually be done however, you really needed to find a place to live for when you arrived. Considering the substantial loans taken to reach this point already, your first home has to obviously be purchased on an extremely small budget. Luckily, after a few hours of google searching, you came across a fairly large and apparently nice, at most worn down, self-owned condo based in the photos in a relatively safe area, all for a surprisingly low amount of money if purchased upfront due to lack of residents. Asking your parents for one last parting gift, which had actually went more smoothly than expected, you quickly agreed to the terms of the contract and hastily made preparations for the big day. Unbeknownst to you though, there was a reason for this building's vacancy. As the stories go, ever since the first tenets set foot inside, all those who moved to this establishment have always ended up going missing and are never seen again. The at the time rumors were so bad a police officer was sent to check the place out, but even he disappeared. Can you believe there are things that have occurred that are even stranger then that? There are people who have come out of that horror attraction who people know never set foot there in the first place. Not even the homeless around the neighborhood have dared approaching the place. Yet, here you are, one of the few brave souls live within the property lines in recent memory. Will you discover the answers hidden within the mystery? Will you miraculously be the first person to liver here in peace? Will you forever be changed from the events about to unfold?
