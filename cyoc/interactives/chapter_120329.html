    "Droni cara miine." Sei said in a deep and booming voice

    "Um, excuse me?" Mike asked

    "Uo? Yugo cani sor kiar?" Sei asked tilting his head confused.

    "Can you speak English Sei? You know instead of whatever language you are using." Mike asked ans Sei tilted his head again confused,"Yugo?"

    "Look just nod or shake your head okay?" Mike asked and Sei nodded

    "Considering that you aren't currently trying to swallow me I'm guessing that you haven't changed mentality right?" Mike asked and Sei nodded.

    Suddenly a blinding light came from the armor and suddenly Mike and Sei were inside a cave that was much more suited for a dragon of Sei's size. The cave also had lots of gold and miscellaneous treasures, and Sei seemed to take an interest in that and layed down on a large pile of it. "Sei what are you doing?!" Mike yelled and Sei looked at mike puzzled.

    "Right, dumb question considering you can't talk." Mike sighed and facepalmed
