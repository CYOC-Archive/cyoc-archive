It turned completely grey and the material became a basic cotton. Big sweat stains appeared on it. The new Mitch thought "Of course, my only shirt! I always wear it.", while the old Mitch was mortified at wearing such a 'disgusting' piece of clothing.

Like his old dress shirt, the material changed into a cheap cotton. It started to thicken, and a large pouch formed in the front. It felt nice, warm, and confortable, but Mitch felt something was missing. As if on cue, a thick hood with a white, thick, fluffy interior grew out from the back. The new hoodie started to soil, from months of use, a hole appeared in the left cuff.

The material changed to denim, from the fine cloth it once was. They started to squirm halfway down his ass, and widen a bit. Mitch pulled them back up, but they slid right back down to where they were before, they were too big for him.

The fine, black dress socks changed color to grey, and became thicker, a hole emerged around his big toe on his right foot. "Man, I got to steal me a pair of shoes..." thought the new Mitch, and for a moment the old Mitch found himself agreeing. "Yeah, maybe som- wait! Steal?" Mitch;s morals were changing. Living on the street for so long made him have more of a negative outlook on life. He would lie, cheat, or steal if he had to. Anything to survive. The Old Mitch started to fade away, being replaced with the new street dwelling Mitch.
