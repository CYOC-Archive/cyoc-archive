    Arriving at Pleasure Island during the Year of the Dragon (alternate)

    added by Anonymous A year ago A BM S Mythological Magic Dragon

    The boat reached the island. You were finally here, Pleasure Island. A land where all your dreams come true. They were closed after some financial difficulties but now they were finally open for business again with an all new theme. This year they were doing a special on dragons. Being a huge fan of dragons since you were 3 you naturally bought a ticket.

    You stepped off onto the dock with your roller luggage and bags and checked in getting your arm band.

    Then you made your way down the dirt path coming to a cross road with a sign that read: “European” and pointed left, and “Eastern” and pointed right.

    You went left and kept walking through a forest till it opened up to a clearing.

    You reached the hotel you would be staying at, themed after a medieval castle with a moat. On the outside at least, inside it was a mostly normal hotel but still kept the castle aesthetic in some places. You scanned your keycard then went up an elevator to your room and let go of your bags with a sigh of relief.

    You go to bed and sleep you have a lot of things to do tomorrow, you don’t know where to start.

    Eventually the night fades and gives way to the morning sun it’s a new day. You have a lot of plans for today and you don’t know what you want to do first.
