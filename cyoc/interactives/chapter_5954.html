    Sarah sees her friend Victoria out then stands in the door watching the other lady walk toward the park exit. Idly thinking what a good Spitz Victoria would make the zoo lady blinks and shakes her head. “Been working here too long when I start seeing all humans as a breed of dog!” she tells herself chuckling and shaking her head. A twist of key and the main kennel door is locked for the night. “Now to let the night shift in. Ashley! Lisa!” she calls and is answered by excited barks. Smiling she opens the kennel cage to her left to release two bitch Rottweiler. The pair of dogs both wearing heavy leather collars to protect their throats greet their mistress with glad barks and rapid tail wags. Once having broken into the kennel to steal as humans, the two bitches threw years of training and proper conditioning having become loyal pets of Sarah. Each bitch two hundred pounds of muscle thanks to Sarah having experimented over the years with her canine building.

    “Follow” Sarah tells her bitches as she starts walking down the row of cages. Her bitches follow at her heels stopping at each cage to get the scents of the dogs within. Any new dog finds its kennel getting longer sniffs.

    “Time for sleeping!” Lisa barks in a tone of command.

    “Clean out your food bowls!” Ashley tells the dogs of each cage the pair pass.

    And well the different males drool openly or simply stare at the two giant well muscled dogs that smell female their bitches give mates a bite and set everyone to following the orders. As each kennel that is passed quickly has clean food bowls being pushed out into the walk way.

    “My two good bitches.” She tells her dogs pleased at how well the other bitches react to her pets. Well yes she did have to turn them loose on one or two females that thought themselves above being part of the kennel pack. For the most part the kennel dogs embrace being part of a good once they are firmly shown their pack ranks. Smiling she watches as dogs in each kennel will either lift muzzle to show throat or roll to show belly if one of her girls begins to stare at them too long.

    “Nancy! Wake up!” Riley barks nudging his pack mate as he hears the nightly guard dog patrol getting closer. Afraid of the two bitches he tucks his tail under them as he looks down at the half full dish in front of Nancy that he is not allowed to eat.

    “Nancy!” Joanne barks then gives the other bitch a hard bite which gets her up on all fours with a loud canine yelp. “Now eat all in your bowl and no complaints!” She tells her sister as her tail lifts up.

    “Not hung…” Nancy starts to tell her pack but as she smells the fear coming from both dogs she shivers.

    “Do you want your pack to be in danger of attack?” Joanne asks.

    Nancy whines but begins eating first by trying to snoop the food with her paw. But as she feels her tail get nipped and hears the click of canine nails of the approaching guard dogs she shivers. The pack fright affecting her as well instincts cause her react in a daze. Snapping out of it she whines not wanting to eat. But as she looks down at her food dish she finds it licked clean and the taste of gravy in her mouth.

    Joanne all ready pushing the dish out side the cage with her nose Nancy finds her puzzling the mystery out cut short. The water dish is empty as well and is quickly pushed out by Riley. As the zoo lady passes their cage soon followed by the twin guard bitches.

    “Hello and welcome Nancy.” Lisa calls giving her stump tail a wag.

    “Call here.” Ashley calls as she stops beside her twin at the cage door to the Newfoundland kennel.

    Finding herself pushed forward by Riley and Joanne she moves to the pair as she finds her personal smell sniffed by both female. Doing the canine greeting without even having to think about it Nancy finds herself relaxing. The two bitches giving her warm nuzzles and licks while the other sniffs under her tail she finds herself liking the pair of bitches and returning the friendship.

    “Good sister.” The guard dog pair tells her before moving on which fills Nancy with happiness at being praised. Pressing against the wire mesh of her cage door she whines softly wanting to follow her two friends.

    Ashley and Lisa give each other a knowing looks then quicken their pace to catch up with the mistress who feeds each a treat of cheese.

    “Good bitches making friends with our new arrival.” She praises bending down to kneel by her pets and ruffle their ears. This of course sends the two females into rushes of delight at their mistress giving them her attention.
