    NOW, ON WITH THE EARTH-1 STORY...

    After their slow and leisurely last intimate act for the night, werewolf Ben Twist and his girlfriend, the werepanthress Kenya Adaeze, reverted from their fursonas back to their human forms, for it was a Moon Lake law that no were could be seen actively in public except at authorized events or in dire life threatening emergencies.
    As the sun began to rise higher in the morning sky, of course there was still time for a naked kiss or three before the couple got dressed, gathered their things from the forest floor, and walked back to Ben's car, holding hands along the way.
    "So, what do you want to do today honey?" Ben asked, while stowing everything into his trunk.
    "Other than a desire to be with you, I am open to suggestions," purred Kenya, while nuzzling up to him.
    Thoughts of the mocha skinned beauty with and without fur filled Ben's mind as he hastily suggested breakfast to get his mind off what he really wanted to do.
    "The diner again?" asked Kenya, a little disappointed.
    "Why? Something wrong?" wondered Ben in return.
    "It is a fine establishment, but too many meals from one place does become boring after a while. Besides wanting to show off my culinary skills, I was hoping for someplace a little more quiet where we could just... how do you say, hang out?"
    Ben thought about that for a moment, then realized, "The only options open to us are either your place or mine."
    "My aunt and uncle probably had as romantic a night as we did," said Kenya with a smile, "so I'd rather not disturb them if we can avoid it. What about your place?"
    Ben thought about it for a moment. He had already been to Kenya's house several times since their relationship began, but she to his only twice. It was now a little after 7 that Sunday morning. A decent time for a workday, but on the weekend...?
    "My moms probably had a great time too while I was away, but let me text and ask them," said Ben, while reaching for the cellphone he wore on an over-sized lanyard around his neck to stay in touch with those he loved while in his werewolf form.

    MEANWHILE...
    Nestled within their king sized bed, the married lesbian werecats Samantha and Frances Twist were curled up within each others furry arms, purring happily in their sleep being together, when both heard their respective cellphones chime an incoming message alert.
    Being the Town Chancellor with Mayor Stovall away on family business, Samantha feared checking hers as she groggily reached for it, when Frances announced, "It's Ben. He wants to know if Kenya and he can come over, with her offering to make breakfast."
    Dutifully making sure hers had the same message instead of town business, Samantha turned to her wife and asked, "What do you think?"
    "We'll have to defur and get dressed, but it's up to you."
    Samantha thought about it for a moment. At first she had been a little reluctant to accept Kenya when their adopted son expressed an interest in her, but that was more from not trusting a total stranger and being overprotective of her only child than anything else, but she had come around and was starting to like the werepanthress. "If Ben is as in love with her as we're in love with each other, it will give me a chance to get to know Kenya better. Go ahead and accept the offer," Samantha said, as she started reverting to her human form.
    Frances simply smiled at her mate, for she deeply loved her wife in both personalities, despite the rough edges she still displayed sometimes, and texted a reply to Ben.

    With her bathrobe on, a human looking Samantha Twist opened the front door of her house to retrieve the Sunday Moon Lake Gazette and saw a couple she didn't recognize leaving her next door neighbors the Andersons.
    A quick sniff of the air, since the breeze was thankfully blowing in her direction, revealed a trace of werefox, werevixen, werecat, and a long period of sex coming from next door.
    That will definitely be an interesting conversation topic the next time I get to talk with Martha alone, thought an amused Samantha, as she went back inside.

    "Okay. We're on," said Ben, after reading his mom's reply. "Do we need anything from the store before we get there?"
    "I am uncertain," admitted Kenya, as she got in her boyfriend's car and buckled her seat belt. "What do you have versus what would everyone prefer for breakfast?"
