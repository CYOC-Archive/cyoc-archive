    Jake thought for a little bit and decided to go back to being 50% monkey after all, he had only just become one when he had to change back when Melissa came to grab her stuff.

    "Ok" Jake said as he set the TF gun back to 50% anthro monkey "back to being half monkey" and zapped himself once more.
    It all came back again, the shrinking, fur and tail growing, hand feet and a dopey monkey face with his clothes once again shrinking to fit his new form.

    "There" Jake said in his higher pitched monkey voice, "now what to do next?"

    While he did want to continue clearing out the kitchen's supply of bananas, Jake wanted to further test out his monkey dexterity, especially so with his monkey feet. A good way to do that besides picking things up with his feet could be...

    Jake got an idea and raced back up to the bathroom and looked up at the shower curtain rod, "Let's try this." Jake said with a dopey grin on his face.

    First leaping up onto the sink and then jumped up and grabbed the rod with his hands before swinging himself up and grabbed on with both feet. Then he let himself just dangle like the silly half monkey he was.

    " This is soo cool!" Jake said while caught in a mix of chittering and hoots of excitement.

    Just then another idea popped into his head and he then wrapped his tail around the curtain rod before letting his feet go, truly dangling.

    After a couple minuets he dropped down, landing on all fours and made his way back down stairs. Wanting to watch TV now Jake jumped up on the back end of the couch before dropping himself down on the front seat. He looked at the TV remote which compared to his now 3ft tall body was a bit larger but nonetheless he was able to at least pick it up with his feet and even press individual buttons with his finger like toes.

    Clicking the TV on and turning it to the news Jake sat there for a bit while thinking more about what to do next with the TF gun. He had a good idea about the anthro setting now that he tested it with being a monkey but he was still very curious. "If this is what being 50% anthro monkey was then what would 100% anthro be like?" Jake thought to himself and began to ponder what to do next...
