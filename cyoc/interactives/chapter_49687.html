    The remaining three men looked on nervously as Marcine and Jack left the room, talking incessantly about fawns. It creped them out and frightened them to a great extent. Much of the "transform or dare" was nothing more then a game to them, but now they strongly had a feeling that there was more to it then that. Especially when the one who held power over them was a little glass wolf who talked like she was "Alice in Wonderland". They half expected her to begin reciting Jabberwocky rather then finish the curse they had chosen.

    They were wrong as Alice turned to them. The glass wolf gave a slight smile as she turned.

    "Those who are dared will still be of great service to the Pack," Alice spoke, "your formal positions are gone but you are still to serve in all things... you are to loyally protect the pack from all things. You are never to reveal us and insure that no one else does. For as shadows of your known masters, you shall do as they would do, to protect the Pack. And would break no law to do so. You will also only use your turn to benefit the Pack and its Master, the Big Bad Wolf."

    The two men slowly came to attention and nodded. They were horrified as the "dare" seemed to make them mindless slaves to these creatures, but they also found they couldn't fight it. They bowed to Jensen and then made their way out

    "Did it work?" Marcy asked Jenny as the two watched them leave the room.

    "I think so," Jenny answered, "they'll likely go about a routine that Bart did before... before yesterday when everything got better."

    "What if they do what you did before yesterday?" Marcy asked.

    Jenny nearly snorted with laughter at the thought of that. Two large strong men in women's business suits, pantyhose, and high heels.

    "We'd need to take pictures then," Jenny said with a giggle.

    "Shhh," Kaylee spoke to the two pups, "Alice is coming to the last person."

    Martha lead Bart and Kevin up to the second floor to find Fran and Darla having taken over as the pack's "lookouts" as they waited for the rest of the staff to arrive. Both Bart and Kevin looked a little depressed at not being able to observe how things went, but Martha sensed that even if the people weren't openly hostile, their could be trouble. So, the two pups were being taken upstairs and to the study where they would be out of the way and there would be no fight.

    "Go along into the study," Martha spoke to the pups, "I will be along shortly, and I expect to find you both reading."

    "Yes, Miss Martha," Bart and Kevin answered and went continued on their way.

    Hopefully, Bart's ideas would go well and come the next day, everything would be secure and that pups would not need to rush to stay out of sight. Martha also took the pause to approach the twins.

    "You can relax," Martha spoke, "the rest of the staff essentially did the same job I did before. They shouldn't be expected for another hour at the longest."

    "It seems like you all maintained rather easy hours here," Fran commented, sounding slightly nervous, "we would have been on the job for a couple of hours by now, if we were still our former selves..."

    "... an we wouldn't get off until after two or four PM depending time of year or officers available," Darla finished.

    Master and Mistress Smith worked everyone hard," Martha answered, trying to explain her past, but finding it hard to compare being a maid with police work, "but they were fair and could be generous at times."

    Fran and Darla only nodded. Both wanted to say more, but found themselves too nervous to do so, and Martha quickly left to go into the study.
