    Hannah paced anxiously as she watched Darren enter the basement window. She, like Darren, thought this was probably the dumbest thing they could do on a Friday night. To be honest she had hoped to just hangout with Darren that night and watch a couple of scary movies.

    "Hes taking an awfully long time."

    Hannah turned towards Steven, rolling her eyes at his obvious statement. Steven always seemed to have a way of saying the dumbest things. As she was about to answer, the group could hear Darren scream.

    Shocked the group turned towards the house, too frightened to move as thwyvwatched Darren quickly try to scramble out the window. Darren looked petrified as he now clamored onto the grounds, only to lose his footing. At first Hannah had simply thought he had tripped, until she noticed a hand grabbing onto Darren's leg.

    "Something has him," she yelled as she ran forward. Not surprisingly the idea of Darren getting caught immediately caused most of the middle schoolers to sprint as far away from the house as possible. Hannah now found herself next to Darren, with only a couple of their schoolmates lingering on the street, too petrified to move.

    "I've got you Darren," Hannah called as she now grabbed his hand and began to pull. Darren and Hannah now locked eyes, the young teen frightened beyond words as his best friend pulled him to safety. It was only when the two looked back at the window did Hannah gasp in fright.

    It couldnt be possible! Hannah would never have believed it unless she had seen it with her own eyes. A demon, a real life demon now held Darren's leg and was slowly pulling him backwards. The demon itself appeared almost childlike covered in red leathery skin with beady eyes. As the demon smiled, Hannah could see small needle like teeth glinting in the moonlight. Not knowing what else to do, Hannah kicked the demon directly in the face.

    "Owww damn it that hurt!"

    If Hannah and Darren hadn't been so frightened, they would have wondered how they had not only hurt a demon but why a demon was speaking English. As the two teens now sprinted away from the home they could see Steven and a couple others screaming for them to run.

    Those screams though quickly became more frantic. Steven and the others watched as a large anthro fox man began to form from the shadows. The fox man seemed rather annoyed as he stared at the group, his six tails swaying violently behind him.

    "Run man, run! Dont look back just RUN!"

    Darren and Hannah sprinted from the grounds, not even bothering to look back. If they had they would have seen a small smirk spread across the kitsunes face. As smirk that would vanish shortly when he discovered what the teen had taken.

    Grasped tightly in Darren's hand the teen didn't realize he was now in possession of both the Ring of Age and Transformation.
