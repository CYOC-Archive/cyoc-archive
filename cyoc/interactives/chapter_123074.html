You scream and tug with all your might, but to no avail as your feet and legs up to your knees are sucked in as well. As your head is slowly pulled in, you find yourself feeling drowsy. As a tingling sensation overtakes your aimless form, you hope your death comes quickly.

Your energy, your being, your soul, whatever you want to call it, is being stretched and pulled like taffy, growing large and filling up spaces in the dragon's body. You feel pressure as your form is absorbed into the dragon's tail, hindquarters, stomach, chest, front legs, and neck. You flop the next around as you rush into the dragon's head, it's eyes opening wide as you throw its (your?) head back and let out a her of fire.

    You take a few uneasy steps forward, not accustomed to your size or bulk. You feel a vague ticklish sensation near your forelegs, back legs, and tail.

    (Shit! Are you in complete control?)
    She asks. "Yes." You say. However, no matter how much the tickling continues, you still remain in complete control. (You terrible human, give me my body back!) She cries.

    You simply smile slyly and shake your head. "No can do, miss. You were going to kill me or something when I walked on the cloud." You admire your gleaming red scales again. "I'm going to have a little fun..."
