    The Magic Shop

    From father and son to son and dad

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago AR AP BM S

    "I'm home!" Jeremy shouted as he came in his house. It was unusually quiet, maybe his parents hadn't arrived yet. He dropped his bag in a chair and took a glass of water. He was watching his magic ring all the time grinning at the idea of becoming someone else's age.

    "If this thing is real and it can alter reality then... I could be a grown up or a little kid and no one would care about it." Jeremy thought about swapping age with his father and becoming the man of the house or swapping with sister and getting the attention she got as the little one.

    He was thinking about it that he almost didn't notice his father coming in until they accidentally touched each other in the living room.

    "I'm sorry buddy." He apologized when he suddenly stop moving like he was in a trance.

    "Don't worry, dad. Dad? Dad, what happened to you?" Jeremy said as he watched his 43 years old father standing still. A mystic glow covered his before it moved inside his ring. Then, his father started to change, his arms and legs shortened, his beard disappeared and his height went down until he looked like any other guy in Jeremy's class.

    Then, his small clothing started to change. His tight pants turned into black training short and his ragged shirt became a grey tank top that showed up some of his newly got hair. Finally, a pair of sneakers appeared on his formerly exposed feet completing his outfit. He seemed ready to go to a gym or so.

    "Dad! Watch your steps! You're going to fall on me!!!" Jeremy's younger father shouted as he began to move again.

    "S-sorry." Jeremy apologized realizing that his father's mind has changed too and now he was his son instead. Unfortunately, he didn't get new memories so he had to act accordingly. "I was distracted."

    "I can see it. Are you going to the gym? Can you drive me to Erika's home, we've got a...study date." He asked as he took Jeremy's bag, which was his only a moment ago.

    "S-sure." Jeremy replied not quite sure if he can drive a car despite his new look. He also wondered if the Erika his son was talking about was the same Erika he had a crush on. Then, he followed him as they came out of their house when Jeremy looked at his mother coming home.

    "I'm too old to be her son now. So, what will happen?" Jeremy thought before his mom noticed his presence.
