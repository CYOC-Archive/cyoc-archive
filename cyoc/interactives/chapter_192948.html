    A week and a half ago, Kyra had taken up the courage to go to her former school mate Wyatt's house to check on him. She had only ever spoken to Wyatt once or twice a week at school, she didn't want to admit it, but she had a small crush on him. They both could relate with family issues, where as Wyatt only had his mother and grandmother, Kyra had both of her parents. Kyra's parent's were barely anything but loving to her, both of her parents were into drugs a lot and she would usually take up a beating from them. She decided to venture to Wyatt's house both for company and comfort.

    Kyra was surprised to find two of the most beautiful women she'd seen on the other side of the door when it was opened. She felt compelled to come in due to their overwhelming beauty and found her sexual awakening for the first time when she kissed both of them, then Wyatt entered the fold and she was drawn to him more than she had been before. Wyatt bit down on her neck and at first she was frightened that she would die, but then she felt something inside of her, she hadn't before: power. Kyra and Wyatt then made their way to Kyra's home where she made her parents vampires as well, but made them aware that from that moment on, she was in charge of them.

    Now in the present day, Wyatt and Kyra stood before Lord Miro as he sat in his chair taking in a few breaths and drinking from a wine glass. Next to Lord Miro, the two watched as the ferocious little Ann tore up and ate a vampire who had recently come back with some bad news like a vulture consuming its food.

    "I don't know how much more I can take of this. I send these vampires out and they come back with nothing but news of vampire deaths by the hands of hunters, and wizards. Tell me....Wyatt, wasn't it? Tell me why I shouldn't let Little Ann devour you like a Thanksgiving Meal!" Lord Miro shouted as he pounded his fist down on his chair.

    Kyra and Wyatt whispered to one another for a moment before Wyatt stepped forward.

    "Lord Miro, with all due respect, I believe we've been going about this all wrong." Wyatt said bowing before letting Kyra speak now.

    "Wyatt and I have both come from backgrounds with....hardships. The vampires you've gotten have been....interesting over the years." Kyra said looking at little Ann who glared at her before continuing her meal.

    "Kyra and I believe that we should turn only those who feel like they have nothing left in the world. On the other hand, we have plenty of those that were nothing but homeless, drug addicted, runaways, and I think we should use these as what they are meant to be." Wyatt explained in great detail.

    "And what are they to be used for?" Lord Miro asked as little Ann finished eating the vampire corpse.

    "Cannon fodder." Wyatt and Kyra both said at the same time.

    Lord Miro and little Ann looked at each other and smirked before looking again at Wyatt and Kyra. Lord Miro stood up from his chair and walked over to Wyatt putting a hand on his shoulder.

    "I wish you had been my son. You're so much....more like me than he ever was." Lord Miro said as he left the room with little Ann giving a nod to the two of them.

    "I expect results you two." Lord Miro said as he shut the door behind them leaving the two of them in the room.

    Wyatt was almost shocked while Kyra seemed surprised at what had just happened.

    "I can't believe he said that to me." Wyatt said to Kyra.

    "I can't believe little Ann walked out of here without threatening us." Kyra said as Wyatt chuckled a little.

    "But you and I have a duty to him and I have a duty to you." Wyatt said as he got on one knee.

    "Kyra, will you do me the honor of being at my side as not only my partner, but my wife?" Wyatt asked as Kyra nodded in reply.

    "But what will your mother say?" Kyra teased.

    "Oh please. Stella and Eloise will be jealous that I didn't ask them first." Wyatt said as they both left the room.
