    The pendulum swings to the other side

    The mirror Jennifer explained, "First you need to give control of the body to me. Don't worry. I take care of everything. I give your body back to you when I want to tap out. So relax and take a backseat while I do my magic, okay?"

    Jennifer listened to the offer and accepted it. In that same moment Jennifer's devilish smile returned as she looked and acted differently. Justin opened the front door as he walked inside the house and saw Jennifer in the bathroom.

    "Hey! How in the fuck did you get there?" Justin yelled in surprise as he was removing his belt from his trousers.

    Jennifer was fully preparing what was about to come as she had done this yesterday. What her other self didn't know that when she passed out. She went to provoke Justin more to get more love taps. Wishing for more physical attention. It was clear that Jennifer's mind would choose her as the dominant one. Jennifer pushed her tongue out playfully as she was preparing herself to receive the same treatment as yesterday. But Justin stopped. He was getting weirded out by Jennifer who had suddenly changed and acted on her own will. From Justin's eye she could tell that he saw her as a full born demon, possessed by it, who was begging for the punishment. Jennifer had no fear in her eyes and that made him stop. Justin didn't like how Jennifer was moving on her own but he was curious. He could tell that something weird was going on.

    "Jennifer." Justin opened his mouth. "I've seen a lot of dolls in my life when I was living with my father and I can see that you are one of a kind. The rare collector's item. The missing diamond in my collection that would make my own father jealous. The perfect doll. All I need to know is that if you can do something, anything, to show that you are special."

    Justin pulled up a chair and watched what Jennifer was going to do next. Jennifer used the shower curtain as a prop to perform a little dance to her husband. Justin wanted to deny that he loved the dance. He had to punish Jennifer for not obeying him. It was conflicting. Justin had never seen a doll that enjoyed the punishment was seeking out for it. The two previous doll before her were broken long time ago as their minds weren't strong. Justin was captivated. It was his first time letting his doll to do something. Justin sat there. Watching with conflicted expression as he didn't know how to proceed. Jennifer in other hand knew. She wanted to be in power with Justin. A personal adjudant, second-in-command or that special someone for him. The dance had broken the power dynamic between them.

    Justin let out a loud cough as he stood up. He raised his head and lowered it gently down to look at Jennifer. She looked back and Justin was asking her an odd question.

    "Jennifer. If you can understand me. Tell me. How much is two plus two?" Justin asked sternly. What was she going to do? Was this a trick question? Jennifer could understand what he was asking but should she reveal to Justin that she understood him? It was a tough choice.
