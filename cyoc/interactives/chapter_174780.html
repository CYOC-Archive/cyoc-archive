    While Ty was enjoying the view from the quiet, park bench, a preteen boy strolled right past him, carrying a soccer ball in his arms. He wandered over to the large soccer field where older guys were about to play a short game. The boy stopped along the sideline and shouted something to one of the players. Clearly the players were about 16 and 17 years old while the boy was only 10 or 11.

    “Hey, Shawn! I can play with you guys?” he shouted at one of the older, taller boys who was standing on the field, wearing a jersey and cleats.

    “No, Charlie! You’re too young and too small. I don’t want you to get hurt when the game gets too rough!” the bigger boy replied. “Maybe when you’re a few years older!”

    “I’m always too young, too short, too weak and too small to play with you! It’s not fair! I don’t wanna wait till I’m older! I wanna play NOW!” Charlie shouted back in disgust. “I hate being so small!”

    Ty picked up on the shouting match and he slowly got up from the bench and walked over to the sideline, standing just behind Charlie. Using his wonderful powers, he figured out that Charlie was weeks from turning 11 and that Shawn was his older, 17-year-old bigger brother.

    “I guess it’s time for little Charlie to grow up into a big, teenage soccer star!” Ty grinned, as he carefully focused his powers on the young preteen standing a few feet in front of him. “He needs to grow and gain some height and muscle, right… NOW!”

    Suddenly, Charlie felt an intense tingling sensation which raged all over his short, thin body. His muscles were burning, like he had just completed a grueling workout. His feet suddenly outgrew his sandals, which he was wearing since he was holding his soccer cleats in his hands. His feet grew by three shoe sizes, becoming much, much bigger!

    His short, skinny legs then stretched out tremendously, shooting straight up and making the boy grow over a foot taller. But that was not all. The long, skinny legs next began to fill out with raw muscle as the thin quads filled out with thick, wide mounds of raw muscle everywhere. His big quads now blew out of his thin shorts, ripping apart his pants as they grew wider than his waist. Mighty columns of corded muscle formed like huge teardrops above the knees, making Charlie look like a pro cyclist or a rugby player.

    Next, his calves ballooned from the back of his legs into big, heavy, wide and large melon-sized masses of raw muscle covered in veins like those of IFBB professional bodybuilders. Suddenly, Charlie felt some surge of physical power and strength running through his legs which he had never felt before. He had the energy to run many laps around the soccer field at tremendous speed now, and to kick the soccer ball over great distances and at high speed!

    Next, his torso grew wider, longer and deeper, filling out his stomach with thick, rectangular buttons which are his well-defined abdominal muscles, forming across his torso liked a washboard. They form a solid wall of muscle, becoming a well-padded shield of armor over his core that protects his midsection from any blows.

    Well above the abs, his chest expands into two large orbs of pectorals, big, solid and round, widening the shoulders and giving him that impressive V-taper that all bodybuilders crave. His upper torso now becomes twice as wide as his waist. With large mounds of muscle sticking out by more than 8 inches from his impressive frame. All that growth caused Charlie to rip out of his t-shirt as well, so Ty noticed that he needed to give him new clothes to wear once the growth spurt was over.

    Charlie’s thin stick-like arms inflate into mounds of muscle, with large, round triceps and biceps growing into big lumps of impressive vein-covered muscle on his upper arms while his lower arms grow into big bowling pins. Clearly, the boy now looks like he spent many hours lifting weights in the gym!

    His backside widens with a large slab of muscle, growing from shoulder to shoulder, forming the outline of a large Christmas tree, while the lower section is a firm, large butt followed by firm, thick hamstrings and mighty, solid, large calves. Truly, the young boy has morphed into a hulking, teenage athlete, standing over 6 feet tall and glowing with muscle all over!

    Finally, Ty saw that Charlie was standing there, almost naked. He had outgrown almost all his clothes! So, Ty made a silent wish and decided to make the new teen look like a soccer champion.

    In a blink of an eye, Charlie was wearing white soccer socks and shin guards, the best and latest soccer cleats fitted for his big, size 15 feet, and black shorts with a white jersey which barely concealed the boy’s huge muscles.

    “Hey, Shawn!” Charlie shouted in a deep, loud, baritone voice. “CAN I PLAY NOW?”

    Shawn looked up and gasped. Charlie had grown into a Hulking teen. In fact, Charlie was a few inches taller than him, with more muscle. Not only that, but Charlie’s legs were HUGE!

    “Uhhh… Yeah! Come on! Get on the pitch, dude! We need you!” Shawn shouted back.

    Charlie happily ran on the field and started to play as Ty looked on, enjoying a thrilling soccer game where Charlie scored the winning goal! That made Ty extremely happy, knowing that he contributed to Charlie’s growth.

    As the game came to an end, Ty looked around, scanning the park area for his next “victim” to grow…
