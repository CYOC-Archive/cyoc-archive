    Rick opened his eyes to see that he had shrunken. The world around him had grown enormous, seemingly looking to be a land of giants. The camera, still held in his hands, seemed massive now, and was a fare bit heavier. He laughed, placing the camera onto the floor, and then looked down at his body.

    He wore a marvel t-shirt, with little sport shorts and velcro sneakers. He felt his head up, finding somewhat long hair down to past his ears, although it seemed to be black now, rather than his normal red. The solution to that change was spotted soon after, as upon looking around the living room, he spotted a large family portrait. It consisted of Paul, Olivia, a younger Emily, and a small baby held in Olivia's arms.

    "Ohhh, so I'm Emily's little brother now." He laughed aloud, noting how adorable he sounded. "I wish I got Emily's brown hair, but whatever."

    Rick began to wonder how he could spend the day before using the camera again. Perhaps he could play with some toys, are watch some shows he's "too old" for now while resting in Emily's arms. His mind raced, and as it did, he failed to notice the problem that had just sauntered into the room.

    "Hey Ricky." Emily's voice boomed, slightly startling him.

    "O-oh, hi Emily!" He called back. He was about to ask her what she thought of his transformation when he realized that he had turned her awareness of the changes, and to the camera itself, off.

    He realized just how problematic that decision would be, as Emily simply lifted the camera from where Rick had left it, as she looked over with confusion.
    "Wow, this thing looks ancient." She chuckled. "Where'd you find this baby bro, Mom's closet?"

    "Uh, b-be careful with that!" He called out.

    "Relax squirt I won't break it... hmmmm." Emily suddenly began to hum.

    Rick tensed up. In his haste to play with different forms without concern, he had definitely made a mistake. With his new form, he had almost zero authority or believability with anything he'd say! And to make things worse, Emily now held the reality warping camera, with no idea what powers it held.
