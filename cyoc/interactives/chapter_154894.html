    After a a short time in thought, Steve decided to become an anthropomorphic insect. He always thought insects were extremely interesting creatures. They had intense variety, being 80% of all animal species, despite their size. They ranged in interesting adaptations, ranging from flight, all the way to spiked forelimbs. He wandered what it would be like: walking in a chitinous shell.

    Steve, with a gentle finger, clicked on the button, and waited for it to load as an advert for a new movie popped up, which he quickly closed.

    “Oh my god!” Steve thought to himself as he browsed through the list of incests. There was a truly expansive list of insects for him to choose from, even ranging in gender. He even came across a few other invertebrates, but that was somewhat to be expected, as spiders and such where commonly conflated with insects due to their similarities.
