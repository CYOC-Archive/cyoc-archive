    And there he was, Jen's boyfriend, or well, former boyfriend.

    "Hey, how are you?" Brad said with a kind smile.

    "Um.. Okay, I guess." Jen said timidly.

    "Good, good. Hey can I ask you a favor, see I kinda brought my girlfriend up here for a little alone time, and you're kinda..... everywhere. Can you possibly, um... ya know, leave... please." Brad said winking at her.

    "Sure... Sure, just um, honk your horn when you get back into your car, I don't want to um... hurt you by accident."

    "Thanks a lot, ya know people keep saying you can't be reasoned with, but your alright Big Jen." Brad said as he started to slide down her side. Jen's heart broke, everything she had was now gone, she was considered a colossal freak, a force of nature, and now someone else was loving her boyfriend. She could smash his car with them in it, she was big enough to do so, but she didn't have the heart to. Instead when she heard the honking of his horn, Jen got up and walked away, to where she didn't know.

    Jen's foot falls thundered even though she wasn't stomping, she could see trees shaking around her as she walked through the woods outside of town, Jen decided to try gently going between the trees to see how graceful the gargantuan legs of hers were. A walk in the woods always cleared her head, she had already decided to leave town, it wasn't worth staying here anymore, but without sense of direction would she know where she was going. Then again it wouldn't matter it would all be the same where ever she went, an outcast and freak, but she could get away with destroying things without a second thought, but that wasn't her nature.

    "Well, I may as well get out of here, maybe I can find someone who will fix this." Jen said with a glimmer of hope as she walked through the woods and out onto a highway, she headed north, probably to a bigger city, and hopefully to find someone who would use the curse to fix this mess.
