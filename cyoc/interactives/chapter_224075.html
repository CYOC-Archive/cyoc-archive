    Two Fairies And A Baby

    The fairy - Rose- bounces you in her arms, clearly delighted at the idea of having a daughter. She laughs and calls you Lilac. You fidget and start wailing. Unfortunately it is the only thing you can do. You tried hitting her but your limbs are about as powerful as marshmallow. So you are down to actually acting like a one-year-old toddler. Plus you are in these ridiculous baby clothes made out of petals! How humiliating is that? Not to mention that you are now a girl! How can you start living with this new set of genitalia? Even if, embarrassingly so, as you'll get changed for the foreseeable future, you won't have to glance at your privacy where your privates used to be. Rose - you refuse to call her Mama- laughs at your antics and even congratulates you at acting like a baby so fast. She calls you Lilac and laughs as she tells you all the things she cannot wait to do with you, like going out in a pram and stroller with you in it, rocking you to sleep, putting you in a cradle, bathing you and overall raising you. Ugh! It sounds absolutely sugary to the point of nausea! There is no way you will follow down that path willingly! She better expect an awful brat. But she covers you in kisses, which makes you fidget even more.
    "Calm down, Lilac, or I'll have to put a spell on you so you think and act like the perfect baby girl," she tells you.
    Now that makes you stop immediately. A spell to make you think like a simpering little baby girl, with a mother loving cutesy things? What a horror! You rather obey for now until you can find a way out of this predicament. You might still have a chance then. Without your mind and memories, it will be none!
    By now, Rose rose from the ghround and hovered in a magnificent garden. You are in the fairy realm. As she glides eithout a care in the world, another fairy flies alongside her. She's got a smattering of freckles and long wavy liuscious locks of red hair.
    "Why hello, Rose," she says brightly. "I am happy you are back."
    "The human world wasn't great. But I bought back this little darling!" she smiles widely while shoewing you to the other fairy.
    She squeals.
    "Bty the primordial blooms, she is adorable!" she exclaims.
    She then wriggles her finger under your chin. In site of yourself, you squirm and giggles, making peals of laughter that the two fairies gush over. The red-haired fairy then pokes you in your ample new baby fat, eliciting even more giggles and squirms for you. You cannot stop yourself. And it’s strange because you were never really ticklish before. And Rose goes at it too, the tickling becoming a torture they revel in while you are shaken by giggles that seem very much enchanting to the two fairies.
    At long last, when they both stop, you are out of breath and as energetic as a ragdoll when the red-haired fairy takes you in her arms for fun. She asks Rose where she found you and Rose recounts her story. Autumn - the red-haired fairy - gasps and then declares:
    "She is absolutely adorable! I might go to the human world and transform a human into my own!"
    "That would be a great idea! Then our daughters could grow up to be the best of friends!"
    You gulp. No, it does NOT sound like a good idea, and you wonder what poor people will be dragged in their fairy world just so that these fairies might have little dolls to play house with.
    "But you are a talented spell-caster," ponders Autumn. "I wonder if my human would turn out as cute as yours."
    "Well, you should try it out and see," answers Rose. "But I need to go and get Lilac adjusted to her new life."
