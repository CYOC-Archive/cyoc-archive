    A Threat Against Damon's Family

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by MarvelScales 4 years ago O

    King Damon never forgot about his family on land, but he certainly wasn’t happy to contact them again in a circumstance that involves a fight against a threat. While he tried to act happy in front of Arthur and his new love, Damon now had a look of grimace on his face, he was angered by knowing his former home was in danger.

    The King of The Sea enters the ritual chamber, the same chamber where he was punished to be a shark and uses it to create a connection between him and brother.

    At the human kingdom, King Aaron was sitting with his wife in the throne room when a projection suddenly appears before them, revealing to be a projection sent by Damon. Aaron was initially eager to see his brother contacting him through magic, but the troubled expression on The Sea King’s face said otherwise.

    “Aaron, I need to talk to you, immediately. This is urgent.”, King Damon said.

    “What is it, Damon?”, King Aaron said.

    “Your kingdom’s in grave danger. My son, Arthur, told me that a mad king from another kingdom will try to conquer yours.”, King Damon replied.

    King Aaron’s wife had a worried expression on her face, but King Aaron knew that he had to find more about the threat right away.

    “Do you know more about The Mad King? How did your son even know of him in the first place?”, King Aaron questioned.

    “I sent Arthur out into the world to find a mate, he has grown to a young merman since you last met him. My son found a glooming castle and went inside to overhear The Mad King talking to his men. His plan is to conquer your kingdom and kill your family. Luckily, Arthur turned The Mad King’s daughter, Princess Samantha, into a mermaid and escaped to my Merkingdom, trying to strike The King where it hurts - his love to his daughter.” Damon said.

    King Aaron’s wife was shocked. “What was your son thinking? Now your kingdom’s in danger, too!”

    King Aaron tried to calm down his wife, “I think that’s why Arthur did it. Our human kingdom and the merfolk’s kingdom have been brought together by peace. So if we’re under threat, so are the merfolk.”

    King Damon smiled, “Exactly, brother. Now The Mad King has two kingdoms to fight against and we have his daughter on our side. We can defeat him together!”

    King Aaron smiled back, “It’s great to have you by my back, Damon. Even though The Mad King is at a disadvantage, I’d best get Brendan to get my army ready. You’d better train your army, too. So that way our kingdoms are strong and united as one!”

    The Sea King’s mood was light and confident, “Indeed, Aaron. Let’s take him down as a family! See you soon, brother.”

    “See you soon as well.”, Aaron said as the magic projection fades away.

    “Are you sure you want to do this, dear? Our son’s in the castle military that your other brother leads.”, Aaron’s wife said.

    “Yes. I know that it’s dangerous to be at war with any kingdom, but I know Brendan is a great leader and skilled tactician. Besides, our son never met his cousin before. I think it’s only fitting that Alex and Arthur work together to fight for their homes.”

    Aaron’s wife once concerned face grew a small smile. “Of course, dear. I just hope your brother’s family and ours can all be together for dinner sometime.”

    King Aaron grinned. “Don’t worry, honey. Once it’s all over, there’s plenty of time for feasting when we win.”
