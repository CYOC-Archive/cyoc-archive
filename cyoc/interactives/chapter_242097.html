    Alice also quickly checks her battery level, and even though she still has a little more time, she still wants to say goodbye to Emma. "Of course I'll come with you. I have to get Jake home soon anyway." As she moves away from her place and the jets, she hears Jake sigh regretfully over Mental Link. "Sorry, sweetheart, but I have to get you home before the emitter's battery runs out." She looks at Emma, who grins broadly at her. "So, does your swimsuit disagree and would rather stay and enjoy the hot tub?" "Well, he seems to be having a lot of fun as a swimsuit," Alice replies, smiling back at Emma. "But I think it's really time for us to head home." And as she steps out of the water with Emma she adds: "And I don't want to know what would happen if the emitter's battery runs out while I sit in the hot tub while wearing Jake as my swimsuit." Emma laughs. "Probably something embarrassing for both of you." They make their way to their spot on the meadow, where their towels and bags are, and Alice and Emma start to dry themselves off. "It was really great meeting you and talking about the Transform-O-Matic," Alice says, smiling at Emma. "I'm so glad we had this chance to connect." Emma nods, equally happy. "Me too, Alice. You and Jake are amazing, and I'm so happy to have met you both. And I'm quite sure that Mike and I will try out some new transformations soon too." Alice smiles. "I'm really looking forward to hearing about your experiences when we meet for dinner. I'll text you soon to set everything up." They simply pull their street clothes over their damp swimwear. It's still a hot day, and they appreciate a little cooling off on the way back to their homes. Then they go to the lockable lockers to take out their valuables. In Emma's case, especially the bag with her emitter and Mike inside.

    "So, I'll hear from you soon, right?" Emma asks, smiling at Alice. Alice nods. "Of course. I'll text you later and we'll set up a time for dinner. I'm really looking forward to it." Emma picks up her bag and slings it over her shoulder, making sure Mike is safely inside. "Me too. Have a great rest of the day, and enjoy the rest of your transformation." Alice smiles and gives her a quick hug. "You too, Emma. It was so nice meeting you, and I'm sure Mike and Jake will get along great." Emma smiles and heads off towards the parking lot, waving goodbye to Alice. Alice waves back to Emma and watches her driving off.
