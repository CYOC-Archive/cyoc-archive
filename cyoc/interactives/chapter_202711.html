You grab the gun, run like hell, and make it back to campus without incident.

Your thoughts turn to resentment, and you start to imagine all the things you wish you could make Megan do. Then it hits you: you can make her do anything you want!

Nothing happens, and you look down at the gun, wondering if this whole charade was just a waste of time.
