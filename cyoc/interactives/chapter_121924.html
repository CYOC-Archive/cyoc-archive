Better test them out to see if they are not damaged.” Looking around, she could see a couple of things that could be used for targeting. Getting into position, she did a quick spin and the claws made contact with a few certain objects. Stopping in a stance with all four arms out, the umbrella on the table, a few tiki torches, a swing set for her daughter that’s made of metal, and a couple of branches were sliced off cleanly and broke apart on the floor. Seeing the end result, she looked to one of her clawed hands and was pleased with it. “Looks like I still got it.” She said with a bit of a twisted smile on her face.

    Jeff, meanwhile, watched the change and was shocked of what he witnessed. Not only that his mother went into another step in evolution, but is also adapting to it physically and mentally. That fascinates him, but also scares him. For she sliced a few things in the backyard didn’t show any regrets. It’s almost like she’s acting a little feral with his body. But she still has her human intellect, so there could be some control. “Stay calm, Jeff.” he said to himself, “Just because your mother gained some deadly claws, doesn’t mean she’s going to be dangerous.” He soon watched on and wondered if the changes will end, or it will continue.
