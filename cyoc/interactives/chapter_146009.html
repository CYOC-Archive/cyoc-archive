Once the fur completely covered her face, she noticed her face begin to change, looking less human and more like a rabbit's. Slowly small whiskers pushed out from her small nose, which continually twitched.

As the others now stared silently at the puddle of clothes on the floor they could see movement underneath. To shocked to move the teens watched as a small white rabbit freed herself from the cumbersome clothing, looking up at the others quietly.
