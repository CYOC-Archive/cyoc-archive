    Entity #:704
    Codename: Damsel Weaver

    Appearance: E-704 appears as a fully black tarantula (exact species undetermined) 5 meters tall with 6 large eyes and a large abdomen with a large set of spinnerettes. E-704 also has a thick "penis" somewhere between 1 foot and 9 inches in length attached between its abdomen and body that is hidden unless E-704 is feeding or changing its victims.

    Known Abilities: E-704’s primary ability and characteristic is that it transform its prey, first it will ambush and wrap them with its web, after stripping them of anything, leaving their head untouched unless there are more prey in the viscinity and it wont have the time to change them, in that case it will wrap up their mouth -muting them-, before either attaching them to a larger web nearby or leaving them stuck to a tree, then scurrying off to another prey. If there are no other prey in the immediate vicinity, E-704 will proceed to change its victim via the same way it feeds them. As mentioned, E-704’s penis will be unfurled and then shoved down its victims throat, then it will proceed to throatfuck them over the course of half a minute before climaxing. Over the course of said 30 second throatfuck session, the victim is gradually changed into a well-endowed buxom babe of varied height, skin-colour and race, but every victim is consistently given a curvacious body and breasts in excess of J-cup. After 704’s climax and its victims change, 704 will immediately proceed to wrap up the rest of its victim, notably keeping their mouth open, giving observers the impression of screaming, the reason is presumably to keep from needing to force their mouth open when 704 is feeding them, requiring only the removal of the web covering the mouth.

    The reason E-704 is so effective against GET personnel is because it is capable of teleportation from & into unpercieved areas, allowing it to respond to incursion into its territory no matter where it is at any time, although observation has concluded that 704 cannot teleport with someone, thankfully making it unable to capture and then disappear with someone left unsupervised for but a second. Observation has also concluded that E-704 for some reason loses its ability to teleport if in panic, most prominently if cornered and threatened with flamethrowers. Conversely E-704 has shown the capacity to teleport with its victims but so far have only done this when they were in danger (For a more prominent example, see Experiment Log:704-4).

    Over time, trapped victims of 704 are mentally changed into one of various mental stereotypes fitting of a ‘damsel in distress’, prominent types include princesses (with personalities ranging from childish and pure to mature and arrogant) of various nations and kingdoms despite the russians never having an asian princess that doesn’t know Russian (and despite many of them never having a ‘princess Rosella’), queens (rarest stereotype with only one confirmed case so far) of various nations despite the german reich or the Indians never having a queen nor a princess, foiled knights (they call themselves knights despite not knowing any sword arts nor having any muscle) that claim they’ve been sent by various nations and kingdoms to reclaim their princesses, nobles daughters (typically bratty, snotty, spoiled or scheming in personality) are also present, supposedly kidnapped by 704 when they were going on vacation, commoner babes (typically kind, affectionate and hard-working) of varied profession (like with knights, farmers and other hard-working professions do not have any amount of expected musclemass, instead appearing just as soft and delicate as any other 704 victim) that claim to having investigated a muffled cry before being ambushed and captured by 704.

    Despite various claims from recovered victims that they are from ancient china or the modern United States, interviews depict them living in a distinctly fantasy medieval world with grand palaces, floating islands, large estates, dragons, wizards, animal-like half-humans, demons, monsters, heroic adventurers and intervening gods. Combine this with beyond subpar knowledge of their supposed homelands (Calling the United States a minor kingdom on the African continent is a prominent example), and a less than plausible set of memories (a commoner babe being enslaved 5 identical times by the same ‘gluttonous Noble’ before going home like nothing happened, then getting ambushed in a market square by 704?), analysts and 3s have theorised that 704’s mental effects seems to draw from a pool of memories when it comes to the victims mental replacement, possibly drawing from the memories of its first victims, although that doesn’t explain the fantasy elements in the recovered victims memories.

    E-704’s silk is similar to most spiders silk in that it is both sticky and strong, but unlike other spiders silk, 704’s silk is both sticky and strong enough a ‘small web’ can easily hold an adult human male, although inversely it for some reason can easily be cut with blades without resistance, and fire can be used to clear large amounts of it, although as mentioned, it is not recommended to set fire to 704’s wrapped victims lest it begin panicking and ignore being cornered to save the aflame victim.

    Speaking of E-704, it displays an intense fear of fire when it is brought up, panicking if its webs catch fire and especially if any of its victims is in danger from fire. Incursions into 704’s containment area are therefore to have flamethrowers using methane based powder that have a low risk of setting unintentional fires, these flamers are to be used to keep E-704 away from intruding personnel and to assist in cornering E-704 when the recovery of 704 victims is needed.

    After 704 was successfully relocated to its current containment zone, incident 704-2 gave notice to 704’s agitated state which 704 will enter if no new victims has been caught by it within a period of a week, a seemingly spontanious change when 704 has successfully lived 327 years with less than 1 new victim every 5 years before the capture-spree that lead to its concrete discovery. When in an agitated state, 704 will venture beyond its territory and go straight to capturing personnel within a 100 metre radius, 704’s agitated state cannot stop until it has captured 10 new victims, although it will not stop by itself until it has captured 25+ victims (exact numbers untested, clearance awaited) to add to its collection of webbed and helpless victims.

    Containment: E-704 is to be held inside an artificially sustained forest covering a 5 square kilometre area with a large cave at the centre serving as 704’s primary nesting site. The entire containment pen is located 1 kilometre underground and connected via elevators and hallways to 30 seperate entrances into the holding area, artificial skylighting imitates an identical day-night cycle for the plantlife within. A disposable 6 is to be shown into 704’s containment zone under the excuse of letting them leave (usage of E-374 to trick them into thinking the elevators go up is recommended) every 3-5 days to avoid E-704 becoming agitated.

    Other: The total amount of victims to 704 is to be recorded in Administrative Log:704-2 (current total of 247 victims), should 704 manage to capture valuable personnel, clearance from 4 senior 4’s is required for an incursion into E-704’s territory, due to the mental changes that eventually occur to victims of 704, only personnel that have been captured for less than 48 hours can be prioritised for recovery by incursion teams.
    If recovered personnel show a mental conversion of 60% or above, they are automatically assigned a 6 classification and marked for either E-544 rehabilitation or disposal via higher tf-priority entity testing/sustaining.
