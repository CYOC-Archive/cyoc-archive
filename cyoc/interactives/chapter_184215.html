    "Who's this? What's going on? Why am I in this body?" said a panicked sounding woman.

    Clearly it was no longer Katie in that body. You had to think quickly "Don't hang up. I can guess what happened to you. Did you collide with someone then wake up in the body you currently have?"

    "Yes! Yes! I'm not a woman! Who are you? Help me!"

    "Where are you?"

    "In Rosewood Park. I was sitting down eating my lunch then next thing I knew I was a woman lying on the ground and my body was running away. I chased but couldn't run in these stupid shoes and a dress. For god's sake I'm wearing a skirt!"

    "That all makes sense to us. We are close by. Come to (your address). That's the street with the Spendless supermarket on the corner. We are only a couple of minutes away"

    "Who are you? You sound like a little girl"

    "My name is John and yes at the moment I am a little girl but I wasn't an hour ago. I'm in the same situation as you"

    "Can I trust you?"

    "That's your only choice"

    Several minutes later your doorbell rang. You opened the door to find a dishevelled looking woman in a blue skirt and white blouse (partially stained). You quickly invited her in.

    "Roger" the woman replied "Though I'm not myself at the moment. I was a 28 year old man"

    "Just dial your number then give me the phone" you suggested. She did so.
