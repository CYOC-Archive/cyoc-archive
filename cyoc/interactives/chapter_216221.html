    Early one mid-afternoon while most of the surrounding businesses of this local Magic Shop branch were either slowing down for breaks or going into the typical overdrive of daily grinds “Umm... excuse me?” this 15 year old bob-cut Blond-haired girl with dyed blue tips, wearing a red jacket over a white shirt, green coveralls and white trainers call as she entered the store. “May we help you?” the current staff manager asked as he approached. “Maybe. What I’m looking for involves boosting my attraction in my part-time work” the girl answered. “Oh I see. Well then let’s go over things and then see what we have that can help” the manager accepted before leading the girl to a corner.

    “Guess giving you my name is a must. Claire Matthews and I’m a freelance babysitter” the girl started. “Fading role. Most babysitting is incorporated these days. Collective groups and unions. Some ideal, some merely cover for quick payment scan-like activities” the manager noted. “Well as such as you just pointed out I have a limited attraction to most potential clientele” Claire admitted “Technically I was part of a bigger collective but left after I found by references of successful jobs were mass-copied for those that shouldn’t have even been allowed around children. I’ll spare you the complete details”. “But your departure also blacklisted you from other groups I gather” the manager noted. “Well no but after what happened I’m not really in the risk mood in joining any of them” Claire admitted.

    “So getting down to it, I need a means of boosting my appeal to the children or being ideal to the parents. Preferably both if possible” Claire then stated. “Hmmm... actually I think I have an idea” the manager responded before briefly walking off then returning with one of the Host Lamps “I believe this will be flexible to your requests”. “Umm... that’s a Genie’s Lamp” Claire responded in confusion. “Not just any Genie Lamp. A Genie Host Lamp” The Manager started “More limited on what can be wished for but limitless in wish counts”. “What’s the catch?” Claire asked. “Well as its name implies no native genie comes with it” the Manager answered while rubbing the Lamp to make a genie outfit appear “Rather someone needs to become the genie though not permanently, just by wearing the formed outfit with the cuffs locked. Just need to unlock the cuffs while outside”.

    “So I can vary between letting my employees being in charge. The children or even the kids” Claire noted in wonder. “Put it plainly, Yes” the Manager accepted while placing this booklet down “A detailed manual over everything involving the Host Lamp”. “It’ll take it” Claire happily announced. “Of course. Let’s make the transaction then” the Manager agreed kindly:
