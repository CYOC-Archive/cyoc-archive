    From the outside the TF Casino looks like any other, with all the flashing lights and poker machines, all secretly rigged to make you lose. The difference is when you play at the TF Casino, you don't just bet your money; you bet your body.

    The Casino utilize three types of chips: Lesser Game Chips (Red), Standard Game Chips (Blue) and Greater Game Chips (Green). Lesser chips are worth $100, standard ones are worth $10,000 and greater chips are worth $1m.

    When you first arrive at the Transformation Casino, you are given $10m worth of Game Chips. The Chips are used inside the Casino, they are the standard currency for all the games.

    The Games themselves exist in great variety, they include TF Slot Machines, Transformation Poker, Betting Hot Tubs and lots, lots more.

    The way the TF Casino is different to other Las Vegas Casinos is their trans formations; The more money you lose, the more you transform. If you lose:

    A hundred dollars or less; Minor body modifications (Only Human TF's).
    A hundred to a thousand dollars; Gender change or Major body modifications (Only Human TF's).
    A thousand to ten thousand dollars; Minor body modifications (Humans or Natural Animals).
    Ten thousand to a hundred thousand dollars; Major or Complete body modifications (Humans, Natural Animals or Inanimate objects).
    A Hundred thousand to a million dollars; Minor body modifications (Humans, Natural, Fantastical Animals or Inanimate objects).
    A million to ten million dollars; Major or Complete body modifications (Humans, Natural, Fantastical Animals or Inanimate objects).

    It is possible for people to have lesser transformations (e.g. Gender change, Strictly Human body modifications, etc...) if they have lost a great amount of money (e.g. 100k to 10m).

    When people leave the Casino, they have a choice. If they want to reverse any transformations they acquired during the experience, then they will need to pay back double everything they lost. If they can't, then they will become the property of the Casino (or if they lost their money during a game with another member of the Casino, their property). As property of the Casino, the person will most likely be transformed into an attractive woman, commonly working naked in the Betting Hot Tub, where they would be treated as an object to the people that came after them. If not then they would probably be sold as a slave, whose only wish would be to serve their master.
