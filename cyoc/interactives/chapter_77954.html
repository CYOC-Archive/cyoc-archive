    homework. Like, it'd take us weeks to do. Everyone was moaning at her:

    "Oh miss do we have to?"
    "This is going to kill my weekends!"
    "I hate this stupid assignment..."

    And then I joined in with, "You don't really want us to do this, do you miss?"

    Her hand gave a little twitch, and then she said, "Actually, you know what? I don't! It'll take me far too
    long to grade it, so don't bother."

    And she threw all the papers in the bin, and we just chatted for the rest of the lesson. I was taken aback at
    this, but no-one else seemed to think it was weird. I went up to my buddy Nate after class to ask him about
    it.

    "Well you know Miss Tarrant," Nate said, "Never can make up her mind about shit. I don't know why you're
    surprised to be honest, don't you remember that time when she told us not to bother with an assignment like
    one week after she'd given it to us?"

    "Yeah," I replied. But actually, none of that had ever happened. I knew for a fact that Miss Tarrant had
    previously been a stickler for getting homework in on time. And somehow, I'd changed that about her. I was
    pretty excited, and I wanted to try something else, see what kind of limits my ability had. Maybe it would be
    just a one-off and I could only use it to avoid that piece of homework. Maybe it'd be a lifelong thing. I
    needed to test it.

    I needed something small but obvious to test it on. And something that wouldn't make me seem like a complete
    lunatic if it didn't work. I was going round to Nate's house that night so I decided to recruit him as a test
    subject. I saw an event I thought he could try going to, so I wanted to test if something like that would be
    something I could 'encourage' him to do. There was nothing on the calendar on that day as I walked in, and
    Nate's so organised that if he's due to do anything at all, even go food shopping or some shit, he'd damn sure
    mark it on the calendar.

    We were playing video games, something like Halo or some shit, and I was losing, like always. While we were
    playing, I decided to make my move.

    "Hey Nate..."
    "Yeah?"
    "You ever thought about showing off how good at this game you are?"

    Nate's hand twitched before he replied, "What, you mean more than I do already? I've got tournaments booked up
    like six months in advance, you know that."

    And, sure enough, as I turned around there was a different tournament marked every weekend on the calendar
    which definitely hadn't been there before. I'd done it!

    And then.......well, I did a bad thing. I got a little horny. See, I also knew Nate liked working out. He
    looked alright, toned and slim, but not how I'd want him to look. I'd realised I was bi a few years ago, no-
    one else knew though.

    "I don't know how you squeeze in all those tournaments though. You do so much other stuff. Like how often do
    you go to the gym?"
    "Three times a week."
    "Six times a week you say?" Fuck, I probably shouldn't have done that. Well, if it didn't work I could always
    say an explosion on the game made me mishear him.
    "That's what I said dude, six times a week. You going deaf or something?"

    from a beer to a protein shake.

    had some crazy ability that meant I could change loads of things, and all I had to do was talk about it.
