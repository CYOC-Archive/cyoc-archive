    Twin Dragons: Gaining Some Experience

    Branch Full view
    added by loony28 15 years ago O

    Aqua's group was flying toward Mystic Forest. Luma was wondering something so she asked "What are we going to encounter?"

    Aqua replied via team speak "'Use team speak now. I fully expect us to find either Luster or Shade there.'"

    "'What do you mean?'" Willow asked.

    "'We have reason to believe that they have been spying on us'" Aqua said. "'Luster has the power to see recent events using faint traces of light left behind. She can not hear anything only see. We believe this since they have had advance warning of some of our plans.'"

    "'So Luster may have seen us going over the map'" Luma said. "'How are we suppose to beat them? Willow and I are newbies not only to being dragons but to the game as well.'"

    Frost was stunned by this and thus forgot to flap falling for a few seconds before he remembered to flap again and regained altitude. Once he did he said "'But dragons are an epic class. You can't play them unless you have a high level character somewhere on your account.'"

    "'Unless the newbies are invited to join'" Aqua said. "'Twister invited Luma, Willow, Silver, and Quake. You have to be a relatively high dragon to invite someone and you can only send out six invites. Twister has used up four.'"

    Suddenly what looked to be dirty yellowish white fire came roaring up at them. It passed close to Luma. She had drifted off to the side a bit just seconds before. 'I'm glad I don't have wings or I would have been hit by that' she thought. Over team speak she asked "'What was that?'"

    "'Bone Dragon'" Aqua said. "'The skeleton of a dead dragon brought back from the Graveyard though only as bones.'"

    They all looked down and the skeleton of a dragon flew up from the forest below. As it did it unleashed another fire ball at Luma who was barely able to dodge it. "'Luma!'" Frost said. "'Bone Dragons are susceptible to Earth and Lightning attacks.'"

    "'Blaze is right'" Aqua said. "'Use your speed to attack and withdraw. Don't stop for too long and don't be predictable with your moves. Do that and you should be able to win and come away with few hits.'"

    Luma nodded and poured on the speed just as the Bone Dragon fired another fire ball at Luma. She opened her mouth and unleashed her own attack. A bolt of lightning hit the Bone Dragon in the tail. She dove under it and let loose another lightning bolt. She continued to trade shots with it hitting it with high accuracy. With each hit a bit of bone would explode. She was grazed only once but the pain from it was incredible. She lost her concentration for a few seconds while she closed her eyes and almost plowed into the ground. She pulled up when she was a couple seconds from hitting. Finally after a long battle her final strike blew it into pieces. She watched as the pieces fell to the ground. She flew back to the others feeling stronger and faster.

    "'Well done'" Aqua said as she rejoined them. "'You are now at level fourteen instead of level six. Your human form will now be that of a fourteen year old girl instead of the six year old girl you were before.'"

    "'Blaze just told me Quake killed a Bone Dragon too'" Frost said.

    "'I have a question now that the Bone Dragon is dead'" Willow said. "'If Luster can only see the recent past she would have to be at the cave. How could she or Shade beat us to the magical power sources?'"

    "'They are a high enough level that they can teleport'" Aqua said. "'Luster using light and Shade using shadow. Once you get high enough you'll be able to do that as well using your force.'"
