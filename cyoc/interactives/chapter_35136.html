    Getting some samples.

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 15 years ago O

    Once again running to your house, you grab a few large jars before returning to the puddles. You carefully scoop up "samples" of the latex pudding, filling each jar with a different color. Since you've already tried the pink puddle several times, you get the goop from others. In the first jar, you manage to get some that's a pleasant shade of light green. The second, you fill from a puddle that's a fairly boring orange. Finally, you find a puddle of black latex, and decide to put that in the last jar. You almost wish that you could take some liquid from each and every puddle, but unfortunately, you don't have nearly enough jars nor enough time - you can't be out all night gathering this stuff just because you're curious about it!

    With that done and the jars all sealed up, you're left with a bit of a dilemma: getting everything home. There's no way you could carry all three feet, the udder, and the three jars. You think for a moment about wearing some of them, your mind returning to the rubber rabbit's paw you're still wearing on your foot, but even that wouldn't be enough. Besides, it'd probably be pretty awkward if a neighbor came along and saw you with a monkey's paw for one foot, a bunny paw for the other, an udder, and all while holding two pink cat's paws and three jars of... something. No, you decide that you're probably best just discarding it all (you aren't really that interested in weird costume bits anyways, just the goo that's been making them), so you toss everything but the jars (including the rabbit's paw that you'd been wearing as a shoe - might as well be thorough) back into the pink puddle.

    Even with that done, you still have some difficulty carrying all three jars, but after a few minutes you manage to lift them (albeit rather awkwardly) and start waddling your way home.
