    "Who- whose test is that?" Emily asked, afraid to know the answer.

    Rick quickly searched through the purse, haphazardly tossing out everything in search of something to identify who the purse belonged too. Finally, Rick found what he was looking for. He pulled out a wallet, and opened it up to reveal that it was the 40 something year old African American woman that Emily had become.

    "I-I'm pregnant." Emily quietly stuttered. "... WITH DAD!?" She then yelled as the situation became clearer in her mind.

    "Don't!" Olivia yelled as she hopped down from the counter top. "Do. Not. Think about it at all." She commanded as she picked up the camera, aimed it at Emily, and activated the camera without hesitation.

    FLASH

    Paul suddenly found himself standing once again. The last thing he remembered, he was taking a picture of his daughter to restore her... but then he had found himself somewhere else. He couldn't open his eyes, but he felt... warm, and safe. The next thing he knew he was back in the kitchen, looking at a still changed Olivia and Rick who seemed relieved.

    "Huh, what happened?" Paul asked, surprised to hear his voice actually sounding masculine for once. He looked down to see that he was infant a man again! There were only two issues; judging by his skin tone he seemed more Italian than German, and he was dressed like a damn butler.

    "Daddy you're ok!" A boy's voice rung out before he was tackled by a teenaged boy.

    "Emily, is that you?" He asked, utterly confused.

    The boy backed off to reveal Emily was once again a teenaged boy. The only difference between before and now was that this new "Emile" seemed to be at least half African American.

    "Yeah, it's me." She sighed. "Boy Dad, you really gave us a scare there!" She chuckled.

    "I'll say." Olivia gasped, leaning against the countertop slightly. "So, what was it like to be unborn?" She asked.

    "UNBORN!?" Paul yelled in surprise. In retrospect, it made sense, but he almost couldn't believe it was true. "I was unborn... wait, who was I... you know, in?" He asked.

    "Um, me?" Emily forced a smile.

    Rick stifled a chuckle, causing the family to look. He finally couldn't;t help himself and let out a laugh, which was embarrassing considering how girlish the giggle was.
    "I'm sorry, you gotta admit, it was a little crazy?" He asked, hoping someone would see what he meant.

    "I suppose so." Olivia said coldly.

    "Alright, we will NEVER speak of that one again." Paul commanded. "I'm just happy you're ok baby... and I'm happy I'm a man again!" HE laughed.

    "Good for you Dad." Emily smiled.

    "Yeah, good for you." Olivia replied sarcastically. "Congrats, you're now the only one of us who is the right gender."

    "Sorry honey." Paul chuckled.

    "Well, let's focus everyone." Emily announced to the four of them, finally collecting herself. "We need to really focus in and just go for one person only for the next few changes! If we just keep switching off we'll never get back to normal." She planned.

    "Good idea babe." Rick agreed.

    "I also agree it's a smart idea, but who'll be the guinea pig?" Olivia asked. "It can't be me as we need at least one adult that is part of the family, so which one of you is willing?"
