The fact that they were both horse Animalians didn't make much of a difference, though there was a lot of staring from the younger kids.

Of course, Shamus and Shannon had already married and it was rather early in their marriage to be renewing their vows, but they were about to deny their old countrymen the delight of a wedding. Especially since Father McConnell had known Shamus for many years and had been waiting for the day when he could perform his marriage rites.
