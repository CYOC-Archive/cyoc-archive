    As the girl that had been Luke brushed curly blond locks out of her face, Jake's mind raced with possibilities. He could change people's genders with this thing. Or it changed reality? The more Jake thought, the more he realized he didn't really know what this thing does. He would have to experiment more.

    "Dork. I thought I told you to beat it?" Lacy growled

    Jake smirked as he realized he had a volunteer. He pretended to walk away from "Lacy's" doorway but he really hid just out of eyesight. He bent the camera just around the corner and hit the button. He could see the air around his former brother's room get cloudy as he assumed reality was changing again. He looked at the picture and he saw an old lady on a rocking chair in a guest room.

    Jake tried not to laugh at the wrinkly old bag as he walked in. She turned and snapped at him.

    "Didn't I tell you to leave Oswald?" The older, female Luke said.

    "Uh, my name is Jake." He replied back.

    "Eh, doesn't matter does it?" She said as she went back to knitting.

    Jake was getting pissed. He was surprised that female Luke was a dick, but even old lady Luke was a jerk. He was sick of Luke always being the worst, and decided to take another picture to see if anything could shut him up. Jake aimed the camera at the old lady and the flash covered her side of the room. It warped again and Luke had changed once more. Now the room had become an unoccupied guest room.

    Jake was confused for a moment until he looked down and saw what looked like a barbie doll. He picked it up and realized it was not a barbie doll, but a "Lacy" doll. The face looked just like a skinnier version of Luke's but now it was plastic and stuck smiling. The doll's hair was now a curly yellow fiber, and it was wearing a solid red dress that looked detachable.

    There was a small button at the base of the neck. Jake pressed it. "Hi, I'm a Lady Lacy doll! Wanna play?" The doll spoke through a voice box hidden under clothes. Jake smiled. "Finally a form that you deserve huh Luke?" Jake said. Jake walked into his little sister Lizzy's room and placed the doll in a dollhouse that wasn't there before.

    "Have a fun existence "Lady Lacy." I'm sure Lizzy will love playing with you." Jake said.

    He didn't know if Luke was still sentient or not but frankly he didn't care. Luke's punishment was now as harsh as Jake wanted it, but he knew a lot more people who deserved more. His controlling mother, obnoxious sister, dead beat dad, and a few school bullies came to mind. Jake was gonna have some fun.
