    Jen pulled out her phone then whispered “shit” under her breath. She needed to head to school. She was thinking about calling it and just staying home. That scumbag Kyle gave her the creeps with that phone call today. She kind of just wanted an excuse to miss school. While she was thinking about this she realized she was pulling her car into the student parking lot just now.
    Weird, she thought she was still debating heading somewhere else but here she was… at the school. “Ehh, its starting out as a weird Monday but I can’t let it get to me.” She got out of her car and headed to her locker.
    Pulling out her books for class she checked herself in her mirror attached to the locker door before shutting the door and heading down the hall. Halfway to class she saw a familiar face. It was Adam her boyfriend. He noticed her and walked up to her. “Hey babe” he casually called out to her. As they got close he grabbed her and kissed her lips.
    “You look kind of out of it today Jen, is everything alright?”Adam asked with concern.
    Jen looked at him and gave him a half hearted smile “nope, nothing wrong at all, just had a weird morning is all but nothing to worry about. I need to head to class, see you at lunch?”
    He sighed and nodded “Yeah babe, see you then.”
    She headed towards class still feeling out of it.
    Finally getting to class she walked towards her seat seeing Lisa one of the cheerleader’s idly flipping through her textbook waiting for someone to talk to or class to start. Jen got an idea to surprise her she snuck up behind her and kissed the top of her head.
    Surprised by this, Lisa swatted Jen away and turned to look at her, an annoyed expression plastered on her face. “What the fuck Jen; what was that about?”
    Jen realized it was very weird of her to surprise her friend by kissing her “I-I’m sorry, I don’t know why I did that I just wanted to surprise you but I think I am a little out of it today. Had a weird call from that freshman Kyle today and it has been messing with me all morning.
    “Kyle Armstrong? How did he get your number?
    “Hmm, no not Armstrong, Kyle Perkin’s, you know the geeky one we pantsed like a week ago.”
    “Not ringing any bells. Are you sure I was their?
    Jen froze at this; she knew all 8 of them were there. It was kind of an elaborate prank so they wouldn’t get caught but everyone saw him get pantsed.
    “Yeah you were their everyone was.” Jen answered looking for any sign Lisa was joking.
    Weird, but I don’t know a Kyle perkin’s here. Is he new?
    Jen gulped heavily she was not feeling too well right now. The bell rang and Mr. Nelson began looking around the classroom getting ready to start the day. Jen said “I need some water I’m not feeling well.”
    Before Mr. Nelson could protest Jen was out the door.
