    Absolutely fascinated, Camille took a moment to process the wonderous new maid and giggled as the overzealous plant creature hummed pleasantly and busied herself with food from inside. "I like her!" Camille pointed her thumb towards the house. "The kid has enthusiasm!"

    "Yeahhh." Ty blinked. "I may need to turn that down."

    "No! Don't you dare!" The deer chortled. "I like her just the way she is. She's like a perfect representation of our weirdness. Beautiful, eager, and kind of dumb."

    Laughing together, Ty and his doe wandered into Camille's restored forest and playfully began to chase and dance around one another.

    "We need more of this!" The deer waved her hand across her creation. "Through this whole valley."

    "Well, it's your forest." Ty shrugged. "You go ahead and do whatever you'd like."

    "Oh, I intend to!" The deer cracked her hands together and grinned mischeviously. "Let's start with this!"

    The doe raised her hands into the air, smiled warmly, and let a flood of golden energy waft from her fingertips into the air. Ty stood back and watched as the trees grew, the forest canopy thickened, and all around them the flowers, plants, and grass grew richer and more diverse. Ferns sprouted from the ground and unfurled into huge fans, tropical trees and shrubs erupted and swelled to size, color and vibrancy increased everywhere. Ty gasped as Camille's already alien creation grew even more so, resembling a lost prehistoric world, or another planet! The deer dropped her hands in sweating exhaustion and laughed manically.

    "That's just the warm up." She raised her eyebrow to Ty and winked. "Now here's the show."

    The deer took a deep breath, gathered her magic, and swung her hands into the air casting miracles. Above, the clouds suddenly darkened and swelled. There was a huge crack of thunder and it began to suddenly rain. The summer warmth grew around them, bolts of lightning flashed improbably across a clear blue sky, and the deer laughed as the rain fell in a torrent and freshly watered her new forest, which was more of a jungle now.

    Ty stood back and watched in awe as the deer goddess kept going. Now her jungle began to spread outward, a huge river ripped open in the ground, feeding several smaller channels, and all across the valley the same alien forest spread out. For yards if not miles it became impossible to see anything but the huge towering trees and the thick jungle, all the while a warm and pleasant rain fell, feeding it all. Behind them, Ty looked back to the waterfall against the valley, watching the rocks raising into a hardened gray cliffside, the waterfall deepening and roaring to feed the tropical paradise below.

    At nearly 30 degrees constant, the temperature held fast and a mist emerged from all the rain.

    Ty looked around them in wonderment, hearing the excited chirping of birds above, and looked back to Camille to find her completely naked, panting, and staring at him with the most sensual expression she could muster, which given her perfect sexiness was considerable. She slapped her tight ass, shaking off the dew from her fur as the rain abated instantly, and grinned. "There. There's your fucking queen of the forest, little man."

    Ty's mouth dropped as Camille turned, rubbing her hands over her sides, and struck a delicious pose with her mane of brown hair waving in the breeze. The sun shone through the canopy and she seemed to be positively glowing.

    She threw her hair into a ponytail, grinned, and hoofed over to the new river, jumping in up to her hips and wading into the gentle flow. The water was pure and crystal clean. A couple of salmon swam by. Camille grinned, pointed her fingers, and snapped. The two fish bubbled and curled, twisting and writhing as they both began to transform.

    "We're gonna make a lot of new friends today." She smiled. "Starting with these two."

    Beneath her, Camille watched through the clear water as the salmon twisted and grew in size. Their expressions changed from dull unintelligence to realizing shock. Fins popped out, tails enlarged, and torsos grew shape. She sighed dreamily as two hunky fish men shot up from the water, standing just a smaller than Ty, and gasped as they took their first breaths above water. Fishy webbed hands and glimmering scales shone in the sunlight. The two piscines blinked at each other, then turned to regard Camille, and finally Ty.

    "Hi, boys!" The deer said excitedly. "Welcome to your new lives!"

    The two salmon waved politely, shyly, and seemed to pull back into the water nervously. Camille laughed.

    "Uh... w-what is this?" One of the fish said. He stared back at his slightly less muscular friend and they exchanged strange looks. "We were swimming..."

    "I think she changed us." The other fish whispered, his voice slightly higher, maybe a little effeminate. "Uh... thank you, miss?"

    "Camille." The deer bowed politely.

    "Okay." The manlier salmon said. Ty realized that the two were getting closer and closer. They were holding hands. He gasped in realization and chuckled.

    "I want you to go and enjoy your new lives. Swim, splash, go exist. You're free to do whatever you want." Camille grinned warmly, seeing the two holding hands.

    "Uh... whatever we want?" The softer salmon laughed nervously. "There... aren't any... rules?"

    "None." Ty said with an understanding nod. "You guys are safe here."

    "Oh, that's lovely!" The softer salmon sighed in relief, touching his scaly chest. His hand held his partner's happily and they exchanged a quick loving kiss. "Do you think we might have names to call ourselves?"

    "You go ahead and give them to yourself if you'd like." Camille smiled, pulling back onto the riverbank. "I think I'm done naming people."

    "Oh!" The more effiminate fish nodded. "That's very kind! Well, I suppose we ought to think about that, right?" He nudged his 'friend' and they both chuckled.

    "Thank you so much!" The other fish smiled, exposing his sharp teeth. "This is really amazing! Is there anything we can do in return?"

    "Oh!" Camille slapped her knees. "I suppose there is! When you find other fish like yourselves, would you kindly touch them for me? They'll change just like you have."

    The two seemed to sparkle for a moment in gold and both touched themselves in amazement.

    "Oh, yes! Of course!" The gentler one nodded. "Yes, I'd be happy to!"

    With that, they both waved an eager good bye, and still holding hands they splashed into the water and began swimming with their powerful tails.

    "Gay fish." Ty laughed. "How about that?"

    "Eh, it's a free forest." Camille shrugged. "We're starting this right, dammit."
