    Dr Jenkins quickly grabbed the nearest serum from off the desk in front of him before hiding behind the door. He waited nervously with the syringe raised, trying to breath as quietly as possible.

    After a few moments the door beeped and began to open. A female shape cautiously entered the room, clearly acting as if something was wrong. “Mr Carter?” she called out quietly. “Are you in here?”

    Jenkins waited until the woman was fully inside before he made his move.

    The female farmhand screamed as Jenkins caught her off guard, jabbing the syringe into her exposed neck. The woman struggled to get free, but most of the liquid had already entered her bloodstream by the time she escaped his grasp.

    “You!” she yelled “what the fuck have you done? Where’s Mr Carter?”

    “Oh I’m surprised you can’t hear HER.” Jenkins smiled as he saw the despair creep over the woman’s face. “She’s enjoying her new life as a dumb animal, and I suggest you prepare yourself for a similar fate.”

    The woman paused as the realisation hit her. “You- you’re the one that was sent here for punishment. Doctor Jenkins isn’t it. They warned us that you were a snake. You- you’ll pay for what you’ve done you bastard!”

    “Oh sure” Jenkins taunted, “and what are you going to do about it? In a few minutes you’ll be a simple animal and I’ll be long gone.”

    The woman turned, gripping her stomach in pain as she fell to her knees. She waited for the doctor to take his eyes off her before reaching for her radio. “Security this is Farm Living. Wanted individual Doctor Jenkins in the store room, outbuilding 5. Send hel-”

    “You insolent little bitch” Jenkins retorted furiously as he grabbed the radio before smashing it on the floor. “This won’t save you, you’ll soon be nothing but an animal forever.”

    “You...with...me” was all the farmhand could muster before the pain became too great.
