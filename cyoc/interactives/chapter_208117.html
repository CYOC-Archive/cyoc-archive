    Embarassing Encounter

    added 10 months ago AR Toddler

    To Selina's misfortune, the cell phone she stole had a very low battery and since she still hadn't decided who to call, the cell phone's battery ran out quickly. Now Selina had no choice but to try to find another way to escape from the mall.

    Coming out of the small tunnel, she noticed a big problem. Some of the guards were approaching the food court so if she tried to get out of the play area, they would spot her.

    There was only one way to get out of the play area without being discovered, but it wasn't entirely pleasant. Right now, some of the people who worked in the mall's daycare were about to pick up several of the toddlers who were playing in the play area. If she snuck into that group of toddlers, she'd be taken to the daycare and she'd go unnoticed by most of the guards.

    But there was another problem, all the toddlers in the area were much younger than her. So she had no choice but to take more pills so she could camouflage herself among the little kids.

    So Selina proceeded to take the bottle out of her coat and proceeded to take two more pills. Soon, she began to regress so much that now her clothes were too big for her.

    Fortunately, there were several unattended bags in the play area, so she grabbed the nearest one and proceeded to look for something she could wear.

    From the clothes that she found in the bag, she took out an orange romper, orange socks, and a diaper. And although she had difficulty putting on those clothes (especially the diaper) she was able to dress appropriately.

    Now that she was fully dressed, Selina proceeded to find something where she could store her pills and flash drive. And luckily she managed to find something appropiate: a stuffed cat. So Selina proceeded to put the pills and the flash drive inside the stuffed animal. Lastly, Selina proceeded to put her old clothes and her cell phone into the bag she found. Now she was finally ready.

    By the time the guards finally reached the food court, Selina had already joined the group of toddlers who were being taken to the mall's daycare.

    When they finally arrived at daycare, one of the workers proceeded to take Selina and other toddlers to the nap room. Inside the room, each one of the toddlers was placed in an individual crib.

    As Selina was about to be placed in one of the cribs, she noticed something familiar on the face of the woman who was about to put her in the crib. And before long, she finally realized it. That worker was none other than her older sister, Margaret.

    This really surprised Selina but she managed to remember that a week ago her sister had told her that she had just started working at a daycare.

    Knowing that her older sister worked in the mall's daycare…..
