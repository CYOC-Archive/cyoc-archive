    Chronivac Version 4.0

    Emily becomes mentally impaired

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 months ago O Mental

    Emily's mother hesitated, considering the weight of her decision. She knew the power of the Chronivac and the potential consequences of altering someone's mental state. However, she believed that this could be an important experience for Emily, teaching her empathy and the importance of treating others with dignity, regardless of their perceived status.

    After a moment of contemplation, she made her choice. Carefully, she sat down at the computer, her fingers hovering over the keys. She knew she needed to choose a scenario that would allow Emily to understand the struggles and challenges faced by those who may not be as privileged or capable as she was.

    Deciding on a course of action, she navigated to the menu for mental modifications and clicked on "cerebral palsy". She paid particular attention to set the duration for the weekend and that Emily would have all her memories, including this weekend, when she transforms back on Sunday evening. Also she configured the Chronivac so that nobody except her and Maidy would know about these changes.

    She double-checked her settings, ensuring that the experience would be temporary and reversible. Taking a deep breath, she clicked with shaking hands "save" and prepared herself for the unexpected.

    As the Chronivac began its working, Emily suddenly felt a strange sensation wash over her. Her thoughts became muddled, and her ability to process information slowed significantly. Panic set in as she realized her surroundings were becoming harder to understand and navigate. Simple tasks that she once took for granted now required immense effort.

    Emily stumbled, grasping for support as her world became increasingly complex and overwhelming. Her once quick wit and sharp mind were dulled, replaced by confusion and frustration. She dropped a pillow she was still holding, but when she bend down to pick it up Emily lost her balance.

    As Emily fell, Maidy, still in her robotic form, rushed to catch her, supporting her gently. Emily's eyes widened in surprise as she registered the act of kindness from the machine she had been mistreating moments earlier. The contrast between her treatment of Maidy and the assistance she was receiving now struck a chord within her.

    "Maidy... thank you," Emily muttered, her words slow and deliberate due to her changed mental state. The sincerity in her voice was undeniable, and for the first time, she saw Maidy not as a mere machine, but as a sentient being capable of empathy and compassion.

    Maidy, purred softly and offered Emily a hand to help her on her feet. "My pleasure, Miss Emily. It is my duty to assist you," she said, her robotic voice conveying genuine concern.

    Emily hesitantly took Maidy's hand, surprised by the warmth it conveyed. With Maidy's support, she struggled to push herself up, her movements sluggish and uncoordinated. As she stood, she leaned heavily on Maidy, grateful for the stability provided by the maidbot.

    As the family gathered around the dining table a little bit later, the aroma of a homemade meal wafted through the air, filling the space with warmth and comfort. Emily's younger sister, Lily, animatedly shared stories from her day at preschool, her curly blonde hair bouncing as she spoke. Their father, Tom, chimed in with tales from his work as a veterinarian and the various animals he had treated today.

    Emily, struggling with her altered mental state, found it difficult to keep up with the rapid conversation. Her thoughts were sluggish, and her vocabulary was limited. Despite this, she felt content simply being surrounded by her family, soaking in their love and support like she never did before.

    Maidy, ever attentive to their needs, circulated around the table, skillfully ladled steaming soup into each bowl, refilling water glasses and ensuring that everyone had everything they needed.

    Emily's mother sat at the table and watched the changed dynamic of the family. Her eyes were particularly focused on Emily, who was visibly struggling with the usage of her cutlery and feeding herself.

    Noticing her daughter's struggle, Jeff's mother instinctively reached out to assist Emily, but before she could, Maidy gently took Emily's spoon and guided it to her mouth, offering a supportive smile. Emily, initially resistant to accepting help from the maidbot, reluctantly allowed Maidy to feed her the soup. For the remainder of the meal, Emily relied on Maidy's assistance, silently acknowledging her help. To her surprise, the act of sharing this intimate moment with Maidy, despite the circumstances, fostered a sense of connection and understanding.

    As dinner continued, Emily's mother noticed that Emily was looking more and more tired. Suddenly she yawned widely and rubbed her temples. "I'm really tired. My head hurts," she said slowly.

    Understanding the toll the mental changes were taking on Emily, her mother gently suggested, "Why don't you go ahead and head to bed, sweetheart? We can finish up here, and I'll come tuck you in soon."

    "Can Maidy bring me to bed, please", Emily requested, her voice soft and weary. There was a newfound trust in her tone, as if she had come to rely on the maidbot's assistance without question.

    Maidy nodded enthusiastically to her request. Gently, she helped Emily rise from her chair and supported her as they made their way upstairs. Emily leaned heavily on Maidy, grateful for the extra steadiness provided by the maidbot's strong, yet caring presence.

    Once they reached Emily's bedroom, Maidy assisted her putting on her pajama and in getting settled under the covers, pulling the blankets up to her chin. "Goodnight, Miss Emily. Sleep well," Maidy said, her synthetic voice filled with genuine warmth.

    "Thank you, Maidy," Emily whispered, closing her eyes as she snuggled deeper into her pillow. "You've been really nice to me tonight."

    Maidy hovered near the bedside for a moment, ensuring Emily was comfortable, before quietly exiting the room to give her some privacy. As the door closed behind her, Emily's mother watched with a mixture of sadness and joy. She knew the weekend ahead would be challenging for her daughter, but it was heartwarming seeing Emily and Maidy forming a bond based on mutual understanding and support.
