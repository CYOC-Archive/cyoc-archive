    Meeting One Of The Three Little Pigs

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover A month ago AR BM TG O Mythological Magic Kid

    You looked at the three different pig-men. You knew enough of your basic fairy tales to realize that you were in front of some sort of humanised versions of the three little pigs. And thus you knew which one would have the best house or rather the most solid one. To protect yourself from the Big Bad Wolf - unless it was different one? - it was a no-brainer to choose which man to speak to. So you made your way to the one wearing the gear of a construction worker. You stepped towards them and in no time they had all noticed you. You tried not to gulp audibly and not show your fear. You might be a little girl but inside you're a dude, damn it! You inwardly steeled yourself. This was not the moment to get scared!
    "Er... Hello," you said in a voice that had more waver than you would have liked.
    "Hello," said the construction pig.
    You noticed that the man had little tiny tusks protruding out of his lower jaw. He also had big muscles. He was sweating, so you had clearly interrupted him right in the middle of his work.
    "Who are you? Are you lost? What do you want?" asked the burly man.
    It was odd to stare up at a large man, considering that barely less than an hour ago, you were quite tall yourself. It was unnerving. Also, should you tell the complete truth? Would the man believe you? You decide against that. You'd tell him later if needed.
    "I... I need protection," you blurted out. "There is a Bad Wolf out there and I am afraid he'll eat me. Or attack me. I am on the way to my grandma's and... er... Please, your house looks thick enough. to keep away a bad wolf that might attack me. Please, let me stay, at least for a little bit."
