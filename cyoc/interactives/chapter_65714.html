    You have arrived in a forest clearing, far away from the temple, and you are alone with a small demon next to
    you. He's panting and sprawled across the floor.

    "That last one took it out of me!" He wheezes "Can you find me something to eat? I'm starving here"

    You nod, and find some exotic looking fruit from nearby trees. His eyes light up when you bring them to him,
    and he eats ravenously. In fact, before know it, he's eaten the entire armful you picked just a minute ago
    already. He slumps back "Ah yes! That was perfect" He leans up again, and looks at you with an interested eye.
    "I think I've picked the right guy for the job!" You smile "anyway, lets try to find somewhere to stay for the
    night"

    You and the demon start wandering round the forest, and eventually come across a small cave. Along the way you
    have fed the demon more fruit, and once you cut yourself on a branch, and he just licked up all the blood.
    There's something about your blood that he loves! By the time you arrived at the cave you notice that he's
    looking much healthier and livelier. You however are panting from exhaustion!

    "Oh dear, boy, how is it that you are the one struggling when I'm the one who's been trapped in that infernal
    place for so long?"

    "Well I never was as fit as I'd have liked to be..."

    "Well, how about a little reward for getting me out of that place and feeding me?"

    "What are you talking about?"

    "You just wait and see, you're going to love this!" He sits you down in front of him with surprising force,
    and places himself about two feet away. He then starts chanting in the same demonic language as before.
    Suddenly the air in the cave seems much thicker, and it gets much darker. You look around bewildered.

    "Who turned off the lights?" You ask the demon, but he's not listening. In fact when you look to him, he's
    floating a few inches off the ground. You open your mouth to ask another question but are stopped when a
    sudden constricting feeling hits your stomach. You clutch your waist, and are surprised to feel something
    different from the layer of fat that normally ringed your stomach. It felt like it had all gone! You lift up
    your shirt, and before your own eyes you see a skinny waist grow hard and defined. Six ridges push their way
    through your skin and proudly present themselves. The constricting feeling, which you realise is more
    pleasurable than painful, spreads upwards to your chest. Two heavy slabs of muscle solidify below your neck,
    jutting out and overshadowing your newly grown six pack.

    Suddenly it dawns on you that you too are floating in the air. You and the demon were both a few feet in the
    air, and surrounded by an aura of darkness. You straighten up your body and feel the demon's power seep into
    you. You look at your arms and see that where once there was flab, there are now two large, defined biceps.
    You're not sure how it happened, but all your clothes have vanished, and you can clearly see well developed
    legs below you. This felt good! You feel strong and powerful, and regret having wasted away all those years
    being a nerd.

    The transformation complete, the demon and you slowly drift back to the ground and gently land. You have a
    huge grin on your face, and are already flexing and experimenting with your new muscles. The demon watches
    with amusement. "Glad to see you're enjoying yourself! If I'm going to be bound to somebody for eternity, I
    wouldn't want them to be a weakling like you were"
