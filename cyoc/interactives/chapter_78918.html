    Matt wasn't sure if he should be pleased with the change or not. He could barely stand because he was taller than the ceiling allowed, and he was far too large to sleep on his bed. In fact it creaked under his weight as he sat on it. He tried using the bracelet, but for some reason it refused to work. On him anyways. When his wish to shrink down a little failed to work after multiple tries he decided to wish for a larger bed. In a matter of seconds his bed had grown to fit his new size, and weight. "Maybe this won't be so bad," Matt muttered, his voice quite deep now.

    Of course his clothes had torn when Cody had grown him, and so he quickly wished for his entire wardrobe to be resized to fit him. It worked like a charm. He quickly stripped off the remnants of his old clothes and got to his feet, though promptly hit his head on the ceiling. As Matt looked himself over he took in his giant, muscled body. He really was quite a sight, with each giant muscle perfectly toned and hard as rock. From his giant pecs and washboard abs to his bubble butt and giant shoulders. Everything was huge and powerful. Even his manhood was huge. Either way he quickly pulled on a giant pair of boxers, followed by a t-shirt and shorts. With that done he squeezed through his bedroom door and into the upstairs hallway. He wanted to find Cody and see if there was a reason the bracelet wasn't working anymore.

    "Enjoying being huge?" Cody asked, appearing from his room.

    "Yes and no," Matt replied, "The size is nice, but it's hard to move. Could you maybe take some of it back?"

    "Nope, sorry big bro," said Cody, "I don't want it back, and even if I did I couldn't. It's simple really, I met this little imp when I was six, he was caught in a rat trap and I helped him out. He rewarded me with that super growth that left me eight feet tall and giant when I finished growing. The other day he came and told me I could give it away, but once given away it'd be gone for good. So I gave it to you."

    "Wait, so I'm stuck like this?" Matt asked.

    "Not quite. You can give all that size away if you want, but there's a cooldown period. You have to pass it someone else, but you can only do that after spending a month that way. Sorry big bro," Cody said, and retreated into his room.

    "So I guess the bracelet is currently acting against itself," muttered Matt in thought. "Maybe this won't be so bad, I can still change other things, just not myself," he added, "I'll just make the most of it and go somewhere. Only question is where to go."
