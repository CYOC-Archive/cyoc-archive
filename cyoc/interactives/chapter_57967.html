    On the way to the sewing room...

    added by Sweetums77 12 years ago A O

    Jen practically ran from the kitchen, the thought of those awful reindeer cookies made her stomach churn. She couldn’t quite believe all this was happening, Kyle’s curse was coming true, she and the rest of the squad were becoming reindeer girls and everything was slowly starting to develop a slight Christmas theme.

    As Jen rounded the corner on her way to the sewing class room she glances up at the doors to the auditorium, only someone had removed the sign that said “auditorium” and replaced it with “Toy Dept.”

    She glanced around nervously, “This is ridiculous” Jen whispered to herself and then tried the door. It was locked, but she could hear the sounds of workmen inside constructing what she figured must be the new toy factory. Unable to open the huge oak doors Jen was forced to continue her journey to the sewing room, she reached up and rubbed her painful horns with one hand, now they had broken the skin the pain had dulled. She just hoped they would grow in quickly and get it over with, like her tail, that hadn’t been painful at all, in fact she hadn’t noticed it, but it did feel good on her.

    “No! Jen you’re not a reindeer!” She said to herself over and over, a little mantra trying to keep things normal.

    “Ah! Jen!” Jen immediately stopped and turned around, she recognised the voice of the principal, but as she turned she gasped when she noticed who was stood with him.

    “Hey Jen!” Kyle said from behind the Principals back, the pair had just come out of the Principals office and were holding two large boxes of paper. Kyle didn’t look any different to her, but then again she’d only noticed the changes in the cheerleaders so far...

    “Jen, Kyle has been telling me that some of the girls haven’t been attending sleigh practise... is this true?” The Principal said sternly.

    “I... uhm... err...” Jen stammered, glaring angrily at Kyle, what the hell was going on?! What was he doing standing there smirking at her.

    “Jen, as head Reindeer I expect you to make sure the whole team is ready and together to pull that sleigh. Do you want to ruin Christmas for everyone?” He barked more loudly. “As I’m sure you know Jen, me and Kyle have a huge amount of work to do getting everything ready, and if Kyle is going to be able to deliver these presents we need a good reindeer team!”

    Jen noticed every time he said “Kyle” his face twitched, like he wanted to say something else instead...

    “I understand you’re about to go for a new harness fitting?” The Principal said, his voice softening slightly.

    “Make sure everything fits right and we can get a good shine” Kyle said, barely able to contain his enormous perverted grin.

    “Shine?” Jen said slowly.

    “I guess that hasn’t come through yet... hmmm, I guess it’ll remain surprise.” Kyle said smiling, he and the principal turned away from her and started walking away.

    “See you at practise Jen!” Kyle called back to her.

    Jen stamped her foot; if the principal hadn’t been there she would have killed the little creep, as it was she was stuck heading to a harness fitting... She gulped... dreading what was behind that door... she considered going back to the kitchen... but the reindeer treats still sounded horrible...

    Sighing she spotted another “Sleigh Training” Flyer on the wall as she pushed open the door to the sewing room and gasped...
