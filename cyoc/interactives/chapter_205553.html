    Clown for hire

    Barry was a 16 teen year old with dirty blond hair who was looking for his first job. Walking around town he saw a poster for the carnival in town he walk over to the carnival and up to the main office. He asked if there were any available positions thinking he could work one of the carnival games. The manager said oh we absolutely do and you need no prior training to be hired. We have an opening for a new Circus Clown. Barry was a little confused how he would do that with no prior training but he accepted the offer anyway. Overjoyed the Carnival Manager brought Barry over to the main circus tent, into a small office room there. He brought over the Head Clown of the Circus named Popsy she was an average height Whiteface Clown with a hot pink nose and hair. She was wearing a frilly pink dress and purple clown shoes. Barry introduced himself to her and she did the same. The carnivals Manager who’s name was Mr. Presto told Barry that Popsy here would be his boss whenever he came into work. With that Mr.Presto left Popsy to show Barry around and get him ready for his first day tomorrow.

    Popsy gave Barry a tour around the whole circus along with bringing him in back to clown alley where he would report to work. This was when Barry was introduced to two other clowns Tootsy an August Clown Girl an Alfie a Whiteface Clown Boy. Both of these clowns were still getting ready for the show that night, they introduced themselves. Barry noted that they both seemed to be his age. Popsy sat Barry down in front of a rack of costumes. She told him to pick three different ones. This would be what he would wear during his shows. Barry picked out a blue jumpsuit with rainbow Polka Dots. That had ruffles at the neck, wrists and Ankles. The next costume he picked out was a bright red and yellow striped shirt with a big bow tie around the neck with a matching pair of red with polka doted hula hoop pants. Finally the third one was an other jumpsuit and rainbow stripes pants and a light blue shirt with a hoop in the middle. Each one of these costumes had a matching pair of clown shoes. Barry asked what next and Popsy responded by asking him his favorite color. Which he responded with red and then she asked him his favorite shape where he said diamonds. Finally she asked which he liked more the sun or the moon and he responded with the moon. Popsy asked him to go put on one of his costumes while she gets something ready. Saying ok Barry grabbed the blue jumpsuit and the collar and went into a dressing room and put it on with the matching shoes. He took off his clothes except for his underwear and slipped into the jumpsuit where the jumpsuit came right up to the base of his neck were the frilly collar sat. Putting on the shoes he looked at himself in the mirror and thought he looked dumb kind of liked it. Tripping over his shoes as he walked out of the dressing room. Popsy helped him up and handed him a clown nose. She told him that everyday when you come in for work put on your clown nose and you’ll be all ready for work. Grabbing the tennis ball sized red sphere, Barry placed it on to his nose. He started to giggle as he waddled over to a mirror he watched as his skin started to turn as white as the snow. The color originated from the clown nose. He watched as his hair started to curl and turn bright red. And then his eyebrows disappeared, reappearing as tall arching black lines on his forehead. Where then blue diamonds appeared around his eyes. Then finally his brain was flooded with all the knowledge he would need to be a circus clown. Laughing he told Popsy that his Clown Name would be Bazzle the Clown.
