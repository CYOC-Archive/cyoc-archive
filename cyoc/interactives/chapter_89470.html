    Jamie found herself transported to another hallway except this one was short a jutted off in several directions. Jamie ran down the hallway not thinking taking left and rights before finding herself competently lost. This place is a maze Jamie thought, how am I ever gonna to find that key? Jamie brought her hand up to her face to wipe the sweat off her brow and was greeted by a surprise. She looked at her hand shocked. Her fingernails had gotten more sharp and claw like and several tiny brown hairs were growing up her wrist.

    Jamie screamed "What's happening to me?" and then it hit her, this is what the game of meant by "forms". "This is no time to get upset" Jamie said "Brad is still counting on me". Jamie pushed on through the maze, still as lost as she was when she started.

    After 20 minutes of searching Jamie decided to take a break. She looked at her arms and noticed the hair slowing spreading up them. It's getting worse she though to herself and she took out a make up mirror from the front pocket of her overalls and took a look at her face to see if there were any changes. Her face still looked normal except for her ears which had expanded to the size of small dinner plates. As she inspected them thin whiskers pierced her upper lip and grew out a few inches. "I'm becoming a mouse and this is my maze!" Jamie didn't know how much longer she had until her transformation was complete.

    Over the next hour Jamie continued to wander aimlessly through the maze as her body continued to change. First her teeth lengthen out of her mouth making the front and bottom rows much bigger. Then her fur continued to grow all over her body until she was covered, shortly after that her nose pushed out and reformed into a mouse muzzle. Last off a pulling sensation from her spine stopped Jamie in her tracks. Jamie grabbed the seat of her overalls as they slowly tented up. "Ahhhh!" Jamie yelled as a long flesh like tail ripped from the back of her overalls and dropped to the floor. Jamie was now a complete mouse. She stopped and began to cry "Where is the key?" Jamie was starting to feel like this was hopeless until something wonderful happened.

    Jamie could smell cheese, her mouse nose was picking up the scent. She wiped the tears from her face and began following the trail, every step it getting stronger. After another 15 min she came to another long hallway with a pedestal at the end. Jamie ran to the end of the hallway. She could feel her tail dragging across the floor, it was cold. She finally reached the end of the hallway and found her first key tied to a piece of cheese. She grabbed it a jumped up and down in excitement "I did it, I did it!" Suddenly there was a bright flash of light and Jamie found herself in the main hall again.

    Jamie looked over her body and saw she had returned to normal "Wow I'm human again" she looked behind her and saw even the hole in her overalls her tail made was gone. Almost like they had never been ripped. Jamie took a quick second to enjoy being a human again before bringing the key Brad statue.

    One down four to go, Jamie though before heading to the next door.
