    The Bikini Spell on Jessica Kramer

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 12 years ago BM

    Once at school, Kain was eager to start casting spells. He immediately notices a very attractive teacher and sees that her name is Miss Kramer. He approaches her and says "I'm new here - could you help me find my class?" She looks at him closely, "What's your name?" The intense stare of her blue eyes scares Kain - is this a test, he wonders. He starts to mutter a spell.

    "What are you saying?" she asks. He keeps muttering until Miss Kramer notices something strange happening to her. "What are you doing to me?"

    Suddenly, Kain has transformed her clothes into a string bikini. He realizes he had cast his wish fulfillment spell on her.

    "Oh, I'm sorry!" Kain says.

    Miss Kramer stood there, frozen in disbelief staring down at her bikini-clad body.

    "I've put a spell on you. But I can fix it. Let's go somewhere where nobody will see you," Kain explains.

    Unsure of what to do, Miss Kramer follows Kain to a secluded spot behind the school. Kain looks at her body. Her blond hair and tight body look perfect in the skimpy bikini that shows off her breasts and legs.

    She finds her voice to speak again.

    "Are you some kind of sorcerer? I know a lot of the boys at school have crushes on me, but this - you put a spell on me?! Look at what you've done to me!"

    "You look gorgeous, I have to say, Miss Kramer."

    She reluctantly smiles.

    "You can call me Jessica. After all, you've practically seen me naked. This is barely a bikini, isn't it? You can see nearly every inch of me. You don't leave much to the imagination in your spells do you?"

    Kain appreciates her sense of humor. He didn't know any humans that were so calm.

    "Are you going to be able to break this spell you've put on me?" she asks.

    "Yes, but I'm not sure how yet. I'm a wizard's apprentice. Let me at least give you my sweatshirt to wear over your...you know, swimsuit." Kain takes off his sweatshirt and tries to put it on Jessica but the power of the spell flings it right off.

    "The spell I've put on you is too powerful. Let's get out of here so no one sees you. I'll figure out how to undo this."

    "Wait - you want me to go somewhere with you? What else are you planning to do to me?"

    "Miss - Jessica, I feel really bad for doing this to you. You seem very nice. I'm just going to focus on breaking this spell."

    "You have put a spell on me, so I'm pretty much at your mercy. No turning me into a frog, ok?"

    "Who would want to do that to you?"

    "My ex-boyfriend might." Kain was glad to hear any boyfriend she had was an "ex."

    He took her to his car and they drove away. But Kain had a sinking feeling that for the wish fulfillment spell to be broken, he'd had to fulfill his wish -- which involved much more than seeing Jessica in a bikini.
