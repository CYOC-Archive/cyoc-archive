    You heard a mechanical hatch open up, and a cool breeze touch the base of your spine for a brief moment.

    "Horns lost!" A headband-like mechanical arm is fixed around your skull and causes your temples to warm up.

And all of your teeth have been turned into molars, perfect for a grass only diet, and little else. You can't see them, but you also know that a pair of sharp horns now adorn the sides of your head, along with your longer cow ears.

On the screen, some kind of imagery is shown, but you don't know what it is. However it is calming you down significantly. Soon you stop worrying about most things, even about becoming a mutated cow. The visor returns to the wall, and you just idle in place as you are moved into the last empty stalls in the milking parlor. The side wall opens up and you are lead out after your nose is pierced with a ring, and your left ear is tagged.

    You waddle into place above some kind of box, and begin munching on the trough full grass before you. You suddenly hear a buzzer and feel 4 cold tubes slide over your teats. You look around for a moment, the the milking begins, and you grin as your overfilled udder is made to feel really good as your milk is rapidly drawn out of it. You go back to eating while you are milked, all the while feeling really good.

He begins touching you, playing with your hooves, pulling your long hair, your tail, etc... it's pretty annoying. You moo loudly at him and look at him with frustration from having your good feeling ruined. This causes him to back off for now, so you return back to your eating.

They only collected the excess. And while it wasn't as large, your huge udder still squished against the inner sides of your hind legs. So you protest this by staying standing in the pen. The scientist guy watches you, so you moo loudly at him and move your head down to look at your udder and the box in the floor.

    He gets the message and moves over and presses a button. The suction cups return from the box beneath you, and you are milked once again. This time until you are adequately emptied. Though your udder still is much larger than the other cows. At least your legs are not being forced apart, and only a little bit of udder squishing is happening.
