    Erica did a mental double take, "Did he just say 'his city?' "

    The body of Tamthis', once again Tommy's after what seemed like eternity fell to the
    sandstone floor limp, severely bruising his muzzle in the process, he was going to feel
    that when he got back to his body. Tommy's spirit uploaded itself into the threads of
    magic that held the city and it's NPC like citizens together, becoming his own spreading
    drop of ink following after Tamthis' spreading drop of ink.

    Kyriel couldn't help but think it was odd looking at what was Tommy's body now... still a
    tall humanoid fennec kitsune, still draped in an oversized version of Karther's cape and
    still showing off it's body for all the world to see, physically, not a single thing had
    changed.

    Erica repeated herself as if no one had heard her, "Did he just call this 'his city?' "

    "Yes, it seems he did." Jasmine responded at last in a totally calm voice, Tommy's 'residue'
    as his awareness spread through the Karthis looking his Tamthis persona appeared to be
    looking the throne room from imploding on them. Jasmine wanted to make a few gestures
    however, "If that kitsune hadn't chosen to flee, I wish at least that he had undone this
    curse upon my shell before doing so."

    "Is something wrong with being in the body of a female and a fox?" Lisa asked in an even
    tone.

    "I gave no protest to this form purely for the sake of the performance of us being the
    kitsune's women during at stealthy entry into the city. Having my shell still being in a
    shape altered by a kitsune flusters me."

    Erica asked the obvious question, "Why not just undo it yourself?"

    Kyriel was the one to speak up, "Because... mixing magics... can always lead to
    unpredictable side effects and unforeseen out comes... while Jasmine can remove the leaf
    curse on her own, doing so might backfire, in particular magic based so heavily on
    organized chaos like kitsune magic... Karther was engulfed in it, likely why he funneled all
    of his insanity into the castle walls to keep from losing all direction and lucidity."

    "Good text book answer." Jasmine said to the cherub. Truth be told, Jasmine had little faith
    in the barely reconstructed Ego of Tommy being able to overwhelm, or subdue the
    remains of Karther's programming, but she had told him he had twenty minutes, and that
    was precisely what she intended to give him. 'Also, I never imagined that Tabris' offspring
    would actually succeed in digging out the remains of the lad's Ego to begin with... let us
    see if I am surprised twice in one day...'

    "Do I have to keep asking this question? Did he just say 'his city?' His city? The city
    magically created out of nothing but sand? The city populated with nothing but people
    who were swindled into agreeing to become citizens here? And what did you mean 'shock
    of the new city' er.... Jasmine." Erica realized she didn't have a clue what to properly
    address Jasmine as! She had been given the fundamentals of her new race, including that
    unlike Angels in Christianity, Islamic Angels couldn't disobey or deviate from order even if
    they wanted to! With that in mind, Erica realized she should have been very very VERY
    grateful that Jasim's orders were to unleash the Dijinn on Karther IF Tabris' group failed...

    Jasmine looked at Erica like the incredibly younger Angel had asked a incredibly stupid
    question, then got a look of realization, "Oh right, you and your sisters who chose to
    become Messengers were sculptured to specialize as soldiers so you wouldn't have sensed
    it... the illusion barriers are gone, the Veil has been removed... operation Return has
    begun."

    All three younger Messengers gasped at this.

    "Dad/Mom, you have your wish." Kyriel whispered. "For better or worse, the kingdom of
    illusions is no more."

    "Wouldn't it have made sense to wait until AFTER we had Tamthis dealt with?" Erica asked
    exasperated.

    Jasmine shook her head, "Tis not ours to reason why, only to do as commanded. I had no
    idea this would happen when it did until it had happened. Perhaps whoever has done so
    wished to make sure to get humanity's attention with a large enough incident."

    Lisa sighed, "I guess what's done is done and what's done can't be changed so we had
    better deal with what's happening now. ... And Erica has a point though... Tommy did call
    this 'his city' and he did say he would not let us dismantle it anymore than Tamthis."

    "Exactly," Erica groaned, "How are we gonna keep from ending up having to fight kooky
    fox king number three?"

    "Tommy's not like that!" Kyriel snapped startling everyone as she stamped her foot. "He's
    not evil! And he doesn't want to conquer the middle east!"

    "Kyriel, Erica didn't say he did." Lisa intervened, "But what are we to do? Nearly everyone in
    this city is here because they were kidnapped or lied to. The whole REASON why T' came
    to help us was because Karther was filling up his castle with people he was plucking away
    from their lives at random. Well, most at least." Lisa thought of Alexandra, what the
    kitsune had said suggested that she just might have, just might have been given a choice,
    though knowing Karther it wasn't likely an informed one.

    Erica rubbed where her eyebrows would be if she still had them, "Not to mention all the
    conflicting clans here are only not killing each other because Tamthis -programmed-
    them to get along. Like robots."

    Kryiel spoke with total confidence and faith, "After Tommy beats Tamthis, he'll let everyone
    here go. There aren't many natural born fennec kitsune left, but Tommy knows he wont'
    need to create slaves to find volunteers."

    Erica leaned in close, "You're saying that as if you believe Tommy's gonna go back to a
    nicer version of bastard Karther's plan."

    "... When Tommy assimilated Tammia and some of the other subparts, he effectively
    inherited Karther's position as prince of all desert foxes.. including the task of getting the
    fennec kitsune back on track.. I was a part of his mind for a short bit... he's... I think
    seeing this city and the fennec kitsune as his responsibility has become internalized." She
    looked at them sternly. "But that DOESN'T mean he's going to turn into another Karther!"

    ERica said, "So HOW is your dad's/mom's best friend kicking his worst part's butt gonna
    keep Jasmine's hypothetical General Ripper from blowing up this place?"

    Lisa spoke, "With all due respect elder Jasmine, I feel that you're jumping to conclusions...
    you don't attack something just because it randomly appears in the middle of nowhere...
    and if Return has really begun, there are now more than a few cities now that have
    suddenly appeared on the maps. They'd only attack if they linked this place to the villages
    that had suddenly been de-populated in the last few days.. and right now they're two
    related and unknown phenomenon. -After- Tommy destroys or assimilates Tamthis," Lisa
    looked kindly at Kyriel, "If he really has internalized the mantle of leader of the fennec
    kitsune, he'll have enough time to peacefully contact the leaders of the lands."

    Kyriel looked at Jasmine, and fought against the part of her wanting to call the older angel
    'mommy.' 'My parents are gone. My mother and my father gives themselves so I could be
    born. Jasmine is not my mom.' Not that there was really a set president for such things,
    since Angels did not normally biologically reproduce.

    Erica still had a malcontent look on her face, "But that still leaves the question, how do we
    keep the locals in the city from killing each other once they're no longer fox zombies?"

    "We don't." Jasmine said plainly, "If that is their choice, that is their choice. If they chose to
    remain here that is there choice. If they choice to kill each other, that is their choice. If they
    chose to go home and leave the fighting for another day, that'll be their choice too... we
    are here but to remove the threat that the current kitsune prince represents to the
    mundane and mythical worlds."

    While Erica and Lisa stood shocked, Kryiel spoke up smiling, "Dad/mom would be
    surprised and impressed miss Jasmine... you respecting human's ability to make their own
    decisions."

    "And what about you little cherub?" Jasmine asked calmly.

    "I do what my dad/mom would have done... remind them they have a choice not to kill,
    which many people in a blood feud forget."

    Erica and Lisa looked at the cherub, with a slight look of awe on their faces. 'I knew there
    was a reason I could still think of Kyriel as a leader in spite of her being so cute now.' Lisa
    thought. The fact that the two of them could even for a moment defer to Kyriel's point of
    view rather than that of the senior Angel, reminded that the sisters were no islamic
    messengers.

    "Come on," Kyriel flew past the three older angels (flying was faster than walking for her
    now), let's get Tommy's body out of here in case this place starts to turn inside out before
    Tommy had put Tamthis in his place!

    Alexandra entered the courtyard, the water was still flowing and the plants were still
    green but that was it. All the fountains and sculptures wavered as if they were made of
    jello during an earthquake. Still, they were away from any tall buildings as much as they
    could be without trying to rush through the entire city which Alexandra considered a fool's
    gamble.

    Catching her breath Alexandra laid down poor Jennifer and Kathy... 'Why I am I helping
    them at all?... I guess because we are all victims at this point of my prince's madness.'

    She saw sand object split in two right down the middle then seal back up together again...
    she saw faces of kitsune come out of the walls of sand just long enough to be noticed
    before fading back in. She shuddered. Was this city going to die too? How many times was
    her life going to be stolen from here?

    When she saw the shadow fall on her, she knew Kathy had apparently woken up.
    Alexandra was no warrior, never was, had no desire to be, but she was a hard worker!
    Kathy had been made not to do any intense action outside of the bedroom. Kathy knew
    how to comment, please, gossip, laugh, joke, and make love, Alexandra knew how to kill
    live stock to prepare for dinner. In short, Alexandra was just stronger than her.

    "KATHY! There is no point to this! PLEASE! I beg you! Just open your eyes!"

    "I'm wide awake traitor!" She hissed.

    "No you're not!... Kathy..." Alexandra realized... this woman was exactly the same as her...
    both of them had their lives stolen and had been custom built to fit someone else's
    fantasy. "Do you really think I'd betray our kind? Our people? Our kingdom?"

    "You have! You want to see our king dead!"

    ".... Lisa, the Angel told me and you... our king is ill, they just want to cure him of his
    sickness..." She didn't say how the sickness was Tamthis' personality, but for Alexandra, it
    was close enough for the truth. She still hadn't forgiven Tamthis for erasing Karther from
    existence... Alexandra had loved him, created or not, they were her emotions, and her
    prince had been stolen from her, forever. Now the being who he had been used as raw
    material for was trying to kill her.

    "LIES!"

    "LOOK AROUND YOU! This city is him and he is this city... can you say this city looks well?"

    Finally, somehow, Kathy DID take in her surroundings. "This... this is nothing but a trick!"

    "It's no stage performance... !! Kathy look out behind you!"

    "You insult my-" Alexandra dove down and tackled Kathy just as six scimitars smacks into
    the sand stone.

    Like a snake in the grass the insanity had looked for a host, a medium, anything to create
    another outlet from the insanity that was growing greater and greater within Tamthis as
    he spread his mind over this city and suddenly found new awareness within it as well that
    were not welcome breaking his center even more.

    It had found Jennifer, still unconscious, still lacking arms after having lost four. The
    dismembered shiva vixen had the sand plunge into her as the two vixens had fought one
    another. In a matter of seconds Jennifer had become seven feet tall, had grown SIX arms,
    and now had a scimitar in each, the sloppy programming not being nearly complex
    enough to deal with diverse weapons. While Tamthis would never ever consciously harm
    Kathy even to save his own life, Jennifer was under the control of Tamthis', formerly
    Karther's insanity, the broken weapon mindlessly seeing anything alive and bled red as a
    target.

    'Kill.. kill...kill..." The Shiva vixen's mouth spoke.

    "What?..." Kathy looked completely shattered.

    "I told you! Your master is sick! It's spreading to everything!" Alexandra snarled. Alexandra
    was being reminded far too much of her own state a short short while ago at the news of
    her prince gone, bu Alexandra had the adrenaline rush to keep thinking straight look
    along to pull Kathy away from the mad shiva vixen.

    Alexandra didn't know where she could run... or where she could lead Kathy if the harem
    vixen had a nervous break down.. an Alexandra didn't like the taste of just leaving the
    kitsune to her fate.

    The mad shiva vixen rose her six swords into a splash pattern around herself and prepared
    another series of mad slashes. Five seconds and four blurs later the shiva vixen was
    disarmed, on the ground, restrained, and was being unhexed for the second time that
    day, this time with safety measures thrown in prevent such a thing again, it being much
    simpler this time since the brainwashing was the incredibly sloppy 'zap and go' variety.

    Alexandra and Kathy huddled together forgetting their previous fight and stared in awe at
    what they saw.

    "You... you saved me?... Natalie... Lucy? What are you doing with them?" At this point, the
    minor possibility that Alexandra might have been telling the truth had finally dawned on
    Kathy's made from scratch mind.

    Natalie merely whimpered and rubbed against Kathy, not what she had come to expect
    from the alpha female of her king's giant muscle bound killing machines. Lucy, sighed and
    closed her solid black eyes. "I've... finally woken up."

    "So... are you one of them now?" Kathy asked.

    "No," Vicki said putting a hand on Lucy's still sandy shoulders, "She's no angel." Lucy
    might have had the appearance of one, but Vicki now knew that meant the same as
    wearing a police officer's uniform. Luna and Vicki in a united effort obliterated the cheap
    programming inside Jennifer, snapping her back to reality... she groaned and looked at
    herself... "Dangit... still not male or wrinkly... looks like I got that return with interest
    though huh?"

    "If you can joke then you'll be fine." Luna said.

    Lucy dismissed the sand bound holding down Jennifer, Vicki this time had been careful to
    leave some defenses in place to make sure Lucy didn't snap back into insanity or under
    the city (meaning Tamthis' ) control.

    "Lot of stuff happen to you?" Luna asked Vicki.

    "Yes, you?"

    "Same. Natalie okay?"

    "I was able to repair her soul, but her mind is gonna take a lot longer to fix, yours?"

    "'Hers' is ..." Lucy said not liking to be talked of in third person, "Is... is whatever 'me'
    means anymore! Before at least I was still a living being! Now I'm just sand in the shape of
    the a anthro vixen! I'm not anything!"

    Luna broken into Lucy's lament, "LUCY! I love you! You're my sister! You're a part of our
    family! Same as Keiko, Erica, Kyriel, Charlene and all the rest! But we really don't have time
    for you to have a break down right now! Let's hurry to the throne room and just maybe we
    can catch a glimpse of the finale before everything ends..."

    Vicki, "Luna, you should know now, as what we are, you should know, most endings, turn
    out to be just new beginning."

    Luna, "Nice big sis but not that relevant! Let's move it!"

    'What am I feeling?' Kathy thought, 'Within the wall of city I feel my king, who is yet not my
    king, and one who is not my king, and yet is my king... am I going mad?' Kathy asked
    herself becoming more sane than she had been in her entire existence.

    'Us? We're your sisters, true you weren't part of the harem for very long before T' jumped

    'It's true.'

    'No I meant, the japanese don't have thanks giving.'

    'Oh, right.'

    'Are you two crazy?'

    "" Yes.""

    The mental image of the magic web first became a copy of Karther's castle, then it became
    a baseball stadium from Tommy's home town... then it became a hot-springs house made
    to look painted like an anime.

    'AKANE! Cut it out!' Keiko shouted the place then became woods that spread out infinitely
    in all directions. 'Basically, we offer our imagination at your disposal for deleting what's
    left of bastard Karther's brainwashing and insanity.' The web of magic from their
    perspective became shapeless again and they were all once again formless masses of will
    and awareness. 'Besides, we figure you need help with the kitsune magic thing.'

    'Don't worry, I can handle it, thanks for the help though, I'm sure it'll make things easier.'

    'Hold on! Was that Kyriel just now?'

    'Just the part of her that's now a part of me, don't worry about.'

    'While you three had been blabbing... I've been absorbing more of my city's magic back to
    myself...'

    'What?' Akane said surprised, 'Dang! The meta-magic plane should be like in games where
    speaking is a free action!'

    'Don't worry... without the part of Kyriel that became a part of me he can't control or
    regulate it anymore nearly as good as he wants to think... he created this web when he was
    still whole... now he's just parts... Once again Tamthis.. you have nowhere to run now...
    either became a part of me, or vanish into oblivion.'

    'Enough... it'll be you who'll becomes a part of me.'

    'Ohh! Me Akane love trash talk before final final confrontation taking place in final final

    'Prepare yourself then for game over with your save data erased! I am the king of all
