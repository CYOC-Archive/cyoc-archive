    Someone to cuddle

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 5 years ago A BM S

    Matt had been a good friend of Jake's ever since he first enrolled. So he wanted to give Matt a change he could appreciate. Matt often told Jake that him and Randy love to cuddle under a warm blanket while hanging out in the dorm. Jake thought, despite Matt's masculine physique, he was actually a big teddy bear that couldn't help but show a sensitive side. Than Jake had his idea for Matt.

    Jake typed that Matt will become a burly bear complete with a big muscle gut, and soft warm fur. He added his fur color would match his natural hair color, and adjust his clothes so the would fit his new physique. It was at that moment, the door knob rattled and Matt walked right back in the room. "Hey. Sorry that took so long. What ya doing?" Jake pressed enter and closed his laptop. "Just finishing a paper." Jake lied. "So I don't know if your into this, but there's a poker tournament running on tv right now. That sound like somthing you'd like?" Matt asked. Jake never really cared for poker. He was more interested in watching Matt's changes which seemed to have started already as he saw the back of his neck grow scruffy brown fur. "Sure. Might be intresting." Jake went to sit by Matt on the sofa.

    Matt picked up the remote to turn the tv on and Jake took notice of another change. His nails were growing into dark claws, and his hands had grown black pads. The fur that started from his neck had spread to his forearms already. "Oh, looks like Daniel can pull a full house with this deck. You have no idea how rare that is." Matt was showing excitement as his changes progressed. His ears became furry and rounded, and his belly was starting to expand outside of his shirt, with fur spreading downward. Matt tried adjusting himself to sit more comfortably just in time for Jake to see a stubby tail emerge from his rump.

    Matt was getting noticeably bigger within a few seconds. His arms, legs, and belly probably packed an extra 30 pounds to him. It was the last deal of the night and Matt was paying close attention, he didn't notice Jake was just watching him change the whole time. The fur finally spread to his head, and his nose had turns black. His face started stretching toward, and fur covered his entire face, completeling his transformation. "So Marcela comes out a winner tonight. What a game." Matt said shutting the tv off.

    "I think I'm about to hit the hey. Or just hibernate without the winter." Matt said putting his furry arms around Jake. "Woah! Relax! We're just friends, o- oh wow, that's soft..." Jake started to embrace Matt's bear hug, stuffing his snout into Matt's furry belly. "You enjoying yourself?" Matt asked. "I'm afraid I won't let go." Jake was reluctant, but was able to break from Matt and the soft trance he found himself in. "Oh! Sorry... I came on to strong... I couldn't help it..." Matt apologised. "Uh... don't worry about it. I won't mention it to Randy." Jake said standing up from the couch. "Anyway, I gotta get back to my room. Thanks for letting me hang with you." Jake said walking toward the door. "Thanks for coming. Gets lonely here sometimes." Matt responded. Jake left through the door avoiding hitting his tail.
