    Jamie woke and reached for the zipper, but he couldn't find it. You had changed in the night, you fit him like a zentai suit instead of a sleepsack. He stood up. The world looked fuzzy through his fabric covered eyes. Jamie groaned in disappointment that you sealed him in. Jamie, decided to pack his things up and go back to the factory to get you fixed. Or at least replace the zipper that disappeared in your spandex fabric. "You know, I should've brought a spare sleeping bag the moment I got you." Jamie says out loud knowing you'd hear him. You understood that w/ a grunt as Jamie drives carefully back due to having you cover up and distort his vision.

    Back at the factory, Jamie, still sealed up by you, approached the front desk and talked to the zebra pattern zentai drone. "Hey, my sleeping bag got me sealed up in here, and I was hoping you would help us get separated." Jamie says to the drone turning around to show no zipper track on you.
    "Oh my, that is a problem!" the zebra drone exclaimed. "Can you explain what had happened?"

    Jamie tells the drone that everything was fine until he slept in you and woke up looking like you. The same thing happened when a wild animal turned into you when Jamie realizes it was you that got turned into a zentai sleeping bag. And finally ending the story of Jamie sleeping inside you, only to realize that you sealed him in.

    The zebra drone then talks, "I see. Well, I am afraid we can't help free you from your friend, at the moment. The only thing we can do is to fuse you two together. You don't like hearing that, nor did Jamie as he groaned. "Did you guys even manage to make a reverse process plan for people who wears living zentai like my friend?" he asks. "Well we did, but most of our customers that do get into this situation, usually accepts to be fused w/ the suit. And both the wearer's and the living suit's individuality are still intact. Minus one miscellaneous problems." the drone stated.
    "What are these miscellaneous problems?"
    "Well, if you aren't w/ the hive, you don't have your language to speak, only just the muffled sound of grunts or groans. The fusing process tends to distort the person's speaking problem w/o a mouth. For joining the hive, you can retain it even w/o a mouth. And most of our customers wanted to retain that." You or Jamie didn't like one of those situations. Thus if you and Jamie are going to be fused, there is also the other choice of turning into another object. You don't like the fact your friend is turned into another object while you be his living "skin".

    Jamie sighs and accepts his fate, but asks one more question to the drone, "Well, if we do get fused together, and if you do get a chance to make a reversal process, what will happen if two fused individuals take the reversal process?"

    The drone then thinks about the possibilities and answers, "Well, this is just a theory, but if we do schedule a reversal process and it becomes a success, we might give people a second chance to live as normal organic people." the drone then goes to the theory of both individuals fused, "However, in this theory of a person and their living suit fused as one, the chances of reversal is... Well, lower than seventy percent, as the chance of the owner's health may be fatal, only the suit may only be intact since he/she was mostly just a suit before the fusion process. But as we said, this is just a theory." the drone then adds another one, "But in your situation of being sealed, there is a chance of you having minor to moderate health problems while the living suit will still go unscathed. We do not know the damages of it to be done for both theories."

    If you had a heart, it would've sank by now, but you can feel Jamie's thought about that theory, he doesn't like dying, but if it means dying as a human being and save his sleeping bag friend to be sent to another caring owner, then so be it. Reading Jamie's thought, he wanted to get out of you as you think the same way. But the other thought is being fused together w/ you w/o joining the hive. He can deal w/o speaking any human languages. That you don't want him to do. But as a sealed suit, you can't project much thought into what you say and let him choose you and his fate.

    What will Jamie choose?
