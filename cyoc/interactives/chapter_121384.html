    The Magic Shop

    Two bunnies are even cuter than one!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by JosieHadley 6 years ago A BM S


    You look down at your hands. You are surprised to find the same white fluffy fur covering them. You have not changed. You are still a bunny. You think the spell must have failed.

    "What the hell!? Why am I a rat!?"

    You turn and see another small bunny, identical to you except covered by black fur. It is looking over its body, inspecting it. A pile of dark, baggy clothes lay in a pile at its long thin feet. A loud squeal distracts you both. You turn to see Brooke unchanged, smiling like a loon and clapping wildly.

    "Oh my God! This is so perfect! Now I have two cute little bunny pets!" says Brooke happily.

    "Hey! I am nobody's pet! I don't know what you did to me, but you better undo it ... RIGHT ... NOW!" says Claire angrily.

    Her rabbit foot repeatedly thumps on the ground in frustration. Claire is unaware of how adorable it looks. And how much danger she is in.

    Brooke ignores Claire's request and walks over to her.

    "Now let's get a good look at my new bunny pet!" says Brooke, as she bends down and picks Claire up by her ears. She easily lifts the small bunny off the ground and holds her at eye level. Claire's feet swing wildly as she kicks nothing but empty air below her.

    "Let me go! Put me down!" screams Claire.

    "Don't be scared, little bunny. I am not going to hurt you. I am going to take good care of you. And we are just going to have the bestest time. You are just too cute as a bunny, " says Brooke, as she pinches Claire's tiny pink nose. "So much cuter than you were as my nasty old roommate."

    Brooke transfers Claire to her arms and gives her a big hug. Then she looks down at you.

    "Oh, don't think I forgot about you, little bunny. You are just as cute and I will still love you just as much as my new little bunny, " says Brooke, as she reaches down and pulls you up into her arms with Claire. " I don't know how you did it but thank you!"

    Brooke gives you both a big hug, squeezing you tight and pushing you close to Claire. Claire glares angrily at you as her furry cheek is pressed against yours.

    "I am so going to kill you for this, " says Claire under her breath.

    "But I didn't mean to! It was an accident, I swear!" you say.

    Brooke grabs you both by the scruff of your necks and holds you up to her face.

    "Now, now, little bunnies. No fighting! You are going to be together for a very long time, so you better learn to get along. I am sure you will be the best of friends in no time! But right now Mommy needs to get some sleep. She has to pay attention in class tomorrow. And since you have both been so rambunctious I think I need to cage you," says Brooke.

    Brooke rifles through her closet and pulls out a large clothes basket. Brooke flips it over and herds the two little bunny girls inside. Then she places many heavy books on top of it. The roofs sags, making the tight space even more cramped.

    "Sorry about that, little bunnies. But I promise to get a better cage for you tomorrow. Goodnight!" says Brooke.

    Claire moves as far away from you as she can, given the small space of your new prison. You still try to appeal to her.

    "Claire, I didn't want this. Any of this. If you just..."

    Claire just pouts and ignores you, looking away from you.

    "Shh!" says Brooke loudly. "I need to get to sleep. No talking! Don't make me get up and punish you!"

    You sigh and quickly shut up. You definitely don't want to get punished. But you do want to talk to Claire. With her help, maybe you can both get out of this mess together. But it looks like you will have to wait to discuss an escape plan with her. For now you decide to just get some rest and slowly drift off to sleep.

    What do you do now?
