    They look at you a little further and the young boy walks off to look at the other human furniture around the room as the parents decide you will make a good footrest for their living room.

    They walk off and pay for you before you are loaded into their boot ready to be taken back to their house.

    The arrive back and get out of the car before the man comes back and picks you up. It’s dark out before you are taken in and placed in front of the sofa before the parents and the kids head off to bed.

    You lay there looking up a the ceiling as the night passes before you hear laughter of the kids coming down the stairs with the mother first thing in the morning. The mother walks over to you and sits down on the sofa above you with the young girl sitting on her lap before bringing her dirty feet down upon your face.

    You look up as they settle on your forehead and mouth and can see the young girls feet swinging on her mother’s lap.

    The mother moves her foot and uses her toes to pull open your mouth before placing her heel in there with her other foot crossed at the ankle.

    The boy walks towards you in the corner of your vision as he raises his small foot and stands in your forehead and hugs his mother digging his small toes into your eyes without a thought for you beneath him.

    The mother starts running her soles along your tongue cleaning the dirty and grime off her feet as she plays with her children with the television playing cartoons in the background.

    The boy steps down off your forehead and goes back to playing as the mother learns forward and looks at you with her feet being cleaned before taking her feet away and putting the young girl on her lap straight onto your face as a seat so she could play and walks towards the kitchen.
