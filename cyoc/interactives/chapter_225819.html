    Such A Common Wish For Teens...

    added by Elflover A month ago O Magic Reality alteration

    That's when there was a knock at Brandon's door. He jumped in surprise. He had been so taken by his own worries and problems that he had become oblivious to all else! He had invited his good friend Ralph over. His mother must have opened the door for him and let him in. Ralph was used to coming to his best pal's house, just as Brandon was at his. They were true friends and each had his own family problems. Not exactly the same but enough to understand each other. They had similar worries and teenage issues.
    So Brandon jumped from his bed and opened the door. And indeed it was Ralph.
    "Hey. Sorry I didn't hear the doorbell."
    "It's okay. No harm done," smiled Ralph.
    They both sat on the floor, discussing topics like school, sports, hobbies and other things. Eventually they'd play a few games and pass the time pleasantly. However, it wasn't so pleasant for Beldazar. The genie was fuming. Oh, how he wanted to grant a wish and be mischievous and naughty about it! This would be so entertaining for him! And teens were the most likely to wish without thinking, hence allowing him to bend the words and provoke grotesque situations! And now there two teenage males! It should have been easy, alas for him it clearly was not as simple as he'd anticipated. Damn these teens in the full swing of puberty who couldn't be as quickly thoughtless as they were supposed to. Luckily for him, Beldazar's own little wish go granted, as the boys started to speak about girls, a prime topic of conversation for young males like them. And indeed they went on about which girls they'd like to date, which they'd like to have sex with and how great life could be with the girls of their dreams.
    "I wish we were iresistible to girls and had sex all the time," said Ralph.
    The genie cursed under his breath. Curses! It wasn't Brandon that had said that! Too bad because it wsas the perfect wish, with lots of options to render it most naughty and fun. Blast these two...
    "Yeah, I wish so too," said Ralph longingly.
    The genie smiled widely. NOW THAT he could work with! He pondered a little how to interpret the wish, found the perfect idea and let the magic escape, engulfing everything into a new reality.
