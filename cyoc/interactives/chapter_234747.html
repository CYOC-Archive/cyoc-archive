    While Allison was thinking of an effect, she glanced over the list again. She was having difficulty picking from all of the listed options. Of all of them, one stood out:

    Diaperfication: The wearer of this diaper is transformed into it. Then, they are transported to another place where another diaper user will wear them. After they’ve been used, they’ll be transformed back to normal and sent back to where they were before transforming.

    “Gross!” Allison thought, “Who would want that?”

    She then realized that she was the one that wanted to wear diapers as a 17 year-old, so she couldn’t really talk. She kept scanning the list before landing on a pick.

    “Secrecy,” Allison thought, “I want to be able to wear these anywhere and anytime, without anyone realizing a thing. Even if I leak, or poop myself, nobody will connect the smell or sight to me.”

    The diaper in her hand flashed, temporarily blinding her. When Allison had blinked away the lights, she saw the diaper had turned a light purple, with a little cartoonish decal of herself on the front. Unbeknownst to her, Allison still had the “Diaperfication” option on her mind when she made her pick. The pack of magic diapers had trouble deciding between it or secrecy, so it picked the thought that occurred first.

    Her hands trembling with excitement, Allison pulled down her pants and taped on the diaper. Instantly, she felt heavy. Her body shriveled up as her clothes fused to her.

    “What’s going on!?” Allison thought as she looked at herself.

    Her skin was becoming light purple, her limbs shrank and flattened. She tried to move off the bed, but whatever was happening caused her to be immobile. Her legs just crinkled when she tried to move them. As she leaned her head forward, it suddenly bent down to her bosom and fused itself. After a couple seconds, Allison was able to see again out of her chest. The girl’s insides morphed into gel, her back turned into cotton, and her shrunken legs did a split to the sides. Suddenly, Allison realized what was happening.

    “Did I…” she thought, horrified, “Pick that diaper transformation thing?”

    With one last bit of the transfiguration, Allison’s body folded in over itself and shrank. The girl was now just a mere diaper. She was almost too shocked to process it. Then her vision went black for a minute. The diaper-girl screamed in terror, but nobody could hear her. She then remembered the description of the option she accidentally picked. She had been sent elsewhere, to someone who also wears diapers. Her vision was returning, but she was still in a dark place. Eventually, Allison could hear footsteps…
