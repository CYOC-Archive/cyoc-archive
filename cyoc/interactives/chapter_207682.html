Beside the machine, she could see a high amount of electricity inside the wall of the basement.     She walked over to said spot, wondering what could cause such high amounts of electricity. She bumped her hand against the tiles a few times and suddenly, a small space opened up. Inside, she saw a high voltage stream of electricity flowing around a box. She knew this couldn't be some kind of normal electricity, it was way to powerful for that. This mechanism was created and charged by her mother to protect that box.

    ...a belt?

    That was it? She hoped there would be more to it, but no, it's just this. Though she must say that it didn't look like any regular belt. It was made of a firm metal which looked like shiny silver. It wasn't bendable for regular humans by any means, so the belt was a bit different than usual ones. The buckle, which was looking more like a big, oval piece of silver, couldn't be adjusted in any way. The only thing it porvided was an opening for the other end of the belt.

    Curious as to what was so special about this belt, Rachel put it on without thinking too much about it. If only she knew that her mother hid it away for a good reason. After the buckle made a pleasant clicking sound, Rachel knew it was locked. She walked over to a mirror and inspected it a little more. It was a good chunk too large for her now small waist, looking more like a belt meant for men. But after a few seconds, the belt seemed to move by itself, shrinking down in size until it perfectly fit snugly against her toned waist.

    "What the..." she mumbled.

As she wanted to take it off again, she tried to press down on the buckle from up front, but instead of opening the lock, it emitted a deep green color for a second where she just pressed the buckle and returned to its original color.

    *Cool, it shows some colors. Mom must've been keeping this thing for the memories. Maybe it was a gift of my dad?* She thought and questioned herself.

    She laughed it off, but soon after the belt send out a shock through Rachel's entire body. Though strangely the shock didn't feel like electricity, but... off somehow. No matter what it was, it sure was a good feeling it provided.

    Rachel believed her mother hid the belt as to not accidentally have it get into the wrong hands. She thought that her mother created the belt in case her powers would someday not be enough to stop a thread or a foe. As much as she believed her own reasoning, she wondered how her mother didn't use it all the time, given how good it felt to wear and how much power it provided in an instant.

    But as all good things in life, this belt wasn't what she believed it to be. Sure, Veronica hid it away from everybody, but not only because it shouldn't get into the wrong hands, but also since it was a device of evil. Veronica didn't know where the belt itself came from, only that she took it from one of her strongest foes. This belt has granted him such immense power to make her superpowered body struggle in a fight. After she took it away from him, it was clear that the evil man wearing it was actually just some regular, corrupted by its power. Veronica quickly caught wind of that and instantly hid it away in her basement as to never see it used ever again. And that would have happened if it wasn't for her own daughters insatiable curiosity.
