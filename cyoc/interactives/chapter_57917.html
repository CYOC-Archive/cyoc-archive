    Was It Just a Dream?

    I didn't look forward to the store closing since listening to the girls talk and seeing
    them browse through the clothes relieved the boredom that I'm sure I would feel once
    the store closed. Alas the time came for the store to close and I was left with my
    thoughts. Maybe I'd be so bored that I would block out large chunks of time. After a
    few minutes the mannequin across from me moved. She was a young girl wearing a
    red jumper but otherwise I thought she was dressed like me. She looked at me and
    said, "Don't worry. Once Mommy gets here you'll move too." She slid down off the
    alphabet block she was sitting on and a few more mannequins came over. There were
    about eleven in all and none of them were adult mannequins.

    The mannequins seemed to be waiting for something and I didn't have long to wait to
    see what it was. The girl who had winked at me came through them and smiled at me
    again. I felt like I should know her but I couldn't think of how I should. She touched my
    cheek and said, "Become animate my daughter."

    I didn't think that would work but then my hand twitched and I blinked. I was so
    startled that I gasped and leaned back. "I'm alive again," I said in wonder.

    "No," the girl said shaking her head, "you're only animate which is different from being
    alive. You're still a mannequin and if I wanted to I could disassemble you."

    I looked down at my arm and saw that there was still a seam there and I knew that the
    girl was telling me the truth. I looked up at the girl and asked, "How were you able to
    do this?"

    "Well I'm a goddess now," the girl said smiling. "I have to apologize for not believing
    you when you told us that the Greek gods were real."

    "What are you talking about?" I asked confused.

    The girl smiled and said, "I'm your mother Rick." This left me speechless so the girl
    continued, "I see I need to explain a few things. I think you should sit down for this.
    Come on over to this alphabet block and sit. Remember to lift your right foot up so the
    rod isn't inside you anymore." Once I had sat down the little girl who had been sitting
    here crawled up into my lap and I naturally put my arms around her which comforted
    me. The girl who claimed to be my mother sighed and said, "It started about three
    months ago. We noticed something was wrong so we went into your room. Well you
    obviously weren't there and we saw the website burned into your computer screen.
    We called the police and filed a missing persons report. A few hours later a rainbow
    appeared in the front room and we were surprised to see an image of a man in the
    rainbow and even more surprised when he spoke to us. He told us what had
    happened and that you were now a girl. He said he would arrange for us to talk to you
    so we could make a decision about whether or not to join you here. He called the next
    day and apologized but he had to send you to look for others who had been drawn
    into this world. He kept calling to update us and during the last update there was a
    bright light. The next thing I knew I was laying in this store as you see me now. I got to
    my feet but stumbled and fell into Chrissy who you're holding now. Well anyway when
    I touched her she started moving. I became frightened and ran but I touched other
    mannequins and they started to move also except they called me Mom. That's when
    Zeus and Poseidon popped in. Well since I had seen and talked to Poseidon before I
    wasn't so frightened. They explained that I was now something like a goddess and that
    I had the power to animate mannequins. I asked them where everyone else was but
    they couldn't give me an answer. They couldn't tell me where you were even. They
    said that there was something blocking them like you were dead. Poseidon was
    adamant that you were alive since your pearl and bracelet hadn't returned to him unlike
    your sisters' items. Then two days later your items did return to Poseidon. He's been
    depressed ever since."

    "Ok I believe that you're my mom but I'm not dead," I said shaking my head.

    "I hate to say this but you are," my mom said. "Your body was transformed into a
    mannequin which isn't alive. The only reason you're able to move now is because of
    my magic."

    "But I've been aware since I was transformed into a mannequin," I said.

    "That's because your soul was trapped within your body," my mom said. "The same
    thing happened with Chrissy."

    "Can we be changed back?" I asked. I didn't relish the thought of spending the rest of
    my life as a mannequin.

    "Not at this time," my mom said shaking her head sadly. "We had tried with Chrissy
    but no one was able to transform her in any way. So I'm afraid that you and Chrissy
    are mannequins for now."

    I looked down at Chrissy and thought that she needed the comfort as much as I did.
    Chrissy looked up at me and said, "The dead are not dead."

    There was a very loud crack and I bolted up into a sitting position my breath fast and
    shallow with my heart beating a mile a minute. I looked around and saw that I was in
    the home again and all the girls had been startled awake. The light in the room was
    dim and after a few seconds I heard the unmistakable sound of rain falling outside. I
    got up and looked out the window seeing that the rain was coming down hard. I
    started thinking back on what I had experienced. Was it just a dream or had it been
    real? My head was saying that it was just a dream but my heart was saying that it was
    real. I looked back at the girls getting out of bed and thought about the words Chrissy
    had spoken. If it was a dream they would make sense. What if it wasn't a dream
    though? Everyone here should be dead but they're not. The only thing I could think of
    was that maybe I had come back in time if it wasn't a dream. I looked back out the
    window and watched the rain stop. Given what had happened to me so far it wasn't an
    impossibility that I could have jumped through time. I then thought about my other
    dreams and wondered if they had been episodes of time jumping and if anyone else
    was experiencing them.

    It was after lunch when a package came for Gabriella. We found out that the girls
    always got a present for their birthday and today was Gabriella's fifteenth birthday. No
    one knew who sent them but whoever did send them was a good Samaritan and the
    girls made sure to thank him or her in the paper. We went up to the bedroom so
    Gabriella could open her present. We were just as excited as Gabriella as she opened
    her present. What was inside was a wooden box and I started to get the feeling that I
    should know it. Gabriella opened the lid and read aloud, "These vials contain potions
    to transform you into any animal or change your sex and age. Each vial has a picture
    of an animal, human, or the symbols for male and female on it with the age vials
    having the age printed on them. Each vial must be fully drunk for the full effects. There
    is a reversal vial that will reverse any and all transformations the subject has
    undergone. When you have drank the vial just replace it in the space it came from and
    it will automatically refill. Both humans and animals can be transformed with these
    vials. Enjoy." She looked up with a smile and said, "Yah right. Like I really believe
    these will transform me."

    "Those really will transform you Gabriella trust me on this," I said hoping that she
    wouldn't drink any.

    "Oh come on Lisa," Gabriella said rolling her eyes. "You can't possibly believe that I'll
    be transformed and I'll prove it." She grabbed a random vile and drank it. The vial
    wasn't very big and it only a couple gulps to drain it. She replaced the vial and said, "I
    didn't transform."

    "Uh Gabriella the vile did refill," Denise said sounding scared.

    Gabriella looked at the box and we all watched as she started to change.
