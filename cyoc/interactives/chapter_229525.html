    Using The Chronivac On Tourists

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 19 days ago TG

    Ricky squared his shoulders, determination flickering in his eyes. "Fine, you're on!" he declared, his competitive spirit rising to the challenge. Max grinned, pleased to see his brother's enthusiasm. "Great! Let's make this interesting," he replied, his mind already racing with ideas.

    The two brothers quickly hatched a plan, eager to put their newfound wager into action. With mischievous grins, they set off to find their next unsuspecting target.

    As they wandered through the bustling hotel lobby, Ricky couldn't help but feel a surge of excitement. Despite his initial reluctance, the prospect of staying transformed into a girl intrigued him. It would be a bizarre and amusing experience, to say the least.

    Their eyes fell upon a group of tourists chatting animatedly near the reception desk. Max nudged Ricky with a sly grin, silently indicating their chosen victims. With a shared glance, the brothers wordlessly agreed on their target.

    As they approached the unsuspecting group, Ricky's heart pounded with anticipation. This was it – their chance to test their skills with the Chronivac. With a quick exchange of glances, Max and Ricky simultaneously activated the device, casting their transformation on the unsuspecting tourists.

    The effect was instantaneous. The tourists blinked in confusion as their bodies morphed and shifted before their eyes. Shocked gasps filled the air as they realized what had happened. Max and Ricky watched with barely contained excitement, eagerly awaiting the outcome of their prank.
