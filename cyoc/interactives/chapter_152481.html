She now floated underwater, breathing normally thanks to gills which now graced the side of her neck. So far she had been lucky on the outside, her only changes over the course of three days had been her gills coming in allowing her to breath/speak underwater, her hair changing density so it didn’t float but instead rested like it would in air, her hair changing color from blondish brown to a neon pink color and her eyes changed to a green color. The only other change had been her hands becoming webbed. Aside from her hair and eye color she still looked like herself, that couldn’t be said about her twin brother John.

    John wasn’t taking the change well, none of her brothers or her father were but she couldn’t blame them. They weren’t just becoming monsters they where switching genders, John kept adjusting his new Bra and his breasts where bigger then hers and growing. His hair was still brown, but it had grown and with each passing hour he became more of a women. Her twin younger brothers Noah and Lucas where also changing genders however both of them had lost their hair and their gills where on their sides making wearing a bra harder. So maybe in an attempt to hold off the change, both of them where refusing to wear the sea weed/sea shell bras the others where wearing.

She didn’t want to face that her boys where now well on the path to becoming mermaids. She herself had lost hair and was approaching being bald and her skin had taken on more of rough feel.     “Yes.” Confirmed her “father” or would she now be her mother.

    “Don’t worry we will get through this together.” Confirmed Mike as she hugged her daughter wondering what the next few days would bring.
