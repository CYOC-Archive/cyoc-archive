    The growth of the world population along with some heavy disease in the diary sector led to
    a severe shortage of milk over the world. These problems were still a secret, cause politicians
    wanted to prevent riots and protests.

    Fortunately scientist came up with a solution and so the government established this secret
    organization to produce milk from human females. The subjects were mostly loners,
    runaways and other people who could disappear easily. The scientist gave them a special
    medicament which transformed them into young girls, so they were more fertile and live
    longer. Also the used hormones to increase the milk production of the victims.

    Now that you were in your kennel, hooked up to the milking machine, you saw more of your
    surrounding and the processes in this facility.

    Along the floor were more rows of kennels with girls in similar positions. Most of them were
    pregnant, a few not. In one kennel you saw a young boy humping one of the girls.

    The most of your impressions were still blurred. But suddenly you heard a familiar voice in
    front of you.

    "I see you already adjusted to your new life."

    You looked up and saw Lisa standing in front of you, wearing a blue business suit.

    "As you can see your our newest addition to our little farm. At first: Don't take it personally.
    We just ran a bit short of females in the past and your would make a fine diary cow, so I
    chose and lured you to become one.", said Lisa while she walked around you.

    "Remember that drink I made you yesterday evening? That was the serum which transformed
    you. But let's forget the past. Let's talk about the future. You see, the world ran kinda short
    of milk and we find a new way to produce it. Most time we didn't even need to kidnap people
    off the streets. Well, to produce such an amount of milk on a daily base, our livestock must
    be almost constantly pregnant, so we kept some males to impregnate you and the other
    girls. In a few months you will give birth and be impregnated again. oh, we also use a serum
    so that 99% of the births were female. You can see we got a great herd over the time. This
    isn't all.", she said and pointed at the rows of kennels with hooked up girls.

    "There were more than this hall. Even more than this one building. Oh and nevertheless this
    is all secret. Officially these facility doesn't even exist, but still the government support our
    work. You can see that it would led to some severe discussions about human rights if this
    would be public." Lisa laughed at this last words.

    During the whole monologue you wasn't able to answer. The pleasure of the milking which
    surged through your breasts and through your body let you not phrase one word, but only
    moans escaped your lips.

    Lisa watched you a few minutes more, while you were still lost in pleasure . Then another
    woman entered the corridor and walked up to Lisa.

    "Excuse me, Ms. Director. We need your expertise in the controlling room.", she said.

    "Well, my dear. You see the work called. It is hard to be the director of this whole program."

    With these words Lisa turned around and followed the other woman out of the room.
