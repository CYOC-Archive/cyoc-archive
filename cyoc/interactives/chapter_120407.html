    The person in front of your eyes was standing in front of you, as if they were expecting this. You look left and right, and she's the first thing to grab your attention, besides the forest around you. The cloaked figure then pulled down her brown hood to reveal a young woman, likely in her teenage years- About the same age as you, to be exact.

    She was human, but just not... Normal. Her long hair was silky white, and her eyes were unnaturally ice-blue. She looked at you, with a serious expression on her face. You certainly couldn't believe this, but the girl certainly did.

    You nervously ask who the girl is, and why you're right here. Of course, you were a little frozen in fear. You couldn't even muster up the courage to simply stand up. The girl doesn't answer right away, as she stares at you even more intently. In fact... She doesn't answer at all.

    In the air in front of her, a light-green symbol appeared, in a flurry of emerald particles. This girl... It looks like you were right in assuming that she wasn't a normal person. She was doing some weird special-effects crap! She must be an actress! ...No, not likely. Why would a movie studio knock out some random person just to film a scene? Besides, special effects are computer-generated, not created in front of someone's eyes.

    The symbol wasn't of any language you knew about. It didn't look like a Greek letter or a Chinese Character you knew about. ...From what you could tell, it was likely not of any language used around the world.

    Mesmerized by the symbol, you nearly forgot that you're in front of a strange woman doing something to you. Panic settles in again, as you realize that you can't move any of your limbs. You shout at the girl, demanding an answer out of her. But the girl doesn't respond in an normal manner. She just silently keeps on, ignoring your plight.

    The symbol in the air flies onto your chest, alongside the particles, which fade into your body like there was nothing there. But there's a searing pain on your chest- formed by the symbol that embedded itself into your skin. The pain burns everything, until your vision fades to white.

    For a while, you thought you were dead. But it was then you came to. You were in a grandiose room, locked in some sort of cage. It wasn't that small. It was quite spacious, actually. ...It was then you saw a large mirror taking up the majority of a wall. It was then you saw that your body had changed into...

    What do you do now?
