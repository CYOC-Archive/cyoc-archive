My lazy self got me to start writing things that I don't really want, but always desired. I started to make myself more like a super human. My agility and strength were increased. My intelligence was now like 2 Stephen Hawkings combined and I have the eyesight of an eagle. I let myself transform and boy was it amazing. All kinds of thoughts were coming in my head. Then an even greater idea came to mind. I wrote "Jon was the richest man in the world" and my room started to transform. It expanded to the size of a whole apartment complex. I found the door and went outside. My house was extremely huge with multiple floors.
