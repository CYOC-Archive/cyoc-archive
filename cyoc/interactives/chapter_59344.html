    Mistress Yu is Your Mom's Friend

    You're at a girls clothing store when the pain at the base of your spine returns. For a
    moment you're not in the store anymore and you're hearing worried voices. "Her body
    is still resisting the treatment," the voice of Matron Nikl said. "Her mind's in danger of
    being lost."

    "I want you to be careful Jill," the voice of Mistress Yu said. "If her mind slips too far I
    want you to come back. I don't want to lose you."

    "I'll be careful Mother," a girl's voice said. You recognize it as the girl who goes around
    waking everyone up in the mornings. She didn't look like Mistress Yu so she must be
    adopted. "I'll do my best to bring her back," Jill continued. You feel a hand in yours and
    a hand on your forehead.

    "Saphira what's wrong?" your mom asked her voice full of worry.

    You're back in the store and looking into the concerned face of your mom. "I had a
    hurt," you said reaching behind you and rubbing your tail.

    "Come into the fitting room so I can take a look," your mom said and almost dragged
    you to the room. Once inside she had you hold up your dress and pulled down your
    panties exposing your tail. She carefully ran her fingers over your tail and then pulled
    something out of it. "You had a nail clipping sticking in your tail," she explained pulling
    your panties back up. "Now let's finish our shopping."

    About a couple hours later you were standing in front of a door as your mom rang the
    doorbell. It wasn't a house you were at but a big building, one that you felt you should
    know. The door opened and your mom said, "Hi Yu."

    "Hello Claire," Yu said smiling. "Come on in."

    You followed your mom, sister, and Yu into a nice living room. A girl came in and Yu
    said, "Jill I'd like you to meet my friend Claire and her..." she got a confused look on
    her face before finishing with, "daughters?"

    "This is Jackie and Saphira," your mom said with a smile. "Saphira decided that she
    wanted to be a girl and she does make a cute one."

    "Yes she does," Yu said nodding.

    "Saphira why don't you go with Jill while your sister and I talk with Yu," your mom said
    in a tone that made it clear it wasn't a question or request.

    Jill led you to her room which just about screamed girl. You just had time to take in the
    pink walls, bedding, the vanity, the dresser, and the dolls on the bed before Jill said, "I
    need you to listen to me Saphira and listen carefully. You've accidentally been given a
    double dose of the treatment. Your body is resisting it which is why you're here in the
    past. It's also causing some mutations in your body the most notable of which is the
    formation of a vestigial tail. It may continue to grow though if the mutations continue."

    "So my tail may get bigger?" you asked confused. "What's the treatment?"

    Jill frowned and looked conflicted. After a moment she said, "I'm not supposed to tell
    anyone this but seeing as this is a special case I think Mother will make an exception.
    The treatment is an injection designed to make your body and mind more like a girl's.
    It usually works slowly and most of the boys that come to the school only need one
    treatment in order to become girls. The treatment jumps you forward in time a bit to a
    point where it would finish transforming you. That point moves closer each time the
    treatment is administered. However there has to be a week in between each
    administration or there can be side effects. The side effects include the body resisting
    it and the person being thrown back in time and of course mutations."

    "What?" you asked not understanding what she just said.

    Jill sighed and said, "The treatment makes you a girl when it's done right."

    "I want to be a girl," you said bouncing up and down.

    Jill smiled and said, "My mother can make you a girl if you decide to come back with
    me. If you decide to stay here then we won't be able to make you a girl."

    "What about Mommy and Jackie?" you asked not wanting to leave them.

    "You won't be leaving them," Jill said with a smile. "If you come back with me you'll still
    be living this life. Besides I can get your note and you can rip it up if you want."

    At the mention of the note a little flame lights in your mind and you ask, "How do you
    know about that?"

    "I'm in charge of putting a boy's personal things in storage when they come in," Jill
    said with a smile, "and I saw the note. I knew what it was since it had come to me one
    day. I was a 37 year old guy named Rick and I made a wish similar to yours. I made
    sure that my wish included being transformed into a girl though. But anyway will you
    come back with me?"

    What do you do now?
