"Mom, do you have a ring too?" Chad asked while he took dinner with June. "Mine is not a ring. Look." She pointed at her ears. "The spots in my earrings work like the jewels in your ring."

Chad was smiling at the idea of becoming a superhero too, but his mother had already told him he should kept his magic powers a secret as long as possible.

    "Mom, will you help me to train my powers tomorrow?" Chad asked her after he brushed his teeth. "Of course, honey. I will show a few practical tricks that could help you in your daily life." June replied as she watching him going to his bedroom. "And do you think I will learn to use my unique power?" She didn't answer immediately, she just smiled before she left. "Maybe. He knew he won't be able to sleep until he finds out what is his unique power. So, he sneaked out of his room and went to the backyard.
