    “Don’t forget the fries and soda,” Frieda looking at her phone.
    “Three medium fries and three medium colas,” Benton speaking into the drive through speaker.

    Minutes later he pulls out of the drive through lane and heads to the hospital. Benton tries to eat a few fries only to have Frieda slap his hand away from the bag.
    “Wait until we visit mom,” Frieda demands.
    Benton snorts at being denied a small snack; he glances at Frieda as she re-secures the bags, Benton speaks up.
    “Have you informed Eric and Spencer regarding what to expect tomorrow evening?”
    I informed them both,” Frieda answers; “I also told to bring their overnight bags with them to school and drop them off in the town car.”

Remembering the notes Frieda took regarding the grievances the staff had; Benton knew that was a task he was not looking forward to.
