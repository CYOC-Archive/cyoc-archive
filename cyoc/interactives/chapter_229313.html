    You woke up after sleeping for several hours, you check the time on your smartphone and it's already night.

    You feel your stomach growling and you want to get some food on the fridge, as you try to stand up, you immediately fell back to your chair as you struggled to stand up.. Something feels off.. No.. Something is wrong!

    You can't feel your legs! You panicked and tried to stand up even harder, however you just wobbled and struggled to the point that you toppled down to the floor with a loud thud..

    You look down at your legs, or what used to be your legs.. it is now a long blue fish tail which is 6.5 foot long, you scream in horror as you realize what happened to you.. you have been cursed into a merman..

    You look around and saw your ripped pants and underwear on the floor, even your socks are also torn, you then looked down at your tail, it's so long and also heavy, you touch it and you can feel every part of it, from the scales to the fins, you even tried to pinch on your tail fins to see if this was all a dream, but it wasn't since you felt pain as you punched it..

    You then removed your shirt to see how the tail is attached to you, and to your surprise, the scales and skin seem to blend together, very perfectly that it doesn't look like a costume at all, because unlike this fish tail you have, the costumes obviously can be distinguished because it doesn't blend to the skin perfectly..

    You don't know what to do, and for some reason, you don't feel dry at all, and you can still breathe air, you begin to ask yourself on how is it possible..

    Now.. you try to think about what you should do..
