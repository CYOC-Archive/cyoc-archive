    Ty’s Power- Caught on Video, Broadcast on CNN

    Dustin, Ty and Peter were surely glad to be back in their hotel room after the episode in the pool hall. They were dead tired, Dustin and Ty watched TV, while Peter fell asleep on the small, singles bed. He was wiped out, since he battled the three frat boys and freed Ty and Dustin at the same time.

    Dustin and Ty were watching French TV but could not understand it, so they flipped through the channels until they came across a news program that was in English. It was CNN. They didn’t want to watch the news so they went through all other channels, but CNN was still the only one that was broadcasting in English.

    They watched the CNN news broadcast for a few minutes, until they showed the picture of the front entrance of a building that looked very familiar to Ty and Dustin.

    “Hey Dustin,” Ty began, “Isn’t that the pool hall where we just came from? It’s on TV!”

    Dustin looked at it.

    “Turn up the volume for a sec, Ty!”

    Ty had the remote, and turned up the volume on the TV.

    “A strange occurrence was captured on video today, thanks to security cameras that are secretly mounted in this pool hall. Several teens were playing pool, when a discussion broke out, ending with one of the teens going to the bathroom. The following events were caught on video tape at the pool hall.” A reporter spoke while standing in front of the pool hall.

    The image on the TV showed what Peter had done to the three frat boys. One became a baby, the other a hamster, and the other disappeared in thin air, just to end up frozen and shrunk, inside of an ice cub which was magically transported inside the freezer behind the bar.

    “Oh NO! They broadcast what Peter did on CNN!” Ty shouted!

    “Great! You better wake up Peter and tell him. He’s famous now!” Dustin said.

    “Not a chance, he HATES publicity and can’t stand it when people know about his powers! Oh great…” Ty wondered out loud.

    Dustin quietly walked up to the bed. Peter lay there still, flat on his back and sound asleep. Dustin reached out with his hand and tried to touch the athletic, Canadian teenager on his arm, but a spike of power came from Peter’s skin and shocked Dustin, making him jump backwards!

    “Holy cow! I can’t touch him! You wake him up, Ty. After all, you guys are related!” Dustin said.

    “Hmm oh yes, we’re doing it wrong. His powers take over and protect his body when he is asleep! I need to wake him up mentally!” Ty said.

    Ty closed his eyes and concentrated, and Peter slowly turned over on his side. He groaned and stretched out his arms, let out a yawn, and opened his eyes. Then he sat up on the bed as he was slowly waking up.

    “Hey Peter,” Ty said, “Come watch this thing on the news… It’s about you!”

    “Huh?” Peter said.

    “You’re world famous now!” Ty said out loud.

    The reporter continued. “The personnel at the pool room reported that a teenager, likely an American, turned a college student into a baby, another in a hamster, and he made a third disappear. The bartender then saw this boy open the freezer door and levitate a block of ice, with a miniature figure of the third college student in it. He had never seen anyone display such incredible power in such a way before. The first college student actually tried to fight the boy, but he single-handedly took him out by hurling him against the bathroom door. Three American college students had confirmed that this boy possessed some sort of incredible power, and transformed them with incredible ease. All we know now about this mysterious teen is that his name is Peter.”

    Peter was astonished. He gasped in horror, as he watched a replay of him using his powers, broadcast around the World on CNN.

    “No way! They video taped me!” Peter exclaimed.

    “What are you gonna do?” Dustin asked him.

    “Just ignore it, I guess. Eventually people will forget about this. Just right now, I guess I might be recognized but I’ll just have to try to ignore it! What else can I do?” Peter wondered.

    “Well, let’s go to the restaurant downstairs in the lobby and get something to eat!” Dustin suggested.

    The boys walked out of the room, and took the elevator down. As soon as they walked into the lobby, they noticed something weird.

    People were staring at them, and some were even pointing at Peter. Wherever they went, people would stop talking and stare at him, often in awe or with their mouths open.

    When they walked into the restaurant, Peter suddenly got the feeling that every single person was looking at him. He fought hard to ignore the attention, but couldn’t help feeling uncomfortable.

    Once they had their dinner and walked out, Peter, Ty and Dustin were met by several reporters with microphones and cameras! Everyone was taking pictures of Peter! All he could do was smile and keep on going. Peter had hoped that this was just a fad and would eventually fade away. Thousands of people would have seen him on CNN, but he thought that it would soon pass over and he would eventually be forgotten. He disliked the publicity and didn’t want to be a celebrity, and fought hard to be as down-to-earth and as normal as possible.
