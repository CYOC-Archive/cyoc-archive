    He looked into the freezer and found a couple of pizza pockets that were still good. He tossed them into the microwave and set the timer. As the microwave hummed, he couldn't help but wonder what Jill's date would be like. Would it be another fusion? Would it be someone he knew? Would they understand Jill?

    While the pizza pockets spun in the microwave, Sean could hear the sound of drawers opening and closing in the bedroom upstairs. He knew that Jill was searching for the perfect outfit for their date. The thought of his parent's new life was still a bit strange, but he was happy that they had found someone that accepted them for who they were.

    The microwave beeped and Sean grabbed the hot food, juggling it over to the counter to avoid burning his fingers. He took a moment to watch the steam billow out of the small plastic packets before tearing into one. The warm cheese and sauce filled his mouth with a comforting taste. He had almost forgotten about the date until the sound of the shower turned on. The three-headed creature had to navigate the bathroom with a bit more coordination than usual, but they managed well enough.

    As he ate, Sean heard the rustling of fabric and the occasional clinking of jewelry as Jill tried on various outfits. He knew that each head had different fashion preferences, but he was surprised by the variety of sounds. It was like listening to three different people getting ready for a night out. One head was going for a more casual look, tossing jeans and a t-shirt onto the bed, while another was pushing for something more professional, laying out a sleek dress shirt and tie. The third, Jack's, was advocating for something flashier, with a few glitzy accessories.

    Finally, after what felt like an eternity of indecision, Jill's body descended the stairs. Sean couldn't help but stare. They had chosen an ensemble that somehow combined the styles of all three personalities. The outfit was a tailored suit jacket, left open to reveal a snug, low-cut top that emphasized their new F-cup breasts. The pants were skin-tight, showing off the toned legs they'd acquired from the fusion. The look was completed with a pair of stylish sneakers that added a touch of youthful flair. The three heads had agreed on a minimalist makeup look, with just enough to highlight their best features without being too overpowering.

    "What do you think?" they asked, spinning around to show off their outfit.

    Sean's eyes widened as he took in the sight of Jill's new look. "Wow, you guys... I mean, you, look amazing."

    Jill's heads each gave a little nod of thanks, and the body spun around to show off the full ensemble. The suit jacket flared out a bit more dramatically than typical women's fashion, but it was clear that Jack's influence had been at play in the selection. The tight pants hugged the powerful legs, a clear nod to Lee's former physique.

    Sean couldn't help but feel a twinge of jealousy, watching the confidence that exuded from the three-headed fusion. It wasn't that he didn't support Jill's new identity, but the stark change was a stark reminder of how much his world had shifted in such a short time. He took a deep breath and pushed the feeling aside, focusing instead on the excitement of seeing his new family member embracing their new life.

    "So, what's the plan for the date?" he asked, trying to keep the conversation light as he finished his snack.

    "We're going out to dinner, then a walk along the beach," Jill replied, their collective gaze meeting his. "It's supposed to be beautiful out tonight."

    The doorbell rang, and all three heads snapped to attention. "That must be them!" they exclaimed, their excitement palpable. Sean felt his heart flutter, a mix of nerves and curiosity for what the evening had in store for Jill. He followed them to the front door, watching as they checked their reflection in the hallway mirror one last time. The sight of the three heads peering into the glass, making sure their hair and makeup were just right, was both amusing and heartwarming.
