    They seemed to recognize him as having always been a lion cub... but then that was part

    wants to be free... yet I'm restraining her. Doesn't that make me wrong? For holding a
    child back? Not letting her be free? Is it wrong for me to love Opal? I barely know him... yet

    "You think its wrong for you to love?" Sarah asked.

    "No... it's that everything happened so fast so out of control, I don't know what to think
    anymore," Caroline sighed, "and a part of me wishes we can free the world... I mean look

    "Kurse wouldn't have made those situations any better," Sarah warned, "she might have
    even been willing to make the fighting worse for her own amusement."

    "We know," Jasper said in a frustrated voice, "we all know... It's just so hard to sort things

    "What about you?" Sarah asked the second biggest she wolf after the Japanese wolves,
    although Fran had said something to a wounded Chiyoye that Julie was of the opinion that

    "It's all so sad," Amethyst whimpered after a moment, "Kurse made me a bubble head from
    the very beginning... probably to match Caroline's transform for me to become this...
    busty... and well to be honest... I WANT to go back to that."

    "What?" Sarah gasped.

    "I was always happy, nothing ever got me down, and I still functioned well within the
    Pack," Amethyst spoke, "Now... after seeing what Kurse did to us... made us do to the
    people on that yacht... made us fight with our pack mates... and then being freed... I'm so
    sad... guilty... ashamed. And it hurts."

    "But you got your smarts back," Sarah pointed out.

    "What good is being smart when your miserable," Amethyst finished.

    "I want you all to talk with me about these things," Sarah said firmly, "You need help... I
    can see that. And I want to help."

    "Why?" Tourmaline asked, "we barely know each other."

    "But we're pack now," Sarah answered, "the past doesn't matter. I'm sure Jensen wants us
    to be happy and to function well with the pack and in society. And if you're miserable... by

    "You aren't a psychiatrist," Lawrence commented, "HOW can you help us with all of this? I
    got a little boy's voice in my head that only wants to run around, chase cars, bark at
    squirrels and howl at the moon... and he's conflicting with the rest of me. He isn't in
    control, but that doesn't mean it's not difficult."

    "I can help you by being a good pack mate," Sarah answered, "I helped all of us get used
    to being wolves... I helped Ulysses get used to being too young to take a mate... I'm sure I

    "Cobalt thinks we could turn this island into a resort," Caroline commented, "a place where
    people could live a little more freely..."

    "Who would come here though?" Sarah answered, "the pack and the pride are horrified by

    "But Kurse is dead," Lazuli spoke, "she can't threaten anyone ever again."

    "Doesn't mean that the fear and consequences of her actions are gone," Sarah warned,
    "just because one evil is defeated doesn't mean that the consequences aren't there."

    They all slowly sighed, unable to really counter Sarah's logic.
