    When the light faded Mia was staring blankly up at MaKenna. She was now five years old. Her long dark hair was now up in pigtails. Her shirt was gone, only the diaper remaining. It had changed too, now that she was small enough a proper Pampers diaper with Elmo was dawning her rear. She was still sucking her thumb as a smile appeared behind her hand.

    "MaKenna!!! What are you doing wif dat silly gun!" She giggled, legs slightly wiggling from the excitment.

    MaKenna blinked. Guess the gun was a dud, nothing happened. No noise even. Bit of a let down to be honest. She set it down on the arm of the couch, paying no mind to set it down carefully with it being a pointless toy. She stepped behind Mia and took her place back on the couch behind the child in her care.

    "Nothing sweetie. I thought it might be a fun toy but just turned out to be a dud!" She leaned forward and tickled Mia. Mia in turn giggles and squeeled.

    MaKenna turned back to her original goal for going in the kitchen, the sippy cup for Mia. She leaned over and grabbed one of the cups of cocoa. Being as careful as possible, she poured the steamy chocolatey liquid into the sippy cup. Making sure it wasn't too hot, she leaned forward and pulled Mia up on her lap. She was such a small and petite girl she may as well be craddling a baby! She held the sippy cup in front of Mia's mouth, who happily accepted it with both hands and began drinking.

    The show continued on for a while, a scare here or there but surprisingly when MaKenna would casually slide her finger in the leg lining of Mia's diaper, there was no soggyness. She was handling it like a champ.

    "Wow Mia! I am surprised! You normally aren't this brave with scary shows"

    "I learned from you! You are da bravestest girl I ever knew! And.. You..." Mia paused. Suddenly a look of brief strain and concentration crossed her face.

    MaKenna giggled and sniffed. Yup, that was a guarrantee, Mia had pooped her diaper. She stood up, craddling the girl.

    "Kiddo made a poopy didn't she?! Yes she did?!" MaKenna teased, tickling her care once more.

    Mia giggled in MaKenna's arm, and noticed they were headed to the nursery. "I can't keep watching while you change me?"

    "Sorry poofy butt, I didn't bring your overnight diapers out to the living room. And based on how stinky that diaper smells and the cocoa you just drank I feel we are gonna need them."

    Mia blushed, but she kinda liked the care that MaKenna gave her. She was gently set down on the soft padding of her changing table. MaKenna was an expert diaper changer, which made sense for how often she changed Mia. She was wiped clean and given fresh baby powder, before the big boys were wipped out. Large overnight Pampers. They had similar designs to the ones she just messed, however they had easily twice the padding and usually were coupled with plastic pants. Sure enough, once MaKenna had fastened them around Mia's waist, pink plastic pants were slide up her legs and the elastics snapped around her waist and thighs. It felt much better to have a clean snug diaper on.

    "Thank you MaKenna!" Mia exclaimed.

    "Oh don't thank me yet... Cuz I hear there is a tickle monster loose...." MaKenna picked up Mia as she gulped. MaKenna set Mia down on the ground, letting her stand. "You better run!"

    Mia didn't need to be told twice. The small child with a very large padded rear started waddling like crazy, crinkling as she ran into the living room, MaKenna close behind laughing like a maniac with wiggling fingers. She was chased around before being pinned to the couch, MaKenna about to do her worst. That's when....
