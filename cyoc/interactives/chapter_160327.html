    Alice noticed that her measurements had slide bats next to them, who were currently set at 100%.

    Moving her height slider up to 125%, she saw the 3D model on her computer stretch up. It looked too comically thin though, so she found herself raising the weight slider up a bit to 110%. It still looked very skinny, but not to such a degree.

    Bellow the sliders, Alice noticed a fill up box “Alter reality accordingly”. Reading the description, it said “If marked true, reality would alter to suite the changes and everyone, with the exception of the user, will think the changed reality is how it normally is. It would likely include changes to clothing and furniture, and would probably include physical changes to other people, the amount of which can be altered bellow.” Alice marked the box as true.
    Under that box, there was a slider called “The amount of physical effects of the change on others” which had a slider between 0% and 100%. With some confusion, Alice placed it at 0%.

    After all of those, there was tab called “Initiate changes”. Alice clicked on the tab, and afterwards, she felt an amazing warm feeling all over her body, as her view became higher and higher. She was getting taller!
    It was an enjoyable situation for her, as her body rose higher and thinned down.
    She noticed how her clothes changed to fit her, becoming longer and skinnier, and understood that it was reality changing to suite her new body.

    The whole change took a few minutes, and at the end, according to the display, Alice’s height was 6’4.25” and her weight was 138.6lbs.
    “Holy shit, I’m huge!” she screamed, while standing up from her chair.
    She lost her balance trying to stand on her longer legs, and after stabilizing herself, she saw how everything looked shorter, though she realized how her chairs and the computer table were higher and how her bed was much longer, presumably to suite her new height.

    After that, Alice looked at her mirror, which became a full length one, and was greeted by a body like the 3D model in the computer. Very tall and very skinny, though not to the point that it looks like her body was severely underweight.

    After that, Alice heard a knock on her door.
    Walking towards for door, she covered that small distance faster than she was used to, even though she wasn’t used for her long limbs.
    Opening a door, she was greeted by her 6’ father, but instead of looking up at him, she found herself having to bend down to meet his eyes. It shocked her for a moment.
    “Alice, are you alright?” her father asked. “You look shocked”.
    “Yeah dad, I’m fine” she lied. Her height was completely normal for him!
    “Alright then, I thought I heard a scream from upstairs for some reason...” he replied and left.

    Going back to her computer, she decided to see what else that amazing app can do.
