So far her efforts in fooling the producers had succeeded but she wondered just how long she could pull it off.

Jennifer even began sleeping with a night light on in her room as her childish fear of the dark slowly began to return.

The doctors had mentioned some side effects of the procedure and she hoped it was only temporary. Still she immediately made an appointment to see someone about this. For now though Jennifer was achieving her dreams, as she now climbed into bed, she closed her eyes anxiously awaiting tomorrow.
