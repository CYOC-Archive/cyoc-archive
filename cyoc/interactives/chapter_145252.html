    Of course, with a class mainly consisting of boys, it was already rowdy and loud as the teacher had stepped out to grab some extra worksheets to pass out. Fortunately, Kathryn had ran into him on the way and, with a simple flick of magic, convinced him to take a sick day off and go home to rest. The class had finally taken notice of her and her new pet boys, with everyone in a curious uproar about who she was, where their teacher was, and why she was allowed to bring in pets.

    'Ha! Worked like a charm! I mean, it is a charm too~' she thought to herself, mentally huffing in confidence in her magical abilities. "Your teacher had to step out for a while on account of suddenly coming down with a cold," the witch explained, her smile widening at the sight of several students now taking interest in her pets as they groomed each other. "Instead, I'll be substituting for him at the moment. So as in introduction, why don't you say hello to my kitties? I see some of you are already interested in them~" she continues, now drawing the attention to Tim and Kevin.

    Now with all eyes on the two, the entire class was now ensnared by the visual appearance on the two, allowing her to silently work the same magic she used on them to the rest of their classmates. 'Soon to be playmates in a few minutes!'

    The students at first were just enamored by their cute appearance, then started petting them while falling under the same trance as Tim did when he first saw his friend, and finally mimicking their behavior as furring ears began travelling up heads, whiskers sprouting from cheeks and eyebrows, and tails ripping free from their confining pants and undergarments.

    It was an entertaining sight to behold as an entire class of middle school students were now writhing playfully around as adorable wide-eyed cat boys. It was until Kathryn saw a flash of a skirt when one of them as they rolled around with another did she realize something. While the class majority was boys, there were still a few girls attending as well, and her spell was derived from her two first cat boys... meaning that it would specifically change those affected into cat boys as well, gender and all.

    "Whoops! I guess if it wasn't before, now I have myself a litter of cat boys," she says, a bit surprised by her slip-up, but quickly thought nothing of it as it wasn't a huge problem. "Besides, you girls are still cute as ever, even with a different kind of package now!" she tells one, the former human girl not paying attention to her confession as she pets 'his' cat-eared head.

    "I suppose it's about time we head back home while everyone else is in class! What do you think, my kitties~?" she asks the former class. All of them look up with wide cat eyes, their tails waving in anticipation as they give a resounding meow in agreement. She smiles happily, taking a few minutes to conjure up the same collars as Tim and Kevin's, casting the same spell to keep up the illusion. Though, illusion or not, it would have been quite a strange sight to see a line of cats obediently following their new owner to her house.

    One would think that her modest home couldn't possibly be enough space for a classroom sized group of cat boys, but space would never be an issue for a witch, as she had cast a spell on the inside of her house to make the interior larger on the inside than what is seen from the outside, essentially turning her small house's space into that of a mansion's area.
