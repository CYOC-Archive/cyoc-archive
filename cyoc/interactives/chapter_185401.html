    "Oh you wanna play that Lara? Alright we'll play!"

    Nora, blinded by rage and jealousy decided to rejuvenate herself, thought she would become even more beautiful. So she squeezed the rejuvenation button, losing 1 year per minute.

    Only this time the changes were different, instead of becoming more beautiful and becoming less imposing, her forms began to retract in her late twenties. As Nora turned 21, she decided to calm her anger and hit the stop button. Unfortunately she hadn't become any prettier than Lara but just smaller, with a half-adult and adolescent face, barely formed forms and oversized clothes. Her jumpsuit was now very loose, she could still pass for one in her size but it would fall into the category "very oversize".

    "oh no I really look like a teenager now"
    Nora began to regret getting carried away so easily, it reminded her how much she has regressed mentally.
    Despite everything, she still decided to try the dance floor where Lara was on fire.
    But when Nora arrived most of the people laughed at her outfit. Her makeup didn't match her age now and with her outfit we thought of a teenage girl who thought she was 10 years older. She did not even approach Lara, she knew she would not give a damn about her and had no solution but to go home.

    While Lara was now living her best years again, Nora felt much less powerful than before compared to the world around her. Well, she wasn't at the worst age possible either, but that wasn't what she wanted at all when she bought the stopwatch.

    On the way back, she had difficulty walking in shoes that were a bit too big and had to tighten her belt as much as possible so as not to see her jumpsuit sliding down her body.
    Back home, she was too tired from that day and the alcohol had too much of an effect on her to think about the next day, she wallowed on her bed still dressed and slept until noon.

    The next day she woke up with a hangover she hadn't had for a long time, took off her jumpsuit she slept in and found herself with panties that were two fingers away from sliding down her legs.
    Her bra had now also grown too big and she decided to just wear a sweater she had bought yesterday but which was now too large too, she thought back to the $ 500 she had spent and all the clothes she can no longer wear because they are too large or over her age.

    She now had to plan her day and wondered what she's going to be able to do.
