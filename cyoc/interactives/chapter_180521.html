    Messy Marcia

    Marcia’s eyes lazily drifted open. She stretched, smacking her lips as she gradually woke up. The movement made her very aware of a strange feeling behind her butt. Her eyes snapped fully open. There was something sticky, cold, and mushy pressing against her butt. And, to top it off, there was definitely a putrid smell emanating from her.

    “No!” she whimpered, patting her butt. The patting only served to further spread the mess. “No!” Marcia sat up. Once she was face-to-face with the bars of the crib, the events of the night before crashed over her. She was currently a toddler in a diaper and crib. Yes, she had pooped herself while she slept, but at least she was wearing the appropriate protection for it.

    As Marcia moved around in the crib, she realized that her diaper was also damp and clammy. She lifted her clothes. Sure enough, her diaper was yellowed. She let out a soft swear.

    “Wow, I’m not used to hearing someone your age saying words like that,” a voice said. Marcia looked up. Rusty stood in front of the crib, smirking at her. “You look like you could use a change, Marcia.”

    “Unfortunately, yeth,” Marcia sighed.

    “No problem. I’ve got like, six nieces, and I’ve changed all their diapers.” Rusty took Marcia out of the crib. “Phew, you stink!” He carried her out of Christine’s room and back to the nursery, where he removed Marcia’s clothes and diaper, then wiped her down. To Marcia’s surprise, the diaper she was then put in wasn’t a reusable one, but a modern disposable one. In addition, she was put in a white T-shirt and pair of pink overalls that clearly hadn’t come from Christine’s stash of toddler clothes.

    “Where’d you get thethe?” Marcia asked, idly tugging on the overalls.

    “Christine sent out the butler to pick up some stuff for you this morning,” Rusty replied.

    “Ah. I’m glad.”

    “Me, too. Those other clothes really didn’t suit your personality.”

    “Well, leth get back to work!” Marcia chirped. She paused. “Unleth…you don’t want me to work on thith job anymore.” She swallowed nervously as she waited for Rusty to reply.
