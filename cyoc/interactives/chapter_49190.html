    Circe is looking for Puppils

    "Join now!"

    Our Glorious Leader Circe is looking for gifted students to pass the art of animal transformation magic. Auditions are open for everybody! If you think you have what it takes! Your welcome to our academy. This week Monday-Friday the new students for this year will be selected.

    If You want to try please come to Circe's Castle for an audience, 5 lucky persons will be chosen as puppils!

    ps: No this is not a trap, we need to make people more interested in magic!

    The Swine Chief

    -------------------------------------------------------------------

    This message also reached Hexxus the Wizard, he was a 18 year old boy with a love for magic. His parrents were killed by Circe 10 years ago, since then he studied magic arts for his revenge.

    The Evil Wizard Hexxus descended from his Arctic Citadel.

    "Bwahahahahhahahahaha This will be my glorious return to power! I shall have my revenge upon Circe" Hexxus needed a plan.

    He could easly beat Circe and beat her to death. But he was a cool and evil Wizard. Just chrusing magical opponents wasen't his style. Besides He could learn some more animal magic. Untill now he had mostly studied the dark arts.

    "Mmm I need an Awesome plan for a Awesome wizard. Tum Tum Tum, where to find one...?" Hexxus was thinking. "Aha I got it!" Hexxus started to chant his words of Darkness.

    "By the power of Awesome. I- AM - AWESOME!" A note of paper fell from the sky and flew to Hexxus.

    Hexxus opend the letter and read it:

    " To defeat Circe in a awesome way!

    Circe has the annoying thing to be so full of herself and her magic. The best way for a awesome victory is a plan of deceit. For example "Turn yourself into an even more handsome boy and bring Circe Castle down from the inside! Agree to work for her while you hatch a plan to let Circe humiliate herself"

    "Mmmmm" Thought Hexxus that would be a good plan indeed, imagine if I had to make my own plans, pfff what a time I would have to invest.

    "But how to make myself even more handsome...." Hexxus never cared about outer appearance. His skill of Darkness made him into a magnificent being and he had no need to improve himself. He would need a disguise.

    "Small details, annoying, I will go undercover as a student to Circe. After I get accepted I'l think of a good plan" Hexxus used his magic to turn his hair slightly brown and his eye's green.

    "That would do it, To Circe Castle!!!!!"

    Hexxus thought of teleporting to Circe Castle, But he coulden't do that. After all that would be to advanced for a mere student. How would he get to the Castle then?

    "By the power of Awesome. I- AM - AWESOME!" The note came flying to Hexxus

    "Mwahahah I am the best!" Hexxus opend the letter:

    "Public Transportation"

    "........." Hexxus turned to paper, he assumed there would be an awesome way on the backside.

    "Walk"


    Hexxus grumbled and flew to the nearest bus station.
