She's in our year, and she's the biggest nerd ever. Welllllllllll... more like the tiniest. She's as petite as they come, but her brain's the size of a mountain."

Jinx is a teenage Wiccan in our grade who Liesel's training to assist her as Clan Seer. She'll move into Karnstein Manor when she turns 18. She's one of the cheerleaders."

    The yokai all nodded.

    "And isn't there a werefox skulk camped on the edge of town?" Tatiana added. "There might be some new werefox kids here."

    "I think so," said Selena. "Be on the lookout for pranks, everybody."

    Tatiana then looked Selena seriously in the eye. "There's something you should know, Lena. Liesel's visions are telling her that Tori Vordenburg is close. She's already somewhere in this state."

    "Thanks for the warning, sweetie," Selena said. "I'll be careful, I promise." She turned back to the yokai exchange students. "Alright, time to head on in! Don't wanna be late for class, right?"
