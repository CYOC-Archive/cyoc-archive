    As Brenda changed Mark was oblivious to these changes and he like a
    scared little rabbit headed to the boat.

    When he hit the clearing he saw half a dozen boar men in front of him.
    None of them were looking in his direction so he backed up into the
    forest underbrush. As he crouched down he saw several others coming
    down the beach oinking like pigs with a word here and there of
    English.

    What the hell happened here he asked to himself.

    As he sat there he felt a stick poke him in the back. As he turned to
    move the branch from his shoulder he looked up and there was the
    biggest fattest boar man of them all.

    Get going boy.

    Mark tried to jump up and run but he was no match for the boar man.

    The boar man held him by his shoulder so Mark couldn't straighten up
    and get to running as he was forced into the clearing.

    The other boar men surrounded him and he realized that he wasn't going
    anywhere.

    The big boar man pierced Marks nose with a big ring and attached a
    chain to it so he could lead his new acquisition around properly.

    You are going to be my new toy.

    Mark was led back to the area where he saw Brenda as a half pig woman
    quickly changing into a full sow stuffing her face at the trough.

    The boar man chained his human into his own sty and told him to eat.
    Mark looked at the trough and his stomach churned. He was going to be
    sick.

    I SAID EAT!

    Mark picked up something that looked the least nasty and put it in his
    mouth. Mark took little bites and his eyes opened as he realized that
    he liked the taste. Soon Mark was stuffing his face with gusto. He
    felt himself growing. His stomach painfully full but he just couldn't
    help it.

    If Mark had been listening he would have heard the discussion about
    his future. The big boar man was asked by one of his generals, So when
    ya gonna turn him into a pig?

    I don't know. I might not turn him into a pig at all. I want to see
    what happens when he stuffs himself with the special formula in the
    food. So no one gets to fuck him till I say. You know what will happen
    to you if anyone does.

    They all shuddered.

    Within a week Mark had lost his muscled body and was close to 250
    pounds with a big ball belly. He was laying in the mud his shirt
    buttons undone to relieve the pressure and his pants were unbuttoned.
    When he wasn't eating he would sit up against the pen his legs spread
    wide to allow his belly to sag between his legs. His shirt stained
    with food and his face growing a beard. He wasn't able to shave so he
    became more and more grubby and slobby as the days went on.

    The boar men would come and rub his belly and poke him as he grew
    softer and softer.

    Brenda was bread daily as Mark just ate and ate. She was growing but
    not as fast as Mark was. But then she had a litter of piglets growing
    inside of her.

    When mark had reached a bit over 800 pounds the big boar discussed
    Mark's new direction.

    Lets turn him into a sow now. He will be good for breeding.

    The big boar thought about that.

    Lets just feed him till he explodes.

    He is good as he is now lets just use him as a funny human.

    As the big boar heard the different options he thought to himself that
    maybe he would send Mark back to his own life. He would never be able
    to find the island again and if he did they could change him into a
    sow at that time.

    The big boar stuck Mark with a needle putting him to sleep as he was
    moved to his boat. When he awoke he no longer remembered the island.
    His mind had been stripped bare of his ordeal but there was no way he
    could explain his new form. Where was Brenda? He thought, not knowing
    she was on her second set of piglets.

    When he was rescued the tanker had to use a crane to hoist his big
    body out of the small personal boat. He found his appetite had been
    greatly enhanced by the months of stimulant added to his food on the
    island so there was no way he was going to lose the weight. And as he
    motored into his old job on one of those electric carts there weren't
    many that recognized this tub of lard but if they looked close some
    would see the old Mark in the new supersized version.

    Mark would go out in his the boat he was found in from time to time to
    try to figure out what had happened to him and Brenda but he never
    found the island again. Mark had never had a boyfriend before but now
    that he was the supersize one there were all sorts of men after him.
    Some chasers, some bears, but all liked the new Mark. He was so much
    less arrogant and more cushion for the pushin.

    What do you do now?
