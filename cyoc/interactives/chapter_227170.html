    “Tell me which human painted this again?” said a rather beautiful women, with long golden blonde hair as she looked at the painting hanging on the wall.

    “Leonardo…something.” Said an equally beautiful women, with a short shoulder length pitch black hair as she walked up to the first women. “Sorry I am not totally sure sister.”

    “Not that it matters all that much.” Said the blonde hair women as she moved aside, she was wearing a rather revealing flowing dress which seemed to drag across the ground without leaving a mark. The dress had an open front and back sticking to her side and was made out of glowing golden leaves. “I must admit Artemis, I think our old grounds in what the humans call it now…”

    “Greece.” Answered Artemis

    “Yes, Greece would be better.” Said the blonde hair beauty as she moved some of the hair behind her ear to show far longer and far more pointed ears.

    “Some levels I agree.” Confirmed Artemis “The humans there did worship me as a goddess there, goddess of the moon I believe.”

    “Makes sense you are the Goddess of the Moon Elves.” Answered her sister “Just like I Amaterasu was worship as a sun goddess in Japan even though I am Goddess of the Sun Elves.”

    “You rule the day, I rule the night, we are co rulers.” Said Artemis as she stepped closer to her sister, standing side by side it was clear that they were sister. Artemis was wearing a silver cocktail dress with a black corset adding more to her sexy goth look. Her skin was paler than her sister and her black hair seamed to shimmer with starlight when she moved her hand through it.

    Both of them had tiaras in their hair, one with a sun symbol one with a moon symbol.

    None of the humans around them paid them any mind thanks to the spell over them, they had walked into his private party hosted inside this museum like they owned the place which they could. The spell hid their ears from view and made them look less beautiful but still they where getting looks from many of the guests, both males and females.

    Both of them had awoken to find that the eleven race they ruled over gone. That was a major surprise to both of them, elves and humans always seemed to have the best relations, more so than humans and any of the other magical creatures.

    “So you are thinking this city for the capital of our new kingdom?” inquired Amaterasu

    “One I was considering.” Answered Artemis as she snapped a glass of wine from a passing waiter. “I feel Europe will be best for us and I like this city…Paris myself but if you want to go somewhere else, I am open to other ideas.”

    “I see.” Said Amaterasu as she considered what to do, she had liked this city when she toured it and that massive palace outside the city was rather beautiful. With some improvements it would be up to their standards. Still making a new eleven kingdom in the heart of Europe would be an issue, but generally humans go along great with elves so maybe it wouldn’t be as hard.

    “So sister what do you say?” inquired Artemis
