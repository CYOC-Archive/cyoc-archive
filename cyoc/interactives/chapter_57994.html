    "I don't know or really care about the model. I just want to become
    Sam Nolan's car. I want to find out what the BMOC and his girlfriend
    Annie the head cheerleader are up to...you know they're always in his
    car. They eat lunch in it, and well, being the car will be better
    than being a fly on the wall," Ryan explained nervously.

    "Dude, you just want to watch those two horndogs go at it," laughed
    Jeremy, "So will you be able to turn back or do you need to select a
    specific time frame. You know you'll remain a car until midnight on
    December 24th or will you just change back when you like, and what
    happens if they're inside you when you change back?"

    "Well, I don't want to stay his car forever, because he'll probably
    trade it in in a few years. I'll set the transformation for one week,
    and then I'll try changing back early to see if I can, if not I'll be
    me again in a week. I'll make the change during third period, so I'm
    sure no one will be in me when I change back, so that eliminates that
    problem. Meet me at the end of 2nd period tomorrow in the school
    parking lot."

    Jeremy understood Ryan's concern about those two: Sam and Annie were
    student leaders with vested interests that didn't necessarily gibe
    with Jeremy and Ryan's interests. They would rather see the library
    shut down or academic programs cut than have a signle cut back to the
    athletics or cheer organization.

    Ryan was going to be a spy car.

    Jeremy had been so riveted by the discussion of the Renaissance in his
    2nd period European History Class, he nearly forgot about Ryan's plan.
    He was late getting to the parking lot. Ryan was already there, and he
    was behind Sam's car stripped down to his white briefs.

    "Ryan, why are you naked?"

    "Cause my clothes probably won't change and I don't want to ruin
    them," Ryan explained, as he pulled off his tighty whiteys.

    "And what about Sam's car? Don't you think he'll remember he doesn't
    have two of them?"

    "Already thought of that - that's the other reason I'm taking off my
    clothes, Sam's car's going to need something to wear."

    "What?"

    "Watch."

    Ryan put his hand on Sam's yellow Trans Am, and his hand became shiny
    yellow too. The car creaked. The sound of metal bending and
    scraping could be heard as the car seemed to contract, as Ryan started
    to expand.

    Jeremy stared as Sam became the Trans Am, and the Trans Am became a
    man.
