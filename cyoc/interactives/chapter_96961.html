    Carrie felt a tingle in her face. She could feel her skin rippling as her bones and flesh reshaped. It was creepy but painless like all of the other changes. Still, he was changing her face. That was her! That was her identity. It unnerved her to her core. When the changes ceased, she could tell something felt different, but it was hard to see her reflection in the glass. All she could see was Todd's grinning face looking down on her with satisfaction.

    "What did you do?" she said to the glass, and then let out a squeak as the voice that had just come out of her mouth was suddenly much higher and younger sounding. "My voice?" she chirped, touching her throat. The sound of it rang in her ears like a bell.

    Suddenly the door opened in front of her and she tumbled to the ground in a heap of unbalanced flesh.

    "How do you like it?" Todd asked as he held the door open for her.

    "Like what?" she replied sounding like a petulant toddler.

    "Oh," Todd realized. "You can't see it. Hold on a moment." He ran in the library, leaving her lying on the ground against the door too shocked by the situation to do anything. He returned seconds later carrying her bag. After stepping over her prone form, he offered her a hand and stood her up, leading her by her hand to a nearby bench as if she were a little lost girl. He even hefted her up onto the bench, lifting her like a child under her arms and grunting heavily at all of her weight. "Oof, there you go," he said as she settled into her seat. He sat down next to her. "Have a look in your mirror."

    "My mirror?"

    "I thought all girls had little compact mirrors in their bags. Don't they?"

    Carrie dug around her bag and found her mirror. She opened it and gasped like a child on Christmas morning. "I- I- I'm young?"

    "Well, your head is," Todd corrected her.

    She ran a small hand up to her smooth round cheek and touched a fingertip to her tiny button nose. It was the face of a toddler, and it must have looked ridiculous when seen just above the cleavage of her giant tits. "W-why?" she asked and understood now why her voice sounded so child-like.

    Todd shrugged. "Seemed like it would be cute, and it is." Then, he frowned slightly. "Although..."

    "What?"

    "I think I want to make one more change."

    "Wait, wait!" Carrie pleaded, but he only lifted the remote and started to press buttons. "No more, please."

    He showed no sign of hearing her. He only pointed the remote at her and pressed a button. This time the tingle was centered on her lips. She felt them swell. She held up her mirror and watched as her childish lips grew thick and plump until they dominated her face. When the change stopped, she had an enormous pair of bee-stung lips that looked too large to be natural but still small enough to seem almost possible on her young face. She touched an uncertain finger to her bottom lip and was rewarded with a pleasurable feeling. She moaned slightly in her childish voice. She pursed her lips and ran a finger along them as they tingled with delight. It was as if she had a new vagina on her face. They were so sensitive! It made her want to suck her thumb or a Popsicle or... something else.

    "I see you like them," Todd said, and Carrie froze having forgotten about him for a moment while playing with her new lips. She knew exactly what her lips were meant for now.

    "Why?" she asked pointedly. She was tired of playing games.

    "Why not?" he said. "Because I can. Don't they feel good?"

    "Yes," she said finding it a bit harder to shape words with her large lips. "But this is wrong. Turn me back."

    "Maybe."

    "What do you mean?"

    "If you help me with my experiments, I will turn you back. I'll even repay you for your time. But, if you leave now or try to get me in trouble, then I'll just do what I want with you. Maybe I'll just leave you as you are now, or maybe I'll make it even worse. It's your choice."

    "Some choice," she said. "Be your experiment or be a freak?"

    "I could make it very enjoyable," he said. "And, if you really help me out, I could reward you in any way that you could imagine."

    "Hmm," she said. "That's some offer."

    Todd smiled as Carrie pondered making a deal with the devil.

    A few moments later she said, "Alright, I have decided that..."
