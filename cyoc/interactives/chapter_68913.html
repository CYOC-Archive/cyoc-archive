    Dave kicked a rock as he walked down the mall with his hands in his pockets. He was your average, awkward 18 year old guy. He glanced over
    into the glass window of one of the shops as he looked at the unathletic, lanky, average frame staring back at him. He ruffled his wavy brown
    hair to get it to sit right when he noticed a bright, new shop in the reflection behind him. He turned around and read the sign above it "The
    Clothing Store". Strange, he thought, he'd never seen this shop before, and he knew the mall very well, but it looked brand new. "Couldn't hurt
    just to take a look" he thought to himself. He walked through the small open entrance, a small chime beeped as he walked in.

    The place was huge for a mall clothing store, like it was bigger on the inside, there was a wall to his left, turning right he saw isles and shelves
    stretching on for a huge distance. The isles seemed to stretch on forever, but they were half concealed behind a long, high front bench.
    "Welcome, come in!" a tall, handsome clerk said to him, pressing a button to lift up the gate on the bench. Dave was 6'1" himself, "This guy must
    be 6'5"" Dave thought to himself as he caught himself checking out the man.

    "What's with the bench?" Dave asked, "Oh just security, and we really want people to come in a get a proper look." the clerk said responding with
    a smile. "What's your name?" he asked, "Um, Dave. Why do you ask?", "Oh, well, I'm your personal shop assistant for your time here today, we
    value customer satisfaction very highly." "Uh, okay; I think I'll be fine though." "Oh, no, no, no, I insist! Besides, store policy. By the way, you can
    call me Calvin" he chuckled.

    "Um... sure, okay, so what do you guys actually sell, the name's kind of vague..." Dave asked. "Oh every piece of clothing you could imagine at
    very competitive prices, male, female, you want it we've got it." "Okay..." "What's up Dave, down in the dumps? Well how about we get you a new
    look!" the clerk said excitedly. "Um... yeah, why not!" Dave responded positively, glad that someone was taking an interest in him. "Well how
    about we go to the mens section!" Calvin said, "Follow me!"

    Dave followed the clerk through countless isles, making many turns along the way "How big is this place?!" Dave thought to himself. As they
    walked along he saw all types of things, dresses, suits, every kind of shoe possible, even a bunch of costumes; and all in many sizes.

    Finally they slowed down, another bench, "Through here, this is the mens section." The carpet on the mens section was blue, distinctly different
    from the black carpet behind them. The walls on either side had many doors, each leading to fitting rooms.

    "Okay, so let me see, what would look great on you?" Calvin said to himself, clearly intending Dave to hear. David darted his eyes around in
    nervousness as the good looking man inspected him. "Well, you definitely need to change that white t-shirt, your shorts too, your trainers too
    actually, you need your own style!" He said to Dave.

    "Okay, so what do you think I should try?" Dave asked. "Oh I know exactly!" he exclaimed. The other people in the area, all also with their own
    assistants, didn't seem to mind the clerks excited outbursts. Dave noticed at this point that all the other assistants with people seemed to be very
    attractive, some male, some female. "Odd that this place's employees are all so good looking", Dave thought.

    The clerk picked up a very large basket at the bench and signalled for Dave to follow, they weaved throughout the isles as Calvin picked up
    dozens upon dozens of clothes out the isles and shelves, neglecting to even check if they might fit Dave, but Dave went along with it. Soon the
    basket was overflowing with clothing, so much so that the clerk had filled a second basket.

    "Now, this way!" he said, leading Dave through a door on the wall, there was a narrow hallway inside that stretched onward for a short distance
    then turned left into a larger open changing area with a mirror. "You guys must really value privacy to have all this" Dave said, "Oh my, yes we
    do." Calvin said as he sat the two baskets on a seat near the mirror and rummaged through them. "Now what to try on you, Dave..." he mumbled.

    "Ah here we are! This will suit you perfectly!" he exclaimed as he handed Dave.....
