    Jonathan has been having a bad year. He lost his security guard job 9 months ago when his company downsized and has not been able to find anything new because of the state of the economy. His girlfriend left him soon after he lost his job since he couldn't pamper her as much on the little money he recieved from unemployment. He doesn't even have that anymore since his claim ran out 3 months ago. With no money and all his savings gone just to keep the utilities on he has become 2 months late in his rent and is set to be evicted by the end of the week.

    So while Jonathan knew it was a scam, he was desperate enough to try anything when he found the TF Star site. He got a good chuckle at all the transformations available and could limit what people could do to him but since each transformation was worth money he clicked on accept to all of them when setting up his account. Besides, none of this could be real and he knew he would never recieve any money from this place but at least reading through all these crazy options like gender swap, penis growth and animal transformation kept him from thinking about his impending homelessness.

    As he went to finalize his account he recieved a popup from the site. It said that he could recieve a 1,000 dollar signing bonus if he agreed to not change his options and not opt out of the program for 1 month.

    "What the hell, why not?" he mumbled as he clicked yes to the signing bonus. He then saw a congradulations screen that told him his account would be approved by mail tommorrow and he would recieve his signing bonus then and start getting transformations after that. Then he was sent back to the TF Star main page and saw something that surprised him. His picture was right in the center of the main page as the new TF Star even though he never uploaded a pic. Not only that but people were already starting to bid on him.
