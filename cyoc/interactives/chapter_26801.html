    "Well... Mark you wanted to come here you go in first!" Eric says
    "Oh come on lets go in at the same time strength in numbers... right?"
    "Unless the person in there isn't a witch and the sight of so many uninvited guests frightens her and she refuses to let us use a phone" Tyler comments
    "Well then you should go Tyler you will seem less of a threat given your so small..." Clara says not missing chance to add a sarcastic comment.
    "Ladies first..." You say in response.

    You all hesitate for a moment deciding who it should be to go in first when Mark finally steps forward.
    "I got us into this I'll go in first" Mark says
    a few seconds pass
    "Mark wait" Clara says going in after him
    "I knew this was a bad idea to begin with" Eric says walking inside
    "I guess we are all going in" Tyler says following behind Eric by a few seconds.
    Sighing you wait a minute before you walk in last.

    Inside you find yourself alone
    "Hay where did all of you guys go?" you shout into the house with no answer.
    "Hello! Hello?! Hell-" you start yelling
    "Hello" a old lady appears behind you cutting you off
    "Oh hi how did you get behind me you scared me"
    "magic"
    you laugh "oh ok, do you know where my friends are?"
    "Your friends?" she says looking confused
    "Ya four others a girl and three guys, they just came in here a few seconds ago"
    "I don't remember anyone else coming in come on in sit down make yourself at home"
    "I came in right before them you couldn't of missed them" you respond now suspicious of the old lady

    She leads you into a room with a table.
    "Sit I'll bring you some tea then we can get to looking for your friends"
    "I thought you said you didn't hear anyone else" you yell at her as she walks into another room
    "I did but if what you say is true they must be here somewhere" she says coming back in carrying a pot of tea and a cup.
    "No thanks I'm not thirsty" you say eying the dark liquid suspiciously
    "I insist it will help you relax" she movies the cup near you the alluring scent of the drink makes your throat go dry.
    "On second thought I'll just have a sip" you say picking up the cup and drinking in a little as the old woman smiles at you.

    What do you do now?
