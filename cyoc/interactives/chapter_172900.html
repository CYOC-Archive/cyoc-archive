    At the Mall

    A few days later, Will and Jake went to the mall. They brought the ring along with them. Will didn’t really care about using it here, so he was going to let Jake use it instead.

    “Remember,” Will told him, “change in the bathroom; we don’t want people noticing it. Be back here in 4 hours. Got it?”

    “Ok!” Jake said, dashing off to the nearest bathroom. There he entered a stall and locked it, making sure no one else was present in the room. Jake twisted the ring, and immediately he took on his enhanced form, a handsome and muscular preteen who was seriously hung and who was wearing clothes a couple sizes too small.

    When he exited the stall, Jake saw himself in the mirror. He flexed a little and posed, to watch his muscles bulge under his shirt. He flashed a smile that would make a girl weak in the knees.

    With the self-admiration done, Jake left the bathroom and walked into the mall. There were so many places where he could go, so many things to do.

    Where would Jake go now?
