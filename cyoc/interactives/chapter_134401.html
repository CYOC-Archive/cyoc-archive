    Ty's Power

    Ty reverses roles between a geek and a jock

    While Ty was strolling around the large gym near his high school, he spotted a nerdy kid having a conversation with a jock close to the entrance to the gym.

    “What are YOU doing here, Tom?” the muscle guy said to the chubby, overweight kid. “You don’t even belong here! This place is for jocks and people who want to work out!”

    Tom, the chubby freshman, was tall for his age, with blue eyes and blond hair. He was 6 feet tall, but he was seriously overweight. He was fat and he was gasping and wheezing as he waddled around the vast weight room.

    “Leave me alone, man! My parents got me a gift card to this place, so I could do cardio on the treadmill! So, get off my case, man! Not everybody is born with great genetics like you!” Tom shouted back.

    Ty overheard the conversation and he became angry. Cole, the tall jock, was giving Tom a hard time because he had just joined.

    “I don’t want you working out here when I’m over here, do you hear me? Don’t be using the weights or the machines when I’m here or else I’m gonna kick you out!” Cole sneered at him.

    Ty had heard enough of all the bullying at this point and he decided that it had become time for Cole to get a taste of his own medicine. So, he made a silent wish and he just looked on from a distance as he waited for the changes to occur.

    “I can use the gym whenever I want to!” Tom shouted as he wheezed and puffed. “I can come here and…. Uhh….”

    Suddenly, Tom felt a bit…. Lighter. The fat around his arms, his neck, his legs and his big belly started to melt away! He was still the same height as before, but he was becoming a LOT thinner!

    “HEY! What is going on here?” Cole gasped as he started packing on some blubber around his big, sculpted body. His legs started to bulge with fat while his arms developed thick layers of lard which were hanging from his triceps…. His legs were no longer so defined, and his belly began to inflate, no longer covering his six-pack!

    “Whooaaa!” Tom gasped as more fat melted away from his body, and in its place, rock-hard muscles began to take its place as big, bulging pectorals began to form across Tom’s chest. Tom’s arms formed large, hard triceps and biceps where he had nothing but fat. His large stomach disappeared, and he now had washboard abdominal muscles!

    “Hey! I got big muscles all over me!” Tom grinned as he looked down at his own body.

    “HEY! NOOOO! My muscles! I trained HARD to gain them! WHAT’S HAPPENING?” Cole shouted as he ran towards the large full-body mirror. And that was becoming difficult for him to do because he was now hauling all that fat on his frame!

    Cole now had large rolls of fat hanging from his thighs and his lower legs, along with his large buttocks and his huge belly. Cole was even BIGGER now than Tom was a few moments earlier!

    “Wowww! Look at me!!!” Tom cheered as he also walked up to the mirror, now looking like a total teenage bodybuilder with ripped abs, bulging arms, thick quads and round calves sticking out from his legs. And, all his muscles were covered with long, thick veins.

    “NO WAY! GIVE ME BACK MY MUSCLES!” Cole shouted to Tom.

    “Uhh…. May I suggest you start by running on the treadmill? And…. I won’t hold you back from working out! Have a great time here at the gym, dude! I’m gonna go lift some weights!” Tom grinned as he flexed his big arm, allowing a huge bicep to swell up on his arm.

    Ty couldn’t help but chuckle from a distance as he happily approved of the neat role reversal which he allowed to happen. The bully became a chubby guy while the fat kid became a muscle dude. And, everyone was happy in the gym. Now, Ty could walk around and look for his next “victim” ….
