Her lips inside her white muzzle were now a dull grey-blue, and turned down in a slight frown. Her eyebrows were now arched grey lines high on her forehead that added to the shocked look on Kara's face, lines almost like wrinkles showed on her face and her blonde hair was now matted with black tips and streaks of grey. Kara's feet were also nearly three times as long, and at the very center of her face, her nose was now swollen and bulbous, and red as if she'd been drinking.

His skin was how a dull orange, with even more exaggerated patches of white around his eyes and a white muzzle around his mouth, which was also turned down in a mournful expression. Black had appeared over his chin and cheeks like a very dark stubble, and his nose was big and round as a tennis ball, and black as coal. Greg's feet were big and almost as wide as swimming flippers; he'd have a rather awkward time walking in them with his new girth.
    And on his head, Greg's hair was a thick, curly fringe surrounding a pate as smooth and bald as an egg.
    They felt their own and each other's faces, looked in the mirror, and Kara rubbed her boyfriend's bald spot. They both squeezed their bulbous nosews; Kara's didn't do anything but Greg's gave out a loud WHOO-WHOO! like a foghorn.

    "But now I gotta tell you, you two are a sad hobo and sad bag lady clowns-the very lowest of the low. You Kara, now act as the family maid having to keep the house clean and all the house chores. And you Greg, in exchange for Kara's family allowing you to live in you van on their driveway, have to mow the grass, clean the gutters, rake the leaves and all the other dirty and lowly jobs.
