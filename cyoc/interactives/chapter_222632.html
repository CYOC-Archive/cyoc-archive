    "Yeah, bro, you might be only 21 or 22, when you become human again. Some dogs have 2, 3 and even 4 litter per year. Of course, the more litters you bear each year, the sooner you get to be human, so if you play hard to get with Brutus or the other studs, you might end up a lot older," Brad commented as he walked away. Chad's new canine hearing picked up Brad muttering softly to himself, "Course there's no guarantee that ya won't still be female, if I change you back," then he snickered to himself.

    Chad was horrified. The heavy muzzle of the stud dog brushed against Chad's cheek and ear. Then the beast spoke.

    "Yeah, Chad, I knew you were destined to be my bitch, the moment I laid eyes on you," Brutus said in a gruff but sultry voice.

    "You can speak?" Chad gasped.

    "Ever since I was a two year old boy. I wasnt always called Brutus the Stud you know. Aaaah, yeah, you're still a little tight, but you'll loosen up nicely. Can't believe it took Brad to figure out that the only bitch for me to stud was you. I practically mounted you every time I got near you. Finally, my knot is where it belongs."


    Chad's mind was crashing with too much data. One thought suddenly rose to the surface. "Wait you were human?"

    "Yep. Until a couple years ago, when Brad offered me a ride. Next thing I knew, I waa kenneled and growing fur."

    "Wait, you said your name wasn't always Brutus. Did I , do I know you?"

    "Oh, we know each other in the biblical sense. I consider us married for life. So when Brad makes us human again, you'll still be my bitch. Don't care whether you are male or female, so don't worry about that. Just stay still, and enjoy me knotted inside you."

    "What makes you think Brad will turn you human?"

    "To test whether he can turn you human. Smell all the dogs in the kennel. Your brother is a cheap ass, all of them used to be people he gave rides to. He never bought a dog for this kennel since he opened it."

    "But I got my dog Bounder here when he was a pup."

    "Oh, yeah, I remember that kid. He was Mikey that 7 year old kid who disappeared riding his bike. Was on the milk cartons for weeks."

    Chad was silent. He had less confidence than Brutus that any of them would be human again. Brad wouldn't risk life imprisonment by changing them human and letting them go.
