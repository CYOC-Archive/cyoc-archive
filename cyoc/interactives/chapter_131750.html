    Braiden doesn't know what to do. He's anxious, what if he screws up?
    "What are you doing Braiden?" Asks Brodie.
    "I don't know. I'm worried I'll screw up."

    The music stops playing. "Shit," says Callum as Max, "Going to be late."
    "Thanks Braiden," says Brodie grabbing his bag.
    "I've got class here for the last block so don't worry too much," says Braiden. They all walk out. Feeling extremely sorry for Maggie. As Braiden walks out he sees someone sit on her. "Poor Maggie," he mutters to himself.

    Callum walks into class and everyone stares at him, "Sorry. Lost track of time."
    "Don't make a habit of it Max," replies the teacher. Callum feels weird being referred to as Max. Even if that's who he is right now. Callum sits down to a seat he automatically knows is his. He automatically pulls out the appropriate books and stationary. He does it without fault. He quickly looks at the girl beside him and asks, "You still up for the weekend?"
    "Yeah," replies the girl, "Where is it again?"
    "We'll meet in the mall."
    "Okay." Callum is shocked. He knew that by heart. He quickly thinks to himself, 'Are my memories being replaced?" He quickly thinks about where he was born. 'Melbourne,' he thinks to himself. 'No. I was born in another place. Where was it again?' Callum is terrified, his memories are being replaced. He wants class to be over ASAP, he doesn't want to loose anymore memories. 'Max is probably having the same issues too,' he thinks to himself.

    Class can't end soon enough for Callum. He's lost a few more memories like old friends and pets, replaced by Max's memories. "So you going home after class?" Asks a boy. Callum recognises him as his best friend Eric, "Yeah," he replies automatically, "Wait. No. I've got stuff to do."
    "Okay. After that you want to hang out?"
    "Sure."
    Class ends. Callum grabs his bag and quickly heads to the library. He has to meet up with Braiden ASAP. He walks into the library where he sees Braiden sitting on a familiar chair. He looks around and sees no other students. "Braiden," he says. Braiden jumps, "Oh sorry."
    "You've got to fix us now."
    "I'm working on it," replies Braiden.
    "My memories are being replaced with Max's."
    "How bad?" Asks Braiden concerned.
    "Can't remember where I was actually born, or my early friends and pets."
    "Fuck. That's not good."
    "Tell me about it. Max is probably experiencing the same thing but in reverse."
    "I'll text him."
    "How?"
    "With your phone."
    "How exactly?"
    "Uhm," says Braiden confused.
    "Shit," says Callum.
    Braiden quickly pulls out his phone and texts.

    A minute passes and Max in Callum's body appears. He looks concerned and looks at his original body, "Memories being replaced too?"
    "Yeah."
    "Thought it would just be a body swap and not a mental swap too."
    "So did I. But life's never that easy."
    "I think I've solved it," says Braiden in the background.
    "You sure?" Asks Callum as Max.
    "I'm sure of it."
    "Do it then," says Max as Callum. Braiden aims the phone at them.
