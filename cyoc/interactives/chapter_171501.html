    When you open your eyes your not in the cave anymore. You try to get up only to find your hands and feet tied together behind your back. You attempt calling for Jason only to find yourself gagged. You hear a familiar voice above you.

    “...Yes commander we have captured the escaped female.” It was the lizard guy from earlier, he had his hand on the side of his helmet and was talking to it. His armor still had a hole in the chest plate, but inside you saw a scaly six pack that seamed totally uninjured. Maybe they can regenerate?

    “The Tyger male know as Jason that freed her has been dealt with. He won’t be causing us any more trouble.” He said, your eyes widened. What did they do to him? Was he dead? He didn’t give you any answers, instead his visor glanced down at you while he waited for a reply.

    “Understood commander.” The lizard man took his hand from his helmet and turned to you.

    He then lifted you up to were you were on your knees.

    “Consider yourself lucky female.” The lizard man hissed in your ear. “Your about to meet the Great Commander in the flesh.”

    He pulled some kind of device from his belt and put it on you. It was a collar of some kid, and it felt incredibly tight. You probably could have resisted more, maybe even try out your skunk powers for the first time, but you were still in shock over the news involving Jason. He then pressed a button on the side and the jungle disappeared.

    ...

    In a flash of blue light you now kneeled in a vast grey metal chamber. The chamber was freezing, even through your fur you shivered as your knees touched the ice cold black floor. The room had vaulted ceilings and iron pillars on either side. In between the pillars sat lizards, crocodiles, and snakes of different colors working on large boxy terminals. However the most eye catching feature was definitely the throne in the middle.

    Sitting on an elevated plateau that gave them a view of presumably every screen in the room and also having a few holograms around it. This was definitely meant for someone in charge. And sitting there was none other than....
