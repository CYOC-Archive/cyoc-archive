    Growing the Soccer Goalie

    Jess’ head was spinning with all the thoughts and ideas that were racing through her mind. Did the weird Chinese man at the strange store called “Scents, Spells & Sorcery” seriously give her the ability to change her boyfriend Alex any way she wanted to?

    Alex was also 18 but he was very average standing at only 5-foot 6 inches tall and weighing just over 170 pounds. He was short and average height and weight, which explained why he was the goalie on the soccer team. Deep down, she wished Alex was bigger, taller and a little more muscular.

    Gosh, was this true? Could she really change him physically, just by thinking it and imagining how she wanted her boyfriend to look? She started to imagine what Alex would look like with slightly bigger pectorals. She just loved the idea of having a boyfriend with big, round pectorals. They would be so nice to hold, hug and to cuddle up with in bed. The more she thought about it, the more excited she became as she approached the parking lot of their apartment.

    Alex had just finished cooking some hamburgers on a pan on the stove. He turned everything off and he had walked into the bathroom so he could take a quick shower. He had removed his clothes and stepped into the shower stall and turned on the lukewarm water, when he was overcome by an odd sensation in his chest.

    Somehow, unexpectedly, his pectorals started to balloon from his chest! They grew together, both sides, and at the same time, in one short growth spurt, inflating by at least three inches from his chest, growing forward and sideways while adding significantly more weight to his upper torso within a matter of seconds!

    KKKKKKKKRRRRRRRRRR!

    “ACK! What the hell?” Alex gasped as he felt a dramatic increase in weight in his upper torso. The increase was so much, that he fell forward in the shower, losing his balance due to the sudden growth spurt.

    “WHAT HAPPENED?” he panted, as he felt large, heavy pectorals hanging from his chest. He rested with his hands on the tiled shower floor before he slowly worked his way back on his feet. He rinsed off and turned off the water, quickly stepping out of the shower and grabbing a towel so he could dry off. While he dried off, he leaped before the full-body mirror so he could see his own reflection.

    “What… What’s going on here? What happened to my chest?” he wondered out loud as he examined his large, bulging pecs in the mirror.

    Just then, Jess walked in the door.

    “ALEX! I’m home!”

    “I’m in the bathroom!” Alex shouted back. “GET OVER HERE! Something happened… Something WEIRD happened to my chest!”

    Jess opened the door and saw Alex with his large, bulging pecs.

    “IT WORKED! It REALLY worked!” she cheered as she saw what had happened to her boyfriend.

    Alex’s jaw dropped wide open.

    “What are you talking about, Jess? Do you know about this weird growth spurt I just had?”

    “YES! I know ALL about it, because… I CAUSED IT!” Jess shouted. “I CAN CHANGE YOUR BODY! Isn’t it great?”

    Alex just stared at her with a very confused look on his face.

    “Say what?”

    “Let me explain, sweetie!” she replied. “Then I’ll try something else on you!”

    What was Jess going to change about Alex, next?
