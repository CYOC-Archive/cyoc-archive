    Nate stood up and allowed the scanner to scan him and he saw his full body image along with all his stats show up on the screen. He then sat back down at the computer and looked through the transformation packages again. He finally settled on the werewolf to start. Nate had been a life long enthusiast of the werewolf and had long wondered what it would be like to turn into one.

    He pressed the preview button and watched on screen as his image slowly turned into an anthropomorphic wolf, with grey and black fur and a lupine snout.

    "Cool," whispered Nate and he pressed save, figuring that just saved the animation file for later. He genuinely did not believe a computer program and emitter could physically transform him.

    However, he was quickly proven wrong when he started to feel an itching sensation all over his body. He caught sight of his arms and was shocked to see that grey and black hairs were growing in. Not only that, but his nails were growing out into claws. He felt a dull pain in his mouth and a tugging at his ears as well. He jumped up and ran into the bathroom. He was greeted with quite a sight. His ears had grown to points while his teeth were growing out into sharped fangs. His face was framed by a dark shadowing of stubble.

    "Oh my god...oh god..." he said, taken aback by how much deeper his voice sounded. He watched as the stubble grew out into fur around his face, traveling down his neck and past his collar. He tore open his dress shirt with his clawed hands, his palms now looking like paw pads. He revealed his furry chest and smiled, revealing a mouth full of sharp fangs. He could feel his muscles swell and contract, growing and reforming. His chest swelled and barreled out. He felt his socks tear away as his feet reformed and became digigrade paws. Nate looked at his new feet in awe.

    "How much further is this going to go?" he growled.

    Almost as if it were responding, Nate felt a pressure building at the base of his spine. Slowly, a lupine tail grew out. Nate reached back with a clawed hand and tore a hole in his pants for it. As the transformation slowed, he felt a pressure build in his face. He forced his eyes to remain open as he watched in the mirror as his facial muscles contracted and released. He watched with rapt fascination as his face slowly pushed out into a lupine snout and his ears moved to the top of his head.

    Nate looked in the mirror at his newly transformed body. He couldn't believe it was really him, but he liked it. He practiced snarling in the mirror and then let out a howl. It began to sink in that this was really happening. He slowly made his way back to the computer. There was a pop up screen asking if he wanted to save transformation. He clicked on the not now button and a new box appeared asking how long he wanted to transformation to last. He set the dial to three hours. He figured he had been through such a dramatic transformation, he might as well enjoy it for awhile.

    After that, he walked over to his back door, opened it and ran out into the cool night air. He was going to have fun as a werewolf for awhile.
