    "Well, that's weird, but I kind of like it.", Elizabeth concluded giving Madame Illusia the US $2,000 she asked for plus another US $7,000 bonus making Madame smile. "Very well!", madame replied, "I'll install three different copies of your daughter on three different 20 TB enterprise quality hard drives with a few games that may interest you. I think I'll give you three games, a sims like game so that you can control her environment, but not her You also can't change her physical or mental age. However, you'll be able to control the furniture, food, entertainment, housing style, income status, and her clothing. You'll be able put in any item or have her wear any garment or choose her housing for anyone from newborn to elderly would need. That way you can spoil her how ever you like. In the second game, it will be raising your kid simulator, but again you won't be able to change her physical or mental age. Again, you can earn prizes in the form of new more grown up toys, clothes, food, and personal hygiene items to interact with her. However, you'll have to start with the items at the very beginning of the game which are nothing more than baby items. If she cooperates with you and behaves, she'll have the option to remain her current age of twenty one years old, but if she doesn't cooperate with you, she'll become a year younger mentally and physically until she behaves again in which case she can grow up. On her birthday, she'll be able to age up another year like she does in the real world....but only if she behaves and is raised correctly. That means on her next birthday, she can age up to a maximum of twenty two years. The third and final game will be a game designed for a young girl of three to seven years old called Amelie's Adventures. You will first have to tell the game how old you, the player are. Since I understand that you aren't really a child of three to seven years old, you'll just have to enter a number that suits you. The game is a simple children's education game guiding Amelie through her day at school. Her grade level, the activities she will do, the difficulty of the activities, and the clothes that you have an option to dress her in will depend on what age you chose at the beginning of the game. And so she could be in nursery school, pre-k, kindergarten, first, or second grade. Amelie will be regressed to the age you chose both mentally and physically during game play, but will be restored to her proper physical and mental age when the game closes. Finally, I have two more programs to tell you about. Using a webcam and microphone, you can talk to Amelie and she can talk to you any time her computer is on. The other program simply has her files in it. Each file will have one year of Amelie's life in it. If you erase a file with a lower age number, all other files with a higher age number will be erased as well. This will cause Amalie to regress both mentally and physically. If you do choose to erase her file, Amelie will be aware of it and you'll be able to watch her regress on screen. However, I'm not sure if she'd like it or not. Be warned, if you erase those files, they'll never come back except through time once a year on her birthday when the next higher number will be added to the files. In other words, she'll have to grow up naturally again." With that said, Madame Illusia magicked the three hard drives. Imediately, Amalie faded into green ones and zeros that got sucked up into the three hard drives. Amelie had screamed when she turned into ones and zeros, but that quickly faded into nothing. With nothing left to do, Elizabeth took the hard drives, said her goodbyes to Madame Illusia, drove home, and replaced the existing hard drive in her computer with Amelie. She booted up the computer.........
