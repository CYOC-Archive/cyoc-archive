    Double your fun

    Previous chapter

    added by Rusty Raccoon 14 years ago A

    You click on the fox tail. In your excitement, you accidentally click twice before clicking save.

    Several other options come up but you don't pay much attention to them as you click through them. When you hit enter, a wave of dizziness has you holding onto your desk to keep from falling over.

    The room seems to be getting warmer.

    You look at arms that seem thinner but are also gaining a layer of bright orange fur!

    It's real!

    Forgetting the dizziness you feel, you leap from your chair and fall onto what you expected be your rear end. Instead, a sharp pain shoots through your entire body as you land on something soft and warm, something you can feel the rest of your body land on.

    You lean back and see not one but two large orange tails tipped with white. Their yours!

    A sinking feeling hits you as you stand up. Your shirt grows so large that it reaches your knees, your jeans and underwear fall to your feet and your socks grow to be far too large.

    Your entire room is getting bigger!

    "What's happening?" you cry out.

    Your voice is higher, sounding much like it did when your were a kid! A realization hits you that it's not your room that's growing; it's you that's shrinking!

    Your shirt, pants, underwear and socks all turn into a warm light blue wisp of energy that flies away from your body, leaving you naked. It's not a problem as fur has covered your entire body, white on your chest and orange elsewhere.

    You acquire a rather nasty headache but at the same time seem to be remembering and understanding things you couldn't before. You hold your head only to discover that your head is fuzzy, your ears are triangle ears atop your head and that a few strands of hair have replaced your human hair! The headache thankfully only lasts for a several more seconds. When it passes, you feel different but not in a bad way.

    Knowledge that once seemed impossible to gain is now part of you. Comprehension that felt far beyond anything you could ever have is now child's play to you.

    The wisp of energy flies over to you and covers your hands and feet, forming into gloves and sneakers as it dissipates. The dizziness fades and you feel complete.

    You run back over to your chair, your steps seeming far lighter then before. When you access your computer, it seems different. You were good with computers before but now - now what was once a challenging task is almost a joke. Things that once seemed impossible are starting to feel like something you wished you'd done years before.

    An image on the screen, that of the two tailed fox you accidentally turned yourself into looks at you. The image is adorable, that's for sure. You'd never admit to anyone, especially a guy! Maybe you'd admit it to a girl but you'd be blushing the entire time. You realize that the fox -- you appear to be younger then you were, maybe even a kid.

    You look down at your body and then at your gloved hands. Despite wearing only gloves and sneakers, you don't feel naked. You feel comfortable. Sure you could wear clothes but they'd just get in the way.

    A look in your bedroom mirror confirms that this really is you!

    Before you'd wonder how any of this was possible. The idea of a virus, nano-machines or even some bizarre form of radiation altering your DNA and messing with your cells may have occurred to you. How such a thing could be possible or could do it this quickly was beyond your grasp. Now however -- now, you're able to imagine all sorts of possibilities, maybe even ways of making your own machine to make this possible.

    "This is amazing, I understand things," you say. "I have to make this stick; I don't want to go back to being dumb!"

    Not that you were dumb before, more you were if anything, above average. Now, you're so far into genius that even above average seems less then stellar.

    Your improved ears pick up the sound of an ice cream truck. You smile and lick your lips. Despite being a genius and a fox, you're still a kid! In fact, you can swear you can small the mint chocolate chip even from here!

    The question is, what does a humanoid twin tailed fox do in a world of humans (well, after he's had his iced cream of course!)?

    What do you do now?
