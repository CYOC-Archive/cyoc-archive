    Geff became 19 years 364 days 23 hours and 50 minutes old upon the completion of the Chronivac's setup
    routine. The Chronivac's programmers considered this story line something of an "Easter Egg" because really,
    who would go to the trouble of recreating the world to live in the hardship and unclean drudgery of a peasant?

    Geff had gotten his bearings several moments ago and had come to realize what a terrible and boring mistake it
    was to allow the machine to pick his character. He knew he should be heading inside to give his mother the
    thirty copper he made this week with the eggs he had gathered moments ago from the chickens, but he didnt want
    to play this game. He knew he would go inside, hand over the eggs and coins, eat dinner, go to bed, wake up at
    4:30, walk to work, work till nightfall, and repeat.

    On top of this he was pretty close to dumb and didn't like the feeling. Geff wanted to express his frustration
    but no longer could think of anything to do other than split wood for the fire or have sex, though he didn't
    have a girlfriend and wasn't interested in the animals.

    Geff's mind went blank while trying to figure out a way to pick a new character like a gladiator or druid,
    something fun. While Geff was staring into space a daydream started and Geff was assaulted with images which
    were surprising and confusing. He tried to stop it, to break away from the trance he was drawn into, but more
    and more it became like a video as a story unfurled itself in his mind.

    At first he saw a man. In most images he seemed plain, and initially, he faded into the various scenes as a
    background character, sometimes he was a woman or even an animal. The odd thing was that the person Geff saw
    was never the same, always shorter, fatter, taller, hairier or more muscular, thinner, sometimes raggedy. Even
    a child or elderly in some images. Geff could always recognize him somehow, it was odd. Initially Geff thought
    he was imagining himself as a druid or wizard as the features of himself and this man were somewhat similar in
    many of the images. Then Geff saw the man standing over another man who was kneeling and pleading. The
    following image was the same but the kneeling man was lying on the ground dead, desiccated somehow. The
    standing man looked bigger, more muscular, and full of life.

    Horrified, this was the first Geff noticed that he wasn't in control of this movie and tried to stop it. It
    only sped up and became short movies instead of images.

    Geff watched helplessly. He saw the man, this time as a woman, stand over a frail sick person on their death
    bed, there was a golden glow that went from the woman into the dying person. Somehow Geff knew the others
    gathered near the bed couldn't see it. The woman left but the focus lingered on the sick person. The next
    scene showed the person well and sitting in a chair with their grandchildren around.

    The next scene was of the man in a crowd, someone brushed by and suddenly the mans coin purse was gone. It was
    stolen by a greedy thief who had a job, food, and no family to support. As the visions went on their intensity
    grew and Geff was now feeling the emotions of the scenes that played before him. Geff watched, incensed, as
    the man found the thief and Geff was, himself, wanting to deal out punishment.

    Watching the man find the thief and address him he could see the thief's energy and read the his history, it
    was a strange sensation to Geff, but instinctively he knew he could trust that there were no redeeming
    qualities in the thief. He was a bane on society, preying on the weak and manipulating the powerless. Geff
    could see the mysterious man knew the same and was eager to watch the thief die. Much to Geff's surprise,
    death didn't come and he could almost hear the conversation. A flash of light came from the man and struck the
    thief and the man grabbed his money and walked away. Geff knew the light was invisible to all but him and the
    man but the scene lingered on the thief. Slowly Geff saw that the thief was changing.

    It took several moments but the thief became a woman, no, a whore. Time passed in the video and Geff saw that
    the whore worked hard for their money. On top of being changed into a woman the thief could no longer steal,
    Geff saw her try, the money immediately became red hot in their hand and they dropped the coins, clutching
    their burned hand. Only money freely given could be grasped. Geff felt it was a worthy punishment but thought
    it was letting them off a little easy.

    Geff faded out of the trance slightly to realize that the man is just and powerful though he still didn't
    understand what was happening. The mental release was temporary as his mind was suddenly hurled back into the
    story of the strange man.

    There were several more scenes and with each one it seemed like his connection to the man was stronger, from
    each movie he could see deeper into the surrounding circumstances like he was there, knew the characters and
    could hear the conversations.

    The scene was dark he could hear whispers, giggles and some small chatter. As his eyes adjusted to the dark he
    could tell the man was in the form of an older teen or young man roughly Geff's age. There was a young girl
    with him. She seemed strangely familiar but Geff couldn't place from where, Geff's night vision was
    supernaturally enhanced in that he could now make out everything. The strange man, teen here, was getting it
    on with a girl he met in the town he was passing through. The man knew this was the girl he wanted, he (and
    Geff within the vision) could see she was healthy, as well as kind, good hearted, moral and just. The man
    wanted to bless her though Geff couldn't understand how having sex was blessing her, regardless of how good it
    was.

    Geff could see their aura's and could tell both were fully enjoying themselves. They both simultaneously
    climaxed and Geff saw a slight red glow from the man's groin enter the girl. The scene suddenly cut to the
    woman crying several days later, Geff knew the man had left and the girls heart was crushed.

    Unlike the normal "movies" this scene kept following this girl rather than jumping back to the man. The girl
    had met a new boy a week later and the relationship escalated quickly. The girl had sex with this man too and
    still had a red glow from her encounter with the strange man who had now seemed to have left the story. The
    woman's stomach began to grow and Geff realized that she was pregnant with the strange man's child.

    The vision responded to his recognition and zoomed past the pregnancy to the birth, the rebound man was there
    glowing with pride, apparently under the impression that the child was his. The child came out, a boy, and was
    glowing red. Again, Geff knew only he could see the color, some sort of indication of power.

    Glimpses of early childhood passed and started coming at blinding speed as time sped up, and Geff realized the
    truth of the situation. The images halted on a barn and slowly zoomed in. Within the barn the child of the man
    stood glowing in a radiant red aura, the untapped power practically kissing the rafters. At the heart of the
    overwhelmingly vibrant aura stood Geff.

    Geff was staring at himself from third person view, and just as he could read other people's auras from within
    the vision, he read his own. Geff saw he was the child of a god and himself immortal unless he chooses to die.
    Just as he had seen his father do, he could change form at will, or the form and minds of others. He could
    create, he could destroy, he could magically empower objects, he was unlimited.

    Geff reeled for a moment from the understanding. He could see his arms outstretched, head skywards, and his
    unseeing eyes milky white in the throws of the vision. Once he had fully understood and accepted his godhood
    the aura around him shrank and intensified, more and more till it was no longer red but white. Then even more
    until it wasnt a color, just blinding. Looking at himself was painful. There was some sort of explosion of
    power from within Geff which even pushed away the magical eye which Geff was viewing himself through. Geff's
    perspective was thrust out of the barn and into the woods and up into the air, hundreds of feet away, it kept
    rocketing back without loosing speed. The force of the explosion was magically equal to the sun but like an
    EMP burst only effects electronics, this didn't hurt his non magical surroundings.

    The vision Geff was under dissipated after his perspective was rocketed multiple days distance away and miles
    into the sky. Geff realized he was staring at the ceiling with his arms wide open like he was trying to give
    the sky a hug.
