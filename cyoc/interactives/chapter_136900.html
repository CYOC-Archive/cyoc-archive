When you're on someone's profile, it will show a full scale view of the person's body with a list of body parts and statistic that can be modified. Of corse, how much the user can edit depends on what kind of transformations the person chooses. The rarest chosen category is the chimera category.

    The more you transform people, the more points you gain and the more transformation options you unlock. For example, for the chimera category, you can unlock the ability to add non animal parts.     Stan felt a jabbing pain in his upper back, and he involuntarily hunched over a little bit. His back felt heavier as a growing mass worked its way out between his shoulder blades. At first, he thought it might be a dorsal fin, but it was much too wide. Then he thought it was a camel's hump, but started feeling very muscular, so that he could move it back and forth, just like his tail. The hump stretched out and became longer until it was a pole-like appendage several feet long. He moved the bulge to the right and, looking over at it, he noticed it was furry with big dark brown splotches.

    Instead of tapering to a point, the far end of the growth started to widen into what was clearly recognizable as a muzzle. It started to dawn on him that his "hump" was actually a very long furry neck. New nostrils opened, and he was suddenly assaulted by a range of new smells coming from behind him! Then he noticed the mouth and focused his concentration on it. He watched, wide-eyed as the jaw dropped open and a long purple tongue flopped out. It was a bizarre feeling--he could feel his human tongue in his human mouth, but he also felt his animal tongue sticking out of this new mouth. After some more careful concentration, he managed to lift the tongue back into his muzzle, which he then carefully closed.

    "Weird..." said his human mouth.

    Horns covered in soft fuzz sprouted from above the muzzle, and so did to long triangular ears. His hearing suddenly became more acute as his new pair of ears started moving around, scanning for sounds around him.

    At last he recognized clearly what grew out of him. It was now very obviously a giraffe's head and neck, with two new eyelids tightly closed. Building up his courage, he opened up the giraffe head's eyelids and was marveled at what he saw.

    He now had two distinct fields of view. The old one remained fixed over his right shoulder gazing at the giraffe head. The second was stationed on the giraffe head itself. Giraffes have eyes on either side their head, instead of the front, so Stan had a very wide field view up there. The giraffe head was still facing to his right, so that meant the "right" eye was pointing almost directly behind him while the "left" eye was facing ahead. Thus, with his four eyes, Stan was able to see almost anything in front of him, behind him, or to his right.

    Stan took several minutes just standing there getting acclimated to this before he finally began to experiment some more. He moved his big giraffe neck from the right side of his body to stretch straight out of his back. He now had two heads facing in directly opposite directions. He could see forward, left, and right. His ears twitched. His rat tail swished back and forth.

    Stan lifted both of his heads up and bumped his muzzle against the ceiling. Ouch, that hurt! Better tilt the giraffe head down...

    "Now I can see the ceiling and the floor at the same time!" he exclaimed, his giraffe ears tilting back toward the sound of his human voice.

    Stan wonder what would happen next to his body.
