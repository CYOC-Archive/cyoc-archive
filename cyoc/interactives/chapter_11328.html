Not only that, but Peter ran faster than the others, outscoring even the best player on the court by a long shot. When he got close to the hoop, Peter was able to jump considerably higher than the others, always finding the easy basket while hovering above the rest due to his three powerful legs.

    “Well, now you have something else you can change about Peter! You can grow him additional arms and legs whenever you want to!” Ty grinned back.

    “What are you going to do about Eric?”

    “Oh, let’s wait for the basketball game to be over. Eric is still too skinny and too short, don’t you think?” Ty asked Allen.

    “Well, it was fun, but…” Eric said in a sad tone of voice, while looking down at his third leg.

    “What? Oh just keep the leg, Eric! No big deal!” Peter assured him.

    “But my family is going to FLIP when they see me walking in the house with three legs, Peter! You gotta hide it or something. Can’t you make it disappear?” Eric wondered, feeling sad that he had to get rid of the extra leg.

I’ll make it so you can keep the leg as long as you want. If you don’t want it anymore, then just tell me and I’ll make it disappear! Have fun with it, okay?” Peter replied, while grabbing hold of Eric’s shoulders.
