    “Uhh… Dan… Danny?” the teacher stuttered, when she caught her first glimpse of the classroom filled with clones of the skinny, freckled red-head boy.

    “Yes, Miss Henderson” all the Danny’s said in unison.

    “What… How…what’s going on here?” the teacher stuttered, while showing a very confused look on her face. “Every single one of you looks like Danny!”

    The freckled boys just shrugged and giggled, as they turned around and stared at one another.

    “All… Alright…” the poor teacher continued, as she tried hardest to understand what had happened to her entire Kindergarten class. She had no clue that it was Ty, who changed all the kids into clones of Danny. “Danny, could you please raise your right hand?”

    Suddenly, every single Danny in the classroom raised their right hand and held it up as high as possible, while staring at the confused teacher with wide grins on their freckled faces.

    The teacher’s mouth just dropped wide open. She thought she had lost her senses completely, and Ty knew it!

    “Ex… Excuse me…” the poor lady said, as she stumbled towards the classroom door. “You can lower your hands now. I’ll be right back…”

    “Yes, Miss Henderson!” all the clones replied in choir.

    The poor teacher ran over to the Principal’s office, where she banged on Mister Johnson’s door.

    “Open up! PLEASE! I have a classroom full of kids, who all look exactly alike! One minute they were all playing around, as I turned to write something on the board. When I turned around, every single one of them looks like Danny!” she shouted at the Principal.

    “What? Let’s go look to see what this is all about!” Mr. Johnson said. The short, overweight man got up from his comfortable chair, which was behind his oversized desk, before he stumbled over to the door and walked down the hallway to the classroom where the strange transformation had taken place.

    Unknown to the teacher, Ty decided to quickly change everybody back to the way they were, before he had transformed every boy and girl into an exact likeness of the freckled Danny! Within moments, half the Danny’s changed back into girls, and the clothes transformed into the clothes the kids were wearing before Ty had changed them. All the boys changed back as well, except for the real Danny, of course!

    Finally, Mr. Johnson opened the classroom door, only to find all the kids neatly sitting at their desks, while they were patiently waiting for their confused teacher to return.

    “Oh my G… God… They… They’re all normal…” Ms. Henderson stuttered in shock, when she realized that all the kids just looked like normal, and there was only ONE Danny in the classroom!

    “Miss Henderson” the principal said calmly. “How about you just take leave for the rest of the morning, and I’ll just get a sub to fill in for your class? I think you’ve been working to hard and need a day off…”

    Mr. Johnson had another teacher supervise the classroom, while he sent Ms. Henderson home and called the School District Office so that they could send in a sub to cover the Kindergarten class for the rest of the day. Ty could understand all this, and turned to Danny after he heard everything.

    “Cool! We’re getting a Sub!” Ty cheered.

    “Yeah! That means we get to play games and do exactly whatever we want!” Danny chuckled. “That was a really cool trick you pulled!”

    “Oh, I got more tricks up my sleeve! Just wait till I show you how I can change some of my classmates!” Ty laughed in return.

    “Why didn’t I think of this before?” Ty thought to himself. “I’m going to have some fun during my day in Kindergarten!”

    With that said Ty turned around and looked for a kid to change…
