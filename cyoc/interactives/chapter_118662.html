Nate was still 12 years old, but he was big, tall and very muscular! However, he was very protective of Ashton, who was now in high school.

    Ty silently wished for Nate to grow even bigger and stronger when necessary, in case Ashton ever got bullied or if Ashton was in any kind of danger. Nate would always have the strength and the size to help out his older brother, no matter what happened.

    So, it happened the following day that Ashton got bullied at school while Nate was in middle school. One of the bigger kids threatened Ashton and demanded that Ashton come up with his lunch money. When Ashton refused, the big kid threatened to beat him up after school.

    When classes were out, Ashton was walking home from school, which would mean that he had to pass by Nate’s school anyway. Nate was about to meet up with his brother when Terry, the big bully, approached Ashton in front of Nate’s school.

    “So, where’s the money, you twerp?” Terry asked home.

    “I don’t have it! Get lost!” Ashton replied angrily.

    Terry became furious and he shoved Ashton to the ground, just as Nate arrived!

    “Hey! That’s my brother!” Nate shouted.

    Nate came running over to the sidewalk where Terry was standing. Terry gasped at the hulking size of Nate, who was 7 feet tall.

    “Who… Who are you?” Terry shouted. “Go away! Leave us alone!”

    “No! That’s my brother who is on the floor over there! I saw you push him!” Nate shouted as he walked up to Terry. He was very angry.

    As Nate walked up to Terry, he started to GROW! Very quickly, Nate grew even taller and more muscular!

    Nate’s legs and arms grew bigger and even more muscular, making the boy even TALLER than he already was! His chest bulged out even more and his entire torso widened until he was as big as a brick wall!

    When Nate stood before Terry, he was like a giant! Terry was literally staring right into Nate’s massive abdominal muscles.

    “Uhh… Holy crap! You’re a giant!” Terry gulped as he slowly backed off while Nate walked closer to him.

    “Go pick on somebody your own size!” Nate growled, towering over Terry by more than two feet! “I’m Ashton’s brother! You mess with him… You gotta answer to ME!”

    “I’m outta here!” Terry shouted, as he turned around and ran away.

    Nate then turned around and picked up Ashton, who was just gawking at his gigantic, younger brother.

    “Uhh… Thanks, big bro!” Ashton smiled, as Nate easily picked him up and carried him home in his big, hulking arms.

    “No problem, Ash…. That’s what brothers are for…” Nate smiled, as he easily carried his brother home. “I’ll protect you!”

    Ashton began to feel happy about his big, hulking “little brother”, who wasn’t so little anymore…
