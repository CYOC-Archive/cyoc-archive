    Upon seeing this he wandered towards it and opened the door.
    As he did this the room seemed much much bigger. He took a step out of the house and looked at it from the outside, however he arrived at the same conclusion the space inside the hut was much bigger.
    He entered deeper inside the hit and looked at the various potions and racks.
    At that in stand he heard the leaves and twigs snapping outside indicating that someone was getting closer.
