    here."

    Tanuki stared into the other boys' eyes for a long time, then he sighed. "Fine.
    You see, my mom died in a car accident last month."

    "Oh my God," Lago whispered.

    "I'm sorry," Jake added.

    "No, it's nothing anyone could prevent. But my stepdad, well, let's just say he
    never cared for me. Him and my stepbrother have been cruel to me ever since;
    I had to get away. I saw the ad for this job, but I didn't realize what it was until I
    was already on the boat. By then, I didn't care what I had to do, as long as I
    was far away from home as possible."

    "Well, don't worry, you've got friends here," Jake told him.

    "You guys really want to be friends with a depressing guy like me?"

    "You're not depressing," Lago assured him, "Just hard lucked. Of course we
    want to be friends with you."

    "Gee, thanks! But, I have to ask, why are you guys here?" Tanuki asked.

    "Well, I'm gay and this is the only place where a minor is allowed to have sex

    But first, Brer, how about a round for the boys?"

    The bear behind the bar brewed up a batch of brown drinks and passed them
    out amongst the waiters. Ozzy took a different drink and downed it in a few
    gulps. The boys were more hesitant; the drinks were strong and burned a bit on

    another would be assigned as their relief if they needed a break or were asked
    to provide "entertainment". Patrons could either pay for each item at a time, or
    set up a tab which would be paid in full and checked off before they could
    leave. As far as the other part of the job went, the waiters were required to
    either accept or decline politely. If they accepted, they would check in with Brer

    Otherwise, your table assignments are on the bar. And feel free to drink up, just
    don't get so drunk you can't work. Otherwise you'll be on janitorial duties."

    second row filled up quickly as well. Within 15 minutes the whole club was full
