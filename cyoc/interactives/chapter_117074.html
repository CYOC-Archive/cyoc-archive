    You rode on Tiamat holding on using your claws, and you continued until you made it to a room that was full of other dragons. You were shocked about the variety of types too, especially there were eastern dragons, wyvern dragons, serpent dragons, and western dragons like you. "As you see I can have a variety of dragons in terms of types, it's what I'm known for. Also you will be learning proper draconic in time my dear, but thankfully all of my intelligent children have known English for at least 15 years now, but sometimes they struggle so help them if you can. I will be nearby just in case there are any misunderstandings, so don't worry." Tiamat said

    Tiamat approached a small group of children and they greeted her in a weird language that you never heard before, and she responded in the same language speaking in sentences almost. "There we are, I told them that you are a prodigy and about your ring. Specifically that you were just barely less intelligent then they are, but your English is better then theirs is, now climb down and meet them." Tiamat said laying down and her stomach and stretching out one of her wings.

    You struggled a bit climbing down, but made it to the ground without stumbling. You looked over to see six dragons walk over to you. There was two male wyverns that looked like twins, a female flying serpent dragon, a female western dragon, a male western dragon, and a female eastern dragon.

    The dragons all gathered around and sat down near you and looked at you happily, and the serpent dragon coiled herself like a snake right beside you. You felt shy, and nervous with all of the attention you were getting, and fell silent. Just then serpent dragon right next to you spoke up and said,"Hello there sister, I'm Niralia what's your name? Uh, did I speak that correctly?" The serpent Niralia asked (she has yellow scales)

    "Oh, I'm Sirica, sorry I just feel a bit shy right now. I just feel overwhelmed at the moment. You also did say everything correctly up until you said "speak" unless it's formal use the word "say" when you ask that question." You said

    "Oh wow, mother was right about you. So young, but here you are talking in complete sentences, and using English too. I'm Ailin by the way, nice to meet you sister." The female eastern dragon, Ailin said (she has red scales)

    "I'm Brakix," one of the wyvern twins, Branix said (both twins have green scales)

    "I'm Granix, and it's obvious that me and my brother here are identical twins." The other twin, Granix said

    "I'm Higon, I'm eager to learn just how smart you are compared to us actually. Not to cause stress for you of course." The male western dragon, Higon said (black scales)

    "Finally, I'm Vala, If you would like any advice on draconic come to me, and maybe I will come to you when it comes to using English contextually." The female western dragon, Vala said (purple scales)

    "Well, It's nice to meet all of you. I honestly know next to nothing besides my English, so thanks." You said

    "Heh, well you do have that going for you, and in my case that's saying a lot." Higon said

    "Okay seriously, I can do without all of the praise. I know I'm smart and all, but as I said my English is all I have right now. Besides that everything is still kind of confusing for me, especially considering I just hatched." You said

    "That's okay Sirica, we can get to know each other later." Brakix said

    "Definitely, you can just meet up anytime for advice too if you'd like." Granix said

    The two twins looked at each other and then back to you and said,"Goodbye for now," and walked away.

    "I guess I'll go too then, c'mon Higon." Vala said

    "I'll stay here with you Sirica, wait Ailin so you still have that English story that mother gave us?" Niralia said

    "Of course, we can't read that well in English though. I doubt Sirica can either, because she just hatched and all." Ailin said

    "Actually I'm quite fluent in English both verbally and contextually, so I can read it just fine. You can ask mom if you want and she'll say the same thing." You said

    "Really? That would be a big help for us. But it's pretty long, so are you sure about it?" Ailin asked

    "Yep, I really have nothing much to do otherwise." You said

    "Well if that's the case then we can just relax here." Niralia said coiling herself around you, and placing her head next to yours.

    "Alright, give me a moment real quick." Ailin said and she suddenly vanished, and reappeared seconds later with a book.

    "Here we are, now we can begin whenever you like sister." Ailin said lying down next to you and Niralia, and opening a book similar to a large chapter book with at least 200 pages to it.
