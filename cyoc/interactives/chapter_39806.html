They all liquified. The Jen clones vanished. "Crap! Water clones nya! I had to switch to Sharingan nya! Better mix 'em." 1 of her eyes changed to Byakugan. A giant matal fist came from nowhere and flew at Jen. "AHHHHHHHH!!!!" The fist stopped short. "Huh nya?"

    "Is it safe for water nya?" asked Jen.
    "Yeah why?" asked Domon.
    "Tora, ushi, tora, ushi, saru, saru, u, hitsuji, hitsuji, i, tori, saru, saru, tori, tori, tori, hitsuji, hitsuji, hitsuji, uma, ushi, uma! Water Style! Giant Vortex Jutsu!" A vortex of water appeared and washed the dirty gundam.

    "The corelander can stretch to fit another pod. I'll prepare it. Then I'll inspect the new gundam." said Rain.
    3 hours later...
    "Well, I'm done!" said Rain.
    "How's my new gundam nya?" asked Jen. She was breathing heavily.
    "It's very strange. There's no battle data, everythings been erased, including attacks." said Rain.
    "So it won't run?" said Domon.
    "Unlike when you first got the Burning Gundam, this one will run even without battle data. It's strange though, as if it's never been used before. Not only that, it's short for a gundam. Well, all I need to do is customize it and program some attacks into the computer. Any preferences Jen?" asked Rain.

    "I can't nya. I used too much... chakra... fighting nya. Naruto, I... can't... fly... anymore nya." She fell from the sky. Naruto jumped and caught her.

    "Yeah... just... exausted nya." said Jen. Naruto put her in the backpack and Jen slipped her tail into the pot. She fell asleep.
    "Well, we better be more careful now that Jen is asleep. Let's get moving!" said Naruto. Rain put the new gundam in a pod on the corelander and they took off.
