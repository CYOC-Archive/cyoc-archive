If you shouted a command in a noisy building like this, only some of the people would fall under your control, while people further away would be unaffected, and potentially run away and call the authorities if they see suspicious behavior.

You certainly couldn't have someone bowing in front of you kissing your feet right in the middle of this crowded mall. Depending on what you wish to do with your subject, you may have to lure them into a private location with you.
