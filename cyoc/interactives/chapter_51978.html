    Ami looked over a long piece of parchment showing the wolves and pride and passengers,
    vertical lines of kanji written down, "In this timeline the passengers are not completely
    cursed of the insanity Kurse left behind, and a lingering evil perhaps, which destroys the
    whole point of everything we've endured to reach this far. And there seems to be some
    odd something trying to kill the newborn Glass whose still insane, and thus kill her
    original self in the process."

    Amy shook her head, "I think that's just Kurse's shade trying to cheat her way to create
    MORE conflict. Trying to create NEW spawn of chaos, selfishness or what have you the
    pack or pride or ourselves would have to deal with... .... that timeline needs to be cut off
    now, or we'll simply continue in a downward spiral of conflicts until our will to move
    foreword is completely destroyed by the concept we'll have nothing to look foreword to
    other than a never ending stream of conflicts."

    Ami slapped her forehead, "And there's also the fact that Glass in this timeline was made
    from a completely different person. That's going to be nearly impossible to reconcile."

    Amy looked over her shoulder, "Though it does provide a way for Glass' mother and her
    possible new male companion to have a 'relatively normal' life where Glass isn't like a
    unnatural being among her own family."

    Ami shook her head at her other self, "We both known that there is more to family than
    just genetics. Her mother loves her, and so will her new father eventually... turning them
    into glass wolves like her is not the way."

    Amy nodded, "I understand. But what about the timeline reality where Trevor reunites with
    his family and accidently transforms them?"

    "That is sadly an inevitability, now that Trevor is finding the courage to finally return to his
    family, the rules of his transform say that once he interacts with someone he loves, and
    who loves him back, they will change, the transform failed to ever say what KIND of love,
    and we both know Amy, there is more than one type of love. You never had siblings, but I
    have."

    "Okay, granted, what of the one where it'll fall upon Glass' shoulders to fully repair the
    unbalance between order and chaos and the souls of the twins being born through their
    'parent/original'?' "

    "The only -real- problem is that is very simply Lawrence and Opal, who are male, and
    spirits of mortals can not ride on seed into their new mother."

    "... Another solution will have to be then?"

    "it may in fact solve another problem that has existed for Ulysses since he was brought
    into the pack... but weather it causes more problems will be for later perhaps. Then again,
    with the Curse's days now number as it is now just a mindless spell, they'll have plenty of
    time to turn to real life problems."
