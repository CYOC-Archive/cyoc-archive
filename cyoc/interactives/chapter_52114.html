    "Wait! Not yet, Jen!" Naomi yelled.

    "What?" the porcine Jen sniffled. "I don't want to be like this for much longer."

    "But I haven't told you what I want to do yet, you stupid blond." Naomi fumed. In her anger she was letting time slip by.

    "Hey! Don't call me stupid. I'm not stupid." The one thing that Jen hated most in the world was being called stupid.

    "But now I have to think quick. I thought that we were going to decide if I get transform of dare. Then what you would give me and then you would ask." Naomi was not calming down. In fact she was becoming more panicked as time seemed to run out.

    "But I don't want to be a pig, Naomi!"

    "And I want to help. But not before I get what I want. God, you really are a moron sometimes. Maybe when I get the power of the curse I won't turn you back." Naomi was smugly turning her back on Jen even while her anger filled mind raced to decide between the two options.

    "Hey! If you don't turn me back then I will change you into something nasty."

    "You only get to do that if I pick that option." Naomi was gloating now. She was going to pick dare then Jen would have to do something simple to get turned back to normal.

    "That's not true. If you don't pick soon then you get both."

    In an instant as the words sunk in the power had shifted from Naomi offering to help a friend to Jen. How long had it been? Naomi's eyes widened as she realized that she had been mean to an emotional woman that was part pig and all pissed off.

    "Jen, please, I'm sorry," Naomi started. "I'll help you just calm down. Please, we're friends."

    Jen snorted in a very piggy way. "Well, Naomi, it's too late, you get both now. So who is the real moron?"

    Naomi was afraid. "I so sorry, Jen. But you know what they say: whatever you call me bounces of me and sticks to you?"

    The pig-Jen paused for a moment in thought. "You're right, Naomi." And she laughed a little wicked laugh that was terrible to hear with a piggy squeal at the end. "For your dare I dare you to transform me back to a normal human girl. And for your transformation you just gave me a good idea. Naomi you will transform to become a rubber girl."
