    You walked around the mall a little bit more carrying your book around. However, none of the other stores seemed to impress you enough
    to look around. You then finally decided to leave the mall and run back home, you ran faster than usual since dark clouds were in the sky,
    signaling that there was a chance of rain.

    When you arrived inside your house you took the book out of the bag and set it on the table. You played video games for about an hour
    when you then decided to read the book. You were surprised beyond belief after you read the inside of the cover. It was different from
    when you read it in the bookstore. You continued to read through the pages as you became more and more shocked with every page you
    turned. From what you've read so far, you can only assume that this book is intended to be a spell book. Thoughts kept running through
    your mind saying that it was impossible. That there was no chance that magic even existed, but something inside you encouraged you to
    at least try it out. In a weird sort of way, you kinda wondered what it would be like to be younger again.

    You turned back to the first page, where the most basic spells were. You found what you though was the most basic, easiest spell on the
    page. It allowed you to look at a photograph of yourself and have the picture turn into a kid version. The spell did nothing to the real you
    whatsoever. You read the instructions carefully as you were now ready to perform it, if it was real. You walked up to a shelf and grabbed
    the picture of your senior photo. You then looked back in the book and began to cast the spell.


    In the photo I hold that shows only me

    Will now be changed so that I can see

    The childish version will be viewed as pleased

    Let the photo change and be at ease

    Youthica Photo Manipla



    You then smiled with amazement as you saw the picture starting to change. The hair grew into a completely different haircut. The whole
    body shrank as it was clear that it was becoming more childish. The facial features and other things were now changing making the photo
    of you now a kid, maybe at the age of seven. These spells were actually real.

    You undid the spell and you thought of what to do next. You were a little frightened though. These spells could be too powerful for you to
    handle. Would you take the risk? Another thought popped into your head though, you could call your girlfriend over to help you. She could
    help you cast the spells and fix anything that went wrong. She would be excited about this magic stuff. Although you could call your
    brother Josh. He always seemed to be the kind of guy that wanted to go back to "the good ol' days."

    What do you do now?
