She looked down and saw that she wasn't wearing any clothes at all. One of the men came over and poured some water over her. It wasn't enough to fully relieve the tightness of her skin but it did provide some relief.

    Darwin found himself on the plains with no clothes on. He looked around but couldn't see anyone. He decided to try and find the others. Unfortunately he didn't get far before he hit what felt like a wall. "Damn!" he cursed at himself. "I should have known that I wouldn't be able to move far. I guess I'm stuck here until my turn. Since Angel's turn is being skipped this round I should be transported back at any moment."

    Ester landed in the forest and her clothes were gone. She saw Angel fingering herself and started to finger herself as well. She knew that both of them were going to be too busy to roll the dice. She wondered how she had physically ended up in the forest. She was glad that she was though as she could think clearly again.

    Micha found himself/herself in the Desert Sultan's oasis palace. He/she didn't know how he/she knew that but he/she did. He/she looked down at himself/herself and found that he/she was wearing different clothes. He/she was wearing a tan silk dress, silk underwear, and silk lined sandals that were probably made with very expensive leather. He/she looked around and noticed that there were two guards with wicked looking scimitars standing a few paces behind him/her. He/she guessed that they were his/her bodyguards. This didn't give Micha any confidence at all.

    Darwin saw the light and felt the tug again. When he landed he was back in the apartment with the game board. There was another card sticking out and he picked it up reading aloud "Each player that is in the world will be able to hear the one who's at the board speaking." He looked at the pawns and said "Well that makes things a little easier. We should try to get into a group again. How do we do that though when three of us are trapped in different areas? I guess we should try to rescue Kari and maybe see if we can free Micha too. I could probably let Richie cohabitate with me. He doesn't seem to be that far from me." Suddenly Kari's pawn moved a five spaces to the south-west. If Kari kept going in that direction she would be in the desert and maybe the oasis that Micha found.

I'm going to roll now and hope that something good happens." He picks up the dice and takes a deep breath. He briefly wondered if the pheromones had worn off of Angel since her turn was skipped. "Well, here goes nothing" he said as he rolled the dice.
