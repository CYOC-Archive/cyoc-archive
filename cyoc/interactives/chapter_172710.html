    Do witches dream of plush ferrets?

    added by TransforMarv 2 years ago A AR BM I TG O Female Anthro Magic
    Author note:
    I feel like my habit of trying to include as many options as possible has reached its logical conclusion...

    ...sitting in the middle of a large, open field. In any direction you look there's only endless gras and a light blue sky without clouds. Or a sun, for that matter. Looking down you realize you're completely naked, but somehow it doesn't bother you when you feel like it should. As you start to wonder how it can still be so bright and warm without a sun, you notice another person sitting right beside you. Weird, have they been here the entire time?

    It is another small girl, only about four inches smaller than you, with freckles and red hair. In fact, the exact same shade your hair has. Then realization hits you, that's how you looked like before Tommy became your familiar. But then how is this possible? Does it mean this here is a dream? Technically this is your first proper sleep since becoming magical.

    You ask the other you what's going on, but instead of answering she seems to focus on something behind you. Turning around you see two more people approach, which you recognize a bit faster this time. The first one is a red-haired woman, the first form you took after Marisa gave you her magic. While it has been six months since then, it still feels not that long ago to you. The other is a young girl, but with several features of a ferret like paws, ears and tail, your anthro form, so to speak, from when you put on that (ac)cursed nightgown. None of them say anything, they just stare at you with serene smiles. Looking down you notice two ferrets on the ground before you. One was staring at you with the same eyes as you, the other was an unmoving plush animal.

    These are all the forms your body has taken since you have met Marisa. It’s weird to see them lined up like that. And this should've been it, but more started appearing and none are forms you’re familiar with. Instead each one seems to be a different mix of the forms you have taken. An adult woman with anthro features, a human-sized ferret, inanimate plush children in both human- and doll-size, a plush ferret that could actually move. They all line up with the others and soon form a giant circle around you. At once they all start to hold out their hands (and paws) out to you. Are you supposed to take one? This dream is getting weirder by the minute.

    Speaking of dreams, the blue sky has vanished, replaced by a white nothing. The green field also seems to be getting smaller and its edges are coming closer fast. Either whatever is causing this wants you to decide quickly or it's a sign of the dream ending. You look over the other yous again. They haven't moved from their spots. You would like to take one of their hands, but based on your experiences with magic this could very well be another, quite literally, life-changing decision. The edges are approaching faster and faster. Quickly you decide to take the hand of…

    What do you do now?
