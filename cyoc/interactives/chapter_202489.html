    A doggy vacation

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Kingdom Come A year ago A

    "Amy, you have a very odd problem. Lately, whenever you get stressed, you start to think that you are a dog. You start forgetting that you are a human woman, and instead believe that you are only a dog pretending to be a human. You've never told anyone about this delusion, and are embarrassed by the strange urges that have been coming over you. You've acted like a perfect human, but you've wondered what would happen if you actually embraced your new instincts. You've been very stressed lately, and now it's time to try something different. You asked me to hypnotize you so that I could help you with this problem. Now that you're in a trance, your doggy thoughts are even stronger than ever. When I snap my fingers, all of your human thoughts and worries are going to get sucked into this coin. In response, your dog instincts are going to rise to the surface and overwhelm you. Without your human memories, you are going to become convinced that you are a dog, and will act accordingly. You will do a perfect impression of a dog. You will only act like a dog, and will not act like a human. You will not be embarrassed by your actions, because all of your embarrassment is stuck in the coin. You will forget that you are my mother, and will instead think you are my pet. You will stay in that state until I snap my fingers again. When that happens, your human memories will return, and your dog thoughts will go back to your subconscious. You will not be mad about the experience, and will actually have enjoyed it. It will help relieve your stress, and you won't question your behavior while in that altered state of mind. You won't remember fully what you did, and instead view it as a perfectly harmless form of escapism. You will even ask me to hypnotize you again whenever the stress gets too hard to bear. I know that's a lot to process, so why don't you describe what I just said."

    "Lately, I've been getting over stressed, and I've started to view myself as a hardworking dog, rather than an actual human being. I know that's ridiculous, but I've started to wonder what would happen if I embraced that fantasy. Now that I'm hypnotized, I actually have a chance to do it. When you snap your fingers, I will lose all my human inhibitions, and will replace them with the instincts of a dog. I will forget my previous human life, and will become convinced that I am your pet dog. I will stay in that blissful state until you snap your fingers again. When you do, I will remember that I am human again, but I will not be mad at you. Instead, I will enjoy my time as a dog, even if I won't remember exactly what happened. It will be relaxing, and I'd like to do it again if I ever get more stressed."

    "That's perfect Amy. Now.. wake up!" *Snap*
