    Peter was a young man in love with a beautiful woman named Rachel.

    They'd known eachother for years, and had always been good friends.

    He was finally going to pop the question, but first he needed a ring, which brought him to
    this store.

    "How may I help you?" the old man asked.

    "I'm looking for a very special ring for a very special lady." Peter replied.

    "Ah," said the old man "I see. You should know I take heart matters very seriously. I think I
    may have just the thing."

    He pulled out a glass box of 12 rings, each one with a different animal symbol on them. A
    rat, an ox, a tiger, a rabbit, a dragon, a snake, a horse, a sheep, a monkey, a chicken, a
    dog, and a pig.

    "Are you familiar with the Chinese Zodiac?" The old man asked.

    "Yes." Peter replied.

    "Then do you know what year your lady was born in?" The old man asked again.

    "I should hope so!" He said.

    "Then just pick the ring with her animal on it" the old man said."These rings promise to
    bring any lady and her groom-to-be a life-time of happiness."

    "How much are they?" Peter asked.

    "Normally, each ring costs $50," the old man replied "but since I sense purity in your heart,
    I'll only charge you $10."

    "Thanks." Peter said.
