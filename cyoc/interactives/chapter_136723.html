    There were a slew of children in the front seats of the audience, and they began to shout:

    "Turn them into dancing bears!"

    "Make'em teddy bears!"

    "Make them ballerinas!"

    "Turn them little!"

    "Well, the audience has spoke, and I shall oblige with your wishes my dear audience!" The ring master looked to the hypnotized couple.

    "Tayla and Jared, listen to them very carefully, everything I say to you is true, no ifs, ands, or buts... It simply is. There is nothing to worry about, nothing to think about, let my voice become your thoughts, let what I tell you become reality. Do not be afraid, do not worry, don't fret, there is nothing to be scared of. Be happy. Everything is going to be fine.

    "Jared, your name is now Jayla, Tayla and Jayla, what a cute name, you two must be twins, identical twins. But that must mean you're a girl, right Jayla? Yes, yes it does. Both of you are so cute, utterly cute and small for our audience. And you know what's cute right? Stuffed animals! But you're obviously alive, so you're LIVING stuffed animals. A couple of cute little teddy bear sisters! It's so cute when our teddy bear sisters dance for the audience, isn't that right? Yes it is. You WANT to dance for our audience, it's what makes you happy, it's what makes you fulfilled."

    Tayla, and 'Jared' (Jayla), grinned wider and wider at the ring master's words, happy woolie thoughts filling their heads as he spoke. They were teddy bears. They were sisters. They wanted to dance for the audience, oh so badly! It was what they wanted more than anything in the wrong!

    "And when I clap my hands three times, you will no longer be in my power and you will act according to your own wills, but everything I've said will be the truth and your undeniable reality. Do you understand? Yes? Good." The ring master clapped my hands three times.

    A purple smoke bomb went off. When it cleared, there were now two little woolly brown teddy bears where Tayla and Jared had been... They were dressed in identical looking purple ballerina dresses (with a hole for their round stubby tails) and ballet shoes, with purple bows around their necks. Beady glass black eyes stared out at the ring master. Only their little brass name tags could really tell them apart.

    "There we go, you two are teddy bear ballerina dancing twins! Now how about you give the audience a dance!"

    Totally of their own locomotion, the two teddy bears obey, in spite of who they used to be not knowing much about ballet, the two teddy bears twirled and skipped across the center ring with grace that you wouldn't have expected from the little living playthings. Music from Swan Lake began to play from somewhere. The audience was complete in awe of the feat, their eyes completely glued on the sight. One of the teddy bears tossed her sister into the air, who did a couple majestic poses, and was caught just as gracefully! The two teddy bear sisters faced their audience and bowed, paws clasped together.

    "Ladies and gentleman, give it for our new star performers, Tayla and Jayla!"

    The crowed clapped wildly. Tayla and Jayla felt their cotton hearts swill with pride at the applauds, the sisters resisting the urge to look at each other satisfied at a performance well done.

    "Now, I leave their destiny in your hands my audience. Shall take I restore these cute little teddy bear sisters to the humans they once were? They seem rather happy if you ask me. But if you wish me to restore them, then I shall do so. Otherwise, Tayla and Jayla have a very happy life ahead of them!"
