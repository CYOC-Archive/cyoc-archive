    The Corrupter glowed with dark pleasure as it felt the temporary restraints that mortal, Jim had placed upon him, disappear. The Corrupter knew that it was only a matter of time lost control and would no longer be able to contain him. Even still, the speed with which Sister Mary had acted to free him deserved a proper reward.

    In the act of releasing him from his fleshly prison, the Corrupter gave his seed to her and like the whore she truly was, she drank freely. It was not taking long at all for the seed to sprout, to grow until it totally melded with her. She would not be a human possessed by a demon but a demon entirely as fitting for his consort. She would be queen for her loyalty when he was yet weak.

    Magnificent black wings grew from her back and a long, barbed, whip like tail grew until it was over four feet long. She grew taller until she was a little over seven feet tall and yet she did not equal any part of the Corrupter's vessel. Jealousy, Hate, and Greed flooded her already wicked soul. She only used these motivations to strengthen her desire to serve and pleasure the Corrupter. For she also had the fear that just as easily as she had been given it could be taken away.

    The Corrupter looked around the room and stopped his pleasuring with his consort. He was back to his majestic height of 8' and his tail and wings were back. His skin was the color of freshly spilled blood which covered heavily muscled skin. "What kind of Queen would you be if you didn't have servants? Bring the other girls to me and they shall become our slaves."

    Consort Mary liked the idea very much and after one last lingering look at her King and God, she went and to go fetch Tania and Jessica.
