    "Okay, Courtney. You have had your fun. Now wish us back to our old bodies, " said Ty nervously.

    "In a minute, in a minute. I mean, you had this power your whole life and I have only had it for a short while. It hardly seems fair to give it back so soon, " mused Courtney thoughtfully.

    Courtney looked up at him with a strange smile. "You know, it's really weird being the younger sister. I don't think I like it. In fact, I wish that I was the older ...."

    "Not anymore. At least not tonight. Unless you want Mom and Dad to think you are crazy and lock you up in a nuthouse. See ya at home, " said Courtney as she raced off.
