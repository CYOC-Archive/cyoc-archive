Eventually, with Dustin kicking mud at him and laughing, the three of his tormentors left. Mark to the bar to watch the game with his buddies. June to go do the shopping and fool around with a coworker in secret. Dustin had gone for his ritual wandering around town.

Nobody had, their suburban home was typical and forgettable on the street and besides the rattle of a lawnmower a few houses down, it was typically dead. Breathing a sigh of relief, Ty wondered some more about his current issues. If he could change anything, did that mean himself too?

Sighing heavily, he began to cut the grass, exerting his lean and weak body against the mower and the grass. Slowly, halfway down the first stretch of yard, he noticed his arms and legs felt less strained. Looking over himself in disbelief, his skinny arms had a bit more shape to them, though hardly much.

His scrawny legs now had firm definition, his flaccid arms now had small biceps when he flexed, and his chest and abs were even more pronounced. Watching in amazement, barely feeling anything at all, he watched himself steadily but slowly build physique, and felt hot excitement rush over his once typically nerdy form.

When his waistband began to pull painfully and his shirt was stretched over handsome pecs, Ty gulped and stopped his strengthening, not wanting to get overly big. He quickly finished the lawn, raking the trimmings with ease, and even though he could have simply willed it, the euphoric feeling of being powerful and confident in his abilities was a huge satisfaction.

He was certainly defined and masculine, with runner's legs and a proud broad chest. He smiled at his pathetically tight shorts and shirt, and decided that while he liked himself big this way, it would be very noticible if suddenly the little nerdy kid had blown up overnight. He reduced himself to a firm but still strengthened version of his former self and felt relief as his strained clothes loosened again.

What would have taken him hours to do normally was only twenty minutes to do. That made him smile wider, and he found himself wondering what it would be like to go around town on a warm Saturday with godlike powers. He washed up, relishing a warm shower, and got dressed. But he didn't like his clothes, which were nerdy and consisting of too much plaid, so he changed them into typical cargo pants and a loose band t-shirt. Satisfied, he headed out. He had to be incognito after all.
