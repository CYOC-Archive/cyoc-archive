    Jeff sighed, pinching the bridge of his nose as he looked over the trio. At the rate things were going he would inadvertently create a new first grade class.

    In the course of an hour, Jeff had transformed three of the most intimidating teachers he knew into first graders. Ryan, the energetic and friendly boy. Tommy, Jeffs shy and sweet natured adopted brother. And Jane, a spunky mother hen of a girl who seemed to look out for both her friends. While thankful to not have his former teachers breathing down his neck, he wondered if suddenly being the children's caretaker was much better.

    Returning to the lost and found, Jeff now began searching for clothes for both Tommy and Jane. Both children were still swamped in what remained of their adult clothes, namely tops that hung on them like dresses, brushing the children's shins as they padded through the school. As he glanced over the discarded items, he was surprised as Jane now gently tugged on his arm.

    "Can you put my hair in ponytails," she asked. Jeff glanced at the girl his eyebrow arching at her request. "You mean a ponytail?"

    "No ponytails," Jane stated, the girl now taking her long chocolate hair and bringing it to the side. Jeff nodded in understanding, realizing the girl wanted a pair of pigtails on the side of her hair. "Okay Jane one sec," he said. A quick search of the area led Jeff to see pair of rubber bands. Separating the girls hair into equal halves, he now tied a pair of bushy pigtails on the side of Janes head. "Okay what do you think?"

    Jane now padded towards a full length mirror, Jeff wondering if the adult turned child would react to her diminished appearance. Instead the brunette girl turned to Jeff, flashing a toothy grin as she gave him two thumbs up.

    "Jeff," Tommy asked meekly. "Do you see any clothes we can wear?"

    Jeff frowned, the options were certainly limited at this point. Most of the clothes left were stained. He couldn't even find shoes for Tommy and Jane at this point.

    "Too bad the Chronovac can't make clothes," Ryan stated. Jeff paused, Ryan may have inadvertently given him an idea. "You three have a seat," he said, motioning for the children to sit on a bench. "I'll be right back."

    Quickly stepping into the office, Jeff now retrieved the former adults clothing. Jeff had not known what to do with their former adult clothes, simply stuffing them into a locker before leaving. Pulling the clothing out, Jeff not neatly laid it on a desk as he quickly began scanning through the Chronovac app.

    "I'll be damn," he whispered. "There is a clothing option." Bringing up a menu, Jeff now punched in some information before pressing a button. Holding his breath, he now watched as a beam of blue light washed over the clothing.

    Jeff was astonished, watching as the massive clothing of Ryan and Jeff now began to shrink. The polo shirt and khakis of Ryan, quivering as they grew smaller. The material transforming into a t-shirt with a superhero on it while his khakis morphed into a pair of sturdy blue jeans. Even the man's shoes, socks, and boxers changed. His sneakers becoming a pair of light up velcro sneakers while the boxers transformed into a pair of briefs with cartoon characters.

    Ryan's clothing was similar. The windbreaker shrinking, the material transforming into cotton as it became a long sleeved tshirt with a dinosaur on it. Ryan's pants also shrinking, transforming into a pair of jeans like Ryan's. A small chuckle now escaped Jeffs lips as he noticed the man's boxers and sneakers transform into the exact same pair that Ryan had. Apparently the boys had similar tastes in regards to shoes and underroos.

    Janes clothing also underwent a transformation. Jeff unsure what the womans skirt, heels and nylons would become. To Jeffs surprise, he now watched as the skirt shuddered, expanding slightly as the color lightened into a sky blue knee length dress. As this was occurring, Jeff watched as Janes heels and nylons shrank and twisted becoming a pair of light blue sneakers with pink socks. Even Janes former underwear underwent a transformation. The silk panties shrinking, the material becoming a sturdy cotton that was adorned with unicorns. Satisfied with his work, Jeff now picked up the children's clothing and returning to the kids. "Look what I found."

    "Our clothes," the kids exclaimed happily. Jeff now handed the excited first graders their outfits, telling them to change quickly. In only moments Jeff beheld is qork and smiled.

    The trio was now indistinguishable from any first graders. The clothing they wore fitting them as if their parents had dressed them that morning. "Okay you three," he stated. "Lets get out of here."

    As Jeff now led the three first graders out, he wondered what exactly his next move should be. He could try leaving the school to figure things out or try dropping the kids of in the first grade classrooms across the school. Considering how his luck was going. Jeff wondered if he would be adding any more children to this growing first grade class.
