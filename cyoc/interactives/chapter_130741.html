Concentrating on your tail, you can feel that it's also restrained... however, it's secured in a way that leaves your lower quarters exposed to the elements. A draft that winds its way through the room makes it painfully clear that, while you were unconscious, you'd been stripped of all you clothes.

    Your heart starts to race as you think about what must have happened to the others. I hope they're alright, but where could they be keeping them? You wonder, And what are they doing to them? Judging by your current circumstance, you can assume nothing good. You've gotta get out of here.

    You try struggling against your restraints again, but it's no good. You're strung up like a hog ready for the barbeque. In the distance, you hear someone... some-thing... approaching your room. You can't quite make out what it is you're hearing, but the footfalls - if that is what you hear - are anything but human.

    Finally, they stop at what, you assume, must be the entrance to your room, a door behind you slides open and fills the room with light from the hallway beyond. You get a glimpse of your small, barren, white-tiled room, there's some sort of screen just in front of you. A large shadow moves into the door frame, casting darkness over everything once more. You don't move.

    The figure moves into your chamber, a sound like knives scraping and clacking against the tile dig into your ears while you continue to pretend being unconscious. You hear the creature flick a switch and wince as bright, florescent light illuminates your world.

    "Ah, good, you're awake..." The voice is not loud and, as a matter of fact, it would seem downright friendly if it wasn't for the way the voice seems to fill the room and command your full attention. You crack your eyes open, the first thing you notice is your snout strapped shut in some heavy, leather muzzle. Next is how painfully bright the room is, featureless white walls surround you except for what you thought was a screen, but, in actuality, looks more like a blacked-out window. This really does look like an interrogation room, just without chairs and a desk... And you doubt there will be a good-cop-bad-cop routine.

    You let out an involuntary shiver as the creature behind you grazes your uplifted tail with sharp, pointed talons. They move up and down your sinuous length in an almost caressing motion, another shiver runs up your spine as it begins to speak again, "Mm, you are quite the find, I'd say..." The powerful voice sounds as though it could belong to a collector musing over a particularly interesting item, "Strong with dominant tendencies, but... lacking. There is too much of what you used to be still in there, trying to control a power it knows nothing about."

You feel incredibly exposed as your captor continues to appraise you from behind. Something sharp trails its way down to your exposed anus, you thrash in your restraints to escape the touch, but it's no use, you aren't going anywhere.

I'm going to enjoy breaking you." The creature walks in front of you and your eyes go wide as you aren't sure you can believe what you see.

Large eyes with reddish-gold irises stare at you, unblinking, taking in every detail of your naked, draconic body. Knife-shaped, brown and black feathers decorate the monster's entire upper body, ending at the elbows of its arms where appendages, that look like the claws of a bird of prey, make up its forearms and taloned hands. These talons reach up to stroke your snout through the muzzle and you have a strong urge to snap at them.

    Your eyes trace further down its body where, oddly, the feathers taper and fade into a golden pelt of silken fur. Contrary to what you thought you were going to see, strong, furred legs ending in leonine-like, digitigrade paws grace the lower half of this creature, complete with a tasseled tail. He turns to the windowed wall in front of you and presses a button. Slowly, a light illuminates the room beyond the glass, you gasp when you see Anders strung up in the middle, much the same as you.

    "HNNDRRS!" You attempt to get the wolf's attention, but your muzzle barely allows you to make more than a loud humming sound. The wolf appears to be struggling against his bonds when two figures step into the room. One of them is kitted out similarly to the guards you first saw and the other is a large bull-like creature who is wearing nothing. Your heart begins to race as you begin to struggle again, What are they gonna do with Anders? You try to figure out how you're going to help your lupine companion, If I can just loosen these straps I might... A strong grip on the back of your neck stops your struggle, the pressure increases to a painful level as the gryphon quietly discourages you from struggling any further.

    "Wait... and watch." He commands, the voice filling your head. You can't seem to focus on anything else anymore, your eyes are drawn to the room opposite you. A small whimper makes it past your muzzle as you realize you can't ignore the gryphon's words. "That's right, you belong to me now. Everything you were, everything you have become, it's mine. All mine. Soon, even your mind will accept its new master." The gryphon has moved behind you again, stroking your tail and your ears with his dexterous talons.

You feel warm, safe... protected... You shake your head as unbidden thoughts enter your mind. You don't want this, your body tenses as the monster rakes his claws down your scales. It seems that the figures beside Anders were waiting for a signal because as soon as the gryphon leaves your side, he waves a hand at the two on the other side of the glass and they begin to prepare something.

Anders' eyes trace the object with fright, he glances through the glass at you, the look of a cornered animal on his face. Unable to take your eyes off him, you start to tear up when you realize there is nothing you can do. You let them down... no, worse, you betrayed them and led them into a trap that you should have seen coming.
