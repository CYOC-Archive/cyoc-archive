    Bessie woke up feeling well rested. It felt like she hadn't slept this well in a long while. She sat up, her vision still somewhat blurry.
    'Oh right, i took over the castle.' She looked around and became somewhat confused when she saw her robe lying on the bed, neatly folded,
    and noticed that she was on the ground. 'What happened after that?' She looked down and nearly jumped up in shock.
    'Why am i pink?!' She ran over to a mirror, quickly discovering how much she had changed.
    'What, why am i- MY HORNS!' She touched one of the nubs on her head, that had been a massive horn not that long ago.
    They had been one of the few things left of her former masculine self, but had now been reduced to cute little nubs with ribbons on them.
    Her fur, wich had been somewhat shaggy in some places, had been cropped down to an even length and dyed a soft pink colour.
    She blushed when she noticed, that the shorter fur made her seem more naked than ever.
    Bessie dashed back to the bed and hastily put on her robe. She was startled when she suddenly heard someone knocking on the door.
    It confused her for a moment, before she vaguely recalled the automatons she had made yesterday. Was it one of them?
    Bessie hesitated for a moment, but decided to allow her in, thinking she may know what happened. "Y-you can come inside!"
    The door opened, revealing a metallic woman wearing maid attire, that Bessie recognized was in charge of taking care of pets,
    wich only served to further confuse her.
    "I came when i heard movement in your room Mistress. I have a progress report on the training of your new pet with me."
    "Uuh, you can leave it over there." She pointed at a dresser close to the door.
    The automaton put down the items in her hands, before bowing and turning to leave.
    She was about to exit the room, when she stopped and turned around. "May i speak my mind Mistress?"
    Bessie felt somewhat embarrassed by being called 'Mistress'. "S-sure."
    "That new look suits you well Mistress. I am now certain you will be thrilled once you see your pet." She bowed again.
    "I will take my leave, Mistress." She turned around and left, quietly closing the door behind her.
    Bessie blushed again when the synthetic woman mentioned her appearance. 'I guess i need to get used to being seen like this.'
    She sighed and sat down on the bed. All of the new, confusing information exhausted her.
    Her eyes wandered over to the dresser. Should she take a look at what the automaton brought?
    'She mentioned some sort of report... what was she talking about?' Thinking about it reminded her of something else.
    'Didn't i write down some plans before the ring's effect ran out? There's probably a lot of stuff i need to do as soon as possible.'
    She was about to stand up, when she saw her reflection again. 'maybe i should figure out what happened yesterday first.'
