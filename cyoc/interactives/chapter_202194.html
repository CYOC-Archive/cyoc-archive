Natalie Christoph is a short, pleasantly plump tanuki anthro, with less general magic skill, but great shapeshifting ability. Though they have their disagreements, they're friends with each other and me, and have agreed not to pull any major pranks on me."

    I thought to myself, “Aw, what gives? Can it really not do magical creatures?”

    But, just as it was fading, I saw Brenda's ears grow light brown fur, bend into a triangular shape, and move up the sides of her head, twitching slightly.

    Ah, so that explained that.

    "Yeah,” Natalie chimed in, “and she doesn’t have a lot left to lose.”

    Brenda growled, three fox tails rapidly shooting out behind her.

    I did my best to suppress a giggle. “Honestly, I just think your real forms look better than your human disguises.”
