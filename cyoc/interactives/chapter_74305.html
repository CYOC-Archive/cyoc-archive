    An hour later two cars left the driveway of Tim's house. One heading to the local radio station, the other driving to the military base at the
    outskirts of the town.

    The privates Felos and Bastet parked their car some big hoarding with an ad for pet food, only some hundreds feet down the road from the
    entrance to the military base. Both unloaded their equipment and headed to the front gate.

    Private Daniel Williams was bored. Mostly because must guard the gate and that since three days, only because he made a joke about his officer.
    Well, in the aftermath it wasn't such a god idea telling this particular joke while, unknown to him, his colonel stood right behind him. In the end
    he should be lucky that his punishment were only two months of gate duty. Most time it was a pretty boring duty. In this boring town never ever
    happening any interesting.

    And so, Private Williams was pretty much surprised when two figures in trench coats, hats and sunglasses approached him, looking like right out
    of a bad spy thriller. He was even more surprised when one of the cloaked figures put a hand under his coat and pulled out a strange looking
    gun and firing straight at Private Willliams chest. With a sudden burn in his chest the soldier collapsed to the ground of his gate house while the
    two figures passed him with out taking notice, and at first he was sure he would die, hit by a strange laser gun. But after a while the burning
    feeling expanded to the rest of his body and a touch with his hand assured Williams that he was relatively unharmed. Also the feeling wasn't
    really an unpleasant burning, but more a tickling. While he wondered what just happened and if he should raise alarm he noticed that he was
    now smaller than before. In fact he seemed to be shrinking. Still utterly confused what all this meant and if it was just some strange dream while
    he slept on duty, Williams scratched his head and felt his furry rectangular ears. Looking at his hands he also saw that they were slowly
    transforming into paws covered in a black fur. Whoa, that must be an hell of a dream, thought Private Williams while he felt his face changing and
    his whole body constricting. Perhaps the fish in the cantina last evening must bad. Really, really bad, and now he had an hell of a tuna-induced
    hallucination.

    Well at least something interesting happened, even if it's just a and dream, thought Daniel Williams while he transformed more and more into a
    black cat, his tail just emerging from his rear. When his transformation neared its end and Private Williams was just a black house cat at the
    ground of his gate house, one last change took place changing his sex from male to female while a complete new train of thoughts entered her
    new cat mind. At first the permanent boredom vanished, while she suddenly found the remnants of her uniform very interesting. After some time
    of shredding the clothes, she found the blinking lights of the computer at the desk of the gate house far more interesting and watched the fora
    while. The cat former known as Private Daniel Williams found somehow found her new form and life so much better and more interesting than
    her former dull life as a human and soldier. Also she smelled a certain scent in the air, telling her that there were some tomcats in the military
    base and this fact seemed to be VERY interesting.

    In the meantime Bastet and Felos where systematically striding through the military base, zapping every soldier they encountered into a more
    fitting cat form. Under their coats the wore an ablative, full-body armor which protected them from the occasionally shots of the opposing
    soldiers, not that there was a lot resistance cause they surprised most of them without a warning. On their way to the headquarter they were
    leaving a path of confused cats inside the remains of army uniforms.

    At this point Bastet and Felos part of the plan was working perfectly in time, while in the meantime Captain Purrfect and his squad were doing
    there part at the radio station.
