    JuJuDee laughed at the laptop screen as she saw her son Peter become PeeWee and his friend Leo become Leozo. She thought the three boys looked hilarious and like three jolly friends ready to have fun. But then the program shifted to its next target. The Chronivac hummed to life once more, its LED lights flickering with anticipation. With a sudden surge of energy, the device initiated its process, targeting Michael, her husband, as its next unwitting subject.
    **********
    Michael Mitchell was sitting in the living room, listening to the sounds of a house full of his children and their friends playing outside and inside. He sat on the couch reading a newspaper enjoying the afternoon. Unbeknownst to him, the Chronivac's whims had targeted him as the next subject for its clownish alterations. As he read, a subtle but peculiar sensation tingled at the back of his mind, a sensation he dismissed as a figment of his imagination.

    “See ya dad, heeheeyaha, I’m heading out for a few hours. I'm doing a show with my school clown troupe.” HONK! HONK! JJ said as he squeezed his nose and he passed Michael waddling towards the garage to his unicycle.

    Michael put down his newspaper to look at his rotund, whiteface, and oldest son. There he was with his red afro and white skin, wearing his blue jumpsuit and ruffled collar that he liked to wear for gigs. “Thanks for the update, see you late tonight.” Michael said as he went back to his paper, but now like the rest of his family he was now a clown, and unlike his son PeeWee he was unaware that he was human before or anyone else was.

    His skin was a light orange and there were now white circles around his eyes and his mouth. His lower lip was now red and turned up in a smile, additionally like his son his cheeks were now a rose red. Like JJ, Michael had bright red hair but he was bald, and his hair was long and straight.

    Michael was wearing a clown tux that was purple with yellow cuffs at the end of the sleeves of his jacket and also at the end of his baggy pants that were also purple just like that jacket. His shirt was white and had many rainbow polka dots spread throughout it. His tie was the same bright red as his hair, and it matched his large red clown shoes that fit his size 20 feet. It also matched their size as the tie was now triple the standard size, stretching down to his legs. Additionally he was wearing white gloves like his son and his wife.

    The most significant thing was his nose was now a red sphere the size of a tennis ball with nostrils below for breathing, and he squeezed it as he chuckled at some of the comics that he was reading in the paper. Unaware that he had been human before and now thought of himself as Mickey the Auguste Clown.

She decided to head downstairs to see him, and get started on dinner and baking, so she waddled out of her son's bedroom and headed for the kitchen. The Chronoviac however was still humming and carrying out its commands, and its next target was…
