    A lesson in over indulgence

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by TaleSpinning 2 years ago O

    Dumb Nami made me spill that little Drew kinda made me blush that one time... Still it was nice to finally be able to play some games. I still was aware enough that I knew that I had played these games often, but my little body didn't have any of those experiences. So I was happily playing with Nami. My body eventually got a little tired, but I wanted to play... Nami did bring some of the sheets from her bed and let me sit on her lap as we got comfortable wrapped in sheets and playing the game. At some point though I just couldn't keep my eyes open, and with Nami's nice soft warm body and the soft blanket I drifted into the land of slumber.

    One or two hours later Nami's alarm clock activated waking us both up. Nami slapped the alarm to quite it and meanwhile I was trying to get up like a good little girl, but I was just so sleepy... by the time I got myself to the kitchen table for breakfast I fell asleep. Next time I woke up I was laying on one of the couches in my... well the Mavis' living room. Meanwhile Haley was on her phone on the other end of the couch. As I unconsciously grumbled while getting up Haley finished up what she was doing and said "Hey Kiddo, spent a little too much time playing games last night?"
    I nodded and said "Sorry..."
    Haley sighed and said "Yeah this situation is a little annoying, and I'll have something to tell my little sister of mine when she gets back home... Still I can understanding you overindulging yourself a little. It's getting around noon how about I make us some macaroni and cheese for lunch?" It sounded good so I nodded yes. A few minutes later of waiting while watching some cartoons and a smiling Haley came over with a large bowl of mac'n'cheese and a... big mug of Soda?!? Haley put a metal straw in it so I could drink from the mug without worrying about spilling the large cup. I quickly started sipping the concoction of bubbles and syrupy sweetness.
    Haley chuckled and said "I guess all Ms. Yami doesn't give you a lot of sugar. It's nice to indulge a little bit but we need to know when to not indulge too much Jane..." She then noticed that I had sipped most of my beverage and then went to the kitchen and brought out the rest of the liter bottle of cola. "Want the rest?" The young woman asked and she knew the answer as my eyes lit up in excitement. She refilled my glass and said "why don't you eat something as well?"
    I went for the Mac'n'Cheese next and took a bite, and for some reason it seemed cheesier and sweeter and squealing in delight I asked "What did you do?"
    "This is processed, I guess you like it better than probably your organic stuff. Let's just keep it a secret from your mom an- wow your mowing through that, how about I refill your bowl?" My excited engorging self was just shoveling the food into my mouth. As I brought my head up to breathe Haley poured in some more of the cheesy noodles leaving a few for herself.
    Feeling content I lifted myself from the bowl and said "Thanks for the food!"
    Haley looked to my bowl and cup and said "You're not done."
    I asked "But I'm full..."
    "Your bowl and cup aren't empty. So you aren't done." I looked to my big mug and bowl and they were both still half full... I'd eaten about one and a half bowls of noodles and a lot of soda... Sadly my little Jane self was never given too much food in excess so this was a lesson I'd rue for awhile. It took awhile, but I ended up choking down the rest of the sweet food and drink. I was groaning laying a bit on the living room table. Haley chuckled and said "I hope this is a big lesson on not overindulging yourself. You need to learn when too much is too much."
    "...I'm sorry Miss Mavis..." I said readjusting myself on the couch. My stomach felt like it was bursting.
    "It's alright now, just know that this was a lesson to... Oh..." Haley must've seen what I looked like and carefully picked me up and rushed me to the bathroom. I felt a little shaky after the toilet flushed. Haley let me lay on the couch and watch some cartoons. She brought me a glass of water and asked "Want some tea with a little honey?"
    "Just a little..." I said.

    Throughout all this my big boy self semi-aware thought "Well I ate myself into puking, somehow managed to skip school today, and am having some meaningful time with my seemingly reclusive sister... How is this nothing but a win all around?.. Well besides throwing up..."
