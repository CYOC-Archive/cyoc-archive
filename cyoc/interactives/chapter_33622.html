    The Madison house was the creepiest house of the whole neighbourhood and it was abandoned since the last owner died few years ago.

    But this night two boys wanted to sneak into the house and look if the ghost stories about were real. Cody was a hyperactive fifteen-years old, who used always the principle 'First doing, then asking'. He always got strange ideas and was a bit megalomanic. Julius is Cody's best friend and at the same age. He also knew Cody's oddities and tried all times to correct them. He was often sarcastic and his motto was 'How more stupid can it become?'.

    Although Julius tried to stop Cody, both climbed through the kitchen window and entered the house. In the room stood a lot of old and dusty stuff. Some of them looked a bit weird, but nothing really scary. But when they went up to the attic, they found a strange room with a library full of books about magic, some weird items and a wand oon a table.
    Instantly Cody grapped the wand and ran through the room.
    "Whoa, Julius, look this. All this magic stuff. I think it is real. Think about all the things we can do."

    "Oh, no. You don't really think some of this is real, Cody."

    Meanwhile Cody was reading in a big and dusty book and mumbling some words. The wand release a spark and hit pencil which transformed into a flower.

    "Okay, that was strange.", said Julius,"What aare you reading there."

    "It is a powerful magic book. Together with the wand it gives me a great power."

    "And you know this because..?", asked Julius.

    "It stands here in the book. Okay, this is my plan: With my new magical powers I can rule this world, but to get sure I need some more magical artefacts.", explained Cody with an evil grin.

    "Okay, emporer of the world. And where will you get this artefacts? These are only some magic tricks, no real sorcery."

    "Oh, you don't know. Here was written that there is another dimension next to our dimension, which is full of magic. That is the place where these stuff come from. I will create a portal to this dimension and try to conquer it and steal these artefacts. Then I can conquer my home world." Cody began to laugh in a manic way.

    "Oh, no. Not again. Even if that is right, you can't control these powers. Stop now!", pleaded Julius.

    But before he could say something others, Cody recitated a new spell and opened a whirling vortex which sucked the two boys in. After a wild fly through the portal Cody and Julius were on a lone island in the near of a woody coast.

    "Hey, what are we doing her, Cody?", asked Julius.

    "As a new evil wizard I need my own dark citadel in this world.", answered Cody.

    "You mean, we are in this magic dimension now?"

    "Yeah, it was called Antaria."

    "But will our parents don't miss us.?"

    "Oh, no. They won't notice anything. When we were back they will think we were there all the time." With another spell Cody created a tower of dark stone and a wall of the same material around the tower.

    "Not bad, huh?", said Cody,"It is a bit small, but there a great caverns under the ground."

    Julius, who has just accepted the fact that he was in a strange dimension and his best friend was now a megalomanic evil wizard, was to impressed to comment this quote. With a move of the wand, they both teleported into a large room on the top of the tower with a huge window on the side of the coast. In the middle of the room was a huge black throne, some other furniture you expected to see there and there is a huge door to the halls. Aalso Cody's clothes changed. He wore now a dark robe and looked like a real evil sorcerer - except the fact that he was only fifteen years old, so it looked a bit funny.

    "So, this was the first step.", said Cody,"Now I need some minions."

    He created a transparent orb which showed him the creatures of the forest around the tower. When he saw some lizards and snakes, he got an idea. He teleported them into the room and casted a spell on them. Instantly they all transformed into anthro-lizards and anthro-snakes with armors, lances and swords. Even the snakes who still slided on their tails had now two claw-like hands and held crossbows.
    With a wave of his hand Cody sent his new minions to their places and they all obeyed.

    "Yeah, I think now I have all what a dark lord need.", said Cody and sat down on his throne,"Oh, I forgot one thing. I need strong person next to me who command my guards and is my personal bodyguard. I think that you are perfect for this job."

    Without loosing another word Cody aimed the wand on Julius and hit him with a spark. Instead of Julius there stood now an attractive, curvy girl with light green eyes and and c-cup breasts. Her curly red hair felt down to her neck and she wore a plain balck tank top, a black skirt, black boots and a sword hung at her belt.

    "Hey, why you have transformed my into a girl?", shouted Julius indignantly.

    "Because the companion of an evil wizard is all times a girl. Also I have given you a lot of new powers. You have now excellent sword-fighting and acrobatic skills and you can change yourself into demon with wings, horns, tail and so on. See it as a new experience.", explained Cody.

    "As if I have another choice.", said Julius sarcasticly.

    "Oh, and your name is now Julie and not Julius. That fits better.", said Cody,"And I think Cody is also a stupid name for an evil wizard so I am Kenan the dark lord for now on."

    "Hey, dark lord.", said Julie, what is when I use my new skills now and take your wand and your book." Julie jumped to Kenan and wanted to grap his wand, but suddenly she stopped in the air.

    "Don't be silly, Julie.", meant Kenan.

    "Don't call me this way!", grumbled Julie.

    "I have cast a obedience spell about you and all my minions. Noone of you can disobey a command of me or harm me in any way. Even as the commander of my guardians, these creatures obey me more than your. And I command you that your name is Julie and that you are proud of your name.", said Kenan.

    "Yes, Sir. My name is Julie, Sir.", said Julie and left the room, but not without smashing the door behind her.

    "Yeah, I see. This is the beginning of a great dominion. What shall I do at first?"
