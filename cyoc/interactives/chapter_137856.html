    Kidult Chaos: From Nicole to Nikki the Kidteen

    added 5 years ago A AR BM S Mythological Mental Kid

    Nicole was eighteen years of age, with waist-long, straight red hair, and green eyes.

    She was going to go shopping for clothes at the mall by herself, or at least that WAS her original plan, but due to her parents being gone for the weekend, she had to watch her three little sisters, and her cat.

    Her ten-year-old sister Jaden was a spoiled brat who felt she could get away with anything, her five-year-old sister Cali was still very nice and bubbly and always wanted to play with her big teen sister, and Angie who was just a baby was pretty well-behaved as far as baby girls go, and her laughter was one of the few things that kept Nicole's spirits high during all of this.

    Her cat Daisy was a green-eyed calico, and was always there to comfort Nicole by rubbing up against her.

    "Big sis!" Cali told Nicole with tears in her eyes "Jaden took my dollies and won't give them back!"

    "I'll deal with it in a minute." Nicole gently told her "I'm on the phone right now. So Megan, you still going to buy that new car you wanted today?"

    "You bet!" Megan, Nicole's best friend, happily said over the phone "I've saved up enough money, and NOTHING'S going to stop me from getting my new-"

    She was cut off when a flash engulfed everyone, and when it cleared, Nicole found she was only five, and swimming in her clothes. Nervously, she picked her phone back up.

    "Nikki?" she heard her now much younger friend on the other end call her "Are you there? I don't know how, but I just turned five, and my clothes don't fit my anymore!"

    "Yeah Meg," Nikki nervously replied "Same here!"

    With another flash, all of Nikki's clothes had shrunk to fit, but that's not all she noticed: she saw that Cali was now a three-year-old fairy with long pointy ears, dark pink butterfly wings on her back, dark pink hair, light pink skin, and red irises, while Angie now had point ears, small white feathery wings on her back, and a golden halo floating over her head.

    Lastly, Daisy was now an anthro calico cat, and a baby, wearing only a diaper with a hole in the back for her tail, and looking confused as to what happened. She looked like she'd come straight out of a kid's educational cartoon show.

    "Um...Meg?" Nikki said as she looked at them.

    "Yes, my bestest friend in the whole wide world?" Meg replied in a sugary-sweet way over the phone.

    "I'm gonna havta call you back later, 'kay?" she told her nervously.

    "Sounds good!" Meg replied happily "I'm gonna find my new car so I can go vroom-vroom! Hee-hee-hee! Later! Bye-bye!" (click)

    "So you gonna help me now, big sis?" Cali asked with her sparkly fairy eyes.

    "Um, sure." Nikki nervously replied as she hopped out of her chair.

    "I shall go and assist you as well." Angie said, much to Nikki's surprise as she hoved with her feathery wings "I can sense that Jaden...or Jadeth as she is calling herself now, has become an imp because of the naughtiness in her heart, and so it falls to me as a cherub and your sister to help you in this endevor against our imp sister."

    "Yeah!" Daisy said with a smile on her feline face "I wanna help too! I'm sure if we talk to her she'll listen."

    This was getting to be too much for little Nikki to handle, but her as the oldest sister, she had to be the "big girl" of the group. The fact that she was still the oldest in the house despite being only five did help boost her confidence some.

    "Let's go then." Nikki said as she led them up the stairs to where Jadeth was waiting "If she won't listen to us, then when mommy and daddy get home, I'm tellin' on her!"

    As Nikki bravely marched up the stairs, Cali and Angie fluttered behind her, while Daisy playfully skipped behind them, giggling to herself. Without a doubt, this was the weirdest day any of them had known.
