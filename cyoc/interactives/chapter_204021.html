    Marcia felt the years tick by as she continued her frantic search. Once the braces vanished from her mouth she knew she was back to 6th grade. Then feeling her shirt brush against her chest without even a training bra to separate the two meant she was back to elementary school.

    She had almost given up hope when finally she saw it. Inside of a unicorn lunchbox attached to her current bag was a gold amulet that might just...

    "Aah!" Marcia shrieked in surprise when something tapped her back

    Only as the bag fell from her grasp did she realize the feeling was a pair of fake ladybug wings strapping to her back. Watching helplessly as her bag shifted one last time, the lunchbox vanished as the now small, plush ladybug bag landed softly on the floor.

    At the same time the physical changes came to an end. Marcia felt a sudden breeze as her pants and shirt merged into a dress. The soft cotton underwear was growing smaller and somehow softer. Looking away from the bag, Marcia found herself wearing a small red dress with black spots and with black leggings tucked into a tall pair of red rain boots with similar black spots. A pair of fake ladybug wings was strapped around her back over her red rubber raincoat.

    The banshee quickly lowered her back down. Still slightly above the ground, Marcia found the banshee's grip release and found her knees buckle from the unexpected jolt. Falling down she heard an unmistakable thump as the her boot covered feet landed on the ground.

    As the banshee observed, Marcia saw affixed to her coat was a little tag

    My name is Marcia ______
    I am _____ years old

    It then listed a phone number and address.
