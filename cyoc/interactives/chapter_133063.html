    The Re-born of Earth

    As soon as Pat clicked "SAVE ALL CHANGES" both of them felt the flow of energy that they're used to, especially Pat who received a more powerful energy through his body than Jack did, then Jack said something in the alien language and text popped up on the translation computer "ARE YOU READY FOR THIS?" and almost immediately they started to change, Pat felt his bones stretching and rising in height as his cat tail, frog eyes and upgraded neck returned back to human.

    Also Jack the alien felt himself growing in size buy certainly he was more interested in Pat changes than his own changes, Pat felt a tingling sensation all over his body and he noticed that all the hair was retracting in his body, he wanted to see his own change into such a powerful Martian so he ran to the mirror where Jack watched his transformation a few moments ago, Pat's head started aching and growing so he had to close his eyes for a while, his head reached alien proportions and when he could open his eyes he watched a massive bald head.

    "CAN YOU UNDERSTAND OUR LANGUAGE NOW?" Jack said in the alien language and Pat was about to grab the language translator but he noticed he COULD understand him! "Yes" Pat said, his voice was trembling but he couldn't talk alien just yet " JUST CONTINUE ENJOYING YOUR TRANSFORMATION BROTHER, THIS IS THE BEST PART" Jack said strongly and so Pat nodded.

    By the moment Pat's skin was already gray and his eyes was turning dark and expanding within every second until they were full black as the night, Jack was right he had a wider field of view and the ability to see both infrared and ultraviolet light, it was so extrange but he was LOVING it. Next, his ears started folding back into his massive head until they merged completely leaving nothing but gray skin Pat was surprised he could still hear everything and even more cleary, now his nose flattened and disappeared in his face leaving only his tiny nostrils. His lips disappeared, his mouth now a line across the bottom of his face. He smiled to Jack, revealing very sharp teeth, and when he moved his tongue around he felt a second row of them.

    As Pat changes continue both Jack and Pat were getting taller and Jack's spacesuit darker. "LOOK AT ME, I'M GETTING SO BIG" Said Jack, Pat tried to answer but he felt the transformation changing his voice and couldn't speak for a moment as two more voice boxes grew in his neck, which is getting longer and skinnier, same for his arms and legs.

    When Pat tried to talk again he wasn't talking English instead he was talking Martian "I'M TALKING LIKE YOU, FINALLY" said Pat his voice was even lower than Jack's, "YES, YOU ARE BROTHER, AND SOON WE WILL RULE OVER EARTH" Jack said euphoricaly.

    The brothers were too excited for even notice that Pat's hands and feet are now completely Martian, and the fleshy antenna was starting to grow on his head, and it wasn't growing slow, in fact it only took a pair of seconds to the antenna to grow the entire length. Pat was so exited he was an entire Martian by now and felt so powerful and full of energy, but the changes haven't finished just yet and felt a sucking sensation down in his nether region as his equipment retracted into his body.

    "UGH MEN I'D REALLY LIKED TO KEEP THAT" Said Pat with a sad tone. "SERIOUSLY BROTHER?, WE'RE GOING TO RULE OVER EARTH, ISN'T THAT ENOUGH?"
    Replied Jack. "I KNOW IT WILL BE FUN BUT WITH OUR EQUIPMENT WE COULD HAVE EVEN MORE FUN WITH EARTHLINGS AND EVEN WITH OTHER MARTIANS, DON'T YOU THINK?" Argued Pat. "MMM, OKAY YOU CONVINCED ME, LET'S USE OUR CHRONIVACS LATER TO FIX THAT" Said Jack. "FUCK YEAH MEN" Shouted Pat.

    While the Martian-twin-Brothers discussed, the final stage of the transformation took form when the black-Jet spacesuits appeared over the brother's bodies and both of them reached the six-feet-six height, This transformation was complete.

    The brothers found themselves inside some kind of futurist room that they remembered as their bedroom in the Martian secret base, the room wasn't so big, but that's fine because now they like to be very close of each other, they had tons to explore in this new version of the reality. But they know what to do first...
