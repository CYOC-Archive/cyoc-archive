    Floyde bit into the doughnut as Marianne disappeared into the
    elevator. He was chewing his Krispy Kreme when he noticed a funny
    taste. He knew how Krispy Kremes were supposed to taste. He made a
    face and spit out the doughnut without swallowing. He grabbed his
    coffee and took a swig. He swilled it around in his mouth and spit it
    out.

    "Damn, bitch, she tried to poison me! Probably a laxative or worse
    yet maybe Viagra or Cialis!" he scratched behind his ear. He was
    starting to itch terribly. Maybe he had swallowed some of it.

    He looked down at the tainted doughnut and frowned. It had been a
    real Krispy Kreme before she had tampered with it. He shook his head.
    He didn't want to throw out all the doughnuts, but he couldn't chance
    that she hadn't tainted all of them. Still. He smiled. He walked
    across the alley and set the box on top of the trashcan. He didn't
    like the bums that he was constantly chasing away from there, perhaps
    Marianne's doughnuts would scare them away. He went back to his post
    and realized the doughnut with one bite missing was still on his desk.

    About ten minutes later, the Chinese Restaurant delivery boy showed
    up.

    "Got an order for Mary," he said.

    "Wait a moment," Floyde ordered, "Let me pay for that, and I'll take
    it up to her."

    "Thanks for the tip! Mary never tips," he said as he headed back to
    his car.

    As soon as the delivery boy was gone, grateful for Floyde's generous
    tip, Floyde opened the containers until he found the chop suey. He
    broke up part of the tainted doughnut and stirred it into Mary's
    chopsuey. Then he closed it up. Then he added a bit of the bread
    crumbs to orange chicken and the broccoli beef. He was going to give
    Mary some of her own medicine. He put everything back in the bag, and
    phone Mary.

    "Marianne, they just delivered your Chinese food. I've got it down
    here at the desk," he answered.

    "What? Why didn't you send him up with it?" Mary said in an irritated
    voice. She looked down at the creme filled candy which she had
    injected with some liquid from the frog vial. She had planned to give
    it to the delivery boy - half the time he brought her food cold.

    Then she blinked in surprise, "Floyde, did you enjoy your doughnuts?"

    "Oh, they were delicious, I can't believe I ate every last one. Oh,
    Stu's here. Stu cover for me will you. I'm going to take Marianne up
    her lunch," he rambled, "Oh, I'll be up in a minute with your lunch."

    Marianne blinked. She wondered what monstrosity would crawl, creep or
    waddle out of the elevator. She could imagine the delivery boy
    horrified at Floyde's transformed appearance and throwing the food on
    his desk. Floyde didn't seem to have noticed he had changed. She was
    surprised though that she hadn't heard Stu scream. She braced herself
    for Floyde's appearance, and was visibly shocked when she saw Floyde's
    usual beaming face and Pillsbury doughboy shaped body coming toward
    her carrying the big paper bag.

    Floyde belched as he set the bag down. He started to scratch his neck
    and muttered, "Strangest thing, I'm suddenly itchy all over. Well,
    thanks for the doughnuts."

    Mary raised her eyebrow. He must be changing slowly. Perhaps mixing
    that many vials caused a delayed reaction? She asked, "Wait, Floyde,
    how much do I owe you?"

    "Nothing, Marianne, it's the least I could do after you got me all
    those doughnuts."

    "Oh, well, that's sweet of you," Mary said glancing down at the
    injected chocolate. Maybe she had used too little formula in the
    doughnuts? She looked at the chocolate again, and said....
