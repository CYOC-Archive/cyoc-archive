    "I wanna be a..." Kate stopped to think for a moment. She came here out of sexual desire, originally planning on an anthro transformation. However, if she could be anything at all, she may change her mind. She had always had a doll fetish, and was even more turned on by the idea of becoming one. It would be the ultimate experience for Kate, even if the transformation meant she couldn't move. She was still unsure, however.

    "It's okay, take your time to figure everything out," Professor Juniper put the syringe away, "It's a big decision, I know. You're essentially picking what you want to be for the rest of your life."

    "Wait, these transformations will be permanent?" Kate was surprised, she thought the transformations only lasted a few weeks at the most.

    "Yes they are, but you didn't come here just to be something else for a few days." Professor Juniper explained. She was right, if Kate was going to transform herself, she'd want it to be permanent.

    "Okay," Kate made up her mind finally, "I know that I filled out for an animal transformation, but could I change it?"

    "Of course you can. Just tell me what you'd want to change into instead, and I'll go get the form for it." Professor Juniper answered happily. Kate took in a deep breath.

    "I'd... I'd like to be a doll instead." She answered, still nervous.

    "Alright, let me just get my clipboard so I can write down what you want your features to be," Professor Juniper grabbed her clipboard, and flipped to a blank page, "Do you want to be porcelain, marionette, plush, or plastic?"

    "I'd like to be porcelain." Kate answered, growing excited.

    "Okay," She wrote down Kate's answer, "What height do you want to be? The tallest we can do is 24 inches."

    "How about... 18 inches tall. Perfect doll size." Kate answered, having to think for a moment.

    "Okay, 18 inches..." Professor Juniper wrote her answer down, "Do you want to be animate or inanimate? There's a couple differences with each one, but I'll go over the similarities first."

    She paused for breath, and flipped to a page on her clipboard that had the doll changes listed. Kate's eyes were on Professor Juniper.

    "With both, you will no longer need to breath, eat, sleep, use the bathroom, etcetera," She read off, her eyes moving across the page, "You will need to move your head to move your vision, and you will not be able to move your finger or toes. Your eyes will also become glass doll-eyes. Oh! And you won't be able to break if you're dropped."

    "Okay..." Kate said to indicate she was listening.

    "With the animate version, you'll obviously be able to move around, and with the false jaw that comes with the porcelain transformation, you'll be able to talk, and blink. Your joints, like your wrists, ankles, knees, elbows, and shoulders, will become ball-joints," Professor Juniper paused for breath, and glanced at Kate who was listening intently, "With the inanimate version, you will not be able to move at all. You'll have no ball-joints either, so whatever pose you take when you transform will be the pose you have forever."

    "Wow, that's a lot," Kate remarked, taking a deep breath, "I need a moment for this one."

    "There's actually a bit more," Professor Juniper gave an apologetic grin before continuing, "With the animate one, you'll be able to lock your pose. However, if you lock your pose for longer than 24 hours, you'll become inanimate. With being inanimate, you'll become solid porcelain."

    "Okay. Well, I guess I'll go with animate, because if I want to, I can become inanimate." Kate answered.

    "Alright..." Professor Juniper flipped all the way to the back again to write Kate's answer down, "So, the final one, and the most important one, is do you want your mind to be altered by this transformation? This is an option for any potentially inanimate transformation."

    "What do you mean?" Kate asked, her nervousness returning.

    "If you do want your mind to be altered, then you will eventually believe that you had always been a porcelain doll," Professor Juniper explained, her tone very serious now, "You'll lose memories of ever having been anything but a porcelain doll, eventually completely losing yourself in your doll form. The process would be accelerated by doing or experiencing something that would be pleasurable for a porcelain doll."

    "Like what?"

    "Like if you get a new outfit, or if someone mentions you being pretty, or holds you like a doll," She explained, "Stuff like that. When you do eventually lose yourself, you'll be put into a state of constant orgasmic bliss, so there's that at least. The process happens faster with inanimate dolls than animate ones, too."

    Kate thought for a moment, and looked back up at Professor Juniper.

    "I'm gonna say no..." Kate was quick to answer this time. Professor Juniper put her answer down on the paper, "Why would anyone want that? It's basically suicide."

    "Well, some people find the idea of 'constant orgasmic bliss' to be very tempting, some people do it because they want to forget their lives, and some do it because they simply want to." She answered, putting her clipboard under her arm.

    "Is there a less severe one? Like, is there one where I can retain my memories, but still get pleasure from doing doll things?" Kate asked, hoping the answer was 'yes'.

    "That will happen for any potentially inanimate transformation," Professor Juniper answered, making Kate smile. Professor Juniper then remembered one more thing, "Oh! I forgot one last thing. You'll be able to feel anything that touches your outfit as if it was part of your skin. For example, let's say a fly lands on your skirt. You'll feel it the same as if it landed on your skin."

    "Wow." Kate remarked.

    "Now, with that out of the way, I'll take you over to the porcelain doll clothes room, and let you pick out your outfit and makeup while I go make the pill for you." Professor Juniper put her pen in a pocket on her lab coat.

    Kate sat up, and followed her out of the room.
