    Suddenly the cave shook violently to the right and both you and the red dragon were slammed hard against the hard cave
    wall at the same time as your extra sensitive finned ears funneled
    the loud noise of a impact occurring outside the cave.

    You were a bit too dazed to have noticed a chunk from the ceiling come loose and fall to the cave floor.
    Beside you,the red dragon you incorrectly thought was
    Alise lay prone on the floor.

    Basically when the force had flung the two of you to the wall
    the dragon you called Aster had taken a harder hit then you,and was critically injured now.
    Groggily you got up and looked at the sparking wires and alien dragon spacetech that hung from the hole
    in the cave ceiling to the junk of synthostone and electronics
    that now lay on the cave floor.

    You ignored the comatose male dragon as it slowly dawned
    on you that this wasn't a natural cave by any means.

    Your curiosity aroused you crept towards the male,whom a
    part of your mind was telling you was likely a imposter.
    Feeling around with your talons you found it
    a Communicator
    that rather then buttons like a human device
    had holes in the stone like square slab.

    The device was awfully big and awfully light and you
    wondered how the imposter could possibly have hidden such a huge device on his body without you seeing it before.
    So carefully you picked up the alien device and as you brought it
    closer to the other dragon the Communicator began to grow
    transparent until although you could still feel it
    in your foretalons,it was invisible.
    So you pulled it away from Aster and immediately the Communicator became visible again.

    Suddenly there was another impact sounded from outside the
    artificial cave and both you and the comatose male slid in the
    direction of the other side.More of the cave ceiling came
    loose,revealling more wires and stuff up there in the space
    above the cave ceiling.
    Concentrating on the Communicator you began poking your
    talons into the deep recesses of the slab and with
    what sounded like a dragonish noise from the Communicator
    you found the prone Aster was now modestly clothed
    in what appeared to be some kind of dragon uniform.

    All business now,you noted that the device clothed/unclothed
    the comatose Aster when you had the device pointing at him.

    Quickly you also pointed the Communicator at your own dragon body
    and soon had yourself clothed properly in what
    looked like a colourless grey burlap sack like material.
    The imposter still lay senseless against the wall and it
    occurred to you that your
    dragon captors might not appreciate if one of their own
    folk died while you held the balance of his life in your foretalons.
    So you rushed back to his prone form and poked around in his uniform till your talons closed around something that slightly
    looked like a cross between a stone key and a stone pass-card.
    It had bumps,peg like bumps ,on one side and taking a guess
    you placed the key-card against the various depressions in the
    Communicator till with another of those dragonish sounds
    the Communicator vibrated in your claws and the invisible
    exit door on the artificial cave wall opened and you
    gazed apon
    another clothed dragoness like yourself staring at
    you and the scene in the cave.....
