    "That's sillier than any of the shoe mantras," she noted.

    "Well, it's good to be silly when you are a clown. Just keeping it clowny." he replied.

    She sighed and felt the tingle of the dust on her scalp and she nodded before speaking. "Bibbidi Bwoopidy Bops...Turn me into Roly's Flip Flops!"

    And almost instantly she felt a strange sensation all over as she began to shrink again, the borrowed clothing from the clown billowing all around her. "Here we go again!" she squeaked.

    Having an idea of what came next, he picked up the shrunken Jenny and set her down on the floor and smiled down.

    "Woah" She said as she looked at him then down to his feet. "I suddenly have the urge to have you step on me."

    "Perfectly natural for flip flops, but if you insist now, I think I can oblige." He chuckled as he raised his foot over the shrunken girl and she giggled in delight and watched it come down, silencing the giggling with a cartoon splat.
