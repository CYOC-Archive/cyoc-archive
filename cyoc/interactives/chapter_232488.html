    “You still need a little help?”, Amber asked, breaking the silence that had fallen over the room. The only response Hwan could still give was a feeble thumbs-up, which ended up being more like flailing her hand around a bit. Amber just giggled. “I figured you would. Don’t worry, I’ll be as gentle as I can.”

The sensation of being rolled up was incredibly strange - parts of her body were touching that shouldn’t have been able to, but she could only watch as Amber started at her feet and pushed the mass of plastic closer and closer to her head.

    She was left as a wadded-up cylinder that Amber could easily carry in one hand, thankfully with her eyes still facing outward on its front. Hwan couldn’t appreciate that fact for long, though. A moment later, Amber gave her a little kiss on the forehead (through the translucent surface of which, Hwan’s mouth could just barely be seen), then stuffed her next to the other girls and zipped the bag closed. They were all plunged into darkness now, an almost complete sensory deprivation broken only by the muffled sound from outside and the occasional quiver of one of the other girls - some of them still had a bit of air trapped inside.

    The sound that Hwan could hear changed as time went on, from the trunk of a car rolling down the highway to the busy conversations of an airport terminal. Possibly the riskiest part of the plan was getting through the TSA line. Reports of human trafficking, unsurprisingly, had skyrocketed with the popularity of Fruits and their transformations. It was of course impossible in most cases for authorities to tell whether the thing being trafficked was once human, so enforcement was practically nonexistent. But if a security guard opened up their bag and connected the dots, Amber could be in big trouble before anyone thought to give the other girls enough air to explain the situation.

    Fortunately, it seemed that nobody suspected a thing, because the sounds of the airport soon gave way to the rumbling of a jet engine ready for takeoff. They were actually getting away with this, Hwan thought with excitement. Maybe all of her worrying was for nothing.

    But then again, she had little to do in these long, boring hours other than to worry. She was completely alone with nothing but her own mind for company, despite the fact that she was pressed tightly between friends who must have been equally bored.

    Hwan didn’t even notice when she started to doze off - in her inert, senseless state, there was little difference between being awake and being asleep. It certainly helped to pass the time, though, because the next thing she knew, her vision was filled with light again.

    In the privacy of their hotel room, Amber quickly got to work setting up the air compressor and bringing her assorted passengers back to life. But, as promised, she gave Hwan at least a partial inflation from her own mouth, before her lungs started to tire and she switched back to using the air hose. It didn’t have the same personal touch, but Hwan was a lot more relieved to be mobile again than she was disappointed to not have Amber’s lips a few inches above her featureless crotch.

    Panama City Beach had always been a rowdy place during the spring break holidays, but this year promised to be a lot crazier. Unlike most other states, Florida had done basically nothing to regulate the flow and usage of Lilith Fruits, making a place like this a nationwide destination for transformation tourism. Even from a distance, you could see it - a flock of colorful man-sized birds taking flight over the crowd here, a sand sculpture suddenly coming to life and walking along the beach there. You could probably find any variety of Lilith Fruit imaginable in a place like this, and probably a few that were beyond your imagination too.

    That, not the beach, was the real reason the floaties had wanted to come down here. Well, except for Hwan - she had no desire to add another complication to her two overlapping changes. She was content to stay back in the small hotel room with Amber for most of the weekend. It had only one bed, but the other girls were expecting to find someone else‘s place to crash for the night, in whatever form they might end up with.
