    Cody was going to school with his best friend, Jeff. They both were 16 years old. They saw several half monkeys on the streets, and they got used to it.

    Cody was lucky until now, there were now signs of infection on his body. Jeff was less lucky: in the last week, he had became quiet hirsute. By now, his forearms and legs were covered in a thick layer of hair, and he had a great amount of chest hair. But he was a slow changer, others usually finished a full transformation during a week, or shorter. In school, half of the guys were even more changed, being covered in fur, having tails and other simian things.

    The day passed quietly, nothing important happened in school. After PE lesson, they went to have a lunch with their friends to the cantine.

    "Did you see Jack, he has now such a hairy shoulders...with orange hairs...I bet he will be an orangutan" told Jeff.
    "Yes, you are right" agreed Scott, an other untransformed guy "I can not imagine how they can deal with this much hair, but you know about it a lot Jeff, don't you?" he pulled some of Jeff's long black armhairs "It wasn't here a month ago, and now...well, your arms and legs are so...you know...disgusting"
    "Beware of what you say, you might be infected too sometimes"

    Cody didn't wanted to participate in this argue, he felt a bit strange. He ate his food, but he felt very warm...and uncomfortable. He felt an itch at the base of his neck, and on his arms...and it was spreading. He felt a pain in his spine. Jeff realized it and he asked:

    "Cody, are you okay, you don't look good" told Jeff
    "Yes, yes I'm okay" lied Cody "Maybe PE lesson was just too hard"
    "You just need some rest bro" told Scott, when he took a look at Cody's hands "Jesus Cody, look at your hands, you are infected too...and shit, hair is poking out of your shirt's collar"



    Cody looked down at his hands "Shit.." he told, and slowly started to panic "Shit...oh shit..shit shit shit no..." he said, while he pulled up his sleeves. His arms now were as hairy as Jeff's. He started to scratch his chest with this hairy hands under his shirt "shit, my chest is covered... oh no" he felt a thick pelt under his hands, and what was worse, he felt it thickening under his fingers. While he was horrified by his new chest hair, the others could see a beard forming on his face. But the worst part just started to happen now...



    Cody felt a huge pain in his spine, he felt that it is elongating. He tried to stand up, and wanted to run to the bathroom, when the pain became unbearable, and he felt to his back onto the top of the dining table. Several students came closer, to see what is happening. Cody felt his body growing, and his clothes were getting too tight for him. He teared off his shirt, reveling his very hairy torso to the others.



    He was getting taller, and the hair on his chest turned into fur. He saw his arms..they were a lot longer, covered in a thick layer of fur. He felt a bump at his rear, and his feets hurt like hell..."Please help...get the shoes off" Jeff was the fastest, he took off Cody"s shoes, and a difformed, monkey like leg/hand appeared there. His trousers were damaged by now, reveling strong, muscled and long fur covered legs. Finally, a long, hairy tail shot out Cody's rear, and his face changed more simian...and the changes stopped. By now he was totally covered in pelt. Cody was no more human. But he wasn't a monkey too. And he had to deal with his new body and future.
