    Pleasure Island

    Come to think of it, you didn't have a good look at the drinks...

    You gave a little smile to the bartender and asked him, "Care to show the list of drinks? I'm interested in seeing what other drinks you have."

    The blonde nodded, "Yeah, me too. If that special mix was that good, I wanna see what else is available!"

    The bartender nodded, eagerly fetching the two of you the menu. Looking over the plastic-covered paper menu, you saw a litany of drinks with assorted names and descriptions. You and the blonde browsed the paper's list for a moment, though the drinks were seemingly rather mundane, the snacks equally so, though no doubt quite tasty.

    "Do check the other side. The personal mix I gave to the two of you is there as well." The bartender noted. You both flipped the menus over to see the rest of the drinks list.

    The first one was the bartender's "Bar Special", with a description of a salty yet sweet flavor combination made with a secret recipe. The drink also described how the drink would lift spirits and open one up for new experiences in mind, body and soul. You looked to the blonde, who shrugged, "I guess the drink did make us consider our options, hm?"

    You gave a little nod. You had to admit, you did feel much more comfortable and confident now than you did not too earlier today. The two of you then continued browsing the list:

    Elven Easy Breeze: A highly refreshing drink made with strawberry vodka. Many drinkers speak highly of feeling lighter on their feet, improved skin as well as of gaining more compliments.

    Drow's Draft: A crisp cocktail that helps with finding that exotic tanned skin you just might be looking for, with a bold blueberry flavor followed with a sweet aftertaste. Many mention opening up to new experiences.

    Catspaw: A special herbal tea-flavor mix that lets you mellow out to enjoy the sun. Many satisfied customers speak highly of how limber and flexible they feel after the drink.

    Wolfsmead: Based on a black velvet, this drink has an earthy sidetaste. Drinkers often mention heightened senses and experiences, as well as that they made long-lasting connections on full moons, which seemed to draw out an as-of-yet unseen boldness from them.

    Hell's Bells Special: A bit like Wolfsmead, but with a special spicy kick to it. While not enjoyed by many, the ones who have drank this speak of feeling unstoppable.

    Dragonsblood Ice Tea: A drink for those who want to really go all out, as the drink incorporates a number of potent Pleasure Island-exclusive beverages into its many-layered experience. Many speak of feeling great to the point of flying off.

    Mermaid's Blood: A cocktail with a salty-sweet initial flavor and a floral aftertaste. Satisfied customers speak of help with their diving hobbies, love life and helping clear their singing voice.

    Devilish Highball: A whiskey highball with a light, invigorating lemon flavor, a favorite among those who want to keep going all night long. Many customers, men and women alike, speak of improved charisma and looks thanks to the drink.

    Hermaphroditus' Special: A lightly salted nutmeg-based cocktail. A niche taste, but which gives you that little bit of extra to help you leave any prospective hookup, date or lover satisfied. Often enjoyed with another drink from the list above.

    Momma's Nog: A warm, mellow cocktail with a milk base, served in a sizable pint. Drinkers speak of fullness and contentment, as well as happy accidents that often lead to long-lasting relationships.

    Aphrodite's Afternoon Tea: A floral tea spiked with the house rum, light on alcohol but refreshing. Many speak of feeling "radiant" and of an exceptionally lively night afterwards, as well as feeling much better overall, though that it might be all the compliments speaking.

    There were some more other options, but you were already intrigued by your options.

    "Well, anything strike your eye?" the bartender said with a little smirk. The blonde gave you an eager smile, "I'll see what she picks and decide then."

    Her gaze did imply she had some ideas, especially as her eyes darted between you and the bartender. You looked at the menu, pondering on your choice...
