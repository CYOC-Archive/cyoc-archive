    You put on the VR headset and immediately blackout. When you awake, you find yourself completely naked in a gorgeous meadow with various wildflowers scattering the landscape. There is a river in the distance, surrounded by fruit trees.

    All five of your senses were amazingly realistic. You could see the wildflowers swaying in the mild breeze. You could smell the floral scent in the air. You could hear the bubbling river in the distance. You could feel the soft grass tickling your bare skin. You could taste a mild sense of fruit on your tongue.

    A text box appears and explains the rules to you.

    The meadow is roaming with monster girls and kemonomimis.

    They will all try to transform you into one of them by various means. Slimegirls will absorb you into their body or feed you their slime, bunny girls will try and feed you carrots, plant monsters will try and splash you with pollen or nectar, etc.

    Along with these basic monsters on this part of the meadow, across the rivers are even more dangerous and rare ones. There are four rivers in total, and you have to cross them all to finish the level and escape.

    There are special items to disable the monsters and help you across the level, however none of these items will kill, and if you kill a monster with your bare hands, you will incur the wrath of every other monster girl.

    If you lose your life, your body will be absorbed into the simulation, and this will become your new reality.

    Good luck!
