Stop thinking of me as Joe and you can do what you'd like with me. That way you get one experience before you become a woman."

    "It does, my goddess, so I will," he said. "I'll be just outside." He looked at George and warned with a pointed finger, "Be careful you do nothing wrong."

    George looked terrified. I laughed and said, "Don't worry, dear Bozantum, George would never do me wrong." With that Bozantum walked out of the room and closed the door behind him. I looked back at George who still seemed unsure of what to do. "Well, come on, strip!" I commanded.
