    Stuck in their computer

    You're temporarily blinded by the light, and you stand there, blinking, as your vision clears to reveal Rebecca and Sam. "I can't believe he fell for that!" Says Sam. "Told you he would," Rebecca replies, "I'm just glad you still had that holograph projector, I'm not sure we could have gotten him if he didn't think the other girls were here, even with the emotion field!"

    While they chatter, you just sit there, dazed, and look around. In front of you, there's a big window, which you take to be the screen, but in every direction there's only a black, floor with a grid on it. The sky is eerily grey. You turn your attention back to the two girls on screen, they were talking about a hologram projector, those don't even exist, right? And what's an emotion field? For that matter, what did they do to you, this should be impossible! Your thoughts are interrupted by your name, "Kyle, Kyyylllleee, Earth to Kyle. There ya go!" Sam says, before breaking out laughing, "He's still spacing out even in a computer!" Ignoring Sam with a smirk, Rebecca says, "So you're probably wondering what happened, it's simple, we trapped you in a computer." You had figured that much out for yourself thank you very much, "But how did you do it?" Ignoring you, Sam starts up again, "Anyway, we wanted some people to try this out on, and I figured that we'd probably be able to get you easily enough, I've seen how you look at me." Sam takes this opportunity to lean back in her chair and cross her legs, clearly enjoying this. "Also, your an orphan, so it's not like they'd let you go here if you told them, you only get to go places because they can't keep track of anyone with their incompetence." At this, Rebecca turns to Sam and says, "Actually, we should check, we know with Jen, because she told us, but we don't know with him."

    You don't hear anything past 'Jen'. They apparently got her too. She's also an orphan who lives at the same orphanage as you, she's your best friend and almost a sister to you, you figured you got an invitation here only at her request, guess that's not the case though. "Kyle!" Rebecca shouts, drawing you back into the real world, "We need you to tell us if anyone knew you were coming here." You didn't of course, you wouldn't have been able to come if you did, but you aren't going to tell them that. "Yea, didn't I tell you they're...." Your interrupted by Sam doing a fist pump, "We got him!" With a glare, Rebecca says, "Don't tell him!" Smirking, Sam replies, "Well what's the fun in that? Hey squirt, what's your favorite color?" It's purple, but if she wants to know that, you aren't going to tell her. "It's..." you say, "Purple!" Sam finishes! "Yea you were going to say yellow, but get this, we can see what you're thinking!" Rebecca just shakes her head before clicking a few things, "Anyway, we have to let the computer update soon, so I'll just drop in Jen and leave you guys alone until tomorrow." You take small pleasure in the fact that Sam's smirk changes to a pout before the screen disappears. Leaving you alone in the emptiness.

    It's only empty for a moment though, as walls and a roof appear around you, leaving you alone in a room, then a bed appears, before finally, you hear a thump on the other side of it. You run over to the other side only to find Jen lying on the floor, naked! You stop mid-run and stare, you never realized how attractive she is, she usually wears baggy clothes but without them.... No, she's your best friend, practically your sister! "Having fun Kyle?" you hear Sam's voice in your head, you can hear the smirk in her voice. That creep... You can't believe you liked her.... Jen still hasn't moved so you call her name and she doesn't respond, so you walk over and shake her. Nothing happens, so you worriedly shake her harder. Suddenly she jumps away from you, screaming. She looks around and stops, before looking down and shrieking again, "Look away, Look away!!!" You just stand there, partially in shock, "I said look away Kyle!!!" She yells even louder. You swiftly turn around, and you're rewarded with giggling sounds in your head, they are going to pay....

    Jen looks around the room a bit, before giving up and saying, "Don't turn around, this room has nothing loose. Kyle what's going on?" At this moment the lights go out, leaving you pitch black. "Your on the bed right Jen?" You ask. "Yea?" "I'll take the floor then", you say, as you sit down to explain...
