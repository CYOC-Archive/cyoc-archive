    Since it was a hot, summer day, a couple of kids living in a large apartment complex had decided to take a dip in the pool which was conveniently located right in the center of their apartment complex, between the tennis courts and the indoor weight room.

    It was a blistering 90 degrees outside, it was 5PM in the afternoon, and the kids were just dying to go for a swim after a long day of studying and attending classes at the local high school or middle schools. So, these kids, who all knew each other since they all were neighbors, they met at the pool, bringing along with them inflatable rafts, toy water guns, and a few waterproof beach balls to toss around in the pool.

    There was Alex, a shy 12-year-old boy, who was short and skinny. He had short, black hair and deep, brown eyes. He wasn’t very athletic at all, but he loved to swim in the pool just to escape the heat. Then, you had Michael, the tall blond boy, who was age 13, and he was very athletic. Not too muscular, but Michael was in good shape because he played soccer on weekends. Lastly, you had Adrian, age 15, who was tall and skinny as a board. Adrian was more nerdy than athletic, but he loved to hang out with Michael and Alex and he would do whatever the other two boys did, just so he didn’t have to spend the afternoon all by himself.

    There was a sticky patch of blue liquid drifting around in the pool, appearing like paint or an oil slick, hanging around near the surface of the water. It was darker colored than the rest of the aqua-colored water, so most kids avoided it when swimming in the pool. At some point or the other, Alex’s right arm brushed up on the odd-colored liquid in the pool, and the boy’s arm absorbed the strange chemical right away.

    Right away, Alex started to feel an odd feeling in his arm, so he swam to the shallow end of the pool where the steps were, so he could sit on the edge. His right arm started to swell up with incredible muscle, with his biceps, triceps and forearm bulging with powerful muscle.

    “Holy crap! What’s this?” Alex gasped, as he witnessed his right arm suddenly swell up with muscle. From there, his right pectoral quickly bulged, and then the left pectoral caught up as well. His chest became hard and bulged, making the boy look like a junior bodybuilder! Within moments, his left arms swelled up as well, with his biceps, triceps and forearm inflating until both arms matched each other in size.

    “Whoa! Look at my arms! I’m huge!” Alex cheered, as Michael and Adrian stopped swimming and looked on.

    Within moments, Alex’s stomach was covered with the most developed abdominal muscles ever! His was ripped with muscle, displaying the most amazing washboard abs, only bodybuilders have after years of working out!

    “Dang! Check out Alex’s abs! How did you get those abs like that?” Michael wondered out loud.

    From there, Alex felt his quads bulge tremendously, on both legs, growing thicker and wider at an amazing rate, until they were as wide as tree trunks! Alex had legs as big as pillars! He had cords of muscle running from his waist to his knees, all covered in veins! And, finally, his calves ballooned to the size of mighty soccer balls, sticking out from the back of his legs!

    “Holy crap! What happened to Alex?” Adrian gasped out loud as Alex got up and stepped out of the pool, flexing his arms as water dripped from his incredible, muscular body. The 12-year-old boy had the physique of a junior bodybuilder!

    “Ohhh…. I’m so STRONG!” Alex grinned, flexing each bicep, one at a time.

    Alex then walked up to Michael and lifted the boy up and in the air, carrying him to the deep end of the pool and throwing him in the water! Then, Alex did the same to Adrian!

    Little did Alex realize that one of the boys had touched the blue patch of tainted water, which had originally caused his muscles to grow.

    Who was the next to experience an amazing muscle growth spurt?
