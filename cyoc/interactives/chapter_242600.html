    Confronting herself

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by TheMysteriousFox. 9 days ago O Reality alteration

    Initially, Jennifer was confused on how she was back. She'd undone the very thing that caused her existence, and yet here she was, alive. With an opportunity right in front of her.

    The girl immediately jumped up, briefly seeing that she's back in her own body. "Y-You!" She yelled, watching as the boy whipped around to face her, just as surprised as she was prior. "You're the one who... who set up that lie!" Clearing her throat, she took a step forward, still somewhat disoriented. "You lied to everyone! Tried to crash the sleepover! E-Even..." Her voice went quieter. "Made up everything I thought I'd known..."

    The boy in front of her stepped back. "Wait- Aren't you- How are-" But he was interrupted.

    "E-Explain!" The little girl yelled. "Why?! Why did you do that?" She challenged. "What made you want to do something like that?!"

    Jeff quickly tried to speak up. "I-It seemed like a good idea at the time!" He argued, clearly not prepared for a confrontation. "It gave us a chance to relive how things used to be, and... and it seemed like a good way to bond with Lucy, and-"

    "So why create me for that?!" Jennifer interrupted. "Or Felicity! Why not do it as yourselves?!" She angrily stepped forward. "Why create such a big lie to hide everything?!"

    "I didn't think you two would become your own people." Jeff revealed. "I thought it'd just be me and Alan, but in different bodies! The backstories were for a cover!"

    "That's not an excuse!" Jennifer stepped forward again. "You could've used your existing relationship with Lucy as an explanation! Not... Not create entire fake lives!"
