    Bill selects the merge/split option on his wrist-puter and circles the area where Justin was connected to him. Helpfully he carried him to the sink which he turned on to give him the ability to shower. Meanwhile he rejoins with the solid crotch area making him hole again. On his back there still was the futuristic diving gear he had from his experiments in the sink.
    "Hey I've got an idea", Jeff said, "I know of this place that has a lake and is rather deserted. We could go there." Justin looks back at Bill having cleaned himself sufficiently and started up his flight pack. In mid air he selected the size option to turn himself human sized again. In a flash there were now 3 fairly bulky men standing in the rather small bathroom. "Well it's getting cramped in here", Bill exclaimed. "Let's go there", cheered in Justin. Jeff read out the coordinates and the three entered them into their wrist-puters. As they were dematerializing Bill realized something, "Wait, shouldn't..." and the 3 were gone in a flash of light.

    Split seconds later on a calm lake surrounded by mountains a flash of light appeared. What materialized was a fairly large humanoid figure. As it formed it showed that it was wearing an exosuit, but this one was multi-coloured. The left side was green, the middle was blue and the right side yellow. On its back there was a garbled mixture of Bill's diving gear and Justin's flight pack, probably useless for either of these functions. Below the now wider helmet there was something that could be described as a man's head with three faces, though this doesn't fully describe it. The three faces were the ones Bill, Jeff and Justin had previously when in their toy form. However Jeff's right eye as also Justin's left eye, and Justin's right eye was also Bill's left eye. Between those eyes there were 3 noses and below them 3 mouths, nearly touching each other. As the materialization was finished Bill continued "...we teleport to slightly different positions?" The three suddenly were confused, they moved weirdly even without wanting to move. It took them a while to realize what's been happening. Bill said, "I think we have somehow fused together". He concentrated on moving his right arm and with some minor struggle he was able to move it. "Hey, that's my arm", Justin cried out before Jeff exclaimed, "You mean _our_ arm". "Well you brought us into this mess", Justin tried to shout at Jeff, but since they both shared a head, this was more of an excercise of futility. "Now, would you both shut up and not mess with me, I'm trying to get us fixed", Bill said, and Jeff and Justin tried not to move the limbs. Bill managed to control their wrist-puter and found an option to teleport to multiple locations. He selected it and entered 3 distinct locations nearby, "Here we go"
