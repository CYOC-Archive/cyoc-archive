    Passion Amongst the Dragons

    You were a female dragon, and you realized this when you looked down and saw two giant wings, and a reptilian slit. You had crimson scales, and your body looked exactly like Michael's except you were slightly smaller and you had a feminine build. "I uh...don't know how to react to this, but I'm sure that you're just going to freak out." Michael said

    "I...I'm a female dragon now. H...how did this happen? Why did this happen?" You asked staring down at your new slit mesmerized by the way that it felt. Your voice was now feminine, and had a sweet soft and innocence to its tone.

    "I saw something through the smoke, but I couldn't make out sense of what it was. Are you okay? Your change was more...elaborate than mine." Michael said

    "I think I'm fine, but this feeling is so weird to me. I'm female now Michael." You said

    "I know, but you may want to get up right now. Because whoever or whatever that was knows where we are now, and we need to get out of here." Michael said

    "Oh uh okay then." You said and Michael held out his claw and you used that to stand yourself up. You then looked Michael in the eyes and for some reason you felt attracted to him, and you blushed. Michael seemed to take notice that you were blushing and you saw him blush too under his green scales.

    "If...uh you don't mind me saying, you kind of look...nice like this Henry." Michael said

    "I can say the same for you Michael. Do you think it's because we're both dragons now and I'm female now?" You asked

    "Maybe, but let's not worry about that right now. Can you walk, or run? I can help you if you want me to." Michael said

    "I think I can walk, but running is a different story. Where are we going?" You asked

    "A home that I made, we can live there while we figure this out." Michael said and you nodded.

    You followed Michael as he helped you move on all fours, and for some reason you felt passion towards him like you've never felt before. As Michael helped you, you felt safe and you knew that he would protect you given the situation. Eventually the two of you approached a dark cave, and Michael lead you inside.

    "One thing that I've learned about dragons is that darkness is easy to see through, and yes I do live in a cave but it's only temporary." Michael said

    "Okay, but what do we do now?" You asked

    "We will try to figure this out, and I will try my best to train you to use your instincts. For now we will share the same nest, but I can help you make one if you'd like." Michael said

    "Sure, thank you, I'm not very tired though." You said

    "Yeah, I'm the same way, I think that we're nocturnal naturally. So what do you want to do?" Michael asked
