    I immediately lifted my hands up for balance. The nails were somehow even painted.

    “What did you do?” I asked trying not to panic.
    “I made you more approachable.” Lenexa said.
    “Why?”
    “Because that lonely guy over there has a magic box with pictures that can help me.” she said, putting her arm around my shoulder.
    “And if I talk to him, you’ll turn me back?”
    “Of course. You’re my anchor to this world, not a doll to play with. I wouldn’t do this unless I thought there was a better way.” she said as she lightly squeezed one of my breasts.

    “Oh, the usual, what he knows about the gods, about the wonderful heroine Lenexa, and how I might contact the other goddesses.”

    “Hi.” I said in the sweetest voice I could muster. It sounded like Mickey Mouse if he was sucking helium.
    “Hello.” said the nerd as he shut his laptop to make better eye contact with me.
    “Hi.” I said again, using a much deeper voice.
    “Can I help you?” he asked with a blank stare. I involuntarily giggled.

    He tried to maintain eye contact, but I could feel his gaze lower to beneath my neck.
    “I’ve seen you in my Greek History Class.”
    “Really?”
    “Ya.” I replied, and my heart stopped, when I realised my index finger was twirling a lock of my hair.
    “So…”
    “I was wondering if you could help me with something.”
    “Sure, ya, anything.” he said.
    “The legend of Lenexa. I think she was related to Hera, and the Amazons.”
    “Give me a second.” he said as he opened his laptop and started typing in some stuff.
    I could feel Lenexa’s spirit guiding my movements slightly like animators do in Claymation. I gave her a harsh stare. She gave a huge smile and took a step back.
    “Ya, there is nothing on her.”
    “Are you sure?”
    “Yes. There is nothing on the internet, there is nothing in my notes. But it’s strange, not even a minute before you got here, so guy came up and asked about her too.”

    I shrugged and gave and forced smile.
    “So what’s your name?” he asked.
    “Uh…uh…Heather.” I said after looking at the posters and signs around us for some name to use.
    “Well, that’s a lovely name Heather. I’m Mike. Please to meet you.”
    “Hi.” I said as I reached out my dainty hand to shake his. His hand shake was just as awkward as mine. Clearly not a lot of people made physical contact with him. He also hadn’t showed that day. My nostrals seemed to be a bit more powerful since my transformation to Heather.
    “Well, sorry to waste your time.” I told him as I stood up.
    “No, it’s fine. I enjoyed it. I learned something.”
    “Me too…um…bye.” I said as I turned around and kept walking.

    Lenexa squeezed my ass, causing me to let out a feminine yelp, as a quickly strode away from Mike.
    “See, I told you that was a waste of time.” I told her.
    “No, I learned something.” she said.
    “What was that?”
    “That you are adorable.”
    I raised my middle finger and stuck it out at her. She didn’t get the meaning of it.
    “Can I change back now.”
    “Right.” she said, and with a bit of a shock, I felt myself instantly grow larger as my proportions returned to normal.
    “I learned that someone erased me from existence.” she blurted out.
