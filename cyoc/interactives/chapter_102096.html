    ... Was out in the open area of the roof of the school filled with Gargoyle students (About fifteen to thirty) chatting to one another. Max felt like he's the only human turned gargoyle in the class and was starting to feel embarrassed. That is until he felt a human hand on his left shoulder. "You ok Max?" He turned to look who was talking to him and sees a young man who looks young in his 20's or a bit later than that. His eyes are golden yellow, his hair is white as snow or silver-like and his face is calm. He wears a white scarf but his outfit he wears is ebony black and a sketchbook and a sheath of a sword is hanging in the back by the straps of his outfit. "Y-yeah. I thought this is only a gargoyle class, you're a human." Max said. "I'm your instructor for this week. Your real defense instructor is on leave for a week." The white haired man said. "Uh.. I know this is awkward but-" he was interrupted by his week long instructor, "It's ok, I know. You were human yesterday." Max was dumbfounded and asks, "How did you know?!" the instructor pointed his golden yellow eyes and replies, "My eyes indicate your soul is human along with the other gargoyle students. They let me know who is human, who is something else. And I say you're not the only one here Max." This makes Max feel more relieved. "Thank you instructor..."
    "Exodus Nobeless Alphania. You can just call me Exodus or Exo, Max." Exodus said with a gentle smile. "Come on, meet up with your new classmates and I'll start the class in a moment." Exodus said as Max nods and goes off and away from Exodus as he heads to the edge of the roof.

    Class soon starts and the students began to quiet down to murmurs as Exodus begins to speak loud enough for them to hear, "My name is Exodus, I'm your week long instructor for this beginning part of the new defense class-roof of C9. And I welcome you. Your real instructor, Darius is out on sick leave. So, we're gonna start on our first lesson." He then snaps his fingers and several crates came out of nowhere and landed beside Exodus and the murmurs grew a bit. Max was wondering what is in there. "Before we get to the basic lessons of flying and other neat things I need to ask you guys if any of you are students who doesn't know how to use offensive spells like throwing a fireball or a bolt of lightning so here we go." The students begin to murmur again as Max looks on as Exodus stands in front of the cases and opens them up with a kick showing a variety of firearms from WWII to modern day engraved with enchanted letters on the side. "These weapons are enchanted with exorcism and other spells to combat the demons or other creatures of pure darkness. They won't kill your fellow classmates, but they can sting like hell for at least a couple minutes. However, if you non offensive spell mages not into these, we can go into the old times and use these." He then moves on to another set to the left and opens them the same way and shows a variety of bows, crossbows, and swords from different parts of the world and engraved with enchantment as well. "And one rule, do not temper with re-enchanting the weapons you have picked for hurting your classmates; if you do, you will be punished by me, your instructor, or the headmaster and your weapon is confiscated. Do you understand?" They nodded. One of the gargoyle students raised her hand and asked him, "Uh, does instructor Darius knows what you're doing? I thought weapons were not to be permitted in class?" Exodus replies, "Don't worry, I told the headmaster and your instructor of the situation. Besides, that only applies to normal mages beneath you guys that are not allowed to use or bring weapons to the school. You students are different and you're told to protect the school remember?" The female "ooh'ed" and lowered her hand just accepting on what's going on. "One more thing, they will be So raise your hand if you don't know offensive spells." he called them out. Between one-fourth and one-half didn't now how to including Max. He then picked Max, "OK Max, you choose first. Firearms? Or Ancient Times? Or Hand to Hand?"
