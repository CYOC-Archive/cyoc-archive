    “I don’t want to get bullied again, Ty!” Kent groaned. “I’m so sick of it! Why don’t these dumb jocks just leave me alone? I’m tired of this!”

    “Kent! Nothing’s gonna happen to you! Quit worrying, okay?” Ty assured him, as he rested his hands on Kent’s shoulders.

    “Listen, I consider you my little brother. I’ll be watching you wherever you go. Nobody will even touch you, okay? You’ll be the biggest, the strongest, the smartest, and the tallest kid around, whenever the need comes up! Just trust me on this, okay?” Ty added. “Just relax!”

    Kent just took a deep breath.

    “Hey, time to go to class. I got History next. And You?” Ty asked him, as he got up and grabbed his backpack.

    “I have P.E., the one class I hate the most. And guess who’s in the same class with me?” Kent grumbled.

    “Darren?” Ty wondered.

    “You got it! Oh, how fun…” Kent replied sarcastically.

    “Don’t worry, Kent!” Ty told him, as they walked out the cafeteria. “Big brother is watching you, everywhere and all the time!”

    “I wish you really were my brother…” Kent sighed.

    Suddenly, Kent’s jet black hair began to change color. The straight hair became a little curlier, and within moments Kent was blond. His glasses disappeared into thin air! Kent’s eyesight was suddenly improved, so he didn’t need glasses anymore. Ty wished for Kent to have 20/20 vision! Kent’s face became longer, although his body was still a few inches shorter than Ty. Other than that, Kent now looked just like a younger version of Ty!

    “There, now you ARE my little brother!” Ty chuckled, as he surveyed his handiwork.

    Kent and Ty parted ways, although Ty was watching him, using his power. Ty could see, hear and feel everything that Kent saw, heard or felt.

    Kent walked to the locker room and changed. As soon as he walked into the gym, Darren walked up to him and blocked his way.

    “We need to talk, shrimp!” Darren growled.

    “Uh oh….” Kent whispered.

    Luckily, when Kent looked around, he noticed that Peter was also in the class with him. But, could Peter help him out, in case he needed help?
