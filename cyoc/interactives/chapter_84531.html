    "Isn't there anything you can do?" Sally asked nervously.

    "Well, we were talking with Talia, and she actually came up with a stopgap," Snowy commented, "It would allow Charlie to be re-vaccinated against turning people into Scorpkats, but since the transformation would begin and put on hold... the re-vaccination wouldn't outright prevent finishing the complete transformation back into a one hundred percent Chakat and you would still be producing the new vaccine... or at least that was hir idea..."

    "What is it?" Charlie asked curiously.

    "What do you know about the manticore?" Maurice asked.

    "The manticore?" Sally asked.

    "A mythological creature, like myself, and would be a sort of naturally existing Kat... though unlike sphinxes, manticores were more brutish than tricky and while humans at times revered sphinxes for our riddles and cunning, they hunted manticores into extinction for fear of being eaten," Talia spoke up beside Midnight, "but in body design they had had the head and body of lions with the tail of a scorpion. I figured that since Maurice and Snowy are the original designers of the virus they could a way to begin your return to being a Chakat, but would have a sort of time delay or pause to it that would allow you to keep at least the scorpion's stinger that would allow you to provide the base for the new vaccine."

    "They were Persian maneaters weren't they?" Midnight asked nervously, cuddling up to hir mate's wings.

    "The mythical ones were, but they were all hunted into extinction and their remains burned completely," Talia assured hir, "No trace of their DNA remains. And Charlie would merely become a Chakat-Scorpkat hybrid."

    Maurice and Snowy nodded and punched a few buttons on a nearby computer where they had been working at earlier to pull up a an image of Chakat that resembled Charlie before the Vatican outbreak, with the only difference being that shi had a scorpion's tail.

    "Can you do this?" Sally asked.

    "With Charlie, yes," Maurice nodded, "with Brent, no. When Charlie vaccinated hir, Brent gained the same sort of transformation lock that the Foxkat Vaccine provided with the previous strain. We did vaccinate Brent with the Foxkat vaccine in case shi fully joins the team and his here from time to time and might come in contact with the old strain of the virus."

    "So you will need to recover from what those Scorpkats in the Vatican did," Snowy commented, "and feel fortunate that Charlie can go back."

    "I can recover," Sally answered, "I just need time to recover..."

    Maurice and Snowy nodded.

    "How long will it take to get this reversion serum ready?" Charlie asked.

    "We have the working theory right now," Maurice answered, "but it will likely take a week or two to engineer the retrovirus to pause at your tail until we inject a final "hormone booster" and to make sure all the right animal DNA is in order."

    Charlie looked to Sally who looked hopeful at getting hir soft and fuzzy mate back, and to feel hir purr against hir.

    "The only thing left is to figure out why the scientist engineered a new virus and the vaccine to that virus," Snowy then spoke.
