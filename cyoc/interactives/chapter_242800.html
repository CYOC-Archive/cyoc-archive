    "Actually..." Alan started, clearly thinking about something. "All of the friends Lucy invited are girls, right?" He asked, and upon the nod of confirmation from Jeff, he continued. "Why don't we turn into girls, to fit in some more?"

    Jeff shrugged. "I mean, I don't see a problem with that logic." He first edited his own profile to make himself a girl, changing his name to Jennifer, a name that called to him for some reason. Moving over to Alan's profile, he asked, "Any name in particular you want to pick?"

    "Well, my mom did tell me that if I was a girl, she'd have called me Elaine. So I may as well go with that." The boy answered, and Jeff got to work, setting that name when he made the transformation turn Alan into a girl. With that decided, Jeff gave one last look over the changes, then clicked on the confirm button, initializing the transformation.

    Almost immediately, the two boys felt a powerful pressure on their heads and feet, the feeling of an invisible force attempting to crush them vertically, their heights dropping rapidly. The sensation was far worse than either were expecting, a pain flaring up as both boys felt their balls get sucked back into their bodies, the testicles digging through their skin as their internal organs restructured themselves, their dicks shrinking and inverting into a tunnel straight through their bodies, leading to a newly forming womb.

    As their bodies shrunk and reformed, both ex-boys felt a dizziness as the Chronivac supplied them with new memories of their altered reality. Jennifer was lucky she was already sitting, eyes closed too tight to see Elaine stumble around a bit, before eventually collapsing on the ground to try and ease the pain in her legs. During this time, the room began transforming into that of a 9-year-old girl's.

    No more than a few minutes after the change was initialized, the pain and pressure ceased, both girls' clothes being shrunk and transformed to better fit their new forms. "Ugh... That was horrible..." Jeff, now Jennifer, groaned out, recovering from the aftereffects first. "A-Alan? You alright?" She asked, finally opening her eyes.

    On the ground in front of her was a girl, no older than Lucy, wearing a pair of tight-fitting black leggings, along with a white t-shirt with rainbows designed onto the sleeves. Hand on her head, she looked up. "I-I regret everything..." With a groan of her own, a sharp gasp followed as she realized. "W-We'll have to go through that again when it's over, w-won't we..."

    The shorter girl looked up at the one in the chair, long yet messy black hair going down her back as she wore a pinkish white sweater, a red heart above the chest - Sewn on, not part of the design. Below it was a skirt, a pattern of interchanging colored stripes with frills at the bottom, and beneath that, a pair of shorts were visible, stitching near the hem of the skirt suggesting they're also one piece. The shorts were loose enough to where it was impossible to tell if anything was on under it, but the leg holes tight enough to prevent anyone from looking up, even despite Elaine's view perfectly lining up with it.

    "Yeah.." The girl in the chair replied. "This better be worth it..." Standing up, Jennifer took a moment to adjust to her new size, as well as clear her mind of the haze caused by having memories forcibly inserted into it. Upon spotting the rainbow sleeves Elaine was wearing, she couldn't help but ask in a teasing tone, "Did you take wearing your pride on your sleeve too literally?"

    The latter looked at the former, confused, for a moment, before looking at her sleeves and seeing the rainbow designs, eyes widening as she immediately went defensive. "I'm not-" She started, before cutting herself off, the defensiveness disappearing alongside it. Pausing for a moment, she finally mumbled, "...Actually, I guess I would be considered gay right now... so, um, maybe?"

    Jennifer giggled at her friend's reaction. "'Least you're honest about it." Once the giggling stopped, she spoke again. "Anyway! What now?" She asked. "We've got some time to kill before Lucy's friends arrive, so, aside from getting used to calling each other by our new names, what should we do while we wait?"
