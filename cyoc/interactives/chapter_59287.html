    And so, 400 years later, the rain came again. But, it never stopped. For years, it rained. By the time it did stop raining, the world was a huge ball of water. Every single fox person had died. All, exept two. The two that survived were the ones that were buried. Danielle and Brandon. They made thier way out, and rid of the water.

    "We have much work to do." Bradon said, no expression on his face.

    "Yes, we do." Danielle replied.

    After many months, The world was reshaped into what it used to be. But The Two Didnt Die. Instead. The made an island in the sky; Using all the powers necesary. On That island, The two fox people turned into the great Trees: Danielle to the Tree of Hope; for everyone needed hope to get by in times of depression. However, Brandons Violent side got the best of him. He became the Tree of Vengance; The tree of Warfare, to be exact. He Had the power to grant vengance to anyone who was treated Unworthy as a same people. Every 10 Years, A sacrafice is made to the two trees. The Tree Of Hope gets A sick, Young kit that wont survive The next winter. The Tree of Vengace gains another Soul of A Male Prisoner who betrayed his land. With these Sacrifices, The land was garunteed goiod for hunting, as well as making new homes to those who need them.

    If a sacrifice was not made to one of the trees, A plague was brought about the land. Either White Throat; a condition where a fox persons throat fur turned white, and they would be marked as unholy and killed, or PRIMA; A condition that fox people get when they are int the rain for too long. The slowly turn brown, and turn into a smaller counter-part of the holy trees.
