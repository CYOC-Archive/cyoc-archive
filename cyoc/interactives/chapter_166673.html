    Mika and Claudia now found themselves in a brutal battle with Malak. The magically enhance demon was more than holding his own, withstanding the coeds onslaught. Mika now ducked as her flaming tigress fiance sailed passed.

    "I'll swallow your soul," Malak bellowed.

    Mika smirked, tightening her grip on her enchanted blades. "Come get some."

    Malak roared, leaping into the air as he brought his fist crashing into the ground. Mika dodged, whipping the blades around as she slashed Malak across the chest.

    "We need to finish this," the kitsune Mika said. Mika nodded, she sensed the others were in trouble. They needed to end this fight and help.

    Claudia extricated herself from the rubble she found herself in, gazing at her fiance the flaming avatars face formed into a smile. Mika was moving at speed Claudia did not think possible. The young woman now deftly dodged the demons attacks, slashing it with quick and brutal strikes. No matter what Malak tried, he couldnt land a blow on the enhanced sorceress.

    Mika looked up at the mammoth demon, seeing a look on the creatures face. Malak was afraid, he had no counter for Mikas speed and strength. The enhanced demon flailed wildly attempting to crush Mika. Mika could only smirk, she had this victory won. Dodging another strike, she now whipped her blades around her leaving several slashes across the demons legs and chest. Malak collapsed the demon snarling as he gazed up at Mika.

    "It's over," she smiled.

    Mika kept high into the air, performing a flip as she sent the blades rocketing at Malaks chest. The sound of flesh being cleaved pierced the air as the blades went straight through. As she withdrew the blades Malak let out a blood choked gurgle as he collapsed to the ground.

    "Mika are you okay."

    Mika turned towards her fiance, watching as Claudia's flaming tigress form dissipated, revealing the coed. Before she could answer she heard a roar behind her.

    Turning the two coeds were shocked to see Malak on his feet once more. The demons face filled with murderous rage. Before he could attack the two were shocked to see a gigantic sneaker come crashing down on the demon, a spray of viscera coating the two coeds.

    "Sorry."

    Mika and Claudia craned their necks upwards as they beheld the teenage Samantha. The Samoan teen had used her magic to increase her height to around 70 feet tall. As Samantha lifted her foot they could hear her let out a disgusted moan.

    "Oh that's just nasty."

    Mika and Claudia agreed, gazing at the puddle of organic material that had once been Malak. "Yeesh," Claudia grunted. "Dont think hes getting up from that one."

    As the coeds gazed at Malaks remains they suddenly felt an incredible tremor shake the park. As the three braced themselves they cautiously looked around. "What was that," Mika asked.

    "I dont know," Claudia said quietly. "But we better find the others."
