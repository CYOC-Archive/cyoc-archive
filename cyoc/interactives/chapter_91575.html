    Cathy slowly got up. As she did, golden patches of fur appeared all over her body. It quickly formed a fine coat that enveloped her body. Her ears moved up her head, growing large and somewhat pointed. Cathy found that her hearing had improved dramatically.

Her blonde ponytail turned red, and grew longer, until the band snapped, letting her majestiv mane fall around her shoulders. She had grown by at least 2 feet, and her clothes tore as her arms and legs grew larger with the formation of new muscles.

    Her nose flattened against her face, and she smiled as it pushed out into a lion's muzzle. Long whisker grew above her lip, and her eyes changed, her formerly round pupils becoming slits.

    Cathy stood upright, a seven foot tall lioness with beautiful, long, red hair.

    She smiled, showing off her new fangs to Scott, who cringed in fear.

    She reached into her purse, and pulled out a quarter. "You said you wanted payment?" She chucked the coin at his face. "Here. Go buy yourself a gumball. And choke on it."

    She turned around, and slapped him in the face with her tail. Then, she strode over to the entrance, opened the door, and left Scott forever.
