    Hydro Massage to Hyper Muscle

    As Ty strolled around the large gym, he passed by the expansive front desk near the entrance where people were gathering around and asking for information regarding gym memberships, classes, and sessions for tanning and massages. He spotted one teen who were standing on the opposite side of the large front counter where a huge touch-screen computer was set up. One of the staff members was helping the boy, and Ty picked up on the conversation as he walked by.

    “Sorry man, but the hydro massage beds are only for those members with our premium memberships who are over the age of eighteen. It says on the computer that you’re only sixteen years old.” The lady said to the boy.

    “But my dad is a member here as well. I just started working out and I’m a little sore. Is there really no way I can just get a few minutes on the massage bed?” the boy asked sadly.

    Another staff member walked up. He was younger and he looked the boy over.

    “Are you Derek?” he asked the teen.

    “Yeah!” the tall but skinny teen replied. He had just started working out, wearing just a t-shirt and shorts. He was almost 6 feet tall, but he had very little or no muscle on him, which was the whole reason why he started coming to the gym.

    “This is what I’m gonna do. It’s a bit slow right now, so I’ll give you ten minutes on the massage bed, alright?” the man said.

    “Aww, nice! Thank you so much!” Derek replied.

    Ty heard the conversation and thought this was a fun way to use his powers in a very subtle way at the gym.

    “They’re letting the kid on the hydro massage after all. Hmm… How about… I wish that for every minute the kid lays on the hydro massage bed, he gains five pounds of raw muscle, evenly distributed over his entire body!”

    “Go into room where the glass doors are and lay down on the bed on the left. The bed starts automatically in one minute. Use the touch screen to adjust the speed and the water pressure!” the man explained to the teen.

    “Thanks, mister!” the teen responded politely as he walked over to the small room and opened the tall glass doors. He laid down on the large, wide bed on the left as he waited patiently for the bed to start.

    Derek lay there and relaxed in the quiet, dark room. The large bed was soft and comfortable. The only thing he could hear was the sound of soft, relaxing music playing in the background. This was all interrupted when the water jets inside of the bed kicked in, and the massage started.

    The hydro massage bed would run a metal bar containing water jets across a rail inside of the bed. The bar was hooked up to a water pump which used warm, distilled water. This water get sprayed against the inside of the upper layer of the water bed, which provides the sensation of a water massage to whoever lays on the bed, So, for the next ten minutes, Derek laid there on the bed as he felt the tingling sensation of the water jets spraying against the inside of the top layer of the bed as the jet sprays traveled from the boy’s neck all the way down to his feet and then back up to his neck again, going in a constant loop.

    Of course, nobody realized that Ty had added an additional feature to the hydro massage bed, thanks to his powers! While the boy would lay there, his muscles would gradually and slowly increase in size for the duration of the session, which was usually ten minutes. Derek’s arms grew thicker, and his shoulders became wider. His pectorals bulged a bit from his chest while abdominal muscles formed over his stomach. His quads enlarged and become wider and thicker while his calves inflated, all at the same time!

    When the massage bed stopped and the session ended, Derek slowly opened his eyes and stood up. He felt strange. He felt bigger, heavier and stronger. What happened? He walked out and strolled to the men’s locker room where he saw his reflection in the large mirror for the first time.

    “WHOA!” he gasped when he saw what had happened. The boy had gained about fifty pounds of muscle!

    “What the hell?” he gasped, flexing his huge arm. “I was skinny as a rail a minute ago! Where did all this muscle come from? Did this happen during the massage?”

    Derek walked back to the front desk and contemplated what to do next….
