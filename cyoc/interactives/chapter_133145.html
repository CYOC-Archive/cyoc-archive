    11:47pm. Mary struggled to get to sleep for half an hour. It was all the noise from outside, so loud it felt like the sound waves themselves were invading Mary's home. Mary slowly moved to the bedroom window, exhausted and wanting to sleep. She noticed next door a party was going on, an outdoor party. "Who would be running a party like this?" Mary whined, scanning the outside view from her window.

    Just as she thought. Teenagers. Loud, obnoxious teenagers. Trying to get drunk and trying to get laid, with the worst music possible playing as loud as Krakatoa erupting.

    Mary just rolled her eyes, until she noticed on her bedside table, something that could change the atmosphere of the party.

    The Vials.

    Mary had an idea. An idea that made her smile like The Grinch. Spike the drinks with the vials, and the party will be over.

    11:53pm. Mary was dressed in all black to hide herself from anyone spotting her tails and her new cat ears that were conveniently hidden away in a beanie. Mary was hiding behind a white plastic table covered in a big table cloth. On top was a bunch of red plastic cups. Mary opened her handful of vials and poured them at the same time into the red plastic cups.

    All she had to do is wait...
