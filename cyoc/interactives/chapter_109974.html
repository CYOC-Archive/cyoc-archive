    The Flower Fairy and the Flower Shrine

    After about ten minutes admiring the flower, Kylee finally suggests you both head inside. Mere moments after you're inside the house, the flower stirs. The blossom slowly opens, and a bright, rainbow colored glow shines out from within. From out of the glow emerges an 8 inch figure; a girl with pink hair and a gown of flower petals. Affixed to her back, were four large, fairy wing shaped flower petals; and if you were to examine her closely, you would note the gown itself grew from her body as well.

    The flowerlike fairy looked around in confusion. "This isn't the woods this flower should've led me to." Setting feet resembling dress shoes with thorn-like high heels down onto the stone walkway, her petal-like wings coiled tightly into flower buds before folding up behind her back, resting over the back of her skirt. "What IS this place?" She asked herself in awe. "It's like... some kind of flower shrine. Did somebody move the flower here? It would explain why the portal wouldn't work before; it must've been cut off from the soil."

    The fairy looked up at the fountain, glimpsing the figures upon the rim. Curious, she unfurled her wings and fluttered up to get a closer look. "It seems whoever built this shrine also has a reverence for our kind." She commented upon seeing the fairy statues decorating the fountain. A more thorough examination of the surrounding area revealed the rather hard to miss house near the front of the garden.

    Never has the fairy seen a structure like that before. "What in the many realms is that?!" She then remembered something from her studies on The Mundane Realm. "Wait, could that be a dwelling of humans? Did they build the flower shrine? I must find out!"
