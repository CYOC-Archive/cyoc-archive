    Quickly thinking Erin replaced shop with Lake giving,

    "Tina, Trish, and Sindy are trotting on two cow legs while going to the lake to get a drink. This would get them out of the building she thought, fumbling the confirm button.

    "oh I know So like how about we checkout that lake nearby, you know the one south of second avenue, behind the industrial district." Tina said to her friends, clearly seeing they were both as thirsty as her.

    "Yeah that sounds nice, I hope it's not too far my hooves are killing me" Trish said, lifting up her leg and giving it a shake.

    "Yeah mine too, maybe we can sit down there for a bit and relax too" Sindy said, none of them seeing anything odd from the situation.

    " I know, so are mine but its like not that far comeon" tina said motioning for Sindy and Trish to follow.

    "moooo" they both said in unison, Tina guessed it was a confirmation before turning around to walk out of the mall.

    Erin who overheard the whole conversation decided to give it a few minutes before following, considering she knows were they're heading. After a brief wait and a few moments to herself Erin got up and left to head to lake.

    Upon arriving the girls marvelled, crystal blue water lay before them, stretching almost as far as the eye can see, surrounded by a set of grazing fields with some animals of all kinds. a summer breeze lofted through the air rustling their hair as the sun was slowly setting upon the night sky as early evening approached.

    "It's nice out here, peaceful even," Tina remarked as she stood up and approached the lake taking in its scent with her protruding nose.

    "Well what are we waiting for lets drink! " Trish said as she and Sindy joined Tina

    arching their backs they dunked their mouths in the lake, using their thick cow tongues to drink the water, gallon after gallon. occasionally having to steady themselves on their slightly weaker human forearms.

    Erin arrived just as the three began drinking, slightly taken back with how casually they were drinking lake water, hopefully, they have the stomachs for it she giggled to herself as she opened the app,
    cycling through many many suggestions, she was looking for something specific, something she thinks the girls have needed for a little while.
    giving a smile even the grinch would frown at, she saw.

    "Tina, Trish and Sindy are drinking water from a lake while using their hands to balance"

    Perfect she thought, speedily replacing hand with hooves and hitting confirm.

    Instantly upon hitting confirm the girls arms began to morph, fingers melded together as the skin began to harden like before and it slowly formed a hooves. The girls continue to drink unaware of the sounds of their bones cracking and remoulding themselves, spreading up the arm to the shoulder as new joints and bones sprout until their entire bone structure resembles that of a common cow.

    Finding their footing a bit more the girls no longer needing to steady themselves they drank in earnest, quenching a mighty thirst. Soon their bellies began to fill as their bodies bloated out, each of them having the shapely body of an adolescent calf, yet to reach full size and maturity.

    Erin laughed a bit, not as much as before though... somehow seeing them peaceful like this kind of took the fun out of toying with them. Looking down at her phone seeing the countless changes she's made, Erin sighed a little.
    perhaps she should just undo everything, or maybe just finish them off? they seem happy.
