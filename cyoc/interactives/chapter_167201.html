    "I have an idea..." said Des calling Ganymede over. "We need to give a kid a taste of true love to get back into Bliss' good favors. We have a woman looking for her husband, and her husband doesn't remember who he is and is currently somebody else. Ganymede, keep the woman here, and prepare a drink to turn either of the boys into husband material. Whichever we find first will fix at least one of our problems and buy us time to figure out the other, at least temporarily."

    Anton's face contorted, "True love DOESN'T work like that..."

    "But shifting perspectives along with your blessing DOES." Des interrupted... "If we find the boy we scared off, we can combine our powers and give the kid a wild ride until we find this John guy and restore your original true-love scenario, then we can rejuvenate the kid and send him on his way with stories of nailing a MILF on his vacay. Besides, if Ganymede can make John forget his decades long love of her in a minute, he can remove love that lasted a few days."

    Anton huffed but didn't dispute the logic and technically, Shannon was now under Des' purview since her love of John was no longer reciprocated.

    The gods nodded in acceptance of the plan and Ganymede described the boy John had become, then called to Shannon as she was about to exit the bar. "Sorry miss, were you looking for someone in particular?"

    Shannon headed back to the bar unsure of what to do with herself, "Yes, my husband, he said he was coming for a drink and I was wondering what happened to him." Ganymede casually slid a drink over and lulled her into a common trap the gods used. She would stay there for days so long as she gets an occasional conversation, refill, and can use the restroom.

    Anton, Logan, and Des slipped out looking for either of the youths. Des was hoping to find Derrick first to make up with Bliss, Anton was hoping to recover John first, and Logan didn't care but was enjoying his brother's rekindled sense of purpose.
