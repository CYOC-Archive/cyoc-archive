    Suddenly, it felt like the wind was picking up; the crops swayed with the sudden breeze and birds flew in all directions.
    "Sure is windy, huh?" Cally asked.
    "Funny, ol' Doc Hubert didn't say anything about a windy day..." Daisy said while looking up.

    Cally immediately noticed two things: Daisy's smile dropping, and the wind moving in a circular motion.
    'Now what?' she mentally groaned.
    She craned her head to the sky and was greeted by the roar of a helicopter overhead.

    It lowered into the crops, the letters on its side were plain as day: CDC. More choppers decended on the farmland as armored men dropped in and tackled the girls to the ground.
    "What the hell's going on?!" Cally yelled in surprise.

    A man in a hazmat suit came in and took cheek swabs from both girls. When Cally's was run through a small handheld device, it turned green.
    "We found our serum! Return to the Safe Zone, bring the civilians with us!" the man yelled.
    Before either girl could even ask, they were yanked apart and ushered into seperate helicopters.
