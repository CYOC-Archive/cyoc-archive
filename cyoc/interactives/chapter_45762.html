Would it work to decrease the masculine or feminine attributes of the target without actually changing the target's gender?

The hair on his arms also vanishes and his shirt changes, just as his pants did, again becoming more tight fitting and gender neutral in appearance and color, changing from gray to red.

    All in all the results are very interesting.

    Instantly his hair lightens to blond and flows down his back in long, silky tresses, stopping when it reaches waist length.

Between his hair and body he looks very much like a very flat chested woman with unfortunately narrow hips, "So, yeah, you can hang out in the arcade while I get my hair done, then we can do something together."
