    As Gloria went to a nearby playground not far away to play on it, Katie and Kellie looked over at three women who had been sitting on a bench since they arrived. The three of them all looked like they were in their early or mid fifties at best.

    "Those three look like they'd be nice for Gloria." Kellie suggested as Katie nodded an the two of them proceeded to talk them first.

    "Hello there, it's a really beautiful day, isn't it?" Katie asked the three women.

    "I suppose so, the sun is out, the weather's nice and there are children playing everywhere." A woman with black hair who looked like the oldest of the three said to Katie.

    "We're babysitting, are you three babysitting too?" Kellie asked them now.

    "Oh no, all our daughters are well grown up now. But they're too busy to have kids of their own. We're supposed to be meeting them here soon." A woman with blonde hair that had lost it's bright shine explained to the girls.

    "But as usual, they are late. I told my girl, she always has to be on time when she meets her mother." The woman with grey chestnut hair said tapping her foot.

    "She'll be coming soon enough, Jackie, you just have to be a little more patient is all." The black haired woman said to the chestnut haired woman named Jackie.

    "I know, Diane, but I kind of think your daughter and Felicia's daughter are sort of a bad influence on her." Jackie said as she pointed to the third woman as well.

    "Oh come on now, they're just young. They'll understand responsibility one day when they have kids." Felicia said.

    "If they ever have kids." Diane sighed.

    Kellie and Katie both knew what they would have to do now with the three women and pretty soon their daughters.

    "It was nice talking to you three, excuse us." Katie pardoned her and Kellie as they went a little ways away and Katie wished for the three of them to return to being 10 years old and become friends with Gloria.

    Katie and Kellie watched as the three women began to regress. Their hair began to get brighter again and their skin the wrinkles on their skin they had smoothed out over time. Now when the three of them were reaching their thirties, the three of them were almost in awe at their beauty that they had regained, their additional weight also soon disappeared and Katie and Kellie had to admit when the women became teenagers that they were very beautiful. Now however, they were going backwards in puberty and lost their breasts as their chests began to flatten, and now their clothes transformed as well to fit their new 10 year old ages.

    Katie and Kellie now approached the three to see how their transformation went.

    "Hello there girls." Kellie greeted them.

    "Hi Miss Kellie, Hi Miss Katie." The three adorable now 10 year old girls greeted.

    "Did you three know that Gloria is over at the playground right now?" Katie told them.

    "She is? That's just like her to get started playing before we get here." 10 year old Jackie said.

    "Come on, let's go over there already!" 10 year old Diane said as she began to ran over and catch up with Gloria.

    "Hey, wait for us!" 10 year old Felicia said as she and Diane ran right behind her.

    Katie and Kellie watched as Gloria happily began to play with her friends. New memories began to come into their minds as Kellie had usually watched the three other girls along with Gloria to earn some babysitting money. Katie and Kellie sat down on the bench where the former women had been and wrapped their arms around each other's shoulders as they sat down and watched the four girls continue to play.
