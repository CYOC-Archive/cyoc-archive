    Professor Simmons cleared his throat and tried to make his first wish make the most of his remorse. "I make my first wish for all those who here and about were changed from human's to become either part or totally goat's of form and mind!"

    A growling roar came from the Medallion as if the evil spirit was less than thrilled to revert those changed to goats back to their original state.

    Suddenly the camp was filled with moaning and groaning as the reformation began. Pan too, fell to his knees and then flat face down to the ground.

    A screaming noise came from the nearby corral as Jimmy, Wilma, and Simon too were changing back to gain once again their humanity.

    Seeing that his wish was working and working well the Professor walked toward the corral and two oversexed horses.

    "Jenny, John, come to me and be welcomed back into your humanity!" called out the Professor with an offer to return to what they once were.

    Quickly John plodded over his huge draft horse legs and hooves carried him toward the Man of the hour. A black shaft still slimed and wet from his last conquest swung long and free as he took each step forward.

    "Jenny, come girl and find your true self!" called the Professor.

    Jenny stood looking about and just turned her dun coloured rump to the Professor and hung her head.

    "Really Jenny, you would prefer to be a stallion animal rather than be what you were born?" asked the Professor. "Please come girl and join your twin brother in being human and achieve rather than live for sexual pleasure as this animal!" beckoned the Professor.

    Jenny had made her decision and stood among the circle of female mules.

    "My second wish of the spirits is to change John and all those humans who were made horses expect Jenny who whenever she might wish for it will then change back to her youthful girlish self!" ordered the Professor.

    Again the Medallion spirit let off a grumbled groaning growl.

    John the draft horse seemed to melt in size as his horse body took on human qualities. Somewhere out in the plateau to the West stood two happy horses who began as John was to change and gain their humanity. Even as they were changing a certain Python snake made stallion stood content now to graze and ignore the groans of his companions.

    It was almost dark when sweaty and naked Abner and Louise walked into the encampment. Wilma was first to see them and grabbed two blankets, ran to greet them back to those who loved them for more than just a mate.

    As the campfire roared in a blaze the Professor sat in a circle of quiet students all whom were reflecting about the experience of being made animal. Each told of his or her feeling as they changed and the monstrous lust for sexual pleasures or conquest and domination they felt. A circle to themselves the native porters sat talking of their change and some who would have remained as nannies' and those who were happy to be human again.

    Frank the prime speaker for the student group took notice that Jenny was not among those saved from beastlier existence. "Where's Jenny?' he asked aloud.

    All turned to look at the Professor some with expressions of wonder and others with faces filled with disgust as if he'd left her as an animal for some evil reason.

    The Professor looked back into those faces looking at him and told of Jenny's action back at the corral. He told of his portion to which his wish for those as horses to return to being human would when she might wish to be such, would then happen. Till then as John had asked to do she was to be shipped home as a stallion and left to John for safe keeping. Then all faces melted into a look of satisfied contentment knowing that maybe someday Jenny would be herself again.

    "Professor, make a wish for your own future!" suggested Frank as the others looked again.
