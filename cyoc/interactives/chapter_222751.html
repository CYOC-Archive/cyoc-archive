    "You still haven't answered my question" the woman asked, Antonio looked up at her in annoyance, he didn't even know this woman, why was she trying to pry?
    "How did you survive a fall like that sir?" She repeated, Antonio let out a sigh as he leaned back, holding the bar rail for support letting out a sigh of relief before looking the blonde woman in the eye "I guess....i was just lucky, better to count my stars than question how I survived, I'm sat here and that's what matters to me" he said with a groan, sighing in satisfaction as the barkeeper slid a shot glass of whiskey into his hand, raising the glass in thanks to the man he threw his head back, downing the drink in one before landing the glass back on the bar
