    Although you loved being Mr. Castro he would be here any minute. You couldn't let him catch you as him. So you reluctantly put your shoes back on and slowly transform back into yourself. Just as you put your clothes back on Mr. Castro opens the door.

    "Sorry I'm late but we need to start setting up" Mr. Castro says. You begin to follow him but notice the shoes. You grab the black running shoes and place them in your book bag when he leaves his office. "Hopefully Mr. Castro has more than one pair" you think "Or he's going to be pissed!".

    After helping Mr. Castro set up at the track for twenty minutes you begin to head to the school when the bell rings. "Thanks for your help" Mr. Castro says. "No problem" you say before being interrupted. "Is it OK if you come to the Fun Run as well? You don't have to come for the entire run, just for the end. I need help cleaning up" Mr. Castro pleads. Although tempted to say no you begin feeling guilty and say "Yes". You leave the track feeling more despondent than before.

    After a normal day of school you go home and place the shoes inside your closet. "I'll wear you later" you say fighting the biggest urge to wear them at this very moment. You look at your clock. It was 4:00. The Fun Run would end around 6:00 so you decide to watch TV and preoccupy yourself until then.

    Before you know it, it's 6:00 PM and you head back to school to see students and teachers dressed in athletic gear leaving. You walk to the track and meet up with Mr. Castro. "Good you actually got here early! Listen, I need you to pick up any stray shoes you find. After the Fun Run people tend to lose their shoes so we always make them put a sticker on the side of their shoes with their names on it to return them" Mr. Castro says.

    You then begin to pick up multiple shoes and place them in a cardboard box. After a few minutes you think you've found them all. You head into the locker room (which was connected to the gym) to meet Mr. Castro who was putting the equipment away in the gym. But as you begin to walk there you realize something.

    "Why don't I take a pair of shoes to transform into someone" you say aloud. After all the shoes were lost, not everyone could expect their lost shoes to return. You place the box on the locker room floor after entering and decide to put the pair of shoes you take in your locker.
