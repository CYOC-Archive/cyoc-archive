    “I’d hate to be the next person he has to pull over. Probably take them straight off the road with his bare hands.” Hugo continued, “But I guess that’s the perk of being the only giant police officer in town.”

    “Are you interested in joining the police force just so you can become a giant?”

    “Who wouldn’t want that? But then, I’m guessing I need to be selected for it. It’s not like we see any more giants on the police force.”

    “How about you go apply? Not like it will hurt to try,” suggested Jordan.

    “I think I will,” said Hugo. He stood up and left the dorm in a rush. Jordan figures it will be some time before he hears back from his roommate. At which point, he said aloud: “Hugo will be accepted into the police force and allowed to become a giant like Officer Write.”

    An hour passed by, and Jordan could begin to feel small tremors beneath his feet. The sound of thumping grew louder and louder until it stopped momentarily. The next thing he heard was the loud tapping on his window. Knowing his dorm is on the second level, Jordan initially thought it odd for someone to be able to reach so high. It was then he realized it had to be Hugo. He opened the blind to find a giant eye staring back at him.

    “Hey, Jordan! Check it out!” Hugo stood massive and proud of his new size and stature. He was the same height as Officer Write just as he said, as well as being bulky with newly grown muscles. At his full height, Hugo’s dick and balls were level with where Jordan’s dorm was.

    “Holy shit, dude,” Jordan called out while pretending to be surprised. “Looks like you got it.”

    “I know, right? As soon as I filled out the forms and did the physical, they gave me an injection, and now,” Hugo then gestures to his body. “I start training in the morning. This is going to be awesome.”

    “Well, try to use this new power responsibly,” said Jordan. He mentally acknowledged to himself how the irony was not lost on him.

    “Trust me, dude. I will.” Hugo made to leave, and said, “See you around. And don’t get up to no good, you hear?” His thunderous steps echoed out before fading in the distance.
