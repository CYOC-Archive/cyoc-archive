    The forest was dark and getting darker as Johnny stumbled along through it. But just as he was about to think he was totally lost, the tree's ended and he ran out into a open field. In the distance he could yes the lights of a broke down old farm house. Unsure what to do, Johnny decided to head towards the lights, he was scared and paranoid. Having seen and over heard Zeke and the cop, but figured he could at least steal a truck or car and get back home. Slowly he snuck up towards the old farm house, he was almost to the beat up old pickup when a dog began to bark. Suddenly a door swung open and a old farmer carrying a double barrel shotgun stomped out.

    "You bastards better leave my place and get back to town...or I'll blast ya to kingdom come!" He ranted into the darkness. "I don't bother you so's you aint got no call to come bothering me!" So he knows something strange is going on in that town! Johnny thought. Maybe I've found someone who'll help.

    "Down shoot...please." He called. "I'm not from town me and my family were just passing throught." "They've got my folk's and they're looking for me!" "I need help!"

    "I don't believe a word of it!" The old farmer yelled. Were suddenly a soft female voice called.

    "Now papa..he's in trouble them townies are tryin' to get him." "We gotta help." The old farmer turned and looked at the pretty young girl standing in the doorway and scowled, but uncocked the shotgun.

    "Well....ok but come out here in the light so's I can see ya first."
