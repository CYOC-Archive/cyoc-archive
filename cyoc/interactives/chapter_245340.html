You pull up your shirt and see white fur forming on your belly while orange fur spreads from your sides. Your eyes widen. You touch your new-grown fur moan. It's so warm and soft. No human clothing you have ever worn comes close. You go ahead and rip off the shirt, leaving your furry, fox-tailed body fully nude.

    "Aah! What is this?!" comes a frightened voice from beside you. It's the man with rabbit ears. He's reaching down the back of his pants. The glow has faded and he is trying to identify something behind him. "You--" he says "--please, I can't see back there--tell me--" His voice trails off as he quickly turns his back to you and lowers just the very to of his pants. Something white and fluffy pokes out above them and starts to wiggle around. Something about seeing this triggers some urge or instinct inside you, like you want to give chase. Maybe even bite something? You zone out for a second watching it flick around, then you shake your head to clear your thoughts. You answer his question: "It looks like a rabbit tail." Something in you likes the idea of running as fast as you can to catch up with something that looks like that. "It looks good," you say more to yourself than to him.

You logically think you should be terrified of becoming an animal. But you can't help but feel some suspenseful excitement about maybe getting a little bit more like a fox...
