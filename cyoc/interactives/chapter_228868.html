    While trying to decide of what to do, Jeff’s phone suddenly rings, the teen looks over and sees that it’s his best friend “Juan Alverez”.

    Juan is a 15-year-old Spanish boy, he has a nice olive skin tone and an athletic body, brown eyes and brown hair.

    “Juan, what’s up,” Jeff asks when answering the phone, “I’m kind of… busy”.

    “I just wanted to ask, would you like to go to the beach,” Juan asks, “it’s such a hot day and a nice swim would do good”.

    Jeff thinks for a while, ‘hm… actually that is not a bad idea,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I could transfer the program onto my phone and have some fun with it at the beach’. Imagining transforming other beach goers, Jeff grins, “sure, I’d love to go,” he says.

    “Cool, how about we meet in ten minutes,” Juan asks.

    “Sounds good, I’ll see you in a few,” Jeff says and ends the call.

    Jeff then hooks his phone to his Laptop and transfers the Chronivac onto his phone, he then puts on his bathing shorts and a t-shirt, then grabs a towel and is soon on his way to meet up with his friend.

    When Jeff and Juan greeted each other, the two start to walk towards the beach, which is only a 30 minute walk from where they live. As they walk, Jeff decides to let Juan in on the App, Juan listens closely and with interest as Jeff explains.

    Juan grins once Jeff finished telling him about the App, “hehe, we could have a lot of fun,” he says, “what would you think about transforming into a surfboard?”

    “Hm… only if YOU will let me transform you into a surfboard too,” Jeff says, “the thought of being a helpless object, is not a pleasant thought!”

    Juan grins, “okay, I promise,” he says, “how about you become a surfboard for an hour or two, and once you transformed back, I’ll become your “surfboard” for one or two hours”.

    Jeff smiles, “that sounds fair,” he says, “let’s find somewhere secluded and private so that I can transform without being seen. I don’t want anyone know about the App!”

    Juan nods, “understood,” he says.

    The two make B-line towards a cliff face where there is a small patch of trees, enough to not be spotted.

    “This is perfect,” Jeff says, “the trees and bushes obscure us pretty well and there aren’t many people around”.

    “You better take of your shirt and bathing shorts,” Juan says, “or else you’ll destroy them when you transform”.

    Jeff blushes, “I really would… not want to have you see me naked!”

    Juan snorts then laughs, “really Jeff,” he says, “as if you don’t have something I don’t have! I am not gay, so chill!”

    Jeff hesitates, he then sighs, “okay, fine,” he says and starts to strip.

    Juan grins, “sexy,” he says jokingly once Jeff is naked.

    Jeff blushes hard and scowls at his friend, “sh-shut it!”

    Juan chuckles, “chill, bro,” he says, “I’m just teasing!”

    “I know,” Jeff grumbles, starting to type into the App, setting up the transformation, “it’s just so… embarrassing being naked in front of my best friend!”

    “I suggest, once you have transformed, that we’ll leave your belongings here,” Juan suggests, “no one will notice them, we’ll have to return to this place anyway for you to transform back and for my transformation to go unnoticed!”

    Jeff nods, “okay, but hide my stuff behind some rocks to obscure them more,” he says and Juan nods, “okay… I think everything is set! I chose to be a surfboard for two hours, so keep your eyes on the time! I don’t want to end up transforming back into a naked teen in public!”

    Juan chuckles, “I promise I’ll keep tabs of the time”.

    Jeff nods and after taking a deep breath he confirms the input, he then feels a tingle run through his body, causing him to shiver, “that tingle felt weird,” he says, “almost like pins-and-needles”.

    “Hopefully the transformation is painless,” Juan says and Jeff nods.

    “I… I think the change is starting to,” Jeff then says, “my… my skin feels weird!”
