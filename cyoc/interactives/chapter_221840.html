    Movie Night

    Previous chapter

    added by LuisJM A month ago AR BM S

    After a long drive, the bus stopped at the Movie Megaplex. When the door opened, Abigail came down from it carefully and waved to the bus driver. Turning to the theater, she marveled at the sight of it. This is no ordinary movie theater, this megaplex is the epic cinema center where not only recent genre movies are played, but also classic, indie, foreign, and unique films are being shown. Abigail has always been a big movie buff since she was young. In fact, when she was a kid, the first movie she watched was Gone with the Wind. And while long and tiresome, she appreciates how much of a masterpiece it was, even if there's the whole controversy within the Cancel Culture community. And since she doesn't have much of a life left, she really wanted to see one more flick before her time is up.

    Looking up to the theater, she was in awe at the sight and said, "Wow... This is some movie theater. In my time, theaters are a few rooms. This place has multiple! I don't know where to start."

    She soon looked to the amulet the stranger gave her and was a little unsure of what he said. "The man in the bus stop says that I could wish to be young again, to live a longer and more carefree life. I find it all hard to believe. Hmm... Oh well... At least it looks nice on me." She looked up to the megaplex and said, "Now, let's enjoy some films while I can. Could be something special."

    Walking with her cane in hand, she entered the theater and marveled as how big it was. With three floors of theaters, neon lightning, and a concession stand in the middle, she couldn't believe how huge it was. "Golly! This place is massive! You could get lost in a place like this."

    Looking at the now showing board, she could see a variety of movies that are being shown today, all of different genres, different themes, and different varieties. Seeing the list, she was a little flustered and said, "So many movies. I don't know what to watch today."

    Looking at the choices, she found what movie she should watch.
