    His body began to bulge and grow.He could feel his bones creaking

    as they grew and changes.His scaly hide began to stretch and tear as

    it could not contain the strong bands of muscles rippling over his

    mighty dragonic frame.His wings stretched out to a wingspan twice his

    growing length.A barbed tail as long as the rest of his body coiled

    about.He could feel the venom sac forming within his lengthening

    snout.He coiled over his long neck to look over his still growing and

    altering form.God,he was going to make a beautiful dragon.

    Then things started going horribly wrong.Suddenly he felt a weight on

    his back.It was a suit of armour with a saddle attached.On ear side

    of the armour sprouted the gun barrels of 20mm cannon,50 cal machine

    guns and lasers.Under his wings grew a metallic exoskeleton with

    racks of missiles.Another great weight settled down on his itching

    back spines.It was a steam powered jet engine.His exoskeleton

    continued to extend into a pair of airfoils.Then he felt a throbbing

    in his growing talons.On each of his 3 claws was attached a sharp

    metallic blade.Then Jon felt the tug between his legs and felt his

    insides writhe as his sex was remade.Just ahead of his hind legs he

    could feel two swollen organs start to form.He instinctively

    crouched down to protect his udders.He could feel the tingle as his

    teats rubbed along the dirt.He screamed out "No,this can't be

    happening"Then he felt his dragon helmet starting to form around his

    too human skull.This was the helmet his dragon brain would need to

    access his exoskeletons propulsion,weapons,communications and sensory

    systems.However,he wasn't dragonic enough.His screams of terror and

    pain turned to bellows and roars as his skull,brain were reshaped.He

    closed his blue eyes in pain,and could feel his human blood boiling

    away as it was replaced with black venomous dragons blood.As he

    opened his still lengthening snout,a jet of steaming venom shot out

    of his fangs and burned into the ground far below.There was one

    last writhing and twisting and turning of his neck as his neurons

    and nerves were refashioned and his human thoughts wiped out.

    Then her eyes reopenned.Her glowing yellow slitted eyes surveyed her

    beuatiful body.She pecked at her dark green scales with her forked

    tongue as she surveyed the golden weapons suit which clothed her.She

    gave a thought,and her 50 calibre guns started tracking the little

    mammals near the ruins.She was Princess Ymir,daughter of the Dragon

    Emperor,and she had been sent to this miserable backwater of a planet

    to teach the mammals the teachings of her people.As she looked down

    she though of her betrothed Yorrk.They had been apart for 5000 years.

    As a huge tear slid out of one eye,she shook her head."No that isss
    the passst" she thought.Now where was her rider.She stood up on her

    hind legs and bellowed out her summons.One of the goverment police

    ladies was stumbling around in a daze,but when she heard the dragon

    call she straightened up and headed towards Ymir.As she got within

    100 feet of the warrior princess dragon her hair began to lengthen,

    her uniform began to tear and slip off and her body grew tanned,tall

    and muscular like a amazon warrior queen.She tossed aside her police

    issue revolver.She knew Ymir's body armour contained more advanced

    weaponry she would have access to.Ymir settled down on her chest and

    belly and the new dragonrider climbed up the steps built into the

    armour and settled into her saddle.

    Then the mighty Ymir reared up 50 feet in the air.roared her speciews

    battle cry and launched herself into the air.Her past forgotten,she

    only lived to bring her fathers plan for this miserable backwater

    planet to fruition............
