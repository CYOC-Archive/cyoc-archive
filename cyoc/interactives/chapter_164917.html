    Jenny is still fiddling with the controls when "Jennifer" takes the tablet from her hands. It shushes her protests.

    "Don't give me any attitude Jenny. You'll get your toy back later. Right now it's bathtime."

    The tablet is placed on top of a high shelf. Spotting the welfare agent's card on the table, it asks Jenny to explain as it slips the card into a pocket. It then takes Jenny's hand and leads her up to the bathroom. It tisks as it examines the state of her room when they pass through.

    "I leave you alone for one day and this is what you do? I'm very disappointed. You won't be getting your tablet back until this room is clean."

    It fills the tub with water and bubbles and has Jenny climb in. Jenny watches it retrieve the agent's card and make a call. Thankfully, it seems to have a pleasant chat, apologizing for her mistake and assuring the agent that Jenny will never be left unsupervised like that again. After hanging up the phone Jennifer smiles at her.

    "Good news dear. The nice agent says with her work done you'll be clear to get all your citizenship papers."

    Jenny decides that news is good enough to deal with the minor inconveniences of not having proper control of the android yet. Jennifer then gets to the business of giving her a bath. Being bathed by someone else is an odd experience for Jenny, but surprisingly pleasant.

    Once she's clean, Jennifer pulls the plug on the bath and dries her off. Jenny heads out to get dressed only to be told to wait for Jennifer to pick an outfit for her. The android dresses her in a pair of overalls with a large Care Bears patch on the pocket and a hot pink shirt. Her hair is then put into a tight braid to keep it out of her eyes.

    "There you go! All ready for the day. Now I'm going to go make breakfast. This room better be clean by the time I'm done."
