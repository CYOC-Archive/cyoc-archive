    “As in…to become a seal I’ll need to put on…one of these skins?” Rachel guessed. “Indeed so. Once fully suited up and soaked you’ll be transformed and after drying off correctly the skin will become a suit again which can be removed” Gloria explained “Also the skin is only counted as yours while transformed so no need to worry about any idiots stealing it and bounding you to marriage” then puts on a stern face “That said please always remember rules 3 and 5”. “That if I fall pregnant I cannot be reverted until after giving birth and that children cannot ever become human” Rachel recalled. “Indeed but for reference the drying out reverting process here won’t happen in that case while if your already pregnant you won’t transform at all” Gloria assured “Special safety and legal detail to help avoid mishaps or attempts and hiding certain dirty secrets in select cultures”. “Fair enough” Rachel accepted.

    After collecting a Spotted Seal skin-suit Rachel was instructed to strip naked, figures really considering what she knew about ho Selkies worked. While large though the suit was actually rather tight as she moved to stuff her legs into the lower tail part and after that her arms into the points leading to the front flippers which led to a slight struggle for Rachel to pull the Seal head over her own but thankfully she managed and was currently able to see through the eyes of the suit if somewhat obscured.

    “All suited up then?” Gloria asked as Rachel lay on her stomach within the suit in the middle of the room. “Best as I Can be I guess” Rachel admitted. “OK now this might be a bit shocking” Gloria announced as she tabbed away a bit on a nearby console to her from nearby the door that suddenly coursed a suddenly downpour of water to hit Rachel…

    …and as a result the shift Rachel had been told about happened as the obscuring faded while Rachel from within the skin-suit merged with it as the seams sealed and faded and everything human besides her mind and memories within the seal was replaced by seal bones, muscles and blubber. “WHAT THE FUNK WAS THAT FOR?” Rachel demanded. “Invoke the transformation before sending you out on the truck that brings guests to and from the Seal section of the area” Gloria explained just as a knocking came from this other door opposite.
