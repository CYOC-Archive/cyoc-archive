    Anthony Tillson, his wife Emily, and his 7 year old daughter Jennifer, all headed to the front desk of the resort.

    “Welcome Transformation Resort, may I help you?”, asked the receptionist.

    “Yes, the name is Anthony Tillson, I have a reservation.”, replied Anthony.

    The receptionist typed in the name on the computer and the data came back. “Ah, the Tillson family. Your reservation is for whole summer, correct?”

    “Yes, this is the first time we had a vacation since our honeymoon.”, answered Emily.

    The receptionist smiled, “I see you’ll be staying in the Age Wing, nice choice”. The receptionist pushed a button and a young woman walked out from the door behind the desk. “Lisa here will help you with room.”

    Lisa walked from behind the desk. She was wearing a white blouse with a red vest with a gold name tag was pinned to it. Her skirt went to her knees and her blonde hair was pulled into a pony tail.

    “Lisa, here are their keys”, said the receptionist as she tossed the keys to Lisa.

    Lisa caught the keys, “Okay follow me and I’ll get you to your room.”

    Lisa guided them to an electric trolley. After loaded their luggage, the family crawled on board. She then drove the Tillson’s toward the Age Wing.

    “Welcome to the Transformation Resort. We have many attractions for your entertainment. There is the Water Park, a Zoo, many Pools, the beach, shows, stores, lots of restaurants, and plenty of playgrounds. At the Zoo, some of the animals are guests who are staying at the Animal Wing.”, explained Lisa as she drove the trolley.

    “Mommy look a waterfall.”, awed Jennifer as she pointed to the crystal clear water fall on their right.

    “Its beautiful honey”, smiled Emily.

    When they got to the Age Wing, the Tillson’s were awe struck on how beautiful the buildings and layout were. The building where the rooms were formed a circle around a large garden. In the center of the garden was a golden fountain that at the center was a large gold hourglass where the water cascaded down the sides. As Lisa guided them through the garden to the opposite end, the Tillson’s saw many children running around, a few young teenagers making out of a bench, and one young teenaged girl sporting a small pregnant belly.

    As they walked passed, they overheard the young girl talk to her belly as she rubbed it. “Having fun in there Suzy”, she cooed.

    They followed Lisa to their room on the second floor. “Here we are suite 206.”, she announced and opened the door.

    Inside the suite were two beds. It had a leather sofa with coffee table and a large screen TV. There was also a small kitchen and breakfast table, if they decided to cook anything. The deck opened to the center garden and had to chairs on it. Emily was in joy when she saw the large bathroom with a four person jet tub.

    “I know what I want to use tonight.”, smiled Emily looking at the tub.

    Lisa helped bring in their luggage. “Before I leave, I need to explain a few things.”

    The Tillson’s dropped what they were doing and paid attention as Lisa started explaining.

    “In the Age Wing, we can age or regress the guests into whatever age they want. Our rooms are designed to does this very thing. It can help you change your age just by thinking about it. All you have to do is to think about the age you want to be and the room will do the rest. Your clothing and luggage will change with your age including the beds and any necessities you’ll need. Allow me to demonstrate.”, stated Lisa.

    They watched as Lisa closed her eyes and suddenly started to change.

    “She’s getting younger”, said Jennifer pointing to the shrinking Lisa.

    In fact Jennifer was correct, Lisa was regressing quickly, already into her early teens and still going. Her breasts were deflating behind her vest, which was the only thing not changing. Her blouse had changed into a Tee-shirt and her skirt shorted to a mini skirt. Once she left puberty, her Tee-shirt and skirt merged into a pink sun dress. A red ribbon now appeared on her pony tail. Young Lisa opened her eyes.

    “See it’s easy peasy”, smiled Lisa in a higher pitched voice. On her badge it showed her name and her current age of 6 years old.

    “You’re a kid like me”, giggled Jennifer.

    “Yep, I’m six years old again”, giggled Lisa as she spun around.

    “Amazing”, awed Emily.

    “I agree”, was all Anthony could say. He knew the resort could do it, but knowing and seeing were two different things.

    Lisa stopped spinning and looked as serious as a 6 year old could. “Okay first off, let me tell you the rules.

    Your age does have a limit from conception, a fertilized egg in mommy’s tummy, to really really old at one hundred. If you go past one hundred, you will instantly die from old age. If the oldest in the room goes below ten-years-old a babysitter or a substitute mommy will be assigned to the room to take care of you. If you regress past newborn, you would be put inside mommy’s tummy we would assign you with or the closest mommy at the right age to have a baby. But if you regressed past conception, you will not die, but become just an egg in mommy’s ovaries and will need to wait until mommy and daddy make you again.

    And lastly, when you leave the resort, the age you are currently at will be your new age. Reality will change to match your new age if you happen to change it.”

    Anthony noticed that Lisa was speaking more like the girl she appeared than a grown woman.

    Lisa smile showing a missing baby tooth. “Need anything else?”, she asked.

    “I think were fine, thank you”, replied Anthony handing a twenty dollar bill to the little girl.

    Lisa giggled, “Yippy, thanks mister. I’m going to go play now before I go back to work.”

    Lisa skipped out the suite door and closed it behind her.

    “So what you want to do first?”, asked Anthony.
