Please don't make my Daddy a clown. We always call him Pappa Bear. Would you please turn him into a bear instead? I'll even give you my teddy bear! PLEASE?" she begged.

When this big light turns green, i need you to push that button with the bear on it, ok? And you can keep your teddy bear too, ok sweetie?"

    "Ok Missy," the officer said. "In a few minutes you will have turned Daddy into a literal Papa Bear!" Go into the other room and you can wait for him to come out.

He tried not to imagine the final result, being trapped as a clown for months until his trial. The last thing Tom remembered was a collar going around his neck and a high pitched whine as it turned on.
