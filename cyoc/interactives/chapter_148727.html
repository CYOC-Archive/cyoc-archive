    "We are going to be learning about how to be sweet little boys and girls. What do you think of it?" Miss Circen announced which got a few chuckles from people thinking she was joking.

    "Glad to see some enthusiasm. Now first off, I'd like to do a little questionnaire that we can do as a whole class. Let me just open up the questionnaire." Miss Cicren then began typing on her computer to get the questionnaire opened.

    At first Kyle thought this was some joke, but as time progressed it seemed Miss Circen was quite serious. Kyle took note that he wasn't the only one to be confused at the lesson, most of the girls and one or two boys that weren't mesmerized by the teacher.

    As Miss Circen finished tinkering away at the computer, she finally managed to get the questionnaire loaded. Up on the screen, kyle took note of how baby like it all was. The colours were rather simple and hand drawn pictures, presumably drawn by toddlers, littered the screen.

    "Now time to start." Miss Circen announced as she loaded the first question. "Question 1: what is it that toddlers are unable to control? Could we start a discussion here."

    It was an odd question for Kyle to hear but he thought about it and didn't have to think hard. It was a simple answer and it was that toddlers couldn't control their bladder.

    Raising his hand, Kyle decided he should answer and Miss Circen seemed to take notice.

    "Yes little one?" Miss Circen smiled and cooed, treating Kyle like a baby.

    Kyle didn't seemed to take notice, somewhat feeling accomplished for being noticed by Miss Circen. "I do believe that the answer to that question is the toddlers can't control their bladder." Kyle proudly announced, like a proud little kid.

    "Very good Kyle. Yes, toddlers don't know how to hold there bladders. Can any of you hold your bladders?" Miss Circen oddly asked which made Kyle think she was joking.

    Of course they could hold their own bladder was what Kyle was thinking. They weren't toddlers, they were high schoolers so they should be able to hold it in.

    Amidst all his inner monologues, Kyle began to feel an increasing discomfort around his crotch area. Reaching a hand down to investigate, he had realised that he just went, despite thinking he could hold it in.

    Blushing furiously, he wanted to raise a hand up so he could clean himself in the restrooms. Although he wanted to, he felt compelled not to and just sat in his seat, listening on to the lecture.
