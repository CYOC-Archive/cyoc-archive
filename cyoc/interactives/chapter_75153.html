    "My name's Will, it's nice to meet you." Will said.

    Loveysnark giggled. "It's nice to meet you too. We should get going. The dinner rush has probably calmed down
    by now."

    The couple walked a couple of blocks down to the right zone, and found their destination pretty quickly. Of
    course, this being Flotham, they weren't going to a normal Italian place. No, they were going to the mafia's
    favorite Italian place.

    "Isn't this place a little... dangerous?" Will asked as they waited for the Maitre 'D to find them an open
    table.

    "I guess, but the bread sticks are totally worth it." Loveysnark replied.

    As the pair had dinner, Will found himself distracted. He tried to follow the conversation and eat his spaghetti, but there was always a thought at the back of his mind. This woman, this beautiful, charming woman,
    is going to die. Some asshole was going to end up shooting her in the head for a quest. At a certain point, he
    knew he needed to tell her. So, after they had both finished dinner, Will prepared to do what he knew he had
    to.

    "Loveysnark, there's something I need to tell you." He said.

    "Oh God, this never ends well." Loveysnark said, already bracing herself for whatever Will was about to say.

    "Alright, it's kind of complicated, and I'm not sure how to really describe it, but I'll do my best." Will
    said. Then he told her everything. Well, everything except the part where she aided and abetted, but she
    didn't really need to know that part yet. It took a long time, but Will finally got to the point where he
    thought he had accurately described what was going on. Loveysnark was speechless.

    "That's... wow." She said.

    "I know it's a lot to take in, but you have to believe me." Will said.

    "So why are you telling me all this?" Loveysnark asked.

    "Because I don't want you to die. If I win, which I have to now, I'll use my wish to take you back with me and
    make it so no low level creep can gut you just because he needs some XP." Will replied.

    "But what about everyone else?" She asked, looking around at the nameless NPCs wandering about.

    "Don't worry. Unless you know a superhero, villain, or member of groups that help those directly, everyone you
    care about should be safe. You can't kill people who aren't against you." Will said.

    "And since I'm a cop, I can be killed?" Loveysnark asked.

    "There are quests that encourage it. That's why I have to win and get you out of here." Will said.

    "OK. How about this? If you can take me to your world, I'll believe you. If not, I'll be forced to think
    you're a psycho. Either way, thanks for dinner, it was great. And if you really aren't crazy," Loveysnark said
    as she wrapped Will in a tight hug, "thank you. Thank you so much."

    With that, the girl of Will's dreams left for the night. Will had to win now. He couldn't let it end like
    this. He couldn't let her just die.
