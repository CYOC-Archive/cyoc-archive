    Jen was never one to have a great imagination so she decided to herself that she would stick with the theme and also turn someone into a pig. "I don't know" she told Circe passively, "I guess I'll pick a pig too."

    Circe smiled at Jen and said "that's quite alright my dear, it's my favorite too. So appropriate, don't you think, for most men?"

    Circe remembered how she had been pestered for several days by a door to door salesman selling memberships to a new gym. He left a card so she decided to give him a call. She asked him if he could come over with the membership papers and he said he would be by in 10 minutes.

    "I have just the victim for you Jen, why don't you slip into some nice workout gear?" Circe said.

    Jen stepped into a room and changed into a form fitting unitard that showed off her amazing figure. As she stepped out Circe smiled and the bell rang. "Why don't you take this from here dear?" she said.

    Circe and Gina walked into the back while Jen went to the door and opened it. Standing before her was a very handsome young man, probably in his mid twenties, with an amazing physique that startled and excited Jen for a moment.

    "Hello" Jen cooed. The man was equally struck by Jen's beauty as he stammered for words "H...h...hi, I'm Peter. I'm here about the gym membership" he said as he held up some papers.

    "Oh yes" Jen said "please, please come in" . Peter walked into the foyer and Jen closed the door. "Why don't we go into the dining room" Jen said as she pointed to the large dining room off the foyer. They both walked in with Peter letting Jen lead the way.

    Peter sat down and took out his papers. Jen said "let me get a pen and my credit card" as she walked away towards the kitchen. While in the kitchen, Circe handed her a large container that looked like a protein drink but actually contained Circe's famous "pigging powder" . Circe whispered "two scoops should speed up the transformation better than one".

    Jen walked out and said "Peter, would you mind giving me your opinion on this new protein powder. I tried it and it tastes a little different than what I'm used to. Maybe it's just me. Would you mind trying some and letting me know?"

    "Sure" Peter said, unaware of the true contents.

    "Thanks" Jen said as she took the powder back into the kitchen and mixed two scoop with a bottle of spring water in a blender bottle. Jen walked out shaking the bottle and smiling at Peter. "Here you go" she said as she handed the bottle to him, opening the cap first. "Where do I sign" she said.

    "Right here" Peter said pointing to a line at the bottom. He took the drink and drank it all down, with Jen quietly smiling as she signed the papers.

    "So" she said "what do you think"

    Peter looked a bit puzzled and said "hmm..definitely tastes different, almost has kind of a bacon flavor to it."

    Circe and Gina giggled from the kitchen while Jen smiled to herself. "I thought the same thing" she said.

    Peter then started looking unsteady "whoa" he said "I'm feeling a little weird. I'm getting awfully hot"

    "Oh dear," Jen said "hey don't you take off that shirt" Peter started to take off his tee shirt revealing a great upper body that almost made Jen gulp.

    Jen started to notice small, coarse hairs, like quills growing from Peter's back. "Better?" she asked.

    Soon the hairs grew more rapidly and the hair on his head fell off. Peter was in a stupor now, unable to speak, uttering grunting sounds instead.

    "Don't fight it Peter, you'll be better off if you just let it happen" Jen said to a puzzled Peter.

    The transformation soon quickened in pace, as Jen took off Peter's pants while he lay sideways on the floor. Soon his head was more pig than man and his hands and feet were hardening into trotters. The transformation was seamless without any loud noises.

    After a couple minutes, the transformation was done. Standing in the dining room on all fours was a large male pig probably about 220 pounds. Peter was sniffing around instinctively for something to eat.

    "There there now Peter, there's plenty to eat...in the STY!" Jen laughed, pleased with her success.

    Peter still had his human reason and figured that the powder and the yougn witch were his undoing. He obediently walked out to the back and into the sty as Jen led him there. "That's a good piggy" she said as she tossed him some acorns to eat.
