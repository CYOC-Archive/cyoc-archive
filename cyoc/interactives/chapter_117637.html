    Colin looked at himself; he wasn't exactly a looker, having struggled to get rid of his acne since he was ten and he loved him some junk food if his rotund stomach was any indication. Then he decided to wish for something that always got girls' attention: money.
    "I wish I was the richest guy in town." Colin wrote in the book.

    As the ink turned gold, the wish was made true. The first thing upgraded with the power of money was Colin's phone, morphing from a flip phone to an Iphone 6. Next, his acne vanished and his weight dropped by the power of affording dermatology treaments and a personal karate dojo. His wardrobe was updated next, with a stylish polo, designer shorts, and limited edition jordans.

    He looked on his Iphone, and saw that he had no college, but a meeting with someone under the description "Game Commission." In his info on Facebook, he was listed as age twenty-five and single with a pic of his family in Hawaii.

    'I can commission games now? THIS IS THE BEST DAY OF MY LIFE!!!!' the gamer said as he ran off.
    *******************

    His place was completely different as well: no longer a modest apartment, it was a full blown manor with a gate possessing his initials.
    He pushed the button on the gate.
    "I'm home!" Colin said.
    "Very good sir, your guest will be here shortly..." a british voice said.
    Colin walked into the spacious manor as an old butler welcomed him in.

    "Sir, your suit is hung up in your room." the butler said.
    "Very good, Melvin." Colin said. His meeting was over initiating the development of the game's sequel.
