    "Let's go to the Kiddie Korner," Leah suggests. It's the portion of the park reserved for toddlers and babies. It even has a fully staffed daycare if parents want a break. Rita nods.

    "Dressed like you are, I think it's the best choice," she says. She takes Leah's hand and the two walk to the Kiddie Korner. Leah is nervous about sticking out, but when they arrive, she sees she didn't need to worry. Some of the toddlers wandering around with visible diaper bulges are bigger than her. Not a single person even glances their way.

    Rita and Leah look around at the ride options. The most fun-looking one is the sole rollercoaster in the section. It's short and slow, but Leah prefers it over the teacups. Unfortunately, it looks like everyone else does, too, because it has the longest line of the rides in the section. Rita paid extra for the fast line option, but even that will be a wait.

    "We'd better get in line before it gets even longer," Rita says cheerfully. They get in line. Leah is quickly getting sick of holding Rita's hand, but knows she has to in order to keep up appearances. As the line slowly inches forward, Leah becomes aware of a growing pressure. She tugs on Rita's pant leg.

    "Auntie Rita!" she hisses. Rita looks down at her. "I have to, uh, potty." Leah quickly uses the juvenile word to keep suspicion off her. Not that anyone's paying attention to them. Rita looks at the line behind them. It's gotten even longer than when they first joined the line. She crouches down to talk quietly to Leah.

    "Can you hold it?" she asks. Leah shakes her head. "If we get out of line now, we'll have to get in the back. And we'll spend the whole day waiting for this one ride." Leah nods sadly. "Look, just use your diaper."

    "What?!"

    "That's what it's for! No one will care. There are plenty of kids using their diapers within sight of us right now! I'll change you after the ride if you need a change."

    Leah sighs. She doesn't want to use her diaper, but Rita has a point.

    "Okay," she says softly. But even after agreeing, she can't bring herself to use the diaper on purpose. The pressure builds and builds, until, when they're next up to go on the ride, Leah can't hold it anymore. In front of the ride operator, Rita, and everyone else in line, Leah bends her knees into a squat and...
