    Gaming Present

    Branch Full view
    added by LuisJM 6 years ago I S

    Thinking of what gift he would become, Jeff decided to become a gaming console. He knows that his friend likes to play games, even old school gaming. So what better way then to become an actual gaming console for Lachlan. The problem is, what type of console he should be? He could be one of the latest consoles of today. Then again, there are the old school type of consoles that has some playability. What could he be?

    There is the juggernaut gaming giant Nintendo that has all of the great consoles from the original NES to the recent Nintendo Switch.

    The Playstation is a great gaming company with four consoles and huge collection of games.

    There's Xbox and its three consoles for the hardcore of gaming.

    Then there's the classic consoles that have history and legacy which includes the SEGA consoles from the Genesis to Dreamcast. Atari's retro like consoles with their small pixel like graphics. And the two Odyssey games that started the whole gaming franchise.

    There is also a chance that he can turn himself into multiple consoles, be that Nintendo, Playstation, Xbox, SEGA, Atari, or Odyssey. He could even turn himself into a complete console library for his friend to play with.

    Thinking of all of this, he thought long and hard on what console, or consoles, he can be. After a long debate, he came up with a decision.

    What do you do now?
