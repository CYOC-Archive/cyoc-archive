She started by running to the bathroom, making sure the coast was clear as she navigated the halls. She took off her entire outfit and set it in the sink, turning the water on to see if could quickly hand wash it. Using the other sink, she tried to rinse off her cum-stained fur, even though she wasn’t fond of the cold water. Using a piece of cardboard, she tried to scrub out the stains form the damp clothes. After a good 30 minutes of her rushed cleaning attempts, Kylie finally felt satisfied with her efforts. Grabbing her clothes from the sink, she made extra sure she was alone before heading up to the stage lights to use them as an improvised heat lamp. She sniffed herself, and didn’t like what she smelled. She stunk like sweat and sex, and she saw no way of getting rid of that smell, so she elected to just keep herself at a distance from other people. Feeling that her outfit was dry enough, she reluctantly out it back on and headed out to her favorite diner.

    Taking a few shortcuts to avoid heavy foot traffic (and a detour through a flower shop to mask her smell), she finally made it to the diner. Giving herself a minute to cool herself down, she gave herself a once over before walking up to a dark diner. None of the lights were on and it looked like no one was there. She had no idea how to respond, so she called Bun, thinking if anyone had an answer she would. After three failed attempts, she decided to take a deep breath and step inside the diner. She was not ready for what awaited her inside...
