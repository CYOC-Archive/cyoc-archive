    “Tina-” Leah starts. Tina pops a pacifier into Leah’s open mouth before she can say anything else. Leah takes the pacifier out. “Tina!”

    “We forgot pacifiers at the other store. Just try a couple brands for me, please.”

    “But I need to-”

    “Less talking, more sucking,” Tina scolds. Just then, Leah notices a few people looking their way. Hurriedly, she puts the pacifier back in, sucking on it loudly, trying to dismiss any suspicions from the strangers watching.

    They continue to shop, Tina pushing the cart with Leah in it. The need to go is getting stronger by the second, but each time Leah opens her mouth to tell Tina, Tina pops a new pacifier in. When she’s finished having Leah try all the brands, she asks Leah which one is best. Leah makes the mistake of saying that they all sort of blended together, which makes Tina have Leah try each one again.

    Halfway through the second round of pacifier testing, Leah knows that she either uses the bathroom now or winds up in another dirty diaper. She takes out the pacifier.

    “Tina, I need to-” Without even looking at her, Tina puts the pacifier back in. Before Leah can try again, the cart hits a display stand. Once again, the sudden jolt breaks Leah’s control. Her bowels evacuate, filling her diaper with mushy poop, closely followed by her bladder flooding the diaper with urine. Tina’s nose wrinkles. She looks at Leah.

    “Leah, did you make boom-boom again?” she teases. Leah removes the pacifier.

    “That’s what I was trying to tell you! I needed the bathroom, but you wouldn’t let me ask!”

    “Sorry, sweetie. Let’s get you changed again.” Tina pushes the cart over to the bathrooms, then brings Leah inside. When she lays Leah on the changing station, she makes a discovery. “Wow, you leaked! Good thing you’re not wearing any bottoms over the diaper!” Leah turns beet red.

    Tina has just opened Leah’s diaper, leaving her messy and wet privates on display, when someone else enters the bathroom.
