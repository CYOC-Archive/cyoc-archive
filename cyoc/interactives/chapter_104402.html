    "For as long as anyone within the valley could remember, the goddess known as Bountellia cared for the land by providing nourishment and assuring that every harvest would be plentiful, the people of the valley looked up to her. To show their thanks for everything she had done for them once every four years the most beautiful young woman would be chosen and after a vigorous ceremony would be brought high atop the hill where Bountellia's temple resided, there the woman would be left and "offered" to Bountellia to do with her as ever she wished. Of course this was thousands of years ago and such stories are all but fables, or are they? That is the question you are going to answer, your archaeology team has just dropped you off at the entrance to the fabled temple of Bountellia in hopes that you'll be able to uncover it's mysteries, some say there is a grand treasure waiting within, other claim all that's inside if the skeletal remains of the women brought there to be sacrificed. Be quick however, you only have forty eight hours until you team returns to pick you up in time for a major storm to his the valley. Good luck out there."

    "What are ya waiting for kid? Get going." Andrew slowly walked between the two large doors which served as the entry way, once inside however they immediately slammed shut.
