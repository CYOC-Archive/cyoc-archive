    Reality now altered with the presence of Darren and his friends. In Darren's time, he had cast a spell bringing both Sachiko and Raiden into existence. Now, with their arrival in the past, the spell could be completed. Across two timelines now things began to change. For Akira and Ayami, those changes were more noticeable. Ayami, the one time corruption demon began to change. The succubus changing into an angel of maternity, her batlike wings becoming angelic as her skin went from crimson to a more blueish hue.

Now Akiras position as head of security had two purposes, the protection of the next generation of magical children and to take care of his infant daughter. In both timelines evryone was aware of the miraculous birth of Akiras daughter. Akiras student Natalia MacGregor would even act as an occasional babysitter for the infant kitsune. The changes though were not limited to Akira and Ayami as both Rexie, Mia, Fukushu, and Abolith had been affected as well.

    Rexie and Mia's changes were subtle. The imp familiar becoming an angelic one. The mischievous cherub now taking the appearance of a white tabby cat with blue eyes instead of a black cat with red eyes. Thus in turn made Mia a white wotch instead of simply a witch and although Ayami and Rexie had changed; their personalities remained the same.

    Fukushu and Abolith, while now parents, kept the birth of their son Raiden a secret. While overjoyed to at having a son, the pair still plotted the downfall of the wizards. After their latest scheme involving a rogue demon hunter, the pair had withdrawn as they formulated a new plan to assassinate the wizards.

    "Darren," Gail called, quickly rushing to the teens side. "Neela is he okay?" Neela the teenage prodigy and doctor now studied Darren carefully. "You're exhausted," she said. "You definitely need to take it easy."

    "I'll rest when we stop the Normal," Darren groaned, struggling to stand as he once more fell over. "You can barely stand right now," Ewart exclaimed. "You need to rest!" Darren sighed, grumbling as he now sat on the ground. "Fine," he sighed. "And while I'd like to find Abolith and Fukushu they weren't very fond of people or wizards at this time."

    "Well what about finding the wizards then," Steph asked. "I mean, if they're here shouldn't they know about what's going to happen?"

    "Shes got a point," Neela shrugged. "But who's going to look for them. You're in no condition to go Darren."

    "We could go."

    Darren and the others now paused, looking over at both Sachiko and Raiden. The two kits had been quiet since their arrival, after everything they had endured, it surprised them that they remained so upbeat. "Are you sure," Darren asked.

    "We are," Raiden said, the anthro foxes youthful face now sporting a serious expression. "We want to help save the future."

    "We can do this," Sachiko added, the white kits tail swishing behind her. "We'll find the wizards and then come back here."

    "Okay," Darren sighed. "Just be careful. I don't want you two getting hurt." Sachiko and Raiden nodded, the white kit grabbing her friends hand as the two anthros now sped off. As they did, Darren leaned back, sighing heavily. Hopefully they weren't too late to save their future.
