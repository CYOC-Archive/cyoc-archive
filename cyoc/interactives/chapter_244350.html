She grasped her face in shock. Jennifer quizzically touched her new part, they were warm, it felt like any other part of her body, a light yank and she could feel how they were attached to her scalp.

Sure she liked them, their big paws, how they held their tail in the mouth, they were pretty cute. But most favorite? Jennifer shrugged, it must have been so, it was true she wasn't terribly surprised at the revealing fact. Especially not after the revelation of her being cursed. Oh right.

She lied a lot, not really because she was a liar persay, but what exactly qualified as a lie? Telling someone what they want to hear would technically be a lie, or stating something she believed to be true but actually wasn't could be one too. There was lying by admission or just lying by accident. Jennifers face grew more worried, she was completely screwed.

She pounded her fist into her palm as she finished getting ready for school. Her eyes unknowingly changing to a ice teal, the curse knowing Jennifer did infact, not have this.
