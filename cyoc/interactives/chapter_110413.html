"Julie Stovall's exact sentence has yet to be determined, and she would still have to face charges in regards to murdering poor Debbie in the ladies' room.     "Not that I'm agreeing with Julie's actions, but I can see why she ran," said the were-badger, while gathering her things.
    "The wiser course of action would have been not to have gotten in trouble with the law at all," observed Tachibana. "No were, let alone the Elders' Council, should take such matters lightly. We all have our roles in life. Unfortunately your client chose the wrong one. Hopefully in time she will learn the error of her ways."

    "Just because Julie Stovall is facing possible life without parole if convicted of the murder charge that will eventually be leveled against her doesn't mean she will be totally cut off from the world.
