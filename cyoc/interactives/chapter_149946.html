But I also have a grand single suite available, with private accommodations not far away for the kids. It would give you all some privacy, and I have to add that the grand suite is very luxurious. The kids can choose their own themes as well, which usually makes the vacation more fun."

    John, Derrick's father, looked at his wife and grinned. "Well, we are here for a getaway. And maybe its better if the kids have some independence. We don't want to limit their fun." She did not looked convinced, but Derrick and his sister both were excited by the prospect. The receptionist gave his mom a wink. "The grand suite comes with room service, a private masseuse, nd its own indoor pool."

    That was enough to push his mom over the edge, and she sighed and nodded. "OK. But you both need to be careful. Just because you can do something crazy..."

    His dad patted her arm and cut her off. "Whatever they do is only for two weeks. It doesn't matter if Derrick ended up as one of the minotaurs - he'll be perfectly normal again by the time we have to leave. Isn't that right?" He asked the receptionist.

    She chuckled and nodded. "Oh, we guarantee that everyone is perfectly normal when they leave." But Derrick heard her softly mutter "Whatever they happen to be." No one else picked it up, and he realized his hearing was somehow enhanced. The receptionist seemed to catch his expression and flush slightly, but Derrick grinned and said "I like that last part." The rest of his family thought he meant the guarantee of being made normal, but the receptionist's surprised grin and wink told him she understood exactly what he meant.

Julie, his sister, was assigned a green-skinned girl who must be a dryad, and they went off giggling together. The receptionist looked at Derrick thoughtfully. "You've obviously been to see Hermes." Derrick heard a clatter and turned to see a young centaur approach. Looking to be in his late teens, the centaur was glossy black all over, animal hide covering his entire body except for the broad nostrils. Up close, he was a lot horsier in the face, yet Derrick found the look very attractive. The centaur was ripped, with muscles bulging in his arms and chest, and grinned with a startling bright smile.

    Champ chuckled. "My roommates usually end up as stallions or mares in the animal stables, but there are some centaurs and equids who used to stay with me, too. We still spend a lot of free time 'together'."

He also caught the fact that it sounded like all of Champ's former roommates were still changed. Hermes must have done something inside Derrick's head as well, and even though he was aware that this could be permanent and he could end up as a monster or even a normal beast of burden, Derrick wasn't really concerned about any of the options. "Um, if it's my choice, I'd like to..."
