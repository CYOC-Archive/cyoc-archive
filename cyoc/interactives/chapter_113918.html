    Now what we humans didn't know was that there aren't many
    species of dragon,but only one.

    Just like a newly fertilized human egg,a human fetus,a newborn baby,a toddler,a child,a teenager,a young adult,a middle aged adult
    a elder adult,a aged geriatric adult and finally a old ddying/dead adult
    don't represent different species,but are just different stages
    in the life cycle of a human,so too the various shapes and sizes of dragon
    were just a different stage in the life cycle of one individual
    from <conception> to <soul death>

    Claudia in her scaly red winged self
    emerging from the mudbath was,essentially the new but
    unfertilized dragon egg in a dragons life cycle.

    If she'd known what being a 'fertilized' ovum entailed,she probably
    never would have entered Tia Matta's spa or left it for that matter
    after her metamorphoses into a unfertilized 'egg'

    However 33 hours later,after Tia Matta had dealt with Claudia's
    issues,the East India dragon had gone through the late Claudia's purse and discovered she had a twin brother named Erik.

    And just like human eggs can split and create twins from one egg,the same
    magic that had created Claudia was now working on her unsuspecting
    brother Erik.

    So Ta Matta phoned him up and used her powers to get him
    to tell all.

    Thirty three hours after Erik had lost contact with his sister,he'd
    started having weird dreams about blood sucking leeches,red scales and black manes sprouting out of his sisters backside.

    So Tia Matta flew over ,rounded up some unsuspecting humans and
    the whole party headed to Erik's remote cabin.

    Tia Matta gave the three young female security guards she'd
    paid handsomely a VHS tape from the 1980's called
    <Dragonslayer > to watch whilst she did her thing with the guy.
    She looked at the log beam hanging high in the ceiling,paced off some measurements along the floor and had the gals fill up a
    six foot diameter tub with about three feet of water,"not too cold,not too hot"

    That tub of water just happened to be directly directly under the log beam 13 feet above it.

    The 3 gals were puzzled by all this behaviour,but the pay was good so they settled in the couch in Eriks living room and got down
    to watching <Dragon Slayer>

    then convinced a shivering Erik to go to
    the hothouse sauna he'd built with her.

    Erik was a bit of a repreesed virgin and Tia Matta had to employ her magics more then once to convince him to let her enter the steam room,nude,with him.

    First rose the steam,then Erik shrieked as he noticed the layer of mud slowly rising around him,and Tia Matta for that matter.

    The fact she seemed to think this was all perfectly normal
    really got him shrieking even loder and he tried to get off the bench
    and cross the gripping mud to the door,only to fall on his back
    and give one last scream as the mud poured into his mouth and throat.

    He didn't blank out,but could only scream in his mind as he /and then she,felt the changes to her
    body.
    When she finally broke free of her rock hard cocoon of dried mud,
    to,a still terrified Erika tore
    bits of mud off her heaving scaly red bosom with
    her clawed hands .

    She squawked in horror as the mud fell away from her red scaled bats wings,away from her black slitted glowing
    yellow eyes .

    Naturally she pulled away from the
    mud in the now hot as hades hothouse.

    She yelped in pain as her black furred mane was still stuck in
    the mud,so some patches of black dragons fur tore out of her sensitive
    hide and stayed behind.

    Then came the ever building waves of thirst from her parched
    mguts and Erika croaked
    out
    "Thirsty dying thirst"

    She tried ti stand and fell over on limbs that suddenly had no muscle to them.

    "The first stage is always the hardst" came the deep feminine voice nearby

    Erika turned her head to look over.

    A huge green scaled and black wing feathered dragon was crouched where nude Tia Matta had once been

    "Thirsty " pleaded Erika as her neck began to bulge and gill slits began to
    form in it.

    "Damn,you changing a lot earlier then your twin did" said
    Tia Matta as she scooped up the altering Erika and burst
    out of the hothouse,heading for the cabin where the three
    female humans continued watching another movie they'd replaced <Dragon Slayer > with

    "You shouldn't have done that" came Tia Matta's voice behind them

    They spun around to find Tia Matta standing there,a croacking "thirsty thirsty thirsty" Erika behind her.

    "Did you three get to the part in the movie where the momma dragon
    hangs the maidens upside down from the cave ceiling..."

    Tia Matta's words were punctuated by the pops as Erikas swelling
    neck began to lengthen and swell even more with flickering
    gills,her arms and legs shrank away and her lengthening torso
    began to wiggle as the red scales fell away to the floor,revealling the slime oozing off her past sickly white skin.

    Her yellow eyes began to bulge outwards as the wellow grew even more yellow,her black pupils narrowed as the black in them turned blood red .Where her legs used to be a spiny tadpole like tail pushed out and her head reshaped itself till what it was nothing human.
    all big googly eyes,and spread impossibly wide boneless toothed
    circular mouth.

    The creature Erika had just turned into,the first
    stage of her dragon life cycle,"a fertilized dragons egg" so to
    speak,or a Blood Beast in blunter terms,thrashed about as her human thoughts evaporated away and her first dragon thoughts and insticts
    came into existence

    -she was a she
    -she was a dragon
    -she was awfully thirsty
    -she couldn't breathe

    Her dam,Tia Matta gently lifted the newly born dragoness
    off the floor and carried the slimy wriggling Blood Beast to the tub of lukewarm
    water and the Blood Beast calmed down as she could breath again

    In short order the three human girls ,screaming till Tia Matta
    gagged them,were hanging trussed up upside down from the
    log beam,wide eyed with horror,as their human blood fell in streams
    to the tank 13 feet below them.

    Baby began her first feeding,the streams of human maidens blood
    pouring into her wide open mouth as she quenched her thirst.
