    Another Dad, Another Life

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 3 years ago O

    Hear the door open as a man steps in. He somewhat resembles Colby except he was a bit thinner, taller, and had a full beard. "Oh Colby, I didn't expect you home so soon" the man says before you recognize him. It was Colby's Dad! How had you forgotten? You quietly try to sneak the cookies back but Colby's... well technically your "Dad" catches you.

    "Colby didn't we agree that you would try to eat less junk food. If you keep this up we might have to get you on a diet" your Dad says. You gulp as the dreaded thought of only eating healthy food crosses your mind. Then you shake your head and try to remain calm by rationalizing. "Why were you so worried?, You think, you weren't really Colby". In fact it shouldn't bother you, but as you walk away from the kitchen and to the stairs your stomach begins growling.

    "Colby I never knew you had to eat so much" you think trying to calm your stomach. It seemed eating only 5 cookies was not going to cut it for your appetite. You head back to your room and realize you've gotten a message from George who had put Colby and you in a group chat.

    George: "Your little brother is so annoying! But it beats my older brothers"
    Colby: "You're so lucky! Your two older brothers are awful! They keep being jerks"
    You: "Colby I'm so hungry!!!! Also your dad said he might put you on a diet"
    Colby: "He better not or I'm not going back to my body"
    George: "Me neither"
    You: "Very funny. See you in the morning"

    You turn your phone off before you think back to Colby's text. Was he serious? There was no way he would give up his own body just over a diet...would he? You shake your head and try to forget about it. "I'm just a little shaken up from being Colby that's all" You think but you continually begin to ponder what would happen if you lost the magic socks and you were stuck in Colby's body for the rest of your life! You get up and frantically go looking for the magic socks which you find on top of Colby's dresser.

    "You had never been a person for this long" you think as you place the socks on your feet. Should you just quickly try on another pair of shoes or just stay as Colby until tomorrow morning?

    What do you do now?
