    The Project Begins

    Years ago, during a great war, a privet research and devilment team was secretly hired to look in to the possibility of a selective mutagenic drug, a formula that could be used to create supper powered Human-Animal hybrids. Project: Sasquatch.

    There first attempt used a combination of great ape, kangaroo, elephant and half a dozen other D.N.A. sequences plus artificial D.N.A. codes to fill in the gaps. It was designed to change a normal human into a giant hairy Sasquatch like creature that hade the strength to life a truck over its head without breaking a sweat, legs that could jump over a house and skin that could stop bullets.

    The project was considered a failure, over one hundred men and women wore given the drug during human testing but the only reactions to it seemed to have would be an increase to the amount and growth rate of body hair on the subject, also, female test subjects stated lactating large quantities of milk. Something changed inside them as while; it didn’t so much make them hornier as it made them more concerned with reproducing, almost all of the people given the drug went on to have many children. Three out of four of the children born to them tuned out female, and although they appeared normal at first, when they hit puberty they stated to develop the same side affects as there parent(s). The mutant D.N.A fused to there own by the experiment had another unexplainable effect, the participants preferred the company of each other to that of regular people, even if they didn’t know that the other had the same artificial D.N.A as them, some how they where inherently drawn to each other.

    Five children per family, it’s about the average, but the number of kids one family can have can get very high. Fraternal twins and triplets are more common among there females compared to normal women. In the most extreme case recorded a single women had seven at once and before they where a year old she had another five. She didn’t survive, but all of her children did and where sent to orphanages across the contrary, most where adopted by loving human families. Under normal circumstances these people go completely ignored by the rest of the population, a small variation of humanity that no body cares about. In the end the project was buried, the original generation was sworn to secrecy not even able to tell there children of there origin and project: Sasquatch was forgotten.

    That was, years and years ago! It is now the year 20XX, the children of the project are about to enter there fifth generation, most of the first generation are gone but not forgotten by there children, they now number in the tens of thousands but are spread thin across the world. Rich people, poor people, criminals and saints, the children have grown up to be normal, ordinary, extraordinary people with no idea the fate they shared. Despite the altercations in there genetic code, they were just normal people and would have remand so if not fore the greed of a single man. Jacob Black, a rat among men, he was dirty, greasy little creep, working in the smallest office in the decaying remains of the lab project: Sasquatch was born in, when he found a small safe containing the last of the formula in it. He secretly tested the formula on the rats in his apartment, and then the stray cats and dogs in the streets. The smallest amount would make any female mammal lactate permanently without hurting it. It was worth millions to any one in the dairy industry, so he sold it to a major corporation. With in a year, the formula was pumped into every cow that they own. The milk was completely harmless, but something in it was the last piece of the puzzle, a catalyst that would “awaken” any of the projects children.


    This is the story of one of the awakened children, a young adult born again as a Sasquatch. It is a story of monsters and men, true love and devastating loss, blood, death, laughter and happy endings. Yet it’s still a good story.
