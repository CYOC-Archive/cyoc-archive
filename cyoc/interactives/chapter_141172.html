Its large, gaping mouth full of teeth and the glow-tipped appendage that gives it its name makes the angler fish a very distinctive fish. But many species of anglerfish are known for a particularly bizarre method of reproduction. Sexual dimorphism was very extreme, the lure-wielding creatures that are the common image are the females of the species. Evidently, its natural hermaphroditism and distinctive appearance was appealing to some people. Though uplifting one has only become recently viable with the uplifting of Cousteau and the start of Animalia Abyssus. Without the beaked whale's genes for rising and diving, a deep sea fish would come apart if brought out of the crushing darkness.

They were looking for an angler fish that had already formed a mating bond with a male, preferably with him being on her lower belly. And with miles of dark ocean to search, it would take weeks before they found what they were looking for.

A particularly long lure waved out in front of her mouth. And nestled on her lower belly, just in front of her pelvic fins, was a small lump that was the vestigial body of her mate.

    "Indeed," said Harold. The drone opened a compartment, revealing a special bottle designed for deep sea pressures. The bottle was filled with a white fluid that contained the specialized Animalian formula a deep sea fish would need to be able to go to the surface. It also contained some of the Hermaphroditus strain in case the angler fish wouldn't hermaphrodize on her own while uplifting.
