    The Magic Shop

    A new game

    A young man named James was exploring the store, looking for something interesting. He almost passed the game before giving it a double take. It was called Magical adventures, he thought it was a stupid name, but he loves Tabletop games, and so do his friends. James took a look at the back of the box and read it.

    • Prepare to take your players on a grand and truly magical adventure, full of changes!

    • Gender-Changes, Age Progression/Regression, Animal transformations, Shrinking, growing, body changes, and many more! You truly are limited by your own imagination!

    • Even something as simple as Character creation will blow the Adventurers minds!

    •Be transported into the world of Magical adventures!

    James was truly excited to play with his friends. After he bought it he went home and Read through all the instructions. On the front of the instructions it read "WARNING! YOUR PLAYERS WILL BE PUT INTO YOUR WORLD AND EXPERIENCE REAL WORD CHANGES, ALL CHANGES ARE PERMANENT UNLESS THE PLAYERS WIN THE GAME!" James was still excited, he read all of the instructions and built in modules. It is stated that Magical Adventures is built for One-Shots. And the best part was that none of this was stated in the players manual!

    James set up the table and invited his gaming group over. While they were arriving James set a character sheet and a Player manual at each of the seats for them.

    Characters:

    James Frank: 24 year old Male, He/Him, Brown eyes, Dirty blonde hair, loves giving his players a hard time. [Forever GM]

    Wolfgang Jager: 18 year old guy, He/him, Blue eyes, blonde hair, 2nd generation German immigrant, only plays Beast characters because he's a furry. [Lupine Bard]

    David Goodman: 19 year old guy, He/they, Green eyes, dyed purple hair, Wolfgang's boyfriend. [Kobold Artificer]

    Elizabeth King: 25 year old trans gal, She/her, green eyes, Brown hair, has an obsession with Elves. [Drow ranger]

    John Miller: 23 year old dude, he/him, hazel eyes, dark hair, Typical dudebro who only plays [Human fighter]

    The four sat down and were surprised at how simple the character creation was. The four players were asked to sketch out their Characters, Wolfgang put the most detail and effort into his Character Fenrir. He ended up helping David draw his Character Galax. John basically drew himself but old. And Elizabeth's Drow Lex looked beautiful, she even drew Lex's companion sir Hoots-alot the owlbear Chub.

    This is where the Crazy stuff happened. Within the Blink of an eye, the four players were no longer in the room. They were all in a field, and they looked like the characters they drew, everyone panicked, except for Fenrir who was quite happy being in the body of a 7 foot tall anthro grey wolf. A voice was coming from their Amulets, James' voice. "Welcome to the world of Magical Adventures!" He laughed. "Where the hell are we?!" John asked, sounding panicked. "As I said, the world of Magical Adventures!" "What happened is the better Question!" Galax yelled. "Well you see, I found this game at that old Magic shop. And-" He was cut off. "That place is actually magical? I would've gone their awhile ago if I knew that!" Fenrir interrupted. "As I was saying... and it sounded like a perfect game for you guys! So I bought it and... well you guys are here!" "I didn't sign up for this Shit!" James yelled. "Actually... this might not be so bad." Lex said, "Think about it, who hasn't wanted to be in their fantasy worlds as their Characters?! This is gonna be great!" "Yeah, I have to agree. Being a wolf is badass!" Fenrir gloated. "If I knew this was gonna happen I would've chosen to be a Lupine too." Galax grumbled. "I feel badass now that I think about it..." James smiled as he grabbed his Giant sword from behind his back, "I FEEL GREAT!" "I'm glad you all see this as nothing but good!" James sounded pleased. "Well... what's our first Quest?" Fenrir asked.

    What do you do now?
