    It began when I made her clothes disappear

    Starting it small...

    Joey could understand exactly why Amanda thought he was crazy. Magic reality altering ring? Of course that sounds absurd! Well, fortunately for Joey, he had the power to quickly make a believer out of the skeptical Amanda.
    He gently touched his precious ring with his other hand, scowling slightly as he concentrates to work the magic...
    Amanda stood silent right in front of him, hands on her hips and with an adorably snarky smile on her beautiful face, waiting for Joey to humiliate himself, presumably.
    Joey took a couple of seconds to decide on what aspect of Amanda he should change. He didn't want her to freak out and scream so he quickly settled on a small, innocuous transformation. Her hair color.
    Amanda blinked a couple times, looking impatient. "Ehm, are you finished yet? I should be getting back to work, you know, as fascinating as eh, magic may be a topic..."
    Joey made an impish smile. "Check this out Amanda" and he gives her his smartphone with the internal camera switched on.
    Amanda makes a silent gasp when she sees herself on the screen... Her hair was black? Like pitch black, as if it were dyed! But she was a natural blonde and she never dyed her hair, how was this possible?
    "Is this some kind of camera filter or something?" She asks, her voice a shocked whisper.
    "Nope!" Joey chuckles, "No filter, no trick, check for yourself!"
    Amanda gives him his phone back and quickly pulls a straight lock of her hair in front of her face so she can see it. Joey was right, it really changed color! "O my god!" She gasps again, "But, how, how can this happen? I mean it's not possible, things just don't work this way, right?"
    "Not conventionally. I told you, it's magic! You believe me now?" He asks her in a cheerful, friendly voice.
    "I guess I do, I mean, here I am with jet black hair and I'm pretty damn sure I've been a proud blonde all my life..."
    "Heheh, yup. So what do you think, this is fun no? Do you like your new hair color?"
    Amanda touches the tip of her cute, dainty nose pensively. "Hm, I'm not really sure? I mean, this is kind of scary but it could be fun I suppose...? Can you change it back, like undo the change you did to me?"
    "Yup. I sure can, easily!" Joey assures her before closing his eyes briefly. When he opens his eyes again, just like that Amanda was back to her original blonde self. "Already done! Here, check it out." He holds his phone in front of her face again to confirm he's telling the truth.
    "O my god. This is... pretty amazing! Really, you got me curious now... uhm... oh sorry, I didn't even catch your name?" Amanda asks feeling a little embarassed.
    "No biggie, the name's Joey and I'm happy to hear I piqued your curiosity... You want to see more?"
    Amanda bit her lip. "Mhh I don't know Joey, I mean this is kinda weird... What exactly do you mean, more? What else can your magic do?"
    "I'm not quite sure. Anything maybe. That's the fun part, no? I want to experiment, explore my power and see its limits, if it even has any... How does that sound?"
    Amanda makes a cute, nervous smile, pulls her silky hair behind one ear, then nervously tidies up her blue dress. "You want to experiment on me...? Like physically transform me into someone else...?" She swallows nervously.
    "Yup." Joey answers concisely.
    "That sounds... kinky... and also pretty scary, Joey..."
    "Are you scared of me Amanda?"
    "Well, maybe a little... gulp... I mean, should I be scared...?"
    Joey laughs. "Ha ha of course not! Come on, we'll just have some fun. Some kinky fun, as you yourself put it..."
    Amanda smiles half-heartedly and makes a nervous giggle. "Okay... Fun, he heh... But uh, I mean you can always just change me back to normal if I get scared and don't want to ehm, play anymore, right...?"
    "Of course. If you get scared, you can say the safeword."
    "Okay... My safeword will be... uhm, librarian, ok?"
    "As you wish Amanda." Joey says, amused.
    "Are you gonna turn me into someone else now...?" Amanda asks meekly.
    Joey leans in closer as she stands perfectly still, frozen by the surreal situation, and he gently caresses her smooth cheek with one finger. "Shh. You are really pretty, Amanda. I'm gonna have so much fun with you!" Joey tells her, sounding perverse. Then he closes his eyes, preparing to work his transformative magic on her...
