    Garrett the Muscle Boy

    Just when April had hoped the family would finally get to enjoy some peace and quiet, the doorbell rang.

    "I'll get it!" Garrett shouted, as he dashed for the front door. When he opened it, he stepped outside and closed the door.

    It was Jim, the kid next door who always liked to bully Garrett around. Luckily, thanks to April's new "power", she could hear and see everything that Garrett heard and saw through his eyes and ears.

    "Dude... You got a Dollar?" Jim asked Garrett.

    "A Dollar? No? Why?" Garrett asked innocently.

    "You said you were gonna loan me a dollar, Garrett! You weasel!" Jim growled, as he grabbed Garrett by the collar.

    "Hey... man... take it easy..." Garrett gasped...

    April sensed that her little brother was in trouble, and she realized that Jim was capable of seriously hurting Garrett since Jim was taller and older and bigger than him. Jim was 3 years older, and at least 6 inches taller!

    "I'll show him..." April mumbled to herself. "I'll teach him what happens when he messes with my little brother!"

    Jim grabbed Garrett by the shirt collar and practically lifted him off the ground.

    "Listen here, twerp... Either you give me my money, or I'll turn your pretty little face into ground beef, do you hear me?" he threatened Garrett.

    "I-I..." Garrett stuttered out of fear, when he suddenly felt an intense, tingling feeling go through every muscle in his body.

    Within seconds, Garrett's pectorals suddenly inflated like boulders, bursting out of his shirt until they were the size of beach balls! Garrett's arms suddenly grew and thickened until they were three times as thick, with bulging biceps, that were the size of bowling balls! His abs burst out like buttons on a tiled floor. His entire body grew twice as wide and twice as deep as before, and he practically tore his shirt to shreds in the process! His quads and his calves inflated to the point where Garrett was suddenly becoming bigger and heavier and taller... MUCH taller! Garrett was now 5'6" tall... 6ft... 6'6"... 7ft... Within seconds, Garrett was seven feet tall, and bulging with muscle! He was so big and heavy, that Jim could no longer hold him by the collar! In fact, Garrett was towering over him by over a foot!

    "Whaaaaaahhhh!!" Jim shouted, as he watched Garrett grow and hulk out before his very own eyes! "Nobody grows that fast!"

    "Wow... Look at me!" Garrett chuckled, as he looked down at his new, super-muscular body as he pulled his torn clothes off of him. He was only wearing his underwear.

    Jim was trembling with fear. He quickly turned around, and ran away from the house, as quickly as possible, leaving Garrett behind, in front of the door. April just opened the door to let her "little" brother in.

    "Get in her, big guy, before you catch cold!" she instructed Garrett.

    "Look at me, April! My muscles are huge!" Garrett said to her, as he walked in the door. Of course, he barely fit through the door, since he was so tall.

    "I know... I kinda like you like this. I may keep you this big for awhile... Let's see how you do as my big brother for a few days, okay?" April suggested.

    "Sure!" Garrett smiled. "I love ya, big sister!" He gave his sister a big hug with his huge arms.

    "Now that you're big and strong, you can put those muscles to use and do some chores around the house!" April said. "Afterwards, you can go out with your new muscles, okay?"

    Garrett was already pondering what he'd go do and where he'd go with his new muscles.
