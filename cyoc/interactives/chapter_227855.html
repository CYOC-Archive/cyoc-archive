    Getting ready for the party

    Now that Jeff had finished helping his mother, he received a message on his cell phone. It was a message from one of his friends asking him if he wanted to come to a party. Jeff was about to say yes but then he remembered his mother.

    It was then that Jeff decided to quickly check what his mother was doing and soon discovered that she was now in the living room sitting near the TV watching a children's program.

    Jeff knew he couldn't leave his mother alone but he really wanted to go to the party. While he could easily hire a babysitter, he wasn't sure if his mother would be very happy with this. So Jeff decided it was better to consult with her.

    "Hey mom, wouldn't you mind if I left and someone came to take care of you?" Jeff asked her.

    "Nope, not at all," his mother responded.

    Upon hearing his mother's response, Jeff believed that his mother didn't mind at all, but what he didn't know was that his mother wasn't paying attention to him and she was just repeating what the characters in the TV show were saying.

    Jeff proceeded to text a friend of his, Mandy, to take care of his mother. And then, Jeff headed to his room to set up some things in the Chronivac for Mandy's arrival. And soon, several things changed.

    Now there was a new room which was a nursery. There were now baby gates in the house and there was a large supply of diapers and baby food at their disposal. But the most noticeable change was that of Jeff's mother. She was now inside a playpen in the living room which was full of toys, stuffed animals, among other things. But not only that, his mother had gone from being a 3-year-old girl to a 2-year-old toddler girl. This change was made by Jeff so that his mother wouldn't cause Mandy too much trouble. Jeff also configured the Chronivac so that no one but him could make changes of Jeff.

    Meanwhile, Jeff's mother was still quietly watching TV. She was too distracted to notice the changes Jeff had made but soon she began to notice them when......
