    The quartet's plan was to secretly fill the water pistols of their costumes with punch and get Melissa Jenkins to "drink" some when they "accidentally" squirted her.

    "It's been spiked," admitted McCoy.
    "What do you mean 'spiked'? Somebody put nails in it?" asked another raccoon cowboy.
    "No boys. That means somebody, and we happen to know who, put something in the punch bowl to make people sick," said Harold Jenkins, moving a step closer to the one closest to the punch bowl.
    He was doing his best to control his anger, but there was no denying that the weretiger before them wasn't a happy man.
    "Err... I don't smell anything," commented the were-raccoon closest to the punch bowl. "Just how sick would someone get if they drank it?"
    "VERY sick," replied Harold Jenkins in a low growl, bearing all his teeth for the quartet to see.

    The four were-raccoons looked at each other, wondering what to do next, when a voice asked, "What's going on over here?"
    Everyone turned to look and saw werevixen/high school student Migumi Tachibana, the head organizer of the dance, approaching.
    Virginia bowed slightly, out of respect for the Tachibana name, before answering, "Somebody spiked the punch and we're waiting for a fresh bowl."
    "What? Again?" said Migumi in return. "Happens at least once every dance. I'll just dump this, wash out the bowl, and then make some fresh. Back as soon as I can," she promised, before grabbing the punch bowl and walking away with it.
    Both weretigers were visibly relieved at this new development, but the wereraccoons grew concerned. Even if they had been successful, would they have been guilty of poisoning someone? Would that land them in jail? How would they ever collect on Decrux's promise of hot sex then?
    Just as they turned to leave to figure out what to do now, Harold Jenkins said in a polite voice, "Oh boys..."
    This made the were-raccoons stop and turn back around to face the weretigers. "Yes, sir?" their self elected leader said.
    "If you see her before the police do, tell Alice Decrux that we're on to her schemes, and the last thing she ever wants to do is cross our paths again," said Harold, as he put an arm affectionately around Virginia's waist.
    "Yes, sir!" promised all four were-raccoons, as they turned and left the high school gym where the dance was being held as fast as they could.

    OUTSIDE, BACK IN THE SCHOOL PARKING LOT...
    "Okay. What do we do now?" one were-raccoon asked the others.
    "I have no idea," replied another. "I'm just glad we're not going to jail!"

    "Say. If Decrux goes to jail, that means she can't reward us," realized another.

    "But she doesn't need to know that. We can always say we squirted that girl with the punch in our water pistols like we planned. Then, if Decrux asks any questions, say we didn't stick around to see what happened next because we wanted to have fun with her," explained the first.

    There, she saw another empty punch bowl and ladle sitting on one of the metal food preparation tables.
    Checking the refrigerators, she found the rest of the premade punch in an over sized container.

    Dang! There's too much to do as it is! I've got to get back to the party! she realized, while grabbing the new bowl of punch. I'll have to come back and dump out the spiked stuff later, thought Megumi, walking back to the dance while leaving the original punch bowl out on the table where she left it.
