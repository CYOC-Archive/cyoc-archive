    Slithorin does Adam

    Once she was sure Ty's prying eyes could no longer see her Slithorin

    crept out of her lair with her egg and deposited it in a safe desolate

    spot somewhere.Then she cast her spells and entered our world.

    Slithorin was as dragon as dragon can me,and that extended to

    correcting the bloodline of her future mate.It just wouldn't do to

    let Joey's ancestors remain as humans.She did her research,discovered

    the various creation myths and scientific theories for the origin of

    the human race and did some exploring through time.To her surprise

    the Bishop of Usher had got it right after all.While she coiled up

    as unobtrusively as possible she watched while some big dude on a

    cloud sailed around saying "Let there be light" and other irrelevant

    stuff.Finally he got around to creating Adam,and while Adam lay

    sleeping the big dude took one of his ribs and created a blond

    bombshell named Lilith.God then woke them both up and told the two

    newly formed humans to go forth and multiply.Then he sailed off into

    the cosmos with his chorus of angels.

    Slithorin quietly bided her time while Adam and Lilith giggled and

    ran around and played.Slithorin was a feminist and always thought God

    was a woman.She was royally ticked off and dearly wanted to bugger up

    Gods Master Plan and strike a blow for womens rights.Adam and Lilith

    hadn't gotten down to the multiplying part,but she could tell from

    the way they were moaning and groaning it wouldn't be long.

    So she flapped her wings,cast her spells and her body began to shrink

    and change.Were once stood a hissing coily dragon now stood a dark

    haired beauty with green eyes and light brown skin.Slithorin marched

    forward and picked up Lilith and heaved her into the bushes,saying

    "move over sister,this ones mine"Then Slithorin blinked her eyelashes

    and smiled down at a startled Adam and said "hi Adam,my names Eve!"

    And that ladies and gentlemen,is how Slithorin became the mother of

    the human race!
