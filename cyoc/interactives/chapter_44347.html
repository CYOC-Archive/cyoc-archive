Kurt hadn't noticed that his nails were sharper yet, the effect of his family's talisman beginning to work its magic.

    Rose pawed at her bell, giving a giggle. She was easily amused by the trinket. Though this was Animal Academy she wasn't sure if the bell was a prank or part of the studies. Rose had joined to be more like the creatures she read about in her books. Beasts of the world, having adventures and living in wild passion with one another. To her this was her best chance to live her dreams. Unknown to Rose a mix of brown and black fur had began to spread under the ring around her neck.

"Its a basic test, just to examine your overall body size, weight, height, and senses. This allows us to create a learning curve for you."

Underneath the mask Juna's own furs began to grow across the bridge of her nose, the first step for her at Animal Academy. Cassie raised her arm and turned around, getting the group to follow behind her to the med-ward.
