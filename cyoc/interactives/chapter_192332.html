    Rachel in Wonderland: Toy Story

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago S O Shrinking

    After taking as little of the potion as she could, Rachel places the bottle on the floor next to the package and waits for the results. Fortunately Rachel didn't have to wait too long as her body began to shrink, along with her Alice in Wonderland outfit.
    "Aaa, that feels better." Rachel said as she began stretching her shrinking body and limbs. As Rachel reached her original size, she quickly realized that she was still shrinking.
    "Ah crap, I was afraid of this." Rachel said to herself. The shrinking spell continued unchecked, taking poor Rachel down to half her old height and then passing it. Without any way of stopping it, Rachel could only sit and watch as the world around her grew to gigantic proportions. Finally the shrinking spell subsided, leaving Rachel at 11.5 inches tall. Rachel picks herself off the floor and straightens out her dress, thinking about the new problem she has fallen into.
    "well this is just peachy, I wanted to get smaller but not this small. At this size, I'm practically a toy now. I didn't even drink that much of the shrinking potion and I still ended up being so tiny. It must have been more potent than I thought. Oh, what'll happen to me now?" But just as Rachel was starting to get worried, a bright light shined out of the package then quickly faded back into nothingness, as if it were trying to get Rachel's attention. Rachel wasn't sure what to make of the light or what it meant, but she knew she had to find out what the package wanted. Besides, it was better than standing around in her room the size of a barbie doing nothing. Rachel walks over to the package, which was now a foot taller than she was, then jumps up over one of its flaps and grabs the edge and pulls herself up far enough to look inside. Rachel search for the source of the light within the package, but only saw lots and lots of packing peanuts inside. Suddenly the flaps began to move on their own and lift themselves towards the ceiling, causing Rachel to slide head first into the package and deep within the packing peanuts. Rachel pulls herself up and stands neck deep in the peanuts, seeing the flaps still pointing upwards.
    "What was that for?" Rachel said to the package. Then the flaps started moving towards the center of the package, readying to be sealed shut. Rachel tries to get out before it was too late, but the peanuts were hampering her movements. Rachel could only watch as the flaps slid on top of one another and closed off the package, trapping her inside.
    "HEY! LET ME OUT OF HERE! COME ON THIS ISN'T FUNNY ANYMORE! OPEN UP GODDAMNIT!" Rachel shouted at the package, but it was too late. Suddenly the package and everthing that came out of it completely disapeared without a trace. The room returned to normal and another Rachel appeared on the bed, reading a copy of Alice's Adventures in Wonderland.

    Meanwhile, the package carrying the real Rachel materializes inside a delivery truck driving down the highway. The package lay labeled with a new address, waiting to be taken to its next destination.
