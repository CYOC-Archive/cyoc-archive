    "Ehh, I wish this slave forget that this was a genie lamp.", you said with panic in your voice.
    You didn't want to loose control of the situation again.

    "Eh, wait did I say?", asked the asian slave while she blinked with her eyes. "Hey wait a
    moment! Is that a magic lamp in your hand? You just wished for something!"

    Oh crap,oh crap! You wished her to forget about the lamp , but with this wish you reminded
    her of the lamp again.
    "I wish that nobody notice that this is a magic lamp.", you tried to repair your last wish.

    "What are you talking about? So this is indeed a magic lamp!", said the slave.

    What? Oh no! You used up all your wishes again! You sit down and started to cry.

    "Hey, give that to me! This is my ticket out of this!", said the slave and grabbed the lamp out
    of your grasp.
    "I wish to be the empress of this world and this slave to be my personal slave."

    You suddenly felt this sense of vertigo the same way as when you changed first. Your
    surrounding changed completely. You were now in a throne hall with chinese decor. At one
    end of the hall stood a big stone throne which looked like a curled up dragon. On the thorne
    sat the former asian slave, clothed in the dress of an old asian empress. You noticed a chain
    in her hand which led to a collar around your neck. This the first time you looked at your
    body after this wish. You still looked like a porn version of Jasmine with blonde hair. But you
    were now clad into an asian geisha outfit complete with the make-up.

    You began to wonder what this once more changed world would look like. But at first you
    must find a way to get the lamp back and get out of this trouble. Until now you just changed
    one master for another.
    "So, slave, I wish you will obey every command from me!", wished the new empress.
    "You seem to now a lot about this lamp so it could be useful to have you near me. But first,
    what is your name?"

    "I am Jasmine." You didn't wanted to answer, and certainly not with that name, but somehow
    you was forced to do so.

    "What do you know about this lamp?"

    "It is a magic lamp. The owner gets three wishes. After that a new owner must use at least
    one wish, before the previous owner can use it again."
    Again you didn't wanted her to know this. Now it would be even more difficult to get the
    lamp from her.

    Damned! What shall you do know?
