    A diaper dispenser?

    Previous chapter

    added 5 years ago AR BM

    Looking through the glass Paige saw that the vending machine was weirdly filled with different sizes of diapers. All the slots were filled with a different size, the first had one fit for a newborn, the second had a baby sized one, the third held one for a young toddler, the fourth held toddler sized training pants (which she guessed could be considered diapers), the fifth one contained child sized diapers, the sixth had ones sized to fit a teenager, and finally the seventh slot contain adult sized padding except these looked babyish and had designs on them. Another odd thing Paige noticed about the machine, aside from what it contained, was that it had two keypads to the right of the glass, one positioned halfway up and the other placed towards the very bottom. Even the retrieval slot was placed low to the bottom and didn't have a flap covering it, the whole thing lying wide open.

    The whole thing was completely strange and weird.

    "Huh, I never expected Zane to be into diapers" she questioned out loud, "But why did he stock it full of ones for younger ages...it doesn't make any sense."

    Checking the sides of the machine Paige saw that they were painted half baby blue and pink with the words "Diaper Depot" inscribed on both sides in large lettering with a baby block decal font. Underneath were the words "For those who want to experience their baby side". There was also the image of a pacifier and baby bottle, one in the bottom left corner and the other in the top right corner. The reverse was true for the other side.

    Paige,

    If you're reading this then you noticed the Diaper Depot first, one of my more genius inventions and one that I think you'll very much enjoy. Know that this single machine is fully stocked with an infinite amount of diapers for you to use. Don't bother asking me to explain that as you wouldn't understand. Anyway there's no need for money, just enter your selection on the keypad and the machine will dispense the appropriate sized diaper. Don't worry about any of them not fitting you, trust me they will. If you don't believe me try it out and see what I mean. Also know that if you want to stay as you are just keep using and dispense the same sized diaper. Before I go I suggest that if you want to really enjoy my invention then chose one of the younger sized ones first. But I warn you not to choose the newborn size unless you have someone with you otherwise things will be very difficult for you.

    P.S. If you want to go for a mental change as well to really get into it there's a dial hidden behind a panel on the lower right-hand side. Adjust it accordingly but don't go all the way unless you have someone you trust completely nearby.

    Your hubby Zane,

    After finished the letter Paige tossed it aside before getting on her knees and curiously inspecting the area of the machine the post script mentioned and easily found the panel where it said it would be. It didn't have a lock and was made to be easily opened. Opening it up she found a large turn dial inside with the words "Mental AR" above it. The dial turned a red arrow in a half circle with three equal sized lit up colored quadrants; green, yellow and red. Looking closely Paige saw the words "Completely aware" next to the green quadrant, "Half aware" above the yellow quadrant, and "Barely aware". Currently the needle was pointed all the way to far left with the words "Fully Adult" and the far right had the words, "Fully Baby".

    Despite reading all this Paige wasn't sure what the dial did and figure it was best to leave it alone for now. Closing the panel, she stood up and faced the machine again.

    As Paige stared at the babyish garments behind the glass she found herself wanting to actually try one on. Checking to make sure she was alone and before her nerve failed her she reached up with her hand and typed a selection into the keypad before pressing enter. Seconds later the ring unwound and her prize dropped into the retrieval slot with barely any noise.

    Paige had ended up choosing the ________ diapers.
