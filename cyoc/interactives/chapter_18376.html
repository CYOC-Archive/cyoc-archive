    When Peter walked on to the basketball court, he spotted Jamie, who was one of his classmates.

    “Can I play with you guys?” he asked his friend. Jamie, however, didn’t recognize him, since Peter still looked like a little eleven year-old boy!

    “We’re all high school guys, little dude!” Jamie said to the little Peter. “You’re too small to play with us!”

    Peter became upset when Jamie said this.

    “Jamie! It’s me! Peter Karlowski!” Peter shouted back at him.

    “No way, dude!” Jamie chuckled. “Peter is a big guy! You’re just a kid!”

    “Come on! Look at me, Jamie! It’s me!” Peter pleaded with him.

    Jamie walked up to him when he noted the familiar face on a kid’s body.

    “But… You look like you’re five years younger! Aren’t you supposed to be sixteen or seventeen years old?” Jamie wondered, as he stared at the little kid with blond hair and blue eyes.

    “I am seventeen years old! I’ve just been transformed into my eleven year-old self again!” he answered. “Just let me play with you guys!”

    “But, Peter! Look at yourself, dude. You’re too small! I don’t want you to get hurt while playing with these big guys…” Jamie began to say to him, when Peter suddenly felt something come over his body.

    “Whoa! It’s happening again… Here goes!”

    Suddenly, the little boy started to grow taller. Luckily, his clothes grew with him, as he suddenly shot up, while passing through puberty in a matter of seconds. The boy, who was barely 5 feet tall, slowly started growing taller and taller, while he started to become bigger and more muscular at the same time as well. His chest expanded, his torso grew longer and wider. Not only that, but his arms and legs grew thicker, longer and more muscular. Within moments, the little 11 year-old kid now looked like a 14 year-old teenager.

    “Holy cow! I can’t believe this! I’ve never seen anybody grow that fast before!” Jamie exclaimed, as he continued to stare at the teen.

    Suddenly, the growth spurt started up again, and the blond boy grew a little taller, as his face looked older and his voice had become deeper. His chest, arms and legs became more hairy, and he slowly became slightly more muscular as he continued to grow taller. Peter was back to seventeen years-old again, and he was about 6 feet and 3 inches tall.

    “There!” he grinned, as he swung his arms around. “Now, can I play?”

    “Peter! You’re back!” Jamie cheered, as he grabbed hold of Peter’s hands. He couldn’t believe what he had just witnessed.

    “Of course, Jamie! I told you it was me!” Peter laughed. “Now, let’s play ball!”

    “But… But… What is this? First you were a little kid! Next thing you know, you’re going into your old self again! What is this? How did you do that?”

    “I didn’t do anything! Allen’s doing it to me! I’ll explain later! I just change a lot…” he snickered. “Let’s play! I’m tired of playing with little kids! Come on!”

    Jamie passed the ball to him. As they played basketball, Peter just felt happy to be back again. Occasionally, Peter would hear Allen’s chuckle inside of his head. He had already gotten used to the fact that Allen could change him into something else at any time!
