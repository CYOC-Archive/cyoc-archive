    “Oh no! He’s disappeared!” Mrs. Johnson said, in panic.

    “No, he’s still there! He’s just too small for us to see. I can only find him if I shrink myself down to their size, or smaller…” Peter explained to her.

    “Well, what are you waiting for, my boy? Please, get them back!” she replied, as she became even more concerned.

    Peter just stared at her. For a moment, she felt like she was growing taller. Then she realized that she was the same, but Peter was now actually shrinking, and his basketball gear was shrinking with him. Peter rapidly shrunk down to about 5 feet, when he hopped on the bed. Then he continued to shrink, until he was about 3 feet tall. He then stepped on to the desk, which was next to the dresser. There, he continued to shrink down until he was one foot tall. In the meantime, Mrs. Johnson just stood there, as she watched the 6 foot 3 inches tall teenage boy shrink to just a foot in height.

    Peter asked her to let him stand in her palm of her hand, so she could set him on the dresser. To Mrs. Johnson it seemed so strange, to hold a tiny human being in her hand. She gently set the tiny Peter on the dresser, close to where the magazine was. From there, Peter continued to shrink until he was only 4 inches tall.

    “I’m going to disappear now, but I’ll be right back, okay? Don’t go anywhere!” he said to her. She could barely hear him, so she had to move her face closer to the dresser so she could watch Peter as he continued to shrink to nothing on Jenny’s dresser.

    “Alright, Peter, thank you. Please get my daughter!” she said to him, with tears coming down her face as she watched the teenager shrink into nothing. When Peter was gone, she pulled up Jenny’s chair and she decided to sit down, reading the message on Jenny’s computer. It was just too much for her, and she felt scared that she might never see her daughter again.

    It was a vast, barren landscape which closely resembled that of a desert. Everything was made out of wood particles; there were mountains and valleys which had rectangualr shapes to it. It wasn’t a real desert or canyon, no this was the top of a wooden oak dresser, but seen from the eye of a human being who had become so tiny, that she felt like she was looking at everything from a microscope.

    The landscape was rough, there was no level surface to walk on, and Jenny was constantly trying to maintain her balance. She was in another world, in one where there were no people. She was in the world of microscopic organisms, and she wondered if this was going to be her fate. How was she going to get out of this place? Would she die in this place?

    Suddenly she heard a male voice call out her name.

    “Jenny? Jenny? It’s me, Peter! Please say something if you can hear me…” Peter said.

    Jenny heard him but she couldn’t see him.

    “Peter? I’m here? I’m… I’m… I don’t know where I am!” she shouted back.

    “Just keep on talking, Jenny… I’ll find you in a few moments…” Peter replied.

    “I’m so happy you came for us. I was really wondering what was going on, ever since we started shrinking at school! Please come quickly Peter! This is such a weird place! I can’t believe where I am now… But I can’t wait to get out of here as soon as possible… Where are you, Peter?” she said out loud, as she looked around.

    “I’m right behind you, Jenny…” Peter said calmly.

    Jenny turned around and saw Peter standing right behind her, just a few feet away.

    She slowly became angry as she walked towards him. She wasn’t aware of the fact that she was still shrinking, while Peter had stopped shrinking already. So Peter appeared to be growing bigger and taller to her.

    “You incredible JERK! How DARE you shrink us out of existance?” she began to shout at Peter, as she pounded her fists against his growing chest. “And why? What did I do to you? You have NO RIGHT to shrink us into nothing! Who do you think you are? After this is over, I will NEVER want to speak to you again, Peter!” she said, as she began to sob while leaning against Peter’s growing body. At first Jenny reached to hism shoulders, now she was barely coming up to his torso and she was now looking at his quads when she looked straight ahead.

    “How come you’re still growing?” she said, as she tried to wipe the tears from her face.

    “I’m not growing anymore. I forgot to stop your body from shrinking even further.” Peter said quietly, clearly feeling hurt by Jenny’s angry remark. But Jenny now realized that she needed Peter in order to grow back to normal!

    But luckily Peter didn’t hold it against her. Jenny stopped shrinking, and she slowly walked back to the teenage boy, who appeared like a giant to her. She was just as tall as his legs.

    “Listen, Jenny. I can perfectly understand why you’re angry at me. The whole thing was my fault, I made the mistake and I’m responsible for it. I’m sorry for the horrible experience I made you go through. And if you don’t ever want to talk to me again, well I can only accept that.” Peter said in a sad tone of voice. Jenny felt strange, getting an apology from a kid who was over twice her size! But she felt relieved that Peter was honest about the whole thing and that he apologized to her.

    “I forgive you, Peter. We all make mistakes, even a Wizard can make a mistake now and then!” she replied, smiling back at Peter for the very first time.

    “Well, let’s find Mike so that we can all grow back!” Peter said to her. Peter allowed Jenny to grow a little until she was just as tall as he was. Then he gently grabbed her arm and wished for them to appear wher Mike was and for them to be Mike’s exact height.

    Immediately Peter and Jenny were in a white surrounding, with large black circles surrounding them. They were walking on a smooth, white surface which seemed like plastic, and the black circles were dots of ink.

    Peter and Jenny quietly walked on past the giant black objects. It seemed like there were thousands of them, for this is all they could see, even past the horizon.

    “Mike? Hello Mike! It’s Jenny and Peter!” Peter shouted out loud.

    “Mike? MIKE!!” Jenny repeated, when she saw Mike walking past another black circle.

    Mike and Jenny then ran towards each other and they embraced. The hugged and kissed, although it was getting more difficult for them to hug one another since Mike was still shrinking. This time, both Jenny and Peter still appeared to be growing to him, and he soon found himself looking up to Peter and Jenny, who were becoming like giants to him.

    “Oops, I better make you stop shrinking, Mike. Before we lose you again!” Peter said, as he silently commanded the shrinking to stop.

    Then Peter grabbed both Mike and Jenny by the arm, and ordered all three of them to grow to exactly one foot in height.

    Mrs. Johnson was sitting in Jenny’s chair, when she noticed something happening on the top of the dresser. At first, there was a small speck. Then there were three specks next to each other. Within moments, she recognized the tiny figures of Jenny, Peter and Mike, as the three continued to grow rapidly.

    Mrs. Johnson’s eyes lit up and she became elated when she recognized her daughter. At the same time, she stood in awe when she saw how small they were.

    Within moments, they were about a foot tall. Mrs. Johnson extended her hand with her palm facing up, and she allowed the three tiny teens to walk on to her hand. She then placed her hand on the floor, so the three walked off and stood there.

    Peter once more grabbed Mike and Jenny’s arms and commanded them to grow back to their original sizes as fast as possible! Within moments, the three of them were growing past 2 feet, 3 feet, 4 feet in height. Jenny stopped growing first, when she reached her original height of 5’4”. Mike continued to 6 feet, and Peter continued to 6’3” since he was the tallest of the three.

    Jenny and her mother embraced right away, while Peter and Mike talked.

    “Mike, I owe you a huge apology! I’m sorry for blowing up at you in school today. I was just frustrated, stressed and overwhelmed by all the things I had going on this weekend, and I just lost it when you asked me to shrink you. I’m sorry; there was simply no excuse for that. I feel really bad about this…” Peter said calmly.

    “Forget about it, Peter! I’m just happy you came after us! I’ll have an incredible story to tell in my biology club about this!” Mike said excitedly, as he placed his hands on Peter’s shoulders.

    “Is that why you asked me to shrink you?” Peter wondered.

    “Oh yeah! I just wanted to experience it and feel what it was like. I wanted to be in a microscopic world for just a little while. I know I was asking the impossible, but everybody around school told me that you were the guy who could make my wish come true!” he replied, with a wide grin on his face.

    Peter’s jaw dropped wide open when Mike told him this, for he realized that more people knew about his powers than he thought. For a moment, the thought of the whole school knowing this upset him and he began to feel sad, but Mike picked up on it and decided to cheer him up.

    “Peter, you’re the most famous guy at school! Everybody knows who you are and what you’re capable of doing! Trust me; your secret really isn’t a secret anymore. The bullies fear you, the geeks admire you, and the jocks respect you. That’s why you’re so busy when classes end every day! Most people will stand in line to talk to you, Peter! So cheer up and enjoy the spotlight!” Mike explained to him.

    “Hey Mister Wizard, can I ask you for a favor?” Mrs Johnson said to Peter, as she tapped on his shoulder.

    “I have here four empty bowls. Can you fill them up with ice cream?” she asked Peter. And Peter loved to grant wishes like this, and he immediately used his psychic powers to find out what the favorite ice cream flavors were for Mike, Jenny and Mrs. Johnson. Suddenly, each bowl was filled with a different color and flavor of ice cream!

    “I’m going to have to go on a diet after this…” Jenny said, as she took her first bite.

    “Maybe Peter can make you shrink again!” Mike replied.

    “I think we’ve had enough shrinking and growing for one day!” Peter said.

    And the four burst out in laughter.
