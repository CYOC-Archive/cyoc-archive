    going to your room

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by holy devil 14 years ago A

    You and Torian get up from the table to go to your room. Torian has you put your hand on a glowing orb, and he says "Take Tom to the guest room on the second floor, in the east hall."
    The sphere glows brighter and eventually everything around you goes white, and then when everything goes back from white, your are in the guest room.
    "This is spectacular!"
    You look around the room and there is all kinds of stuff everywhere.
    You lie down on the bed, thinking you can get a couple minutes of rest before seeing what is in the room.
    When you lie down, you see a stick note stuck to the ceiling of the covered bed. You look at it and read it.

    ----------

    Help! I have been trapped here and I can't get out, don't eat food the minotaur offers you and don't eat anything from the vending machines! I know you will get hungry, so eat as little as you can manage and still be ok. I don't know what they put in the food, but it is not good. If you figure out how to get out please try to save me!

    ----------

    Oh shit! you think to your self. You already ate a ton of food at the dinner. You look at yourself and see of anything has happened to you because of the food. You are startled when you see scales covering the bottom half of your legs and feet. You look for a door in the room, and all you find is the orb. When you try to use it, it glows red and then disapears from the room. Well, I had better try to stay calm, you think to yourself.

    You decide that it would be a good idea to look for a trap door or something in the room, but then you think "Half of my legs are already covered in scales, I suppose it wouldn't hurt to eat a little more of that food, I'm already hungry"

    You wonder how after less than half an hour you are already hungry, and then you realize that the minotaur wants you to eat as much food as you can and something was probably put in the food to make you hungry. You decide that you don't want to starve, so you fill a large backpack with food from the vending machine and start looking for a trapdoor.

    When you finally find one in the closet under the rug which is under a wardrobe full of clothes, and have moved all of the stuff off of it, you climb in with your food, and eat a chicken leg.

    You look around, You are in a small tunnel with gas lights lighting the way down the tunnel for what looks like about a hundred feet. You walk down, not aware of the scales spreading up your legs as you eat the piece of chicken. After walking for about 100 feet, you end up in a large cavernous room, you see a large pool of what seemed to be water. It was dark, but there are some sparsely scattered crystals, and then you see it, leading from the room are hundreds of tunnels all going in different directions, one of them had what appeared to be magic lights lining it, so you put your bag of food down grab a couple pieces of food from the bag, and walk down the tunnel.

    You eventually come across a person setting up lights and say "hello, who are you?"

    He turns around and you see that he is an anthromorphic crow. He asks "What are you doing here?"

    Seeing him reminded you of your own changes, you look at yourself and see to your horror that you have changed even more, your entire body is covered in green scales.

    The anthromorphic crow shakes you gently by your shoulder, "What are you doing here?"

    "I-I don't know, I was taken in by the minatour-"

    "Torian? You had better be careful, that food in your hand, it didn't come from the palace did it? How much food did you eat?"

    Still distracted by the scales on your body you say, "I... What?"

    "Did that food you are eating come from the palace"

    "Yes..."

    "How much food did you eat so far?"

    "I've been eating the whole time"

    "OK then, how long have you been here?"

    "About half an hour, why?" You ask, completly forgetting about what is happening to you.

    "It's too late, you may as well eat that food then."

    "Too late for what?"

    "For you to become human again."

    "How long have you been here?" you ask.

    "About two days. Lets go back to the cave, I need to talk to you."

    The crow tells you about what the Minotaur does to the people he changes, and you agree to help him map the rest of these tunnels to help fututre people who get down here.

    You go back to the room to grab some things the crow needs, and some more food, and while up there, you notice yourself in the mirror. Your mouth has pushed out into a muzzle, and now has a split snake-like tongue, your eyes are orange, and your pupils are now vertical slits. You don't have any hair, but instead more scales, and two horns, maybe 9 inches long. You look behind you and see a tail, covered in scales sticking out from the what was the end of your spine. Your have elongated toes and only have three on each foot, each with a sharp black claw at the end. Your hands are also covered in scales, and other than that and claws, have remained mostly the same. The thing that stood out the most though was the wings coming from your back. They could be folded down to be fairly small, but they could also be expanded to get up to a 17 foot wing span. You decide it would also be a good idea to bring a mirror too, so the raven could see himself.

    What do you do now?
