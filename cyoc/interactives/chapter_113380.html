    You are Vail a 9 year old (soon to be 10) black scaled kobold, and you awoke one morning in your nest excited for today. For you today was special it was your coming of age ceremony, which meant that you could go out into the world in search of exploring, and experience. You got up out of the nest to look around the area to find something interesting, as you wait for your ceremony to commence. "Hey Vail!" A voice called from behind you, and you saw a familiar face, your best friend Aquil.

    Aquil was female, with blue scales, and she was already at surpassed the age of coming about 2 months before. "Aquil!" You called back, and the two of you hugged each other. "My coming of age ceremony is today I can't wait until it happens so I can choose what I want to become." You said excited. "I know, it's almost too much to bear at first, but don't worry it's okay." Aquil said encouraging you. "Anyways let's meet up after your ceremony, we can talk more about it once your done, bye." Aquil sai waving goodbye, and you did the same.

    You suddenly had a vision, you saw a vision in a dark void,"Hello lost soul how is everything in this pointless existence?" A voice asked then your vision cut off. You've been having visions like that your entire life, but you didn't want to tell anyone, because nobody would believe you. Nonetheless you had to focus on reality, so you headed back to the nest.

    Once back in your nest your obsidian scaled mother approached you,"Vail it's time for your coming of age ceremony, come let's go to the elder." Your mother spoke, and held your hand as you walked aside her. Once you arrived at the elder's hut she looked up at you,"Ah Vail I've been expecting you today. As for your mother please leave Vail, and I here in private. I'm sure you know how this works." The elder said, and mom nodded, and exited the hut.

    "Now, dear Vail before we begin let not a word leave this hut, at this very moment. After this ceremony this conversation would have never have existed, understand?" The elder said, and you nodded. "Good, now many think they choose their profession, but we'll let your mind, and soul decide that for you. Not to worry though most either get what they desire, or discover that they appreciate their calling more than what they desired. Now please aproach the orb before me, and let's see what your mind desires, and what your soul yearns for most." The elder said, and you did as she instructed.

    You sat before the elder and peered into the orb, and both of you reached out to it. In the orb you didn't see desires, but it was your visions lining up in chronological order presenting your reincarnation. Then the orb went dark, and you looked up at the elder who was shocked beyond expression. "You knew about your reincarnation?" The elder asked, and you responded,"Well, not exactly I saw these events, but all in random visions, and all scrambled. It was only now that I realized what they meant when they were presented before me like this. I didn't want to tell anyone, because they wouldn't understand, or believe me." You said. "It's a good thing you didn't, now allow me to explain everything. Beings who remember reincarnation are special only 5 beings are in existence, well 6 now." The elder said writing on a scroll, and placing it in a container.

    The elder then gave you the container,"Now when the human arrives here later today at this hut give them this, and tell them that I want them to read it immediately, and only them. This is not for your eyes only the human's. Once he finishes go with him, and don't worry about your parents or friends, you can see them all again eventually. Wait here for the human, and remember not a word leaves this room, this conversation never existed." The elder said, and you nodded.
