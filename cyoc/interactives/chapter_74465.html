    I took the seat willingly as she pulled out a clipboard with a few sheets of paper.

    "These are all standard NDA's," she said as she handed them to me. "I'll need you to sign everything before we
    go over the details of your employment here." I read them quickly but carefully, seeing nothing amiss in them.
    I signed them with limited hesitation, despite the minor reservations I was having at her rather forceful
    demeanor toward me.

    She smiled slightly as I handed the clipboard back to her. "Good. You DO understand that this means that while
    you're welcome to leave, and I'm welcome to terminate your employment at any time, you can't discuss ANY
    ASPECT of what you're asked to do, doing, or have done in relation to the agency, yes?"

    "Right, pretty much standard NDA stuff," I replied. "I double checked before I signed."

    She smiled again. "Responsible. Good. That's the sort of photographer we want here. Our market demands a
    higher than normal level of discretion, both on the consumer end and on the modeling end of things."

    "I'm assuming by photographs of a fetish nature, there's going to be some nudity?" I asked, which only got a
    smirk.

    "Yes, every shoot you're doing will have nudity, as well as a high sexual content," she said, watching me
    closely.

    "So it's porn?" I asked.

    "Not exactly..though some would probably class it as such," she replied. "The sex is a byproduct."

    "Umm..byproduct of what?" I asked.

    "Transforming into a werewolf," She replied, now staring at me and waiting on my reaction.

    "I don't do photoshop stuff, at least not THAT good," I told her.

    "I'm not asking you to, beyond basic airbrushing that you'd do to any model," she replied.

    "So you're going to have a special effects make up person here then?" I asked, still confused and slightly
    unsettled. This was weirder than I'd been expecting.

    She shook her head again. "No. The only makeup is what the girls would be wearing, and most of that would
    simply be anti-glare."

    "Umm..I'm a little confused about the werewolf thing then.." I trailed off, completely lost.

    Ms. Adair sighed, looking slightly annoyed. "When a woman transforms into a werewolf, it sends her into what
    could best be called a state of heightened sexual arousal. Your job is to both get quality shots of the
    transformation from start to finish as well as the pleasuring. I just want to make sure you can handle
    yourself professionally enough to get the shots."

    "Umm.." I couldn't help but snicker. "I can't say that I've ever been asked to photograph a woman while she's
    masturbating and shapeshifting, Ms. Adair. There's just no way I can say that I'll be able to remain impartial
    and professional." This had to be the most bizarre joke I'd ever had played on me.

    "Well then," she said, staring daggers at me. "I suppose we'll have to test you then."
