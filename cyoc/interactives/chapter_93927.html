    It was an ordinary day when you had gone out to get the mail. As you sorted through it you found a pink and blue envelope addressed to you. There was no return address on it and you knew you should be suspicious. However your curiosity got the better of you and you opened it. Inside was a letter and a debit/credit card with your name on it. This just made you even more curious and you read the letter.

    <i> <b> Dear sir/madam,

    Have you ever felt that you were in the wrong body? Maybe you've felt like you should be a different race or sex. Maybe you've felt that you should have a rounder or more angular face. Wouldn't it be great if you could change those things and more? Well now it is possible.

    It is with great pleasure that we inform you that you have been selected to test our product, the SecondBody Bodysuit. This bodysuit allows you to have the body you want for as long and as often as you want.

    If you wish to participate in testing our product simply activate the enclosed card by **/**/**** and we will send you a random bodysuit. Once the bodysuit is picked out we will email you a list of measurements and sizing tables for various clothing brands, and how much money is available, so you can purchase clothes that will fit your SecondBody if need be. If you do not wish to participate then you do not need to do anything.

    Part of participating in this test is that you have to wear the bodysuit for at least eight hours each day. The bodysuit you will receive includes a special sensor that will allow us to monitor if it's being worn or not. You will be paid if you wear the bodysuit for eight hours a day but you will be paid more if you wear it longer than that. Once the bodysuit is picked out you are the only one that will be able to wear it.

    We realize that the bodysuit that's randomly picked out may not be what you really want. When the bodysuits become available to the general public you will be able to purchase one at a steep discount. You will of course be able to keep the bodysuit you are sent free of charge.

    At this time we only have human bodysuits but we are working on other types of bodysuits.

    Sincerely,
    Candice Cashmere
    President and CEO of SecondBody Bodysuits. </i> </b>

    You thought about this wondering what you would get should you accept. Not knowing was kind of appealing to you as you could end up a different race or sex, maybe even a bit younger. Besides what could it hurt? With that in mind you activated the card, gave them your email, and waited for the email to arrive.
