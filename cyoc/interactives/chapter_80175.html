    Then the program asked him if he wanted a job. This surprised Nate but Nate figured the job was beta testing the game so Nate clicked, "yes".
    Finally the program asked him if he was ready to transform. Nate clicked, "yes".

    Then Nate suddenly stood up an walked out the door. Nate tried to stop himself but he was no longer acting under his own control. He kept

    Another manager said, "When other museums hear about that statue, they'll try to buy it from us. They'll offer us big bucks for that statue. We
    need to establish that statue is ours and demand a ten million dollar price tag for it."

    they discover I got a job as a statue?
