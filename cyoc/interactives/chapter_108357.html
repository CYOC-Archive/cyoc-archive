    So! You're pretty sure you've gone insane. You've been lead to a deserted temple by a leopard with human intelligence, and now you're being talked to by the goddess of the jungle. You might as well say hello to the psych ward right now. "Uh... if you don't mind, goddess..." you say to the voice, "Could you, like, maybe show yourself so I can take comfort in the fact that I'm not insane?"

    The voice is silent for a moment but then says, "Very well, mortal. If it helps you." There is suddenly a deep rumbling noise and the walls and floor of the temple begin to shake, sending jets of dirt and dust swirling in the moonlight coming in from the cracks in the ceiling and walls. There is a sudden jet of bright orange-yellow light that appears out of nowhere right in the area of the throne. You throw you arms over your eyes and scream as your yells become almost inaudible over the loud rumbling noise. About fifteen seconds later, it's over.

    Taking down your arms, you see a woman in a green dress and flats sitting on the throne with her legs crossed. She looks completely normal, except for the fact that she has the head of a leopard. She smiles as best she can with her muzzle and says, staring at you with her yellow eyes, "Is this well enough, mortal?"

    The sight of a woman with a leopard's head is enough to break you. Something snaps in the back of your wind and you scream, much to the goddess's dismay. Turning, you run out of the throne room, heading back into the hall that leads to the doors out. The goddess yells out in shock and then yells something in a long-dead language. You hear a roar behind you and turn, just in time to see the leopard that lead you to the temple bounding out of the door to the throne room and towards you, taking huge strides with it's thick tail whipping behind it.

    You suddenly trip on a protruding stone and go down hard on your stomach. Quickly flipping to your back, you look up just in time to see the leopard springing into the air towards you, growling, fangs bared, huge paws outstretched towards you. You throw your hands over your eyes, preparing for death...

    But nothing comes. You take your arms down and, much to your shock, the leopard is gone. You quickly look to the left and the right, wondering if it has hidden, but there is no place for it to hide here. Suddenly, a sharp pain in your gut pauses you to double over in pain. You feel a pressure rising in your throat as it travels upwards. You try to hold in in, but it escapes your lips in the form of a loud feline-like roar. As you roar, your nose becomes triangular and your mouth and nose push out into a muzzle complete with fur and whiskers. A lump rises in your throat and you try to scream, but all the comes out is another roar. You bring your hands up to your new muzzle to feel it, yowling in terror, but you notice your hands bulging into huge, furry four-toed paws with wicked harp claws on the end.

    You suddenly feel warm as a thick fur coat grows all over you. You feel your butt, thighs, stomach, and neck swell out with muscle as the fur washes over them. A long, thick, tail pops out of your rear and you feel your toes merge into paws and extend as well. The terror of the transformation is too much for you, and you fell yourself slipping into unconsciousness...

    About fifteen minutes later, you wake up. Groggy and wondering what happened, you lift your head up and look down at your body. You almost scram in shock. What you see is the massive body of a jungle leopard. You try to scream, but your muzzle doesn't seem to want to open. Suddenly, you get to your paws and begin padding towards the throne room, having no control over your new body. You try and do your best to resist, but you can't seem to move anything willingly.

    It's freaky walking on four legs, everything is much taller than you remember. Before you enter the throne room, you find yourself tuning towards a nearby pool of water looking into it. The wise face of a leopard stares back at you, with no indication that you were once human. You open your muzzle and examine your sharp new fangs, running your thickened, rough lounge over them. Your muzzle suddenly snaps shut and you pad into the throne room.

    The goddess is still there. She has her fingers together and looks very pleased. You sit down on your haunches about fife feet away from her and involuntary bow. You then get back up and stare at her. "That was a very big mistake. I was going to offer you godly powers. But no, you just had to run away in fright. So I had my leopard posses you, and now you are my servant."

    On the outside, you're a calm leopard watching it's mistress. On the inside, you'd a terrified human who wants to scream. But the goddess suddenly speaks again, saying:
