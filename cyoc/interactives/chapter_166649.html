    Ty's Power

    Ty becomes a female firedrake thrown into the Viking Age

    added by Anonymous 3 years ago A S TG O

    There were two smaller males amongst that group of elders and one
    older and bigger female Scandinavian firedrake.

    And the firedrake thought she knew who this Ty was.Which if
    she was correct,meant the human would behave in a certain way in the next few minutes.

    The old girl hadn't been too impressed with the choices of the two
    old geezers and she'd been even less impressed with the endless
    crowds of servile docile and totally without energy humans she'd had to turn into
    sorry excuses of dragonkind.

    So when the latest feeble minded excuse of a servile human
    stumbled before her eyes the firedrake decided to do something about
    it.A thousand year old memory had been sparked somewhere
    in that reptilian brain of hers.

    She reared back her neck and filled her firecrop till it swelled
    and glowed red hot.
    Had Ty just stood there she would have ashed him on the spot.
    Likewise if he had run away from her she would easily have been able
    to flamebreath to a range greater then he could run before she Got Him.

    Instead he dashed toward his Doom,screaming some silly
    human scream at her in defiance,expecting her to flame away.

    Instead the female firedrake,who was a bit bony in old age
    leapt forward,grabbed the human in her huge forepaws and breathed
    a swollen firecrops worth of fire down Ty's throat.

    And she held him firmly with a crocadillian-demon smile that
    betrayed no true emotion on the firedrakes part
    and made sure the load of fire stayed flickering and churning away
    inside her quarry.
    Ty didn't like what he was feeling in his insides.Basically though
    he looked human on the outside on the inside he was
    transforming into the innards of a female firedrake.
    And though his captors scaly eyelids were shut at the moment
    Ty knew she knew.
    As he gazed at that monster her eyelids slowly rose up
    revealing the glowing slitted eyes of a firedrake.
    But then for a second or three she revealed to him the eyes she'd once
    had ,the gigantic blue eyes of a human boy,Ty's eyes but bigger.
    Then the aged firedrake that had once been a young human boy
    continued his transformation.
    She flung him into the air in front and above her and threw a spiralling funnel of her fire to enclose the human and hide his transformation from the others,dragon and worthless humans alike.
    The fire collapsed in on itself and vanished
    but where there should have been the cindered corpse of Ty or
    perhaps a new female firedrake there was nothing.
