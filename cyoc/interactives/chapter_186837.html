There had been a last few wishes she'd not truly heard but been bound to grant anyway, forcing her to work her magic at a great distance - but for the most part, Raize's life had become utterly dull. No mermaids stumbling across the genie, no divers pulling her up, not even a real sense of the world around her lamp as anything other than deep, dark, and damp. The home of someone with riches, she suspected - a fool, she hoped, with a great deal of money and power she could put to use. Her hopes were higher than ever as she waited to be summoned forth by a rubbing of the lamp, and indeed, she soon found herself being drawn towards the exit... but not by the call of a new master.

    Some other force, a greater magic than even she possessed, was prying her out of the lamp. Raize tried to hold on, but her relative lack of experience with her cosmic powers was on full display as she was forcefully ejected, still bound to the lamp yet now forced to appear outside of it. Her smoky form floated in confusion as she glanced around, finding herself in a large, sweeping chamber - some sort of throne room, in fact. And where there was a throne, there was royalty: she looked towards the big chair to find that there were two big chairs, opulently decorated, each one seating one of the most beautiful women that Raize had ever seen.

Each one was flanked by an equally goregeous lady, Arabic for the blonde, Indian-looking for the brunette. Their garb easily identified them as a pair of queens accompanied by assistants... no, consorts, Raize was certain of it. This one spoke almost like an American, yet with a squave, charming undertone to everything. "What is your name, genie?"

You speak with Queens Mary II and Victoria II, Sovereigns of the United Kingdom." A pair of women married to each other and on the throne - she could only imagine the magical power they must have needed to make that happen. "You have been pulled from your lamp by Farrah and Darzala-" Raize's eyes went wide as the accompanying women turned their legs to smoke, revealing themselves as genies with trails leading back to the queens themselves. "-so that we might negotiate."

    Another woman with short white hair entered the room as Mary began to explain. "Our lovely companions sensed your creation, and your being trapped within the ocean. It was a miserable fate, one we sought to rescue from without delay... so that you might... join us."

    Darzala took over explaining next. "You're new at this, and you've yet to fully develop your powers - but Farrah and I make for excellent teachers." She floated away from Mary to circle around Raize. "You see, our devious little monarchs here have a delectable plan..."

    "Quite wonderful, in fact," Farrah agreed. The white-hiared woman was staring at Raize's chest as she stopped next to Victoria. "They seem to free our race, and to use our magic to remake the world into something better, something beautiful. An entire army of genies, the likes of which can make the universe their plaything."

    The idea filled Raize with excitement, but it wasn't without issues. "And you think you won't be stopped by someone? SALEM? The Council? The Guild?" She almost spat out the name of her former allegiance. "They'll throw everything at you to keep the world the way it is."

    Mary laughed. "Oh, don't worry. We've set the dominos in motion - soon enough, they'll all have their hands too full to worry about two strange, beautiful queens who almost seem to have magical luck." She squeezed her blonde wife's hand. "And in the meantime, we will gather together more and more liberated gnies to fight for their freedom alongside us... with you among the first."

    The white-haired woman finally spoke up as she stepped forward, a smile on her face. "My name is Chloe, and... I am to be your vessel, just as my highnesses have become vessels to their own genies." She adjusted her dress to show off more of the impressive bustline. "If your essence is bound to my body... you will be practically without limit in your capabilities. We will help to herald a brighter, more beautiful world, together."

    "And what's to stop me from taking your body and making the world over all by myself?"

And this poor girl could use some work, honestly - she had plenty of ideas for how to sexy up this woman. "So... let's say I'm open to your little scheme. What's the whole deal? What do I have to do - and what do I get?"

"Our terms are quite simple: you don't bring the Council down on our heads until we're ready, you do as you're told when we make a request-"

    "-and you never so much as think of backstabbing our beloveds," Farrah finished.

    Gah, so many rules, so boring. But it beat the crap out of staying in a lamp. "Fine. It's a deal." She was about to ask what was next, but a snap of Darzala's fingers answered that question. Her link to the lamp was suddenly and violently severed, sending a chill of shock up her spine as her smokey form began to lose cohesion.
