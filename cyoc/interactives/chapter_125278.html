    Ty sighed as he look at the young Eileen. "Eileen I need to tell you something." Eileen looked at him with a hopeful look on her face, "Eileen remember when I told you that the universe altered when I altered Dustin?" Eileen nodded as a confused look spread across her face. "I remember Ty," she said. "What does that have to do with me?"

    Ty gave Eileen a concerned look. "Eileen," he said. "The same thing is happening to you. Your becoming a little girl." Eileen turned her head giving Ty a befuddled look. "What are you saying Ty," she asked. "Are you saying that I'm actually becoming a ten year old girl? A real ten year old girl?!"

    Ty nodded and hung his head. "Yes," he muttered. "I'm sorry but the longer you're stay ten the more you become a real ten year old."

    Eileen giggled, which took Ty by surprise. "That's ridiculous," she laughed. "Sure I look like a kid and even feel like one but I still remember being an adult. Besides what about Becca she's still my daughter. What would happen to her if I'm a child."

    Before Ty could answer Eileen's cell phone began to ring. Curious Ty and Eileen walked over to where it rested. "It's Becca," Eileen said. Eileen picked up the phone and looked at it. Slowly Eileen handed it to Ty. "Here," she said. "I can't answer like this she'll never believe it's me." Ty took the phone, answering it and activating the speaker phone option. "Hello," he muttered.

    "Oh hey Ty," Becca answered. "Listen I wanted to call and say I would be running a little late coming home." Ty nodded before answering. "That's fine Becca," he said still slightly confused. "I just wanted to thank you again though for babysitting my little sister while she's home sick." Ty's eyes bulged as Eileen mouthed the words little sister.

    "It's no problem really," Ty said casually. "She's been a perfect angel the whole time. Becca giggled on the other line. "That doesn't shock me," she said. "She's always been very mature for her age. Besides I'm pretty sure she has a bit of a crush on you." Ty and Eileen blushed fiercely at that comment.

    "Just make sure she gets plenty of rest today," Becca said. "I don't want to her to miss anymore school." Ty answered ok before saying goodbye and hanging up. Eileen had a look of shock on her face as she gaped at Ty. "Kid sister," she exclaimed. "I've become my daughters kid sister!" Ty apologized as Eileen made her way towards the couch. The young girl tucked her legs under herself as she began to rub her forehead. "This is incredible," she said in awe. "The universe really did change me into a kid."

    Ty walked over towards the couch placing an arm around the young girls shoulders. "I'm sorry Eileen," he said. "This must be awful for you. Maybe I should make you your right age again?"

    Eileen smiled and looked up at Ty. "Don't worry Ty I'm not mad," she said sweetly. "I'm just in awe of this power you have. I really am just a kid again, I wonder what else has changed?"

    Ty stared at the young girl dumbfounded by her answer. "So your ok with this?" Eileen smiled as she gazed up at Ty. "Yea I'm ok," she said. "You can fix it just like you fixed Dustin. Now I'm just wondering what to do next?"

    "Well what are you thinking?" Ty asked. Eileen gave Ty a mischievous smile as she pondered her decision.
