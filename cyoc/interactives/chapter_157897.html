    "Hello Lady Kellie! My Goddess and Lady Lilith are waiting for you!"

    Kellie startled a bit when an animatronic fox woman bowed to her. Unlike in some horror games, the animatronic was in perfect condition, and avoided the uncanny valley, her face looking like one of those anime girl masks you saw at conventions, but made for that of a cartoonish but still attractive fox woman. The rest of the robot's body was designed to have an attractive figure, with carefully applied artificial fur.

    Kellie knew that voice from somewhere...

    "Vixi?" Kellie let a small gasp.

    "yes Lady Kellie. After Lady Lilith's assassin murdered me, but then the Goddess and Lilith chose to reconcile, it was decided reparations had to be made. This machine was purchased by Lady Lilith, modified by the Goddess' magic, and now serves as my vessel. Lord Jack's magic ensured that my family would take the change well."

    Kellie shuddered. She hated to admit it, but with all the vicious back and forth within Lilith's group, the building tensions between the two groups of magic users, the kitesune effectively ending their isolationism (if not by choice), and the looming mysterious vague threat of WHATEVER was going to be their doom... Kellie had forgotten about the furry turned anthro who was SUPPOSED to be an ambassador to the kitesune, only to be murdered by one of Lilith's demons. It was a chilling reminder of Lilith being a cold-blooded killer of someone whose only crime was their death being convenient... but... the rewritten reality performed by Baba Yaga... it HAD resulted in Lilith and Circe BOTH being good enough for each other that they WEREN'T cold blooded killers now right? Kellie had staked so much on the belief that Baby Yaga's plan had succeeded.

    "And... how are YOU taking the change?"

    "I am happy I am able to still serve the goddess, and she has turned an enemy into her strongest ally. The light of the goddess shall spread."

    "Do you remember... dying?"

    The animatronic fox hugged herself. "This is a new body... but I can still what was done to me sometimes."

    Kellie patted her on the shoulder. "If you need professional help, I can help you get some."

    "Thank you. The Goddess and Lady Lilith are waiting. I am looking forward to seeing what miracles you can perform with the Goddess' wisdom and Lady Lilith's cunning."

    "I'm going to need it."

    While the group of friends were uncomfortable about having to sign a disclaimer about trying any of the rides, that they wouldn't sue Circe for any result transformations.

    Frankin insisted that it was, "A standard legal disclaimer theme parks that deal with magical transformation all use."

    Claudia wondered, "How many theme parks that offer magical transformations can there be?"

    "Actually," Mika said,, "My family, I mean, my kitsune family, in my eighteen extra years growing up we visited a couple across creation. Earth isn't the only place in existence after all."

    The after some debate the group had gone with a water ride, the kind you all got into seats, were given lift vests, and were strapped in ... and awaaaaaaaay you go! Down the rapids made to LOOK and FEEL dangerous, but in fact were made so the guests were never in any real danger. There were also very handsome looking otter anthros on standby along the ride just in case however!

    As the group was splashed with the water, their laughs became higher pitched, their expression more wide-eyed and innocent. The seats actually changing size with them as they shrank down, the rapids become less intense as they went down, they also didn't notice as brightly colored fur covered their bodies (though one of them had brightly colored scales instead). Nor as their clothes morphed into brightly colored water proof diapers.

    By the time the ride ended, it was gently drifting down a manufactured stream with painted happy faces on all sides. They saw the pretty colorful spirals on the walls, and their eyes became the same rainbow spiral. They furry toddlers all grinned, not noticing or caring if they wet themselves, the diapers were enchanted to be self-cleaning anyway.

    Finally the ride came to a stop. And a pair of anthro kangaroos unlocked the seats, and led the peaceful furry toddlers into a play room full of child safe toys, crayons, sparkles, and everything else they could possibly need to be happy.

    The ride, and connected daycare center, had in fact been a combined effort by Merlin and Baby Yaga as their own addition to Circe's park. The transformations had the same kind of power as the rings of age. But Merlin's trickster wizard's license did not extend beyond his own 'Spells R Us' shop. As such, the transformation was only permanent for those who had nothing to return to in their lives, giving such people a fresh start.

    The kangaroos' pouches also now contained a pocket universe to place their wards into if the transformed toddlers were in any danger due to outside forces, and the Kangaroos, while genuinely nurturing and loving, had been enchanted to be able to punch out an iron golem in one hit if need be, and able to 'leap' to some secondary safe locations if the park itself became unsafe. The kangaroo anthros themselves willing to protect the furry toddlers with their lives if that was what it came down to.

    All to ensure that Kellie's friends would all have a happy safe time while she was engaging in a hard-knock's training session with the first wife of Adam and a greek goddess. Her friends happily playing patty-cake while she was learning to dodge, block, or redirect fireballs, polymorph spells, and self animated vorpal swords. (Though Lilith had given her word to Merlin that Kellie wouldn't lose any body parts from their lessons).

    The Kellie's friend definitely enjoyed themselves.
