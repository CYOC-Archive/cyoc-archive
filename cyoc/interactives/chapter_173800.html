Once he (when John had been in his old body) had been out in the garden doing something near the fence when this girl had walked out with Bill and climbed into Bill's Porsche. She was drop dead gorgeou and John had found himself tongue-tied. Bill had waved to John and said hello but John hadn’t replied. The girl had just given him a pitying smile.

She was stuck as this redhead and didn’t even know what her name was – unless of course she was the Janelle, that Sara had talked about? Unlikely John thought. Sara didn’t have any friends as young or as good looking as this girl. John assumed she was now in the body of one of Bill’s floozies.

She had to get out of this house. She could not be found here when Bill and Sara returned. But she could now also not return to her own house. She tried to calm down and think of a way out of her predicament.
