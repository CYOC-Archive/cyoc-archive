    Beverly hope that her next day of work wasn't as insane as the last. Sure she was still a catgirl maid, but that transformation was in the past, okay, it was still going on, but at least thanks to the magic involved she skipped the adjustment period and it felt completely natural, in fact, becoming human again felt alien to her.

    And she could finally settle into a routine, sure, Fifi was still working at the Inu Cafe, but at least it was just a job, hopefully.

    Her first customer was a collage guy with a slick haircut and glasses. He selected something OFF THE MENU for her persona. Beverly felt her muscles become those of a body builder, her maid dress was a green camouflage pattern, and a scare formed across one cheek, and her hair become more rough and wild, and she actually gained BROWN FUR, while somehow having a shield tattoo on one arm. Her fangs and claws became pounced.

    "WHAT KINDA GRUB DO YA WANT MAGGOT?!"

    "I think this'll do," He calmly elected from the normal menu for food.

    "Alright little man! So tell me! Do you really think a little twig like you can handle a woman like me?!"

    "Sir. No sir!" He responded like a trained soldier.

    "And you're not gonna unless you man up soldier!" Beverly rolled her eyes as she delivered his order, "Ugh, civvies."

    After he left and Beverly was back to 'default' she said, "That was freaky... but kinda empowering."

    And the next customer was, "MOM!?"

    "Not so loud sweetie," said the little girl in the black dress with a constellation patterns made from diamonds with a hammer at her side. "I'm sorry for being here, I promise I'm not here to spy on you. I just wanted to make sure this place was on the up and up. And... your boss' 'magical trickster' license is up to date... And as long I'm here I thought I'd try the food." Then her mother selected from the menu.

    Beverly felt herself get younger, her maid uniform turned pink with sparkles, and gained more fluff and long ribbons, and a white staff with a yellow star appeared in her hands. With a big heart logo on her chest.

    "Magical Girl Heart-Beat is here to help your day!" You struck a pose with a sideways victory sign. You felt so happy, like nothing could stop you! With the power of love and friendship you knew everything would turn out okay!

    "A BLT on rye please."

    "COMING RIGHT UP! I won't give up no matter!" She said and skipped away with your mother's order. "And I want to say, that you and I have a bond that'll never break!"

    "Yes dear."
