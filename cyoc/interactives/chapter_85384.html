    "Well... Michelle... things should be easy, you never told us you married Selene," Elizabeth gave a smile.

    That only gave you a more confused look and actually raised a more surprised look on your wife's face. Selene looked over to you, raising one eyebrow.

    "Michelle..." your wife looked to you and then her eyes widened, "Elizabeth... you didn't...?"

    "Well me and my friends did," Elizabeth answered, "a man saw us and was enticed by our magic... but he was a good man and he became Michelle, here. We didn't even know he had a wife until he snapped out of our magic's hold on her. Michelle actually wanted very much to come back to you."

    "And you couldn't let her go? Let her become human again and come back to me? Michael would have never betrayed you," your wife argued.

    "You and I both know that a mermaid or siren can't let a human male know our existence," Elizabeth answered, "either they become a siren with us... or we drain them of their life force."

    "But Michael was a good man!" your wife argued.

    "It's the rules, Selene, you know that," Elizabeth answered, "and since Michelle is married to you, her problems are lightened."

That was something you were not prepared for, and it was driving you crazy.
