    The Magic Shop

    Emily and Rick join a new family

    Emily saw a bunch of kids her age in the store. She decided she could pick a girl and use the twinning mode to get the gender she wanted. She settled on a black girl with braids. She took a photo.

    *FLASH*

    She looked down on herself. She was a girl as she'd wanted, but more noticeable than that, she had dark skin. A quick glance in the mirror showed she in fact looked identical as the girl she'd taken a photo of. Of course. Emily figured the twinning mode was just a more precise version of the blend in mode, but no, it makes her into an exact copy, a twin.

    "Found anything yet, Emily?" she heard an older woman ask her.

    She turned around, and it wasn't Rick. It was a chubby black woman. She must be her mother now.

    "Er, not yet mom," Emily replied.

    Where was Rick/Rebecca? She scanned the store for her and quickly found her. She was at the checkout, about to leave. Emily had to act quick, lest they get seperated. She raised the camera and aimed at Rebecca. She could use the twinning mode to make herself a twin of Rebecca, but she didn't really feel like being middle-aged. Instead she could use the 1st setting to make Rick a twin of her.

    *FLASH*

    In Rick's place was a girl looking identical to both herself and the girl she'd taken a photo of. She was, now instead of being about to leave, browsing through the store, still very much unaware.

    The girl Emily had copied approached her. "Emily, what do you think of this one?" she asked, holding up a purple shirt with a glittery decor.

    "Um, it looks good." Emily replied to what was now her triplet sister.

    "Okay!" The girl was happy with Emily's opinion. She then went to their mom and showed the item she'd picked.

    Emily wondered what she should do now. She'd ended up back as a girl as she'd wanted. She was a pretty cute kid too. Though, she'd lost Rick as her mom and gained him as her sister. It was fun watching him, now her, acting completely like a small girl, skipping through the store.

    "Rachel, Emily, you need to hurry up and pick something out!" Their mom ordered.
    Emily guessed she just pick something, and grabbed some shirt her size of the rack. Rachel picked out a pink singlet with a princess on the front. Emily giggled at Rick's girly tastes. They returned to their mom, and then they went to checkout and paid for the items. They then left the clothing store and reentered the mall.

    Emily was walking along her new two sisters and mom. She still had the camera and was still eager to test it out some more. What does she decide to do?
