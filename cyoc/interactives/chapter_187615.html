He'd never have to stop sucking his thumb or using a pacifier. He'd never have to give up drinking from a bottle or sippy cup. And he'd never have to get potty trained and could stay in his diapers until the end of time. All these thoughts made him internally smile at his new future as a baby. Meanwhile Greg had resumed hugging his legs, rocking back and forth as he whimpered.
