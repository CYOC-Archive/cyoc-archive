    Vague requests to A.I.

    added A year ago BM Mental

    Jeff stared at the AI Chat box unsure what to type... if he said the wrong thing the machine which promised it could alter reality would have pretty free reign to change all aspects of his life in so many different ways...

    Jeff zoned out imagining different outcomes from different prompts, the ways it could go wrong, the ways it could go right, the ways it could be neutral...

    "Maybe I should just manually change..." Jeff looked back at the previous page containing 6,247 "packages" by default and another 12,268 he could choose from online... not to mention the user created 48,763 packages he could also review and use...

    "Ugh! How... Why is this so difficult... I can edit my stats directly, have more package options than I know what to do with... and still have no idea how to make my life any better!"

    'Make my life better!' Jeff typed in to express his frustration, and angrily hit enter...

    'Request accepted.
    Common modifications based on your (AGE), (GENDER), (SOCIAL CLASS), (FAMILY DYNAMICS) are:
    Grades
    Athletic Ability
    Social Skills
    Independence
    Appearance
    Age Restricted Experiences
    Prepare for modification.'

    Jeff looked on with growing apprehension unsure how those aspects of his life were going to change. A warmth filled his body and his eyes closed as his room faded away.

    A moment later Jeff awoke on his bed. Sitting up, his room was mostly the same though some of his décor had shifted... at first glance he recognized everything and couldn't pinpoint what was out of place.

    His computer beeped an acknowledgement. "A better life is now yours. Enjoy!" Jeff stared at the popup for a second, then dismissed it, going directly to his profile for review... Again, he had the sense that things were different but couldn't pinpoint what had changed.

    As much as Jeff wanted to mull over his profile he was running late, he had to:
