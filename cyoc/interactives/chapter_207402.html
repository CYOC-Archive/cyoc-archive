    Ethan is a 45-year-old scientist, he has short black hair with some grey areas, his eyes are a hazel-nut brown and his body is pretty athletic. He is also extremely kinky, which means: he enjoys “playtime” with his wife, especially when she plays a pet, like a dog or cat. Ethan kind of started to find the “standard” role- and petplay sessions boring and wanted to “spice” stuff up, so his scientist brain started working, trying to come up with a way to make their “fun time” a little more… interesting.

    One night, Ethan is lying awake in bed, his mind racing, ‘for weeks I have been trying to come up with some idea, of how to make things much more fun for me and my wife,’ he thinks to himself. The man lets out a sigh, he looks over to his right, his wife snoring next to him, he then decides to watch some YouTube videos, to get his mind a little clearer. Ethan scans through the videos, when his eyes suddenly land on a video with the title “Human To Animal TF Compilation.” The man tilts his head, the preview shows what seems to be a woman with dog ears and tail looking very surprised or confused. Ethan shrugs, ‘looks interesting enough,’ he thinks to himself, so with the volume low, he starts the video.

    Most of the clips show women and even men under hypnosis, drinking some sort of potion or a witch or wizard casting a spell. “Really interesting,” Ethan whispers, “yeah… magic could really solve my problem,” the next clip then really gets Ethan’s attention. The clip is done in the first-person perspective, the scene starts with Ethan just seeing a hand out stretched holding some sort of device, which kind of looks like a mobile phone. The image then changes to a full screen which shows the device, Ethan notices that the drawn screen shows the outline of a woman, the buttons of the device have either words such as “hypnosis”, “Age”, “Gender”, “Animal”, and some have symbols such as a Tail, the head of a dog, ears of a donkey and some other symbols. The image changes again, this time Ethan sees a woman, her back turned to the person holding the device, as she seems to be cooking. The next image then shows the person holding the device press a button and a beam of light seems to hit the woman. The next image shows the woman suddenly on her hands and knees, a tail growing from her back, that is when the screen turns black and the clip ends.

    Ethan’s eyes are wide, “THAT device is what I need,” he says, then he thinks for a while, “I wonder,” he mumbles and with that he gets out of bed and quietly walks down to the basement where he has his lab.

    Ethan spends the rest of the night and the following weeks down in his lab, secretly working on his version of a transformation device, he hasn’t told his wife nor his two sons “Branden” who is 16 and “Ben” who is 10, what he is up to. Finally, after almost six months, Ethan successfully designed and created a actual device which is able to transform anyone and anything into anything the mind comes up with. The device has no power limit, which means in the wrong hands it could become extremely dangerous, but Ethan is to excited to try his invention out that he does not care or think about the possible consequences. The scientist stretches and yawns, he looks at his watch, “two in the morning,” he mumbles, “I better get to bed. I can test this thing out tomorrow evening, I wonder what my wife will say.” Ethan gets up and leaves his lab, he turns off the lights but, due to tiredness, he forgets to close and lock the door to his lab, the device lying on the table.
