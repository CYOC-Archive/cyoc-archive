    By the time they got home, Leah had wet herself yet again. Tina sighed, but brought her teenage stepdaughter in for another diaper change. During the change, Leah continued to suckle on her pacifier, seemingly unbothered by wetting herself, wearing a diaper, and being changed. Tina wondered if maybe Leah was already getting used to this.

    After the change, Tina put Leah in front of the TV. She felt bad for her stepdaughter and decided to put away everything she’d purchased on her own. When she checked in on Leah, she was laying on the floor, still sucking her pacifier, thoroughly engrossed in some cartoon for babies. Tina frowned. This was getting weird.

    “Leah, do you want lunch?” Tina called. Leah clapped her hands happily and nodded. “What do you want?”

    “Ba-ba!” Leah chirped. Tina blinked. A bottle? Well, she did have the supplies for that. Tina made up a bottle of baby formula and brought it over to Leah. Leah spat out her pacifier and grabbed the bottle. She guzzled from it hungrily.

    While Leah had her “lunch”, Tina went back to the changing supplies. She had heard a hiss coming from Leah, and knew her stepdaughter would need a fresh diaper. As she gathered what she needed, she noticed the package the pacifier had come in. She picked it up. Her eyes widened.

    “This pacifier has a magical spell on it,” the package read. “Once suckled upon, whoever uses it will behave like a perfect baby, even after the pacifier has been removed. Caution: This effect will take place regardless of age. Adults will mentally become babies if they use this product.” Tina looked over at Leah, who had finished her bottle and was now laying on her back, playing with her toes. The pacifier had made her mentally become a baby.

    Tina took out her phone and pulled up the website for the company that made Leah’s pacifier, hoping to find something to reverse this. However, she soon noticed some products that were more interesting to her than returning Leah back to her normal self. After perusing the website, Tina decided to buy…
