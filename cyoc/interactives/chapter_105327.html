    "Rachel, could you come over here? I've got to show you something!" Jacob exclaims into the telephone.
    "Sure thing, Corny," he hears, a moment later.
    "Thanks," Jacob replies, "And don't call me that!"

    While he's waiting, Jacob sets up a new "SCAN!" profile, and positions the Cronivac unit in an optimal spot. Soon after he finishes, Jacob hears a knock at the door. "Jake! Let me in!" a voice screams from the other side.

    "Coming!" Jacob yells as he runs to the door, unlocks the 20-point security system, and lets his visitor, Rachel, in.

    "So what is this thing you just had to show me?" Rachel inquires.

    "It's a device-"
    "Ok..."
    "You didn't let me finish. It's a device that allows us to change anything, and I mean ANYTHING," Jacob says, "We just need a scan of it."
    "Anything." Rachel skeptically replies.
    "A-NY-TH-ING!" Jacob exclaims, while waving his arms in a sort of semicircular arc.
    "Here, it would be easier to just show you. Uh... Go stand over there {points to a hastily taped X in front of the Chronivac}. I need to get a scan of you first."

    Jacob finishes starting the "SCAN!" program, while Rachel walks to the X.

    "Ok, SCAN!"

    Again, a bright blue flash overcomes Rachel's figure, and just a second later, not a moment more, another dialogue pops up, this time reporting:

    ============================================
    PAGE 1: FREQUENTLY USED OPTIONS
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    NAME: Rachel Smith
    AGE: 27
    SPECIES: Human
    GENDER: Female
    Breast Size: A
    HEIGHT: 5' 6"
    WEIGHT: 120 Lbs
    HAIR COLOR: Raven Black - Dyed blue
    EYE COLOR: Hazel
    OCCUPATION: Store Manager at Central City Fotex Copy n' Paste.
    CLOTHING:
    TORSO: Grey bra, Cornflower Blue tank top
    LEGS: Pink panties, White tracksuit leggings.
    ACCESSORIES: N/A
    ============================================

    "How?" Rachel wonders out loud, looking herself over, "How does this work?!"
    "I know, right?" Jacob replies, "But that isn't even half of it. Watch at this"

    {Rachel}
    Hands > Fox Paws

    "SAVE!"

    "What the..."Rachel exclaims, as her nails start to elongate, becoming black, becoming claws. Her fingers reduce in length, becoming about one joint each. Her pinkies and thumbs recede, pinkies dwindling to nothing, thumbs becoming dewclaws. Her fingers become stiffer, hands becoming paws. The skin on her palms and "fingers" puffs out and become paw-pads. Black, rough paw pads. A light coating of black fur starts to spring up on her skin, growing thicker, fluffier, until Rachel's hands are totally paws.

    "How? This is amazing! Jacob! This! Is! AWESOME!" Rachel blurts out, "Though it might help to make my hands, you know, able to actually manipulate things. Just a suggestion."

    "Ok." Jacob shrugs.

    {Rachel}
    Fox Paws > Anthro Fox Paws.

    "SAVE!"

    Suddenly Rachel's paws start morphing, digits becoming longer, more mobile, 2 jointed, dewclaws becoming thumbs, paws becoming the weirdest mix of hand and paw.

    "Ah. Now that that's taken care of," Rachel says, striping out of her tank top and leggings, "Can you do more? Like make me more fox?"

    "I'll try it..." Jacob replies.

    "What? What's wrong?" Rachel asks.

    "It's just that we don't know the limitations or side effects, or anything about the Cronivac." Jacob muses.

    "Eh, It'll be fine," Rachel says to him, "Try it."

    "Here it goes," Jacob says.

    {Rachel}
    Tail: Yes
    Ears > Fox Ears
    CLOTHING -delay
    {Rachel: Specifications}
    Tail:
    Length: 3' 5"
    Pattern: Fox
    Emergence: Burst
    Duration: 3 seconds
    Ears:
    Duration: 3 seconds

    CLOTHING:
    TORSO: Cloth Breast Covering
    LEGS: Special Design Loincloth
    {What Rachel would be wearing: }

    "SAVE!"


    "My tailbone feels tingly," Rachel wonders out loud.
    "Like bur-OW!" she says, as her spine extends three feet and 5 inches from her tailbone, forming a tail and bursting her panties.
    "That...hurt..." she moans, hands resting on her knees.
    Meanwhile, Rachel's tail has been growing fur, rusty red-orange everywhere except for the tip, which is a creamy white.

    Rachel's ears also change, becoming furry fox ears and moving to the top of her head.

    Finally, a loincloth and bra cloth spontaneously appear on her body.

    "Hm. Interesting choice in clothing, Corncob."
    "I thought it would be nice for a vixen like you."
    "Ok. What next?"
