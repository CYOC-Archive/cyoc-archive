    "Well, I'm sorry, but you need to make sure your wishes are specific," said the genie. "Keep that in mind for your future wishes."

    Nate grumbled, "Fine, you want specific? I wish that Teresa Klotz was not literally a Playboy centerfold, but was an actual, living foxy female with a body of the type that could get her picked to be a Playboy centerfold, with an attractive face, a thin but curvy body, big breasts, and she prefers to wear skimpy clothes."

    The genie made a gesture, and smoke started to billow out of the centerfold in Nate's hand. He dropped it as Teresa began to emerge, now dressed in high-heeled sandals, a miniskirt, and a midriff-baring shirt with a low-cut V-neck collar.

    Nate could see the fat on her body melting away as her facial features shifted. Acne and stubble were replaced by smooth skin. The hair on her head was lengthening and gaining a glossy shine. Best of all, from Nate's point of view, her shirt was being stretched outward as her breasts swelled.

    Suddenly, Nate noticed her shirt was swelling in four more places, and the razor stubble was back on Teresa's legs...and her arms...and her stomach...and her face? Hair was sprouting all over her body -- no, actually, it was fur, white and reddish-orange. Her ears were lengthening, and somehow traveling up the side of her head. Her nose and mouth were stretching out into a muzzle as her eyes changed from elliptical to round. Nate could see a fluffy tail swishing back and forth behind her. Teresa had become a fox- woman.

    "This is not what I had in mind, either," complained Nate.

    "What do you mean?" asked the confused genie. "You said you wanted an actual, living foxy female. Skimpy clothes, thin but curvy, big breasts -- she's got six big breasts, in fact."

    "What's going on, Nate?" asked Teresa.

    "Teresa, I'm sorry I screwed things up," he said. "I found this genie, and I made a wish --"

    The genie interrupted. "Before you go on, let me point out that your wish changed the fabric of reality itself, as all wishes do, and so this is normal for Teresa."

    "It is?" asked Nate. He'd never been a furry fan, but now that there was one standing in front of him, he couldn't help but get a little turned on. The new Teresa did have a gorgeous body under the fur, and those boobs did seem like they'd be fun to play with...
