    The day was abnormally warm for an autumn afternoon. There were almost no people in the water, mostly because people didn’t expect to be such a warm day after all – the life guards were relaxed at their seats enjoying the sun bath, when the sun was eclipsed.

    No there was no eclipse; it was just the shadow of TWO huge behemoths walking towards the lake! Most of the life-guards were have heard about the giant 9 year old kid named Ryan, an ebony muscle titan who was supposed to be the Earth’s strongest man – but there were two of them! This other kid was every bit as big and muscle as Ryan, and they could pass by twins, if it weren’t by his different skin complexion and hair shade, but when it comes to muscle they were absolutely massive and ripped.

    Each giant carried one boy in one shoulder, and they talked happily amongst themselves.

    “Hey Peter, how do you feel being a giant?” Allen asked still marveled at the power of Peter’s grown muscles.

    “It’s AMAZING!” Peter flexed his biceps and placed his friend sat on them – “I feel I could do whatever I want!”

    “Welcome to the club, big brother! I am so glad I can call you big brother once more!” Ryan commented also flexing his biceps and making Ty sit on it.

    The life-guards watched in awe as the impressive duo reached their posts; actually they were so tall they were looking down to them, even at the high cabins.

    “Good morning, guys! How is the water? Too cold?” Peter asked with his usual sympathetic tone.

    Jason, the 18 year old life-guard spoke first – “Y-yeah, it can be a little chilly for most of us!”

    “There’s no problem my big brother Pete can’t solve!” the ebony colossus said smiling.

    “We could do some warm up before getting into the water!” Peter suggested blinking to Ryan who smiled right away!

     

    When the life-guards opened their eyes once more they were looking at two HUGE platforms filled with tons and tons of metal plates, and each one of the giants had at least 50 plates on each hand, and they were curling the amazing load like it was nothing.

    “Peter, only 3000 pounds? Come on! You can do better!”

    “Okay, okay!” Peter focused and then the plates turned into huge containers, each one surely over 40’ high and over 200’ long, and yet they lifted them containers like they were mere dumbbells.

    For over one hour the impressive muscle titans worked on their muscles, each time flexing, squeezing and posing to their audience.

    “So, Jason, who you think is the biggest?” Ty asked to his already Life-guard friend

    “Gee, it’s impossible to say! They’re so fucking massive!” Greg, the other life-guard replied.

    Suddenly, Ryan dropped his weights down and rushed into Peter, grabbing him on his arms and running towards the water, and there he threw Peter at least 50 feet into the water – but Peter was smart enough to float and stop right before he splashed into the chilly water.

    “It won’t be that easy bro!” Peter said and he flew right into Ryan’s direction who son was caught and the duo flew over 100 feet above the water.

    “You won’t drop me will you?” Ryan asked, a little nervous.

    “Yeah, and you would just fly, you silly!” Peter said as he hugged Ryan even tighter, flying right into the water, splashing loudly into the lake.

    Right after that, the giant brothers were splashing huge amounts of water into each other, laughing and playing like two normal brothers, there were tons and tons of muscle having fun at the once calm waters of the lake, and fortunately there was nobody in the water, because the waves were pretty strong for those who are not around 20’ tall.

    The brothers resumed to their wrestling play, which meant, soon they were throwing and lifting each other around like they were just normal kids.

    Finally, they were tired, but their amazing strength was equal, their muscles were so big, they have actually grown more massive and ripped with all the exercise they had done.

    Meanwhile the audience applauded – Ty, Allen, Jason and Greg cheered for their muscle titans who laughed and flexed for them with pleasure!

    “WOW! Peter you’re so strong! I never knew you knew so many movements!” Ryan said as they returned to the beach.

    “Thanks bro, I learned a few things, I can teach you later, but you are too strong too!”

    “Let’s face it! We are exactly as powerful!!” Ryan laughed and they hugged tightly, their massive muscles could barely touch each other. Peter flexed his chest and Ryan flexed his abs, then they exchanged positions and finished with a double front double biceps!

    “WOW!” it was al the four teens could say at the sight of the muscle brothers. They approached the other boys and soon they were sharing a real banquet.

    “The food is delicious!” Peter said with his mouth full.

    “Yeah! Totally!” Ryan added

    “I think you and Ryan are not just equally powerful, Peter, you’re equally famished!” Allen teased as the brothers “fought” for the last cake; each one had eaten over 12 whole cakes each.

    “What are we gonna do now?” Peter asked Ty with his eyes full of joy!
