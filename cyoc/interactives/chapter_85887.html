    Tanya the Fairy

    added 9 years ago BM S

    Tanya looked herself over, and smiled.

    She then spoke:

    "I was scared at first,

    but now I see,

    that I make such a cute,

    adorable fairy!

    My pointy ears,

    my flowery dress,

    my butterfly wings,

    you're truly the best!

    I'm a fairy now,

    and it's all thanks to you.

    My dear Tricia, can you please,

    be a fairy too?"

    Tricia couldn't believe it! Not only did Tanya love being a fairy, but she spoke in rhyme, and she wanted Tricia to join her!

    "Oh Tanya," she smiled "how can I say no?" so she used the remote to make herself a fairy with pointy ears, a flower dress, and butterfly wings.

    The remote became small enough for them to use. They looked at eachother, and smiled.

    Tricia: Tanya, my friend,

    this is grand!

    Let us go hand-in-hand.

    We'll make fairies across the land.

    Tanya: I'll help you out,

    for better or worse,

    but who shall we make

    a fairy first?
