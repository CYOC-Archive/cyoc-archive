    CoSondra had been coming on to Mr. Mendes since he moved in. Even if he wasn't gay, CoSondra would not have been his type. Marco put the cat down and
    said, "Excuse me", and went to the bedroom to change into clothes he kept at the house.

    CoSondra turned to Ric and Josh, "So where is Francisco keeping himself...seems his new pet tom-cat tried to nail poor Ginger today." Ginger let out a snarl. The
    boys just looked at CoSondra. They had always heard she too had practiced some magic...now it seemed that she also talked to Ginger the cat. "You see boys",
    CoSondra continued, "I can talk to Ginger..the rumors are true...she's my familiar."

    Ric spoke up, "Sorry CoSondra, but Mr. Mendes went out for his jog and we found the cat this morning and let him out...we didn't mean to hurt Ginger." Ric was
    hoping she would leave after the explanation. "Oh boys," CoSondra started, "Ginger's fine...I had her fixed...Frankly I thought you two were at that old wizard's
    estate sale and that darling Francisco came home a bit ago!?...OOOO, look, you have a book of transformation...'fess up boys...did you transform your gaurdian?"


    Ric took a gulp, she knew too much. Ginger jumped up on CoSondra's shoulder as she picked up the book. Ric tried to deny most of what she had guessed. He
    said they found the cat on their way back from the estate sale and that the book was a fake. She didn't buy it. "Boys, I know about this book...I've seen it work."
    Then CoSondra picked up Mr. Mendes, "And such a shame...after all this time I finally get to see sweet Francisco naked...only he has black fur and is on four
    paws...tsk tsk." Francisco hissed at CoSondra.

    "Don't worry boys, the book is safe. Even if I did take it, it won't work outside this house without the owner's permission...you boys being the owners. But my
    brother, the antique dealer, would love to have this book."

    Marco walked out at that time in only his shorts and a tank top, "Well CoSondra, I see you're putting a little pressure on the boys." He put his hand out, "How

    CoSondra did not look angy, just mischeivious, "Oh sweetie, don't monkey with me...just because I can't take doesn;t mean it won't work in this house." With that

    Marco's ears started to get bigger, his arms got longer, his aarm pit hair started to spread...all of his hair started to spread. His feet looked more like hands and he
