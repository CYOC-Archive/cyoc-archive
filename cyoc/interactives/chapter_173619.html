    You Bolt And Make A Run For It

    Your mind raced along with your heartbeat as you drew closer and closer to damnation. You looked back at the former humans that filled the yard and the fields beyond and it made you feel sick to the core. You thought about your girlfriend Jessica and how no-one would know what happened to either of you if your transformation became permanent.

    As you thought more about what Vicky and the attendants had put you and Jessica through, you felt a building wave of hatred and anger creeping into your mind. The resulting rush of adrenaline caused your thoughts to clear somewhat as your human survival instincts began to reawaken. You started to panic as you returned to your senses. You didn’t want to be stuck as a horse forever. You had to do something now!

    As you hastily tried to formulate an escape plan, it soon became clear that you weren’t the only one that wanted to avoid this cruel fate. You looked back when you heard a loud series of whinnies coming from behind you. You looked back to see Samantha rearing-up as she fought desperately to break free from her handler.

    As the angry black horse continued to resist, you felt Vicky’s grip on your lead rope loosen as she too seemed to be distracted by the commotion. You knew this was it. You wouldn’t get another opportunity to escape after this. You had to act now!

    With a huge whinny of defiance you suddenly yanked hard on the slack lead rope that Vicky was holding. As you had planned she was caught off balance and taken completely by surprise, which caused the rope to slip through her fingers as she crashed to the floor.

    Hearing Vicky’s cry of pain and subsequent flurry of curses made you feel satisfied as you started to bolt away from her. Getting some form of payback on the vile woman made you feel good inside. But you didn’t let this distract you as you headed towards the Stables’ outer perimeter.

    As you galloped along you couldn’t help noticing that all of the nearby horses were watching your antics closely. You also spotted that a number of nearby attendants were having problems controlling their own charges as they too seemed to want to break free. As you noticed this you figured that you could use this commotion to your advantage.

    With this in mind you headed for a nearby group of six horses that looked like they too were destined to be mated in the near future. Indeed you recognised three of them as the young males that you had spent the previous night with. You were pleased to see that they too were busy fighting back against their captors.

    You decided to give the young studs a hand, after deciding that the resulting chaos would help you to escape. With this in mind you charged and headbutted one of the attendants who had his back turned, before you turned to give a hard kick to another who tried to restrain you. This had the desired effect as the testosterone-fuelled males overpowered the other stablehands and started to gallop in all directions.

    Just as planned, chaos started to erupt around you as startled attendants and horses began to panic from the commotion. You noticed that a number of other horses had also taken this opportunity to enact their own escape plans, which only added to the confusion as they too tried to run from this living hell they found themselves in..

    After a few more minutes spent dodging rampaging horses and fighting off any attendants in your way, you found yourself nearing a gate in the Stable’s perimeter fence. In all the confusion the gate had been left slightly open. A couple of attendants were struggling to cut off this escape route, but they were currently preoccupied battling a large black stallion.

    Seeing this as your opportunity you charged at the attendants guarding the gate. After sensing that you weren’t going to stop, the two attendants acted smartly by diving out of the way. This gave you and the stallion a clear exit which you soon took full advantage of.

    You split up from the stallion as you headed for the cover of a heavily wooded area that lay nearby. Soon the chaos in the Stables grew quiet as you fled deeper into the trees. After a few minutes you were completely alone in the forest, with perhaps an hour or two left until you returned to being human.

    You decided to head away from the Stables as your body and mind continued to surge with adrenaline. Now you tried to focus on avoiding capture, at least until the Stable’s magic wore off...
