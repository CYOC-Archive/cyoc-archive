    Author’s Note: I was recently going through some old interactive TG stories from various authors and saw a bunch of different prompts that really inspired me. I thought that perhaps it would be fun to revive/continue some of them. Credit for the ideas and prompts belongs to those who originally came up with them at the time, such as vanillathunder, BostonRocko,onlyonekey, and others.

    A few of these are also (relatively) original ideas I decided to include in the list.

    Cheaters Never Prosper: After going home with a girl from a club, you discover she has a boyfriend and the only way to avoid his wrath is to pretend that you're one of the girl's girlfriends.

    The Strange Case of Mr. Jake L and Ms. Heidi- You are cursed to turn into a girl at night, with interesting results.

    Disney Magic - You go to Disney World with your little sister, and end up becoming something she would love.

    Fill-In Babysitter: When you are asked to babysit a neighbor's daughter, increasingly bizarre circumstances lead you to end the night dressed a girl.

    The Understudy: Through some error, you end up the understudy for a female role in the school play. But when the lead actress becomes ill, it's up to her understudy, (you), to take her place.

    The Night Job: When a young hotshot banker and his buddies visit a strip club one night, he ends up aggravating one of the dancers whom he doesn’t know is secretly a witch. As punishment she places a curse on him which transforms him into a sexy stripper every night and he is forced to work at the club every night if he ever wants her to remove the curse.

    Karma(this one didn’t have a title per se, so this is a fill-in one): A wife of a chauvinistic habitual cheater finally has had enough of his shit and decides to teach him a lesson. What better way than to turn him into the exact kind of woman he's always wanted. Will he learn his lesson? His, or rather her wife is not going to make it easy for him.

    The Mansion(Original): Matt and his friends win a trip to a Playboy Bunny esque mansion. Little do they know that all of the beautiful women there used to be guys….

    Day Shift(Original): Kevin’s girlfriend is tired of him sitting around all day doing nothing and decides to get him a job. She decides if he likes going to Hooters to flirt with the waitresses so much, he’d fit right in as one of the girls…..

    Back 2 Skewl(Original): Hank’s girlfriend has trust issues. And control issues, for that matter. Hank knew about all that. But when they break up, he finds out one thing he didn’t know: she’s a witch. And she’s decided he’s better off back in high school as a ditzy cheerleader.

    Sofia(Original): Sean looks down on the secretaries at his workplace. When he insults the wrong woman, she decides he should spend some time in their heels.

    The Evil Wizard(Original): Aran is the court wizard in the kingdom of Turos. He’s also the power behind the throne, easily manipulating the ruling family. Will the young guard Szen uncover the evidence of his treachery, or end up as just another vapid servant girl?
