Not even the specific events at the pride's picnic was even on Martha's mind at the moment. It was Eric and how fast and how serious his relationship with Julie Stovall was becoming. The weremer might be aware of Eric being her one true love and being very willing to wait for him, but both were fast approaching a point where waiting might not be an option for either. And the fact that true love was pushing that issue.

For a few moments they were silent, but Martha's worries couldn't be held within her any longer, especially with William saying that Eric and Julie had talked to each other as though they had been married for a few years. She was glad to see Eric coming out of his shell, but much of it seemed to be happening too fast.

    "It's good that it'll be for love... but..." Martha said slowly, "would be wrong to let it happen? Melissa told me that this time is approaching fast... that their love is so strong that it'll happen before the end of the year."

That's something we couldn't have expected."
