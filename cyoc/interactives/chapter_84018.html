    "Great! The park is doing more business every year! We get a lot more beaver and moosemorphs than we did down here. Guess its a Canadian thing, eh?"

    "No, thank Ninisinna! I have a good friend on the legal staff there--a foxmorph named Mike--and we went through it and couldn't find anything. It looks like the rot was limited to here."
