    Together Breakfast

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 5 years ago BM O

    Luke and Suzie walked downstairs, and went into the dining room, where they saw their dad--and their's parents friends, and Luke's friends, and each was conjoined with someone else!

    Luke was worried that Suzie would freak out seeing bodies with at least two heads, until he remembered that she was still attached to him. Luke remembered what had happened yesterday, that he, his dad, and their friends wanted to be able to stay conjoined with at least one other person without having to split up eventually. Clearly, the wish had come true, and it seemed that they were still able to split and fuse--at least, through the suit, or through Ethan and Luke's power if anyone wanted more than two heads. But that was yesterday: the wish could have changed the situation when it happened at some point during the night. It was only the first day of Christmas break, but someone's wish had come true early: the question was, who wished it?

    Luke decided to shrug it off and join his family and friends for breakfast. The two sat down between Mark-Max-Zack and Brian-Nick. Leo-Donnie-Raph-Mikey were on the other side of the table, eating and talking with the others. Kevin-Allan-Dean were next to the quadruplets, eating their breakfasts and not saying a word. Then there were the dads. On one body were the heads of Luke's father Ethan, Mark-Max-Zack's father Scott, and Brian-Nick's father Peter. On another was Kevin's father George, Allan and Dean's father Andrew, and the quad's father Sam. Three of the gang's mothers were present on one body: Kevin's mother Martha, Allan and Dean's mother Rachel, and the quad's father Ida. Then, another body walked in from the kitchen: it was Luke's mother Alisa, Mark-Max-Zack's mother Sharon, and Brian-Nick's mother Kate.

    "Suzie," said Alisa, "you know how we feel about you being attached to your brother."

    "But it's not fair!" cried the young girl. "I don't have a sister to be with!"

    "We know, sweetie," Rachel said. "But it would be too--unusual for a boy and girl to be sharing a body."

    After hearing this, Luke could tell that his little sister wanted to be alone for a while, and the look she gave him proved it. Luke decided to see if his power was still there, so he focused, and managed to split himself and Suzie back into two bodies. Suzi ran off to go hide in her room only a few seconds after this was done.

    After breakfast, Martha, Rachel, and Ida decided to go talk to Suzie while Alisa, Sharon, and Kate asked Ethan and Luke to separate them so they go out shopping. The two did so, and the three women left to get their purses. Meanwhile, most of the dads decided to go across the street to Scott's house for a few drinks in the man-cave in his basement.

    "Sounds great, but Ethan and I want to talk with the boys about something," Scott replied. The way he was looking at the teens and Ethan let them know EXACTLY what he was talking about, without letting the others know what. Clearly the rest of the family/families still did not know about any of this--at least, other than the fusing.

    The four other dads decided to let Ethan and Scott have their word with their sons, so Ethan separated Peter from their body and joined him into George, Andrew, and Sam's body. The four-headed dad(s) left to go across the street, and as they did, the moms came back downstairs--separated--to take Suzie for a walk. Once the moms left, this left six bodies in the house: Ethan and Scott, Brian and Nick, Leo-Donnie-Raph-Mikey, Mark-Max-Zack, and Luke. Despite Luke wanting to be conjoined with one of the others, he knew that there were urgent matters to deal with.
