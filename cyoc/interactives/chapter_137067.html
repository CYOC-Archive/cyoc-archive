    As Ty laughed on the bench, an Auguste clown with an orange hat approached him from behind.
    You should try being a clown too *hyuck*. He said as he slipped a necklace around Ty’s neck.
    Ty jumped off the bench and attempted to remove the necklace but it was to late as it popped and quickly grew a purple one piece suit with multicoloured patches over his body. He looked down and let out a large laugh before closing his mouth out of surprise and embarrassment. His feet grew to a size 30 and were enveloped in large red clown shoes. His hands grew large white gloves as his 2nd and middle finger fused together. His hair popped up into a big rainbow Afro as his skin turned Snow White. Finally he felt a pop and the smell cotton candy as a large red nose appeared wher his old nose used to be. He tried removing his nose but only resulted in honking it causing a loud honk to eminate from his nose all whilst a rush of joy swept through his body as he laughed. He honked his nose again and again before realising what he was doing. He fished through his pockets looking for something to help him. He found lots of clown props from magic kits to juggling balls to a horn before he found a full length mirror? He didn’t question his discoveries, he only cared finding out what happened. He looked at himself before putting his head in his hands hoping it would go away but he looked back up and his clown costume was still there. He buried his face in his hands once again only to find 2 buttons on his right palm. One was labelled with an E and the other was labelled with an N. Knowing that he couldn’t fix what had happened yet, he clicked the button
    He looked into the mirror to see if anything had changed only to realise that his eyes had turned into purple and green spirals and were spinning almost hypnotically.
    He panicked at this new site and fell over his shoes falling on his nose which then honked. Be got up and bumped into the clown that had transformed him. Ty looked at him to apologise for falling into him. The clown looked at Ty, No probl... He was stopped mid sentence as his eyes began to spiral like Ty’s. Ty was going to run away but decided to test whether his eyes did what he thought they could do
    Jump. Tyler said to the clown standing there in trance. The clown jumped. Sit Ty exclaimed with a smirk on his face. The clown immediately sat down. Pie me *hyuck* Ty said with a massive smile on his face, but then the smile faded, why did I say tha AHHHHH. Just as he started deliberating what was happening, the clown threw a pie in his face. Ty couldn’t help but laugh hysterically. Then he stopped,what is happening to me, Why would I want him to pie and why did I start laughing like that. BECAUSE YOUR A CLOWN OF COURSE *hyuck* said a voice
    Who said that. He said with a stutter
    He looked around but everyone else was busy clowning around
    I’m in your head silly. The voice said
    Who are you! Ty demanded
    I’m your inner clown, I’m the one who makes you act like a clown against your will once your transformed although since you were the one who wished jewellery to transform people to clowns *hyuck* you seem to be able to resist me a little more
