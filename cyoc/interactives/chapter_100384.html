    I grab the wand and hop out of my car a block away from her house. No reason to make her suspicious if she happens to see me. I wish myself invisible and walk up to her house and ring the doorbell. If her parent's schedule hadn't changed, she'd be the only one home, but I wanted to double check before starting.

    Sure enough Lindsey opened he door. "Hello?" She called as I slipped past her into the house, "Anyone there?" Probably assuming it was a prank, she closed the door in a huff and headed back upstairs to her room. I was tempted to just follow her immediately, but I needed to check that no one else was home, just to be safe. After checking through the house, I waved the wand and made another wish that no one in Lindsey's family would come home until I left the house.

    With all that settled, I snuck upstairs to Lindsey's room. Lindsey was lying on her bed reading a book, and I took the chance to look her over. 17 years old, the same age as me, she still had a relatively lanky, boyish frame. To me though, that only combined with her glasses to make her endearing. She wasn't exactly "beautiful", but she was cute in her own way. Her looks weren't why I liked her though, she was funny, intelligent, and most of all, kind, or so I had thought. I wasn't willing to giver her that label any more after her betrayal.

    Deciding I had reminisced long enough, I flicked the wand and revealed myself in a puff of smoke and crack of thunder, making the door slam and lock in the process. Yea, it was a little theatrical, so what? Lindsey yelped as this happened, almost falling off her bed. "Ted?" She asked frightfully. I smiled in response, "Hi Lindsey".
