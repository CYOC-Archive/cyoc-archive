    Considering all that was going on between the A-BC and the UFC-A, it was decided that it would be easier for Animalia Ambassadoria to cover them by splitting their attention. Samson was kinda wanting to look at the A-BC, though he wouldn't be partaking in it himself. Not that he couldn't compete, but he ought to give the other competitors a fairer chance since he already had a medal for bodybuilding.

    Abby and Samson looked at the competitors in the heavier classes. "Hmm, they've certainly been able to bulk up really well," commented Samson.

    "Well, the Animalia virus doesn't really leave people scrawny," said Abby.

    "True, but it doesn't completely hone them," said Samson, "These people have to finish honing their bodies the rest of the way."

    "There is a noticeable difference," said Abby. She had been able to watch her boyfriend's progress as he brought his muscles into sharp details. Samson's definition hasn't really faded that much, partially because of his various farm duties. Abby wouldn't have considered herself to have a taste in bodybuilders before, but she did like the way Samson bulged.

    "Getting them to this state is not easy," said Samson, "Especially for the big ones."

    "Why them?" asked Abby.

    "Because they're so much stronger that they need bigger weights in order to really push themselves," said Samson, "And getting them really heavy weights isn't easy."

    "Good point," said Abby, "I guess it would be harder for an elephant to push himself to the edge than a dog."

    Jessica and Roger were looking at the smaller classes of bodybuilders. "You know, you don't really see much of rabbits at these things," said Jessica.

    "Well, I guess some people never consider rabbits to be the strong types," said Roger, "Usually we're just the runners."

    "There's more to rabbits than just speed," said Jessica, "You saw how big that Rockwall guy got. Bunnies could be big bodybuilders if they want to."

    "Do you want to be one?" asked Roger.

    "No, not really," said Jessica, "But I'm pointing out that we rabbits could."

    "But there are plenty of other species one wouldn't consider the bodybuilding type," said Roger, indicating a few cats.

    "Animalia's filled with all sorts," said Jessica with a shrug.
