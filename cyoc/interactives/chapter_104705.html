    It looks like a frat party. There are several people and long tables of food with kegs of beer. Andrew isn't sure if it's an actual frat party or not. He and the game are on a table currently being ignored.

    Upon closer look, Andrew discovers it's not just one board game. He stands on a checkerboard and sees a backgammon board to his right. There are a few other boards including life, sorry, chinese checkers, aggravation, risk, stratego, and trouble. All the necessary pieces are present except several of the pieces have been replaced by humans. One of the knights is an anthro human with horse features. He is naked except for horse accessories like a bridle and saddle. The knight says, "Looks like the claw grabbed another victim."
