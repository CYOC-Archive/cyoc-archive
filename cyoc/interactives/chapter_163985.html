    "What time is it? Like 8PM? Just about?" Jake asked. He didn't even look away from the screen and just kept grinding for the item he oh so desperately wanted.

    "It's... 7:30, if we order something now we can probably get it before 8." James said. "How does pizza sound?" He asked.

    "Pizza sounds good man." Jake said. He continued to tap away at the controller.

    "Ok, got anything you're hankering for?" James asked.

    "Nah, just get me a pepperoni and I should be fine." Jake said.

    "You're boring when it comes to pizza. Fine, you're getting a pepperoni." James said.

    "Thanks dude." Jake said. James dialed the local pizza place and placed his order. Three extra large pepperoni pizzas. Jake didn't seem to care about how much James just ordered, he just continued to tap away at the controller without a worry in the world. James took this time to look through the Chronivac, but there was a knock at the door before he could change anything.

    James paid the delivery boy and collected his pizzas. He set two on the counter and the third next to where Jake was gaming. Jake started eating his pizza, one slice after another. Sure it was a bit slow, but it was at a constant speed. James could only watch as he stuffed his face. He barely made it through one slice before Jake finished his entire pizza. A loud growl filled the room. Jake was still hungry.

    "Is there, uh, anymore?" Jake asked.

    "We've got a whole bunch left. Pause your game and come get it." James said. Jake got out of his beanbag chair and walked to the counter, his long tail dragged behind him. James looked at the T-rex man's belly and noticed that it hasn't really changed at all. Jake looked at the pizza only missing once slice then back at the empty box right next to the beanbag.

    "Did I really eat that much in the time it took you to eat one slice?" Jake asked.

    "Yeah, maybe it's because you transformed, your body is trying to make up for lost energy." James said. He hoped that Jake wouldn't figure what he had done out.

    "Maybe..." Jake said grabbing another slice of pizza. He opened op his scaly maw and nearly ate the entire slice in one bite. James couldn't help but stare. "You ok dude?" Jake asked.

    "You almost ate that thing whole, even after you just ate an entire pizza." James said.

    "What can I say, the transformation made me hungry." Jake said grabbing another slice and this time swallowed it whole.

    Jake kept up that speed until there was no pizza left. James didn't even have to feed him any. James wondered how Jake's stomach could even hold that much food without bursting. Jake's belly looked ever so softer, just barely, but it was softer. James made a mental note that Jake could stuff his face beyond that of any human, he'd just have to figure out what Jake's limit is later.

    "Well, I'm off to bed." Jake said. He turned off the Xbox and cleaned up the pizza boxes.

    "Well good luck with that." James said.

    "Yeah, yeah, goodnight." Jake said closing his door. James thought of what to do next.
