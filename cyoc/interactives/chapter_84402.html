    Jack smiled and eagerly wandered downstairs, then out into his yard. The two werewolves he'd watched fuck and transform were still in the street, howling loudly and drawing more people to them, all of whom eagerly disrobed and changed, sometimes finding partners to pleasure, sometimes pleasuring themselves. A couple of times, the wolves looked his way, and Jack realized he had only wished for immunity, not safety. "I wish I would never be in any physical danger from the werewolves," Jack said, and the werewolves kept their distance.

    He watched every change, every bit of socialization between the wolves, making eager notes, regretting only that he'd not seen a change up close. The distance had made it very hard to keep track of everything, though it was clear that the disease he'd created had kept to the parameters he'd wished for. As the werewolves loped off (roughly seven of them, all told) he debated following them, then paused.

    "I wish the two college girls who lived next door had been infected and would let me watch them change," Jack said rather absentmindedly. He didn't even notice that he'd done it, nor did he notice the pulse go out from his ring. A moment or two later, two girls emerged from the duplex next door and wandered over to where he stood.

    "Hi.." the first said. She had long blonde hair and was dressed in loose fitting clothing-a long skirt that hung to her feet and a thrift store t-shirt. "Jack, right?" she asked. She looked mildly confused, and her attention went from Jack, then up at the night sky and the moon, then back to Jack, then over to her roommate.

    "Uhh..yeah," Jack said, smiling confidently as he realized what happened. "Kimberly, right?" The blonde nodded, then gestured to her friend. "And this is Tabby. I'm not sure if you've met or not."

    Tabby was dressed in boy shorts and a sports bra. She had rather short brown hair, cut tightly against the back of her neck; it didn't even go past her ears. She smiled at Jack, then at Kim in a knowing way. She glanced at the notes in Jack's hands. "You some sort of scientist?" she asked. Jack nodded in response. "You see what happened a few minutes ago?" Jack nodded again.

    Tabby's eyes glinted in the moonlight, and a shudder went through her. "I think you're going to see it again..." She smiled playfully at both of them, and sweat shone on her skin.

    "I feel..I feel weird.." Kim said softly, rubbing her temples. Her ears seemed to be poking through her hair now, and she had the same mild gloss of sweat on her skin, or at least what skin he could see.
