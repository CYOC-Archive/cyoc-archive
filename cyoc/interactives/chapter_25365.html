Yet, it could save your butt here. However, if you can get to your own book first and recover from the spell, then you could win.

You turn to where the dragon was, and there is another mouse there, and you are unable to see where the man went, but he is probaly somewhere around where his clothing hit the ground.

    You've just slain a dragon. True it was a mouse at the time, but that still probaly makes you some kind of hero.
