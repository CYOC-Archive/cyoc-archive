    Victory!

    Rebeca heard a rustling in the bushes. She, Will, and Logan stood up. Out of the forest came the players they
    had sent our after the Marauding Trio. They hadn't noticed until now, but Rebeca had a gold question mark
    above her head. The players came over and each turned in the quest. As they watched this happen, Rebeca
    noticed that all of them took the trinket. The party left soon after getting their rewards.

    "Are they gone?" Will asked.

    "Yep." Logan said.

    The three orphans began celebrating. They had completed the challenge! As they danced around, they were
    transported back to the white hall. Gabe and Cal were surprised to have returned, but clearly understood what
    happened.

    "You did it?" Cal asked.

    "Nope." Will replied.

    "What do you mean? Did we fail?" Gabe asked, suddenly very worried.

    "Relax, Will is just messing with you. We got a bunch of other people to do the dirty work for us." Logan
    said.

    "Yes, you did. I'm not sure if I should punish you or congratulate you. Still, you technically completed the
    challenge." Said the Sprite, who had just come in. As he entered, the challengers were returned to their
    original forms.

    "Alright, it's good to be back." Said Will.

    "You said it." Rebeca said.

    "Hell yeah!" Shouted Cal.

    "Yes yes, you're all glad to be back in your normal bodies. As happy as I am for you, and I legitimately am,
    we need to keep going." The Sprite said. "So, who's ready to begin?"
