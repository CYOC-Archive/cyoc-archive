    You see various animal people passing by, occasionally accompanied by a guard. A distressed catgirl comes by, with a guard by her side. She is obviously trying to escape, and you quickly whisper to her:"Hey, if I help you will you help me?", vaguely hinting at the guard. In the passing-by, she quickly nods at you, and you know what to do. With a bit of careful growth manipulation and a lot of effort, you make one of your roots grow out of the ground and around the guard's leg, tripping him flat on his face. The catgirl quickly jumps up, and lands a footpaw straight on the guard's body, knocking him out for a long time. She stands over his body and snarls a bit, "that'll teach you how to treat a lady." She then turns to you. "Now, how can I help you?" You explain to her you want to get out of here by any means possible, even if it means moving around in a flowerpot. "Don't we all", she says, "I think I might know something, but all the stuff is locked up in guarded storage rooms and I won't be able to get into one of those alone. We're gonna need help, and there's plenty of people passing through here...no matter the cost, we're gonna tear this place down. This bastard is gonna pay for what's done to me." She snarls and looks at herself, tail swishing in frustration. "Anyway, let's get mr. busybody here out of the way before anyone notices." She dumps the body in the bushes and the two of you start thinking about the best course of action...
