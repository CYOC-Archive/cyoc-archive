    Meet Rebecca Clarkson, a 24 year old English teacher with short pearl blonde hair and a beautiful pair of blue eyes. She has light freckles around her cheeks and nose, hardly noticeable however. She has a rather decent height of around 5'8" and a skinny figure. She has a rather light bust around a 32CC and a particularly perky rear that doesn't stick out a lot at all. Overall, she is a beautiful woman.

    Rebecca is extremely intelligent and understanding - even the majority of her peers think so. Unfortunately only a minority of them are captivated by her radiant features. So she is pretty much treated like dirt. As a teacher in a secondary school, she is hardly given any respect from the students. And the majority of other teachers resent her because of her prettiness and charm.

    Rebecca knew today was going to be like any other day. She was going to have to endure it all over again.

    Rebecca's sister Katie woke her up as she stayed asleep past her alarm once again. Katie was taller than her by an inch at 5'9". She had the same bra size and basically wore the same size clothes. The only difference between the two was that Katie had purple dyed hair that she kept in a sort of short choppy bob cut. She had green eyes as opposed to blue. Coming from a Welsh background meant they both retained the accent.

    "Hey! Hey, Rebecca! Wake up, will ya!?! You're gonna be late for work again," exclaimed Katie.

    "Aghhhh, " grunted Rebecca.

    "Hey, come on now ... I know its painful but its not going to last forever. You've been doing this for years and your contract at that school is nearly over."

    "..."

    "Look - all you have to do is go in, do your job well just like you always do and come home relieved that you're one day closer to freedom. And hey - you get to watch movies with me all night. Well, until you fall asleep half way through one, of course."

    Rebecca smiled at this and rolled over.

    "Alright, I'm up now. You can toddle off now. Go make me toast or something."

    Katie smiled back, got up off the end of the bed and waltzed out of the room. Hearing the thuds as Katie went downstairs, Rebecca shot up out of bed and let her feet touch the cold wooden floor, squealing almost as the cold pierced the soles of her feet. After a second she placed her feet back down and got up. Stretching and yawning she threw her arms up then brought them back down as she walked to her wardrobe. She was looking to change from the white bra and pants she slept in. Looking upon the many hundreds of pieces of clothing, she picked out her particulars and went to get dressed.

    A few minutes later she brought herself downstairs and went into the kitchen. She was greeted by a plate of honey on toast and her sister at the opposite end. Katie smiled at her before taking a bite of her toast too.

    "You look amazing today, Sis!"

    "Pfft - I know your lying, but thanks anyways."

    "Why don't you ever believe me when I say it? Everyone knows you for your looks and personality, ya know. Its not a common thing to have both Sis - embrace it."

    "Yeeeeahhhh yeeeeahhhh, gimme a break, Katie. Haha. Anyways, thanks for the toast. I'll see you later," Rebecca said before grabbing her keys, hearing the jingle as she put them in her pocket and closed the door.

    Rebecca sighed. She knew it was going to be one of those days ....
