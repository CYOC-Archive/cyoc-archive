    Mary's Destiny

    The temple was a place made for pigs, with delicious food, warm, chemically balanced mud, and an ideal ambient temperature and atmospheric composition. It was as close as any place on earth to Her Glorious and Radiant Sty. Mary and Cassandra, both in sow form, reveled in the pure contentment and timeless happiness that was the ideal of the sisterhood of swine worshippers.

    It was a little different when Mary woke up to discover herself human again, but covered with the same mud she had found so sensually satisfying as a sow. Cassandra, also in human form, was laughing. "The adjustment does take some getting used to." she said.

    "Why am I human again?" Mary asked, with a disappointed tone in her voice.

    "The goddess chooses different people for different destinies. Jess and Rob are pigs now--they will live long and contented lives here, and Jess will birth and nurse many healthy litters, some fathered by Rob and some by other boars. When they die, they will enter Her Glorious Sty for eternity. But the goddess has something different in mind for you. You are to join our sisterhood, and work for the advancement of her holy cause. You will retain the ability to shift into sow form and gain the ability to control it, and your training will remove the impurities you have absorbed from drinking the wrong potions. In time, you may become the greatest of us."

    Mary was thrilled by the possibility of entering the sisterhood and learning pig-based magic. "Ok" she said, "but while I'm human, how about a shower?"
