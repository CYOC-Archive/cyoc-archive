    The new stats on Chris's screen were all different. He had to guess that they were accurate for an average 13 year-old kid. As he explored the screen, he noticed that the various stats were alterable. Curious, he clicked on height and changed 5' 2" to 5' 10". A small popup appeared - 'New Package or edit to current?' He grinned - this was like his Mom's GPS. Selecting 'edit to current' caused some of the other states to change. Weight bumped up a little more than 20 pounds, and penis length increased to 4 inches. He grinned and edited the penis to be 10 inches long, and selected the 'uncut' option. Changing hair color to red caused skin tone to switch to pale and a freckle option appeared. Chris chose 'heavily freckled' from the options, and then changed the eye color option from brown to green.

    Another set of stats was labeled 'Personality.' Most of the stats here were set for someone much nicer than his roommate - a Boy Scout! And one from the 1950s at that. Chris chuckled at the thought of his rude, crude and lazy roommate Anthony being a throwback to some ancient Disney movie. Based on the settings, he did not drink, smoke, use bad language or drugs. Intelligence was high, and so was his sense of ethics and morals. Chris found the social settings - Anthony had a high likeability rating. That got reset to the highest level.

    Awareness? Chris thought about that. If this thing really worked, Anthony would notice changes soon. Chris read over all the possibilities and set awareness of others to 'None' and awareness of subject to 'Full.' This brought up another option for 'Reality Adjustment?' Sure thing. A few more options made Anthony a bed-wetter, more athletic, and seriously attracted to large animals.

    'Save and apply changes?' Chris selected 'YES' and watched as the timer reset. He had to follow Anthony and check this out! He hit exit and grabbed a jacket. The program beeped and another box appeared. 'Apply changes to all subjects?' Chris misread this as 'Apply all changes to subject' and hit yes, then headed out as the program shut down. Except that is went to a status screen instead. It showed eight reports for subjects - all were students living in the rooms around Chris and Anthony, and all were about to become identical Boy Scouts!
