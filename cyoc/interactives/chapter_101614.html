    "How many wishes do I get, slave?" asked Cindy.

    Alibaba shook his head in shock. The impertinence of the mortal! He had never been so insulted. "Madame, are you addressing me? I am no one's slave. And my name is Alibaba. I expect you to show me the proper respect and address me as such."

    Cindy rolled her eyes. "Whatever. Just answer the question."

    Alibaba was furious. He was going to make sure this mortal got what she deserved as soon as she made her wish. "Leon had two wishes left. But he used one to transfer the other to you. So you only have one wish left."

    Cindy scoffed. "That's all I need. I already know what I want. Genie, I wish that regardless of what happens Leon will remain my loyal slave and do whatever I say."

    "I'm afraid I can't do that. My magic won't allow me to control minds, or allow others to do so," said Alibaba.

    Cindy stomped over to Ali, and slapped him on the cheek hard. "Impertinent imp! You claim to have power, yet are limited and weak. How dare you deny me what I want! You aren't powerful, you are pathetic, just like this worm," said Cindy, as she kicked Leon. He scrambled back towards her, and began to lick her boot.

    Alibaba glared at her. This woman would be punished for her insolence. Severely.

    "Oh, don't give me that look. You are my servant, even if you deny it. But your weakness has given me an idea. Maybe I was a little short sighted. Why have one pet, when I can have thousands? And what better way to do that than through subjugation. Subjugation and domination by someone with power. More power than any mortal. More power than a genie. More powerful than any being on the planet. Genie, I wish to become the most powerful creature in the world, " said Cindy, as she stood up and raised her arms in triumph.

    "Your wish is my command, Master, " said the Alibaba sarcastically, as he smiled widely and nodded his head.

    "That's it? " said Cindy, as she stared at her unchanged body.

    "Give it some time. The magic for such a 'powerful' wish takes some time to complete," said Alibaba.

    Cindy was beginning to get uncomfortable. Her boots were pinching her feet, and her skin itched. It felt like her panties were riding up into her ass.

    "Well, how do I perform magic? Do I need to say something, or just think about it?" said Cindy.

    The genie chuckled. "Oh, I am afraid you can do no magic. You requested power, but you didn't specify what kind of power," said Alibaba with a smile.

    "What are you talking about .... " said Cindy. She was interrupted by a severe pain in her abdomen. She bent over and gripped her swelling abdomen. Her corset burst apart, revealing a scaly belly. She lifted her hands, and saw them growing longer and thicker, stretching into a blood red claw. Blood red clawed toes tore through the toes of her boots.

    "What... What have you done to me ..." grunted Cindy through clenched teeth. Her jaw was stretching longer, forming a snout with sharp teeth. Her hair fell off in clumps, as two long twisted horns grew from her skull. Her ears receded into her head. Her corset dropped off, torn apart by her widening chest and the sharp spines growing out of her back. A thickening tail whipped back and forth above her ass at the base of her spine.

    "Why, my dear, I am simply giving you what you wished for. You are becoming the most powerful creature on the planet. The strongest and most physically powerful creature the world had ever known. The biggest and baddest monster to ever walk the earth. Quite simply, you are becoming a Dragon!"

    Cindy groaned, in frustration as much as pain. The genie had tricked her. She wanted to be a powerful Queen, not some kind of monster. How could she rule now? She grunted as her body continued to expand, piling muscle upon muscle on her powerful limbs.

    "Come, Leon. I think we have had our fill of this beast. Let's leave her to her fate," said Alibaba.

    "Mistress!" yelled Leon, as he tried to rush to her side. Alibaba pulled Leon back, and began to drag him out the door.

    "NO!" shouted Cindy. Her words were hoarse and gruff. They were barely understandable. "Mine .... my ... pet...."

    Cindy's growing body filled the small apartment, her tail whipped around the room, blocking the exit. Alibaba just smiled and shook his head.

    "Do you really think me so helpless? You have no idea about the true nature of power. Here, let me give you a lesson, " said the genie, as he and Leon blinked out the room, teleporting back to Leon's house.

    Cindy screamed in frustration. Her precious pet was gone, stolen by the evil genie. She had to find it. She called for her pet, but her words became a hacking cough. As she cleared her throat she spouted flames, setting fire to her apartment. The other tenants fled in terror, but not before calling the police.

    But Cindy was long gone by the time they arrived. She had flown away, searching in vain for her pet.
