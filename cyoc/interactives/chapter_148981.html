    It had been an interesting month for Kellie and her friends since the hourglass was used. While an uneasy peace existed between Kellie and Lilith, it did not mean that things had returned to normal.

    Normal was the furthest thing from what Kellie now resides in. Thanks to Lilith and Circes joint amusement park the sight of anthro people (and anthro demons) was quickly becoming common place. Ever since the two ancient witches had joined forces Kellie had seen more than her fair share of anthro people mixing around town. While the population had leveled out (maybe 10% of the town) Kellie was still trying to get used to naked anthro people happily mixing around town. It drove Kellie crazy that Circe and Liliths amusement park seemed to be one of the most popular places not only in town but in the state. The two ancient witches were making more money than they had ever dreamed of and were now thinking of expanding to other states.

    As Kellie now sat in English class with her friends she glumly stared ahead at their teacher Miss Warner or Cindy the anthro zebra woman. Cindy was still in her anthro form yet school policy dictated that anthro people needed to be dressed appropriately while at school. Far too many parents had complained when anthro teachers began teaching class. While they didn’t necessarily have an issue with them being animal people, the idea of nudity in school made far too many soccer moms clutch their pearls in fright. As a result the school board asked that anthro people be clothed while on school grounds.

    “Now class,” Cindy stated as she now stood before the class, her tail swishing through a hole in her skirt. “What can we say about this scene in Hamlet?” Some hands shot up to offer an answer as Kellie stared ahead unfocused. While worried that Lilith and Circe could use these anthros to attack that didn’t seem very likely now. Usually Kellie enjoyed Miss Warner’s class but she found herself distracted as she mulled things over. The sound of the classroom door being ripped from its hinges caused everyone to scream in alarm. Cindy turned, startled before sighing in annoyance. “Bob What are you doing here?”

    Bob, the demonic zebra anthro, now stomped into the classroom. The gigantic demon had remained in his zebra anthro form, it was just nightmarish in appearance. Huge muscles, a sinister feral face and cloven hooves like an ox cracked the tiles as he approached his girlfriend. “I am sorry Cindy,” he growled. “I did not realize you were still working.”

    Kellie gazed up at the demonic anthro, stifling a giggle at the beasts appearance. Unfortunately it appeared Bob hadn’t gotten the memo about not being nude on school grounds. The demon zebras massive member now dangled freely, carelessly swinging from side to side. This was unfortunate for Karen as she gazed in abject horror as the offending member now sat six inches in front of her face. “Bob sweety,” Cindy said kindly. “You know I love when you visit but you know the rules, or did you forget again?” Bob gave Cindy a confused look as he now tried to remember what she was talking about. Looking down at Karen he could see the look of shock on the teen girls face as she gazed at his nude anthro form. “Oh,” he said quietly, turning away from Karen. “Sorry Cindy I forgot.”

    Cindy smiled, slowly approaching her demon boyfriend. Standing up on her toes she now gave the fiend a gentle pec on the cheek. Bob blushed, flashing a smile that was meant to be warm but instead came across as menacing to everyone but Cindy. The demon zebra now handed his girlfriend a small bouquet of flowers before stomping out of the classroom, clumsily trying to put the door he had smashed back in place.

    As school ended Kellie and her friends now found themselves in the parking lot. School would be ending soon and a summer of fun was hopefully in their future. Turning towards her friends she smirked as she saw Karen still appeared she’ll shock from her meeting with Bob. “So interesting day of class,” she grinned. Karen frowned, shuddering as she thought back to English class. “Don’t remind me,” she grumbled.

    Kellie and her friends laughed jokingly at Karen’s expense as they headed for the cars. “So,” Katie asked. “We gonna practice some more today?”

    “We should,” Kellie stated. “I mean, yeah things are okay right now but we have to be prepared.”

    “Thought we were getting pretty good already,” Lisa said smugly.

    “Don’t get too cocky,” Kellie added. “We have to stay sharp, Jack is still on track to take over as fr Morgan LeFay. We need to get better.”

    “That reminds me,” Karen said. “I mean I know we’ve been working hard and getting better but have you thought about giving the other magic. We might be able to train them now.”

    “She has a point,” Mary Elise shrugged. “Right now Lisa and Karen have about as much experience as we did when we started training them.”

    “And they have more control over it than we did,” Katie added, causing Mar Elise to frown.

    Kellie nodded, perhaps now was the the time to give the others magic. “Sounds fair,” she said. “If we do thanthough we should each take one apprentice to train. There’s five of us and four of them. If we focus on training them one on one then we should be able to get them to catch up pretty quickly.”

    “Okay,” Kate said. “And have one extra to help out where needed. The only question now is, who do we take as an apprentice?” Kellie and her friends now thought carefully about who they would train.
