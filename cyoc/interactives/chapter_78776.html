    However, when she looked around the racks, one thing that caught her attention wasn't a costume, but a collar
    meant for an animal. As she looked at the collar closely, the old man who owned the shop appeared out of
    nowhere.

    "I see you've taken an interest in that collar," the old man said, startling Jessica.

    "Um, Yeah," Jessica said, "I bet this thing contains an entire costume magically, or turns you into a certain
    animal."

    "Amazing," the old man said with suprise, "How did you figure out that it can turn the wearer into an animal
    so quickly."

    "I KNEW IT!" Jessica cried before feeling embarrassed, "I mean, I found out how this place has real magic from
    my fried Emily."

    "That's interesting," the old man said, "I think I know who that is, I'll have to ask her about you when I see
    her again. Will you be taking that collar?"

    "Of course," Jessica replied.

    "Good," the old man said, taking the collar from the rack, and bringing it back to the counter, Jessica
    following her.

    "So what animal does this turn me into?" Jessica asked as she paid for her new collar.

    "Well that depends on your decision," the old man explained, "You see, you tell me what animal you want to be,
    and I will give this collar the proper enchantment to allow you to change into said animal at will. You can
    turn from human form, to anthro animal form, to full animal form. The best part of this is that while changed,
    you will appear to be just a regular person in an animal costume in anthro form, and while in full animal
    form, you can still talk like a human if you wish. The only way around people noticing you is if someone
    notices you while you are in between transformations. Now that that's out of the way, what animal would you
    like to be?"

    Jessica became more excited than ever after hearing all of that. She could be any animal, anthro or otherwise,
    whenever she wanted. She thought hard about what animal she wanted, and after a lot of thinking, she made her
    decision. She wanted to be a(n)...
