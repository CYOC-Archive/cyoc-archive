    VexCo Nuclear Technologies is the new American leader in nuclear energy. However,
    in 2020, their plant in Foresttown, Kentucky experienced a small leak. As they were
    cleaning up the spillage, one of the workers got exposed to the waste and was
    instantly transformed into an anthromorphic dog. He was immediately taken for testing
    where it was found the the type of nuclear waste made in that particular factory had
    mixed with a rare element found in the surrounding land. When a person was to touch
    the mixture, they became an anthromorphic version of the animal that most
    represented them. More tests were done and it was found that if animals touched the
    waste, they would become anthromorphic versions of themselves capable of speech
    and rational thought. Before long, many rich people and other investors were giving
    huge amounts of money towards the research. By the year 2040, research was
    complete. There were no harmful side effects to touching or injesting the mixture and it
    was discovered that inhaling a ballon full of helium would reverse the changes. It was
    also discovered that if two anthros bred, their children would be born as an anthro
    hybrid of whatever their parents were. However, if these children drank the mixture, it
    would transform them into whatever form they desired.

    By 2045, the FDA approved VexCo's request to market the mixture and it was soon
    stocking the shelves. Everyone who had ever wanted to be an anthro could now do so
    at a small cost. The waste was otherwise useless to VexCo, so the product was
    extremely cheap. It is now 2060. The world is now more than 60% anthros. Most of
    these anthros were former pets that were given the mixture. As with any other race,
    there are many prejudices against "furries". The Anthro Rights Organization (ARO)
    was formed in 2049 and continue to represent anthros everywhere. The first anthro
    president was elected in 2058 and the United States is now the only "free anthro"
    society. Other countries are not as accepting of anthros.

    However, there was one thing the mixture did that VexCo didn't know about. It only
    happened to an astronomically small percentage of people who took the mixture. If a
    person who drank the mixture had a certain gene mutation, the mixture mutated this
    gene further and not only made them an anthro, but gave them a superpower. This
    mutation is physically undetectable; and because so few people have it (approx. 1 in
    10,000,000) there are no DNA tests administered for it.

    This story will follow the life of someone who, whether they themselves drank the
    mixture or not, are affected by the product in some way. Perhaps they drank the
    potion. Perhaps one of their friends or family did. Maybe they gained powers from it.
    Maybe someone they know did. Or maybe someone who gained powers takes
    advantage of them. Whatever the case, VexCo's revolutionary product is sure to cause
    some excitement.
