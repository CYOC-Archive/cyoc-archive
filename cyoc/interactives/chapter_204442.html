    Ivan and the mall-goers had a nice relief station going. Supplies were plentiful, sleeping and resting areas were made, and communications were set up to get in contact with loved ones and for help. After contacting the local emergency services, it seemed the rest of New York was also affected and quarantines were being set up. No one was happy with the idea being isolated, but there were many others like Ivan setting up to deal with the situation. Only a short time had passed so far, so the mall goers would do their best until major relief efforts were organized for the city.

    Clothing was one of the major issues to deal with. Many mall-goers were just naked at first, but as things calmed down, clothing was brought back to the relief station for both comfort and modesty reasons. Some wore children's clothing, bearing the humiliation, others wore over-sized clothing like Ivan. No one was too concerned about who wore what, other then the most grumbliest of mall-goers.

    Some mall-goers were concerned about the more in-human of lolis. How did those animal ears and tails worked? How the heck did the ones like the fairies even manage to exist, or fly? What was with some of the huge dicks and boobs? There was even some mentions by a off-duty medic helping out that it was uncharacteristic to have sensitive genitals, much less a sex-drive for their apparent ages. Just one more thing to worry about, but not a top-priority.

Faces and eyes felt a bit characteristic. Even some bodily proportions felt flattering, ignoring the cases of something like huge boobs of course. Even the term of "Loli" began to be regularly used among the mall-goers to describe themselves, if only just to describe their bodies and situation.
