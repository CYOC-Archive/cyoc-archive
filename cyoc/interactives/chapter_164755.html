    Trish arrived at the football field at Corby High to see the Cheer Squad getting ready.

    "Hey gals, I'm here!" said Trish as she saw her friends.

    "Okay everyone, Since Trish is here let's start practice." said Mrs. Anderson, The Cheer Coach who was a former Cheerleader herself.

    Practice was doing well for everyone as the did their usual routine until Trish saw her friend Angela, A Brunette haired Cheerleader was limping.

    "Hold on Mrs. Anderson." said Trish as she checked on Angela.

    "Are you okay Angela?" asked a concerned Trish.

    "I think I pull a muscle in my leg." said Angela.

    "Hold on, I'll get you to the Nurse." said Trish as she helped Angela out.

    "Thank You Trish." said Angela.

    Afew minutes later, Trish returned to the field after helping Angela at the Nurse. The Cheer Squad continued practice for another hour until Mrs. Anderson told them it was time to stop.

    "Okay everyone, Good practice, Now hit the showers." instructed Mrs. Anderson.

    "BTW, That was nice of you to help out Angela." said Mrs. Anderson.

    "Just doing what any Cheer Captain would do." said Trish.

    Trish had then joined her fellow Cheerleaders in the Showers, and Locker Room. As Trish was changing into her underwear, One of her Cheerleader pals, Mei a Japanese girl was looking at her.

    "You like what you see?" said Trish in a slightly humorous tone as Mei looked embarassed.

    "Sorry, I didn't mean anything." said an embarassed Mei.

    "It's ok, I don't mind if girls are into me." replied Trish.

    "Wait, you're okay if I'm into Girls?" asked Mei.

    "Yeah, If you love women, That shouldn't be my problem." answered Trish.

    "Thanks." said Mei.

    "You're welcome." said Trish as she put on her normal clothes which was a red halter top, and blue capri pants with red heels.

    Trish walked out of the Locker Room, and headed to the Nurse's Office to see if Angela was ok.

    "You okay there Angela?" asked Trish.

    "Yeah, The Nurse said that I just have to take it easy for a day or two." said Angela as she placed some ice on her ankle.

    "Thanks for helping me out." said Angela as she hugged Trish.

    "No prob." said Trish.

    Trish, and Angela were walking down the hall when they saw the some of Corby High Bats Football Team.

    "Hey there Jared." said Trish.

    "Trish, How's my favorite Cheerleader doing?" asked Jared a Brunette guy wearing a Red, and Black Letterman Jacket with Jeans, and Sneakers.

    "Doing well sweetie, What about Tiger?" said Trish as she approached Jared.

    "Same, Next week we're against the Marsh High School Hawks." said Jared.

    "Maybe after the Game, We can get dinner." offered Jared.

    "Of course, If you guys win, Maybe Dessert will be on me." said Trish in a seductive tone as she then kissed Jared.

    "I'm really going to win this game now." said Jared as Trish then walked away.

    An hour later, Trish returned home, and was back in her bedroom. Unlike Jeff, Trish's Room was pretty clean, and had afew Cheerleader Posters. Trish looked over at the Desk to see the Chronivac along with her framed photo of her, and Jared.

    "What the heck is this?" questioned Trish as she looked at the device.

    After a minute of inspection, She learned all about the Chronivac.

    "Interesting, I wonder what I can do with this thing?" pondered Trish.
