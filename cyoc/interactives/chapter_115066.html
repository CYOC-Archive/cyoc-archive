    Callum is rubbing his eyes. "Dude. Maggie, what the hell is up with your flash? That was like ridiculously bright."

    There's no response from Maggie. When he looks around, he notices that Maggie and Brodie are no where to be seen, but Maggie's phone is on the seat where she'd been sitting a moment before. There's some sound coming from the phone. Braiden leans over to pick up the phone just as the sound stops. He shops it to Callum and it appears there's an ended YouTube video on the screen.

    Braiden hits the replay button and the video starts to play, showing Maggie and Brodie in front of a white background, both looking confusing and scared. "Callum! Braiden! Is that you?! Something weird is going on."

    Brodie pipes up. "Yeah! I think we've been suc-"

    His voice cuts off as the video ends, the replay option coming up.

    Callum and Braiden shoot a look at each other, then Braiden hits the replay button again.

    Brodie starts from where he was cut off. "-ked into the phone. I don't know how!"

    Maggie leans closer in the video. "Guys! You need to help us! Maybe it was that app I-"

    Again the video ends once again, leaving the blurry paused image of Brodie and Maggie on the screen.

    Braiden turns the phone around in his hands. "Wow. This is a pretty elaborate prank, eh?"

    Callum looks around the library, half expecting his two friends to pop out from behind a bookshelf at any moment.

    They do not.

    Callum takes the phone from Braiden for a closer look. "Yeah, I guess. Kinda weird though, eh?"

    "Well, yeah, but Brodie's kinda a nerd. Maybe he programmed the phone to do something like this."

    Callum hit the replay button on the video again. Maggie continued from where she left off. "...installed. Guys! This isn't a prank! I swear. Something feels really weird."

    Brodie pipes up with a whine. "I'm not a nerd, Braiden! You've go-" And yet again the video ends.

    "This is kinda spooky." Callum admits.

    "Those two are probably on a web cam or something," Braiden says, taking the phone and turning off the screen. He slips the phone into his pocket and looks around. "Let's take a look around and see if we can find them."

    Callum looks at the pocket where Braiden slipped the phone, feeling a bit unsure before nodding. "Ur...sure, sounds like a good idea."
