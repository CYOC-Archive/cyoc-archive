    Both the raccoons jump at you in an attempt to steal the keys. One raccoon runs up your right leg as the other jumps onto your chest. They both run up onto your right arm, their claws digging into your skin.You hold the keys tightly as you try to shake them off. You shake your arm as one of the raccoons fly into the dumpster. The other raccoon goes for your hand, and after a bit of a struggle you are able to get him off.

    The two raccoons are now lying on the ground, but appear to be okay. You catch your breath and start to move away from them. You consider firing the keys at them, but you don’t want to make the situation worse than it already is.

    They appear to say something to each other, but you can’t understand what they are saying. You decide the best option now is to just run away. As you start to get away they catch up to you. The first raccoon jumps on your back and gets onto the back of your head. The second raccoon jumps onto your leg and begins to crawl up to your arm. The one raccoon moves onto your face, blocking your vision. The other raccoon moves to your hand to pry the keys out of it.

    You are able to get the raccoon off your face, as it falls to the ground. As you shake the raccoon off of your hand, he falls off. As he falls off of your hand, the keys fly out of your hand right next to the raccoon.

    The raccoons say something to each other, and then the one fires the keys at you. There is a blinding light. You feel a warm and a funny feeling spread around your body. It feels like you are falling. You feel dizzy and look around a bit. At your eye level you see two raccoons across from you. You hold your hands out and see two dark grey arms cover in fur, with two dark paws. You know exactly what happened.

    You are speechless. One raccoon says to you “That’s what you get!”. You can actually understand him now. You turn around and see a long black and grey tail. You are still shocked. “I was planning on changing you back, what was that for?” The other raccoons still seem mad. “We couldn’t trust you and had to get the keys for ourselves.” The second raccoon chimes in “and they were really shiny”

    “Well I have to change my friend Keith back”

    "Not until we change ourselves back”

    You sigh, now that they have the keys you should probably play along if you want to be changed back. What do they do with the keys?
