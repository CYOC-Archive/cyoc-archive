    After getting out of a Sunbuck's coffee store and grabbing one of their espressos you went out on a leisurely stroll through the city. Taking a final sip of your coffee you sat down in the middle of a small park. You took a few minutes to look around and then you got up to walk away as you looked up to the sun which you felt compelled to look directly at for some reason. You kept staring for awhile as your vision started to become white and you felt... fuzzy all over? When your vision came back you saw that you were in a hospital bed with an unknown man reading a book near you.

    You asked "What's going on?"

    The man said "A part of a prophecy unknown to many started... Or in another word the phoenix spirit inside of you is starting to awaken, and your body is starting to react to that spirit... Then again I'm just some crazy man following superstitions so... Let me finding the princess in a specific spot in a specific place as told by a prophecy not persuade you into believing I know what i'm talking about... Mostly because the justice system says I do not and cannot claim I know what i'm talking about... Just know that you may wish to prepare clothes the size of a baby or toddler."

    The man sat up and said "You can contact whoever you wish. The hospital attendants are very active here so just call them and you can contact whoever you want. If you want to contact me... well that should be simple... again the hospital here is very good and have kept a record of me here... So by now." The man then walked out leaving you confused. What was that man talking about? Some Prophecy? Scientists had proven you cannot accurately predict the future like in a Prophecy... and clothes for a baby or toddler? What could that mean?

    A few moments afterwards some guards from the castle came barging in with a stretcher and some first aid supplies. You assumed they came with an ambulance as well. Sighing you said "Just get me back to the castle... Today's just been weird, may need to check my magic." You helped the guards get you into the stretcher as you were brought back to the castle, and put into your bed as a priest examined your body for magical imbalances. The examination showed nothing too odd going on with your magic and you were left alone to eat your dinner. Shortly afterwards you went to sleep.

    In your dreams you saw a flame in the middle of a void. You heard a voice say "Hello my child, You feathers haven't really came in have they yet? That's fine, but soon you may need to develop more as a Firelight. Allow yourself to be cloaked in the magical flames and become a true Firelight." Suddenly you felt a searing pain as if you were on fire. The sensations were painful in the dream as you felt like you were dying. You cried out "Help me!" But it was in the middle of a dream and you had a small feeling that no one was coming.

    When you woke up you saw your mother looking down above you and you were completely awake immediately. You rarely ever saw your mother or father so you just was completely surprised that she showed up. Your mother asked "Child, are you alright?" you nodded and your mother continued "What happened last night? There was a large light coming from your room, and the attendants who investigated were shocked to see you as you are. Did something happen yesterday when you were in the city?"
