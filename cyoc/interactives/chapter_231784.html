Her arms ached with exertion as she reached the top, but the view was worth it. The ball pit stretched out below her, a mass of color and movement.

She hit the bottom with a splash, the water sending shockwaves through her tiny frame. She scrambled out of the pool, laughing uncontrollably, and ran over to the ball pit, joining her friends in their game of tag.

    Mika was having the time of her life on the rock climbing wall, her nimble fingers and strong legs carrying her up to the top with ease. She grinned as she reached the summit, the view from up there was incredible. She could see Tricia, and with one shot, Tricia soon found herself sprouting into a lanky tween physique. Mika stuck her tongue out and beemed with pride.

    Tricia responded by jumping down and blasting Mika in return, her aim impeccable. As they both stood there, panting, they could feel their bodies change once more. They grew an inch or two, their clothes feeling a little tighter around their gangly limbs before they engaged in a full-on fire fight.

Samantha was telling Katie about her crush on Jake, and Katie, being a few years older, listened intently. She gave Samantha some advice on how to approach him and make him notice her. As they talked, they both felt a strange sensation washing over them. They looked down at their bodies and gasped in surprise - they had both grown a few inches taller, and their clothes felt slightly snugger. They exchanged glances, realizing that they were both hit. They turned around to find Claudia, who was still nice and small.

Mary was hiding in the tunnelways, while Claudia could be seen floating on a pool float, soaking up the sun's rays. "Guess she was the first one out." Samantha shrugged.
