    A huge smile was on Kyle's face, 2nd period had just ended, and already the entire student body was buzing with the news that the "Transform or Dare" curse had struck again. How the stuck-up but beautiful cheerleader Jen had been reduced to the homliest girl in the entire school. What had baffeled everyone was her decidsion to come to school so soon, or ever for that matter ever, so soon after being transformed forever into such a sad little creature. Laughter and ridiccule followed as she slunk from one class to another in her new humiliating form. But Kyle knew why because he had placed the curse on her, and poor little Jen had been stuck with both parts of the curse. And now for her "Dare" and so he could get the revenge he wanted, he had caused her to fall madly and hopelessly in love with him, so he could use her and reject her. Just then he felt a tap on his sholder. He turned.
    It was then he stood face-to-face with the wretched little thing he had caused the once proud Jen to become. Why this was funnier than he could imagined (why he almost felt pity for it), there standing a good foot shorter than him (Kyle was one of the smaller boys in his class) was the pale, freckled, pare shaped little Jen. Looking at her short tightly permed bright reddish-orange hair, he shot out, "What do you want Little Orange Toad girl?". The large groupe that had been following burst into laughter and mockingly repeated her new nick-name.
    Tears began to flow down Jen's now ridulous face as she timidly said, "I've been looking for you all morning, Kyle, I thought maybe we could walk to next class together.". "What makes you think I'd ever want to be seen with a dog like YOU?", Kyle retorted. Between sobs Jen began to plead, "Please Kyle, I realy like you and I'm so sorry for how I've treated you in the past. I'll do anything, carry your books, do your chores, buy you things, do your homework, and... you can have me when ever you want.", she sheepishly added.
    This was too good to be true thought Kyle, now he was going to let her have it, "HAVE YOU? Why you pathedic little thing, you need to take a good long look in the mirror! I don't think anyone no matter how desparate is ever going to want you again! But as for thouse other things, hmm, perhaps I'll let you just so you can be of some use to me." Kyle cruelly countinued, "Of course I'm not going to let some former air-head cheerleader touch my homework!"
    And for the first time since the horrible curse had been placed on her a smile spread accross her tear soaked face. "But Kyle, my love, if only you'd get to know me better, I'm a straight A student, I really am quite cleaver.", replied Jen. A look of worry spread accross Kkle's face as she countinued. "As soon as you did this to me my mind became obsessed with tring to find a way that you, my love, and I could share this love I feel for you. So I got on line and researched the curse." Kyle was too stunned to speak, he knew what was coming next.
    "Kyle, my love, Transform or Dare, she tenderly wispered. Kyle looked at the now homly girl and thought there was no way he was going to let her reduce him to that, so without thinking, he blurted out..."Dare!". "Oh my love, perfect!" And with that she layed out the rules of his curse, "Kyle, from this day forward you will love me with a love never before known to man. You love me for th rest of your life, marry me after collage, have lots of kids and live happily ever after. And Kyle, you'll love me tenderly and passionately when ever I request it. Oh and one last thing," she added, "your love for me will be so total that you'll never want to use the curse again. Now kiss me hard!" And Kyle knew his fate was sealed.
    Suddenlly he ran to and took her in his arms as his hands slid down to grope huge fleshy hand fulls of her beachballs sized butt. He stared under her thick orange mono-brow into her longing blue eyes, the only remnant of her former self. His hands slid up her back and into her short springy hair, he had to turn his head sideways in order to avoid her large nose. There lips met as his tounge ran back and forth accross her huge buck teeth, and then found it's way into her mouth. He gently pulled back, lovingly stared into her eyes and loudly declared, "Jen, I'll love you 'til the end of times!" And all the watching students exploded into laughter.
    And somewhere deep inside Kyle, a tiny voice screamed out in shame and revulsion. And in Jen a simular little voice celebrated a sweet revenge.
