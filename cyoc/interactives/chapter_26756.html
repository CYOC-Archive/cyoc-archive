    (Note: I accidentally wrote Mark in Eric's description so just to recap Mark is the adventurous one and Eric the the blond surfer)


    You, Erik, Mark, Clara, and Tyler bicker for a few minutes till you look at your watch
    "Guys we waisted thirty minutes doing nothing already lets just pick something and do it!" you yell
    "Fine is Mark wants to see his little witch house in the woods we can do that" Clara said disappointing Eric. Mark was happy in his small victory and the fact they were finally going to see the witch house for themselves.
    "We waisted a good thirty minutes waiting for you all to decide so lets hurry and get to the woods before too much more time passes and we don't get to do anything"

    You all bicker for a little while while you follow the road west to the woods. When you finally reach the woods its almost noon you only had a few hours left till you were expected home.
    "So this is it... looks kind of... stupid" Eric says sighing
    "Relax wait till we find that house" Mark says defending his choice
    "So... do you even know the way to that house" you ask
    "Um... ya its directly in the woods theres markers and paths everywhere I've walked them a hundred times" he responds
    "So you went before?" you ask again
    "Not exactly but I know where to go trust me" he says smiling
    "If I die out here I'm going to claw my way out of my coffin and beat the living crap out of you!" Eric sneers
    "Shut up and lets get this over with we are dead meat if we don't get back in time" Tyler says
    "Well if no one is going to actually move I'll go first" Clara says walking into the forest.
    "Wait! you don't know the way!" Mark rushes in after her shortly followed by the rest of the gang.

    a hour and a half later...

    "We there yet?" Tyler complains.
    "No" you reply

    another half hour passes...

    "We there yet?" Tyler complains again
    "NO! and quit asking you'll know when we get there" Mark yells at him annoyed he can't find the house.

    yet another hour passes...

    "We there yet?" he asks again
    "NO" the rest of you shout at once
    "Do we even know where we are?" Eric asks sincerely
    "Well I er... um... not really no." Mark admits causing everyone to look angrily at him. "Hay we haven't passed a single marker or path yet what am I supposed to do?"
    "Turn around when you first realized we were lost might of been a good idea" Eric says "I knew this was a bad Idea we should of gone to the beach instead of doing this exploring crap!"
    "When we get out of here I'm going to have to kill you" Clara says angrily
    "Why wait I'm going to kill him now" Tyler says moving toward Mark
    "wait come on now..." Mark says backing up. When Tyler continues to move forward Mark speeds up and eventually breaks into a run.
    "Wait hold on" You yell running to catch up with them the remaining people glance at each other and follow quickly behind.

    after about five minutes of a fast chaise though brambles and dense woods you all stop suddenly.
    "You hear something?" Mark says in a very horror movie way
    "No I don't why?" Eric says actually worried now
    "Thats my point Theres no sounds at all and the sky shouldn't be that dark... What time is it?!" Mark says with panic in his voice.
    "Hold on let me... wait where is it? My cell phones gone!" You yell.
    "Wait mine is to I we must of lost them in the run!" Tyler yells each of you suddenly realize all of your cell phones are gone.
    "This isn't good" You say stating the obvious
    "Thats not all... look" Mark says pointing to a dark path leading though a bunch of dead trees. "Thats the path to the witches house"

    A chill runs down your spine this is how almost every horror movie starts and since its happening in real life this isn't a good thing.

    "Well I'm not going to wait for you people to grow a spine follow me if you want" Clara says heading down the path as it covers with fog. You each give each other nervous glances and slowly follow after her.

    After what felt like another half your you come upon a old mansion that despite its age and decaying walls looked fairly well built, but what was such a big house doing in the woods anyway.

    "Look! Some one is inside the house theres a light on and you can see something moving inside." Mark says
    "Maybe they have a phone or know what time it is?" Tyler asks
    "Maybe... Well who wants to go in first and check?" Eric asks looking nervously at everyone.

    What do you do now?
