    Ty's Power

    They're all back home 9 years after they left

    So Slithorin let Joe in on her true draconic identity and listened as he described the Offworld that the
    six of them came from

    that was called Grovers Mill,Pelugia,Earth.

    She especially interested in the real estate business goings on in this Grovers Mills,and
    Joe was somewhat unsettled by the fact she had claws rather
    then fingernails at the end of her
    fingers.

    Slithorin kept a portable abascus and idly slid the beads back and forth as she calculated the
    risk/return of doing business
    in Grover Mills.

    She scoffed at the notion her Tenant Wizard from Hell would help them
    get back to Grovers Mills
    and after a while asked Joe if he had any

    "artifacts" from his homeworld.

    The dragoness in disguise held no interest in his non functioning
    iPad and other electronic gear
    but took a keen interest in a battered
    cartographic map of Grovers Mills he kept on his person.

    Slithorin greedily examined the map and kept peppering Joe with various questions throughout the
    night.

    She loaded him up with coffee and so whilst the others
    were snoring away Joe was awake
    when Slithorin said

    "I think we can do it at the nearby Glen of Clashing Rocks.We don't need that $%&^^$#$ tenant of mine
    to cross over into Grover Mills from here."

    That perked up Joe,who'd left his car running when Ty had hauled them all
    off nine years earlier.

    It was clear that after nine years,Ty was incompetent and incapable
    of returning them to Grovers Mills.

    So Slithorin and Joe talked in whispers as they made plans
    to defy Ty and get everybody back to Grovers Mills.

    So the next morning boastful Ty barked out orders to his
    five companions.

    "We move at dawn" he bragged,even though
    dawn was long gone.

    Joe talked quitetly to Mike ,Brian and Christine about
    Slithorins work on getting them home.

    Christine,who'd lost nine years of her life her,readily agreed and some armtwisting
    later Mike and Brian came aboard.

    In short order Christine had administered the knock out drops to
    Dustin and Ty and the seven of them,headed to the Glen of the Clashing Rocks
    and before the noon hour Slithorin had openned a portal and through it they all went back to
    20167 Grovers Mills.

    They were home again,but
    girlfriends,boyfriends,relatives and parents had sometimes moved and Joe was horrified to discover his
    aunt,had gone to Court
    to have him declared legally dead so she
    could collect the
    Life Insurance Policy
    she'd placed on his head.
