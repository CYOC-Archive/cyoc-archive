    Fun With Dresses

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago I

    As Jane scrolls through the different kinds of clothing, the option for Dresses, catches her eye.

    Different kinds of dresses popped up on the screen. She clicked on one of them and from there she saw that she can edit any attributes of any dress in practically any way she wanted. She changed a slutty cocktail dress into a formal gown, and back again, changing colors, along with size. She was having so much fun with it.

    She saw an option saying “owner:” that was blank.

    “Owner? Why does that matter. I’m on my brother’s account anyways, shouldn’t it default to him paying? Oh well, I’ll write my name on that and let him know later, assuming it doesn’t cost too much,” Jane said to herself.

    She wrote her name down by the option for “owner”.

    She also saw the option saying “how long do you want this?” She clicked permanently. “We’ll, I sure hope it’s permanent. Why should my dress expire?”

    After playing around with the options further, she found a design she liked.

    “We’ll, let’s see how much this costs before I purchase it…”

    She couldn’t find a purchase button, but did find a button that said “confirm”. She selected it, assuming that it would bring up a price, but it didn’t. Instead, a pop up saying “transformation set to begin in 15 seconds”. The time started to count down.

    Jeff opened the door, to see Jane on his computer.

    “No Jane, don’t touch that… what did you do?” Jeff asked.

    Jane panicked, “what? This? I didn’t do anything!”

    “Did you at least change the target of the transformation, from Jeff, to literally anyone or anything else?” Jeff asked.

    “Is that what that option was? I thought it was just the account,” Jane said.

    A pop up on the screen said “Transformation beginning.”

    Suddenly, Jane was beginning to stop thinking about Jeff as her brother. To her, he started to appear like he was her dress.

    “Oh no, what are you doing to me? What did you select?”
