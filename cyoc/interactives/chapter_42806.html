    Thinking that you still had the upper hand on the situation, you just clutch the werewolf fang in your hand as you keep walking down the path. As you did, your body had already grown taller and hairier, making you look like a real man. Not sure of where you were going, you decided to go over to Robert's house to visit him and rub it in his face that you out-smarted him.

    As you keep walking, your body starts packing on muscle and you're still unaware of your changes. To someone else who saw you, they would ask if you have been a bodybuilder for a while and you would reply yes, as that was what you thought now. As you keep walking toward Robert's, your hair has grown even thicker, to the point where it was fur and it slowly enveloped your whole body. You scratch at your chest mindlessly, not noticing that your hands were now paws, with sharp, black claws and the fang was gone... absorbed by your body.

    From walking through the forest, you end up at Robert's house, but in the back yard. Not wanting to take the time to walk to the gate door, you hop over the waist high chain link fence with ease and continue up to the back door. With every step that you took now, your body changed even further. Your feet had become paws as well, shredding through the shoes, which you chucked up to being normal for you and another pair gone to the wayside. With another step, your face pushed out into a muzzle, your ears elongated and grew to a point. With your next step your nose turned black and moist like a true wolf's snout. Your next step, bringing you almost to the house, your new sharp and somewhat long fangs filled your muzzle while your tongue flattened out and widened a bit. With your final step before you knocked on the sliding glass door, you felt a little tension in your pants from your tail and other things growing. Not liking the discomfort, but feeling it was completely normal, you rip a hole at the base of your pants and your new tail popped out. You sigh in a bit of relief.

    As you knock on the pane of glass, you see your reflection on the glass and admired your werewolf form, enjoying every piece of your body as if things had always been that way. Not seeing anyone coming, you go to knock on the glass again, but then try the handle and find that the sliding glass door was unlocked. Being good friends with Robert, even though he liked to trick you often, you just let yourself in knowing that he wouldn't mind in the least. As you slide the door to the right and step in, your new eyes pierced through the darkness and noticed that no one was around. As you shut the door behind you and step in you call out to see if anyone was home.

    "Hello!? Robert, you home?"

    You say, enjoying the low rumbling of your own voice, not thinking anything of it because to you... that is how you have always been. Suddenly, you hear a voice calling out your name.

    "Yeah, is that you David? Why don't you come upstairs, I am in the middle of beating this new video game I bought the other day."

    Thinking nothing of that, other than the fact that he was messing with you even further, your shrug your werewolf shoulders and fly up the stairs with a couple leaps. As you enter the room, you see Robert playing a video game that looked like he was a werewolf in the game. Robert was skinny, pasty skinned, with an auburn head of hair, who was 18 as well, but couldn't even grow any sort of facial hair. Without turning around to look at you, he just starts talking.

    "Hey man, I am glad you had the chance to come over! I definitely wanted to have you check out this new game, it's where you are a werewolf..." and then Robert trailed off to an inaudible tone.

    "Yeah, well if it wasn't for your friend pedaling me some odd items, I would have been over here quicker. Being a werewolf in real life though is definitely better than playing one on a video game." You say in your deep and gruff voice.

    At the sound of your words, Robert pauses the game, sits there silently while scratching his head under his short length of middle parted hair.

    "Hold on... what did you say? For a second I thought you said a friend of mine was trying to peddle you something and that you are a werewolf..." Robert asked as he turned to look at you, but then stopped in shock and total fear.

    "What? You have known that I have been a werewolf ever since my 17th birthday and you were always ok with it before, so what is the deal. Oh... yeah, there was a guy trying to have me pick some items. I took the werewolf fang from him since I knew you sent him to find me and play a joke on me. Ha ha, very funny, but I saw through it and now I'm here..." You drift off as you saw Robert's even paler than normal face.

    "Dude... I... don't know... what you... are talking about... but I never sent anyone to find you to do anything. Also... you... you've never been a werewolf until apparently now, but that is TOTALLY COOL!"

    You look at Robert with a furled brow, not sure why he said what he just said, but you shrug it off since he was probably still messing with you.

    "So... can I try out that video game?"
