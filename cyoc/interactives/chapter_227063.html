    "I wish I had a girlfriend with big boobs!" Kyle cheered to the Genie.

    "As you wish." Genie says as he claps his hands together.

    Seconds later the doorbell rings and Kyle rushes to the front door to open it. Behind the door is a woman in her mid 30s with hourglass figure was looking at Kyle with sultry eyes. She had a brown bobcut hair, a gray virgin killer sweater, fishnet thigh highs and black army boots. She wore some kind of perfume and her tits were indeed larger than her mother's. She also had a left ear piercing that hold a thick ring.

    "Hi..." The woman greets Kyle with very suggesting hand wave. "Kyle, I assume?"

    "Uhm, yes? But who are you?" Kyle asks.

    "Well, my name is Amanda Williams and for some reason I had this crazy thought. Like, I really wanted to come here and declare myself as your new girlfriend." Amanda begins to ramble. "So how old are you and what's your name?"

    "It's Kyle, nice to meet you, and I'm 18-years-old...." Kyle greeted Amanda

    "Like wow, I'm blushing here. Can I come inside? Are your parents home by any chance?" Amanda asked as she forcefully invited herself inside to house.

    "Woah there." Kyle said as she tried to keep her near the porch. "We've just met and you waltz in like we've known each other for longer. Also my mom should here soon so."

    "Hmm? I see. By the way is this too weird?" Amanda asked as if your words were foreign to her. "Like, you are my boy-friend, right? Don't you find it normal that your GIRL-friend comes and visits you? ALSO, don't worry abooout it, I will talk with your mom and everything will be a-okay"

    Kyle sighed and asked Amanda to wait in the living room as he run back to her room.

    "Genie!? Why is my girlfriend like that?" Kyle demanded. "She's not what I had in mind!"

    "I am so sorry my master... I might had misunderstood your wish. I thought by asking for a girlfriend you meant anyone who is single and near you. I am sorry to say but once a wish is made it can't be 'unwished'." The genie apologized.

    "Okay, but can you make her younger?" Kyle asked. "Like I don't want to date someone who could be same age as my mom."

    "Then you have to say those magic words to me, my master, while holding the lamp. Those are the rules." The genie bowed.

    Suddenly Kyle heard shouting in the living room. Kyle peaked from the doorframe and heard that his mom was having a shouting competition with Amanda.

    "Oh dear god... Genie... uhmmm ohmmm... I wish.... I wish that...." Kyle muttered to himseld while holding the lamp in his hand.
