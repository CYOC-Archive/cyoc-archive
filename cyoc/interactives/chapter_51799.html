    Birthday Bash

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 13 years ago BM

    "Been a week already, huh?" Ty asked Peter. They were sitting on Trevor's couch and enjoying the beverages at the now-fifteen-year-old's birthday party. He had received his powers earlier that morning, but was holding off on using them until his friends left- plus he had used them earlier that day, for what Ty was sure he knew. They had returned the boy back to normal after their first meeting, and had hung out with him for a bit during the week. Now they had come to the party to help him with his powers. Michael even came along.

    "So it seems," Peter agreed, "I hope he hasn't used his powers too much... he might have done something he'll regret later." Peter sighed, and took a sip of his soda can. "We'll see for that, though."

    "Well, he seemed normal when we got here, so that's a good sign," Ty commented.
    "When's the party end, again?" asked Peter.
    "In about 30 minutes. I bet Michael's still raiding their fridge, too," Ty said, laughing lightly at his joke.
    "I bet," Peter said, laughing with him.

    The party ended, and Trevor waved goodbye to his friends that came- aside from Ty, Peter, and Michael of course.

    "Well isn't that a shame..." Trevor's mother said, looking into their fridge, "We're all out of food... Your father and I will have to go to the store..."

    Ty stifled a laugh- he had been right about Michael's whereabouts.
    "Hey, Mom? Can I sleep over at Ty's house?" Trevor asked.
    "But we don't want to impose, sweetie."
    "It wouldn't be any imposition, Mrs. Herrickson," Ty said in his most polite tone possible.
    "Then I guess you can, honey. But behave while you're over there!" She warned her son. But he was already gone packing his things.

    The trio soon after arrived at Ty's house. "Well, time to start messing around!" Ty announced as they walked into his room, "I wish Mark and June would fall into a deep sleep and wouldn't wake up until morning."

    "Good idea," Peter said, "But what do we try first?"

    "Well, it is a birthday," Ty suggested, grinning. Peter got the message, and nodded.

    "What? What am I missing?" Trevor asked, confused.

    "You'll see..." Peter said, grinning. Suddenly, he started getting taller, and more muscular. His chin and neck slowly grew hair on them, and his jaw looked squarer. He looked like he was about 25 when he was done. His clothes looked a little tight on him, but he made them grow a little after he was done.

    Ty had changed himself, as well, his changes looking similar to Pete's, though he started gaining fat after a tiny bit. He ended up much fatter than Peter, and looked about a year older, as well as having a goatee rather than a five-o-clock shadow. He also made his clothes grow to fit, which had looked much tighter on him than they had on Peter.

    "Oh, I see!" Trevor said after they finished. He nodded, and started getting taller, as well as filling out a little with muscle. he started getting fatter, too, like Ty, but was more concentrated in his gut than Ty. However, he forwent the facial hair, but did get long sideburns. He looked about 24. His clothes had also become very tight, though he only made his pants grow significantly- his shirt he had only grow a little bit, so he could show off his gut.

    "How about you, Michael?" Ty asked. Mike nodded, and soon found himself growing taller, as he had the first time he met Trevor. However, he was growing a lot fatter, and when he finished, he look about 27, and 600 lbs.

    "Wow!" Mike said, looking at himself.
    "How about we go somewhere, now? No one will recognize us," Peter suggested. His friends nodded. "Where to, then?"
