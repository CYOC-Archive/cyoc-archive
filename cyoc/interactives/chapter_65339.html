    Girl, Interrupted

    The Dragon peered at the stage, scowled, and then turned in on his magnificent cape. In the blink of an eye,
    he was gone. Kylie searched around the room for him with her magic, eventually finding him right behind her,
    beating the snot out of her booki!

    Blast! Kylie had been hoping for The Dragon to run away with Wonder Gal after prying her off the loins of
    Mister Irresistible, but instead he's ruining her plan! Tyler's pathetic attempts at hypnotizing Wonder Gal
    into turning on her teammate were in vain, as Laura now took on her mantle of Wonder Gal seriously. With
    enough willpower, she too turned on Mister Irresistible's animal magnetism, joining The Dragon in apprehending
    him. While she handcuffed him, The Dragon shielded Tyler's cock from further menace by drenching it in an
    synthetic chemical that reversed his power. It slowly dawned on Kylie that her plan had made no sense at all,
    probably because Tyler's pheromones had messed up her head.

    "Hey!" cried out Kylie at The Dragon. "Watch this!". Kylie was about to use her magic to turn The Dragon into
    a dragon butterfly, but The Dragon was wise to her act and simply threw gas pellets her way, immediately
    knocking her unconscious. When she woke up, Kylie found herself...in a prison cell! Wonder Gal and The Dragon
    were standing over her, smirking.

    "Kylie The Sorceress, welcome to Darkgate Prison. Or should I use your real name, Kyle Perkins?" proclaimed
    The Dragon.

    "What the...?"

    "Yes" said Wonder Gal. "We know exactly who you are, Kyle Perkins. You used the Transform Or Dare? curse on
    Jennifer Smith yesterday morning, tricking her into giving you unlimited transfiguration powers over reality.
    We have the phone records. But you left enough of a loophole in your wording for Jennifer to get some
    momentary revenge by turning you into a female version of yourself. It didn't do anything to stop you, we
    know. You adapted very quickly to being female. But you still decided to show up at her party and terrorize
    the school. It was only by your own inexperience with your powers that you created us at all. You, in fact,
    were your own undoing. Or rather, your lack of experience was your own undoing."

    "And now you're here. You won't terrorize anyone again" proclaimed The Dragon. "Your magic powers have been
    turned off, and now you're stuck as an attractive yet physically weak teenage girl. For your own safety, you
    are in solitary confinement. You will receive daily therapy until you are fit to re-enter society."

    The two superheroes stood over her, waiting for Kylie to respond. She didn't. Kylie was in too much shock over
    what happened. Wonder Gal bent down to touch the witches hand, and briefly consoled her. "I do have to thank
    you for one thing. Without you, I could never live out my dream of being Wonder Gal. From one former Glenville
    High student to another, thank you."

    Kylie stared at Wonder Gal with a blank, catatonic, almost dead look in her eyes. Wonder Gal took Kylie's
    witch's hat, which was laying next to her feet, and put it on her head. The Dragon just stared.

    "I suppose I should also thank you...for all you have given me." Kylie stared at the formerly average Robby,
    now bustling with muscles. She knew that beneath his mask held the lady-killer face of Brian Wade, billionaire
    playboy.

    With a moment of silence, the superhero and the superheroine left, locking up Kylie in her own solitude.
    Inside, she was screaming.
