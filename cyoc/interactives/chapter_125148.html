Her nipples vanish as green scales form over her boobs. Her skin around her legs begins to stick together. Lots of green scales form over her tail. Her feet began to web out into a a giant yellowish fin. The last stage of her metamorphosis was a rather loud crunch, her lower bones just lost definition, making her tail extremely flexible.

    It took five minutes for Kendra to catch her breath. Turning into a mermaid without water felt like running several miles without stopping. It’s also now that she realizes she’s still wearing her glasses. She sits up and takes them off, looking at her tail. Her visions blurry now, but it clears up when she’s in the water.

    After jumping in, a smile forms over her face. While transforming into a mermaid wasn’t always pleasant, she’ll never get tired of that pleasurable feeling whenever a mermaid comes into contact with water. She flicks and swishes her tail, dancing underwater. After an hour of swimming, she floats to the surface to relax, not really caring if she’s missing classes.

    Kendra always LOVED being a mermaid, but it took some time for her to get used to it. Since she’s been landlocked her entire life, she only went to the beach whenever she visited her uncle’s private property. Unfortunately, her parents didn’t get along with her uncle, neither did Kendra herself. Still, just being at the beach always felt home to her. She couldn’t explain it, but it’s like every time she’s at the beach, her soul comes to life, like she is one with the ocean. She would always try to recapture that feeling by increasing her bubble bath time at home, but it’s never the same.

    Then, on her 18th Birthday at her uncle’s, Kendra found herself laying down at the beach alone, relaxing in her blue bikini and a red flower behind her ear, along with her new diamond sunglasses she got for her birthday. The sun was high, the sky, the only sound she could hear was waves, the sand was soft, and the smell of sea salt breezed through the air. It was magical. By the time the sun was setting along the orange and pink sky, her soul couldn’t take it no more. Her blue bikini snapped open as her breast expanded and her legs sprouted a mermaid tail. Her yellowish-green scales sparkled brightly against the sun. Kendra gasped in shock, squealing in overjoyous glee. Acting on some mysterious instinct, she bellowed a beautiful, chorus like siren song, one with no words.

    In the present day, Kendra sees that it’s 5:45 pm. Panicking, she climbs out of the pool to dry off. Her changing back into a human takes about the same length, but thankfully not as painful. She puts on her clothes, including her ripped shirt, and walks home.
