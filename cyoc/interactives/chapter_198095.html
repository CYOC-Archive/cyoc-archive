    Morgan smiled at Chloe. "What can I do for you, Chloe?"

    "I've heard rumors that you're a witch," said Chloe. "If that's true, I need your help. I've run away from my abusive aunt. And I don't have anywhere to go. Now, I met this homeless chick who says she tried to join a vampire clan but turned out to be allergic to the transformation venom. Yeah, I know she was probably just on crack or meth or something, but... it spoke to this childhood dream my dead sister and I shared. We'd promised each other that somehow we'd find a way to become vampires and have adventures together. If there are vampire clans out there, that's who I want to take me in. Problem is, I'm not a vampire. And even if I were, I wouldn't have a freaking clue where to find a clan, so..."

    Chloe clasped Tatiana's hand in hers. "Natalie? Natalie Elliott?"

    Tatiana's face split into a joyful grin. "Yeah, it's me, Little Sis! I really did it; I'm an honest-to-God vampire. Only, my name isn't Natalie Elliott anymore. It's Tatiana von Karnstein."

    "Ooh, very vampire-y!" chirped Chloe.
