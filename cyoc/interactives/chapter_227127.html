    Chris and his friends decided to keep their info on the MonsterMe.com website to themselves.

    But they hadn't reckoned on Mr Robbins, the school gym teacher.

    Mr Robbins was a man with a pudgy belly, brown hair, and wearing his trademark black and red striped shirt, brown running pants, and red lace-up sneakers.

    Despite being a teacher, Mr Robbins preferred the students addressing him by his name, Alfie.

    He was on playground duty at the time, and somehow or other, he overheard them talking about the MonsterMe.com website.
    But neither Chris nor his friends noticed him, hiding behind the wall near the supply shed, so no one could see him eavesdropping.

    "A website that tfs people into monsters?" He mused. "This I need to investigate. It might be fun!"

    -----------
    When school ended, Alfie got onto his scooter and rode home to his apartment.

    he typed MonsterMe.com onto his laptop. He went to the site. and a box showed he needed to register. After he registered, he saw a full body picture of himself on the screen, with his name, details, and all other stuff like that.

    He clicked on his full body image, and saw a dizzying array of options to choose from. Options ranging from skin color to scaly or furry, to options for claws, fangs, horns and tails among many other things. He couldn't help but feel overwhelmed by the options. Each option had a sliding bar that allowed him to select the degree of change. There was even color palettes to choose the color for his monster self.

    Alfie's jaw dropped. He had to pinch himself to see if he was dreaming. How could the site do that? What else could it do? Who's running the site and what are they getting out of this?

    All the same, he decided to have a go.

    The first thing he did was make his body increase in size slightly.
    He then removed the picture's clothes, so it would be naked.
    Then he changed his eyes to become solid black and beady.
    Then, he added insect antenna to his head, and changed his hands to have 3 digits on each hand - 2 fingers and a thumb.
    He made 2 arms similar to his own (with the 3 fingered hands) appear from his little round belly.
    He reduces the length of his legs.
    He made his feet have 2 toes on each foot.

    Finally, he turned his monster form's skin bright yellow, with a clammy feel to the skin.

    And he set the awareness settings so that he and only he would see and feel the transformation as it happened.

    then, a message popped up. it said, "Shall we initiate tf now?"
    'Yes/No'

    "Ummm...I think it already did, but sure."
    He clicked on Yes.
