    You get enough satisfaction out of the glasses to put them away for now as you walk out with Casey to join the other girls for lunch. Lillie and Stephanie are chatting it up while enjoying the taco and burrito pack on the table. You figure now is a good enough time to get the girls introduced to each other and see how their lives have changed.

    You introduce Stephanie and Casey to Lillie, who you turned at the supermarket. Lillie met you in high school and had a crush on you instantly, but figured you were like the others that didn’t like skunks, so she kept her distance. During summer vacation of your freshman year, the two of you meet out of town and she opens up her feelings to you. You give her a warm hug and you two start out being good friends. You shrug off all the bullies and haters and everyone slowly develops a genuine respect for you. Just before junior year, the two of you kiss, making you two an official couple, and you two make it public by going to prom together. Since then, the two of you do everything together, she even moves in with you and your parents love her despite her tendency to have atrocious smelling farts and walk around naked all the time. The girls clap and Lillie gives you a kiss. Then Casey goes next.

    Just after graduating high school, you run into Casey, who was trying to get an arts degree so she could be a teacher. Unfortunately, the colleges kept turning her away due to her being a skunk and loving skunky smells a bit too much. With nowhere else to turn, she was forced to live on the streets without a penny to her name. She would spend her time staying in the library as long as she could before getting kicked out, just trying to keep some semblance of her knowledge in tact. You found her after one such occasion getting kicked out of a library. You offer her a place to stay, and Lillie immediately becomes like a big sister to her. You get her a job at Taco Bell so she can have some money to herself, and she makes sure you are rewarded for your efforts. She’s become the employee of the month several times despite wearing nothing but an apron since she’s been hired and is well on her way to managing this restaurant. She couldn’t be happier since she’s met you. The girls clap again, and it’s finally Stephanie’s turn.

    Funny enough, you met Stephanie at a Taco Bell during one of Lillie’s strong craving days. You two weren’t in an area where skunks were known or liked, so it was even more surprising when you heard cacophonous farts and flushing inside the women’s restroom. You tell Lillie to check on it while you order her food. You walk towards the women’s restroom and knock on the door, hoping that Lillie is ok. The door creaks open slightly as a noxious green fog billows out of the door. You are simply blown away by the sheer amount of stink in one room, as if Lillie had stomach problems inside. You take a deep breath and burst inside to find Lillie rubbing the stomach of a skunk twice (maybe even three times) her size, only causing the fog to get more and more dense. It starts to feel sticky as you ask Lillie what’s happening with your hand pinching your nose. She tells you to wait outside as she pushes you out of the bathroom, leaving the skunky fog clinging to your clothes. You step outside and take a minute to air out, changing your shirt and throwing the old one in the trunk. Going back inside to set the food down, you see Lillie walking out with a naked, chubby skunk named Stephanie holding her hand. She introduced herself to you and Lillie and thanked you for your kindness. You ask her if she has anywhere else to go, and she says she doesn’t have a home, so the two of you decide to take her with you. She’s an excellent housekeeper, and an even better pillow whenever any of you get tired, so she usually stays home unless she’s hungry. Out of the three of them, not only is she the youngest and largest, but also the most food-sensitive out of the three. You try to give her a special diet, but just like Lillie, she gets a craving for Taco Bell, and every time she eats it it’ll put her in a bathroom for an hour minimum. She loves her new family and wouldn’t trade it for anything. The girls clap once again as the tray of food is finished.
