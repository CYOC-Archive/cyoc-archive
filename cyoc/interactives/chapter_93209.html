    The door opened and two men entered the bar; one was a very large man whose stomach was big and round and practically hanging over his belt. He had black hair and a beard and a receding hairline, both speckled with grey hair. The other man was thin as a rail and didn't seem that much older than he had been when he was in high school, his blonde hair buzzed at the sides and longer on top.

    "Hey TIna!" the larger man waved jovially at the bar.

    Tina nodded at the big man, "Oh hey Bradly." She then nodded at the thin man. "Hey Mik. The two of you will be having the usual I assume?"

    The big man grunted and nodded.

    "Alright, I'll get to you in a second. I've got a couple of orders to take care of still."

    "There's no hurry" said Mik casually, "You can take your time."

    They both took seats next to Jason who was trying to look inconspicuous to the new arrivals. Impossible given the fact that he was a three year old toddler wearing nothing but a diaper sitting in a bar filled with adults.

    Bradly looked down over at Jason and gave him a friendly grin. "Hey, what's with the kid?"

    "He's a newbie," explained a nearby patron. "He tried the Youthenizer and I guess he must have drank too much"

    Bradly apparently thought it was funny because he then let out a loud boisterous laugh, "No kidding! Nice diaper there little guy."

    Hearing that Jason pulled the pacifier out of his mouth and angrily stuck out his tongue.

    The larger man held up his hands, "Hey there I wasn't trying to be mean or anything. Me and Mik have been down that road as well, haven't we Mik?"

    Mik looked up hearing his name. "The Youthenizer? Oh yeah I remember that stuff. Remember the first time when we tried that Bradly?"

    Bradly nodded. "Yeah, yeah. It was fun at first, but it turns out there's not much you can do around town when you're fifteen years old."

    Mik nudged Bradly. "You were fifteen. I was five. I gotta say it made things pretty awkward."

    Bradly turned his attention back to the toddler. "So what do you think kid? How's the drink?"

    "It's, uh...different." Jason wasn't quite used to just how babyish his voice now sounded.

    "I know what you mean. Just be careful not to drink so much next time and you won't have to spend the whole day in diapers" he chuckled patting the toddler on the back.

    "Actually this is the age I wanted to become so I could wear diapers" Jason admitted blushing a little in the process.

    When he drank the youthenizer he had drank enough so that he'd be young enough for diapers, yet not so young as to be practically helpless so he settled on becoming a toddler.

    "Is that so?" Bradly gave him a thoughtful look as if contemplating something, "Well you've got balls I'll tell you that! What's your name by the way?"

    "Jason"

    "Well nice to meet ya Jason" he said giving the toddler's a friendly handshake which he eagerly returned. Even though he just met these two Jason could tell they seemed like real nice guys.

    At that exact moment Tina came back, expertly carrying two pint glasses filled past the brim with brew but somehow not spilling a single drop. She placed one in front of Bradly and the other in front of Mik. However before he could grab his glass Bradly pushed it away with his hand.

    "Yeah know what? Forget the usual Tina, me and Mik instead will have some..."
