    An anthropomorphic female otter becomes a zoo animal

    added by Raven Cross 2 years ago A Anthro Aquatic

    A 4'5 ft tall anthropomorphic female Giant Otter was standing at the employee access to the otter habitat with a man. This man is the zoo owner. The anthro otter was wearing blue jeans modified for her tail & a pink short sleeve shirt. She was also barefoot (barepawed). She was on a tablet finishing filling out paperwork. Soon she finished & handed the tablet to the man. He checked it over & stated "It all looks right. You are legally a zoo animal now. And our property." The anthro otter purred in happiness. She had her vocal cords completely changed to an otters for this day. The female anthropomorphic Otter stripped out of her pants & shirt making her completely naked. She knew that zoo animals were forbidden to wear any clothing; ever. They had to be naked at all times; period. The zoo owner opened the employee access door & ordered "Get in there Flower." The anthro otter looked confused at the zoo owner as he pushed her through the door a bit forcefully with a forceful "I said get into your new home Flower." Now she got that 'Flower' was her new name. She entered the door. Before the zoo owner told the female anthro otter two things: "You are an animal period. You will be treated as any other otter here from now on. And if you ever try to escape you will be captured like an animal." Flower nodded understanding. She quickly saw the large body of water & the large rock in the middle of the water as well as all the otters. Some of the Giant Otters came up to this newcomer otter. The 4 otters that came up on Flower reared up to sniff her. They were taller than her. She reached out & patted one of them. That otter a male growled at her angry followed by the other 3. Quickly realizing her mistake she quickly vocalize what she knew meant sorry. The male otter didn't accept that & Flower got worried. The male otter pushed her down & then got on top of her still growling. Knowing enough about animal behavior Flower let out a very submissive tone. She didn't try to fight him. Quickly he stopped growling & let out a tone that Flower knew was akin to 'You are mine.' Flower let out a tone that she was akin to agreeing. The male otter now satisfied with this new female (though odd looking to him) learning her place got off her. Flower waited a bit to get up & saw the 4 otters walking away from her. 3 of the otters slipped into the water while the last one (the male one) looked back at her & chirped at her to come on. She came over & hopped into the water. She had never skinny dipped before but she realized that's what she would be doing from now. Being a zoo animal she was forbidden to wear any clothing after all. Very quickly all the otters were chirping & like swimming with Flower. They had accepted her as one of them & Flower realized that. And she couldn't be happier. The zoo owner had watched all of what occurred from the monitoring room. He smiled knowing that Flower was accepted by the other Giant Otters.
