    "Yes...mistress." Jen smirked.

    "Ah yes, well it turns out that Dara got invited to Jessica's big birthday party. Jessica had a favorite party game, guess what it was?"

    "Oh no, Truth or Dare?"

    "Bing-go! She made everyone play, boys and girls. So eventually Dara ended up in a dark closet with Jessica. And so the legend goes, said the following:

    My Curse Upon Ye, Slayer of My Blood! By the Life Ye Took, the Most Precious Thing, I Curse You! Transform or Dare!"

    Jen shivered.

    "Yeah. Well Jessica was scared, but like all rich girls, she wasn't going to back down to anyone. They say she came screaming out of the closet, grabbed the first person she saw, and said "Transform or Dare!" to them.

    A lot of kids turned up missing that night, and the survivors were too shocked to tell the police anything useful. The usual culprits, drugs and Satanism were drug out, but nobody ever saw Dara or Jessica ever again."

    "That doesn't help much..." Paige put her fingers to Jen's lips.

Or maybe after so many times, it loses power. But it's said if you go to the old Calver house in the dead of night, and go into the closet, a voice will say 'Transform or Dare'."

    "Oh God, is that how Kyle got the curse?"

    Paige nodded. "Probably, and there's no telling what he was cursed with...but that trick with the ring probably let him fix his curse."

    Jen got an idea. "No, I bet it didn't. That's why he hasn't gotten the ring back yet. The curse is stronger than the ring!"

    "Could be? Well ok, so how is the curse stopping him from getting the ring back?"

    "Not sure, but I bet that's it. I also bet if we go to the Calver house...he'll be waiting."
