    Entity #: 333
    Codename: The World In the Palm Of Our Hands
    Known Abilities:
    Entity #333 is a small minature globe of the Earth, that rests upon a small polished wood platform inscribed with the initials Y.W.H.

    The first and most obvious feature of Entity #333 is that when picked up by a human being, its size will shrink or grow until it is perfectly capable of being comfortably held by the holder with a single hand.

    When picked up by a new holder, Entity #333 directly transmits information about its power and how to use it into the holder's mind.

    The third trait is its reality altering capabilities. When touched and the speaker states a command starter with the phrase "The world would be better if...", the region of the world that was being touched will experience whatever wish the speaker said. For example if the speaker touched the globe on what would approximately be Paris, France, Paris and everything in the radius proportional to what was being touched on the globe would be affected with rest of the world unchanged.

    The radius of this ability depends on the size of the speaker's finger/which finger used (thumbs would create a greater radius while pinkies would have a much smaller one. If the entire hand is used then the radius would proportionally become just as large as the area covered by the hand.)

    The changes occur instananeously and always occur in a way that does not negatively affect or backfire on the speaker.

    Touching the Entity with an inanimate object such as a pen and attempting to speak a command causes no effect.

    Due to the power of this object there have been harsh restrictions on testing. Because of this, Entity #333's true power and potential is unknown but is theorized to be infinite.

    Any agent that is found with intentions of getting access to Entity #333 is to instantly be apprehended and shortly terminated.

    All attempts to damage or otherwise destroy Entity #333 have failed. It appears to be completely impervious to all methods known to man. It is unknown whether Entity #333 could be used to destroyed itself. All attempts to do so or speak another similar wish that would prevent Entity #333 from being used permanently, have ended with the speaker attempting to enact a different command. Whether this is because the speaker had been intending to betray G.E.T from the start or because Entity #333 manipulates their mind as to ensure its continued existence is unknown.

    Entity #333 requires to be used at least weekly, if it is not used within that time period it will vanish and reappear at a nearby location, usually in an area where someone is likely to come across it.

    Containment:

    Entity #333 is to be stored in a high security vault far away from any human settlements at all times. Its primary storing location being in a small platinum safe surrounded by all sides by highly corrosive acid. Weekly, a 6 with no prior knowledge of Entity #333 is to be escorted by 8 guards into a chamber where the safe containing Entity #333 is lifted and unlocked in front of them. The 6 is to touch a portion of the globe uninhabited by humans, such as the Pacific Ocean, and state a predetermined wish created by G.E.T beforehand for something inconsequential to occur.

    After the wish has been made, the 6 is to be brought into the a different room while the Entity is locked and submerged into acid once more. The 6 is to be terminated on the spot thereafter.

    In case of a containment breach, all G.E.T staff will be put on high alert until the Entity has been safely recovered. All available staff will immediately be dispatched to all nearby towns and search for Entity #333. All search participants involved will always have a death switch implanted in their skulls and will constantly be monitored. Should there be any issue in monitoring, such as a faulty body camera, that employee will be terminated on the spot with cleanup crews being dispatched later and medical records being altered to say they had suffered from a sudden fatal heart attack.

    Other:

    As of now there has only existed one containment breach involving Entity #333, which resulted in the deaths of 94 employees. Only 8 had been terminated for reasons other than them being suspected of attempting to use Entity #333 for themselves.
