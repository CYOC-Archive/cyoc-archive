    Gloria checked her phone, sighing as she saw the time. “Hey guys it’s starting to get kinda late,” she said. “We should probably head home.” Despite some minor protesting the others agreed. As the group of 10 year olds now burst through Gloria’s front door they couldn’t help but laugh as Rexie and Ayami gloomily stood in the doorway.

    “Thanks for letting us go with you Mia,” Gloria said happily, giving the young witching a hug. “Thanks for going with me,” Mia replied. “We definitely had a lot of fun.” Gloria smirked glancing back at her friends who were now sorting through their candy. Glancing down at her ring, Gloria now made a wish to return everyone to their original ages.

    Gloria and her friends gasped as the magic washed over them. Glancing down at their bodies the girls watched as their bodies began to grow, puberty returned to the teens once more as they grew from young girls to teenagers once more. Sighing in relief Sasha now stood, Happy to be back to her old height. “Geez never realized how short I was at 10,” she whispered. The others giggled softly as they now stood and stretched before smiling down at Mia and Zack.

    “Thanks again you two,” Felicia said happily. “Well have to get together again soon.” The two 10 year olds smiled, hugging Gloria and the others as they turned to leave. Gloria watched the two children make their way to the front yard as Ayami and Rexie continued to glare at her and her friends. “Hey you two stay our of trouble,” Sasha said as she now stared eye to eye with Ayami. The succubus gave the teen a mischievous smirk before turning. “Can’t make any promises,” she grinned. “And who knows next time, things might not be so pleasant.”

    Sasha returned the succubus grin as she watched the two demons now head off into the night. “Don’t worry about them Sasha,” Gloria said as she placed her hand on her friends shoulder. “I don’t think we have anything to worry about from those two.”

    “How do you know,” Sasha asked. Gloria could only shrug in response. “Gut feeling, now let’s go check out our haul.”

    Ayami and Rexie sighed as they trailed behind the two excited 10 year olds. After having to play nice for the last couple of hours they were both itching to cause trouble. Unfortunately for the two they had given their word to both Mia and Zack. Maybe when they returned to the other timeline they could cause some chaos. As Mia now began to cast the spell to return Ayami approached her imp companion. “Are you looking forward to returning home as I am,” she asked.

    As Rexie was about to answer she felt an odd tug. Glancing down at herself she noticed another tether trailing off her. Confused the imp turned, shocked to see an adult witch, a grown man, and a teenage girl. “Mia,” Rexie asked, slightly confused. The adult Mia smiled, raising a finger to her lips to quiet the familiar. When the young Mia and Zack vanished to the other timeline the two adults stepped forward, Yuriko trailing behind them.

    “Wanted to see you before you headed back,” Mia said happily.

    Ayami and Rexie could only gape at the two adults, unable to comprehend what they were seeing. “Before you ask it’s time travel,” Mia stated. “Really can’t go into the details but wanted to say hi and thanks, for everything you’ve ever done for me. Just, try to stay out of trouble okay?”

    “Goes double for you Ayami,” the adult Zack smiled. “Thank you for always looking out for me but try not to get into too much trouble.” Ayami and Rexie could only nod drunkenly as they now vanished into the other timeline. Both demons couldn’t help but be shocked and happy. It seemed the future Mia and Zack were doing well thanks to them.
