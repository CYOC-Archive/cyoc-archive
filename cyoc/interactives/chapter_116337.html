    The next morning, the dog butler began to appear in each room and quickly dismiss the Johns who had purchased the girls time the previous night. The girls were then given a drink that turned them back into human form. Then it was time for breakfast.

    Alexandra and Caroline were the first up. It wasn't even clear if their John, the horse businessman, had stayed the night. As they sat down at the table they shared an awkward look.

    "So.." said Caroline.

    "I know what you're thinking. God last night felt incredible." Said Alexandra.

    "I know! It feels awful to admit, because of how disgusting this all is. But man, that horse dick really does a number on you!" Caroline admitted.

    The girls shared an awkward laugh before getting some food, carrots and some dry oats. It appeared that some horse instincts hadn't left them entirely.

    Next, the bovine Abigail, elephantine Lexi, and rhino Hanna were led in. They drank their serums and returned to human form right before everyone's eyes. Hanna and Lexi hugged before heading to the showers. After sitting down, Abigail was the first to speak.

    "So was that as good for you guys as it was for me?" She asked.

    The other girls nodded with slight smiles on their faces. They knew they shouldn't have enjoyed this as much as they did.

    Finally the six pig girls were led into the room. Or rather four pig girls and 2 regular pigs were led in. They were administered their serums and within a few moments the fat pigs returned to their human forms of Antonia, Alyssa, Rebecca and Tess. It took a bit longer for the two sows to revert to their original forms of Kirstin and Beasley. The two girls looked at each other awkwardly as they were on all fours upon reverting. Unlike the rest of the girls, none of the former pigs went to the showers upon finishing, they all headed straight to the breakfast table and each took a hearty helping of food.

    "Ugh. You guys smell awful!" Lamented Hanna.

    "I dunno, I think we smell pretty good, you guys are the ones that smell funny to me!" Antonia said with a laugh with letting out a fart. The other former pigs joined in her snorting laughter. None of the other girls did.

    Lexi was more interested in what had happened to Kirstin and Beasley: "Hey what gives? You guys were just full on pigs!"

    "Oh yeah, our guys wanted that. They called it going feral." Beasley said.

    "Excuse me! My guy wanted that, you invited yourself along and ended up fucking him later in the night!" Kirstin said.

    "So what? I was a mindless sow!" Beasley spat back. "You're really that protective of Mr. Redneck Pig Dude? It's not like you didn't fuck my pig too."

    "I guess you're right..." Kirstin said, a little embarrassed of how into things she had gotten.

    The dog butler reappeared and spoke up. "Ladies, your days are free to spend however you like within the confines of the mansion. The entirely of our amenities are available to you until 6:00 PM tonight. Please do enjoy your time." Said the dog. "And to our former sow ladies, please do shower after breakfast. That is not a request."

    "Aww man!" Said Rebecca.

    And so the ladies began their first free day of their new lives, with no idea of where things would be taking them next.
