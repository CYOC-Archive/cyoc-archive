    Animal Control collects you & takes you to the pound

    added by Drakkenfyre 8 years ago A O

    A white truck pulls up, and a man in a brown uniform gets out. He's got a badge and the patches on his sleeves read "ANIMAL CONTROL".

    "Let's see," he says bending down, and inspecting your collar, "Just as I thought no license."

    He unhooks your leash, opens the door on the back of the truck. Inside there are animal crates. Some have dogs in them. Some have cats. Some are empty. He opens the nearest empty crate, and tugs on your leash. You hesitate.

    "In boy," he orders and you obey. He swats your butt as you enter the stinky cage. He closes the crate and latches it. Then he slams the doors on the back of the truck. A minute later, the truck is roaring down the street.

    As Braiden comes out of the store with his bag filled with his purchases, he sees the white truck pull away. Then he notices you are missing. He turns and asks a homeless guy sitting by the store entrance.

    "Where's my dog boy?" he asks.

    "Not licensed, or at least no tags. Animal Control took him. Can you spare a few bucks?"

    Braiden reached in his pocket and handed the homeless guy a couple wadded up bills.

    As he walked away, Braiden mumbles, "Kyle you're more trouble than you're worth."
