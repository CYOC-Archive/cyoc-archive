    Jake shouted "Calm down, everyone! We won't be able tO do anything if we keep flailing about!"
    Jason, Josh and Jim agreed. However, the three new boys were clearly horrified and were still shouting.

    Joe angrily replied, "this is all your fault, you freaks! If you just me alone, we wouldn't be in this mess."

    Jason retorts, "Excuse me!! You're the one who started calling us freaks!"

    Jim cries, "Yeah!" and slaps Joe's head. Joe kicks his leg and hits Jake's leg.

    Jake shouts, "Hey watch it!"

    One of the new kids in the back screams hysterically, "Why is this happening?!!" he flails his arms about and hits Jim and Josh.Jim and
    Josh respond by slapping the two kids in the back. Soon, all the boys were slapping, pushing, and screaming at one another.

    Lines were drawn. It was Jake, Jason, Josh and Jim on one side. Joe and the 3 new boys on the other. The whole school stood lookig
    at this huge mass of boys fighting on the floor. The crowd was afraid to do anything since they may get absorbed too.

    Joe and his gang tried to pull away from Jake's team. Jake responded by going the other direction. Their enormous body was pulled by
    8 different minds. Jake was starting to worry. He is losing control of this body. There's too much conflict. Suddenly, he felt a tingling. His
    brothers felt it, too. Joe was still too busy trying to pull to notice.

    The body was expanding again. It stretching and suddenly the two groups separated! Joe and his gang were on one body. Jake and
    brothers were on another body. Jake of course was relieved.

    Jake said "Okay so everyone who matters still here?!"
    Jason responds, "Yup. Four heads, four arms, two legs. Yup."
    Josh said "Present! Although I'm beside Jim now. "
    Jim said, "you're a better shoulder than pimple face Joe anyway"

    After the separation, Jake and his brothers were rearranged in their new body. Jake was now on the left and controls the legs and front
    left arm. Josh was on the right controlling the front right arm. Jason was behind Jake and controls the back left arm. Jim was behind
    Josh and controls the back right arm.

    Joe and his gang shared only 2 hands and 2 legs. Joe angrily, "Great! I'm still a freak!"
    The 3 three new kids didn't take that too well. they started to slap Joe.

    Joe screams "Enough! I'm in charge here!!!" The other boys stop. Suddenly, they seem to freeze in place.
    Joe smiles maliciously. "Good! Now to take care of some pests." He looks towards Jake. "You're going to pay for what you did to me."
    Joe has taken control of the body. The other boys' heads were shriveling like raisins. Joe's body was also growing. Soon, he was
    towering over the other students. His head crashed and broke the ceiling.

    Jake nervously remarks, "This is not good."
    Jim replies, "Now may be a good time to run." The other students and teachers were already running away in fear of the giant Joe.

    Joe screams at Jake, "Now you're mine!!!"
