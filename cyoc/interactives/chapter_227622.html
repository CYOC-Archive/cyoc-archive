    Jake goes to see his parents and finds out a secret

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago AP BM Muscle

    Jake decided let Elliot have his own fun at the contest and decided to go find his parents instead. He had to wonder if they would even recognize him as he was now. "I doubt mom and dad would even know me if they looked at me. I don't even look anything like dad now." Jake said to himself as he looked around the room before departing and grabbed his cell phone and key card before leaving.

    Jake left his hotel room and began to wander through the hotel looking for his young parents, they had to be around 21 or so and he also had to remember that he couldn't exactly call them 'mom' and 'dad' right now as to them, he would seem more like a brother or even a friend. As he walked through the hotel, he did see that he was getting several looks from the hotel staff and even some of the other guests were looking his way. There were two pregnant women in bikinis looking at him with a smirk and he even saw two girls at 10 years old looking at him.

    "I may be ten, but I know I'm really forty and that guy is a total 10 out of 10." One of the ten year old girls said.

    Jake blushed a little bit feeling embarrassed, he like the attention he was getting though it did feel a little weird getting compliments from children even if they were actually older than the age they were. As Jake began looking outside for his parents, he actually noticed his mother, Julia, looking a little sad and he didn't see his father, Adrian anywhere in sight.

    "Uh, excuse me miss, are you feeling okay?" Jake asked in his deep voice. "Oh, I wish I were feeling okay. I came on this stupid vacation with my sons and husband and now my dumb husband found a friend of his and they've been talking with each other for the last two hours. God, I can't believe our therapist thought this would be a good idea." Julia said.

    "You're going to therapy?" Jake asked not knowing his own parents had been going to therapy. "Yeah, we've been keeping it secret from our sons so they can enjoy themselves here. But honestly I haven't actually been having any fun yet." Julia said.

    "Yeah, honestly on a hotel where you can transform yourself into just about anything you want. I haven't been having too much fun yet. I mean I just got out of my hotel room and look at me here I am talking with a young beautiful woman who doesn't know what to do with herself." Jake said to Julia.

    Julia turned her head finally to look at Jake and her eyes were astonished at the man in front of her. Her face then looked to form a thought as she looked to her young husband who was still talking to his friend.

    "You know what? why don't you and I go do something together for fun? It might be exciting to have fun with a total stranger than a family member. Oh, what was your name?" Julia asked.

    "Oh, I'm....Jay. You can just call me Jay." Jake said to Julia. "Cute name, you can call me 'Julie'." "So Julie, where do you want to go? There's a muscle contest on the beach or we could go to the bar?" Jake or Jay asked Julia or Julie who looked to be thinking on the matter.
