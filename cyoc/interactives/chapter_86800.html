    Mary gasped as she watched the ring appear on her new finger. She turned, her eyes bright and wide to Katie, then threw her arms around her friend. "Oh Katie! Thank you! You don't know what this means to me!"

    Katie smiled, "Your ... well your the closest thing I've ever had to a best friend. I trust you with my life....and I want to share everything with you."

    Mary blushed, combing back a few errant strands of hair. "Thank you. Is it OK if I try it out?"

    "Yes of course, but who are you going to try it on?" Katie asked.

    "Your mom." Mary said. Noticing Katie's surprise she held up her hands. "I know its tough for you to want to make a wish on her, because your afraid. I think I can word one just right so that she's one of us, and nothing bad will happen to you. Scout's honor!"

    Katie pondered her friend's words. She had been tempted to do it herself, but was still afraid. Finally she nodded. "Alright Mary. I trust you. Make the wish."
