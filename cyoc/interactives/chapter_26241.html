    Kats work gets graded

    added by Ape-Boy 16 years ago A

    "So what now?" Dan asked his sister while playing with his new tail.
    "We have to leave quickly first we have to convince mom that I dealt with you then when she thinks I'm on her side we can both sneak out, with my new powers we will be able to survive quite luxuriously. Only problem is what happens when mom and the rest of the family find out we left..."
    "We'd be screwed" Dan now had managed to be able to lift his tail slowly but still lacked fine mother skills in it.

    As all this was going on their mother was swiftly moving though the nether-space about to arrive inside their room.

    The next fem minutes they went over their story and what they were going to do after.
    "Ok so when mom goes to sleep you know what to do right?" Kat asked Dan who nodded in agreement. As Kat went to the door to find her mother a dark cloud appeared on the floor. It swirled upwards creating portal out of which their mom flew out screaming and landing on her back.

    "Stupid nether-space portals I'll never get used to them" she said as she cleaned herself off. Looking around the room she judged Kat's work.
    "Hmmm no struggle, Dan seems to be submissive but ill have to test that, and book was thoroughly read... Kat I'm surprised at you" Kat looked very nervous. how had she found out so quickly? "You did a excellent job I thought you would just cast the first spell in the book you found but you head a large portion of it and found a spell that is almost impossible to reverse!" Dan glared as Kat when he heard that bit of news.

    "But for your actual spell I'll have to give you a "C+" you left him far too human." pulling out her wand she points it at Dan "Hes much too big your pets should always be smaller then you" with a wave of the wand Dan shrinks a foot "His posture is more human then I would like" With a second wave Dan's arms and legs burn causing him to fall to the ground, but he quickly gets up but realizes his arms are longer and his legs are shorter "And you left him with a shred of dignity" Dan's clothes disappeared into white smoke, and in his a banana appeared.

    Dan couldn't take it anymore he was angry and humiliated. He jumped at his mother trying to grab the wand.
    "Kat you didn't restrain him? that drops you another letter grade he could of attacked you at any moment." she said raising her wand and making a swift downward motion with it. As Dan pounced a Cage appeared in a poof of red smoke causing him to slam into the bars.
    "Hay let me go I don't want to be a caged animal" Dan screamed
    sighing Kat's mother turns to her "And he can still speak? Kat you obviously didn't try very hard Your lucky I'm not making you try again on your boyfriend. Dan was still screaming to be let out and for someone to help him when his mother glanced over at him and pointed her wand directly at his mouth. Dan fell back as if the air was knocked out of him. Regaining his breath he tries to speak but all that came out was a series of hoots and screeches. Dan covered his mouth in surprise, removing his hand he attempted to say a word any word but all that came out were disappointed "oo"s.

    "Much better" their mom says "Now I got you a gift" she says holding out a ominous looking black box.
    "What is it?"she says opening the box and finding a collar.
