    "Come on we got to get to magic island where they can't catch you." The man said in a paniced tone. I was unsure what to do, so he just grabbed me by my waist and carried me downstairs to a van. At first it felt like some kind of molester kidnapping a child kind of thing, but when he sat me down in the van, I saw other people waiting there.

    "I thought you said the kid was 14 years old, not 4?" A woman in the back said.

    "He must have figured out his powers. Now where are the keys?" The man said. A dude in the passenger seat passed the keys to the man and he turned on the car and slammed on the gas peddle.

    "Where are we going?" Another boy in the van said.

    "We are going to get on a plane that will get us the closest to magic island." The man said, " Lucy use your creation magic and make us passports and tickets to England."

    "Got it!" The woman in the back said. She started making some sparkles and slips of papers and books started apearing out of no where.

    "Hey, John is it?" The man said to me, "I'm going to need you to change your age back to normal. The passport will throw things off if they see a 4 year old version of you."

    "I need clothes." I said softly. No one except for the guy in the passenger seat heard me. "The kid said he needs clothes." "I'm on it" Lucy said.

    I was handed a pair of shoes, socks, shorts, and a shirt. I stared at the woman to let her know she forgot something.
