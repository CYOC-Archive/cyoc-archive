    The next morning found Amber in a place she would never dare to have ever dream; she was now a succubus which was already strange enough considering her family which ran a highly religous mega-church but she was also lying beside her best friend now lover Katie. Katie was a succubus as well and the two of them had just had the most interesting night they had ever had. Amber sat up and looked around the room, pieces of clothing where over the floor and while they hadn’t used the handcuffs or blindfolds which were in the night stand but it was still an night of first.
    Amber slowly got up and slipped back on her night gown and looked back at the sleeping Katie and smiled, she was her best friend, now she was her lover and maybe much much more than either of those. Slowly she walked over and looked at the time it was nearly lunch time, they had to talk to their friends to decided what the next step would be. Slowly she walked over and shook Katie awake, she knew from sleep overs in their youth that Katie was hard to wake up. Finally she did.
    “Sorry, but time to get up everyone is probably wondering where we are.” Explained Amber as Katie nodded before going up on her elbows and kissing her again.
    “So tonight?” inquired Katie as she pulled back.
    “Sure.” Confirmed Amber “Your room or mine?”
    “Mine.” Said Katie with a grin. “I have some ideas.”
    “Good.” Said Amber as both of them got dressed. They opened the door to see Jade and Molly standing there about to knock on the door.
    “Well, that is where they where.” Stated Molly
    “Interesting development.” Stated Jade with a smile. “I would ask did you use protection but the goal is to get pregnant.”
    “But…” started Amber then she remembered that Succubus could get pregrant through same sex relationships. She didn’t know how that worked, she would have to find that out.
    “I think it is time we used our little announcement plan.” Advised Molly “My Parents called this morning, I told them the car lie but that will not last another day.”
    “I told my parents the truth.” Admitted Jade with a shrug, that wasn’t unexpected Jade’s parents where only members of the church because her father’s IT job with the church’s IT department required it, unofficially of course. Neither of Jade’s Parents where believers in the faith and they had let their daughter be a normal women. “Truth be told my mom wasn’t found of Succubus, she would rather have been mermaids until I explained the computers and water do not mix well.”
    “That is another thing we need to focus on, finding jobs we are going to be staying here so we need jobs. We can’t treat this like a vacation.” Advised Katie
    “Besides those bible camps none of us have really had a vacation.” Countered Molly before looking at Jade. “Well besides you.”
    “I am an outlier I know.” Confirmed Jade “So are we agreed, we make the call and then go see about some jobs.”
    “Agreed.” Confirmed Amber as the other two nodded their heads. Jade pulled her cell phone and made the call.
    They had been planning this for months and as such they had planed for when their parents found out they had abounded the church. The abandonment of three daughters of major mega church leaders wasn’t something to go unnoticed or even un-hunted. There where some cases of people being forced back into the church. There was a possibility their parents may try to find them in some way. So they decided to turn to a lawyer to hide where they went.
    For Miss Jessica Taylor she had a mission to destroy the mega churches she had once been apart of, the almost cult like faith they preached and the almost total control over the area was something she hated. So when three daughters of church leaders had asked for her help in escaping she was more then willing, she didn’t really approve of becoming creatures to escape but if it was the only way so be it. She took the call in her office and smiled, when she got the green light to announce that the daughters of church elders had run away.
    “Press conference or should I go to the Parents first?” inquired Miss Taylor
