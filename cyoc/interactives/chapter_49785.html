She opened her mouth to tell him what happened, but the words that came out weren't what she wanted. "Hey, Jake, I've got a great idea. You know that game coming up against Carolstown High? The undefeated team? Well, I have a great idea. All you have to do is let me Transform or Dare you, and take both, I'll make you the best high school football star in the world. I just got it from a friend, it'll work great!"
