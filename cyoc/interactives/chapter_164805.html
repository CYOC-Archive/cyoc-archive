    Briana is a 30 year old unemployed woman who lives with her parents. She felt like a very unlucky woman, as she never had a boyfriend even though many praised her beauty. This would not be a problem for her, especially if she got a stable job, but for some reason she always ended up working for companies that ended up being closed due to some illegality. She lived with her parents because she was unable to pay a rent, but she was tired of them charging her to get a good job and go find a place to live on her own.

    To save as much as possible, Briana rarely leaves home for anything, until something totally random happened and could be the lifeline for her. She was playing some card games online until in one of the games an old acquaintance ended up entering the same table as her, that was Emily, a former classmate from school days and that she hadn't had contact in a long time. (Emily had put an image of her favorite band in place of the photo, but Briana recognized her first and last name.)

    "Are you Emily? How long have we not seen each other. It's me Briana, we studied together until high school." Briana said in the table chat

    "So it was you. I already imagined it, but your hairstyle and hair color are different from when we last met and I was sure it was you. So, how are you?" Emily said

    "I would like to say that I am great, but I can't find a good job that will allow me to become independent from my parents." Briana said

    "It is a pity to know that. But I can help you with that because where I work they are looking for new employees and they pay very well. If you don't mind working on a farm I can help you get hired." Emily said

    "As long as it's a decent job, I'm accepting anything." Briana said

    "I promise you will not regret it." Emily said

    During the match they entered the private conversation section, where Emily passed on information about the farm where she worked. Briana wrote everything down and promised that the next day she would be there.

    The next day, Briana went to the farm where Emily works, where she met a young girl and said "Hello, I'm sorry if I'm disturbing you, but I just heard that you are hiring here."

As soon as the girl stopped in front of her, Briana watched her more carefully and realized that she had hooves where her feet should have been. Looking at her in the face, Briana realized that she also had a pair of horns on her head, and then a movement behind the girl caught her attention and she looked to see that the girl had a cow's tail.

    "Yes, we are hiring. May I know how you heard about us?" the girl said, not caring how Briana looked at her bovine parts.

    "I ended up talking to an old friend yesterday and she said she worked here, you must know Emily." Briana said, trying to act as normal as she could

    "So you are the friend Emily mentioned that she could come here today. Since this is the case, I'll let her show you the farm before you confirm that you really want to work here." the girl said, turned on her back and shouted "Emily, come here. Your friend is here."
