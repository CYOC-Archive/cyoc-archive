    The Dark God

    "Hello? Is this thing on? Oh, there you are my little human. Don't speak now I have a lot to explain." A dark voice in your mind says. Though right now you couldn't really react, it was like your mind was completely shut off and you could only listen as the voice in your head continued.

    "You can call me whatever you like, Satan, The Devil, or maybe even Chaos incarnate. But regardless soon my time will be up." the voice suddenly went quiet for a moment that felt like eternity before it spoke again. "Anyway, He will soon put my spree to an end and the cosmic balance cannot have something like that happen without me having something to say about it. So before I die I want to share the small remainders of my divine power all across the multiverse so new others can pick up where I left off and rise to power as a God!" The voice exclaims.

    "Now you won't be as powerful as you might think, but given some time, strategy, and whole bunch of worship from mortals your influence will spread across the multiverse like a plague. Unless of course you get taken out by a rival. But that won't happen now will it?" The voice says, childishly giggling to itself as it says that.

    "Anyway I don't have much time left so let's wrap this up quickly now, eh? So basically if you accept you get to ascend to Godhood and spread your influence across the mulitverse and become all powerful. Whatever you decide to do with that power is up to you but I think we all know what you'll do. Since I'm assuming you'll accept I'll help you get started with all the paperwork."

    "Now... which divine domain of Creation do you want? Whichever one you choose will give you full control over that domain so choose carefully! Might I add, whatever domain you choose will heavily affect your appearance afterwards."

    What do you do now?
