    The apartment door opening sounded like booming thunder to the two doll sized men. Although they had been climbing around over their giant possessions for a while, they spent most of the time in the dollhouse among the things on their own scale. Something like the door opening, the regular world intruding into their tiny hideaway, was terrifying. From inside the house neither one of them could see who it was.

    "It must be Sheryl," said Steve. "Or Billy." He was torn. These were people he trusted, but at the same time the thought of seeing them as 40 foot giants made his stomach churn with nervousness. David started looking around for his discarded loincloth. If it was Sheryl he didn't want her to see him naked. They were both naked having given up on the doll clothes. But they had discarded them elsewhere and there was nothing to cover up with as the echoing footsteps crossed the room toward their little house. The two little men looked at each other uneasily. "I guess we should go out there," Steve said. "We did shrink out of our clothes after all. They won't care if we're naked."

    "That's easy for you to say," said David. "Sheryl's your girlfriend and Billy's on your team. They see you naked all the time. But I guess it doesn't matter." He led the way to the exit door, leaving Steve to shudder about the image that had arisen of himself showering with his team.... but him the size he was now with the rest of them towering naked giants. Someone just had to find a way to grow them back!

    A pair of giant legs were squatting just outside the dollhouse door. Male legs in jeans, the bulge quite prominent at the end of the valley they created, which the little men inadvertently walked right into. But this wasn't Billy come to rescue them.

    It was Scott!

    "Uh, Scott, uh....." David stammered. "How did you get in here?"

    "Door was unlocked," boomed the giant. He was grinning down at the two of them as if they were his birthday present. "I was coming by to see if you wanted to go to that new sex exhibit at the museum but I guess you two have that covered." He raised his eyebrows.

    "You were never normal, David," Scott smirked. "But tell me what happened."
