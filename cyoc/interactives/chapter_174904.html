    Jeff smirked as he thought of what the world would be like if he made everyone a toddler again. Could the planet still even function with the human race as a bunch of babies? Curious Jeff located the select all stat box for the entire Earth, which had the options for race, age, gender, etc. Clicking on age he made the age range for the entire planet between 2-3 so everyone would either be a 2 or 3 year old toddler. His heart pounding with excitement Jeff clicked MAKE CHANGES.

    Suddenly the room around him began to grow as he started to shrink. Jeff looked down at his body to see it getting younger, his clothes changing along with his age. When he finished regressed Jeff saw he was now a 3 year old wearing only a shirt and a Pampers disposable diaper, the image of Elmo smiling up at him from his crotch. Happy to have landed on the older end of the spectrum Jeff, "Holy crap, it really worked! I'm a baby again!" he said in surprise, grinning at his younger voice. Looking around his room he saw it had remained relatively unchanged, with the exception of his bed being replaced with a crib and a fully stocked changing table to the right of his dresser.

    Eager to see what happened to the rest of his family Jeff hopped off the chair and toddled out of the room. Though his legs had become slightly unsteady due to the youthening he quickly got used it. However the diaper between his legs forced him to adopt a bit of a waddle, in addition to making crinkling sounds with every step he took.

    Leaving his room Jeff's first stop was his sister's room. Opening the door a crack and peeking through he saw his sister sitting on the floor, playing with some dolls. She was now a 2 year old toddler, her hair done up in little pigtails and wearing a dress with a short skirt, seeing a glimpse of a diaper underneath. Glad to see he was still the older sibling, if only barely, Jeff suppressed a giggle when he noticed she too now had a crib and changing table. Leaving before she noticed his snooping Jeff moved on. Taking a look at the upstairs bathroom he saw that there was only a bathtub and sink, no toilet whatsoever, which made sense since the whole world wasn't potty trained anymore. Before heading downstairs Jeff took a quick look inside his parents room, finding the same differences.

    Carefully descending the staircase one step at a time with one hand on the railing Jeff entered the living room to find his mom and dad both there. His father, now a 2 year old was sitting on the couch. The toddler was wearing only a diaper and sucking his thumb as he watched the news which featured a regressed newscasters wearing baby sized suits. Oddly enough this made sense as it was Saturday and his father sometimes went shirtless on the weekends, so it was only natural his baby self would do the same. His mom was like him a 3 year old and dressed in a pink shirt with her diaper out in the open, her long hair coming down to her shoulders. She was currently sitting on the floor and playing with an assortment of toys.

    Jeff figured she was the best person to talk to, and was about to when a human shaped robot walked into the room carrying a basket full of laundry. Setting it down on the floor next to the couch it began folding the clothes which he just barely managed to notice were all toddler clothes. The robot looked almost exactly like the ones from the that iRobot movie but without the creepy human face. Instead it just had a lightly blue glowing v- shaped visor where its eyes would be.

    Stunned Jeff quickly figured that in order for the human race to survive being regressed they'd need robots to help keep things running. Although he had mixed reservations about having a machine change his diaper.
