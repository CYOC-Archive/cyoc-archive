    He blamed the virus, for he believed she must be acting under it's influence - otherwise she would never do something like this, so he thought.

    Queen Maria still loved John, and she still wanted to marry him. She gave orders to all her loyal subjects not to infect him, but to bring him to

    Today, he was on patrol through the now ruined city.

    With him was Rene, a 27-year-old black woman with her hair tied back in a ponytail.

    She had been recently assigned to be his new partner after the virus had claimed his last one, so she was more than a bit nervous.

    "We haven't seen any of the goat-freaks all day." Rene said as they walked in an alleyway "I tell you John, I've got a bad feeling in the back of my

    "Just keep your cool, Rene." John told her "As long as we stay level-headed about this, we're fine."

    As soon as they got to the end of the alleyway, a satyr jumped out at them.

    He had green eyes, and thick, red hair covering his whole body except for his face. He had a small goatee. He was about 27.

    "Hey there, good lookn'." he said to Rene "I'm Greg. Do you have a name to go with that beautiful body of yours?"

    her if we found you, which I just did, and don't worry about ME transforming you - she's reserved that pleasure for herself. You coming?"

    "He's not going anywhere with you!" Rene spat at Greg.

    "Yes, I'm coming." John replied.

    "What?" exclaimed Rene "Why John? You're one of our best!"

    "This could be my chance to see her." John said to her "If I could talk some sense into her, she'd call this whole thing off, and we could win
    this!"

    "No," Rene said "I won't let you go with him."

    "Actually," Greg said, smiling cleverly "you're going to help me take him there."

    "Why would I do that?" Rene asked.

    "Did you feel a small prick in the back of your neck a minuet or two ago?" Greg asked.

    "Yes." she replied "Why?"

    She then tried to shoot herself, but her gun became a set of panpipes with a shoulder strap. Her ears then popped into goat ears with black
    hair to match the hair on her head, and freckles appeared on her face.

    "Sorry beautiful," Greg said to her, as goat horns erupted from her forehead and curled back around her ears "but I can't have you wasting
    yourself like that. I hope you understand."

    She then turned toward John.

    "Listen to me John." she said as her hair grew past her waist "You don't have to do this. Just run - NOW!!"

    "I'm sorry Rene," John said as her fingernails became hard and black "but if there's even the slightest chance that I can get through to her, I

    "Aww, that's sweet." Rene said as her pupils became rectangle-shaped "Her Majesty is very fortunate to have a man who loves her so much. I
    just wish I had a man like that."

    "Maybe I can grant that wish." Greg said slyly. He took her by the hand, hugged her, and kissed her passionately on her lips. This sped up the
    process, and caused her to burst out of her clothes.

    She was now a full satyress, with thick black hair covering all but her face, stopping at the top of her neck.

    John followed them to a helicopter.

    Greg got in the driver's seat, Rene sat next to him, and John sat in the back.

    "I'm sorry you got dragged into this, Rene." John said to her.

    "Don't be." she said smiling "Now I've got me a man of my own, and he's soo strong!"

    "Would you mind not distracting me while I'm piloting, beautiful?" Greg asked with a nervous smile.

    "Sorry cutie." Rene replied smiling "I promise I'll be a good girl - for the rest of the fight anyway. Hee-hee!"

    They flew all the way to Athens, Greece, where Queen Maria's place was. It was very big and ornate obviously constructed by experts.

    When they got there, it was a huge room with pleasing fragrances, and soft carpets couches and pillows, and lying on the largest couch at the

    necklace, and a leafy laurel on top of her head. As she laid there, eating grapes (and the seed with the branches too) from a large basket of

    "Thank you, Greg." Maria said "You've done well. Now please, leave so that the two us can be alone together."

    "As you wish, your Majesty." With that, Rene could no longer contain herself - she jumped on Greg's back, and the two raced out of the room,
    and down to one of the unoccupied lower rooms were they could get to "know" eachother better. Rene could be heard giggling all the way.

    "Come here, my lover." Maria said, smiling and playfully motioning with her finger.

    John sat on the left side, while Maria was on his left, lying on her front, and looking up at him.

    "So you've finally given up, have you?" she asked playfully.

    "I haven't given up on saving humanity, or you." he bravely replied.

    "Hee-hee! Save ME?" she asked with a look of mirth on her face.

    "I know that you are under the control of the virus, and that it's making you do all this." he told her "The Maria I know would never act like this."

    "Eee-hee-hee! I guess you don't know me as well as you thought you did!" she said, grinning like a little girl.

    "What do you mean?" he asked.

    "Silly John." she replied, smiling and batting her eyelashes at him "This is what I wanted from the beginning. Mankind is so corrupt and cruel. It
    was on a path of self-destrution. When I heard the stories about the vase containing the ancient magic to repopulate the satyr race, I jumped at

    follow gave me the courage to smile again. However, in spite of all I've gained, I still lack one thing - a husband to rule beside me as the new
    satyr king. We will have many children, as will all our kind. I told you we'd get married and have the happiest wedding anyone's ever seen. This
    is exactly what I had in mind right from the start!"

    "If the virus keeps spreading," John said "humans as a species will become extinct, and nothing will be left to remember of it. I came to speak
    with you, hoping you'd listen to reason and stop this before it's too late. Please Maria."

    "I do," he replied "and that's why I can't let you do this. Please. It's not fair to the humans that are left if they don't want it."
