    As Jacob the pilot climbed inside his jet fighter Matt, they heard a shout. "Hey what are you doing here?", a figure in ragged clothes, armed with a broken shovel shouted and seemed eager to attack. Jacob grabbed the transformation gun, aimed and quickly zapped the intruder.

    He quickly became immobile as his body expanded and forced him into a sitting position with his legs angled in front of him. His arms were forced out to his sides and became flatter before they split into two. Gradually his face flattened except for his nose tip which became more pointy. His thighs merged to his body leaving him sitting, leaving him sitting on his bum and his feet. Then his body turned more tube like and into a brownish green. His head, now essentially a cylinder with a prop sticking out of it was a bit larger than the rest of his tube-like body. Along the border to his body there was a set of rivets connecting it firmly. A hole started to appear on his back, caving in and revealing a full cockpit as a narrow windshield and a set of crosshairs pushed up. His feet turned into small wheels, easily holding the front part of his weight while the legs pierced through his wing-arms holding them in place. On the top pair of wings two pipes started to appear. Gradually the back of his body curved upwards and formed a flat surface to form the tail rudders. In all of this a wheel had formed where his groin had been, also lifting him off the ground. As the final touch a painting of a jolly rogers flag appeared on the tail rudder.

    Matt, who had meanwhile turned into his hybrid form which looked a bit like a human wearing a jet-pack, meanwhile leaped into the cockpit where he started to work on the computer controls to make the plane loyal to him. "Jee thanks boss," the plane exclaimed, but Matt continued to work on the menus, defining proper "human" form for the plane.

    Meanwhile Jacob came closer to the plane and asked, "So what's your name?". "Hal", was the answer.
