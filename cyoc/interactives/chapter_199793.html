    That night after Caleb and Gloria had met with Rei and Nat, they went to the Moreland home for a nice dinner with the Moreland family. Julia Harris had come over to check on Jacob Moreland once again, but she also had come by to help Rick Moreland out with the family dinner as the two of them had hit it off well on their first date and decided to see each other again.

    "I can't believe you two hit it off so well." Gloria said surprised at Rick and Julia.

    "Well what did you expect to happen?" Julia asked.

    "I don't know, there's either some kind of magical age shenanigan that goes on or a family member spies on you to see how you're doing." Gloria said.

    "Like hell I'd ever do that." Jacob Moreland said as he got beer out for him and his brother.

    "Ah, ah! No beer before dinner, either of you." Julia said taking the beer from them and putting it back in the fridge.

    "It's just a little beer." Jacob argued.

    "Yeah, but you've been gaining a little weight since you....'retired'. Besides, I don't think Nina would approve of you drink before you eat." Julia said.

    "Where is Nina anyway? I haven't seen her for a little bit." Rick asked.

    "She's been in the bathroom, I think she's having....her monthly time in there, maybe." Jacob said as he sat down at the table.

    Connor and Spark were busy setting the table for everyone and seemed to be in a little race to see who could finish first.

    "Alright, first place! What do I win?" Connor asked Spark.

    "This." Spark said as she kissed him on the cheek making him blush. Spark then noticed Gloria come into the room.

    "Oh! Perfect timing, Gloria, could you please...?" Spark didn't need to finish as Gloria simply nodded and used her ring to turn Spark into a human and Connor couldn't help but smile at his punk looking now human girlfriend.

    "Damn Spark, is that you? You look pretty badass for a young human girl." Julia complimented.

    "Thanks, Connor and I are totally going to get married one day." Spark said wrapping her arm around Connor making him blush.

    "Alright everyone, sit down, the meal's almost ready." Rick said as Gloria, Caleb, Spark, Connor, Julia, and Rick all sat down at the dinner table.

    "Right now that we're all here, we can..."

    "Wait, mom's not here." Caleb pointed out.

    "I'll go get her." Julia said as she got up from her chair and headed towards the bathroom.

    "Now, Caleb, what were you saying about another vampire at your school?" Jacob asked.

    "Another damphir actually, just like Duke." Gloria explained.

    "Well I guess we'll need all the help we can get for the foreseeable future." Rick said.

    "Uh, yeah, we are seriously going to need more help soon." Nina Moreland said as she came to the table with Julia following behind her.

    "True, we need any allies, weapons, and..." Jacob began saying.

    "No Jacob, I mean help around here for us...for me specifically." Nina said.

    "Why you?" Jacob asked raising an eyebrow.

    "Jacob, you are going to have to cut back on the beers and get in shape to help me out around here because, well due to our little 'fun' we had some weeks back......I'm pregnant." Nina said making the entire room go quiet as everyone was surprised by this news.
