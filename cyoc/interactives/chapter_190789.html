    Lisa just grinned as she stared at Jeff, who now looked like an exact copy of her. She also had complete control over her double, and she could change Jeff’s attributes back so he would look like himself again, whenever she wanted.

    “I got a great idea for an awesome experiment!” she blurted out.

    “Since you can now be me and yourself, under my control, you can go to school as yourself in the morning and I can change you back into a copy of me when it’s time for soccer. I can control your body when you’re on the pitch so you’ll still play like me, so nobody will know that you’re my doppelganger!” the original Lisa explained. “That way, I can stay home and do the schoolwork while you go to class, take notes, and you show up for soccer practice!”

    “Ohh, that’s brilliant!” Jeff grinned. “So, when I come home, you’ll share the homework with me?”

    “Of course! We’re both straight-A students! We’re not getting kicked off the soccer team!” Lisa smiled. “Plus, you like being me at school, right?”

    Jeff could only smile, since he was still an exact copy of Lisa.

    “Yeah! I like having your body. It’s great!” Jeff nodded.

    “I’ll only change you back when you go to class, so you don’t get marked for being absent too often. You’ll need to attend class as me now and then as well. We can’t be missing too many classes!” Lisa noted. “Some days you’ll have to change back between Lisa and Jeff at school.”

    “And with you being home all the time, you can take care of all the homework, right?” Jeff grinned.

    “Right. You go to class and soccer while I’ll do the studying. Together, we’ll get good grades in all of our classes!” Lisa smiled as she reached over and kissed Jeff on his cheek.

    Jeff spent the night at Lisa’s house, sleeping next to her in her bed. He was in a copy of Lisa’s body all night long. He had to wait till the following morning, so Lisa could change him back so he looked like himself again.

    After they woke up the next morning and had breakfast, they showered together. Once they got out of the bathroom, Lisa changed Jeff back to himself.

    Jeff felt his body grow a few inches taller and he regained his muscles as his hair became shorter. His facial features changed, becoming more masculine with the straight jawline. Lisa strengthened his arms and legs with some added muscle, all thanks to the Chronivac app on her phone. Lisa also gave him well-defined abdominal muscles and big, bulging pectorals. Finally, she used the Chronivac to dress him up in a tank top and blue jeans with tennis shoes and athletic socks.

    “There! You’re ready to go to class in the morning. I’ll direct you to all the classes and I’ll make you take notes. You don’t need to worry about anything. Let me be in control, okay?” she said to him. “After classes are over, I’ll change you, so you become a copy of me and I’ll get you suited up in your soccer kit!”

    “Nice! I’m just going along for the ride!” Jeff grinned as he felt his arms and legs move, completely under Lisa’s control.

    Lisa made Jeff walk to campus, which was only two blocks away. All he had to do was look around and greet people. Lisa made him walk to campus and go directly to the classrooms where he needed to be. She sat him down in the seats where she wanted him to sit and she made him take all the lecture notes remotely as she listened in, via the Chronivac app on her phone. During recess, she made Jeff sit with her friends from the soccer team and she made him say the right things. Finally, once the last period was over, she led him to the locker room, where she changed him once he got in a dressing room.

    “Time for you to change into a copy of me!” Lisa ordered, as Jeff carried his soft bag into the dressing room which contained Lisa’s soccer uniform and cleats. “Get ready!”

    Jeff braced himself for the transformation. He was about to change into Lisa so he could play soccer as his favorite soccer star. He was secretly hoping he could remain as her for much longer than one day…
