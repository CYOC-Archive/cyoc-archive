    “Yes.” Confirmed Stacy as she sat down on the coffee table, before looking at Josh and Brianna. “Don’t worry, you two will be were-cats as well soon. I have been recalled.”

    “Basically, we are likely moving.” Explained Brianna “We are going to go meet with Stacy and her pride and see why she has been recalled, either way we will all become were-cats.”
