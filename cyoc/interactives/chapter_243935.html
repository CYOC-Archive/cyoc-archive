    It has been 4 weeks & Lisa has changed more. Lisa now has full fox paws for feet & she actually he is digitigrade now. Lisa has a full fox & fox ears on the top of his head. He has lost nearly 2 foot in height by now (and of course lost weight). Lisa is also completely covered in fur; even his human hair is gone. So Lisa no longer wears anything anymore. Also Lisa has been 'Marking'. He has been 'Marking' in the backyard. And now does his 'business' outside like the animal he clearly becoming. And also the mental changes.
    Lisa was out in the backyard when he heard a nervous sounding voice. "Excuse me....mister....fox...." Lisa turned to see what could be only described as an anthromorphic brownish female rabbit; she was alot smaller than Lisa too. And nude too. "I... don't... want to have to...live as a rabbit..." The anthro rabbit nervously messing with one of her ears. "Well we got no choice what we are becoming missy." Lisa informed being a bit mean. "Kill me as a rabbit. Please." The anthro rabbit cried. Lisa walked over to the anthro rabbit & placed a hand on their shoulder. Lisa's teeth started to sharpen & her nose turned black. "You'll be my first meal as a fox." Lisa said in a predatory tone. The anthro rabbit. "Thank... you..." The anthro rabbit said scared but thankful. "How long you..." Lisa started to ask. "A 5 days..." The anthro rabbit answered. "I think I got longer myself. Maybe a week or so." Lisa informed taking his hand off the anthro rabbit. "Can...I stay...in your..." The anthro rabbit asked. "Yes. Yes you can stay in my future owner Jake's house. So the 2 entered the house together. Jake spotted the 2 & Lisa explained what the rabbit had asked of him. "Ok miss rabbit you can stay in the guest room until you are done." The anthro rabbit with a painful groan & some crunch noises getting a little shorter. "No....talk.... to me....as pers....hum..." The anthro rabbit tried saying but the words weren't coming properly anymore. "I think she doesn't want you treating her as a person anymore hun." The anthro rabbit slowly nodded. Jake said "Ok." understanding & he walked away with a "Potty outside rabbit or you will be gone before you done." Lisa lead the anthro rabbit to the guest room. The anthro rabbit looked back to Lisa & he told her "This is your room intill you finish becoming a rabbit." Lisa then left the room leaving the anthro rabbit there. The anthro rabbit climbed up on the bed & sat down on it. The anthro rabbit didn't understand most of what Lisa & Jake had said in the last few minutes. The anthro rabbit was totally forgetting English; all languages now.
