    "Kelly"

    You give a satisfied nod at your handwriting, you've always prided yourself on your careful and unique style. You take a moment to check your long wavy brown hair in the mirror. It's your first day at an all-girls boarding school, you've heard a lot about the school and your family is immensely proud of you for being accepted and, if you're honest, so are you. This school is a big deal.

    Hundreds of stories floated around about the school, all the incredibly successful alumni, the amazing teachers, the proud student sports, even some bone-chilling legends about the place. This school is in a league above the rest, and you desperately want to fit in.

    You think about some of the odd legends around the school, obviously, none of them are true. Tales of ghosts and monsters and strange happenings. It's all nonsense, and yet, you wonder where they all come from. Why are there so many stories? One story, in particular, comes to mind, a particularly scary story that was originally told to anyone who would listen by a girl who had been expelled. A story that claims every girl in the school is...
