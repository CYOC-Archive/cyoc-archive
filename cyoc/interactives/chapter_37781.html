David stirs and rustles in his bed, annoyed with having to come to terms with starting yet another brand new day. For David, life in general was what it was. There was nothing special about him, or so he thought, and his days were just like any other self-respecting working adult. David worked a nine to five job, with the weekend off, which was a very coveted "banker" hour type deal. For a while, David was one to covet those hours, as he wound up working in places he really didn't like to just get by working all kinds of different shifts, changing every now and then when the job or the job's scheduling changed.

    Groggily, David sat up in his bed, still covered by the warm and thick sheets, while rubbing his eyes to get the sleep out of them. Frustrated that he just couldn't stay home and enjoy a day like a weekend, David scoffed inaudibly, then flung the covers off of his lower half. David was average in all senses of the word. He was six feet tall; long, slender legs and arms, with relatively no muscle definition to him since he didn't find time in his "busy" life to hit the gym; he had dark brown hair; hazel to blue/green colored eyes and could barely grow any sort of defining facial hair. Swinging his legs from the bed, he stood up in his red and white, vertical striped pajamas while his upper half was unclothed, then walked over to the window to greet the ever pushy sun.

    Pulling down on the shade and releasing, the shade drew up quickly, going back inside it's circular and spring loaded casing. Just as quickly as the shade went up, his half naked torso was bombarded by the light and the warmth of the sunlight. Even though he was slow to get moving in the morning, which was his most dreaded part of his daily routine, David smiled to the sun as he did each and every morning. He was quite happy to be alive, as his recently dead parents had left him well off, making life much easier to live since he didn't have to worry in the financial department. Quitting work to live his life was something David wanted to do desperately, but he also saw the money that was left to him as not being his. David respected that his parents thought well enough of him to leave the entire estate to him, including the six million dollar mansion, that was built before 1900 A.D.

The floors were speckled marble, having hint and streams of black scattered about the otherwise purely white tiles. It looked as if in the forming of the marble, someone had poured black liquid marble in the still forming stone, creating beautiful and fluid arrays of black and white stone forming some of the most exquisite patterns he had ever seen. The bathroom counter-tops were covered in solid, black and white dotted granite. The sink basins were pure white marble with polished brass Kohlr fixtures adorning the counter, which poured water into the basin beautifully being a solid and clear stream, which left the skin of the hands feeling pampered while being silky smooth and soft. The mirror, which was placed right at the top most rim of the counter, spanned the entire length of open floor-plan of the bathroom. On the other side was a normal toilet, placed in it's own separate room within the room, as his parents never thought of needing a "special toilet" when they were just going to use it to do their "business" then leave. There was a jacuzzi tub right next to the western wall of the toilet. Beside that, there was a free standing, full-body shower which was one of the things David immediately enjoyed once he first moved in, surrounded by thick glass cubes which made it virtually impossible for anyone or anything to see through it.
