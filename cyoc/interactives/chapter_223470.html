    Rick Gets Curious

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 6 days ago AR I S O

    Rick was not sure upon which age to pick. All sounded interesting. He then noticed an option that appeared quite intriguing. It was indeed one he had not thought about seeing. It was one labelled "Unborn". So... This meant he was going to get back to the state of a foetus? The idea had some merit in its very curiosity. Being older or younger would open up a world of new sensations and offer experiences but to be not born yet... Well, this must be quite extraordinary. To feel sensations he could not remember feeling... Wow!
    But Rick then thought about a problem. If he was a foetus, or an embryo, an unborn version of himself, would this not be dangerous? He'd be without any way to survive, out of a womb and out in the open. How would he breathe, even? But then he saw a notice that this procedure was safe as he'd be back in a womb for the duration of the experience.
    "In that case, yeah, it really sounds interesting," smiled Rick.
    He then grabbed the pill proposed and he popped it in his mouth, happy to live out an experience like that. That would certainly be breaking his mundane life! He immediately felt himself shrink, and he was both scared and filled with anticipation. Soon he'd be back into his own mother! Amazing! And indeed soon, though he couldn't move or see or speak, he could feel himself in a moist, very warm place. It felt extraordinarily safe and he could hear heartbeats around him. His mother's heartbeats. And muted sounds from the outside world. Rick fell into a deep, peaceful slumber. So safe. So loved. He could sense it. And it was unbelievably soothing...

    Too bad Rick hadn't thought about the precise term of "duration". Because once regressed to an unborn baby, he had unknowingly been picked up by a member of the personnel and placed into a tube, to be inserted into a womb. Until he was ready to come out. Except as an embryo, Rick could not speak his opinion or protest. So the resort had taken custody of him and placed him into a surrogate mother. For a price. Which meant Rick had become a custom-made baby! He had perhaps changed sex or even changed species. He was maybe just slightly different. But at any rate he'd still have a surprise when he'd be born again...
