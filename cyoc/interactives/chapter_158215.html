    Sitting in his dorm room, Colin scrolled on his computer looking for the right site. He was in a hurry since his 3d modeling class assignment was due at midnight and, of course, he had waited until the last minute. The assignment was for an animation class and it was supposed to be to create a simple 3d model. Simple if you had downloaded the software. Colin hadn't, and he couldn't even find his syllabus to check what the name of the software. So here he is, in the final hour, searching a bunch of random websites. The only thing he could remember was that it started with a "C."

    Not having much luck, he was about to give up on the 86th page of results when he saw something. "Chronivac." This must be it, he thought. He began the download and watched as the file saved to his computer. Colin, though stressed, knew that he only had until midnight. After that he could hang with friends.

    The software loading bar finished and the installation window opened. He quickly agreed to terms and waited for it to finish. Finally, a new window opened up. It was the Chronivac. Before he could start a new project, a window popped up asking to sync with contacts. That was odd, he thought, but why not. He clicked yes.

    The window was an empty project. He could see on the top bar an option to begin a new project. He selected it.

    Choose from template

    A template!? This assignment is going to be so easy! he thought to himself.

    He clicked and waited as the screen loaded.

    Select Name

    He was shocked. It had a list of all his contacts. What was this program?

    He hovered over the names and decided to choose his best friend Sarah. He felt a cool sweat as he anxiously waited to see what the program would do. Finally it loaded. He couldn't believe it. It was a full 3d rendering of Sarah's body on the screen. On the right side, a tool bar appeared with the options

    Age
    Gender
    Ethnicity
    Height
    Weight
    Eye Color
    Hair Color
    Body Measurements
    Desires
    Awareness

    Colin quickly read through and was terrified. He immediately quit the software in fear. He sat for a moment thinking about what he just saw. Was it real?
