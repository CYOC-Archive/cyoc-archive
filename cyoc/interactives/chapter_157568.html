Braiden opens a heavy steel door which groans as it is opened. They all walk into the busy, dirty factory floor. Conveyor belts run across the floor and up to the ceiling. It used to be a toy factory but went out of business at the end of the 20th century. It's been sitting unused ever since. Left to rot.

    The group form a circle.
    "You figured anything out?" Asks Max.
    "I have actually. I may be able to switch your bodies back," replies Braiden.
    "It's progress," says Callum.
    "So you want to do it?"
    "Definitely," reply Max and Callum simultaneously.

    Braiden opens the app and sets it all up, "I can guarantee it'll succeed. I'm confident, but not 100%"
    "Just do it," says Max.

    Braiden presses the button onscreen and the world goes white. Callum experiences the same weightless and sterile mouth as before. Then he is pulled back down to Earth. The light fades and Callum sees Max's body in his sight. He smiles and says, "Yes! It worked! I'm me again." Max also smiles as he too is in his original body. He looks at Callum and sighs. They then look to Braiden who also looks relieved.

    But before they can celebrate any longer. Another bright light blinds them. They all feel that weightless feeling. Including Braiden. Braiden is panicking. He must've done something wrong. The panicking continues until weight comes back and the light fades.

    All three stare at each other confused. "What the?" Braiden finally breaks the silence.
    "We're different kids," cries Callum.
    "And you're also rather short," says Max.
    "How the hell did this happen," says Braiden in a foreign, childs voice.

    Callum looks down and jumps. He's wearing a navy blue hoodie and black jeans. On his feet are black converse low tops. He then sees his reflection on an old piece of metal on the conveyor belt. A fairly normal looking kid. Not ugly though. A fairly round face with some squaring off at the chin. His hair is blonde a in a small faux fauxhawk style. Blue eyes that seem to be affected by the surrounding lighting.

    He looks to Braiden. Braiden is a fair amount taller and has grayish green eyes. His hair is best described as a curryish blonde with a fringe sweeping to the right under a red beanie. He is wearing a light grey t shirt with a white Nike logo in the center. He is wearing grey jeans with red high tops.

    Callum then focuses his attention to Max. Max has the body of a child model. Youngish but mature at the same time. Blueish grey eyes and blonde hair that is in a bigger fauxhawk than Callum. He is wearing a white t shirt and black shorts. Along with some Vans high tops. He too is also so much taller than Callum.

    Braiden starts to speak, "Um. We've skipped to next year."
    "What?" Says Max.
    "We're in 2021."
    "Does that mean we're in grade 7?"
    "Yep," says Braiden.
    "At least that's solved," replies Max.

    Braiden and Max look down at Callum and sigh, "Sorry Cal. I had no control over your height. Otherwise you wouldn't be so short," says Braiden.
    "It's fine," replies Callum, "Just a little annoying."
    "Great," shouts Max as another bright explosion blinds them.

    The light fades and they find themselves back at school. But on the first day in the morning. They all let out a long sigh, "Not again," says Max. The school bell rings and the friends are forced to split up in their new bodies.
