    Over the next six months, a word became more and more present in the transgender community, a word first whispered in the more obscure corners of the internet, and then spoken more and more loudly.

    The word was Glendale.

    +++++++++++
    Jennifer Smith took the last sip of coffee and prepared herself for the day's clients. She heard a light knock on the door and grinned. She never got tired of this part.

    "Enter" she stated.

    The six-months-pregnant girl dressed in a maternity maid's uniform opened the door. "Madame's first client of the day" she murmured, eyes downcast.

    "Ah, Kylie. How is the baby coming along? You ever hear from the father? You do know who he was, don't you?' Jenifer knew perfectly well who the father was and that Kylie hadn't heard from him--Martin had left town and was currently an enforcer for an urban drug gang, with a half-a-dozen pregnant girlfriends that Jennifer knew of--but she enjoyed rubbing it in. Martin's absence had meant that when Kylie had been kicked out of her house--Kyle's parents were still insisting that the strange girl was involved in their son's kidnapping--there was a vacancy for a maid. Teaching Kylie her new place in the world--Jennifer had insisted that her new maid drop out of school--had been a refined pleasure.

    "No, madame, I have not heard from him" answered Kylie, with a curtsey.

    "Show the client in."

    One thing about this line of work is that Jennifer never had to worry about clients being late. Usually they were at least an hour early. She went through the papers on her desk--the psychiatrist's letter, the waiver of legal responsibility in case of regrets, and the verification that $5000 had been deposited into her account--and raised her eyes to the client, a short, stocky woman with a prominent tattoo of a wrench on her upper arm. Jennifer knew the client was a car mechanic who had been saving for this for a long, long time.

    "Jack Molayt?"

    "Yes, Miss Smith."

    "You brought a change of clothing?"

    "Yes, Miss Smith."

    "Ready to say goodbye to the tits and cunt?"

    "Very much so, Miss Smith."

    "Then lets get to it."

    Jennifer walked around her desk, seized the client's face, and planted a deep kiss. The client grew about six inches, developed muscles and prickly facial hair, and judging from his ecstatic expression when Jennifer broke the kiss, was feeling all the other changes of the "Glendale process (ftm version)."

    "Thank you!" he said, with a voice that had notably deepened.

    "You're welcome." Jennifer replied. "Kylie will escort you to the changing room." Kylie reentered and led the new man to the changing room. Jennifer grinned. It was nice that her clients were always so happy and grateful when they left her office, but the main thing was that this was the easiest job ever.
