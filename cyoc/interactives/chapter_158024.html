    The Zoo

    Jenkins gives the female officer the chance to escape the Zoo

    added by Anonymous 3 years ago A S TG O

    Now Jenkins was a lot smarter then the suits gave him credit
    for..

    After all his great great great grandfather Dr Alfred Jenkins
    had owned the island the Zoo sat on in the 19th century.
    Alfred had also developed the first animalizer serums
    and been doing just fine as a mad scientist till
    Patterson's great great great grandfather
    Rothschild Patterson
    had swindled it out of him in a crooked cardgame in
    a Deadwood saloon.

    So there was no love lost between the Jenkins and Patterson
    clans
    and Jenkins,having some of his great great great granddads
    looks and smarts,had a few tricks up his sleeve.

    Nothing as useful as a andidote for the serum now coursing in his blood but he had kept a second Security Card on his
    person ,which Patterson's goons knew nothing about.
    Just as importantly,although he had never developed a
    working antidote,he had figured out the formula for the
    psychological antidote to the mental changes that the serum
    produced in its victims. He'd placed that data on the same
    Security Card.

    So there in the Cave Jenkins was less concerned about his
    impending transformation,which he couldn't do anything about
    anyways,but getting the hidden Security Card to someone
    who could put it to good use later on and hopefully restore
    Jenkins mind,if not his body.

    Suddenly the false wall of the Cave slid open and Jenkins could
    see two figures inside
    a monstrously huge male dragon,roaring away and all that,
    shaking off the tore uniform of the formerly human male
    officer from the Stable.
    Being a smart fellow,Jenkins knew immediately what
    Patterson was turning him into
    a female dragon to keep the other dragon company.

    But of more immediate interest to Jenkins was the tiny crouching
    figure of the unchanged female officer,who dashed out of harms
    way from her former partner and hid behind some rocks,behind
    which Jenkins could see the softly blinking green light of
    a Security Card Reader.
    So ignoring the first warning signs of his change
    Jenkins held back a scream of pain as he dashed to join the
    fugitive female officer.

    Jenkins picked up a small rock and lobbed it at the security glass
    behind which Patterson and her goons stared at the scene
    in the Cave gloating.

    Almost immediately the male dragon roared and charged at the
    security glass,banging against the thick shield.
    So in the few seconds of inattention this gave him
    Jenkins slid his Security Card into the Security Card Reader and the Caves other exit appeared,leading to the
    outside world.
    He then gave the female officer the Security Card and tried to explain to her in a matter of a second or three that it
    held "the antidote"

    Then he pushed her out of the Cave and as she turned around
    and stared at Jenkins in
    puzzlement
    he hit the Emergency Close Bar on the
    Security Card Reader,trapping him inside the Cave with the furious male dragon.
    Then his changes began in earnest and soon the female
    dragon he transformed into lost any human reasoning......
