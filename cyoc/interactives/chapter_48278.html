    "I was wondering do you have any anime-themed costumes?" you ask since both you and Jack are kind of fanboys.

    "What kind of anime costumes?" the clerk replies.

    "You know, the ones were..." you pause, trying to think of the best way to describe what you are looking for. "Do you know those characters that are people with animal characteristics; like the ears, eyes, claws and tail of an animal. Kemonomimi, I think is the term for it; my friend calls them 'Petting Zoo People'. Do you have any idea what I am talking about?"

    The clerk smiles. "Of course, just follow me."

    He leads you into another room and you whistle silently to yourself as you see the contents. Row upon row of racks containing naked and incredibly realistic Kemonomimi-style bodysuits; male and female, some modeled after famous characters like Inuyasha or Yoko Kurama. Others seem to be based on original characters.

"We promise full tactile response, full facial control and no restrictions on normal bodily functions be it for using the restroom or for your adventures in the bedroom. These costumes are adjustable; so very much one size fits all. They also can be worn equally well by both genders. There is a reason our store is called Second Skin after all."

    The clerk responds by taking a bodysuit of the rack, a female one modeled after Horo from Spice and Wolf, and unzips the back. "Inside you will see a pouch specially designed to hold the male genitals and give the male wearer the appearance of a real vagina. Hell, our technology will even make it feel real, which should come in handy if you and your friend want to experiment. Also, the suit has adjustable padding and a corset designed to give a male wearer a more female bodyshape, not to mention an electronic voice changer."

    "Our prices are competitive. We can work out a monthly payment plan if need be," the clerk answers. "Plus we have rental options if you can't afford one now. We even have rent with option to buy packages. So do you need me to help with something else or do you want to browse a bit on your own?"
