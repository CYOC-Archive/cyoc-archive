    Ravenwood Summer Camp is a sizable yet out of the way camp located within a forest from which it gets its name. The forest is vast and far away from much civilisation, making it a perfect to spend a summer in the wild. Many fun activities are present to get its campers. Long nature walks, many lodges filled with equipment for sports and to top it all off a massive lake located just outside the camp is a favourite location among campers.

    There may not be interne, computers, good reception or many digital devices however the campers will soon find themselves the activities are more than distracting.

    It all seems like just an ordinary summer camp, advertising its fun outdoor activities and attentive counsellors. However one key difference is present. Only girls attend this camp for long. And any boy who chooses to step into the bounds of Ravenwood Summer Camp is unlikely to stay that way for long.

    And that is where you come in. For one reason or another you will soon be arriving at this camp. Who are you?
