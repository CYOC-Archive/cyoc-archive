    added by Leedeel 2 months ago A Bovine Reality alteration

    Jen was a 4-month-old cow on a dairy farm who lived a simple life. She was suckling at her mother's teats while her younger sibling lay on the grass nearby.

    When the change occurred, she found herself indoors, sitting on a soft surface. Her mind buzzed for a few seconds until she came out of it. She was wearing clothes and had a noticeably smaller frame. Her younger brother was on the floor playing with a toy. A television was playing in the background.

    "Why am I like this? Wait I'm always like this." she was confused at first, but soon thought nothing of it. The audio from the TV caught her attention.

    "...two manburger patties on a sesame seed bun," a commercial blared, showing a delicious burger that made Jen hungry.

    "Timmy want manburgers for lunch?" she asked her brother.

    "Yeah!" he answered excitedly.

    "Okay I'm gonna go get dad!" she said and ran off. She knew that her father was probably working outside. She put on her shoes and stepped outside, walking in a now bipedal stance.

    She looked around and saw her father in the distance, walking into a barn. She walked along the fenced area for the humans to follow her father, watching the humans along the way. They were such simple animals. Some were eating grass, some were lying down, and some were simply standing and staring off into the distance.

    Her daddy taught her all about humans. Females were used for milk, while males were raised for meat. Of course, their farm was mainly for dairy. Once their males grew old enough, they were sold off to a much bigger farm where they would be fattened and slaughtered. Jen didn't see any problem with that. After all, humans weren't intelligent. They likely wouldn't even know.

    She entered the barn and saw her father in the process of milking one of the humans.

    "Daddy, when are we gonna go get lunch?" She asked.

    "Hey Pumpkin, sorry for the delay. I just saw this human here ready to be milked so I thought I'd just get it done." He explained with a laugh.

    "But me and Timmy wanted to get Manburgers!" She sighed.

    "I know I know this'll only take a bit longer." He explained.

    "Okayyy." She said, before walking over to the human. "Which human is this anyway?" She asked.

    "Well I've just memorized their names, but remember you can always check the person tag." He reminded her.

    "Oh yeah." She chuckled, before pulling under the human's ear. "Oh right this is Shelly!" She said happily.

    "That's right Jen, it's Shelly." He said milking away. "Fiona's our current breeding human, but I'm thinking we'll give her a break for a bit and switch over to Shelly here. She's starting to get a bit feisty."

    As her dad was finishing milking Shelly, Jen saw another human enter the barn. This one was male.

    "Someone seems interested in Shelly at the moment. I think I'll leave you be for now. Come Jen, I'll get you an Timmy some manburgers."

    "Yay!" Jen said as they walked out of the barn. Her dad shut the barn door on the way out.
