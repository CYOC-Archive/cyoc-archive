She saw a little boy in a green hat and green clothes.
    "Who is that?" asked Sam.
    "You're the anime and manga expert nya, I'm the gaming expert nya. What else did you think I did on rainy days nya? That's none other than Link nya, Hero of Time from the game series The Legend of Zelda nya. Judging by his outfit I'd say... Majora's Mask nya, sometime afterwards maybe nya. Oh, and Navi the fairy too nya." said Jen. She ran over to him. "Hey Link nya. Wake up nya." said Jen.
    "Uhhhh... What?! One of Ganondorf's monsters! Die beast!" yelled Link. He swung his sword very fast but Jen used her newfound speed to vanish and reappear behind him. A long crack opened in the ground where Jen was. "Wha- How did you?! You dodged!" said Link.
    "Calm down Link nya. I have never met nya, nor do I have any connection to Ganon nya. I'm just a fellow warrior that has fallen victim to a curse nya. I just wanted to ask you to teach me to fence nya. My sword skills are kinda pathetic nya." said Jen.
    "Sure, why not? What do you say Navi?" asked Link.
    "I don't trust this creature. I sense a lot of dark energy around here." said Navi.
    "Oh, that's just Hiei nya. Don't worry nya, his bark is bigger than his bite nya." said Jen.
    "I still don't trust you." said Navi.
    "Link, why don't you use the Mask of Truth nya. That will clear things up nya." suggested Jen.
    "How did you- well, it's a good idea." said Link. He reached into a small pouch and pulled out a mask that shouldn't fit inside. He put it on, and took it off a few seconds later. "She speaks the truth. So, what's your name?" asked Link.
    "I'm Jen nya. Oh, can I see your Triforce mark nya?" asked Jen.
    "See! I told you she couldn't be trusted!" said Navi.
    "Don't worry Navi, her heart is pure." said Link. He held out his left hand. A triangle with an upside down triangle in the center was on the back. It started to glow.
    "That is so cool nya!" said Jen.
    "You think so? Hmm, it doesn't usually glow. Wait, what's that mark on the back of your hand?" asked Link.
    "Oh, this is the Shuffle Crest of the Queen of Spades nya. Huh?" said Jen. She noticed Link had bowed.
    "My apologies, I didn't realize I was in the presence of royalty." said Link.
    "No, I'm not royalty nya, it's just a mark nya. No big deal nya. You're very polite though nya. AHH! Oh yeah, ooh right there nya." said Jen. Link was scratching behind her ear. She was purring.
