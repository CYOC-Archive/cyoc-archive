    "I'll name you Annabelle after my great great grandmother" the lady said. "Annabelle Marie Le Fay. You will answer to that name."

    "I'm not a girl" I said trying to sound as defiant as a four year old can.

    "You are a girl now" the lady said. "You will need to get used to it because I will not be changing you back."

    "But what about my life?" I asked unconsciously whining.

    "You have a new life now" the lady said. "Though if you want to know what will happen in your old life you will be just one more missing person."

    "They'll come here asking about me" I said still trying to sound defiant. "They'll see my van out front."

    The lady smiled and said "Thanks for reminding me about that." She started to chant again and the door unlocked and opened by itself. I saw my electronic pad float out the door and into my delivery truck. Once it was in my delivery truck faded out and it was soon gone. I stood there too stunned to move an the door closed and locked again. "That takes care of that" the lady continued. "They'll find your delivery truck on a road outside of the city. It will look like you just wandered off. Now to take care of your clothes." She muttered another chant and my clothes disappeared. I was now standing there in front of the lady completely naked. I instinctively put my hands over my crotch. The lady smiled and said "There's no need to cover yourself like that. You're my daughter now. I think it's time to take you to your new room and get you dressed."

    "But... but..." I started to say.

    "No buts young lady" the lady said.

    "But my life" I said.

    "This is your life now Annabelle Marie Le Fay" the lady said in a stern voice that made me flinch and shrink back from her a bit. "Now come along." She grabbed my arm and pulled me through the house to a back room.

    In the room there was a single bed big enough for me with pink bedding and frills. There was a small white night stand next to the bed with a Disney Princess lamp on it. There was a white dresser that had pink knobs on it. A closet was on one wall and there was a Little Mermaid toy chest. The lady took out some sort of underwear from one drawer and some socks. From the closet she took out a green dress. She approached me with them and knowing what she meant to do said "No" and turned around to run out of the room. Unfortunately the door was now closed and I had the sinking feeling that I wouldn't be able to get it open.

    The lady grabbed me and tried to dress me. I squirmed and made it difficult for her until she said "Annabelle Marie Le Fay, if you do not stop and let me dress you I will change you into a doll and dress you then." This scared me enough that I let her dress me. The clothes felt pretty good but the panties felt a little thick and had a like a plastic cover over them.

    "Now you look pretty" the lady said once I was dressed. "Now I wonder if I should make myself younger again to take care of you."
