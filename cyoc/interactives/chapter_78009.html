    I want to be a nerd

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 10 years ago AR BM S O

    We knew that you would be led here to this page, it has already been imprinted in your mind. A benefactor of
    yours has paid a large sum of money to see that you receive the proper transformation. Yours is a special one
    indeed as you will soon find out. In fact, it has just begun.

    All of a sudden you begin to feel a spasm travel through your whole body. You can feel your self changing.
    Your body began to change – though it is impossible for you to notice. Your muscular frame shrinks down a bit,
    and you have lost 2 inches of height. You feel your vision begin to get blurry. Your body shrinks about two
    more inches of height. Your eyesight grows even fuzzier, and you feel an object appear in your pocket. You
    discover that it is a pair of thick glasses for you to wear. As you put them on, you discover that they have
    fixed his eyesight perfectly.

    You can feel the strength leaving your limbs. You can feel yourself tingling again, and now your features
    became slightly more pinched, and your body becomes smaller and thinner.
    You feel as your ears are growing bigger and starting to stick out more. Now you feel your nasal passages
    tighten, your voice no doubt becoming more nasal in sounding. Now you feel your teeth shifting position in
    your mouth, becoming buck teeth. Your teeth are soon complimented by a nice set of heavy metal braces that is
    now weaving it's over your teeth before settling into place.

    The changes intensify,and can be felt across your entire body. Your shrinking again – so much so, this time,
    you practically have to stand on tip toes to look out the window; before the changes the bottom of the window
    came to your chest.

    The changes pile up. Your hips have changed alignment, giving you a crooked, knock kneed gait. your skin has
    became filled with freckles, muscles are retracting into flesh, leaving you with spindly, wimpy looking arms
    and legs. Your hair has become courser and greasier, making it likely impossible to comb and always sticking
    out in every direction. Thoughts change as the mind swims with knowledge of chess, video games, and anime as
    years of football expertise fade away.

    Once you open your closet don't be surprised to find that all of your clothes have been replaced. White
    buttoned shirts, bow ties, corduroy slacks, and other ridiculous clothing exists in the place of the T shirts,
    jeans, and your leather jacket that you once coveted.

    Look at the mirror on the wall behind you at your tiny, nerdy, hunched over figure. Your teeth are out of
    place and misshapen, glasses make your eyes seem buggy, and your hair is a mess. The face you see is greasy
    and your skin is pale because you never spend much time in the sun.

    Feel as your body starts to spasm on its own accord, and now a warm, calm feeling falls over you. Feel your
    strength leaving your limbs.You can feel yourself tingling again, the transformation slows as your features
    become slightly more pinched, and your body became smaller and thinner again. Your current clothing is melting
    away into nothingness, but don't worry when you try on your new clothing, everything will fit perfectly,

    Enjoy your new life.
