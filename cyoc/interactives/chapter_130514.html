    What being a cat truly means

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by John Redux 5 years ago A BM S O

    You wander in circle around those strange mirrors, searching for an interesting one that could entice you into crossing it.
    While some look indeed terrific, like those of the Dragon and Deer Hunter, you seek for something much more simplistic and easy-going for starters.
    That is, if the possibility of going back and forth is not that constrained each time and again. You were lucky with the one bearing this German Shepherd, but it might not happen again.

    You suddenly stop in front of a mirror situated next to the tiger's. It's the one that bears inside what you had briefly identified as a house cat while running across the room.
    Looking further, you then see how much of a good candidate he is : it's a Siamese cat, with creamy and dark brown fur coat, and two brillant crystal blue eyes that intently stare back at you. He is sitting on his haunches, with his tail lazily flicking behind him - all while purring softly.
    You are literally mesmerized by this cat, and trying to stop staring at him is albeit difficult, but doable.
    For a brief moment, you hesitate. Looking around this queer place inside this stranger manor, you are not that much on board with the idea of doing it again, and the possibility of going back home and pretend all of this never happened crosses your mind more than once.
    But deep inside, your curiosity is screaming even stronger to be quenched : you kinda want to know what it would mean to discover the life of such a cute feline. Yours so far, on the other hand, had been filled with anything but exciting moments, and now that such an opportunity is offered to you, declining it to return to your dull livelihood feels a bit stupid and narrow-minded. You have to give it a try at least once.
    Besides, if it's not your cup of tea, there's always the possibility of coming back and do whatever you had in mind.
    Your decision is made.
    Determined, you slowly walk forward, while the Siamese cat gets up and trots the same in front of you. Ultimately, you do cross the mirror, apprehensive.

    You suddenly land on the floor, taking you by surprise and making you yelp and fall, before you retaliate and sit on your legs. Your body feels so weird now !
    You get a glance of your new arms and hands, reduced to front legs and dexterous but thumb-free paws, and wiggling them proves that they are still under your control.
    You also get a glimpse of your face, as something a bit pushed faraway shows up, along some long strands of whiskers. You raise a paw to touch the top of your head, and make contact with your twitching and now sensible ears.
    Bobbing down your now very flexible head, you stare at your belly and pelvis, your fur coat keeping you warm and your manhood, while truly animalistic in conception, seemingly untamed.
    You also try to look behind you, as you did receive the feeling of something moving behind at the base of your back. You get a glimpse of what definitely seems to be your tail, and you discover that you can move it on command.
    But what amazes you more than your full control of your body, is your sharpened senses. What smells you sniff ! What slight variations of sounds you can perceive ! And what a fantastic body you got yourself as a bargain!

    Taken aback by the situation, you decide to look around your surroundings. It looks like you are in what's appearing to be the entry hall of some sort of house or flat, with a rack for coats and some shoes laying right under them. You also see the entry door, and what seems to be the beginning of a living-room on the other side.
    You wheel around from where you stand, and indeed see a huge frameless mirror affixed to the wall.
    But most importantly, you look at your own reflection, one of a Siamese cat. You take a good look at yourself, your wonderful body, face, sharp eyes and fangs, paws and legs, tail and everything.
    It's you, but at the same time, it's a different you. Quite likely a better you.
    Content with those observations, you groom yourself a little bit, cleaning your fur and ears, which makes you feel quite happy and relaxed by the end.
    You get closer to the mirror, and hesitantly enough, you touch it with your claws.
    Fortunately, the same water-ripple feeling that you had experienced before shows up before your eyes, confirming that you can go back safe and sound.
    But why would you do such a thing ? You haven't done anything yet as a cat, and this body has yet to be fully tested !
    You turn around, facing the living-room, and trots towards it, ready to discover your new life alteration.

    What do you do now?
