    Jennifer knew that she had to warn Ariel before Johnny took over and made her as helpless as she was. At the very least, he had to stop Johnny from doing harm to anyone. She wasn't sure how she was going to do it, but doing something would be better than nothing.

    By this point, the locker room was nearly empty, so it would be fairly easy to locate Ariel. Grabbing Ariel's towel, she wrapped it around her and paced down the locker aisles. She frowned once she reached the last row of lockers only to find no one.

    "She must have gone into the bathroom. No way she ran out naked." Jennifer guessed. She ran towards the bathroom and heard faints sounds of crying coming from one of the closed stalls. She could see a pair of woman's feet underneath. That was a good sign. By now she should of been transformed into Johnny by the curse, but that was not the case.

    "Ariel, is that you in there?" whispered Jennifer. After what she had been through, she did not want to traumatize the girl even further.

    "Go away Jennifer! I don't know who or what the hell you are, but I want you stay the fuck away from me!" Ariel whaled.

    "Listen Ariel. You have every right to be upset, but you are in grave danger! Johnny cursed you!"

    "Not this crazy talk again. Jennifer, or Johnny, whatever you like to be called, I swear I will call the cops and get you arrested if you do not leave me alone. My dad is a powerful person, and he will make sure you won't see the light of day!"

    "Ok, Ariel, I am going to leave now. Please forgive me though. I know you won't understand, but it wasn't my fault." With those words, Jennifer left the bathroom and headed back to her locker. Ariel was still Ariel. She didn't understand how she hadn't turn into Johnny. Maybe the curse ended with her?
