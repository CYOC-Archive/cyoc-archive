    Your name is Chase Michaels. You have never been to a restaurant provided by the Magic Shop, until you find a restaurant called "Neptune's Rice Field". As you can see from the sign on top of the title, you could see Poseidon make a sushi roll with rice, seaweed, and tuna fish, using a knife, and a trident.

    As you go inside, you could see that no one else was here yet, but you see an Asian chef cutting fish to make something. The Asian chef had a ponytail, while at the same time had a handlebar mustache, while using a knife like the Poseidon on the sign. "Konnichiwa," the chef says, noticing you come in. "My name is Roshi. Would you like to order?" You never had sushi before, so you decide to give it a chance, and see what happens. To do that, you take a look at the menu above the counter.
