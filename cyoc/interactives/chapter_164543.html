    "I'll just duck out of view and wait until she's gone, she's probably dumb enough by now to fall for that." Meredith thinks.

    Meredith walks into the nearest building. As she walks in she realizes it's a bar. Looking around she sees that since it's early in the day, it's pretty empty in here. Meredith heads over to the bar and decides she'll just get a drink and keep her head low.

    "Just get me something cheap, I'm not picky." Meredith says to the bartender.

    "Something cheap eh? You sure a cute thing like you doesn't want something sweet and exciting? And what about your friend, she want anything?" The bartender replies.

    "My friend? Oh no..." Meredith turns around just in time to see Crystal walking up to the chair next to her.

    "Oooh a bar! You totally trying to party without me? *giggle*" Crystal practically yells to Meredith, way louder than the music playing. Crystal turns her head over to the bartender.
    "I'll like take 2 sweet drinks. Marti... martitty? Like whatever, you know what I mean. *giggle*"

    "Sure thing" the bartender replies.

    Meredith tries to think of a plan to get away from Crystal without causing a scene. Assuming Crystal now has the same "bimbo powers" the other 2 had, she's really dangerous to be around.

    Meredith's planning was cut short by the bartender. "Hey would you two ladies want to get a booth? I heard you talking about partying tonight and figured I'd poor you some extra drinks. Free of charge. Not that I want you two gone, just figured you'd be more comfortable."

    The thought of more drinks excited Crystal. "Oh totally! Thank you soooo much!"

    Meredith decides to just go to the booth so Crystal doesn't get suspicious of her. The two sit down and are greeted by the bartender bringing over a tray of 12 drinks.

    "Woah 12?! And these all look like they're loaded with sugar, anything a little less sweet?" Meredith says.

    "Yeah, figured you two could handle it, plus I'm not charging you for these, you should be grateful." The bartender says.

    Crystal jumps up and kisses the bartender on the lips for 5 seconds straight. "Mmmmwah! Thanks so much! I'll make sure to talk to you later. " she says with a wink. The bartender just smiles and walks back behind the counter, unaware of what has just happened to him.

    "Like these look so yummy!" Crystal exclaims as she picks one of her drinks up and downs it almost instantly.

    Meredith determines it'll be fine to have 1 or 2 drinks, as long as she doesn't go too far, she doesn't want to let her guard down.
