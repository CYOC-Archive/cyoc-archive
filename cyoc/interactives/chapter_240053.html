    Two Little Minions For Quinn

    Early the next morning both Len and Ben head to Quinn’s room, knocking on the door, “Quinn, you awake?”

    “Yeah… come in,” they hear Quinn’s voice say.

    When Ben and Len enter the room, closing the door behind them, they find Quinn sitting in bed, the wolf-boy looking tired, “you look tired, Quinn,” Ben says.

    “Well, I couldn’t sleep,” Quinn says, “what do you guys want?”

    “We wanted to ask…,” Ben says.

    “… if you could transform us…,” Len continues.

    “… into a tiger-boy and Malinois-boy, and maybe give us magic powers like you,” Ben ends the sentence.

    Quinn chuckles, “you are so cute,” he says, “sure, I could do that, but I don’t think Dennis would want me to transform his brothers”.

    “Pff… he doesn’t need to know,” Len says.

    “Ya! Can’t you use your magic to make Dennis and everyone think we have always been a tiger- and Malinois-boy,” Ben asks.

    Quinn thinks for a while, “okay, but I’ll only do it if you two promise to do anything I say, no matter what,” he says, “and accept me as your mentor, I’ll teach you everything you need to know, especially how to use and control your powers”.

    “We promise,” the boys say without hesitation, “we’ll do anything you say, just make us like you!”

    “Do you want us to call you “sir” then,” Ben asks, tilting his head.

    Quinn chuckles, “up to you, I don’t really care, as long as you show respect towards me and do as I say!”

    The boys smile and salute, “yes, Quinn! We promise,” they say, “we’ll be good!”

    Quinn grins, “good, because I could need some help”.

    The boys tilt their heads, “at what?”

    “I want to help all those poor people out there,” Quinn says, “show them and help them find their true purpose, make them happy”.

    Ben and Len smile, “we like making people happy,” they say, “of course we’ll help!”

    Quinn grins wide, “perfect,” he says, sitting on the edge of his bed, “then let’s get started,” he says, raising his hands and aiming them at the boys, starting to mutter in a weird language, the boys’ eyes widen when they watch as Quinn’s hands begin to glow in a blue color.

    Both boys feel their bodies tingle, before they begin to itch, their eyes widen as they look at their arms, seeing fur begin to grow, their nails lengthen into claws and paw pad appear on their palm and soles of their feet. The boys then yelp when tails burst from their backs, before they groan as their faces push out into cute muzzles, their teeth becoming pointy and sharp. Their ears grow and become pointy as they migrate to the top of their heads, their sense of smell and hearing increase, to their surprise they feel themselves shrink and become younger, until two six-year-old anthros, a tiger-boy and a malinois-boy are standing before Quinn. The former boys then feel a huge energy fill them, causing the two to smirk as they feel the immense magical power flowing through them, then with a quiver in the atmosphere the reality is changed so everyone, except Quinn and the former boys, think they had always been like that.

    “Amazing,” Ben says, looking at himself, “I feel sooooo powerful! But I gotta get used to my squeaky voice!”

    “Ya! This feels amazing,” Len says, “but why did you make us little?”

    Quinn grins, “because, it’s easier to teach you the ways of magic,” he explains, “the younger you are, the better, because the more you learn, the more powerful you’ll become!”

    The two grin, “we promise to be good students,” they say, “we can’t wait to get started with our training!”

    Quinn smirks, “I’ll make sure that you two will become the strongest and most powerful,” he says.

    The former boys smirk back, “and we’ll promise to help and obey you,” they say.

    Quinn smirks, “then let’s start,” he says, and for the next four hours, he teaches the former boys the rules of magic, how to control their powers and also teaches them spells.

    “This is so much fun,” Len says, as he levitates Quinn’s desk.

    “Yeah,” Ben says, staring at a clone of himself he had conjured, before causing it to vanish.

    “I am proud of you, boys,” Quinn says, grinning, “you mastered even the most difficult spells faster than I have ever seen!”

    “Well, we have the best teacher,” the two say, grinning at Quinn.

    Quinn grins wider, “I think you have learned everything,” he says, “you are ready to help me make everyone so much happier!”

    Ben and Len smirk, “we are ready,” they say, “what’s our first task?”
