    Jeff still felt bad for giving in to Jenny like that. Moreso, he was worried about her mom. Sure, she could be a bit overprotective, but he didn’t want to ruin her life or anything. He had to see if she was okay in a universe without her daughter. Jeff transferred the Chronivac to his phone so he could use it remotely, and took a walk. Jenny only lived around the corner so it wasn’t hard for them to walk the distance.

    When he got there, he noticed the pale light of a tv shine through the window next to the door. Peering in he saw what he wanted. Jenny’s mom, or just Karen now, was slumped on the couch, barely awake. She looked miserable. Entering her forties with no family or future to speak of must have really done a number on her. She still looked like a MILF, a total catch for any middle-aged man. But she couldn’t really be called a MILF without being a mother, could she?

    Jeff couldn’t stand to see this. An innocent woman, getting her life turned upside down by her stupid daughter’s wish. She looked like she downed an entire bottle of wine, and would just be making sweet love to her hand for the rest of the night. Jeff looked at his phone and came up with a way to turn her life around.

    Jeff typed in some changes and ran the program. Through the window, he watched Karen’s body shift. She seemed to shrink slightly, as her hair lost its gray streaks her body became firm and tight, while her breast became large and perky. Karen was now a perfect copy of herself when she was 18 years old. Jeff didn’t bother with changing her memories again, she has had enough of that, he just shifted the world to think she was a very successful 18-year-old. But to Jeff, she looked like Jenny. A taller, bustier Jenny.

    With her younger age, came a much lower tolerance to alcohol, something Jeff hadn’t considered. This made her keel over on the couch, puking up all that she had drank. Then she fell to the floor in her own mess. Jeff started panicking, did he just kill her. Luckily, he knew where she stored the extra key to the house. He used it many times for surprise visits to Jenny.

    Walking up to Karen, he checked her breathing, then her pulse. All was good, just out cold and covered in puke. He figured he shouldn’t leave her like this so he carried her to the bathroom. He pulled off her soiled clothes and threw them in her washing machine. Then he drew her a warm bath and cleaned her sleeping body. While she soaked, Jeff finally got the chance to admire her body. She looked so much like Jenny, and he really wanted her right now. Still, taking advantage of her now was unfair.

    He grabbed Karen a change of clothes and dressed her, before putting her to bed. Right as he was about to leave, her eyes drifted open. “Why’s a big strong man in my house?”

    Jeff was startled to say the least. He tried to mutter something and leave, but she grabbed his arm. “Oh, come on, you’ve done so much for me already. What’s a little more?” Karen dragged him into her bed. He tried putting up a fight, but she was weirdly strong for an 18-year-old. Jeff didn’t stop her as she climbed on top of him. The rest of the night… was a blur.

    When Jeff woke up the next morning, still in Karen’s bed, he thought about the morality of what he did. He slept with his girlfriend’s mom while she was drunk. Not only that, she would be very confused when she wakes up in a younger body, and with a womb filled to the brim.
