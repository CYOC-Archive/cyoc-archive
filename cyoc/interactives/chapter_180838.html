Jeff realized this must have been part of the awareness options he'd selected. Since he was aware of the changes he'd made to the world, his mind wasn't used to everything, but the Chronivac had altered the history of the planet, so though everything was new for him, his body had always been this way and was used to it, even if his mind wasn't.
