    "So, witch, have you come to fulfill your end of the bargain?" asked Christina.

    "Oh yes, " said the witch, barely able to contain her laughter. "This is just what you need."

    She held out a large goblet, filled with a green thick liquid.

    "Where are my warriors?" asked Christina.

    The witch shrugged. "The woods are a dangerous place, and I have many enemies."

    In truth, it was the witch herself that had dispatched them. Christina may have been too powerful for her transformation magic, but her pig-men were no challenge for her. She did not need any witnesses to the creation of her potion, regardless of how stupid they were. And it had made her search for ingredients much easier.

    Christina eyed her skeptically. "And this will really work?"

    "Oh yes, " said the witch. "It should produce the desired result."

    Christina signaled one of her guards, and he took the goblet from the witch. He began to drink it, and the witch paled.

    "Your highness! You must drink the entire potion for it to be effective. If you share it, it will not be potent enough to have any effect, " said the witch quickly.

    Christina grunted, and the soldier lowered the goblet from his lips with clear disappointment. But Christina grunted again, deeper and more threatening, and the guard quickly brought the goblet to Christina. The witch winced as small drops of the potion sloshed out of the goblet as he walked.

    Christina grabbed the goblet from his hands. In her large hooves it looked like a child's toy. She eyed the witch with suspicion.

    "You know if this is poison, my guards will kill you. You may have enough power to stop a few of them, but even your magic is no match for an army, " said Christina.

    "Of course, your highness. I would not dare try to harm the Queen. Now, drink, " said the witch eagerly, as she motioned for Christina to lift the cup to her lips.

    Christina was still suspicious, but what could she lose. She was already a monster with unending hunger. What could be worse? And if the witch betrayed her, she meant to follow through on her threat. She had already ordered her village to kill the witch if she attempted to harm her.

    Christina gulped down the liquid. It smelled awful, and had a greasy, slimy texture. But she had eaten worse, and it had not yet bothered her. She swallowed the entire contents of the goblet quickly and with little difficulty. She threw the goblet down in triumph.

A thick green mucus trailed from his snout to her teat. The piglet squealed, shaking its head to try to rid its self of the gelatinous liquid. Christina could see that the piglet's snout looked different, smaller and misshapen, almost as if it was melting. Strangely, she did not feel disgusted, but rather felt good, almost like the feeling of euphoria she got when she ate.

    "Witch! " said Christina angrily. "Your potion - it's not working. It's not doing what it's supposed to."

One of the female pig-people tried to pull the small piglet away, but it only seemed to push the piglet further into her body. Soon, the pig-woman's hands were deeply embedded into Christina's body.

As her body grew her features became softer, and less defined. It looked like she was slowly melting. Her entire body began to take on a green sheen.

And it will solve both of my problems at once. It will get rid of you, and you will consume your little army, " said the witch.

    "No, " said Christina softly. "They will kill you."

    "Oh, I don't think so. I think they will have their hands full dealing with you, " said the witch.

    "No .... wouldn't .... harm .... them," Christina struggled to say.

    "Oh my poor pet. You must not know much about slime creatures. I don't think you will have much of a choice, " said the witch. She pointed to the ground, and Christina saw that the grass below her was slowly being dissolved into her gelatinous body. Suddenly, tendrils of green mucus shot out onto the pile of food, and began to rapidly absorb the material into her body. As the material slowly dissolved, her body began to swell larger.

She could feel them struggling as they were dissolved. Her mass increased exponentially, and she felt the familiar euphoria of a satisfied meal. But it was short lived, as her hunger resumed.
