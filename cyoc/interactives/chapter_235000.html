Where there is water, there are usually bugs, and where there are bugs female lizards will be searching for food. Before Jeff knew what he was doing he was moving toward the source of humidity. He hadn't planned on getting this into being a lizard but procreating was on the forefront of his mind. Talk about lizard brain!

One male was copulating with a female, several other males were encroaching from the east and there was a lone female hunting for food.
