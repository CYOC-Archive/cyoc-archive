    Well something was off in this laboratory that Harry Smith's dad
    Parnell Smith and Harry's late mother Quinella Smith
    had worked in together till the accident that had claimed
    his mother 5 years earlier.

    Harry's mother and father had both had a interest in
    myths and monsters from myths but the two researchers
    had come from different fields.He'd been a archaeologist
    and historian who had been more comfortable in a library
    of ancient Greek scrolls or at the tiller of the Smith's
    beat up sailing snaubrigg sailboat the Laughing SeaDragon
    whilst it was Harry's mother Quinella who had explored monsters
    and myths with what Harry knew was shape shifting tf capable
    DNA splicers and what Harry knew darn well was
    a Kirlian Dimensional Flux Capacitor powered tf cabinet.

    After the accident Harry's father Parnell had put away
    all his late wives high tech dimension hopping machinery
    into storage at the boathouse the Laughing SeaDragon was stored
    in these days and sworn off his beloved Greek scrolls on
    Cyclops,sylla.basilisks ,dragons,manticore and other mythical
    monsters.
    Yet here all Harry's mom's old stuff was,a week after Harry's
    dad Parnell had vanished somehow.
    Harry knew something bad was going down or already had.
    Maybe his dad was already dead ?
    Harry looked about ath laboratory for a clue,a message
    and then remembered
    Before the accident 5 years earlier both Harry's parents and
    Harry had left messages to each another via a small hole behind
    a loose brick in the wall as you exited the laboratory.
    Maybe Harry's dad had left a clue for his son behind that
    loose brick?
    Sure enough in the message hole lay a set of keys Harry
    recognized.
    There was the key to the deck hatch of the Laughing SeaDragon
    a key for the snaubriggs engine ingnition and two
    keys,one for the door to the boathouse and another key to the
    automated boat cradle launch the Laughing SeaDragon sat on normally when not on voyages.

    The message was obvious"Go to the boathouse"

    So a little while later Harry Smith approached the door to
    the boathouse and could immediately hear the hum of machinery
    inside the building.

    Cautiously he opened the door and although the main lights
    were off Harry could tell by the day light that the Laughing
    SeaDragons launch cradle was not positioned like it should be
    but was ready to launch the 66 foot Hudson Nonsuch through
    a huge circular ring of metal from which Harry could see
    sparks of energy that he knew darn well was caused by
    a Kirlian Dimensional Flux Capacitor of unimagined power.

    " Holy Bleep "thought Harry "The Laughing SeaDragon is ready
    to be launched to another dimension"

    Harry could see stacks of boxes of vials and machines his
    mother had had a hand in creating before her death half a
    decade earlier.

    Basically this sailboat was loaded to the railings with tf stuff.
    In the back of his mind a little voice was telling Harry Smith
    that maybe going to the Land of Oz was not a good idea.There
    would be no munchkins on the other side of that ring,more likely
    a 50 foot tall Cyclops lobbing a boulder at Harry and the
    Laughing SeaDragon.
    So slowly Harry started backing away and turned a corner
    in the darkened boathouse.
    And bumped into 'her'
    "Oomph" said both Harry and her as they collided and fell to the
    floor.There was the clatter of a box falling to the floor and the breaking
    of glass.The shadowy figure Harry had just bumped into must
    have been carrying something to the Laughing SeaDragon when the two collided.
    She lay there crying in pain and Harry,ever the gallant gentlemen
    got up and walked over to help up the mysterious figure
    with a girls cry of pain of a voice.
    She raised what Harry thought was a girls arm and hand and as
    Harry's own hand clasped her proferred limb he realized he
    was gripping the hoofed fur covered forelimb of a 'creature'
    As the main lights suddenly switch on and the hydraulic pumps
    that operated the snaubriggs cradle launch both Harry and Meena
    the winged unicorn girl stared at each another in shock.

    Meena got her voice back first
    "A human.Harley told me he once was a human but I didn't believe
    him.A human a real human boy,Wow,wait till I tell the other
    kids humans are real."

    Then minding her manners Meena the winged unicorn girl
    realized that
    this real human boy was going into shock on her
    the Laughing SeaDragon was perhaps 3 to 5 minutes away from launch.

    So,since winged unicorns are bigger,stronger and faster then humans she switch scooped up Harry ,what stuff she could
    from the wooden floor and with a flap of her wings and her
    powerful hing legs leapt Harry,her and the stuff aboard the
    Hudson Nonsuch just as it began rolling forward to
    the other side where Harry's dad was.....
