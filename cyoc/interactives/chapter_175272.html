Sitting on a tree branch which itself looked unchanged was a squirrel but it now had a pair of long sabre like teeth like on a sabre tooth cat even animals where not safe from this event.

She also looked younger maybe 30 but it was hard to tell, she almost seemed to glow.

She let out a scream as she felt two new limbs grow out of her shoulder blades, they moved away first being covered with perfect skin before silver feathers began to grow in. She let out a scream of pain as her wings spread out striking a lamp which fell over but did not break which was good considering it now looked like an oil lamp.

    She regained herself as her wings pulled in back behind her, the tip of her wings went just past the top of her head down to just past the knee. They were heavily but seamed to balance her well even so. She knew what she was now…minus the halo of course she seamed to be an angel. She could just see the dark fur around her legs, deer antlers coming out of her hair and leather straps over her chest. For a moment the two just looked at each other.
