    Gorgon stones: sibling to Scuplture

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago I

    (Everyone please note it's rude to copy and paste other people's episodes.)

    You hit your big sister right between the eyes with the gorgon stone. She then stood at attention, staring at space. You wondered what she was up to. You thought of her shouting a moment before and she wordlessly assumed her nagging pose before. Startled, she then went back to her normal pose. You thought of her posing with one hand on her hip and another on her shoulder, giving a 'come here handsome' glance. She assumed that pose next. You think she looks good like that.

    In the next instant, her clothes vanished, and she became a nude polished marble statue. Then, a block of granite appeared appeared below her, raising her up, and a plaque appeared 'Bewitching Glance' with your name as the by line.

    You were in shock for several seconds as you pick up the gorgon stone you threw and put it back in your pouch.

    You gingerly touch your former sister, and to your shock you can sense her mind in there. What shocked you more was that you sensed she was HAPPY.

    Her personality had changed little if at all. She'd always wanted to loved and admired for her beauty, and she would be, she always feared old age somewhere in the back of her mind, and now she had nothing to fear as she'd last for eons as these transient things of flesh, even her own creator would turn to dust. She'd felt people were there to just admire her before, and that's what she thought now. Nothing had realistically changed.

    Creator? You check your big sister's room to find it now a spare room. You also find your sister missing from family photos around the house. You check the basement, and find a sculpture's studio.

    Your sister has truly ceased to exist, to have NEVER existed to begin with, and Bewitching Glance on the other hand had now always existed since you sculpted her.

    What do you do now?
