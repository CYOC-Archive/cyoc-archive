    Steph then said something that would stun you, even more than the idea of you transforming into a lesbian. "I've technically already changed once, you wouldn't remember, but we used to both be guys."

    "What?!" You exclaim.

    "When the bar changes you, it changes the world too. It can sometimes leave holes, and there's a chance one or two people who are close to you will remember, but usually it's only people who frequent this bar who will notice anything new," Steph continued.

    "Wait, when did you change? Was it highschool? That's when I remember you coming out."

    "No, me coming out in highschool is just something Liz's spell filled my past in with when rewriting it. Liz's morales can be a little sketchy, but she does not mess with the underaged. Her spells are set not to work unless you are legally an adult. And that's when I was changed, the day after my 21st birthday," Steph mused with a nostalgic grin.

    "Me and my first girlfriend wanted to celebrate, and we somehow happened to walk into a lesbian bar. We had a very enjoyable first night. But after the initial hormonal rush wore off... The enchantments encourage you to accept and like your new self. Even men and straight women who were previously happy with their old selves usually decide to stay as lesbians. But I guess something about Kassie's changes clashed. I loved the new me, but Kassie asked Liz to change her back. We mutually allowed the new history to erase our relationship. And that was how my first relationship ended."

    "Anyways, enough of that. I can barely even remember what it's like to be a guy, but I know one thing for sure, I don't miss it. You're going to love being a lesbian too!"

    You found the idea of being aroused by your own body intriguing. Would it be like squeezing an extra girl into the limited space while making out with Steph? You weren't planning to have children anyway, and since this was magic as opposed to surgery, you could probably change your mind later if things didn't work out. You did still have a few concerns though.

    "Won't I feel as if I am missing something? And I've heard rumors that girls go through some sort of ordeal every month."

    "Periods? I can't say they're fun, but the transformation will make everything feel natural to you. The mental changes will leave your ego intact while making you able to instinctively handle your new life."
