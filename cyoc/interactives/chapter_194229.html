    "Here we are, Jake," his father said sadly.
    "Well, seeing as how school is still better than prison," he said, looking at the castle-like building. Jake got out of the car, grabbed his suitcase and walked towards the school, and when they were at the school gate, his mother started crying. "Mom, why are you crying?" asked Jake confused.
    "Honey, it's time for you to know the truth about this school," his mother said between sobs as her husband tried to calm her down.
    "In this school you will become a mythical creature and be sent to another world to live as a new creature with a new identity." She said, still crying. Just then the headmistress of the school walked in, she was dressed like a typical office girl, except she had long pointed ears and long blonde hair, she was an elf. "You must be Jake," she said with a smile.
    "Yes," Jake said nervously.
    " And yes, what you heard is true, you will become a mythical creature and in this school you will learn how to live with your new body and then they will send you to a new world." She said with a smile. Jake was shocked. "Mom, Dad, you can't be serious," he shouted and tried to run, but his legs didn't seem to listen and stopped in place.
    "Sorry Jack, there's no turning back and more bad news,you won't be able to choose what kind of being you become and the gender is also random," she said sadly.
    "Are you kidding me?" He asked the sad parents " you're sending me away to become a monster and there's still a chance I'll be a girl, I never want to see you again, I wouldn't expect such a betrayal from you". He said sadly and angrily, then took his suitcase and went inside the school.
    After a while the headmistress came up to him again and said "I'm going to show you a list of what you can become there are more than one but these are the possibilities we have room for" she said and handed him the list Jake looked through it for a moment and then handed it back to her.The headmistress then poured all the possibilities into a ball and shook it and then pulled out the answer.
