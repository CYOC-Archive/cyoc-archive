    World of Giants.

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by No One 4 years ago BM S O Muscle Group Reality alteration

    No one really knows why or how. But one day in the middle of the summer, without warning. Everyone became giants.

    Houses were destroyed, clothes were torn off peoples bodies, plains and trains crashed, those inside subway tunnels practically suffocated to death. Cities were left in total ruin, very few people had a place to call home big enough now.

    Everyone kept their intelligence, and along with their increased size, people grew stronger, not just proportionally to their hight. There was a definite increase in muscle mass from before the giant change to after. That’s not all, judging by the orbital pattern of the Earth, human’s now lived much longer. Life was measured easier in centuries instead of decades. There were no clothes big enough for humans at the time, so people quickly got over their social biases about nudity until enough wool and other materials from the now micro sheep and silk worms could be collected and processed.

    The changes only effected Humans though. As a result many of the resources we relied on like: livestock, Wood, steal, and farming were now in shorter supply not ment for a population that now needed something like 2 million calories a day. Because of this, some of the newly made giants resorted to using their new bulk to break into others territory and steal from them, this intern lead people to retaliate. Now because no one made guns big enough for 10- 20 story humans (sizes vary but generally it’s between those two numbers with most people who are done growing) many used clubs or blunt objects like steel beams and pillars. Over time things got so bad it was easy to forget that once so long ago we were simply neighbors in a small town. Now we were colossal Titian’s ingaged in a life or death battle over weet. As a result of starvation and fighting, the world population was now a lot smaller in number.

    When communication with other countries was established it was found out to be just as bad everywhere in the world. Everyone was now a 10- 20 story tall giant and everyone was fighting except those trying to fix things. It seams that what ever increased humanity’s size also increased their temper. Battles would go on for years without stopping devastating what was once a beautiful country town or city.

    Soon the world was ruined beyond repair, with naked colossi roaming the the woods up to their ancles for a place to call home, not even noticing the ruins of a civilization that achieved so much more for being so small.
