    Tossing and turning for 40 minutes finally forces Jenny to simply sit up in bed. An exasperated sigh escapes her lips as she gazes at her bed.

    It still seems impossible that her queen sized bed seemed to fit her perfectly not too long ago. Now the child feels absolutely tiny laying in it. Freeing herself of her covers Jenny scoots out of bed, falling the tiny distance to the floor.

    As Jenny stares at the bed she has a momentary sense of vertigo. The mattress rests chest high to the small girl now. At first Jenny was embarrassed about having to scale her own bed but quickly grew to accept it. As she looks at it though an odd thought begins to creep into her mind.

    Maybe it's the energy her young body has but, looking at her bed now she has an almost uncontrollable urge to jump.

    "That's ridiculous," she scoffs, crossing her arms across her small chest. "Jennifer Mitchell you're a grown woman not some hyperactive child." As she gazes at her appearance in her bedroom mirror, she begins to rethink that statement.

    Jenny had decided to wear a peach color night gown to bed that night. The night gown itself was cool and comfortable enough that she could easily snuggle into her covers without getting too hot. Unfortunately though it made her appear every bit the innocent 7year old she had become.

    Jenny's golden blond hair fell to her upper back, her sky blue eyes appearing wide and innocent. As she studied herself she could see a childish body with two thin legs poking out the knee length nightgown, complete with tiny barefeet. To any that saw her now they would think she was nothing more than a little girl fighting her bedtime.

    The thought in her head now was becoming much clearer. She was by herself. Who would know? Clambering back onto the bed Jenny took an initial bounce, feeling her cheeks blush crimson.

    "This is ridiculous," she whispered. "I am too old to jump on the bed." Another unconscious bounce made Jenny pause, this was kinda fun.

    One bounce turned into two bounces, within moments Jennybfound herself giggling girlishly as she now bounced higher and higher.

    Part of Jenny was completely embarrassed to be acting like this, another part though tried to rationalize it. She was so small now that she couldnt do any lasting damage, why not have some fun.

    Higher and higher the girl bounced. Jenny found herself laughing with joy, her hair flying around her as lept higher into the air. Jenny was surprised now at how high she was going, maybe, just maybe she could touch the ceiling.

    The city minutes passed before the tiny girl plopped down into a giggling heap. Jenny was shocked and amused at how much fun something as simple as jumping on her bed could be. As odd as it sounded this simple activity had been the most fun she had had in years. As the girl now lay on the bed, she was surprised how quickly exhaustion overtook her. Quickly checking her alarm, Jenny now laid her head on the pillow, the sounds of childish giggles echoing though the room as she fell into a blissful sleep.
