    Let's ask the book what a woman looks for in a man!

    Branch Full view
    added by drdudo@hotmail.com 18 days ago O Magic Reality alteration

    Having already understood the magnitude of the power that Vedasto now had in his hands and contemplating the possibilities, Vedasto came up with a fun idea to start experimenting with how to make the world a more enjoyable place. He thought about what information he wanted the book to show him now, and he said it out loud too.

    "Ok book, I'm going to think and I want you to show me... all the information regarding what a woman looks for in a man, show me what the preferences of each woman are regarding what they want in a man, what type of men women like, what they want in the person they want to be their boyfriend, their husband, the man of their dreams, show me everything!!"

    The book heard Vedasto's request and responded, immediately a gigantic amount of text began to fill the pages of the book, as expected when faced with such a complex and complicated question, Vedasto obtained an even more complex and long answer. The book described in detail in paragraphs, and more paragraphs, the text was so long and detailed, that it would possibly take Vedasto more than a full day to read all of it. But Vedasto did take the time to read part of the text.

    "--Each woman of the human species looks for something different in a man to become something of interest for her, and so the reasons to feel a desire to give the male of the human species a chance, and to even be able to fall in love with the person and dare to start a family with him, vary greatly from person to person because each person is a different world. A woman who prioritizes her superficial interests over her sentimental or romantic needs wants a man who has a stable job, high income or a salary that at least can maintain a life with her as well as some luxuries, blah blah blah...other types of women want a man who doesn't raise his voice at them and always says yes to everything blah blah blah...blah...more blah a many women like a man whom is funny and that makes them laugh constantly, but within a barrier of respect blah blah, certain women who prioritize their sentimental needs want a sentimental man who dresses in pink, who always listens to them, who is never aggressive, that he pays attention to them all the time and fills them with praise that makes them feel special….blah blah for many women the first and foremost thing is the guy's personality blah blah blah some women are lesbians blah blah blah some women loves femboys blah blah---..."

    Vedasto actually got just the kind of response he expected, a long and complex response that went on for pages and pages listing the thousands and thousands of different types of women and kind of preferences that each one can have with men, the main qualities that every woman tends to look for in a man, was a very complicated topic and the book was careful to explore every possible nuance of the subject. Even pages later the book began to specify more, having a complete list of what Vedasta imagined all the women on earth were, and what each of them was interested in a possible partner at the moment.

    Honestly, if this demonstrated anything, it was that the book fulfilled everything it promised, to show all the information about the universe in which it was found, in the most precise and exact way possible.

    And now Vedasto smiled mischievously again, because he was now with this topic open.

    Why not do something fun and reduce all these different reasons why a woman is going to be attracted to a man, to just one...?
