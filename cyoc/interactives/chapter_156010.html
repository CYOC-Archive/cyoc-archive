    Jim strolled downstairs, where his sister was watching TV. "Where have YOU been?" she asked, in between mouthfuls of cereal.
    "I've been right here," said Jim.
    "Nuh-uh," snarked Judy. "I was in your room and you weren't there."
    "What were you doing in my room?" asked Jim.
    Judy was quiet.
    A couple of hours later, their parents came home. Jim sat through dinner and conversation with his parents almost unable to stand it, waiting for tomorrow to come so he could shrink again with the balloon. He could go anywhere. This would be so cool!
