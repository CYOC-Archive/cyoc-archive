    fail and suffer. The world has no room for idiots.
    “So David what does A squared plus B squared equal?” She asked, nodding toward the hulking captain of the
    basketball team. He was actually a senior and big for that even but he had failed his first run through the
    class and needed to pass it now or risk not graduating.

    adjustment to grudgingly act like a basketball player it had not given him the knowledge and skill that only
