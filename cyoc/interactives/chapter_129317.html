    Beginning to miss his old clones, but not wanting to give his body too many features, Ty decided to clone himself into separate bodies again. After discussing this with his second head, both heads agreed, and Ty began the wish. First, he wished for new clothes, ones that would be able to modify themselves whenever any change took place. In place of Ty's old clothes appeared a black long-sleeve shirt with sleeves extending out to the fingers and with white patches around the shoulders and armpits, and black pants. Then, focusing on his cloning, Ty managed to split his second head into a new body--the clothes split with him, until there were two perfectly identical teens standing next to each other. The two continued splitting until there were exactly eleven standing in the front hall.

    "Wait, won't it be a little confusing if we're all named Ty?" asked one of the teens.

    The others agreed, and each clone choose a name they wanted to have while they existed. If the name they wanted had already been chosen, they would choose another. In the end, the names were as follows for Ty's ten clones: Will, Matt, Dan, Reuben, Simon, Greg, Mitch, James, Henry, and Ben.

    Having cleared things up, the eleven teens went their separate ways for the time being. Some left the new house to explore the town, the others went back to explore more of the infinite domain that the house now offered.
