    "Those bitches think they can humiliate us? They're gonna be worse outcasts than lesbians," said Claudia before turning to Kellie, "Make those guys into their kids."

    Mika frowned at that. "Claudia, I hate them as much as you, but isn't that taking it too far?" she asked.

    "There's nothing can do that it can't undo," said Katie, "After we change them back, they won't even remember they were changed unless we want them to."

    Mika wiped the tears from her eyes and asked, "So does that mean you can erase my parents' memory?"

    "I'm...not sure about that," said Katie, "We could make Ashley and Jennifer less credible so your parents woudln't believe them."

    "And what could be less credible than a pair of underage sluts?" asked Claudia viciously before turning to Kellie and saying, "Do it."

    Kellie nodded before looking at Nate and Alex. "I wish those two were Ashley and Jennifer's babies," she said. The effects of the wish quickly became noticeable. Nate and Alex shrank down rapidly through their teens, all signs of puberty being erased. As they passed into their childhoods, they forgot what conversations they were having with Ashley and Jennifer. In fact, they were forgetting a lot of things at a phenomenal rate. The looks on their faces became more vacant as they became chubbier. Their clothes were shrinking with them, becoming more childish in design. However, their backpacks went through a extensive change as the straps on the front covered more of the boys' bodies while the backpack part flattened as a new pair of straps appeared. In the blink of an eye, Ashley and Jennifer were suddenly wearing the new baby harnesses, Ashley carrying Nate while Jennifer carried Alex. They weren't especially heavy since they were now both less than a year old.

    But the changes were not limited to the two boys. Ashley and Jennifer's bodies also altered by their new relationships. Their breasts grew larger, filling up with milk. But any boosts they would have gotten from that were negated by the way their bellies flabbed from stubborn pregnancy weight while their faces became a bit more lined and bags appeared under their eyes.

    While their memories of the previous reality were still clear, Katie, Kellie, Claudia and Mika became aware of new memories for this new timeline. Ashley and Jennifer had been very popular and pretty girls at school. So much, that even seniors were interested in them back when they were still freshmen. But unfortunately for them, they were easily tempted and both got involved in one-night stands. Even worse, their illicit trysts bore fruit, something neither father wanted to do with. So now Ashley and Jennifer had to deal with raising two kids far sooner than they should have. They hadn't been thrown out of their homes, but their parents made it very clear that they were the ones who had to take care of their children. Needless to say, their social standing at school plummeted, making them even bigger outcasts than Katie and Kellie.

    Claudia nodded and said, "That should do it."

    "But does still mean they overheard us and told my parents?" asked Mika with worry.

    "Do you remember your parents still disowning you?" asked Kellie.

    "I'm...I'm not sure," said Mika, "My memory still feels muddled."

    "Either way, you won't have to worry about me abandoning you," said Claudia.

    "I think it's time we head back to my place," said Katie, "My aunt should know about this. She might have an idea of how to set this right."

    "I might already have an idea," said Kellie before turning to Mika and Claudia, "You don't have to stay with your parents if they don't except for who you are and who you love. We could make you two old enough to be living separately from them. They might not even remain your parents afterwards."

    "That's...a lot to take in,' said Claudia.

    "I think we should think that over more," said Mika before looking towards the underage moms, "How long are we going to keep them like that?"

    "Until we find a way to make sure you two are alright," said Katie. She didn't feel a lot of sympathy towards Ashley and Jennifer. She had been aching to change them back into the brats they were ever since they mocked Kellie. Kellie insisted on not doing that as their insulting had unintentionally brought them together. But their new predicament was karmic enough for Katie's liking.

    "Let's find Samantha and Mary Elise and go," said Katie, "The sooner we're back at my house, the better."
