    Fenrir Hellwolf was one of your first characters on Planesbreak and possibly your favorite. After nearly years of playing as him, you managed to create one of the most top tiered characters made, earning a place in the top hundred players.

    Mechanically Fenrir was a Werewolf who made a pact with one of the game's Demonlords turning him into a half demon giving him several demon related abilites such as fire immunity and the ability to drain the life force out of others, as well as never being able to age. Aside from that Fenrir also has the battle mage class allowing him to cast spells and fight on the frontline as a force to be reckoned with.

    Fenrir's Magic specifically specialized in Demon, Nature, and Arcane magic giving him a wide range of abilities and potential strategies including being able to corrupt enemy monsters and turning them into his own demonic servants.

    Fenrir also has his own player home which appears as a giant floating airship complete with battle stations, cannons, it's own crew comprised of imp NPCs and as well as several cosmetic rooms such as a research lab, a sex dungeon (It's refered to as a Torture Room In Game), and a giant treasure room filled with gold, magic items, and high level gear.

    You weren't sure if all of those things you described to the the genie would be included into the wish but you did hope they would.

    After taking about a solid ten minutes to describe the entirety of your character to the Genie, she nodded and with a simple snap of her fingers said
    "As the line goes, your wish is my command, Master."

    Suddenly the world around you went white as you felt the transformation begin. Seemingly infinite power began to flow through your veins as, years worth of magical knowledge was poured into your head. As you smiled and laughed at the pure glee you felt as you realized your wish was coming true you heard the voice of the genie enter your mind one last time.

    "There is a twist however Master. You weren't careful with your wording so you will truly become Fenrir Hellwolf as you wished at the price of losing yourself and being overwritten with his own personality!" The genie screamed in laughter before you felt her vanish into existence.

    Suddenly dread began to fill you as slowly thoughts that were not your own began to fill your mind. The personality of Fenrir Hellwolf which you constantly roleplayed as was beginng to take over. You were losing yourself. Even when you felt the power the transformation was giving you, you desperately with all your heart tried to resist. However it was futile as slowly you began to not even care, as your emotions faded away as well.

    Your last thought before you completed faded was "What will my girlfriend think?"

    Fenrir Hellwolf, a sleek dark furred anthropomorphic wolf with glowing purple eyes, a long thick tail, two demonic bat wings on his back, digitgrade legs, and a completely visible long canine sheath and large endowed balls, stood buck naked inside a park, his keen ears hearing the voices of several humans a short distance away from him, his sharp eyes seeing the ever looming presence of skyscrapers in the horizon.

    Where was he? The demon wolf thought. Why was he stark naked? Wheres his gear? Back at the airship? His arcane senses didn't detect his airship and his rowdy crew comprised of imps at all, meaning they were either out of his range or... he was somewhere else.

    Pondering, the demon wolf began walking to the voices he heard earlier. Thinking that he might as well gets some servants to help him out. Never once thinking he was originally just a regular human teen who wasn't careful what he wished for...
