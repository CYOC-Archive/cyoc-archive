    added by dragontransformation 4 years ago A BM S TG O

    Now just like in a classic horror movie Aaron felt himself having a rising terror in his racing beating heart that he wasn't done
    with John the Demoness in the form of a black scaled dragoness.
    So he rushed out of the dorm room in brainless fear and
    plunged over the rotten railing and fell twenty feet to the
    stone floor below.
    He would most certainly have been dead except the workman
    who'd been sent to repair the rotten railing that day just happened to be underneath Aaron as he fell.
    Aaron certainly took a bruising but the workman
    underneath him who had taken the impact rather then the
    floor was most assuredly dead.
    As Aaron lay there stunned he smelt sulphur and brimstone and
    the light latin chant in the background.
    Aaron thanked Him but as he felt for the chain necklace
    with the Cross it wasn't there.
    It was then that Aaron realized that John and Hell in general
    weren't done with him yet.Hell seemed to have kept him
    alive a little longer.
    So Aaron moved to the other side of the country,finished his
    education,got a job and a wife and tried to live a normal life
    for the next thirteen years.
    Yet the unHoliness continued and Aaron accidents sent many
    a innocent to a untimely end as they got in the way of him dying.
    Aaron checked demonology books and wasn't too surprised to
    learn that three centuries earlier a new demoness in the shape
    of a dragon suddenly appeared in the books ,her name was
    John and she was the dragon mate of the Dragon of Hell.She
    did nasty demoness stuff according to the Books
    including being the demoness who had torn Faust
    apart on his final night on Earth.
    And there were pictures of John in her
    many forms as
    of course a feral dragoness
    a giant female wasp
    a serpent
    a Night Mare
    a female Cyclops

    So Aaron came home on the 13th year to a note on the front
    door saying his wife and the kids had gone for a visit
    for her parents for the weekend and that the Fuller Brush
    salesman would be calling in a few hours to finalize
    a sale.
    There was no Fuller Brush saleman to be seen but his
    briefcase with its spilled contents lay under the porch.
    Aarons feet and hands ignored the screams of protest from
    his terrified mind and they opened the door and walked in.
    The smell of sulphur and brimstone were unmistakeable
    on the floor in front of Aaron were a pair of mens shoes with
    socks
    in them

    like the Fuller Brush salesman had been yanked out of
    his shoes and socks by a unhuman force and speed.
    Aarons feet ignored his continued mental screams as he realized
    his body was sprouting black scales,his claws had just burst out
    of his disintegrating shoes and the weight of the expanding breasts on her scaly chest and the horns spiralling out of her
    reshaping dragoness head seemed to lighten as her neck
    strengthened,her wings tore apart her clothes and her
    tail was whipping violently back and forth behind her.
    As Aaron moved towards the living room her body was
    reforming into the same kind of creature John had started as
    13 years earlier.A black scaled dragoness.
    She screamed in mental protest but her body ignored her and
    walked into the livingroom
    where another dragoness
    John the Hellspawn lay seated on the couch gnawing on a bone
    left over from her meal of the Fuller Brush salesman.
    ******************************
    Aaron was never seen again but in books of demonology a
    new demoness made her appearance
    a demon in the form of a dragoness who served other demon
    workers from a cauldron well stocked with human bones
    Her name was Aaron
