    Another friend of Ty and Dustin, a boy by the name of Jason, walked into the bathroom and quickly moved to the front of the crowd since he was curious to see what was going on. He was a good friend of Ty and he was a bit of a goof.

    "Aww, look at the little baby!" Jason sighed as he walked up to Ty. "Is that Dustin?"

    Ty was still holding baby Dustin in his arms but he was quickly getting annoyed with his loud crying.

    "Dustin... Ssshhhh! Stop! Stop your crying!" Ty hissed, but it was useless.

    "Can... Can I hold him?" Jason said to Ty. "Maybe he just wants somebody else to hold him?"

    "Sure... You can hold him. He's crying just to piss me off!" Ty sighed as he handed the baby to Jason.

    As soon as Jason started holding little baby Dustin in his arms, Dustin quit crying.

    "Heyyyyy! Hey there, little Dustin! Heyyyy! Don't cry! Good boy! There!" Jason grinned as he smiled at the baby, who started smiling back at him.

    "There, you can have him back. I think he just needs somebody to talk to! I babysit a lot, so..." Jason explained, handing the baby back to Ty.

    Unfortunately, as soon as Ty received and held Dustin in his arms, Dustin began to cry again.

    "Ohhh NO! We need another baby for Dustin to play with!" Ty groaned, setting Dustin on a blanket which magically appeared on the bathroom floor.

    "Oh... Well, uhmm..." Jason mumbled. "I'll keep him company."

    Ty looked at Jason, who was almost 6 feet tall and very muscular.

    "You don't mind? I'll grow both of you guys back later tonight cause having Dustin as a baby is gonna drive me nuts eventually..." Ty grinned. "You don't have to do this, Jay!"

    "I think it will be fun. You can turn me into a baby!" Jason shrugged. "I don't care!"

    Jason had always been easy-going and carefree around Ty.

    "Alright! You're gonna regress to a one-year-old baby, same age as Dustin is now!" Ty ordered.

    Jason was overcome by that familiar tingling all over his body as Ty's power started to work in him.

    "Ohhh wow... This is cool..." Jason grinned as he began to dwindle before everyone's eyes.

    Within seconds Jason shrunk down from being 6 feet tall to being 5 feet, and losing hair and muscle on his head.

    Seconds later, his hair on his chest, his legs and his arms disappeared as Jason was now 4 feet tall and completely smooth.

    His face was much younger, looking like a 12-year-old boy, rather than 16.

    Jason regressed further, just smiling away as he felt excited about becoming a baby again. He was now 8 years old, with much shorter legs and arms and a smaller torso, losing all his muscle definition.

    "Ohhh wow..." he squealed, hearing his higher voice. His shoes were too big and his socks sagged around his short legs. His underwear dropped down from his waist and his t-shirt was much too big around him. It was draped around his shoulders like a pajama.

    "Let me take that off!" Ty said, pulling the t-shirt over Jason's head. He was completely naked now. But he didn't care.

    Jason regressed to 2 years old and dropped down to his knees, crawling around since he lost the ability to walk.

    "Ohhh... This is cool... Disizkewl...diskkool..." he mumbled, losing the ability to speak.

    Finally, Jason was a 1-year-old toddler, same age as Dustin, with a diaper appearing around his groin.

    Baby Jason quickly crawled over to Dustin on the blanket and the two babies just stared at each other. At least now Dustin quit crying. Jason then touched Dustin on the shoulder and started mumbling to him.

    "Goo-goo Gaaah boogaaah!!!" Jason mumbled.

    "Ohh thank goodness Dustin's happy now!" Ty smiled. "Now we got two babies!"

    Ty just looked down and smiled as the two babies played. He decided to teleport them back to the house so he could enjoy an afternoon of peace and quiet, since the two babies would eventually doze off to sleep.
