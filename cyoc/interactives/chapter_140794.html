    Gohrion was scared of how Deidre will react, but decided, that it would be better if he explained what happened to him.
    Maybe she could help him deal with this somehow.
    Deidre's Reaction wasn't exactly what he expected it to be.
    The first Thing she did after walking into the Room, was to look at him with sympathy, before walking up to and hugging him.
    "I saw you when i woke up, Goh. I thought about waking you up, but decided to let you sleep a bit longer and think about what to do."
    What she said made sense and the amount of Care her Words carried touched him. But this wasn't something 'fixable'.
    If a God wanted him to be a Kid, then it would be a bad Idea to undo it, if that's even possible.
    "I don't think this is something we can do anything about, Deidre. I accidentally damaged a Temple of Khalios Yesterday. I think he did it, you know about the Things that happen to People, who don't want to accept a God's Punishment." Said Gohrion in his childish Voice.
    Deidre looked at him in shock. "So you have to grow up again?" "I guess so." He said in a quiet tone.
    They stood there for a Moment in silence, when Gohrion's Eyes suddenly lit up.
    "Wait, wait, wait! That means i can become a Wizard! Apprentices start learning around the age of 10 and i have more than enough
    Money saved up to go to a Magic School! Newage even has a lot of good ones!"
    It felt like everything was falling into place for Gohrion.
    He finally had a Chance to fulfil his greatest Dream after more than 4 Decades!
    Gohrion looked deeply into Deidre's Eyes.
    "And... didn't you say that you maybe wanted to adopt a Kid?"
    She had told him several Years ago that she always wanted to have Children, but was never abel to have her own.
    And her Profession made adoption difficult, because few People would trust an Escort to take care of them.
    Deidre was stunned for a Moment.
    "Goh... are... are you sure that you..."
    She felt overwhelmed and speechless, as Gohrion looked at her with hopeful Eyes.
    "Would you..." She paused for a Moment. "Would you be willing to treat me as your real Mother?"
    Gohrion hugged her, as he quietly answered. "Yes, i'd love to."
