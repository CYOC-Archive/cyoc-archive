    Jeff Wants to be a Cloth Doll

    added A year ago I S TG

    Jeff decides to become a doll for the day.

    “My little sister and her friends are always so hyperactive and giddy when they play with their dolls,” he mumbles to himself.

    The teen has no intension to end up as one of his sister’s dolls. He is simply curious to know what it would be like to be a doll. Jeff opens up the toy folder and selects “Dolls.” Then he scrolls through the options presented to him. He is amazed of all the different kinds of dolls to become. He could become a barbie doll, a baby doll, a cloth doll, and many, many more.

    The boy decides to become a simple cloth doll.

    “It’s only for a few hours,” he mumbles, “My sis is going to a friend’s house for the night, so it’s not like anyone will play with me.”

    Jeff selects the “cloth doll” option. A message pops up asking him to pick a target, so he selects his profile. Next, a window opens to show Jeff what he is able to change, so he starts to edit the default settings:

    Type: Simple
    Gender: Female
    Feeling: Yes
    Hearing: Yes
    Sight: Yes
    Communication: No
    Mobility: No
    Mechanics: None
    Reality Alteration: None
    Clothes: Light Green Dress, White Bloomers, Light Green Hair-bow
    Hair color: Bright Orange
    Hair length: Short
    Hair style: Bob

    Jeff looked over the settings he had made, he decided to make his new form a girl doll, and change his clothes and hair to fit. He decided to not alter the reality so his family would simply think that he is at a friend’s house, and not a cloth doll. The time now is just after 1:00 PM and he set the duration of to last until midnight, so after 11 hours he’ll be back to his old self. Jeff also set the transformation to be instant, so he wouldn’t have to wait to become a doll. Satisfied, Jeff nodded and pressed the confirmation button. He then jumps up from his chair and hurries towards his full length mirror. In his excitement, Jeff forgot to set the Chronivac and his computer to shut down once the transformation is completed, and he doesn’t lock the door to his room.

    Jeff stops in front of the mirror and waits. At first nothing seems to happen, but then Jeff notices a strange pins-and-needles sensation run through his body. He feels himself become excited.

    “I wonder what will change first,” he mumbles to himself.

    His entire vision goes black for a few seconds. When it comes back, Jeff is sitting on the floor. He feels significantly smaller, and his skin appears to be made of felt.

    “Woah,” he tries to say, but he doesn’t have a mouth.

    Staring at the mirror, he looks at his new form. The teenage boy is now a cloth doll resembling a small ginger girl, complete with light green dress and hair-bow.

    “It worked!” he mentally shouts. He tries to jump up, but nothing moves.

    “Oh right,” Jeff thinks, “I’m inanimate now.”

    It hit Jeff just how boring it was going to be as a doll. Nobody was there to move him or play with him. That was until the door to his room opens and someone enters.
