Did Jake have any more wishes he wanted to make? Was growing older the "cost" he had to pay for his wish, or was this part of the wish and the Magic Mirror changed something else? If it was the price, could he wish for something else to return to being a kid again, or was he stuck like this?

Oh, and you can't grant wishes unless you return to the mirror." Thought this wasn't a completely selfless wish. Jake figured that if he was nice to the mirror, it would be nice back to him.

    The Magic Mirror was shocked. No one ever wished for something like this, and honestly, it wasn't sure if it could grant a wish like that. After all, it never got a wish request like that before. Should it even try, or should it try to convince Jake to make another wish? What a strange situation it found itself in.
