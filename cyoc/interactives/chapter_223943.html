Once she was fully awake, Alice quickly realized that she was no longer in her living room. Lifting herself into a sitting position, Alice started talking to herself as she scanned her surroundings.
    "What's going on? Where am I? {pant} Okay, think. Last thing I remember was pushing that blue button on the remote and being pulled into some kind of- WHAT THE HELL?"

Instead of jeans and a tank top, Alice was now wearing a cerulean blue dress with a collar, puffy sleeves and a petticoat-filled skirt that went to her knees. She was also adorned with a white pinafore apron with two pockets on the skirt. And on her legs and feet were white stockings and black mary janes. Getting on her feet, Alice checked herself over and realized that she was also wearing white bloomers and a black headband with a bow.
    "The hell? Why am I dressed like this? Is this some kind of sick prank or something?"

    "Did ... did I just get sucked into the TV? But that's impossible, isn't it? And wait a minute. If this really is the inside my TV, and I'm wearing these clothes, then that means ... {gasps} NO WAY! I ENDED UP IN ALICE IN WONDERLAND! AND I'M ALICE! I mean, technically I was already Alice. But now I'm 'The' Alice. So, if that's what's really happening to me, then what part of the story am I in right now?"
