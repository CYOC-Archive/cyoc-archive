    The different scents Dan is picking up causes the teenager to forget why he sneaked out of his room, ‘must… investigate… scent,’ he thinks to himself.

    Dan puts his new nose to the ground and starts sniffing, he then, on all fours, slowly and silently sneaks down the stairs, following the strongest and most interesting scent, the one that makes his still human cock becoming even harder.

    As if in a trance, the teen follows the scent down to the main floor, he can hear laughter and chatter coming from the living room, but he does not care as he follows the scent down the stairs which lead to the basement.

    Dan follows the scent, unable to stop himself, not even thinking of what he is doing is wrong, soon he finds himself standing in front of the basement door, which leads outside. He lowers his head, after looking up at the closed and locked door, and takes in a deep breath, ‘source… outside… must get outside… must investigate,’ he thinks to himself.

    The teen, using his still human hands to help him reach the lock, he unlocks and opens the door quietly, Dan then drops back onto all fours and starts to head outside, continuing to follow the scent, ‘something smells… really good,’ he thinks to himself, his nose stuck to the ground as he walks along the path leading to the back yard gate, ‘got to know what it is’.

    Dan reaches the back yard gate and opens it, soon the naked and half transformed human is out in the back alleyway, looking around, he then sniffs the air and starts to head in the direction of the scent, which seems to be leading away from his house and further into the dark alleyway.
