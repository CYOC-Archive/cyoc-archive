    Deja Vu

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by harethe 20 years ago A AR O

    Well i'd never known how fond mom was of her son Dansallignatious,

    so what happenned next was a bit of a rude shock.As soon as we brought

    my brothers altered body into the lair we carried him to a nearby stone shelf.

    My mom Jamignatious snuffled the corpse,then gave a keening roar that

    shook the cavern.Then she sagged down on her front legs and rolled over

    onto her side with a BOOM!She had died from a broken heart.The rest

    of us stood around the two lifeless corpses and started getting annoyed.

    Moving my brothers remains out of our home would be easy enough,but

    dragging out moms huge bulk would prove a chore.Unlike C S Leis s

    assertion,we do not eat our dead.

    We secured the remains and their two souls,then went into another cavern

    to set up a new plan.With King Greensparrow deposed and dead,the fortunes of the

    Clan Greensparrow were looking pretty bleak.And with mom gone,i'd be getting all

    the loving attention from my brothers,dad and uncles.We all wanted to change recent events.

    The nice thing about being a beast is that we have no inner voice telling us about

    the concept of time and any rules of time that apply to the mammals.Thus it wasn't long

    before we decided the best thing to do was to insert the souls of my mom and

    brother into a strategic time in the past,and hope they'd get it right the second

    time around.

    We shot the breeze all night long ,came up with our plan,made arrangements and

    winked out of existence as mom and brother rewrote recent dragon history.

    ==============================================================

    Lord Greensparrow and Lady Jamie Greensparrow had been lovers ever since

    they discovered that they both owed their long lives to the same means.Earlier

    in the past they had fused with dragon familiars,which had given them long

    life and vast powers,but also came with a price.

    The two humans ran swiftly down the country road,hand in hand.The

    Ladys dragon Jamignatious grumbles inside her mind,eager to be free in her dragon

    body,with all the pleasures that came with it.Beside her Lord Greensparrow was

    dealing with his own trucculent beast,and hoped they 'd get well away before the

    dragons took over.

    However the scent of cattle wafted by and the two dragons couldn't

    wait any longer.THEY WANTED OUT NOW,despite the pleadings of their

    human hosts.Soon the familiar itching and the straining of their spines began,

    and it wasn't long before Lady Greensparrows feminine curves undulated

    wilding from the assaults to her shapely frame.The two of them crawled on feet

    and hands that were quickly reforming into talons.They tumbled down into the

    hollow and embraced each another with a rising animal passion just before all

    the screaming began.

    In one of the farm cottages a farmer picked up his sword and went out to render

    assistance to whomever needed it.He could har the screams of a man and a woman

    coming from out of the hollow.Then there was silence,deathly silence.A sense

    of deja vu overcame came and he turned around,ran inside his house ,bolted the door

    and began to pray.

    Thus he never saw the two great horned heads that slowly rose out of the

    hollow ten,fifteen then towering twenty feet in the air.
