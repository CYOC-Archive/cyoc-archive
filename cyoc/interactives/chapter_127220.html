    A visitor from the future needs Ilvir's help

    added by Anonymous 5 years ago BM O

    The noise repeated itself and this time there was no mistaking what it was

    a stone striking a body

    Ilvir heard the feminine cry of pain
    the sound of a body falling to the ground and the sound of more stones being picked up
    and a angry sounds of a crowd

    "Death to the Witch" the murmur came

    Ilvir rapidly ran around the bushes and came apon a scene of cloud people villagers
    both male female
    adult and child

    getting ready to hurl their deadly ammunition at the target who lay
    wobbling in a crouching position

    A target who wore a suit Ilvir was unfamiliar with
    but which troubling enough
    bore the colours of the Sky Kings men

    And whoever this figure was wore a helmet that was peculiarly deformed
    and had glittering green glass where
    eyes should have appeared.

    Then the next hail of stones flew and Ilvir heard bones crack.

    Damn,here he was gawking away and staring at
    simple minded cloud peasants
    stone this mysterious suit wearor to deah.

    Spurred to action Ilvir chased
    the protesting farm serfs away
    then returned to the victim,who lay on his/her side and wheezing and making
    painful breathing.

    Clearly the person needed air and Ilvir
    felt around the base of the helmet and

    KLIK

    the helmet seemed to come free and Ilvir just about dropped it

    for he now knew why the helmet was deformed

    its wearer had the somewhat extended jawline of a firedrake complete
    with little flickers
    of flame that raced back and forth on her

    the victim was clearly female

    scaly skin.

    Yet her hair and unseeing milky white eyes were those of a cloud person
    and her hair certainly was.

    She was a hybrid from two planes
    of creatures.

    Ilvir felt over her suit which seemed more
    a heavy fire padded bit of armour
    and could feel her bosom still rising
    and falling

    more weakly with each passing minute

    Ilvir felt under a fold of the suit and pulled out a well thumbed book and what
    he knew was a Healer's
    medicine pouch.

    Frantically he tore open the pouch
    and saw many medicines,of a refinement that seemed beyond
    the skills of current bottlemakers
    and one fell into his stony
    palms

    Glycera Aqua

    the medicine to maintain a pulse in a fatally wounded
    patient

    Which seemed what Ilvir had here

    He took the bottle and was surprised when a small needle suddenly
    emerged from its cap

    Guessing how it worked he plunged the needle into a throbbing
    vein in her neck.

    She jerked on the ground,as if in great shock

    but Ilvir knew not else what to do

    Then came the whispers of the returning serfs
    come to rob the corpse of their victim
    and this time Ilvir did more then merely shout them away

    he hurled the stones at the peasants
    to chase them away good.

    He returned to his patient and felt about under that
    suit with many pockets.

    Many pockets stayed sealed but which ones
    did open to his touched revealed
    many finely refined devices
    he knew were not from the past or the present.

    The medical book itself
    was a 30th reprint,one hundred years in the future
    and

    not surprisingly

    had been written by Ilvir himself 50 years in his future.

    The subject

    Medical Treatments for Corrupted Patients

    Ilvir picked up her helmet and gazed through its lenses from the inside

    It was a machine and clearly
    its light allowed detection of
    Corruption

    Ilvir pondered these strange matters all night
    and the next morning,Ilvir's greatgranddaughter
    a Healer from 125 years in the future

    woke up from her deep healing sleep
    and said

    "Greatgrandfather Ilvir?"
