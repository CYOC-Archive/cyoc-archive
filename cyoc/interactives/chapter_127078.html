    It had now been a quarter century since the flooding of Earth had first started.

    The technologists were all but a dying profession but they had finally figured
    out what had caused the problems in the
    first place.

    25 years earlier a scientific research team had been trying to drill through the Mantle at
    the Marina Trench and had succeeded
    all too well.

    They had released the magma,but also the magic which
    had lain bottled up inside the Earth all these long
    aeons.

    Apparently magic is heavier than most other stuff.

    Being down 37,000 feet below the surface of the
    Ocean ( Pacific?) it hadn't been possible to seal off the hole they'd drilled.

    Hence the Trench was no longer the Trench
    but a rather huge deposit of glowing green
    Magicgneiss,the very valuable magic enriched
    mineral.

    Whilst the magic had been gushing out like
    Old Yeller
    it had done irreparable
    damage to space,time and reality.

    So now there were nations of zombies,plenty of psycho lizardmen
    and the island of New York was now
    a nature paradise again,with
    16th century natives merrily paddling
    about in their
    birchbark canoes,none the wiser that
    25 years earlier a city had once towered where
    tall oak trees now towered.

    25 years earlier you had transformed into a flme breathing
    western drgon in body,if not in mind
    and that had been the problem.

    For twentyfive years you'd had
    mental conversions where for several hours each day you were a western dragon in
    mind as well as body,which meant
    fighting knights,kidnapping maidens and eating
    dwarf ridden ponies.

    The thing was your alter ego was mentally
    a female,which meant everytime the thoughts
    changed your body went female dragon as well.

    A rather painful physical tf to be sure.

    It was only in the last few months tht medicine men had started refining all that
    glowing green magic ore in a attempt
    to turn Earth's monsters
    such as you,back into their original human body and mind.

    Alas over those 25 years your dragoness alter ego had slept around
    and laid eggs and now there were plenty of scaley little nasties you'd
    borne into the world.

    So the magic counter tf had not occurred
    but had simply totally buggered up the balance
    between you,the human in a
    male dragons body
    and her,the female dragon monster.

    What do you do now?
