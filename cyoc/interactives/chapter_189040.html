Amazingly he had climbed the stairs in the 4 inch stiletto’s without even thinking. There was a long hallway at the top of the stairs and Matt recalled the map of the mansion he had scrutinised earlier in the day. This should be a wing of bedrooms. There was an open door halfway down the corridor the light was on in the room. Matt decided that was as good a bet as any.

There was a pale pink upholstered lounge with a matching single chair but they were empty. The talking was coming from through a secondary door leading off from the bedroom.

    He quickly got a reply “Senorita, I was just showing the senora the gown you are wearing to dinner”
