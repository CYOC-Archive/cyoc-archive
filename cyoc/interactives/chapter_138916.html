    Aurora was still sitting on her haunches in the garden, feeling the fur reaching out for her head. Soon enough, the transformation would come to an end, and she did anticipate the event with glee.

    As her neck was engulfed within her furred coat, she felt a funny stirring in her throat. She tried talking again, but it was not like before, where she had somehow the chance of being able to speak and be understood by Jessy : her voice was sluggish, incomprehensible, and nothing but a series of grunts. But finally, she was then barking all along like a true dog, albeit with less impact and resonance.
    While she was pondering what it would've been if she gave herself a human voice in the Chronivac options, the stirring kept spreading to the lower parts of her head : her jaws were aching like fire, as if something was pulling them forward. Her soon-to-be muzzle pushed to the point of being noticeable in her vision, while her teeth sharpened to accommodate with her new regime. Aurora even felt the new texture of her tongue, deploying a sloppier one that started producing a small, but constant stream of drool, which was nothing but a mechanism allowing her to bear intensive heat.
    As she was amazed by all of this, Aurora's ears curled up by themselves, falling by her sides like a true Golden Retriever, and her eyes darkened to a rich brown colorset. It was the sign that nearly market the end of her changing.
    She somehow knew it, but then her head started feeling funny. It was only her brain re-adjusting as the head reduced a bit in order to be proportioned to her body.
    Thoughts about her former male human existence were clouded, not really disappearing, but rather put on hold in a part of her smallish brain. Dog thoughts were developed, simpler and far more adequate as the Reality Shift truly kicked in. She felt weird, as if she was supposed to remember something, but there was nothing but bliss coming out of her mind. And Aurora was fine with that.

    And by then, it was definitely over. The former male human known as Jeff, enticed by curiosity, had now been switched to Aurora, the female family Golden Retriever, as she was happily barking and strolling around the garden.
    The Chronivac, having completed it's task, simply went on stand-by, awaiting the duration of the command to come to an end.
