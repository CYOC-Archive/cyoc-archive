    Helen dried up her hands, and walked out of the public bathroom. The first thing she saw was the two girl, standing in front of each other, the attractive one was being held against the wall by the other, slightly shorter girl. They were looking at each other affectionately, not even taking a second glance at Helen passing by. As soon as they tried making a move towards the bathroom however, Helen grabbed the shorter girl by her shoulder, and whispered in her ear:

    "Listen, woman. 'You've caused us enough trouble already the past month. Give it a rest already." The girl stood still for a moment, just like the other one, both had different intentions however. As the attractive girl tried to speak, the shorter woman turned around. Tears had formed in her eyes, as Helen's saying had created a new mindset inside her brain. She looked at Helen with a begging face, but after a few seconds she ran past her, leaving the other girl flabbergasted.

    "Wait Kylie, where are you going? What is this all of a sudden?!" Apparently the girl who ran away sobbing, was called Kylie. Helen now approached the other, a hand gently gripping the girl's shoulder. The woman, who had no idea what was going on, positioned herself hostile towards Helen, slapping the hand of her shoulder. "What do you think you are doing?" She asked, fiercely. Immediately Helen grinned, wondering what she was going to do with her. She didn't know her name yet, but since the Chronivac changed anything mentally with that command, she might as well just call her Max or something.

    "Max, I'm sorry about Kylie, I should've come here earlier, are you alright?" For just a mere second, Max wanted to reply harshly a second time, but as she had mentally changed, nothing really came out. It was after a while that she replied, still a bit confused, "Y-yeah, I'm alright. We just had a bad encounter again. I just can't find the words what it was all about again." Helen looked away from her, thinking. Apparently, with lack of information, it would all pretty much be a blank backstory. In the conversation with Alan, Roger and Dave, she had already talked back a lot of memories, thus they knew each other pretty much in no time again. With Max it was a whole different story. They hadn't even talked with each other before, not even when Helen was still a guy.

    "The breakup of you two has been 4 months ago already, why had Kylie been stalking you ever since, it also makes me nervous you know?" That wasn't true, at least, not until then. Apparently the command also automatically changed her mindset if she said anything about herself. Now, every time she thought about Kylie, she shivered a bit, random memories appearing of how that girl kept looking at her and Max 'in the past'. Helen looked around the now abandoned mall again, Kylie had disappeared, at last, and Max was still in front of her, not having reacted to her statement yet. As Max tried to say something about it, Helen held a finger in front of her mouth and continued,

    "But let's not care about that now. That wasn't what we were here for, right?" Just before Helen had noticed how the Max and Kylie, previously still in a relationship, had something naughty in mind, before entering the bathroom she was in. Now that she and Max were alone, why not continue the story-line for Kylie? The blue eyes of Max met the brown ones from Helen, who was looking at her, biting her lip in a to her now very sexy way. The two were the same length, but Max' blonde hair was longer, curled the same way as Helen's.

    It was then that Max opened her a side pocket in the handbag she was holding. Helen's curious eyes met the object Max took out of it, and as their eyes met again, Helen flung herself towards Max, kissing her intimately...
