She put one foot in front of the other, trying to get used to walking on all fours without falling to the ground. Will managed to make her way, not without tripping over her paws first, to the door. She shook her head seeing that she had stupidly closed the door behind her. That was when her improved hearing picked up the sound of the apartment door opening, Will instinctively wagged her tail, she could only hope that Megan would be able to help her. It was then that Sarge took Will's distraction as his cue, jumping up and onto Will's back, causing Will to whine slightly.

Brushing back a few strands of her brunette hair behind her ear. "Will, are you here?" She said, noticing a pair of large boots on the ground next to the door.

She opened the door, expecting/hoping to find the dog just sitting their on the bed. What she saw came as a surprise, seeing two nearly identical black Labrador retrievers going at it. She smiled in slight embarrassment, watching for a few moments as the spectacle continued. Megan shook her head, laughing slightly as the larger dog easily outmatched the somewhat smaller one. "I guess there's no stopping you two." She said, as she started to turn away.

    At that Will let out a loud whine, hoping to catch Megan's attention and make her realize that this wasn't right...
