    Returning to childhood

    added 10 years ago AR

    Rick, currently twenty-seven, was slim and athletic, but not particularly muscled or toned. However with his dirty blond hair, blue eyes and slight tan he could easily look like a stereotypical Californian surfer if he wanted to; which he sometimes did. But he didn't have the build for that, and he always felt that look worked more with younger people so he wore his hair short. So with that thought in mind he decided to use the Age Room to get younger.

    As the instructions said he concentrated on the age he wanted to become, and focused on becoming it. Then, with a feeling like having warm water poured over him, he began to shrink. His clothes got looser as he lost height and size, shrinking down to about four feet and five inches tall, while his body hair retracted into his skin and his skin became smoother and more youthful. His face got more rounded and young looking and, within only seconds he was a ten year old boy once again, draped in over sized clothes.

    Rick quickly stripped them off and went over to the mirror and stood there nude, getting a look at himself; he was his old ten-year old self. His hair had brightened to a sandy blond, but the length hadn't changed. His eyes were even more blue and looked quite blue in his tanned, and quite cute young face. Not it was definitely his build he wanted to fix, his face had always been a strength. He remembered that when he'd really been ten he had once gotten an ice cream from an old woman 'just for being so cute'. There were some things he wanted to fix on himself though, but he'd get to that in a second.

    Now he needed clothes, so Rick looked over the closets until he found one labeled '10 year old; male'. He opened it and saw such a massive variety of clothes inside that he felt momentarily overwhelmed. After a moment though he was able to see that they were divided into sections: underwear, shorts, pants, shirts, jackets, socks, shoes, hats and accessories.

    First Rick went to the underwear and found a pair of blue plaid boxers that he quickly pulled on and found they fit perfectly. Next he went to the shorts section and found a pair of navy blue plaid cargo shorts that he pulled on and tightened slightly with a dark cloth belt that he looped so the end of it hung down.

    From there the boy went to the shirts and decided on a black tank top with red trim. He didn't bother with jackets, as he wasn't going to the colder parts of the resort today, and instead moved on to a pair of ankle socks and black high top sneakers. Finally he grabbed a black graphic beanie with a brim, but didn't put it on just yet.

    As he was about to leave he stopped at the accessories section and decided on a dogtag chain necklace, a studded leather bracelet for one wrist and a black sweatband for the other. Now he was ready to go to his next destination; the Gender Wing.

    See he remembered that the Gender Wing also allowed one to change their physical appearance, and that was his next goal in becoming a skater/surfer boy.
