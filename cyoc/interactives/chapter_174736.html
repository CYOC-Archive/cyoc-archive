    Jared didn't want to face what he'd done so he stayed hidden inside Jasper the rest of the trip. The only thing Jared made sure to do was to stop Jasper from getting kinky again.

    They got back from their camping trip to find Uncle Joe had come for a visit.

    Jared asked, "Why did you tell Uncle Joe about me being a horse sometimes?"

    Mr. Marshall said, "Joe knows horses better than I do. He still has a full ranch with twenty horses. I was hoping he could guide us in making this profitable."

    Uncle Joe said, "Yea, my brother doesn't know horses too well. He likes them too much as pets. He isn't willing to make horses work like horses are supposed too. If you have Jasper's body Jared, then you shouldn't be wasting your time giving kiddie rides. You should be trained as a racing horse. I've decided to take you to my ranch and train you to be a racer."

    Eric piped up, "Can we come too and help?"

    So it was all arranged although Jared had second thoughts about it. Uncle Joe, Jared, Carl and Eric took off for the long trip Joe's ranch.

    As they got to talking, what happened on the camping trip came up. Uncle Joe said, "I don't mind if the three of you are gay. Makes no difference to me."

    Jared said, "I'm not gay. Jasper did that."

    Uncle Joe said, "I think Jasper is just your subconscious. I think you're suffering from multiple personalities and need to have your personalities integrated into one."

    Jared tried to zone out and not hear what the guys were saying. Jared knew that Jasper had been the horse long before the two of them started sharing bodies.

    The first few weeks at the ranch were awkward. Uncle Joe kept trying to treat Jared like a horse and Jasper like a human. Uncle Joe said, "Apparently we still have to break this wild one." So Jared found himself naked and tied outside while Uncle Joe tried to ride him.

    Uncle Joe said, "Jared, you have to obey your rider."

    Jared kept protesting that Jasper was the horse and Jared didn't have to be broken. Finally, Uncle Joe said, "I'll prove that you and Jasper are one and the same."

    Uncle Joe took Jared to a psychiatrist and convinced Dr. Lao that Jared had multiple personality. Dr. Lao said, "You must want to be a horse especially since you can actually change into a horse. I don't normally treat horses but there is a drug that will help you merge your human personality and your horse personality into one personality."

    The drug worked and Jared found himself slipping back and forth between Jasper and Jared more easily and sometimes even being both at the same time.

    Then Jared didn't mind being naked and saddled like a horse or being forced to race. Uncle Joe even used the tawse on Jared and showed Jared how it didn't hurt that much to be tawsed. It just prompted Jared to run faster.

    Finally it came time for the first race. Uncle Joe told Jared, "Here's the deal. If you win, you get a break from training but if you lose, I'm going to train you even harder."
