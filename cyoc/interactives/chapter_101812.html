    "Anderson..." muttered Jennifer, looking at a clipboard. "Here we are, under the subs. Sorry we've yet to have the pleasure of your company, I'm sure you'll be a great addition to our staff," added the Vice Principal, while reaching down to a card table to give Martha a temporary ID badge and a packet of material covering the day's seminars.

"Go down the hall, first right, last left, or just follow your nose," said Jennifer with a wink, acknowledging that Martha was a sister were.

    Then, as she approached pastry display...
    "Well, a new face!" said someone behind Martha, as she was helping herself to a Boston creme to go with her coffee.

They're planning a long cruise together. One of those romantic, see the world, bucket list things. So she'll be gone for at least a year, but then they plan to come back to Moon Lake to live out their golden years."
