    So I can change more than one person at once.

    I wondered if this ring HAD limits. It scared me that if I got angry or depressed and just wish for the world to blow up. Or wish I was king of the world.

    I thought about the newest scientific studies suggested that maybe dinosaurs had feathers and not just scales. Maybe the reason we became dinosaurs as I thought of them was a safety measure built into the ring to prevent me from making a wish that would be misinterpreted and backfire.

    I wondered who the old woman was, she had given me basically the power of a god, but she hadn't been wearing any jewelry or anything special when I saw her, maybe she didn't NEED the ring to change reality. She was out there, she had given me this power, and I was scared to think how powerful she was if she gave something like this as a gift. I wonder if I had run into one of the ancient gods. Still, she had given me no restrictions on using the ring other than it would fulfill my fondest wishes, and had used her own power to make me wish the ring could never be forced off me.

    I had wished that police officer into a little dinosaur girl, and she had REMAINED a police officer, and no one found her riding a motor bike odd and she rode it with the skill and experience of any traffic cop.

    'I wish, unless I want it to or need to, that any changes I make with my wishes would be seen and accepted as normal except for me.' Then added.

    'I wish my wishes would come true via the magic of the ring only when I am intentionally wishing for them to come true via the magic of the ring.'

    I wondered again if these were indeed changes to reality, or just the ring making people see it as normal.

    I made a similar wish on the construction worker, going from a middle aged male triceratops, to a little hatchling one, her equipment shrinking to match her size. And the chat she was having with the person next to her about the professional and finer points of construction didn't miss a beat. It was actually cute hearing her say all those expert things in that adorable voice of hers.

    I considered doing the same to the bus driver, but I didn't want to risk THAT test yet!

    A old woman got on the bus on the next stop, then she was naked reptile child running around. A thirty something old woman who had gotten on the last stop told her to sit down. A minute later the two of them were playing patty cake in their seats next to each other.
