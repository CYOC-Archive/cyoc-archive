    Jen said "Alright then! So from what I can tell only the sensory organs have a good idea of what is going on, and only I have a high enough IQ to make any meaningful decisions right?" Most all of Jen's body said yes where Jen continued "So how about we do this? Since the sensory organs can only transmit their sensory information directly to me... They sense things, tell me what's going on, and then i'll make a plan for what we should do. If anyone here has any problems they can complain and I'll see what we can do about it. For example legs and feet, if you are tired i'll ask eyes to find a place to sit, and if we find a bench or somewhere somewhat clean to sit would it be alright for you to walk to that place?"
    Jen's feet said "Sure" although one grumbled "Guess we'll have to carry you all."
    Then Jen's butt said "Better make sure it's clean! I deal with enough shit!"
    Jen's right hand said "Oh sure you "deal" with it, but usually i'm cleaning it up! among other duties."
    her left hand said "Come one righty, this has been going on long enough... Let's just make an agreement and hopefully become functional."
    Left boob said "Come on wher-"
    To which everyone interrupted and said "Shut up Stacy!"
    right boob said "indeed, in hindsight trying to get every body part to be independent was a horrible idea."
    Jen said "The body wasn't meant to have dozens on independent personalities. When we can get everything working we should work on our reaction time."
    heart asked "why?"
    Jen said "well when I had just one personality the communication between sensory organs and me were really fast, and communication between me and the muscles were also really fast... Now we are using language. Ugh we must have been debating for hours..."
    heart asked "Why do we care about being fast?"
    Jen said "Because we are a cheerleader, We need fast reaction times." The muscles all groaned to which Jen asked "What's wrong with cheerleading?"
    Left leg said "Well all that running, jumping, and splits are killer on us."
    Jen said "I felt you yelling a long time ago. I get it's all taxing work, but-"
    Right leg said "Well then why don't you keep running and jumping by yourself?"
    Jen said "I'm just a brain! If I die then what the heck can all you do?"
    Right boob said "Come on muscles, it's a tad strenuous however it's good work and there are perks to being a cheerleader and you get stronger over time!"
    Left boob said "Indeed Perks!"
    Jen said "God, come on girls settle down and get a little less perky." then continued "Ok then we are all in agreement to the base rules which i'll list off. If anyone has any new problems bring them up." Jen then started "1. When there is something controversial our body votes and majority wins... 2. We respect one another enough not to threaten to stop working about something trivial... 3. Sensory organs talk with me the brain and I the brain relay the information to appropriate body parts... 4. During times of low action we can all mingle among each other... 5. Clothes are fine, better to follow social trends... 6. If anyone has any immediate complains we will see if there is anything we can do to remedy it." No body part objected to which Jen said "Ok then. Let's start working together. Eyes please open your eyelids." Light went into the eyes and they gave information to the brain to which Jen was able to see her surroundings. On their feet Jen was able to send signals to her eyes really fast on the ends of "look over there.... now there!" Then she said "Alright then... there's the door... Come on feet and legs on our way to the door, and when we get close enough righty and arm why not open the door for us?"
    Jen's legs moved her body forward to the door. They were a little slow but they managed to walk down the stairs in their pajamas. There was a faint scent of bacon in the air which your nose picked up.
    Jen's Stomach said "Food! Let's eat!"
    Jen said "We're on our way to the kitchen. We don't want to accidentally fall over under this."
    Jens ears told her "Mother's said Good Morning. what should w-"
    Jens mouth opened and spoke "Morning..."
    Jen said "The heck mouth? I didn't even think about what specifically to say!"
    Jen mouth said "Well it's just a generic thing... You never think about the response so..."
    Jen thought for a second and said "K, for those things I never really think about anyways just go ahead and do things automatically... However when we need to think please consult with me."
    In the end they managed to get something to eat, get themselves dressed, and managed to walk to their bus stop and get in. Today was a cheerleading practice day...
