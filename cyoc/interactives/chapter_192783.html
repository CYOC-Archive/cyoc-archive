    Rachel in Wonderland: Stuck in her House

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago S

    "Ow." Rachel said as her head hit the ceiling of her room, causing her to lean forward as she grew larger and larger. Soon Rachel's increasing size forced her into bending her knees and finally into sitting down on the floor with her legs stretched out.
    "Okay, I think this is large enough. Can I stop growing now? Please?" Rachel said to her body as she continued to gain height and her legs now long enough for her shoe-covered feet to touch the opposite wall, all while moving the furniture out of their positions with her expanding mass. Eventually Rachel's head touched the ceiling again and her knees had to be bend upwards as she found herself continuing to grow, breaking the furniture around her.
    "Ah crap, why didn't I wait and eat the cupcake outside? At this rate I'll destroy the whole house." Rachel said. At that moment Rachel heard her mother knocking on her bedroom door, wondering what was going on.
    "Rachel. Are you okay in there? I heard something break." Rachel's mom said.
    "Mom, I'm growing larger. You need to get out of the house." Rachel said, now nearly cramped in her own room.
    "Your what? Rachel, open this door right now." Rachel's mom said. Suddenly the floor and walls beneath and around Rachel started to crack from her increasing weight.
    "MOM, THERE'S NO TIME LEFT! YOU NEED TO GET OUT OF HERE, NOW!" Rachel shouted to her mother. Rachel's mom saw the cracks forming on her half of the wall and immediately started heading downstairs and running for the front door. At this point the flooring and walls gave way, causing Rachel to brake out of her room and continue growing inside the rest of her house. Rachel's mom makes it outside just in time to see a large black mary jane shoe bursting out of the front door and moves out of the way. Rachel's right leg then burst out of the dining room window, with Rachel's mom standing between both legs, then both her arms burst out of the walls, and finally her head bursts out of the roof. At that point Rachel's body had finally stopped growing and Rachel started looking around at all the damage she had caused. Rachel's mom stared in complete confusion and was utterly speechless.
    "Um, hi mom. Are you okay?" Rachel asked her mother, who waited a few seconds before responding.
    "Rachel. WHAT ... THE ... HELL?" Rachel's mom said.
    "We'll mom, you remember that scene in Alice in Wonderland with the White Rabbit's house? Yeah, same thing but I'm Alice. Sorry." Rachel said.
