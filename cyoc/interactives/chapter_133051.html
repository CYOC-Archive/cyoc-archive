    One More Round!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 5 years ago AP BM S

    You wake up the next morning feeling a little odd. For one thing you feel a little drunk. This must be a hangover you think as you rub your head. You hope it goes away as you rise up. You check your alarm clock. It's pretty early in the morning. You find yourself not tired even though you came home from bowling last night pretty late.

    After the game ended the three of you went back to Colby's house where the three of you changed back. You soon discover why you felt odd this morning. In the bathroom the same scrawny man you had turned into was staring back at you. Your pajamas seemed to have torn a little considering your new size and height.

    Then you remember something...did you actually change back? It's a bit of a blur but you feel that you did but you're not sure. In the end you grab the magic socks from your dresser and out them on. You grab your shoes and place them on your feet which is pretty difficult with you extremely long feet. After a few minutes nothing happens.

    You grow concerned but then relief when you see yourself growing smaller. In no time you are back to your normal self. You grab your phone and message George.
    "Hey did I turn back into me last night?"
    "No, you were too drunk so we snuck you in room"
    "Ok, hey are you ready for another round?"
    "Sure am, I'll meet you at Colby's"
    You then call Colby's and tell him you and George are you're way. "OK, come on over my parents went grocery shopping and my brother is asleep in his room. As long as we do the bother him, he won't bother us" You say goodbye get changed and head outside.

    You meet up with George a block away and walk with him to Colby's house discussing what you were going to do. "Let's go bowling again, that was fun" George says and you agree. It was pretty enjoyable. You arrive at Colby's house where he opens the door and shushes the both of you. The three of you make your way up the stairs and into his room and lock the door.

    "OK, who wants to go first?" He asks. "I do" you say. You already have the magic socks on and put on a pair of shoes. Suddenly you feel a little bigger as the floor become more farther. "So guys how do I look?" They stare at you. "Look for yourself" You turn to the mirror on Colby's dresser and gasp.

    You've becomes Colby's brother. Your hair was a really light orange and your skin had become really pale with freckles everywhere. A little bit of stubble was on your lips while a patch of hair was on your chin. Your figure had also become a bit more muscular you saw two pecs poking out of your now small shirt. "Why did you have your brothers shoes?" You ask in a slightly deep voice. It sounded exactly like Colby's brother's voice.

    "I just grabbed shoes from around the house, anyway you had your turn now it's mine" Colby grabs the socks from your feet and puts them on himself. "Wait I don't want to be your brother" you whine. "Too bad we all need to transform quickly if we want to get to the Bowling Alley before my parents come home which means we go with our first form" Colby grabs some cowboy boots while you sigh.

    "Do you at least have some clothes that fit?" Your new body had made your old clothes really tight. "Don't worry I have some sweatpants and a large tank top" Colby says. You take the clothes and head to the bathroom to change. "Hey!" You turn around. "Sorry I got mad earlier, I just really want to go bowling" You smile and go into the bathroom.

    After you change you walk back in to see a slightly short man with a beard and big belly in Colby's clothes. "No way, I want someone else" George laughs and grabs the socks. "Sorry but in you words we go with our first form" George puts on some cleats while Colby changes into some of his dad clothes. But even his dad's clothes are a little tight on his belly.

    You watch George grow a little taller. Long black hair emerges which reaches his shoulders. His legs become more defined as his body grows a bit more muscular. His skin darkens and a college soccer player is soon looking at the both of you. "This body isn't too bad" George says as grabs a baseball cap and stuffs his new long hair under it.

    You chuckle. George doesn't have to change clothes considering his old clothes fit him just fine. You put the magic socks in your pocket. Soon the three of you were off and just as you made it to the front door you heard Colby's brother bedroom door open and his footsteps coming down the stairs.

    What do you do now?
