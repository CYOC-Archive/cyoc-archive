    Jeff walked around Kat’s family’s house for a while, until he was absolutely sure no one was home, no one human at least. He then ran around to the backyard and found what he was looking for: Kat, in feline form, getting mounted yet again by the neighborhood tomcat. He knew she liked it: after all, it was Jeff and the Chronivac who turned her from a crush who had cruelly turned Jeff down into a cat in constant heat.

    When the cats saw him, the tomcat dismounted and they both ran. Jeff didn’t care about the tomcat, but he knew Kat could still understand him. “No, wait!” he shouted after them. “I came here to turn you back!” He took the portable Chronivac out of his pocket and waved it around to show her.

    Kat didn’t come any closer, but she stopped running. “That’s okay, you don’t need to come any closer,” said Jeff. “I can do this from here.” He typed a bunch of commands into the Chronivac, double-checked his work, and then hit the big TRANSFORM key.

    He watched Kat. Sure enough, her legs started stretching out and turning a paler shade, her tail vanished behind her, and her face shifted around all its features. In a matter of seconds, she was a human again, back on all fours. She was wearing the same clothes as the day she had been transformed several months back, which Jeff had made sure the Chronivac would do.

    Kat examined her newly normal body, as if seeing it for the first time. She then turned to look at Jeff, with a tense look on her face that made it obvious she didn’t know whether to thank him or run. She chose the latter, bolting towards the street.

    “Hang on,” Jeff said in a calm, confident voice. “Stop right there.” Suddenly, Kat stopped.

    “Turn around and walk towards me,” Jeff said, and Kat complied.

    “What the fuck?” she said, a confused expression on her face as she walked.

    “No talking,” said Jeff, and Kat fell silent. “At least, not unless I ask you a question. You know what, and any time I give you an order, say ‘yes, master.’ I like the sound of that. And any time I ask you a question, your answer should end with calling me ‘master’ again.” He smirked. “Do you understand?”

    “Yes, master,” said Kat, whose terrified expression revealed that she was starting to understand.

    “Great,” said Jeff. “I think you can tell that the price you’re paying for getting your body back is being my permanent slave. So, just to be clear, never, ever harm me, and never, ever tell anyone that you’re my slave.”

    “Yes, master,” said Kat in a resigned voice.

    Jeff had a raging boner from all this. This was too hot.

    “All right, slave,” he said. “Say goodbye to your old life, and come with me. We’re going back to my house.”

    “Goodbye, old life,” Kat said, a tear falling from her eye.
