    Dale's and Pamela's Costumes

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by loony28 15 years ago AR

    You turned down the street you took before. Pamela had expressed doubts that the store you spoke of was down this street. You assured her that this was indeed the way. Soon you came to the park and Pamela asked "When did this park spring up?"

    "I don't know" you replied. "It was here when I came by earlier."

    "That's strange" Pamela said. "When I came by before going to your house it was a different park."

    "It's part of the magic" you said. "I found it on accident. Maybe you're seeing this park now because I'm taking you to the costume shop."

    "Believe it or not, it makes sense" Dale said. "I take it that we have to go in the park to get to the store."

    "That's right" you say smiling. You then walk into the park followed by Dale and Pamela.

    Once inside Pamela asks "Which way do we go now?"

    "Back out the way we came in" you said. At the perplexed looks on their faces you add "Trust me" and go back out. You're joined just seconds later by Dale and Pamela. They gasped as they saw that they weren't in the same city anymore.

    "Where are we?" Dale asked.

    "I don't know" you said. "All I know is that the mall with the shop is here."

    "I see the mall" Pamela said. "Are you sure that it's safe though?"

    "I'm not sure how safe most of the shops are" you said. "I'm pretty sure that the costume shop is safe though."

    "Well we came this far, we might as well keep going" Dale said.

    You walk to the mall and enter. The same guy that had handed you the bag full of vouchers before handed two bags to Dale and Pamela. You then lead them to the costume shop.

    Inside Jessica comes up and says "Hello Megan. I see you couldn't wait to see me again."

    "That's part of it" you said. "The other part is that my roommate and his girlfriend want to buy costumes. Jessica, this is Dale and Pamela. Dale, Pamela, this is Jessica."

    "Hello" Jessica said shaking hands with Dale and Pamela.

    "Hello" Dale and Pamela said.

    "Well what can I help you with?" Jessica asked.

    "We were looking for some costumes" Dale said. "Megan said that the costumes you sell are guaranteed to fit."

    "Yes they are" Jessica said. "Did you have any in mind?"

    "I just want to look around and see what strikes my fancy" Pamela said.

    "Me too" Dale said.

    "Alright" Jessica said. "The changing rooms are in the back in case you want to try any costumes."

    Dale and Pamela went off looking for their costumes while Jessica and I chatted. A few other customers came in and left while they were still looking. At one point I needed to use the restroom and Jessica told me how I needed to use the restroom wearing a skirt.

    Finally Pamela came back with two empty hangers, a third hanger with a werewolf costume, Dale's clothes, and a baby girl. Jessica looked at her and said "I see you decided to go with a mother/daughter theme."

    "Well it's been a little fetish of ours" Pamela said. "Dale would dress up like a baby and suck on my tits."

    "I bet you had to talk him into being a girl" Jessica said.

    "Yes I did" Pamela said. "What finally did it was my suggestion that we can switch which one is the mother and which one is the daughter."

    "Sounds like a good idea to me" Jessica said. "Come on and I'll check you out unless you want to get a wig for Dale when he's the mother."

    "I want him to pick it so we'll come back later" Pamela said.

    "Alright" Jessica said as she rang up the purchases.

    After Pamela paid for the costumes she said "Why don't we get some dinner in the food court Megan."

    "Sounds good" you said.

    "I'll go with you" Jessica said.

    "What about the store?" Pamela asked.

    "Harold can handle it for a little bit" Jessica said. "Just let me go tell him I'm going to get some dinner."

    "I thought you were the only one working here" you said.

    "We actually have three people working this shift" Jessica said. "Harold and Stacy are content to let me handle the customers. That's ok with me though. I get to meet most of the customers this way."

    "So let's go have dinner already" Pamela said.
