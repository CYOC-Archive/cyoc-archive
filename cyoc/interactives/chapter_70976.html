Then he looked for an option to go with a hybrid form, halfway between a human and an animal. Being a full satyr was fine, since they were already hybrids. But if he was going to turn Dawn into something, like a jungle cat...maybe a cheetah...he wanted her to keep her upright posture and thumbs.

It's gonna be fine." He quickly pressed a button that would change the world's perception of what was happening. If he read the buttons right, it would make everyone perfectly fine with the idea of Dawn being a human-like cheetah. He looked around, and saw that fewer people were staring now.
