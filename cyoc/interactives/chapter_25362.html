    "Excuse me sir."

    "Huh?" You look up from your work to see one of the office secretaries standing in front of your desk, holding a brown box.

    "Someone just dropped this off for you." She leaves the box on your desk, and walks off.

    "I didn't order anything, it's not a holiday so most likely not a present. Weird. Wander who sent this."

    As it's a distraction from work, you quickly open the box to see what is inside.

    You start to look inside the box, but then pull your head back quickly as a knife flies up from the box.

    "Aha!" A high pitcher voice squeems from the box. "You can't escape from me!"

    You glance inside the box to see what exactly is shouting, and see a very small man with a white beard, a red hat, and overalls. He looks, like a lawn gnome.

    "Who sends someone a killer lawn gnome in the mail!?" You scream.

    "Hey! I'm not a lawn gnome! I'm just a regular gnome! Now get down in my reach so I can stab you. I built this knife myself. It's designed to allow me to both fly and attack at the same time."

    "Wait, what?" You stare at the gnome. "That, doesn't make any sense."

    "Oh watch you fool."

    The gnome holds the knife up in the air, as the blade begins to spin around on the handle, and sure enough the gnome begins to fly.

    "Ha! How can you stop me when I can fly!"

    The gnome stops speaking, because at that moment, he flies into the ceiling, head first, and quite hard. He looses his grip on the knife, which spins out of control and lands in your computer, while the gnome thuds to the ground unconsciuos.

    "Alright that was.... Just weird."

    Then, suddenly the gnome disapears in a puff of smoke.

    "What the.... Wait a minute."

    The Book of Myth. It could summon monsters and mythological creatures. Then.

    You quickly run and find the secretary who gave you the package.

    "Where was the man who told you to give me this!?" You shout at her.

    "Oh I met him in the parking lot a nice man."

    You quickly run to the parking lot, hoping to catch the man before he escapes. Was he someone else after the books, or was he sent by whoever has the Book of Mind? You wouldn't know until you caught him. However, it would be best to prepare. You quickly open your bookbag to retrieve the books, then stop. It could get difficult flipping between both books nonstop in a battle, it may be best just to use one to fight.

    What do you do now?
