    Entity #527
    Codename: The Ouroboros Ring

    Appearance: E-527 appears as a jade ring sculpted into an Ouroboros snake eating its own tail while dormant, and will automatically resize itself to fit the ring finger of any potential wearer.

    Known Abilities: When worn, E-527 enters its inactive state, virtually indistinguishable from its dormant state. While inactive, E-527 can only be removed by the wearer themselves and cannot be removed by any other means, even removing the wearers ring-finger does not deactivate it (at this point even the wearer cannot remove it and the entity must be activated (which would regenerate the finger) for the entity to become dormant).

    Aside from the entity’s primary function, it possesses a healing ability should the wearer suffer from a variety of wounds including scars, missing limbs, deafness or blindness (in this case gained through previous injury and not natural causes) and open wounds. Notably, E-527 does not affect any disease nor does it actually heal anything before the rings activation with the total amount of healing done being proportional to the time the ring has been active.

    For E-527 to activate, one of three conditions must be reached. Firstly, the ring will activate if the wearer finds someone they find arousing (if condition 3 is active, a minor mental change makes the wearer capable of feeling aroused by either gender). Secondly, the ring will activate if the wearer feels threatened, is being threatened, is under threat, OR is attacked with lethal intent (see incident log 527-5 for more details). Thirdly, the ring will activate if it has been worn for more than 2 hours straight.

    Upon activation, E-527 will visibly melt into the wearers skin, whom will then rapidly transform into a 7'5" tall anthropomorphic snake with a 10 foot long tail with a colour pattern unique to each user of E-527 (see log 527-3 for list of users and colour palettes and more details). Unless the first condition of activation has been reached the wearer will be devoid of any genitals and markers of gender. If the first condition is reached, the wearer will become the gender opposite the target (IE, if the target is male, the wearer becomes female, etc.) but if the target happens to be hermaphroditic the wearer will automatically default to being a female.

    It is important to note that during the duration of either condition one or two that the wearers consciousness (henceforth referred to as simply Ouroboros while the entity is active) becomes feral-like in disposition, focusing upon fulfilling the conditions requirement without caring for anything else. If the first condition is activated, Ouroboros will, as mentioned previously, immediately turn into the gender opposite the target (notably, their ‘assets’ will become quite large, when male, their penis is upwards of 1 foot in length when erect while, when female, their bust size is measured to be upwards of J-cups).When Ouroboros has changed to the gender opposite their target, they will immediately seek to engage in sex with the target regardless of environment (if the environment poses a risk to the target (both pre- and post-rape) Ouroboros will first make sure the target is in a safe position). It has been deduced that Ouroboros adjusts their target upon coming in contact to be more ‘sufficient’ for them, with a normal female target becoming stretchy enough to take the male Ouroboros entire foot-long penis and their entire load of semen without injury. And male targets ejaculate much more semen than natural into the female Ouroboros raping them and the durability to withstand their rather extreme ‘mating tactics’ like a female target.

    Once an Ouroboros is finished raping a target one of two things happen. If the Ouroboros was male, it will immediately turn inactive, reversing the transformation and E-527 reappears on the wearers ring-finger. If the Ouroboros was female it will instead try to find a secluded spot within a period of up to 10 minutes after which they will immediately begin to lay between 10 to 30 unfertilized eggs, each 1 foot in height and 10 inches in diameter at the thickest. The female target however, like the female Ouroboros is impregnated by the male Ouroboros. This pregnancy lasts unlike the female Ouroboros for 10 days, during which the target has to deal with an unnaturally large belly containing between 5 to 10 large eggs just as large as those laid by a female Ouroboros. Interviews with numerous female targets of different wearers of E-527 describe the act of laying said unfertilized eggs as ‘painful yet very pleasurable’. Becoming an Ouroboros’s target does not appear to have any long term effects but it appears to positively impact the targets family-making with males recieving an increased sperm production paired with sperm ‘potency’ increasing the chance of their partner being impregnated with multiple children. Female targets are more discernibly affected by the Ouroboros, as like a male target, a female target has a vastly increased chance of impregnation paired with a large increase in total children they are capable of giving birth to, with a significant chance of having multiple pairs of twins among their offspring with a much shortened pregnancy period.

    If the second condition is fulfilled then the Ouroboros is feeling threatened and will go on a rampage if provoked, Here we enter the physical abilities of an Ouroboros and why threatening one is a bad idea. An Ouroboros in the second condition wil immediately attack the person or object responsible for the conditions activation with superhuman speed and strength letting them both speed across a long corridor at above 90mph and pry open reinforced doors and ’light blast doors’ or rip apart an M1 Abrams. Similarly, an Ouroboros possesses extreme durability and healing factor capable of growing back entire limbs in seconds while their sheer durability requires anti-tank weaponry on the power of armour-piercing sabot rounds to be damaged to any meaningful degree (see incident logs 527-1, -2, -3 and -5 for more examples and details). Neutralizing a condition-2 Ouroboros requires the usage of another combative entity to be worth it (f. Ex 577-3, 893, 352, 1344-1-x, 3782, 127, 335 and 444), otherwise security personnel dealing with a condition-2 Ouroboros are to stand down and not provoke it and instead move to calmly restrain E-527’s wearer when condition-2 has been fulfilled.

    After this is done, E-527 deactivates and the wearer returns to normal with E-527 present again on their ring-finger, with fully lucid memory of everything their body has done while E-527 was active from either the first or second condition.

    When the third condition is reached and E-527’s wearer has worn it for more than 2 hours straight (time spent in either condition 1 or 2 does not in fact count), they will immediately transform into their genderless Ouroboros form. Unlike with condition 1 or 2, condition 3 lets the wearer keep full control over their actions albeit with a minor mental change making them fully used to their Ouroboros form and how to move it. It is important to know that if condition 3 has been reached that condition 1 and 2 can activate just like normal but upon either conditions fulfilment the Ouroboros will not deactivate and revert back to their human form. Infact if condition 3 has been reached, the Ouroboros must either be neutralized to deactivate or must consume half of their tail, essentially recreating the ouroboros symbol.

    Update: Following long term condition 3 testing it has been observed that after 12 hours since activation, a condition 3 Ouroboros will automatically assume a female gender without condition 1’s activation.

    Containment: When not in use, E-527 is to be contained in a standard issue Useful-Entity-Lockbox sealed via a biometric scanner in an entity-locker inside its assigned site’s upper entity-storage room restricted to senior 5’s and above.

    Other: Because of E-527’s high transformation priority and the fact the wearer is technically under constant effect from E-527 from the moment it is placed on their ring-finger, E-527 is deemed a useful entity for use to negate the effects of low-priority entities both during testing, field-work and to assist in the neutralization of troublesome entities or groups.

    Update: Following the long-term Ouroboros activity experiment with senior agent Ethan Fulveson (whom during the duration of the experiment is to be referred to as Ourie (or 527-2 in logs), as requested by said person), any cross-entity testing and standard testing involving the entity being deactivated is required to be given the green-light by up to 5 members of upper management ( IE. 2’s) and is therefore unavailable.
