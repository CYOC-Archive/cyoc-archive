    Inside the box was a cerulean blue dress with puffy sleeves, 2 white petticoats, a white pinafore apron, a black headband with a matching bow, a white sleeveless undershirt, a pair of white bloomers, a pair of white hip-length stockings, and a pair of black single-straped mary jane shoes. Upon looking at these items, Rachel immediately recognized them as the signature outfit of Alice in Wonderland. Rachel has always loved the books and has seen every film and cartoon adaptation. The animated Disney film in particular is her absolute favorite, having seen it so many times that she knew ever line by heart.
    "I've never seen such a perfect recreation of alice's dress before." Rachel said to herself.
    "I don't know where this came from, but I'm definitely trying this on." So Rachel took the box of clothes upstairs to her room, locked the door and stripped out of her current clothing except her white bra and panties. One by one, she assembled the outfit onto herself. First the undershirt, then the stockings, then the bloomers, then the shoes, then the petticoats, then the dress, then the apron and tying the back with a big bow, and finally straighten out her hair and place the headband on top of her head. Upon looking at her reflection in the full-length mirror, Rachel gasped at what she saw.
    "Wow, this looks even better than I thought it would. I really feel like Alice in Wonderland. My Goodness. This is the best thing that could have happen to me today." She smiled and began curtseying to her own reflection, then started twirling around her room enjoying the feeling of being Alice. Afterwards Rachel lays on her bed and starts thinking about how boring her life has been up to the point she got the package and sighs.
    "I wish my life could be as exciting and nonsensical as Alice's. {laughs} If I had a world of my own, everthing would be nonsense. Nothing woud be what it is, because everthing would be what it isn't. What it is, it wouldn't be. And what it woudn't be, it would." Rachel then gets up and looks over the empty package, happy to receive such a wonderful gift from it, only to realize there's something still inside.
