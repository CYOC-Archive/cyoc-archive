Also, the data stated that Tanner is in second grade as opposed to third, unlike Derrick. At this time in the year when Tanner was originally eight, he was in second grade. Here we go.” After Tanner settled in his younger body, he went into Derrick’s room. “So, now what do ya wanna do “little bro”?” Derrick asked. “I dunno” Tanner replied.
