"And as for Jade", he looked over at her seemingly empty shell and tears welled up in his eyes, "I wanted to punish her for all the bad things she and Kat ever did to me".

    "Look at her!" Rachel threw a hand in the shells direction. "She's some kind of disgusting freak". "What will her family say if they see her like this?" One cautious eye oozed out from the shell followed by the other. "They'll call the government and some scientist will want to cut her up and..."

    "She said something about inheriting the power for her eighteenth birthday".

    "Interesting", Rachel mused to herself. She was cute when she was pondering. "Kat was pretty mean to you in the past, but I think turning you into a frog was a bit over the top".

    "Maybe". Rachel waited a full minute before saying anything else. The only sounds were crickets and the disgusting "slosh" "squish" Jade made as she moved. "We have to assume that the book is not safe in anyone's hands". She glared at Dan as she spoke. "We need to find your sister, and quick, before she hurts herself or, more likely, someone else. Where do we start?"
