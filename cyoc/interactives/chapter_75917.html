    Void... an empty space. That is where I suddenly found myself, if it can even be considered a place... there was nothing, yet somehow there was ground beneath my legs, i realized i was on my knees, unclothed, naked for anybody to see yet there was nobody to see me, nor was there anything, the ground was solid but otherwise featureless, no dirt or stone it wasn’t smooth or rough, this place was the dark silent hell i had been sentenced to and i lay on the ground in despair, no reason to get up to try to move to do anything but embrace what had now become my sad lonely fate.

    Suddenly i wasn’t alone though, somehow, I looked up at a woman, she looked like some kind of werewolf except she was a lot more human, she stared down at me, was she pitying me? who was she? what is she doing here? questions like this flooded my mind but before i knew it she was walking away,...

    I wasn’t fool enough to pass up the opportunity i was given, someone was here i didn’t have to be alone, i spang up to my feet and chased after her as fast as they would carry me suddenly until she came to a halt.

    The next thing i noticed was that we wee on the edge of a forest, as if it had always been there yet i hadn’t seen it before, i was so focused on the wolf girl that i hadn’t noticed it appear but how could i have missed it... she looked at me and suddenly my thoughts stopped as if in fear of her. no... it wasn’t in fear, something about her bled kindness, the expression on her face was a smile of such peace and serenity that given the chance i was quite sure that it would bring even a fierce and intensive battle to a halt instantly if not faster. It was something so gentle that it seemed to reach deep down into your very soul and tell you everything would be alright and it then became clear that it most certainly wasn’t fear my thoughts seemed to stop out of, they had been subjugated to that smile of hers in reverence of something so beautiful, a simple facial expression that had the power to bring violent uncertainty to a calm peaceful stop, i was equally subjugated to this smile, a feeling of awe permeated my entire being at this woman who i had just met, she gazed deeply into my eyes as if asking my trust, her gaze speaking for her, assuring me that if i merely placed faith in her she would free me from this lonely prison...

    My choice was obvious, how could I choose anything but to trust her, I didn’t have many other options really and I was sure she would bring me away from this wretched prison. she took the fact that i hadnt backed away as my acceptance to the deal and while most people would complain and argue that such was hardly considerable as agreeing i would not dare hold such a thing against her.

    i felt my feet stretching as some of my toes fused together and grew black claws. i felt coarse pads ballooning out from the balls of my feet, it was an odd sensation as my feet took on the appearance of a wolf’s hind paws, it was the most liberating sensation however, as if i had been in a cage my entire life and now for the first time in my entire life i was becoming truly free, as if my body had been held down by lead weights as long as i could remember and suddenly they were falling off, i was becoming whole and complete, i felt it deep inside the feeling was permeating to every inch of my entire being i begged for more i wanted it so badly it was like the most amazing drug ever and i had become horribly addicted to this wonderful sensation.
