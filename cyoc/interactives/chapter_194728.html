    Before she could decide, Janet noticed a book was laying on the floor next to her. Janet figured that it had been knocked off the bookshelf upstairs and landed on the main floor, something that has occurred before. Upon picking up the book, Janet turned it over and read the title.

    ALICE'S ADVENTURES IN WONDERLAND / THROUGH THE LOOKING GLASS

    Janet smiled as she saw this and began to reminisce. When she was younger, Janet's Mom would read from this very book to her every night chapter by chapter. Janet had also watched the animated disney film (as well as other adaptations) almost a hundred times over. To say that the story had left an impact on Janet's life would be an understatement. Janet even dressed as Alice for halloween one year when she was 10 years old. Janet remembered loving that costume's toon accurate design of a cerulean blue knee-length silk dress with puffy sleeves, white silk pinafore apron with matching tights, black single-strapped mary jane shoes, and black headband with a bow on top. Even after Halloween, Janet would wear it every chance she got and imagine herself being Alice on her many misadventures. Eventually Janet grew too big for the costume and had to give it away, much to her disappointment.
    Looking back on her childhood, Janet began to imagine herself dressing up like Alice in Wonderland at her current age and playing the role like she did all those years ago, forgetting about the amulet around her neck. Even though she wanted to dress like Alice again, Janet didn't want to actually become her. As soon as Janet thought of this however, the amulet started to glow and work its magic on her. Janet noticed this and immediately realized what was about to happen.
    "WAIT! STOP! I DON'T LITERATELY WANT TO BE ALICE! CANCEL IT, NOW!" Janet shouted at the amulet, but it was too late and she could only stare as her clothing began to change.
    Janet watched her reflection and saw the sleeves of her shirt puff up a bit and a collar form around the front neck. Her frilly skirt became silk smooth and expanded to her knees. Both clothes then changed from their respected colors to blue and then fused together into a dress, while a white petticoat appeared under the skirt. Her white panties changed into white bloomers. Her black stockings turned white and stretched to her hips, while her shoes changed into mary janes. A white pinafore apron appeared over Janet's head, landed over her shoulders, covered most of her front and tied itself around her waist with a big bow on the back. Finally a black headband with a matching bow materialized on top of her head, completing the outfit.
    Janet stared at her reflection for a minute, making sure her transformation was finished and examining her new clothes as well as the amulet still around her neck.
    "Man, I need to be careful what I think about with this thing on. I don't want to make any drastic changes just yet. At least it only changed my clothes into Alice's outfit, which actually looks even better than the costume I originally had. Maybe being Alice won't be so bad after all."
    Janet continued to admire her Alice in Wonderland outfit while she thought about what to do next.
