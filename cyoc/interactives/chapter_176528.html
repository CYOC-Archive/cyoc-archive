    You decide to generate a new sentence, to see if there might be something more entertaining to change.

    “The board members are all wearing suits.” You look around the room. Every single board member is a man. Smirking, you change “suits” to “dresses”. Immediately, the men’s outfits change. However, other than being on men, the dresses don’t look out of place in the boardroom. You generate another sentence.

    “The board members are all wearing professional dresses.” Well, that was easy. You change “professional” to “slutty”. Promptly, the business appropriate dresses become skimpy, brightly colored, and cling to the men’s bodies. To your delight, they are also wearing high heels to match their new slutty attire.

    “Alex?” At the sound of your name, you look up. Linda is looking at you. “Are you taking notes?”

    “Yes,” you say, quickly taking out your notepad.

    “You better be,” Linda says. “You’re on thin ice already, since you decided to show up in an outfit that doesn’t match dress code.” She gestures to the other intern, Clair. Clair isn’t wearing a dress like the board members, but a just as slutty mini skirt and crop top. “Use Clair as an example of how to dress in the future.”

    “Got it,” you say. Linda continues the meeting. You discretely generate a new sentence.

    “Alex isn’t following dress code.” You don’t really want to wear what Clair is wearing, or what the board members are, so you change “isn’t” to “is”. You look back at Clair. Clair’s outfit now is exactly like yours, though the board members are thankfully still in their skimpy dresses. You’ve gotten out of this sticky situation, but are upset with Linda, so you decide to take some revenge and make a new sentence.

    “Linda’s outfit is perfect for a CEO.” Sure enough, while Clair and the board members have all had their clothing changed, Linda hasn’t. Her behavior at the beginning of the meeting is perfect inspiration. You change “CEO” to “baby”.

    Immediately, Linda’s clothes morph from a gray power suit to a thick pink diaper and matching frilly pink dress, so short that it doesn’t even begin to cover her diaper. To your delight, even the gum she was chewing on has changed, becoming a sparkly pink pacifier clipped to her dress.

    “Any questions?” Linda asks around her pacifier. Everyone shakes their heads. “Good. Alex and Clair, follow me.” The room slowly clears out. You and Clair follow Linda out of the room and down the hall. Linda waddles due to her incredibly thick diaper, and suckles on her pacifier so loudly that you can hear it from where you are.

    This is great. But all the changes you’ve made cause you to have a few questions, so you take out the phone and generate a new sentence, hoping to clear up some of your confusion.
