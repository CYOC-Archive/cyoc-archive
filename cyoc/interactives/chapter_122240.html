    There was a quiet and deep silence. His parents were on the floor, still as unliving clothes. He stared at them and became furious.
    And that was just the first thing he had to try with his new power: to restore them into humans again. He quickly wished to turn them back into their normal bodies, and also made it so they didn't remember anything that happened in the last few hours.
    Dustin was changing, he was maturing. He was discovering he really loved his parents, and he had to be grateful for everything they did for him. He didn't want them to be scared by what could happen from now on with that free powerful monster going around. So he wished they could return to their normal lives and were unaware of what was happening.

    He saw how both underwear lying on the floor quickly morphed into their parents again, their clothes reappearing from nowhere and covering them. Dustin stared at the magic happening in front of him. Yes, it was real, he had the power. He stayed still watching how their parents returned to their normal lives. He was about to cry.

    "You okay, son?" his mom asked worried.

    "Y-yes, m-mom... It's just that... I love you both." Dustin said feeling thrilled. Then he went upstairs, wishing they could not disturb him for now.

    He entered his bedroom and locked the door with his mind. He smiled as he realized he was getting used to it so quickly.

    He looked at his own reflection at the mirror. He now could change anything, including himself. And that was the next thing to do. He couldn't know what was going to happen from now on, but he had to be prepared for anything. He decided he needed new Ty-proof clothes, which would be indestructible and impossible to change or disappear by Ty's power.
    He imagined himself wearing a superhero-like costume and, in a flash, his new clothes materialized into a full body tight fit spandex suit, all his old clothes diseappearing. It was shiny red with black gloves, boots and briefs.

    "Cool!" he exclaimed excited.

    He figured he could also change himself a bit. He was a jock, but now he had a chance to become even bigger and... why not? older. He quickly aged 10 years and morphed into a grown up version of himself. And he also gave himself a huge muscle boost, turning him into a stocky and sturdy man, with bulging muscles everywhere.

    He couldn't help but smirk at his new body. Things were going to be fun if he managed to defeat Ty.

    But he had no time to waste. Dustin quickly wished to teleport himself where Ty was right now, and it happened faster than he expected.

    Dustin looked around the school. It was a complete mess. There were a lot of rodents getting out scared from a classroom where he could heard Ty's evil laugh. There were also insects and inanimate clothes too. He guessed all of them were students or the school personnel.
    Dustin quickly wished for everyone to return to their human bodies and to put them in a safe place, so all animals and clothes disappeared before the transformation.

    "What's going on...!?" Ty shouted angrily. The devil kid turned back and saw the new Dustin.

    "Are you the responsible of this...!? Who the fuck are you...?" Ty asked the young disguised man.

    "Don't you recognize me... brother?" Dustin smirked.

    "D-Dustin...!? How the hell... YOU'VE STOLEN THE FUN. YOU DON'T KNOW WHAT YOU'VE DONE!" Ty said aggresively without even wondering how Dustin had got the power.

    Ty started to morph slowly into a giant green dragon, destroying the whole school building and becoming ready for a fight with his brother.
