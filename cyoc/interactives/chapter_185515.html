    - "Yeah, okay, sure." said Jake thinking the old man will do some cheap magic trick or something.
    - "Alright than put on the ring." said the owner pointing to the box.

    He tried to check if he is okay, called for him, but nothing happened. He was not able to speak nor move. He was frightened.
