    The New Andrew

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Wolfy 13 years ago A BM


    "Hey there Ty! Can I come in?"

    "Yeah, yeah sure you can buddy. What's up?"

    "Not much, bored over at my house so I thought I would come and hang out with you."

    "Ah... I see. So what do you want to do?"

    "I am open to whatever Ty, you know me and how I just go with the flow."

    "That's true... well why don't you come in and we can find something to do."

    Andrew walked in the house and went directly into the living room to see what Ty was doing, while Ty closed and locked the main door. A part of Ty wanted to make a wish so that only he could open any of the doors or windows, which could be used as exits, but Ty decided to wait and see if that was going to be something he would have to resort to if Andrew tried to leave before Ty was done with him. With the door locked, Ty walked into the living room where Andrew was just staring at the 65 inch television screen and what was on it.

    "Ty... what game are you playing?"

    "Oh... it's a redo of an old video game for the NES. It's called 'Werewolf"."

    "Oh my god, are you serious? I used to play that game all the time! When did you get this, when did this come out and can I play?"

    "It actually is still a beta that only a select few were chosen to try out on the PS3 and I finished downloading it a few hours ago. I've played through the single board a couple times now and it just happens to be my favorite one!"

    "Really! Which one?!"

    "The werebear portion and yeah, you can play."

    "Oh thank you, thank you, thank you! This was always my favorite level, other than the werewolf ones of course. I wonder what the transformation is like now in such a higher rendered version?"

    "It's awesome, it looks pretty real!"

    Andrew just smiled, placing all of his attention on the screen and started playing. As he played, Andrew forgot to swallow his own saliva, which was evident as Ty watched drool pour out of the side of Andrew's mouth. Ty just left him alone, knowing Andrew was enjoying himself. When Andrew got to the area where you got to transform into the Anthrobear for the first time, he paused the game. Without taking his eyes off the screen, Andrew wiped the drool from his mouth, while taking in a deep breath and unpaused the game. Andrew made the move and the cutscene came on where you could see your awesome and fully rendered character go through the full transformation into a werebear. Andrew literally jumped off the sofa and celebrated after pausing the game. Ty could only chuckle at what he saw, knowing that Andrew was really enjoying himself. Before Andrew started playing again, he said something under his breath...

    "What I wouldn't do to be a werebear like that..."

    Hearing that, Ty got an idea and it was a good one. Ty wanted to give Andrew what he wanted, knowing that in doing so, Ty may get exactly what he wanted from Andrew. Under his breath, Ty made a wish. "I wish Andrew would become a werebear, with everything Andrew wanted physically and after he is done transforming, Andrew will thank me however he wanted."

    As Andrew kept playing, he noticed his vision got blurred and he had to stop, not wanting to die or lose a life. Before Andrew knew it, his vision came back, but his stomach churned a bit, making him pretty nauseous.

    "Um... Ty, I might need... your... bathroom... ... I don't... feel so good."

    "Ok Andrew, not a problem. Just let me know when so I can help you if you'll need it."

    "Thanks buddy... you're such a great friend!"

    As Andrew finished talking, he looked down at his hand as he felt the room spin and noticed that his hands weren't really hands anymore. They were a combination of human hands and what looked like bear paws.

    Not knowing what else to do, if anything, Andrew shouted "Oh my god, this is so cool!"
