But before Molly had time to object, he put the pacifier back in her mouth. But the fact that he didn't bother to clean it after picking it up from the floor was the least of her worries, for he suddenly picked her up.
    "Sheesh, she's kind of heavy for being a little kid..." Jackson commented, ignoring her squirming as he carries her up to their shared room. Charlie's following behind, a content smile on his face as he's not at all bothered by her visibly flustered expression.

Meanwhile, She pokes at her diaper - just wearing something like this felt humiliating and wrong...even with how she looked at the moment.

Problem was...it was stuck inside the diaper, and she already knew that she didn't have the strength to tear, either... Scared and frustrated, she covers her face behind her chubby hands, rubbing her forehead.
