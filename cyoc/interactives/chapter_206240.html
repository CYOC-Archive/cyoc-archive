    Rock tiger

    added A year ago A I Furniture Feline

    John was alone in his home, his parents and siblings won't be back until tomorrow and he had no pets nor even a plant. So, he had just one but dumb idea, put it on a rock.

    He went to his backyard and looked for the nicest rock he could find. Then he took it and put it on the kitchen table and proceeded to put the collar on it.

    Almost instantly, some blue energy lights came from the collar and moved through the rock's surface. John noticed that the rock was changing its shape and looking more like an animal. In that moment, John was thinking about a baby tiger and that was the shape the rock was taking!!!

    When the rock had the exact shape of a baby tiger, the animal statue began to move. Erratically at first, but very natural after a few seconds, it even blinked but he had no pupils, so it was barely perceptible.

    John felt confident enough to rub the tiger's stone skin and the creature reacted like a puppy would do. Then, John tried to hug it, but it was too heavy that he can hold it for more than a few seconds.

    He put it on the ground and looked for something to feed it, but the creature didn't accept any of the food John was giving to it. That when John got the idea of giving it some rocks which the tiger accepted delightfully and ate them like dog kibble. John was surprised of noticing that the tiger was growing as much as the rock it ate.

    He liked his new pet, but now he was wondering what would his family think about it. Should he hide it or it's better to face the problem directly? He wasn't sure but while he was thinking his phone started ringing. It was his sister, what will he do?
