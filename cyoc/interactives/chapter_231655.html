    The Body Part Manipulation Gun or BPM Gun for short, was a miraculous tool that could change body parts in many unthinkable ways.

    The gun had the appearance of some kind of ray gun, counting with its respective trigger and a screen with buttons to select its modes. It had a manual with the following instructions:

    "Thanks for using The BPM Gun! This wonderful tool will help you to fulfill your deepest desires using their variety of modes that can be used in phenomenal ways! To the point you could look like a totally different person, or even not human! The modes the BPM Gun counts with are:"

    [REMOVE]
    - Select this mode on the screen and then shoot your target. A 3D model of target will appear where you can select what body part(s) do you want to detach from them, then press OK to save the changes.

    [SWAP]
    - Select this mode on the screen and then shoot two or more different body parts (from the same or different target(s)) to swap places between them. The manual showed an image of a man using the gun on himself to swap one his hand by one of his foot.

    [MERGE]
    - Select this mode on the screen and then shoot a body part with another or more to merge them into a completely new part (the results of the fusion are customizable). Note: it can work with inanimate things but should be at least one real body part in the merging.

    [ATTACH]
    - Select this mode on the screen and then shoot one or more body parts (or inanimate thing) to select it/them, for the next step press OK and shoot a target to make it/them part of the body of the target. You can choose in what parts of the body the body parts are placed.
    - This mode also counts with a sub mode called FUNCTION. As the name suggests you can choose to add a function to the placed body part. Showing some examples with images like attaching a third arm and assigning to it the function of a tongue to make it produce saliva, or attaching a dildo and giving it the function of a real penis. Note: the functions assigned only work when they're attached to a host's body, they lose their function if they're detached.

    Additional notes:
    - The BPM Gun can't create or erase body parts from nowhere as it just manipulates already existing ones.
    - The functions of the BPM Gun can apply to animal body parts and inanimate things in some cases as previously stated.
    - If an inanimate object already has a function like a blender or hose as some examples, the user can choose if the object keeps or not its function when attached to an target's body.
    - The BPM Gun also counts with a reality alteration option, where you can choose if people would be aware of the changes you did with the gun, you can also choose who are or aren't aware of it.

    The gun was ignored by many that managed to look at it, to the point that cobwebs and dust appeared on it. Until today, the shop received a new client that finally bought it and looked very convinced after hearing the whole explanation of its uses and how it worked. Their intentions with the gun where truly a mystery.
