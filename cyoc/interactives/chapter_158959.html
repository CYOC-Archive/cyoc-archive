    "Are most werefoxes Japanese?" Rachel asked.

    "We think that is where the werefox strain for the were-virus began... but given debates over the scientific and mystical origins of what makes a were a were, that's something that may be hard to pin down," Megumi answered, "as the Red Fox, which most werefoxes' were forms are based on has had a large global range even before British lords brought foxes to North America for sport hunting. It's why things like the Mandatory Were Law exists. It's all stuff kept within the community, just in case someone is transformed while pulling the prank."
