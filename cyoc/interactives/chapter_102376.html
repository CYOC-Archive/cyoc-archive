    Night of the Imps

    added by The REAL Nobody 8 years ago AR BM S TG O

    The date was 2013, April 13th. The location, Hell, Michigan, United States of America.

    The time was 1 PM, 13 minutes and 13 seconds.

    Humanity (what precious time it had left) would call it the time of the arrival of the Innocent Malice. Those who would soon rule the world instead, called it 'The Day We Started Having Fun And Nobody Could Tell Us To Stop.'

    Come claimed it was the rapture, and God had forsaken his children, and all that was left for the demons to claim humanity has their own. Others insisted they had to be invaders from another universe. Others suggested they were tulpas, beings created out of humanity's collective belief.

    The first imp herself, Princess Lilligoth, declared that she'd come into being because she felt like it "Wanna make something of it?" And she'd do whatever she felt like, whenever she felt like, however she felt like it. And you could stop her, go ahead and stop ya buncha weenies!

    She appeared in what counted... more or less, as the center of Hell Michigan, Declaring the 266 people who currently lived in Hell Michigan were now her subjects and minions, and would have the honor of being the first. Some laughed, most ignored her, a few fainted at seeing a little girl in such a risqué outfit.

    She had bright red skin, spiral yellow eyes, black horns sticking out of her head, ghostly white short hair, her finger and toe nails razor sharp... and wearing what might counted as a little girls' white swim suit. Some wondered if this was some sick creatures' idea of a joke.

    Then Princess Lilligoth touched her first human, a middle aged man, they burst into flames, their skin turned bright red like hers, their hair turned white, their eyes becoming the same yellow, a different set of horns grew from his, her head along with a set of cloven hooves Lilligoth (PRINCESS Lilligoth) didn't have, along with a long spade ended tail.

    The man who used to be there had been named Howard... now Ho'lith cheered and grinned insanely at her princess, showing a full set of fangs that the Princess of Imps showed her only subject. Her domain would be expanding every fast.

    This marked the beginning of the end of the human race, and the dawn of the kingdom of the imps, none would be spared, none would escape, and the end result would be a world of pure chaos without rules, without parents, and no mercy. A world of wild demonic little girls were the only rule was anything was okay if you could get away with it. Where brutal rough housing was the name of the game, and cheating wasn't the question, but the answer.

    Ho'lith herself didn't give a fart about the human she'd been a few seconds ago, he could go suck an egg if he was still around, as for his wife and kids, she'd add them to her Princess' kingdom soon enough.

    Only a few people were running yet, the rest thought it was a magic act. The Princess and Ho'lith grinned at each other, and leapt at the crowd. Let the games begin.

    Later, Princess Lilligoth would arbitrary pick out her royal court from whoever she happened to feel like out of her first 100 victims... to be her generals in the surprisingly brutal and one sided battle of some fiendish little girls against 6 billion people armed with tanks, missiles, guns, atomic bombs, and the like... humanity had been doomed almost from the start to lose.
