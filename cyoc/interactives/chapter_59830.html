    Ty's Power

    Kent inhales all of Jeremy and they merge

    Ty was sitting in his room, wondering what to do next, when he heard the front door to the house open and
    close again. Then he heard his stepmother shout something.

    "TY! Kent is here to see you!"

    "Okay! I'm in my room!" Ty shouted back.

    Kent was that shy, geeky boy with glasses who was in Ty's class in school. Kent has always been very nice to
    Ty, and he was very timid. In fact, Ty was one of Kent's only friends in school. Kent really didn't talk to
    anyone else, because he was so shy. He wore glasses and he always wore a buttoned shirt with jeans or slacks.
    He didn't play sports, but he was very smart and he always aced every math and science test in school.

    "Hey, Ty..." Kent spoke softly, as he peered through the door opening. "Can I... come in?"

    "Of course, dude! Get in here!" Ty grinned, while keeping an eye on the invisible cloud of air, that was
    floating in the room, which was Jeremy.

    Kent quietly opened the door and walked in, quietly closing the bedroom door behind him. Kent then walked up
    to Ty, passing right through the cloud, that was Jeremy. Ty realized that Kent must have inhaled some of
    Jeremy already.

    "Whatcha doing?" Kent asked him.

    "Oh, just hanging out. What about you?"

    "Nothing much. Wanna play some video games?" Kent asked.

    By now, Kent had inhaled all of Jeremy, since he was standing right at the spot, where Jeremy was standing
    before. In fact, Kent had looked down and spotted the pile of clothes on the floor, but he thought that those
    were Ty's dirty clothes, so he didn't say anything.

    "Sure, let's sit on the floor and play some games on the Xbox!" Ty suggested.

    "Great! Let me get these clothes out of the way!" Kent said, as he moved the clothes out of the way with his
    shoe.

    Suddenly, another voice spoke up, coming out of Kent's mouth.

    "Hey! Be careful with my clothes! Don't make them dirty with your shoes!" Jeremy's voice spoke up angrily,
    coming from Kent's mouth.

    Kent gasped out loud and stopped.

    "What? Who? Who said that? That wasn't my voice!" Kent exclaimed.

    "That's me! You're getting my clothes dirty with your shoes!" Jeremy grumbled.

    Ty laughed out loud. He realized that Jeremy had merged into Kent's body since Kent had inhaled him fully.

    "Whoa! That voice! It's coming out of my mouth!" Kent gasped.

    "I turned Jeremy into air and you just inhaled him!" Ty explained to Kent. "You know Jeremy from school?"

    "Jeremy? The dude who plays on the soccer team?" Kent wondered.

    "Yeah! That's me! And, guess what! I got practice tomorrow! So I need to use your body!" Jeremy's voice spoke
    again.

    Kent's mouth dropped wide open as he tried to understand what has happened to him, while Ty tried to come up
    with the right words to explain to his shy friend that he was now sharing his body with a classmate who was a
    member of the school soccer team...
