Rather than climb the steps like a 'big boy' he got down on his hands and knees and crawled up them one by one. Looking like a baby who hadn't learned to walk yet Thomas giggled at the fact he had temporarily given up his 'toddler' status by embracing such an infantile form of locomotion.
