    Sharon was taken aback by the new voice alongside realizing she was speaking underwater, then again as a mermaid it should have been expected, so looking around hoping to find the second voice’s source Sharon soon found herself face-to-face with this new face. A face belonging to a woman in her mid-20s with flowing light-brown hair and a figure matching maybe the best cheerleaders from professional sports matches and who herself was a mermaid, same tail and top type as Sharon herself even in colour only even the top was real scales.

    “So where’ca from?” the new mermaid asked in what to Sharon was a strong Brooklyn accent “And what’s ya’ name?”. “Oh... sorry” Sharon started while briefly peeking back at the resort “I’m Sharon... Sharon Anderson. Here on vacation with my husband and kids”. “Oh you’re that kinda newbie” the new mermaid responded “dang in that case I was-” but then she noticed Sharon was still their “Oh... my bad. Bet’ca kinda confused on what’s going on aren’t ca?”. “I’m guessing you’ve been here...longer or more frequent than me?” Sharon guessed. “Yep... name’s Juna” the mermaid greeted “so where’s your fella off to?”. “He should still be at the pool.
