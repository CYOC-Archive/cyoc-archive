    Emily chuckled at her brother-turned-sister before giving her own wave back. “Bye you two!” Emily stepped out the door and exited her house, taking in the fresh air. She had big plans today. With her transforming powers, the world, nay, reality itself was her oyster! The possibilities are endless!!!

    And that was Emily’s plan today. She wanted to see how far she could push herself and her powers and that meant transforming the entire world! She had already done it once by accident when she transformed everyone in the world into a copy of herself, both in body and mind. Altering the world required a lot of power though, and the resulting change left Emily completely drained of her powers! It would slowly build back up over time, thankfully, but Emily had to wait a whole 5 months to make a reality change on a worldwide scale again and fix everything!

    Which is why Emily had to think carefully about what to transform the world into. If she wasn’t careful, she could be stuck for 5 months with a reality she hates. Emily thought long and hard about this change. She had already decided to make everybody unaware of the change, but unaware about what?

    Maybe she could do something with gender? Swapping everyone’s gender was a pretty classic transformation. Maybe she could swap gender roles instead? Have women be the dominant gender and make men submissive instead. Or perhaps Emily could make a single-gender world? She was bisexual and loved the idea of only women being around, but an All-male world sounded intriguing too. Or maybe she should just do away with gender altogether?

    Instead of gender, how about she change everyone’s race? Emily loved Japanese culture, what would the world look like if everyone was from that one country? She could do the same with a different country and culture too! Spain, the UK, India, anywhere!

    How about she messes with everyone’s ages? Emily thought it would be funny if everyone regressed to Erin’s age and kids ruled the world, with no adults to stop them. Maybe Emily could transform everyone into a teenager? It would certainly be interesting to live in a world full of people her age. Emily thought back to her middle school recital and how she transformed everyone there into drunk college students hosting a rave. What if she did that again, but on a larger scale? Emily did enjoy aging herself up into a MILF from time to time, revelling the feel of being sexy and mature. What if she made a whole world of just MILFS and DILFS?

    Or instead of age, Emily plays with the time period! This wasn’t time travelling like aging or regressing people’s ages, but instead changing the world so that people were that time period’s version of themselves. Emily always wondered what she and her friends and family would look like if they were living during the 1920s, 50s or 80s! Maybe she could go even further and make the world prehistoric or futuristic.

    Emily thought back to changing people. But instead of gender or age, how about something completely different? Maybe she could transform the human race into animals? Emily did enjoy transforming herself into a cat every so often. Maybe instead of animals, how about something more mythological? Maybe she could become a mermaid and have everyone live underwater? Or perhaps a world full of vampires and have it so it’s permanently night? How about a world full of angels where everyone’s kind and pure of heart? Or the opposite where everyone’s a mischievous demon or succubus? Emily thought maybe a Fantasy setting could be better, having a variety of races like elves, dwarves, etc.? Or what if everyone wasn’t human but instead a robotic/android version of themselves instead?

    Emily kept thinking about possible transformations, but her mind kept going back to when the world was just clones of herself. The narcissist within her loved seeing her beautiful self everywhere she looked, even delving into some carnal pleasure with her clones. It was also very peaceful as well, nothing but Emilys giving each other lovely compliments and playful caresses. Then Emily wondered, maybe instead of herself, what if everyone was a clone of someone else? “That could also be fun… and sexy.” She devilishly thought.

    Eventually, Emily made her decision. She was ready to spend 5 months in her brand new world. With a loud clap of her hands, another worldwide reality alter occurred, the world oblivious as their very lives shifted.
