    The Zoo

    All dressed up and nowhere to howl

    added by Skye 2 years ago A

    Feeling panicked, you grab the closest costume to you and ran to a dark corner of the store. The costume was fluffy to the touch but you couldn't see what it was because it was too dark. You slip your feet into the suit, it felt like a paw so you could tell that it was some sort of animal. You put on the rest of the costume and discovered that it was a one-piece suit head and all. It was quite small but there wasn't time to change now so you forced your way into the costume but it was very tight so you had to bend down a bit. You tried to zip the costume up but the paws prevented you from moving your fingers easily so you had to rub your back up against the wall to close the zipper. The costume was surprisingly comfortable as it hugged every part of your body including providing a tight soft space for your head. Despise the suits jaw moving as you tried to speak, your voice did not come out. Rather a quiet dog's whine. "Wait... I can't talk?" You thought but before you could do anything more the lights flicked on and you saw yourself in the mirror beside you. You were inside a pure White wolf suit but due to your short stature and small suit, you looked more like a dog than a wolf. "Well well well... What do we have here" you turned around and saw the store clerk staring at you. You tried to talk but all that came out was a whine. The clerk grabbed you by the suit's scruff and dragged you to the children's area of the zoo. "The children are going to love playing with you 'doggy' " the clerk said and laughed to herself. After the clerk left you tried to undo the costume but your new paws prevented you from opening it. What now?
