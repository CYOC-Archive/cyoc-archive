    Casper considered transforming the last few humans on the beach, but decided against it, something about doing so felt arbitrary. Like it was cheating somehow.

    He noticed a handful of Synthetics had followed Ashley's example, and wore sun dresses or swim suits, but these all seemed to be for aesthetics rather than any practical purpose, and the nudist on the beach were still the vast overwhelming majority.

    The big example was a beautiful white wolf woman walking across the beach with a yellow sun dress her tail swinging in the air, holding an open parasol.

    He did notice what the hotdog vendor was selling (now a rubber light brown monkey with cartoon like eyes) was now more suited to Synthetics than humans, maybe they were simply his larger customer base here. After all, this did seem to be the 'go to' beach for Synthetics now.

    He also noticed that many of the younger girls had again been 'upgraded' in the chest department. One of the naked busty rubber bunnie girls stepped on a rusted nail hidden in the surf, and her foot suffered no damage while the rusted nail bent sideways. It seemed being barefoot wasn't really a danger to Synthetics. Then again, suffering from the elements, and modesty, all seemed to be meaningless concepts to their kind.

    He saw a couple policemen going down the street on bicycles. He used the ring, letting them unconscious decide themselves. One became a wolf, and the other a cougar. Though it seemed they stayed true to the template established before, with POLICE in yellow on their deep navy blue backs, and their skin being sky blue. Casper wondered if they were born or made like that, or whether it was a form of dye job or modification. Either way, they pedaled bare footed on their bikes still. Both wearing their duty belt, radio, hat, and their badges hung around their necks with their barcodes.

    But both were effectively naked. A blue patch over their nozzles. They continued on their circuit without even stopping.

    It seemed the powers that be didn't mind him changing police, then again, they WERE still doing their duty to the best of their ability, so it wasn't like had made into his minions or anything.

Casper felt the water pressure on him, but his body resisted it the way the bunnie girl had resisted being punctured by the rusted nail. Their voices couldn't travel farther than a six feet or so at most, but as long as they didn't accidentally didn't float to the top, Casper had to admit, the view underwater was fantastic!

    He was naked, underwater, seeing a view most humans never would. It was incredibly seeing the deep blue sea beyond and before him.

    The diver practically flew underwater. Ashley and Casper sat down underwater and simply watched him as he went about his work.

The girl became a purple feline bombshell that left Casper's jaw hanging open and actually made Ashley elbow him a bit. The lad became a golden antelope that made Janet an Casey drool (if Synthetics actually did that).
