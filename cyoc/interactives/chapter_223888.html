    At the S.P.E.A.R HQ, the gym was currently being occupied by two of it's members, Sgt Cole Harris and Hailey Meyers, who was still getting used to being a vampire. Behind a two-way mirror, Director Sarah McMillan was watching the two of them wrestle with equipment on each of them. As she had been watching the two of them for the last ten minutes, Jacob Moreland came into the room to watch as well.

    "May I assume she's gone rogue on us?" Jacob Moreland said half joking.

    "She hasn't, she's been progressing very well in her physical training. She's got a good heart Jacob, you need to get it through your head that she doesn't want to devour you or anyone here." Sarah said to her.

    "Yeah, I thought that about a few of them early on in my career. Once I had a guy I trusted on the job, he said he didn't want to kill any of us in our group, then of course, he leads us into a trap. Bastard, I killed him without hesitation when I got the chance." Jacob told.

    "Yes, but since then you've been associated with a former vampire prince, a vampire mother and her daughter, and now Hailey here. Which reminds me, has Duke considered joining up with us yet?" Sarah asked as they watched Cole pin Hailey down on the ground and he patted on the ground slowly to ten before Hailey managed to find the strength to push him back up and pin him on the ground and patted the ground quickly to ten.

    "Duke just wants to live a regular life right now with his girlfriend. As for the girl, right now, I tolerate her more than anything. Though even with her vampire strength, I didn't think she'd pin Sgt. Harris down that fast." Jacob said.

    "While we're on the subject of recruits, have you considered your own boys for this?" Sarah asked.

    "That's a conversation for another day, I've got to go meet with Henry and Anna." Jacob said leaving the room.

    Sarah watched as Hailey helped Cole up off the ground as they shook hands with Cole congratulating Hailey for winning.

    "Hailey, great job today. If you're done with your training take a rest for the rest of the day, you've earned it." Sarah said through the speaker as Hailey smiled.
