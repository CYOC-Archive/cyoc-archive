    Everyone in the cabin was a little bit uncomfortable but they tried their best to get to know one another. Sarah decided to try and strike up a conversation with Caleb first as she believed he had the most experience out of anyone in the room.

    "So Caleb, you and your family have gone up against plenty of the supernatural before, right?" Sarah asked.

    "Indeed we have, seen all sorts of people and creatures from the good to the bad." Caleb replied nodding.

    "...And the bad? Would those be the same kind of bad creatures that took your father's arm?" Sarah asked.

    Caleb didn't answer for a moment and instead took a deep breath and nodded remembering the moment. "It was alright, she was a bad kind of creature."

    "She?"

    "A vampire."

    "Yikes, that woman must've been fierce to take his arm off like that." Sarah said.

    "She was actually an 8 year-old girl, or at least looks like one. She's the most ruthless vampire I ever saw. She tore my dad's arm off with glee and smiled while doing it." Caleb explained recalling the night.

    "Jesus." Sarah said shaking her head almost unable to process seeing that.

    Michelle was over near Nat and Rei looking a little uncomfortable and awkward.

    "First time being amongst people of the supernatural? Don't worry, they're all just normal, just a little different in little ways." Rei said trying to reassure the woman.

    "So what kind of supernatural being are you?" Michelle asked.

    "I'm not, I'm a little Russian, and a little Japanese mixed in with America. But my girlfriend and future wife is a damphir, that's part vampire. She gets it from her mom, but she's one of the good ones, and hot ones." Rei said whispering the last part making Nat blush as she nudged her girlfriend.

    "That reminds me, you two know all about the uh...events from last year, right?" Michelle asked. Rei and Nat both nodded. "We lived it." Nat replied.

    "Well, Agent McMillan originally wanted to find her old boss. But do you two have any idea what might have happened to a local cop that disappeared after turning in her ex-police partner? Duchess Jenkins, I think was her name." Michelle said looking through her phone.

    Nat and Rei looked at each other and shook their heads.

    "If I had to guess though, she might have gone off with the vampires." Nat concluded.

    Michelle nodded and then looked at the photo they had on file of her. Michelle felt her heart beat a little when looking at the picture of Duchess Jenkins. She privately wanted a sure answer on Jenkins, but she didn't want to tell Agent McMillan about it just yet.

    Dave and Greg were sitting with Bear Jr. staring at him and unsure of just what to say next.

    "I know what you're thinking gentleman, yes, I know I'm a better and before you say anything I've heard enough bear related jokes from my wife over the years. So please don't call me 'Yogi' or 'Pooh' or anything like that, I'm simply Bear Jr.." Bear Jr. said as the two still sort of sat quietly.

    "I thought your name was Baby Bear?" Dave finally said as Bear Jr. sighed while pinching his nose.

    "My father was named Bear Sr.. My name by birth is Bear Jr., 'Baby' was a nickname my mother gave me when I was young. The only people to call me Baby are my mother and my wife." Bear Jr. explained.

    Greg looked over at Goldie and then back to Bear Jr.

    "So...how did you and her?" Greg said making hand gestures.

    "Magic, marriage, and being able to turn into a bear helps. I hope that's all your questions about my...private life." Bear Jr. answered.

    While everyone else was talking to the agents, Connor, Spark, Calista, and Miles were trying to talk to Chenoa about skin walkers.

    "Chenoa, please, you have to tell us anything you know about skin walkers. Or at least the ones that live in this area." Connor asked.

    "I cannot I am sorry. All I can tell you is that they're dangerous and can turn into animals to hide themselves." Chenoa said.

    Miles gently took Chenoa's hands and looked into her eyes to plead.

    "Chenoa, I myself promised as did everyone here to help find your parents. Please can't you tell us anymore?" Miles pleaded.

    Chenoa shook her head while resting it on Miles's chest.

    "I'm Sorry Miles, I cannot, you are very kind as are your friends and maybe these new people. But my people are forbidden to talk about skin walkers with outsiders. Please do not hate me for this." Chenoa said apologizing.

    "I could never hate you Chenoa, I know the feeling of keeping up tradition." Miles said as he gave Chenoa a small hug.

    "Maybe Goldie and Bear Jr. have a little more information. Or even Caleb." Calista suggested as they left Miles and Chenoa alone.
