    Kayla grabbed her purse and went out the door digging through her purse, she found her keys. She put them in the car door but for some reason they wouldn't turn.
    She pulled them out looking to see if anything was wrong with them but as far as she could tell they were still alright. Trying again they once again didn't want to turn but then suddenly turned as if they had never been jammed.
    "Huh, that was odd," She thought to herself, but then shook it off.
    She started the engine and taking it out of park, started to back out of the garage. She lost control of the car however and her new Mercedes hit the wall well off in the opposite direction she was supposed to be go.
    This wasn't her day, yes, but still she couldn't figure out why she had so much trouble on a simple turn that she made every day. Especially since she was the best driver in her family, though she did have a brother and a cousin (female) who could give her a run for her money.
    Correcting herself, she regained her skillful driving abilities and made it to work with no further problems.
    She went through security but her card had to be scanned twice before it registered her as an employee. She once again blue it off and continued on her way.
    She was pleased to notice that there was a new and very handsome intern today and she considered whether she seduce him or not.
    In the end she decided to just walk as seductively as she could, smile showing off her perfect teeth and the rest would be up to him on whether he followed her or not.
    The day passed by slowly and unfortunately she had not seen the intern since that morning and was beginning to doubt herself.
    "What's wrong? Am I getting too old? No, I'm only 30, but then again it does look like I've been letting myself go, I mean my lovehandles haven't been this pudgy since I lost my baby fat. I should probably go on a diet." she thought to herself finishing up her last bit of work. She got up from her desk, turning off the computer and headed for the bathroom. For some reason she couldn't put her finger on she felt somehow wrong going in there almost as if she wasn't supposed to. She shook it off after all it was just the ladies restroom and anyone woman who worked there could use it.
    She washed her hands and looked in the mirror.
    "Huh, I guess I haven't gained as much weight as I thought." She said out loud before leaving the bathroom.
    She headed home from work and was pulled over for speeding.
    "Is there a problem, officer?" She said trying to make her best flirt expression but something deep inside her held her back.
    "Yes, do you know how fast you were going?" He asked her.
    "About 70, but isn't the speed limit 75?" She asked him.
    "Can I see your license and registration please?" He asked her ignoring the question.
    She pulled out her license and for a few seconds instead of seeing her face on it she saw it was of a teenager named Kyle Perkins before reshaping itself in her image. Just then the memories of her former life as Kyle and transform or dare came back to her. She had to get out of their, she had to change back.
