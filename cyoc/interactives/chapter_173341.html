Kylie dodged out of the way, only to see her victim charge at her. In a split second, she grabbed a leaf and placed it on her head before Kylie could do anything. Like a scene from an action movie, Marina’s transformation caused a puff of smoke, temporarily blinding the succubus, and allowing her to escape her grasp and get behind her. While doing so, one of the leaves she threw earlier hit the door and transformed it into a coin. She tucked, rolled, and picked up the coin before the smoke cleared. Marina was able to jest before taking to the hallways at top speed, “Sorry, I’m not into sub play.” Marina was running as fast as her small legs could carry her, her balls hung at her side so they would obstruct her movement. She looked behind her and saw flames and magic flying like crazy, blast after blast firing from the direction she came from and aiming at her. With all the noise going on, the employees all check to see what was happening, only to be hit with wild blast from Kylie. She made it to the stairs to escape just before Kylie hit the door with her magic. She ran to the next floor below her and tried to think of a plan, not really noticing how everyone around her was looking at her funny. She decided she would use her tricks on this floor to either make Kylie blast herself, or trap her using her leaves, but she had to act fast as she would soon be facing her captor again.

    Kylie was furious, she did not want her little pet escaping her. First she gives her a gift, then she enjoys the change, and then she agrees to indulge her fantasy. That was how it should’ve worked, that’s how it worked for all of her other pets and toys, why did this one not work like those? “Where is my devilish little tanuki pet hiding?” She burst through the stairwell door and sent it clear to the other side of the hall, causing a crater to form in the wall. She used her magic to phase through the walls and turn anyone she saw into a mindless sexual slave, draining them of their energy so she could keep going. This wasn’t a large floor, she couldn’t have gone far. And that’s when it happened.

    Marina had placed a few leaves in select rooms, turning objects in those rooms into still versions of her, while setting up a transformation web for anyone that tried to follow her using a talisman on a string, she managed to get outside and watch the scene unfold from afar. Kylie would pick up a statue version of Marina and try to out a spell on it, only for a leaf and a piece of furniture to show up. This went of for several rooms before she lost all control and dashed in a fireball towards the elevator, not noticing the clever trap waiting for her. Like a slapstick cartoon she hit the talisman square on her forehead, fell down through the elevator shaft and ended up right where Marina was waiting. Marina giggled at the former demon, “now that form suits you, you troublemaker. You don’t deserve what you’ve been given.” She watched as Kylie’s pointy tail turned furry and her existing fur turn brown. Her ears rounded slightly and her face extended, her teeth growing to hilarious proportions. And it didn’t stop there, her ass exploded out of her outfit, her feet turned into hooves, and her face extended into a muzzle. The tanuki just turned a succubus into an ass. Marina felt a new sense of accomplishment, she was now a transforming superhero with fat balls. What would she do next with this newfound power?
