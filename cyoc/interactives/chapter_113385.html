    Bones.

    Some as intact human and farm livestock skeletons,but most
    shattered,bleached white and chewed apon by something with teeth
    sharp enough and jaw muscles strong enough to have bitten
    through horse and human femurs with equal ease.

    Her heart began to pound and as the fear swept over her
    Alisa could see the fan pattern of where the bones lay.

    They'd been tossed out the side of the windmill.

    Somewhere below this floor that suddenly seemed to be swaying,something
    had tossed out the remains of its meal onto the hillside
    a long time ago.

    Alisa screamed again as the floor below her trembling knees seemed to give another lurch and she fell on her chest.

    The floor WAS tilting downwards,like a funnel and
    Alisa found herself sliding towards the hole
    in the center.

    Frantically she tried to stop her slide
    towards the hole
    but her fingernaiils just couldn't
    seem to sink into a crack or
    knot in the suddenly tilting slippery
    wooden floor.

    Over she went and landed on her back on the grain sacks
    fully of the green and red mold.

    She lay there in the mold for several minutes,her heart beating faster with each passing minute.

    Slowly she pushed herself up with her hands,the
    oozing green and red mold spurting forth black ichor
    as her hands crushed it.

    There was some light in the cavernous space under the floor high above,and Alisa could
    see the huge skeleton that lay curled along side
    the walls.

    Something with horns,a long set of jaws with daggerlike teeth in them andwings and a tail that had a sort of bony set of pitchfork like
    curved spikes at the end of its tail.

    <A dragon>
    Alisa knew

    And from those closed bony toothy jaws ran the trail of the green and red
    mold that ended inside the ruined wagon box.

    The mold she was positively smeared with.

    Her heart beat even faster

    Then came her first convulsion and she could feel the pressure
    from her clothing as the fabric began to split and tear away from
    Alisa's growing body.

    She screamed as her feet suddenly burst out of her shoes and doubled in length,talons sprouting where her toenails used to be.

    She had to escape,but as she reached out a hand to push herself up,that arm suddenly bulged and expanded as musles formed and
    rippled under her darkening skin.

    Then the other arm bulged bigger at the same time as her
    bra suddenly felt loose.

    She looked with increasing horror at her exposed
    chest at her shrinking breassts,their nipples already nothing more then a vanishing light red smudge.

    By the time she got onto her bulging legs with the
    scaly green dragons feeet,she was already too big for her human clothes
    and they lay in torn fragments around her.

    The stabbing pain started in her insides as her ribs
    split and grew and reformed,her internal organs reshaped
    and her torso first doubled and then tripled and then just kept growing
    non-stop till it was just as reptillian and big as the skeleton
    dragons had been.

    Alisa nearly gagged as a swelling full sized dragons forked
    tongue forced its way out of her too small human n=mouth.

    She would have screamed in pain as her gums,jaws and nosed was pushed out of her face as her skull grew thick,heavy and big
    into a proper dragons snout.

    She tried to cry,but only drips of dragon blood dribbled
    onto her increasingly scaled dragons face and snout.

    He nose melted into her
    snout as her nostrils swelled in size proper to allow the emerging dragoness
    to breath through them.

    Then came the wave of fire in her dragon lungs heaving in her
    huge and still expanding chest.

    By now Alisa the dragoness 's head was poking out of the
    hole in the
    third storey floor
    and as her pelvis suddenly snapped and reshaped the big beasts weight took
    the floor down with her as she thumped heavily
    on all four legs onto the ground.

    In all this the dragon had barely registered her growing wings,her lengthening
    neck,,already extremely long spiky dragons tail
    complete with spiky pitchfork tips
    and a second wave of patterned green and
    red scales sweeping about to give her a second layer of scaly hide.

    Alisa bellowed in pain and surprise as the fiery blast furnace inside her
    heaving scaly chest burst forth from her lungs and shot
    into,not only her maw,her nostrils and her swelling glowing red hot
    slitted eyes,but also into her horns,her massively thumping dragon pump,her blood and every scale of her.

    The fiery firedrake charged forth and smashed through one
    of the cargo door,too dimwitted
    to just go through the hole that the earlier dragon had left
    behind.

    As she swung all four legs in a naturally fast dragons
    gallop,Alisa left behind her humanity in the
    blazing windmill as she crunched all the brittle tiny bones \she
    trod on in her escape.

    Finally she stopped miles away and shook her horned head as she tried
    to recall what a "Alisa" was...
