    up at dinner, it was in the bag.

    In fact, for the most part, it did go pretty well. The food was a bit expensive, but Ted had expected that. They chatted and laughed and had a

    bit too hard. He shrugged it off, but it kept bothering him.

    "Are you alright?" Lucy asked, "You keep smacking your lips."
    "Do my teeth look big to you?"

    "I dunno, they look fine."

    she was getting annoyed.

    He found the men's room and began bearing his gums at the mirror and poking at his canines. He couldn't be sure, but they did look a bit
