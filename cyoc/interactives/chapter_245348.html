    Chronivac Version 4.0

    Ken discovers the “change the world” option

    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous Yesterday O

    Ken scrolled through the infinite options the Chronivac offered while Jeff was waiting sitting on his bed. Then Ken found an interesting option, the one to change the world.

    “Have you found what you wanted?” Jeff said from the distance trying to peek in the screen but couldn’t see anything with Ken in front.

    “Ummm, I’m still searching” Ken pretended to look for something while reading the option he just found.

    “This setting allows you to change anything in the world as you desire, can be swap the entirety of humanity’s genders, turn some people into some random animal or making people unable to age after certain age or making the whole world worship you. You can go even crazier like making adults have goat heads or people’s legs turning into tentacles, the posibilites are endless and the only limit is your imagination!” Was what Ken saw on the screen above a text box to type any change he wanted to do to the world, he grinned knowing THIS was definitely an option made for him.

    Ken kept checking the option noticing there was even more customization, like if he wanted to make his worldwide change temporal or permanent, if people would be aware of the change or act like always have been like that. There was another setting that not necessarily changed people but make something become true, like dragons, mythical creatures or magical objects among many other things.

    “Interesting… I have a great idea in mind!” he thought while mischievously grinning as he began to type the change he wanted to make in the whole world.

    “Hey Ken, are you done? You’ve been there for a while” Jeff asked.

    “Yeah yeah I just have to finish the last details hehe…” said the boy with mysterious intentions.
