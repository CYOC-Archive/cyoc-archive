After a few moments Airhead recognized him as the always smiling, janitor from the Mayor's office.     Charlie the custodian was on his way home after a long shift. An issue with the building's heating meant that in addition to being tired he was particularly sweaty this evening. As he strolled through the park something peculiar caught his eye. A shiny red balloon was tied to the park fence, bobbing around as though calling to him. As Charlie approached the wind seemed to make the balloons movements more wild and erratic almost as though it was happy to see him. Just as he began to wonder why there was a balloon there he noticed a sign detailing the hiring price.

    "Looks like my lucky day." chirped the old man's gruff voice, as he fished out a few dollars from his wallet to tuck under the sign. He hummed a cheerful tune and undid the string tying the balloon to the fence. "I think I'll call you Red."
