    Jeff selected the '10s farmer clothes option and another window popped up allowing Jeff to customize his options a bit. He was glad for this
    because he would be able to take the Chronivac with him back in time although it would be changed. A computer in 1910 would be absurd.
    But he could have a typewriter with a book of instructions so that if he typed certain codes, following the instructions in the book, he could
    accomplish many of the transformations that a modern Chronivac could perform. So Jeff felt comfortable about trying this out because he
    could always return if he didn't like it. Jeff pressed enter.

    The first thing that happened was that things began to disappear. First his clothes disappeared. Then his room. He could see his neighbors but
    didn't know if they could see him but then his neighbors disappeared. Everything went back to a more primitive and natural environment and
    Jeff couldn't see any humans or any man-made anything. Jeff began to wonder if he was going to be lost in the wilderness. At least the
    wilderness seemed pleasant. There were plenty of trees and some of them looked like they had fruit.

    The Jeff himself began to change. He became smaller and skinnier. His hair became shaggy. He still had muscles but they weren't as well
    defined. His foreskin grew back. His overall appearance looked clumsier and more awkward. He'd turned into a lanky farm boy.

    Then he heard hoops and hollering and through the bushes came three young men as naked as Jeff. Strangely, they looked like his friends
    Mark, Ryan, and Steve but they looked like 1910 lanky farm boy versions of his friends.

    "Hey, Geoffrey. Don't just stand there. The swimming hole is this way." Steven shouted as he passed.

    Geoffrey wondered whether the Chronivac had pulled his friends along with him on this time traveling adventure or whether it was just
    coincidence that he found himself among friends.
