    "Are you sure?" asked Justin. "None of us have known anything about anything like that, or we would have told each other."

    "You mean that we've been transforming in our sleep?!" Leo exclaimed.

    "Not that I've seen," John replied to his son. The other dragons agreed.

    "Well, it can't hurt to try," said Kyle. "Who's going first?"

    "What?" Peter asked.

    "We were each able to transform in our sleep, and we've all talked about that when we thought they were dreams," Kyle explained. "That proves that we clearly have the ability, only this time we'll be doing it consciously."

    "Or, rather, TRYING to," Diana corrected her brother.

    "Besides, how do we know if we can do it completely?" Matthew added.

    "We can't know until we try," Kyle replied, "so which of us is going first?"

    The twelve looked around at each other, as if they were all expecting someone else to volunteer instead of them. The five adult dragons simply watched, wondering if their children really could pull it off. After a few seconds, some of the teens were starting to get a little frustrated.

    "Oh, whatever--I'll do it!" Dustin shouted.

    "Wait, what about me?" Justin asked.

    "Well, since we're twins we could try it together," Dustin suggested.

The two brother focused with all their might, and focused on letting it out with all their might. As the scales continued to spread, their torsos began to grow longer. The two also wished away their clothes as well--it seemed a bit silly to wear them like this, and their was nothing that truly needed to be be covered anymore thanks to their wish. As the scales continued to reshape and recolor the pair, their underbellies turned a golden yellow. The scales then reached their necks, and as their faces become covered in black scales, their jaws and noses began to grow out, and their hair began to change color as spines grew in a straight line down their necks and backs. When the transformations were finished, Dustin and Justin had transformed into two long, black dragons--the only difference was the color of their hair and spines--gold for Dustin, silver for Justin.

    "That's amazing!" Rachel smiled.
