    Callum stares down at the shattered phone, he can't believe he's ruined his and his friend's only chance at bring human. He whimpers. Callum is suddenly hit with an electric shock, he yelps in pain and involuntarily jumps. The phone starts spewing smoke, "Shit," says Braiden, "That thing is about to explode." The smoke obscures everything, they'd be lucky to see 5 cm in front of them.

    The smoke disappears suddenly, it's jarring for everyone. It just vanished without warning. The 3 dogs look at where Callum was and are shocked.



    "Callum?" Asks Maggie.
    "Yeah," he replies still in dog talk.
    "What happened to you?"
    "No idea. I don't think I can speak English. I think I still have to use dog language."
    "But you're human," says Braiden.
    "Yeah. But judging by what I'm wearing I'm a dog slave."
    "And?"
    "It means people can actually have sex with me."
    "Oh."
    "Yeah. I'd much rather be a dog. At least then my senses are better."

    "The phone's reformed," shouts Brodie. Callum sees it in the same spot it started spewing smoke. Maggie looks at Callum and says, "Here's your chance to redeem yourself." Callum crawls forward to it. He tries to take the mittens off but they're too tight, "I can't. These mittens are too tight." Just then Callum hears and feels a leash clipped onto his collar. Callum looks up at a teen boy. In his late teens. Blonde hair and blue eyes. The teen reaches down and grabs the phone, "That's where it went." He then pulls out a few more leashes and attaches them to the dogs. "Come on. We're going home."
