He then sighed satisfied as he rubbed the soles of his feet together, finally free of their prison.
    While Mark had occupied Rick, Justin had, with a sigh, taken his own discarded bowl of chips and now held it towards Rick in an attempt to get him silent. He knew he wasn´t the one to drop their chips, but he was still the one who had crushed them somehow.

You don´t even take a plate or a bowl normally. You have no problem eating anything from anywhere, even the ground, right? As long as someone says you should eat something that isn´t too big, hurtful to you or living, you eat it without problems, right?”
    As it clicked into Justin´s mind, a small smile crept onto his lips as he answered: “Yes, I eat with my fingers, as utensils are too hard to work with. I eat everything I get told to, as long as it isn´t too big, could harm me or is living, even from the ground.” He then looked towards the ground by the couch, where the crushed chips laid, half hidden under Rick´s discarded socks.
    Just as Chris returned from the kitchen, Mark said to Justin: “Go on, I won´t stop you from eating the crumbs from the carpet. You can also have the socks, as Rick doesn´t need them, right Rick?” Again, Mark had a wide, evil grin on his face.
    Rick, perking up from the chips bowl on his lap, turned towards Justin before confirming: “Yeah, I don´t need them, Justin can have them.”

He had to suppress his laughter as Justin really ate Rick´s discarded, dirty socks without a blink of his eyes, as if that was normal. To him it was after all. He showed a little disgust at the taste, but swallowed them like all the crumbs.
    Only when the carpet was clean of crumbs, Justin accepted his drink from Chris, who had waited for his friend to finish before sitting down himself. Justin sat back in the armchair, sipping his soda. Whenever he burped, his face contorted in disgust for a second, and Mark was sure he had to retaste Rick´s socks then, making his evil grin even wider if possible. Right now, Justin was like a living trash can.
    Mark turned back towards his brother, as of right now, he was the only one without a clear change. That little bit about trusting his brother wasn´t really what he had wanted to do, it was just to stop his brother from figuring anything was amiss. “Chris, are you alright? You looked uncomfortable walking on your feet.”
    Chris turned towards his brother with a small smile, not seeing any reason to not answer his brother´s concern. “Everything is okay, no worries, Mark. I hit my toes as I walke…”
    Mark interrupted his brother with another stomp, erasing the concept of walking from Chris´s mind. He was quick to replace it, however. “No Chris, you didn´t hit your toes. It is just uncomfortable for you to walk on just your feet, as you normally walk on all fours, right? Only if you are told to you walk on just your feet, as you feel uncomfortable doing so, right?”
    Chris nodded at that, repeating: “Yeah, walking on just my feet is uncomfortable. Walking on all fours is normal.”
    Mark grinned at them, before standing back up from the armchair he had sat in since he started changing their minds. “I will go back to my room, don´t be too loud, okay.”
    As Mark left them, they started a new game, retaking their old positions. Just that Rick tried more than once to play the controller with his feet now, making Mark laugh after entering his room. That had worked far better than he thought it would.
    He made a few small adjustments to the profiles of the three. Chris wouldn´t hurt himself when he walked on all fours. Rick also wouldn´t hurt his now always bare feet, while Justin wouldn´t get any stomach ache from whatever he ate. He also couldn´t hurt himself if he ate something pointy or sharp, and he could really digest everything he ate. After all, it would hurt if whatever he ate would just come back out.
    Then, to kill some time, Mark started to read something, laying back on his bed, enjoying the changes he made and planning what he would do next.
