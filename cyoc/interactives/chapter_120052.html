    A few weeks after Damon/Blackfire got pregnant...

    Damon/Blackfire woke up at dawn with her mate Kel'tor already awake and laying right beside her. "Good morning Blackfire." Kel'tor said and held out his claw to help Damon/Blackfire up. Damon/Blackfire decided to commit to being Blackfire, and decided to leave what was left of her past behind her, especially considering she's going to be a mother soon.

    Blackfire's stomach was now swollen up to the size of a beachball, and was mere inches from touching the ground. "Thank you, I can't wait to lay our beautiful eggs." Blackfire said nuzzling Kel'tor's muzzle.

    "I'm glad you are, and I believe that you'll be a perfect mother." Kel'tor said

    "The suspense is just so agonizing for me right now. This is just so exciting for me right now, I can't wait!" Blackfire said excited.

    "You're certainly much more excited now then when we first met. Why is that exactly?" Kel'tor asked

    "Well, I honestly was just afraid that you would have your way with me and then leave me alone to raise our hatchlings." Blackfire said

    "Wait what?! Why would you think that? If a dragon would to abandon his mate during pregnancy then he would have a large bounty on his head, it's criminal if anything to do such a thing. Where....why do you believe that?" Kel'tor asked and Blackfire paused nervous.

    "Well...I...I had very weird parents growing up from a welp. They died eventually, so I just went into the world using what I learned from them, and any humans that were willing to help me survive." Blackfire said

    Kel'tor facepalmed in his claw and then looked up at Blackfire and asked,"Were your parents....human?"

    Suddenly a surge of fear ran down Blackfire's spine and she roared screaming,"Of course not! If they weren't then how am I a dragon!?"

    Blackfire then slapped Kel'tor with her tail and he stopped and turned to Blackfire and began trying to calm her down. "Woah now, I see how my words may have been misinterpreted, so let's try this again. Do you know if your parents have been humans at any points in their lives? For example they may have been born as humans, but they could have been cursed into dragons and then mate later on after they accepted their lives." Kel'tor said

    Blackfire calmed down and sighed,"I.....don't know, I can't remember them all too well. But if you think they might have been then I guess that's an explanation we can accept. Sorry by the way, I don't know what keeps getting over me."

    "You are forgiven, pregnancy can play with your emotions and nerves. If I must teach our welps about our culture then so be it. If we want them to become strong and successful then we must raise them as dragons, not....well...like you. No offense, but it's true." Kel'tor said

    "Oh, none taken, it's just tha...." Blackfire began but paused when her womb released some sort of clear liquid and the two dragons looked back to watch.

    "It's started, just try to spread your legs as much as you can Blackfire." Kel'tor said

    "Good thing I'm a nest, heuugh!" Blackfire growled clutching her stomach, and getting into a position to spread her legs.

    "They're coming, I'm here for you. Shall I get a boulder so you can bite down on something?" Kel'tor asked

    "Nooooaaaahh!!" Blackfire roared as she felt a round object push up against her vaginal wall.

    "Oh god! I feel the first egg! It's aaaahhhh!" Blackfire roared as loud as she could pushing the object out of her womb and a slimy white egg plopped into the nest.

    "K...Kel'tor..."Blackfire said breathing heavily

    "I'm still here for you, but there's still more eggs to go. Keep it up sweetie." Kel'tor said and moved the egg aside for others to drop.

    "Okay.....okay......here's the secondaaaaah!!" Blackfire roared and another egg plopped into the nest.

    "Great job honey, keep going." Kel'tor said

    "That didn't feel as bad as the first egg. Oh, here's the third eaaaaaaahhh! Ohhhhh." Blackfire said pushing out a third egg.

    "So it is true that pain turns into pleasure when it comes to laying eggs, personally I thought it meant when it comes to raising a family in general, but now I suppose that my assumption is false." Kel'tor said

    "Shut up and let me focus hun." Blackfire said then pushed out another egg and she moaned.

    Blackfire continued moaned each time she pushed out an egg until there was an overall total of 9 eggs and she collapsed on the ground next to them. "Finished....whew! I'm beat." Blackfire said


    "You can sleep, but do so while you warm our eggs if you will. Your a mother now and I'm a proud father. I wonder if our hatchlings will be special like you. Of course I'm referring to the females, if it were my son it would be rather weird, but maybe they're special in a different way." Kel'tor said

    Blackfire got up and wrapped her body around her eggs from her tail to her head and covering them all with her wing. "That....was an interesting experience. I'm glad to feel myself again too." Blackfire said and sighed in relief.

    "I love you Blackfire, now especially more than ever." Kel'tor said

    "I would like to do this against sometime, and when I do there's no other male to do it with than with you. I love you Kel'tor, you are my king." Blackfire said and Kel'tor walked over to her and the two of them nuzzled each other's muzzles.

    "It's only a matter of days now, shall I gather friends and family to prepare?" Kel'tor asked

    "Why not, I'd love a baby-shower for our hatchlings. Who knows, we may just have twins." Blackfire said smiling.

    "True, rest now my love, and allow me to contact the others." Kel'tor said and Blackfire nodded.


    Blackfire eventually fell asleep with her eggs and she was happy overall and now she was content with her fate. "I love my life now, and everything is perfect." Blackfire thought as she drifted off into slumber with her eggs warming below her wing.
