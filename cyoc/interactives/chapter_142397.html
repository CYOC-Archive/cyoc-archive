    Kellie, Jackie, and Ann looked around the beach to see who to experiment the ring on. Ann soon noticed something down the pier and had surprised eyes. “Hey Jackie! Look who it is!” she said to her friend. Jackie heard her and looked to see what she’s pointing at. It was than that she saw and Ann was seeing.

    On the pier is a lone fisherman who was fishing for his catch. At forty-five years of age, he was a middle age man with muscle that were once tones and hard, but had now become soft with a bit of flab. His stomach has a bear gut that would resemble that of Santa Claus with his pecs has nothing but flab on him. His face was in much worse shape and he had wrinkles on his eyes and mouth, circles around his eyes and a salt and pepper beard on his face. His black hair had a gray ring around it and he was completely bald on top of his head. He was also wearing swim trunks and sandals with a Hawaiian buttoned shirted opened up to expose his belly.

    Jackie looked to the middle-aged fisherman and was surprised that he was there. “No way… Is that Jake?”

    Kellie turned to the two and asked, “You know this guy?”

    Jackie turned to her and told her about it. “Of course, he works at the same company Ann is and she has a complete crush on him. Yet she never musters the courage to speak up.”

    Ann heard that and was a little sheepish. “I don’t know how to approach him. I mean, he is pretty much a loner and never had a girlfriend in his life. Maybe I would be a good fit, though I don’t think he is interested in little old me.”

    Kellie looked to her and was a little coy about it. “Old?” she said, “Hardly… You are now a spunky mid teen girl. You now have a life ahead of you and a ring that can change whatever age you want.”

    “Yeah… But do you think middle age man would want to go out with a 15-year-old girl blossoming to woman? I don’t think so. I don’t think he will like me this way.” she said feeling a little dejected.

    Kellie though looked to the ring she has and decided to help Ann out. “Maybe we can arrange that.” She said with a slight smirk.

    Jackie looked to Kel and knew what she’s planning. “You’re planning to use your ring to help with Ann’s relationship?”

    Kellie turned to her and said, “Why not? This ring certainly improved me and Katie’s love life. Why not help Ann out with her’s. After all, we’ve done some really good with this ring, with the slight hiccups. So what do you say, Ann? Do you like the idea?”

    Ann heard this and was a little hesitant about it. Sure, she would like to take Jake out on a date, but using the ring to change his age is a little aggressive. Then again, it seems better than hesitating to ask him out. So with a quick sigh, she said, “Fine… We’ll go with you’re plan, Kellie. So what do you have in mind?”

    Kellie smiled and thought of what to do with Jake. That’s when she came up with a good option.
