    Back To Being A Toy

    added by Elflover A year ago A BM S O Mythological Magic

    Your happy time was cut short by a voice you recognised - and was not happy to hear.
    "My sweet baby boy! What happened?" it exclaimed.
    You turned and saw your caterpillar torturer.
    "What's happened to you?" it says, aghast.
    "I prefer fucking to tickling!" you laugh at his face.
    Your new boyfriend laughs too, loudly too.
    "You are horrible! Return at once! I'm your daddy!" the caterpillar fay shouts out, furious.
    "Never! I'm through being your toy!" you yell.
    "Oh, you deserve to be punish!" it answers back. "And since you like piggies so much..."
    He throws fairy dust at you and you feel yourself shrink and transform. It's fast and painless and soon, you are back to being the fat baby you had been, with folds of fat. You have a diaper again, decorated with piglets also in diapers and with rattles. You are rosier and chubbier than before. Yes, you truly look like a piglet now, even though you look human. Even your stupid wings are gone.
    "Much better," smiles your torturer. "I can't wait to give you all sorts of piggie-themed toys and things. And to tickle you again. Believe me, you will not escape me again!"
    You gulp. Your torturer means everything he says and you know it. It's back to the crib and tickles again. And who knows what he plans to do more...
    "As for your friend," he smiles evilly.

    He throws more fairy dust on Will. What does he intend to do?
