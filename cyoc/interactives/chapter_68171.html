    Cody and Dustin decided to go to a restaurant where they were serving a buffet that day. When they arrived,

    looked around the large restaurant.

    At the end of the large dining hall, there was a small table where a nice lady was scooping up some hot chili.
    She was serving customers who were standing in line, and only one other student from Dustin's school was
    standing in line at that time. Dustin got up and walked up to the line where Eric, one of his classmates, was
    waiting his turn in line.

    "Move over, you dweeb!" Dustin growled, as he rudely pushed Eric out of the way, just as Cody was walking up
    to the line. Cody saw everything that had happened between Dustin and Eric.

    Now Ty had cast a spell on Dustin and Cody to make sure that Dustin would be reminded not to bully anyone
    while Ty was away. He would make Cody grow bigger and more muscular if Dustin tried to do anything. This was
    that time!

    As soon as Cody saw what had happened, he was overcome by that weird feeling in his muscles, whenever Ty's
    powers begin to affect him!

    Cody felt his thick legs inflate even more, growing wider and deeper with outrageous muscle! Thick veins
    surfaced all over his wide legs while his calves bulged even more so, becoming as hard and as round as
    basketballs. His huge abs grew more defined and his pecs inflated to become even bigger than before!

    "Ohhh man! I'm growing again!" Cody groaned.

    At the same time, Dustin began to shrink! He suddenly lost six inches in height, dwindling down from 6 feet to
    5 ft 6 inches tall! Now, Cody seemed incredibly tall and muscular!

    "Uhmm... Dustin! It wasn't your turn in line! Let Eric order his chili first!" Cody stated, as he walked up to
    Dustin, who was now a lot shorter than Cody.

    Dustin was clearly intimidated now that he had shrunk and Cody had become even BIGGER!

    "Ohhh... Sorry, Eric..." he said, stepping back and letting Eric get back in line.

    "Good!" Cody smiled, folding his big, thick arms before his huge chest.

    Later on, Ty walked in the restaurant and heard what had happened. He was very happy that Cody had stepped in
