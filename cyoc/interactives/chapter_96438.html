    Patrick made a show of thought, after which he said, “Hmmm...I’m not sure...you have any ideas?”
    “Why do you think I was asssking what you wanted to do?” asked Jack, smirking slightly. He thought about some of the possibilities before saying, "Firsssst, could I pleassse have my normal mouth back? This hissssing is driving me crazy." Pat smirked and turned to face the computer again. He pulled up Jack’s virtual model and clicked on the button next to it that said “undo previous changes”. A list of the changes he had done to Jack appeared on the screen. He undid the forked tongue and the fangs, but kept the ability for Jack to unhinge his jaw. When all that was done, he clicked the “save changes” button. Pat swivels his head back towards his friend and says, “There, that should do it.” Jack nodded, examining his now (almost) normal mouth in the mirror on the back of Patrick’s door. "Thanks," Jack said. "And now, secondly, could you get up out of that chair? I have an idea that I'd like to try." Pat obliged and let his best friend sit in front of the computer. Pat realized on getting up that he really had to pee, so he left the room and headed down the hallway to the bathroom.

    Jack clicked on his avatar and saw a list of information come up that described him down to the last freckle. There were the usual thinks like age, date of birth, parents, and the like, but there were also some unusual things such as penis size (down to a hundredth of an inch, mind you), current mental state (which was currently set to "awake and aware"), and current physical state (which was currently set to "mobile/awake". At the bottom of the list was a field that read "miscellaneous", which was blank. Jack clicked in the text box and typed in, "has a button at the nape of his neck that, when pushed, depending on the setting, renders him immobile and essentially makes him a posable doll or into a life-size marionette complete with a ventriloquist dummy mouth and bright pink circles painted on cheeks. Can still speak, though." He then went back to the main screen and clicked on Pat's avatar, bringing up a list similar in nature to his own. In Pat's miscellaneous box, he types, "is hollow (now essentially a human bag of holding) and has a button at the nape of his neck that, depending on what setting is selected, pops open the top of his head or a door in his chest. both open to bottomless storage spaces. A third setting makes the roman numerals I-XII appear in a circle around him and his arms immediately spring into position to show the current time; he is totally unresponsive in this form: he doesn't speak unless he's chiming the hour, his eyes move back and forth to the rhythm of an internal pendulum, and his tail becomes the second hand." Jack clicked back to the main page and looked over everything he had entered. Satisfied, he set the awareness so that only he and Pat would know that anything was different and clicked "save all changes".
