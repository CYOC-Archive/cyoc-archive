    and mortal mythicals under her jurisdiction. they provided her with offerings in return for
    good fortune, and Keiko was allowed free run of the settlement visible or not. It was even
    stranger was how she didn't mind the lack of twenty first century technology. Besides

    settlement had barely changed a twinkle in the last fifty years if not twice that!

    Keiko knew all this for she had formed a connection with this place the more she stayed
    here, the more she understood it, and the more it understood her. Already the people
    spoke of the kitsune spirit that walked among them.

    him in line for the short amount of time she planned to be gone.

    new to be terrified of.

    "Well, if you don't want that, I -suppose- I could do some other modification instead."

    "Please! I don't want my boss to be angry at me! He's a corporate vampire!"

    "I didn't know that was what he was at the time!"

    "Nobody knew HOW people who crossed him disappeared! Just that they did! The boss got

    Yeah! Sure! Just please fix me!"

    "Centuries?" The new dyraid was crying now.

    "Yip! Yep! So get used to your body. I could have left you as a human face on a tree, but I

    "What about my ID, my keys, my-"

    "They're somewhere inside you right now I imagine along with your clothes... go luck
    getting them out of yourself, ha-ha! Well, have a nice life! Maybe you'll get your head on
