    Jeff skimmed through all the packages and wondered what to use next. Just then, something caught his eye as he said, “Evolution Package? What’s that?” Curious, he starts to click on the icon and new page appears followed by a message. It reads…

    ‘Hello, and thank you for choosing the Evolution Package. In this package it’s real simple, evolve or devolve any living thing on the planet. The steps are easy to understand.

    1. Choose any living organism that exists on the planet. It can either be a tree, an animal, or an actual human being.

    2. Make a choice between on whether you want to evolve the subject or devolve them.

    3. Use the drop down option to evolve or devolve person by hundreds, thousands, millions, billions, trillions, or zillions.

    It’s that simple.

    Warning: Using this option could lead to some serious consequences. Devolve the subject too far will make the subject cease to exist. Evolving too far could be even worse. There’s no telling what would happen if you evolve any living being on the planet, including a human. The end effect, no matter how far forward you will go, could lead to something beautiful or monstrous. Choose wisely and be cautious.’

    Jeff read the message was pretty intrigued by it. Evolving or devolving anyone he wants? That is something that he might like. But reading the warning label about that package, he begins to worry. What if he goes a little too far with evolving or devolving anyone. Especially if he evolved someone to far ahead that he or she will be turned into something much worse. So he decides on testing it on one person before he could move on ahead. But who will he use this package on? He could try to use it on himself, but that will risk his livelihood. So who?
