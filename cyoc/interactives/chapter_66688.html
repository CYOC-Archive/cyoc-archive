    Dana stood, her eyes drifting over the many varied forms that stood before her, each and every one of them
    originally humans as she had been... oh it seemed like so long ago now, a dream long forgotten. But now things
    had changed forever, but the least she could do was offer some consolation to those first victims, and by
    doing so, begin implementing a new hierarchical structure to maintain a sense of balance on this plane.
    "Long ago, you where reduced to mindless demonic beasts, with no mind of your own, driving by pure instinct
    and desire, but tonight that will change. As the first of Astara's victims, I will now return to you, in part,
    that which she took from you all." As she spoke, Dana silently, casually gestured toward the beings before
    her, many of whom immediately clutched at their skulls, gasping in shock, terror, and pain, their memories and
    thoughts returning to them. Many of them fell to the ground, crying, while others just felt deadly silent,
    their eyes gaining a haunted look, the expression of horror crawling over their faces.

    "There is nothing that can be done about what happened, and our world can never be restored to what it once
    was, but as the Princess of this plane, I will grant you all mercy, you will become the Lords and Ladies of
    this plane, serving under me, but you will have the will do do as you wish, no longer slaves. Only remember
    that I am now ruler, and I will have no tolerance for anything that will send my plane into chaos once more,
    any attempt to do so, will end with the guilty parting regretting it for eternity." There was another wave of
    her hand, and the audience of demons began to change, falling to the ground as their bodies began to contort,
    gaining the forms of Incubi and Succubi, each regal in their own way, uniquely identifying them as the new
    royalty. Quietly they stood, looking quietly at themselves, most of them shocked into silence, their minds
    still processing all of what was happening around them, and Dana knew that the questions would soon begin...
    but before anyone had a chance to speak, one of the Succubi, Dana could not remember her name, but knew she
    had once been the nurse of the school, stepped forward, elegant, and incredibly beautiful, speaking softly.

    "I thank you for your Kindness my Princess" Promptly the succubus offered an elegant bow, and completely
    caught Dana by surprise, but the Princess courteously inclined her head in return before her eyes turned to
    study the rest...
