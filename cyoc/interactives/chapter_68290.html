    Ty's Power

    A little kid grows so he can work out in the gym

    Ty was wandering around the weight room at the YMCA when he spotted and overheard a middle-aged man talking to
    a young boy, who looked like he was the man's son.

    "Now, Sam, just take your book and go and sit quietly in the locker room, okay? Wait for me there and read
    your book while I work out!" the man said to the boy, who looked like he was about six years old. The boy had
    short, black hair, brown eyes and he was wearing shorts and a t-shirt with tennis shoes.

    The father then turned around wearing a towel around his neck and left to go work out in the weight room,
    leaving the boy all by himself in the stuff locker room, with just his picture book.

    "I'm so bored right now... I wish I were old enough to work out in the gym..." the boy sighed out loud.

    Ty heard Sam express his wish and thought that this was the golden opportunity to use his powers on the boy in
    order to make his wish come true!

    No sooner had the boy expressed his deepest desire, or he was overcome by a sudden, strange, tingling
    sensation. The boy just got up to stretch his legs, after sitting on the hard wooden bench, when he felt a
    weird jolt going through his legs and his arms.

    Sam's hands and feet began to grow! His fingers were growing longer and longer as his hands increased in size.
    Sam felt his shoes get tighter and tighter to the point where they were getting painfully tight. He quickly
    untied his laces and removed them, before his feet burst out of his shoes. When he looked down, he noticed
    that his feet were bigger and they were still growing!

    His legs were getting longer by the minute! He was getting taller! Not just his legs, but his arms were
    growing as well! What was going on?

    As his body was expending in every direction, small patches of hair began to sprout on his legs, his arms, and
    in his armpits. He was maturing! Not only was he getting bigger and taller... he was actually getting older!

    "Hey... What... What's happening?" he asked himself, taking note that his voice sounded a little deeper as it
    began to crack.

    Muscles began to form on his arms and legs, and small ridges were forming over his flat stomach, giving the
    first signs of his abdominal muscles.

    His calves began to inflate slightly and his pectorals were more defined. He was becoming slightly more
    muscular as he stretched several inches taller. He had actually passed through puberty! he had become a
    teenager!

    "This... This is crazy!" Sam whispered as he turned around and walked over to the large mirror in the locker
    room. He was no longer a six-year-old boy. He had aged into a fourteen-year-old teenager within seconds! He
    was also eight inches taller than before!

    "Okay... This is really, really weird..." he said to himself, as he studied his reflection in the mirror.

    His hair had grown longer and thicker and a thin patch of hair had started to sprout over his chin and just
    above the lips. His voice was noticeably deeper than before, and everything around him seemed smaller because
    he had grown taller in a matter of minutes.

    He opened the door and walked out into the weight room, where he spotted his father. He had expected his
    father to look surprised. Instead, his father looked pleased when he saw him.

    "Come over here, Sam! We can work out together, now that you're old enough!"

    That just sounded like music to the boy's ears! He started lifting weights with his dad right away!

    Ty was looking on from a distance and he just smiled as he studied the results of his handiwork, as he turned
    around and surveyed the gym for another person to assist using his awesome powers...
