    Rachel in Wonderland: Chess Pawn Rachel

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago S O Shrinking

    Rachel walks over to chessboard and sees most the black and white chess pieces set up, ready for someone to play with them. The only piece that was missing on the board was the white pawn that Rachel had put in her apron's pocket before she went through the mirror.
    "So this is where that chess piece belongs to. I figured as much." Rachel said as she reach into her right pocket. But to Rachel's confusion, the white pawn piece was no longer inside her apron in either pocket. Instead, Rachel found a small glass bottle of clear liquid with a pink tag tied to its shaft. Written on the tag were the words "DRINK ME" in red letters.
    "Where did this come from? I thought this would be like Through the Looking Glass and not-" Rachel suddenly notices something written across her chest-covered apron in black letters. Upon futher investigation, Rachel identified the letters witten on her as the word "PAWN", though she didn't know what to make of it.
    "I don't get it? Why have I been labeled as a pawn? And how did I end up with a bottle full of potion meant to make me small- Ooohhhhhhh." Rachel smiled as she looks back at the chessboard with all the other pieces set up, waiting for the last piece to start playing. Rachel realizes that in order for the game to begin, she would need to drink from the bottle of potion in her hand and literately become the missing white pawn.
    "Ok then, I'll play you're game. Though I should warn you, I'm really good at chess." Rachel said to the chessboard, opening the bottle and bringing it to her lips. She hoping that the chess pieces would respond back, given everthing that has happened to her so far today, but they remained silent. Before she could second guess herself, Rachel starts drinking from the bottle and didn't stop until it was completely dry. She then placed the now empty bottle on the floor and waited. Then Rachel felt a slight vibrational tingle sweep over her whole body and she started to shrink. Rachel couldn't help but giggle as the tingling sensation were making her very ticklish. When she was small enough to stand on the chessboard without knocking the other pieces over, Rachel steps up onto the platform, walk over to her designated spot, and waits for the shrinking spell to end. Finally the shrinking stops when Rachel reaches the same height as the rest of the pawns on the field. Then suddenly the colors of Rachel's Alice costume began to fade away, leaving her wearing a pure white outfit. Then a round hat magically appears on top of Rachel's head, shaped exactly like the top of a pawn. With the full transformation finished, Rachel was officially ready to start playing chess, as a tiny pawn no less.
