However, all this success had an unseen cost. Joanne had been raised to always keep her emotions in check, to never let an outburst potentially tarnish her image. And there were so many social obligations she was involved with as part of the upper class. And none of them allowed an outlet for all the pent-up stress and emotions she kept bottled up inside. And sooner or later, it became too much for her to repress.

The two of them had been arguing through most of Nicole's adolescence, but it eventually came to a head and Nicole finally left, taking only a few of her belongings and moving to Animalia. After that, Joanne's issues started boiling over. At first, she tried to keep up a veneer that everything was fine, but the paint quickly started to chip. After that came the alcohol consumption, starting with wine but then going to brandy, tonic, and pretty much anything to keep from being sober. Eventually, depression managed to overcome even the buzz of alcohol.

Admittedly, Joanne did manage to slit her wrists, but her servants had found her, staunched the bleeding, and got her to a hospital before it was too late. At this point, extreme measures had to be taken. The servants were of one mind that Joanne needed to be converted. It may be the only think to pull her out of her funk and it would be able to bring her closer to her estranged daughter. It took a while to get the idea through to Joanne's head. Part of it was because of the depressing haze she was in, part of it was the morphine she was on to keep the pain of her wrists down. When Joanne understood what they were telling her, she immediately said she wanted to be the same species that Nicole was. She didn't even bother to check what that was. All she wanted was to get her daughter back.
