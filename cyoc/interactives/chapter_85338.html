    Meeting Ms. Edith

    In the gym the new cheerleaders were greeted by a young woman in her late twenties. She was athletic and fit, wearing white and pink gym clothes. She waved at the new girls and a huge smile appeared on her cute freckled face while her ponytail with her long red hair bounced behind her.

    "Hello girls, I am Ms. Edith.", introduced the woman herself, "Some of you may remember me as your former teacher, but after a talk with Coach Randall, he persuaded me that it would be much better for you and me and the whole school to be your new cheerleading coach."

    The new coach smiled happily at the perplexed girls, while some of them like Nina, Brianna and Madeline had problems to accept that this happy, bouncy person in front of them was once there former young but grumpy math teacher. She also seemed to like her new body and position.

    "Ehm, Ms. Edith, you're sure that everything is alright? I mean with this whole situation and everything?", asked a worried Madeline.

    "Of course, Madeline, don't worry your cute little head. We are doing this for the school. And I am sure you all will be as happy about your new life as me very soon.", answered Ms. Edith and patted Madeline's head, still smiling.

    "But enough with the chit-chat, we have to start with the training. The next game is four days and Coach Randall wants us to cheer the guys to an impressive winning."

    Then Ms. Edith began to give the new girls instructions about the training. They began with stretching, then some fitness, followed by some figures and at the end there even able to do some routines with music. Somehow the new cheerleaders felt compelled to follow Ms. Edith's instructions even when they weren't comfortable with their new role in this scheme.
    For Madeline it was even more embarrassing cause at the end of the routine she must do a split and was then carried at the top of a small pyramid while Nina and Brianna doing high kicks at the side of the figure. How did they managed to do this? As a guy Matt was never able to do a split let alone the other things her new flexible body did during the training.
    At least they didn't do any embarrassing chants in the training. Well at least not yet.
