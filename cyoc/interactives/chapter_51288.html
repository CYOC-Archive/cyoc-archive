I would trust Yvonne with my life. If you showed her your, er…big secret, she would take it to her grave, if you wanted her to keep mum.”

You both like fantasy literature and films, you have similar concerns for the protection of nature, and so on. Also, you both had no siblings, and felt you were outsiders during your childhoods.

    “Next month. 28th of April,” you say.

I wish I’d met you before I got snapped up by Dirk, my ex.” She tells you more about him. How, during their year-long relationship she came to realise that Dirk and his sister Delilah were senior members of a criminal gang, with connections to corrupt policemen in the regional force - and how she fled for safety, knowing that Dirk still wants her as his trophy girlfriend. Meanwhile Delilah, it seems, would be prepared to silence Sam, if necessary, for what she knows.
