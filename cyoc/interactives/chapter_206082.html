    Ethan began to flex and pose just like the guys in the story did when they transformed. Oh, but it was one thing to read about it. It was another to experience it. The way his muscles balled up with strength, the hardness he felt when he touched them. It wasn't the only thing to harden either. The teen felt like he had died and gone to CYOC heaven. His body was just so bulky now. In fact, he seemed to be bulkier than his brother was with around the same amount of tone. Hmmm, although now that he looked at himself, his body seemed to be tanner, and his hair might have been thinner. Ethan paused for a moment but decided that he didn't care. He had the body that he always wanted, so why did he need to be concerned about these smaller changes?

    "I need to watch that tape again! No, I need to tell all my friends! No! I need to jerk off!" Though while the new gym rat was contemplating what he was going to do next, Ethan had accidentally knocked over the pile of VHS tapes.

    "Hey, anyone home!" A familiar voice cried out. Oh shit! It was Ethan's stepdad, Dirk DiLaurentis. Ethan thought he was going to be out until dinner, but it seemed like that wasn't the case. Crap, how was he going to explain this to his stepdad? Ethan was pretty sure there were no reality changes to help him out, so quickly, he hid in his brother's closet.
