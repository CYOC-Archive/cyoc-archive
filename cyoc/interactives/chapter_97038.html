    "This is probably the coolest thing that's ever happened to me!" said an excited Leo.

    "You mean us," laughed Jake.

    Of course, every one of these brothers were surprised by this: they were all one body, but still had their own head out of eight!

    "Who do you think is gonna show up next?" asked Dustin.

    "Hopefully someone.." Peter started.

    "...who will want to keep this game going!" Allen finished.

    Right on cue, the bell rang; Leo's brother Donnie had come to check on Leo, since he had been gone for some time. Jake, Allen, and Dustin moved the left leg towards the door, while Peter, Leo and Steven moved the right leg; Ty helped move both. Once at the door, they opened it to the dropped jaw of Donnie.

    "What the hell?" Donnie asked; he was extremely focused on Leo's head.

    "Come in, Donnie." Ty said.

    A little while later, after explaining the whole thing, Donnie spoke up. "So you have these powers now, Ty, and they allowed you all to fuse together?"

    "Exactly." Dustin answered.

    "You ever wonder what it's like to be closer as brothers?" Leo asked Donnie.

    "Uh-huh. Have room for one more in that body?" Donnie asked.

    "Of course we do." Ty said. "I wish that Donnie could join us." Donnie then moved towards the body, slowly shifting towards the widening left end. As Donnie's body touched against Jake's side, his right half disappeared, and Jake's arm was covered by Donnie's skin. As their skin finished merging, the waist widened to match the extended shoulders. Their clothes also fused, resulting in jeans with for two legs and a shirt with holes for two arms and eight heads. Indeed, they were now a six-foot body with two legs, two arms, and eight heads.

    "Oh my god!" Donnie shouted. "Look at us now!"

    "I know, it's crazy!" Leo said.

    "This keeps getting cooler by the minute!" Jake said.

    "How could it NOT get cooler?!" Steven asked.

    "This is still a great idea, Ty." Allen said.

    "I know." Ty said. "And I keep making it so that we're full-blood brothers and that everyone thinks we've been related like this our whole lives."

    "If two heads are better than one, imagine how eight compares!" Peter said.

    "I am still saying that this is the best idea we've ever had." Dustin said to Ty. "I'd like to see how long we can keep this up."

    "Well, I still have my powers and we're all totally for it," Ty smiled. "We'll see what happens."

    At this thought, all eight heads looked back and forth at each other, happily chatting and smiling about these ideas. Each of the brothers then began to wonder what they would do next.
