    "It wasn't my phone. You definitely sounds like my dad!" Kyle said. "What's going on Luke? What are you hiding from us? We won't leave until you tell us! What did that fruit do to you? We're your friends, you can trust us." Luke sighed, he felt cornered. He realized that his only choice was to tell them the truth.

    "F, fine. Hear me first and then I'll let you in." Luke said and he noticed that his friends had stopped pushing the door. "Ok, this is what happened..." He told them everything that happened to him since the moment he ate the unripened fruit. He intentionally avoided to tell them about his lame dad bod or his disgust of his body hair. "And now I'm here... trying to convince you..." When he stopped talking, a brief silence came and then Jake asked.

    "So... You are an adult now, huh? Can we see you?" Jake slowly opened the door while Luke made a few steps back. His stomach was gurgling again and he knew that he won't be able to contain his need to go to the bathroom for much longer. He sighed once again while he watched his friends coming in.
