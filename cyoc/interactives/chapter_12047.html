    April, Bets, Chasity and Diane managed to squeeze through the doors as the bus stopped at the mall. April and Chasity had an easier time of it, as the two girls were the smallest at 350 an 360 lbs each, but the 420 lbs Diane had to take a deep breath and force her way out, and Bets (who weighted a staggering 580 lbs!) had to inch through sideways, her elephantine hips now far wider than the doors of the bus. She found that once she was outside she could move slightly more easily, but with her now bright pink hair twisted into a beehive, face covered with thick, clownish make-up and her nearly globular body covered with a gargantuan pink "mu mu" decorated with yellow flowers, she was quite a spectacle.

    The other girls wern't much better. All of them had thick foundation, blush, lipstick, mascara and eyeshadow that made them all look like members of the Mimi Fan Club. Diane's once long, brown hair was now a wild, orange afro and her clothes had become a tight red jogging suit with yellow trim. A yellow head band made sure her hair was fluffed up and out, making her look even more bizarre.

    April wore a lime green polyester pant-suit with a bright yellow, ruffled blouse. Several pounds of fake jewelery hung from her and her hair was set into a high, wide bouffant of bright yellow.

    Chasity found she was squeezed into a bright purple mini-dress of jiggling spandex, along with pink fishnet stockings and several pink and purple feather boas. Her hair was now purple and bound in twin pigtails decorated with pink bows.

    To say they looked foolish would be an understatement.

    They started for the mall, but it soon became obvious that Bet's wasn't going to be able to keep-up with the others. She waddled along, gasping and tired, for almost five minutes before panting "No...more...please. I just...can't...keep up...with you."

    They stopped at a bench and saw that everyone was looking at them as they passed. April, Chasity and Diane were humiliated by their appearance. Bet's was just bushed...and hungry.

    Actually, they were ALL hungry. All four girls felt their now vast bellies begin to grumble.

    After getting her breath back, Bet's said "I suggest that we all split-up and search for the fat man. One of us is bound to find him eventually. Once we do, we say we're sorry for mocking him and beg him to make us normal again. OK?"

    They all agreed to the plan, and all four went off to search for him. Each girl also made a detour to by some snacks before they really got to searching. After all...they were hungry!
