    The future isn't too rosy

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago A O

    Now a quarter century ago when it had become clear that global warming was irreversible and that Climate Change was going to be a stark extinction event
    with too many humans and too little time to deal with it
    various programs had begun in order to
    avoid a total extinction.

    It had begun with the blunt sledgehammer approach of the
    Euthanasia Lotteries
    and then had come freezing human volunteers and shooting them off to other stars aboard the first generation of
    nearly three times the speed of light
    starships.

    Since nobody had figured out how to unfreeze the frozen volunteers yet that was a leap of faith for those humans,who might not survive the process.

    The research in unfreezing folk had led to the
    Species ReAllocation Programs
    where the solution to too many humans
    was to transform some of them into other
    critters
    such as lizards and snakes.

    In your case you had decided to get out of the human race whilst the terms were still favourable and a decade ago had
    volunteered to be transformed into a giant
    serpentess,.

    The Government had been offering terms where
    you spent 11-1/2 months as a giant limbless snake
    and as part of the deal got to spend
    2 weeks each year as a
    'human'
    sort of a vacation in
    between working
    and you were currently in 'human' form
    catching up on what you'd missed for the previous 50 weeks.

    As a 'human' in the Species ReAllocation Program
    you carried a Species Passport as well as a
    boltgun
    just in case some overenthusiastic Government agent
    decided to euthanize you
    by mistake.

    So to your surprise you saw a face you recognized from a
    decade earlier
    approaching you
    the specialist who had guided you in your
    travels of leaving the human species...

    What do you do now?
