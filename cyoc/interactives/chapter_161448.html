She could even create new people and delete preexisting ones, turn fictional beings real and vice versa, alter history…

    At first she simply opened her mother's profile and made her approve of her relationship with Jeff, but she felt it was too little. After all, since the Chronivac couldn't work, why not go a little crazy with it?

    So, not closing her mother's profile, she created a new one. She made this new being a male devil and named him ‘Lucifer the Second’, stated that he refused to do evil and, along with most demons, had rebelled against his own father, the original Satan himself. Once Lucifer the First was killed and the few followers he had left defeated, his son draw a christian cross on hell's ground, at the very place Satan died, then prostrated himself with all other demons, declaring their renouncement of the fallen angel's teachings and their acceptance of whatever punishment God deemed necessary.

    Since all demons following Satan's son were sincere, he accepted to pardon them. But because Lucifer's rebellion had caused sins to exist and be present on earth, they would stay as demons living in hell with the mission to tortures the damned, destroying the sins corrupting those souls. God also decided, to prevent another betrayal like the one Lucifer had perpetrated, that young angels, demons and humans would now go to school together, despite demons being immortals again, like their angelic cousins, while humans stayed mortals.

    Then she got back to her mother's profile, and made her a childhood friend of Jeff's own mother. She also precised that she was born human, Lucifer the second and herself fell in love and, with God's benediction, was turned into a succubus so death wouldn't separate them. Changing Jenny's mother, Shannon, into a demoness had been doubly necessary since love didn't work exactly the same for angels and demons than it did for humans. While the feeling itself was the same, for the immortals, love manifested as some kind of a magical link, to the point of making the difference between being in love and being married inexistent. Anyone passing a married angel or demon could feel they were, feeling some sort of aura from them, and when their spouse was with them you could tell they were together.

    Opening her own profile, Jenny edited it so she was born from her mothers union with Lucifer the Second, making herself a succubus, just like her mother, except that she was born one. While her mother decided to still go by Shannon O'Brian, even after marrying Satan, Jenny's full name was now Jennifer Lucy Lucifer and, if her father ever decided to step down, (which he wouldn't) she would be the one to become Lucifer the Third. She also wrote that Jeff and her used to be childhood friends before they started dating, with the precision that they truly were in love with each others.

    Of course it should have mean that Jeff would have to become a demon in the future, but she had a last idea in mind to prevent that. Opening her boyfriend's profile, she wrote that the love he shared with her somehow caused a classmate, another succubus, to feel he was stealing hell's princess, like Jenny was no more than a trophy, an object to posses, and had used knifes to simultaneously transpierce both his stomach and his heart. Thankfully, with the help of her best friend, a black-skinned angel-girl named Alice, Jenny had been able to stop time for both Jeff and his aggressor and teleported them to hell in a hurry. There, demonic doctors had donne the only thing possible to save her boyfriend, replacing his stomach and heart with the ones of the succubus that had nearly killed him.

    Jenny then clicked on ‘Valid’.

    While still technically a teenage boy, Jeff now had all the ability of a succubus, he also needed feed the same way rather than how he used too. But for now, while he had been cleared from the hospital, he still needed to stay in bed, hence why he currently was in Jenny's queen-sized one.

    And the succubus that had almost killed him? God make her die, her soul now tortured so it could be cleaned of all sins.
