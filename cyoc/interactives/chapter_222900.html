    The powers of this items have been studied deeply and an actual classification of them has been made, varying from:

    Creature treasures: the carrier of this kind of object can transform at will completely or partially into the creature the treasure represents, varying from animals, mythological beings or monsters. For example there a person wearing a tiger collar can transform themselves into a fully feral tiger, anthropomorphic tiger or just transforming some parts like growing tiger claws and fur.

    Special treasure: this is a completely other kind to the other two, since these treasure don't actually transform the carrier into something, more like they give their user a power related to transformation, like merging with others, shrink or grow, change their gender or age, and an infinity amount of different types.
