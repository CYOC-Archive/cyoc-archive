    “I’m left-handed, the sign of a witch.” she whispered.

    What the fuck?

    “WHAT!?” you yelled furrowing my eyebrows, just barely keeping yourself from hitting something, “they send you to die on such a flimsy pretense?”
    Ruby was shaking with closed eyes and repeating “Please don’t kill me. Please don’t kill me.”, while crying, which shook you out of it.
    “Ruby.” you said softly, and she looked you in the eyes. Hers were red from crying and it hurt your heart seeing her face like this.
    “I’m sorry for losing my temper.”, you hoped that this will calm her down for a bit.
    “Huh?” she said in a tone expecting punishment “you will not kill me?”.
    “No, enough injustice was done to you, today.” you said, still angry at her village, “Do you have still somewhere to run to? Family outside your village?”
    “My living aunt voted to send me here, too. They say that bad luck will overcome the village, if I stayed. Only my late father would have kept them from sending me away.”
    Those superstitious bunch of assholes!

    “Ok, Ruby, you are welcome to spend your youth here. I realize that it chains you to me, but that’s the best I can do, for now.”
    She looked at you with big eyes and an open mouth and you booped her nose with your pinky. “Hello is there a Ruby there?”
    “Sorry mistress, but I expected to be bound to your service my whole life”
    You giggled like the child you are, “No, I’m sorry, but you just looked funny like that.” the effect was diminished by the evidence of her earlier crying, but you wanted to make yourself less threatening to her. After you said that she frowned at you, but then it broke to a smile, go you!

    “Your maid gave it to me, before sending me in. She said it suited your taste.” whatever you paid her, she needed a raise.
