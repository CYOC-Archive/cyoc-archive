    A New Assignment

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 3 years ago O

    Shannon Davis has had an interesting career, serving an international division of intelligence known as IAA, the International Anomalies Association. She had been serving the organization for 8 years at this point, having joined at the young age of 19 as an intern. Now she was a 27 and was a well decorated and experienced agent.

    Her day started off rather slow in her appartment, the curtains being the only thing stopping the morning sun from flooding her bedroom. Her brown hair slipped off her shoulders as she sat up and stretched her arms for the ceiling, letting out a drawn out yawn.

    A light beeping noise would catch her attention as she turned her head to the nightstand. The cellphone left there was emanating a familiar alarm for Shannon. With a soft exhale, her hands reached for the phone in order to see what HQ had for her today. Must have been a new assignment right? Her amber eyes focused on the text.

    "Agent Davis, we have an urgent mission for you involving activity reported at Havenview University. We've determined you are the best qualified for an infiltration mission. Please meet us at 1100 hours at the Metamorphosis Department, this mission will require a physical disguise. - Director Hermes"

    "God, seriously? A college? I thought I was done with that crap.." Shannon grumbled as she finished reading the message. This sounded like a long term mission, though Shannon was usually ok with those, given she didn't have much of connections, no close friends or relatives. It was better that way, no one else worrying about her.

    Shannon proceeded with her morning routine in a rush. Though she also tried to savor the moment some given she had no idea how long she'd be gone. Looking at her reflection as she brushed her teeth, Shannon would wonder what she'd be disguisng herself as. The technology at IAA was impressive, they had figured out how to genetically modify the appearances of any human. Race, Sex, Age, all putty in their hands.

    Shannon quickly got herself dressed up and took a hastily made breakfast, then got herself out the door. The car ride there was insignificant, so let's jump to what's important here.

    The Metamorphosis Department seemed busy today, then again it was a while since she had been here, who knows what new projects they had been working on. Though Shannon would find a familiar face waiting for her.

    "Ah, Ms. Davis. You've arrived just in time." It was Dr. Naga, an older but polite man whom had worked in this department for 25 years. He practically founded the department.

    "Morning Doc, I'm guessing you guys must have achieved some sort of breakthrough?" Shannon would as ask, gesturing to the other activity around the lab.

    "In a sense, but unfortunately I can't elaborate on it just yet, Ms. Davis. Besides, we have a mission to prepare you for yes? How long has it been since your last metamorphosis?" Both Shannon and Dr. Naga move away from the main laboratory and over to Dr. Naga's office in order to go over this mission.

    "3 years? I think? I know it's been a bit, who am I supposed to go undercover as anyways?" Shannon inquired while taking a seat in a chair in front of the doctor's desk, her arms folding as she relaxed into the material.

    "I have the information about that right here." Dr. Naga would pull a manila folder with her initials on the tab. Internally, Shannon would gulp when seeing it. The reality of this was setting in and the suspense was building inside her as Dr. Naga set the folder down in front of her. "We've arranged a profile for your alias, along with necessary documents so no one will suspect you're not who you're saying you are while you're there."

    This was her job, and she's done this before, why was she feeling so nervous? This was supposed to be completely safe. Taking a slightly shaky breath, Shannon reached for the manila folder and flipped open the first page of the profile, revealing that she was going to be..
