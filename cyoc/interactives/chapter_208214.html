    As I stepped out of my family home, my head started to return to its normal state, this made me realize that my mom was right: I had gotten up much later than usual. 'Damn Aliens,' I thought with a frown, 'if I would go my usual route, I'll be to late and Yui will be all over me. I knew several shortcuts, but all of them had some inconvenient aspects to them,' my frown soon faded though, when I remembered that I had the Device. 'Why should I care what Yui will do,' I think to myself, as I look down at the Device in my hand, 'I have THIS,' so I pocketed the Device and started my walk to school.

    While on my way to my school, I am thinking about several ways I could get Yui off my tail... okay, yeah... humans don't have tails, but you know what I mean. Anyway, the most obvious one being the “command” function. It would be fine if I'd use the Device for something like that, right? I started thinking, 'could this be some kind of prank by Kiyoshi? Nah... he wouldn't be capable of something like that. Even abduction sounded far more logical and likely, but speaking of him... is he aware of what happened to me last night,' I ask myself, I knew I had to find out.

    When I arrive at my school, I see the Kiyoshi also arriving at the same time. “Hey J-Man,” he greets me, “why'd you just up and leave last night? I told you to keep your eyes out for aliens!”

    'Guess that answers that,' I think to myself, I then wondered if it would be a good idea to tell him the truth. Knowing him, I'd regret it, but I felt like I couldn't keep this bizarre experience to myself.
