    A collar with a silver bell on it "this is nice" she pulls it around her neck and snaps the latch "This good enough?" she looked up and a man came from behind the curtain, he had short blond hair and was in a suit with a blue tie "Yes welcome the M-high!" he exclaimed as he walked over and hugged her "you'll be in my room 3-B my lovely student! go to the room now and meet your other class mates once I'm finished up here I'll be there" he got off and patted her head "little kitten" Juna walked away into the hallway "that guy seemed nice, but what was with the box and why did I have to put this on" she wondered as she stepped into the class room.

    Kids were everywhere, some in desk, some up around talking with others, some reading books on the floor "wow..." Juna looked around the room in aw when she saw Rose siting in a desk looking out a window, she walked over to her "hey looks like we're in the same class" Juna said with a smile, Rose looked up and hugged her "Juna!" she smiled being pulled by Juna who was a bit taken aback "That seemed out of place." Juna pulled Rose off her and sat down "guess your really happy we're in the same class huh?" Rose nodded "yes, now I'm not alone in more ways than one!" Juna opened her mouth to ask what she meant when the teacher chimed in "Hello all! let me personally welcome you to M-High now lets start to get to know each other, first by taking attendance" he opened a booklet, "Teacher when you call on us should we say what species we are?" Juna looked over at a raccoon draped in the school uniform holding one of the sleeves up. "What... the..." she thought to her self as some of the other kids around her started turning into animals "You're all here to learn to become more human... but there is no rule against revealing your species or dropping your transformation so... If you want to reveal it go ahead!" Juna felt her self slipping in the chair her view getting lower "whats going on!?" she said quietly, her ears started getting covered in red hair slowly moving to the top of her head getting pointed, her nose turned pink an flat while it pushed out into a small muzzle dragging her mouth, her upper lip curved and thin whiskers sprung out of her face, she shrank in her seat getting tangled in her clothes as her legs got smaller and her knees bent backwards,being covered in red hair, her toes started to come together as the bottoms of her feet grew pads and her nails grew out and curved, she fell from the desk and fought her way out of the clothes, once out her back cracked an a tail grew from it covered in puffy red fur swishing back and forth, her arms shrank and her finger became shorter as they began to merge like her feet, nails lengthening, pads forming, fur spreading across them, she walked 3 steps on all fours before turning to see Rose, but it wasn't Rose it was a brown cat with white paws, after that Juna fell over and fainted.

    Some of the other kids came over to see but the teacher pushed them aside bent down and picked her up "Its probably just a small bit of anemia she just needs rest, I'll take her to her room your all counted as here for today" He walked out of the room cradling Juna.

    Rose turned back and followed after him while the rest of the kids went back to what they were doing before he came..


    Juna woke up having her eyes meet with a white wall, she stretched and saw her arm it was normal "oh it was just a dream thank god" thing jumped onto the bed and she got up "good to see your up!" Juna sat up "thanks Ros... where are you?" she looked around and saw a cat on her bed "I'm right here silly" the cat said "oh right right.. WHAT!?" Juna pulled back and slammed the back of her head into a wall "Ow...." she leaned over holding her head "are you OK!? for a cat you don't have very good reflexes." the cat laughed "what do you mean? why are you talking!?" Juna shouted "Your a cat silly, a mystical one why else would you be here? and what do you mean why am I talking this is normal." the cat stared at her with a tilted head "You OK? need something?" Juna looked up and the cat had its paw on her fore head "you don't have a fever." the cat jumped off the bed and turned into a clothed Rose "well I'm going to get the school nurse just in case!" she ran out the door

    Juna just sat there "Whats going on!?" she thought "what happened to me, why is she a cat, what is this place, what time is it!?" her teacher walked in "its 8 Am you slept all of yesterday away, and I was looking so forward to getting to know you too kitten" he sat down on the bed "My name is Glenn, whats your's kitten?" he patted her head "I'm Juna, mister Glenn tell me whats going on?" Glenn lifted his hand from her "Well this is a place for mystic animals to learn to act human for the cases if homes are torn down or something like that, to tell you now I'm really a fox, so you came along already a human so we couldn't take you but you begged to get in so, we gave you something to give you animal form many things really you just picked a collar, you wanted to come here as a human high school student right? Well in the start we're going to teach easy human things so you should breeze right by, but next term you should be learning all you need to miss Juna." Juna smiled "this place is a bit odd but at least I can get a diploma." Glenn smiled back "There is the silver lining for both you and the school you seem like you can work with anything so I'm sure you'll have human form down in no time." Juna looked down "Nroooo..." she was a cat again leaning against the wall "ow did I not notice..." she laid down "Don't worry you'll get use to it Kitten" Glenn stood up and walked out

    "how will I face my family like this" she dug her head into the bed
