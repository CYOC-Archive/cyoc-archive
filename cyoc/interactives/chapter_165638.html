    This was all a mistake; Tom was trying to be a good Samritan, but the cops didn't understand. Tom was in New York City in the parking lot walking to the store when he saw a man in a ski mask robbing an elderly woman at gun point. Tom always carried a legally concealed firearm, and when he saw the situation, he couldn't just ignore it. He cautiously pulled his weapon and approached the crook from behind. "Drop it punk!" he demanded in a loud voice, while cocking his firearm so the thug could tell he wasn't bluffing. 'Chhhhk-chk' The man complied and Tom got him on the ground with his gun and ski mask removed from him. When the cops came, Tom did all the right things; he surrendered his firearm immediately when the NYPD arrived and handed them his concealed weapons permit. Unfortunately, the woman who was being robbed had fainted and all bystanders that actually saw what happened were long gone. The only witnesses left reported that they saw Tom standing over a man and an old lady while holding a 'really big gun'.

    Tom was arrested and taken to jail while the robber was free to go. When he got to the small, overcrowded jail, the police explained that he would be locked up until he paid bail, got acquitted at trial, or voluntarily chose the MORPH program. "I haven't done anything wrong! MORPH is used for real criminals! I want my phone call!" Tom demanded. Tom called his wife, Beth, and explained what had happened. Beth immediately got the kids in the car and headed to the jail, which was an hour drive in rush hour.

    The NYPD officer offered Tom the his choices again and Tom refused. "Ok, its your funeral," the cop told him.     "Your family is here; come with me," he said. Tom followed the officer to the visitor room where you can see the other person through the glass; his wife and kids were there. He picked up the phone handset.

    "Hi sweetie, hey guys," he greeted them. "This is all just a huge mistake! I rescued that woman, but the only witnesses left saw me holding the gun; they didn't see me take him down! This is all so unfair! There's no way we can afford the half a million bail payment, or hire a lawyer. They're telling me the only other way to get released is the MORPHPFVTAEOR program, but I didn't do anything! I don't deserve that!
