    Ty's Power

    an altered home

    Ty trailed behind the now 15 year old Eileen, listening as she rambled on about how amazing his powers were. He was only half listening, though, wondering what they would find upstairs in this altered reality. He found himself especially curious as to what Eileen's room would look like. Ty had already inadvertently changed things so that she not only thought of herself as a teenager, but her daughter now thought of her as a sister! Apparently they had a whole new set of parents as well - he'd rearranged the whole family dynamic!

    Reaching the top if the stairs, Ty started to get a glimpse of how much had changed for the world to accept Eileen as a teenager. He knew Eileen's bedroom had been located on the far right, having seen her in her window on occasion - the single mother sometimes forgot to lower the blinds. Now though, the new 15-year-old took a sharp left, towards a room Ty had known to be a spare guest room. As Eileen pushed the door open, Ty's eyes went wide.

    Instead of a spare bedroom, Ty now observed what appeared to be the typical teenage girl's bedroom. Posters of musicians, clothing scattered messily throughout the room, even the occasional stuffed animal lay strewn about. "Sorry about the mess," Eileen giggled. "Really should of cleaned up, but I wasn't expecting company."

    Ty said it was fine as he watched Eileen haphazardly throw objects into her closet. Quickly gazing inside, Ty could see it appeared to have shirts, sweaters, jeans, and even a few dresses, all sized for Eileen as she currently was. "I thought your bedroom was on the other side of the house?"

    "I wish!" she scoffed. "My folks have the big bedroom. Becca's is a decent size, but I get this tiny, little room with this itty-bitty closet. What I wouldn't give to have a walk-in closet!"

    "The room's not that bad," Ty offered. "It seems like there's more room for stuff in the back of your closet, right?"

    Eileen had been perusing the jumble of outfits inside her closet but as her friend spoke, she realized there suddenly WAS more space behind the first rack of clothes! As she pushed hangers aside, the fabric seemed to part and align itself to the right and left, opening up into a huge walk-in closet. Shelves appeared along the back wall with drawers and even a vanity space in the center; there was even room for a plush ottoman in the center! A skylight allowed a shaft of sun to filter down into the new space, catching on a small chandelier of crystals and refracting the light into a prism of rainbows that danced around the room.

    "Holy..." Eileen turned slowly as she took in her magically expanded closet. "Dude! This is freaking awesome!" she gave a cute, excited hop as Ty joined her in the new space, throwing her arms around her friend and giving him a tight hug, her small breasts pressing against his chest. "It's like Mary Poppin's magic bag or that tent in Harry Potter - much bigger on the inside!

    Ty shuffled awkwardly as she released him to twirl around once more, discreetly reaching into his pocket to adjust the slight erection her excitement was giving him. Eileen plopped down on the ottoman, her eyes finally coming to rest on her magical friend.

    "You're seriously incredible, Ty. I can't seem to wrap my head around it, no matter how many things your alter and change." She twirled a lock of her dark hair around her finger as she gazed shyly at her longtime crush, who was turning out to be WAY more amazing than she could have ever imagined. "What should we change next?"
