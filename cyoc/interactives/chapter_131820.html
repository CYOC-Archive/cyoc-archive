    The car moves off yet again. Each time Nathan rolls into a metal bar. It's painful and cold, especially considering he's wearing nothing. The car makes a rapid stop and Nathan rolls into the rear of the back seat. He let's out a groan in pain.

    Minutes pass. The car has since been turned off and Nathan is relieved he's not rolling into any metal bars. Suddenly the boot door opens. Nathan goes into a fetal position, light rushes in and he sees Kaidin standing there. "So you want me to go in the boot?" He asks.
    "Yes honey," replies his and Nathan's mum. Kaidin removes his bag and hops into the boot. The bootlid closes quickly, hitting Kaidin's ass. He looks at Nathan and asks, "Have any idea what she's doing?"
    "No. I was in my puffy jacket form and she was going to take me to a charity shop. But it seems turning human changed that."
    "You want something to wear?" Asks Kaidin.
    "I guess." Kaidin blindly reaches into his bag and hands some underwear over. "It's wet. But that's from sweat."
    "Okay," replies Nathan struggling to get the underwear on in such a cramped place with no lighting. Nathan puts the underwear on and he feels a little less vulnerable. "I've got a school shirt if you want. Saw no point of wearing it considering it's too cold to wear anything other than a hoodie." Nathan grabs the shirt and puts it on, again with great difficulty. "It's a good thing I'm on the shorter side," he laughs.

    After what feels like an hour. The car engine turns off. There had been the sound of wind signifying highway travel. The bootlid opens and their mum stares down at them. "We're here," she says before shutting the bootlid again. Gas suddenly fills the boot and the two boys pass out.
