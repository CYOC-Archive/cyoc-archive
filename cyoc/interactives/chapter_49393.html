    "By the Keys of Zeraphan, if I have to listen to anyone more of this someone will pay!!"

    Kaylee, Fran, Darla, and the cubs charged on the intruder, who froze them in their tracks with the slightest of a gesture.

    Turning to Jensen, he said: "You're the one I came here to see."

    "Give me one good reason why I shouldn't tear your throat out...human!!"

    With an offended look on his face, the man said: "First off, I'm not human, and secondly...I'd like to see you try!!"

    With a mighty howl of anger, Jensen charged the intruder...who calmly sidestepped the attack and Jensen crashed into a wall, causing a picture to fall and break.

    "A rare Picasso!! You should be more careful, you ham-fisted bun vendor!!" With that, the gentleman restored the painting with a wave of his hand, and bound Jensen to a chair with unbreakable bonds which was sunk into the floor.

    "Now as I was saying....where are my manners? Allow me to introduce myself, I am a Spirit of Change...my real name is not pronounceable by mortal tongues. I am the spirit that some mortals...your ancestors actually...bargained with so many years ago."

    "You see, your ancestors dabbled in witchcraft and summoned me. In exchange for power and prestige...they would play a little game with me...thus beginning Glendale's little tradition."

    He turned on Jensen angrily. "The game was to be for my amusement, and to feed the chaos energies...not for indulging your little lupine fantasy!! By using the game to transform everyone in town into either your sluts or your spawn, you undermine everything I have worked for...and I will not let a spoiled little child undo my work!"

    He paced around the room, taking all of it in. He finally stopped in the center of the room.

    "We'll start from the back and go forth."

    He snapped his fingers, and Kaylee's parents quickly reverted to human child, and then back to their proper ages and genders. A second snap restored Mr. and Mrs. Smith to their proper states as well.

    Seeing the anger and frustration in Jensen's eyes, he said: "Now you know how it feels to undo another's work. Conjuring a rolled up paper, he smacked Jensen on the snout. "And by the way, you can't use the Dare to make someone choose Transform. It's against the rules. Otherwise, the game would be just Transform *SMACK* dumb doggie!!"

    Looking at Fran, Darla, and Kaylee, he said: "Transforming a man into a woman, and your sex slave...(said with a disgusted tone in his voice)...If I had a Dramalach for every time a mortal used that one..." He shook his head. "Just because you drape them in animal skins does not make it original. Time to fix that."

    A snap of his fingers, and Fran and Darla turned human female, then back into Frank and Daryl. Looking at the two, he said: "The mental conditioning is broken, you are back to normal. I will send you back to your lives with none of these events having happened. All shall be as it was...except for your friend who was turned into a pig. He has passed into Death's domain and is therefore beyond my influence. Don't worry, the ones responsible will pay for their actions."

    A wave of his hand, and all vanished except for Kaylee and Jensen.

    He regarded them carefully. "Now what to do to you..."

    "When I get out of here, I'll...*SMACK*"

    "BAD DOGGIE!!" He smiled. "I know just the thing..."

    "Your parents are going to forget all about you...they never had a son or a daughter." As he spoke, Jensen saw the mansion restoring itself around them...all traces of him ever living there were erased away.

    "Now for you." He snapped his fingers, and both Jensen and Kaylee felt their fur start to sag, their eyes got big and had a plastic sheen to them, and their paws and tails became cartoonish in shape.

    "What have you done to us? Stop!!"

    He shook his head. "I'm afraid the changes are permanent. You have reverted back to what you are...with a few differences" He gestured, and a mirror appeared. In it the could see themselves as Kyle and Jennifer...except that Kyle had large breasts and a vagina, and Jennifer was well muscled and had a penis as long as his/her forearm.

    "You will still think of yourselves as Kaylee and Jensen, but feel more at home wearing the fursuits." As they looked into the mirror, they saw themselves garbed in mascot versions of what Kaylee and Jensen looked like.

    "I am not without pity. You two will be living in a community that is open and accepting to you. You two will be a happily married couple, and you will have many children. Your new life begins...NOW!!"

    With a flash they disappeared. The spirit looked around one last time.

    "Well, everything seems to be in order...except for one last thing.
