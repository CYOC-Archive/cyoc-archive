    In the room Katie had been placed in a woman would eventually open the door and walk in.

    She looked like she was in her mid 40s or so. Her face was still pretty for her age but she had some clear age on her face around her forehead and cheeks. Her skin was an olive color and her hair dark brown and greyish cut short only a few inches long and combed almost like the female version of the clean cut look you might expect on a male government agent. She wore a pair of frameless glasses and a black skirt suit. Her figure was a bit plump but still well shaped with a rather large bust and hips. The type of woman who gained weight in a flattering way that always made Katie jealous.

    "Hello. I'm Agent Bell, and I'll be going over what's next for you." she said pushing up her glasses and then looking to a tablet she had been holding in her hands. Katie then jumped up from the chair she had been in and yelled.

    "It's been hours!.... I think, you people took my phone. What the hell is going on and where is my husband?!" she blurted out with some clear signs of frustration and worry in her voice. It was only a few hours ago that she had seen a murder so she was still pretty understandably shook .

    The agent would simplly sigh and move to sit down at the table in the room moving her hand to suggest Katie also sit down.

    "Relax I'll explain everything. Basically this is the Witness Protection Bureau. A government agency. Your husband is safe and fine right next door I'll be talking to him after we're done. You reported a murder involving a very dangerous and well connected man. I'll be honest if the police know you reported it, then that pretty much means they know as well. Meaning you will be in danger until this can be taken to court. Now the reason you're here is we would like to offer you protection for your cooperation." She paused a moment tapping some things on her tablet as she read things over before starting again.

    "Now you don't need to accept our offer, but if you don't want our help well.... good luck." she said with a bit of a sinister cut to it suggesting she'd likely be killed. After a moment Katie responded processing all she had said.

    "Okay.... I get it, but why the van and why did it take so long... I want to see Brandon." Katie asked getting a bit anxious at all this information. Agent Bell nodded and then tapped on her tablet showing a live feed of Brandon bored in the waiting room.

    "He is right next door. Now... the reason it took so long is because of the protocol we use here... pretty much no one will know you're here but me and no one will know you exist but me. It's... a bit complicated to explain but its for your own protection. You see the government has actually developed some pretty amazing technology." She would then show her a video going over the nanobots and the painless process used to convert people into all new people.

    "So... you agree to accept our help then?" she asked opening an e-contract for her hand print.

    Katie paused a moment and placed her hand on the screen and it confirmed making the Agent nod and give a lack luster smile.

    "Great, so I'll leave you with this tablet, browse your options and I'll go talk to your husband." she said putting the tablet down on the table for her and moving to head into the next room.
