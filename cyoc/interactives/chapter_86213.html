    An unfamiliar scent perks your interest. Your new body seems to react to it, but you cannot place it. Only after following it to its source do you realize it is coming from the other side of the fence, in your neighbor's yard.

    You've never really paid attention to your neighbor's dog, but she's always seemed rather friendly to you. Of course, that was when you were just another human. Now that you're a dog, you're unsure how she will react. Nervously, you eye her through the chain-link.

    She saunters over toward you, and you tense a bit until you realize she is wagging her tail. You exhale in relief, then begin to get a bit excited. Now that you're a dog, maybe you can understand what she means when she barks! Talking to dogs would definitely be an interesting experience. Since you can only make dog-like sounds now, you try to get a conversation going with what you hope is a friendly yip. You bark a little too, but not too loud or aggressively.

    All she does in response is pant happily with her tongue lolling and give you a look that you feel is somewhere between patronizing and confused. You keep trying, but when she finally responds with a few woofs, you realize you still have no clue what she is trying to communicate. Disappointed, you realize that even though your body may be that of a dog, you still have the mind of a human. Your body may be able to mimic the instincts of a dog, but you do not have the life experience necessary to truly be a dog amongst other dogs. This saddens you somewhat, and in a sense you realize that you are now cut off from both humans and dogs, unable to truly communicate with either. You begin to truly hope that you will not remain a dog for too long.

    While your human mind is philosophizing, your canine body, on the other hand, is interested in other things. While you have no interest in your neighbor's dog other than as a possible party to converse with, your male body does feel attraction. You are pretty sure your neighbor's dog is spayed, but your canine body doesn't seem to care much. A female is a female, after all, and you have obviously never been neutered. You dismiss these urges out of hand, but a part of you does remain curious as to what it would be like. After all, things would be pretty interesting considering the breed of dog you turned into. You cannot help but be amused that you turned into...
