The former bully now found himself the tormented as athletes tried to shove the small kid into lockers on occasion. Mary, Katie and Kellie felt slightly bad for the boy but realized it was karma. Kevin had inflicted so much misery in his time that being the bullied for a short time would help. Kellie and the others would keep an eye on Kevin from now on, making sure that he could at the very least go to school without fear.
