    Being held in a cage while someone is carrying you is bothersome. Every time the person who has you captive takes a step, the cage shakes. However, this weirdo who has named you "Blackfrost" sets you down on a bench.

    "Ya, know what, Blackfrost? The kingdom needs new War Gryphins like you. But when a gryphin is born with the same colors for fur and feathers, that's rare. So you'll be trained by the King and Prince themselves."

    Suddenly, he sees a girl walking by. "I'll be back in a minute Blacky." He says. He then puts a cloth on your carrier and ran off after the girl. "Perfect, I'm stuck in a cage, and I'm no bigger than a cat! Plus I'm a completely different species!" Though what sounds like perfect English to your ears, sounds like a bunch of squawks and screeches to everyone else.

    Your caws and squawks attract a nearby villager to come towards your direction. 'I wonder what that could be' the villager thinks. The villager lifts up the cloth exposing you. Both of your eyes widen. Some stranger found you and you can't communicate. While the other persons' eyes lit up because it's a rare female baby gryphin.

    Your terrified because you don't know what they'll do to you. And you don't know if they'll take you to their home or sell you or take you to the palace. You didn't have enough time to figure out what will happen because they put the cloth on your cage, picked you up, and ran.

    What do you do now?
