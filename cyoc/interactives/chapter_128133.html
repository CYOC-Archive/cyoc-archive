    "Kellie!!" Lisa and Katie exclaimed. Kellie smirked as she looked down at her seven year old girlfriend and her aunt. "Morning girls, sorry I've always wanted to say that. "

    Katie gaped at her girlfriend, who looked to be in her mid thirties now. "Wha...what are you doing here?" She asked.

    Kellie smirked smugly as she narrowed her eyes. "Well I was coming over to see you last night," she said. "Imagine my surprise though when I look into the house and see a pair of 10 year olds."

    Katie now blushed as she glanced at her feet, unable to respond. Lisa stepped forward in her place to answer. "This wasn't Katie's fault," she stated. "I took the ring and made a wish. I wanted to cheer her up after the crummy week she had."

    Kellie now narrowed her eyes as she looked over the tiny redhead. "Well I'm glad you were honest Lisa still, I'm afraid both of you need to be taught a lesson."

    Katie and Lisa both felt their stomaches begin to churn as Kellie slowly began to pace. "I told you to be careful with the ring Katie," she lectured. "I had hoped you would of seen that but unfortunately that doesnt seem to be the case. So this is whats going to happem now."

    Kellie paused and looked down at the two seven year olds im front of her. Both girls now wore a nervous expression, like children awaitimg their punishment. Kellie could only smile as she thought of the best possible punishment.

    "You two are obviously too immature for this," she said holding the ring up. "So I think for now I'm going to leave you like this. Im your mom and my parents are your loving grandparents."

    Katie and Lisa's eyes began to dampen with tears as they stared at the menacing adult in front of them."No...you....you can't," Lisa wailed. "Im a grown up! I....I have a boyfriend! Jason....Jason will be looking for me. He loves me."

    The sacrene smile Kellie now wore was both soothing and menacing at the same time. "Oh Lisa dear," she soothed. "Your much too young for a boyfriend. Besides, I didn't make me and my parents 20 years older for nothing. For now you two are going to grow up all over again. Maybe this time you can learn some responsibility."

    Lisa began to wail as Katie sobbed quietly looking at her girlfriend. She was heartbroken that Kellie could be so mean. "Why," she whispered in between sobs. "Why arr you being so mean to us."

    Kellie's face softened as she looked at Katie. Although angry she did still love the girl. Now though that love was more maternal than anything else. "Oh sweety," she cooed. "I'm not being mean. This is tough love, I want whats best for you and Lisa. Your my daughters now and until I think your ready, your going to be under my care."

    Katie now joined Lisa in loud sobs as the two seven year olds hugged eachother for comfort. A smug smile spread across Kellies face as she looked at them and then the ring. Maybe she had finally gottem through to them how dangerous the ring was. Now she needed to decide how far she was going to take this.
