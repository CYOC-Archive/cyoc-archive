    Jennifer Nunez, age 31, has gotten fed up with her job and would have loved to go on vacation right about then, and so she did. She had given birth to 4 kids, each 3 years old, at age 28. She has 2 girls and 2 boys. The oldest is Tommy, since ehe was the first out of her womb. Then Came Ashley, Kelsey, and Jamie. Jennifer decided to breast-feed all of them, but sadly, she wasn't able to since her breasts couldn't produce as much milk needed to feed all four of them. As they arrived at the resort, they were given their room. When they got into their room, Jennifer noticed a letter sitting in her bedroom. The letter wrote:

    Dear Jennifer,

    First of all, allow me to welcome you to the Transformation Resort. I hope this trip is worth it. Now I’m
    pretty aware that you have chosen the Age Wing of the resort. To that end let me give you some rules and
    advice.

    As you already know, this resort can transform their guests into anything and anyone they want. The Animal Wing, in fact, can turn you or turn parts of your body into that of any animal you'd like. But of course there are a couple of ways to do that. The room your in is one of them. It can make you change by just thinking about the animal you want to be. If you don't want to transform in your room, then there are plenty of other options. Match the animal with the habitat they live in. There are also restaurants that can change you with food.

    Just a bit of a warning before you start. If you stay in that form for too long (more that 24 hrs), then you'll be prevented from transforming back until you notify our staff. If you turn into a fish or any aquatic creature, then you'll be inside a fish bowl with someone carrying you around. If you need help getting around, just notify the staff with the "help comms" located in every hallway of the facility. If you want to transform into anything large (Elephant, Giraffe, etc.) then you'd have to do that outside. No transforming into extinct animals! Doing so will lead you to be kicked out.

    Once again, thank you for choosing the Transformation Resort and I hope you have yourself a safe and wonderful
    stay.

    "I wonder what we're going to do!" Said jennifer
