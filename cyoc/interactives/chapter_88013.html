    Katie and her friends arrived at the local collage and slipped in effortlessly, no one really noticed the girls weren't students, no one was really going to asked them if they attended the collage or not. The party was in fully swing. Thee seemed to be a number of people dressed up as rubber animal people, but only Katie and Mary for some reason seemed to notice. Mika and Claudia looked confused when Katie talked about it.

    A woman dressed as a rubber lizard woman under a Princess Leia, waved at the girls politely as they passed. She was arm in arm with a rubber ram man wit a ring around one hoof dressed up as Indiana Jones over the rubber goat costume.

    "You snuck in too huh?" The woman in the lizard costume said.

    "What?" Katie gasped.

    "Sorry, it's all over your faces, you snuck in here because yo knew it would be a great party too. Don't worry, we won't say anything." The lizard woman said.

    "Actually, we're from a community collage over in the neighboring state." Said the rubber red and purple rubber ram man,

    "Nice rubber lizard woman Princess Leia costume."

    " . . . right . . . costume, lizard woman." Her eyes shifted about.

    Katie and the rubber ram man noticed at about the same time both of them had rings of the same style and design, but with a different colored and cut gem.

    -My Master is cooler than your master!-

    -No, my wielder is better than your wielder!-

    "Uh, I think our magic rings are talking smack to each other . . ."

    "Okay, I'm Casper, I turn humans into rubber animals people to help them be happy."

    "I'm Katie, I turn people into kids to help them be happy."

    "Alright, truce, and neither of use our magic on each other or their friends so we can both just enjoy the party?"

    "Deal!"

    "This is Ashley Simmons by the way, the first person I transformed, and my girlfriend, and no, my ring doesn't make people fall in love."

    "Nice to meet you."
