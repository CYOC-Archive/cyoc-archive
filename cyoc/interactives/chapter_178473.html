His arms now forelegs and legs now hind legs. He saw his belly and flanks as being that of a young horse.

His horse instincts told him to run away, to be free but for some reason he also had a feeling of belongings with this man. So, he didn't fight and as a docile creature the halter snugged down over his face.

Memories were short now and Jeremy was fast losing his identity to that of a horse.
