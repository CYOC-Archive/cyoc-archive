    Matt peered through the grimy basement window as best he could. Through the rain and mud spattered glass he could just make out Brett's figure lying on a workshop table amidst the brushes, paint cans, nails, and other objects.

He couldn't just go ring the doorbell and ask if he could go down in the basement to see if the shrunken teen was okay. He thought for another moment and then grinned. Of course, he can shrink himself now. He'll just go in the basement the same way Brett did.
