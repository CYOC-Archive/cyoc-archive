    Mike was immediately amazed and intrigued, wanting a chance to really fix everything he felt was less than ideal with himself, his life, and his family... Seeing that Jeff was right here he decided to seize the opportunity.

    Jeff was excitedly rambling about some of the weird package features so Mike cut in. "I can tell you want to try it, and honestly I do too, why don't you pick out something you think Id like and apply it to me. we can go back and forth with it."

    Jeff excitedly pulled up his dad's profile and Mike stood up and walked a step or two away giving his son a tiny bit of privacy. "There!" he cried.

    Mike felt himself deflate from 210lbs to 170. His pants changed to fit and Jeff beamed at him... "Very nice. I haven't been this fit since I was in high school. My turn. Move over"

    Jeff scampered out of the way and stood where his father was a moment ago eager to figure out what his dad will think up for him.

    Mike made only two tiny changes, In the relationship dropdown he selected jeff to be his brother and he unchecked the box for subject to be aware. He activated the program and looked at Jeff excitedly, Just as Jeff had done to him a moment ago.

    "Are you done?" Jeff asked confused.

    "Yeah"

    Jeff looked around and glanced in the small mirror on his wall, "What changed?"

    "Oh, I changed your age. Made you a high school student again."

    Jeff's jaw dropped after a moment of reflection, "Oh no, Mike, Dude! You left the subject... ME... unaware of the changes. My entire life has been rewritten. I don't know how old I was, or if I had a college degree, or I don't even remember ever having sex man! Ugh! It's alright, we can fix it... I wasn't married or had kids or anything like that right?"

    Mike laughed, "No you were single and between jobs but we can fix all that in a minute and a half... pick something for me and we can rewrite your back story."

    Jeff made a face but wrote it off, clicking a few buttons, "See, that's how you make someone younger and not ruin their lives." Mike regressed from later 40's to early 30's, having met his much older wife in college and having his, now 12 year old daughter when he was 21 and his now wife was 30.

    "Point taken, I'm sorry." Mike sat down at the pc, "So, how does Doctor Jeff sound? Jeff, Attorney at law? Mom and Dad would be so proud."

    "Jesus, Mike, I don't even know! In my reality you have taken over for mom and dad and have been raising me with your kids. I've felt like nothing but a burden to everyone because Mom and dad are too old to raise me, and you shouldn't have to be responsible for me. You're my brother... And it blows my mind that this is all made up. I was fully grown 10 minutes ago but now I'm hesitant to swear in front of you."

    Mike chuckled, "Porn star? Actor? Stunt double? We can always change it to something else but if you don't pick I will..." Mike starts typing, "Wor...ld Fay... mous... Gay... Porn..."

    "Ah! MIKE! Stop! That thing WORKS. Do you really want the family jewels in film! Do you really want Mom and Dad to have to go through that?"
