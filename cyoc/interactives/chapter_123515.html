Sophie WAS a good friend, and not to mention that many students from Lyre joined Animalia the previous year when they found that a mature Animalian was bigger, buffer, and more athletic than they could hope to be otherwise, not to mention the more "adult" things that many chose to join Animalia for.

    Jessica's first day had been rather quiet, but not one without her own self-realizations. School at WSA really wasn't all that different from what it was like at Lyre the previous year. The real difference was that the desks and tables were built to last and built to deal with many of the weight issues that larger Animalians presented that had been difficult the previous year. Many of the desks were solid metal that had been polished smooth and painted so that light wasn't needlessly reflected off of them. They were also easily adjustable to adapt to the height of the Animalian in question. There were levers on the sides of each desk that would allow students to adjust each desk as needed, and that came in handy during second hour. The Animalians that had been at her assigned desk were an Elephant and a Hippo that had been at the zoo when they were uplifted. When Jessica and Sammy arrived, they had to turn the levers and lower the desk so that it would fit the converted rabbit and uplifted hamster's smaller height. The chairs, which were built more like an office chair, complete with strong wheels had similar features. Getting the desks in the right height and place had been the biggest thing for day.

    "So how have you and Roger been making out?" Sammy asked to Jessica.

    "Making out?!" Jessica almost jumped at the question, nearly missing the chair as she an Sammy sat down.

    She then looked over to her former pet with some surprise.

    "Yeah," Sammy nodded, "You were busy with your friends on the Animalia Ambassadoria team that I didn't get to see much of you over the summer. I wanted to make sure things were okay."

    "Oh..." Jessica answered, slightly embarrassed as the first thing that had flashed through her mind was her and Roger in the back of her parents car making out with Roger going for her chest and her going for his pants. She tried to put that out of her mind, but a lot of it simply wouldn't go away.

    It was something that Jessica had been noticing a bit more, particularly after turning fifteen just before school started. She guessed that related to essentially growing up as an Animalian. In this it was something that was something of a living experiment. Most Animalians were double dosers, largely the result of either finding a mate rather quickly or the fact that they were already adults when they became Animalians. In this it had been observed that adult Animalians had naturally high libidos, likely to help increase the population, as Jessica had noticed that Abby and Samson, Sarah and Travis, and Malice and Simon often had sex with each other as often as they could.

    "And Roger and I could well end up the same way," Jessica privately realized to herself, "Some of my clothes are feeling a bit... tight... and I'm not mature, yet. I'm still growing... Eventually... I'll be built like Judy. I may not be as big as Abby... but I will be quite voluptuous and strong... and Roger..."

    Jessica soon had to stop thinking on that realization as that had only lead her starting to become slightly aroused. It only reminded her that she was growing up. She may not have gotten the double dose to go straight to being a physical adult the way Abby, Sarah, and Malice, but that didn't mean that as she matured she wouldn't catch up with them.

    "Roger and I are doing fine," Jessica eventually managed to answer Sammy, "He's been nice and has gone along with our Animalia Ambassadoria trips... Which has been fun, though we'll probably see a lot of that go down now that school is starting again. How are things with you and Samantha?"

    "Very good," Sammy smiled, "We're mates now... double dosed and everything... but we'll still be here... learn things and find ways to contribute to Animalia. And I owe it all to you, Jess... for being a good owner before I was uplifted and being such a good friend."

    "Thanks, Sammy," Jessica answered.

    Of course, the word that Sophie and Roland had been attacked had made Jessica's private wonderings about going through puberty as an Animalian seem a bit childish, as it did lead to some negative consequences, but in the end, the consequences that came down proved to be less what was feared. The only thing now was to move forward... Privately, she also wondered on how fast Project Thylacine would evolve into Project Dinotopia and what that could ultimately mean for Sophie. She remembered Sophie saying she was interested in the idea of being a pterodactyl at the hospital, but that'd also soon relate to other issues that went beyond some of the animal's instincts.

    There would also be parental worries, that Sophie's parents had. The Browns and the Wesleys probably had those fears too, when Abby and Sarah were first changed, but they were a different situation. Abby and Sarah were both transformed by accident and double dosed before they realized just how far they were going. Of course, for the Wesleys, some of those concerns were ultimately less when it was found that Sarah's father had been something of a closet furry who jumped at the chance, and Sarah's mother went along with him. However, her own parents had been hesitant when she voiced wanting to be an Animalian to assure Sammy that things would be fine between them, and while they ultimately gave their consent to her being an Animalian, they didn't allow for a second dose. Sophie's parents had similar concerns unless they changed their mind, Sophie would not get to be a pterodactyl until she turned eighteen.

    Though, as the group walked in to begin work on things like homework and so on, Jessica privately wondered on when would be the best time for Sophie. Her waiting until she grew up might mean the issue of instincts might be Sophie's only hurdle... but then a part of Jessica's mind hoped that Sophie's parents would change their minds, at least for a single dose, so that she and Sophie could grow up into adult Animalians together. Roland could join them as Sophie's mate... and their whole group could be complete.
