    The Magic Shop

    Elvira Snidely,mother of the DRAKON THREE

    added by Anonymous 6 years ago O

    Now even a lowly despised magical coin has to have a mother
    and a father.

    So there was bored Nurse Snidely at the Nurses Station when another of those bratty teen girls rushed in.

    This one was named Chelsea,complaining about stomach pains.

    Nurse Snidely gave her the appropriate medicine and was about to dismiss the kid when she remembered
    the kid had mentioned the troublesome magical coin that had caused
    her grief had the image of a dragon on it.

    Well decades earlier Elvira Snidely had been a promising Magic Coin internal circuits
    designer and had designed the prototype
    DRAKON THREE magical coin.

    It had never gone into mass production and there had only been 33 three of them made.

    Supposedly all had been destroyed,along with the blueprints.

    It only held 3 minutes power put had contained some of Elvira Snidely's best work.

    She was still ticked off at Mr Blowfish
    for having her baby destroyed.

    So with the slowly rising hope,Nurse Snidely asked
    the teenage girl if she could take a took at that magical coin.

    Chelsea handed it over,glad to be rid of it.

    Snidely had to keep from shouting out in joy,as the
    coin was a DRAKON THREE prototype
    Serial Number 14

    Knowing that Chelsea might not give it up if she knew its true value to its creator,Nurse
    Snidely said she'd send the thing over for Specimen Analysis to determine
    its faults.
    She pulled out the standard issue Magic Coin analysis form
    and as per regulation,Chelsea
    filled it in and signed it in order to get a
    replacement magic coin,this one a nifty new
    NIKO THIRTY THREE
    with magic good for 33 minutes.

    Snidely of course thought the NIKO was
    poorly designed trash.

    So she put the DRAKON THREE into the specimen bottle and waited for the teenager
    to get lost.

    When no one was looking Elvira Snidely hastily rescued
    her baby from the specimen bottle and tossed in a doomed NIKO to replace it.

    Then after closing she headed home with the magical coin she
    had created that had somehow survived the destruction of its siblings
    decades earlier.
