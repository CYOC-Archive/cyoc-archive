    "No! No! Anything but that!"

You'll lose your old memories fast and be left to lay and hatch an egg, which you will consider your own. Matt will have lost all of his memories by then too, of course. No upright walking for you from now on. You have wings and a tail that feels happy to feel the wind and the breeze and the soil beneath it. However, you see that other things require your immediate attention.
