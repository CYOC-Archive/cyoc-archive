Josh and Kyle were hiding in the kitchen around the corner, paralyzed with fear. "Guys?! Where are..." Owen looked down and saw half of the eagle chain on the floor. "Hey what's this? Looks like and eagle."

    Kyle and josh stared at each other with horror. Josh's half of the chain was missing. "It must have come off when we fell." He whispered.

    Without knowing, or thinking, Owen put the chain around his neck and smiled, "Looks good one me." But around the corner Josh and Kyle's body jerked, and started to walk, and head down the hall. Owen saw them out of the corner of his eye, so he turned to great them. "Oh there you boys are, how's... it... going..." Owen's jaw hit the floor at the sight before him.

    Before anyone could talk, Kyle and Josh started to separate. At first they were happy. They were coming apart, and glad to be doing so. But almost as soon as they were apart, Kyle and Owen were being pulled together. Once they were close enough, their bodies started to merge, the same way Kyle and Josh had earlier.

    "What the hell?!" Owen yelled.

    Kyle stared at Josh, "That's what he meant! Who ever wears the chains becomes fused together!"

"Oh, so from my understanding..." Owen quickly took the chain from his neck, and threw it over Josh's head. With in seconds, Owen and Kyle split apart, then Josh and Kyle fused back together.

    "I have to go to work." Owen grabbed his wallet and was heading out the door. "Besides, the two of you could learn to get along that way."
