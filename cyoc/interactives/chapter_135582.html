    "And the wheel lands on Paul. Now Paul, who do you choose to challenge?" asked Marcus.

    "I'll start by calling up Jana," Paul replied. Everyone else went and sat down on the on-stage chairs.

    "The first challenge is a simple one. It's called High or Low. In this challenge a card will be dealt to each person and they must guess whether the next card will be higher or lower than the card given to you with aces being the highest. Okay, Paul will go first," explained Marcus.

    "And Paul's card is... an ace! Paul, higher or lower?" Marcus asked.

    "Ummmm, lower. Isn't that kind of obvious?" replied Paul.

    "Eight! Paul is correct! Now for Jana." Marcus dealt a 6 to Jana. "Jana, higher or lower?"

    "Well with 8 higher options and 4 lower options I'm going to have to go with higher," replied Jana.

    "Five! Sorry Jana but you are incorrect. Paul, you may take a seat, Jana, it's time to spin the wheel! Remember the body parts that can be changed are: Arms/Hands, Legs/Feet, Upper Body, Lower Body, Size, Head, Mind, and a possible full transformation, which is an automatic elimination!"

    "Of course I'm first," Jana said rolling her eyes.

    The wheel spun and landed on Legs and Feet

    "Well, that's not the worst option," Jana said.

    "Okay Jana, go stand in the red square in the corner," Marcus instructed.

    Jana slowly walked to the square. A ray gun then dropped down from the ceiling. Jana's expression changed from annoyed to frightened. The gun aimed towards her legs and shot a ray at them. Jana's feet condensed into the shape of a paw with pads forming on the bottoms. Short sleek hair then started growing up her legs. Jana wobbled out of the square back to her seat trying to adjust to her new legs.

    "Alright, it's time to spin the wheel again! Next up is Jana! Who would you like to compete against?" asked Marcus.

    "I think I'm going to choose Katie, this should be a piece of cake considering how much younger Katie is," Jana replied.

    "Oh, so we're going to play it that way, huh? Well you are going down!" Katie exclaimed.

    "The next challenge will be Ping Pong cups! In this challenge you will have to get as many ping pong balls into a plastic cup in one minute. This challenge is all about accuracy and timing, age has no advantage here Jana," Marcus said, "Alright first up will be Jana."

    Jana started by doing some overhand throws and got 3 in. 30 seconds in she realized that it wasn't working too well. She decide to try to bounce it into the cup and got 4 more making a total of 7.

    Katie went up to the table next. When the timer started Katie tried the bouncing method but got nothing and then tried the overhand throw and got nothing again. With 15 seconds left Katie's face lit up with an idea. She grabbed a handful of balls, climbed up onto the table, ran down to where the cups were and quickly placed them all in. The timer hit 0 just after Katie put her eighth ball in.

    "And the winner is Katie!" Marcus exclaimed, "Jana, now it's..."

    "Wait! Wait! How is that fair!? She cheated!" Jana interrupted. "I win! I WIN!" She angrily shouted.

    "I said you just need to get the balls in the cup, I didn't say how, just that you had to get them in, so Katie wins! Jana, it's time to spin the wheel again!" Marcus continued.

    Jana angrily stormed down to the wheel and shot Katie a dirty look and mouthed "I am coming for you."

    Katie went to sit down as Jana angrily spun the wheel as hard as she possibly could. After a while of spinning, the wheel landed on lower body.

    "Go to the red sq..." Marcus started.

    "I KNOW! I KNOW!" Jana yelled.

    As Jana stepped into the square the ray gun came down again and aimed at her crotch. After the ray shot, the hair on Jana's legs started growing up to her belly button. A big tail then ripped through her pants and thumped to the ground.

    Jana slowly trudged back to her seat as Marcus went to spin the contestant wheel again.
