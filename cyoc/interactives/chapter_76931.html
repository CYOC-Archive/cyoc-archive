    "Don't you all notice how NO ONE is freaking out about this? Look."

    The rest of the schoolgirls toggled their eyes towards the opposite ends of the street. All looked well. There

    as they walked around the hideous ogre...nobody seemed worried. It was as if the ogre were a totally normal
    occurrence. It obviously wasn't invisible to the pedestrians all slumming about, since they were all giving it
    a healthy personal space to roam about in. When it approached someone, a man, he politely walked out of the
    way, and as he walked past, he looked over his left shoulder once more before finally moving on with his
    errands or where ever he was about to go.

    Back at the hotel, the possessed Tim and newly sired vampiress Vanessa lied down on their luxury king sized
    bed, eating popcorn and watching the confused speculations of the girls of St. Francis. At their feet lay Sam,
    Tim's black cat who used to be Vanessa's(formally Victor's) best human customer. He yawned in boredom, lazily
    eyeing the fishbowl full of betas on top of the dresser that Tim had bought at the hotel's pet shop.

    "Mistress, so now that we're here, how do we go about your plan for acquiring power here on Earth?"

    "First we must neutralize all the mortal witches and sorcerer's who protect this realm, and then once we have
    earned enough of their power, we move on to other places such as Morheim, Mount Olympus, Asgard, and all that
    other fruity godling stuff. And the souls. Can't forget corrupting more souls."

    "Mistress! Do you honestly think that you could accomplish all that by yourself?"

    "With the right amount of prep time, yes. The first thing that we do is visit an old friend of mine. He has a
    grimoire that...that turned me into this beautiful she-devil you have right before you."

    "You mean that you weren't always this sexy and powerful?"

    Tim sighed. "No. No, I used to be quite a geek. Well...the corporal part of me anyway. Not the part that
    really matters, but the one that allows me to stay in this realm.'

    "Oh, I see" said Vanessa, who went right back to eating popcorn.

    "Yes" repeated Tim monotonely.
