    It had been eight years since Jessica,Ambers mother ,and
    Amber had last seen the likes of Mythic Lands on
    Animal Resort Island.
    Amber had been 13 at the time and the mother and daughter
    had crossed over on that self same ferry to have some mother
    daughter quality time together.Eight years earlier Jessica hadn't known that her recently divorced ex husband was quite THAT bad and and they had all hoped to put the ruined family back
    together somehow.
    Alas Ambers twin sister Anna had gone on a cruise with her
    father whilst Amber and her mother had headed to the Island.
    To be honest the Island group vacation package had been a
    complete disaster ,what with Island Tour Guides posing as
    cockatrices,basilisks ,pegsi and manticores doing a pretty bad job of it.
    The disaster,the real disaster had occurred on the final day of their vacation,when mother and daughter had been group
    transformed into two dragoness's.
    The problem was that was the exact day that Anna,Ambers 13 year old twin,had returned to the cabin she and her dad shared
    aboard the ship and found her naked father and a nude young woman making love on Anna's bed.
    Anna,always of a fragile mind,had a nervous breakdown and
    the ships medical doctor had sent a urgent message of the crisis
    to Anna's mother with details.
    Amber had found then message directed to her mother
    and since she was already a flamebreathing dragon at the moment
    had fled the Island on her wings,a huge heavy rock in her
    foreclaws,and attacked her father as best a angry young dragoness can.
    Somehow everyone had managed to avoid dying over the next while but any hopes Jessica had of putting the ruined family
    back together had died on that day.
    The good news was that as a dragoness Jessica had found the
    Owner of the Animal Resort,pleading him for assistance to stop her daughter Amber from murdering her father.
    The filthy rich but aging transformation tycoon
    Tiverius TransDem
    had been in dragonform himself at the time,and had taken a liking to Ambers mother.
    They had married afterwards and Tiberius had left it all to
    Jessica ,on the stipulation that either she or her daughters
    Amber or Anna give him a heir(s)
    He had left his seed,in its various mythical animal forms,in the
    cryogenic storage vaults so that his wife Jessica or her daughters
    could produce the required heir at their earliest convenience.
    Tiberius had always had a bad heart and three years ago
    when Amber was 18,he had been in dragonform flying between
    one resort to another when his heart had given out and his lifeless form had plunged into the bottomless depths,never to be recovered.

    That had triggered the clauses of Tiberius TransDem's Last Will
    and Testament,and Jessica had encouraged Amber and Anna
    to secure their future by producing a heir,none too successful
    up till now.....
