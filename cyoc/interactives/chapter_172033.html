    The Turner House Halloween Party

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago AR AP BM Reality alteration
    Author note:
    Author's Note: This isn't exactly a new story, but rather an expansion on the Halloween Party that Gloria Turner threw at her house one Halloween Night which lasted a few hours and had a few more stories than just the ones that focused on her and her friends.

    33 year-old Casey was walking her son, Cole, down to the next house for Trick-or-Treating on Halloween night. Casey hadn't really wanted to take her son out tonight as she had just wanted to stay home and smoke, but Cole insisted as he had gotten his pirate costume not too long ago and really wanted to wear it.

    "Okay Cole, the next house is coming up, just remember to knock at the door and then we can move it along." Casey said as they approached the house.

    "Sounds like a party's going on mom." Cole noticed looking at the house.

    "That's nice, but we can't be at this house all night." Casey reminded her son as they approached the house. Cole knocked on the door and a girl in a pirate costume answered.

    "Aaargh matey, trick or treat!" The pirate teen girl said handing Cole his candy.

    "Alright, you got your candy, now let's go." Casey said as she nearly left with Cole.

    "Wait, don't you want to come inside? We've got some more treats inside." The pirate girl said.

    "Please mom? It looks like fun." Cole begged.

    Casey rolled her eyes.

    "Fine, a few minutes." Casey said as she and Cole entered the Turner Halloween house.

    Casey and Cole weren't aware of it, but when they entered the house, they came under the spell of Gloria Turner, who was the hostess of the party. She made it so anyone who came into the house would become a teenage to enjoy the party. Cole aged a couple of years before stopping at 15, his costume also altered so it was more appropriate for his age. His mother, Casey regressed back into being a 14 year-old girl now wearing a female pirate costume. Their minds also altered so they now believed they were boyfriend and girlfriend.

    "I said a few minutes before didn't I, Cole? Screw that. We are staying as long as we want." Casey said.

    "I'm fine with that. Just me, you, and our pirate booty." Cole grinned as he held Casey tighter.

    Outside, 45 year-old Donovan and his daughter, 8 year-old, Sierra were approaching the house. Donovan hadn't been a big fan of the devil costume his daughter had chosen, but he was glad she was having a fun time.

    "Daddy, can I go up to the door by myself?" Sierra asked her father who looked at the house with a little bit of hesitation but eventually nodded his head in agreement.

    Donovan watched his daughter went up to the door and a few teen girls invited her inside. Donovan got worried now and ran up to the door and knocked a little loudly. A 15 year-old girl in a revealing devil costume opened the door.

    "Excuse me, did my daughter just come in here? We're trick or treating, I don't want her mother to worry." Donovan asked.

    "Oh, sorry, I haven't seen her. You're welcome to come in and look for her." The girl said as Donovan came inside.

    Donovan's mind became hazy as the looked around at all the party-goers and his clothes changed to that of a devil costume and he looked around back at the girl who he now seemed attracted to, which might have been weird if he wasn't 15 years old now.

    "Did you say you were looking for someone?" The devil girl asked.

    "I think....maybe I was looking for you." Donovan said as the girl giggled.

    "I'm Donovan, but you can call me Donny." Donny said introducing himself.

    "I'm Sierra." The girl said as they looked into each other's eyes and felt some kind of spark between them.

    Young 7 year-old Lydia now came up to the porch with her mother, Joyce, dressed up a sock hop costume. The teens invited them in and Joyce at first was adamant, but eventually carried Lydia inside as she got into the beat of the music and began to dance with Lydia.

    Lydia also became interested in the beat of the music as she danced alongside Joyce and the two became twin sisters dressed in sock hop costumes. Everyone clapped at their dance routine and asked where they learned such cool moves.

    "Me and my little sister here have been dancing for years." Lydia smirked pointing at Joyce.

    "Little? You're only older than me by three minutes." Joyce laughed as the two enjoyed some snacks.

    Devan, dressed up as a magician was in a bit of a hurry to trick-or-treat at Gloria's house and almost wanted to leave immediately, but his mother Iris, suggest they go inside to see how the party was going.

    Iris went inside first and became a 15 year-old girl in a magician's costume. She then looked over at Devan, who while he may have only been 14, Iris was still attracted to her young boyfriend.
