    "Allen! I'm so short..." Peter grumbled, as he looked up to his best friend.

    "Oh don't worry, little buddy... I'll make you grow taller, but you'll need to maintain the same weight!"

    Suddenly, Peter felt that his head was rising up further from the floor, as his entire body started to grow taller.

    Just then, Allen's cell phone went off. Allen answered it and turned around, with his back facing Peter, while Peter's body continued to grow taller.

    Peter was now 5ft... 5'2"ft... 5'4"ft...

    "Hello? Hey! Sure! I'm fine! Wanna come join the swim team?..." Allen said, as he continued to talk on the cell phone, while he was unaware of Peter's growth.

    Peter was now at 5'10"ft... 5'11"ft... 6ft tall...

    He gasped as he looked down, realizing that his growth spurt wouldn't end, unless Allen stopped it using his power.

    For a brief moment, he looked normal, when he reached his normal height. His muscles were just right, and he was completely in the right proportions again. However, he continued to grow taller and taller...

    As Peter continued to grow, he noticed that he was becoming thinner while he was growing taller! His muscles slowly shrunk down, as his legs, arms and torso continued to stretch.

    Meanwhile, Allen continued to talk on the phone, while Peter continued to grow...

    Peter was now 6'9"ft... 6'10"ft... And growing...

    "Sure! Yes, we have a great group of guys on the team..." Allen continued to tell the person on the phone. "You'll love it!"

    Finally, Allen hung up the phone. When he turned around, he gasped out loud, when he realized that he was looking at Peter's stretched-out quads!

    He looked up and realized that Peter had grown to 9 feet tall!

    "Uhh... Allen! I'm kinda tall and skinny now!" Peter shouted, as he looked down at his best friend, who stood 3ft shorter than him. "Make it stop!"

    "You got that right!" Allen chuckled. "You're tall... and VERY SKINNY!"

    Peter looked down and realized that he was right: His arms were long and thin, not to mention that his legs were just a few inches thick, but they were almost six feet in length!

    "Here you go, stretch! I'll make it stop!" Allen laughed, as he fired some magic at his friend, whose body he could control completely.

    Peter’s legs alone were over six feet in length, but his torso was incredibly narrow! All his muscles had disappeared, and his arms were only about an inch thick!

    “Well, you wanted to be tall… You got it!” Allen laughed, as he looked up at his stretched-out friend.

    “Yeah… but I didn’t want to become a bean pole!” Peter said, as he rolled his eyes.

    “Don’t worry, buddy…” Allen laughed, as he rubbed his hands together. “You’re going to grow and shrink a lot more. I will only keep my power, as long as I make you grow every day! So…”

    Peter sighed. What was Allen going to do next?
