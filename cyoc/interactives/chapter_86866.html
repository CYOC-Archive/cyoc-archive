He decided to go online to pass the time until dinner was ready. Turning on the laptop and waiting several moments for the OS to load everything into memory, the Chronivac program popped right up as that was the last program used before the PC was put to sleep. The emitter chirped, begging to be put back on the charger, to which, Jeff obliged.

    "I'm only happy insofar as I can serve you, earlier you put your desires on hold to cater to my needs. I understand that it was the most efficient thing for you to do but feel shame in that I have delayed you, I can feel that you are eager to move forward with working upon yourself."

I have excess mass to maintain a relatively significant appearance alteration for you, including body and clothing, for an extended period of time. Some of the nutrition I absorbed was specifically for you, like the calcium for your bones. May I direct the remaining nutrition towards you as I originally planned?"

    Jeff stood up and walked to the mirror in his room. He barely noticed his dad coming into the house."Okay Great, I'm going to challenge you quite a bit right off the bat and see how you maintain it, then I'll work my way down to find your limitations. Sound good?"

    "Ingenious."

    "Alright." Jeff stripped out of his clothes. "I'd like to be wearing a black business power suit with a white shirt and a blue tie."

When the process was finished Jeff looked very well dressed in a suit that appeared to be tailored perfectly to him, upon feeling the cloth Jeff was unable to tell that there was anything special about it and the shirt and the jacket felt like two different materials.

    "How long can you maintain this appearance?"

    "Indefinitely, assuming you eat or drink every few days to provide me sustenance."

    "Okay, then lets make it harder. The suit looks great but I don't seem to fit it very well, could you make me look and sound 25ish and maintain the suit."

    "Of course!" Great seemed excited by the challenge and Jeff felt and watched as his jaw muscles expanded and reformed to give the appearance of adulthood. When he smiled there were a few small wrinkles under his eyes and when he raised his eyebrows in surprise there were wrinkles on his forehead.

    "I like it but still look like a kid who maybe spent too many summer days in the sun which gave him wrinkles. Add stubble then tell me how long can you maintain it?" Jeff asked aloud, not yet used to the telepathy, his rich, adult, baritone voice resonated out like it was always there. Small hairs started to poke out from nowhere and soon looked like a natural growth of a few days.

    "I can still maintain this indefinitely with you consuming nutrition every day and a half. It may be prudent to tell you that since my first attempt, I have learned how to access the nutritional stores in your body and bloodstream. Everything will depend on how often and how much you replenish yourself."

    "Oh, two last requests then. First, make me look buff and tell me how often I'd need to eat."

    The suit changed only slightly by pushing out at the chest. "Eat till you are full daily"

    Jeff rubbed his face admiring his growth, well, not his, but still. "Great, could you make this real?"

    "I can mimic natural facial hair growth if that is what you are asking."

    "No, I must be close to growing facial hair. Could you please stimulate my hair follicles to grow a beard like this by themselves?"

    Jeff looked at the whole picture he had created, he looked like a short, young, business man. He could be a lawyer, or a car salesman but the point was he no longer could pass for a 13 year old.

    "Could you remember this as my 'business look'?"

In less than a minute he had reprogrammed his parents to be open and accepting of anything that he says, does, or looks like, as though it happens all the time and is completely normal. The only flaw Jeff could see with this is if they didn't realize it was him to begin with then they may not be able to accept it as normal, but he didn't have time to account for everything, it's dinner time, he could tweak them afterwards.
