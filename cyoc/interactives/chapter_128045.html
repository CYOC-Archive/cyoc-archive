    The curse goes a bit haywire

    added by higeman 5 years ago A BM O Anthro Reality alteration

    Feeling the slightly heavier sides of her head, she whimpers in despair at the sudden feeling of her ears. Getting out of bed and walking over to the mirror, she groans sadly as her eyes made contact with reflection of her wide ears.

    "I have got to find that little jerk before this gets any worse!" she says to herself in frustration, her ears flicking in response to her emotion which only angered her further.

    Going over to her dresser, she halfheartedly hoped to find any form of upper clothing only to find her bras and skirts, which now all had fuzzy cow patterns on them. Seeing no other choice, she put them on, still feeling a bit awkward with the draft in between her legs. She didn't even get a chance to see her bed was now made of straw, albeit still with pillows and sheets.

    Stepping down the stairs, she began to dread her personal embarrassment she was going to have when her mom saw her changes.
    'She'll probably be treating me like a calf she raised as a farm hand would...' Jennifer thought to herself as she reached the bottom of the stairs, her feet feeling a bit stiff that morning.

    Turning the corner into the kitchen she's expecting it to be bad... only for her to find the worst.
    There her mother was, but she wasn't fully clothed. Instead, she was wearing a cow spotted skirt and bra just like Jennifer's, with a cow tail swinging happily against the skirt and her wide cow ears swiveling in her daughter's direction as she turned to greet her with a long moo. Without thinking, Jennifer moo'd in response before quickly stopping herself like before on the bus.

    "There's my little heifer! How did you sleep? You look a little restless." her mother asks, her tail wagging in concern.

    "I-" Jennifer started, wanting to explain the whole situation. But she knew it would be pointless with the curse in effect. Speaking of which, what had happened!? It looked like the curse began turning her mother into a cow as well! Wasn't the curse only supposed to affect the people in their school? The curse must be going out of control with its reality changes.
    "I- It's nothing, mom. I just slept on my tail wrong." she says, wincing at how easily she said like it was a normal thing.

    "Alright, if that's what you say dear. Anyways, you're just in time to have some breakfast with us before you head to school!" her mother said contentedly, motioning towards the stove. Martha was preparing breakfast as usual, but instead of her usual maid outfit, she was only wearing the apron of it, which of course had the same spotted cow design, and nothing else to cover her backside and her tail. Turning around with bovine ears flicking, she greeted Jennifer with a morning moo before bringing over three large bowls of oatmeal to which they started digging into. After the bowls were emptied, they all let out a content burp in satisfaction. Jennifer could only sit there, having it occur to her that they didn't follow their usual table mannerisms. ...Or used utensils for that matter.

    *HONK HONK!*

    "Sounds like the bus is here! I'll see you after school, mom!" Jennifer said as she rushed out the door, her feet slapping the sidewalk no longer feeling awkward much to her dread. "Be sure to have a good time my little heifer!" her mom responded, causing Jennifer to blush out of embarrassment and despair from the curse.

    Getting on the bus, she sees that the bus driver had started to show obvious changes, as her normal attire was now reduced to the same skirt and bra, her ears flicking idly as her tail swayed through the new opening that opened up behind her seat. As expected, the other students had experienced the same transformation on their ears, but thankfully it seemed to stop there. Sitting down with Denise was a mistake on her part as she let out another moo, causing the same chain reaction as it did yesterday, only now Jennifer moo'd for a bit longer before catching herself.

    As the bus drove on to school, Jennifer noticed in horror as the whole neighborhood was starting to change in the same way, even stopping next to a police officer and her cow spotted patrol car. Even the crossing guard had changed, his uniform being replaced with only cow spotted boxers and a cow tail and ears attached to him. She could have sworn she saw horns poking from his hair, but the bus moved on before she got a closer look.

    Eventually, the bus arrived on school grounds to which Jennifer discovered...
