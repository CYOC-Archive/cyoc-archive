    Levi Takes The Keys

    After Levi and Lincoln have left, you let out a sigh and look down at the keys, ‘why,’ you ask yourself, ‘why did I have to find these cursed keys?’ You scowl at the keys in you hand, you then put the keys into your pocket and decide to take a little nap. ‘I hope the “dogs” and “squirrels” have returned by the time I wake up,’ you think to yourself, you walk over to the couch and lie down, covering yourself with a blanket, it does not take long before you drift off.

    Levi and Lincoln are in their room, Lincoln is playing a game on the computer, while Levi is sitting on his bed, reading a comic. ‘I wish I had those amazing keys,’ Levi thinks to himself, ‘yes, they messed up Darren’s life, but I think they are still awesome. If I’d had them, I could do so much great things.’ The 10-year-old boy looks over at his brother, a grin appearing on his face, ‘if I’d have the keys, I would be the leader of the family,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I got to get those keys, I hope Darren has lied down, he looked tired, that would be my chance to take those keys.’ Levi gets up from the bed, “Lincoln, I need the boy’s room,” he says, walking towards the door, “I’ll be right back.”

    “Yeah, sure,” Lincoln says, not really paying attention, “whatever”.

    Levi walks out of the room and slowly and quietly walks down the stairs, he finds you lying on the couch and smiles when he hears your snoring. ‘I bet he put the keys in his pocket,’ the 10-year-old thinks to himself, he then quietly walks up to the couch and looks down at you. Levi takes a hold of the blanket, ‘I hope he is a deep sleeper,’ he thinks to himself and slowly starts to pull the blanket off of you.

    You don’t notice a thing, as Levi pulls the blanket off of you, you are lying on your side and once Levi has pulled the blanket down to your knees, he notices a bulge in your pocket.

    Grinning to himself, Levi uses his pick-pocketing skills to carefully take the keys out of your pocket. Levi smirks when the keys lie in his palm, he then covers you back up and tip toes away, ‘step one is complete,’ he thinks to himself, as he walks back up the stairs to his and Lincoln’s room, ‘step two: taking care of Lincoln!’

    “I’m back, bro,” Levi says, sitting down on his bed and picking up the comic he was reading.

    “Okay,” Lincoln simply says, still playing the game on the console.

    Levi looks over at Lincoln, thinking about what to do, ‘I could turn him into some bug and trap him in a bug box,’ he thinks to himself, ‘or I could change him into some clothing, I could do with a cool outfit. Or I could make him think he is a dog, hehe, that would be hilarious! Or maybe I could transform him into a little bronze statue of a naked boy and sell him on Ebay!’

    Levi licks his lips, overwhelmed of his imaginations what he could do to his brother, the 10-year-old is also already planning what to do with you. The young boy in truth hates his parents and finds his twin brother really annoying, in his mind, you are the most coolest person, he’d rather have you be a part of his life than having to be stuck in this hell.

    The 10-year-old smirks, you already have made the first step into creating Levi’s new and much better life, by transforming his tyrant parents into dumb squirrels, now it is up to Levi to continue! Once he had taken care of Lincoln, Levi plans to use the keys on you, transforming you back to normal. He then would use the keys to make himself look similar to you, becoming your little brother. Levi would also alter the reality, to make people think that the reason he is living with you is because your mother had an affair with a man, who, once finding out that your mother was pregnant with him, just disappeared. Your mother decided it would be best for you to take care of Levi, since you had more cash than she had. Levi smirks, he’d use the keys to make you rich, the plan was fool proof and Levi feels himself become excited and giddy, ‘I can’t wait to start my new and better life,’ he thinks to himself.
