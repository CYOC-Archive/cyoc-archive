    The Magic Tank Top of Muscle and Maturity

    “Well, look over here. I have something nice and light and wonderfully comfortable for you, which you can wear all summer long and even when you work out in the basement or in any gym, for that matter!” she says as she walks up to a rack and pulls a neat tank top with white and grey camouflage colors. The tank top has the words ‘Muscle Titan’ printed on the front. “Isn’t that sporty?”

    “Oh, that looks so COOL!” you grin widely as you grab the hanger than the tank top hangs from. “I like the colors!”

    “Go and try it on, uhh… What’s your name again?” the lady asks.

    “Tyler. My name’s Tyler!”

    “Well, this tank top has you written all over it. Go try it on, in the dressing room over there!” she insists, as she pushes you towards a door, leading to a nice, roomy changing room with a bench and a large mirror.

    You step into the room, and you close the door behind you. You remove your sweaty t-shirt, and you slip on the colorful, camouflage tank top. Right away, your body is overcome with a weird tingling followed by a sensation of excitement and a wave of confidence as you stare at your own reflection in the mirror. Your arms are fully exposed, causing you to flex and show off. There isn’t much muscle there, but for some reason, the tank top makes you feel stronger than before. You flex again, and you could have sworn your arms were starting to form a small bulge!

    You open the door, and you see the redhead patiently standing there, waiting for you.

    “I like it. It fits me perfectly!”

    “Good! It’s a special day and you will grow into it as you work out!” she says as she leads you to the cash register. “That will be one Dollar, Tyler!”

    “Just a dollar for a tank top? Wow!” you smile as you hand her a one-Dollar bill.

    “You can keep it on, young man. The tank top will help your physique to grow bigger, stronger, and more mature over time. Once you outgrow it, you can pass it on to someone else who may need it!” she says, as she hands you the receipt.

    “Thanks!” you say, as you walk out proudly while wearing your cool, new, sporty tank top!
