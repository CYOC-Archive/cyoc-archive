    They got to the park too late in the day for most things, but the hotel pool is still open. When they get up to their room, Leah eagerly opens her suitcase to put on her bikini. But she doesn't find it. Instead, she sees a toddler's one-piece. Complete with room for a swim diaper and swim diaper cover. Oh, and there's a swim diaper cover as well. Leah slams her suitcase closed and glares at Tina. Tina merely shrugs.

    "Listen, Leah, you're posing as a toddler this entire time. Swim time included!" Tina pulls out a package of swim diapers from the diaper bag she packed for Leah. "But don't worry, I came prepared for plenty of swimming!"

    Tina removes Leah's diaper, cleans her up, and then puts a swim diaper on her. Leah insists on dressing herself the rest of the way. When she finishes, she looks in the mirror. All she sees is a toddler girl with an obvious diaper bulge.

    "Remember, Leah, you don't actually have to use the swim diaper," Tina says as they head downstairs to the pool. "It's only for show. If you need to potty, just get my attention or your dad's attention. We'll help you out." Leah decides to ignore Tina using childish language in reference to her. She needs to get used to it for this trip. And hopefully only for this trip.

    They get to the pool. Tina is attracting attention immediately. Her figure is stunning and she's wearing a bikini, after all. Tina and Leah's dad set stuff up in a corner of the pool area.

    "Go ahead and play, Leah," her dad says to her. "We'll catch up." Leah nods. Once again, the diaper she's wearing forces her to toddle instead of walk normally, but she makes her way to the pool. As she stands by the stairs leading into the pool, she realizes that there's a huge problem.
