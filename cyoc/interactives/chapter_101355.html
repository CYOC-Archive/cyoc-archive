    "Usually, yeah. But it turns out Eric has a daytime date with Julie," Nate informed Ben. "Which works out for them since we have to be at my dad's induction ceremony into the Skulk tomorrow night."

    The three of them mixed and mingled with all the other werewolves present, but not too many of those attending had already assumed their furry forms yet.
    The one thought on Ben Twist's mind was getting to his girlfriend as soon as he could, when he heard a very familiar female voice behind him seductively say, "Hello, my werewolf."
    "KENYA!" Ben shouted, as he turned and hugged the lady. "Not that I ain't very happy to see you, but what are you doing here?"
    "A werewolf friend from the college dropped me off, although he's not a member of your pack," she began. "Besides using this as part of my goal to start connecting myself more with the community, since this is important to you and you are important to me..."
    Ben smiled before kissing her. "There are a couple of people I'd like you to meet if you don't mind."
    "It will be my pleasure," said Kenya, taking his hand in hers.

    Soon he found them and introduced Kenya to Nate and Alex.
    "Gentlemen," said Kenya, shaking hands first with Ben's friend and next door neighbor, before greeting Nate's boyfriend.
    The couple couldn't help staring at Ben's lady. She was easily a couple of inches taller than him in her human form, with rich mocha colored skin and black hair that cascaded above her shoulders. Yet the golden eyes of a werepanthress, along with the toned athletic body and her very good looks combined to make Kenya a truly exotic beauty.
    "You both go with Benjamin to the local high school?" she asked.
    "Yes," said Nate, before briefly explaining what few classes the three of them shared along with eating together during the Junior class lunch period.
    Kenya started telling the boys a little bit about herself when Austin's son AJ, (Austin Perry Junior, technically) and beta pack leader called the meeting to order, before he officially introduced and welcomed Tad Decker into their wolf pack.
    Then, with the meeting officially over after the group howl, AJ announced it was time for the pack orgy!

    As everyone who had not already done so started stripping off their human clothes and wolving out, Ben turned to Kenya and said, "What would you like to do now?"
    "You do not want to stay with your friends and fellow pack members?" asked Kenya, as she spotted the one called Tad Decker going off with a she-wolf who had all white fur.
    "Once upon a time, maybe," admitted Ben, "but orgies were already losing their appeal for me before I met you. Yet, if you want to stay?"
    "No. There is only one werewolf in my life," said Kenya with a smile, while wrapping her arm around him. "Besides, I feel that love should be something truly special between two people who care about each other," she added, as she started to lead him away from the party. "We still have a couple of hours left. Why don't you show me the town from your perspective so I get to know Moon Lake better, then you can take me home before my 10 pm curfew, since I still have college tomorrow."
    "It would be my pleasure," Ben said with a smile before kissing her on the cheek. Then he led them to his car.
