    Jacki pulled the camera from he dog's mouth and quickly ran over to grab a towel. As she cleaned it, she began to wonder where on earth she got it from. From her memory, she didn't remember purchasing a camera quite like this. Once it was cleaned up, Jacki took another look at it. It seemed like an old polaroid, but with a bunch of strange markings all over it. She decided to see how a picture would look with it so she looked for a target.

    She saw Laura reading a newspaper but she knew Laura hated to get her picture taken while she was pregnant. She turned to see her baby girl bouncing up and down. She seemed almost like she wanted a picture, but Jacki worried the flash might startle her. Then she saw her dog, but she was still a little pissed at him so she definitely didn't want to take his picture. She then realized she could do what a lot of kids like to do and take a "selfie."

    Emily watched in horror as Jacki turned the camera around and readied the camera. Emily tried to tell her to stop but she could only babble in her highchair as the camera flashed. Emily felt a bit of vertigo as reality around her changed. When she was able to come to, she found she was no longer in the highchair, but was rather on the floor.

    Emily looked down to see that she was on a blanket surrounded by children's toys. Emily looked down and found she was still a baby, but was now only wearing a diaper. She looked around and found the house was now a mess. It looked like it hadn't been cleaned in a while as it was very cluttered with all types of baby related stuff and some garbage. Emily suddenly heard a noise behind her.

    She turned to see a little girl who was probably the same age as she now was. She was wearing a pair of overalls with a diaper clearly on underneath but again, no shirt. She had curly blonde hair and was sucking on her toe. Emily quickly pieced it together that it was probably Jacki after the camera altered her. Emily then remembered she still needed to get to the camera so she began to crawl. She barely got far before Laura walked in, still pregnant as ever, but only now she looked extremely frazzled. She had no makeup on, her hair was a mess, and a stain or two was on her clothes.

    "No! No no." Laura said as she quickly picked Emily up and moved her back on the blanket. "Mommy needs you to stay on the blanket ok Jacki? Or wait, Emily? Shoot who did I give the pants to today again? Oh it doesn't matter. I need to get this place cleaned up before that asshole shows up." She said panicked as she raced into the other room.

    Emily quickly pieced together how reality changed. Since Laura was the only one in the room, she became the defect mother of both Jacki and herself. Because of this and no-one with Laura to help her, she was quickly losing control of her life. She clearly never cleaned and likely didn't even have a dog anymore. Her life was such a mess that child protective services were coming! Emily began to panic. If she couldn't get the camera before then, she might never change back!

    As Laura began to vacuum, Emily used this as an opportunity to secretly search for the camera. Even if she found it, it wasn't like she could lift or aim it. She would have to find it and then immediately use it on herself. She just had to hope for the best. That's when...
