    The day was coming to it's end and this time Mary's door didn't open at all for the usual flock of men. Sylvia kept Lukas busy as it seemed that their intellectual abilities were switching. Jennifer saw how Lukas was able to hold her instinctual habits while Jennifer still wasn't able to. Lukas's mannerism didn't look proper for a four year old as at some point it seemed that Lukas was educating her mother how to speak properly. Jennifer in other hand was struggling with her own problems. The belly felt like a cocoon that was growing beyond what was possible. The imp that was poking at Jennifer's cheek had also grown into 2-feet humanoid being. It still being smaller than Jennifer while it remsembled an adult human that looked like mix between her mother and Jennifer.

    Somehow Lukas was able to see the little imp as well as it was interested on it's antics. Sylvia was too dumb to even notice that her child and Jennifer's brother were able to see something that wasn't even there. Maybe Sylvia had lost her ability to imagine things. Maybe the intellect would tank at some point but that made a weird question pop in Jennifer's head. How old was she anyway. In the dream it talked about a second pregnancy and now one of the two things had been reflecting in this current reality. What if she a misca- UGH! Jennifer's brain begun to heat up as she couldn't finish her thought. The imp tried it's best to entertain Lukas while Jennifer suffered from a partial amnesia. It was like she couldn't think anything about the 'first pregnancy'. Thing were quite strange. Jennifer looked at the imp which gave a loving smiling back at her. Jennifer felt a tear pushing through her eye as she had no idea why she started to cry in first place.

    Before the night hit. Out of nowhere Sylvia came to give a big hug to Jennifer and told her, "Be good little me." Then went back to the second room which belonged to her and left Lukas to sleep at the bathroom. This left Jennifer and the little imp in to the same room. The thing kept rubbing Jennifer belly and slowly falling asleep on top of it. Jennifer couldn't help but close her own eyes as well. The day had been awful and she hoped that the train ride would end at some point. Sadly, Jennifer didn't know the destination as the train kept going forward.
