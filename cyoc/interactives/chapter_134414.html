    Martha didn't know what was going to happen, but a million dollars was a lot of money, and she wasn't going to waste an opportunity like this. The scientist turned, walked to a separate room where the test tubes of lotion are stored, and closed the door behind her. There were walls of test tubes on every side, filled to the brim of the same lotion, with slightly different tints of colours. The only main difference between the wall of lotion-filled test tubes and the lotion just given to Martha was that the other test tubes were labelled with names. The names of animals. Several different types of animals. With names of people bracketed next to the animal name.

    Martha wasn't the first one.
    She wasn't the first one to test the lotion.
    All the scientist needed to know...

    ...was what animal is Martha was going to change into...
