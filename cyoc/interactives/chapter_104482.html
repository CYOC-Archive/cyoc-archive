    Just as Eric was slowly becoming resigned to his fate a pair of strong arms wrap around Julie and wrench her of Eric. Pulling both out of their respective dazes. Julie quickly looks at the person who dared spoil her revenge. ready to murder them. When she see's that it was the school principal, all the rage and anger within her is replaced with fear. Fear knowing she has been caught trying to change a normal against their will. With out saying a word the principal strong arms Julie to the office and gives her the most menacing death stare she has ever witnessed. Meanwhile a teacher who was with the Principal when they both heard Eric's outburst, Escorts Eric to the nurses office.

    As Melissa and Martin where continuing to have their augment over Martin's extra-marital proclivities, his office phone rings. Silently thanking the ancestors for the interruption he grabs it and answers it.
    "Mayor here."
    "Mr. Stovall, this is the principal at Moon lake High. I'm calling to inform you that your daughter was clearly caught on school grounds trying to violate the town's statue 1A law."

    Martin face goes ash as he falls in his office chair over the news. Melissa, who overheard what the principal said on the phone also reacts in shock.

    "I'm sorry to call your direct line." Continued the principal. " I tried calling your wife but got no response from her work number or your home number."
    "Tha... that's fine." Martin interrupts. My w...wife is here with me. We will both be over at the school in ten minutes to discuss this in person. Has the victim's family been notified?"
    "I have yet to contact the police, or the Andersons, sir. But I intend to. I will see you and your wife at the school shortly Mayor." The Principal shortly hangs up afterwards.

    *****

    The principal hangs up as Julie is in his office. She heard what her father said on the other end. Unable to stop the principal, she watch in horror as the Principal rings up the Anderson's to inform them what has just transpire. After a brief exchange the principal looks at Julie and then promptly calls the Police chief.

    Chief Cooper's phone rings "Chief Cooper speaking."
    "Good afternoon Chief. this is Principal Johnson over at Moon Lake High."
    "Oh. Hello Johnson" Chief responds. "How are you doing?
    "Sadly, Chief I did not call for small talk. I have here in my office Julie Stovall. I caught her just as she was about to commit a sexual act on school grounds." The Principal , remarks coldly.
    "Oh My. Has Martin been informed?"
    "Yes Chief. But that is only have the issue." The Principal continues. "The other have is, Julie was committing said sexual act with a non were. If I did not stop her, she would of violated the town's statue 1A law."

    Chief Cooper is floored. First the drama with the rogue were vixen. The Lawson's and the nosy FBI agent. Now the Mayor's daughter. can't I catch a break between crises for once? Quietly thinks to himself.

    "I'll send down an officer to take the required statements." The chief responds exasperated. "Has the victim stated they want to press charges?"
    "That's up to the Anderson's Chief Cooper. Since their son Eric was the victim I felt it was only fair to inform them what has just transpired. Also, I inform the mayor. Being his daughter the aggressor in this event. So be fore you send down the Officer to take witness statements, you might also want to come down as well."

    "Eric Anderson was the victim?" The Chief respond. "First the rouge on William. Now this! Great. I'll be down as well."
    The chief hangs up and promptly grabs the aspirin from the top drawer in his desk.
    "Why did I have to get out of bed today?" The Chief pops a couple of Aspirin just as he calls for a deputy.
