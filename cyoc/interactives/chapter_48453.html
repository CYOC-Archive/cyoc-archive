    Princess Amelia: Kidnapped!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 13 years ago O

    Andy looked in the mirror, spinning around a bit, examining himself. "Well, I think I'll go for a walk.", said Amelia. She grabbed her parasol and left the room. The maid caught her in the hallway. "Milady! Where are you going?" "Um... I wanted to walk around town. Is that a problem?" "We can't have you walking around without an escort! Here, guard!" A guard wearing a suit of armor ran over, his armor clanking. "Her Majesty wants to take a walk. You will escort her." "Hello, your majesty! I am Sir Barton. I will guard you with my life!", said the guard, making exaggerated gestures. Andy didn't know whether to feel flattered or embarrassed.

    In town, the citizens of Berimont crowded around Amelia. "Oh, wow, it's the princess!" "She's so pretty!" "How noble of Her Majesty to grace us with her visage!" Sir Barton had trouble keeping them all back. "Stand back! The princess will not be bothered!" Andy blushed a bit, causing Amelia to do the same. Was he really stuck here? Just in this town? Why couldn't he go somewhere else?

    Suddenly, Amelia was grabbed and pulled into an alley. "Hello, your Majesty.", said the cloaked figure who grabbed her. Amelia couldn't tell who it was, besides the fact that the person was female. "Who are you?", said Amelia. "Why did you do this?" "I am simply a friend, who wishes to see you freed from the shackles of this city." Amelia's face changed to one of interest. "Ah. I see you know what I mean. I can take you away from this place, Amelia. I can free you." Andy didn't want to be stuck here, so naturally, he agreed. "Very well. Come with me." A portal appeared, and the cloaked figure walked through it. Amelia stood, hesitant to enter. "Amelia! Princess! Where are you?!" Barton's yelling seemed to draw closer. She ran into the portal.

    On the other side, Amelia landed on the ground. Looking around, she seemed to be in a dungeon. "What?! No way!" She ran to the door of the cell she was in. "Hey! Lady, where are you?" "Hello, my child." The cloaked figure stood outside her cell. "Where am I? What's going on?!" The figure laughed and removed the hood of her cloak, revealing the face of a woman, most likely in her 20s. "I am the sorceress Vanessa. I know exactly who you are." "What is this? What's going on? Answer me!" "I need a subject for my experiments. A royal subject would be exquisite." "Experiments?" Andy really didn't like the sound of that. Well, that, and the fact that he's in an even smaller cage than before. "Garth, come here." A very large man walked over. He appeared to have the face of a bull, but the body of a muscular man. "Grab her." The cell door swung open, and Garth leapt onto Amelia, grabbing her weak arms with his massive hands. "Ahh! Let me go!" Garth began following Vanessa up a staircase.

    At the top of the stairs, they reached a room that looked like something from a mad scientist movie. Bookshelves lined the walls, and tables with vials and test tubes were everywhere. Garth dragged Amelia to a stone slab on the wall, chaining shackles to her wrists. "Very good, Garth. Now, let's begin the experimentation..."
