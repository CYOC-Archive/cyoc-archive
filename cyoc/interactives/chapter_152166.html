    A Light in the Darkness

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 5 years ago BM S O

    "Hi!" A chipper voice spoke from a young girl, glowing a pretty shade of blue. Her ears were long and pointed, her eyes framed in blue eyeshadow, were like an anime character's, and long antennae grew from her forehead; but the most striking part about her was the huge set of butterfly wings rising from her back, still fluttering and holding her aloft in the air despite being the same size as Lisa; but a bit thinner and... oddly developed for a 9-year old; and her flowery mini-skirt and tank-top left little to the imagination.

    "Can I come in?" She continued.

    "S-sure?" Lisa hesitantly, and unsurely answered, and the fairy landed, her wings falling down and were now fluttering like a cape as she walked past the girl. at rest, she could see the beautiful and elegant patters on the wings. Lisa had to admit her envy to herself, she always did love butterflies, and she often wished to become a fairy with butterfly wings, herself.

    "Huh, I was expecting a room a little... smaller..." The fairy commented. "Oh, what am I saying... humans grow much bigger and much slower than fairies... comparatively speaking. "I mean, what are you; eight, nine? A fairy would be an adult like me by now!"

    "Excuse me, but... who are you!?" Lisa shouted, though, trying to be respectful, and quiet as she could.

    "Oh, of course, how rude of me!" She said with a curtsy of her tiny flower skirt. "My name is Bellebleu; and I've come to grant your wish!"

    "My wish!?"

    "To become a fairy; of course!" Lisa's eyes grew wide at Bellbleu's announcement.
