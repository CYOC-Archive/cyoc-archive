    Jeff thought of which option he could pick for the Time Travel package on the Chronivac. The age time travel seems like a fun experience and the reality time travel is really interesting thing to experience. But the one thing he was really interested in is the history lesson package as he enjoyed history in high school. Yet even if he enjoyed it, he always felt that it wasn’t as exciting as before. So maybe this type of time travel could be really fun. After all, it did say that he would be a ghost in a specific time period, so that he won’t get involved and affect his future. So clicking on it, there was a new page that popped up and said…

    ‘Welcome to the History Lesson page of the Time Travel package. This is where you can go not only back in time, but also forward to the future as well. The rules of this page are simple. You can pick a specific time line that you wish to visit, whether it is writing a specific even of history or by typing a certain year in time (be that past or future). Say, for example, you want to go to the American Revolution; you simply type the name or the specific year of the event. Then you will be whisked away to that time line.

    Once you type down the event or year, you can choose the duration of time travel. It can either be in a flash, or set up a specific number and timeline so you can watch the world around you would go backwards. Such as one second would be a minute, hour, day, week, month, or year into the past or future. There is also a section where you can go back to your own time after a certain amount of time. But there is an option where you can remove that part and remain in the timeline you have chosen for life.

    Now as far as the whole affecting the future when tampering with the past, there is way to prevent it. See, once you choose the specific time in the past and future, the Chronivac can turn you into a ghost so you can witness the event in history without tampering a specific part of it. Once you become a ghost, you won’t age, you won’t go hungry, nor you won’t feel tired. You will remain what you are when you choose this program and will be completely immortal in spiritual form. Course there are times when users of this program used their ghostly forms to possess people in the past just to get a really good experience. But we suggest you won’t do that, as possessing important people would result in the tampering of the past as well.

    That’s all there is to it. Hope you enjoy this package and the Chronivac. Enjoy your going through time and have a nice day.’

    As you read through it, he was very interested in this program. Not only that Jeff can go back to the past, but also the future? That could be very enjoyable. And being a ghost in the past, that too will be fun to experience. Of course, he won’t possess people, as that will cause some problems in his timeline. But at least the program gave him a little warning. Once you were done reading, you wondered which event in time you like to visit.
