    Trent, a 15 year old boy, went inside the Store of Needs with his "friend" Alice, his classmate/childhood "friend"
    Trent sighed looking through the store and asked "So what exactly do I need to get so I can get this punishment over with?"
    Alice grinned and said "Ah don't be like that, you lost the bet and now you just gotta get some transformation item and wear it at school for a week."
    Trent looked around and wondered out loud "Isn't some magic item supposed to reveal itself to me or something?"
    A wandering magician/store employee found the two and said "If you don't really care about what you get there's a special giant discount capsule machine. We got a lot of magic items that act a little weird so we put them in the capsule machine. They are still safe to use, but we just don't feel we can put them out on the shelves. You'll still get stuff you need so you don't have to worry about getting something useless. Just go to the back right of the store." The wandering magician than went off to help another customer.
    Alice said "Well they show up just when you need them... Very useful those people. So wanna try that Capsule machine?"
    Trent sighed and said "Sure why not, I was just going to stick my hand in some discount bin and pull something out... Well let's go to that thing then." A quick walk latter they approached a giant coin operated glass cube filled with a bunch of spheres with magic items inside. Some were the size of a sticker, others the size of a a person, but somehow only one would ever come out.
    Alice looked at the machine and read the instructions "Put in 75 cents... turn the crank... and get the item... you must use the item as soon as possible, magic will be used to help that if needed." Alice pulled out 75 cents and gave it to Trent. "As for our bet I'll pay for the magic item... although you should be the one cranking the machine."
    Trent took the 75 cents and went to the machine and put in the small payment and started cranking the handle. Once the machine was given the money it whirred to life flashing all sorts of colors and then put out a magic item, but what exactly did he get?
