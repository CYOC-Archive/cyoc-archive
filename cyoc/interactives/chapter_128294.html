    REALLY Tiny Kelly, Megan's Town Planning

    Previous chapter

    added by A Non-Existent User 5 years ago S


    Kelly took aim and fired on Megan. Jessica was caught in the blast. Megan shrunk down to normal height and six-inch tall Jessica was still sitting in her lap.

    "Aww man, Kelly. Why did you have to ruin all of my fun!?" Megan shouted.

    Jessica climbed out of her friend's lap and ran over to Kelly.

    "Make me big again!" she asked.

    Kelly enlarged Jessica back to her normal height. Megan then came over.

    "Kelly, make me big again! I want to be giant!" Megan said, stamping her foot.

    "Shut up, twerp. You'll hurt yourself," mocked Kelly.

    "Gah!"

    Megan screamed as she tackled her sister. The blow activated the Reducer and Kelly was caught in the blast. She slowly shrank and stopped at 3 and 3 quarter inches tall.

    "Megan! Now look what you did!" Kelly yelled up at her sister.

    Megan and Jessica laughed hysterically at Kelly's squeaky voice. Megan picked Kelly up and began to play with her tiny body.

    "Hey! Stop it, Megan!" Kelly ordered.

    "Shut up, twerp!" Megan laughed at her newfound sense of power. "Well, I may not be a giant, but at least I can still boss around my new 'little' sister!"

    Jessica and Megan played around with Kelly's tiny body.

    "Hey Megan. Let's see if she can live in the dollhouse!" said Jessica.

    "Yeah!"

    They headed upstairs into Jessica’s room and broke out her toy box, putting Kelly next to it. Jessica shuffled through her toys, throwing things behind her back in the process. A 12 inch tall 'Optimus Prime' toy landed 1 inch away from Kelly.

    "AGH!" screamed Kelly as she dodged the robot. "Watch it you little freaks!"

    "Little?" Megan laughed.

    Kelly realized the irony and simply sat down, accepting her new size.

    "Oh my god! Megan look!" Jessica said holding up a Tinkerbell outfit made for a doll.

    "Ha! Kelly would look so cute in that!" Megan replied joyfully.

    "Hey! You can shrink me, you can humiliate me, but you CANNOT make me unfashionable!" screamed Kelly.

    Megan didn't acknowledge her and began to remove her clothes.

    "Get off!" Kelly shouted, trying to ward off her sister.

    "Oh my god, she's so cute!" replied Jessica as she finished dressing Kelly in the Tinkerbell outfit.

    Kelly was a bit embarrassed by her clothes.

    "Kelly, no need to try to hide," Megan said as she picked her sister up.

    "Megan, please! Stop this and make me big again!" pleaded Kelly.

    "Hmm...okay, Sis. Just stand over here," Megan said.

    Kelly quickly did so. Megan aimed the device and the beam enveloped Kelly.

    "Thank you, Megan," Kelly said with a smile. Just then Kelly began to notice that she was actually getting smaller. "Hey! What are you doing!?"

    "I'm making you smaller! You will be no bigger than an ant! And I will then rule over you!" shouted Megan.

    "Megan, you sure about this? I mean, I can hardly see her," asked Jessica.

    "She had it coming, always picking on me. Now I am in charge," responded Megan.

    Kelly inspected her new surroundings.

    "Oh man, this is bad."

    She heard a booming voice miles above her. She looked up and was awestruck by what she saw: two giant faces beaming down at her tiny body.

    "AGH!!!" she screamed at the top of her lungs.

    Megan and Jessica barely heard a squeak and peered down at the tiny teen.

    "Come here, tiny," said Megan as she picked Kelly up by the arm with her index finger and thumb.

    Kelly couldn't manage to say anything as she was brought up to her sister's gigantic eye.

    "I wonder if we can get more of these? We can create our own little city!" said Jessica joyfully.

    "That would be interesting..." Megan said. "Do you have anything we can keep tiny here in?"

    "Hmm...." Jessica responded as she tried to find something. She returned with the top of a water bottle. "Put her in here. We can cover it up with some plastic."

    Megan dropped Kelly in and they covered the container.

    "Oh man..." Kelly said as she traveled inside her tiny new home.

    "Where to?" Jessica asked.

    "We'll drop by my sister's high school. Maybe we can grab a few of her friends," Megan responds. "A science experiment, if you will."

    "Oh I will," responded Jessica.
