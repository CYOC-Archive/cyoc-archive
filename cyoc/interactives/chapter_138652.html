    Ty was walking down the hall to his room when he heard something. He thought he had heard a faint hello coming from his friend Sammy's room. "That's weird." He though. "Usually she's left for work by now." Ty figured she might be stuck or something so he grabbed a spare key his friend gave him from his room and went back.

    Ty unlocked the door quietly and turned to see Sammy saying something about hell. He shut the door and walked over to greet her. Before he got there he said "Oh sorry Sammy. I thought you needed help or something!"

    Sam jumped as he didn't expect one of the few people he liked on this floor, Ty, to just walk in. Ty and Sammy were much closer than Ty and Sam had ever been so this surprised her a crap ton. As she screamed, she had accidentally clicked the camera option, now catching Ty in the pic.

    Sam felt dizzy again as the world spun. She started to fall when she felt Ty catch her.

    "Woah babe, are you ok?" Ty said in a heartfelt tone.

    Sam looked up and saw Ty had changed. He looked a lot tanner than before. His hair was darker, and on top of that he looked a few years older. "Wait." She thought. "Did Ty just call me babe?"

    She looked down and saw her unkept hair was now fully styled and straightened. She was also wearing a cute skirt with a matching top. Her nails were painted pink and she felt like she had recently taken a trip to the salon. She also realized that she was wearing a freaking wedding ring!

    She checked and Ty was as well. Ty helped Sam up as he jokingly said "What's the matter, you forget how to walk or something?" Sam sarcastically laughed as she looked around her room. Not only was it clean, but it was somehow girlier than before. She saw another more noticeable change, which was an added picture frame. In it was Ty in a tuxedo, kissing the now female Sam wearing a wedding dress. Sam couldn't believe it.

    "Okay babe you're starting to worry me a little. Maybe I should take you to the doctor?" He asked.

    "No! No uh, babe, that's fine. I'm fine. I'll be late for work at Wendy's anyway." She said.

    Ty just looked concerned. "Babe stop messing with me. You quit that job like a year ago remember? My job pays for both of us so there was no need?"

    Sam was blown away by this revelation but tried to play it off. "Yeah, I know Ty. I was joking?"

    TY looked more relieved. "Okay as long as you're good. I'm gonna go to work. Text me if anything like this happens again okay? Love you." Ty the kissed Sam on the lips and left the room. Before closing the door he blew a kiss. Sam played it off as he did so but the minute he left she started to quietly freak.

    "Oh my god I'm married to my neighbor." She said shocked. "I also don't need to work anymore." She said with a little more optimism. She sat down on the couch, instinctively closing her legs, and then said in an even happier tone. "I don't have to work anymore."

    As much as she was shocked by being a woman, the changes weren't too bad if she didn't need to go to Wendy's. She checked her phone and saw a number of chores and fun hobbies she apparently did to keep herself occupied. None were anywhere close to the level of annoying that was fast food. She looked up and knew what she was gonna do.
