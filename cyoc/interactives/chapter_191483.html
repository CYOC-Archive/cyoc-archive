But now, it seemed that the hair covering Liam's upper body had decreased in amount rather than increased. His jawline was clean of any stubble, and his hair was more sparse across his chest and abdomen.

    "Well, I can appreciate it if that's what you're asking," Max said, looking Liam over, "But what changed your mind on it?"

    "I looked into your room to check on you - and I saw how you were looking," Liam said with a grin, "So I figured - if one little drink got you looking this fucking good, how about two drinks for little old me?" Liam flexed his arm again and said, "Well, I bulked up like fuck, and now you've got a lot more of an impressive roommate."

    "What clothes are you gonna wear though? You DID bulk up a lot..." Max cupped his chin, realizing the fact that Liam probably hadn't thought this over. Another thing occurred to him, "Well, there's that, and how did this stuff affect you so fast? I thought it---"

    "Probably some variance with the substance," Liam shrugged, "I'm not looking this gift horse in the mouth."

    Though Max saw something seemed up with his buddy's physique, particularly in a few areas, he had no idea what that might be leading up to. So for now he just stated, "Just be careful with any more usage of this stuff. I mean, we don't know what in all it can really do."

    "We know it made you and me into fucking gods among men." Liam gloated - the feeling of being really, truly big for the first time in his life was almost...intoxicating. Especially when it came out of nowhere like this. If there was any chance of him losing this or being outshined, he wanted to make sure he maintained his edge. For right now he was bigger than Max in a number of places. And Liam liked that - quite a lot at that.

Liam smirked, "Should be fun to hit up the party circuit, maybe even dose while I'm on the floor." Max nodded along, he himself was eager to get back out there on the party circuit, and with his new body he knew he'd be a hit. He just worried that Liam was gonna push himself too far with these dosages...

But now that he was looking and sounding like this, it seemed Liam was interested in making up for lost time. Max didn't particularly like how Liam was bigger than him, but he didn't want to just fall into using this stuff like Liam did. He cupped his chin...
