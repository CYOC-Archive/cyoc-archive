    All You Can Shop Till You Drop

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LuisJM A month ago AR BM S

    After a Long bus ride, Abigail could see the mall and signaled the driver to stop. Once the driver reached the bus stop, she got out and waved farewell to the driver. Looking to the mall, she could tell that this was her favorite place to visit ever since she was young. She remembered that it use to be a small outdoor outlet with a few ma and pop stores. But now, it's a huge complex filled with high end stores, lot end stores, food court, restaurants, movie theater, and a multitude of attractions. It wasn't like the shopping district she once knew and love.

    Looking to the huge complex, Abigail said, "I must admit, of all my years, I am surprise how much this mall has changed. Once a small outdoor area with few stores, now it's a huge grand complex with so many shops, dining, and entertainment. Really missed what it once was in my days."

    She soon looked to the amulet and wondered what the man at the bus stop said. "Still, the thought of what the man said at the bus stop still haunts me. This amulet can make whatever wish I want? Sounds impossible. Still, it looks good on me. Maybe I could get something from the store that matches this. I don't have much time, might as well look myself good."

    So she entered the mall through the door and could see how big it was. Four floors, a lot of stores, and a variety of food and entertainment and you got a mega complex meant for everyone. Seeing it all, she knew that it wasn't the place she once knew. "Jeepers, this place is massive! You could get lost in a place like this."

    She looked to the directory and wondered where to go. "Now let's see... Which store should I go to first?"
