    The mother and daughter dragon entered the spa building which on the outside looked mostly like a cave opening, and on the inside... It mostly looked like a cave, but it was very roomy even for the car sized Suzan. The most decorated parts of the building were mostly the walls and ceilings.
    "Isn't it amazing what the beginnings of magic architecture can make?" Denora telepathied, and then realized her question became rhetorical because her daughter couldn't reply. At the front desk was a giant woman with bulky muscles who stood tall enough to stand at a similar height as Denora... and she also had only a single eye.
    "Denora! It's been awhile, and whose this little... ah, girl hard to tell for me and reptiles." The cyclops said in a deep voice. Suzan was a little unsure about the conversation since Denora was communicating directly into the Cyclops' brain. "Alright then! We'll start you two off with massages, then go for a nice lava bath, and we'll figure out what ya'll want from there."

    The walk to the massage tables reminded Suzan of walking with adults when she was still a little kid considering how big everything was. It made sense with how big mother was, but it didn't quite help calm Suzan's nerves. There seemed to be maybe one or two other customers, but on Suzan's mind was less the other dragons being pampered... but more a large rock golem who was even bigger than Denora standing by Suzan's massage table. The adult giant monsters seemed to think everything was fine, and that Lucy the rock golem was quite gentle. It took Suzan some time to get onto the massage table, and couldn't quite get out of the habit for curling on the table as she braced for impact. While her mother got what she was expected and allowed the Cyclops' hands do their magic Lucy had to kind of stretch and flatten Suzan out from the ball she was curled into. After being forced into the proper position Suzan felt the boulders that constituted Lucy's hands start pressing into her body slowly crushing her body. Good thing for her she was a dragon with a resilient body so relatively this was a nice massage rather than being crushed to death. The first minute Suzan was in shock from the thought of what the situation was, and then she started enjoying herself. Each inch and curve the boulders went around to massage out the stress allowing Suzan to get a better feel for her true body. The fear she had about being handled by a large rock monster vanished and the massage time went at what felt like the blink of an eye. From the skull, to the wings, all the way to the end of the nerves of her feet Suzan really felt her body. Feeling good Suzan just lay on the table for a bit after the massage then got up and hopped off the massage table.

    They were then escorted to the lava bath where there was already a red dragon in the pool about the size of a truck. Denora and the dragon looked at each other for a moment and they bowed to one another. Then the red dragon looked to Suzan and their thoughts entered her head in a somewhat gender ambiguous "voice". "Greetings, I was named Tiamat. It's nice to meet another true dragon." then there was a silence as Tiamat looked to Denora and they shared a small conversation. Tiamat looked back to Suzan, but no thoughts were exchanged. Suzan felt very awkward as she slowly inched her way into the lava bath. It felt very hot till she got used to the temperature. The physical experience was pleasant, but it was obvious that Denora and Tiamat were having some conversation... maybe it was about how she seemed to be a bit magically inept. Feeling a bit irritated Suzan used her transformation magic and her scaly skin became fleshy and squishy. Still submerged in lava her dragon constitution allowed her to not burn to death. Her form was that of her human, but scaled up to the size of her dragon form.
    "Alright! I'm getting a little irritated through this leaving me out of any conversation thing! Am I some disappointment, because I can't fly or use telepathy!?!" Suzan spouted out.
    Denora looked to Tiamat once more and then spoke into Suzan's mind "Um, first I'm not sure Tiamat has any mammalian instincts, but he is a he you know?" Suzan looked down at her nude human-looking form, and shifted into her humanoid-dragon form.
    Tiamat spoke into Suzan's voice "I was a little disappointed that you seemed to be corrupted by humanity, but your magical skills are very good. I picked up telepathy while working on my english which took awhile, and it also took me months to really learn to fly. Yet in my whole life I haven't had much luck with transformation... much less to the amount you've been able to do." For sure between the two young dragons Suzan was the most recognizably expressive, but Suzan would be thankful that in her draconic forms you couldn't really tell she was blushing.
    "Um... I'm feeling a little too hot, I'm going to chill in the lobby for a little while!" Suzan said before running out of the lava bath. Sitting with her back to a wall Suzan cooled down for a bit while getting over her embarrassment... It's not her fault she imagined puberty wouldn't have this many changes...
