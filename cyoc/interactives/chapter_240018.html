    This story is very, very loosely based on the TV series Extraordinary. In this world, everyone has a superpower of some sort. These powers can range from mundane to incredible. We meet our main character, John. John is a man in his early 30s, he is in decent shape and works a pretty standard office job. John’s power is telekinesis, meaning he can move objects with his mind. This power is not the most powerful in this world, but is much more “super hero” like than a lot of powers in this world. John is feeling underwhelmed and unfulfilled with his current line of work, so he is trying to put together a team of other members with powers that can be used to help the community.

    John is in his garage, interviewing potential team members.

    “Come in!” John responded to a knock on his door. A young man, in his early 20s, walked into the garage and took a seat in the folding chair across from the folding table John was seated at.
    “Hello, my name is Paul” said the young man.
    “Hello Paul. What kind of powers do you have?” asked John
    “I have the ability to increase the size of men’s genitals.”
    "You gotta be shittin' me," John exclaimed, leaning back in his chair, his eyes wide with incredulity. He glanced at the clock on the wall of his garage-turned-headquarters. It was already past midnight, and he had heard quite a few powers today, but this was a new one.
    "No, man, I'm dead serious," said Paul, his voice earnest and slightly nervous. "It's all I've got. I can make a guy's dick grow, like, right in front of your eyes."
    John rubbed his forehead, trying to process this bizarre claim. "Okay, okay," he said slowly, "But how does that fit into the whole 'helping the community' gig?"
    Paul looked at him hopefully. "Well, think about it," he began, "There's always someone who feels inadequate, right? And with my power, I can give them a boost of confidence, make 'em feel like they're on top of the world, you know?"
    John's skepticism was palpable, but curiosity got the better of him. "Alright, let's say I believe you," he said, "What kinds of changes can you do? Are they specific and targeted or just general changes?"
    Paul chuckled, a bit embarrassed. "They, umm.. they can be specific changes. Like, if you wanted a fat knob, I could just grow the cock head. Or if you just wanted length, I could just grow the shaft longer. Or if you wanted one or both of your balls to be bigger, I could just make them grow. Or…”
    John cut him off, “Okay, that’s fine, I think I get the point.”
