"Maybe I should try mixing someone else into the mix to try and change things for the better." He thought as he spotted a janitor walking by the couple. Emile reacted quickly and took another shot of the family, this time with another person mixed in.

His light brown hair grew out and then became styled in pigtails as his clothes became a little pink dress. The woman, former man, shrunk immediately down smaller than the new little girl.
