Suddenly,you stop. Sticking out in the middle of one of the shelves, is a peculiar book. You don't know what has come over you, but you feel drawn to the book. Pulling it out, you notice the title. "Shapeshifter Spells: Mythological Monsters". Figuring it's a fantasy novel, you open the book, noticing it's table of contents. Each one has a symbol instead of a name next to it.
