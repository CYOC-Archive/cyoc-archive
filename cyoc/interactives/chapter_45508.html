There really was nothing he truly wanted to play, but there was one thing that really seemed out of the ordinary: An Amazonian Druid. It seemed a really interesting concept, and as long as he was stuck with things he wouldn't normally play, he could easily try some things out. A strong woman seemed to fit the bill there.

Her name was Lykopis, and Charles had no idea what that meant. Google said it meant She-Wolf, but not knowing anything about the story or the background of the name meant it's true impact was lost on him.

He closed his eyes and felt the suit working it's magic, giving the feelings all over his body of that which was his new form. It was like waking up from a longer slumber at first, and he discovered this was because Lyko logged out from her bed.

It was marble and gold trim with partly see-through purple cloths coming down where there would be windows. There was a high ceiling and a wide doorway with white, yellow, and purple beads covering the passage. It was lit with the dim moonlight coming from the balcony.

    Her bed, though, was soft and warm, with white fine linens and large plump pillows. It was larger than she was, and seemed to hold her weight well. Laying on her side, she could see out beyond the balcony at the stone and sea out beyond, the scent of the ocean air wafting in from the ocean beyond.

She had on an armored thong, and tight metal wraps around her mid-section. Her boots were thick and came up to her thighs, tight leading up to her firm ass, but revealing mostly skin at the front of her upper legs. Her hair was golden, curly, and long... tied back behind her in braids leading down to her heels, though a few curly locks allowed to drop around her face.

His male mind had to wonder if the player had even been female, but he let that thought drift away. Lyko's arms, legs, abs, and exposed breasts were all a feeling of power, while the absence of his penis between his legs let him know he was playing a real woman now.

She took a deep breath and reached for them just as she heard a disturbance coming from the hallway. Charles turned her head to look up the hallway, wondering what was to come next.
