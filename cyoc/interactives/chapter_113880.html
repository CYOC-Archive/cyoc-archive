    You woke up the next day to see Chill was sitting at the entrance of the cave. Your first thought was to look down to see if you were pregnant. Surprisingly your stomach was extremely swollen, and you were going to freak out, but then you calmed down, and thought about it, and what you came up with actually made some sense. Your thoughts were,"How am I this pregnant?! Wait wait calm down Aqua, it's bad for the children if they are stressed. Still though how long did I sleep, and how? Wait Amber, and Pitch mentioned how long dragon-kin live. Damn it my slow ass aging process must have effects on my sleep schedule."

    You sighed and sat up, and Chill turned around and walked up to you. "I see you're awake, and not a moment too soon." Chill said kneeling down to you.

    "How long was I asleep?" You asked Chill

    "Well... about 2 weeks, however that's normal for a mother bearing eggs. I slept for about 9 days, and saw your stomach, and Amber has made preparations with me." Chill said

    "Well thank you, I guess." You said staring at your swollen gut, and then your swollen breasts.

    "Sooo odd question to ask about, well for me at least, but how many do you think I have in me? Eggs that is." You asked

    "Well Pitch was here about a day ago, and said 6 which is good overall. I expect you aren't really familiar with the concept, especially since females aren't exactly common here." Chill said

    "Actually in terms of waiting time I'm really not." You said

    "It's fine, I am one of the few here who has a sister, and a mother who explained this to me willingly. The breeding program finalized the incubation chambers, and tested them multiple times to make sure that they are safe. Anyways after you lay you your eggs they will be warmed by you until they are moved to an incubator, and then Pitch, and I will handle them." Chill said

    Just then Amber came around the corner, and both you, and Chill watched her enter the cave. "I hope I'm not interrupting anything. I told Pitch that I'd be here, and he's on standby for your eggs, and I'm also here to say that Aqua will get assigned to a new male, whenever she likes after she lays. So Aqua whenever you're ready we have another volunteer, after you lay of course." Amber said sitting down.

    "Well tha........" Chill began, but you began roaring, and screaming in pain.

    You knew what was happening, you were in labor, and you were laying your eggs now. You were breathing heavily, while Amber went for Pitch, and Chill encouraged you to push out the first two. You wondered why the first two, but your focus was on pushing. You roared as you pushed out the first egg,"Don't worry, they say that the first one is always the most painful of the clutch. The rest should be substantially easier than the first." Chill said. You then roared again pushing out the second egg, and Chill was right it didn't hurt as much. You continued pushing out your eggs, and Pitch showed up momentarily after you finished.

    You were breathing heavily after laying the last egg,"That...*huff* that was a trial all on its own, and none of you tell me otherwise." You said breathlessly.

    "I can't really argue that considering I can't really say anything here." Pitch said,"You alright with me taking your eggs to the incubator Aqua?"

    "Sure, I just need some time to catch my breath." You said

    "I'll just stay here with Aqua, Chill you should go with Pitch as a father just to make sure your eggs aren't harmed." Amber said

    "Sure, see you Aqua," Chill said as he, and Pitch loaded your eggs onto a cart that had perfectly sized egg holders, and walked off.

    "This was the most tiring, yet interesting experience of my entire life." You said

    "I bet, and I would ask how it felt, but I already got an idea from the others. They say good luck by the way, and not to worry overall. Anyways I just want you to know I actually found something, well related to your situation." Amber said

    "What is it?" You asked

    "Well some of our dungeon hunters found some ruins, and they tell of a male human, becoming a female dragon-kin. The ruins also said that that this former human, which is you, will help make our race dominant, alongside humanity, and dragon kind. This would be done through......well breeding, and you are said to produce more eggs than a normal dragon-kin once you've mated with a number of males. Exact amounts aren't given, but all things considered you are actually seen as positive overall. Guards have actually asked the breeding program about this, and are checking all of the females for memories, and offering our program a promotion of sorts. I you come clean, and you probably have to considering they probably have a mind reader checking the females, you could be considered legendary in a sense." Amber explained.

    "Well that isn't like any legend I have heard of, but in a way your tribe does need females, and just population in general so I guess I have to come clean. Especially since there's a mind reader checking us in case of liars." You said

    "Well as long as it's your call I'm fine with it, just make sure we see each other again I guess." Amber said,"Should I point the guards in your direction then?"

    "Please don't, of you do you might make me sound like a criminal. No offense it's just I have had misunderstandings involving authorities back on my world." You said, and Amber nodded,"I'll wait here then."
