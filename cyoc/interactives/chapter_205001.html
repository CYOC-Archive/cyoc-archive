The dozens of dogs were excited to finally have more room to be in. At the far end of the area near the large fence facing the road was a white poodle, looking at the people on the streets, barking in their direction.

    “How is it possible I’m the only female dog here?” David thought to himself. As soon as they let the dogs out, he looked for a nice place to not be surprised from the back and laid down. Waiting for either Jennifer to arrive or to be forced into his jail cell again. He didn’t know until now how much he missed the wind running through his fur. Being at the outside again, even in a bigger fenced area was still a nice change from his small confined area. He had to try again to show those people he was a female dog. He had a plan for this, he only needed to wait until they start leading the dogs back inside into their cells. This time it has to work.

They are my only company, but I can’t understand them and no human understands me while I understand them. This sounds like the beginning of a bad joke.” he thought while letting the dog continue, knowing it was already too late to resist.

    David looked through the fence at the other side of the street. Just a small tiny metal fence between him and his freedom. Well kind of. He spend his time watching people and cars drive by when at the blink of an eye he saw a shop at the other side change its outer appearance. It took him a minute to count two and two together, but it was the magic shop. It had appeared again at a random location. If only he could get over this fence and run over. The owner would surely understand whats happening. He saw the door at the shop opening. It was the owner stepping outside and placing an advertising sign next to his door.

    “I can see that young man. I saw your missing posters around town and knew something happened. Not the first time someone messed up.” he answered him.
    “You don’t know how happy I am to be able to talk with a human being again. This entire last week was pure hell for me. Can you bring another zipper and get me out of here real quick? Please I will pay you anything.” barked David while still being mounted.
