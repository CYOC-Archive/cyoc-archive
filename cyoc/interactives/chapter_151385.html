    Getting ready for a night in the 1920s

    added 4 years ago TG O Mental Historical

    Yawning you disregard your concerns and look back over at Jimmy "I'm just going to get changed into something a tad more confrontable, be dear and don't peak." You say cheekily as you walk into another room which you feel is the right place to go.

    Opening up the door and heading in, you find yourself in a bedroom. A nice looking bed in the corner, a vanity table and a closet are the first things you notice. You close the door behind you while giving Jimmy a teasing look and saying "Feel free to help yourself to some of the cookies in the jar over by the kitchen."

    With the door closed you now have a the room to yourself. You suddenly spin around admiring all of it. It seems familiar, as you spin you gain more flashes of other times you've been in this room despite this being the first time you have ever seen it. In your confusion you plop down back first onto the bed.

    The confront of it is enough to do away with your worries as the dizziness from the alcohol and from spinning just now comes in full forces as more flashes of a young woman lying down on this very bed come to you. Rubbing your eyes you get up and sit by the vanity table.

    The same woman you saw in the mirror at the speakeasy stares at you, face red and blushing of alcohol and smiling like a woman living the dream. The face no longer even phases you mildly as you begin taking off the beads and flower and other decorations from your head. You then proceed to do the same with your jewellery which you place carefully into a jewellery box.

    You stop briefly when you see the final remaining piece of a jewellery is a necklace. Something about it is important, but its hard to recall exactly what due to your exhaustion from the long night and the dizziness running through your head. Deciding to think on it more tomorrow you simply take off the fancy jewelled silver necklace and place it carefully in a special part of the box specially made for it.

    With that weight off your shoulders you begin washing away the makeup you had applied earlier. As you do you do get a nagging feeling through your dizziness that you need to get that necklace.

    'Oh it's just a brought necklace a while ago, ain't nothing special 'bout it. Although... maybe if Jimmy likes it...' that last thought causes a grin to form on your face as you finish taking off the makeup. You look at your face, even in its natural state it looks absolutely stunning, You then start putting things away.

    You close your jewellery box and notice something you didn't see before, the name 'Anna Graven' is engraved on it's frame. You blink at it and study it for a moment before your confusion washes away.

    'Well 'course it has my name on it. Its my box. Jeez louise, this night's got me in much more of tizzy then I thought' you think to yourself before shaking your head and putting away the box. You then walk over and open the closet to reveal a row of fancy dresses.

    You begin taking off your dress until your are completely bare. A desire suddenly forms to just come out and see Jimmy like this, something which greatly alarms an increasingly shrinking part of your mind. However to that small part's relief, this time you simply just giggle and go "Not yet" before putting the dress in the closet along with your heels.

    You then take out a pink night wear lingerie which you slip into effortlessly. You look at yourself in a mirror. The smiling body of a gorgeous your woman showing more skin then she ought to with a man in her home stares back at you causing you to giggle to yourself once again at how scandalous this all feels. And you love it.

    "Oh Anna Graven, you are living the life you always dreamed off" you remark to yourself as flashes of a young lady dreaming of a life of glamour and excitement run through your mind. Opening the door slowly you walk out in full view of Jimmy, who was eating a cookie which the sight of you causes him to drop. You slowly make your way over to him, making sure to let him get a good view of your legs and swaying hips as you tip toe to him, grabbing him.

    "Well here we are all alone in my apartment, a guy and gal, if people knew they'd start talking 'bout nothing polite" you say while easily putting on a seductive voice.

    "Well no one going to know are they doll?" He says in a similarly flirty tone causing you to giggle and go "No. And that's exactly how I like it."
