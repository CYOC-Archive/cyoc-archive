This was like a treasure for a nerd like Aaron, who collected action figures and sought them out every year when he attended the latest Comic-Con convention. He quickly shuffled over to the bookcase where he admired the figurines on the shelves. The bigger, more muscular ones of warriors, soldiers and barbarians were the ones which caught his attention.
