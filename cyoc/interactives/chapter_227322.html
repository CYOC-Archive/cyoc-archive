    It was another beautiful day at the Transformation Resort when the taxi pulled into the hotel's loading area. As a porter rushed to get the luggage taken out, the occupants of the taxi stepped out.

    One of them was Ashley Simmons. An elegant, professional-looking 35-year-old woman, she had her curly blonde hair tidily styled to her shoulders. Her fit figure was hugged by a well-tailored pantsuit.

    Following her was her mother, Mildred Simmons. 62 years old, she had a full figure and was wearing a colorful Hawaiian dress, her gray hair tucked under a wide-brimmed straw hat and her eyes hidden behind dark glasses.

    Ashley couldn't help but smile as her mother gawked at the hotel.

    "You look like such a tourist, mom", she said, tipping the porter as they walked into the hotel.

    "This place is so big, my goodness!" said her mother, a blush creeping into her face as she took in the surroundings.

    It was hard to not stare. Inside the lobby, the tasteful classical-styled decor had nude statues and friezes of people in various stages of transformation. One statue showed a woman looking at her hands as they became hooves. Another showed a tiny man sitting on the rim of a teacup. There was a relief sculpted on the wall of a man turning into a woman and a woman turning into a man... or was it the other way around?

    "Having second thoughts?"

    "It all looks so... oh dear... l-lewd..."

    "Relax, it's supposed to be a nice restful vacation for both of us". Ashley smirked. Her mother was normally outspoken and demanding, and it was fun seeing her get flustered for a change.

    At the desk the clerk confirmed their registrations with a smile. "So... Ashley and Mildred Simmons... yes, there you are, welcome to the Transformation Resort! I hope you enjoy your stay with us. You will be in room 471". He handed them both card keys and pamphlets as he continued to explain what they might expect from their stay. "You will of course have access to all the transformative materials in your room, but if you want to spend time with others who have picked a certain type of transformation, we have different wings of the hotel open for you to socialize in".

    He handed them another set of brochures. "You also have access to the grounds of the hotel, including the AR and AP fountains, the Wild Animal Preserve, The Amazing Leslie's live show, and many more besides. Be sure to ask for our food and drink specials too".

    With that Ashley and Mildred walked off to their room and settled into it. They had asked for two separate beds, but the room looked even more luxurious than it did online. There was a table, a spacious bathtub, a hot tub, a huge closet, a widescreen TV, and of course a massive cabinet full of powders and liquids.

    "So, what shall we do?" said Ashley, putting her bags down.

    "What have they got?" said Mildred, her enthusiasm returning as she started looking through the options. Ashley suspected her mom had a hidden kinky side - not that that was something she wanted to know more about - and seeing how excited she was getting didn't help.

    Well, did she have a choice? The original plan was for her to visit with Henry, her boyfriend of two years. But after a messy breakup - the latest in several messy breakups, unfortunately - she decided to take her mother along in a last-minute decision. Not like her job as an executive gave her many breaks anyway, it would have been a shame to let the extra ticket go to waste.

    "Maybe I should try getting younger", said Mildred.

    Ashley shuddered. "Can we not? Aunty Eleanor said you were quite the handful when you were little".

    "Ugh, you're so serious Ashley. Maybe we should turn you into a cute little puppy".

    "Then it'll be your job to clean up after me", said Ashley with a big grin as she started unpacking. "Besides, maybe we should explore the place first before making a decision".
