    Jamie's Little Problem

    added 11 years ago AP

    Jamie then started to worry about the little baby who was Tricia, and tried to get her to calm down.

    “Don’t cry” plead Jamie as she gently patted the baby on the head, but that didn’t help at all as Tricia continued to wail on.

    Jamie then looked back down at the remote said to herself, “if this button made you smaller… then this one must make you bigger!”

    With that Jamie pressed the button down on the remote as a beam of light hit Tricia again. This time Jamie watched as Tricia body begin to stretch. Tricia quickly started to get older and bigger her body began to rise from the clothes pile. Rising taller and taller, suddenly Jamie lets go of the button, causing Tricia to stop growing.

    “You’re like me now” said Jamie as she pointed at Tricia. Tricia was now not only about the same height as Jamie, but she was also the same age as Jamie, making her 5. After giving Jamie a few clueless blinks, Tricia hops down from the couch, leaving behind all her clothes except for her shirt that was now like a dress on her tiny body. Tricia continues to run around the room with new burst of energy, giggling all the way. Jamie was confused at the sudden behavior of her former babysitter, but when Jamie looked up again, Tricia was suddenly gone.

    “Tricia!” called Jamie as she looked around her house, but little Tricia was nowhere to be found. Climbing up the stairs, Jamie saw that the lights in her room were on so she walked inside to check it out, only to find Tricia making a mess in her room, flinging her toys all about.
    “Tricia!” yelled Jamie “clean up this mess right now!” she demanded.

    “No!” Tricia repied not even bothering to look at Jamie.

    Jamie began to get frustrated quickly making her way out her own room. Jamie then slammed the door of the bathroom, locking herself inside, which is what she normally does when she gets mad.

    “Stupid Tricia!” groaned Jamie “If only I was bigger, then she has to listen to me!”

    But that’s when an idea hit Jamie’s head, looking at the reflection in full body mirror, Jamie saw that she was still holding the remote in her hand. Lifting it up, she twisted the remote towards herself.
    “If this button made Tricia bigger, then it must make me bigger too” she said finally pushing the button down. A beam fired from the remote hitting Jamie right in the chest. Jamie feeling the weird sensation sweeps across her body, letting out moan as she begins to change…
