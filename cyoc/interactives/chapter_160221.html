    Subject awareness: On
    World awareness: on
    User awareness: on

    then, a message popped up. it said, "Shall we initiate tf now?"
    'Yes/No'

    he clicked 'yes'.

    Then, He entered Will's name on the site, and he saw the picture of his bully's second mate on the screen: a tall, slightly muscular 21 year old with black hair, wearing a blue striped shirt, black jeans, and grey shoes.

    He selected the 'click and change' option again.
    it showed the full body picture of Will.
