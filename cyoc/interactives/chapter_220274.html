    Rick glares down at the shorter teen, “careful, Jeffy,” he says, “I am in a good mood at the moment, so don’t push your luck. Hand me your lunch money and your phone!”

    Jeff, not wanting to receive a blue eye before the first class, hands Rick all of his money, but does not hand him the phone.

    Rick takes the money and puts it into his pocket, he then holds out his hand, “the phone now, if you don’t mind,” he says.

    “N-no,” Jeff says.

    Rick’s eyebrows narrow, “what was that,” he growls.

The larger teen suddenly grabs Jeff by the collar and pushes him into the lockers with a loud “clang”, while he grabs Jeff’s phone with his free hand, starting to twist it, trying to free it from Jeff’s grip. “Hand.     “Now,” Rick says, twisting a little more, “if you don’t want to pass out,” Jeff reluctantly surrenders and once Rick has the phone, he lets go of Jeff who slides to the floor, panting. Rick looks down at the phone in his hand, then at Jeff, “now, that wasn’t so hard was it,” he asks, “now, let’s see what it is you desperately didn’t want me to see,” he then says.

    “Y-you don’t even know my p-password,” Jeff says, still on the ground.

    Rick smirks, “oh, don’t I,” he asks, “what was your birthyear again… aaah yes 2000… and there we go,” he says after typing in the code.

    “F-fuck,” Jeff says.

    Rick smirks, “you should pick your friends more wisely and stop hanging around weaklings,” Rick says, “or you shouldn’t tell your friends your password! Haha… now let’s see here… hm… what is this? “The Chronivac”? Is that some new nerdy game,” he asks, looking down at Jeff who stays silent. Rick scowls, “not wanting to tell, eh? Well, no matter, I’ll just have to read the manual,” he says and opens the App and starts to read the manual.

    Jeff is frozen in fear, anger and defeat, ‘shit, if he figures it out, who knows what he might do!’

    After reading the manual, Rick’s face takes on a disbelieved expression, “yeah right,” he says, “as if there is some program out there that is able to transform humans, animal and objects into various things, alter the reality, alter someone’s mind and do all kind oof BS!”

    “S-so, does that mean I can have my phone back,” Jeff asks, hopefully.

    “Nah, I am curious,” Rick says, now sounding thoughtfully, “what if this actually works? I could have a lot of fun with this, but I need something or… someone to test it on,” he grins down at Jeff, “and you just volunteered to be my little “guinea pig”,” he then aims Jeff’s phone at Jeff and Jeff hears a “ping” sound, his eyes widen in horror, he had been scanned! Now Rick will be able to do anything to him!

    “Pl-please Rick,” Jeff says, almost begging, “please… d-don’t do anything to me!”

    Rick smirks, “why would I,” he asks, “nah, I got to know. Let’s see, as a test, let’s do something subtle… how about changing your black hair into a nice girly pink”.

    Jeff suddenly feels his head tingle and when he looks up at Rick, seeing the other teen’s surprised expression, which quickly transforms into an evil smirk, he knew that he now has pink hair.

    “Haha,” Rick laughs, “it actually works! AMAZING! Hehehe… I think I’ll have some fun with this, thanks Jeff!”

    Jeff is horrified, “pl-please Rick,” he says, “d-don’t…”.

    “Shut it, Jeff,” Rick says, he then grins, “oh, I have something sweet planned for you, but first… let’s change your hair back to normal… there… hehe, now the real fun can begin,” he smirks as he starts typing away on Jeff’s phone.

    “Hehe… you’ll see soon enough,” Rick says, “and you’ll enjoy it… or not, I don’t really care… I know I WILL enjoy it… a LOT,” he then confirms whatever change he had done to Jeff.

    Jeff feels his whole body and mind tingle, ‘what has he done to me,’ the boy thinks to himself in fear.
