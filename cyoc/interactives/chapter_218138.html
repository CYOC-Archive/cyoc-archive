    The Witches Ink is a tiny little locally owned tattoo studio situated in a quaint little corner of North Philly. While the vast majority of the shops business is simple mundane tattooing and piercing it does offer some more niche services that live up to the shops name. Spells quite literally carved into the flesh using a fusion of tattooing and centuries old witchcraft practices all preformed by the proprietress herself: Astrid Hellgren.
    While the number of effects these spells could have are potentially endless those that shape the body are most often preferred. Spells that can shrink or grow an individual, alter their face or change their gender (The Witches Ink is quite popular in the local trans community Astrid herself being trans) are the most common. Even hand made temporary tattoos that offer lest permanent effects are sold.
    By far the strangest service they offer however, is the living tattoo. Using a carefully guarded secret method Astrid can extract an individual's soul and infuse it into her ink the result being a talking moving tattoo. Those who wish to become ink can do so for a small fee either being tattooed onto a friend of family member at a discounted price, or being sold to one of Astrid's many other patrons. Walk-ins are available today!
    Now with introductions out of the way, who are you? and which of the witches ink's services are you interested in?
