Hollywood still has its moments, but a lot of today's stuff seems to be remakes," agreed Karen, who was not surprised Peter already knew about the classic movie channel available on cable. "But I can't believe you somehow always seem to miss Abbott and Costello. Who's on first? is such a comedy classic routine."
