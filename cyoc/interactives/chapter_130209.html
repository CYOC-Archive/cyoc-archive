    Derek was weary as he walked into the magic shop. He had read online on some of the lesser known forums that the shop was known to give people strange yet incredible powers with its products and he was desperate. He had had enough. For the past 6 months he had dealt with his terrible roommate, Cole, for too long and he wanted a way out. Being a straight A student and the captain of the university's soccer team, Cole thought he was the shit and he acted like it too. He used women who fell to his boyish good looks and berated and abused all those who envied him. Derek, being the guy's roommate, meant that he got the worst of it.

    It didn't help that Derek had such a hard crush on him. Sure, his personality was terrible, but in Derek's opinion the captain's ass and looks were enough to make the college sophomore go head over heels for the man. The fact that Cole knew about his sexuality made it even worse. Derek constantly got verbal abuse and no matter who he complained to, no one listened. No authority figure at the small university would dare oppose the star of one of school's only good sports team, causing Derek to be out of luck.

    Well, he was out of luck until he found the magic shop. He had had enough of the nonsense, so he was going to use something from the shop to make his roommate pay.

    His eyes quickly landed on a strange, shiny packet. "Truth Tongue!" it advertised. "Make your speech the truth with whatever you say!" Intrigued, Derek picked up the product. Ultimately, thanks to his roommate, most of the university knew about his sexuality, so it wasn't like one more stereotypical feature would make things worse. He went over to the cashier and paid for the product, perturbed by the cashier's odd smile.

    As soon as he walked out of the store, Derek opened the packet. The stud, once in his hand, immediately flew into his mouth, piercing his tongue and snapping into place by magic. Derek feeling power surge through his veins, began to plot who his first victim would be.
