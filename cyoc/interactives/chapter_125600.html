    Amy frowned a bit as she considered. She was completely unaware how she was slowly aging up. "I don't really know," said Amy, "I hadn't thought about that."

    "You sure?" asked Katie, "You don't want to be any particular thing?" Her wish would mold Amy into what they talked about, but it wouldn't be much if she couldn't get Katie to talk.

    "Well, maybe something like my dad," said Amy as she started growing a bit more, "He's a big businessman."

    "And what kind of business-" started Katie before they both heard a buzzing sound. They looked around to see where it came from when Amy felt in her pocket and pulled out a cell phone. She answered it and said, "Hello? Yeah, I'm fine. I know, I shouldn't have wondered off like that, but I was... Huh, that's weird, I can't remember why I did that. Maybe I just wasn't paying attention. Don't worry, I'm fine. I'm just sitting on a bench near the playground. No, you don't have to come look for me. I'll find you later. Ok, I'll be fine. Bye."

    Katie noted that if Amy had a cell phone before, she wouldn't be crying about being lost. But then, six year olds don't normally have cell phones, even in this modern age. But Amy looked about 10 now. Katie was going to have to guide the conversation a bit if Katie was going to have a particular adulthood. "So, you want to be in business?" asked Katie.

    Amy was uncertain what Katie was talking about for a moment before saying, "Yeah, why should guys get to be the big moneymakers? Girls are just as smart and capable as they are. Heck, we're smarter than a lot of them, even if they won't admit it. It's time we started taking charge."

    Katie watched with interest as Amy's words started to fuel her change. Her T-shirt was losing its design as it was transitioning to a white button-up shirt. Her jeans were becoming a lot smoother and darker as they became dress pants. Her sneakers were also transforming, laces and details melting away as they started to form into high heels. Her pig tails were moving toward the back of her head, where they'd doubtlessly become a professional bun. By now, Amy was 12 going on 13 and if Katie were to judge, she was probably going to change into a very beautiful and sexy woman. The main question is what kind of business career will Amy get?
