    Wearing His Brother

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy A month ago O

    Grinning to himself, Jay grabs the second shoe and walks to Jeff’s bed, “let’s hope the SHOES are the right size,” the young boy says, a part of him feels excited, while another part is still a little creeped out.

    Jake sets the shoes down in front of him, he then first opens the right shoe, the one that had materialized from thin air, and slips his foot inside. The 12-year-old is amazed, “perfect fit,” he says, wriggling his toes, then tightening the straps. Jake then grins down at the shoe that had been Jeff, “now… time to wear my brother,” he giggles, feeling somehow excited and giddy. The boy opens the “shoe” and slips his foot into the opening, even letting out a sigh of comfort at the feeling. Jay tightens the shoe laces and gets to his feet, he then takes a few steps, he then starts to jog, and soon he is running around Jeff’s room. The boy stops and jumps up and down on the spot, before grinning down at his new shoes, “blamy, these shoes are much more comfier than I thought,” he says to himself, “hehe… and they are a perfect fit! I can’t believe I am actually wearing my brother. Welp… he was a lazy git anyway, hehe… he does make better shoes than a brother. Hmmm… maybe I’ll keep him like this, besides, he’d beat me up once I’d change him back to normal.”

    Jay is about to leave Jeff’s room when he looks over at Jeff’s Laptop, ‘hm… that program would come in handy,’ he thinks to himself, grinning, ‘I could have so much fun. Maybe I’m able to transfer it onto my phone.’

    So, still wearing his new “shoes”, Jay heads into his room and grabs his phone, soon Jay has transferred the Chronivac, including Jeff’s file, over to his phone, which caused the Chronivac to vanish from Jeff’s Laptop.

    “Now, what should I do,” Jay asks himself, he then grins, “I think I’ll go for a walk, maybe to the park or Zoo. Hehe… I could also call some friends, hehe… I could have some fun and try some more stuff out with the App.”

    After a while, Jay decides to go for a walk, so the 12-year-old grabs his keys and leaves the house, Dean had already left before Jeff transformed.

    While Jay happily walks down the path of his house and turns down the street, Jeff himself feels miserable, ‘damn that twerp,’ he grumbles mentally, ‘I can’t believe he transformed me into shoes. I bet he knew what he was doing and just pretended to not be aware! This was his plan all along! If I ever get turned back, I’ll make him feel sorry… maybe a few weeks as a mutt would teach him a lesson.’

    What Jeff is unaware of, Jay has opened the “chat function” of the App and is reading Jeff’s thoughts, ‘so, planning to take it out on me, ey,’ the 12-year-old thinks to himself, frowning, ‘just because you did not think off turning off your Laptop? You always blame ME if you make a mistake! I am tired of you treating me like shit. Well… hehe… hope you enjoy being my shoes, you should start to get used to it, because I think I’ll be keeping you like this for a while!’
