    Regina felt herself in tune with the magical energies of her home dimension. It was good to be home. She and Sunny were enjoying a leisurely lunch at an outdoor cafe far from Regina's home city.

    Sunny was gazing around in awe at the beautiful lesbians of the Great Change world. Ever since Regina had let slip about what life was like where she was from, Sunny had been pestering her for a visit. And with Regina purged of the Immortality virus and Aziza off on a quest in another dimension, why not?

    One reason why not was that Regina felt a little guilty about Barry, who had given up both gender fluidity and his/her home dimension for Regina, who had then taken off with no forwarding address. Regina would have to see Barry and explain about Sunny while she was here, but she wasn't in a hurry to do it.

    Sunny was a cheerful and optimistic sort, excited to be visiting a new universe with lots of beautiful women and learning more about her lover Regina's history. But she was not incapable of fear, and something about the woman approaching their table made her the scariest woman Sunny had ever seen.

    "Hello Regina" said Savannah "What brings you back to our humble universe?"
