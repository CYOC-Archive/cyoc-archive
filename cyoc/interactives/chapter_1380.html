    The green door

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 21 years ago

    Green is your favorite color so you quickly run in and close the door. You hear a loud thump followed by alot of whining.


    You realize it is pitch black in the room, so you feel around the wall for a light switch. Ahh you found one.


    Click, the room now lighted reveals a room filled with stuffed dead animals and a bear skinned bed.


    You all of a sudden feel sleepy and head towards the bed. You sit down on the bed, when the fur blanket attacks you, pulling you down to the ground. Some how
    you escape, you run to the door to escape, but the door won't open. You hear a nose behind, as you turn around everything in the room disappears. By know you
    noticed that you are naked, with bear slippers on your feet. You go to take them off but they aren't slippers, they ARE your feet. Just then the fur flys up your legs,
    up your chest, down your arms, and covering your face. You feel intense pains as all your bones snap and reshape themselves. You are now a bear from the head
    down to your feet. And all you can think of is maybe I should of went in the red door.

    What do you do now?
