    "I think I've had enough fun being small," Sasha said. "Can you make me bigger now?"

    "Sure," Nicole said, picking up the gun.

    Nicole fired the the Reducer at Sasha and the three girls watched as the black girl returned to her original 5'8" height. Once at her normal height, Sasha pulled her own clothes back on. Nicole and Cassandra soon followed suit.

    "As much as Cass wanted a go," Nicole said, "I think we let the thing charge up a bit before we do any more major size changing."

    "I get it," Cassandra said. "We don't want it to die on us leaving one of us tiny or giant. I wish I had gotten a turn, but I totally get it."

    "I have figured out how to do precision work," Nicole said with a grin. "Did you want bigger boobs for a few days?"

    "Maybe."

    "Or would you like me to try something else?"

    Cassandra thought for a moment. "Let's go with F-cups and re-size my current bra to match. And then make the bikini in my car big enough to fit someone twenty-five feet tall."

    "Okay, let's grow your boobs and bra, and then we'll grow your bikini."

    Nicole picked up the gun and started making the proper adjustments. Once finished, she turned to Cassandra and said, "Strip down to your bra. I want to make this as easy as possible."

    "Sure."

    Once she'd stripped, Nicole fired the gun. Cassandra's boobs and bra grew in unison, gradually getting bigger until the filled the set requirements.

    "Wow, these are awesome. I'm sure to a date tonight for sure."

    "Remember," Nicole said, "not a word to anyone about the gun." Her friends nodded in agreement. "Now, go get the bikini and meet me out back while I get this set right."

    Out on the patio, Cassandra laid out the bright yellow bikini on the ground. Nicole shot the fabric and the three girls watch it expand. Soon the bikini cups were a foot-and-a-half wide.

    "I thought it'd be bigger," Cassandra commented.

    "Sorry, that's the size you specified," Nicole replied.

    "Oh well."

    The two friends left soon after that and Nicole went inside and found that the charge port was the same as her phone and so powered the Reducer down, no need for unwanted accidents, and plugged it in. She was almost done with this housesitting job and when she got home, she'd have more time to study her new toy.
