    Both girls stare at you, awaiting your decision. Brooke thumps her large paws impatiently. You think it over and realize both girls are somewhat right. Going back to the magic shop would be the best idea, the old man obviously knew what happened to you, so he'd know how to fix it. However, Brooke had a point. As little bunnies, there was no way you were going to make it back to the store safely. Plus, you didn't want to leave Brooke alone. Despite all she did to you with the ears, the swimsuit, the whole pet thing, you still loved her. You look down at the spellbook, and have an idea.

    "We could try another spell from the book, maybe fix it ourselves" You say

    Claire slaps a fuzzy hand to her face. "Did you not hear me, idiot. Mixing and tangling magic is very dangerous. All you would do is make things worse"

    "Or we can just stay bunnies, live a relaxed life full of luxury. I kind of like this cute body" She says, tugging on one of her floppy ears. Despite how much you loved her, she could be really crazy at times. Did she really just want to give up and stay a bunny?

    "No Brooke, you're probably enjoying this but me and Claire aren't. I don't want to be a bunny, or a girl. I just want to be myself again" You say. Brooke pouts and turns away from you. "Besides, I wasn't changed by the spellbook, only by the ring. I could change one of you two back at the very least"

    Claire thinks for a moment. "You know what, that might work. If we weren't corrupted at all by your magical energy, either me or Brooke would be able to change back. I don't know about you though..."

    "Still, if one of us can become human again, we can safely get back to the shop." You say "Then we can fix everything else"

    "Alright, Claire. Just cast a spell and change me back. I'll fix everything." Brooke says. Both you and Claire stare angrily at Brooke, and her ears flop down sadly.

    "No way. I'm not trusting you after what you did to your boyfriend. If we remain bunnies and you change back you'd probably just keep us as pets or something." She says. Brooke sadly hops away, muttering "I would have treated you well, you know." under her breath.

    You hop over to Claire, who flips through the spellbook. You all look really cute as bunnies, like you came straight out of a cartoon. Strangely, you can see why Brooke wanted to keep you as a pet.

    "Alright, here" Claire says "I'll cast this spell on the two of us. I don't know exactly what it'll do, but if all goes well both of us should change back"

    "Whaaaat, what about me!?!" Brooke squeals angrily

    "No way. You're staying a bunny until me and your boyfriend figure this all out."

    "Oh come on! Thats not fair. You better cast that spell on me too" Brooke says, stomping her feet on the ground. She looks absolutely adorable when she's angry.

    "Oh please, you're the one who said we should all stay bunnies forever. You shouldn't mind this, then. You'll be lucky if we ever change you back at all because of what you've caused."

    You stand next to Claire, who begins reading a strange incantation. You feel a tingling go through your body, and your vision goes black. When it returns, you see...
