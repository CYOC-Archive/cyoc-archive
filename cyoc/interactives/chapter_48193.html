    After you and Matt finished dancing around in joy, Zedis invited all
    three of you in for tea and something to eat, after Hector, you, and
    Matt got dressed. Happily, all of you sat down around the table; you
    and Matt sat at opposite ends. Zedis summoned all of the refreshments
    with magic. You and Matt had three helpings, neither of you had a good
    meal in several days.

    Unfortuntly, this happiness did not last long. During the meal, You
    felt a sudden pain in your chest. Looking down, you noticed your chest
    was glowing. You lifted your shirt, and were startled to the curse
    symbol glowing. Matt must of felt similarly because he too lifted his
    shirt; his symbol was also glowing. Suddenly, you and Matt fell to the
    floor, screaming in pain. It was unbearable. Your whole right side
    felt like it was dying. Hector and Zedis jumped up.

    You and Matt began convulsing. Zedis quickly ran over to you, and
    began chanting words that you could not understand. He was examining
    what the symbol was doing. He finally woke up from his trance, and
    yelled at Hector to carry Matt over by you. Hector laid Matt on the
    right side of you, and the magic of the curse began to take effect.

    You and Matt were mering together once again. Once the process was
    finished, the pain ended as abruptly as it began; you and Matt were
    once again sharing a body from your necks down. The curse symbol was
    complete once again on your shared chest.

    Both of you struggle to catch your breathes. Breathing hard, you say,
    "What...happ..ened...Why are...we like this...again."

    Zedis explains, "I don't know for sure, but I have a theory. My
    potion, of course, did separate you, but the curse merged you back
    together."

    "That's obvious!" Matt angrily exclaimed.

    "Please, let me continue." Zedis said. He cleared his throat and
    continued. "I believe that the curse keeps the two of you joined, but
    the curse also allows you to separate at will."

    "What?" you and Matt exclaimed.

    "Yes, I believe you can use the curse to separate yourselves once you
    learn how, but...there is a catch. I think you can only remain
    separated for a short while. If you exceed that time, the curse will
    slowly kill you until you merge together again. And unfortunately...I
    can do nothing about it. The curse is permanent.

    What do you do now?
