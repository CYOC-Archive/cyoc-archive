    Tricia was now a 6-year-old toon skunk. Her fur was a hot pink with a white stripe running from neck to tail. Her white hair was up in pony-tails
    held up by pink ribbons. Her fur covered her so well, that even her private parts couldn't be seen. She looked in the mirror, and loved what she
    saw! "This is so cool!" she said "I've always loved furry toon kids, and now I'm one!" She thought about it and realized something. "It won't be any
    fun if i'm the only one, though! Ah! I know!" She took the remote with her outside, and she met a guy in his twenties. "Uh mister," she said "could
    I talk to ya fer a sec?" He turned around with a grimace. "That's a nice costume kid," he said "but Halloween isn't for months, and you just missed
    the local furry con!" "It's not a costume," she said smiling "it's real! Go ahead an' feel it if ya like." "Sorry kid," he said "but I don't have time to play.
    Now go bug someone else!" "Maybe ya can MAKE time!" she said smiling "What's yer name?" "It's Dan," he said in anger "now go!" "Pleased to meet
    ya, Danny!" she said smiling "I'm Tricia." "It's Dan!" he said very annoyed. "I like Danny better." she said smiling. With that, she fired the remote
    control at Dan, and he shrank into his clothes. When he came out, he was a 6-year-old toon bear cub with light blue fur that covered all. He
    looked at his paws in amazement. "Whoa!" he said "What did ya do?" "You were trapped," Tricia said "so I freed ya! Don't ya feel better?" "Yep," he
    said smiling "sure do! Thanks a bunch! Sorry I was mean to ya." "It's ok." Tricia said A woman in her twenties saw what happened, and was scared.
    Tricia saw this, and turned to face her. "What's your name?" Tricia asked. "Sally," she replied "and I saw what you did - I'm calling the cops!" Before
    she could move, Tricia fired the remote at her, and she shrank into her clothes, and out came a 6-year-old toon bunny with light purple fur that
    covered all, and a big pink bow in her purple hair. She looked at herself and smiled. "Hee-hee! I look cute!" Sally said. "Thanks!" "Yer welcome!"
    Tricia said. "An' ya know what? This is just the beginning!" Tricia then used the remote to give both Danny and sally their own remotes, but these
    were simpler - they had just 1 button. "All ya havta do is aim the remote at someone, push the button, and they'll turn into a 6-year-old toon
    furry," Tricia said "but if they'er already 6 or younger, it won't make them any younger." "Cool," said Danny "I wanna make more people happy like
    me!" "Yeah," Sally said "me too!" "Alright then," Tricia said "let's spit up so we can get as many people as possible to join us and be free!" And so
    the 3 set off in different directions, determined to turn anyone they saw into a furry toon kid. Who will you follow?
