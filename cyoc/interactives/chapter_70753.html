    Pretty normal, you could call it boring life of long nights studying to make average grades, working to stay
    afloat and staying close to my friends. I’m probably just like a lot of you, or at least I was. I know most
    people use this site to post fictional stories but something is happening here, something weird and I don’t
    know where else to turn. I wish I could say I trusted my friends but I may not be able to soon. No one would
    believe me if I started a regular blog. There’s no one else I can go to, so I pray you guys will read these
    entries and believe me. I’m going to try to post here everyday, to learn as much as I can, to share
    everything I can. I need to get this to people who will believe it, I need to warn people. I don’t want
    what’s happening here to ever happen again to anyone.

    This is Day One. I’ll start at the beginning, or at least the beginning of my involvement. I can’t tell you
    my last name, or what school this is or even what state I’m in. I can’t let them know I’m on to them. You

    knows Friday… Yeah, well this is a bit worse. This wasn’t an ordinary video, there’s nothing ordinary about

    to student. Its supposed to be an instructional video on how to be a real bro. You know the type, good with
    ladies, hit at parties, tight bro packs. People like that are popular in college so its no wonder why people
    jumped to watch it at first. It was supposed to just be a how to, give them some points on how to talk and

    and make a good early impression. At first it was funny, they tried to imitate the video. They tried to
    dress and talk like real bros but it was just really sad and fake. But as the days went on that changed.
    They changed. The first few people started getting more built. They started getting really tan. It was
    slight at first, it began with them developing a little more tone and over a matter of days the developed
    months of muscle. Not all over, just their upper bodies mostly, they all got six packs and big pecs and arms.

    salon. They started wearing tighter clothing and showing off whenever they got the chance. They’d take
    pictures constantly and start pulling up their shirts in front of girls.

    Still, no one really gave it much thought. They put in a little extra effort at the gym, we told ourselves.
    They were getting more sun, its college, people want to change. They were proud of their accomplishments.

    started talking differently. Their voices were deeper, which shouldn’t happen at this age, they were smoother

    conservative of them made drastic wardrobe changes. Polos, short preppy shorts, snapck backs, expensive shoes
    and sandals that they shouldn’t have been able to afford. Eventually even their hair changed, they all got

    blond. And they weren’t just doing worse academically they actually seemed dumber, a lot dumber. They only
    talked about their bodies, and picking up chicks. They partied and drank all the time seeing who could chug
    the most, or the quickest. Soon they all started looking the same, all started acting the same and all only
    hanging out with each other. A pack of good looking zombies. They were few at first but the video started
    spreading slowly.

    None of this mattered to me at first, until a few days ago. Robert, a kid my age, approached me. We weren’t
    like great friends and all that, he was a little nerdy and antisocial but nice enough so we got along. He
    seemed completely out of his mind with fear. He told me about how some of his friends had watched the video
    and had started changing. He broke down a bit as he described how they began drifting away from him.
    Robert’s friends were, like him, mostly nerds. It was hard to believe that they’d be lured into the bro life
    but Robert insisted watching it had actually changed them. I felt like he was hiding something so I pressed
    him for awhile. He admitted that his friends had come back and had forced him to watch the video earlier that
    day. He told me he was already starting to have weird feelings. He wouldn’t elaborate he just shook his head
    sadly. I noticed his arms looked a little bigger than usual but dismissed him as being overly dramatic. If
    only I had listened.

    I went back to see Robert today. I opened his door and was greeted by a loud and deep “aye bro!” There was a
    crunch and I noticed I stepped on a beer can, even though Robert didn’t drink, or didn’t before. He was had
    several cans on his desk next to him. He was dressed like the other bros and was definitely more muscular
    than he had been, Hell he was muscular period. I asked if he was Robert, I wasn’t even sure. Only his
    strange blue eyes held any trace of the nerd that had once come to me begging for help. He told me to call
    him Robby now and eagerly showed off his abs to me with a big cheeky grin that seemed so alien on his face,
    altered as it was. “You like bro?” He asked me. I shook my head and he just laughed. “Just checking you
    aren’t a faggot bro.” It was so unreal, this, change that had happened. “Robert… Robby, whatever, what the
    Hell happened to you man? You weren’t like this…” His eyes narrowed. “Don’t try me! I know you’re just
    jealous.” He calmed down again and shrugged before going back to drinking. I let myself out without another
    word. I didn’t know the person in that room, and I didn’t care to.

    That was hours ago but it still feels like I just left that room. That’s what this video can do to people,
    that’s what its doing. How many other people have seen it? How fast is it spreading around? Have my friends
    watched it yet? God, I hoped not. I recalled Robert recounting the slow turn, the drift, and then forcing
    him into… that thing. I couldn't bear it, if that ever started happening to my friends. But I know them
    better, Robert was just a nerd. My friends are great, they're stronger than that, stronger than this stupid
    video, I hope. I hope this all blows over, that it all just ends. Thanks everybody for listening. I should
    go, I need to try to get some sleep. Signing off for tonight.
