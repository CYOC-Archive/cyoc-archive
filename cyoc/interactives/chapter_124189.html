    I left the valley and headed to the wizard city of Vale to shop for some uncommon books on magic it get a better view on the lesser know magics and the limits of any there are. My experiments in casting spells had show no limit to me but my mana limit. I left Mary behind to not get in deep shit with other wizards.

    It took me several days to get to the city. While I can teleport no one had thought about making that kind if spell which is why I'm looking for at least uncommon books if not managing to get my hands on rare ones. I reached the city and booked a room at a Inn for three days before I returned to my home. I looked around the city getting a couple of books as I looked around on the first day. On the third day I was shoulder by a passerby and found a note in my pocket. 'Meet us here at midnight.' It said with a location of a Inn. I went to the meeting point and looked around carefully finding two men sitting at a table and the waiters looking at the two carefully. I was glad for my cloak as I sat at the table.

    "We heard you are looking for rare books." One of the two told me. "And if I am?" I asked him. "We have a few you may be interested in." He told me. "It depends if they are what I'm looking for to buy them." I warned them." We understand and will look for them if you will like us to, for a cost." The other one spoke up. "Very well, may we take a look?" I asked them. I followed them through the streets to a alleyway. One of them knocked on the wall which opened up and I found myself in a shop. I looked through the books and picked up two of them. I paid for them and returned the Inn. I packed my items and removed a tracking spell I felt being casted on me and placed it on a bird roasting on the roof. I was glad I paid for the room before hand upfront as I left the city. I got ten miles away before teleporting to my home. I removed my pack and joined Mary on my bed and fell a sleep.
