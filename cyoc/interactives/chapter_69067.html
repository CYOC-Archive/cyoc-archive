    A knock on the door is heard, “Come in,” Amanda said and a man comes in “Joshua, I’m glad you could make it on
    such short notice.” Joshua replies “No problem sweetheart, anything for you.” He looks at me, “Is this the one
    described as sister Sarah’s brother?” Amanda nods “Meet Jeramy, from the looks of it, the most unluckiest
    human on campus.” “Perhaps we can change his luck.” Joshua said “Did you ask him?” Amanda says “Yes, he said
    he’s not sorority material.” Joshua laughs and looks at me “Well Jeramy, you may not be Lambda Beta Pi, but
    you certainly are more like Alpha Tau Omega.” I said “Alpha wha?” Amanda says “Alpha Tau Omega is a fraternity
    version of Lambda Beta Pi.” I think of Joshua’s offer and looked at Sarah, “For Sarah, I’ll accept your offer.
    I’m in.” Sarah smiles “I promise you, you won’t regret it.” Joshua “Good for you. We’ll have the ritual
    tonight.” Amanda picks up her cell phone “I’ll call the grand Alpha and let him know that we have found the
    last male candidate.”

    Joshua holds out his hand, “Come Jeramy, let’s meet the rest of the pack.” As I was about to leave the house,
    Sarah says “If you see my boyfriend Jeff, tell him Sarah says hello.” She gives me my backpack “Don’t forget
    this.” I took my backpack “Thanks.”

    Joshua took me to the Alpha Tau Omega house and see all of the frat werewolves are in the public room “Is this
    a little late for visitors?” says a blond haired one. Joshua says “This is no visitor Stephen, Jeramy is going
    to be our new packmate.” “When is the ritual?” says a brown haired boy. “Tonight Jeff.” Replies Joshua. I said
    to Jeff “My sister, Sarah says Hello.” “Sister?” replies Jeff “That’s right,” Joshua says “Jeramy is Sarah’s
    brother. The more reason why he is going to be part of our pack.” Soon all of the pack stood up to greet me,
    “Careful,” Joshua said “He’s been badly bruised.” Joshua’s phone rings and he answers “Hello? Oh Amanda. He’s
    right here.” He hands the phone to me “Hello?” Amanda on the other line “Okay, the grand Alpha is going to
    help you tonight, Joshua and his pack will escort you to the secret ritual while me and my pack will join in.
    Brittany says Hello and couldn’t wait when your officially part of the pack. Put Josh back on.” I handed the
    phone back to Joshua “Yes dear…okey….Of course I know where it is….Okay, we’re on our way.”

    “Come everyone,” Joshua says as he hangs up his phone “Time for the ritual.” Joshua hands me a red robe. “Here
    you go Jeramy, when we reach the Luna’s temple, put this on.” We left the house and followed Joshua to a cave
    in the forest outside the campus and soon ran into Amanda and the girls by a cave, “This is it.” Joshua says.
    Amanda says “Everyone put on your robes and go in.” We changed into the robes and entered the cave, the huge
    room resembles a temple. Where a man with a green robe comes in as he pulls down the hood, it’s Dean Woods,
    “You’re the grand Alpha?” I said. The dean just nodded “Alpha Tau Omega and Lambda Beta Pi are my secret
    weapon against troublemakers. You look like you had more of the share of them then anybody. Let’s fix that.”
    He pulls out a chair “Sit here and we’ll begin.”

    I sat down and everyone else sat in their respected seats while Amanda stood under the Lambda Beta Pi sign on
    the left while Joshua stood under the Alpha Tau Omega sign on the right. Sarah stood right beside me. The Dean
    stood behind me “On this night the moon god Luna will grace us with her presents, so everyone remove your robe
    remove your human skin.” I started to hear growling and cracking of bones as everyone removes their robes
    revealing their bestial forms. Soon everyone except me are werewolves, including the dean and my sister. “Oh
    mighty Luna.” Growled the Dean, “We have a sacrifice for you, please honor us with your presence though sister
    Sarah.” The dean removed my robe. Sarah lets out a growl. The dean continues “Now Luna, give us a sign by
    biting brother Jeramy and remove any impurities from him.” Sarah bit me on the shoulder, it felt
    excruciatingly painful but I showed no signs of pain knowing that it could ruin the ritual. Amazingly the pain
    started to subside as I saw my bruises start to heal themselves, Must be working because I know werewolves
    have amazing healing abilities. “Yes,” the dean growled “Luna accepted Jeramy.” On cue, Sarah let me go with
    her jaw and whispered “When prompted, think wolf and let the change happen, don’t fight it.” Into my ear the
    Dean then growls “Now brother Jeramy, stand and remove your human skin.” I did what Sarah said and thought
    wolf, soon pain began to course through my body as fur began to sprout all over my body. Claws grew from my
    fingers as my hands grew. Pretty soon my feet grew into digigrade paws. I ignored the pain. Pretty soon my
    muscles began to get bigger. I felt something growing from the base of my spine, I felt my tail forming. Ears
    got pointed and moved to the top of my head. My face pushes out as my teeth formed into fangs and my mouth and
    nose became a lupine muzzle, I let out a roar as the transformation is completed. The dean pulls out a full-
    sized mirror and growls “Behold and see your new form brother Jeramy.” I looked into the mirror and see my
    reflection, I looked at my hands and then felt my muzzle and open my mouth revealing razor-sharp fangs. This
    is incredible. I showed my appreciation as I let out a howl of approval. The dean and both Alpha Tau Omega and
    Lambda Beta Pi including my sister howled back.
