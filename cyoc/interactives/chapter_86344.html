    (Author's note: This is a direct Sequel of the Chakat Virus Storyline.)

    It had been near a century since the first outbreak of the Chakat Virus in Fee City. The original team had decided to stay behind. Maurice, Snowy, Sally, Charlie, Jess, and Nichole. Flake, Alexis, Danna and Mikki also stayed along with Sammy and Gloria. Midnight, Talia, Lia and Luna traveled the world. Not to mention their first generation of children stayed behind. But along with the rest of the kats, eighty-percent of them all left. Only those who resided in Janace stayed behind. There was no more traces of the virus in existence. It was changed to a serum to change those who were severely ill, or wanted it. Majority of the world was humans again but cut down to less than five billion people. Knocking it down from ten. After several trips back and forth. Some humans did go with them. It was nice and calm the past year and it would seem that all of them were just as young as they were last century. Chris, the dolphkat had ran for the presidency of the U.S. and successfully won. Hir running mate, Christina became the vice president. Anyway most of the world was finally normal at that time.

    Charlie was walking around their home, seeing some of the places empty. When he looked at the general store, it had shut. It made hir heart sink as it was hir granddaughters' store. Shi had became the copilot with Nemo to go to the new world. Janace and Snowflake's first born children ran it and now it was closed. Shi had started hir own business but was getting ready to go to Seattle for a business meeting. Shi had set up a main headquarters there for it was the only place it'd be approved. What was it shi specialized in? Well, shi had discovered a new plant in Janace and found it worked like a medicine. Shi started to sell it to hospitals and drug stores. Shi did go to med school fifty years back and got hir license to make it. Of course with hir partners Ronnie and Mina.

    What this medicine did was relieve stress like nothing in the world did. Or that's what shi thought. Shi was on hir way to Seattle to meet with the board, of which the two main board members was Ronnie and Mina.

    --------------------------------------

    It was near morning when the mantikat arrived at hir office. Shi walked in and smiled. "Well, hello Ronnie. How's the kids?"

    "The newborns are as wild as the last." Shi smiled.

    "Good. I'm still wondering why you waited over a century to have children." Shi asked Ronnie.

    "Well, we got too busy with the outbreak victims." Shi replied.

    "Well, atleast it's all over. Everyone's relaxed about it." Charlie smiled. They were sitting having coffee looking over. "Well, how's the new product comming?"

    "We found a new ingredient to put in it."

    "Oh?" Charlie walked over and sniffed. "Smells rather pleasant."

    "Oh, it should. It's a flower recently discovered."

    Charlie turned. "Mina. How are you?"

    "I'm well, Charlie. We had someone go look for something new and this is what they found." Shi would point to a strait white flower.

    "Well, shall we go to our meeting?" Shi asks.

    "Yes." Ronnie said.

    --------------------------------

    After the meeting, the new medicine was distributed through out the city of Seattle. Charlie then would stay with Ronnie and Mina overnight before taking the plane back home.

    Come morning Charlie was searching the web and found info about the flower. "Ronnie, look at this."

    "Comorana, native for dragon." Ronnie looked. "Well, I wonder why it's called that."

    "Well, the shape. They believed the flower was showered with the blood of an actual dragon."

    "Wait, you mean like our very own Gloria?"

    "Not quite. You said you found this flower in norther England?"

    "Correct." Mina answered.

    "Well, let's just hope these stories aren't true. We don't need a repeat of the chakat virus."

    Ronnie laughed. "I would agree."

    Mina would flip on the T.V. and the news would show. "Well..."

    A reporter would be on the tube and Charlie would be looking over. "Wha... oh great."

    "What?"

    "Not again." Charlie would be watching, this bringing back a memory of Fee City.

    "Should we get Maurice on the horn?"

    "No, we're going back to Janace ourselves. Looks like it's time to revive the Kat Cops." Shi rubbed hir head. "I wonder what the source is."

    "Well, let's go." Mina said.
