    'But that doesn't matter, Beta-Fran and Beta-Darla aren't leoman, and they're part of Chief
    Gillie's police.... And Chief Gillie cares about them as if they were pride mates.'

    While Ulysses knew it was lupman tradition to make multiple mates, what history he had
    read implied this had come about, or rather, held onto out of the lupman's old habits of
    fights for rank among the different levels of the pack, civil wars BETWEEN different pack
    branches, and of course wars with humans who had seemed to be lupmans direct
    competition.

    For this reason, Ulysses wondered if the whole multiple mates thing had become simply
    outdated. After all, the ranking system was something Alpha-Jensen wanted to do away
    with (which prickled some packmates the wrong way as it was).

    Gillie thought as the four dirty orange cubs hugged her as if she was a beloved aunt. 'I
    wonder if I should have the hypnosis done early... I wonder if I can take all of this before
    the curse is killed... but even if I'm hypnotized to think that my memories of being human
    and a man are all just a dream, the curse will still be there for me to see reality when it
    changes. So I'll be back to be confused, freaked out, and likely question if my 'dreams'
    were really dreams... sigh, I had just best wait until the curse is taken out of commission.'
    Any other thoughts Gillie had on the subject were distracted when the one with wilder fur
    than the rest began to tickle her.
