    Sitting there, tail twitching slowly back and forth, Melissa stared at the note, trying to decide what to do with it. A voice she recognised asked, "What do you have there, princess?"

    Turning, the little mouse girl sees her mother standing a couple feet away from the couch. Before she can say or do anything, the woman strides across the room and taking the note, she says, "Let me see what you have there."

    Scared suddenly, Richard fully in control now, she waits with baited breath as her mother reads the note, wondering what mommy will make of it and whether or not she can see the wish Richard had made.

    Grinning, her mommy turns to her and asks, "Is my little girl playing a game? Very well...Mommy shall join in to."

    Melissa slides off the couch, her skirt sliding up for a moment, but her mother doesn't notice this bit of unladylike behaviour. Instead she has taken the note and headed for the kitchen, while behind her Melissa follows silently.

    Sitting at the table, Melissa's mommy picks up a pen that she had left on the table the previous evening and tapping it against her head, she asked out loud, "What would I wish for."

    Looking at Melissa, she comments, "I could make you human...but I so happy to have a daughter and you are really cute as a mouse girl. So what shall I wish for." A pause and then, "I know exactly what I should wish for."

    Placing the tip of the pen to the paper, she recited out loud as she wrote, "I wish that when Melissa was two...roughly a year ago...someone left for me two point six billion dollars and for Melissa fifteen point nine billion dollars, all tax free. Plus, there was a will with it from an unknown great uncle that explained both me and my daughter where now exempt from paying any form of taxes in the United States."

    Folding up the note, her mommy turned to her and asked, "Now what happens?"

    Melissa just stood there for a minute, wondering what her mommy didn't see her wish, unaware that suddenly the last of her transformation was suddenly occurring. As she thought of what to tell her mother, white fur blossomed across her body until it blanketed her from head to toe. As this took place, her hands and feet shifted from five digits to three, with a thumb still being present on her hands. As the fur began to cover her tail, her feet became more digigrade and she began to rapidly shrink.

    In a matter of seconds, just after her mother had made her wish, Melissa had suddenly fully completed her transformation and had become a three year old Anthropomorphic/Therionthropic mouse girl. Her mind frame and thoughts shifted to that of a very young female and with tail twitching and one of her feet shifting about...every once and awhile she would stand up on the tips of her toes without giving it much thought...she told her mommy, "I don't know. I haven't figured that part out."

    Setting the note down, Melissa's mommy leaned forward and scooped the little mouse girl up, suddenly happy that she had a daughter...even if she was of a different species.

    Nuzzling the girl, her mommy said, "Well, you'll figure it out in time. Now, what does my little girl want for breakfast?"

    As Melissa told her mother what she wanted, the note rested on the table for a bit and then, as if being summoned, it vanished. After a bit, it reappeared at a very familiar middle school and was once again found by another student seeking to make a wish.

    But who was this student? It was....
