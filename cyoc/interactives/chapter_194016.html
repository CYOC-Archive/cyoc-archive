    Gloria to Glenn

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymonium A year ago AP TG

    Inside of Ashley, Charles' sperm, thanks to the magic of the room, copied the DNA of Gloria's biological father (a safety feature that had been installed on these rooms to ensure that even if a guest was unconceived, that they or a version of themselves would inevitably be reconcieved. This wasn't fully advertised to either the guests or the temp mothers as management didn't want it put out there that they were capable of completely eliminating a person from existence like that--that would only get government agencies involved and no one, least of all the sorcerers who'd set up this resort, wanted.

    Ashley was all nerves and frantic the moment after Charles had begun to get control of himself once again through breathing.

    "Age her back up and bring her back out if you want to check, even if you regressed her to before conception, our lovemaking should've brought her right back, right? That's what you always hear the girls telling the guests when they want to be that young."

    Ashley knew Charles was just trying to make her feel better, but Ashley couldn't help but feel that they'd done something wrong. Still she thought about being pregnant and the pregnancy progressing, and with that she could feel something within her begin to grow--and stretch out her belly as she did. With a great sigh of relief as she passed the first trimester and returned to the second one she'd been in at the start of their lovemaking, Ashley felt much calmer... Charles had been right. However that relief didn't calm all her fears, and so she kept on wishing the baby inside her to grow--just to be sure. She passed the five and half months she'd been pregnant with at the start, and soon passed into the third trimester, feeling a rather active baby moving around and kicking inside of her.

    Before she was too large to move properly Ashley urged Charles to help her to the tub in the bathroom and to start running some hot water in there for her to give birth in. The last thing she wanted to do was upset laundry with the messiness of birth that was likely to come.

    With a bit of assistance, it wasn't long before Ashley found herself being guided into the large Jacuzzi sized tub that could fit them both. The water was neither too hot, nor too cold, but just how she liked it, and so she pushed the final weeks of pregnancy forward until she felt a contraction. Charles helped her with the la maz breathing exercises all guests and their partners were required to take--the owners foreseeing the need for these classes with their guests abilities to become so young.

    It wasn't long with the sped up birth that Ashley had found she'd managed to push out her baby, which Charles managed to catch as it swam as naturally in the water of the tub as it had in the womb, and with a gentle grasp, he lifted the baby boy up into the air and gave a gentle but firm tap on its backside to help it to breathe. It was then that Ashley was greeted to the sight which caused her very spine to tingle. Gloria had gone in as a girl, but now had come out as a boy.

    "Ash, what's wrong?" asked Charles as he cut the umbilical cord from the crying baby he held.

    "She is a he now..." said Ashley.

    Somewhere in the suite, the maternity bag that had been Gloria's transformed suitcase transformed to add clothing for a baby boy, and with it a baby book that now listed the baby not as Gloria, but as Glenn.
