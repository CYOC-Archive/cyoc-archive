    You find your self in a giant stone chamber broken by green vines letting natural light in. You were at the top of a set of long stairs. A beam of light cast over your stone form grinning in pride you no longer felt.

    The temple was abandoned and clearly had been for many thousands of years, leaving your expertly crafted form alone with only nature to keep you company. Vines and small critters sprawled the steps before you. It wasn’t hard to imagine you once being a very important goddess in a now extinct civilization.

    A fox of wild mammals really had no place in a modern pantheon. So they placed your form in a more savage time and place. A place long abandoned of worshippers leaving you alone with the mammals as they scurried and hid from larger creatures in a temple of worship now given over to nature.

    This would not stand. You would find a way to be worshipped. Make new followers, even by force if necessary. All would bow before the wild Fia goddess of all warm blooded beings in the jungle and forest. Soon enough.

    You just had to find a way how...
