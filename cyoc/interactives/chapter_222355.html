    A Giant Genie

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover A month ago O Mythological Magic

    The smoke filled a large portion of the area Andrew was in, and when it cleared it revealed, standing before an amazed Andrew, a giant genie. Indeed, this genie was huge. He easily reached three metres at least. His skin was green and though he wore no hair, whether it was facial or on the head, or even body hair, he wore loose Middle-Eastern clothes. He looked down with a self-assured smirk at Andrew who could only be impressed. Impressed, and a bit scared, naturally.
    "Greetings, master," boomed the genie.
    "I... You are... You're a genie? For real?..." stuttered Andrew.
    "Indeed," smiled the genie.
    "And you can grant wishes?" asked the teen.
    "Correct again, young master," the genie said while showing a dazzling white smile.
    "Do I get three?"
    "Of course not! Where does this idea of getting only three wishes come from? No, you get as many as you want, until someone else uses the bottle or you are incapable of wishing out loud. If you were mute, I'd make an exception, of course, but you clearly are not."
    "I see," Andrew said as he pocketed the bottle swiftly.
    Better keep this safe, then. He ten noticed everyone within the arcade was standing still, even in mid-jump or in mid-movement. It was incredibly weird to witness. He asked the genie what was happening.
    "As I am introducing myself to you, I made time stop. I'll return it to normal when we stop conversing together. Or I'll just make myself invisible. But we can wait a bit. Have you a thought about your first wish?"
    Andrew pondered the question with excitement. As many wishes as he wanted! Incredible! He could make a trillion wishes if he so felt like it! Awesome!
    "All right, genie. You master has chosen his first wish!" he said, eyes sparkling with joy.
    The genie smiled, hiding his own anticipation. Andrew did not know that this genie was a very mischievous one and that he had a humongous kink for making his masters shorter, whether through size or age. He loved being a giant and he loved even more twisting the wording of wishes so as to make his masters little and helpless. Not to seriously harm them, but to have fun with them and put them in embarrassing situations. Reducing his masters to helpless little living dolls ripe for tickling was his greatest kink of all, in fact. So he waited for the foolish youth's first wish, ready to entertain himself greatly.
