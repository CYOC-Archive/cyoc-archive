And his ears were now pierced and sported some tiny studs.

    The school was just a few blocks away. Roy felt a wave of happy nostalgia wash over him as he arrived on campus. His own high school days had been considerably happier than the past few rough years he'd experienced. It might be fun to re-live being a carefree teenager.

    Approaching the main school building, Roy spotted a small cluster of girls dressed in cheerleader uniforms identical to his own. They were gossiping amongst themselves, chatting away happily. Roy smiled and wondered if he should try to blend in with his new peers... or should experiment with his new high-tech toy a little more!
