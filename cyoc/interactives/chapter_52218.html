    Ty's Power

    Drinking for Growth

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous 13 years ago BM S

    Parker was about to do his regimen on the treadmill next to Ty when he decided to fill up his plastic water bottle with some cold water first. He walked up to the water fountain and pressed the button, allowing the cold water to come from the spout. He then held the open water bottle right in line with the water stream, filling up the bottle all the way. He then screwed the cap on tight, before he stepped on his treadmill and started walking.

    "Working out today?" Ty asked him, just as he started to go a little faster.

    "Yeah... I'm on the soccer team at school and this is a great way to work out for me... You know... Legs and running and endurance and all that..." Parker replied.

    Ty just grinned as he watched the water take effect on Parker.

    Parker's long, skinny legs already began to show signs of increased muscle size, as he occasionally grabbed his water bottle and took a few sips from it.

    Ty chuckled as he looked on. Parker took his bottle and drank a big gulp of water, as he increased the speed and started running a little faster. Ty glanced over, and noticed how Parker's calves had increased a little bit in size, and his quads had swelled some more.

    Finally, Parked started to slow down, and he eventually came to a stop. He stepped off the treadmill, and he was completely covered in sweat. He walked over to the water cooler, to refill his empty bottle, when Ty snapped his fingers and made him freeze.

    "Soccer player, huh?" Ty grinned. "Let's increase the muscle growth in your legs a bit and make them grow twice as fast..."

    Ty snapped his fingers and smiled as he watched closely. Parked filled his water bottle, and started drinking from it. He was incredibly thirsty!

    Immediately, Parker was overcome by a powerful prickling feeling in his legs! His calves began to swell bigger and bigger. They became harder and harder, until they were as big as footballs! Ty decided to be generous, and grew them a little more!

    His calves continued to inflate, until they became as big as melons. They were like big, round, heavy, and hard spheres of muscle, implanted in the back of the boy's legs, which would allow him to leap and to vault at an amazing speed.

    Finally, thick veins began to surface over the massive calves! Truly, his calves were enormous.

    Next, Ty directed the magic muscle-water to work on his quads. Those thick bands of muscle, located above the knees and below the waist, began to inflate rapidly. Thick layers of muscle began to form on the front of his quads, making them look like giant teardrops. His quads also grew deeper and wider, with thick muscle forming on every side. The added muscle forced the boy to spread out his legs a bit, since he was packing on the muscle so quickly. In fact, his legs were running out of space really quick, so there was no room to go but up!

    Parker's legs no started to grow longer, pushing the rest of his upper body further away from the ground. He was becoming a little taller, thanks to his increasing legs!

    "Okay... your legs are big enough..." Ty laughed. "You're ready to play in the World Cup now!"

    Ty snapped his fingers and the growth stopped, and Parker unfroze.

    Walking on those hyper-muscular legs was like walking on clouds, as the teen slowly strolled back to the treadmill to retrieve his gym bag.

    "Okay Ty, I gotta go... See ya, man..." Parker smiled, as he turned around and walked out the door.

    Ty could only grin as he watched his handiwork walk by.

    "Another happy customer..." Ty grinned, as he turned around and wondered who would be the next person to drink from his water fountain of muscle growth...
