He only wanted to make one of these shopping trips and to just quickly fill Alicia’s list. If she wanted a new handbag he would buy her one. She could always come back and exchange it herself if she wanted.

She took the items up to the till and put them onto Rachel’s room account. “Thank-you for shopping with us Rachel. Should you have any other needs I would welcome you or your sister back” Henry smiled back, took the bags Emily proffered and left.
