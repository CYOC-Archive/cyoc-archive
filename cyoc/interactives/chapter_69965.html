    For Alex, things did not turn out so well. When he was lead into a building he suddenly felt that he was pushed into a narrow box by two farmhands. Then suddenly he felt something shoved into his ass. It was his first meeting with a cattle immobilizer, basically an electric but plug that restrains cattle. Next he felt a hot pain on his flank ad knew he had been branded. Then he felt the same pain where his horns had been as they were removed with hot tongs so he could not hurt anybody with them anymore. But this pain was nothing compared to what came next. He saw the beautiful blond female vet he had noticed before kneel down and then a horrible pain shot through him as shed clamped his balls off using a strong rubber band, a so called Elastrator. She patted his back while pulling out the restrainer from his ass and said in a soothing voice: "Don't worry. The worst is over. This will hurt for a few days and then your balls will fall of just like that. And in a few months you'll make some wonderfull Pastrami." When the gate in front of him opened he was to shocked by this prospect to move. But a sudden shock from an electric prod reminded him that his whishes did not matter any more. Docilely he followed another farm aid to a cattle box and even did not object when he was chained to his place. "By the way, please try to not buckle when you shit as your instincts would tell you.", the aid warned him, "You have an electric line above your ass to prevent that. We want you instead to remain straight and shit into that canal behind you. Less work for us, you know."

    Alex felt humiliated and hurt. He hoped it was all a bad dream but then his dying balls hurt too much to allow for that. Indeed the next morning he woke up to find himself still in the same situation. He felt hungry though and started to munch at the special fattening feed in the trough in front of him. It tasted better than expected. The only downside was when it all came up again and he had to munch it again as was the way for cattle. When he felt the urge to go, he of course forgot about the electric cattle trainer and recived a shock into his butt which for the future reminded him of what to do.

    A few days later his balls finally fell off. Alex even felt a sort of relief at the end of his pains down there. But he also realized that he now really was not much more than a pile of raw meat. He dulled away more and more through the lack of contact and excitement and hardly noticed how he grew fatter and fatter. This is, until one day the truck came.
