On the right is your own teenage male head, with its short and scruffy hair, wearing an expression of horror. Dark red surgical scars and stitches mark where the two heads have been attached to the unnaturally pale body.

If you don't learn to get along with your new friend, we're going to have to call this experiment a failure, and you'll become one of our patients."

    "Okay, you two." Says Dr, Stein, suddenly cheerful, "I'll let you rest for now, then I'll introduce the newbie to your room and your duties as a nurse." She heads towards the door that leads out into the hallway, pausing to dim the lights. "Feel free to talk and get to know each other, after all, you'll be together forever."
