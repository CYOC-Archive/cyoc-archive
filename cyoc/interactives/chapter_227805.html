    Being Invited To A Costume Party

    added by LittleBoy A year ago O

    Before Jeff can decide his attention is suddenly drawn to his mobile phone which suddenly goes off causing the 16-year-old to jump and let out a yelp of surprise.

    Jeff quickly grabs his phone and notices that it’s his best pal Dean, who he knows since kindergarten, “dude! You scared me,” he whines when answering the call.

    Dean can be heard chuckling on the other end of the line, “sorry mate,” he says, “I am calling because, it’s my birthday tomorrow and I decided to do a costume party. I remember you always enjoyed dressing up as much as I do, I especially remember that one Halloween when you went as a Wolf. I remember you telling me that you love those animals and wished to be one… hehe… so, I figured you’d might want to come to my party?”

    Jeff blushes as he is reminded of his childhood dress-ups, “um… y-yeah,” he stammers, “I… I honestly still enjoy dressing up and imagining being an animal. Sadly, there aren’t any stores around that sell high quality and realistic animal costumes, all there is are these cheap and bulky mascot-like costumes”.

    “Yeah, hey! I’m not creeped out or judging you for your fantasies,” Dean says, “I actually think it’s kind of… cute!”

    “DEAN,” Jeff shouts, “cut it!”

    “Hehe, sorry mate,” he says, “anyway, if you can’t come, because you have no costume, I’ll understand. I should have contacted you sooner… hehe”.

    Jeff is silent for a while, ‘I really would love to go,’ he thinks to himself, ‘it’s been a while since I hung out with Dean. It’ll be nice to see him again, but without a costume I… wait! The Chronivac! Maybe I could…’.

    “Jeff,” Dean asks.

    “Dean, I just remembered something,” Jeff says, “would you mind if I would call you back in a few with my decision?”

    “Sure,” Dean says, “talk to you later, bro,” he then hangs up the call.

    Jeff puts his phone down and opens the transformation options tab of the program, “let’s see if I can’t get lucky,” he says to himself.

    The teen scrolls through the various options until he finds a option labelled “costume transformations”, he opens it and scrolls through the various types of costumes, his eyes then sparkle and a grin appears on his face when he finds what he’s been looking and hoping for: “Fursuit Transformations”!
