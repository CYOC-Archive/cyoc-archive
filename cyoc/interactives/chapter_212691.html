Akira was very traditional in his mannerisms, a sort of old fashioned mindset, which Marisa found endearing. The kitsune was polite, respectful, with a protective air about him. During her interviews, she often felt as if she were talking with an uncle or even a serious minded older brother. For this reason, she felt completely at ease with the fearsome warrior.

    "We're ghosts," Sylvia replied. "I'm guessing you could sense that." Akira nodded, watching as the teenage ghost now began to circle him, studying the kitsune carefully. "Wow," she stated in awe. "I thought you were a myth. I didn't think kitsune actually existed." Sylvia now reached out, grabbing one of Akiras tails gently as she ran a hand over it. "Wow it's so soft. Tanya come here, you have to see this. A real live kitsune and he's...kinda of fluffy."

A dhamphir, two wizards, a fairy, a demon, the son of a hunter, and another human. Couple this with the ghosts and Akira could see that the group was quite powerful. Sighing he realized something about them as well.

    "Hunters are quite dangerous as well as resourceful," Akira stated. "I have faced off with only two over my centuries of life. In both cases the fight was quite difficult."

    "Well you did lose to me and Caleb," Spark added, causing the kitsune to frown.

    "I was taken by surprise," he grumbled. "I was not prepared for the fight, I still want a rematch though fairy, when your partner returns from college." Spark looked to respond, only for Connor to throw his hand over his girlfriends mouth.

    "Can you not get into a pissing match with him please," he groaned. Spark frowned, nodding before crossing her arms across her chest as she smirked at Akira. If the fox wanted a rematch, she would gladly accept. At the end of the day whether it was video games or a fight, Spark was super competitive.

    "Mr. Akira," Rei said quietly. "Look, I understand why you would be concerned. We all are but look, Tanya has powers now and Sylvia, well, she's an impressive witch. Just watch."

    Akira now gazed at the pair of ghosts, Tanya smirking as her body began to shift. A small set of horns protruding from her forehead as bat like wings sprung from her back. The ghost now smirked, watching as her curves increased as she struck a seductive pose. "A succubus," he asked.

This guy Kane, he's already hurt too many people. I know you think I'm not ready but I have to try. If he hurts anyone else and I know I could have stopped him, the guilt will just tear me apart."
