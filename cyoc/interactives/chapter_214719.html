The grey slowly swallowed by his thick dark hair, his face becoming smoother as he retreated in age. Quickly descending through his 30s and 20s, Doug and Nancy watched as Luis's body swelled, becoming bulkier yet also firmer. Obviously, despite the injury from his youth, Luis Calderon had tried to maintain his physical fitness.

As he exited his 20s the pair watched as his facial hair began to recede leaving his face smooth. Luis body also slimmed slightly, the man now teen losing the gains from college. Doug was impressed though by his physique.

The teenage boys body was amazing, Nancy's memories recalling being amazed at the six pack he sported.

Doug noticed the jacket sporting Varsity patches for both football and wrestling along with his graduation year. Finally his business slacks transformed into a pair of blue jeans compete with a pair of black Airforce sneakers. The office now shifted once more, Luis Calderons name vanishing from the golden name plate. Luis now blinked, an impish smirk crossing his face as he gazed at the two teens. "Oh man you should of seen your faces," he laughed.

    Doug and Nancy gawked as memories of this new reality came into focus. Luis was the most popular guy in school, as well as a bit of a prankster. The two teens now understood that Luis had set this up to rattle the two lovebirds, sneaking into the Principals office to summon them here.     "For what," Luis exclaimed. "Making out with your girlfriend? Cmon man a Junior and a Sophmore making out in an empty classroom is hardly the end of the world! Now, let's book it before Principal Collins comes back." Doug and Nancy simply shrugged, watching as the mischievous Senior now quickly exited the office. Doug and Nancy now turned to one another, still stunned by what they had done.

    "He doesn't remember," Doug said in awe.

    "I guess that's a good thing," Nancy shrugged. The pair now gazing at the coin wondering what they should do now.
