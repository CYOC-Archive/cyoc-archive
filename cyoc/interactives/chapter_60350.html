    In one community, they had found a different way of dealing with transformation slaves.
    Although the slaves were still kept naked, they didn't want to see a bunch of naked people
    walking around, so the community decided that the slaves should be transformed into
    clothes. This wasn't a law. Some masters wanted their slaves to be able to come and go
    running short errands and so those slaves walked naked beside their masters. But since
    slaves could not travel very far from their masters anyway, it seemed more convenient for
    the masters to wear their slaves as an article of clothing.

    Luke had been interested in transformation and his friend John was about to have an
    eighteenth birthday. Luke decided that he would make a great birthday present for his
    friend. Then he could enjoy and explore transformation all he wanted. Luke liked John and
    trusted him. They had spoken many times about this although Luke never let on that he
    was talking about himself. He always spoke as if he was asking John what John would do if
    he had a transformation slave. So as far as John knew, Luke might give him a
    transformation slave for a birthday present but John didn't know that Luke planned on
    being that birthday present. But Luke had learned enough about John to know that he'd be
    happy as John's transformation slave.

    Among the things that Luke knew is that John, although straight, would not want his slave
    to be his sexual partner and therefore, Luke would stay male. Luke also knew that John
    was not really into age play so Luke would stay somewhere between the age of 14 and 40.
    However, outside of that, Luke could be changed in a variety of ways. Luke was sure that
    he would spend some time as a dog playing fetch or as a horse giving John rides. Luke was
    sure that he would spend some time as John's clothes or other useful items. Luke was sure
    that he would be shrunk sometimes really small or enlarged to giant size. But most of the
    time, John would keep Luke as Luke with only small modifications. For example, John
    thought it would be cool if he needed a lawn chair that Luke could become that lawn chair
    but still retain a mostly human look and even be able to listen and talk to John while being
    a lawn chair, or while John lounged in Luke the lawn chair, Luke could still use his arms to
    give John a massage.

    Luke also knew that John wasn't really into the slave traditions. Some masters liked to yell
    at their slaves or punish their slaves for no reason except the joy of dominating. John
    wasn't like that. John would allow Luke a lot of freedom to do as Luke wanted so long as he
    was around to do or be what John wanted.

    The only thing Luke didn't like about John was that John had a thing for playing humiliating
    pranks on the guys. John was straight and not interested in having sex with guys but there
    were many times when John had depantsed other guys in public or tricked guys into
    embarrassing situations and Luke knew that if he was John's transformation slave, that he
    would be the easiest target for one of John's pranks. Luke knew that he was giving up all
    sense of privacy by giving this power to John, that John would make sure that Luke's
    genitals were displayed often and in very humiliating ways. In fact, John had made it very
    clear that the first transformations made would make it nearly impossible for his slave to
    hide his modesty. He described transforming his slave so that the slave's butt cheeks
    would always be spread wide open letting his asshole be easily seen and making his
    genitals stick out in front of him on permanent display.

    But Luke decided that, despite the certainty of humiliation, being John's transformation
    slave was what Luke wanted to be.
