    "My name is Richard Hark and I agree to the terms and conditions of the Swap-Meet". Richard said in front of the automated camera.

    "Richard Hark. Hmmmm... I don't see your name from the list."

    "Wait!" Richard said. "Can you recheck the list again?"

    "Already did. Back and over. You're name is not on the list."

    An alarm blared in the white clad room. The only source of color, which is the monitor, turned digital white. Motions underneath the floor unseated Richard. He tried to get back on his feet but the floor and walls are shifting.

    Suddenly, multiple glint of red appeared. It surrounded Richard from all sides. He looked at them clearly and realized that they were the blades used at every Swap-Meet. But the blades, they are different. They glow red instead of blue and the fingerprint scanner is gone.

    In one moment, the blades rushed as fast as the wind. It sliced Richard with great accuracy. Before Richard could scream, he realized that he doesn't even have a body anymore. Let alone a neck to house his voice.

    In an instant, Richard was gone. His body parts are collected by the chute and separated according to parts. There was silence. The room came back to what it was before. The once white monitor turned black.

    "Oh dear." The robotic voice said. "Looks like his name is on the list."

    Tim breathed in the heavily sanitized smell in the air. He was new to the 'meet' and he's not going to waste this once in a life-time opportunity to change for the better.

    At first, he couldn't quite believe that such even existed. But when his friend Carl showed him the new arm he got, Tim was ecstatic to come here.

    "On the left, you could see the negotiation booth." The presenter pointed at the three new members of the meet. "That's is where you can look for parts you want. On the right is the Whole Body Exchange. Like the name suggest, you exchange your whole body."

    The warehouse car they ride moved on and lead them to other parts of the event. The place was swamped with people far and wide. There are also androids, like the presenter, walking around and guiding people.

    "On the left people is the latest innovation the company has to offer. Animal Parts Exhange. With cutting edge innovation from our licensed Swap-Blades, we can now graft animal parts to animal recepient. We can guarantee you that we have every animals you can ever think off."

    "On the other direction, you can see another innovation our R&D department invented. VR Worlds is the future. Tired of using our products in the real world? Why not bring our products to fictional ones?"

    "As for the last attraction, our oldest and a forewarning to all non-registered visitors. The thrift shop. Non-registed visitors are chopped to their finest pieces and ripe for your picking. Although, we can't assure your the quality of the products."

    There's a lot of choices to choose from. But Tim know that he can only choose one.
