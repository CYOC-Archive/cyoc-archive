    Logan ran to get his backpack full of space rocks, but Hypergirl grabbed him.

    "Seriously? I have super strength and super speed. Do you really want to do this?" She asked.

    "I do." Logan's new friend said. She got up and punched Hypergirl between the shoulder blades.

    "Ow! You bitch. You have no idea what you got yourself into." Hypergirl said.

    "Try me Ms. Rack-tastic." The woman said.

    Hypergirl picked her up and threw her into the wall. She got up and charged at Hypergirl. As the two collided,
    a huge sphere of light erupted between them, knocking both girls back. Logan's ally got up first and shot
    lightning at Hypergirl. Not expecting an attack like this from who she thought was a civilian, Hypergirl was
    thrown back again, colliding with the wall of the train car.

    "Grab the Radonite and let's get out of here." The electric powered woman said. Logan complied, and the two
    started running for the back of the train.

    By the time they had gotten to the next car, Hypergirl was back on her feet and flying at them. Logan's ally
    closed the door and zapped it, but the electricity did nothing to stop the superheroine. She tossed both Logan
    and his friend aside, and grabbed the Radonite. The woman shot her with another bolt of lightning, stunning
    her momentarily. In that moment, Logan had grabbed the Canister out of the backpack and was running for the
    door. Hypergirl recovered quickly, and tackled him into a booth. Logan's friend jumped on top of her foe and
    cupped her hands over Hypergirl's ears. In a blinding flash, lightning erupted between her hands, zapping
    Hypergirl in the brain.

    "That won't keep her down for long, we need to move." The woman said. Logan was smart enough not to argue, and
    got up to run through the door.

    The pair of villains got out of the train easily enough, then they climbed onto the side of it so they could
    keep going. When Hypergirl flew out the back of the train and in the opposite direction, they jumped off into
    a patch of grass.

    "You still have the stuff?" The woman asked.

    "Yeah. Who are you? How do you do that electricity thing?" Logan asked.

    "You've probably heard of me actually. Name's Circuit." She responded.

    Of course. TransformaZone's version of Livewire. Logan should have known. Of course, that didn't answer the
    even more important question. What would they do now?
