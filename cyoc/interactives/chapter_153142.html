"...and the boxers, if you are turning into a woman I need to see how your little buddy is doing."     "Already taken care of. Not sure how long I can convince them I'm not in any danger or anything, but-"

Get dressed up, get your key, and go to your room. It should have some one size fits all pajamas and clothes for a women so... We'll see how this goes, and come in tomorrow for an examination."
