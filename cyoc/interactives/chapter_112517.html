    Transform or Dare?

    Jenny transforms her class

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago A BM I S O

    "Too bad for you", Jenny said. "For I wish that I'm allowed to do everything, everywhere".
    As if the eighteen year old Ms. Raven had a moment of sudden epiphany, her young face relaxed and her mouth curled insecurely. "Of course", she realized and smiled. "Sorry, Jenny, I forgot it was you. Is there anything else you want to do before we start?". Now that she thought about it, Jenny indeed had to settle some old scores with her classmates. With 31 students constantly being trapped in one room, of course there had emerged some rivalries during the years. In truth, nobody but her friends Kate and Josie got along well with Jenny. Nobody had really acknowledged her superiority in looks and worth. They had solely complained about how full of herself, arrogant and ruthless she would be. Like always, Jenny also didn't care about their views this time. But this time she had power. Power she could gladly use to reduce the size of her overcrowded class.
    Jenny rose and addressed the pupils. "So you wanted the bitch, heh?", she said and smiled. "Here she comes".
    Keen on her ability to change everything into something else, Jenny reached her arm out and pointed at four nerdy boys she barely knew and never had much use for. Two of them turned into glamorous sapphire earrings while the other two boys took the shape of a pair of shiny high heels she had once seen in a store. "I wish to wear them", Jenny said, and so it came true. She riveted on an obese girl next to her who immediately turned into the fitting sapphire bracelet which she had in mind. A crazy idea played in her mind; the idea of completely changing her class.
    "I wish that the people I transform remain conscious in whatever form they assume", she said and started pointing her finger at her classmates like a conductor. Letting her imagination run free, the girl who could wish for everything abused her power by forcing them to shapeshift into all kinds of things: A girl turned green and became a plant in a pot; a boy froze and his cells converted into chocolate; another boy morphed into a rooster while his girlfriend turned into a hen. Jenny giggled as chaos broke out around her, even though a previous wish disallowed others to 'question the changes she made'. However, her classmates realized the horror and some ran for the door. With a glance of Jenny's magical eyes, they quickly shrank to the size of a peanut. A girl's tongue stretched like taffy and turned into a snake while her legs molded together and became a long snake-tail.
    "Don't you see?", Jenny laughed, feeling like an evil witch. "I can do anything to you. Anything". She nodded at a boy who tried to escape through the window, but he instead hit the ground transformed into a cheeseburger. Excited over never having to spend money ever again, Jenny sought for other things she might desire; things that weren't purchasable. She didn't have to ponder long, for Jason Sullivan, the boy she had stalked for over two years now, often was the central theme of her fantasies. And now she had the chance to make her move.
    "I wish Jason to appear in front of me", Jenny said, and her crush, the only men she thought was worth of her beauty, teleported right where she looked at. He seemed a little baffled and confused, then noticed the craziness around him and opened his mouth. Luckily, Jenny knew exactly what to do...
