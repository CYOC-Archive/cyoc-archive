    Heading back to his room, he decided to look through what else he could do in the meantime...



    He looked at the time. 1:20AM. "If she's still awake, then there's something wrong... Alright, It's time." He heads out, and goes back to Alice's room, deciding who he'd put her on while he walked

    Walking in, he finds her asleep, just as expected. "Here we go... I hope she stays asleep..." He goes over to her, and pulls the sheets off of her before stripping her of her clothes. He knew it was time to put her on...
