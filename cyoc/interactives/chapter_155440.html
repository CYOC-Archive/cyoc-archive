    It was a vial labelled
    "Dragoness Fantasy Earth"

    Trthfully Mary didn't know what she was supposed to be
    fantasizing as a dragoness
    but this vil seemed to know and so Mary drank it
    all down.

    It took about three minutes before her changes started
    and somewhat to her distress Mary found she was growing
    in size and her body was becoming less anthropomorphic
    and more like the four footed batwinged flamebreather
    of legend.

    Mary was distressed because she was worried when all was done she wouldn't be herself anymore but a big nasty
    monster that ate humans.

    So because she was distracted with this possibility the
    rearranging dragoness didn't keep track of the fact that the
    rest of the world was transforming as well.
    This was a powerful vial and it not only was changing Mary
    so she could more properly experience life as a fantasizing dragoness but was also changing everyone and everything else
    on Earth to make this a Earth in which
    dragoness's could have to time of their scaly new lives.

    Which meant some humans suddenly found themselves also
    transforming into dragoness's,some stayed unchanged whilst
    quite a few transformed into the dragonish equivalent of 'Prince Charming,the King,the Queen ,etc

    So finally Mary came back to her new senses in a cave
    and was most pleased to discover she felt mentally unchanged
    in any major way.
    After getting used to walking on all fours the dragoness explored
    the area around the cave and was surprised to discover there
    were two other dragoness\s sleeping in a nearby cave......
