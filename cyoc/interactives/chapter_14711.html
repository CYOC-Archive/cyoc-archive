    Fluffy Pink Rabbits

    The sound of Tina giggling made Felix nervous. He was shrinking he could tell that much but his head swam, this wasn't like being changed into a donkey. Then he noticed his hands, slowly changing from hooves to white fuzzy stubs. He remembered then that his sister had wanted to by a giant stuffed animal at the mall, but she didn't have the money. She had asked to borrow it but he remembered what his response was.
    Felix gulped, he new what was happening, Tina had figured a way to set the device to change him into that Pink Rabbit she saw. His body already furry, pink and quite--Felix was stunned to hear himself thinking such things--cute, was nearly transformed. He could feel his mind giving way to fuzziness and dreams of peace and cotton were the last things that Felix felt before the changes became complete.
    Tina ran down the stairs, and into the garage she didn't want her parents asking questions about the enormous pink rabbit she was going to bring up to Felix's room.
    She brought Felix back to the Cronovac and pecked out some commands, looking through the list of mental changes she altered his mental state, setting awareness to 60 percent. Then she looked for telepath abilities as an option, yes she found it! She set Felix to 100 percent.
    "What have you done to me!?" Felix's voice cried in her head.
    "Well, you remember I wanted that stuffed bunny, now you get to be the bunny!" Felix cried out, his mind was screaming.
    "Please change me back, I don't want to be like this. I'll by that bunny for you I promise!"
    "No thanks, I like this bunny much better."
    Felix felt himself being lifted up, being brought to out of his room. He felt sick, being a stuffed toy wasn't something he liked to think about.
    "Tina," Felix heard his mom calling.
    "Do anything to make her aware of this and I'll erase your past human self, you'll be a self aware plushie forever," Tina said squeezing Felix's furry arm.
    "Felix bought it for me, is it great," Tina replied.
    "Thats great, just keep it in your room, its too large to be toted around the house like your other stuffed animals," he heard his mother reply.
    "Okay mom," Tina said back rushing into her room and putting the bunny down on her bed.
    Felix wondered what was going to happen next, was he going to be a Stuffed Rabbit forever? Or would Tina change him back someday, what would happen when his pal's find him missing. These things raced through his mind until exhausted by the day's events he fell asleep.
