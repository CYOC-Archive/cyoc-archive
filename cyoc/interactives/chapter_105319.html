You scream in frustration and bang your fists on the bars. You close the blinds back up and sit on the bed, hanging your head in your hands. You yell out in shock as you feel like you've sat on something. You look at your rear and notice, to your horror, a small nub of a tail, just starting to form, growing out of your tailbone. You scream into the gag again and tug on the nub, but doing so only causes waves of pain.

    "Here I come, my pet!" you hear the girl's voice call. You quickly jump back onto the bed and position your hands and feet exactly as you left them, hoping that the shadows will block the fact that you aren't tied down anymore. The door flies open seconds after you settle and the girl enters, shutting the door behind her. She smiles wickedly and picks up the whip in the corner. "Time for more punishing." She whispers, and saunters over to the bed. She lifts the whip up over her head again, and....
