    "I think I want you to be a girl my age, Wren. Taller than average height, since I'm going to be tiny all my life." Sammy decided.

    I COULD CHANGE THAT, MISTRESS.

    "You could, but I honestly don't mind. I might need a bodyguard though, to fend off bullies, so in addition to being a tall girl, you're going to be strong and tough for your size. Very strong and tough. But slender, so nobody will expect it. Long, curly black hair, I always thought that was very pretty. You should be pretty too, of course. Unlike me, you'll be able to have a great tan, and legs that go all the way up." She grins.

    AS YOU WISH.

    A little girl, tall for her age appeared. She had tan skin, big green eyes, and very pleasing features. There was a strength to the cast of her features, and she had a steely gaze. Long, curly black hair cascaded down her back, and she was very trim and fit, with a pretty good figure for a little girl. "Does this form please you, Mistress?"

    Sammy blushed a bit. Wren was a very pretty girl, and she couldn't help but notice. "Ah, two things. Don't call me 'mistress', I'm your best friend now, Sammy. And two, um, you're naked."

    Wren looked down at herself. "So I am." She made a gesture, and she was now wearing the uniform of the private school they would be attending. The blouse, tartan skirt, and bobby socks looked quite fetching on her.

    'When did I start having feelings for girls? Or a fetish for schoolgirls?', Sammy thought. Could be genetics, she supposed. In her previous life, ok, she'd had some fantasies, but she'd suppressed them. "Wren, do you mind if I check on something?"

    "Of course not...Sammy." Wren had a neutral expression on her face.

    "Could you...would you...kiss me? Like...for real?" Sammy blushed.

    Wren tilted her head to one side, but said nothing, instead, she moved in close, tilting Sammy's chin upwards. Wren was tall enough that Sammy had to stand on tip-toe. Brushing her hair aside, Wren leaned in, and pressed her lips against Sammy's. The touch felt electric, and Sammy felt her lips part as Wren delicately inserted her tongue.

    The kiss was amazing, the best Sammy ever felt. When Wren finally stepped back, Sammy felt weak in the knees, and there was a warm sensation in her crotch. What the hell, she was six, for Crissakes! "Uh...uh...oh wow."

    "Did I perform to your satisfaction, Sammy?" Wren looked down at her.

    "I think...I think you just ruined guys and girls for me. I hope you don't mind being my girlfriend when I get older." Sammy caught her breath.

    "I could be your girlfriend now. I would...like that." A tiny grin formed on Wren's face.

    "Might have to keep it on the down-low. Officially, we're too young." Sammy had no idea how her parents would react to her coming out before the 1st grade!

    "Your body seems to think otherwise, based on your reaction. I suppose it's both a consequence of having an adult soul in a child's body...and of course, the fact your parents are both nymphomaniacs."

    "Gah, Wren...you sound so clinical." Sammy complained.

    "My apologies, my kind don't feel emotions the same way humans do. I am very fond of you, but I'm not certain I can feel love. Does that distress you?" Wren looked concerned.

    "No, not really." She hugged the taller girl. "You do so much for me, I can't help but love you."

    "We will need a way to explain my presence, I...would prefer to be at your side always."

    "Uh, maybe my parents could adopt you? Like, you're the kid of a friend of theirs, but your parents died, which is why you're so...laconic." Sammy's vocabulary had actually improved in her new life. Helps when you have an author for a parent.

    "It is done. We share a room, and a bed. We're young enough that it does not seem unusual. In addition, your affection and kindness has done more to break my 'shell', as it were, than anything else. That explains our closeness, why I do not like being apart, and why I will be so protective towards you." Wren exerted her power. The Ring was really just a formality, at this point, though Sammy wore it always (Wren had made it invisible to others).

    "How protective?" Sammy looked up at her friend.

    "Mother lions will pale when compared to me." Again, that tiny grin.

    "Oh man, school is going to be so much fun!"
