Miggs blinked, and a moment later Regina's upper body turned to face the succubus, sliding effortlessly right up to her and raising herself up to where her head went up passed the height of Miggs.

Miggs took a step forward into the light to be better seen by Regina when the naga slid backwards and to the side to keep her distance. "No, you are not the one. "This cannot be forgiven… my life turned on its head already, I'm a monster now… there is nothing left for me here," she said, motioning out the barn door towards the house. "I'm to loose my home and family and now my chance to be human?" Her three hands not holding the vial all clenched up into fists. "Were it within me, I would kill you where you stand… but I refuse to be the monster you have made me," she said, realizing that this succubus had revealed she was the same as the one who transformed her and thus must have changed her appearance.

Pacing for a turn, she watched as Regina's head-snakes followed her movements for a time before getting bored and mulling about behind her head again. Miggs looked back towards Regina and said, "I can discover if he is cheating on you." Regina's head turned to look at her. "It will not repair the damage I have done to you… your altered form… but I feel that your pain has driven you to murder. Would your pain not be even greater if you discovered you were wrong, later?" she asked.

He is a monster, a rat, and I would have no trouble discarding his corpse as I would any other rodent from the kitchen," she said, glancing away. "I don't know why," she said, though Miggs guessed. In Regina's current form, Oscar would probably just about fit for her dietary requirements… "If he had betrayed me, I would surely want him dead. If he lives, he will leave me and take all he owns and I will be alone in this world," she sighed, knowing she could never return to her parents home. Not like this.

She put a hand on Regina's shoulder, but also another hand onto her head. "But I must ask you… if I discover that he has been unfaithful to you… may I drain away his life force? You were, perhaps, going to kill him anyway… and if I do not have a male now after my transformation, I will die," she said, looking into Regina's eyes.

Her eyes closed as the fire of the change burned within her, demanding it be completed with the energy of a man. But part of her didn't care for her husband as much as it had when they were first married. She knew in her heart that Miggs would find Oscar guilty… and if he was, then there was little else she could do. Here in the land of Vermillion, she would be lucky if the men in charge of the laws would even hear out here case. If Oscar didn't get what he deserved this way, then he would never be brought to justice.

    Regina's tongue flicked out as she tasted the farm air. Turning back to Miggs, she nodded her head, "If he has been unfaithful… but… if he doesn't love me… then it is all that is befitting of him," she sighed. "He will meet no other justice here in Vermillion."

    "Vermillion?" Miggs asked, opening her eyes back up and staring at Regina. "We are in Vermillion?" This is no good, she thought, Vermillion is nine days travel from Quell. We'll have to leave right away! "We have little time then. I know of the lawlessness of these lands. Oscar will be seen to, and I will drain him," she said, hoping Regina didn't question that. At this point, she would not make it to another man in time… she would have to drain Oscar or Yaun. "If there is nothing else for you, Regina, then I would ask you come with us. The prince and I seek to cure ourselves of our forms. Yaun too. We would be happy to see you returned to normal as well," she flinched as the pain returned to her head. "It's my fault, and I need to repair the deed. Please, come with me… I have little time," she said as she began walking quickly to the house.

The little-rat man had shifted form into something more finished since the last time she had looked him over. He was wearing a kitchen apron as a shirt on his much smaller frame. Only once Miggs stepped over to him, moving passed Yaun, did she understand his desires, It was then that she knew where he stood with Regina.
