    "Oh, Mr Robinson." She cooed, "Come in, I believe Alex and Marcus are out by the pool,

    She led the way through the house through a large glasshouse into the garden at the rear

    motioned for Mr Robinson to take a seat.

    "They were here a moment ago, I wonder where they can have got to?"

    She pulled Alex aside out of earshot of Mr Robinson and spoke to Alex in a conspiratorial
