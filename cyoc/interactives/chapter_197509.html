    "I know!" Ash couldn't contain his chuckle as he thought about it more. "How wild do you think they're going to get? It's supposed to take 2 weeks but how long til they really give into their animal sides?"

    "Dunno," Ethan said. "But I can't wait to see it happen. Maybe they'll burst out of their clothes. Or maybe they'll just stop wearing them! Wouldn't that be great?"

    Ash laughed at the thought of the jocks running around campus without any clothes on. "But is it going to turn them fully into an animal or just like one of those furry things online? Like one of those guys who wears mascot costumes all the time?"

    Ethan shrugged his shoulders. "Don't know. But I guess we have two weeks to find out. Eh?" He pulled up into Ash's driveway and then the two went inside. Margie was sitting on the couch. "Hey there hot stuff," Ethan cooed.

    "Dude," Ash gave him a swift punch to the arm. "Leave my sister alone." Margie looked like she tried to ignore Ethan. She kept her face buried in her phone as he spoke. Acting as though she was texting one of her friends.

    "C'mon... I'm feeling good today. Why can't I have a little fun," Ethan walked around the couch. "Maybe she wants a real stud," he added a flex.

    Margie glared at Ash. He knew she hated it when Ethan would come over. "Boys," she groaned as she got up and went to the kitchen.

    "C'mon babe!" Ethan went after her, barreling through Ash. "Just give me a chance! I'm way better than Brian."

    "Ashley!" Margie called from the other room. "Get your friend. Now!"

    "Ethan!" Ash yelled. "Just drop it!"

    Ethan glared. "Fine. I don't want to be with some stuck up bitch anyway."

    Ash punched his friend for real this time. "Quit it," he ordered. Ethan's face soured, but he listened. All Ash could do was apologize to his sister, but she seemed less than enthused to hear it. It would probably be best just to drop it and move on as quickly as possible. Ash quickly grabbed a couple of Gatorades out of the fridge then headed back towards his room. However, when passing the front door he noticed Ethan's backpack was still over there. A bit of anger rushed through him as he thought about how annoying his friend could be. And if they were going to use those samples on the jocks, then why not him? Turnabout being fair play and all.

    Ash rushed over and grabbed a vial. He poured it into the Gatorade and headed back to his room. Ethan had turned on a video game. But while it was loading he must have gotten board as his shirt was off and he was currently flexing in front of the mirror. "What's your sister see in Brian anyway?" Ethan grumbled. "I'm tall. Somewhat hot. Pretty sexy. And I work out. Just look at me." He struck another pose. "What's Brian got that I don't?"

    "My sister's interest?" Ash answered.

    Ethan shot him a glare and snatched the Gatorade from Ash. "Whatever," he plopped down onto the beanbag chair and grabbed a controller. Ash plopped down next to him and watched as Ethan took a drink. Now he was just curious how long before the potion took effect. But he realized he didn't look to see which one. 'Oh well, guess I'll just have to wait to find out...' Ash thought to himself.
