    Jeff grinned when he realized what he had done to Emily, his little 10-year-old sister. He had changed her into a little boy after he had made her shorter, and now he looked like a little tomgirl. He almost considered leaving Emily that way, until his mom sat down with Emily and had this conversation, which Jeff overheard since the door to his bedroom was left wide open.

    “Hey, Evan. How was everything at school today, buddy?” mom asked the boy, who now had taken on the name Evan.

    “Umm… I kept on getting picked on by Greg Grumble! He is so mean! I hate him!” Evan hissed. “He’s nothing but a big bully towards me!”

    “Why is that sweetie?” mom asked the younger bro.

    “He keeps on making fun of me because I’m the smallest kid in my class and I just hate that! Can you put me in another class, mom?” Evan just sighed. “Maybe I can stay home and be homeschooled by you?”

    “I’m going to have to call Miss Woodland again. She needs to keep you away from that bully.” Mom sighed as she took her cell phone and called the school.

    “Uh oh, shrinking him down so much wasn’t such a good idea after all!” Jeff mumbled. He had made Emily shorter and now, as Evan, he is being teased about being short in school. “I gotta help my little bro. Hmm…”

    Jeff turned towards his PC, and he pulled up Evan’s new stats on the Chronivac app. Evan was still shown as 10-years-old and only being 3-foot-8 inches tall.

    “Even if I make him taller, that won’t stop the bullying.” Jeff mumbled as he adjusted the height to 4-foot-8 inches to his little brother. “I gotta do something to stop the bullying in school.”

    Then, Jeff saw the options for muscle and strength on the Chronivac screen.

    “Aha! What if Evan suddenly decided he wanted to take up weightlifting rather than spend all his free time playing video games?” Jeff grinned.

    Jeff made some adjustments to Evan’s profile, including adjusting “Muscle growth potential” and “Muscle growth following exercise” to 90%.

    “It will become very easy for the little bro to pack on some muscle from now on. Plus, he will just LOVE lifting weights in the home gym in our basement and he will totally lose interest in video games.”

    At that very moment, Evan dropped the XBOX controller and he rushed down the stairs to the home gym, which sat mostly unused. It was only their dad who used it on an occasion after spending thousands of Dollars on a smith machine, barbells, dumbbells, cables, a weight bench, a treadmill, a preacher curl, a leg extension machine, and other gym equipment. Even changed clothes and put on shorts and a tank top before he started his daily workout in the gym, which was long and grueling. He worked out every single muscle group every day, pushing his own body to lift more weights all the time while growing and becoming stronger very quickly.

    When Evan walked back up the stairs, he was hot, sweaty, and his muscles were bulging all over! No longer was he the short, skinny kid in the house. Now, Evan sported big, round pecs, thick, muscular arms, he had a ripped six-pack, while sporting broad shoulders. His quads were as thick and as wide as steel pillars and his calves were massive. The kid started to look like a junior bodybuilder.

    Mom had just hung up the phone when Evan got back on the couch.

    “I’m very proud of you, Evan. That Greg kid got suspended from school after you stopped him from bullying another boy in your classroom. He was pushing this kid around and I guess you put a stop to it by shoving him against the wall. I guess he’s scared of you now, huh?” mom smiled.

    Evan just raised up his fist and flexed his arm, causing a huge bicep to swell up.

    “Yup! Greg doesn’t mess with me anymore!” Evan cheered. “I’m too strong for him now!”

    “Well, get showered and put some clean clothes on! You are all hot and sweaty!” mom ordered.

    “Alrighty, mom!” Evan just smiled as he sauntered his way into the bathroom.

    Jeff watched the exchange and wondered how he could change Evan even more?
