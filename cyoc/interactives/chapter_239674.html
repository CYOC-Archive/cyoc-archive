After saving Rocky’s change, Henry switches over to Rex’s the alligator lizard’s file and changes his age from three to 12 and his species to human, then changes his name from “Rex” to “Max”. Once the reptiles changes have been saved, Henry opens the transformation tab and selects the option “animals”, then “feral”, then “reptile” and finally “children’s python”, and connects Bill’s file to the chosen transformation. A new window opens in which Henry does some changes, changing Bill’s age from 11 to two and activates the animal instincts, but sets them to medium, ‘having the instincts at medium, shouldn’t influence Bill’s human thinking and mind,’ he thinks to himself, he then changes Bill’s name from “Bill” to “Slither”, before saving that change and then focusing on Jaden.

    “Y-yeah… that’ll be best,” Jaden says, also blushing hard.

Tails begin to grow from Bill and Jaden, Jaden’s tail merges with the boy’s crotch, hips and butt, while Bill’s tail seems to merge with the boy’s legs. Noticing his legs merging together, Bill decides to lie on the floor, his arms already shrinking back into his body, while arms and hands begin to grow from “Rick’s” changing body, while his tail seems to split down the middle, already looking like feet and legs. Jaden lowers himself onto his hands when he feels his spine force him to lean forward, “Max” meanwhile looks less like a lizard and more like the human boy he’s becoming.

    While the changing boys hairs retreat back into their heads, blond hairs seem to grow from “Max’s” head, while brown hairs grow from “Rick’s”. “Max’s” eyes change color from yellow to brown, and “Rick’s” change from golden to green both their pupils become round.

    “Lizzi’s” eyes become golden and slitted, while “Slither’s” become yellow and slitted. Both their faces push out into muzzles.

    After almost an hour, the transformations finish and the two new human boys carefully get to their feet, swaying slightly since they aren’t used to walk upright.

    Henry gives the four a few minuted to adjust, “so, how are you guys feeling,” he asks.

    “Weird,” “Max” says, his eyes widen in surprise, covering his mouth with his hands, “I… I can talk!”

    “Rick” chuckles, “well, of course you can talk,” he says, “man, so weird hearing my voice without the hiss,” he chuckles.

    Henry smiles, “looks like you two are feeling just fine,” he says, “why don’t you try walking on two legs, while I check on your “pets”?”

    “Max” and “Rick” nod, beginning to take small steps as they begin walking back and forth.

    Henry smiles, “you’ll soon get used to your new bodies,” he says, “let’s put you back in your terrariums where you’ll be safe”.

    After putting the new reptiles back in their terrariums, Henry turns to the two new human boys, “so, what would you guys like to do?”
