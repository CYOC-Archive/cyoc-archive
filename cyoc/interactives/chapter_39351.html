    Mr. and Mrs. Ford took you and Emily out to their van and buckled you into booster seats. They gave each of you a juice and then you were off to your new home.

    As he was driving Mr. Ford kept glancing nervously in the rear view mirror. "Will you stop that?" Mrs. Ford asked.

    "I'm just making sure that we're not being followed" Mr. Ford said.

    "Acting like that will only draw attention to us" Mrs. Ford said. "Now stop constantly looking in the rear view mirror."

    "Alright I'll try" Mr. Ford said.

    Mr. Ford was able to cut down on looking in the rear view mirror and soon you were pulling up to a house outside of the city limits. Mr. and Mrs. Ford took you inside and up to a bedroom.

    The bedroom didn't look like it was meant for little girls. There was a bed that grown-ups slept in and the walls were plain white. Mr. Ford left and Mrs. Ford said "Ok girls, let's get you in some new clothes."

    "I like this dress" you said. "I want to keep wearing it."

    "Me too" Emily said.

    "We're going out to eat and those dresses are too nice to get dirty" Mrs. Ford said. "You don't want to get those dresses messy now do you?"

    "No" you and Emily say together.

    "Then you need to change clothes" Mrs. Ford said.

    Mrs. Ford helped you and Emily out of your dresses. She even had you take off your panties and the ribbon in your hair. As both you and Emily were standing there naked Mrs. Ford seemed to be listening to something. "We need to hurry or we'll be late for dinner" she said. "Put these panties on." She hands you a pair of panties with the Little Mermaid on them and Emily gets a pair with Hello Kitty on them. Next you're both dressed in pants that just pull up and have no buttons or zippers. You get a pink short sleeve shirt with Princess on the front and Emily gets a yellow one with Belle on the front. Mrs. Ford puts socks and shoes on you. Your shoes are pink with Princess on them and Emily's are pink with Disney Princesses on them.

    "Ok time to go" Mrs. Ford said and leads you out of the bedroom. She and Mr. Ford put you in your booster seats and you pull away from the house. After a few moments Mrs. Ford said "I think we're in the clear."

    "You removed everything the girls were given?" Mr. Ford asked.

    "Yes I did" Mrs. Ford said.

    "We still have the band-aids we got when we woke up" you said.

    "Rats!" Mrs. Ford said. "I overlooked those. Take them off girls and hand them to me. We'll get you new ones if you want."

    Something in Mrs. Ford's voice said that you needed to do as she asked. You peeled off your band-aid and handed it to Mrs. Ford. Emily did the same and Mrs. Ford threw them out the window.

    "Did they give you girls anything else?" Mrs. Ford asked.

    "No" you and Emily replied.

    "Good" Mrs. Ford said. "Let's hope they haven't spotted our new van. Should we explain what's going on to them?"

    "What do you girls remember about your past lives?" Mr. Ford asked.

    "What's a past life?" you asked.

    "Their memories are still blocked" Mr. Ford said. "We should wait until the serum works."

    "They're going to be confused if we don't tell them" Mrs. Ford said.

    "With their memories still blocked they'll be confused by what we tell them" Mr. Ford said.

    "I think I can explain it so they understand" Mrs. Ford said. She turned around to face you and Emily and said "We're hoping that your memories will return soon. Right now you need to know what's going on. We're with a group that helps kids like you. The people who you saw are bad people. They were going to send you to a place where you would have been hurt. The clothes they gave you and the band-aids would have allowed them to find you. We're going someplace where you should be safe. I'll tell you more when your memories return."

    Mrs. Ford turned back around and you drove in silence for a little bit. After a bit though you said "I need to go potty."

    "Me too" Emily said.

    "We might as well stop and get something to eat" Mr. Ford said.

    You pulled into a McDonald's and while Mr. Ford was ordering your dinner Mrs. Ford took you and Emily to use the restroom. Once done you sit down to eat. You and Emily each had a Happy Meal and it filled you up. Once you were done eating you played with the toy a bit. As soon as Mr. and Mrs. Ford were finished eating you left and were on your way to where ever it was they were taking you. You didn't know if their story about the people you had met so far being bad was true but you couldn't help but trust them.

    You fell asleep about a half hour after you ate and only partially woke up when Mrs. Ford picked you up and carried you into a house. You felt her lay you down, take off your clothes, and put pajamas on you. She told you good night and you fell back asleep.
