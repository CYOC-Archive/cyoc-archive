    Meanwhile Tyler started to wake up in a pitch black room.
    "Ugh where am I?" Tyler says getting up in the darkness.
    "You are where you have pushed yourself to be. Yet you are unhappy I can sense it" a voice comes from somewhere in the darkness
    "Who are you?" Tyler asks waving his arms around trying to find any objects around him.
    "I am whatever you want to call me a witch, sorceress, daemon, but none of those titles are actually what I am." the voice responds
    "And what is that?" Tyler asks
    "Lets not worry about depressing details. The important question is who are you?" The voice asks
    "Tyler... so can you please tell me what the hell is going on?!"
    "Tyler, why did you come here?" the voice asks
    "Because it seemed funner at the time then going to the beach"
    "And that was reason to barge into my house?"
    "Look we didn't know this place even existed when we decided to come let alone there actually be someone in the house, I didn't really want to come even I just thought it was a better idea then the beach."
    "You mentioned tis 'beach' again why do you speak of it with such distaste"
    "Because as you probably saw from when you dragged me in this room and dragged my friends wherever you put them, that I look puny next to them. I hate looking the worst of all my friends"
    "Interesting perhaps I could help you"
    "What do you mean?"
    "Don't waste time thinking about how just think about if I could make you look the best of your friends would you do it?"
    "Who wouldn't!"
    "Well then go put something on, grab that pole and follow me outside." the voice says as a door opens a ways off letting light and a cold breeze into the room.

    "Wait what do you mean put something on..." Tyler says then shivers, looking down he realized he wasn't wearing anything, and that the cold air wasn't helping.
    "What the hell?! What kind of perverted lady are you?! You took my clothes off!" no answer "What am I supposed to wear" he says looking around next to what looked like a pool scrubber was a speedo.
    "You've got to be kidding..." Tyler yells "Fine I'll wear it but I better not catch you watching me" Putting on the speedo Tyler found it to be incredibly loose threatening at any moment to fall off. Cursing the woman under his breath he heads outside to find her.

    The light was blinding for a moment since Tyler had been in the dark for a while, but as his eyes focused he saw he had just come out of a little shack behind the house that was next to a uncared for grimy pool.
    "So now what?" Tyler asks the old woman next to the pool
    "Now you clean" she says gesturing to the pool
    "You kidding? I'm not doing that! Give me my clothes back I'm leaving" Tyler says throwing the poll down and storming to the old woman.
    "Clean the pool and the body you seek is yours. The more you clean the closer your body will be to what you desire, as for your... attire it will fit soon enough" the old woman replies.

    Tyler had a look of doubt on his face, but surprisingly he picked the pool scrubber back up and started scraping the algae off the pool walls. Tyler was doubtful for the first hour or so of scrubbing and cleaning. Finishing cleaning a small corner of the pool he sighs and looks for the old lady. Walking to the old mansion he sees his reflection in the window. Surprised at first thinking it was someone else looking at him he realizes the reflection was him! He looked bigger, better, and more handsome then before. A grin came across his face as he continues to clean imagining what he would look like in the end, and what his friends would say when they saw him. Tyler ran back to the pool picked up the pole and continued to clean the pool almost as if he was in a trance.
