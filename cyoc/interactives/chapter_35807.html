    That night after the party Declan was taking his bath and thinking about his life now. Steve had said that their friendship would be awkward now that Declan was a seven year old girl, people might talk. He knew that Steve was right about that which put him in a depressed mood.

    The bathroom door opened and Declan's mother came in asking "Ready to wash your hair Daisy?"

    "Mom, I can wash my hair by myself" Declan said.

    "You are a seven year old girl now and your hair is longer than what you're used to" his mother said. "I want to make sure all the shampoo is washed out. Besides, I had washed Katie's hair until her birthday this year. If you remember I had washed your hair until you were nine the first time around. So you have two years of me washing your hair unless I transform you into a baby girl."

    "Please don't change me into a baby girl Mom" Declan pleaded.

    "Well then, you'll let me wash your hair" his mother said.

    "Alright Mom, you can wash my hair" Declan said in a defeated tone.

    "That's my girl" his mother said while she reached for the shower head. "I know you're sad about your friends not believing you but you wanted to be a girl. Now you have to live with that choice."

    "Could I be a teenage girl Mom?" Declan asked.

    "I've given that some thought and I don't think it's a good idea" his mother said.

    "Why not?" Declan asked.

    "Because your hormones might lead you to do something that could have serious consequences" his mother said.

    "I can handle them" Declan said. "I was a teenager before."

    "I know that sweetie" his mother said, "but you're a girl now. Your hormones are different now and when you're a teen they'll be raging through your body. Being thrust into that instead of growing into it could mess with your judgment."

    "But you had thought about it" Declan said.

    "Tell you what sweetie, after we get your paperwork done tomorrow I'll let you become a teen for a day" his mother said. "If you can handle it then I'll let you stay a teen. Will that be ok?"

    "I guess it will be" Declan said.

    While they had been talking his mother had washed his hair. She rinsed his hair one more time then said "Time to get out and get ready for bed."

    Declan didn't argue with his mother as he got out of the tub and wrapped a towel around himself. His mother helped him dry his hair and brush it.

    When Declan entered his room he saw that his mother had put out a new pair of white panties with pink hearts and a pink nightgown. Declan put them on and went to bed thinking about tomorrow when he would be a teen again.

    Declan's mother woke him up the next morning and helped him get dressed. She put him in a cute yellow sun dress and braided his hair. They then went down to breakfast.

    After a silent breakfast the two of them go to get Declan's name and birthday legally changed.

    It took Declan's mother awhile to find the office since it wasn't one that saw much traffic. It was in a new state of the art building on the outskirts of town. Declan couldn't help but notice that it was close to where he found the medallion.

    Once inside they were quickly shown into an examination room since there was no one else in the office. Declan had hardly sat on the examination table when a woman in her mid 30's came in. "Hello, I'm Dr. Mezzner" she said. "I understand that your son has been transformed into a young girl."

    "That's right" Declan's mother said.

    "And this is your son?" Dr. Mezzner asked nodding at Declan.

    "Yes" his mother said.

    "How did this happen?" Dr. Mezzner asked.

    "He found a medallion and decided to transform himself using a pair of his sister's panties that he had taken two years ago" his mother explained.

    "OK" Dr. Mezzner said as she jotted down a few notes. "Now, you want to change his name from Declan to Daisy and his age from 16 to 7. Is that correct?"

    "Yes it is" his mother said.

    "OK, do you have the medallion with you so we can verify that it did transform your son?" Dr. Mezzner asked.

    "Yes I do" his mother said as she reached into her purse. She brought out the medallion and showed it to Dr. Mezzner.

    Dr. Mezzner held a strange device over the medallion and then over Declan. "Well this medallion did indeed transform you" she said. "Now I want you to take off your clothes so I can examine you."

    "Why do you need to do that?" his mother asked.

    "It's just routine to make sure everything is ok" Dr. Mezzner said.

    "Alright" his mother said. "Daisy, do what Dr. Mezzner says."

    "OK Mom" Declan said as he started taking off his clothes.

    Once he was naked Dr. Mezzner said "Now just lay back and relax. This won't take long."

    Declan did as Dr. Mezzner told him. He laid there for a few minutes until Dr. Mezzner told him he could get up and put his clothes back on.

    Once Declan had his clothes back on Dr. Mezzner said "Everything looks OK. We'll get the paperwork done and filed today. You were a good girl Daisy so you get to pick out a little something."

    Dr. Mezzner opened a drawer and Declan picked out a pink plastic bracelet.

    On the way home Declan decided that now that his name and age was legally changed, he should start referring to himself as a girl and her new name.

    "Since you were good I'll keep my word and let you become a teenage girl for today" her mother said.

    Daisy thought it over and decided that her mother was right. If she suddenly became a teenage girl she may do something stupid. "Mom, I want to remain this age for a couple more days to get used to being a girl" she said.

    "Alright sweetie" her mother said. "We'll wait a couple of days."

    Daisy sat back and was determined to make the most of her situation.
