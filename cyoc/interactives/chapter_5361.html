It tried in vain to escape from the clutches of the vines but it was useless. It was outmatched and offically caught.

She was magical indeed and she faught very well with the magical book that lay on the floor. It was tempting to look inside it but your attention was back on the wolf when Sarah yelled a commanding phrase to the wolf that seemed to have a charm to it.

    "Speak!" She said and the creature cooed and whimpered in defeat.

    "Why for you capture me in such a manner?" The creature asked in a husky wretched voice.

    "You were going to eat us!" You answered quickly.

    "Oh really? It was you who attacked me," The creature protested.

    "No, you were leaping for Sarah's throat and tried to kill her," You quickly fired this at the creature in defense.

    "Sarah? I no eat Sarah. I see no Sarah here! Only a girl and boy," The creature growled. "A very tasty and juicy girl and boy," the wolf added and licked it's fangs.

    "Silence," Sarah said.

    "You ask me to speak and now you silence me? Make up you mind, girl. How long do you plan to keep me entangled, anyways. I will not harm one that can stop me in mere minutes," The wolf growled.

    "I will not ask you again. Where is my grandmother?" Sarah demanded.

    The creatured cried and whimpered. "She is dead! All are dead. I ate them! I ate them! They were good and juicy and I ate them when they least expected it! I ate them! I ate them! I ate them!"

And it was on a page that seemed very interesting to the situation you were in. In fact it might have given Sarah hope. It was opened to a resseruction spell and you lowered your head to Sarah's ear.

    "We could bring them back." you whispered.

    Sarah heaved and could barely understand your words.

    "We could bring them back. Your magical, Sarah. You can do anything," You say to comfort her.

    "Some magic is forbiden. Everything has a consequence," She sighed and whiped her tears. "Right now, all I want to do is to rip the creature into shreds," she gritted her teeth.
