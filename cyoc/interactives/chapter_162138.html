Thankfully she didnt need to wait much longer as Kellie charged forth, knocking several Voids back. "Took you long enough," Katie smirked.

Kellie would have a clear shot at whatever this creature was, focusing her magic into the shield she had conjured she intended to deliver what she believed to be a death blow.

A lesser creature would have had their head taken off, for Malak though it simply turned his head to the side.
