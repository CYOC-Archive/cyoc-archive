    a puberty starter be?

    Jeff had already been in puberty for a few years, being 16, so he doubted the package would actually be useful
    to him but he was curious so he clicked it. He could always go back to the endless list of other packages.

    Welcome to the Puberty Starter Package!

    Unlike the other packages we offer, this package is best explained with examples. It is a more invasive option
    than many of our other packages or making individual modifications.

    Please select an available target for an in depth explanation of effects
    Jeffry (Jeff)

    Jeff was more intrigued than ever as this was the first package he encountered whose effects weren't obvious.

    His was the only name on the list as his sister had gotten tired and went downstairs. After picking his name,
    the emitter kicked into action taking a second to re-read his stats and then next screen appeared.

    Thank You!

    The target you selected is in the middle of puberty, the first option is to select a age past 11.386 for
    puberty to begin. This will lessen or remove the primary and secondary sexual characteristics but will not
    change the subjects age. The subject will appear younger but will still be 16.247 years old.

    primary and secondary sexual characteristics pushing the subject's puberty status towards or past completion.

    Based on the selected profile, the target can be modified to appear anywhere between ages 10-30. Family,
    friends, and anyone that knows the target will treat them like they are 16.247. However, those who don't
    specifically know the target will treat them like they are [age not selected] years old.

    Specific goals our testers in the target's age group primarily used this function for:
    Appear slightly older for sports performance/social status

    Appear younger to minimize female monthly issues
    Appear older to obtain restricted content including: Movies (R-NC17), Alcohol, Drugs, Pornography

    Note: Using this option automatically sets world's and target's perception and reality to believe the changes
    are normal to facilitate integration. If the target is the user you will have the option to maintain knowledge
    of previous history for future reversion if desired.

    It finally made sense to him. The age progression changed where he was in his life. If he became 21 he would
    no longer have 16 year old friends in high school, he would have new 21 year old friends in college or work.
    Additionally he would be expected to support himself. If he didn't change the world perspective on an age
    modification like that he would run the risk of an awkward encounter with a family member.

    With this option he will still be 16, able to hang with his friends and go to highschool, but he would be an
    adult or child to strangers so long as he acted as old as he looked.

    Below the personalized description was an entry box saying "Please select an age to appear between 10 and 30:"
    and below that was a box saying "Retain minimal memory of original life: X"

    His mind swam with possibilities and fun he could have with this but he also had concerns, he decided to type
    in a number and see what would happen.
