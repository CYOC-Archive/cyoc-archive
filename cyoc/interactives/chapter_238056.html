    The Tickler Tickled

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 9 days ago A S O Shrinking Magic Feet

    While Tarzan swings rapidly from vine to vine, you see the ground rush as a blur below you. You are firmly attached to Tarzan's floor but it is still scary. The speed does have the advantage of dissipating the foul smell of Tarzan's foot away from your nostrils. It is disgusting and it feels like you are rolled up in dirt. No wonder, because Tarzan's feet are all grimy and brown and green with dirt and stains. He never washes his feet! Can't he wear shoes?
    And even as you move just a little, the jungle man feels the ticklish sensation and twitches his foot. He lets out a little giggle too. So the jerk of the jungle is ticklish? This gives you a wicked idea. Though you cannot do anything now, as the ape man is still using his arms and hands to travel across the tropical forest. Indeed, if he plummets down to the ground, you are sure to be squished to death like a lowly insect. That would be horrible, so you can only bide your time and watch as the ground still rushes past at what seems like the speed of lightening. You can't wait for this sick traveling to end.

    At long last, it seems, Tarzan enters his home in the trees, thankfully landing on the foot you aren't attached to. As your head stops turning, you decide to act. You grab his sole and toe and scratch them something fierce. And it works! At once the muscular mighty ape man starts roaring in laughter and hopping around, giggling uncontrollably. Soon, he is on his back, desperately trying to reach you and gears streaming down his eyes, shaking like mad because of spasms of laughter. You continue, delighted to torture the man who made you miserable. Revenge feels good!
    Alas, it stops when Tarzan manages to reach for you and to untie the rope and grab you. He stops laughing while you are a prisoner of his hand.
    "Tarzan knows you bad man, sent to be punished," he wheezes, furious. "And Tarzan will punish you. Now you be the tickled. And Tarzan will tickle you silly!"
    His eyes gleam with wicked thoughts, and you wonder what he has in mind. Something not good at all for sure...
