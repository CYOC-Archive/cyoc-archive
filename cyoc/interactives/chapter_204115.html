    The Magic Shop

    Tricia Plays Dress Up

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago O

    Before doing anything else, Tricia wanted to see if the remote had the option to allow her clothing to be changed as well. As excited as she was to alter herself, Tricia didn't want to accidentally ruin any of her clothing during the transformation process. Looking through the clothing options on the remote, Tricia eventually found the button to allow her outfit to auto change with her and switched it on. Afterwards, Tricia decided on her next transformation.
    "Since I already have clothing options on anyway, might as well try it out and see if it works." Tricia said to herself.
    Thinking it over for a bit, Tricia decides to change her current attire into a ball gown (because it would be the most drastic). After going through and making a list of all the changes she wanted to make, Tricia settled on a light blue ball gown with puffy sleeves and a large white ribbon tied on the back, white silk bra and panties, two layers of petticoats, white stockings, and light blue heeled slippers.
    Tricia then hit ENTER on the remote and waited. Soon she felt something covering her feet and saw the slippers fully materialize. Then she felt her underwear shift and her socks stretching up her legs and growing longer. Tricia notices the sleeves of her shirt shrink up to her shoulders and expand out a bit, while the rest of the shirt tightened around her figure. Her shirt and her sweatpants then turned light blue, changed from cotton into silk, then merged at the waist. The legs of the sweatpants then fused into a single fabric, becoming a skirt. Then the skirt grew longer and expanded outwards, while petticoats appeared underneath and gave it a bell-shape. Then finally a large white ribbon materializes out of thin air, wraps itself around Tricia's waist and ties itself into a massive bow on the back.
    After it was all over, Tricia looked down at herself and her reflection with awe and amazement. Never in her adult life would Tricia ever think she would be wearing anything like this. Tricia giggled with excitement and began dancing about in her apartment.
    "This is so cool. I feel like a princess in this outfit. I mean look at me, I'm practically a doll now-"
    Tricia then stops in place and looks at remote still in her hand.
    "Actually, that's not a bad idea."
