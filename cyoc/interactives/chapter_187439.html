    As Jessica looked through the options after doing her scan she was soon gob-stricken by this display she hadn't expected even with all the other mythology options on offer at the store....

    ...Nymph suits, as in the option to become a nymph, a walking embodiment of nature. sure she had heard rumours that GenTech had created suits that supported the growth of nature alongside their already high track record of clean production but still... to find this opportunity was like fate itself willed it.

    It wasn't just the opportunity for herself either, Jessica read from the display how nymphs linked to these trees GenTech exclusively created could use the fluids from their bodies to create and spread plant life... only issue being the creations for the first year or so being much more vulnerable to careless pollution then normal and while most of the world was moving to clean energy and production their was still those that ignored the dangers for greedy profit at minimum costs.

    But what was even better for Jessica was that the trees could be shared out with currently a maximum of 8 users a tree and it just so happened Jessica was part of a group of fellow college girls who'd rather if possible spend time cleaning the pollution and help nature recover than keep up her debts with the latest trends and fashions, she knew it, she needed to call her friends into this.

    "Hi Jess. what's up? I heard you went straight into town after classes today" Jessica's friend Eva inquired as she answered her phone. "Yeah to checkout the most recent GenTech store Eva and you'll never guess what I found" Jessica assured. "well don't leave me hanging, tell me" Eva insisted. "seems the rumours of GenTech nymphs are true... I found the options here" Jessica answered in glee. "you serious?" Eva questioned. "yes. help me call the others down here. I want to share this with you" Jessica continued. "alright. I'll call Jade and Luna. you alright with Dawn and Daisy?" Eva accepted. "all 6 of us is perfect. just don't take to long alright" Jessica agreed.

    About an hour later with all members of the group assembled in the store's 'others' section with all 6 girls scanned "see... "more to the point. pulled together we can share a tree. let alone get a suit each" Blond-haired Dawn noted in wonder. "Yeah but sis, what are the odds of us getting a chance like this again... I mean I've heard the trees are in limited supply to where we can place them. company protection or not" Dawn's fraternal yet highly resembling twin Daisy returned. "I agree, we can't this pass us up. I say we go for it" redheaded Luna insisted. "I agree as well. besides the park across the street from my place is huge and ideal" Short-cut raven haired Eva then accepted while giving a good location to place their tree.

    "so your going for nymphs with a shared tree then?" the catgirl Jessica had spoken with asked. "we have. so how does this work?" Jade returned. "well for this we start by getting you each a nymph suit but even after assigning them to your bodies they won't work until your tree is pleased. we also need to sign papers to the effect which includes the location you want the tree which depending on whether or not we have agreements for a tree to go there might take anywhere from a week a several months" the catgirl explained.
