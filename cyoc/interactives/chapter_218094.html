    Settling in her sports car, Sharon noted on the gps the address that Alice had indicated , asking her to join them at home after having recovered a certain "box".

    Starting the car Sharon still didn't know where to put her head. Admiring herself in the rear view mirror, Sharon could only marvel at the beautiful businesswoman she had become. By removing 14 years of weight from her back, the beautiful blonde was going to be able to take over the business with renewed energy, not to mention the fact that these "magic" dice seemed to work endlessly. Sharon knew a few high-ranking contacts who would be willing to put up astronomical sums just to gain a few years.
    Sharon's mind plunged into calculations, theories and marketing plans, tons of ideas came into her head in a few seconds. However, this intellectual agitation made her lose all notion of reality and she almost ran a red light, stopping dead at a crossroads.

    "Thirty seven years you have walked this Earth. Now this number is but thirty three. Each roll shall return more of what you have lost."

    Sharon shivered at the sound of the mystical voice as she noticed that the die she had placed on the passenger seat had fallen forward as a result of the stop. The light was still red and Sharon had time to feel the effects of the mysterious orbs of light taking effect. Approaching her early thirties the blonde beauty felt her body slimming down once again, Sharon remembered that at this age she maintained an intense diet and practiced yoga very regularly. She noticed the few wrinkles that were still visible fading away and her skin becoming a little smoother. Sharon was amazed at how flat her stomach was when she ran her hand over her blouse and saw how big it had become.
    As the orbs disappeared Sharon still looked like a professional woman, but her business jacket was so wide that the sleeves covered her wrists.
    This last change had not destabilized the young executive, she certainly looked a lot younger and to be heard may be a little more difficult, but this small price is well worth it to have a dream body.
    Moreover she had an incredible physical form, who would not want that?

    Arriving at the address indicated, Sharon quickly found a place to park. Getting out of her car, the young executive took stock of herself. From now on she would have to avoid rolling the dice, her body had drastically slimmed down since she was in her fifties and her clothes made for a middle-aged woman with generous curves was definitely a few sizes too high for the slim young woman, she had to constantly pull up her baggy slacks. Her blonde hair was shining and Sharon found it pleasant to run her hands through it for a short while. She went around her car to try to find her fallen dice, unfortunately it seemed that it had fallen into a gap between the seat and the gearbox, fortunately Sharon's slim hand would have enough space to catch it,
    "Okay watch out Sharon" thought the blond woman as she passed her hand.
