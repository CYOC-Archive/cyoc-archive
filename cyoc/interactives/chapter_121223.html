    Thanks to Ms. Bookai's "Disappearance" the first half of English class was spent standing around in the hallway waiting for the school to call up a substitute. Once the substitute did arrive, the rest of the class period was spent with the substitute lazily reading off the text book without really checking what page he should be reading. With half the class fallen asleep, Jamal was left sitting at his desk thinking. He hasn't even bothered pulling out any school supplies.

    With the shock of seeing Ms. Bookai's sentience disappearing Jamal soon realized what he had done. He basically murdered Ms. Bookai. Robbed her of her identity and humanity. He is a MONSTER! And what about Tam and his older sister Deena? Are they going to be stuck like that forever? He hasn't figured out how to defuse things yet. If these gloves can only fuse things together, then he has pretty much stolen three girl's humanity away forever. Sure, his sister might be annoying but he defiantly didn't want to kill her! He acted without thinking. He got to find a way to fix this... and as soon as possible! If only he can remove these damn gloves!

    Amidst all this pondering, the class has ended. The next period is lunch time. The plan was to meet with Gen and impress her with his new powers. But after unintentionally transforming Ms. Bookai by complete accident Jamal doesn't feel like interacting with anyone else. Wouldn't want to risk fusing anyone with a locker or something.

    Sitting in a isolated corner, Jamal pulled out his PB & J sandwich. Maybe eating will clear his head a little. It's a little ironic that a guy with the power of fusing things together is eating a sandwich that brings Peanut Butter and Jelly together. However, as Jamal held the sandwich to his mouth, his gloved fingers brushed against his lips.

    With a sharp "SCHWOOP!" Jamal suddenly couldn't chew the sandwich anymore. But Jamal was more worried about who he might have fused this time. As he turned his head around to check, he realized he couldn't take the sandwich out of his mouth. Pulling on it makes his mouth hurt.

    As Jamal says "Whaaaaamm ma eck" instead of 'What the heck', he realized that his sandwich is moving with his lips. As Jamal grabbed hold of the sandwich, he could feel his hands through the sandwich.

    Finally, Jamal fully understood what just happened. He has somehow fused his mouth with his PB&J Sandwich.

    Making sure no one saw him, he sprinted to the restroom. After making sure the restroom is empty, he locked the door and looked at the mirror. He opened up his PB&J muzzle and saw that he still has a tongue. It's half the length of his muzzle and it's made out of bread and soaked in peanut butter and jelly. In fact, his muzzle is now secreting peanut butter and jelly as saliva. Luckily, he can still move his tongue around and articulate words. Another thing he notice is that the crust around the edges of his muzzle looked rugged... Like teeth.

    Jamal let out a sigh. Until he figures out how to unfuse things, it looks like he's going to be stuck with this new muzzle. It's weird though. Jamal thought the glove's power fuses the entire object and being together. But instead of his entire body being fused with the sandwich, only his mouth was fused with the sandwich. It seems if he only focus on a specific body part, only that body part will be fused. Interesting. It also seems that if his hands touch more then one thing, they fuse when their pushed together. Just like his lunch and his mouth.

    But no need to worry about that now. He needs to leave before anyone sees him like this. But were does he go? Jamal can deal with his dragon mom when she finds out he skipped out on half of the school day. She's like an angel to him. He Just need to get out of here and not bump into anything.

    Grabbing his backpack, Jamal made his decision.
