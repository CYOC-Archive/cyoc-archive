    Author note:
    I am not the best with writing transformation descriptions, as you can see here, and doing something very close to a basic human form is oddly quite unexplored territory for me. So I'm sorry if this is underwhelming, I will try to work on it in the future or focus more on animalistic changes in general. Hopefully this is still a serviceable chapter though.

    A few minutes have passed by now with no change occurring, the medical researchers all looking at each other with growing fear in their eyes as one of them attempted to speak through the intercom once more in the least emotional voice he can muster, "Do not worry, the formula simply will take time to get into effect. Everything is still going as intended,". With a nod from Taylor, the doctors began to calm with this newfound amount of time. With a few soon going off to look into the formula once again, and the rest measuring the amount of gas within the chamber and on ensuring the 'volunteer' didn't get too suspicious yet.

    Luckily though, it seemed like that merely more time was exactly what they needed, as soon, they began to see her body tense up. Taylor gritting her teeth as she felt her form begin to rapidly heat up! With her fat being metabolized into proteins and acids for her newfound muscles! Luckily not growing too fast, or too intensely for her, as they built up, keeping her slim and yet much stronger than before. But despite this seemingly unimpressive development, things would grow more interesting as she began to stagger around, her muscles continuing to burn as they grew more dense. Sweat now beginning to moisten her clothes, as she kept on burning up... A few of the doctors there began to appear worried, they can't have their volunteer die from dehydration- Or much less overheating! But then...

    They saw her regain her footing, as her compact muscles began to gradually swell up! From biceps and triceps, to calves and thighs, to even her abdominal section bulging out into a faint six pack. Who knows how this will develop later on?

    However, that isn't all for these first few changes, as Taylor watched her skin steadily begin to crawl, extra cells popping up in hastening speeds until she found it to appear oddly... Leathery. The gleam giving it an odd sort of rubbery look too. Not just that, but as the scientists saw, her skin was discoloring as well due to her currently packed in cells... However, despite the toughness of her skin, some doctors noted down how this was likely not the only shift her skin was going through. In fact, one even thought that maybe this was simply an upper layer, with what they could see as something getting ready to just tear out of it and reveal itself to the world.

    Whatever the case is, there is one thing that would be certain, as the 'volunteer's' eyes widened in horror as she found her own flesh feeling so rigid and inflexible... This wasn't what she signed up for...
