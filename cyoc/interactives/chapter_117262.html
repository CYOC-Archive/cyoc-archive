    Kim goes with the bright red meeple, and Ian lays claim to the black one. once all the pieces are on the board, the start tile suddenly flashes for a moment as the formless tokens begin to change and warp.

Kim's token seems to be frozen mid lunge, her right hand balled into a fist. and last but not least. Ian's token changes to a kneeling version of himself, hands raised to the sky in prayer or something like that.

    "..I-I guess that is what they meant with the tokens changing to depict us." you say slowly, a little shaken by the sudden change.

    "so.. is everyone ready?" Aldrik asks as he glances at the bord, grabbing the dice from the table as he looks around the room. after three nods he takes a deep breath amd rolls. "well.. lets get this game on the road."
