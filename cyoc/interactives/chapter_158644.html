    The beastly Chase with the full cosmic powers of a genie stared down at the hapless looking gods he hovered over. Even though they outnumbered Chase 6 to 1, they could not feel that they were anything but outmatched. They looked at the genie's bottle, hopeful for anything that would give them an edge. The crimson supergod laughed, and then bellowed out with his perfected voice:

    "You nerds really think I'm gonna get sucked in there? I specifically asked for the POWERS of a genie, not to be a genie, idiots! Hahahaha, this feels so incredible!" Every word he spoke shook the earth below them, his chiseled muscles echoing off his amazing power. "Before our inevitable battle, I will give you one chance to keep your pitiful powers. Submit to me, and I will show you a world of raw beastiality you could only dream of. He offered a hand, still flying hundreds of feet off the ground. The gods looked at each other with a sense of nervousness.
