    Jake wanted to keep Matt’s change a bit simpler than the others. Jake looked around for inspiration and saw a picture of Matt with an arm protector with an eagle sitting atop it. Jake saw the picture before. Matt had told him his parents took him out to a bird exhibit where he saw all kinds of exotic avian creatures. The eagle stuck out to him however because that was the one his parents let him hold. Jake thought he’d give a little more meaning to that photo. He pulled up Matt’s profile and entered that Matt would grow eagle wings, a beak, and tail feathers. He also added his feet would become talons as well. Jake than awaited for Matt to return so he could see the change himself.

    A couple minutes later, Jake heard the door knob rattle and Matt entered in once again. “Hey, I guess they forgot I borrowed this and they already bought another copy, so they let me keep this. I think we got something to watch for the night.” Jake hit enter and closed his laptop. “What are we watching?” Jake asked. “Stranger Danger. It’s a comedy. Me and Randy watched a few nights ago, but I’m down to see it again.” Jake stood up and started walking toward the couch. “Sure. I heard good things about that movie.” Matt put the disk into the DVD player. “Here’s the remote. I’m gonna get myself a drink. I’m felling a bit peckish.” Jake nearly burst out laughing at how ironic that sounded. “Yeah. You do that.” Jake said trying to contain his laughter.

    Jake was hovering over the play full movie option when he looked over at Matt getting himself some water. Nothing seemed to be different at the moment, but he figured there was a delayed effect when entering changes manually. That was when he heard Matt struggle. “Oh shit! *sigh* stupid feathers.” Jake watched as Matt turned around to pick up the glass he dropped to see his arms were getting covered by feathers. “Got it.” He started bringing the cup back up to the faucet to fill it with water. When Matt turned back around, Jake noticed a bulge in the seat of his pants. Matt was struggling again as he felt the bulge with his back hand-er wing. “Ugh... get out of there!” Mat said as a fan of feathers shot out, ripping his pants slightly. “Aw, now I have to fix all my pants this way.” Matt said as if growing tail feathers was a normal thing for him.

    Matt finally got the water filled with his fully developed wings and went over to sit with Jake, trying to avoid his tail. “Sorry about that. I’m a bit clumsy today for some reason.” Matt sat down as Jake looked at his lips. Matt’s lips were turning yellow, and his face was starting to push out. “No worries. Let’s start this thing up.” Jake pressed enter on the remote and the movie started. Jake took one last look at Matt to se his nose was flattening, and the yellow was spreading to his nostrils. His face fully extended and curved down completing his beak. Jake heard some ripping noises from below. Both him and Matt checked and saw that his shoes were torn by Matt’s new growing claws. “Ugh. Why do I even wear shoes?” Matt slipped of his shoes to reveal his fully developed talons, which were same color and texture as his beak.

    Jake took a look at Matt, who was now an eagle man. His wings were wide, like he could cover his whole body with them. He folded them down sighing in a depressing manner. “You feeling okay?” Jake asked thinking Matt didn’t like the change. “Oh... sorry. I just can’t stop thinking about Randy. He’ll be gone a whole week. What if he already found another partner?” Jake came closer to comfort him. “I doubt it. I don’t think any person on that trip is as unique as you are.” Matt’s blush showed though his beak. “Heh. Yeah. Guess we both are in a way.” Matt said referring to Jake. “I still can’t believe he likes me. We’re so different from each other.” Jake went back to his position and smiled. “I’m sure he’ll be dying to have your wings around him by the time he gets home.” Jake told him. “ he always likes that. You really know how to make me feel better. Thanks.” Matt wrapped Jake in his wings, as Jake pushed him away. “Whoa, whoa... save it for Randy please.” Jake joked, as Matt laughed. The two continued to watch the movie.
