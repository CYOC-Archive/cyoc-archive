    It began when I made her clothes disappear

    From Mama’s boy to butler.

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by WriterNoahDWJ 19 hours ago AP O

    “Sebastian come on up.” I announced. Everyone clapped in applause as Sebastian nervously walked towards the stage. Sebastian was about my age and is living with his mother, he has quite a reputation as a momma’s boy. All the boys hate him because of that, the girls on the other hand kind of like him due to how he treats them. It’s just his mother always cause problems to the girls that try to get close to her son.

    He was the perfect subject for this trick because hopefully this trick will get prevent him from having a mother complex. He was even wearing a suit which is perfect. Soon he was on the stage and I shook his hand.

    “Thank you for volunteering Sebastian but I don’t recall this party being a black tie event.” I said earning a chuckle from the audience. Sebastian looked embarrassed as he shifted a bit.

    “But this outfit was picked out by my momma.” Sebastian said earning a groan from the audience. Using my hand to discretely point at the box with no silts, Mrs. Brockman moved towards it.

    “Don’t worry Sebastian in this case she pick the right outfit.” I said causing Sebastian to cheer up. That is when Mrs. Brockman open the box door. “Now I need you to step into that box.” This cause Sebastian concern as he hesitantly start walking towards it.

    “Wait are you going to turn me into a girl?” He asked concern. I shook my head.

    “No Sebastian in fact I’m going to turn you into a man that would make your momma proud.” I answered which cause Sebastian to perk up as he rush to get in. I chuckled to myself as I walked over to the box. As soon as Sebastian got into the box, Mrs. Brockman closed and locked the box. I silently made the wishes for this transformation as I smile. This was the box I use to make Brad appear so Sebastian wouldn’t hear what I was about to say to the audience.

    “Now I was serious about making him a man his momma would be proud of; however he will no longer be a momma’s boy. Isn’t that exciting?” I asked the audience. The girl’s cheered while the boy’s cheered less. “Now me and my assistant are going to spin this around.” Me and Mrs. Brockman will spin this box around clockwise three times then we stop.

    “Then we top this off with a tap from my cane.” I said. I tap my cane on the box as golden wave shoot out of the box. “Now let’s see what Sebastian has turn into.” I said as I unlock the door and open it.

    To everyone’s surprise a tall and older man step out of the box. It look like Sebastian but he was older now like his late 50’s judging by the specks of grey hair and few wrinkles. His posture was different as well; gone was the shy timid nature and in its place as serious professional nature. The girls in the audience seem to swoon while the boys grumbled over how nice Sebastian aged.

    “The Brockman family’s butler Sebastian everyone.” I announce as the audience got out of their stupor and start clapping. Sebastian looked around as if he didn’t know how he got here. However with the effects of my wish on her fading, Mrs. Brockman decided to let her presence known to Sebastian.

    “About time……you show up Sebastian….where have you been….all afternoon?” Mrs. Brockman as she heavily breathed harder. Sebastian turn towards her and was in shock.

    “Madame Brockman what are you doing up? You should know better to be up and about in your condition.” Sebastian said as he got to her side and draped her arm over his shoulders. “Let’s get you to bed; Madame.” He said as he started guiding her only to stop when he notice Nadia. “Mrs. Beaumont help me out here, I’m not as young as I use to be.” He ordered.

    “Oui Monsieur!” Nadia said as she sprang up from her seat and moved to the other side of the Mrs. Brockman then draped Mrs. Brockman’s other arm over her shoulders. Sebastian then noticed Bailey.

    “Enjoy the rest of your birthday party Lady Bailey. I’m afraid that your mother must retire early from the party.” Sebastian said as he & Nadia guided Mrs. Brockman out of the room.

    “Give it up for my temporary assistant Julie Anne Brockman everyone.” I announce and the everyone clapped in milded applause as they left the room. I waited for the applause to die down before I address the audience.

    “All right folks. Final trick for the show. This time I need a cheerleader for it. Any volunteers?” I asked then lots of hands shot up which gave me a good idea at my choices.

    Brad seemed to invited most of the cheerleaders no matter the grade. Well now I guess Bailey invited them due to obligations but however not all of them show up. All eight senior cheerleaders showed up while; only six out of eight junior cheerleaders, four out of eight sophomore cheerleaders, and two out of eight freshman cheerleaders showed up. Now depending on which cheerleader I choose will determine my final act.

    The senior cheerleaders were all white girls wearing other counties clothing so that’s a race change. The junior cheerleaders costumes made them look like animals so animal change. The sophomore cheerleaders dress in outfits that to me; make them look like perfect statue material. Finally there wasn’t enough freshman cheerleaders to come up with a perfect transformation for them so I’ll make it up as I go along.

    Who did I pick?
