    Claire was stunned, how had this happened? Claire now found herself pacing, their had to be an explanation.

    Going through her day, Claire tried to figure out what could have caused this, it had been a regular day. She had gone to work, had lunch, come home, nothing out of the ordinary except for her shoes.

    The shoes!

    Glancing at her discarded shoes, Claire was puzzled, they hadn't had a heel before. Placing them back on Claire was rewarded to find her height had returned to its previous 6'2". Stepping away from the tape measure confirmed this ti the woman, only confusing her more.

    Claire now made her way back to the package, finding a small piece of paper inside. As she read it her eyes went wide. "These shoes can change my height!"

    Claire had no idea where Kim could have gotten these shoes. What mattered now though was what they could do to Claire. The leggy blond now sat down on her couch, removing her heels. "So I can add inches to these shoes from my own height," she whispered. "And then take those inches away. Making me shorter."

    An impish grin crossed Claire's face, from what she read she could always change her height back if she wanted to. For now though why not have some fun.

    Claire now muttered the words flats, watching as the shoes shrank down. Placing them on her feet, Claire stood fonding that indeed she now stood only 5'8".

    "I wonder," she thought. Taking a deep breath she now muttered two inch heels, an odd feeling crawling over her. Looking downwards, she smiled finding the heels had indeed grown 2 inches. Stepping over to the tape measure, Claire now slipped her shoes off, a slight case of vertigo overtaking her as she measured herself.

    "5'6"," she exclaimed. Glancing around her apartment finding it to be just a bit roomier than before. Claire was short, but she could be even shorter. Shrinking the heels back to flats Claire now steeled her will, as she placed the shoes back on Claire bit her lip in anticipation as she spit the words out.

    "Six inch heels!"

    Claire giggled as the odd feeling washed over her. Taking a deep breath, Claire now kicked away her heels, gasping in shock as she now found herself much, much smaller.

    "Oh my God," she whispered, looking at the tape measure. "I'm only 5 feet tall!" 0claire giggled almost girlishly, Kim had given her an incredible gift. The only question now was what to do?
