Knowing that Peter and Ty had the ability to hear his thoughts, he thought of the following. “Don’t use your magical powers in here, for you’ll cause a panic. Fight him at his own game, and just beat him at pool!”

Every time he tried to line up his cue stick and aimed for the eight-ball, his shot would be off by just a bit and the eight-ball would bounce off the edge. This gave Bo the opportunity to clear all his solids off the table, ending the game when he knocked the eight-ball in the corner pocket. Max was very disappointed, but felt like Bo was just lucky and beat him.

He became thirsty from walking, what seemed like miles and miles of hardwood floor, when it was just a few feet to the bar, overlooking the pool hall. Once he got there, he began to climb up one of the bar stools, that was towering over him.

    “Over here, mister bartender!!” Brian shouted, waving his hands over his head. The bartender looked over, until he noticed the tiny human being standing on his counter.

In the meantime, Peter continued to roll all his balls into the pocket, and it finally came down to the eight-ball being the only ball left on the table. Peter tried to sink the ball but missed, leaving the game in Max’s hands.

But it didn’t happen, he missed his shot again, and Peter ended up sinking the ball on his last turn.

    “Not so fast, Max! You think you can bully us around, like a big, overgrown gorilla? Well, then you’ll be one too!” Peter shouted back at him. Suddenly Max froze before the door. He looked down, as he felt his skin itching all over. His arms, legs, and chest were all covered with long, black fur! He began to bend down as his spine was reformed, and he was suddenly standing with his legs spread further apart than normal. His hands and fingers became thicker, as he watched his hands changed color in horror.

    “What is this? What are you doing to me?” he said, as he felt his body changing into that of a large, silverback gorilla! Peter and Ty just stood there and smiled, as Dustin, Bo, and Rob gasped in horror as the large, muscular man found himself slowly being transformed into a full size gorilla!

    “OH NO! STOP IT! I’M TURNING INTO A… A… OOH OOH OOH!!!” he said, as he lost the ability to form words and now was left with just making grunts and snort sounds.

    Rob grabbed Peter’s arm as he pulled the teen towards him.

    “What are you doing? The people in here are going to cause a major panic!” Rob warned Peter cautiously.

    “Oh, don’t worry. He’ll change back as soon as he’s out the door!” Peter grinned.

    Max’s human face began to shift, as his mouth was pushed out further and the eyebrows became thick and deep. Max had a silver colored line of fur on his back while the rest of him was covered with black fur. He then stumbled out the door and walked out on to the parking lot, where he slowly began to change back. In the meantime, Brian had observed the incredible transformation while standing in front of one of the tables. He was completely in awe, and began to cry out to Ty.

    “Ty! Ty, don’t forget about me! TYLER!” he screamed, but Ty couldn’t hear him. Brian ran over to where Ty was standing, and climbed up his tennis shoe. Ty and Peter were just looking on as Rob and Dustin were playing their game.

    “Boy, you really taught Max a lesson alright!” Ty said to his cousin, who was standing next to him.

    “Well, I really didn’t play that great, but I felt like I had to do something. I couldn’t stand the way he was yelling at us in the beginning!” Peter responded, explaining his actions to his cousin. Then Peter looked over and noticed tiny Brian crawling up Ty’s t-shirt.

    “Oh, sorry Brian. I’d better grow you back!” Ty replied, as he looked down at the small man who was climbing up his shirt’s sleeve.

Ty grabbed the 6-inch student by his shirt and gently lowered him on the surface of the pool table. He then made his clothes disappear, and immediately Brian felt his body becoming completely covered with a thick, layer of fur.
