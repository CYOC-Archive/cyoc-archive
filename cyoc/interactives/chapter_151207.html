    Jeff wakes up as a Jeep in the garage. He doesn't remember what happened. Greg must've edited his memories again. He just stands there on four tires gathering dust. Drive me! he thinks. The sound of the toilet flushing every 10-20 minutes prompts Jeff to remember that Greg was drinking. Probably has a hangover. Suddenly, Jeff starts to become human. He's horrified to discover that Greg left his phone in the Jeep. Before he became completely human Jeff managed to extract it while his butt cheeks were still huge before the phone would have been trapped inside him.

    The code is 10-5-5-7 which spells J E E P on a phone keypad.

    He examines the APP, he cannot make things worse, can he? He uses the F1 and looks up restore command. He executes it. Suddenly, Jeff remembers who and what he is, he isn't a Jeep that can become human, he is a human. He cannot believe he agreed to be Greg's Jeep. He needs to get to the Chronivac before Greg touches the key fob again. Jeff takes a step toward the door from the garage into the house, and it opens. There stands Greg.

    "My phone!" he shouts, and clicks on the key fob. Jeff turns into a car. The ton of steel and rubber is now parked on top of Greg's shattered phone.
