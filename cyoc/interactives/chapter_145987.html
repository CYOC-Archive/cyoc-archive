    Dustin glared at the little redhead baby that Ty was holding in his arms.

    “TY! What the HELL did you just do?” he shouted. “Are you out of your MIND???”

    “Awww…. He’s so cute! Look at him!” Ty giggled. “He’s your little brother, Dustin! Here, hold him for a sec!”

    Ty handed Jeremy to Dustin. Dustin just made an ugly face by sticking out his tongue.

    Jeremy opened his big, blue eyes and stared at Dustin’s face.

    “Ghoaa… Gogoo…. Whaaa…. Ohhooo…. WHAAAAAHHHHH!!!!” Jeremy started screaming out loud.

    “DUSTIN! What did you do to our little brother? Give him back to me!” Ty ordered him as Dustin gave the baby back. Right away, Jeremy stopped crying as soon as he saw Ty’s face again. “Aww…. You scared him! Poor, little guy! Come back to your brother Tyler!”

    Ty ran his finger over Jeremy’s belly, tickling him and causing the little baby to laugh.

    “Hi hi hi hi!!!” Jeremy finally giggled again.

    “See? He wants to be with ME! That’s my little bro! Aww…. He’s sooooo cute! I can’t wait to see what you’ll look like as a big, tall and handsome teenage boy!” Ty smiled.

    “You gotta wait fifteen years before he’s old enough, Ty!” Dustin sighed.

    “Screw that! I’m gonna make him grow into a big, teenage dude right NOW!” Ty stated, making a silent wish as he set the baby on his bed.

    Immediately, the baby started to grow! His limbs, his torso and his head started to expand in all directions! The baby quickly aged into a little boy, with short legs and arms. He raced through childhood as he grew into a young kid, going from a preschooler to a grade schooler. Within moments, he was about 7 years-old, and still growing. He was getting taller and taller, with muscles popping up as he reached age 8 and 9. His legs and arms grew thicker and longer, with hair sprouting as he reached 11 and 12. His chest grew wider and more buff as he became a teen as puberty set in. The kid was now a teenager, passing the 5-feet mark. He was now blossoming into a tall, athletic teenage boy with red hair and big, blue eyes. He almost topped out at 6 feet as he reached age 16, with big, muscular arms, legs and a large chest and well-defined abs.

    “WHOA!” Jeremy exclaimed as he stood up from Ty’s bed. “It’s great to be back!”

    Jeremy walked over to Dustin, when he realized he was taller and more muscular than his “bigger” brother. He flexed and smiled as he walked up to his older “brother”.

    “Nice! Jeremy is the tallest guy in the house!” Ty smiled. “With the biggest muscles!”

    “Ohhh, brother….” Dustin sighed as he rolled his eyes and walked past Jeremy, looking up to his bigger, little brother before he walked out of the room.

    “How’s it feel to be my brother, Jeremy?” Ty asked him. “You’re my big little bro!”

    Jeremy walked up to Ty and looked down at him. Jeremy towered over Ty by at least 6 inches.

    “It feels awesome! So, what do we do next?” Jeremy shrugged.
