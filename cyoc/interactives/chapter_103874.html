    Come on boy lets go to the vet.

    The vet? You wonder.

    Come on. She wags a dog toy in your face and unable to resist you follow her into the car and lay on the back seat your legs and arms under you like a real dog. But you aren't a dog. Some how you are being treated like a dog and when you look at yourself in the rear view mirror you see yourself. Naked but you are the same person.

    Come on baby. Lets go.

    As you go into the vets the counter agent says what a good puppy you have. So what are we going to do today? Grooming? Shots? Tagged?

    Well the most important thing we need to have done first is he needs to be fixed. I don't want a horny puppy humping my leg.

    I heard it but for some reason I couldn't leave. She was going to have me neutered. Not just having my tubes cut she was going to have my balls cut off. Oh my god.

    She talked to the lady and had me go over to the scale to be weighed. It said 180. Such a big puppy the lady said.

    As I was led to the back they had to have help to get me up on the table and in came the Dr. He talked to my owner and I couldn't believe another guy would do this to another man.

    He told her about the calming after the procedure. And he told her of the body changes if she doesn't watch him carefully.

    It was moments later and I was being prepared to be snipped. How could I not be running away. I was shaved and put out and when I woke I was back at her house with a plastic cone around my neck. My crotch was numb and I was groggy. I got up and crawled over to my dish and ate the dog food she had for me and crawled back to my bed and went to sleep.

    For several days this was my routine and then feeling began to come back to my crotch and it was still painful but not so bad.

    Months passed and I had seen the vet several times to check on the progress and was tagged. I was groomed which meant I was shaved. I now looked more like a pink pig but she treated me like a dog and I acted like a dog.

    6 months later and I was brought to the vet. As we checked in they had me on the scale again and I was up to 250.

    When we went into the Dr. he told her that she needed to watch my weight. Puppies that gain weight get very lethargic and without their balls they tend to gain easily.

    I had noticed that I was becoming very sleepy laying on my bed most of the day but being somewhat animated when she came home.

    She said she understood and she would work on that.
