    It had been a long day at class when Sarah finally made it back home. It had been another boring day at school, and she could finally relax. She booted up her computer to check her e-mail and found an e-mail from one of her friends.

    "Huh, what's this..." she said, the link was to a website that had some sort of weird questionnaire. "Huh, Animal Transformation Questionnaire? I wonder what this is about..."

    She clicked it and the first question popped up on the screen.

    Section 1: Background

    (Multiple choice)


    1) If you could tranform into an animal, would you?

    Sarah thought for a moment, that was kind of weird, but she shrugged and typed in yes.

    2) What animal would you tranform into?

    That made Sarah pause, but hey, it was all for fun right? She thought for a moment, she had always loved horses since she grew up on a farm. If she had to choose, that would make sense, so she typed in Horse.

    3) What method would you prefer to tranform under?

    a) Scientific means

    b) Magic

    c) By another animal

    d) Other (Please specify)

    This took a moment to decide, its not like Sarah had ever really thought about this. She typed in B and C, that seemed simple enough.

    4) Reason why you were transformed?

    a) Revenge

    b) Accident

    c) Volunteer

    d) Other (Please specify)

    "Okay, reasons, reasons..." Sarah said, brushing a strand of red hair from her eyes before typing in B.

    5) Where would you prefer to transform?

    a) Home

    b) School

    c) Farm

    d) Other (Please specify)

    Well, that was an easy one, unless horses magically showed up in her classroom, definitely C.

    6) How long do you prefer a transformation to be?

    a) 5 minutes

    b) 1 Day

    c) 1 Month

    d) Other (Please specify)

    That was odd, did it mean how long it would last to transform, or how long the transformation would last? She shrugged and typed in D and one hour.

    Section 2: During the transformation------------------------------------------------------

    (Out of 5)

    1) Tail growth

    /5

    Well, if she was going to be a horse in this hypothetical situation, wouldn't that make sense? She hit 5.

    2) Feet and Hands

    /5

    She figured that she might as well go all out so she hit 5 again.

    3) Head and Ears

    /5

    She paused a moment, but hit 5 again.
    4) Coat (Fur, feathers etc.)

    /5

    She hit 5 again
    5) Bondage

    /5

    Now this one made her pause, she had never considered herself into Bondage, but that was an odd question anyway so she hit 0.

    6) Clothing restraint

    /5

    Well, clothing wouldn't be worn by Horses, so she hit 0 again.

    7) Transgender

    /5

    While she wasn't homophobic or whatever, she didn't think of herself as being into women so she hit 0.

    8) Speech change

    /5

    This was a little trickier, but after a moment she hit 2, at least she'd be able to talk a little in this hypothetical situation.

    Section 3: Aftermath----------------------------------------------------------------------

    (Yes/No)

    1) Mind loss

    What was the point of being a horse if she couldn't enjoy it? She hit no.

    2) Animalistic instincts (If above was No)

    Well, she might as well at least have the instincts, so she hit yes.

    3) Irreversible

    She thought a moment and typed in yes, but with the option to transform back and forth at will if she wanted to.

    4) Used by Transformer

    She very quickly typed in no.


    Section 4: Other things-------------------------------------------------------------------

    (Any)

    1) Anthro or Full?

    Well, that was easy enough, she typed in full.

    2) Would you leave your family to become an animal?

    She typed in yes for this, unless she was transformed at her family's farm.

    3) Would you fight the change or accept it?

    She typed in that she would fight it at first, but come to accept it.

    4) Would you enjoy the change?

    She typed in yes.

    5) Would you act like your animal even if you kept your human mind?

    She thought a moment, well, it would at least be kind of strange and noticeable otherwise, so she typed in yes

    6) Would you live in the wild or become a pet?

    She typed in pet and hit enter.

    "I don't even know what to expect here..." Sarah said.

    The screen turned white for a moment as a green progress bar appeared. The bar vanished after a few seconds and an image of a pasture appeared. It seemed to be empty for a moment before it was joined by an image of two horses appeared at full run. Sarah smiled and saved the image, she might as well, she did love horses after all.

    She let out a yawn, she figured she might as well get some sleep. She shut off her computer before heading to bed. Images of horses running going through her dreams.

    The screen flickered on a moment before showing the words, PROCESSING REQUEST... and after a moment it read, HORSE REQUEST TO BE GRANTED... ENJOY YOUR NEW LIFE.

    With that it shut off again. Sarah had no idea what would await her the next day.
