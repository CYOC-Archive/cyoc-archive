    As Steve looked through the various option presented to him, one option stuck out of the rest, “Reality Adaption” it read.

    “Reality Adaption? does that mean I'll have everyone think I've always been like this?” Steve asked himself as he looked through the screen upon the info it brought for it, seeing the information about it was right on the money.

    “I guess that it dose.” Steve says, being unsure of himself. “still, it would also mean that I don’t have to hide away in secret. even if there was no secret to begin with anyways. well, guess there's Only one way to find out.”

    Steve decided to click on the option as it soon showed a loading bar. in front, a labeled appeared as it read: “Reality Adaption Commencing”. it was slow at first, but it sped up quite quickly in no time.

    “Oh, god, I can’t wait!” Steve says excitedly. Steve then looked at the percentage going up that was on the servo screen.

    30% complete

    45% complete

    60% complete

    Steve's growing anticipation for the bar to reach 100% was showing as he felt a cold chill profusely running down his back.

    70% complete

    75% complete

    90% complete

    “C'mon…C'mon…almost...” Steve chanted as the bar had finally reached it climax.
