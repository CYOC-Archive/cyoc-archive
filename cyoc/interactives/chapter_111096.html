    "Frost is there a place where you can gather the dragons in one place?" You asked," I have something big to announce."

    "Yes mistress I'll have soldiers escort you there."Frost said

    "Thanks I don't know where everything is so thanks." You responded

    "No thank you Mistress Crystal." Frost responded," Soldiers guide Mistress Crystal to her temple," Frost gestured to two male dragons and they obeyed.

    "I'm fine just thinking of how dedicated you must have been to make this the dragons I mean."You said,"the view is quite amazing." You said gazing down the mountain

    "Yes we hoped that it would attract your attention."He said
    "Yes," you said, "it certainly did."

    Once all of the dragons were gathered you looked down upon your disciples in awe.

    You shook your head and announced,"My fellow dragons we are not alone on this world."You started and stared and watched,"there are other dragons on this world than you realize, and with those marked to lead like me, but don't worry I will lead you through it and help you. There are eight other species of dragons each with their own leader who bears a mark like mine, and each just as capable as you or I these species include fire, nature, earth, water, electricity, wind, light and darkness, and we must befriend to be in every way. I will communicate with the other leaders telepathically and we will achieve complete peace with them later on as we let them into our lives they will let use into theirs that is all." You finished looking out into the crowd and saw them all stareing at each other nervously.

    You then turned around to see Frost,"Is this true my mistress?"he asked and you nodded in reply

    "You can reach the others by focusing on their element however you need to be asleep."the voice said

    "I have a question Frost, do I have any specific quarters or am I just staying in that cave with you."You asked
    "W....with me!?" Frost said nervously

    "Did I just break him?"You thought
    "It would be an honor Mistress Crystal." Frost replied," Here after everyone leave allow me to show you around the mountain."

    You walked with Frost and he showed you the armory, the residence, and the breeding area. You saw a special nest for someone important,"Let me guess that one's for me isn't it?" You said pointing to the nest.
    "Well I had it built just in case you wanted to breed when I found out you were female is that odd."Frost asked.
    "Not at all." You said
    As dusk set you grew tired and Frost escorted you back to his house where you slept.
    "So who do you contact first?" The voice asked.
