    A untold amount of time has passed.

    Arianna is not quite sure what she is feeling right now. Hate? despair? Anger? Acceptance? Indifference? Lack of interest?

    Princess Arianna had a lot of time to think.

    Being a statue mean she can't move and sleep. That also means she can no longer feel her body. She no longer feels the pains and aches from standing and carrying her own weight. She's not even sure if she remember what it's like to have a aching back and aching feet. In fact, she no longer feels hunger. She... no longer feels anything....

    Well, that's not entirely true. The curse still allows her to see, hear, smell, and feel through her stone cold skin. She could feel the subtle changes in the temperature. She could feel the pain of her stone flesh cracking with age and erosion. She could feel the plant life slowly rooting itself into her stone flesh. She could feel the occasional bird distribute it's droppings onto her body. She could feel animals and monsters big and small make nests on her body, be it a bird's nest on her shoulder or head, or a rodent making a home in her frozen and wide-open mouth.

    She should be angry at all of this, and she was at first... but after awhile... she realize she can't do anything to stop nor control the situation.

    In her first days as a statue, she was internally crying, trying to yell with rage to the heavens to let this stupid curse wear off!

    ...But it never did wear off.

    And she was stuck watching life go on in the forest around her.

    She occasionally finds one of the bandit queen's victims wonder around the forest, but they always ignore her and fall victim to one of the many traps and residents of the forest or or somehow miraculously managed to escape the forest. Either way, watching the other victims try to survive the forest was one of the only forms of entertainment she could find. Some of their fates were hilarious! Some were turned to stone like her, some killed each other, and other were turned into fellow residents of the forest. Either way, she soon understood what the bandit queen meant by entertainment.

    Yet... None of the visitors to the forest realized that she was once a princess. And no one made any attempts to save her. If anything, they were too busy protecting themselves. The sheer number of times she was ignored was one of the many reasons she gave up calling for help. Some of them even tried to rape her statue form! Such idiocy!

    Eventually, Arianna gave up any hopes of ever being rescued or ever freed from this curse.

    For some reason, the curse never wore off like the bandit queen said, and Arianna eventually caring about whether she ever get freed or not.

    Besides... It was... almost peaceful no longer having any real needs. To no longer take mandatory baths or to constantly brush her hair... To have any kind of desire what so ever. She no longer needs to eat or rule a kingdom. She no longer has to worry about getting married or worrying about what's being provided to her. She no longer has to worry about any benefits or responsibilities...

    In fact, she's glad her body is deemed worthy enough to take care of the animals and plants. They're the only things that keep her company. And she's glad that her fellow residents of the forest thinks she's worth any kind of company.

    Occasionally, she tries to talk to the animals and plants. Nothing can hear her thoughts, but talking to anything helped kept her sanity.

    In the end, Arianna decided that she can live with this. She's just glad she can see life and and world move around her. It comforts her that lifeand the world goes on. When her body finally gives out and crumbles to dust, she can die happy knowing that she's one of the lucky few who doesn't need to worry about hunger and desire...
