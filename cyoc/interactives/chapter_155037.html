    Bedtime for Thomas

    Branch Full view
    added by GeekoBear 3 years ago AR
    Author note:
    Sorry to whoever was working on this story, I found it intriguing though after it was picked up again. I also thought Thomas was a cute little guy and felt that he needed himself a pacifier. I hope it is ok with whoever is interested in this story.

    “..........get you in your bed there kiddo? You’ve had a long day of starting your toddler life, and we’ve got some more fun planned for tomorrow.”

    Thomas giggled and kicked his little legs at the sound of that, he was excited to be a toddler again and to have fun with his new mommy tomorrow. His eyes soon started narrowing and he was looking sleepy after the mentioning of bed. It also didn’t help that he was enjoying his current placement being held by his mommy while sucking his pacifier and in pajamas. Sarah noticed this all too well.

    “C’mon little guy, let’s get you into bed.” she stated as she walked over to the bed and pulled his sheets out while holding Thomas on her hip. She was really getting the hang of being a mom, this was fun for her. After that, she laid little Tommy in his bed and pulled the sheets over him and tucked him into bed. She then found a stuffed Sully plushie from Monsters Inc and gave it to him. Sarah smiled at what she saw before leaving the bedroom and turning out the lights.

    Thomas has his plushie cuddled next to him while giving his red pacifier a few sucks. He really was a cute baby boy, Sarah hoped she could be his mommy for a very long time. “You all ready for night night time?” she asked in a very light tone fit for a mom. Thomas shook his head and started closing his eyes while sucking his pacifier. Sarah smiled and patted his behind, “you keep that diaper dry now you hear?” she teased him, “knowing you though I’ll probably find some poo poo in here in the morning time right?” she said while patting his diaper again. Thomas blushed and smiled around his pacifier, his grin big enough to see behind the big red shield covering his lips. He started sucking that pacifier again, it was cute watching the big red shield bob up and down around his lips.

    Sarah giggled and started walking towards the door. Thomas said “goodnight mommy” the best he could around the nipple in his mouth, but it sounded more like “Goo’nigh bobby.” Sarah understood him well enough to know what he was saying. “Goodnight my little prince, sleep tight!” Sarah turned out the lights and walked out the door to the living room.

    Thomas found out he had a nightlight after the lights were turned out and was excited to know he could be safe from the monsters in the dark that he started thinking of. ‘Ok, now I’m starting to think like a toddler,’ he thought with his adult consciousness. He didn’t care, he was all tucked in bed, had a plushie, and in top of that he had a pacifier to suck on. He was a very happy boy going to sleep in his Lightning McQueen bed, he felt like Lightning McQueen was gonna protect him from all the booger bears that could get him while he was asleep. He could also feel like he was being comforted by the red car that was draped across his comforter. He started to think of what Sarah called him, ‘her little prince,’ he thought. He smiled around his pacifier thinking about being a little prince for his mommy. He soon felt his eyes shutting and let them close as he was sucking his pacifier into dreamland. He was really gonna enjoy this second babyhood.
