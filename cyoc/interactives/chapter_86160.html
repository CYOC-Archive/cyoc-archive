    To the Zoo with friends

    It was a nice quiet day at the potter park Zoo. Four friends, name Damon, Zach, Chris and Roger, had came to the zoo to see many animals. They pay for their own ticket and headed in. They begin to discus what to do.

    "Alright guy's let's all split up and come back here when were done" said Damon. "Sounds good to me" said Zach. "I agree" said Chris. "What time should we meet back here?" Roger ask Damon.

    Damon looks at his watch. "It's 11:00. So how about around 4:00 then" said Damon. "Alright we'll meet back here in less then 5 hours" said Chris. After that the four friends left on their own.

    There was one animal he went to see first. The Elephants. He couldn't explain it. But when he always go to the zoo. The elephants were the first thing he go see first. After buying some peanuts. Damon heads to the elephants den.

    He get's to the elephants den. An elephant then comes right up to him. Damon had visited the zoo many times. So the elephants had gotten to know him well. He gives the peanut, to the elephant, and the elephant eats it.

    "Thank you Damon" said the elephant. Damon was surprise. "You can talk?" Damon ask. "Yes" said the elephant. "Since when?" Damon ask. "For a long time. We don't usually talk to humans unless we like them. Damon may I ask you something?" The elephant ask.

    Damon recognize it to be female. "What is it?" Damon ask. "Would you like to be an elephant like us? Were always looking for new elephants to be in our heard" said the elephant. "Become an Elephant? How do I do that?" Damon ask.

    Suddenly the elephant wrap her trunk around Damon. "Like this" She then blows her trunk into Damon. "Mmph" Damon struggles. "Please Damon don't struggle. Accept your new changes" said the elephant.

    Damon's skin then turns grey and grew taller as his clothes rip apart. His hands and feet then change into his new elephant feet. His nose then turns into his new trunk. His ears then becomes big and floppy.

    Lastly a tail comes out and Damon was an elephant. She then let's Damon go. "I'm a elephant" said Damon. "Yes and now you can be with the heard" said the elephant. "How do I get in the cage?"

    One of the zoo staff finds Damon and leads him into the cage as they close the door. "Welcome to your new home Damon" said the elephant. "Yeah I hope I can get use to my new body and home" said Damon
