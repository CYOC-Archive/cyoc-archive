But now that he has this Chronivac in his possession, he can create the actual girlfriend of his dreams. He looked through his options to create his perfect girlfriend.

    He started to look through the menu and found a package that says ‘People’ on it. Curious, he clicked on it to see what it was all about. Opening it, there was a message that stated…

    ‘Welcome to the People Package, where we turns those around you to different people. The process is very simple. First you pick the person you want to change, whether it be a young kid, an elderly person, or a complete stranger. Then on the text box, describe what you want the person to turn into. You can turn this said person into whatever you want, whether it be changing genders, age, species, race, mental, breast size, the sky is the limit for you.

    Examples include…

    An adult woman to young boy.
    An elderly man to a blonde bimbo.
    A toddler girl to a male body builder.
    A teenage boy to a catgirl.

    The sky is the limit for you. But that’s still an option, as some people do not want to repeat it. Still it’s up to you if you want to do that.

    Thank you for choosing the People package. Hope you enjoy it.’

    Reading through it, Jeff was amazed that this package actually exists. A package that can turn one person into a different one? That means he can turn anyone he wants in the girlfriend of his dreams. It will be perfect. The problem is, who can he turn that person he chose into that girlfriend? There are a lot of people he can choose from his friends, to his neighbors. It’s difficult to find the suitable person to become his dream girlfriend. But then, there was one person that he thought of the most. His own father.

    Since his mother died, Jeff’s dad had to be in charge of the house and balance between work and his son. It was a difficult time for the two of them. But he did manage to get by as his work paid well and didn’t have to worry about working the late night shift. After all, he’s never a late night worker. Jeff loves his dad so much since he’s the only family member he has. So he thought, why not make his father his dream girlfriend? However, dad does have a good life at work and couldn’t let him be his girlfriend all day. But then, he decided on how to handle something like that.

    See, Jeff’s father works from nine to five all day while the rest of his time is at home all night. So when he returns at around 5:30 to 6:00 in the afternoon, he has dinner and sleeps before getting up at seven and leaving at eight. So he came up with a really good idea. Being that he comes back before six, he would start the transformation of his father to his girlfriend for at 6 and it lasted an hour until the changes stop at seven. Then the transformed girlfriend he created will last all night until six in the morning, where his dad will change back to his old self for another hour before waking up at seven. That way when he wakes up, he will have no recollection of what occurred at night and act normal over it. Course, Jeff needs to make sure that the mental changes are on just so his dad won’t remember what happened at night. Seems like the perfect plan and that he can have his dad and girlfriend without any consequences. However, there is that thing called having sex with a girl. But he’ll figure out something like that in the near future.
