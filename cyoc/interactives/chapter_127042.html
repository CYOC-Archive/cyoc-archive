    You’re embarrassed, frustrated and just want to get out of here. You don’t feel like selecting any options so just mash the random button. THANK YOU. The machine flashes.

    “Okay, what now?” You ask yourself. You try to nudge your hand again but it doesn’t move. You try to unfasten the straps but they’re still stuck too. “God damnit.” The machine begins to move and shake. ADMINISTERING SELECTION, PLEASE WAIT. Flashes on the screen. Wait, what?

    You feel the surface under your finger retract. You try to pry your hand free but it’s no use. You feel a sharp pain on the tip of your finger. You gasp involuntarily at the shock more than the pain. You feel burning hot liquid course through your veins. Your heart is pumping intensely now. Finally the strap releases your hand.

    You immediately yank it free. Looking down at the source of the pain the cover has already gone back into its place. You look at your finger, there’s a small mark but otherwise fine. That strange liquid has dispersed. You have no idea what’s just happened. Pissed off, frustrated, and scared you snatch your card and head out the door.

    It’s still raining but you don’t care. You’re just glad to get out of that store, go home, and figure out what that machine did to you. You feel mostly fine, your vagina feels a bit odd but you don’t worry about it.

    By the time you get home you start to feel VERY light headed. You stumble into your dorm room and hardly remember to shut the door behind you. You know what you’re feeling is probably very very bad. But you can’t help how good it feels. You’re actually even a little aroused right now. However, the light in the room begins to fade as you fall unconscious.

    What do you do now?
