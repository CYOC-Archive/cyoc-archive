    Transform or Dare?

    A deal with a devil

    Silvia knocked on the bathroom door as Jennifer sat on the floor in her own thoughts.

    "Sweety. There's a phone call for you from ____ . He wants to talk with you." Silvia said as she handed the phone to her daughter.

    Jennifer had to shake her head as the name was blurred like as if someone had censored it from ear. It was weird but Jennifer picked up the phone and put the receiver on her ear.

    "Heeeyyaaaa!" The male voice greeted. "Calling a bit bad time?"

    "Huh!? Who are you?" Jennifer asked as she lied on the bathroom floor. "Wait a minute... That voice... You... You are that boy from yesterday...?"

    "Wow. I wasn't sure if you could remember me but I guess it wouldn't make any sense not be able to hear me at least. But okay, last time we talked over phone I remember it was your turn to say the line." The voice laughed

    "Huuh? Sat what line and what phone call?" Jennifer asked as she continued to massage herself.

    "Ga ha ha ha. Perfect. Okay here's the deal. I know a guy who cursed you. " The voice said on the phone.

    "You do? Who?" Jennifer said in surprise.

    "Can't tell you directly since I want you to make a small deal with me. Remember last time when I asked you something and it caused a big fuzz. To be honest I wasn't expecting that from you and thought you would just agree with me right there and then. So. I'm reintroducing my offer." The male voice cackled.

    "Become my boyfriend and I can help you to find whoever cursed you. I know how these things works. I guarantee it." The voice continued.

    Jennifer sighed and was feeling how her last embers of sanity were reaching to it's limits.

    "OKAY! You win! What do I have to do!?" Jennifer shouted.

    "Okay. Neat. Since you can't remember just repeat after me: 'Transform or Dare, pause. I, Jennifer Smith, transform that I am _____'s girlfriend and there's nothing that can block or deny my access to attend school until I either graduate or I am kicked out by school's principal.' Understood?"

    Jennifer was quite puzzled by the request and asked if there was a catch by saying those words.

    "No catch. None whatsoever. That's the only way." The male voice said. "I'm just throwing you a bone here. Come on... Just repeat what I told you and I can help you to solve who's behind all this."

    "Okay, okay. Sure... Transform or Dare...." Jennifer begun and took a small pause. "I, Jennifer Smith, want to transform that I am this phone caller's girlfriend and there's nothing that can block or deny my access to attend school until I either graduate or I am kicked out by school's principal."

    Something inside Jennifer's brain begun to spin. It was like a CD-player or old PC's harddisk writing new data inside Jennifer's brain or replacing the old ones with updated ones. Her world begun to expand once more as she was able to read the text on her phone and was able to speak more coherently. The small bathroom didn't change at all and stayed the same and as Jennifer looked outside while holding the phone. Then she saw...
