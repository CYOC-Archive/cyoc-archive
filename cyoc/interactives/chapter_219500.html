    Who will be the first to drink it?

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 months ago O

    The twins ended up agreeing to drink of that woman's potion although they had a small argument about who would drink it first.

    The first to drink of the potion was Thomas. At first he wasn't so sure this was a good idea as he didn't know if the potion was going to affect him badly. But when he drink it, he noted that it was quite sweet and didn't seem to have affected him at all.

    Meanwhile, instead of drinking the rest of the potion, Elizabeth watched as Thomas proceeded to dress in the children's clothes that were in the chest. She even giggled when she saw Thomas fully dressed in his new outfit (which was a black shirt with spaceships and brown shorts).

    But before Elizabeth could joke about this, she noticed that something was happening to Thomas. He was starting to shrink and before long he didn't look like a college student anymore, but more like a 6-year-old.

    Unexpectedly, Elizabeth heard the witch ask her something from behind.

    "What's wrong? Aren't you going to drink the potion?", the witch asked Elizabeth.

    "I think I'll pass", Elizabeth replied.

    "Oh, that's fine. In that case, you'll have to spend the night here. In the meantime, I'm going to send your brother home," the witch told her as she proceeded to take out a small wand.

    "Wait, I'm not leaving without….", young Thomas tried to say before the witch tapped his head with her wand. And instantly, Thomas was no longer in the cabin.

    "Well, since you are going to stay in my cabin, you will have to choose between these 2 places to spend the night. There is the guest room and the basement. Which of these do you prefer?", the witch asked Elizabeth.

    "The guest room, without hesitation," Elizabeth replied.

    "Very well, then let me guide you to the guest room."

    So at that point the witch proceeded to take Elizabeth to the guest room. But when they arrived, Elizabeth was horrified to see what the inside of the room was like, since it turned out to be not a normal room, but a baby's nursery.

    "This is where you will sleep. Rest well," the witch told Elizabeth as she proceeded to slowly close the door.

    "Wait, you really don't expect me to spend the night here do you? I don't even fit in the crib!" Elizabeth exclaimed before the witch closed the door.

    "Ummm, you're right. But that can be easily fixed," the witch replied as she proceeded to take out her little wand again and tapped Elizabeth on the head.

    Instantly, Elizabeth had been turned into a 2-year-old toddler girl. She was now wearing a green romper, little green socks and a diaper.

    Elizabeth couldn't believe it, that crazy woman had turned her into a little tot. Although she felt a slight urge to cry, she tried not to do so as she proceeded to suck her thumb.

    "Awwww, you look so cute now," the witch said to the now toddler Elizabeth as she proceeded to pick her up off the floor.

    "Don't worry, Lizzy. Maybe a little nap will cheer you up", the witch coos, poking Lizzy's nose.

    Eventually Lizzy was tucked into the crib with a snuggly baby blanket, a stuffed kitten, and a pacifier in her mouth.

    "Sleep well, little one", the witch told her before proceeding to leave the nursery.

    Just as the witch left the nursery, Lizzy sat up, instinctively sucking on her pacifier, and began to think about what she should do. This lady clearly was still messing with her and surely she was not going to return her clothes the next morning. She had to do something or else she'd be stuck as a toddler.
