    "It's this incredible program called the Chronivac and it can change anything about a person. I was thinking maybe we could use it together?"

    "Wow, amazing! We definitely should try this out."

    Jeff smiled and was happy about it. "Great!" he said, "So what do you want to do with the Chronivac?"
