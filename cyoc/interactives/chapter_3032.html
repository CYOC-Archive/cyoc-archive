    Ty had been watching the friends excitedly from behind the pole near the pool.

    "Before I show myself, maybe I should make some changes to good 'ol me."

    Since Ty couldn't enjoy watching himself change slowly, his transformation went by pretty quickly. Within a matter of seconds he was about 20 years old, about 4 inches taller, and a lot more muscular. He then added a few more touches, including a slight goatee, and a trail of hair that went from his belly-button all the way down to his large member, one that men of all ages would envy. He took off his shirt and pants, and turned his boxers into a tight black speedo.

    "Wow," he said in his baritone voice, "that felt good. Time to have some fun."

    When he walked towards a lounge chair, he smiled as he noticed the stares, both from the yound ladies and young men. Everyone at the pool area seemed to be at least 20 years old or younger. The youngest there seemed to be Paulie's little brother, Tony, who was 10. He was sitting at one of the tables, bored, watching his older brother and his friends horsing around in the pool. Because Ty had altered everyones' minds but the four friends', Tony had always believed that his older brother was 20.

    Suddenly, people started noticing music playing. It was coming from down the street.

    "ICE CREAM TRUCK!" yelled a girl, who appeared to be about 15. Everyone immediately got out of their chairs and headed for the truck, which was now heading down the street, except for Brian's gang (too preoccupied with their transformations), Tony, who had no money, Ty, and the life guard.

    The life guard sighed as he watched the people chase after the vehicle. It was obvious that he was bored. "There's no reason he can't have some fun," Ty said to himself. Because the life guard was already in shape, Ty saw no reason to change his body. This time he wanted to change the guy's mind a little. He now recognized the young man as Derek, one of the seniors from the high school who'd be turning 19 soon.

    "Derek, you will now think of Brian, Ian, Paulie, and Hank as your best friends. Go have some fun with them."

    Immediately, Derek jumped out of his chair. "Dudes, wait for me!"

    He took a dive into the pool. The other four guys looked at each other, confused at first, but then decided to just let him join in. It wasn't the weirdest thing to happen that day. Plus, the more the merrier.

    Ty then saw Tony, still bored.

    "Poor guy," Ty thought to himself. "Maybe if he's bigger his attitude will change." Ty smiled as he watched Tony change. A minute later, he appeared to be about 18 years old. He almost looked like Paulie's twin, except about 2 years younger, which didn't make much of a difference. He looked down at himself, shocked.

    "From now on you will believe that you are 18, as will your brother and his friends. You haven't been 10 for 8 years"

    Immediately, Tony's look of shock disappeared from his face. He started to grin.

    Brian and his friends were starting to get tired. "Let's just lay in the sun for a lil' while, k guys? We can swim more later." The rest of the guys agreed. They got out of the pool, and all 6 guys, including Tony and derek, approached some lounge chairs to lay down. They closed their eyes.

    "Time for some more fun." Ty smiled wide. All of the guys' bathing suits, including Ty's, disappeared. They were naked. Ty chuckled a little.

    Ian felt a cool breeze rushing "though his bathing suit." It felt good, almost as if he was... naked. He opened his eyes, and looked down at. He then looked at his friends.

    "Guys, we're naked!"
