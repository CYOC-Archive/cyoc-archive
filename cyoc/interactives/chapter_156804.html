    A load scream broke the morning air, as Rose snapped awake and looked around, the scream had woken up Taylor who looked around confused. Then they heard the crying for help coming from the bathroom down the hall. Jumping to her feet Rose ran toward the bathroom and tried to open it, but it was locked.

    “Open the door!” yelled Rose as the rest of the house arrived, however one member of their group was missing Lisa.

    “Is this what I think it means.” Whispered Monica softly as everyone looked at each other.

    “Taylor…go back to my room and get my medical bag.” Ordered Rose as she ready herself for what she feared was on the other side of the door. Taylor rushed to the room and grabbed a small bag, Rose’s Medical bag was nothing more then an upgraded first aid kit. By now the sound of screams had stopped replaced by soft crying.

    “Lisa…it’s alright.” Reassured Rose as she put on gloves and a mask. She didn’t have scrubs and the like but she hoped this would be enough to prevent any infection. However since they had lived so close together for over two weeks and since the incubation period of the monster flu was two weeks the odds where that all of them had already been exposed, possibly only a day or hours away from the start of their own turning. “Can you open the door?”

    The crying stopped for a moment as the door unlocked.

    “Stay back.” Ordered Rose as she opened the door and closed the door behind her. The shower was still going and Lisa sat on the floor with only a towel wrapped around her. She looked normal, nothing seamed out of the ordinary only that Lisa was in tears on the floor.

    “Lisa…is it?” inquired Rose with fear in her voice as Lisa nodded her head.

    “My…” whispered Lisa her voice soft and frightened. “My back.”

    With care Rose moved around to get a view of Lisa’s back, she was on the floor which made it hard for her to get a view. Rose had to sit on the rim of the shower, turning it off before hand so not to get as wet. Still with Lisa’s long blonde hair it was hard to get a good view. With care she lifted the blonde locks up, only to see two lines of bright red bumps running vertical to each other by the shoulder blades. It wasn’t a rash or anything else, these where two solid lumps.

    “Alright, I am going to take some photos for the doctors and then we will get you dressed.” stated Rose

    “Where will I go without infecting everyone?” inquired Lisa

    “We are already likely infected.” Explained Rose as she pulled out her phone and took some photos of the red marks. “I know a professor of mine who may help…”

    “Help find out what I am becoming.” Answered Lisa “How…how long do I have?”

    “Ten to fourteen days.” Answered Rose, that was normal change rate of those infected. “Alright, let’s get you dressed.”

    Rose stayed in the room and helped Lisa get dressed before opening the door to see that the rest of their friends stood in the hallway waiting for her.

    “She has it.” Confirmed Rose

    “Alright, lets get you back into bed.” Said Monica as she offered her aid to help Lisa to get back into bed.

    “No…don’t touch me you will get infected.” Said Lisa

    “Lisa…we live in the same room and we all share the same kitchen and two bathrooms. None of us have gone anywhere in days we are all infected.” Stated Monica as she offered her help again. “So please let me help.”

    With some hesitation Lisa took the aid as Monica helped her into the room they shared and helped her to get back into bed.

    “How bad?” inquired Stacy

    “Two large red lumps on her back by the shoulder blades.” answered Rose as she showed the photo on her phone.

    “Any idea what she is becoming?” inquired Amanda as all four of them quickly began to do searches on their phones. Tracking symptoms of the transformation was now everywhere on the internet, there was even reaction videos of people reacting to their changes on social media and video watching sights.

    “Wings.” Said Taylor “She will have wings of some kind.”

    "A lot of monsters have wings, so we have no idea what she is becoming." stated Amanda as she looked at Rose. "Does this mean we could all become the same monster?"

    "Yes and No." answered Rose which wasn't an answer. "We may all have been infected by the virus but we could all be becoming different monsters or the same monster, there is no way to know without blood tests."

    "And the CDC is overwhelmed with blood tests that they can't keep up." added Stacy, as she took a deep breath and crossed her hands over her chest. "I saw it on the news, major outbreaks across the nation. It may not be wings it could another set of arms, that is how it appeared on this site.”

    “Either way, we need to all check for signs of the changes and inform the local CDC and Monster Department office to inform about the change.” stated Taylor

    “I’ll take care of that.” Said Rose as she looked up the number for the local transformation center. The rest of the house each went to their own rooms and began to check themselves for signs of the virus. Rose stood in the hallway and began to dial the number.
