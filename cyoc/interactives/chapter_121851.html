    "Why, Cailin? Why?" Mona asked as she looked on in horror.
    Cailin took aim and shot the man in the head. The blood trail from it splattered across the other girls as they gazed in shock.

    "No, 'Why MEI, why?' Mona...." Rachel said as she shot a glare at the asian.
    "You would rather..." Mei said as she got slapped by Rachel.
    "I'D RATHER HAVE SOMEBODY NORMAL WITH US SO PEOPLE CAN BELIEVE US?! Seriously Mei, WHAT THE HELL WERE YOU THINKING?!" Rachel yelled.
    Cailin just stood there, trembling uncontrollably as she fell to her knees.

    "Who's gonna get us across the state border now, Mei? Huh?! What are we gonna do now?!" Rachel yelled.
    Cailin began to quietly cry into her hands; she hadn't even tried to resist Mei's command to shoot. She had been weak.
    In the background, the other girls argued and screamed loud enough to hear from a city block away.

    "Hey..." Rachel said.
    Cailin looked up, with tears and snot running down her face, at the Samus look-alike placing a hand on her shoulder.
    "I'm sorry, but you helped Mei kill the guy.... so, we're leaving you out here with her." Rachel said.
    Cailin's jaw dropped as every girl except for her and Mei took the Seven-11 truck and drove off.

    "Well, I guess it's you and me, eh Cailin?" Mei asked with a weak smile.
