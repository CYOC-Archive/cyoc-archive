Most of these kids gave seem like they'd be just as happy being shoved into a closet for hours on end.

    "I used to be like, really tall. And my hair was really cool, like in a mohawk!" Fern held up her emerald-green hair, and decided what she'd do with the next pair of scissors she finds.

    "I was dating my girlfriend Sabr-Sa-Sabin-" Calce was flustered. Why couldn't she remember her girlfriend's old name?"

    "My name's Zitrona, silly!" She said, giving her girlfriend a peck on the cheek.

    "Alright, that's a great start! I want you all to keep thinking about how you can be yourself, while still sticking in with, y'know, the rules."

    The kids were all apprehensive - something in their mind told them that being themselves was the worst thing they could do.
