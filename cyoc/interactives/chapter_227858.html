    Max Goes First

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by awsomerguy1 A year ago O

    Max hesitated for a moment, glancing between Sarge and Sam. The idea of getting a haircut from a former Marine seemed a bit unusual, but he figured it could be a new experience. Besides, Sam's haircut did look surprisingly good.

    "Yeah, why not? I could use a trim," Max agreed, taking a seat in the barber's chair. Sarge nodded and draped the black cape over Max's shoulders, fastening it around his neck.

    As Sarge prepared his tools, he observed Max's demeanor. He sensed a reluctance in Max, a resistance to the idea of a military-style transformation. Sarge decided to take a bold approach and go for a cut that would not only be transformative but also push Max out of his comfort zone.

    "So, Max, ever thought about challenging yourself with something more structured?" Sarge asked, picking up the clippers. "How about we go for a classic high and tight? It's clean, disciplined, and it might give you a taste of a more focused look."

    Max raised an eyebrow, "High and tight? Isn't that a bit too military for me?"

    Sarge, with a determined expression, replied, "Sometimes a bold step is what's needed to bring about real change. A high and tight will not only give you a disciplined appearance but also a mindset that can take you far."

    Max, though a bit unsure, nodded his consent. Sarge went to work, swiftly and assertively shaping Max's hair into a high and tight cut. With each pass of the clippers, he pushed the idea of military discipline aggressively.

    "You see, Max, discipline is not just about the exterior; it's about cultivating a mindset that can conquer challenges. A high and tight symbolizes strength, focus, and commitment."

    As Sarge continued the haircut, he spoke about the benefits of routine, commitment, and the transformative power of a disciplined look. Max, initially resistant, found himself immersed in Sarge's persuasive words.

    Once the haircut was complete, Sarge turned the chair to face the mirror. Max examined his new high and tight, a radical change from his previous hairstyle. Sarge's aggressive approach had left its mark.

    "It's different, but it does give off a disciplined vibe," Max admitted, running his fingers over the short sides and back.

    Sarge, satisfied with the outcome, responded, "Exactly, Max. Embrace the change, embrace the discipline. It's not just a haircut; it's a mindset."

    As Max paid for the haircut and left the shop, Sarge felt a sense of accomplishment. Two recruits down, one to go. The aggressive push had worked for Max, and now Sarge was ready to tackle Jake's transformation. And once all their haircuts were done, he could get on to their clothing. Casual clothing would just not do anymore.

    Jake was quite reluctant to get a haircut, which made Sarge want to teach him discipline, and that it wasn't always about what he wanted, but what he needed....
