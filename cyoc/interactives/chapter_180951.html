    September 1 - Day #183

    Dear Diary,

    I wonder if this is how it feels to be popular like one of those guys in movies who has girls around him all the time? For the past week, each class I go to I get at least 2 girls who want to fuss with my hair. At this point I have lost all care and just let them do whatever they want with it. It honestly is way easier to just do that then to argue with them. They know way more about it then me anyways. Even Jane is now fussing with it whenever she comes over, which also has been happening quite a bit as of late. I'd say they were actually dating if they weren't cousins.

    One thing I am not too happy with is the annoyed looks of the guys whose girlfriends are messing with my hair. Seriously, this is out of my hands at this point. If you don't like it, then you stop your girlfriend from touching my hair. Don't just scowl at me about it.

    -Ben

    ------------------

    September 18 - Day #200

    Dear Diary,

    Well I have made it to day 200. 104 days to go until I can get this hair cut down to my usual size. It is down to my shoulders now. All of it. Even my bangs. Also, I have been forbidden by the girls in my classes from ever putting it up into a manbun.

    Also, due to schoolwork and other things, I think I am losing weight at a fairly steady rate. I think it may be a side effect, so I will check with Will tomorrow. This better not turn into some kind of major health risk. I feel perfectly fine, so I doubt it is of much concern.

    -Ben

    --------------

    September 19 - Day #201

    Dear Diary,

    So about my weight loss. That apparently is a side effect and is nothing to be concerned over. Will said to just keep it in mind but unless I start to feel different, or I'll, then it is likely fine. So I guess that is good to know.

    I also made another cake to celebrate another 100 days closer to a quarter of a million dollars!!

    -Ben

    ----------------

    September 30 - Day #212

    Dear Diary,

    Jane put my hair into a ponytail today. I guess it is long enough now for a short one. It wasn't too bad, and for once most of the other girls didn't mess with it. So that was another plus. However, she then asked me that before we cut it off at the end, if she can once do my makeup and make me look like a girl, just to see it. Of course, I said no. But she wouldn't let up.

    Somehow it came to the topic of Halloween and she asked if she could do it then, and I go to her friend's party in a girl costume for Halloween. I was about to say no again since I didn't even have a "girl costume", but then Will said I could wear the maid costume. Damn it Will, why the hell do you even still have that? I again tried to refuse, but after hearing about the readily available maid outfit, Jane was not taking no for an answer. She even threatened to take her time with undoing what was done to me if I didn't. If Will wasn't there to add to her flames, I bet I could have talked her out of it too. Fucking damn it.

    Now I guess I'm going as a maid to a party this year. Yay. I also am apparently going in heels, since those are the shoes that came with the costume. Will did say that if I humor her for now, he will do what he can to expedite the creation of the reversal serum. So I guess something came out of this in my favor.

    -Ben

    P.S. Jane also mentioned getting me breast forms as well. Is this what being from qn uber rich family is like? You just casually spend a ton of money for just a single night?
