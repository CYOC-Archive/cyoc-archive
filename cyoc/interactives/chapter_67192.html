    James Gets a Spa Day

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Atsuyo 11 years ago O

    James woke up this morning to find out, that he was late for work again. He was 35 years old and worked in a crumby office job. He didn't like his job at all, but it paid the bills. He remembered his boss telling him that if he was late for work one more time, that he could say goodbye to his job.

    It was now 9 o'clock as James bolted through his house to get ready for work. His alarm didn't go off again this morning! He appeared to have everything. He did well in college to get this job. He was 6'4" and somewhat athletic. Usually went for a run every day after work. He had blond hair that most men would envy and women would swoon for. He had deep blue eyes that shone in the light. He was quite masculine, and built.

    He didn't have time to do much this morning as he rushed. Just got dressed, and headed to the door. No time to shave or shower. He wore a nice, navy blue suit, and white dress shirt with a light blue tie. He hopped into his car and checked himself in his rear view mirror first. He didn't look his best right now, with his unkempt hair and scruff beard. James started his car and off to work he went.

    While he was driving, his cell rang. It was his boss. James answered his phone.

    "H-hello?"

    "Where the hell are you James?!" he heard his boss' voice on the other end of the line.

    "I'll be there soon, just..."

    Before he could finish, his boss butted in.

    "I don't want to hear it. You're fired. You can come clean out your things this week."

    James was distracted while on his cell phone, and almost hit the car in front of him. Luckily he stopped in time. His boss had already hung up the phone. He didn't want to pick up his things right now. He was a little angry. Who knows what he might do, so he just turned around and headed home.

    Stepping out of his convertible and up to his steps, he noticed something sticking out of his mailbox. It appeared to be some kind of ad for a spa of some sort.

    It said ReGenesis on the ad. Normally James would just throw something like this out. A coupon caught his eye. It was a coupon for a free first visit. It appeared he would be able to get any treatment for free his first time. The ad also said no appointments necessary.

    Hell, he didn't have anything to lose, so he figured he would go and try it out. Anything to turn his bad day into a good day. He hopped back into his car without changing clothes, and headed over to the spa.

    He got out of his car and went inside. There he was greeted by a pleasant receptionist.

    "Hello!" the receptionist spoke smiling at James. "How can I help you today?"

    "I... um got this in the mail..." James spoke quietly.

    "Oh perfect!" the beaming receptionist replied. "Now what were you wanting for your free treatment?"

    "Well, I'm looking for something to relieve some stress..." James said. "I actually recently lost my job and could use the relaxation."

    "Oh! I have the perfect treatment for that honey," she replied motioning for him to follow her. "Right this way please!"
