    Ms. Adams dropped you off at your house and Lisa said she'd come over as soon as she packed up some clothes.

    "Is that you Stacey?" your mother asked from the kitchen.

    "Yes Mom, it's me" you said.

    "Don't forget to put your dirty clothes in the wash" your mother said.

    "Ok Mom" you said. At least this was the same. Your mother always reminded you to put your dirty clothes in the wash the minute you came home. You know you need to do it.

    After you put your dirty clothes in the wash you head to your room to do your homework and to take a good look at yourself. When you get to your room you see that it's different. The walls were a pastel pink, the carpet was a soft brown, the curtains were pink lace, the bedspread was the same pastel pink as the walls, there was a dresser with a few porcelain dolls on it, a glass case with some more porcelain dolls in it and some trophies, a desk with a mirror
    on top, a computer on a computer desk, a full length mirror on one wall, and a nightstand by the bed with a picture of you and Ryan at the beach.

    You set your book bag and purse down. You dig out your drivers license to see what your height and weight is now. You find out that you are 5 foot 5 and 107 pounds. You have blue eyes and strawberry blond hair. You go over and look at yourself in the full length mirror. You have a decent figure with what you would guess is average sized breasts. Your hair is almost down to your slim waist. Your face is a little rounder than before with your cheek bones slightly higher. Your nose is a bit smaller and your lips a bit
    fuller. You notice that your ears are pierced. You didn't notice any earrings in your locker so you hope that you didn't take them out earlier and lose them. You go over to your purse and look for them there. You found a pair of ruby studs in a little baggie.

    "Stacey" your mother shouts up to you, "hurry up and get your homework done. We'll be leaving in an hour and a half for the banquet. Be sure to wear your blue dress and sapphire earrings."

    "Ok Mom" you shout back to her. You wonder what banquet your mother could be talking about.

    "Stacey, Lisa's here" your mother shouts up to you. "I'll send her on up."

    "Ok Mom" you shout back.

    In a few seconds Lisa came into your room. Hopefully she could tell you what banquet you're suppose to be going to. "I forgot to tell you something before" she said looking a little guilty. "Tonight is the girls' basketball banquet."

    "I was wondering what banquet my mom was talking about" you say as you pick up your book bag to get started on your homework. You took out your folder with your assignments in it and are surprised to discover that they are all done. You knew that you had some work that needed to be done in your previous reality but that doesn't appear to be the case here.

    "Don't worry about the banquet" Lisa said. "I'll help you get ready. Right now though, let's see if we can find a way to get you back to your reality or at least give you memories of your life in this reality."

    "Sounds good to me" you say as you log on to the Internet.
