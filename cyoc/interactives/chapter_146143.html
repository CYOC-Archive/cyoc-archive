Then get to work on those chores, I don’t want to be embarrassed in front of my friends.” Ty nodded quietly as he made his way into the kitchen. June had cooked up some grilled cheese sandwiches earlier, which despite being cold were still quite good. As Ty now ate quietly, Eileen looked out of his pocket, glaring at June before turning up towards Ty.
