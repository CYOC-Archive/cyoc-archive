    From Sleepover Dimension To Fae Forest

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 6 days ago AR O Magic Mental Baby

    The Sleepover King was very happy with himself. His magic had performed magnificently! True, the boys were now babies and if he could choose the King preferred kids of four to twelve. However, transforming the Cyber Space Warriors had been great fun! Getting them regressed to that state was quite entertaining. They had acted all mighty when they were the Cyber Space Warriors! Well, look at them now! Though the Sleepover King had to admit that the Cyber Space Warriors were much better now, as babies. But even as little kids, really. And was he lucky! It was very rare for children to end up in his dimension. He had to coax them when he entered the human realm on very exceptional nights. Usually other supernatural being brought him the children, all happy to be having fun eternally. Yet over time, children demanded to return home or to grow up. After spending so much time not getting any older in the Sleepover dimension, such things was just not possible. Offended by such ingratitude, the King just sent them in the Fae Realm, where the children would become fay creatures or fae children or pets. The fae were delighted and he was rid of little brats that could not be happy with being children forever.
    But babies were fun! Alex, Carl and Luke were. Or rather they were at first. As time and time went by, the Sleepover King found these boys boring. They were just babies, and quite a few older children had ended up in his dimension, and these were already a handful, if very much entertaining and cute. Besides, more and more the three babies were acting like brats, indicating that over time his magic was fading and that these three were regaining their memories. So the King decided to put the three babies into three wicker baskets, complete with bonnets and lots of frills and left them in the fae realm, which was sure to attract any creature there. The tree babies were fuming but it took no time for a shadow to fall over them.
