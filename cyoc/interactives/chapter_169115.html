    Now had Rhonda been versed in reading and writing runes
    she might have had a easier go at reading the various
    newspapers that were stuffed here and there at the various
    gathering places.However,besides the plain language surnames
    "Prissica" and "von Keischo" that were liberally sprinkled in sheets
    of printed runes the dragon morph has no clue that by
    allowing Abigail Priss(ica) to kiss her Rhonda had just made herself
    and her mortal family the C-List Mortal Enemies of the
    von Keischo polictical family dynastic powerhouse and their
    followers.
    Margo Keischo was of course a younger member of that
    von Keischo family of politically influential wizards and warlocks
    and surprisingly she and Abigail,just by virtue of their surnames
    were heirs to the two century long Prissica/von Keischo Feud
    that had claimed the forms and minds and souls of the feuding
    enemies.

    So although young Margo and Abigail were merely B List Mortal Enemies who were already plotting pranks,a future where they
    spelled each another out of existence as Abigail and Margo was
    a very real possibility when set against the harsh reality of the
    von Keischo/Prissica history of blood blood resulting in spells that
    were definitely nastier and ill intentioned then the mere pranking
    going on at St Chariots.

    Though Abigail disliked Rhonda she just by being a Priss(ica)
    knew that someday she and Margo Keischo would be going at
    each another,continuing the Feud.

    So if Margo Keischo could look forward to her politically influential
    relatives seeing she got to be a Councillor or possibly a Member of
    Parliament
    what
    could Abigail Priss(ica) look forward to in her medium to long term future.
    Well as Abigail undoubtedly knew,and Rhonda probably didn't
    Abigail could claim Adolphus Prissica as a ancestor.
    Adolphus had been a warlock who during the Napoleonic Wars
    had been dabbling in magic tech and more industrial level
    manufacturing processes to mass produce a magi tech chemical
    he'd cooked up that he had named after himself
    Prissic Acid.

    And over the previous two centuries,whilst the von Keischo dynasty had built up their power base in politics
    the Prissica dynasty had built factories and gone into
    magi tech research,development and marketing of all lines of
    magic tech chemicals,including good old Prissic Acid.

    Which meant
    Abigail got some of her strength from having to help the workers
    at the plants take apart,move and put back together 75 pound
    Prissic Acid condensers and retort support stands.

    She also of course,being a potential kidnap victim of a von Keischo
    paid operator had taken basic martial arts training to better survive any such Kidnapping for Ransom attempt .
