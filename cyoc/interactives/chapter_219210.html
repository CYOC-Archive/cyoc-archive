    Enjoying The Day, Then Returning Home

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy 3 months ago A Magic Mental Canine

    After spending a few hours with his new friends, the suit realizes that the sun is starting to raise, the suit wills Jeff to let out a low growl, to signal the suit’s unhappiness, it wasn’t ready yet to give the human back his body. The suit thinks for a while, the human will continue to sleep until the suit lets him free, the suit had followed the pack for miles, by the time he would arrive home, the boy’s parents would be awake, so he wouldn’t be able to sneak back inside. The suit decides to spend the day exploring the city, maybe it could learn more about how to be a real dog? Making up his mind the suit lets out a bark to let the alpha know he’ll be parting. The alpha barks back, as if to say “see you soon,” the suit turns and hurries back the way it came.

    As the suit arrives back in the town center, it suddenly realizes that it feels drained, ‘must… eat… hungry… need energy,’ it thinks to itself, it lowers it’s head and sniffs the ground, soon it picks up the smell of something delicious and follows it to a nearby trash can. The suit rears up on its hind legs and sticks its head into the bin, soon it finds a half eaten hot dog and wolfs it down, making sure Jeff chews it before swallowing, ‘need… more’.

    For the next two hours, the suit continues to rummage through bins and dumpsters, eating, or rather willing the human inside of it, to eat everything it finds, no matter how old it was. Finally satisfied, feeling it’s strength returning, the suit decides to explore neighborhood near Jeff’s home.

    As the Suit passes a garden it hears something, curiously it follows the noise, as it peeks through the dense bush, it sees a young boy playing with a dog.

    “Who’s a good boy, Max,” the boy, who appears to be around 10, asks the dog, “you are! Sit! Good boy!”

    The suit watches the boy and dog closely, ‘am good boy… but… need learn commands,’ it thinks to itself, so for the next hour or so, it mimics the dog’s behavior, and memorizes the commands given by the boy, willing Jeff to memorize them also. After two hours of “training” and learning every single dog command, the boy and dog walk into the house and the suit continues on its way, wagging happily and feeling proud that he has been a good boy, learning all those commands.

    As the suit walks down a side street, sniffing at every corner, even lifting its leg to pee, and once it feels that Jeff needs to poop, it squats down and wills Jeff’s body to go poop. Jeff groans in his sleep as he shits on the tar below him, the suit willing Jeff to memorize: this is the way to go to the toilette, whenever he needs to pee and/or poop.

    After relieving itself, the suit continues down the street, as it rounds a corner, something on the ground catches its eye, and it takes a curious step towards it. Looking down at the object, it realizes that it is an open magazine, a picture of a puppy and the words. “Reasons to buy or adopt a puppy than an older dog”. The suit, reads the page curiously, thanks to its human side, it is able to understand the words. After reading the article, the “dog” sits down, its head tilted, ‘puppy… me be puppy? No… am already good boy,’ the suit gets back on its feet and starts to continue on its way.

    As the sun sets, the suit decides it is time to head back to the house, it really had a lot of fun, but it needed rest, so it is time the human gets his body back. The suit sneaks into Jeff’s room through the open window, which luckily is still open. As it stands in the middle of the room, the room is suddenly filled with cracks and pops as the suit changes from feral back into an anthro, the zipper reapears and the suit unzips it, then it pulls its head off and Jeff’s head flops out, the boy still fast asleep. The suit then has the “body” climb into bed, before slowly pulling itself off of Jeff’s body, it then slithers towards the closet and closes the door. Once the door is closed, the suit climbs up the rack and hangs itself back on the hook.
