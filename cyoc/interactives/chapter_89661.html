    Brought Before the Queen

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 8 years ago O

    "The Queen;" the fairy said; "she regulates travel between worlds; but she may just as well declare you remain here."

    "Remain here?" You said, worriedly. "But, I have a life back home."

    "Don't worry, that's likely to only happen if she does not trust you to keep our existence a secret. Which I see no reason not to believe so."

    "Well, that's a relief."

    The fairy guided you across the walkways until you were standing before a highly ornate bridge leading to the large tree you saw before; but this time you saw a grandiose castle encircling the trunk. The structure seemed grown straight out of the tree itself and sported a gilded frame and decorated with all kinds of jewels and flower blossoms throughout the facade.

    A pair of fairy guards clad in bark-like armor standing at the bridge's threshold stopped the fairy. "What business have you at the palace?" one said.

    "And with a human?" Asked the other.

    "I've come to beseech her highness to grant this human safe passage home. She entered our world by accident."

    The two guards looked at one another and one of them said "You may proceed, but with my accompaniment."

    Your fairy escorts ushered you along into the palace, carrying you up to a high, large balcony with giant (for your size) double doors. After explaining to the other guards, one of them flies to the bridge while the others open the door into a magnificent throne room. Brilliant but gentle light shone from the pillars of crystal leading up to a raised area with two flowers, both with petals in a configuration resembling chairs, one was silver colored and quite splendid but simple compared to the larger golden one next to it.

    Upon the flower, sat a beautiful woman wearing a luxurious pink gown with a red corset, all made of flower petals. From her back rose shimmering multicolored wings like panes of stained glass. Atop her head of violet hair was a crown of golden flowers of multiple varieties, long pointed ears poking from the sides and between the bangs shone a star-shaped purple gemstone embedded within her forehead, flanked by several smaller stones.

    The fairy guard bowed before the queen, spreading her wings out as she did and said "I beg your forgiveness in disturbing you before your slumber my Queen, but we have a matter that only you can attend to."

    The Queen looks at you and says "I see..." looking at the fairy who found you, she continued, "you were wise to bring him to my attention, and to change him into a girl."

    "Him?" the guard asked.

    "I can tell this human has been enchanted, and not just with a shrinking spell;" the queen said to the guard before turning back to you; "but what I don't know is how you came to be here in the first place."

    "I was exploring a cave, then I fell into a hole; next thing I knew, I was here."

    "So you deny having performed an incantation of some sort to open a portal?"

    "Frankly, until I met this fairy over here, I had no idea magic was even real; but all I ask is to return home."

    The Queen started to think, glancing at the silver flower for a moment before rising out of her throne and fluttering down in front of you. Up close, you could see she was a vision of beauty, she appeared no older than you, but her eyes held a wisdom beyond that. Her cheeks sparkled in the ambient light, as did her pink lips and matching frame of eyeshadow around her violet eyes. "You all are dismissed. I have matters to discuss with this human in private." She said to the fairies in the room; and they left.
