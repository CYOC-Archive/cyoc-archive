The lizards began fleeing as fast as they could in fear, and Stalker roared into the sky while covered in blood from what almost appeared to look like a genocide took place. Stalker then began calming down slightly, and you emerged from your position and asked,{{Stalker what was all that about?}}

    {{Noted}} Stalker said seeing a small waterfall nearby, and beginning to wash off.

    You then turned to the humans whom were in iron balls and shackles, basically working what appeared to be a quarry. The humans seemed scared and confused all staring at you and Stalker, but mostly Stalker.

    {{Hey uh, can we communicate with humans? If so any suggestions on a name?}} You asked

    {{We dragons can communicate with any mortal being, a few immortal beings we can't though like undead, gods, and on occasions other dragons who have a communication ward. Oh also we're immortal so there's that, "ageless until unnatural death" is always what I say.}} Stalker said and you were utterly shocked.

    {{Maybe I should have worded that differently, dragons do age, but once our prime is reached there's no going beyond. Anyways please just free the humans they're not good for anything at all if in shackles. Also just make up any damn name you wish, as long as it's a word that can be pronounced then you should be fine.}} Stalker said

    You turned to one of the many humans and this one was male, and looked like a teenager , and got close to them trying to focus on them, and trying to think of a name for yourself. {{Greetings, human...}} you began and the human looked up and said,"Why?"

    {{Why what?}} you responded

    "Why do you do this? You...things just are so weird sometimes, some protect us, some destroy us which is it? And considering your friend there I think between his actions and your own....damn it!" The boy said

    {{You seemed confident for one that both is chained up, and can't think of a word to say. Answer me this is your pride that of bravery, or stupidity because you aren't in much of a demanding situation yourself. Nonetheless I am not your enemy so I'll do you a favor, and this will probably go for all of you too.}} you said, and saw the iron ball chained to the human. You then pulled it apart from the base of the chain, and it seemed like you were basically tearing a piece of paper considering how simple it was.

    "Y...you..." the boy began

    {{Now this may be something of a difficult question, but do you know who is responsible for this enslavement?}} you asked

    "Just those damn kobolds, why?" The boy said

    You shot a glance at Stalker who was just finishing washing off the blood, and he looked at you cocking his head to the side,{{What?}} Stalker asked

    You turned back to the boy,{{Just because us dragons have our territory, and seeing your kind enslaved like that was enough to send my friend Stalker here into...well you know}} you said

    "What does that have to do with us?" The boy asked

    {{That's a question that I'd prefer if you would ask Stalker and not me. This is his territory by the way, at least it is in the dragon community. My name is Jagged by the way}} you said thinking back to those rocks you almost died to.

    You then turned to Stalker,{{Hey Stalker this human...}} you began

    {{I was listening in, and thanks for not answering that question...Jagged. Does that name originate from that whole falling experience earlier or what?}} Stalker asked grinning

    {{Stalker! Too soon, also I almost died. If you hadn't saved me then...are you just teasing me because you had to same my tail, because I didn't know I could fly?}} You said

    {{Maybe, anyways just free the other humans, and tell them to try to free others. The sooner we do so and leave the area the better, you may as well tell the humans to do that too. I'll talk to the human here real quick and do the same, and spread out to cover more ground, not that it's needed considering our size.}} Stalker said

    You nodded and went off to free the other humans.
