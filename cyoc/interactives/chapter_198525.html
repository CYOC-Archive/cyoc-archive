    Kyra was brought by Selene into a room with the other members of the five present for the Ritual of the Elite. She still couldn't believe that Lord Miro had chosen her to stand amongst the other members of the five, now she would be seen as more than just Wyatt's hand and wife, she would have the admiration, respect, and fear of the other vampires.

    The other members of the five were on their knees facing Kyra as she entered into the room with an old wooden mixing bowl in-between them.

    "For many years, the five operated in secret from the world. Three would work in the shadows, while two would strike from the shadows. Though we recently lost a member, we shall gain a new member in our ranks. Being a member of The Five doesn't just mean being one of the highest ranking vampires, it also means you are to bring order to this chaotic world and you five are ones who represent that. Savageness against the gentle, expertise against the incompetence, influence against the inhibitions, strength against the weakness." Selene said as she said with little Ann, Ken, Cassidy, and Duncan representing said traits.

    "What your predecessor had, you shall now represent apathy against the empathy." Selene continued as she began combining some ingredients into the bowl that Kyra both recognized and hadn't seen before.

    "This brew represents all that you will gain and all you will achieve. A single drop of blood from the others is the last ingredient I require." Duncan, Cassidy, Ken, and little Ann all poured one drop of their blood into the bowl which Selene mixed together.

    "Now all you must do is drink from the bowl and you will gain more strength than you already have." Selene said as Kyra nodded and took the bowl from Selene's hands and drank from it.

    She was confused at first feeling nothing before she felt heat coming from her mouth like a hot pepper, sweat began perspirating from her body then she felt her body shake as her muscles grew more and her hair grew longer now as she struggled to pull herself together.

    'You can do this! You can dammit! You are Wyatt's hand! You are his wife! WE REPRESENT THE FUTURE OF THE VAMPIRE COMMUNITY!!!' Kyra thought to herself pushing through the pain as she pounded the floor. Finally it seemed like the effects had passed as Kyra looked at her new body with amazement.

    "Congratulations Kyra, the Ritual of the Elite is complete. You are now part of The Five." Selene said as she and the other four nodded to her.

    "My thanks to you Selene for the ritual and your teachings." Kyra thanked and bowed her head as well for thanks.

    Kyra now made her way through halls as others saw her and nodded in respect. She soon reached Wyatt's room where he was going through plans for the prom.

    "You've gone through the Ritual of the Elite? Sorry, I couldn't join you, I had...to plan." Wyatt said as he turned to face Kyra he was stunned by her beauty.

    "The ritual was a success, I am now a member of the five, as well as your hand, your partner, and bride." Kyra said as she removed her clothes and Wyatt took in all of her new physique as she approached him. Kyra kissed him on the mouth with her tongue and he almost forgot what he was doing.

    "All we've been accomplishing so far, where else can we go from here?" Wyatt asked as he undressed.

    "Not now, but in time, I wish to discuss with Selene how vampire women may be able to have children." Kyra said as Wyatt almost seemed surprised by this response.

    "I am looking out for the future of our kind and your new heritage. Lord Miro Tepes, his son, Wyatt Tepes, and one day, the sons and daughters of Wyatt Tepes." Kyra explained as they went to Wyatt's bed.

    "God, I knew there was a reason why I made you into a vampire. You are everything I could ask for." Wyatt said as he and Kyra proceeded to enjoy the rest of their night.
