Now she looked no more than 18 years old, trim but with breasts that seemed a bit too big for her frame, dressed in an outfit that was little more than a black bra and ruffled microskirt, platform-heeled shoes on her feet, platinum blonde hair extending to her waist.

    Jennifer was smiling, but looked like she wanted to cry at the same time, seeing her altered daughters. "Hi, girls. You look -- you look beautiful, too."

    "Thank you, Mommy!" said Emma, and Kim said, "Thanks, Mrs. Jones."

    "You don't seem too upset looking at us," Kevin observed.

    Jennifer shrugged a bit. "I've been watching the news. You four look just like I was expecting -- plus, you know, there's me." She waved a hand toward herself. "You should come in and watch the news. They're saying Jason's going to give a press conference soon."

    "Okay, yeah, let's go inside, girls," Kevin said. "You can start using Mommy's makeup remover."

"I tried -- first thing I did after I got off the phone with you and looked at myself in the bathroom mirror. Nothing worked. And I got my nail clippers out." She held up one hand to show the inch-long manicured fingernails. "I cut 'em, they grow back right away."

    "Wow, really?" Annabelle was looking at her own fingernails, having obviously heard her mother.

    Kevin sighed. "Okay, we'll just -- we're not the only ones who this has affected. There were, what, 20,000 people at the concert tonight --"

    "Wonder how many of them got new cars," Jennifer said, now looking over at the Ferrari. "I just wish..."
