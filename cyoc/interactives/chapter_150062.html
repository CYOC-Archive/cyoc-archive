    Arnold ran after Samantha, trying to match her speed.

    Samantha was holding his Game Boy, one that his mother and father bought him for Christmas. It was still December. Late December. Of the same year that he got that present, and as soon as he got something nice, it was Samantha's personal duty to take that away from him.

    Samantha was his sister, obviously. Several years older than him, but she still manages to play the innocent one. Samantha was always one to break the rules when her parents did as much as blink or took attention away from her. She was endlessly repeating college, always hiding out somewhere just to pounce on the unsuspecting Arnold.

    Arnold was always the one to take the blame. He expected nothing less but for his life to be someone else's punching bag. That someone else being Samantha.

    He turned the corner, hoping to see Samantha somewhere close, as he was nearly out of breath. He didn't. He tried to rest someplace. The closest he found was this old trinket shop, dubbed the "Magic Shop". He went in and almost passed out at the counter. He rested for a bit, to catch his breath, when someone handed something to him.

    "Take it"

    A controller of sorts. A game controller, maybe?

    "You'll need it. Just don't be reckless with it"

    He looked up, as he saw the hand of an old man, with a pipe in his mouth.

    "I-I'm sorry, I can't. I ha-"

    "No charge. Just take it"

    He couldn't believe it. He thanked him and carefully took the controller, walking back home. As he opened the door, Samantha was gloating. "I'll have to thank Mom for *my* Game Boy"

    Arnold was distracted, so he didn't care, which made Samantha all the more angry. Simply, she hung the Game Boy over the fish tank. That got his attention.

    "DON'T"

    "Why not? You're not playing it"

    "You know what..." The controller started to vibrate, almost glowing, but neither of them took any notice. Soon, a bright flash was going to cover the whole room, leaving only Arnold out of the light. The world and reality were about to change for everyone else, especially Samantha, who was targeted by the controller, ready to hear any phrase that could alter her reality.

    "You're just a..."
