    Slumber Party!

    added 6 years ago A AR TG

    You and your foxy friend wander over to the room where the remaining partygoers crashed. You didn't get a chance to look at them on the way in. As you entered the room, Sam turned on the lights. Sam gasps at what he sees. Girls of various ages and forms, collapsed on the ground and couches in the room.

    You look on the ground and see a six year old catgirl (you can tell by the bottle sitting next to her). She has short pink hair, with point cat ears and a long tail has wrapped around her sleeping body. Her ears twitch cutely as she sleep, unaware that she had changed into the adorable little creature you see before you. Like you, her clothes had changed. She wore a small pink shirt that showed off a bit of her tiny belly. She had some pink shorts on, and pink and white striped thigh highs on her feet. You wanted to go over and pet the kitty, but stopped yourself.

    The next girl sleeping on the couch obviously didnt have much to drink. She looked to be around 18 years old, a foxgirl like Sam. She was draped in a business suit now far too large for her. She had large DD cup breasts, wipe hips and a plump butt, and the fluffiest tail you have ever seen. You see a bottle next to her and notice its completely empty. You wonder what happened to whoever drank that, until you notice a pile of discarded clothing around the bottle, and the fox ladys belly pushing out slightly. You giggle, knowing there was now a baby foxy inside her.

    Looking over to the floor again, you see two 7 year old bunny girls, leaning against each other. Their floppy ears dropped down over their faces. Long blonde hair, tied up in side ponytails (on a different side for each girl). Their now dainty feet were also painted with pink nail polish.

    You and Sam looked around some more, the room was just full of little girls.

    "This is bad, really bad. Who did this!" She shouted.

    "It was me, little one." A sultry voice says from behind you.

    Both you and Sam turn around and see the red haired girl from the party. The one who served you the drinks.

    "Hey, you're the one who-" You begin, before she places a finger on your tiny lips

    "Made you so pretty? Yes, that was me. But admit it child, you like what I've done to you" She said.

    You blushed and looked down to your feet, kicking them shyly against the ground. You really did like what happened. The world just seems so much more...fun as a little girl. The woman touched your head and you felt tingly, your mind going blank. You...wanted to wear pretty dresses, and play with dolls, and play with all you friends, who were sleeping in the other room. Sam notices as you giggle happily, and stomps her feet.

    "No! You need to fix us right now! I can't live my life as a little girl!" She shouted

    But the woman just laughed at Sam. "Oh honey, you haven't even tried it. Your friend here seems to be enjoying herself, so why can't you?" The woman stops and thinks for a second, before she has an idea. "I know what you girls need! a good old fashioned slumber party!"
    She snaps her fingers and the world goes dark for a second. When you regain your vision, you are in a completely different room.

    The walls are pink, with flowers and butterflies painted on it. There is a large fluffy bed at the edge of the room, where the fox lady now sleeps. You look down at your feet and see that you are out of the pajamas. You are now clad in a short pink dress that covers your tiny body. Looking down to your feet as well, you see the catgirl from earlier painting your nails a bright pink. To your right and left, you see the bunnygirl twins doing your hair into twintails. "This is amazing!" You squeal, as the girls around you giggle. The catgirl then finishes with your toes and sits between your legs. You sit up and look at her hair. It was short, but you could do something with it! You wanted to make her look just as pretty is you did. You reached your hands up, pulling her short hair, ready to fasten it into a ponytail. As you worked with her hair, your own began to change. It grew lighter and lighter, becoming a bright pink, just like the girl in front of you. Your ears vanished, and then appeared again on top of your head. They twitched and perked up happily. A hole appeared in the back of your dress, and a long, pink cat tail slithered out. By the time you were a full catgirl, you had finished braiding your sisters hair. You lean forwards and give the girl in front of you, now your twin sister, a hug. You both giggle and go off to play dress up with the bunny girl twins.

    Meanwhile, Sam sat off in the distance with the red haired woman, watching you happily transform into a catgirl. He looked at the fox woman on the bed and felt an increasing connection to her. But he shook off those thoughts, not wanting to give in.

    "Come on sweetie, don't you want to have fun with the other girls?" The woman whispered.
