You were almost entirely covered in dark scales which stinged your skin while emerging from it like a wave of spikes. Which would be fascinating if you'd see it in a movie and not from first person perspective. You then felt a horrendous headache as your head compressed in size and thinned out as well, your teeth falling out while you coughed blood from your gums. You are a snake but at least you kept your human mind and you're not the size of a snake from Harry Potter, but still quite big. The scientist satisfied with your completed transformation, didn't even notice that something was off, or that the syringe broke. For a second you just watched your brother shiver and the scientist full of himself wave at you. "What are you looking at mindless beast, obey and devour your past brother, this instant" - Shouted the scientist. THATS IT, you could pretend to be what he just called you and trick the scientist and maybe escape this prison with your brother, since you still had your humanity in your mind you would probably be able to accomplish that. You shake your massive head dramatically and stretch your cobra hood and slowly start to slither closer to your brother, scientist clasping his hands together with a grin also steps closer satisfied that his serum worked. You both creep closer your brother and you hiss theatrically to show off. Your brother steps back in a steady pace until he falls onto his back tripping over a cable near the wall. "Kevin, I know you're still in there, I beg you stop this, defy this madness, we can escape, I'm your brother" - He cried as you were just a few meters next to him. "See Mike, there is no escape, it's all just a scene for a specific audience to watch this drama show.
