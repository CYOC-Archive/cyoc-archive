    Sid, he was a garbage truck driver.

    He was tall and lanky, for his age. He had brown hair and a small goatee, and neutral skin. He wore a black shirt with a white skull on it over a blue shirt with sleeves, a high visibility waistcoat, dark green work gloves, yellow ski goggles, a green military-patterned baseball cap (worn back to front), with blue jeans and black lace up work boots.

    He was a garbage truck driver, who drove round the suburbs, collecting bins full of trash from the houses in said suburbs.

    One day, Sid had finished his work for the day. he went back home, and sat down on the sofa.

    It was then he found a piece of paper on the sofa, with a link to a website called MonsterMe.com.

    He went onto his computer and entered the site link.

    The website pulled up and prompted him to register.

    he quickly created a profile for himself. He waited as the website loaded to the next page.
    He was shocked when a full body image of himself loaded on the next screen, with his name, details, and all other stuff like that.

    Perplexed as to how the site could have a full body picture of him, and his details, he decided to try out the settings to make his monster form.
    he saw a dizzying array of options to choose from. Options ranging from skin colour to scaly or furry, to options for claws, fangs, horns and tails among many other things. he couldn't help but feel overwhelmed by the options. Each option had a sliding bar that allowed him to select the degree of change. There was even colour palettes to choose the colour for his monster self.

    He lifted his goggles, showing his brown eyes. He stared in awe at the site.

    He decided to start by removing his footwear, leaving his shirts and jeans. He then changed his ears to be pointed, and his eyes would be black and beady.
    For the hands, he had the fingers become elongated claws, with clawed nails. He gave his toes the same claws.
    he added a small thin tail with a triangle end to pop out from his behind, and finally, the added horns to pop outta his hat.

    FInally, in came the fun bit - the colours.
    He chose bright blue for his hair, lavender for his skin, and made the soles of his feet yellow.

    For length of transformation, he selected a minute. For duration, he selected an hour.i
    This was, of course, to try it out, to see what it was like for a little while.

    Satisfied, he clicked preview and watched as his digital icon slowly transformed into the more monstrous self.

    A new message box popped up: Initiate Transformation now? Yes/No
    "Ummm...I think it already did, but sure." he clicked on Yes.
