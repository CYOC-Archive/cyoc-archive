    You sighed, coming to terms with your new self now that you're a western dragoness. Your scales were bright blue, and your wings were navy blue with iridescent flaps. You had spikes that ran down your back to the tip of your tail, and you had what appeared to be a hooked spike on the end of your tail. You inspected your surroundings once more, and you appeared to be in some sort of cave. Stalactites were above and stalagmites were all around you, and the cave seemed spacious.

    You looked outside and saw snow filled winds blowing, and behind you was a small pool of lava. "Guess that explains the wind." You thought to yourself, and you walked towards the opening of the cave, but suddenly you hit your head off of...something. You looked to the exit to the cave again, and shook your head. This time you reached out to the outside world, but all that was there was a flat surface.

    "It's fake?!" You thought to yourself then roared in anger, and fire shot out of your mouth. You paused for a moment then looked to the wall and saw that your fire left a scorch mark, but didn't burn. You then looked around and saw an vent on the ceiling of the cave, and you were puzzled. "Am I in a building?" You asked yourself in your thoughts then you saw something that was off about the cave, aside from the wall and vent. You saw a weird looking stalagmite, and you noticed that it was different from the rest because it was free standing.

    You tried pushing the fake rock over and failed, but you saw something behind it. You then put all of your weight on the fake rock and it fell over, and behind it was a metal door half the height of your body. On top of the door there were words and they read,"7" clearance"

    "Wait I'm 14 feet tall? Or at least roughy that I guess." You thought to yourself, but then saw a dark rectangular frame over the door about eye level with you. You couldn't tell what it was except for the fact that it had a dark reflective surface. "Is that a window?" You thought to yourself peering at the glass, and then you sighed and blew a puff of smoke out of your mouth.

    "Am I some sort of test subject? I mean this has to be a government facility, but in that case what are the tests? Or am I just being studied for science or something?" You thought to yourself then decided to just lay down and wait for something to happen.

    "You shouldn't be here you know." You heard a familiar voice in your head.

    "It's not like I can do anything about it, so why try to escape?" You thought then you heard a growling sound from the voice and it said,"Those humans took you here from the cave, and brought you here so why not attempt escape?"

    You paused for a moment then realized that the voice responded to your thoughts and then asked,"Wait you can hear my thoughts?"

    "Of course I can, we are now connected by the curse of your punishment." The voice said

    "Some punishment, I've come to terms with it for now. Wait, why didn't you transform those humans earlier?" You asked

    The voice didn't respond, and about 10 minutes later you figured that it had no reason for why it didn't. A few minutes later you saw the door to the room open up and you saw what appeared to be a small drone fly in and it began circling you. You lifted your head to see the drone and it paused for a few seconds as you looked at it, and saw a cameral attatched to it. However after those seconds passed the drone retreated to the door.

    You paused wondering why that just happened then you closed your eyes and eventually fell asleep. When you woke up you saw....
