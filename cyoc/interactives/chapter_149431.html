    Now as we all know
    before Lance turned to a relatively honest life
    he had been a go-between drug shipper between the
    East Side's Chinese Syndicate and the West Sides Irish American
    drug distributors of Lung Ecstasy.
    Made somewhere in mainland China and in heavy demand by
    mere humans who wanted to get as close to a TF into
    a furry or a scaly as possible.
    There was Panda Ecstasy for the furries and Lung Ecstasy
    for the wanna dragons.
    It didn't actually turn a user into a new species but it was
    capable of causing some body modifications such as
    fingernails turning in to black reptilian claws
    and a sweeping extra mane of white and brown hair on the
    spine of its users.
    So old Lance had a good reason to be less then grateful for
    his dragon tattoo,since he'd seen firsthand the damaging
    life altering effects these drugs were causing in the city.
    ************************************
    So along he and airhead bimbo Tina thundered on the motorcycle back to Monica's place,where her still human
    sister Alicia might be.
    Finally they arrived at the place where Lance parked the
    motorcycle and fished out the keys where he knew Monica
    kept a spare set.
    Unknownst to Lance,as he had thundered by Mickey Rourke's Flower Shop,one of the members of the West Side
    saw him.
    Knowing that his boss Spike had a old score to settle with
    Lance the observer phoned up his boss with the news and pretty soon
    Spilke Flannagan and about a half dozen henchmen were on
    their way.
    *****************************
    Nobody was on the main floor and no one answered Lance's increasingly
    frantic calls of "Alicia,are you here?"
    They might have missed the clue but as they were rushing
    out again Tina spotted the bottle and the spilled pills lying
    beside the closed basement door.
    She picked it up and chewed on one of the pills before
    asking her boyfriend
    "Lance ,what is 'DRAGON RAPTURE'?'
    Lance froze in his midstride and spun around
    "DRAGON RAPTURE"
    He looked at the closed basement door and knew exactly
    where Alicia was and what she must have been doing.
    The door was locked from the otherside but Lance pounded at it
    this the lock gave way.
    He ran down the steps two at at time,Tina somewhat
    slower behind him.
    Nude Alicia lay moaning on a plush carpent in the
    center of the huge basement cavern,both sealed and unsealed bottles
    of the shapeshifting drugs
    DRAGON RAPTURE and LUNG ECSTASY around her.
    Her chest was heaving up and down and on a nearby chair
    were Alicia's clothes and a paperback novel of the face of a
    young woman dripping venom out of her elongating fangs.
    Clearly Alicia was pushing things...
