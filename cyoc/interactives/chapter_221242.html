    Amory Alters Eric

    added by LittleBoy A month ago A Feline

    Amory takes Eric’s phone and starts to scroll through all the different files and reading the manual, “wow, this App is amazing,” he says, sounding excited, “all the stuff it is able to do.”

    Eric, not yet used to his nocturnal side, looks not as excited, he looks more bored and tired, the Jaguar-boy lets out a huge yawn, “yeah… very cool,” he says, the tone in his voice showing how uninterested he is, “I feel so… tired,” he adds as he yawns again.

    Amory looks up from the phone, ‘this would be my chance to alter him a little,’ the former adult feral Jaguar, now 16 year-old Jaguar-boy, thinks to himself, grinning inwardly, ‘it is his own fault for making giving me human intelligence.’ Eric yawns once again, trying his hardest to keep his eyes open, “you only have been a Jaguar-boy for a day, so you are not you are not yet used to being nocturnal,” he says, “you still have some time until you have to head back to camp, so why don’t you take a nap? I’ll watch over you, so don’t worry.”

    Eric looks over at Amory, “I… I don’t know,” he says, “as much as I want to rest, I… I’d rather stay awake. Besides… aren’t you tired yourself?”

    Amory chuckles, “I have been a Jaguar all my life,” he says, “so, I am used to it, besides, with this new young body, I feel so energized, that even if I wanted to I could not sleep right now.”

    Eric yawns, “still… to be honest… I don’t trust you having my phone,” he says, “I don’t want you to alter me while I sleep.”

    Amory’s smile fades and he narrows his eyes, “I thought you trust me,” he says, sounding hurt.

    “S-sorry… Amory,” Eric replies, “it is just… that I still have to get to know you more. I always… have been… careful around new people… I always need time until I can… trust someone.”

    Amory tilts his head, he then smiles, “don’t worry, Eric,” he says, “I promise I won’t do anything. You really need to rest, you can trust me, I promise!”

    Eric is about to argue back, but as he opens his mouth he yawns instead, he then sighs, “okay… I am to tired to argue,” he says, lying down on his side, “please… don’t… make me… regret it,” was the last he side before he slipped off into a deep sleep.

    Amory grins to himself, the second Eric passed out, he looks back down at the phone, ‘now, time to start working on my mate,’ he thinks to himself, opening Eric’s file.

    First, Amory makes sure that Eric continues to sleep through the whole process, next he changes Eric’s stance from “Anthro” to “Feral”. Amory grins as he scrolls down to Eric’s memories, deleting all of them and replacing them with memories of having always been a feral Jaguar-cub and living in the jungle all his life, causing Eric to forget his friends. The Jaguar-boy then alters Eric’s vocal cords, causing Eric unable to talk and only able to make Jaguar noises, only Amory would understand what Eric is saying, since he as the knowledge of “Jaguar-language”. Amory leaves Eric’s aging set to “human”, he then licks his lips as he moves to Eric’s gender, changing it from “male” to “female” and changing his name to “Erica”. He then looks over the changes and nods, “that should do for now,” he says, “I can change something later if needed,” Amory had turned off “Erica’s” awareness, he then made it so the phone was no longer belonging to the former human boy, instead the phone now was his. Pleased with everything, Amory confirms the changes and lies on his side to watch his soon-to-be female change.

    As he waits, Amory already develops plans for Erica’s former friends, all he needed to do is to scan the whole group without them noticing. Amory then tilts his head, if he is going to take over Erica’s old life, he should at least alter himself a little, Erica is 15, so he should make himself the same age she is. When looking down at the phone, he grimaces, knowing that, since he won’t be wearing any clothes, he needed a place to store the phone, maybe by creating a “pocket” in one of his legs, which no one would notice, since it would blend in with the rest of his fur pattern.
