    Amy's a were-dragon vampire woman

    Now what Amy didn't know was that in 1110AD Aquitaine a Anglo-Saxon
    princess named Lady Amelia,pregnant with child,had been bitten by a
    were-dragon,which had caused her to turn into a were-dragon.

    What that had meant for Lady Amelia was a complete change in appetite
    and lifestyle.

    During nights she found herself turning into a 20 foot tall dragon,gobbling
    up knights,serfs and maidens with a zesty new craving.

    However she'd also discovered the other side of her new existence,that during the
    daylight hours she returned to her pregnant human form,alas with with altered female
    body whose nipples were glowing red,whose irises were glowing red,whose lips,in both places
    were a glowing red,all set against a pale bluish tinged skin.

    To add to her worries,in that un-dragon state,she had to deal
    with a awakening craving for blood,a aversion to painful acidic
    sunlight and a heart that had a habit of shocking to a stop if she got too near
    moving water,silver or a crucifix.

    IOW a dragon-vampire woman .

    So she took to resting during the daytime hours in a coffin down
    in the castle dungeon or caves and shaded spots at other times.

    Lady Amelia had feared the baby within her would end up like a unnatural
    creature like her,however when Hubert Lusignon was born,he turned out to be
    perfectly human,albeit with a few extra powers thanks to his hellspawned mother
    the dragon-vampire princess.

    So the years had gone by,and Lady Amelia was relieved to find that
    as a dragon,she felt no need to gobble up Hubert on her nightly forays as
    a scaly flamebreather in the countryside.

    And Hubert had learned to dampen her reign of terror as a dragon,keep
    her locked up in a dungeon cell when the blood-fever came now
    and then,and carry a extra set of robes to drape over his huge monstrous scaly mother
    when the night ended and she shrank back into a human form.

    So it was that when he saw Amy standing there roaring in terror,he thought
    it was his mother,who lost it now and then as a dragon.

    So Hubert advanced on his "mother" and once he realized she didn't
    recognize him,he pulled out a girdle made from woven oak branches and rowan
    leaves,and expertly tossed the ring over the small monsters head.

    The were=dragon shuddered on its huge hind legs,then toppled over on its side
    and began to shrink and alter till Hubert found himself staring at the
    naked form of a pale skinned bluish tinged young woman who was not Lady Amelia
    but clearly was of her kind,a dragon-vampire woman.

    So Hubert covered Amy's naked human form with his mothers garments
    and carried her swiftly back to the Castle Lusignan.

    Lady Amelia arrived about 3 hours later,under the shade of leafy tunnels,whilst
    the Sun rose higher.

    Mother and son stared down at the dragon-vampire woman,and wondered why
    they'd escaped their notice before.

    One thing the were-dragons and vampires in the area were good at
    was keeping track of each another,yet here was a new monster to deal with....
