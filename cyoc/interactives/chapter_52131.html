    Spell misfire

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 13 years ago BM TG

    Kat knew exactly what she was gonna do to Dan, knowing of a
    transformation spell she read in one of mom's spellbooks. She
    remembered it well enough, she figured, and seeing what it would do to
    Dan would be nothing short of hilarious. She starting chanting, not
    recalling everything immediately and stumbling at a few points, but in
    her mind this would suffice. As she finished, a small beam shot from
    her hands and a bright light enveloped a fearful-looking Dan. Kat was
    already smiling victoriously, not noticing that the ray of light
    between her and Dan hadn't disappeared, and that the light was now
    also covering her. "W-what is this?", Kat stammered, "This isn't how
    it normally--"

    At this point both their bodies started wildly warping in all
    directions before the spell started drawing them together, flinging
    their respective bodies together into a single amorphous mess before
    the effect wore off and normality returned...slightly. Part of their
    body was still theirs, most notably the head, but for the rest their
    bodies had been amalgamated into one. From the neck downwards, their
    new body had the properties of both, any movement currently being very
    difficult due to getting instructions from both heads. Their current
    clothing consisted mostly of tatters, few of it having survived this
    mishap.

    Both started to get their bearing after the confusion of what just
    happened, and gasped in unison as they looked down at their body.
    "Kat...what is this, what did you do to me?!", Dan said. She humphed,
    "I dunno, I was just trying to transform you for a laugh and I think
    something went wrong. But forget it, I'm not sticking to you any
    second longer than I have to, so I'll just cast a simple reversal
    spell..." She once again started chanting, though this time nothing
    seemed to happen. She tried it a few more times, never witnessing any
    change in her current situation.

    "Argh! Don't tell me that I've actually lost my power because of this!
    There has to be a way, there has to be, can't let anyone see me like
    this." Dan raised an eyebrow, "You? What about me? You ever think
    about the problems this causes for anyone beyond yourself? And anyway,
    can't we have mom fix this when she gets home?" Kat violently shook
    her head, "No way, she is never to know this ever happened. Who knows
    what she'd do if she found out." Dan sighed. "Then what do you suggest
    we do?"

    "First, let's make sure we can actually get anywhere like this. Either
    stop trying to move on your own or help me stand." With a lot of
    effort, they finally managed to stand and start moving, in search of a
    way to solve this problem...
