    A real doll house

    Mary looked around and saw that she was standing in some sort of forest clearing, everything clearly smelling of fresh plastic. She hit one of the fake trees to see if it would fall over, but in her current diminutive state it felt as hard as real wood. In fact, everything felt more real at this scale. She saw the tip of a roof just beyond the trees, so she decided to walk in that direction. When she came closer, she saw that she was approaching a wooden doll house that was fully decorated. She checked out the door and it was openable, so she went inside and stepped into a hallway. "Damn, this house is detailed", she thought. She opened one of the doors and entered a fully decorated living room, figuring most of the furniture here was plastic anyway. She sat down in a chair, but it felt very soft and real. A TV was not present, and was probably too much trouble for Jess anyway to include, but for the rest everything was working exactly as it should. She climbed the wooden stairs onto the second level of the dollhouse, and first Mary went to the bathroom. She twisted the silver knobs on the plastic bath and surprisingly enough, actual running water came out! "Damn, so much stuff...Jess must be intending to have me live here for a long time", she figured. She checked out the bedroom in which there was a tiny wooden bed with cotton sheets on it, and a window with a view on the walls of her world. She turned around and checked out the closet...a whole range of doll clothes, ranging from very modest to a kinky rubber suit. Intending to leave this for what it was, Mary turned back to the window and nearly scared herself to death when she suddenly saw a giant eye staring at her! The eye went a bit further away from the window, and revealed itself to be the face of Jess looking in. "Hope you like your new house", she boomed, "I might grant you a few things later on, but for now this'll have to do. The best thing is, you'll have zero privacy at any given point; if I want to, I can watch you while you're bathing, sleeping, eating, no matter what. Ain't it great being so dependant on the whims of a goddess? I might even release this little fellow", she showed a large white mouse in her hand, "into your world for some fun. This rodent versus the other rodent...which means you!", she cackled and left for now. Mary was afraid what Jess' next move would be, given her twisted sense of humor.
