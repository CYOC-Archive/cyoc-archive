    "So what are you going to do tonight?" Jan asked as she and Rick walked out to their
    cars after work.

    "I'm going to go out to dinner and a movie," Rick said with a smile.

    "You know what I meant," Jan said with a slight shake of her head.

    "I know," Rick said chuckling. "I'm not sure what I'll be doing tonight. Maybe I'll make
    someone a doll for me or Megan."

    "That would be interesting," Jan said looking thoughtful. "I just wish I could control
    dreams like you can."

    "I keep telling you to do reality checks throughout the day," Rick said. "Also when you
    go to bed to keep telling yourself that the next thing you see will be a dream. Doing
    that will enable you to realize that you're dreaming when you're dreaming."

    "Well I did realize that I was dreaming last night but the second I realized it I woke
    up," Jan said looking down with a sad look on her face.

    "Well that usually happens at first," Rick said smiling. "Now you need to realize that
    dreams are actually a different world. You've heard that if you die in your dream you
    die in reality?"

    "Well yes but that isn't true," Jan said.

    "Well it is true," Rick said sighing. "Fortunately it's not that easy to die in the dream
    world. Do you know why you would die in reality if you die in your dreams?"

    "I would guess it has something to do with shock," Jan said unlocking her car.

    "It would appear that way at first," Rick said unlocking his car. "However the real
    reason is something else entirely. The real reason is that the dream world is a parallel
    world. Each of us is linked to a body in the dream world which is sleeping when we're
    awake here."

    "Well then that would mean that when we die here our dream bodies die too," Jan said
    making a leap of logic.

    "Well you would think that but that's not the case," Rick said frowning. "Our dream
    bodies are much harder to kill than our waking bodies. I could disembowel your dream
    body completely and you probably wouldn't die. If your waking body dies then your
    consciousness just transfers to your dream body."

    "I see," Jan said with a smile. "Will you make me into a doll for you?"

    "If that's what you want sure," Rick said with a shrug. "See you in the dream world
    tonight then."

    "Right see you tonight," Jan said waving then driving off.

    A few hours later Rick was getting ready for bed with a smile on his face. It was the
    weekend and he didn't have to work for the next two days which meant he could
    spend more time as Annabelle. He loved being Annabelle because he felt more
    comfortable as her than he did as himself. He ran through what he wanted to do
    making sure that he didn't forget anything. With a smile he slid between the sheets
    and fell asleep.
