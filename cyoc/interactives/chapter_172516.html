    "Oh I think I do, you know the rules of study hall. No phones or electronic devices. Shall I read what this says to everyone?"

    "I wish everyone but Mr. Conners and I would fall asleep." The sound of heads hitting desks could be heard, as well as a few snores. Noelle stood up. "You have one chance to give that back, right now."

    Mr. Conners looked around, wondering what was going on, but he sneered. "I don't think so..."

    "I wish Mr. Conners would give me back my phone.'

"I wish Mr. Conners was now Miss Conners, the new teacher's aide. She is just out of college, but looks 19. She is very pretty, and treats everyone with respect. Everyone but myself will think she has always been Miss Conners. She will remember her past life, but be unable to explain what has happened to her, or act in a way contrary to her new personality. And I wish that she never bother me again."

She smiled at Noelle, then quickly went back to her desk. Noelle couldn't help but check out the young woman's backside.
