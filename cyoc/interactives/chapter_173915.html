    Dog to Dad

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago A BM S

    Jason looked at the two dogs playing and felt very satisfied with his work. He loved seeing his dad completely lost in the transformation he caused. No longer was Michael a dominant bear, but instead just a mutt susceptible to his own instincts. Jason thought it must be a fun existence, having nothing to worry about except sitting around and playing. Maybe the occasional bitch would come by to chase after, but for the most part it was all pleasure no work. He thought about what to do next with two potions left. Thinking for a moment, he considered his options. He wondered if Jake, the original dog, would feel the same pleasure to be human. It was an interesting question he simply had to know the answer to. Because Michael was the last person to touch Jake, Jake should turn into Michael if he was hit with a potion right now. Jason managed to separate the two dogs without touching them to not change any results. With Jake by himself happily munching on a plastic chew toy, Jason threw the potion.

    Jake was soaked in the transformation potion. It drenched into the dog’s black fur, getting all the way down to the skin. It was another perfect hit! Jake didn’t even flinch at getting a little wet, ignoring the splash in its entirety. All there is to do now is to wait for the changes to take hold. Jason waited patiently for the first sign of abnormality, unsure of what it could possibly be. All the possibilities excited Jason, as he could only imagine what a dog becoming a human would look like. There was a grumbling sound coming from Jake, sounding like he was hungry. Following the grumble came the first change, an expanding stomach.

    Jake began to take on weight in his stomach. At this point, the dog didn’t even budge from his position, completely oblivious to the transformation. The dog went from well kept and slim to quite a heavy boy. It looked like his owners had overfed him every meal of the day for years. Still Jake did not move, but he began to subtly growl like he was confused.

    Following the sudden weight gain, Jake began to lose hair all around his body. The dog had a rather unkept coat, but it began to look rather ridiculous with the hair he was losing. Bald spots formed and the dog looked like a total joke.

    That’s when the posture changes came in. Jason heard the sound of cracking bones reshaping to fit the new body type the dog would have. Jake howled in pain as his legs and armed extended out, gaining quite a bit of length. Jake finally began to notice his surroundings and his changing body, no longer able to ignore the rapid changes.

    Jake’s paws at this point retracted. No longer did he have long dark nails, but instead they shrank into human hands. His feet shortly followed, giving way to entirely human feet as well. They were not longer covered in hair, revealing the skin underneath.

    Jake began to look less and less like a dog, but instead like a weird dog-human hybrid! He began to stand in a more human manner, no longer contained to a dog’s posture. His body became clearly defined. Jake sported his father’s old gut and his large commanding body. He gained significant height, reaching his father’s 6’2 original height. He looked exactly like Jason’s father in all aspects, except he still had his muzzle.

    Jake’s face began to pull away, losing much of the hair that was left. It retracted into Michael’s rugged but handsome mug. His father’s face was still quite hairy, so Jake retained a large burly beard that covered much of his face. With a few final crunches, the transformation was complete.

    Before Jason stood Jake, a naked version of his father. The once dog had become human… and he was quite handsome. His belly lay out completely bare as Jake rubbed it slightly with his new hands. Jake’s eyes went from dark black to a new brownish color, the same as Michael’s eyes. Jake then spoke to Jason saying,

    “That was really strange… what did you do to me.” Jake inspected his body slowly. Rubbing all the new bits and pieces methodically and with great interest.

    “I see you’re liking the changes”, said Jason with eagerness. “Is it everything you imagined and more?”

    “Maybe, I think I want to test something things out first though.”

    Jake continued to rub himself. It was incredibly satisfying for him, feeling all the sensations on his skin, and thinking new thoughts he never had before. Jason was quite happy with the outcome and was excited thinking of what to do next. He could always…
