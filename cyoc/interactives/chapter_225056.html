    The Madame Illusia

    Experiencing daycare for the first time

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago O

    "I think I'd rather go back to college", Damon said.

    "Excellent. But what do you say about your girlfriend? Does she also want to receive a better education?", Madame Illusia asked him.

    "I'm not sure but I think not", Damon replied.

    "Why don't you ask her to come on stage too?", Madame Illusia asked him. And that's when Damon's girlfriend, Monica, came on stage.

    "Tell me, young lady. Would you also like to go back to college?", Madame Illusia asked Monica.

    "Honestly no. But...."

    "What's the problem, darling?", Madame Illusia asked.

    "You see, I was never able to get into kindergarten, pre-K, preschool or even daycare. My parents thought they weren't that important so they never got me into them", Monica confessed to Madame Illusia.

    "So you want to be able to enter one of them so you can feel what you could never experience?"

    "Yes. Especially daycare. Oh, how I would have loved to have spent my days there as a toddler!" Monica answered her.

    "Aww, don't worry darling. Thanks to my magic, I can make that happen."

    "Really? Thank you very much!" Monica exclaimed as she proceeded to quickly hug Madame Illusia.

    "You're welcome, darling. But before that, I need to know something very tiny before I make that a reality," Madame Illusia replied.

    "And what is that?" Monica asked, somewhat confused.

    "It's something very simple. If you wanted to enter daycare, would you want to do it still as an adult or as a 2-year-old girl?", Madame Illusia asked Monica who stopped to think about it carefully.

    "I think I would like to enter as a 2-year-old toddler girl. I think it would be very strange for a woman in her 30s to enter daycare with other toddlers. But what is going to happen to Damon, my friends or my parents?"

    "Don't worry, darling. Except for Damon and me, everyone will think you're still a 2-year-old toddler. I will also make sure that your parents like that you are attending daycare”, Madame Illusia replied.

    "Really? Thank you very much, Madame Illusia," Monica responded quite cheerfully.

    "No problem, dear," Madame Illusia replied as she proceeded to take out her wand. "Now.....it's magic time!"

    At that moment, Madame Illusia cast a spell on both Damon and Monica and in a short time the two immediately disappeared. The audience was left speechless when they saw this event but in a short time they began to applaud with excitement.

    Meanwhile, Damon and Monica had been teleported to a different location. The first to wake up was Damon who discovered that he was now at his old university but this time instead of studying Engineering, he was now studying video game development.

    Meanwhile, something different had happened to Monica. Just as she began to wake up, she noticed something quite surprising. She was now in the playroom of some daycare. The whole place was huge and colorful and packed with toys and activities. Not to mention that there were a lot of toddlers and babies all over the place.

    But what surprised her the most was when she discovered that she had become a 2-year-old toddler girl. Now she was wearing a yellow striped t-shirt and a blue dungaree with a rainbow pattern in the center. She was also wearing little yellow socks and her hair was tied into cute pigtails with yellow ribbons. Lastly, she noticed that she had a very obvious diaper bulge around her waist, which didn't surprise her since at this age it was normal for toddlers to wear diapers.

    "I can't believe I'm a toddler again! This is going to be so much fun!" Monica said to herself.
