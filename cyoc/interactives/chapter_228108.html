    The Magic Shop

    Undercover as children's toys

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago A I TG

    "Oh, that was a little quicker than I expected" the chief reacted a little awkwardly before standing up and walking around to the front of his desk to examine the two piles of clothes that his transformed agents had left behind. On top of James' crumpled up garments lay a very pretty looking Barbie style doll in a presentation box, though it's name on the packaging had been changed to Jamie. The brunette girl was wearing a pretty pink dress and tiara though other outfits were apparently included in the box.

    "Strange, I would have thought they would make her the doll" the chief muttered to himself, before realising his two agents were still in the room and he should not act as if they were the mere inanimate children's toys they were disguised as "Okay, Nicola, I am going to have to retrieve you. Please excuse me a moment."

    The chief looked a little embarrassed as he realises whatever Nicola had become would need to be freed from inside her panties, where a large bulge indicated she was now located. He reached inside tentatively and was able to untangle a cute wind up frog, the type of toy aimed at entertaining a very young child, from Nicola's underwear. He then placed the frog on his desk, wound it up and set Nicola hopping involuntarily along it, reaching out to catch her before she plummeted to the floor.

    "Sorry, I had to check you were in working order. The last thing we want is you being returned to the store" the chief apologised as he set put Nicola and James alongside each other on his desk and tried not to think how ridiculous he looked briefing a doll and plastic, wind up amphibian.

    "I apologise for this not being the most glamorous mission, but you should be able to remain deep undercover like this. I will need you to listen out for anything pertinent while you are in this form. While the house is empty or the residents are sleeping you will be restored to human form to explore and report back any intel you have heard. Do not attempt to get dressed, your modesty should not get in the way of your mission. Also, be prepared to be turned into anything at any time your handlers deem it necessary. I wish you both the best of luck."

    At this point a young man dressed in a delivery drivers uniform arrived holding a box.

    "Are they ready?" He asked tentatively.

    "Yes, package them up. They are going in!" the chief nodded.
