    "Well, I want to learn all about a man's muscles! How big they are, where they are and what they're called! Surely you got muscles, right?" Jeanie asked the boy.

    "Uhh... Well... Yeah! Of course I got muscles, but..." Marcus stammered.

    "But, what? You're a male, aren't you?"

    "Well, yes! But, I'm only a kid! I got muscles but... My muscles aren't very big!" Marcus explained. "I'm too young to have big muscles!"

    "Oh! I can make you older or I can make your muscles bigger! Easy!" Jeanie just smiled.

    "Oh, no! Please don't make me older! I want to stay a kid!" Marcus pleaded with her.

    "Very well! Let's see what you look like with BIG muscles!" Jeanie chuckled as she snapped her fingers.

    FLOOMP!

    In an instant, Marcus' thin pectorals suddenly inflated into large spheres on his chest. His biceps and triceps grew into large bowling balls on his arms. His shoulders became thick and wide, due to his bigger pecs. His torso widened considerably as well as well-defined abdominal muscles formed on his stomach, neatly in rows and columns, shaped like bricks. His quads grew into mighty pillars of muscle and his calves inflated into huge soccer balls implanted on the back of his legs.

    "WHOOAA!!!" Marcus shouted as he felt his size and weight increase by over a hundred pounds in just three seconds!"What did you DO to me???"

    "I grew your muscles!" Jeanie smiled as she looked him over. "And, you look just adorable!"

    "My... You... I'm HUGE!" Marcus exclaimed as Jeanie made a mirror appear before him. "I... Wow! I'm a bodybuilder!!"

    "Yes! So..." Jeanie said as she walked up to Marcus and squatted down before him. She pointed her finger at his chest. "So, what are these?"

    "Those are my pecs! They were thin but... You made them big and round!" he grinned and then giggled when she poked his right pectoral with her fingertip.

    "And they're so hard! I love your big, hard pecs, Marcus! What about these blocks down here?" she asked, pointing at his abs.

    "Uhhmm... Those are my abdominal muscles! Gosh, I can't even see them anymore because my big pecs are in the way!" Marcus sighed.

    "They feel so nice!" Jeanie smiled as she gently ran her palm of her hand over his thick, hard, super-defined abdominal muscles. She loved the feeling of her hand rubbing his mighty, washboard abs. She felt the outlines of the cobblestone, rectangular muscles and the deep grooves between them.

    "Now, tell me about those thick legs of yours! I love them!" Jeanie cheered as she began to rub his thick, solid quads.

    "Uhh.... Those are my quadriceps! You made them extra-huge!" he responded.

    "Yes, I feel these long cords of muscle! They feel so... hard! I bet you must feel so manly now... so STRONG!" she cooed.

    "Uhh... Yeah... I bet I can jump pretty high and run pretty fast with these big legs!" he nodded.

    "Well, Marcus. I need to take Patrick home, soon! But, I want to visit you some more and explore your muscles. Perhaps make them even bigger!" Jeanie smiled as she stood up. Just then, Theo and Patrick came running towards them.

    "Uhh... So... Are you gonna change me back to normal?" he wondered.

    "Heck, no! You look great with those muscles! This IS normal!" Jeanie said, waving her hand.

    Marcus blinked. He suddenly remembered that he had to go to the home gym which was in the basement for his daily workout.

    "Okay, nice to meet you!" he sighed.

    "Your muscles will grow very easily, Marcus. You look cute with big muscles!" Jeanie smiled as she walked away with Patrick. "Ta-da!"

    "Will I ever see you again?"

    "I'll find you. Oh, all your clothes will fit you now!" she added.

    Marcus' clothes appeared on him, adjusted for his new, muscular frame.

    Then, Jeanie disappeared from sight.
