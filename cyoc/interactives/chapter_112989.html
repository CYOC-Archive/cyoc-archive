    Tachibana pursed her lips after listening to the sheriff and the FBI agent sitting across from her at her Estate. The elder were-vixen was only made aware of Peter Talbot being shot at only a few hours ago. Trying to get ahold of Leon to find out more information from a more direct source; she was unamused that the were-lion king was not answering his cellphone. Finding out that the perp that shot Peter, was the son of a mobster and said mobster was in town already raising a ruckus trying to get his son out of jail; Tachibana was growling, clearly upset.

    “I know the situation may seem dire,” the sheriff sighing; “but we wouldn’t be asking if it wasn’t important.”
    “As an FBI agent,” Brooke Reed speaking; “I am duty bound to make sure Daniel Parker is brought back to San-Francisco to face the charges he is on the run from.”

    Tachibana stares at the were-grizzly and the new werewolf. While Tachibana was no stranger to Sheriff Cooper’s were-grizzly fursona; Seeing Agent Reed’s werewolf form resembling that of an arctic wolf. While her fur was not pure white, a noticeable shock of black could be seen on her head and the tip of her tail. The amber eyes staring back at Tachibana; denoting the seriousness of her intention, Tachibana sighs as she knows what this might mean.

    “We will need to issue warnings for the local were population,” Tachibana grumbling but gets interrupted.
    “Samantha has already started on that,” Sheriff Cooper answering; “it means no shifting after sunset when outside and the worst of it all-“
    “The Labor day picnic,” Tachibana shaking her head; “yes this is serious. Sheriff, Agent Reed,” Tachibana staring at them intently; “are you certain that this Frederick Parker will cause problems to our town?”
    “His son is languishing in jail for shooting Peter Talbot,” Sheriff Cooper responding; “sending in a cadre of lawyers to try and hoodwink Judge Flagg into letting the man go free didn’t work.”
    “Let’s not forget,” Agent Reed growling; “that Frederick Parker managed to slip the FBI agents tailing him back in San-Francisco. Freddie the shark,” Agent Reed adding; “is currently under investigation of running a protection racket and several unsolved murders. I was informed of this when I called the director requesting agent backup upon seeing the man in the court house for his son’s arraignment.”
    “Alright,” Tachibana waving her paws signaling the agent to stop; “I’ll send for Daisuke he and his security officers should be able to help you out.”

    Before Daisuke could enter, Tachibana’s great granddaughter Megumi enters into the room. The young were-vixen rushing up to Tachibana and giving her a big hug; Tachibana was surprised to see the girl.
    “Well child,” Tachibana speaking in Japanese; “when did you get back?”
    “This morning grandma-san,” Megumi answering; “Tokyo was fun with mom.”
    “I’m sure your dad must be happy to see you back,” Tachibana smiling; “speaking of your dad where is Daisuke?”
    “With mom,” Megumi grinning; “you couldn’t peel those two off each other if you know what I mean.”
    “Well,” Tachibana shaking her head; “remind your father there is some important business that has come up and I need him now,”

    Both Agent Reed and Sheriff Cooper watch the little were-vixen dash off, her tail happily swaying as she leaves the room; neither understanding a single word the young girl said to her great grandmother. Tachibana turning to look at them could only smile.
    “I don’t often get to see my great granddaughter Megumi," Tachibana sighing; “my duties at Tachibana Enterprises and as a council member-“
    “I’m sorry I’m late,” Daisuke dashing into the study; “my wife and daughter only just returned from their summer tour of Japan.”
    “I know,” Tachibana answering; “Megumi was just in here. But now that you are here,” Tachibana getting serious; “we have a major situation brewing and your assistance is required.”
