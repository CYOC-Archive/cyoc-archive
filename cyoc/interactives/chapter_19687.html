    "Wait hold a sec, can I talk to you in the other room" I said to Amanda; she nodded and followed me into the kitchen

    As soon as we walked into the kitchen I wished Samatha could hear us talk about her till we left the room and that she would stay standing where she was.
    I then look at Amanda in a funny way.
    "Amanda you told her?!" I said kinda shocked she gave our secret away
    "Well I couldn't lie could I, I'm not the sort of person who can lie well especially infront of my best friend, and besides I thought you knew because of the ring" she said, I then made a wish to check and she was right.
    "Well I guess I'll have to lie for you, just play along or I'm going to have to make a wish to make you agree so we can sort this mess out" I said; Amanda nodded and she then followed me back into the living room.

    "Sorry we were gone" Amanda said as she came in
    "No, no problem" Samatha replied
    "I'm afraid Amanda got too into our fantasy's that she started making up a lie, and even I find that easy to do when I'm so into something I like" I said

    "Ah ok then, I was sure Amanda was lieing because she never told me something that silly in my entire life" she said, Amanda started to get cross but I wished for her to cool down and relax.

    "Oh its Art" I said and I wished for her to agree so there would be no trouble, I also wished that she would not think about it again and only think of it as a nice gift I gave Amanda.

Well you see I was at the libary and I was looking for a book which was missing and well I asked her if she could help me and well upon finding it we spend quite a while talking about us both liking Magic and it well escaladed from there" I told Samatha, I then winked at Amanda and she smiled.
