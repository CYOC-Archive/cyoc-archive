    "I think this should be fun," Patrick said, suddenly excited.

    John and Carrie smiled as they stepped in, not entirely knowing what was in store for them. Patrick walked in, thinking of what fun this would be. He would have loved to be turned into a dragon! What fun it'd be, to have a treasure hoard, fly around, and breathe fire whenever he wanted. Plus he could be quite huge.

    Then the changes started, as Patrick smiled even more. He looked down at his changing body, seeing his clothes entirely vanish. But he didn't need them, since dragons don't need to wear clothes after all. Patrick's skin itched, as it begun to turn a mixture of green and yellow. Patrick smiled even more as his neck lengthened a bit, front becoming the dragon's underbelly of yellow scutes. His skin turned green, and begun to dry out, becoming scaly. Patrick's body begun to pull down a little, as his legs became shorter and stockier.

    Patrick lost a finger on each hand, claws erupting from his fingertips. His face finally squished a bit, before springing back up. It was clearly a reptilian muzzle now, fit very well in his fingertips. A couple ridges like a dinosaur, especially a toon one erupted from his back, running all the way down from his head to his tail. Wait, the tail, he didn't have a tail!

    He then heard a couple thuds from behind him, the dragon looked behind with glee - there it was, a cylindrical and reptilian tail! Fit for a dragon. Soon, he felt a squirming at his back as two large wings erupted from his shoulders. Patrick couldn't help but giggle- his voice became much more high pitched now.

    The pod then opened, revealing exactly what he had become - a toony dragon!

    But something seemed wrong. He climbed out and gasped again. The world was much bigger. He was supposed to be bigger! He was still a baby!

    "Awww, you look so sweet," a booming voice said.

    Patrick looked up and almost gulped, but then he smiled again.

    "Mommy!" he said.

    Carrie was right there in front of him - towering over him as a brilliant purple dragon, and next to her was Jack, a red dragon.
