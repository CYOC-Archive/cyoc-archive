    "Okay Jen, My previous main pupil used fire style which I have copied. Now that you can use Sharingan, you can use fire style. I'm sure you will find it easy considering the circumstances. Let's start with something simple. The Fireball Jutsu, observe." said Kakashi.

    *A quick note for those who don't know, in Romaji, a japanese form of writing using english letters, the some vowels are prounounced differently. E is eh, like what canadians say at the end of every sentence. Also, I is a long e, you know eeee. And U is ooh, not yoo, ooh. Also, R & L switch pronunctiation, arigotou*

    "Did Negi show you the 12 basic hand signs?" asked Kakashi.
    "Yes he did nya." said Jen.
    "Good, watch my hands intently. The fireball jutsu hand signs are; ushi, tora, uma, i, ne, tori. Fire Style! Fireball Jutsu!" yelled Kakashi. A large ball of fire came from his mouth. "Now you try."
    "Right nya. Ushi, tora, uma, i, ne, tori! Fire Style! Fireball Jutsu nya!" yelled Jen. An even larger fireball was produced.
    "Whoa! Stop! You'll attract too much attention!" said Kakashi.
    Meanwhile, back at the village...
    "Huh? THAT'S SASUKE'S JUTSU!!!! I'M COMIN' SASUKE!!!" yelled Naruto.
    Back at Kakashi's training ground...
    "Well, I guess you being part phoenix makes you a fire style powerhouse. Now let's do something else. It's simply a larger version of the jutsu you just did. Just put more power in it. On second thought, practice that one in the desert or something. You'll burn down the whole forest." said Kakashi.
    "SASUKE!! I'M COMIN'!" yelled Naruto.
    "Naruto, Sasuke isn't here. I'm just teaching Jen here the Fire Style." said Kakashi.
    "Sorry nya. I guess I got a little outta control nya." said Jen.
    "Oh man! I knew it was too good to be true. Well, mind if I observe?"
    "If my pupil thinks you'll distract her then no! Well Jen?" asked Kakashi.
    "You can stay. Just stay quiet, maybe later we can spar." said Jen.
    "Okay, you'll like this one. The Phoenix Flower Jutsu. Ne, tora, inu, ushi, u, tora! Fire Style, Phoenix Flower Jutsu!" A series of small fireballs came from his mouth. "Your turn."
    "Right nya. Ne, tora, inu, ushi, u, tora! Fire Style! Phoenix Flower Jutsu!" a longer series of even bigger fireballs came from Jen's mouth.
    "Wow! Those are way bigger than Sasuke's. I remember when he used that on me. I never saw it comin'" said Naruto.
    "Thanks nya." said Jen.
    "Well, that's it for Fire Style. Now I'll teach you the only jutsu I didn't copy from someone else, Chidori. Of course, it's just child's play compared to what Naruto's gonna teach you. It goes like this; ushi, u, uma, saru, uma!" His hand began glowing. He started running at a nearby boulder. "Chidori!" The boulder was destroyed. "Your turn." said Kakashi.
    "Right nya. Ushi, u, uma, saru, uma!" She began running at a different boulder. "Chidori!" The boulder was destroyed and a large crack appeared in the ground behind it.
    "Right, you graduate. Naruto she's all yours." said Kakashi.
    "I come to find out what that blast was and find she's already done huh. She really is a quick learner. Naruto, do you have those Training Regiment Papers?" asked Lady Tsunade.
    "Sure do! Here you go!" said Naruto.
    "Hmm... and your reason for this?" asked Lady Tsunade.
    "Well, it's how I learned everything. I don't know any other methods." said Naruto.
    "Very well, your free to go." said Lady Tsunade.
