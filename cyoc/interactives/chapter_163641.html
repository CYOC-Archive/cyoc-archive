    Abigail watched as the 5 of them start growing.

    “AHH coach is getting smaller!” - Britney

    “No, we’re getting bigger!”- Vanessa

    “Woah!” - Gloria

    “What was in that water?”- Anne

    “This is so cool!”- Kim

    “HAHAHA! I knew you’d like it!” Abigail said proudly. “Now you’ll be unstoppable, no other team can beat you now!”

    They stopped growing. They were all so big, Abigail only reached their ankles now.

    “Coach Is so tiny now, like a doll” - Kim

    “Coach why didn’t you grow with us?”

    “It’s not like I’ll be the one playing. Anyways, lets check your heights now” - Abigail

    The height order had changed, they were all roughly 20-35 feet tall. She did some quick maths and found their exact heights and their height charts from smallest to biggest.

    Anne: 21 feet
    Kim: 25 feet
    Gloria: 28 feet
    Britney: 30 feet
    Vanessa: 35 feet

    “Wow Vanessa, you went from shortest to tallest” - Gloria

    “And Anne is now the shortest, kind of Ironic isn’t it?” - Britney

    “Coach why don’t we all grow to the same height? So it’ll be fair?” - Vanessa

    “I don’t want to risk growing anyone and having the entire gymnasium destroyed.”- Abigail

    “Oh alright” - Anne

    “Now let’s see how well you all play now” - Abigail

    Everyone on the team played better than any professional team. She could take them to Nationals and they’d win by a landslide. Sadly playing ball was a hobby to all of them.

    After practice, they were all drenched in sweat.

    “How are we supposed to change and dry off? Everythings too small” - Gloria

    “Just take a break, I need to go take a call.” - Abigail. She then left the 5 giants alone with the growing and shrinking water.

    Anne saw this and thought only a little wouldn’t hurt, besides, she was supposed to be the tallest anyways. She tried to make a grab for it but Britney grabbed her.

    “Anne no, you might destroy the whole building if you drink that. - Britney

    Anne still managed to grab the water but Britney accidentally slapped it out of her hand. It went up in the air, and the water came out of it.
