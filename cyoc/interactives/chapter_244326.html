    Gloria thought carefully about the choices. Based on what little she knew of them, she sorted them out carefully into two columns.

    On screen, an animation showed two ballot boxes filling up with votes.

    "Alright, votes are coming in fast!" said Lesily, bouncing on the balls of her feet excitedly. "Here we go, just a few more..."

    A little chime rang, and a big "100%" flashed on the screen, followed by the results:

    FAMILY 1: Dave, Kevin
    FAMILY 2: Rose, Terri, Jessica

    "Aw, that's cute", said Lesily with a big grin. "Looks like you decided to group the boys and the girls separately. Not that they might stay boys or girls for long..."

    She turned to the five on stage and gently nudges them, separating Dave and Kevin from Rose and the daughters.

    "Okay!" Lesily clapped her hands as they looked at her. "Now to see what bright future the audience decided, starting with the boys, shall we?"

    A roulette-wheel animation started spinning on screen, and when it stopped the results appeared.
