    Carrie made the decision to try getting away in the nearby part, as she made her way in she could hear some kids giggling but wasn't willing to stop and ask what it was about.
    She ran down a trail till she reached a clearing, in the middle was a bench that sat underneath a lone tree, Carrie decided she has gone far enough for now and decides to take a break on the bench.
    Meanwhile John decided to ask around to find where Carrie went.
    "Yeah I think I saw her *giggles* she went into Nekomimi Park, it's the place of one our favorite games, all you have to do is follow the trail and return back here without turning back." Said one girl.
    "Doesn't sound too bad."
    "That's where the fun part comes from, you have to come back here, but only humans can get to this side of the park.*giggle*"
    "Wait, what?"
    Back in the park, carrie was relaxing on the bench, but unknown to her was the kid hiding in three branch above her.
    A sudden giggle was the only warning before the mysterious girl dropped on top of Carrie and gave her a kiss on the lips.
    "I win-nya!" Said the girl.
    "W-why'd you do that?" Carrie asked, flustered, but she soon noticed the girls features, she had long pink hair that went down to her shoulders and was wearing a japanese schoolgirls uniform like the one Carrie was wearing earlier, but the big thing was the pair of cat ears on her head, and the tail swishing behind her, both the same pink as her hair.
    "Nya? Nyu mean you don't know-nya?"
    "N-Nyo... Wait, did I just lisp-nya? Nya?" Carrie suddenly realized her she meowing while speaking. "What did nyu do to me-nya?"
    "Easy, I caught nyu, it's a game we play between the two halves of the Be-A-Kid Zone-Nya, it's called hunt. See, Nyekomimi aren't allowed on the nyuman side, and nyumans aren't allowed on our side, but us nyeko have the ability to turn nyumans into nyekomimi by kissing them-nya" The girl explained. "It should be starting right about now-nya."
    After she said that, Carrie's hair turned bright blue and her hearing went out, just to come back in a way she never experienced before, it was like she was blind all her life and only now could she see, her hearing was so good that she felt like her eyes weren't needed anymore.
    "Wow... It's amazing-nya, how did I go so long without these-nya?"
    "If nyu think those are great then this part is going to be a real treat-nya!" as she said that, Carrie felt a pulling sensation from her spine and found herself looking over her shoulder and watching as a little blue snake wiggled it's way out from under her dress, and in that moment she found herself wanting to lean forward a bit more, that's when she realized her legs were slightly shorter and in a quick movement she found she could walk on either two legs or four.
    After getting used to walking on all fours Carrie felt really comfortable.
    "I guess I should introduce my self, I'm Nyeko-Carrie-chan-nya... erm, Nyeko... Nyeko-Carrie-chan... Nya?"
    The girl just giggled. "I'm Nyeko-Lily-chan-nya." Lily said. "So why'd you come in here if you weren't playing hunt-nya?"
    "I was playing tag with my husband-nya, I wasn't really paying attention-nya..."
    Suddenly the two heard a noise and their ears turned towards the direction it came from, they were footsteps.
    "I think that's him-nya!" Neko-Carrie-chan said.
    "hehe, in that case this ones nyours Nyeko-Carrie-chan, have fun hunting-nya!" Neko-Lily-chan then returned into the tree.
    "Hehe, I think I will, I'll see nyu after I give my sweetie a kiss-nya!" Carrie then ran into the bushes and moved through them silently, at least so long as your not a nekomimi.
