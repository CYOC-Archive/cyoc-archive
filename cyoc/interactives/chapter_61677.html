    Solange sighed, rubbing her temples. "I'm going to go home..I need
    some rest if I'm to be here in the morning. I..I want to call out, but
    it is best not to raise suspicions, yes?"

    Lisa nodded, "Everyone..we need to act as though NOTHING is wrong.
    YES, protect your children. Travel in pairs...but go to work. Go out.
    No need to give the government any extra ammunition to justify more
    bloodshed at this point."

    The pack all nodded slowly, though Debra barely acknowledged she'd
    spoken, instead staring out the window. A chill had gone through her
    when Lisa and Brenda had their dominance issue. From time to time,
    she'd caught sight of a rather ruthless streak in Lisa, and she'd seen
    it again tonight. It terrified her and repulsed her. She was no
    fool...she could see Lisa debating the merits of a war.

    "You riding with me, Lisa?" she asked quietly.

    "No..I'm going to do some..thinking," Lisa replied thoughtfully and
    free of discernable emotion. "I'm sure I can get home safe by my own
    devices."

    Debra sighed and left quickly, clearly bothered by the cold,
    unreadable tone. Brenda nodded, leaving quickly as well. She was still
    clearly angered, but didn't want the fight that too much more attitude
    would bring.

    "Lock the door on your way out.." Solange offered. It was her office;
    she had every right to TELL Lisa to lock the door, but it was clearly
    just a request or suggestion. LISA ruled this pack; of that there was
    no question.

    Ebony and Sharon left after quiet goodbyes, and now it was just Anaya
    and Lisa alone. As the door shut, and the quiet hispanic woman who
    bore more than a passing resemblance to Angelina Jolie stood there, it
    was her voice who broke the silence.

    "You're right," she said softly, the barest hint of a smile in her
    eyes. Anaya was no fool; this was the sort of moment she'd needed to
    gain status in the pack.

    Lisa sighed heavily. "At least one person understands that." The
    facade of being diplomatic began to fade. "They ALL seem to think that
    we should sit and do NOTHING, like helpless pups!"

    Anaya moved slowly closer, sashaying with a slightly sexual quality in
    her walk. "That's the last thing we should do," she said, her voice
    low, a heavy, smoky undertone in her words.

    "I'm going to trust you with a task," Lisa said, reclining back in her
    chair, pushing it away from the desk.

    "Anything for my alpha," Anaya replied, walking closer. "ANYTHING."
    She leaned forward over the desk, letting her ample chest lay on the
    top, her cleavage spilling out of her half-buttoned and cleavage
    bearing shirt.

    Lisa smiled, staring at Anaya's chest, before glancing up to make eye
    contact. Even the alpha wasn't immune to pheremones, and the high
    adrenalin of the night had only made her senses sharper, and therefore
    more susceptible.

    Anaya took the subtle cue, moving around the desk to massage Lisa's
    shoulders. "You're tense...you need to..blow off some steam.." Lisa
    moaned softly, smiling as Anaya worked on her muscles. "So tell
    me...how can I serve you?" Anaya whispered into Lisa's ear, her lips
    brushing against the lobe.

    "Recruitment," Lisa told her. "Starting tonight at my gym. I want to
    keep this pack female ONLY," she said, emphasizing the point. "Use
    your...feminine wiles.." she smirked, "and get us a couple new
    recruits from the lockerroom..." There was another pause. "And force
    it on them if they resist." The cold tone was back, cold and ruthless.

    "As you wish," Anaya said, nuzzling her face against the nape of
    Lisa's neck. "My alpha."

    Lisa reached back, stroking Anaya's face tenderly as she tilted her
    head to the side with a slight shiver. "Such a loyal pack member.."
    she softly whispered.

    Anaya leaned in again, whispering seductively once more, "Is there any
    OTHER way I could...SERVE you?"
