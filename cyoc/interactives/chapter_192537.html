    Transform or Dare?

    Daikaijū Jennifer

    Previous chapter

    added A year ago O

    "You know what Kyle, I think your pretty pathetic if you think cursing me is gonna improve your position with the rest of the school. Even if the curse is real and I end up losing everything I know and love, it's not gonna make any real change in your life in the slightest because you're a loser now and you'll remain a loser afterwards and the fact you don't realize that is really sad. So go ahead, give me both, and let's see if your worthless life actually improves in anyway while I end up as some colossal freak of nature that fights Godzilla or something. What do you say to that, Kyle?" Jennifer said hoping to call Kyle's bluff. But Kyle wasn't bluffing and responds back.
    "Wow Jen, I wasn't expecting you to go all psychological on me. I'm almost impressed. But I don't care about my school status, all I want is to get revenge on you. And since you just gave me a possible outcome of what I MIGHT turn you into, I think I'm gonna use something similar to that as your curse. Now listen carefully, for your transformation, you will turn into a giant monster. Now the actual method of how, where and when you'll change will be left to chance but it will happen at some point today. Once you start becoming a giant monster, you will no longer be able to speak any human languages. You can only make sounds any other monster would make or remain silent depending on what you turn into. And for your dare, once you fully transform into a giant monster, you will immediately start going on a rampage, leaving me unharmed if I happen to be in the path of your destruction, and you won't stop until you get tired and fall asleep. Well, that's all I can give to you. I would say its been nice knowing you but that would be a lie. Good luck in your new life, you're gonna need it." And with that, Kyle hung up the phone and left Jennifer to ponder at what he just cursed her with.
    "Am I really gonna turn into some horrible giant freak of nature?" Jennifer thinks to herself. Then she tries to deny Kyle's claim.
    "No, of course not, Kyle's just trying to scare me. The curse isn't real, there's no way that could really happen to me." Jennifer then locates the rest of her clothing, packs everything she needs for school and walks out the front door. Still, the thought of Kyle's curse hadn't left Jennifer's mind and she wondered. If she were to become a giant monster, where would her transformation take place?
