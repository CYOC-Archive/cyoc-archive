    Meanwhile, on a certain college campus, Jin was heading to his next class, feeling more confident than ever before. As he was walking, he noticed the stares of both men and women around campus. Usually, none of them wouldn't pay him any mind as the nerdy Asian walked by, but now, all of them seemed to be interested in him, if not enamored. A smile and a wink were all he gave to his admirers. He would have flexed for them as his brother did, but Jin wasn't too confident flexing yet. He hadn't researched the correct form or practiced it like Danny had.

    Speaking of his brother, Jin had to admit he was wrong to judge his brother for working out and focusing on bodybuilding so much. He used to think that what Danny did was a waste of time. That he should have been focusing more on his studies and his future. Now, though, Jin finally understood why his brother trained so hard and kept track of what he ate and how long he exercised so meticulously. The feelings of overwhelming strength he felt and the boundless energy he had were intoxicating. Jin couldn't wait for his first workout with Danny to see just how far he could go with his new body. The attention he received from everyone was a nice perk, as well.

    Speaking of attention, it seems like Jin had attracted the attention of someone he knew.

    "J-Jin? Is that you?" Jin turned around to encounter one of his DnD friends, Art Morris. Art was a tall, thin, and somewhat scruffy young man staring at Jin with a shocked expression. After all, the last time Art had seen his friend, he was your stereotypical Asian nerd. Now Jin had gained at least 30lbs of muscle.

    "Yeah, of course, it's me," Jin decided to flex one of his arms for Art. If only to see how his friend's eyes widened as his bicep rose and hardened. "Don't worry, I'm still me where it counts. Just buffer and a lot more confident."

    "If you say so..." Clearly, Art was still a bit wary of this situation. Not that Jin could blame him. The changes started after Art got sick with food poisoning four days ago. So coming back to see his friend look like a different person must have been shocking, to say the least, "But how did this happen?"

    "Well," Jin began. He wanted to say he had no clue, but now that he thought of it, Jin might have a hunch on who was responsible for the odd going on that had been happening. "Tell you what. Tomorrow I'm going to the gym to work out with my brother. Why don't you tag along? Maybe we could figure out what's going on there."

    "The Gym near your house, right? Sure... I guess I could use some exercise..." Art was still wary, but at least he had agreed to Jin's invitation. After that, Jin talked a little while longer with his friend before heading to class. Jin smirked as he left. He couldn't wait until tomorrow to ask Danny if all of this was his doing and if he could secretly change Art, as well.
