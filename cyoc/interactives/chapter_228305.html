    School Full of Anthros

    added by Maxxatom A month ago O

    After choosing the school, your parents help you pack up and drive you off to your new school. Seeing the campus out all of you in awe, the grounds were massive, stretching literally as far as the eye could see. The students and teachers all seemed to be bipedal, but all of them looked like some type of animal. Both you and your parents saw horses, lions, bears, snakes, eagles, and even a few bees, all walking around and living peacefully together.

    After what feels like a 10 minute drive from the entrance, your parents finally reach the admissions office and drop you off. In front of you stand a massive elephant anthro with tiny glasses, offering you a large, leathery hand to guide you inside. You wave to your parents as they drive away, you struggle to pick up all your bags as you walk inside the largest office outside of a city building you’ve ever seen. The ceilings are at least 50ft high, and the doorways are massive (though with the size of your elephant guide, you can see why).

    “Mr. Morgan, I presume? Welcome to your new life and Ecohearts Taming Academy, where we foster the best life for all creatures. Seeing as you are a new student, we just have to ask you a few questions. Bear with us, this will only take but a moment.”

    You giggle to yourself at the pun, but this was it, finally time to make your new life over. No more bullying, no more loneliness, no more pain. It’s a new start for a new Morgan.

    What do you wish to become?
