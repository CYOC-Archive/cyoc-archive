    Alice awoke some time later, and surveyed the chaos. While that had been extremely enjoyable, she realized she'd crossed a line, and she needed to fix this. But how? "I wish everyone in this building who was transformed will remain asleep until I clap my hands." That should buy her time.

    She'd destroyed families, relationships, and lives. She could just change everyone back and make them forget this all had happened, but she rather liked her small army of cute lesbians. She aimed to keep them, if she could. "I wish everyone here was perfectly clean and healthy, and wearing fresh clothing appropriate for their physical age and gender."

    In a flash of light, everyone (including her, oops) was wearing a new outfit. For most of the girls present, said outfit was a tartan skirt, bobby socks, Mary Janes, and a white blouse. "I really need to start being more specific with these wishes." Now they all looked like Catholic school girls!

    'Wait, maybe I can use this...' "I wish all the children who were too young to have sex will be returned to their homes safe and sound. Everyone besides myself will believe they have always been girls. If one of their parents is missing or is present here, the remaining parent will encounter someone new who will be perfect for them and their children as a replacement parent. Everyone will believe that person has always been their parent. If both parents are missing or is present here, the children will instead gain two new parents, who are perfect for them and each other, and everyone will believe those have always been their parents."

    She paused to take a breath as various children vanished from the museum. "I wish that there was a private school/orphanage for girls in a nearby very nice and wealthy suburb. The school will be very prestigious, with the best possible staff and teachers, all of whom care for the children in their charge very much. All of the girls present in the museum will be students at the school. All of the girls present will be friends and get along well. Everyone besides myself will believe we have always been orphans, cute girls, and students there. Everyone here will retain all of their knowledge, even though their memories may change due to this wish."

    Another breath, these run-on wishes were tough! "I wish everyone here will be in love with me, though this love will not harm or damage any existing relationships they have with the other girls. I wish that the school will be very open and supportive of our relationships and sexuality, and we are allowed to have sex with one another. I wish that all the girls here will be completely healthy and immune to any sort of disease for the rest of their lives. I wish that all the girls here were comfortable and unashamed of their sexuality. I wish that all the girls here were comfortable and unashamed of being naked, they enjoyed not having to wear clothing, and that the school has a clothing-optional dress code."

    "And when I clap my hands, I wish we were all in our dormitories at school, which are very nice." And with that, she clapped her hands.

    She found herself in a beautiful room for two. The floors and furniture were hardwood, everything was comfortably lit, and it looked more like a fancy hotel than a room at an orphanage. Laying on one of the beds, stirring, was a familiar looking 12 year old girl with blonde hair and green eyes. Alice blushed as she recalled what she had done with the girl recently.

    The girl looked a bit confused, then she smiled. "Alice, I must have fallen asleep on the way back from the museum."

    "Yes, that's right. I carried you to your bed." Alice lied.

    "Oh Alice, I wish I'd been awake for that. You've been so good to me." The girl's smile brightened. "I dreamed that we did something naughty at the museum."

    "Did you? What was that?"

    The younger girl blushed. "Uh...you were laying down, and I crouched over you, and you did that thing with your tongue."

    "Did you like it?"

    The girl nodded. "Uh-huh."

    Alice grinned. "Do you want to do that now?"

    "Oh, Alice, not until lights out. You know the rules!"

    Alice walked over and sat on the girl's bed. "Well surely we can do something."

    The girl fidgeted with her hands. "You could kiss me. If you want. I know we're...just friends and roommates, but I really like you, Alice."

    Alice leaned in close and kissed the girl, who shyly responded. She was a sweet kisser, Alice realized. "We don't have to be just friends. If you wanted to be my girlfriend, I'd like that."

    The girl's eyes got very large. "Really? But..I mean...all the other girls like you too, you could have anyone...I'm just a kid, and I'm scrawny with no figure..." Alice silenced her with another kiss. "I don't care about that, you're a wonderful person, and I like the way you kiss."

    The girl blushed even more furiously. "Yes, I'd love to be your girlfriend! Oh Alice, you've made me so happy!"

    The girl was named Amy, as Alice found out later. She was a very sweet and charming thing, and if her body was still underdeveloped, well, that didn't really matter. Of course, she quickly found out, even when two girls were in a relationship, that didn't preclude having sex with other girls. They were all sisters, after all, they had been taught to share everything. Alice had reached into her purse and given the other girl a beautiful silver ring set with an emerald, however, so everyone would know Amy was HERS.

    And at lights out, they were both very naughty indeed. Life was good.
