    Gnaw was standing there holding a green scaled dragon girl,"Hey guys, I'm back this is my new daughter her name is Jade." Gnaw said. "H....hello," Jade said, and Hunter looked at Jade oddly,"She said that pretty well, and she's only two as well." Hunter said.

    Jade then looked at Hunter,"How do you know dat?" Jade asked

    "Umm why's Jade already saying sentences? Mine can barely walk, much less speak." Hunter asked

    "I know why, my mom always said that I was speaking sentences too when she first found me. Dragons are smarter then most species so that should be normal, but it's good by the time I was eight I was already adept in AP calculus. Jade will just have to find other dragons to play with, if not she could always visit me. Also, further along with life there's a thing that females do that guys won't understand much, so when that happens call me Gnaw." Sierra said, and Gnaw nodded as Sierra left.

    "Anyways have you filled out Jade's documents yet?" Hunter asked

    "Yep, just finished them, and I thought that we should pay a visit. So where's your kid?" Gnaw asked

    Winter was hiding behind Hunter,"He's here, come on Winter it's fine their friends." Hunter said as he picked up Winter.

    "Wow, the only thing that would make things more ridiculous between the two of you would be if he was a tiger like you." Gnaw said

    "I know right, here come inside, this is Winter and he's a snow leopard." Hunter said,"My dad is coming over later, and Sierra already payed us a visit to possibly set up a play date with our kids."

    "Wait, Sierra has kids? Crap why didn't I notice, she's a year older than us?" Gnaw said

    "Well that makes two of us, anyways she has two tigers cubs that are identical, apparently." Hunter said

    "Interesting, maybe I should find friends for Jade here who are older, but not to the point of bullying." Gnaw said

    "I like that, Dada." Jade said to Gnaw making him blush slightly, as the two entered the room.

    "Can Jade walk?" Hunter asked

    "No, we tried that, but she's still too weak, maybe try again in a while." Gnaw said.

    "Well, Winter seems to have a fierce playful nature, it's cute to watch." Hunter said as he was holding a piece of yarn with a ball on it and Winter was batting at it.

    "Actually I think it's because of his instincts, remember how mesmerized you used to get when we had tried to hypnotize you when we were kids?" Gnaw said

    "Mes..mes..mer..riss..ed?" Jade said

    "Oh Jade don't worry about large words like that until you're older, I think you dad will agree. Besides I see where your coming from, and sometimes I still get caught in a trance. Besides the only thing I recommend is to avoid touching Jade, or Winters heads. I don't know when this issue will end, but I heard that right now they're rather soft, and you could really mess up their minds if you do it." Hunter said

    "Alright noted, and Jade, Hunter here is right you shouldn't worry until you take some spelling lessons. Got it?" Gnaw said, and Jade nodded.

    "Guess you got lucky and really didn't need to prepare much with her, well not until the things that Sierra said start kicking in, and when they do good luck." Hunter said

    "Things?" Jade asked

    "Nothing you need to know about right now, like with the big words you'll learn about it when you're older." Gnaw said

    "Hey, if you plan on staying until my dad arrives we can see if Jade can teach Winter a few words." Hunter said

    "That's true, so Jade do you want to help teach our little friend here some words you know?" Gnaw asked

    "Okay," Jade said cheerfully
