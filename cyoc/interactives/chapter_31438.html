    The first thing Luke noticed when he went into the mall was one of those little kiosks that sold all sorts of jewelry. What really caught his attention was that they were having a sale where if you got your ears pierced for ten dollars you got another pair of earrings for free. Since he wanted to get something new to wear he figured that he might as well go the whole way and get some jewelry as well.

    At first he just wanted to find two pairs of earrings he liked, but the necklaces and bracelets caught his attention as well. He was drawn to how feminine they looked and how feminine he would look wearing them. In the end he decided his first pair of earrings would be ones with little white pearls and his second pair would be the ones in the shape of butterflies. The best part about of the butterfly earrings was that they were part of a set with a necklace and bracelet that had butterfly charms on them matching those on the earrings. They looked just like something a girl in her early teens would wear, but he liked them.

    After paying for his purchase he set out to find a store where he could get some bras and panties and wondering how he get fashion tips from Tiff without letting on that he was interested in dressing up like a girl.

    He felt a little odd buying bras and panties since he was the only guy in the store and the way the cashier looked at him made him decidedly uncomfortable, but he left feeling triumphant. Now he owned two bras, one pink and lacy and the other more plain and practical, as well as several pairs of panties in assorted styles.

    Now all he had left to do was get a new top and some new pants. For a moment he had considered that he might want a skirt, but he decided that was too sissy and just because he had discovered how nice it was to wear girls' clothes didn't make him a sissy. Girls' clothes were just so much more comfortable and better looking and that was all there was to it.

    Picking out shirts took him a lot longer than he expected because of all the different types there were to look at, but eventually he decided on four that he thought would look good on him.

    It was when he was picking out jeans, admiring a pair adorned with sequins, that he heard his sister's voice. He ducked behind the rack of jeans, hoping that she would walk on by.

    "Katie, I really don't think you need new jeans, now can we please get out of here and get back to the house?"

    Luke rolled his eyes, listening to his sister arguing with her friend about clothing. It was just like girls to get wound up about stuff like that. Of course as nice as girls' clothes were he could sort of understand.

    "Come on Tiff, just this one pair of jeans, then we'll have to go back to your house so I can change. After that we can go back to that dusty old place."

    "Alright. Huh, Luke?"

    Luke smiled nervously at his sister, "Hi Tiff, what a surprise?"

    Tiff squinted, studying him like he was one of her freaky experiments. A look of horror spread across her face and he was sure that she had realized that he was wearing her makeup.

    "Oh no! Luke, you ate one of those fruits, didn't you?"

    Luke nearly went limp with relief, if her dumb experiment was all she was worried about he was probably off the hook, "Yeah, but just one. The pink plum looking one."

    "We need to go now," she grabbed him by the wrist and began to drag him away.

    "Not until I get my jeans!" Katie protested.

    Tiff looked exasperated, but relented, "Fine."

    Luke broke away from her grip, "There's some stuff I need to pay for too."

    Apparently Tiff didn't want to argue with both him and Katie because she sighed.

    "Fine, but hurry and when we get home you're going to tell me everything."

    "Sure, whatever," Luke walked away with Katie to pay for his new clothing.

    It was a shame he bumped into Tiff like that, because he would have liked to have gone shoe shopping before leaving.
