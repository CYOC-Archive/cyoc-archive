    Just as Sean was thinking of all the devious things he could do with this, a new window popped up.

    "Enjoying Chronivac? Well, as a special offer, we are going to give you special access to the newest version for FREE!

    Here are all the features you will have access to:

    -Newer, more specific transformations.
    -"Inanimate" option added
    -New Game Modes

    And More!

    Sign up today!"

    Without hesitating, Sean clicked okay and was immediately brought to another 5 minute download process. Once it was done, the interface had expanded and there were all new tabs. One tab in particular caught his attention: "Roulette"

    Once he clicked on it, a big red button appeared on his screen, and then a text box that read:

    "Welcome to Roulette!

    Roulette is a type of randomizing transformation game. Can't pick a transformation? Test your luck on Roulette.

    Further More, Roulette is compatible with multiple users. Whoever clicks the button gets the transformation. Gather friends and see who gets the luckiest."

    At that moment, Jay and Dan walked into the apartment. They were shocked to see their newly buffer roommate.

    "What happened to you?" Jay exclaimed. "When did you get muscular?"

    "Oh, yeah, this?" Sean cockily flexed his newly toned arms. "This came from a little something I bought."

    "Okay, what was it and how can I get it?" Dan asked.

    Sean then spent the next hour or so explaining what the Chronivac was.

    "And I had an idea." He opened Roulette and explained it to them. "Maybe we could take turns and see who gets luckiest."

    Dan's mind was cranking until he got an idea. "Or, how about this: we keep using this and getting whatever we get. Whoever of us quits first get stuck with whatever they get forever and whoever wins gets to use this program all they want and decide if they want to change the second place person back to normal or if they too have to stay like that forever."

    Sean and Jay discussed for a brief moment. "Okay, but you have to go first." They both decided on.

    "Okay, fine." Dan said without a hint of fear or cockiness in his words.

    He moved the mouse to the button and read a side message that said "transform Dan?"

    "Ready?" Dan said, more excited than scared. "3, 2, 1, GO!" And he clicked he button.
