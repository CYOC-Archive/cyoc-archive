    Have you ever saw a child and thought, “wow I wish that were still me”. Everyone missed their childhood youth at some point, so why miss it.

    Introducing the new innovation in self care: Regressive Care.

    At Regressive Care, you’ll be allowed to purchase any products ranging from a simple lotion to medication to allow your body to temporarily regress back to a certain age.

    Even if you are not satisfied with Regressive Care, we will provide a 30 day refund if you return the product. Anything that is partially used more than 1/3 of their container shall not be returned.

    When you choose to be regressed to an age where you don’t have any clothing for, our employees will offer any protective undergarments that will fit perfect for your desired age.

    Locate the nearest Regressive Care store location near you to visit your lost childhood today!
