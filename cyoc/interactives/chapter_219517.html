    You thought you might as well indulge the young lad Robbie having always been a good boy but he’s far too young to drive so you were going to him one better. You knew that you’d have to speak quickly and precisely because you were still audible. Thinking about it for a few minutes as the young children stare bewildered around the house and discuss it amongst themselves you hurriedly spoke almost like an auctioneer.

    “I wish that Robert would go out to the driveway and transform into the car of his dreams, everyone outside this house would think it was natural for him to do that, and Katherine would calmly sit on the sofa for an hour!”

    The kids looked around terrified before both Katie and Robbie drifted off into a trance and began doing as they were ordered Kate plunked down onto the sofa completely turned out with a slightly bored expression on her face as Robbie wandered off into the driveway following him through the door he left ajar you stared in fascination as he started hunching over in pain his boyish yelping and grunting. Gaining weight throughout his entire body you could only watch bewildered as his clothes ripped off from his body as his arms became more rubbery looking. Crying and grunting in pain his anus extended into a tailpipe while his skin turned metallic and yellow as his boyish privates withered away. You couldn’t help but let out a small chuckle hearing his cries becoming gurgling honking noises, at this point Robbie’s hair receded into his morphing oddly shaped head as his metallic teeth fused together into a grill shaped forced smile and his now big bulky morning body really stepping up the transformation as his eyes began resembling headlights. A few moments later you were staring at a shiny brand new yellow Porsche similar to a toy you remember Robert having.

    An elderly man clipping his rhododendrons nearby commented in disgust
    “I knew that kid had a fatal illness where he was going to permanently transform into a luxury car but that was revolting!”
