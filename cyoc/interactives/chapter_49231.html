    While all of this was going on, someone had walked into the greenhouse
    to watch this horrid event take place. He smiled as he saw his plants
    take up the next twin. Travis suddenly had a second wind to fight
    back.

    With large amounts of adrenalin pumping through his body, he had a lot
    more strength than before. The vines holding him strained as he pulled
    his arms away from them. Right as the pink flower was about to engulf
    his head, Travis got his left arm free. He punched the flower away
    from his face. It seemed to cry out when he hit it.

    He then pulled his other arm free, and began ripping the plants away
    from his legs. He ran to brother, and tackled the figurine. He hit it
    with enough force to knock himself and what used to be his brother
    onto the path.

    The plants did not bother him as soon as he was on the path. All the
    plants returned to how they were, as if nothing had happened. Thats
    when Travis noticed that he had broken the statue of his brother to pieces. At first, he was horrified that he had destroyed his one
    chance at maybe saving his brother, but then he noticed something.

    Among the pieces of wood and wax, there was a small 10 inch teen boy
    who looked exactly like him. Suddenly, if glowed slightly, and then
    began to grow. With in a few moments, the teen was full size. The teen
    looked around, unsure of what happened. Then his eyes landed on
    Travis. "T..tra...travis...Travis!" He called.

    Travis looked at the identical teen confused, and then realized it was
    his brother. "Conner!" Travis cried. He jumped him, and the two began
    rolling on the floor, bear hugging each other.

    The man saw this, and was really angry. Somehow this boy managed to thwart his darling plants. No matter he thought. He could always turn
    them into his wax figurines himself...but. Something about these two.
    He came up with a brilliant idea. Identical figurines of horror might
    be good as a set, but one figurine of the two might be better.
