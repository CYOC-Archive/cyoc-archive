    But before Ted Green could complete the deed, there was a knock on their motel room door.
    "Not expecting anyone, are you?" he whispered, to which Marci shook her head negatively.
    Reluctantly getting up, FBI agent training took over as Theodore Green approached the door at an angle so that he wasn't directly in front of it.
    "Who is it?" he called out.
    "Lila Carpenter. I brought lunch," came the reply.
    Ted turned to look at his wife. Marci just shrugged her shoulders and nodded her head that he should let their visitor in. Although grateful to the lady as their benefactor, Lila Carpenter had made it quite clear that she wouldn't mind an encore–or more–with Ted and/or Marci.

Moe's Pizza has this meat lover's special that just about every were in Moon Lake swears by," she added, as Ted closed the hotel room door behind her.

    "It's a clothing store here in town that especially caters to weres," explained Lila.
    "Samantha told me about it. I was planning to take him tomorrow," replied Marci, getting up to take a better sniff of the pizza aroma coming from the boxes.
    "Good. Now, I'm sure you two have worked up a serious appetite, so dig in," said Lila, while grabbing a slice for herself. And I certainly know what I want for desert! she silently added, while eyeing both Marci and Ted Green.
