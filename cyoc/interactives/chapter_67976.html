    Ty decided to wait and see what happens to his new body. Ty decided to run laps. First, with an interesting wish, 'I wish that the
    testosterone in my body increased three-fold.' He felt a weird jolt down his feathery body. He ignored it and went to running laps. After 2
    laps, his legs were starting to get tired, and he noticed them filling out with new muscle while running, his taloned feet getting larger. He
    thought it was an optical illusion, and went back to running. After the fourth lap passed, he noticed that he was taller, much taller, and
    his legs were huge! Pulsing with muscle, and veins sticking out like a blue spaghetti noodle. This was weird and awesome at the same
    time, but his legs now looked disproportionate to his body. He went to weight lifting again, and chose the 'bicep flex' excersize. He
    started this time with 75 pounds. Getting tired after say 20 reps each arm, he stopped for a minute, and noticed that his pecs had bulged
    out a little more and his arms were huge! His wingspan had also grown two feet wider so he observed after unfolding his wings. So the
    wish he made was making his muscles grow drastically faster than they should have been. What did Ty do next?
