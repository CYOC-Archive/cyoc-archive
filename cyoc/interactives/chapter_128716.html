    After some thought and consideration, I decided against taking a second break... at least not yet. For those who read my writer's note from "The Toy Box" let me clarify. It's not that I'm running out of ideas for the basement arc. Far from it. I actually had a lot planed out for the the basement arc. There are Ideas for the basement arc that I want to write out some day. It's just that the basement story arc started feeling more like work then something fun to do on my free time. It's also because the basement storyline gotten so complicated, that even I had a headache keeping track of every moving part. There was so many little details to keep track of, I noticed that other writers had a hard time keeping track of everything too. In other words, that story line became too hard to add on to. It was too complicated for it's own good... At least from my point of view.

    So I decided to start a fresh new storyline. The story was in the middle of the sky king arc, and then the basement arc interrupted it. The basement arc was starting to feel like filler to the Sky King arc. So this time, I want to try a different storyline. Keep in mind, I haven't given up on the basement arc. I will most likely adapt some of it's concepts and ideas into this storyline. But that is down the road. I (hopefully) learned my lesson. I had been brainstorming a lot of ideas involving the cloud plane arc for weeks during the time I was focusing on the basement storyline and now it's time to finally put them to use.

    I've been itching to return to the Sky King arc for weeks. This storyline has a lot of ground to cover if it's to catch up to the anonymous writer who wrote "Getting closer to the Castle". I learned a lot from the basement storyline and I'm willing to use what I learned to kick this story into high gear! And I hope you're willing too!
