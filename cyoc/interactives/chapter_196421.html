    "Oh a classic, uh, hang on." Elena got out her laptop and booted it up. Then she googled "classic convertible" and found a red Alfa Romeo Duetto Spider. "What about this?"

    Sahar nodded enthusiastically. "Oh I like that one, Elena! Yes, I think I can arrange for that. Perhaps my 'father' owns a fleet of such cars?"

    "This guy isn't real, is he?"

    "Oh, I'm sure I could find such a person if needed. Shall we go for a ride, then?"

    "Darn. I'd love to, but I need to finish my paper. It's why I was going to the Library, stupid internet is busted."

    "Hmph! I am the daughter of a rich man, surely I would arrange for the best internet." There was no flash of light, but something had changed.

    Elena checked and sure enough, her laptop was getting an internet signal. A very strong one, at that. "My laptop probably can't use all this speed, but thanks, this helps."

    "Of course." Sahar pulled up a chair (that hadn't existed before) and sat close to Elena. There was a pleasant scent coming off of her that reminded Elena of her Dad's old cedar chest. Sahar leaned her head on Elena's shoulder as she worked.

    It felt nice, and Elena was inspired to be very productive. About an hour later, she saved her paper and emailed it to her teacher. "There, done."

    "I redecorated a little while you were busy." Sahar said, sitting upright.

    Elena looked around. While the decor of her room was mostly unchanged, the walls having posters of anime characters and fantastic landscapes, one third of the room was now decidedly more...girly, for lack of a better word. There was now a second dresser, and a comfy-looking couch. Her bed had been replaced by a large, King-sized mattress that looked quite comfy. "One bed?"

    "Oh, forgive me, did you want separate beds? Surely there is no stigma for two girls to share a bed?"

    "No, no, I guess that's fine. I'm told I snore though, and I move around a lot."

    "These things do not bother me. I do not need to sleep, though it feels good to relax. I made a few upgrades to your home, courtesy of my 'father' and his money."

    "Wow. You're amazing, you know that?"

    "I am glad you think so, Elena." Sahar seemed pleased. "So, what shall we do now?"
