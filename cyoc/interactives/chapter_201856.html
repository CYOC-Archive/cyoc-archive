    "That was so cool," Tricia sighed with delight. She had returned to her original proportions; her body, height, and hair were back to normal, and she was clothed once again. She was laying on her bed, on her stomach and swinging her legs in the air as she looked over the remote's other options.

    She thought it'd be nice to have a change that would let her go out in public. Shrinking or growing was too obviously supernatural if done in exaggerated amounts, but a little change couldn't hurt. She wanted something more than just that though. A complete makeover.

    Her mind flew to a few years back when she had briefly wanted to try an ultra girly phase. Unfortunately she didn't have the money to buy an entire set of outfits, accessories, and wigs. Her parents had refused to buy that kind of stuff for her. Now, on her own for the first time and armed with the remote, she figured it might be worth a shot.

    As she scrolled the the options, Tricia realized she could do far more than just give it a try. She found that the remote could give her any type of clothing and jewelry she wanted, alongside the physical types of changes she had already tested. Tricia also had some ideas for new physical adjustments to make sure she was the cutest and girliest girl she could ever have hoped to be. She went over to her mirror and began to work.

    First, she used the shrinking button to take herself from 5'4" down to 4'10". The usual shifting of her clothes ensued. Even a mere six-inch shrink left her with baggy pants and sleeves that threatened to swallow her hands. She stood there for a moment and scrunched her toes in her loose socks before sliding her too-big sleeves off her hands so she could still use the remote properly.

    She pressed another button and her skin became smoother. Scars and blemishes were wiped away as her skin became uniformly soft and clear. She zapped herself a third time and felt her breasts slowly shrink inside her bra cups,, going from C-cups to B-cups. One more zap caused a small bit of fat and muscle to either disappear from her frame or shift, leaving her thin and dainty, but with an acceptable bit of shapeliness to her form.

    Now it was time for the clothing. She disrobed completely, taking note of her body's changes in the mirror. She was quite short for her age, with a slender neck and thin, lissome limbs. Her complexion and form looked healthy, yet as if she consciously avoided too much sunlight or too much physical exertion. The smallest hint of rosiness colored her cheeks, and she licked her pink lips, finding them softer. Her breasts had been reduced to a humble B-cup. She looked the epitome of a cute and dainty little girl.

    Seeing herself like this made her feel girly as well, and she clasped her hands in front of her, kicking a dainty foot up behind her as she did so. She had always been happy with her body and despite her interest in changing styles, the prospect of losing it, even for a little while, made her a little nervous. Fortunately, it was counter-balanced by the excitement she felt at becoming a totally new person. She knew she could use these feelings, drawing from them the inspiration to create her wardrobe.

    The question was what direction to take the outfit and hair? She considered doing a stereotypical schoolgirl outfit in mostly navy and white, a flowy sundress look, or a more over the top, fancy and frilly outfit. There were other directions she could go too, like the kawaii or pastel fashions.

    She eventually settled on a style close to what she wanted to try in the past...
