    “Well I have some good news and some bad news.” Said the Doctor as the entire Maxwell Family held their breaths waiting to hear what cruel fate was going to fall on them.

    “The Good news is that while all of you are transforming you are all becoming the same type of monster therefor will have each other for support.” started the Doctor “The Bad news is that you are becoming what we like to call class A monsters.”

    “What does that mean?” inquired Mike Maxwell

    “We have divided the monsters into different classes, class D is basically human with some minor changes. These are your people who get cat ears, a monkey tail or scales instead of skin nothing else. They can return to their lives in society with no issues.” explained the Doctor “Class C is like me, a faun, we have major changes but yet can live in normal arrangements with only slight modifications. Class B is on some levels the same as Class C only with major modifications or with effects on humans. These would be your succubus/incubus or your Nagas, they can all live in normal society with major modifications.”

    “And Class A?” inquired Susan already fearing the answer.

    “Is the class that can’t be returned to normal society, I am afraid you will all be moving to one of the new monster cities for your turning and your new life.” Stated the Doctor with a heavy sigh. “You will not be able to live in this town once you turn, I am sorry.”

    “This is not fair, not fair.” cried Jennifer as the 9-year-old broke down and even the twin boys which where always wild seamed to stunned to say anything. The elder twins, seamed too shell shock to even wrap their minds around the idea, a few days ago they where more focused on finishing their senior year of High School and going off to college, now they had no idea what was about to happen to them. Only that on top of changing into some monster, they would have to say goodbye to their home town, their friends and the entire life they knew and moved maybe to the other side of the world depending on what they became.
