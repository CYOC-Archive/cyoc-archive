Reiko's regression was just about the same, once she regressed back into her teenage years, she was almost an identical twin to Mika with her hair regaining its color and actually growing long enough to stretch down to her back and her clothes adjusted now to something that Gloria and the others were wearing.

    "Uh, hi. You're Reiko, right?" Gloria asked.

    "I'm Gloria, and this is Jackie, Diane, and Felicia. Pleased to meet you. Forgive me for being rude but do you know that guy that our other friend is talking to?" Gloria asked pointing to Hideo and Sasha.

    "Oh, that's Hideo. He's nice, but I don't think he's not really my type." Reiko explained.

    "Really? I think you two might hit it off." Jackie suggested.

    "No....besides it looks like your friend is hitting it off better with him than I ever will." Reiko said as they saw Sasha giggling about something Hideo said to her.

    "So Gloria, you tried to solve one problem by creating another, now how are we supposed to fix this?" Felicia asked.
