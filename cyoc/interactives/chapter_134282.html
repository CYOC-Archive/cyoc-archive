    Mad Science

    Second chances do happen. Even for loosers.

    Previous chapter

    added by AnarchyNinetyNine 5 years ago AR BM S O Reality alteration

    The game goes on. Kyhle's team is getting annihilated, they haven't even scored as opposed to the other team's 7. They're utterly powerless to stop the annihilation.

    The end of game buzzer rings. The game is an utter massacre. 10 points against none. Kyhle and his team are visibly drained, all that hard work gone within the time frame of a game. Back to square one essentially. The other team sing their song while Kyhle's walk into the changerooms.

    Riley couldn't believe it either. He had never played or watched basketball before but some of the penalties were stupid to say the least. Officials were definitely on the winning team's side. No doubt about it.

    Riley notices someone on the other team staring at him. When he looks at them they turn around and act like nothing happened. He is actually somewhat concerned, he isn't nearly an adult anymore, he's 12 at most, and nowhere near as built as before. He doesn't want to tell his 'mum' just in case the staring was a fluke.

    Kyhle walks out of the changerooms and up to the grandstand, he sighs in disappointment. His mother then says, "Wasn't your day today. Anyway, you still made it to the final."
    "I guess. But it shouldn't have went the way it did," replies Kyhle still disappointed.
    "Maybe. But there's always next year."
    "I guess." Suddenly. Out of nowhere, there is a bright flash and a deafening roar.

    The bright light fades slowly. Kyhle feels funny. "What the hell happened?" He says in a higher voice.
    "It just went bright and loud," replies Riley.
    "Why does my voice sound like yours?"
    Riley looks at Kyhle and his eyes go wide, "You look just like me."
    "What! You can't be serious!"
    "See for yourself," says Riley holding up a phone in selfie mode.
    "What the fuck? Why am I your twin?"
    "I don't know man," says Riley, "But something similar happened to me not that long ago."
    "What do you mean?"
    "I was originally named Callum and was turned into the potato that you made into mashed potato."
    "Okay. No surprise there," says Kyhle.
    "Huh? What do you mean?" Asks Riley confused.
    "Isn't that how it works? People get turned into food and have the chance at becoming something else once eaten."
    "Oh man," says Riley, "Those girls really fucked up."

    "Come on guys," shouts an unfamiliar voice. Riley and Kyhle turn to see someone on the opposing side shouting on them. It's the guy who was starting at Riley before.
    "Yeah. You two are now my little brothers," he says.
    "No way," says Kyhle, "Where's mum?"
    "Not here. Since as far as the world's aware, you two have always been my younger brothers."
    "This is bullshit," says Kyhle.
    "Watch your voice or I'll wash your mouth with soap."
    "I don't fucking care. You've kidnapped us."
    "Alright. Soap in your mouth once we get home."
    "Fuck you," replies Kyhle. Riley just stands there silent and confused.

    The guy sighs, "Look Kyhle. If you want to win a Grand Final I'm the one you want. I've won 3 in the past 4 years."
    "But. But. You kidnapped me."
    "Not really. I'm going to look after you."
    "Oh fine," sighs Kyhle in defeat.
    "Good. Now I'll just need to change a few things about you so I don't confuse you with your brother."

    Riley watches as Kyhle's eyes turn light green and the shaved side of hair grows back. Kyhle also grows slightly taller. Not by much but enough to be noticeable.
    "Wait. Does this mean I'll change schools and teams?" Asks Kyhle.
    "Yeah. You'll go to the same one I go to."
    "Okay. Guess I get a better education too."
    "Exactly. Told you you'd be getting a better life."

    Before too long. Kyhle and Riley are following their new older brother to their new home. Riley nervously asks, "What's your name?"
    "Matthew," he replies.
