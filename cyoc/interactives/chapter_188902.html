    Waking up in a nursery

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago AR Baby

    Sarah slowly began to wake up from her long sleep only to realize that she was now in a completely different place than before.

    She was now inside a giant crib, with a very comfortable blanket, a few stuffed animals and a butterfly crib mobile on top of her.

    Sarah also noticed that she was now fully clothed. She not only wore a diaper, but she also wore a yellow footed onesie decorated with baby ducks. Even she had her hair tied into cute pigtails.

    And the last thing she noticed was that she was now in what appears to be an ordinary nursery for some baby just like her. Although it was more noticeable that it was for a baby girl since much of the nursery was painted pink. Even the walls had various pictures of princesses, fairies and unicorns.

    Sarah didn't know where she was now. She did not seem to be in the spy agency daycare, much less in the daycare where she had to infiltrate. She had to find out herself.

    At that moment, Sarah noticed that there was a baby monitor near her crib. She immediately began to wail, hoping it would bring someone over.

    Promptly, the door to the nursery was opened. Then someone walked in and picked Sarah up.
