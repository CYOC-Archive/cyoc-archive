    One day, a couple went hiking in the snowy alps. The man and woman spotted an unusual site: a young girl who was no older than seven years of age. She had fair skin, blonde hair that was waist-length, rosey cheeks, eyes with no whites or pupils showing - they were just pure sky blue and filled with sparkles the a starry sky. She also had a pointy nose that was three inces in front of her face, and large pointy ears that were three times larger than normal ears, and an infectious grin that seemed to smile wider than any human should be able to.

    As for her outfit, she wore a green dress with red trim, white leggings with red stripes, green buckle shoes that curled upward at the tips into points.

    When this mysterious girl saw the man and woman, she couldn't help but laugh and giggle.

    When asked what was so funny, this child elf told the two adults that she wanted to be their friends. So she breathed smokey breath that was white with red stripes, and smelled like peppermint. When the smoke cleared, where the man and woman had stood before were now an elf boy and elf girl both the same age as the one who transformed them, both wearing similar elf outfits, and both had the same happy grins on their faces.

    They all agreed that they had much cheer to spread.


    That was over a month ago, now in various cities across the world, these seven-year-old elves have spread. Humans have fought back, only for weapons to be turned into useless toys by their elf magic. Speaking of elf magic, everything they touched became Christmas-themed, and just right for their small size.

    While there were many who resisted, there were also some who wanted to become elves so that they could be happy too.

    These elves remembered their old lives as humans, and retained all their knowledge from before, but they were much happier as elves, and they all saw eachother as one big family.....as brothers and sisters. They were always kind and polite to everyone, and shared all they had with anyone they met. Thanks to their elvin magic, they never ran out of what they needed, and could always produce gifts to give to eachother whenever they wanted.

    They ate mostly candy, but suffered no negative effects because their bodies worked in such a way that any sugar they consumed made their magic stronger. They never needed to use the restrooms anymore because their bodies didn't produce waste, and they never needed to change clothes because their bodies never produced bad oders, but they always smelled like sugary sweets.

    One thing to understand: these elves were not trying to takeover the world, in fact, they had no malicious intent at all! Rather, all they wanted was to spread joy and cheer to everyone, and be friends to all.

    Other things to note, were that these elvish kids would talk with and understand anyone, and any animal, breaking all language barriers so that nothing would hinder making more friends, and for those who were already seven years old or younger, their ages didn't change, but their intelligence was evened out so that they knew as much as those who had formally been older. Even babies who became elves knew how to talk now, but still managed to maintain their innocent personalities.

    The elf childeren loved to have snowball fights, and since their magic always ensured there was snow on the ground, they could always have them. If any snowball thrown by an elf hit a human, they'd become an elf, and would happily want to join in on the fun the others were having.

    Some had even taken over a few news stations, and were happily reporting how far their elvish friendship had spread.
