    "Oh damn it." Kelly cried out at the sight of you. One would figure any other girl would be shocked to see their boyfriend turned into a blue rabbit but not Kelly.

    "Wh... what... what happen to me?" You can only stammer. Your voice is slightly different. Credit for that goes to your new teeth.

    "You were supposed to completely transform into a damn bunny. Not half-way." She through down the rabbit cage she had bought at the pet store and walked over to the mantle to think.

    "You did this to me? But why?" You took a few wary steps towards her but you were hardly accustomed to your new feet. Falling forward you catch your self in an uneasy position on all fours. You look up at her.

    "It was supposed to teach you a lesson. Spend a week as a bunny and maybe you'll learn to listen to me for a change." She looks down at you. The sight of her once rather boisterous boyfriend now reduced to quivering on his new hands and feet as a half bunny was oddly empowering.

    "Ch... change me back." You plead.

    "I can't. Spell was supposed to last a week. Who knows though. Damn quack said it would turn you into a bunny too but that clearly didn't happen." She kept thinking.

    "I can't be a rabbit."

    "You clearly are a rabbit, sort of." Kelly looked dejected.

    Just then she noticed the plate with the rest of the carrots on it.

    "Wait a minute. Maybe you had to eat them all. Yeah..." She looks at you, clearly weakened by the transformation, and hatches a plan.

    Bounding over to the table, she grabs a carrot. Racing back, she flips you over and pins you on your back. Snapping off the tip of the carrot, she starts to force it towards your mouth. Your muscles are too weak to get her off you even as you struggle.

    "Come on, baby. Take your medicine."
