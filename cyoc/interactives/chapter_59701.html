She only realised that she was still holding the box when she tried to close her door and realized that one of her hands was occupied.

    "The box!" She exlaimed out loud almost as if suprised that she still had it.

The thought of what it could be was eating away at her, so she took the syringe out once again. This time howeevr she searched the box a little harder before sitting down on her bed with its contents.

Hesitantly she drew the tip of the needle to the cover over the top of the vial. The vial itself was filled with a clear liquid, almost as if nothing was actually inside the little glass capsule. She punctured the cover and sucked up about half of the syrum before btter judgment overtook her curiosity.
