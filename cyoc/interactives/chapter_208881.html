    "Jacob." Jacob finished her sentence. "And what are your names?"

    "We've just been calling each other what part of the body we are. I've been Left Leg, Right Leg here has helped me hold the body up, then there's Left Arm, Right Arm, Chest, and Head." Left Leg explained.

    "It just makes everything simpler." Left Arm chimed in. "It got hard to remember who controlled what, but this way we work much better as a team."

    Jacob looked incredulous but didn't push the matter further.

    "Alright, I can remember that." Jacob responded. "Do you know what happened. Or how to fix it?"

    The body in front of them turned around and Head looked down at them. "We've been trying to learn how to walk and bend over so we can pick up our phones. Over there. But we have been falling a lot. We've been a team for a long time, but we haven't been able to complete this challenge."

    "@AlexJones was right the whole time! Bill Gates and his covid vaccines caused all of this!" The included picture showed a bunch of people fused together in different configurations in a mall.

    "WTF, my roommate turned into my dick... #GreatFusionWave". Jacob felt relieved there wasn't a picture for this one.

    "Um, guys... I checked Twitter and everything is about people being fused together. They're calling it the Great Fusion Wave."
