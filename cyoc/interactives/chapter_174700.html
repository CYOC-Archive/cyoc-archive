    "Finally done and with good grades"
    These were the words of Chris Miller a 19 year old college freshman. He was finishing up on his last final exam for the fall semester. Although this fall semester was definitely not what he wanted he made it through with a 3.9 GPA. Chris was looking forward to going out to his college in New York and enjoying NYC. But because of the virus his plans for this semester were cancelled so he made to learn remotely. Although it was not bad because he got to stay in his epic family mansion in Montana. The house had everything a movie theatre, indoor virtual golf simulator, a gym any division 1 football program would kill for, and 20 acres of land with a lake and lawn managed by robots. Chris lived with his father Jim Miller who is a successful lawyer and business man in the state. Normally Jim would be home with Chris working remotely but once a month he goes to the office today was that day. He wrote Chris a note on his door saying "good luck champ. Chris's mom Dianne sadly died 4 years ago after a nasty battle with stage 4 lung cancer. She loved Chris very much and did all the things Jim does with Chris. She was from a family wealthier than even Jim and her wealth in real estate and near-recession proof stocks and bonds was passed on to Chris and his sister Gianna.

    Mentioning Gianna, she was the only thing that made this whole staying at home order horrible. Gianna was Chris's bitchy little 17 year old sister. Gianna is a senior in high school, she is a perfect 4.0 GPA student since kindergarten, she is head of the math honor society, the science honor society, head cheerleader, captain and all time leading goalie in both school and Montana soccer history. She is 5'7'' and has the body of an angel with ample breasts, long wavy brown hair, a waist and ass to die for. She has a super high metabolism and is always in shape and doesn't take much effort at all. She sounds like the perfect package right? Well that is until you meet her and see her venomous and manipulative personality. She can ruin your day and make you feel like you're the asshole.

    Chris was praying she didn't choose to attend the same college he was attending in New York because if she did their dad would force them to live in an off campus apartment together so the family could stay together. Jim tried his hardest to make Chris and Gianna get along but she is the absolute worst. Ever since they became teens she has always teased Chris especially when it comes to weight and his luck with the ladies. Chris has had a problem with weight since he turned 14. No matter how hard he tried the blubber would just stay there and this made Chris feel bad. Once he started getting rejected by girls at school she had a field day. She bullied Chris into not going into prom. Even academically she would insult him "a 3.9 is good but is shit compared to my 4.0". How much he hated her.

    That is when a knock on Chris's door could be heard.
