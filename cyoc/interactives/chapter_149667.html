    Gradual changes

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by space_man2 4 years ago BM

    Andy decided that he'd take things slow. Savor every bit of changes coming his way. He could make drastic changes from the beginning, but where's the fun in that? The conscious praise of others is half the reward of his eventual change.

    The first changes were minute. You can't notice that they were gone unless you peered closer. One such change is how Andy removed the panda eyes that he inherited from his family. As he looked in the mirror, he was amused by the fact that looks better without them.

    One of the first people to notice was his sister. She asked how he removed it within a single day. Of course, Andy wouldn't tell her. He told her that he slept for 8 hours, something his night owl sister wouldn't dare do.

    The next day, Andy made changes about his height. He made the change gradual by making himself grow half an inch every day and capping it at exactly 6 feet. No one noticed until a week later when Andy's gymnasium coach commended his growth spurt. The nerds of the class fuming. Every professor is commending him now, both in academics and in sports. Well, life isn't fair. More so when the one you hate has a wishing app.

    In the next few weeks, Andy made changes to his face and his body. He made his smoother and clear. His eyes who were previously a dull shade of bluish-gray, are now icy orbs that melt the heart of whomever he looks at. Along with his height spurt, he made gradual improvements with the bulk of his body. Within 15 days, he had the body akin to people who train in gyms for years.

    As time pass, more and more people praised the changes happening to him. Girls who previously avoided him are now the ones initiating small talks during breaks. The cool kids went over his lunch table and asked how he's been. The jocks stopped picking on him, they even invited him for the midterm tryouts. And the nerds, their group splintered off. Half of them apologized for being salty about him for half the school year while the remaining ones grew bitter with age.

    With just one app, everything changed for the better. The school was within his palms and he's savoring every moment of it.


    That was until the same thing happened to another.

    Henry Jackson, a student in the same year as Andy. There was the sure sign of changes happening to him as well. But with a low intellect person like him, Andy knew that he'd be impatient. His chin becoming sculpted within a single day was the kick that made Andy realize that he had the same app as well. When his chin returned back to its curved shape a day later and blamed it on allergies, Andy knew that his suspicions are real.

    From this point forward, Andy has multiple paths he could walk on. One thing is to confront him and ask him directly. The problem with that path is that he's part of the nerd group who hated him. Henry would be reluctant to even talk to him.

    Another path Andy could see is ignored him to his antics. Leave him to his own devices and confront him when necessary.

    What do you do now?
