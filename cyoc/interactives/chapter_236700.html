Leah's grin widened, forcing her to grip her pacifier with her teeth; somehow, the universe has blessed her with another teen experiencing an embarrassing infantile situation, and she's going to milk this for all it's worth.
    "O-oh my god, where did you come from?" The teen asked, immediately turning her body so that the accident was no longer in view---not that it mattered, the smell was unmistakable.

    "I-I don't know what you're talking about, little girl, you must be-"
    "Oh my god, you're LYING about it?" Leah says with a laugh, "Like, it's super obvious. Lying about it will just make you seem more like a baby than you already are."
    The older teen scoffs,
    "Y-You're one to talk!"
    "Yeah, I am, cause I'm wearing a diaper. You know, the thing you're meant to poop in, and might be something you need."
    "I-I do not need diapers!"

The section of the structure was a bit more cramped---and while it was fine for Leah, it left the taller teen a little unsteady on her feet. The teen started sniffling, looking to be on the verge of tears. She looked over her shoulder at her behind, and immediately started sobbing. Leah's fun waned just a bit, rolling her eyes at the melodrama; her accident isn't a fraction as bad as her's at the giftshop.
