    Amanda just decided it would be fun to use her app on all of the other customers around her. She looked over at the blonde girl who looked like she was in her senior year of high school. The girl was obviously going through social media on her own phone and took a picture of herself as likely to show what she looked like before she got her hair done. Amanda then saw an Asian woman in her mid twenties, she was looking at her watch as if she had somewhere else to be. Then there was the guy in his late twenties dressed in baggy clothing like he didn't care how he dressed. Amanda noticed a 40 year-old man next to him, who looked like he was waiting for someone and she looked to see his gaze fall upon Mrs. Parker, they must be related in some way. Two people who she knew were definitely related were the mother and her young son who looked to be six or seven years old.

    Amanda first decided to use the app on the mother and son. Though she knew, she could only use it on one of them, so she decided to use it on the son.

    A 7 year-old impatiently waits for his haircut.

    Amanda decided to change 7 to 37.

    A 37 year-old impatiently waits for his haircut.

    Amanda looked up to see where the boy once sat, now sat a man close to the woman in age. She also noticed that the two of them were wearing wedding rings. The two were now husband and wife.

    "I told you, I don't need a haircut, I look fine." The man quietly argued with his wife.

    "You'll look better once you get it cut." The wife said as she moved her hand gently across her husband's head.

    Amanda smiled, thinking that the man and wife must be in a good relationship from that exchange. She then looked over at the Asian woman who sat next to the blonde girl.

    A 24 year-old Asian woman waits for her haircut.

    Amanda saw that the woman was rolling her eyes at the blonde teen who was doing typical teenage stuff on her phone. Amanda thought that the blonde girl could use a friend, so she decided to make the woman a teenager.

    A 17 year-old Asian woman waits for her haircut.

    Amanda looked up to see where the woman sat, now sat a teenage girl who was chatting it up with the blonde girl. The two of them were giggling and taking pictures with each other. Amanda now looked over at the redheaded man in baggy clothes.

    A redhead in baggy clothes.

    Amanda knew what to change for that right away and decided to swap out 'baggy' with 'fancy'.

    A redhead in fancy clothes.

    Amanda now saw that the man was no longer dressed in baggy clothes but instead fancy looking clothes that he definitely didn't have on earlier. Amanda finally looked over at the 40 year-old man was waiting on Mrs. Parker who was just about getting finished with her haircut. Amanda wondered how he would like it if his wife was a lot younger. Amanda now focused her phone app on Mrs. Parker.

    A 40 year-old finishes her haircut.

    Amanda changed Mrs. Parker's age, but she didn't want to make her too young.

    A 30 year-old finishes her haircut.

    Amanda looked to see Mrs. Parker was younger and had less wrinkles on her face than she did before. The now young Mrs. Parker and Mr. Parker finished at the register as they left.

    "Ms. Bailey? Rachel will see you now." Latisha said from her desk to Amanda who took another look at everyone else and smiled.
