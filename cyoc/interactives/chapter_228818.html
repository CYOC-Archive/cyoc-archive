    The Zoo

    The hauntings of Savannah continue. Oh Dear!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago A O Adult

    As you continue to get an education, every day you felt it as Savannah's apparition was walking several blocks behind you, projecting her energies onto you from where she was as she followed you everywhere you went.

    Her menacing hateful angry red tear filled eyes piercing into you, filling you with dread, at your final rejection she turned hateful. You went and obtained a solid 10 foot by 10 foot block crystal of salt, and, cutting it into seventy pieces, you called out to God Almighty and asked him to have Jesus bless the salt crystals and coat them in his blood.

    Several days to two weeks go by without a change, Savannah heard you praying, and became enraged, and so your bed was thrown at you and you barely shut the bedroom door fast enough, before hearing the bed crash into the door, a message on said door in blood appeared saying, Never enter this room again if you value your life Jack!

    The Lord hearing your desperate plea, and seeing the seriousness of your situation, granted your request, and Jesus blessed the crystals and covered them in his blood. A golden light shone on the crystals and after a few seconds they turned a golden red.

    They began to emit a divine protection and your first task is to reclaim your bedroom. You place a crystal on the floor in front of you and begin removing the door. Pulling out the hinge bolts you pull the door out of the doorway.

    Savannah's ghost had left the house to wander the neighborhood an hour ago and saw you sitting on the couch, watching TV, as she left, as you often do. Almost immediately after pulling the door off it's hinges you felt rage engulf the house, hearing the familiar thudding of running hooves, you pale in fear. Savannah knows and is running back this way. You give the broken bed a heave back into the bedroom and climb over it, You repeat the chant given to you by the archangel michael.

    You say, "I Jack Parkinson in the presence of God Almighty, hereby declare in the name of God The Father, God The Son, and God The Holy Ghost, with this blessed crystal as the anchor for my humble prayers, declare this place HOLY GROUND. A bright golden flash of heavenly light engulfs the bedroom and yoou now are sitting on an unbroken bed. Milliseconds after Savannah bounces off the divine barrier, snarling in hate, contempt, and rage the house shakes at her screams.

    You see her leave and don't hear from her for several months, in the mean time, you placed six more crystals in the shape of a cross, five of them embedded in the upstairs walls, one buried in the back yard, one on the tv table, the last one on the dining table, Completed the circuit and Savannah was banned from your house forever!

    The first time the bloody messages appeared on your parents wall while on spring break, the first one said, "I will follow you across the world until you die Jack. You gave your parents seven of the seventy crystals. Your parents being Christians took the salt crystals gratefully, placed them in a cross shape and praying, declaring by the name of God, that their house and grounds are HALLOWED GROUND. Savannah weakly, and uselessly, beats against the barrier to no avail.

    She glares at you from outside the barrier, writing a final message, saying "Well played Jack, Well Played!!!!
