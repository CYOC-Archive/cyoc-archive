    A Costume Party Draws Near

    You open the front door only to see the mail man's truck drive away. So you check your mailbox to find a envelope. You reenter your house and opened it in the kitchen to find a invite. That's odd... You haven't gotten any kind of invite for a long time. Upon further inspection, you find out that it's to a Halloween party held by a popular girl at your college. Oh right.... Halloween is a week from today. Still, she didn't invite you last year, so you wonder what changed her mind this time. Much less how she even got your mailing address. You shrug and return to your bedroom. At least you now you have a reason to try out your grandfather's junk.

    And so you examine the contents of the box until you pulled out a headband with bunny ears. The invite specified that you need to have at least one piece of costume on to even enter, so you figured a basic headband should do the trick.

    You slipped it on and then examined it on the mirror. Despite seeming like it was too big to fit on your head (most likely due to it being bought by a old man) it seems to stay on your head as if it was the perfect size. In fact, as you examened the head headband in the mirror, you noticed that you cant see the headband anymore. In fact, you can't feel it on your head anymore. Instead, you feel a rush of new sensations coming from the top of your head. The feeling of the air touching the hairs of your... ears? Come to think of it, you are hearing new sounds all of a sudden. You reach up to touch your bunny ears to find out they feel very real. In a panic, you tried pulling them off, and to your relief, they slipped off like a regular headband. You glance down at the rabbit ears in your hands to find that they are back to being normal costume headbands.

    Was that real? Or were you imagining that? They looked and felt so real on your head. You then glance down at the box full of costume parts. Upon further examination, you find a lot more appear in the box then you could imagine. It's as if it's bigger on the inside then it is on the outside. It also seems to contain a greater variety of costumes then just animal parts. You see some skin colored cloth to cover your hair and other body parts, presumingly with the function of temporary erasing body parts. As well as some scuba gear... some dildos.... condoms... make-up... and just a huge variety of things. Before you can question why your grandfather even had some of this stuff, your curiosity started to take hold. The feeling of having real rabbit ears felt so alien and exciting. It makes you wonder what were the functions and limitations of these costumes where. Should you dare try again? Or should you lock them away somewhere and don't touch them again?
