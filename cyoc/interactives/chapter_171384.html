    Control to Match Her Age

    Mia has been waiting rather impatiently for a while when she suddenly feels a pressure in her bladder. She hurriedly crosses her legs and tugs on Makenna’s clothes.

    “Makenna, I have to pee!” she whines.

    “Just hold it, okay?” Makenna says impatiently. Mia begins to dance, the need to pee growing.

    “Makenna!”

    “One second, we’re almost done.”

    “But-” Mia’s control breaks. Her bladder empties. She lets out a wail. “Makenna! You wouldn’t take me to the potty, and now I peed myself!”

    “Oh, no!” Makenna says. She grabs Mia’s hand. “Come on, I’ll take care of you in the bathroom, okay?” Mia nods tearfully. Makenna brings Mia to the nearby bathroom.

    “What are you gonna do?” Mia asks, wiping away her tears. “We don’t have any new clothes for me.”

    “No, but I think the ray might be able to fix that,” Makenna says. She takes it out of her purse and begins to fiddle with the controls. “All I have to do is make your clothes be changed to someone a bit younger than you currently are.”

    “Younger?!” Mia squeaks.

    “You couldn’t hold it for five minutes without having an accident. I think you should be downgraded from big girl undies.”

    “…Fine.”

    “Go ahead and use the toilet while we’re here, just in case,” Makenna says. Mia removes her bottoms and sits on the toilet. As she does, she realizes why her underwear felt different from before. Instead of panties, she was wearing training pants. Makenna notices as well and snorts softly. “I guess you were already downgraded from big girl undies.”

    “Sh-shut up,” Mia mutters. She tries to use the toilet, but nothing comes out. After a bit, she gives up and hops off the toilet and redresses.

    “Done?”

    “Yeah.”

    “All right, let’s get you in something new,” Makenna says, pointing the gun at Mia. Mia is engulfed in a pink light. When the light fades, her clothes have changed to something that would be better suited for a toddler than a five-year-old. To add insult to injury, she can tell that her training pants have been downgraded into a full diaper.

    “Makenna, this is for a baby!” Mia whines.

    “Hey, I did my best. Come on.” Makenna drags Mia out of the bathroom, says goodbye to Mark, and the two then leave.

    As they walk down the street, Makenna looks at the gun, tempted to continue testing it.
