The woman hoisted herself up from her chair, cursing a bit under her breath as she struggled to get to her feet. "Need another diaper change?" she huffed, albeit with a small smile. After all, she couldn't really be mad when looking at that baby's cute little face...her baby's. The mother pat her belly before lifting up the dozen-pound or so child.

    A small sniff indicated changing wasn't the problem, leading to Rebecca try rocking Eric next. As soon as the baby lifted his stubby arms up though, Rebecca knew what the issue was. "Oh I see...you're just hungry, huh?" She couldn't help but roll her eyes - again with a smile - as she pulled her blouse and bra down. The baby eagerly began sucking at his mother's now-exposed breast, milk flowing into his mouth. As Eric continued to drink, the mother couldn't help but reminisce...especially with all the little things that added up to remind her that she only had this body for a few months.

    Yes, she was a male in the prime of his life, going to college with his beautiful girlfriend, Emily. Now though, she was the stay-at-home mother to that very same girlfriend - the one sucking on her fat tit. Not to mention a housewife...Greg had made things much easier though, especially since Emily hadn't retained her memories of her original self - not even her original gender! Rebecca wasn't sure if it was because of that damn camera that changed them switching her awareness off at the last moment somehow, or the fetus' undeveloped mind unable to retain such detailed information, or something else entirely. She supposed it didn't matter, so long as the camera was still missing...

    Unknown to her though, the camera had fused together with Emily - or rather Eric. Sucking his mother's breast while she was thinking about the camera seemed to give them a greater connection than before, igniting some sort of instinctual use of his new powers! Just how strong were they though, and what would they do?
