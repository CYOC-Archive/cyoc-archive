    She carried me into the mountains where with dizying speed
    she plunged towards a cavern entance.

    In the cavern running around on all fours were other dragons like
    her.

    She settled down on her 4 legs and dropped me into a
    pile of brush and twigs just before she landed.

    Ignoring me where I layed stunned,she ran up to the
    the dragons and grunted and roared at them in dragontongue.

    As she waved her front talons about I noticed that on one digit
    sat a ring similar to my own,except bigger.

    As I looked at the other grunting beasts I noticed that
    they too sported a ring similar to my own.

    It suddenly struck me that I'd been had by the old lady.

    I looked back at my accursed ring finger and noticed the
    dark green scales,tiny but definetely dragon in appearance,that
    had sprouted from other side of the cursed ring.

    I quietly got up and tried to sneak out of the cavern
    unoticed by the monsters.

    I was about 10 feet from the entrance when the ring began
    to glow and I watched in horror as my ring finger began to
    crackle,grow and become more gnarled as it changed into the
    huge dragon digit covered in green scales.

    A massive hunger was stirring in my belly and my joints began to
    ache,

    As I struggled to reach the edge of the cavern I realized the caverns
    walls were throbbing,and pumping draconic energy into me
    through the ring.

    I continued to drag my enlarging dragonnish body to the
    entrance,as I realized that wearing a dragons ring in a dragons
    cavern was turning me into a dragon.

    I nearly made it,but my emerhing female scent alerted the other
    dragons to my birthing.

    I suddenly felt a weight on my spine somewhere behind me,as
    one of the other females of the band stepped on my tail.

    Beside the beast stood the old woman who had given me the ring.

    "Nine rings " shouted the old woman with glee.

    As I panted through my lengthening snout,I could feel
    the cursed dragon ring enlarge as my body creaked,stretched
    and grew in all directions.

    Then the change was done and I shuffled onto all
    fours,

    Instinctively I now knew dragontongue,and listened as the other
    eight dragons intoduced themselves to me.

    I wasn't surprised that they were all former humans,transformed
    into dragonesses when they'd been tricked into putting on one of
    the cursed dragon rings.

    What do you do now?
