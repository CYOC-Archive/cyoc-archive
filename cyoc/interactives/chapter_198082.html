    "But what are you going to do with me?" Doris asked a little scared

    "And that punished is?" Doris asked even more scared than before.

    "We'll have to turn you.......into a boy" Ricky said now with a sinister voice

    Immediately, Doris was left with a horrified face upon hearing said punishment. She even started acting very scared. She tried to get out of Chloe's arms, she screamed many times not to be punished and even she started crying saying that she "wanted her mommy".

    After a few cries from Doris, Chloe and Ricky couldn't take it anymore and started laughing like they had no idea.

    At that moment, Doris began to calm down little by little and saw how the 2 adults were laughing exaggeratedly.

    "Did you really think we would turn you into a boy? You really should have seen your face." Ricky was still laughing at Doris's reaction

    "So you're not going to turn me into a boy?" Doris asked still a little scared

    "Of course not. Do you really think we're that malevolent? What you did wasn't even that serious." Chloe replied to Doris

    "Although we do have to give you a little punishment for trying to escape. But before that, let's change that wet diaper you're wearing." Ricky told Doris as he proceeded to lead her to a changing table.

    Doris couldn't believe it. How could they joke with her with something like that? And worst of all, she believe them. She even wet her diaper from fear.

    While Ricky was still changing her diaper, Doris proceeded to ask him about her punishment.

    "And then what will be my punishment?"

    "Your punishment? Oh yes. Well, here our punishments are somewhat light to be honest because we don't want to be really cruel to the children here. And our punishments themselves are usually: Put them in timeouts of between 2 to 5 minutes, put them to sleep early or put them in a playpen.
    Although there are 2 punishments that we only use in really special cases.
    If by any chance there is a child with a very bad behavior, then we take him to a special room with nothing "fun" so he can reflect on what he did.
    And if by any chance there is a child who manages to escape from here, then we have no choice but to make him a little younger so that he cannot escape again.
    Although you haven't done anything serious yet, you haven't even managed to escape completely, so you have nothing to worry about," Ricky replied to Doris.

    Doris was somewhat relieved since none of the punishments were really serious, in fact, they didn't even feel like punishments. Although the last thing did worried her a little. So if she starts to misbehave, will they take her to a special room where she won't be able to entertain herself? And if she by any chance manages to escape from this place, they would have no choice but to make her younger? That did generate some fear in Doris because she did not want to be reduced to a baby who cannot talk or walk.

    "And voila, you have a new diaper." Ricky had finally finished changing Doris's diaper and proceeded to get her down from the changing table.

    "And now Doris. I’m going to take you to your 'little' punishment."
