    "Well, we need your help to get us three to the sea." You reply.

    Neil was a good fella, about as skinny as one could get but he was smart. He actually went and found a wheel barrel and brought it up. "Luckily Caroline loves her garden." He comments as you help Topaz into it first before he takes her to the truck and returns. You then aid him in loading Caroline before helping yourself onto the edge. You watch as he returns. "Okay this will be tough."

    You look at him. "I know, but forget the wheel barrel. Just bend down and swim my arm over and lift me up, set me into the wheel barrel, it's that simple."

    James nods. "Right." He then helps you in and wheels you right to the truck, the girls helping get you in. He gets in the driver seat and head off.

    You then wrap your arms around both Topaz and Caroline, kissing both on the lips. The two rubbing your chest with their webbed hands. "Girls, you spoil me."

    Just then James stops and backs into the ocean some. He stops just with the back tires under, jumping out after applying the parking break he opens the tail gate, without a written invite Topaz jumps in, followed by Caroline and yourself taking up the rear. You look at James and smiles. "Thank you my friend."

    "My pleasure, and you keep those girls away from nets." He said.

    Topaz swam up to him. "Would you like to join us, James?" She asked.

    James shook his head. "Not at the moment, but you do me a favor, make sure Neil and Caroline come visit." He went to plant a kiss on Topaz's cheek, but she tricked him, kissing his lips. She then dragged him under, removing his clothes. She knew once they were fucking he wouldn't stop, so they continued until he climaxed.

    James looked as he started to change. Once he changed he found he was more stronger and loved it. You look at the two lovebirds and smile. "Come, before anyone comes after us."

    James and Topaz nodded as the four of you head down into the sea.

    After hours of swimming you come across a place that is lined in pearls. You realize your old boring life is no more, but you now have a new exciting life ahead of you. "Topaz, why not we do this, we see stranded people out at sea, if it's one or two, we convert them into mermaids?"

    Topaz smiled. "That's a splendid idea." She giggled. "But first welcome to our new home. It's empty now but with us four, it'll be populated in no time."

    You look at it. "Wait, is this... is this the lost city?"

    She nodded. "Welcome home. This is Atlantis. Well there are at least ten, this is just one of them. All of which ended up under the water somehow." She giggled as you four enter the city and start making it home.
