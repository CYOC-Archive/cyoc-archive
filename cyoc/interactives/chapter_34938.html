    Transform or Dare?

    Jennifer loves herself, kinda....

    With Kyle's words still in her head, Jennifer goes to the kitchen for some quick breakfast before school. In the kitchen Jennifer starts by grabbing the orange juice, but she was having trouble with the lid. Suddenly the lid gave way and Jennifer got a face full of OJ. "Uhg, great, first dweeb calls with some weird curse and now this." Jennifer quickly moves to the bathroom to clean her face.

    After splashing some water on her face, and wiping the orange juice clean off, Jennifer looks into the mirror to make sure she got everything. While looking into the mirror, Jennifer starts thinking of how good she looked today. The way her cheerleading outfit tightly clung to her body was making her feel a little horny. "Ha, I think I'm turning myself on. But what girl wouldn't want this body." Jennifer then found it strange that she only thought of girls wanting her, not guys. However, the weirdest thing was what she heard next.

    "Hmmm, I know I would want that body, but I've already got it, and yours too!" Hearing a voice that sounded very close to her own, Jennifer spun around only to find herself face to face with herself. "Huh....w-what?" was all that Jennifer could say. "Jen, you alright?" her clone asked, with a note of concern in her voice, "you don't look so well." "I don't feel so well either" was all Jennifer could say before passing out.

    While Jennifer's body was passed out, her mind found itself in a dark room. In the room, a girl who looked just like her came over and sat on a chair next to her. "Who are you? Are you me?" Jennifer asked her clone. "Ha, don't be silly Jen. It's me, Ashley, your twin sister. Duh." Jennifer was even more confused now, as she never had a sister, much less a twin. "I'm sorry but...I don't remember." Ashley smiled and got up from her chair. "Well then, let me remind you." With that, Ashley wrapped her arms around Jennifer and began kissing her.

    During the kiss, a flood of new memories came to Jennifer. She remembered her twin sister. She remembered all the fun the two had during their life together. She remembered crying and laughing, both in her sister's embrace. Then she remembered experimenting with Ashley. Kissing Ashley. Loving Ashley, and being loved back. She now remembered her twin, her lover. As soon as it all hit her, Ashley stopped the kiss and simply said, "Time to wake up!"

    Jennifer suddenly found herself back in her room, not knowing how she got there. "Ah, your finally up." Jennifer looked to the door to see Ashley walk in. "You've been passed out for over an hour. I was really getting worried. Oh, and don't worry about school, I called them and told them what happened." "Uh, thanks Ashley" was all Jennifer could manage. She still couldn't believe that she suddenly had a twin. "Well you seem to be fine right now, and I checked to make sure you didn't have a fever while you were out. So we got a free day out of school now, and it's Friday. What do you feel like doing?" Ashley asked, giving a strange smile.

    "Uhm, I don't know. I kinda just woke up. What do you want to do?" Jennifer asked back. Ashley came over to her bed and sat down next to Jennifer. "Oh Jen, you know what I want to do, but are you up for it?" And before Jennifer could question Ashley as to what she meant, Ashley planted a deep kiss on her sister’s lips. Jennifer, being under the curse, found herself not only returning the kiss, but loving every second of it too. Ashley and Jennifer continued to kiss while Ashley crawled onto Jennifer. Finally they broke their kiss and just held each other for a few minutes.

    Thanks to the curse, Jennifer now had a twin sister whom she loved very much. She now began to wonder what else she could do with the curse now that she possessed it...
