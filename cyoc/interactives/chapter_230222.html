    “Yumi”, who is already sipping on her second beer, does not notice a young man, maybe around 25, enter the bar since her back is turned towards the entrance.

    William “Will” Baker is an athletic young man with short blond hair, blue eyes and a pale, but healthy, skin tone. Despite his good look, Will does not seem to be able to get himself a girlfriend, every girl he speaks to is either already taken or just does not seem to be interested in him, ‘what is it about me that women seem to dislike,’ he had asked himself, ‘do I smell weird? Is it my personality or black humour’. The more women reject him the more frustrated Will becomes, the young man had already accepted that he might forever stay single, until he stumbled upon the App-version of the Chronivac. After testing the program, by making himself younger, Will came up with a plan to finally get the girlfriend he deserves.

    Will now is scanning the bar, searching for HIS perfect female, when his eyes land on the young Asian woman sitting at the bar, ‘damn, her backside already looks stunning,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I couldn’t ask for a better girlfriend, especially since I find Asian women are the sexiest in the world. Let’s see what the App says about her’.

    The young man pulls out his phone and aims it at “Yumi”, activating the scanner, he then opens the “woman’s” stats:

    Name: Yumi Tanaka
    Species: Human
    Race: Japanese
    Gender: female
    Age: 22
    Height: 5’ 2’’
    Weight: 119 lbs
    Breast size: Medium C-cup
    Sexual Orientation: lesbian
    Attracted To: athletic girls
    Relationship status: single and searching
    Occupation: unemployed
    Languages spoken: english and japanese
    Interests: anthropomorphic animals, sports, gaming, hunting
    Awareness: off

    Will scowls when he reads that Yumi is lesbian, ‘that won’t do,’ he thinks to himself, ‘she’s MINE. I’m sick and tired of being single,’ and with a short “correction” of the “girl’s” stats, “Yumi’s” stats now read:

    Name: Yumi Tanaka
    Species: Human
    Race: Japanese
    Gender: female
    Age: 22
    Height: 5’ 2’’
    Weight: 119 lbs
    Breast size: Medium C-cup
    Sexual Orientation: straight
    Attracted To: athletic boys
    Relationship status: single and searching
    Occupation: unemployed
    Languages spoken: english and japanese
    Interests: anthropomorphic animals, sports, gaming, hunting
    Awareness: off

    Smiling to himself, Will confirms the change as he makes his way over to the bar, at the same time, “Yumi” feels her mind tingle a little but shrugs it off. “Yumi” doesn’t even notice her phone vibrate in her pocket as a warning pops up in her Chronivac-App: “WARNING: subject has been scanned a second time. The subject has been altered, do you really wish to continue? If no choice has been made in five seconds, the alteration will be accepted, this will cause the first change to become permanent!”

    A timer then starts counting down, when the timer reaches zero a new message pops up, “alteration confirmed! Adjusting stats!”

    Will by now has reached the bar and sits on a free stool next to “Yumi”, “hello miss,” Will says, smiling kindly, “I couldn’t help but notice your beauty from afar. I hope you don’t mind me giving you company?”

    “Yumi” turns her head and notices the young man sitting beside her, ‘man, he sure is handsome,’ she thinks to herself, not realizing that something is wrong, “oh, I don’t mind at all,” she says, “my name is Yumi Tanaka. And you are?”

    “Will… Will Baker,” Will says, “I haven’t seen you around here before. Are you on vacation? Where are you from? Your English is pretty good”.

    ‘This guy is curious,’ the “young woman” thinks to herself, ‘he seems to be interested in me, and I must say… I would not mind to get to know him… closer. But I feel like something is wrong, I just can’t seem to figure out what. I mean… he seems like a nice guy… it won’t hurt to tell him somethings about me’. “Yumi” smiles at Will, “oh, I’m on vacation, I live in Ohio,” she lies, “I just arrived here today and I’m currently staying at a friend's house”.

    “I see, I live outside of town,” Will says, “not to far though, I have a cabin in the woods, I like it quiet”.

    “Cool, must be nice,” “Yumi” says, “though I can imagine that it can be pretty lonely, that is if you live by yourself”.

    “I actually do, and yes, it sure can be lonely,” Will says, “I’ve been trying to get myself someone who’d like to give me company, but no luck so far”.

    “That surprises me,” “Yumi” says, “you are not bad looking and you seem like a nice guy”.

    Will smiles, “thanks, Yumi,” he says, “you seem like a nice young lady. So, what nationality are you? You look Japanese”.

    “I am,” “Yumi” says, she then chuckles, “I know we Asians all look alike, but you Caucasian people also all look alike… I mean… you look as though you could be European!”

    Will chuckles, “I guess so,” he says, “you know, I don’t mean to be so direct, but… I think Asian girls are the hottest in the world,” he then says, blushing a little.

    “So, I guess you are attracted to Asian girls like me,” “Yumi” asks.

    Will nods, “yeah, I… I always dreamed of going to Japan,” he says, “and imagining having a Japanese girl as my girlfriend… that is also a huge dream of mine. I don’t want to be so direct, Yumi… but… you are exactly the type of girl I’d love to date!”

    “Yumi” is taken aback, “are you actually asking me out?”

    “Well… um… y-yeah,” Will says.

    “Yumi” looks at Will for a few moments, ‘he sure looks cute, especially when he’s nervous. He is also really good looking,’ she thinks to herself, ‘I don’t think it would hurt to give it a try. I mean… a part of me feels as though dating a male is wrong, but why would I feel that way?’
