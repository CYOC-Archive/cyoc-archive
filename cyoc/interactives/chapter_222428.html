    Yes, you were a male genie trying to get revenge on your ex-girlfriend but now you are Lolita, her dream girlfriend. And your body is that of a model. Moreover, although you remember being male, you see no problem in becoming Stacy's trophy wife. In fact, you swear to yourself that you would be the absolute trophy wife for Stacy. The idea would have been impossible to you as a male but now all your magic is geared towards cooking, cleaning and pleasing Stacy in every possible way. Notably sexually. The mere notion of squeezing her, of embracing her is enough to get you all excited and jumpy with excitement. You want to clap your hands and jump up and down in joy. You are Lolita, Stacy's trophy wife! Hooray!
    "My second wish is to be infinitely wealthy. We will both have wallets that never empty, credit cards that always will hold money and no one when will question it. And a lavish house to live in."
    What a wonderful wish! How practical! How thoughtful! You clap again your hands in pure bliss and twirl around like a ballerina, granting Stacy's second wish on the spot. Stacy like you silly, so that's what you'll be. Immediately, you both are transported into a gigantic house, with all sorts of great facilities. At once you dream of cooking delicious meals for your one true love. She will love you in a maid outfit, in an apron, and you would keep this house great and spotless for your sweet, sweet Stacy! Oh, the little meals you'll prepare for her! And to think of the bedroom... You blush and giggle at the thought of such sauciness!
    "My third wish is for you to become pregnant with our dream children, my silly Lolita" says Stacy.
    You stare at her. You are not disappointed or anything. Just... surprised.
    "But... But where do you want them to com from?" you ask in bewilderment.
    "We'll select some jerks and then you can tailor-made after my desires. You'll then regress them into your belly and you'll let them grow there, to give birth later. I'm still working so you can carry them."
    You nod. What a splendid idea! Stacy's babies would be so much better than idiots in this world. Of course, it would! And the idea of carrying Stacy's perfect children... Sensing them grow within you, bringing them to this world and raising them with their Mommy Stacy! Oh, the domestic bliss is enough to make your heart explode! Stacy's given you, a genie, the most perfect life ever!
    "And I already know who I'll pick," says Stacy with an evil grin.
