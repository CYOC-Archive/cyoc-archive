John used to be an avid lover of the sea as a child, thinking about the various creatures that swimming in its depths. But as he grew older and became more interested in science, he felt that it was time to leave his childhood fantasies behind and be logical.

    “Could I… Have a chance with her?” John wondered

    How could he even have a chance with her? She was a beautiful mermaid while he was an average looking human. His parents wanted grandkids, sure, but would they accept it if their grandchildren were merfolk? How do merpeople even mate? Would this mermaid even be interested in sex? How lonely must a man be to think he has a chance with a mermaid?

    The logic was now at the window. At this point, John didn’t care about his circumstance. All he knew that he had to catch her with his fishing rod.

    All John had to do now was wait.
