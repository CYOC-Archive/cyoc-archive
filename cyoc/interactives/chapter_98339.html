    Amongst all the servant uniforms, you find something unexpected. A black latex werewolf mask. You pick it up and glance around the closest, no other masks and no other pieces to a full werewolf costume, it's completely out of place. Suddenly you hear someone downstairs and jump. You quickly and quietly sneak your way out, listening carefully as to avoid them. Soon you manage to escape, relativley easily and start walking home. After you walk about halfway there, you realize something, your still carrying the mask, you accidentally stole it! You freak out for a moment and realize it's such a big house, they won't notice it's gone, even if they do they'll just think they lost it. Feeling more relaxed you arrive home, hop in the shower and head to bed. But much to your dismay you can't sleep, you toss and turn for hours until you just get up for a bit to help yourself relax. You walk over to your dresser where you set the mask. You pick up the werewolf mask, noticing that despite being made entirely of black rubber it's pretty detailed. Just messing around you slip it on. It fits like a glove much to your suprise, maybe you can wear it for halloween. But something strange starts to happen you feel a bizarre sensation come over your face. You try to lift the mask off, but it remains rooted in place and pulling it hurts as if were your own skin. You touch the snout, but unlike how it would easily give way like before it stays solid as if bone remained underneath. You scream, but the mask opens as if it were your own mouth, revealing sharp rows of teeth releasing not your own voice, but a deeper more animal one. You examine the rest of your body, it's still normal. You try to calm down and think of a plan.
