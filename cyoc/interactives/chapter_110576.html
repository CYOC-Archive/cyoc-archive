    “You better have a dam good reason for wanting those transporting Julie Arrested for murder,” the Alpha growling.
    Tachibana smiles as she tosses a smart phone to him; “did you really think I wouldn’t know of the side trip your Beta's took? If you view the last communication from that phone you will see something that you better pray, Douglas here doesn’t see.”

    The Alpha runs through the phone to see a video clip of Beta One signaling a werewolf to bite the throat out of Alyssa. The Alpha drops the phone; his blood running cold. But before Tachibana could retrieve the phone Douglas Cooper snatches it up and view the footage.

    Tachibana had to dodge out of the way as Douglas angrily swipes at the Alpha werewolf. Snarling and growling.
    “YOU MURDERED MY COUSIN!” Douglas screaming and growling; “Now I’m going to kill you!”
    The Alpha completely surprised at the were-grizzly’s speed was immediately put on the defensive as he was struggling to fend off a pissed off were out for blood. Fortunately for the Alpha several council security guards stop the assault by using stun guns to immobilize the sheriff.

    “Let me make this perfectly clear,” Tachibana looking at a stunned werewolf; “you will inform those hunting Julie to bring her back ALIVE! And you will hand over your Beta and the other werewolf for the murder of Alyssa Cooper. DO I MAKE MYSELF CLEAR?”
    The Alpha looking at the knocked out sheriff and then the were-vixen clearly ready for a fight, sighs; “very well; I will instruct them to capture and not kill.”
    “And don’t think for one second I don’t know of the spies you have in and around town,” Tachibana glaring; “I will not have you trying to initiate a coup d'etat while we are all occupied with Julie Stovall and the mess your group just made.”
    “But-“ the Alpha tries to speak but is quickly silenced.
    “Daisuke, Yamoto,” Tachibana summoning her grandsons and speaking in Japanese; “I want every single member of Tachibana security not guarding those Julie might target to patrol the town. Employ the silver weaponry. If those we know to be Luna Brotherhood spies try and act, then terminate them.”

    Both Daisuke and Yamoto bow and rush out to make the necessary arraignments, while the Alpha silently swears as he didn’t understand Japanese but knew enough to know that Tachibana wasn’t messing around. Getting on the phone he calls his Beta.
    “I want a sit rep.”
    “Julie Stovall, managed to escape,” Beta one speaking; “dam fire from the van is making it impossible to catch her scent.”
    “You have a spy in your group,” The Alpha answering; “Tachibana knows you had Alyssa murdered while still handcuffed and her cousin the Sheriff is going to be out for blood.”
    “Well that’s fucking lovely,” Beta one snarling; “do you know who is the fink?”
    “That’s for you to find out,” the Alpha snapping back; “oh and one other thing. Julie must be captured. Alive.”
    “But your prior orders-“
    “Did you not here what I just said,” the Alpha barking; “Tachibana and Sheriff Cooper know of Alyssa’s death. The council wants Julie back alive!”
    “That may be difficult,” Beta One sighing; “Julie could be armed. The silver hunting blade is missing from the van.”
    “Bring. Julie. Back. Alive!” The Alpha stating, before disconnecting his call.

    Beta One cringes as the Alpha barks out new orders. Not liking the fact that there is a council spy in his party, he howls to get everyone’s attention.
    “Change of plans,” Beta one speaking; “the Alpha just called. He wants Julie back alive. Not killed.”
    “Why the change,” Beta two speaking; “what happened?”
    “Tachibana and Sheriff Cooper know of Alyssa Cooper’s death. Some one here is a spy and had the incident recorded and sent to Tachibana and the council.”
    “So we are fucked,” a werewolf in the group speaking.
    “Pretty much,” Beta one answering; “and on top of that, Julie has the silver hunting knife that was in the van.”

    Several werewolves growl; wanting blood but being denied thanks to orders from on up and a spy no one knows of.
    “We need order,” Beta two speaking to Beta One; “at this rate the pack is going to go renegade and hunt anything with a pulse.”
    “I know,” Beta One growling; “but we need to find the spy.”
    “Leave that to me,” Beta two nodding; “I’ll find him.”
    “Thank you brother,” Beta one grinning; “that will be a load off my mind."
    Beta Two nods as he helps restore order to the pack. All the while silently texting and recording everything being done to ensure that the elder council’s wishes are made. 'I'll be home soon Zylina. I just hope your Uncle doesn't do any more stupid stunts during this mess,' Beta Two thinking as he helps get the pack in order.
