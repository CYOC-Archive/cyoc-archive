    Power Play, the magnificent and spectacular adventures of Dawn City's might team of teen heroes! High schoolers by day, heroes on weekends (metaphorically), these six heroes, Force Man, Aquaboy, Red Lightning, X-Stream, Persecles, and Power Kid. Together or individually, they protect the good people of Dawn from the criminal minds of Jester Lord, Gellunguy, Black Atom, Leech & Adhesiva, Gorgon Gal, Johny DarkNight and more.

    As they fight to protect the city, will they discover the identity and allegiance of the beautiful and mysterious thief, the Black Rose? Witness and behold the spectacular and death-defying feets of the teen heroes of Dawn City:

    Force Man, a powerful hero with the strength of 30 men, the capability of a soaring eagle, and the invincible body of a machine, he leads the fights against evil and his nemesis the Jester Lord. Clad in a dark blue, skin tight armless lycra suit, red blues, white belt with golden emblem, and dark blue eye mask, Force Man proudly fights and wears the shining white F emblem on his chest with pride. Behind the blue mask is Justin Macy, a young blond haired, blue eyed lad with a kind personality just trying to pass the eleventh grade.

    Then there's Aquaboy, a muscular lad clad with a green and orange spandex suit and orange mask with the power to control water and command the ocean's creatures. He fights against those like Gellunguy, the horrid mutant sea beast who wishes to convert man to fish. This muscled land shark is swift and strong and is invincible in the sea. His true identity is 16 year old Mitchel Rineman, a local school boy on the swim team.

    Coming fast and strong is the amazing Red Lightning. With the power of thunder and the speed of light, his enemies will only see the blurring red and gold of his spandex suit. He's an unstoppable might only matched by his notorious enemy, the wicked Black Atom. The muscular speedster battles for truth and justice, just like his alter ego Marek Simpson, an 18 year old high school senior football captain.

    Then their is X-Stream, the boy with the power of x-ray vision, heat beam blasts, and power punches. Villains beware when they see the golden x on the lad's emerald green suit and his mighty silver gauntlets. The muscular lad is strong and swift with his X-merrang. Only his archenemies, the deadly twins Leech and Adhesiva, can give him a run for his money. Known only by his friends, X-Stream is really Patrick Ward, a young, rich 17 year old lad who uses his wealth to help humanity.

    Following his friends is the super strong Persecles, the greek myths reincarnate. With his godly strength and accurate swordmanship, he brings back the legend in his leather singlet and flexible armor. His muscular body is intimidating to all, even to the deadly Gorgon Gal. With the power of two legends, is there anything he cannot do? The might myth is common 18 year old Nick Barkly, a wrestling champ and descended of Hercules himself.

    Finally, there's the young hero, Power Kid. The ever power-shifting lad with the power of stretching limps, psychic shields, and enhanced muscles, he is known to increase his strength to overpower the villains. Wearing only a tight blue and red singlet and mask, Power Kid is a power young force. He battles the evil master mind Johny DarkNight, a mad scientist. Behind the might powerful year is Wesley McKnight, a simple chest club member who you should never underestimate.

    These young teens are about to go in an adventure unlike any other. Alone or together, with the occasional assistance from the Black Rose thief, these boys battle evil!
