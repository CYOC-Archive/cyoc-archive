    Roberta's race changed

    At first Jake couldn't tell what had changed. He looked around the room but saw nothing. He figured "Roberta" could have changed by she was holding him facing away from her so he couldn't see. It was then that Jake saw his hair change again. It roughened in texture and began to curl tremendously. He looked at his arm as it turned darker.

    Jake was placed in a playpen and was finally able to see Roberta. She was now an African American version of herself. She had long, braided black hair and a larger caboose. She looked down at Jake and smiled.
    "That's right baby girl. Just wait there Janel, Momma's gonna be right back." She said as she walked out of the room.

    Jake figured that Roberta was the only one who changed, but since she was Jake's biological mother, Jake changed to accommodate this reality. So now Jake was a black baby girl. Practically the opposite of what he once was. He sat up in his pink diaper that had once been his sister and gazed across the room to see Luke the dog chewing on the camera.

    He tried to get the dog's attention as he heard his "mother" closing in...
