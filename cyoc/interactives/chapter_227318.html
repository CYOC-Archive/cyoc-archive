    Going into a new world

    added by bejjinks A year ago O Reality alteration

    Andrew was not a happy man, seeing the US in crisis because of Trump. He grew up a Christian conservative with actual real ethics but the more he learned about science, the more he realized that Christianity was not really that ethical.

    His resources were strained. It was so hard to afford anything with the moronic MAGAs blocking the government preventing the government from addressing real problems. He worked like a dog at his job as a tree trimmer, and it was thankless exhausting work. Every day, he wondered if anything could be done to change the world.

    One day, he came across a book, a blank journal. He had purchased it from an estate sale. Some old woman had died and it was in a box full of other books. He hadn't found anything in the pages when he was at the sale but figured he could use a blank journal. After a few months, he found himself in his garage. Then, somehow, a miracle occurred. The words sprang across the book before his very eyes to think about something he wanted to know and then turn the page. Was this book part computer? He immediately thought.

    The book explained immediately its origins and that it could rewrite reality itself, giving him powers over how the rules by which at least Earth was governed.

    "Well let's give it a try and see what I can do?" He thought.

    He got to work. He wrote:

    "Children are respected and not abused. Children are taught ethics and to obey good laws but if a parent or priest orders a child to sin, that parent or priest is put to death. The country is ruled by a seven branch government with many checks and balances in place to keep any individual or group from acquiring too much power because absolute power corrupts absolutely. Wealthy business owners are still entitled to their profits but if they are found guilty of OSHA violations or other forms of cheating their employees, the wealthy business owners are to be punished and they are not allowed to pass the penalties off to their employees or customers. Everyone will be held accountable to the law regardless of political standing or socio-economic standard. I live comfortably in a large house and I am highly respected in my community because they know I am ethical and judge fairly. The USA is the most powerful country in the world with no rivals. Every human is respected and given the dignity he deserves."

    Andrew smiled as he finished and closed the book. There was a flash of white light.

    Suddenly, he found himself in a large house, with a spacious kitchen. The children ran in. His son was wearing pink and his daughter was wearing blue. His wife followed.

    There was a swimming pool out back and the house was filled with wonderful electronics including a dishwasher to wash his dishes and a roomba to vacuum his floors.

    "Honey, would you like a massage." His wife asked.

    It seemed that all was well with the world but he hadn't really looked outside his own home so he got on the internet and did some research. Crime still existed so Andrew grabbed the book and started writing.

    "Privatized prisons are illegal and the government runs the prisons now. There is no recidivism because instead of sentencing criminals to a period of punishment, criminals are sentenced to rehabilitation institutions where they are kept for however long it takes to get them to stop committing crime."

    Later, a walk through town revealed that everyone was happy and dressed in all sorts of colorful outfits. There were fewer of the old problems, no racism, no sexism, no ableism, just a simple community enjoying their FREEDOM and LIBERTY.

    Andrew felt like exploring what else he could do to improve the world.
