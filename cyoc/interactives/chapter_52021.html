    As Andrew continues walking through the arcade, he stops by a game called "Sexy Sorceress". He stops and stares at the
    game's title screen; the magic girl seemed to be staring out at Andrew as if she were real. He dismissed it as a foolish
    thought, but almost immediately she started moving in a third dimension, and soon she was standing in front of Andrew.

    The female wizard was skinny and surprisingly short at about 5'2. Her blue eyes and long black hair were elegant, and her
    mature, slender features made her look more mature than she actually was. She was wearing a black dress with a black and
    blue military shirt on top, like one would see in movies about European wars, only much more fashionable, comfortable,
    and tight. She seemed to be eyeing Andrew, and he realized that his wish must be working on the sorceress.

    "You're quite handsome, you know." The wizardly girl spoke, her voice surprisingly dominative for such a small person, "I'm
    glad you came to my game today."

    "So can you, like, leave the game whenever you want?" Andrew was nervous speaking to a video game character, and it was
    creeping him out!

    The sorceress laughed, "Of course! Do you think a woman who possesses such power as I do would be stuck in an arcade? I
    never found a man attractive enough to get me to leave my own world. I desire only sexy men and destruction. Destruction
    is something your world lacks. In my world, wars never end, and villains are always in abundance! A man like you I could
    never find in either world, though. The only ones whom I liked never believed in magic and power. But you do!" The small
    woman placed her hands on Andrew's shoulder, and he realized she was levitating above him, their bodies uncomfortably
    close. "I think you and I should join, no?"

    "Err," Andrew stammered, "I think that would be great,... uh..."

    "Oh, my name! I'm Gale Angel. No need to tell me you're name, I know it already. You might want to change it later though;
    you're name isn't nearly as beautiful as mine." Gale laughed again.

    "You're quite the narcissist." Andrew said, unpleased.

    "Yes, that's true." Gale looked disappointed. Andrew was surprised she wasn't furious. He was going to apologize, but the
    Gale cut him off, "How about I make it up to you by letting you choose what to do? We can do anything with my magical
    powers!" She twirled and struck an action pose.

    "Well, what do you ant to do?"
