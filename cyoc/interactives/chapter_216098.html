    Skin As Smooth As Silk And Satin

    You browse the aisles, curious. However, you already know what you would like to purchase. It always makes you a bit shameful but it needs to be done. You are looking for a jar of body wax. You aren't the hairiest female in the world, far from it, but it has become over time a real complex and pet peeve. There are girls with more body hair in your school but you are in the top tier for sure. You manage to control the problem by buying a lot of razors, wax, depilatory creams and spending a lot of time with tweezers and razors. You've done so since the sage of twelve and it's become a habit over time. In fact, you've become such an expert that you learned by trial and error which products were best and truth be told, a lot were just lies promoted by advertisement. A lot of disappointments and you wonder if this store will hold its promises or, as most often occurred, it was all just nice words and a beautiful package to better hide the lies. And this store dedicated to the beautification of the body certainly knows the importance of the packaging on display. Although here it seems the focus was on originality too. This store appears to cater to fans of fantasy mainly. The packages all represent imaginary and mythological creatures. This raises your doubts as to the seriousness of the products but you do find the little aisle dedicated to hair removal. You expect to find the usual brands yet even there, much to your surprise, the store has its own fantasy-based items. You look and find some promising-looking jars of body wax yet the prices are not too cheap. Just decent, actually. They do bear their odd mascots and name though but it might be worth the try. The names are tempting, actually.
