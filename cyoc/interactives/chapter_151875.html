    "Alright now!" he barked, in full drill sergeant mode, "You are now all Animalians. Do you know what that means?" he asked. After receiving some answers that were clearly sexual in nature, amongst others, he quickly set them straight.

    "While that wasn't exactly the answer I was looking for, being an Animalian means, among other things that there are things you can do, and things you can't eat due to your new species. So I'm here to teach you about all that as well as new health habits that will keep you from going soft."

But what wasn't helping was the way that he laughed at their complaints and/or what they said about how tired they were as he continued to push them through it.

    "I'm gonna need a Long Island Iced Tea after this." complained one cat Animalian.

    "Ouch! My bikini zone is chaffing!" complained a mouse Animalian.

    "Energy... depleting... need... sugar!" complained a robin Animalian.

    "Must... never... run... again!" complained a bull Animalian.

    While technically an after-school program, it was seemingly just as, if not more, brutal as standard military training. But it also seemed that Sgt. Bash was capable of making compromises for some people that others found unfair.

    "Why does Sylvia get to do the thigh spreading machine only halfway while the rest of us have to do it all the way?" complained one of the newer horse Animalians.

    "Sylvia was recently "marked" as the Bavarian Warmblood she is, and she's still a little tender from it." explained Sgt. Bash.

    "I saw the video of it on the Animalia Website." said another new bull Animalian, "It's already over one million views, and there were several comments that it was 'cruel and unusual punishment' for her being a chronic flirt."

    "And this isn't, private?!" asked Sgt. Bash, going into full drill sergeant mode so fast it frightened said bull Animalian quite a bit.
