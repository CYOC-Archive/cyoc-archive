    The field of home experimentations

    added 5 years ago A BM S O

    There was no denying that for Mr. Klein, such a surreal event could screw one's mind, as he grimly notted when seeing his wife which was feeling sick.
    But him... He wasn't distraught.
    Well, not that much, but still.
    As a researcher, it was his duty to observe phenomenoms and draw conclusions out of them.
    And he had already noticed how the device looking like a phone had made several drastic changes upon these animals, changes on a DNA-rewritting scale.
    He also suspected the animals to be more than meets the eye, and recieved confirmation when watching the screen of the device : the name of his son Brody, along with those of his friends, were recorded as the current subjects.
    The device had also scanned it's immediate surroundings, listing a certain Marcus Klein among the persons present in the room, and a Victoria Boones, his wife's former family name.
    Marcus Klein made a surprised sound, and considered his options. Looking at the Kangaroo, he then decided to press a button, downright zapping him.
    Seconds later, there was now a carbon-copy of the previous Swiss Shepherd. And then an Arabian Horse. And then a Boxer Dog.
    The trio of friends were shocked beyond relief : what they couldn't achieve hours ago, Brody's father accomplished it in the blink of an eye.
    Finally, the teen showed up, still wearing his former clothes when it had initially started. Mrs. Klein looked both at him and her husband toying with the device.
    " Oh my god... " she started. " Brody... "
    " Wow, dad ! " chirped Brody, pleased beyond words. " I don't know how you did this, but it's perf- "
    His words died out the moment Mr. Klein had pressed yet again the device, instantly making him back into a Boxer.
    Everyone but a beaming Mr. Klein seemed confusedly shocked by the outcome, while Brody angrily barked.
    " This device so is fascinating, " did he spurted out. " I would've given everything in my life to get a hold of it. Wow... "
    " Honey ? " meekly asked Victoria to his husband. " What are you talking about ? "
    He turned his head to his beloved wife.
    " I'm literally making History. "
