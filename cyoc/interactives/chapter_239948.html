    From Teen To Baby To Adopt

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover Yesterday AR O Mythological Magic Baby

    “So, any picks yet?” the woman asked with a little smile and eyes shining with interest at what the teen might choose.
    “On second thought, maybe this isn’t such a good idea,” Amy said in a rush.
    While it had seemed intriguing - hell, even tempting, to be honest - at first, Amy then thought how crazy it was. Who wanted to be an object? To be used? Especially by babies!!?? Known for getting so dirty and soiled so easily?!!? This was just madness! And Miss Luna! She could do magic and she catered to these tastes and needs that were rather twisted. Nay, sick? What was wrong with her? Why did she just cater to baby stuff? Talk about a waste of potential! Or was her magic simply limited to baby things? Could it be possible? Either way, whether Miss Luna could help it or not, it was really a crappy brand of magic. Why could she not do more impressive things? Alas, unbeknownst to her, Miss Luna had a lot more magical abilities than Amy suspected and the sorceress was currently reading her thoughts. She felt a bit annoyed at the teen's arrogance and meanness but she knew how to remedy that. Miss Luna thus acted as though she was being normal, asking a normal question.
    "Why would you say so?"
    "Well, it's baby things and... well, I don't like babies that much," stammered the teen.
    "Why not?"
    "Well... I think they drool, vomit, and emit foul things and odors," answered Amy.
    "So do you," said sensibly Miss Luna.
    "Yeah, but I can control myself. And I don't think babies are that cute."
    "Yet I am ready to bet that you were an adorable baby once."
    "Nah. I don't want to think about it. And I'm leaving," said the girl, turning around.
    This place was too creepy!
    "I am sure that with a little change of perspective you won't be so squeamish about being a baby," said Miss Luna.
    With a spell and a puff of smoke, Amy was gone, replaced by an adorable little baby of six months. Distraught at being so small and helpless, she started crying. Miss Luna picked her up with a smile, and diapered and clothed the little baby girl. She stuck a pacifier in her mouth. As she had everything in the store, Miss Luna dressed Amy in a pick onesie and then in a dress that looked very old-fashioned in style. She set her afterwards in a wicker basket all in frills and added a unicorn plushie. Miss Luna last opened a portal and gave the basket to an extremely ugly green creature in Amy's opinion. But what made her want to really cry was the fact that she was being sold off to an auction. In exchange for diamonds offered by the ugly little monster to the witch. She'd be bought by some weird creatures to be adopted! Yet despite her cries, she was taken away, off to an uncertain fate within a realm inhabited by fay creatures. Who would adopt her? And how would she cope being a baby again?
