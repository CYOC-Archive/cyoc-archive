    Amanda was in the middle of trying to decide what to change next, but her thoughts were suddenly interrupted by a flash of red from her phone. She looked down and was faced with a scary looking screen. It was bright red and had dark black text, reading "ERROR; YOU HAVE EXCEEDED THE WORD COUNT DESPITE PREVIOUS WARNING. YOU HAVE ALTERED "4" WORDS INSTEAD OF "1" WITHOUT SUFFICIENT PAYMENT. PAY NOW OR CLICK HERE ---> 0."

    "Oh geeez, I thought it was just a virus before." She thought flustered. When she changed Ky, a message had popped up saying something about upgrading to a full version or something but she didn't take it seriously. Now however, it appeared to be legit. Try as she might she couldn't leave the message screen. Her phone wouldn't even turn off! She was left with two options, select the pay now button and give freaking one hundred thousand dollars or select the mystery option.

    "Dammit... well I don't have that kind of money." Amanda sighed. "I guess we'll see where this goes." She hoped tapping on the circle.

    She felt odd for a moment, like something was fuzzy, and then she vanished. All of her desk items vanished with her, and there was no trace of her ever being there. The front door to the store opened and Latisha walked in alongside another woman. This woman had dark curly hair and tan skin, with way too long nails and bouncy breasts that almost knocked over the box she was carrying.

    "Thanks for coming in on such short notice Frankie." Latisha sighed. "We've been short a receptionist for a few days now."

    "Oh it's no problem girl." The woman chuckled through chews of her gum. "I'm like, always ready to help out my girlfriends."

    "Thanks hun." Latisha laughed, before abruptly stopping to clutch her stomach.

    "Somethin da matter?" Francesca asked.

    "Oh it's nothin hun... just a little weird feeling." She explained.

    "Be careful Tish, that's what I felt when Robbie was cooking." She laughed talking about her first born.

    "Don't scare me like that!" Latisha chuckled before walking off.

    Little did she know that her and her beefy husband Tyrone had made a little more than a stomach ache a few nights ago. Latisha was face down in her pillow, her fat as raised to the sky. Tyrone was tightly gripping her curly fro as he pounded her from behind. He had decided to not pull out that night but since Latisha was on the pill they figured it was fine.

    Of course, magic was about to ruin the odds as Amanda had been recreated as a recently fertilized egg inside her former coworker. Not only that, but as further punishment for her actions Amanda's awareness was fully stripped from her, leaving her to live a new life from scratch.

    The question is, where did the app go now that it lost it's user? Who has it now?
