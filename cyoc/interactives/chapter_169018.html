He crossed his arms across his torso to try as if to hold in the heat of his body, but the biting cold still nipped at him through the thin pullover he opted for instead of a thicker sweater or even a sensible windbreaker.

The Commons door swung open, and warm air, heavy with the scent of many foods, greeted him. Swiping in, he went upstairs a level and looked for his friends. Sure enough, they were at the usual area.
