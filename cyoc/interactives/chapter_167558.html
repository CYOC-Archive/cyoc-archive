    Cole sighed heavily, straining against the zip ties he was bound in. It had been a week already since he was taken into custody.

    Trapped in a dark room, made uncomfortable, and continuously woken up the former Marine smirked. He had used these same techniques in his time as a Marine recon leader, they were trying to break him down and get information from him. "Idiots," he mumbled.

    A bright light now illuminated the room. Cole squinted, his eyes slowly growing accustomed to the light as a figure entered the room. Cole now watched as the familiar figure drew a chair next to Cole. "Dyson right?"

    Dyson said nothing, opening the dossier as he began to scan through Cole's service record. "Sgt. Cole Harris," he stated. "Marine Recon leader. Awarded the Silver Star, Bronze Star, Distinguished Service Medal, and a Purple Heart." Dyson closed the dossier quickly, a small smile on his face. "Huh a genuine war hero, I'm impressed."

    Cole said nothing as Dyson now lifted Coles head. "Guess the Purple Heart is what finally put you out of service," he said. "Understandable, I mean I've seen your wife, shes quite a catch."

    Cole grimaced, straining against his bonds as he glared at Dyson. "Where is she?"

    "Shes safe Sgt.," Dyson smirked. "Shes in our custody but were taking every measure to ensure her comfort. We just need a few questions answered."

    Cole grinned, taking a deep breath as he stared at his captor. "DHS you said," he asked. Dyson nodded as Cole began to chuckle. "Met a few of you in Afghanistan. Wanna be tough guys, spend all your time in the gym working out. When shit hit the fan each and everyone of them pissed their pants. You should know better Dyson, you cant break a Marine. This shit right here, this is what I call an easy day."

    "Why is it the jarheads are always such alpha males," Dyson chuckled. "I've read your dossier Cole. Your father was a decorated Major that died recently under suspicious circumstances. You have three other siblings. Julia a doctor, Derrick a school teacher, and Claudia who is about to graduate college. You also have a son, a son that seems to have mysteriously vanished since you were detained. DHS would rather talk with you first."

    "Leave them out of this," Cole grunted.

    "I'd love too," Dyson grinned. "Just tell us what you know. We already know your sister Claudia is connected to this somehow. Hate to see her join you in here."

    Cole glared at Dyson before spitting in the mans face, a sadistic smile spreading across his face. "I'm not telling you shit," Cole grinned. "And if you touch my siblings or my son I'll kill you."

    "It seems your not ready to talk," Dyson stated, wiping his face clean. "I'll give you time to think. Just remember Sgt. Harris, the longer you take the more danger you put your siblings and your son in. Do you really want to have to bury another family member?"

    Cole growled, watching as Dyson stood exiting the room as the lights went off yet again. The former marine now strained once more against his restraints, this was but the first meeting with his captors, future encounters would not nearly be so pleasant.
