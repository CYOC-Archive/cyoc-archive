    Jeff thought of what he would change into with this upside down option. But then, he thought of something that was always intriguing. Ever since he was little, he had a strange fascination the Black Widow female spider. While other kids are scarred of them, Jeff always like them. How they move, how they act, how they feed, and how they spin their webs. A part of him feels like he would hang out with them. To be one with them. So with this program, he can turn into the Black Widow and live with them for a long time.

    Thinking about it, he made his decision, "Why the Hell not. Since I like Black Widows, why not be one with them. After all, this Upside Down package can make it happen, right?"

    So he started to go to the second portion and begins his work. First he switched the drop down to species, then another option showed up at what creature he wants to be. So he typed Black Widow and worked his way down. Once he set up the second part, he looked skimmed to see if he needs to change something or not. Here's what the second option says...

    Species: Animal
    Type: Black Widow
    Gender: Female
    Subject A Mentality: Yes
    Subject B Mentality: Yes
    Awareness: On
    Clothing Change: Yes
    Reality Change: Yes
    Start of Change: Genitals
    End of Change: Head
    Duration of Change: 1 Hour
    Remaining Changed Until: 1 Year

    He choose yes to his mental and his second subject option on so that there won't be confusion of the Black Widow and he won't get scarred as the changes progress. I did have to leave the awareness on as he wanted to feel everything that happens to him, even if he's just going to be a huge thorax that spews out web. Also chose the changes to start on his dick and end in his head just to he could witness his body changes before the head would change last. He even selected yes for both the clothing and reality option as well since he doesn't want his family worried at where he would be. Though then Jeff thinks about it, clothing is impossible since Widows don't wear clothes, unless your Scarlett. The hour change is would make the changes nice and slow but he wanted to remain change for a full year to experience everything for long. After all, to a spider, time has no meaning.

    Looking through his option, he smiled and knew that everything checks out. "Looks like everything is ready. Time to make the changes."

    He clicked on the apply option and was waiting for the changes to commence. But then, a warning appeared on the computer and it say...

    'Warning: By accepting the changes, you are subjugated to a full year as a different being. If you die or get impregnated during that time frame, the effects will be permanent and you will never be able to change back. Are you sure you wish to continue?'

    Jeff read that and knew what it means. There is a risk of using that Chronivac and by wanting to be a Black Widow for a year, would have some consequences. And since the spider would have control of his body, it would do whatever it wanted. He wondered if he would want to commit to a year as a Black Widow's thorax. But then, he shrugged it off and said, "You know. It's only a year. It's not like the Widow would do something foolish like getting killed or fucking another Black Widow. Let's just hope it doesn't wind up someplace that could be dangerous."

    So taking that said risk, he selected the Yes option and another message said...

    'Thank you for using the Chronivac. Enjoy your change!'

    Stepping out of the desk, he stood up and waited for the changes to start. "Okay... Here goes nothing!" he said as he could sense the changes to start.
