    "Justin, don't you dare...!" His mother began, but before she could even finish her sentence, Justin made his wish.

    "I wish myself and Sean were twenty-one and that Heather and me would have no memory of this conversation and that I forget everything about her ever being my mother." Justin wished.

    Heather didn't change much but her eyes glowed and her frustration turned to cheerfulness as she smiled and looked at the now 21 year-old Justin.

    "Don't tell me you're scared Justin." Heather smirked.

    "Scared? No, no, of course not." Justin said a little hesitant.

    "Don't worry, just close your eyes and relax." Heather whispered as she moved into kiss Justin.

    All of a sudden, Sean accidentally opened the door on them.

    "Oh, oh, god. I am so sorry." Sean apologized.

    "Seriously? You could've knocked, jerk!" Justin said throwing a pillow at him.

    "Ugh, let's get going, we're going to that party anyway." Heather said rolling her eyes and going out the door.

    As Justin was headed out the door, Sean noticed the ring Justin had on his hand.

    "Hey, what's the deal with that? You're not married already are you?" Sean joked.

    "Nah, just an old family ring. I'm gonna sell it down at this store for some extra cash." Justin explained.

    He was half-lying to Sean, although he still knew what the ring did, he didn't want to use it again with things being fine the way they are.

    So Heather, Sean, and Justin went to Merlin's Magic Shop to sell it.

    "Ah, welcome back." Merlin said to Justin.

    "I've...been in here before?" Justin asked not quite remembering how he got the ring in the first place.

    "No, I just say that to all my customers." Merlin joked.

    "Anyway, I can give you three hundred for this old thing, sound good?" Merlin said.

    "Sounds great!" Justin agreed as they took the money and left.

    As they left, Justin and Heather held hands.

    "Oh, looks like I already have a new customer." Merlin said as the next person for the ring walked in.

    Who could it be next?
