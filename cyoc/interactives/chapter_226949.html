    Marilyn and her husband had decided to go for a walk in the park to clear their heads and sort out the latest events. Then they happened upon a crying girl, she had a purple hair with green tips and heavy makeup, the elderly couple could somehow sense that something was different about her but still they went to see what was wrong.

    “Whatever is the matter dear?” Marilyn said and placed her hand on the girl’s shoulder.

    When she did so the girl looked up and her eyes were completely black, the darkness was spreading until it completely covered her body. With a scram from the teen girl a large energy shot out and threw the old woman back. Martin immediately went to help his wife and they both saw the girl become enveloped by a monstrous creature. The monster had glowing neon blue and pink energy that violently swarmed. This energy was held back and constricted by a shiny, glossy black material that held back the angry energy, molding it into the form of a pigtailed little girl. The creature wore a black and white Japanese style schoolgirl uniform.
    The creature let out screams but was quickly silenced by the shiny black material. Occasionally the glowing energy would break through but eventually would be restricted by the black material.

    Before Martin could say or do anything Marilyn was already up and had said the magic words and transformed into her teenage, magical girl form.

    “I am the pretty guardian of the solar system! I fight for love and justice! My name is Guardian Mars!” The red head announced, two flaming swords in her hands.

    “M-Marilyn?! What are you doing? And where did you get those swords?!” Martin shouted.

    “Heavens Martin, just go along with it, I have flaming swords!” Marilyn replied and launched herself at the Jōkikuma.

    Marilyn’s attack was stopped by the uniformed monster shooting out glossy black tendrils that wrapped themselves around her swords and contained their fire. The monster threw her into a tree, splintering the trunk, Marilyn had lost her swords and couldn’t dodge the incoming attack as the schoolgirl monster had grown many tendrils and shot out at the magical girl.
    Marilyn had closed her eyes in anticipation for the strike but when it didn’t come she opened her eyes and saw her husband in his teenage magical form as well, his large shells blocking the attack.

    “Looks like I’m your knight in shining armor.” The blonde said.

    “Be quiet Martin, this is not the time to be making jokes.” Marilyn said blushing.

    A loud scream echoed from the Jōkikuma and it launched more furious attacks on the two. With Solar Sentinel’s shield having the magical ability to negate any impact for the user.

    “Are you two flirting?! Why do you get to be happy! When I can’t!!!” The Jōkikuma screeched becoming more furious, the black restrictive material striking Martin’s shield with increasing intensity.

    Martin then shoved his shield back, deflecting the attacks and giving him an opening. Delivering a kick he was surprised to see it not phase the supernatural monster.

    “If Harry can’t be with me then your girl can’t have you!!” She screamed.

    She wrapped her constricting black clothing around his leg and slammed him into the ground, creating a small crater. Martin coughed up a little blood and just barely managed to pull up his shield to stop the Jōkikuma’s next strike. Marilyn managed to retrieve her swords and slammed them together, creating a Collin of fire right at the creature, she flinched at the attack. Martin noticed the schoolgirl monster’s attack was made from her restrictive material, exposing the raging energy beneath.

    Martin rushed to his wife and carried her away from the monster.

    “What is this thing anyway? It looked like it came from that poor girl.” Marilyn asked.

    “I don’t know but I think I know how to defeat this thing: this black material is her defense, but it’s also her offense, when she uses big attacks like that she exposes the energy inside and your fire managed to harm her exposed energy body.” Martin explained.

    “But what about that girl this thing came out of? What would happen to her?” Marilyn asked.

    Martin looked at her and Marilyn raised her eyebrow.

    “Martin Lee O’Toole! This is not some battlefield in the jungle, we have to help this girl not blow her up!” She argued.

    “Give me a break Marilyn, she slammed me into a crater!” He replied.

    “Quit bickering like an old married couple and die!” The Jōkikuma yelled and rushed towards them.

    The school uniformed creature’s advance was temporarily interrupted by a flash of light and the pink cat creature from the house appeared.

    “Guardian Mars is right kyu!” He said.

    “See Martin—wait what are you doing here?” The red head asked.

    Pokoyo then explained the Jōkikuma to the two and Marilyn looked at her husband.

    “See: if we defeat the monster we can save that young girl.” Martin said.

    “I still think we should try to talk to her.” Marilyn said.
