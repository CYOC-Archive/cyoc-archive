    Kaylee almost said 'yes master' but thought better of it.

    So Kaylee thought instead of her earlier suggestion to get ride of the doors, 'It's not like

    and sound some. And most of the door frames are a little too small for us anyway and
    decidedly too small for Master anyway. And I'm betting everyone who works and lives here
    will be Transformed or Dared soon enough.'

    Kaylee took notice of the reddish brown fur that matched her own on Bart, and the black
    fur on Jennifer The First that matched her Master's. 'Hmmm, are they become -our- cubs?
    Or are they just changing to match the pack? There was nothing in the transform about
    that, was there?' Kaylee wanted to bring this up, but again didn't want to distract her
    Master.

    Making sure his parents eyes were on him, Jensen with one big arm tossed their bundle of
    clothes into a waiting waste basket. "You won't be needing those ever again!"

    The humiliation, vulnerability, and unfairness of their situation proved to be too much as
    the two exposed nine year olds covered in fur began to sniffle the details of the transform
    had flown over their heads and frightening imagery began to fill their now active
    imagination.

    Jensen was slightly regretful that the twins would take a while to completely dismantle the
    two cars and couldn't join him and his mate. He was sure that they would have enjoyed
    the show. But a stitch in time saves nine, and the sooner the biggest pieces of traceable
    evidence were gone the better for himself and his pack. Not that he had any clue how
    much time it actually took to dismantle a couple of cars.

    "You're bad!" Jennifer shouted, and covered her mouth, now a muzzle, he had wanted to
    say something a lot worse to her child, mostly along the lines of telling him how much of
    a total spoiled brat she/he was.

    "Big and Bad, Jenny." Jensen nodded.

    The semi-wolf nine year holds covered their heads as the ears on the sides of their head
    vanished and new triangular one popped up on the top of their heads. The changes in
    sounds disoriented the two, a few seconds later their muzzles completed and a whole new
    world of smells hammered through their heads causing them to fall on their heads which
    cause them to yelp and stand up quickly as their fluffy black and reddish tails had
    completed their growths.

    Jennifer I was scared that she'd be soon seeing the world in blacks and whites but her
    vision remained apparently the same.

    "I'm beginning to wonder if I should have gotten camcorder, then again I wouldn't want a
    tape to fall into the wrong hands before they became paws." Jensen giggled at his own
    words.

    Bartholomew and Jennifer I looked at their little hands, now covered in black or reddish
    fur with claws, the insides of their hands were now covered with callous, they tested their
    altered digits morbidly. Their hair became much shorter, until in Bartholomew's case it
    left him 'bald' with only the fur on top of his head.

    "Ya gotta stop it! Ya gotta! Ya gotta!" Bartholomew tried desperately one last time actually
    stomping his paws.

    "Oh there's nothing I could do if I wanted." Jensen said his smile never leaving as he held
    up a paw to his own muzzle gentleman like, "Once a Dare or Transform has been spoken,
    it can't be taken back. Ask Kaylee, she knows best, she was the one whose lame brain idea
    as Kyle that gave me the new big bad me! I repaid her in kind soon enough."

    Kaylee herself smiled at the memory of earlier today, her biggest screw up as Kyle had
    resulted in the best thing had ever happened to her.

    Bartholomew and Jennifer I looked at each other in the eyes fearfully, as looked as their
    eyes changed twisting into something that was no longer human. Both realized fearfully,
    besides the length of their fingers and the addition of a thumb, plus the position of their
    spines that had them stand up on two legs and face foreword, there was nothing really
    human about either of them. They looked more like cubs of a species of wolf that had
    become tool users much like some apes had millions of year ago.

    Both hugged themselves inches away from each other and moaned once more as their
    muscle mass increased, staying nine year olds, but having the muscular of a knight's page.
    The hunched foreword ever so slightly.

    Jensen figured at this point their physical changes were round about done, which meant it
    was time for the rest of them to be rewired.

    "Now both of you stand up straight and side by side." Jensen said, they obeyed but he saw
    the fear in their eyes. This just meant he got to toy with them a tiny bit more.

    "Now how old are you both?"

    "Forty!"

    "Thirty eight and a half!" Jennifer again started at her own answer.

    "Nuh-uh-uh!" Said Jensen waving a claw. "Do you LOOK that old? Tell the truth now."

    They looked at each other and bowed their heads, "No." "Not really."

    "Now how old do you look?"

    "Nine." "Nine and a half... okay nine."

    "Now how old are you?"

    They looked unsure.

    "Say it!"

    "NINE!" They yelped together.

    "Now what was that answer?"

    "Nine." "Nine." They said more relaxed this time, the looks in their eyes spoke of utter
    defeat. If they weren't going to squirm anymore, then that was that.

    "Now answer each question as fast you can. What are your favorite things?"

    "Spiders." "Flowers."

    "How old are you?"

    "Nine." "Nine."

    "What are you?"

    "Wolfies." "Puppies."

    "What are your social security numbers?"

    "555-650-001." "555-499-002."

    "How old are you?"

    "Nine!" "Nine!"

    "What's your favorite food?"

    "Ham." "Deer."

    "how old are you?"

    "NINE!" "NINE!"

    "What are your PIN Numbers?"

    "1929." "1777, and really ya know the N in PIN stands for Number so really-"

    "-How old are you?"

    They look at each other and shrugged, "Nine." "Nine."

    "What are your names?"

    "Bart." "Jenny."

    "Anything else?"

    "Nope." "Nu-uh."

    "What were your names?"

    "Bartholomew Benedict Smith, blagh!" Bart rolled out at his tongue in disgust at the name.
    "Jennifer Elizabeth Smith The First." Jenny giggled like it was a game.

    "Who do you work for, who do you love?"

    "The pack." "Our pack."

    "Who is your pack?"

    Bart crossed his arms in a know it all manner, "The Big Bad Wolf Pack!"

    "You're our pack!" Cheered Jenny at Jensen and Kaylee.

    "What's your education Bart?"

    Bart cross his arms again and this time his legs too with a 'smart yet cool guy' self
    confident look on his face, "Master's Degree in Business an' Economics." He said with a
    drop of arrogance.

    "What are you and Bart going to do Jenny?" Jensen asked.

    "Gonna make sure dis' place stays up and runnin' for ya and the rest of da pack!" Jenny
    said smiling with her muzzle showing lots of fangs.

    "Is there anything you want to do with those?" Jensen pointed at the clothes in the waste
    basket.

    Bart and Jenny instantly went over to the waste basket, and tore the expensive pants and
    jackets to shreds and calmly retrieved a couple set of keys a remote and a pair of wallets
    then tossed the rest back in the garbage, putting the said items on the desk. Jensen made
    a note that those clothes would have to go in the fireplace and not in the garbage.

    "Anything else?" he asked.

    "Naw." Bart shrugged.

    "Not really." Jenny answered.

    Jensen looked gleefully at the two new naked wolf cubs. Then he looked at the the still
    human Perkins.

    "Jenny, Bart, it's time for you to play a little game with the two humans who butted into
    our pack's home." 'Not that they'll have a choice what to answer due to the Dare.'

    "Really?" "Cool!"

    Jensen remembered his previous error. "Actually just Bart, Jenny you can come here and sit
    on my lap and watch."

    "Okay!" Jenny nodded the wolf cub climbing into the towering wolf beast's lap.

    "Now Bart come here and I'll tell you what to tell the two humans."

    "Whao' kay." Bart nodded and brought up an ear to Jesen's muzzle as he spoke.

    "Aren't you guys excited?" Kaylee said to her parents hugging them as she continued to
    weight them down. Both human beings still felt sick to their stomachs at the imagery
    playing out before them. "You're gonna be part of the pack in no time!"
