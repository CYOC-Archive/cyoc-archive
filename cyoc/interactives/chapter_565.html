Because of his size, he used suspenders to hold up his jeans instead of a belt. He had long, frizzy hair. And he wore a t-shirt that had a Saturday morning cartoon character on the chest!

    They did not see the man's eyes squint at each of them, and the energy that shot from those eyes was invisible, so no one could see it.

    Bets was the first. She felt a funny feeling all over for a second. Then it passed. The same happened to each of the girls. It was so fast, none of them decided to say anything about it.

    They left the mall, got on the bus, and headed for home.
