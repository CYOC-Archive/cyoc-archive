A realization that the placement probably meant it could have different settings for different people soothed her annoyance and spurred her to inspect each setting and read each informative tooltip fully.     Kendra looked around the room. Nothing seemed different. Wait, that wasn't true... There was another dresser over by closet. That hadn't been there before!

They all focused on a newborn who as an embryo had split into twins in the embryo, then the twins had partially re-merged but had not done so fully. For some reason the idea of someone riding her edged on her nerves and the grimmace she had in a few of the pictures revealed that was not a new feeling.

Or, she mused, she could experiment a little further. Or maybe involve someone else... Either with or without their knowledge....
