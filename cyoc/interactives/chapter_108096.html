    “Jack Crawford is behind the whole event,” he answers; “my contacts at the New York Stock Exchange confirmed it all.”
    “I’m sure my brother would like to read that in the paper,” Lance looking his messages; “anything else?”
    “Crawford managed to get some of the voting shares,” the business reporter answers; “apparently some of the former execs that Benton kicked out, tried to contact him to sell their stock back to him. Since he didn’t respond, they took that as a clear indication that Benton wasn’t exercising his first right of refusal clause.”
    “Okay,” Lance thinking; “get it all typed up and ready for tomorrows edition. And make sure-“
    “Fair and neutral,” the business reporter finishes. Before leaving he looks at Lance, “your lady friend. The one whose scent I can smell all over you. Is she the one?”

    Lances grins; “it’s looking that way.” Lance looking at the reporter, “Now get to your article.”
    “Oh one more thing,” the reporter pulling out some documents and handing them to Lance; “what do you know of the Wáng Technological Consortium?”
    “That it’s a company in china,” Lance looking at the papers; “what of it?”
    “They have a large number of voting shares of Knight Industry,” the reporter comments; “the interesting thing is, up and till two weeks ago, Far Houjin and his grandson Kai Hǔ Wáng where last seen in Shanghai. Afterwards they disappeared. No one has seen them.”
    “How does this have any bearing on the Jack and Benton story?”
    “If Jack Crawford got those shares,” the reporter doing the math; “he could in theory have enough leverage to kick your brother out.”
    “Run it as a side story,” Lance thinking; “Two of china’s prominent business men vanish, how does this effect the global economy as a whole. Or something along those lines; do the necessary research before submitting the article.”

    The business reporter leaves; leaving Lance to look at one of the articles from a Chinese paper translated into English detailing Far and Kai’s absence. Members of the company stating the two are on some spiritual retreat but maintaining regular contact. Lance deciding to send a text to his brother
    ‘Aware of Jack Crawford’s business dealings. An article in the gazette will appear in tomorrow’s edition. Care to talk about Far Houjin and Kai Hǔ Wáng disappearance from the Wáng Technological Consortium?’
