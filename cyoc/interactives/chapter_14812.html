    The water fountain of muscle growth

    As Ty strolled through the crowded gym, he looked for one area where lots of members liked to go. Then he noticed the water fountain, which was hanging from the wall, at the very end of the aisle separating the weights section from the cardio section in the large gym.

    To the left of the water fountain was the day care center, where parents could drop off their kids while they enjoyed their work out. To the right of the water fountain was the entrance to the locker rooms, which lead to another hallway. On either end of that hallway was a separate locker room; one for men, and the other for the women.

    Ty realized that the water fountain was right at the center of a high traffic area! He immediately began to wonder how to cast a spell.

    He decided that one person’s muscle growth would be in proportion to the amount of water consumed from the water fountain in the gym. One person would have to drink as much water as was in a small 12 ounce can of soda in order to gain 5 pounds of muscle! That way, not everyone would grow, only the ones who would drink the most.

    Ty made his wish, and he then walked over to the area where the stationary bikes were lined up. He sat on a bike that was directly facing the water fountain, so he could keep an eye on the people that would drink from it so that he could enjoy watching them grow!

    He set the program on the control panel of the bike to the easiest level, and he immediately began to pedal. He had given himself 45 minutes to do his cardio workout on the stationary bike.

    Already, one slender, young woman from the aerobics class came out of the classroom and took several sips of the cold water from the water fountain. She was so thirsty, that she walked away but immediately turned back to drink some more. When she had consumed at least 12 ounces of water, she began to feel a tingling all over her body.

    Immediately she felt the muscles in her arms and legs grow a little, and she realized that she was now a bit stronger, and heavier. With renewed energy, she ran back to the classroom and joined the other ladies in the aerobics class!

    Ty saw the look on her face and he noted the increase in muscle. He just grinned, as he continued to stare at the water fountain while anticipating his next lucky victim…
