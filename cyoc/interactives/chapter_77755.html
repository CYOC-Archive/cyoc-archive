    Erin looked around in shock, suddenly realizing what her words had done. She turned to see Mike still standing
    there unaffected. Then she turned to look at me.

    "What did you do?" She asked nervously.

    "Exactly what you wished." I replied subtly. I wasn't about to transform Mike when I didn't have to, but the
    wish had to granted, thus Mike was no longer Erin's brother, nor a child in this family. i guess that meant he
    was no longer my brother either by extension. This was still rather unfamiliar ground for me as well.

    "What are you doing here." Dad'd bellowing voice took us all by surprise. I had never heard him sound so
    furious. "Who are you, you little perv?"

    As Erin and Mike looked confused I suddenly realized that only us three, having heard the wish knew the truth.
    I became aware that I wasn't fully dressed and that Mike must seem like a peeping tom, spying on us like he
    was. To be honest it wasn't that far from the truth. Dad was utterly livid though. As Mike pleaded that he
    lived here, Dad pulled roughly by his collar and dragged him down stairs.

    "Why doesn't Dad know who Mike is?" quizzed Erin, her eyes drowning in accusation.

    "Hang on, this isn't my fault, I have to grant wishes you know that," I protested. "What should I have done?"

    "Wait, I didn't mean it."

    "But you said it," I cut her off. She really needed to be more careful with her words around me. Perhaps this
    will serve as a lesson. Still this as following Erin downstairs i couldn't help but feel sorry for Mike.

    "Mike, I'm so sorry" called Erin as Dad physical threw Mike out the door.

    "She did this" Mike was pointing towards me and Erin, I couldn't tell precisely which tough. "that bitch did
    this."

    Bitch! I'd have been a girl all of five minutes but I was not going to let anyone call me that. All pity for
    Mike was gone, the little creep deserved to be kicked out for leering us alone. Mike was still hurling insults
    at us as dad slammed the door in his face. I turned to see Erin looking upset.

    "We have to do something" she whispered.

    "I say we leave him." I replied. The bitch comment was still ringing in my ears. "Anyway I can't simply undo
    your wish."

    "Fine," She said thinking as we wondered back into her room. We looked out through the curtain at Mike sitting
    on the sidewalk.

    "I know you did this,you freaks have some sort of magical powers." Great advertise it to the whole
    neighborhood.

    "Come on, bitches" My blood was boiling. "You can't leave me out here like this."

    "He's right" said Erin "I wish..."
