    Sighted on Campus

    Nate padded through the woods, getting used to walking on all fours as he pondered what to do. The first thing he needed to do, as his stomach was dictating, was find something to eat. He was hardly suited to go through the line at the Garvey cafeteria like he was. So, hunting it would be. Besides, he had all these new instincts and heightened senses screaming at him. He could hear better and had a much sharper sense of smell as well. He slowly made his way through the woods when he spotted the majestic creature in front of him, a deer. Well, he would've thought it majestic in his prior human state. Now all he could think was, dinner!

    The deer barely had time to react before Nate had leaped on top of the deer. He dug his huge, sharp fangs into the tender flesh of it's neck and ripped outwards, quickly killing the animal. His wolf instincts now taken over, Nate made short work devouring the tasty morsels of the beast, sating his hunger in the process. Satisfied, Nate left the remains for the other woodland creatures to consume as he continued his way, trying to decide what to do.

    He caught sight of the lights of campus on the other side of the river and a mischievous thought occurred to him. Perhaps, I could sneak over and maybe scare a few of the students and faculty. Start a few rumors. After all, what is the point of being a werewolf if there isn't anyone who knew there was one on campus?

    Nate kept to the shadows as he quickly ran across the bridge over to the College side of the river. He darted along the coastline of the river on the other side, keeping to the outskirts of campus. It was there that he spotted his ideal victim. Kelly Weathers was taking an evening jog. Kelly was a self absorbed and annoying girl who had a certain level of celebrity from her YouTube Channel, Kelly's Styles, which was just as vapid as it sounded. Tonight, she was going to get the fright of her life.

    He slowly stepped out of the shadows and walked into the light beaming down from one of the path lights. Nate let out a low growl as he bared his large fangs. Kelly stopped dead in her tracks and visibly started to shake in fright. She turned and began running in the opposite direction, screaming for help. Nate followed in hot pursuit, keeping up with her each step of the way. He kept his focus on the back of her legs as they kept running and running, changing direction with her each step of the way. She could feel his hot breath on the back of her legs and cried out louder. He was so focused on following her, he didn't realize where she had led him until he was in the middle of the courtyard outside the student union.

    Nate froze as he looked around and noticed several students staring at him, their phones out and recording. They began to circle around the beast, talking amongst themselves. Nate looked around, trying to find an exit point. He bared his fangs again and let out a growl. It did nothing to sway the kids except make them back up a bit.

    "What do you think it is?" asked one student.

    "I have no idea. Some kind of wolf or bear?" responded another.

    Nate let out another growl as he circled around, debating his next move. This was not a brilliant idea, he thought.
