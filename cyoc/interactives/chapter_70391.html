    Younger and Unaware

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 11 years ago AR AP

    Sarah used the program to make herself 10 years old. Strangely, the pregnancy remained though unfortunately
    for her, her mental age became also 10 years old and forgot about the program.

    “Hey! You promised me!” exclaimed Greg

    “Too bad!” teased Sarah

    Sarah quickly ran off from the bedroom and ran all over the house, all while having fun. Greg tried to catch
    Sarah but just couldn't catch her.

    “This isn’t working, I need another plan” panted Greg.

    Greg then decided to go back to the program. Sarah noticed this and tried to stop him but Greg made it in time
    and locked the door right before she could get in.

    "Let me in! Let me in!" whined Sarah.

    "Too bad!" replied Greg loudly.

    “Now to fix this, once and for all” said Greg

    Greg pressed a few buttons and pressed enter. Sarah noticed she is growing up quickly until she reaches to
    early adulthood. Greg was watching the process from a eye window on the guest room.

    "I'm back to normal? But that means ... ! " thought Sarah.

    "That's right!" shouted Greg though the door.

    Sarah then noticed her belly is getting big really fast. The guest room quickly changed to a nursery again.
    and then she felt the contractions, soon giving birth to Anna again.

    “I guess I did deserve that” said Sarah still reeling from the pain.

    In a few moments, Anna became 20 and looking angry at Sarah. Meanwhile, the nursery changed back to
    Anna's room and the Sarah's labor pains vanished.

    "Hi Anna." said Sarah.

    "Hi what!? You made me go back nearly all the way! You're lucky you're my mother!" yelled Anna.

    "But ... " retorted Sarah.

    "But what!?" yelled Anna "and now it's your turn" she said calmly.

    "I heard that" said Greg and set Sarah's age to 1

    Sarah then noticed she is shrinking and quickly realized what's happening to her now.

    "No, Noooooooooooo" cried out Sarah as she regresses.

    Sarah soon became a 1 year old baby. Sarah picked her up and Greg opened the door at age 20.

    "At least this is over, I can't believe my mom would do this" said Anna.

    "Eh, just because people are decades old doesn't mean that they are not decades mature" replied Greg "I made
    her unaware this time, I'll change her back"

    "Okay I guess so but for now, let's have some more fun together" giggled Anna

    "First, I want to do something" said Greg.

    Greg went back to the computer and pressed some buttons and Anna followed him, carrying Sarah. In an instant,
    Jenny and Tim reappeared on Anna's bed with two cribs.

    "How did you do that?" asked Anna with amazement.

    "I clicked my section and dragged it to yours, recreating our babies then set their age to 1 and made it done
    in an instant" responded Greg.

    "Wow. That's amazing!" said Anna.

    "I know, now we can have our fun." said Greg.

    Anna placed Sarah on the crib next to Jenny and she and Greg start kissing.

    What do you do now?
