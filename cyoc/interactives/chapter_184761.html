    The fields of a fox

    ''What the...'' You enter the door and are immediately taken aback. Where you had expected to find an actual room, you had arrived somewhere you at first thought was the outside, had the outside been a massive field of emerald green grass brightly illuminated by the sun standing at noon. And looking back, there was no door. ''Uhm...'' You quietly mutter as you look around in what amounted as confusion. Out of all your friends, you yourself were the most easily frightened. This whole situation was at first starting to become eerily similar to standard haunted houses, which you were no fan at all of, but now this situation was becoming incredibly bizarre instead of scary.

    By this point Sutane had arrived and was paying close attention to your behaviour, the way you were looking around told her you were confused and she giggled. ''これはすべてすぐに理にかなっています小さな子供(This will all make sense soon little child.)'' Sutane said, mostly to herself since you couldn't hear her. ''最初に行く場所を教えましょう(Lets give you somewhere to go first.)'' She continued amused as she spun around you, thinking about how cute you'll look as a young little kitsune and how she'll go about transforming you. Jorōgumo enjoyed directly shaping someone from inside, Yuki-Onna preferred shaping them from the outside with her touch and Sutane enjoyed watching them change not from herself, but from a little bit of magic. The result tended to sway from what she intended but that just made it more interesting to watch.

    You were busy looking for the door when you noticed something in the corner of your eye, looking at it you noticed that a long strip of tall grass had been replaced by stone tiles forming a lightly curving path towards a large shrine far off in the distance you were pretty sure weren't there before. Sutane giggled some more. ''続ける。(Go on.) 私が私の子供を待たせないでください。(Dont keep me waiting my child.)'' She whispered into your ear from behind, her massive spectral bosom mere millimetres from your back, fully aware you couldn't hear her motherly tone.

    Having nowhere else to go other than maybe aimlessly wandering the fields of tall grass (which just reeked of horror movie potential), you reluctantly began walking the stone path toward the large shrine in the distance, carefully watching around for anything odd. Sutane floated closely, grinning from ear to ear (metaphorically) as she carefully tapped you on the head, releasing a tiny bit of magic. You didn't even notice the touch, nor did you notice the very beginning of your change as slowly but surely, your skin began shedding one small straw of hair, after the other, and your height lost a millimetre, before slowly losing another...
