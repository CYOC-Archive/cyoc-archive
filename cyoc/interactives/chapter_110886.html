Malice's attention was likely on the hope that one of Animalians who had come to the party was one of the uplifted police dogs from the Lyre Police Department or one of the uplifted wolves from the Animalia Police Department.

    "Of course he does," Malice chuckled, and poked Sarah's left breast with one paw, "He sees this generous swell and that ends up being a bit intimidating."

    "Yeah, sis," Malice agreed, "He seems nice enough, and he is a dog."

    "Okay... but remember, we'll have to introduce him to mom and dad," Sarah pointed, "As well as which ever police officer you successfully woo."

Malice and Sarah did get a few of the sausages that Abby's brought and they enjoyed them, but they didn't mention anything to Abby, as Sarah had the sense that the dish reminded Abby that because she had become a mare, a female horse, that she couldn't eat sausages. As they began to finish, the group began to break up again for a moment. Jessica took Sammy off to meet with the female hamster that Clara had found and Abby and Samson moved off to see what plans Clara had for their group with regards to panel talks on how they as a group had got on with the Animalia Outbreak, with Abby and Sarah being accidentally infected and later with Jessica joining of her own volition and Sophie remaining human. Sarah knew when and where this was to be at this party, but for the moment, they weren't required for that yet. That gave them the time for some socializing and seeing what was going on.

    "Well... that was good," Sarah gave a contented sigh.

    "Yeah," Sophie agreed, "and we got about an hour before we're supposed to go for the Animalian/Human cooperation and friendship panel presentation for the party."

    Sarah then felt Malice hug her from the side.

    "So, let's go see if Travis came," Malice encouraged Sarah, "Sophie and I will come and help keep him around so you can say that you like him."

    "What if his parents don't like me?" Sarah said nervously as they stood up.

    "Not like you?" Sophie asked, "Sarah... out of you, me, Abby, and Jessica, you were always the most adventurous and the most open to all of this Animalia stuff when it came out. Yeah, you're a double doser and are physically older and sexier than you were before, but that hasn't changed anything else about you. The worst you'll face is that they'll ask that you let Travis mature before the two of you officially become mates."

    Sarah looked to both Malice and Sophie. Both of them smiled confidently back to her.

    "Okay," Sarah spoke, "let's go see if he's here..."
