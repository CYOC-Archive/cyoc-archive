    "Chad! Leave him alone, man.... What's the problem?" Peter said to him.

    "Stay out of it, Peter!" Chad sneered back.

    "No! Quit picking on the freshmen, dude... that's lame!" Peter shouted back.

    "Oh yeah?" Chad said, in a threatening voice.

    Chad dropped Trey against the wall, before he turned around and started to walk towards Peter.

    "Look, Chad... You gotta quit picking on the-..." Peter began to tell him, when he was suddenly overcome by an intense tingling feeling in his chest.

    "Ooohhhh...." Peter groaned, just as Chad came up to him.

    Suddenly, Peter's body started to expand in all directions! His chest inflated like a large balloon, forcing the buttons to pop off his shirt!

    POP! POP! POP!

    Chad's mouth just dropped wide open, when he saw Peter hulk out of his clothes.

    Peter's arms became twice as thick, with soccer-ball sized biceps popping up. His chest ballooned to the point that his shirt ripped in four pieces!

    RRRRIIIIIPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPP!!!!!!!

    His jeans tore by the seams as his legs turned into massive redwood tree trunks, right before Chad and Trey. Trey was trembling with fear, since he had no idea, what was happening to Peter.

    Finally, the growth stopped, leaving Peter standing almost a foot taller than Chad.

    "Uhh... P-p-peter... I..." Chad stuttered. Peter was now hulking over him.

    "Get out of my face, you bully! Scram!" Peter ordered him, as he slowly walked closer to Chad, who was now a foot shorter. Peter was now 7 foot 6 tall!

    "I'm outta here!" Chad cried, as he turned around and ran out the door, at the end of the hallway.

    Peter turned to face Trey, who was trembling in the corner. His face was pale with fear, when he saw Peter walking towards him.

    "W-who a-a-are y-y-you?" Trey whispered. "Don't hurt me... I didn't do nothin'... I'm always the one who gets picked on..."

    "Well, Trey... I'm Peter... I won't hurt you..."

    "How did you get to be so big?" Trey wondered.

    "My friend, Allen, can make me grow. So can Joce. Would you like to meet them?"

    "S-s-sure.... I wish I could grow big like that..."

    "Let's walk over to Ty's house... We'll see what they can do for you!"

    Peter turned and walked out the door, holding the door open for Trey. Trey was only 15, but he looked smaller and younger than his age.

    The two slowly made their way to Ty's house, where Ty, Dustin, Allen and Joce were hanging out.

    In the meantime, Chad had gotten together with his friends from school. He told them all about Peter and Trey, and the thugs weren't happy...

    “I’ll have to deal with Peter myself” the gang leader growled. “We will rule the school and we will get protection money from these little freshman brats!”

    “But… I saw him hulk out right in front of me! I swear! He just started to GROW!” Chad said to Andrew Nelson, who was the gang leader. “I have never seen anyone grow like that!”

    “Hmm… I need to see that for myself!” Andrew Nelson growled back.
