    You found some interesting people that you would think would work well for your little elimination tournament. You had found seven people who lived fairly different lives. You easily was able to gather them into a little area in what would otherwise be in the middle of no where. Using tricks you could've gotten anyone to participate without them knowing what was going on. Then you would be able to record what would happen and then release the footage after everything was recorded. If everything was released after the events occurred then people would want to watch to see what had happened instead of trying to stop what was going on.
    The area where they would be interacting in was a small terraformed comet which was basically a mini earth and had basically only a large house and some facilities to maintain everything. Everyone was in the living room and chatting a little you said over a speaker "hello hello? Testing testing one two three testing. May I have all your attention?" Your voice was a little high pitch kind of like an anime mascot character
    A body builder man by the name of Sono said "You gonna tell us what we're going to be doing here? I got a very strict training regiment and can't afford too many days off!"
    You stated "Well I hope that you can do some training here, because you aren't going to be leaving any time soon... Let's just say your kind of prisoners here forced to play by my rules."
    A bit startled a 5 year old girl named Sara said "But... I've got Kindergarten and my family to go back to... my friend and everyone... they'll get worried..."
    A teenage son of a wealthy businessman named Mark said "They probably don't even think anything wrong is going on. My father was contacted and I came here for a great business opportunity. My father is the guy who runs the company who makes CO2 scrubbers and oxygen generation plants, or the company that is trusted to get all the oxygen to space colonies and space ships... If I'm here and my father was properly convinced I don't think anyone will think anything wrong is going on... Especially since we are on a secluded comet in the middle of no where. Let's just follow his rules so we don't become unneeded casualties."
    Sara started whimpering a bit then Sonia a female university student said "Can't you be a little more sensitive. She's just a little kid and you don't need to scare her at a time like this. Im just an ordinary student of little consequence so I doubt that we're just here to be high value prisoners for some ransom."
    You said "Hello Hello?!? I will explain what the rules are and what we're doing here. I will be recording your every movement and interaction here for my little social experiment. You all are very different and I want to see how you all will interact, and then change your relationships. Every so often I will ask you all to vote to remove one person from this experiment."
    Tristan a cop asked "So you took a bunch of civilians and a cop prisoner and ask them to kill each other off? I don't care how well you can convince people, but at some point people will find out what you've done and then you'll face the law."
    you chuckled a bit and then said "Woah Woah! who said anything about killing others? I just said they would be removed from the experiment... What exactly that means... I'm not going to say. They could be brought to a heavenly place as payment for being part of this experiment, they could be brought to some place of punishment, or I suppose if your boring the person who was removed could be killed. then again I just may have everyone removed put in some other comet until the experiment is over and then you'll be sent home."
    Mary a buisnesswoman in her mid thirties said "I'd hope we'd be compensated for this time. we are prisoners though so I doubt you'd just be giving us stuff besides the essentials to live."
    You said "Oh i'm gonna give you guys more than just the essentials. I'll also provide entertainment and other comforts, I want to see how relationships work so it'll be important to make sure that you are all in comfort."
    An old man by the name of Jeff chuckled a bit and said "Well at least I don't have to worry about my old bones breaking then. I just ask that you don't give the young ones too much stress... by the way kid I believe we haven't heard you say anything. Do you have anything to say?"
    Slave number 112, a somewhat small 10 year old kid, said "I'm just here because my ownership was changed... I don't get what you people are saying."
    You said "As you can see you all are from different walks of life. You have a body builder, a little girl, the son of a wealthy buisnessman, a model student, a police man, an entrepreneur, an old man, and a slave. I'll just leave you to your lives for the moment and try not to interfere. Really nothing is against the rules here so do what you want and you may notice a few changes every now and then. Now go live your lives here." Then you stopped your transmission leaving the group by themselves. What happened next then?
