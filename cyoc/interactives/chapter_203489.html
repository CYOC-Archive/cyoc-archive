    Carolyn has plans to hit the bars with her friends for a fun Friday night, so as soon as she gets out of work and picks up the skirt from Suzi's she goes straight to the gym. But by the time she gets home and finishes showering, she's already half an hour late to the bar where everyone was going to meet up. She dries herself off quickly and rushes through her makeup, and then she rushes to the closet to pick out an outfit. She takes out a nice top that goes well with the skirt she just got back, and to top it off, it's purple, like that cute bra she's planning on wearing tonight. She puts on the skirt in a hurry, then returns to her coffee table to pick up the bra she dropped there, still folded up over itself. She picks it up, and notices to her surprise a small gun that drops out. But it doesn't look like a handgun.

    *********

    After spending all day idling alone at Suzi's house, Carolyn's arrival is a breath of fresh air. She's also pretty cute: you wouldn't mind her wearing you for a while. You're still pretty surprised she hasn't noticed that you're wrapping up the transformation gun, but maybe she's just in a hurry. She never takes you out of her purse for the rest of the day, but you gather she takes you into some kind of office environment and then a gym, or at least some place where you hear a lot of lockers slamming. Just as you're starting to get bored, she opens up the door again. You hope this time she'll finally put you on, but no dice: she just grabs you, stuffs you in a gym bag, and drives home. What's she waiting for?

    She goes home and still doesn't take you out of the purse once she arrives, and your frustration continues to grow. You can hear a shower in the distance, and you hope that when she's done she'll come back for you. Sure enough, she does, but when she picks you up, the gun falls out. You can tell she didn't expect that to happen when she recoils the way anyone would if they found an unexpected handgun in their possession. Carolyn holds you in her left hand but pays you no attention as she stares at the gun. You watch her read the settings and do a double-take. It looks like she's putting two and two together.
