    So the doctor leads C0 through a corridor with giant tubes. Some of the tubes held the clones C0 just played with, others have new clones in them, each slightly different from C0. As C0 walks by one of the tubes, C0 sees a version of him that is completely white. C0 then asks the doctor,
    "How about this one?"

    "Sure, so you want C7, the albino?"

    "Yes, sir"

    The doctor presses a button and a claw from the ceiling lowered down and grabbed the tank with C7 in it. The claw whisks away and the doctor tells C0,
    "Come on, we don't want the machine to wait."

    C0 follows him to a huge machine, where C0 just sees the tube with C7 locking and connecting with the machine. C0 then turns his focus to an empty tube. The doctor then tells C0, "Please get in the tube."

    C0 nodes as he climbs in, hearing the loud clank and hiss as the top locked. Strangely, in the bluish transparent liquid, C0 could breath just fine. Then the tube shakes as he is lifted out and dropped into the other side of the huge machine. He hears hissing again as the tube establishes connection with the machine. The doctor shoots him a thumbs-up as he presses a great, red button. Suddenly C0 blacks out.

    When he wakes up again, he is not in liquid anymore. He looks around and sees the machine, except that in his tube was his own body, and the other tube housing C7 was empty. The doctor smiles at him as he looks down at his body. His skin was a blinding white, and some strands of white hair fell in front of his eyes. He looks further down and sees a slightly shriveled cock and just mountains and valleys of snow white color on his skin. C0, now C7, watches as the doctor hands him a mirror. When he looked in, his eyes were a color between blue and pink. His snow white hair was a little longer and his eyebrows seemed to match in color. The doctor then says,
    "So what modification do you want first?"
