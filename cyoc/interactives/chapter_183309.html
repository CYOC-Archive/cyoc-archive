    Teri needed to do something quickly. Mr. Forester had seen her with the phone and leapt from the bed, and the sentence in the app read:

    “The enraged man rushes towards the naked woman to get back the phone he wants.”

    Working her fingers she highlighted the word "man", trying to ignore the fact that Forester was now moving towards her. Just as his hands touched the phone she wrote in the most harmless thing she could think of -- and a tiny furry body ran into her shin, followed by a very angry yet very cute little meow. She looked down and could not help but smile at the results of replacing "man" with "kitten".

    Mr. Forester was now an adorable feline, mostly covered in thick white fur with the exception of some large splotches of black. He lifted one tiny paw in front of his big green eyes in amazement and then looked up at Teri. The amazement in the kitten's eyes shifted suddenly. Sharp little claws popped from the paw and Mr. Forester the kitten slashed at Teri's leg.

    "Ouch!" she yelped and hopped back from the cute yet vicious attack. Three little scratches were now bleeding on her shin. "Kittens are supposed to be cuddly not mean!" she said as she lifted the phone again to get a new sentence.

    This kitten hates the transformation and wants to be a man again.

    Teri realized that Mr. Forester must have been touching the phone and so remembers being a man. What to do with a very angry little ball of fur?
