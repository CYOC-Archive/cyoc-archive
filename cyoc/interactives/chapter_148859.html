    Ca11-3

    added by Anon 4 years ago BM Mental Robot

    "Repeat after me. I am a robot. I am programmed to obey any and all orders that my master, Lydia, gives to me. I have no will of my own. My master's will is my will. Obeying my master is logical. I carry out all of her orders immediately without questioning her. I cannot harm or kill my master. I am skilled at almost anything. I have multiple technological features, including a ray gun that turns whoever it shoots into a robot who obeys my master like me. Whenever my master says I have a new feature, I immediately obtain it. I do not need to eat, drink, use the bathroom, or do any human thing unless my master says I do. I do not obey anyone but my master unless my master says I do. I never rebel against my master or her wishes. To rebel or disobey her is illogical. I do not remember my life as a human. I do not remember ever being a human. I do not know about the Wonder Watch or the Ring of Three."

    The second the repetition was finished, Cally's mind shut down. While she was offline, her skin smoothed down, becoming flawless and metallic. Antennae sprouted from the top of her head. Her eyes faded into clear lightbulbs as her mouth became neutral in expression. On one eye appeared an eyepiece with a blue band going around her now metallic head.

    Meanwhile her brain was being replaced with a computer hard drive. Instructions were uploaded into her hard drive as well as certain technological features. Once that was finished, she began rebooting and she awoke. Her eyes fell on Lydia and her hard drive analyzed her with ease.

    "Master identified," Ca11-3 droned as she stood at attention.
    "How may I serve you, master?"
