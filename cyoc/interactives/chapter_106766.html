    What a mess...

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 7 years ago O

    Well here you are, standing naked right behind a skunk woman who wants to turn you into a monstrosity like her. You reflect on this and just imagine how unbelievable this would sound if you managed to escape this nightmare and told someone your story. You just thinking about this whole situation frustrates the hell out of you. This stupid bitch thinks that she can have her way with you. Oh what a grim mistake she made ever crossing paths with you. Out of nowhere though she threatens "Hey there hothead you might wanna cool down or I'm gonna spray the hell outta you." You must have been visibly showing your anger to a point where she noticed. "My bad," you say hiding your anger "it just sucks that I won't be able to be myself anymore." And then she says "Sure does!" That's it, you've had enough of this. Using your raw strength you
    kick her ass making her fall forward. "Oh your-" is all she can say before you stomp on her back, immobilizing her in the process. You bend over beside you while your foot rests on her back and pick up a good sized rock. She absolutely looks horrified at the sight of you picking up that rock. She gasps and tries to muster up anything before you bury that rock in her head. "W-wai-" She catches you off guard before she finishes her sentence and wiggles free from your foot. You stumble back a bit and she starting getting up and begins to raise her tail for the purpose of spraying you. You act quick and with a swift, yet, powerful swing, the rock comes into impact with her head. A bloodcurdling scream emits from her. She falls back down and you stop on her back once more. Then you unleash a barrage of swift and strong swings at her head. Each impact comes with a scream quieter than the last and more of her blood everywhere. After a good 15 or so swings she is motionless. There is her blood spread allover the cave, adding to the dampness of the cave. You can hardly even tell what was inbetween her shoulders anymore. It just looked like a pile of red mush now.
