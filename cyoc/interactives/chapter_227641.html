    Things are looking bad for Pleasure Island and the people who run it. For decades they have been able to make lots of money by turning kids into animals and selling them back to the population as animal demands were high. Their most popular were Donkeys but they were not averse to turning kids into other animals and selling those other animals too. Other animals sometimes included cats, dogs, canaries, parrots, cows, horses, goats, sheep, squirrels, pigs, chickens, ducks, geese, or any other animals that could be raised as pets, livestock, or even food. However recently all demands for real world animals have come to a halt and now Pleasure Island's livelihood is in danger.

    Luckily for them, since it is 2024, the year of the dragon, dragons have decided to come to Pleasure Island to give the owners of the island a deal. The dragons want brides of their own, dragonesses so to say. They don't care if the original gender of the new dragonesses were boys or girls, as long as they become dragonesses and spend eternity with the dragons, they will be happy. The dragons give the pleasure island staff a small amount of their fortune, gold, silver, platinum, jewels, treasure etc. to make it reality. With this visit and starting capital the Pleasure Island staff have an epiphany, they will turn boys and girls into dragonesses and sell them to the dragons. This is the only way for Pleasure Island to avoid bankruptcy and turn around their net losses back into net profits.

    The Pleasure Island people renovate the island so that one half of it is a Europeon setting, and the other half of it is an Asian setting. This will appeal to both sides of the world that have dragons.

    You and and a bunch of other kids are the first boat to arrive at Pleasure Island, the brochure said that this time it was about dragons, you always loved dragons ever since you were 3, you are 10 now. There are so many choices as to where to go and what to do, what will you do when you get there?
