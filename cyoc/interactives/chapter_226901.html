    The Cutest Find For A Fay

    added by Elflover A month ago AR Mythological Magic Kid

    You then think if you want to use the fountain of age or stay as a kid longer, so you decide to see if you can find the Fountain Of Age. Being a kid sounds scary, and very much so, right now. Everything seems threatening and about to attack you all of a sudden and at every turn. Besides, you're still in your underoos! You need to get to the Fountain Of Age and fast! According to the map you can get a shortcut through the woods. So you get walking through these woods, even though you are still in your underoos. You hope you won't meet anyone bad and the lights in the distance are there, if remote. You feel so scared, in fact, that you know you'd scream in fright at the first thing to startle you and you keep reflecting that having a mommy with you would be very, very nice indeed. The sooner you reach that Fountain Of Age, the better. You won’t feel so exposed and vulnerable then.
    However, after a while, you are forced to admit that these woods, incredibly, appear to go on and on. How big can they be? And there's a strange fog, located just above the ground too. You walk and walk but it is like you are not advancing a step. It is incredibly hard and long, upon your little legs of a four or five year old. Then you get so exhausting that you lie down against a tree trunk, thinking about just a rest. Alas, after closing your eyes you fall asleep for good, soundly so. You curl upon yourself and even unconsciously slip your thumb into your mouth. The fog got you good. Its sedative effects cannot be resisted, just as the forest's magic engulfed you without you being able to doing anything about it. The forest is magical and has swallowed you into its folds, thereby transporting you into anoother world. A world of mythological creatures, as well as fay beings.

    But you don't stay alone for long. Soon, a creature come upon you. The creature smiles, elated at its luck. It is a fay creature and fay creatures just adore human children. They make the perfect offspring and little pets to raise, pamper and love. Fay creatures reproduce with great difficulty and humans are very, very malleable to transformations. They can be transformed into the perfect child and coddled accordingly. So for the fay creature who picks you up gently, not even waking you, you are about to be part of a new family and make some fay parents very, very happy.
