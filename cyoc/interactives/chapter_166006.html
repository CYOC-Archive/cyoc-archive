I guess I have to install any other program needed myself.” Before Theo could say anything, Sam ranted again: “Okay, why is there not enough space left to install any other program? Who would write so much nonsense in a program of a toy, when it blocks any additions? *Sigh* I will need to overwrite the other program then, if that is at least possible.”

    Sam answered with a frown: “So you can´t just overwrite the program, great. That leaves only one option. Sorry little teddy, but the only option is to delete your old programming. Don´t worry, you won´t be damaged, and with the other programs you will be better of then with that crap that is there right now. To much useless data that can´t be taken out.”
    So Sam started the process of deleting the “Theo.exe” out of the teddy. Theo himself was frozen just as he wanted to plead more, and couldn´t do anything to stop the app from erasing him. He screamed mentally as he could see everything inside his mind before it vanished into nothing, be it knowledge, memories, feelings or anything else.
    Sam hadn´t known that the “Theo.exe” was linked to the memory chip in the bear, but as he couldn´t stop the process anymore he shrugged it off. Either whoever had programmed the teddy had a backup ready, or it was just lost then. He would only make sure nobody could track the changes back to him.
    Soon Theo ceased to exist in the now blank teddies mind. His soul, however, remained trapped in the teddy, still with everything that was deleted from the body connected. But now he was a mere watcher of what happened to his body.
    Sam was happy to notice the deleting was finished, and quickly started to install the programs he wanted or would be needed later. To be save he installed the simple “Children_Toy.exe” first. With it came everything the teddy would need in this setting, behaviour to humans, vocabulary fit for children and so on.
    After that, Sam installed a second program, which he had wanted to begin with, the “Sex_Toy.exe”. It was by far smaller then the child one, as the bear wouldn´t need much speech as a sextoy, therefore no new vocabulary was installed. As an inanimate teddy robot he had no real feelings that got installed either. Most what the app installed for this program were options how the pleasure his owner and such.
    Theo was disgusted when he saw what exactly was installed, but could do nothing as Sam activated the “Sex_Toy.exe” and used his bear body as a masturbation aid, letting the bear work up his cock for over ten minutes before shoving it up the bears rear to get his release. The options Theo´s father had used made the insides clean itself, therefore Sam wouldn´t need to clean it himself. He just needed to clean a bit on the outside before he could switch him to the “Children_Toy.exe” and let him play with the kids.
    Theo was by now not only disgusted, but horrified by how he was used, but still couldn´t do anything. Soon the teddy was happily playing with the kids, just as he was programmed to. Nobody noticed his changed behaviour, and as Theo wouldn´t live at his home for some time, nobody noticed. He either remained in the facility or was checked in the company. And as nobody beside his father know what the teddy was, all they said was he was working fine.
    Theo´s mind, trapped with his soul inside the teddy, was going crazy, as Sam often used his as his sextoy, and the kids weren´t that good to him, either. More then once they had nearly ripped an arm or leg out when they fought over him, and while Theo wasn´t able to control his body, his soul still felt the pain.
