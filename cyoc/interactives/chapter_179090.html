    Chronivac Version 4.0

    The New Programm For Production Has Arrived

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 4 years ago O

    Mr. Johnson, the 40 year old CEO of BMW, is sitting in his office when there is a knock on his door, "Come in," he says. The door opens and his secratery enter.

    "Sir, this package has just arrived," she says.

    Mr. Johnson's eyes sparkle, "I hope it is the new advanced program I have ordered," he takes the box from his secratery and opens it. "Chronivac 4.0," he reads the title on the box containing the DVD, "I have a different name of the program in my mind." The man shrugs and opens the box, "maybe they changed the title at the last second and it slipped by me," he says as he slides the DVD into the main computer of the factory.

    "What is the new program capable of, sir," the woman asks.

    The CEO looks at her, "well mrs. Davis," he says, "this program will turn this manufactory into a fully automatic compound, in other words: we won't be needing human workers anymore! The program will be linked to all the robots, security system, scanners and so on. This way we are able to build cars and motorcycles in a high speed."

    "That sounds amazing sir," mrs. Davis says.

    Mr. Johnson starts the upload of the Crinivac to the main system of the facility, "I was thinking to organize a tour of the very first full automatic car manufactory," he says, "could you spread the word to the press?"

    "Sure, when would you like the tour to happen?"

    "This coming Saturday," he replies, "start is 8am."

    The secratery nods and leaves the office to notify the press.

    Two days later a huge crowd arrive to check out the most advanced manufactory in the world, under the crowd is the 16 year old and car-fanatic "Jaden Smith". The teen was one of the first to arrive at the manufactory early in the morning, "this is gonna be such a awsome day," he says excitedly to himself, "I always wanted to check out a car manufactory and see how cars a being built."

    The doors of the main entrance open and the secratery comes out and starts to speak into a microphone, "welcome to BMW, the worlds most advanced car manufacturer," she says, her voice blaring out of hidden speakers. "We have not counted with so many people, we are overwhelmed and thank you for coming," she continues, "now, before we proceed, here are some rules: 1. stay together, 2. no "solo-adventure" and 3. no touching of the cars, car parts and machinery. Now follow me."

    The group follow the woman into the building, she leads them through a huge lobby towards a thick metal door, she stops and turns to the group. "Behind this door is where the "magic" happens," she says jokingly, "please remember the rules and stay together, I don't want any of you getting lost," with that she opens the doors with her key-card and they walk through.

    Jaden finds himself in a huge room, his eyes widen as he watches thousands of robots working on cars, some robots carrying car parts, others welding them together. There were robots polishing cars on one side and motorcycles on the other side, there were conveyor belts everywhere, like a huge roller coaster. "This is awesome," he breaths in amazement, not paying attention to what is happening, he tries to follow every word of mrs. Davis, but the noises are pretty loud and the more he watches the robots works the more his focus is stuck to the production.

    The teenager starts to walk away from the group, no one notices, to get a better look. He does not notice the group starting to exit the room through a other door, only when there is a loud "hiss" from the doors is when he is snapped back to reality. Shocked, he realizes he is all alone in the production room, he runs towards the metallic doors and starts banging on them, "HEY, SOMEBODY GET ME OUT OF HERE," he calls, but the metal doors are so thick that he can not be heard.

    The teen does not notice a drone flying towards him, stopping behind him and starting to scan him. Suddenly there is a alarm, which causes Jaden to turn around and find himself face to face with the drone. "Um... h-hi," Jaden says awkwardly, "c-could you may-maybe let me out?" As he starts to back away he is suddenly grabbed by a huge robot, "HEY... L-LET ME GO," he shouts and starts to struggle, trying to break free. The boy is unaware that the drone mistook him for a "car-part" which "somehow" left the production line. Where will the robot take him? What is going to happen to the poor boy?
