    "...you will switch personalities with the person you lied to. So for instance, if you lie to the school principal, you'll gain her stern demeanor, dated fashion sense, and conservative world view. She in turn would gain your sense of elitism and entitlement, your vanity, your oversexed behavior and fashion sense, and so forth. No one except you and me will notice anything different. Now I dare you to hang up the phone and forget this conversation."

    Jen hung up the phone, blinked for a second.

    "That's odd, I must have just checked out for a second there."

    She then shrugged it off and continued getting ready for school.
