    "Thank you young man. Your very kind." Said the old women. "Your welcome." Damon was about to leave.

    "Wait I want to thank you. Why don't you sit down for a bit while I make something to drink."

    He didn't want to be rude. So he takes the old womens kind offer as he sat down. The old women came back with drinks.

    Damon drank his. "You know. You kind of remind me of someone. Someone I use to know a long time." Said the old women.

    "Oh really?" Said Damon. "A young prince who I help a long time ago. Telling me he no longer wanted to be a prince. I believe his name was Prince Damon."

    Damon almost spat out his drink. Prince Damon was his ancestor. "You knew about my ancestor?" He ask.

    "Ah so your his decendent then. Yes I knew your ancestor. I gave him a potion and he became an animal. I don't remember which one though." Said the old women.

    "But how can that be? My ancestor is human." Said Damon. "I made a clone of him. So his family wouldn't worry."

    Damon realize she was a witch. He looks at his drink. Wondering if she did anything to it.

    The witch look at him. "Oh relax boy. I did nothing to your drink. I put no potion, or spell, on it."

    Damon just sigh with relief. The witch goes into another room and brought out some potions.

    "But if you like. I can change you if you want. These potions are the same potion I gave to your ancestor."

    Damon wonders how old the witch is. "So what do these potions do anyway?" He ask her. The witch smile.

    "So you are interested. One potion change you into an animal. The second potion makes you older. The third makes you younger. The fourth potion makes you taller. The 5th potion makes you smaller. The 6 and last potion changes you into a mythological creature. So which one you like?"
