    As the door begins to open, you notice Selena coming in wearing a black bikini top, and a towel around her waist. Selena was even wearing a sun hat over her head to where two holes on opposite sides of the hat showed her horse ears. As Selena entered the room you were in, she notices you on the mattress looking through your middle school album.

    "Maddie, everyone is going to the creek, this afternoon," Selena says. "You shouldn't be the only one alone here." Selena then here's you crying, and sees tears running from down your face. "Why are you crying?" Selena asks. "Did someone upset you? It wasn't Rodrick of all people, was it?" You then drop the photo album in your hands, and picks it up. "Are these pictures of your old life?" Selena asks you. All you do to respond is shake your head up, and down to respond in a yes motion.

    "Aw, you looked like such a cute boy back then," Selena says. "Though from these pictures, I see no one got along with you back then. Hey, who's this?" Selena then spots a photo of the girl you had a crush on, and you tilted back down to cry again. Selena then reads the word above your crush's photo, labeled 'school crush', and her eyes open. "Did you have a crush on this girl?" Selena asks. You shake your head up, and down to say yes, and your face turns red. "Well what happened to her?" Selena asks. The only words you had for that answer were "move" and "end". You then continue to cry, while Selena puts together your words in her head.

    "Oh, she moved away from your school at the end of last year," Selena says. "My, that must be why your parents sent you here." All you could do while sitting there, was crying on the mattress, remembering those tragic scenes, when Selena hugs you. Not only that, but she kisses you on the lips to get you on the cheek to stop crying. "Not to fret, my little sister," Selena says.
