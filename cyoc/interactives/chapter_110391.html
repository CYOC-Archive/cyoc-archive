Then we both thought the two species couldn't mix. And now she's pregnant. And I know what you're gonna say. Yes it's mine. She was a virgin when I met her. So was I. But now we're going to be parents. And at least with this ring, we'll be ready for anything. And I really do love her, Jake. With all my heart."

"Well it looks like you got everything figured out, Chris. You sound like a grown up." "Well I am. Now. But don't worry about it. For now I want to hang out with my good friend Jake. But I need to keep an ear out for Atla in case she needs me. Is that okay?" "Sure thing, Daddy-O. Let's eat."

Superstrength means a super appetite." I wish for all the food to heat up/cool down and we dig in. The Dynamic Duo are back in business.
