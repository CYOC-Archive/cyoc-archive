    One second there stood Milo in his old clothes, pressing his lips to the soap that was Chad. The next second there stood Chad naked, and a bar of soap dropped down to the floor, on top of Milo´s clothes. It took some seconds for Chad to realize he was human again.
    “Yes, I´m back. Thanks Milo” Chad said loudly, only to realize that Milo wasn´t there. Looking around in confusion, his gaze fell on the bar of soap on the ground near his feet. Slightly panicked, Chad picked the soap up. “Milo?”
    “Chad, what happened to me?” Milo thought in panic to him. Chad´s thought answer made Milo cry in horror. But Chad quickly saw why Milo was so horrified by his new status. “Don´t worry, we will find a way to change you back, I won´t leave you this way.” That calmed Milo down. He had feared that Chad would leave him behind, now that he was human again. “You helped me, and I will help you. The only question is how?” Chad thought about it. As Milo suggested that Chad could also kiss him, Chad refused. “If we just switch back, it won´t help us on the long run.”
    After some brainstorming, mainly from Milo´s side, they decided that Chad would try to find either Keith or Chris, and then force them to change Milo back or Chad would press the soap on their lips. That should switch them around like it did for Chad and Milo, therefore leaving either Keith or Chris as the soap and Milo human again.
    Chad picked up Milo´s clothes and the cellophane Chad had been wrapped in, and left to find his own locker. There he could get clothes for himself and his bag to put Milo´s in. Afterwards they moved out of the school. Milo had known where Keith lived, and so Chad walked there.
    Luckily for Chad and Milo one of the windows was open, so Chad quickly climbed in. As it was still deep in the night, everyone was asleep. Finding Keith´s room Chad pushed the slightly ajar door open and moved in. Quickly deciding that waking up Keith wasn´t a good idea, Chad just pressed the soap on Keith´s open mouth.
    The sudden touch woke Keith up, but before he could even react, the switch happened. While Keith was now a bar of soap and still to groggy to notice his fate, Milo was human again, if naked and on top of Keith´s bed, lying on the soap. Keith´s blanket and pajamas fell flat under Milo.
    “It worked, Milo.” Chad whispered, and Milo looked at him, satisfied to be human again. Then he noticed he was naked now and got red. He took Chad´s bag and quickly redressed into his clothes. “What now?” Milo asked yawning. “We go over to mine. There you can sleep, and tomorrow we will talk to my parents.”
    Before leaving, Chad collected the soap from the bed. Keith was by now fully awake and in panic. How had Chad escaped? And what happen to him? But Chad put him in the bag before leaving, not in the mood to think about Keith right now. Soon he and Milo were at Chad´s, and both were sound asleep while Keith was trapped in awareness in the darkness of the bag.
