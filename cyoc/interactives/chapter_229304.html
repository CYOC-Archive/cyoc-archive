    "Have you thought about sharing your life with someone else in a deep, peculiar but exciting bond? We have an idea for you! We present you The Merging Program!" A new announcement was made.

    Due the massive success of ANIMALIA INC's invention, the company eventually developed and released a new project in no time. This one coming out to the public too, being called "The Merging Program."

    Like their previous and popular animal transformation option, customers could choose this alternative in the company's transformation centers. As the name obviously suggests, the volunteers to this change could fuse to turn into a single being of their choice, with multiple possibilities too, like feral animals, anthropomorphic animals, mythical creatures and a large variety more, kinda the same as the individual transformation, but now with more than one people sharing their bodies to turn into the thing they desire. Furthermore, they would able to choose the result of their merging and how they are merged, a two headed creature? A person's body working as a tail? Another one being the lower half of a centaur? All covered!

    Similar to the animal transformation, this invention also had a couple of rules, applying the previous 13 too.
    1- All the subjects in the merging must be willing to do it and know what they are getting into.
    2- The result of the transformation must be at least 40% part animal, the fusion can't result into a being with little to no animal features.
    3- The merging is completely permanent and the subjects can't be separated, but can participate in another merging again if they want.
    4- The resulting merge must have at least 1 head.
    5- The subjects in the resulting merge that don't have at least a head aren't considered people or animals anymore but just a body part. E.g. if two people turn into a centaur, one being the upper half and the other the lower half, the one being the lower half should be treated as just a body part as they no longer can talk or interact with anyone else. But if two people merge into a single anthro animal with their two respective heads, each head can be treated as they normally do.
    6- Also for the subjects of the resulting merge that doesn't have at least a head a.k.a. body parts, they could choose to see through the head(s) of the body at any time they wanted, they could also feel and hear in their respective part but can't talk or eat since they don't need it, as the head(s) of the body is/are in charge of eating.
    7- The subjects can choose the gender of their resulting fusion, being able to choose to have one penis, one vagina or be hermaphrodite having both of them, having to choose which animal reproductive organ would be as it can't be human ones. They can select to have more than one penis and/or vagina as long as they are all from different animals (e.g. a dog penis and a lizard penis, but not two dog penises or two reptile ones).
    8- Before the merge, the subjects must choose how they divide the control of the body. E.g. if one controls just the arms, other the legs or one the tail. They can also choose if anyone can control any part or if some of them is limited to control a few or none of the body parts. This can't be changed after the merge is done.
    9- The subjects can choose the age of the resulting merge as long as it is in the range of their ages. E.g if a 19 years old merges with a 32 years old, the result can't be younger than 19 or older than 32.
    10- If one of the subjects is UNDER the 18 years of age and participates in a merge where the result is 18 or older, the subject is legally considered an adult.
    11- If one of the subjects is OVER the 18 years of age and participates in a merge where the result is younger than 18, the subject is legally considered a minor.
    12- If two or more subjects want to participate in the merging but all of them are under the 18 years of age, they must have a parent or guardian permission to do it. Or merging with someone 18 or older respectively.
