    The monster girl enchantress

    added 2 months ago BM O Mythological

    Amelia Dragonia was a young witch in her early twenties studying of a secret magic academy, she got to be part of it due her talent with magic and amazing abilities even without mastering her abilities. Despite everything she was known as a real troublemaker, annoying other witches, stealing spells or causing a little bit of problems to her tutors. She secretly practiced her own magic to reach her full potential and make everyone obey her, going with the alias of "The great enchantress" which not everyone took seriously.

    As days passed the leaders of the academy started to notice the evil potential of Amanda, so they decided to have a private conversation with her.

    After the talk they concluded for Amanda to leave the academy, she was against this proposal so they eventually had to cast spells to stop her, she tried to escape managing to steal a map that caught her attention, she left the academy successfully without a trace, the leaders would try to find her but didn't see it as a problem, for now...

    Amanda followed the map showing a giant temple at the end, called "The seal of the monster girl cube", she read a little about it, a mysterious cube capable to transform the user into any monster girl, with the risk of the cube taking control of themselves.

    Amanda encountered the temple, easily avoiding traps that wore out, then was when she found the chamber, a small room with the cube in the center, bound with some magical chains that she easily erased due the seal losing its power after many years, she had the cube in her hands.

    A strange aura started to surround her, unreadable glowing letters appearing with strange symbols, the cube started to float in front of her, it looked like a plain and boring metal cube but now was filled with glowing purple symbols of monsters and landed on her hands again.

    Her hands started to glow with the same purple aura, she felt the power of the artifact flowing through all her body, trying to take control of her mind... But Amara was stronger taking charge over the cube, now the cube obeyed her. She laughed wickedly, her plan of becoming a powerful enchantress was starting ready to use the cube.

    What monster does Amara become?
