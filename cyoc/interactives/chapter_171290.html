    John had a second thought about where they would do business as the mall was dying. What if they took a truck around and made food like a food truck?
    Stew was fine with making a food truck and after they figured out all the permits and everything they had opened their Food Truck with a menu including... basically anything you could name for $10. Stew would handle the finances and John would be the chief in charge of obtaining and cooking the ingredients. Then John figured they'd put on the menu "$20 to add any health boost, Immediate results or your money back."
    As they opened up at a park a nearby jogger jogged by and asked "What do you mean by immediate, and can I ask for anything?"
    Stew said "As long as it's food. We probably can't make it just like your mother can, but we should be able to make basically anything you want. Also by immediate we mean near instantly. If you don't get your results we'll give you back the $20."
    The jogger thought for a second and said "Can you get me a stack of ribs and a health boost to enhance my muscles?"
    Stew heard the grill sizzling and said "We're going to try to. That'll be $30" Cash was exchanged and then a few minutes later the ribs came out.
    The jogger was suspicious about ribs that only took a few minutes to make, but they were hot and glistening with juices and BBQ sauce. There was a decent amount of them as well. After eating a few rib sections he wiped his hands and noticed that his arms were significantly bulkier than before. Giving a squeeze he felt that they were pretty hard as if made primarily of muscle. Looking around his muscles were growing, and yeah he had to admit those were some pretty immediate results. Considering he'd happily pay $30 for ribs of these quality he was getting a great deal and continued to chow down, enjoy his meal, and wonder where the rest of his ribs went when he devoured them all and got lost in time. J&S TFFT was in business.
    Meanwhile some passers by noticed the food truck, a man whose sweatshirt and pants were tight with muscle chowing down on some ribs, and put 2 and 2 together.
