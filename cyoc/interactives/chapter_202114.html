    A Family on Morph Island!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago O

    You step out of the boat, unsure of what to think about this.
    You came here as vacation with your small family- your husband and two kids, Jamie and Linda.

    Ever since those two saw the advert on TV, they wouldn't stop asking about going to the Island. They even got your husband in on it.

    Now, six and a half months of saving money and allowance accumulation later, here you are.

    You grab a brochure and open it, scanning its contents.

    Jamie was hopping in place next to you. "Mom, can we see?" He asked you. You show it to your family. Inside is a huge map of the island. The map was well drawn, depicting all sorts of animals. You assume from the adverts this was all some kind of large zoo, but clearly that wasn't the case

    You noticed on the side there was a short description.

    It reads,
    'Welcome! In order to transform, all you do is go to the place that hosts your animal and ask a member of our staff to give you a serum. Happy TFing!'

    Transform? Your kids have a tendency to pretend to be dragons and whatnot, so you assume that this might be the case here?

    You look up and notice your kids are zoning out. Maybe it's best not to dwell on it now... "Hey, let's decide where to go!" You said. You look at the map. There's:

    Changing Forest
    Transformation City
    Morph Farm
    Reform Mountains
    Shapeshift Poles
    Morpher's Desert
    Transformer Marine Life
    Shifting Jungle
    Animorph Aviary
    Etomorphology
    and Converting Mythologic Center.

    You ask your kids which one they want. After all, it was their-and their father's-idea to come here, after all.

    They choose...

    What do you do now?
