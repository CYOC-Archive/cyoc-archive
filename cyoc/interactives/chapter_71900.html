    "Actually," Began Jeff, "Since this thing actually works, I want to try something different."

    "Certainly, what would you like?" Melanie inquired from her desk.

    "I don't know how to say this..." Suddenly Melanie interrupted Jeff.

    "You want to be a girl? There is no shame in that, it can easily be done."

    Jeff's face burned bright red, and Melanie assured him that most men at least try being female for a while when they use the Cronivac. They both decided to save the transformation until after he was fully human.

    Jeff showered, spending a lot of time cleaning himself, both because of the nature of his mess, and being unfamiliar with hooves. After the shower, Jeff entered another part of the lab, and was led to a surgery table. Unfortunately they were out of pain killers, so the surgery would be quite painful.

    "We can do this one of two ways, the quick and messy way, or the slow and steady way." I can either get your Cronivac profile set up before hand to change your limb, and amputate in one quick slice. this will be quite painful but the pain will last only for a few seconds, per limb."

    Or, I can remove the limbs slowly, which will be less painful but will hurt longer. this way i can prevent you going into shock."

    Jeff chose the quick option, but informed her he may change his mind if it hurts to much. He then bit down on a piece of rubber and prepared for the first amputation. The pain was intense! Jeff screamed into the piece of rubber and soon it was gone, he looked down at his right arm, where his hoof used to be and saw a human arm finish developing, his right arm was back to the way it used to be!

    the process continued with a break between each limb, and after an hour and a half, Jeff was restored.

    Jeff thanked Melanie, and she reminded him of his earlier request. "Are you ready to cross the gender gap?" She asked him. Jeff told her he was ready as he ever would be. Melanie moved to the Cronivac and changed the gender tab to female, then hit enter. As Jeff changed Melanie told him the only thing differnt would be his gender, and he could alter himself further with the new cronivac he would be given. Jeff, Now Jenifer, looked over herself and it sunk in, he was now a she!
