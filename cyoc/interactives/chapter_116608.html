    This is No Fairy Tale Story

    From time to time you went outside to judge time based off of the moon, and about halfway into the night you caught the scent of blood, different than the deer blood. You then heard a distant male voice, and it said,"So this is the hut of that witch hag."

    Your senses immediately were in high gear, so you waited in the shadows until the man was at the entrance. The man resembled a knight, or wandering warrior in full armor with shield and sword. You rushed at the man picking him up using your hand, and gripping him with your claws. You slammed the man into a tree, and he appeared to be caught off-guard, and dropped his shield. The man swung his sword at your arm, but you grabbed his arm and you heard a crackling noise and the man dropped his sword. "You seek the death of my mistress, so I seek to feast upon your bones human." You growled

    "Wait, you talk? Your a familiar, her familiar! When I get the chance I will come back with more you beast!" The man yelled

    "Well that hardly matters now you know where we are, so you must die. What is your name?" You asked

    "Wh...what?" The man asked

    "As it is true that I am a beast, I must serve my mistress, and kill whoever approaches. I am not a complete monster you see, humanity still lingers wherever it does within me. But my taste is for blood, and I yearn for the taste of yours, and while I may feast on your bones such as your femur, I will bury the rest, and enscribe your name inside my den for memoriam. It's either you tell me, and die and be remembered, or die and be forgotten." You said smiling

    "C..Curtus..D..David..." the man said cowardly

    "Have some god damn pride in your death, werewolves can't die so easily. Farewell Curtus David!" You said before plunging your claw through the warrior's neck, and decapitated him by pulling it off of his body.

    "My first human victim, should I really eat this one." You thought to yourself, but your instincts kicked in, and your stomach growled.

    "Okay me, you made your damn point." You said before tearing apart the armor, and layed the carcass on the bridge to the house before tearing into it. You devoured everything from Curtus, but his head which you buried with the bones and the armor you didn't want. By the time you were done dawn had come, and Galga walked down the stairs to see you on duty.

    "Good morning, Mistress I hope that I didn't cause too much noise." You said chewing on one of Curtus's femurs.

    "I assume someone came by considering the blood on your mouth." Galga said

    "Yes, a single man who claimed to be on a quest to kill you. I buried what bones of his I didn't want, and I'm going to scratch out his name in my cave in small letters. I think that I will be doing this quite often in the future, so I may not bury everyone that I kill, but he was my first. I won't describe his blood's taste, but there's a lot of it still on the bridge." You said

    "Then I have more human blood to use, you may do what you wish until dusk." Galga said and you nodded.

    "I'll just take the femurs and leave then, perhaps I should build a nest in my cave. Not of bones, obviously." You said, and left the hut.

    You worked your way to your cave, but then your bladder stopped you, so you pissed on a tree like a dog would, and took a shit right beside it. "Geez, I wonder if all that meat, and blood is going to affect my digestive area?" You thought as you took one of the largest shits you think you ever could. You looked at your shit, and the only thing you could think of was that you just shat out the deer you ate.

    You then proceeded to your cave, and scratched out Curtus's name on the wall. "You were the first of many probably, I hope that there's no hard feelings...sorry." You thought and yawned, tired from the nighttime guarding. What now?
