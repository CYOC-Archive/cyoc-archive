    Ty's Power

    Shapeshifting into a mythical beast

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago O

    Ty suddenly gets the idea on what to change into. A mythical beast! But what kind would he want to be, as there were so many. You got the choices of werewolves or minotaurs, orcs or goblins, harpies and mermaids. There was so many to choose from, but which one would he want to be? He wasn't big on fantasy creatures, so he decided to think about it some more.

    He thought about it for a while, trying to figure it out. If he was going to turn into a mythical creature, he would have to alter the world on how people will react to seeing him in his new form. Though I guess that depends on if Ty wanted negative reactions when taking a certain form. He decided that, he would find the best choice for him.
    His thoughts kept going back to the fact that he wanted to be either a dragon or a griffin. With his new abilities, he wouldn't have to worry about dying, or being killed by anyone, so he could kill someone if he saw fit. Finally, after hours of contemplating, Ty figured out what he wanted to be...
