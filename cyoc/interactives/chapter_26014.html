Her and Mary had come here a couple of times when they were younger, and Mary was now the care taker of the place. Mary then got off of Jess and openned the gate so Jess could trot in.

Next she tried a few jumps, as there were a few boxes and things lying about. She bounded over all of them with ease, even with the weight of her body.

    However, after sometime Jess slowed down a bit, and heard a noise comming from behind the barn of the corral, so she went to investigate. Jess went over, and using her muzzel, unlatched the door to the barn, and walked to the back. Once she reached the back, she came across a large, black stallion. She was amazed by his size and how awesome he looked in the sun light. It was also about this time that the stallion noticed Jess, and came over to her to investigate this new mare before him.

    Jess decided to try and play it cool. "I'm completely horse in body, so the stallion shouldn't have any problems with me", Jess thought,"infact, I might be able to see how horses interact with each other when people aren't around." And as she was thinking, the stallion came over and began to sniff around her. He was looking all about her, and finally started to nip playfully at her. Jess, not know much about how horses socialize, decided to be playful back, and nipped a little too.

    Meanwhile, inside the farm house Mary was reading messages left by the care-taker she had hired to watch the place on the weekdays. She was almost done when she picked up the last one, which read,"Hey Mary, I tried to call you yesterday but you weren't answering so I decided to leave you this message. That farmer from down the street brought his stallion by and left him in the back of our barn. It appears that his gate was broken by the storm on Tuesday, and he needed a place to keep him. I told him you would have no problems with this, and he's agreed to pay you a storage fee for the time that we keep his horse here. I will tell you more later- Jake."

    "So we got to take care of a stallion now, great", Mary thought,"well I had better see how Jess is doing." And with that Mary began collecting the mail so she could head back out to the corral. However, in the back of the barn Jess was starting to have her own problems with the stallion.

She tried to nuzzel with him, and she tried just walking away, but the stallion just wouldn't quit. Finally she decided to go to the back gate and neigh for Mary to come and help her out. However, when she got to the gate, she felt the stallion try to jump up onto her. When he did this, he startled Jess, and she tried to run, only to find he had her trapped between the gate and his body. Now with no where to run Jess felt him mount her again, and this time he stayed on. Jess, started to put things together, and realized that he was tying to mate with her. "Sure",Jess thought,"I am fully horse in body, but I don't want to be mated." And with that she tried to neigh her loudest to get Mary's help.
