    After a day Diana Holden and Latrisha Fernandez had already regrown into five year olds, they were both obedient and well behaved for girls of their age. They both wore matching pinafore dresses with collared shirts and wore their hair in pigtails.

    Felicity dropped off the two at Dr. Eastly’s lab to continue the aging process.

    “The boss has you babysitting again?” The purple haired scientist asked.

    “Not exactly babysitting when they’re so good and do nothing but study.” Felicity huffed annoyed at watching the five year olds.

    “What did you expect from the Boss’s daughters?” Dr. Eastly replied.

    “Adopted.” Felicity corrected.

    “Am I detecting some jealousy?” The doctor asked with a smirk.

    “Ugh, good thing you’re a doctor, because I’m going to puke.” Felicity replied in annoyance at the doctor’s insinuation.

    Then the room became enveloped in vines that flowers bloomed from, the two girls excitedly ran towards the goddess who embraced them.

    “ΑΓΑΠΕΣ μου! You’ve gotten so big already, I just wish you could stay this small and cute forever!” Demeter cooed.

    Felicity rolled her eyes and made for the exit but was stopped by Dr. Eastly,

    “Don’t forget your lessons lesson with Choon Hee.” The scientist reminded.

    “Oops” Felicity scoffed and used a small shadow tendril to knock over a nearby beaker on her way out.

    “Oh to be a teenager again.” Dr. Eastly mused with a smirk.

    Demeter used her vines to clean the mess and released her adopted granddaughters who lined up in front of the chronobio-excelerator for a series of injections. Dr. Eastly began spastically fiddling with the controls as the girls entered the machine. It came to life and the chamber glowed brighter and brighter, the Lolita scientist pulled a lever and a blast shield blocked the light, allowing her and the blonde goddess to stop shielding their eyes. Then sparks started popping from a nearby machine, Dr. Eastly looked to the woman,

    “That’s nothing.” She reassured.

    Then another mechanism caught fire. Then Dr Eastly struggled to haul a fire extinguisher and Demeter helped her pit it the small fire.

    “I’ll fix that later, it looks like it’s done.” The scientist said.

    When the doctor powered down the machine the chamber opened and Latrisha and Diana emerged now returned to their original teenage selves except their Normal parenting has changed their personalities: each of them had fully embraced conformity and the views and goals of the Normal. Demeter immediately pulled the girls into a hug and was told to step back by Dr. Eastly.

    After a psychological evaluation Dr. Eastly had confirmed the experiment as a success and documented their changes:
    Where Diana was originally a punk girl with disregard and distain for authority, and had compassion for and sought out to fight injustices in the country. She was now quiet and somewhat reserved, she believes that people are too stupid to govern themselves and that the Normal will bring order to the world. She’s also self conscious about her lesbianism and believes that affection should be reserved for privacy.
    Where Trisha was delinquent punk and a feminist who believed men were the root cause for the world’s problems. Latrisha was conservative but stern in her approach to things, while still a lesbian she believes that her and Diana’s relationship should have clear gender roles, with Trisha being the more “masculine” of the two. Her feminism and hatred of men has mostly been replaced with respect for men and a kind of jealousy.

    After several other evaluations and tests the two girls were redressed in their old punk rock clothes and style, both of them now disliked this but knew that to avoid raising suspicion they would have to act like their past selves in order to compete their mission: to infiltrate Nat and her friend group in order to supply the Normal with valuable intel on their enemies.
