    Mythical Smoothies

    Healthy drinks be them smoothies, juices or some other type of drinks are extremely popular and common in the world. They use fresh fruit, nuts and other ingredients to make healthy tasty drinks and deserts. There are countless numbers of them, and new ones open up on a daily bases. The most popular of these shops and one that has grown rapidly over the years goes by the name Mythical Health.

    Their drinks stand out for four reasons, first is that they are extremely tasty to the point that some of their more loyal customers have said they are addicted to their favorite drink. Second while not dirt cheap they are on the lower end of cost yet extremely good; this has caused some people to drink them multiple times a day. Third, they play into their name, Mythical Health by giving their drinks fantasy inspired names, the mermaid diet smooth, the Centaur Horse Power, the Succubus temptation, the Harpies Flight for example but with dozens of others. They do many regional and holiday specials which people love, the one thing which was strange was that each drink had a male and a female version which where slightly different mixtures. They claim it was to help the body because man and women are different but if a man order a female drink or a women a male drink they would serve them with a smile.

    The fourth and last reason why people love Mythical health is their patient additions to each drink which boosted the bodies own systems. People found that drinking their products did help them loss weight, or helped cleared skin, helped with pain, boost energy or dozens of other things. It did not help people loss 20 pounds over night, but some had claimed that drinking their drinks every day was a key part of their diets. The FDA and other government regulars had studied each of the mixes and proclaimed them harmless and maybe even a little helpful. However, Stars and celebrities sang their praises which caused Mythical Health to grow even faster in terms of numbers of stores and popularity.

    With this combination Mythical Health as become so common, that there is hardly a town which does not have one and it is almost easier to find them then other massive fast-food chains. In some larger cities there is a Mythical Health on every block and you can’t look in a trash or recycling can without seeing one of their cups.

    People love their products.

    What people do not know is that it is all a front, the owners/creators of Mythical Health are all that remains of some of the actual mythical creatures who once shared this world with humanity. They had been hunted down by humanity, killed in brutal ways and forced into hiding using ancient magic to pass as human but that is about to chain. They where about to come out of the shadows in a huge way and replace the bulk of humanity with their own kind.

    What those who visited Mythical Health did not know that each of those adds on where in fact magical formulas and when the time was right it would trigger a change. When the time was right the true magical creatures who ran Mythical Health would send out a wave of magical energy and those who had drank one of their drinks within the past 72 hours would find themselves transformed into the mythical creature their drink was named after.

    For example, A women who had drank the Mermaid’s diet drink would find herself a mermaid, a man who drank the slightly different Merman diet drink would become a merman and if a women had drank the Merman’s diet drink they would find themselves a merman and if a man had drank a mermaid diet drink they would find themselves a mermaid.

    The time was soon at hand and the world would never be the same.
