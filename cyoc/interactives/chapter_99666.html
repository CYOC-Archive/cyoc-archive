    The PC War

    added by Rawshock 9 years ago O

    Everyone can see that there's a massive ideological conflict raging across America today, and that college campuses are the frontline. Every day it seems like the PC liberals or intolerant conservatives get more extreme, and that always causes the other side to get even more extreme to match. There is perhaps no better example than the two interns of a genius quantum physicist on a reasonably prestigious university on the East Coast...

    In a lot of ways, Dr Klein's interns Chad and Deshawn were as different as could be, total opposites. Deshawn was a black and proud, queer as a three dollar bill, Sanders-supporting atheist liberal Democrat, while Chad was a white and proud, straight as an arrow, Trump-supporting Christian conservative Republican. But in a lot of other ways, perhaps more important ways, they had their similarities. Both were very intelligent (despite their claims that anyone with the other's political views had to be a complete moron), which was how they had landed the highly competitive internship with Dr. Klein. Both were from wealthy families and used to getting whatever they wanted but still willing to work hard. Both were self-important, totally confident that they were right about everything, and totally committed to doing whatever it took to achieve their own vision of a better campus, country, and world. And, most importantly of all, both had stolen one of Dr. Klein's prototype Reality Alteration Devices.

    The RAD did exactly as the name promised, changed the world. With the device, which resembled a tablet, you would type or speak the changes you wanted to occur, and the device would peer through the multiverse to find an alternate reality where what you said was true and transpose it onto our reality, rewriting the past, present, and future so that whatever you had entered into the device was true and always had been. Everyone would get new memories to match the new reality and only those who made the change or saw them do it would remember the old reality. Being a prototype, the device had a tendency to be unstable and produce unexpected changes and could therefore be quite dangerous. Chad and Deshawn were both very aware of this, but as far as they were concerned they had no choice. They couldn't just stand back and let the other side destroy first their college and then their country, they had to act!

    And so, both Chad and Deshawn stole a RAD when no one was looking. The PC war was about to escalate to a whole new level...
