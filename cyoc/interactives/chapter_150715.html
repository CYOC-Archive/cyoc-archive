You're headed for the Blue Oyster where you usually go. It is not a gay bar specifically but it is a place where gays, straights and many others can intermingle, play some billiards or darts, and listen to an eclectic blend of electronica, indie, dark cabaret and other rock genres. The bar's management specifically emphasizes in all their promotional material that they want to maintain a blended atmosphere where anyone can feel welcome even if they are not typical gays or typical straights and that suits you and Dean well. Both of you were raised typically and have been average your whole life and both of you wish to explore the atypical. However, neither of you are ready to commit to one "lifestyle" yet. You don't want to be labeled. You just want to experiment.

    The Blue Oyster does have a fake cage set up at one end. It's fake because even if it was locked, whoever was inside could still get out. You have seen different guys and gals play around inside the cage. They're not allowed to have sex anywhere on the premises of the Blue Oyster but as long as they don't get too hot and heavy, the Blue Oyster turns a blind eye to playful teasing, foreplay and even nudity. The Blue Oyster does care a great deal about consent and uses a heavy handed approach to get rid of sexual harassment but as long as everyone seems to be enjoying themselves, the Blue Oyster is happy. Both you and Dean have tried spending time in the cage hoping to get a girls attention but failed to do so.

    You and Dean would both label yourself as 1 on the Kinsey scale, mostly straight but open to trying anything if the mood was right. Neither of you has actually tried gay sex but you've talked about it. You once found a test online that expanded upon the Kinsey scale asking not only about sexual activities but also about social activities, aesthetic preferences, and romantic preferences. The test results said that you were a heterosexual, homosensual, homosocial, aromantic. That is, for sex, you preferred women but for almost everything else, you preferred men. Specifically, you preferred Dean. You've always wanted Dean and have even gotten erections thinking about Dean but for some reason that you haven't figured out yet, thoughts of Dean just haven't brought you to orgasm. You haven't told Dean yet and Dean hasn't taken the expanded test yet so you don't know how Dean feels but you still love being with him whether he feels the same way toward you or not.

    Tonight, you are in a more contemplative, watchful mood. Dean gets into a game of billiards and you decide to just sit and watch. You think back about the settings you chose on the Chronivac. It asked for a breed and you didn't have time to read the entire list but the list was ordered so that within the first five breeds listed, you had a nice variety to choose from. The top five on the list were as follows:

    Arabian Horse: This horse is full of style. Arabians are regal horses with a long tradition as being the mount of choice for kings and royalty because of how elegant and majestic they are.

    Shetland Pony: This cute little pony is playful and fun. Shetlands are adorable ponies that can be a little mischievous but don't be fooled by their tiny size. They can handle being ridden by big men.

    Clydesdale horse: This horse is full of muscle. Clydesdales are the second largest horse breed but they make up for their lack of height by being better show offs. Clydesdales look more impressive than the largest horse breed, the Shire horse.

    Przewalski horse: This wild horse is stockier than domesticated horses. Przewalskis are the only surviving horses that have never been domesticated.

    Morgan horse: This horse is one of the friendliest and easy to work with. Morgans are great for riding or pulling light carriages. They make great companions because they love to bond.

    You chose to turn Dean into a Morgan horse.

    As you watch Dean play billiards, you notice him getting friendlier toward the guy he's playing with. You suddenly realize that as his Morgan horse behavior kicks in, he might wind up bonding with this other guy. You can't have that so you quickly get into the game making sure Dean stays focused on bonding with you.

    You also notice that Dean is getting more frisky. He doesn't just walk around the billiards table. He skips or jumps or even jogs around the billiard table. It's like he's developed a case of ants in the pants.
