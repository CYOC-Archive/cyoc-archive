    "Hello everyone, and welcome to a new episode of Animalia Ambassadoria." Began Malice, kicking off the latest episode.

    "First off," said Sarah, "Abby and Samson aren't here with us today because they're practicing for their part in the upcoming IAC."

    "Secondly," said Roland, "we'll be showing you some of the things going on because the IAC is only one week away.

    "And Finally," said Sophie, "We'll be visiting Animalia School of Modeling to talk with some of the models there and meeting with some new mothers."

    ---

    "The gift shop is full of unusual knickknacks that can be only found here." Said Roland as Animalia Ambassadoria roamed around the new Gift Shop. There were many comments on the kinds of things there, from silly t-shirts to cups, and every other souvenir in between.

    ---

    "Hello Maggie." Said Malice as they entered Animalia School of Modeling.

    "Oh, hello everyone." Said Maggie, as she saw the group.

    "We heard you just got a large amount of offers from various sponsors." Said Simon.

    "We did, as well as a large amount of fan mail." Replied Maggie.

    "And how did that go?" Asked Malice.

    "They range from the kind and polite, to the request-to-date, to the downright creepy." Answered Maggie, shuddering at the last one.

    "Dorks." Said Sarah.

    ---

    "Hey look, it's Canary Diamond!" Said Sophie as they met her on their way to the hospital.

    "Oh, hello there." Said Sera, taking notice of them.

    "How are things going on with you?" Asked Sarah.

    "I'm doing well." Answered Sera, "I just got done with my first album, but that's not all."

    "Really?" Asked Jessica.

    "Because of the many extremely rich people showing up here, I just got a special gift." Said Sera, pulling out a necklace. It was one of those necklaces that were solid pieces of gold, but its central decoration was a very rare canary diamond.

    "Who sent that to you?" Asked Roland, a little surprised, "Just the canary diamond alone is worth $10 million!"

    "That's just it." Answered Sera, "There was no name on the note. It just said that 'it was only fitting for me to have this.'" This kicked off a large amount of comments, from "secret admirer" comments to "conspiracy theory" comments.

    ---

    "Hello Clara." Said Jessica as they met her at Animalia Airport "How are your new cubs?"

    "They're doing well." Answered Clara, "Rick's parents are going to help babysit them while Rick and I look over the future Animalia Colony.

    "Oh, that reminds me!" Exclaimed Roland, slapping his forehead, and pulling out a pad of paper from his backpack, "Here is a list of what needs to be done to repair the hotel." He said, handing the pad to her.

    "Thank you for the list." Said Clara, "Rick and I will be looking it over for two days, before we return for the IAC opening ceremony."

    "Oh, here's an update from Melbourne." Said Sophie, noticing an update on the website. "He said that they successfully uplifted the caiman he caught, and she chose the name Mandibula. Also, the new archaeological site isn't the legendary city of gold, El Dorado, but it's actually an ancient, pre-Columbian burial ground."

    ---

    "That's all the time we have for today." Said Simon. "See you all next week at the live coverage of the IAC Opening Ceremony on ABC."
