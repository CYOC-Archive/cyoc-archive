    Mark looked around. He had now two computers with a flat screen monitor, keyboards, mice and two speakers each, including the cables connecting them. Besides that he had both their laptops next to their former seats and the drinks and snacks the group had on the table. Not to forget Geoffs other furniture, but for now the other things were more important to him.
    “First I want the monitors to turn into transport boxes in a fitting size for you two. Therefore they must expand in deep to contain you. The screen can become the grid to close it. The keyboards can change into cushions for inside the boxes. And the mice will turn into rubber cat toys that were indestructible.”
    Both Daniel and Geoff would look horrified seeing their monitors bloat bigger into hollow boxes for transporting their new bodies. The screen seemed to melt away, leaving a black grid in the now bigger opening. Meanwhile the keyboards grew a bit in size while the keys melted into a even surface. The whole block that was Geoffs keyboard turned into a formfitting, dark-blue cushion before levitating inside his changed monitor, now a dark green transport box. Daniels did the same, only it was a light green cushion inside a gray box. The mice changed into mice toys made of rubber, the cord turned into a long string while the plug stretched into a pole. The string was detachable, making it a toy for the cat to play alone or with their owner.
    Mark could hear both former young men curse him for this. He grinned when he continued.
    “Now we need two litter boxes for you. The computer housing will turn into the litter box themselves while their interior and the remaining cables change into the needed cat litter inside, with will be able to absorb anything that's left on it with some time, so it don´t need to be cleaned. The speakers can change into your bowls for food and water. Said food will be what snacks are left on the table for now, so the snack-bags will turn into packages of dry food. The bottles will also turn into food, but that should be canned food for you. To the cats it will taste like normal cat food, but your minds will taste different things out of it, similar to the baby bottles of Aidan and Jeremy it can be various things good and bad. The same will apply for anything the cat drinks out of their bowls.”
    Still screaming and cursing Mark, Daniel and Geoffs could only watch their beloved computers turn into their new toilets. They could see the interior break apart into the cat litter, followed by the remaining cables, all fitting inside the newly formed litter boxes. The speakers simply hollowed out, falling on their back while the front vanished. They then grow rounder into bowls with letters on the side, but they faced away from the cats mostly. On Geoffs former speakers they could make out a green G, while on Daniels was a yellow D. To them that meant that there would be standing their names. They hadn´t even noticed that on the table now stood two packages of dry food and several cans of canned food instead of their former snacks and drinks.
    But Mark had a two things to change for his new pets, but one at the time. “Now then, we still need something for you to scratch at, so lets turn the furniture here into a big scratching post.”
    Everything unchanged that was remaining in the flat beside the laptops slowly levitated into the air, gathering in the center of the room. Then the various objects, from the couch and the chairs over the desk and the shelves to Geoffs bed connected and deformed, turning into a scratching post that could fill a room alone. Cushions from the couch and bed turned into many padded stages to relax on, while the various wooden objects turned into caves filled with padding and naked posts to connect everything. Then the books that had been around, together with other decorative objects like the one painting Aidan had gifted to Geoff turned into ropes, which in turn wrapped around the posts for the cats to scratch on. While to Daniel that wasn´t so bad, Geoff was crying mentally. He had lost everything that he owned now.
    “Now all that remains are your laptops on the floor. While you have that big scratching post I think you will need a corner or two to also scratch on. Your laptops can become scratching plates for said corners.”
    Now both men screamed while crying mentally when their laptops swung open. The backside turned wooden with small holes to fix them on the wall while the front-side turned into a rough surface to scratch on. Mentally both men saw their cat bodies scratch away on the former screen and keyboard, ruining the whole thing.
