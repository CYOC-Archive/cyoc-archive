    Finding out your step-mom is a pregnant elf

    As you walked into the woods you got a bit of a tingling feeling in the back of your spine. Christian was a nice lady and you didn't have anything against her other than she felt somewhat mystical, but you weren't sure that if you continued into the woods you would come out.
    Christian noticing your hesitancy in your steps said "Don't worry. It's not like I gain some special powers just by being in the woods. I just wanted to talk to you... Away from where your father might hear. Plus the woods are nice, safe, and would like to know what I am going to offer you."
    Walking farther into the woods with your step-mom you asked "Well what's the offer?"
    Christian was smiling as she walked in front and said "First I have a few details to reveal to you." she then turned and then with a wave of a sparkling hand said "Glamor off." and then her form shimmered making her ears pointy and a few wrinkles go away. "I happen to be an elf!"
    "Kinda figured that. With your glowing eyes, vegetarian life style, and overall connection to nature I guessed you probably were an elf or something."
    Christian pouted and said "Fine, but did you know that I'm also pregnant?"
    Your heart beat for a second or two in silence as you tried to think of something to say, "Congrats?"
    "The young half-elf is what I wanted to talk to you about. As you know elves live for a long long time. Half-elves for a slightly shorter but essentially similar time length. We also mature a lot slower, and... Well the young one is your sibling. A half-sibling, but strong enough for there to potentially be a bond."
    "And what? You're afraid that the half-elf kid will be sad when I die?"
    "There are several socio-economic issues as well... But how would you feel if your sibling were to rapidly grow, have a full life, and die before you even started puberty. It would feel concerning, especially if they were the only one in your life doing it."
    "Wait, only... What are you talking about?"
    Christian raised her eyebrow and stated "Your father didn't tell you. I've invited him to come live in the elven country, and there we can perform the ritual of lust to make him a full elf. Since your a minor you'd come with us, and because you have no one to perform the ritual of lust with... There are three possibilities for you."
    You had no idea how you felt about being forcibly relocated to another universe or something but asked "What are they?"
    "As soon as my child is born you can perform the ritual of motherhood and be reborn. The issue with this is that while your soul and consciousness gets transferred into a new born elf... Not a lot else gets transferred so while you can live a whole new life as an elf it may not feel like you exactly." You didn't look amused as you walked forward and Christian sighed and said "Second option is that you would be bonded with a creature like a Dryad which would increase your life span considerably. From what I hear the process is painful though, and... Well as your marriages go Till death do you part with the Dryad in mind and body. Plus you'd essentially be a plant for the rest of your life."
    You sighed and said "Also not the most appealing idea."
    Christian smiled slightly and said "Well... There's another possibility that's obvious. While in the elf country you could try to attract someone into doing the ritual of lust so you could become an elf like your father. It would essentially mean you'd lose several hundred years of potential life span, but that may be tricky..."
    "How?" you wondered "My dad could do it with you. Seemingly rather easily."
    Christian chuckled and said "I'm what you might call a special case, an oddball really. I am one of the few elves selected to go find mates in the human lands. I was curious and very much willing to get a human mate. I'd be afraid most elves would either see you as some pet, some human out to hunt them, or something far from a romantic partner. I would say give up the heartache and just become my child. You and this young one would be practically twins."
    You thought to yourself and making a decision decided to tell Christian...
