But there are some very nice parts of Hell. The ones that can't be helped, however, like I said, they are usually imprisoned and kept away from the general population.     "Ha! They wish! Luci HATES cult leaders like you wouldn't believe! They become Damned all on their own, without any help from us, claim to be working in our name, and worse, drag down innocents with them! There is a special place in hell for cult leaders and televangelists." Gemory smiled, but there was no mirth in that smile.
