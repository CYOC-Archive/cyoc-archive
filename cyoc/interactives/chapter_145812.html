    Officer Ryan Conner exited the interrogation room with Tom and Alice before turning to a right and continuing down the narrow hallway towards the chief's office. Tom and Alice proceeded to go the opposite way back towards the front desk area. Ryan walked down the hallway at a moderately quick pace. If the two Weres that threatened Tom Herald were the same ones that were present during the shoot-out with Dymixious Drake, then Chief O'Hara needed to know about this.

    Ryan stopped in front of the door to the chief's office and knocked three times, which was followed by a "Come in" from the other side of the door. Ryan proceeded to open the door and step inside the office, closing it behind him. Chief O'Hara was sitting behind his desk, writing something down on a piece of paper. He continued what he was doing before putting his pen down and looking up to see who came in.

    "Ah, Ryan," he said. "What can I do for you this morning?"

    "Sir, Tom Herald is in the reception area of the station as we speak," Ryan said calmly, trying to mask his worry.

    "Tom Herald..." the chief repeated. "Isn't that the same person who wishes to expose the Were secret to the world?"

    "Yes sir," Ryan said, nodding his head, which seemed to intrigue the chief.

    "What does he want?" the chief asked.

    "He came in to report a threat," Ryan explained. "He said that two werewolves that may or may not be from Moon Lake threatened him while he was in Seattle. He said that their names were Quintus Fives and Gustavus Amehr."

    "Weren't those two men present during the shoot-out with Dymixious Drake?" Chief O'Hara asked.

    "Yes sir," Ryan responded. "They were very...passionate..about wanting Mr. Drake dead for nearly exposing the Were secret. My fear is that they'll begin plotting to kill Mr. Herald as well."

    "That is a possibility," Chief O'Hara said, processing what he was told.

    "So then, what should we do Chief?" Ryan asked.

    "We should start by keeping an eye out for any suspicious activity around the town," Chief O'Hara answered quickly. "As long as Mr. Herald is present in Moon Lake, we must do our best to keep him safe in the event that Fives and Amehr try anything funny. Has the Skulk gotten involved yet?"

    "Yes sir. Two agents brought Mr. Herald and his bonded mate to the station."

    "Well that makes things a bit easier. I assume that they'll be willing to cooperate with us. For now, just tell Mr. Herald that we'll be working to find out where Fives and Amehr are and keep him safe from them."

    "Will do, sir," Ryan said.
