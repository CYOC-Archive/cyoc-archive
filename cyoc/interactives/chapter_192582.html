The transformation was very subtle, and he couldn’t feel many of the changes when they were happening, but there was no doubt that he came out of the school completely different than from when he walked in. He was as small as a small child, thin to the point where he questioned if he even still had bones anymore, and a solid color of red fabric for skin with flower patterns sticking out.

The fabric that consumed the top part seemed to be tulle, and spread up until where Dan’s hips were. The bottom part was the same fabric as the rest of his body.
