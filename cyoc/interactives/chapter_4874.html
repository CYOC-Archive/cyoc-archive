    Jennifer happened upon Kyle's house just as he was getting home. "Kyle, yoo hoo!" she called out in fake sweetness. "It's me, your favorite victim!"

    Kyle was suprised at first, and then put on an air of annoyance. "I couldn't change you back if I wanted to, you know. You'll just have to get used to your new form. You know, like everyone else has."

    "Oh, but that's not what I wanted to talk to you about." Jennifer got almost up in Kyle's face. "Can we speak inside?"

    Kyle was taken aback by this, and then he remembered the last part of his curse. Grinning evilly, he opened the door and followed her in. As soon as he closed the door, Jennifer turned around and brought her face right next to his.

    Practically muzzle-to-nose with him, Jennifer smiled. "I've been thinking about this cold you gave me, the one that's changing me. I thought you'd want to hear something. Ready? ...ah-CHOO!" Jennifer sneezed right in Kyle's face, and then began coughing in it. Kyle tried to squirm away, but Jennifer already had grabbed his shoulders. The tips of her new claws started poking his skin through the thin material of his shirt.

    "What are you doing, you freak?"

    "Well, I figured if I have a cold, I might as well share it. I'm still contagious, aren't I?" Kyle broke her grip and made a run for it, but Jennifer was prepared for this. Kyle, Transform or Dare?"

    Kyle froze, not expecting this. He'd forgotten that by using it on her, he had given her the same power he once had. "Dare." He didn't want to know what she would have in mind if he had chosen Transform. Or worse, failed to answer and gotten both.

    "You think you're so smart, well that's not going to save you. I dare you to be my servant for the rest of your life. You will never disobey me and you will never want to see me hurt or sad. You will never try to escape from my power, even if you ever become aware of it. Is that understood?"

    Kyle tried to fight the magic that was altering his mind, but it was no use. All he could do was say, "Yes."

    "Very well. For my first command, you're going to come here and let me infect you with my cold, the same one you gave to me."

    Kyle didn't want to, but he had no choice. Reluctantly, he walked over to Jessica and stood in front of her. She coughed on him and sneezed, and he stood there and inhaled as much as he could.

    Finally, Kyle gave a cough of his own, and let out a sneeze. Jessica grinned, seeing her plans coming through.
