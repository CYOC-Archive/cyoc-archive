As the door swung shut behind them the statues and book teleported back to their hiding space and the children forgot they had found the items. Going back through the corridor the kids went to the coffee shop they had passed on the way to the room. "Hey Mom and Dad, Julie and I want to explore for the rest of the day, is that okay?"
