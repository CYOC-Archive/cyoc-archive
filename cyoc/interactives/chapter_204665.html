    Making her way back to Christine’s cabin, Laura felt a little awkward, she was now very young and her clothes, besides being roomier on her regressed body, were made for a businesswoman in her prime not for a young girl who has just entered adult life. She was glad to have found lots of teenage clothes so she could change her current outfit with something more comfortable.


    "Hey I m back". Opening the door, Laura entered the cabin to see Julia sitting on the bed with piles of clothes scattered all over the room.

    "Sooo as I can see you found nothing that fits you…that’s not very much surprising but hey I brought things for everyone." Laura said dropping everything on the bed wich caused Julia to notice Laura’s changes "Thanks but…you look different did the dice rolled again ?" Questioned Julia even if she already knows the answer.

    Laura sighed, "Well yes as I keep saying those things WANTS to be rolled there’s nothing we can do about it…"Laura said, "but I refuse to just keep rolling until I m back in diapers I m sure there’s a way to break this things"

    "Wait if we break them what will happen to me ? I don’t want to have to grow up again !" Julia claimed.

    "Hey it’s okay it was just a suggestion, we need to take a decision anyway" Laura snapped, "maybe we could also give them away." The idea of just giving the dice to some stranger was the best way to get rid of the dice honestly but Laura knew how these things were dangerous and wasn't too keen on the idea of putting another person's life in danger, the last thing they could do is take them back to where they came from.


    As Christine exited the bathroom she got surprised by Laura’s youth, she had retreated back in her teens as well. "Well looks like now we’re both back in high school" Christine stated with a smirk.

    Laura frowned "Hey hold on I m still in college" she replied. Laura was glad she was still in her 20s but any more rolls would put here between eight and eighteen, she quickly got the idea of turning so young out of her head.

    The three girls now tried several outfits, after changing panties and bra, Christine was now wearing a nice orange sundress while Laura was wearing sweatpants, t shirt and a black hoodie. "Oh my- Laura you really are a head turner you know, and what do you think of me ? Do I look good ?" Christine said giggling like a girl.

    Laura rolled her eyes, what was she thinking about ? Yeah okay she was pretty and all but It was almost like her mind was regressing as well.

    Stepping in the bathroom, taking a good look at her reflection Laura felt a bit awkward as she now looked exactly like a teenager, not to mention her latest regression, her stomach was flat as ever, her hair were healthy and she was in a really good shape, okay maybe there were some good points… But what shocked her most was how Christine seems to like it, she remembers Christine saying she wouldn’t mind being a teenagers again but being so much close to relieving puberty wasn’t something she should be happy about.

    While Laura complained about having to dress like a teen, it was nothing compared to what Julia felt when she had to dress in this pink dress, she would have preferred to wear her own clothes despite the huge size problem than this. She felt humiliated but didn’t complain since she was standing here as a nine years old only by her fault.

    "Hey I know this is hard but we’ll find a way to reverse this…just don’t try to do something bad or stupid" Laura tried to comfort Julia, she can't even imagine what it's like to be a kid again...and maybe showing some kindness to her will make karma be on her side .

    "Thanks but I always found a way on my own so don't worry for me" said Julia coldly.

    Laura only nodded to Julia reply,"Right…so what’s next ?"
