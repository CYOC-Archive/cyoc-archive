    As they wander into camp, they are surprised to see no kids, but rather various adults milling about and unpacking cars.

    "Huh, must be some sort of adult weekend here" Mutters George.

    "George, there's someone with a uniform, perhaps he can help" Todd says, gesturing to a man giving directions in the center of camp.

    The man was fairly young, probably in his mid twenties and had a handsome, confident jaw, well defined musculature and a striking pair of almost badger-like stripes running through the full jet black hair on his head.

    George glanced up at the young mans hair and grimaced "Idiot thinks that weird badger hair works, what a tool" he mutters under his breath.

    "Hey man!" Todd says to the worker "Is there a phone we can use? Our car broke down and we need to get to the airport"

    Smiling handsomely, the councilor replies "Sure! You can use mine." he reaches out a hand "Names Alex, I'm the head councilor here, if you have any questions just ask!"

    Todd shakes his hand and grabs the phone Alex then hands to him.

    "Repair man says they can't get out here until morning. I guess they are about 2 hours away and short staffed" Todd says to Alex and George after making the call.

    "For cuking christ...." George mutters

    Ignoring him, Todd turns to Alex "Any way we could bunk at a cabin here for the night?"

    "Oh for sure! You are in luck, its adults weekend here so we have plenty of spaces and no kids to worry about waking you up in the morning."

    "Well at least that's a positive" George replies

    "Why don't you take cabin 15, just down the road to the right, you can't miss it!" Alex says, directing them.

    "Well, what are we waiting for? Lets just get in that cabin so I can take these dang shoes off. These things are meant for business not hiking" George says, gesturing to his business shoes. Having only recently left a meeting both men were still in their suits and lacked a change of clothes.

    In scilence the two walked towards the cabin.

    After a few minuets, they arrived. George, naturally, was unimpressed.

    "So this is the shithole you've forced me to sleep in. God I should never have taken that project with you" George said as he made his way inside.

    This was the last straw for Todd.

    "For Fucks sake! You've been complaining non-stop and have yet to take ANY initiative to solve things or admit that some of this is YOUR FAULT." Todd yelled at his stunned partner "You act like a kid! I swear you must be 7 years old under that adult exterior."

    George was taken aback , rarely had anyone stood up to him. He didn't know how to reply, so he just sneared at Todd in silence.

    "You know...getting stuck at a summer camp is a perfect punishment for you. You act like a little kid, might as well be treated like a kid at a kids camp! Now...I don't want to hear another fucking wine from you tonight, I'm exhausted and going right to bed you fucking 7 year old girl" Todd shouted.

    With that, both men were silent. The next minutes were awkward as they each prepped for bed. Soon, however, the silence met darkness and both men stewed in bed before drifting to sleep.

    While they dreamed, neither noticed that at precisely 3:00 am, a flash crossed the room and every thing seemed to shift and change....
