    As Damon flew around the park. The young parrot spots a gardener planting flowers in the garden.

    He decided to greet the gardener and flys right to her. The gardener, name Emily, was just finish planting some flowers and about to plant some more.

    When she suddenly heard a squak as something flew down. She look up and sees a cute and handsome parrot by the garden.

    "Well hello there. I didn't think I find a handsome parrot, like you, in the garden." Damon just squaks a little hello and does a little tap dance.

    "Such a polite little bird." Emily looks through her sack and finds some nuts and berrys as she offer Damon some.

    "Here little guy. Some nuts and berrys for you." Damon began to eat some. But making sure he didn't hurt Emily.


    The two of them were becoming fast friends. "How would you like to live with me? I have a nice little garden you can live in."

    Damon squaks out his name. "Damon huh? That's a nice name for a parrot. I have a feeling we'll be good friends."
