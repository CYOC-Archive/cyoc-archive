    Beth was an average 15 year old girl. She had a 16, soon to be 17 year old brother, a lovely mom, and she had a great dad who had unfortunately passed. Like any teen, she had certain expectations for when she got home.

    This was why she was shocked to see a man and a 13 year old girl hanging out in her living room when she opened the door. There was bit of screaming, running around, and attempts to call the police while the man tried to tell her he was her mom.

    While that seemed impossible, the girl started saying things only her brother should know, and mentioned the chronovac. Jeff had mentioned that he had bought one but she had doubted that it would work.

    This is how they ended up right back in the living room, with Beth trying to absorb everything. "So Jeff turned mom into a man so he wouldn't be the only guy, and now mom is the only guy." She looked at her new little sister. "Doesn't that seem counter productive?"

    The younger girl shrugged. "I was curious."

    Her new dad spoke up. "I also made her younger since you always wanted a little sister."

    Beth looked at 'Jesse'. She squirmed slightly under her gaze. She seemed unsure of how her sister would respond. Beth couldn't help but find her cute. "Well, that's true. This might be fun." Beth said as she warmed up to the idea. "How long will she be that way?"

    "Well Jeff said I could make it last as long as I wanted, so when I noticed I timer lock as part if the controls I decided to use it."

    That got Jesse's attention. "Wait...what?"

    "I set it up so none of us can change you back until the timer hits zero. Granted, I locked you sex as a girl, not your age, and you won't automatically change back. We just can't alter that part of you yet."

    "How long?" She asked.

    He smiled. "Wouldn't it be more fun if you didn't know? It could be a weekend or a few years. I think you will commit more to the roll if you don't know how long you will be like that."

    Jesse nodded, a bit unsure. "I...guess."

    Beth took the chance to think abou her families current situation. Suddenly inspiration hit her. "Hey, since each of you used the chronovac, can I make a change as well?"

    They looked at each other and Jesse responded. "I guess. What are you going to change about yourself?"

    Beth smiled. "Who said I was going to change myself?" She gestured down at her body. She was proud of her generously curvey body. "I'm incredibly hot. Besides, you changed mom and she...uh he changed you. Maybe I was to change someone."

    "Who would that be honey." Her 'dad' asked. Beth was getting used to thinking of him that way surprisingly fast.

    "Let me think. Maybe I'll change Jen? How does she feel about her boyfriend being a girl now?"

    Jesse blushed. "She doesn't know."

    "Right, please let me be there when she finds out." Beth told her.

    Despite what she said, she could change herself. She could change her whole family. She could change them from white to something more exotic. Then again she could be older if she wanted. She didn't feel like being younger, since that would undermine Jesse being her younger sister. Unlike the former Jeff, she wasn't going to defeat the purpose of the previous change.

    She was also a bit bi-curious and was considering changing her boyfriend. Scott was a great guy, but maybe he would like to see how the other half lived. Beth had been joking about changing Jen but she was a bit of a tomboy and maybe a new teen boy could help Jesse consider a more permanent stay on this side of the gender fence.

    Beth hadn't been interested in the chronovac but now the possibilities expanded in front of her. She couldn't help but feel excited. After thinking she decided...
