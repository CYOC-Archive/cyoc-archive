    Emily couldn’t take it anymore; the tears started flowing as she looked at her two sisters before her.

    “I-I don’t know which one of you did this last night and you better hope I don’t find out, b-but if you give me back my underwear now I promise I, it won’t be to bad…I will go easy on you. Please?” she said holding back her quivering voice until the end.

    Sarah debated whether or not she went to far with this, the look of desperation on her sisters face was… un-nerving. But thinking back to the last couple times her sister was being unbearable she decided it was to soon to back off. This was her revenge; her sister was unbearable all the time. It was time she got punished for it.

    Lauren stood up first glairing at both her sisters and said “ I have nothing to confess to and now because of this I have more chores to do; so go cry to someone else, I am out.”

    This put Sarah in a bad position, right now Emily might guess it was her. “What are you talking about? Are you insinuating I did this? You gave me more work to do…and on that note thanks for leaving everything on the table for me to pick up for you!” she stomped over to the table and began picking up the dishes so she could start cleaning them.

    “I hate both of you!!” Emily screamed before running up stairs, she made sure to check Lauren into the wall as she ran past; just for good measure.

    As much as Sarah actually hated the additional chores she was smiling as she did them. She was able to fool Emily, and she didn’t have any idea of who the culprit was.

    The bus just came into view and Laruen called out. Lets go the bus is here, as she grabbed her bag and left without checking on her sisters.

    Sarah ran upstairs to grab her backpack from her room before her door slammed into her face.

    “oh did that hurt? Good.” Emily said before running to her room and grabbing her bag before jumping down the stairs.

    Confused Sarah checked her room and saw her underwear drawer open, she knew it she closed it as she checked the trash she saw an unsoiled lavender pull up crumpled up on top.

    Realizing what happened she grabbed her bag and made a quick detour to Emily’s room and opened her underwear drawer pulling out a fresh clean pull up before slamming it shut while shoving it in her bag and running downstairs just as the bus doors shut.

    She waved her hands and kept running before the air brake sounded and the door opened again to let her on.

    The bus driver gave her a nasty look, which Sarah brushed off and sat next to Amy, her best friend.

    As the bus pulled away Sarah smiled thinking about how today might actually be fun and interesting before starting to talk to Amy.
