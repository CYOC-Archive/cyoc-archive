    Jeff motions towards the bedroom's door. "I think I'm going to leave now".

    Annabelle smiled wickedly, making sure to keep eye contact with Jeff the whole time. "Why don't you have a seat on your bed, big brother?"

    "How do you feel Jeff?" Annabelle asked, her eyes now illuminated with a blue glow, signifying her full new hypnosis power.

    "What's happening to me?" Jeff replied with a hint of concern, even if his face didn't show it.

    "Oh, nothing. Aren't you feeling relaxed?"

    "Yeah, I'm actually getting drowsy". His eyes now had blue spirals around them, almost like a cartoon. Anna knew he was almost ready.

    "Good Jeff. When I snap my fingers, you will fall into a deep sleep, deeper than anything you've ever experienced. In this state you will want to follow my commands."

    This seemed to alert Jeff, whose droopy features suddenly sprung back to life. "Wait Anna, are you hypnotizing me?" He tried to break eye contact, but her glowing eyes were just too alluring to stop staring at. Suddenly Annabelle snapped her fingers. Jeff immediately closed his eyes and fell into a deep sleep, still sitting upright on his bed. To test it, his sister gave him a hefty shove and received no reaction from Jeff. She even giggled and slapped his face!

    "Jeff, I know your subconscious can hear me. When I snap my fingers again, you will wake up not as a human boy but as a young kitten. You will think I am your owner and will behave like a real cat." Satisfied with her commands, she woke Jeff back up.

    Jeff opened his eyes and stared at the girl in front of him. Confused, he could only give out a whimper: "mroooowlllllll?" Annabelle slowly approached the tentative "kitten" and rubbed his hair. Jeff's reaction went from a fiery snarl to a more comforted look of satisfaction as she began to play with his hair and scratch his body. He even gave out a long "purrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrr" as she rubbed his belly.

    Annabelle now had a "cat" familiar, but he still didn't look the part. What should she do next?
