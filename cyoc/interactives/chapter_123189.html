    Megumi looked at her cell phone and was still dreading her call to her grandmother. She took a deep breath and started to dial the number. After a few rings, she could hear a familiar female voice. "Hello?" the voice said in Japanese.

    "Hello Grandmother," Megumi answered in Japanese.

    "Granddaugher! How is the dance going?"

    "Fine, so far. Everyone seems to be having a good time" Megumi paused before she continued, "There has been a problem."

    Akane listened over the phone, and then asked, "Oh? What kind of problem?"

    Megumi took another breath and said, "Someone spiked the punch with some sort of chemical. Several people drank the punch before I took it away."

    Megumi heard Akane sigh before replying. "I see. This does not make me happy, that someone would do such a thing on a day like today. Was anyone hurt?"

    Megumi thought for a bit and then replied, "Not that I am aware. We did have an incident when one of our own kind almost changed a normal girl against her will."

    "That is not good." Akane paused as she considered her next move. "I will be sending your father down to invesitgate. Such a thing should not happen."

    "I see," Megumi replied, sounding a little disappointed. "Tell my father that I shall meet him in the school kitchen. We are reviewing the footage to see who had done this."

    "I shall inform your father," Akane paused for a moment. "Despite this incident, you have done well. Stay safe and wait for your father."

    "I will, grandmother."

    With that said, the two said their goodbyes, and Megumi hung up her phone. She sighed as she folded her arms. She didn't want to get Akane involved, but knowing her, she would want to know. And with the Tachibana clan on this, heaven help those who spiked the punch if they get caught.
