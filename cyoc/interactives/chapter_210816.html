    Pam was a typical 30 year old girl. Good life, good boyfriend, loving family, etc. However she was a pathological liar. It wasnt just small lies but big ones. She'd lie about her grandma dying just to get out of work early. Or lie about going into labor to get out of a speeding ticket. It has worked but at times it hasnt. She has lost good friends, jobs, ruined relationships, etc because of lies but despite this she still lies.

    One day she was at a store where she saw a cute blouse she wanted and there was only one left. She was about to grab it but someone beat her to it.

    "Oh this is cute. Great for my grand daughter." said an elderly woman

    "I saw it first." said Pam

    "But I got to it first."

    "I need that blouse. My cousin is dying of cancer and used to have the same blouse but lost it when her house caught fire. This would brighten her day" Pam lied

    "Oh your poor cousin. Maybe I should let you have it to give to them. Unless you were lying."

    "Oh I wasnt lying. My sister needs it."

    "I thought it was your cousin?"

    "I mean..."

    "Tsk...tsk. Lying never gets you anywhere. I bet you lie all the time to get things. Maybe you need to learn to tell the truth."

    The lady then went over to Pam and bopped her in the head.

    "There, now you will always be truthful."

    Pam knew the lady was crazy as she of course still wanted to lie. But what she didnt know was the woman was a witch. She placed a curse on her. Now whatever Pam said even if a lie. Would become true. No matter what.

    "You go ahead and buy the blouse for your sister, cousin, or self. I dont care. Just be careful with your lies." said the woman leaving behind the blouse
