    Author note:
    Roy in Ashley’s Life will now be referred to as Ashley and the former Ashley as Roy from now on for obvious reasons.

    The thought of being in this position seen as a girl for possibly the rest of his life disturbed Roy to the core. When he thought about it more and more and even though he had never been one to go into hysterics or the effects of doing so he promptly passed out on the floor beside Stacey as she cried her eyes out.

    Coming to Ashley felt even more different then before and it wasn’t just the new dress she was wearing white with pink hearts but just in general. Getting out of bed and feeling her long hair fall down past her shoulders Ashley yelped in stunned revelation at her height that had shrunk by over a foot and now found herself shorter then Stacey. Ashley found herself in Stacey’s sister’s room and she noticed how everything looked so much bigger then before her height shrank. Looking down at the rest of her body Ashley was immediately met by two large orbs in her field of vision “NO” Ashley thought. Looking at her hands and feet made Ashley even more disturbed because they looked just like that of an older teenage girl as a growing thought entered her mind “she didn’t, did she?” Rushing to the bathroom Ashley screamed at the reflection in the mirror. She looked just like Ashley and was now fully a girl. A probe down below proved beyond a shadow of a doubt that Stacey had turned the former Roy fully into her younger sister.

    Ashley ran back into her room. The poor girl hadn’t been able to find Stacey anywhere as she seemed to be home alone. The question was how she did it as she fidgeted walking back and forth on the floor in her bare feet and dress. Ashley got her answer soon enough when she spotted a note on her bedside burrow written in Stacey’s handwriting.

    “Dear Ashley,

    I know that you’re probably upset right now seeing what I did to you but you have to realize I did it because I love you both as my friend and now my sister. We don’t know how long Roy is gonna be in a coma and we have to make the best of the situation for now. Even though you are seen as everyone as a girl and my sister you really were not one and both of us could see that. I figured out that if I pointed the device at people in photographs and had living people as the primary target I could swap out their looks with the person in the photograph. In your case I targeted Roy with Ashley’s body and gave you the body of my sister that your meant to have in this role if your ever going to fit into being part of my family. Roy now has your male body and that look obviously fits. I want you not to hate me but to understand me and how I’m trying to help you fit in mentally to your new role. I redressed you in that pretty dress to try and facilitate your entry into womanhood on a more feminine track but I can understand if you ‘re pissed off at me for it. I have the device with me and I’m out grocery shopping. When I get home we can talk again.

    Your Loving Sister,
    Stacey”

    Throwing the note over her shoulders Ashley angrily yelled out “What the hell am I supposed to do now?”
