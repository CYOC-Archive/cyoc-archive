    Johnny the lone bachelor gets VIP status

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous A month ago O

    Johnny was a bit bitter. Single despite trying like 3 different dating apps to no real success... and he was a bit chronically attached to his office at and away from work... Then he won a raffle for the Transformation resort. He didn't think he'd win it, but... he had saved a lot of vacation time and his management was happy to let him have the vacation. He'd probably still skimp on a lot of things and enjoy the "complementary parts" He used all his good luck winning the raffle for free tickets and a few coupons he didn't need to-
    As he was checking in Johnny felt himself in a white room with another receptionist who said "Congratulations, the last VIP member has checked out and our automatic raffle has selected you as our current VIP. You will have your room upgraded to the VIP suite, all expenses paid, and an assortment of perks such as... you'll have an extreme amount of customizability on everyone here. From yourself, to other guests, and to some extent our staff you'll be able to change everyone in ways not normally provided. Including awareness and mental features."
    Johnny felt a bit pale... "You mean If I wasn't I'd be subject to the whims of someone else and possibly identity death'd?"
    "Well, you'd be changed back on exiting. Although we can handwave the change back for you upon your stay ending. That isn't one thing you can change by the way. You're end date is rigid."
    Johnny wasn't sure he'd suggest the transformation resort to anyone else at this point, but then again... it was a niche somewhat fetish resort to begin with. Still with this power in his hands he could... have some more fun. Really the all expenses paid.
    "To customize just think about who you want to customize, think about the white room, time will stop, and you can adjust the change to your heart's content... For the most part." Then the white room ended and Johnny got his room key from the receptionist... It was in the middle of the three resort wings allowing him to do all sorts of things in his own room... if he already didn't have the ability to change everything about himself on the fly already, he did suppose it meant people could change their age/gender/species in his room at will though.
    There was so much to try to do... Especially since Johnny didn't have to pay an additional dime. Johnny looked around and wondered what he should try first.
