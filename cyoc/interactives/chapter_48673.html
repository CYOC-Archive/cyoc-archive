    Chuck, the big, fat kid, was chasing after Neil, hoping to get him to hand over one of his cookies.

    “Hand over your cookies! Give them to me, now!!” Chuck shouted, as he ran after Neil, who was shorter and younger than him. Luckily, because Neil was smaller, he was also faster. However, Neil was getting tired of being chased around by the big, overweight bully.

    “Leave me alone!” Neil shouted back, as he dashed ahead of the chubby boy far enough, that he was able to hide behind one of the bushes.

    “There! I lost him…” Neil whispered to himself, as he watched Chuck walk away from a distance.

    Neil wasn’t aware of two other boys, who had spotted him, and who had walked up to him from behind.

    “Looks like you’re hiding from someone, huh?”

    “Whaaaah!” Neil shrieked, as he turned around in panic.

    “Sorry… I didn’t mean to scare you…” the boy with the blond hair and blue eyes said to him. “You just look like you’re hiding from that other guy over there?”

    “Y-y-yeah… That was Chuck… He was after my cookies…” Neil replied. “But I only have two left… I ate all the others…”

    “Oh… well don’t worry… we won’t hurt you! By the way, my name is Tyler, but you can call me Ty!” the blond boy said to him.

    “Oh, and I’m Dustin…” the taller boy added. “Why was Chuck running after you?”

    “Because he likes to bully me… Only because he’s older and bigger than me! I just hate that!” Neil answered out of frustration. “He’s nothing but a big, fat jerk! He can get away with it because he’s bigger and stronger than me; that’s all!”

    “So, he’s bullied you before?” Ty wondered.

    “Yeah! I’m sick of it! I wish there was a way to make it STOP!” Neil groaned.

    “Well, Uhh… What’s your name again?” Ty asked the boy, who looked like he was about twelve years old.

    “Neil… My name is Neil!”

    Ty pulled a deck of cards out of his pocket and fanned the cards while holding them in his hand in such a way so that a small piece of every card was visible.

    “Tell you what, Neil…” Ty explained. “These are Human Transformation Cards. Each card describes a really cool type of transformation, which will help you in your dilemma. Pick one card by pointing at it with your finger, and I promise you that I can allow the change, which is described on that card, to happen”.

    “What?” Neil replied, not completely believing Ty. “You’re kidding, right?”

    “Nope! Trust me….”

    Neil studied the cards that Ty held in his hand, and he tapped the edge of one of the cards, which Ty held. Ty then pulled that card out, with his other hand, and read the text that was printed on the face of the selected card.

    “You will gain forty pounds of muscle and grow a foot in height…” Ty read out loud, before turning the card over so that Neil could read the text on the card himself.

    “So when will this happen… WHOA!” Neil gasped, when he was suddenly overcome by an intense tingling feeling throughout his entire body!

    Suddenly, the boy’s body began to expand in every direction as a result of a MASSIVE growth spurt! Neil grew from 5 foot 6 to 6 foot 6 in a matter of seconds, as his arms, his legs, and his torso began to stretch like a rubber band! Not only did everything grow longer, but BIGGER as well! His hands grew much larger at the same time, and his arms grew thicker, while his feet started to expand. He suddenly went from size 8 to size 17 in a few seconds!

    Neil’s legs widened as his muscles began to inflate at the same time as well, with his thighs expanding first. Thick layers of muscle formed on his quads, making him look like a runner or a gymnast, while his calves began to blow up like two balloons that were attached to an air-pump.

    “Oohhh OOOOOHHHHH!!!” Neil groaned. The sensation of growing bigger while his muscles inflated at the same time was just incredible for the boy, who was becoming MASSIVE! Within seconds, he went from a skinny little kid to a strapping athlete!

    Neil’s chest bulged out and sideways as mighty pectorals began to push out while these rock-hard muscles continued to fill out his t-shirt, until they just burst out of it by ripping through the cotton. Then, his sleeves ripped apart as well, when his massive biceps blew right through them. On every side of his new body, Neil was feeling overwhelmed by the incredible increase of strength that was taking place in his body.

    Finally, his flat tummy had now morphed into the torso of Olympians which was adorned by a washboard of abdominals. Large rows and columns of muscles, which looked like tiles on a bathroom wall, now covered his stomach.

    Finally, when the growth had stopped, Neil looked down and rubbed his hands over his chest and his mighty abs.

    “Man, I can’t believe this! You weren’t kidding!” Neil said out loud. “Nobody will pick on me like this!”

    “I think Chuck will leave you alone from this point forward… Shall we put it to the test?” Ty suggested.

    “Uhh… Sure!” Neil said.

    Ty snapped his fingers and suddenly Chuck appeared, standing right before Neil. Only this time, he was looking up to the muscular boy!

    “Hey… What… Neil?”

    “Hi there, Chucky!” Neil grinned, while folding his huge arms before his bulging chest. “Long time no see!”

    “Uhhh….”

    “I’m out of cookies, so quit bugging me about them! Do you hear?” Neil said to him in a stern voice.

    “Y-y-yeah…” Chuck stuttered out of fear for the bigger and stronger boy. It was obvious who the boss was now.

    “Now, leave me alone and don’t bug me anymore!” Neil shouted at him.

    The chubby boy turned around and ran away out of fear!

    “Thanks, Ty!” Neil said, before he turned around and started walking towards the other end of the park.

    Ty and Dustin just stared after him, happy with their latest transformed person, wondering who they could transform next.
