    Now that Kazumi was in the suit's second form, she could stand upright. She even had tiny little claws she could wiggle around. As she turned tail and began slowly stomping her way back to the ocean, Kazumi was tearing up inside.

    This whole day was a dream come true...

    Hell, this whole job was!

    Staying up and watching Kaiju flicks at night as a kid, Kazumi could've never imagined herself getting to play Gojira himself.

    But here she was, in the flesh.

    Kazumi just knew that this month was gonna be fun.

    And even though there was chance that this series might not get a second season, Kazumi hoped that the effects crew would let her keep the suit so that she'd never have to let go of her dreams.
