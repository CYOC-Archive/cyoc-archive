    Alice leaned back in her chair, letting out a long yawn.
    "Jeez... Do you EVER sleep? I hear you yawn every two minutes?" Roy commented as he passed, stopping up as he noticed the die on her desk. "Huh? ...A twenty-sided die?" He asked, that little detail suddenly grabbing his attention.
    "Oh, er... Yeah... I got this thing sent to me in the mail for some reason..." Alice explained. "...And there was this strange note, too. I think somebody's pranking me." She continued, digging in her pocket for the note. "...It said something about... eh... what was it again?"

    They were in their classroom, both 17 years old.
    Alice was a fairly uninteresting person, besides her being known as somewhat of a weirdo among her classmates. With blonde hair and brown eyes, c cups and a fairly average body.
    Roy was her childhood friend and had red, short hair, green eyes and was fairly athletic, being part of the soccer team and all. He was fairly popular as well, partly because of his pleasant personality.

    "Okay, found it!" Alice exclaimed. "Let's see here... Yeah, apparently, the thing will change you depending on what number you get... Sounds legit, doesn't it?" She asked sarcastically.
    "Yeah... Very." Roy answered with the same amount of sarcasm as he picked up the die, rolling it nonchalantly, landing on 18. After a moment of waiting and nothing happening, he chuckled. "Yup... Sure works..."

    "Hey now! What'cha two doing?" A voice suddenly asked before revealing themselves. Taylor, a friend of Roy, leaned over the desk, glancing down at the die. "Playing a game or somethin'?"
    Well, they don't really have a board or anything, if you haven't noticed..." Suddenly, Cynthia approached them as well, with her friend, Wendy, close behind.
    "Um... Excuse us..." Wendy mumbled, waving lightly before hiding behind Cynthia's back. More or less everyone besides Roy knew of her crush on him, which explained why she was embarrassed.

    Taylor was another member on the soccer team - the goalkeeper, in fact. With blonde, spiky hair, blue eyes and an athletic build, and with an energetic personality to boot.
    Cynthia, though not really a friend of Alice's, was still quite mature for her age. With long, brown hair and brown eyes, a well-developed body.
    Wendy was fairly short and petite, with black hair in a bit of an emo style, green eyes and glasses. She was known for being pretty quiet and shy...though, as of yet, nobody knew of her deep, kinky desires...

    "How about we all roll it just for fun?" Taylor suggested, not aware of the magic the die held. Everyone else just shrugged, it was not like they had anything else to do...
    "So... Who goes first then?" Alice asked, looking at everyone.
