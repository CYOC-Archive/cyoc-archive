    The Path of Change

    "WEll, Dustin," Ty suggested. "Since it's such a nice Saturday
    afternoon, how about we go outside? Maybe we can change a few of our
    neighbors?"

    "Sure! The weather is so nice outside, since it's summer and it's not
    raining today!" Dustin said, as he walked over to his own bedroom
    since his window looked out over the sidewalk which ran right in
    before the front lawn of the house where Ty and Dustin lived.

    Ty then joined Dustin in his bedroom and the two brothers opened the
    large window which looked out over the front of the house from the
    second floor. They had a great, clear view of the sidewalk below and
    in front of them. There were several people walking past their house.

    "Hmm... Isn't that the boy who lives right down the street from us?"
    Ty pointed out, as he pointed his finger at the 12-year-old boy who
    was riding his skateboard over the sidewalk, as he was about to pass
    right in front of them.

    "Yeah... I think his name was Sean!" Dustin replied. "He's like 12 or
    13 years old..."

    "Oh! let's have some fun with him!" Ty cheered.

    "What are you gonna do to him?" Dustin wondered.

    "I'll grow his muscles a bit..." Ty announced.

    "While he's skating? He'll fall off his board!"

    "Oh! How about I suspend him first!" Ty said, as he snapped his
    fingers just when Sean passed right in front of Ty and Dustin.

    Just when the boy rolled right in front of them, Ty snapped his
    fingers, causing time to stand still for the teenage boy on his
    skateboard. The boy was wearing a tank top and shorts with sneakers,
    so it was easy to see that he was very skinny and short.

    "Okay, Dustin! Now... Watch this!" Ty ordered.

    Instantly, Sean become a LOT more muscular! Sean's chest pushed out,
    his abs become a lot more defined, his calves and quads grew thicker
    and wider, his biceps bulged from his arms and his shoulders became a
    lot wider. His traps and delts grew on his shoulders and lats formed
    on his sides. Sean now became just like a muscle kid!

    "Wow! He looks different!" Dustin gasped.

    "Yeah! he looks awesome now!" Ty said, as he snapped his fingers
    again.

    Sean resumed skating but the boy quickly stopped and jumped off his
    board and stood on the sidewalk, admiring his muscles by flexing his
    arms and his legs!

    "Whoa! What the heck?" he whispered, as the boy stood there, gawking
    at his bulging biceps. After a few minutes, he jumped on his board and
    continued on his way.

    Dustin and Ty then looked and spotted someone else passing before
    their house on the sidewalk...
