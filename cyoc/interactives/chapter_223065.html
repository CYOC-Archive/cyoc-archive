    Before Jeff is able to decide of what to try out first, he hears his mother calling for him, “JEFF, CAN YOU PLEASE DO THE LAUNDRY?”

    Jeff grumbles and scowls, “every time I am about to do something, she always has to interrupt,” he mumbles to himself. He looks at the program, thinking if he should close it, but he shakes his head, “I won’t be gone for long,” he says to himself, “it is not like anyone ever wanders into my room anyway,” with that Jeff heads downstairs.

    While Jeff is in the basement doing the laundry, which is more than he’d like, the door to his sister’s room opens and a young girl, around 10 years of age, with smooth long black hair that reaches just past her shoulders, steps out.

    Mandy, Jeff’s sister, walks straight for Jeff’s room, she knocks on the door, “Jeff, may I come in,” she asks, not hearing any reply, the girl slowly opens the door and sticks her head in, noticing that the room is empty, Mandy steps in. “I don’t think he’ll mind if I borrow his colouring kit,” she says to herself and walks directly to Jeff’s desk.

    When Mandy reaches the desk, she notices that Jeff has left his Laptop running and a weird looking game seems to be active. The little girl tilts her head curiously, “why is there a 3D image of Jeff on the screen,” she asks herself, “maybe it’s some kind of 3D image altering game? Maybe Jeff wants to see what he looks like as a girl or animal?”

    Mandy’s curiosity gets the better of her and she decides to check the game out, the girl climbs onto Jeff’s chair and starts to read through what is on the screen:

    Name: Jeff Davis
    Species: Human
    Race: Caucasian
    Gender: Male
    Age: 16
    Wearing: Blue T-Shirt, Black Boxer Briefs, Blue Jeans, White Socks, Black Sneakers
    Sexual orientation: Straight
    Relationship status: In a relationship with Holly Drews
    Personality: Dominant, Curious, Mischievous, Adventurous
    Interested in: Dragons, Demons, Lizards, Snakes, Sports, Hanging Out With Friends
    Awareness: on

    “Wow,” Mandy exclaims, “Jeff surely has created an interesting character,” the girl assumes that the “game” is some sort of character creating program for some fantasy game. Mandy then notices a tab labelled “Transformation options” and when she opens it her eyes widen at all of the supposed transformations: inanimate, animal, race, age, gender,…!

    Mandy clicks on the “animal” option and a long list with every known earth dwelling animal pops up: dogs, horses, reptiles (even dinosaurs and dragons), cats, fish, lobster,…! Facinated, Mandy opens the next option: inanimate, which lists various types of inanimate/object transformations, like: clothes, furniture, vehicles, toys (even sex toys), food (vegetables, fruits,…),…!

    “So, apparently,” Mandy mumbles to herself, “if I choose the 3D-Jeff to become a horse, then the image will transform into a horse? It even says that it’s possible, instead of full animal transformation, there is the possibility of body part transformation,” the girl giggles, “Jeff would look so silly with a horse’s tail!”

    Mandy continues to fantasize what she could do to Jeff’s “character”, “I could transform him into a cute little baby,” the girl says, excitement in her voice, “or maybe I could transform him into a toy, maybe a doll? Or maybe come kind of clothing? So many possibilities… it is hard to decide what I’d like to do… hmm… I am kinda curious to see Jeff as a girl… having a big sister would have been better than a stupid bossy big brother!”

    The girl leans back in the chair as she starts to think about what to do.
