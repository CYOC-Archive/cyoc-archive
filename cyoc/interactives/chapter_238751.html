Her hand was covered in blood as well, and her whole look appeared to be dizzy and aloof. The first thing to come out of her mouth was, “Jeff! I need you to change me back.”

    “Why what happened? Why are you bleeding?” Jeff asked.

    “Oh this,” Jenny raised her bloody hand, “I was making breakfast this morning, and cut myself on the knife. It doesn’t hurt though, so I’ll probably be fine.”

    “As for everything else, I’m pretty sure your machine did something extra. Just yesterday I stubbed my toe and it took me a whole hour to come down from the orgasm. Which was pretty awesome at the time, but this morning my mom was acting different.”

    “So, on my way downstairs, I slipped and tumbled all the way down. It felt great, I just hope nothing’s broken. More importantly, when she rushed over, I thought she was coming to check on me. Instead, she kicked me in the head, HARD.”

    “It felt awesome, but I’m pretty sure it’s why I still feel dizzy. I don’t know, I might have a concussion. It’s so hard to tell without pain. So please Jeff, please change me back to normal before this gets out of hand.”

    Poor Jenny, is all that Jeff could think. He certainly didn’t want to cause any permanent injuries to Jenny. He was about to use the device again, but then another thought came to mind. Right now, no one could help her. Any attempt someone made would just hurt her more, so he could do the cruelest things to her without consequence.

    He looked at Jenny, still dazed and not fully there. With a sly grin he said, “Sure, I’ll help you.”

She reached up to touch the blackened ring around her eye. The sting sent shivers of pleasure down her spine. Clearly Jeff didn’t plan to change her back today. She knew staying here would be dangerous, so she pulled herself together and limped back home.

She would have to send messages to Jeff, going to him in person seemed like a recipe for disaster. She walked into the kitchen and began washing her face. Her mom happened to be there, which worried her a little. As she tended to her wounds, her mom commented that she looked like she had a great time.
