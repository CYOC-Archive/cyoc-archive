She declared, watching as the other girls got up, Felicity hesitating a little longer. Tanya got the remote to pause the movie, as all five girls made their way to the dining room, taking their seats, Lucy and Jennifer sitting next to each other.

    "U-Um.." Both girls went red again, but Lucy was the one who confessed. "I-I might've... kissed her..." And after a moment, she also added, "A-And, um, I-I was actually the one to ask her..."

    The latter confession got their attention more. "Wait, what?" Natalie looked between the two girls, surprised. "I thought... I thought that was why Jennifer asked you to talk." Then the smug look returned. "Or did something else happen be-"

    "N-No!" Jennifer interrupted. "T-The only thing we did was kiss! A-And wash our hands after, to get ready for pizza!"

    That sparked another bit of interest. "Washed your hands?" Tanya pointed out. "Why were you in the bathroom, then?"

    "P-Private place to talk!" Jennifer stammered out. "I-I wanted to talk to her about something private, th-that's all!"

    Luckily for both Jennifer and Lucy, the sound of footsteps gave them a slight relief from their friends' interrogation. "There you guys are!" Diana exclaimed as she entered the room, pizza in hand. "I went to the living room and nobody was there! So I looked around for..." Then she paused, noticing Jennifer and Lucy's faces. "Woah, you're even redder than before!" The girl pointed out, to their embarrassment. "What'd I miss?" She asked, setting the pizzas on the table and heading to an empty chair.

    "Well~" Natalie began in a singsong voice. "Jennifer and Lucy were just explaining how they were kissing in the bathroom~"

    "Guys, stop~!" Lucy complained, burying her face in her hands. "A-all we did was talk for a bit, then kiss after I asked her out..!"

    Jennifer, too, tried to hide her face, before she was struck with an idea to redirect their attention. "A-And besides! Diana's the one who was all too willing to sleep with Felicity!" The girl pointed out. "She even brought that topic up!"

    Attention turned to the girl that just came back. "That's... true, actually." Tanya noted. "Diana was really quick to offer to sleep with Felicity, without even being asked by anyone..."
