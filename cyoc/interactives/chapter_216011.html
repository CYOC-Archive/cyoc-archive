    Thompson's Second Chance

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LuisJM 5 months ago AR BM S

    Miss Thompson was going about her lesson and somewhat glaring around. A number of students were taking notes with pen and paper... Others weren't taking any notes at all... Obviously some were doodling, but Thompson was a little less annoyed about that. They were honing a craft after all... Although she hoped Mr. O'brien would properly teach them perspective and proper technique... Their dedication to her lessons would surely show in their grading after all. However there were those who used laptops to take notes. She was sure that Miss Suzie up in front of the classroom like the good little noodle she was took notes incredibly efficiently... After all it wasn't like all the students were crowding to see what she was doing so it must not have been entertaining. It was those near the back with laptops she was pretty sure were on Reddit or Facebook or Tictoc... Complete slackers... Yet she couldn't call them out on it without proof, and walking all the way back there every so often was just tiring... So she continued the lecture.

    As she was lecturing, she felt a bit of a shiver and thought it was just the air conditioner. So she continued with her lessons without realizing what is actually happening. What she and her students didn't know is that her energy was slowly returning and feels a little more active then before. Even elderly, she was slowly regaining her strength. What was happening is that her body was starting to get a little younger as she was going back days a second. Already, her posture was strengthening and her body was looking less bony. Even her snow white hair on her bunned up head was showing streaks of gray slowly scattering about. Even her clothes that look like a an old vest with a conservative blouse, long tube skirt, and a pair of loafers with ankle socks were slowly altering, changing colors and styles. Pretty soon, this elderly woman left her nineties and entering her eighties. Yet she nor the students know of this, yet. Not to mention Thompsons strange regression is either a gift or a curse. Course, what is happening is that one of the students was using the Chronivac on the laptop and is testing it on the teacher. And regressing her is a way of giving her youth back, but maybe a second chance. The problem is how long or when the regression stops.
