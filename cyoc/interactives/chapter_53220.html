    Ty casually walked through the halls of his school as if he owned them that morning. Everyone seemed to ignore him, as usual. When he reached his locker, there he found his friend, Aden. He was 5'7" and wiry, but solid. His hair was blonde, and his eyes were green. In all honesty, Ty though he was rather attractive for a nerdy guy. Though Aden had a crush on a closeted gay swimmer, named Leandro. Tall, charmingly handsome, perfectly tanned, impressively strong; all of those descriptions did no justice for the hazel eyed, black haired god that was Leandro. Seth also mentioned Aden almost everyday, it was almost annoying. Today was different, though.

    "Ty, Lee rejected me again... I thought if I worked out a bit he'd like me but..."

    "Aw come on Aden, just forget that guy!" Ty said, giving Aden a stern look.

    "But he's so amazing in every way. He's not just some sexy guy, he's also pretty smart... I wish I were perfect for him..." Aden muttered with downcast eyes.

    "What does he want you to be like, anyway?"

    "I thought he'd like guys his size. So if I bulked up I'd be good enough, but I haven't grown much... And he's 6'2", so there's a 5 inch gap... I'm just guessing that's what he likes, though. He probably just wants a guy he won't hurt when he messes around with them. He's so strong, so I could imagine him hurting his partners... That and his package is probably too huge to comfortably fit. If he was even out of the closet, then I could at least get an idea of what he likes... He says he likes me, but he says it just wouldn't work... I don't even know what he wants..."

    "Leave it to me, Aden. I'll help you out." Ty offered, a sly looking crossing his face.

    "How?"

    "Don't worry, leave it to me..." Ty reassured, quickly walking off towards the pool's locker room.

    Leandro usually did laps in the pool for the first period of school instead of attending normal gym class. It's probably because he's the fastest swimmer and the coaches want him to get faster. Sure enough, Travis was already changed into his black swim briefs. It was a sight that most people should pay to see, it was just that good.

    "Hey Leandro, can I talk to you?" Ty asked.

    "Oh! Buenos dias Ty... Uh... Sure, bud." Leandro awkwardly replied, placing his hands over the front of his swimsuit. He probably felt uncomfortable since it's not exactly normal to have conversations in the locker room.

    "Aden asked you out again and you rejected him. Why don't you like him?"

    "Esperar! Esperar! He told you?!" Leandro shouted, clearly taken aback.

    "I'm his friend, so yeah he told me. Just tell me why, he's really torn up about how you didn't even give him a chance, you know." Ty said, trying to lay emphasis on the guilt-trip.

    "Oh... Poor Aden. I like him. He's really special... But he's too small for me. I don't wanna hurt him." Leandro said, his downcast looking matching that of Aden's.

    "Oh damn, he was right then... What if I said I could help you two out?"

    "You can? I don't get it... But if you could... This locker room would need some serious cleaning up." Leandro replied with a mischievous grin. "I have a thing for guys with glasses... Especially him. If he were almost as big as me, he'd be perfect."

    "Ah, okay. So you like being the bigger guy, right Leandro? Well, don't worry, you're both going to have fun real time soon. You should discuss this with Aden over at your house, you know? Just so he can understand and there'd be no hard feelings" Ty said nonchalantly before seeing himself out of the locker room. Leandro was left absolutely confused by what Ty actually meant, but figured it was worth a try.

    Ty popped open his phone and sent a text to Aden, saying "Hey, Lee wants to see you at his house after school."
