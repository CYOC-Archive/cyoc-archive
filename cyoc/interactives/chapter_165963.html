And the best part was that it’s monthly membership was so much more reasonably priced than any other place she’d heard about. The front entrance had a fully stocked nutrition bar, so she’d never have to worry about packing snacks, and the weight room had all these cool kinds of machines for muscles she never even knew could be trained. And most extra of all, was the fact that each of the locker rooms had their own saunas next door (which she was never brave enough to try, but maybe some day...?)

She wasn’t sure when exactly they were closing, so she went to go change quickly into her athletic gear, a cropped tee and a pair of leggings, before heading right over to the machines. She got halfway through her third set on the seated row before someone came up to her:

Even from underneath the black, sleeveless top he wore, she could tell his core was shredded to the extreme just from the way the spandex hugged it. She knew it would be him right from the first day, when she saw his warm, encouraging personality stand out among his friends. God, how an affectionate animal of a man like him could just melt her. And to top it off, he was a natural ginger with a dusting of light golden hairs along his beefy arms that gave him a kind of exotic flair. At times, she likened him to this romantic picture of an Old Norse warrior stuck in modern day.

Would he even be okay with finding out what she was into? Would she be alone forever unless she found someone who was? All these familiar doubts kept on stewing in her mind, until she stopped and remembered what was different this time: the vials that were sitting in her purse. She picked one up and gazed at it thoughtfully.
