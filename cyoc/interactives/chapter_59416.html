    65 million years ago the world was dominated by dinosaurs. That is until several meteors streamed into the atmosphere and exploded.
    The dinosaurs that were caught in the blasts either died immediately or died from radiation sickness. Others slowly starved to death and
    thus ended the reign of the dinosaurs.

    However the meteors didn't just wipe out the dinosaurs but bathed the earth in strange energy. This energy mostly lay dormant for a
    long time. The few times it wasn't dormant some animals would spontaneously transform. Sometimes it was a small transformation
    enabling the animals to have a better survival rate. Other times the transformation was big completely changing the animal into another
    animal, a tree or flower, or even rocks and dirt.

    Then the race of man came and they learned how to use this energy to cause transformations. These people came to be known as
    shamans, witches, wizards, warlocks, sorcerers, sorceresses, and even gods and goddesses. The energy they controlled became known
    as magic.

    There was still wild magic which would become active at times and transform people and animals, even inanimate objects. Out of this
    wild magic weres were born as well as fairies, leprechauns, elves, centaurs, merfolk, vampires and other creatures.

    Ordinary people were afraid of those with magic and the magical creatures. So afraid that they started hunting them in the hopes of
    eliminating what they feared. They would usually come in the night while the magic user or creature slept and surprise them. This
    usually resulted in the magic user or creature not being able to defend themselves and being being killed. Of course not everyone was
    afraid. Some people held magic users and creatures in high esteem and so some of them did survive by virtue of these people not
    hunting them. Some of them went into hiding and were thus able to survive as well.

    Now magic is resurging in the world creating more magical creatures and more people are discovering that they can use magic. Will the
    old fears come back though? Will magic users and creatures be able to survive in a world that doesn't believe in magic?
