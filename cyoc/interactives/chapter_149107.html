    “Ann,” Sebastian asked. “Why does that name sound so familiar?”

    “Because in my daughter Katie’s timeline she was Kellies sister.”

    The group now went silent as they pondered what Jack said. “Any reason you didn’t mention this,” Ayami grumbled, crossing her arms over her ample breasts. It’s a whole other timeline, one that hasn’t been touched by any of us. We could influence that timeline, turn it towards darkness. But there’s a catch.”
