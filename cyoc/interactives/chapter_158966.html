    Naomi could see how disturbed was her friend and was thinking the curse must be true, or her friend wouldn't have been so shaken. But before she cool try to comfort Jennifer, Jade burst out laughing before saying "Good one Jen, you almost got me!"

    Fuming, Jen said "Okay girls, Transform or Dare?" Failing, in her rage, to notice how Naomi paled at Jade's reactions.

    The African-American girl, scared, timidly answered "d-dare" while her Asian friend scoffed. "Wait, you really believe this crap? That's hilarious! Transform, just to see what crap you'll come with it."

    At Jade's answer, a feral grin appeared on the aspiring kunoichi's face. "You remember how I will become master's obedient pet dragon if I fail a becoming a good kunoichi? That's exactly what you will become, a dragon utterly devoted to master, and be rather protective of him. For today you'll only be a human sized one, but during the night you'll turn fullsized. But it's not all."

    "Unlike on legend, you won't be a dragon that collect a hoard, but one with magic that somehow create all sort of riches under her while sleeping. You won't let anyone other than master touch said pile of treasures without master's explicit consent. You'll also know all ninja secrets and, while you won't be able to work as one since you're a dragon, you'll be more than willing to help any potential ninja to learn the trade as long as they are as loyal to master as I'm becoming. In my case, and my case only, you will make me learn techniques for both kunoichis and shinobis. All others will only learn the ones appropriates for their gender, which includes the techniques common to both men and women. That said, you'll make sure that I'm turning into the best ninja that ever was and will ever be. You'll also construct a new, spacious but totally impenetrable fortress for master, and so fast he will be able to moves there tomorrow."

    "For everyone outside of Naomi, you, me and master itself, you'll always have been a dragon. They'll think that dragons are a a elusive species of magical being communicating telepathically that, once they hatched, live eternally. As such they rarely reproduce and when they do they generally abandon their eggs, letting them die. And they die fast! It ultimately caused master to find a mysterious, unknown egg one Friday evening. He decided to do everything in his powers to help said egg hatch and, the following Sunday, you hatched, and master was the first being you ever saw. As such, you see master as your parent even if you know, intellectually speaking, that it's not the case and so decided to call him ‘master’. It also created a mental link between the two and you, making him able to telepathically speak to you, but only you."

    "Speaking of master, your new magic will make it so he know all the truth about you, but also what most of the world think is you origin story. It will also make master and anyone loyal to him as eternal as a dragon, but without changing their species."

    Once Jennifer finished her long description of what would befall Jade, said Asian girl was about complain about how long-winded the future kunoichi's ‘nonsense’ had been, but she suddenly felt the tip of her fingers and toes turn into sharp talons, along with new knowledge invading her mind. It now was her turn to feel rage and screened "JEN!" before, in a sudden stroke of genius, adding "TRUTH OR DARE?"

    Jennifer's psyche having already changed too much for her to do anything that would definitively prevent her from becoming Kyle's loyal ninja, choose dare. Jade tried to dare her former friend to do something that would prevent her success, causing Jen to join her in dragonhood, but because part of her transformation was already said to make Jade help Jen become a kunoichi, she was unable to do so. In the end she opted to say "I dare you– to fall hopelessly in love with— MassteRrrrrr!" Finishing her sentence just as her transformation did the same.

    The future kunoichi was already feeling the change in her emotional state, but knew she couldn't confess her new feelings to masters, (and wouldn't) it wasn't proper. As a ninja, any non-professional relationship with her master was forbidden.

    Jade, for her part, had surprisingly turned into a western dragon. She was a quadrupedal being covered in green scales that looked like they were made out of jade, a long snout full of sharp teeth and able to breath fire, long golden horns on the top of her head with a mop of black hairs reminiscent of her former hairdo between them, a long prehensile tail and the two enormous wings one would expect on such a being. She also had no less than four eyes, two on the front of her head with a nearly human vision if you excluded how well she saw at night, and two on the side of it with something more akin to how a pray, like a rabbit, saw the world but, again, with a better night vision. All of said eyes were golden with slited pupils and shone with both great intelligence and a recently gained but deep hatred for Jennifer.

    Trying to forget her sudden funk, Jen slowly turned toward her remaining friend, the now shaking Naomi. "Your turn, now."
