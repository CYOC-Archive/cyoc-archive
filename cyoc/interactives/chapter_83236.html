    Ashley is Just Some Stripper Matt Hired

    "I warned you," Matt said, then smirked, causing Ashley's eyes to grow wide with fright. "I wish that you weren't even a member of this family. I wish that you're just some stripper I hired to entertain me for the night."

    "What?" Ashley said, shocked. This was going too far. She didn't deserve this kind of punishment. She turned and dashed out of the room, then headed to Lisa's room and opened the door, causing her sister to yell out.

    "Who are you? What are you doing in my room?" Lisa demanded.

    "What do you mean 'who am I'?" Ashley asked, then she looked around the smaller room, that just a moment ago she was going to apparently be sharing with her sister. There was only one bed. It was clear that only one person used this bedroom and that person was Lisa. It was true then. Ashley really wasn't part of this family now, was she?

    "Whoa, who's the babe?" Cody asked, walking by in the hallway.

    "She hasn't said her name yet," Lisa said. "I thought that maybe she was one of your girlfriends, but ..."

    "She's with me," Matt said, interrupting. "I hired her for the night."

    Ashley glared at him. She wasn't his property. And just because he had apparently "rented" her for the night, it didn't mean that she was going to perform for him. Not in a million years.

    "You think I could borrow her?" Cody asked Matt.

    Ashley couldn't believe this. She was actually getting hit on by her own brother. Except ... he wasn't her brother anymore, was he? Now they were little more than complete strangers.

    "I'll think about it," Matt said, then pulled Ashley back into his bedroom, closing the door behind them. "See? No one remembers you. So do as I say or I'll make things even worse for you. Although, if you do everything I say," he said, before shrugging. "Maybe I'll reverse some of my wishes."
