    Jason held the skin open as Hana walked over and stepped inside of it. The audience had seen many people exit skins, but this was the first time they saw someone entering. As Hana slid in, her body seemed to impossibly shrink as she conformed to fit Charlie's form. Jason zipped her inside and took off the zipper.

    "There are endless possibilities, and I open them all to you tonight." Jason handled her the zipper, and she wrapped it around her head, just below the hairline. "Remember, there isn't just one way to try out another life."

    Pulling the zipper, the top of her skull popped open, and Chad came out dressed in a suit. The crowd cheered loudly as he impossibly grew from the opening. Chad picked up his niece's skin and shook it as if he was shaking dust out of a towel. It flopped in the air.

    Chad held her close to the ground and stretched open her head. "Grand finalé. Ready Terry?" Terry seemed to hesitate. "Come on, my sister would only let them volunteer if they didn't see any if, and I quote, 'the debauchery you and your friends get up to.' Make sure you agree what is and isn't allowed to be done with your body and that you lend it to someone you trust. You can add layers like I did if you want to try a variety of things, but I won't be letting anyone out till tomorrow at noon."
