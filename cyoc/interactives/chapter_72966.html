    Rain's Diary

    Branch Full view
    added 10 years ago O

    Rain read her diary, learning about who she was.

    She learned that her name was actually Jennifer Marie Stewart, and her sister was Allison Nicole Stewart.

    She learned that she was older than her sister by only a year, and that as the two grew up, they were closest
    friends. One day at school however, Sunny was heard singing a song she had heard on television by a teacher,
    and it wasn't long before she was entered into various talent shows. As the years went by she earned a little
    notoriety. Jennifer grew a little Jealous however, reading in her diary that she felt her parents paid little
    attention to her. Soon it became obvious she started acting out in an attempt to get her parents notice.

    It worked for a while, her black outfits and makeup drew concern from her parents for a few weeks. Then things
    went back to normal, however Rain didn't care, she had new goth friends who respected her. Friends which
    helped her come up with her new name.

    As time moved on she came to love her new darker personality, until one day she had a fight with her parents.
    Someone had hit the family car the first time Rain drove after getting her license, but her parents had blamed
    her for the incident. She ran away from home at the age of 16. She had an older friend drive her a few miles
    away to live with some older friends. Spending most of her time partying and drinking. she even 'experimented'
    with a few things during this phase.

    After a while though, her parents found her, they patched things up and Rain went home. Things were better
    than ever, (Her parents ever referred to her as Rain now, which had never happened before.) and according to
    her diary her mother made a joke about the girls being so different Allison should have been called Sunny, and
    the nickname stuck.

    Rain finished out high-school, a year later after missing a year, but she studied hard and passed almost top
    of her class. Soon she was off to college for a nursing degree. Spending her school days in more, average
    attire. She shared an apartment with her sister sunny, Sunny's boyfriend Jay and Jay's friend Dan.

    According to her diary dan tried a few times to flirt with Rain, often in silly embarassing ways, and in the
    past she thought him a 'potential passtime' if she ever felt she was in the mood.

    As for relationships, she had a few short term relationships that quickly got physical, however she always
    used protection, and lots of one night stands during her 'wild year.'

    Rain hadn't even considered sex since her encounter with Quaid, but now there was a little curious voice in
    the back of her head...
