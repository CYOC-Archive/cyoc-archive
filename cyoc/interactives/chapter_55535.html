    "Not in the way one would think," Amy spoke, "while Kurse's death in the short term restored Fox and Elise... the fact that they had let Kurse in and that Kurse put all of her attention into mutating them, each of the four beings they were gained very independent personalities, personalities that could not easily survive inside an individual being."
