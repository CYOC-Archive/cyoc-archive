    Laura Wants to be a Diaper Model

    added 3 years ago AR Toddler

    Laura smiled at Anna.

    “I wanna be a toddler,” she said firmly. Anna clapped her hands joyfully.

    “Really?” she asked. Laura nodded. “Oh, honey, that makes me so happy! So happy that I think we should go out to celebrate. What do you think?”

    “Yes,” Laura chirped. Anna removed Laura’s nightgown and slipped a sundress over her shoulders, then put Laura’s hair into pigtails.

    “Oh, you’re too cute,” Anna sighed. She picked Laura up and nuzzled her. “I can’t believe you’re mine.” Laura giggled.

    “I’m just happy to not worry about work or bills or even using the potty,” she replied. Anna beamed at her.

    “You’re right about that! You don’t need to be potty trained for years. In fact, you don’t need to start until you’re five, at least. If you want, I could even keep you in diapers until you’re seven!”

    “Hmm.” Laura thought on that. “Maybe. We’ll see how it goes.” Anna nodded.

    “Of course. Baby led potty training. That’s a thing, right? It’s just that my baby is a bit more mature than most.” Laura’s stomach rumbled. “Oh! That’s right, I need to take you to get something to eat!” Anna set Laura on the ground and began the process of getting ready herself.

    After Anna had left the bedroom to use the bathroom, Laura looked around. Spotting a bag of baby supplies, she walked over to it, toddling due to her thick diaper. She dug around in it and eventually pulled out a pacifier, which she popped into her mouth and sucked on eagerly.

    “Adorable!” Anna gushed. Laura turned. Anna whipped out her phone and took a picture of Laura. “Wow, you could be a model!”

    “There are toddler models?” Laura asked. Anna nodded.

    “Oh, yes. Mostly diaper models.” Laura cocked her head curiously. “I’ve got a friend that works as an agent for models. I know you said you didn’t like the idea of working, but being a diaper model would be easy. All you’d do is sit in diapers, looking cute, and probably using them.”

    “Hmm.” Laura sucked on her pacifier as she thought. Being a toddler would be pretty boring. It might be nice to take up something where all she has to do is wet and mess herself. And the money would be useful for both her and Anna. She nodded. “I like it!”

    “You want to be a diaper model?”

    “Yes!” Laura felt a pressure in her bladder. She got to her feet and squatted, wetting herself. “I’m good at using diapers!”

    “You certainly are! You’re just the perfect daughter,” Anna said ecstatically. She picked Laura up and began to change her again. As she changed Laura’s diaper, a thoughtful expression settled on her face. “Hmm, now that I think about it, it might be difficult to suddenly have you as my daughter, when I’ve never been pregnant and you’re technically missing.”

    “Oh. Yeah.”

    “How about we stop by the magic shop after breakfast? The owner should be able to help us with that, right?”

    “Probably.”

    “Excellent!” Anna finished changing Laura’s diaper. She picked up her new daughter and grabbed a diaper bag, then headed out.
