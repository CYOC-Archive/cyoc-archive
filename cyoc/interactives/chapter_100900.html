    Transform or Dare?

    Girlier and Sweeter

    "Time's up," Kyle said gleefully just before Jennifer could make up her mind. With the return of his voice, Jennifer came back to her senses.

    "Whatever, like the curse is real anyway," she scoffed.

    "'Like, whatever,'" Kyle responded in falsetto. "You're such a trendy girl, Jennifer. You go along with the flow in your little cheerleading pack. I bet you'd do anything to stay popular."

    "It beats being a little freak like you, dweeb." Jennifer wanted to hang up the phone to punctuate her declaration, but something was holding her back. She paid it little mind, though. At least staying on the line allowed her more opportunities to mock him.

    "I think it's time you had a change of heart," Kyle said, his voice suddenly turning sinister. "After all, there are other ways to get people to like you. I'm going to start with your transformation. You will change into a super girly, loli girl. All of your clothes and your room will change to reflect your new style and you won't be able to wear anything that doesn't go with the super girly style you now have.

    "And for your dare, I dare you to say transform and dare to me. But when I say dare, you have to dare me to use the curse on you again and you'll take dare as your option."

    "Yeah right", said Jennifer. "As if I..." Suddenly, she felt a shiver as her preppy clothes began to dissolve before her eyes, leaving her in nothing but a frilly pink bra and a pair of pink silk panties that had little white bows. Her blonde hair gained enough volume to spread out past her shoulders as it grew down to her butt in huge, soft ringlets. Then her hair became pink at the tips as highlights appeared elsewhere. Her eyes seemed to grow slightly larger, more expressive, and turned bright blue.

    Then clothing appeared on her body. A poofy pink dress with white lace. Its knee-length skirt was filled with layers of petticoats. The dress had puffed shoulders, but long, white opera gloves appeared on her hands and forearms.
    Jennifer looked at her new appearance in shock and horror. She screamed and was about to tell Kyle off, but suddenly, her mouth acted on it's own.

    "Transform or dare?", said Jennifer against her will.

    "Dare", said a triumphant Kyle.

    "I dare you to use the curse on me.", she said with tears forming in her eyes. This was horrible. There was no telling what Kyle would make her do. She was now dressed like she was going to a tea party instead of High School. Kyle was probably going to make her do something that would make her look like a slut.

    "Alright then", said Kyle. "Transform or dare?"

    Jennifer tried to say something , but it was like her lips were literally sealed tight. When time was up she finally yelled, "You sick freak! I'm going to make you pay for this."

    "Actually, I think you'll thank me", said Kyle. "Because for your dare, I dare you to have a personality that fits your new look. You'll now be a sweet, energetic, happy girly girl who loves the loli look and is super nice. Oh, and you won't remember that I've used the curse on you. Let's see just how unpopular you really are as a nice person. And for your transformation, you'll become "

    Jennifer was about to retort when suddenly, her mind fogged up. She continued to stand silently as her personality was overwritten. After a while, a bright smile appeared on her face and she giggled.

    "Sorry", she replied cheerfully. "I lost my train of thought. Who is this again?" But there was no reply as the line went dead. Jenny shrugged her shoulders and hung up before grabbing her plush kitty purse and skipping off to the bus stop. She was looking forward to today.
