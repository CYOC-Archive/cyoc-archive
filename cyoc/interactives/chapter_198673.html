    Changes in the Afterglow

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago A AR O Clothes Reality alteration

    Ollie fell back against his pillow and basked in the warm wonder of the afterglow. He never imagined experiencing something like his again, let alone as a completely different thing. He felt spent, his young body exhausted as he closed his eyes and breathed through his new beak. He didn't notice the ring warm on his finger as subtle changes traced over his body, making him leaner, slightly younger and yet stronger. His talons flexed as his sneakers shifted and fell away soundlessly leaving them bare as his room reworked around him, his bed becoming a nest. Soon, he lay in a space that looked like it belonged to a young teenaged boy, but one that was distinctly a young teen or even preteen. A few clothes scattered on the ground, an open book turned to a page with a naked female bird on it next to him. The ring warmed one more time as it faded away, leaving Ollie transformed and now transported into a whole new world and life
