    Rem walked out of the bathroom, grinning the largest smile she ever had. Walking around, there were so many opportunities for change. What Emily and Rick both agreed on, and the Rem had inherited, was that transforming people and reality along side it was hot as hell. Rem saw families, groups of teens, individuals trying to shop, and all sorts of workers.

    Rem looked over the camera, then at the crowds of people too. That's when she decided what she wanted to do. She figured that fusion would be a little bit too difficult to keep track of if she wanted to fix things. Plus, fusion meant she'd only be loosing potential targets! Rem was set on going back to the mode that she felt that Emily and Rick had learned the most about; Role swapping.

    Rem wandered down the mall's hall, looking at each and every opportunity she had.

    Nearby to a shoe store, Rem spotted a couple on a shopping trip, though one was clearly enjoying it more than the other. The girl was a short, busty blonde who wore a cute crop top and jean combo. She was currently staring at the shoe store's window, gawking at some different pairs. Next to her was her boyfriend. He was holding her hand, but was looking at his phone with a solid frown. He had shaggy brown hair, a sports jersey and jeans.

    Rem felt very passionately about romance, likely because her mind was a fusion of a loving couple, so seeing a potentially unhappy relationship made her want to play match maker. After looking around, she found the best option in a younger teenaged girl.

    She was super short, with long black hair. She seemed to be Asian, with dark brown eyes, and wore a cute frilly dress. She was also shopping, though looking through the window of a dress shop nearby. Rem snapped a sneaky photo of her with the role swap mode, then turned to the couple. She made sure to avoid catching his gf in the shot before taking the second picture.

    Instantly, the couple were totally improved, at least according to Rem. The blonde was in the same position, still browsing away, then looked up to her boyfriend to point happily.
    "What about these baby?" She asked, unaware she was asking an entirely new person.

    "I think you'd look great baby." The new man laughed.

    He was still Asian, but now had short hair, was very tall, and showed off some muscle mass. He wore a nice button up with slick, tight pants, showing that this new boyfriend was much more into fashion when compared with the old one. Speaking of, rem turned to see that the former bf was changed too.

    She had long brown hair, as the curly locks reached her tight new jeans. Her shirt had a portal companion cube on it, and it showed off her c cup breasts. She was smiling, browsing her phone as she walked along. Instead of looking for dresses, she walked by the store, and walked into the nearby video game shop.

    "Now everyone gets to be happy." Rem chuckled to herself.

    Rem looked around, and saw countless opportunity. She knew Rick and Emily would agree judging by how turned on she was... or, was she both Rick and Emily? Were they now different? Did she even exist. Rem VERY quickly pushed those thoughts aside, deciding to focus even harder on the camera of change while she scoped the mall for more potential changes to make.
