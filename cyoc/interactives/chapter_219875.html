    Skinsuit from an Isekai'd World

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous 2 months ago O Male Magic

    "Ahhhhhhhhh!!!" The sounds made by a human body in the face of approaching death seemed an impossibility. It was a scream shoveled from the deepest fears of your soul. I hit the ground at speeds that should have made me a red splatter on the forest ground. Before that could happen, my crashing body gradually halted as a magical force cushioned my fall. I landed with my flesh and bones intact. Although, my sanity might have taken a beating since I just faced death twice today!

    "The fucker," I cursed at the heavens above. I know he could hear me.

    I looked around and saw towering trees around me. There are trees as far as the eyes can see, and their foliage nearly covers the skies. Of all the places that son of a bitch could put me, he placed me in a place where I could die any minute. The forest was home to blood-vicious beasts, poisonous plants, and vector insects that carried filarial diseases. This place was a death trap.

    Although... It doesn't seem like the case. It was quiet where I landed, except for the calls of birds and scurrying critters. It doesn't seem like I'm in a rainforest, so the chance of getting vector-borne diseases was somewhat reduced. The only problem now was poisonous plants, and I didn't know batshit about what plants to avoid. It is probably safe to avoid touching them while I find the nearest form of civilization.

    As I trekked across beaten paths and forest undergrowths, I wondered what the orb given to me by that god was.

    [Status!]

    "What the fuck!?"

    In front of me was a status window, much like the HUDs from the various RPGs I played. Instead of seeing it on a screen, the window hovered over me. It has my name, age, and gender - as well as the single skill under its tab.

    [Skinsuit Touch Lvl.99]

    "Skinsuit touch? What the hell is that?"

    The status window expanded to another window detailing the "Skinsuit Touch."

    [Skinsuit Touch allows you to turn any creature into a skinsuit. A skinsuit is a rigid and hollow body you can enter and wear. In doing so, you'll take control of the skinsuit and assume their appearance. It won't give you access to their memories, but you'll have their skills, powers, blessing, mannerisms, and muscle memory.]

    "How the hell is that going to help me leave this forest?" I walked for hours, but the forest went for miles. The sun was at its peak, and I was parched beyond relief.
