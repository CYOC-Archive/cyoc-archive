    “Ye-yeah... don't worry,” Jeff says, “I am just tired and not in the mood”.

    Mike sighs, “okay, I won't pressure you,” he says.

    Jeff/Jane relaxes, “thanks,” she says, “I am really tired, why don't we head to bed,” with that the “woman” gets up from the couch and walks out of the living room.
