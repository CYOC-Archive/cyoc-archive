    "I don't know why you can say that. How am I supposed to act like your mother?"

    "You are still our beloved Jeremy, but you also have June's body. You will know how to act like a woman, don't worry about that."

    "And what about the soccer team? And my parents?" she said.

    Ty snapped his fingers again. "I already fixed that, don't worry and enjoy being our mother."

    "Ty..." she gulped. "I don't know if I want to be your mother... I mean..."

    "Of course dude! You will do a great job, I know!"

    "Yeah, forget about your old life for now and try to live as our mother." Dustin also tried to help, but Jeremy wasn't sure yet. "I can't wait to see what happens from now on!"
