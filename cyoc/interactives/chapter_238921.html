    After discussing things for a bit the two come up with a plan which they put into action. They waited for a bit for the four identical girls got together by the pool before turning the Chronivac on them.

    What they had put into the Chronivac was a mental change that made it so the girls now thought they were members of the cheerleading squad of the school, here to cheer on the boys.

    What the boys did not notice were the other changes that happened as a result of the changes made to the girls minds.

    Since there were now supposed to be cheerleaders here a car with the rest of the squad pulled up in front of the hotel and the girls went to check in. On the way they were met by the coach Gracey Greys who now, because of staff shortages was the coach of both the boy's team as well as the cheer squad.

    "I'm glad you could make it girls" Gracey said as she made sure they all got checked in and to their rooms. As she started to leave she said "get into your swim suits and we will all meet at the pool to talk things out. The quads are already there so make it quick.'

    Back at the pool the boys were thinking they had made the perfect change so that the girls would be hanging around for some time.
