    Anna and Greg drove in silence, things in the caves got dicey but they were able to put a stop to the supernatural threat.

    “Would you just say it!” Anna said in frustration.

    “There’s nothing to say—she was a very beautiful woman…before she turned into a monstrous sex goddess with four faces.” Greg replied.

    “She had me under a spell, I wasn’t in control of myself!” She growled.

    “I believe you!—she was very…persuasive.” He quipped while making boob squeezing gestures.

    When a knock from the back of the van Anna gave Greg a look and he blushed.

    “You still have her?!” She asked in shock.

    “I couldn’t just leave her like that, we’ve got stuff at the lab that can fix her!” He defended.

    In the back a lust crazed, naked Kayla O’Neil was rubbing herself against the glass window that connected to the front seats. She moaned and shouted lewd things at the two agents.

    “You’re dragging her to the lab, I can’t believe I’m going to say this but I’ve had enough sexy women for the day.” She said.

    “Fuck that! I’m having containment bring her in, she’ll rip me apart!” He replied.

    “At least you’ll die happy. ‘Death by Sex’ I’ll be sure to put that on your gravestone.” Anna said with a laugh.

    Greg was not amused and suddenly hit the breaks sending the sex crazed blonde flying in the back, knocking her out.

    “¿Qué carajo Greg?” The werewolf shouted.

    “Calm down Anna, she’ll be fine. Probably.” He said and started driving again.

    When the two agents got back to S.P.E.A.R they watched as the containment team wrangled Kayla inside a glass container and brought her down to the labs.
    Anna went to Kayla’s room once her treatment was finished.

    “You are like so not getting into Beta Beta Epsilon!” She shouted, back to her normal self.

    “That’s fair.” Anna laughed. “Once you’re cleared to leave you will be taken back to campus once the cleanup is finished.

    “I can’t believe our sorority mother was a sex demon thing.” The blonde simply stated.

    “Don’t worry, you and the rest of the campus will be safe now.” The black haired woman assured.

    “This is like so effed up! I don’t think I can go back there, I don’t think I can go back anywhere. What if some monster tries to kill me again?!” The coed cried.

    “We have a special supernatural counseling program to help you readjust to normal life.” Anna offered.

    “—or you could join us? Help protect the public from threats they don’t even know about!” Greg said imitating the Director’s recruitment pitch.

    “I don’t think that’s a good idea, she’d need tones of training and I don’t think she has the mental fortitude for—“ Anna was interrupted by Kayla.

    “—are you like saying I’m stupid?!” She asked.

    “Wrrowr, this one’s got some fight in her, reminds me of someone I know.” Greg said before being chased out by his partner.

    “Listen, I’m not saying you’re not smart or anything. It’s just this job is dangerous and it’s not for everyone, just get some rest and I’ll schedule you for some counseling.” Anna said and left the blonde to rest.
