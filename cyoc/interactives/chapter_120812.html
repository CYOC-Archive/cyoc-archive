    After their latest tryst with each other, Virginia was lying atop of her man, her head snuggled against his chest. She sighed blissfully before getting up, separating her from him. Harold looked at her smiling as she changed back to her normal human form from behind as she walked to her dresser. She looked at him from over her shoulder and smiled as she said, "As much fun as it would be to continue our little romp, I think it'd be a good idea to check on Melissa. She's probably wondering where we're at."

    Harold continued to look at her as she started to get dressed. "I suppose you have a point," he said thoughtfully. "Although, I think I left all of my clothes back at my place...so..."

    Virginia smirked as she threw one of her spare robes at him in response. "Uh-uh, mister. You're not getting away that easily."

    He picked up the robe as he started to change back to his human self. He looked at it as he sized it up on his body. "Well, I suppose it'll work. Looks a bit small if you ask me."

    "Well, it's meant for me," Virginia replied. "And you are a bit bigger than I am, even in human form." She paused as she stared at him, licking her lips slightly. She turned around towards the door as she said, "Let's get out of here, before we get things started again." She walked out the door with Harold following closely behind, putting on the robe before running out.
    ------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Melissa was still eating her cereal with a rather goofy grin on her face when Virginia and Harold came walking into the house. After noticing her, Harold quickly and quietly walked upstairs to get a proper change of clothes while Virginia went into the dining room.

    "About time you two came in," Melissa said with a rather wry smile on her face. "Did you two have a good time? I mean, I could hear the yowling coming from the backyard..."

    Virginia coughed as her face went red and then said, "Well, your father and I...well...um...we enjoyed ourselves." Melissa smiled wryly as she continued to eat her breakfast. It wasn't long before Harold joined the two ladies fully dressed.

    "Good morning, Mel," Harold said rather cheerfully.

    "Morning, dad," Mel replied. "Someone's in a good mood this morning."

    "Well, it is a good morning, and we have some big news to share," Harold said as he sat down in a nearby chair. Virginia sat down near him. "As you may remember from last night, we're getting married. Well, we've decided to get married this December."

    Mel accidentally choked on her cereal as she heard the noise, and after a bit of coughing, she asked, "That soon? That's only like two months away."

    "We know," Virginia said, holding Harold's hand. "It's just...we love each other so much, we couldn't wait."

    "Wow," was the only response that Mel could give.

    "So, what's going on with you and Trevor?" Virginia asked, noting that Mel was still normal.

    "Oh, that reminds me," Mel said as she remembered, "Trevor invited me to come to the Halloween dance tonight."

    "Oooh," Virginia squealed, "that sounds so exciting. I'll be there, but just to act as a chaperone."

    Harold looked at the two women and asked, "What's this Halloween dance you keep mentioning?"

    Virginia smiled and said, "It's one of the big dances that the high school holds. It's up there with homecoming and prom. It's the one time that we allow our were students to come in their fursonas, and in costume if they wish. It's not uncommon for students to be in their fursonas and in costume at the same time. Us teachers go to make sure that the students behave themselves while at the dance." She paused to think for a moment and then turned to Mel and said, "And whatever you do, don't go into the woods behind the school during the dance."

    Mel tilted her head a bit and asked, "Why not?"

    Virginia leaned back in her chair and said, "Remember how your dad and I acted last night? Well, for these kinds of dances, it's not that unusual for the kids to go into the woods for some love-making. Our jobs as teachers is to make sure that doesn't happen at the dance. We can't stop the romantic feelings, but we can make sure that the expression of such love happen at the proper place. Just don't be surprised if there are a few kids there when the night's almost done." Mel blushed when she heard this, and Virginia noticed this. "Don't worry," she said to Mel, "we'll make sure that nothing happens to you that you don't want."

    Mel smiled and said, "Thank you. Speaking of the dance, Trevor's going to be here at around noon to pick me up."

    Harold nodded and said, "I see. Any idea as to what you'll go as?"

    Mel shook her head as she replied, "Not really. I'm sure we can think of something."

    Harold shrugged and then turned to Virginia as he asked, "And could I join you, Virginia? I'm sure that an extra pair of eyes would be helpful."

    "Sure," Virginia said as she turned to Harold and smiled, "but no fun until after the dance is over."

    Mel rolled her eyes and said, "You guys, get a room."

    Virginia stroked Harold's chin and said, "Mel has a point, and I do believe we do have a room upstairs so..."

    Mel hid her face in her hand as she said, "Could you guys wait until after I leave?" Harold and Virginia chuckled at Mel's rather embarrassed response.
