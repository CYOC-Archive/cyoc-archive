    From Queen to Princess. Lili and Cece's day at the park

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by O 4 years ago AR Mental

    Lilith and Circe were certainly enjoying themselves as they raced from one ride to the other. As Circe had promised each ride had transformed the girls into various anthros. Already the girls had been ponies, cats, dogs, and even a lizard. While they giggled and took pictures, they quickly used their magic to change back. Half the fun of being a magical child was using that magic on themselves. As the two now happily ate some ice cream, Circe wondered what else they could do.

    "Having fun demon queen," she smirked.

    Lilith paused. A Queen? That didnt make any sense. Queens were old and stuffy, having to make rules. Being a princess was so much more fun. "That's demon princess," Lilith snorted. "Princess Lili of the demons."

    "Your lucky," Circe sighed as she took another bite of her ice cream. "I wish I was a princess."

    Lilith now gazed around Circes park. Rides, food stands, and games surrounded the two girls. As Lilith now gazed at the far end of the park her eyes grew wide. "Well a princess needs a castle," she stated. "Isnt that a castle right there?"

    Circe perked up as she looked at the fiberglass castle that housed her office. "Your right I am a princess."

    "Princess Cece ruler of the fun park," Lili exclaimed.

    Cece giggled as she looked at Lili. It was odd, she remembered that the two of them did not like eachother when they were big but she couldn't remember why. The hourglass had transformed the two witches into naive young girls, albeit young girls with alot of magical power.

    "Hey Lili did you know I have a crown," she whispered.

    Lili's eyes went wide at this news. "You do," she gasped. "Can I see it?"

    Cece nodded grabbing her young friends hand as they raced towards Circes castle. "Cmon demon lets go see Cece's crown." The demon could only nod as it barreled after the two young witches, Circes anthro followers jumping out of the way as it sped after the girls.

    Circe had decorated her office to resemble a throne room out of pride. Before being reduced to a child Circe had used it to impress any that journeyed there. Now though it appeared to the two 6 year olds as the most magnificent thing either had ever seen.

    "Wow," Lili whispered in awe. "Its so pretty and big."

    Cece nodded,placing her small hands on her hips as she beamed with pride. "I made it myself," she boasted. "Look I even made a throne."

    Lili looked at Cece curiously, not quite understanding what it was until Cece pointed towards the large golden chair. "Oh cool," she exclaimed. "Can I sit in it?"

    Cece nodded, taking Lili's hand as they raced towards the golden throne. Leaping into the air the two girls landed on the velvet pillow with a small thud, giggling as they began to make themselves comfortable. Fortunately for the two, they could both easily sit in the throne together. Their small feet dangling off the edge, coming nowhere near touching the ground.

    "See this is the crown I made," Cece said happily. Lili watched as Cece placed a golden crown on her head, stifling a giggle as it slipped down over the young girls eyes.

    "Its too big," Lili giggled.

    Cece huffed as she now fiddled with the crown finally getting it to teeter on the top of her head. "When I grow up it will fit me," she stated.

    Lili smiled pausing as she now thought about what Cece had said. "Cece," she asked. "Do you remember being big?"

    Cece paused. They had been big girls hadn't they? As she tried to think she found it difficult. "I dont know," she mumbled. "I think we were big girls. And we were mad at eachother. Why were we mad?"

    Lili shrugged as she tried to think about her relationship with Cece. "I dont know either," she said quietly. "Wasnt it because of Baba the butthead and that mean girl?"

    Cece giggled. It seemed to make sense. "Yeah Babas a butthead," she laughed. "Maybe she ends up littler than us."

    "So that's why we were mad," Lili asked. Cece could only shrug, she couldn't remember why her and Lili would ever be mad at one another. Sure Lili was weird and had weird demon friends but she was weird too. It only seemed to make sense that they would be friends.

    "I bet whatever it was it was stupid," Cece huffed.

    Lili agreed as the two 6 year olds began to drum their dangling legs softly against the throne. "So can I be a princess with you then," Lili asked.

    "Yeah let's play princesses," Cece exclaimed. Taking the crown off her head she now rested it on Lili's head giggling as it fell down over her eyes.

    "This is going to be the best game of princesses ever," Lili squealed. Cece couldn't agree more as she now asked her followers to bring her and Lili juice boxes. As the two 6 year olds laughed and talked they didnt realize that the hourglass had finally finished its magic. Instead of two adult witches ready to destroy eachother, the two of them were now innocent 6 year olds interested in playing with one another and with little knowledge of what they used to be.
