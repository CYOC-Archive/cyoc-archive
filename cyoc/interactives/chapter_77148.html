    After looking over the choices, Ronald saw one that finally caught his eye. “What’s ‘The Enchanted Forest’ like?” He loved camping in the woods, so this was a natural draw to him.

    “Ah, that area had just opened to the public today.” The uniformed woman said. “That’s where you can become and live among the woodland races; elves, satyrs, dryads; even witches and wizards. Would you like to be our first guest there?”

    “Of course!” Ronald enthusiastically answered.

    When Ronald and the woman reached the lobby of the Enchanted Forest, he was given a paper. “Read this over,” the woman said, “sign here on the bottom, and choose what being you wish to become. A liaison will then meet you shortly to change you into your chosen being and accompany you through your experience.”
