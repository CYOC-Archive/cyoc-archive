    You decide calling your friend Jake is the best option. This is too much to handle, you need someone else there, to help you figure out what the hell’s going on.

    You quickly walked home, and dialed Jake.“Hey Jake, you need to meet me at my house ASAP. Something insane just happened…”

    15 minutes later, your best friend since elementary school arrived at your house, looking confused as ever. You explain what happened, and he’s got some questions already. Where did it come from? Can you influence what it changes in the slightest? Does it have to be around your neck for it to work?

    “I honestly don’t know man. All I heard was that creepy ass voice, and that’s it.” You say. “Well should we, I don’t know, do a tiny shake to see if anything changes?” Said Jake. “I’m not sure, that seems kinda risky…” you say. You’re not sure how this thing even got here, and terrible things could happen if you don’t make the right move. “Well we need to find out if it works somehow. Give me that.” Says Jake, as he grabs it from your hands. Before you can even stop him, he gives it a small shake, and you both black out.

    You wake up, with you and Jake laying on the sidewalk. You both stagger to your feet, and try and collect your thoughts. As you squint your eyes to help them readjust to light, you notice something peculiar about Jake’s eyes. They’re green. Jake has had brown eyes his whole life.

    “Uh, Jake, I think it worked. Look in the mirror…” you say. He walks over to your foyer mirror, and looks in shock. Everything else is the same as always, the only different thing about him is his eye color, now being a pale green.
    “Woahhh that is trippy” says Jake. Why did he do that so recklessly? This thing can screw up your lives forever, and he shook it “Just to see what would happen”? That is
    INSANE. “Jake what the hell!? You could’ve gotten us killed!” You shout in anger. “What? It’s not like we’re in danger or anything. I did us a favor! Now we know it works!” Jake says, defending himself. “Man, give me that back. I can’t let you hold it anymore.” You say, slowly walking over to him. Jake slowly backs up, but you suddenly burst forward and grab onto the bottom of the crystal. He starts yelling at you, and you yell back at him as you both tug it back and forth, shaking the crystal so much it starts to glow.
    Then you both realize you’ve messed up.

    “Wait no no NO NOO STTTOOOOO-“
    Everything goes black.
