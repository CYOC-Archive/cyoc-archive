    Jared just rolls his eyes when he hears Jeff's whine and plea, “I regret to have allowed our minds to be connected,” he says, “be thankful that I allow you to stick around. I can easily make you permanently mute, disconnect our minds or just simply make you disappear,” the former human boy says, he then smirks, “and no, you will be staying as you are, permanently!”

    Jeff can't believe what he is hearing, what happened to his little brother? Did the comment he wrote into the Chronivac somehow corrupted him? Jeff is about to protest, but stops himself, not wanting Jared to make his situation worse.

    Jared grins when Jeff does not respond, though due to their mind's connection, the former human boy can sense that Jeff would rather protest. “Smart choice... brother,” the ten-year-old says with a chuckle, “now, I don't want to hear anything from you until I address you again. It is time for me to call my two buddies over to show them my power.” Jared grabs his phone from his discarded jeans, “I'll make mom get rid of the pesky clothes, a half human half horse hybrid like me does not need mortal clothes.” The newly created centaur then first dials the number of his pal “Dean”, simply telling Dean to hurry over, he does the same with his second pal “Zoran”.

    Zoran and Dean seperatly find themselves overcome by a huge urge to head over to Jared's place, even though they had chores to do.

    Meanwhile, Jared makes his mother throw all of his clothing into the trash, at first the woman was shocked to see her young son's new appearance, but with the help of a little bit of “magical convincing”, she simply shrugs and does as told.

    After two hours, Dean and Zoran arrive and they head straight to Jared's room, they knock on the door.

    “Enter,” Jared says, grinning at his two friends as they enter.

    “You called us over, Ja...,” both boys start, then stop when they see Jared.

    “Ja-Jared... what the hell,” Dean asks.

    “Wh-what happened to you,” Zoran adds.

    Jared's grin grows wider at the sight of his friend's surprised expressions, “something wonderful,” he says, “close the door and I'll explain.”

    Dean and Zoran close the door to Jared's room and then simply just stand there, frozen in shock staring at Jared, who towers over them, thanks to his stallion body.

    Jared then explains to the two what has happened, how he found this strange “game” on his brother's laptop, how he out of fun entered “Jared Rules The World” into the program and suddenly found himself gifted with a huge power which grants him powerful transformation powers.

    Dean and Zoran look at each other, both thinking that they are just dreaming, they then turn back to Jared, “h-how did you found out about y-your powers,” Dean asks, unable to hide his fear, Zoran too has a fearful expression on his face.

    Jared grins, he explains what has happened, “after I tested a few little things on my dumb big and annoying brother,” the boy continues, “I decided that, I'd need a much more suitable appearance. Something that signals others that I'M in charge, so Jeff has become my horse part.”

    His friend's eyes widen in shock, “wh-why...?”

    “Because, he is... I mean WAS an ass,” Jared says, his stallion tail swishing a little, showing his frustration. The former human boy then smirks as he adds, “well, now he really is nothing BUT a ass... MY ass,” the boy laughs.

    Dean, who is a black haired boy with an athletic built, brown eyes and a natural brown tan, due to being half Mexican, looks fearfully at his friend, “s-so... why did you call us over,” he asks nervously.

    Zoran, who has short blond hair, a white tan, freckles around his nose, blue eyes and an athletic body, “y-yeah... wh-why did you call us over,” he asks.

    Jared grins, “to use my powers on you of course,” he says, “BUT instead of forcing you into becoming something, I'll give you the opportunity to choose to become something you truly want to be.”

    Both boys look at each other, “wh-what if we are happy the way we are,” Dean cautiously asks.

    Jared's grin turns into an evil smirk, “then I'LL choose for you,” he says, walking over to his two friends, “you will transform into something, no matter if you want to or not.” Jared stares down at his friends, an evil smirk on his face, “so, what will it be,” he asks.
