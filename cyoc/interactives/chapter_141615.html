    Rose laid on the ground, she was happy that she was a unicorn, but two things disappointed her.

    The first one is the fact that she expected to become a bipedal unicorn, to have a somewhat human appearance, the second thing was that she expected to cast some form of magic, since unicorns were considered to be one of the prime examples of magic users.

    Rose looked at the empty vial beneath her feet, she tried to move it delicately to check if there was an additional description along with the instructions and side effects, she stomped one of her hooves angrily, and when she did it, her horn began to glow with a silver light, and the vial started floating.

    Rose was shocked, did she do it? She tilted her head a little, and the vial moved! She was controlling it! Rose then inspected the vial, noticing a small piece of paper on it, using her new telekinetic powers, she took off the piece of paper, unfolded it and slowly read it.

    "Due to the complex nature of wereunicorns, the effects of the transformation will split in five parts." It read, "Each one occuring after exposure to moon light." It continued, Rose then started reading the parts, "On the first night, the subject will have a bipedal form instead of a quadrupedal form. On the second night, the subject's magical capabilities will improve. On the third night, the subject will be capable to heal any wound or disease and break any type of curse. On the fourth night, the subject will be capable of changing forms and gender at will. On the fifth night, the subject will be capable of granting wishes once per year. (Note: the wishes will only affect the subject or the victim, not its surroundings.)"

    After reading this, Rose was shocked, she could do all of these, and the only thing she needed to do was to wait. She was thinking about searching for Mary, but she was scared, what if a werebeast or another unicorn finds her out and mates with her? She would be permanently stuck as a unicorn.
