Draw, on guard, stab, slice, sheath. They would protect Sammie and Rosa and everyone else. Draw, on guard, stab, slice, sheath. They would -

    "I'm fine, I've felt a lot better since I - y'know - that accident."

    "Call me Susie. And, to be fair... I don't think I do. I think the Authority changed me." Miki stared at the dean's short stature and pigtails. "Like, mentally. I feel like a different person.

    "I can't focus on anything, I'm losing taste for the things I like, I feel so cruel. It's like I've become my own daughter."

    "Susie, let me ask you something. What did you study at university?" She thought back.

    "That was Chekhov, and it was actually about gooseberries. He got it published in Russian Voice in 1898." She responded instantly.

She was sitting right next to Anacostia, a ~13 year old bunny girl with a dress that made her look like a baby doll, and she was going in on some mac and cheese. Ashley kissed Ana on the cheek, seemingly not noticing that it was covered in cheese sauce.
