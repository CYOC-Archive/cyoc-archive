    Jeff's last stats flashed as those of a pickpocket as the computer faded from existence-taking the Chronivac with it. Jeff looked down as he began to change into his new form. His clothes changed from his loose fitting t-shirt and blue jeans to a rough cotton tunic and a pair of cotton pants. His shoes morphed from engineered tennis shoes to medieval boots. His hair grew longer and dirtier. Within seconds he had transformed from a 21st century teen to a medieval pickpocket. As he took in his drastic change in attire and surroundings, he began to feel gravity pulling him down. He looked to the ground and saw he was about to fall on one of the many roofs of the city. Without any time to think, his body reacted. He pulled up his legs as he landed and rolled on the roof like he had seen on so many action movies. He stood back up and looked down on himself. 'How the hell did I do that?' he wondered. He looked around the roof he was on. It was the only solid looking roof on the street he was on. He looked to his left and saw the tower that seemed to defy gravity. To his right was the elaborate gate. Directly in front of him was the stone castle. Behind him was the road leading into the village. Jeff looked at all the people bustling below him. He had to wonder if any other's had come across the Chronivac and were randomly sent to Thayluric. He blinked. 'Thayluric? Where did I get that from?' As soon as he had thought it, more memories of this new world sprang into his head. The Tower to the left was the Wizzard's Tower, the gate to the right led to the Dwarven Mines, and the stone castle was where King Malraich resided. Jeff also remembered facts about the Adventurers, the nearby forest, and various hiding places throughout the Thayluric Bowl.
    Sometime during all of this recollection, a peasant had noticed Jeff standing on the roof.
    "Oy! Get down from there ya stupid boy!" he shouted. Jeff looked down at him.
    "What are you doing up there?!" By now he was starting to attract a crowd. Jeff quickly jumped to the street and apologized profusely to the peasant (who apparantly owned the cottage he had been standing on). After the peasant had left Jeff decided to walk around the village. He established his bearings and was practicing his newfound skills in theft when He noticed someone following him in the crowd. Upon closer observation, Jeff realized he was being followed by....
