    As Connor and Spark were headed out of the door, they were stopped by Leah for a moment.

    "Hey, you two weren't leaving yet, were you? Come on, there's still plenty of time to have some fun." Leah encouraged.

    "Sorry, Leah, Connor and I have to head outside." Spark apologized as she and Connor almost exited.

    "Oh, I almost forgot." Spark said to herself as Leah was still dancing but Spark used Gloria's ring on her once again just as the five girls that Spark had beat up were coming towards her.

    "Hey you little freak, move it. I got an appointment with an asshole." The girl called Missy said to who she assumed was a girl before but realized her mistake when she saw it was Mrs. Leary.

    "Young lady. How dare you use that kind of language on me. I think you're parents will want to hear of this." Mrs. Leary said as she pulled Missy away.

    Meanwhile outside, Spark listened with amusement as she heard Missy being taken away.

    "I'm sorry about that. No matter what school I go to, I find there's always going to be a bully." Connor apologized to Spark.

    "It's alright, I gave her and her dumb friends what they deserve, now she's going to get a mouthful from her own parents." Spark smiled but looked down at her dress and shoes.

    "But I hate that I look awful now, Gloria helped me pick out this dress and now it's all messed up." Spark sighed.

    "Well, you still have her ring, so you can fix it with magic." Connor reminded her.

    "Oh right, well this won't take but a quick minute." Spark said as she almost used the ring before Connor halted her.

    "Actually, before you use it, I....wondered if you used it on both of us to....see how we'd look as adults." Connor suggested as Spark was up for it.

    Spark used it on herself first and aged into her mid thirties, with her dress fixed and fit to her age, she looked at herself in a mirror she had gotten that was inside a purse.

    "Wow, Connor, I can't believe I look....this....good." Spark said as she now looked at Connor who was also aged into an adult in his mid thirties looking like a mixture of his father and brother. Spark may only be a human in disguise, but she felt her heart beating right now.

    "So Spark, how do I...." Connor began to ask before Spark leaped up and kissed him on the lips, it was shocking for Connor at first but then he slowly began to kiss Spark back.

    Spark broke off the kiss and stepped back in embarrassment at what she had done as she combed her fingers through her hair.

    "I'm...sorry. It's just these complicated human emotions, I don't know whether it's being human for only the second time in a while but you know, emotions are complicated unlike a human where I can't exactly control my emotions, it's all just so..." Connor gently turned Spark toward him.

    "You know, you talk too much when you're nervous." Connor said as she kissed her again and this time they didn't break off for some time.

    "I know you're Caleb's guardian right now, but I wondered if I might make....a different kind of vow?"

    "What kind?" Spark asked.

    "I promise," he began. "To treat you as my girlfriend, my fiance, and my wife, until the end of my days. To provide you with the comforts you deserve from me as your boyfriend, your fiance, and your husband, and to defend your with my life if need be. So help me god. Do you accept?" Connor asked.

    Spark held back her tears as to not cry.

    "Connor, it's....it's beautiful, but I...I don't know if I can accept. I mean I'm not a human, I'm a fairy, and you're not an adult you're only a boy. I want it to happen, but I don't know if it could happen." Spark said.

    "But Spark it can, with Gloria and her friends magic, we can be human or fairies whenever we wanted and be together whenever we wanted to be together. You may think this is just a crush, but I've known you longer than any girl. So I'll ask you again, do you accept my proposal?" Connor asked.

    "I will accept on terms, one: I will be your girlfriend, two: I will marry you when you get older with age not by magic if you don't love anyone else before then and three: just make me happy." Spark said.

    "Deal. Now I got a text from Caleb, he and Gloria are coming to pick us up soon. How about one last dance as adults before we go back?" Connor asked as Spark nodded as they danced while listening to the music playing from inside.

    It wasn't long before they turned themselves back into 12 year-old kids and got picked up by Gloria and Caleb. Connor and Spark both agreed that it was a pretty amazing night, now they would just have to tell his parents that he was dating Spark.
