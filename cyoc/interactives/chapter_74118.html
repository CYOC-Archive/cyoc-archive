    Ty Teaches His Foster Family Life Lessons

    Ty looked around and saw that his old room was now just a guest bedroom. Apparently, they had not taken in any foster kids in this
    reality. Just out of curiosity, he decided to see whether they were any better as people. Shrinking himself down to an inch tall, he snuck
    around and watched his old family. Sadly, nothing had changed except for Ty's presence.

    Mark was still the dishonest, scheming businessman he had always been. Ty over heard a phone conversation he was having with a
    client; he told the client he would need $10,000, but Ty saw that the renovations would only cost $8,000. Using his powers, he made the
    client become angry with Mark and fire him as contractor. Ty decided to get revenge and teach Mark a lesson. Every time Mark told a
    lie, he would be forced to tell a secret. He chuckled silently to himself when Mark called another customer; when he said the job would
    take no more than three days, he instantly revealed that he had used materials that werent to code to save himself money while still
    charging the same. As the crook beat himself up for his unprecedented slip, Ty moved down to the kitchen.

    June was a housewife, but she made those reality TV shows look tame. She saw her neighbor's husband getting out of a car with a
    woman. It was clear by the way they were talking that she was just a business partner. That didn't stop June from calling her neighbor
    and telling her that he was flirting with another woman. She watched out the kitchen window as her neighbor flew from the house to the
    curb and began screaming at her husband. While June laughed sadistically, Ty decided to punish her as well. If she tried to cause
    trouble, she would be overwhelmed with the desire to do something nice instead. June staggered back a bit, then ran outside to the
    neighbors she had just lied to and explained she had made it all up. After slapping June across the face, the couple returned to the
    house together. Instead of retaliating, June gave the other woman fifty bucks and apologized.

    he regained his composure, Ty found himself...
