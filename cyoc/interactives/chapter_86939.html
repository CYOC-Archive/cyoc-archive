    Hair of the Dog...

    Now that your ears have fur, you are aware how different the fur on your ears is from your hair, both in color and texture. You never really liked your hair, since it alway seemed limp, wiry, and altogether annoying. You don't want to change your anatomy any more, but you decide you want to risk changing your hair. After all, fur and hair aren't all that different, and you definitely like your fur better than your original hair. If you can change it, it will match your ears and look more like a complete costume. However, you don't want to spray your head on the off chance there could be unwanted side effects.

    Even though the only thing you want to change is your hair, you know you can't wear the ski mask. Instead, you put on a thick sports headband, hoping it will keep your hair away from your skin so that any extra BOD does not drip. You put the glasses and the face mask back on, although you have to tie it lower now that your ears are too high to tie it around. Spraying some BOD into your gloved hand, you rub your gloves together and run them through your hair as though you were applying shampoo. You are careful to avoid your ears, which now stick out from inside your hair rather than around the edges and twitch whenever your hands get too close. You work the BOD in as well as you can without getting much on your scalp. Eventually you do being to feel a tingle, although it is not as bad as the itching which you suffered when you sprayed yourself earlier.

    You walk over to the mirror, grabbing a small towel on the way. You put the towel on your head and rub carefully, trying to make sure there is no leftover BOD. When you feel certain your hair is dry, you drop the towel and take off your headband, glasses, and mask. You are astounded at the difference in your appearance. Instead of a rather pathetic mass of hair, you now sport a head of thick, lively, colorful fur which matches your ears. You take off your gloves and run your hands through it, amazed at the volume and the smooth feel. The sensation also feels extremely satisfying, similar to the enjoyment you felt when scratching behind your ears. Eagerly, you pick up your brush and run it through your long fur, your ears happily perking up in pleasure. You wish you had changed your hair first instead of accidentaly changing your nose.
