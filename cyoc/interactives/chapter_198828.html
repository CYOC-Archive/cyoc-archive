    Renata Hamilton wasn't about to let the recent weirdness surrounding her sibling ruin her weekend. Whatever happened in the bedroom was between them and Mom. Renata just wasn't going to think about it when there was fun to be had. After a morning of catching up on her social media accounts, Renata met up with her friend (with occasional benefits) Jezebel du Charme for an afternoon at the movies.

    The theater was having some kind of vintage B-movie event, and Renata and Jezebel ended up seeing a campy 1966 flick called Space Vixens from Venus! together. After an hour of watching alien fox women infiltrating Earth and corrupting its women with their lewd ways, Renata and Jezebel emerged from the theater laughing their asses off.

    "Oh man!" said Jezebel, running her hand through her dark curls. "That has to be one of the most epically goofy things I've ever seen!"

    "Ditto, Jez!" Renata laughed. "Action hero or not, who actually shouts 'you sultry space temptress!' in a fight? Shouldn't Dirk McClane be a little to busy actually fighting Queen Vulpessa to wrack his brain for all those fancy words?"

    "My personal favorite was 'beguiling, bush-tailed succubus' personally," Jezebel chuckled. Her basketball-sized boobs bounced as she laughed, straining against their tight tanktop.

    An unfamiliar girl approached them. "And how about those horrible makeup jobs?" she said, snickering. "Could the seams of the space vixens' prosthetic muzzles have been any more fucking obvious?! Hey did you catch that screw-up during the landing scene? One space vixen steps on the tail of the actress right in front of her, and it came clean off!"

    Renata tried not to stare at the new girl. She was absolutely striking. Her hair was as vivaciously red as Renata's. It was a bit shorter, stopping just past the girl's shoulders, but its thick curls made up the difference with sheer volume. Her full lips were ruby red, and her almond-shaped eyes were a distinctive gold color. She also had glorious curves, complete with tits to rival Jezebel's. But there was something more to her than looks... a raw animal charisma that Renata couldn't help but respond to.

    Renata tried not to get flustered. "Yeah, I can't believe they were dumb enough to use that take." Renata fought to reassert her flirty, dominant nature. "Say, what's your name? I don't think I've seen you around here before... except in my dreams, maybe."

    "My name's Ruby," said the girl. "Ruby Reynard. I'm not surprised we haven't met. I've only been in town for about a day. My family move around a lot, you see. It was my idea to come to Stokerville. I was hoping to smooth things over with an ex, Valeria. We didn't part on the best terms, and I was hoping that we could come to some kind of understanding." She fluttered her eyelashes at Renata. "But I think I have a better idea."

    "What?" asked Renata.

    "Why don't you come with me and meet my family?" asked Ruby. "I'm sure they'd adore you, cutie."

    Renata's heart fluttered. Ruby called her a cutie! She wasn't used to being prey rather than predator, but she wasn't about to question it, not if she got some nice, curvy booty out of it.

    "Sure," said Renata. "That'd be awesome. Where do you guys live?"

    "We have a few mobile homes on the edge of town," said Ruby.

    "A few?" Renata repeated. "Just how big is your family?"

    "Pretty big," said Ruby. "My parents are in a group marriage. Seven wives, two husbands. And then there's us kids. There's twenty-one of us, ages one to eighteen."

    "Pretty unconventional family," Renata remarked.

    "It's just how we roll," said Ruby. "We don't fucking care what anybody thinks."

    "I can't wait to meet them all," Renata smiled. She took Ruby's hand. "And to get to know you better."

    Ruby fluttered her eyelashes fetchingly again. "Fair warning, some of my siblings may want to join in."

    Renata laughed. "Fine by me!" She turned to Jezebel. "You wanna come with us, Jez? The Reynards sound like just your type."

    "I would," said Jezebel, "but I have a date tonight. Maybe later."

    Renata nodded and left with Ruby, hand in hand. Renata waved goodbye to Jezebel with her free hand.

    Jazebel smiled to herself as Renata left with Ruby. "Well," she whispered to herself, "life just got interesting. That was a werevixen if I ever saw one. Looks like you're gonna learn about the supernatural, Renata. Maybe then I can let you know I'm a succubus."
