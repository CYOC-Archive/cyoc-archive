    Ryan was now fully Jason in all his current form and mannerisms.

    He was smoking and drinking and becoming the total slob that Jason was
    and Ryan had discovered as he had changed. Jason had kept this part of
    himself hidden but when he went home he found himself doing very un-
    Ryan like things.

    Left to his own devices Jason would drink to excess, smoke like a
    chimney and eat like a condemned man. And so he did.

    When he returned to the test site the next week for his checkup he had
    become a chain smoker and was half drunk stumbling into the office.

    So what's up doc. Ryan/Jason slurred, giggling at his little pun with
    the doctor.

    Get undressed we need to make some measurements.

    Ryan/Jason did as he was ordered but getting out of his pants almost
    had him taking a header into the examination bed.

    Woa! Jason said trying to stabilize himself.

    Ok now I see drinking and smoking has really settled in.

    Yea yea. I hear that all the time. But then as he was saying that
    Ryan/Jason tried to remember who had said that to him. It seemed as
    though people had said that to him for years but he hadn't smoked
    before he started this test. And his drinking was limited to
    occasional nights out with the boys but now he seemed to do it when he
    woke and didn't end till he passed out.

    Ryan/Jason stood up and the Dr made some measurements. Chest 42
    inches, Waist 38 inches, neck 17 inches, biceps 12 inches, legs 18
    inches, weight 198. Jason was in fairly good shape but that 38 inches
    concerned the Doctor. It should have read 36 but that wasn't too off.
    The other measurements were spot on.

    Come back and see me next week.

    Ryan/Jason got dressed and stumbled out the door and went home.

    When he returned the next week he had changed a bit. He seemed to
    tolerate the drinking a bit better now and even though he was drinking
    more he wasn't stumbling around like he had the previous week. He was
    smoking just as much as he had the previous week. And his shirt no
    longer fit. He hadn't tucked it in because it wouldn't fit and the
    last two buttons couldn't even fit together exposing the bottom of a
    wife beater t-shirt that rode up almost to his belly button.

    Ok you know the routine get undressed.

    Ryan/Jason did and the change was noticeable. Apparently Jason, left
    to his own devices would be a fat chain smoking drunk. But he had
    enough money to keep himself in good shape and his place cleaned up.
    Ryan/Jason was showing what Jason would become if he didn't have those
    controls. Ryan's chest measurements had grown a bit, but his waist had
    gone up to 44 inches. Ryan/Jason's but had grown to be fairly rounded
    And what was once a furry chest going down to a furry set up abs now
    became a furry chest going down to a budda belly with a treasure trail
    into his crotch. His weight had gone up to 233 pounds.

    You need to cut down on the eating and drinking and while your at it
    you smoke way too much. The doctor told Ryan/Jason going in one ear
    and out the other. Here I need to give you some supplements to try to
    control your cravings.

    The doctor poked a needle into Ryan/Jason's voluminous ass and sent
    him on his way telling him to come in the next week.

    He had gone home and it seemed that the supplements had controlled him
    for a bit. His drinking went down to a manageable case and a half of
    beer a night and his smoking was no longer a chain smoking frenzy, but
    his eating increased. It seemed as though his drinking and smoking had
    his hands doing something besides eating and over the next week he had
    been eating more than he had ever thought possible. When he had gone
    in for his checkup he was wearing sweat pants and a very large Hawaiian shirt.

    As he waddled into the Doctors office his feet in flip flops, the only
    shoe he could wear since he could no longer bend over to tie his shoes
    were at 45 degree angles to his belly. He waddled down the hallway
    having to rest against the wall several times before he made it to the
    examining room. He sat down on the doctor's chair with a noticeable
    groan.

    What happened the doctor asked.

    Well I think I got the smoking and drinking in check but that left
    plenty of room for eating.

    The doctor helped him off with his shirt and pants and made some
    measurements. Ryan's belly was 53 inches and his weight had gone up to
    312 pounds. In a week?

    We got to get your eating in check.

    The doctor gave him a prescription and gave him some samples before he
    left.

    Now fill this and it should help you with your eating.

    Ryan went home and had his roommate fill the prescription. But his
    eating was no wear near controlled. It seemed as though it was in
    overdrive.

    When he returned the next week for his appointment he was now well
    over 450 pounds. The doctor was astounded by the growth. It seemed as
    though it was accelerating.

    What happened?

    I don't know I took those pills you gave me.

    As Ryan/Jason took off his shirt and sweats the bottle fell out of his
    pocket and the doctor looked at the pills. It was the label for the
    prescription he had given him but the pills inside were totally wrong.
    They were a powerful appetite stimulant.

    Where did you get this filled? The doctor asked.

    I don't know my roommate Percy filled it for me it was just too hard
    for me to get to the pharmacy.

    Ok Ok.

    I need to talk to your roommate next week could you see if he could
    come in with you. The doctor hadn't told Ryan/Jason about the pill
    switch but he did tell him that he needed to stop taking them.

    I am going to need his help to get your appetite under control so tell
    him we need his assistance and that I need to discuss the particulars
    with him.

    So Ryan went home and told Percy that the doctor needed to see him at
    the next appointment.

    Ryan told Percy the lie that the doctor had come up with once he had
    seen the pills.

    When the appointment came around Ryan/Jason had leveled off and was
    slightly over 500 and he and Percy walked down the hall way to the
    examination room.

    The doctor made his measurements and was pleased with the slowing of
    his growth.

    Well it looks like maybe we have turned the corner on this. You have
    still grown way too much but at least it has slowed down.

    Can I talk to you Percy alone.

    Percy followed the doctor to an adjacent waiting room and asked Percy
    what he was doing to Ryan/Jason.

    What do you mean?

    I know you have been giving him this appetite stimulant. Why have you
    been doing that?

    I didn't do it you have no evidence.

    Oh don't I?

    The doctor pulled out a tape showing Percy encouraging Ryan's
    excessive consumption. That wont hold up in court. You can't prove
    anything but that I have been helping my roommate.

    You know your right I have nothing that will hold up in court. But I
    am annoyed someone is playing with my project and giving me this
    almost useless data.

    Percy tried to push past the doctor feeling a prick in his butt cheek
    as he went by and when he awoke he felt different.

    Ok Percy glad your awake. Ryan/Jason went home several hours ago. I
    told him you needed to get all the information for your diet. And
    while he has been gone I made a little change to you. I hadn't planned
    on going this way but you gave me an idea and I have been working on
    this alteration for the past week since I had found out what you were
    doing.

    The doctor held up a mirror to Percy and looking back at him was John
    Goodman or a reasonable facsimile. His clothes didn't fit and he was
    strapped down with an IV in his arm. You know that appetite stimulant
    you gave Ryan well this one is quite a bit stronger and giving it to
    you this way it will stay in your system for over a month.

    When it wears off you will be bigger than Ryan.

    The Percy part of him was screaming in fear but the John Goodman part
    was fine with the weight. He had come to terms with his weight long
    ago and now that was part of Percy's makeup.

    Percy went home and found his roommate taking up most of the sofa and
    he sat down next to Ryan/Jason. Who are you he asked. It's me Percy he
    responded.

    What happened to you.

    I thought it would help if we both went on the diet together so I
    asked the doctor to make me big and he was able to do this. Percy
    didn't want to tell Ryan what he had done.

    Oh ok.

    Ryan/Jason leveled off at 508 pounds but Percy/John kept going up and
    up.

    When they waddled in the next week they Percy was still a bit smaller
    than Ryan but gaining quickly.

    Please stop this doctor.

    It will stop in three weeks but it is slowing down. I figure you will
    settle out at about 700 pounds and it looks like Ryan has stabilized.

    Don't fuck with me boy you might get your due.
