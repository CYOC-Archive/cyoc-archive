    "excuse me young lady. can I help you?" this wired young adult employee called as she approached Alexis. "looking for a transformation for the week. one that's really me" Alexis kindly answered. "still if your a first timer I insist on helping you to avoid problems like failing to transform or it turns out you lying about your parents" the employee responded.
