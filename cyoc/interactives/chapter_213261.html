    Blaze had been living at the zoo as one of their kangaroos for little over a year now. He even has a Joey by the name of Hopper. He couldn't be happier. Squeaky the gray weresquirrel let Blaze out of the kangaroo enclosure & he hopped out. Before the door could be closed Blaze heard from behind him a child voice in Werespeak ask 'Can I come with you daddy?' Squeaky replied that with a bit of surprise 'Your joey is a werekangaroo too?' Blaze spun back around to see his joey son Hopper & he asked again 'Can I daddy?' Blaze was shocked because he didn't know. Quickly getting over his shock he turned back to Squeaky & asked 'Is it ok if he does?' Squeaky chuckled 'It is because he's one of us.' So Blaze told Hopper it was ok to come with him; this made Hopper really happy. So after that the 3 headed off to meet the other wereanimals. They entered the room to see all the wereanimals there. Rusty the werefox (this is Mike; the staff renamed him) immediately said 'You can't bring your Joey here Blaze. He isn't a wereanimal like us.' Hopper then asked 'What is a wereanimal?' There was shocked stares for a few seconds. 'My word Blaze. You didn't tell us your son was a wereanimal like us.' a female werealligator said with a laugh. Blaze answered admitting 'I just found out myself tonight.' Hopper now getting mad yelled 'What is a wereanimal?!' The werefox Rusty got up & said friendly walking over 'I'll explain it to you little one.' Blaze informed Rusty 'He's named Hopper.' So Rusty explained everything about what wereanimals are. He even went on to explain all the rules about living at this zoo. Hopper had no questions about anything he was told. He agreed to follow all the rules he was told (even though he had been doing that already). Rusty & a few others even showed their feral forms to Hopper. Then Hopper being a Joey which is a kid kangaroo said like a kid 'Rusty I am faster than you.' Rusty taking that as a challenge said 'I bet you're not.' Hopper replied to that 'I bet me a kangaroo is faster than you.' Seeing where this was going a wereotter opened the door. Rusty dropped to all fours & shifted to his feral form. 'On all fours nothing is faster than a fox. I will run circles around you.' Rusty said with a superior tone. Blaze with a sigh told Hopper 'Be back here before sun up so we can go back to our home.' Hopper said 'Ok dad.' And with that Rusty & Hopper took off out the door. The werelion said aloud 'I never thought I'd see it. A fox & a kangaroo becoming friends.' Blaze asked confused 'What you talking about?' The werezebra answered 'They just became friends obviously.' Blaze looked out the door seeing the 2 racing & heard 'I might lose this!' from Rusty. And then from Hopper 'You will!' It was plain as day to Blaze the werekangaroo. His son just made a friend. Blaze was happy for that.
