The gargoyle leaned in and smelled both Erick and Johnny, and it seemed oddly calm about it.

    "Greetings trespassers, I see that my years of neglect and lack of experience have had no effect on my intimidation. Now state who you are, and why you are here now, or else I will be forced to kill you both." The gargoyle ordered

    "I...I'm Erick and this is Johnny, me and a group of friends including Johnny, his brother Ryan, and two others named Mike and Sei came here on a dare just to look around and see what the place here is like at night. I went one way, Ryan went another, Johnny here went alone and Mike and Sei stayed together. Johnny and I here were both human too before we entered and then we had our own separate experiences." Erick said nervously

    The gargoyle then glared at Erick and saw the crown,"Were you planning on taking this?" The gargoyle asked pulling the crown off of Erick and holding it up in front of him.

    "Well...uh...yes? I found it and tried it on and transformed into a dragon, and ever since then it's been hard for me to put it down, but if you really REALLY do want it back then you can have it." Erick said thinking that he has become a bad person, and was having second thoughts about his wording.

    The gargoyle sighed,"If you really wish to claim a cursed item then keep it by all means. I have no use guarding the possessions of those who are deceased. As for your friend here, you both have nothing to fear from me unless you wish to destroy the property." The gargoyle said and returned the crown to Erick's horn.

    "W...wait what you aren't going attack us for trespassing or anything?" Johnny asked

    "Normally, I would maul you to death on sight, but things here have been dreadful lately so seeing new faces is comforting, even if they are just juvenile young adults who take without permission. I guess the most I can do now is merely welcome you to Spelstorm Manor." The gargoyle said

    "Welcome us....wait please can you help us find our friends? We're trying to get out of here because of the transformations." Erick asked

    "Yeah, and is there a way to turn us back human again?" Johnny asked

    "I can see your concern for your allies, and I can help you with this. However, all transformations that happen in Spelstorm Manor are permanent, they can be altered further too after transforming yes, but you can never return to your original form that you had upon entering." The gargoyle said

    "Aw man, what's mom going to say about me now. I'm literally proof that hell extsts at this point." Johnny said

    "Okay, I wasn't going to say anything about this Johnny, but you're kind of slowly burning my stomach right now." Erick said

    "Oh crap, sorry dude." Johnny said moving to Erick's side

    "Okay back on topic, can you at least tell us where our friends are?" Erick asked the gargoyle

    "Yes, but it is not my duty to aid you physically. All of your friends are in quite interesting situations, but nonetheless dire. What most humans don't realize is that Spelstorm Manor contains every cursed item in existence, and that those same items decorate the mansion. I myself can see through the eyes of the various false gargoyle statues here, and I can see your friends. One is in the exotic animal quarters which has cursed items from many animals, and if he touches one then two come out of opposite gender. Another is in the cellar and is currently being an arrogant and stubborn fool and is toying with the spirit of the young deceased Master Christopher. That human will likely become Christopher's friend if the young master likes him, or a slave if not. The last is being cautious, but I see another creature with him that's stalking him in the secret enclosed garden, perhaps the human has become prey, or a possible interest for the stalker. Maybe I should take interest in that last human, for this creature is completely unknown to me, and could damage or destroy the property. If this creature attacks either your friend, me or destroys property then you have my word that I will protect your friend." The gargoyle explained

    "If you could protect him then thanks Mr. Gargoyle sir. Also if you see them tell them to meet back here if you can please." Johnny said, and the gargoyle nodded, and flew off down the hall.

    "Wow, that's a lot to take in." Erick said
