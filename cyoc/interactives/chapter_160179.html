    Timantha had borrowed one of Carla's more business casual clothes when she went to work. For the first few minutes the job seemed more or less the same. Boot up the office computer, and start to get to work on the project she was assigned. Then one of her "favorite" co-workers Joe walked by Timantha's Cubicle and said "Yo Tim, Do you sti-" Joe cut himself off as he saw that there was a cute girl where Tim should've been.
    Timantha looked and asked "Do I what?"
    "Um... Who are you?" Joe checked outside the cubicle and saw Tim's pinned comic clippings outside... yep... those were Tim's.
    Timantha said "Oh right... Things were more or less normal that... Well least I know they didn't put out a memo. I am Tim as weird as that sounds. I'm helping with a special experiment, and well... So far so good in a way."
    Joe wasn't sure what to say but asked "So... What should I call you?"
    "Well they kinda nicknamed me Timantha, but feel free to just call me Tim. I am still Tim after all at my core. So what did you need?"
    "Well... I was wondering if you still had a copy of the source code... I was tinkering a bit too much and kinda broke mine."
    Timantha chuckled and said "I'll send it through an email. You should have it by the time you get back to your cubicle." Joe nodded and left. Timantha sighed, Joe was a nice looking guy. Tall, kinda handsome, not a lot of fat... Tim had a crush on Joe in the past, but gave up when he noticed that Joe was for sure straight... Although... Was Joe looking at her like?... She'd have to confirm whether or not Joe may be getting a crush on her.

    A bit later another co-worker came by. A nice young lady named Jessica who initially came by asking "Say Tim, Do you still have-"
    Timantha turned and saw her nice long chestnut hair, and decent curves... then said "Yes, I am Tim. Technically Timantha, but you can still just call me Tim."
    "Wait... but-"
    "It's a weird experiment going pretty well right now."
    "Aw, I didn't know my favorite gay friend wanted to transition!"
    "I didn't, I just kind of did when a friend of mine did and they convinced me to try this experiment as well." Timantha saw that Joe had sent her an e-mail and read it.
    "Well I think we need to go out for drinks... or maybe just help you shop for a new wardrobe."
    "This isn't necessarily permanent. I'm probably going to change back relatively soon so..." Timantha continued reading the e-mail and saw that Joe was inviting her out for dinner a few hours after work as thanks for the source code.
    Jessica a bit of an easedropper read it over Timantha's shoulder and grinned as she said "So, we go out and get you a nice dress... Maybe some perfume, and we see where this goes?"
    Timantha nodded.
