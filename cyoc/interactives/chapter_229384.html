Dan would be as flexible, durable, and indestructible as whatever inanimate object everyone sees him to be. Her brother would be be usable, while being able to feel, see, hear, and taste everyone done to him. Kat didnt want to kill her brother, just make him suffer. She put the spell book away satisfied and went to join her family in the living room.

    She spots Dan trying to get up from the couch and her parents bringing in the last if the party stuff from the car. It looks like they havent seen Dan yet. She walks past the couch and hugs her parent. They smile at her, greeting her 'happy birthday' with a kiss on her forehead. It was then, that her dad saw Dan with surprise and wide eyes.

    "Sweetie, why is a sex doll on the couch? And why does it look like a real person?" Her dad asked in shock. Apparently Dan was a HyperRealistic Sex Doll, Kat had to cover to mouth to not burst out laughing at finally knowing what Dan appeared to be to everyone. Dan, on the hand, was horrified at the news. Dan had been mistaken for a sex doll by his parent. Would this count as mistaken identity that he has reas about before? Did kat actually cast some spell on him. Now that he knew what he looked like, he couldn't move. He wouldn't be able to move while people can see and think about his 'inanimate' form. Kat could hear everything he says though.

    Kat grinned internally as she started making excuses in her head. "It was a gift for my birthday. I was just looking at it."

    Her parents shook their head at hearing someone just gifted their daughter a realistic male sex doll. "Well put it away, i will not have it out while you have your party. Better yet, i will confiscate it for now." Her mother says, but it was her father walking over to the couch to lift the doll up to put away.

    Dan had been trying to speak his parents the whole time, but only Kat could hear the fear and desperation. "Mom! Dad! I'm your son! I'm not some sex toy! Listen to me! Hey! Don't do this..." Kat could only nod at the decision, while smirking at her parents not hearing Dan and the predicament he is now in.

    Dad looked around the living room and found the box Dan supposedly came it. The box still all the accessories to go with Dan new form. Dad set the doll back in the box and secured it in place. Dan was still begging to be heard.

    Finally Dad closed the box and Dan was in darkness. Dad looked at his daughter. "We will keep this until after the party. We will discuss what to do next." Kat just nods. Dan could only hear Kat going to help their mother setting up, while dad took the box and carried it away to set aside for later.
