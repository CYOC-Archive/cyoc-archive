    She pulls out the card and it reads "You Have been Granted One Wish.".

    "Perfect, I got a wish. I wish we we're both back to normal." Molly says holding the card up so they both can see. It shifts slightly and says "As you wish."

    Slowly Molly begins back to her old self, a reducion in breast size, and her clothing changing back to normal.

    While they are both doubled over in pain on the ground, odd since this didn't happen for the last transformation. Andrew(Andrea?) notices that she isn't changing back to a guy but just back to how she was before the drunken wish was cast. She was changing but not back to himself, but back to herself. The card seemed to of got the impression that Andrew being Andrea was normal.

    After it completed Andrea sat against the wall tired from 3 transformations in the last 10 minutes or so. "What happened? I'm not back to normal!"

    "Maybe it only remembers one wish back and takes everything before that as normal. I should of worded it differently, seems my second wish also has changed." Molly said, getting up as she scrounges around for another nickel.

    Having found one, which actually belonged to Andrea she sticks it in the machine and turns the knob, however no card comes out. The knob is jammed and refuses to return to its normal position. Rattling the knob makes no different. Angered by this Andrea gets up and kicks it and a card pops out. However the knob is still jammed.

    "See everything works if you hit it hard enough," she said picking up the card. However it didn't seem to be a wish card, instead the card read. "The holder of this card has already made thier one wish," then it slowly blurs and reads "Be careful what you wish for."

    "Boy isn't that some wisdom." she mumbles as she hands the card to Molly.

    Picking it up she inquires, "Be careful what you wish for? Wait does that mean we can't make another wish?"

    "Seems like it." Andrea sighs, sad knowing she is trapped this way.

    "Thats not hard to solve though, we just need to find someone who hasn't made a wish. Simple." Molly grins as pulls Andrea's arm and her with her back into the main room of the arcade. "But the coin slot is jammed."

    Then someone walks up, to them, and grabs them by the shoulder, "I'm sorry we're going to have to ask you to leave."

    "But why?!"

    "No kicking the machines." He says as he ushers them along to the exit.

    Soon they are expelled from the arcade, annyoingly cheerfully Molly says "Its okay we can come back tomarrow."

    However Andrea is a bad mood and can only be pessimistic, but also very observant, "Tomarrow is a holiday, their not open on holidays."

    "Things just seem to keep getting worse for you, sure you didn't walk udner a ladder or something?" Molly asks a little tired of Andrea's mood, however Andrea is still irresistable to her and can't stay very cross.
