    After reading through the table of contents in the 'Shapeshifter's Guide', Wally settled on a great spell to try out in the chapter titled Multiplication on page 97. "How about Spell 41?" Wally suggests, showing Roger the page. "A Self-Supernumerary spell... You got any ideas on what to multiply?" "I do," Wally answers. "Could I try it first?" "Sure." After Roger hands him back the book, placing the amulet around his neck, Wally proceeds to study pages 97-99.

    The spell begins with the words 'Urod Nede Minon Qualo...' and ends with "Nupto Rennu Imm Lopi Wadort", requiring two words to fill up the spaces for the desired change. Luckily, page 99 has a glossary and translation to make it easy. Wally gets that should one arrange the spell to be uttered as 'Armo foro Nupto Rennu Imm Lopi Wadort' would give the caster a second set of arms. Once Wally completely read the pages, he wrote down the intended spell and checks it twice to make sure it is correct. He then stood up and began the incantation. "Urod Nede Minon Qualo Hedo Cuplo Nupto Rennu Imm Lopi Wadort."

    As soon as Wally finished the incantation, he began to feel heat emanating from the amulet as it feel his whole body was on fire with the burning sensation. His vision blurred for a second before he grabs the table to steady himself. He looked at Roger, who was staring at him, his eyes wide, his mouth hanging open. "What's wrong?" Wally said, noticing an echo to his voice. Wally turns to look to the source and finds himself bumping at another Wally, the two sharing one normal body from the necks down.
