Kat had completely immobilised him inside the bedsheet. Struggling to see much beyond a greyed out snake towering above him getting bigger and bigger as he was lifted up.

    Having caught her prey Kat used her tail to lift Dan up, slithering it under the fabric and wrapping it round his frail body tugging just enough when wrapping the next layer to not break any bones but enough to cause major discomfort.
    "Don't struggle now" Kat said, her parting words to her meal as she unhinged her jaw in preparation.

    Feeling like he could pop at any moment Dans eyes began to bulge as the blood began to accumulate, the feeling in his limbs fading. Desperately Dan bit down on the tail wrapped around his neck, hoping in some way it could save him from this ordeal. Flinching Kat hissed as she yelled " OUCH YOU FUCKER and here I was, trying to be nice about it well fine " Kat finished as she snickered " This is how it would of happened in the wild anyway" Suddenly Dan could feel Kats muscles tensing as she began to constrict and bind him further. After a few minutes Kat stopped uncoiling her meal to reveal a flattened mess and judging by the wounds dan wont be alive for much longer but just long enough Kat hoped as she slid the still morsel down her throat. Finding out that the crushed body was much easier to swallow than she thought.

Unable to move Dan could only wait as he was moved to the digestion chamber, acidic fluids and dissolvents filling the sac. Soon the hair on his body was dissolved and he could feel chunks of flesh fall away as the muscle surrounding his bones became lose and broke off. Eventually the liquid submerged him completely as he was slowly torn apart only when his skull began the dissolve and his brain exposed did he pass, Until then feeling all the pain.
