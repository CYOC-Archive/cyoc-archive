    Ty pulled the chair out from his desk. "Sit down, Dustin, and don't say anything."

    Dustin looked mad and even more confused, but sat down in the chair.

    Ty considered the half-dressed Dustin.

    "No, sit like a pretty girl would." Dustin shifted in his seat, crossing his legs, pointing his toes, and folding his hands in his lap.

    "Dustin, you're going to forget how to sit any way other than the way you are sitting right now. You can only sit like a girl would sit. You'll also walk like a girl, run like a girl, throw like a girl, and just overall act like a big sissy. You won't be able to not act this way, no matter what you want." Dustin's eyes widened in fear. Good, thought Ty. I wonder what his big macho friends are going to think?

    "After we're done here, you're going to go back into the bathroom and shave. You're going to shave your face and your whole body, so you don't have any body hair, just like a girl, and you'll have to keep doing that." Dustin moved his mouth like he was trying to say something, but nothing came out. He finally just started shaking his head, no.

    "Okay, since you seem to be enjoying this so much, lets add a couple more things." Ty allowed himself a big toothy smile. It was fun having this sort of power over Dustin for a change. "You can no longer wear anything but girls clothing. Your have to wear a bra, you have to wear panties, and you will desperately need to wear makeup. You know you don't want to wear makeup, but you won't be able to stop yourself."

    "Dustin, you will be completely unable to tell anybody why you are doing these things. If you try, you'll only be able to say that you act like this because you want to, because it feels right and natural. You will be completely unable to let anybody other than me think that you don't want to act in these ways."

    Ty figured he'd need a big finish. "You will forget the name Dustin entirely. Your name is and always has been...um...Daphne." That'd fix him. "No matter what evidence people present, you'll know that your name has always been Daphne, and there's nothing odd about you having that name at all."

    Dustin just sat there hanging his head. "Okay, Daphne, you can talk and get up if you want."

    "Bastard. I'll kill you!" Dustin launched himself off the chair.

    "Stop!" said Ty, and Dustin froze in his half-raised position. "You'll be completely unable to physically threaten me in any way. Even if you want to, you'll just end up acting docile towards me. Okay, you can move again."

    Dustin got up. "You are one sick fuck, Ty." He paused, then angrily stalked towards the bathroom, his motions now filled with an almost exaggerated feminine grace. "Dammit, I have to shave."

    Ty grinned. That was going to take his macho bullying 'brother' down a peg or two!
