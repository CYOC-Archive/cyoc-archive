    The Magic Shop

    Life isn't this good...right?

    added by gojitron 2 years ago O Reality alteration

    I looked at her with a blink as the bell had went off...she was kidding right?

    My name is Ashley Togashi, I'm a half japanese, half caucasian woman of 24 and black hair who probably can't believe her luck. I mean seriously no, I can't. I WON all of the products? How is that possible?

    I looked to this brown haired woman in a white uniform while in my black tank top and jeans. "Doesn't this set you guys back...a lot?" I asked seriously.

    She looked at me with confusion. "Well...no. We sell enough of the other product to more than make the difference."

    "But...you guys are barren..." I said frankly with concern.

    She chuckled humbly. "You should see us on Saturday nights. Now dear I know you are taken aback by this but think about this as a way to enhance your home life!"

    "I live alone in a fraternity house no one uses." I say with a flat face and groan.

    "Oh you're a college girl! Well we can still move the product there too. We have a system for this. Soon you'll have party nights with all your friends!" said the woman with a smile.

    Oh God, if only she knew. My frat house is cursed and dingy. It's on its last legs. I was forced into that place as a challenge from another frat house so I could hang out with them. It's dirty and I think it's infested with something. No one in their right mind would party there. I was just getting Halloween decorations to make it a haunted house for crying out loud.

    "Listen umm..."

    "Coral."

    "Yes Coral...I don't think I have space or even standing to grab all of this...can I sell it?" I asked nervously.

    "Dear... what's the hold up? A good thing has happened to you. What is your name?" asked Coral.

    I sighed as there wasn't anyone here to be embarrassed in front of so I could just be straight. "My name is Ashley Togashi and I feel as if this prize is going to waste on a average student with a lackluster frat house as her living space. Besides...the other students think I'm a joke...well most do."

    She frowned and took out a clipboard with multiple paperwork on it. "Listen Ashley dear. You can't go living your life being controlled by what people expect of you. Fill out this question filled paper work on your living space and yourself so we take care of everything."

    "I mean...thanks. I guess I should just take this opportunity in hand...how long before you move stuff you want to give me?" I asked out of curiosity.

    "Pretty quickly. Like faster than Prime plan quick." She said with a flaunting smile and her arms crossed.

    "Okay... should I help your workers get a layout of the frat house?" I asked, wanting to be helpful.

    She then gave me a ticket...for a free spa treatment. "Let us take care of all the trimmings dear. Go have a good time while we're at it."

    I took the ticket nervously and then looked at her with a dumbfounded look. "Can I at least buy something?"

    She then put on a gold necklace with a white jewel on its pendant around my neck. "Necklace of protection, 30$."

    I stood silent for a moment and said, "Okay."

    ....
    ....
    ....

    Aaaaah...holy...shit... great massage. I had been doing manual labor for quite some time in my life to know what true liberating relaxation felt like and boy did I enjoy my current status now, all nice and clean. At least I could drive back to university grounds knowing that was-

    Screech!

    ... enough?

    Kill me... just kill me. I was now looking at a fully renovated frat house where the old one used to be. It went from rickety and rotten to a fucking masterpiece. The wood was replaced and the metal parts too. What was more interesting was that the house went from short two story, to long and wide three story. It also didn't look like it was sinking into the small swamp like lake in the back. In fact that lake was now frickin clean. This place also had a garage...but of course as I pulled in there was no people and still I was still isolated from the other houses at probably the outskirts of the University Grounds. Guess they couldn't change everything.

    My grades were good so the landlord had the decency to let me stay here even though she said this isn't a place to stay in her opinion...but now it's a full blown sorority house. I'm technically house leader here. I could take over and invite people...

    Though when I got inside I sort have doubled back on that idea. The inside was perfect. It had new appliances, new toiletries and kitchenware, all seemingly made for bigger groups of people. There wasn't food...yet anyway...but I'm pretty sure with this much space, several fridges could hold several weeks worth. And the TV'S...dear God they are big. Big enough for big time movies...or so I thought as I went down basement level to find a theater.

    Oh God...I need to check my room. My room was high on third floor that used to be second. In it I found that my room was tricked out with games, movies and assorted items that I LOVED. Anime, posters, comics, clothes....all of my entertainment options had been accommodated for and more. It was like the entire house needed to be accommodated for entertainment...and did all in a matter of hours...

    Finally I found a universal remote item on my bed that had a not by it. The remote itself had a touch screen on it plus buttons. It looked very...alien... but I brushed it off. I looked at the note though and read from Coral:

    "Dear Ashley, don't settle for less. Go big. Make some friends why don't you? Sometimes things don't take lots of time to kindle. Sometimes it happens right away if you're confident enough. Be careful with this remote though...it's powerful. It made the house renovation easy! It's truly the most advanced universal remote out there try it out on all the appliances we gave you."

    What?

    Okay seriously... what? This remote built the house? Bull...must be bull...or is it? That spa treatment was...too good. Like rich person good. Something didn't add up. I looked at the strange ominous remote in my hand. This couldn't be that powerful. Could it?

    Okay... I needed to see just what kinds of "entertainment" I was allowed to have...
