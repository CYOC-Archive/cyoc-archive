    Kevin's father Edward squinted towards the PDA in Jeff's hands. "What is that? What are you doing?"

    "And ... done," Jeff announced.

    Edward blinked, then suddenly felt odd, for some reason.

    "What's all the noise in here?" another man asked, walking into the hall. He put his arm around Edward's waist, lovingly.

    "Oh, it's just a misunderstanding, honey," Edward said to the other man, who he now believed was his gay husband. In fact, Jeff made it so that Kevin's father was now an openly proud gay man, married to another gay man, and both men believed that this was normal for them. Jeff wasn't really sure who this other man was, though. Apparently the Chronivac program had automatically taken an already-gay man and made him the husband of Edward.

    "Who's that?" Edward's husband asked, noticing Jeff.

    "Oh, this is our son's new boyfriend," he said.

    "He looks kind of rough," Edward's husband whispered in his ear.

    "Well, you know Kevie. He likes that kind of boy," Edward said.

    Jeff looked around and saw that all the pictures that had shown Kevin posing with different girls had changed. They now showed the more effeminate-looking Kevie posing with a bunch of muscular hunky-looking guys. It looked like in this new reality, Kevie was no longer in the closet, and he had two (gay) parents who completely supported his gay/Goth lifestyle. It was amazing how all of reality had changed.

    This Chronivac program was a blast! What should Jeff do now?
