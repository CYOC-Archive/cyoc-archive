    Kyle was relaxing after school, after seeing Jennifer growing more confused as she turned into some kind of furry was great. Her mother must have visited some weird site, one could only guess what could be happening over there Kyle thought to himself with a smirk. "Tomorrow will be something for sure, who knows what the Smiths would look like when, or even if, Jennifer came to school" Kyle let out a laugh, "Maybe I'll head over there if she doesn't show up."

    Kyle felt a prickly feeling growing from his feet as he absent mindedly scratched his ankle, feeling something foreign, something furry. Kyle raised his eyebrow as he leaned up from his chair, looking at his shoe, a thin line of brown barely peeking out of his socks hem. The feeling of uncomfortablility was only growing as Kyle winced, grabbing his shoe and pulling it off.

    Kyle's eyes grew wide, his foot now replaced with....a paw? "What the fuck?" Kyle's mind was racing, could he have already been cursed back by Susan and was forced to forget? Another surge as the fur began to inch it's way up his leg, now covering up to his calf. "What the FUCK!" Kyle shouted, the change was growing more and more. "It's has to be Jennifer's mom, she must be doing something that's changing everyone." Kyle looked down as his palm began to change too, the creases thickening as dark leathery pads began to form, his nails darkening as they started to grow longer.

    Grabbing his wrist Kyle bolted from his living room. Tripping in his haste, forgetting to put his shoe back on, if it could even fit anymore. Kyle staggered out of the house looking around, he had to figure out what was happening before it was too late.
