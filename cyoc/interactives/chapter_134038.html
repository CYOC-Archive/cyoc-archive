    Because of the successful delivery of 18 anaconda Animalians, America was shaken up somewhat though not as much as other times. The comments on the Animalia website quickly went into the tens of millions, but there were other things that resulted from it.

    ---

    Not only did the announcement of the 18 arrivals to the anaconda triumvirate, as they were now known, catch the attention of Guinness World Records, who delivered them an official certificate of recognition, but it also caught the attention of all the major news agencies such as Fox, Headline News, World News Tonight, and similar ones, not to mention the news/comedy show Highly Questionable. However, thanks to the relatively new PR-MD offices, they were able to keep things respectable, though everyone seemed to agree with the three anchors of Highly Questionable that the Anaconda Tribunal don't need any more children for a while.

    Also, to the Anaconda Triumvirate's surprise, they got more than $1 million dollars from the California state government to help care for their new offspring. Mary Piper also helped them decide on names for all their offspring, though most of her advice seemed to be "find a distinguishing feature and assign a name to that", but Mary's family was more or less helpful, both with names and health. Their 'help' seemed to warrant a new Cheaper by the Dozens episode starring both the "Piper in-laws", as they were now known, AND the Anaconda Triumvirate, but that would have to wait until the Piper Brood got back from their colony tour.

    "That's a quack cure, grandpa." said Mary to one of the SKYPE on Mieni Atoll.

    "You just didn't have any stomach for the Piper Applesauce Cure." replied the grandpa.

    "Not the Piper Applesauce Cure." groaned one cousin.

    "The last time MY family tried that," said one uncle, "there was applesauce everywhere."

    ---

    Because of the fact that a large number of families vacationing on Mieni Atoll had small children, there were a number of floatation devices designed to both teach them to swim and keep their heads above water, but these had to be modified to support infant Animalians, especially the Dinotopians. Fortunately they were easy to adjust to new body types, mainly being foam 'bladders' strung on a belt, and thereby easy to adjust and modify.

    Susan, Tzan, Chipper, and Brazen Hammer payed a visit to Ran and Gaz and their new pups, but Susan's joy at seeing them left almost everyone holding their ears. But only one day later, Summer heat hit both Susan and Tzan, and it was up to Brazen and Chipper to "cool them down", so to speak.

    ---

    It was rather fortunate that there were post-pregnancy workout classes to work off the baby weight, because most women felt that they would never lose their baby weight because they were too busy taking care of their new children. With help from the classes, they were able to reassume their pre-baby beauty.
