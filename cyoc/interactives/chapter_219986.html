    Such potions shouldn't be used until they were fully-prepared. The consequences of drinking a half-finished potion often aren't dire, but they would require more work to fix. And Morgana wasn't about to tarnish her reputation by selling an unfinished potion. Besides, her other two customers would be an easy fix.

    If Morgana didn't have more pressing concerns with her other customers, Summer Vogel and Kaylee Lewis would have been the first ones to arrive. As such, Morgana used her PMA to befuddle their way, put a few obstacles and distractions in, misdirect them a little. But now it was their turn. With an adjustment of power, Morgana cleared their way.

    Summer and Kaylee walked in, looking a little hot and tired from their longer-than-necessary walk. "How did I keep missing this place?" asked Summer, wiping some sweat from her forehead, her sandy blonde hair looking rather damp from the perspiration.

    The much darker Kaylee gave her an annoyed look. "I'm really starting to doubt your sense of direction," said the African-American girl.

    "Hey, it wasn't my fault those sidewalks were closed," said Summer.

    "Ladies, ladies, no need to fight," said Morgana, "I'm quite free to help you now."

    Summer sighed and said, "I really hope you can. Because we want to be-"

    "Breeders instead of warriors?" asked Morgana.

    "How'd you know?" asked Kaylee.

    "Trade secret," said Morgana, "And as for your request, I've got good news and bad news. The good news is that your transformation will be easy to do."

    "So what's the bad news?" asked Kaylee.

    "It's out of my jurisdiction," said Morgana.

    "What do you mean out of your jurisdiction? You've helped changed us into werehyenas in the first place," said Summer.

    "That is true, but there are certain...social observations that have to be made now that you're becoming an official pack," said Morgana, "And there are some things that need to be handled within the pack. Werehyenas have a somewhat strict regulation of their breeders. For one thing, it's a major taboo for breeders to use their cocks on anyone but other breeders. And strictly speaking, only the pack's shaman should be changing warriors to breeders and vice versa."

    "What for?" asked Kaylee.

    "Well, as I understand it, some of the more traditional werehyenas would see a warrior becoming a breeder as a demotion and should only be done as punishment or if the pack's numbers have fallen too low. And as such, breeders being promoted to warriors is either due to a reward or because the pack needs as many fighters as they can get for some conflict," said Morgana.

    "Breeders aren't exactly helpless creatures," said Kaylee. Yes, the breeders her friends became weren't as muscular as she had become when she changed, but they hardly looked frail compared to humans.

    "True, but other werebeasts can be a good deal stronger than a breeder werehyena," said Morgana, "Be thankful there aren't any werelions in this state. And they're unlikely to move in here while the werewolf pack is still in power. Lions and wolves aren't quite as traditional enemies as, well, cats and dogs, but they're extremely unlikely to share territory."

    "So what you're saying is that we have to go see Taylor about becoming breeders," said Summer.

    "Yes, though it shouldn't be too hard for her to manage after some guidance from her mentor, Shaman Kweli. But I can send word to her to tell her to teach Taylor the spell," said Morgana. She was planning contacting Kweli anyways so that she can tell Enzi that the potion to restore hir testicles was ready and shi could come up to Stokerville.

    Kaylee gave a groan of frustration and said, "All that walking around for nothing."

    "Me and Riley will make it up to you at Chloe's party," said Summer, "And believe me, shi really knows how to use hir dick."

    Morgana felt a twinge of guilt for delay their arrival only to redirect them elsewhere. "Wait a moment," said Morgana, "I've got a little something for you so you haven't wasted a trip." She refiled around in her discount charms, most of which only worked for 24 hours. She eventually produced a pair of garnet earrings and held them out. "Here, I'm sure you'll get some mileage out of them," she said.

    "What are these?" asked Summer, taking the earrings.

    "They're synchronizing earrings," said Morgana, "Each of you put one on."

    The girls shrugged before they each affixed one earring. "So what now?" asked Kaylee, scratching the back of her head.

    Summer jumped and said, "Something just touched my head."

    "As long as you two are wearing the earrings, you'll feel everything the other one feels on top of your own," said Morgana.

    Kaylee's eyes lit up. "So sex between us would feel double as great?" she asked.

    "Indeed, though the earrings will run out of power eventually. Also, you'll feel each other's pain as well as pleasure, so try not to do anything stupid while you're wearing them," said Morgana.

    "Is that so?" asked Summer before pinching her arm.

    "Ow!" yelped Kaylee, "Hey, I didn't pinch you!" She smacked Summer on the back of the head, only to wince and rub the back of her own head.

    "And among those stupid things you shouldn't do is get into fights," said Morgana flatly.

    "I'll definitely want to share this with Riley later," said Summer, "The pleasure part, not the pain part."

    "Ok, can we go to that party now?" asked Kaylee.

    "Sure, it's going to be more fun than before," said Summer.

    "You girls have fun," said Morgana, "Hopefully you'll both be breeders when I next see you again." Once the two girls left, Morgana went back to check on her potion. The potion has gone through its final simmering stage and now was ready. All that was left for Morgana to do was call in her last foreseeable customer today.
