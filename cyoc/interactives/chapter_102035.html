    The New Bestet

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by JesseDracoWalker 7 years ago A

    You were walking as a question came to mind. "Anubis, will we be able to... Appear human?"

    "What do you mean?"

    "Consider this, you are a mortal, it has been more than two thousand years since Anubis... Was seen, how would you react if you saw him?"

    "I'd be frightened..... Hmm, I see your point. But don't worry. Despite you becoming the new Anubis, you can be how you want to be seen. Don't worry... You'll figure it out. But you either may work storage or together. You lot decide."

    As you walk alone with your thoughts and Anubis, fur spreading slowly up your back, you spot a shadow in a chamber, it's Emily. Walks lined with cats. "Bastet..." Anubis said to your mind. "Let us observe then talk to her. We cannot enter right away."

    --------------------------------

    Emily walked down the dark corridor trying to find her way to the others. "Guys, where.... Ooo cats. I love cats."

    "What a purrrfect thought. You are not alone there."

    Emily turned around. "Who.... Are...." She looked from the feline to a statue then back. "Funny... You look like Bastet..."

    "Very observant. I am she. I am glad to meet you Emily. The popular girl in school, great friends, a loving mother and father...."

    "...a spoiled brother." Emily adds.

    "Actually one who looks up to you. But it is no coincidence that you are here. You see, one of you friends has already met with one of my fellow deities, Anubis to be specific and accepted the task, will you become the new Bastet?"

    "I don't know, I mean I think it's cool and all but why?"

    "Over the millenniums, child, fewer and fewer believed and we grew weaker and well we have little to no influence and you five can change that. Be the mother to all."

    "And if I decline?"

    "You will return to your field trip, and I'll find another. But, you love cats, well, you'd have many as you pets and many worshippers."

    "Well... I think...." Emily paced back and forth. "I'll do it."

    "Wonderful." Bastet walked up and hugged her fading. "All the people are your children."

    Emily looks forward right at you. "Hello.... Anubis."

    You smile. "Hello Emily.... Or should I say Bastet?"

    "Well to our followers it's Bastet. You... Emily."

    "Shall we see who the others get?"

    "Yes."
