"Each player will choose you to tell the truth about something or dare you to complete a task. Sure, he was a bit winded but after finishing them, he smiled. "That's what you could do if you lost some of that weight. Eh?" Adrian's arms crossed against his chest. Then rolled the die again. Another four. "Show what you know," he said as he grabbed the card. "Where do all the missing jockstraps and equipment keep going?" He paused as the other three looked at him. Everyone on the football team knew they had a thief in their mists, however, none of them could prove it. Just random days there would be someone's missing jock strap or socks or jersey. Sometimes people would notice before practice and sometimes after. No one knew, because there was never a day where at least someone was accounted for. And the thief stole from literally everyone. Including Coach Bradshaw.
