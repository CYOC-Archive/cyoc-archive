    “I’m here!” Rob’s voice said, coming from inside of Peter!

    “Hey, this is weird!” Tim’s voice said, also coming from inside of the giant teen.

    Of course, Ty and Allen were reeling with laughter! Allen laughed so hard, that he fell off the lounge chair, and Ty almost felt a pain in his throat from laughing, which angered the giant teen.

    “Let’s splash him!” Tim said, using Peter’s mouth.

    “I say we jump in the pool!” Rob answered.

    “GERONIMOOOO!!!” the giant, naked teen shouted, as his overgrown body came down, plunging into the pool and completely drenching Ty and Allen, before Ty could even finish saying his wish!

    “Haaaahahaha!!!” Ryan laughed, since the giant muscle boy had just witnessed the part where the giant Peter plunged into the pool, completely covering Ty and Allen with a huge wall of water!

    “Peter! This is awesome! You’re a giant, like me!!” Ryan cheered, as he dove into the pool, splashing even more water over Ty and Allen!

    Just as Ryan came up and looked Peter in the eye, he looked down and noticed that the giant Peter wasn’t wearing his Speedo!

    “Hey, what is this?” Ryan said angrily, as he folded his muscular arms in front of his huge pectorals. “You should at least wear your Speedo or your swimming trunks before you go swimming, big brother! Since when do you swim naked?”

    “I was… We WERE wearing our Speedos!” Peter shouted back at Ryan. “Ty made us outgrow them!”

    “No problem!” Ryan grinned. “I’ll fix that! I wish for giant Peter to be wearing a giant Speedo right now!”

    Ryan also had the power to change things, and suddenly the giant Peter was wearing his Speedo!

    When Peter and Ryan turned and saw how Ty and Allen were still laughing at them, even though they were completely drenched while still wearing their school clothes, the two giants decided that it was their turn to have some fun.

    “SPLASH THEM, BRO!” Peter shouted, and both Peter and Ryan used their giant arms to drench Ty and Allen even more!

    “AAARGGG… STOP!!!” Ty screamed back at them. “I’m SOAKING WET ALREADY!!!”

    “No! Ty fused Rob and Tim inside of my body and it made me grow to your size!” Peter replied.

    “Hi Rob! Hi Tim! I’m Peter’s brother Ryan!” Ryan said to Peter! “This is so cool! I want you to stay like this for the rest of the day, so we can play and have some fun!”

    Peter immediately felt Ryan’s power work inside of him, and he rolled his eyes as he chuckled something to himself and the two other guys who shared his body.
