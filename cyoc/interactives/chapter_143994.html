    You inwardly beg.

    As if your call was heeded, your scales shine once more. But its light was far dimmer than before, just enough to be a beacon in the darkness. Tears threaten to fall from your serpentine eyes, just happy to bring some relief to your friends.

    You give a hiss, a low, reassuring hiss. You look to each of them in their eyes, finding steely determination hidden beneath tiredness. Maybe, maybe they all just needed some sleep…

    They sleep...

    You rise awake from your dreamless sleep, finding yourself feeling just a bit better. However, something within you just screams that it hadn’t even been an hour since all of you went to sleep.

    Well, not all of you. Jessie was using your tail as a makeshift pillow, conforming to her head softly. Sam herself didn’t really sleep. She stood upright, aware, but asleep at the same time. Some sort of rest but aware state?

    “We’re ready then?” Sam stands, awaiting her newfound friends' response to her call. She smiles when you and Alex also rise up, stretching and Alex somewhat unconsciously posing.

    You turn towards Sam, her already walking to the exit of the store room. She turns to you, standing in line with the door, her slender form framed by the dim light seeping through the door. “So..., are we ready?”
