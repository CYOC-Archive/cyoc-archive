    Mary opened the door and peered into the empty classroom.
    The light's were on, and Miss Ethel's bags were by her desk, but Mary had come in early to prepare for her grand presentation.

    After looking around, she walked over to her desk and removed the engraved wooden box from her backpack.
    Mary grinned with anticipation as she opened the precious container and peered inside.

    So many choices... she whispered to herself, as she gazed over the row's of potion's.

    After looking them over for a second she looked up at the clock.
    School will starting soon, I better pick one quickly, she thought.

    Suddenly, she had an idea. Her eye's peered over to Miss Ethel's desk, and she spotted her teacher's familiar coffee mug.
    What if...she started.

    She hesitated for a minute, thinking about her potential action.
    "Well, I geuss it would be a really good prank, and It shouldn't cause any harm." She reasoned, knowing the vial's didn't effect a person's mental state.

    Mary got up from her chair, and looked around the room again.
    Miss Ethel must be at a meeting this mourning, she decided.

    She quickly rushed over to the coffee mug(which was thankfully already half empty), and poured in two random vials, before rushing back to her seat and closing the box of vial's.

    Mary breathed a sigh of relief, thankful that nobody had spotted her.
    She put the box away, and peered at the two of the vial's she had used.

    "Growth" the vial read, "Causes subject's to grow proportionally." It also had a measurement of how much growth the subject would experience based on amount ingested. Based on the amount she had used, Mary estimated that Miss Ethel would grow about 2.5 - 3 feet in height.
    Well why not, she thought, Miss Ethel wasn't exactly a giant at 5'2.

    She turned to read the next vial...

    "Oh hello Mary, your here early." said a familiar voice.

    Mary quickly slipped the vial into her Jacket.
    "Hi Miss Ethel." she stated

    Miss Ethel was about 33 years old, with very few wrinkles and lush red hair. She was wearing her usual light blue dress, with formal dress shoes. She was one of Mary's favorite teacher's, and she was widely regarded for her enthusiastic personality.

    She smiled as she crossed the room and sat at her desk.

    Mary looked at the clock, which showed that the rest of the class would arrive any minute.
    Her heart was beating with anticipation, and she carefully looked down at the second vial she had used...
