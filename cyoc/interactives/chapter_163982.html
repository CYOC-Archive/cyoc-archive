    "Can someone pick my mom off the floor please," Anjelica asked sheepishly. Stunned by this teenage girls appearance Ann helped assist her girlfriend onto the couch, before staring at Anjelica. "So your Jackie's daughter? Does that mean..."

    "Probably best not to ask too many questions Aunt Ann," Morgan smiled.

    "Okay question," Mary Elise exclaimed. "With all the future kids we've met how in the blue hell can your apprentice come back? Jackie's not pregnant is she?"

    "What no," Morgan exclaimed quickly as Anjelica blushed crimson. "We're actually from about 34 years in the future."

    "Thirty four years," the group exclaimed. "How!"

    "Well I am Morgan Lefay," Morgan smirked impishly. "Or well I was. Regardless with a little help from Lynne and Grandpa Jack. We were able to figure out a way to bring Anjelica with me. Probably why you felt a bit of a tremor, took quite a bit of magic to get Anjelica here when she hasnt even been conceived."

    "Grandpa Jack," Katie whispered. "You mean my father." Morgan nodded as she now scanned the group. "Yeah him," she said. "He figured you could use all the help you could get. Villar is paranoid right now, ever since Aaron and Haley showed up."

    "So the future is changing again," Gloria asked. A sad smile now spread across Morgans face as she looked at Gloria. "Not as much as we would like Aunt Gloria," Morgan said sadly. "Villar is trying to kill Lilith now, we have to try and stop him."

    "Is that even possible," Kellie asked in awe. "With him it is," Morgan said shuddering. "He's figured out a way to kill the immortals. Merlin, Lilith, Tatsu, everyone of the leaders of the magical families is in danger. Grandpa Jack knew that if the leaders were to die it would be total anarchy here."

    "So what do we need to do," Gloria asked. Before Morgan could answer Haley stepped forward, narrowing her eyes as she stared at Morgan and Anjelica.

    "Hold up one sec," she exclaimed. "The Morgan I know is gun shy about useing her full magic. Shes all worried about going crazy and letting her dark roots shine through."

    Morgan chuckled softly narrowing her eyes as she began to change. The group now watched as Morgans blond hair began to darken, her ears beginning to elongate as her eyes glistened with a dark power. As the transformation completed the group beheld the Morgan LeFay of legend.

    "The teenage me was worried about the darker aspects of my magic," she grinned. "As I've gotten older though and with the help of my mothers I realized something. My origins may be darkness but that's not what I was raised to be. I use my magic for good, the way my mothers taught me."

    "So your still a mommy's girl then," Haley stated bluntly, causing Morgan to laugh. "Guilty," she laughed. "By the way Lynne told me to tell you Bite me."

    Haley now laughed, shaking her head. "Yeah the sounds like Lynne," she laughed.

    "Am I missing something," Katie asked.

    "Haley and Lynne butt heads all the time," Morgan said. "Everything between them is a competition. Their two sides of the same coin."

    Haly scoffed at the notion as Morgan now turned to her apprentice. "As fun as this is we do need to hurry," she said. "Liliths life is in danger. Hopefully our combined power is enough to stop Villar."

    Katie and Gloria looked to eachother and nodded. To save Lilith they would need to move fast.
