    The paper read: "Cutie Girly Plush."
    "Wha...?" exclaimed Chad, utterly confused and terrified simultaneously.
    What the meaning of this? But the fur was spreading and... And it did feel synthetic. And worse, it was really coloring itself a soft blue pink hue indeed! It was reaching his torso and showed no sign of stopping! Chad started to cry and he screamed some more and it utterly engulfed him, then reaching his insides, turning all of his body into stuffing and soft plushy polyester. He could still move and talk, he had kept his size, he could move his mouth and blink he plastic eyes but otherwise, he was a giant stuffed animal. A round, girly one at that. Mortified, he touched around his face after staring, appalled, at his new hands. They were round, with no fingers, a useless thumb and a white round patch in the middle. And they were a soft pink color, which covered the rest of his body, apart from his round stomach, also white. His face might be the same but he could feel two round ears and a heart-shaped bulbous plastic nose. He had become a giant teddy bear! A living harmless teddy bear! And a girl and pink one at that! Horrible! And he had no more genitalia! No more dick! He was just something that was adorable! And not in the good way like girls who wanted to have sex with him! He was just a toy for little girls now! Pink! Cutesy! His macho mind reeled under the humiliation.
    "It can't be happening! Help!" he shouted.
    Even his voice was cutesy and feminine! What a nightmare!
    "Like your new body? I think it's an improvement, personally," smirked Rick as he came out of the shadows and confronted the humiliated bully who deserved it.
    "You? You did this? Change me back!" demanded Chad, quite ridiculously so in his teeny tiny adorable voice.
    "Out of the question. Where I am sending you, you'll become a source of joy. Something you could never become here. Enjoy your new life!" laughed Rick, snapping his fingers.
    "What? No! Wait..." said Chad.
    But it was too late. Already he was transported to a new location. Rick smiled widely. The giant creature from another world he had promised Chad to would have so much fun with him...
