    Rick's cab pulled up to the resort and he got out after paying the driver. After he removed his luggage from the trunk he took a moment to look at the resort. All he could think was that it was huge. He had heard that each of the three wings were a small city unto themselves but he thought that was just talk. Now standing out front he knew that to be true. He grew excited as he thought that the resort's transformative powers were true as well. He picked up his bags and walked inside.

    The lobby was all Mahogany wood and there was a crystal chandelier hanging in the middle. The lighting was soft and the whole atmosphere was warm and inviting. A clerk looked up as he approached the front desk and she said "Good afternoon sir and welcome to Transformation Resort. My name is Amy. How may I help you?" She said this with a genuine smile on her face instead of the fake one Rick had seen on so many other hotel clerks.

    "Good afternoon to you too" Rick replied. "I have a reservation here. Name's Richard Moss."

    "Alright sir" Amy said still smiling. She typed a few things into her computer. A minute later she looked back up and said "Your rooms are ready sir."

    "Rooms?" Rick asked perplexed. He had reserved a room for himself so he couldn't see why Amy had said rooms.

    "Each of our guests have three rooms" Amy said. "One in each of our three wings. This is so you can fully experience what our resort has to offer."

    "I see" Rick said nodding like he really did.

    "Here's your card key for your rooms" Amy said handing you a plastic card in a small envelope. "The trolley hub is just down the hall. The trolleys run on a schedule day and night. There may be a short wait but there are seats in the hub to sit on if you'd like. Here's a map of each of our wings and the timetable for the trolleys. I'll get someone to help you with your bags."

    "That won't be necessary" Rick said.

    "Very well sir" Amy said. "Enjoy your stay."

    "Thank you" Rick said as he picked up his bags and started down the hall. He emerged in the trolley hub and seeing that there were no trolleys present he sat down on a chair. There was no one else in the hub so far. He studied the maps and trolley schedules. Opening up the envelope containing his room key and noticed a little pamphlet in there too. Having nothing better to do he read through it. It explained what each of the wings did and how to activate the transformation. There was an advisory with the Age wing saying to call the front desk if one planned on regressing to younger than sixteen and they were alone.

    Rick looked up and saw three trolleys coming into the hub from the wings. Now he had to choose which wing to go to.
