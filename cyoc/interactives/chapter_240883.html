    “Kyle? Well beggars can’t be choosers. Come on up!” I announce as Kyle Hopkins got up from his seat and headed straight to the stage. A few good looking boys that would be classified as a Casanova with his handsome face, 5‘11“ tall, and physique. He was muscular and was surprising wearing a wrestlers outfit for his costume which confuses me because I believe he was on the wrestling team, but I don’t hear him being acknowledged that much. Which makes him the prime candidate for my next trick.

    “Thanks for volunteering, Kyle But you do know what I was trying to imply right?” I asked Kyle as I shook his hand but Kyle just chuckled as soon as we finish our hand tag.

    “I suppose it would be giving little Hugh here some more meat and more height.” Kyle said as he gave Hugh a pat on the back. Hugh just pout at the jab about his body which frankly shows a sharp contrast to Kyle with Hugh’s plain looking face, 5‘6“ tall, and flexible but plain looking body. All of a sudden, many members of the audience mostly female start to protest a little as if I’m taking their favorite toy away.

    “Well…yes, but are you okay with this? I mean, I just sound of the audience. They would be disappointed and basing on the costume…wait a minute, isn’t this your wrestling uniform? You’re on the wrestling team?” I asked which seems to add a little bit of fuel to the fire in the audience. Kyle just took a sigh as he took a breath in.

    “Even though I have the uniform, mascot of the wrestling team is more of a fitting description of what I am.” Kyle answered, which caused a lot of gasps from the audience. Okay now I’m intrigued and by the looks of it Hugh was as well before we can ask, it seems that Kyle was going to answer. “I’m gonna be honest. I don’t like being on the wrestling team because all I am is basically a mascot. I’m not allowed to participate. I’m just sit there dress up and be an eye candy for the girls that show up. In all honesty the team is not very well and I’m just there to draw in the female crowd.” Kyle said causing all the boys in the audience to pout while the girls just smiled.

    “Well than let’s fix that.” I said as I motioned both volunteers to the two boxes I used on Ashley and Bessie. As we did, the girls in the audience started booing followed by a smack by their respective boyfriends or boys sitting next to them. I motioned Monique to come help me move the boxes so the sides were facing the audience with the doors facing away from each other.

    I motioned Hugh to enter one box and he obliged. I then close the door and locked it before repeating the process with Kyle and the other box. I made a couple silent wishes as I moved to in between the boxes. I snapped my fingers and a wand appeared to my hand. I tapped the boxes with the wand and four tabs appeared but they appeared odd.

    “Now these tabs are folded up because they tell four things about our volunteers. For example, this top tab tells about how good looking they are.” I said, as I unfolded the top tabs Kyle’s say Handsome while Hugh’s say Plain looking. “While the next one down is about their body type.” I proceeded to unfold those tabs; Kyle’s said Tall & Muscular while Hugh’s said Average & Flexible. “Then the third ones is supposed to tell how strong they are.” I then proceeded to unfold those tabs: Kyle’s said Strong while Hugh’s said Average. “Finally these last sets tells us something special about the individuals.” I finally unfolded those tabs; Kyle’s said Hidden Talent: Wrestling while Hugh’s said In love with Ashley Jackson.

    Everyone looked at Ashley who was blushing like crazy, she probably suspected Hugh had a crush on her but this confirmed it and it seems she has a crush on him as well. This should be fun.

    “Well first let’s exchange the first three taps.” I said, as I went to switched around the tabs from one box to another. Kyle’s box now said Plain looking, Average & Flexible, Average, and Hidden Talent: Wrestling while Hugh’s box now said Handsome, Tall & Muscular, Strong, and In love with Ashley Jackson. I turn to face the audience to gaged their reaction; some were confuse at why I didn’t moved the bottom tabs. Most of the girls were just pouting in disappointment.

    “Now the reason why I didn’t move around the bottom tabs is because well it would defeat the purpose. But however, I will admit something about Kyle’s bottom tab has to change.” I said, as I went back to Kyle’s box and took off the last tab. “After all given Kyle‘s new build, it would be impossible for him to be on the wrestling team.” I pretend to look like I was contemplating for a while before getting an idea. I made a silent wish before tapping the wand on the tab before placing it back on Kyle’s box. It now said Hidden Talent: Gymnastics. I turned back to the audience, wishing my wand turned into a staff.

    “Now for the final part.” I announced making the silent wishes to make these permanent. I tapped my staff on the floor and a wave of golden energy shot out encompassing the room. Everyone in the audience blinked once or twice as I went over to the boxes and unlatched them.

    “Now come out boys and let’s see the new you.” I said as the doors from the boxes opened. From Hugh’s boxed came a 5‘11“ tall, muscular, handsome 17-year-old boy wearing a Saytr costume which now due to him being taller and muscular; looked more right. The only downside was his legs weren’t real goat legs but was wearing fur jeans. From Kyle’s boxed came out a average 5 foot six tall plain but flexible 18 year old boy wearing a gymnastics leotard.

    Both boys look at each other as they examine them their new selves, with Kyle giving a few stretches. I glanced at the audience real quick to see that the girls who were popping for Kyle looked disappointed however, a few were gushing at Hugh. Ashley looked like a blushing bubbling mess. I turned to address the two boys.

    “So how do you guys like the way you look?” I asked. Kyle, who was giving a few practice bounces stopped and look at me.

    “This feels awesome thank you.” Kyle said with a smile on his face.

    “Thanks.” Said Hugh.

    “Give it up for our two volunteers.” I said as the audience erupted into applause. Hugh walked off the stage while Kyle summer salted.

    I watch as few of the girls pouted as the newly handsome Hugh walked right past them before stopping in front of Ashley who got out of her blushing stupor. Hugh gently pulled Ashley out of her seat before taking the seat for himself, then guiding her to sit on his lap. Boy Ashley was a red as a tomato from sitting on her new boyfriend’s lap. I cleared my throat, gaining the audience attention.

    “You know, I enjoy these last two group volunteers. Let’s try one more time. Any volunteers?” I asked as a lot of hand shot out. I looked through the audience carefully before smiling.
