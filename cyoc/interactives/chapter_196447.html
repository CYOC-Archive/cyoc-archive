    Nikki's Immediate Surroundings Appeared To Be A Luxurious Bedroom, Study Area With Books Lining The Walls Bookshelves Filled With Thousands Of Books A Closet With Hundreds Of Dresses And Other Clothing She Would Take Years To Try Them All On, If She Took The Stairs Up She Would Find A Marvelous Bathroom With Everything She Could Possibly Want And Shower And Bath Separate Multiple Sinks A Toilet That Looked So Expensive She Was Reluctant To Use But Seemed To Be Specifically Built With Her In Mind And Downstairs She Would Find A Enormous Kitchen With Cabinets Filled With Everything She Could Want And Even Though She Had No Real Talent For Cooking There Were Every Conceivable Appliance The Fridge Was Loaded With Ingredients And Next To The Fridge Was A Cookbook With Extremely Detailed Instructions On How To Make Anyone A 5 Star Chef

    Nikki Spent Days Living In This Tower Before Something Finally Happened A Knight In Armour Arrived

    Nikki Immediately Rushed Over And Said "Oh My Hero, You Have Come To Save Me" (Nikki Thinks Lucas Did This And Is Playing Along)

    The Knight Removes His Helmet And Appears Sir Lance Feefer

    Nikki Immediately Drops The Act And Says "Sir Lance--?? Wait Sir Lance Wait Why Am I Saying Sir Lance?? Well Whatever Sir Lance What Happened To Peasant Lucas I Mean Peasant Lucas Why Am I Adding Peasant ??"

    Sir Lance Says "My Princess You Needn't Worry About Some Peasant Your Knight Has Arrived"

    Nikki Says "Please Sir Lance Where Is Peasant Lucas? And Why Have You Done This To The World"

    Sir Lance Says "Fine If You Don't Wanna Play Along Princess I Could Always Make You Act Like Your Supposed To!"

    Nikki Says "Wait Sir Lance I Princess Nikki Of Brighton Humbly Beg Your Forgiveness You Must Forgive Me For I Have Been Locked In This Tower By The Evil Dragon And Await True Loves Kiss To Free Me From My Bonds"

    Sir Lance Says "And So It Shall Be Done"

    Princess Nikki Backs Away And Says "Please Wait Sir Lance My Twin Sister Princess Maxine Is Another Tower Suffering The Same Fate As I, I Beg Of You To Save Your Kiss For Her As She Is The True Heir To The Brighton Kingdom And Thus Is Worthy Of Such An Honorius Knight Such As Yourself, But Before You Leave Please Send Word To The Nearby Villages That I Princess Nikki Shall Do The Honour Of Marrying A Peasant Should They Come To Save Me And Grant Me True Love's Kiss"
