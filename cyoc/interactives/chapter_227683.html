    "Hey does anyone have a map of the island," Luca asked. For a moment the group stared at one another, deep in thought before Set spoke. "I think so," she said, slightly unsure. "No we definitely have one," Matt interrupted. "Set help me check the backseat."

    Quickly the mummy and dragon began rifling though papers. Despite Bong trying to maintain the interior, the car was used on a regular basis. Set now haphazardly tossed aside a coloring book, much to Jolies dismay. "Hey I haven't finished with that," she groaned. "I'm just moving it relax," Set called. Reaching into a pocket in the seat, she now fished a paper map of the island, displaying several strategic areas.

    "What are you thinking," Bong asked, watching as Luca now looked over Matt and Sets shoulders. Luca's eyes went wide as he jabbed his finger into a part of the map. "There," he said. "Its an armory not too far from here. I say we raid it and use whatever in there to blow the keep sky high." The others all looked to one another before shrugging their shoulders. It wasn't the worse idea they'd ever come up with.

    Fifteen minutes later Bong and the others found themselves within one of the many armorys around the Serpent Society's island. With the chaos the group had caused, the trip was mostly uneventful, allowing them to break in with relative ease. "Geez," Bong said in awe. "These guys have more guns than some countries."

    "And that's why I needed your help," Matt stated. "The Serpent Society has threatened my people long enough. I'm hoping the damage we do today will just make them a footnote in history." Set and the others nodded, gathering up ammunition and weapons as they went. "Set come here," Bong exclaimed. Set quickly ran towards her friend, the mummy worried about why Bong was telling. "What!" Bong smirked, pointing towards a large APC carrier complete with Vulcan machine gun. "Holy shit," Set gasped. "Think you can drive it," Bong asked, holding out a key. Set grinned, snatching the key as she raced towards the vehicle.

    "Bong! Bong! Can I have this?"

    Bong turned, her eyes growing wide as she stared at Jolie. "Where the hell did you find a Stinger missle?" Jolie giggled, her.eyes wide as a maniacal smile spread across her face. "Over there,," she said pointing. "And look! Someone drew a smiling devil face on it. Can I keep it? I want to call it Mr. Smiley!" Bong just nodded, watching Jolie squeal with delight and hoping she wouldn't regret giving the necromancer an anti aircraft missle launcher.

    Gazing out from the guard tower, Zeke sighed. After the initial attack the island had gone into lockdown, a thorough search of the island conducted to find the assailants. That was an hour ago and with the patrols back, Zeke was only now beginning to relax. Maybe whoever had attacked was gone?

    Straining his eyes, Zeke could see one of the societies APC's rumbling down the road. It made sense in his opinion, the society was probably regrouping, looking to take the fight to these dragon lovers. As the APC approached, he signaled for the gate to open. Everything seemed normal until he noticed a white haired girl emerge from the roof hatch. Zeke didn't recognize her but her maniacal laughter echoed throughout the island as she produced a massive rocket launcher.

    "SAY HI TO MR. SMILEY!!!"

    The assault was over in 15 minutes. Jolie obliterated the guard tower, punching a massive hole in the keeps wall. This essential gave Bong and the others two entrances that they exploited to the fullest. As Matt emerged from the car, transforming into a full grown gold dragon, the others let loose with a torrent of missle and machine gun fire. The keep became a woodchipper. When the last bullet was fired all that remained if the Serpent Society was charred bodies and bits of organic material that were vaguely human.

    Setting the keep ablaze, Death Riot and Matt stole the ferry back to the mainland. The mission was a success, Death Riot acquiring a new vehicle and Jolies brand new Stinger missles. As a bonus they even acquired around $800,000 in cash, which Matt wanted half of. At the end of the day, they relented giving the dragon his cut. As Luca said as the dragon counted his money, its impossible to get a dragon to give up his horde.
