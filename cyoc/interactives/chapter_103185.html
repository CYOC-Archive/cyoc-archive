    Just to be Safe

    Ty was an avid reader, and his favorite stories were those involving the supernatural. Remembering that those that had powers like Ty's often did stupid things to cause them to lose their powers or give them to some one else, Ty decided to put in some protective measures. These are as follows;

    1) No matter what changes he makes to himself, Ty will always have his powers.
    2) If Ty swaps bodies with some one he'll still be able to use his powers, however the other person will not.
    3) Ty's powers can not be transferred to another person.
    4) From now on Ty must be fully conscious to make a wish.
    5) If Ty transforms himself into an animal he will never lose the ability to speak English, but he will gain the ability to make animal noises if he so chooses.
    6) If a transformation would cause certain death, then the transformation can't be done.
    7) Unless otherwise stated, everything belonging to the person being changed will adjust according to the changes being made.
    8) Unless otherwise stated, no body will notice any of the changes. Ty is the only exception to this rule.

    Ty was sure that with these rules in place he could avoid most of the pitfalls of having powers like his. But he knew that he might need to add to/change this list later, so he kept his mind open to all the possible things he could do with his powers.

    This train of thought brought him back to the week he had to play with Dustin. Before he could start he needed to know exactly what he had on his hands. Ty wished for a sheet of paper with all of his and Dustin's stats printed on it. The paper appeared in Ty's hand and he took a close look at it. This is what he saw;
