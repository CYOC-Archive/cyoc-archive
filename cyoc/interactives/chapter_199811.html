    The next few days were a horrible blur of fading in and out of anasthesia and sleep, finding stuff different, barely able to freak out before more work was going on. After just the first afternoon, she found herself in an empty cell, with nothing but a mirror and a tray of raw meat. She peeled back her gums, almost flinching in terror. All her teeth had been removed, and in their place, small needle-like fangs had been surgically implanted. Her Hollywood smile was gone. A voice crackled on the loudspeaker. "Traxilaxxians are carnivores, so your teeth and digestive system obviously had to be replaced." She turned ruefully, looking at the uncooked meat. Well, she was hungry...

    Later, after scarfing down the meat in a very unladylike way, she was dragged off and strapped to another table, tubes and wires being fed into her. People bustled about, ignoring her as she pleaded unsuccessfully. She glanced to the side, seeing a horrible sight. All of her blood was being drained out of her body. She turned wildly to wonder what was going on and saw another tube, seeing some blue liquid being pumped into her! And worse, where it was going in, her skin was turning a deep blue colour, spreading across her entire body.

    After a couple of days of this, Lily awoke wearily in the cell again, looking into the mirror, unable to even react in horror anymore. She was completely blue, and bald now, her hair having fallen out in clumps after the blood transfusion of Traxilaxxian blood. Atop her bare head, two parallel lines of spines had been implanted, leaving her with six short sharp spikes sweeping back from her head. Small patches of dark black scales were scattered over her forearms, shoulders and back. Her mouth was full of fangs, both of her breasts had become engorged to the size of volleyballs, the nipples a darker blue than the rest of her. She could feel a phantom limb behind her, but it wasn't. A small tail flicked to and fro above her buttocks, a thin coating of scales coating the upside of it. She was definitively not human anymore.
