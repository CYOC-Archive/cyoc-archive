    The girls moved to the bedroom. Jessica closed the door so they had privacy, then asked Bobbi “What should we swap first? Legs or faces?”

    “Let’s do our faces first” replied Bobbi. She offered the masks which she had been carrying.

    Jessica accepted the Hilary mask, then stood in front of her mirrored wardrobe door and pulled the latex mask over her own head. The mask instantly made her look a perfect likeness of a 68 year old Hilary Clinton as she had been during the 2016 election campaign.

    The former Jessica, now speaking with Hilary’s voice turned to Bobbi and said “I like to count to 60 in my head before removing the mask. I don’t want to take any risks”

    “Same” replied Bobbi. “Better safe then sorry. It must take the mask some time to absorb the features of your own face”

    After about a minute, the new ‘Hilary’ removed the latex mask but maintained her Hilary Clinton head “I wonder if I am actually a 70 year old woman now or whether the changes are just cosmetic?” She mused “It hasn’t altered my mind so I’m thinking the changes are just skin deep”

    “Your arms swelled to head size earlier, so it must be more than just a skin deep change” replied Bobbi as she waited for the now discarded mask to change to Jessica’s likeness.

    Soon the mask had changed to reflect Jessica’s features. Bobbi picked it up and found the neck opening. The latex stretched very easily and it was a simple job to place it over her own head. The mask had enough elasticity to constrict around her neck. When Bobbi looked in the mirror all she could see was Jessica and Hilary Clinton. She would be saying goodbye to her own head for the next two days.

    The new ‘Jessica’ waited a minute before removing the latex mask. As she expected she retained Jessica’s facial features and hair. She also now had Jessica’s voice. “I still find this absolutely amazing. Have you any idea how this works?”

    ‘Hilary’ replied “No idea. We will have to go back to that shop and ask. I wonder when they will reopen” Indeed the two girls had returned to the shop the previous Monday only to find it temporarily closed due to a ‘family bereavement’ according to a note on the door.

    By now the mask had changed to Bobbi’s likeness ‘Hilary’ placed it on her own head and promptly became ‘Bobbi’. The former Jessica was happy to become young and attractive once more.

    “Shall we swap legs?” Asked the new ‘Jessica’ handing the mask to ‘Bobbi’

    “Oh no! Your turn to be deformed”

    After a moments reflection ‘Jess’ agreed “Fair enough” She then sat down on the side of the bed and removed her heeled sandals. She picked up the mask which had once more regained Hilary Clinton’s features, before saying “I’ll see how much I can stretch the latex up my legs. If I can stretch it up past my knees I could just swap skirts with you and no one will see the line where my skin changes colour”

    “Worth a try” agreed ‘Bobbi’

    ‘Jess’ placed her foot into the Hilary mask then held the neck up as far as she could. Initially it reached the top of her shin but with just a little pressure she was able pull the stretchy neck of the mask up over her knee. Indeed as she pulled the mask up, which still resembled Hilary’s head, it appeared to absorb her leg.

    “Your leg is getting shorter” observed ‘Bobbi’

    “Do you think I should stop?” Asked ‘Jess’ “It’s as if if my leg is being absorbed into the mask”

    “Up to you, but I must admit I am curious”

    The new ‘Jess’ hitched up the skirt she was wearing to her waist so they could both watch as she the pulled the Hilary mask all the way up until the neck of the mask reached the top of her thigh. ‘Jess’ now looked like a one legged woman but instead of a stump she had Hilary Clinton’s head

    “That’s as far as I’m willing to go at the moment” “Jess” finally said.

    “You have gone further than I expected” her friend replied.

    ‘Jess’ waited a minute before peeling off the latex. As expected her leg was a copy of Hilary’s head. The two girls then watched the discarded mask and were very relieved when it began to lose Hilary’s features and transform into what resembled a stocking.

    ‘Well that’s a big relief” observed ‘Jess’

    Bobbi picked the latex stocking up. When it stretched out they could see it had toes with painted nails.

    “Shall I?” Asked ‘Bobbi”

    “Please”

    The swapping process proceeded smoothly. First ‘Bobbi” acquired a leg to match her face. Then ‘Jess’ was able the swap the Hilary stump for a leg that matched her own face. Ten minutes later the girls had repeated the process with the second leg.

    “Should we see how the guys are going” asked the new ‘Bobbi’
