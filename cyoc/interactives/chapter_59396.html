As he wandered the streets, he came up with a plan to get money. His plan was to start his own buisiness. To be specific, planned to have a courrier service. And unlike other services, his would offer 100% secrecy. Once he had outlined the plan, he set out to execute it. He found the building that he and Damion were now on top of. It was abandoned, and no one would probably buy it, so he emptied out the A/C structure and tapped in to the building's electricity. Once he had that done, all he needed were courriers to move the information around the city. Unfortunately, few people that disagreed with the government had dared announce their opinions, so there were few people suited for the job.

    "Well, I was the only one on my team and I had the entire city to cover. The only way I could do my job effectively was either take steroids or something more... 'practical'."
