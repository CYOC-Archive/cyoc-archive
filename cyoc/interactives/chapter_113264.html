    The nasty dragoness Crossung the Ugly

    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous 6 years ago A O

    Now Wizard Darduct's face had grown grave when you'd first mentioned
    the name Crossungs so you settled into your quarters and then
    used your feminine charms to ask the guards a few questions about
    Wizard Dardact's past.

    What you pieced together was that Wizard Dardact had once
    had a wife who couldn't concieve.

    So Dardact had gone off to some distant dragon cave in another
    kingdom,collected the bones from some dragon .

    Apparently this dragon had practiced the dark arts,burnt down villages
    gobbled up villagers and generally wholly deserving of the dwarvish
    windlance bolt she'd got in the chest during one night burning a village.

    It had been a mortal wound and she'd died shortly after returning to her
    cave in the mountains.

    So Wizard Dardact had collected her bones,powdered them up and fed
    the potion to his wife,who had gotten pregnant and
    died after giving birth to their baby human daughter
    Ungalina.

    On her 16th birthday ,Wizard Dardact,who couldn't see a curse if
    it walked up and shook his staff,had concocted a
    beautifying potion to please his deperate daughter Ungalina.

    For you see Ungalina had been the ugliest 16 year old girl in the
    kingdom.

    So Wizard Dardact had convinced kind King Rufus to give Ungalina
    the beautifying potion.

    So whilst the Courtiers had kept straight faces the King had given Ungalina
    the potion.

    She drank it down all at once and was thrilled when the King and all those in the Courtroom
    gazed apon her face and told her she was the fairest maiden in the Northe Countrye.

    They were still keeping a straight face.

    The romantic dream for Ungalina lasted for three days,when the village idiot,who wasn't in on the joke,told Ungalina matter of factly that
    her face was as ugly as it ever was,if not more.

    So a shrieking mad with grief and rage Ungalina
    had locked herself up in her dads laboratory in the West Tower.

    There she found the dragon bones,and her fathers journal
    of how he'd used them to concieve her.

    Well if it were a choice between people fearing and hating her or
    laughing at her,she much preferred the former.

    Knowing full well what she was about to turn herself into,the furious
    maiden had ground up the dragon bones and tossed them into the big iron
    cauldron that sat in the center of the laboratory,stoked up the fire
    ,tossed in a few newts eyes for good measure and danced
    around the bubbling cauldron chanting various evil spells
    and then drank down a good deal of the mixture
    and just hung around waiting for something to happen.

    Her dad spent the next few hours pounding at the locked
    door,but there was no answer from within.

    Then as midnight approached Wizard Dardact ,sensing the gathering of powerful magics
    within his laboratory,managed
    to get everyone away from the West Tower just before a still
    human and still ugly as ever
    Ungalina,but with a serious case of stomach burn,,had
    a sense of what was about to happen to her
    and rolled over on her hands and knees,and exploded into her new dragon body,soul and mind.

    The west tower blew apart as the huge new black scaled flamebreathing dragoness
    was born,and Crossung the Laidley Orm began her new existence.

    Which meant of course Crossung Farm had a serious side to it...
