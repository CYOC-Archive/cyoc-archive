    Kyle's Prison

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 12 years ago BM O

    Kyle woke up Saturday morning. As he began his day, he wondered what had become of Jen, whom he had cursed the day before. "I bet she's a puddle in some alley somewhere.", thought Kyle. "That bitch got what she deserved." Kyle got dressed and went downstairs to watch some TV. Just as he sat down, the doorbell rang. Neither of his parents were home, so he got up and answered it. "Hey, Kyle!" Kyle was a bit surprised. Standing on the porch in front of him was the red-haired girl who had tormented him at school, Jen. She stood there, smiling at him. "I was in the neighborhood and thought I'd stop by." Kyle tried to keep his cool. "Well, Jen. I didn't expect you'd return to your original form." Kyle smirked. "I thought you'd be too chicken to absorb anything. Who did you swallow up? Your parents?" Jen chuckled. "No. I'm not completely heartless. Let's see... some woman I saw gardening, a little girl named Lisa, her mother, Sarah, and Sarah's wife, Dave. Those are all the people I've absorbed."

    Kyle leaned against the door frame, trying to appear calm. His thoughts raced as he tried to predict Jen's next move. "Is she going to absorb me?", thought Kyle. "I should run. No! She'll just stretch or something." Kyle stood there as Jen stared straight into his eyes. "Um... Would you like to come in and sit down?", asked Kyle. He wanted to seem as polite as possible. "Sure, Kyle." Jen didn't seem upset with him at all.

    They sat in Kyle's living room in silence. Jen stared at Kyle the whole time. "So... Jen. I know that you've been a bit of a bully to me, in the past. I'd just like to say that I forgive you for that." Jen's face froze. The same icy gaze broke for a moment as she had a face like a deer in headlights. Suddenly, Jen began to laugh. Kyle sat there as she laughed for a while. "Oh, Kyle... I don't care about that at all. I can see that you're just trying to talk your way out of some kind of punishment. Well, I want to thank you. Now, I have this body. It's a lot better than my old one. I couldn't be happier." Kyle breathed a sigh of relief. "So, you're not going to absorb me?" Jen smiled. "Not exactly."

    Jen's arms turned into goopy tentacles. They wrapped around Kyle, becoming a cocoon that covered all but his head. Jen began to grow taller, until she reached the ceiling of the room. She pulled Kyle toward her, pushing the cocoon into her stomach. It bulged like she was pregnant. She released Kyle, who was sitting in a small chamber inside her. "Have fun in there, Kyle! It's your new home!" Kyle frantically tried to push his way out. "Help!" Jen laughed. "Oh, Kyle... You're trapped!" Her abdomen was left transparent. "I'm gonna preserve you as a trophy. People will look at you and know that you made ME happen!" Jen waddled out of Kyle's house, beginning her rampage.
