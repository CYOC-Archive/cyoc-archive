    The two wolves come upon a deer. Gen was still full from his previous meal.

    Maria however pounces and attacks the deer. She begins to tear it to Pisces. Gen sees she was use to being a wolf then him.

    The deer eventually succoumbs to its attacker and dies. Maria looks at him.

    "Don't you want some Gen?" She ask him. "I already ate a deer before meeting you" said Gen.

    "Suit yourself" after finishing eating the deer. Gen and Maria continues their journey.

    Gen realize how much he was as a wolf then he was a human. The two stop for a rest.

    "So Gen tell me about yourself" said Maria. "Well I'm a kind Prince love by my people and hope of one day become a king. I hope I could make peace with the fairys. But as you can see. It didint go so well" said Gen.

    "I see. I was a beautiful princess and I was love by my people as well. I've heard about the fairys and wanted to see one. But my fate ended the same way yours did. Being a wolf is fun" said Maria.

    "I agree. But I want to live like a prince again" said Gen. "I feel the same. Hope we can change back. I want to know more about you"

    Maria was starting to have feelings for Gen. Gen was starting to feel the same even if they just met.
