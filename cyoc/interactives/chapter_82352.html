    The Dragoness stared at Ty with her huge, amber eyes. "You have incredible power, child... that is why it must belong to the dragons. I
    shall transform you." She said, her voice deafeningly loud.

    "But..." Ty began, gulping as his puny form hardly even compared to her big toe, "What if I don't WANT to be a dragon?"

    "Did I ask?" she said with a chuckle, and suddenly, sending Ty backwards in fear, she stood up. Her form towered over Ty, and she began
    to step closer and closer to him, each step making powerful tremors in his wake. Thinking fast, Ty said "I wish Nazara wasn't so big!"

    As she walked, the pounding of her footsteps drowning out Ty's voice, she began to feel strange. Her regal dress felt loose, and were the
    walls getting bigger? Before she could finish that thought, however, a moutain of fabric fell on top of her, covering her completely.

    Ty couldn't help but grin. For a powerful dragoness, she was quite stupid. He walked over to the squirming pile of fabric just in time to see
    her fumble out of the neck hole, completely naked. She was too shocked to cover herself up. "Why, you! I-I am no mere dragon! I
    summoned you here! Do you not fear the full extent of my abilities?" She strut up to Ty and grabbed him by his neck, causing him to panic
    as the dragoness help him in the air, sucking the air out of him. "You have made a foolish mistake, human. For I wish-" "Wait!" coughed
    out Ty between gurgles. She stopped, sighed, and dropped Ty to the ground. "Now what?"

    "I wish you were powerless."

    "Ha! Foolish human, do you really think that would work?" She let out a hearty laugh, which served to discourage Ty, despite his previous
    victory. "I wish that you were my slave, and would do my bidding."

    A pause.

    Nothing.

    "W-what? I wish you were my slave! My servant!"

    Again, nothing.

    "...I wish I was back to my former size!"

    Still nothing.

    "How...? Mortal, how did you...?" she said, now beginning to back away from Ty, his form now a symbol of fear. Ty shrugged. "I don't
    know. I guess it comes with these powers, huh?" The dragoness began to fume. "What do you mean you don't know?! Do you have any
    idea what you've done?! I'll kill you!" With that, she lunged at Ty, and pounced him to the ground. She raised her sharpes claw and let out
    a gash on his chest, tearing his shirt open. "I wish you would freeze!"

    Silence. Ty stared in awe as the dragon-woman appeared as still as a rock. He gently pushed her off of him, causing her to fall on her
    side, still stuck in the same pose. Ty admired her body for a moment; for a dragon, she looked pretty human-like. And damn good, too. But
    Ty knew he couldn't leave her like this, so he made a wish.

    "I wish..."
