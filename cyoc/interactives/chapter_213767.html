    Every wish has consequences

    "So any ideas?" Mindy asked the others.

    "What if we wish we were famous?" Samantha asked.

    "No, I don't think Liz would be very comfortable with that wish." Mindy replied.

    "Or what if we wished we were taller?" Samantha suggested again.

    "Sam, you're already too tall. Exactly how tall do you want to be?"

    "I can't believe all of you seriously believe all this." Kat told them in a very grumpy tone.

    "Oh come on, it won't do any harm if we try." Mindy responded to Kat.

    "What about you Tabitha? Do you have any suggestions?" Mindy asked Tabitha, who was a bit distracted.

    "Me? Well, what if we wished we were younger?" Tabitha asked Mindy shyly.

    "Younger? But, how young do you mean?" Mindy asked Tabitha somewhat intrigued.

    "I don't know. Maybe 5 or 6 years younger?"

    "Well, that doesn't seem like a bad idea to me."

    "Wait, are you really thinking of wishing we were younger?" Kat responded to Mindy somewhat worried.

    "Of course. I think it could be fun. Or what do you think Samantha?"

    "Yeah, I don't think that's a bad idea either."

    Kat was shocked to see that her friends were really buying into all this about the ring, but she knew very well that she would not be able to convince them so she had no choice but to let them make the wish.

    Meanwhile, Liz was still sitting on the ground, curiously watching how the rest of the girls were arguing until she suddenly saw that all of them were heading towards her.

    "Well Liz, all of us already discussed it and we think this should be the wish." Mindy told Liz as she proceeded to hand Liz a piece of paper.

    Liz proceeded to take the paper and began to read it.

    Mindy and the rest were a bit intrigued to find out if Liz would agree. But they would be relieved when Liz nodded to them, which meant that she agreed with the wish they planned to make.

    "I wish we were all younger." Liz read the paper out loud.

    And immediately something started to happen that shocked everyone (including Kat). They all saw that Liz’s ring started to glow before exploding in a bright light that engulfed the entire room. And in a matter of seconds, all of them had been turned into......
