    So it was that the mountain dragons had taken the furious
    Lady Charna,apparently transformed permanently into her alter ego ice dragoness form to the special Akham Asylum where criminally insane dragons and special prisoners were
    held.

    The cold blooded,(quite literally ) ice dragoness was kept away from the smaller mountain dragon prisoners because the
    mountain dragons had no natural defence against her icy breath.

    Lady Charna herself however had no intention of spending the rest of her unnatural life in a solitary cell by herself where
    the only sound she could hear all day was the "hummm "
    of the freezing coils that kept her cell at the frosty temperatures
    her ice dragon body found comfortable.

    The evildoer had hidden a supply of species potions at
    one location known only to herself but more importantly
    before she had began her murderous plans months earlier she
    had taken apart that clock that allowed her to revert from a
    ice dragon to her normal mountain dragon form,
    and duplicated the parts from the crucial timekeeper and hidden the disassembled duplicate cogs,springs and porcelain
    housing at various hidey-holes around the Mountain Kingdom.
    And if she could get her claws even on just one of those duplicate clocks,she could put it back together in the Asylum
    change herself back into a mountain dragoness and get out.
    So Charna did research on the other crazy dragon criminals,
    mad scientists and special prisoner dragons who shared
    this prison with her till she found Renfrew,a meek and mild
    RainSwamp Dragon who just happened to be the nutter who
    had tried to conquer the Seven Dragon Kingdoms three years
    earlier with his army of mechanical steam driven dragonessbots.

    Charna ,being a dragoness herself,had some thoughts about Renfrew's fixation with female robot dragons,but kept it
    to herself.
    The Supervillainess checked about and made contact with Renfrew and over the next while Renfrew got three of his
    still functioning dragonessbots to locate the chemicals and one
    of the gearworks clocks Charna disclosed the hiding location
    off.
    Then on the chosen night of her escape the ice dragoness set the
    functioning ticking timepiece to ring on the crucial 11 chimes
    and she felt her body change and her Mountain Dragoness body
    re-emerge.
    Not the kind of dragoness who likes a messy escape,Charna
    waited for the regular guard to shot up at her solitary cell
    door.She screamed for help and the guard rushed in,only to find Lady Charna as herself.She grabbed his snout and poured a ice
    dragon species potion down the guards throat.
    As the guard felt his body temperature plummet and the icy blue scales overtake him,he must have realized what was happening.
    However soon his body was bursting out of his armour as he changed into a ice dragon.
    Charna didn't give the guard musch time to enjoy his new species as she gave him a whack to the back of the skull that
    knocked him out.
    So with the former guard,now a ice dragon male,sprawled on the perch,Charna closed the cell door on him,made her way
    quietly with her box of species potions and that timekeeper
    to Renfrews cell,openned its door with the ring of keys she had
    taken from the changed guard

    Akham Asylum with those three dragonessbots of his......
