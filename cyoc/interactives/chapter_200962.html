    A Rescue Plan

    added by O Yesterday A AR BM S O Mythological Anthro

    What was supposed to be the happy culmination of their high school lives had now become a somber affair. Duke, Lynne, and Yuriko had been kidnapped, taken somewhere by the vampires. As everyone now gathered inside the Moreland home, they wondered how they were going to rescue their friends and family.

    "How are the others," Felicia asked. Katie now sighed, Lisa and Jason were despondent, Cole and Kumiko trying to comfort them inspite of losing their own daughter. "Not good," she said sadly. "Kellie is trying to help but she's pretty torn up about it as well. She feels awful that Lynne was kidnapped."

    "At least she stopped them," Caleb grumbled, the teenage hunter sullen as Julia Harris tended to his wounds. "I wasn't good enough to save Duke."

    "Caleb," Gloria replied sadly, taking her boyfriends hand. "You did your best. If it wasn't for you Sasha would have died." Caleb could only sigh, glancing at the raven haired wizard, Sasha appeared shocked, staring off into the distance. "I'm sorry Sasha I tried, I really did!"

    "I know," Sasha sighed. "And I'm not mad at you Caleb. You saved me, you did more than any of us could have. Thank you, for saving my life."

    "The time for self pity has passed," Akira now exclaimed, the kitsune angrily gazing at all in attendance. "As we speak those we care about are in peril. We must act!"

    All were silent, anxiously watching the enraged kitsune. "How did you even know the kids were in danger," Naomi asked.

    "We kind of figured it out after Merlin got arrested," Ayami stated, the succubus wearily staring at the Moreland family. "He used his one call to get a hold of his daughter Kellie. After that she called Circe's attorney Franklin. We posted his bond and got him out but they figured something was up. That's how we all ended up in in this timeline."

    The others simply nodded, everyone was still numb from the attack. The vampires had caught everyone by surprise. "So anyone have any ideas," Diane asked.

    "I was able to get some information out of the two vampires that attacked the Harris children and my daughter," Marissa stated. The others now turned to the vampire, her damphir daughter and her friend. Marissa, Rei, and Nat had proven themselves to be worthy allies, defending Mika and Claudia's children. Still, a certain amount of discomfort existed as they now found themselves inside a demon hunters home. "What did you learn," Akira asked.

    "According to those two Miro plans to depart for his castle as soon as possible," Marissa explained. "His entire purpose was to kidnap at least one of the children."

    "And he got two of them," Gloria sighed.

    "Umm sorry to interrupt," Nat said meekly. "But, um, this is all kind of freaking me out. Wizards, succubus, werewolves, and a kitsune all hanging around a demon hunters home?"

    "Natalia," Marissa exclaimed, a mixture of annoyance and embarrassment in her reply.

    "I'm sorry mom but I can tell your nervous too," Nat stated. "I mean the Moreland family is kind of a boogeyman to vampires. That and I didn't think kitsune even existed, let alone hangout with succubus."

    "Kids got a point," Rexie shrugged. Nina now stepped forward with her husband, a warm smile on her face as she tried to put Marissa and her family at ease. "I know things are a bit strange," she said. "But I want to assure you Marissa. We don't see you as an enemy."

    "We have talked enough," Akira now yelled, the kitsune angrily slamming his fist into the Morelands kitchen table. "The children are in danger!" Akira now beckoned to Ayami, Mia, Zack, Rexie, and himself. "We shall find the children and punish those who have harmed them."

    "Are you sure its wise to take those children," Marissa countered, not intimidated by the kitsunes outburst. "These children have faced horrors far worse than vampires," Akira scoffed.

    "Yuki's are god daughter," Mia stated.

    "We'll always protect her," Zack added, boy transforming into a 7 foot tall werewolf. "Nothing can keep us from going!" Marissa gawked, staring up at the massive werewolf before turning to the succubus and imp by the children's sides. "We go where they go," Rexie and Ayami shrugged.

    "Stop," Gloria stated. "Look fighting amongst ourselves will get us no where. We have to work together." Akira sighed, apologizing to Marissa before turning to Gloria. "What do you suggest?"

    "Mr. Moreland," Gloria asked. "Do you think you can find out where the vampires are?"

    "I have some connections," Jacob stated. "Give me and Jim an hour. We can find what you're looking for."

    "I want to help to," Ann stated. The others now glanced at the grown down woman, Ann sporting a determined look on her teenage face. "Remember those suits we built? Me, Steph, and Megan put them in storage, they're still operational and I have a feeling you're going to need all the help you can get."

    "Well you can count us in," Diane stated. "Dukes our friend and we're not letting Gloria do this alone."

    "I'm going to," Katie added, looking at her sister Gloria. "We have to save our sister Glo." Gloria nodded, glancing first to her friends before glancing at the odd gathering of allies before her.

    "We're going to need everyone's help," she started, looking to Caleb. "If we're going to save everyone, we have to work together. We make a plan and then we show them why you should never mess with our family."
