They also have lots of brief flings with the people they meet on their travels. They're very passionate and exciting lovers, but they're incredibly fickle. A werefox caravan tends to leave a trail of broken hearts behind it."

"Humans may be willing to settle for working a boring, dead-end job all day to come home to a constrictive, monogamous family at night, but werefoxes don't see why we have to play by society's rules. We go wherever we want, supporting ourselves by doing odd jobs when we feel like it and by stealing what we need when we don't feel like working. Sometimes we even steal for just plain fun!
