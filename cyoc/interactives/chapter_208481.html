But he's just a kid, and he cannot steal this off of his finger. He opened his palm and let the kid enjoy his time with the ring. All of this happened in a moment smaller than a fraction of a second. By the time Bob regained his vision, he saw a sight he could only find in mirrors and dreams. He was looking at himself, but this duplicate looked a whole lot bigger - and quite older than he remembered.
