    Sarah Meyer had always found the idea of animal transformation interesting. In particular she liked the idea of being turned into a farm animal, she had considered becoming a horse mare but she wasn't 100% sure what she was going to pick as she headed over to the center.

    "Hello, you must be here for the transformation process," the receptionist with a smile. "Welcome, what would you like to be turned into today?"

    "Well, umm..." Sarah said. "I'd like to be turned into a farm animal, but I don't know what I want to be turned into. Do you have any suggestions?"

    "Well, we have a few options for that," the receptionist said and set out the packets. "Do you have any in mind specifically? And you are aware that you'll likely be put into the breeding program. We need more of all animals beyond just transformed humans."

    "Yeah, I know, I understand," Sarah said with a blush, she had become very much aware of that, her parents joked that it meant they'd have to visit their grandchildren on the farm. "I was thinking a horse, but I don't know."

    "Well then, just look over your options and let us know," the receptionist said and left the girl to decide.
