    Chad saw a bespectacled young man looking at him. It was Jasper, a bookish classmate he loved to torment. He always had his nose in a book and Chad loved externalising his fears, frustrations and anger on him.
    "I have read a warlock's grimoire. Interesting spells he experimented. I've transformed you into a faun. And soon, new thoughts you cannot resist will fill your brain. You'll become a much better creature, not the jerk you've always been. You'll be a new faun to grace the world. Soon you will be assaulted with desires worthy of a faun. You'll look desperately to settle down and to start a family. You'll want to desperately adopt and be a good father. You shall look for little girls especially to take in and become a good father to them. God knows why fauns love adopting little girls, but there you are. Have fun."
