    5 Genders Instead of 2

    With the flash of the camera’s power, the world was radically changed in the blink of an eye. Instead of there being 2 genders for humans, the camera had now increased the total number of genders in the world to 5.

    The first of the new genders the camera created was called ‘Malemen’ (abbreviated to MM). Malemen have male-presenting bodies with a penis, much like the original Male gender from the old world. All Malemen often act very masculine in nature, dress in masculine clothing and keep their hair cut short. They have masculine names and use He/Him pronouns.

    The next gender created were ‘Femalemen’ (FM) who have female-presenting bodies yet have a penis for their genitalia. Femalemen also sport their own pair of breasts, much like all other genders that have female-presenting bodies. However, theirs are quite small compared to the other genders’, ranging from A to C cup. Most Femalemen are rather toyboy-ish in nature, acting 70% masculine and 30% feminine. They dress in masculine-fitting clothing and keep their hair short to medium length, no longer than above their shoulders. Femalemen use feminine names and use She/Her pronouns.

    Next were ‘Femalewomen’ (FF) who are equivalent to the original Female gender. They have female-presenting bodies with a vagina. Their breast size tends to be on the larger end of the scale, ranging from C cup to F cup. Femalewomen all act in a very feminine way, wear feminine styled clothing, use makeup and keep their hair length long and styled. Just like females did before, Femalewomen use feminine names and She/Her pronouns.

    The next gender to be created were ‘Malewomen’ (MF) who have male-presenting bodies but have a vagina. Malewomen don’t have breasts however as their male physiques prevent them from growing any. Most people of this gender behave much like femboys, acting 70% feminine and 30% masculine, the opposite of Femalemen. They dress in a feminine way (some even wearing skirts and dresses), wear light makeup and have their hair cut to a medium to long length. Malewomen however use masculine names and use He/Him pronouns.

    And lastly, the fifth and final gender created by the camera were ‘Hermaphrodites’ (H) or Herms as they are informally known. Herms are special compared to the rest of the genders as despite having feminine-presenting bodies, they have both sets of genitals with both a penis and a vagina. They have the largest variety in breast size, ranging from as small as A cup to the enormous G cup. While most herms act and dress in a mix of masculine and feminine attributes, some choose to be exclusively feminine or exclusively masculine in their mannerisms and clothing style. Herms use unisex or genderfluid names and use They/Them pronouns.

    In terms of sexual activities and reproduction, anyone with a penis is able to impregnate and everyone with a vagina is able to be impregnated and experience a menstrual cycle. Additionally, anyone with feminine-presenting bodies are able to lactate. Sexualities no longer exist as everyone is now attracted to each other. However, Malemen have a slight preference towards Femalewomen and vice versa, the same goes with Femalemen towards Malewomen and Hermaphrodites also have a slight preference towards members of their own gender. Society now uses the term "sexfriend" instead of "boyfriend/girlfriend" when dating and "sexpartner" instead of the old "husband/wife" terms for marriage.

    With all the genders fully created and fleshed out, the camera set about transforming each and every person, reassigning them a new gender and giving them all a brand new life, erasing their memories of the old world. In the end, each gender received an even 20% of the world's population.

    But what became of Rhianna and Emma, the two blonde cheerleaders who caused this whole reality rewrite? Did they end up staying as they are, two Femalewomen? Did they revert back to their original forms, Rick and Emily? Or did they receive brand new genders and lives courtesy of the Camera of Change?

    Who did Rhianna and Emma become?
