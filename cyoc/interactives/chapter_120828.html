    You want to be the God Of Water? Sure why not?

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago O


    Suddenly for a brief second, your mind is given a brief second of thought and personality. In those seconds you try finding where you are but sensed nothing. You see nothing, you feel nothing, you smelled nothing, and other than the ever constant presence of the voice you heard nothing either.

    "So... don't keep me waiting now... which divine domain do you want mortal?" The voice says, a sigh of impatience in its voice. You should choose quickly.

    You've always liked the water and the sea so in your haste you blurted out "I choose the water domain."

    "Water domain huh? Plan on being the next Aquaman? Although if you play your cards right you might become the Aqua GOD!" The voice says.

    Suddenly you feel yourself thrust downwards into water, and for a second you felt that the lack of air and the high amount of pressure would kill you, but as quickly as the sensation of drowning started it disappeared and you felt... whole.... it was as if the water itself was a part of you now. Every fiber of your being was water, the ocean, and the numerous plants and sea animals that lived within the vast reaches of the aquatic world.

    You felt every wave that crashed onto a beach, every drop of water that made up a lake, every movement as fish swam within you.

    "You probably feel really strange now don't ya? Welcome to Godhood then, but you can celebrate on your own, I'' not interested in that kind of stuff. You'll discover your powers on your own and you might eventually be able to snag yourself another divine domain from some other incompetent god if your lucky. For now let's continue to the next step." The voice says in your mind.

    You give a small nod, still in awe of the feeling you felt within the large mass of your new watery body.

    "Now, as a God you'll need a new world to begin your conquests, at the start you won't be able to fully manifest into a world but as you get more divine power you'll be able to affect things on a grand scale but for now the most you can do is very little. So next up is to send you to a world where you can begin to spread your influence onto the natives. Remember your followers are your life, the divine power you have right now will only sustain you for so long so you'll need to harvest divine power from your followers. So speaking of which, which world do you want to start in?"

    What do you do now?
