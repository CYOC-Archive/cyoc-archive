    What's it Like to be a Fairy?

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 7 years ago BM S O

    "Lilly?" You ask.

    "Yes, Amber?" Lilly answers as she begins walking you back into the tree.

    "What's it like; flying?" Lilly looked at you silently for a second. "When I saw you flying off before, you looked so elegant and graceful; not like when you were carrying me up here. So, tell me, what's it like; or is it pretty mundane to you by now?"

    "Not in the slightest, we fairies love to fly; it's quite liberating, actually! Even in this confined space, I always feel so free when I fly; even more so since I met you."

    You blushed. "R-really!"

    "M-hmm." Lilly nods. "I haven't been this happy in years. Everything's been so routine and boring all alone here. I'm glad you showed up." Lilly smiles; you smile back.

    The P.A. system suddenly spoke: "The Zoo will be closing in fifteen minutes."

    Lilly's ears and wings drooped. "Oh, I guess you're going to have to leave, soon." Lilly started gathering up your regular clothes. "We'll just get your clothes back on and..."

    You grab Lilly's arm. "You know what, I think I might just want to stay at my new friend's house tonight."

    Lilly was shocked. "What, really?"

    You smile and nod.

    Tears welled up in Lilly's eyes as she dropped your clothes and hugged you. "Thank you; thank you so much!" After breaking the hug, she says. "Amber, for being such a great friend, I'm going to give you a gift."

    "A gift? What is it?"
