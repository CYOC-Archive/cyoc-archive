    Silky had went with the other team, her, Foxy, and Pinkie. The two were ready for anything, yet, unlike Snowflake and Janice, they hadn't witnessed what happened in Philadelphia, so they stayed tight with Alexis and Snowy. The remaining group stayed at HQ to act as reinforcements. It was different on the main land where they could get there with shorter time, but they were, if the islands weren't here, in the middle of the Pacific ocean. "Alright, you three, be ready for anything, and I mean anything." Came Snowy's voice. "Anything can and will happen." Shi looked at the children.

    Silky nodded. "Yes Aunt Snowy." Shi walked with them, Foxy and Pinkie between Alexis and Snowy, only Silky dared explore around them. "Makes me wish that we were furless." Shi commented about the heat.

    "Well, hun, we make do with what we got." Alexis stated. "Should've been there in Egypt." Shi giggled. Silky was the first to find some kats going at it. Instantly shi vaccinated them and went on.

    "You know, to be honest, I've watched films like that. So none of it bothers me. It's not what they do, it's where." Silky stated as shi got three more. Foxy and Pinkie had theirs ready as Alexis and Snowy got a few as well.

    Alexis looked. "Not Smut films, was it?"

    "Uh... yes. But it was cause mom and dad left the door wide open." Shi shrugs. "But hey, they do what they do."

    Alexis looks. "I'm going to have a talk with your mom when we get home." Shi said as shi walked along.

    Silky stopped as shi heard something, ear twitching, shi followed the noise, getting seperated from them. When shi got there shi found something. It was a baby kat, crying in the trash can, blood on it's front leg. Silky looked around and reached in, picked hir up. "Anyone here?" Shi called. When no one answered shi walked out, holding the kat. This little one had to have been a newborn. Shi caught up to Alexis and Snowy. "Alexis!"

    Just as soon as shi walked up, Alexis went pale as shi saw the infant. Shi looked at Alexis. "We'll take care of things here. You get hir to a doctor now. Maurice is waiting at HQ."

    Silky nodded and ran as fast as hir feet could carry hir. Alexis had decided to search all alleys just in case as well, Snowy staying with the remaining two on the street. "Listen you two." Snowy said. "You need to be brave, and ready your dart guns. Okay."

    They nodded.

    Silky kept running and running, not stopping. By now hir clothing was coated in blood, but shi did what shi could to keep hir from bleeding out. Silky ran past a guard and right into Maurice. Maurice looked and took hir and the infant in to get mended. Shi turned out to be a boarkat. A type of pig, and Silky stayed by hir to ensure shi'd make it. Maurice walked out and cleaned hirself off and only checked on them every now and then. Silky started to call the boarkat Pumba, after the one shi seen on the Lion King. Maurice chuckled and seen how motherly Silky was being already, and at hir age.

    --------------------------------------------

    Snowy, Alexis, Foxy, and Pinkie continued to get their area. Finding no children only finding a Tigerkat, eating a pig. They just walked around, injecting hir with the stuff as they got closer. The kat, just fell asleep so they went about their duties. "Luckily that was a normal pig." Snowy said. "Seems some have became very carnivourous." Shi sighed. "What have me and Maurice unleashed on this world?" Shi stopped and sat down, rubbing hir head.

    Alexis turned. "Snowy, what are you saying?"

    "Alexis, it's our fault. Had we not made that God forsaken virus, none, and I mean absolutely none of it would've happened."

    "True. But... we wouldn't have our families, and we wouldn't have Foxy, Pinkie, nor would you have Snowflake. Now dry up and let's get this done. It's not your fault that people use your creation like a wmd." Shi hugged Snowflake. "Come, let's finish up, so we can go home to plan our next move." Shi said.

    Snowflake looked, as shi nodded, ears still pinned back as the four continued on. It was another hour or so before they finished their side of Honolulu. Thankful that none of this covered the entire chain. As soon as they got back, they had made preperations on going back, the national guard finishing up with the clean up.

    ---------------------------------------------

    Once they got back to Janace, they were relieved to find the other team had made it home. Everyone was back together, Silky carrying the little Boarkat like shi was hir own child. Once the children were reunited with their parents, Brent came out and announced. "Dad's awake. I think it's time for him to meet our families. And good timing too." Shi said with a smile.
