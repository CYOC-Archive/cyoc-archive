    9. Tropical Fruits Of Magic
    added by Elflover 2 days ago A BM O Magic

    You rush towards the bowl containing the fruits. The damned fruits that looked so, so tempting. They all look very exotic. So ripe. So colorful. So delicious. Your belly hungers for one. At least one. And your mouth waters at the thought too. You advance towards it, but you can only advance on your knuckles, like a true ape, your paunchy belly jiggling happily, all overjoyed at being free. You really, really look ridiculous, you think. A real freak! You need to fix this and not start thinking like a dumb ape! You are NOT one, dammit! You curse but only simian sounds and grunts escape your throat. You need to repair the damage fast indeed!
    Alas, as you stop in front of the bowl, the smell overpowers you yet again. You sniff appreciatively. Oh, they all look so ripe and delicious! You want truly to sample them all and fill your belly with them! Is it such a problem, really? All this sweet food in your tummy? Who cares oif you stuff yourself? Your stomach has enough space for off that fruity goodness! And as you look at your paunchy belly, it wriggles a bit. You poke at it, giggling stupidly. It's so soft! So squishy! Look how it wriggles and jiggles! It's funny! You chuckle and decide to grab a fruit for good. The biggest. No, the juiciest! No! The sweetest! Completely hypnotised by your desire to eat, you grab the most colorful one and sink your teeth in it. You relish in its pulpy delicious taste. It is just as the transformations start that you recall you had planned to eat one to become human again. But will it work?
