    This is a remake of the Johnny Fishernon storyline, started 8 years ago.

    it all began with a famous sea captain, called Johnny Fishernon. he had a good life as a sea captain. He was married to a woman called Winona, and they had a son called Ryan, who shared a good knowledge of fish just like his dad. They lived on an island not too far from the coast.

    Johnny was a man in his mid-40s, with ginger hair in a mullet, brown eyes, and a beard that went around his mouth, connected with sideburns. His clothes consisted of a navy blue Captain hat, a pale cream sweater, blue jeans, and black boots.

    His job was a fisherman, catching fish in the ocean, and taking them to the docks.

    Johnny Fishernon liked fish a lot. He liked all the different types he could find. From sharks and turtles to salmon and whales, he liked all the fish in the sea.

    He liked to explore the ocean in his boat, and see all the fish he could find.

    But sometimes, he wondered what it'd be like to BE a fish, swimming around carefree in the water...
    -------------

    One day, he has just finished work, and now, he was lying on the deck of his boat, hands behind his head, thinking about all the fish in the sea.

    And he was wondering what it would be like to be a fish.

    'it would be nice to be a fish.' he thought. 'swimming carefree in the ocean, not have anything to worry about; no clothes, work, money, just being free to swim carefree in the oceans and rivers* and ponds* in the world.'

    (*-Johnny wants his fish form to be able to survive in saltwater and freshwater.)

    "How I wish I could just turn into a fish." Johnny says loudly.

    Suddenly, Johnny could feel a tingling sensation strike his whole body.
    And almost as quickly as It arrived, it faded.

    Johnny immediately began to feel funny, as he sat up, and stared out longingly at the calm, cool sea ahead.

    Turns out Johnny had always having dreamed of being a fish. And now, he was gonna transform into whatever fish he wanted. 'Damn,' he thought, 'there are so many fish in the ocean, but what should I transform into?'

    also, would he become part fish or full fish?

    'maybe,' he thought, 'i don't have to be a full fish. I can be part of a fish, just to see if I like it or not.'

    He also knew that fish needed to be in the water to breathe, so he thought carefully. The dolphin had lungs, but the others didn't. and he didn't want to have an instant tf, especially in his house; that meant he'd flop about on the floor, slowly suffocating due to lack of air. Then, there was the size of water needed for the fish to breathe. Running his bath deep could work for the smaller options (carp, goldfish, etc.), but the bigger ones (dolphin, shark, swordfish, etc.) would need more space, like the ocean.

    He stared at the ocean around his island home. He was starting to feel like becoming a fish.
