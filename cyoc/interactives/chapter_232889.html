    The body part casino

    added by Anonymous 10 months ago O
    Author note:
    This is a world where humans, anthros and any sentient being can exist, for interesting combinations.

    There was a magical, special but secret place where people could do the unimaginable. It wouldn't be wrong to call it a casino since there was games like poker, blackjack, slots and many more we know, but people there didn't bet money, no, something more special... Their body parts!

    You could see normal people enter and leave with an extra pair of arms, cases of women exiting crying after losing their breasts, or even humans leaving with animal features and vice versa, each day you could see a brand new combination!

    The body part casino could be considered illegal? Yeah, but none needs to know that more than its respective rules:
    1. You must me +18 years old.
    2. The players must decide the game and the body parts they will bet before starting.
    3. At least one body part should be bet, doesn't matter if it's only one player betting their body part.
    4. One player can bet from one to multiple body parts as long as they are from them.
    5. If there are only two players betting, they should decide what gets the winner and the loser (can also choose to set no body parts for the loser).
    6. If there are more than two players betting, they should decide what will get each position (1st, 2nd, 3rd...).
    7. The winner(s) have to option to reject the body part they got if they want but the loser(s) will obligatorily get the body part they received (if there was a body part assigned to the losers).
    8. The player(s) who won a body part and rejected it can decide to give it to its previous owner or leave it in the casino storage to redeem it later or use it for future bets.
    9. The player(s) who got a body part can decide in what part of their body they will place it. Also once they redeem the body part(s) they had in the storage their change is permanent unless they want to bet the part again.
    10. Once a game is finished a player can propose a double or nothing, but the rest of the players must agree.
    11. A player can decide to bet themselves to transform into a body part, deciding if their transformation will be partial or complete, all players must agree. They will be entirely conscious and couldn't turn back unless the one who wins them decides to turn them back. That one was written in red on the board of rules.
    12. The changes in the body of the players will be done after the game ends.

    With all that in mind who knows what more stories will be told in this fantastic place.
