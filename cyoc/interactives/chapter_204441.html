    Across the yard, you see Rebecca chatting with Makayla. You had not really gotten to know Makayla much, but you recognized her from some of your classes. Wanting to potentially stop another transformation, you quickly walk over.

    "How interesting. Are you certain?"
    "Of course! Why else would I ask you?"
    "Fair point, well I suppose I ought to let you get back to enjoying the party"

    Just as you arrive, Rebecca leaves, but gives you a side eye. 'Crap, she's on to me. I need to be careful' you think to yourself. Lost in thought, a tap from Makayla startles you. "Hey, would you like to dance?"
    Your a bit surprised by this - you know Makayla asked for some odd transformation and she is not wanting to watch it, but instead dance with you. "Please? You know... I kind of have been wanting you to ask me out but you never had so, it would mean a lot."

    As far as you had recalled, she was just a girl in your classes, sure you introduced yourself and studied together once but to think that she was into you. As much as you wanted to get to the bottom of what was going on - you also did not want to miss a shot with Makayla.

    "Um.. you know what? I'd love to!" you reply.

    "Great! Oh hey a new song is starting, this is perfect!"

    Makayla grabs your hand, and leads you over to a more open space. She's a little shorter than you, and makes for an excellent dance partner. After the song ends, and there is a small pause - you decide that you need to know what she wanted with Rebecca.
    "So, what were you talking about with Rebecca" you ask her.
    "Oh well, you know, girl things. Thanking her, asking her for a favor. Nothing important"

    You know she's leaving something out. Just as you begin to ask her - you realize her tan skin lighten hue, and begin to take on a bluish tint. Your eyes widen, surprised that she had apparently asked Rebecca to be transformed.

    "Wait, did your favor include, well, this?" gesturing at her changing composition.
    "Maybe it did" she smiles at you. "But there's a little more." She picks up your hand, and you realize that its becoming the same shade.
    "Woah what!?" You look at her, terrified of whats happening. Reaching to your back pocket, you try to grab your TF-Note, but you can't seem to find it.

    "Oh, looking for your notebook? I grabbed it while we were dancing, you won't be needing that in a moment"
    "I need it to stop this"
    "Which is why I took it from you"

    You suddenly fall down, but you are still standing. Looking around, you realize that you and Makayla have shrunk.
    "Listen, I don't want whatever this is"
    "Well I do, and you just happened to be a cute guy that I wanted to be paired with"
    You try to speak, but you are met with a mouthful of grass. Looking at what was once Makayla, you see your fate. A light blue pump. Your body contorts to match her shape, sitting on the ground.

    A minute later, Rebecca walks up, towering over you. "Well, you certainly made a good choice in style. I think I'll keep you two for myself. And, I won't have to deal with that guy from the library, he was getting to smart for his own good anyways."

    What do you do now?
