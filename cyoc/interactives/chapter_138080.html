    Matt was ecstatic that he finally got the Chronivac to work. He had to show Chris his progress, but he was at school. ‘’Might as well try to understand this program a bit more before show boasting’’ Matt thought. Going back to his seat in the rear of the cafe Matt reopened his laptop and started the program.

    Matt scanned the tables and decided that he would try and change the blond woman drinking coffee a few tables away from him. Mike scanned her and all her information came up to the screen, her name, age, height and more.

    Matt needed to come up with a change that he could see in order to confirm his earlier findings taking a sip of his late he thought about the possibilities when he heard a door open and saw a tear faced Mike walking on shakily hooves behind the counter. Larisa seemed annoyed with her colleague and although Matt could not hear the conversation it was clear that Mike was being send home.

    Matt watched as Mike made his way to the exit it was clear that he was not used to his new body and hiss walking was clumsy. Strangely no one reacted to his physical presence but only seemed to notice the way he acted. Matt also made a mental note about the clothes Mike was wearing. He had no shoes or socks on but all other clothes seemed to be fitting fine as if the universe had adapted to his new form.

    When Mike reached the door and left Matt’s view Matt refocused his attention on his chosen subject. Seeing Mike leave the Cafe gave Matt inspiration to do another animal transformation but decided to keep it small but visible so he could observe the change.

    Matt choose the girl’s ears and moved the bar from human dog / golden retriever to 100% a nice touch taken the girls natural blond hair. Happy with his choice Matt continued and got the normal messages.

    "Check emitter and confirm status. Y/N."

    He wanted to reach the options screen he had when he changed Mike so he entered "N".

    “Check emitter bypassed. Accept defaults? Y/N”

    Again simply repeating his former actions he choose“N”.

    “Defaults not accepted. Please choose Emitter settings:

    Transition Period? (in mins.): 1 ----- ∞
    Transition Pleasure?: Y/N
    Alter Reality?: Y/N
    User Aware?: Y/N
    Subject Aware?: Y/N

    Go Back - Start”

    Matt put in almost the same inputs as before but with some exeptions. Having learned from watching Mike change he guessed that Subject aware meant the awareness of his target and put it to “N” this time in order to prevent her from running away and have a better look at the change.

    Again the timer started to count and Matt twirled the end of his trunk with his fingers resting his big tusks on the table.

    1:00... 0:50....

    Matt’s eyes flared up when he saw the first blonde hairs grow on the girls ears but tried to play it cool. Looking like a stalker was not in his best interest after all so he leaned back and started sipping his latte.

    0:45... 0:30...

    Matt’s neck was staring to hurt holding up his heavy tusks but he was too captivated watching the girls in front of him develop to really care about it. Instead of just a few stray hairs her ears now had a light dusting all over them and were stretched to an unnatural point for a human. To Matt’s delight the girls did not seem to notice anything of the change and just continued drinking her coffee.

    0:25... 0:15...

    The girl’s ears were now steadily moving up her head she scratched her elongated ears taking a bit too much pleasure out of it and left them back flapping over the sides of her head.
    Sadly this was the moment that the girls grabbed her bags and went to the toilet.

    0:10...

    Matt could just barely see the woman’s ears finish migrating to the top of her head when she closed the restroom doors behind her.

    Matt was very happy with the results of his experiment he achieved precisely what he wanted to. Now that was a very cautious experiment but now the time has come to test the limits for this machine.

    Matt finished his late and chose his next subject
