He fell to the floor surprised, still unable to see the two tricksters. Nolan made a quick adjustment to that fact on his watch revealing the new Daniel to the office staff.     He looked down at Nolan more confused then ever. “he’s safe at home. They’ll all be fine. Now cut lose and run wild. Your my show of strength, now do something to kill me.”

He rolled onto his stomach, his tentacles flopping behind him and slithering into other cubicals about the space, elating screams from his coworkers that served as a domino effect. Causing the rest of the floor’s inhabitants to turn their attention to the swelling monster at its center, causing more screams.

    Knowing what he had to do now he took Nolan in one of his monstrous hands lifting him into view of the suit’s not one row away from them. They quickly drew their guns from under their coats and in fear began firing at the creature. Dan lifted his other arm in defense by reflex, but the rounds never struck. Rather then question what happened he returned with a lout booming shout in their direction. A long snapping forked tongue lashing out of his mouth.

From his sides two warm shafts of flesh spilled out, tearing what remained of his shirt to bits and collided with the floor. It branched out into fingers and a joint formed in its middle, creating two new long, and misshaped arms hoisted his torso upwards. Still holding Nolan in the air with his original arms (him screaming for his life it seemed) he lifted one new limb forward, its elbow hitting the ceiling and its palm crushing the cubical and it’s screaming occupant, who vanished as soon as the brown flesh touched their head.

    The suit’s emptied their guns and drew another magazine from under their coats. Slamming them home and continuing their fire. They watched in fear as the monster’s head lost its hair, three rows of fish like fins sprouting in their place, its ears grew long and pointed feet, maybe yards at a time in length. A seam appeared on its forehead and swelled into an gigantic eye that consumed most of its head, the original pair pushed to below it’s sides . Its screaming maw filled with ragged teeth as its tongue split in two, then forked, then split again and forked. All the while growing larger and larger. It leaned forward and crushed a man beneath one of its massive breasts, the man vanishing beneath its brown supple flesh.

    Behind it they watched in horror as tentacle after tentacle filled the office floor, scooping up people and objects, crushing them into nothing and repeating the process. Their was a crash as one of the arms smashed into the floors glass walls. High winds poured into the space kicking up papers into a flutter.

    When they returned their attention back to the threat it was just in time to watch it hoist the flailing office worker into its mouth, its mangled teeth chewing him to nothingness. With a gulp and exhale it fixed its attention back onto the suit’s its free hand stretching towards them only to stop feet away, a close call considering it’s size.

    Their was a wrenching of metal and stone as the creature seemed stuck between the floor and ceiling. Its progress halted its hand flailing and grasping air as it struggled to reach them.

    They breathed a sigh of relief.

    Before they watched its fingers swell larger, its arm swell larger, its body swell larger forcing the floor and ceiling to bow around its growth. Dust and debris crumbled onto their shoulders as their feet shifted from the fissure that had formed between them. Through the crumbling office they watched at the creature smiled with its razor sharp teeth and giant gleeful eye and raked it’s long and colossal arm through the buildings corner wall and into them.
