    Marcus becomes a human muscle balloon

    When Marcus woke up the following morning, he stood up and walked outside only wearing his shorts. It was a beautiful day at the camp, and he was looking forward to being outside and playing games all day long. What really got his attention early that morning was the big, bright object which was floating high above him in the sky. Once he stepped outside the cabin, he spotted a big, bright hot air balloon, floating a thousand feet above him. The balloon was made of multiple colors, so it really stood out across the clear, blue sky.

    “Awww, I wish I could go ballooning!” Marcus sighed as he stood there, staring at the balloon.

    “Hi, Marcus!”

    “Whaah!” Marcus yelped as he heard a familiar voice coming from behind him. “Jeanie! Don’t sneak up on me like that! Please! You scared me!”

    “Oh! Were you looking at the balloon?” she smiled, pointing at the large, colorful object. “Isn’t it pretty?”

    “Yeah…” he replied. “I wanna do that so bad!”

    “Well, of course! How about this: Whenever someone rubs your crotch, your body will fill up with air, and you’ll rise and float through the air like a big, human balloon!” Jeanie grinned.

    “Uhh, what? Wait… Which part of my body will fill up with air?” Marcus asked her specifically.

    “You tell me! You know your male body better than I do!” she responded.

    “Uhh… How about my muscles, like my pecs, my calves, my quads, and my biceps?” Marcus replied while he looked down at his own body.

    “Alright! You’ll go ballooning for ten minutes, depending on how long we rub your crotch. Every rub is ten minutes!” Jeanie cheered as she snapped her fingers. Immediately, Marcus felt a strange, tingling sensation as his calves, his pecs, his quads, his biceps suddenly had a weird, rubber feeling to them. Those muscles felt like they were made of some thick rubber on his body.

    “Hey! What did you do?” Marcus wondered.

    Jeanie then squatted down and rubbed Marcus’ crotch with her fingers, causing the boy to groan loudly. The sensation was incredible.

    What happened next was amazing. Marcus’ biceps, his pecs, his calves, and his quads began to tingle intensely as those muscles suddenly filled up with air as if air were being pumped into them.

    “Whaaaah!” Marcus screamed loudly as those muscles all grew bigger, at the same time. Within moments, his feet left the ground since his quads and calves were large mounds of balloon-muscles which were filled with air! Finally, as his pectorals and biceps filled up, his body began to rise slowly and steadily. Very gradually, Marcus began to ascend into the sky as Jeanie and the other campers looked on from the ground.

    “Whooaaaa! I’m floating! Ohmygosh! I’m a freaking human BALLOON!” Marcus shouted as he was floating further away from the ground. The funny thing was his own body was the balloon. He didn’t need to hold on to anything because his own muscles were pulling him higher into the air.

    Luckily, there was no wind, and Marcus ended up floating above the campground. He still saw his cabin and the link from high in the sky, when about ten minutes had passed. Then, very gradually, his balloon muscles started to deflate all at the same time, causing his body to descend very slowly. After about a minute or so, Marcus’ legs were pointing to the ground and his body was just being held up by his large balloon-pecs and biceps, which took longer to deflate. Finally, the muscles had deflated back to their original sizes and Marcus touched down on the grassy field. The campers ran to greet him as he landed near the shore of the lake, just a few hundred feet from his cabin.

    “You’re so lucky, Marcus! You’re a human balloon!” James said.

    “We need to send you up in the air with a camera next time!” Sam even suggested.

    “Can I rub your crotch next, so you become a balloon again?” Jason asked him.

    Three other boys had joined the crowd and they all stood in line, hoping to get a turn at rubbing Marcus’ crotch so they could see him ascend into the air as a human balloon.

    “How do you guys even know I’m a balloon?” Marcus wondered.

    “Everybody knows. The whole camp knows that when we rub your crotch, you become a balloon!” Sam answered.

    “Uhh, great!” Marcus sighed.

    Who will try it next, and when and where will Marcus go ballooning, next?
