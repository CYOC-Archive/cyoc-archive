    Henry continues driving, he only stops twice to fill up the “Jeep”, after a six hour drive, Jeff notices Henry turn off the black tar and onto a beaten up sandy path with lots of holes.

    “Pl-please be c-careful,” Jeff says as he is bounced around when Henry drives over some holes, “m-my aches c-could break.”

    Henry chuckles, “I’m trying my best, Jeff,” he says, “but this path has not been maintained while I was gone. I’ll fill up the holes tomorrow, don’t worry, it’s not far to my Ranch.” After five ten minutes, Henry finally pulls up to a huge house, which looks more like a ruin, “oh, boy,” Henry says, stopping the “car”, I should fix up the house first before I transform you back.

    “Oh, okay,” Jeff says.

    Henry takes out is phone and opens the App, after a few minutes, the man hits confirm, Henry grins as he watches the ruin start to shift and repair itself. An hour later, the house is back to it’s original beauty, “there, much better,” Henry says, he then turns his attention to Jeff, “time to turn you back into a human,” he says. Henry opens the file of the “Jeep” and types into the App, “all done,” he says, “here we go,” and confirms the change.

    Jeff feels the familiar tingling and soon feels his body shift, in a matter of minutes, Jeff is finally human again. The teen is wearing a skin tight full body suit, the suit is blue and silver with black boots and black gloves, Jeff’s hair is back to black and his face has the normal skin tone. Jeff looks down at himself, “finally,” he says, “I am myself again! Thank you so much, Henry. I really missed being a human, I hope I’ll never have to be a car again!”

    “My pleasure, Jeff,” Henry says, “but, you might need to become a Jeep again, I got to get around, you know. But I promise, if I don’t need the “Jeep” you’ll be your normal self.”

    Jeff sighs, “okay, fine,” he says.

    “Good,” Henry says, “now, come inside, and I’ll show you around.” So Jeff follows Henry into the mansion, unaware that he is in the hands of a mad scientist, who already has a few “ideas” in his sick mind, what to do with Jeff.

    Jeff follows Henry inside, unaware what the man has planned, that he no longer will have a free will, that he is now Henry’s personal “lap-rat”, Jeff’s only purpose will be to let Henry do with him how he pleases.

    Henry snickers to himself under his breath as he imagines all the amazing experiments he could and WILL do with Jeff. The man leads the teen down into the basement, he walks up to a huge shelve, filled with wine bottles. “Here is the entrance to my secret lab,” Henry says, he then pulls out a few bottles which hide a digital code-lock. The man punches in the code and the shelves hiss as they start to slide apart.

    Jeff’s eyes widen, “whoa, so cool,” he says in amazement.

    Henry grins, “wait till you see my lab,” he says and leads Jeff through the door. When they walk through the door, it hisses shut behind them.

    Jeff’s eyes widen even more and his mouth hangs open as he looks around, the boy finds himself standing in a huge pearl white room. There are hundreds of cabinets, which line the walls, the cabinets are filled with jars and other lab equipment. In the middle of the room, there is a big metallic operation table, Jeff also can see a wall with a huge monitor and a desk below it. “This place is massive,” he says in awe.

    “Yep,” Henry says, “it took years to build it.”

    Jeff nods, “I can imagine,” he says, the teen is so overwhelmed that he does not notice Henry pulling out his phone.

    Henry opens the Chronivac-App and opens Jeff’s file, which he downloaded into his App while Jeff was still a Jeep. ‘It is time,’ Henry thinks to himself, ‘time to make this boy my loyal android, he’ll obey me without question. When I tell him to transform into a Car, Woman, Kid, Animal, or whatever I come up with and need in a certain situation, he will not hesitate to do as commanded!’
