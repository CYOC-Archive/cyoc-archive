    Jennifer couldn't think of a way out of the Chef's trap. She bowed her head in defeat. "You win. Give me the antidote."

    Garçon smiled and walked over. He passed her the vial, and she drank it in one gulp. Instantly, the pain in her stomach went away. And at the same time, her catsuit, unable to hold in her fat any longer, ripped into pieces. Jennifer fell flat on her ass, naked and humiliated. She began to cry.

    The chef, rather than gloat over how the proud Latex Ranger was now a big plump heifer, quietly handed her his stew pot. Without hesitation, Jennifer tipped the pot into her mouth and drank every drop. Suddenly, nothing mattered anymore and all the fallen hero could think of was eating as much delicious food as she could. The chef put his hand in hers and calmly walked her out of the school.

    The Latex Ranger died that day. Jennifer, taking the unassuming name of the Hostess, honored her promise to help Garçon fatten up the world. Wherever they went, men and women disappeared, only to be found dead weeks later and horribly obese. As time went on, the Hostess found herself starting to enjoy being a villain's sidekick. The chef treated her with the utmost respect, and whenever he made a tasty new dish, she was always the first to sample it. As promised, the antidote made it so that no matter what she ate or how much of it, there were no ill effects. Her body slowly began to shrink from a size 13 to a size 11, giving her a curvy and highly attractive figure that made it much easier to lure in new prey.

    One night, as soon as the victims were carted away, the chef surprised the Hostess with a shiny diamond ring cut from the finger of a newly deceased trophy wife. He asked if she would be his wife, and she said yes. To consummate their marriage, he gave her the fantasy she had always wanted: to be covered in thick, dark gravy and then placed in a giant cauldron filled with alternating layers of vegetables, meat, and pie. For nearly a week, she lived like a complete pig, eating, sleeping, and snorting happily as she worked her way down to the bottom. Afterwards, her husband lovingly rinsed her off.

    But even as the wicked chef and his wife carried out their dastardly plans, one man swore to stop them. Knowing that he was to blame for giving the Latex Ranger her powers, he decided that there was no other choice. He had to become a superhero himself.
