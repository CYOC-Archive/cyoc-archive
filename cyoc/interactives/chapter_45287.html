    As the flames died from Harriette's attack the dead wolves transformed back into who they were before. Suddenly Elizabeth shouted "MUMMY! DADDY!" and started to rush forward. Allison caught her around her chest before she could get too far. Allison didn't know which ones were Elizabeth's parents but there was no mistaking that whichever ones they were they were dead.

    The wolves were backing up now and Marry said "They're scared of us."

    Marry started to move toward the door but Allison said "Mary stop! There are a whole lot more waiting outside.

    "Allison's right" Jen said. "There's many out there and more coming."

    "They got into the hospital and have transformed nearly everyone there so far" Allison said.

    "Mummy, Daddy" Elizabeth said stopping trying to get to them. Then she turned around and cried into Allison's belly.

    Allison looked up and said "We need to get everyone to the assembly hall. The wolves are building up power. Jen, I have an idea how to get everyone out."

    "What is it?" Jen asked.

    "I'll tell you when we get to the assembly hall" Allison said. As the announcement was made for the students to assemble in the assembly hall she quickly led the others to the hall. They were the first ones there and once there told the rest what she had planned. "The wolves are building up power so we have to get everyone out in one shot."

    "How do yo plan to do that?" Shaela asked. "I can do a mass portal but only twelve at a time."

    "You and Arcturo will be doing it" Allison said.

    "We still can't portal everyone out at once" Shaela said.

    "You want us to use the energy from you transformations" Arcturo said.

    "Right" Allison said.

    "We still won't be able to portal everyone out" Shaela said.

    "I think if we hold our necklaces together when we transform it should release enough energy to portal everyone out" Allison said.

    "Do you know how dangerous that could be?" Shaela asked. "It could cause a massive explosion."

    "I thought that it might" Allison said. "Though I don't think we have much of a choice. It would take too long to do it otherwise. I think we'll be cutting it close as it is."

    Shaela sighed and said "I guess we don't. Alright, we'll try this."

    "Transform back" Jen said and they did.

    "I want to bring Sapphira" Mina said. "She's my parakeet."

    "Alright" Allison said. "Arcturo, please go get Sapphira."

    "Where is she at?" Arcturo asked.

    Mina told him and he was soon back with a blue parakeet. Sapphira settled on Mina's shoulder and said "I knew you were suppose to be something more. What's your name now."

    Mina was a bit startled by Sapphira speaking but she quickly got over it and said "I'm Mina now."

    While they had been talking the assembly hall had filled up. Allison looked around and said "It's time. Everyone who isn't a wolf is here. There's not a moment to spare either." Allison could sense the wolves power building and they were just about ready. She really hoped this would work. "Elizabeth we need your help."

    "But... Mummy and... Daddy" Elizabeth said through her tears. "They're... gone... like... the rest of... my family."

    Allison knelt down and looked Elizabeth in the eye saying "They would want you to help us to get everyone out of here so they don't suffer like they did. Please Elizabeth, there will be time later for you to cry later but right now I need you to help us."

    "Ok" Elizabeth said. "How can I help?"

    "I'll need you to hold onto your necklace and say 'Mystic Power Activate' when I say so" Allison said. She looked at everyone else and said "Ok, we'll need to make sure that our necklaces are touching each other. Take them off and hold them out so they do." Allison took the lead and took her's off then Jen did. After the others did too and they held them together so they were all touching. Allison took a deep breath and said "On three we'll transform. Shaela, Arcturo, be ready to portal all of us."

    "I don't like this but I'm ready" Shaela said.

    "I'm ready too" Arcturo said.

    "Everyone ready?" Jen asked. After everyone nodded she started the count instead of Allison. "One. Two. Three."

    A bright light emanated from where the necklaces touched. Everyone in the assembly hall had to shut their eyes. The light quickly faded and when everyone could see they found themselves in the safe house. They also saw Jen, Marry, Harriette, Mina, Allison, Elizabeth, Shaela, and Arcturo lying on the floor unconscious.
