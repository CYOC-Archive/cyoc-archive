    There were beautiful-sculptured ancient Egyptian words on the base of the bust. You took your flashlight closer, trying to figure something out with the little amount of Egyptian words you learned from the school history class.
    "A...person, with something... into him, will be... Something? " You read the content as much as you can, trying to comprehend it. That's when you sensed the movement above you.
    You looked up with fear. And found the head of the bust turned at your direction, with his red glowing eyes staring at you. You froze, not knowing what to do.
    All of a sudden, you found the words on the bust base glowing in a golden color, so does all the wall pictures. The room was brilliantly illuminated. You looked around, eyes quickly going through all those symbols you could not understand.
    Before you could realize, those words, somehow, hypnotized you. You felt your body being controlled by a shapeless force and slowly turned to face the bust without your command. You struggled hard, only to find yourself staring straightly at the glowing red eyes of Anubis. His red eyes gradually became brighter and brighter.
    You, helpless, desperately tried to look away or close your eyes. But you simply couldn't move even a little bit. Now, you do not know what to expect.
    "Ah!" You were almost blind by the sudden flash from the eyes of the Anubis bust. With the return of body control, you managed to step back but tripped over a tilt brick on the rough ground. By the time your body hit the ground, the surrounding light went off, leaving only the dim light from the flashlight you dropped unconsciously a moment ago.
    Still quite afraid of what just happened, you reached your tumbling hand to the flashlight. Exactly when you felt the weirdness on your hand. Why did it felt so warm? You decided to take a closer look.
    By the time your hand was lighted up by the flashlight. Your were definitely shocked at what you saw. While your hand still remined its shape and size. It looks totally different, totally covered with thin black fur. Five golden shiny claws stretched out at the tip of your each finger. Looking at the front of your hand, there were also 7 paw pads. Five on each finger tip, and two on the top and bottom of your palm.
    In case it was just a illusion after over shocked, you checked the other hand, wishing to see a human hand.
    It was gone as well. You put both hands up in front of our eyes. They seemed to be the same now. Rubbing them with each other, they no more felt like your hands, but like a furry pair of paw, of an animal.
    Now all you could do was to stare at them, your new hands, in awe.
    What is happening to me? You asked yourself nervously. And what I am supposed to do now!
