    Growing family

    Ty had a great workout with the boys in the daycare. He'd never felt camaraderie quite like this before and wanted to keep it going.

    After a very successful "leg day" (because you should never skip leg day, as one of the slogans on the wall espoused), he hit the showers with Steve and another of his new lifting buddies, Noah. He'd bonded with the two in the hack squat machine and in the squat racks. His buddies were chattering excitedly as they both swore their legs seemed to have swollen at least three inches and their calf muscles nearly 4. They didn't realize their new friend Ty wanted to give them their best pump and give them something to motivate them to come back to the daycare.

    In between sets, he'd learned both live across town and regularly have to beg their parents to bring them here. Now as they stripped down, the boys' excitement waived as they lamented to Ty about how they wish they could share this hobby with their parents.

    "Mom doesn't get it at all, and though I think my pops understands, he's a little scared of me," Noah said.

    "Yeah, I know what you mean," says Steve. "My mother and father can't believe I grew so big in only a few years."

    He flexes a bicep bigger than all of their own parents' heads, just to prove a point.

    "They're not even sure where these genes came from, even though I am most definitely their offspring. And my brother is totally jealous! I look like the younger and more muscular version of him!"

    Ty got an idea. He was gonna have a little fun with the parents and caretakers who picked these (and all the other) boys up from the daycare.

    First, he made it so anyone picking up the boys would have a body in the same league as their behemoth sons. However, he placed an inverse wording so that the younger you are, the more muscle you put on. Older brothers would get closer in size to their kid brothers; dads would get less, but still enough to stand their own next to their powerful sons.

    Then he made it so all the parents would be men, just like their boys. This was a boys only club and Ty liked it that way.

    Finally, he moved the gym that the daycare was attached to to the gay neighborhood in town. These dads from all over purposefully moved to this part of town to both settle with their partners and give their sons the camaraderie they need.

    He made the wish silently so that he could see the results, even if he didn't get to watch the growth.
