    Kyle grinned unseen on the other end as he spoke up. "Sounds good Jen, let's do this. Jen transform into a Gynoid, the one I am thinking about right now. That is my fantasy. Your transformation will start when one of us hangs up. While I have your ear I can tell you about my fantasy. Want to hear about your change before this call is ended Jen?"

    Jennifer was frozen on the other end of the phone. She had no idea what a gynoid was but a vision of her as some kind of snail flashed into her mind. She started to cry. "Please no, I don't want to be a snail. Don't hang up, please."

    As she sobbed on the opposite end of the phone Kyle laughed so hard that it took him some time to recover. "Jen I swear you really are more tits then brains. A gynoid isn't a snail. It is a robot designed to look human. But I'm not gonna hang up until I tell you what you will become. So get your crying out of your system and let me know when you are ready."

    Jennifer did stop crying soon after, a robot wasn't as bad, but she knew nothing about robotics. Heck, everything she knew about computers was based on social networking. She spoke up in a timid voice to let Kyle know to continue. "Alright then... explain this to me you nerd."

    Kyle paused for a minute and then replied. "Naughty Jen, trying to be passive aggressive maybe I should just hang up now..."

    He waited for a few seconds and then resumed. "But I want your mind to understand what is happening to you. As I said a gynoid is a humanoid robot, but in my fantasy you are much more then that. You will be an amazing machine capable of so much. First off your form will be changeable. Like you said I'm a nerd and got my idea from reading about the T-XA. You will be more advanced then that, able to replicate yourself to any mass for duplication and able to reduce your mass as needed. You will be able to take control of human life forms if I order you too but otherwise you will adhere to the three roles of robotics. Just so you know Jen they are; First a robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm, second a robot must obey the orders given to it by human beings, except where such orders would conflict with the First Law, lastly a robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or Second Laws. These will be second to one core law, and that is to obey your owner at all times. Your owner's orders will take precedence over all laws and right now I am your owner. You get all that Jen?"

    Jen doesn't really understand everything, but got that Kyle would be her owner and she was gonna be some kind of shape changing robot. She begins to tremble a bit. "Yes, I get that you are gonna lock me away as some kind of personal toy you freak."

    Kyle chuckles softly. "Watch it Jen or I might just do that. Right now your primary operational mode will be what I call Jenny Mark one. You will have mostly full control of your body and won't have to obey every order in that form. You will have a control panel on the back of your neck though, and anyone that tries to use it will be allowed. I will add extra modes to you later, but mark one will be your main form. You will respect me and call me master when we aren't being observed. If your owner is ever female you will call her mistress. You will continue to live out your normal life in your mark one form and will be unable to tell anyone about being human at one time. Others won't think it odd or strange that you are a robot and any form you show up in public in will be okay by public standards. In this form you will have limited control of your appearance. You will be able to change your clothes with a thought, as they will be part of you. So what do you think Jen?"

    Jen still listening calms down a bit. She starts to think that it won't be so bad, being able to dress herself in the way Kyle described actually sounded fun. She smiles and speaks up. "That doesn't sound too bad Kyle."

    Kyle remains silent a moment and speaks up again. "That is only the start my dear Jenbot. I want you to be able to live a normal life so that I can have more fun with you. Your other programs won't be so... simple. You will also be set to send a reproduction of yourself to me, one that I will be able to upload new programs into. You will still feel, and have emotions. Your machine A.I. will link perfectly with your current mind. Maybe the best way to explain it is that you will be like a robot with a soul. By default you will also feel what your copies feel, so if I decide to make your copy masturbate to climax during fifth period you will be squirming in pleasure in your seat. We are gonna have lots of fun Jen. I think it is now time to start."

    Before Jen could yell wait Kyle had hang up the phone. Her body started to feel weird and tingly all over she started to panic as knew what was gonna happen. Slowly she started to transform.
