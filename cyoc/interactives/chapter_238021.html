    The apartment was silent except for the low hum of energy resonating between them. Mathew and Daniel stood together, their massive, goo-coated bodies gleaming in the dim light, every pulse of the substance that covered them sending shivers of strength through their forms. But as Daniel’s hand traced the dense, rippling muscles of Mathew’s arm, his touch lingered. His fingers glided along the grooves, following the lines of power etched beneath the goo, captivated by the sheer rawness of his friend’s form.

    “Mathew…” Daniel’s voice was soft, almost reverent, his gaze never leaving the mesmerizing lines of Mathew’s towering body. He could feel a strange heat building inside him, an irresistible urge to get *closer*, to become one with the force he felt emanating from Mathew. His fingers sank into Mathew’s shoulder, his grip tightening as he let himself be drawn further into the sensation, his own body beginning to melt, softening, merging.

    Mathew felt it first as a subtle warmth, then as a surge of deep, throbbing pleasure. He looked down, his glowing eyes widening as he realized what was happening. Daniel’s form was shifting, liquefying, the goo coating him thickening and warming as it began to flow, slowly melting into Mathew’s own. The sensation was electric — he could feel Daniel’s essence merging with his, could feel the strength and presence of his friend being absorbed into him, amplifying every fiber of his being.

    “Daniel…” Mathew’s voice came out in a low, guttural growl, his words melting into a deep rumble of satisfaction. The pleasure was intense, almost too much to bear, each wave rolling through him as his body began to expand, muscles swelling, stretching as Daniel’s form melded with his. His skin rippled, stretched, growing thicker, more powerful, every inch of him surging with newfound strength. He felt his arms grow denser, broader, his legs thickening as his body pushed upward, inch by inch, beyond what he thought possible.

    Daniel’s gooey form continued to flow over him, seeping into his skin, filling every gap, merging into every muscle. Mathew’s chest swelled, his shoulders broadened until they brushed against the walls of the apartment, the overwhelming bulk of his monstrous frame pressing outward, unstoppable. His hands flexed, fingers stretching and thickening, each new layer of strength sending a fresh wave of ecstasy through him. He threw his head back, letting out a primal roar as his growing form filled the room, his powerful body pulsing with the combined strength of both men.

    Walls creaked under the strain, and with a final, irresistible surge of growth, Mathew’s broad shoulders smashed through the apartment wall, chunks of plaster and brick crumbling around him. He didn’t even flinch, his monstrous form barely registering the destruction as his body continued to expand, the goo coating him thickening, becoming almost armor-like, a dark, pulsing shell of power that radiated menace and raw, boundless strength.

    Panting, growling in pleasure, Mathew looked down at his hands, his massive arms, feeling the new weight and power of his form. The remnants of Daniel's essence pulsed within him, a deep connection, a bond that had transformed him into something more than he’d ever dreamed. He was colossal, indomitable, every inch of him charged with the power and joy of transformation, his monstrous body now towering far beyond human limitations.

    With a slow, satisfied grin, Mathew stepped forward, leaving a trail of purple goo and shattered wall in his wake, his powerful frame filling the space with a presence that was both beautiful and terrifying. There was no going back now — only forward, only... more.
