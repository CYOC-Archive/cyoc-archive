    Rasler had gotten out of the hospital since there was really no reason for him to be there. Rasler had today off, while I had school. I suppose his parents were still in shock that he almost got hit, and just wanted him to stay home. I was at my home too, but I was getting ready to leave for school. I quickly did my whole morning routine. Hopping in the shower, drying my hair afterwards, brushing my teeth and then getting dressed before heading down for some breakfast. Today’s routine was different. I blame that magic eight ball. I was still curious about it, and I just had to get at least a few questions out of that outlandish woman. I dove into yesterday’s pants pocket to retrieve the wretched thing. I peered into the looking glass, and before I knew it, I had some chick sitting on my bed. Sadly enough, this will probably be the only time I ever have a woman in my room, let alone on my bed.

    "Good morning. I see you are getting ready for school." She curtly said, looking displeased for some reason.

    "Yeah I am... Uh, what’s with the attitude?" I asked, wondering if I offended her somehow.

    "Well, for starters, you made only two wishes! My previous owners made at least ten within a twenty-four hours. Now that you’re going to school, I know there will be no more wishes for a few hours as well. I’m quite bored, to say the least." She explained, crossing her arms while shooting me a dirty look.

    "Oh... I uh... Didn’t know. I guess you actually LIKE granting wishes. May I ask a few questions?" I asked, even though it was redundant to ask her if I could ask her a question.

    "Of course." She replied, relaxing slightly.

    "You know when you did that second wish thing? Well, everyone forgot what happened except Rasler... What’s up with that?"

    "Well, everyone forgets what a person looked like or what happened to them before the change took place. However, the person who has something changed about them will think that they just had a strange dream or some such, while accepting the new change as if they were like that to begin with."

    "In other words, if I made a midget eight feet tall, he’d act like he was always eight feet tall, but would think that he was actually a midget at one point? They think it’s was a vivid dream or something because it can’t be true if that’s not what they see." I tried to make sense of it all. I suppose I have to be careful about what I do, or else the FBI will be ramming down my door to arrest me for breaking the laws of physics and what not.

    "That is correct. I do hope you keep the wishes coming. You can use wishes to better other people’s lives as well, so keep that in mind. Many of my previous owners were too selfish to wish for others, but there were a few who decided to unselfish."

    "Okay then. Er, one last question and then you can go. What’s your name?"

    "You can just refer to me as Nino." As soon as she said her name, she had disappeared back into the magic eight ball.

    I was getting use to all these strange happenings. It’s not like it was a hard thing to grasp, it was just so very... Strange... Awkward... Bleh! I was going to be late for school! Before I could panic, a sly grin crept across my face as I looked at the ball in my hand.

    "Well, she did like granting wishes. It’d be rude to deny her the satisfaction… I wish I was transported to an empty bathroom in my school..." I paused for a minute and quickly added "I mean an empty boy’s bathroom!"

    "Heehee, it’s almost as if you knew I was going to send you to a female’s bathroom. Ehem! So you have wished it, so it shall be granted."

    With a cliche puff a smoke, I was there. My books, my body, my brain... I think my brain came with me, anyway. I felt sorta loopy. But I was definitely in an empty stall in the boy's bathroom. I proceeded on my way to my locker as usual, wondering if Nino had hinted for me to use my wishes on others just so she could do what she does best... That was probably the case. Maybe I should take her up on that offer sometime soon.
