    Before Jeff is able to decide, the boy realizes that he needs the toilette, “god damn it,” he mumbles, he stares at his Laptop, “I’ll be right back,” he says, before jumping up from his seat and hurrying to the bathroom.

    While Jeff is “busy”, he is unaware of the door to his 10 year-old sister’s room opens and the young girl stepping out, heading to his room.

    Amy, who has long and smooth black hair, which reaches the middle of her back and is always styled in a pony tail, brown eyes and a average slim body of a 10 year-old, opens the door to Jeff’s room and steps in.

    “Now,” the young girl says, “let’s see if Jeff has some coloring pens,” Amy walks up to Jeff’s desk, she always sneaks into Jeff’s room, because Jeff always shoos her out when he is in his room. Amy is not fond of Jeff, he always bosses her around, and she finds him boring and annoying, the worst part: Jeff ALWAYS gets the most attention, since he is the first born, he also always gets the best gifts, this causes Amy to feel extremely jealous and angry. No one in the family is aware of the girl’s mental instability, Amy believes and is convinced that SHE should get all the attention and all the cool gifts, after all: she IS the princess, so everyone should treat her as such!

    When Amy reaches the desk, she notices that Jeff’s Laptop is on and a weird game is running, the little girl narrows her eyes, “did he get a new game,” she asks herself, “why does HE always get the coolest stuff!?”

    The little girl examines the “game” a little closer, she then tilts her head when she notices a 3D-image of her brother on the right hand side of the screen and on the left she sees Jeff’s name, age, gender and… other things.

    “What kind of game is this,” Amy asks herself, “it looks so… weird,” her curiosity gets the better of her and she decides to take a much closer look.

    Amy sits down on Jeff’s seat and grabs the mouse, she moves it to a tab which is labeled as “manual”, when she clicks on it, a window pops up with the details and information about the “game”. The more the girl reads, the more her eyes widen, then a huge, almost evil grin appears on her face, “if this is true, and this game is able to do all of that,” she says, “then… hehe… I’ll be able to make sure I will be treated correctly!”

    After reading the manual, Amy returns to the “folder” tab, the little girl scowls when she spots a file labeled with her name, “so, Jeff was planning to use this game on me,” she asks herself, “probably planning to get rid of me in some way! HA! Tough luck! Just you wait!”

    Amy opens her file, then she opens the transformation tab, which has all the various transformation options. The girl then opens the tab labeled “Fantasy”, scrolling through, Amy soon finds what she is looking for: “witches”!

    The little girl grins evilly, when she selects the option the screen changes, here Amy is able to choose how she wants to look like and what powers she wants to have.

    Amy decides to stay like she is, her hair is already black, like a witch’s anyway, the only thing the girl decides to change is her eye color: witches have green eyes! First, though, Amy decides to choose her powers: shape shifting, hypnosis and transformation.

    After saving those settings, Amy returns to her file and changes her eye color from brown to green, then she sets her age to be permanently 10, never wanting to age, and sets the “worlds awareness” to off, which causes every one on the planet to be unaware of what she is, once the transformation has ended.

    Grinning evilly to herself, Amy confirms the change, the girl feels a tingling run through her body, causing her to shiver, but then the little girl feels something else: a huge energy filling her body, growing stronger and stronger, causing the little girl to feel oh so powerful, then it is over as quickly as it begun.

    Amy, knowing that she is now a real witch, grins evilly, “now I can do what ever I want,” she says to herself, “no one will ever know! No one will be able to stop me! Now, I am the one who rules this world, like a true princess!”

    The little girl quickly wipes every evidence of her being at Jeff’s Laptop from the boy’s Laptop, then changes the screen back to the way she found it: Jeff’s file.

    After the girl has erased every trace of her being on Jeff’s Laptop, the girl heads back to her room and closes the door, not a second to soon, as she hears the door of the bathroom open and then the door to Jeff’s room close.

    Amy grins evilly to herself, “hehe… oh Jeff, you have no idea what is coming,” she whispers to herself, “now, what should I do to you first? Or should I test my new powers out first, before starting to take revenge on Jeff? Either way… Jeff will get what he deserves for making MY parents ignore me!”

    The girl sits down on her bed and starts to think of what to do, a wicked smile on her face as she remembers that Halloween is only two weeks away. Imagining of all the fun she will have causes her smile to change into a huge evil grin, oh yes! Halloween this year will be a Halloween NO ONE will ever forget!
