    Ty's Power

    From brawny to scrawny

    Ty and Aiden were walking down the hall after science class. Aiden was easily carrying two heavy backpacks with his bulging arms and Ty wasn't carrying anything at all. Since Aiden was all big and buff now, he didn't mind carrying Ty's bag as well.

    The two boys had just turned the corner as they were heading towards the library when three football players jumped out of hiding behind the wall! It was Tom, Rob and Jim!

    Jim grabbed Ty and pushed him against the wall while Rob and Tom tried to wrestle Aiden to the ground but Aiden was too big for them.

    "FREEZE!" Ty shouted, and everybody was suddenly suspended in time.

    Then Ty unfroze Aiden as well, who quickly allowed the two goons to drop away from his shoulders.

    "When are these three idiots gonna learn?" Ty groaned, as he and Aiden stepped back while the three bullies lay on the ground, frozen.

    Then Ty waved his hand and they became unfrozen again.

    As they slowly got up, Ty decided to punish them.

    "I'm gonna take away all your muscles. Your bullying days are over!"

    The three boys stared at Ty in confusion.

    "I'm gonna give all your muscle to Aiden!"

    Suddenly, the three football players felt their muscles dwindle rapidly, leaving them all three as skinny, scrawny and weak young men.

    "NOOOO! I spent four years in the gym lifting weights in order to build muscle and now they're all GONE!" Jim cried out.

    "Where did my guns go???" Rob shouted, realizing he was as skinny as a stick figure.

    "HEY! I'm small!" Tom exclaimed, noting how much weight he had lost.

    Suddenly, Aiden let out a loud moan as his muscles inflated even MORE!

    Aiden's pecs, his quads, his biceps and his calves grew even MORE astounding as he gained all the muscle of the three football players in a matter of seconds, leaving the geeky boy as a muscle GIANT!

    "WHaaaaahhH! I'm outta here!" Rob shouted as he ran off, with the other two behind him.

    Ty looked up at Aiden with a huge smile. Aiden was bigger than EVER now!

    "Whoa!" the boy sighed, as he flexed his giant arms.

    "Are you still gonna tutor me in geometry?" Ty asked the Hulking boy.

    "Sure... Umm... Mind if I work out in the gym first?" Aiden asked him shyly. "I kinda want to test my strength..."

    "No problem. Message me when you're done!"

    Then Ty walked to the library as Aiden headed off to make an impact in the gym as the biggest guy in school...
