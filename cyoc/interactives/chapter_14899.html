    It happened to be the night that all the teachers were meeting in one of the largest classrooms at the school. There was a special meeting taking place, where the principal would get together with the different faculty and staff to discuss the different issues and problems that were facing the school. They would also discuss how to find solutions to the problems and what they had to do to contain the increase in bullying, theft, and other petty crimes on the school grounds.

    All the teachers were discussing the increase in student crime, when there was a light knock on the classroom door. Paul opened the door, and quietly walked in. He didn’t go unnoticed, for one of the teachers stopped speaking, and asked Paul what he was doing at school at this hour.

    “I came to drop off some things for chemistry class, sir…” he replied politely. The teacher then continued his speech, as Paul walked in. He quietly walked into the room, and quickly made his way to the very back of the classroom, where he waited for the others to arrive.

    Tyrone and Randy had just pulled in the school’s parking lot with their van. They got out and opened the door, just as they heard three motorcycles pull back in the driveway!

    The ChaosRiders had made their way back to the schoolyard and they parked the bikes and immediately ran towards the building. Ty and Peter immediately put their psychic powers to use, as they tried to figure out where the box was and where Kent was at that moment.

    Suddenly, Ty heard a screaming coming from one of the cars. They walk over to a large, white van. When they open the large, sliding door, they find Kent, all bound by a thick rope, as he was lying on the back seat of the van. Ty immediately cut his ropes and remove the gag, which was covering his mouth. When they looked the other way, they noticed Randy and Tyrone, who were carrying a big box!

    Kent was incredibly relieved to see Ty, and the two boys embraced each other, as soon as Ty had removed the ropes from his body.

    “Guys…” Ty said to the others. “We can’t go in there with Kent! It’s too dangerous… He’ll get hurt and the goons will recognize him!”

    “What should we do with Kent?” Peter asked Jason. “He’s Ty’s little brother and we don’t want anything to happen to him…”

    “We’ll make Kent into one of the ChaosRiders!” Jason said to his friends. Then he turned towards Ty. “Ty, give Kent the ability to receive the muscles, the strength, and the power of any of the villains he might defeat in battle!”

    “Roger that, chief!” Ty replied cheerfully.

    “And their tattoos!” Kent added on, since he noticed that the three goons had tattoos on their arms as well.

    Ty walked up to Kent and placed his hands on the boy’s shoulders. Immediately, his magic powers started to work on him, and Kent slowly transformed into one of the ChaosRiders! He grew taller and slightly more muscular, and he suddenly had the same mask on his head as the others. He had leather straps and shoulder pads, with the same spikes as the other three heroes.

    “When we defeat any one of the bad guys, then his muscles will be taken away, and they will grow back on our bodies, along with their tattoos!” Ty cheered out loud.

    Paul was pacing around the classroom, while waiting for the guys to show up. Suddenly he heard a commotion out on the parking lot! He immediately ran out of the classroom to see what was going on. When he realized that several people were running out the door to the parking lot, he immediately ran after them!

    “Darn it!” he shouted. “Who called those guys?”

    Suddenly Ty, Peter, Jason and Kent appeared in the parking lot, and Ty grabbed Paul from behind, as he was about to press the button on the remote. Everyone panicked, as a battle started between the ChaosRiders and the goons!
