The added appendage felt even better than he thought as it helped keep his balance better. He scratched at his head as the wild hair around his beard and head actually grew into a proper mane. Even better was feeling his ears rise up and round out. They flickered with excitement as he stared down at Alan. The 4 inches of difference in height had grown to 8 as Zack's body stretched upwards to a full 7 feet.

Zack glanced over at Drake for a moment. "However, that doesn't mean I'm not going to start making some changes. First, let's actually make you a better best friend..."
