    Ethan stopped the car at the back entrance and they both got out, Ethan got his bat ready while Johnny retrieved a tire iron from the trunk. Luckily the back entrance was open and they entered the Mall.
    "Where should we start first?" Johnny asked.
    "I'm thinking we should get to the sporting goods shop, there should be weapons, maybe even a few guns." Ethan answered.
    Johnny nodded his head and the two made their way there. The whole Mall was totally deserted so far, which Johnny consisidered a good thing but Ethan wasn't going to let his guard down. He knew this place wouldn't be abandoned unless there was a reason.
    The two teenagers made it to the sporting goods shop and entered, just as Ethan had thought, there were guns. Most of the guns were locked behind glass, but Ethan found the keys behind the desk and the two loaded up 4 duffle bags: many shotguns, sniper rifles, hunting rifles, handguns and even full auto rifles, and a shit ton of ammo. Ethan also picked up a crossbow and quiver, which he strapped over his back. They also took machetes and hunting knives. The bags were heavy and the teenagers had to pretty much drag them back to the entrance they came from. As they were making their way through the mall Johnny stumbled forward and one of the pistols he had in an outer pocket fell out and slid under the rail to the lower floor. Ethan was about to shout back at his friend when they both heard a giggle echo from below.
    Ethan and Johnny both looked at each other in horror but Ethan gently set his bags down and grabbed his crossbow. Signaling his friend to stay there, he slowly crept to the railing with his crossbow at the ready. He brought his sights over the edge and saw a voluptuous naked woman, keeping his cool he kept his aim at her head as he looked left and right for any signs for more bimbos. The teen couldn't see any more so he steadied his aim and pulled the trigger, the arrow flew into her head and she fell down with a slick thump. Ethan loaded another arrow and went back to his bags, with a nod to Johnny they continued on.
    Then the two boys heard footsteps and turned to see a massive mob of naked women running towards them,
    "We're almost there run!" Johnny shouted and they both ran as fast as they could with the heavy bags of guns.
    Both teenagers struggled to keep ahead of the sexy mob, their arms hurting from the weight of the bags. Miraculously they made it to the exit and flew out the door, shoving the bags in the car. Ethan was starting the car and Johnny was closing the passanger side door when a bimbo grabbed the door. Panicking, Ethan slammed down on the peddle and the car speeded off to the road, the bimbo being torn up by the road and eventually Johnny managed to close the door.
    "No wonder that place was abandoned! It's like a bimbo nest or something!" He exclaimed.
    "Heh, should've figured a bunch of bimbos would set up shop in the mall." The other boy remarked.
    They continued driving and happened upon a gas station.
    "Let's get loaded up and then get some gas for the road, mabey some food too." Ethan said as he stopped the car.
    Each teen had a pistol holstered to their side, magazines, a machete and a hunting knive on the belts they looted from the mall, around their backs were one machine gun and one semi auto shotgun. They also had a pistol tucked on their backs and bags that had extra ammo for the guns. Ethan also had a quiver attached to his bag and carried his crossbow.
    Johnny had his rifle ready and they both entered the gas station, luckily no one was there, and the two gathered food and water and loaded them into the truck, they also filled up many gas canisters and loaded them as well. Once they were done they got back into the car and drove off to Johnny's house, his parents probably weren't home but Johnny's sister, Tiffany, was. Ethan never told anyone but he had a crush on Tiffany and needed to make sure she was alright.
