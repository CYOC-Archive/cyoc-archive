As Jacob saw that his owner was done entering the change that would change him back to the form he believed was his true one, he stood there ready for the changes.

    Quickly he started to shrink and loose definition becoming a vaguely human shaped blob. This time, however, the blob split into 2 identical humanoid parts which both individually grew props, motors batteries and a base-plate and used their props to dampen the fall into the horizontal position. Apparently Jacob didn't notice that he was duplicated as his two copies both texted Matt that he should go on with the correction before turning themselves off which was indicated by the power LED going out. Matt somehow must have duplicated Jacob, and looking back at the computer screen he found a setting "copies" he previously overlooked which was set to two. Quickly he selected one of the Jacobs and pulled out the memory card before inserting it into his computer. There he undid the changes, making him believe again, that usually he was a human, and that he was in fact his friend and not his owner. The saved the changed and replaced the memory card into the Jacob he selected and turned him on.

    "Did it work?", Jacob (1) asked. "Well do you still think you were born as a drone?", Matt asked. "No, I don't think so. It's so weird, just a few minutes ago I thought I had always been a drone. This way it makes a lot more sense." Then he saw his copy, "Hey, did you get another drone?" Matt confessed scratching the back of his head, "I must have copied you during your last change. Haven't changed the mind of the other one yet". Jacob (1) became curious, "I wanna watch how you change him". Matt removed the memory card of Jacob (2) and placed it into his computer. Stored on it were many files which Jacob (2) watched curiously. Eventually Matt got to the file he had edited before and changed it back. "So, those files contain all my memories? Everything I think and feel and am?", the drone texted. "I think so, and I can even edit them, as you've seen before". "Now could you look into the ideas folder, I think I have a little surprise for my copy", Jacob (1) texted and Matt opened it. The file names seemed fairly cryptic, but somehow they made sense to Jacob who texted one of the file names to Matt with the instruction to change Jacob (2) into this. He set up the changes and replaced the memory card. Before he turned on Jacob (2) he started the changes.

    Near instantly the drone changed into a blob which grew a bit gaining more weight and becoming more massive. It shifted into an upright stubby humanoid form about 25 centimeters (10 inches) high. Gradually it got more and more details. At first the feet changed into boots, looking fairly mechanical made out of dark brown plastic with a set of screw holes in the back. Then his legs changed. They became equally brown plastic cylinders connected to the boots via a joint. At the knees the thighs widened dramatically forming an angular shape that narrowed a bit at the hips. There a set of joints formed, partly obscured by the the thighs. Between the joints a band formed with a little hatch bot at the front and the rear. Above the joints a black belt formed, with a simple silver belt buckle appearing on the front. On it, a set of 6 small pockets appeared seemingly hanging from it. On his back a plate, held by a set of screws fixed that belt to his body. Above the buckle, a set of 4 dark grey plates appeared surrounding a green LED. Around them a rim formed. At the same height, a ridge formed, separating the chest from the abdomen. Both parts changed to slightly brighter brown. The upper part bulged out to form a pair of chest plates with one of them sporting a pilot's wings emblem. On the back an array of rocket nozzles formed and 2 tubes extended forming themselves into wings growing a membrane each. Then the changes reached his arms. Ball joins formed at the sited of the fake fur collar that lined his chest. Bulky angular shoulders appeared which extended to arms which grew in bulk below the elbows with mechanical hands. His head formed a helmet like shape with a recessed face that grew a light brown fur, a mouth with noticable fangs and a fairly big pointy nose. On his forehead a sort of visor shield appeared, a cross between a medieval knight and a pair of goggles. With the final changes a pair of oversized mechanical ears appeared on the helmet.
