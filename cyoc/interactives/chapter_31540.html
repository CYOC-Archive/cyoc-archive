    You Join the Raccoons...literally!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 15 years ago A AR BM S

    You remember seeing how the squirrels mercillesly attacked the humans. How was that saving the world. You silently steal a laser dagger from Zark and move towards the squirrel leader. Zark moves to stop you but you wink without the Squirrel leader noticing. He grins as you move forward and prepares to "squirrelify" you. You suddenly dodge the weapon and kick upward catching the evil leader in the jaw. However he retaliates and uses a blaster on you. As you lie there dying you glare and with the last of your strength you leap up. Then you drive the dagger into his evil heart. He screams in pain before he drops dead.

    Zark and his army run to you as you lay there dying. The group effortlessly annihilates the rest of the squirrels. Zark himself takes a syringe and injects you with it. He then picks you up and teleports to his ship.

    Your eyes open to see bright white lights. You also see yourself surrounded by female raccoons who mutter incoherent words about you. However you do hear the words 'brave', 'converted', 100%, and 'reward'. You get up slowly and look around you notice that you've slightly shrunk. Finally one of the ladies presses an intercom button. Zark comes running in and sighs seeing you're okay.

    "You had us worried there kid. Not many survive the way you did against Nux. But you killed him single handedly! You've got a lot of potential. Sorry I had to transform your body. But it was the only way I could save you in time. It looks like you're age went down a little too." You're startled and leap out of the bed and run to a mirror.

    You are now about the size of a 15 year old teenager and coated in grey fur. A long raccoon tail with 15 black rings swings behind you. Your chest is white and you're slightly impressed you're muscels have bulked up a bit. However the fact you're no longer human overcomes you and you start to cry. Zark comes over and hugs you and comforts you. Finally you recover and go sit on the bed.

    "So now what am I going to do?" You ask. "I can't live on Earth like this."

    "Well you could live on my planet. You're a regular hero to us. Not even the best of us could take down Nux and you did it single handedly. I know its not much of a reward. But we'd be honored if you would join us. Sure you'll have to have more training and start your life all over again. But you'll be regarded as a hero on our planet. So what do you say? Will you be my son?

    "Sure why not? I don't really have much of a choice. And since those squirrels are responsible for whats happened I'd love to get back at them!"

    "Good. It won't be easy in the military though understand. So what will you do start a new life on our planet, or join the military?

    What do you do now?
