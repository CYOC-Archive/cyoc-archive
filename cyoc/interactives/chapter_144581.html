    Finding Damon in the sewers.

    Branch Full view
    added 4 years ago A O

    Monica begins to look for Damon. She look everywhere but haven't found him yet.

    The only place, she hasn't look, was in the sewers. Monica enters the sewers as a horrible stench reach her nose. She almost threw up.

    "Eww. Don't know why Damon might be down here." Monica looks around and try's to find Damon.

    She found other rats. But none of them were Damon. Eventually she found a human size rat.

    "Damon!" She ran over to him. "Monica what are you doing here?" He ask. "Looking for you. I didn't think I find you here." Said Monica.

    "I was looking for a place to hide. I knew rats hide in sewers. So that's where I went." Said Damon.

    "I'm just glad your alright." Monica hugs Damon as she felt his soft rat fur as Damon hugs her back.

    "We need to get out of here. This isn't a very good hiding spot. It stinks down here." Said Monica.

    "You'll get use to the stench." Said Damon. The two of them then begins to leave the sewers.
