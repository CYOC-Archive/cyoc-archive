    Prince Jasper laid on the bed in the Copperstone residence guest room. When the amulet was removed, he had been returned to his princely form. With one major change. He sat up, groaning slightly from the effort. This far along, moving was difficult. Within his belly, he felt a few kicks.

    “Hush,” he scolded the babies he was carrying. Despite being a man again, he was still pregnant, complete with having a womanhood to birth his offspring. Anya said that once he gave birth, his manhood would return. Jasper couldn’t be more eager for that. Though Anya had been kind enough to satisfy his sexual desires by using magic to give herself a manhood whenever he got in the mood.

    Jasper stood up and began to waddle downstairs. The witch midwife would be arriving soon to tell him of what he was carrying, as well as trigger labor if he was far enough along. Anya had done some magic and determined he was pregnant with twins. A boy and a girl. Though she wasn’t sure whether they would be horses, human, or some combination of the two. The witch midwife would be able to answer that question when she came.

    “My Prince,” Anya greeted him as he waddled into the living room. She guided him onto a couch. “How are you feeling?”

    “Ready to give birth,” Jasper grumbled. “I am sick of hiding inside.”

    “I told you, my hometown is full of witches. With so many of us, errant spells are common. You are not the only pregnant man right now.”

    “I do not wish to be seen in such a vulnerable state.”

    “Ah. Yes, I understand,” Anya said, nodding. A servant walked in, a middle-aged blonde woman with her. “Laressa, thank you for coming.”

    “It is no problem,” said the woman, Laressa the witch midwife. “Now, let me examine you, my Prince.” Jasper laid on his back on the couch. Laressa waved her hands across his body. “Hmm. I think you are ready to give birth.”

    “I am?” Jasper asked eagerly. Laressa nodded. “What of the babies? What are they? Human? Horse?”

    “Let me trigger labor in you. You will find out when they come out,” Laressa said vaguely. Jasper groaned. He would have liked to know what to expect. Laressa waved her hands, muttering under her breath. Jasper let out a cry of pain as he experienced his first contraction. His labor had begun.
