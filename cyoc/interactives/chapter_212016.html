    Skylar's Transformation

    added by Elflover 8 months ago A O

    Herbie barely had time to enjoy what would occur to Jeff, now a chimp. Before letting a concerned citizen call authorities because of a chimp running around loose, of all things, he wanted to see what how Jeff would react to bananas. But he didn't have time because things were about to get more interesting. The rest of the team was entering the locker room. Would they find Jeff the chimp? Or would they get to the water with the transformative agent before? Frankly, Herbie wished for the second, although the first would be fun enough.
    And indeed, the second seemed to be the likeliest. The team filed in, chatting and grumbling about the practice. They were completely unaware that a chimp, a dangerous animal ultimately, was very close by. And how could they?What were the probabilities, after all? Herbie smiled widely as the players started to use the water cooler. Finally! Not only would the effects be a surprise, but he would get great pleasure in seeing those jocks get their just deserts! He didn't know what the results would bring but he'd be sure to enjoy them! Maybe even participate if it was entertaining enough. Soon, all the team had quenched their thirst, curtesy of the coach who had made them train thoroughly and deal with their parched throats thoroughly. A jock named Skylar was first and soon enough, as he undressed, he started feeling rather strange and even dizzy...
