    The Zoo

    Two New Anthros In The Making

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 17 days ago O

    Author’s Note: Noticed everyone seems to forget the protagonist in the other chapter options, so I decided to fix it.

    “Yeah! I want to be just like all of you!” Chris said enthusiastically.

    You continue to look at the teen with a shocked expression. Why the hell was this kid so eager to turn into an anthropomorphic animal?

    “Great!” the lion girl says with a laugh. “Although there is one condition.”

    “What? I’ll do anything!” Chris responds desperately.

    “At least two humans are needed to complete a human to animal transformation,” she explains before pointing at you. “I believe your fellow human will have to agree to begin the ritual.

    Chris looks at you with a wild desire in his eyes. He looked beyond reason. You back away from him slowly, trying to keep him from making any sudden movements.

    “Listen Chris, I’m happy for you and can accept you wanting to become an anthro animal but… I don’t want to.” You hold your hands up as you continue to back away, glancing in all directions for an escape.

    “And I’m sorry too,” Chris says before motioning to something behind you. Turning around you find nothing before feeling a blunt object hit your head. Instantly, you pass out.

    ***

    You awaken tied to a chair, your head slightly throbbing. As you look at your surroundings you see Chris sitting beside you in a chair, completely untied. The two of you were in the middle of a large glowing circle that had been drawn on the ground with strange symbols.

    Suddenly, a hooded figure appears before you. It was impossible to discern what it was as its clothes keep it completely hidden from view.

    “Wha-what’s going on?!” you shout, struggling to break free from your binds.

    “Oh, you’re awake. That’s great!” Chris says with a wide smile as he stares at you with content. “They just finished the ritual so we’re about to become anthros!”

    “What?! No! Let me go!” You continue your pathetic attempts to break free as the hooded figure places their hand on the circle.

    “Look! That’s what we’re about to become!” Chris said as he pointed to the figure.

    The hooded animal begins to pull on their hood, revealing their appearance and the form the two of you were about to become.

    What do you do now?
