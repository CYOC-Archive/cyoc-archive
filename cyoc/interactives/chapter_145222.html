    It had been the first time in centuries that the Unicorns had gathered together. It hadn't been easy, but once word went out the members of the once great herds had gathered together in the forest. However, there were only a few dozen of them left, most of them having been hunted or killed when their forests were torn down by humans.

    Now they were gathered to figure out how to deal with this problem.

    "Ahem, I am glad you could all come," a white mare with a golden mane said as she drew the attention of the other Unicorns. "Our once proud race has been on the decline for the past few centuries. But the humans have forgotten about us, they simply think of us as mythological creatures. Unfortunately, if we want to actually survive as a species, then we are going to have to adapt, and bring more members into our herd."

    "And how are we supposed to do that?" One of the stallions said with a snort. "It's not like we can mate with other species and this is all the Unicorns left in the world. What would you suggest we do, Marigold?"

    "What I suggest is that we use our magic to create new Unicorns," Marigold said as she swished her tail a little. "I have been experimenting with transformation magic..."

    "Transformation magic? But that's one of the forbidden arts!" One of the mares protested. "Our Goddess would..."

    "Our Goddess came to me in a vision, she spoke to me and said this is our best way to do this," Marigold said as she shook her head. "I've experimented with it on several occasions, the problem, however, seems to be our sapience to use a human term. I started it by attempting it on equines as they are our closest relative, but the sudden sapience drove most of them mad. Unfortunately, this means that the only beings we can transform are humans. But we should not force this on them, I will teach all of you these arts and it will be how we will survive as a species, forming great new herds."

    "And how do we know this will even work?" The stallion from before asked though he had to admit he was curious.

    "I will demonstrate, come forth Steven," Marigold said and they all blinked in confusion when a human male who was completely naked walked into the clearing. He blushed a little when he realized all the eyes were on him.

    "Do not worry child, tell me, are you here out of your own free will?" Marigold asked.

    "Indeed, I wanted to take your offer as soon as you offered it," Steven said with a blush. "I didn't expect it to be in front of so many Unicorns though."

    "Do not worry, you are to be a demonstration for them," Marigold said with a smile as she gave him a wink. "As you can see, there are many humans who enjoy this kind of thing that this can appeal to. In addition, humans on the verge of death can be offered a second chance at life as a Unicorn and many of them will grab that chance. Now, for the demonstration."

    She turned to Steven and her horn glowed brightly as her magic enveloped the young man. The transformation started quickly as brown fur sprouted out of his body encompassing his skin. He fell forward onto his hands as they formed into hooves and his chest barreled out into that of a Unicorn. His black hair formed its mane and his face pushed out to form his muzzle as his horn grew out of his forehead.

    A few of the mares blushed a little when they realized they were staring at the new stallion's massive cock that had slid out of its sheath. Marigold smiled as she lifted her tail to him. "You will find that he is fully functional as a stud, as is any that we transform, in fact, the process increases their libido temporarily and sends new mares into heat to improve chances of offspring. Once that is done, it will be the duty of the herd to make sure they get acclimated to their new lives."

    She smiled back at Steven and gave him a nod. "I will teach you all these arts, and soon we will repopulate our species. This is our best chance at survival as a species, and humans are overpopulated. This is simply a way to balance things out."

    The others nodded in agreement as they came to agree with Marigold. The time would soon come where they would learn the arts and bring more Unicorns into their herds.
