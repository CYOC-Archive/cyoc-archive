    Mike the Resistant but Gushing Jeannie

    added by Cuttingroom22 2 years ago O Mental

    Seeing the ecstatically happy and now beautifully dressed woman before him who just happened to be his father and his genie at the same time Jeff had an idea as to how to take this experience to the next level and get a little fun out of it in the process. Knowing that the original mental changes to his father came about through the chronivac machine and not any wishes of his own directed at her Jeff figured that any further changes to her mind would have to be done through the machine and not through wishes as they wouldn’t have any effect by the latter way. Asking Jeannie to stay were she was Jeff hopped back over to his desk where the chronivac machine lay and began punching in edited instructions as to the makeup of Jeannie’s mental state as a genie. Looking with a smile at his finished work several minutes later Jeff was just about to hit the enter key when he heard Jeannie in the background ‘Master what are you doing? Can I serve you in that same task in anyway?” Hovering his finger over the key Jeff looked back at his genie and with a big smile said ‘no Jeannie I got this’ while slamming down on the key. Jeannie’s eyelids bulged wide before they suddenly shut and her head drooped downward as if she had nodded off while standing.

    Several minutes passed before signs of life began to stir once again on Jeannie’s features. Jeff had stood up and had become concerned but those concerns were swept away when Jeannie came to and rightfully reacted. Popping her head back up slowly and fluttering her eyelids before slowly yawning and stretching her arms over her head Jeannie leaned on one leg in a sign of unexpectedness while twirling her hair with her fingers in one hand and smoothing out her dress with the other. In a listless and tired voice that oozed sexiness Jeannie asked “Master what the heck is going on, why am I in this delightful and enjoyable body and am dressed in this wonderful outfit? I’m your father for darn sake.” There was no anger or excitement in the speech and not even mystery almost as if Jeannie already knew why she was in the current form she was in and lazily asked the question for her own amusement. With the smile returning to his lips after for so long being concerned for Jeannie Jeff opened his mouth and triumphantly spelled out to his former father why she was sporting that body and role. ‘Well Jeannie and I am going to call you Jeannie for the last twenty minutes your mind had been rewritten because of an auto-correct spelling error in the original transformation instructions. The error had turned you into a female genie with the mind of one to boot. I have ultimately decided to give you your original mind back but with some modifications in place judging by how you find yourself now. I allowed you to have back your memories of your life as my father and your original mindset but now you answer to the name of Jeannie and are my personal wish genie. Jeannie you are forbidden from showing or feeling anger or cursing, you must grant any wish I desire, you will be happy and ecstatic to please me, and I also programmed that you think, act, and dress feminine and derive even more pleasure from me your master seeing you act and dress as a girly girl.’

    Jeannie took all this in and didn’t even pick up her eyes or tilt her head up as she continued looking at the ground and twirling her hair. When Jeff had finished his speech Jeannie’s eyes slowly moved upwards and met his as a big toothy smile formed on her lips and giggles began to escape her breath ‘thank you master for telling me this as it makes me very happy. I wished you had made me aware of the changes earlier and maybe not been so drastic with them but how can I complain when I’m here to serve you my Master and I love being so sexy while in your presence.’ Jeff couldn’t believe his ears the mental reconditioning had sailed through with flying colors and now he could move on with the rest of his plans.
