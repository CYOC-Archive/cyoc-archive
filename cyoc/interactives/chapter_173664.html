    Leah's Baby Disguise

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago AR O Clothes Baby

    Leah knows that if she ups the “baby” look, Tina won’t recognize her. She spots a nearby pacifier and quickly pops it into her mouth, then removes all her clothes, leaving her in just her diaper. After a moment’s internal debate, she grabs a pair of safety scissors. Her hair is promptly trimmed and styled into a very babyish hairstyle. It might be difficult to deal with when she goes home at the end of the day, but she’s concerned about the current moment.

    Tina’s voice sounds. Leah quickly copies the behavior of the younger daycare attendees. She lays on her back and grabs her feet, rocking back and forth on her back, suckling loudly on the pacifier.

    “I was hoping to take a look at the kids,” Tina says, coming into view.

    “Sure thing!” Riley says. Tina comes over and looks at the kids in the “baby room”. Scared Tina will still recognize her, Leah decides to pee. Warmth floods her diaper. Even from this angle, she can see it turning yellow. Tina’s gaze completely skips over her.

    “Cute,” Tina says, then leaves. Relieved, Leah sits up, still sucking on the pacifier.
