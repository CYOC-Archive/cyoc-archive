    Edwin shook his head, moving away from the rose. There was no way that anything in the shop could be close to magical.... right?

    Edwin continued browsing the magic shop, out of sheer curiosity as to what these fools passed off as 'magic.'

    It was nonsense, that's what all of it was. Magic wasn't real, end of story, that was the gist of it. Edwin chuckled to himself, almost feeling sorry for any that asked otherwise.

    Passing by one of the shelves, however, a large golden book caught his eye. Edwin stopped to look closer, seeing the edges covered in a coating of solid gold.

    "Magic Spells: Everything You Need To Be A Wizard?" Edwin said to himself, reading out the title. "What stupidity is this?"

    He flipped it open, brushing through the pages. He found the spells to be written in Ancient Greek, which did make it seem a bit mysterious....

    Edwin frowned, he wanted to see for himself if this would work. He turned to the transform one's self section, beginning to read a spell.

    "Magics above and Magics below...." He recited in Ancient Greek. "Turn me into....
