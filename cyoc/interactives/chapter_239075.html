    Part of Oscar didn’t want to force the change on Lindsay but he knew he didn’t really have much of a choose. He could go out and infect others with this gift and get some food by hunting down and killing humans for food instead of turning them. He could do all of that and hope someone else infect Lindsay but that was too risky.

    Someone else could infect Lindsay and possibly claim her as a mate or worse kill her and eat her for food. Now he had to do this.
    He moved his hand toward the closed window and extended one of his new razor-sharp claws. With hardly any force his claws broke the latch on the window. His new leopard form was strong enough to flip cars and his skin was tough enough to take multiple rounds from anything smaller than a heavy machine gun without drawing blood.

    Lindsay didn’t hear the latch break; her headphones were in and she was somewhat singing along to the song as she was working at her desk. Her back was to the window, so she never saw Oscar open the window or him climb in.

    If she had she would have seen a tall and extremely fit creature which bares a strong resemblance to her boyfriend in the face and somewhat the body. The rush of cool fall night air coming into the room did cause Lindsay to stop her work. She put down her pen and started to turn off the music on her laptop.

    She never got a chance as Oscar moved with impossible speed and yet too quiet for her to hear with headphones in. He was on top of her within seconds, he sank his fangs into the back of her neck as one of his hands moved to silence her scream pain, at the same time he spun her out of her chair and onto the floor.

    Lindsay was struggling against him a released his bite, almost instantly what would have been a life-threatening wound on Lindsay’s neck healed.

    “I am sorry Lindsay, but the pain will be over soon.” Reassured Oscar “It’s me Oscar don’t struggle; you will feel better than you ever have in your life in a short while.”

    Lindsay seemed to lessen her struggle with that, as Oscar moved to kiss her neck. That was something she loved as a human. She let out a soft moan as he moved his hand away from her mouth. She didn’t scream but let out a slightly louder moan as the change was about to happen to her.
