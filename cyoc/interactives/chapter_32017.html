    Progressing, yet regressing

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 15 years ago AR AP

    You start to feel desperate. You had to try to change back. Handing the woman back the five dollar bill she had given you, you start to look around for a clue to which bottle would change you back.

    Another red one? No, don't risk that, it only made you a little older, and, even though it changed you before, who's to say if it will change you back? It might make you more of a girl.

    How about a green one? You pick up a bottle of green liquid and look into it. The small, slow moving bubbles looked like skulls. Better not choose that one.

    That blue one seems to look safe... Grabbing it from the shelf, you unplug the stopper and tip the liquid into your mouth.

    The instant you empty the bottle, you begin to feel odd. You begin to regain your lost years! As you grow taller and taller, your excitement grows as you think you might just get back to the age you had been... but to no avail. You stop just short of your target of 18, probably 15 or 16 years old. Looking down at yourself, past the new mounds on your chest, you notice your clothes are back to what they were before you had become a baby, only now obviously made for a girl's body.

    You start to take a step away from the woman's stand, thinking you would try to find a different way to change back to a man, when the woman said again, "The changes are not yet over." You froze in your tracks, wondering what could possibly be happening now. You took another look down at your body. Only another instant later, your clothes started to change.

    The pants grow shorter and shorter, your shirt starts to fuse with your pants... Panicked, you see your reflection in a full-length mirror next to the stand. Instead of your previous shirt and jeans, you saw you now wear a very short, pink, frilly dress. For a moment, you notice, under the petticoated skirt, a pair of pink panties, but in another instant, they are gone, replaced by a big, pink diaper.

    You look to the woman and start to say something, but she silences you by forcing a pacifier into your mouth.

    What do you do now?
