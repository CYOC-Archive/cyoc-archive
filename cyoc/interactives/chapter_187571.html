    "Okay..." Jason thought. "I could change them one by one, but that'd take forever." He reasoned. "But, if I try to change the place as a whole, that probably wouldn't work well either... so I guess I gotta do something in between!"

    Jason selected karate studio again. This time, he wasn't looking to change what kind of location it was, but rather the specific activity. He finally loaded a sentence that would work. "The class right now is focused on couples." It read. It looked like they were still couples, but instead of a class to help for childbirth they were with their pregnant wives performing aggressive kicks?

    Jason didn't want to perform any accidental abortions, so the first step was to get the women to be no longer pregnant. He selected "couples" and changed it to "families" then glanced up to see if this had worked.

    To his relief, it had! The instructor looked the same, but had a nicer smile across her face. Now there were quite a number of more people, all various ages of children. The women were now no longer pregnant, all doing much better with their kicks and looking much less tired. One couple were holding the hand of a small boy, who was gleefully trying to kick.

    Another couple looked a little more exacerbated, trying to convince a snotty looking teenaged girl to join in. An Asian couple were chatting casually with their twin tween boys while they were doing their routine half-heartedly. An hispanic couple were heavily involved, and impressively enough the woman held a small baby. She was latched on to her mothers swollen breast, which stunned Jason as the plump woman somehow fed her child while still doing the same routine!

    "Okay... so at least no more pregnant people..." He muttered.

    After a couple more resets on sentences, Jason reached one that he figured could do what he needed. It read, "This karate class is filled with people of different ages." He smiled, figuring this would hopefully return them to normal. He then altered "different" to "similar" instead. He looked up from his phone to see his plan had kinda worked.

    Instead of the college to high school range that occupied the majority of the original studio, it now seemed to be primarily late Middle school to early high school. The instructor was also much younger. The curly haired Milf was now a youthful college student looking goddess. She was very fit, and seemed fully focused on her routine.

    There were a mix of younger and older kids, all trying their best to keep up. Some looked familiar like a freshman looking Latina girl who was focused and a middle school Asian girl who looked quite similar to the man from before, but others seemed totally different such as a group of bratty white girls who were barely even trying to perform the kicks as the blabbed about random gossip.

    "Ugh, come onnn." Jason grumbled. With every step closer to their normal forms, he felt like he took 2 steps back. At least there weren't any unborn people, but their ages, genders and races were all over the place! Was this even worth it? He could try to do one by one but he wasn't even sure who anyone had once been, other than maybe the instructor.

    Maybe he could try to just change the damn studio again to something better or more appropriate and move on? Or maybe, he was thinking too hard about all this. If reality changed, nothing was "wrong" right? Like, they all remembered this as how they've always been, so maybe Jason shouldn't worry about it? Whenever he changed one person it was easier, but it dominoes to unexpected changes the more people and places that get involved.

    As Jason thought, that's when...
