    Transform or Dare?

    Kyle couldn't believe it, Felicia had said yes! This would be his first time. Even if that first time had to be as a walrus, he didn't care. At that moment, he *was* a walrus and so was Felicia.

    They made it to shore and checked to see if anyone was watching.

    Fortunately, they had privacy.

    "Kyle," Felicia said. "Don't worry about it. No one will know it's us. For the moment, let's not be Kyle and Felicia. Right now, now, let's just be two walruses in the throws of passion."

    Kyle blinked. "you mean it? Felicia I -- Felicia, I've never felt anything like this."

    Felicia licked Kyle's muzzle. "It's all right Kyle. Let the instinct guide you. Right now, you are a walrus. Don't try to be Kyle, at least not Kyle the human. Just be the walrus you."

    Kyle felt his walrus parts responding. He'd heard of how large walruses were back there and given what he was feeling, he believed it.

    He manoeuvred so he and Felicia were belly to belly and put his flippers around her. The connection returned.

    Felicia's thoughts, emotions and even hormones danced with Kyle's in a virtual pool that was their connected minds. If one would imagine it, perhaps one might see an endless field of stars and two whisps of energy in the shape of a walrus, one pink and one blue. Both moved toward each other and then cobmined to form one form that had a swirling of both colours.

    Kyle not only sensed but also felt Felicia's arousal. She felt his as well. Her walrus instincts were in full swing. Something seemed different about her in a way that profoundly excited him. She was still Felicia but the part of her that was human was feeling much more walrus in nature, but with human intelligence and knowledge as a guide. This side of her was ready, boy was it ever ready!

    The act only strengthened their connection. Both felt each other's physical pleasure as well as their emotional pleasure. The outside world seemed to cease to exist for both.

    For several moments of both confusion and bliss, Kyle felt as though he was Felicia. He understood her thoughts and felt the act from her point of view. It was bliss, it was ecstasy and, given the pleasure he was giving her, certainly boosted his self confidence.

    Indeed, if all good things must come to and end then all great things must as well. Kyle felt a building pressure, Felicia knew what was coming and her body readied itself.

    In several moments of bellows that were to a walrus what cries of pleasure are to a human, Kyle and Felicia both climaxed.

    Kyle withdrew from Felicia (and indeed, he needed a few moments to realize that he *was* Kyle and that he was the he). Both lay there panting and looking each other in the eyes with smiles only walruses could produce.

    "That was amazing," Kyle said.

    "Incredible," Felicia said. "I have to say Kyle, you make one special walrus."

    "So, what do we do now?" Kyle asked.
