    As the mist solidified into a human form, Jenn's eyes widened and both she and Emily turned away. "Uh. Jody.... He's shucked down to his nether regions."

    "I'm guessing you mean 'naked'. Well, you gave me the ability to give him a body. Not clothes." Jody tilted her head. "Though I'm not minding the view. Damn."

    From the corner of Jenn's eyes, not that she was consciously looking that way, she say the figure stand up. He looked to be in his early twenties, blond with highly feathered (if not outright spiked) hair, about 5 foot 8, and his body was lean and extremely well muscled. Not to mention he was very well LOOKING UP AT HIS FACE! His face was a mix of Caucasian and Asian with very striking and strange sea-green eyes.

    "I'm out," John said in a voice very different from that he had in the game, but at the same time still with the same accent. "Holy shit, I'm out! This is awesome!" He jumped up and down in excitement.

    Jennifer tried hard to keep his eyes on John's face as he bounced up and down. Jody, who was back to being glow-less, had no problem keeping her gaze below John's waistline. Emily was still standing with her back to him.

    "Thank you!" John said as lunged forward and hugged Jody and then Jenn in turn. "Thank you! You have no idea how much of a hell this has been!" Jenn went stiff with the hug, but in moments he had let go and grabbed Emily from behind to hug her. "You have no idea how many horrible smells exist in movie scenes nobody mentions, not to mention trying to find a moment to have a toilet available on-screen."

    "Yeah," said Emily. "I remember when you asked me to pause psycho for three minutes. Can you get some clothes on?"

    Jody just continued grinning. "There's no need to rush this."

    "Huh. Oh, uh sure. Sorry. Where can I-?" He looked down at himself. "This isn't me."

    "I didn't know what you looked like. So I just figured I'd make you look like a real-world realistic-like version of the guy in the game." She stepped up to him, within a few inches. "And I have to say I think I did a damn good job."

    Jennifer saw John's member twitch. "Okay!" she shouted as she started looking around. "Towel. Blanket. Actual real clothes." She started hunting for a linen closet, a bedroom, or ANYTHING where she might find something to cover him up. Much to Jody's annoyance, they had him in some of Jody's brother's spare clothes.

    "On the bright side," said Emily. "At least my mother won't recognize you."

    "What's she going to do?" asked Jody. "It's not like she has control of the curse anymore."

    Emily swallowed. "She has other means of making people's lives miserable. She... has skills."

    "Like what?" asked Jenn. Emily bit her lip.

    Then John spoke up, "Like killing people. She's a retired assassin."

    "What?" Jody blurted out.

    Emily hit John in the arm. "You're not supposed to know that! How do you know that?"

    He shrugged. "I overheard things a few times. There was one time when you were young and curious about the movie your parents were watching in their bedroom, so I appeared in it. They kept you out of the room, but I was still there. And I guess they weren't paying attention, because they got uh... intimate after they put you to bed, and they talked for a bit."

    The three stared at him, a few disturbed.

    "Did they talk about her killing people after or during the intimate parts?"

    "Jody!" shouted Emily.

    John bit his lip and said nothing.

    "Hold up!" said Jennifer. "Was she the one that killed your dad's captor?"

    Again, Emily didn't answer. She kinda looked ashamed. John smirked, "They had to had met somehow. And trust me, if I had known that when I was younger, I would had tried much harder not to piss her off time to time. Anyway, um. New questions. Where am I going to stay? Also, what year is it now anyway? It was kinda hard to keep track exactly."

    Emily did finally answer this time. "It's been a little over a decade, John. It's 2005," she said with sympathy.

    John blinks in shock. "Holy shit. I'm in the future! Oh my-!" His mock horror instantly vanished. "Yeah, I kinda figured it's been about a decade since you're like high school age. But uh... am I lodging here or is there someplace else?"
