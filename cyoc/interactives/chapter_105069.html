    Aside from the bizarre experience the experiment turned out to be quite boring. C0 had gotten used to his roomates being basically big babies that played around the mat. The most normal of them was C3 who still looked like a boy, but still acted way beyond his apparent years, C0 would sometimes play with them but usually got bored and watched tv. Finally the doctor came back and took the clones away.

    "Well that was quite the experience don't you think?"

    "You can say that again. So what's next?"

    "Well this is were things start getting interesting. You see we are not only creating clones, we are actually creating the market for genetic modifications."

    "Aha... like giving people superpowers or something?"

    "Less comic book, but in a way. The trouble is genetic manipulation of a mature life-form is extremely complicated and yield such a small result. However if we apply them to clones, well you see the difference between C1 and C2. Now the next step of course is that people want those modifications for themselves. The technicality is very complicated but basically we are able to insert the mind of a person into a clone... in theory."

    "In theory?"

    "That's were you come in my boy. We'll insert your mind into one of the clones and see the results."

    "Hell no, I don't want you to extract my brain!"

    "Nothing that barbaric, we wouldn't hurt you. It's a machine, your body will stay in stasis, is completely safe we have tested it on various animals before."

    "Is it reversible?"

    "If we can transport it once, we can do it again."

    "Wow... so in which clone I'll end up?"

    "We kind of want to circle you through a bunch but since this is you, there's no harm in letting you choose. Come with me and you can see the selection."

    What do you do now?
