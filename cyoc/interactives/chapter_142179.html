The faint beeping of my neighbor's alarm clock from the other side of the wall told me it was 6:30-ish. My allergies were acting up again, as they did every spring. Everything else was normal, but I had a distinct feeling that something was wrong about this morning. Reaching for my phone, I pressed the power button to read the date: April 16th.

But then, where did the watch come from? What happened last night that I didn't remember?
