    That was the straw that broke the camel’s back. Or in this case, Tyler’s eyes.

    Tyler’s vision imeadily began to tear up as a mind numbing pain started searing the back of Tyler’s eyes. The skin around Tyler’s eyes turned in to a smooth grey color as his eye sockets started stretching out into a hammer shape. When the pain finally subsided, Tyler opened his eyes only to find that he can’t close them again, much less blink. The weird thing is that, Tyler can now feel... something throughout the whole room. But that is quickly ignored by how quickly his eyes are drying out. Another thing to note is how much wider his vision is, but he can no longer see the gap between his eyes leaving him to manage two separate visions at once. Oh sure, the gap isn’t that big, leaving him with only a far sighted wide vision, but he can’t focus on anything but far away. Even so, The even slightest notion of split vision is leaving him with a headache.

    “Oh, Tyler. Looks like you found out what’s it like to be a hammerhead shark.” Madame smiled.

    “Hammerhead Sharks are known to have the ability to sense electrons.” Madame explained.

    She must be talking about that strange feeling in the room.

    “Is this what this is? Just turn me back already!” Tyler exclaimed.

    “Na ah ah. Animals are not allowed to make their own choices. Only REAL men can do that.”

    A thought in the back of Tyler’s mind tells him men might not that different from animals. However, Tyler didn’t have time to think on that thought as the skin around his eyes began to turn green with scales.

    “Looks like the next change is starting. Let’s have a closer look then.” Madame exclaimed.

    She emphasized her point by leaning closer to Tyler. Unbeknownst to Tyler however, Madame has magically enhance the audiences vision to have a better view of Tyler. Of course, she castes a spell so no one will question their better eye sight. Nether the less, Tyler’s Transformation continues.

    The scaly green skin around Tyler’s soulless black eyes began to fold up. Next, Tyler’s black shark eyes began to bulge out like a bollon is being inflated behind it. Tyler soon found that he could move his eyes again, but as soon as they started moving slightly apart, Tyler’s headache intensified. If Tyler had a slight headache when he had hammerhead shark vision, he has a brain splitting headache now with chameleon eyes. It’s gonna to take him a while get used to this, but for now, his chameleon eyes are swiveling wildly in all directions. For now, his brain is figuring out how to manage two eyesights at once. The last thing the onlookers noticed was how a bright green reptilian slits appeared in the center of Tyler’s black shark eyes.

    Meanwhile, Madame was very amused by how this is turning out. It is rare for someone to spin a hybrid panel, much less twice. She has a good feeling that this young man will quench her of boredom. Every time she and her son settle down, it’s the same thing. Some fool calls her out, she punishes them, and they leave with a whimper. But this time, she hope it will be different for Tyler. Hopefully, she is not betting her hopes on this idiot because she’s getting soft with age. Nether less, She wants to see a TRUE hybrid. Madame feels like she should telepathically take control of the wheel and make sure Tyler get hybrid panels only... no.... she doesn’t need to go that far...

    “Alright Tyler, LOOKS like your getting acquitted with those chameleon eyes, but “eye” SEE that EYE need to spice things.”

    The men in the stands simply grouned at Madame’s bad attempts at making puns. She turned and looked toward the audience.

    “After Tyler finishes his eye transformation, Tyler will be dealing with new rules involving the wheel from this point forward. Whenever Tyler spins a change to a body part that’s already transformed, instead of the new one replacing the old one, the two transformations will merge together into a new hybrid part. The same thing will happen if another transformation changes the same body part. For example, Say if Tyler spins chicken legs. Instead of replacing his goat legs, the chicken legs will become a hybrid of chicken legs and goat legs. If Tyler spins spider legs on his next spin, the goat and chicken hybrid legs will become a hybrid between goat, chicken and spider. Tyler’s remaining human parts are also vulnerable to this new rule. If he spins rabbit arms, instead of being replaced by rabbit arms, his human arms will become a anthro hybrid between human and rabbit arms. Keep in mind that any effect these body parts have on the overall body still applies. For instance, just like how his bull testicles increased his musclinality, any female parts like cow udders will increase Tyler’s femininity. But let’s make things more fun. Hybrid panels will now combine whatever body parts that falls under it. For example, say if Tyler spins a hybrid panel, then he spins crocodile head, the hybrid panel will randomly pick a body part for the change to be place. So if it lands on feet, Tyler will gain hybrid crocodile head feet. In addition, to composate for the new rules, a few replacement panels will be added to the wheel. A replacement panel will simply replace Tyler’s current body part with the one that get spin next. I don’t think I need a example for that. Lastly, I will add original body part panels along with other panels to give Tyler a sporting chance.”

    Madame was out of breath by the time she was done. She looked over to the audience and saw that they were more focused on Tyler finishing his transformation then listening to her speech. In a annoyed pout, Madame snapped her fingers and all the men who didn’t pay attention to her speech (which were almost all of them) grew bunny ears. Of course, Madame’s magic prevents them from noticing their change until after they leave the tent, but by then, Madame and her tent will be long gone.

    Meanwhile, while Madame was ranting about her new rules, the audience were silently watching Tyler as he finishes the third phase of his hybrid transformation. His reptilian shark eyes began to feel like burning coals as they began to segment into multiple compound eyes and bulge out from his chameleon sockets. When it was over, Tyler’s headache went from mind splitting to mind breaking. To the audience, it looks like Tyler has Hammerhead Shark eye sockets with Chameleon eye sockets on the ends, with bulging crystalline compound house fly eyes on the ends of the chameleon eye sockets. What was a little unsettling was that reptile eye slits can be seen on every eye segment.

    Once the transformation was done, Madame’s magic kicked in and reorganized Tyler’s brain so it can handle the compound chameleon eyes. Tyler can now make in detail what his body and the surrounding room looks like. He can even see the goat horns on his head in detail. But before Tyler could examine his body, Madame spoke.

    “Now that you are out of your mind numbing stuper, let me repeat myself Tyler.”

    Tyler didn’t need to move his head or eyes to see Madame grinning at him like a raptor.

    “You now have a chance to win back your original body if you can spin the normal panels. The normal panels will transform you back to normal one body part at a time. The Original Body Panel will transform you completely back to normal. If you can transform yourself completely back to a man, I will let you go.” Madame stated.

    ‘Football Scholarship, here I come!’ Tyler thought happily.

    With renewed vigor, Tyler trotted over to the wheel with hope in his inhuman eyes. With a burst of strength, Tyler spun the wheel willingly.
