    Unable to decide what to do with Mary, Jess thought of things she could do to herself instead. She stroked her furry body, and felt she wanted to stay this way. She didn't want to stay this size though. Heading back to the vials, she removed the shrink and growth ones. She didn't really want to shrink, else she couldn't have fun with Mary later. Slipping it back, she poured a good half of the growth vial into her mouth and swollowed hard. She walked back over to Mary, wanting to taunt her a bit. The changes were already takeing place though, and Jess found herself growing to fast to get out of the house in time.

    The cage mary was in quickly got pushed aside, as Jess expanded rapidly, and already filled the room. Soon the cieling gave out an aweful crack, as Jess pushed herself into the upstairs. Soon she had smashed walls, and was taking up most of the house. It was only a few more seconds before she exploded through the roof with and almighty BOOM!

    Standing shakily, Jess opened her eyes and looked around. The growth slowed to a stop, and the Coyote-girl saw she was almost eighty feet tall. She looked around the reminent of Mary's house for her cage. It was dificult as it was so small, but once she had found it she lifted it to her face. Baring her giant teeth, she began to laugh. Mary's tiny ears were on the verge of bleeding from such a loud noise. People in the neighborhood had started to panic, and began running for cover in storm shelters or basements.

    "Now I am a Coyote GOD!" she boomed.
