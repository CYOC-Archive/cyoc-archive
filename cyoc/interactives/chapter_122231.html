Then, he began to grow. Seemingly in unison every part of his torso began to beef up. chest and arms thickened, rounding out further, and his belly began filling out as he put on pounds and pounds of muscle and fat within seconds.

    The young man stumbled out of his workout gear and headed to the scale to watch his weight increase. His legs swelling thicker and more muscular with each step. By the time he was able to weigh himself he was already up to 250 pounds and climbing fast. His watched in awe as the numbers rapidly increased and each muscular curve of his body pushed out further and further. Soon it was 270, then 280, then 290... and he began to have difficulty seeing the numbers on the scale beyond the two curved of his chest and his newly formed belly, which was growing just as fast as the rest of him.

    He looked into the mirror in front of him to gawk at the changes happening to him. 320 lbs and swelling, and looking already like a massive and powerful man. He readjusted his stance as his thick thighs began pressing against each other, the width of his neck now exceeded the width of his head. Soon his frame was extending beyond the limit of the narrow mirror. When he looked down he could see were his pecs and a big round belly now, but in his refection showed just how big he was growing.

    380...390...400...410.

    His whole body was growing impossibly bulked up. His arms growing so thick that bending them completely was now impossible, his traps so massive that they raised up nearly to his ears. He no longer could see the readout of the scale, but if he could have, he would have seen ERR in big red letters. He had exceeded the 450 pound weight limit for the scale, and his body looked it. As he past 500 pounds his whole frame took on the look of meaty, powerful spherical mass. His pecs rounded out to an extreme, his shoulders, his legs and his titanic, beach ball sized belly. His back was now was thick, and his belly so massive that his arms now lay far out to his side even in resting position.

    As the behemoth continued to swell larger and larger, he wondered if he would be able to move. He experimentally shifted his 650 pound bulk off the scale, crushing it. The sensation of walking was now forever altered. His extraordinary thighs now competed for space, pushing his legs apart and making his motions slow and heavy, like the massive beast he had become. As he grew beyond 700 pounds the quarterback worried about his mobility. He could feel his traps now pushing up against his head, limiting his ability to turn his head. His arms were pushed out nearly totally to his sides. He felt his shoulders pushing up against the cold metal of the lockers, despite his distance. He couldn't see the limits of his body, he could only feel his massive frame bumping up against things which should have been much farther away. All the while each curve of his overblown body grew more extreme.

    His titanic bulk swayed as his heavy steps pushed him back to the chronovac. He was now settling in at his final 860 pounds. He surveyed his body the best he could, running his hand over the insanely bulked out mass. He was unsettled by how constricted his motions felt. His arms had grown so incredibly thick that he lost a great deal of flexibility. He stomped slowly back toward the chronovac, wondering if he could make all this mass more manageable when...
