    Jeff is still sitting in front of his Laptop, still trying to decide of what to do, when a knock on the front door causes him to groan, “why is it, that every time I’m in the middle of something important, that I get bothered,” he grumbles.

    Being the only one home, Jeff sighs and gets to his feet, heading out his room and down the stairs to answer the door.

    When Jeff opens the door, the teen’s expression turns from annoyed to surprised, “Tyrone? What are you doing here,” he asks, “this is a surprise, come in,” he then steps aside to let his friend in.

    Tyrone smiles, “well, we haven’t seen each other in a while, so I decided to just drop by to say hello,” he says. What Jeff doesn’t notice is that Tyrone is holding his phone, unaware to Jeff, Tyrone activates the scanner of the App to scan his friend.

    Jeff smiles himself, “yeah, we do,” he says, “would you like something to drink?”

    “Please, could I have a Soda?”

    “Sure, why don’t you head to my room,” Jeff says, “I’ll be up in a few minutes”.

    “Sounds good,” Tyrone says and stars climbing the stairs and soon enters Jeff’s room, grinning to himself, the teen sits down on Jeff’s bed, not noticing the Chronivac running on Jeff’s Laptop, and looks down at his phone.

    Tyrone opens the transformation option and selects the option “Island”, grinning evilly, Tyrone then starts setting up the transformation:

    Size of Island: 270.271 square miles
    Location of Island: Caribian Sea
    Rip Cage of target: Mountains
    Blood of target: Magma and Groundwater
    Hair of target: Vegitation (Jungle, Grass, Bushes,…)
    Cock of target: Jetty, dock and small harbour
    Skin of target: sand and soil
    Mouth of target: inactive volcano
    Nose of target: caves in mountains
    Eyes of target: security cameras which are placed and installed all over the island

    “That should do it,” Tyrone mumbles to himself, then he hits confirm and waits to see what would happen. Tyrone has left Jeff’s awareness on, but he hasn’t activated the reality alteration, which means that Jeff still exists, but no one has an idea where he is. The teen smirks to himself, “while my island is creating itself,” Tyrone says, “I could start to get the other stuff I want and need: I need a yacht, helicopter, jet, mansion, new phone,… so much work, but in the end: I’ll be the richest person on this planet!”
