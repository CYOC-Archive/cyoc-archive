    It was your average Friday afternoon. People are walking around and getting lunch. For Harry, well, he’s getting something to take a trip back to childhood.

    Harry walked into the store. He didn’t see a worker, so he decided to look around. Most of the things he found were either meant for babies, toddlers, and kids with bedwetting problems.

    “Looking for something specific sir?” A woman asked behind him. He didn’t hear her coming, so it gave him quite a shock.

    “Yea uh, do you have anything for someone like me?”

    “Oh yes we have a wide range of diapers for those with incontinence! Please follow me!”

    Harry started following her, but tried to explain his dilemma.

    “I’m not incontinence! I just want to enjoy childhood again. Do you have anything temporary I can use?”

    “Oh, why didn’t you say so? It’s in the same section as incontinence diapers.” She led Harry to the back room, where everything looked like an adult baby paradise.

    Diapers and adult baby clothes on shelves. Toys designed for ‘big kids’ in boxes. Changing powder, lotion, baby bottles. They had it all.

    Harry was led to an aisle filled with underwear, pull-ups, and diapers.

    The woman grabbed a box of diapers, “These are our oldest brand. They’re like diapers, but they also change you into an infant between the ages of newborn to 2. If diaper changes aren’t you’re thing, you don’t have to worry. These change themselves.”

    Harry looked at the box. Being that young sounded cool, but he had no one to take care of him. Maybe he could call a friend to babysit.

    “Right here are our pull-ups for mommy’s big boys. Unlike the diapers, you have a better range of age here. Ages 2 to 6. Sadly, theses don’t automatically change into a clean pull-up, but the box does restock itself every 24 hours if empty.”

    Pull-ups were better for Harry if he wanted to play around, and ignore going to the bathroom. But again, he needed an adult to supervise him. Now that he thought about it, he needed an adult for all of these.

    “And last but not least, our own brand of underoos. These give you the age range of anyone between the ages of 4 through 12. Slip them on and you become a child again. I forgot to mention that any clothes you’re wearing with the underwear and diapers will change with you. So if you have unwanted clothes, just make them into their toddler version.”

    Between 4 and 12. Harry could work with that. The only problem was bathroom breaks. Choosing was such a problem.

    “I understand these options are too much for some people, so take you’re time. And if you’d like, I’ll allow you to test run some of these.”
