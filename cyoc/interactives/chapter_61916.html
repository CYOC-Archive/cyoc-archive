    Tom:Uh, mom, dad, you guys love me, right?

    T Dad:Of couse.

    T Mom:Yes, dear. We'll always love you no matter what.

    Tom:Even if I did something without asking you?

    T Dad:What are you trying to say, sport?

    Tom:Earlier today I got some drinks from a nice bunny woman. I drank one, and I gave you the other two. We were humans before, but the drinks turned us into our inner toons. I remember because I'm the one who got them, but you two don't remember being human. She gave me these pills before I left - she said they'd bring your human memories back. I'm really sorry. You two were being so stiff - I wanted us all to have fun, but that's no excuse for being selfish and going behing your backs - I should've asked you two first.

    His mom and dad looked at eachother, and then looked at him. Surprisingly, they weren't mad, but understanding.

    T Dad:Boy, we must've been pretty big sticks-in-the-mud if you were willing to go that far.

    T Mom:We don't blame you one bit, dear. You don't need to give us those pills.

    Tom:But I want to.

    They each took a pill.

    T Dad:I'd rather not remember being a boring human.

    T Mom:But if it will make you feel better, then we'll do it, dear.

    They both swallowed their pills, and in an instant, remembered everything. They looked at eachother for a bit, then they looked at themselves, and then they looked at Tom. Tom braced himself for punishment, but his goat mom picked him up, hugged him warmly, and gave him a big, wet, toony kiss on his cheek.

    Tom:I don't understand - you're not mad?

    T Mom:Only a little, but we're so proud of you for telling the truth.

    T Dad:Yep - that took REAL guts, Tom.

    Tom:You're not gonna punish me, are you?

    His mom had a very serious look on her face.

    T Mom:Yes, Tom. For the rest of this vaction, we want you to...

    Her face broke out into a goofy toon smile.

    T Mom:...have as much fun with us as possible!

    She then gave him another big, wet, toony kiss on his cheeck, and gently set him back down.

    Tom:So, were're cool, rigtht?

    T Dad:Sure. Sorry if I blew my top back there - I over-reacted.

    T Mom:I'll admit, I like myself much better this way, and you make such a cute toon mouse, and your Dad's a really handsome toon badger - I'm glad he's my husband!

    At this, Tom's dad smiled, and blushed.

    T Dad:Uh, Tom, not that I'm in any hurry to get back to normal, but is this permanent? Are we stuck like this?
