    "Hey, uh, Braiden?" asked the young athletic man who entered his bedroom, "Not too early, I -uh, whoa! Is that the horse suit I'm going to wear?"

    "Nah, you're right on time, and yes, that's the suit. You want to try it on now? See how it fits, and get used to it?" Braiden asked with a grin.

    The big jock picked up Callum and began disassembling him. He tried the head on.

    Callum felt the big blond guy's head inside him. He felt the hot breath. Who was this guy? Why did Braiden have him turn into a pony suit for someone else to wear. He was relieved that he had made himself with adjustable straps. He heard the guy's thoughts in his mind. The guy wanted to be a pony slave? Callum could read the guy like a bunch of youtube videos. They weren't necessarily in the right order. One was a memory of the guy when he was like 5 years old playing cowboys with his buddies, but one of the littler kids was riding him. He was the pony. "Braiden" thought Callum prompting a more recent memory.

    It was cut short by Braiden taking the head off of the guy who was named Aubrey, but whose team mates called Chet from his middle name. Chet did seem like a good name for a horse. Callum was kind of excited to be worn.

    "Put the head on last, and I think you'll need to be naked, if the suit is to fit properly. The horsetail will give you a massive wedgie otherwise," Braiden advised. "I'll give you some privacy."

    Braiden carried Callum's head out of the room, and Chet proceeded to strip, and begin to strap himself into the black leather suit. It was a tight fit.

    Callum's head though disembodied could feel the older guy's muscular body inside his stretching formerly hollow leather one. It was sort of like a rapid burst of puberty, Callum thought, but he wasn't sure. He couldn't hear the guy, only feel his body. He could smell Braiden's hand which was cradling his head. He must've been eating peanut butter or something with peanut butter earlier.

    "Thanks, Callum, without you, I wouldn't have Chet," Braiden said as he ran his fingers through Callum's mane.
