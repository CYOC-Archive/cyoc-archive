    "I, HAVE HAD, ENOUGH!!!!!"

    The two Lisas and Gloria watched as a now very angry young girl began to glow. Before anyone could say anything there was a brilliant flash of light that knocked the three back.

    "Mom you okay," Gloria said quickly as she saw Lisa steady herself.

    "Yeah I'm fine," Lisa nodded, bracing herself against the wall. As the two looked over at Lynne they were shocked by what they saw.

    Lynne was her 14 year old self once more. Somehow the girl had broken the spell Gloria had cast. Gloria shrank back slightly as she looked at her little sister.

    Lynne's eyes seemed to be glowing a sickly white as her hair flew around her. It seemed as if a mighty wind was blowing around Lynne now causing her hair to fly around her, as she angrily gazed at her family.

    "Lynne what are you doing," the future Lisa asked, shocked by her daughters power. "H..how did you age yourself back to 14?"

    Despite Lynne's rage, she would respect her mothers wishes and not reveal the future. She would however caredully choose her words so that her mother understood.

    "Guees you forgot mother," she growled. "But that distant relative of ours placed a charm on me when I was born. I can break the rings enchantment if I choose."

    While Gloria and present Lisa did not understand what Lynne was saying, future Lisa knew all too well. "Kellie," she thought as she gazed at the furious girl.

    "Lynne," Gloria said meekly. "M..maybe you should calm down."

    Lynne whipped her head around glaring at Gloria with a ferocity she didn't think possible.

    "You," she growled in a low threatening tone. "You..you told my mother that this was my fault. It doesn't matter that I told your friend I wanted to be left alone and she kept bothering me. No....no you and your friends wanted to play the victim. EVEN THOUGH SHE PUNCHED ME IN THE FACE!!!!!"

    Gloria shrank back as the two Lisas turned between Lynne and Gloria. "Wait you told Sasha you wanted to be left alone," the present Lisa asked.

    "Thats not what you told me before I got here," Future Lisa added as she frowned at Gloria.

    Gloria felt herself go pale as she stared between her mother, the other Lisa and a very scary looking Lynne. "Well yes," she admitted. "But why am I being held accountable for what I wrote in my diary. I'm not the one who told you, future Gloria was."

    "And I'll ask her about that when I get home," Future Lisa frowned. "Dont get me wrong I'm stiil upset about Mr. Harris and the principal but if Lynne wanted to be left alone you should of respected her wishes."

    "Would it have really mattered," Lynne roared, her voice shaking the house. "Youve ALWAYS taken Glorias side. ALWAYS. You never wanted to believe anything I said. It was always her."

    Gloria slowly began to back away from Lynne, fearful of what the girl could do. As she did Future Lisa stepped forward, she had never seen her daughter display this much power or rage.

    "Lynne you know thats not true," Lisa tried to soothe. "I love you. Your my daughter. I want whats best for you."

    "As long as I fit HER mold," Lynne raged. "You never excepted the fact that I'M NOT HER!!!"

    The two Lisas exchamged worrisome glances as they cautiously looked at Lynne. Neither woman knew how to handle this, or what the girl could do in this state. Before they could act they could see Lynne's hair return to normal as her eyes became normal once more. Taking a deep sigh the girl now looked at her family.

    "You know," she sighed heavily. "The last thing I wanted to do was lose my temper with you."

    As the girl sat down on the couch she could see her family slowly approach. "Stop," she moaned. "Just...stop...please."

    Lynnes family complied as she wearily runned her eyes. "Look," she groaned. "I'll take the wrap for Mr. Harris. I'll even take the wrap for the principal. But....there is no way in hell I'm apologizing to Sasha. She wouldnt leave me alone AND she hit me."

    "T..thats fair," Lisa stammered, still frightened by what she had seen.

    Lynne now glared over at Gloria, feelimgs of hurt amd betrayal now clouded her feelings for the sister she once idolized. "As far as you and me go," she frowned. "We're done. We're sisters in name and name alone. You stay with your friends, Ill make my own."

    Gloria could not even offer a response she watched Lynne slowly get off the couch. "Mom..I mean young mom," she sighed. "I'm sorry Ive been a pain in the ass. I know I'm a handful, and...i probably have ruined things for you and dad. I'll....i'll see if I can find some place else to stay."

    As the now tired girl slowly made her way upstairs her family stood in stunned silence, still trying to wrap thwir minds around the power they had just seen.
