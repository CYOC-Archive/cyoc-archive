    Younger, But Not Too Young!

    Miranda, ignorant to the power at her fingertips, decided to change the sentence to “This 55 year old female is in the bathroom.” Not overly young, but old enough she could still pass more as a miss and attractive, not ma’am, old, and worn out.

    She started to feel a shiver down her spine, like the winds of change were . This stupid “app” was clearly a joke, but it wasn’t like she could actually get younger, right? After all, who would give anyone that kind of power, much less leave it somewhere like a bathroom?

    Her back straightened up more, showing her change back from eighty back through her seventies. A few wrinkles disappeared, making her face just a little bit less weathered. Maybe her glasses were smudged, but it looked like she was getting younger. But that’s impossible! A little burst of energy didn’t mean she was turning back time; it was just a trick of the light.

    Soon, her seventies were gone, leaving her whittling through her sixties. The old woman’s hair started to get longer, a few streaks of black shining through and spreading. More wrinkles and the crow’s feet that once adorned her were continuing to vanish out of thin air, leaving a more youthful look. Eyes once covered in cataracts were growing clearer, leaving her reading glasses as more as extra help, rather than a necessities for her day to day routine!

    Once sixty was done, the process continued, her hair mostly black with a few grey hairs now. Her once sad and deflated breasts rose and filled back to a perky DD, matching her now fixed posture and energetic body. The formerly elderly broad couldn’t believe this… she was truly getting younger! Lost height was getting restored, a younger yet mature look remained at a proud 55 years old!

    This certainly wasn’t a dream, and if it was, Miranda didn’t want to wake up. Truly a miracle or a blessing that she found this phone do-hickey! Aches and pains were gone as if they were a nightmare, far away from her current predicament! Sure, 55 wasn’t perfect, but it was loads better than an old and decrepit 80 year old woman!

    She did a small jump for joy, surprised that her clothes, once an old simple floral pattern dress, was now a red button up blouse with blue jeans to compliment it! Orthopedic tennis shoes were swapped for a more classy pair of strapped high heels, giving Miranda a sense of power!

    The question was…. What to do with this power?
