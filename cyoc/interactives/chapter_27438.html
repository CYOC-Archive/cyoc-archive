    The newscaster's voice continued as the astronauts listened, stunned.

    "My next guest is psychologist Dr. Ngozi Cavendish, who has been studying the psychology of the new women and has some startling findings. Dr. Cavendish?

    "Thank you. As you know, both born women and new women have exclusively lesbian sexual preferences, whatever their orientation before the change. Sexually, no one misses men."

    "I certainly don't! And can I just say you look lovely today?"

    "Thank you! The interesting discovery, though, is that the sexuality of the new women has been altered in other ways as well. To put it bluntly, they're submissive--they sexually enjoy being dominated by born-women."

    "Really?"

    "No doubt. And there's another aspect to it as well. For example, before the change did you have a hard driving, alpha male type executive working here? Maybe a bit of a bottom pincher?"

    "A few!"

    "And what are they now?"

    "Well, the former head of the news division is now the assistant tea lady and pleasure girl."

    "And I'm sure she does a wonderful job of it! Essentially, we've discovered that the more stereotypically male a man was before the change, the more submissive she is now. Could you bring her in and I'll demonstrate?"

    "Certainly! Becky" the newscaster raised her voice "Come in here right now!".

    "Ah, hello Becky. How are you enjoying your new form?"

    A new voice joined in. "I just want born-women to be pleased with me, ma'am."

    "For those of you listening to the radio." The original announcer broke in "Becky is dressed in a very revealing and sexy outfit that shows off all her wonderful new assets."

    "Yes." Dr. Cavendish responded. "And our studies show that that's how new women like Becky, macho men before the change, like to dress. But Becky, I don't think you're actually revealing enough. How would you like to perform your job naked, so born-women can get a good look at all of you?"

    "That would be wonderful!"

    "Then go ahead."

    "Becky is taking her clothes off now. And I must say she looks scrumptious!"

    "Now Becky, I understand you were quite the sexist before the change."

    "That's all behind me now, ma'am."

    "Speaking of behinds, yours is quite lovely!"

    "Thank you ma'am."

    "And" Dr. Cavendish continued "Here's where it gets interesting. Just as the new women have had their psychologies altered to become submissive, like the lovely Becky here, so the born-women have acquired dominant and in some cases slightly sadistic personalities. Which is why I brought this paddle. You know what it's for, Becky. You seem quite excited!"

    "Oh ma'am, are you going to paddle me with that?"

    "I certainly am. Now spread yourself over my knee!"

    The next few minutes were filled with the rhythmic slap of a paddle on a well-cushioned butt and Becky's moans and screams of pleasure.
