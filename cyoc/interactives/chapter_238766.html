    For short, monster TF device, was a smartphone-like machine that seemed to be made for people that loved monsters and freaks, being able to add multiple body parts such as extra eyes, arms, heads or genitals, decreasing or increasing their size, and put animal or plant parts in their monstrous bodies like tentacles, claws, wings, vines, flowers and a infinity etc. The possibilities were endless and the limit is your imagination.

    To use it was simple, as the device only counted with a touch screen the user could select the changes they would like to do like the previous told examples, if they didn’t want to think too much about the customization of their monster form the device had some monster presets saved like lamias, demons, centaurs and almost any mythical creature you could think of, but you could also select some of the presets and modify them as you wish.

    There were three other additional important features. One being the duration of the transformation which had 4 options: temporal (you had to set the time), permanent, transform at will (where the selected target of the transformation could switch between their human and monster form) or condition (where you set the condition(s) that have to be met in order to transform, like touching water or a full moon appearing for example). The second was fusion, where if this option was checked two or more targets could merge to transform into the specified monster. The other and most important feature was the abilities, the user could decide the powers of the monster form such as creating fire, go through walls, mind control, laying eggs and an infinite etcetera (more than one ability can be chosen).

    After setting all their changes and preferences the user just needed to select their target, which could be themselves or anyone in a radius of 15 foot then press the TRANSFORM button and let the changes happen.

    After some time the device gained some dust and cobwebs appeared around it, until one day a client decided to buy it.
