    his mom stood and wiped off Elleena's mouth. Jamal once again sees another act of favoritism between him and his sister. Deena was always being babied like this for her to end up a spoiled brat, but Jamal wondered how would Elleena react to this. More importantly how did she fit 2/3 of the soup in the serving bowl!

    "Stop it mom!! that's embarrassing" Elleena blushed red as she held off her mother's attempt on wiping her mouth, taking the tissue off her mother's hands and wiped herself off.

    Her mom looked disappointed for a moment as she sat back on her seat. She wanted to pamper her sweet little girl even though, she was already in college. 'being a mother doesn't stop when her children go to high school', or in this case 'college' as she would say, although she's favouring one of her children over the other.

    Jamal reached for another serving of soup when his mom popped another question. "So how was school!? Did you get higher scores on your quizzes today!?"

    Jamal smiled as he announced his accomplishment. "It was great, I got B on all subjects, but English with B+!" Jamal was proud of what he had achieved. Spending all his time studying finally paid off. for him that always get Ds most of the time, it was a huge step up!

    "Look at your sister now. She got a scholarship for her college because she worked harder, than you do. Please Jamal grow up and put more effort in your studies!" his mom showed a face of concern, but all that Jamal could see was her cold eyes looking down on him.

"Elleena, Jamal both of you take a shower after you finish eating your meal!" she declared, as she drank water in the kitchen. "you two both smell like sweat, but before that Jamal wash the dishes for me."
