    As you get into the wolf costume you can't help but notice how odd and skin tight it feels.
    Pulling it up around your body it feels like part of your own body. Almost like a second...
    skin... Oh no!

    Turning around you see Jack pouring a potion over himself. The seams appear on his suit
    as the potion works it's way through his fur. He chuckles, "Actually, you seem to be the
    one who's stuck as a wolf it would seem." He grins, removing the mask off. "You spilled
    the potion to the WOLF costume. THIS would be the bear potion." Jack starts to crawl out
    of his suit as you feel something strange happening to yourself.

    Your body starts to shimmer and shift. The fur digs into your skin and replaces your own.
    Your hands start to become claws and your feet into paws. Your entire bone structure is
    changing as the costume's tail merges with your spine. The second skin is a second no
    more, it's now your one and only skin, sans your still human head.

    Perhaps you spoke too soon. The now human Jack has snuck up behind you with the wolf
    mask and slipped it over your head. In no time flat it reshapes your skull, forcing your face
    into a long pointed muzzle.

    "ARF!" You shout in a panicked bark. "What the heck was that for!?"

    Jack chuckled, "Just a little bit of payback my good friend." He patted you on the back. "I
    can't believe you were just going to leave me as a bear!"

    "But but... How are you back to normal!? The potion was spilled!?" You shout confused.

    Jack picks up the bottle he just finished emptying. "This was the bear potion. You spilt
    the WOLF potion my friend." He lifted up the empty bottle. "Property of The Second Skin.
    It seems that we both shopped at the same place."

    "Wait a second... Arf!? So you're saying now I'M the one who's stuck!? Oh man..." You
    can't help but notice your ears folding against your head as you let out a high pitched
    canine whine.

    What do you do now?
