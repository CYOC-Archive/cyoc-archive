    Brad wasn't happy at all with the loose sexual ethics he had created.
    His first thought was to change Meiko's sex drive.
    He grabbed the slider and pulled it way down, he hesitated for a moment,
    as her father he wasn't comfortable with Ro-Meiko having
    sex at all, but pulling it down to 0% might cripple her mentally and
    take away her ability to enjoy sex forever. He decided that putting
    it on 2% was probably manageable, he liked the thought
    of her saving herself for her future husband.

    When he thought about it he didn't like the thought of his wife
    having sampled so many other man before him. He wondered
    what personality changes he could give her to make him feel a
    bit more secure in his dealings with her.

    He admitted to himself that his desire was to impose his own
    values on Tiffany. So he lowered her "Dominance Over
    Husband" to 20% so that she would be more inclined to bend
    to his will. He clicked around a bit until he found her "Relationships"
    tab and clicked on himself.

    Legal Relation: Husband
    Biological Relation: None
    Emotional relationship: Sexual/Romantic
    Emotional investment: 50%
    Binding agents: co-parent, sexually pleasing, routine
    Unbinding agents: sexually restricting, clashes with family

    Brad looked over the stats. He didn't really know to what standard
    of measurement Tiffany's Emotional Investment in their relationship
    was being held but it seemed to him she was considering leaving him.
    The Binding agents had to be the things keeping them together and
    the Unbinding agents the things driving them apart.
    He selected 'sexually restricting' and pressed 'delete', a warning popped
    up. THIS CHANGE WILL CHANGE THE SUBJECTS SEXUAL ORIENTATION
    FROM STRAIGHT TO "asexual with exception: exception(Brad Weir)" CONTINUE?
    "Yeah, that was the point" Brad murmured as he clicked "OK"

    The unbinding agent dissapeared and Emotional Investment climbed up to
    67%. Brad thought this would probably mean she had a 33% chance of leaving
    him. He selected the value and filled in "100%", another pop-up appeared;
    "PLEASE SELECT AND AGENT TO MODIFY" he could do that, he clicked
    'sexually pleasing' to make his sexual encounters with Tiffany important enough
    to her to that she would be entirely emotionally invested in their marriage.
    "WARNING! SUBJECTS SEXUAL DRIVE IS AT FULL CAPACITY. INCREASE
    TO EXTRAHUMAN LEVELS?"

    He wondered, would he make his wife, who he had ensured could only be
    attracted to him, unnaturally lustful? Maybe it would be better to increase
    her investment in raising their children together with him. Was the stat even
    really how much she wanted to stay with him? Or was it how much her relationship
    with him affected her emotionally? His mouse hovered between the "ACCEPT" and
    "CANCEL" button.
