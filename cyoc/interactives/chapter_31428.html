    No! I can't do this! I need to become a guy again!
    Ada thought to herself. She was resisting the urges to get it on with Calli for fear of her personality changing for forever. But it wouldn't matter if the coin landed on tails.
    C’mon coin, don’t screw up now! I wish I was a guy!
    She threw the coin in the air. It rose up and then fell down; spinning as it hit the ground. After what seemed like an eternity, it stopped and showed its result:

    Tails.

    No! Ada screamed in her mind as the resulting wish backfired. Not only was she not going to be a guy again, but now she was a girl for forever. What was left of her male mind was trapped in a female body and was slowly be consumed by female influence.

    “Ada, now we can make sweet love.”

    Ada sank to the floor in horror.
