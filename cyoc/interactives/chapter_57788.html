    Glendale had become a place with the magic of an entire planet condensed into a single
    population center, powerful enough to rewrite reality itself by a simple inverse triggering
    of the basic law of cause and effect. And for those whose families had spent generations
    there, that magic had became an inescapable part of their very being.

    As Marie-Elize had said, those for whom the magic had integrated into themselves: their
    magic now existed independently of the game. This included the Leoman cascade, Penny
    and Amy's gift, Alice and her half-sisters/cousins, the down right -frightening- amount of
    power Marie-Eliza now contained, and of course the magic that ran through the Omegas of
    the pack.

    The Omega's children's genes did not carry their parents' mutation, but if the children's -
    magical- heredity resulted in a mutation similar to their parents, the curse would have
    nothing to do with it. And while DNA testing could pick up traits handed down via DNA,
    magical heredity was not something geneticists knew what to look for or if it could even BE
    detected that way.

    If Cansandra, Lilly, Laura, Zulema, Giselle, and Gretchen mutated due to the magic that
    came from their parents, continuing the line of Shiva wolves started centuries ago, the
    game's continued or discontinued existence would not play a role.

    As for Jay, Pence, Toni and Paula, they were abandoned pups, and were Anne's adopted
    children and not part of the Shiva line. Their major concern would be when Anne's
    pregnancy became impossible to deny and they'd wonder if their mother had tired of
    having pups not related to her by blood and Anne having to carefully explain to her
    children that family was NOT just about blood.
