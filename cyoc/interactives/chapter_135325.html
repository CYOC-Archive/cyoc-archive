    added by Bear 5 years ago A BM O Equine Reality alteration

    Jeff starts to write down the changes...

    Targets Name: Rosie

    DNA(s) being added:

    Percent ratio: 50%

    Morphing duration: 60 seconds

    Pleasurable: N

    Alter Reality: Y

    Aware: Y

    ...yet he didn't know what animal to change Rosie into. Cat? No. Dog? No. Lizard? No. He wanted to find an animal that would be so embarrassing for Rosie would change into but nothing would fit. Camel? No. Horse? No. Donkey...? "Donkey!" he thought "That's perfect!".
    He begin to add donkey into the DNA added option and immediately hit enter. All he had to do now was sit and wait.

    Rosie started brushing her hair, getting ready for whenever she wants to go out with her friends, when she noticed that her ear was feeling strange, like there was something brushing against it. She soon knew the answer why, as grey fur started sprouting around her ears, and her ears were stretching like toffee, reaching the top of her head. The fur started to spread around her entire body, as her knees bend inwards. Rosie grits and grinds her teeth, the sharp pains almost being too much to handle. Her feet became stiff, as they turned black and her nails surrounded her feet, turning them into hooves. Her hair was turning into a darker shade of grey to contrast the lighter colour of her new fur. Her teeth turned slightly thick, more buck-teeth-like in appearance. The quick pull of her spine extending into a tail made her bray in shock. She covered her mouth, noticing that her arms were covered in fur and her nails were darkening to match her new hooves. Her jaw was stretching into a small muzzle, lengthening her nose, flaring her nostrils.

    She finally looked at herself. She looked like a donkey. Her feet were hooves, she now had a tail, as well as donkey ears. Her eyes widened. She couldn't believe it. To test if she really had a tail, her hand slowly reached behind her, grabbed and pulled her tail. The amount of pain that she had, when she pulled her tail, made her bray in pain. She really started to panic. How would she explain this to her friends? Or worse, her parents? "MOM!" she yelled "COME HERE, PLEASE!". After a short while, her mother entered her room. "You know you didn't have to yell" Lauren, her mother, said. "LOOK AT ME! WHAT'S HAPPENED TO ME?!" Rosie shouted to her mother. While she didn't appreciate the yelling, she did see what was wrong. She looked at her up and down to see what was wrong, and in the end, she didn't know, so she decided to end the conversation quickly without accidentally hurting her feelings. "It looks like you're getting taller, wow! Maybe now you'll be taller than Jeff, huh? Catch-ya Rosie".
    Rosie couldn't believe her own mother didn't know that she changed into a donkey. "NO, NO, NO, LOOK AT ME! I'VE CHANGED". "I know, you've grown" Lauren responded. "NO, LOOK, I LOOK LIKE A DONKEY!" Rosie screamed. "I know, and you still are the cutest little donkey I've met" Lauren responded, grabbing her furry cheek and shaking it softly. Rosie didn't say anything. She just saw her mother walk away like it was normal for her to be a donkey person. She went back into her room, trying to find anything to prove her mother wrong. Her clothes, that will change her mind, until she saw every piece of clothing accommodate her new donkey body, having holes where her tail would fit. She started to look elsewhere, until she thought of the perfect evidence that she can show her mother to prove that she had changed.

    The family album.

    She found it lying on the coffee table. She grabbed it and- She couldn't believe it.

    Every photo was changed. Every photo since the beginning of her life had changed. She flipped through the pages, that couldn't happen to all the photos. Flipped a page. Photos changed. Flipped another. Photos changed. Another. Changed. Another. Changed. She still flipped through the book, again and again and again, all the photos shown her in her new donkey body. She started to weep. Her whole family thought that she looked like a donkey. Maybe not even the whole family, maybe the whole world.

    Jeff was satisfied. He thought of what he could do next with the Chronivac...
