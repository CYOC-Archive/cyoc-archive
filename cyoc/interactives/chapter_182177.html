    "What?!" Philip Shouted "No! CANCEL! CAN-"

    Before Philip manage to think, he was sucked through a black hole. As he flew through,
    he didn't feel the surge, that he had started to feel when he was sucked in the hole.
    And gradually everything went black. Philip had passed out.
    Before passing out, Philip sensed he was changing, he had been waving his arms frantically
    to try stop, grab, hold onto.. something whatever to reduce the suction further down into the black. He sensed he couldn't any longer. The arms formed a circle.
    like he is holding on to something. And his arms seems locked in this position. Now Philip had passed out.

    In the transformation process Philips human form started to change,Philip was becoming a white fabric. All white except from right below the previous elbow, there an emblem at sorts started to take form. It looked like an V at sorts.

    What wasn't mentioned. Was that Transdem labs had made a lucrative deal with Amazon, in a scheme to lower purchase cost, for items the customers had purchased, Transdem would provide job seekers through the Chronivac, where Trandsem would cover wages, while Amazon got to deliver premium goods to the customers. Instant guaranteeing customer satisfaction.

    During the transit through the black hole everything went superfast, Philip was matched up with the endless amount of order no. Amazon processes at any given time to find a match.
    Finally when Philip had reached through the black hole, he had been matched with order. no
    71946732, and were already at the amazon sorting facility. Ready to be packed and shipped.

    Philip was just about to wake up, he didn’t get to see much, as when he opened his eyes, he could see there were light. He tried to say something, but he couldn't say or make any sound at all. This frightened him quite much. What happens now.? If one could see that Philip had a vision, one could have seen it on his eyes, if only one knew where to look. But honestly. Who would look for some eyes at a random White Nike pair of socks, when socks in general have no eyes at all. The whole process took about 20 seconds before it remained pitch black again. Philip was now on his way, to whomever had ordered something as commonly as socks.
