Occasionally materializing some popcorn or the meatloaf their mother used to make. Sometimes they laughed, sometimes Julia had a lot of questions for Cynthia, and other times they were just left to think or just chat on occasion. The two were stretching while the monitor was displaying Cynthia washing her pregnant self in the shower. Cynthia said "I've been having an odd thought... Aren't we kinda like sisters in a way?"

    "Well, we're like Twins. Been together for basically all our early and a lot of our adult lives, inseparable, and we have a lot in common."
    "Yeah... I remember about you talking about Joe comparing me to coffee... Maybe that rubbed off on you."
    "Maybe... still though, assuming our relationship with Joe doesn't make things too complicated. Wouldn't you rather be friends? Then since we basically share the same memories of childhood, school, and whatnot... I don't know just think about it ok?"
