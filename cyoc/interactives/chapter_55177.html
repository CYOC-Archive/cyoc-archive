    It all began when one of the boys dropped a bag; his friends stopped
    as he went to pick it up, and Harry felt that it was the appropriate
    time to strike. He casually strolled by the leaning boy and gently
    dragged the stick across his arm. The unfortunate boy had bent over,
    intending to retrieve his bag. He was just about to grab it and began
    to shift his balance, however his arm was suddenly shorter, and the
    bag wasn't in his hand.

    His friend only kept staring as the boy suddenly felt the cold
    concrete on his legs. He looked down only to find a long black skirt
    covering his much thinner legs. His sneakers had become girly slip-on
    shoes, and thin silk socks reached far up his exposed legs.

    "Where did all of this come from?" He shouted as he stood up, but only
    more shocking discoveries were to come. Harry watched as the girl
    stood up and looked down at her body in disbelief. She stood much
    shorter than the boy who was just sitting where she had been and had
    an abundant amount of orange hair atop her head. As she tried to look
    down at herself, she found her vision obstructed. She brought her
    hands to the strange mounds on her chest, and released a gasp upon
    touching them.

    "No way this is real!"

    She then lifted her skirt, revealing her striped panties, and felt her
    body.

    "What happened? This isn't my body!"

    Harry laughed at the boy's new voice.
    The other boy noticed the laughter and turned to face Harry.

    "Hey, what are you doing? Did you cause this? Stop laughing and get
    over here."

    The boy came over and grabbed Harry's shirt. He was quite large and
    strong, with rather thick arms, and a serious looking face. Harry knew
    that he could fix all of that however. With a tap of the stick, Harry
    found that he was being grabbed by a thin brown haired girl in
    pajamas. She had her hair in two buns and stood barefoot on the
    ground, in nothing but her yellow night pants and thin pink dotted
    white top.

    She noticed that something was wrong when she found herself look down
    at Harry's chest, when before she had been looking down at his face.
    She looked up and saw the smile on Harry's much bigger face. She let
    go of Harry and stepped back, realizing that something had happened.
    In fear, she looked down at herself, and saw her petite body wrapped
    in girl's bed clothes. Harry then grabbed her gown and playfully shook
    her around.

    "You sure act tough for someone so cute."

    The girl became more frightened and swung a fist at Harry. It had
    little effect, as the girl was used to much different arms. Harry just
    wrapped his arm around the girl and pressed her into him.

    "Have fun girls!" Harry shouted. He didn't feel an ounce of regret as
    he turned right back towards the main street.
