    built, tan and his hair was blonde but Josh recognized his friend in there. And he wasn’t happy at all. Alex
    just stood there looking dumbly at his old friend.

    “Alex what happened to you?!” He grabbed his friends now broad shoulders and shook him. “What did they do to
    you? You look like a dumb surfer!”

    Alex could barely remember this guy. But he was getting kinda angry at being yelled at and shaken and didn’t
    like being called ‘dumb.’ He felt a kind of electricity building in him. Like a static charge.

    “Dude like fuck off im not dumb, and uh I wanna go to the beach and stuff so move.”

    “Alex come on snap out it you hate surfers remember they’re dumb! You shouldn’t be dressed like this or

    yelling thought Alex. I don’t hack stuff. Alex finally pushed the kid off and as he did a bolt of
    electricity shot out from him and into the other boy. Josh stumbled back and started to feel weird. He
    clasped his head with his hands as he got dizzy and it felt like something was sucking all the intelligence
    out of him. Alex watched with curiosity as Josh stumbled around the room.

    “No, no no! I don’t wanna be dumb to!” Josh cried out realizing whatever happened to Alex had spread to him.
    He wanted to run away and escape but part of him was calling him to the mirror. The surfer part of him really
    wanted to watch himself. He saw his eyes, bright blue and almost retarded looking. They no longer glowed
    with intelligence but they looked a lot hotter part of him argued.

    “Alex cut it out I don’t wanna join you!”

    “Uh dude youre getting totally awesome!” Alex had no idea what was going on but he was amazed watching Josh’s
    greasy hair get shaggy and blonde as it fell past his shoulders and smelled strongly of salt water. His ugly
    face changed with high cheek bones, a killer smile and a smaller nose. Some teenage hairs grew in on his lip

    Josh pulled at his clothes as his shirt vanished altogether. His chest grew like Alex’s but clearly much more

    “Fuck yeah dude can we like surf now?” asked the new Josh.

    Alex gave a big dumb smile “oh yeah dude lets go surf!” And the two reunited friends grabbed their boards and
    left.
