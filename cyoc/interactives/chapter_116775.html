    Young enough for diapers

    "You there" she said pointing a finger at Gloria.

    Gloria was overjoyed that out of everyone else she chose her and quickly made her way on stage to stand before Lesily.

    "So tell me what's your name?" Lesily asked.

    "Gloria Cardon."

    "And how old are you?" she asked.

    "I just turned thirty last month" Gloria replied to which Lesily chuckled, "Aw yes the big three-o, most woman think that after they turn thirty it’s all downhill from there." She then winked, "But lucky for you I can make you whatever age you want as you just saw with my last volunteers. So what'll it be Gloria, younger or older?"

    "I want to be younger" Gloria said without hesitation.

    Lesily then smiled at her, "And how young do you want to be?"

    "Young enough to need diapers again" she answered with an obvious blush. When that ninety five year old woman Wendy got regressed back into a newborn baby girl she had been focusing all of her attention on the diaper she was wearing rather than the regression itself. Even as she was being carried off the stage she had stared longingly at the infant's attire. In that moment Gloria had wanted nothing more than to be young again so she too could wear and use diapers again. That and the idea of being a baby again and getting to be taken care of sounded like the ultimate pampering to her, which is what she definitely need on this vacation of hers.

    "But make me a 3 year old toddler" Gloria quickly as she didn't want to be so young that she couldn’t walk or anything.

    "Sure thing, now just listen to my voic-" Lesily started to say when she was interrupted by Gloria.

    "Wait one last thing, could you please make it so I still have my adult mind. I don't really want to have the mind of a three year old during my vacation"

    "I suppose I can do that" Lesily replied complying with the request, "In order to avoid the mental regression all you have to do is focus intensely on your real mental age and thoughts while I'm physically regressing you. Now normally I regress a person in stages but since you're only 30 I feel like I can regress you all the way back to a 3 year old in one go" she explained.

    "Then do that" Gloria replied, "I want to be young as soon as possible."

    "My, my someone's impatient to become a baby again" Lesily chuckled causing her to blush yet again.

    At that point two of Lesily's assistants brought out a large table, placing it in the middle of the stage.

    "Just lie down on this table Gloria and then we can begin your return to toddlerhood" Lesily said with a grin.

    Gloria did as she was told and laid down on the table, folding her hands while she stared up at the ceiling. Lesily then walked over and stood beside her.

    "Alright now Gloria while still remembering that you're 30 I want you to think back to when you were a 3 year old toddler. I want you to think back to your days in diapers, when you were a little baby girl who walked about the house wearing nothing but a diaper because you weren't potty trained yet and still needed them" she said in a gentle voice.

    Hearing Lesily speak Gloria felt herself begin to grow younger with each passing second as she envisioned her baby self in her mind. After a minute she had regressed back to a 20 year old, her shapely athletic body being replaced with the one she had in college, which was even shapelier but not as muscular. Suddenly her mind was hit with old memories and she wanted to hang with her friends, and then there was that final exam she needed to study for. Realizing what was happening Gloria told herself that she wasn't in college anymore, and that she was an adult with a job.

    She then felt another shrink in height as another minute went by and found herself suddenly 16 again. The newly teenage Gloria wanted nothing more than to go to parties and text her boyfriend that she loved him. Once again with a huge mental effort she pushed such thoughts aside and reaffirmed her true age as she went through what she could only describe as a reverse puberty.

    As she went past the age of 10 and reentered her childhood thoughts of no responsibilities and endless playtime tried to cloud her mind. But like before Gloria managed to push them away reminding herself that she was an adult who did have responsibilities and didn't have time to play around. Looking down she noticed that she was 7 years old and knowing she was almost at her target age filled her with relief as she starting to get tired of resisting the mental regression.

    "You're almost there Gloria" Lesily encouraged her, "You're still an adult but you're also about to become a baby again, one that still needs diapers. I want you to remember how it felt to wear one and use it as well. Remember how it felt to poop in them without any control whatsoever and how your mommy and daddy would then take care of your stinky mess by changing you into a fresh new diaper, only to start the whole cycle anew."

    Gloria didn't have any trouble remembering as the memories of her babyhood came rushing back to her, but instead of pushing them away she allowed a few of them to fill her head. She remembered what it felt like to push out a solid mass and then continue to play with her toys despite the warm load in the back of her diaper. She then remembered her mother having to chase her down to change her because she liked staying in her messy diapers for as long as she could. And then there were the diaper changes and boy were there a lot of those. Gloria smiled at those recalling how her mommy and daddy cleaned her poopy bottom before powdering her and putting her in a fresh diaper. She would then smile afterwards knowing that she’d use this one as well and need to be changed again in a few hours. And yet as Gloria reminisced the mental regression didn’t overtake her mind and her adult mentality remained intact.

    While she was busy taking a trip down memory lane her body continued to shrink growing even younger. Her arms and legs becoming pudgy with baby fat as her chest had become flat and her hair shortened as she became a three year old toddler. As for her clothes, which had changed countless times in the past five minutes during her regression they all disappeared to be replaced with a single article of clothing, the only one she would ever need from now on: a thick disposable diaper. Feeling the soft padding hugging her rear Gloria let out a squeal of excitement and kicked her legs enjoying the crinkling sounds it made when she moved, happy to finally be diapered at long last.

    Lesily then waved her hand in front of her face, snapping her fingers to get her attention.

    "Gloria could you be a dear and tell me how old you are mentally?”

    “Im tur dee wears old” Gloria replied caught off guard by her new lisp.

    “Good and can you tell me what nine times nine is?" Lesily asked the baby girl lying on the table.

    "Eddie won" she replied before sticking her thumb in her mouth and sucking on it.

    Lesily then turned to address the audience with open arms, "And there you have it folks a thirty year old woman regressed back into a three year old toddler, and she still has her adult mind to boot. I am I great or what?"

    The crowd of people applauded loudly at the performance, and few people were shouting for her to regress them next.

    Meanwhile Gloria who was still lying on the table was busy admiring the diaper she was now wearing, touching it and squeezing the padding with her fingers as if to verify its realness and that all this wasn’t just some amazing dream. As she was doing this she found herself thinking back to what Lesily said about pooping her diapers, her hypnotic words echoing in her head causing her to unconsciously release her bowels. The toddler was surprised when she suddenly let out a muffled fart as she heavily pooped her diaper, filling the back with a warm smelly load.

    “Miss Wesly I mada poopie” she called out drawing the attention of Lesily who quickly smelled what was wrong.

    “Uh oh ladies and gentleman it looks like my job isn’t over quite yet. It appears Gloria here just made a poo poo and needs her diaper changed” Lesily announced waving a hand in front of her face while the crowd of people awed at the fact that the new baby girl had an accident.

    “But not to worry I’ll have her changed in a jiff, my little gift to her to celebrate her new life as a baby.”

    At the snap of her fingers one of her assistants came on stage carrying a bunch of changing supplies which she placed on the table next to Gloria. Looking to her side she saw a container of baby wipes, a bottle of talcum powder and an unopened package of baby diapers. Seeing the face of the baby on the side of the package made her giggle as that’s what she was now, a little baby girl in a poopy diaper who was about to be changed.

    “Okay you little stinker, it's time to change your diaper” Lesily said as she reached down and untaped the toddler’s diaper, opening it up to reveal her mess. Lifting her head up and looking pass her chubby belly Gloria saw the huge amount of poop she made and grinned in happiness. Pulling the messy diaper out from under her she quickly balled it up before handing it to one of her assistants, “Please dispose of this for me” she said. The girl nodded and took the wrapped up dirty diaper away. Lesily then pulled out a few baby wipes and began to gently clean the poo from her butt with Gloria giggling as she was wiped down. Once her bottom was clean she added a bit of baby powder before opening up the package of diapers and pulling one out. Unfolding it and sliding it under her Lesily pulled the front up snugly between her legs and taped up both sides.

    Gloria grinned as she finished happy to be diapered once again, kicking her legs in satisfaction and causing her padding to crinkle.

    "Tank you" she said revealing a mouthful of baby teeth.

    "You're welcome sweetie" Lesily said as she popped a pink colored pacifier into Gloria’s mouth which she eagerly began to suck on. She then picked up the toddler and handed her over to another woman who walked onto the stage, "Gloria this here is your new caretaker, her name is Tanya and she's going to take care of you now."

    “Hello there cutie” Tanya greeted smiling down at the adorable baby girl in her arms.

    “So tell me Gloria what do you want to do now?” she asked.
