    Jill the Werewolf: Better be upfront about things

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Unnamed 3 years ago A

    Seeing the two boys frozen, Juliana mock-pouted and asked "Does my grand-daughter have that much effect on you, boys?"

    While his grand-son was still unresponsive, Danyor was already coming to his senses and answered by another question. "Do you know she's a werewolf?"

    Not seeing any advantage in trying to deny it, Jill answered "Yes, she does. But how do you?"

    Hesitating a moment, Danyor finally answered "My family is known for its mastery of the art, or as you call it, magic. That said, the flow of energy in you feel very different than what I'm used to from other werewolves. Yours feel calm, appeased, like the surface of a lake. I never felt anything quite like it, it's quite refreshing."

    Then, noticing his grand-son was still unresponsive, he asked the boy "You're okay, Lash?" Then back at the girls "Excuse him, but the younger members of the family all got a special training to detect werewolves more easily, which seems to make someone's presence too much for him."

    "It almost sound like your family actively hunt werewolves." Said Juliana, a suspicious tone in her voice, while her grand-daughter tried to hide behind her, going so far as turning into her wolf form.

    "Only the dangerous ones, which before today meant all werewolves we ever met, to be honest. It's kind of my family's duties since most are the results of my father's foolish actions. I know he was grieving about my sister, but…" It was clear Danyor wouldn't elaborate more, not that Jill and Juliana needed to.

    "How did you turn the curse into a blessing?" To everyone's surprise, it was Lash who asked. "Oh, and sorry for zoning out like that, especially for so long, but I never saw anyone with their internal magic flowing so harmoniously, not even among the more renewed mages of the family. It's quite breathtaking."

    If she had been in human form, Jill would have blushed. That said, currently being a wolf didn't prevent her from answering, somehow. "I simply accepted the wolf side of me is just, well, a part of me. All thanks to my grand-father. And thanks about harmonious magic thing Lash, even if I'm not totally sure of what it means."

    "I would like to meet your grand-father," said Danyor "er…"

    "Jill, but you won't be able to, pappy isn't among us anymore, otherwise granny wouldn't have any reasons to date you."

    "Oh…"

    "Jill," called Juliana, "Since your sudden transformation earlier ruined your clothes, why don't you take your bag and go change? And if you could guide Lash to the guest bedroom on the way, I would greatly appreciate it."

    Once the young ones were away, she wondered "When you said he was recuperating from a hunting accident…"

    "… It was a werewolf-hunting accident, yes." Confirmed the old Gypsy "He discovered his best-friend was a werewolf and wanted to break his curse, but it was already too late and was forced to kill him instead. Its a miracle he survived, let alone that he's not going to have much sequels."

    "So this curse can be broken if its carrier become dangerous?"

    "Only if it's still in the first stage, the one during which they only transform during the night of the full moon. Although after meeting your grand-daughter, I suppose it could still be transferred to someone that could accept the wolf as part of themselves if the second stage is reached. I fear there is no saving a state 3 werewolf, however."
