    Jim followed Zane to the men's bathroom and was about to open the door and go inside, but he paused when he heard Tim's cellphone ringing and him answering it.

    Leaning against the wall, around the corner from where Tim was, he listened in on the blond twink's conversation.

    "Oh, hey, Katie. What's up?" Tim asked, cheerily.

    Katie? That couldn't be my friend Katie, could it? Jim thought. Suddenly, that weird assumption came back. Maybe Timothy was Tiffany after all. But how could that be?

    "No, I haven't told him yet. I ... I just can't work up the courage. Maybe Kyle could do something about that?"

    Kyle? He's not talking about Kyle Perkins, is he? Jim thought. Could it be that Kyle had changed Tiffany into Timothy? Considering that he had been able to change Jen into an effeminate teenage boy, though, meant that he had some sort of transformation power, right? But where did he get it from? He obviously wasn't using the Transform or Dare power, or was he?

    "I know, I know. He'll only do what he wants. I swear, that boy must have an agenda to turn every girl into a gay man. Not that I'm complaining, though. I love being a boy who loves other boys." A pause, then "Katie, it's really not that bad. You'll see. It'll be okay." Another pause, then "Look, I've gotta go, okay? See ya later. Kay, bye."

    So Timothy really was Tiffany. And now Katie was next? How was Kyle doing all of this?
