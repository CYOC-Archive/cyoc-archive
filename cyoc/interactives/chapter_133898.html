She stormed off, feeling her 180 pairs of legs trail behind her. Eventually, she reached home, opening the unlatched door - her mom usually arrived home by now, as she worked for a half-day kindergarten. Jen found her sitting at the table.

She felt her mind shift, realizing she now had no parents; no one could birth a creature as long as her, so she was now an orphan, a rejected experiment of science. She walked to the local park, crawling herself into as tight a ball as a girl with 800 legs could, and cried.
