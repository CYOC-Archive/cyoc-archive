    " I wish for you Eden, to be my perfect wife that follows the criteria that I have previously mentioned and that you will retain your wish granting genie powers" Chris says to the lovely genie that had appeared before his eyes just minutes before. Chris had come to the conclusion that since he had been given unlimited wishes by Eden, that he would never want her to leave his side but that if he had found the perfect wife, he would have just thanked the genie and probably forget about her along the road. Besides, Chris had felt bad for Eden, considering that she must have been trapped inside the lamp for an unimaginable amount of time because of the fact the existence of Genie's had fallen into legend. If he decided that he didn't like anything about Eden personality or appearance wise, he could change it with a single wish and even if Eden complained she would be forced to agree and would love the changes anyways.

    "Just like I had expected you to say Chris. I'm so happy that I made the right choice in my master. Since I somehow managed to get away from my previous master, I have been looking for someone that I would always appreciate was my master and would be smart with his wishes. After all that time I finally found that person...you Chris. I have been watching you since the day you were born from afar and saw how you are always so generous and compassionate to other people and you have never gone back on your word. I already developed feelings for you before you became my master but I'm so happy that not only will those feelings be transformed into true love, but that you will return that same love back to me. Before I grant this wish I just want to say thank you for everything and I can't wait to see what is in store for our future" Eden said to Chris.

    "I can guarantee it will be interesting and that I don't want to wait any longer either Eden" Chris replied back to his soon to be wife. It was in this moment that Eden had closed her eyes, began to mumble under her breath words in her native language created by the original genie, and when she said the final words had snapped her fingers and everything had gone white.

    When Chris had awoken he looked down at himself to notice that he was now wearing a tuxedo along with other formable attire. His eyes than turned to gaze down at his ring finger and saw the golden band that now lay on it. Chris then repositioned himself to face towards where his now seemed to be wife was standing before the wish was granted and saw Eden standing their looking down at the floor where he now sat. Eden now wore instead of the arabian outfit from before the without a doubt most beautiful looking pure white and silver wedding dress Chris had ever seen in his life as well as a matching golden ring to his own. His new life was just beginning and what was the first thing Chris had planned to do.
