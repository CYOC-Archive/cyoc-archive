    They day passed quickly, as did the night, the next morning Elliot begun his journey to the prime café, the rest of the team stayed behind to learn how to pilot, drive and control the vehicles and equipment at the outpost they stumbled upon. He wondered what was in store for him.

    Not long after leaving the cave he spots the bright and tall buildings of a metropolis faced him, the city of Jahee. However as he entered the city, he was faced with a shock, everyone, was an animal, but not just an ordinary animal, these animals were walking on their hind legs, walking, talking, even dressed like humans. Knowing that he would obviously attract attention, he decided to give his new found ability a crash course. He began to think, in his mind, he pictured his body, and then thought of fur, tabby fur, growing over his smooth body, whiskers sprouted as his upper lip splittened, his ears, grew to a point and slowly slinked their way up the side of his head, also gaining fur, his hands grew fur as a long cats tail sprouted from behind him, using his sharp claws, he quickly cut a hole for it and stared as the final changes took place, his nose changeing, his feet, eyes and finnaly, his mouth pushed out into a small cats muzzle. Where had once been a human being, stood a tall hansome tabby cat, he took the liberty of aging himself to 20, one thing he discovered he could do. "Maybe this is not such a bad power after all?" he thought to himself.

    He steped out of the ally and continued on his way, 5 minutes later he was in the Prime café, he was guided to a table to wait for his "Contact". As he sat there, he noticed thanks to his ageing and his new animalistic traits, he seemed more agile, graceful, light, intelligent, but freakish of all, he knew stuff, he didn't know, he knew how to ride a bike, drive a car, even fly a plane. "Prehaps its an effect of the aging?" he thought to himself. The bell rang and he turned, he saw something that he though never existed. A child, a young daughter, golden retriever apparently and her two parents. It then dawned on him how much like this dimension was like his. He turned his attention back to the table, a few minutes later, a Lizard, wearing a long trenchcoat entered, he sat down oposite. Instantly Elliot started in quite whispers, so not to draw attention. "Ok, who are you? What am I doing here? and why?"
    The Lizard took off his glasses "calm Mr. Baker, first, I am Hector Zachia, the other two questions, I can answer just as easilly, you and your freinds are "The TF team"." he hissed silently
    "The TF what" Elliot replyed, baffled.
    "The TF team, it was foretold that 4 people 3 male, 1 female would come and save us from out opression." He said
    "Opression?" Elliot said intreged. "Go on". The Lizard started to inform Elliot on the goings on "For years we have been controled and rueled by the TF master and his lieutenates, hurting us, not giving us a good life, he is like you, human I mean, he hates us, but wants to rule this dimension and yours." the lizard blubbered
    "But why is that a problem?" Asked the feline
    Hector replied "If he suceedes in ruling both worlds, kaboom, both worlds will be destoreyed."
    Elliot answered "Well, I'm sorry, but all I wanna do is get home"
    Hector announced desperately "Listen Elliot, please, there is a resistance rising up against him, to get back, you have to get his power by earing the title of ruler, so until then your stuck here..."
    Elliot knew that he had to do what the people wanted him to do, even if he didn't want to get involved, it was 2 worlds at stake. He was about to ask more questions when 2 shady character in suits came up behind him "Elliot Baker, The TF master wants to see you".
