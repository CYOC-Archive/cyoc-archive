    You, Chris and raccoon Jake were peacefully sleeping in your living room when you started to hear some weird noises. You opened your eyes just a little, only to realize that a mysterious green light was covering the whole place. You suddenly completely opened your eyes and noticed that the light was coming from Jake, even the weird noises were coming from him.

    You tried to wake Chris up, but he was deeply sleeping. You hadn't any other idea, so you only kept watching how the light increased and the sounds became louder. You can't believe Chris didn't wake up after that.

    Without knowing what to do, you carefully approached to Jake but before you can put a hand on him,te light blinded you temporary. When you was able to open your eyes again, you realized that the sounds had ceased. But most importantly, your friend wasn't a raccoon anymore. He was totally naked, and it was the first time you've seen him in his birthday suit, but at least he was human again!

    "Mmm... It's cold." You heard Jake's muttering in his sleep. However, unlike Chris he immediately heard himself and woke up surprised. He realized that he was human again and began to laugh and smile. He was very very happy. After a moment, he noticed that you were seeing him and he realized that he was naked. "Holy shit! How long have you been there?"

    "Half an hour." You replied with a grin on your face. "I saw you turning back to human."

    "Oh God! How embarrassing!!" Jake said. "But it was only you, right?"

    "Yes, Chris is still sleeping." You said. "I tried to wake up but..."

    "Thank goodness. If he sees me naked he'll be making fun of me." Jake blused.

    "And how does it feel?"

    "What?"

    "Being an animal." You said. "How does it feel?"

    "Wierd. Although it's kinda exciting as well." Jake told you. "I've always wished to transform like Ben 10 since I was a kid. I know that's a cartoon but now that I've seen it's possible I want to change more!"

    "We need to test it before." You said. "For now, we've learned that it's effects are temporary. Who knows, maybe we can have our own transformation adventure with this."

    "That would be awesome! I've always dreamed of becoming an alien like Four Arms or Cannonbolt!" Jake seemed too excited.

    "Those are cartoon characters. I don't think they exist in real life."

    "Until today, I thought that transformations were impossible but now things are different and the possibilities are endless!"
