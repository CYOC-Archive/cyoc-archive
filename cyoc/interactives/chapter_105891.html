    Ty's Power

    From nerd to nature spirit.

    As he read his mind, Ty discovered that his name was Carlen and he lived for the renaissance fair. Carlen had also dreamed that he could return the forest to the way it looks in fairy tales. But, Carlen's wish was more specific than that. First Ty granted his wish that he would be muscular, and taller like a guardian as he always wanted to be one, but he also added a personal change. After all, Ty thought, Carlen is really more of a female name. Also, everyone looking at him would ignore the changing person as if he wasn't there.

    As Carlen got up and left, he didn't seem to notice how tall he was or how much muscle he had put on. Ty watched him walk away from the tent. Carlen suddenly looked down at himself and began to feel his new body - amazed at how good he looked.

    Now Ty granted the rest of his wish- that he wanted to BE a nature guardian. Ty granted Carlen the power to command everything about his new woodland home.

    Carlen felt a surge of power, so sudden it confused him. He watched in curiosity as black tiger-like claws grew on his now strong yet feminine hands. He felt strange as his legs grew even more muscular and more feline shaped.
    His shirt and pants filled as his body grew larger and much more shapely as his, now her chest ballooned outwards until she had an obvious two new features. Her shoes were crushed by her huge tiger paws as her toes spread and grew thick. Carlen felt her ass expand as an orange furred wolf tail with a white tip stretched her pants apart, then orange tiger striped fur began to spread across her body, and her new chest and belly fur took on a bright white tone.
    Carlen ran her now long fox tongue over her sharpening teeth, and smiled. She understood now her wish was coming true. Eagle wings sprouted on her back and tore away the rest of her clothing.
    Carlen was growing to 20, 30, now 40 feet as her furry face pushed out to a muzzle and her hair became long and black. Now that her body was changed, armor that resembled petrified wood appeared where her clothing and shoes used to be, while a petrified wooden crown and gauntlets appeared on her head and hands. Normally this whole spectacle would cause quite the commotion, But she noticed that she had been as invisible as the wind while she was changing. Pleased with her new purpose in life, Carlen the forest spirit appeared in Ty's tent one last time and gave a thankful bow before vanishing into the nearby forest.
