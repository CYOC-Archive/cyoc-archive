    Connor tugged at his shirt collar again as Spark nudged his shoulder for the third time. "I told you before, quit messing with it." Spark said quietly to her boyfriend as they sat at Starbucks waiting for her friend, Calista along with her husband, Mitch, and their daughter Maggie.

    "Well I know you wanted to meet with your friend Calista, but did we really have to get all dressed up?" Connor asked motioning to his clothes. Spark had gone back and forth at home on what the two of them should wear when meeting Calista and her family, she eventually settled on Informal wear for the two of them.

    "I know, but it would look a little odd for people to see a couple of teenagers talking with adults, they'll think we're like...interviewing about babysitting or something." Spark said.

    "Are you nervous?" Connor asked. "What? Me? No...hell no, I'm just meeting an old friend and her husband, along with their kid, and she's probably going to show me she's a great parent and I'm never to be a good mo..." Spark trailed off as she began to hyperventilate before Connor grabbed her hands and she began to calm down.

    "Hey, she's still learning. You don't automatically learn to be a parent. You know, I don't think I've told you this before Spark, but I'm always nervous about how our relationship will go from where we started. We've faced a rogue demon hunter, vampires, skin walkers, and more. But this is....still scary to me sometimes. But every time I look at you and see your face and how you always persevere no matter what, it helps me to go on too." Connor informed his girlfriend as Spark took a deep breath and smiled.

    Spark then gently held Connor's hand kissed him softly on the lips and smiled. "Connor Moreland, you are incredible...all the time." Spark sighed dreamily. "I can't wait to walk down that aisle," Spark said as she began to daydream. "Me in my wedding dress, you in your suit,"

    "Uh Spark?" Connor said trying to get his girlfriend's attention. "Then I'll say:...." "Spark, Calista and her family are coming." "Calista and her family are...What?! Oh my god, oh my god, how's my hair? should I have put on more make up? less?" Spark asked in a panic as Connor again took Spark's hand gently and she took a breath and began to calm down. She was ready to sit and talk with her old friend Calista once again.
