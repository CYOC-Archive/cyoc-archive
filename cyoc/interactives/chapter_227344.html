    After leaving the gym Felicity ran into Winery, the girl looked at her skeptically.

    “You’re the new girl right? Take my advice: don’t join the cheerleading team, at least not until Mandy Thomas graduates.” The werewolf princess said.

    “Yea I saw that, what a bitch. I already have an asshole telling me what to do I don’t need another.” Felicity replied.

    Felicity didn’t know why she said that, she had no love for these supernatural beings as Miranda or “Mandy” did but the way she ordered everyone around seemed to piss Felicity off.

    “You got a dick stepdad or something?” The werewolf asked with a smirk.

    “Something like that.” Felicity smiled back.

    Winery motioned for the door and they both left the locker room, the blonde princess pulled out a joint and lit it up when they arrived at the back of the school. She then offered it to Felicity, who accepted it, taking a puff before passing it back to Winery.

    “Saw your car, your parents loaded or something?” Winery asked after a hit and passed it back to the other blonde.

    “You could say that. I heard your family is pretty well off too, where’s your ride?” Felicity deflected after another smoke.

    The werewolf girl swiped the joint from the other girl and started walking towards the back parking lot. Felicity followed and couldn’t help but let out a laugh when she saw the car. It was an old dusty red 2009 Chevrolet Silverado 3500, the drivers side door was blue and the hood was from a completely different car, the truck bed’s door was gone with a cage gate in it’s place.

    “T-this is your car? Couldn’t get daddy to spring for the 2010?” Felicity chuckled.

    “Ha ha. I don’t care what you say, I like it, it’s been modified for off road use and I don’t have to worry about getting any dents or dings.” Winery defended.

    “Yea, it will blend in with the other dents.” Felicity added and swiped the weed back.

    Winery took back the joint before getting into her truck and starting the engine. She told the other girl to get in and when she did she feigned giving Felicity the weed before tossing it out the window, pulling out from the school.

    “Asshole. Where are we going?” Felicity asked.

    Winery smiled and continued driving.
