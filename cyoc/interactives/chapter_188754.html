    A place for tots like Judy

    added 2 years ago AR Toddler Baby

    A few hours passed and Judy was still sleeping peacefully. Soon she would begin to gently open her eyes and little by little she would begin to wake up completely. Judy hoped that the whole regression situation could have been a bad dream, but as she sat down, a weird crinkling sound was heard. At first, Judy wondered where that sound was coming from until she looked down and noticed that what was making that sound was her super hero diaper on her waist. Apparently she was still a 2 year old toddler. She then began to look around and gasped in shock.

    She was no longer on top of the building but in what appeared to be a big crib, with lots of stuffed animals and an extremely comfortable blanket. Did a family find her and now she was in their nursery? Or did someone find her and take her to a nearby daycare center? She had to find out herself.

    At first, Judy tried to fly, thinking that she still had her powers, but the only thing she could do was perform a small jump which caused her to fall on her bottom. Apparently, she did not yet have her powers in her toddler state.

    So the next thing she did was crawl to the edge of the crib. Upon reaching the edge of the crib, Judy tries to stand up thanks to the bars and thus be able to have a better view of the place where she was now.

    The first thing Judy saw was that there were a lot of giants cribs that were spaced around the room in a sort of circle around a large open area in the center of the room. In the middle of the open area was a large rug, and in the middle of the rug was a rocking chair. And not to mention the large number of toys that were scattered all over the place.

    Lots of changing tables lined one wall while on another wall stood lots of high chairs. And the colorful walls of the place had many colorful cartoon characters. Also from what Judy saw is that the place had a great security system. From security cameras, baby monitors in some of the cribs and even a security door.

    This was not just any nursery. This must be the most gigantic nursery she has ever seen. It would be a very difficult job trying to escape from this place.

    Judy try to check the whole place again in case there was something she hadn't noticed yet. And she found something that kinda surprised her.

    Judy previously believed that she was the only person in the place for now but she realized that there were in fact other children in some cribs who were completely asleep. Some seemed to be the same age as her and others seemed younger. If she hadn't miscounted, there were about 8 in total and they were all girls.

    At first that didn't seem so weird, but soon Judy realized that the crib she was in right now was silver and had the initial "P". It was the same color as her outfit and had her same superhero initial.

    And not only that, the rest of the cribs were painted in different colors and each one had a different initial.

    In fact, next to her crib was another dark green crib with the initial "SG". And in that same crib there seemed to be a blond toddler girl of about 2 years old sleeping peacefully.

    Seeing that little girl, the initial and the color of the crib Judy quickly remembered her friend Helen. She was also a superhero, whose name was "Speedy Girl", she was quite fast and wore a mostly dark green outfit. But the last time Judy saw her was two weeks ago.

    Were the rest of the toddlers and babies in the nursery also female superheroes or was it just a strange coincidence?
