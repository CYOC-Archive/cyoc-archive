"We discovered the situation while... investigating another matter concerning Jack Crawford," she added, not wanting to mention the involvement of two white furred weretigers from China. "However, any information that could be used against Benton Knight was all that Crawford was after.     "Perhaps with Doctor Twist and everyone else's help I have grown and matured, but trust me Tachibana no Akane," began Benton as calmly as he could, "if Jack Crawford was within my reach at this moment, you would need a jigsaw puzzle expert to reassemble him when I was done."
    Tachibana simply nodded her head in acknowledgement, noticing that Benton's fists were clenched to the point where his fingernails were starting to draw blood from his palms.
    "You'd have to get in line behind me first," snarled Frances, who was doing her best to try to calm down, despite the situation.
    "Now honey," began Samantha.
    "Please Sam, don't honey me," replied Frances, knowing that her wife hated the masculine short form of her first name. "The people I see and treat put their trust in me to help them with their problems. If anything said within the confines of my practice is ever made public, their lives and my own might be ruined."
    "A serious point that will be brought up at the Elders' Council inquiry tomorrow morning," said Akane.
    "I'll have to call my office manager and have Rudy reschedule everything that was on the agenda for tomorrow, let alone find a new security company and system while having the place swept for other potential leaks," realized Frances.
    "Because of the nature of the situation, Tachibana Security can handle that for you at cost," offered Daisuke Tachibana, who had been standing near his grandmother the whole time, just in case he was needed.
    "Thank you," said Frances, in the closest thing to her normal voice since Akane broke the news to them.
    "I understand your concerns and frustrations, but let us handle this," advised the Skulk leader and head of the Elders' Council, something to which both Frances and Benton readily agreed publicly.
    However... A Code Omega never worked against Crawford, but there must be some way to get that bastard and his bitch out of my fur once and for all without getting into any trouble with the Elders or the law, thought Benton, while his wife moved closer trying to comfort him.
