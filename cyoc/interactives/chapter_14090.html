    "Good morning Professor Attwater!" you say as the thin man walks past you as if your just part of the furniture.

    Unknown to the Professor is the men waiting inside his office. A foursome of government scientists wait and talk eager to be informed of the newest experiment announced by the Professor.

    Even as you help the janitor clean the outer office the heated conversation inside can be heard with ease. You begin to dust down the door to the Professor's office when it opens slamming you against the wall.

    An angry Professor give you a quick look and then stops as you rub your head to remove the sawdust.

    "You want to to earn some good money there Boy?" he asked of you.

    "Me Sir?" was the response you give.

    "Here gentlemen is our first case scenario, a willing young man of good health ready to further the knowledge of science." announces the Professor as the other men look first at you and then among themselves with glee.

    Soon the men leave but not without shaking your hand and reminding you that what you do will help your government fight the war against terror. You smile to each and feel the honest joy of helping your country.

    The Professor then tells you to move your belongings to the nearby medical lab were for the next two months you need to reside. As you hear his orders the thought of earning what was generally promised brings up the obvious question.

    "Sir!" you ask. "How much did you say I get paid for doing this type of work?"

    He frowns at first then blurts out a figure of $3,000. per month.

    Wow, you think $3,000. dollars per month was more than you might have earned the entire summer working as a janitor.

    Late that afternoon you have all your clothes and stereo packed and in your VW bettle. A few short good bye's to friends and your off to the Medical lab parking lot.

    What do you do now?
