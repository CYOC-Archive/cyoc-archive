    “I know what your thinking, and I don’t like that smile you've got on your face. You want me to put on a costume for you this weekend?” You said. “One condition. Nothing, completely ridiculous and nothing that makes me look out of a porn magazine. “

The store would close about a week or two later, so now is the best time to get a costume for cheap. You notice a shelf full of animal ears, most of which are cat ears of various styles, some with fur, some just a wire frame, and some with an included tail.

Looking at the pair, you quickly decide against the ones with tails included, they are too big and the tail would make it annoying to sit down. The wire ones would just scratch your head. You eventually pick a headband with felt black fur on the top.
