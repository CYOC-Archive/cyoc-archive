    A odd disease has been spreading around the world changing society to it very core however this epidemic isn't like any normal infections. As the effect do not cause any viral infections instead it transform it host into superhero! Initially symptom include an increase in body temperature and the skin start gaining a gold-esque texture like quality. The final stages of this disease causes the body to become fully golden where the infected will compulsively spin in a rapid motion until the transformation into a superhero is complete. Officially the governments off the world calls the outbreak Super humana mutatio morbo or S.H.M.M. for short. It is more colloquially known as supermosis by the common people. the federal disease research institution has sent an investigator who goes by the name Jackob Hart to look into this precellular outbreak in hope of finding a cure ,accompanying him on this task is the sarcastic scientist Christina who majored human biology.

    As Jackob and Christina embark on their journey to investigate the S.H.M.M outbreak, they soon realize that the effects of the disease are much more widespread and profound than they initially thought. They encounter individuals who have transformed into heroes with extraordinary abilities, ranging from super strength and speed to the ability to control fire or fly.

    However, they also witness the darker side of the outbreak. Some individuals who have transformed become uncontrollable and wreak havoc, leading to widespread destruction and chaos. The governments of the world are struggling to cope with the sudden influx of superpowered individuals and are trying to find a way to control them.

    Despite the danger and difficulties they face, Jackob and Christina are determined to find a cure for the S.H.M.M. They delve deep into the biology of the disease and the mechanics of the transformation, determined to unlock the secrets of the virus and find a way to reverse its effects.

    As they get closer to uncovering the truth about the outbreak, they come up against powerful forces that will stop at nothing to keep the secrets of the S.H.M.M hidden. In the face of danger, Jackob and Christina must use their scientific knowledge and bravery to save the world and find a cure for the outbreak before it's too late.



    Jackob and Christina first start by looking into a local case of two twins Sasha and Sophia, both who are in the initial stages of supermosis.

    As Jackob and Christina begin their investigation, they focus their attention on the case of the two twins, Sasha and Sophia. They interview the family and gather information on the twins' symptoms and progression of the disease. They also take blood samples and perform various tests in an attempt to uncover the secrets of the S.H.M.M.

    As they delve deeper into the case, they come across several inconsistencies that lead them to believe that there may be more to the story than what is initially apparent. They begin to suspect that there may be an underlying cause for the outbreak, and that the disease may not be a naturally occurring phenomenon.

    Despite the challenges they face, Jackob and Christina are determined to uncover the truth and find a cure for the S.H.M.M. As they continue their investigation, they come across a mysterious organization that may hold the key to unlocking the secrets of the virus.

    As they work to uncover the truth, Jackob and Christina must also navigate the dangers that come with being involved in such a high-stakes investigation. They face opposition from those who want to keep the secrets of the S.H.M.M hidden, and must use their wits and bravery to stay one step ahead of their enemies.

    Despite the obstacles in their way, Jackob and Christina remain focused on their goal of finding a cure and saving the world from the devastating effects of the S.H.M.M outbreak.
