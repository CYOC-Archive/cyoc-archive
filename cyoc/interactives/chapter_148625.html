    The cursed bell

    added 4 years ago A

    After a while looking in the shop, I stumbled a rather large cowbell but this one didn't have a stick to be hit with but instead it had a collar like the ones that you would often see on dogs. It seemed pretty interesting because I usually think of cowbells as musical instrument and not something to be worn by a human or even a cow for that matter. It looks and sounds just like any other bell that I've seen but nonetheless I bought it anyways. When I arrived at home it was pretty late because the shop was very far from my house. When I got home I was exhausted and didn't want to do anything other than going to sleep. So I took a quick bath before immediately jumping onto my bed. Just before I went to bed I decided to put on the bell that I bought. On the bell there was a warning that said:
    "Warning! This bell is cursed. Do not wear it at all costs. Anyone who ignores this warning will be turned into a cow permanently."
    I thought "Yeah right, how could a bell turn me into a cow?" and put it on anyways. But only if I knew what was to come. The next morning when I woke up the room looked and felt bigger than usual. "Maybe I'm just seeing things" I thought to myself and I walked slowly to the bathroom and looked into the mirror. But what I saw wasn't the face of a human but instead it was the face of a adult male cow. At first I thought that I was just dreaming and then I looked at my legs I didn't see two feet but instead it was two large hooves. "No, it can't be" I thought to myself "I couldn't have transformed into a cow". Suddenly, I was bumping my head into walls hoping to wake up from this horrible nightmare that was actually reality. I tried to call for help but all that came out were moo's. After a few tries, I finally gave up and accepted the reality that I was actually a cow. At that moment, the most reasonable to do was to escape to a farm. Luckily, there was one nearby and so I tried to run as fast I could which was difficult because I wasn't used to moving on all fours but I made it nonetheless. On the field, there was a barn and a silo nearby and I also saw a few animals. Never in my life have I felt so free and independent. I then explored the barn and saw only a few animals and I was shocked because how could a farm this big have so few livestock. The barn was big and very comfortable. Suddenly I felt very hungry and the grass outside looked super tasty. I ate a bit and it was so good that I literally had no word to describe how good it was. In my head, I thought that maybe life as a cow may not be as bad as I assumed it to be. In fact, it may actually be better than life as a human because I don't have to worry about things like school, work and taxes. It has been about 2 or 3 years ever since I became a cow and I have adapted to farm life even the thought of being milked and having to plow dirt isn't as bad as it used to be when I first came here but instead it felt like something natural like eating or sleeping for a human. Every single day is almost the same. Same routine and same activity but I never got bored. In fact, I actually enjoy having the same schedule.This morning I woke up, ate some grass and I rested a bit before I have to do my daily work of plowing the dirt. It felt just like a normal day at the farm. That is until...
