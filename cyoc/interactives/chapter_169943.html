    Alpha began, "Sir, you are our only priority, we will still take a shot for you if we are-"

    O' Brien glared. "You aren't take to take a shoot for me, doll, you will obey."

    The statues that had been Beta and Delta were balanced on a stand with a metal pole going directly into their bodies.

    Alpha remembered how her raw material had thought the dolls were all identical zombies, she couldn't help but feel insulted by this. They were not identical, each of them was a unique prize: their personality and figure decided by their raw material's ideal woman, while their species and other details decided by the tastes of their zapper.

    And they were not mindless, Alpha was perfectly capable of rational thought, she simply understood the reality that she was a plastic doll to be owned, not a person to decide their own destiny. Even calling herself 'she' was just for convenience sake, 'it' was a much better description! It honestly carried with it a sense of liberation that her raw material hadn't thought possible. No doubts about her place in the universe, no existential worries, no stress of clawing up a social ladder. She simply existed, and her singular purpose was to serve another to the best of her ability. Her only regret was that it hadn't happened sooner!

    "Don't worry sir, I'm certain they'll be made mobile again once the game is over, even if I'm sure they're perfectly happy as mannequins."

    "What are you basing that first part on?"

    "... That this is a game Sir, and you are the player, we're just the poker chips! And it would be bad taste to reduce the desired result of your prizes, if you wanted statues you'd have zapped them as such!" said the plastic black tigress. She wrapped her arms around him, pressing her plastic body against him. Breasts with no nipples, and the space between her legs as featureless as an anime character's. She had no belly button, those were for creature born, she was made. "We'll never leave you Sir, never abandon you, never be lost, never forget you, never discard you."

    "Until I'm zapped."

    "Then we'll welcome you as a sister doll or statue, but as our Owner, we'll do EVERYTHING to make sure that doesn't happen."

    "And when I die of old age?"

    Alpha looked at him flatly. "I don't think about that, and neither should you Sir!" She said almost sounding stern. "Live in the moment, we'll be here to catch you if you fall."
