    They Both Transform

    added by Xander6981 9 years ago A BM

    That night, after dinner Nate and Kris were both in the living room watching TV. It was getting increasingly darker outside. Kris kept looking over to Nate for signs of the impending transformation, but none were apparent yet. He was starting to think that woman was full of crap.

    Nate was starting to feel restless and off. He got up and walked out of the room and into the kitchen. He grabbed a Coke out of the fridge and opened it, taking a sip as he closed the door. He walked over to the window and looked out into the night. He felt a sudden pang of pain in his gut and put the Coke down on the counter. He felt another pain and dropped to the linoleum floor. Kris heard this and came running in to the kitchen.

    "Oh boy! It's happening!" he exclaimed.

    "What are you talking about?" grunted Nate.

    "The lady next door. She turned you into a werewolf!" replied Kris.

    "That's ridicu..." started Nate, when he looked down. He saw there were new, dark hairs growing on his hands and arms, with more growing in every second. Nate cried out, pulling himself up and running to the bathroom, with Kris quickly following. He flipped on the light and checked himself out in the mirror. The same dark, scraggly hairs were growing all over his face, neck, and under his shirt. "Wh...why would she do this?!"

    "Because you said you were bored."

    Nate felt a sudden, burning pain in his fingertips and looked down to see his fingernails split open and thick, sharp claws grow in their place. He looked back in the mirror. The fur was growing in thicker, a rich brown and black in color. His lips had darkened and his teeth were growing out into large, sharp fangs.

    "My, what big teeth you have," said a female voice. Nate and Kris both looked over, seeing Kathryn standing in the doorway of the bathroom.

    "Dis you douth thish to me?!" cried out Nate, his voice distorted by his large fangs.

    "Yes, my dear, and your almost done." replied Kathryn.

    Nate cried out again as his chest and back started to expand outward, ripping apart his shirt, revealing a densely furred, muscled torso. He feet stretched out, reforming into digitigrade paws and his ears grew to points as Nate's transition from boy to Wolf Man was complete. He let out a low growl that turned to a snarl as he checked himself out in the mirror. He then threw his head back and let out a loud howl.

    Kathryn smiled at the new werewolf. Kris looked over to Kathryn, smiling. She smiled back, although more deviously.

    "He looks so cool! My own brother, a werewolf!" said Kris.

    "Yep, and your transformation should start any moment now," replied Kathryn.

    Kris' smile disappeared with a look of shock. "My transformation?"

    "Yes. Why settle for one werewolf, when I can have two?"

    "But...but..." started Kris when he started feeling itchy all over. He pulled off his shirt and looked down at his formerly bare chest as the black hairs started growing in. "This is so...cool!"

    He ran to the mirror and watched as the same changes overtook him. He watched as the fur grew in and his chest and back expanded out and his feet reformed into paws and his nails tore away as large claws grew out and his teeth grew out into sharp fangs. He checked out his new wolfy self in the mirror and let out a howl as well.

    Kathryn smiled at her handiwork and gestured for her two new werewolves to follow her. All three walked out of the bathroom and into the kitchen. She threw open the patio door. "Now then my new werewolves, I release you onto the public! Go hunt and have fun! Be wild!"

    Nate and Kris ran out the patio door into the cool night air, anxious to have fun and hunt as newly created werewolves. Kathryn closed the door and walked back to her house, wondering what sort of mayhem they will cause and what more she can do herself.
