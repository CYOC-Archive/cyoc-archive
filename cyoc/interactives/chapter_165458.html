    "I'm really not in the mood to transform myself today," Rosa explained. "But that doesn't mean we can't have some fun, you and I." Rosa lifted a small box off of her cart. "Ah, here it is. I think you'l like what I have here, Gloria. Don't be scared at first, just enjoy the process."

    Gloria began to tremble, her mind spinning at what dangerous creature Rosa had in that box. She tried to run but Rosa slammed her foot down in font of her.

    "Ah ah ah, no running. A small, bug like you wouldn't want to end up underfoot, would you?" Gloria got the message and stopped moving.

    "Now, say hello to some of your fiends." And with that, Rosa opened the box and poured two dozen roaches down atop Gloria. At first Gloria panicked and flailed, but after a moment it became apparent that the other bugs shared the same body as her and it wasn't as frightening as she first thought.

    Rosa noticed the change in Gloria's demeanor. "That's right, make friends. They're nice bugs."

    Gloria tried waving her antennae at the other roaches, attempting to communicate. Were these other transformed guests that Rosa had captured? Once again, Rosa was astute at reading Gloria's body language. "Oh, no. No. These are not humans. These are regular old roaches I've captured around the hotel. They have no idea you're not one of them. The only thing they know for sure is that they're all male and you, my dear Gloria, are female."

    Rosa grinned and leaned in closer. Gloria suddenly realized that every other bug was slowly approaching her, all twitching in some kind of mating dance that was supposed to impress her. But she was not impressed! She was...

    "Oh!" What was that? One of the bugs had poked her rear end. She spun around. "Ah!" Another one did the same. Then another. Then again. They were all pressing a very specific spot on her bug body. She couldn't tell what spot, but they all seemed to know. All Gloria knew was that it felt really good. She began to relax and flap her wings. The other insects took this as a sign to move to the next level.

    Rosa laughed as she watched the first male mount Gloria and begin to wiggle his body, swiftly ejecting his sperm. She could tell Gloria enjoyed it, as confused as she was. And then a second male climbed on. And then a third. They all politely took turns, Gloria sinking deeper and deeper into a blissful haze as the night went on. She could barely tell which way was up anymore, much less why a bunch of insects were turning her on. But it didn't matter, she simply turned off all rational thought and enjoyed the bliss of a bug being mated with.

    As the last male finished up, Rosa swept them all into her box, including Gloria.

    "So sorry, I know you're a guest, but I can't have you laying your little babies in my nice clean room. You're going to have to come with me."

    Gloria wiggled with panic as Rosa closed the box over her head, but there was nothing she could do to stop it.
