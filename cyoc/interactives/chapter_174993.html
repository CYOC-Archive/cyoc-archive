    With a boom, the meteor broke up into uncountable pieces, raining down chunks of rock into the cities, towns and estates of the kingdom.
    In those rocks one found metals and minerals and coal. Places they hit some times split open to show metals, minerals, and coal, but also artifacts from the past appearing as well.
    The dwarfs and the tropical islands had some steam punk tech already.
    Duchess Jenifer Smith had sevel chunks of the meteor land on her lands and the land of the five lands owned by her friends.
    In fact she was one of the first in the kingdom to learn to smelt the rock to find the metals, minerals and coal. She also learns the rock makes good building stuff.
    Jenifer Smith`s land is a big market/bizarre with the most advanced forging stations in the kingdom even before the meteor hits.
    One of her manors is a twenty four seven party were adventures trade things.
    One of the chunks pierces one of the mounds on Duchess Jenifer`s land, when they move the chunk, they find the mound was in fact a tomb of a past Duchess called Lustsinda. Sevel gems in the tomb are found to be lusttite. Lusttite is found in the meteor rocks.
    Sellers of steam engines pop up in towns, cities and bazars.
