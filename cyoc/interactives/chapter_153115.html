    By the time Brian woke up for school the next day, he had mostly pushed the encounter with Madame Illusia to the back of his mind - his thoughts were mostly on getting thru school and seeing how everyone reacted to the brand new him. He as usual had to drive in the family's old beaten up truck down to the school, and even after breakfast (which involved a downright curious amount of greens such as salad and carrots), he was feeling odd. It was like the truck was really too small for him, even though by all logic he fit in it really well.

    The ensuing classes all the way through were unique in how oddly uncomfortable he was. He'd gone to school in a larger outfit - a plaid shirt buttoned up and a very large pair of jeans fastened on with a belt. But just being in the classrooms was too awkward. It was like he was really not meant for this kind of location. He squirmed in his seat numerous times, and, during a test, had to be told to keep himself still because his rocking back and forth was making a racket.

    It was a very odd feeling for him to feel downright eager to go to Phys Ed, when he was usually dreading that class due to Jack's prominence in it...but here he was. He got undressed for PE and immediately noticed his hair. Taking off his shirt netted a strange sensation on his neck...did some of his hair grow a little on his neck? He also saw that his chest and torso were both a deal hairier than normal, and his skin tone looked kinda weird.

    It was also when he met Jack for the first time that day. Jack seemed to be staring at him when the pants came down, and Jack outright demanded, "Okay freak-o, what kind of crazy steroids are you on to be almost as buff as me!?"

    Jack was indeed one of the biggest students at the whole school, and at eighteen he was used to being prominent enough to throw his weight around. But now, staring at Brian, he realized he couldn't really push Brian around any more.

    And Brian saw this...and quite liked it. He decided for once to try and go after Jack, "What's wrong Jack? You like what you see?" Brian promptly flexed his arms, smiling at how it felt. He noted that Jack was indeed staring a bit more. "Yeah, you like what you see."

    Jack immediately snapped back at him, "I got a girlfriend, motherfucker!"

    Brian responded just as quickly, "And bisexual is a thing too. You should know - you made my sex ed class miserable too." And with that Brian got into his PE clothes. His nails felt strangely hard, but it was definitely refreshing to be out there, exercising his muscles and showing off for the girls. He noted his PE shorts had a prominent bulge at the crotch, though nothing too overtly noticeable, and it seemed to grab the attention of the girls in his class.

    You know what, Brian decided, fuck it - I love PE from now on. Let's me run around, enjoy the air, like I was meant to!

    His head hair was feeling oddly longer, but he didn't really care. By the time PE was over, Brian felt like he was on top of the world. He could go out there and finally enjoy his time at this damn school. Maybe even try out for a sports team! Maybe...maybe even kick Jack off and take his place. Brian could outright imagine it;

    Him striding out and hearing people chanting his name...everyone who shit-talked him finally getting proven wrong. He was gonna be so much more than just a farm boy!
