    For a brief moment, I was afraid that Alexa might have changed her wish while she chased Mindy. My fears were quickly allayed when Alexa playfully licked Mindy’s face. “Oh, sweet Mindy,” Alexa said. “I’m not going to hurt you. In fact, I wish you would change back to normal.”

    Slowly but surely Mindy was restored to her normal body. Alexa let go of her so that Mindy wouldn’t be caused pain by the differences in their body sizes. When Mindy was back to normal she stood up very slowly. She was, understandably, visibly shaken and there were tear marks on her face. Alexa did not change back to normal. She continued to talk to Mindy through her wolf form. “Now,” she said, “I think we have an understanding. You may have power over me professionally, which you’re never going to get to use since I’m not coming back to modeling, but I have total power over you, whenever I want it. Agreed?”

    “Y-yes,” Mindy said softly. She wiped another tear away from her face. “I’m s-sorry for doubting you,” she said.

    “That I know,” Alexa said. “Going forward I don’t want you to go ripping up that contract or anything. The only thing I’d like you do to is not be such a conniving bitch. Well, wait. Actually…you know what? You can still be a conniving bitch at your little corporate, climb-the-ladder game. But stop being such a bitch to the models. I know some of them may deserve to be treated badly, especially if they’re like you, but just stop it. Don’t lie to them. Tell them the truth and maybe they won’t hate you. Then you know if you get in trouble, they’ll rally for you. You remember those times when that happened, how the people at the top always caved? Of course you do.” She was definitely rambling at this point. “So just do that and you won’t have to worry about me bothering you, OK?”

    “How w-would you know?” Mindy asked.

    “I could wish to read your thoughts,” Alexa replied. “I’ve already done it once, so I know it works. Not that you need to doubt that after what just happened to you.” Mindy nodded in agreement. “So, I wish that you were wearing your dress again.” The dress reappeared in perfect condition on Mindy’s body. “I wish that you would return to the location you were before you came here, that no one would notice you just reappeared, that you would look as you did when you disappeared from that location, and that you would not tell any other person about what happened here but always remember it yourself so that you will change as you have promised.”

    Mindy disappeared from sight. We were left alone again. “See?” she said to me. “You don’t have to worry about me hurting anyone physically.”

    “Physically being the key word,” I said. “We’re certainly hurting Rick psychologically.”

    “Pfft,” she said. “He deserves it.”

    I had to agree. “Yea, probably. I just don’t want to get too power-mad is all. You said something about helping people after we dealt with Mindy.”

    “And I want to,” she said. “But it’s late and I’m tired.”

    “Back to your place or mine?” I asked.

    She shook her head. “Oh, no,” she said. “I want to spend the night out here. So transform yourself and get ready to go. After that hunt, I need you inside me.”

    I made a wish that no one would notice us in the forest under any circumstances. I changed back to my werewolf form and we fucked again. We wished that we would stay in our werewolf forms until after we woke up in the morning. That way we would not revert in the middle of the night. As I drifted off to sleep, I wondered what we would do next.
