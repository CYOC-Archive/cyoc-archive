    As the couple leaves, Carrie suddenly stops moving.

    “Wait Todd, we shouldn't leave these kids by themselves” said Carrie.

    “Why not?” asked Todd.

    “What would happen if people see a bunch of adults making out in a
    children’s classroom?” asked Carrie.

    Todd thought for a few seconds and it hit him.

    “Oh crud! We better do something about it!” said Todd.

    They then hurry back to there. Luckily, no one saw what was happening
    in the classroom and the ‘adults’ fast asleep, presumably from making
    out so much.

    “I better turn them back to normal” said Todd.

    “Wait, how about we do something first for a few minutes then turn
    them back” said Carrie.

    Todd gives some thought then he gets an idea. Todd presses a few
    buttons on the remote and fires at the class. First, the six women in
    the class have their breasts growing by a cup bigger next their
    bellies start to show. Soon they become nine months pregnant. When
    they reach that mark, sleeping babies suddenly appeared on their
    deflated bellies. Next, Todd returned the class back to their original
    ages and added their original clothing on them. When the process was
    finished, it was like nothing happened.

    “Nice, when they wake up, they’ll think it was just a dream” said
    Todd.

    “But what about the babies, we can’t just leave them” said Carrie.

    “We’ll figure something out” replied Todd.

    Todd uses the remote on two of the babies and Mrs. Brown. They
    disappeared with Mrs. Brown nine months pregnant with twins. Todd then
    regress Mrs. Brown’s pregnancy to one month. The two then carry the
    other four to search for suitable mothers. While in the hallway, they
    see the school nurse walking by, reading her papers. Todd then regress
    two more the babies into the nurse in an instant, leaving her a month
    pregnant with them. When they arrive at the festival, they start
    searching for women.

    "Todd" said Carrie as she nudges him "How about these two?"

    Todd sees a female duo at one of the mini-game booths

    "That will do" said Todd grinning.

    Todd then regresses the last two babies into each woman in an instant.
    Leaving them one months pregnant with a baby.

    “Problem solved” said Todd.
    “Now then, let's have our ‘education’” said Carrie.

    The couple then head to Carrie’s house and have a little make out of
    their own.
