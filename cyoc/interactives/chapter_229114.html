    -thanks again for taking out all this boxes out the attic, honey

    - its no problem grandma, we're almost done here... hey grandma whats this box, you said rising a box the size of a shoe box marked with a label that says confidential CT-ULTRA

    -oh thats some old papers from when your grandpa worked with the goverment back in the 70's, im sure they were declassified by now.

    -So it is that proyect CT-ULTRA, i read about it on the internet, the idea was to create like a psychic super soldier

    - haha thats so silly, but i dont know your grandpa never really told me what he used to do there

    - can i keep this one?

    After you finished helping your grandma clean up her attic, you took the box with you to your apartment, got comfortable in your bed and decided to open it, inside it the was a thick folder with a confidetial label and a smaller box, you decide to start reading the confidential file
    most of it starts with tecnical jargon that you dont understand something about the collective unconscious afecting reality, after that blue prints of weird machines that make no sense to you either, but one blueprint had a note the got your attention the note said functional prototype developed and the prototype looked like a pair of rings or maybe wrist bands , after this blueprint there was a section of experiment logs:

    experiment log 1:
    "today we manage to create a working prototype for the combiner wristbands, our acidental first success occoured when while working on the prototype i touched an apple while holding a pen, this resulted on both items combining into a pineapple, this result is unexpected to say the least but such is the nature of the forces we are working with."

    a pineapple out of an apple and a pen thats crazy, you though and decided to fast foward a couple of pages.

    experiment log 20:
    "Today we had our first experiment with human subjects, while our success in predicting the outcome of given combination of objects or animals have been moderate at best, the higher ups got inpatient and ordered us to proceed with the human experiments, as the goal of the whole proyect its to produce super soldiers and the human voluntiers come from the army, we decided to combine them with a comic book as the biggest representation of what the concept of super is on the collective unconscious, the combination was a success thanks to the security function where both wristbands have to be active for the objects the wearer is touching to combine, unfortunally the result of the combination wasnt the expected, the subject seemed to have loss muscle mass instead of gaining any super power, we decided to keep them in for observation"

    experiment log 21:
    "After 2 weeks in observation the subject of our first human experiment didnt present any of the desired effects quite the contrary not only had their muscle mass decreassed, their vision had gotten worst now requiring glasses and they have develop allergies theres also being a change in personality and interest towards what could be described as nerdy interest, on the good side their average iq seem to have increased, not that it matter as the higher ups seem to think the experiment a failure and the combine bands to be to unpredictable to be of any safe use, they have order the close of this proyect so this is likely my last log entry"

    damn thats crazy for real u though outloud this time, so grandpa created so bands that combine whatever you touch while wearing them uh? you open the smaller box and as expected to metalic wrist bands looking like old analog clocks but instead of a clock head they had just a single power button, well if this crazy things are real you'll need to test them yourself but what to try them on first?

    What do you do now?
