    Annika Rosewood was the school fiend, and everyone knew it.

    But she was different. Annika slept with as many guys as she could, but, for reasons no one could wrap their heads around, she was also a radical feminist. She ran the Feminist Club at the school, chewed out boys (and teachers!) for being sexist, and always did her history reports on powerful women like Susan B. Anthony or Gloria Steinman. But she enjoyed a "little death" as the French call it at least once a week. During a good week, she could get a guy every other night. On a bad week, usually around finals, she got none or only one guy the entire week.

    But for some reason, Annika never did anyone more than two days in a row, even on the weekends. Which meant she had sex at most every other day. Even guys like Dylan Morrow, who had her at least one of the seven days, were never able to convince her to do it on a day where she had had sex the day before. "Sorry, not tonight. Tons of homework." she would tell the crestfallen guy quickly before running off to her next period.

    Despite her love of the craft, she excelled in school, getting straight A's and managing to maintain a 4.0 GPA her first three years of school. But what everyone didn't know was that Annika was a practicer of the dark arts. She knew her magic, and she knew it well. Her mother taught her it, as had her mother before her, and so on, going back to when her ancestors had first arrived on the Mayflower in 1620. Annika's mother and father loved each other, but Mrs. Rosewood made it clear that she was not going to fall into the typical housewife routine. Her and her husband ran the family flooring company together, as partners. She was recognized as the greatest businesswoman in the whole state.

    They lived in a huge mansion overlooking town. The house had over 100 rooms, about 25% of them in the basement for practicing each of the different areas of the dark arts. There were also cages for the "victims" of Annika's practices gone awry. The entrance to the basement was behind a secret door which was behind a bookshelf in the fake basement, which looked like a normal fully furnished one. If you managed to get through that, you had to get through the Rosewood's animal guards, which consisted of many bloodthirsty savages who had been bred for only one thing: killing anything that wasn't the Rosewoods or their approved guests. More than a few burglars to the house had lost their lives that way. Even if you somehow managed to subdue the animals, you'd get hit with a beam of magic that would transform you into either a new bloodthirsty animal for guarding or an abomination of nature that would get locked up in the basement cages. And even if you somehow managed to sidestep all that, the next door fell into a bottomless pit. Nothing could get past that except for the Rosewoods and their approved guests. After that came the lesson rooms.

    One of Annika's hobbies was "punishing" sexist pigs she saw at her school. Her mother, a feminist herself, approved of this hobby wholeheartedly. Some of her sex escapades were just a ruse to seduce and punish the pigs she saw. 1/3 of the pigs were now guarding her magic chambers, 1/3 were locked up in the basement cages, and the remanding 1/3 you don't want to know about.

    So now that you've heard of Annika, her magic, her family, and her house, where would you like to start?
