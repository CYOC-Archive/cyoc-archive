    "Hello, you must be Nancy. Do come in!"

    Nancy was welcomed by a woman in her late 30's, all dressed up for an event that she was going to with her husband, so Nancy had to be the babysitter for their child. Nancy was in her late teens, almost ready to leave home and get a bachelors next year. She was rather causal about the way she looked. She wore a grey beanie, a blue unbuttoned shirt which under that was a plain white singlet, jeans and the shortest pair of boots that she could find. "Thank you, it's quite cold out there" she responded to the mother.

    As she walked into the house, she noticed that it was quite fancy. It wasn't a huge house, but it was the biggest in her neighbourhood, and it looked a bit posh. She was impressed by how they could afford a nice house like this. "Now, we won't be back until midnight, so please help yourself to some food that we have bought just for you. I'll get you introduced to Jamie" the mother said "Jamie, come say hello to your babysitter!".

    Jamie didn't like babysitters. He hated almost every one that came to babysit him. The last one that he had was too strict on him and what he wanted to do. He wanted to stay up like his parents, watching TV and eating ice-cream, he didn't want to sleep. But he smiled about this babysitter, he had something he could do with her. Little did Nancy know, Jamie, this six year old boy, had a power bestowed upon him by a higher power. The power to change someone. Nancy didn't know that he had this power, nor did she know that he was going to use it on her for this whole entire night.

    Jamie came down the stairs carefully, making sure not to trip, to see who his new babysitter was. Nancy smiled at the cute six year old "Hello Jamie!". "Hello" Jamie replied, smiling, knowing that she would be his "playmate". "Anyways, we had better go. Now you take care of yourself, young man. Love you, Jamie" said Jamie's mother as both his parents left to go to their event. Nancy turned to Jamie "Okay, Jamie, what do you want to do?"
