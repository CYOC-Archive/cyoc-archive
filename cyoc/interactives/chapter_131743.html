    "...I think that I want to go with the cinnamon roll." Dave decided.

    "Y-yeah," Daniel muttered, quickly stepping into the machine. Immediately after Daniel stepped in, the machine's door slammed shut behind him. "H-hey!" Daniel shouted. "Don't worry; that's supposed to happen," Laura explained. "Now, brace yourself. You may feel a little pinch."

    Suddenly, mechanical claws shot out of the walls and floor of the machine, clamping themselves onto Daniel's arms and legs. "What the-" Daniel blurted. "Don't worry; that's also supposed to happen," Laura informed. "The process tends to be pretty violent." "W-wait, violent?!" Daniel asked. Laura simply slapped her hand down on a big red button.

    Suddenly, the machine began to spew a strange doughy substance all over Daniel, covering him so quickly that he didn't even have the time to react. "Glargh!" he gurgled, trying his best to spit the gunk out. To Daniel's surprise, the gunk quickly drained off of his body.

    However, the gunk took a few things with it, such as every single one of his defining features. Daniel currently looked like a blank, vaguely human-shaped mold. "Hmmmm?!" Daniel murmured through his now nonexistent mouth. "HMMMM?!" Where were his testicles?! "And now..." Laura muttered, pressing yet another button. Suddenly, the machine began to get a lot hotter.

    "Hmm hmm?" Daniel murmured, blindy glancing around (as his eyes had been melted away, too). It was really hot in the machine, so hot that Daniel felt like he was baking! Apparently, his body felt the same way, as it began to swell from the heat, giving off a particularly strong scent of cinnamon.

    At this point, the mechanical claws began to pose Daniel's featureless body. First, the claws pulled Daniel's fingerless hands up into his chest, as if they were cupping something particularly weighty. Next, the mechanical claws pushed on Daniel's back, forcing Daniel to thrust his chest out. As Daniel's body swelled more and more, the swelling began to congregate in certain areas, particularly at Daniel's hips, thighs, and chest.

With her newly thick thighs and bodacious behind, Daniella was completely a woman, and that was without mentioning her new massive breasts, which were proudly placed on display by her mitten-like hands. "Mmmm!" Daniella murmured, very perturbed by her recent transformation.

    Then the machine's mechanical claws returned, this time weilding various cooking tools. The mechanical claws whipped around Daniella's body, shaping and sculpting all of her features through an excessive amount of frosting. "Mmmmm?!" Daniella muttered confusedly, "Mmmmm Mmmmm?!"

    When the claws finally receded, Daniella's form was complete. Like a cinnamon roll, every part of Daniella's curvy and well-browned body had a swirl of cinnamon travelling around it. Daniella's once featureless face was decorated with frosting, giving her big round frosting eyes and a wide and inviting smile. Daniella's bountiful breasts were now covered in a coat of frosting, giving them a delicious sheen as Daniella's mitten-like hands offered them forward.

    "Ooooo... How delicious!" Laura sang happily, "How do you feel?" "Hmmm mmmm hmmm mmmm!" Daniella tried to shout. However, mouths made of frosting can't really do much more than smile. Daniella tried to step out of the machine, but baked goods don't really walk very well, even if they used to be human. In fact, Daniella could barely move! The best she was able to manage was an alluring wiggle and a few murmurs.

    "Now," Laura began, "Since you didn't really specify a time limit, I assume that you'll want me to keep you like this until you get sold." "Mmmm mmmm!" Daniella murmured, trying her best to shake her head back and forth (the best she could manage was an appealing shake). "Glad to see you're excited about this!" Laura chuckled, "Until you're sold it is!" "Mmmm MMMM!" Daniella tried to shout. Laura walked away and brought back a trolley with a human-sized cage loaded onto it. To Daniella's despair, Laura loaded her into the glass case and wheeled her out of the laboratory.

    "Now, where should I put you?" Laura muttered, thoughfully tapping her chin.
