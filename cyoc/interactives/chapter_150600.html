    Vicky looked at the rows of clothes, as Laura found a red nose... at least... was it a part of the costume? Forget about it now, she had to find a costume and quick. She wanted out of this place as soon as possible. This was easy...

    Or so she thought.

    Her tastes in costumes dictated what she wanted and so many of the costumes looked so, so dull to her. It was like her mind was playing tricks on her. She could have grabbed the first thing and left, but her mind was still choosing.

    "These don't interest me at all!"

    Laura placed a gloved hand on her shoulder. "I'm sure you'll find something, Vicky!" she said as she honked her... nose? Vicky was sure that Laura didn't have one of them with the costume.

    After half an hour, she found something different. A kangaroo costume. That looked... oddly fun to be in. She nervously looked around, wondering if anyone else was there, as she stripped down and got into the costume. Oddly enough, her hair was still visible in the costume, making her look like it was just her as a walking, talking kangaroo. Laura just laughed, in a higher pitch than usual. "You look silly, Vicky! I love it!"

    Vicky tapped her feet in annoyance. "Alright, let's just get out of here!"
