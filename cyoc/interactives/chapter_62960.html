    Wait a second!

    added 12 years ago O

    "Time out, time out," you say with irritation, going so far as to make
    the "T" sign with your hands. "GENIE!!"

    Time seems to pause, and the genie appears. "Yeah?"

    "Dude..this is NOTHING like The Howling. You haven't even seen it
    before, have you?" It hasn't really occurred to you that perhaps
    taking that tone with an immortal of ridiculous power might not be the
    best choice.

    "Actually," he said with an equally irritated (if slightly stoned-
    sounding) tone, "I have. However, I assumed you wanted your own
    ORIGINAL adventure. The Howling was a horror movie with some action.
    You simply reached the action point in your own adventure."

    "But.." you grumble..."What the fuck, dude? Seriously??"

    The genie looks irritated at this point, clearly not a fan of having
    his wish granting powers critiqued.

    "Fine," he said, his eyes narrowing. "I'll give you EXACTLY what you
    want. I'll make DOUBLY sure you get what you wish for."

    "It's about fucking time," you mutter, glancing at the trio of hot
    women, frozen in time who are most certainly NOT werewolves.

    "Alright then. Which Howling movie are you wanting to have happen?" he
    asked with a smirk.

    "Is it the original, where werewolves have a secret colony, which is
    visited by several humans, most of whom do not survive the
    experience?"

    "Is it Number Two, which takes place in Transylvania, where nearly
    EVERYONE is a werewolf, lots of sex occurs, but there's MOST
    DEFINITELY hunters?"

    "Is it Number Three, in Australia, where the werewolves have pouches
    and are hunted by the government, and vulnerable to strobe lights?"

    "Is it Number Four, where a whole town of people are werewolves, and
    are in league with Satan, and a husband and wife are unsuspectingly
    lured in?"

    "Is it Number Five, where there's a solitary, serial-killer type
    werewolf slowly killing off members of a small group of people as they
    try and figure out who it is?"

    His smirk is plastered across his face as he stares at you.

    "Would you rather it be Number Six, where the werewolf is in a circus
    and a prisoner of a vampire who can trigger his transformations?"

    He lets out a small snicker.

    "Or would you rather it be Number Seven, with an unknown werewolf
    killing a whole town of folks in anticipation of her ability to turn
    other people into werewolves?"

    What do you do now?
