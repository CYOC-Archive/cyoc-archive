    The other girls looked at her, a little dubious but after a moment Claudia brightened up. "Hey, there's that frozen movie. My neice was watching it one day and she loved it. Why don't we rent that and a few other things!"

    Katie nodded, turning to Mary Elise. "Mom left us some money, will it be enough?"

    Mary looked to it on the counter, "Not really but I have an idea. Do you still have my purse?"

    "Yeah, why?" Katie asked.

    "Well if its changed to suit my old age, I had a bunch of money in there, heck I had a credit card and all that. Maybe we could use that to order things. I mean, its not like I need the money too much anymore if I'm a kid."

    "That's actually kinda a good idea," Samantha had to admit.

    Katie nodded and once Mary got her purse our her suspicions were confirmed. Everything else had been changed to suit her new age, but a quick wish and the bag reverted from its youthful vibrant color to something an older woman would have carried. Mary got out the cash and went to make the phonecall for the deliveries. "Lucky for us theres a place that does pizza and movie deliveries!"

    Katie nodded. "Mom's got a lot of ice cream and other stuff too! This is great! We just have to wait for the delivery person to arrive.

    "Alright, let's go watch some cartoons, while we wait." Tricia said.

    Katie nodded and started after them, and the girls gathered in front of the tv. After a minute or two though, Katie was surprised to find how fascinated the littler girls were and how bored she was. These same cartoons would have been great if she was a ten year old, but now as a teen, she found herself wanting to watch different things. She looked to the ring and felt its presence in her mind, telling her that this was natural due to her changed age.

    She pondered a moment, considering this. If the others were little girls for the weekend, why couldn't she be an adult. A moment later the doorbell rang and Mary Elise answered, and brought in no less than four pizzas and a pile of movies. She'd spent quite a bit of money, grinning. "Ok girls! Lets party!"

    The girls swarmed the food and Mary Elise turned to katie. "Ok Katie, time to make ourselves kids again."

    Katie looked to her and nodded, smiling, and then made her wish.
