    Early, the next morning came that you were awoken to a scratching sound on your door. Opening the door to see who it was at the door that you hear your sister. "Jeff come here, please! I need help!"

    following your sister down the hall, You see her walks into the bathroom and turns on the lights. you began as to wonder what's the matter with Suzi that you trailed off a moment, but your eyes were soon lock on her face. "What did you do to yourself???"

    Her face was shining a sparkly dark shade of black. She had a little streaks of red hair and fur painted across her face. "I wanted to have my face painted like you! so I went to your room and found this face painting kit in the pocket of your clown costume. when I took it downstairs to my sleepover party that we all had fun painting our faces on what we wanted, but I can't wash it off of my face now." she said as she gave off a neigh.

    "Uh oh, We need to get mom." you say. Running downstairs that you burst quickly open into your parents room.

    "Mom, mom! we need help!" you yell in the room.

    "Roooaaar! GRRRRRR!" you hear two growls break the silence. flipping on The lights that and you see your mom, painted head to toe as a lion as you soon see your dad painted head to toe as a bear.

    "What Happened?!" you and your mom exclaim. your brother soon enters as he too was now painted head to toe as a dalmatian.

    "Suzi found paint in my clown costume. did this to herself and the others over her sleepover party." you said, word for word on what your sister said to you.

    "Your dad found paint in the room where the sleepover was. he thought he'd surprise me." your mom said.

    before you could ask on any way to take it off, your mom pulls out her makeup remover from her purse. "Try this. This stuff should be able to take off this stubborn makeup." she said.

    one by one, you, Your brother, Suzi, and your parents went into the bathroom. You squirt the solution on a washrag and scrub your face vigorously. each of you Look back at the mirror that the paint wouldn't come off.

    you see that your clown makeup still hasn't budged. Suzi was scrubbing her face with the makeup remover as she still Looked like a painted pony horse she always wanted.

    "Am I gonna look like a this forever? I can't wait to go to school this morning!" she asks you as she once again, but louder, gave a 'NEIGH!'

    it was moments later as we figured to use to our new painted selves. "Breakfast is ready! come and GRRRReated, it smells GRRRReat!" mom yells.

    walking into the kitchen, you see all of Suzi's friends coming up from basement as each of their faces was painted like some type of creature. There's a raccoon, a black goat, a jackal, a tiger, a rat, a rabbit, a red fox, a giraffe, a snow leopard, and a golden zebra. you sigh out loud as to wonder what their parents will say, or even think when they see their children have been transformed permanently by Suzi in to animals.

    once we ate our meal that your bear dad gave a quick lick on your mother's check as he was ready for work, before having him return back for a real kiss on the lips. "go get big boy." she said softly and with that, he was off to work.

    "Well kids, I think you all look great! There's nothing to be worried about." mom says.

    "But mom, I'm a clown! I can't wash it off. how am i going to explain this to everyone at school! What'll the kids say?" you whine.

    "It's show and tell today isn't it? Take the paint with you and say the facepaint is your show and tell. Ask for volunteers and paint their faces too. That way, you won't be the only one! maybe they'll like it." your mom said. it wasn't long till the bus arrives to pick you kids up for school as you all were loaded up to be taken to school.
