    added A year ago AR Baby

    Lizzy thought that the best thing would be to try to find some way out that could help her escape, but to do so she had to find a way to escape from this baby walker. It wouldn't be an easy task since her feet didn't reach the ground properly, but she still wanted to try.

    It was then that Lizzy tried several times to get out of the baby walker using all of her baby strength. But no matter how hard she tried, it didn't work since she just ended up bouncing on the walker.

    The next thing Lizzy tried to do was try to swing from one side so that the walker could fall. And although she was able to move the baby walker a little, at the same time it only ended up making a lot of noise because of the multiple toys that were in the walker. Lizzy hadn't noticed the toys before so out of pure curiosity she played with them a little. It was then that Lizzy began to forget her main objective and began to get distracted in the walker.

    A while passed, and Lizzy was still playing. But it wasn't until she heard a voice that she finally stopped what she was doing.

    "Awwwwww, what a cute baby"

    At that moment, Lizzy turned around and was surprised to see that it was a 30-year-old red-haired woman carrying a camping backpack. And not only that, the witch was also next to her but now she was wearing casual clothes and her hair was now more colorful than before.

    "She's very cute, right? She just turned 1 year old", the witch told her.

    "Really? Then she's definitely going to look beautiful when she grows up", the woman told the witch.

    "I'm sure so. By the way Olivia, could you do me a favor? Could you put Lizzy in her crib. I need to go get her food."

    "Sure”, Olivia replied.

    So while the witch proceeded to leave, Olivia began to walk towards Lizzy and began to lift her out of her baby walker. Lizzy wasn't sure who this person was and why she was here. Although one thing she was sure of was that she certainly didn't like her babytalking.

    "Who's a chubby baby? Yes, you are. Yes, you are", Olivia said as she tickled Lizzy. Lizzy tried to resist but she ended up laughing out loud. Olivia continued pampering Lizzy until she finally decided to put her in the crib as the witch had instructed her.

    "Your mother is really lucky to have a beautiful baby. Although I'm surprised that only you are here. I wonder where her other daughter is?", Olivia commented.

    Lizzy was surprised to hear what the lady said. What would she have meant by another daughter? But before she could figure it out, the witch had returned and she came with some baby bottles and some plates that contained small pancakes, small slices of banana, blueberry and scrambled egg.

    Lizzy noticed that she was carrying two bottles and two plates. Did she plan to feed her twice? Or is it...oh no. Was she really going to do what she thought she was going to do?

    "Thank you for placing her in her crib", the witch told her as she proceeded to place the food on a small table.

    "No problem. By the way, speaking of my room..."

    "Oh yeah, I forgot. There aren't enough rooms here so you'll have to stay with Lizzy", the witch replied.

    "What? I mean, I wouldn't mind sleeping in a nursery but it doesn't seem right to stay in your daughters' room", Olivia responded.

    "Don't worry. I'm sure none of them will mind you staying here"

    "Really? But where will I sleep? I don't think I can fit in the crib"

    "That can be easily fixed", the witch replied as she proceeded to take out her little wand again and tapped Olivia on the head.

    Just like Lizzy, Olivia was transformed into a 1-year-old baby girl who now wore an orange onesie patterned with oranges, little orange socks, and a diaper.

    Lizzy was horrified after witnessing how that woman had been reduced into a baby like her. She even watched as the witch placed the now baby Olivia inside the crib next to her.
