    "What's up?"

    "Maybe they've just discovered THEY've got tails," Josh mutters.

    Jesse shakes his head. "They haven't. Something else is going on..." Reaching down to tuck his tail behind his knee, he starts heading for the door. "Let's see what it is, yeah?"

    "But..." Josh frowns uncertainly.

    "I'm not staying here all day. Come on!"

    "What d'you mean?" Josh asks, as Jesse jumps from the cabin steps to the gravel path three feet away.

    "Or maybe it's them," Jesse muses. "Whatever, I know I gotta watch this..."
