    Karina came to me saying that there was a couple wanting my services and they were waiting for me in a separate room, commenting that it was a couple in which both, the man and the woman, were very tall.

    When I arrived in the room and saw the two, who at this time removed their face masks and stared at me, I froze and a dread took over me and I remembered everything, and I mean the two months I was abducted. Those tall people with bluish skin and completely orange eyes, they were those aliens who had abducted me. The man stood at his probable 10 feet tall while the woman was 8.5 feet.

    Having recovered my memory, I now remember that when they abducted me they sent me directly to their home planet via a teleportation device, and there I was held captive while they studied me. They weren't hurting me or anything, but I feared when the moment would come when they would dissect me and that's why I took advantage of a slip by one of them and managed to escape captivity.

    I remember so well now how escaping was the worst thing I could have done. I got lost in an alien jungle on a planet with an ecosystem totally different from Earth. That red sky where the night was almost non-existent thanks to the two suns that illuminated the planet, the purple vegetation and animals that I had never seen. I was lost for what I thought was days, despite not knowing how to calculate the time being there, and a big problem hit me, hunger. Fortunately I had found water on that planet, but I had no idea what was edible. In desperation I picked some fruits from a tree that reminded me of oranges, but purple in color, and ate them until I felt full. I don't know if it was because I was starving, but the fruit seemed very tasty, but after eating I felt weak and drowsy, losing consciousness shortly after.

    Having no idea how long I was unconscious, when I regained consciousness I found that I had been found and recaptured, and the aliens were discussing something in their native language, so I didn't understand anything. I believe that was when they decided it was time for me to be returned, as it didn't seem to take more than hours for me to be teleported back to the ship near Earth and put to sleep, only to wake up again in my house with no memory of my time with them.

    Noticing my reaction to seeing them, Becca and Karina became defensive and prepared to protect me.

    "Hold on, we mean no harm to you." the alien man said

    "We're just here to confirm some worrying information we've received, so we need you to answer a few questions so we can decide what action to take." the alien woman said

    "Please don't take me again." I begged, completely terrified

    "As long as we don't come to the conclusion that it's dangerous the situation won't happen at all, but as it happened due to his exposure to our home planet's atmosphere we couldn't just ignore it." the alien woman said

    "I don't believe you. Look how Jane is doing, you must have done something to her that traumatized her." Karina said

    "This was another mistake of ours for not learning to communicate sooner. The time she was in our custody in a controlled environment, which simulated the atmosphere of this planet, we tried to make sure she was safe, but we were unable to understand each other as we didn't know each other's language. Seeing her fear and how she nearly died fleeing we thought it best to return her to her home without her memories of her time with us. Even so, there is something in the minds of earthlings that has not allowed our technology to erase the memory completely, just block it, and just seeing us it seems that this block has been broken." the alien woman said

    I still needed help calming down enough for us to talk properly. When I felt better the aliens started the main topic of their visit.

    "Are you feeling better now? So to begin with, as is the custom among Earthlings, I'll introduce myself first. My name is Ztok and I am an emissary of the federation of the planet Neyo." the alien man said

    "And I'm Kmit, his assistant. As you can see, before contacting you this time, we made a point of learning your language and culture." the alien woman said

    "Aren't you lying that you didn't come just to capture me?" I said

    "No, if we wanted to do that we would have done it without any difficulty." Ztok said

    I felt safer after that and answered several questions. In the interrogation we learned that the Neyans wanted to approach the earthlings and after confirming that they could survive without problems on our planet, they took me to their planet and they were exposing me little by little to the elements there, always checking the effects they had on me. me, but full exposure to the atmosphere, water and the fruit I ate took a toll on my body and is probably what made my power start to develop.

    They came to the conclusion that I wasn't dangerous and that since I wasn't using my power to change people against their will, they didn't have a problem, just as there was no law on their planet or on Earth, so they would let me continue with what I was doing, but that there would be visits from them from time to time to confirm that everything was still in order and to see that the changes taking place in me would not become dangerous for myself.

    It's hard to believe that now even aliens are coming to visit me, but at least now I know better where my powers come from. Now I take my leave by the hour. Where will this stop? I suspect there will be a big change in the coming years.
