    Twister, Blaze, Quake, and Silver are now resting on one of the high
    iron peaks of Ignis Ile, watching the sunset. They had just finished
    crossing the burning dessert an hour ago and are exhausted. Well, at
    least Twister and Silver are. Blaze and Quake are not because the
    elements of the island have strengthened them. Silver is just now
    starting to feel that strength from the iron mountains.

    Silver, while gasping for air, commented, "This island is huge! And
    that glowing cave up ahead...it's ominous looking."

    Twister replied, "That cave is called Devil's Eye, because of the
    fiery glow in the day and the ominous glow at night. And
    unforutnately, the source of magic must be inside the there."

    Blaze asked, "How do you know?"

    "Simple, I can feel the incredible power of the source. Open yourself,
    and you will be able to feel it."

    Blaze and the other two closed their eyes and opened themselves to the
    world around them. They could feel the power of the island rush into
    them, and they could sense the overwhelming power of the source in the
    cave. "Alright everyone, lets head for the cave." Twister yelled as
    she took off toward it.

    Once they were in side, they had to land. The cave got so narrow, that
    they could not fly, and they had to walk almost in single file. In
    what seemed like hours, they finally made it through the long tunnel
    to the belly of the cave. Which turned out to be a giant pull of
    molten iron. The power of the source was incredibly powerful inside
    that cavern.

    Quake commented, "We must be right on top of the source. Who wants to
    take a molten bath?"

    "I'm afraid none of you will live long enough to." Said a mysterious
    voice. Ahead of them, the shadows on the ground rushed to them. With
    no time to react, the group was ensnared in chains made of shadows.
    The same voice began laughing as a giant black dragon rose from the
    shadows. Shade had captured them...
