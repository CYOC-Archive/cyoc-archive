    Sitting in the back of the store was an old looking machine; in fact, it looked like a miniature cash register.

    On the left of the machine was a slot machine like lever. In the middle were a bunch of uniquely ornate switches; these were the settings and the lever was the “enter” key. The back of the machine had a key slot; above it, it said “reversal”

    The machine’s settings were varied; one of the switches was ethnicity, which was the longest toggle, compared to some of the smaller switches that simply had two toggles. One of these was the “awareness” switch; it simply had on and off, and the “sexuality” switch, which simply had “Bisexual” and “Lesbian” modes.

    Of course, Joe didn’t know any of this when he saw it in the magic shop. When he saw it, though, he thought it was beautiful. “There’s got to be a ton of gold here. If I can get it for a good price, I can probably flip it.” He said, picking it up, before he noticed the perverted writing on the side. “Ohh! I bet a creep’ll pay a good amount of money for this.”


    He went to the front desk, lugging the heavy machine with him, and finally put it down.


    “Hmm, a nice purchase indeed! In fact, I have some instructions that go with it. However, I’m sorry to say I don’t have the reversal key that came with it.” The cashier said, handing Joe the booklet.


    “That’s okay. How much do I owe you?” Joe asked, not really caring.

    “Well, since there’s no reversal key, I don’t think I should charge you. Enjoy it! But be careful.” The shopkeeper said.

    Joe couldn’t believe his good luck!

    “Oh thank you so much! I will enjoy it!” Joe said, leaving the store.

    “*sigh* I guess that’s another mess I’ll have to clean up eventually...” the shopkeeper said as soon as Joe was out of sight.
