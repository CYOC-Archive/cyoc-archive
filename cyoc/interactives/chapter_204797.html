    After waiting for a day for Susan to act on her urges so that she can finally jump bodies, Jen decided to take the matter in her own supposed hands and as soon as the second day of school ended she made Susan go directly home. Of course she did forced her to act normal and made her make all her homework and do all her chores as an excuse for her family to not suspect anything of why she decided to ditch cheer practice and hanging out with the squad after that.
    For Susan, altough weird at first, the situation made sense even if she didn't knew what possessed her to do this, after all she was the richest girl in town, even before the curse with Jen, and thus didn't need good grades, just good enough to pass, her family wealth will pay for the rest of her life.
    After all the things done, Jen made Susan go to her room for the night and made her extremely sleepy so she can work on transforming her and finishing the conditions for her to jump to the next host.
    First she gave her a wet dream about becoming the new queen of an alien race and loving it all, little did Susan knew that by the time morning will come she will be that queen. If you wonder why she loved all of it, well Jen always knew how to push the right buttons when it come to Susan, so she kinda liked that she was her first victim.
    During that dream Susan actually ended up transforming about a little over 50% of her body which left Jen with a choice, keep her like the anthropomorphic version of the alien queen, with 6 arms that looked somewhat insectile, 5 legs to match, an egg filled abdomen, tentacle hair that can be used to transform new victims into drones, insect mandibles outside her somewhat still human mouth full of fangs placed like those of a lampray, slited insectile eyes similar to those of a mantis shrimp and back limbs similar to those of a praying mantis, and focus on the mental changes or keep going to full queen and then make her mind match the body
