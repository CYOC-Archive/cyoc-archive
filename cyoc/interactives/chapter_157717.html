It's hypothesized that subjects return back to their human forms after a certain time period passes, and transform back into werebeasts every night.

    What the scientists didn't know was that the substance was already sent to numerous locations, and would soon come into contact with more individuals. Who will be the unfortunate or fortunate to come into contact with the substance?
