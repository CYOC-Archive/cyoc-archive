Experience had taught her that if she became a kid without the others they would ignore her and she really enjoyed being around them. Throughout the night Kellie kept reminding Katie she was the baby sister. Katie had found that annoying and wondered if it would continue if they were both younger. If Katie decided to do that should Mika and Claudia join Kellie in little sisterhood or should Katie keep them for herself. The possibilities were endless.
