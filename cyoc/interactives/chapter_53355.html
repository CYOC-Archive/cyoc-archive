    Daniel put his hand over Alex's mouth, "Anyways, like I was trying to say before he opened his mouth..." Daniel gave Alex a glare, "Josh went to go find it."

    "I see..." Owen said and nodded his head.

    No one said anything for a few minutes, but Daniel and Alex were still grunting and nudging each other. They were trying to get as much distance between them, and try to get comfortable, but they both knew they it would never happen as long as they were conjoined.

    "Ya... at least you two don't constantly fight." Daniel said, "And it's hard not to when your attached to this guy!"

    "Ya! You're lucky..." Alex commented as he and Daniel exchanged another set of pushing, "You're stuck father and son. While I have to be with this asshole."

    Kyle and Owen watched as Daniel and Alex managed to push each other off the couch and onto the floor. They imminently started to wrestle with each other, and banged into the coffee table. A glass that had been sitting on it fell off and broke.

    "Oh good one!" Daniel shouted, "Now look at what you've done!"

    "Hey, you pushed me..." Alex retorted, "It's your fault!"

    "Stop it!" Kyle yelled. He and Owen had gotten up and were staring down at the conjoined pair. "First off, both of you broke it. You're together in a body for crying out loud!"

    "Yup." Owen nodded beside Kyle, "And, we aren't happy to be stuck like this either."

    "And it hasn't exactly been smooth sailing for us either." Kyle finished. He and Owen started to walk to the kitchen to grab the broom, but Kyle had turned to the bathroom. The two of them jerked back and forth for a second as they caught their balance. "Where are you going?"

    "To get the broom..." Owen stated, and began pulling Kyle to the kitchen.

    Kyle grabbed onto the back of a chair and pulled the other way, "It's in the bathroom." Owen shook his head and sighed. They went to the bathroom and came back with a broom. Daniel and Alex watched as Kyle and Owen tried unsuccessfully to sweep the broken glass. They were mumbling to themselves as they went to grab the dustpan.

    They had cleaned the mess and gave each other dirty looks. When they came back to the living room, they sat down beside Daniel and Alex, who had worked together long enough to sit back on the couch. Kyle looked over at the other pair, "And I don't think it's lucky being stuck father and son..."
