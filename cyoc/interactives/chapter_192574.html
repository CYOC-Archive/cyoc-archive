    Husband and Wife

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago AP O Mythological Magic

    When Lilly walked out the door, Dan noticed that she was wearing a white dress this time. She delicately walked down the steps to meet up with Dan, and kissed him.

    Dan and Lilly flirted with each other, holding hands, telling jokes all the way until they reached Lilly’s house.

    “Well the party you invited me to tonight is at like 8? Before then, in like an hour, Kat is having her party. Want to come to that too?”

    Dan did want Lilly to come, just so they could spend even more time together. However, the words didn’t intentionally come out of his mouth. It was as if someone else forced him to ask her that.

    Lilly responded with “Absolutely! You can hang out with me here until then if you want.” The word “absolutely” came out of Lilly as if she was forced to say it too, even if it was something she was going to say anyways.

    Dan spent the next hour watching a movie with Lilly’s head on his shoulders, holding hands, and cuddling. When it was time to go to Kat’s party, Lilly’s hair and makeup looked as if a professional worked on it.

    With each step Lilly took towards Dan’s house, her dress looked more and more long and formal. When they entered the door, they saw something they didn’t quite expect.

    The party looked like a different dimension, with magic creatures and family members performing magic all over the place. Music he never heard of before was blasting. Dan recognized many female family members, with a lot of his male family members looks like different mythological beings. They all suddenly turned to Dan and Lilly.

    “Everyone come and look to see the results of Kat’s first spell. Per family tradition, she casted a spell on Dan that would transform him into his inner self. Some of us here are results of this tradition too. In ten seconds after Kat comes out in front, we will see the final transformation. Kat, come up here!” you hear your uncle say. Though he looks like a gnome.

    Kat stepped forward, smiling. The crowd counted down from ten.

    As the crowd chanted “4, 3, 2,” Dan felt something form in his pocket.

    “1!”

    Dan immediately pulled out a ring box from his pocket. Lilly gasped in excitement. “Lilly, my darling, I love you more than anything in the world. Will you be my wife?”

    “Oh my gosh yes! I do! Yes yes yes!” Lilly cheered. It was at that moment when Dan realized Lilly was in a wedding dress.

    “And so, Dan’s inner self is… a loving husband to Lilly. Wow, I would’ve never guessed,” Kat said.

    The entire party cheered! Kat received many congrats on a successful spell while Lilly and Dan received many congrats on Dan’s proposal.

    Kat was a little jealous that Dan got so much attention on her birthday, but part of her was a little happy. She never really liked Dan, but maybe he wasn’t as bad as she thought.
