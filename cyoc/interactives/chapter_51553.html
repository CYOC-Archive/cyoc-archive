    Katie, trapped in Jimmy's body, had just arrived at the pool. She saw her car parked closer to the entrance, and so obviously they had gotten there first. It took her awhile to get used to handling Beth's car, and Jimmy's body. Even more of a challenge, however, was how to get her body back. Katie had been able to see Jimmy when she uttered her unknowingly potent words. She assumed she had to do the same again, as she had tried wishing on the way over and nothing had happened. She realized after these failed attempts that she was actually somewhat grateful that it had not succeeded, since she was in the middle of executing a turn off a busy street. As much as she was clearly pissed that Jimmy was playing dumb about stealing her body, she didn't want to kill him...he was pretty cute, after all.

    She had caught a break, though. Jimmy was obviously distracted by his new body, as he had dropped the ring on the way out to the car. Katie had noticed it gleaming on the off-white concrete. Just as she noticed it the first time, it seemed to glimmer almost as if it were trying to get her attention...

    She was leaning on the car, facing it, with her arms crossed and her head flat on the hood of the car, probably looking quite distressed to any passersby.

    "Oh well," she thought, "I have to try. I mean, I can't stay in this body...I don't know how to be a guy!"

    She lifted her head off the car, and began to take a deep breath in preparation of her attempt, when a new thought entered her mind. Well, more accurately, a new option entered her eye.

    A pointed *blip-blip* of a high-end sedan, gratefully announcing that its doors had been remotely knocked, drew Katie's attention to the source of the sound. Two girls from three grades above her and Beth, Carrie Queen and Quinn Powers, had just arrived, likely with the same idea as Beth and 'Katie.' Katie had never experienced sexual attraction from a male perspective before, but boy did it feel good...distracting, frustrating, and a little uncomfortable (until she finally stopped staring, urged by a creeping hardness in her shorts), but still somehow...great.

    The two girls weren't the most gorgeous girls she had ever seen, but each had their appeal, and Carrie, since she turned 16, had been rapidly coming into herself. The two had been best friends since she could remember, and had always been big on swimming and (beach) volleyball. The two were shoe-ins for the High School Volleyball team, especially now that Carrie had grown a couple inches (though not just in height).

    Maybe it was spurned on by the natural enthusiasm of her new body. Maybe she had always been a little jealous of Carrie. But Katie suddenly wasn't so worried about how she was going to get her old body back anymore...

    "Here goes nothing ..." she thought, and then spoke aloud, with all the confidence she could muster "I wish I had Carrie's hot body" ...
