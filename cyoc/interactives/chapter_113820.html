    Mark decided that was enough of being his deceased mother, it was time to return to normal.

    ''I wish I was no longer my mother, and that I was Matt again''
    He started shrinking, as was to be expected, but he shrinked much beyond what he was before, and he saw the world change around him, seemed to change into a kindergarten. Why this had happend he simply couldn't figure out, but then he came up with a theory, perhaps you can't say ''and'' after a wish, and since he only wished to no longer be his mother, he became a random person...? That seemed to be the only plausible conclusion

    He looked down and realized he was extremely young, and still a girl, based on the pink dress he had on, he even felt a thick underwear, and raised his dress up, only to realize he was wearing a pink pull up with princesses on it. He tried to walk around a little and, while he could do it, it was atcually pretty hard without falling over. He decided enough was enough, and should quickly make a wish that would return him to normal, he atcually realized he hadn't even checked if he still had the wand, but luckily it seems it transports with the person who makes a wish, he still had it.

    However, before he can make a wish, a girl that appears to be 5 years old comes over to him, and he realises just how small he atcually is.
    ''I'm playing dress up with my friends and need that wand'' She says, Matt decides not to answear her for now, since he only needs to think of a good wish, and just as he's about to make a wish, she just rips the wand out of his hand, and runs away!

    This was bad. He needed to get that wand back, or else he'd be stuck as a little girl!
