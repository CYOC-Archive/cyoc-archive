    added by Elflover A month ago S Shrinking Magic

    "No! Let go of me!" you scream as you are hoisted in the air.
    This is terrifying! You're meters above ground! Well, meters now, because it wouldn't be that bad if you were still of a human size. Now, you're minuscule and it makes a BIG difference!
    "I can't, or else you'll crash for sure! Learn to enjoy it. You're with a pretty fairy now, me!" she says as if what you'd said was the stupidest thing in the world.
    "Change me back! I don't want to be with a fairy! I'm a human male and I want to remain so! So please change me back! This instant! I don't want to learn how to be tiny! And I want my clothes back too!" you shout in what you think is righteous indignation.
    "But it's much better than being a big stinky hairy man! You should thank me!" she says back with a frown.
    "I want to be a normal-sized guy! What do you know anyway? You're a fairy and..."
    You cannot finish your sentence because you're suddenly pulled out of the fairy's grasp. A male fay creature caught you in his big hand! And his smile doesn't look very benevolent.
    "A tiny transformed human! What luck!" he says happily.
    "Release that mortal this instant!" says the fairy angrily, buzzing around furiously.
    "Oh, it's definitely not a fairy, although he possesses the size of a fairy. I heard him. It's a human male all right. His penis and balls are further proofs of this. And humans are so much easier to transform that other fay creatures like you and me," says the creature, holding you in his hand firmly. "So you are going to change him further according to my own specifications. Or I'll squash you or hurt you."
    The fairy pouts but then nods.
    "Good! We're on the same terms. Here's what I want..."
    You gulp. Uh-oh...
