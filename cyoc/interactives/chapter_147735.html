    Damon starts to wake up and realize it wasn't a dream. He was going to start his new life.

    He begins to pack up his bedroll and place it on Sebastian. He then feed his horse a carrot and pets him.

    "It's a new day for us Sebastian. A better life for me and you." Sebastian was his only friend when he was a prince.

    Sebastian remain loyal to the young prince as he get's on his stallion and starts to ride off.

    In a few minuets. Damon comes upon a village. Realizing he'll need to find a new place to live.

    He tied up his horse to a post and heads into the inn. He sat, by a table, as a women sat by him.

    "Never seen your face before. What's your name?" Damon knew he couldn't use his real name. So he came up for a new name for him.

    "Colton." He replied. "Nice to meet you Colton. My name is Elizabeth. Tell me are you new here?"

    Damon, or Colton, nods his head. "I'm just looking for a new home to live in." Said Colton.

    "I see. Well don't you worry. I'll show you around and I'm sure we'll find a nice home for you." Said Elizabeth.

    "Thank you." Said Colton.
