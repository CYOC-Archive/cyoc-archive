    "But I wasn't bitten by a Werewolf," you said perplexed.

    "Well I see that I need to give you a little lesson," Mary said with a slight smile. "At one time there were no Werewolves but there have always been Hell Hounds as far as we can tell. One day a man was walking at night and he came across a Hell Hound which attacked him. The man was able to kill the hell hound but he was mangled. The doctors treated him and he lived but when the full moon came he transformed into a huge wolf like a Hell Hound. The only difference between a Werewolf and a Hell Hound is the Werewolf's eyes do not glow and they can walk on two or four legs. This first Werewolf was just as savage as the Hell Hounds and he killed many people including babies before he was finally killed. A few of the people he had attacked survived and they in turn became Werewolves themselves and spread it to more people. Some of them did have children who were born Werewolves and some of them had children and so on. With each successive generation they seemed to hold onto more of their human minds when they transformed. Now today you can find Werewolves that are the mindless violent type, those that have their human minds while transformed, and those between the two."

The second part involves you soaking in another potion and then we wait until the first night of the full moon to see if you'll become a Werewolf and if you'll retain your human mind if you do."

    "Yes I have," Mary said. "Jade, this is my husband Nick and Lily and Krystal are our daughters."

    "What happened to them?" you asked.

    "Come and see for yourself," Mary said and went to the back door.

    You followed Mary out back and two statues of girls, one appeared to be about eleven or twelve and the other appeared to be about six or seven. The odd thing was that they were wearing ordinary clothes. "Why are those statues wearing actual clothes?" you asked.

    "Those statues are Lily and Krystal," Mary said sadly. "Those are the clothes they were wearing when they met a Gorgon. A Gorgon's gaze changes flesh to stone but not clothes which is why they look like that. Most people believe that once you're turned to stone by a Gorgon you're dead and can't be made flesh again. However I found a book that claims a potion can be made to bring them back to life. One of the ingredients is a Gorgon's blood but it must be from the Gorgon that turned the person to stone. So far the potions I made haven't worked. Maybe the potion never worked but I'm not giving up until I've tried all the potions I can. Now I need to prepare the potion so Nick will help you into your potion."

Well he died because of that mistake and a villager had seen it and thought the potion was actual silver. That's how that myth began."

    "I'm sure you want to know about the park," Nick said. "Well the entrances appear at different points in the world at different times. Inside the park the possibility of being transformed is quite high. There's a mall, a carnival, a mansion, an all boys school, an all girls, school, a playground, and others. Each of them has various means to transform you. Also various creatures in the park can transform you too such as the Gorgons and Werewolves.
