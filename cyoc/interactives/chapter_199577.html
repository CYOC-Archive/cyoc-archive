    You start swinging your head wildly to get your father out of your maw. You're partly successful as the slimy contours of your father slowly are dragged out of your maw. As you thrash your head with all your might your father finally escapes your gaping maw. You can't be thankful enough, you managed to free him, maybe he will survive somehow.

    Or so it appeared, because in reality it was your captor who saved your father, he took him out of your maw by the tail and put your father into a fish bowl. Either way, your father was safe... for now.

    Two scientists came to you and picked you up together because you were quite big. They moved you into a big metal cage that was in the back of the room all the time. As they went out of the cage they complained about your mass and the fact that you should have been transformed into a smaller snake. Sadly you didn't know what you became specifically.

    You raised your head off the floor to see what's going on with your father and mother. You slightly backed further into the cage as one of the scientists walked over to you. He knelt down to be at your eye level and started explaining: "Because of your mother's choices you have been transformed into a female Burmese boa. Now you will choose what your mother will be transformed into. If you won't comply, we will choose for you randomly. Perhaps if you will behave during further tests and games we might let you go. That's all I've wanted to tell you." With that the man slowly stood up and left the room leaving you in the room alone with your father in the fishing bowl.

    What's more frightening is that he mentioned you becoming a female, so it was true. Not only did you become a snake, but also a female... Can you be any more humiliated? Now you're forced to choose an animal into which your mother will painfully transform, this is the worst day of your life.
