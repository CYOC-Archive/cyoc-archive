    In a few days and after a few checkups, it is now safe for Ashley to go home. Amber then makes up a story to everyone, saying that she and her daughter will be going to Europe to return home. It convinced everyone and they wished for the best.

    The next day, Amber with the regenerx, a sleeping Ashley and Michael on a bed; ready for Amber and Ashley to switch back.

    “Well, this is it” said Amber.

    “Yeah” said Michael with some sadness and happiness “I’ll have to take care of you like a baby now but I’ll have my wife back”

    “In a few years, I’m sure we’ll talk about this little adventure” said Michael.

    Amber looked at her dad and said “Goodbye dad“ then she looked at her mother and says “Goodbye mom”

    Amber then drinks some of the regenerx and connected with her mother. First, she checks on Ashley, fortunately, Ashley remembers her time in the womb and everything else.

    “Good, she remembers everything” said Amber.

    Amber then transfers her age to Ashley.

    Baby Ashley was growing bigger from a toddler then to a 5 year old kindergartner. For Michael, it was vice-versa déjà vu. At the same time Amber regressed back to a teenaged girl once more. Her breasts started shrinking as she regresses. Amber then paused in her transfer for a moment, she was now a child of 12 years old and so was Ashley. Amber looked at her mother as a child.

    “So this is me as kid”, said Amber with wonder “Mom sure is pretty”.

    Amber then transfer started again. Ashley became a teenager with puberty hitting her fast. For Amber, puberty disappeared completely as she re-enters childhood. Ashley’s hair darkened slightly as she passed into high school age. Meanwhile Amber’s hair lightened as she grew closer to kindergarten age. Baby fat began to appear on Amber’s limbs as she shrank into early childhood. When Amber paused again, she was now 4 years old and Amber was 20. Little Amber waived at her father, signing goodbye for now. Michael noticed that Amber’s breasts had started leaking milk. When he went back to check up on Ashley, she was back to being a newborn infant.

    Ashley soon wakes up and sees that she is an adult once more and looking better than ever. She is a couple of inches taller and has bigger breasts. Ashley guessed that Amber took real good care of her. Michael and Ashley then hugged from the joy of being reunited.

    “Welcome back honey” said Michael with tears.

    The two then stopped hugging each other.

    “So you got what you wanted?” asked Michael.

    “I did, now I have the ideas and drive for my next novel” replied Ashley.

    Over a few days, everyone knows Ashley returned with her daughter and everything was back to normal. Amber wrote her womb experience and called it “The nine months journey in the womb”. It soon became a bestseller and the book of the year. Ashley couldn't be more pleased.
