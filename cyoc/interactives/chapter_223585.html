    The room you had rented for the vacation was the Age Room. All visitors had to do was select the age they wanted to become from a machine and gulp the corresponding pill. A second red and yellow pill was offered too so as to cancel the effects of the first. It was simple and you thought it would be great to try. You and your girlfriend were 25 both and of age but you thought it'd be great to see each other as little kids and older people. What was best, there were rooms beyond dedicated to the ages of the visitors, once the pills were swallowed. So there was a daycare, special rooms for toddlers, children and even a wing resembling a retirement home! Even better, there were special rooms with crazy decorations for those who wanted to try put kinks and sexual things once they were older than 18. Because everyone had to be at least eighteen to participate, naturally. You did not want to admit it to Daisy, your girlfriend, but you had a specific kink you wanted to try out: you wanted to be a baby and suckle real milk out of her. This was the perfect opportunity to do it! And you could both try loads of other crazy things, whether sexual or not.
    "Are you sure, Lucas?" asked Daisy.
    You were both in front of the machine.
    "Sure!" you say enthusiastically. "What would you like to try? Come on, it'll be great!"
    You kiss her and she smiles. You then wonder what you should try at first.
