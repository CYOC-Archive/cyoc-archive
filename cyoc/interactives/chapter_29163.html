    The genie appeared in front of Ddaniel.
    "You are now in my kingdom. It is indeed only a little town with a forest and some hills around it, but you will see that it is a beatifukl place. All people in this world are old masters of mine like you, too. Now you will live here for the next one hundred years without aging or dying. But here is a lot of work to do, so you will get a job and the special magic of this place will see that you do your tasks right. Did you understand that?"
    Daniel, who was all the time transfixed by the horror of being abduct into another world, only nooded. He couldn't believe that he will work the next one hundred years for this genie.
    "Ok, now let us look what kind of work you can do. Oh, I know! We need a new nurse because our old nurse had worked off her years last week. That will be your new job."
    "Hey, isn't this more a job for a girl than for a boy, is it?", Daniel protested.
    "Oh, if you have a problem with this, I can fix it.", answered the genie with a wicked smile.
    There was pink smoke again and Daniel couldn't see anything.
    When he opened up his eyes he stood in front of the mirror which hung on a wall in the flat. The person in the mirror wasn't a 17-year old boy.
    Instead of them there stood a 17-year old girl with bright pink hair which reached down to her shoulder wearing a white one-piece dress, a pair of white stockings and a nurse cap on her head. The girl had CC-sized breasts and a attractive female shape.
    The genie stood now behind Daniel.
    "You are now Danielle, the new nurse of the afternoon shift. You will help Doctor Rodriguez by his work. If you don't act how I want I will punish you. Oh, and at least. If you meet me again in this world you only call me "Mistress" or "Lady"!"
    With this words the genie dissappeared.
    Seemed like I have no other choice, thought Danielle, I must act like a girl to weather this situation.
    Then Danielle began to explore her new life.
