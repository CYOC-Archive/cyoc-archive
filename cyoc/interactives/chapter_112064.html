Time skips and adding more victims later on are allowed. The victim(s) will subconsciously act more like the animal they are becoming and take on their eating habits, but still retain self control up to a point near the end. At which point the animal mind becomes more dominate, eventually gaining full control with only a short reclamation if given their human name. Upon becoming a full animal they will leave into the surrounding woods, sexually preying upon new victims if they are found aimlessly wondering the woods.

All of these changes occur while none of the current occupants are looking at that location and the animals just don’t care when they do see the alterations.

After becoming complete sooner or later, any accommodations for them will mysteriously disappear, releasing them into the woods.
