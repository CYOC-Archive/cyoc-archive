    Getting to Know Geno

    Out of the black mist came Geno, and he smiled walking up to Bloom, and Natalie.

    "Hey, Bloom I got done early, and dad said I could visit for a bit. Who's this Bloom?" Geno asked referring to Natalie.

    "This is Natalie she was a friend of mine in my mortal life, but got corrupted. So my mom undid her corruption by making her this, and I explained what happened with me. Now she's bound to my soul, and we're basically friends for eternity now." Bloom said

    Natalie was blushing cupping a breast, and said,"I'm not used to this body yet, so I'm just trying my best to adapt."

    "I see, don't worry about doing that unless humans are around you, they for some reason hate that. Besides that I can definitely see that considering I'm undead, thanks to immortality I don't decay which is nice." Geno said,"Nice to meet you by the way Natalie."

    "Should I go tell Flora of your arrival Geno, or just stay here?" Natalie asked

    "Not really, Flora already knows most likely considering she's probably protective over Bloom after being separated all those decades." Geno stated

    "How long exactly have I been gone?" Bloom asked

    "Not long, only about 89 years at most, anyways we can do whatever we want for the time being." Geno said

    "Okay, but first just a quick question. Out of curiosity can you pull off your skeletol limbs, considering that there's no muscle or anything?" Natalie asked

    "Actually yes, my skeleton limbs are held up by magic, and I can control them too, but it's hard to make them do anything more than flop around, I can't feel them at all." Geno said

    "That's pretty neat actually, humans can't do that, and every other skeleton I see just falls apart, and dies by disintegrating, or shattering apart. I also think that I may try to see some kids later on, once I get the hang of my body first. I doubt mother wants to leave Natalie, and I alone too, after my death I mean." Bloom said seeing Issilka up on a ledge, and go into the shadows moments later spying on them.

    "I wouldn't blame her honestly, that's how most mothers are. That is if they don't kill themselves from guilt first. I understand that notion completely all things considered." Natalie said

    "So, are any of your memories returning Bloom? Dad has been busy trying to patch them up, as well as his regular duty of death." Geno said

    "Aside from a few that came back before the two of you first came here, nothing new. If you can see if you can check up on me, your dad is obviously doing something right. Also thank him for me please Geno." Bloom said, and Geno nodded

    "Just another note Bloom try avoiding situations where you are alone for the next 10 years. Dad says that there's high probability of something happening to you during that time period. Anyways I have to leave, bye Bloom, bye Natalie." Geno said disappearing into a black mist.

    "He seemed nice, oh god! I just realized that I can bear children now." Natalie said

    "Hey don't think of that, you have reason not to mate, because you're immortal right?" Bloom said

    "True, but I think we should find your mother now, as Geno said she probably doesn't like us being alone." Natalie said
