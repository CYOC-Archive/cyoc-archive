    To the costume shop...run by an old wizard

    Suzy had a revelation. Maybe she could hide her changes! She put her suit back on and grabbed her keys. The next stop, a costume shop! Suzy grabbed her heels and put them on as she knew she didn't have a choice. Going into public meant wearing shoes. Jumping in her car, Suzy made haste to the mall. AS far as she knew, it was the only place with a costume shop.

    As she was driving her hands started to tingle. Suzy tried not to think about it as she pulled into the mall parking lot. After getting out of her car, she stuffed her six foot tail into the spandex suit. Her hands tingled again as she walked, rather uncomfortably, to the door. As she reached her hand out she saw why her hands were tingling, they were growing in front of her eyes! Trying to hurry, she walked as fast as she could through the mall to the costume shop. Her hands continued tingling and growing as she got to the costume shop. When Suzy reached out for the door, she jammed her fingers! Suzy was not used to these larger hands. Carefully, she opened the door to the costume shop.

    "Hello Suzanne, how may I help you?"

    Suzy saw an old man wearing what looked like a bathrobe. "How did..."

    "I'm a wizard, don't ask again," the old man replied.

    "OK...So I'm here..."

    "Because you were a bitch to a leprechaun who put a curse on you, then tried to help you, and you couldn't follow two simple rules. Now you need something to hide that tail of yours."

    "Uhhh...yes."

    The wizard disappeared to the back room leaving Suzy to walk through the store. Suzy noticed man old trinkets and vials all around the place. The wizard returned with a pair of gloves and what appeared to be a black cat suit of some kind.

    "Here is what I have to offer you. This suit will stretch to fit whatever your body type is. You must wear the ENTIRE suit for the magic to work."

    He handed Suzy the suit, along with a pair of cat ears and a nose with whiskers attached. Lastly, there were the gloves, which after looking at harder, looked like cat paws.

    Suzy walked to the back dressing room and took her shoes off to put the suit on. Her feet grew out to the new size 26.5 size. She put her legs through the suit and brought it up to her waist. She noticed that the feet were padded on the bottom. Carefully, she slipped her tail into the tail hole and then stretched the fabric over her stomach. Finally she put her huge hands through and zipped it up. Next, she put on the cat ears and nose. The whiskers tickled her cheeks. Last, she put on the glove-paws. Once all were on she stepped out of the dressing room.

    "OK so what does it do?"

    "Well my dear, once you leave the store, that suit will make everyone see you as completely normal. There are rules for this suit though. One: Only wear the suit for 12 hours at a time. If you wear it longer, your body will absorb the magic and then it will no longer be a costume. Two: When you take it off, don't waste time. You have only 3 minutes to take the entire outfit off. If you don't, there will be some side effects."

    "How much?"

    "For you, it will be $550."

    "That's ridiculous! $550 for a cat suit?"

    "Well, I know Cornelius personally. You really were a bitch to him."

    Suzy sighed and grabbed her wallet. It was a bit more difficult with the cat paws but she managed. Suzy thanked the wizard and stepped out of the store. Once she did, the entire suit seemed to get tighter. She felt fur growing on her tail and her hearing had become much clearer. Her sense of smell also heightened. Looking at her hands she couldn't feel all of her fingers anyone. Now she felt 3 instead of 5. Finally it dawned on her, she now WAS a cat girl!

    A few men passed by and looked at her. Suzy became very tense, her tail swishing back and forth. When they kept on walking she sighed. So she was normal now!
