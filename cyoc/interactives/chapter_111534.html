    You took Bir'tal back to your cave, and explained what happened and the guilt that Bir'tal is facing right now because of it.
    "So since your pregnant the curse becomes permanent I guess." You said
    "I'm soo sorry!" Bir'tal said yet again
    "Geez you know how to pick them Mal'air." Far'ron teased
    "Shut it," you said slapping her with your tail," just show him your rings."
    Sal'kor walked up to him and Bir'tal examined her ring.
    "Damn, this is some enchantment I can't break it but it tranforms the wearer into a dragoness, amd reduces them to their prime if past that, and as you said causes for permanent curses include pregnancy, failure to resist self pleasure, eating raw food like a dragon would, and just anything to do with being a dragon in general." Bir'tal said
    "So we're stuck like this until we die?" Sal'kor said
    "I know someone who can remove the enchantment, but mating season is still so young, how will you hunt, how will you deal with your own hormones, and how will you get through without getting pregnant is your real concern that is if you want to turn back there's a lot of benefits to being a dragon over being a human you know." Bir'tal said
    "What about our families?" Kri'tal asked
    "Well dragons live longer lives than most beings alive over 100,000 years in fact."Bir'tal said,"I understand that some of you who have families so try to last, but it's unlikely that your enchantment will allow you to last until mating season is over in the next 500 years your pretty much screwed if you want to get out of it."
    "Your kidding me you don't know anyone who has the power to break the curse, and not fuck us in the process?" Far'ron said
    "Unfortunately all of the people I know are just as desperate as I am this time of the millennium, Mal'air knows how I was." Bir'tal sighed lowering his head
    "So we have no other choice then we need to accept our new lives." Sal'kor said," I mean once we get a chance our families will be dead by time so we might as well start new ones right?"
    "Morbidly put but yes." Bir'tal said," please don't make me an enemy over this I'm just trying to start a family like every other dragon out there."
    "Speaking of which," you chimed in," can we get to your cave soon, because any longer at this rate won't end well for us." You said with a swollen stomach clenching it with your claw.

    "Shit," Bir'tal said,"don't worry my cave is only about half a mile from here can you hold it till then?"
    You nodded the others helping you up.
    "This way." Bir'tal said leading the group.

    When you got to the cave you were going into labor as you approached the cave. The others layed you down inside the cave as you prepare to begin.
