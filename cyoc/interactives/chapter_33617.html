    At the station all of you went into an empty office and your step-father shut all windows so nobody could see or hear from outside what you were doing.

    "I'm sorry, but I must bring you to a safer place as fast as possible.", said your step-father,"Outside are too many intimidated and nervous people, so it is better that as less as possible people know that you are vampires. All over the city areself-declared vampire hunters and a few lunatics who want to kill everyone who looked like a vampire. It is chaos."

    "Thank you for all.", said Sarah and you all nodded,"What we need now, is a plan to stop this insane."

    "The only possibility for that is to find the real murderer.", meant Alice.

    "Okay, but you don't know anything about the murderer. How want you find him.", asked your step-father.

    "With the help of Alucard! I know he isn't a fan of us, but we saved his life.", you said.

    "Yes, but we also turned him into a six year old vampiress and I think he won't be thankful for that.", interjected Sarah.

    "Yeah, I know. But he is the only one who can compare the girl form the attack with the vampiress with the tape. If it is the same then we have a clue."

    "Mmh, this sounds like a good plan. I have brought Alucard in one of the arrest cells, so you can take him with you, but you need a safe place to operate. Sure, Alucard is now on your side, but there are more dangerous hunters like him.", your step-father said,"I think the most dangerous hunters are these one who aren't so present in the public."

    "We can use Sarah's house, can't we.", you meant.

    "Yes, we can. But for the first time Alucard arrives and leaves this house only with blindfolded eyes."

    After some minutes of discussing the details, Sarah, Alice, Megan, Dagger, you and the unconsciuos Alucard were on the way to Sarah's house.

    "Hey, Alice." You were so curious that you must ask her."What is the name you want to give Alucard."

    "Oh, I have planned to call her Annebelle fOr now on."

    Inatantly you began to laugh because you tried to imagine how a person like Alucard was called Annebelle.

    When you arrvied at the house there was also a surpise for you. Together with Brian, Jason, Tanya and Blade there was another, unknown man sitting on the table in the dinning-room.

    "I want to introduce you my friend Sebastian Frost, most time only called Frost.", introduced Blade the newcomer,"He is also a vampire hunter and lives normally in Europe. He wants to visit me and so I met him in the city. He is also on our side and wants to help us."

    After you all introduced yourselves, you walked to Frost.
    "I have thought that it is unussual that a hunter use his own name. The most hunters have the name of her weapons."

    "Yes, that's right.", said Frost,"Most of us use weapon names like Blade and Dagger, but I use crossbows." He pointed to the two crossbows which laid in a corner of the room."And I thought that this is a really stupid name, so I only use my last name."

    After the dinner Megan and you went to bed while Sarah explained Tanya all about her new life. The three hunters left the house to sleep in hotel not far from the house and Alice went upstairs to look at Annebelle.
