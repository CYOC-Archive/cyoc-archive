    You put the VR helmet on your head and as the Virtual World boots up, you find yourself in a medieval kingdom, your body looks just like it does in the arcade back in the real world, however there is a bit of a difference, you are wearing armor, have a sword, and a shield.

    You are escorted to the King’s castle by his guards and he tells you the whole story. You have been cursed by an evil Dragon to become his Dragoness mate and the only way to break this curse is to kill him. You have only 31 days or a whole month to kill the Dragon or you will turn into his Dragoness bride and be with him forever unable to return to the arcade back in your own world.

    Along the Way you might encounter female and male monsters like Kobolds, the Loch Ness Monster, Basilisks, Plesiosaurs, Mythical Salamanders, and the like, if you get caught by them and can't beat them in battle you will get raped by them and turned into whatever killed you.

    Your Sword is the only thing that can hurt and kill the gigantic flying fire-breathing lizard, you must kill him in order to escape this world and get back safely to the arcade in the real world, with your shield and armor, you must get from your kingdom to his castle, there are many obstacles, traps, monsters, enemies, and bosses in your kingdom, along the way to his castle and in his castle with the Dragon being the final boss.

    You start with 3 lives, and you have Armor, Stamina, and Life Gauge. If your Armor gauge empties, you will lose your armor and get hurt more easily. If your Stamina gauge empties you will be too tired to continue and must stop and rest for the rest of the day and night. If your life gauge empties, you will be too hurt to continue and be at the mercy of whatever enemy did you in turning you into their female mate and costing you a life. If you have lives remaining you will respawn as your human self and still have a chance to kill the Dragon and his minions. If you have no more lives then you will be stuck as the female mate of the enemy that defeated you. If Time runs out you will turn completely into a Female Dragoness and the Dragon will come and kidnap you ending your game and any chance of returning to the arcade back in the real world as a human boy.

    There are items that you can find to help you. Armor repair kits, food, and medicine. Armor repair kits will patch up your armor, food will increase your stamina, and medicine will replenish any lost life points.

    Good luck!
