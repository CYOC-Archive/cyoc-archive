    Dean and Sam were of the guys whose bodies became more muscular, shoulder muscles being larger, but gaining a soft and round contour; with a thick and coarse pelt hair, it sprouting quickly and spread like a wave all over their bodies.

    Some lanky tall Caucasian dude stood on the dock, he helped all that disembarked to stand around as his explained a few things.

    According to what was told to them, Sam and Dean were to join the Bear group, as they being older than most there, and of a mature bodily form, their purpose to the Island would be different than those younger males, as they were thought of as if Jackasses.

    Everyone there on the dock followed the instructions, all eager to participate in whatever lurid delight came their way.

    All went on through three separate gates, those deemed as being worthy Jackasses spread out along the boardwalk, looking with longing eyes at all the blatant sexual disgraces the island had to offer.

    Those determined as of the wolves went off down a pathway, in the distance their path split, one part entering the doorway to some Zoo; and the other offered passage to being in the wild.

    Dean, Sam, and about nineteen of the older fellows on the boat all walked straight ahead, their party led by the tall man.

    After passing out of sight from all peering eyes, as behind a thick hedge, the pathway stopped at a circle, there presented before them were three doors.

    To the right was a door and above it hung a sign that read, "Pig Tight," to which several of those there quickly entered and were gone.

    To the far left was another door, this one had also a sign above it, spelling out, "Bull strong," and in the center was a wide door, and on its sign was written, "Horse high."

    The tall man then addressed all those still remaining there poised and wondering.

    "Men, as those who just chose so quickly, their mindset felt inquisitive about learning of hogs, swine, pigs, sows, and the duties boars play in such a realm.

    The remainder of this group needs to chose each for them self which of the remaining doors they feel a sense of wonder; to this door you should then walk through."

    Dean felt a growling down in his gut, as if something there were somehow growing. He belched and so did several others, they together as a group stepped quickly through the door marked as "Bull Strong."

    Sam stood there stunned that his brother chose without consulting him, and he tried to walk in after his brother, but discovered that door as then locked.

    The tall man scoffed at Sam, informing him and the others there of the one door left open to them. This the door of "Horse High" slowly peeked open, as a cloud of white steam engulfed the remaining men, and they like the others then stepped boldly through that final door.
