    The Ahn'Ger Stone: Playpenned

    added by NotAboutCoffee 5 months ago AR AP TG Magic Male to female

    "Alright, Billy, Let's put you and your sisters in your play area, so Mommy can do a little work, OK? Then we'll have some nice ice cream later when Mommy's done." Willow said, First placing Bill into a guarded off room. This was soon followed by Tammy, and then Gloria, but when Willow picked up Gloria, she noticed Gloria was holding onto something.

    "Gloria what do you have here?" Willow said, as she took a look at the mysterious green stone. "Where did a silly little girl like you get this? This isn't one of mine... is it? I haven't dated anyone long enough recently to earn something as nice as this...." Willow trailed off, as she got lost in the stone's beautiful shine.

    Only to be brought back to reality by the tears of the daughter she held in her arms.

    "Oh, I know you don't want this pretty thing taken away, but Mommy doesn't want little Gloria to get into trouble with this thing."

    From the other room, Bill heard this. He rolled his little baby eyes. This was trouble enough already. Maybe it was a good thing that Gloria had it taken away... but now it meant that someone who had no clue of its power had it in her hands... but hopefully Willow wasn't the angry type.

    Soon, Willow placed Gloria in the play room with the other kids.

    "Now, please be good for Mommy. I have to finish up this project for Mr. McGavin so that Mommy can pay the rent! So please! I know you probably don't understand a word I say, but please, let Mommy have work time."

    With that, Willow walked off and sighed. Trying to live her dream without Greg there to help out with the kids was rough. Getting her first true assignment, animating for a show called "Pirate Pete's Perilous Plight" and getting to work at home for it was lucky enough. Willow still worked hard during the day to pay the bills and hard at night to keep her kids happy... but soon it would all be worth it.

    Willow sat at her desk, and fiddled with the stone in her hand.

    "You know... I'm already getting some new ideas from this stone... I may have to have a storyboard session soon..."

    Option 1: Mr. McGavin calls Willow and informs her that funding was pulled from the show. Angry and upset, Willow makes a wish.

    Option 2: Willow tries her best to work, but discovers that Bill made his way out of the play area. Frustrated, Willow loses her temper and makes a wish.

    Option 3: Frustrated at her lack of progress, Willow makes an angry wish towards herself.
