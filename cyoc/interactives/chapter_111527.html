    "I wish that I had a giagantic sky castle floating in the sky with a livable atomosphere a large garden the size of a football field, invisible servants that's are completely loyal to be, several bedrooms, a large farm where my servants raise crops and animals, along with my sky castle hovering about one thousand feet above New York City. I also wish that only I and guests of my choosing can enter it and also that a giant imprenatrable dome around it protecting it from all harm. I also wish that if I say the command word "Sky Castle" I will be instantly teleported into my bedroom in the Sky Castle. My last wish is that while my Sky Castle is mostly medieval it also has many modern elements like air conditioning, several game consoles, a flat screen tv, and other elements as well."


    After saying that I grin heavily. I say "Sky Castle" and feel a rush of air and find myself in the most grand bed room I have ever seen in my life. Decorated with silk curtains, pillows, and royal decorations along with a king size bed I saw my bedroom. It was perfect. There was a minfridge by the bed, a large 26 inch flat screen on the wall, several game consoles along with games that were supposed to come out in several years and several wardrobes filled with the fancies clothes ever. I leave my lavish bedroom and I quickly find out that the rest of the castle is also very luxurious. Grand ornate windows decorate the stone walls and soft carpets take up most of the floor. I examine the living room, the several guest rooms dotted around the castle, the kitchen which was filled with hundreds of foods and ingredients, there was also a indoor basketball court, a large swimming pool, a race track, and even a skydiving chamber!


    You nearly wanted to scream in excitement but then realized something. You are above New York City in your Sky Dome. Was there any commotion on the surface?

    You say "Sky Castle." and feel another rush of wind and find myself in my bedroom once more. You strip myself naked since the clothes you were wearing was really uncomfortable and jump on my bed. You grab the tv remote and turn the tv on to the news channel. As you had expected you were right.

    On the screen there was a blond news woman with a microphone, behind were what seemed to be New York, with thousands of people looking at the sky in fear and wonder, cameras flashed everywhere and it was noticeably dark, not quite night time, but more like as if there was something covering the sky.


    "In today's shocking news, a giant dome has appeared suddenly in the sky above New York City. This dome had no warning and is now covering about a third of New York. Citizens are nearly rioting at this point demanding answers on the strange floating dome. Police are attempted to calm citizens though it is clear there is a great unrest. Many citizens claim that the dome is of extraterrestrial origin. Could this be true? Have aliens finally made contact with humankind? Find out after this commercial break."


    You felt guilty not because you did any harm but because you were clearly causing a commotion. You nearly wanted to reverse your wish but felt that the people of New York would eventually forget about it. You tried changing the channel but quickly realized that nearly every channel was focusing on your sky dome. Oh well. It's not like it wasn't to be expected. You were feeling pretty hungry so you decided to order some grub from the kitchen.

    "Can someone please get me a bag of chips and a ham sandwhich?"

    After a few minutes you hear footsteps come from the hallway and see the door open. No one came through, but you did see a plate with a large 12 inch sandwhich along with a bag of chips float in the air and land in front of you.

    You say "Can I also get a glass of water." And you hear the footsteps leave and then return, a floating glass of water this time appear through the door and place it on your bedside table.

    You find yourself creeped out by the servants and make a wish to fix that.

    "I wish my invisible servants were visible and all appeared like regular humans."


    Suddenly beside you bed appears a man wearing a suit. Tall and with a short combed beard. Standing ina formal posture you looks at you, waiting for his next order. Outside the door you now see several other different men walk past your door, some carrying plates and others cleaning.

    You smile and give the man a simple order.

    "Give me a massive blowjob."
