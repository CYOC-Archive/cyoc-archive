I ran some tests on Helen’s were-birth control pills; thinking they were a bad batch, putting the doctor in a severe hormonal imbalance. However that is not quiet the case,” Tora sighing; “the pills she was taken were geared towards a were-lioness.”

Encouraging the need and desire to reproduce; its why for some hybrid-weres their were-birth controls can get really expensive. They have to be carefully tailored to their unique physiology. The problem with the birth control pills,” Tora shaking his head; “they are all the same size, shape and colour. Helen Tompkins was mistakenly given pills meant for a were-lioness.”
    “So she rides out the heat cycle and gets a proper batch for her were-species,” Regina looking unconcerned; “I still don’t see why you are saying these two need to be together.”
    “Helen Tompkins heat cycle,” Doctor Tora answering; “is indicative to a normal going through the same symptoms of an incomplete transformation. Fever, shakes, impulsive actions and an insatiable desire for a certain person. In this case; its Peter. If she doesn’t get relief soon,” Doctor Tora looking serious; “Helen Tompkins could die.”

    “NO,” Peter roaring as he leaps off his bed and grabs the doctor; “where is Helen! Tell me before I tear this hospital apart looking for her.”
    CALM down Peter,” Leon using all of his strength to wrest the were-lion from the doctor.
    “Helen is my mate,” Peter roaring; “I will not calm down until she is all right. Let me go father,” Peter now growling and baring his claws as he struggles to be free of his father’s grasp. Doctor Tora getting up after being knocked down reaches into his lab coat for a syringe.
    “Leon keep your son restrained,” Tora approaching and sticking the needle in Peter. Hearing the younger were-lion roar in pain as Tora injects the contents into him; he steps back as the struggling were-lion manages to break free from his father's grasp; swiping the man with a clawed paw before passing out.

    “After we had a hell of a time keeping Peter sedated during his emergency surgery,” doctor Tora watching the were-lion slump to the floor; “a few of the staff came up with this Diazepam and ketamine based sedative. Fast acting and twice as strong as the standard sedatives used,” Tora Tachibana placing the used needle in a biomedical waste bin; “he will be out for a couple of hours.”
    “What do we do until then,” Leon gasping; stunned at how strong his son was as Regina looked at the deep scratches on her husband's face.

It won’t stop Helen’s heat cycle, or her desire to be mated by Peter” doctor Tora talking; “but it should help prevent her from ovulating during the many bouts of intercourse she and Peter will undoubtedly have.”

    “It’s better than nothing,” Andy final speaking up; “and Mrs. Talbot, while I’m certain you are only looking out for the safety of your son, you must understand, Helen is my twin sister and I don’t want her to die from some medicine mistake that was made. Plus,” Andy looking at the distraught were-lioness; “Peter still owes me an apology for breaking my arm.”
