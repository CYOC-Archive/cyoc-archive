    We walked into the mansion with great anticipation. Our anticipation turned to apprehension as we began to
    look around. The place was very dark, and it became darker still as we closed the front door behind us. It
    seemed that curtains covered all of the windows and very little light was able to peak in from outdoors.

    Immediately in front of us was a grand staircase. It looked like the one in “Gone With the Wind” since it was
    very wide at the bottom and narrowed gradually as it reached the next floor. To either side of the staircase
    were two rooms that had ceilings that reached all the way to the third floor, save for a small area that was
    the second floor. The second floor started at the top of the stairs, but it pushed back into the house rather
    than taking up the entire level. The two rooms we saw appeared to be living rooms.

    We decided to explore the house in a clockwise manner. We entered the living room on the left. The furniture
    remained in the mansion but it was all covered in white sheets. For now we decided not to look under them,
    for we wanted to see all the mansion had to offer. As we further explored the house we discovered a multitude
    of rooms. We found a huge dining room, which appeared should only be used if a lot of guests were at the
    house. Adjacent to the dining room was what we determined to be a ball room.

    We found our way into the kitchen next. It was bigger than any kitchen I’d ever seen in my life. One could
    easily run a restaurant out of this kitchen. This was going to be a great place to improve our cooking
    skills. There was a small eating area adjacent to the kitchen as well.

    Eventually we discovered a huge indoor pool. Next to the pool were a sauna and an area that appeared to be
    made for spa services. The pool area seemed to provide the only direct access to the grounds aside from the
    front door. There was an empty garden that showed now evidence of being tended in some time. The garden also
    sat right against the mansion walls. There was a backyard that appeared rather plan, which eventually gave
    way to the woods.

    The woods extended in a very wide range from left to right, practically as far as we could see. The trees
    were extremely tall. They were taller than any trees I had ever seen previously. Something told me that we
    should stay clear of those woods. Considering how big the woods appeared to be, it gave the appearance of a
    place where one could too easily get lost.

    We returned to the staircase and ascended to the second level. At the top of the staircase was a hall that
    extended in each direction. We could look down into the two living rooms from it. On each end of the hall
    was another staircase that led to the third floor. We made quick inspection of the second floor which
    contained rooms that were meant to be used as offices, a gym, and other indeterminable functions.

    In the third floor there was nothing but huge bedrooms. They all had beds that could easily fit three people.
    There was also a large balcony at the back of the third floor. It looked like the perfect place to just lay
    in the sun for several hours.

    “This place needs a lot of upkeep,” I said.

    “Isn’t that what your ring is for?” Nicole asked. “I mean, this place isn’t exactly ‘move-in ready.’”

    “I can’t argue with that,” I agreed. So I wished that the house was in pristine condition, with all of the
    furnishings uncovered and in top condition. I also wished that the house was completely clean, that the
    kitchen was stocked, that the pool was filled and ready to use, and that all of our clothes and possessions
    from our apartment were in the house. We spent the next half hour or so making sure that the wish fulfilled
    all of its intentions.

    Once our wish inspection was finished, and we determined that everything we asked for was done, we headed for
    the pool. I bent down and put my hand in the water in order to gauge its temperature. “Nice and warm,” I
    said. Suddenly a figure jumped over my head and directly into the pool. The splash of water got a good
    portion of my clothes drenched.

    I turned around and saw Nicole’s clothes in a pile. I looked back at the pool to see Nicole’s head emerge
    from the water. She smiled at me and then laughed when she saw that I was wet. “Might as well join me,” she
    said.

    I took off my shoes and then my socks. I smiled at her and said, “Might as well.” With that I jumped into
    the pool.
