    A Trip to the Town at Dusk

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Dragonman 5 years ago O Mythological Magic Canine

    You looked between the two towns, and the constant growing hunger in your stomach made you anxious. A human soul would fill this gap nicely and perhaps even more than you'd expect so you decided to run off towards the closer of the two towns, playing it safe for the time being.

    Once you got to the town's entrance you noticed the gate shut, but an opening below the wall just big enough for your smaller body caught your eye, so you forced yourself through it as best you could being mindful of your tails. "Urk! Come on now!" You growled then with a sudden *pop* you were out on the other side.

    You stared at the opening now and you sighed,"Note to self, maybe I should dig that out some more before attempting something like that again. But for now, let’s see what unlucky criminal crosses me." You then turned around and began walking through the alleyway.

    Unfortunately for you as it may seem the only humans stirring were that of guards, and you had a feeling that they wouldn’t take too kindly to you snooping around in the dark. Nonetheless you were starving so you had to eat something right? The run here only made your gluten worse, and the more that you think about it the more that you think you made a mistake coming here.

    After a while of wandering in the dark you noticed something that made your ears perk up, a soul within a house, and it was moving. Unlike most souls indoors which were still with sleeping individuals this one was moving slowly too. Either someone was just waking up in the middle of the night or this was the thief that you were looking for. You immediately got on guard and you began stalking the house from the shadows, and eventually you peered through one of the glassless windows to see your potential victim, and it was....
