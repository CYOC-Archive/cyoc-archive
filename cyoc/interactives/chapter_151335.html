    When Jeff emerged from his sleep, he felt strange. He had made such a weird dream. His sister had turn him into a doll ! As he opened his eyes, he was shocked to see her giant face in front of him. he checked his body and found a Barbie one… it wasn’t a dream.
    As he moved his arms, he saw Sarah open her eyes and look at him. she smiled and said in a sleepy voice.

    “Good morning dolly.”

    She stretched , stood up and brought Jeff back to the desk.

    “Time to get dressed for your new wonderful princess day!”

    Sarah opened the doll’s clothes box again, and picked out a pink princess dress. She took off the outfit from the doll in front of her and dressed her with the gown, adding pink matching heels and a tiara. She checked her work and seemed satisfied. His sister picked Jeff up and brought him back to the dolls corner, where the other ones where already aligned, facing and bowing to Sarah. He was placed among them once more.

    “Do not forget the bowing, sweetie” said Fiona’s voice.

    He turned to Sarah and bowed. He heard her say that she had heard their prayers and would honor them if they were good.

    “Be grateful , dollies, cause you’ll get to be played with soon”

    She turned and went out of the room, abandoning her brother to her dolls again. Many congratulated him for either his new outfit or the honor he had been granted to share the night of the goddess just like only a few of them.

    Time passed and Sarah went back a few times in the room without showing interests for her dolls while she was dressing, reading a chapter from a book or drawing. In the mean time, Jeff had to suffer his new life : tea time, fashion magazines time, lessons about clothes and doll’s ways with Betty and Mary that were still watching after him… The recently transformed doll tried to stick to her role, but it wasn’t always easy. He was probably missing some girly reflexes… he both wanted to get them quick enough to undraw attention on him, and feared to acquire them because it would actually mean becoming the doll his sister meant him to be.

    On the early afternoon, Sarah finally came back to her room and headed to the corner where the dolls were. She was carrying a bag from the close toy’s store. All the dolls aligned next to the doll house and bowed to his sister. Internally furious, Jeff did the same, half forced by his two new friends.

    “Hello hello beloved dolls ! Your goddess is in a good mood today and is willing to fulfil some of your wishes.”

    She giggled, sat on the floor next to them and started taking things out of the bag to give them to the dolls. she handed a flashing wand to Fiona, a dress to Betty, a windsurf to a Barbie dressed in a swimming suit… and to his surprise she turned to him.

    “I got a surprise for you as well Lily !”
