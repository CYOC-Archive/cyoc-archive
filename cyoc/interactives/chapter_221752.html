    Ty felt incredibly happy. He had just grown the muscles of a boy in the library, the last place where kids think about adding or growing muscle! Ty was about to leave when he heard a sound coming from the other end of the small library. He looked over and spotted a kid shuffle around, setting his backpack on the floor with a sigh. Then, he threw a large, heavy physics book on the table and opened it. The boy looked Asian, while wearing glasses with short, straight black hair.

    Ty learned that the boy’s name was Hak-Kun, and that the teen originally came from South Korea. He was rather quiet and reserved and he often got bullied for his accent and for being a bit on the short side. But Hak-Kun was very smart and bright, and he was a straight-A student in all his classes, except PE.

    Ty saw that Hak-Kun started to study and began to take notes from the large book. So, Ty decided to have some fun with him. After all, who said studying physics had to be boring? Our Korean kid needed some help in PE class, and Ty was determined to give the kid a hand. After all, he hated seeing such a smart kid getting bullied in school.

    “For every line of notes, he writes in his notebook, he will gain five pounds of raw muscle!” Ty whispered.

    As Hak-Kun started to write, he began to feel his pectorals bulge slowly and gradually. He wrote a few lines and then stopped. What was going on?

    He pulled on his shirt, which was feeling a bit tight. Then, he picked up the pen and continued to write.

    He wrote a few more lines as his torso widened. His arms began to bulge as his biceps and triceps started to grow. When they grew enough for Hak-Kun to notice, he stopped again.

    He looked sideways and noticed how he suddenly had some muscle on his arms! Where did that come from?

    He didn’t know what to do. But he wanted to finish his notes, so he continued writing.

    This time, his quads bulged, and his calves slowly inflated with solid muscle, growing thicker and wider on both legs. Another minute had passed, and Hak-Kun needed to stop again.

    This time he walked into the boy’s bathroom, which was a few feet away. There, he stood before the large mirror and let out a soft gasp. He was becoming more muscular! But, how?

    Then, it happened! He was hit by some invisible wave! A blast of energy hit his body from head to toe! Every muscle in his body doubled in size, and he instantly grew into a high school muscle kid!

    Within seconds, Hak-Kun found himself standing there, in the bathroom, with wide shoulders, bulging pecs, washboard abs, and arms as big as pythons. His legs were as wide as pillars. He looked like one of those kids who had spent the past year lifting weights in the high school gym!

    The boy adjusted his glasses. He was a nerd, after all. Now, he was a muscle nerd! But, what was happening to him?

    Just then, Ty entered the bathroom and walked up to the Korean muscle kid.

    The poor kid had practically burst out of his t-shirt by now. His jeans had ripped at the seams due to his expanding legs. He needed new clothes!

    “I guess I need to fix your clothes, huh?” Ty said to him.

    “Wait, what? Do you know about this? How I got these muscles?” Hak-Kun asked him, as he stood there flexing his arms.

    “Of course! I’m the reason why you gained all these muscles just now!” Ty shrugged. “You need a good grade in PE class… And we need to stop this bullying!”

    Hak-Kun’s t-shirt suddenly grew and adjusted for the boy’s larger frame, and the jeans changed into large shorts, exposing his thick, muscular legs. The t-shirt converted into a tank top, which allowed him to show off his thick, muscular arms!

    “There! You’re all set! Ready to face all the kids in school, including the bullies!” Ty grinned.

    Ty snapped his fingers and Hak-Kun disappeared, teleporting to the crowded hallway where the classrooms were.

    Several of his tormentors spotted Hak-Kun. This time, however, they decided to leave the Korean kid alone. When they saw all that muscle on his frame, they realized that bullying this boy was not worth it.

    Meanwhile, Ty had to decide whether to stay in the library, or go somewhere else, to another part of the school, and find other guys to grow?
