    Spa and Regression

    Ashley decided to take the AR Spa.

    “I’m going to the AR Spa. Where are you going?” asked Ashley

    “I’m going to the AP Spa. I can’t wait for my girls.” replied June.

    “Well good luck” said Ashley.

    The two went to their separate ways.

    Ashley arrived to the AR Spa. The AR Spa is a spa that makes people younger as long as they are in the water
    at a rate of 1 month per second. Fortunately, it does not affect pregnancies and if the person regresses to 10
    or under a caretaker will come in for that person.

    “Now to feel pregnancy throughout my youth, until 15 years old of course” said Ashley.

    Ashley got her swimsuit and laid down on one of the AR Spas. Ashley noticed that the people in the pool are
    getting younger and saw she is getting younger too. She noticed she is getting shorter by a few inches; her
    breasts went from a D cup to a C. Her curves also receded somewhat and got back the freckles she lost years
    ago. Ashley felt somewhat disappointed with this. A few minutes later Ashley became 15 years old and is no
    longer regressing.

    “Good thing I’m not getting any younger or else things would get bad” commented Ashley.

    Once a pregnant woman regresses to 15 years old, she can’t regress anymore, it’s a safety precaution to
    prevent any birthing complications.

    She then saw two little girls looking no older than 8 swimming throughout the pool. Ashley noticed there was
    no caretaker for them so she swam to them for their safety. When she arrived the girls already lost a year.

    “Where’s your mommy?” asked Ashley.

    “I don’t know” replied one of the regressing girls.

    Ashley then noticed that one of the girls’ swimsuits was wearing the employee symbol.

    “Nuts, one of the caretakers must have eaten something she wasn’t supposed to” guessed Ashley.
    “It looks like I’ll have to fix this.”

    “Girls, let’s get out of the water, we don’t have much time!” said Ashley

    “No!” said the girls.

    One of the girls splashed water onto Ashley’s face and they swam away from her.

    Ashley swam but just couldn’t catch to catch up with them. Meanwhile, the girls are getting smaller. Soon
    floating tubes appeared on their limbs. When they reached 2 years old, they have a floating ring on their
    waists. Thanks to that, Ashley soon caught up with them but by then, they are the middle of the pool.

    “Phew, now to get them out of the pool” thought Ashley quickly.

    Luckily, the pool is only a yard deep so Ashley walked as fast as she could while pushing the girls’ floating
    rings all while the girls are regressing. Their hair soon became wisps and bodies became smaller while their
    floating tubes are bigger. They soon got to an end of the pool. Ashley then picked the girls out of the pool.
    Unfortunately, she forgot that the water on the girls’ bodies still regress them. A few moments after the
    girls got out, they disappeared and Ashley’s belly grew to nine months with triplets. Her breast also regrew
    back to D cup.

    “Gah! So close! (Sigh) I guess I have to be their mother now” said Ashley managing her new belly.

    “Sorry Heather, but it looks like you have a roommate now” said Ashley.

    Heather felt the presence of two more babies. She guessed Ashley unbirthed them but it was okay for her though
    it does feel uncomfortable.

    Ashley then decided to leave the AR Spa while adjusting to her new belly. She went to the AP Spa to check on
    June. There she found June and two 8 year old girls.

    “Hi June. How is everything?” asked Ashley

    “Doing fine, I see that the AR Spa did some good for you” said June, commenting Ashley’s bigger belly and
    younger appearance.

    “I’m going to the AR Spa for some ‘fun’ want to come and watch?” asked June.

    “Okay, I’m curious what you’ll do” replied June.

    The girls then head for the AR Spa.
