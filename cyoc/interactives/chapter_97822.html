    at them. You take notice in a big one. He's a sheepdog, a really handsome one. He looks at you with his long, beautiful face and you look at him back. He's a stud, and
    you are very grateful that you get to be so close to him.

    For now, you move away to inspect your kennel. It's quite nice and comfy looking. You can already tell that you're going to love it here.
