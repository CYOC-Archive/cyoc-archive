    Emma chuckled to herself as she watched the group of obnoxious middle schoolers walk away. She had taken a photo of them from behind, but only because one of them was her younger brother. The photo came out and she smirked.

    Dead center was her little brother Gavin, walking towards Auntie Annes while on his phone. She noticed the chubby middle schooler was clearly unaware of her presence, that his clothe didn't;t match well, his brown hair looked like a mess, but most importantly of all was that he was playing Pokemon Go. She couldn't believe anyone would still be playing that, and couldn't wait to slip this by some girls on the cheer squad that Gavin had a crush on.

    Beside Gavin were others that she didn't really care about. They were just his nerd friends. The only one she actually kinda like was Tina. She was a cute black girl that was a year younger than her brother. She had a frizzy afro, some acne and dressed conservatively, but not only was she sweet but Emma always bet that she had the potential for a puberty boom and Tina could join the team one day.

    The other two with him, however, Emma couldn't care less about. Alex was some ginger doofus with more pimples than her brother, but was taller and skinnier for his age. He walked beside the other friend which Emma couldn't;t even recall correctly. Was it Jake, or jeff or something? All she knew was that the kid was half Mexican and always tried to peak on Emma whenever he was over. Luckily Tina would scold him, which only made Emma like her more.

    "Em, did you just take a photo of your brother?" Rhianna asked, pointing to the group which was repeatedly moving further away.

    "Like, so what if I did." Emma smirked. "And like, so what if I maybe show this to Brittney later?"

    "O.M.G." Rhianna scoffed. "I like love you sis, but sometimes you can be a total bitch." Rhianna teased, rolling her eyes.

    "Ugh, like, fine." Emma groaned. She handed Rhianna the camera and stored the photo of her brother in her purse. "I maybe won't show the others what a loser my bro is, and like, if you hate my pictures so much you can hold this fossil."

    Rhianna was about to protest when Emma practically shoved it into her friends hands.
    "Like, come on Ri, we got dresses to look for." She laughed as she strut off.

    Rhianna sighed, she didn't get what was up with her bestie today. Not only that, but she had this weird feeling in the back of her mind that something wasn't right. She glanced back down at the camera, and then back up at her friend who had taken another few steps away.
