    Couple at the Shotel

    "None of the advertisements said anything about this... That this place is run by kids!" A brown-haired, 22 your old man said in bewilderment, standing in the middle of the Shotel's lobby with his girlfriend next to him, who looked just as confused by the staff.

    "This is obviously some...way too elaborate prank... I have no clue who'd bother to set it up, but that's the only explanation I've got..." He continued, looking back down at the phone in his hand. He had the hotel's website up, and looked it over again - nope, not a single note about the young staff...
    "E-Ethan, calm down... People are staring..." His girlfriend whispered, taking his free hand. They had already spent money booking a room and on travelling - it would be a waste to just leave. Ethan sighed...
    "Right, right... This is still weird, though. If it ends up being a scam, I'm finding whoever owns this place..." With that said, he and his girlfriend go to the reception.

    The boy attending it looked no older than 10, but he was still polite and surprisingly patient with them, and they soon enough got the key to their room and were on their way.
    Ethan himself was pretty tall, but with an otherwise average build, and with brown eyes. He had a bit of a tan, and had dirty blonde, sort of curly hair and a short beard growing out. It was hot outside, so he wore a white, short-sleeved button-up shirt and black, knee-length shorts as well as black sandals and some sunglasses.
    His girlfriend, who's name is Sonja, is the same age as him. She's pretty average in height, but has rather shapely hips and is a bit on the busty side as well. Her hair reaches about her shoulders, with a hazel color, and is pretty wavy, and her eyes are green. She's wearing a sort of loose, pink top that would expose her midriff if she wasn't wearing a white tank top beneath. Additionally, she's wearing white tights that go down to her ankles and a skirt with the same, pink color as her top, as well as sandals of her own.

    In any case, they unlocked the door to their room and got inside - as they had ordered, it had a double bed and TV, and of course there was a fridge with probably way too expensive food inside... After putting their stuff aside, he looked over to Sonja.
    "So, what do you want to do now?" Ethan asked, picking up a brochure that laid on the table. "There's a pool, game room, a nearby park, restaurant..." He read up, glancing up at Sonja every once in a while.
    "Do I have to pick...? You know I don't like to..." She mumbled, twirling some of her hair around her finger. "...You decide. I-I'll go along..."
    Ethan shrugged, knowing that he should have expected an answer like that. She's sweet, and far from bad looking, but he still hasn't solved her confidence issues...

    "Right, let's see here..." His voice trailed off as he looked at the selection one more time...
