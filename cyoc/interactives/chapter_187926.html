His thoughts like unpreventably seemed to go back to the call he had with Jennifer this morning and what she would do to him. Kyle wasn’t sure how long he could make it but decided that he would try to hide from her as long as he could.

    ***
    Jennifer after the little trip to the highway told her diligent chauffeur to make a little detour to the mall. In a bit of a hurry she went into a shop for animal equipment and just after a short while found exactly what she was looking for. It only costed a few bucks.

    As Jennifer left the store she looked at it thinking. She figured that it maybe could use a little personal touch, and so tried out something new. She concentrated on her arm and made a small silver bracelet appear around it with a pendant consisting of three 3-D printed words, colored in the same silver as the bracelet. They were only held together by the touching connections between the letters of the words under and upper rims.

    Jennifer loosened the pendant from the bracelet by opening the little lobster clasp. Now was the time of truth. Would it work?

    She lay the pendant on a wooden bench at the parking area of the shop and went away a few meters. As she came back everything seemed as she had hopped, the pendant hadn’t disappeared. This way it would make a perfect addition to her little gift for Kyle. Jennifer already giggled by the thought of her plan and most satisfiedly went to her car which door got humbly opened for her from her hunky, servile chauffeur. ‘I really could get used to that’ she thought, then enjoying the ride to her school.

    They had arrived, but before leaving the car Jennifer shortly checked herself in the side-view mirror, trying something out once again. She laid her left hand on her bosom that then only by her thought grew bigger at minimum one entire size from her former B-Cups.

    After a short touch at her drivers hand and some new orders he drove away again a few seconds later when Jennifer left the car; having no memories of their ride or Jennifer at all and heading to his workplace as if nothing out of the ordinary had happened.

    The first class was over and Kyle will have English next. However, he didn’t immediately rush to his locker to get his needed books out of it, but waited until most pupils had again disappeared from the floor and got inside the classrooms. His thoughts again drifted a bit, figuring if maybe the spells haven’t worked. Until now he just had to strangely repeat what Jennifer said to him but other than that nothing had happened that would be magical in anyway. Maybe Jennifer had (somehow, whenever) hypnotized him and with that forces him to say these words, maybe after a trigger word. Then she would just delude him, just scarring him to make him do what she wants. ‘It could be’ he thought. Somewhere deep inside he at least hoped that it would be like that. Then Kyle’s thoughts returned to his current situation.

    Nobody to be seen anymore. Now he felt safe to go out into the open, but still was looking around alertly, ready to hide as soon as he would see Jennifer approaching.

    But for now, no Jennifer to be seen. So he swiftly walked to his locker and opened it.
