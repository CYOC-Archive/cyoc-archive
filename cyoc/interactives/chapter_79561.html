    "So Ash, or should I say Ashley, why don't you tell the story to your family."

    Ash's family all look at him with confussion. Ash tried to say something, but nothing was happening.

    "Oh that's right. I remember. I made a wish that you could never tell anybody what happened twenty years ago. Well, I wish you would tell your family what happened."

    She magically held it in for twenty years, but now she could finally talk about what happened all those years ago.

    "I was born a girl. I was girl up until I was 17. Brie and I were BFFs. I wanted to have a lovely family and give birth to my babies. Then Joe here (he tried to say something like "perverted" or "son of a bitch" but he couldn't) said I was a terrible person and deserved to have my dreams dashed." Ash was crying now.

    "That's enough." said Joe and Ash silenced up.

    Joe continued "Yes, that is what happened. You became a man, married your "BFF" Brie and had two wonderful kids. Now, I hoped me changing your life forever would have helped you out as a person. It sure did. You became a wonderful human being and raised a fine family. So, I'm going to reward you."

    Ash stared at Joe all confused as he family already was.

    "I'm going to give you your body back...aged to 37 years of course."

    Had this happened a long time ago, Ash would have been thrilled to be a girl again. But he had a family he cared for and it wouldn't be right if he was female again. So, he shook his head no as he still couldn't speak.

    "You don't want your old female body back? I mean you were pretty and I'm sure twenty years wouldn't have changed it too drastically." Joe looked over at Angie. "Look at your daughter, that's pretty much what you use to be. You looked exactly like your father was at her age sweety." she said to Angie.

    "Now, we did this last time with no clothes so....I wish you all were naked."

    Suddenly the family's clothes vanished. Fear in all of their eyes.

    "So, let's get rid of that male prison you have been in the last twenty years and give you the body you would have had if I had left you alone. I wish you were female again!"
