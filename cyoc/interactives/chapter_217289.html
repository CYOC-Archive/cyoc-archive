    Tyler Shrinks To 6 Inches

    So, Tyler just stood there as he watched the wheel spin around and around, slowing down with every pass, until it stopped with the big, red arrow pointing to the text which read: "Shrink To 6 Inches".
    "Excellent!" said gleefully Madame Illusia.
    She simply loved these transformations which promised to lead to other changes, all of which were certain to be fun! All those possibilities! To be both god and bad. And this arrogant young fool certainly deserved to be punished. He was the sort of bully the world could afford to live without. Yes, she was going to have so much fun with that one!
    "Ready to be taken down a peg or two?" she laughed as the transformation took place.
    Tyler had barely the time to comprehend what happened to him. The world rose dramatically around and suddenly his height and athletic body was a thing of the past. He all at once inhabited a world of giants. He was puny! The size of a little doll! And all eyes were upon him, all stared at his minuscule form. He attempted speaking but stoped fast. His voice did not carry at all, even if he was spared the humiliating squeaky tone that might have expected. And his clothes had disappeared, leaving him naked with a pile of mammoth garments that Madame Illusia made vanish. Appalled, Tyler covered his groin with his two hands while everyone laughed, delighted at the doll-sized jerk now naked and leaving his butt for all to see. Even if there was not much to see now. The young man was completely, utterly humiliated. But the worse that it was not over yet for him.
    "You are much more pleasant now!" laughed Madame Illusia. "You look like a little doll! Much nicer! But you don't look fully the part yet!"
    Tyler gulped. This did not sound very good... And he was right.
