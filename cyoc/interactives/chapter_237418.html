    Surrounded By Gorillas... And Tarzan.

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 15 days ago A O Magic

    You just stare at Tarzan, who frowns at you. He issues a few grunts from his mouth. These grunts seem foreign to you so you just continue looking at Tarzan. He pronounces a few more incomprehensible sounds but it is impossible for you to understand. Or to reciprocate as your throat cannot make human sounds anymore. It is frustrating. Even your curses and insults produce nothing. You really are a dumb ape now. This makes you angry. You feel that you did not deserve this, unaware that with all of the awful acts that you've done, maybe it is quite fitting for you. After all, you WERE a bad man. You were sentenced for a heinous crime, and you had done countless others before, for which you were not judged. Yet in this moment, you can only resent the whole world for being now an animal, and deprived of all human comforts that you know you'll miss sorely.
    But suddenly, Tarzan calls out loudly to something and soon enough you see gorillas coming and staring at you from the ground. They're about a dozen and they don't look friendly. Actually, you notice from your hands that you yourself are probably a gorilla. Tarzan growls, grunts and speaks to all the assembled apes, who then answer in kind. The ensuing dialogues annoy you and frustrate you, the issue being that you cannot understand any of it. Then, quite suddenly, Tarzan throws an orange powder at you, which from the tingling sense that it is triggering a new transformation. You think it cannot be worse than this, but you are massively wrong...

    What do you do now?
