    Walking out of the Aviary, Jeff noticed that one of the upcoming areas of the zoo was the Aquarium. Thinking that sounded like a fun time with his new app, he headed down the path. Soon Jeff found himself in front of a large building whose facade was absolutely covered in ocean and marine animal theming, with crabs, octopi, jellyfish and sharks against an ocean wave background. Near the entrance was a welcome sign depicting a cartoon shrimp dressed like a pirate, encouraging guests to head on in.

    Inside, the theming continued, animals painted on every wall, and appropriately chosen animal statues flanking the entrances to the various exhibits including the rainforest, swamps, and the deep sea.

    As Jeff pondered the possibilities, he took notice of a small group of female employees, six in total, chatting casually nearby while on their lunch break. Smiling at his new test subjects, Jeff whipped out his phone and scanned the young women through the Chronivac. As he was doing so he heard a small snippet of conversation from one of the girls, whose name was Ashley: “It’s a shame they had to close down the animal shows. I know it’s best for the animals but they’re part of the reason I wanted to work here in the first place. When I was little I dreamed about being a trainer and teaching the whales and sea lions all sorts of tricks.”


    That was all Jeff needed to hear as watched the other girls nod in agreement. It might be unethical for normal animals to perform, but what about some willing anthro girls? “Well Ashley, I’m gonna make you and your friends wishes come true!” He thought, punching in the commands. He started by changing their professions from tour guides to aquatic performers.
    He decided he’d split the changes evenly: 2 anthro sea lions, two anthro bottlenose dolphins, and 2 anthro orca whales. He also added a stipulation that the changed reality would include a reopening of the water shows later that day. With everything taken care of, Jeff pressed the Enter button with a satisfying click.


    The threads of reality became unraveled once more, as did the uniforms of the six girls, thread by thread their shirts and pants fell away into thin air, revealing for the briefest of moments the six girls' lovely human forms. But then just as quickly the next changes occurred. The six naked girls went quickly on their hands and knees. Four of them arched out their backs, sticking their ample buttocks into the air as their frames became slightly curvier with blubber. Jeff could already see small but rapidly growing nubs that were going to become dolphin and orca tails. The six girls had also spread out their fingers and toes, allowing for the slow but recognizable growth of webbing between their twitching and spasming digits.

    As anticipated their changes began to diverge. Two of the girls, named Alexis and Brianna shuddered in pleasure as a thin coat of brown fur raced over their filling out forms from the top of their heads to their flipper-like feet. Their faces deformed into muzzles and their ears became pointed ear flaps.

    Moving his eyes from the sea lion girls, Jeff gazed at the next pair, two girls named Marissa and Celeste. Their bodies were quickly taking on the shining grayish hue of bottlenose dolphins as their new tails developed flukes. Their faces pushed out into beaks as their nostrils sealed, a blowhole forming atop their heads.

    Then lastly there was Ashley and Vanessa, and by far they had the biggest change both literally and figuratively. As their bodies swelled with blubber and muscle they steadily began to increase in height, going from just over 5 feet to easily six, patterns of orca black and white adorning their skin. They gritted their teeth in pleasure, showing a toothy grin as they too had their faces dramatically change.

    At last Jeff saw the results of his changes as the six new animal girls began to recover. They sat up and took a look at each other for a few seconds, Ashley the Orca piped up: “Alright girls, break time is over! Let’s get another practice session or two before our debut today. We want the audience to be shocked and amazed by the Six Sensational Sirens!” The six of them scrambled to their feet, running off past the crowds who gave them no more than a second glance as if six naked animal girls were a completely normal occurrence.

    Jeff stared after them for a while, then considered his next move. He thought about peeking in on these practice sessions, but perhaps he should check out another exhibit and then catch the show later.
