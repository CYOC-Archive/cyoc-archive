    It was his younger brother Zach who came to his door. "Hey Damon are you here? I was wondering if you want to hang out today"

    Damon decided to have some fun with his younger brother as he imagine himself visible. "Hey Zach how have you been?"

    Zach was surprise. "Damon how did you do the and why are you naked?" He ask Damon. "It's one of my powers. I can make myself invisible. Watch"

    Damon imagines himself invisible and was gone. Zach was impress. "Wow. How did you do that?" Said Zach. "Wait here while I'll put some clothes on"

    Damon goes into another room and place some clothes on and was visible again. He then goes into the room where Zach is. Damon picks up the lamp.

    "It's this lamp I found. A genie is inside and I already made my first wish. It gave me powers to do whatever I want" said Damon.

    "Yeah right. Genies aren't real" said Zach. "I'll show you" Damon rubs the lamp and the female genie appears before her master.

    "Hello master. Have you decided on your second wish?" Zach was surprise. "A real Genie. I can't believe this. So they are real" said Zach.

    "Genie will it be alright if you grant my brother wishes as well?" Damon ask her. "Yes as long as your brother is holding my lamp"

    Damon gives Zach the lamp. "Here Zach why don't you make a wish" said Damon. Genie then tells Zach everything that she already told Damon. "So three wishes. Oh I wonder what I should wish first" said Zach.
