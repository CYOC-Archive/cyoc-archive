    Mrs. Robertson was a girl, a woman to be exact. She was 34 and had a good job as a teacher she taught a class of all girls in an all girl school. One day Mrs. Robertson woke up at her desk well into third period, she had no idea how she'd gotten there but she soon forgot that. There was a boy in her class, she saw him and walked twords him. The boy screamed, Mrs. Robertson smiled she liked it when boys screamed.

    She lifted the boy over her head and dropped him in a puddle of feminizing water. She didn't know how it got there but she used it anyway. Soon the boy had become a girl the screaming had become nonexistent and her class went on. After class was over a student of hers pulled Mrs. Robertson aside. She spoke to Mrs. Robertson words of lies and blasphemy. The girl told Mrs. Robertson that just that day she was a boy and so was Mrs. Robertson. The kids name was Laura even though she claimed to be Luca, Mrs. Robertson didn't care. She took Laura and shoved her back into the feminizing water, after twelve seconds she pulled Laura back out. "Who are you?" Mrs. Robertson asked. "I'm Laura." Laura replied. Then Laura walked off to talk to her friends. Mrs. Robertson was a girl, is a girl, and she will stay a girl forever.
