    "What is it?" Isaac asked curiously.

    "I also took something from the mansion. A pen." Nat admitted.

    "A pen? Where is it?" Isaac asked. That pen could be be magical.

    "It's in my pocket." Nat pulled out a normal looking ink pen. Except that it's obviously from the material plane.

    "Keep it safe." Isaac said.

    Nat nodded and put it back in her pocket. She then preceded to give back Isaac's mirror.

    "Sorry for taking it without asking." Nat apologized.

    "It's okay." Isaac couldn't help but smile. Nat is starting to feel like a little sister to him. Maybe it's because she looks like a little kid?
