    Rachel finds another Alice in Wonderland outfit inside the package, only this one appeared to be slightly bigger than the one she was wearing right now. Rachel was absolutely confused by what see was seeing.
    "I'm certain there was only one set of clothes in this thing. I must be going crazy." Rachel then thinks about her mother and concludes that this outfit must be for her. So Rachel picks up the package, unlocks the door and leaves her room, and walks over to her moms room and knocks on the door.

    Kimberly Clark, an attractive woman in her early 50s, had just gotten up and was about to change out of her nightgown when she heard her door being knocked.
    "Hey Mom, are you awake?" Rachel said.
    "Yes Rachel, what do you need?" Kimberly answered.
    "I wanna show you something that you'll really like." Rachel responded back. Kimberly then opened the door and sees Rachel, suprised at the Alice outfit she was currently wearing. Like Rachel, Kimberly had been a huge fan of the Alice stories and adaptations for all her life.
    "Wow. You look so cute in that dress Rachel. Where did you get it?" Kimberly asked.
    "It came in a package this morning." Rachel said handing the package over to her mother. "There's an extra outfit inside and I think you should put it on."
    "Oh that's sweet of you Rachel, but I don't think I should. What if someone I know sees me in it, I'll be a laughing stock. And besides, I'm too old to be playing Alice." Kimberly said with a slight sadness in her voice.
    "Come on Mom, when are we ever gonna get a chance like this again. I really want to see you as Alice in Wonderland, pleeeaaassseee." Rachel's wide-eyed stare made Kimberly chuckle a little bit.
    "Ok honey. I try it on, just for you." Kimberly shut the door and began to change. After a few minutes, Kimberly gave the all clear and Rachel opened the door and walked into her mom's bedroom. There Kimberly stood in her Alice dress with a get big smile on her face and a teardrop coming from her left eye.
    "Wow Mom, you look great. You'd make a better Alice than me."
    "Oh thank you sweetie, but I think we'd both be amazing Alice's."
    "I love you Mom"
    "I love you too Rachel." And they then proceeded to hug each other. After several seconds, they stopped hugging and suddenly realized that their Alice outfits were starting to glow with light. Soon the glow was completey covering both of their bodies in blue and white light.
    "Mom, what's happening? I'm starting to feel all tingly."
    "I don't know Rachel, but I'm feeling strange myself."
    Both Rachel and Kimberly stood in place, uneasy at what was happening to them.
