    I just turned into a PINK CAT?!?!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LesterSmith 3 years ago A BM

    Suzie came to on the floor of a small room in the police station. The last thing she remembered was talking to an officer about the MORPH program. Looking at the table, she saw the stack of papers she needed to sign; she walked over to the table and started reading. The more she read the more nervous she became. In the packet she saw all the warnings. "The MORPH program is designed to encourage non-violent offenders to return for their court date by taking away their appearance instead of requiring bail to be paid. Inside the machine, your body is processed through a 3D printer and transformed into another form using a combination of permanent makeup and living 3D printed flesh that becomes part of your body once applied."

    "I don't think I can go through with this," Suzie said to herself. Flipping through the packet, she saw her own signature on the last page. "When did I sign this? I don't rememb......" she trailed off. Suddenly, she began to realize what had happened. The trip through the machine slowly was coming back to her. "OH NO, WHAT DID I DO?!?!" Suzie cried out. She ran over to the mirror. As she glanced into the mirror, she screamed. She wasn't herself- Suzie's body was GONE. In its place stood a pink and white cat-woman. Her skin appeared to be painted head to toe to resemble pink and white fur. Looking at her face, she realized what she had felt on her face. The machine had 3D printed a cat muzzle on her face! Her nose was wider and now resembled a cat's nose, and her upper lip protruded out to meet her nose. Her ears now were triangles sticking off the top of her head, and a tail hung down from her backside. She grabbed the tail and pulled. "OWWWW!" she cried. Her tail was connected to her new body and she felt a sharp pain in her tail when she pulled it.

    "OH NO!" she exclaimed as the full gravity of the situation dawned on her. The mist that she felt was some type of permanent makeup or body paint, and the warm touch that she felt was 3D printed flesh bonding to her body! This wasn't just a simple makeup job, her body had literally been changed. She put her clothes back on and knocked on the door. The officer came back into the room.

    "Well, Suzie, you're free to go. Just remember, once you get home, your collar will activate any time you attempt to leave your house, and it will make you think, feel and act like a cat for a week. After a week is up, you will be back in your right mind, but any attempt to leave will again turn you fully into the mind of a cat."

    "Wait! How will I finish school?" asked Suzie. "I was just about to finish my senior year, and I can't do that if my mind turns to mush every time I leave the house! It's bad enough looking like a HUMAN CAT, but I can't graduate if I turn into a cat when I try to go to school!"

    "Well," said the officer, "I guess we can make an exception for your case. Just make sure you stay out of trouble, or I will activate the collar! You may go now. Make sure you come back on time for your court date, or you won't be allowed to go through the Un-printer to turn back."
