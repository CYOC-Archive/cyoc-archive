    Amber watches the woman as she walks into Doctor Long's office, still sucking her thumb. She shakily picked her phone back up and looked at the new sentence.

    'The pregnant woman is in her Gynecologist's office'

    "Not much I can do with that" she whispered. Unless she suddenly wanted to change where she worked, there wasn't really many options she liked. She generated a new sentence.

    'The pregnant woman hates being spoken to like a toddler'

    Amber snorted, imagining what Doctor Long could possibly be saying. It crossed her mind that the changes were having unintended side-effects. She hadn't made anyone speak to the woman like she was a toddler, yet that was more than likely what she was experiencing at the moment. She deleted 'hates' and replaced it with 'loves'. "Go big or go home" she said, generating a new sentence. She's already started turning the woman into an adult sized toddler, why not go further?

    'The pregnant woman is looking for a babysitter for her niece and unborn daughter'

    Amber frowned at that. Why would she be looking for someone to babysit her niece? And why was she already looking for a babysitter for her unborn daughter? Surely she'd be around for the first few months on maternal leave?
