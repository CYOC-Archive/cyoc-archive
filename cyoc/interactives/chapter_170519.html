I don’t know you and you are definitely not my boss.” Joe was stunned further by this admission but her temper was rising. Even as she was pulled to her feet by Rachel she had already begun huffing. Joe shot out “what the hell Rachel of course I’m your boss, I’m Joe Daniels and I’m really sick of you and everyone else giving me lip today.” The giggling had stopped by Rachel and instead she had began to get angry herself “You are not Joe Daniels I don’t know who you are or what your doing in this building but you don’t belong and I’m going to go tell Mr. Daniels about you and the commotion your causing I was trying to help you too! Joe grabbed Rachel by the wrist as she turned and asked “Rachel what is going on with you today of course I’m Joe look at me! Saying this even Joe had began to look and feel worried. Rachel with more strength then Joe thought possible rips her elbow away from Joe’s Dainty hands and says with an angry scowl “my boss doesn’t dress like that, and he definitely isn’t a girl” before she stomped off towards Joe’s office. “What the hell is she saying I’m not a girl” Joe thought as she looked down at her body and checked that everything was alright. Joe even dismissed the clothes claim by Rachel “there is nothing wrong with me or my clothes” she thought not even realizing the difference in looks brought on by the small red tube top, short denim skirt and ballet flats that she was sporting.
