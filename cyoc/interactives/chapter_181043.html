    Parker looked up at the restaurant that had just been built at the edge of town literally a day ago. He hadn’t heard anything about it, or any plans for the town to construct a new restaurant. In fact, it was built so fast. He could’ve sworn that it was just an empty lot yesterday. Had it really been that quick, or did he just not pay attention? And the name was a bit off putting. Bio-buffet? What a weird name. Either way, he was curious to see what the place had to offer. Not to mention he seemed to be the first one there considering no cars were taking up the lot. “Well, this ought to be exciting. Being the first customer at a restaurant might just be an honor.” Parker said as he made his way inside.

    When he took his first step in the glass double door, a scent had engulfed his nostrils. He didn’t know if he liked it or hated it, but it was overwhelming as all hell. “God! A bit much don’t you think?” He says putting his shirt over his nose. A moment later, the scent disappears and he finally gets a chance to look around. Turns out the sign wasn’t lying. There stood a large buffet table full of exotic (and some questionable) delicacies. Then he looked to his left to see a man with a smile so big it was a bit off putting. “Welcome to Bio-Buffet fellow human.” He greeted Parker. ‘Fellow human? Is this some kind of theme?’ Parker thought. “Um, yeah. Nice to meet ya.” He greeted back. “Pleasure is all mine ‘first customer’! Which by the way is a special offer! Since your the very first customer, you get an all you can eat pass free of charge!” Parker couldn’t believe his luck. He got to be the first customer of the restaurant, and got his meal free. How could he say no.

    During his time around the buffet table, Parker was shown by the host what kind of food is on which table. He was explained that this food was of the finest cuisines all around the globe. Of course Parker thought it to be a bunch of bull, but when he looked at the food all around, he couldn’t help but be a bit tempted to take a bite out of everything he was shown. Even if some of it seemed to be glowing, it all looked appetizing for some reason. “Well, I’m off to my podium again to help anybody else if you need me. Please enjoy!” The host says as he seemingly floats away.

    Parker looked at the selection he was presented. But what would he go for first?
