    Before you could think of a plan, Clia pierced your chest with her spiky crystals with energy bolts! You stand there in shock and saw the amount of crystals that pierced your body, so much blood pouring, you cough some out and saw your body going pale.. You stare at Clia in shock, unable to move from the amount of crystals that pierced your body.. "Hehehehe.. Did you really think I would let you escape? Too bad, I already told you that you will never escape this place!"

    "D-damn it- COUGH! COUGH! FUCK! FUCK!" You try to move but your stuck.. You try so hard yet to no avail.. Is this really your fate? Is this how you leave this world? How about the people you love? The friends and family that worries about you? How can you come back when your slowly beginning to lose your life?

    "F-FUCK! ARRGHH! WHY!?" Tears began to fall from your face as you feel sadness and anger. This is it.. Clia has won and now will make you fade away from this world, even if you try to scream as hard as you can, you cannot deny the inevitable.. You will die, and you will die without anyone knowing how you died..

    "HAHAHAHAHAHA! NOW THAT YOU ARE GOING TO FADE FROM THIS WRETCHED PLANET, ANY LAST WORDS!?" You stare at Clia angrily and upset, she just laughs at your misery while you cry and sob from all the pain and the inevitable fate you will go to.. You said the final words you have..

    "I-I'm sorry everyone.. I couldn't come home.. I.. hope you can all.. be hap.. py.. without.. me......."

    You feel your body grow cold, you stopped sobbing as your head looks up on the sky.. Your vision darkens and feel your body go numb, it feels as if your very soul is leaving this physical plane, till your hearing stops and now you feel nothing.. you see nothing.. you hear nothing.... Your just floating at the darkness feeling nothing.. It's quiet peaceful.. maybe dying isn't so bad?
