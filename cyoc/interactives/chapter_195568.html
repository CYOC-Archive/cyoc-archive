    Amy grinned wildly "Nice job Beth you won!" Beth smiled "I couldn't have done it with you guy's help." Josh chuckled " True now for the finals." Amy looking "So give Beth the good Josh." Josh hastily opened his laptop " Ok the other guy is named Aaron Strom he used very defensive tactics his main fighter is a card called The Living Fortress Arthurian." Beth shook " Any data on this guy?" Josh looked back at his laptop "Arthurian is a gigantic castle-like entity with hundreds if not thousands of cannons it's body is covered in strong brick-like armor plates it stands on four kegs that resemble Castle towers it's head is located inside the central tower only it's eyes are visible. Simply put this fighter is a literal tank." Beth rubbed the back of her head "Sounds like this Aaron guy is no pushover." Amy nodded "Well time for my final match." Beth said "Wish me luck." Beth stood opposite to Aaron "So you're the one who's been giving all the other fighters a rough time huh?" Beth nodded "Let's see what you're made of girl." Aaron taunted "I summon you Live Fortress Arthurian!" Beth stared on horror as appearing on the field was a humongous castle like creature "I summon you Elvin Warrior Emily!" Beth called as Emily appeared on the field "That is a big bug." Emily mused "Equip Hard shell battlements and Castle fortification to Arthurian!" Emily watched as several thousands cannons appeared along Arthurian's walls and various gun ports and the creature's already sturdy body become even harder when various fortifications appeared covering nearly all of Arthurian's vulnerable spots save for it's enormous eyes "Equip Elvin Long bow and Elvin mystic sword to Emily!" Emily felt her sword appear in her hand and the long bow appear on her back "Go wipe out the Elf Arthurian." Aaron spoke "It would be my pleasure master." Arthurian rumbled "Rook Cannon Preparation." Emily quickly ran from Arthurian's heavy cannons as the moved to target her Emily rushed to a noticeable blind spot behind the hulking behemoth's walls taking notice that the cannons couldn't point straight down "Bingo." Emily said " A blind spot." However this victory was short lived when Arthurian began to move it's tower legs "Where did you go little Elf?" Arthurian rumbled irritatedly "Come out so I can stomp you!" Emily quickly switched to her bow and notched an arrow knowing that it wouldn't do much to the hulks armor but it would do a lot of damage if the arrow hit the behemoth's one kink it's unguarded eyes Emily rushed out from the blind spot and shouted "Hey Jumbo over here!" Arthurian recentered itself to better look at Emily "Got you." Arthurian spoke "Now you see me." Emily began "Now you don't." Emily finished firing two arrows at Arthurian's Central tower the arrows quickly found their mark and hit dead center in Arthurian's eyes blinding the behemoth to which the beast began to stomp violently in an attempt to squash Emily who dodged very nimbly and slashed Arthurian's unprotected underside "ROOK CANNON FIRING!!" Arthurian yelled as his cannons open fired however his attack was fruitless as he no longer could see his target so his guns ended up firing ever which way while Emily attacked Arthurian repeatedly "Stupid Castle the Elf is underneath you STOMP HER ALREADY!" Aaron screamed getting noticeable frustrated "I am sorry master the insect has blinded me." Arthurian rumbled apologetically "My attacks are useless if I am unable to see." Emily quickly finished off Arthurian cementing Beth's victory "NO YOU USELESS PIECE OF RUBBLE I WAS SUPPOSED TO WIN NOW THIS STUPID GIRL!!" Aaron screamed "I NEVER WANT TO SEE YOU AGAIN ARTHURIAN." With that Aaron threw Arthurian's card "I release you Arthurian now leave you stupid bag of rubble." Aaron promptly threw out his entire deck Beth walked over to the titanus castle "How about I take you Arthurian?" Beth asked "But I am useless master said so." Arthurian responded Beth shook her head " You are wonderful and strong plus I could use a defensive fighter on my team what do you say?" Arthurian thought for a moment "I accept you will need my cards though." Beth chuckled "Way ahead of you." Beth pulled the deck that once held Arthurian "Capture The Living Fortress Arthurian!" Beth said and with that Arthurian was captured
