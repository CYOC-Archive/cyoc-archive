    "I see that you're awake" you hear Sarah say and you turn around to face her.

    "This is very funny Sarah" you say. "What did you slip me?"

    "I didn't slip you anything" Sarah said. "I told you that I was a vampire and that I was going to make you one too."

    "We can't be vampires" you say, "we cast a reflection in the mirror and vampires don't cast reflections." It was as you said this that you started to wonder about your earlier hypothesis. Everything that you've heard about vampires says that they don't cast a reflection. If this was a hallucination about you being a vampire then why are you casting a reflection? Also, why are you a little boy?

    "You must be quite confused right now" Sarah said. "I can understand that. I was confused when I first became a vampire myself. I'll answer any questions that you have."

    "How can we be vampires if we cast reflections?" you ask.

    "The type of vampire that we are does cast a reflection" Sarah said. "It allows us to move about among humans and not be detected. In fact we are indistinguishable from humans."

    "That doesn't make sense though" you say. "You know that I'm into vampires and I've never heard this."

    "It is true" Sarah said.

    "Alright let's say that you're right about the vampire bit" you say. "How come I'm a little boy ?"

    "With the type of vampire we are" Sarah says, "the female gets to choose the gender and age of the new vampire. The male vampires don't have this option."

    "Why did you turn me into a little boy?" you ask.

    "I've wanted a 5 year old son for awhile now" Sarah said. "Since you liked vampires I figured that you wouldn't mind being a vampire."

    "How does one become a vampire?" you ask.

    "With the type of vampire we are the only way is to have sex and then drain the blood of the new vampire within 30 minutes" Sarah said.

    "So you killed me!" you shout.

    "We do not kill anyone" Sarah said. "We have a special enzyme that keeps the person alive even if we drain all the blood from the person."

    "How were you able to eat garlic?" you ask.

    "We can eat anything humans can eat" Sarah said.

    "Where are we?" you ask.

    "We are in one of our clan's houses" Sarah said.

    "I guess that's all the questions I have for now" you say.

    "I'm surprised that you didn't ask why my draining you was so pleasurable or what your new name is" Sarah said.

    "Would you tell me those things?" you ask.

    "Sure" Sarah says, "as to why it was pleasurable, it's due to that same enzyme. As for your new name, I think it will be Chase."

    "So what happens now?" you ask.

    "Well if you feel up to it you can meet some of the others" Sarah said.

    "That sounds nice" you say, "but how do we get out of here? There's no door that I can see. By the way, do I have to call you Mom."

    "You don't have to call me Mom" Sarah said. "There are sons and daughters that are older than the vampire who turned them. As to how we get out of here, we turn into mist."

    "How do I do that?" you ask.

    "It's relatively simple" Sarah said. "However it takes practice and focus so I'll be bringing you in and out for a couple of days. Now take my hand and hold on tight."

    You do as Sarah told you. You start to feel lighter and then your seeing 360 degrees all around you. Not only that but you're seeing from many different points in space at once. Your vision seems to be coming from tiny droplets of water. You then see yourself moving towards one of the walls and slip through a tiny crack. In a couple of seconds your on the other side of the wall and reforming.

    "Now you see why it takes practice and focus in order to turn to mist" Sarah said.

    "I could barely make sense out of that" you say.

    "You'll learn to make sense of it" Sarah said. "Just wait until you learn enough to spread out as mist. We can observe a whole room this way."

    "You can spread out through an entire room as mist?" you ask.

    "Yes and so will you" Sarah said.

    All of a sudden you notice a strange hunger/thirst. "Sarah" you say, "I'm feeling something strange."

    "That would be because you need blood" Sarah said. "We'll go to the dining hall and get you some

    What do you do now?
