    "Can't until late. After supper, my folks are supposed to start giving me dance lessons," replied Eric, to which Julie stopped in mid-step and started giggling.
    "What's so funny?" Eric asked, uncertain what the 'joke' was.
    "My parents are doing the same thing. I can't dance either," admitted Julie, looking at her beau. "I try, but it seems like I've got two left flippers," quipped the were-mermaid.
