    added by Zoran A year ago I Robot

    Jeff decides to become an Android, “I think it'll be cool to be some kind of robot,” he says to himself, so he open the file. The teen notices that he can choose to either be a unfinished Android, which would need to be built, or a finished Android with an outer shell/skin. “Hm... I think it'll be safer if I were complete,” he mumbles to himself, “that way I could move around and no one could tamper with me.”

    Jeff is about to select the file, when something falls off of his shelve, the boy turns and groans when he sees his trophy from his soccer match has fallen to the floor. With a sigh, the teen gets up from his seat and walks over to where the trophy has landed. After he put it back in place, he returns to his computer, he takes his mouse in his hand and looks back at his trophy with a scowl, “don't dare move, you,” he mutters. He then shakes his head and, while still staring at his trophy, clicks on the file, he does not notice, that his mouse had moved from the “finished android” to the “unfinished android” file. As his computer chimes, Jeff turns back to the screen, a message has popped up asking him to choose a target to be transformed, so he selects his file and confirms it.

    A new message appears, this time asking him if he'd want to be gender less, 'a robot with a penis would look weird,' he thinks to himself, so he selects “yes”. The next message then asks him out of what material his “skeleton” should be: metal, fiberglass or plastic, Jeff selects metal and confirms it. The last message asks if he is happy with his settings or if he wishes to change some stuff, Jeff thinks for a while, but he decides he is happy with what he selected, so he confirms the setting.

    Suddenly, Jeff feels a sharp pain in his head and his mind goes blank as he slumps into his chair, he is unable to move and talk. His eyes are wide open and he just stares straight in front of him, Jeff's hair on his head and body and even his clothes suddenly vanish, his skin, penis and testicles fall off, leaving behind a scary skeleton. The boy's eyes then suddenly fall out of their sockets, falling to the floor and rolling around before lying still. Jeff's bones then change from white to a shiny silver as they become hard metal, then his whole body falls in on itself until he is just a pile of metal. His nerves and muscles have become cables, hydraulics and electronics, his heart is now a battery in the shape of a human heart, his lungs have become ventilators and his brain has become the computer.

    Jeff's skin, which has peeled off of his body becomes a soft latex material, which resembles "real" skin, his penis and testicles morph and transform into a strange device with a big screen and lots of different buttons, which turns out to be the handheld version of the Chronivac. With a loud "crack", a huge hard metalic box suddenly appears, and all the parts of the "unbuilt/unfinished Android" fly inside it, the lid of the box then closes and with a strong plastic seal, seals the “unfinished android” inside.

    Jeff is unaware of what has happened, and he has no idea that he might be in serious trouble, he had forgotten to add a time limit to the Chronivac when to change him back, also the Chronivac is not connected to him, Jeff is just a lifeless pile of metal until someone builds, programs and activates him.

    After it is all over, the Chronivac on Jeff's computer chimes, signaling that the transformation is completed, then the program shuts off and sends Jeff's laptop into sleep-mode, that is when Jeff's door opens and someone enters.
