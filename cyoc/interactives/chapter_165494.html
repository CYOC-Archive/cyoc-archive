    "Actually, could you help me understand the curse so I can use it to the best I can? For starters, what do you mean when you said you shouldn't have read that book?" Asked Jen, now curious as to the details around the town curse. If she could learn the secrets behind it, maybe there would be some loopholes or other magic she could use.

    "Oh my, are you really interested in it that much?" Replied the pregnant women, now very excited to have met someone else who was showing an interest in learning about the town curse. "If you want I can make a deal with you? First, let me unbirth you, and before you ask, I am not actually pregnant, so you wont need to share the womb. Well about not being pregnant, I guess I could be considered, but it isnt how you are thinking. Truth be told, I have a pregnancy fetish. Ever since I learnd of it, I have always wanted to be pregnant. So when I discovered the book, I used the curse to allow me to be so, whenever I want with however many I want. In other words, right nkw I am pseudo-pregnant. All that is in my womb is artificially created fetuses made by the curse. They do not ever get birthed or larger than 9 months of development, but do require all the work of being pregnant, as well as the other side effects like random cravings and lactation."

    Listening to the women's story, Jen was getting more and more excited. There definitely was more to the curse than she could ever have imagined.

    "So, going back to you. You will replace my artificial fetuses, essentially becomming my unborn daughter, though who is already well into their teens. In return, I will teach you everything I know. Once you have learned all that I can teach, I will give you the option to be birthed. Keep in mind that this also means that you would become my real, blood related daughter. Alternatively, you could exit my womb with magic, going back to this moment in time, keeping your current life as is, but with all the knowledge you learned. We would still remain friends, maybe even friends with benefits, but nothing more. Either way, you should expect to be in my womb for at least 3-5 years worth of time since there is a lot to learn and memorize. The decision is yours to make, but don't take too long, I am currently holding off your current curse from taking effect, but I can only do so for another 5 minutes at best. Oh one more thing, if you choose to let me unbirth you, the curse you are afflicted with will be removed entirely. If you dont wish to go along with my deal, I will remove your curse, but you will no longer have if to give to someone else. Ok? Now choose.. Oh, by the way, my name is Evelyn Evelyn Krista."
