    "Well, ladies, the freezer's getting a bit empty. Time's a coming when the butcher will be coming by to dress
    a few hams," she said evilly, "But ya'all have a get out of sty free card, if you can recruit new sow boys.
    Heh, heh, now how d'ya do that ya may ask?"

    She winks and points at the moon visible rising over the ridge. "It'll be a full moon tonight, and you'll each
    become human women for as long as the moon is full. Bad news is ya'all be turning back into sows come
    moonset, so you want to bring your dates back to the restaurant before moonset, so we have new pigs for the
    slaughter at the end of next week. The more ya'all bring the better because well even if ya'all bring one guy
    back, but we need more one-a-yall will be up for slaughter. Don't think of running off now, stray pigs always
    end up as bacon."

    You scream in pain as your rib cage flattens and spreads. It's hard to breathe as your organs reshape. You

    brothers were bi and HIV positive, but your descriptions were vague enough to be any guy at the frat. So when

    "We sowrority girls always book a restaurant and bus and then raid a frat house for hot guys," you lie, "And
    then it's back to our place." You bat your eyes, and watch as the senior who made your life hell practical

    gay, they made life hell, but couldn't throw you out. Now you might let the guy pork you after he's porked out

    "We'd love to escort ya'all to dinner, and home after our traditional Silvery Moon Dance & Photo," Eldon said
    with a grin.

    "Wonderful, Donny," you cooed. How he hated to be called Donny, but coming from a hot chick, he seemed
    pleased.

    What do you do now?
