    A relaxing shower sounded like the easiest way to experience the transformation. He checked the option on the card and turned it over to the back side.

    Mental change options: please select a percentage of how far you would like your mind to be changed along with your body.

    0%- Your mind I’ll be unaffected and only your body will change.

    25% Your mind will be slightly affected. You will have some female urges and desires but they could be ignored if you desired.

    50%- Your mind will be split 50/50. The female side of you will be strong and always pulling you to do female things. However if you maintain a strong mental discipline you can control them.

    75%- Your female mind will become dominant. Your female mind will control your body and act accordingly however you can retake control for a limited time each day.

    100%- Your mind will be completely changed. While you will remain conscious and aware of your new body and mind. You will simply be a passenger inside your new body. You will still feel and experience everything, you are now simply on autopilot.

    Below are personality options for your new body. This will affect how your body appears as well as the personality of your new female mind. (If you choose to increase the mental changes.)

    Gender Bender- Your body looks exactly the way it would had you been born female. No surprises, a simple change.

    Bimbofication- This option Maximizes your sexual characteristics. Very large, perky, round breasts. Hourglass figure. Large tear drop butt, etc. Also comes with the strongest sexual desires mentally.

    Party Girl- This option raises the sexual characteristics slightly but raises the mental changes greatly. Desires to party, dance, dress up, and be the center of attention are always present.

    Model- This option raises the sexual characteristics to a higher degree but focuses them more on elegance and beauty. Longer legs, greater curves, elegant hair and face. It also focuses the mental changes on making you look as beautiful as possible all the time.

    Athlete- This option doesn’t raise your sexual characteristics but instead raises the body stamina and performance abilities. It changes the mental state to focus on fitness and a great desire to wear fit clothing such as yoga pants, sports bras, etc.

    Girly Girl (Princess)- This Option raises the sexual characteristics modestly but raises the feminine side of the body and mind to the max. The desire to experience all the most feminine things possible is strongest with this option.

    Edwin was a bit stuck on what choices he wanted. Should he keep things modest or go all in? Did he want to stay in control or let his new female side take the reigns?
