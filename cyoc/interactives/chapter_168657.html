    Its 1942 and Facsist Agent Bruinhilda from the Axis (spy)
    and counterspy Agent X ( Allies) had been dangling from ropes
    in a awfully deep tunnel/shaft leading hundreds of miles below to
    the Land of the Giant Radioactive Dragons,a place of immense radiation,bursting erupting magma,lava,radioactive steam,tsunami's of towering wet cement,raging oceans of
    boiling bubbling liquidy radioactive motar mix and other Fiery Realm of Hades stuff.

    Not a place to visit if you're a carbon based lifeform.
    However a great place to tan for a silica,calcium,stone,magma
    etc based lifeform such as a dragon.Which there are plenty of down there.

    Ever since 1903 humans have known such critters might exist
    down there.The Curies after having discovered radium,pitchblende and radiation,had gone a little silly in the head
    and speculated that mythical dragons might actually have/do
    exist based on recent strange discoveries miners were making in
    radioactive materials mines in Transylvannia.The miners are
    working the deposits of drako-Thorium 666 and they were finding
    the odd bits of intact rock in these beds of radioactive greyish
    glowing green mortar mix like beds that have been down her under
    immense pressure of the rock above for about 66 million years,
    about the time the asteroid hit Jurassic Earth and wiped out the
    dinos.
    The Curies,always fascinated by radioactive stuff speculated
    that the drako-Thorium beds are the crushed and pressurized remains of dragon corpses.Basically a kind od dragon that was
    based on silica atoms rather then carbon atoms.Essentially a
    walking flying nuclear power plant with wings,tail
    firebreath that existed wayback when and might still be around deep in some radioactive hellhole of a realm today.
    The proof the Curies had offered the astounded and flabbergasted
    British Museum of Zoology for this theory was that the fragments of rock the Curies viewed as radioactive dragon bone piles appeared
    to be in the shape of a bit of a daggerlike tooth,a bit of a reptilian like scale,a bit of the finger joint like dragon finger bone and other
    still uncrushed relics strew in the drako-Thorium deposits.

    Since what had been discovered and shown to the zoologists
    by the Curies amounted to perhaps .033% of the hypothetical
    silica creatures skeleton the most the zoologists had been willing to admit was
    66 million years ago radioactive silica creatures had existed
    They were of a reptilian nature
    Since even after 66 million years of immence pressure
    under mountain and ocean some remains still remained intact the
    original creatures must have been of immense bulky size )
