    Tylie's constitution gradually got better... never to the point where she was comfortable walking long periods, but to the point where existing wasn't torture. She enjoyed her relationship with Carly and Ian when it lasted, but when word got out she came from heaven... and that Ty was giving her caretakers a second chance... She was never able to live a normal life. She was able to observe a lot of the rituals, was pampered by the mortals, and died both a maiden and pretty unhappily.

    Her family was waiting for her on the beach as she was brought back to heaven. She felt the lovely warm waves brush against her skin as she lay in the shallows. Tears streaming down her eyes "I'm finally home..." she muttered. The once little girl in heaven was now in her adult form. She didn't gain much weight as she didn't eat a lot, and was rather skinny thanks to rarely using her muscles. However her family saw that her breasts were still the size of bean bags. When Tylie went to the boob shrine when she was 12 Ty had to show that he loved the girl from heaven A LOT.

    Tylie started to get off and looking at her chest in disgust let those beanbags become completely flat. Those breasts caused her nothing but trouble thanks to all that extra weight, back pain, and surface area to feel that imperfect fabric. She stood up and started walking to her family who were waiting. "Mom, Grandma's, Grandpa I'm back! I'm finally back!"
    Victoria went to embrace her daughter and asked "So how was Earth?"
    Tylie started tearing up and said "It was terrible, everything was so bad, and no one told me everything was so hard!"
    Joe kinda chuckled and said "Maybe we should've gotten her a little more active in her early life." Victoria gave a little glare to her father.
    "It's alright sweety, We got your favorites right here." Victoria said as she materialized a perfect plate of chicken wings. The other recently dead were watching Victoria crying at how hard Earth was. The woman who had the least strife or hardship was crying that Earth was torture.

    Cynthia said "Well those friends of yours from Earth died before you. Would you like to go see them?"
    Tylie thought and said "Couldn't I invite them to a small get together? Ooo! Maybe I could show them Ty! Did Dustin wake up yet?!?" Her family had kinda gotten used to their youngest member being... quiet. They were thinking about how they were going to deal with the around 100 year old woman acting as energetic as a toddler.

    In his main consciousness Ty saw that Tylie was a lot happier, but then went down to Earth to talk to June and Mark. The old couple had done well for themselves, and while still considered devils by every culture on Earth managed to maintain a small collection of friends... who then died but then they were able to make other friends. They were never well off, but they were never starving. What concerned the immortals on Earth was what would happen now that Tylie died?
    Ty popped up in their household when no one else was around and asked "So hows my least favorite foster parents doing?"
    The couple took the greeting in stride as Mark said "Well Tylie was a handful... We tried to give her as normal as a life as we could, but with the way the rest of the world works... Along with the girl being made of tissue paper and wafers there wasn't a lot we could do."
    Ty nodded and said "Yeah, I was tempted to intervene but decided to let things ride out... I have an idea or two about how to do this better in the future, but for now... Just enjoy your time on Earth I guess." At which point Ty went back to Heaven as he plotted about the next time he'd send a Heaven born to earth. Maybe he'd also visit Tylie, it was his fault that for a little under a century she was miserable.
