However, at first glance, none were close enough to touch, and then they saw some fallen rocks that made a bit of a path. The person carefully jumped across them. They stumbled a bit when one of the rocks shifted but regained their balance. They hopped onto a small piece of solid ground before a crystal twice as big as them. When their flashlight was shown on it the gem looked ordinary like a cloudy diamond. Though when there was no light it shifted to every color imaginable like a lava lamp being pushed by a wave. For a moment the explorer thought they shouldn't mess with it. Then their curiosity got the better of them. They decided to chip off a tiny piece to take with them and find out if the crystal was truly valuable. After grabbing their pick they chipped a decent-sized chunk. However, as they were hoping over the rocks a low hum could be heard. Turning to the source they were shocked to see the peaceful colors on the gem now being violently moved across it. The explorer glanced around as the phenomenon spread to every gem in the cave. Even the one in his bag seemed to react. The hum grew louder as the water began to get choppy. Sense danger the explorer hopped across the remaining stones and ran. The moss and mushrooms started to lose their glow and life as the tunnel cracked. The explorer soon rushed out of the mine. Once the light died down a bit they saw every color from the crystal being blasted into the sky. They then felt their pack shake. Realizing they had a crystal they tried to take the pack off. Looking down they not only saw they were naked but they weren't even human. They would later find out it wasn't just them.

With such a chaotic situation unfolding the governments of the world tasked their top scientists and engineers to find out more about this.
