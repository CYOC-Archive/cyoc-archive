    Entity #1000
    Codename: Null-Mind

    Known Abilities: A man-sized, slightly transparent crystalline structure encasing a perfectly round orb with a stylized, black lock-symbol on it. The orb is the core of a powerful hivemind. Small parts of the crystal break off and turn into small, sentient puddles of latex searching to assimilate other beings. Once a victim is found, the latex will attach itself to their genitals, forming a black, round bulge with the same lock-symbol in white, preventing any outside access to them. These so-called nullbulges can’t be removed by conventional means except for foundation tech.
    Through targeted stimulation the victim’s will is slowly broken down, until it becomes fully susceptible to the Null-Mind’s control. It's at this point that the victim’s consciousness becomes connected to the hivemind. They also gain the ability to expand the latex of the nullbulge over their whole body, giving them a faceless drone appearance. They prefer this form when alone or in the company of fellow drones.
    The goals of the Null-Mind are still unknown. Some drones went on to cause major chaos, some sought out higher pleasures and occasionally they even improved the victim’s lifes.

    Containment: Due to its unpredictable and potentially corrupting nature it was locked away in an airtight vault in one of our highest-security facilities. The door features a special opening mechanism that requires the person to have their crotch scanned.

    Other: -There doesn't seem to be any possible pattern as to which people can end up assimilated. Looking into the backgrounds of some rescued victims suggests it’s drawn to negative emotions.
    -Since containment the drones’ goals seem to have unified to the release of the core from our facility. Additional crotch scans for all workers in random intervals are recommended.
