You sighed a little as between the rain and your wet clothes it felt like you were getting a bit itchy. Your homeroom teacher "Madam" Lambert was at her desk along with a few other early birds who were trying to finish up some homework or study in the minutes they had till class started.

You started scratching a bit more at some of the damper parts on your clothes. Then the next group entered in as some high pitched shrieks ran into the classroom as some of the sophomore cheerleaders that always came together rushed into the area without any rain. Heads turned to the... well not girls anymore... Their clothing was soaked, some a bit see through... not that that was the biggest issue as... Well they were BIGGER. Some were just a bit older having just gotten splashed a bit from under an umbrella, others were in their older twenties or early thirties, and only one student was missing... the guy who rode his motorcycle to school and was held back a few years... Some people were wondering if he aged too much on his bike and... may have gotten into an accident. No one in the classroom knew if the water's effects were linear or not so... maybe if he was left out in the rain too long...
    There was an announcement from the intercom that came out as the principal said "There is some weird effects going on with the weather. It is important we all stay inside until we learn more, and to stay dry."
    One of the cheerleaders muttered "Least the water here seems to have lost it's effect..." her clothes had been drying a bit, but she hadn't seemed to have still been aging while the water lingered in her clothes.

She took a long drink of nearly half the bottle while sitting and didn't seem to notice the stares that were going her way. She didn't grow any bigger or anything. The water bottle wasn't contaminated by anything. It was just regular tap water, just some normal water with some minerals and stuff in it. Her stretched out clothes ended up being a bit baggy on her now scrawny figure by comparison. and in the following minutes it seemed like a lot of people were trying to get whatever water was available to drink in the class. Although there of course were still those who were enjoying their bigger forms some wanted some of their youth back.

Everyone went to their seats as she continued "Today a miraculous event has occurred, but we still have a lesson plan to get back to. I'll try to keep thing more concise today, but we are still here to learn." She then proceeded with her lesson plan, and at the end the period bell had rang and... the rain was outside.
