    While Jeff is thinking about what he could use to test the program on, he hears the front door open, “mom, I'm back,” he hears the voice of his 10-year-old brother “Jared” call out.

    Hearing his brother, Jeff grins, “I could ask Jared,” he says, “Jared is always so curious and I know that he has a few desires. He even told me once, that he'd like to know what it would be like to be an animal”. The teen gets up from his chair and walks to his door, he opens it and calls, “JARED, COULD YOU COME IN MY ROOM FOR A MINUTE. I'D LIKE TO SHOW YOU SOMETHING”.

    Jared hears his big brother call for him, he raises an eyebrow, 'weird, he never calls for me,' he thinks to himself, 'he's happy when I am not around, I wonder what he wants to show me'. The boy walks up the stairs and enters his brother's room, “he-hey,” he says awkwardly, “what is it,” he asks. Jared is a slim 10-year-old with an athletic body, he has short black hair, like Jeff, but in a spiky-style, he has green eyes and a natural brown tan.

    “Hey Jared,” Jeff says, “I know you might find it weird that I call you”.

    “No shit,” the young boy says, “even you talking to me feels weird”.

    Jeff nods, “yeah, I know,” he says, “we don't have such a great “brother relationship”, but maybe now we could change that”.

    Jared tilts his head, “how do you mean,” he asks.

    “Well, I got this strange program in the mail today,” Jeff says, pointing at his screen, “apparently the program is able to transform people, animals and objects into various things”.

    Jared again lifts an eyebrow, “ooookay,” he says, sounding skeptical.

    “I understand your skepticism,” Jeff says, “I am skeptic myself. I wanted to test it out on something, to see if it really works,” he continues.

    “Test it on yourself,” Jared says.

    Jeff shakes his head, “I'd rather not,” he says, “because, if something would go wrong, I'd be unable to cancel the transformation. I thought of using maybe a sock of mine or a toy, but then I heard you returning home and remembered you telling me about your desires...,” Jeff then pauses.

    Jared understands what his brother is trying to says, “so, you want ME to be your guinea pig,” he asks. Jeff nods, “I... I don't know, Jeff,” the boy then says, “what if something goes wrong,” he asks.

    “I'd immediately stop the process,” Jeff says, “I promise, I don't want you to get hurt, please bro”.

    Jared looks at his brother for a long time, then he sighs, “okay, fine,” he says, “but, I don't want to be stuck as a random thing”.

    Jeff smiles, “thanks bro,” he says, “and don't worry, I can set the transformation to only last for ten minutes or five minutes”.

    “W-will it hurt,” Jared asks.

    “No, I can set it so it is painless or pleasurable”.

    “Okay then,” Jared says, “so, how will it work,” he then asks.

    “I need to first scan you in order to get your stats,” Jeff says, “stand in the middle of my room”.

    Jared does as told, once in position Jeff aims the scanner at his brother and presses the scan button. The ten-year-old yelps as he is suddenly hit with a red beam of light, the beam travels up and down his body, then it vanishes.

    Jeff and Jared then hear a “ping” coming from Jeff's computer, “okay, looks like it worked,” Jeff says.

    Jared walks over to look, he sees a folder with his name, he watches Jeff open the folder and both gasp as Jared's stats are filling the screen. “Man, the program knows everything about me,” he says, “my name, age, gender... even very personal stuff,” he says, blushing.

    Jeff nods, “yeah... actually pretty scary,” he says, he scrolls down and they see a section labeled “desires/wishes”, next to it was a box which is filled with all kinds of stuff, such as “animal”, “girl”, “I want to have a tail”, and lots more. Jeff can't help but chuckle, “really Jared,” he says, “one of your desires is to be a girl,” he asks.

    Jared blushes, “I... I am ju-just curious,” he says.

    “So, Jared,” Jeff says, “what would you like to be”.

    After a while of thinking, Jared says “I... I want to be a sports bra”.

    “Why a sports bra of all things,” Jeff asks curiously.

    Jared blushed, “um...,” he says.

    Jeff chuckles, “man... you know, you really are a little perv sometimes”.

    Jared scowls and grumbles, “I am a boy...,” he says, “it is in our nature”.

    Jeff grins, “what ever you say, bro,” he then turns to his screen, “so, what color would you like to be and for which age class?”

    Jared thinks for a while, then he says, “I'd like to be a black sports bra. Make me a bra for 16-year-olds, I guess cub size C?”

    Jeff nods and starts to type, “do you want the transformation to be instant or slow so you can watch yourself transform?”

    “Slow,” Jared says, “I'd like to watch the transformation. Could you please make it painless?”

    “Sure,” Jeff says, “for how long would you want to stay a bra?”

    “I dunno...,” Jared says, “ten minutes?”

    Jeff nods and types the changes into the Chronivac, he then looks over the settings, “looks in order,” he then says, he turns to his brother, “are you ready?”

    Jared gulps and nods nervously, “I... I guess so,” he says.

    “Don't worry bro,” Jeff says, “I left your awareness on, so that you are still you.”

    Jared takes a deep breath, “okay, do it,” he says, so Jeff clicks the confirmation button.

    Jared shivers a little as he feels an odd tingling run through his whole body, “man, that felt weird,” he says.

    “What did you feel,” Jeff asks.

    “A tingling sensation,” Jared replies, “you know... like pins-and-needles type tingling”.

    Jeff nods, then he lets out a gasp, “J-Jared.”

    “What?”

    Jeff points, “y-your skin is black and... you look smaller,” he says.

    Jared looks down at his hands and his eyes widen, “holly mother of Jesus,” he says, “not only does my skin look black... it does not even look like skin anymore, it looks more like... cloth”.

    Jeff watches as Jared continues to shrink, his neck and head start to merge into his chest, suddenly Jared falls forward as his legs bend upwards. Jeff jumps forward and catches his brother, lying him gently on the ground. He then steps back and watches as Jared's arms and legs bend unnaturally, “man, that looks painful,” he says, “how are you feeling?”

    Jared manages to say, “I feel no pain, only weird numbness,” before his mouth and his whole head vanish into his chest. Jared's right left hand grabs his right foot and starts to merge together, the same happens with his other foot and hand. Suddenly Jared's clothes vanish, luckily the former boy's crotch is nothing more than the elastic band of the bra, so no “boy parts” can be seen, soon a normal looking sports bra is lying on Jeff's floor.

    Jeff's eyes widen as he stares at what has become of his little brother, “it... it actually worked,” he says, the teen turns to his laptop and sees that a timer has appeared, counting down from 10 minutes. Jeff turns back to the bra, which a few moments ago was his little brother, Jeff decides to snap a picture, to show Jared that it had worked once he is back to normal.

    After snapping the picture, Jeff takes the bra and lies it on his bed, he then thinks what to do. Jeff decides to just sit next to the piece of clothing, which once was his little brother, and wait for Jared to transform back into his true self.

    Jeff hears a “ping” coming from his computer, he looks over at the screen and notices that the timer has run out, which would mean that Jared will be transforming back into a human. The teen looks down at the bra and sure enough, the bra starts to morph. After half an hour, Jared is back and the Chronivac made sure that the kid is dressed.

    “That... that was intense,” Jared says, sitting next to Jeff.

    “So... how was it, being a piece of clothing,” Jeff asks.

    Jared tilts his head, “it was... interesting,” he says, thoughtfully, “and weird... I felt the urge to have someone to wear me... it is a little hard to explain. It felt as if... as if I was not complete...”

    “Wow...,” Jeff says, “so... now that we know that it works, and that you can return back to normal. Would you like to try out more? Become something else? Or... do you want me to turn into something for a change?”

    “Hmm... I dunno,” Jared says.

    “Since I used you as a guineapig,” Jeff says, “I'd allow you to decide what I should become. I'd also allow you to type into the program and decide to surprise me or tell me what I'll become. What do you say bro?”

    Jared thinks for a while, then he grins, "I think it's your turn to become something," he says, "but I am not going to tell, I want it to be a surprise," with that Jared sits down at Jeff's computer and starts typing.
