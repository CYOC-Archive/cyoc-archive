    Now the future Sky King
    called Prince Ida
    in his youth,had come into the
    Skylan University with a
    Cloud humans POV
    on the various sentient talking creatures
    that populated the Sky Kingdom.

    There were cloud humans such as himself and Jeremy Dashwood

    there were the Gifted Beasts
    such as the dancing winged unicorn girl and his roomie
    Exalt the Latex Bat

    and there were the Monsters
    such as giant air dragons,hydras and other man eating nasties

    And as we all know the general view of cloud people is that
    they aught to give Monsters what they need
    and what all Monsters need is to be slain

    Which is great if you're a crowd of human peasants breeding like idiot rabbits and occupying all the land
    in sight
    but not so great if you're the Dragon those peasants are encroaching apon.

    Alas Prince Ida must have been sleeping when
    Professor Ball
    who taught part of Field Studies at Skylan University
    gave his lecture on Origin of the Species and his guest lecturer of the hour
    was a scholarly researcher named
    Charles Darwin.

    As Professor Ball had stepped away from the lecturn
    Mr Darwin had begun talking about a voyage he had undertaken on the Royal Sky Scientific Research Ship Beagle
    a decade earlier
    when he had gone to a remote part of the Cloud Plane where he'd studied two
    isolated groups of Gifted Beasts
    and Monsters
    dragons on one island and winged unicorns on another.

    Being a remote part of the world the Monsters were still relatively friendly
    and so Charles Darwin had recorded
    their language
    and then recorded the language of the
    winged unicorns.

    What the researcher had discovered was that both groups shared a roughly similar language
    that Darwin called Ormish.

    So from a language POV
    winged unicorns on one island were relatives of
    the dragons on the other island.

    After the RSSRS Beagle returned to the Sky Kingdom Darwin had
    proceeded to study
    other groups of Monsters and Gifted Beasts
    in the Sky Kingdom
    and found they ALL had a language related to Ormish.

    Which meant the commonly held view that Gifted Beasts were somehow
    transformed humans,or vise versa
    was wrong.

    Cloud humans were actually the odd man out.

    However as Prince Ida lay there moaning and groaning on the grass with a pistol ball in his arm
    he spoke to the winged unicorn lass in Cloud Human English,which meant
    nothing to her.

    Exalt the Latex Bat,who was hastily tying up a strip
    of cloth around the wounded
    future Sky King
    turned and translated what Prince Ida had just
    uttered in Ormish
    "What is your name sweet nymph?"
    To which the winged unicorn girl replied in Ormish
    "Tabithagar"

    Prince Ida
    then asked his friend Exalt what language they were
    conversing it and Exalt said
    "Ormish,common root language of Monsters and Gifted Beasts.Didn't you listen when Professor Balls guest Charles Darwin gave his talk?"

    Which is when the seed first got planted in the future
    Sky King that his subjects were not just
    humans and Gifted Beasts,but also included
    Monsters.

    This was a more demographically complicated Kingdom he would rule someday then he
    realized.

    And Monsters needed protecting too!

    What do you do now?
