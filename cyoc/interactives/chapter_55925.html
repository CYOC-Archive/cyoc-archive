    Both boys were pretty steamed. Each of them was trying to move, but they weren't working together, so they flailed about on the floor, rolling one way than the other. They had resorted to pushing on each other again, and blaming the other one for what had happened.

    "This is stupid." Josh said as they finally managed to sit up.

    "Ya, it is." Kyle agreed, "Of all the people on the face of the earth, I had to get stuck to you."

    Josh just looked at Kyle for a moment then shook his head, "At least we can both agree on that." They sat and stared around at the broken glass, the chairs they had knocked over, and what remained of the chain. "Now what do we do?"
