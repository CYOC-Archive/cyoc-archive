    “Your sampler, sir. One cosmopolitan, one old fashioned, one glass of champagne and one bloody marry. Enjoy….” The waiter smiled as he slipped away.

    “Mmmmhmmm….” Lori smiled as the light shimmered across his glossy dark hair.

    “Oh right, just you, me, and 44B…” Lori laughed, her cheeks flushed from the champagne.

    “Oh thanks… room 32D…” Lori sighed.

    “Oh…. uhhhh… how long were you watching?” Lori sheepishly smiled.
