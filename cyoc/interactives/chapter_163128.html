He landed between a wind up frog and a rubber duck in cold water that clearly hadn't been intended for a human to swim in.

    "Well done for being a sport" Elspeth smiled approvingly down on James as he climbed somewhat ungraciously out of the toy pool "Now, it's time for us to go"

    "Like this?" James complained more than asked, gesturing down at his less than formal attire.

    "Can I at least get dry first? I'm cold and wet?"
