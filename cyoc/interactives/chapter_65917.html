    A Real Beast

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 11 years ago A BM

    Nate felt another wave of pain come over him and he cried out, collapsing to the floor. The changes were happening more rapidly, and therefore more painfully. His fingernails grew out into long, sharp claws, the same with his toenails. He watched as his hands and feet stretched and reformed, resembling large lupine paws. He felt an itching sensation as the familiar brown fur started to grow out over his body.

    "Oh god, what have I done to myself?" he cried out as the changes continued.

    His chest started to barrel out and reform, along with his back muscles. Blood dribbled from his mouth as large fangs tore out of his gums and his face started to push out into a snout. Nate let out a whimper from the pain as his body continued to reform and change, turning him into even more of a monster than before. His boxers tore away as the changes continued. His body reshaped to walking on four legs as his face finished pushing out into a snout and a large, furry tail grew out.

    Nate slowly walked back to his bedroom, wanting to check himself out. What he saw shocked him. There was nothing left that was recognizably himself. In it's place was a muscular, four legged beast, with shaggy fur, large fangs and claws. He looked...scary. But yet, at the same time, Nate was kind of excited. Provided he eventually turned back, this was rather cool. He'd undergone an even more dramatic transformation into a total beast.

    Nate growled at himself in the mirror and walked closer to the reflection, checking out his large fangs and dazzling amber eyes. He still couldn't quite believe it, but it was him.

    But the question now was, what to do now?
