    More Hooves

    added by zc 17 years ago A

    This time it came from her feet. She tried reach down with her hooves but her huge udder got in the way. She stumbled over to her bed as she began to lose her balance and sat down on it.

    As she lifted her feet into view she could feel that something very odd was going on inside her shoes. It felt like her feet were twisting and stretching. She tried to reach down again but she could only just barely touch the laces with a hoof.

    Suddenly a wave of pain shot through her feet. She heard a loud rip and could see something black sticking thorough the toes of her shoes. When she was finally able to shake the shoe off her foot she was shocked be what she saw.

    Her feet had grown much longer and thinner and had begun to sprout black and white hairs. She could also see her shins seen to shrink as her heels came closer to her knees. As the fur continued to spread up her legs she could see her toes had fused together and were covered in black enamel. They had also grown much thicker and pointier and their tips. Another wave of pain shot through her as the bones in her legs and feet rearranged themselves. When the fur finally stopped spreading just above her knees her transformation halted.

    When she looked down she no longer saw normal legs and feet. What she saw instead were complete, bonified, over-sized, bovine hooves. Not semi-hooves like her hands, but complete cloven hooves. What was even more astounding was how hew legs looked.

    She no longer had the long slim legs of a cheerleader, but half-human half-cow legs. Her legs looked normal up to her knees (excepting a bit of fur). But under her knees her calves had diminished to nothing but small bone and a joint connecting at a backward diagonal to the heel of her much longer cow "foot".

    After taking a few moment to get her bearings she could no longer take the curiosity. She gingerly stepped down from her bed and slowly placed a cloven hoof onto the rug. Then she moved the other onto the floor and pushed herself off the bed. She was surprised at how much easier it was to hold up her large, full-body udder than it was with her old feet. Encouraged by this sensation, she tried to take a step, but she fell flat onto her udder. Luckily the rug absorbed much of the impact but her udder and teats were still sore from the fall. She realized that she had been trying to walk like a human.

    After a few more tries Jennifer was finally able to pace slowly around her room. Her udder swayed lazily from side to side as she moved and her teats jiggled with each step.

    As she finally began to feel accustomed to her new legs, she began to feel something else!
