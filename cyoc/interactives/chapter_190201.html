    Unknowingly to the general public, there are stories of human like animals that roam in the night every full moon. While far fetched, these creatures are very much real. Not only that, but they live amongst normal humans. They are called ‘Werebeasts’.

    What exactly are werebeasts? They are basically what humans would compare werewolves to, humans that can shift their bodies to anthropomorphic animals. However not quite the same. Firstly, it’s not just wolves. Werebeasts can take the form of any animal in existence including extinct species. While the full moon transforms them to their animal counterparts by force, they can shift their bodies anytime at will. Emotions such as fear or anger can also force transformation. Also, wither it be a carnivore or herbivore, they will always bare sharp fangs no matter what their species or form (human or animal) they take. Werebeasts are mostly born through natural means, but they can convert normal humans through a bite with their saliva seeping deep into their bodies. This is mostly used as a precaution to make sure humans who find out about their true forms do not speak about the werebeasts publicly. Note that the human who is bitten will instantly transform into the werebeast species that bit them. Finally, Werebeasts retain their human thoughts and intelligence, however their animalistic instincts tend to show when they get to comfortable even in human form.

    While the werebeasts live in secret, there are some humans who have discovered their presence. How do their stories play out? How do werebeasts react to this? We shall find out soon enough. Whose adventures do we follow?
