    The words "no, this isn't a joke" appeared on the paper.

    1. If you were a boy, would your handwriting be sloppy?

    No.

    3. If you were a boy, would your hair be straight or curly?

    Jessica had always liked curly hair, so she wrote curly. She felt an itching on the top of her head.

    4. If you were a boy, what would your favorite color be?

    What a strange question, Jessica thought. She had read somewhere that boys tended to like blue, so she wrote "blue." The pen's ink changed from reddish-pink to blue.

    9. If you were a boy, would you get piercings and wear jewelry?

    Yes, but I might grow facial hair later.

    Eight, Jessica wrote--he wanted to be strong and agile, but he didn't want athletics at the center of his life.
