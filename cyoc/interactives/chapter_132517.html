    Before any of the teens turned fruits can react Callum is being held in a hand. Callum screams hoping that the guy holding him will hear them. The guy doesn't and puts his other hand to the top of Callum. Callum sorrowly speaks to his friends, "It was nice knowing you. You've been the best friends ever, and nobody can replace that." Callum's banana skin is peeled revealing the fruit inside. Callum looks up at his makers brown eyes. He would be crying if he could. Suddenly Callum feels himself disconnected from the rest of his body. He then notices he's in darkness and covered in saliva.

    Teeth crash down onto him. He screams sharply in pain. He's being pulverized alive. With each closing jaw Callum resembles less a banana and more like the mush he will become. His eaters tongue throws the thirst bit of banana mush Callum down the throat and into the stomach. Callum has to suffer through the pulverizing for several more times before it's all in the stomach. Callum tries to communicate with his friends but he can't.
    The remaining fruit watch as Callum's eater walks away like nothing happened.

    Braiden is quickly blinded. He feels something warm around his pear body. He feels warm air hit his skin. "Fuck," he sighs before feeling teeth crash down onto him and separating his body causing him to scream. Braiden tries to communicate with Maggie and Brodie but fails. He feels the teeth grind him down into mush then he feels himself traveling down the esophagus which guides him down like a snake.

    Brodie and Maggie fearfully watch the guy who ate Braiden walk away. They just watched two of their closest friends get eaten within a minute of each other. "You've got to turn us back now," cries Brodie fearing for his life.
    "I would if I could," replies Maggie annoyed.
