    A box full off bracelets

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 20 days ago O

    Elliot was surprised to see that what was in the box were not CDs but bracelets. And not only that, they were also of different colors and several of them had different themes. There were some that bore the name of a rock band, others metal, others had the name of a pop singer, etc.

    Elliot didn't know that her boss was planning to sell bracelets and other things. She suppose that it was to boost the store's sales, but why had her boss never mentioned it to her?

    Going through the box again, Elliot found a bracelet that caught her attention. Not because it was different from the others, but because it was based on one of her favorite artists. Although she was not sure that she should take it, she finally decided to do it with the excuse that she just wanted to try it on.

    After putting it on, Elliot was surprised at how much the bracelet fit her and how it matched her dress.

    What musician was Elliot's bracelet based on?
