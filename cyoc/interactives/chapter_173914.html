He also had gained 11 bellybuttons and he had also gained 10 nipples.

    'Yes/No'

    he cast his glasses aside, as he could somehow see better without them.
    Then he proceeds to undress; taking off his lab coat, and pulling off his shirt. He kicked off his shoes, peeled off his socks, loosened his belt, and pulled down his jeans and underwear as well.
