    Coming Sites at Mesozoic Enclave

    added by Alphagor A month ago O

    "Hey everyone, it's Sophie and Roland continuing our coverage of Mesozoic Enclave," said Sophie, "Since the city isn't quite complete, there are some places that aren't open yet."

    "The one right behind us is a good example," said Roland, pointing back. The pterodactyls were standing on a building still under construction. It was quite clear that it was going to be a skyscraper like Skybax Heights when it's finished. "While it may not overshadow the skyscrapers in Denver, the Roost is most likely to be the tallest building in this area," said Roland.

    "When it's completed, it'll have an observation deck, a rotating restaurant, and plenty of apartments for pterosaurs and other flying Animalians," said Sophie.

    "And I hear there's going to be another casino that'll make up the base of the tower," said Roland.

    "You sure? How many casinos can one town need?" asked Sophie.

    "Obviously you haven't spent enough time in Vegas," said Roland dryly.

    ==========

    "Perhaps our viewers from the most recent generation don't understand the charm and culture of shopping malls," said Sophie in a somber tone, "These large buildings were once the most popular places for teens to hang out and convenient locations for all kinds of shopping. But now with online shopping becoming more prevalent and teens finding other places to hang out, the shopping malls are slowly dying out and may become extinct within the next few generations."

    "So where better place for one than in a city of dinosaurs?" asked Roland, indicating another large building which was still under construction, but looking close to being completed. "The Great Indoor Marketplace is the one place to go for most Dinotopian needs. As you can easily see, Dinotopians need different clothes to fit out different body shapes, which typically require holes for tails and sometimes wings. It's also where you can get up the upscaled furniture and appliances you'll see in Dinotopian homes."

    "And before you ask, no, none of the appliances incorporate any animals who make sarcastic remarks about their jobs," said Sophie, "Just because we have a stone age aesthetic doesn't mean we're cave people."

    "That's right," said Roland, holding up a fake computer, "We have technology." He then used the fake computer to repeatedly a large fake egg until it cracked open. He reached into the egg and pulled out some gummy worms. He offered them to Sophie and said, "Candy?" Sophie theatrically rolled her eyes.

    ==========

    "And finally, here's a place that's actually fully built and operational, Mesozoid Enclave's Dino Dome," said Roland, showing the packed stadium.

    "Yep, the IAC games for Dinotopians are split between this Dino Dome and the one back in Dinosaur City," said Sophie, "Having two domes means they can do more events simultaneously."

    "Including the Flyer Games," said Roland, removing his shirt to reveal an IAC competitor uniform underneath, "Which me and Sophie are going to participate in."

    "Of course, we won't be so unsporting as to root for ourselves while we're reporting," said Sophie as she took off her own shirt to reveal her uniform, "But we would like our viewers to watch the games as they play out."
