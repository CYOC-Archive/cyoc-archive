    Mr. Thomson looked at the generated sentence and decided to make a change. He deleted "Toddler" and replaced it with "Child". He hesitated for a moment, wondering if this would actually work, but his curiosity got the better of him. He pressed "Confirm."

    Despite his expectation for something to happen, nothing seemed different. The kids were still sleeping peacefully in their seats, and nothing around him had changed. Shrugging it off, he decided to generate another sentence. The app produced: "Jack's young boys sleep peacefully."

    Curious about the app's capabilities, Jack decided to make some alterations. He changed "young boys" to "toddler girls." But that’s not all, he also replaced "Jack" with "Jackeline". He watched in amazement as all of his children suddenly transformed into 2-year-old toddler girls. They were all still fast asleep, but now they had pigtails and tiny dresses on. But he was even more amazed when he saw he was no longer a man, but an attractive and strong woman. Jackeline couldn't help but laugh at the unexpected turn of events. This app was more powerful than she had initially thought.

    As the van finally pulled up to their home, Jack's wife, Helen, came out to greet them. "Welcome back, dear! How was your day?" she asked, oblivious to the changes that had just occurred. Jackeline exchanged a knowing glance with the kids, who were still sleeping peacefully.

    Once Jackeline was out of the van, both mothers carefully carried the sleeping toddler girls into the house and placed them in their nursery. After the long day they had, the two mothers decided to rest and catch up on some much-needed sleep.

    Before drifting off, Jackeline introduced Helen to the mysterious app and told her about its incredible abilities. Helen's eyes widened as she grasped the potential of the app. Together, they started experimenting with it, making various changes to their lives. They made their house bigger and their bank account healthier. But most importantly, they decided to make all the changes permanent and ensure everyone's memories were consistent with the new reality. This way, everyone would think that this was how things had always been, except for Jackeline and Helen, who would retain the memories of the changes they had made. In Jane's case, although she would think she was always a girl, she would still remember the phone and her life as an adult. After these changes, Helen hid the phone in some room in a secret place that only she knew about.

    The next morning, Jane began to wake up. When she was totally awake, she realized she was now in a nursery. And not only that, but she was sleeping in a crib, and all of her brothers were now toddler girls. And just like Jane, they were in cribs, and were still asleep.

    Just as she was trying to process everything, Helen entered the nursery to check on the girls. Jane pretended to be asleep, listening carefully as Helen cooed over her sisters. "Good morning, sweethearts," Helen whispered, checking each of the girls. When Helen reached one of her sisters, she said something that caught Jane's attention.
