    Ultimately Mia decided to turn away and let the issue rest. Sophia hadn’t done anything with the app yet as far as she knew. Another hidden glance over to her showed that the cat girl at the check-out desk seemed to have decided to do the same. Still better safe than sorry and so Mia chose to target someone else with the app.

    Her choice ultimately fell on the two football players, who were still very unsubtly checking out the sorority girls. Both looked like your stereotypical jocks, the only outstanding difference she could make out from the distance being the hair color, one was black, while the other brown. She might have been a fairy doll, but as a fellow female their behavior was still discomforting to her. Time to change that. Mia targeted the black-haired one.

    “The football player is constantly checking out the sorority members of the opposite gender.”

    Sometimes it felt like the app knew exactly what she planned to change. “Opposite” was edited into “same”.

    “The football player is constantly checking out the sorority members of the same gender.”

    The jock was immediately replaced with a lean-muscled female athlete with shoulder-length black hair. While it didn't actually stop the glances, she was at least putting in some effort now to hide them. But why was she even looking across the whole library, when her companion was admittedly equally good-looking? She sported a similarly athletic build and her brown hair was in a ponytail. Maybe the app could answer that.

    “Georgia secretly despises her teammate Whitney.”

    Well, that explained some things. It occurred to Mia that she had never learnt the changed jock’s original name. Though that didn't matter now. Mia was more focused on improving this relationship. She switched “despises” for “loves”.

    “Georgia secretly loves her teammate Whitney.”

    Georgia’s whole body language changed, as she suddenly appeared more nervous and was throwing actually hidden glances to the girl sitting beside her. Similar to what Whitney has been doing the whole time.

    Just to make sure, Mia stealthily looked over to Sophia, but the swimsuited girl was entirely focused on the football players, too. Admittedly, they were kind of adorable now.

    But seeing those two oblivious lovebirds fumble around the matter was now frustrating in a different way. There had to be a way to speed things between the two of them with the app. Again Georgia was targeted.

    “Human football player Georgia is too shy to admit her feelings.”

    It was like the app was begging her to do a more drastic change. But what change would give her more confidence to act? After some deliberation she decided to turn “human” into “oni”.

    “Oni football player Georgia is too shy to admit her feelings.”

    Georgia grew at least by a whole foot, probably more, but it was hard to say sitting down. Her whole skin had turned red and two prominent horns in the same shade were poking out of her head. Despite all this, the powerful female still continued shuffling around like a little girl and did not make any advances on the white lizard girl beside her. Did she not notice Whitney’s snout having turned pink from all the blushing?

    Mia looked back down at the sentence and quickly noticed her blunder. Made sense she was still too shy when the sentence was literally saying that. She pulled up a new one.

    “Georgia is too scared to confess her love.”

    This time Mia chose the most direct approach. “Too” was switched for “not”.

    “Georgia is not scared to confess her love.”

    Right when Enter was pressed, Georgia finally began to display the confidence oni were known for, just like Whitney actually. Both leaned closer to each other, and while Mia couldn't hear what they were whispering about, but judging by their excited expressions, it was something good. Not long after both were hugging and even discreetly kissed each other, with Whitney’s long tail snaking around Georgia’s waist.

    Mis felt great having brought these two closer together. The gothic lolita doll was a bit envious of others that her own face couldn't properly portray how she was feeling right now, though then she'd probably attract too much attention. She glanced another time towards Sophia. A big, smug grin was projected on her screen face. Did she do something as well? Mia had turned a football player into a female oni and helped her get together with her female anthro lizard teammate, who had been sitting beside and crushing on her the whole time. Nothing else had changed to Mia.
