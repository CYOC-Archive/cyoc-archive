    Life sucks, Aidan will suck something

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Dominoday 9 days ago O Mental

    Mark already had an idea what he would make Aidan do, and he would see if he changed him the whole way or switched midway. But for now, he liked his first idea, so he went on with it.
    “Aidan, you will have a high desire to suck on something all the time, and can´t really resist it. Only if you want to speak, eat, or drink something you will not have that need. But the moment you are finished, you will even more feel the need to suck on something. You can suck on anything, and will choose what is near to your mouth. But if someone takes it away, you will look for something else as a substitute.” Mark explained his idea with a smirk. It would be funny seeing Aidan suck on his brushes and pencils, or, if nothing else was near, his thumb.
    Aidan had blanched at Mark´s command, and resisted the raising need to complain first: “Mark, what the hell is that? I will never suck you off, you asshole. I´m not interested in you that way, and thinking about you that way is making me sick.” While he took a few seconds to get his breathing back under control, he raised his left hand and started to suck his thumb like a big baby, not that he could resist the need right now. It really felt natural and good to him.
    Mark himself had blanched at Aidan´s accusation. He had not thought about something like that, and he didn´t fancy Aidan sucking him off. But he laughed when Aidan really started to suck on his thumb like a baby would, as it was more of what he had thought about before. Maybe he should get him a pacifier as a present?
    Aidan, still within his two minutes of remembering, nearly spat out his thumb at Mark laughing, but thinking it was for the wrong reason. “That won´t happen, never, asshole. Get out of my flat or I will kick you out. You … What are you laughing at. Mark?” Aidan had lost his steam the second he forgot what he was so angry about. By instinct, he retook his thumb into his mouth, happily sucking away on it.
    Mark took a bit to calm down his laughing, and Aidan retaking his thumb had made it even harder. A few minutes later, with Aidan still sucking on his thumb while watching him, Mark finally answered: “I´m sorry, Aidan, but I can´t explain what made me laugh. I think it has to do with the feeling of power over others, but not knowing what to try first. Some ideas are just funny for me, but don´t worry, it´s nothing I would do to you. Maybe someone I hate enough, but even than it´s not likely.”
    Aidan sighed around his thumb before shortly taking it out: “It´s a relieve to know you won´t make me a laughingstock or something embarrassing, but please Mark, just don´t change me, okay? Or at least let us plan together what you can change about me.”
    Mark had to fight off another fit of laughing. Aidan having no idea made this even better, he was so trustful in him, he almost felt bad. “We will see, Aidan” he said, not planning on “planning with Aidan” ever. Where would the fun be if Aidan could plan with him? He wouldn´t be happy with Mark´s ideas, and Mark had the control over Aidan to do as he wished. And in the end, Aidan would think it was normal and be happy with it anyway.
    Aidan accepted his answer, happily sucking again on his thumb. He had feared Mark would do something bad to him, but now he had hope that he could stop Mark from doing anything he didn´t like. He had no idea what already had happened, and the light itch at the back off his mind that something bad had already happened was so small that he ignored it.
