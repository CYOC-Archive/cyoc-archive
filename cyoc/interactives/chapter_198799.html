    After an hour, I was panicking so hard, that I felt like I was about to get knocked out. However, I thought I saw something, so I quickly snapped back to reality.

    It seemed like a person walked past one of the aisles. Was I not alone? Maybe they could help.

    “Hello? Who was there?” I asked out loud. No response.

    “Hello! Anyone there?!” I repeated, louder.

    I heard my voice echo, and that was the only response that I heard at first. Then, I heard another noise. It seemed like footsteps.

    I quickly ran towards the direction that I thought the footsteps were coming from. Unfortunately, I didn’t see anyone. Maybe I ran the wrong direction? Maybe I misheard where it was coming from. Maybe I was just going insane.

    I sighed and turned around, only to be shocked by who I saw.

    Amy was standing right there.
