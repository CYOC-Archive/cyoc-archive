    A world of anthro dogs

    Branch Full view
    added by higeman 5 years ago A BM

    The group of dogs completely forgot all of their humanity as they played and happily chased each other around the room. That's when the Corgi accidentally knocked the phone right side up and his paw stepped on the screen, pressing some random buttons.

    Because it was still on the option for dogs, it still had the settings for canine manipulation only. That's when the accidental button presses, gone unnoticed by the Corgi, had switched to 'Anthropomorphic' and 'World Alteration'

    With that, the phone flashed much brighter than before, even passing through the confines of the house and enveloping the entire world.

    When the flash faded, everyone human had been abided to the phone settings. Where there had been humans, now were now dog people, unaware of the changes that had taken place. Breeds of all kinds roamed the new anthro canine world with some special to their surroundings, like the people closer to the arctic being of husky and malamute people and German Shepherd and Doberman people in Germany.

    Though now that everyone was human dog hybrid, their minds have also shifted as such. While sentient and being able to do things human beings could do, the had much more simpler thoughts now, with conflict only being reserved for judicial matters. Clothes also became none existent and everyone was shamelessly walking around nude, though their fur did a good job in covering their once private areas.

    Ultimately, there indeed was world peace, as everyone now just wanted to play and have fun with their fellow dog person, including the Shepherd, the Corgi, and the Ainu anthros still having the time of their lives in the anthro Boxer's home.
