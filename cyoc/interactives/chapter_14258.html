Looks like I’m gonna be callin’ your school too.” Butch said. “I just can’t figure how this happened. You’re mom is gonna freak, but there’s not much that can be done about it, is there? Well, I’m gonna make a call to your school and tell them you’ll be out for the rest of the week.

He was really rather happy with his full beard, though he knew he couldn’t keep it. He’d have to shave it off so he could go back to school, but nothing said he couldn’t grow one now. Still, his new bulk and body hair would be hard to explain. He was one month away from the end of his junior year… if there was just some way he could stay out until summer vacation. They might believe a new beard and a bigger body after the summer break.

    “You had a beard when you were my age?” Andrew said.

    “Yeah, you’ve seen my high school pictures.” Butch said.

    “One thing is for sure, son, you’re gonna have to shave that off; now, if you want to grow it after that, then that’s fine by me, but sudden unexplained beards are just not normal. You’re increased muscle is something you might be able to hide with your sweats. Thank God you don’t have a gym class. Now, I’ll make you a deal!” Butch said with a grin.

    “What sort of deal?” Andrew asked suspiciously.

    “I’ll use the clippers to take off most of your beard and teach you how to shave if you use the clippers to trim my back hair down and clean up the hair on the back of my neck. The rest I can reach myself. After that, we’ve got to go shoppin’ for more clothes, I doubt either of us have much that fits well anymore.” Butch said.

    Andrew made a face, thinking about using the clippers on his dad’s back, but agreed. Both of them headed up to the master bathroom in his dad’s bedroom.

    Andrew decided it was best to not tell his dad about the ointments and return them to the Chinese guy in that shop.
