But Eric wanted to go between Julie Stovall not being there, getting to see Spencer and Frieda in person once more, and wondering how his "peers" were going to react between what had been going on the last two days and the fact that he was now a werepanther.

    In his normal form, Eric found his clothes were a tad tight now that he was a were; but Martha promised to take him shopping this coming weekend, so he would just have to grin and bear it for now.

    "I don't need to pick up Frieda too, do I?" Ben asked casually as he pulled away from the Lawson house.
    "No. She has her own ride to school," was all Spencer would say. Neither of them actually had a class with her, but they would see her in the hallways throughout the day and Eric shared Freshman lunch with the werepanthress, since Spencer was a Sophomore.
    She and Eric held hands, sat close, and chatted about stuff for the remainder of the trip to Moon Lake High. By mutual consent during their Skype chat anything truly private, like scheduling their next rendezvous with Frieda, would remain strictly between the three of them alone.
