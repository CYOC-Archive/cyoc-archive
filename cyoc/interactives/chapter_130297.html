    The Morning After

    Eileen woke up the next morning sore.
    'What happened last night?' She thought, 'I remember I had the weirdest dream...'
    She tried to sit up but soon found she couldn't as an enormous snake was sprawled out on top of her.
    'Oh my god if wasn't a dream... What'll I do? I don't want to turn Eric back to normal, I quite like him this way... I wonder what else I can do...?' She thought, finally managing to pull herself up into a sitting position. 'I know! Our house will grow and change in order to accommodate Eric and ensure he's comfortable in his new form. Eric will now have to listen to any order I give him, no matter what it may be. I am his mistress. He cannot harm me. No one will find anything about this odd, the house will appear normal to others and Eric will resemble no more than a ball python. We have enough money to not need to work a day in our lives, but I do. However I get to make my own hours. Eric and I will live in a nice house in a secluded area. No one will know we are gone. In fact our neighbors will forget we ever lived there.' She smiled to her self.

    "That should cover everything!" She exclaimed. She felt the coils that surrounded her move a bit, "Did I wake you up?" she asked Eric looking around trying to locate his face, she looked up and she saw it. His head was now the size of her pillow. Her eyes wandered across the rest of his body, she realized that now he was probably close to the length of two or three foot ball fields and as wide as a car. Safe to say. He was huge, in many more ways than one.
    Looking around the room Eileen saw that it had in fact grown and there was plenty of space now, there was even tree decorations for Eric to climb on as well as the ceiling beams. She looked at the window to find the curtains were shut.
    "Eric could you open the window?" She asked. In a flash it was open, she had forgotten about her wish to make him listen to her commands.

    'What now?'
