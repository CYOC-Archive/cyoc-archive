    Kyle adjust his tie. He looked himself up and down. He looked fine in his suit. He was taking Shannon to the
    Opera. Okay, so it was the matinee performance, but he had plans for Shannon tonight. After enduring three
    hours of opera, she'd owe him. He reached into his breast pocket to check that he had the tickets, but his
    fingers would not grasp. He looked at his hand. The back of his hand was hairy and get hairier by the second
    as he watched. His clothing suddenly became really scratchy - it was like his suit was made of straw. It was
    made of straw and it seemed to be reweaving itself around his body. His stomach rumbled, and the building in
    front of Kyle seemed to be growing bigger. He reached out with his hand to ring the doorbell. His paw pressed
    the button just before it grew out of his reach.

    He stared in disbelief as he clothing became a basket and blanket around him. His precious tickets were there
    along with a blue envelope that probably had been his wallet before. He looked up as the door opened and
    Shannon looked around and then looked down at him.

    "Aw, cute! A puppy!" Shannon said swooping down and picking up the basket.

    Puppy Kyle barked, "Woof! ---Woof?"

    "Aren't you the cutest, little puppy! Oh, and here are the tickets from Kyle, so you must be from Kyle too,"
    she said as she sat down with puppy leaning up on her chest and licking her face. She opened the envelope.

    "Oh, darn, Kyle can't make it to the opera!"

    "Woof?" Kyle barked trying to communicate - what? I'm right here.

    "Oh, don't worry, puppy. Kyle's sending his buddy Jim to escort me.
