That is part of the reason why they are drawn out so long. Another reason is that because there are so many competitors, it can be hard to rule out which ones don't make it to the finals. Typically the finalists are the most exemplary, the ones who have honed their already impressive forms into something borderline Olympian. As in the Olympian gods, not Olympic athletes.

    Nio had been rather nervous about hir chances here. When shi converted, shi became much larger than average for a jaguar Animalian, even taking into accord that shi was a herm now. Shi had assumed shi would be a shoe-in for bodybuilding competitions and lucha libre matches. But while shi was indeed above average for a jaguar, shi was actually more middling when it comes to Animalians on a whole, specifically for hir new weight class. Even being a herm didn't improve hir chances as there were herm horses and herm bovines competing. (Was a herm bovine considered a bull or a cow?)

    Nio's main source of worry was that shi had told hir family that shi would be able to make hir living by competing and shi was now finding that shi wasn't as high-ranked as shi thought. However, a talk with Lexia reassured Nio that shi wasn't as in dire straits as shi thought. Animalia was willing to support hir for room and board until shi was fully on hir own independent feet. Moreover, hir friends with benefits, Milla and Everix, showed up on Mieni to support and cheer hir on. That raised Nio's spirits quite a bit.

    It was a good thing that Nio's expectations were reevaluated because shi didn't end up taking first place. But shi still took third, much to hir surprise and relief. There are some people who couldn't stand the thought of being second-best, but Nio wasn't one of those. Shi was grateful that shi won a medal at all, even if it was only bronze. Not only did shi win some prize money, but shi was getting recognition now, a bit more than shi anticipated. For the first, second, and third place winners in each weight class and sex division were also featured in a nude photoshoot on Anka Islet.
