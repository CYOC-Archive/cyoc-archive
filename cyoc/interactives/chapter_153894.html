    Dan was finally out of the view of the curious masses, who probably looked up and thought they saw some sort of horrible monstrosity flying over their little township. He bet that there would be some assortment of asinine conspiracy theories and urban legends made purely due to that. But that was far from Dan's chief concern right now. He was out of a house, out of a home, and he was now covered in this bony feeling exoskeleton. He wondered as he looked himself over how tough this stuff was.

    He felt his body, noting that everything seemed to be coated in it, and the formation of his exoskeleton led to the formation of a more thorough insectoid abdomen and thorax. He felt that area too, noting he felt warmth there - apparently that's where all his organs were currently residing. What about the rest of him then, Dan started to wonder...

    He'd been holding his breath so to speak for a little bit, but now he finally opened up those odd openings all over him and breathed in again, feeling able to really get himself moving. Dan noted his exoskeleton felt cold and chilly, but it was strong, just knocking on it left Dan feeling like he was kinda wearing a suit of armor, especially with him at this size and scale. His human eyes gazed over himself, and he started flying through the meadow and attached woodlands nearby.

    He did note it seemed flight was coming more naturally to him now, and he guessed that was a good thing. Best to be a decent flyer when he needed his wings to function for him to get out of dangerous situations.

    Of course being a teen, he started to wonder about what else how sex was supposed to work for him now. He noted it seemed there were some odd plates down there, and at a mental command he felt them open up and produce his modest organ and slightly atrophied balls for a moment. He quickly returned to the normal look - not confident in doing anything with that at this stage.

    Dan was just about to think about getting a bite to eat when he felt a stinging pain ripple through his body, and he almost fell to his knees, grunting, "Aww...shit...!" He felt something insurgent raising in his abdomen and thorax, his teeth grit tight as he tried to urge this to completion. "Come on...just...get it done!" He felt two more legs - at first smaller - emerge out of his body shortly behind the original legs, taking a moment to grow and develop.

    They started out covered in exoskeleton, and looking right at the start more insectoid in appearance and function. Dan waved his head back and forth as he tried to center himself. He gulped and then it finally stopped, leaving him with two more limbs. For six in total. Dan hoped that would be the last of these additions to his total limbs.

    It took him several minutes to get used to walking on his new limbs, as the nerves and connections formed therein. Dan however got used to what was going on there, and found he could easily walk on these new limbs plus his old ones. Dan started to feel pretty hungry and so started to examine some of the local wildlife - flora and fauna both - before making his decision. He was feeling fucking starved from the experience of growing and changing this much...
