    You take the object in your hand and give it a look over. Its a necklace consisting of a golden chain and a dark purple stone that seems to nearly pulse with an unknown force. Something inside you begs you to put the necklace on, you attempt to resist but the urge becomes more forceful until you feel you have no choice but to obey. When you put it on you feel a jolt as a purple light sweeps over you then disappears. You notice the stone's color has faded to a milky white. Suddenly you hear a strange female voice in your head.

    "Well, well what have we here, a boy? This is most interesting"

    "Huh? What? Who are you?" You reply cautiously.

    "Oh me? I'm your new friend... We'll be sharing this body for a while, my name's Cindell" The voice replies in mock friendliness.

    "Share... this body? What do you mean" You respond suddenly alarmed.

    "I'll just be straight and to the point." She responds Cooley, "I'm a 300 year old witch who's spirit is bound to that there amulet, by putting said amulet on you have released my spirit into your body. You may attempt to remove the amulet and hence remove me however you would soon find that would be a futile effort as my spirit has already transferred into you and I have no plans on moving out anytime soon."

    "What!?", you cry with a hint of fear. "You can't do that... I won't let you stay in me... I-i-i-i'll kick you out somehow!"

    "Oh my dear boy you seem to not understand. You can't kick me out, and while i cannot control any of the physical actions of your body i still have all my former spell casting powers. SO if I'm not happy I might feel inclined to make a point... and trust me... you do not want that to happen." She replies with a hint of malice.

    "Get the hell out of me you bitch!" You (mind)shout at her. "You can't just come into my body like a parasite and makes threats against me!"

    "Now, now, now." she replies with a fake wounded tone. "I can't believe you would think so lowly of me! I'm not here as a leech, if you help me out i'll help you out. I could make all your wildest dreams come true! And all i ask is you take a bit out of your time to help me out..."

    "What are you talking about?" You inquire suddenly interested.

    "Well you see..." She begins to recite her tale "I was murdered by a group of pirates many years ago, and while i extracted my revenge on them i still feel a little unfulfilled. I plan to travel to the village of Port Craig and bestow some curses on the descendants of those filthy pirates. If you help me with this task i will help you out... However, if you defy me... I will make your life a living hell!"

    "Look lady, after considering your proposal i must have to say..."

    What do you do now?
