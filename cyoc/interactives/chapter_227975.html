    Edna walked through the entrees hall of the house she was currently living in and placed down the laptop bag and took off her coat. She scanned the house, everything seemed normal as she walked into the kitchen to find a teenage girl looking down at her phone.

    “Had a good day at school Lindsay?” inquired Edna as she went to the fridge and got herself a bottle of water from the fridge.

    “Normal, got an 85 on my history test.” said Lindsay, the senior in high school.

    “Good, but you can do better.” said Edna as she looked toward the table where another girl, who was 14 years old already working on her homework. “Do you have your homework done?”

    “Not yet.” confirmed Lindsay “I am taking a break.”

    “You haven’t even started yet.” stated her sister, Stacy as she looked up from her books as her older sister gave her a death glare.

    “You better start your homework before your father comes home.” stated Edna, she was walking a tight rope. She wasn’t either of these young women mother, she was just their father’s girlfriend.

    “I know.” said Lindsay as she got up and walked toward the table and took out her book bag and started to get to work on her homework.

    “I am going to take a bath, long day at the office.” announced Edna as Lindsay nodded as Stacy gave her a thumbs up.

    Edna walked toward the master’s bedroom walking past the photo’s of Lindsay’s and Stacy’s birth mother who had lost custody over a decade ago. Their mother had surrendered herself to the demons which where drugs and other additions.

    Once Edna was in the Master bedroom she picked out some cloths and headed to the master bedroom and locked the bathroom door.
    Once there, flames began to lick up her body as her human body disappeared in fire. With a small burst of fire her 40 year old human body she was using as a front was gone.

    In it’s place was Edna’s true form.

    Her true form, a form she had taken since the end of the great war between heaven and hell where she had fallen was what humans would call a demon. Her black hair was now totally pitch black while her skin had taken on a deep red tone. A pair of demonic wings graced her back for a moment before she pulled them back into her body. Her demonic tail moved back and forth with ease. She was now slightly taller, she lacked some of the long goat like horns of some demons instead she had six sharp but short horns coming out of her hair. She had gone from what some would call a MILF to something anyone would want to grace their bedroom.

    She cracked her neck as she ran the water in the tube and dropped a bath bomb before she began her soak. Her mind was racing even as she tried to relax.

    She had come to Earth to find love and she had, only issue was that she had fallen in love with a parent. That was an issue but one they had delt with in the past, if they followed the normal protocol they would transform the kids and transform them as well. Edna really didn’t want to do that even though she loved those girls like they were her own.

    “Having fun sister?” inquired a voice as Edna opened her eyes to see a flaming portal floating by her tube.

    “I was brother.” answered Edna as she looked at Lucifer, King of Hell and lord of the damned, her little brother. “Let me guess, my time is up?”

    “Yes, you have 48 hours to tell them or come back with all of them or none of them and rule over your area of Hell.” said Lucifer

    “I understand my Lord.” said Lady Eisheth (Edna) ruler of the 8th circle of Hell.

    “Good luck.” said Lucifer as the portal closed.

    “Crap.” cursed Edna as she thought about what to do.
