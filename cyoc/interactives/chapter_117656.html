    "Having fun froggy?" Kayla winked at James who was looking up at her with a mixture of anger and apprehension "It's cute that you are still trying to resist my spell. Completely futile of course, but cute nonetheless"

    "Why are you - gribbit, griiibit - doing this to me?" James demanded to know.

    "Because I can" Kayla responded nonchalantly while inspecting her fingernails. Any pretence that she was anything other than malicious had now been dropped.

    "Will you - griibit - reverse this eventually?" James asked hopefully.

    "Maybe" Kayla shrugged "Probably not though. You won't be very interesting once you're just a regular toad. I expect I'll find some other sucker to toy with instead."

    "You can't do this!"

    "I think we've already established that I can" Kayla sneered "But don't get mad at me. I'm sure spending the rest of your life as a toad won't be so bad. And I'll let you retain your human mind so you can appreciate every moment of it. And because I'm such a nice witch I'll preserve your human lifespan as well, but you'd better watch out for angry predators because I won't be sticking around to save you from those. I hope hopping around chasing bugs all day every day doesn't get boring for you."

    James could hardly believe what this bitch was saying. It had to be a joke?!

    "Oh, that reminds me. I had an idea to make this more fun and speed things up a little. From now on every time you eat a bug or fly you will transform further. Pretty cool huh?"

    "Well I just won't - gribbit - eat any then!" James retorted.

    "We'll see about that" Kayla winked. She waved her hand and all of a sudden James became ravenously hungry, and there was only one thing that he knew could satisfy his craving - insects. He knew that was down to Kayla's magic, but that didn't help to stop his knew urges.

    "Haha!" Kayla laughed as she saw the conflicted and confused look on James' face "This transformation will be finished in no time. Oh, and one more thing, you'll find I've made your tongue more effective for snaring flies. Check it out and see how nice I am to you. So long sucker, I'll be watching!

    Kayla the vanished, leaving a very hungry James to contemplate what had just happened.
