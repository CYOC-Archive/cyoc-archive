    “Interesting” said Sam looking over the teddy´s data: “There is enough data saved in you to resemble a human. I guess they had a copy of this human’s memories implanted in you. But I would guess anything older than maybe five years is too much for a teddy, that’s why there are many filters in you.” He tipped at something in the settings.
    “What do you m…?” started Theo, but suddenly went silent. In his mind he panicked at that. “What have you *BEEP* done?” he screamed mentally.
    “There, now nothing of that creepy personality talking anymore, I have your voice disabled so I can work without you complaining or asking to much.” Sam said grinning.
    Theo´s panic raised at that. He would be unable to speak, let alone explain his situation now. What if this man edited him to much? He wasn´t some dumb teddy bear, but a human after all.
    Sam was unaware of Theo´s panic, still thinking of him as a teddy bear which was overprogrammed. To him it was just too much data in that tiny bear, so he looked over the data to decide what to do. He didn´t want to damage the teddy, what if the company could track the changes to him?
    Soon he found something helpful in the options. “Ah yes, that should work for you, little bear.” And then he tipped something into the app before activating the changes.
    “Don´t change me, you *BEEP* *BEEP*” Theo screamed in his head, but it went unheard. Soon the changes started, and Theo gained a strong headache. It was over in mere seconds, but to Theo it felt like hours. Through the headache he didn´t realize what was happening to his mind, and as it lifted, Theo couldn´t make out any diference from before. The only thing confusing him now was why he was so little. After all, he was a big boy for his four years, so why was this unknown man so big?
    Sam grinned. With the app he had blocked most of these memories that had been in the teddy bear. He had decided on blocking any memories of this “Theo” that were above being four years old. Beside that, he had blocked most of the connected knowledge. This gave the little bear back his innocence.
    Grinning, Sam then used the now innocent little bear as his masturbation tool in the supply closet for nearly half an hour. Afterwards he went to the bathroom to clean both himself and the bear.
    To little Theo this was weird. He still had no idea why he was smaller than he remembered, and he also noticed how limited his movements were. And then this unknown man used him like some tool, sticking his big rod into his ass or simply rubbed his body around it. His young mind couldn´t grasp what was been done to him.
    After cleaning the bear out Sam deleted this short scene, from him entering the supply closet until now out of the bears memories. He had to be sure that the company wouldn´t find out what he did. Next he reactivated the teddy´s voice and the additional programming for his behaviour, before dropping him back in the bag. He would soon need to watch the kids again, and they could play with the little bear.
    With the additional programming Theo remembered being a teddy bear as punishment for being a bad boy, but nothing he remembered before this morning explained for what exactly he was being punished. When he later played with the kids in the group, it didn´t really feel like a punishment, as he had lots of fun.
    It was only short before Theo had to be collected that Sam unblocked his memories again. The last kids that played with him noticed him pausing for a second, and afterwards he wasn´t as eager to play with them as before, but maybe he was just tired by now.
    Theo suddenly remembered everything, and had mixed feelings about it. What the kids had played with him was mostly embarrassing, so he was a little thankful for not thinking about it while he played. Then there was anger. How could his father block his memories? Besides, this way the playing itself wasn´t really a punishment, right? And then there was this weird feeling as if something was still missing. It couldn´t be much, but it annoyed him.
