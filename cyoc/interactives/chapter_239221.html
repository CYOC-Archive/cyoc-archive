    After a while, Jeff gets an idea, the teen always loved reptiles, especially lizards and snakes, he also remembers that his little brother Henry had been upset, because the next two weeks was “show and tell” at his school, each day a different kid could show their pet and tell a little about the animal, though Henry had no pet, but he always wanted a snake. Jeff smiles wide, if he could convince his brother Henry, then both of them would get what they wanted: Jeff could enjoy being a snake for a while and Henry would have his desired pet.

    “HENRY,” Jeff calls, “COME HERE FOR A MINUTE!”

    A minute later the door to his room opens and his 12-year-old brother Henry enters, “what’s up, Jeff,” he asks, “usually you call me if you have something to complain about. What have I done this time?”

    Jeff chuckles, “you haven’t done anything,” he says, “actually, the reason why I called you is that I wanted to show you something”.

    Henry tilts his head as he steps closer, “what is it?”

    “Well, I got this program which is able to transform anyone and anything into everything imaginable,” Jeff says.

    Henry raises an eyebrow, “bullshit,” he says, “as if something like that exists”.

    Jeff grins, “watch this,” he then turns to the screen and changes his hair color from black to blue.

    Henry’s eyes widen, “no way,” he breaths, “okay, I guess it’s no BS. So, what do you want with me? You’re not planning to transform me into something, are you,” he asks, narrowing his eyes.

    Jeff chuckles, “to be honest, I was thinking about that,” he says, “BUT, I have a much better idea!”

    “And that would be,” Henry asks.

    Jeff grins, “you mentioned that your school is doing a “show and tell” where students can bring their pets to class and tell something about them”.

    “Yeah?”

    Jeff’s grin widens, “I know you always wanted a pet snake,” he says, “like me, you love reptiles, and I always wanted to know what it would be like being a snake or lizard! Are you catching my drift?”

    Henry’s eyes widen, “y-you want to become my… pet,” he asks in disbelief.

    Jeff nods, “only for two weeks, maybe a month, just to get some experience what it’s like beiing an animal,” he says, “but most importantly, I want to see you happy!”

    Henry thinks about it for a while, he then grins, “let’s do it,” he says, sounding excited.

    Jeff smiles, “cool, so, what do you want me to be? A lizard or a snake?”
