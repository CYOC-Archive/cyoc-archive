    After calling his sister, Dan and Ellie head to the park, there they meet Kat at the entrance, Dan manages to convince Kat to follow him to the girl’s bathroom, unaware that Kat knows who he is and that all this is part of her wicked plan to get rid of Dan once and for all, and replace him with her new brother.

    Ellie, inside of Dan’s old fairy body, hides inside one of the stalls, and when Kat enters they both grin at each other, “well, time to transform you back,” Kat says.

    “And you promise to train me,” Ellie/”Dan” asks, “I wanna be as powerful as you!”

    “Of course… Dan,” Kat says.

    Soon “Dan” is back to normal and when “Ellie” finds out that SHE had fallen for a trap, the now 10-year-old feels used and even begins to cry.

    “HAHA! Look at how pathetic SHE is,” Kat says.

    “HAHA! Yeah, I can’t believe that our plan worked so easily,” “Dan” says, “sorry, ELLIE, but I hated being a girl, being a boy is far more interesting. Also I hated to be such a weak human, now as Kat’s new and better brother, I’ll become sooooo powerful with her help… hehe! We’ll have so much fun! Putting spells on ALL those weaklings out there!”

    “I… I can’t believe you you two,” the new Ellie says, tears rolling down her young face, “why are both of you so evil?”

    “We aren’t evil,” the fake Dan says.

    “Yeah, we just want to make the world a better place,” Kat says, “now, I think you looked so cute as a fairy… ahaha! I think transforming you back into one, one who has no magic powers and has no idea how to fly… hahaha! Only a matter of time until you are eaten by some animal!”

    “Dan” smirks, “yeah, and we won’t have feel guilty if you get eaten,” he says.

    “NO! YOU CAN’T,” the “girl” yells, but it’s to late, already she can feel her body shrink, wings grow from her back, her clothes changing into a female fairy’s leotard, “Kat… I hate you!”

    Both Kat and “Dan” laugh, Kat pulls a jar out of her handbag and once again, Dan finds himself trapped in a glass prison.

    “Let’s dump her in the forest,” the fake “Dan” says.

    “Great idea,” Kat says.

    Kat and “Dan” head to the forest, Kat grabs the jar containing her former brother, now a weak fairy-girl, out of her hand bag, and without a single glance, throws the jar as hard as she can, deep into the thick brush of the forest and the two teens simply walk home.

    When the jar lands, it shatters, “Ellie” tumbles out, landing on the grass, “FUCK YOU BOTH,” the little fairy yells, though she knows it’s no use, no one would hear her and those two evil people are long gone. The little fairy sobs, “what am I supposed to…,” she is suddenly cut off when she hears a noise nearby and the sound of chirping.

    Turning her head, “Ellie” freezes in fear as she stares up at a bird, which stares down at the small creature, “nononono! Please don’t eat me!”
