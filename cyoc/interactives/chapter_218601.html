    As Doug stood there, contemplating his choices, the door to his office swung open, revealing the school's gym teacher, Ryan Krebs. With his imposing presence and chiseled physique, Ryan was the epitome of athleticism. His dirty blonde hair and piercing blue eyes only added to his charismatic charm. However, when it came to matters of magic and the supernatural, Ryan was a staunch skeptic.

    Seeing Doug's puzzled expression, Ryan raised an eyebrow and asked, "What's going on, Doug? You look like you've seen a ghost or something."

    Doug quickly composed himself and replied, "Ryan, you won't believe what just happened. I found this box on my desk, and inside, there's a note describing a magical ring. It claims that wearing one of these pairs of underwear and the accompanying ring can transform the wearer into the corresponding age of the underwear."

    Ryan burst into laughter. "Come on, Doug. You can't be serious! That's the most absurd thing I've ever heard. You really think a pair of underwear and a ring can alter someone's age? Sounds like something out of a children's book."

    Doug felt a pang of doubt creeping in. Perhaps Ryan was right, and he was allowing his imagination to run wild. He began to question his own sanity, wondering if he had become too wrapped up in the mystery of the box.

    "You're right, Ryan," Doug sighed. "It does sound ridiculous. But there's a part of me that can't help but wonder if there's some truth to it. The note seemed so genuine, and I can't shake this feeling of curiosity."

    Ryan's expression softened, and he placed a hand on Doug's shoulder. "Look, Doug, I understand the allure of the unknown, but sometimes we have to use our common sense. Let me put an end to this once and for all. Give me the ring and the underwear. I'll wear them, and when nothing happens, you'll see that it's all just a hoax."

    Doug hesitated for a moment, torn between his desire to explore the possibilities and his growing skepticism. Finally, he reached into the box and handed Ryan the magical ring, along with the three pairs of underwear
