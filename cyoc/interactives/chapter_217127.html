    The Magic Shop

    Daniel The Magical Pet

    added by Elflover 4 months ago A O Mythological Magic Canine

    The store owner went to the origin of the loud barking hurting his ears and his eyes were greeted with the sight of a yellow Labrador dog. It was male and looking around wildly, also turning around madly, its eyes darting about. The store owner understood quickly what must have occurred. A customer, male certainly, considering that the dog was male too, had broken an item and angered a magical creature and entity, and here was the result. He resisted the urge to sigh and throw his palm to his face. It did not happen often but it did once in a while and it was a hassle to take care of. The owner would have to decide what to do. Did the victim deserve to be changed back to their original appearance? Or was it more profitable to change them further or into something else? After all, there were many customers for many things in numerous realms. He had a dog with a human mind on his hands. That was a good start. From that, he could do much, as soon as he thought about a good finished product for a customer. The possibilities were plentiful and he would have to sort them and come to a sensible decision. In the meantime, he locked the dog into a cage with other supernatural and magical animals, also in cages or aquariums. Daniel was still furious at his own situation and he kept barking. He did not want to be a lab! He did not want to be sold as a pet! He wanted to be human and normal again! However, he was unaware of what much more could occur to his appearance...
