    Ty's Power

    Four heads are better than two

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 7 years ago BM O

    "This is pretty cool." Allen said. "It's not everyday that you and a friend become THIS close."

    "Glad you're enjoying it, 'cause that literally makes two of us." Ty joked.

    Dustin and Peter weren't exactly feeling the same as Ty and Allen. They were starting to enjoy being in a friendship this special; if Ty and Allen were sharing a body as well, then what would be special about them? However, they were also happy that the four of them could experience the same situation: having two people fused with two heads. And that got the four of them thinking...

    "Do you guys know that old Leapster commercial?" Dustin asked. "That one that had the boy with the red shirt?"

    "Yeah." said Peter.

    "Uh-huh." said Allen.

    "I think I know where you're going..." Ty said.

    "Then you know that the boy gets more heads as he describes his Leapster?" Dustin asked.

    Ty, Peter, and Allen all knew what Dustin would ask. "Yes, we wanna join all four of together!" they said.

    "Then I wish that we could fuse together to imitate that commercial. But I should warn you that we'll start with one head, and each one will grow as a different feature is said." Dustin said.

    The two bodies (with two heads each) were covered with a bright light. When the light cleared, they were back at Dustin's house; Ty had also wished for them to do this in private. There was now only one normal body with Peter's head, but that was the wish Dustin made, and all four guys were inside the body. When a Leapster was place in their two hands, the imitation began.

    Peter began, "All I want is a video gaming system!"

    After the body widened to the right and sprouted Dustin's head, Dustin said, "And a digital art studio!"

    "Hey dude!" Peter said. "Dude." Dustin said.

    Their body widened further, and Ty's head grew saying, "And my own kickin' video player."

    Peter and Dustin both said "Duuuude."

    Once more their body widened, growing Allen's head for him to say, "Plus, a comic book reader!"

    The boys had now all emerged, resulting in a wide body with four heads. From left to right, they were Peter, Dustin, Ty, and Allen.

    Ty then belched at Dustin. Dustin said "Dude?" Ty answered "Sorry dude."

    The foursome then declared, "Only Leapster let's you have it all!"

    "No offense, guys," Allen interrupted, "but I think this is getting a little boring."

    "The four of us stuck together?" Peter asked.

    "No, this imitation." Allen said.

    "Yeah," Ty said, "We should do something else."

    "Like what?" Dustin asked.

    The boys thought about this for a while. What should they do now?
