    Dog Food

    added by Drakkenfyre 8 years ago A O

    You plod across the floor staring at the red bowl by the glass back door. You see kids playing in the next yard, you're sure they can see you on all fours crawling toward your breakfast bowl. You stare down in distaste at the hard dry bits of dog food in the bowl.

    BING.

    "Oh, I forgot to add the hot water," Sam says hitting her head with her hand. She opens the microwave and removes a steaming measuring cup full of bubbling water. Then she pours it over the dry dog food. You crinkle your nose at the smell, "Gravy." She says, then orders, "Eat it."

    You swallow and lean in sticking out your tongue to taste the stuff. You are really hungry, so you take a bite. You look up over the bowl and see the kids next door have stopped playing. They're lined up staring through Sam's backdoor window at you. You swallow. You stare for a moment, then look down at the food, and finish your meal without looking up.

    "Hope you're done, Kyle- that name has to go. I think maybe Ky-ky or Kee-kee? Well, it's time to take you to the vet and have you fixed."

    "Hm?" you say with your eyes wide open. You want to say more, but you know you cannot speak until ordered to by your mistress.

    Sam snaps the leash to your collar and leads you out to your old pickup truck. You start for the cab, but she shakes her head and tugs your leash. In the back of the pickup is a large steel animal crate. You climb up into the back of the pickup and are locked in the animal crate. She slams the tailgate, and goes back in the house.

    What do you do now?
