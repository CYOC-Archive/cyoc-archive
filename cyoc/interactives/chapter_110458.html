    “At this hour,” Amanda looking confused; “it’s almost two in the afternoon? What could they have been doing?”
    “Making me a brother or sister,” Spencer smirking.

    “And a few others,” Spencer nodding; “but first I want Frieda to go up against you.”
    “What,” Frieda asking as she just comes from the kitchen with soda; “I don’t know how to play.”
    “I’ll show you,” Spencer grinning as she turns on the console.

    “They’re fine,” Benton sipping his coffee in the kitchen; “it doesn’t sound like sex; but there is a lot of groaning and giggling.”
    “Check up on them,” Amanda staring at her husband; Now!”

    Benton just grumbles as he takes his coffee and heads to the living room. Entering the room he is greeted by the scene of Eric and Frieda playing a violent video game with Spencer whispering to Frieda what buttons to push on the controller.
    “Try as you might,” Eric grinning; “Goro just punched your head into your rib cage. Brutality.”
    “My goodness,” Benton looking shocked; “what the hell was that?”
    “A great video game to relieve stress and anger,” Spencer answering; “want to try?”
    “No,” Benton looking shocked; “I just came in to check up on you three. Besides, that doesn’t look like the game for me.”
    “Guess you were right Spencer,” Frieda grinning; “my father is a stick in the mud for trying new things.”
    “I am not,” Benton answering back; “in fact I’ll show you. Move over Eric,” Benton taking a seat on the couch; “now how do you work this controller?”

    Thirty minutes later, Benton jumps up in the air victorious; after getting pasted by his daughter Frieda with the predator character; Benton finally got a victory using the Shao Kahn character.
    “Ha,” Benton grinning; “now that I proved you wrong that I’m not a stick in the mud, what kind of pizza do you want?”
    “Meat lovers,” Eric, Spencer and Frieda say in unison.
    Benton chuckling as he gets up and heading to the kitchen; he overhears Spencer and Frieda going a few rounds as he walks into the kitchen.
    “They’re fine Amanda,” Benton sitting back down; “playing a video game, nothing to be concerned with.”
    “You were gone a while,” Amanda looking at Benton; “what happened?”
    “Frieda kicked my ass on Mortal Kombat,” Benton answering; “I think she let me win that last round.”
    “What,” Amanda looking confused.
    “It's a video game,” Benton answering; “now where is the number to the pizza place? Got to order a few meat lovers pizzas.”

    After Benton left the living room; Eric, Spencer and Frieda quickly removed their clothes and embraced their fursonas. Eric a pure white were-panther; Frieda a were-panthress with russet highlights in her grey fur. And Spencer; a black furred werewolf/panther/feline hybrid. Each having intense green eyes; Eric observes as he watches Frieda finally beat Spencer.
