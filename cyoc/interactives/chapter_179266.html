    Eileen's new room

    Ty trailed behind the now 15 year old Eileen, listening as she talked about his powers. The teen though was more interested in what awaited them upstairs.

    While Ty was certainly interested in Eileen romantically, he was curious what Eileen's room would look like. Ty had already altered reality so that Eileen not only thought if herself as a teenager but, her daughter considered her a younger sibling. Had Ty now altered reality to affect how Eileen's bedroom looked as well?

    Reaching the top if the stairs, Ty's answer was immediately answered. He knew Eileen's bedroom was located on the far right, Ty had in occasion seen Eileen in her window. The eccentric mother sometimes forgetting to lower the blinds. Now though, the teen took a sharp left towards a room Ty knew to be a spare bedroom. As Eileen opened the door, Ty's eyes went wide.

    Instead of a spare bedroom, Ty now observed what appeared to be the typical teenage girls bedroom. Posters of musicians, clothing scattered messily throughout the room, even the occasional stuffed animal lay strewn about. "Sorry about the mess," Eileen giggled. "Really should of cleaned up a but but wasnt expecting company."

    Ty said it was fine as he now watched Eileen, haphazardly throw objects into her closet. Quickly gazing inside, Ty could see it appeared to have shirts, sweaters, Jean's, and even a few dresses, all sized for Eileen as she currently was. Ty now decided to ask Eileen about her current sleeping arrangement. "Eileen I thought your bedroom was on the otherside?"

    "I wish," she scoffed. "My folks have the big bedroom. I get this tiny little thing with this itty bitty closet. Man what I wouldnt give to have a walk in closet."

    "The rooms not that bad," Ty offered. Eileen simply shrugged, perusing the outfits inside her closet. Ty now decided he wanted to know Eileen's opinion on this, the adult Eileen to be specific.

    Eileen rubbed her temple, blinking as if coming out of a daze. She remembered what had just happened, Becca coming home, being reduced to a five year old, her own daughter believing her to be her kid sister. She gasped, she remembered agreeing to let Ty think of herself as a teenage girl and almost....

    Eileen blushed, she remembered how flirty and adventurous she had been as a girl. She also remembered how the first time she had tried to go that far she had gotten cold feet. Ty, despite his omnipotent powers, had treated her kindly and not taken advantage of her.

    "Eileen?"

    Eileen turned, taking stock of herself. She was still 15, a barefoot teenager in a dress she would not be able to fill out for a few more years. "Wow," she gasped. "That was incredible. I actually thought I was 15! And then you took me back even further."

    "Sorry," Ty stammered, feeling as if he had done something wrong.

    "No Ty it wasn't a bad thing," Eileen laughed. "That was quite smart and amazing! Becca actually thought I was her 5 year old sister."

    Eileen now gazed at the room, furrowing her brow. "Is this my room," she asked. Ty nodded as she now carefully studied everything. "Amazing you actually conjured up an entire room for me," Eileen smiled as she studied the clothes inside her closet. "You have pretty good taste."

    "Thanks," Ty said meekly.

    Eileen chuckled, rubbing her chin thoughtfully. Ty's power was truely amazing if he could not only change her but change the fabric of reality. She now wondered what else he had in store for her.
