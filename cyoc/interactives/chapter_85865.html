    “Tiffany,” Jenny begins, “I may be able to help you with that.”

    “Really, how?” Tiffany said with little indication of hope; as if she heard things like this before.

    “This ring can… do things; things which… cannot be explained by science.”

    “What, like magic or something? Give me a break!”

    “It’s true; in fact, it’s what made your clothes vanish earlier today; but if you need further proof…” Holding her ringed hand up, Jenny says, “I wish my hair was pink.” From scalp to end, Jenny’s hair changed to a vibrant pink color.

    “It… really is magic!” Tiffany said.

    “And I can use it to help our situation.”

    “How?”

    “I could make our parents accept us as lesbians.”

    Tiffany grabbed Jenny’s shoulders. Her face scowled, she said, “Don’t you dare!”

    “But, didn’t you just say you want them to accept you?” Jenny asked.

    Tiffany let go of Jenny. “More than anything; but I want it to be of their own free will. They want to impose their beliefs on us, but if we use some magic trinket to alter their beliefs, we’ll be just as bad as they are; maybe even worse because we’re practically brainwashing them.”

    “You’re right, Tiffany; this is too dangerous a precedent to set; and too dangerous a power to wield. I wish this ring lost the power to alter free will!” Sparks flew out of the ring, as if it was short-circuiting.

    “Did it work?” Tiffany asked.

    “Well, let’s find out.” Jenny answered. “After I make this wish, start kissing me.”

    ”Okay.”

    ”I wish to be straight.” As Tiffany agreed, she grabbed Jenny and started to kiss her. Jenny was no less enjoying this than she was before; and a slip of her tongue into Tiffany’s mouth told her that the wish worked. However, Jenny was still wistful. “Still I wish I could use this ring to help us in some way.”

    After a few moments, Tiffany spoke up. “We could wish ourselves away to somewhere where we’ll be accepted as lesbians.”

    “Isn’t that like running away from our problems?” Jenny asked.

    “Think of it more as escaping a place that’s just not ready for us quite yet.” Tiffany answered.

    After a minute or so, Jenny agreed. “I wish for me and Jenny to be taken to a place where two girls in love is accepted.” The ring instantly sprang to life; a swirling mass of light and color encircled them. The two girls held each other tight as it felt as they were floating in midair. The sensation was brief as suddenly; they were standing on the ground and the light faded out to reveal where they had been transported.
