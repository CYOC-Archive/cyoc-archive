    "Oh man, I'm so sorry I got you stuck down here, I was playing football with one of my pals when I had felt something against my back and next thing I know I see you fall down here!"

    "Whoa, wait a sec, what about your friend is he here too?" you ask, maybe his friend could get help.

    The teenager's trembling increased, clearly he couldn't cope with stress very well, you think.

    "No, Kev, saw what had happened called me a murdered and ran away so he wouldn't be blamed. I tried going down the hole to see if you were alright but then I lost my footing and fell. At least I know your okay. I'm so so sorry, please forgive me!" the teenager says with his shaky voice.

    "It's Jo-Joshua, Joshua Pattingham." Joshua says.
