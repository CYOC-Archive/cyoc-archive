Your reflection blurs as you squeeze your eyes shut, drowning in waves of exhilaration.

You reach up, feeling your skin, or rather, the surface of the symbiote that’s now a part of you, alive and tingling beneath your fingertips. It shivers at your touch, responding to your movements, as if thrilled to be with you.
