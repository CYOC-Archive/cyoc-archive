    As Yevgeny crept through the Palace halls, he kept his ears primed for any noises. When he walked past the closed doors to the music room, he heard muffled grunts and groans. He opened the door a crack to look inside. On the couch in the room, two fully nude young people were having very vigorous sex. Judging by the woman's blonde hair and lovely features, she was one of the octuplets recently returned to her proper form, though Yevgeny was uncertain of which one. The man, however, he knew. Owain had been on the side of Antyny until he lost his father in the war. After that, he switched sides to the Royalists and began acting as a double agent for them. Yevgeny had heard that Owain managed to talk his way into an engagement with one of the Princesses. This must be his betrothed.

    The Princess began to moan more passionately, as Owain's thrusts increased in tempo. Yevgeny quickly closed the door and resumed walking through the halls. He passed by a bedroom with the door wide open. Inside, the Crown Prince of Estrellia, Julio, was fucking an incredibly busty redheaded Princess.

    "Oh, yes!" the Princess screamed. "Put your seed in me, Julio, make me heavy with child!"

    "I will do just that, my love," Julio grunted. Yevgeny silently closed the door. As he traveled through the Palace to find Antyny, he passed by six other couples, all Princesses engaged in incredibly passionate sex with their betrothed. He rolled his eyes. Anya had been given the task of casting a spell that would keep the octuplets out of the war and safe from harm while the throne was taken back. Of course she went about this by using a sex spell. She may have left the harem, but she was still a concubine through and through.

    Yevgeny could hear faint sounds of battle as their forces fought with guards. The septuplets would be joining him soon to find Antyny. What they would do when they found him, they hadn't yet decided. But by the end of the day, he would no longer be King.

    No doubt Antyny was already on edge from noticing his prized sons turning to lovely daughters. Hopefully, he was so distracted dealing with that, he wouldn't notice Yevgeny and the septuplets until it was too late.
