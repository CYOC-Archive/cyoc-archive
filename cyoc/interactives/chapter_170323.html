    Rachel thought long and hard and decided she’d use the camera on her friend. Sure, she enjoyed her friendship with Emiko, but Rachel’s curiosity was too big. Besides, if she didn’t like Emiko as a sporty girl, she could always change her back. At least she hoped. Hell, she didn’t even know if she would even be able to transform Emiko into what she wants at all!

    “Alright Em, I’ve finished up here,” Rachel announced, grabbing the bag containing her purchased items. “Now what store did you say you wanted to go to next?”

    “I’m sure I told you before, I wanted to go to Barnes & Noble.” Emiko answered, her mind still a bit foggy after the photo Rachel took earlier at the food court.

    “Well let’s head there then.” Rachel said, deciding she’d try and change Emiko there.

    As the girls approached the store, Emiko entered while Rachel stayed outside with the camera.

    “You go on ahead Emiko. I wanna stay out here and play with the camera a little.” She said, doing her best to remain inconspicuous.

    “Alright then. I won’t be long anyways.” Emiko replied before continuing into the shop.

    Rachel raised the camera to her face and aimed at Emiko. She focused really hard on what she wanted Emiko to become before pressing the button.

    Emiko browsed the books, looking for anything that peaked her interest. “I’ll tell Rachel that I have to leave after this.” Rachel thought to herself. “Gotta make sure I get some practice in for that upcoming maths test.”

    FLASH

    “-Gotta make sure I get some practice in for that upcoming soccer game.” Eliza corrected herself. Wait, why was she in a bookstore? She quickly left to meet with Rachel outside.

    “Hey Rach? Stupid question, but do you know why we’re at Barnes & Noble?” Eliza said, walking out.

    “Uh… we were just getting books for… school.” Rachel barely managed to get out as she inspected her creation.

    Gone was the nerdy asian Rachel once knew and instead stood a fit, causcasian girl of the same age. She was similar to Rachel with yoga pants and a pink athletic top cradling her b-cup breasts. Her short raven black hair had been transformed into wavy brown hair that reached Eliza’s shoulders. No longer was Rachel the tallest of the two as Eliza’s new body made it so that they were both equal in height.

    Uh, Eliza?” Rachel said, suddenly remembering her friend’s new name. “You remember how we met, right?” She wanted to know how much reality had been altered.

    “Of course I remember, girl!” Eliza giggled. “We met on the track team two years ago.”

    Rachel suddenly recalled a set of memories of her and Eliza, alongside her original memories of herself and Emiko.

    Eliza then snapped Rachel out of her trance. “Sorry Rach, but I’ve gotta head off now, soccer practice is tonight.”

    “Oh that’s fine. I need to get going myself.”

    “Hey! Why don’t you come with me?” Eliza chirped. “You’ve been telling me for ages how you’ve wanted to join the soccer team and now’s the perfect chance!”

    Rachel recalled the memory of her telling Eliza she wanted to join, despite already playing numerous sports.
