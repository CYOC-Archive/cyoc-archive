    Ty's Power

    The Rat's Away, The Mice Will Play

    With Dustin out of the house for a few days, Ty could finally do all the things he liked without having to
    worry about his big evil step brother bullying or bossing him or his friends around all the time. So, Ty
    wished for a large bowl of popcorn and he sat on the couch watching several of his favorite movies that Dustin
    never allowed him to watch.

    Then, Ty went into his bedroom and wished for Dustin's big TV and Xbox to magically teleport into his bedroom
    so that he could play all the games he wanted. He spent the entire day playing all the games he could think
    of, until he grew bored of it.

    Finally, he got on the computer and he messaged all of Dustin's friends and girl friends, since he wished to
    know all of his passwords to all of his accounts online! He checked all of Dustin's emails, his chats, his
    messages, and even his cell phone! It was great! Ty had total and unlimited access to everything that Dustin
    had!

    Lastly, Ty wanted to have a friend over, which was never allowed because Dustin always had his friends over
    and then Ty was stuck in his room all alone. So, Ty thought about the one kid who Dustin hated because he was
    a bit nerdy and small and skinny, and Dustin always made fun of him at school. Ty thought of his friend Andy,
    so he picked up Dustin's cell phone and called him, wishing for Andy to answer the phone.

    Ty talked to Andy and invited the geeky boy over. Andy was about to walk over, which wouldn't take long anyway
    since they lived just a few blocks down the street from each other. Ty wished for Andy to appear in his
    bedroom and the short, nerdy boy instantly teleported to his room!

    "Hey! Ty! Whoa! How... How did I get in your room?" Andy wondered out loud. The short, skinny boy with black
    hair was only 2 inches shorter than Ty, but he always hung out with Ty at school. He hated Dustin with passion
    and no wonder: Dustin treated him terribly, so he was always nervous about going to Ty's house.

    Ty wished for Andy to know and understand everything about his powers so he didn't have to explain anything to
    him anymore.

    "Whoa! That's so cool, Ty! So, you can do anything just by wishing it?" Andy asked his friend.

    "Yeah! I turned Dustin into a rat and I sent him into the sewer for a few days so we can hang out together!"
    Ty explained. "I'm gonna have to bring him back, eventually!"

    "Aww crap! You better get rid of me, then!" Andy sighed.

    "Well... How about this... I'll get rid of you, but I'll bring you back after Dustin is returned to normal!"
    Ty grinned. "I promise you, he won't bug you! He won't even try!"

    "But Dustin ALWAYS picks on me! Because I'm so small and weak and timid..." Andy cried out.

    "Dude! I got it! I will create a dimension for you when your muscles and your body will grow!" Ty cheered, as
    he snapped his fingers, making Andy's clothes disappear and re-appear again on a pile in the corner of Ty's
    bedroom. Andy was left wearing only super-stretchy Spandex pants in the center of Ty's room!

    "Whoa! My clothes!"

    "Now! Go and grow for me in the G-Zone!" Ty ordered.

    Ty snapped his fingers and Andy vanished into thin air.

    Andy found himself floating in space... But there were no stars. Everywhere he looked, he was surrounded by
    darkness. He was in the G-Zone! This was a special dimension that Ty had created for him!

    "Whoa! I'm in some weird dimension that Ty has created for me! This is so cooool..."

    Andy just floated around in nothingness. He was all alone. He drifted around like he was swimming in water.

    Then, suddenly, his body began to tingle!

    Slowly, his muscles began to swell! His chest began to inflate, as well as his calves, his quads, and his
    pecs. Every muscle was growing!

    "Whoa... I'm growing! he whispered. "I wonder what Ty's doing now?"

    Andy realized he was stuck in this dimension until Ty would teleport him out...
