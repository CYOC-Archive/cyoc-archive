She lives with a werewolf pack that doesn't know she's started this herehyena pack, and she'd be in a ton of trouble if she changed race." A peculiar look came over Abbie's eyes. "I might be interested in a change, though."

    "Sure," said Abbie. "I'm a foster kid, and my foster family, the Kriegers, are neglectful at best and abusive at worst. I'm thinking about running away, and changing my race would make it a lot harder for Mr. and Mrs, Krieger to find me."

Whatever the reason, all I've known is bouncing from shitty foster home to shitty foster home. My only escape's been basketball. Oh well, it beats getting your left nut ripped off, I guess."
