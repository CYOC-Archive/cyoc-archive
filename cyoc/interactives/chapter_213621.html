    Georgia gaped. "So, can you punch a hole through the wall?"

"I am superhuman, now, Mistress, or rather, super lupine. You may not grasp it, but I can do most any impossible task you set me."

    Now standing at a massive 15 feet tall, Butternut's shy exterior and dainty frame had been replaced by a behemoth of pure muscle. Her pecs alone would be the size of waterbeds, and ripples with each heaving breath. Her tree trunk arms were stuck to her sides, pinned by the walls of the tiny room. Her legs, each easily three times the thickness of Georgia's body, were thick with veins, protruding even from beneath her now short, coarse coat. Behind her ridiculously small spectacles, Butternut glowered at her mistress, her lip curling into a fanged sneer.

    "Do you realise what you have done?" she rumbled. Her voice was far lower than before, a dark, aggressive, dominant growl. "Do you have any clue what the fuck you just did?"

    Georgia, too awestruck to speak, shook her head.

    Butternut gave out a growl of frustration, her jowls snapping together irritably. "You know how I said I was 8 times stronger before? Take a guess how powerful I am now."

The wolf held onto Georgia tight, moving even quicker than her own shockwave to encase her mistress in her massive paws, her indestructible muscle a perfect shield. "I can survive a nuclear war. I could survive the death of the universe. I could obliterate you and everyone on this planet with nothing but a clap of my hands. You have made a god, Mistress, do you understand?"

The predatory look Butternut had, her intense, intimidating appearance made her instincts scream to run from the monster, but she could feel her arm encased by Butternut's thumb and forefinger, and knew it would be trivially easy to snap should she move.

I'm not going to do anything with it. I'll control the power and use it only to serve you best, Mistress. After all, I was put upon this Earth only to fulfil your every desire. Should you wish for me to continue growing, for me to become larger and stronger, I will have no choice but to obey. But please, Mistress, allow me to stop now.
