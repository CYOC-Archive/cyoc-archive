    Ty decided to have himself a good time at Bunky's Gym. He earned himself a free workout, so he figured he
    might as well enjoy himself. He walked over to the front of the vast weight room, where the treadmills and the
    stationary bikes were positioned neatly side-by-side, and he decided to sit on one of the bikes at the very
    end so that he wouldn't be bothered. On the other hand, he was seated on the bike which was the closest to the
    door, so he would see every single person who would come in and leave the weight room area.

    Ty started pedaling and just looked around, as he stuck his ear buds in his ears and selected an album on his
    iPod Touch music player. A few minutes had gone by, when two people walked through the door. It was a father
    and his teenage son. They had sparked Ty's interest, so Ty paused the song on his iPod so he could listen in
    on the conversation between the man, his son, and the trainer.

    It was obviuous that the trainer was giving a newcomer's tour of the facility. These two were new members and
    they had just signed up. Interesting! The man was over six feet tall, he had a bit of a beer guy and he had a
    bald spot on his head. He wore glasses and he came dressed wearing a t-shirt and shorts. The kid was about 5
    foot 6 inches tall, he looked like he was about fourteen years old, he had long straight jet black hair, he
    had long but skinny arms and skinny, thin legs. He was completely smooth and hairless.

    The trainer led the pair around the gym, showing them all the machines, the sauna, the locker room, the
    computer lab, the classroom, the aurobics room, and where the pool was located. Then, the father and son had
    filled out paperwork before returning to the weight room. They had dropped off their bags in the locker room
    and now they were ready for their very first workout at the gym.

    Ty was curious and he silently wished for the boy to suddenly have a desire to use the empty bike right next
    to his own.

    The boy then walked up to the bike and adjusted the seat. Then, he climbed on and looked at the display before
    turning towards Ty.

    "Uhh... Excuse me? Do you know how these machines work?" the boy asked him.

    "Absolutely! Just start pedaling and then press the Start button. Once the display comes up, press the number
    on the Level indicator and you're all set!" Ty explained, while pointing to the diaplay. "I'll help you, since
    I'm doing the same exercise as you. My name is Ty, by the way!"

    "Oh... Nice to meet you... I'm Isaiah!" the boy replied.

    Then, the father walked up to him.

    "Don't wear yourself out! This is your first day!" he said to his son.

    "I'm not a little kid, dad! I can handle it!" Isaiah replied.

    Ty just grinned. He could tell that the boy was annoyed by his overprotective father. Maybe it was time for
    the boy to become a little older and bigger... perhaps even a little bigger than the father? Then the boy
    could tell the father take it easy instead!

    Ty smiled as he silently wished for Isaiah to grow a year older and gain 10 pounds of muscle for every minute
    he exercises on the bike!

    The skinny boy, who probably weighed about 150 pounds, quietly started pedaling away. The timer on the bike
    started ticking as well, indicating the duration of the session.

    As the timer kept on running, the boy's legs started growing longer and his arms became stretched out as well.
    His torso grew wider and patches of hair started to sprout all over his chest, his arms, and his legs. His
    chest started to bulge outward a bit while biceps started to form. His calves also inflated, while his quads
    began to fill out his shorts very slowly. The timer indicated the first 5 minutes, so he had gained 5 years in
    age and 50 pounds in muscle!

    As time went on, more hair appeared on his arms, his legs, his chest, and even on his shin. The boy suddenly
    has a growth spurt, and starts growing taller and taller as well. His legs grow even longer as he gains more
    muscle at the same time while he continues to age as well. His voice grows deeper as a smooth mustache appears
    above his lips. The boy becomes a young man now, no longer a teenager.

    Ten minutes have passed and he has gained 100 pounds of muscle and he is now 24 years old. He weighs 250
    pounds!

    Finally, he stops pedalling and looks at Ty. He is now 6 foot 3 inches tall, and as he gets off the bike, he
    towers over Ty as he shakes Ty's hand.

    "Thanks for helping me!" he says in a deep, baritone voice.

    Then, he walked up to his father, who was lifting dumbbells.

    "Be careful, pops! Don't wear yourself out! This is our first day!" the man said to his father, who was three
    inches shorter than him.

    Ty only laughed as he looked on from a distance.

    He was already wondering who to grow next...
