    Terry decided to see if she was up for it, and so looked her way and asked, "Hey...uh, Sarah...?"

    "Yeah Terry?" She brightened up. He was so cute like this. So much smaller, grinning so confident seeming. She was reminded why she was so taken with him.

    "Can I uh...kiss you?" He blurted it out, and was kind of surprised by how taboo it felt.

    She assented, but was quickly feeling like maybe they should get to work soon on putting this to rights. But then...Terry was so incredibly into this, and she didn't want to hurt his feelings by stopping too soon. This might be all he could do at present...
