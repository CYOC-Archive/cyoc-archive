Unlike this shitty situation he’s got himself involved in.

The class was silent, even the professor himself.

It is a bummer that he cannot see the memories of the vessel he possessed. Instead of friends, three large men stood menacingly over him. One at the front and two at the sides.

Eating your own lunch while we’re here starving because you failed to do your job.” The one in the middle and most probably their leader said. It’s cliche really. Even those at his sides said their yeas and nodded their head at their leader’s statement.

    “Why don’t you get your own food?” Tak took a bite of his lunch.

    “Wh- wha?” The leader was taken aback. He did not expect that response. “I think you’re not getting the picture here. You think we’d just let you go?”

    The surrounding crowd must have sensed trouble brewing. They were so kind to give them space.

    Tak wiped off his lip and stepped into the open space at the back of the classroom.

    “Where the hell do you think you're going?” The leader snarled. He must be so angry because his forehead is turning red and veins are popping out.

    “Aren’t we going to fight? Or are you just going to waste your spit by barking at me?”

    “Little puppy got his canines out,” one of the minions butted in.

    “Let’s see how this little puppy reacts when the big dog shows him a real bite.” The leader snapped back.

He expected something fast, something this body’s eye could not keep up with. Tak stepped to the side and tapped a small punch at the bully’s jaw. It hurt the bully but it also damaged Tak’s knuckles. Fuck. This guy needs to work out.

But they saw it and it was real. Genta was taken aback further than he already was. He didn’t even see where the punch came from.
