    Serys rode for what seemed like hours. She loved riding by the country side; the vast swaths of open green land interrupted only by tiny homes and the various herds of animals that the farmers who lived there tended to.

    Feeling the cool wind blow past her, Serys felt a sense of calm. When she went on her royal visits to these lands, everything was strictly coordinated and arranged. Having the freedom to see anything she liked was a nice change of pace.

    As she rode ahead, she thought she could see a stranger on the side of the road. As she got closer, she became startled by what she saw. A man whose body appeared to be...decomposing? He was missing one eye, his skin was grey and pallid, and blood dripped from his fingernails.

    "Turn back now, or you will join our ranks." the man said in a threatening tone.
