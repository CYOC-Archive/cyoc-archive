    "Oh?"

    "First off, in case you haven't heard, Julie Stovall's Statute 1-A hearing begins tomorrow morning at 10."

Plus, my parents are supposed to be in Seattle sometime tomorrow and driving down to Moon Lake for the holidays."

    "Yes. Don't know how long past Christmas they're planning to stay, but while they are here, I'd like you to meet them."
    "I'd love to," said Lila, hoping that meeting would go well. "And your niece Frieda?"
    "Still hoping to arrange that too. Over the weekend at the earliest," explained Lance.
    "Maybe over a nice dinner for all of us?" suggested Lila, as she laid Lance down on the bed and started snuggling next to him.
    "It would have to be somewhere here in Moon Lake, since Benton can't leave town until he proves at least 30 consistent days of mental stability to the Elders' Council."

    "Hmm...." said Lance, nuzzling against her. "I am more tired than I thought. The paper, Knight Media, family matters, the Elders, the trial...." he said, before yawning.
    "I know dear. But my big strong werepanther will be well rested and ready to tackle everything anew in the morning. I love you Lance Knight," she said happily, before giving him a gentle kiss on his muzzle.
    He gave her a quick peck on a furry cheek before saying, "I love you too *yawn* Lila Knight," as he drifted off to sleep.

    As she held her boyfriend close until slumber claimed her as well, Lila had only one happy thought on her mind. He does love and intends to marry me someday! as she drifted off to pleasant dreams of being the future Mrs. Knight.
