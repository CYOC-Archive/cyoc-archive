    Getting excited about the change process he clicked the Apply Changes button. The little hour glass appeared, and much to his dismay, after the previous message box disappeared a new one took
    its place.

    "What now?" he wondered. The message box stated that the program detected significant changes that would be very noticeable and more input was needed...

    Perception of Reality:

    World:
    [ ] New reality accepted as normal. (History will change to make sense of situation)
    [ ] Leave reality unchanged

    James thought about both options and upon selecting one the popup box grew and included additional details. He was pleased that the Chronivac didn't hype him up again by making him click apply
    when it really had more questions for him

    Subject:
    [ ] New reality fully accepted as normal. (Perception of history will change to make sense of situation)
    [ ] Aware of new reality, perceptions from former reality. (May gain new habits/memories to sustain new reality/will be aware)
    [ ] Subject will find changes abnormal.

    User:
    NA *** User is Subject, please use Subject section to select effect

    James clicked all the options to see if anything new would arise, he noticed an advanced tab but skipped over it as he felt he had plenty to work with here. He saw a the bottom he could Start
    Modification, Save Reality Settings And Return To Traits, or simply Return To Traits.

    He just wanted to test the device but how permanent did he want the test? If it works as expected he could do anything! The potential of the machine hit him full force as he knew there were
    significantly more settings. He realized that if his test works he could charge money beef up other guys, replacing gyms, and make billions.

    He knew with sudden clarity EXACTLY what he wanted to do...
