    "Transform!"

    There was a moment of silence, before the voice over the phone spoke again.

    "Quite the bold choice, don't you think?"

    Admittedly, thinking it over now, Jen regretted her choice.
    If the rumors had been true, she just chose to have her form be altered in any number of ways, losing the body she's grown so proud of maintaining over the years. And, more likely, if the rumors hadn't been true, she had just chosen to indulge in it as if it were real, leaving her vulnerable to ridicule and jokes made at her expense for the foreseeable future.
    Before she could voice any of her regret however, Kyle's voice came over the line again.

    "Well, it's too late now anyway. You'll just have to deal with the repercussions of your choices."

    Knowing that she was about to get an earful over something she wouldn't care about, Jen rested her head in her free hand and sighed.

    "There's a friend of mine that's been pretty inconvenienced lately. Not only have you and your friends been relentlessly bullying her at school, but she's also been lacking in terms of the clothing department, so to speak. She isn't exactly in the best of financial situations from what I've gathered, leaving her to settle with uncomfortable hand-me-down clothing, and also making her quite the target for wealthy high class girls like you to pick on. So, why don't I just kill two birds with one stone here?"

    Jen had barely focused on any of what Kyle had just rambled on about, too caught up in thinking about the stress of anyone at school finding out she actually took part in this dumb prank, along with various other things like the case of her missing underwear, or what should be done for her 19th birthday party in a week. With so many other things on her mind, she was growing impatient, and just wanted Kyle to get out his dumb revenge fantasy already so she could move on to better things.

    "Yeah yeah great, look I really don't have the time to indulge in this dumb kid's game with you right now. I gotta find my underwear and finish up prepping for school, so like, if this really means that much to you, just leave me a voice mail and I'll listen to it, uh... never, okay? Don't call me again."

    With that, Jen hung up, getting back to preparing for her day.
    On the other end however, Kyle continued on, stating the rest of the conditions for Jen's chosen transformation.

    "... Underwear, huh? Not a bad idea..."
