    Jeff's world shook for a moment as the changes took place. Jeff looked at Bull's broken pipe on the floor with frustration and irritation. He'd just given him the pipe after all, and already his daddy broke it! Jeff wanted a moment to himself to be able to ponder his next steps. Grabbing the pipe and leaving Bull in a hapless daze, Jeff found himself walking through the halls while typing in the Chronivac. He restored the pipe to an unbroken state which also filled it and made a set of matches appear.

    Absentmindedly Jeff had made his way outside not realizing there even was an employee smoking area at the school but he was thoughtfully puffing his daddy's pipe as though he had been doing it for as long as Bull had. Jeff was trying to figure out how to make the party come together but all he could think about was food and beer. He could really go for one of Ed's cigars too but he currently has a fresh pipe, maybe later.

    Jeff was sure that once he got some food he could focus on his party plans. Expertly holding the pipe in his mouth with his teeth, freeing both his hands he again typed in the Chronivac causing a fresh large pizza to appear, 3 16 oz cans of gas station ice cold beer, and a cigar and lighter for later.

    Jeff was happily consuming everything, over halfway done, when he got a look at himself in the mirror. He was much beefier than the 18 year old version of himself should be... His brain rapidly put the pieces together and after a few more pieces of pizza and finishing the second beer, he confirmed on the Chronivac.

    He told the Chronivac he could steal traits from his Bull daddy when needed, which would leave him less coherent. He currently has all his daddies acting as mindless drones. The Chronivac must have taken this to mean the stealing ability Jeff described should be applied to all his daddies instead of the one, and with them all being mentally subdued, the Chronivac took this to mean he is currently taking on all his Daddies traits right now.

    Jeff was blessed with thick muscle, the ability to take a hit, and a penchant for cigars and cheap beer from Ed. He also now has an endless appetite which will never make him sick along with excess padding from Cliff. The intelligence to put this together along with an appreciation for pipes he took from Bull...

    With a heavy sigh, Jeff realized that while he wanted the janitors to work efficiently, he was going to need to keep them this way and let them work, or stand over them to keep them from bickering.

    Jeff consciously released Bull noticing his beefy padded chest deflate a tiny bit, though between Cliff and Ed's impacts working together, the change was truly negligible. The only thing that Jeff did notice was that he didn't want to smoke the pipe sitting next to him any more, however, Jeff crammed the last slice of the extra large pizza in his mouth and happily chugged the last beer. He used his shirt as a napkin because he forgot to create some when he was making everything else. With that taken care of and a heavy buzz settling in, Jeff gleefully lit up the cigar he had been waiting for.

    Not what he expected but it could make things a tiny bit more interesting.
