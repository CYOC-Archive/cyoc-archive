    Hearing what his sister is telling him causes Dan to start feeling terrified, he knows his sister well enough to know the difference between when she is just joking around or being serious, and judging by the expression on the girl’s face, Dan is more than sure that she is not joking.

    “H-hey… calm down,” he says, starting to slowly back away, “I… I haven’t done anything! There… there is no reason to transform me into some kind of animal! Be-besides… transforming me into an animal… might b-backfire… I mean… hiding an animal isn’t easy… m-mom would for sure sense that something is wr-wrong”. The teens eyes widen in horror when he realizes what he had just said.

    Kat smirks, “thanks, dear brother, for making a good point,” she says, “I think I have a much better idea how to get rid of you, then MY parents will focus their attention back on me, the way it SHOULD be!”

    Dan turns on his heels, but before he is able to run he suddenly feels his body go rigid, and finds himself standing straight, unmoving, like a statue.

    “Not so fast,” he hears his sister say, the girl steps into his line of sight, grinning, in one hand suddenly holding a… stick? Wait… is… is that a wand?!

    Now Dan is beyond terrified, “pl-please… d-don’t do this,” he pleads, “please… I’ll… I’ll do anything! But pl-please… d-don’t do what… what you are thinking!”

    Kat’s evil grin widens, “your pleading isn’t going to persuade me,” she says, “haha… you look so pathetic! I’m doing my parents and the world a favor… now, let’s get started,” the girl then flicks her wand at Dan and a string of pink light shoots from the tip, hitting Dan in the chest.

    When the string of light hits Dan, the teen immediately feels weird, when he looks at his sister he notices that she seems to appear taller than before and she keeps on grow… wait! Kat is NOT growing… it’s HIM that is SHRINKING! His clothes also begin to change, his shirt merges with the waistband of his jeans, both then change their texture into more of a stretchy material like latex or lycra, taking on a nice turquoise color.

    ‘Is… is she transforming me into an insect,’ he asks himself as he feels what seems to be wings growing from his back, ‘is she planning to… to step on me?!’

    Kat watches her brother shrink, the girl grins, “oh, I am not transforming you into some insect, dear brother,” she says, “and before you ask: I am able to read your thoughts!”

    “If… if you aren’t transforming me into a bug or insect,” Dan says, “then… then what am I becoming?”

    “Hehe, you’ll figure it out sooner or later,” the girl says, “I have big plans for you, dear brother. You stay right there, I need to get something to put you into to,” with a mad cackle, the girl heads into the kitchen.

    When Kat returns a short while later, Dan sees her carrying what appears to be a large glass jar, “oh no you won’t,” the transforming boy, who by now is not taller than a toddler, says, “you are NOT gonna put me into a freaking jar!”

    “Oh, I sure will,” Kat says, “after all, I need to transport you somehow!”

    Dan doesn’t know what to say, after a few minutes the teen feels that he no longer seems to be shrinking, he now finds himself being not taller than maybe three inches, and no further changes seem to happen. The shrunken winged boy then suddenly realizes what his sister has done, “y-you transformed me into a f-fairy!”

    Kat cackles, “I sure did,” she says, “haha… don’t look like that! Be grateful that I haven’t turned you female! Haha!”

    Dan suddenly feels his ability to move return to him, the transformed boy tries to flap his wings, but instead he flaps his arms, “the hell?!”

    Kat bursts into laughter, “oh, I haven’t given you the knowledge of how to fly,” she says, “hehe… MAYBE you’ll learn how to fly AFTER learning how to control your wings! HAHA! Oh, and before you ask, yes, male fairies do have magical powers, BUT I made sure that you don’t know how to tap into that power… at least not without some help from another fairy, and since fairies don’t exist in this realm… HAHA… it won’t be long for you until you’re eaten by a bird! HAHAHA!”

    Dan is horrified, he tries to run, but it is pointless, he is to small and his legs are to short, and soon he finds himself in the strong grasp of his evil sister and shortly after he finds himself trapped inside the glass jar. The transformed boy bangs on the glass with his tiny fists, “LET ME OUT,” he yells.

    “HAHA… you are so cute,” his sister says, “now, what to do with you? I could give you to some kid. I could just dump you somewhere in the forest. What do you think? Any ideas,” she then asks, grinning evilly and Dan gulps hard.
