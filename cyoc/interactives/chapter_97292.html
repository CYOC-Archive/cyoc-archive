    The elephants all gather around you in a circle and begin to chant. "Tiberius, god of the Elephants, we ask you today to aid us in this transformation. This rascal snuck into our pens, and we need an extra extra extra large elephant for our Master. Hear our plea!"

    The sky darkens to the color of an elephant's hide. A great rumbling noise erupts from the sky. All the elephants. Close their eyes for a few minutes, chanting softly. Then they open them. "IT IS DONE!" They bellow into the sky. "BEGIN THE RITUAL!"


    Suddenly, you begin to feel very bloated. Your stomach feels like it's about to pop as more and more gas builds up inside it. The pressure is so great you open your mouth to scream, but this proves to be a fatal mistake. You let out a loud trumpeting as the foul-smelling gas blows out of your throat. You start growing at an alarming rate. Your clothes get very tight within a few seconds, and they rip apart into ribbons of fabric as your body expands more. Your skin turns a deep grey color and gets very rough. You scream again and try to bring your arm up to feel your skin, but your arm feels stiff and unmoveavle. Your fingers ball into fists and flatten out, becoming elephant's feet. You feel like you need to sneeze, and when you do, the sheer force of it blows a smaller circus tent off to the side over. Your nose suddenly shoots away from your face, becoming thick and getting longer and longer every second. Your mouth is forced open as two huge tusks shoot from your mouth.

    Your torso begins lengthening, and you collapse to the ground it shock. This can't be happening. Your ears pop and the explode into very large flaps of tough grey skin. Your head grows very large as your mouth is pushed farther away from your eyes, which move to the side of your head and turn dull and brown. Your trunk flops over your mouth like a snake. Your whole body is now about twenty feet long, and you're still growing. Thunder booms overhead as your legs and arms start growing long. They explode with fat and muscle, easily growing to ten times their normal size. Your legs flip the other direction,and your knees attach themselves to your balooning stomach. You get up on all fours as the elephants watch with obvious delight.

    Your stomach expands out at an alarming rate, growing huge and fat. It touches the ground, it's so big. Your grey, rough skin is pulled very tight around your huge belly. Then your butt explodes in size, dwarfing your bulging stomach. A ropey tail with a tuft of hair at the end shoots out of your huge butt and grows to five feet in length. Your neck thickens with fat and muscle, which you need to hold your gargantuan head up. Your butt, stomach, and thighs are still growing huge as your legs lengthen even more. You watch as you grow taller and taller, larger and larger, the other elephants shooting at away from you. Your back is now very wide and your stomach pushes out from it for six feet on either side. Your hips widen immensley as your shoulder blades do the same. Soon you're so large that the other elephants have to move out of the way of your expanding frame. A shudder runs through you as the last of the changes occur.

    The sky clears and you open your eyes. You feel very large and very powerful. You look down and see, about twenty feet away, your huge front legs touching the floor of the pen. You shake your head and your flappy grey asking jiggles. Your turn your massive head and look back. The other elephants have been shoved into the corners of the pen by your huge stomach, hips, and butt. You must easily weigh over 8 tons. You're about two elephants put together. You take a few steps forward, your frame swinging side to side. The pen rumbles and almost cracks under your weight. Each step you take sounds like an earthquake. You look around at the other elephants and start crying. You're most certainly the biggest elephant that has ever lived. You open your mouth to scream, but your release an ear-bleedingly loud trumpeting noise that makes all the tents shake. Your foul-smelling elephant breath kills the flowers in the flower boxes near vy. You look for some kind of sympathy, anything, from the elephants, but they are all ended slightly toward you.

    "ALL HAIL TIBERUS, THE NEWEST AND STRONGEST MEMBER OF OUR CLAN!" They bellow into the stormy sky. Then they all turn to you. You take a step back in shock, your unimaginbly large rear end hitting the side of the pen. "SO, KING TIBERIUS" they other elephants bellow at you. "AS KING OF OUR CLAN, HAT IS YOUR FIRST ORDER OF BUSINESS?"
