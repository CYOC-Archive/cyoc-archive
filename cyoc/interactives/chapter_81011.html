    Gabe was running. He didn't know what from, or if it was actually behind him, but he was running. He was dodging
    trees, hurtling rocks, flipping over some random unconscious girl, it was exhilarating. It was like the whole
    world had slowed down for Gabe to just run. He felt like everything was falling into place. He had managed to
    beat the last challenge easy, and he would beat this one too. Then again, if he didn't win, at least he wouldn't
    lose. According to the sprite, this was one of those games where you either get a point or you don't.

    Fortunately, Gabe had managed to win the last game, where losing actually lost points. In fact, Gabe was
    reasonably sure everyone had won the last challenge. Everyone except Logan. Gabe stopped for a minute. Logan was
    the only reason Gabe had won that last challenge. If he hadn't followed Logan, he probably would have ended up
    stuck in the first city with no way out. Now, Logan didn't know he had helped Gabe, but it was still important
    to repay him.

    So, Gabe started to look for anyone else he could. Hopefully, he would find Logan and help him. Otherwise, he
    would just have to try to not die.
