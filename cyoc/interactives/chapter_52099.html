    As she relaxed in the pool, her chest felt cold. When she looked down she noticed that the water, which came up to her chest, now only came up to the top of her stomach.

    "Odd, I must have stepped back into the shallower part of the pool," she thought.

    She stepped forward in the pool and immersed herself to her chest again. Not five minutes later she felt a chill again as the air whipped past her wet chest...and stomach. Without thinking she just moved forward in the pool again. This continued happening every few minutes until she reached the end of the pool. Her chest and up were out of the water.

    "What is the depth of this pool? It must be one of those lap pools or something."

    Suzy waded over to the side and looked at the spray painted depth. "10 feet...but thats impossible. My feet are on the floor of the...."

    She remembered back to what the little man said. Ten hours a day and don't take them off and put them back on...oh no. Suzy forgot about the second warning and had indeed started another enlarging process.

    Looking around, she did not see anyone so she walked to the shallow end of the pool. Stepping out she surveyed the situation. Looking down, her swimsuit was straining because her entire body had grown!

    Yesterday morning, Suzy was a fit, 5'8" woman. In just one day she had become an overly pregnant looking, big-footed, huge handed, muscular freak! As for her height, she could only guess she was between 11 and 12 feet tall. Suzy grabbed her towel and shoes and went back home.

    At home, she found a measuring tape. She was 11'4'', exactly twice her original height! Looking at the clock, it was 1:30 in the afternoon. Realizing that she would not shrink back to 5'8'' until midnight Monday morning she thought about what to do now.
