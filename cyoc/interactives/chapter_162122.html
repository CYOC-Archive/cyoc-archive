    Villar smirked, gazing at his lieutenants eagerly. They had done as he asked, spooking the wizards and forcing Merlin to merge himself into a single entity once more.

    "What is our next move my Lord," Casseus asked.

    "The wizards are panicking," Villar stated. "Merlin has merged himself, bringing his essence from the surrounding timelines to this place. His death shall allow us to conquer both timelines in one fell swoop."

    "How do you intend to kill Merlin," Fukushu asked. "He is immortal."

    "Even immortals have weaknesses my old friend," Villar smirked. "Believe me I can destroy him."

    "Useing his daughter," Fukushu grinned, to which Villar nodded.

    "What would you have us do," Abolith asked.

    "Pendragon is busy forming alliances with other magical clans," Villar stated. "For now they are out of the picture. The wizards in this timeline though are still quite formidable as Casseus was generous to demonstrate."

    The others now chuckled softly as Casseus grit his teeth. "I could have destroyed them if I wished," he growled. The others ignored the silver haired mans protests as they shook their heads at their companions failure.

    Villar now raised his hand, causing the group to grow silent. "Gloria and her friends are quite powerful despite their age," he stated. "What I fear more though is the girls sister and her friends."

    "Katie O'Toole," Abolith asked, to which Villar nodded.

    "The elder sister and her friends must not return to help the younger wizards," Villar said. "Therefore, after discussing my desires, Casseus has devised a plan."

    Casseus now stepped forward, a smug smirk on his face as he gazed at the group before him. "We are to attack the older wizards where they currently reside," he stated. "Katie and her new wife are currently enjoying their honeymoon. Malak shall take a group of Voids to occupy them."

    "And destroy them," Malak snarled.

    "Not yet," Villar said quietly. "I simply need you to keep them from returning here. That is until I attack."

    "You my Lord," Abolith asked.

    "Yes," Villar smiled. "I will engage the young wizards in combat. We have let the young wizards test themselves against the Voids and ourselves. For now they may believe they have a chance of defeating us, I will shatter that illusion."

    "What of the rest of us," Fukushu asked.

    "My old friend you get to go to college," Villar smirked. "A large group led by the one known as Mary Elise reaide at a college known as Dartmouth. Take a squadron of Voids and reek havoc."

    Fukushu nodded as Villar now gazed at the others. "Abolith," he said. "The two known as Claudia and Mika are currently residing in the city of New York with their friend Ashley. Go forth and show them what power truely looks like."

    "Casseus," Villar stated. "The one known as Samantha has followed her husband to Florida for the time being. Her husband appears to be an athlete of sorts as well as a fledgling wizard. Your cunning and power should be able to handle the dragoness."

    Casseus nodded as Villar now stared intently at his followers. "Remember you are to delay the elder wizards no more," he stated. "We do not wish their deaths at least for now. Simply keep them from assisting the younger wizards while I attack. If all goes as planned we shall occupy this timeline to such an extent that they are unable to regroup before it is too late."

    Villars followers now nodded, fading into the ether with a platoon of Voids by their sides. The first strike had been a success, now the first battle in the war would be fought.
