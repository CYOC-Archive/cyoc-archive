First he changed their financial situation so they were now rich enough that his father no longer had to work and could retire early if he wanted to. It was the least he could do after everything his dad went especially since his mom left. Next he gave them a better house, a bigger two story suburban one that upper middle class people have. Finally he tackled the last item on the list and got them a live-in female caretaker to watch after his dad for however long he was regressed. His desktop computer even got an upgrade, becoming a nicer and more expensive model than the previous one.

    Getting up from his chair Jeff left his room to inspect the house, going from room to to room to see the changes he made. Everything was mostly the same save for the new furniture added to furnish the new spaces. When he found the nursery it was exactly as he had ordered with all the necessities, a crib, a changing table, a dresser and a box full of toys; just like his dad wanted. Speaking of his father Jeff decided he should check on the little guy to make sure he was still doing alright on his own.

Looking up at Jeff with a big grin on his face he said, "Hi Jeff, great work with the house! And I see you've met Karen here, don't mind her she is just changing me cause I ended up making a big stinky" Mike replied with a giggle.

Unfolding and slipping a brand new diaper under him she started sprinkling some baby powder. Jeff was thankful for that step as the sweet lavender scent got rid of the lingering odor hanging in the air which had been grossing him out. "You know with the way your father acts it's hard to believe he's an adult who was regressed due to a virus. But if you're going to be stuck as a baby I guess you learn to love acting the part" she smiled as she taped up the new pair of padding. That comment made Jeff realize the Chronoivac must have changed reality to explain why his dad had suddenly became two since he didn't click the alter reality button.
