    Kyle was sitting on his couch in the living room, eating some chips
    and watching TV as normal. He didn't have class

    until this afternoon, and he was making the most of his time off.

    Suddenly he was jolted out of his seat by a loud crash coming from the
    back of the house. It sounded like someone

    had ran a truck into the back of the house or something! He ran from
    the living room into the kitchen at the back of

    the house, ready to give the driver hell. As he entered the kitched
    however, all thoughts of anger evaporated as his

    eyes went wide, taking in the scene. The door to the kitchen lay
    smashed on the ground, broken glass sparkled in the

    morning sun. It looked like something had nearly cracked it in half
    before it tore violently off its hinges. But

    before he even had time to think about that he realized he wasn't
    alone. There was someone in a huge furry coat

    standing in front of the open fridge, nearly blocking his view of it.

    "Hey! You!" Kyle began to shout, but the rest of the words fell from
    his lips as the person in the coat turned

    around.

    His jaw dropped as he realized it wasn't a coat at all, it was a huge
    creature of some kind!

    His eyes ran over the features, his mind refusing to process something
    he thought was impossible. It was a giant

    wolf, standing there in his kitchen eating the steaks his parents were
    defrosting in the fridge as if it was the

    most normal thing in the world. It almost looked to Kyle like it was
    smiling, if grinning from ear to ear with razor

    sharp teeth could be called that.

    Kyle jerked out of his daze as the wolf-creature tossed the last of
    the steaks in the air like a peice of popcorn,

    catching it between those sharp teeth before swallowing it whole.

    "Mmmmmmmm.... That's real good Kyle," the beast growled at him as it
    licked the juices from its muzzle with a long

    muscular tongue, "You wouldn't beleive how hungry I am. I've had a
    very, very, busy morning."

    "Shit shit shit shit shit...." Kyle muttered under his breath, fear
    starting to take hold of him as adrenaline

    rushed through his system.

    "... What... What the fuck are you?!" Kyle managed to choke out in a
    high-pitched voice.

    "What's the matter Kyle," the monster chuckled at him, "Don't you
    recognize me?". With that the huge beast loomed

    closer, covering the distance between them in a single stride. It
    lifted him like a ragdoll and held him in the air

    against the wall by his collar, "I know I've changed a bit since we
    last talked on the phone, but really, I was

    expecting a little more from you..." The muzzle of the wolf-creature
    was only centimetres from his face now, he

    could feel the hot breath as it breathed slowly in and out, the smell
    of blood still fresh on its lips.

    His eyes went wide as realization dawned on him, "J... J.... Jennifer?
    " He sqeaked.

    The beast dropped him to the floor and stood straight, "In the flesh."
    it roared and then burst into a series of growling chuckles.

    Kyle got his first good look at the transformed cheerleader and was
    amazed at the transformation that had occured. The previously snobby
    and aloof female who had spent hours cultivating a sexy and attractive
    body had been replaced by a huge towering mass of muscle, claws, teeth
    and fur. A very MALE mass of muscle, claws, teeth a fur.

    Before Kyle could do anything the Jennifer bent low, stooping so her
    wolven head was next to his and whispered darkly into his ear, "And
    I've got a little present for you Kyle. I think you'll like it very
    much." He could almost hear the lips pulling back from the sharp teeth
    in a monsterous grin, "Transform or Dare, Kyle."

    "Oh Shit." Was all he could think.
