    Sitting at his computer desk in his room, Eric was absently humming the opening song to the movie Frieda and Spencer were watching. Despite not seeing the movie, the bond he had with Spencer and Frieda allowed him almost watch the movie by seeing it through their eyes.
    “I wonder if this is going lead to costume ideals for the Halloween dance,” Eric musing as he tried to work on the last of his homework. “They’ll have a lot to do to convince me from my plan to dress up as the pink panther,” Eric looking online for non-toxic pink dye. While not in his fursona, due to a broken nose he received from his math teacher; Eric was a were-panther. Pure white fur when he is in his fursona, with a barely noticeable patch of brown at the top of his head. Eric got the ideal of dressing up as the pink panther after the first time he went hunting with Frieda and Spencer.

    Coming back with his muzzle stain with the blood from his first hunt; a buck that spencer killed. Eric fondly remembered as he, Spencer and Frieda came to the soundproof basement of his house. Making love one final time before falling asleep; the feeling of being full and content, his girls resting by his side as they slept soundly. Eric was hoping that he could have that feeling again soon.
    “When this nose is finally healed,” Eric musing; “and this were-alert is over, we three are going to have one hell of a rut.”
