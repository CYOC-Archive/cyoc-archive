    Ann sighed happily as her and her wife parked in her sisters driveway. After hearing about her niece's return from the past, she had been anticipating a visit to see her.

    "Earth to Ann," Jackie exclaimed as she put the car in park. "Hey angel you with me?"

    "Sorry," Ann blushed. "Just kind of lost in thought. I've been anxious to see Lynne since she came back."

    "I understand," Jackie said warmly. "I mean we all missed her. Plus I can imagine you have alot to talk about. How many times did her magic mess with you?"

    "Too many," Ann laughed. "But her arrival in the past did work out for the best."

    Jackie and Ann smiled as they held eachothers hands. Lynne's arrival had altered the past but it had been for the better. Ann and Jackie fondly remembered the early parts of their relationship as they tried to traverse the glaring problem of their age difference. When Lynne arrived with her friends Jackie was able to borrow the Ring of Age from Tricia and was able to grow closer to Ann and her friends. It resulted in the two growing closer and a realization that they were willing to wait for eachother, something that would not of occured without Lynne's arrival.

    "You know as big of a pain as the girl was she really did alot for everyone," Jackie sighed. "Never would of believed that, especially considering we went to school together. She thought I was a dork."

    "Well you are," Ann laughed, causing Jackie to playfully shove her. "Keep that up and your sleeping on the couch," Jackie smirked. As the two exited the car, they happily approached her sisters home.

    Lisa sighed as she approached the door. Hopefully this was Katie and Gloria. As she opened it she was surprised to see a 30 year old Ann and Jackie. "What are you two doing here," she asked, hugging her sister and her wife.

    "Well finally able to get some time away fron work," Ann smiled. "Remember I said I would stop by with Jackie todat?"

    "Oh thats right," Lisa exclaimed hitting her forehead. "Forgot you mentioned that, Ive had alot on my mind."

    "I understand," Ann smirked. "Big sis always has alot on her plate."

    Lisa frowned before giving Ann a sly smile. Ann had wished for her youth back after Jackie graduated from college. As a result Lisa found herself going from the youngest to the oldest sibling. Unfortunately for her Kellie and Ann seemed to poke at her about the fact they were young women now, while she was in her 50s. "Yeah keep it up you," Lisa playfully grumbled. "If you and Kellie keep making these jokes I might have Katie and Gloria put me back in grade school, who'll be the old lady then."

    Ann chuckled as she took Jackies jacket and headed towards the living room. "So where's Lynne," she asked. "She still at school?"

    Lisa paused, Ann per usual had awful timing. If Lynne came downstairs chances were her teenage self would follow. She wasnt in the mood to try and explain things right now, especially if it could alter the timeline. "Shes upstairs probably sleeping," she said quickly. "Let her rest besides I want to kmow how rhings are going at the shop."

    "Well Stephanie and Michelle have been holding down the fort," Ann said. "Ive been trying to not work as much in my "retirement" but you know how that goes."

    Lisa smirked as she looked at Ann. She, Jackie, Michelle and Stephanie decided to open a auction house after Ann left her company and reclaim her youth. The four friends opened it together as a passion project, putting Stephanie in charge of the day to day operations. Unfortunately for Ann things had been busy forcing the couple to spend more time thwir than they wished.

    "Im sure your loving being busy Ann," Lisa smiled. "Retirement just isnt your style even if your a millionaire."

    As the two talked they failed to hear Lynne and Lisa quietly make their way downstairs. Lisa gawked as she gazed at the two young women talking with her older self. "Is that Ann and Jackie," teen Lisa whispered.

    Lynne nodded as she looked at the teen. "Yeah thats them," she said quietly. "Guess they stopped by to visit."

    "How come Ann looks so young," Lisa asked. "I mean my Aunt Ann...I mean sister looks like she hasnt aged a day."

    "Ann made herself younger when Aunt Jackie graduated from college," Lynne stated. Teen Lisa stared at the couple as she tried to possess this information. She remembered bits and pieces about Ann and Jackies relationship. The two said they would wait for one another, she just never expected Ann to make herself so much younger. Furrowing her brow she now stormed dowmstairs, looking to talk with the two.

    "Lis...mom WAIT!"

    Lisa had heard her daughter yell and now groaned internally. As the three adults turned they could see two auburn hairrd teens approach. Much to the adult Lisa's chagrin it appeared her teenage self was leading the charge.

    "Ann what the hell," teen Lisa exclaimed. "You made yourself THAT much younger?!? When did this happen?"

    Ann gazed curiously at the teen, a look of confusion and shock spreading across her face. "Lisa? Is...is that you?!?!"

    "Yeah its me," the teen answered grumpily. "Well kind of a past version, its kind of a long story but thats besides the point. When did you decide to do this?"

    Ann amd Jackie looked flabbergasted as they looked between the two teens and the grown Lisa. "Ugh," Lisa sighed. "I cant believe I was this big a pain in the ass."

    "Lisa whats going on," Jackie asked.

    "Ann, Jackie," Lisa said. "Thats me, well the young me from about 15 years ago when I was pregnant with Lynne."

    "Cant be," Ann exclaimed. "Shes just a kid."

    "Im 13 Ann," Lisa grumbled. "So technically Im a teenager and I need my sister, no wait...ugh...my daughters help before things get too weird."

    Ann and Jackie now stared dumbfoumded at the adult Lisa as she buried her face on her hands. "Its complicated," she moaned. "But yeah she needs our help."

    Ann and Jackie now sat back preparing to hear what the two Lisa's had to say.
