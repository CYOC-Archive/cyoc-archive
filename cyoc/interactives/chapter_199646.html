    Elena didn’t know what was going on, one moment she was walking down a darkened street to head back to her corner to pick up her next client the next moment she was falling. It wasn’t that she had slipped in her high heels, she had done that before. No, she was falling and falling as she became surrounded by purplish, black light.

    “We got one.” Came a voice from somewhere below her as she came crashing down hitting a matted floor. She tried to stand up or at least roll over but found none of her muscles where working, she could move her eyes and breath but other then that she couldn’t move a muscle. She couldn’t even speak yet alone scream.

    “Wow…we got a good looking one.” Said a new voice as the sound of footsteps came from her right, picking up her bang taking it from her hands. She heard whoever it was going through her purse. “Elena Johnson…. American….age 27…judging by the outfit and the items in here, she is a prostitute.”

    “That will hurt her on the market, wouldn’t it?” inquired a younger voice

    “A little, but some like the fact she has experience.” Stated the 1st voice “And she is a very good looking women, a good breeding age and as long as her blood tests come back clean for STDs and pregnancy she will fetch a good price.”

    “A good price.” Thought Elena was this human trafficking. She felt a pinch as a needle went into her arm.

    “Should only be a few minutes.” said the second voice as he walked away, before the sound of a door closing reached her ears. The moment she heard that door she was able to move again, she bolted upright grabbing for her knife, but her purse was gone.

    “Looking for this?” inquired the younger voice as Elena looked toward the door only to see that it was made of glass. Standing there was something impossible, from the waste up it looked like a boy in his early 20s but from the waist down was the body of some kind of snake. She was so focused on the snake man she didn’t notice at first that he was holding her knife. “Don’t worry you will not need it.”

    “What…what…” started Elena

    “Don’t be rude.” Said the 1st voice, an older voice as another creature this one looking like a centaur only with the body of a zebra came out of the darkness. “Forgive my young colleague, he is a new hire and hasn’t had much contact with humans. I will cut right to the chase; you have been transported to a sort of holding area between multiple magical worlds which lie outside your human world. In a little bit, once we get your blood results back we will be offering you to some of our clients as a possible new addition to whichever magical race or family wants you.”

    “What?” asked Elena still not understanding.

    “We are going to show you to a few magical creatures and whichever one pays the most for you you will become said creature.” Said the snake man bluntly as the centaur man eyed him. Elena turned and looked at him, who nodded his conformation.

    “This can’t be real?” stated Elena

    “It is very real.” Said the Centaur man “Now just relax, you will be going to a new home, possibly a new family soon.”

    With that they where gone as Elena just stood there in a small cell which was only large enough for her to lie down on the floor with a small bathroom/sink in the back. She didn’t know how long she had been in there, minutes turned into hours and hours felt like days. She guessed it was only 3 to 4 hours but felt like days before a female demon lady came up.

    “Well good news, your results are clean and you are not pregnant.” Stated the demon lady “And we have a buyer for you.”

    Before Elena could say more the floor began to light up as some kind of glowing symbol appeared as fear filled her body.

    “Beginning transformation process now.” said the demon lady
