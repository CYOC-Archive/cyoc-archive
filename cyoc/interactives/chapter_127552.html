    James looked on in wonder, as he and Carrie grew tails, shrunk, and sprouted whiskers.
    “Okay, What the heck is happening to us?!” James practically shouted staring at Carrie in dismay.
    “Just enjoy it!” Carrie answered back, as a small wave of pleasure seeped through them.
    “How do you know what you’re doing?!” James yelled when his knew cat ears popped up. He yiped when his nose began to change.
    “Oh James, it will be over in a moment, don’t worry!” Carrie replied.
    ‘I don’t think I can!’ James thought to himself. He had never been so freaked out in all his nine lives!

    Wait... Where did That thought come from?
    ‘Uh oh.’ James thought.

    In a few moments, it was finally done done, much to the relief of James.
    Carrie looked at him, her eyes shining, {My, what nice fur you have!}
    {I don’t have fur,} James meowed.

    Meowed?

    - Silence -

    {We’re cats, aren’t we.}

    {Yup!} Carrie laughed when he finally figured it out. Well, as much as a cat can laugh anyway. But that didn’t matter, the look on Jame’s face was priceless!

    {How can we still understand each other?} James asked.

    {Obviously because we’re both cats!}
    Carrie decided that she was definitely enjoying this much more than her boyfriend was. He did say he liked cats, didn’t he? So why not want to be one?
    At that moment though, something caught both their attention.

    A mouse.

    Sadly for them not a real mouse, just a toy. Janet would never allow mice in “her” house.
    But how did the cat toy get there?

    Then Carrie remembered that she had planned on buying a cat sooner or later. With the fact of telling her father that, he had probably left the mouse as a gift for her.
    [How nice of him!] Carrie thought, then turned back to James.

    He was buizy licking himself in the corner nearest them.
    Quickly he stood up, quite embarrassed.

    {Sorry, my paw got dirty, and then my cat like instincts got the better of me.}

    {You’re fine,} Carrie meowed back, {After all, that is what cats do a lot, licking themselves anyway.}

    { I wonder if we can talk and understand real cats.} James said, while filicking his black and white tail in the air.

    {Maybe,} Carrie said, {But first, I should tell you why we just transformed into cats...}

    James sat up straighter.

    {It’s because of the charm bracelet, well, coller,} she began, just now noticing that that’s what had happened to the bracelet. It did make sence,{Anyway, my Grandma gave it to me 6 years ago. It has a bunch of different charms to choose from to, but the best part is that it’s magic.}
