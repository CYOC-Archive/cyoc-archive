    Mary Drinks A Few Vials

    added by Howard 6 years ago A BM S O Muscle

    Mary opened the case and saw the variously labelled vials, she looked at each one and decided she would have a quick test subject first. She grabbed her dog from the other room and sat him down quietly. She got a vial that had the image of a pig on it and took the cork off the top. Mary poured the vial down the dogs throat and waited for him to finish. He looked up at her and she could tell he didn’t like the taste. Mary then decided to have a look for herself, she grabbed one that had the image of a bunny on it and started to chug it down. She managed to get a third of it down before spitting it all out. She placed the empty pig vial and half drank rabbit vial back in. She sat down and watched TV with her dog calmly for a while before her dog barked in a way she had never heard him do before. His stomach then fell out fatter and his nose grew bigger. His fur started to sink into his body and his long tail shrunk down and turned into a curled noodle tail. She looked at him finally and her dog had turned into a pig. Now she knew that the vial works. Then as she was going to phone the vet, just as she went to the kitchen phone her body shrunk half of what it was. She looked at her hands and they were starting to pad. Her feet grew a tiny bit longer and broke her shoes. Her body grew small amounts of fur everywhere and her head grew bunny ears with little to no fur. Her teeth grew larger, just the front ones, they grew just like a rabbits. She closed her mouth and looked in the mirror, she saw what looked to be a girl with bunny features and fur. She ran to the box and opened it again, the previous vials appeared to have refilled somehow. She sat down and looked through each and every vial. As she did she at one point sprung up like a spring as her butt grew a large ball tail. She found a vial that looked like a person, she drank it and nothing happened, she kept looking, she took out each and every vial until right at the bottom of the case there was one labelled, “Undo” which she grabbed and drank. This one unlike the others was instant, she grew back to her height and her new additional parts had disappeared. She placed each vial back into its place and closed it. Then she saw her dog, which was a pig. She grabbed the dog vial and poured it in his mouth. She watched for him to change back when instead she felt kind of strange. Her arms started bulging. Bigger and bigger until they were the size of her waist, which was also growing now. Her stomach became toned and a six pack grew out of it, her whole body grew with more and more muscle. She finished and she was ripped, and so were her clothes. She looked at her dog which had now been changed again and picked him up like he was weightless. Mary was curious to see what the other vials did to her, and what they did to her dog as well. She grabbed the case and put it on the table and opened it. Wondering what else she could do.
