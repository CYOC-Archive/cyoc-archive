Not only had this mermaid ratted her out when she checked out Damon, but now she just likely put her new love in danger. It hurt Charlotte deeply.

She simply just wasn’t interested in the way merboy and mermen attempted to woo her and the situation only got worse as puberty was generous in giving her a beautiful body.

    It wasn’t as if she hated male merfolk, she was sure they meant well. But she hated her parents trying to handpick for her when it comes to boys. She wanted to fall in love naturally, and that natural love was felt when she laid her eyes on Damon.

When she found out Damon shared the same feelings for her, Charlotte was already amazed. But once Damon expressed wanting his kingdom and hers to heal and find peace, Charlotte felt as if her heart completely belongs to Damon.
