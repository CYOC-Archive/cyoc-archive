    Kat's Plan Thickens

    added by LittleBoy 7 days ago O

    Still laughing like a psychopath, Kat carries the jar, which contains her brother-turned-fairy, into her room and sets it on one of her shelves, without a word the girl then hides the jar behind some stuffed animals and shortly after, Dan hears the door to his sister’s room close.

    ‘What is that crazy bitch up to,’ he asks himself, ‘why hasn’t anyone realized that something is wrong with her? Not even mom or dad seemed to have noticed that Kat has some mental issues!’

    What Dan doesn’t know is that his sister meanwhile has dialed the number of a 10-year-old girl, whom she met two months ago at a summer camp where Kat from time to time works as a volunteer. The two girls became friends, one day, while Kat and the girl were doing some work around the camp, the young girl had said, “Kat, from what you have told me about how mean your brother is to you, how he stole all the attention from you, I kind of wish I were your brother instead! I’d make a far better brother than him!”

    Dan is unaware that Kat is following a sick plan she came up with when she discovered her powers, the 18-year-old grins as she puts the phone to her ear, “my plan is going better than I thought,” she thinks to herself. In her sick and broken mind, Kat really believes that, when her new friend and stupid brother have swapped bodies, that her parent’s attention will shift back to her, because “Dan” isn’t a boy but a girl “dressed up” in a “Dan costume”, however this will of course not happen, just because the soul had been replaced, the body would still be the same.

    “Hello,” Kat hears a young girl’s voice answering.

    “Hello, Ellie, this is Kat,” she says, “you know, from the summer camp a few months ago?”

    “Ooooh, yeah, I remember,” Ellie says, “how have you been, Kat?”

    “Oh, I’ve been okay… well… you know how my life is,” she continues.

    “Yeah, I’m sorry,” Ellie says, “I really wish I could replace your brother!”

    “Well, there is a way, remember I told you that I’m a witch and that I’d receive my powers when I turn 18?”

    “Yeah?”

    “Well, I have received my powers,” Kat continues, “I immediately tested them on my brother and transformed him into a male fairy!”

    “A fairy? But… I mean… wasn’t that a mistake?”

    “Nah, that fool doesn’t know how to use his wings, let alone know how to tap into his magic powers,” Kat says, “at least not yet”.

    “So, what is your plan?”

    Kat grins, “I’ll give him to you, and once he had learned how to fly and tap into his powers, I have no doubt in my mind that he’d try to somehow get back at me for transforming him,” she says, “being a perv as he is, I have no doubt that he’ll plan to swap bodies with you and then threaten to tell everything to my parents”.

    “Eeew… having a boy steal MY body does not sit right with me!”

    “Listen, this is what we’ll do: you wanted to be my brother, right?”

    “Yeah?”

    “Well, when he swaps bodies with you, he’ll lose ALL his magical powers and would be stuck as a girl forever, unless YOU’D use your new powers to make ME transform him back into a boy,” Kat says.

    “Ooooh… I get what you up too,” Ellie says, “okay, I’ll agree to your plan, but only if you promise me to train me in magic, I mean… we’d share the same blood, so technically I’d have powers too, right?”

    “That’s correct,” Kat says, “the idiot is so dumb that he doesn’t even know that he too is able to do magic”.

    Ellie giggles, “it’ll be so easy to pull this off,” she says, “and we both will get what we want: I get an awesome sister and you’ll get a brother who’d never treat you badly!”

    “I knew I could rely on you, Ellie,” Kat says, “I’ll meet you in an hour at the entrance to the local park!”

    “I’ll be there!”

    Kat hangs up the phone and grins, “hehe… and the best part: my parents will never know that I got rid of the “original” hahahahah!”
