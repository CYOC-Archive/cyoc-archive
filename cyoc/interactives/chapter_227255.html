    Chronivac Version 4.0

    Ben Calls And "Jeff" Downloads The App-Version

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy Yesterday O

    As the clone and Jeff walk down the street, Jeff is instantly distracted by all the smells and sounds, which overwhelm him and he soon finds himself sniffing at everything. This causes Jeff not to notice his clone taking out his phone as it starts to ring, and the clone answering it.

    “Hey Ben,” the clone says, “I saw your text. Sorry, I haven’t found time to answer, but to answer your question: sure, I’d like to come around with “Jeff” and meet Rex”.

    “Sweet,” Ben says over the line, “I think they’ll get along just fine. How old is “Jeff”, Rex is four years old”.

    “I’m not so sure, to be honest,” “Jeff” says looking down at the dog, “I assume around one or two years old. By the way… why did you get a dog? I thought you wanted to wait”.

    “Um… well… I couldn’t,” Ben says, “I actually wanted a puppy, but since I’m still at school and my parents both work, that would be to much stress. But my parents said, if I’d get a much older dog, who already is trained, they’d be okay with me having one. So we headed to the shelter and found Rex, he is such a well behaved boy, we all are very happy with him”.

    “Jeff” smiles, “I’m happy to hear that,” he says.

    “Yeah, I’m so happy to have him,” Ben says, “you know, it’s kind of a pity that “Jeff” is a male. If you would have gotten a female who at least would have been two years old, we could have bred the two. We could split the cash”.

    “Jeff” chuckles, “I would have declined anyway,” he says, “it’s just to much work caring for pups AND going to school”.

    “Yeah, I guess you are right,” Ben says, sounding a little disappointed.

    “Hey, Ben,” the clone says, “you’ll find a good female for Rex, but there really is no rush”.

    “Yeah, I guess I’m thinking to far ahead,” Ben says, “anyway… when do you want to meet up?”

    “How about on Saturday,” “Jeff” asks.

    “Sure, let’s say 12pm?”

    “Sounds good to me”.

    “Cool, I’ll see you then, mate,” Ben says.

    “Yeah, see you soon, bro,” the clone then ends the call.

    Ben looks down at his phone after “Jeff” hang up, ‘damn it, but I was expecting that,’ he thinks to himself, ‘it seems that I need to do it in secret. With the App it won’t be difficult to make “Jeff” a year or two older and change his gender to female. Hmmm… I shouldn’t have deleted Jeff’s file, oh well, I’ll just scan him when we meet on Saturday’.

    After talking to Ben, “Jeff” looks down at his phone, ‘I should get myself the App-Version of the Chronivac,’ he thinks to himself, ‘it could come in handy, and I could have some fun with it at the park. Hehe… I could have some fun with Ben… maybe transform him into a little boy or girl… hehe,’ so the clone downloads and installs the App.

    Jeff is still busy inspecting everything and is unaware of what his clone has arranged.
