    "They all sound fun, but at the moment I'd like to go the pool." Mary said.

    Bill spotted Mary leaving the changing room. She was wearing a teal two piece. Her brown hair wet from the shower, slicked down her back.

    "Let's go." she said. She seemed both nervous and eager.

    The pool are was large and complex with several pools, slides, hot tubs, and more. Not knowing where to start, they stepped right ahead and entered the pool right in front of them. Billy stepped right in. Mary started with her toes, testing the temperature.

    "It's warm." she commented. She entered the water. "It feels good."

    "Yeah." Billy agreed. "It feels really relaxing.

    The couple slowly walked through the pool, the waterlevel was high enough for them to comfortably submerge themselves, but low enough, so most adults could stand.

    There was something very relaxing about the pool. It was warm, but there was something more. Billy guessed the pools were having some age-related kind of effect. He looked at Mary, and sure, she seemed older, not by much, but just enough for him to notice. Slightly less fresh skin, and were her boobs larger?

    "Have you noticed?" Mary asked him. She was checking him out.

    "Yeah. We're getting older."

    "Uh-huh."

    Mary leaned on Billy's chest. She let a funger trail on his now hairier chest. They relaxed like that for a few minutes, touching skin.

    "This doesn't seem to be happening too fast." Emily commented. "How old are we now? Early thirties?"

    "Maybe." Billy thought that seemed right. Mary did kind of look like she were about that age. He hadn't paid too much attention too how he'd changed himself. It was pretty subtle.

    "Look!" Mary pointed to the side. "Let's try the slides!" She grabbed Billy's hand and dragged him along.

    They arrived at where the slides were. The water here was shallower.

    There were several long stairs leading up to the various slides. Some kids were eagerly running up. This area of the pool had more people than where they just were, but it was not too busy. The couple followed a group of kids up one of the staircases.

    They arrived at the top, and there was the tunnel slide. A sign specified only one at a time.

    "You can go first." Mary pushed Billy ahead.

    "Thanks." Billy guessed this was less Mary being polite, and more her wanting a guinea pig. Though, looking down he couldn't really get a good look of the slide's end feom this angle. Mary probably have to go in blind even if she goes second.

    Billy laid himself in position for the slide, and then pushed himself down. The slide wasn't very steep, but he was moved along by the water stream. He tried paying attention to his body, to see if anythijg was changing, but then he caught speed. Suddenly there was a turn. This was kind of fun. Another sharp turn and Billy felt himself ride along the wall. This was really fun. Billy was completely engtossed with the ride until it ended, where he was spat out into the pool.

    Billy now noticed he'd changed. He was a skinny kid, 9 maybe 8. He wasn't too intersted in looking himself over. He wanted another go at the slide. Then, he saw a young girl, who must be Mary, popping out of the slide. She was submerged in water for a few seconds until she gathered her bearings and stood up.

    "That was amazing!" Mary exclaimed and raised her hands in excitement.

    "Yeah!" Billy agreed. "Let's do another one!"

    The two kids ran up a new staircase to try a new slide.
