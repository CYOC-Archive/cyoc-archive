    Damon starts to have another dream as he found himself alone in an unknown location once again. He recognizes where he is but still doesn’t know much beyond from that.

    "Hello? Anyone here? Charlotte?" As he looked around. Damon felt a strange sensation through his fish body again.

    Damon gulped as he knew what was going to happen, but he then hears his wife’s voice.

    “Damon? Damon, over here!”. Damon looks to his left to see Charlotte, but his eyes widened at seeing her body. He was so used to seeing his wife as a beautiful mermaid that seeing her with legs and her once revealing body covered by a silk dress seemed surreal to him.

    “Charlotte? You’re a human!”. Damon said as he looked at her legs.

    “Damon! Your tail is growing!”. Charlotte said as he looked at his growing tail.

    Damon cringed as he suddenly felt his tail grow once his wife mentioned his tail. He turns his eyes to his tail. When he first had this dream, he was too panicked to look carefully, but now Damon, comforted by the presence of his wife, paid more attention to his tail.

    The growth of Damon’s tail originated from the part where his human skin transitioned to scales. He could see his tail lengthening more and more from that spot.

    “Woah…”, Charlotte said as he looked at Damon’s tail. Damon turned his head to see his wife crawl to his side. His wife stretched out her arm and put her hand on Damon‘s growing tail.

    Damon reluctantly let out a moan. As freaky as the situation was, the feeling of his smooth scales being touched by hands still felt good, really good - especially when they came from the gentle touch of his wife.

    “Damon, this is kind of cool!”, Charlotte said in fascination as Damon’s tail grew and grew.

    Charlotte was awed by this sight, it seemed his tail wasn't going to stop lengthening anytime soon. In her mind, Damon's tail getting longer only meant there was more of Damon for her to love. Even Damon himself was starting to like the feeling of his fish tail getting longer, no longer near as panicked as he was in his last dream.

    At this point, Damon’s enormous tail was much longer than his human upper body - Damon wasn’t even sure if he even can consider himself “half-human, half-fish” anymore when the fish part of himself made up much more than half of his body.

    Nevertheless, he felt much safer in this weird dream with his wife by his side and managed to speak, “T-thanks, Charlotte. Your legs look really cool as well.”

    The married couple looked at each other in the eyes and smiled.

    No matter how weird the dream was, they were at least together.
