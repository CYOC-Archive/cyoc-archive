    King of Dinosaurs is Born

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LuisJM 25 days ago A Mental

    Kieran was amazed at what has happened to him. One minute, he was in his room playing with his dinosaur toys, wishing to be his favorite. The next he was turned into his favorite by a wish he just made. Now he has become a big ferocious Tyrannosaurus Rex with nothing of the weak 10-year-old boy remained. He was happy that he had become the dinosaur that he wished to be, but he never thought his wish will alter the world around him in some way. Of course, that wasn't the case since it was Jeff, who turned into a velociraptor in the first place, destroyed the computer that had that Chronivac on and caused the Prehistoric Wave that is bringing the neighborhood, and soon the world, back to before mankind, or anything warm blooded, ever existed. But Kieran doesn't know about it as he watched the world he once knew deteriorate to make way for the world of the dinos.

    Even if his eyes were to his side, he could see clearly what is happening to his street. He could see houses collapsing and crumbling away as extinct trees grew in to take their place. He looked at roads and sideways cracking and breaking apart as wild grass seeped in and spread. He witnesses cars rusting and deteriorating as more prehistoric fauna grew in and covered it up. Even with his new height, he watched as the city itself was toppling down as a volcanic mountain grew in the middle of it and was spewing out lava. But the one thing he was noticing out of all of it, is the people. All around him, he could see neighbors, friends, strangers, and even pets and other animals, taking form of other dinos in the most comedic of ways. Sometimes they grew out of their clothes and become the beasts they are. Others have parts of their bodies grew as their human selves remain intact. But there are few that haven't changed at all. They just stood here naked in a delay change as they watched the world around them go back in time just like Kieran was witnessing. Still, he was fascinated by it all and couldn't wait to live in a world where Dinosaur's exist and play for real.

    While he doesn't have a voice to speak, his mind is still intact as he said, 'Wow... This is amazing. My home and neighborhood is erasing itself from existence to make way for the new one while I become the most dangerous of beasts. This is going to be so awesome! Can't wait to roam around this world to play with some real dinos.'

    As he was thinking about that, he felt a little woozy as he sway is huge head left and right while his tail was swaying.

    'Whoa... What was that... I feel a little...weird...'

    What was really happening was that his mind was changing after the body took on the metamorphosis. In his head, his brain was slowly shrinking as his human memories and knowledge was draining away, bit by bit.

    'My head... I feel like I'm...forgetting something.'

    What he was doing today before the Prehistoric wave happened. Gone.

    'I feel my mind getting...smaller....simpler'

    Memories of his family and friends. Gone.

    'What was...I...doing.'

    His years in school. Gone.

    'Can't...Think... Everything...Foggy...'

    Years of his own existence. Gone.

    'Feel...Something...Awakening... Have...Urge...'

    Looking at the last of the human world fading away while the remaining humans and animals finished
    changing, his stomach was growling as he hadn't eaten before the changes took place.

    'Hungry... Food... Plenty...'

    The T-Rex slowly lifted his body up.

    'Hunt... Kill... Eat...'

    He lifted his head up to the sky.

    'Survive...'

    He opened his jaws.

    'RULE!!!!!!!!!'

    He let out a loud roar as it echoed across the Jurassic jungle as the last of Kieran was forever gone as in his place physically and mentally is a feral Tyrannosaurus Rex. Bending down, he snarled as he stomp his way through the grassy path looking for his first meal.
