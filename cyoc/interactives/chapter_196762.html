    After thinking what she should do all the way to the clothing shop, Emily finally made up her mind. "I am so sorry, Ricki..." She thought with a devilish smile on her young boy face. "... but you just make such a good mom for me to turn you back now!" Emily couldn't help but admire how feminine Rick had become, it was impossible to believe that this happily married woman was a college aged boy just a couple of minutes ago! And it's not like if everything was lost, Emily could restore his awareness or turn him back when she wanted, so everything was ok. Thinking about everything she could do now, she chuckled a little, fascinated with the power she possessed, and this was of course noticed by her, or better say, Emile's mom.

    Rebecca smiled to herself as she saw her son laughing. Even if sometimes Emile could be a little annoying and liked to play tricks on her, she still loved him more than anything in the world, and seeing he was happy was the world to her. She hoped her son loved the clothes she was going to get him. "And maybe if he is good for the rest of the game, maybe we can do another trip to the toy shop..." Thought the woman to herself.

    While Rebecca was thinking to herself, Emily was already planning going back to camera-related activities, that was the reason she had come to the Mall at the first place, after all. However, after the Rick-Rebecca little incident, Emily decided to make sure everything was working as intended with the camera, she didn't want to make anyone else aware on accident or something worse, after all. Checking on the camera and making sure she and only she was aware, she decided to check the mode of the camera back to the random, original one. "Now that I have seen everything the mode can do, is not really fun anymore, I will go back to random changes for now." Thought Emily as she started looking for people to take a picture of.

    The first group of people Emily saw were 4 teenagers killing time, but they didn't seem to be enjoying themselves all that much. Luckily for them, Emily was there to make everything better! She aimed the camera at them and took a picture, ready to take a pic of them.

    *FLASH*

    Using a camera with one hand while trying to keep up with your mom was harder than Emily anticipated, but that was nothing to worry about. When Emily looked back at the group, they had changed completely, and were now a family of 3 formed by a mother, a father and a little girl around Emily's new age, happily playing with a doll. The group of friends had just turned into a much happier looking family and a doll.

    Satisfied with the change, Emily searched for something else, and after a quick search, she landed her eyes on an adult couple, apparently in their early twenties in age. The couple, formed by one man and one woman, seemed to be arguing about something Emily couldn't hear, but that didn't stop her from pointing the camera at them and taking a picture of them.

    *FLASH*

    When Emily looked back at the couple, instead of a man and a woman, there was only one 5 months pregnant woman around Rebecca's age in there, happily walking around and window shopping a little.

    Emily was going to search for more possible pictures, but before he could take another one, Rebecca called him. "Well, we are here now! Did you have fun with the camera, sweetie?" Said the mother with a smile, happy to see her son was having fun.

    Emily looked at where they were exactly, realising they were in a clothing shop specifically made for little kids, which made sense seeing how Emily was a little boy now. "O, I had tons of fun, mom! This camera is amazing!" Said Emily with a chuckle, enjoying her status as a kid again.

    "Well, that's wonderful! You can still play with it in the shop, just try to not make any other customer uncomfortable, ok sweetie?" Said Rebecca in a motherly tone, making Emily chuckle a little.

    "Of course mom, I promise I won't be annoying anyone!"

    "That's my baby boy!" said Rebecca as she gave a smile to her son and entered the clothing shop.

    Meanwhile, Emily was thinking to herself a little. Rick was actually being a great mom so far! She made the right decision to not change him back, Emily was enjoying herself a lot, but she still thought she could be having an even better time with Rebecca! "If she is treating her son like this, just imagine how she would treat her daughter instead!" Though Emily, both as a result of girls being cuter and as such receiving more love from their parents, and because she WAS a woman before, she would feel even better if she was a girl again. And so, she decided to turn herself into a girl her current age before going back to playing with the camera, but the question is, how did she do it?
