    Suddenly, the door to the computer lab swung opened, and three short, skinny boys walked in. Each was carrying a thick book in their hands. All three boys were wearing glasses. They were Albert, Isaac, and Thomas. They were three computer- and math-geeks!

    “We need to solve all three-hundred math problems by next Friday, in order to win the contest!” Isaac stated.

    “Yeah! Then we can win the Geek-Week Award!” Albert added.

    “Ooh!” Thomas gasped, when he saw Ty and Dustin, along with another extremely muscular boy, who looked like… “Jimmy?”

    “Hey, guys!” the huge muscular boy said to them. “Ty, these are Isaac, Albert and Thomas!”

    “Holy Electrons! JIMMY! What happened to you?” Albert wondered out loud.

    “Great Einstein! This is incredible!” Thomas shouted. “Look at his muscles!”

    Ty picked up on Jimmy’s thoughts right away. He realized that Jimmy wanted nothing more, than to see his Geeky friends become big and muscular. These three boys got picked on all the time and they were frequent targets of the bullies in school, because they were so smart.

    Ty folded his arms and stared at the three geeky teens.

    “So, what do you think of your friend, Jimmy?” Ty asked them.

    “He looks so… BIG!” Thomas replied.

    “Hey, guys! I’m still a geek!” the huge muscle boy replied. “I want to help you with Geek-Week!”

    “Where did he get all those muscles from?” Albert wondered out loud.

    “Isn’t this cool?” Jimmy cheered, as he flexed and showed off his huge biceps. “Those bullies won’t pick on ME anymore!”

    “Yeah, but…” Isaac pondered. “It is physically impossible for someone to grow that big in such a short amount of time!”

    Ty folded his arms across his chest as he stared at Isaac.

    “Wanna bet?” Ty asked the geek.

    Suddenly Isaac was overcome by an incredible wave, like a shock of electricity hit every muscle in his body. Then, his body began to GROW in every direction! His skeletal structure started to change! His bones started to crack, His fingers grew longer, his arms grew thicker, his legs grew thicker and wider and longer. He was just getting BIGGER in every possible area of his body!

    “Whoa! What? What is going on??” the boy gasped, as he felt an AMAZING growth spurt.

    Isaac sprouted like a weed, growing from 5 foot 3 inches tall to almost 7 feet tall, in a matter of seconds! His ankles showed at the bottom of his jeans and his shirt sleeves were too short for his long, thick arms. The bottom of his shirt was sticking out, since his torso also grew thicker and wider and longer.

    “AaaaaahhhH!!!!” he shrieked, as his muscles began to inflate everywhere, in a dramatic fashion! His biceps ballooned to amazing sizes, bigger than bowling balls. Great mounds of rock hard muscle formed over his shoulders as delts, triceps and lats grew to amazing sizes. Fantastic pectorals filled out his shirt until his clothing ripped from the collar to the bottom. He ended up tearing the torn shirt off of him.

    “Ooooh! HELP ME! SOMEBODY HELP ME! AAAARGGG!!” he screamed, as his muscles continued to expand.

    His legs also expanded and grew, until his jeans tore apart with huge gaping holes, which ripped the fabric to shreds. His growing feet burst out of his sneakers, but giant toes popping out of his soles of the torn shoes. Isaac had practically destroyed his clothing by bursting out of them through his dramatic growth spurt.

    Huge quads filled up his huge, pillar-like legs, and soccerball-sized calves formed his giant calves. His legs were thicker than the columns which supported the building. Finally, when the growth had stopped, Isaac was standing even bigger than Jimmy!

    “Oh… My… Gosh…” Isaac panted. “I… I’m so BIG!”

    Jimmy just smiled as he looked up at the huge, muscular geek who was now standing before him.

    “Now, let’s put some clothes on the big boy!” Ty chuckled. Ty pointed his finger at Isaac, and instantly, the towering, seven-foot-tall muscle-boy was wearing a large tank-top, and basketball shorts, and size 18 sneakers.

    Ty grinned as he walked up to Isaac. He came up to Isaac’s chest, as he looked up and admired his handiwork.

    “So, how are you feeling, big guy?” Ty grinned.

    “I-I have to admit…” the giant, muscular geeky boy replied, as he slowly grew accustomed to his new size and strength. “This is quite a new experience…”

    “Well, you’re a muscle-dude now!” Ty stated, as he looked at Thomas and Albert.

    “So…. Who’s next?” Ty asked them.
