    Miranda was in pain, more pain than she had ever felt in her life. She had volunteered to come on a rescue mission and now she would soon be the one who needed rescuing. She looked down to see the knife in her stomach and wanted to remove it, but knew how dangerous it would be if she did. Miranda looked up to see Aloe'la fighting against Ezekiel Kane who they thought they had control over, but not as much as they once had thought.

    "Rotten old bastard! You don't deserve heaven or hell for what you've done!" Aloe'la shouted as she fought against Kane who was surprised at Aloe'la's fighting abilities.

    "Very impressive fighting talent you have there, I'm sure the Normal wouldn't mind having you on their side." Kane mocked.

    "I serve my Queen, my people, and my friends, none of which you're a part of!" Aloe'la shouted as she managed to get in a few good hits on Kane before she smacked the fairy away who landed on the ground and returned to her human body form.

    Tears poured down Miranda's eyes as she thought it would be all over for her. 'Amara, mi amor, I'm sorry...' Miranda thought to herself. 'Papá, I wish you could have seen me grow into a beautiful woman.' Miranda thought of her father now. 'Mamá....please, don't be sad for what happens to me next.' Miranda said as it seemed like her last tear fell down to the ground and she blacked out.

    *********************************************

    Miranda opened her eyes and she was in a park now. She felt her cheek and saw blood coming out of it. She then heard her Mamá coming over to her in a panic.

    "Miranda, mi preciosa niña, are you okay?" Gabrielle asked her daughter. Miranda looked around and remembered where she was at. She was at the park when she had only been 8 years-old. She had tried to play with some other Spanish girls her age at the park, but they played a cruel prank on her and beat her and called her a 'mixed up all wrong'.

    "Oh, Miranda. I'm so sorry you had to go through that." Gabrielle apologized as she helped Miranda stand up and dusted her off.

    "Is it true? Am I mixed up all wrong?" Miranda said trying not to cry.

    "You're not 'mixed up all wrong at all. I'm Black, You're father is Mexican, and we have two beautiful multiracial children who come from two different yet unique families. There's nothing wrong with being who you are mija, but you're always going to face bullies in the world. Just remember if someone's bullying you, stand up to them and I know one day you'll have friends to back you up. Do you know why?" Gabrielle asked as Miranda shook her head.

    "Because you're Miranda Desoto. You don't fall down to bullies, you stand up brave and strong." Gabrielle said as she hugged her arms around her mother."

    "Gracias mamá." Miranda quietly said as she hugged her mother.

    ***********************************************

    As Ezekiel Kane was about to pound the life out of Aloe'la, they both looked over at Miranda who woke with a start as her eyes seemed to blaze like the sun. She looked down at the knife in her stomach and used her powers to pull out the knife as she placed her other hand over the wound and it seemed to heal like nothing had happened. Then the next thing that happened also surprised them as Miranda seemed to use her powers and aged herself up into a beautiful 21 year-old woman.

    "SOLAR GUARDIANDS, I SUMMON YOU TO ME!" Miranda said, but it sounded like she was speaking with other women at once. Just as she spoke her summons, the other Solar Guardians assembled before her.

    Jenna, Missy, Lilith, and Marilyn were all shocked for a moment when they were teleported to Miranda's location but just like Miranda, they gained their Solar Guardian forms and were all turned into their 21 year-old forms in their uniforms and just like Miranda, they all seemed to speak with many voices.

    "EZEKIEL KANE, YOU HAVE LONG BEEN HUNTING AND KILLING THOSE YOU SEE AS WEAK, INFERIOR, AND EVIL. YET NOW YOU HAVE ENDANGERED THE LIVES OF INNOCENTS ONCE AGAIN. BABES WHO HAVE DONE YOU NO WRONG. HOW DO YOU PLEAD?" The voices asked him.

    "You can frighten me all you want, but I know is his all some act. None of you can actually kill me you don't have it in your hearts."

    "HOW DO YOU PLEAD?!!!" The voices asked him again.

    "Fine, you want to know how I plead? Not guilty, all those that I killed in the past, they deserved it. Those on the supernatural end and my own family, they didn't have the guts to do what I did. What I did, I always did for the good of the world." Ezekiel Kane answered.

    Solar Guardian Pluto then stepped forward. "WAS MY DEATH MEANT FOR THE GOOD OF THE WORLD?" Solar Guardian Pluto asked.

    "You? How should I know, I've never met you before." Kane argued.

    "BUT YOU HAVE, KANE, YOU HAVE. WE MET WHEN I WAS A MERE BABE IN THE CRIB. YOU SOUGHT TO END MY LIFE, BUT BY A MIRACLE, I SURVIVED." Pluto's voices said as her form shifted from a baby to a child, then a teenager, then the guardian Pluto once again.

    "My daughter?! No, that's....that's impossible....you....you died all those years ago." Kane said with fear in his voice.

    "I LIVED, AS YOU SHOULD HAVE LET ME LIVE KANE. NOW WE ARE YOUR JUDGE, AND JURY, BUT NOT YOU'RE EXECUTIONERS!" The Solar Guardians said.

    "What? Why?! Why don't you just kill me then?!" Kane demanded to know.

    "IT IS FOR ANOTHER TO DECIDE YOUR FATE, EZEKIEL KANE, ONE WHOM YOU STILL HAVE UNFINISHED BUSINESS WITH." The Solar Guardians said as they used their wands and struck Kane who looked over himself confused at first.

    "What did you do to me?!"

    "YOU ARE HUMAN, ONCE MORE, NO MORE VAMPIRE POWERS, NOW, BEGONE TO YOUR FINAL JUDGE!" The Solar Guardians said as they teleported him away.

    The imbued Solar Guardians went over to Aloe'la's body and healed her of her recent wounds.

    "M...M....Miranda, is that really you?" Aloe'la asked shocked.

    "YES, AND NO. MIRANDA IS IN HERE WITH US, WITH ALL OF US. SHE IS THE CURRENT HOST OF THE SOLAR GUARDIAN SUN OF BOTH PAST AND FUTURES. SHE SUMMONED US TO SENTENCE JUDGEMENT ON EZEKIEL KANE ONCE AND FOR ALL. HIS FINAL JUDGEMENT SHALL COME SOON." The Solar Guardian Sun said to Aloe'la.

    "So are you all just like this forever?" Aloe'la asked.

    "NO, THE OTHERS SHALL RETURN FROM WHENCE THEY CAME AND I SHALL RETURN TO MIRANDA'S SOLAR GUARDIAN FORM. SHE WON'T REMEMBER EVERYTHING, JUST TELL HER THAT SHE....KICKED EZEKIEL KANE'S ASS. NOW IT IS TIME, SISTERS. RETURN FROM WHENCE YOU CAME!" The Solar Guardian Sun said she struck the ground with her staff and the rest of the Solar Guardians were gone with only Miranda back in front of Aloe'la in her normal Solar Guardian attire.

    "Oh my head. Aloe'la, what just happened? Did I attack Kane?" Miranda asked feeling a slight headache.

    "Yeah, Miranda. You kicked his ass. Now come on, we've got some vampire kids to rescue." Aloe'la said as Miranda regained her legs and the two went to go rescue the children.
