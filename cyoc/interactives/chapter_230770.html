No, dear brother, I’m totally normal and besides, Mike feels the same way, YOU are in the way of OUR happiness, but soon everything will be as it should’.

To not cause panic, Lucia decides to cause everyone, except herself and Mike, to think that “Valentin” has only a younger brother.
