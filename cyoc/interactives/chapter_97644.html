    Dudes by the Dozen

    added 7 years ago BM O

    Prior to this day, these boys existed in multiple families, let alone multiple bodies. Four of them already existed as brothers: Leo, Donnie, Raph, and Mikey. Ty and Dustin had existed as brothers as well, and they had a wide circle of friends, including Leo and his brothers. The thing that they appreciated the most is how close their circle was: they were eleven guys who were now fused together in one body. They had two arms, two legs set wide apart, a very long body, and eleven heads on two shoulders. Their clothes had fused each time, and the giant number on the front told how many people were in the body: it was an 11 now, but soon it would change.

    When the doorbell rang, Peter called for the person to come in. It was Damion, a friend of Mason, and he stopped coming in when he saw the boys on the couch. He wouldn't have been surprised if they were in separate bodies, but his surprise was beginning to turn into anticipation.

    "Can I join?" Damion asked. "By all means," Ty answered. "Just touch your side against Raph's." As soon as Damion did, his left side disappeared into the giant body, which expanded itself out towards him. Damion's clothes fused into theirs, and their shirt became twelve wide strips of color. The 11 on the shirt shifted towards the new center, and became a 12. Sure enough, The guys had become one long body with two legs, two arms, and the heads of twelve different guys--Damion, Raph, Donnie, Jake, Allen, Dustin, Ty, Peter, Steven, Leo, Mason, and Mikey.

    "How cool can a body get?" Damion said with excitement.

    "As cool as we can make it!" Mason answered.

    "Brothers should be close, but I'm sure we really stand out!" Raph said to Leo, who agreed.

    "Good luck finding ANYONE as cool as we are now!" Mikey said.

    "If this is even remotely a dream, then I don't ever want to wake up!" Donnie smiled.

    "Nostalgia's got nothing about this!" Jake cheered.

    "Neither does Ripley's!" Steven joked.

    "Even I hardly believe this, and I'm literally a part of it!" Peter declared.

    "Twelve guys in one person..." Allen said. "This is really getting unique."

    "Congrats, Ty!" Dustin said. "The twelve of us have become the closest circle of friends to ever exist!"

    "Well, we still have more friends..." Ty said. "We'll see what happens."

    Whatever happened, the boys wanted to go through with it--literally TOGETHER.
