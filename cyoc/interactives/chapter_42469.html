You simply lie under the covers and stare up at the ceiling, thinking about how the interview went today, and what you should do to prepare for your next one. It feels as good as yesterday, and you're suddenly reminded about how good the water felt in that lake in the park. You linger for a while, unwilling to shut the water off, but eventually manage to coax yourself to do it.

    You turn to grab a towel and feel something on your shoulders. It's your wet hair. It's slapping your shoulders and the top of your back. You run the towel over it several times, then step over to the mirror to take a look.

    You're not at all pleased to see that your hair has suddenly gone through a growth spurt. It's now over a foot long in back and just beyond chin-length on the sides. Strangely, your bangs haven't grown much at all. The bangs in the middle of your face still end at your eyes. The bangs get progressively longer as they approach your temples, where they hang at the level of your nose. Clay was right about your hair looking fluffier. The spandex waistline of the underwear is barely holding it to your torso, and you have to go three notches past where you usually secure your belt. The cuffs fall over your feet so you can barely see your toes.

    This definitely isn't right. After all you ate yesterday, your waist couldn't have gotten smaller. And why are your pants too long?
