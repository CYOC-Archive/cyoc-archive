    While the whole group was starting to shatter itself because of Callum's actions, the Phone started a rapid scan of its surroundings.
    It asserted the current location, and noticed that the subjects had gone beyond the decisive momentum of validating the changes, registered as " Reality Iterations Processus ".
    The Phone scanned the subjects a second time, and soon found out the sole reason why no decision was taken : they had been modified to the point of not being capable of handling the device.
    Checking the options chosen, the machine stumbled upon some lines checked :

    > IF SUBJECT INCAPACITED OR CHANGED BEYOND DEVICE USE :
    > REALITY ALTERATION = TRUE
    > ORIGINAL SUBJECT SITUATION PRESERVER ACTIVATED = FALSE
    > BACKUP FILES OF SUBJECTS ORIGINAL STATE : FALSE

    Since Maggie hadn't been able to properly tamper with the device beforehand, it read in short that in case of severe changes, reality had to be re-ajusted so that it was locked as the current one.
    Which meant that Brody, Maggie, Callum and Braiden had to be saved as the animals they currently were permanently.
    But the device knew that some world lines had to be drastically changed.
    For instance, there was no possibility that Brody's former family owned a horse, compared to the other subjects which were more common.
    The device ran a series of test simulations to find the perfect situation for the quatuor of animals, and after a few potential good ones, he registered the one that made the most common sense.

    In short, Brody was to be sent in a set of stables outside of town, impregnating mares until his supposed death.
    Maggie was supposed to be situated in a zoo in Germany, which owned several cold-blooded creatures such as her. She was to be the newest addition.
    Callum was designed to have been born in the jungle of Amazonia, fitting perfectly in the environment where he would have grew up.
    Lastly, Braiden was to be put in the Seine river in France, living half-way from Paris, but just enough so that he would not be caught by fishermen.
    The device confirmed those changes.

    > REALITY ALTERATION PROCESSING... PLEASE WAIT...
    > REALITY ALTERATION DONE
    > TRANSPORTATION OF SUBJECTS DONE
    > MIND ALTERATION DONE

    Brody found himself in an enclosed inside building, waiting to be shown the next mare that he would copulate with.
    He happily munched on his food, being tired after getting sollicited by horseriders and anticipating the next obedience lessons over time.

    Maggie was enclosed in a small dark room. She had no clue about what was happening, and started to get anxious.
    Hopefully, the light came back in, and she saw what was apparently a huge vivarium, filled with some of her cold-blooded comrades.
    She boiled with excitation at the idea of living here.

    Callum woke up at sunrise.
    The jungle started to fill with noises of all kinds, which decided him to take off the branch in which he slept, seeking food for the nest and his companion.
    Occasionaly, he would stumble upon human tribesmen, seeking bigger preys, not bothered by a common jungle parrot.

    Braiden was wandering aimlessly in the deep and muddy depths of the river, eating bacteriae and smaller organisms.
    His life was really was simpler, and he did not have to be concerned about anything particular.

    > REALITY ALTERATIONS CONFIRMED
    > TRANSPORTATION OF DEVICE TO ANOTHER LOCATION
    > LOCATION PATTERN : RANDOM = TRUE
    > SHUTTING DOWN UNTIL FURTHER ACTIVATION
