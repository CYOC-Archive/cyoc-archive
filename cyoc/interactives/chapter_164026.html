She was working from home, however, as Megan was still breastfeeding, and she was due to give birth any day. And the reason she had bred with Bill was because they had a romantic relationship, which they were keeping secret, since it was considered scandalous. But it wasn’t scandalous for a single human woman to breed with her bull servant, whether for children, calves, or just for fun.

Changing things would have to wait until she gave birth. Sherry got to her feet and waddled over to her bed. She felt confident in her ability to deliver on her own. Another contraction hit her. She moaned loudly. Bill, still a walking cow, but now naked, appeared in the doorway.

She was about to change things to normal, but hesitated. Bill had a much larger dick as a cow than he had as a human. She would honestly be a bit sad to see it return to its smaller size.
