    ginat bugs, and others.

    The twins were excited because there were a lot more choices in
    characters tonight then there were in the past. They were moving about
    as formless ghosts through a see of 3D pedestals that contained two-
    person characters. "Oh man, look at the choices." Tom commented, "I
    have never seen so many."

    "Yeah! The game must be getting more popular." Dan replied. As they
    continued looking for a pair they liked, one pair grabbed Dan's
    attention. "Hey what about these two. They are dragon twins named
    Blaze and Frost."

    "Dragon twins, let me take a look at them." Tom quickly moved his
    ghost form to where his brother was. He read aloud the description,
    "Blaze and Frost, Dragon twins born of opposite elements. Blaze is a
    fiery red dragon with the power to command fire. Frost is an icy blue
    dragon with the power to command ice. The twins lived in the Land of
    Dragons all of their lives until Mordred the Great concurred the land
    and enslaved the dragons. Now, Blaze and Frost are fighting to free their kin."

    Dan commented, "Their twins, their dragons, their powerful, their
    perfect for us. How about we be them, and if we pick them, I call
    Blaze."

    "I agree, they are perfect for us. So, I guess that I will be Frost
    then." Tom replied. "Alright lets get this game going." Both the twins
    pushed the 'Enter the Kingdom' button that was just hovering out there
    near their incorporeal hands.

    A moment later, and the twins were on top of a mountain looking over a
    dark kingdom....
