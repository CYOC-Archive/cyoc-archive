    Becoming The Seed Of A Fairy Flower

    added by Elflover A year ago BM I S O Mythological Magic Vore

    You pound on the closed petals, wanting out desperately. But they hold firm and moreover seem to harden more and more. The perfume becomes stronger and without being pungent to the point of becoming nauseating, it is starting to have an effect on you. The interior of this bud is pink and so is the dim light inside. But the fragrance of this bud and its petal is making you dizzy. You start yawning and even laughing like an idiot. This perfume is a drug which is forcing you to relax. You sit down on the soft powdery bottom of the bud, releasing a cloud of yellow dust. It is pollen and inhaling it makes you even more relaxed and sleepy. You are rapidly covered in the pollen and you just want to lie down and close your eyes. You feel so tired... Oh, you know it is only an effect of the flower but a part of your mind wonders if it's really with the effort to try escaping. It is so comfortable here, and so warm... The rational side of your brain falls asleep just as you shut your eyes, letting yourself surrender to the safe cocoon you are in. It's like being in the bosom of a caring mother. You feel protected and happy. As a reflex, you even put your thumb in your mouth. You think of a wonderful, wonderful womb in which you are nestled as you drift off to sleep. You even feel being gently rocked, the flower drifting in the wind to reassure you even more.
    Yes, because unbeknownst to you, the bud needs you sleeping and completely relaxed. It sees human as malleable within its petals and indeed that is what humans, and only humans, are once they are within its grasp. By a sheer reflex, you curl up in your sleep like a baby, and the pollen you are coated in hardens in a round shape. You merge with that shape and soon you are a seed. A little, very, very precious seed. You can be swallowed or inserted into any fairy creature, male or female, and he or she will become pregnant with you, with or without the intervention of another male. Best of all (for the fay being), the carrier of your soul will then be able to decide which characteristics from his or her own heritage he or she desires to be transmitted to you. Of course, if a male gamete reaches you, it opens up the possibilities even wider. The seed that you are thus sells for a fortune in fairy world.
    So after a week or so of maturing, the flower opens, revealing the seed that you now are. The fairy that let you here comes by, enchanted by the seed you've been transformed into. You can hear and feel, but you cannot see or move. You are a seed, after all. And you are appalled at what the fairy tells you as she grabs you and hoists you up in the air with her. She's going to sell you to a fay couple, or a fay creature that is single, desiring of becoming a mother or a father and allow her (or worse, him) to insert you in his or her body or to swallow you so you can become their perfect child! No way! You'll have no choice in your appearance! You don't want to be a fairy! Or worse, a girl! You don't want to be the child of a pregnant male, fairy or otherwise! And you might lose all of your memories too! You try to protest, but of course, it's no use. You cannot speak. You cannot move. You can only listen in as the fairy is off finding the fay creature intent on having you within her womb. Or his, which would be absolutely disgusting, not to mention spending months developing there... Ugh! You can't imagine being birthed again! It's foul! It's vile! And who will the fairy decide you ought to go to?
