    Miggs escaped the chaos, but ended in the wrong world

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by planeswalker barrin 13 years ago A BM TG O

    (Author's note: I kept the first-person narrator for the crossover part from the original. Ideas of the crossover part mostly from nothinsp and tei.)

    Miggs tried to fly to the portal in front of her, but it was very tricky to use these unfamiliar wings. She was almost there, only 50 feets above the gate, when she noticed that the gate was shrinking. In a last attempt to reach the gate before it closed, she folded her wings and dived down.

    Only 40 feets - The gate had the size of a castle gate.

    Only 30 feets - The gate had the size of a garage gate.

    Only 20 feets - The gate had the size of a normal door.

    Only 10 feets - The gate has the size of a large window.

    Only 5 feets - The gate had the size of a small window.

    Only one feet...

    In the very last moment Miggs dived through the closing gate and entered the world behind.

    She was glad that she made it in time. But she wasn't glad, that she had no idea how to stop her dive and approached the ground with a dangerous speed. She tried to reopen her wings, but all she could do was to change a bit of the direction. On moment later, there was a loud noise and then long silence.

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------

    Jess was the one, who heard the sound first. Even if she was the same species as me now, her ears are always better than mine. It was a long whistling sound, which ended with a short splash. I looked up and saw that Mike and Aaron stopped there small morning squabble and noticed the sound too. Together we went to the nearby lake. What ever caused the sound must have landed in the lake.

    As we reached the lake, the sight left us speechless. Which seemed very strange if you consider our own appearance. In front of us stood a naked green woman with large bat-like wings on her back. She was completely wet and was now cursing and grumbling when she walked out of the lake. Suddenly she lifted her head and saw us. She was shocked and began to cry.

    "Now this can't happen. This is all wrong!" She looked up in the sky.
    "Damned! Damned! This is the wrong world, there is also the wrong number of moons! I took a wrong gate."

    "Ehm, sorry to interrupt your complaining. But did I guess right if I say that you are not a victim of the witch?", I asked politely.

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------

    Miggs stared at the strange catgirl in front of her.

    "What the hell are you talking about! I don't know any witches. I must leave this world as fast as possible. Do you know where the next dimension gate is?"

    "Eh, no? But I think I can help you.", said the catgirl. "By the way. I am Jeremy. What's your name."

    "I am Miggs.", said Miggs with a puzzled face, "But isn't Jeremy a male name, even in this backwater world?"

    "Yeah, it is.", answered the girl smiling, "All of us are transformed and transgendered victims of the witch. I supposed to be a male human. Come, I explain you everything on the way."

    As Miggs followed the catgirl, she noticed the rest of the group. Jeremy saw his look and introduced the rest.

    "Enthusiastic green girl who talks to the tree is Mike. She's a dryad. And the harpy with the black feathers and red hair is Aaron. She can be very rude and fierce, but she has a soft core. And that is Jess our only 'male' member. She used to be a girl before. Hey all. This is Miggs, she is from another world. I think we shall bring her to the witchs cottage."

    On the way to the cottage Miggs couldn't fight his curiosity and continued to ask Jeremy questions.

    "So why did you keep your name?"

    "Cause it's remember us what we were, also it helps to fight same of the nastier urges of our curse."

    "Like what?", asked Miggs curiously.

    "Hunting.", said Jeremy simply. "Jess was once a male naga who treid to kill us before we stopped him."

    "Oh, I am sorry.", apologized Miggs.

    "No problem. By the way. I suggest you was also not born as a green demon."

    Miggs blushed. "Is it so obvius?", asked she.

    "Remember who I was. I know what signs I must look for." And so Miggs told his story to the four mythics.

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------

    When we was near the cottage Miggs turned to me and asked me another question.

    "Why do we go to the witchs cottage. I thought she did this to you, why should she help us."

    "Oh, the cottage is empty. We defeated the witch a few days ago. Jess, show it to her."
    Jess walked next to us and showed her the glass jar where Jess kept the snake who was once the witch.

    "She is already dangerous, but now she is reliant on us, sow e kept her to get a cure from her. But until now we've got no results. But in the cottage of the witch is a huge globus which she used to travel to other worlds and catch her victims. We can use it to get you into your homeworld.", answered Jeremy.

    "I still ask myself why we help here?", grumbled Aaron next to us. "We know for only a hour."

    "Cause here in the woods we stick together. We are a family.", said Mike, who walked on my left. "And perhaps we find a cure for our curse in Miggs homeworld.", added Jess.

    This seemed to be enough reasons for Aaron cause she jumped into the sky to fly above us, but even there we could here her grumbling.

    "Don't mind her.", said Mike with a smile. "She is only envious cause you have wings AND hands. Also I think its time for her to lay her next egg."

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------

    Without any further complications Miggs, Jeremy, Mike, Jess and Aaron reached the cottage.
