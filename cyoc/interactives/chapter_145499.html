    Giving the Team a Boost

    Ty and Dustin took a seat in the stands. They noticed that the skinnier football players were having a lot of trouble, getting knocked around pretty easily by the big guys. And there were a lot of little guys. They weren't just freshmen and sophomores or even just JV players.

    In short, the Trojans football team sucked.

    Ty also noticed that the team wasn't taking up the whole field. There was a group of high school nerds sitting off at the far end, doing homework outside on a beautiful day. There was also a group of boys ranging from 10 to 14, some athletic, some not, playing tag.

    Ty began to concoct a solution. He began to recite his wish, saying, "I wish that everyone on the football team under 220 pounds would gain 60 pounds of muscle or as many as it takes to hit 220, whichever comes first, and 6 inches in height. I wish that the nerds sitting on the field would all gain 100 pounds of muscle, a foot in height and a desire to play football. I wish that all those younger kids would age 4 years and then become subject to my first wish. I wish that all these boys were extremely handsome. And I wish that they would be aware while changing, but after the change, they are no longer aware." Ty had to take a deep breath after saying all that. Then he sat back and watched his handiwork unfold.

    He first looked at Kevin and Simon, 2 smaller football players. They were both sophomores and average in looks and height. Both weighed about 140 pounds. But suddenly they shot up to 6'4" and packed on muscle under their shorts and tank tops. Soon they were 200 pounds of thick muscle, with big pecs and biceps and cut abs. They also became very good-looking. The rest of the team soon followed suit, with even freshmen becoming 6'2" and 180 pounds.

    Next were the nerds, among them Cal, Finn, and Oliver, who were 130 pounds and 5'8". They were thin looking, but not for long. 2 minutes later, they and 2 other boys were sitting in their clothing remains, wondering how they had gone from nerds to 6'8" 230 pound sexy hunks with huge muscles and desires to play football. They ran off and joined the team.

    Finally were the kids. One of them was Austin, a 120-pound 14-year-old. He soon became a 160-pound 18-year-old. Then the first wish took effect, bulking him to 220 pounds. Handsome and big, he joined a group of seniors.

    Ty leaned back, satisfied with his work.
