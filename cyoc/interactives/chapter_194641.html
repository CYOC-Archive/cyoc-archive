    "Who said that?" Madeline asked as she pointed to a bunch of boxes where something was definitely hiding behind. It's voice was sweet and squeaky like a cartoon character. She took a few steps until a little white bunny suddenly jumped up in front of her. It was coughing a little and moving almost like an human, so Madeline realized that it wasn't an ordinary animal.

    "Hi, I'm Joy, your animal companion and guardian of fertility." The bunny said while he uncovered a red heart-shaped jewel on his forehead. "I'm here to guide you into the world of magical girls!"

    In a normal situation, Madeline would have freaked out after seeing a talking bunny but for some reason, she wasn't. Inside her, something was telling her to trust this creature and accept what was happening. However, she still needed some answers.

    "Let me give you a brief explanation." Joy said. "All over the globe exist relics called Keys that connect the human world and the magical one, you have found one, the wind wand which gives you the power of fertility. It also enhances your speed and agility. You are going to use those new abilities for the greatest good."

    "So, I'm going to fight monsters and villains like a superhero?" Madeline asked with enthusiasm. "I'm ready!"

    "Good, but first you need to learn to control your powers." Joy said. "Speed and agility are naturals on you now, but you also have fertility magic that you must learn to use in battle."

    "Fertility magic? Like making a tree to grow?" Madeline wondered as she looked at her magic wand.

    "Yes, but you can do a lot more better things than that." Joy said. "Umm... let's see... Is there someone else here?"

    "Yes, my son is downstairs." Madeline said.

    "Excellent, he would be useful." Joy happily replied. "Let's go to meet him!"

    Joy jumped to Madeline's head where he stayed like a pillow while she went to her son's bedroom. She can heard music coming out of his room. Something that he usually do when he's playing videogames. She carefully opened the door and watched her 11 years old boy focused on his TV, he can't see nor hear.

    "Now, point your wand to him and said Be fertile!" Joy commanded. Madeline had no idea of what's going to happen to her son but she obeyed any way. So, she raised her wand and pointed it to her son. She inhaled and said the incantation.

    "Be fertile." For a brief moment nothing happened, but suddenly a pink cloud appeared around him until it covered him completely. A few seconds later, the cloud dissipates and Madeline noticed that his son has changed...
