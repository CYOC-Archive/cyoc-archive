    Becoming God's servant

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Unnamed 9 years ago O

    In her dream, Janith heard a voice. To her, it sounded a lot like the voice of Morgan Freeman. But somehow she knew it was, in fact, God's speaking to her. "I know you're a good person, Janith. But I also know it wasn't always the case, I know you used to be like most succubi, who are some of the worst demons."

    "And I'm truly sorry about that your holiness, venerable and divine lord who look after everyone of his faithfuls. But my past is what it is. As much as I wish to change it, I will never be able, and can only accept it's burden." Said the demoness.

    "You can't change it, that's true." Acknowledged God. "But you can achieve forgiveness for your sins."

    That greatly surprised the succubus "I… Can? Even after all I've done in my past, I still can?"

    "It's true Janith. But I must warn you, it's going to be far harder than it sound." Informed the all father.

    "However hard it is, I will try."

    God was truly pleased by Janith's answer. "Good, because I have a few missions for you. Before you, other demons have tried to change side. But unlike you, neither me or my angels were able to contact them before they were either dissuaded to do it or killed. Janith, any demon that wish to change, I want you to help them do so. Make your house a sanctuary for them, Janith. That angel I've send to you, Angelica, she will help you protect them. But that's not your main mission."

    "It… It's not? But then, what is my main mission?" Asked the succubus.

    "Your oldest daughter, Julie, might be half-succubus. But her soul is extremely pure. In the past, only one human soul was as pure as Julie's, it was the soul of a maiden called Mary. She truly was a saint, and faithful like no one ever was before her. So I have offered her a gift. The joy to be mother wile still a virgin, the honor to have me as her child's father in the more basic way, instead of only as the 'all father', creator of everything.

    I don't know if I will give the same gift to your daughter, or another of similar value. But I want you to protect the purity of Julie, Janith. I want your daughter to stay the living saint she currently is."

    "I will." Answered Janith. "But promise me that, before making her pregnant, you will ask her. I don't want any prophet to be an unwanted child."

    "I wouldn't do otherwise!" Assured God "But you must know that, when I made Mary pregnant, I didn't plan for Jesus to become a prophet. Alas, the need to make him become one did arose later."
