    As she walked out the door, Margaret suddenly froze in place. Noah felt the world shifting again as Noah was able to watch the changes now. Margaret immediately shrank from her normal 5 something height down to less than a foot. Her clothes shrank with her but also began to change. The color shifted to a bright pink and it fused to become one dress.

    Her hair changed too as its texture began look more like a raggedy Ann doll rather than actual hair, although the style and color remained. Her skin changed texture too as it suddenly transformed from actual human flesh to something more akin to a pillow. Her proportions changed to resemble a toddler rather than an adult, and then she suddenly fell over.

    Noah looked and saw two buttons and a line of thread that slightly resembled Mrs. McCoy, but forced to smile. Noah picked it up and examined it. No genitals, no vital signs, completely stuffed. Even after all he saw, he couldn't believe that had worked. Margaret McCoy was now nothing more than a doll.

    Before he could examine it anymore, Stacy walked by his room before looking very angry.
    "Hey dork, what are you doing with my Margaret doll?" She yelled before swiping her former mother. "I told you to stop stealing stuff as pranks it is not funny!" She said before huffing away to her room. Noah glanced inside to see Margaret the toy get placed on a shelf by her former daughter.

    Noah shut the door to his room and started laughing. It wasn't the laugh of someone who had pulled a prank or heard a good joke, but one that hid anger and joy at the same time. "Finally." Noah thought. "Finally I have the power to put these assholes in there place."

    Noah grabbed the journal along with a pencil, and made his way downstairs. He walked downstairs to find his adoptive family, their extended family, and all of Tina's bratty high school friends talking away. Of course none even acknowledged Noah as he walked over to the empty living room to sit and ponder his next change.

    He had noticed a red headed woman he had never seen before, and after a quick glance at a photo in the living room, his suspicions were confirmed. There was a wedding photo he had seem many times before, but where Margaret McCoy once stood was now the woman Noah had never seen. Noah held in his hands the power to alter reality itself, and he just couldn't wait to get some revenge.
