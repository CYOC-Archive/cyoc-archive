    Uncle watched Sarah over the next few weeks very closely. At first she was as nervous, like a cat on a hot tin roof. Then like she slipped into the daily groove, her nervousness was almost forgotten.

    Many a time though she was seen looking into the glass enclosure where the Medallion stood floating in a solid plastic cube. Now as it was the powers which were could not be reached for, or reached out to do anymore harm.

    In the third month the pregnancy was showing growth as her belly rounded out. Sarah told her Uncle of her plans to go full term and deliver the new life. "It's part of my experience in life!" she would explain to those who ultimately knew.

    The Museum's new look and the work Sarah did in procuring the best and most original details for the barn scene made the day. She would sit for an hour a day on a tall wooden stool set in the big box stall. Her memories of being one whole night held as a heifer in a stall of that type held many clear memories of what stood about.

    One night Sarah went into the "Jewelry of History" exhibit and knelt down outside the glass case. A cold stare, Sarah made as she looked the Medallion's blue stone straight in the eye.

    "I thank you for letting me see the other side of life! I would wish if we could only touch for yet to return to what life that was. Medallion, Medallion I wish, I may, I wish I might, that with your power I could return to such delights!" said Sarah with fond hopes as her little friend inside stirred.

    Getting up slowly she moaned from back pains and had she remains looking at the Medallion, Sarah might have seen the blue stone glow.
    Feeling a inner need to be with those who knew of such wants Sarah began to search her desk for the name of that one farm.

    At last, "Winter Farms & Petting Zoo" was scrawled on a piece of paper. It was past eight in the evening but if she jumped into her car the farm was a hour and a half away. Soon enough Sarah wrapped in her thick coat was driving ever faster toward that interesting farm.

    Even as she drove the daily rituals were at her fingertips. Popping open the medicine bottle of HGH she took one out as per normal. Then looking down she grumbled to herself and shook out six more than normal. A mouthful of pills she choked down with a chug of pure bottled water. Now she needed to get to the farm soon before the medicine took it's hold on her system.

    At last the lights from the upstairs bedroom lighted the front walk to the house and porch. A ringing of the doorbell brought a thundering sound of footsteps coming to meet the caller. The door opened by Miss Winter and her more than friendly smile.

    "I'm Sarah Benning, I'm told that you have had an experience with the Brazilian Medallion and it's powers of change!" asked Sarah.

    "Yes my dear girl we have, please come in and have a warm cup of tea!" offered Miss Winter.

    Sarah was seated in the living room proper and when Miss Winter returned with the tea she announced her lover AJ. As AJ. stepped around the corner and into sight Sarah started to laugh. Miss Winter looked at AJ. in a questioning way, as he looked at her the same.

    Laughing, "I'm sorry but he looks so perfect as a Satyr! I have memories of being a heifer for one long week and then regaining my humanity. Somehow I thought that cows were the only thing it changed people into, oh but then Detective Martin told Uncle and I of horses, goats, and bulls which some people became. I came here to stay and have this baby as his father was a Holstein bull. May I please stay here with you?" asked Sarah now very serious of face.

    "Why not!" said A.J. as Miss Winter nodded her head an agreeable yes as well. The two ushered Sarah up to a small bedroom apart from their huge love making room.

    Sarah sat on her bed undressing and with a happy thought she remembered her pleading to the Medallion a few hours ago. "I wish I may, I wish I might, that with your power I could return to such delights, to live here as I lived there, a heifer giving her first birth to this calf!"

    Crawling under the covers Sarah ran her wish over again and smiled, "Too bad it can't come true!" she thought.

    Suddenly on the room's ceiling a glowing blue haze was forming. Like a cloud of blue stone's power the cloud descended over Sarah. Sarah's eyes were wide as she watched in terror and glee. As it touched the bed and wrapped around Sarah's body she felt the power.

    Laying still her belly full of HGH helped as the Medallion's power did it's work once again.
