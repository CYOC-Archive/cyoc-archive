    After playing video games for a while with Adam on his lap, Cody decided it was time to do something else. He noticed ( and smelt) that Adam had once again made a mess in his diaper however Cody had been careful to add multiple layers so that Adam was very well padded. The diaper still have a ways to go before it would burst and Adam seemed to enjoy it being squishy.
    Cody lifted Adam up and held him in one arm while walking over into the kitchen. Placing Adam into a big highchair Cody thought about what he would feed his newfound baby.
    "Would baby like some milk?"
    Adam shook his head and pouted clearly not interested in the drink offered to him.
    "Huh...when adam wasn't mentally regressed he didn't like milk either..guess it really is him."
    Judging off of how Adam still had the same interests as he did when he wasn't acting like a wittle baby Cody got him a bottle full of mango nectar , Adam's favorite . As Cody held the bottle above Adam who was reaching his hands up for it in his high chair Cody laughed seeing how helpless Adam really was. He finally , much to Adams delight, removed Adam's pacifier and put the bottle in his mouth. While Adam sucked happily away at his juice Cody grabbed a bib and wrapped it around Adams neck incase he made a mess while eating ( which he probably would.) Cody reheated some fried rice from the fridge and began to spoon feed it to Adam, making little airplane noises as he put the food in. Adam was a very messy eater and Cody would constantly have to wipe rice off his face.
    After they had finished eating , Adam looked very tired. Cody knew Adam hated naps but he bet that he could trick Adam into taking one, especially when his belly was full. He picked Adam up and held him bridal style in his big arms rocking him back and forth. At first Adam looked confused but once Cody stuck a pacifier in his mouth he quickly drifted off to sleep in his arms. Cody carried him over to the giant crib the remote had created and placed him inside of it, tucking him under the covers.
    "Sweet Dreams Cutie."
