    Your Mom, the Kitsune

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 10 years ago A BM S

    Before handing it over to your little brother nick, your mom had memorized a spell from the book to transform herself with.

    She was alone in her room "Here goes," she says "Kit,kit, will-o-the-wisp!" and in a puff of smoke, she is now a beautiful nine-tailed fox.

    She's the size of a wolf, with golden fur and long, gold hair on top of her head, red eyes with slit pupils.

    She smiles as she feels her tails with her front paws.

    "This feels great!" she says to herself "Wait." she realizes "I can still talk. I guess that makes sense since I'm a magical animal."

    Now your mom didn't know about the creature she had become, how mischievous kitsunes were, so a mischievous smile slowly crept across
    her canine face as thoughts began to enter her mind about the "fun" she could have as a kitsune.

    "Maybe I'll stay like this for a while," she said to herself while grinning "Sneak up on my kids and surprise them, hide under the covers and wait
    for my husband to come home only to give him a big lick in the face, or maybe I'll just dig around in the trash and see if there's anything to eat
    - that actually sounds pretty good right now. Hee-hee-hee! I know!"

    She then sat on her bed, dialed a random number with her paw, and used both to put it up to her ear.

    "Hello?" said an adult male voice she didn't recognize.

    "Excuse me," she says, trying to keep a straight face "but do you have tuna in a can?"

    "Yes, I do." he replied.

    "Well you'd better let out of there - I don't know HOW that big fish can fit inside!" and she burst out in laughter that sounded like a combination
    of female human, and fox yipping. The angry man then hung up the phone.

    "Oh, yip!" she happily exclaimed "That was sooo much fun! I can't wait to pull more pranks, arf! I'm gonna have a blast, yip!"

    She then quietly sneaks out of her room.
