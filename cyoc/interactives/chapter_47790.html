    Jon ordered a beer to start things off, and he ordered a lime daiquiri for Alexis. The bartender threw a large glass under one of the taps and quickly filled it to the brim. The large foamy head spilled over the glass and onto his three-fingered hands. He set the glass on the bar in front of Jon.

    "For a pig dude, he's pretty fast," Jon quipped. Alexis frowned, hoping the man didn't hear the remark. He seemed to be doing something suspicious when he got to fixing her daiquiri. She couldn't see the glass, or what was in his hands as he mixed the drink.

    Jon grabbed his glass and was set to chug a good portion of it, but Alexis touched his arm. At his puzzled expression, she shook her head and glanced at his beer.

    Before Jon could say anything, the bartender placed the daiquiri in front of Alexis. She thanked him and waited until he walked down the bar a good ways.

    "Did you see what he was doing to my drink?" she asked Jon.
