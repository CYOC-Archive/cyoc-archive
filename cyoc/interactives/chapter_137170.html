    Pete, he was a 26 year old man,. He had light brown hair, a skinny body, pale skin, and a small bristly moustache. He wore glasses, a red sweater, blue jeans, black socks, and brown slip-on shoes.

    One day, he was sitting at home, watching tv, when he suddenly began to feel lightheaded.

    He starts to hear a voice from his mind, a voice that wasn't his own.

    "Pete, a great power has occurred within you..." the voiced seemed to say.

    "W-what?" he stumbled with his words, rubbing his head with his hand.

    "Pete!" The voice shook him awake.

    "Y-yes?" Pete looked around, trying to see where the voice came from, before realising that he was the only one in the room.

    "We, of the great intergalactic entities, have granted you with a power, a power that only you possess, the power to change!"

    "Oh, okay..." he didn't know what to say, he couldn't believe it, and he was confused by it. "Why?"

    "Every species in the universe has granted one person to possess a power that will last them a year, and we have granted you with one power with which you can do anything with".

    "So, what power do I have? Super strength? Teleportation?" Pete questioned.

    "You have the power of change. Whatever animal or insect you think of, you will start to change into that said animal or insect. Your mind will still remained unchanged by the transformation, but your physical appearance will change. No harm will come to you as you change".

    "Oh, brilliant" Pete exclaimed, even though he was slightly disappointed that he didn't have super strength.

    "Go forth and explore the changes, Pete" The voice said, as the sound slowly started to fade away.

    Pete was excited. He had the power to change into any animal, any insect, he had to try it out, but he didn't know where to start...
