After about 10 minutes the computer processed the data and a 3-d picture of Scott came up on my monitor. Using the control keys I was able to turn the figure of Scott around and view it from any angle I wanted. There were many options displayed, I could make Scott aware or unaware of the changes, also make others aware or unaware, if I made others unaware, they would believe that Scott, or the subject as it was still referring to Scott, had always been the way I had changed him.
