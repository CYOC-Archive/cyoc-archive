    The year is around 68,999ad according to your calendar, and humanity is presumed extinct. However before the end humanity made it into the stars and established the most powerful empire the universe has ever known. In the beginning, humans found themselves alone in the universe and expanded unchallenged for thousands of years. They were in an underpopulated area of the galaxy, and this allowed them to lay claim unprecedented amounts of space and colonize them with increasing ease thanks to their near limitless resources. It was to the point that when they finally did encounter another race capable of space travel humanity had territory ten times theirs. But this is not that story. Instead this story takes place long long long after. On a single planet known as Alger.

    In it’s hay day, Alger was a pleasure world (an entire planet devoted to decadence and pleasure) and not just any pleasure world, Alger was THE pleasure world. When you thought pleasure world, you thought of Alger. This was due to its unmatched ability to cater to every single fetish imaginable in the galaxy. From furry to food to muscles to lolis, Alger had it all in abundance. Even a terraformed environment of perfectly engendered biomes. It was most famous for its gene labs, able to cater its cloned sex slaves to whatever the guest wanted. Animals, aliens, mythical creatures, sex bots, clones, holograms, virtual reality, food, feet, butts, dicks, boobs, hair, scales, fur, slime. Any fantasy you had could become real on Alger. Provided you payed of course. Then came The Collapse and Alger was abandoned. The only inhabitants left being the sex slaves. At first they had no idea what to do and just waited for more guests.

    For two of the planets rotations, creatures who’s only purpose was to fornicate waited. Then they couldn’t take it anymore and began looking for answers and a good fucking as well, they didn’t find much of either at first. After a while the robots designed to maintain the planet broke down sending the world into nature disaster as the carfuly crafted resort environments broke down. Tides rose, earthquakes shook continents and entire cities where destroyed. In the aftermath the survivors where left with little choice but to fight and fornicate one another for their place on Alger. Over the millennia some have evolved to the chaotic environment of Alger. Three thousand years ago the oceans rose to their current height swallowing most of the land, this proved a massive boon for the merfolk, but also spread their forces too thin for them to launch any more large scale attacks outside their home land. Surrounding Alger is a graveyard of star ships forming a ring of scrap metal, the reamnants of an ancient space parking lot. Maybe some of the ships still work? The moon is also dominated by an ancient ad for Velger Berry flavored soda. There are many many many factions and races on Alger, but here are the main players:

    Main Group: “The Elite”

    Sub Group 001: The Staff

    Location: Flooded Capital

    Races: Animal/ human hybrid maids

    Info: The Elite are the “ruling class” if something like that can be called to exist of Alger. Made up mostly of Animal / human hybrid maids. Most have human bodies with the addition of animal ears, tails, and eyes. Others appear as anthros and some are humans with unmatched beauty. They maintain the buildings and technology of Alger in the vane hope that one day their maker will return.

Many casinos, malls, and hotels rest in the Flooded Capital all kept in working order by the Staff. The main building they sleep in is a massive hotel 3,000 miles by 3,000 miles and thousands of stores tall. The second largest structure on the world, only surpassed by the space elevator. Males become butlers and bell boys females become maids.

    Location: Flooded Capital

    Races: Clones

    Info: Basic clones of various people and even a few celebrities most modified to be loyal to their masters. Do not reproduce manually, instead rely on cloning tubes. When one clone dies they simply make a copy of that clone. Over the years this cloning of clones has lead to some defects.

    Location: Fantasy Rock

    Races: Elves, Dwarves, Harpies, Lamias, Centaurs

    Info: Fantasy Rock is an artificial mountain and the third largest structure on Alger. Inside is a vast array of environments most of which in disrepair. Many races consistent with human mythology can be found here. They are the descendants of gene modified clones. Most of the races here are all female.

    Some of the races have left Fantasy Rock to live among the Savages.

    Location:

    Current: All across the Main Continent (Frether)

    Race: Anthros
