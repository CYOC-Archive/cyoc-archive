So don't worry about your theme park having no one to entertain!

    "We suggest you make arrangement for your park to be self-sustaining once you die of old age (unlocking immortality is not impossible but generally to be considered by the majority of our clients to be more trouble than it's worth).

    "But how can you do all this I'm sure you're asking? Is this all an elaborate prank? Hardly. You are not on TV.

    "This trinket will able to transform anyone it is used on (except yourself) into a loyal and obedient nudist humanoid animal that suits your tastes and preferences, combined with their own views of what personality traits makes an ideal servant/employee/partner/etc.

    "Once you have made your first transformation, the fundamental traits will then be permanently locked. So chose wisely.

    "Classic flesh and blood? Straight forward, easy too relate to, but also requires food, shelter, facilities, and more respect on your part.

    "We also have the option of gynoids, robots, living rubber/latex/plastic dolls, animatronics, plushies, and toons. Most of these don't require to eat or sleep, but it might be harder for you to relate to them personally. There is also a joke option included where you can turn subjects into anthro animal shaped buildings, statues, fountains, reliefs, hedge figures, etc, that retain sapience but are completely defined by their purpose that isn't locked out by choosing one of the other options.

    "Whoever you convert, will be loyal and obedient to you, and will consider you, and then your vision for the park, to be their highest calling in life. And the more employees you have, the larger your park will grow. You can grow until entire land masses all have a particular theme for guests if that is your wish.

    "And do not worry, people will think of those you've transformed as 'normal' and will not think about or question too deeply their friend, spouse, or relative's new career path or species or gender. Good luck."

    "P.S. For the sake of your sanity and emotional stability we suggest you convert a friend, relative, or spouse early on to serve as a confident. Those you chose as confidants will have a higher level of awareness of the changes in existence, but will be just as driven to help you and your vision."
