    Despite being alone with a madwoman, you burst into laughter. You laugh so much that it causes tears to spring to your eyes. Laughing even harder the tears fall from your eyes and trickle down your face.
    Doubled up at the counter, panting for breath, your laughter finally subsides.

    When you recover, you resume control over yourself. Straightening up, you face the old woman. She is staring at you with a look of thunder upon her face. Your previous humour disappears promptly and is replaced with slight fear. What if she's crazy enough to carry a gun?

    "I knew you wouldn't believe me," she says in a low voice.

    "I'm sorry," you begin. "Bu-"

    Before you can finish she thrusts the video tape in your arms. You're surprised at her strength, as it sends you tumbling backwards.

    "Watch the tape!" she demands. "And stop it on one of the trailers. See what happens. Then maybe you'll believe me."

    You regain your footing and stare down at the tape. Overwhelmed by surprise, you remain frozen.

    Regaining your composure you glance back at her, only to discover that she's left the video store. You are left stunned by the bizarre encounter.

    What do you do now?
