    "Alllllright contestants! The most chosen answer all was... ( drum roll )
    The Capabilities Maze!!!" you begin rattling off,
    "So then Welcome! To another exiting episode of The Games! Featuring the one and only: Dessert Team!!
    Yes, it is another fabulous day for The Gamers to have there very first, Competition.
    Each team will have 24 hours to complete this Maze, but just because you reach the end first does NOT meen you have won! It all really depends on each teams upgrades." You think for a moment on how to word this, then continue with, "The team that gets the most upgrades, is the only one allowed to cross the finish line.
    But wait, there's more! You may be thinking, ' How in the world are you supposed to get an unlimited of upgrades, to be more than some others team? '
    Well, that is where the 24 hours rule comes into play. As I said before, you have 24 hours to get as many up grades as possible, then be the first to find the finish line!
    So ending the rules of The Maze there, I will now list out the teams who will be competing:
    Team 1, the Trees!
    Team 2, the Desserts!
    Team 3, the Stuffed Animals!
    And last, but certainly not least;
    Team 4, the Doors!
    All teams will have 30 minutes to quickly prepare themselves and then head to the starting line!" With that, you turn off the camera, and wait for the timer to go off...

    Meanwhile down on the field, the Desserts are scrambling around, trying to get ready.
    Aaron, Carol, Zoe, Amanda, Cassie, and Annie are each trying to do a different job.
    Aaron is yelling for them to consintrate;
    Carol is recovering from getting stuck in jelly;
    Zoe is raceing around trying to do everything;
    Amanda is freaking out;
    Cassie is helping Aaron with yelling;
    And Annie?
    She is trying to figure out which person to help out!

    "Listen up!!!" Aaron shouted, "We only have a limited time, but it's not like the universe depends on us! Just calm down and try to do your part. We leave in 20 minutes."
    "Well,that didn't really help," Cassie stated dryly, "After all, what help is a marshmallow in a maze?"
    "Positive thinking, remember?" Aaron answers, "Now if you excuse me, I need to go stop them from all running into the jello..."
