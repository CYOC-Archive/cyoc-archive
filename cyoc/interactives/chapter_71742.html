    We drove to the country where there is a barn that the football team usually partied at before a game.
    Fortunately there was a game tonight and it was early.

    Jack set up a table and a plastic punch bowl. He dumped the cases of cola into the punch bowl and several
    bottles of liqueur to spike the soda. Leaving the alcohol bottles laying around the table and hiding the cans

    Everything being ready for the boys made it easy for them to take a drink and get to partying.

    As they chugged we watched as the changes started on the guys. For the most part these guys were already big

    each one of them.

    Now Nathan Caruthers was the quarterback and even though he was one of the biggest drinkers he did try to
    limit his drinking when he saw what was happening to the others. Jack too aim and slobified Nathan. By this

    But he will be huge. I can't do that.

    GET ME THE PUNCH BOWL NOW!!!
