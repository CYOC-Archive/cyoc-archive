    Janet looked at her new, mature body in the mirror and saw that she was still wearing the amulet. She had to find out if she could change back. She closed her eyes and imagined herself as she was before. After about five seconds she opened her eyes. She saw in the reflection plain, ordinary 16-year-old Janet Wilson. All the strength and power she had felt inside her was gone.

    Janet took off the amulet and headed back to her bedroom. She placed it on a table next to her bed before changing into her nightgown. She turned off the light before going to bed, feeling slightly depressed. For a few minutes, she was a super-powered woman. Compared to that, being a normal teenager was mundane by comparison.

    The teenager closed her eyes and fell asleep. Several hours later she woke with a start. She looked at the alarm clock next to the bed. It showed 1:23 AM. She got out of bed and picked up the amulet and looked at it. A part of her wanted to transform back to her adult alter ego. Another part of her wanted to try some other superheroine. Or maybe, she thought, she should go back to bed and decide what to do in the morning.
