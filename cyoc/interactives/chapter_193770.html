    A Fellow New Jock

    "Ok follow me," Matt said as he led Theo to a storage closet. Now that he was back at his old school, Matt knew exactly where to go. Which included the knowledge of the secret spots students frequently visited when skipping. Matt jiggled the loose doorknob and opened the closet to find it empty. He breathed a sigh of relief as he let Theo enter the room before locking it behind them.

    "Alright put the jacket on now!" Matt said, a little eagerly if he had to be honest.

    "Fine! Fine! Just don't look at me if I start to transform..." Theo said as he placed the jacket over his shoulders.

    Theo stood still wearing the too large letterman jacket and waited before he felt a small sensation of heat overcoming his body. Was this part of the transformation? If it was he couldn't risk Matt seeing him.

    "Matt! I think it's working so, could you look away now?" Theo said as he could feel the warmth envelop his whole body. Matt frowned, now that he wasn't the one transforming he wanted to see the transformation from an outside perspective. But he did promise Theo he wouldn't look if it worked, so he turned around toward the wall.

    Theo let out a groan as he felt himself growing. His body began to grow as it reached a height of six foot even with his limbs growing proportionally. At his new height, he was only a few inches shorter than Matt. Then the more "interesting" changes began.

    Theo felt his body change as his muscles grew and developed at an exceedingly fast rate. His arms bulged as he began to gain muscle over his body, his legs widened, and his frame broadened. Theo's pecs expanded growing into two large plump but sturdy pecs. Unlike Matt, Theo was developing a different build matching his prior skinny-fat figure. Theo was more beefy than muscular in contrast to Matt; however, it meant he had more strength behind his new muscular build.

    Theo's hair remained the same but his face developed a small trimmed brown beard along with large bursts of body hair appearing over his arms, legs, chest and stomach. Theo's changes finished just as Matt asked if he could look.

    "Sure dude." Theo said in a baritone voice, much deeper than Matt's.

    Matt couldn't believe his eyes at the man he was staring at. Although he knew it was Theo, the changes had made him nearly indistinguishable from his old self, Was this how Theo felt when he saw Matt in his new form? Matt would describe Theo now as a body-builder or someone who rathered enjoyed working out considering his impressive muscular physique. Just like Matt, Theo's clothes had grown to fix his new body and apparently the letterman jacket shifted to fit Theo's beefier build.

    "Theo you should probably check your student id," Matt suggested remembering how insightful it would be into knowing how reality had shifted for Theo. Matt saw Theo place his hand into his pocket as he pulled out his wallet to see his id. Before quickly demonstrating it in front of Matt.

    Matt read the information on the card:
    Theodor Baker
    Senior
    Central Beach High

    It seemed that Theo had also become a senior which meant he was also now Matt's age so Theo would have to be 17-18 now. But just like Matt, Theo didn't seem to concerned at the age loss as he posed his new biceps. "This jacket is amazing! I'm super buff now almost like a body-builder." Theo said as he peered at his new muscles. Matt felt that Theo also did appear a bit different than how he would expect a surfer to look. He didn't have a tan and he also seemed to have too much body mass to surf easily. Was the jacket not only solely responsible for turning its wearers into beach bums?

    "Hey Theo what sports do you do?" Matt asked, beginning to realize the purpose of the jacket.

    "Well after you left, I decided to join the wrestling team." Theo said absentmindedly as he looked into his reflection from his phone's camera. Matt then realized the true purpose of the jacket! To transform them into the epitome of their sport. Theo looked like the perfect wrestler with his new body. Likewise, Matt felt he had the perfect figure to surf now.

    "I wonder how reality changed?" Matt said aloud as they both stepped out of the storage room. The bell rang as they closed the door and their classmates bombarded the halls as they raced to the doors. What should they do now?
