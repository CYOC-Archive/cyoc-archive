    After Mr. Hyun finally got his conversion, and delivered his report on why Animalians wouldn't make good super soldiers (based off of what was now personal experience) to Chairman Kim, he had to go to Claw Coliseum to begin his acclimation classes. Fortunately he wouldn't be the only one taking part, since Lunn, Mr. D.'s tenants, and Andrew and his son Jeff were also taking part in the most recent "class" of converted Animalians. What was also fortunate was the fact that, while Chairman Kim wasn't too happy about not getting any planned super soldiers, he gave him "ambassador" status, though even Mr. Hyun didn't know if that was a good thing. But there was a minor incident when Andrew and Jeff stepped on the bus to Claw Coliseum.

    "Man, why did I think this was a good Idea?" panted Andrew before stripping off his jacket, revealing that he had no shirt underneath his jacket. Part of made it surprising was not only the fact that he was shirtless, but that his new mane seemed to blend into his body hair.

    "Sir," said the bus driver, who impressed everyone with how calm he was about it, "Please go back into your home and get a real shirt or you'll end up with a fine for indecent exposure." Then there was a lot of laughing as Andrew sheepishly went back into his apartment and came out with a real t-shirt.

    ---

    Another new thing that Mr. Hyun learned was why it seemed that Animalians were staunch pacifists but loved competitive sports.

    "The reason for this," answered the bus driver when Mr. Hyun asked about this seeming paradox, "is that many animals, especially male ones (but sometimes females) had to compete with each other over matters of territory, mates, or even food. As Animalians, they can put on a bit of a show while doing what feels familiar to them while putting on a show. But here's something to help you feel better, Mr. Hyun: You don't have to take part in the UFC-A if you don't want to."

    "Good to know." said Mr. Hyun.

    ---

    After their first class, almost everyone was heading out. Andrew was staying because Aslan was teaching him about the duties a king had toward his pride, much to everyone else's amusement. But Mr. Hyun thought something strange was going on when he got back to his and Ling's apartment. The lights were on low, and sensual music was playing. When he got to his bedroom, he got the biggest shock in his life. Ling was laying on his bed with nothing but transparent lingerie that Mr. Hyun didn't know that Ling owned.

    "You proved yourself to me. Now let me reward you." said Ling.

    ---

    When Mr. Hyun woke the next day, he was surprised to see himself taller and more muscular than he was when he got his initial dose.

    "Now you're even better than before." said Ling, who was reaching over his shoulder, showing that she was somewhat more impressive than she was from her initial dose.

    "Uh..." said Mr. Hyun, who was still very surprised about his new, double-dosed form.

    "No, don't speak." said Ling, "For some moments in life, there are no words."
