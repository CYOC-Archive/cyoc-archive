    There was also another one covering Animalia School of Modeling. When not performing at the Meat Market, Camille Chameleon was able to get a successful career steering would be models away from drugs. Part of this was her own near death experience by heroin overdose at the IAC fashion show, but was also supplemented by her own moderate fear of needles. Dr. Jones, before his break was able to diagnose it as an associative fear of needles.

While doing it right was the primary purpose of the lesson, there were many who were more concerned about the physique of the models rather than the clothes themselves.

    Yet another was covering training sessions at the Golden Mountain Dojo, especially Liao's demonstration of her mastery of the urumi sword whip. While her English wasn't perfect yet, she was able to tell some of her story to how she became part of Animalia, and especially Tuxing's little sister.

    "I've heard that the people of Sri Lanka take wielding the urumi one step farther then they do in southern India." said one spectator as Liao began another demonstration 'drill'. "They introduce multiple blades attached to one handle, and even dual wielding."
