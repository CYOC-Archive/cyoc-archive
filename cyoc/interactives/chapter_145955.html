    Matt was a 35 year old man with a muscular build, a heart as big as his muscles, electic blue hair, blue eyes, and he wore a lime green shirt, blue sweatpants, and black shoes.

    One day, as he was walking through the park, almost immediately, he felt a sharp tingle. the tingle spread all over his body, from the top of his head to the tips of his fingers to the ends of his toes.

    "What was that?" he muttered.

    His clothes began to stretch and split, as he grew too large for them to contain.

    "This is amazing," he said aloud, not quite sure if he was having a bizarre dream or not. He chose to believe it was not a dream because the sensations he felt were quite intense.

    his shirt split in two, and his pants expanded bigger and bigger till they ripped clean off. his toes burst outta his shoes.
