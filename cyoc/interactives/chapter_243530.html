    Abducted By A Gillman

    added by Elflover 16 hours ago A BM TG O Magic Aquatic Male to female

    You had all you wanted to be happy. Everything was so blissful. And the mermaid life sounded just plain wonderful! So you were now female... So what? You felt super comfortable already and besides all other merpeople confirmed that you were a beauty. What could go wrong? With that sweet thought in mind, you fell asleep peacefully. Already you were forgetting about your old human life. Not that you were actively forgetting oit, just that your new life appeared a million times more interesting. So why bother with these pesky surface concerns? You clearly were better here.

    Or so you thought, because as you lied asleep, rocked by soothing little sea currents, you suddenly woke up, startled in your subconscious by a strange presence. You jumped up, startled. A strange presence it was indeed! It was a... a gillman! Complete with greenish blue scales, tiny tail and fish-like face. You assumed for a few seconds that this was normal but that hope vanished soon. But... a gillman here would not be unexpected, right? Yet the creature deftly dashed towards you and it clamped its large webbed hand on your mouth. With its great strength - more than you'd ever expect - he grabbed you and escaped. There was no merman or mermaid about so in no time the city vanished in the distance. You struggled fruitlessly. You tried to get out of its clutches. What did this monster want with you? But the lust in its eyes made the answer quite clear...
