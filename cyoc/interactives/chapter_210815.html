    The human & his Furry Animated Plush Fox

    added by Raven Cross 9 months ago A I Anthro Canine

    Alice was at the grocery store with her 4 ft Furry Animated Plush Fox Red. It was named Red because it was an anthro red fox plush. Alice had bought this plush from a toy store 3 years ago. She loves it dearly & knows it loves her the same. "Owner. I'm bored." Red complained. "We'll leaving soon Red. So have patience." Alice told the plush fox like you would tell a kid. Mentally the plush fox was a kid. "But I want to go now." Red demanded stomping it's footpaw. "Soon." Alice told the plush fox. "But I want to go now." Red demanded being really bratty now. Alice now noticed people were looking at them & both heard people talking. They both could hear them wondering why Alice was putting up it's defiance. Realizing that it didn't appear normal to be talking to her Furry Animated Plush Fox like a human child she quickly bent down & picked up Red. "Do what I say & shut up plush. We will go when I'm ready." You said forceful & superior to it. Red appeared shocked Alice had spoke to it like that sense Alice didn't normally do that. Alice told it making it clear "You are my property Red. Please remember what you are." Alice put the fox plush back down. Red didn't say a word again. After they got in the car Alice spoke to Red again. "I'm not trying to be mean Red but you got to remember you are a Furry Animated Plush Fox. You are not a person. You are property. My property. I bought you." Alice told the plush fox. "I know that." Red stated knowing that. "You are not a human child. You are a Furry Animated Plush Fox." Alice stated the obvious. "I know." Red said agreeing. "Then stop acting like a human child brat." Alice ordered. Red looked at it's fox plush body & ran a handpaw through some of its fake fur on it's chest. "I did not birth you. I bought you. I could sell you to someone whenever I want." Alice informed the plush fox starting up the car. Red looked at it's owner & vowed putting a handpaw to it's chest "I will from now on be a perfect plush." "Good good." Alice said pleased. Sense Alice has had Red she learned that once this plush fox made a promise it meant to keep it. "Where we going next owner?" Red asked curious. "The hardware store. I need a plunger." Alice answered. "Do I need to come in with you this time?" Red asked curious. Alice thought about it & said "No. I'll leave you in the car." The plush fox said to Alice "I'll take a nap for the rest of the trip if that's ok." "Sure Red." Alice told it fine with that. The plush fox went limp & fell over a bit lending now on the car door. Alice looked over at the Furry Animated Plush Fox & just was still in awe that that thing 4 years ago was a human. Like everyone else she couldn't figure out how a virus (or anything really) was capable of transmuting an organic being into an object. The virus had completely transmuted Red into his current walking, talking, sentient anthro plush fox form. It like was like something from a work of fiction; but it is real. The Furry Animated Plushes are real. 10 years ago the Furry Plush Virus arrived. There is no cure for it. Nothing that can be done about it & no way to stop it. It isn't going away any time soon. Soon Alice got out of the car & locked the doors. She quickly noticed 2 figures. One was a man & the other was appeared to be someone nearly transformed into a Furry Animated Plush Raccoon. The man was crouched down in front of the other figure who was sitting the ground legs out stretched. "Let me help you get those clothes off honey." The man told the nearly turned Furry Animated Plush Raccoon as he helped it remove it's pants & underwear. Soon the figure had fully transformed into a Furry Animated Plush Raccoon & was completely naked from the waist down. The man helped the newly transformed Furry Animated Plush Raccoon to it's footpaws & then helped it remove it's last 2 articles of clothing; it's shirt & bra. Now naked (as all Furry Animated Plushes are) Alice could see the mental changes happening. All Furry Animated Plushes remember only being Furry Animated Plushes. "Be...owner...Brad." it (the raccoon plush) begged of the man almost looking like it may cry. The man looking like he may cry too put a hand to the raccoon plush's cheek & said with a smile "Silly plush. I am your owner. Remember?" The raccoon plush nodded & replied as if remembering that "Silly me. You do own me." The 2 then went off together abandoning the raccoon plush's clothing & belongings. [That raccoon plush must of been his wife.] Alice thought figuring that. It made Alice a bit sad that that happened to them but at least they are going to remain together as owner & property.
    The Furry Plush Virus can be so cruel sometimes.
