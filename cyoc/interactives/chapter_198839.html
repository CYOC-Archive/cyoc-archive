    Amy turned her head 90 degrees to look directly at me. Her mouth smiled so wide that it looked like she was a demon.

    No, she had to be. Her teeth seemed sharp, her eyes looked like they were growing, her face seemed to inhuman. Who was she?

    She started to hover slightly in the air. My heart was beating faster than it ever has before. She started to slowly hover towards me, before accelerating faster than I could blink. Suddenly her face was right in front of mine, then it disappeared.

    I fell backwards, sweating uncontrollably. I turned around and bolted, trying to get as far away from her as possible.

    As I was running, I could still hear her giggling. As inhuman as it sounded, I could still tell that it was her voice. The same type of voice she would make when she played a practical joke on me.

    I stopped and tried to catch my breath. There was no way I could keep running.

    “Alright Amy, you win. You’ve had your fun. Can you please stop and let me know what’s going on!” I said, terrified, trying to treat this as if it was one of her pranks.

    “Hahaha, did you see the look on your face?! Priceless!” Amy laughed.

    Suddenly, she teleported right in front of me, looking like her normal self.

    “How the… how are you doing this?” I asked. “More importantly, where are we? How can we get out?”

    She smiled. “It’s my realm. A world made just for me. Do you like it? I can go shopping anytime I want, and I don’t have to pay!” Amy shouted.

    “A world for just you? Great, can you get us out?” I asked.

    “I could. But… you do realize something right?” She asked. “You do realize that I’m basically a goddess here. I have infinite power, I can do anything I want. I have no limits…”

    I stood still, unsure what to ask next. I had to choose my words carefully; becoming as powerful as a god could change how someone thinks. She might abuse this power.
