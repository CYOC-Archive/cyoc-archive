    Gloria and Lynne try to prove themselves

    “Hey dad where are you,” Kellie yelled as she entered her home. Kellie was at a loss, she hadn’t been expecting a visit from her nieces, let alone one from the future that claimed to have been taught spells by a future version of herself. Trying to prove this to be true would prove to be something she could not handle herself, hopefully Merlin could provide some better incite. Looking back at Gloria and Lynne she wondered what their plans could be.

    Kellie could tell that her abilities were superior to Gloria’s, while the girl did have some very powerful magic within her, she was only now beginning to come into her power. Lynne though could prove problematic. Kellie could sense an incredible amount of magic within her. The girls power could very well be greater than Kellies despite her age. The only advantage Kellie had was her training, if this was a trick she would need to rely on her experience to save her friends. Glancing worriedly at her friends she hoped that this wasn’t a trick, the last thing she wanted to see was any of them hurt by this mystery girl.

    As the five teens looked around Kellies home they could hear movement in Merlin’s study, turning they noticed the elderly wizard exit the room, a small pink box in his hand.

    “Kellie,” he said kindly. “How was your day?”

    “Quiet up to a few minutes ago,” she sighed. “These two showed up on our front porch, they claim they’re my nieces.” Kellie paused as she gazed at the package in Merlin’s hand. “So..um.. what’s with the package.”

    Merlin looked down at the package a small frown appearing on his face. “This,” he said sternly. “Is a little curse known as a Bimbo box. It appears we’ve angered Morgan, she appears to have quite a grudge against your family. I just confiscated this from the other timeline, she was going after Ann and her friend.”

    Gloria paled as she took this information in. “Are Ann and Jackie Okay?”

    “Yes they are fine,” Merlin stated. “I was able to intervene before anything could happen, hopefully she enjoys the rest of her evening with her friend.”

    “Um what the hell is a Bimbo Box,” Lisa asked.

    “Quite simply it increases ones libido while decreasing the individuals intelligence,” Merlin stated.

    “Huh,”

    “It makes you horny and stupid Lisa,” Kellie sighed to which Lisa let out a simple oh.

    Looking worriedly at Merlin, Gloria and the others Kellie tapped her chin thoughtfully. “Crap didn’t realize she was getting that bold,” she said. “What is she after anyway?”

    “I think I can answer that,” Lynne said stepping forward. The others now turned as the auburn haired teen looked anxiously at them. “She umm...she has always wanted to make me her apprentice,” she said. “Apparently she thinks I would make a really good apprentice, someone that could cause a lot of chaos throughout the multiverse.”

    “I think you already do that now,” Gloria muttered, causing her sister to frown at her. “Seriously,” Lynne whined. “Your gonna start with that again, right when we’re starting to get along.”

    “Girls,” Katie said quickly before the two could break into a fight. “Can we save the sister fight for later. We still need to confirm that you two are who you say you are? Merlin, can you help us out with this?”

    “That could prove difficult,” Merlin stated. “Unfortunately there are no blood relations present to compare these two too. Even testing of the auras would be difficult seeing as we have no one we can compare them too. If we had someone from the other timeline than perhaps, however, useing those who are available right now would be fruitless.”

    The teens now anxiously looked at one another, with Lynne and Gloria unable to prove they were who they said they were would make moving forward difficult. “Can I say something,” Gloria asked tentatively. The others nodded as the girl stepped forward. “Lisa you say you know the Gloria of this timeline. What if I show you that I’m her doppelgänger, would you believe me then?”

    Lisa nodded, still unsure of where the girl was going with this. “I suppose,” she said, slightly unsure.

    “Okay I’ll prove it then,” Gloria stated, as she held up the Ring of Age.

    The others watched as Gloria’s ring began to glow. Slowly but surely they’re atched as the 14 year old girl began to age backwards. Gloria’s coltish body raced back through puberty as she grew shorter, in only moments the blossoming 14 year girl became an adorable 10 year old child with long flowing blond hair. Lisa’s eyes went wide as she stared at the girl.

    “Wow you weren’t lying,” she exclaimed. “Your the spitting image of Jason’s sister.”

    Gloria smiled as Lynne smirked impishly down at her sister. “What,” Gloria groaned impatiently.

    “Nothing,” Lynne smirked. “Just like you better when your younger and shorter than me. You just too damn adorable”

    “Quit being an ass Lynne,” Gloria grumbled. “It was the only thing I could think of.”

    As Kellie and the others gazed at the young girl and her teenage sister they now began to consider whether what the girls were saying was indeed true and where they would go from here.
