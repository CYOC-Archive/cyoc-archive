So, to compensate for that, Jeff had always fantasized about a world where every male human looked physically female except for the thing between their legs. Breasts, huge ass and long hair would all become staples of being a man just like they are for women.

    "Let's see how to do this." Jeff mumbled to himself.

    Opening up a tab on the chronivac, Jeff found a display that had two models on it. One of the models was described as 'average female human' and the other showed 'average male human'. Each of the models also had things like average height, average weight and many other 'averages' on it. Jeff understood well how to use this for his world changing transformation.

    Going over to the model for the average female, jeff copied also all the average traits of the female, to the male model. The only things he didn't copy were the averages related to the vagina and womb, but everything else was copied. Even stuff like the average milk production during pregnancy was copied over. However, when it went over to the male, instead of being an average for milk production during pregnancy, it became average milk production during day to day life.

    "I suppose men will now just lactate now."

    Once all of the desired traits were copied over, Jeff decided to alter some of the averages for both men and women. Deciding it would be funny, Jeff altered the average female height and weight to be what it used to be for men, while for men he wanted to make 'shemales' have on average G cup breasts, unlike the average C cup for women. Besides those last few things, he kept everything else as is from fashion to stereotypes. If need be, he could always alter things more to his liking.

    Now all that was done, Jeff hit enter on the transformation and waited for it to happen.
