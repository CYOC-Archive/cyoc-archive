    John's eyes open, he looked around the room, wondering what's going on, it seems like the Hypnosis didn't work. He looked over to the hypnotist, who has a large grin on his face. He attempted to explain that the hypnosis didn't work. "Rwor!" John growled. What was that? "There's a reason why I'm considered the master of Hypnosis, not only am I a master of the mind, I am also the master of the body!" Mesmero gloated. John Growled again, wanting to be turned back.

    "Be a good tiger now, Shasta."

    John's mind clicked just like that. Now he was Shasta.

    "Good kitty!" Mesmero pet Shasta, earning a chuff from the big cat. The audience cheered! This was fantastic! John's little girl ran on stage and pet Shasta, earning more Chuffs from the tiger. "He's now Your father again." Shasta was turned off. John was back. He freaked out, he's never heard of Hypnosis like this! "Go sit down, I have one last trick for the audience!" Mesmero demanded, making John's kid sit down. "Now, focus on me everyone." John wondered what was going to happen now. "Focus on what life was like before you saw this." The hypnotist grinned. "You've never seen this, as far as your concerned, you've never met John, he was not the father of two kids." John jumped, what was he trying to do? "Repeat what I said." "We don't know who John is." The audience repeated. "Good boys and girls! Now I do believe we are out of time. Thank you for all this!"

    The audience left, leaving only Mesmero and John. "Tiger." He commanded, Shasta was back. "Go into your cage." Shasta was back within his cage. "Father." John was back. "You're probably wondering John, what's going on, right? Well I'll tell you, we get our animals from this little performance I do, I'm the brother of the man who owns the circus, I was gifted from a young age, I know how to manipulate the mind so well that the body believes it needs to change." He explained. "You're our newest tiger, for our tiger exhibit."
