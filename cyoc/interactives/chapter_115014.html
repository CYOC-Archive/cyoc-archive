    Josh was just your average 12 year old high school kid but that all changed on day when he found a certain spell.

    Josh had to walk to school everyday and he always walked past this magic shop. He never thought much of it though because he though that magic was a load of rubbish. One day on the way home from school, he decided that he would go and check out the shop anyway, just to confirm that magic isn't real.

    The door opened and Josh stepped inside that shop. There was only two or three people in the shop apart from the woman at the register and it was very quiet. Josh walked up to one of the shelf and started seeing things like: the sword of trapped souls, a super strength crystal kept in a locked box, a cape that allows one to fly and much more. So far, nothing caught Josh's eye until he got to the spells section.

    There was all sorts of weird and wonderful spells but the only one that stood out to Josh was the body stealing spell. Sadly this was also in a transparent locked box so Josh asked the woman at the register to get the spell out and she obliged. Once the woman handed the spell over to Josh, he looked at the instructions.

    It said:

    To activate this spell all you need to do is say...................................And everything around you will freeze. Once this happens, look for that body you want to take over and push yourself inside that body and you will become one with the other person. It won't be easy to gain full control over your new body at first though because the previous owners soul will put up a fight but all you need to do is push yourself deeper until all they can do is give up their control to you. Once this is done, the over soul will have no power over the body anymore and will just sit in the subcontious, powerless, until you leave the body for another one. Once you have full control over the body, you will also be able to access the previous owners memories. To leave the body for another one, all you have to do is say the activation for the spell again and your back to step one.
    WARNING, Once you leave your original body, it will cease to exist and reality will be alter so that nobody remembers you exept for other master magic users.

    After reading the description, Josh was even more intrigued than before but that activation code was missing so he asked the woman about it and she said that he needed to buy the spell to learn the code. Josh asked how much the spell was and he was supervised when the woman said that it was only $6 so Josh bought it with the last of his pocket money.
