    Leah wakes up to a full feeling in her bladder. Odd, given that her diaper is soaked. Before Leah can do anything but notice her full bladder, it promptly lets loose, soaking her diaper even more. Leah shudders at the warmth spreading through her diaper, but she’s so used to it at this point, she doesn’t care. She also doesn’t care that she got so little warning before she peed; that’s just how things seem to be going when she holds it in for too long.

    Amber enters the nursery room, an evil smile on her face. She picks Leah up.

    “Ooh, Miss Teen Baby went wetties in her diapy,” Amber says in a baby voice. Leah blushes. “Don’t worry, Mommy Amber will take care of Miss Teen Baby.” Amber brings Leah to a changing table and changes her diaper. “Good thing you’re in the extra thick diapers. Otherwise you’d have leaked.” Leah blushes even more. “Come on, time for breakfast.”

    Amber sets Leah on the floor and leaves the nursery. Leah gets to her feet and takes a few fumbling steps. Walking is difficult, but she shrugs it off as being due to her incredibly thick diaper. She has no choice but to toddle around. By the time she gets to the living room where her playpen is, she’s already felt some pee entering her fresh diaper. Weird. Why is she peeing accidentally when she doesn’t even need to go?

    Amber sets Leah up in her playpen again, giving her a baby bottle full of formula and a small container of cheerios for breakfast. As Leah watches the baby shows Amber insists on putting on for her, she continues to occasionally pee her diaper. Gradually, she realizes that it’s not just that she can’t control her bladder when asleep. Now, she’s regressed so much that she can’t control her bladder while awake! She really does need diapers!

    Leah knows she should be horrified by this discovery. But for some reason, she’s fine with it? Leah finishes her breakfast. Amber takes her bottle away and gives her a pacifier. Leah suckles happily on it as she continues to watch her new favorite shows and use her diaper.

    Amber barely keeps herself from laughing maniacally as she watches Leah. Her plan is working! She’s really turning Leah into a baby! And Leah is fine with it! She’ll put Leah down for a nap in a few hours, and during that time, she’ll play the next video, which will make Leah…
