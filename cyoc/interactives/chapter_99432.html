    As Oliver was running a long, he ran to one of his favorite trails. It was covered with trees and was beautiful during the summer. He loved feeling the wind on his face while he ran. As he was running and jamming out to "Dead or Alive" by Bon Jovi, he noticed something in the bushes ahead. Has he got closer he slowed down. He noticed a small-ish black box lying in the bushes. He ran past it. He thought about it.

    *What was in that? Should I go check it out? Did someone get rid of it on purpose? WHAT WAS IT!?* Eventually he turned back to check it out. He bent down and picked it up. Inside was a very slick and modern silver ring. Oliver didn't have anything like that and really liked it. He thought, *Wow, now I know how Gollum feels about this stuff.* Underneath it there was a note. The note read:

    "To whomever finds this ring, it belongs to you- do not worry! It has magical powers beyond your dreams. When worn, this ring can transform the wearer. There are a few different things it can do. If you wear it and think about someone you know, you can become a copy of them. If you wear an article of their clothing you will *become* them, and they will become you, without the ring and they won't know it. If you wear an article of clothing that is bigger or smaller you can change size to fit those clothes. You can wear clothes of the other gender and become that gender. Sometimes the ring will even learn new tricks! Good luck, have fun, and be careful!"

    Oliver stared at the note in disbelief. This was a dream come true. He was never truly happy being who he was and now he had the opportunity to completely change his life and become whoever he wanted... he could try things out and find out what he liked until he found the right life to slip into. He slipped the ring on his finger- it was a perfect fit- and continued on his run thinking about what he would do first.
