    Anyone familiar with the internet (and since you would have to have
    lived under a rock to not be) will know that there is no shortage of
    ads promoting products able to reshape and alter ones' body. Phony
    diet pills, cheap exercise equipment, and a plethora of products
    promising an increase of 1 to 3 inches are everywhere. To date, none
    of them have proven to be worth the digital paper they are printed on.
    However, all is not lost. For the truly discerning customer, there is
    one product that delivers exactly as promised. Hidden in the deepest,
    darkest parts of the internet is an ad that reads as follows:

    NEW! From Spells R' Us and Industrial Body Magic, the Complete
    Transformation Package! The only deal out there guaranteed to change
    your life forever! For the first time in one place, Spell R' Us' Ring
    of Three, Watch of Wonder, Medallion of Zullo, and Amorous Amulets
    combined with IBM's Universal Remote, Everlasting Transformation Test
    Tube, and Master PC. Now available for the low price of $19.95 plus
    shipping and handling (normally a $100 value). Order today and receive
    a free Gender Ring. Offer not available in Canada.

    If you were to click on the link that said "details", you might find a
    set instructions that looked a little like this:

    The Everlasting Transformation Test Tube
    The Everlasting Transformation Test Tube changes the user differently
    depending on its color. The colors and their effects are as follows:
    Blue- Transgender
    Red- Age progression 5 years
    Yellow- Age regression 5 years
    Green- Anthro. Will change you to an anthro form of the first animal
    you speak or hear
    Pink- Beautification
    Purple- Height growth 6 inches
    Black- Height shrinkage 6 inches
    White- Cancellation. Will revert you back to normal.
    Orange- Weight increase of 30 pounds
    Brown- Weight decrease of 30 pounds
    White with Black dots- Random change
    Drink the entire tube or serum may be ineffective. Vial automatically
    refills after each use.

    Ring of Three
    The ring of three allows the wearer to make any change they desire,
    mental or physical, to the target person using a three word
    description. To use, place the ring on your right index finger, rub
    the stone three times, point your finger at the target, then say the
    three word command. Target will be forced to comply with the command,
    be it a transformation, action, or alteration. Like all Spells R' Us
    products, the Ring of Three is equipped with our patented reality
    alteration spell. After a short period of time, reality will
    automatically adjust so that none but the user remember the changes
    made using the Ring of Three. This is done for the users protection.
    Warning: Frequent use of the ring may temporarily overtax the spell,
    leaving the wearer exposed to possible discovery and repercussions.

    The Wonder Watch
    The Wonder Watch is meant to allow its user to precisely control the
    world around them through advanced hypnosis. Used in the same manner
    as any other hypnosis aid, the user need only hypnotize any person to
    gain complete control over both mind and body. To do so simply wave
    the watch in front of the desired targets eyes to induce a trance like
    state. After, that, exerting control over the targets mind and body is
    simple. Just hypnotize them into repeating a hypnotic suggestion about
    themselves. The statement should be in the first person i.e. "I am
    ...". As soon as the words are said, the changes will begin. Be warned
    that hypnotizing more than one person at a time is not recommended.
    Also, unless specifically ordered not to, the target will remember
    both the changes made and who caused them for a short period of time
    until the reality altering protection spells placed on all Spells R'
    Us products have time to take effect. This can of course be very
    inconvenient, so it is recommended that you plan your hypnotic
    suggestions wisely.

    The Universal Remote
    The Universal Remote works in much the same way as any remote, except
    that in this case, universal means universal. The Universal Remote
    grants the user complete and precise control over the form of any
    living thing. Simply capture the profile of the desired target by
    pointing the remote and pressing "Select" then use the screen
    interface and buttons to alter the target profile. Changes will occur
    once "Apply" is pressed. Alternatively, changes can be made without
    profile capture by pointing the remote at target and pressing the
    various preset change buttons. However, this is not recommended as
    record of changes will not be stored in memory making reversal
    potentially difficult.
    Warning: Universal Remote provides control mainly over physical
    characteristics of target. Mental control over target through use of
    the remote is somewhat limited. Plan accordingly.

    The Amorous Amulets
    The Amorous Amulets are a matched pair of necklaces meant to bring you
    and your loved one(s) closer together in a manner never before
    possible. To use, simply place the necklace around the neck of your
    partner. This will link their body to your mind. After this, making
    your desires come true is simply a matter of will. Concentrate on the
    image of what you wish your partner to become and their body will
    reshape into your fantasy.
    Note: The Amorous Amulets allow for physical changes only. Mental
    changes may be side effects of use, but the amulets cannot be used to
    make specific mental changes.

    The Master PC
    The Master PC software is designed to give the user complete and
    unlimited control over reality. Simply install on any computer,
    desktop or laptop, and the user interface will appear. Type any
    statement or command into the interface and click "Execute" or hit the
    "enter" key and watch as your commands become reality. Make each
    command as specific as possible. The program will try to interpret
    each command as best it can, but mistakes are possible, if not likely,
    with vague or incorrectly entered text. The Master PC can be installed
    on any number of computers, but be warned the software comes with no
    built in safeguards to prevent unauthorized use.

    The Medallion of Zulo
    The Medallion of Zullo allows the user to take on the appearance of
    anyone he or she desires. In order to take on the appearance of a
    specific person, place the Medallion around ones neck, then touch an
    article of clothing belonging to the person to the Medallion. The body
    wearer will then transform until it is an exact duplicate of the
    person to whom the article of clothing belongs. The transformation
    process takes approximately one half hour, however the process may be
    sped up by prolonged contact with the article of clothing.
    Alternatively, an article of clothing is used that has not been worn
    by a specific person before, the wearer of the Medallion will be
    transformed into someone who would fit, both in terms of physical
    dimensions and personality, the article of clothing in question. After
    the transformation is complete, at least twelve hours must elapse
    before the Medallion can be used to return the user to their original
    form or take on a new shape. It is prudent to remember this at all
    times.

    Gender Ring
    As its name suggests the Gender Ring allows its wearer complete
    control over their own gender, as well as that of those around them.
    Now one may ask what is so special about an object that controls only
    gender in a collection of much more versatile artifacts. The answer
    lies in the unique way in which this control is exercised. Once the
    ring is put on, the users body enters a state of flux. During this
    period, the wearer possess the sexual organs of both male and female.
    In all other ways, the wearer will remain unchanged until the first
    time the wearer is sexually stimulated. At that point, the users
    appearance will change according to the nature of the stimulation.
    That is, the user will become progressively more male or female
    depending on which set of organs is involved. Removing the ring will
    not restore the users original appearance, but does allow for multiple
    simultaneous users. The period of flux ends after 24 hours at which
    time the user will be rendered completely male or female depending on
    which gender the user most resembles at the time. The ring can be used
    as many times as desired.
