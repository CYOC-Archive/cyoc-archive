    A Man's Best Friend is his Dog.

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Bear 5 years ago A AR S O Mental

    "I'd like you to..." Kyle thought about it for a moment. What was something about Ally that annoyed him so much? Finally it clicked. "A dog, she would never let me have a dog!" Kyle thought. Ally had "allergies" that prevented their mother from getting one, when in reality she was annoyed by dog hair. This was his chance to get some much needed payback. He wanted a dog, and in some way or another, he was going to get one.

    "I'd like you to tell me your feelings about dogs" Kyle softly said to the hypnotised Ally.
    "I don't like dogs. Their fur gets everywhere, even in the air, and it annoys me. That's why I prevent Mom to get one" Ally said in a monotone voice.
    "I want you to forget your current feelings about dogs. You like them, you love them, and their fur doesn't bother you" Kyle said.
    "I love dogs, I love their fur, they are so cute" Ally replied.
    "Ally, what should our family dog be like?"
    "Obedient...friendly..."
    "Exactly. You're a good girl, Ally. You are obedient and friendly"
    "I'm a good girl. Obedient and friendly"
    "Yes, good girl. Good dog"
    "Yes. I am a good dog"
    "Yes. A goo-AAAH!"

    Kyle started to see a small tail form behind Ally, and fur started to grow on her arms. Kyle panicked.
    "Wait, wait, you're still human. All human"
    The fur reseeded back into the skin and her tail reshaped back into her spine.
    That's when Kyle had another idea. Why not make her half-human? He was a furry after all, so why not? He had always been teased by her, since his birth even.
    "Ally, you are obedient and friendly..."
    "Obedient and friendly"
    "Yes, you are a good girl"
    "I am a good girl"
    Kyle started to see the fur slowly coming back.
    "Ally, you are a good 15 year old girl, aren't you?"
    Ally shrunk slightly, as her age changed to become a 15 year old.
    "Yes, I'm a good 15 year old girl" Ally said, in a high pitched voice.
    "Yes...you also are obedient and friendly...like a dog"
    "Yes, I am obedient and friendly like a dog"
    The tail was starting to grow back.
    "Ally, you have a tail like a dog. What do obedient and friendly dogs do with their tail?"
    "They wag them. Like a good dog. I am like a dog"
    "Ally, what covers these good dogs?"
    "Fur covers them. I'm like a good dog"
    "Ally, you are a good dog girl"
    "I am a good dog girl"
    The fur grew even more as she finished that statement, covering her in golden retriever fur. Her tail grew to dog-sized length, almost continuing to grow as she was still under hypnosis. Her nose turned black and wet, her teeth sharpened and became more canine in shape, her ears stretched and were placed on top of her head before they drooped down like a happy dog. Her tongue lengthened until it couldn't fit in her mouth, leaving her to pant as the fur continued to grow and grow. Her hands reshaped themselves into paw-like hands and her legs reshaped to make them more dog-like. She could still stand up like a normal human being. Finally her jaw stretched to complete the look of a dog.
    "Ally, remind yourself. Who are you?"
    "I'm a good dog girl, obedient and friendly, I love your family for I am your adopted sister and pet".
    "That's right...good girl...what else did I say?"
    "I will forget that you have hypnotised me"
    "Correct...when I snap my fingers, you will become a good dog girl".
    Kyle snapped his fingers. Immediately, Ally reacted in a dog-like fashion, by hugging Kyle to the ground and licking him with love. "Hi, brother! I'm so glad you're home! We are going to have so much FUN!" Ally said with so much passion.
    "Okay, okay, just got off of me, alright!" Kyle said. Ally immediately followed Kyle's orders and sat next to him, like a dog, slightly rocking back and forth with excitement. Kyle was happy that this worked out. He finally had a dog, and his sister was a lot kinder. Things were great for him...

    ...until he saw his mother arrive.
