    The Reverend Church, disguised as a demon, thanks to the magic’s of Beelzebub woke from his slumber in a less then austere room with a splitting headache. Heading to the nearby table with a pitcher of water and bowl; tries to get rid of the pain with a splash of water. Less then thrilled at being given a room that had no washroom, the demon that work for Beelzebub could only chuckle as the demon told the Reverend “Damnation without relief. Just like in your bible.”

    With the water not working, the reverend heads back to the bed and tries to pray to god for salvation. All he got in return was another needle like jab to his head. He shot up in pain, doing his best not to scream. It is at that moment when a voice in his head finally appears.

    “It’s about time I reached you,” The voice booming in the Revernd’s mind. “Don’t worry about others hearing us, “The voice continuing. “Only you can hear me And just think of your responses, we are communicating telepathically.”
    “Who,” Reverend whispering as the pain in his head refuses to abate.
    “Kusheil”, the voice sharply responding. “I was able to locate you and the rest of the Iron Knights thanks to the blessed blades you all have on you.”
    “My prayers have been answered,” The reverend tearing up. “You came to deliver me to the glory of god!”
    “Hardly,” Kusheil scoffs; “you have yet to kill a demon.”
    “Easier said than done,” Revernd Church snaps back. Beelzebub has us all separated and running around like headless chickens.”
    “Not my problem,” Kusheil dismissing Church’s excuse, “Cromwell the milquetoast of your group killed three. That Muslim Bashir managed to maim one from what I heard.”
    “Bashir attacked my daughter turned into demon,” the reverend providing a brief synopsis of their first few hours in hell. “But from what I heard from questionable sources; an angel claiming to be the daughter of god managed to heal her.”
    “Penemue,” Kusheil chuckles, “your son’s fiancé; the one who you slapped on earth. Jehovah is not happy about that.”
    Revend Church was about to respond when another headache brought him to his knees as Kushneil continues to speak; “Who FYI is up here in heaven. A location currently denied for you. And will be if you don’t kill some demons and kick off another holy war.”
    “But Beelzebub,” Revernd Church tried to continue before he was cut off again.
    “Beezlebub is fair game as far as I’m concerned,” Kusheil snaps back. Not caring what happens to the human in hell. “If the opportunity presents its self, kill him. No more delays,” Kusheil orders; “I want the peace between Heaven and Hell to end before this Triple wedding in Purgatory happens. Make it happen!”
    That final statement of Kusheil hitting the reverend with such force that he briefly passes out from the pain. But with the sun for hell shining through the dingy window in the cell; Reverend Church struggles to get up. Knowing that he is caught between a rock and hard place; he ponders what his next move will be. Listen to Kushiel and kill Beelzebub, or bide his time as the foul demon commands.

    “I need to discuss this with the others,” the reverend struggling to get up. Just as he was standing the door to his temporary quarters open.
