Shaking his pudgy middle, Aaron nodded to himself as he retreated and planned on doing some laundry for the sake of not looking like a total slob at the gym for his "orientation" as he settled in on his decision for the day. Mallory didn't rule his life, not anymore, anyway. Today he was a new man, he was a new Aaron. Today, he was "Aaron Sullison" and he was going to be the gym fanatic these weird obsessive gym goers would fawn over every sweat slicked second, even if it killed him.

    The thought died down as he pulled himself out the house and began to feel slightly self conscious as he jogged to the gym's location. It wouldn't be too far, claimed it was a little closer to the city side, but nothing a good brisk run wouldn't beat, he needed the cardio as he huffed in stride. A neighbor waved overhead nearby, yelling out to him that he dug his fashion sense. Aaron blushed, attempting to hide his anxiety, but tried to take the compliment in stride, another neighbor, an older woman that had seemed kindly greeted him in kind, with a bold "Keep it up, big boy! Momma loves a man with some sweat!" To which Aaron caught himself from stumbling, quickly stopping at the end of the block breathing hard as he felt himself seize up as he gave himself the once over. He didn't feel so out of shape as before, but the comments, it wasn't exactly expected for a man at 47, not that he really minded but he was a married-

    It was fine. Needed, even. He was a new man. He was "Sullison". This was totally expected! But try as he might, he couldn't shake the feeling that something was off and quickly set aside his feelings, opting to take his phone out of his shorts and check his steps. 1,589 steps and counting, the gym not being too far away as the locale had changed from the modest homes to the more modernized suburban neighborhoods and gentrified restaurants. If he continued his pace, he would find the gym in no time!

He chuckled, muscular arms crossed over his chest in a well fitted purple shirt with gold lettering and an emblazoned logo of a bodybuilder flexing a lat flare in a white circle.
