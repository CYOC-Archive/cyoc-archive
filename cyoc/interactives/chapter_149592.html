    Several Dintopian couples had gotten married by now after Project Mesozoic became available for the public. But some Dinotopians wanted to wait until Dinosaur City was open first. It wasn't just about starting new lives there, but because the buildings were more in scale to suit them. Probably the most notable couple to be getting married in Dinosaur City were Tyra Wrex and Rex Ramos. Rex had proposed to Tyra after an extensive examination of Syntixisaurus rex DNA contained enough genes from Tyrannosaurus rex to be considered a subspecies. Though getting the wedding set up took a little longer than just waiting for the city to be opened. Heather and Jeremy had been delayed in moving to Dinosaur City, but now they were here, they could finally get started.

    Being married in the middle of July instead of in June was mildly inconvenient. It was rather hot this day, though the ceremony was inside Dinosaur City Hall. Though on the other hand, there was a healthy distance between Heather's anniversary and what will soon be Tyra's. At least the city hall had plenty of room for all the guests, which didn't just include Dinotopians. There were quite a few guests from Heather's wedding, such as the White family, Dr. Stein, and Countess Irena. Tyra had also invited several of the people she worked with at the Meat Market. Of course, they were all tastefully dressed. Tyra may be a shameless flaunt, but even she wouldn't her own wedding to be too lewd.

    Everyone watched as Tyra came up the alley. Heather's wedding dress fit her well after a few adjustments. She walked along as there wasn't really anyone to act as the 'Father of the Bride' and Tyra assured everyone she was more than old enough to give herself away. Heather was just glad that Tyra hadn't altered the dress to expose even more cleavage or leave any other skin uncovered. Tyra had explained her lack of modification in that regard. "This is matrimony, we're talkin' about, not just a fancy date," said Tyra, "Me and Rex are enterin' a sacred commitment that means more than just sex. This is a pact of love and I love that Rex Ramos just as much as I loved your grandpappy. To be honest, I love Rex a little more because I was hitched with your grandpappy mostly cause someone would have me and support me."

    As Tyra stood at the altar with Rex, she thought of how things were so different from her first wedding. Her first wedding had been at a little chapel that only her kin and groom's kin attended, along with a few friends. She hadn't been a particularly pretty bride, which was part of the reason why she was marrying more for comfort than love. Tyra wondered what those people at Effie Wilcox's wedding would think when they looked at Tyra Wrex's wedding. In all likelihood, most of them would throw a fit.

    But now she had just become Mrs. Ramos and this was the one of the happiest days of her life. The wedding reception was pretty big, considering how many Dinotopians there were to feed. And she got to have a pleasant chat with her granddaughter afterwards. "So, I guess now that you're married, you'll be settling down now," said Heather, "No more wild dancing at clubs now."

    "Oh, I'm hardly just gonna stay at home and mind the eggs," said Tyra, "And believe me, there will be eggs come next breedin' season."

    "I suppose I should expect that," said Heather. She had a feeling she would making her own eggs next breeding season too. "But you can't really be a mother while working as an exotic dancer," said Heather.

    "Well, we'll just see how my athletic career takes off," said Tyra, "I gotta finish off those games before me and Rex go on our honeymoon."

    "So which ones are you in again?" asked Heather, "I remember Track and Field, the Hunter Games..."

    "Don't forget the bodybuildin' competition," said Tyra.

    "Of course you'd do that," groaned Heather before asking, "So where are you going for your honeymoon?"

    "We're plannin' on goin' to Irena's casa," said Tyra.

    "Does it have enough room for you two?" asked Heather a little doubtfully. After all, Tyra was 11 feet tall and Rex taller still. They wouldn't fit inside regular homes so easily.

    "Course we will. She's been puttin' in new places for that colony," said Tyra, "Ya'll should come with us to see what she's been up to."

    "But it's your honeymoon," said Heather.

    "And I tagged along with your honeymoon," said Tyra, "So it's only fair you come along on mine. I think you'll like Spain."

    "I think I might," said Heather, "I hope those games won't take too much time."

    "They won't be when I'm through with 'em," said Tyra.
