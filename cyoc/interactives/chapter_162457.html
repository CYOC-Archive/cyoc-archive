    “Kait! Wook!” Laura says, pointing out the store. “We get cwoves dere!”

    “Yes!” Kaitlin and Laura get to their feet and toddle over to the store. Luckily, as it’s the middle of the night, there aren’t many employees. Despite being nude toddlers, Kaitlin and Laura are able to sneak in the store without incident.

    “Where go?” Laura asks. She sticks her thumb in her mouth and sucks on it for comfort.

    “Um…” After a moment, Kaitlin points a direction. “Dat way.” The two carry on, somehow managing to avoid employees or customers. Eventually, they find the section for toddlers and infants. “Let’s get dwessed,” Kaitlin says. Laura nods. They have a limited selection of clothing within their reach, so they have to settle with clothes they otherwise would not choose. Laura heads for the changing rooms when Kaitlin clears her throat.

    “What?” Laura asks. Kaitlin pulls a package of diapers off a shelf and rips it open. Laura’s mouth drops open. “No! No diapy!”

    “We poopoo and pee at hotel,” Kaitlin reminds her. Reluctantly, Laura goes over to her friend and takes the diaper that Kaitlin hands to her. Clothes and diapers in hand, Laura and Kaitlin go to a changing room and clumsily get dressed. Taping the diapers on is more difficult than either of them expected, but eventually they finish.

Kaitlin’s dress has a breast pocket that they put the dice into for safekeeping. They apparently put the diapers on just in time, as Kaitlin watches Laura squat.

    “Lowa! What do?” Kaitlin says, shocked. Laura blinks.

    “What?”

    “You peeing?”

    “No!” An expression of horror settles on Laura’s face. “Me peeing!”

    “Well, finish and we go,” Kaitlin says, crossing her arms. After a moment, Laura stands up again. Kaitlin takes her hand and pulls her out of the changing rooms.

    “Where go?” Laura asks, shifting slightly at the sensation of her warm and wet diaper.

    “We weave!” Kaitlin scoffs. After grabbing some more diapers, the two begin to head for the entrance.
