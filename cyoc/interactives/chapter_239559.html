    The Lightning Strike

    At an empty room

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 5 months ago O

    Jeremy woke up in an empty room with no windows and a metal door who cannot open from his side. He looked down and he noticed that he wasn't in his dad's body but his own. He was just wearing plain white briefs. He started to panic, was he abducted? Is this a prison? It's not a coincidence this happened after getting his power. His mind was thinking a lot due to anxiety and he was even sweating and hyperventilating.

    "What happened?" Jeremy heard from behind and when he turned around he noticed that Ryan was also there wearing the same briefs. Last time he watched him, Ryan was a husky dog and now he's back to normal too. "Jeremy?" Ryan said when he noticed his now twin brother. "What's going on? Where are we?" Ryan tried to stand up but it seems he was too weak for that. Like he was still numb from the waist down.

    "I don't know." Jeremy replied and he also tried to stand up but with similar results to Ryan. "We have been kidnapped. I, I barely remember what happened before I wake up here. I was dad and I was peeing. Don't ask me why..." Jeremy blushed a little in shame before he kept talking. "Then I remember some soldiers coming into the bathroom and then... Blank. I don't remember anything else." Ryan was trying to stand up all the time but he just can't.

    "Good morning, boys." A male rough voice sound through a speaker on a wall. The brothers turned their heads on that direction. "You should be wondering why are you here. Aren't you?" The voice took a brief pause and kept talking. "You're here because we know you're special. You have a power." Another silence. "Normally we would make some test to know what power do you have, but in your case it was pretty obvious when he got you."

    "Shape shifters." The man said like he was a little bit impressed. "You're the first ones in our base with that kind of power." Jeremy and Ryan were thinking a lot of stuff at the moment but they didn't say a word. "As you may expect, we should make a lot of tests to prove how powerful is that power of yours. So, you're going to stay here for a while, but don't worry, we have already told your parents about this... kind of..."

    "What do you mean with kind of?" Ryan shouted but he didn't get an answer. "What are going to do to us?" Jeremy asked but he also got just silence. "Boys, I will give you only the necessary information. I suggest you to don't waste your energy making useless questions." Ryan and Jeremy stared at each other with some anger and fear in their eyes. "You can rest for a while, one of my men will give you water and food in a moment."

    After that, the voice stopped talking and Jeremy and Ryan stayed at the empty room in silence for a while. Then, a knock at the metal door took their attention and they noticed someone was opening a small gate to bring two containers with food and two bottles of water. Then the gate shut down and they heard a few steps from the distance. Ryan and Jeremy stared once again at each other before they took the food. They were worried of their future ahead...
