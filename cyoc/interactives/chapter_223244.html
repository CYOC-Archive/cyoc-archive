    Name: Mandy Davis
    Species: Human
    Race: Caucasian
    Gender: Female
    Age: 10
    Hair Length, Style, and Color: Smooth long, Pony tail, Black
    Wearing: Dress with flower print, White panties, White training bra, White socks, No footwear

    Interested in: Cats, Horses, Unicorns, Faeries, Dancing, Gymnastics, Swimming, Hanging out with friends

    Mandy then looked into ways she could change the model. First, she went into an option labeled “Conditions.” Once pressed, it displayed a whole slew of different things to apply to the girl, such as skin conditions, mental disorders, chronic diseases and much, much more. Not knowing much about what was listed, Mandy just picked something random: full incontinence. She clicked it, and hit enter. Then the program asked if the girl wanted people to be set in the reality, meaning people would believe she always was incontinent. Again, Mandy didn’t know what this meant, so she just accepted.

    Afterwards, the girl was booted back to her file. It had the changes she made listed at the bottom, but she noticed something else.

    Wearing: Dress with flower print, Size 7 youth diaper, White training bra, White socks, No footwear

    “Diaper?” Mandy thought, as she adjusted herself in the seat.

    Something felt strange, however. The girl’s undergarments suddenly felt bulkier. A thought came into her head.

    “No way,” she thought.

    Mandy lifted up her dress, and sure enough, saw that she was adorned in a white diaper.

    “I’m in diapers?!” the girl panicked.

    She realized just what this program could do. It was capable of changing reality itself.

    “That’s so cool!” Mandy said aloud.

    Then, she heard footsteps approaching the room. In a panic, she locked the door. The handle jiggled for a bit.

    “What the…” Jeff’s voice said, “Mandy! If you’re in there, I’m going to kill you!”

    The door handle jostled roughly. The girl was panicking now.

    “Why does Jeff have to be such a jerk,” she thought.

    Then she got an idea. Quickly, she went into Jeff’s file and started altering things. She went into the “Inanimate Transformation” option, and typed in “Diaper.” Then, she selected the first option that popped up.

    “Let’s see you be mean from around my waist,” Mandy smirked, as she hit enter.

    Quickly, Jeff’s entire file changed.

    Former Name: Jeff Davis
    Object: Random Diaper
    Brand: Any
    Gender: Any
    Age of User: Any
    Other statuses: Any
    Senses: Fully Active
    Consciousness: Active
    Others Awareness: Inactive

    As Jeff finally got the door open, Mandy hit enter. What would’ve been a yelling fit at the girl’s expense was suddenly interrupted by complete silence. The girl turned around in the chair, and saw a diaper laying on the floor…
