    This new power I had, the power over truth, I wondered how far I could push things.

    I had already changed Nate, why not change everyone at our college. I walked back to the couch where Nate was still playing his game.

    "You know, I am glad you are helping me with my workouts, having to do this on my own for my Physique course can be pretty boring sometimes.", I added.

    Then I felt myself change, some of my spare fat instantly disappeared and was replaced with some tight muscle. I felt myself getting lighter then heavier, nowhere near how much Nate weighted though.

    "I am glad you are having a good time too, buddy. Maybe we can do more than just 2 days together in the week and actually go for more. ", Nate said while still focused on his game.

    This was my chance to change things some more.

    "Yeah, working out 4 days working out with you is not nearly enough for me.", I added and felt myself growing a big bigger.

    "But I also do my workout classes on my other days, it's hard to find time to rest", my clothes started feeling tighter around me.


    "Was I going too far? I just put fitness classes in my college... hopefully this does not cause some huge paradox", I thought.

    "Ha! Easy game", Nate exclaimed as he just won another game. Of course he'd win, I made him a master at it just a bit earlier.

    "By the way, Francis, will you get going for advanced fitness classes next semester or will you be joining me for bodybuilding?", Nate said.

    Oh god...

    I had to pause and think for a second...

    "Having the top bodybuilding student as my best friend an tutor sure is a big plus there, so I may go for bodybuilding!", I replied.

    Nate laughed and nodded in agreement. His body was packed with big muscles, he probably gained another 20 pounds right then and there.

    "Besides...", I said...
