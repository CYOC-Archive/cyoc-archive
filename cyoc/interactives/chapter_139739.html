    Richard grabbed the cuffs off the table and walked up to Lewis, the teen bodybuilder.

    "Yo, Lewis, wanna try something cool?" he asked him.

    "Sure, what is it?" the big muscle kid shrugged, smiling as he flexed his big pectorals. "I gotta hurry cause I wanna hit a quick work out in the gym!"

    "You're a gym rat, huh?" Richard smiled as he slipped one of the cuffs on Lewis' right arm. His arms were so thick and muscular that he could barely fit the cuff on it. "Wow, your arms are huge!"

    "Of course! I like being strong!" Lewis grinned, flexing his arm and causing a huge bicep to swell on his arm.

    Then, Jason walked up to Diego, who was very athletic as well.

    "Put this on, little bro!"

    "What is it? What's this for?" he wondered.

    "This will make you... stronger!" Jason replied.

    As soon as Diego had the cuff firmly wrapped around his wrist, both Lewis and Diego were instanly drawn towards one another thanks to the strange powers of their cuffs.

    "Hey! What's happening?" Lewis said.

    Diego and Lewis were pulled towards each other like magnets! Once the boys touched one another, they were surrounded by a bright, white light, which blinded everybody in the room! Then, while nobody could see it, the bodies of Lewis and Diego were absorbed into each other, merging together, and blending onto one big, tall and muscular, male human being who was the sum of the two being being merged.

    Since Lewis was 15 and Diego was 8 years old, their bodies merged and reshaped into a much bigger, taller and muscular human being that was about 23 years old and very muscular!

    When the bright light faded away, the result of the merge was on display before Jason and Richard and the others: Lewis and Diego were GONE. In their place, was a tall, hunky man who was now looking down at himself.

    "Wait... What? What happened?" the man said in a deep, baritone voice.

    "You merged! Those cuffs made you guys merge into one new person!" Richard explained to the towering man, who was over 6-foot-9 inches tall.

    "WOW! Look at us! We're massive!" the man said as he looked down at his bulging pecs, his thick arms and his legs which were as big as tree trunks. "I like this!"

    "What shall we name the new Lewis and Diego?" Jason wondered.

    "How about.... Luciano?" Richard answered.

    The towering bodybuilder just smiled. He was very happy.

    "What about Isaiah and Johnny?" Richard said.
