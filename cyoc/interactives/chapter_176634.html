    Life as a Dragon’s New Tongue

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago A I Dragon

    As the dragon lies asleep, you begin to contemplate your new body: nothing but a dragon’s tongue. Over your first hour as a body part, you begin to realize that the little bit of control that you had over yourself gradually fades away. As you try to will yourself to move, you find yourself unable to budge. Now that you’re trapped as an innate dragon’s tongue and the initial shock of your transformation begins to fade, you began to notice more details about your environment. Despite being this dragon’s literal tongue, all of your senses were still intact. Though since you were a tongue now, taste was your strongest sense. Being within the dragon’s maw and covered in his saliva was far from a pleasant experience. Additionally, you had to deal with his constant breathing, filling your senses with foul dragon breath. The dragon held his mouth open slightly as he slept, letting light illuminate your new home. You could see the dragon’s razor sharp teeth all around you, in addition to the grooves along the roof of his maw. Behind you, of course, was his throat. Seeing this so close caused a slight fear of falling and being consumed by the dragon, but this was a pointless fear for a tongue to have.

    As you continued to take in your surroundings, your whole world suddenly shifted. You were thrust out of the dragons mouth before being slid across his upper lip as the dragon subconsciously licked his lips. Just as quickly as it started you were pulled back within the dragon’s maw and left to rest until another bodily function required you again. This experience caused a deep humiliation to surge through you; To be used as nothing but a part of the creature’s body, without even a thought given to the fact that your soul occupied this organ. Driving this degrading use home was the fact that you had no way to show that you were anything more than this dragon’s tongue. No matter how much you attempted to struggle, you’d never be able to cry out for help or beg for mercy. You were trapped as this dragon’s tongue and would be used for whatever he (or his bodily functions) deemed necessary.
