    Katharine lay there on the bed, legs spread, deep in the throes of passion, unaware of the show that she was putting on for Jake downstairs. She couldn’t remember the last time she felt this way, She had her share of “private time” in the past, but none were this intense. She continued to thrust her fingers deeper into her folds, each one sending her closer and closer to an edge that still seemed so far away, and still wondering what exactly came over her that caused her to act like this.

    Then suddenly it hit her like an explosion. Starting from between her legs she began to violently spasm and shake, growing in intensity as it spread outward, as if she had been hit with a thousand volts of electricity. Her legs and feet began to shake and spasm as her toes curled, digging into the bed.

    “OOH! OOH YES!” She moaned, gripping the sheets tightly as sweat poured down off her glistening, body


    Back downstairs, Jake had made a couple quick changes to his first wish as he...observed the beginning of Katharine’s transformation. Indeed, her skin, normally, a healthy pink Caucasian, was rapidly beginning to turn pale blue, starting from her face. If she was truly becoming a vampire, Jake’s preferred monster girl of choice, he would need to make a change for her survivability, and so he made three wishes. The first wish was that Katharine would not be harmed by sunlight, but would merely appear as a normal human during the day, and assume her “true” form at night. Although Jake did enjoy the fact that Katharine was becoming a vampire, he did not want her to actually harm anyone, so wish no.2 was that she would prefer the blood of animals, and that her nightly “excursions” would instead be filled with supernatural, nonlethal hijinks. His third wish was to simply to change the time of day so that the transformation could continue.

    Katharine basked in the afterglow as her new blue complexion raced down her body and her brown hair turned jet black, ignorant to the fact that it was now suddenly nighttime. As the silvery moon beamed down upon her from an open window, Katharine’s mind,still full of desire, began to drift, thinking of tonight’s “entertainment., and how much fun she would have with the humans residing in the town unaware of how “exciting” their night was going to get. As pleasing thoughts of the terrified faces and screams of people entered her mind, a tingle began to spread in her face, shifting her features to be ever so slightly more batlike. She smiled a bit as her ears slowly elongated and became pointy, and her nose ever so slightly piglike, much like a real vampire bat. Jake could see that the trademark fangs of the vampire had already come in.


    As the tingle faded, Katharine’s eyes opened, now a supernaturally shade of red. Still smiling, she slowly made her way over to the full-length bedroom mirror. Although the lack of reflection is touted as one of the many telltale signs of a vampire, it was mainly due to the fact that many older mirrors contained silver, the metal bane of the supernatural. Katharine’s mirror was a modern one, allowing her to model her new vampiric self. As Jake got a better look at Katharine’s new form, he noted that, although she was a knockout at a human 33 year of age, this new vampiric Katharine, appeared to be younger, closer to his own 17, slightly more batlike visage aside.

    After admiring herself in the mirror, Katharine turned her attention to her discarded sweater and jeans on the bed. Wrinkling her new nose in disgust, she pointed and snapped her fingers. Instantly, the discarded clothing began to distort and transform, becoming undergarments, a gothic lolita style dress, boots, and fingerless elbow length gloves(all red and black naturally).

    Getting dressed, Katharine left the bedroom and began to make her way downstairs. Acting quickly, Jake changed the TV display back to normal, just in time to see the vampire girl that was formerly his mother descend the stairs.

    “Hi Jakey!” the new Katharine said cheerfully, emphasizing her greeting with a twirl. Jake did a split second double take. His mother never called him “Jakey” before.

    “Um…. Hi mom.” Jake said, not sure how to respond. The new Katharine pouted a bit. “Oh Jakey, you know you don’t need to call me that when it’s just the two of us. Call me Katie!”

    “Looks like the ‘girl’ part of my wish got taken literally,” Jake thought.

    “So uh,’Katie’, you got anything planned for tonight’s, uh, ‘hijinks’?” Jake said, attempting to make conversation with the reborn vampire girl in front of him.
