    Oh my snakes in an academy

    A Necklace with a scale attached to the end which Juna quickly wraps around her. "Good choice young lady, your class room is the last one down the hall way to your left" the voice said. Juna thanked it and went on her way.

    As she walked down the hall she began to feel light headed and dizzy. She stopped for a moment to gather her self, when suddenly she fell over with an intense pain coming from her stomach. Juna rolled onto her side rubbing over her belly, "C-can sssomeone help me!?" she whined with her tongue flicking out slightly, but no one came around to assist her. As she laid there the pain was slowly increasing causing her to wraith about screaming and hissing. Completely oblivious to the fact her skin was becoming very dry with cracks all around as if she had scales.

    "PLEASSSSSSE!!" she yelled as loud as she could still getting no one to help her as her struggle continued. Juna's arms started to wither away, all material from muscle to bone was being slowly burned away only causing her more pain. Eventually they just ripped off with Juna wraithing around, replaced by a patch of smooth scales. With this Juna became hysterical going into denial. "Thiss... Thisss isss madnesssss I.. I'm turning into a ssssnake! This can't be real!!"

    Juna began to repeat this over and over again as her head started to smush down into a snake's muzzle, her hair falling out everywhere, and her cries turned into the shallow flicking and hissing of a snake. She would have started crying at this point if she could have, her mind going mad from it all. As she felt her legs merging together, the bones shifting and cracking causing her more pain. Only for a short time though as the merging didn't take long and she had a snake's tail.

    The scales on her body turned a greenish brown color, while the ones on her underside turned white, as her breast shrank away into nothing! Thus her transformation was done, she had no idea what kind of snake she was, why she was a snake, or anything else for that matter. Juna was slowly loosing her self as she felt a parental need settle in, she was holding eggs in her tummy and instinct told her the time to remove them would be soon.
