    it was then the 2 human-faced fish saw a funny looking shape in the water. They swam towards it. As they came nearer, they saw the shape become more clearer.

    Immediately they recognised the shape - it was a human shape.

    In fact it was a human.

    A human that was ACTUALLY breathing Underwater!

    Yup.
    You read that right.

    The human was actually breathing Underwater!

    And, The human in question was a fat man with tan skin, a big round belly, blue eyes, grayish white hair with sideburns on his face, and a big grayish white moustache.

    Bizarrely, the only clothes the human was wearing was a straw hat with a black and white striped band round it, and a pair of yellow XXXL y-front underpants.

    "Hey," Johnny said suddenly, coming closer, "I know this guy! He's Charlie Cockles, a fishmonger who I deliver fish to!"

    He & Ezekial came closer, and they saw Charlie Cockles for themselves.

    Charlie Cockles saw the 2 fish with human faces coming towards him.

    "Whoa!" He exclaimed. "Two fish with human faces! And one of them seem to be similar to that workman who delivers fish to me... uhhh what's his name... Jackson?"

    Johnny sighed. He knew Charlie Cockles all too well - Charlie could never remember his name.

    What, pray tell, happens next?
