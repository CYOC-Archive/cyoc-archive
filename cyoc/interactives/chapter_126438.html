    The two girls continued their stroll, the path lead them past the forest, Raith pointed out a dark van, parked in the nearby woods. A man in black hooded robes could be seen unloading something from the vehicle, though it was too far for the high school girls to see closely.
    "Look." The pale blonde said,
    "I bet it's drugs--" she continued but was cut off by Juliet,
    "I bet it's none of our business." She scolded.
    "Come on!? Aren't you a little bit curious?",
    "It's probably nothing.",
    "Well I'm gonna check it out",
    "Of course you are.",
    "Awww, you know me so well."
    With that said the blonde stealthy maneuvered her way to the van, when the mysterious man had his back turned, she rummaged through the van.
    Juliet was on her toes. If the stranger turned back it would get bad, when it seemed the hooded man would turn, her friend snuck away at the last minute.
    Juliet's breathing returned to normal,
    "You were so lucky he didn't catch you, I mean someone dressed like that, who knows what he would have done?" The dark haired girl lectured.
    "Yea well he didn't. Besides, I got us some souvenirs!" Raith chimed and dropped a silver ring in Juliet's hands.
    Juliet studied the ring; it was made of actual silver, with a strange symbol(The symbol will be described/shown in a later post) she didn't recognize. Raith slid the ring on her left ring finger and admired it,
    "Thanks I guess." Juliet said skeptically, pocketing the strange ring.
    The two quickly made it to Juliet's house and parted ways, the raven haired girl just now noticed that it was getting dark, it was strange, she could have sworn they were only out for at least an hour. The 16 year old dismissed this and got to her room and went to sleep, all the while the ring seemed to consume her dreams.
