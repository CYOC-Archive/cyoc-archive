Kat used her magic on him just like she had on Dan before, making him remain behind when the party was over, before she went to switch her clothes.

By now the costumes had merged to their wearers, making them unable to take anything off anymore. When Oliver was the last remaining human next to her, Kat used the same spell as before, only made it act faster. It took only a minute for his snake costume to take over his body, covering all open parts and reforming them.
    As Oliver´s panic suddenly stopped on the outside Kat knew he was ready to be renamed like the others. So she renamed him as Slither.
    Slowly, one after another, Kat let the animals around her vanish. Each one was transported to either the pet shop in the neighborhood, the farm on the outskirts of the city or to the zoo. All would act completely normal there, as that was part of the transformation they had went through. The only thing Kat made sure off was that none of them would get killed by either humans or other animals.
    When only the dragon Drago (Dan), the lion Simba (Aaron), the tiger Tabby (Miriam) and the snakes Curly (Julia) and Slither (Oliver) remained, Kat was satisfied with the number of pets she would keep. But neither a lion nor a tiger was a normal pet, and they also weren´t compatible, so with a grin she cast at them again. Soon instead of a lion and a tiger there stood two smaller house cats, which only looked like a lion and a tiger. The snakes were made smaller as well, and Kat placed them in a terrarium together.
    When only Drago remained, Kat turned to him. “Now then, brother, I hope you had fun on my party. Just like the other four I will keep you here as my pet, but instead of making you small you will remain as you are. Only additional to being my pet you will also be my servant. Is that okay for you, Drago?”
    As much as Dan tried, he couldn´t get any control back to himself, or he would have used his now sharp teeth to bite Kat. Instead the created Drago answered: “I will be honored to serve you, mistress.”

    What Dan didn´t know was that Kat´s magic made both cats and snakes act that way, much to their human minds. But without control there was nothing they could do, and slowly they submitted to their new minds.
