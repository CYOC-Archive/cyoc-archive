    Bradley and Ryan were staring at each other while Cletus entered to a big red farm. It was impressive that such a big place was run by only a man. They suspected that something was strange around the place and it's owner.

    Ryan looked around and noticed that there weren't too many animals, just a few pigs, cows, a horse, some chickens and a turkey. He wondered why a big place like that was almost empty. He was thinking about when Cletus came back with an old plow machine and a bucket. He gave the bucket to Bradley and ordered him to collect the eggs. After that, he gave the plow machine to Ryan and told him he will plow a big terrain.

    Bradley obeyed immediately and left but Ryan was struggling a lot with the old device.

    "This thing is heavy." Ryan complained. "It's going to take me forever!"

    "Good, you have nothing else to do, Bigfoot!" Cletus smiled while he slapped one of Ryan's butt cheeks. "Now move that pretty big ass of yours before dark."

    Cletus left the place leaving Ryan still struggling with the machine, however, after a few minutes and noticing that Cletus wasn't around. He stopped working and went to see what was Bradley doing. He passed next to a stall where the horse was deeply staring at him. He didn't care at first, but somehow he realized that it's a mare. Why does it matters? He wondered why he kept walking.

    When Ryan arrived to Bradley's place, he noticed that his friend had already finished his chore. He was just looking at the chickens while the wind gently moves his body hair. Near him there's the bucket filled with a lot of eggs. Something surprisingly good despite there weren't too many chickens.

    "Oh, Ryan. I had seen you." Bradley said as he turned around. "Don't you think this place has a mystical aura? It's very calm but at the same there's a lot going on."

    "No, I don't think so."Bradley replied. "I was thinking about leaving. I guess we can get something better. First we need some clothes and then we run out. I don't want to see that man's face again in my life!!"

    "That's too bad because I'm just behind you." Ryan turned around and noticed that Cletus was behind him with too big overalls meant for them. "Hey Blondie! Good job." He said as he threw an overall to Bradley. "You earned it." Ryan can noticed the happiness in Bradley's face. "However, you Bigfoot, you didn't finished your work."

    "It's not fair. Bradley's chore was way more easy than mine." Ryan complained with a kinda childish attitude which didn't match very well with his manly look.

    "Huh? It's not the difficulty, but the way you do it." Cletus said. "If you were struggling, you just had to tell me and I gave you a hand. Or better, a hoof."
