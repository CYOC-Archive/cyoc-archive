    His friend didn’t take long, she was already there in a couple of minutes, Jeff opened the door and greeted her to later move to Jeff’s room.

    “So what were you about to show me?” Aubrey looked around in curiosity.

    “This!” He pointed at the computer, the screen showing the title “The Chronivac” like some kind of old application.

    “Oh? A retro game? I love those”

    “Way better than that!” After a minute Jeff decides to tell her everything, deciding it would be more fun to try the program with a friend, so he starts explaining the “game” to his friend.

    Aubrey’s eyes are wide as saucers by the time Jeff finishes, “there’s no way!” She says.

    Jeff chuckles, “absolutely,” he says.

    “Sounds like you’d want to try it out with me,” Jeff says.

    “Oh or course I do~” Aubrey response, “I already have some GREAT ideas in my head.”
