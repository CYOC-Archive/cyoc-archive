    After some thinking, guilt washed all over Emily. What ws she thinking about? Betraying his friend, using him, just to make some easy bucks? No! He would have never done this to her! She must speak with him. The question was, how?
    An idea formed in her head. If treating Tom as Tomfoolery and calling him a mare changed his mind, maybe she could change Tomfoolery into Tom the exact same way.

    In the barn, Tomfoolery was struggeling. Her body felt so hot, and she found herself thinking about Marine Star. The sturdy, beautiful stallion was a fever dream for every mare-like her. She found herself thinking about their last encounter, and the quick gaze she took upon the stallion's massive member... She wondered again, how would it feel like? She became hornier to the thought, and in every passing minute she felt more aroused, more needy... she wanted thim. She was in full heat, and she wanted a big, strong stallion inside of her. She shivered, waiting for her owner to come back and help her release...
    Emily's steps made her ears twitch. She walked to the front of her stall, eager to see her owner. Strangely, her owner didn't looked so happy. Tomfoolery whinneyed, trying to express her care. What has happend to her?
    Emily stroked her mane, whispering to her ears. "Tom, are you there? Do you understand me?"
    A wave passed through the mare's mind, and she looked at her owner in confusion. Who is Tom?
    Emily looked like she's going to cry. "Tom, please, tell me, are you still in there?"
    Tom... she has heard this name before. Maybe it was the other human's name? No, Tomfoolery didn't thought so. The name was very familiar, and hearing it over and over again made her more and more conflicted.
    "Tom, do you rememebr anything? Do you remeber who you were?"
    Tomfoolery neighed. The name felt so familiar, she could think it was her own! And what does Emily means by 'before'? Tomfoolery was Emily's mare since the day she wa born... did she? Another wave passed through her mind, and she could hazily remeber her standing beside Emily, not as her mare, but... as what?
    Emily continued petting her muzzle, tears falling from her ears. "I am so, so sorry, Tom. Please. Do you remember? You were my friend, my boyfriend, a human, can't you remember?"
    Another wave passed through the mare's mind. Emily was... her owner. No, It didn't sound right anymore. She was... her friend?
    "Tom? Please!"
    Tom, Tom, who is Tom? Why did the name sounded so familiar?
    Another wave passed, and a memory blinked in her mind. Her name... His name was Tom. Yes! That sounds right! And he's male, he was sure about that, and-
    In an instant, all of his memories flashed back into life, and Tom let out a frightful whinney as he remembered everything. He was Tom, Emily's boyfriend, and they planned on turning him into a horse for the weekend...
    The mare's eyes sparkled with inteligence, and Emily let out a relieved sigh. "Tom! Please, tell me it worked. Can you understand me?"
    Tom neighed... and nodded. Emily let out a shameful laughter. "Thank god... Tom, I'm so, so sorry, I don't know how could I ever do this, I'm..."
    Tom whinneyed in confusion. "What is it?" he nickered.
    "I, I, I don't know what came over me, but I've thought about how much can you sell a foal for, and, well... I was planning on breeding you with Marine Star against your will."
    "What?!" Tom neighed and stomped the ground in anger. Yes, he remembered it now! Emily has used the mind spell against him, turning him into a mare!
    "I'm so, so sorry," she said, crying and stroking Tom's fur. Tom was trying to hold his anger, but he just couldn't. Maybe it was something of the mare that was inside him, but... He realized he just couldn't be angry at Emily. She was her girlfriend, but even if she fell to her ambitions, she did redeemed herself and freed him... And, anyway... The memory of his meeting with MArine Star flashed back into his mind, making him shiver in shame, The stallion... he was intoxicating. Tom just couldn't stop thinking about him. After all, he was in heat, and he and Emily did thought together about having him breed with her horses... of course, as a stallion, but... was really such a bad idea?
    "Tom, I've came here to change your mind back, and I'll get it if you don't want to be my friend anymore, it's fine, I'm so stupid, it's all my fault, and you'll change back soon-"
    Tom stuck his muzzle in her hands, snorting. Emily looked at him in wonder. "Wh-What? Tom? Does it means... that you're forgiving me? Really?"
    Tom nodded, stomping the ground with his hooves. Emily looked back at him with a huge smile. "Thank you, thank you, thank you so much, I'm so sorry, I should've never done this, I'm so-"
    Tom snorted again, and tried to roll his eyes. Emily giggled. "You've always been so caring and forgiving, Tom, thnak you so much." She looked at her friend-turned-mare deep in his eyes. "Tell me, Tom... what do you want to do now? Do you want me to leave you alone until you'll change back? Do you want to go on a ride? Do you want me to bring you someting, anything? Or maybe... you do want to get to know Marine Star after all?"
