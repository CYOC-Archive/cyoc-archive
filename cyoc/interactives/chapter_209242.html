    Henry and Susan are quickly distracted by a scream from the living room. The scream came from Janice, "What the fu....aAHHHH!" Was all they could hear through the closed door. They then heard Ashley and Jacob start yelling about something, followed by the sounds of riping clothing. Then they heard snaps and cracks which they could only assume we're Janice's bones expanding and bending.

    "What's happening to you Janice?!!" Cried Ashley to her sister.

    "I DONT KNOOOOOO!!!!" Janice tried to reply as her voice become obviously deeper and more bull like.

    "Oh wow!" Susan started. "Isn't this kind of hot? Turning people into animals against their will?" She asked her brother. Henry was too preoccupied listening through the door to acknowledge his sister.

    "It's kind of satisfying taking away someone's humanity and turning them into a beast. A lower lifeform." Susan continued.

    "Wha....what..is that a penis? Janice?" Ashley screamed at her sister.

    "No....NO....NOOOOO" Again Janice's voice trailed off into the sounds of a fully grown bull.

    They heard loud thuds and clomping sounds and they both knew that Janice was most likely much larger now and on all four hooves. They heard a crash as the large animal had obviously broken something of value.

    "We probably should have done this outside." Henry spoke up finally.

    "Maybe you're right, but too late for that now. But speaking of outside...." Susan thought for a moment. "I have an idea for a game."

    She ran back to the PC and started typing. Henry continued to listen to the panic in the other room. It sounded like the transformation was over, however the panic talking of Ashley, Jacob and the sad moo'ing of a bull could still be heard.

    Henry turns back to his sister. "What game do you have in mind?"
