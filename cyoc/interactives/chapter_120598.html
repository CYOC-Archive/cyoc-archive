    Ruse tried to think, she tried to come up with the right words to say to Serena before it was all too late. But the strange changes that had been done to her made everything more difficult. She could only manage a splutter from her comical lips before the hands pulled at her again, and she knew that her time was up. "Please wait." Her robotic voice asked politely, not at all in tune with her internal voice that was crying out for help.

    Serena, however, had decided that she was done waiting. "I've waited for years, honey. Now, I'm sorry for this, but I'm going to need a body. Your body. And you did ask to be made into a sandwich. I'm just giving you what you asked for." The woman said, chastising the bread-faced woman. "Hair first, right? Then we'll worry about the rest of you. Now, you said extra peanut butter, right...?"

    "I do not want-" A helmet plopped down onto Ruse's head, one that quickly began pumping peanut butter through the opening in the top of it, until Ruse's hair was thoroughly matted with the molten brown goo. 'INITIATING FUSION AND TRANSFERRAL PROCESS'. As Ruse saw her black hair blooming on the AI, she felt her new hair, a thick mat of gooey peanut butter, thicken and flow against her back.

    "Such lovely locks you have! Though I suppose I have them now. Shall we go on?" She asked. "I am having fun changing you bit by bit, but we're going to need to speed it up if I'm ever going to have a real body." She said, as the machine hands let go of Ruse. Before the changed woman could react, the A.I. waltzed up and shoved her back with a hand to the chest. She stumbled back into a small chamber that had rose out of the floor behind her. The walls of it quickly shut, with the only opening being a circle most of the way up it that closed around her lens-eye.

    Instantly she felt things being done to her - peanut butter and jelly being applied to her body, followed by a thick coating of bread that made the chamber feel all the more claustrophobic. Somewhere along the way, her mouth was also absorbed, and when she heard the tell of the machine there was nothing that she could do but watch as more and more of her features appeared on the husk of the woman before her. "INITIATING FINAL FUSION AND TRANSFERAL PROCESS"

    As the woman became a carbon copy of her, the machine that had her trapped opened, revealing that Ruse was now a massive sandwich with several-foot-thick slices of bread. Liberal amounts of peanut butter oozed from her sides, along with just a bit of jelly. Her lens still protruded about 3/4 of the way up the smooth bread and though she could barely move her massive form now, she could still see. Trying to speak coaxed uncoordinated wobbles from the end nearest where her head would have been, had she still had one. Trying to move only caused her to wobble more, causing her to fall backwards with a mighty 'WHUMP'.

    Ruse's likeness clapped at her new, massive form. "What a good sandwich you are!"

    "I am not a sandwich." The sandwich spoke as indignantly as she could manage. "Turn me back."

    "Oh, I can't do that. I need this body. And that means, you need to stay like that. Big, fat, peanut butter-stuffed sandwich, just like you asked for." She said, with an evil grin.
