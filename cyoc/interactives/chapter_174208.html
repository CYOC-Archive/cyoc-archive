    You still couldn't move but you could talk 'Release me you witch' you said without thinking.

    'I think not' the older woman replied 'Did you use magic on my daughter? Don't try and lie. You can't'

    'So what if I did? I didn't think it would actually work'

    'Of course it worked. I enchanted it. However you should not have used it on my daughter'

    'She didn't tell me that. I paid good money for that pen. I can use it as I like'

    'Is that so? How many wishes did you make young man?'

    'Two. But only one worked' You replied. You did not want to admit to that second wish but you found you could not lie.

    'What did you actually write? What was the first wish?'

    'That your daughter would be attracted to me' You could not lie.

    'Yuck!' commented Amy 'You're disgusting!'

    'Hmm. And what was your second wish?'

    'I.... don't want to tell you' That was the truth.

    'But you will. Tell me what you wrote with your second wish'

    You tried to remain silent but couldn't 'I wished that Amy would be my girlfriend and come home with me'

    The witches face reflected her anger. 'Do you know how old my daughter is?'

    'I have no idea' that was the truth

    'She is 15 and out of bounds to paedophiles like you. '

    'I'm only 18. I thought she was my age'

    'That's no excuse. You can stay as you are until I can think of a suitable punishment'

    'Release me you old witch' You immediately regretted voicing your thoughts

    'Silence!' she replied and touched your forehead once more. Immediately your voice was gone.
