    "I'm looking at being a genie for awhile... I know it sounds off fir this kind of store but I at least what a chance at it" Alexis explained.

    This was a rare event at the store the employee noted privately. see unlike the other transformations who were sent to pocket dimensions in the cosmic reality, genies instead had their own solo occupied ones inside the expected magic lamps kept in store for such occasions and as expected these ones had openings to interact with the normal world for the transitional granting of wishes until like Alexis's request a staff member was sent out to retrieve said lamp ready to end the stay. at the same time there were set rules to using genie magic given out in these contracts to agree to the change as well as wannabe genies setting out their own rules so long as there was on conflict with the mandatory ones. "well... if your sure, this way please" the employee accepted.

    soon Alexis had collected a proper genie costume consisting of baggy purple Egyptian styled trousers with a gold waist belt, a pink midriff exposing top that still covered her chest well, white ankle strap sandles, a blue crop vestments, a purple arm scarf and golden bracelets and circuit with jems encrusted on the bracelets "all set then?" Alexis asked the employee as they reached the checkout. "not completely" the employee answered.

    popping behind the counter for a moment the employee fished out the required papers for Alexis's genie contract alongside a well designed genie's lamp "alongside payment you need to agree to the terms listed here though so long as they don't contradict you can set your own" the employee explained "also the lamp instead of going in with you must be hung outside the changing stall and you need to write down the address you want to be sent to after the process". "that's better than I was thinking... now lets see" Alexis accepted as she went through the details

    for the mandatory rules;
    1) unless permanent all wishes must be of a temporary nature
    2) no wishing someone into a genie or wishing one's self free
    3) killing with genie magic directly is forbidden
    4) no tampering with the forces of life and love

    soon Alexis got to writing her rules down;
    1) no purely selfish wishes, all wishes must benefit those close in heart to the wisher
    2) only 7 wishes per master and no wishing for more wishes (but no wishes are lost for trying)
    3) wishes for transformations can only last for an hour

    "Ok, all set" Alexis announced as she signed her name and the details of where to be sent while paying the asked price. "then one last thing" the employee insisted "once ready inside the stall you need to chat 'fac me ipso mihi somnium' to undergo the transformation". "umm alright" Alexis accepted as she headed into her chosen stall, hanging the lamp on the outside hook as she did so.

    "well... here it goes" Alexis announced once all dressed up and her normal clothing stored away "fac me ipso mihi somnium". suddenly Alexis found herself floating as the room shifted into a more Arabic styled bigger room filled with cushions and candles, also her mind was painlessly filled with all the knowledge needed to use her magic not only to grant wishes but also to alter her lamp's inside to better match her likeing. outside while this happened the employee watched as the lamp glowed to show Alexis had become it's occupant.

    "right then... while I wait" Alex said to herself back inside the lamp as she magic up her TV and gaming consoles alongside making them workable without electricity alongside a mini-fridge holding some drinks "just have to wait to be called now" Alexis announced to herself as she booted her game up while waiting to reach her assigned destination:
