    Beldazarr, The Djinn of Mind and Body

    Not so good wishes

    "Hi, I'm Sam, djinn of stories." A voice coming from the green pen at Bobbi's hand told him. "I have almost unlimited magic powers that are at your disposal while you write them down using me as a pen."

    Bobbi can't believe it. He has always dreamed about having magic powers to change his horrendous life. He immediately trusted into Sam's words without asking any question. He quickly wrote something in a notebook to test his new magic power.

    "I live in a giant palace full of servant and luxuries. Everybody loves me and is happy to work for me." Bobbi smiled while he wrote what he's saying. As the words appeared on the paper magic sparks started to surround him and change his reality.

    "It's done, master." Sam's voice sounded but he seemed pretty tired. "But I must tell you, alter reality is a hard magic. You have made a lot of changes and my magic is almost empty now. If you want to use more magic I recommend you to undo some previous wishes to take that magic back."

    "I thought you said unlimited powers..." Bobbi replied. "Oh well, I already have a pretty good life without a palace or servants. You can undo all of that. I want to do something small instead. What do you recommend me?"

    "I'm particularly good at transformative magic. It's all djinns speciality." Sam proudly said, he didn't sound tired anymore as the previous wish reversed. "You can pick a person and I will change them into whatever you want."

    "That sounds interesting." Bobbi said. "Let's see..." He looked through his room and noticed an old lamp. "Ok, then I'll write that this lamp is going to turn into a black cat."

    As soon as he finished writing, the lamp began to morph. It grew cat ears, a tail and legs, until it finally was a cat... But just in shape. It didn't move at all, like if it was a taxidermy statue.

    "I forgot to said. I'm not very good at mind magic." Sam said. "That's my brother, Nate, power. I recommend you to use my magic on living creatures."

    "Anything else I should know?" Bobbi asked while he seemed more and more unimpressed with Sam's magic powers. Sam told him that's all and know he can use the power as he wants.

    "Undo the last transformation." Bobbi said and the words vanished on his book at the same time the lamp gets its original shape. "I need to find a person to test this magic..."
