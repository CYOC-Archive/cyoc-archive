    Jake awoken at about 9:00a.m. About two hours later from the week days he had classes. He stretched out his arms, letting his tail out the covers. He stared at it with envy and petted it, sending chills up his spine. "Another day, another change." He said quietly, hoping not to disturb Leo's sleep. However, Leo wasn't in his bed for some reason. " He must've stayed up all night playing with his pouch... Or he was watching some new 'Dragon Academy' episode." Jake thought he'd have to catch up with the anime as well.

    Opening the door, Jake could see Leo passed out on the couch ,with his hand in his pouch, with the TV on. It seemed to be on channel 48, confirming his suspicion. Jake was about to wake Leo, when the door knocked. The knock woke Leo, feeling all groggy. "Ugh... Who could be here at this hour?" Leo asked not knowing the time. "It's 9:00." Jake said. "Exactly." Leo obviously wasn't a morning person. Jake went over to answer the door. Opening the door, he saw no one. However, upon looking down, he saw a package, labeled to him this time. "Sweet! It's here!" Jake said closing the door.

    Jake's tail was wagging mad as he made his way to his desk. Leo looked up to see Jake's package and perked up a bit. "Is that what I think it is?" Leo asked. "Oh yeah! Looks like we're going to be busy today!" Jake unwrapped the bubble wrap revealing his new chrome laptop. "This thing has more RAM and battery power, which should add a bit more quality to life." Jake said peeling off the plastic on the screen.

    After getting fully updated and adding his profile to his new laptop, Jake was ready to install the Chronivac. The Chronivac started running, but the U.I. seemed different from his old computer. "Huh... That's odd. Is this a newer version?" The Chronivac's new U.I. had the update in text, explaining bug fixes, and new features. "Percentage bars, color palettes, environmental changes... Danm, there's alot more in this version!" Jake and Leo were looking in awe at how much was added to the Chronivac. Both were wagging their tails non-stop. Now was the time to test it out.
