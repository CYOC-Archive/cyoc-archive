    From the small hole in the back you could only make out a few details at first. Around you you felt like you were tossed amongst a mix of soft, plush fabric and hard plastic material. Through the hole you saw what looked like fur or fuzz of some kind?

    ‘Where the hell did she put me?’ You think as you try to shift yourself from within the bag.

    Eventually you finally got a look at what you were laying in. Around you you saw teddy bears, Barbie dolls, and all other manner of toy in this pile Izzy had left you in. Still confused you try looking upwards away from the pile and get your answer.

    Above you you saw the front of the Little Luvs Daycare Center, an all girls daycare that watched kids of all ages! You suddenly realized Izzy had thrown you into a bin of donated toys! It would appear she was happy to indulge you in your fantasy to be a doll by donating you! But the last thing you wanted was to be forced to be some toy for a little girl! Terrified of this you redouble your efforts to escape but it’s no use! The little hole is all you could force as you realized you are stuck here until this place opened in the morning!

    Reluctantly, having no other option, you tried to find a comfortable spot amongst the toys and tried to fall asleep, weary of what the morning wound bring.




    You don’t know how long you’ve been out when you’re suddenly awakened by a swaying motion. Your eyes blink open as you peer out from your little hole, bright light shining in as you feel the bin your in swaying. You gulp as you realize someone has carried your box into the daycare!

    “Alrighty,” you hear a feminine voice say from above you as you feel the box set down, “lets see what we got today.”

    You remain frozen, terrified of this woman discovering you within the bag as you doubt she’d believe someone else put you in here! The next few minutes you hear the woman remove toys from the bin, remarking on their quality and any damages these donated toys may have. Finally, you feel her grip the bag you’ve been stuck in.

    “Now let’s see what’s on here,” she says as she unties the top of the bag and reached her hand inside.

    You wince nervously as her manicured hand grabs you around your waist and pulls you into the light to see her. The woman appears to be in her mid to late twenties. She is wearing a pair of leggings and a blouse which opens up a bit to reveal her very large cleavage. Her hair long, curly hair cascades past her shoulders and her eyes are a piercing blue. The last thing you notice is a small name tag hanging on her shirt near her tits that reads “Anna.”

    “Oh goodie,” she says as her pink lipstick covered lips curls into a smile, “a new baby doll! The girls are gonna love this! And it’s such nice quality too! Hopefully you last a little longer than the last one.”

    You didn’t like the sound of that as you can only imagine what happened to the last baby doll, but you stayed motionless in her arms as she carried you across the room.

    You saw in a corner of the room there appeared to be a playhouse, no doubt used for when these kids played house. She knelt down inside finding it difficult being an adult, and brought you in. You saw quite a few toys and play clothes scattered around but in the corner you saw what looked like a crib!

    Anna set you inside the crib and smiled down at you before setting a blanket across your body.

    “Perfect,” she smiles down at you before checking her wristwatch, “oh the girl’s will be here any minute!”

    With that the gorgeous daycare worker walking out, leaving you alone in the playroom. Immediately you sat up in the crib trying to think of a plan!

    You could definitely escape from this doll crib however you weren’t sure how easy escaping this daycare might be! No doubt it’s pretty fortified to prevent a child from escaping and at your size you may be outmatched.

    You also realize you might be able to try to blend in? You know they watch babys at this daycare so maybe you could avoid becoming a plaything and simply blend in as a baby girl if you can find a convincing outfit!

    Lastly, you may have to resign to your fate and pray these girls aren’t too playful with a brand new baby doll! You know time is of the essence and decide to…
