Permanent. That's what he went to a sorcerer in the darkest reaches of his jungle. The sorcerer gave him this shrinking potion. With that he could protect his friends and punish the evil-doers. Tarzan just had to bring the shrunk hunters to the sorcerer, always on the lookout for humans to transform. These are bad men so Tarzan thinks it is not evil. Soon the hunters will be hunted.
