    Optimistic about her situation, Selena climbs into the shower, the bathroom begins to
    steam up. She gets out of the shower as her boyfriend enters the room. Sleepily he looks
    over at her with a sleepy smile. Selena smiles and wraps the towel around herself and
    walks out of the bathroom. Her boyfriend follows her out of the bathroom brushing his
    teeth. "C'mon, get changed, lets go get some lunch." Selena pauses for a second, should
    she tell him? He goes back into the bathroom and Selena rushes into the bedroom. She
    rips frantically rips through her drawers until she finds a turtle neck, a black leather
    trench coat and low rise pants. Now Selena was beginning to get a little upset, the
    catwoman form was definitely something she enjoyed but now she was becoming a little
    uneasy when thinking about having to hide it everyday.

    After lunch, her boyfriend headed back to work. She looked down at her hands, they were
    black latex and clawed. "I can't stay like this, there has to be a way to get this suit off". She
    grabbed the card in the box and called the Bodyshapers number...no answer. Selena went
    to her room, stripped naked and cried in the mirror at her new shape. It still felt like a
    suit, it was her skin but it felt like a suit, the more she thought about it the more trapped
    she began to feel.

    She grabbed the mask, to see if there was anything inside of it to clue her into how it
    worked. It felt like a normal mask. She placed it against her skin on her face and it didn't
    stick or feel strange in anyway. Upset and somewhat irrational, curiosity got the better of
    her and she quickly pulled the mask down over her head. She felt the back of the mask for
    the space to pull it off...It was still there. She lifted it up a little and she began to feel her
    face transform back and forth a little as she wiggled it up and down, teasing it on and off.
    She decided she's try it out, what did she have to lose? She climbed out the window and up
    onto the roof. She decided she was going to have some fun and took off.

    Later that evening, returning home Selena climbs back in the window. Her boyfriend
    grabs her and throws her to the bed and begins to make out with her. He moves to pull off
    the mask and with a tug she screams. "What?" That hurt. He laughs and reaches down to
    pull it off. Confused he turns on the light, and inspects the mask. "What is this?" Selena
    stands up and reaches behind her neck "hold on". She reaches for the spot to remove the
    mask...it's gone. She screams.
