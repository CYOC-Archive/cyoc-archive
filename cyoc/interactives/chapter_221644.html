    Herb was a 30 year old man, resting in a beach chair on his laptop in generic Hawaiian clothing. It was a sunny day and he was at a private beach and house, enjoying his vacation and solitude. His job allowed him to afford this easily, but as much as he wanted to enjoy it naturally, he couldn't keep himself from the internet and all the content and information it gave him.

    Speaking of which, he came across the "mutate-me.com" site somehow while surfing for transformation stories, preferably of the monstrous kind. He loves stories that explored them, although he was disappointed in the lack of material for the ideas, or the content beyond power fantasies. Even with this site, he was convinced it was just a program to auto-create stories or prompts. Either way, he was going to have fun with it.

    Herb was surprised with how in-depth it went, although it lacked a physical representation of the monstrous forms or modifications. For example, it described how many fingers a SINGLE limb could have, if the fingers had smell sensors, and even if a touch from the fingers were poisonous. Heck, if the poison was lethal, painful, and/or an aphrodisiac. The site could even give advice for flavor as it seemed to pay attention to what Herb looked at or lingered on.

    Finally, Herb felt he explored it enough to actually try something. After some thought, he decided to try...
