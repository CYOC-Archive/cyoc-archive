    With Jeff’s clawed hand firmly grasped, his mother began leading him to the living room where his dad and older brother sat watching a football game. Jeff didn’t understand why his mother was so intent on showing him off, but he assumed she wanted to scare them since she was known to be a prankster of sorts. Following his mother, since he thought he had little choice, Jeff still wondered what she was up to. Jeff felt that little good could come from putting a werewolf before his other family members. Jeff thought that his dad and brother could have scared her good and this was payback, which would be pretty funny since Jeff really was a werewolf, for now at least.

    At the bottom of the stairs, his mother stopped and Jeff did the same. Where they were was the perfect place to stage surprise events, since both of them were obscured by the dividing wall from the living room to the main hall. Looking back to Jeff, who stood there with a raised brow, she chuckled silently. She then made motions with her hands as instructions for Jeff to follow. First, she motioned for him to silently go down the stairs, then growl loudly. Then, when his father and brother went to make a move, Jeff would rush in and scare the crap out of them and then she would come in while pointing and laughing at their expressions. It figured that she was up to no good, but at least he wasn’t at the butt of the joke as well. Jeff knew his father and brother to be macho men, so it would be interesting to see their reaction. The only thing was, Jeff knew that there were two .308 rifles in the living room since they were avid hunters and he just hoped that he would not get shot because of his mother.

    Jeff’s mom watched the two in the living room intently, ensuring that they would not know what was coming and when she was satisfied, she motioned for Jeff to get going. Slowly, on the balls of his wolfish feet, Jeff moved silently as if he were stalking some sort of menacing prey. Still oblivious to what was going on, the two watched the game and Jeff decided to up the ante a bit. Jeff growled loudly while he hid around the corner of the opening and motioned for his mother to go in the room as well. As she walked in, catching on to what he was up to, Jeff growled again, louder and longer than just before. Playing into it all, Jeff’s mom knew she was up to bat once more.

    “Hey, did you guys hear that? Sounds like some dangerous animal outside.”

    “Helen, what in the world are you talking about? I didn’t hear a damned thing and you are taking me away from one of the biggest games in a long time!”

    “Pete, I could have sworn I heard something” Which she said louder, which Jeff knew he should make some loud noise.

    With a bit of gusto, Jeff howled at the top of his lungs which made everyone jump, including his mother.

    “What in the Sam hell was that?”

    “See, I told you Pete! There is something outside of the house!”

    “Helen, that don’t sound like it was outside… sounds like it was inside the house somewhere.”

    “Well, how do you think an animal that sounded like that could have gotten inside the house.”

    “Helen, I don’t know. What I do know is that if that animal tries to attack us, it has another thing coming.”

    Helen paused for a second, knowing that Jeff was right around the corner and was sure that he heard what his father said. She wasn’t sure if this was such a great and funny idea anymore. Pete was now on his high alert and Ben was just a notch below his father’s intensity, so things could go wrong quickly. Knowing the Jeff would be able to understand her coded message, Helen made a nondescript comment.

    “Well… go ahead and do what you think is best, if it is needed… of course.”

    Back with Jeff, hearing the entire conversation, Jeff pondered what his next move was going to be. Would he go out there, scare them and laugh? Would he go out there, scare them and get shot? Would he go out there, watch his family go for their guns and keep them both from shooting him the best way he could? The possibilities were endless, since the situation could change at a moments notice and all he could do was wait and see what the reaction would be. He knew that no matter what, he would be fine since werewolves were immortal and he would heal pretty quickly from any injuries that he would be inflicted with. With that in mind, Jeff growled as loudly as he could, jumped into view which startled Pete and Ben, then rushed towards them as if he was going to attack. Catching them completely off guard, Jeff stopped in his tracks as looks of complete horror was upon their two faces.

    Looking toward his mother, she just pointed and busted out in laughter.

    “Wha… what the hell Helen?! How is this funny?”

    “Oh man, you two should see the look on your faces right now!”

    “Mom, seriously, there is a blood thirsty werewolf just waiting for us to make a move to eviscerate us and you think it is funny?!”

    “It is when the werewolf is your brother and this was payback for the shit you two pulled on me a couple days ago, which almost gave me a heart attack.”

    “You… you mean that is Jeff!?”

    “Yes honey, that is your son Jeff. I turned him into a werewolf using the chronovac to scare the shit out of you both. From the looks of things, we did a good job at it too!”

    “Alright, if that really is Jeff, let me see his face!”

    “I… I don’t know if he can change back into his old self without using the chronovac. I completely changed his appearance and I think it is permanent until I use the chronovac to change him back.”

    “Well Helen… if that creature does not prove himself to be our son Jeff in the next couple of minutes, Ben and I will do our best to pump him full of lead and take him out.”

    “Hey Pete, remember he is a werewolf nonetheless, so if you have silver bullets, you would do well. I don’t think you have any of those laying around in the near vicinity, so my guess is that you wont be able to harm him like you want.”

    “Damn it woman, the creature didn’t need to know that we didn’t have anything to kill it with. Still, I would really like to see proof that it is Jeff because I find it a little strange that this creature hasn’t done anything more than what it has.”

    Helen looked at Jeff and smiled. “Alright honey, if you can, change back into your normal self.”

    Jeff looked at his mother with a slight bit of concern, not knowing if he could even do that, but it would be cool if he could on his own. Thinking about being human, Jeff focused as best he could and slowly felt a tingle course throughout his entire body. The transformation that he underwent only minutes ago felt like they were going in reverse, and as he was under the feeling, his family watched in amazement as the werewolf turned back into Jeff, the boy they all knew. When Jeff was human again, or so they all thought, he stood there in the clothes that became tattered from his original transformation. Astounded, they all stood there in silence for a few seconds. Both Pete and Ben were stunned by his transformation before them, since neither of them thought that it was even possible to have something like that happen. Jeff was just amazed that the experience that he had was that of an actual werewolf, changing back and forth between forms was amazing. Now that he was human again, he felt a bit sad since he was not a werewolf any longer.

    “Jeff, how did you do that?”

    “Just like mom said, she turned me into a werewolf using the chronovac.”

    “Can that turn the subject into anything a person wants?”

    “Yeah, I suppose so. Why, do you want to try something out dad?”

    “I think it would be interesting to become that which we hunt, to understand their movements and their instincts so that Ben and I can become some of the best hunters around.”

    “Dad… I really don’t think that is a good idea. I mean, if you turn into a buck, then run around in the woods with other deer, you could be shot and killed. You could be blinded by a car, be hit and killed. The only safe form to take, in all honesty, is the same one I did so that if you were injured, it wouldn’t matter because it would heal up right away or much quicker. Things that would kill one of us, only damages a werewolf for a short period of time.”

    Pete sighed, knowing that his son was right. It would be a bad idea, since they have heard about several guys getting killed by hunters who weren’t paying attention, let alone being turned into an animal and having an even higher chance of being killed with absolutely no repercussions to the person who did the shooting in the first place.

    “Alright Jeff, you are right. Why don’t you go back upstairs and enjoy that chronovac some more?”

    Jeff smiled from pointed ear to pointed ear, having wanted to check out the chronovac since his mom used it to make him what he wanted to be for so long. Jeff was very curious about what the program could really do! Jeff ran out of the living room, up the stairs and into his bedroom. Immediately in the doorway Jeff could see his computer screen and there was a blinking message on it. When he approached his computer, Jeff’s eyes went wide and his heart soared.

    The message read: Warning – Permanent Species Alteration. The subject is permanently the species requested due to the combination of human and animal hybridization. No further alterations can be made to the subjects species type.

    Once Jeff clicked the “Ok” button, he knew he got what he wanted. Jeff truly wanted to be a werewolf, well… now he really was one.

    After the message went away, Jeff noticed a few new options.

    WEREWOLF PHEREMONE LEVEL: 1 (increasing this number will cause all men to want to be a werewolf)

    WEREWOLF ATTRACTION LEVEL: 1 (increasing this number will cause all men to want to have sexual intimacy with other werewolves)

    WEREWOLF DESIRES: Gay (this is not adjustable. While in werewolf form, the subject will only have physical intimacy with male werewolves. In human form, they will be as the previous setting states.)

    Jeff knew that this could get very interesting and possibly a bit dangerous.
