    "A dragon!" Andrew exclaimed

    "Excellent choice! Please choose a type of dragon now," the voice exclaimed

    "Wait, types, there’s types?!" Andrew shouted

    "Why yes, allow me to help explain each," The voice replied then a list suddenly popped up on screen of all of the dragons and their types.



    Red dragons- Typical and traditional dragons with varients of red shaded scales. They live a tribal life near/on volcanic mountains and have thick natural scales as armor (increased armor on level up). They are proficient in brute strength and fire magical elements. Males typically are warriors, and hunters. Females are generally caretakers, breeders, farmers, and occasionally hunters in this race.


    Green dragons- Dragons that occupy the forest and hidden groves within it. Green dragons are very adapted to nature and its surrounding plantlife and fauna as none seem to fear the beasts. Green dragons are very diplomatic towards those who share respect for the forest, most commonly fairies and elves, and possess many powerful magics depending on the season, and most turn living beings into plantlife and fauna. Males and females generally are equal in this culture, although most live in solitude, and will engage most likely engage in regular conversation and mate with members of the opposite sex and share interests like any civilized species would.

    Blue dragons- Blue dragons are sea creatures that possess no flight capabilities whatsoever. However they are extremely intelligent and are sought after by wizards and sorcerers as mentors. They have dominant control over water magics and can turn beings into sea creatures. Males amongst this species are gentlemanly and treat women chivalrously, and while this can be seen as sexist in most cultures the females and males fight on equal terms, although most tend to avoid combat at all cost which could explain this fact.

    Yellow dragons- Yellow dragons are rare creatures with the power to channel storms through their bodies. Many yellow dragons are known to live near large settlements or cities and are devoted to a king or lord. However, this is only due to one of two factors, the first of which is if the ruler, being it king, queen, or both, promised the love of their child to the dragon. Therefore the dragon will appropriate the young prince or princess by assisting in teaching them for their king/queen, and once they come of age will transform the child into one of their own species to consecutively breed at least three clutches of eggs for the sake of their race. Females and Males are equal, but only because so few exist and many only choose to breed this way because they are raised to believe only one gender of their race still exists, and sometimes get desperate to populate. If a male dragon is also promised the love of a prince the most likely outcome will be that the prince will be changed into a female dragon upon his coming of age, and vise versa with female dragons and princesses.

    Purple dragons- Purple dragons are dragons highly inlfuenced by their magical roots, and are the only source of magic in its purest form. Purple dragons have complete control over this pure magic, and many talk about reaching a state of divine enlightenment. Most purple dragons follow the faith of a god who they claim is the source of their magic, and the more devoted they are the more powerful they become. This species has no single settlement, and their magical abilities seem almost limitless as many have taken form of different races, as well as humanoid versions od themselves. These dragons are also extremely populous and are known for their breeding cycles every few months, and their willingness to breed with humans, as well as convert humans to members of their own species. Therefore, the childhood of purple dragons is very short. Although most live far longer than humans a purple dragon reaches adulthood only after a couple months, and proceed to assist their parents with offspring before going out to continue the cycle. Males and females are as equal as they are in the society they dwell in, and are diplomatically peaceful as long as you respect their deity.
