    Two Problems At Hand

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 25 days ago AR Toddler

    Determined to reverse the transformations, Vanessa decided to experiment with the camera. She snapped a picture of Frankie, hoping it would restore her to her original age. But instead, Frankie morphed into a random object: a rubber ducky. The sight was hilariously absurd, and Vanessa couldn't help but burst into laughter.

    Undeterred, Vanessa tried again, taking another photo. This time Frankie transformed into a pug. Giggles filled the room as Vanessa found herself entertained by the bizarre transformations. They continued their attempts, each photo turning Frankie into something entirely unexpected: a chicken, a hamster, a balloon, and even a seal.

    After ten failed attempts and uncontrollable fits of giggles, Vanessa decided to give it one last shot. And just like the other failed attempts, this photo ended up transforming Frankie into something humiliating.

    Frankie stared at her reflection in disbelief. She was now a 2-year-old toddler girl, dressed in a girlish onesie, a pacifier dangling from her mouth, and a diaper bulging around her waist. She looked up at her best friend, Vanessa, who was trying to suppress her laughter. Vanessa couldn't help herself and made a playful comment, unaware it would push Frankie to the edge of frustration.

    "I'm sorry, Frankie", Vanessa said between giggles. "I can't seem to get it right. But hey, at least you make a cute toddler!"

     Frankie clenched her tiny fists, her face contorting with anger. "Stop laughing, Vanessa! This isn't funny!"

     But Vanessa couldn't help herself. She burst into a fit of giggles, unable to comprehend the absurdity of the situation. Frankie's transformation was both baffling and hilarious.

    "Okay, that's enough!", Frankie snapped irritably. She was completely fed up with her friend's teasing.

     Trying to regain her composure, Vanessa wiped away tears of laughter and reached for the camera. But before she could snap another picture, her cell phone interrupted her. She put the camera down on the bed and rushed to answer the call, leaving Frankie alone with the camera. Frankie seized the opportunity and swiftly snatched the camera, gleefully aiming it at Vanessa.

    Before Vanessa could end her conversation, she turned around to find Frankie holding the camera with a sly grin. Panicking, Vanessa shouted, "No, Frankie, don't!" But it was too late.

    Frankie managed to capture Vanessa's image before Vanessa could stop her. In a matter of seconds, Vanessa transformed into a 2-year-old toddler girl, clad only in a diaper.

    Frankie erupted into laughter, finding great joy in Vanessa's predicament. However, the tables turned quickly as Vanessa's laughter turned to tears, fueled by embarrassment and frustration.

     "Frankie, this isn't funny anymore!", she scolded. "Give me that camera!"

    Angered by the constant teasing, Vanessa attempted to snatch the camera from Frankie's tiny hands. Frankie, with all her little energy, got off the bed, trying to escape from Vanessa. Meanwhile, Vanessa waddled clumsily but with determination.

    "Give it back, Frankie!" Vanessa squealed, her voice high-pitched and filled with frustration.

     Frankie giggled mischievously, holding the camera high above her head. "Not a chance, baby V! You're too slow to catch me!"

    Vanessa pouted, her chubby cheeks flushed with exertion. "I'm not slow! You're just too fast!"

    Thus began a wild chase around the room, with each girl desperately trying to take control of the camera. The room became a whirlwind of chaos as the two toddlers chased each other, giggling and taunting one another.

     "Bet you can't catch me!", Frankie teased, her childish voice filled with glee.

     "Oh, just you wait, Frankie!", Vanessa retorted, her voice equally defiant.

    At one point, in an attempt to gain an advantage, Frankie dashed out of the room, disappearing from Vanessa's sight. Desperate not to lose her friend, Vanessa wobbled after her, her little feet struggling to maintain balance on the carpeted floor.

    Frankie toddled towards the stairs, thinking that then she could easily pass Vanessa. But in an unexpected slip, she tripped and the camera slipped from her grasp, tumbling down the stairs, bouncing relentlessly until it reached the bottom, broken beyond repair.

    The silence that followed was only interrupted by the sound of their quiet sobs. Tears streamed down their chubby faces as they turned to each other, their frustration replaced by realization.

     "This is all your fault, Frankie!" Vanessa pouted, sniffling. "If you hadn't taken the camera, this wouldn't have happened!"

     Frankie's lips quivered as she retorted, "You're the one who transformed me! You could've just tried to fix me instead of laughing at me!"

     Their cries turned into a cacophony of tantrums and accusations, their voices echoing through the walls of Vanessa's parents' house. But after a while, the two finally began to calm down.

    Frankie sniffled and wiped her tears with a stubby arm. "We need to stop crying and start thinking a solution to this, Vanessa."

    Vanessa hiccuped, her little chest heaving with sobs. "O...Ok".
