    At first glance, Mino Security Solutions (MSS) seemed like any other security guard firm. Far from any sort of private army, they marketed themselves as providing guards who would do the sleepy overnight jobs that were often expected, although day and more active ones were available too. Look over a graveyard, warehouse, or other properties. Even doing items such as mall security, house calls, or delivery. Outside of some patrolling, the job was often just sitting there passing the time. Perfect for any sort not looking for any major responsibility. Of course you might actually NEED to do some sort of security, which is where MSS differed from others.

    You were going to be transformed to be suited to the job. MSS tackled the niche of the best security was full on scaring trouble makers away or subtlety keeping track of those looking to cause trouble while evading traditional security systems and personnel. Either way, MSS often chose forms perfect what was needed.

    A ghost to haunt a house for a client? Sure! Become an Alligator to catch poachers in the act? You betcha! Become a Minotaur in Labyrinth? Of course! And it didn't have to be a perfect match, as long as it worked. And ultimately they don't want YOU to get hurt or be uncomfortable, so there might be special tweaks to your form. The only major draw back is that you may need to spend days, weeks, or possibly even longer (although restricted to Veterans or the more adventurous employees) in the form doing the job. But besides the benefits, MSS will do it's best to make sure you'll WANT to be a long time worker.

    You came into a local office, talked with an interviewer, and they saw you were suited to be an employee. They pulled up a list of jobs for you to pick from...
