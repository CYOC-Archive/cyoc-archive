    "Charles. We'll call him Charles Kang." Said his Mom.

    Charles Kang. Born on the 20th of October.

    His mom, Sally, was so happy to see him. After the 9 months of waiting, finally, he's in her arms.

    Few years later, Charles grew up into a very active boy. At the age of 12, he was a player of soccer in their school. He made a lot of friends. He got good grades. His parents were very happy to raise a very good son.

    Although not every story is happy. Charles had also been in a lot of fight which he didn't initiated. A lot of bully would tease him for being Asian. Most of them were jealous of Charles for even being in the Soccer team. Getting a lot of friends, boys would often be mad at him too when Charles get too close to girls.

    This time, Charles arrived at his home, hiding the wounds on his body. He quickly ran up his room, avoiding his parents. He never wanted them to see all the bad things that happen to him. He locked his door and jumped onto his bed. He softly began to cry and to pity himself.

    Not all awards nor kindness paid off, he thought. He would still get hurt no matter what he do.

    *Bing!* He suddenly heard a noise from his computer on the right corner of his room. He noticed that it was left open. He thought he might've accidentally left it on. He walked near his computer to see a pop-up on the screen.

    Charles read the text:
