    Jeff looks at Azrael, “what do you want to do,” he asks.

    “To be honest,” Azrael says, “I’d like to stay at least for the night, I’m okay with meeting the rest of my new family tomorrow”.

    Jeff nods, “I’d love to stay the night,” he says, “let me call my mom,” he then takes out his phone and dials his mother’s number.

    “Hey Jeff,” his mother, Mary, says as she answers, “where are you?”

    “Hey mom, I’m at a friend’s house,” Jeff says, “I’d like to stay the night here, will that be okay?”

    “Sure, just make sure your robot doesn’t cause any trouble,” Mary says.

    “Mom, you know that HIS name is Azrael, I wish you’d stop calling him a “robot”! He’s more than that,” Jeff says, smiling at Azrael, “he’s like a brother to me, and I wish you’d treat him as such!”

    Azrael’s eyes widen, “you… really mean it,” he asks, “I… I’m not just some… machine?”

    Jeff nods, smiling.

    “Fine, I promise to treat him as a member of the family,” Mary says with a sigh.

    “Thanks mom,” Jeff says.

    “I’ll see you and Azrael tomorrow, be sure to be home at latest 1pm,” Mary says, “you still have jobs to do!”

    “Yeah, I promise we’ll be there,” Jeff says, ending the call, “well, looks like we’ll be staying for the night,” he then says.

    Dennis and Quinn beam, “sweet,” they say.

    “I’m ready to meet the rest of my new family,” Quinn says, getting to his feet.

    “Okay, Ben and Len should be in their rooms,” Dennis says, “or together in one of their rooms”.

    As they head out of Dennis’s room, they notice a new door with a sign that says, “Quinn’s Wolf Cave! Stay Out If You Don’t Want To End Up As A Snack!”

    Quinn snorts, “these signs are weird,” he says.

    Dennis chuckles, “yeah, well… we are a weird family,” he says, “why don’t you check out your room before you meet the rest of your new family?”

    Quinn nods and opens the door to his room, stepping inside the wolf-boy’s eyes widen, “it’s huge,” he says, then grins, “I’ll surely love it here!”

    “Glad you like it,” Dennis says.

    After exploring Quinn’s new room, the four head to Ben’s room where they find Len and Ben playing some video games, the twins grin when they see Quinn, “Quinn! How’s our favorite fluffball doing,” Len asks, a cheeky grin on his face.

    Ben narrows his eyes, “have you made sure that you have no fleas?”

    Quinn narrows his eyes, “watch it, Ben,” he growls.

    “Whoa… chill, bro,” Ben says, “I’m just teasing!”

    “Well, I don’t like it,” Quinn says.

    “Sorry, I promise to be good,” Ben says.

    “Me too,” Len says.

    “Good,” Quinn says, looking over at Dennis.

    “You’ll get used to their teasing,” Dennis says, “but if I were you guys, I’d not tease Quinn to much, you know he ha a short temper!”

    Ben and Len nod, “yeah… we really don’t wanna end up as some wolf snack!”

    Quinn grins, “oh, but you look so juicy!”

    Ben’s and Len’s eyes widen, “y-you’d really eat us?!”

    “IF you annoy me,” Quinn replies, grinning, he then laughs when the boys faces turn pale, “just teasing!”

    The boys pout, “not funny!”

    “Well, let’s make a deal: No mean pranks or teasings!”

    Ben and Len nod their heads, “deal!”

    Quinn smiles, “well, I think we’ll get along pretty well,” he says.

    Ben and Len smile, relaxing, “yeah… we will!”

    “Let’s head down and see if we can find mom and dad,” Dennis says.

    “Sounds good,” the others say.

    Jeff, Dennis, Azrael and Quinn turn to leave, “hey, Quinn,” they turn back around as they hear the boys call out.

    “What’s up,” Quinn asks.

    Ben and Len rub the backs of their heads, “we… we wanted to ask you something,” Len says.

    “And what would that be,” Quinn asks.

    “Well,” Ben says, “we were wondering, if we were like you, what animals would we be?”

    Quinn raises an eyebrow, he then grins, “well, I’d say, Len would be a tiger with blue fur and white stripes,” he says, then turning to Ben he adds, “and Ben would be a Malinois, which is a Belgian shepherd”.

    The two boys smile, “cool,” they say, “hehe, would be easier to keep us apart!”

    Dennis leans in to Quinn, “you aren’t planning to transform them, are you,” he whispers.

    “No, only if they ask me to,” Quinn whispers back.

    “Good,” Dennis says, then raising his voice he says, “let’s head downstairs,” with that the four leave.

    Ben and Len turn to each other, “wouldn’t it be awesome if we’d ask Quinn to transform us into a tiger and Malinois,” Ben asks.

    Len smiles, “yeah, being a human is boring, it would be far more interesting if we were like him,” he says, then grins, “maybe he’ll even give us magic abilities? Hehe… just imagine the pranks we could pull on people!”

    Ben grins, “I like that idea,” he says, “let’s ask him in the morning!”
