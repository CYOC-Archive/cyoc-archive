    CGMRG: Doll or Pool Toy?

    added by TransforMarv 2 years ago A BM I O Feline

    Royce stopped as a potentially important memory resurfaced. She quickly checked the settings to confirm it. “I think I found a solution, Dina,” she explained to her twin. “The gun can also create non-organic maids, specifically plastic and pool toy maids. Those probably won't need any food, or produce any wastes for that matter, they’ll only need room to sleep, maybe not even that. We could save so much by creating mainly plastic or pool toy cat girl maids. We should immediately test that.”

    Dina immediately found herself agreeing with her sister, mostly because her plan involved creating more maids. The only matter now was where to find someone that fit their criteria.

    As the two cat girl maid mistresses were thinking about that, they didn't notice two college-age girls entering and directly walking past them towards the manga shelves.

    “If I may interrupt your thinking, Masters,” Riley spoke up. “Those two customers, who just entered, might make for great maids. These twins are regulars in this store and the only company they always have is each other. They once told me their parents died a few years ago and they've been on their own since then.”

    “That's perfect!” “Thank you so much!”

    Riley just blushed at being praised by her two masters.

    “I’d say one plastic and one pool toy and then we’ll compare.” Dina nodded in response as they faced the twins. Royce shot the first twin and quickly changed the settings before aiming at the other. Both gained the expected cat maid features while their hair turned blue, one a light shade, the other a dark shade. Their tails had the same color as their respective hair. Next their maid uniforms appeared, which again had a lower cut neck and shorter skirt than Royce and Dina’s outfits. Royce had the same interests as her twin after all. Interestingly the one with light blue hair gained a dark blue dress and the one with dark blue hair got a light blue dress.

    But that wasn't where their changes stopped. Plastic sheens spread over the skin of the cat girls. Once they were fully encased the light blue-haired girl’s became soft and rubbery, while the dark blue-haired one’s skin hardened. Mostly unseen to everyone the pool toy maid gained a barcode on her butt and her belly button turned into a valve at the same time as the plastic doll’s joints turned into ball-joints.

    Their transformations finished the non-organic cat girl maids turned around to face their masters and bowed. Aside from their different color schemes, and the fact one was a living doll while the other was a pool toy, they looked perfectly identical.

    “My name is Puru,” the light blue-haired pool toy cat girl maid in a dark blue dress introduced herself.

    “And my name is Pura,” the dark blue-haired living doll cat girl maid in a light blue dress continued.

    “How may we serve you, Masters?” they finished together.

    “Do you two require any food to live?” Royce asked straight to the point.

    Both shook their heads. “The pleasure we get from serving you is the only sustenance we need,” they answered together again. Royce hoped they only meant this figuratively.

    Still, this was a great breakthrough for the cat girl maid master twins. These new options would give them much more freedom in the creation of more maids.
