    'It's strange,' Kaylee thought, 'When my parents were still human I told Master I calmly
    accepted whatever fate happened for them... but I really was scared for both Master and
    my parents when the fight broken out. Though it really isn't right to call Kevin and Marcy
    my parents anymore. We are all part of the pack that serves Master, the Big Bad Wolf.'

    Given Bart had described the basement as the 'dark and stinky place' the she-wolves and
    their sense of smell were still stuck eyeballing for it's location carrying around the giant
    rug. Not that the rug was really heavy for them at all, but it was an odd shape.

    With Little Red Riding Wolf leading, in spite of her internal monolog managed to lead the
    she wolves in the discovery of the basement door, it wasn't locked, but Kaylee was
    tempted to rip off the hinges all the say. It was an annoyance for the seven foot talk she-
    wolves to squeeze through the door carrying the huge rug.

    While their night vision took care of the 'dark' park of the basement, the 'stinky' aspect
    was different all together. The she-wolves started at the smell. Their noses didn't just
    detect the scents a human being would, but could practically smell the entire -history- of
    the basement, every broken pipe, everything and anything that had even slightly gone to
    mold.

    "Whao." The twins chorused.

    "My sentiments exactly." Kaylee said.

    The smell of ashes and wood chips made finding the secondary furnace a breeze. Much
    like the smell of iodine and anti-botics had made Darla finding the first aid kit a breeze
    set aside from the gas furnace.

    "If there's a leak at least sister we'll know it instantly." Fran said.

    "Right sister." Darla nodded.

    Fran went over the wood furnace (having used one before when her family had gone to
    that cabin in the dead of winter that one year), and began set up the heat.

    Kaylee thought, "You know with our fur we aren't going to be getting much use out of
    these things, as mammals we produce our own body heat."

    Fran and Darla nodded, "Yes Alpha female." They said in tandem. It gave Kaylee a warm
    fuzzy feeling when they did that, it reminded her of just how powerful the Transform was.

    Darla and Kaylee unrolled the bloody and peed on rug that had been once upon a time
    quite a price tag. It might have been more logical to simply scrub and wash it, but such an
    act for the Smiths was hardly out of character.

    Soon a fire was going in the stove much to Fran's satisfaction, and the heat slowly radiated
    out to the she-wolves.

    "Let's get this done as fast as possible," Kaylee said, "I hate leaving the master alone."

    "He's not alone." Darla pointed out.

    "He has the cubs with him." Fran finished.

    "You know what I mean!" There was also the matter of the bandaged leg.

    "Yes Alpha female."

    Kaylee finally remembered what Jensen had sent his mate along for and began to with her
    claws and strength tear the rug into bite size pieces for the furnace. With amazing
    efficiently the twins began to hand off and feed the rug to the waiting furnace, the smell
    of their master's blood and the marking of Marcy and Kevin coming out in the flames.

    Fast they worked, and diligently they worked too, after all, the master given them this task,
    and thus it was their reason for living at the moment.

    "You are still thinking about Julie aren't you sister."

    "Only a little."

    "Whose Julie?" Kaylee asked.

    "My/Fran's wife." The twins answered.

    "Oh."

    "And I am only thinking of her a little. She is nice but the master is nicer. And I love my
    daughters but I love the pack more Alpha Female."

    "Well, you still have the game right?" Kaylee asked, "Maybe you can Transform or Dare her
    and your daughters into nice new ladies for the master to mate with."

    The idea of her daughters being age progressed into women and making love to the
    master didn't feel wrong to Fran in the least. "I'm sorry Alpha Female, but it's simply too
    risky. My wife right now has nothing but a bunch of dead ends to search. And my family
    vanishing might cause more questions than answers. I can risk master or his pack like
    that."

    "I see." Kaylee wanted to say something more, and thus would have easily said more and
    then some, but remember what the master had been saying about her impulsiveness and
    thought that running out into town and transforming Fran's family right now WOULD get
    to much attention. The pack was less than a day old. If they moved too fast they'd trip and
    fall on their face.

    Sooner than later, the rug was nothing but ashes and little bit going up the chimney.

    Kaylee looked around the basement, like any basement it had a very impressive collection
    of junk that was screaming to be sold off on E-Bay and would otherwise never see the
    light of day again.

    "Next order of business after we've had time to settle down is organize and sell this junk!
    It's just taking up room."

    "Of course Alpha female." The twins echoed.

    Kaylee imagined how large master's pack could grow, and as self proclaimed alpha female,
    she also imagined herself the one whose duty it would be to figure out and consider
    people who would make useful pack members. Of course, using the game on a person to
    person bases was going to get clunky fast. 'Maybe we can figure out a way to use the
    Transform and Dare to trigger a big bad wolf transformation automatically when certain
    conditions are met? Of course we'd need to transform someone who'd contain the trigger.'
    Kaylee could be clever when she wanted to be, she could just be a bit oblivious at times.

    "Let's get back to Master Jensen, he knows this place better than any of us, and we'll need
    him to figuring out the ins and outs of our new home."

    "Right Alpha female."

    "And I'd rather think it's best that he doesn't have to worry about having all the cubs to
    himself." Fran said.

    "Or him all to the cubs." Darla added, the twins giggled.

    "Don't worry about that, the master will handle them more or less or a little bit, but all the
    same we shouldn't dally when there is much to be done."

    Fran lead the way, not without the rug to carry and knowing where they were going, the
    she-wolves moved incredibly fast, nearly knocking the basement door off it's hinges as
    they went.
