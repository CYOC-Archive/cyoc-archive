    "What's going on?" Maedel asked.

    "The prince was a girl, there's an army following her, we have to leave or Morgana will turn us into her body parts." I said.

    "Shit. OK, let me help." Maedel said.

Due to the whole "prisoner" and "magically cleaning my clothes" thing, Morgana didn't have a lot of stuff in my room, as such, it was easy to pack. They both lit instantly, but Morgana was too busy making our escape carriage to notice. Even when we were a few miles away, I could see the glow of the fire.

    "Morgana?" I asked, "What do we do now?"
