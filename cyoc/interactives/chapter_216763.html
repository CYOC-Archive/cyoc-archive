Anything from the lost property will do" Mr Jacobs was aware that the young girl seated before him was wearing nothing but a towel.

    He then turned back to the girl he thought was Ruby "So Ruby. How did you get to the boys change room? The front office told me you were there not 5 minutes ago?"
