    You awoke feeling woozy unable to see or think straight at all. You are barely able to stand, and can only see the pink gas. You try to find Alisa, but you can't see anything through the gas as you walk around. You try calling out into the gas, but there was no response. You figured that she had passed out as well, and is still incapacitated.

    "Alisa!" You call out again, but to no avail so you decided to stop trying. Suddenly the pain in your spine returned making you fall to the ground. You screamed in pain, but fought against the pain as much as possible trying to crawl along the floor. You continue to crawl, and you see a figure laying on the ground that you think is Alisa. You motioned to crawl towards Alisa, but the pain immobilized you, and was now in your stomach. When you finally gained the strength to continue you saw your hand, and were shocked as it wasn't a like a hand at all anymore. Before you could depict exactly what your hand had become the pain returned, and caught you off guard causing you to pass out again.
