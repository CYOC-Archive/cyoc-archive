    You three watch as the light strikes a nearby man jogging. As the light fades you watch as a large dog runs out from the light. The dog stops as he realizes that something feels different. The three of you watch as what had just been a hulking dark haired man, began to inspect himself and realize that he was now a massive Rottweiler. “Well I guess this thing can turn people into dog breeds other than German Shepard”, you comment as the Rottweiler begins to look around panicked.

    You begin to wonder what you should do now that you have yet another dog. Even worse if you try to tell that man that you turned him into a dog he could attack you just like the bitch, and you are not confident that Keith could help protect you from the monstrously sized dog. Suddenly the bitch begins to bark and catches the dog’s attention. The dog gives a confused look at first, but as the bitch continues barking he turns his attention to you.

    You feel Keith tugging on your sleeve and see him looking at you worriedly. “Dude, what’s wrong”, you ask as Keith continues tugging on your sleeve. As you are trying to figure out what it is that Keith wants you here the Rottweiler let out a bark and begin to growl as does the bitch. “Oh, shit”, you yell as you realize that Keith was trying to warn you that the bitch was selling you out!

    You quickly grab the keys as you a Keith rush off you your car and the bitch and the Rottweiler begin chasing after you. You open your door and allow Keith to jump into the car. You follow Keith in and slam the door shut just as the two angry dogs begin jumping on the side of the car barking and growling at you wrathfully. Unsure of what to do you crack your window slightly and yell out over the dogs’ barking, “Listen here bitch you are the one that caused all of these problems.”

    Keith gives a little woof and shakes letting his collar jingle reminding you. “Okay, you didn’t cause all of this but you caused most of this”, you correct yourself.

    Your yelling does not stop the two pissed dogs and you look at Keith as you try to decide what to do. You and Keith were already safe in the car so you could always just abandon the bitch, her pup, and the Rottweiler. Then you could go home and try to fix Keith and the cop with the keys. Or you can test the keys on one of the dogs other than Keith so that you don’t risk doing something worse to your buddy. What will you do?
