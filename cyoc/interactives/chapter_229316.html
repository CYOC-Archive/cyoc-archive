    Before Jeff is able to come up with a story, they suddenly hear some say “WTF,” when they turn around, both of them widen their eyes: Valerie out of confusion and Jeff out of shock as they notice the REAL Cornelia standing a few feet away, just staring at them, her eyes wide and a confused look on her face.

    ‘Fuck,’ Jeff thinks to himself.

    Valerie looks between Cornelia and the “girl” she thought was Cornelia, “okay… what the hell is going on,” the pink haired girl asks, turning to the real Cornelia, “since when do you have a freaking twin!? And why have you never said anything?!”

    “Because, Val,” Cornelia says, scowling at the imposter, “I do NOT have a twin,” both girls stare at the “clone”.

    Jeff gulps, knowing that he has some explaining to do, ‘better tell them the truth, lying to them will just make matters worse… for me,’ he thinks to himself, he then looks around, “could… could we go somewhere more… private? I promise to explain everything!”

    Valerie and Cornelia look at each other, “okay, follow us,” Valerie says, “you better come up with a GOOD explanation why you look exactly like my friend!”

    Jeff follows the two girls to a park which has a small wooded area, once they are in the small forest, Valerie and Cornelia turn to the fake, “now… spill it,” the real Cornelia says.

    The “girl” takes a deep breath, she then explains everything: who “she” really is, how she became Cornelia and why.

    “So, let me get this straight,” Valerie says, rubbing her temples, “you claim to be that loser Jeff, who has got some kind of weird App which supposedly is able to transform anyone and anything into various stuff. You didn’t believe it yourself and decided to do a test by transforming yourself into Cornelia because you have a crush on her and out of curiosity!?”

    Jeff nods, “that… that sums it up,” he says.

    Valerie and Cornelia look at each other, “you know,” Cornelia says, “I actually believe he… him,” she says.

    “How so,” Valerie asks, “she could just be lying”.

    “Why would I lie and come up with some “fantasy” story instead of, like, just saying that it’s a coincidence that I appear too look exactly like Cornelia?”

    Valerie squints at the small “girl”, “she has a point,” the real Cornelia says.

    “Okay,” Valerie says, “IF it is true… prove it!”

    The real Cornelia nods, “IF you really are that perv Jeff, I demand you transform back! I do NOT want you being me!”

    “I… I am not a p-perv,” the “girl” mumbles, “I… I was j-just curious!”

    Both Valerie and Cornelia snort, “yeah… right,” Cornelia says, “if THAT were the only reason, you would have transformed into a female version of yourself. BUT since you have a crush on me, I suspect that you were planning to have some… “fun” in MY body!”

    Jeff, knowing that Cornelia is right, blushes hard and turns away, “I… I’m s-sorry,” she mumbles.

    “A simple “sorry” won’t cut it,” Cornelia says, Jeff looks up at the girl, the look in her eyes telling him that she wants MORE than just a “sorry” in order to forgive him.

    “Would… would you forgive me,” Jeff starts, not thinking about the consequences, “if… if I’d allow you… and Valerie, but especially you, to… to use the App on me… transform me into anything you want… would… would that make you forgive me?”

    Valerie and Cornelia look at each other, Jeff waits, feeling fear creep up in him, ‘wh-what the hell did I just suggest,’ he asks himself.

    The two girls then turn to look at the “other” Cornelia, Jeff gulps when he notices the weird and creepy glint in their eyes.
