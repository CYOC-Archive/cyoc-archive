    “Hey, I’ll catch up with you guys! I just remembered that I need to make a quick… phone call!” One of riders shouted to the other three, as she peeled off and rode towards the barn.

    It was Maggie on her big painted mare.

    “Flynn! Hey! Hold up!” She called to him as he was just about to enter the barn.

    Flynn stopped and quickly turned around, surprised to see the rider approaching him.

    “Hey,- Maggie, right?” Flynn asked with a little nervous smirk.

    “Right! Good job!” Maggie said, with a big smile.

    She was pretty, but she was also about three times older than Flynn. He could imagine her being a real hot ticket in her younger years, with her beautiful smile, large chest, and plump rear. Truth be told, Maggie married a big name doctor years ago. He had since passed away and she inherited a load of money. She used most of it on herself; nutritional programs, personal trainers, basically anything that would keep her looking younger. Fortunately, the money spent was paying off, without knowing better, anyone would think Maggie looked 10-15 years younger.

    “What’s an attractive young man like yourself doing working at a place like this?!” Maggie asked.

    Flynn put a hand on the back of his head and rubbed it nervously, knowing he couldn’t give the real answer.

    “Well…” he started, “I’ve always wanted to work with horses and… I’ve always heard that horse girls were the sweetest and most attractive.”

    Flynn smirked and winked at Maggie, being a flirt, but not meaning it due to the age difference. He was very charismatic and had a way with women. Maggie blushed and looked away, licking her lips.

    “Well…” Maggie started, as she set her reigns down and rubbed her thighs, “I can prove it to you~”

    Flynn smirked and eyed Maggie’s horse, then her. “Yeah, I’d like that. I know what would be even better!”

    “Oh really?!” Asked Maggie, excited.

    “Yeah! This!” Flynn said as he snapped his fingers.

    Suddenly, Maggie felt numb from her waist down. She became very nervous and concerned, but realized she was frozen and couldn’t speak. She watched in horror as her horse’s head and neck slowly seemed to shrink, getting smaller and smaller as it sunk into the big torso below. On its own, Maggie’s body slid forward off the saddle to where the horse’s neck once was. Her legs fused and melted into the horse body, her tight riding pants and boots falling to the ground. Maggie’s chest begin to tingle and swell, the top of her button up riding coat popped open, and two large boobs inflated out, taking up the once perky shape they used to be. All of the wrinkles and creases on the woman’s body all faded away as her skin tightened up to its younger appearance. Maggie now appeared to look as she was 21 again; large chest, tight stomach, stunning face, long flowing hair. Horse ears slowly emerged on both sides of her head, one black and one white. The horse body also seemed to change. It got younger, stronger, almost curvier. The tail grew longer and fuller. The bits in the back grew tighter and hornier. Maggie finally was able to control her body again. Her much larger and younger body.

    “My god! Look at me!” Maggie screamed with a smile, checking herself out in the reflection of Flynn’s car. She unbuttoned the rest of her coat, took it off, and sent it flying. “I feel amazing!”

    “Glad you like it!” Flynn smirked. “Ready for some fun?”

    “You betcha!” Maggie exclaimed and followed Flynn into the barn.
