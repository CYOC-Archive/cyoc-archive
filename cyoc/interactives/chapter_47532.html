We changed that by making it brand new and we changed the color to pink. We also made the windows look like they just had new panes of glass installed.

It did make the rooms look much nicer. We made similar changes to our bedrooms. Valerie made her room all purple, while I went with all pink. We didn’t change what possessions we had, though some were tucked away in the recesses of our closets. Those were mainly the things that identified us as males.
