"I'm sorry i can't take the time to help you more thouroghly, but I have uimportant buisness. Perhaps this will answer your questions." He says, as he hands you a piece of paper, and shoves you out of his shop. As you pass through the doorway, the shop's door closes and the entire store dissappears. You head back home, hoping to read the paper in your room.

    The paper turns out to be an instruction manual of some sort. It reads:
    Holder of this amulet, be warned. This amulet was once able to function correctly, but has been broken. It used to change objects or people at the holder's will, but was hit by a powerful spell and has been working incorrectly ever since. The amulet constantly builds up power, and when it reaches its peak, will discharge this onto a random object in the vicinity.
    Red will cause a change related to the color red.
    Blue will cause a change related to the color blue.
    Yellow will cause a change related to Gold or Yellow.
    Green will cause a change related to green.
    White will cause a change to the mind, or if it is an inanimate object, will give it a mind and life.
    Grey will freeze time around you for a day.
    Pink will cause a change in gender, or if its an inanimate object, will change the object to something similar of the opposite gender.
    Purple is a random transformation color, with the transformations not being related to anything.
    Black will change someone into an inanimate object, or the reverse for an inanimate object.

    There were others, but were lost when the amulet was broken. Each buildup of power in the amulet takes roughly a day. The only way to get rid of the curse will be if all the colors have been used. During that period, the amulet will go dark, and you may put it somewhere or give it to someone. But, if you are touching the amulet after the one-day transfer period, you will have to keep it all over again. While the amulet is going through its color cycle, you will be trapped in the same day until all the colors have been used, meaning that you cannot go forward in time. That is my extent of my knowledge on this Amulet.
