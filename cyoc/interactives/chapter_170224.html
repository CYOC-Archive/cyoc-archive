    Maid to Serve

    Rebecca decided that if she wanted to revert what Olivia had accidentally changed, she needed to take a photo of both herself and her sister. It’d be less suspicious that way, rather than taking a photo of just Olivia. Rebecca knew it wasn’t ideal as she’d be changing herself, so she’d have to remember to change back to her sexy 28 year old self afterwards.

    “Hey Liv? Do you mind taking a picture with me?” Rebecca asked her sister, doing her best to not sound too suspicious.

    “Oh, with this?” Olivia responded, pointing to the camera on the table beside her. “Yeah I’d love to! It’s been so long since we’ve taken one of us together.”

    Rebecca reached for the camera and positioned it so the two were in frame. “Say cheese, girl!” She instructed.

    “Cheese!” Olivia smiled back.

    FLASH

    Oliver blinked as he recovered from the sudden flash. “For the last time Rebecca. Don’t take our picture without asking first!” He seethed at his maid.

    “I’m very sorry sir.” Rebecca apologised back. She hated disobeying her master, but at least she, Oliver and his wife Emily were back to their normal selves.

    “Relax babe. She’s been extensively cleaning all day. Let her have a little fun for once.” Emily said to her (new) husband, now completely unaware of the camera’s powers.

    “You’re right. I’m sorry Rebecca, but I do wish you’d have asked beforehand.” Oliver told Rebecca.

    “Of course. It won’t happen again.” His maid responded.

    “Now then Rebecca. Would you be so kind as to excuse us? Me and my lovely wife would love to have some... alone time by the pool.”

    “Oooh. I like where this is going.” Purred Emily, embracing Oliver.

    “Of course sir.” Rebecca said as she smoothed out her maid dress.

    “Oh! And Rebecca? Could you put that camera and this… doll on the ground away in the house?” Emily asked, picking up the toy off the ground.

    “As you wish madam.” She grabbed the two items and hurried off to the house, leaving the two lovebirds alone.

    Rebecca was proud of herself. She had successfully fixed the changes caused by the camera so now Oliver and Emily were back to being the 35 year old husband and wife couple Rebecca knew them as. And best of all, she somehow went unchanged, remaining as the 23 year old maid of the couple.

    Rebecca placed the camera on the counter inside, only for it to start producing a photo. She watched on in anticipation, only to be shocked to find out who was in it.

    “W-what!?” Was the only word Rebecca managed to form as her mind began to race. If the photo was of the three of them, then that means they’ve changed! But Rebecca thought she had fixed everything. Who was she meant to be before? Who were Oliver and Emily meant to be before? Rebecca remembered her childhood, when she was first hired by Oliver and the multiple hours she put into taking care of his house. Was all of that a lie?

    Rebecca thought about taking a photo of herself then and there, but then she thought about Oliver and Emily. Those two deserved to be changed first as they were her employers, but she couldn’t disobey their request to not take anymore photos of them. Maybe she should at least tell them about the camera. Or maybe she should get rid of the camera all together. Everyone seemed to be happy, but could Rebecca live with herself, knowing her life is a lie?

    What does she choose to do?
