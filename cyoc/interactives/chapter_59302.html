    Alexis' Choice of Balloon

    Needing a change of subject from his sudden growth of a furry plush bunny tail, Jon looks over at his sweetheart and asked, "Alexis, what balloon did you choose?"

    Alexis grinned, giving her cheeks two cute dimples. Jon felt his heart race at the sight and had the urge to be cuddled by her. He wasn't always the best boyfriend for cuddling and hugging, but the draw to be held in her arms, stroking his soft fur. Fur? His body! He wasn't a bunny, but a man! He cautiously reached a hand back and pressed a tentative hand to his tail. It collapsed easily, as you'd expect a plush animal to do. It was so soft and had deep think fur. Moving his hand around, he could feel fur extending from the tail outward. What would it feel like for Alexis to run her fingers over that, Jon wondered.

    "Well!" Alexis exclaimed, bring Jon out of his unintended drifting of thought. Alexis' previous eagerness for her balloon had returned and she gleefully hugged Jon and pulled her choice of balloon down from it's lofty height so that they both could see what it was. She tugged the piece of overly inflated latex playfully down.

    "So, I got a," Alexis said, smiling.
