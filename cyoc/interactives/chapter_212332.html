When Dan reaches the driveway of the house, he takes out his phone and opens up an App, where he stored all of the operation codes from the house and “car”. The teen grins to himself as he selects the code to open the garage, “let’s see if Jeff is doing fine,” he says to himself, he also deactivates the house’s security system with his phone. The boy chuckles, “it feels so awesome to have such unlimited power, not only over someone, but also over SOMETHING!”

    Dan opens the door, that connects the garage to the main house, and steps inside, he walks up the stairs to the first floor. Using the flashlight of his phone, he scans the doors, he smiles, when he notices that the doors have signs on them with the names of the occupants, “this makes it easy to find Jeff,” the boy whispers to himself. Moving his flashlight over the doors, he soon finds a door with a sign spelling Jeff’s name, which is between a door with the name “Ben” on the left, and a door with “Greg” on the right. Dan grins, “when I am done with Jeff, I might have some fun with either Ben or Greg… or maybe I’ll have time to have some fun with both.”

    Dan quietly opens the door to Jeff’s room and slips inside, he carefully moves through the room and finds Jeff lying in bed. The 15-year-old grins down at the two year older teen, ‘first I need to get him into the garage,’ the boy thinks to himself, ‘a small command will do the trick. Jeff won’t notice a thing and be unaware the whole time, like as if he were sleepwalking.’

    The teen opens the Chronivac App and selects Jeff’s file, he then opens the “command” tab and writes “head to the garage”, then he confirms it.

    Immediately Jeff slowly sits up in bed, his eyes are open, but he seems to not notice what is going on, Dan notices that Jeff’s eyes have a blank look. Jeff slowly and quietly gets out of bed and, without bumping into things and without tripping, heads out of his room and down the stairs, heading straight for the garage.

    Dan follows close behind, the 15-year-old grinning to himself, ‘hehe… he acts like a mindless puppet,’ he thinks to himself. They soon arrive in the garage, Jeff stops in the middle of the garage, facing the doors, which Dan had closed before entering the house. In the light of the garage, Dan’s eyes widen and he lets out a silent laugh, “haha… he looks so silly with that blue skin,” he says, “I guess the “paint” of the car has become his skin. It will be interesting to see what else will change about Jeff the more Greg and his class “upgrade” the “Jeep”.”

    The teen then opens the Chronivac App on his phone, “time for you to become the Jeep again, Jeff,” he says, “I have something I need to do.” Dan activates Jeff’s transformation into the Jeep and watches in amazement the process occur. First, Jeff’s cock and balls fall off of the boy’s body, landing with a metallic clatter on the floor as they become the keys, which Dan, after he laughed, picks up and stuffs them into his pocket, patting it through the jeans.

    After a few minutes Jeff is no more and the blue Jeep Wrangler is standing motionless in the garage. Dan opens the driver’s door and gets in, “now to do what I came here for,” he says, not wanting Jeff to awake, he uses his “code App” on his phone to boot up the Jeep’s computer. Dan first deletes the Chronivac from the computer, normally this would course the Chronivac on Jeff’s computer, Greg’s and Dan’s phone to lose connection to Jeff, but Dan added a function which would cause the Chronivacs to still be able to transform Jeff into a Jeep and human. In other words: Dan has just simply deleted Jeff’s knowledge of the Chronivac, which in turn causes Jeff to forget being transformed into a car, in other words: the "deal" he previously made with his dad and Greg has been erased from Jeff's mind.

    Dan then checks how much Jeff enjoys being a car, he notices that the percentage bar is at 30%, the teen grins, “I wonder what will happen if I set the percentage to zero! I assume Jeff will constantly whine about being a car, and how much he hates it. This will cause Greg to start become annoyed and maybe even consider to have the “radio” fixed or the system checked to have the “error” erased and silence Jeff for good, or at least turn Jeff’s voice into an AI-System with just a limit of words. Hehe… that’ll be fun to see!”

    The teen gets out off the Jeep, closes the door and lies the keys next to the car, he then transforms Jeff back into a blue human and has him put his Pajamas back on. “Hm… there must be away to fix this,” Dan mumbles, “it’s a waste of time if Greg has to wait for Jeff to undress before becoming the Jeep. Maybe there is a way that would cause what ever Jeff wears to stay intact when Jeff transforms into the car, maybe by becoming a PART of the car, like covers for the seats. I need to talk to Greg about that.”

    Dan then sends Jeff back to bed, “now, what should I do,” he asks himself, “I could leave, since my job here is done… or I could have some fun with Greg and/or Ben… hmm… What should I do?”
