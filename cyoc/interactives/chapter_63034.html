    adorable with these."

    something that would befit a faun's traditional image. She increased the amount of freckles on her arms and
    upper chest. Then she pondered the weight slider. She shrugged and clicked it, nudging it up a few pounds.
    Fauns were usually seen to be very childish in spirit and in form, so she figured a little baby fat would add
    to the perception.
