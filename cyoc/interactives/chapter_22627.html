    Karissa glared into the depths of the amulet, her desire born of anger and hate, and disgust and revulsion of her own transformation and the revelation of it being Mitch's desire. Suddenly, and unexpectedly for Karissa, the amulet glowed a malevolent red. It had granted Karissa's dark wish.

    Mitch wasn't liking this. His dream, a dream he had never thought possible had happened. But, just as things were looking to be perfect, the carpet was pulled from out from under him. He didn't like the look in Karissa's eyes. Once the amulet glowed, he knew he was in trouble.

    The feeling itching all over his body made Mitch try to scratch everywhere all at once. Quickly, he saw that he was growing fur. Soft brown fur! It grew in thick and full, and covered everything. Mitch then felt his ears lengthened and his face push outward. A donkey! He had to be turning into a donkey! Well, Karissa sure wasn't getting revenge on him, he loved donkeys and why not be one? Relieve flooded over Mitch as he continued his change into a full donkey. He swelled in size, his arms turned into legs and he grew a tail. In no time flat, a large donkey stood and brayed happily at Karissa. His revenge was Mitch's desire all along.

    Karissa had been watching Mitch's transformation, and was happy to see that he had enjoyed it. The two met eye to eye. A dark smile crept over Karissa's slightly larger mouth. Mitch suddenly felt something was not right. That smile, what was she up to?

    The transformation wasn't complete yet. Mitch felt again a tingling all over him, it felt like his muscles were weakening. He had a hard time mustering the strength to move any part of him. His body stiffened and his legs straightened. He felt full, stuffed and unable to move.

    Karissa moved over to Mitch and ran a hand over Mitch's fluffy fur. "Ah, now that's better, I've always loved getting new stuffed animals!" Karissa laughed and patted Mitch on his rump.
