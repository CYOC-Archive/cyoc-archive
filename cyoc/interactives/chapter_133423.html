I want to become someone else next" Before Ian could stop him he grabbed the medallion and picked up a pair of socks which looked toover familiar to Ian. Kyle grew as he became a copy of your previous body.

    The whole time the three of you have changed the car had been parked in front of your car. "I'm getting some clothes" Kyle got out of the car and walked over to your car. "Stop walking naked in my body" Ian shouted. "Now you know how I feel" Brad said. He was the only one still dressed but his old clothes looked extremely baggy on Kyles body. Easy as pie" Brad eyes widened. "I hope so because this doesn't look so good"

"Sir, can I see your license " Ian reached into his pockets before remembering that he never had Brads license.
