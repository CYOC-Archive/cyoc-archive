    "Not necessarily," Kenzia said with a smirk. "After all, we need to appear as human before we are ready to reveal ourselves. So, with the proper illusion magic..."

    She snapped her fingers and her body changed to her old boring human self dressed in a bikini. She gave them a smirk and shook her head. "So boring... I don't like being human again. But we need to keep up the illusion for a little while at least."

    "I look forward to the day when we can walk openly as we truly are," Alisariah said with a smile. "When Elfkind rules the world once and for all."

    "Then let's make sure that day comes soon," Maraya said as she leaned in and kissed both of them. "How long until she get's here?"

    "About an hour."

    "Then, let's enjoy the hour we do have," Kenzia said as she let her illusion fade away. "And then, we need to plan this."

    "Oh I've already got ideas," Alisariah said with a grin.

    There was a ding as the elevator reached the fourth floor of the apartment complex and opened up. A young woman with dyed blue hair tied into a ponytail and pale skin stepped out. She was dressed in jeans and a t-shirt with a pair of d20s with the number 20 on it over her breasts saying "Yes, they're natural." She walked down the hall past two women who were making out, they barely noticed as she walked past, but she swore one of them looked at her.

    She knocked on the door to Alice's room and waited for a moment before the door opened and her friend greeted her with a smile. "Tara, I'm glad you could make it."

    "Well of course," Tara said with a smile as she patted her backpack. "You wanted to talk to me about an idea for your character arc now that we're getting into it?"

    "Oh yes, come on inside and I'll tell you all about them," Alice said as she gestured for Tara to follow her. She sat down on a chair while Tara sat on the couch, and started to take out her DM notes.

    "So, we agreed that Evenida is one of the last survivors of an old Elven Empire that collapsed recently," Tara said a she read over her notes. "Which is why this upcoming arc will be about another survivor's attempts to recreate the Empire through questionable means. I was thinking that..." she started to say when she glanced over at the desk, spotting a picture of an Elven Sorceress who was dressed in clothes that barely covered her body. "Uh... that she could... possibly be connected to the... actual BBEG of the campaign somehow."

    "Mmm, I had a much better idea," Alice said leaning forward a little, dropping the illusion just enough to enhance her cleavage. "What if ... Evenida decided the Sorceress was actually right?"

    "Excuse me?"

    "Well, I was thinking, the Sorceress doesn't just want power, she wants to create more High Elves to repopulate them after the war," Alice said, allowing her enchantment magic to radiate off of her, beginning to afect Tara's mind a little. "Evenida lost her wife and daughter to the invaders that destroyed her home. She would see this as a way to bring back what she lost, and perhaps get a bit of revenge for them."

    "That's not exactly..." Tara said, her eyes falling on Alice's cleavage... that was odd, she didn't remember her showing that much before. "I mean... that would probably put you at odds with the rest of the party..."

    "What if we convinced them to help?"

    "It's not impossible, but the Sorceress wouldn't allow non-Elves... unless you're suggesting..."
