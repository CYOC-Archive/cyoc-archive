    "OH... SH.. SHUT UP!" yelled Benjamin, throwing a pack of sunflower seeds from the back as Anthony laughed. "Oh yeah i forgot! Anthony responded, tossing a can of soda at each of us. "Just don't spill it like a dumbass inside my ride ya hear! As long as you treat it all well, i'll be happy to drive you dumb lot anywhere!" teased Anthony as the roguish but friendly maverick teased the reserved, but simplehearted Benjamin.

    Benjamin looked out the window at the scenery, enjoying the smooth summer breeze blowing through his hair and across his forehead. He closed his eyes and let his head fall against the window, as he began to ponder over the possibilities of what may happen. He opened his eyes again, and saw that the scenery had changed from green trees to a desert. He marveled out as he looked out the window, middle class, often prone to staying in hotels, yet it seemed this was his first time doing a roadtrip.

    The scenery changed from bright green evergreen woods, Deserts, hot sand and dunes, cacti, rocks, and hillsides.. Earthy Mountains, Bright blue springs.. River Forests and tapering highways. He could see a large mountain range in the distance.. A dusty town stood in front of him.. It seemed desolate and dry, and reminded him of the Mojave desert.

    "So.. Where is Yellowstone.. Anyways?" asked Benjamin.

    "Yeah, uhm, well we're headed towards Wyoming, where we can meet Yellowstone. I guess its mostly known for its active Geysers, where semi active underground volcano faults heat up water to a boil and blast it up to 100 feet high at a place called Old Faithful! There's lots of wooden walk boards we can see, and the place you can feel your bones blasted by hot, warm fresh steam, but it also has a lot of forest with untouched animals, some of the last recovering bison in America, as well as wolves, cougars, and even a few bears! Though you probably won't see much of it yet because its far away from civilization." answered Anthony.

    "Yeah! And if we get bored, there's a place called Grand Teton National park just a hour away from Yellowstone, responds Charles. "It Sounds like a blast!"


    "That sounds cool.." commented Benjamin, gazing at the flashing roads with fascination. "I've never seen a place so different from where I live."

    Anthony glanced back at the two, "If you guys aren't too tired, you can come along with us! In fact, you can join me and Charles if you want Ben! And don't forget, we still have the two others with us, don't worry!
