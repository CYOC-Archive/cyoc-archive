    She stumbled to regain her footing, managing to lean herself against the table. She rummaged quickly through the vials until she found the Human vial. She quickly downed the entire serum.

    After what felt like half a lifetime, the pressure from her udder began to subside. Her fur retreated into her skin, her ears returned to normal, and her teats shrunk back into her body. However, she was still left with a body full of milk. She realized there was only one way to get rid of it.

    Several minutes in the bathroom were all she needed to dispel the gallons of liquid she had magically accumulated. Once she had finished, she returned to the box of vials, picked out the cow one, and threw it into the trash.

    "I think I've had all the milk I can take for now," she said to herself. But this little incident had failed to ease her curiosity of some of the other forms the vials had to offer...
