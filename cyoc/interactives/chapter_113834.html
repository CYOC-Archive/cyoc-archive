    "We came to see you perform." Said Jessi's father as she reached up and hugged his daughter. "Now come on show us around and introduce us to that young man who answered the door."

    Bubblelulu walked to JoJos trailer, he himself was also a parent who had a young son about Amy-belles age, and who was also a clown, but he wasn't a member of the circus.

    JoJo himself was a whiteface clown with a full head of green hair, a large red nose, very skinny, and had a reputation to trip over himself a lot, even by clown standards.

    Bubblelulu walked to the trailer door, knocked, and out came JoJo wearing a a multicolored clown suit with a large bow tie, and baggy pants held up by suspenders.

    "Hello JoJo, you look very nice." Bubblelulu said as he walked out and closed the door. "So do you Bubblelulu, now shall we make our way to dinner." JoJo said as he held out his arm in a gentlemen like way, Bubblelulu took it and they walked to the supper tent.

    "I called in a favor with a friend of mine who is one of the cooks, he got us a table reserved and everything."

    "Well that is awfully nice of you JoJo." Bubblelulu said as she honked JoJo's nose.

    Soon after they arrived at their table, whomever JoJos friend was he put in some good effort to make this first date good. JoJo pulled out the seat for her like a gentlemen, and she sat down. They ordered and they had a nice time.

    JoJo was a good conversationalist. He told her about his life prior to the circus originally he and his wife met together at college together, they were studying business and when they got out they just so happens to be working together. As time went on and they had successful careers JoJos wife died tragically in the crossfires of a shooting. After which JoJo didn't think he would be happy again until he and his son went to their first clown camp and he there found his passion. About a year ago he joined the circus full time having enough money for his son in the future and had been with the circus ever since.

    Bubblelulu told her story of how she had convinced her son to take up closing, and then it looked so fun both she and Amybelle joined in the fun. Soon after thy heard about this circus who was always accepting new members, and next thing you know they were performing in the center ring of the circus.

    She had her suspicions of JoJo being a real clown like her, but she wasn't sure just yet. Just about then dessert came, the dessert was the same for every performer in the circus, tonight it was custard pies, piled very high up. They were just about to
    dig in when a waiter passed by and spilled some pepper on JoJo by mistake. It took him a moment to realize it, but soon his red nose itched, and then a huff, and a puff, and he sneezed comically face first into his custard pie.

    Everyone turned to look, and snickered some laughs. Bubblelulu herself put her hand over her mouth to hide the grin that was spreading across her face. JoJo pulled his face out of his pastry revealed by a custard covered face. He wiped away the cream from his eyes and looked quite embarrassed, he could feel his cheeks turning red underneath the custard.

    Bubblelulu looked at JoJo, even before she joined the circus she loved slapstick acts and it was one of the reasons why she choose to make Jeefo a clown in the first place. Something occurred to her. She looked down at her own Pie, then back at JoJo, and then back at her pie again. She considered it, and then face planted into her own pie. She felt the cool custard engulf her face and get into her hair. She could help but laugh inside. She pulled her face out of her plate, revealing her covered face.

    As she wiped away the pastry she and JoJo couldn't help but laugh at all of this.

    They were still laughing as they left the supper tent, after tipping their waiter and heading back to JoJos trailer to get cleaned up for the movie. He opened the door and the two of them walked in.
