    No way I can catch Pinky if I'm so small, Kelly thought. The only way I can keep up is to grow and be that much bigger than Pinky. I just hope my clothes grow too.

    Kelly used the gun on herself for a short time and noticed her clothes growing with her. Throwing caution to the wind she held the trigger until she felt she was big enough, stopping at about six and a half feet tall. She then ran in the direction of Pinky, unaware of the onlookers who watched the dog grow in the first place.

    When she finally found Pinky, Kelly set the gun to shrink and returned Pinky to normal size. Kelly then thought about her next move.
