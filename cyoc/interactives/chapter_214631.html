    "Nap, huh? Can you check how long of a nap is needed for this thing to work?" Jase asked.

    "Lemme see," Danny replied before grabbing the instructions that came with the Pubertaway.

    "Okay, you're not actually thinking about using this, right? Didn't you just say you wanted to keep your balls dropped?" Scottie asked, visibly objecting to having his other friend on the wrong side of puberty.

    "I did, but look at Danny. He clearly likes being a kid again," Jase explained, "If you ask me, I'd say give it a shot."

    Just as Scottie was about to provide some counterarguments, Danny found the page of the instructions that talks about sleep.

    "Jase, the good news is that we don't need to wait until nighttime to use it. The bad news is that you gotta sleep for at least an hour for it to work," Danny said while pointing to the page he was on.

    "Meh. I'm not worried about that, man," Jase replied, "We still have the entire day. Plus, we've already said we're crashing here for the weekend, so there's no rush."

    "So, does that mean you wanna use it?" Danny asked enthusiastically.

    "Sure, I'll use it. Seeing you like this makes being a shrimp again not sound too bad," Jase said, ruffling Danny's hair following the response, "What about you, Scottie?"

    "I'll pass on this one, guys," Scottie answered, "I'd rather be on THIS side of puberty, thank you very much."

    "Don't knock 'till ya try it, Scottie," Danny said, "I was nervous when I used it last night, but it worked like a charm. Besides, it'll be like old times, like when we were really in middle school."

    "Please, Scottie? It'll be more fun if all three of us were 11 again."

    Scottie was sorely tempted to join Danny on the wrong side of puberty, just as Jase was about to do. He hated disappointing his friends despite his reservations.

    "Alright, alright, but only because I'll feel like a babysitter otherwise." Scottie rolled his eyes, exasperated.

    The trio then headed to Danny's bed. Due to his reduced size, Danny's mom made the bed for him. Laid down on the bed, Jase set his alarm to go off one hour after his nap started. He made sure to leave some room for Scottie. Danny examined the settings to make sure that Jase and Scottie would end up the same as him. In other words, Mode 3 with Gender Bender off. Standing by the two friends, Scottie noticed a plastic container on Danny's desk. The container had a label sticker that says 'DANNY'. Scottie figured that Danny had written his name given that it looked like it was written by an 11-year-old.

    "Hey Danny," Scottie said, "What's that stuff inside the plastic container, and why is your name on it?"

    "That, Scottie, is my maturity. All of this was sucked outta me last night," Danny said while pointing at the liquid inside the container, "The instructions said I should keep it inside a container and out of the device."

    "If that's your maturity, then how do ya plan on getting it back in you? I mean, we can't be preteens forever."

    "Well, even if I wanna grow back up right now, I gotta wait until tomorrow. It'll turn into a gel, and I can shower with this on my body," Danny said, "But like I said before, I can turn back any time."

    "Guys, can ya hurry it up, please?" Jase asked impatiently, "I'd like for my and Scottie's maturity to be sucked out before Danny's balls drop again!"

    "Don't remind me, Jase! Why do we have to share the bed anyway? We probably won't even fit on there," Scottie said.

    "We will in an hour, Scottie."

    "Guys! Can we at least save it for after you two are the same age as me?" Danny asked as he was about to turn the device on. Jase and Scottie apologized before Danny turned to the device.

    "It might take a few minutes to take in, but don't feel bad if nothing happens right away."

    Danny left his napping friends in the bedroom, knowing that they were about to be stripped of their adolescence like him. To pass the time, Danny turned his PS5 on, and decided to play online once more. He was curious as to how others would react upon hearing his younger voice. Unsurprisingly, his other friends reacted in shock.

    "Holy shit, Danny! You really ARE a kid now!"

    "Are you sure you're old enough to be playing online, Danny?"

    "When Jase said you got all of your puberty sucked outta ya, he wasn't lying!"

    "Your balls went back up, didn't they?"

    "You gotta be like, what? 8? 9?"

    "Go easy on the kid, guys. Wouldn't wanna hurt little Danny's feelings, would ya?"

    Comments like these did not get to Danny. If anything, he liked them. He was being treated as if he actually was a kid by his other friends and online strangers. He made a note to keep the noise down because of Jase and Scottie.

    As soon as the hour was finished, Danny returned to his room. With the door closed, Danny remembered that his friends were about to wake up from their nap. Creaking open the door, Danny saw what Jase and Scottie had become.
