    Dave was surfing on the internet when he found a strange spell website, it had a spell for everything, from bringing love, making money and alchemy to, how to make a new body, beginners guide to black magic etc.
    One itemin the extensive list got Dave's attention, listed within "Grey Magic" was an "Emotion Inducing Spell", apparently, with a few words and some concentration, the mage would be able to induce a wide array of different sensations, feelings, emotion, that went from birth-pain to oblivious-happiness, Dave was never the guy that looked into this stuff, and he was originally looking for a way to get girls, "magic could do it" he thought to himself when he saw the results of the search he did for "Get Girls", back to the emotion inducing spell, it didn't require deep knowledge for it to work, it simply required the mage to know and have met face-to-face the person he/she wanted to cast the spell on.
    After reading it, which was written in very strange language, but somehow Dave managed to read it out loud and he tried on himself for "raw peace", as the calmness overcame him, he was sure the spell would work, his first target would be a girl from his school he always had a crush on, Carol, she was 20, black, long hair, about C-Cup breasts, and a nice bubble butt, Dave never advanced on her before, because she already had a boyfriend, thus she refused his attempts on a date, as it was a known fact that Dave liked her.
    The spell would be able to make her like him? the page didn't said of any limits besides to take it slow and be mindful when using it, if strong enough, the target could change their personalities and/or bodies to match their new minds.
    The spell itself could be read out fast, in about 40 seconds and not necessarily loud, just enough so the mage could hear, it was just words, the mage would concentrate on the target, which would need to be within his line of sight, then he would focus on a sensation and it would manifest on that person in strength proportional to the mage's will.
    The next morning, Dave woke up as the sound of his coffee pot announced it was ready, the nice smell helped wake him up, then he saw himself on the mirror, still doubting the spell effectiveness he decided to try on himself again, he concentrated on himself as he spoke the magic words and concentrated on "faith", just pure "faith" on himself, when he finished chanting the words he felt for sure now that the spell would work, he also got a lot of self-confidence and put on a black jacket, rather than his usual green creeper T-Shirt, he happily took a mug of coffee, some bread and went to college, he lived very close to it, it was only 4 minutes of walk from his apartment, Dave got in, greeted his friends, but choose to remain alone and look for Carol, she was attending to physiotherapy, Dave located her class, it was at the 3rd floor of the building, he figured it would be best to intercept her on ground level, so he stood there, at the entrance, shifting his weight between his legs, it seems the faith effect faded, it was in effect for about only 15m, "perhaps if i try again and use on her, it would last longer in her?", still in the entrance he cast on himself another faith inducing spell, as it took effect all his anxiety was being replaced by confidence and he stopped to feeling so nervous, this time he was also believing in magic in it's power and relation to all living beings, to a level where he could see faint purple/blue/pink clouds of smoke through the buildings following people around, he was a little scared of that, but he was sure the things had nothing to do with him.
