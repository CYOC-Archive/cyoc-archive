    "Hello!" The disembodied head said. "I'm Tanya the Dullahan. Welcome to the Monster Girl Cafe's special Full Immersion experience!" She spoke with a chipper voice.

    "What makes it special?" Lydia, your girlfriend asked.

    "Well each Full Immersion Experience is unique to the attraction it is set up by. So this one will specifically turn anyone who enters it into a monster girl."

    "Into a monster girl?" Lydia asked. Slowly she turned and looked at you. "Anyone?" She said with a smirk.

    "That's right!" Tanya nearly shouted. "Even guys can have the full experience of being a monster girl. This includes working at our Cafe"

    You spoke up. "You have to work at the Cafe if you use the Full Immersion booth?"

    "No, but it is recommended to get the full experience. So what do you say? Want to become a monster girl?"

    You were a bit curious. Tanya spoke as if this thing would actually transform you, and you wanted to see how the trick would work.
