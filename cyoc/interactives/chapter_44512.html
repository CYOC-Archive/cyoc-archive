    Absorbing More Muscle

    Curtis was feeling great! His muscles were bigger and he could just feel his increased strength coursing through him.

    He walked by a gym and decided to take a look and check out the membership plans. As soon as he walked in the door, a big, young muscular guy walked up to him.

    "Can I help you?" the trainer asked.

    "Yeah... My name is Curtis... Are you taking new members?"

    "No, not until the fall semester starts, but I can take your name. My name is Steve..."

    Steve extended his hand and Curtis did too. The moment they shook hands, something weird started to happen.

    Suddenly, Steve started to shrink rapidly! He began to dwindle rapidly! He couldn't move at all, and his hand became attached to Curtis' hand! He was stuck as his body dwindled rapidly!

    Within moments, Steve shrunk down from being 6 foot 6 inches and almost 320 pounds of ripped muscle, until he completely disappeared from view!

    "Whoa! What happened?" Curtis wondered out loud.

    Then, Curtis was overcome by a soft rumbling sound that came from deep within his body. His body became heavier, as his muscles started to grow once again!

    Very quickly, his arms bulged out even more, and his shoulders just pushed out forward and sideways, making him incredibly wide for a teenager.

    Curtis' biceps inflated at the same time, until they were as round and as hard as basketballs! His legs became like columns of the state museum, ripped with layers upon layers of thick, hard muscle, which would make him so strong, that he could run like the wind and leap over a small building!

    His calves started to push out, until they became freakishly huge. They were hard and big and round, with large, thick veins covering them. They were screaming with raw power!

    Curtis' abs just popped out, as his torso widened even more. His chest became a wall of muscle, with mighty pectorals sticking out like two giant spheres of rock solid beach balls of might and titanic power.

    "Arrgggggg" he moaned, as his body filled up with outrageous muscles, as he felt his strength triple in a matter of seconds. He was taking in all of Steve's muscles!

    Finally, the growth stopped. Curtis was left sweating and panting, and gasping, when the freakish-looking muscle boy turned and stared at his reflection in the large mirror.

    "Holy crap!" Curtis shouted.

    The wooden floor creaked as the mighty muscle-boy slowly walked closer to the mirror.

    Curtis couldn't even get closer to the mirror, because his huge pectorals were sticking out too far in front of him. He was so muscular, he had trouble walking.

    Then, he heard a familiar voice coming from another part of his body.

    “Where… Where am I?” Steve said.

    Curtis looked down and saw Steve’s face on his right bicep.

    “Steve! You’re in my body! You… You’re one of my muscles now…” Curtis said calmly. “I don’t know how this happened, but…”

    “You took my muscles! My body! You stole my body!” Steve cried.

    “I can’t help it! Really! You’re inside of my body now!” Curtis pleaded with him.

    “Welcome to the family, Steve…” Carter chuckled, speaking from Curtis’ left bicep.

    “Yeah! You get used to being Curtis’ muscles! It’s fun!” Ryan added, from Curtis’ left pectoral.

    Curtis mouth dropped wide open.

    “This is weird. My muscles are talking to me, and they have names… Three people are living inside of my muscles… I gotta get used to this!”

    “Well, make yourself at home. We get to play with Curtis’ muscles!” Carter laughed.

    “Dude… We ARE Curtis’ muscles!” Ryan corrected.

    Curtis turned around and walked out the door, but not without pulling the door to the gym off its hinges. He didn’t have time to adjust to his incredible strength yet… He was walking around with 400 pounds of muscle…
