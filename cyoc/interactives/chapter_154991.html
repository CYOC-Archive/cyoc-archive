Jeff looked down at his feet and decided that having bigger feet would make him more masculine. With his new memories, he thought of possible candidates for the swap before deciding on John's old friend Dave, who was now his old friend instead. Dave's full head of hair caught Jeff's eye and Jeff rubbed the back of his head enviously and thought to swap Dave's full head of hair with his own. Jeff then opened up his laptop and started selecting the swaps that he desired, the time it would take to change, the length of the change and awareness settings before confirming the changes. Jeff quickly took off his shoes and socks and saw his feet slowly but surely expand. Next, he held his cell phone's camera in front of him to see his head and was greeted by thick healthy hair. Dave then grabbed the shoes, put them in a plastic bag and gave them to Jeff, whose feet definitely fit the shoes.
