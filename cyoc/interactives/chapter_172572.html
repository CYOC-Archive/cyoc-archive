Everything was made for a toddler. Toys scattered everywhere, a changing table, action figures high on a shelf with a tv below it. His own bed became one with a spaceship design.

    The man in front of him was different. A man with a receding hairline, unshaded mustache and beard, a beer belly and was completely naked except for the classic white briefs.

    The man scooped Jonathan up and placed him on the changing table. He helped take off his onesie and let it fall to his ankles. Both Jonathan and the man saw how yellow his diaper was.

    “Did my little have a flood? Maybe we don’t need anymore milk before bed now do we?” He removed Jonathan’s diaper and cleaned him up. He searched through a drawer and pulled out two different diapers.

    “Are we going to wear a pull-up or a diaper today?” He asked.

    Jonathan laid on his back remembering that he is four, so he probably didn’t get potty trained fully.

    “Diapee!” Jonathan cheerfully said as he pointed to the diaper. The man chucked and changed him into a diaper. After that, he lifted Jonathan and placed him on the floor.

    “Daddy has something important to do today, so can you promise me to be a good boy today?”

    Jonathan nodded his head and gave his new father a smile.

    His father then gave him some clothes to wear and helped him into it. The jean overalls and white shirt helped Jonathan make sure he felt young again. He remembered the last time he wore overalls was at his grandmother’s house when he would help her in the garden. Just like how it is now, he was wearing a diaper under the overalls.

    After that, Jonathan was left in his room while his father left somewhere. He followed right behind him and managed to go unnoticed.

    In his father’s room, he watched as his father changed from his tightly whities to a pair of white boxer briefs. It was unclear what the important thing was, but Jonathan can tell it was work related based on the button up shirt and pants.

    “My little man will start school soon! Do you wanna try potty training?”
