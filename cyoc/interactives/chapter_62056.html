    "Thank you. I am in this cage for a long time. My name is Nathan, by the way.", said the squirrel and stepped out of the cage.
    "I am Gabriel, and I have no time for someone like you. I must find Tramorn and punish him for his crimes."
    "Gabriel, eh? Didn't sound like girl's name for me.", said Nathan.
    "I am no girl. I am Sir Gabriel, Paladin of the Holy Order, and to my shame Tramorn cursed me before he fled. So I have one reason more to hunt him down, so stay out of my way."
    "Hey, perhaps can I help you. You know talking squirrels aren't something you find in nature. I was once Tramorn apprentice, till I angered him and he cursed me into a squirrel."
    "You was his apprentice? Then you know something about magic?", asked Gabriel hopefully.
    "Yeah, but I can't perform magic in this form, otherwise I have freed myself a long time ago. But I have some knowlegde about spells and curses and the safest way to lift a curse is to kill the sorcerer he spelled it on you.", answered Nathan.
    "So I must still hunt him down and kill him." Gabriel took his sword and walked down the path.
    "Hey, wait for me I want to accompany you.", said Nathan and rushed up to Gabriel and crawled up his leg and on his shoulder.
    "Why? You are a squirrel how can you help me?"
    "I want Tramorn's death as much as you, for obvious reasons. And I have still arcane knowledge to support you."
