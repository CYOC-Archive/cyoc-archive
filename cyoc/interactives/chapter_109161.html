    “Liz Baker,” Lance answers; “she knows through Jack Crawford that you have a medical issue. She wouldn’t elaborate, but she does know that Jack Crawford is planning to force a vote of confidence at the next shareholders meeting.”
    Benton swore; “over my dead body. What’s Liz angle on this, why tell you?”

    “I can’t attend thanks to the council’s edict,” Benton feeling enraged; “Just fucking great!”
    “What if you changed the date of the meet,” Lance asks; “can you not do that?”
    “No, nor relocating it here” Benton thinking; “but perhaps I can have a proxy there in my place.”
    “Oh no,” Lance knowing full what Benton was thinking about; “I’m not heading to New York for you, I don’t even own stock.”
    “Frieda does thank to the trust you have kept safe;” Benton speaking.
    “She is still a minor,” Lance answering; ”and you are forgetting the fact that she has to remain in Moon Lake for her training to take over as were-panther rep in the elder council.”
    “Amanda would do it,” Benton thinking; “but she would probably get distracted shopping up and down Fifth Avenue.”
    “Her favorite street I take it,” Lance chuckling; “but getting back on track, I could talk to the council but I doubt they’ll change their mind in regard to letting you leave.” Lance thinking for a moment and sighing; “I might know someone who could help. But before I tell you Benton, you need to promise me you won’t flip out and go on a tear.”
    “Okay,” Benton answering; wondering who Lance might be asking about, “who did you have in mind?”

    Lance is silent while he contemplates the possible ramifications with what he is about to say.
    “Who did you have in mind Lance,” Benton getting impatient.
    “Far Houjin,” Lance answers; “he got a hold of me earlier today, wanting me to kill the story regarding him and his grandson’s disappearance.”
    “Well fuck me sideways,” Benton sounding surprised; “how did he do that?”
    “By E-mail,” Lance lying; “but he did contact me just before you called me in regards to John Shaffer.”
    “Well that explains why you were testy with me when I called,” Benton answering; “what did the man want from you?”
    “What I said,” Lance answering but omitting the real reason and the fact Far and Kai are in Moon Lake; “to kill my story. I told him no.”
