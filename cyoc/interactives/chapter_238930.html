    Mad Science

    Now Ken's feeling a little underpowered

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by TaleSpinning 7 hours ago A O

    At the start of their date it turned out Kate was... technically naked and her clothes were... her... well her nanobots that she could manipulate at will... Heck in an alley she formed a clump of nanobots and put it on the ground, and they absorbed air as they built themselves into a functioning mirror which she used to change her clothes to some sleek tank-top before the mirror evaporated as the nanomachines turned themselves back into air. She even... swelled a bit... Ken for his part made his clothes a bit nicer... he couldn't make himself buffer, taller, or a girl if they wanted to try a girl's night out since Christoph's trick made him completely immune to the remote... and more than that... Kate's power was integrated into herself and he felt like Kate was figuring out more and more of what it could potentially do. Practically making anything from nothing, turning garbage into nanomachines and then having the nanomachines turn into air... Making a duplicate of her self in everyway...
    Ken stared and asked "Ok... I turned away for a second... which of you is the real Kate?"
    The Kate's looked at each other and then Ken and one said "We both are... It's a little trippy, but... considering what I am... We're two units running off the same software. We both are me." The second replied "Don't you want to have a pretty girl on both sides of you? and be monogamous at the same time?~"...Yep that was something Ken couldn't do with the remote... really Ken's only strength with the remote was it had only a line of sight range... but Kate could let her nanomachines move around, and extend her range like... sentient fungal spores... How far was her connection range? One of the Kates vaporized and Ken thought... it was probably the original Kate... if it mattered

    Getting into the Zoo for their little day was easy as Ken could... have swapped them with people already in there, opened a gap in security, or change some people's minds but... Kate had spread her nanomachines out and effectively had full control of everyone and thing in the zoo... theoretically. She said she only had a single nanomachine in everyone and the connection was interesting, but of course that single nanomachine could turn into enough to completely control someone or an animal. So the next people in line paid for Ken and Kate, the animals got more active when they were around. They took a little gondola over the park and Ken asked "Couldn't you let me do some of the reality manipulation?"
    "What?" Kate asked
    "You've been on the draw to make things happen with your little nanomachine things."
    "Sorry I'm a bit quicker with my body then you are with your point and click remote, but I really need to figure out my whole soul thing..."
    "Soul?" Ken asked
    "I'm an inorganic being, My consciousness is a bunch of binary interacting with my soul... and... being able to shift my body so easily and... the way my soul interacts... It's all connected and it's weird that the connection from my souls are as thin as possible they aren't in danger of snapping or anything. It's like a perfect reception, can get stretched thing but it's all me." Ken didn't know what to say about that, and the rest of the date felt more like Kate messing with her powers while Ken got to watch. When he got the idea to try turning some monkies into people and see what happened... Nothing happened... "Sorry Ken... I think I must've influenced them too much or something and my immunity latched onto the monkeys... I can start detaching but it'll take awhile for the nanomachines to leave without leaving major gaps in their body right away." Kate explained. With a friend like her around... how much use was the remote even? When anything living probably had a nanomachine in it except for him... The trick may have taken some fun out of the remote, but it probably saved him from becoming part of the Kate borg... No he shouldn't describe his friend like that... He just felt a little salty. His birthday gave him a miraculous invention and then his best friend got a super upgrade... it probably had some sorta deal with the devil aspect but... Now that he thought about it his own remote had a similar aspect to it as well.

    The day was ending a bit so Ken said "How about we get something nice to eat? Perhaps we could find our way to a place with a several course meal or something." thinking about it he took Gerome out of his pocket and asked "Hey Gerome, can you find some fine dining experience that costs hundreds of thousands per meal?"
    Gerome said "Want me to set a travel destination for you?"
    Ken shrugged "Sure, thanks bud... want to become flesh and blood again?"
    Gerome said "Nah, having a LOT of fun here... Word of advice... try not to piss Penny off..." Ken nodded and saw the path.
    Ken said "So, We trusting Gerome with guiding us to our fancy dinner?"
    Kate said "Sure, should work well." and the couple headed out for their dinner that they'd probably hijack someone else to get
