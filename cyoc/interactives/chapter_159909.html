    Looking through the options to become a gaming console, Jeff thought of something unique. While video game consoles are unique to turn into and arcade cabinets are retro as it is, being a pinball machine is the most fun thing to be. Going to the shore close to home, Jeff does like to go to this pinball museum at the pier and play with the variety of pinball machines from past to present. Just the way the bells and whistles makes it exciting to score points. The way you target certain areas to get more points is very thrilling. And even the marques showing all the art of the pinball's theme. They are all quite artistic in a way. So if he turns into a pinball machine, he would be played all day, every day. Plus he's going to be on the whole time, meaning he'll be sexually aroused 24/7. So without thinking, he decided to become a pinball machine. But what to choose? That's a huge collection of them from past to present and he needs to pick one that can be the best played. So after much thinking, he knows what pinball machine he can be.
