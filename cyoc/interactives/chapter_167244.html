    Cura is really the Wicked Sea Witch of the Western Ocean

    added by Anonymous 3 years ago A S O

    They all soundly said "NO !!" and a furious Cura revealed her true
    identity as the
    Wicked Sea Witch of the Western Ocean.

    As the cackling sea hag swam away rapidly from the 5 horrified
    humans she waved her magical trident at her waiting troops
    behind some reefs and out came swarms of chittering wing sea monkeys
    who were intent on throttling the five humans before they got a
    chance to die by drowning.
    Now Colton was a minor wizrd apprentice,and as he and the others
    slowly sank to the ocean bed he had a last fit of magic inspiration.
    Glancing at the red Aztec dragon on the swimsuit of Lupe
    he uttered a spell and in a flash of magical energy Lupe transformed
    into a red Aztec dragon with golden feathers.

    There was the bit of startled bubbles coming from the
    snout of the Aztec dragoness that had been Lupe but the
    beast drove off the menacing sea monkeys then grabbed the other
    humans in her claws and desperately sought shelter.

    Lupe the Aztec Dragon quickly discovered that her dragon body
    had no buoyancy and so with her tail whipping her forward
    she looked for a other means to get the humans in
    her claws back into breathable air.
    Off in the distance she saw the glow of a sickly green light coming
    out of the mouth of a underwater cavern.
    With the humans in her claws she entered and rose higher by
    coiling her belly feathers and scutes along the rough stone
    walls of the tunnel-cave till she came to a huge air filled
    cavern.

    Lupe could already feel her body shrinking as the magic
    Colton the wizard in orange trunks had transformed her with
    ebbed away.
    She just managed to drag herself and the others onto
    a rocky ledge before with a final sucking sensation
    she reverted to being her human self again
    albeit a naked human self as her swimsuit had somehow
    gone AWOL.
