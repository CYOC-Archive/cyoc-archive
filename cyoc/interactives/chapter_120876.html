    Take them to Lachlan

    added by Drakkenfyre 6 years ago I

    "They look just like the boys gym uniforms," Braiden said, "But too big for me."

    "I know, we'll take them to Lachlan and ask him what to do," Maggie said decidedly.

    They picked up the shorts and hoodie, and practically ran out of the library, earning a glare from the head librarian as they exited.

    They caught up with Lachlan outside the athletic center. He was Maggie's older brother, and he would know what to do.

    "Lach!" shouted Maggie.

    "Huh? Wha?" he asked looking over his shoulder then checking the time on his phone and turning to face them.

    Maggie quickly explained what transpired with Brody and Callum. Lach kept checking his phone.

    Finally, he said, "Give me the shorts, hoodie and phone."

    "You have your own phone," Maggie said pointedly.

    "I think you switched with me. See I don't have a Pokemon App on my phone, but this phone does," he said turning the phone in his hand to face Maggie.

    "Oh, I guess they got switched," she handed her phone over along with the clothes, and took back her phone.

    The phones looked identical except for the Pokemon App. Lachlan was keying on his phone like made. Maggie figured he was checking her history to see if she'd seen any porn or such on his phone. If she had known it was his phone, she would have looked.

    "Now what about Brody and Callum?" Maggie demanded. He friend was nervous and quiet the whole time. Lachlan was a senior, almost all grown up, and a real big guy.

    "Huh? Oh, you changed them into this clothing with the app on my phone," Lachlan said matter of factly.

    "What?!" exclaimed both girls.

    Maggie said, "Well, turn them back!"

    "Can't now. Got gym. But no worries, you just forget about Callum and Brody, and the incident with the phone in the library," Lachlan said aiming the phone at them. This time there was a greenish blue flash. Drool trickled down Braiden's chin.

    "Hey, Maggie, Braiden, won't you two be late for class," Lachlan said.

    Maggie and Braiden blinked. How did they get out by the gym, they were headed to the library. Maggie glanced at her phone, and saw the time.

    "Oh, my, he's right. Come on, Braiden, we lost track of time. It's nearly one."

    "Huh? Weren't we supposed to meet someone else at the library?" Braiden said uncertainly.

    "Bye," said Lachlan. He turned carrying his new hoodie and shorts and entered the changing rooms.
