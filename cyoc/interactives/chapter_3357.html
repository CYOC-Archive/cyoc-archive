    Question #4- What Kind of dog breed do you want to be?

    You feel that being neutral toward how you feel toward strangers is just the right choice for you. You know from experience that there are certain people out there whom you can make friends with. And you know that there are people out there whom you just want to avoid.

    As you look at the next question given to you by the computer, you remain unaware of the fact that you are starting to bear a slight resemblance to a large, shaggy dog. Your ears have developed points and have started to inch their way up the sides of your head. Your hair has darkened from a shade of dark brown to a dull black. Your hair has also become thicker and unkempt and rugged-looking. If you were able to look into a mirror at this moment, you would see that your eyes are now a dark shade of nut-brown instead of their baby blues. Your eyebrows start to become bushy and lend a sad air to your eyes. As your nose continues to darken and take on a wet looking sheen, your nostrils begin to flare up and allow you to take in smells that you have never smelled before. The new smells in the air make you perk up and smile a toothy smile. Had you seen your smile, you would have seen that your eye teeth are now pointy and sharp.Your lips begin to darken and take on a ragged look. And as for that five o'clock shadow that was on your face eariler, it has now grown into a short but thick beard and mustache. And last of all, your muscular body has become very hairy. About three times the usual amount of body hair that you normally have now covers your body.

    But you are still too wrapped up in wanting to do this canine personality test. You look at the next question and see that it offers a lot more choices than the previous three did.

    Question #4- What kind of breed do you want to be as a dog?

    (A) "I would like to be a Herding dog so that I can get my friends to go where I want them to go."

    (B) "I would like to be a Hound dog so that I can chase and tree something."

    (C) "I would like to be a Non-sporting dog and become someone's pampered pet."

    (D) "I would like to be a Sporting dog and play a nice game of fetch with somebody."

    (E) "I would like to be a Terrier and go hunting after some vermin."

    (F) "I would like to be a cute, little Toy dog and be spoiled rotten with affection."

    (G) "I would like to be a working dog and work somewhere."

    You look at the choices again and think about your current job. You haven't exactly liked your current job with its' odd hours, its' demanding customers or its' staff of neer-do-well employees. But you don't dislike it either because of its' perks. If you were to leave your current job and go find a new one, what kind of job would you want to have? What kind of job would you want to have if you were a dog?

    After spending a couple of minutes pondering about where you stand job-wise, you make your choice. The woman's voice then asks you like before if this is the answer that you want to submit. You click on the Yes button and listen as the computer tells you that you picked...

    What do you do now?
