Alex first checks the systems on the magnetic chamber, then begins shutting down extra unnecessary equipment and instructs you to back up the contents of the doctor's computer to an optical disc in case something goes wrong. Everyone helps move the portable equipment and the nanobot cylinders outside the magnetically shielded lab, while Alex does her best to shield and protect the rest of the equipment. At last, everything seems ready. You step inside the magnetic chamber, followed by everyone else. Finally, Alex sets the timer on the chamber's main computer, grabs a wireless monitor, and steps inside.

    You wait a few minutes, with Alex intently watching the monitor and muttering things under her breath. "Okay, doing good, flux level up to 1.27, rising steadily, Delta at .0371, holding steady. Power set at 600 kilowatts peak, holding at 470... Oh, no. That can't be good!" She futilely presses a button on the monitor, then rushes over to the door, trying to force it open. "We have to get out, now!" You hear her mutter something about "600 megawatts."

    You all try your best to force out of the chamber, but nothing works. Helpless, you can only stand there as you hear the machine begin to hum.
