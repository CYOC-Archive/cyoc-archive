He shrank in height as well and he became 5 ft. 11 in. The baggy clothes he was wearing shifted themselves into clothes more suiting for his new age, 15.

He finally noticed his changes and his clothes shrank to fit his body too. His vision then became a bit blurry but this cleared up as a round eyeglass appeared on his nose.
