    Ric stared with total disgust at his younger brother, who had just
    shrunk back after hulking out for the first time.

    "Man, I can't believe you! How on earth can you cast such a stupid
    spell on yourself?" he sneered.

    "Oh, shut up, Ric! You're just jealous! you always want to be the big
    guy around here when I'm always the runt in the house!" Josh bellowed
    in reply.

    "Whatever, dude...."

    Just then, Mr. Mendes walked into the living room, where the boys
    where arguing.

    "What's the big deal with all the police cars being out on patrol
    today? They must be trying to raise money for the city again..."

    "Whoooooaaaaa" Josh gasped, as he felt every muscle in his body begin
    to inflate, thanks to the weird spell that he had cast on himself
    after reading the verse in the book of transformations.

    Within seconds, about one-hundred pounds of muscle was added to the
    boy's body. His arms thickened, his legs bulged, and his pecs forced
    out and strained his shirt until it ripped. Mighty abs formed on his
    torso and his entire upper body widened several inches. Josh became a
    teen muscle guy before Ric's very own eyes!

    "Ohh boy..." he sighed. "I'm gonna have to get used to this nonsense!"

    Josh just grinned. He was totally enjoying this....
