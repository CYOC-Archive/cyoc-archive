    "His name is Chip!"

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Zoran A year ago A AR S

    "Oh, of course," Karl says, he looks down at the little eight week old puppy who once was Chad, a human, the boy grins, "his name is Chip," he says.

    'CHIP?! WHY CHIP,' Chad shouts in his mind, 'th-that is to cute of a name'.

    The receptionist nods as she fills out a form, she then hands the form attached to a clipboard and a pen to Karl, "please take a seat in the waiting area and fill out this form," she says, "the vet will call for you in a few minutes".

    Karl nods and takes the form and walks over to the chairs, he sits down and a sad whimpering "Chip" lies down next to him. The teen ignores the whimpering "dog" and starts to fill out the form:

    Name of dog: Chip
    Age of dog: 8 weeks
    Gender of dog: male
    Breed of dog: Labrador Retriever
    Fur Color: black with a white patch on the chest
    Chipped: no
    Vaccinated: no
    Neutered/Fixxed: no
    Do you want your dog to be chipped: yes
    Do you want your dog to be vaccinated: yes
    Do you want your dog to be dewormed: yes
    Do you want your dog to be fixxed/neutered:______________

    Karl finishes filling out the form, "I'll leave the fixxed/neutered section blank," he mutters, looking down at the pup, "if you behave I might allow to keep your balls. I might even consider breeding you, but first we need to see how you develop and if you turn out to be not good for breeding...," he stops and grins down at "Chip" who lowers his head, his ears droop as he lets out a whimper.

    A while later a door opens and a nurse pears out, "next up: Karl and his dog Chip," she says.

    Karl stands up, "that's us," he says and walks towards the door, Chad/Chip tries to prevent from being taken in to the room, he lets out yips and struggles. Karl yanks at the leash, "CHIP, BEHAVE," he shouts, Chad whimpers and trotts forward, his tail between his legs, following Karl into the room.

    The nurse looks at the terrified looking dog, "aaaw... poor thing," she says, she kneels down and stretches out a hand, which has a doggy treat lying on the palm. "No need to be afraid," she continues, the smell of the treat starts to fill Chad's nose, it... it smelled really good, he slowly walks forward and sniffs at the treat, then he cautiously takes it from the woman's hand and eats it, his tail wags a little.

    Karl smirks, "there, that wasn't so bad now was it," he asks, "Chip" looks up at Karl with a "I hate you" look in his huge puppy eyes, which only made him look cute, causing Karl to chuckle.

    The woman stands back up, "put the pup on the table," she says, "the doc will be here in a minute".
