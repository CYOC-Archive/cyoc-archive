    While standing before his full-body mirror, Ty could only smile at his reflection. Moments earlier, he was the old Ty, short and scrawny. Now, he was the new Ty, taller and with some added muscle. At 6-foot-5 inches taller, he was even 5 inches taller than Dustin! The additional muscle made him look like a guy who worked out in the gym at least three times per week. What a difference a few minutes made already!

    So, Ty wondered what else needed changing? He looked at his old, worn t-shirt and his colored jeans. His shoes were also so worn, that the soles were starting to come apart. His stepparents had not taken him shopping at the mall in ages. They always took Dustin and passed on Dustin’s worn stuff to Ty. Ty never got anything that was new. It was time for Ty to get some new clothing, for a change!

    First, he had to clean up and fix up his bedroom. Ty looked around and saw his old, stained walls. There were no posters or anything. The door was broken, and the lock was removed. The old, wooden desk was falling apart. His chair was missing one of the armrests. Ty did not have a TV or a laptop, like Dustin did in his room. And he certainly didn’t have a video game console while Dustin had his own PlayStation.

    So, Ty wished for the walls in his bedroom to be painted a fresh, bright white colored paint, getting raid of the old, ugly wallpaper. Then, he wished for a new desk with a new office chair in the corner, to replace the old, broken ones. Finally, he wished for a new laptop on his desk with an additional 27-inch monitor behind it, along with a nice flat screen TV in the other corner which is hooked up to his own PlayStation video game console. Yes, it was time for Ty to get spoiled for a change. Lastly, Ty replaced his old dresser, his old nightstand and his bed and mattress with brand new ones. It was a dramatic change for Ty. His room looked completely different!

    Ty changed his old t-shirt into a nice, Under Armour hoodie and he morphed his old, worn-out jeans into a new pair of tanned slacks. Lastly, he changed his broken shoes into basketball high-tops, a brand-new pair of the latest Air Jordans. Ty sat down at his desk and looked over his new laptop. He was very happy.

    Just then, his bedroom door flew open and Dustin walked in, like he always did.

    “Hey, dweeb. Dad says you need to take out the trash and get the mail before… Uhhh…” Dustin announced before he noticed the changes in Ty’s bedroom. “What the--?”

    Ty then stood up and calmly walked over to where Dustin was standing. Ty towered over him by at least 5 inches. Dustin raised up his head in order to look Ty in the eyes. How did Ty suddenly become so tall and so muscular?

    “What the hell is going on here?”

    “WHO are you calling ‘dweeb’, shorty?” Ty said as he stood right in front of Dustin, who was now looking straight at Ty’s big, bulging pectorals.

    As Dustin looked around, he saw the new bed, the desk, the video game console and the laptop.

    “I made some changes in my life, so you better get used to the new ME!” Ty grinned. “I’m going out to the mall to buy some new clothes. See ya later, LITTLE BRO!”

    Ty walked out of the room and exited out the back door. From there, he snapped his fingers and teleported to the shopping mall. Once he arrived, he wished for a wad of cash in his wallet, so he could do some shopping.

    In the meantime, all Dustin could do is just stand there, staring at Ty’s bedroom in awe…
