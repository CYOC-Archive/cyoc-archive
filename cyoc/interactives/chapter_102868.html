    “What does that mean, in practice?” wanted to know Tabby.

    “First, it mean that the most accepted theory on why a were can't be affected by a different strain of the virus than his own, which say it's because his own strain protect its host from the intruder, is false” revealed Kathy Tucker. “Secondly, it could mean Lucie can only give birth to normals, but I'm not too sure on this one.”
