    Leap of Fluff

    "Thanks anyway, I like having skin and being skinny." you say, patting your stomach "Well...skinnier then you lot anyway." you say, realizing that you as humans go, you're hardly in the best of shape, but you're not as rotund as these creatures tend to be. You turn your attention away from the two plush creatures that spoke to you and plot your path to the hallway in back, managing to sidestep several playful, but still rather genuine attempts by the surrounding plushies to grab you. You're idly wondering if it's their carefree attitude towards everything that keeps them from being a more serious threat when you notice a shadow pass between you and the lights. You look up just in time to see the bottom half of the plush bunny on a downward arc towards you. You try to back away but she lands on you with a heavy "whumph".

    "Gotcha!" the bunny girl says with a smile, her paws pressing against your shoulders as she straddles you with her powerful legs.

    "Get off me!" you cry as you try to squirm out from under the bunny girl. For answer she flops forward pressing her entire body against yours, nullifying any progress you made. She rubs her cheek against yours making a soft cooing sound as she seems to get comfortable.

    "Oh sure, no problem." she says sarcastically "Seriously you didn't expect an expert jump like that to go unrewarded did you?" she asks as she reaches around to clutch your wrists in her paws. She gives a quick peck on the nose, causing your nose to twitch slightly.

    "I've got money." you suggest, trying to twist your wrists out of her grasp.

    "Sweetie, what exactly am I going to buy?" she asks. You continue squirming, as your nose begins to itch. You have a sinking feeling in your gut as feel your nose get softer and a warm ticklish feeling spreads from your nose to you face and cheeks. Your hands begin to swell as they begin feeling warm and soft. You cry out as you feel your new whiskers touch hers. The bunny girl sits up again, still straddling you, but releasing your wrists. You look down at your chest and see fur plush fur growing from beneath your shirt. You try to take it off to get a better look but your large plush bunny paws fumble uselessly with the buttons. By this time your face has changed shape and your ears have grown long and pointed. What was left of your human hair is pushed out of their follicles as it's replaced by white fur.

    "Oh don't worry about those clothes, baby, they'll take care of themselves." she says. As if on cue, your buttons snap and your shirt begins tearing at the seems as your body plumps up. Soon your shirt is ripped to shreds as fur creeping up your arms matches the fur growing down your chest and back. The bunny scoots down until she's off of you completely, allowing you to look at your feeble human legs growing from this large plush rabbit the rest of you has become. She removes one of your left shoe and sock and begins to run her paws along your foot.

    "Wanna get lucky? she asks "Rabbit's feet are lucky." Your left foot begins to swell, your toes disappearing beneath a large plush rabbit's foot. Your leg shortens in contrast as the fur continues to march up from your foot and down from your torso. Your legs shift, becoming large and powerful, quite suited to jumping. Your pants, at this point, don't fair any better then your shirt did, popping sounds of burst stitches reach your newly fluffy ears as they fall apart around you. Your new bunny friend helps you off with your other shoe and sock. At this point you simply watch with confused fascination as the last vestiges of your human body becomes a large plush bunny foot.

    "I'm a bunny." you say.

    "Yes you are." she says and pulls you into a hug. She holds you for a moment before turning your head. She nods over to a confused human tentatively stepping out of one of the back offices. "...and bunnies love to multiply."

    A slight snicker comes unbidden as you imagine how the two of you will change this one.
