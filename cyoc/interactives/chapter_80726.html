    Connie finished emptying her bowels in the smelliest corner of the pig pen. It may have stank but she didn't
    find the smell all that offensive thanks to her piggy anatomy being accustomed and even enjoying such smells,
    much to her disgust.

    Once she'd finished Dili and Connie quickly filled their bellies with warm milk ready for their escape attempt
    both trying to ignore thoughts of how tasty it actually was to them and tell themselves they were only doing
    it out of necessity.

    "Enjoying your din-dins, piggies?" said Sally as she peered over the fence above them. The pig-tail haired
    farm hand looked down on them so smugly that Connie wanted to punch her in her pretty face and she'd make sure
    to do so once she was herself again.

    "Well, well. What have we here?" said Sally as she started to stroll around the pig pen to the hole the pigs
    had dug.

    "Quick! Make a break for it!" Connie snorted but Dili had already taken off. They both toddled through the
    thick mud as fast as their little legs could carry them. It was incredibly sticky and such hard work for the
    weak little piglets but Dili quickly made it to the hole and scrambled through. As Connie tried to follow her
    chubby little belly squashed against the mud and her back was caught against the fence above her.

    "Hurry!" Dili squealed and began nuzzling mud to free Connie.

    They weren't quick enough. Sally was upon them and swiftly lifted Dili and dropped her back over the fence,
    splashing in a deep, dirty puddle.

    Sally knelt down in the grass in front of the trapped Connie.

    "Such naughty piggies," said the teen girl, "I should leave you stuck there by your fat tummy as punishment,
    shouldn't I?"

    Sally stared at Connie like a parent scolding a child. Connie had no choice but to stare back at the giant
    before her. She felt as small as she had been transformed to be.

    "What would you do if you escaped and a wolf found you? You'd be gobbled up for dinner! That's what would
    happen." Sally scolded.

    "There'll be no more mischief from you two." Sally said matter of factly whilst pushing Connies sticky nose
    with a finger. It was easily enough force for Connie to slide through the mud and back into the pen. Something
    that made her feel incredibly small and weak. They probably couldn't defend themselves if they ran into
    trouble after they escaped, she realized.

    Sally swiftly kicked mud back into the hole they'd spent so much effort trying to dig and stepped through the
    gate into the pen where she tied some string to a post in the fence. The other ends of the string were loosely
    tied around the piglets necks.

    "Now go back to filling your bellies like good little piglets." said Sally, "And be careful not to hurt
    yourself with that string!"

    "Stop treating us like children!" squealed Connie. She'd had enough of the babying Sally was giving them.
    She'd been fiercely independent up until her encounter with Morgana and hated being told what she can and
    cannot do.

    "I'm a grown Princess and when I get back to normal you'll be sorry."

    Sally just laughed at the empty threat.

    "Oh I am so very scared of the teeny weeny piggy... Maybe I should go hide in the town in case little piggy
    decides to huff and puff and blow my house down! Or maybe I'll eat you for breakfast before you get the
    chance?" She laughed "Now run along and squeal to mommy about how mean the big lady was to you."
