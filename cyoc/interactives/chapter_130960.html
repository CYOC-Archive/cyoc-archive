    Outside Miss Stanley's English class was not one new student, but three new 12th grade students to the all-girl's school: The first to eagerly step into the classroom being the tall full-bodied Texan blonde with freckled sun-kissed skin in baggy jeans and tank-top, smiling happily at the students to make a good first impression. "Howdy!" she cheerful announces with a short wave of her hand. "I'm Sadie Davis, from down south! Please to meet ya."

    "Such enthusiasm!" Miss Stanley complimented with a brief session of light clapping, some students in the front following compared to the slightly less energetic back. "And we're pleased to meet you." Sadie steps aside for the next girl, the fairly waif-like redhead whose fairly-well off family came from Washington. Standing at 5'3", the B+ student is wearing an expensive orange blouse and skirt as she stepped in with a nervous smile to her face while unsure of where how to make a first impression as she proceeded. "Uh... Hi. I'm Elanor Lawford... Glad to be here." She looked to the teacher, still nervously smiling while non-verbally pleading to be taken out of the spotlight.

    "Glad to have you here, Miss Lawford." Miss Stanley responded over a few chuckles from around the room. "That's
    quite enough of that." the teacher said, turning away from the new girl and waving her hand at the class. Though the
    grins didn't stop, the murmuring definitely had. The last girl to enter is the athletic tomboy from the east coast, wearing cargo pants and a hoodie. Shrugging, she flipped back her hood to reveal her short brunette hair while giving a halfhearted wave of her hand. "Hey! Name's Daz Nat," she says with a smirk on her face.

    Miss Stanley cocks her brow, looking down to her clipboard. "Mmh... Isn't that your name Daisy Natalia?"
    Daisy's expression dropping to one of neutrality and near annoyance, she never really like how girly her name sounded and didn't wanted to be called 'Daisy' like at her old school. "Eh... Yeah. But I prefer to be called Daz," she retorts while turning back to the class with a shrugs while smiling. While not bothered in making a first impression compared to Sadie and Elanor, Daisy seemed to have made a good one on the class.

    Looking around the room, Miss Stanley spotted three empty desks next to each other. "Now, please take your seats so class can begin." Each nodding at response, Elanor relieved to get out of the spotlight, the girls proceed to take their seats. The grinning Sadie, while not a fan of being lumped with others, looked at her fellow newbies while extending a hand to Elanor who genuinely smiles that she might have made a new friend sooner than expected. Daisy leaned back on her chair when noticing Sadie beginning to greet her and nods in response with a "sup?" The lives of these very different three eighteen year old teen girls, either by coincidence, fate, or even karma, appear to have converged for what may be an interesting school year for them.
