Finding herself back at her den, she had no comprehension a whole night had passed as she crawled in.

Standing, on edge, she looked wildly about. Her tail twitched in agitation, she bared her teeth at an unseen enemy. Finding none, she slunk outside the cave and finding it was almost dusk, for which she still did not have the words and comprehension to understand this was the time of day when the sun was setting, she pivoted her eyes, sniffed the air and scanned the surrounding area. Finding nothing to cause her to be the way she was, her animal mind slowly reasserting itself, Jeff pawed at the ground for a moment before her head snapped up. Something catching her attention, she was wary for a moment before she moved off, the oncoming night calling to her.
