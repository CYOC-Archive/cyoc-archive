    ALL HACKS ENABLED

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 12 years ago O

    After selecting the option, Jake felt as if he were falling. He fell for what
    seemed like an eternity, but what really only the span of a couple minutes.
    When the sensation finally stopped, he found himself suddenly lying in a
    hospital bed. He was still in his regular body. A machine to his right was making
    a long note that you hear when someone dies on movies. Suddenly, a nurse
    rushed in.

    The nurse was no ordinary nurse. She was an anthromorphic golden retriever
    with a large bust in an old-style nurse's outfit. Jake was at a loss for words,
    when he remembered that this was the Cyber World. However, he questioned
    the need for hospitals in a fake world.

    "Oh my word!" she said when she saw Jake sitting up and looking around as
    the machine declared him dead.

    She ran back out of the room and returned with a normal man in a doctor's
    uniform. He looked at Jake, then the machine. His eyes widened a bit, but he
    did not say anything. He walked over to the machine, disconnected it, and told
    the nurse to get a different one. He then checked for Jake's pulse, listened to
    his heartbea, checked his reflexs, and all manner of simple check-up
    procedures. When the new machine was brought in, he hooked it back up and
    the same note was given. The doctor disconnected the second machine and left
    the room. Jake heard him talking on the other side of the door as if on a phone.

    "Well, sir," the doctor said, coming back into the room, "I'm afraid I don't know
    what is going on. You have no pulse, no heart beat, nor any other internal sign
    of life. But your body is reacting just as though it were still alive. In a sense,
    you're the walking dead."

    "Wait a minute," Jake said, putting voice to his thoughts, "I'm in the Cyber
    World, right?" The doctor nodded. "Then how could I have any signs of life
    anyway?"

    "Well, you see, each avatar is programmed to show the same signs of life that a
    real person would so that we know when a person is dying in the cyber world
    and will be reformed in the real world. It's a very complicated process that I
    cannot fully explain. However, I have called the creator of the cyber world. He
    will be here shortly to look at you."

    Not more than ten minutes later, a man with a completely white suit and pants
    with a white top hat and cane strolled into the room. He had a brown handlebar
    moustache and goatee. He conferred with the doctor for a moment before
    sitting down on the end of Jake's bed.

    "What's your name, boy?" he asked in a southern accent.

    "Jake, sir."

    "You don't have to call me 'sir', Herb will do. I'm the inventor of this here Cyber
    World. I know its ins and outs, how it runs, how it works, how it makes things
    possible. You however, are quite impossible." The man got up and began to
    pace. "You were found unconscious on a road in Cyber City, the main city in
    Cyber World. You were brought up here with no information saved on your
    watch. The disk inside was a blank. When the nurse came in, you were both
    alive and dead. In other words, you have somehow managed to bypass every
    rule we have for getting into Cyber World. So, unless you want to get into some
    serious trouble, you'll need to explain exactly how you got here."

    Jake told the man how he had bought a display model with a preset disk. He
    told about the invisible yet visible menu full of numbers and letters where he
    had picked the first option in confusion. He told how he had felt like he was
    falling, then was suddenly in the hospital. All the while, Herb sat in a chair,
    taking in the information and processing it in his brain. When Jake finished,
    Herb sat there for a long while, just thinking. Finally, he stood and began to
    pace again.

    "It would appear," Herb began, "That the disk put into your watch was
    corrupted. That corruption made it so that your body in the real world was
    destroyed, and you have become a part of this world. That would explain the
    fact that your avatar shows no signs of life because you aren't alive anymore.
    You are Cyber World. At least, that's my theory. To test that, you will have to
    attempt to do something that normal users cannot do. The simplest thing to do
    would be to change your avatar without a disk. Go on, imagine you're
    something else; a snake, or a centaur, a woman, or a dragon, something."

    Jake thought about himself being a snake slithering along the floor. He
    immediately felt smaller and faster. He had no arms or legs, he was just one
    long figure with a head and a body.

    "Ah, just as I feared. You have become one with Cyber World. You are
    essentially in control of everything now. However, don't think this comes without
    consequences. I can always evacuate the other users and reset Cyber World
    without you in it. You don't have a body to return to in the real world, so if this
    world dies, so do you. However, if you help me maintain order in Cyber World, I
    will let you have free reign so long as you don't destroy my world."

    What do you do now?
