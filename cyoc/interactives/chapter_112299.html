    An employee walks up to you and hands you another map, and says
    "Each transformation lasts anywhere from about 1 hour, during this you may explore around the area and enjoy your time here."
    They start to go away but you stop them.
    "Wait, how long do we know for sure it lasts."
    "OHMYGOD Thank you for reminding me!" He says and hands you 4 small wristbands each with the parks logo, on the inside there is a timer.
    "You just set the timer and when it's done you should adjust back to your own body."
    "Thanks for the help..." you look at his name tag. "Harry"
    "No problem" he says as he walks off.
    You look back at the map and see that it's a small hike towards the beginning trails, so you venture off with the kids. The walk is not bad, as you walk the kids look at the map trying to decide where to go and saying what animal they want to be, you hear a small chunk of their conversation.
    "I want to be a bear, I love bears!" James exclaims.
    "Of corse you do." Lily says as she laughs.
    "Well what do you want to be?" James says
    "I want to be a deer!"
    "Maybe I'll eat you."
    "Shut up"
    Lily taps me and my wife on the shoulder.
    "What do you want to be?"
    "I don't know I'll choose when we get there." Emily says.
    "What about you Dad?"
    "We'll be there in a minute, I'll also choose then."
    You arrive in a clearing, and look around, there are several paths that split off from the main path you are on now, each animal is marked on a sign next to the path. One of the signs just has a question mark on it. You can't see too far into each path. The kids run immediately to which one they want to go to.
    "Okay we'll split up, you kids have one hour and we'll meet up here."
    "Thanks!" They yell as they sprint into their paths. Your wife kisses you on the cheek and says
    "I'll meet you back here." As she walks into one of the paths
