    It was a perfect morning, the sun was warm and the sky had just a few puffy white clouds spaced far apart, there was a gentle breeze coming inland making small gentle white caps on the beach. To make it even better the beach was all but abounded.

    “This is great.” Stated Robert as the 24-year-old man dropped the cooler he had been carrying into the soft sand. He was a fit young man, he wouldn’t say he was overly muscular he had some fat on his chest but it was more muscles then fat. His bright blonde hair and warm blue eyes scanned the area, it was a nearly perfect spot. His black and orange swim trunks fit well enough on him. Personally, he would have wanted to be closer to the water’s edge but the women in their group wanted to be more back to spread out and sunbathed. Not that he overall minded seeing sunbathing women, most of all his girlfriend.

    “Just nice that we all have the day off at the same time.” Stated his girlfriend Rose as she lied out a couple of towels, both hers and her boyfriends however knowing him he would spend most of his time either in the water or trying to get a volleyball game going. Once the towels where down, she quickly began to apply sunscreen, she had pale skin and she knew that all too well. She was a daughter of Ireland for sure, pale skin, red hair which she kept shoulder length at most and deep green eyes. She wore a red bikini top with a pink flower on one side. She didn’t have the largest chest, but it was respectable and in her view she had the best legs of any of their friends.

    “Don’t put too much sunscreen on you will never get a tan.” Yelled her best friend Stacy as she spread out her own towel and lied down to start her tan. With her deep purple bikini which went well with her long blonde hair, beside her her boyfriend was busy setting up. Sam was much more reserve then his girlfriend, with a black and red swim trunks and a black top which he removed to show his six pack muscle. He and Lindsay where the picture-perfect couple, the type of couple that an add group would use to promote tourism. A young hot woman, with all around good features and a young man who had similar good features.

    “You should use some sunscreen unless you want cancer.” Stated a cool voice as their other friend Mike and his girlfriend Mary arrived. Mary was slightly older then the rest of their group being 26 to their 24 but she didn’t look it. Her long brown curls bounced a bit as she stood there in a white bikini beside her boyfriend who was wearing a simple royal blue swim trunks.

    “I agree.” Added a new voice as another women, this one shorter but much more endowed in the chest walked up. While the rest of the women had average size bust hers was far above average and it clashed with her normally shy nature. Today she had worn a light blue one piece swimsuit which looked close to breaking under the strain of her chest. Her long black hair going down to almost her ass was pulled up into a ponytail.

    “Lindsay I was worried you wouldn’t be able to make it.” Said Rose with a smile as Lindsay lied down her towel, she had come alone it seemed.

    “Wouldn’t miss it.” Said Lindsay as the sound of cars doors opening and people yelling filled the air. “Looks like James and Jenny have arrived.”

    “So they have.” Stated Mike in a cool voice as James and Jenny came toward them. The two of them had been on again off again couple going on 4 years now. They fought each other like cats and dogs and slept together like bunnies in mating season. More then once Jenny had had a pregnancy scare but they never seamed to learn from it. Jenny came toward them dressed in a one-piece swimsuit which had so many holes cut into it, it was basically a bikini however the red maroon color did go very well with her chocolate color skin. Beside her and arguing all the way down to the beach with them was James who was fit but not over the top and wore a simple brown swimsuit with a white T-shirt over it.

    “So glad you could make it.” Said Robert

    “Ya, we would have been here sooner but.” Started James but decided the better of it once he got a look from Jenny.

    “Look at us, 9 friends with perfect weather and a beach to ourselves what more could we ask for.” Said Robert as they all cheered but then they heard something coming from some nearby rocks.

    “What is that?” inquired Stacy

    “Someone should go check it out.” Added Mike, not knowing that the sound came from an abounded Mer-child who needed a family and who's magic was waiting as well.
