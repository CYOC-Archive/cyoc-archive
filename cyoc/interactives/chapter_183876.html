    The two of you walk hand and hand down the beach leaving the more popular swimming areas next to the parking lot and heading toward the less traveled part of the beach.

    You two talk, laughing and joking, you smile as she pulled you closer to her pressing your hand between her breasts. You grin a bit as she gave you a playful smile as well as you passed by the last life guard station on the beach, who gave the couple a knowing smile but said nothing.

On top of that bluff was a lighthouse which had stood there for over 100 years. The tall bluff provided not only shade from the sun but also privacy, this was a common area for couples to go to be alone. No lifeguards patrolled this area and the tourists normally only stayed up by the light house.
