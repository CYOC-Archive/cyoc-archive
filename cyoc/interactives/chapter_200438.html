    Outsourcing Diaper Change

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago AR BM TG Toddler Male to female

    “…I’ll bring you to my mom and have her change you.”

    “Wait!” Dan squeaked. Before she could do anything, the girl picked her up. To Dan’s disgust, she could feel the mess in her diaper being spread around by the movement. The girl carried Dan out of the toy department, through maternity, and finally they arrived at the women’s department. The girl walked up to a middle-aged woman who looked a lot like her.

    “Hey, mom?” The woman looked up.

    “What is it, Lacey?” Finally, Dan knew the girl’s name.

    “Some lady asked me to watch her little girl, but then she had an accident and I don’t know how to change her.”

    “I can handle it, no problem,” Lacey’s mom said. “Go ahead and put her in the seat. We’ll get her changed right away.” Lacey put Dan in the seat of the shopping cart, smearing her poop even more. Dan shuddered, disgusted.

    Lacey’s mom pushed the cart towards the restrooms. At the restrooms, she picked Dan up and gestured for Lacey to follow her. The three entered the women’s restroom.

    “Luckily, they have a diaper dispenser and free wipes,” Lacey’s mom commented as she laid Dan on the changing table. “Lacey, go ahead and buy a T3 for me.”

    “Okay!” Lacey put a dollar into the diaper dispenser and pressed a button. A diaper came out. Lacey handed her mom the diaper.

    “Now, watch. Since you want to become a babysitter, changing a dirty diaper is a good skill for you to learn,” Lacey’s mom instructed her. Lacey nodded. “First, we remove her clothes.” Lacey’s mom narrated as she performed each step of the change. “Then, we remove the diaper. Phew! You really made a big stinky, little girl!”

    Dan blushed.

    “Go ahead and throw that diaper away for me,” Lacey’s mom requested. Lacey picked up the diaper with a look of disgust and tossed it into the trash can. “Now, we wipe her down. Make sure you get every inch, okay? You don’t want to leave any poop or pee. It will stink, but more importantly, it’s not healthy for her. She might get diaper rash.” Dan shivered at the coldness of the wipes, but stayed still. “Wow, this is a really well-behaved little girl! If I didn’t know any better, I’d think she was older! But only little girls make boom-boom in their diapies.”

    Dan felt embarrassment course through her.

    “Finally, we tape the diaper back on and redress her. If we had diaper powder, we would put it on before the diaper, but we don’t, so this will have to do for now.” Lacey’s mom sat her up on the changing table. “All done!”

    “Thanks, mom!” Lacey said gratefully.

    “It’s no problem,” Lacey’s mom said airily. She smiled at Dan. “This little darling was a delight to change!” Lacey’s mom went over to the sink to wash her hands. Dan gestured for Lacey to get close.

    “Now what?” Dan whispered to Lacey.
