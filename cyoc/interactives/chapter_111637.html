    The Chaos that is You

    During your dream you saw Cal'kar performing a ritual to enhance his power, however that ritual went wrong quickly and gave him the mark on your body and demonic strength, and as a result he was feared due to the aftermath when he figured out that he healed from wounds within seconds. The ritual alse caused destruction over a 3 mile radius killing hundreds. You saw this and thought," Well that's great I'll jot that down as for reasons he/I was sealed away." You then woke up Firestorm and Topaz were asleep so you took a walk around the temple trying to find more about Cal'kar.

    About an hour later Firestorm woke and saw you looking around and said,"What are you doing Cal'kar?"
    "Well I got more memories about my past when I slept, and they weren't pleasant. To be specific I remember as to how I got my mark and what happened as a result." You said
    "What did you see?" Firestorm asked
    "I saw me or Cal'kar, damn I don't know what to call myself now, but I saw us performing a ritual to enhance our power and it went wrong fast filling me with demonic energy, creating this mark, and turning me immortal. During the aftermath everything within a 3 mile radius was destroyed along with hundreds of lives." You sighed," well that's one reason one was sealed up I guess."
    "Damn, but Topaz explained everything the best she could, and I believe you who ever you are in there at least your better then he was." Firestorm said
    "It's just by the end of this I hope I don't become him," you said," Firestorm what does history say about me?"
    "Well to summarize it all up you went crazy and nobody believed, and it says that you made up a cult who killed in your name, and craved your power, and as a result thousands died in your name." Firestorm said
    "That just makes me even more pissed at these stupid Corrupted Darkness members, because that cult from what I can gather was just as obsessed with me then, as they are now. Hell the grunts who attacked Topaz and I even called me an imposter, until I killed most of them and ate half their souls. I just surprised how used Topaz is to seeing that she witnessed the whole thing you know." You said
    "Really, you let her!?" Firestorm growled
    "I told her to close the temple door and step outside so I could make sure she was safe, and so that nobody could sneak up on her from behind. I never told her to watch she just did and she was surprisingly used to it, even when I ate the souls from those dragons she was fine." You said
    "She's been through more than most can say they have which is bad for a princess to see death, but she was dragged into it." Firestorm said," I want you to know you have my support Cal'kar."
    "Thanks," you said," I still don't know what I can do so bear with me."
    Topaz awoke and said," I see you two have made nicely, did I miss much."
    You told Topaz what you saw in your dream,"That's a lot for me considering that before coming to this world I couldn't even dream of doing something like that, much less the results." You said.
    "I see, so what's our next move?" Topaz asked
    "I hate to say it but I'm stumped I don't know what to do?" You said
    "Well I heard the guards talking and they said that there was another base south but that's all I know." Firestorm said," and then there's the resistance."
    "Resistance?" You asked
    "People who want to overthrow the Corrupted Darkness and retake the throne." Firestorm confirmed
    "Whatever we do let's just not get caught here, and on top of that you two better defend me if we try ro ally with anyone else." You said and they nodded.
    "Okay then you pick." Firestorm said to you.

    What do you do now?
