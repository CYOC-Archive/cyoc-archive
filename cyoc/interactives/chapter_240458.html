    A Twisted Wish

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 2 months ago AR BM O Mythological Magic Baby

    Damon having been erased from existence, his place got emptied through magic of his belongings but not of existence itself. It was simply inhabited by another person, as it would have been without Damon renting it. So the genie's lamp ended up on the dresser of a young student who was twenty and in beauty school. She really wanted to be a model, but had to get a diploma so as to make ends meet. She did fancy herself as not as girly as models could be and she tried to not give off any overly feminine vibes. Little girl things annoyed her. She wanted to be a model, yes, but not the brainless ditzy style dressed cape to toe in pink. The femme fatale sort was her go-to goals. So when she suddenly found a lamp, that had come out of nowhere - literally - she was intrigued. Perplexed, as it was definitely NOT the sort for her to have around. Where had this piece of trash come from? Frowning, she picked it up and looked at it. Her fingers brushed it and smoke erupted. With a shout, Tiffany let it fall to the ground. Before her astonished eyes, a woman dressed in exotic clothes came out of it. She darted her eyes around ad then looked at the young woman.
    "Oh," she said simply. "You are my new mistress."
    "Are you... a genie?" stammered Tiffany.
    "Why, yes. And you get three wishes."
    "Easy," said Tiffany happily. "My first wish is to be the fairest. My second wish is to be eternally young. And my third is to be adored and admired forever."
    "As you wish," smirked the genie.
    She snapped her fingers and Tiffany found herself weak and immobilised in a bundle of soft fabrics, like in a cocoon. Everything was out of proportions, too big and looming. Horrid creatures stared down at her.
    "Here she is," said a creaky voice. "The most adorable babe. Formerly a human babe, now a fae babe. To be loved and cherished."
    "Oh," gushed a deep voice. "Just look at her. The cutest thing I have ever seen. I'll coddle her and love her and treat her like little princess."
    "Yes, she is very cute," agreed another voice. "I cannot wait to dress her in sweet little frilly outfits."
    Two fae creatures then picked up the basket in which helpless Tiffany now was stuck and left happily for their home. Tiffany was a human baby that hd been changed into a fairy baby and sold to a loving male couple of these creatures who would coddle and raise her lovingly. In a very girly environment. Tiffany could only be appalled at her own situation. And she had used up all of her wishes.
