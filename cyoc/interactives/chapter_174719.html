    Mark's afternoon

    Collecting Anna's schedule was easy enough. If Mrs Berken thought Anna was quieter than usual she didn't say anything. She just assumed that Anna already knew everything she needed to know. Mark on the other hand didn't want to give away that he had no idea about how the camp worked. She just pretended to understand what Mrs Berken was saying. It did seem that she would be busy in the morning but then have time for herself between lunch and dinner. Mark was in and out of the office within 5 minutes.

    Mark was relieved to see "Jennifer" waiting for her when she left the office. Kylie and Rhonda were still there with her. They were giggling about something. The guilty look on "Jennifer's" face and the fact that the other girls stopped giggling when they saw her made Mark think she had been the subject of the conversation.

    'How did it go?' asked the real Anna.

    'I now have my schedule' replied Mark waving the envelope Mrs Berken had given her.

    'Well we'll see you two later' said "Jennifer" addressing Kylie and Rhonda 'Anna has promised to show me around the camp'

    'Um, yes, Let's go' said Mark. She was finding it hard to sound confident. 'What did you want to see first?'

    'I was hoping you could show me the games room' said "Jennifer" 'Then we could walk done to the lake and you can show me where the boys camp is' "Jennifer" was already leaving the building. Mark scurried after her.

    Anna did give Mark a tour of the camp. They finished with a walk down to the lake where from the jetty Anna pointed out the boys camp across the other side. 'You can see it's a fair distance. And the adults are pretty strict about sneaking across. You get sent home if you get caught. I think we are much better off with you over this side' Mark wasn't so sure.

    Perhaps sensing Mark's reticence, Anna told her 'Follow me' she found herself walking around the shore of the lake. After a minutes walk they came to a beach where there was a small hut. 'This is where they keep the sailing gear. I want to show you something' Anna walked up to the building and from behind a log retrieved a key. With a smile on her face she used the key to open the door. Then beckoned Mark inside. Mark noticed a couple of small yachts and what seemed like bundles of sails.

    While she was looking at the shed contents Anna approached her and gave her a kiss. It had similar intensity to the kiss Abbey had greeted her with. 'This is a place where we can be alone together. They always do sailing in the mornings and in the afternoon nobody is ever here. Let's get that shirt off you. I want you to experience what it feels like to get your teats sucked' Anna was already lifting Mark's t-shirt

    "This is more like it" thought Mark as a thrill went down her spine. Her teats quickly responded to Anna's tongue. However when Mark attempted to reciprocate Anna stopped her.

    'Unfortunately we don't have time. We will need to get back to camp and get our charges ready for dinner. We'll need to have a rain check. However you now know where we can meet. Each afternoon at 2 pm. See that tin in the corner. Let's use that as somewhere to leave messages for each other. I'll leave first. You wait 5 minutes. Lock the door and put the key back where I got it from. You walk back via the jetty. I'll take the other track straight back to camp'

    'But why can't we go back together? You were just showing me around the camp'

    'Well I suppose we could today, but in future it would be better to not be seen together too much. Being caught together would also see us booted out of camp'

    Mark put her bra and shirt back on. The two of them did walk back to camp together via the direct track but Anna refused to let Mark hold her hand 'We have to appear to just be friends' she said.

    That was the end of Mark's peaceful afternoon. As soon as she entered her dormitory Heather started giving her jobs to do. She had to round up the younger girls then help get them ready for dinner. She found herself on the opposite side of the dining room as "Jennifer" at dinner and could only wave from a distance. After dinner she had to help her charges back to their dormitory then get ready for bed and by the time that was done curfew was upon the camp. Mark had no choice but to put on Anna's nightie and climb into her allocated bed.
