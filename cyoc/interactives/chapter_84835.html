    The gang got together at Andrew's place, Jackson and you had passed through your old house before, you saw the pictures of the new you along with two person that were supposedly your parents, but that you didn't remember. Still it was an empty house full of evidence, Jackson had asked were you would keep the ingredients, and even when you didn't know you supposed seen 'your room' that they should be behind the dresser, and they were there.

    Maybe you are the kid named Will after all.

    Andrew lived a couple blocks away, there only Harry was already there, having explain everything to Andrew. Andrew was a short slim joe, with a freckle face and auburn hair. He invite you inside. "Dom and Tom will be late, they are stuck doing chores." said Harry.

    "What, the parents aren't reprogrammed to let you skip those?" you asked sarcastically.

    "It didn't work, as you hopefully will remember soon." said Jackson.

    We went straight to Andrews room, he was almost stereotypical a geek. Down to the chemistry set... which you quickly deduce was how he did his alchemy.

    "Well did you bring the ingredients?" asked Andrew.

    "Here." Jackson handed them over.

    "Where did you find these?, I've looking all over the net."

    "Ask Will. As soon as you bring him back of course." said Jackson.

    "Yeah... about that." tried to interject Harry.

    "I do not know how." said Andrew.

    "What?"

    "I'm sorry, but a memory potion would be counterproductive. He will try harder to remember memories that aren't there. He might ended up believing them above anything else, or ended up completely blank. Base on what Harry told me it's not even a magic cleaning since you already did it. You want an anti-magic potion something that literally destroy and reverse any acting magic to zero." explained Andrew.

    "Fine then make one of those."

    "Make one of... Have you had any idea what you are asking?! My craft is not simple strutting and kumbaya you are used to. It's a delicate precise work, and what you ask is perhaps one of the most difficult things to concoct. Even if I knew where to start I doubt I can actually pull it through, its a subject beyond my current abilities."

    "Could you give it a try?" you asked, you are as surprised as the others to hear you ask for it,

    "Will, I'm sorry. You have always been cool, but... you know the difference between one of my potions and a poison?" you shook your head. "A 10% margin of error while making it, the more complicated the potion the smaller that percentage grows. For what you asked me it has to be as close as flawless as one can get. You make what you want or you made a poison. Other gradients are simply dilutions, that's how alchemy works and that's why I can't help." said Andrew regretfully.

    "So or only chance is the coven." said Jackson disappointed.

    "Not quite. My goodness aren't you dramatic Andrew tell them the good news." said Harry.

    "Well... while I can't help you personally I have come up with other solutions to your particular ailment. Well one option and please don't take it the wrong way. Perhaps your cleansing wasn't as thoughtful as you imagine, not offense but you are all novices, maybe a voodoo doctor or a priestess can make a better job. And then there's Saru..."

    "No fuck that guy!" yelled Jackson.

    "Take it easy", it was just an option.

    "Well thanks Andrew, see you around"
