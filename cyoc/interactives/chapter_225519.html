    Kellie, Jack, and Ann came into Mary Elise’s home and they tried to talk some reason into her. Then a massive explosion hit the house. Everyone was sent flying and buried under rubble, in the chaos the figure of a girl with four tails walked through the flames.

    Mary Elise awoke strapped to a chair, she was in an white room, more concerning was that her ring had been taken from her. What the hell had happened? That attack must have been some kind of enchanted missile to slip past her senses.She thought of Kellie, Bong-cha, and the others, she hoped they weren’t dead or captured.
    She was ripped away from her thoughts when a suave man walked through a the thick armored door; he wore a sleek white suit with a black high collared shirt. His blonde hair was shaved on all sides except for the top that was slicked to the left, he had piercing dark eyes and was quite handsome Mary had to admit.

    Mary Elise began to struggle against her restraints but stopped when all her strength seemed to leave her; her body became frail as wrinkles spread across her skin, her breasts shrunk slightly and sagged down her chest, her red hair thinned and became white. She could feel pain in her joints when she weakly tried to tug at her bonds and her eyesight became worse, her teeth gradually fell out and were replaced with a pair of dentures.

    “I must say, these rings really are something else! I mean, I’ve heard the reports but wow!” He chuckled in a silky masculine voice.

    That’s when she saw, even with her deteriorated vision, that the man wore not only her Ring or Change but also Ann’s Rings of Age as well!

    “W-what…have y-you done?” Mary Elise tried to demand but her voice was now just as frail and weak as her body.

    “Oh don’t worry about your friends, they’re still alive, probably. I just needed the rings, and you of course. You’ve been quite the thorn in our side, but I think you’ll have mellowed out in your old age.” He laughed.

    The now old woman glared at him with her dulled eyes.

    “Oh! Where are my manners, I should be more respectful to my elders.” He joked.

    “I am Felix Monroe! I serve as the Boss’ right hand…” then he used the ring of change to transform himself into a busty blonde woman,
    “…or his gal Friday!” He giggled before returning to his original form.

    The elderly Mary Elise said nothing, she just kept staring at him.

    “Oh, are you having a hard time seeing? Here.” He said mockingly and used the ring of change to give the old woman a pair of thick old fashioned glasses, complete with a beaded chain.

    “Don’t worry grandma, I think this will be more…suitable for a woman you age!” He said with his cocky attitude.

    The white haired Mary Elise’s clothes had transformed into a typical grandmother outfit, complete with slippers and wool cardigan. He even transformed the chair she was tied to into a wheelchair. She tried to move the chair herself but her arthritis kept her mostly immobilized and unable to move the wheels.
    Felix wheeled her out of the white cell and through the sterile and cold halls of the facility.

    “I know the Boss would want these rings for himself but I think the Normal need new leadership, instead of fighting and killing the freaks I say we change them. And with these rings you’ve given me, I believe that a bright future is in store for humanity!” He chimed.

    The old woman was horrified at his plans, her friends and team were in danger and she was helpless to do anything about it.
    He wheeled her to an elevator and they took it to the top floor and into his office.
