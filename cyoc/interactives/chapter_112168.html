    The Magic Shop

    Risk and reward

    Nate couldn't wait to spend the weekend as a werewolf. Well, not a real werewolf. The sellers on the website had assured him that he would just look like a hybrid of wolf and man without the infectious bite, uncontrollable animal instincts, and invulnerability. The sellers weren't fools. They didn't want innocent blood on their hands or to start some kind of werewolf epidemic. But that suited Nate just fine. He just wanted to run wild like a wolf, experience the world with the senses of wolf, maybe do a little hunting .... but not cause any real carnage or hurt anybody.

    In the privacy of his room Nate carefully read the instructions again. He didn't want to make a mistake. This purchase had pretty much maxed out his credit card (he still didn't how he was going to explain that to his parents) and he doubted he would be able to purchase another potion soon. The instructions seemed pretty simple. Just drink the vial under a full moon and the effects should begin immediately. The instructions stressed that the entire contents of the vial must be consumed to have an effect. There were some other recommendations - about what kind of clothes to wear, warnings not to use it in public (to avoid being shot or harmed by scared people), and other small warnings. No big deal.

    Nate couldn't contain his excitement. It had just turned dark and he could see a full moon peeking behind the clouds. He couldn't wait any longer. He opened the vial and looked at the strange grey mixture. It had an earthy unpleasant smell. He was a little nervous but there was no reward without risk. He quickly downed the contents of the vial in one large gulp.

    Nate had expected to begin changing immediately - growing fur and fangs, his body stretching and changing in impossible ways. Instead he just felt very sleepy and weak. He could hardly stand up. Slowly he lowered his body to the floor, curled up and began to fall asleep.

    Great, thought Nate drowsily. This must be some kind of scam. They just drug you with powerful hallucinogens and make you think you are a wolf. Serves me right for purchasing strange things over the internet. Hope I don't have a bad trip.

    Nate drifted off to a semi-conscious state, disturbed by strange feelings and dreams.
