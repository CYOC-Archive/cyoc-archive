    Jason and Becky started talking for the next few weeks, getting to know each other well.

    They actually had quite a few things in common, sports being one of them. They both enjoyed watching football and talked about it when they texted and called each other. They also talked about the next time they could enjoy a date together once work slowed down.

    That day came on a Friday. They both had the weekend to themselves and wanted to see about going to Tina’s for another ‘baby date.’
    They both agreed to meet there at 6:00pm.
    They wondered about getting baby clothes to take to the daycare, but they figured that the daycare would have some ready for them to wear.

    The time came they both met up at Tina’s after work. Jason approached the bar entrance and saw Becky waiting there. They both greeted each other with a warm smile and hugs.

    “Great to see you again finally,” said Becky warmly.

    “You also,” replied Jason.

    They both entered the bar together and approached the counter for drinks. Seeing a familiar face for both of them.

    “Long time no see you two, “ said Tina, “I am assuming you two met at the daycare?”

    “Yeah we did, and we both enjoyed each other’s company quite a bit,” answered Becky.

    “Well that’s good to hear! I am happy for you two!” Becky stated excitedly. “Be good with her Jason, she’s a cool friend of mine.”

    Jason blushed, “OK, I will.”

    Tina then jumped straight to the reason why they were there, “so, I am guessing you both want another youthie?”

    They both nodded yes and sat down at the bar.
    “Alright then, I shall return with two of them!……..and probably some special garments for you both also,” she whispered that last part so everybody couldn’t hear and winked at them, letting them know that she knew about their hobby of age regression.

    They both giggled and just sat, talk about what age they wanted to regress to.

    “How about we ask her to really enhance the drink, maybe an extra in order to lengthen the time we are young?” Thought Becky.

    Jason agreed, he asked Tina if the had something like that, she did. She had a boost of the youthenizer, one that would last a couple of days instead of overnight. Just their luck!

    Tina came back with two youthenizer plus drinks for the new couple. Jason and Becky grabbed them and clinked glasses before starting to drink them. They almost finished both of them.

    Right on cue, the drinks start working and they started age regressing. They watched each other grow younger and younger until they reached a younger age than they anticipated. They both stopped at around 18 months old.

    They then turned to each other and gave each a wide-eyed look, they were confused. How were they gonna talk to one another?! Jason tried to talk, but gibberish just came out of his mouth, same with Becky.

    “Uh-oh, looks like both of you became younger than you both expected. I hope y’all didn’t plan on talking this weekend, because y’all are too young to even say a word,” said Tina. They both were surprised, however, Tina started getting them out of their pile of clothes and getting them diapers to wear.

    Once Tina grabbed the diapers, she then pulled each of the two babies up onto the bar counter and changed them into diapers quickly. Once they were both taped up, she then retrieved two brand new pacifiers for each of them and placed them in their mouths, “Y’all might wanna use those this weekend, not like any words are gonna come out of your mouths.” Tina said.

    Jason and Becky both started sucking their pacifiers and started adjusting back to their baby bodies again, Jason played with the ring again on his pacifier since it amazed him how calm he could be while sucking this latex nipple. All the patrons at the bar came up and showered them with ‘awwwws’ and picked them up to carry them for a bit. They were both told how cute of babies they really were and started being bounced and given diaper pats. They both giggled and smiled around their pacifiers at receiving this kind of attention again.

    Tina collected them back up and started looking at who to call in order to come get these two babies. Since they were going to be babies for a weekend, she had to determine whether daycare or a friend of hers would babysit them for a weekend.

    She had a difficult decision to make, ultimately, she chose to call……..
