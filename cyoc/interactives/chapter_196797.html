    Not knowing what to do with this new information, you were about to change a word while Sophia quickly changed her sentence as fast as she could. Right before you pressed enter to get rid of Sophia's app, you rethought what you were about to do, and reloaded the sentence, it probably didn't matter that she had the app too.

    "Sophia sweats nervously and looks around, but is now calming down."

    That was.. Odd.. Well, it was probably nothing, you look for the right word to change before settling on "nervously", it was a word that stuck out in the sentence, and you could see the anxiety in the cat girl's screen, so you changed it to "confidently".

    "Sophia sweats confidently and looks around, but is now calming down."

    Looking back over at her, she sat up a bit straighter, and you could see a bit more sweat on her than before, seems like all this did was make her sweat, and like doing so... You were a bit disappointed with this, and brushed your long, green hair out of her face, still plastic, but now you could move your face and limbs freely, even more so than a normal person. You decided that the next course of action would just be to mess around, Sophia wouldn't know, right?

    "Sophia is known around campus for her electrical outbursts."

    Interesting... It seems making her part robot made her give off electricity.. She does have to run smoothly, it made sense. You sticking to your word and trying a funnier change, you changed "electrical" to "gassy", you knew your sense of humor wasn't mature, you just thought it would be funny.

    "Sophia is known around campus for her gassy outbursts."

    Now sitting closer to her, you could hear a quiet noise coming from Sophia's rear, this was priceless... You made Sophia give off gas instead of electricity, it seems she was still mostly well liked among everyone, even her unlimited gas... Amazing... You were glad knowing you weren't the only popular girl with gas issues, maybe you two could be friends now, you think while letting out a loud burp. Reloading the sentence again, you found an intriguing one.

    "The gassy cat girl scratches at her rear, wondering what she could do."

    You were a bit confused at that last part, but that's fine, changing her body was what you were doing, and you were wondering what you could do with her butt now, it definitely could be bigger, and since you just gave her gas, maybe you could change that part of her more? Settling on just adding a word to the sentence, where would you put this word? Or would she change you again without you knowing?
