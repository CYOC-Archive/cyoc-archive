    Samantha's Date

    The beach trip was over, and despite a few hiccups along the way, Katie and Kellie thought that they had a pretty good time at the beach. Now the weekend had come up and the two along with the other girls as well as Aunt Lisa, were helping Samantha get ready for her date with Mike at Katie and Lisa's house.

    "There you are sweetie, you look so gorgeous." Aunt Lisa complimented Samantha after finishing her hair.

    "Thanks miss Lisa, I'm just a little nervous is all." Samantha said to Lisa.

    "Oh, I know the feeling dear, I went through plenty of dates in my life. None of them ever really stuck, but it was still nice to go out every once in a while. Plus it's just high school after all, there are other boys out there." Aunt Lisa told her.

    "It's just about time for him to arrive." Mary Elise said looking at the clock.

    "Don't be all nervous now, he's a really sweet guy." Claudia assured her friend.

    Samantha was taking a few deep breaths as the minutes went by before there was a knock at the door. Samantha was frozen solid not wanting to go over and answer the door, but Mika rolled her eyes and dragged her friend over to the door and opened it, and on the other side of the door was Mike Davis, dressed nicely for their date.

    "Hey Mike, Samantha is all ready for your date, right Sam?" Mika asked her friend.

    "Y...Yep! Ten four good buddy already to go!" Samantha nervously said giving Mike thumbs up.

    The the girls including Katie, Kellie, and Aunt Lisa all shook their heads at her.

    "Uh....yeah, I am too. Come on, my car's right outside." Mike said as he held out his hand for Samantha to take.

    Samantha slowly raised her hand and took his as she exited the house with him.

    Katie and Kellie both watched out from the window as they got into Mike's car and they drove off.

    "Well it looks like they're out for a good night." Katie nodded with a smile.

    "Maybe not." Kellie said glaring outside the window.

    "What?"

    "Follow me and don't tell the others." Kellie said as the two girls quietly stepped outside and hid behind some bushes and Kellie motioned for Katie to be quiet. Katie soon heard a familiar voice talking.

    "Hey Jennifer? Yeah it's me, you'll never believe what I just saw: that wallflower skank Samantha is actually going out on a date with Mike Davis." The voice obviously belonged to Ashley who had been watching everything from not too far away and was talking to Jennifer on her cell phone.

    "Why don't you and I go sabotage their little date as payback for what she did at the beach? Oh yeah, I know exactly where they're going. I'll see you there." Ashley said as she hung up her phone and went to her car to go presumably pick up Jennifer.

    Katie and Kellie came out of their hiding spot and began talking to each other.

    "Oh those two make me so mad sometimes." Katie said.

    "Yeah, but you know we could sabotage their sabotage?" Kellie suggested.

    "True, so you think we should go to where Mike and Samantha went? or do you think we should follow Ashley and Jennifer and make sure they don't get in the way first?" Katie asked her girlfriend.
