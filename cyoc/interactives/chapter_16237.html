    Chronivac Version 4.0

    Jeff accidentally changes himself into his Dad's suit

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Fotek 18 years ago I

    Jeff leaned back into the chair and realised how much taller he was now that he was
    looking at the worlds from his Dad's perspective. He decided that the first thing to do
    would be to change his room so that it no longer looked like the bedroom of a 15 year old
    boy.

    Browsing through the Chronivac's user environment settings, Jeff noticed an option called
    'synchronize last transformation with previous reality' that sounded like exactly the kind
    of thing he wanted and without a second's thought clicked on the button expecting to see
    options for controlling the details of this transformation. Instead, Jeff felt a familiar
    electric shudder and tingle move through his body and with a groan of "Oh shit!" started
    clicking through the options to see what he had done.

    As he clicked into the Chronivac help pages, Jeff noticed that he couldn't feel his feet and
    looking down was horrified to find that his shoes and feet had disappeared. Pushing
    himself away from the desk with his hands he made as if to stand up but instead slid off
    the chair landing on the floor in sight of the bedroom mirror.

    Jeff turned his head to look into the mirror and saw that his legs seemed to be
    disappearing inside the suit pants he was wearing. He began to get a sinking feeling
    suspecting that he might soon be enjoying a very different perspective on the world.

    Determined that he was not going to lose control, Jeff reached towards the chair to pull
    himself back in front of his computer but looking towards his hands notices that his
    fingers were pulling back into his hands which were themselves shrinking. He brought his
    hand up to his face and watched fascinated as it seemed to turn inside out, becoming a
    hollow space disappearing into the arm of his suit jacket. The fleshy colour of his inside
    out skin melded with the lining of the jacket even as the fabric for the sleeve flapped in
    front of his eyes and before he knew what was happening realised that both his arms and
    legs has disappeared, transformed into wool and silk.

    Looking back to the mirror, Jeff looked on as his strong, athletic torso deflated, the shirt
    and tie dissolving into his flesh which itself began to merge with the cloth of the suit.

    Finally, unable even to move his head, Jeff stared as his face in the mirror as it shrivelled
    up, sinking into the collar of the suit coat and finally disappearing from view altogether.

    Jeff lay helpless on the floor. He was fully able to see as if his head was still in place but
    the only thing in his field of vision was a suit coat and pants lying on the floor in front of
    the mirror.

    He felt odd - at the back of his mind, Jeff felt a strange desire to be worn, for someone to
    put him on so that he could transform them as he himself had been transformed. A
    sudden pulling sensation in his arms and legs caught Jeff by surprise and looking in the
    mirror he saw himself being lifted as if by invisible hands. In a moment, he saw his pants
    fold neatly in mid air before a coat hanger appeared and he slipped into it. His coat part
    followed and Jeff saw that he was now suspended from a wooden coat hanger.

    Before Jeff even had time to think, there was a flash of light and he suddenly found
    himself...
