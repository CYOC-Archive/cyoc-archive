    middle man and be judge and jury and remake criminals into useful members of society by
    either turning them into slaves and over-writing the person they already are?'

    Marie-Eliza had a new appreciation for God. 'The problem with being all powerful… is
    doing anything.'

    Marie-Eliza's avatar considered granting Rick's demented wish of becoming a little girl, or
    separating that desire from him and turning it into a twin of her. But Marie didn't trust
    herself with this power, and she didn't dare trust someone else. And a girl born from a
    wish of escapism wouldn't be exactly restrictive on such god like power.

    In one of the many doorless bathrooms of the manor Anne checked the test results… this
    was the forth test. Lupman had perhaps the single least amount of modesty and sense of
    shame ingrained into them and their culture as any of the races of men (gargoyles were
    humanoid by convenience only), but Anne still felt a bit like hiding.

    "I'm going to be a mother . . . " Anne whispered. "Megan, Ashley, Margot, they were all
    right . . . I am pregnant . . . " Anne just hoped she could explain to the twins that they
    weren't being replaced. In the storm of a thousand plots and thousand intentions, Anne's
    pregnancy was just a little thing, such a tiny thing.

    realized she needed to tell her husband and ran like mad though the mansion, finding the
    white wolf formally lithe now muscle bound (she wished she had done a better job of
    helping him realize God loved all them regardless of how they looked).

    She leapt into his arms at once, nearly sending him through a wall and declared,
    "LAWRENCE WE'RE GOING TO HAVE BABIES I'M PREGNANT!"

    Considering everything Lawrence had learned in the last few days listening to Marie-Eliza's
    tale (not knowing he had barely escaped ceasing to exist as Lawrence), and everything that
    had happened to the pack as of late. He had just one reply to that, "Finally, something
    normal." He then kissed and hugged his wife.

    place us in Amy's Shinto shrine once everyone is healed?"

    "Want to bail out? Unlike Amaterasu and Tsukuyomi-no-Mikoto, you and Uke Mochi

    moving world."

    "Meh. This is the path I chose, I'm going to see it to the end."

    "I can understand that… and … actually I'm not scared… I'M EXCITED! I can't wait for it!"

    "Aren't worried it's gonna be like being chopped into pieces?"

    "A little. But everything I've seen about your transformation has proven to be peaceful,
    calm, and serene. And we're not being broken or melted down, you said your Transform or
    Dare prevented that. So we're just being naturally taken apart, and being put back together.
    Let me try something."

    For a moment there were two twin statues of Sarah the she-wolf, both half the size of the
    original, then she was once again the likeness of Ame-no-Uzume-no-Mikoto.

    "And how was that dear?" Monkey King asked.

    "It didn't feel like I was being cut in two, it felt like I was simply in more than one place.
    Since we don't have brains, a nervous system, or eyeballs, or ears, I think our awareness is
    equally distributed among our forms."

    "What about you Uke Mochi? It just takes a thought to walk away. Heck, you could leave
    and come back once the move was done."

    " . . . No . . . I want to see through to the end."

    "Don't be silly… it's not an end! Simply a new beginning!"

    "Oh right, thank you."

    "A little over poetic but once." Thought out Tsukuyomi-no-Mikoto.

    'If you wish me, I will go, I am already pretty much not human, and if you desire one of the

    Father Mike was so distracted by his brother decision (which he was quite steadfast) he
    accidentally sent some of the cure instead of some holy water to the address the Ponies
    provided (in the middle of nowhere but still had a viable location . . . until it decided to
    shift to insure it's occupants happily-ever-after as it nearly had when Kurse was ready to
    turn the world into soup).

    This included instructions for what it for and how to use it.

    The delivery man figuring he was at some estate run by some insane toy lover who got
    their hands on the game, delivered the package and got of there in a flash and felt no need

    The Ponies couldn't hide forever, and didn't plan to, they simply didn't wish for Ponyland to
    become a tourist attraction.

    Night and Day examined the package, read the instructions, figuring it was for them, and
    found a hoe, and began to dig a circle around Glendale's city limits, the work going much
    faster than they thought it would.

    "Hope we don't run out." Said Day, and the salve containing the cure didn't run dry until
