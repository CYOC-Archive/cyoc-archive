    Looking to the tower scarlet could see that Skye's only advantage was her elevated postion. From her perspective the the field looked like a rave. Multi colored lasers fireing off in all directions. Most plowing into Skye's tower.

    If she was going to make it to her friend, she was going to need some covering fire. Since she didn't have any other help in the game, she'd have to make it herself.

    "Maybe... That could work..." Scar doubled back to her previous battle scene. The pile of arms was nowhere to be seen, he must have managed to crawl off somewhere. However his laser gun rested on the floor near where he'd fallen. Skye took her 'mouth' gun in her hair hand and picked up the new one. Two guns was a good start, but not enough.

    A wet flopping sound drew Scar's attention to the massive mouth that had once been her adversary, her 'arm' gun. "Ah, there you are." She whispered. With light steps she approached the mouth. It's tongue licking its lips and occasionally planting on the ground and dragging itself across the ground.

    She reached down with her free hand takeing the gun. Without warning the mouth's tongue lulled over, warping around Scar's arm. Out of sheer reflex Scar shot her 2nd gun into at the orb of flesh with a light trigger pull.

    "Who's there!" The mouth shouted in it's booming feminine voice. On its flesh where Scar had hit, the flesh parted slightly revealing a singular hazel eye. It blinked and darted about before settling on Scar. "You!?" It boomed! "How did... Wait? The guns still work once their dropped?"

    Scar looked at her three guns before looking back at the eye."yeah, looks that way? Sorry for shooting you again."

    "Alls fair in love and war, besides it's kind of boring just being a mouth. Hey? Think you can give me some arms with my gun?"

    "I can try?"

    Scar circled the mouth fireing full blasts into the sides of the former girl. In seconds she was up on eight arms, scurrying in place like a spider. With one small eye facing forward, eight arm legs, and a giant mouth and tongue projecting from its side. Truely an unsettling sight.

    "If you want I can give you another eye?"

    "No this is fine, I think I'll go out on the field and scare some other players like this. Just make sure you use those guns to win this thing newbie!"

    The odd monster then creeped out of the chamber turning the corner and sprinting out of sight. Moments later Scar could hear the booming voice of her new friend roar in the distance and panicked screams of other players; "what the heck is that?" "Shootitshootit!" Fallowed by the far off glow of laser fire.

    "This all worked out better then I planed!" Scar cheered to herself. "Now for the 2nd step..."

    Takeing her third gun and pointing it at her side she fired a full shot. A growing warmth began to protrude from her side, five small digits bulging from it. It quickly grew into a fully functional arm, seated below her original. She repeated the process on her other side, leaving Scar with one hand four arms. Three of which were armd with laser guns.

    She had more fire power but over the walls Scar could still see the light show of the tower beaming at full speed. Would three guns really be enough?
