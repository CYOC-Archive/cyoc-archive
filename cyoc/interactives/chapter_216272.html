    The gas took effect on Tim, and as the film went on, Tim began to grow!

    But the gas had no effect on clothing.

    Tim suddenly felt a funny feeling in his gut.
    "Man...that pizza we had at dinner landed hard there," he said. Then he felt wobbly.

    Suddenly, he started to grow.
    Tim was the first to notice that his shirt and jeans were tighter on him than usual.

    "Ernie," Tim said, "have you done something to my laundry?" he asked.
    "No." Ernie said. "It seems you might be growing. Look, you're almost a head taller than me."

    "THat's preposterous." Tim said. "people don't just grow like this on their own." But as he said this, his clothes started to split. The seams of his shirt began to split apart, and his jeans began to rip.

    (i)I don't understand. What—(/i)
    But Tim never got to finish that statement as he felt his feet hurting.
    He looked down to see his toes popping out of the front of his shoes, as his feet began to swell to much larger than their original size.

    "Holy shit!" Tim exclaimed. "You're right, Ernie! I AM growing!"
    Indeed. Tim's legs grew bigger, and his jeans, including his underwear, split and collapsed.
