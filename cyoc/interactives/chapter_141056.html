    "I feel a bit guilty having Nick be our test subject..." Alice 2 said.
    "Well, I know I don't what to be the guinea pig, so obviously you don't either," Alice 1 reasoned.
    "I don't know how to work this thing at all. I wish I understood this thing at all. I mean what does gift even mean?" Alice 0 asked.

    As they were trying to decide, what they were going to do. Nick started to change on Alice 1s bed. Much like the other 2, his hair started to lengthen and turn red. His mostly naked body started to shrink and body started to lose its excess hair. His boxers started to shift to a familiar red piece of clothing, and a small red top covered his new chest. They were a copy of Alice 0s breast not the new ones. The transformation was now over, and the new girl now opened her eyes.

    "Hey where did Nick's profile go?" Alice 2 said.
    "Huh, you're right I don't see it anywhere did you lose it?" she accused Alice 1.
    "Really, I would've not messed around with it if you didn't," Alice 0 reasoned.
    "Of course, she didn't on purpose," Another familiar voice said behind them. A fourth Alice was now sitting on the bed staring at them.
    "Oh god are you Nick?" Alice 1 said.
    "Nope, I'm an Alice too, but I was Nick," Alice 3 explained.

    Alice 3 now had knowledge of the program and thoroughly explained how it worked.

    "Let me show you something," Alice 3 now took over the computer and started making changes to her profile. "Look across at Nick's room."

    Nick's room with it wide open door now started to mirror the one they were in. The Alices' all realized what she had done.

    "DID YOU JUST KILL NICK?"Alice 0 asked mortified.
    "Alice, he never existed now," Alice 3 justified to her.
    "No, you just killed him," Alice 1 said.
    "Ok, look I know you guys would change me back after I explained everything, so I just wanted to insure myself. You guys would do it too." Alice 3 said.
    "That doesn't justify you doing that!" Alice 2 angrily replied.
    "Then, who do you think you are?" Alice 3 slyly asked.
    "Huh, oh no..." Alice 2 whispered.
    "Oh my god, you must be someone related to him Nick!" Alice 0 realized.
    "Yes, you guys were, and as far as I can tell we'll never know..." Alice 3 said.
    "I think I'm gonna puke..." Alice 1 rushed out of the room and to the bathroom.

    Alice 2 was shortly following after her to the downstairs bathroom. Alice 0 looked stunned at Alice 3 not believing what Alice 3 was implying. Her actions truly surprised her was she truly that selfish? Or maybe it was just the knowledge she had been given that made her act that way.

    "Why can we remember you being Nick but not who they were?" Alice 0 questioned.
    "It's because I set it that way I would change reality, but I made it so no mental changes would happen to us," Alice 3 said.
    "What are you saying?" Alice 3 replied.
    "I'm still you, and I needed to show you the seriousness of this machine. If I hadn't done it like this, what would we have done?"
    "We definitely... would go crazy and start changing the world," Alice 0 grimly said.
    "We would've for sure, but... we still... we still could," Alice 3 said.
