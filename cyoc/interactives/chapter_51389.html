Before he could do anything, a box popped up. "DISCLAIMER: Changes made in this package alter history of selected area as well. Use wisely and in moderation."

    On the screen, a 3D model of the earth appeared. He could rotate it, selecting different countries, continents, the entire world, or just individual cities. When he selected any of these, a box popped up, showing varied stats. He could show how many males and females exist in Asia or whether there is more cats than dogs in New York. He could also alter these ratios. He clicked on the city he lived in, Deerville, and found that 48.993% of citizens are male, 51% are female, and .007% are "Other". He clicked Other to get a description reading "Any gender existing out of the norm of Male and Female, i.e. shemales, hermaphrodites, etc."

    He could change his entire city, the entire US, or the whole planet!
