    Jeff, the muscular cop, is in total disbelief about his situation. He thinks he kind of fainted and fell into a very weird daydream. It cannot be possible, no one can shrink at all. Dave, the trucker, has no idea why it happened.

    (Jeff) Hey Sir, do you have anything to do with that? What are you up to? he said confused but still trying to be in control, he is a cop after all.

    (Dave) I have no idea officer, Gee I have a life. A job, a house, friends, it’s so unfair!

    Then, a car in no-so-good condition pulls over. It’s a bit rusty, there is a crack on the windshield, and it smells like weed. The handles of two lawnmowers can be seen in the trunk. Two boys stepped out, one looks around 16-17 years old, and the other one more around 13. They look quite alike so they are obviously brothers. At first, the two doll-sized men do not pay much attention to them. But they get closer to have a better look, they’ve spotted them, so they stopped out of curiosity. They saw a truck, a police patrol car, and clothes left behind. But they were very surprised to see two action figures without any clothes on. But soon enough, they saw them moving and heard them talking. For the record, the eldest boy’s name is Marc, the younger is Al.

    (Al) whoa Marc, look at them they look so real! So fancy toys just left there.

    (Marc) Maybe somebody is still around... HELLO ANYBODY THERE?

    Jeff and Dave are not sure how to react toward the giant boys. They need help, but will they be friendly or some foes?

    (Jeff) Listen up kids, we are not toys, we shrunk and have no idea why. It’s the law to give assistance to a policeman who requests you to do it, So will you comply or be in trouble?

    Marc and Al were not bad boys, but teens can be a bit rebellious now and then. Besides none of them liked the way a so tiny guy talked to them. As the patrol car was nearby, they presumes he was the policeman who probably made the trucker pull over.

    (Al) Hey, Mr the Officer, you are not in a position to be rude to us. Besides, it’s a felony to be naked in public, and this is a public road. You and the tiny trucker may be prosecuted for gross indecency.

    Marc was in shock to hear what his smart little bro just replied to the rude tiny guy. But, he was very proud he did though.

    (Marc) Hey Al, we cannot just leave them there. Some animals may hunt them, or a bad person may want to hurt them.

    Quickly, the boys wrapped the naked 12 inches tall men in their too-large clothes and carried them to the crappy car. To keep them safe they put the clothes in a box. So the fabric could protect them against accelerations, stops, etc. The men has difficulty budging inside and they were separated by layers and layers of fabric. But they were able to talk to each other though.

    (Dave) Are you alright Officer Jeff?

    (Jeff) Yeah, kind of, but do you realize they may not help us? They are kids and we look like perfect toys, action figures. I doubt they will want to part from us now.

    (Dave) Well, talking to them in a stern tone like you did maybe did not help, don’t you think?

    (Jeff) Well, it’s a little late for regrets now. We need to find a way to contact the police central office to get help.

    Suddenly the car stopped and heard a first car door open, then another one. The box shacked a little, and they know they were carried somewhere as they felt the movements. They heard a boy saying loud: “Dad, we are home!” Then: “Oh that is good, sons, I hope you have had a good day mowing the grass of some of your clients!” After that, one of the boys simply replied: “Yeah it was OK, thanks for asking Dad!”

    (Dave) Oh, looks like there is at least an adult here, maybe he may help us and reason with his sons.

    The box has been carried to the second floor. A little while after, the lid has been removed. Both boys were moving the clothes where the tiny men were wrapped inside. Dave was the first one to feel a giant hand grabbing him by his waist, it was Marc, the eldest. Then, Al grabbed the tiny policeman the same way.

    What the boy have in mind?
