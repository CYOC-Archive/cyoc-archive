    Anthro Costume Nexus: Dinosaur Room

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by No One A year ago A BM O Anthro Reptilian Reality alteration

    The room you entered looked like the inside of a cave, with rough stone walls, and stalactites hanging down from the ceiling, torches on the walls illuminating the room. To your left is a mirror that extends the entire wall, to the right a series of dinosaur costumes, and across an identical wooden door that says “exit” above it.

    Disregarding everything else you go over to the exit door and try to open it only to find it of course locked. You let out a sign of defeat then turn to the wall of costumes. Like the note said this was likely your only way out of here, you had to pick a costume then enter whatever world you ended up in. Many of the costumes here are very high quality. You don’t know what material it is, but it looks and feels almost like real scales. They must’ve cost a fortune if they were made by hand and weren’t just poofed into existence by magic or whatever. Eventually you make your choice and select a costume you felt would best suit you, the entire wall then rotates around locking you into your choice of costume…
