    Mrs. Taranza And Her Two Favorites

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 4 months ago AR S

    41-year-old Maria Taranza is roomed with two of her favorite students: 18-year-old Nancy Lille from France and 18-year-old Lili Pianta from Italy. Both had been here through an exchange program in the past but had wanted their stay in the United States to be more permanent as neither was from the great parts of their respective countries. Maria liked these two because they were among the sweetest students in the class and were always a gem to have in class

    "Wow, this is gonna be such a fun week" Lili says in Italian. Whenever Lili gets too excited or angry, she would often say things in Italian whereas Nancy still could speak very good French but rarely spoke it since she was the only one who spoke it. Both were with some friends Maria knew whom had taken great care of them, kinda doting on them since neither had daughters of their very own

    "Yeah, this should be a fun stay, I agree. Can't wait to see what you all looked like as kids" Nancy adds. Maria then turns on a TV to explain how the stay will function for them. The presentation begins....

    she picked up the envelope and opened it up to reveal a letter then reading it. A woman in a white room is shown on the screen

    "Dear the occupants of this room,

    First of all, my name is Allison Highsmith, I'm the owner and allow me to welcome you to the Transformation Resort. I hope this trip is worth it. Now I’m pretty aware that you have chosen the Age Wing of the resort. To that end let me give you some rules and advice.

    As you already know, this resort can transform their guests into anything and anyone they want. The Age Wing in particular can age or regress the guest into whatever age they want. But of course there are a couple of ways to do that. The room your in is one of them. It can help you change your age just by thinking about it. All you have to do is to picture the age you want to be and the room will do it for you. If you don’t want to change your age in your room, there are other things in the wing that can suit you better. All of our restaurants have a special ingredient on their food for appropriate ages, eat them and you will regress to that age. Our shopping center has different ways in changing your age. We got a pool and a spa for just regression, like a Fountain of Youth. And lastly we have some entertainment to help accommodate your age changes.

    Just a bit of a warning before you start. Age does have a limit from zero (newborn age) to a hundred. If you go past a hundred, you will instantly die from old age. If you all tenants below ten-years-old a babysitter or a substitute mother will be assigned to the room to take care of you. Don’t worry if you regressed past newborn, you would be instantly inside the woman we would assign you with. But if you regressed past conceivement, you will not die, only become just an egg and will need to wait to be reconceived again. And for the clothing and other necessities in your luggage, don’t worry. When you change your age, you clothes will change to match that age.

    Once again, thank you for choosing the Transformation Resort and I hope you have yourself a safe and wonderful stay"

    the TV then turns off

    "100? Who'd even wanna get old in the first place?" asks Lili

    "Y-yeah that doesn't sound all that fun. Maybe a little older to see what our adult selves are like but I wouldn't wanna get that old" Nancy replies

    "Yeah, uh, no. Anyway, how do we wanna start this ladies?" asks Maria, and the three give it a long thought

    What do you do now?
