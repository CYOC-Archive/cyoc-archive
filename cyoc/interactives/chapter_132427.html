    The Magic Shop

    Gloria’s engagement gift

    added by O 5 years ago AR BM

    As promised Gloria and her friends arrived at the magic shop after school. The meeting, while brief, was informative. Merlin would be available to the girls if they wanted to learn more about the ring. The ancient wizard even offered to give the girls magical items of their own at some point. He only asked that they wait until they were a little older.

    Gloria had tried to listen to Merlin but found her mind wondering. She was so happy to have Kellie as a sister that she wanted to do something special for her and Katie. Racking her brain she was unable to come up with any ideas. Groaning in frustration she gazed down at her ring. As she did a thought crept into her head.

    The rest of the week went quietly for Gloria, she was so absorbed in her plan that she never even used her ring once. As Friday rolled around she decided to put her plan into action. Diane and Naomi were going to see a movie together while Sasha was having a father, daughter evening with his girlfriend Claire. As a result Gloria invited Jackie and Felicia over in order to put her plan into action. As Gloria was lounging around her living room she heard a knock at the door. Quickly the young teen sprinted to the front door, flinging it open to see Jackie and Felicia.

    “Thanks for coming over guys,” she smiled as Jackie and Felicia entered to house. “Sure no problem,” Jackie answered. “But what’s with all the secrecy? You said you needed our help with something important.” Gloria smiled impishly as she took her friends upstairs. “I’ll tell ya u upstairs,” she said. “But first we need to meet our other guests.” Jackie and Felicia gave each other curious looks as they followed Gloria to her room.

    As they entered Gloria quickly closed the door before glancing at her watch. “They should be here any minute,” she mused. “Who,” Felicia asked. Before Gloria could answer Katie and Kellie appeared in Gloria’s room. The two college age women smiled as they looked at their little sister. “Hey Gloria,” Kellie said warmly. “So what was so important that you wanted to see me and Katie?”

    “Well,” Gloria grinned. “I’ve been thinking about it all week and I wanted to give you two an engagement gift. That is if you trust me.”

    “Of course we trust you,” Katie said warmly hugging her sister. “And by the way I think that is the sweetest thing in the world. So, go ahead what’s your gift idea?”

    Gloria smiled as she looked first at her friends, then Katie and Kellie. “Okay well here it goes,” she said. “I wish Katie and Kellie were the physical age they were when they went on their first date.”

    Jackie and Felicia’s jaws dropped as Gloria’s ring began to glow. Turning towards her sisters she noticed that the college age girls looked closer to high school now. “Well I was thinking,” Gloria said proudly. “You two had such fond memories of your first kiss I figured I would let you two relive it for the night, with me, Jackie and Felicia as your personal drivers.”

    Katie and Kellie burst into a fit of laughter, causing Gloria to give them a curious look. “What’s so funny,” she asked. A now 17 year old Katie and Kellie smiled at their sister as they observed their bodies youthen. “Gloria sweetly,” Katie said. “Mine and Kellies first kiss wasn’t our first date.” Gloria’s jaw dropped as she saw her sisters become 16 once more. Both girls breast seemed to have shrunk, going from c cups to b cups. Their faces also appeared to be younger as well, leaving adulthood and creeping closer to childhood.

    “What do you mean that wasn’t your first date,” Gloria exclaimed as Katie and Kellie shrank to 15. The two teens giggled as they looked at their increasingly young bodies creep closer to childhood. “Do you want to tell her or should I,” Kellie said, her voice beginning to rise in octave. Katie beckoned for Kellie to answer as she adjusted her baggy clothing. “Well Gloria,” Kellie said, shrinking down to 14. “The thing is Katie and me have been friends for years. We may of had our first kiss at 16 but our first....well date? Was a play date.”

    “Wait a minute,” Jackie said, dumbfounded. “Are you telling me that....”

    Kellie and Katie nodded and smiled as they became 13. “Yep,” they answered with youthful enthusiasm. “Our first so called date, we weren’t much older than six or seven.”

    Jackie, Felicia and Gloria now gaped at Katie and Kellie as the two girls broke into a fit of laughter. “Guess your three are babysitting tonight,” Katie laughed. “Hope you have some fun activities planned for us.”

    Gloria groaned as she looked at Jackie and Felicia. “Really,” Jackie groaned. “You called us over to babysit?”

    “I didn’t know,” Gloria pleaded.

    “It’s the thought that counts,” Felicia said cheerfully. “And who knows, maybe this will be a lot of fun.”

    Gloria sighed as she looked at her now younger sister and her girlfriend. She had been hoping for a fun evening with a teenage sister and her fiancé, instead it appeared she would be babysitting with her friends. Gloria now tried to think of something fun to do for a pair of six year old girls.
