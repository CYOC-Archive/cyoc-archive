    As Kyle slowly chewed and finally swallowed those delightful little berries, he thought about what else he could do with this "curse."

    He came to a few quick realizations...

    A: There was a small problem in how he had worded Jenny's transformation. Namely that anyone made "just like Jenny" would be his problem if they became unable to get home due to being eaten, and that could get out of hand quite quickly...

    B: Several of Jenny's friends were beginning to get a little nervous.

    C: There were a few other "fun" ideas he had in mind...

    "Hey, Sam, I have a small proposal that I think you just might like. If you can turn away from Beth for just a minute..."

    Sam, despite being quite anxious to test out the "new and improved" Beth, decided to give Kyle the benefit of the doubt. He had after all given him a rather sweet deal the last time.

    "Ok, but make it quick. I've got important business over here..."

    Kyle quickly explain out the problem, and the proposed solution. After which, Sam just smiled...

    "You are one devious and perverted little geek, but I'm starting to like the way you think..."

    "Sam, Transform or Dare?"

    "Dare of course."

    Kyle moved in close, so he could whisper his dare to Sam, as it would require a little secrecy.

    "Sam, I dare you to do the following

    First, Transform or Dare Jess, forcing her to take both options, like you did to Beth.
    Next, Dare Jess the same way you did to Beth, and Transform her into a Taffy girl, similar to Jenny, but mobile, able to speak, and subservient to me.
    Finally, you will be the one to take Beth home from now on, in the event that her changes leaves her immobile."

    Sam grinned, said "Thanks" and took another slice of Jenny, from her left knee. As he turned towards Jess, she started to back away, frightened...

    "Please, Sam, no..."

    "Oh don't worry, you're gonna love this."

    It took a few minutes, but he eventually caught her, and filled her mouth just like he had done to Beth.

    "Transform or Dare Jess."

    After the minute was up, he let Jess go, and she crumpled to the floor, very much fearful of her fate.

    "Jess, I dare you to give the curse back to Kyle in a harmless way. Once you have done that, you will transform into a taffy girl, and follow all of the rules that Kyle gave Jenny, except you will be allowed to speak and move. You will however, be completely subservient to Kyle."

    Jess gaped at how her future had just been twisted... She was glad she would be able to speak and move, but to be Kyle's Slave???

    She walked up to Kyle, and did her duty.

    "Transform or Dare"

    "I'll choose Dare."

    Jess knew this would be her last free thought, so she might as well make sure she was a happy.

    "I dare you to give me as much pleasure as you can, each and every night we are together."

    It was technically harmless, but as long as she was going to be a slave, she was going to be a happy one...

    Kyle, for his part, was a little shocked. That "harmless" dare was only just barely. Not that he was complaining, but it did surprise him a bit.
