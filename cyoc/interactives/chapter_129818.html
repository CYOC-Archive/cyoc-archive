    prisoners
    to Slave Island,where
    their
    Cyclops
    partners

    Fel'Hiren had been born into slavery

    The Cyclops were cruel and Fel Hiren
    knew if he did not do anything
    his son Thoren
    would not live to see
    another day.

    a black scaled adult male

    in submission
    and lowered his belly to the rocky ground.

    It was his occasional outbursts

    that had

    Fel Hiren onto the bad books

    Other slaves had warned him through their
    gossip channels

    that
    Master Thugg was planning to teach
    all the dragons a lesson

    and since he particularly
    disliked
    Fel Hiren

    to be careful.

    Alas the adult father
    had made his mistake
    and now
    Master Thugg
    was coming over
    a evil grin on his
    face
    the padlock keys jangling
    on his belt

    to unlock young Thoren
    from the rest of the
    youth slave
    chain gang.


    Clikkkk
    went the padlock
    holding Thoren to the
    chain
    and the Cyclops towered over the terrified young
    dragon
    and began
    uncoiling
    his razor raspy whip
    for punishment.

    "No please no" screamed
    Fel Hirens sister
    Aqualaine and Thorens
    cousins
    also pleaded for
    Master Thugg to
    reconsider.

    "Don't take my son"

    "Take me instead if you must"

    Fel Hiren to the

    Cyclops slave driver

    to vibrate and
    hum and a puzzled
    Master Thugg
