    A Flower Of Sugar And Spice And Everything Nice

    It is the strangest transformation. Frightening for you, of course, but very, very strange. So much it becomes fascinating. Your body cannot move at all, but your arms are forced upwards, your finger elongating, spreading and becoming wider. They take a soft baby pink hue, just like the rest of your body. Similarly, your head flattens and becomes round. The rest of your body follows as well, your chest becoming even more of a circle and swallowing your legs. Your legs become stumps, your feet following suit, then being absorbed by the ball that your chest and belly have become. In fact, it's impossible to distinguish either by now. From your neck erupts yellow, dusty and fluffy appendages. Your face retreats in your neck, erasing your sight and smell. You can still hear, though. In fact, the last thing you see is a close-up of many petals and pistils. A flower. You've become a flower with delicate pink petals. As big as the fairy and wider than her by far, but a flower nevertheless. Without roots, even. So why a flower? To be kept in a vase or cup full of water, to wilt and die? You would shake in utter fear if it was possible. Is that to be your fate? What a horrible prospect! Belle claps her hands in joy and laughs happily.
    "You are so pretty! And you smell like sugar and spice! Everyone will love you!"
    So that is your fate? Just to smell good? A scent you can't even enjoy yourself? What if bees then pollinate you? Is that possible? Will it hurt? Will you sprout seed and make babies that way? How horrendous! But then, incredibly, you feel your new, very strange body harden. What is that now?
    "Oh, you've crystallized! My spell worked perfectly!"
    Crystallized?
    "You are now a beautiful flower to be eaten. Then you'll make the most adorable baby girl in the tummy of the fay that will have swallowed you, male or female! You'll be an adorable and most beautiful fay female ever! Isn't that wonderful? Aren't you lucky! Fay creatures love children, especially little girls. They adore adopting them and coddling them! And to feel them grow in their belly... Many desire only that! And you'll spent months in the warm comforting womb of a mommy or daddy! Aren't you happy? Many will pay handsomely for such as feminine bloom as you. You smell and taste like sugar and spice and everything nice! You'll be utterly irresistible!"
    She grabs you in her hands and flutters away, happy to see to whom she can give you to munch on. You just feel awful. Any fay creature will want to eat you? So he or she can become pregnant with you and carry you because you'll be reborn as an adorable little girl? How vile! And it might be a male? Ugh! You can just hope whoever buys you is not a male fay.
