    The room is dark, nothing can be seen. Callum is still very much awake, bored, but awake. To his pain. He's never been able to sleep in awkward positions, and standing up is an odd position to sleep, even when you're inanimate. Callum has been left to his thoughts for hours, it has gotten to the stage of boredom. Plus the hint of despair he's had for a couple years.

    Callum sees the bedroom door open. But before he can see who is entering, there is a bright flash. Callum has no idea what it is as the next couple of seconds are nothing but empty darkness.

    The darkness gives way to daylight. Callum is confused. It was night time a mere few seconds ago. Now it looks to be dawn based on the golden light seeping through the curtains. He looks around to the best of his ability considering his pupils are fixed in place like every other part of his body. He sees his actual body, still in the same position as before. But suddenly something else catches his attention.

    Callum looks at it and sees Braiden shrunk, inanimated and cased just like he and Max. Braiden is wearing his school uniform, a navy coloured hoody, and some grey shorts. Along with black training shoes. Callum can't believe it. Braiden, his only chance of rescue is now trapped too. Granted he trapped them before, but Callum feels it wasn't supposed to be permanent. Just a temporary joke.
