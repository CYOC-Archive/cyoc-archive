    The idea of the admissions officer calling you mister rubbed you the wrong way, all your life you never really felt like a mister, or even like a man at all. Without having anyone to tell this to, it left you emotionally scarred and distant from even your family members. Well, now was the time to change that! You mustered up enough courage and took a deep breath before replying:

    “I want to become a female fox!”

    The sudden outburst startled the elephant so bad he almost fell from the massive chair he was sitting on. It took a minute for him to compose himself, but once he did, he stared at you directly.

    “You will NEVER make another outburst like that EVER again. Understood?”

    You quiver a bit in your seat as he resumes typing. It may not have been received well, but it felt good to get your desires out. Finally, you would be able to be who you truly wanted to be, and makes friends with someone just like you. Just the thought of not being burdened by your past was enough to motivate you to answer every question that the elephant asked you.

    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

    You were given an injection and a pill to take once a month, the first year would be you changing into your desired form and learning about societal norms as a new anthro, while the remaining years would be you building a social life, family life, or love live with those on campus. With your class schedule in hand and a heart full of hope, you trudge your way to the first year dorms. You can already tell how it’s going to be as you say plenty of kids varying from 14-19 roaming around campus. Each kid had some sort of demi transformation, whether it was fur sprouting, tails and wings forming, scales forming, or some other animal quality. Dodging some kids playing around you take a look at the dorm rules.

    No sex allowed inside the first year dorms.
    No nudity allowed in first year dorms (bathrooms excluded)
    Laundry must be done once a week at minimum.
    Be respectful to each other.
    No mingling of sexes except the cafeteria and lounge.
    No destruction of property. Fine $2000 min
    Any and all question will be directed to your rooming advisor located at the end of the hall of your respective floors.

    With the rules taken care of, you look back at your key. Your room is 2-91A, meaning you are on the second floor, ninth wing, first door on the left. You take the massive freight elevator up to the second floor with your things as you work up quite a sweat walking that long distance with all of your things. Finally getting to your dorm, you are greeted by…

    Who is waiting for you?
