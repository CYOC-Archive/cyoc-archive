    After trying to come up with an explanation to why a part of her seems to feel disgusted about dating a male, “Yumi” mentally shrugs, ‘I assume it’s just because I’ve never have dated someone before,’ she thinks to herself, ‘and since I’m still a virgin, I am a little nervous, but I doubt that he’d want to go THAT far so quickly’.

    “Yumi,” Will asks.

    “Oh, sorry, I was kind of zoned out for a minute,” she says.

    “If… you don’t want to date me,” Will says, “I’ll respe…,” he starts.

    “Oh, no,” “Yumi” says, cutting him off, “honestly, I actually would like to go on a date with you. You seem like a nice guy, it’s just that I’m a little nervous, I mean… I’ve never dated anyone before… and… well… I don’t know if I’m ready for… you know,” now it’s “Yumi’s” turn to blush.

    Will chuckles, “oh, don’t worry,” he says, “I’m not planning on “popping the cherry” this quickly. No, I’d rather get to know you more before going a step further. I mean… we don’t even know yet if we are a “perfect match”, so why rush when it’s not certain it’d work?”

    “Yumi” smiles, “good point,” she says, “so, when would you like the date to happen?”

    “How about tomorrow,” Will suggests, “that is, if you haven’t anything already planned”.

    “Yumi” smiles, “tomorrow works fine,” she says, “when do you want to meet?”

    “How about 6pm,” Will asks, “that would give me time to plan the “perfect first date”,” he then adds.

    “Sounds good to me,” the “young woman” says, “let’s exchange numbers,” she then says.

    After exchanging numbers, Will and “Yumi” continue to chat, joke and laugh, after a while Will checks his phone, his eyes widening, “damn, it’s already 1am,” he says, “I should better get home!”

    “Yeah, me too,” “Yumi” says.

    The two walk out the bar, “so, I guess I’ll see you tomorrow?”

    Will nods, “I’m looking forward to it,” he says, smiling.

    “Yumi” smiles back, “same,” she says, “good night, Will. See you tomorrow!”

    “Bye, Yumi,” Will says, “sleep well”.
