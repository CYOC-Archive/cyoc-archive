    Well, you may as well keep going…
    Another thing you’ve lied about your age to see is cosplay. At the start, it was your teenage brain going “Ogga Bogga, Hot Girl Go Brrrr”, but now, you’ve found an appreciation for the art. Unfortunately, your still developing body didn’t allow for anything. A fat, pimply teenager won’t get anything positive trying to dress up as Beyonetta, or 2B, or Zero Suit Samus. However, one night, a few weeks ago, a thought invaded your mind. ‘If someone had two cybernetic legs, then what’s stopping them from having a different lower body?’ And you’re planning on using that idea. There’s one girl, who made a lamia cosplay, and made that part of the internet go wild; even though the bit they were screaming about was essentially a sock on her legs. You think that someone who could actually move properly would make people go wild!
    You know how to do this… well, kinda. Bio-mechanical engineering is the type that deals with prosthetics, and you’re a child prodigy with everything related!
    Not yet, but anyways. You go to your special notes section, which you’ve learned will just automatically send the info where you want it! No need to spend a couple hours hunting for how to change hair colour anymore. You also decide to improve yourself. No point in going all this way just to be ugly!
    [Iry Fletcher is 6 foot tall. Her hair is ankle length, and deep purple. It is very thick, yet smooth, soft and silky. Very voluminous, too. It will be as low maintenance as possible. It will look as amazing when she takes daily showers with the very best shampoos as if she takes weekly showers with body wash. She has the ability to shift between her main hair and a secondary set of hair, which she can change everything about while time is paused. Her eyes are a piercing purple, but they can also be changed like her hair. Her face is feminine, yet definitely authoritarian. Her body is an Amazonian bombshell, with defined abs. Her breasts are the size of her head, perfectly perky and bouncy, whilst still soft as a cloud when touched. Her ass is sculpted, with only a little squish when held. Her hips are very wide, although they still flow perfectly into the rest of her body. Her bronze skin is nearly flawless, soft as a feather in some places, while hard as a rock in others. She is a quadruple amputee, missing everything below the lowest point of her pelvis, and her arms past her sides. However, due to her genius, she has revolutionized prosthetics of all kinds. Where her arms and legs once emerged, mechanical caps sit, which can attach to various types of prosthetics. Her ‘daily’ limbs look completely natural. No one can tell that they aren’t her real limbs, unless she takes them off, a process which is impossible to be done accidentally, yet still simple and quick enough that she can take off her arms in less than a second. She has many, many different types of arms and legs, and even a few complete lower body replacements, such as horse or spider. During the creation of these prosthetics, she has also discovered a brand-new battery, which has basically revolutionized electricity overnight. This has made her into a multimillionaire, with practically no end to the profit in sight; due to the fact that no-one else can make them, giving her a monopoly.]
    You stop writing, due to a lack of room, before the same shifting occurs, and the book becomes very hot. When it stops, the page is empty again, and shifting through the book leads you to notice your company has a page! You start writing some more, ensuring your company is a ‘unicorn’ as it’s called. You’re a very thorough writer; and you have to stop yourself from full on creating your staff. Now, for the cosplay bit.
    [Iry is very into cosplay. Her attention to detail, combined with her speed and skill and creating cosplay, make her an amazing cosplayer, although she hasn’t attended any events where cosplay would be appropriate, and mostly does it for fun.] You think for a bit, before adding [She will be convinced to attend by a combined effort from PR, Lucky, and Reya]
    Reya is your sister’s best friend… or at least, she was. Now, you’re pretty sure they’re a bit more than friends… She’s also the one that introduced you to Cosplay in the first place.
