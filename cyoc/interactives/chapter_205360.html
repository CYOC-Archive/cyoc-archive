    You look at the next few cards, which mostly deal with your life.

    41. What class would you be in? Lower, middle, upper?
    You pick the upper class, and you don’t notice anything yet. Your home moves to a small mansion, in the very best neighbourhood in the city.

    42. What would your family life be like?
    You get ready to write, and do so, in spades:
    [My life is very comfortable. My Mom and Dad are both from old money, so they haven’t had to work in their lives. They are a very rare sight in these circles; they met each other through an arranged marriage and fell nearly instantly in love. They are perfect for each other. My dad works as the head of finances for an LGBTQ+ charity, and My mom is the IT lead in said charity. They are the best parents one could ask for. I came out to them when I was 14, and while they didn’t understand at first, they found a passion in helping other LGBTQ+ youths. I have a big brother, Mathew, who seems to be following in Mom and Dad’s footsteps; His girlfriend is the eldest from another old money family, and their first date was arranged by my maternal grandmother and her maternal grandmother, and so far, they’ve been perfect for each other two. Nama, (Your matchmaker grandmother,) was the only one in your extended family who accepted you as gay and was only disappointed “Because Nama has to look even harder for a good match for you now!” Speaking of Mathew, he’s the perfect big brother, if a bit overprotective, to both you and Annabelle, your younger sister. She seems to have inherited Nama’s matchmaking ability, if pairing up her friends together means anything. Mom’s side of the family is German, and Dad’s is from Quebec. Due to their cultures, it’s not unusual for me to have a little beer with lunch, or a glass of box wine with dinner. It’s very rare for me to get drunk, though, due to my insane tolerance to alcohol.]
    You’re actually surprised that all of that fits on the card.

    43. How changed do you want your life to be?
    You think about it, and write:
    [Not that changed. Due to my… eccentricity, no one really cares about what I do, so long as I can show up at the required functions and sit there and look pretty. I don’t have security, as no one in my family has ever been attacked. No one treats me differently than normal, even if they’re standing in my massive bedroom.]

    There are four questions left.
    44. If you could have any activities that you could do without losing time, (Example: you walk into the movies at 10:00, and walk out after watching twenty 2-hour movies at 10:01) what activities would you pick?
    ‘Oh, this one’s obvious,’ you think, and write [I would pick exercising, studying, and sleep. If possible, I would also make it so anyone who takes part in these activities with me is also affected.]
    Nothing obvious happens, but time starts winding itself around you.

    45. If you transformed into a girl, would you like to be aware of it?
    ‘I guess I would?’ you think, unaware of your own transformation.
    [Yes, I would like to be aware of the transformation, and also have both sets of memories clearly separated in my mind.]
    Nothing happens, yet.

    46. If you transformed into a girl, would you like anyone else to be aware of it?
    You’re confused about this one; but decide to go with [Yeah, My best friends would similarly be aware of both sets of memories, but won't be able to access the male set until I tell them about my transformation]
    Nothing happens, and nothing will happen.

    47. If you were a girl, what would your height, weight, and name be?
    ‘Well, this Amazonian goddess wouldn’t fit being 4 foot 4, nor would she be 15 billion tons.’ You pick a name, and a nickname, and write:
    [My name would be Evangeline Aeber, my nickname would be V, I would weigh 222 pounds, and be 6’6”]
    The moment you finish the last stroke of the inch sign, whatever has kept you from noticing your change clicks off. You look down, and a sea of cleavage contained by a black crop top with a golden ‘fleur de lis’ on the nipple fills your gaze.

    What do you do now?
