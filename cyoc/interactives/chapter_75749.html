    As you looked around, you notice a new exhibit not far from your family. You pointed to it and said, “Hey dad! Why don’t we go in there?” Your father looked at where you were pointing at and saw a huge T-Rex head entrance with a sign above it that says, ‘Dinosaur Land’. He looked at that and responded, “Dinosaur Land huh? Must be a special kind of exhibit. Let’s go check it out.” The family went into the mouth of the T-Rex and went through the doors. When they enter the place, they were in a reception area with was filled with a prehistoric wallpaper with tree like ceilings and grass like carpets. There was a female receptionist at the desk and she responded in a quirky expression.

    “Hello! Welcome to Dinosaur Land! How can I help you?”

    Your father looked at the receptionist and said, “Yes hi. I’m here with my family for a little fun at the zoo and my son noticed your exhibit in the main lobby. So tell me, what is this exhibit about. Is this like an museum with animatronic dinosaurs or fossils?” The receptionist gave a bit of a smile and responded, “Oh no. We don’t do anything like that. This exhibit is more of a hands on experience. Once you enter this exhibit, your life would forever change.” Your father listened to what the receptionist said and was a little unsure of her words. But looking at you, he realized that you really want to see this exhibit. So he looked up at the receptionist and said, “Okay, we’ll check it out.” The receptionist smiled and said, “Great! Now I want you to walk through those doors if you please. Trust me, you’ll be in for a really good surprise.”

    Your father looked at the doors and nodded for a thank you. Soon enough, you and your family approached the doors and opened them up. When they went through them, they were amazed to see what you saw. All around you and your family was a whole new world filled with exotic plants, a steep river, and an active volcano from afar. But what really shocked them the most is that they were seeing dinosaurs, real life dinosaurs. There were Pterodactyls flying high in the sky, stegosauruses eating some greens, velociraptors on the hunt, and a couple of cavemen scouting the area. It was amazing. You looked around this new land and said, “Wow! This place is so cool!” Your father looked at this too and couldn’t agree with you as well. “Yeah… This place sure is something. Wonder if this is some kind of virtual reality world they created?” Your mother looked at him and told him, “Easy there honey. There’s nothing to worry about. I’m sure this is just something they added to excite every child. Let our son have some fun here for a little bit and then we’ll check the other animals. Okay?” Your father looked at your mother and responded back to her, “All right sweetie, whatever you say.” She gave him a kiss in the cheek as you looked at them a little disgusted.
