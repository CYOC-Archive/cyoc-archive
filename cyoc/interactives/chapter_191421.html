    The magic shop sold a new rare product that was coined by its creator as the X-change pill. This pill was very sinple but had many different uses.

    The X-change pills initially seem like ordinary pills but they are anything but ordinary. When an X-change pill is ingested after about 5 minutes it will cause whoever ingested the pill to instantly transform into the body of whoever they were thinking about at that current moment. The X-change pills will NOT however change reality so people will still remember who you were before and wonder where you want and god knows what would happen if you happened to meet the person who you transformed into while in their body.

    there were 3 varieties of the X-change pill

    Basic X-change: This is the basic pill that will cause you to transform into whoever you think of for about one week before returning to your original form.

    X-change Fertility: This is the same as the basic except you will have increased fertility (about triple the fertility of a normal female) and it lasts for about a month.

    X-change premium: A super expensive variation of the pill which causes the change to become permanent.

    The pills also come in different flavors which affect personality when transformed.

    Basic: keep your original personality when transformed

    Clone: become a clone of the person you want in not just body but also mind

    Bimbo: become a stupid bimbo

    Slut: become a sex obsessed slut

    and more!

    The pills do have one side effect though. If the person who was transformed ingests any form of semen, breast milk, of other sexual fluids there is about an 80% chance that the transformation will become permanent.
