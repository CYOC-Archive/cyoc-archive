    Oddly, this did not make him feel any better. Seeing his own penis length was just as uncomfortable. But why? Was it merely knowing that this device knew such a private detail about him? No, it just felt wrong somehow, kind of like seeing his sister's name next to his own. What was that about? Probably just a twin thing.

    Shaking his head, he put the device away and continued strolling down the beach. The place seemed reasonably busy with beachgoers but not crowded. Really, it was a great atmosphere for a day on the sand.

    "Heads up!"

    The shout made Max glance upward reflexively, only to see a volleyball careening towards his head from out of the sky. Without thinking, he jumped back and brought his hands together, his arms stretched out together. The ball made a satisfying thud as he bounced it off his forearms, redirecting its force back towards its source: a group of teen girls having a casual match in a beach volleyball court.

    The girl who had been running after the ball skidded to a halt in surprise as she caught it easily. "Wow! That was pretty good! Do you play?"

    "Nope. Never have," Max replied, then frowned. Wait, was that true? In the moment, he had known just what to do, and he seemed to recall being on a team at school. No, that was Nikki. Maybe he had seen her play so many times, he just learned the move by sight.

    "You've got some good instincts then."

    Max blushed at being complimented by a pretty girl. She looked rather stunning in her light green bikini, her blonde hair pulled back in a casual ponytail. From her tan, she clearly frequented this kind of place a lot.

    "Say, we could use some help. Wanna join my side?" The girl offered.

    Max was taken aback by the invitation, and he opened his mouth to refuse. Then, he paused. Did he really want to pass up the chance to play with a bunch of attractive girls? Additionally, he had surprised himself with his volleyball skills just now. Maybe he could see what else he could do.

    "Hey, that's cheating!" One of the girls on the opposite team yelled out. "This game is girls only! You can't bring a boy in here just because you're losing."

    The blonde stuck her tongue out at the other girl playfully. Turning back to Max, she shrugged apologetically. "Oh well. Was worth a try. Too bad, huh."

    As he watched the girl head back, Max brought out the Chronovac. Perhaps, it was worth a try. Of course, he just had to fix the one thing keeping him from doing so.

    Bringing up his profile again, he flipped his gender to female, not realizing his awareness was still set to OFF.

    ~Body~
    Name: Maxine Myers (Nikki)
    Age: 14 years old
    Gender: female
    Race: Caucasian
    Height: 5’1
    Body Type: Slim
    Bra size: C-cup
    Hair style: brown shoulder length ponytail
    ~Clothes~
    Face wear: barefaced
    Torso: aqua bikini top
    Legs: aqua bikini bottoms
    Underwear: none
    Footwear: Black Sandals
    ~Mental~
    Iq-90
    Libido-medium

    Maxine blinked, wondering why she was staring at the device. Had she changed something? No, all the details looked right. Hm, she just seemed so scatterbrained today.

    "So, wanna join or not?"

    Glancing up, she saw the blonde girl still waiting for a reply. Quickly, she put the device away in her bag and nodded. "Sure! I'd love to!"
