    Circe went to a room where there were many animals in cages or with leashes. A lot of monkeys in cages. A bear in chains. A few fish and weird marine creatures. Others in different cages, some rather large, actually. Damon noticed that the animals quieted down when Circe entered the room. Well, this didn't bode well... The sorceress placed Damon in a terrarium with a a wide smile.
    "Here you go. Welcome to your new home. Plenty of juicy flies later for you, and a fat slug or worms if ever you're particularly well-behaved. Your warts will be useful. And if you are good, well, I'll transform you into something else."
    Circe looked around at her other prisoners.
    "These ones are waiting to be punished or until I know I can trust them. Or useful as a source of ingredients, if ever they show themselves as unwilling."
    She then left, with a dramatic swish of her cloak and noisy clicks of her heels. This sorceress certainly enjoyed giving herself a style. Damon watched her walk away and then turned himself towards the exploration of his new "home". It was rather large and seemed to have everything a toad could wish for. Mud, a tiny pool, rotting leaves, a piece of upturned log, planted grasses and brown rich earth. And truth be told, Damon felt rather comfortable here. There was humidity galore, keeping his new skin nice and wet. He splashed in the little tiny pond, and smiled inwardly as his fat orange belly made a lot of wet noises and made drops fly everywhere. The underside of the log was dark and cool and comfy. The earth felt soft and squishy beneath his large and long toad paws. Damon knew his new appearance certainly influenced his new tastes. And if Circe had the warts she wished rom him, he could hope to be transformed into something better... Maybe this would not be too bad...
    Damon then shook his head. No! He had to find a way out and become human again! Maybe the other transformed humans around might help him, if only he could communicate with them...
