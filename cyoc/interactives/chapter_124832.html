    Sitting in one of the school's halls, two 16 year old friends were eating their lunch, though they couldn't help but listen to the sounds coming from the roof. And, with the window open right next to them, it was obvious that...something was going on.

    "You know, that new janitor really seems intent on fixing things up." One of the girls, Lyra, commented before taking a bite out of her chicken salad sandwich. She had ginger, wavy hair that reached her mid-back and a slim frame, a bit lacking in bust and curves, and she stood at average height. "Makes you wonder if he really cares for the school, or wants a huge paycheck." She giggled, only for her friend to hush her.
    "H-hey, what if he hears you?" The other girl, Alice, reminded her. She sighed, brushing her strawberry blonde hair out of her face before lifting her water bottle up... It was empty, much to her dismay. She was short, but unlike her friend, sported some rather eye-catching hips, and was pretty busty for her age, too.
    "Well, you know the rumors, right? That he's installed cameras all over the place? Bathrooms, lockers..." Lyra mentioned. "If he's that creepy, then I don't mind him hearing a piece of my mind." She shrugged. Noticing Alice's dismay concerning the empty bottle, Lyra figured she could use a refill, too, and packed her lunch up before getting to her feet. "Anyway, wanna get some water?" She asked, and Alice answered with a quiet nod.

    ---

    "Um... Does the water taste a bit odd to you?" Alice asked hesitantly as they walked back to their usual spot in the hallway. Lyra gave a little shrug, before taking a sip of it.
    "Hmmm... Yup." She hummed, putting her chin in her palm as she thought. "Deeefinitely something odd about it... Must be some zombie virus!" Lyra nearly shouted, jumping towards Lyra, who shrieked and stumbled back in surprise and fear.
    "D-d-don't do thaaat...!" Alice muttered. She wasn't messing around - the water really did taste strange. And, when looking over at Lyre, she could swear that she...looked strange. "Um, h-have you grown recently?" Alice suddenly asked, looking her friend over. For that matter, the collar of her blouse felt...kind of tight.
