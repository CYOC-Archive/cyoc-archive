    As much as Kyle wanted to keep watching the transformation happen, he had to keep his focus on the stopwatch, lest he overshoots like last time. Still, with how Ian and Marco were reacting, plus the way Kyle was feeling right now, they must have been growing up well. Finally, the stopwatch hit 21, which was when Kyle decided to stop the aging. When he looked up, he was astounded by what he saw.

    "Kyle! This! Is! Awesome! Just look at me! I could be a football star, or maybe even a bodybuilder." Kyle thought the surfer at the beach was big, but Marco was on a whole other level. The way his muscles bulged when he flexed was breathtaking. Yet, despite his size, his abs were still rippling. His square jaw with a smattering of stubble was the final signifier that Marco was no longer just some kid who liked sports. He was now a handsome young man and burgeoning athlete.

    "I-I can't believe it. I never thought I'd be this buff." Kyle turned around to see Ian dragging his hands across his newly muscular pecs and down his new six-pack. While Ian was still the smallest out of the three, that didn't make his body and muscles any less impressive. In fact, it was more impressive, considering how thin and nerdy he was at the start. Ian's face was beautiful in a masculine way; he certainly would not have any problem attracting any girl or guy that he wanted.

    Finally, Kyle took a look at his own body and smiled. Kyle was just as big as he was when he became an adult before, but this time he muscles were more defined. Kyle still had body hair like before, but it seemed now he trimmed down his body hair to a more manageable length. Probably so he could show off his muscles better. It felt great to return to be an adult again, and Kyle was so glad he brought his friends along for the ride.

    As the three newly grown men flexed and examined their bodies, they suddenly got hit with a dizzy wave as the reality changes began to take effect. Suddenly the three men found themselves not in Marco's bedroom but a small house. It seems like the three were now roommates and renting a home for college now.

    The three friends were ecstatic about being adults now and wondered what they should do now that there were adults.
