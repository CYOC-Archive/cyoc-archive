    Jen wasn't worried about those words. She had played that game many times as a little girl with her best friends. And was completely confident that just like when she was young nothing would happen.

    “whats your problem faggot? That game is a complete lie. I have played it many times with nothing happening to me or my friends.”

    trying to prolong her blabbering he said “oh really I don't know if I would believe that. The town curse is real but like most powerful superstitions they need to be....activated, I bet when you played it as a kid you just thought it would work whenever anyone said those words. It is almost laughable to think that it would work whenever.”

    jen trying to jokingly humor him right now said “Oh really, and I suppose you actually activated it?”

    “Ha Ha, ya it was no small feat mind you, but long story short I have it and decided your reign of tyranny will finally be over.”

    Oh so you think I and a tyrant in our school? That may be the case but I can assure you it can only get worse for you. This call will cost you. Get ready for hell in school because that is where you will be for awhile.” Jen said the bitterness dripping off of each word she spoke.

    Kyle spoke with no care in the world and said “funny jen I was thinking of sending you there first, at least in a matter of speaking. I was worried you would have actually chose one of the options. But to my relief you didn't as this curse required both parts to complete.”

    whatever I am done with this hope you enjoy your day at school today I will make sure it is a nightmare.” with that she planned on slamming the phone down. But found the phone would not leave her ear her arm was just not responding to her will.

    Kyle started laughing and said “told you I have the power of the curse. Now to start, lets begin with transform

    first you will become a tool to fulfill anyones revenge by fairying their tormentors to there own personal hell immediately, at the cost of the persons soul. The price can't be redeemed until the tormented die naturally but they will belong to hell until then.

    you will send the tormentor to taste the hell they will be sent to a plain where you control everything and will show them how wrong they treated people and make them know what true vengeance is by making them live there sin in different ways show them there wrong ways.

    And your dare is to answer any call you receive if they accept the terms of the deal then you will be forced to abduct the tormenter and farry them to hell. if the tormenter seems genually misunderstood and actually a decent person willing to change you can give them a trial period if they show you they are good at heart they may live or if they are bad, revenge may be dealt upon the wicked”

    and no matter who it is you must deal revenge upon them if contracted to do so.

    People will be able to email you, call you text you anything to get a hold of you so long as the moon is out.

    And finally one random tormented person will message you and will tell at least one other person so word can travel about your “community service”

    That is all for your curse jenny, hope you really enjoy your day” he then hung up the phone.
    Jen didn't know what to think she was going to make sure his life was hell for at least the next week
