    When he got there he met his models. There were 9 boys and 7 girls for this show. That Is when he saw Brad. "Wait" he thought wasn't he supposed to go on a date with Becky, but he is also my feature Model.
    Again his mind was conflicted with reality changing thought. The more he looked at Brad, the new reality was winning.
    "Welcome guys," Alex said getting everyone's attention,"we are just going to try on the clothes to make a few alterations and make sure you know your cues. Brad as my feature Model you of course will come out last." So let's go try on the clothes.
    " Where are the dressing rooms?" Asked one of the newer models.
    "It is a shared room for both boys and girls. The unease of the boys changing with the girls went away and the room held all the clothes for the fashion show as well as make up and hair stations.
    Alex went to the stage to make sure the lighting and sound system was working. Alex also got a chance to think about all the changes he made. He changed his athletic persona into that of a fashionista boy. He ruined his sister's date with Brad who is now a top model. Made it normal for boys and girls to change in the same.

    Brad was the first to make it to the stage. He was a navy 3/4 inch crop top with ripped jeans and a pair of four inch heels. His make was a lite skin tone powder that made sure his skin didn't shine in the lights. His hair was clean shaven which didn't work with his look. "Hey Brad, you hair his all wrong it should be a disconnected pompadour." Brads hair suddenly grew out of his head in to the hairstyle that Alex said it should be. Alex said,"Much Better!"

    Brad smiled knowing was pleasing Alex. Even though he was still uneasy about wearing heels. When Alex asked Brad to be the feature model for the fashion show , he was excited because Alex was the best at the school when it came to fashion. He knew that even though he was straight being in this fashion show was going to help with his future ambitions. He could only play Baseball for so long.

    Alex thought Brad was almost perfect, he had the right hair style, the make up lacked but it suited Brad enough.

    "Ok Brad, let's practice your walk." Alex commanded. Brad got on stage and the perfect straight face, no emotions walk. It was perfect for any real runway. Alex felt it was missing the flare and theatrics that he wanted from this show. "That was great Brad, but I need you to be a little more flamboyant and limber, you don't have to be so straight and emotionless. Let's try this again."
    Immediately Brad was in position again. This time Brad's walk seemed like he was floating his turns were met a playful slap on his ass when he got to the center of the stage he stopped blew kiss to the audience turned did one more slap to the ass and left stage right.

    Brad returned for more notes.

    "Brad that was way better. It was like all the straightness left you. You are perfect being flirty and flamboyant." Brad shook his head and Alex could see that Brad was now completely change. Gone was the jock boy was suppose to go on a date with his sister. What was left a flirty flamboyant model superstar. But was he gay, Alex wanted to know
