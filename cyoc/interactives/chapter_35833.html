    lived with his dad who was usually away on business.

    As the two East Hampton high school students walked together down the deserted street,
    Jennifer explained again to Daniel how only a few days ago, Kyle Perkins had compelled
    her to allow him to attach the cursed collar to her neck, and how even the slightest
    mention of the collar had caused her to become more and more of a humanoid golden
    retriever. Although Daniel always remembered Jennifer as a dog, he felt he had no choice
    but to believe her story given his newly pointed ears.

    of a dog, Daniel seemed surprised. He mentioned that he'd seen her refuse to do lots of
    things she'd been told to do at school this week, and Jennifer agreed. "Maybe," suggested

    turn into a dog, and so the second part of his wish was much weaker."

    Jennifer agreed that was probably why. As the two continued their conversation, she
    noticed Daniel's hand had crept under his shirt and was idly scratching at his belly.
