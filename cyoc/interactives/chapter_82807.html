    Zack burst in, "Hey Harry, I'm sorry to interrupt, but we have a situation out there I think calls for an emergency meeting."

    "Can't it wait until we've sent this dirtbag packing?"

    "Hey now, there's no need for name calling," James grinned.

    "No, I don't think it can. A whole busload of people just came through processing, all of them entering as bucks..."

    "What, even more?" asked Harry, already worried about the current population. It was getting to be that time of year after all.

    "And I don't even know how to describe this lot... picture a whole football team turning 21 the night of a bachelor party?"

    It's a good thing Naomi left when she did. She'd be dying of testosterone poisoning. "So? Just round up whoever you can to try and keep a
    handle on things. Regular security patrols are already a mess today thanks to this creep, you don't need my approval for something like this."

    "Well now you're just sounding like total fascists," remarked the increasingly cocky and bemused Lone Wolf.

    "I would," said Zack, "but word came down from management that this batch contains some kind of VIP. They want us to, and I quote, 'just give
    them a wide berth.'"

    Harry scowled."What kind of crazy back room... Look, I'm going to go find out what this is all about. Just keep an eye on this punk until I get
    back, would you?"

    James chuckled as Harry left. "A bit understaffed around here lately?"

    Zack didn't say so, but he had to agree. The park had been growing in popularity in general, and with this many rowdy bucks in the park at
    once, the huskies would be seriously outnumbered if herd mentality lead them all into banding together to cause some trouble.
