As Miles looked up from his plate to see them reenter, he took a rather long look at Rei's Aunt Violetta and was seeing her in a different sort of light as he felt his heart beat a little faster. Violetta didn't notice it, but the grey streaks in her hair slowly disappeared as she ate and were now just an ordinary golden blonde hair.

    "So Violetta, have you met anyone yet?" Hanako said said as she grinned at her friend.

    "Oh, Hanako, I don't really think there's anyone out there for me. I've got too much of my own career to think about." Violetta said waving her friend off as she looked into a compact mirror for a moment to look at herself and thought a few of her wrinkles were gone but she just dismissed it.

    "Violetta, that is the same thing you said to me before I spoke one word to Hanako." Tomas reminded Violetta.

    "Yes, what was it you told him again before he spoke to me?" Hanako asked smirking at her.

    "Hanako, please..." Tomas groaned covering his face.

    "Oh, I know, mama! She said: Lyubov' tak zhestoka, mozhno vlyubit'sya v kozla. Basically what it means is, love is so cruel, you could fall in love with a goat." Rei said as she and her friends aside from Tomas began to laugh at the saying.

    Miles almost didn't hear the joke as he seemed to be with how Violetta was laughing and noticed she seemed to be slowly losing some weight and looking a little thinner.

    "Ahem, uh, Rei, Spark, can I...talk to you two for a moment in private?" Miles asked the two of the as Spark and Rei looked at each other and nodded then headed to the spare bedroom where they had just been talking to Aunt Violetta some time ago.

    "Rei, did you and your aunt do something to me?" Miles asked sounding a little confused.

    "To you? No, you never came up in our conversation at all." Rei replied just as confused.

    "Well, you guys must have done something because I don't know why, but I think I'm crushing right now on your aunt and I think she's slowly getting younger without realizing it." Miles explained.

    "What? I didn't do anything to her. I certainly wouldn't make her fall in love." Rei answered.

    Spark thought for a moment then gasped and covered her mouth in realization.

    "What? What is it?" Rei asked.

    "You weren't the one who did this Rei, I think your aunt may have accidentally wished on your ring." Spark said telling her friend how she had Rei's ring around her hand when she made the comment.
