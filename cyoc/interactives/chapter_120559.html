    Max Colins was sitting at home watching TV when he heard the doorbell rang.
    "Who could that be?" Max asked himself.
    He opened the door to see that no one was standing outside. He looked down and saw a white envolope that had the word "Max" written on it. He grabbed the envelope and ran inside to open it.
    When he opened it, a rainbow ticket fell out.
    "What is this?" Max questioned reading that ticket which said, "Come One! Come All to the newly opening...ClownLand! Free Admission for those that receive these tickets!"
    "ClownLand? Sounds lame." Max picked up the ticket and threw it away. He sat back on the couch and before he went to sit down he saw the ticket on the couch.
    "What the..." Max decided to keep and put it in his room.
    Four hours later Max went to bed to prepare for the last day of school.


    The last day of freshman year was over for Max. Everyone said their goodbyes and hugged one another. Max started leaving and was straying behind the highschool sweethearts Katrina Reed and Zach Day. Max knew Katrina and Zach but he never talked to them much because of their popularity status. Max then noticed something odd shining from the couples backpacks. He recognized the shine almost instantly, it was the ticket from yesterday.
    "Hey! Wait! Katrina and Zach!" Max yelled running to the couple.
    "Max? What do you want?" Zach asked.
    "That ticket in your backpack I have the same one. Do you know anything about it?"
    "What ticket?" Katrina chimed in sassily.
    "Don't play dumb I saw the ticket in both of your backpacks. What is this ClownLand?"
    "Look. I have no idea. But I want to check it out. Seems fun. Me and Katrina were going to go why don't you come with us."
    "Seriously?"
    "Sure. We'll leave tomorrow let's hang out tonight and you can sleepover and we'll head there in the morning."
    Max nodded at Zach and left with the teens. Although he was containing it Max was excited for this. He left the school ready to explore the mysterious park.
