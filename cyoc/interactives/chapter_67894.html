    Jeff look through all the packages the Chronivac has and it was getting hard to pick the right one. Looking through his options, he noticed package that said, ‘Race’. Staring at the package, Jeff rubbed his chin and said, “Hmm… Race Package? I wonder what that means?” Curious, he clicked on the link to the package and a new page opened up. In that page, that was a message that talked about it. As he was reading the package, it stated…

    ‘Welcome to the Race package. Here in this page is where you can change your race into whatever you want. That means you can change your skin color into any type of ethnic trait you want. You can pick whatever you want, whether it be African, Italian, Hispanic, French, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, etc. Or if you want to go beyond racial tendency, you can choose on being an alien and decided on the features you want your alien to be. As on optional approach, you can change your gender just so you can experience what’s it like on the other side in another’s race. You can also change your reality, make a choice if you want to mentally transform, the duration of the change, or how long you would keep your change. Just remember that altering reality would ultimately change the world around you. Meaning you would be with a different family in a different place. So please be careful if you want to change your reality. Other then that, have fun with it.’

    As soon as Jeff finished reading this, he was quite surprised by that whole thing. He can actually change his ethnic race and probably change his reality to fit his new race as well. Let alone change both his race and gender. In his thought, he though it would be awesome to give that a try. He then looked at his options and finds them to be a bit basic.

    The first is the name box, meaning you have to give the program the name of a real person you want to use. The second is a drop down list letting you know which race do you want to be. Below that is the option if you want to change your gender or not. Then there are the small text boxes that want to know the duration of the transformation and how long you would keep your changed self. Followed by the yes or no option to change your reality. Looking through all of this, Jeff thinks this was a little too easy. But he knows that it would be cool to change his race and experience something completely different. Looking through his options, he began to think what race he wants to be. After giving this much thought, he decided that he wants to be…
