    Edwin thought long and hard about this decision. If he was going to be stuck in a female body for the remainder of this vacation it should be one he at least felt comfortable in.

    For the mental changes he chose 50% he wanted to experience female urges but he wanted to remain in control. He felt a good 50/50 compromise was the right way to go.

    Edwin was about to select Gender Bender as his option. A simple transformation would suffice for himself. However as he went to check that box he heard a noise coming from outside. Looking out the window he saw two other people arguing over what looked accident. He sighed and checked the box rolling his eyes a bit.

    Edwin didn’t realize however that the box he checked was not Gender Bender but was the one right next to it labeled Bimbofication...

    Edwin took his card down to the front desk manager who accepted it.

    “Thank you so much sir. Since you have elected the drawn out change please feel free to relax for the of the day. By sun down the process will be completed but you are welcome to help speed up the process by doing any feminine activities.” The concierge told him.

    “Thanks....Umm...will the changes be painful?” Edwin asked a bit awkwardly.

    “Oh no sir...Our job is to make sure the process is as painless as possible. You might feel some
    Pressure build up in certain spots but no real pain. Your clothing will also be adjusted as you change so don’t worry when you start to notice the changes kicking in. Enjoy the rest of your vacation!” The clerk said waving him off.

    Edwin sighed and went back to his room to relax. There was no going back now...
