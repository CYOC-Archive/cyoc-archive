    Now Seera had originally been a human Viking princess who'd
    been cursed with the dragons curse when she'd foolishly
    donned the Necklace of Freya whilst wearing a milk white dress
    which was supposedly pure and clean,but somehow had one tiny
    spot of sows blood on it.

    That had offended the norse goddess Freya so much that she'd cursed both
    that necklace and her would be accolyte.

    Centuries had passed,during which dragon-maiden Seera had eventually
    died ( that dragons jawbone ) and been awakened to a spiritual
    afterlife as a dragon djinn.

    So when Seera had been summoned by a squealing sow,with he mortal jawbone nearby and the
    cursed Necklace just buried in the sand near that rotting old wooden
    chest,she'd furiously done her groundwork.

    Thus she'd left Wendy the human girl with a bit of the sow
    still in her,enough to curse her Mistress into a dragon-maiden if the
    Necklace ever came to grace Wendy's soft white neck.

    A state in which Seera would be free of her djinn obligations to Wendy and
    could be employed by a new Master/Mistress,perhaps Captain Teague.

    So things came to pass and the Flying Clod sailed back into the waves,with a
    cursed Wendy down below on the bunks,hot feverish and in the initial stage of her
    new life as a flesh and blood maiden/dragon

    whilst in Captain Teagues cabin Captain Teague was trying to extract
    information from the airy insubstantial djinn spirit Seera without using up
    his nine wishes.

    Captain Teague now had one dragon djinn incapable of grasping solid objects such as
    a 200 pound keg of gunpowder and a 100 pound sick young girl capable,eventually,
    of turning into a 2000 pound flesh and blood winged lizard thing that could carry the
    bomb in her taloned forepaws and flying over the pursuing Monitors...
