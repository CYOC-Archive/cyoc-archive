Your whole body should look bulkier, but because you're turning into a Hobbit, it's migrating into your stomach area. Just exercise; you'll lose it soon enough." He then saw her shorter brown hair, that now hung just above her shoulders. "Your hair looks better this way."

He opened it and Suzanne beheld a room that appeared to be little more than a storage room. Computers of all kinds were stashed into the corners of the room, and the bed itself appeared to have been just cleared away of the machines.

    "G'night!" Nick called as he left the room.
