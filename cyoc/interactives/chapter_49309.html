    Connor couldn't believe that he had forgotten one of the most fundamental rules of the
    harem settings. Only guys who never went -after- a harem got one thrown in their lap,
    only the ones who didn't -want- a harem actually got one (it was funnier that way),
    perverts and lechers who endlessly attempted to have one or claim one always met with
    failure, humiliation, and having their butt kicked.

    "Hold on!" Connor protested as he felt his body changing, "If this is that kind of setting,
    aren't I supposed to rise up good as new later not having learned a thing and having
    strengthened my resolve to achieve my impossible dream?"

    Lang had a thoughtful look on her face as Connor felt his teeth twisting into fang and
    cutting his tongue. "True. But since you're supposed to be a central character, that means
    you can't fulfill the role of the relentless pervert now can you? And since you're in this for
    the sex, your only logical fate within the context of the setting is to be one of the harem
    were-wolf girls."

    Connor felt his feet twisting into paws making his shoes and socks worthless, and he saw
    his body forming a black outline and colors becoming much more contrast and flat as
    bright, almost electric blue fur sprouted all over his body. "This isn't fair! This isn't what I
    came for! And if you really are my fantasy how can you act like this?"

    Lang shrugged, "I'm sorry I have to act like this, I really do, but your mind and feelings
    called out, and I answered. Maybe once you'll been assimilated into the setting, we can be
    sisters or friendly rivals instead, don't try to fight it and it'll go much more easier for you."

    "BUT... but who'd be the protagonist then-?!"

    "Likely someone who came to Toon World for selfless reasons, I don't know. But we'll know
    soon enough once he arrives in the setting."

    "Can you... can you please at least transform while this is... happening?" Connor groaned
    as hands and fingers became painted 2-D claws.

    Lang sighed, "All right. It's tradition for the first girl introduced to normally get the guy
    and for the other members of the harem to be introduced in the later early parts of the
    story, so... alright."

    Lang calmly took off her summer dress and kicked off her sandals, leaving her in just her
    bra and panties, then pink and white fur began to spread from various parts on her body
    starting with the ones that would hide modesty.

    At the same time, Connor was getting past his own initial stages of transformation. Then
    he remembered! He still had the werewolf attraction spray! He had no idea what would
    happen if he used it now, but maybe at least he could go out with a bang! However, as
    Connor grab it, it slipped from his transforming paws, and began to roll away on the floor.

    Lang noticed but made no action the subject instead just sighing, "That plot device isn't
    for you."
