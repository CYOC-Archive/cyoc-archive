    He opened his eyes. The light of his computer screen stung into his pupils. He went to rub the brightness out of his eyes, that's when he discovered something. They weren't on stalks. He was with the Chronoivac. He wasn't going to be a slug person. Jeff was in shock, he made it out. He felt normal. He wasn't covered in slime, he had his legs back, and he didn't have a craving for lettuce. To him, he was back, looking like he always looked. But he definitely noticed something else. Wherever the Chronoivac was now, it was dark. Pitch black. The only light that was there came out of the computer screen, and even then, he couldn't see anything.

    "Mom?" Jeff asked. His voiced just echoed.
    "Rosie?". Another echo.

    Jeff didn't know where he was, if he was anywhere. The only sounds he heard were from the computer, the buzzing of the screen. He went to walk forward, behind the computer, until he saw that the computer had no cord connecting to the electricity. There was no wall socket. No plug. Only him and the computer were in this room. Jeff decided to run to see if there was anyone up ahead, but when he did, it bought him back to the same place. Computer and all. He tried again. Same place. And again. Same place. Jeff noticed that a pop-up came on the screen of his computer.

    Reality Error: Change during finalising new reality alteration caused reality to shut down. Please upload previous reality save and continue or create new reality.

    Jeff froze. No more reality. That meant no more food. No more television. No more games. No more Mom. No more Rosie. No more Family. He started to cry, he was stuck in a literal nowhere. He didn't know there was a save option when it came to reality, and now there isn't a reality. No place to call home. The idea that he could create another reality did intrigue him. He wiped the tears from his face and found the option to create a new reality...

    He didn't know where to start first...
