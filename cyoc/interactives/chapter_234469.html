    Yet, you couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The dream that you had experienced haunted you for the rest of your morning. Your heart rate was elevated and adrenaline was still moving through your muscles. You tried to shove the memory out of your mind.

    You prepared for your date. Your girlfriend would be coming around by lunchtime. You felt as giddy as a child. You let the butterflies in your stomach distracted you from you recent nightmare. Today was the day when you would finally tap that.
