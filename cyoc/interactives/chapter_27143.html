    Kody rushed home in his brand new red corvette he had to get there first even if he was the furthest away!

    Meanwhile at the police station Mr. Bishlerp still believing he was Kody walked out of the police station.
    "This day can't possibly get any worse..." he then walked up to the pay phone near the police station and dialed for a cab.

    Kody was going well over the speed limit swerving around cars trying to get home faster. As he approached the rail road tracks the bars suddenly went down as a slow train pushed its way down the tracks going about five miles a hour.
    "This great... just great..."

    A cop listened to his radio as the police chief called for a cop nearby to go check out Kody Whiterman's house down at 384, Baronial terris off of 8th st.
    "I'm nearby that area" the cop replies and heads off in its direction.

    The train finally passed, and with a screech of the tires Kody accelerates past the tracks and heads back home.

    A cab pulled up next to the police station Mr. Bishlerp got in and told the driver where is home was.

    The cop drove past the police station. He had not actually been the closest cop, but he needed something to do. He hated his patrol route and anything even checking up on a house with a open door was more exciting. He also wanted to see the look on this Kody Whiterman's face when he heard what this pervert did to his car.

    Kody slowed down. He was passing the police station, and even with the chronivac he could'nt get away from a ticket if the entire police force was present.

    Mr. Bishlerp's cab pulled up to his house first. He paid the driver and slowly walked up the steps to his tiny home. He walked past his friends car without even noticing it and reached into his pocket for his keys... and found none.
    "Great" he said bending over to pull out the hidden key from under the steps when he hears someone walk up behind him.
    "You Kody Whiterman?" said the cop
    "Yes what now?"
    "Do you know where your car is?"
    "No... what happened?"
    "We found it a couple miles from here. Looks like some kid took it for a joy ride then... befouled it..."
    "Wait what? What do you mean 'befouled it'?
    "Trust me you don't want details, but apparently he was quite creative with the seat belts"
    Mr. Bishlerp couldn't even respond for a moment...
    Kody just then slowly pulled into one of his neighbor's driveways and watched the conversation from afar
    "Any good news?"
    "Nope"
    "Then thanks I guess"

    The cop got in his car and drove off as Kody continued to watch.
    "How did Mr. Bishlerp get back so fast... Doesn't matter I guess its a sign that I didn't mess with him enough"
    Mr. Bishlerp picked up the key and was about to slide it into the keyhole when it slipped from his fingers and fell into the dirt.
    "Now lets see what we can do about this..." Kody says pulling up the Chronivac and selecting Mr. Bishlerp's saved data. Watching his old body struggling for the key he got a idea. Kody typed into the Chronivac remote 'shrink bladder to size of a kiwi' and set the timer for one minute then hit enter.

    Kody watched the teen's expression suddenly change, and suddenly started moving faster for the key. Picking up the key with trembling hands he attempts to reinsert the key, but once again it slips out of his fingers and falls into a crack. Looking around quickly to see if anyone was watching he grabs his crotch with one hand to help relieve some of the pressure, and help keep the incoming flood at bay. With his other hand he continued to dig into the crack for the key with each passing second his actions became more spastic.

    Mr. Bishlerp couldn't hold it any longer he tried to get to his feet and unzip his fly, but it was too late the dam had burst. He got up a damp spot had already appeared on his khaki shorts and rapidly spread from between his hands outward. The chime went off on the Chronivac signaling that the transformation was done. The wet teen leaned over and fished the key out and put it in the door and opened the house.

    Kody watched as Mr. Bishlerp walked inside. Kody got out of his car and followed him inside. As he walked in he heard the shower turn on and Mr. Bishlerp go in. Kody carefully planned out his next actions...
