    The Magic Shop

    Emily runs off with the doll that was her boyfriend

    Emily quickly decided to take that as her cue to bounce. She grabbed Rick tight, made sure the camera was firmly in place in her bag and bolted. As she ran around the corner, she figured she should at least try to look less suspicious. A college student running down a suburban neighbor hood with a doll in one hand would definitely look strange to say the least.

    "Sorry Rick, this is just so we can get to my house safely." She whispered at the doll and quickly put Rick inside her purse while she slowed to a speed walk.

    Rick watched helplessly as his body fell into Emily's massive purse. He wanted to protest but could do nothing. He landed right next to the camera, and as Emily walked Rick's body rolled so that his face now was staring directly into the camera's dark sense. Luckily, it was still clean enough to have a reflection and Rick was stunned.

    What stared back at him was nothing but a toy. It looked as if someone took what he would've looked like as a girl and made a toy based on that. A plastic, children's girl toy was all that he was. Needless to say, he was incredibly freaked out, but also slightly turned on. He just hoped Emily would hurry up and get home already.

    Emily walked into her house, closing the door behind her with a sigh as she leaned against it, happy to have made it home. Rick meanwhile, couldn't tell what was going on as his plastic body flung around the bag uncontrollably. His dizzying perspective ended when a bright light opened above him to reveal Emily's gigantic hand reaching down towards him.

    "YOU OK RICK?" She asked in her normal speaking tone, although since Rick was so small, she sounded like a giant when not whispering.

    He tried to give her some indication that he was alright but nothing happened.
    "I HOPE YOU'RE AWARE IN THERE." She sighed, standing Rick up on the counter.

    "Okay." She thought to herself. "All I gotta do is just keep trying until he turns back right? Nothing to worry about."

    She picked up the camera and aimed it at Rick. While Rick internally sighed in relief, Emily externally took a breath, hoping that he'd have at least a better transformation.
