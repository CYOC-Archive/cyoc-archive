    Chris was at the door, so Mary pulled Mark into the bathroom to hide. "Mary, Mark I'm home early from work. Where are you?" Chris asked.

    "Hi honey, now Mark and I are in the bathroom. It's just....we're different like extremely different, and we have an explanation for why we look like we are. I just don't want you to know that we are who we are." Mary called down to Chris

    Chris walked upstairs to see Mary, and Mark in the bathroom in their transformed states. Mark, and Mary did their best to explain everything to Chris, and he seemed to semi-understand.

    "Okay I believe your story, I know your stories, and I believe you both. The only thing I don't understand is how this happened to you. Also it's pretty odd considering that my son is now my daughter who is a vixen with multiple tails, and my wife is a dragon." Chris said,"God this is giving me a headache."

    "Well, to answer your question we used vials, that had liquid in them to change us. Me because I was curious at first, and honestly couldn't have predicted this. Mark did it because she saw me, and wanted in on it." Mary said

    "First off Mark is grounded for doing this without both our concent for tonight, and second can you show me these vials?" Chris said Mary nodded, and gave Chris the package of vials.

    "Oh come on!" Mark said

    "You brought that on yourself young lady, but at the same time it's only one night so suck it up. By the way while you're grounded think of a new name for yourself that isn't that different from Mark, just to give you something to do." Chris said rummaging through the vials.

    "What are you doing?" Mary asked

    "Well I'm trying to see if there is a vial that can undo these changes..... Damn it, there isn't one." Chris said.

    "Well I have a thought, maybe you can choose a vial, and we can just live somewhere like the woods maybe?" Mary suggested

    "It's not that I'm against that, but why are you suggesting this?" Chris asked

    "Well if you think about it I can't get a job, and Mark can't go to school anymore. We have to disappear or other stuff that I don't want to mention happens. Besides I looked up kitsunes, and this would be good for Mark to discover her element, that is is she can actually use magic. I mean I actually can breathe fire." Mary said.

    "I'm not going to argue that your right, and if we're going to live in the woods we don't need that much clothing, just enough to get through winter, I mean Mark has fur after all. First however let me choose what I want to become." Chris said
