    A broken suit

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 16 days ago BM

    Seeing her new appearance, instead of being surprised Jess was left with a puzzled face. Although she had clearly shrunk, she still had her normal appearance. She even still had that wound that had been done on her arm 2 years ago.

    This didn't make sense. Wasn't this outfit supposed to make her young? Even in the note that came with the suit, it clearly said that this suit would make the person who wears it younger. With all this in mind, it could only mean one thing: She bought a damaged suit.

    Jess was somewhat disappointed but this was something she already saw coming. After all, there was a reason why they had put this in the bargain bin.

    Since this suit was not what she wanted, she tried to press the activation button again only to discover another problem. The button was also damaged. Great, she not only bought a damaged suit but she was also stuck with this gnome stature.
