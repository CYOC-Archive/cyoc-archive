    The Magic Shop

    Titillating Tang

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 2 months ago AP BM O Mythological Magic

    Lina hesitated quite a bit in front of the vending machines. The choice was large and very unfamiliar. Completely unfamiliar, in fact. All logos and names were not known at all to Lina. What were they exactly? What did they contain as ingredients? It was not clear at all. Did they all come from other countries? Where were the usual drinks normally on sale in stores? As she had already inserted her change, she did not want to leave her money to waste. She saw a pleasant logo of tropical flowers and pressed the button. With a few clanks, the machine delivered her ordered beverage. Lina grabbed it and looked at it more thoroughly. It had bright exotic flowers as a logo and was called "Titillating Tang". The name was pretty strange. It looked rather... saucy. The precise ingredients were not written and the can merely said that it held "exotic flower extracts". And hibiscus. Well, that wasn't very clear... But Lina was loathe to throw away a soda she had just purchased. She could at leat taste it and if she did not like it, she could always throw it away into a random trash can. So Lina opened the can and took a quick gulp.
    "Any good?" asked Amy curiously.
    "Yeah, actually," answered Lina with enthusiasm and surprise. "Super fruity and tasty. Not at all chemical. Doesn't feel artificial. I can't quite believe it."
    "The name's super weird. Freaky, even," countered Amy with suspicion.
    "I know. But the taste is great. Try it. You'll see."
    Amy sipped the soda and was convinced at once. In no time the can as empty and in a trash can. Of course, the girls could not know that the soda was a magical potion. A potion to transform drinkers into tropical and exotic nymphs. Titillating creatures for any human, satyr, faun, gillman or creature. Soon, they would transform and be spirited away into a tropical world of fantasy. Their new home. Lina and Amy were about to become titillating nymphs living under the tropics.
