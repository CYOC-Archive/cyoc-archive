    When one walks into the local mall just outside of town they will instantly think that it's just an ordinary mall, and for the most part they are right. It's just an ordinary mall with ordinary people and ordinary shops. At least it used to be that way before the opening of the newest shop, The New and Better, which can be found at the very back of the mall squished in between two large, but very ordinary, clothing stores. Before the opening of The New and Better it's current location was nothing more but a small wall separating the two other stores, one would think it's amazing that they could fit a store, even a small one, in between the two stores. But they did it and as you stand there looking at its colorful sign you wonder to yourself what kind of store this is. You've never heard about it before, nor remember seeing any construction taking place in this area of the mall, the store just sort of just appeared out of thin air. There were no window displays, just a door and sign. Curiosity starts to overwhelm you and you find yourself walking towards the door and opening it. A golden bell rang as you opened the door and you see that the inside of the store was absolutely huge. There were isles among isles of stuff and it appeared they didn't just sell one type of thing. Above each isle was a sign that would light up in different neon colors. One sign said "Clothing and Costumes" and another read "Antiques". What was really weird was that there were isles labeled "Potions" and "Other". For a moment you wondered if this store was a magic trick store, but as you continued to look among the store you come to the conclusion that it was unlike any magic shop you've ever seen. Another thing that made this whole store suspicious was the fact that there were no other people here. No shoppers, no workers, no one. It was like a ghost town.

    "May I help you?" said a voice from behind you that nearly made you jump out of your skin. You were just able to hold in your scream as you quickly turn around and see a gorgeous young woman with long golden hair and sparkling blue eyes. "I'm sorry, I didn't mean to startle you."

    "No, it's fine." you said in response. "I was just wondering what kind of store this was."

    "It's a new kind of store." the lady said with much enthusiasm. "This store has a lot of just about everything. It has clothes, games, antiques, even new technology of the likes the world has never seen before. I assure you, good sir, that there is nothing here that can be found at another store. And since your our first customer of the day, I'll give you twenty five percent off of any of the items for sale here."

    "Thanks." you said unsure about the whole thing. This woman seems way too happy about her job. "I think I'm gonna look around for a little."

    "Alrighty then, if you need anything just let me know." The store clerk walked away into the isles and out of site. You start looking at how huge the store was and you begin to wonder just where to start.

    What do you do now?
