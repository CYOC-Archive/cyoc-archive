    Ms. Holman and the hands-on biology lesson

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 4 months ago A

    "Alright, settle down. Just because you're all over 18 and in the Animal Wing doesn't mean you can wreck the room. Not yet at least", said Ms. Talia Holman. The 30-year-old biology teacher stood a petite 4'9 tall, with short black hair and brown eyes behind glasses. Her petite, slender figure was in a shirt and pants.

    "Aw come on Ms. Holman, we're just having a bit of fun!" said Ahmad Thomson. Ms. Holman was dwarfed by all three of the young men she had been entrusted with, and at 6'2'' the African-American basketball player made her look the tiniest. He had a shaved head and was in a tank top and shorts.

    "Leggo of me!" protested Vincent Yates, pulling Ahmad's arm off from around his neck and shoving him off. The 6'0'' football player wasn't as tall as Ahmad, but he was stockier, with curly blonde hair and blue eyes.

    "Playfighting and roughhousing? Really?" Ms. Holman crossed her arms and stood as tall as she could manage, but the two athletes continued pushing each other around. She commanded very little respect despite her passion for teaching and her empathy for her students. Why was that again? Oh, right. "Fun-sized", one ex had called her.

    "Heh, this looks like fun", chuckled Thomas Nguyen. At 5'11'' Thomas was the shortest of the three, his lean, muscular body honed for swimming. His black hair was in a tidy bowl cut.

    Ms. Holman sighed. "Why did they send the three of you here?"

    Thomas shrugged. "I dunno Ms. H. We haven't been the best at biology".

    The teacher inspected the shelves where the potions and powders were located. "I can see that. Maybe this would be a good time for a biology lesson."

    Her students groaned and rolled their eyes. "On vacation? Really?"

    "Why not?" Ms. Holman smiled. "You could use some firsthand information on animal anatomy. Or the food chain..." After all, the hotel's regeneration protocols made sure that nobody could die within it. Anything that would be lethal would merely result in regeneration at the spa. "Respawning" they called it. Ms. Holman blushed. Was she... really considering that? Pint-sized, mild-mannered, terminally nice Ms. Talia Holman?
