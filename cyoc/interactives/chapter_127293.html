    Then I heard snickering in the bushes I had looked at first.
    “Hehe! Oh my, aren’t we jumpy!” a female voice said, processed with more snickers.

    I raised my tail slightly.
    “And just who are you again?” I didn’t remember ever meeting this squirrel-lady.
    Others sure, but then again, we are a long way away from our starting point.

    I sighed. “What do you want?” I asked.
    “The human male, please.” The squirrel-lady said, as if it was no big deal.
    I just stared at her. Had she really just said that? Or was I dreaming?
    “Uh, Yeah, no.” I said, wondering if she was just joking. Apparently she wasn’t.
