    her there ashamed for what she’s done and spare her from living the cursed life she had or was her parents on
    a bloody rampage and the authorities dismissed Jane as would be victim? You tell me. Anyway when her foster-
    father found out, he went straight to the orphanage to find out only to find it abandoned. On the bright side,
    Jane isn’t a mindless beast because that not what she fears. She has a rival on a cheerleading squad who looks
