    “Ca-can you change my size?” You say blushing looking at the baker.

    “Oh a size change? By whatever do you mean?” The baker cries out excitedly at the notion.

    “Yeah, can I be made to have my size changed?” You say asking the question again.

    “Certainly” He says to you with a cheerful remark.

    “How do you plan on doing that?” You say blushing at the question.

    "I'll add more to your body if you want to be bigger and I'll take parts of you away if you want to be smaller, It's that easy really! Your just a big lump of chocolate so adding or removing mass is the easiest way to change your size, don’t worry I promise it wont hurt you at all.”

    You show a worried look as your face twists at the thought of dismemberment. “Eh-ha, Y-you can just chop me down and that is supposed to make me smaller? W-wouldn’t that hurt?” You cry back in a worried tone.

    “Nonsense, you’re a living candy bar now! You don’t have any nerves to sense pain with! Because your being will stay in your largest piece, I’d have to break you down piece by piece if you wanted to become smaller.” He says trying to reassure you with his smiling happy face.

    “And what if I want to get bigger? You just add more chocolate to me?” You say starting to try to calm down.

    “Yep.” He says looking at you still smiling. “Honestly, you're really easy to change in size, you could do it yourself without me if you really wanted to. You could even just reattach pieces of yourself if they are broken off.”

    “Well that’s a relief…” You say calming down and returning to your normal self. “As long as it won’t hurt…”

    The baker breaks out into giggles as he looks at you. “I promise it won’t hurt. Now did you want to be bigger or smaller?”
