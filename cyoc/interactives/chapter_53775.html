Squatting down next to you, she tells you, "Cupcake must be groomed first," as she runs the brush through your fur coat, something you find to be actually quite pleasant as she alternates rubbing you down with the towel.

    After a moment, you leap up, but force your hind legs to remain on the ground. Falling back, you tell her, "See."
