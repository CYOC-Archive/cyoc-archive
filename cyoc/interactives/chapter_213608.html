Also noted was a batch of still living sperm making their way through Charlene's uterus for a non-existent egg. Ethan clicked on Charlene and explored a few of his options. Without a second thought, Ethan clicked accept and suddenly Jerry's fetus image on the screen began to shrink as the new girl fetus began to grow and appear as a mini-profile right underneath Jerry. A few seconds passed and now Jerry and his new sister were both a month and two weeks old, leaving Charlene in an earlier state of pregnancy, but with the baby girl she wanted due at the same time Jerry was now.
