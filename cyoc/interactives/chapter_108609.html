    "News, infomercial, news, infomercial, news, infomercial..."

While the ground will still be wet come sunset, we're not expecting any precipitation overnight," said the weather man.
    "Well, that's good to know," said Spencer.
    She was about to go back to her channel surfing when she heard a car honking outside.
    With a smile on her face, Spencer shut off the TV, grabbed her stuff, locked the front door of the house after setting the alarm, and dashed in the rain to the Knight family town car waiting in the driveway.

Then again, while I wouldn't of minded it with Kenya if things had worked out, I'm not looking for an actual long term relationship right now. Especially not the kind that leads to marriage and kids. "Alex and I might still have an uphill climb in some circles because we are a gay couple, but we're totally dedicated to each other now and forever," he said with a smile, before kissing his boyfriend's cheek.

"After all, my parents didn't meet until they were both in college, and didn't get married until they graduated that."
