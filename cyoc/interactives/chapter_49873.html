    In a darkened room lit only by the flickering flames of a few candles, and shadowy figure sat in a throne like chair gazing into a crystal ball.

    A few moments later, another person entered the room and bowed before the person on the throne. A moment passed before the figure tore his gaze away from the crystal ball and regarded the person before him.

    "I trust all has been taken care of?"

    "Yes Master. The game has been reset, and all who were victims of the Big Bad Wolf's perversions have been restored to their former selves with no memory of what happened to them...all except Kaylee and the Big Bad Wolf...also, those who were involved have no memories of Kyle or Jennifer having ever existed, just as you ordered."

    The figure on the throne smiled. "Excellent work, Jakal. That fool of a girl sought to subvert our game to fuel her sick little wolf fantasies, and not to sow chaos as it was meant to." He gestured to the crystal ball. "Come and see the fate of these two."
