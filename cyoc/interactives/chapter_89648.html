    The shaking took on the rhythmic pattern of footsteps, but whatever was walking seemed to cause earthquakes with each giant step, shaking the whole house. The giant door to the room swung open like a skyscraper opening up one of its walls. What Jeff saw holding the handle was beyond comprehension.

    It was... himself?

    Standing in the open doorway was a titanic version of his old body, looking just as it had looked only an hour ago, only now it seemed as big as a city building yet perfectly in proportion with the rest of the room. The giant Jeff stared down at her. He did not look pleased to see tiny Jeff below.

    Jeff was terrified beyond words. What the hell was going on?

    "What are you doing in here?" the giant asked, his voice sounded like Jeff's as well only a bit louder. "This is my room."

    Jeff felt like he was about to piss himself.

    A little pee might have dripped out when the giant crouched down and wrapped a hand around her waist. Jeff just let it happen passively lifting her hands out of the way. How could she resist against something that big, anyway. The giant could crush her if he only squeezed hard enough. Thankfully, Jeff's giant doppelganger seemed to want her alive for now. His massive hand encircled her gently but firmly, and then he lifted her up what seemed like dozens of feet into the air and held her before his huge face. His head seemed even taller than she was.

    "Well," the giant asked impatiently. "What are you doing in my room?"

    Jeff sputtered and squeaked, "I-I was j-just... I mean, I-I don't know..." She was too terrified to continue, and, anyway, what could she say that would make sense now? She was even more confused than the giant.

    The giant frowned and turned around, spinning her through the air so fast that it blew back her noticeably longer hair. A couple of stomping steps later and she was being held out in a familiar but giant hallway with the giant.

    "JAMIE!" the giant boomed down the hall. Jeff winced and covered her ears with her hands to block out the deafening voice shouting above her. "ONE OF YOUR LITTLE GIRLS WAS IN MY ROOM!"

    "What?" shouted a little girl's voice from some distance.

    "I SAID, ONE OF YOUR GIRLS WAS SNEAKING AROUND IN MY ROOM! YOU KNOW THEY'RE NOT ALLOWED IN THERE!"

    Down the hall, the door to Jamie's room opened and out of it stepped...
