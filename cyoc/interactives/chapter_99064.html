    After leaving the mists, the crew finally got to get a look at themselves, changed by the mist, minds cleared. They were no longer a crew of Novofortian men, but of orcish women. Their skins were mostly shades of green and greenish yellow, while their hair grew a bit and darkened. Their physique had changed a bit, their muscles had been toned in a way that suited orc women best, with supple breasts and wide hips noticeable through their sailors attire. Their noses changed into a pig-like shape,and their lips have noticeably increased in size. Their eyes appeared to have changed to various bright colors and have developed epicanthal folds. Some of their teeth sharpened and some even grew into small tusks as their ears became slightly longer and pointed.

    "Bleedin' arsewipes! I'm an orc lass!" The foreigner of the crew shouted in shock before pausing to notice his.. her new voice, a very deep yet still feminine voice. She still kept her distinct accent in it, though.

    "We.. we're all women.." Emanuel would say, also shocked at her new voice. She still had her ponytail, but it was darkened like the rest of the crew's hair. Despite her green skin, pig-like nose, changed eyes and ears, and tusks, she still resembled a female orcish version of her male self.

    Emilio would blink repeatedly, rubbing her eyes, jaw open, before shaking her head. She would take a look at herself by way of the ocean. She also looked like her twin sister, but had shorter hair, also darkened from a light brown to a natural black.

    "This is a lot to take in.." Emilio would sigh to herself, before turning to her newly changed crew, "But we must press on! We're still to dock by Nanchou, and we will find answers why we changed, and we will get our old bodies back, or die trying."

    "Brother.. are you sure we can change back?" Emanuel would question.

    "This mist was not natural, someone or something caused it to be here. We will most likely find our answers deeper in Zhi." Emilio responded, with a stern look on her face.

    "While we're changed, we may have to get used to it for a bit.. Manuela.." Emilio would smirk while Emanuel, now Manuela, had a slight grimace on her face. "..Manuela.." She sighed. "Fine.. Emilia."

    Emilia snickered. "We'll be fine.. alright men.. er.. women.. prepare to dock!"
