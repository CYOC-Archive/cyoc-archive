    Todd quickly exited is room as Liz showered. He had nothing for the girl to wear and realized she would probably want something fresh. As he rounded the corner he ran into someone.

    "Watch it asshole," the girl yelled. Todd brushed himself as he looked at the girl. Her name was Tina and she was his neighbor. The blond sorority girl hated Todd thinking him a weirdo, he had tried to be friendly but she was the kind of person who only wanted to talk with you if you had deep pockets.

    "Sorry Tina," Todd said sheepishly. Tina glared at Todd maliciously. "Listen asshole I'm not interested in your apologies just get out of my way."

    Todd frowned as she pushed him aside. Todd was about to continue on his way when he heard the remote beep. Looking at it he noticed a read out.

    "Subject matches body type of previous female subject," he read. "Do you wish to apply changes to new subject?"

    Todd was curious as he rrad over the remote. He could use Tinas clothing for Liz. The only question was how to get them? Noticing a bleeping icon that said delete Todd now pressed it.

    Tina froze in place as she glanced towards Todd. "Huh," she muttered. Todd was curious what would happen, to his horror he now gazed at Tina.

    At first he was unable to see anything happening to Tina. As he continued to watch he noticed the girls fingers begin to shrink. In only moments he saw Tinas fingers vanish, leaving a digitless hand. Tina gaped at Todd in shock as her hands now began to shrink as well. Soon those too vanished, it now looked as if the girl was born without hands.

    The changes didnt stop there as Tinas arm now slowly began to disappear as well. As they did the sleeves now hung empty at her side. Tina looked horror struck as she now stood before Todd armless.

    Before she could say anything Todd could see her mouth begin to shrink as well. Soon enough her mouth vanished, now just a patch of flesh on her face. Todds mouth fell open as Tina stared at him.

    Before he could react he watched as Tinas head began to grow smaller as it slowly vanished underneath her blouse. As this was occuring Todd could see that Tinas legs had vanished in a similar fashion to the girls arms. Once that happened the girls jeans and heels fell away empty.

    Todd went pale as he looked at what was left of Tina. A torso that was growing smaller was all that remained under Tinas blouse. In only moments this too vanish causing the blouse to float to the ground empty. Todd gaped at the empty clothing on the floor now.

    Todd watched as the clothes shimmered for a moment, seeming to fold themselves. Now the clothing appeared as if it had nevee been worn. As Todd looked at the remote he read what happened.

    "Female subject deleted," he read. "Clothing articles sanitized for use by previous female subject Liz."

    Shaking with fear Todd picked up Tinas former clothing. He had erased a person from existence with his invention. While Liz now had somethimg to wear Todd wondered just how powerful his remote was.
