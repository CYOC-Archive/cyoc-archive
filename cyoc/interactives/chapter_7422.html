    Standing at the front door, a small pop bottle you found that is half filled with the potion you wish to toss at Red, you listen, watch and wait.

    "I don't know him but Tom and Billy spent the night as pigs!" argued Red with her older sister Sarah.

    "Ok, but what of him, can't you remember him? you came home in a fit of black evil. I don't think you even remeber slapping me as you stormed to your room and sleep off the evil deeds you did. Now he is here and wants to talk with you, I suggest you talk to him and grant him a wish!" argued Sarah back.

    "Ok, ok, ok!" Red mumbles half a spell which now looms above her head in the air like a thin cloud of smoke. Down the front hall she walks, swinging her hips in a seductive sort of way.

    You see he actions and lick your lips wishing deep in your thoughts of how last night if she were a sow you'd.......

    As Red comes to the screen door your thumb pops the top off the bottle potion.

    "Good day young witch, for I am the man you made a pig last night! I and my friends who also were pigs wish for you to know our what being a pig is really like!" as you quickly bring out the bottle from behind you Red slams open the screen door.

    Looking back you had wondered if using a glass bottle was all that bright. Yet if the potion leaked out of a plastic bottle you'd might of been a pig again.

    Then the most horrible thing occures! Red slams the door open in a fit of quick female witch anger. The glass bottle, open and in your grasp is on it's way to deliver a splashing wash into the face of someone you wish to be a pig too.

    "Kerthunk!"

    The edge of the screen door slams the fingers which hold the bottle. You feel the pain, then see to your horror the bottle break and it's contents fly up into your own face.

    Red and Sarah have the look of being stunned by your anger, actions, and thw words wishing her to know what it was to be a pig.

    As you look at them the light mist of smoke which circled Red's head now rushes to circle you. The potion too, has it's evil intent to work wonders on your person.

    Your short eerking scream of terror and pain are cut short as a true hardy male hog squeal echo's the front porch.

    A scowl from Red and then Sarah as Red says, "You came with hatred for me and to do me harm! Your mouth has spoken a wish that will for many years to come be the guide for how you are to live. Last night a pig you were but now a boar hog shall you become. As that hatred and desire to make me your sow, I here command the spirits to bind and hold you as this hog form till I shall marry!" cursed Red.

    Some clothing is fast getting tighter as some loosen's and falls away.
    The popping of mature hog bristles make the sound of rustling stalks of wheat. What was pig once quickly assumes the pig and then the true hog form. Falling forward you slam into the porch floor. A blink of your eyes make the sensations of hog form even more real.

    "Red how could you!" said Sarah.

    "How could I what, he's made us both sows for at least a day!" said Red back showing disgust for the cursed man and hog before her.

    "No Red, he might be the one to live for a time as this boar. he will learn how lucky he was to have been human! Yet you placed a time curse on him, a time curse! Till you marry, you said, think we as witches age one month for every ten mortal's years! Then too your a teenager, you might not marry till your 20! That, that means he will be like the great sea turtles, by then he'd lived as a boar hog for 480 years! said Sarah with an urgent sound in her voice. "Red change your curse and do it now before he runs away and makes it permanent!"

    You oggle this fearful witch, as lust and instincts build inside your mind.

    "To you who be boar and hog I change my curse, Be it not when I do marry that you change back to the man you were, instead when you have mounted your 480th sow, be you changed back to a man, less you forget this transgression the sheath, penis, testicles of your boar self shall be yours to thrill with, but woe unto you if after being a man you return to the sty for a friendly romp with some sow, then you shall find that sty to your home till the day you die!" recursed Red as Sarah stood agasp.

    Like a jolt of electric shock you jump from the porch and run with all speed for a sty, any sty, any sty at all!

    "Billy, Tom, I command you come to me here and now!" says Red.
