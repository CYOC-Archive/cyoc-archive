    The Narrator: Work Can Be Fun

    It is time for work and you decide to go. You have no need to anymore of course, but you have a feeling you might enjoy it today.

    You take your time finishing your sandwich, get dressed and head to work at your own pace. You are more than an hour late but that hardly matters. You sit down at your desk and boot up your PC. As you do so a familiar voice greets you from behind.

    "Morning!"

    It is Christina, your boss. You have mixed feelings about Christina, she has never seemed to like you very much but you are nonetheless strangely attracted to her. She is older than you, probably in her mid thirties you would guess and short, slim and pretty with medium length mousy brown hair and perky breasts. She always dresses quite formally in the office and is wearing a grey skirt and black blouse. She is smart, and seems to have a sense of humour - you often seeing her joking around with other colleagues, but for some reason she has always seemed unimpressed with and irritated by you.

    "You're in a little late today. Is there any reason for that?" Christina asks.

    "I was enjoying my breakfast" you shrug hardly caring about the consequences now.

    "Can I have a word? In private?" she orders more than asks before ushering you into a side room. You follow her and take a seat as she stands over you with a firm expression.

    "I've really tried with you but you aren't giving me much choice here" Christina speaks in a harsh and unsympathetic tone "Your work is sloppy, if you do it at all. You come in late and leave early and you don't even bother to make up an excuse for it. I am giving you a verbal warning and if you don't up your game it is only going to get worse. As things stand I am going to recommend probation for you on your next report. Do you understand?"

    You simply smile. Today really is going to be fun.

    "Christina quickly reconsidered her mean and unfair words" you begin to narrate "Instead she decided to offer me a raise and exemplary report as well as a grovelling apology"

    You smirk as a look of confusion, then concern replaces your bosses stern and businesslike expression.

    "I'm so sorry, I don't know what I was thinking saying that!" Christina sounded sincere to the point of ingratiating in her sudden change of heart "Please forgive me, it won't happen again. I will of course be submitting a glowing report as soon as I get back to my desk and recommending you for an immediate promotion and raise. I hope that is okay?"

    You nod your approval.

    "Good, thank you" she sighs with relief "Well, I suppose I should go and do that right away"

    "No, wait a moment" you reply.

    "Um, okay?" Christina agrees. You have her on her own now. It seems like as good a time as any to see just what your new powers can do. You narrate once more...
