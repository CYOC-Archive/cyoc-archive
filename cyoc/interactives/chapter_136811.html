    That night you and Tina had slept in the same room. Although Tina mostly seemed really hurt and thinking. You supposed her mother was really important to her, your own mother was very important to you as well and wouldn't know what you would do if anything happened to her... Well you supposed that you may live with Frederic for awhile. The following morning you were packing up and then said to Tina "Feel free to visit in the woods if you ever want to."

    Tina just nodded and then you left back with your father to the woods. It was a rather silent ride as Frederic was pondering about how his research would become more complicated. What would Sara say? What was Sar thinking? Did he owe a lot of child support? After the couple hours of driving the two of you were in the village town. Your mother was back home and being a little home sick you turned your legs into that of a deers and ran fast through the forest. The fresh air and noises of the forest were comforting. In no time you found your home and knocked on the front door saying "Mommy, It's me! I came home early!" A minute past and then a voice came out from the inside.

    Your mother yelled "Sorry, I was guiding a soul." she opened the door and then said "Come in come in!" You walked inside the room which was the same as always as your mother asked "So did you figure out who your father was or at least what your name is young one?"

    You sighed and said "Apparently Frederic was my father so that little nickname Sar is my name..."

    Your mother asked "What's the matter Sar?"

    You said "Well my magic developed a bit, but Frederic's Son... Well he felt like my existence was proof of his father not being faithful towards his dead mother... At least now I know that my magic can change others..."

    Your mother responded "Just give it a little time Sar. Human's live short enough lives that they'll snap themselves out of it so they wont waste too much of their lives. You can just keep helping out around the house. Also I hear that the village elder has been talking about tourism. You could join it and by giving tourists wings they could have special tours even!"

    You sighed and said "if you say so..."

    Years passed, you took up the village elder and gave tours that partially used your abilities to give others the ability to glide to better see the forest. You were really popular, and the outside races were constantly talking about new and curious things. On occasion a guest would propose to you in marriage, you were flattered. The review cards would say you were kind and down to earth, but mirrors and reflections had shown that you had grown up relatively curvy with some nice elven charms. You were living in your own little house in the forest now. Looking through the mail you had gotten included your pay check, and for once a letter from someone. You didn't have many ties to the outside world as you mostly looked for new paths or trails for hikers. Looking on the front it read "To Sar, From Tina." The letter was asking for you to go and have a reunion with Tina. She wanted to make amends and help get over some old feelings. You managed to get some vacation days relatively soon and packing light started to fly your way back to the city.

    A days travel was exhausting for you. Now in the city you were still catching your breath you had been asking around for directions and stumbled your way to Tina's house in the evening. You knocked on the door and a pretty face opened it and you asked "Is that you Tina? it's me Sar." To which the pretty face said...
