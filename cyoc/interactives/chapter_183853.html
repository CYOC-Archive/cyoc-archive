    Before he knew it, Jason realized that Mandy had stopped dragging him, stopping so suddenly that Jason almost tripped over her. He glanced around, and realized where they were.
    "A clothing store?" He muttered aloud.

Mandy then turned and gave him a smile. He smiled back genuinely. Jason still felt guilty about making her more of a tomboy farmhand, but she definitely could still pull off any look.

    "Time for some rearranging." She giggled, as she pulled out the app once more.

    Jason snapped back immediately to his situation.
    "Uh, w-wait, what? Rearrange what?" He asked concerned.

    "Shhh. Come on now baby, you don't gotta holler." She smirked. "Just always felt this darn place had too many useless stores and such, so I reckon I could make some of them into... more useful locales." She explained.

    Before Jason could even process what she had said, Mandy had already quickly changed a word on the phone. Jason's heart practically leapt out of his chest. Was she changing the whole store?! They were standing in it, and if changed Mandy before then what if it changed both of them now?! Would they be aware?! Jason also realized he wasn't holding the phone, so he couldn't even guarantee he'd notice the change!

    "Here we go now." Mandy snickered as she went to press enter.
