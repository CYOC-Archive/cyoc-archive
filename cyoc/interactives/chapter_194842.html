    Janet in Wonderland: Such a Tiny Little Thing

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago S Shrinking Magic Clothes

    After staring at herself for a full minute, Janet decided to walk around the house in her Alice in Wonderland costume. At first she considered changing back into her original outfit, but decided to remain in her current attire and enjoy dressing as Alice once again. Besides, she was the only one there so there's no worries about being caught. Eventually her walking turned into skipping, then Janet started to twirl and dance about as she enjoyed the way her skirt and petticoats shifted around her waist as she moved.
    Janet ended up in the kitchen as she continued her ballet. But as she extended her left arm, Janet accidentally knocks over a cylinder shapped salt shaker onto the floor. Before Janet could react fast enough, the salt shaker ends up rolling under the oven.
    "Oh crap!" Janet curses out loud.
    Janet gets on her hands and knees and lowers her head down to the ground to look for the salt shaker. Fortunately there was plenty of light under the oven so Janet was able to find it. Unfortunately the salt shaker had rollen into the back wall, making getting it out far more difficult. Janet stands back up and considers her options.
    "Well this sucks. How am I gonna reach that stupid thing now?"
    As Janet looked around the kitchen for anything that could help her, she noticed the amulet still around her neck. Remembering that she was still dressed up as Alice in Wonderland, Janet suddenly got a crazy idea.
    "If I used the amulet to shrink myself, I could just walk under the oven and roll the salt shaker back out no problem. But do I really want to change my own size just for a salt shaker?"
    Deep down, Janet really did want to change her size. It's actually part of the reason she enjoyed being Alice so much. With the thought of shrinking eventually winning out, a big smile appeared on Janet face.
    "Yes, I do. What's the point of dressing as Alice if your not gonna go all the way? Alright amulet, make me smaller."
    As Janet thought about shrinking, the amulet began to glow and soon it spread throughout the rest of her body and clothes. Janet suddenly felt something inside her pushing against her skin and whole body began to feel extremely bloated. Janet gasped as everything around her began to shrink as she began to grow.
    "I'm growing? Why am I growing? I thought I was suppose to shrink."
    As Janet grew, the bloating sensation intensified to unimaginable pressures. When her head reached the kitchen ceiling, the pressure reached its climax and Janet found herself burping, farting, and quiffing al at once. The combination causes her to rapidly shrink in size. Fortunately her Alice in Wonderland costume shrank with her. Soon the pressure subsided and Janet found herself laying on her back, staring up at a now much more massive kitchen.
    "Goodness. I didn't expect to shrink like that."
    Janet picks herself off the floor and marvels at her new surroundings. Janet guessed that she was now one centimeter high.
    "Wow, I never thought I would ever be this tiny in my life. I know I'm gonna have fun with this."
