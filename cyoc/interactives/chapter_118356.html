    The art instructor Edmund Cobranegro looms over the four fruits, and picks each up and puts them into the glass bowl he is carrying. He is wearing a paint stained smock, and rubber gloves. He picks up the phone gingerly, and turns it off. Then he drops it into a pocket in his smock.

    The giant teacher picked up Braiden and polished the pear against his smock.

    "NO!" screamed Braiden, "Don't eat me!"

    The three other fruiteens heard Braiden, but the teacher seemed deaf to his words.

    But instead of biting Braiden, Edmundo put Braiden on top of the fruit pyramid he had built in the bowl. He carried the bowl of fruit down the hall to his classroom. The easels were set up in a circle around a high stool. A sheet was draped across the stool. He set the bowl of fruit on the sheet covered stool. Then he turned it slightly left, than back a tad to the right.

    "Ah, per-fect!" he said.

    The bell rang, and his students started filing in.

    "Oh, look! Free fruits," said Carl. He said walking up to the bowl and grabbing Callum with one hand, and reaching with the other toward the stem to peel the banana.

    "NO! Carl!" shrieked Callum and Edmundo in unison. Except only Edmund's voice was heard by the class.

    Edmundo dashed forward and grabbed the banana from Carl.

    "Free fruit indeed. You know from your syllabus that today is "STILL LIFE PAINTING", and you just messed up my model. Now take your seat, and do your best. This little stunt just took 10 points off your grade for today, Carl."

    Callum sighed in relief. The teacher spent about five minutes rearranging the fruits with banana on top of the pyramid, then the dragonfruit, then the pear, and finally the strawberry. Then he tried stacking the strawberry and banana on top of the dragon fruit and pear. Then he went back to the one with the strawberry on top.

    After the first 20 minutes, Braiden started whining about not being able to scratch an itch. Brody freaked when a fly landed on him and shoved it's proboscis into his strawberry skin, pumped digestive juices in, and then started slurping up a strawberry smoothie.

    Meanwhile, the teacher walked around the classroom a few times inspecting the student's work. After a bit, he pulled the phone out of his pocket, and began to examine the phone.
