    Kellie's friends nodded as Kellie and Katie stepped away from the group. As the two now looked eachother Kellie smiled, embracing Katie in a tight hug.

    "Good to see you again Katie," she sighed happily.

    "Good to see you too mom," Katie grinned.

    Kellie now looked her daughter over, curious as to why Katie was now a teen once more. "So two questions, " she stated. "What do I owe the pleasure and why are you younger than the last time I saw you? Not that I'm complaining, its just a....surprise."

    Kellie gave Alt Katue a curious look as the teen blushed slightly. "Okay can I ask why?"

    Alt Katie fidgeted slightly as she tried to avoid eye contact. Kellie sighed, sensing something was amiss. "Okay Katie tell me what happened to you."

    "Not me," Alt Katue said quickly. "Its....Gloria..my sister."

    Alt Katie now explained how Gloria wanted to help Kellie. Katie had refused to allow it, that is until her sister and her friend Felicia found themselves suddenly preparing themselves for a double date with two boys. Although Katie admitted she may be paranoid she needed to make sure Morgan Lefay wasn't involved. "So you think these two boys may have a connection with this reality?"

    Katie shrugged, looking down at her feet. "I don't know, " she mumbled. "I mean I know one of the boys has always been in Gloria's grade but the other is magical. Somehow the ring aged him into a teen, I just need to be sure that it's nothing."

    Kellie smiled, pulling Alt Katie into a tight hug. "Well that is very sweet of you," she said warmly. "We'll figure this out together...just like old times."

    Alt Katie nodded as Kellie now took her hand. "And hey who knows," Kellie smirked. "Maybe after this is done you could hang out for a bit."
