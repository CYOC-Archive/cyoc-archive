    Meanwhile, Talia and Midnight slowly walked through the streets of Fee City. There were a great deal of differences between Fee City and many of the other places that Midnight had been since hir transformation. The city was populated entirely by Kats, but only Chakats or Foxkats. As shi walked with Talia, shi also found that there were also large numbers of Kats that were only six years old or maybe a month or two older than that, about the same age as Janice, Pinkie, Snowflake, Silky, and Foxy back in Janace. Some had begun to wear some of Danna and Mikkie's clothing designs, but to Midnight's great surprise, many went about their business completely naked, and having sex in public was nothing unusual there. Some of these things were understandable to Midnight. Shi knew Charlie and Sally had sex all the time, even while Charlie was pregnant with Fluffy and Mary. The other adult Kats also enjoyed mating with each other when they had the free time, but Midnight had not seen much of that in Janace, though mostly due to humans moving there as part of the UN mission.

    "It would seem that Snowy and Maurice's vision of a free love driven world did come true here," Talia commented as shi looked watching a pair of tiger based Chakats, both heavily pregnant walking together on the other side of the street.

    "Yes," Midnight commented, "and it's become a bit of a nudist colony it seems."

    "Probably the rapid result in which all of the city became either a Chakat or Foxkat before your friends invented their clothing line," Talia commented, "Come along... we have our mission with the old Chakat Industries Labs..."

    Midnight nodded and slowly followed Talia.

    "I'm still not sure what we're supposed to find," Midnight commented, "the FBI seized EVERYTHING when the old Chakat Industries was shut down. There is no lab there, and Maurice and Snowy have both said that many of the Kats here didn't like having their choice in what they were to be taken from them. They love each other... but they don't love Snowy and Maurice."

    "I'd think the idea is to get some clues that were the spy was," Talia commented.

    They made their way along the streets and ultimately came to the old Chakat Industries building. It was still there, but looked as though no one had been there since the facility had been seized by the government. Windows were broken and the building looked like it had been in a general state of disrepair. As Talia looked up she saw a flash of movement up on one of the upper floors, which caught hir by surprise. Why would there be someone in a closed building. Shi immediately spread hir wings and flew up toward the level and entered through an open windows, only to see a shadow disappear down a doorway. Shi moved after it, but as shi arrived, shi found hirself looking down three separate hallways, and to make matters worse, the same scent went down all three hallways. Talia stood puzzled for a moment until shi heard Midnight's calls coming up behind hir.

    "Talia! Talia, where did you go?" Midnight called.

    "I'm here!" Talia called back, "I saw something... but whoever it was gave me the slip."

    "Who could it be?" Midnight wondered, "This place is a dump and likely to fall apart... or become a "Life after People" episode."

    "I do not know," Talia said slowly, "Other than that he is male and is not a Kat or a Taur."

    "Not a Kat or a Taur?" Midnight asked as shi finally arrived.

    Talia nodded.

    "Where did you see him?" Midnight asked.

    "Right over there..." Talia said as shi pointed to the window that shi came into.

    They both approached the area and then looked to see a door was open. Midnight was the first to enter, and found that it was an old office, and it looked slightly better than the rest of the building did, other then the scent of candles and permeated the room and the sight of smoke damage to the ceiling titles. Both also soon found clothing laid about the room and a sleeping bag and a pillow set up in one point. Someone had been living there, and apparently for quite some time. It made little sense, and the two began looking for clues as to who the squatter was and why he was there. Eventually, Talia found a small notebook and ballpoint pen underneath the sleeping bag. Curious she opened it, and found that it was a journal. It read:

    "Monday... Maurice keeps insisting on this hippie fantasy of his. As if the sins of the flesh shall set everyone free and make them happy. All he's doing is sending them to hell. I wish I could persuade him to turn back from this and embrace the Truth as it was written...

    Wednesday... Met a strange Chinese man today, he seemed rather spiritual and talked a lot about Taoism and Buddhism. It made for interesting conversation, though I found little that might convince my brother to abandon the idea that SEX will set people free. Though I must admit that I did not hide my jealousy well and he realized my personal issues with Maurice. I tried to reason that all I wanted was to get him to see the light, but that he wouldn't listen. Then the Chinese man said something very telling... sometimes we must be burned by our own mistakes and flaws to realize what we're doing is wrong.

    Thursday... I called the Chinese man again, and asked him about what I could do to help convince Maurice that his idea is wrong and sinful. He repeated the message that he must see his idea be a force of chaos and inspiring hate to get him to abandon it. I told him I do not want to hurt my brother, but the Chinese man was quite certain that if I did not do as he said, that Maurice would turn the whole world into sex driven freaks and they'll end up damned eternally. I want to save my brother...

    Saturday... I've agreed to do it. It's taken some soul searching, but I've been unable to convince Maurice to turn back. God forgive me for what I am about to do... and the betrayal of trust I'm about to commit... but I'm doing this to save my brother before he is lost..."

    At that Talia closed the journal, "The spy was Maurice's brother..."
