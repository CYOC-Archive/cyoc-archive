    Closer with Mom

    Katie and Martin were best friends. As twin siblings they did everything together and lived a happy life with their handsome father and beautiful mother. Things were never too complicated with the Monroe's until the test came. As was standard for any child after their ninth birthday, they were to be tested for the were mother curse.

    Their mother Claire was worried about the possible outcome. Her older brother had been a were mother and having two mothers around the house 1 week of every month was strange enough, but if she had a twin, or even triplets of herself, it would turn her life upside down.

    The doctor came back into the room with a somber look on his face. "Ma'am, I'm sorry to inform you but both of your children have tested positive for the were mother gene. I'm sure this may come as a shock to you considering the rarity of the gene in girls, but this is a completely manageable condition. The next full moon is in about a week, you should start getting ready." He says as he hands Claire a pamphlet of information on the curse.

    "My brother had the curse so I know how to deal with it. Don't worry kids everything will be fine." Claire says worriedly.

    When the trio gave the news to their father Jack he tells Claire, "You should get Dale here. The kids love him and he'll be able to help us through it."

    Claire spent the night wondering about what it would be like watching little Katie grow older. Her cute chubby face becoming Claire's mature striking one while her body adopted Claire's large beautiful curves while her blonde hair became Claire's fiery red. Her stubborn son becoming her beautiful identical sister. What scared her the most is what would happen to their minds. Were moms' minds were a mix of the original and the mother's and they would adopt a completely different persona while transformed.

    The next day Claire called her brother Dale, the former were mom, to ask him for help with her children's impending transformation. "Of course I can come down sis. They're gonna need all the help they can get to make it through." Dale says.

    When Dale arrives the kids are glad to see their cool uncle. It's hard for them to believe that the jacked, hairy middle aged man before them with a fiery beard and hair used to become a woman once a month. They spent the next few days talking about what the transformation would feel like, what they would experience while transformed into their mother, and how they would cope for the rest of their lives.

    When the night of the change finally came, the family felt pretty prepared. The kids knew what was going to happen and they had their first week completely planned out. Jack had planned on heading out for the night. It would just be the Dale and Claire to help the twins through their first transformation...
