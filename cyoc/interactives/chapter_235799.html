One of her friends gave her a Young Adult novel where the female protagonist went on a blind date with a man, but it turned out he was the young boss of the mafia. There was something about a man who was dangerous but also so loving and protective that made you feel so safe and secure. Though now that she was older, Lilianne knew that it wasn't a good idea to associate with men like that in real life. A happily ever after with those sorts of men usually only happened in fiction.
