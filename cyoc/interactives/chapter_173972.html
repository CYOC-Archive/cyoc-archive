It all looked so good to her, but then she remembered what she was told to pack for lunch this morning: the dog food! Of course! Why didn’t she think of that sooner?!

She instantly fell in love with the taste of the kibble! She didn’t even bother to use her hands to eat it because dogs don’t have hands! They have paws! The cold water was so nice and refreshing and it made her mouth feel so cool and moist after enjoying a big bowl of the delicious kibble. She took big gulps out of it with her tongue which made it a lot easier for her to drink it all up.
