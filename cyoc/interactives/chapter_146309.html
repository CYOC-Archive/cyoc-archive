    Ty was still astounded as he stared at the 13 year old girl that was his foster mom. Eileen had been right, if she were younger than him and Eileen than her arrogance and confidence would evaporate. Not wanting to have June bossing him around again, an idea formed in his head. Now he wondered if he could pull it off.

    "June head back to your room and get changed," he said.

    Eileen and June now looked at Ty curiously. "But this is my room," June said meekly. "No your room is down the hall and too the left," Ty said. "The same place it's always been. Now go before I tell the folks you've been going through her closet again."

    As June shuffled passed, Eileen now approached Ty, a curious look on her face. "What did you do," she whispered. Ty smirked as he leaned towards Eileen. "Wanted to see how powerful I really am," he said quietly. "If this works it's really going to mess with her mind."

    June shuffled down the hall to where Ty had said. The room Ty had pointed to was a spare bedroom she used for storage. June had thought about turning the room into a sewing room but had dismissed the idea. The last thing she wanted to do was sew. Turning the knob she expected to see the various boxes collecting dust, instead she let out an audible gasp.

    "That's, that's impossible," she whispered.

    June gaped at the teenage girls bedroom in front of her. Painted a soft pink, June could see the girl was beginning to transition from child to teen. Posters of teenage heartthrobs hung from the walls while a handful of stuffed animals lay strewn across the girls sturdy looking bed. Gazing at the furniture June could see a sturdy dresser with some laundry poking out of the dresser draws. As she slowly entered the room she noticed a desk strewn with school work. Glancing at the work she held in a scream as she noticed it had her name on it. "Algebra," she whispered. "That's impossible, this cant be. How can I have homework from the seventh grade?!"

    Quickly running to her closet, June threw it open, her eyes crowing wide in terror. Jean's that would be fashionable for a girl in junior high were nearly hung, along with dresses. The types of dresses a young teen would wear for the holidays or to their first dance. As she looked towards the bottom of the closet June was stunned to see sneakers, small heels, and boots all for a teenage girl. Curiously June now placed her foot inside one of the small heels, finding it fit her perfectly.

    "Im.not a little girl," she whispered. "8m not, this is a bad dream." Turning towards the overfilled dresser June rushed towards it. Flinging it open June gazed down at tshirts, bras, panties, and socks. Each article of clothing was something a young teen girl would wear. As June meticulously tried on several articles of clothing her fear intensified. The bras, the panties, the shirts, everything perfectly fit the young teen.

    June collapsed onto the floor, drawing her legs close as she hugged them tightly. As insane as it was, this was her room. June's eyes once more began to dampen with tears, how had this happened? Trying to catch her breath June paused as she noticed a pair of pictures laying on the floor. Tentatively she reached for them, groaning when she saw what it was.

    "No," she whispered.

    The first picture June saw was of Dustin and Ty. It was a picture she had taken not too long ago. Now though the picture included a smiling young blond teen posing for a picture with her two older brothers.

    The second picture was of the blond teen as well. This picture appeared to be the typical school picture children took at the beginning of the school year. As June gazed it she saw it was her and it listed her as a seventh grader this year. Sniffing June now lowered her head as she began to cry softly.

    Eileen had snuck down the hallway with Ty, shocked when she saw what Tt had done. "Oh my god," she whispered. "You did that? You actually made it so it looks like shes only 13."

    "I guess I did," Ty said slowly. "I didnt think my powers could actually do that. I'm surprised that's for sure."

    As the two teens stared at the sullen girl, they wondered what else they could do.
