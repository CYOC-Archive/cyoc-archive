    I need a lamb

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago A AR S

    "A sheep will do!" He looked at you while smiling. "A sheep?" You ask. "You heard me, sheep are cute as hell!" The Sheep prince in the audience cheered as he said he selected you to be a sheep.

    "Now stay still! This'll be over before you know it" he asked of you. "Wait, will I be feral or an Anthro like all of you?" You ask. "Feral of course! This world already has sheep like that." "Okay." "Now you'll be a lamb, I already have a ram and a ewe." "What?" "Oh right, I forgot to mention that the animals in my kingdom become immortal and don't age, to preserve what they look like."

    You gulp as you ready yourself for however you'll be turned. You're in a magic bubble next to the Stage hoisted in the air. He touches it and his Hand glowed Pink, as did his horn? You felt weird, it wasn't painful, more just uncomfortable. Your body shrinking, your bones moving and stretching and just overall changing. You felt stuff grow on your body, it was obviously wool.

    After an hour of morphing you finally are done, you're left panting, you try to say something but all that come out are Bleats. Elias grabs you out of the bubble and holds you close as he leaves to his Carriage, he gets in and holds you close as it starts moving. "Now here's the boring part, it took five hours to get here, so buckle up... What's your name again?" He asks to himself, knowing you can't respond. You try to think to yourself about it but you can't even remember it. "Eh, whatever, your new name is Elias Jr."

    You kinda like the name admittedly. "You're pretty cute y'know." He says while smiling at you. "By the way don't worry about Janice, she's right behind us in our other Carriage, I'm just not having her in here because I'm gonna sleep." You feel better knowing Janice is still gonna be here with you. "Maybe I should turn her into a feral fox... I don't know." Silence took over for a minute. "Wanna know something EJ? I'm only 16, I only go to these concubine things to see if I like any of the People there, if I don't, I leave." He's just sixteen? He looked young sure, but you weren't expecting him to be underage. "Anyways, I'm gonna nap now, you can sleep with me or whatever until we get back to the Unicorn kingdom." He soon passed out, you used this as an excuse to sleep too.
