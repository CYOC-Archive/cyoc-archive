With this miraculous device, you hold the power to change both the physical and mental aspects of all around you within a radius of 20 feet. Keep in mind that for optimal results, the subject in question had ought to stand directly adjacent to the machine.

    Physical: Subject altered in body and physiology may be transformed under terms of age. By this it is referred to that a recipient of chronivac operations can be made via extremely complicated biochemical stimuli to grow younger or older by command.

    A subject's species may also be altered by the device. It is important to note that the chronivac's operations in this prototype are vastly different than those made use of by the Earthling model. In terms of species, a party may be altered in distinguishing genealogy. For example, a lion zooanthro may be transformed into a horse if the appropriate specifications are inserted.

    DEVOLUTION option. We encourage a sparing but deliberate use of this function for purposes of gathering consumer data in both medical and satisfactory interests.

    Mental: A party may be transformed from a standpoint of the mind in a hypnotic pattern or for configuration to a physical edit.

    A subject may be changed mentally in terms of their age (psychologically or emotionally).
    A subject can be made to believe they are a member of a different specie or that they are one of the "lesser developed" members of their kind. I.E., a zooanthro fox can be made to behave like an Earthling relative.

    Perception is perhaps the most key of all of the mental choices. Also referred to as "the awareness option" this specification changes the thinking process of the transformed to either that of the original, unchanged identity, or the newly instigated idiosyncrasies of the edited body.
    By this, an adult who becomes a toddler may either retain their mature attitude and behavior or become in most ways a toddler in mind as well.

    It IS possible to change the sexual orientation of a subject. The edit of sexuality is ONLY successful if the application is both PHYSICAL AND MENTAL.

    When one lacks the support of the other, the original mandate of the body rejects the reformation.

    Additionally, when attempting to convert a party into a bisexual, be advised that complications in personal genealogy sometimes induces and asexual nature or a complete change between hetero and homo. The condition is vice versa when testing an asexual conversion.

    This version of chronivac offers an estimated preview to any changes to be made, though due complexities of individuality, not all may be accurate.

Permanent not being eternal, but actually locking the transformed in their new state in a stasis that can only be reversed through revocation of the file in the chronivac hard-drive.

    Any unsatisfying alterations may be repealed in reverse sequence with the 'UNDO' choice or all at once using 'REVERT.' It is advised that REVERT not be used for cases exceeding 35 consecutive changes at risk of lag in software processing or operational error.

    You should now be ready to utilize the user-friendly interface of your chronivac. If you should like to operate your machine without computer aide, the chronivac's inbuilt monitor drains little battery power and is operated by stylus and touch screen. However optimal efficiency and greatest possible array of transformations is only feasible with computer assistance.

    On the basic PC menu, you will find links to the online chronivac service and maintenance website and consumer forums, where you may chat or seek advise from other chronivac owners.

    Additional information and troubleshooting can be found in the enclosed manual along with your chronivac hardware.

    TransDem also reminds the user of the implied law of consent,in which it is in the best interests of all involved with the chronivac to be changed by choice. It is the responsibility of all participants to respect the rights of sentient beings.

    Thank you for reading and enjoy this fabulous product!"
