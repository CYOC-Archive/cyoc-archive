    Barry’s ride home

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago O

    Barry’s skin finished changing back to normal. Still had the game’s clown make up on since that was normal clown make up. He took off his wide clown costume and hung it back up in his dressing room. He put his game’s uniform back on hat and nose included. He looked out of his room and saw that Alfie now Allen and Tootsy now Tiffany both had their own versions of the Game’s uniform. Popsy walked in and commemorated the three clowns on a job well done. She looked at Barry telling him what a great job he did for his first show. She said alright head on home your next show will be in 2 days. Tomorrow I’ll have you learn the game’s job that you’ll work when your aren’t performing. Saying good night to everyone. He hopped on his bike and pedaled home. As he was riding home he rode past Trevor and his brother on the other side of the street. Barry saw that his brother wasn’t a Blue Jay anymore but was just a kid in a blue Jay costume now. Trevor looked over and saw him. He called him over taking note of his flashy outfit. Trevor asked where he was tonight since he thought he was working. Barry trying the not let him in on his clown secret. He said “oh yeah I was on my break near the end of the night I must’ve missed you”. Trevor not fully believing him since who would give someone a break at the end of the night. Barry brought his attention back to Danny talking about his super detailed blue Jay costume. Danny was a little bit confused how he knew that it was specifically a blue Jay. Danny still kind of giddy about the whole situation said that a clown at the circus turned me into one! Playing off as him being a silly kid. Barry said he needed to get home. He peddled away at a brisk pace.
