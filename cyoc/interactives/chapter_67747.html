    She disguises herself in a suit of armour as a man,and gets her crew and companions
    and the longboat and sets off on her quest

    At the Straits of the Clashing Rocks the ship is wrecked and all
    aboard die except her and Leif the Obstinate

    He commandeers the sole rowboat and heads back to the Kingdom

    She on the other hand is cast ashore on the desolate shores below
    the dragons lair.

    Grimmelda struggles up to the lair and finds the black scaled
    fiercesome dragon wearing the Dragons Crown on a carved ivory throne.

    He points out even if she slays him for the Crown,she's got no
    means of returning to the Kingdom with it,and if she stays,theres nothing
    for her to eat.

    He offers her a solution.He has the power to appear as a noble man
    or noble lady in foreign lands and he's willing to lend her his second
    body and take her back to the Kingdom,where she can use his shapeshifting
    powers to win the Kingdom without use of the Crown.

    Theres a catch of course,she'll need to die in order to be reborn as
    his alter egos,and if they ever meet again afterwards,she'll turn into
    him,the Dragon

    She accepts

    He rears back that long scaly neck ,swings open those jaws and gobbles
    her up.

    When she awakens,she's a shapeshifter lying on the shores of the Kingdom.

    She uses her dragon powers to claim the throne for herself after the King dies.

    She rules as Queen Grimmelda,with the rarely seen Prince Albert her alter
    ego husband.

    Her belly swells up in a false pregnancy,for which she uses her
    shapeshifty powers.Since she's borrowing the Dragons loaned body
    true pregnancy is not possible

    Leif the Obstinate heads off on a secret voyage

    One fine day in Queen Grimmelda's court Leif the Obstinate comes in,bearing a
    stretcher on which sits the decapitated head of the Dragon,still wearing his
    Crown.

    The final condition he laid apon her has been broken.They have
    met again.

    She struggles to remain herself,but as her breasts shrink away
    and her belly flattens out,the throbbing shaft insde her womanhood
    widens and grows and lengthens as he tries to avoid becoming
    the Dragon.

    The male dragon member pushes aside his dress and he glowers down at Leif the Obstinate as horns
    push out of the back of his reshaping skull

    He grows in fiercesomeness and darkens as the throne cracks
    under his increasing mass.

    Grimmelda the Dragon cannot keep within his tiny female shell and all
    bursts aprt as the Dragon is born...
