    Kendra had been studying on making another transformative collar for herself. She knew she’d be able to make one if she set her mind to it. Zoe loved being a fox and being fucked by someone else was amazing. Instead of making Zoe make a pact or erase her memory; maybe making Zoe into a familiar of sorts would work. Not that Zoe would be a fox for the rest of her life, just when she wore the collar. While she was in that form, Zoe could make an agreement to become a familiar. It was crazy but it could work. Kendra texted Zoe as she got the materials needed for her plan.

    Come by my place after dinner for a sleepover. I have something for you!

    Kendra spent the rest of the day making the collar and was done by the time dinner rolled around.

    The doorbell rang and Kendra launched herself at the door. She opened the door.

    “What do you have planned my mischievous vixen?” Zoe inquired with a mischievous grin as she walked in.

    “Oh nothing much. I just need to explain a few things to you.”

    “That’s boring! You said you had something for me!”

    “Oh I do, don’t worry,” Kendra said laughing. She leading Zoe to the sofa. “Magic needs to be kept secret. You can’t tell anyone about it.” Zoe was nodding in confirmation to this, “Normally there are a few ways we mages keep magic secret from others who see it or find out about it. Forced memory loss is one way.”

    “Mmhmm which is what I already experienced,” Zoe said smiling.

    “That was just a weak version. There are others that are complete memory wipes which can make you forget the last few days completely. Those are dangerous on the individual though.”

    Zoe’s face went a little pale at this point

    “The other option is a blood pact. This basically puts a person into servitude of a mage for the rest their life.

    “You’re kidding right?” Zoe was visibly shaken now.

    “I’m not. You can see how serious this is. You haven’t told anyone right? About what happened to you or me?”

    Zoe confirmed silently with a nod.

    “Good. I do have another option for you. Since I know you enjoyed your transformation so much I want to try a third option. You become my familiar.

    Zoe cocked her head in surprised confusion, “What?”

    “My familiar. In other words, just a loose association which puts a single restraint on you. That restraint is to protect me. That means you’ll be prevented from ever talking about real magic to others.” Zoe seemed to calm some with some more information. “The other thing is familiars need to be summoned creatures or animals.”

    Zoe’s eyes grew wide, “You don’t mean…”

    Kendra knew what Zoe was thinking and answered her unasked question, “Not permanently. My thought is you become an animal with the help of a collar and then you agree to be my familiar. You’ll be able to transform back into human form at any time.”

    “That doesn’t sound so bad. Especially considering my other options…”

    “No. Not bad at all. Now for that item I was going to give you.” Kendra walked over to the kitchen counter and picked up a gorgeous red collar embroidered with blue sapphires. “If you agree to be my familiar, I will grant you this gift which will make you into a _______”
