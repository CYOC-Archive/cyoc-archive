    The minotaur growled as it lifted its ax above its head, ready to bring it down and kill Ty.
    Suddenly, Ty's door burst off its hinges and there stood Dristun. Ty watched as his foster
    brother transformed into a Dragon. The two orcs turned to face the doorway, but the
    minotaur kept his focus on Ty. Dristun roared and charged the two orcs, breathing fire at
    them. Both of the creatures cried out as they burst into flames, they then fell to the ground
    smoldering. The minotaur swung its battle ax down and Ty raised his hand. The minotaur
    gave a grunt of surprise as it flew into the desk. It cried out in terror as Dristun covered it
    in flames.

    The Dragon turned to Ty.

    "Tyronius, we must get out of here. First you must turn back into your true form!" shouted
    Dristun.

    Ty closed his eyes and he transformed back into a Dragon. He smiled at Dristun.

    "Lets go!" he said

    A loud crash and a roar from down stairs alerted them that Ty's foster parents had turned
    into Dragons and were also fighting. He and Dristun headed for the stairs and stopped.
    Two human sized weasels were running up the stairs, shouting and wielding spears. Ty
    stood on his hind legs and flapped his wings. The two weasels cried out as they fell back
    down the stairs. Both Dragons then ran down and stepped over the two unconscious
    animals and turned to look in the kitchen, or what was left of it. The rear and side walls of
    the house were lying on the ground grumbled. The roof had collapsed and two dead
    minotaurs were seen lying against the kitchen counter. Jewel blew fire and a goblin fell to
    the ground as it tried to put out the flames. Markal swung his tail and orcs, rats, weasels
    and a minotaur went flying out into the back yard.

    Markal turned to his son and Ty.

    "We must head back to the kingdom, they have found us!" he said as he kicked a ferret into
    the tv. An orc cried out as it leapt through a window and aimed its bow and arrow at Ty.
    Dristun charged the creature and rammed it into the wall, crushing it and breaking its
    neck.

    Suddenly police sirens were heard approaching the street their house was located on. A
    minotaur, who looked to be the captain, made a desperate leapt out through the sliding
    glass doors. The glass shattered and he rolled onto his feet. Jewel covered three goblins in
    flame and then turned to her husband.

    'Then say the spell to get us back home! We can't let the humans see us!" she growled

    Markal began to glow and then a bright light flashed over all four dragons. When it cleared
    they were gone. An orc, who had been hidden by the front door, ran for the back of the
    house. The front door flew open and two swat members stepped into the room. The orcs
    growled and it shot an arrow, hitting a man in the shoulder. The other man aimed his
    machine gun and shot the creature through the chest. The two weasels then stood up and
    tried to run but were also brought down by the terrified man.

    The police cheif came into the house and stared around in shock at the damage and the
    strange bodies.

    "What the heck happened here?" he shouted

    meanwhile...

    Markal, Jewel, Dristun and Tyronius arrived back in Nazara's kingdom. Markal began to
    look around, it was quiet and there were no Dragons guarding the stone gates that lead
    into the montain. They slowly walked up to the open doors and stared at piles of
    smoldering ash and the bodies of two minotaurs. As Ty walked into the mountain hall he
    gasped. The hall was littered with dead bodies of the enemies and the two bodies of
    Dragon guards. Jewel's blood ran cold as she stepped in front of her prince. Dristun
    stepped up to Ty's side and Markal took the rear. They walked through many halls and
    when they arrived at the throne room doors, they saw they were opened and surrounded
    by dead rats and goblins. Another dead Dragon guard greeted them when they stepped
    into the room.

    Ty heard a groan and he turned his head to see a minotaur leaning against the wall with a
    large gash in its side. it was dying. Markal ran up to it, followed by the other three
    dragons.

    "You, what happened here? Where is the Queen Nazara?" he demanded

    The minotaur stared at the Dragons and coughed. It then began to speak...
