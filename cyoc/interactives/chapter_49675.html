    'So now we are a pack of ten.' Jensen thought, 'Myself and Kaylee, the twins, the pups,

    Alice spoke in pace and in a calm polite tone, "Shall over the next twenty four chiming of
    the hour you shall turn into a quite ordinary brownish gold furred deer buck and a quite
    ordinary brownish gold furred deer doe. Any fawns you produce with each other or other
    deer will be perfect game animals and share traces of your golden color and carry on
    those traits. After all the changes are good and done with, you shall forget about anything
    you will have missed from no longer being people, and have no desire to be people, and
    will think only of your herd and it's duty in providing game for the pack. You will only be

    feeling of your children not having to learn twice times and all that rot and feel quite

    grand gratitude towards the pack and Master. You really should use the time you have to
    call your friends and family tell them good-bye and make sure they don't come looking for
    you, and be consistent in what you tell them, but really shouldn't mention the game or the
    pack or you're becoming deer to them, they really wouldn't understand. You really should
    use your turn at the game while you can still communicate to help the rest of the staff
    help the pack be prosperous and safe and stay in control of the estate in the next twenty
    four chiming of the hour."

    it that a curse placed by them would have some leeway?"

    The three remaining men looked at each other fearfully and nodded their concession to
