    Demon Foxes for the Carjacker's Soul

    Previous chapter

    added by The Brilliant Dr. Garfield 7 years ago A BM S

    You walk home smiling. You like your new form. It allows you to punish all the truly evil assholes in the world. Just as a test, you wait until you're walking down a particularly dark street then snap your fingers. There is a flash of purple light and you feel all the attributes of your transformation into an extremely muscular demon anthro black fox happen all at once. Soo, you are in your demon form. Grinning, you snap your fingers again and the changes reverse themselves, as if nothing ever happened at all.

    Exhausted from your fight with the insane football player and your transformations, you open the door to your apartment, enters, close it, lock it behind you, run into your bedroom, collapse on your bed, and fall asleep instantly. You sleep for twelve hours, waking up at 2:30 in the afternoon. You immediately call the place you work, quitting your job as you can just summon money now.

    After taking care of that, you look around your apartment in disgust. It's so dingy and small. You snap your fingers and you're the demon black fox again. Your eyes glow neon green as you focus all your energy on expanding your apartment. The walls rush outward as all your furniture becomes more advanced. Your pathetic thirty inch TV becomes a giant flatscreen. Your dirty couch you got at Goodwill becomes a large leather one. Everything, in fact, becomes more fancy and comfortable. After all the adjustments you've made, you snap your fingers and you're back to normal again. Given how huge your apartment is now, it must take up four or five units. But when you walk outside and look up at your third-story window, it looks as if nothing's happened at all.

    Magic. You gotta love it.

    All too soon, it's 10:00 and you feel a hunger begin to envelope you. Not a hunger for food, but for a soul to consume. You feel your eyes burn green just thinking about it. Not able to resist any longer, you leave your apartment and head into the dingy part of town, looking for an evil soul to eat. Just ten minutes later, you come upon a drunk-looking man trying to break into a car parked in a deserted parking lot. He has a crowbar and just finishes smashing the passenger window when you step up behind him.

    You can feel the evil radiating off of him. With your new intelligence, you know he's going to use this car as a getaway during a bank robbery, where if allowed to go through, fifteen civilians and twenty police offers would be killed. You rub your hands together in anticipation and snap your fingers.

    When the man finally realizes something's standing behind him, he drops to crowbar and turns around to see you, a huge, muscular anthro black fox with glowing green eyes. You're snarling and breathing heavily. He screams and tries to dart to the left, but you grab him and pin him against the car. He screams and bats feebly at your muzzle with his hands, but to no avail.

    You take your paw, tipped with poison-soaked claws, and jam it into his chest cavity. He screams as a jet of green light jets from his mouth. His soul. You eagerly swallow it, obtaining your first soul. Then, suddenly, the man begins to transform. All at once, his whole body is enveloped in a tidal wave of jet-black fur. His face pushes out into canine-like muzzle and his ears grow pointed and move to the top of his head. His hands pop into clumsy claws as his feet extend into paws as well. His whole body bulks up with muscle as a thick, long tail bursts from his rear.

    The man has transformed into an muscular anthro black fox like you, but not nearly as powerful. He looks down at his huge body and yips in terror. You suddenly grab his throat in your huge paw-like hand and he gurgles as you lift him off the ground, feetpaws and tail squirming and kicking in the air. You bring your footpaw down and a crack in the earth opens up, emitting a jet of orange light and a blast of heat. Your tattoos burn with purple light. You suspend the new fox over it and let him drop. He screams as he falls into the chasm of hell and the ground closes up as if there wasn't anything there at all.

    You snap your fingers together and are back to normal in no time. That was... fun. But what should you do now?
