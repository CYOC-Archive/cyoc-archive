There were a few cold snaps in the winter but it was the most the climate had to offer during so-called harsh months. Yes, life was not the most restful but it was more fulfilling than what Jim had lived at college. It had opened his eyes about a lot of things. Moreover, thanks to Mark's third wish, he also had another set of memories now, those of a dryad. Of his life lived as a dryad. These memories were not too invasive, indeed it helped him go through the tasks or challenges characteristic of females but it was still bothersome and even frightening at times. He could recall being raised with a mother and an older sister amidst a group of pine trees, of marvelling in the sweet pine scents and how it was so pleasant, growing up amongst flowers and plants. As a dryad he had learned how to plant all sorts of greens and his skills in cooking were rather decent. Maybe he could start baking again... Well, baking from his twisted memories as a dryad... More disturbing he remembered the triton that had been his mate, and how they'd lived by the coast, where she had learned more, out of necessity, about seaside plants. But Crest, her mate, had succumbed to a shark attack while hunting and she'd gone back to the forest, where she'd seen Conlusious and struck a sort of friendship with him. Then over time it became a mating by convenience.

Conluscious was overjoyed and was positively beaming at showing off his new children. Fertility and prowess in getting large amounts of children were very important to satyrs. Jim called his daughter Seajewel in memory of Triton and the baby satyr was named Swifthoof. Both pleased Conluscious. Jewelweed fed the two new babies and they were then placed into two little cribs. Hopefully she had enough milk for the twins and her stepdaughter.
