    Creating a World Without Men

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago I TG O

    After hours of preparation, Lucy spent even more hours making slight changes to the world. “Well next, we should make women more confident and empowered, so that they can deal with evil men in their lives,” Lucy started. “In fact, let’s make it so that women can breed with other women. No need for men for love.”

    “You’re already going too far, Lucy. You don’t know how much this is going to affect life negatively,” Edwin said.

    “Shut up! I’m tired of hearing from you. I’m tired of men in general. All you and dad have done was hold me back. My ex boyfriends treated me like shit, and it seems all the guys in my life are jerks. You men cause all the violence in the world, all of the war. Do you know what? No more men! That’s it!” Lucy shouted.

    She spent a few more hours inputting more parameters to create her world without men. She shoved Edwin in a different room so she didn’t have to hear about his complaints. When time was come for the Tesseract to activate, she brought Edwin back into the room.

    “It’s starting, I just want to be there to see what happens to you,” Lucy said.

    “What did you do?” Edwin asked.

    “Well, in a world without men, I have decided to give women the abilities that men had, while embracing their feminine side. The world will have a lot more pink, flowers, and frilly dresses, but the women will get the strength to open jars, the ability to have children with other women, and…”

    “What did you do to the men?” Edwin interrupted.

    “Oh, they’ll be fine. The machine doesn’t have the ability to kill. Everyone must still have their senses and consciousness, as if they were human, even if they can’t do anything. So, I turned them all into a variety of feminine things. Dresses, skirts, heels, makeup, purses, but I’ve let some of them turn into women themselves,” Lucy said.

    “Lucy, that’s torture!”

    “Nah, it won’t be. Being an item for a woman to use must be heavenly. Nothing to worry about, except to please your owner. Well, you’ll find out soon enough, unless you turn into a woman yourself.”

    The Tesseract started to glow. Suddenly, a wave of light passed through the room, leaving Edwin with a buzzing feeling.

    “Oh it’s starting! And Edwin, I can see changes already. Your skin looks so…”
