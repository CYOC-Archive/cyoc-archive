    You can hear Footsteps again as you lie down, much more than last time.
    You fearfully look up and see the Couple and a group of People you don't recognize, walk into your Field of View.
    The People with the Couple seem to be Peasants, judging from their Clothing, possibly more Farmers.
    "Well i'll be damned. I guess you were tellin' the Truth." You hear one of them say.
    "And you're saying she really begged you to take her? And even had everything ready to do this?" Another asks, pointing at a Tattoo.
    "She really did! I was just as suprised than you. She even picked all the Tattoos herself. Who i am to stop her?" The Farmer answered.
    "I jas ken't believ them rich Folks sum times." One further in the back says, as he looks at you with condescendance.
    You want to stand up and scream at them, but you can barely even move, much less stand up.
    "Now, now Fellas, let's not be too mean to her. She likes to pretend to be a cow an' so she went all the way an' made herself inta one.
    It's better than all of those Richies who like tis Stuff but still act high an' mighty, right?" The one standing directly to the left of you
    spoke this time. You just want them to leave. You don't care that your chances of getting out of here are higher with more People around, or that one of them seems to be defending you. You'd rather have them leave and take all their staring with them!
    "Wel i gess yer rite abat dat." The one at the back answers to the one on the left,
    his gaze towards you changing from condescendance to mild approval. They stare at you silently for a moment,
    before the one on the right speaks up. "Y'said that she wanted t'be uhm... used, right? But doin' it with an Animal? A'don know..."
    You freeze at his words. What did he mean by use? If he's trying to say what you think he is then...
    "It's not like she's a REAL Cow, right? An' as long as she wants it, then it's probably ok, even if she looks like that."
    The one on the left said, pointing at you. "No un wold wonna be summtin like dat if she din wann id BAD, so leds jus fakkin do id!"
    The one in the back exclaimes, as he starts to lightly push the others into your Direction.
    "Alright, alright, calm down, i'll open the Gate and let you in, but remember that i don't want you hurting my new Cow.
    I don't care HOW rough she wants it, she's the only Cow i have right now. You break it you buy it." The Farmer says,
    as he opens the Gate leading into your Stall.

    What do you do now?
