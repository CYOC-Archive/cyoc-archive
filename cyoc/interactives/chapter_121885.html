    Jane's final moments on land

    After the additional fin incident, the mermaid accepted the inevitable and just let herself be dragged to the ocean. Fighting back was not doing her any favours and she realised there was no turning back as society would just study her. She did not want to have the lifestyle of a celebrity, she wanted to be human again and to be with her family.

    It started to become more embarrassing at the forth mile when a young male teen jumped onto Jane's back and wrapped his legs around her waist. If Jane had any control, she would fight to get him off but the voice made sure to keep her sturdy. Within seconds, her bra was torn away from her leaving her F cup boobs to bounce. Jane looked down as much as she could and she tried to scream but no sound emitted and Jane continued her path to the ocean. Jane's last piece of humanity was taken away just like that and the voice just burst out laughing. The boy was now the proud owner of a giant mermaids bra.

    Once Jane entered the 5th mile of her journey, she could feel something inside her that was all too familiar. The call of the ocean. The main reason why Jane's family lived away from the sea was because the sea beckoned for her and the further away she was, the less she could feel it. Being in her neighbourhood caused no problems with her but now she was close, the sound of the ocean started ringing in her ears. She needed to get to the ocean fast if she wanted the call to cease.

    Apart from the growing crowd. The government were hot on her trail trying to capture Jane but the voice was smarter than her and made sure nothing bad would happen to Jane. She raised a barrier around the mermaid seemed to not have any effect on humans but anything like walls or weaponry do not faze Jane. "I must say, it's a pretty impressive technique created by mermaid magic. It makes me wonder why you wanted to stay human". The voice was making Jane angry. She loved her legs, her home, her life.

    Once Jane neared the coastline at the 10th and final mile, something strange occurred. The barrier that was around Jane grew and grew which slowly pushed the crowd away leaving the beach all to herself. Millions watched as Jane's fins touched the soft sand in the first time in years and her speed increased. She was quickly pushed in the ocean within seconds. Once she entered, she fell to her stomach and her tail automatically propelled her further and further away from land. Out of sight from the human world. Jane could only keep moving forward as the sea became deeper and deeper. Then Jane spotted someone in the distance. With a wide grin on her face, Jane was greeted by none other than Amy, her daughter with a a large golden fish tail which replaced her legs. Around Amy's neck was a necklace holding the biggest pearl Jane's ever seen. "Glad to finally see you in the ocean... mother"
