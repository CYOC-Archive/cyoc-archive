    Dainty Shoes Of Transformation

    added by Elflover 2 days ago AR BM TG O Magic Feet Male to female

    As Chad, now a leprechaun, and excited at the prospect of widely spreading mischief, saw Tom Perkins' feet. He refrained a chuckle. Large, flat, big and hairy things they were! Chad, as a human, had had a secret foot fetish and now it resurfaced en force, with the mind of a leprechaun, a trickster, to boot. Already Chad was forgetting his old life as human. Focused on these feet, Chad scowled. What a shame it was to possess such large feet! And how dirty they were! He then noticed that the other boys likewise had grimy feet and unmanicured toes. Ugh! How could they be so unconcerned with their appearance like that? Well, luckily, Chad thought, he could change that. AND be rewarded for that. Lots, in fact. He thought about his spell, gathered his magic and let the spell loose. At once, all the boys were wearing new shoes. Magical shoes that were going to transform them completely. Tom got silken Chinese slippers. Roy got velvet flat shoes with medieval embroidery. Ralph got the shoes that geishas donned. Arthur received Arabian oriental silk shoes. Billy was offered good-quality leather shoes resembling strongly Mary Janes. All five boys shouted in incomprehension, especially as the shoes were impossible to remove. Chad then revealed himself, jumping upon the bench.
    "Surprise, you fools!" he shouted in glee. "You love treating women so wrongly, then you can become female. And dainty women you shall be! Any fae shall treasure you like a precious thing. And you'll make me rich!"
    He sneered and laughed as the shoes tightened, spreading the spell of transformation up and up the helpless idiots. Tom became a Chinese concubine. Roy a medieval fine dainty lady. Ralph became a geisha. Arthur was transformed into a belly-dancer. Billy regressed and was turned into a little girl with dark hair, bangs and clothes looking like those of a darling Victorian child. All with small dainty and darling feet, to ravish any feet connoisseur. To ravish any fae creature desiring a human mate. Or an adopted daughter, in the case of Billy. With a joyous clap of his hands, Chad transported them to the Troll Market, with each terrified former jock in a giant bird cage. Chad lit up his pipe and waited for customers, sure that these fools would make him rich, and fast at that. After all, they would bring much joy to their new husband, wife or parent. Much more joy than if they had remained human.
