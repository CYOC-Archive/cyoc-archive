    Ty walked back into the school. He felt good for helping Stan he wanted to do it again. He turned the corner and was presented with his next target. His fellow classmate Nate was currently being beat up by two of the school bullies, Chuck and Justin. Nate was always nice to Ty, but was an outcast among most of the other students due to his being overweight and general nerdy demeanor. Still, he wanted to help, but wanted to come up with something special.

    Ty's mind raced as he thought how to help. Justin grabbed Nate's book bag and tossed it down the hall. The zipper burst open and several items fell out, including a couple comic books. The one on top is what inspired Ty: The Incredible Hulk.

    He walked over to the two bullies with a smirk on his face. Chuck and Justin looked up as Ty approached.

    "What do you want, faggot?" taunted Chuck.

    "You two shouldn't do that. You wouldn't like him when he's angry." Ty willed Nate to start hulking out.

    "What are talking about?" replied Justin.

    Nate began to groan as the transformation started. Ty watched with glee as Nate's entire body began to grow, slowly gaining a green tint. He looked down and watched as his sneakers tore away at the seams revealing large, green feet. Ty followed the transformation upwards as Nate's legs grew, his pants stretching and seams popping. His chest and arms began to grow and swell to gigantic proportions. His t-shirt tore down the middle as his shoulders and chest grew broader.

    Chuck and Justin slowly backed away, terrified at what was happening. They couldn’t take their eyes off what was happening though. Ty stood there as well, grinning from ear to ear. Nate slowly stood up, newly transformed into ten feet of super-muscular Hulk. He looked over himself, in awe of his transformation. He then took one look at Chuck and shoved him. Chuck went flying down the hallway, landed on the tile floor and slid several more feet.

    Justin started to cry as he backed into a bank of lockers. Hulk Nate turned and let out a deep, gravelly laugh.

    “Hulk smash!” he cried as he punched his huge green fist into the locker next to Justin. Justin let out a loud whimper. Both Nate and Ty looked down and watched as Justin’s crotch area became darker and wetter. Hulk Nate backed away, letting Justin run away, thoroughly humiliated.

    Nate smiled as he felt himself turning back into his normal self. In a few moments, he was back to his regular size, although much more lean and muscular. He held up his stretched out, tattered jeans in one hand as he picked up his torn up shirt and ran down the hall to where his book bag was. He leaned down and picked that up quickly. He had to get out of there soon, everyone else was going to be back from recess soon. He threw his backpack over his shoulder and ran to his locker.
