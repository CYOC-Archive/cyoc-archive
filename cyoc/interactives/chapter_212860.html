N-no, I mean… Naaat maaai nammm…”

Despite Martin being easily a foot and a half taller than the oldest of the girls, they all looked at him reassuringly, like big sisters trying to settle a frightened child.

    “Naa-nawame’pe, little one. The sun is making you go totally min’owo, heat-crazy, yes?” the oldest girl cooed, and Martin already felt a bit better hearing her gentle words. The girl took Martin’s hand in hers and gentle pulled him toward the water’s edge. “A little water will make it all better, Maa’ti…”

    “Maa’ti…? T-that’s not…” Martin muttered under his breath, suddenly realizing that he was just as naked as everyone else around him.     Martin and the girls reached the edge of the water, and he experimentally dipped a toe into the cool, flowing water. He watched with fascination as the pale color of his foot was washed away with the current, revealing beautiful, red-brown skin beneath. The color of rich earth, the color of life. A pleasant tingle spread throughout his foot as he took a full step into the river, watching with fascination as his big, masculine foot slimmed and shrunk to a petite size, while the brown skin continued to spread upward toward his ankle.

    “What’s the matter, Maa’ti? Afraid a piranha will bite you?” one of the girls giggled, and Martin let loose a high, musical giggle of his own. How absurd an idea, piranha in the village water hole?

    Feeling a surge of confidence, Martin waded deeper into the water, the water coming up to his knees in no time. With every step deeper, more and more of his skin was dyed brown, while his hairy legs became delicate, thin, and completely smooth. He felt a moment of apprehension when the water was nearly lapping at his waist, as if he was about to make a decision that would change him forever. But the gentle, pleasant tingle that affected every place the water touched made him want more, to see what it would do to him next.

    With a deep breath, Martin walked deeper, letting the cool water rise up to above his waist, and the gentle tingle suddenly turned intense, so powerful that it nearly knocked him off his feet. He felt an odd yet strangely pleasant shifting under the water, like a hundred hands gently massaging his body into place, taking what had been broken and making it right again. He felt a gentle push that seemed to go deeper and deeper into his body, nudging parts around to sculpt them into the proper form.

    In a moment of clarity, Martin began to look down, to try to peer below the blue water of the river to understand what was happened, but in an instant a wave of water knocked him off his feet, sinking him below the surface where he floated suspended in an endless sea of blue…
