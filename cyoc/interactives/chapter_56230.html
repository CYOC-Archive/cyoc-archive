    Woof! You're Attached to A Leash, and Your New Master is...

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Rory Stubbs 13 years ago A BM S

    You fall into a wall at the end of your fall, but you didn't change into anything as yet. You get up, feeling confused and lost. Then you colaspe to the floor unconscious, and stay their for
    a while. Then, a man comes along with a collar and a leash in his hand. He spots you lying on the floor and picks you up. You don't even dare open your eyes, then he puts you on a chair and
    puts a collar on you. You didn't see it coming, did you? Then he puts the leash on the collar peg, and says "You, my friend. You are now a dog." You decide not to wake up, but you begin to
    change into a dog. You don't know which type of dog as yet, though. First, while keeping your eyes shut, you take off your shirt with buttons down the centre. He then strips you down to the
    lowest level possible. You're now belittled, sad, broken, dejected, pitful or weak, just naked. Then the real changes begin. Your hands suddenly become all stiff. You try to clinch your
    fist, but you realize you can't. It's just too impossible. Then you feel another wave of pain as your face pulls away from your face. Your feet are also becoming your paws. You don't know
    what the hell is going on. Your teeth become all sharp, and you feel the pain down your backside as a tail bursts out. white fur begins to sprout all around you, and you fall to stand on all
    fours. ~What on earth is wrong with me?~ You think. You then try to scream, but all you can do was bark. It was becoming a bit too much for you. Your eyesight also blindens you so you can
    only see in black and white. Then, you can see that you have prints on your pawws. Then it stops. You're now a Bull Terrier. And your master is...

    What do you do now?
