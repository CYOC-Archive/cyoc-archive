    Without any further delay, you pull back and stretch out the elastic string as you raise the plastic nose over your head, ready to put it on. You soon let go of the string once the nose sits firmly on your own. Something immediately feels different about your face with this plastic appendage on. It almost feels as if the plastic was throbbing with life, morphing into actual flesh and mending with your own skin.

    But you, for some reason or another, do not feel alarmed by this. The sensation actually feels invigorating. You get a notion that you're about to experience something unique and fantastic. This becomes confirmed when you feel little tiny prickling sensations suffice your nose and mouth. If you didn't know any better, you would swear it was fur growing in.

    Curious to have a look, you stand and walk over to an old standup mirror in the corner of the basement. In the reflection you can now see that you now have a slight muzzle covered in striped orange and plain white fur with your upper lip having already formed the classic feline cleft. You lift your lip and notice that your teeth are turning into sharp fanged feline teeth. You are very engaged by these changes that you let a pleasing mew slip out.

    It seems that the act of feline expression has increased the changes because as soon as you mewed, you feel a wave travel down your spine, right to the very tip of the tail bone. Soon your tail bone starts lengthening and pushing against the skin, stretching it outward. A very small formation peaks its way from underneath your shirt and pants. Eyeing it intently, you practice flicking the tail back and forth.

    This causes the tail to elongnate more, fur spreading over it so it's not just fleshy mass of appendage. You mew in approval again, this time sending more fur to spread over your face and up your eyes which quickly begin forming pointed tips and relocating to the top of your head. The fur then travels down your neck while lip whiskers sprout out of your muzzle. Enjoying the sight, you flare your whiskers and then slide your tongue along your new feline teeth.

    You can now feel your tongue changing too, your tastebuds reformulating into bristles that all felines sport on their tongue, their one grooming tool. With a desire to try your new "brush", you bring a hand close to your mouth and lick it. As you do, white fur starts to grow and spread along the hand. You then flip your hands over and look when you swear you feel a tickling sensation on your palms. There are pink pawpads forming on your palms and fingertips. Totally amazed by the extent of the changes, you wonder what more you can expect to experience.
