    "I heard that you have a transformation fetish," I said to her. She immediately stopped walking, turning to stare at me with a look of very poorly disguised alarm.

    "Where in the world did you hear that?" She was trying to play it off like she didn't know what in the world I was talking about, but she was a bad actress. Admittedly, it didn't hurt that I knew the truth: that she now was a TF freaker, even if she hadn't been a minute before.

    I shrugged. "Does it really matter? It's okay... your secret's safe with me," I told her, inwardly laughing.

    "James, you HAVE to keep that quiet!" she said, her voice urgent. She stopped walking, turning to me and staring me down with a mix of concern, fear, and a mild tone of threat. "Yes... it's true. I don't know how you found out, but yes... I like... that. But I CAN'T afford to have ANYONE finding out."

    "So, how did you get into it?" I asked, slightly amazed that the most ludicrous wish I could have imagined had come true. I was VERY curious to see how the universe had altered her to fulfill my wish.

    "Let's go back to my place, and I'll tell you everything," she sighed. "I just don't want anyone overhearing. This isn't the sort of thing I want getting around town."

    I nodded in agreement, and she picked up her pace. We navigated the streets until we got to her brownstone townhouse and went inside. She locked the door and pulled the blinds down, clearly somewhat paranoid even in her own home.

    Cathy sat on her coach and sighed heavily. I could see the slightest blush on embarassment on her face and she rubbed her forehead. "Look, some people, well... just like weird stuff, right?"

    "Hey, hey, you don't have to tell me." I offered, seeing her stumble for words. "I like it too."

    "Really?!" She brightened. "You do?"

    I wanted to say 'duh, that's why I wished it' but instead, casually: "Yeah. Found some stuff online when I was growing up. And... liked it. You know?"

    "Yeah." She said sweetly. "Exactly. So... what's your thing?"

    "A little bit of it all." I admitted, grinning. "You?"

    "Yeah, more or less the same." She blushed again. "But I *love* size."

    "Oh yeah?" I laughed. "Me too." My fingers played with my new ring in anticipation. "I heard you're aware of any changes to yourself."

    "Well... yeah." She said sarcastically. "It's my thing, so I'm always looking, right? Like, hoping something will happen to me." She giggled. "But we both know it can't."

    "I heard you believe what I say." I suggested.

    Cathy nodded, growing bored again. "Yeah. You're a dork, but you're the most honest kid I know."

    "See this ring?" I pointed to my finger. "It can grant wishes! In fact, I can change you any way you want."

    Cathy blinked and leaned in closer. "No way! You're joking right? I mean..." She looked at the ring and seemed to just... know. "Holy shit!" She gasped, leaning back. "You're on the level, aren't you, James?"

    "Like always." I grinned. "Want a demonstration?"

    "Shit!" Cathy bounced on the couch. "Only hell yeah!"

    "Okay..." I whispered. "I wish you were in sexy lingerie."

    And like the magic it was, Cathy and I stared in shock as her freshly restored clothing morphed to lacy back lingerie. Her eyes went wide with shock.

    "Oh... my... God." She whispered slowly, carefully rubbing her lacy garments to see if they were real, which of course they were.

    She went silent for a moment, blushed deeper, and then looked at me seriously. "We... we keep this between us, right?"

    "Yes." I swore honestly.

    "Then..." She blushed deeper. "Can we have some fun... do... do you think? I mean, if that isn't too... weird."

    "No, no!" I said quickly. "Why do you think I was following you?"

    "Ohhh..." She winked. "Okay. Well, that makes me feel better then. Two... TF fans... just goofing around with a magic wish ring."

    She got more comfortable, and struck a sexy pose on her couch. "So... what are you waiting for? Make a wish already, James."
