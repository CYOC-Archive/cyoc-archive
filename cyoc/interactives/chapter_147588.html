    "Oh shut it Megan." Alan snapped back before pouring himself some orange juice. "So, you guys said you were gonna make a magic limit right?" He asked.

    Steve looked at Janet and then back at the kids.
    "Yes, your mother and I talked, and we're thinking 3 times a day. We thought once a day was too little and 5 times a day was too much." He sipped his coffee and looked back at them. "Do either of you have any objections?" He asked.

    "Yeah!" Alan agreed. "With me it's easy, you can just count in my book. You can't tell with Megan's!"

    "And if we go over the limit?" Megan asked.

    "If you go over the limit, then we decide the punishment." Steve answered. "We'll probably make it relate to whatever you did for irony sake, but don't count on it."

    "Oh!" Janet added. "Also, don't do anything too crazy alright? You have a lot of power with those things. You can have fun but emphasis on fun, not horror. I don't want to walk out into the apocalypse or something. Also, don't mess with us if you know what's good for you." She warned.

Megan had to leave first so she ran out to catch the early bus. Half an hour later Alan left for his bus. Both, of course, took their respective artifacts with them, as they were eager to enjoy that school day.

    "How long do you think it'll be before they break the rules?" Steve snakily asked.

    "I'll give them a week, tops." Janet laughed. "Now, for our plans today?" She asked.

    Steve just looked at her and smiled.
