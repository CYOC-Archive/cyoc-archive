    Rachel in Wonderland: Curiouser and Curiouser

    added A year ago S

    Rachel found a small glass bottle of clear liquid with a lid and a tag inside the package and takes it out. She notices something written on the tag and reads it out loud.
    "Drink Me." Rachel said, then smiles.
    "Curiouser and curiouser." Alice's size changes were always her favorite parts of the story. Rachel had gone this far, she might as well go all the way.
    "I guess it's appropriate considering who I'm dressed as. I wonder if it can really shrink me. Hmm, I probably shoudn't, but when else will I get a chance like this. And I am curious what it might taste like." Rachel is about to remove the lid but then stops herself and decides to make another qoute.
    "Mmm, better look first. For if one shoud drink from a bottle marked poison, it's almost certain to disagree with one sooner or later." Rachel lets out a small laugh as she takes the lid off and lifts the bottle's head to her lips.
    "Just giving myself some last minute good advice. Here goes nothing" Rachel takes a small sip form the bottle. The liquid inside tasted very delicious to Rachel, even if she couldn't make out the flavor, and decided to throw caution to the wind and drink the rest of the contents. Giving a sigh of satisfaction, she placed the now empty bottle on her dresser.
    "Well that was refreshing, but I was kinda hoping something would happen to me if I drank it. I really wanted to be smaller. Whatever, still was fun though." Rachel was about to walk towards the mirror when suddenly she felt her belly start to shake slightly. The vibrations spread to the rest of her body and the start to intensify, making Rachel very uneasy (and unintentionally turned on). Then after about a minute, the vibrations began subsided and eventually stop.
    "Wow, what a rush. So that's what being a dildo feels like." But before Rachel could fully recover from that experience, she suddenly began to feel another sensation slowly coming over her body. Rachel felt as if she was being pulled inwards and every inch of her getting constricted. Rachel's vision blurred slightly so she started looking around her room only to find everthing around her to be both farther away and bigger than usual. As Rachel kept starring, she realize the world around her was steadily growing in size. Rachel then looked at the floor and found it slowly getting closer to her by the second.
    "It's real." Rachel says to herself in disbelief.
    "I just drank from the bottle like in the story, and now I'm actually shrinking in size. I really have become Alice in Wonderland now." Rachel was both nervous and excited at the same time. Rachel didn't really think the drink would actually work, even though she wanted it to, but now was very happy to be reliving one of Alice's misadventures. Rachel looked over her dresser and saw the infamous bottle and decided to carry it as she got smaller, just like in the disney film. Rachel grabs it just before she becomes too small to see the top of dresser. Before shrinking, the bottle was the size of a mini coke can. Now to Rachel, it was the size of a liter bottle of coke and getting bigger and heavier by the second. Rachel looked around to see all the furniture expanding and towering over her diminishing self. Eventually, the Drink Me bottle became too heavy to carry and Rachel had to put it down. Rachel then watches the bottle grow to her height and then rise above her. Finally the pulling/constricting sensation subsides and Rachel stops shrinking, leaving her at about 1 inch tall. Rachel stood for several seconds, amazed that she had actually been shrunk down to such a small stature. Everything around her now was so massive yet still recognizable.
    "Oh my goodness. I am so small. Everything's so detailed from down here." Rachel says looking at the floor board as well as the now huge bottle that caused her condition. Rachel suspects that she's small enough to enter the bottle's opening and fall inside, if she wanted to of course. Rachel then thinks about her next move out loud.
    "Well, since I'm officially Alice at this point, I might as well start exploring this Wonderland I've gotten myself into."
