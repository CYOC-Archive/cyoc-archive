    Charles needs a new pair of shoes

    added by Drakkenfyre 6 years ago I

    About an hour later, Cory the shoe heard the locker door rattle. It was Charles. He didn't looked pissed this time, he looked nervous. He stole glances left and right, and over his shoulder. He breathed deeply, and set his bag down and opened it in front of the locker.

    "Charlie needs a new pair of shoes, and Cory's will do nicely. He may be a twerp, but he's got big feet, like me. Gym's coming up next hour, so I'll be able to break these babies in then," he said as he grabbed Cory and his shoe mate. He shoved them in his bag, and closed the locker. He sprinted a few feet, and then began walking casually.

    Cory groaned as he realized his new cramp quarters were shared with Charles' jockstrap, socks, gym shorts and gym shirt. He hoped after gym, he'd be able to change back.

    Charles was pretty big, and his shoes took a lot of punishment as he ran, jumped, and kicked the soccer ball during gym. Back at his gym locker, Charles stripped, stowed his gear, and showered. He then put on his school clothes, but instead of putting on his old sneakers. He put on Cory again, along with the other shoe. Seems until now, Charles always wore the same shoes for gym, as for school. He knew his mother would ask if he suddenly had two pairs of shoes. She didn't have the money for him to enjoy that extravagance. So Charles headed back to the locker in the hallway, and left his old sneakers for Cory. Old shoes for new, thought Cory unhappily.

    He didn't realize Charles had it so bad, until Charles missed the bus- on purpose, so as to save the fare- and walked the 30 blocks home. His feet were hot and sweaty, and Cory felt so sore, he wondered if he'd have been better off letting Charles pound him earlier.

    Charles went straight to his room, and studied for a few hours. He ate an unappetizing leftover baked pasta that his mother left in the fridge. She worked nights. He was having really problems with his maths, and fell asleep with his new shoes on.

    It was six in the morning, when Charles' mother called, "Time to wake up, Chucky!"

    Chucky? thought Cory with a giggle. He couldn't believe that Charles was still wearing him. Charles changed his shirt, but kept his underwear, jeans, socks and shoes on. Then he headed for school. He didn't walk all the way. About halfway there, he bummed a ride with Mickey.

    Cory was getting worried, he had never gone for more than 24 hours as an inanimate object before. He was trying to remember what the exact warnings were in the Chronivac tutorial about overstaying transformation time limits.
