    "Its my daughter Sarah, she keeps going into the woods for long periods of time," Christine said with a sigh. "Its not that unusual, I mean she's always been a bit adventurous, but she's been staying out for hours and hours, even missing meals on several occasions. When she does get home she doesn't even seem hungry."

    "Weird," you said. "Have you ever tried asking her what she's been doing in the forest?"

    "Yeah, but she's not given me any sort of straight answer," Christine said with a shake of her head. "The police haven't done anything because they don't see what the problem is. I thought maybe you'd be able to shed light on it."

    "Well, if she's been vanishing into the forest and coming back that could be a lot of things," you said and looked thoughtful for a moment. "What else can you tell me, has her behavior changed any since this happened?"

    "Well, she's dumped her boyfriend, but he's not exactly a good kid so no real harm there," Christine said with a shrug. "But she's been more distant to me lately, and yesterday I started blacking out on occasion and I'd..."

    She paused a moment and looked at you as she tried to decide how to put this into words. "What is it? You know you can tell me whatever it is, right?"

    "I found myself naked and laying in my backyard," she said and shook her head a little. "Sarah wasn't anywhere to be found and she returned later. I don't really know what might've happened."

    You paused and thought for a moment, there was a few things that could explain that kind of thing. You thought about it for a moment before nodding and looked at her. "Alright, I'll take the case."

    "Thank you, if you could come tomorrow that would be great," she said and you nodded in agreement.
