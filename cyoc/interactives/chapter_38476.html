    "Ke... Kent?" Peter tried to call his friend, but he knew that his voice was too low. He left from under the sofa. When he looked at where he and his friend were some seconds ago, he discovered that Kent had dissapeared!

    "Hahahaha..." someone laughing sounded behind Peter. He turned and saw the giant mouse again.

    "Aaaahhhh!" Peter screamed. "Please don't hurt me!"

    "Peter, calm down! It's me... Ty!" the mouse said laughing.

    "WHAT!?" Peter stopped screaming. "Ty... Is that really you!?"

    "Yes, sorry for scaring you, cousin. I only pretended to play a joke on you."

    "And... and what about the cat?" Peter wondered.

    "Don't you recognize me, Peter?" the cat appeared behind Peter. That voice sounded familiar...

    "Kent!" Peter said.

    The talking cat and mouse started to talk.

    "I was on my room when I saw you coming in. Kent shrank you, then I had got an idea. I could turn into a mouse and scare you." Ty said. It was incredible to see a mouse talking and laughing in front of Peter.
