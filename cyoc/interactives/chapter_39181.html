    Blowing up mother...

    added by Crystal 14 years ago I S

    "Well, as long as the candy is safe, I would like to try one of them." You heard your mother say.

    "Well, I know the ones with numbers on them are based on that age. They also seem to relate to the shape and color, since a Pink candy turned me into a girl. Do you want to try one where we can kind of guess what might happen, or one that's an unknown?"

    "Hmm, since its just for the night, I think I'll go for an unknown."

    With that settled, you handed her one of the white chocolate marshmallow pumpkins with a 17 on it.

    "Here, maybe we can go out as sisters for a night..."

    As she chewed the pumpkin, you noticed that she was starting to swell a bit, and she was turning orange as well.

    "Wow, that was tingly... Hey, whats going on now?"

    You gawk as you watch your mother just continue to inflate, and become a deep orange. Near the end of her transformation, a green stem popped out of her head, and her shape changed from a fat oval, into a distinctly pumpkin like shape.

    "Woah, she turned into a pumpkin..."

    When you touch her, however, you realize that she is very light, and has a hole underneath her.

    "That's odd..."

    When you stick your head into the hole, all of a sudden you can hear her talking to you, inside your head. She's freaking out!

    You calm her down, and explain what you think happened. Then you get a wicked grin on your face...

    What do you do now?
