    Across from the High school was the elementary school where a group of 4th graders had the same dream, to
    become teachers when they grew up.

    The leader of the group was a cute blonde girl named Katie. age 11. Katie was the most determined and straight
    forward girl of the group and was always organized with every thing.

    Katie's best friend Mallory was a cute red head with a short hair cut.age 10. Mallory was the most serious of
    the group and had a small problem with her temper, her parents had to take her to anger management when she
    was only five.

    The third girl was Julie, a blonde like Katie but always wore her hair in a pony tail. age 11. She was the
    second oldest of the group and would be in charge when Katie wasn't around. She was the calm one of the group
    and loved to play doctor.

    The fourth girl was Ashley, a strawberry blonde. age 10. Ashley is American but adopted, her adopted parents
    are Mexican so she grew up speaking English and Spanish.

    The last girl of the group is Diana, a brunette. age 9. Diana is the peppy one of the group and loves to hear
    her self talk, she is the younger sister of Katie.

    It was lunch time at Angel Elementary school and the Group was eating out side at the picnic tables. "Hey did
    you hear?" Katie said. "Five teachers at the High School were just fired for drug use". " hmph, serves them
    right for being so stupid" Mallory said. "If I was a teacher their I would never do something so stupid!".
    "Keep calm Mallory". Julie said. "You don't want to spaz out again". "Yeah Yeah Yeah". said Diana. "like this
    one time when you-"she began. "Quiet Diana" interrupted Ashley.

    After school the girls were walking to Julie's house when suddenly an old lady walked in front of them. "Do
    you mind old lady, were walking here!" Malory said. "Don't be rude Malory." said Katie. "It's alright" the old
    lady said. "Do you want something?" asked Katie. "Yes" said the old lady. "but you must wait till I am
    finished talking before you jump to any conclusions. First, I know about how much you girls wish to be
    teachers when you all grow up, well what if I told you that you didn't have to wait that long. You see, I am a
    witch and I have powers to change people. I can make the five of you young ladies into teachers." With that
    she pulled out five small capsules with a strange glow. " Inside each of these pills is the talent of four
    skilled teachers and one nurse ( she looked over at Julie as she said this knowing her love for helping
    people) you will age to their ages and know exactly how to run the classes you get.

    Later the Group met back at Julies tree house in her back yard. " Should we believe what that old lady
    said?"asked Diana. " Diana, if you want to be a member of the Group, you have to be ready to take risk". said
    Ashley. " Lets each take a pill and go back to our houses to take them, then if it works we will apply for
    jobs tomorrow."Said Katie. The girls each took a pill. Katie got the one Labeled English, Ashley got the one
    labeled Spanish, Malory got the one labeled Math, Diana got the one labeled Debate, and Julie got the one
    labeled nurse. The Old lady told the girls that clothing would appear on them once they stopped changing.
    "Okay". Said Katie. " see you guys in about 45 minutes." and with that each of the girls went back to their
    homes to take their pills.
