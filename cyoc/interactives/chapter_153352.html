    Allison looks around and finds a bunch more white pedal plants,another way looks clear and the last is a cabin,white plants surround it,Allison runs to it but trips and notices a white plant 2 ft from her,she slowly backs away but her bottom makes contact with one behind her and it wraps her and tapes on her,"Oh no no no nooooo!"

    She rubs her eyes like a woke toddler,then notices a diaper next to her,what should be next? She could nap more or maybe make that first wish,but she is also soggy.....
