    “Hello there, please could you help me, I’m looking for a Dr Jenkins…”

    Jen instantly woke from her lustful trance upon hearing the familiar voice. She looked up to see, to her absolute horror; that her wife Abby was now talking to one of the Kennel’s employees. To make matters worse, their teenage twins Liam and Melissa were also in tow.

    Seeing them all here made Jen sick with worry. If this place was anything like the Stables then his family probably wouldn’t be leaving on two legs. She growled and barked urgently, desperately trying to tell them that something was wrong.

    All three of her family members looked over at her, but all they saw was a troublesome Rottweiler bitch, probably unused to her surroundings. To Jen’s dismay, her wife and kids dutifully followed as the Kennel worker led them to Mrs Adams.

    “Um… hello miss, my name’s Abby. I was looking for my husband Doctor Jenkins? I was told that he had gone from his work, and the Stables suggested that I look for him here. I wanted to tell the police but they insisted that my husband’s work was too sensitive. Risk of being leaked or something? Please can you help?”

    Mrs Adams paused in mock respect as Mrs Jenkins started to get emotional.

    “Hmmm, Jenkins…” Mrs Adams pretended to think whilst secretly loving the irony that Abby’s husband was mere feet away from her. “Ah yes, I know. Charming dog… er, man.” Mrs Adams chuckled nervously at her slip up, although, to her relief, her three visitors didn’t seem to notice.

    Mrs Adams resumed after clearing her throat. “I believe that your husband has visited us in the past few days. Tell you what, we can run through the security footage in my office. Don’t worry Mrs Jenkins; we’ll get to the bottom of this.”

    “Oh thank you for your help!” Abby replied hopefully, “come on you two” she said as she beckoned Abby and Melissa to follow her.

    “Ah, ah,” Mrs Adams said holding out her hand, “we have a lot of confidential information stored in this building. They’ll have to wait out here I’m afraid. Don’t worry, we shouldn’t take too long. In the meantime they can enjoy looking at the dogs, Doesn’t that sound fun!?”

    The two teenage twins groaned in unison, unable to think of anything lamer. Mrs Adams smiled to herself, suspecting that their outlook would soon be very different.

    “Now you two behave” Abby said firmly to her notoriously troublesome children. After that she allowed herself to be ushered into the back of the building. Once she was out of sight Mrs Adams followed, but not before she looked back at Jen with a sinister expression.

    Jen whined and scratched against her cage, desperate to convince the evil woman to show some compassion. But Mrs Adams didn’t seem to flinch as she smiled, before following Abby through the door.

    Jen groaned in despair as she suspected that her lovely wife was doomed.

    However, Jen’s sorrow only deepened when she noticed her two children acting strangely. Both were closely investigating the various dogs as they amused themselves in their cages. Soon Liam started sniffing the air, while Melissa’s hand slowly slid between her legs.

    Jen whined loudly, making a couple of Kennel attendants chuckle. She couldn’t let her family be doomed, but how could she stop them? For the moment all she could do was whine and watch on in terror whilst fearing the worst for her family…
