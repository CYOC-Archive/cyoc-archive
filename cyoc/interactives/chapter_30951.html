    "Okay Yukiko! I wish that my brother would shrink until he was tiny."

    Naomi waved the wand at Jiro, and the sleeping ten year old slowly began to shrink pajamas and all.

    Naomi couldn't believe her eyes. The magic was working!

    She watched as the bulge of Jiro's feet under his pokemon sheets slid up towards his body as his smaller head slid down off of his comparatively larger pillow. In just a minute he went from four feet to two feet tall. Soon the boy was nothing more than a dwindling lump under his sheets.

    When the lump became doll-size, it started to move around. Jiro must have just woken up.

    "Hey, what's going on?" said a tiny, muffled voice from under the sheets. Naomi giggled to herself as she watched the lump of her tiny little brother crawl up towards his pillow. When he emerged from under the sheets, she could see that he was only about 4 inches tall now. He stood up and stared wide-eyed at his seemingly gigantic bed and enormous room. He was as small as one of his own action figures now.

    Naomi smiled and walked up to her brother's bed.

    "Good morning 'little' brother," she said as she towered above him, seemingly a giant compared to the shrunken boy.

    "N-N-Naomi!" he squeaked, sounding almost too shocked to speak. "W-What's going on? What happened to me? I'm..." he looked down at his little body "...tiny!"

    "'What's going on?' you say" she replied. "Why, I'm just getting a little payback now that I have the power to get it." She twiddled the wand in her fingers. "You've always been a little pest to me. Now, you're pest-sized."

    "You did this to me?" he asked.

    "I did." she said. "And, I can do so much more now."

    He looked terribly frightened. "W-What are you going to do to me now?"

    She thought a moment as she stared down at her trembling shrunken brother. Just what was she going to do now?
