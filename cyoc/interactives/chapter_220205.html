    It's Zack

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy 2 months ago A

    Jeff is shocked to see that the person who shot him is none other than his best friend Zack, the 16 year-old looks proudly into Jeff’s eyes, unaware that he just killed his friend.

    “Man… I wonder if I could win a prize for this majestic creature,” Zack says to himself, “now I need someone to help me with this huge creature. I have no idea how to gut it and I am unable to carry it out of here by myself!”

    Jeff is horrified, “GUT ME!? DUDE, ZACK, IT’S ME JEFF! D-DON’T DO THIS,” he tries to yell, but of course Zack can’t hear him.

    Zack has his phone out and is calling the ranger and his dad, “hey dad, hey mister Huff, I am calling you both to announce that I have shot the most amazing Buck ever… it is a 15 pointer! What? No! I am not bullshitting you… here, let me send the pictures I have taken!”

    There is long silence as Zack sends the pictures he had taken of Jeff to the ranger and his dad, he then hears Zack chuckle, “hehe… told ya,” he says, “well… this is my first kill ever! I honestly have no idea how to gut it… and I am unable to get it out of here by myself! Okay… yeah awesome… I’ll wait here for you two to arrive! Yeah… see you soon!”

    Zack ends the call and grins down at the dead animal, “man… I just hope we have enough room on the wall for your massive head,” he says, “man… I am so over the moon and proud of myself! I can’t wait and hope the meat will taste good!”

    “WHAT THE FUCK,” Jeff shouts, “NO! NONONONO! YOU ARE NOT GONNA EAT ME!”

    It takes a few minutes, but Jeff soon hears rustling and other voices coming closer, “oh… my… god! Well done, kiddo,” he hears a male voice say.

    “Yeah! Great job, Zack,” a other male says, Jeff assumes that it’s Mr. Huff and Zack’s dad who have arrived.

    “Ok, so we cut around here, slit right up here along the belly up to here. Now we just reach in, cut here, cut here... And see, look at that, we can just pull all the inners out just like that," Zack’s dad says. Jeff mentally screams as he feels the man cut open his body and taking out all of his inners, Jeff is in disbelief and shock.

    “Oh, wow,” Zack exclaims, “that is so easy”.

    “Yeah, it is,” John, Zack’s dad says, he then turns to look at his son, pride on his face, “I am so proud of you, my boy! Well done!”

    “I’ll get the truck,” Mr. Huff says.

    Jeff soon finds himself on the back of a pick up truck and being driven out of the forest, then Zack and his dad load the “Deer” in onto their own truck and drive off to their hunting Lodge.

    Zack and his dad park the car and soon Jeff feels himself been pulled off of the back of the truck, “now, we need to hang the hide up on the rafters in the garage to dry for a few days,” John says.

    “Okay, dad,” Zack replies.

    Soon Jeff finds himself swaying and dangling in the garage, ‘I can’t believe it,’ the teen thinks to himself, ‘am I doomed to forever be stuck as a dead deer?’
