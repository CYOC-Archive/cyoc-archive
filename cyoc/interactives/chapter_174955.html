    Mark was satisfied with his pet Lucky, so he added: “Whenever I say “Reset to dog”, Lucky will be set to exactly how he is right now, beside he will be able to move freely.”
    “What do you mean with reset, Mark?” Lucky asked confused.
    Mark, wanting to see what Aidan would think of his new role, just said: “Lucky, reset to start. But keep the suit as it is.”
    Aidan shook his head when somewhere a switch was turned inside his mind. “What…? He started, only to be assaulted by the smell of his unwashed underwear and reeking socks. Not knowing where it came from, Aidan tried to wave in front of his nose, only he couldn´t move his hands. Looking down, Aidan was shocked by the dog-suit he was wearing.
    “What the hell? Mark, what did you do to me?” he asked angry.
    “So, he doesn´t remember the changes, good.” Mark thought, before answering: “Well, I made you my pet dog. Don´t worry, it´s only a suit, not your real skin. Maybe I will do that later if I like my dog more than you.” Then he laughed when Aidan protested against him.
    “GET THIS SUIT OFF OF ME, MARK. IT REEKS LIKE HELL.” Aidan screamed, and Mark only laughed more.
    “That´s only to you, because the hood was your underwear, while the locking collar was your socks. Maybe I increased the smell on them a bit, but only you, Aidan, can smell it. Not even Lucky can.” Mark explained between his laughing.
    “WHO IS LUCKY? AND GET THIS OFF.” Aidan screamed again.
    “Lucky is your new name as my dog. And as your screams are annoying right now, Aidan, reset to dog.”
    Aidan blinked, and suddenly, he was Lucky again. Not knowing what happened the last few minutes. So, he just enjoyed that Mark was petting him again, not knowing why Mark was laughing so much.
