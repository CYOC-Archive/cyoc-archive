    Mom is in control now

    Nancy had walked into her son's room to ask him if he needed anything from the store. She looked around and didn't see any sign of him. That's when she looked down and saw it. It was a doll that looked exactly like Jake! She picked it up and jokingly pulled the cord on it's back. "-lease help me It's me Jake! I'm the doll! I tu-" the doll said. She dropped it. She was shocked but then quickly realized that that was no hallucination, it was Jake. She could feel it.

    "Jake? Jake is that really you?"" She said as she picked up the doll. Nancy pulled the cord again and the doll spoke. "Yes! Oh thank you it's such a relief for you to know what happened to-" the cord the finished. Nancy pulled it again. "Mom the camera changed me! If you take someone's picture-" the cord ended again.

    She figured out what happened, or so she thought. "I get it sweetie. You took a picture of something and it changed you into that thing right? Well don't worry, I'll let you take a picture of me." She said with a decisive tone.

    Jake was internally screaming that she got it all backwards but she didn't pull the cord after that. She put the camera on the ground, faced it towards herself, and used Jake's plastic hand to put the photo on a timer. She held her son/doll as Jake screamed. They were both about to change.
