    Transform or Dare?

    The utter destruction of the Smith family

    That night Jennifer woke up a the strangest feeling. She was sweating. The whole house and world begun to breath like balloon that was being filled and then emptied. Jennifer saw herself growing older. The belly disappearing during the transformation. Lukas and Abby seemed to grow by two years as well but all of them were shrinking again. Now looking more like a seven year old while Lukas and Abby looked year younger than yesterday. Jennifer felt sick as the belly once more got bigger and pinned her down once more to the floor. Her mother wasn't experiencing the same as her age kept keeping at the same age. The house planket slowly turning into a sawdust as the house disappeared leaving only the cement and mattresses on the floor.

    Justin and Mary looked pretty much the same as they were too growing and shrinking like a tide. Then again, Jennifer was growing this time looking more like a 13-year-old as the belly shrunk again. Then shrinking down at age six with new more massive belly. Jennifer begun to panic as the curse couldn't decide what age they should be at. Too many wrong turns on top of wrong turns had formed a spiral or an illogical loop that was making Jennifer constantly fail. The she remembered. Everytime she got older she was was closing age gap with her mother but the other part of the curse wanted her to be younger. The cement under Jennifer was gone as she now got 14-years-old. Weird high pitched white noise and window screeching filled Jennifer's ear drum as she suddenly found herself in poor state. She was bleeding. The constant shift in pressure was turning into a high pressureed accordion that pulled and pushed Jennifer's brain like a piston getting one step deeper.

    Jennifer coughed and felt that she couldn't stand up anymore. Looking at her own finger's they were getting so short that they were almost missing. Another growth spurt and she had no fingers anymore. Toes were also missing Jennifer had grown jump from age five to year fifteen. Jennifer felt the last pull and just like that. They all vanished. There was no house anymore, no more Smith. A small black hole begun to form on top of the ground where Jennifer had disappeared and slowly expanded pulling anything and everything it touched. Then just like that. The whole universe was erased.

    The curse had erased everything in existence.

    In the silence it was as if a glass had been just broken. Destroying the fabric of reality as it imploded on itself.

    ...

    Suddenly. A light. Colors. A room. The whole humanity came back in one millionth of a nanosecond.

    Kyle was sweating in his room and holding his phone in his arm as it made no sound. Why was he holding a phone? Who had he even called for? No memories why he was even upset. What happened just seconds ago? Why was he sweating. Kyle Perkins decided to sit down on top of his bed and calm down. Kyle's eyes glimmered without him noticing it. The curse had returned back to it's sender once more. To the world where Smiths had never existed.
