    Lotta, Astrid, and Legslie walked on for a while, admiring the campus and sharing laughs. Soon enough, however, it was time for them to split ways, Astrid slowly climbing off of Lotta's equine back as Legslie walked on back to her modified dorm room. Lotta realized she forgot to check on Cowgirl, and almost 6 hours had passed since she last saw her! In a panicked gallop, Lotta set off.

    She arrived a few minutes later to the library, and entered. She turned the corner, unlocking the door and having the remote at the ready. When she opened the door, what she was met with was quite the sight: Cowgirl had gotten HUGE! Her udder now weighed in at 250 gallons, or 1600 lbs of fresh milk. Her teats were each almost 3 feet wide, and her tail had reached a staggering 20 feet long! Her ears were also formidable, an amazing 12 feet long from tip to her now miniscule-in-comparison head and body.

    "OOOOOOooooohhhh finally, sooooommmmoooooone cammmme to rescMOOOOOOO mmmme!" Cowgirl moaned deeply, kneading her impossibly large udder. Lotta laughed at her predicament, and decided to sit on her udder. "Ohhhhh, stooop it!! Get offfff of meeee!" Cowgirl lazily yelled, turning beet red in embarrassment. Lotta then decided to make it even worse for her, turning the remote on herself...

    Lotta increased the size of her centaur body thricefold, now three times larger, and nine times heavier, than she had been before. The weight of her on Cowgirl's udder made her even angrier and more flustered, causing Lotta to laugh as she doubled the growth rate of Cowgirl's udder to 10 gallons every 5 minutes.

    Cowgirl moaned in ecstasy as Lotta let off of her now visibly growing udder, and she turned and left, ducking through and locking the door...
