    Trevor, Riley, Jamie, Beth, and Lizzie were rather nervous when they were called to Anderson's new office. Fortunately, he was willing to negotiate.

    ---

    "Are you aware of the security systems in Claw Coliseum?" Asked Anderson, looking rather disapproving.

    "Isn't that mainly to keep media newsmongers out of certain places?" Asked Jamie, a little nervous about why they were here.

    "It's also to make sure that those who compete in the Games treat their opponents with respect, especially after the games." Replied Anderson, matter-of-factly.

    "Oh?" Asked Beth, dearly hoping they weren't in big trouble.

    "We saw your little 'bet' with each other. Do you realize how serious this could be toward Animalia, especially if the media got their mits on it?" Asked Anderson.

    Fortunately, the bet was rendered null and void and all participants only had to deal with $20 fines for minor unsportsmanlike behaviors, and were allowed to continue to compete in the games. But there was a few things that Anderson needed help with.

    ---

    "I've put up a request for new game ideas for next year's IAC, and I've been getting some strange requests." Said Anderson, earnestly.

    "Like what?" Asked Jamie, privately glad for the minor fine.

    "What exactly IS 'foxy boxing'?" Asked Anderson, genuinely confused about the term.

    "Basically, it's ample girls dressing in skimpy clothes and competing in a more sexualized version of boxing." Explained Beth, having seen an article about it once or twice.

    ---

    Thanks to Beth's help, Anderson was able to veto a number of event suggestions, especially the more sexual ones. The ones that involved inflicting harm in a sexual way were permanently removed from the suggestions. The main reason was to differentiate Animalia from the anthropomorphic animals as depicted on the internet.
