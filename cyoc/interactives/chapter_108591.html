    It was about 1:00 in the morning now and you couldn't feel better. There was no feeling better than eating a soul after all.

    You snap your fingers and go back into your human form, and started walking down the street, only you knowing what had happened to the man whose soul you just ate.
    You didn't bother going back for the newspaper, it was useless now anyway. What you wanted to do was to find the prison filled with the evil souls of humans.

    Your mouth waters slightly as you think of it. You didn't know where exactly to go but you figured that eventually you'll detect a cluster of evil souls. You walked around for several hours, slowly making your way out of the city as you started walking along a highway. You could always go into your demon form and get there faster but you felt no need. Besides it was a nice sunny day. Why not enjoy the weather?

    The highway was bustling with activity, the sounds of cars moving by filled your hearing annoying you slightly. You kept walking though as you slowly made your way to the prison. You had to find directions from a couple of passer us, but eventually the prison was in sight. You made sure not to get to close to the prison, you didn't need to look suspicious after all.

    The prison was called "Winter Slam" something which you considered was likely to be an inside joke. It was a large facility with about three outer walls surrounding it. Sniper towers were dotted strategicly around the prison. The landscape around the prison was mostly flat grassland however there was a nearby forest west of the prison.
    You sensed the presence of mostly guards and staff workers inside the large prison. It seems like the prison has barely opened. It wouldn't be worth to sneak in now. You'll have to wait a while before you go inside. After not wanting to waste time, it looks like you'll have to wait. Figures.

    What to do in the mean time is the question.
