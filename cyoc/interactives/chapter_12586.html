    The Blonde finds a better revenge on Cindy

    Cindy couldn't help pointing and laughing at them as she managed to tear her purse away from the blonde and ran out of the store.

    The blonde, dog and others just stood there steaming.



    This is when I walked in looking for my girlfriend Cindy who had left me in the sporting goods store about an hour ago.

    "Excuse me, have you seen a woman about this tall, hair and so on" I described Cindy to this Blonde woman standing next to a dog...

    "And you are?" the blonde asked me.

    I told her I was Cindy's boyfriend and was looking for her.
    The woman smiled and took me by the arm, leading me back to the changing rooms. "I'll take you to her." she said.
    I noticed as we went she picked things off the shelves, till she couldn't carry any more in one arm.
    She held a large bra, panties and several odd looking spray bottles.
    We got to an empty changing room and went inside.
    But before I could ask why, she sprayed two of the bottles in my face.

    Wham! That is all I remember and waking up sitting in my car.

    "Ma'am are you ok?" a man was tapping at the passenger window.

    I looked at him then down at my clothes and then into the the visor mirror and screamed!
    I wasn't me!
    I was wearing a dress, bra and panties!
    My hair was the same color just long and flowing yet my eyes were green instead of blue.
    The man taped again and I hit the button to unlock that door.
    He opened and got in, looking me over with hungry eyes as he did.
    "I'm a woman!" I exclaimed and grabbed my huge and jutting chest.
    The guy looked confused by my action, not sure if I was joking around or not. "I wanted to see if you were alright." he said, leaning over and putting his hand just above my knee.


    This was a dimly lit underground garage where normally I would have been able to defend myself just fine. Except now, and how I have no clue, I have been turned into a female dressed in a provocative dress, passed out in a car. And worse, I let a stranger inside my car, who is now touching and looking at me with what I could only describe as bad intent.
    "Have you been drinking?" he asked and moved his hand further up my leg.

    What do you do now?
