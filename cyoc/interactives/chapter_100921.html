    "How rude of me", the witch said "you two look so hungry and I haven't fed you. "

    "Problem is, I haven't got anything to offer you" she said as Christina remained frozen.

    "Wait" she cried, "I can at least feed ONE of you". Christina had no idea what she meant but found out soon enough. The witch pointed at her with a laugh and Christina the nice again felt everything get larger. Only this time everything seemed much larger than before. She also noticed her sight had changed and she could see multiple images of everything.

    Suddenly, as if by reflex, she felt herself lift off the floor. She was flying! Then it dawned on her - the witch had turned her into a fly!! Before she could fully comprehend this she saw something large coming at her at great speed. Instinctively she dodged to the side and just missed being hit. After getting her wits back she realized it was the tongue of her companion toad. Even though they had somewhat bonded in their shared predicament, the toad was now more interested in Christina as his dinner.

    Thinking she had to get away quickly, Christina the fly flew off quickly to a high corner of the room. "He can't get me up here she thought." All went well until Christina suddenly felt herself stopped dead in her tracks by some type of netting. She tried to back away but the netting was too sticky and she couldn't escape it.

    Christina the fly was trying to figure what she had gotten herself into when the answer soon came in the form of a large black, multi-eyed, eight legged creature walking towards her on the netting. "Oh no" she thought.

    Christina the fly was trapped in a spiders web!
