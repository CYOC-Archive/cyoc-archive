    "Yes, ma'am," one man spoke, though his eyes drifted to Vixie, though not for too long.

    "See, everything was on the up and up," Trixie commented from another section of the room and while offering a tray of drinks to a both that actually had some Animalians seated at it, "so why not have fun... it'd help keep people warm."

    "Yeah... all those blizzards and super cold stuff has to be rough," Vixie commented, "we've seen the reports on the news."

    Miss Fabina sighed at that. She had seen the news reports two, much of the country, particularly areas east of the Rockey Mountains were being hit by hard blizzards that had closed roads and, in some places, had caught people unprepared. Some of it even went south into states like Texas, which again couldn't handle the cold and certain parts of the state either lose power or have to curtail the flow of power because of that cold. Some snow had fallen in Lyre and Animalia, and the temperatures were fairly cold, BUT that hadn't reached the levels of the blizzards going on further east.

    "One would hope you would recognize that most of the reports on those blizzards was not local to Lyre," Miss Fabina told Vixie, "and I'm sure everyone here has the means to keep warm. But it is still a business and one that does not trade in sex."

    "Couldn't some of the money go to help them dig out when it ends?" Dixie wondered as she came around another corner, "Animalia's Christmas gift to those that got buried in these blizzards?"

    Miss Fabina thought on that and nodded, "I suppose we could contact the organizations that would handle the relief efforts. We can then see if they would accept relief donations for the recovery."

    "And they'd accept it?" Trixie asked.

    "They might," Miss Fabina asked, "and there would be plenty of organizations that would still possibly accept donations that would be trying to help and would be separate from the government run efforts. And the areas worst hit by all this would surely need help or something related to it."

    "Yay!" Vixie cheered as she moved away from the man she'd been rubbing her posterior against, "presents to help people who are cold. Animalia gives the best gifts!"

    There were a few cheers that came from the floor and even from the others presently on duty at the Meat Market. It left Miss Fabina with a small sigh and then a chuckle. The bimbos might be progressing slowly with regard to earning their GED and remained a bit too forward in ways that could get the Meat Market in trouble, but it was fairly clear that they weren't completely blind to outside events and did have some measure of kindness. That was something that Miss Fabina could respect and easily accept, as kindness was always a good thing, and she had to chuckle at how sometimes good ideas came from those one didn't expect to have them and in ways that weren't expected to happen as well. In some ways, that did fit the purpose of the Christmas season.
