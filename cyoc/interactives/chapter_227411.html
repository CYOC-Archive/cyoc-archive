    Terri sighed heavily as she blew a rogue strand of red hair out of her face. Why did her parents have to force her to come on this family trip with them!? Although she was happy to be on vacation away from college, she would much rather be spending the time with friends her age, not with her smothering parents, dweeb sister and annoying brother.

    And what kind of place had her parents taken them all? The Transformation Resort!? It sounded like a complete scam. As if people could transform! Magic wasn’t real!

    She was 19! Basically an adult! Shouldn’t she get SOME control!?

    And as if on cue…

    Terri blinked and in a fleeting moment, she found herself in an endless white void, stretching as far as her vision would see. She turned around, expecting to see her family or even the concierge, but all she found was more white.

    “Hello!? Mom!? Dad!?” The teen girl called out. She was met with silence, until suddenly…

    “Congratulations Terri!” A voice stated.

    “EEP!” Shrieked Terri. Who said that?

    “Ah, my apologies. I didn’t mean to startle you.” The voice continued.

    “Who are you? Where are you!? Where am I!? What is this place!?” Exclaimed Terri. She was starting to get freaked out now.

    “Do not fret Terri, you are safe here. This is not a physical location. It's closer to a world within your mind. Your body is still at the resort check in desk.” The voice calmly explained to the frightened girl. “As for who am I? Am I no one. My only purpose is to inform you Terri that you have been selected to be upgraded to VIP during your stay.”

    “VIP?” Terri thought to herself. Those 3 letters piqued her interest profoundly. “VIP? Do you mean I get, like, an upgraded room or something?”

    “Perhaps it’s better if I show you.”

    Immediately, solid white panels rose out of the ground and floated above it. On each panel appeared easy to read text.

    "The VIP is a visitor who is randomly selected for a bigger experience than normal visitors, " the voice began to explain. "First, the VIP is able to apply transformations to anyone at this resort. This can be any transformation, including some not offered to normal visitors. Additionally, the VIP can choose how aware people are of these changes."

    “Apply transformations to anyone? Hold on, magic is real!?” Terri realised.

    “Correct Terri. Only you and you alone have the power to control anyone in this resort in almost any way you see fit.”

    Terri had finally got what she desired; control. She was starting to feel a stirring sensation down near her crotch as she thought about that. Wait, was she getting turned on thinking about transforming people?

    The voice continued its explanation. "Most importantly, the VIP can do mental and reality changes on guests, a feature that normal visitors do not get that much control of. With this, they can change the thoughts and lives of people."

    “Really? How so?

    “It’s quite simple really,” Began the voice. “Let's say you want a guest here to be your friend. With this you can make them a friend of yours suddenly, or that you were friends for years.
    And of course, once you leave, you decide which changes you made stay and which will revert."

    Terri simply nodded in sheer amazement at the power she held at her fingertips. “This is so… unbelievable.” She muttered. “How would I even go about changing someone?”

    “With these panels here.” The voice answered, a light shining down on the panels that rose from the ground earlier. “Whenever you want to initiate a transformation, just imagine yourself here in this white void and you will be transported back here. Then, you can use these panels to see and edit a person’s life, body and other information.”

    “So I just… think it?” Terri asked, not sounding very confident.

    “Perhaps it will be best if you see it in action for yourself. I will return you to the foyer now. Once there, you should pick out a target and try to transform them. I’ll see you back here soon Terri.” The voice instructed as the light inside the void grew brighter and brighter.

    In another quick moment, Terri was now back in the resort’s foyer with her family, just as her Dad was finishing signing the tablet. It seemed that no time had passed while she was inside the white void.

    “I should probably follow that voice’s advice and test out my new powers… if I even have them to begin with.”
