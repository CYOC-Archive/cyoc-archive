    Ty's Power

    Ty shrinks and becomes a part of Matt

    "I know one way we can always be together!!" Ty said, as an idea came to his mind.

    Ty turned around and wished for Matt to become sleepy.

    Matt yawned and started walking towards his bedroom, where he laid down on his bed. he was sound asleep in a
    minute, while laying on his back. Ty quietly walked into his room and grinned when he saw the muscular boy
    sleeping on his bed.

    Ty quietly approached the bed and then wished for his own body to shrink to about two feet tall. Ty rapidly
    shrunk down, from 5 foot 6 inches tall to only 2 feet tall, to the point where all his clothes just dropped in
    a big pile on the floor in the middle of the bedroom.

    Ty then climbed on the bed and from there he climbed on top of Matt's large torso, gently walking over his
    powerful, washboard abs until he made it to his massive pectorals. Then, Ty wished for his body to shrink even
    further, until he was only about six inches tall.

    Now that Ty was so tiny, he walked all the way up to Matt's chest, and he decided to lay down on his back. His
    head was sticking out just where Matt's neck was, but below his chin. Ty then wished for his body, from the
    waist down, to merge with Matt's massive chest. Ty's legs and groin then merged within Matt's bulging chest
    until his waist, chest, arms and head were sticking out from Matt's pectorals just below the neck. Tiny Ty had
    now become a part of Matt!

    "Time for Matt to wake up!" Ty grinned.

    When Matt woke up, he felt something on his chest, but it seemed like it was just a part of him. When he
    walked to the bathroom and saw his reflection in the mirror, that was when he realized that Ty had become a
    part of him.

    "TY! Holy smoke! You're tiny!"

    "Yeah! And from now on, we'll always be together because... I'm a small part of you!" Ty said, while looking
    up at Matt's big chin.

    Matt could really only see Ty when he looked in the mirror, but he felt that Ty was there whenever he ran his
    hand over his chest, and he felt Ty's chest and head sticking out from his own chest. He found that it was
    weird, having such a little person be attached to his own body like that, but he figured that he would get
    used to it...
