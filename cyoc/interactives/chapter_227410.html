    Decide to let keep Ema around as a human

    Branch Full view
    added A year ago BM

    The year went smoothly for the anthropomorphic cow girl and soon it was time to decide if they wanted to keep this form or change. The morning of their 1 year anniversary Jen heard a voice in her head.

    "Hey! I can talk again! I guess this means our year is up." Ema's voice echoed.

    "Yeah it is." Jen said as she checked the computer monitor. "Shall we return to normal?"

    "About that..." Ema's voice replied. "I rather like being an udder though not being able to talk is annoying. If you want to return to human that's fine. But can I still be an udder on you?"

    Jen thought about it. She did enjoy the fresh milk and the company. She got onto the computer and typed away on the keyboard before hitting enter. All of her cow features aside from her udder reverted to human again. Ema the udder was still hanging between her legs bit Ema's head now sat atop it looking around.

    "You kept me!" Ema squealed happily. "And I can see and talk again!"

    Jen patted the squishy udder that was hanging from her crotch. "Yup. You're too much fun to loose." She replied.

    "Now what?" Ema asked.

    Jen grinned. "While you were silent I got a date with someone really nice tonight." She went through her closest and found a nice red dress and slipped it on. While it did cover Ema she didn't mind.

    "Who's the date?" She asked.
