    Luke, stuck as a wolf?

    added by Dominoday 2 years ago A BM S Mental

    After Kevin and Luke having powered themselves out for nearly two hours, the moved back inside. Kevin was thinking about Luke. Should he turn him back now? He was sure their dad would soon be home, and maybe it would be better to not have Luke as a wolf then. So, after Luke had drunk from a bowl that Kevin had placed down and laid again before the couch, Kevin took the remote again to turn his brother back.
    As he hit the Think button again, nothing happened. As that was the same as before, Kevin waited a bit, but when Luke looked confused at him, he knew that it really didn´t work.
    Kevin panicked a bit. What if he couldn´t turn Luke back to normal? But he remembered than that in the morning, Luke hadn´t been able to say hands until he was in contact with the costume. Maybe he needed to first turn it back?
    Hitting the exchange button next, he watched as the latex encased Luke´s form again, turning his wolf skin and fur back into a latex costume. Luke remained calm, even as the wolf himself didn´t understand what was happening. But his alpha wouldn´t hurt him, right?
    Kevin hit the think button again, but as Luke still blinked at him confused, he could guess it doesn´t work again. Maybe it doesn´t work on the full costume? So, Kevin let it recede by hitting the human button, leaving a naked and confused, wolf-minded Luke before him.
    When the think button refused to work a third time, Kevin really panicked. Something was definitively wrong, and he had no idea how to fix this mess.
    “What´s wrong, alpha?” Luke asked him, voice now nearly sounding normal, if a bit rougher still. As Kevin was agitated in his panic, Luke the now human looking wolf moved towards him on all fours, bumping him with his head, trying to calm him down.
    After a bit of that, Kevin seemed to calm at least a bit. “I don´t know how to change you back.” Kevin said, trying to not return to panicking again. What could he do?
    Luke cocked his head to the side, not seeing the problem at the moment. “While it´s weird and cold being without my fur, I guess I can live like that for now. You will find a solution, alpha.”
    Clearly, the wolf didn´t understand what Kevin really meant, thinking his alpha couldn´t give him his fur back. Kevin realized the wolf didn´t remember being his brother right now. “Have I killed my brother for being selfish? There must be something I can do.”
    It was only after being calmed even more by his wolf-minded brother that Kevin had another idea. “I need to call uncle John.” He thought. It was his present to them, so he should know how to fix this.
    And so, Kevin called uncle John, while Luke sat next to him.
