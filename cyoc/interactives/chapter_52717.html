    "Ya sure!" Ty cheered. He spun them around, and they side stepped their way the the front door. Dustin was confused at first, but he heard Ty wish Allen and Kent were over, so it made sense. Ty walked them over and opened the door, and there stood Kent and Allen. Both of them had shocked expressions.

    In front of them stood Ty and Dustin, wearing nothing but their dark blue jeans, with their chests fused together at their fronts, and their skin mixed together like swirls. Allen lifted his finger and pointed at them, terrified, "What the hell?!"

    "I wish Kent and Allen were fused too!" Dustin yelled and Ty grinned. Before Kent or Allen could say anything, they slammed into each other, chest to chest. Ty and Dustin watched eagerly as Kent and Allen's shirts ripped. As soon as their bare skin touched, it seemed to melt together, then turn back to it's solid state.

    Kent instantly started to push Allen, but he seemed to be too shocked to care about the pain. He looked at Kent, then down at their mixed flesh, "What the hell?!"

    Kent had stopped pushing and was glaring at Dustin. He looked over at Ty with puppy dog eyes and begged, "Ty, can you please undo this?" Ty shook his head with his grin still pastured on his face. "What?! Why not?!"

    "Because..." Ty explained, "I let Dustin have some of my power, so you'd have to ask him. Besides... I like it!"

    "You can't be serious!" Allen sounded angry, "Dustin, undo this. Now!" Dustin had an evil grin, and just shrugged his shoulders. He and Ty walked their way back into the kitchen while talking about what to eat, leaving Kent and Allen stuck looking at each other.

    "What do we do?" Kent asked quietly.

    "We go inside..." Allen grumbled, "We can't be seen like this out in public. Come on." He turned to face the door, and started walking, pushing Kent backwards. Kent tripped on the door lip, but Allen kept them up by grabbing onto the frame. Kent was hanging under Allen by their newly fused chest.

    Kent put on a smile, "Thanks."

    "Ya." Allen said flatly. They righted themselves and sighed. They weren't sure why they had come over, but they suspected that Ty had wanted to give Dustin test subjects, and neither one of them was thrilled about it.
