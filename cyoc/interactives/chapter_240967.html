Her bottom meet the ground with a loud squish, making Leah partially glad for the padding cushioning her fall. Fond memories of play in the tub came back to her, where she would spend hours splashing around in bubble baths. She shifted and struggled until she got her feet under her, then put all her muscle power into standing back up from a squat.
