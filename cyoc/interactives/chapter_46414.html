    Transform or Dare?

    Emma's Sheepish Week Begins

    Jen thought hard about Emma's proposal. She knew she was risking the happiness she felt, but she was sure once Emma was a sheep girl, she'd understand. "Okay, E-e-ema. De-e-eal."

    "I'm not bleating, though."

    "Tha-a-at's fine. Believe me-e-e."

    "So, how long are we doing this for? A week sound good? We'll have all week at school and this weekend. That should be enough time for us both to make our decisions."

    "Oka-a-ay."

    Deciding to find somewhere more private, they went to the girls' restroom. After making sure it was empty, they locked the door.

    "A-a-are you ready, Emma-a-a?"

    "As ready as ever," Emma sighed. She was nervous - she worried about how much of an impact such a major transformation would have on her life. At least it was only for a week. "I just want to let you know I'm only doing this because of how much your friendship means to me." She realized that, deep down, a small part of her was really excited at this opportunity, since it would just be for a little while.

    "You'll lo-o-ove it." Jen smiled. Soon Emma would know how great she felt. She couldn't get the words "Transform or dare" out quick enough.

    Faced with the question, Emma paused a moment and closed her eyes. The curse had been evoked. No going back. She heard herself say, barely louder than a whisper, "Transform."

    Jen then, with a little difficulty due to the bleating, spoke Emma's transformation, being sure to mention her voice would remain the same. Jen also told Emma that only the two of them would know Emma wasn't always a sheep girl. Everyone else would accept it unless Emma told them otherwise. Jen finished up the curse, saying that after a week, Emma could change back to normal at anytime by saying "I want to be human again".

    Before long, Emma began to feel the effects of the curse. She first felt a tingling in her crotch. She pulled forward her shorts and panties and saw that her pubic hair had become a white patch of wool. The wool spread to the sides, wrapping around her body. Emma started to feel changes in her feet and started taking her shoes off as quickly as she could. She liked these shoes and wanted to be able to use them when she was human again.

    As she was doing so, the wool spread down her legs to her feet, which were darkening and changing into hooves like Jen's. The wool then headed upwards. Slowly, her stomach, breasts, and arms were covered with the fluffy substance. The wool continued spreading, stopping at her neck. Now that it covered her body, she felt it begin to become thicker and fluffier.

    Emma looked in the mirror. She couldn't believe it. Ten minutes ago, she didn't even believe the curse was real, but now it had transformed her into a sheep girl like her friend Jen, albeit a sheep girl wearing a tank top and shorts. She didn't know what to think. She just wasn't looking forward to other people seeing her. What if they realized she wasn't supposed to be this way?

    Looking at Emma, Jen couldn't stop smiling. She looked great as a sheep girl! Jen knew she would. She was thrilled her friend decided to share this experience with her.

    There was nothing to it but to go about her day, Emma decided. She had told Jen she'd give this a shot, and no one had thought anything was weird about Jen being a sheep, not even her. But first things first, she had to give Jen her orders as promised.

    "Okay, Jen here goes." The newest sheep girl was glad to hear that she wasn't bleating like her friend had been forced too. "Firstly, I order you to only bleat if you actually want to. You have no idea how long it takes to get through a conversation when you're doing that."

    "Thanks, Emma! Hey! Emma! Emma! I can say it! That was the only part about this I didn't like."

    "Well, let's see if that's still true. Next, I order you to feel about being a sheep girl the way you would feel if you had never received any orders about it. I order you to wear clothes when you decide you want to, and but not to when you don't. I also order you to undo any transformations you are already under besides the one that made you a sheep girl. Finally, I order you not to say anything, do anything, transform in any way, or take any other action in response to an order given to you that you would not voluntarily take if you were not under a curse.

    "There, that should do it, and not a moment too soon if we want to get to class on time. So how do you feel now?"
