    Because "I wish the Protectors were back to normal" was too easy

    A blast of weird green energy later Red 6, in adult form and with her magical girl uniform looking closer to a dress than a shirt and skirt, opened her eyes and found herself in midair and rapidly falling towards the top of some building. "Fuck! Ok time to use Mr. Spider's lessons... just latch my ribbon to the side of that building and..." Red 6 pulled hard to force herself to fall off the ledge instead of pancake herself on the building's roof. After that all she had to do was release more ribbon slowly and slow her fall down... Red 6 not an expert in the maneuver was left with sore arms and legs panting on the side of a building. Thinking about the height from which she actually fell from she probably could've just used some magic to cushion her fall and she would've been better off. "Where am I anyways?" A quick survey from her vantage point showed... it was a city that kinda resembled New York but it kinda of felt different. Red 6 sighed as she slowly lowered herself to the ground where a group of people were surrounding her landing point. Considering it took her a good ten minutes and the news agencies always looking for some sort of scoop were down filming her decent. I guess they wouldn't recognize her as a woman when Red 6 was known as a tween. When the drop was manageable Red 6 dropped down.

    On her way down Red 6 noted a few things... For one the pebble was gone. She wasn't sure what the worse situation was, she dropped it and now it's waiting for someone to pick it up or it didn't come with her. Also that the people and advertisements looked very different... Like she went a few decades in the past... Least all the reporters and spectators gave her a wide birth... Like anyone dressed in her getup that you don't recognize scaling down a building was probably at least a little dangerous. She was pretty surrounded, and was thinking about how she could get home or do something or-

    "Um... Mam? Why were you just scaling down the Protector's building?" Said some lady coming from behind Red 6. Turning around Red spotted a familiar looking woman in a nice dress, long blonde hair, blue eyes, and very familiar features.
    Red 6 said "Um... I just kind of teleported near the roof and... Kinda didn't want to fall to my death."
    The lady smirked and said "Come on in, we need to question you... At least it'll be better than these reporters questioning you."

    Inside and escorted to a Protector questioning cell made of really sturdy stuff the lady said "Well... I'm the hero known as Sprite. You are?"
    Red 6 said "I'm Red 6... and now I'm certain that I was teleported to a different time."
    Sprite easily read the unease on Red's face and asked "So... Were you a magical girl or was there some meaning behind your name? Cause I think the Magical Girl squads were at Red 3 by now."
    Red 6 nodded and said "I am a Magical Girl... The pebble that transported me here had also made me an adult and most of the Protectors into babies..."
    Sprite chuckled and said "I guess you mean in your own time... Mind if I ask how you knew me?" Red was silent for a bit leaving Sprite to sigh and say "Seriously, when you were scaling down our building I say the live TV and you were pretty sure of yourself. Tired for sure but you knew what you were doing. Now that you are right next to me though you seem to be squirming quite a bit."
    Red 6 took a breathe and said "Well... um... You're my mother." There was a silence between the two as Red 6 wasn't sure what she should say, and Sprite was reading this random woman's face. Sprite had to admit that outside of their pigments a lot of their features was pretty similar.
    Sprite said "Well, we're going to need to do a DNA test. You seem like a good woman so... Let's say we let you go through some obstacle courses under closer observation, and when the DNA tests are complete... We'll see how things go."
