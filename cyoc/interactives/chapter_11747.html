    Eventually, the five girls find you in the hallway.

    "Timmy, we know about your problem," says one of them. "We know how to fix it. If you're scared of girls, you should see what it is like to be one."

    "You can't transform me!" you bravely shout. "We're not playing house. You said you can only transform if you play house."

    "Okay," says the same girl. "Then let's play house."

    You all of sudden feel really strange. It feels like things are changing on your body, but you can't see anything because you're wearing clothes.

    But changes are not just under your clothes. Your hair is rapidly growing longer. Your clothes are morphing from boys clothes to girls clothes.

    "You made me a girl?" you say, realizing you have a girl's voice.

    "Don't worry," says another girl. "It's only for a week. One week from now, we'll change you back."

    "Wait until my mommy finds out..."

    "She won't," the girl continues. "We did a special transformation. Not only are you a girl, everyone thinks you've always been a girl."

    "I don't want to be a girl!" you exclaim.

    "It's only so you wouldn't be scared of girls any more," says another girl very calmly.

    "Now I'm even more scared of girls!" you think to yourself. The girls walk away as you wonder what to do.
