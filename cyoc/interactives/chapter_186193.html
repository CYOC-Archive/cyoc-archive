    Bortock was somewhat concerned about the part about 'side effects', but she definitely needed the luck.
    The 'Cursed Hero/Heroine' Skill was what probably got her into all of this in the first place.
    She decided to take the risk and moved the Earring to her left ear, not at all surprised when it slipped into place this time.
    There was a 'ping' and she immediately checked the skill.

    - The Cursed Heroine: You will level up quickly, but evil will be attracted to you.

    She raised her fist victoriously, happy that at least the 'luck is not your friend' part had been removed.
    She glanced over her other stats while she was at it and noticed something.

    Name: Bessie Candywire

    "Wait, my name isn't Bessie, it's Bessie." She startled by her own words. "No, i meant Bessie!"

    WIS: 9 +5
    CHA: 30 +12

    Active Skills:

    Super Restoring Milk lv 1: Once a day skill allows you to produce milk that fully restores the drinker, except for skill uses.
    Dynamic Domestication lv 1: Once a day skill that temporarily switches your constitution with your wisdom and allows the the use of specific spells. More spells unlocked at higher levels.

    Passive Skills:

    -Nature Affinity: As a Beastfolk, you have a natural affinity for nature, and will be stronger in nature magic, herbology, or beast taming, should you choose to pursue those paths
    -Breeder Cow: Blessed with abnormally large assets, high sex drive, and fertility. Grants a random chance for a +1 to charisma or constitution at each level up.
    - The Cursed Heroine: You will level up quickly, but evil will be attracted to you.
    - Sensitive Body: Your tactile senses are highly attuned, and your capacity for sexual pleasure is increased.
    -Cow at Heart: You loose 1 point of intelligence, but are easily accepted by Cows and Cow-like creatures.

    Spells:

    Healing Touch lv 1(available only with Dynamic Domestication): Using breast contact, the caster is able to restores the skills of a target, or by massaging her breasts, restore her own skills(except Dynamic Domestication), allowing more uses of them per day.
    Submission lv 1(available only with Dynamic Domestication): The caster offers herself to a male target. This is an almost irresistible form of long term charm spell which may save the caster from death if a battle isn't going well, but it also charms the caster and causes her to submit to the target.

    Bonded Creatures:

    Greater Chimera lv 80 - You are submissive to this Creature. It will protect you, but is unlikely to follow orders.

    Information:

    Born a bull of the Maltuck tribe of the hills, you enjoyed a simple and free life in the wilds and looked to one day becoming chief of your tribe, until Orc slavers captured you, and you were sold to a horrible sorcerer who turned you into a helpless docile milk cow. You managed to kill the sorcerer. Your future was uncertain, until you bonded with the sorcorer's Chimera, becoming it's mate.
    It will protect you, now that you can no longer do so yourself. Your name became Bessie Candywire after equipping a magical Earring.
    You have become unable to call yourself by any other name, but managed to overcome half of your curse in exchange.

    She stared at the transparent display, mouth agape, not willing to believe what was written on it.
    It took her a while to snap out of it, and ground her teeth in frustration when she did.
    'Well, maybe my luck won't be as bad now at least.' She sighed and looked to her side, where the Chimera stood in confusion,
    wondering why she was having such violent mood swings while staring at the air. Bessie reached over and stroked the monster's neck.
    "Let's just get out of here." She walked out of the room together with the Chimera, after looking over the room one last time,
    to make sure that they hadn't left anything behind. They had almost reached the front gate of the castle, but she still wasn't sure where
    she should go now. Maybe her new skill would allow her to stay at a nearby Minotaur Tribe for a while?
    Maybe she should head back to her own Tribe, even if they wouldn't recognize her. Or perhaps she could become an Adventurer.
