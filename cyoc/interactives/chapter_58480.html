    The Family of Clowns

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 12 years ago BM

    After applying the ointment to Jenny and Jill's feet, Caitlyn next picked up the pen and paper again. She turned to the back and wrote:

    Mom(Anne)

    Whiteface

    Height of 5'7

    Weighs 340 lbs

    Hair Crimson red, curled into afro

    Feet: 35 with eight inch toes

    Slender red mouth always in a smile

    Blue diamonds surrounding eyes

    Light pink blush on cheeks

    Round red nose the size of ping-pong-ball; covered with glitter

    Finger and toe nails same color as hair

    Wears white lace cap on hair

    Wears red dress covered with white polka-dots; dress sleeves are puffy

    Skirt fluffed up with white petticoats

    Wears white bloomers and pink and white striped socks

    Wears red and white polka-dotted Mary Janes with reversed pattern bow over laces

    Belly jiggles when she laughs

    Has a full knowledge of clown jokes both practical and impractical

    Acts very matronly and motherly to all of us.


    Next she wrote

    Mike

    Auguste

    Yellow-Orange Complexion

    Bald with bushy bright red hair around the edges

    Height is same as now

    Weighs 375 lbs

    Feet are size 37 with ten inch wide toes

    White patches around eyes that reach to his forehead

    Patches topped by arched black eyebrows

    White muzzle around mouth surrounded by bright red blush

    Black smile inside muzzle

    Big red nose nearly the size of a baseball; nose makes a big honking sound when squeezed and blows confetti when sneezes

    Farts about as much as I do

    Rides on tiny bicycle he carries in his shirt pocket

    Size 36 feet with ten inch wide toes

    Wears suits of various colors in following pattern: wide jacket, wide pants held up by suspenders that end just below the knees, wide puffy bow tie and stripes socks that reach just past the knees

    Wears wing-tipped hightops on feet.

    Wears a tiny hat on top of bald head; usually at an angle

    Has a full knowledge of clown jokes both practical and impractical

    Acts very much the part of an auguste and does whatever me, mom, or Jill or Jenny tell him.


    When she finished writing, Caitlyn hid her list and reached for the ointment. Stetching out her elastic arms, she went first into her mothers room, then her brother's, giggling all the way. Oh, they were all going to have so much fun!
