    Gen was skipping rocks out on a pond only she knew about. After getting home and greeting her parents, she went out to a forest behind her house. Her house was on the outskirts of town, so it was really easy to just walk out and take in the fresh forest air. As a kit, she would sneak out of the house and play out there. So already found a small cave she could just hang out in as well as multiple hiding place and landmarks only she knows about that she would visit from time to time. She practically has the who place mapped out and she knows how to get around the animals.

    Today, she decided to relax next to a small pond as she thought about today's events. Meeting Jamal, fusing with him, and gaining his fusion powers. It's just so much to process. Especially she has years worth of memories from Jamal. It's just... so much.And really made made her think about her life when compared to Jamal's. In some ways, Jamal had things better then Gan. Gan was a only child, and having memories of growing up with Jamal's older sister kinda... mystifies gen. She always wanted a sibling, and now she has a second hand acount of spending years being jealous of one. It's jst.. weird to think about. Jamal also had some things hader then Gen. He didn't grow up with a father, and his family was generally more tighter on money what with his mom working overtime just to keep things afloat. Jamal may take her fore granted, but Gan recognizes how much she cares for her kids and how tired and grumpy she must be when she gets home. It really made Gen realize how much she took for granted.

    Gen didn't bother to look as she went to grab another rock to throw into the pond.

    FWOOMP!

    Uh oh....

    Gen almost didn't want to look. She was already getting a bunch of new sensations from her hand. But eventually, she did look down to see what her hand became.

    And what she saw horrified her.

    Her hand was now green, constantly giving off a layer of mucus. Her fingers where positioned like legs. Two on the left. Two on the right. Each the same length, but just as dexterous as fingers. And of her middle finger was a neck. A nect that ended in a frog's face. Still crocking. And still breathing it's own lungs in her hand.

    Her hand has fused with a frog.

    But instead of screaming in panic at how deformed her her hand became, she instead just rolled her eyes in annoyance without saying anything. She calmly examined her new hand. The frog that fused with her hand seems to retain it's mind. It can still control her hand. It feels so weird having her hand breath and move it's limbs on it's own accord. But with a simple though, she can control her new frog leg fingers with ease, overriding the frog's thoughts. And their joints allows them to move like frog legs and as fingers.

    It's so freaky, yet so... fascinating.

    Gen was about to pull the frog off her right hand using her right hand. Doing do would defuse the frog from her hand. But right before she could... she had a dark thought. No one was around to see her like this. And she's gonna let this frog go anyways so... why not keep it like this for a few minutes? She was to explore all what this new hand can do.

    And then a even darker thought occurred. What if... she starts fusing her body parts with animals? All the way till her body is completely fused with different animals? She doesn't need to come home until night, so that gives her a few hours. More then enough time too... see what it's like. It could be a plant, a insect, a squirrel, anything living.

    With this thought in mind, Gen scanned her surroundings...
