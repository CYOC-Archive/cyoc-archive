    The End?

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by HyperactiveKitten 16 years ago O

    The portal starts to close after Saphira and Ruby vanish through it, then Saphira suddenly appears in front of you again.

    "I know Ruby did not do these deeds alone, and you are largely to thank." Saphira bows. "You are the true savior of our world, and for that I am eternally grateful... However, I am afraid, we may never see each other again, as the worlds are not meant to mix." She smiles. "There are two things I can do for you though before I close the gateway."

    She reaches forward, and presses her hand to her forehead. Suddenly your fur retreats into your body, your tail melts away, your ears slide down your head become more round and less fuzzy, your muzzle pulls back into your face, and your hands and feet change back to normal. In a few moments, you're back to your old human self! You blust a bit, realizing you're standing naked in front of Saphira after the changes end.

    ".... The other thing I can do, is a gift from our world, to thank you. You shall find it when you return home."

    Saphira vanished, and then the portal closed for good. Not sure what else to do, you quickly unpacked your clothes from your backpack and got dressed, then headed back to your home.

    You arrived at your house, and for some reason felt somewhat empty. The books had brought nothing but trouble, putting you in nonstop danger, but, deep down you thought you had actually enjoyed the adventure. It was over now, but you remembered what Saphira said, about a gift waiting for you at home. You almsot hoped to come home and find one of the books waiting on the kitchen table for you. However, you saw nothing.

    You looked outside, and saw that it was nearly morning, you would probaly have to take a sick day from work just to get a bit of rest. You quickly walked to the phone and reached for your phone book to get your boss' number, and instead of the phone book, pulled out a small blue notebook instead. You open it just for a moment just to see what it is and..... There are spells listed in it!


    Mini Book of Magic


    I. Spell One: Age Reversal
    In order to age one in reverse you must select your target by pointing at them. If you do not point at a target, you will be the target of your own spell. To activate this spell, speak the command workd "Regress" and think clearly how many years you want the client to be regressed. It it possibly to regress one as far as a newborn, but you can not unbirth one with this spell alone. This spell consumes only a mild amount of energy.


    II. Spell Two: Ageing
    This spell allows you to make one older. In order to cast it, you must point to select a target. If you do not point at a target, you will be the target of the spell. To activate the spell, speak the command word "Progress" and think of how many years you want to progress the target. This spell can not age a target to death. This spell consumes only a mild amount of energy.


    III. Beast Shape
    This spell allows you to transform yourself or another target into an animal of your choice. You must speak the command word 'shape' and focus on the animal you want. To designate a target, point at what you what to transform. If you do not point at a target, you will transform yourself. This spell requires very little energy.


    IV. Half Shape
    This spell allows you to transform yourself or another partially into an animal. Point at a target to designate it, or if you don't point at a target you will transform yourself. The command word to inintiate the spell is 'Half'. This spell can be used to make the target any part animal and part human. You can do something qutie simple as just a tail, make one 50% each, or even just have them only retain their abilities to speak in the human tongue. This spell requires a mild amount of energy.


    V. Muscle Growth
    This spell allows one to grant strength and muscles to himself or others to make one stronger, faster, more agile, etc. While how much muscle they wish to give one is up to the caster, it should be warned that making muscles to large can have undesirable consequences. To activate the spell, decide how muscular you want them to be and point at the target. If no target is selected you are the target of the spell. The command word for this spell is ‘muscle’ and the spell consumes only a minor amount of energy.


    VI. Muscle Deflation
    This spell allows you to take away the muscles and strength someone already has. It’s good for if you pick a fight with someone twice your size. The command word is ‘deflation’ and to activate the spell picture how muscular you want them to not be and point at your target. If no target is pointed to, you will be the target of the spell. This spell requires only a minor amount of energy to use.


    VII. Growth
    This spell allows you to increase one’s size. The size you increase one to has no limits, but the spell will require more energy for more extreme changes. The command word is ‘growth’ and to activate the spell speak the command word, and focus on how large you want the target to be while pointing at the target. If no target is selected, then the spell will affect the caster only. This spell requires a mild amount of energy to use.


    VIII. Shrink
    This spell allows you to decrease one’s size. The size you decrease one to has no limits, but the spell will require more energy for more extreme changes. The command word is ‘shrink’ and to activate the spell speak the command word, and focus on how small you want the target, while pointing at the target. If no target is selected, the spell will affect only the caster. This spell requires a mild amount of energy to use.


    IX. Modified Monster Shift
    This spell will allow you to transform yourself or another into a mythical creature that is part human part creature. It can be used to become a anthropormorphic version of a mythical creature that is already part human. To use this spell, simply speak the command word 'modified monster' while visualizing the creature you want and pointing at the target. If no target is selected you are the target. It requires only a mild amount of energy to use.


    X. Modified Greater Monster Shift
    This spell allows you to transform yourself or another into a monster or mythical creature! Simply point at the target, speak the commnand words "Modifed Greater" and visualize the type of creature you want. If no target is selected you become the target. This spell uses a mild amount of energy.


    You looked over the book in astonishment. Saphira had left you a small spellbook with ten spells you could use anytime you want! Anytime you longed for childhood again, or to get away from human problems as an animal you now could.

    Life may return to normal now, but you were not sure it would be anything other than boring.
