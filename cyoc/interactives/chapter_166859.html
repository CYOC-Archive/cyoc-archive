And considering I was sent out to look for a missing merchild, I assume that it. We found the remains of a merman and mermaid from a shark attack, look like it made its way to shore and it found you.     “So this is common, so you can turn us back?” inquired Chloe
    “I can’t.” stated Cura as Chloe glared at her. “While young every merchild has magic as a defense against attack that magic fades around adulthood, I can only erase memories of people who see me, I have done that to a few human fishermen and some limited water magic to defend myself.”

    “Other then that I don’t have any magic.” stated Cura “The only people who could change you back is members of the royal family or the high families, they keep enough magic to possibly transform you back.”
    “Ok…where are they?” inquired Chloe
    “Well not in my village, we are only a few hundred, the nearest place a royal could be would be a full day swim to the south.” stated Cura as everyone’s eyes widened.
    “A FULL DAY!” yelled Ana
    “Two days at least, we would have to swim there and swim back.” Stated Chloe “I have a date tonight.”
    “Sorry.” apologized Cura as she looked at them. “And even if there was a royal, it is unlikely that they would turn you back…different laws would make it hard for them to do.”
    “What do you mean?” inquired Tim
    “Well…” started Cura as she looked at the child. “By our law, if an abounded or lost child transforms a human or humans into it’s new family, by law it is your child and you are it’s parents.”
    “Our child?” inquired Monica as she looked at the merbaby who had finally gotten tired and was now being held in her arms.
    “We found the child and it transformed my wife and I.” said Damon as he swam up to Monica and looked down at the child in Monica’s arms. “We where dragging ourselves to the water when the others arrived and the baby transformed them as well.”
    “I see, the royals may take that into consideration, but I would assume that the two of you will not be leaving this is your child and you are it’s parents.” Stated Cura
    “It transformed those two first, so they are it’s parents not us.” stated Chloe as she looked at Damon and Monica. “I have to go home, my girlfriend is expecting me tonight and…I will not miss it.”
    “Chloe…I am sure Stacy will understand if you miss a date, I am sure the police will notice we are missing and once we transform back we can say we where dragged out to sea by a rip tide.” Offered Tim, he hadn’t missed that he and Ana could possibly go home as well.
