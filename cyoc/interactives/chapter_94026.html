    time went extremely slow for Jeff, he's not really sure just how much time had passed or where he was for that matter. at first he felt the movement of the truck he was being transported on, followed by the pallet he was stacked on being unloaded. he could hear a lot of commotion and loud noises but soon came to a stop. he could only assume he must be in a warehouse at this point. time came to a standstill for Jeff who was trapped in the package of diapers. after several days the pallet he was on was broken down and he felt himself restacked on another pallet which was then loaded onto yet another truck. after several hours the truck arrived at its destination and he felt the package being first unloaded from the truck on the pallet, and then eventually the package being put on a cart of some sort and wheeled out. it felt cooler wherever he was - must be the air conditioning he thought. he felt himself stacked off once again and onto a shelf with several other packages of diapers. he still could not see but he could hear a little bit - music for that matter. from the sound of it he must have been in a grocery store.

    after several hours he felt someone pick up the package and nonchalantly toss it into a grocery cart. the package must have been resting on top of some frozen food as the temperature inside began to rapidly drop. suddenly he heard a loud screech and realized it was a baby screaming near him - this was followed by a woman, presumably the mother, trying to calm the baby. "shh...shh...inside voice miss!" he heard the woman say. they continued shopping for another 30 minutes or so as more and more items began to pile on top of the diaper package. before Jeff knew it he felt the weight decrease around him and then was himself lifted out of the cart and put on a conveyor belt. he heard lots of beeping noises and felt himself picked up once again by the cashier this time which was followed by a loud beep. the package was then grabbed by another and placed in a bag before being once again placed back in the cart. soon they were on the move again and it began to get really hot inside the diaper package - Jeff realized they must be outside now. the sound of car doors opening soon followed and Jeff found the package being picked up once more and placed in the trunk of the car. the trunk was closed loudly and all was quite for a moment until the car was started up.
