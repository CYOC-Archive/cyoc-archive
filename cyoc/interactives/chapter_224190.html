    "Now, what to do with you..." pondered the fairy with a mischievous grin.
    You are greatly afraid of what she might do with you. And quickly her eyes light up with excitement. But you know it cannot be good. No, certainly not for you.
    "Now how would YOU like to be in the jar?" she asks as if she could possibly doubt the answer.
    "What? No!..." you start shouting.
    But it is too late already. The fairy drops you in the jar and closes the lid. You expect to smash all you bones at the bottom but it is just as if you fell off from your bed. But it doesn't make things much better. Thanks to her magic, the fairy closes the lid of the jar. You pound with your fists on the glass, screaming at the top of your lungs for her to let you out and to change you back. But she giggles, waves at you and flies away. And she leaves you alone, in a jar, at the mercy of any fay creature coming your way. Any fay creature that might possess magic. And but worse, you are trapped in a woman's body now! You have curves, small and perky boobs and you can guess that you are pretty attractive, at least in body. You don't really want to check what's between your legs. Or rather, what is no more between your legs... And you are naked! How can you even try to escape and regain your original appearance in these conditions, tiny and stark naked? So you are quite desperate, in a new body and bound for an uncertain fate...
    Furthermore, what you don't know is that the fairy left a message on the jar, unbeknownst to you. A written message on the lid and around it that said: "Please Adopt Me!" Any fairy being was sure to consider you now...
