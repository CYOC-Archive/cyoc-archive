    Emily and the Robosuit

    “I want to try a full robot costume. Can you show me all the ones you have?”
    “Sure, right this way.”
    The woman led Emily to a section where numerous robot costumes were lined up side by side. After they reached the section with the female suits, Emily started browsing them, looking for something she liked. The woman took note of her indecision. “What are you looking for in a robot costume?”
    Emily thought for a bit, before responding, “I don’t want something overly sexualized, that’s for sure, but I want it to be very clearly feminine. I’d like one that’s somewhat formal in appearance, maybe like something that might make me look like a robo-secretary or waitress at a fancy restaurant. I’d definitely like some robo-hair, maybe done in a bun. And maybe if you can find one that look sort of like my face, that’d be awesome.”
    “I think I know one just like what you’re looking for. Let me go get it.” The woman left and came back with a silver and black robot suit being rolled on a cart. Emily closely examined the body. The head and face eerily resembled a silver version of her face, and the black robo-hair was done in a bun. As far as the rest of the body, it had a black primary color on parts like the breastplate, waist, legs, feet, neck, lips, eyebrows, and arm pieces, and the silver parts were the abdomen, joints, accents, and hands.
    It looked very much like her, but she took note of the obviously robotic aspects besides the obvious like joints. The ears were instead replaced with headphone-like covers that had an antenna on each. The feet had no toes, instead being like boots with an axle in the ankle area with silver ridged soles. The boobs, which replicated her modest B cups in terms of size, had no nipples and didn’t call attention to themselves. The waist piece looked almost like a metal diaper in terms of shape. Overall, she really liked the appearance.
    “How is it?”
    “I love it! How does it work?”
    “Well, there are some options. The first choice is if you’re going to pay up front for this or work it off in the store. I think, if you see the price tag, you’ll notice that this is very expensive. You can chose, instead of paying up front, to work in the store, with either the same hours as a normal job or working and residing in the store 24/7, both with all your needs covered until your wages would cover the cost. You’d likely be working in the store for either a month or for about 2 and a half months, depending on which option you pick. You can also choose to keep the job after you pay it off, with even more pay than the average worker in the store. You can also pay up front and still take the job anyways.
    “The second choice is how you’re going to wear the costume. As you can see, the hair and face are exactly as you requested. That’s because they were just made for you and can be detached from the robot head. You can choose to have your human face and hair, just the human face, or you can leave neither exposed and wear the entire helmet. Before you ask what impact that will have on the transformation, allow me to go over the third choice.
    “The third choice is how much of a robot you wish to be. The main body cannot be put on selectively without compromising the transformation, however, the head and all its pieces can. At the very minimum, your entire body from the top of the neck piece down will be fully robotic. However, from here, you can decide how much of the remaining body will be robotic. One thing to note is that if you choose to cover your entire head with the robot helmet, you will not be able to make your head anything less than fully robotic. Thus, if you choose to look like a full robot, you will have to be a full robot. Other than that, however, you can choose freely. You could have your entire head except your face enclosed in the helmet and have your head be mostly human except the covered parts, or you could have it be fully robotic. You could not wear any of the helmet and still make your head fully robotic despite your head looking like your human appearance, or you could choose to have your be fully human besides some modifications to work with your robot body. You could choose to wear the rest of the head except the face and hair, and still have your head be fully human or fully robotic. So, what will you choose?”

    [Author’s note: for the options, I excluded the option of paying up front but working anyway just because I thought it excessive. If someone else wants to have Emily ask to be totally reprogrammed and act as a robot servant in the store, or have her be sold off, feel free to add that option. Also, for reference, the options mean:
    - Job: she gets the job
    - Servitude: She works in the store 24/7
    - Up front: Emily pays up front
    - Human Face: Emily wears the rest of the helmet but leaves her face showing
    - Human Face and Hair: Emily wears the helmet but leaves her hair (in a bun) and face exposed
    - No Helmet: Emily doesn’t wear the helmet
    - Helmet: Emily wears the whole helmet (Robot and Cyborg will not be listed since by default it only makes sense for this path to be Robot, so unless someone else decides to make it a Cyborg path, there is no need to list it as Robot)
    - Robot: Emily’s entire body becomes robotic, no matter what her external appearance may suggest (if any part of the head is left exposed, it is synthetic and anything underneath is totally mechanical)
    - Cyborg: Emily’s head is left human, but the rest of her body becomes robotic]
