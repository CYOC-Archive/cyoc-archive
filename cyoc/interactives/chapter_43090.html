    Soon enough, it was dark out. You reached into the ring box and pulled out the ring. You slipped it onto the ring finger on your right hand and, much to your amazement, found it fit perfectly.

    You walked over to your window and opened the blinds. High up in the night sky, the quarter-moon shone down on you. You read the spell aloud and for a second felt like a complete idiot until you saw the eyes of the wolf on the ring flash brightly for a second. You start to tremble in excitement. Oh my god, this is really happening, you think. You feel a huge chill radiate over your body, followed by a tickling sensation. You reach up with one of your hands and feel your face. It feels rough and you realize it's from the stubble growing on your face. You hold your hands out and realize you have the same thing up your arms and all around your body.

    You dash out of your room and across the hall to the bathroom. By the time you get there, the fur has grown out at least an inch. You notice your fingernails have started to grow out into claws. You make a big, toothy smile and realize that your teeth have grown to sharp fangs.

    You pull off your shirt and see that the fur has grown all over your body, quickly forming a think, luscious coat of coarse fur. You look down at your feet and see your toenails have grown out into sharp, slightly curved claws, just like your fingernails. Your feet start to grow and reform, becoming slightly digigrade.

    You look at yourself in the mirror again. Your nose has grown darker and the end looks more canine and your ears have grown to points. You lips have also grown darker. As soon as it started, everything stops. You admire yourself, covered with brown fur with a hint of muscle definiton, wearing only a pair of blue jeans.

    "Wow," you mutter, with a deeper more gutteral voice. After a moment of taking it in, you throw your head back and let out a loud howl.

    This was definately the best $10 you ever spent. In the back of your mind, you think, God, I can't wait for the full moon.
