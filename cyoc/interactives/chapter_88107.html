    "Well, I've always wished I was..." Nate started to say when there was a knock at the prop room door. Nate's heart leapt in surprise as he watched the door open. His mind raced as he tried to think of an excuse for the six foot talking anthro bunny in the room. He didn't have time as he watched his best friend Jamie walk into the room. Jamie and Nate had been friends since they were kids.

    "Nate?" called out Jamie as she walked in. She spotted Nate in the room, standing beside a large Bunny. Confused, she walked over. "Mr. Bergstrom said you were in here. What's going on?"

    Nate decided to just tell the truth. "Well, you see the top hat on the floor there. Well, this guy was in imprisoned in it. This is Xander, he's a Rabbit Genie."

    Jamie couldn't help but look surprised. "Really?"

    "Yep, it's true. Although it's not something I tend to enjoy advertising." Xander gave Nate a glare.

    "He was just about to give me a wish because I freed him from the hat."

    "Is that so?" asked Jamie. Nate had to admit, she was taking this well.

    "Indeed it is," replied Xander, walking over to Jamie. "Although, I have gotten a better idea. I'm going to give you a wish, Jamie. A specific wish to transform my new friend here in any way you want."

    "What?" asked Nate, shocked.

    "Yeah, what?" asked Jamie.

    "Well, just look at our friend here and wish for him to transform somehow. It'll be fun."

    "What if I don't like it?" asked Nate.

    "Oh, come now. I am a Genie. I have an instinct for honing in on hidden desires and one of yours is to be transformed unwillingly." replied Xander.

    Nate couldn't help but blush a little.

    "Ok, fair enough. I have to think..." Jamie paced a bit. "I got it!"

    "Ok, what is it?" Xander rubbed his hands together.

    "Genie, I wish..."
