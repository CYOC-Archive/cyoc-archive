    You Came To The Store To Become An Object & Be Sold By The Store

    You entered the store called Unique Inanimate R Us. You walked right up to the corner & we asked by the man behind the corner "How can I help you?" Unsure at first you asked "Is this the place you sell objects that were once people? The place where people come to become an object or buy an object that used to be human?" The store keeper replied with a smile. "Yes it is. How can I help you?" "I came here to become an object myself." You informed the shop keeper. "You do know that transformation into an object is permanent right? And you will regarded as nothing but an object from then on." The shop keeper informed you. "I know. I want to be an object." You told the store keeper firmly. "I'll get the paperwork. Wait here." The store keeper told you & went into the back. Soon he returned with a clipboard with some papers clipped to it. You read over the paperwork & explained that you agreed to your own freewill to become an object. That you understand the transformation into an object is permanent. That from then on you will be regarded as nothing but an object. You will never be called by name ever. That you after becoming an object agree to be become property of the store to be sold. Then there was a spot to write in what object you want to become & be transformed into. You wrote down what object you wanted to become. And then you signed your name. The store keeper took the clipboard back & read what object you wrote in. "Come on future object." You were instructed & you followed the store keeper. You were lead to a plain white room & air vents on the ceiling & a chute on the wall marked 'Incinerator'. The door opened with a decompressing sound. "Now after entering this room you are to strip completely naked & deposit all clothing & personal items in the chute to go down to the Incinerator to be incinerated." The store keeper instructed you coldly now. "Ok." You said nodding entering the room. "After you enter this room & strip naked & deposit all your clothes and personal items in the chute the vents in the ceiling will pump in the gas that will transform you into the object you desired. You do not need to breathe deeply or anything. Just breathe normally." You were told. "Ok." You said nodding. "Now I must leave you to initiate my part of this. I will no longer treat you as a human. You are just an object to me now; & I treat you accordingly. And soon you will be nothing but an object. But you will be aware, somehow able to see, hear & feel. But no one will care." The store keeper told you coldly & left you shutting the door on you which sealed with a sucking sound. This is it. You are about to become the object you desire to be. Your life as a human is ending forever.
