I blushed, drawing a bit of a blank, wondering why I didn't think it over before I suggested this idea. "T..type?" I muttered. Runt scoffed and rubbed his temples. "Mythical, animal based? I shook my head a bit, extending out one of my beefy arms. "Heh, maybe not that one!" I stopped him with that, not wanting to think of being just a blob. Anything that might remind me of "I Have No Mouth, and I Must Scream" doesn't gel well with me.
