    Reality-Altering, Wish-Granting Genie

    Try as she could, Emily could not decide on how she wanted to use her powers today. Whenever she was in a creative rut like this, Emily often wondered what others would do if they had her reality-altering powers. How would they alter reality differently to herself? That’s when Emily was struck with a brilliant idea. Why not do exactly that!? She could become a genie and grant people wishes to change reality!

    With a flash of her powers she changed reality and Emily Powell, the teenage daughter of Rebecca and Greg no longer existed. Instead there was Emina, the mythical Djinn who granted 3 reality-altering wishes to those that found her mystical lamp. Emina wasn’t like typical genies seen in movies & TV, who were limited by certain rules. With the extent of her powers, Emina could make anything her master’s heart desired come true, including making them immortal, actively changing the past or future and even affecting a person’s free will! Sure, she was a little mischievous and may twist a wish just to mess with her master, but Emina would typically honor her master’s wish word for word.

    “That’s step one complete. Now I gotta look the part,” Emina said, examining her current look. A teenage girl in modern day clothing wasn’t a proper look for a genie. With another flash of her powers, Emina’s entire appearance changed. She had physically aged herself up from a teenage girl to a young woman, making sure her womanly curves were properly enhanced. Her skin took on a more tanned complexion, becoming a creamy caramel color, while her blonde hair darkened to black to match. Her practical t-shirt and skirt completely disappeared, replaced with a purple silk bedlah top that showed off her midriff and matching salwar pants. Gold earrings, amulets and similar colored accessories adorned Emina as she completed her look.

    “Perfect. I look like a proper Djinn. *gasp* Even my voice changed to match!” Emina exclaimed, noticing the now Arabic accent she spoke with. Eager to get started, Emina shrunk herself inside her magic lamp and whisked herself away to a random part of the world where she would soon be discovered by a fateful person.
