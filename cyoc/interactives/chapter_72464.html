    Suddenly, without any warning, a long tail shoots out the back of Jennifer's dress.

    It had no fur, but it was obviously a cat tail, and two feet long!

    She looked at it fearfully, as did her friends.

    "Uh... Jen." Carrie started "You've got a tail."

    "I can see that!" Jennifer said, and then felt her ears getting pointy, moving to the top of her head to become large triangles.

    She felt them, and her nose flattened, and became a pink cat nose, and she got a split upper lip.

    "I gotta go nyow!" said, coving her mouth "See mew laterrrrow!" and she ran off faster than before.

    She got into the alley, and called the number Mike had given her on her cell.

    "Hello?" Mike answered.

    "What was in that drrrink?" she asked furiously "Mew did this to meow didn't mew?"

    "Ah, your intellect has already increased with your transformation - good - that means it's working like it's supposed to."

    "What?" she asked "The pill?" somehow her sense of taste had improved a great deal.

    "I'll be right over." and he hung up.

    She saw a limo pull up, and the door opened - he was there waiting for her.

    She ran inside, and sat facing him as the limo drove.

    "Why did mew do this?" she asked.

    "To improve you." he explained "Like it?"

    "Like it?" she asked, mad "I-" but she was interrupted by him scratching behind her right ear, which made her purr. She was now more relaxed.

    He then put a pink collar with a big bell on it.

    She soon found herself playing with it.

    "You are my pet now," he told her "and you're going to help me with your friends. Get them to take the other three pills I have, and once the
    four of you are my pet catgirls, you'll work for me at my company, and I'll pay you all in fish. Here's the first payment." he opened a can of tuna,
    and she licked every last bite, gaining slit pupils, pink paw-pads on her hands and feet, and retractable claws on her now wider fingers and
    toes. She smiled "Thank mew, my master." she said happily "I'll go get my friends and give them these pills. I know we'll all enjoy worrring forrr
    mew!"

    "You're welcome, my new pet." he said, stroking her back "Now be a good girl and get your friends, please."

    "Yes, master." and with that, the happy new catgirl went to give the pills to her friends. She was doing this all willingly - she wanted her friends
    to share her joy, but which one will Jennifer get to Join her first?
