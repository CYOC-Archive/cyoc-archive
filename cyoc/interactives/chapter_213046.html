    Uncle Buck was the epitome of fury and though I was proud of my bout of rebellion, small and ill-advised as it might have been.
    "You miserable bastard!" he eructed with rage. "My sexual pleasures will be none for weeks to come, or moons even. I'll be horny, mad with lust and out of my mind for carnal desires."
    He approached you, his muscles ready to lash at you physically.
    "You have only just joined our throng! We offered you a life of pure sexual enjoyment and a long, long one at that! Near immortality! This is how you return the goodness of my hear!" he said while choking back tears from the pain.
    You had to protest his unjust words.
    "You've offered me a curse, not a gift!" you shouted. 'I have been dragged down to being a horny bestial creature! A goat! Not even fully human! A goat is a lowly creature intent only on sex! I had a future! A life! Now I have only bestial lusts. Just one sole basic desire, to inflict sex on all that might cross my path! Where is the honor or greatness in that, I ask you?"
    The satyr leader just scowled and nursed his wounded manhood, so you continued. He might have seen the good sense held within your reasoning, after all.
    "Uncle Buck, you are despicable and I am glad you can't even indulge in your favorite activity! I want to be human again and not have sex as the sole pleasure and goal in life! It's demeaning and unworthy of someone like me!" you shouted out as the two strong male satyrs yanked my arms behind my back with a painful jerk.
    You grimaced but was careful not to let out a cry of pain. You still wanted to appear strong, even transformed into a satyr and at the mercy of these foul creatures. You meant what you had said. Sexual intercourse was pleasant, but to spend one's life bent upon this activity only and intent to solely pursue it with no other care, concern or passion... No thank you! If you were left to live with these satyrs, you'd succumb to their depraved lifestyle, that was a certainly so for the sake of your dignity as a human, you had to get away and find a way to return to your normal human body. At this, Uncle Buck seemed to ponder but then he grinned widely.
    "So... You really want to be a human again, do you?" asked Uncle Buck with an evil glint in his eye.
    You nodded eagerly.
    "Our goat-like sexual intercourse disgust you, as our kind and kin entirely?" asked Uncle Buck again.
    You replied with another nod.
    "You don't like sex in a satyr and equine style, do you? You want to be a human but the mere thought of sex is appalling to you? Even though human males are just as sex-crazed as we are? Even though humans are hypocrites? " said the sly satyr with a plan in mind, clearly.
    "I want to be away from here and be something other than you are! I want to indulge in my own sexual urges while respecting my female partner. Let me leave because I have nothing to do here! Even you must see that!" you said, hanging your head down.
    You felt defeated and exhausted. You were on the verge of tears but you did not care anymore. You had said what you had to. The rest was in the hands of fate and you hoped Uncle Buck, in spite of his flaws, would see reason or at least take pity on you.
    "Well, I see you point of view, human. You wish to be human again. You would prefer a human life. Good. That is your choice. You would enjoy virgins more than females with experience, perhaps? You wish to be something other than what we are? Fine," said Uncle Buck in a slow and sly manner.
    You did not know where this was going and hoped it would turn out for the best. Uncle Buck continued, though.
    "I see that your rebellion might cause a bit of trouble. For the sake of my continued peace and rule here I must make of you a perfect example for my own kin and people."
    "What?... What are you going to do?..." you stammered, frightened.
    Uncle Buck snapped his fingers and the two satyrs holding you seized your throat and forcibly opened your mouth, all while Uncle Buck smiled evilly, reached into his belt and took out a phial, which he uncorked and whose contents he poured down your throat. It was bitter and you couldn’t help but swallow it. Immediately the world started spinning and you fell to the ground, too weak to move. Uncle Buck laughed throatily and all other satyrs were silent, hushed in anticipation of what would happen next. You would have liked to know, but the world went black.
