    Your car's tyres screech to a halt on the tarmac outside your close friend Dave's house. You've been friends with him for about a year now, yet you still don't really know that much about him. You came as soon as you could after you saw that he was hiring at his new farm. You're in a spot of money trouble, as one could tell by the scrappy look of your Ford Focus. But hey, you hear that Dave pays well, so hopefully your friendship with him will help you get a foot in the door! You just need to talk to him first.

    You walk along the gravel and up the stone steps to Dave's enormous, three-storey house. A beautiful Ferrari is parked out front... How can he have the money for this? You knock on the glossy door, and await an answer.

    The door opens, and you see Dave. He's a scrawny young man, with short, black hair and a slight beige tint to his skin. "Hey, dude!" he says with a large grin. "Hi!" you reply. "I heard you were looking for some workers?" "Yes! You know, I thought you were going to apply!" Weird, you think. "Come round this way to the back, all my animals are out there. I'll show you."

    He brings you around to the back. "Just a sec," he says, before running into his house. There are so many animals out here... A lonesome pig lies in its pen... There are loads of chickens... A horse stands out in the field... A dog is sleeping in its kennel... Cows eat grass in their pasture... But one animal in particular interests you.
