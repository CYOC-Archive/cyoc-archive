    Before you can pick anywhere to go, you hear the sound of twigs snapping. Your head snapping up, you look to your left and see a very large tiger emerge from the trees. It's deep green eyes stare into yours. You gulp and begin backing away slowly, but hear more twigs snapping front behind you. Turning, putting your back to the tiger, you see a lioness emerge from the trees, staring at you as well. More snaps from other directions continue until you're being closed in on by the tiger and the lioness, a black panther, a cougar, a leopard, a cheetah, a jaguar, a lynx, a wildcat, and a Ragdoll house cat.

    You gulp again and look for any possible escape route, but see none. The cats close in until they're all standing around you in a circle. Somehow you know that all of them are female. They all stand around you, purring and cocking their heads at you. You just begin to think that maybe they're not going to attack you when all of them lunge forward suddenly, bearing their fangs, and all bite you on the legs. You scream as the pain hits you like a wave and you fall to the forest floor. All the cats step back, still surrounding you in a circle , and sit back to watch the show.

    The first thing you notice is that you're shrinking. Your clothes, in fact, are vaporized as you shrink and shrink, until you're not even eye level with the tigress's head. All your body hair falls out. You yell out in terror, but are shocked to hear your yell suddenly turn into a very high-pitched feline yowl. You clap your hands over your mouth, but are shocked as your hands pop and fuse together into three-toed paws. Your arms are shortened and your shoulder blades are pushed closer together, splaying your paws out in front of you. Your neck thickens and pushes out as your nose becomes triangular and merges with your mouth as they both push out into a muzzle. Your human teeth sharpen and become fangs. Your forehead is pushed back as your ears become triangular and move to the top of your head.

    You mewl in terror and try to run in between the cat's legs, but they move closer together, blocking you. Your stomach, chest, thighs, and butt swell out with baby fat. Your feet extend and inflate into tiny three-toed paws. Your legs shorten until they're exactly the same length as your arms (front legs?). Your hips merge with your butt and your hips widen. Your thighs attach to your stomach and you begin to totter, losing your balance. Mewling in terror, your front paws hit the forest floor, causing claws to pop out of your paws and a thick tail to shoot out of your rear and hang in between your thighs.

    Suddenly, your front and back legs become wobbly and you feel very weak. You whine as you collapse to the forest floor, as weak and helpless as a newborn kitten. You barley register that your fur hasn't grown yet as it does. It puffs out of your skin, getting very thick, zooming up your body, covering every inch from the tip of your tail to the end of your muzzle. Your eyes are squinted shut, but you have just enough time to see your fur to identify what you have become. Your whole world is plunged into darkness as your transformation into a newborn cat is complete. The other cats, seeing what species you have become, growl and yowl in disappointment and leave as your new mother picks you up in her muzzle and begins walking back to the place where she will raise you.
