    An idea shot into Max’s head as he sat up on the bed “oh I know what we can-“ but before he could finish his sentence there was a knock at the door.

    The twins both looked at each other confused “is that dad or mom or whatever they are, here already?” Asked Ricky.

    Max shrugged his shoulders “definitely, Mom said earlier that he’ll be over with our bags”

    Ricky got up to answer the door but before he could start walking Max grabbed his shoulder “hey I dare you to call him daddy” said Max.

    A blush was on Ricky’s face at the ridiculous dare “no way that’s super weird” he said with his arms crossed.

    Max smirked “he would think it’s normal plus if you do it, I’ll turn you back into a boy”

    A sigh passed Ricky’s lips as he’ll do anything to be a boy again “fine” he muttered before stomping his way to the door, he braced himself before turning the handle and opening the door, he closed his eyes as Ricky thought it would help with the embarrassment “hi daddy” he said in a fake happy voice.

    Ricky’s face had turned bright red and the boy at the doorway was quite shocked “well a girl has never called me that before” he said as he lowed his glasses a little to show off his emerald green eyes.

    Ricky crossed his arms and immediately got defensive after his embarrassing first encounter with this boy “what do you want?” Max was sitting in the background dying of second hand embarrassment.

    The boy than started leaning on the doorway “well I’m Tony, me and cousin are having a little party in our hotel room, nothing weird” he clarified “we got a bit of alcohol and a few party games if you and that girl your with is interested, we saw you two running around the hallways and we thought you two seemed like fun so we thought about inviting you two over, so are you two up for it”

    Ricky rolled his eyes as he was unimpressed with Tony “it’s a n-“ but before Ricky could finish his sentence, Max raced to the door and he put his arm around Ricky’s shoulder “what my sister was trying to say was we’ll think about it” Ricky just subtly stamped on Max’s foot.

    Tony smirked “you two are twins cool, well we’re in room 26 just up the hall if you two girls wanna come over”

    Max smiled “we’ll see, bye Tony” he said before closing the door.

    Immediately Ricky brushed Max’s arm off of his shoulder “what the fuck is wrong with you, why would you agree to that?”

    Max lifted up the device “I never agreed to anything, but it’ll be a great opportunity to use the Chronivac”
