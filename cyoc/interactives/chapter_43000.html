    Flee the Moon

    "NO," you scream, bolting upright.


    "Jen, Wait," cried Becky.

    "Relax." "Relax little one," said Mirela. "Jennifer is making a mistake, an understandable one, but she'll be all right I suspect. We will find her tonight."


    The next several minutes are a blur to you. You don't remember how but you flee in panic from your best friend and those two bastards with her, oblivious to their shouts. You run downstairs, barely stopping to open the door and to the bewilderment and amusement of the few patrons at the bar, you dash out fully naked into the lush pine woods.

    You'd always gotten A's in Physical Education at school, but so did everyone, you were never all that athletic. Yet with tears streaming down your face, you manage to bound over fallen trees and avoid stepping on pinecones with your bare feet.

    After several minutes, the haze of panic lifts from your mind and your return to consciousness startles you. You trip and fall naked onto the needle-coated forest floor.

    Your mind swims in fear over what has happened over the past few hours, fear that increases as you notice that the sun has set and the moon is now rising as darkness falls.

    Sitting on the ground you notice that your heart beating rapidly. A warm sensation is filling your body. You scream as the warmth rapidly increases into a blazing heat.

    It's as if your limbs are paralyzed. You can do nothing as the heat intensifies in your feet and hands. A horible crackling sound is made as they begin to stretch. As you throw back your head to scream in fear and throbbing pain, you feel your teeth changing as well. You taste blood as your perfect white teeth become cruel fangs.

    You look down at yourself as a pleasant tickling sensation envelopes your body. Multiple blonde hairs are sprouting from every follicle on your body and new follicles are forming that will give you a lush golden coat of fur.

    "No...please," you scream and realize that your voice is going. As your transformation continues, with your nails on your hands and feet being pushed out to make way for sharp claws, you realize that you are craving flesh and blood, a hunger that you'll have to satisfy.

    It's taken almost five minutes, but it is almost over. You realize that you'll be able to stand on two feet, though in a deep crouch...balanced with your fluffy tail. Thick golden fur covers your body and you can feel the slightest breeze in the air. Intense heat fills your face, followed by excruciating pain as your face extends into a furry muzzle. You howl in fear and pain as your transformation completes.

    You're fully conscious and horrified at what you are and you know that there is only one way to satisfy the bloodlust. You instinctively head towards the docks.

    It's pretty much as you remember it from last night as you crouch in the dense scrub. With the last of the passengers safely on land the same attendent begins to disrobe. His transformation doesn't take nearly as long as yours did, you wonder absently if you'll be able to do that before too long

    You growl a greeting to him as you approach the dock and he submissively reciprocates, acknowledging your status as an alpha female. You feel a swell of pride before you remember that you've been turned into a furry monster against your will, you really don't want to be a werewolf, and you really really don't want to eat human beings...but your hunger is growing intolerable. You notice that the wind is almost calm, the water would be an almost perfect mirror...

    But do you really want to see yourself now?
