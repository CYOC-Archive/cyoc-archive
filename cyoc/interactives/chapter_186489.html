    Franklin Feline was a natural-drawn toon living in a peaceful village nestled in a big forest, named Pine Park. He had clean white fur with gray stripes, which he took good care of, and his usual outfit was a big red bow tie and white gloves. Since he was a toon and covered in fur, he needed nothing else. He had a few good friends, Mike Mallard who lived in the pond, and Rachel Rodent, a tiny little mouse girl that Franklin didn't have the heart to eat. They ended up becoming pals instead, and Franklin protected her from other, meaner predators. Franklin also had an older sister, Farrah, and a mom and dad that were just named Mom and Dad. Franklin didn't question the logic.

    Every morning, Franklin would step out of his tree home and see a great big Y7 logo in the sky. This logo kept Franklin innocently unaware of the adult content that lie elsewhere in Toon World, though it did allow for plenty of cartoon violence and slapstick. Being a Main Character, Franklin was, unfortunately, on the receiving end of it most of the time. But there were no major consequences and he was always feeling fine after a new day. As for tourists, he rarely ever saw any visitors from the human world. Franklin was an unaired pilot from 1990 and only nerdy animation purists ever paid him a visit. He heard some rumors about the Toon World organizers "spicing up the content" to get more tourists, but he never knew what they meant, until one morning...

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Franklin awoke to Ron Rooster squawking his head off, as he does every morning. That bird never lets anybody sleep in. Franklin threw his blanket off and stood up... and was astonished at what he noticed between his legs. A large, pink sausage thing sticking straight out in front of him, with a furry, leathery sack dangling below it. This certainly wasn't part of him yesterday! He rubbed it with his paw to see if it was real, and it was. In fact, rubbing the sausage felt really good in a way he couldn't describe. He kept on rubbing it as he went downstairs, eager to tell his family about the change. They were already downstairs, and from the thrilled looks on their faces, they had noticed it too. Mom had a new pair of furry protrusions on her chest complete with nipples, which poked out over her apron. Dad had a sausage even huger than Franklin's dangling from under his flannel shirt. Even Farrah had removed the pink shirt and skirt she always wears, revealing an open slit where Franklin's sausage would be.

    Franklin's jaw dropped with amazement and he dropped his new appendage. Dad was the first one to break the silence. "Well, what do you think of our new look?"
    Franklin managed to get his composure. "I've never seen anything like it!"
    Mom gave him a sweet smile. "I can't wait to try it out. Maybe I can keep small objects in this little slit for safe keeping."
    "I gotta show everybody! I wonder if everybody else has changed too?"

    Franklin steps outside and soon hears a commotion in the forest. Sounds like everybody else has woken up to something new as well. In the sky, there was a big "X" logo hanging over them. What did X mean? There weren't many words that started with X. That's another thing he was curious about.
