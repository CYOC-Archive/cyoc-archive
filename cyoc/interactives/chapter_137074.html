She doesn't stick around to find out, though, surprising her leggy parent with a sprint away--and out the door. "COME BACK HERE YOUNG L-" is all Jen hears before the door slams behind her, sweat starting to pour off of her. Huh... sweat? She's barely even started running! And then she hears a honk--oh, uh, shit, that's the schoolbus. She shudders, HARD, as something sweeps over her, but she ignores it and runs onto the bus, closing her eyes to try and ignore the feeling.

    Miraculously she gets on without looking where she's going, and then manages to seat herself without looking. Which is good, because she's having trouble opening her eyes. She'd bring a hand up to her eyes to see what's wrong, but she doesn't have that, either. A hand, that is. But she has eyes! She didn't two seconds ago, but now she does.

    ...the hell?

"Oh, Jen!" announces Melissa, startling her. "Who did this to you? Here, let me just..." She feels some hands on her hips as her closest friend, Melissa, pinches her skirt... and pulls it up. And Jen doesn't feel it cross any other parts of her body. It just comes off.

    Jenny blinks, now able to see her friends face through the narrow viewing area she has. She opens her mouth to speak, but just feels something kinda... flex. She blinks again. "Was it your sister again? Always the prankster, isn't she?" Melissa raises her hand up to Jen's face and pats the weird, rounded walls still obscuring her vision. It feels kinda like she's being pat on the...

    Jen doesn't want to come to any conclusions, because accepting what has happened to her might make her have a breakdown.

    But... and while she could be wrong... she... thinks she's an ass on legs. And her eyes are, uh, between her own... yeah. She can't feel anything above her hips, and her mouth is definitely not her mouth, so this is what she's been reduced to?! The walking booty curses up a storm in her own head.
