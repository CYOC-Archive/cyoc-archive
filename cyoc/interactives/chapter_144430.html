    God snapped their fingers and we were in a circular room with 6 sections, each with a large wall full of hourglasses. In the middle of the room seemed to be a sort of control panel. God said "Alright we are currently in the time room. With six sections wanna guess what the purpose is?"

    Zhang raised his hand, got called on, then said/thought "One Prophet for each section from each continent with a significant amount of human life. These hourglasses probably measure the age of human life."

    God said "Correct, small thing I want to tell you all. There is an infinite amount of sand in every hourglass. You don't die from being too old, but from the limitations of the human body. Any questions?"

    You raised your hand and asked "Can we mess with them?"

    God said "Of course, why would I separate them into six sections if I didn't want you to use them. I should've explained this earlier, but the reason I'm bringing you six prophets in is so more people will understand that a God exists at all. You all should fit into my plan so... When this tour is over i'll show you how to use everything here anywhere. So i'll give you some time to figure out how to find people and how to manipulate the hourglasses. Go on my children."

    You all went to a random station, but ended up finding the right station. Not sure where to start you thought about your own hourglass and found that it was a glow none of the others had, and the sand was not moving. You tried gesturing the sand in the bottom of the glass rise and it did, and you felt your body shrink as more and more sand rose. As you stopped the sand from rising you were a little kid again. You let the sand fall again till you were a 25 year old man. Deciding to look somewhere else you had found an hourglass with barely any sand on the bottom. Curious you tapped the top of the hourglass and saw the sand start to drain down faster and an image came into your mind. You saw a teenager in a bathroom with her mother looking at a pregnancy test saying "I... we... our living circumstances... I have to get an abortion I th-" Her mother anticipating it was about to say some of wisdom she had gotten through the years before her daughter was groaning in pain as her womb was expanding as the few cells was quickly turning into a fetus, which was quickly turning into a baby, which quickly came out of her to which you took your hands off the hourglass and the image faded. Note to self, figure out who exactly those people were and help. A few moments later you all had probably messed with someone and god called you all back to the middle.

    God said "Alright, this is your time control panel. If you all get bored of the current time period if you all agree you can mess with this to make time skip forward or fast forward to whenever you want, or if you all see some giant mistake has been made you can just rewind and solve it or not make the mistake yourselves. As this affects the whole universe you all have to agree to use it for a specific purpose. You don't have to use it now, but it's just a very powerful tool."

    You spoke out and said "How about we test it by rewinding back a few minutes?"

    Marie said "What did you mess up bad? I see you made yourself a little older."

    You told them "There was a very new hourglass so curiously I tapped on it and... A mother who just found out she was pregnant... gave birth. She was saying she needed to get an abortion so... Wait what's your stance on abortion god?"

    A couple of the other Prophets were interested and God said "I'm against it unless the quality of life for all involved are drastically hurt because of it. That said with you Prophets that probably should happen a lot less so..." they shrugged.

    Carlos said "Well then... I'd say let's not rewind and then Steve you can help give them whatever they need to be happy."

    You sighed and said "Fine, that works. So god is that all there is for Time Central?" God nodded and then brought you all to the next area...
