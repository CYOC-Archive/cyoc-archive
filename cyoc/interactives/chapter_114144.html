    Your time as a sheep

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago A

    During your journey you often fantasized about being a sheep, but nothing could match the reality of it. You are struggling to walk on your four hooves, with limbs limbs not properly adjusted for such movement. You still feel like a woman, in your mind at least, but your body didn't agree. You cautiously investigate your stall and you recognize with anger, that you aren’t able to open the gate anymore. You try to speak, but all that comes out is a low "Baah! Baa-aa-aah!"
    Fortunately your sheepish cry called the employee from before. “Something wrong, little sheep,” the man asked as he opened the door and saw that you’re a little bit confused, “Don’t worry, everything will be alright one you get outside.” He started to pick up your clothes. As he lifted your boots and jeans your lingerie is dropped on the bottom. With a mischievous smile he picked them up and dropped them to your other clothes into a plastic bag: "I think in next time you won’t have any use of them”. If you still would have been human you would have blushed in this embarrassing situations.
    Hasty you scurry past the man and run out the doorway and out onto the grazing field of the farm. However, before taking another step, you felt the desire to eat the grass. You casually bent down your head and began eating with your large new teeth. It’s absolutely delicious. You spend the next few hours mindlessly munching the grass. Soon as the dark rise you and your herd are forced back into your stall by an annoying shepherd dog. You hear a gate click behind you, locking you in again. You watch, more bored than curious, as a farmhand walk to you. You flinched as an awful pain hit your ear. With anger you recognized that a yellow tag was attached to your ear. As you try to bite the man he laughed “It’s just a tag with your name “Kimi” on it. Or do you like to get confused with a simple sheep and end up on our dinner table?”
    With horror you recognize the first time the fact, that you are completely dependent on the mercy of humans during your stay as a full animal. You fast put that thought aside. You’ll enjoy your stay and next week you’ll be an attractive woman again and enjoy your human life. Go out for shopping or go out for dinner with a handsome guy. But for now you’re a sheep enjoying simple life.
    Without worries in human concerns you spend the next week. In the morning you’re let out to pasture, where you enjoy the delicious grass, as well as the company of the other sheep and at night you sleep in your comfortable stable.
    Then the day come you feared to come. You have to change back and leave the island.
    "Hey Kimi! How are you?” greeted you the employee you get the transformation serum from. “ Now that your stay is over, it's my duty to change you back – if you want. You could also stay, but then you have to work for your stay. Maybe you want to have a family and kids…or let’s say lamps. You can sign in for a certain time…or if you want...for forever. We would certainly find a beautiful farm on the mainland for you. How do you decide?”
