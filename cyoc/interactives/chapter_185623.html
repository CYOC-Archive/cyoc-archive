But ultimately, because we didn't know in the mutation of the were virus when Kellie got bit.     "My dad won't understand that," you sighed, "he might even be more puzzled with you than with me. He might just accept that I'm weird and leave it at that..."

    "Well, the condition of my change was more accidental than deliberate," Roxie answered, "that I can explain..."

    You then nodded. Perhaps Roxie could help explain things to your father on how that change worked and how it affected males that became MILF-wolves. You privately didn't want to admit that you might find yourself attracted to men, but if what Roxie said was true and that she became bi-sexual because the mothering instincts that MILF-wolves had, and a big part of how and why they got their reference to term "MILF" or "Mother I'd Love to Fuck," it would be quite possible that you'd end up in the same condition, but for the moment that wasn't your concern.

    "Thank you," you answered, "and I think I would like everyone's help. Get everything I need and make things easier."

    "Then let's go," Roxie commented, "it may not be super late, but we don't want to interrupt anyone getting ready to go to bed."
