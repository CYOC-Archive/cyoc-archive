    added 4 days ago AR O Mental Toddler Baby

    "Let's go to the playground," Leah said. She grinned at Tonya. "I'll be the baby and you'll be the babysitter."

    "Are you sure?"

    "Tonya, I just pooped and didn't realize. I need diapers," Leah sighed. "And I refuse to dress like normal with a big diaper bulge! Or, worse yet, dress like normal, people recognize me, and then I fill my diaper!"

    "Okay, okay!" Tonya said. "Calm down. We'll do what you said. But first, you need some clothes." Leah's mess had been so big, it got onto her cute overalls. She was sitting in just a diaper.

    "Yeah." Leah put her pacifier in her mouth. "Why don't you choose?"

    "Um, okay." Tonya got up and went to where Leah's babyish clothes were kept. She selected an adorable ruffled pink dress and matching diaper cover. When she got back to Leah, her friend was laying on her back, happily sucking her pacifier without a care in the world.

    "The playground is in walking distance," Leah said after she dressed herself. "But walking is a bit difficult with this." She squeezed her diaper, which pushed her legs apart, giving her a distinctive toddle.

    "Want me to drive, then?" Tonya asked. Leah shook her head.

    "I have a better idea." She grabbed Tonya's hand and brought her to the garage. A stroller was sitting by the wall, the perfect size for Leah. "Think you can push me in that?"

    "Duh!" Tonya laughed, trying to hide how weird she felt about all this. In a few minutes, Leah was buckled into the stroller, the diaper bag was placed in the basket of the stroller, and Tonya set off.
