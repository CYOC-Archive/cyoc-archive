    Race Back To The Village!

    "Everybody nya! The Dark Army is heading for the Leaf Village nya! They trampled Tanzaku nya! Naruto sensei, you're in charge here nya! What do we do nya?!" yelled Jen.
    "I don't know anything about this army but Jen, you should go to Tanzaku and search for survivors. We'll go to the village ahead of you and warn the old lady." said Naruto.
    "Won't they attack me nya?" asked Jen.
    "The Dark Army doesn't work like that. They're programmed to respond to hostileness, like bees. If you ignore them, they won't attack." said Domon.
    "Right nya." said Jen. She flew off towards Tanzaku town.
    "I guess we're on our own now." said Rain. "Now if I can just get this cockpit back into Burning Gundam."
    "I can lift it." said Link.
    "What," said Domon, "a little kid like you? Lift that?"
    "Why don't you give the kid a chance?!" yelled Kuwabara.
    "Seriously, how can you lift it?" asked Yusuke.
    "It's not that difficult." said Link. He walked over to the cockpit and tried to lift. "Hmm, it's heavier than my bracelet can handle." He reached into his magic pouch and pulled out a mask. He put it on and transformed into a Goron*. "Okay, here I go." said Link, his voice a little deeper. He lifted the cockpit with one hand. "Where do you want it?" he asked.
    "Forward, forward, turn a little to the left, there!" said Rain.
    "Good. Ooh, granite! My favorite!" said Link. He ran over to a boulder and began eating it.
    "Whoa, that kid is tough." said Kuwabara.
    "Actually, I'm a goron right now. Gorons eat rocks." said Link. "Sometimes the instincts of the spirit in a mask take over. Besides, I was hungry."
    "C'mon guys! I finished Burning Gundam. All those riding get on the corelander.
    "Okay." said Link. He took off the mask and returned to normal. He put the mask back in his pouch and got on the corelander. Kuwabara grabbed the pouch.
    "How much stuff you got in this thing?" asked Kuwabara. He turned the bag upside down and a pile of weapon's, masks, and other things came out. "Wow, and it seemed so empty. Hey, what's this?" Kuwabara picked up a green jewel.
    "Hey, don't touch that!" yelled Link. "It's the sacred Kokiri Emerald."
    "What about this white one?" asked Kuwabara.
    "Oh that? That jewel is Din's Fire. The power that was in it allows me to do this." said Link. He held out his hand. A flame appeared floating above his palm.
    "Cool, hey there's two more fire dun things." said Kuwabara.
    "Those are Farore's wind and Nayru's Love. Farore's Wind allows me to teleport. Nayru's Love, you already saw that. It protected me from Jen's attack, remember?" said Link.
    "Wait, you can telport?!" yelled Sam. "Can you take us all to the Village Hidden in the Leaves?"
    "No, it only works when I'm underground. I also have to cast a travel point. But if Jen comes back I might be able to. I just have to find that book. It has all the Triforce Seals in it. AHHHH!!! No, not here!" yelled Link. He was grabbing his Triforce Mark.
    "What's wrong kid- AHH!" yelled Domon. "Okay, where did that come from. Huh, my King of Hearts Crest? It hurts!"
    "I thought that only happens when something happens to a Shuffle Alliance Member. Huh? What's that?" said Rain. She pointed to something falling from above, as if being thrown from a long distance.
    "It's Jen!" yelled Sam. Jen crashed in front of the corelander, holding something in her hand.
    "She's got the Triforce of Power!" yelled Link. "Jen! What happened to you?!"
    "L... Link, I... got it... back... for you nya. He's here." said Jen. She passed out.
    "Help me get her onto the corelander!" yelled Link. Yusuke and Link got her onto the floor of the corelander. She woke up an hour later.
    "We can't go to Tanzaku nya! We'll all die nya!" she yelled.
    "Calm down and tell me what happened!" said Link.
    "I... was searching... for survivors nya..."


    * A goron is a race of rock eating creatures that can withstand incredibly high tempuratures. Their all rock diet maks them very strong. They are frequently used as characters in the Legend of Zelda game series.
