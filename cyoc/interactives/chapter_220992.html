    Before any conversations could begin, a young woman who looked to be Wyatt's age came in wearing a regal purple silk dress. She had a cloth covering one side of her breast where a baby could be seen breast feeding on her.

    "Wyatt, what is the commotion in....here. Oh, I see your brother is back again, and he's brought a guest in our home. Greetings and Salutations to you...your lordship I presume by your attire." The lady said in a curtsy and bow to the kitsune lord.

    "Indeed it is. I presume you are the vampire lord concubine?" Lord Tatsu assumed from the baby on her breast.

    "Oh, I can see why you would assume that, but no my lord. I am Lady Kyra Tepes, the wife and partner to Wyatt. I was once human like many here in our home, but I am a full blooded vampire and this child as with my other four are mine which I carried and birthed for my husband." Lady Kyra explained.

    "You carried and birth five children? And yet you are a vampire." Lord Tatsu said in surprise.

    "Indeed, many are surprised to hear that when I tell them. But yes, I gave birth to five damphir children, all heirs to the Tepes throne. Andrew, Brendan, Duncan, named after a deceased member by way his daughter, and of course my daughters, Andromeda, and Nyx here." Kyra said as she finished breast feeding her daughter and showed her to the Kitsune lord.

    "Astounding. My congratulations to you and to your family." Lord Tatsu said.

    "Yes, now, perhaps you would care to join me for some tea as my husband and....brother in-law," Kyra said with noticing disgust in her voice. "have their little chat. I'd be most pleased in discussing the kitsune and vampire territories with you. My husband and I had planned on expanding our territory a little but we needed consulting with someone on the Kitsune areas, we wouldn't want to intrude on your land."

    "Indeed, I must say, Lady Kyra you are certainly wise, Lord Wyatt has chosen a worthy queen. Akira, you and young Duke discuss your matters I will be having tea with the Lady Kyra." Lord Tatsu said as he and Lady Kyra left to enjoy a nice chat and tea.

    Duke watched as Lord Tatsu left with Kyra on friendly terms, while Wyatt seemed to have a bit of temper, he could clearly see that Kyra had come a long way since prom and was very good at negotiations.
