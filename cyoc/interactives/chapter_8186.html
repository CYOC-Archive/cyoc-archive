It was strange to see the changes from a viewers perspective, and Mike had never seen it really happen before. It was an odd sight, so Mike was fixated.

Mike watched as Ty began to look like his own 180 pound frame from day's before. A small stomach, and un-noticable chest. He was big, but not in a way most would notice.
