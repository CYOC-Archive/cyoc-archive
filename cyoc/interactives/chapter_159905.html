    Arcade Experience

    added by LuisJM 3 years ago BM I

    Looking through his options on turning into console gaming, Jeff thought of going a bit further. While turning into a video game console is an interesting experience, he was more into playing arcade games, that includes both old school and new school. He and his family does live close to the shore and then they visit, he always go to the arcade to play some good games. Hell, he knows his patterns of certain games as well. If he turned into one of those arcade cabinets, he might know what it feels like to be played with 24/7. And with the games turned on, he is always turned on in a sexual way every day. So with that said, he decided to turn into an arcade cabinet. But the question is, which arcade cabinet would he turn into. There is a wide variety of games past and present and it's difficult to figure out where to begin. So what can he choose.
