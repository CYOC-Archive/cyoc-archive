    As Bradford was dealing with his new life as a mare, learning to handle being animal and female, as well as living through heat and attempting vainly to resist the advances of healthy studs, not to mention getting pregnant, the lamp you inhabited was picked up by someone else. Bradford, or now Star as his name was, presently had vocal chords which allowed no human speech so for him, or rather her, to make a new wish was a slim chance. Looked like you would have a new master. Actually, you smile as think that tricking people with their own wishes is incredibly fun. Playing with words and meanings that way was extremely entertaining! You are impatient to continue doing so. After all, humans are so naive and stupid... You should know, you were one not too long ago...

    Eventually, the lamp get picked up, examined and (finally!) rubbed. You get out and you see a guy in shady clothing in front of you. You're in a filthy derelict apartment. You grimace. The guy clearly is clearly a delinquent. A criminal, no doubt, and with loose morals and probably an addict and an alcoholic. Great... Couldn't you appear in a five-star hotel instead? There were stashes of drugs and wads of certainly illegal money lying around too.
    "Who... Who're you?" blurted stupidly the man.
    "A genie, genius," you said with all the scorn you could muster into your tone.
    "So... You grant wishes and all?"
    "Yes. Three wishes. So think wisely because the consequences might be..."
    "Too bad you're not a hot chick with big boobs!" the criminal cut you.
    Oh, that one was just charming... You were not going to refrain misinterpreting his words on purpose, with this one... And he was so idiotic that it would be incredibly easy for sure... You just had to get rid of him as fast as possible.
    "Well, I'm a man, as you can see. So what are your wishes?"
    "I want to be infinitely rich, have a big harem and to be a real hottie who has a lot of pussy!" the man says without waiting.
    And without thought. You were right, this was going to be great fun! You'd make sure to get a show to see that jerk's new life once back in your lamp. You smile widely, savoring in advance this idiot's punishment.
    "As you wish!" you smirked.
    The man laughed like a madman. He would get this smile wiped from his face very, very quickly.
