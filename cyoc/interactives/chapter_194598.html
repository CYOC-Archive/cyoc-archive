    Jeff Is No Name For A Dog... Let's Call You "Spike"

    Jeff waits patiently, after a hour of waiting he see someone approach the back door, it is a boy in his age, grinning as he notices Jeff sitting there. Jeff does not notice as he starts to wriggle his bum like a excited puppy when it wags it's tail violently.

    “Good boy,” the stranger says after opening the door.

    “I... I know you,” Jeff says, “we... I saw you at a hotel... I think”.

    Draco nods, “yep, my name is Draco,” the boy says, “but for you I am your master”.

    “Why... why are you doing this to me,” Jeff asks, “I am not a dog...”.

    Draco smirks, “you soon will be,” he says, “and for why I am doing it: I want you for myself. I am gay and as I saw you I fell in love with you. But once I realized that you are straight, I got mad, I have hacked your computer and I have been watching you day and night”.

    Jeff looks at Draco in disbelief, “y-you are stalking me,” he asks, “why not accept that I am into girls,” he continues to ask.

    “Because, when I set my mind on something I can't be talked out of it,” Draco says, “when I hacked you earlier and saw that you have the Chronivac, I saw my chance to finally get you for my own”. Draco chuckles, “you'll get used to it, boy,” he says, “I can't call you “Jeff” anymore, from now on your name will be Spike, understood,” he asks sternly.

    Jeff nods sadly, Draco smiles and Jeff watches as the boy takes off a back pack, he opens it and pulls out a collar and leash.

    “I bought this collar for you,” Draco says, he leans down and fastens the collar around Jeff's/Spike's neck. He then fastens the leash to the collar, “come, Spike,” he says, tugging on the leash.

    Jeff finds himself obeying as he stands up and starts to walk, “no, bad dog,” Draco says, “dogs walk on all fours, so get down,” he commands.

    Jeff actually lets out a whimper, “b-but it is so uncomfortable,” he says.

    “Now,” Draco says stearnly, Jeff whimpers as he gets down onto his hands, Draco smiles and pats Jeff's head. “Good boy,” he says, “that wasn't so hard now, was it,” he asks, Jeff stays silent, grinning Draco leads Jeff to his car and opens the trunk. “Get in,” he orders, Jeff hesitates, knowing that he'll never see his family again, but he knows he has no choice, so he obeys. Draco closes the trunk and walks back to the house, after a few minutes, Jeff/Spike sees Draco return with a huge box. Jeff watches as Draco loads the box into the car, “you won't be needing these things anymore,” he says. Draco then climbs into the driver's seat and starts the engine, “now we have an appointment...,” he turns around to look at Jeff, “an appointment with the vet,” he says, grinning evilly.
