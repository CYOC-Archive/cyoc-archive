Ebony looked down at the ground; at his feet. "Sorry I killed you last night." The dragon sorrowful. "What?!" You yelled shocked by what he said. The dragon walked up to you & put his 3 digited clawed hand through you. You saw his hand past through you; like you weren't solid. Like you were a...oh no. "I'm a ghost? I'm dead?" You asked shocked. Ebony pulled his hand back & he slowly nodded. "I'm so sorry. I was so hungry. I couldn't help myself." He said. Trying to wrap your head around being...a ghost...dead you asked him to explain everything. The dragon explained that last night you came home from work & he attacked you from above; he was flying. That he plasma breathed you killing you. Then he ate you. "Why...did... you...kill & eat me? Why?" You asked very confused. The dragon looked down at his feet again. "Frankly I have been wanting to eat you sense I first caught your scent. A bit after I hatched actually. But you were too big for me to eat intill recently. Intill my growth spurt a few days ago." He explained. It was true intill 5 days he was smaller than you. He grew about 5 foot in those days. You thought about this for a bit. After about 20 minutes Ebony finally spoke again. "You mad?" The dragon asked. You shook your head no. There was silence for another 10 minutes. "You wanted to eat me for most of your life?" You asked curious. The dragon nodded with a "You smelled so good." You questioned curious "Did I taste good?" Ebony slowly nodded with a weak "Yes." "What happened to my remains?" You asked wondering. "Far from here." He answered. "You should of told me you wanted to eat me." You said. Ebony looked at you "What?" He asked. "I would of let you eat me." You told him. The dragon replied with a "What?" You answered explaining "I ordered your egg thinking it was an alligator egg. I intended to hatch it & have the alligator eventually devour me." Ebony yelled shocked "You wanted to be eaten?!" You nodded explaining "I have had a desire for years to be eaten by a reptile. That's why I got what I believed to be an alligator egg. Which infact turned out to be a dragon egg; your egg. You Ebony." Ebony turned away from you "You wanted to be eaten by a reptile. And I'm a reptile." "Yes. And I thank you for fulfilling my desire." You then saw the black winged dragon shaking. The dragon then spun back around & yelled in rage "If I had known you wanted to be eaten by a reptile I would of eaten you a long time ago! Damn you for not telling me!" "Sorry. I didn't know you wanted to eat me." You apologized; not sure if you should of. Some reason that caused the black dragon to laugh. "I could of ate you a long time ago. If I had only I had known." You laughed too saying "If only we had told each other." "Guess you aren't my master anymore. Because you are dead." The dragon stated. "Guess not." You said nodding.
