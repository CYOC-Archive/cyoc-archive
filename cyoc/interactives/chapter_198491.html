    In a research facility across the country, the scientist, Denise, was conducting her own experiments while keeping in contact with Alex.

    “Interesting, you managed to get at least major transformations in one facility? Even seeing one is rare…” Denise said on a call.

    “It’s rare, unless you manipulate the environment in a way to trigger a transformation no matter what,” Alex responded. “At least that’s what we managed to conclude today.”

    “It’s a good thing you let me know in advance. We are prepared in this facility too. Just send me some of your research, and we’ll get some test subjects ready ourselves. The wave should hit our facility in three hours”

    Within two hours, there were already 5 naked subjects, strapped and ready for testing. They tried to resist, but found themselves helpless and drugged.

    “You bitch! Let me out!” A male subject in one room shouted.

    Denise walked over and identified the subject.

    “Ah, Brandon. You ruined my dress in our high school prom by spilling soda all over it. Then ruined a completely different dress in a New Years party. I never forgot or forgave you,” Denise said.

    “Those were years ago… let me out! What are you trying to do to me…” Brandon shouted.

    Denise simply laughed, and then undressed.

    “We can’t choose the transformation, but with the research that has been conducted, we can manipulate how significant the transformation will be. And I have high hopes for yours in particular…”

    Brandon continued to struggle while Denise laughed.

    And within a few minutes, the wall hit the facility.

    Brandon immediately felt buzzing from the wall. “Oh no!”

    Denise laughed, as she felt nothing when the wall passed her. She continued to laugh harder when she saw what was happening to Brandon.

    Brandon’s skin started to change color. It went from a pale color into a sparkly scarlet, with sequins forming all over it.

    “No please! Help!” Brandon pleaded. But it was too late.

    His body began to lose shape, going from a humanoid shape into a flat blob of fabric and sequins. As Denise walked over to investigate, she was shocked to see the blob stretch out and attach to her.

    The blob that was formerly Brandon connected to her breasts, with 2 little arms forming from it. The arms both flung over her right shoulder, and reattached itself to the blob. The rest of the blob started to seep down, becoming flatter and flatter. As the blob began to reach Denise’s hips, it wrapped around behind her, beginning the shape of a skirt. The blob continued to seep down, until it touched the ground.

    The blob that was formerly Brandon officially turned into a one-strap expensive fancy scarlet dress.

    Denise walked over to a mirror and admired herself.

    “Hm, the research was right. Not only can you manipulate the odds of a major transformation, but we can manipulate the type of transformation too. I was lying when I said we can’t choose the transformation. The transformation tends to be ironic, and by pointing out that you ruined my dresses, it became ironic that you would turn into my dress. Now let’s see how the other subjects turned out…” Denise laughed.

    Denise walked over to the next room. There, she had two subjects that also angered her years ago. When she strapped the two subjects, she pointed out that the two girls bullied her back in school for the clothes she wore. She specifically pointed out the time they made fun of her shoes.

    When she walked in, she found two fancy heels, that matched her new dress perfectly.

    “Wow that one was a stretch and it still worked. And the fact that it matched with Brandon’s transformation is surprising. I’m really gonna celebrate tonight after I communicate the results with Alex.”

    She went to check on the other two subjects. She didn’t expect that these would also become clothes, but who knew at this point.
