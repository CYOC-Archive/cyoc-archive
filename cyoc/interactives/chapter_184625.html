    Christina the schoolgirl

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago AR O

    Miss Evert smiled sternly as she handed Christina a classic catholic schoolgirl uniform. The clothes were still neatly folded and wrapped up in plastic. Christina somewhat reluctantly rips the plastic open and lays the various clothing articles on a nearby, mostly clear table to inspect them. A knee-length plaid skirt, long black stockings made of thick cotton, a classic long sleeved white shirt, a striped tie and a red sweater vest with a prestigious looking school insignia emblazoned on the left side of the chest, right over the spot where the breast would be. Miss Evert also brings a pair of black shiny mary-jane style shoes and places them on the table.
    "So you want me to dress up as a college girl or something?" Christina asks the older woman.
    "Not college, high-school student my dear." Miss Evert points out while poking Christina in the chest with a bony finger. "Now I can see that you look very youthful already for a fully adult lady Christina, but I want to test the age-altering properties of this costume. Now get dressed."
    Slightly hesitant about this, Christina does as she's told and starts getting dressed. "So you're going to make me a teenager or what? Is that going to be all this will do to me?" She asks as she's buttoning up in front of an antique looking, tall floor mirror.
    "Hush dear, just dress up and stay quiet." Miss Evert coldly retorts.
    After a couple of minutes Christina was just about done. She did her best to tie the necktie as neatly as she could and then tucked it in under the red sweater vest. She took a moment to look herself over in the mirror. Given her already very pretty and youthful appearance, Christina could easily pass as a college girl dressed this way. She tidies up her shoulder length brown hair and turns around with a shy smile to face the older woman. "Well, I do look okay in this outfit I guess, but I don't really feel any different..." She concludes.
    "Just you wait a moment my dear, you'll see. " Miss Evert enigmatically responds.
