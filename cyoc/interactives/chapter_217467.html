    Sylvia and Tanya looked at the group of teens with smiles and hugged each of them. Nat and Rei gave a large hug to Tanya while Sylvia shook hands with Miles, Miranda, Nat, Rei, Connor, and Spark.

    "So, that's it then? You guys will be leaving soon?" Rei asked the mother and daughter.

    "Well, yes. Our mission is complete, Ezekiel Kane is no longer a threat..." Sylvia explained.

    "No longer racist, sexist, ex-hunter threat." Tanya corrected.

    "Right. So, our mission from the afterlife to the living world was always that we would return to the afterlife once we were done." Sylvia explained fully to them.

    "But couldn't you two stay for a little while longer?" Miranda asked them as they shook their heads.

    "We've already overstayed our welcome here. Plus we've been possessing bodies that were never ours. I've been a middle age woman living in a teen girl's body while my daughter is the opposite. Both of the people we're in have their own lives which they need to get back to." Sylvia explained.

    "Yeah, about that: what about the people who normally associate with the mom and daughter you're in? haven't they been ghosting their own family and friends the last few months?" Miles asked.

    "Luckily, that will be fixed, my mom and I have prepared a spell so that the mother and daughter's social lives will seem to have gone normal. They won't remember or know meeting any of you, it'll all seem like a wild dream to them. Which is for the best." Tanya explained.

    "We can't thank you all enough for what you've helped us do and for everyone else out there who was impacted by Kane. Goodbye to you all and have a wonderful life, treasure your loved ones." Sylvia said as she and Tanya used magic to teleport themselves to their temporary home.

    *****************************************

    Sylvia and Tanya made sure to erase all trace of the two of them having been there in the temporary home and it looked barren now compared to the last few weeks they had been there. They nodded to each other and teleported themselves to the home that their possessed bodies lived in so the mother and daughter would awaken without a clue.

    Sylvia and Tanya dressed themselves in their pajamas and Tanya met Sylvia in the teenager's bedroom.

    "I still can't believe what this mother has to deal with, with this teen girl." Tanya scoffed.

    "It can be problematic sometimes but that's just the way teenagers are, you'll see some..." Sylvia stopped herself before finishing and looked sad and began to cry.

    "Mom? Mom, what is it? Is it Kane? He may be with the vampires, but he's out of our hair now." Tanya said trying to comfort her as she cried.

    "No, that's not it. Tanya, we lost our lives, it just dawned on me on how much I missed all of this, on how I missed seeing you grow. You may be in someone else's body, but I've seen how you act with me. You would have made a great mother one day, but you won't anymore." Sylvia said through her tears.

    "Mom, mom, Sylvia, honey, it's okay. I lived for a while, okay? I lived and it was wonderful, I made friends, I...discovered love..." Tanya said.

    "We discovered love." Sylvia said correcting Tanya wiping away her tears.

    "But I know it's not about how much you do in life, it's about what you leave in another's heart." Tanya said as Sylvia lay down on the bed preparing to sleep.

    "Thanks, hey, how about for a little fun we say goodnight like the mother and daughter we are?" Sylvia suggested as Tanya smiled.

    "Goodnight, Sylvia, sweetie." Tanya said as she bent down and kissed Sylvia on the forehead.

    "Goodnight mom." Sylvia replied back.

    Tanya made her way to the other bedroom and looked at herself in a full body mirror as human, then shifted to her succubus form. She thought of the teen friends, but she mostly thought of Scarlet and the wonderful time she had spent with her. Once her soul was gone out of this body, the woman would no longer have succubus form or powers. Tanya changed back to her human form and lay down to sleep.

    As they began to sleep, Sylvia and Tanya both talked in their sleep.

    "I wish I could be a young girl again." Sylvia said groggily.

    "I wish I was a mother for real." Tanya said groggily.

    Sylvia and Tanya's souls left the bodies they had been possessing and left, but not to the afterlife.

    Scarlet drove through the small idyllic looking mid-western town with a smile. Just a few days ago, she had said goodbye to Bella. It was hard at first, but they both knew they would have to move on. Scarlet eventually came upon what looked like a local coffee shop, she was a little thankful that it wasn't a popular chain coffee shop (not yet anyway).

    Scarlet made her way inside and saw it was a little more crowded than she thought it would be. She made her way to the line to order, when a young girl who looked about 8 made her way around Scarlet cutting ahead of her.

    "Cynthia, please don't cut in front of people." A woman from behind Scarlet said. Scarlett turned to see a woman not too much older than her coming up and grabbing the girl.

    "I'm very sorry for that, my girl doesn't always know her manners." The woman said.

    "That's alright. Every little kid needs to learn." Scarlet said brushing it off.

    "Anyway, if you need to cut ahead of me, go right ahead, I won't mind." Scarlet offered.

    "Oh, that's very kind of you, thanks. We're sort of new to the neighborhood." The woman said.

    "Really? Me too. I'm Scarlet by the way." Scarlet said holding out her hand.

    "Hi, I'm Talia. Just Talia, not Natalia or anything like that just Talia, I know it seems silly." Talia said.

    "No, I think it's a nice name." Scarlet said.

    "Hey, this is a little odd, but since we're both new, do you want to sit down with my daughter and me to talk?" Talia asked.

    Scarlet was almost speechless for a moment before answering. "Yes, I'd like to."

    Scarlet then noticed little wings pop out of Cynthia's back.

    "Oh! You, uh, you didn't see that right?" Talia asked nervously.

    "What? You mean your little girl excitedly popping out her succubus wings? Nope." Scarlet said with a smirk.

    "Wait how do you...?" Talia began to ask.

    "Nirvana." Scarlet said silently with a wink.

    "Oh. Oh...." Talia said with a chuckle and blush which made Scarlet blush as well.

    Both of them realized they would soon have a lot to talk about.
