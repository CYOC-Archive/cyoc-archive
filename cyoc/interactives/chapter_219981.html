And we swapped out numbers when we had to do this project together. Let me ask her and I’ll call you back in a few minutes, okay?... okay… talk to you in a bit… bye,” she then ends the call and looks at Jeff.

    “Oh, Carolina is a classmate of mine,” Jane explains, “we only have each other’s number, because we were on this project together. Now she decided to get to know me better, we really got along pretty well. So, would you like to join in the sleepover?”

    “We could head to the mall and buy you one,” Jane says, “also, you could buy some girl clothes while we are there. We have 3pm, and the sleepover starts at 8pm, so we’d have plenty of time to get some shopping done before we have to head over”.

    “In that case,” Jeff says, “sure, I’d love to join in”.

    Jane smiles, “nice, let me call Carolina and let her know,” she says, “after that, let’s head straight to the mall”.

    Jeff nods, so after Jane has called Carolina back, the two head out to the mall, in order to pay for all of the clothes, Jeff had to spend most of his savings, which he hated to do, ‘after these two weeks, I’ll never be Mayumi again,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I’ll be stuck with all these clothes I’ll never will wear and use again.
