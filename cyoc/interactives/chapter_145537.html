    Ryan interrupted him. The jockstrap waistband was sticking up over the shorts waistband, and Ryan recognized the pizza boy.

    "Hey, Vinnie!"

    Cy looked uncertainly at the pizza boy. What was with the rise? Maybe if he played along, he would find out.

    "Oh, maybe I got the wrong address," he lied. He added, "I'll call dispatch, the new clerk's sevens, fours and nines look alike."

    Vinnie suddenly turned and ran back to the doorstep.

    "Maybe? Probably? See the rest of the family is at my sister's school for an event, and they only give out 4 tickets, so I got left at home. It should be ending soon, and maybe they ordered pizza expecting it to come after they get home. I can call them."

    Vinnie bit his lip.
