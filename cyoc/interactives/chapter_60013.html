    Jill rubbed at Danielle's head again which helped calm her down. Jill looked at Tanya and said, "You don't have to find someone to raise Tanya, I'll do it."

    "Why do you want to?" Emily asked looking like she already knew the answer.

    "I feel this connection to her like I do with my girls," Jill said a frown on her face.

    "Dammit to Hell!" Emily swore. "This complicates things even more."

    "What do you mean?" Danielle asked afraid of what the answer may be.

    "Tanya is your sister now," Jill said sighing.

    "Which means she wouldn't get her powers until was eighteen but she may not now," Emily said shaking her head. Before anyone could ask a question she continued, "Every witch, male and female, usually come into their power sometime in their teens. Witches in your family just come into their power at eighteen and it's only the female members. Though like I said before Kat has been completely cut off from the magic she would have had at eighteen. The same goes for you Danielle and more than likely for Tanya as well."

    "Then that means I'm stuck like this," Danielle said hanging her head.

    "Not necessarily," Emily said sounding thoughtful. "There's a slim chance that we can reverse what has been done but I won't even think of attempting it for a few years." She looked at Tanya before continuing, "I've performed that spell thousands of times and have never had this happen which means there are other spells in effect here. It would be irresponsible of me to try anything else without having at least a basic idea of what spells we're dealing with and who cast them. If I did try now you could be locked into your current forms and age at best. At worst it could be a magical Hiroshima."

    Danielle shuddered at that thought as Jill said, "I guess we have to move back to my hometown. At least it has a perception filter on it so my girls won't seem out of place."

    "What's a perception filter?" Danielle asked looking up at her mom.

    "It's a spell that causes people to overlook some of the magical stuff that happens," Kat answered looking down. Suddenly she burst out, "I'm sorry Danielle. I never meant for things to get like this. Mom should make me a little girl."

    "I don't think that's a wise idea," Emily said shaking her head. "Danielle will become a little girl too and with the way this day has been going that could spell trouble."

    "I only put the mirror spell on Danielle so Kat would share her fate," Jill said looking confused.

    "I figured that you had which is why I looked for it on Kat," Emily said with a sigh. "It spread to Kat at some point, probably when she transformed Danielle with Tanya. Now they'll share each other's fate until it can be safely removed which shouldn't be too difficult. Now we need to tell Tanya who her mom is." Everyone followed Emily as she went back over to Tanya and knelt down in front of her. She placed a hand on her shoulder and with a smile said, "Jill here is your mom Tanya. I had to talk to her to be sure since there's another little girl named Tanya that's missing and you couldn't remember your last name."

    "She's not my mom," Tanya said looking at Jill.

    "Do you remember what your mom looks like?" Emily asked.

    Tanya frowned and was silent for a long time before saying, "No I don't. Why can't I remember her?" Her eyes were starting to fill with tears and she was close to crying.

    "There was a bit of an accident and you lost some of your memories," Emily said with a reassuring smile. "You'll have to trust me that Jill is your mom."

    "Ok," Tanya said getting up and going over to Jill.

    Emily turned to Jill and said, "Get your girls home and pack. I expect to see them in school tomorrow morning."

    "Of course," Jill said waving her hand making a door back to their house appear.

    Kat and Tanya headed to the door with Danielle a bit behind them. As she walked away she heard Emily mumble to her mom, "Make sure they're not spotted. I don't want to have to modify reality any more than it already has been. And you've probably already figures it out but Kat and Danielle can only eat vegetables and fruits now. Tanya on the other hand can only eat meat and she'll possibly eat it raw. It might be that she'll eat meat from a fresh kill so watch her around Kat and Danielle."

    Danielle shuddered at this. Being an anthropomorphic rabbit girl she could be seen as prey by one who was part raptor.
