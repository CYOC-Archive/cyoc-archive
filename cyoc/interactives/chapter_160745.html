That was something that hadn’t changed with me, Scott or Vanessa, either.

Instead of the previous figure that everyone had come to know so well, there now sat a young woman who didn’t appear any older than early-30’s. Her hair extended down so far that the bottom of it was obscured by the desk, but it still had that unmistakable hue. Her skin color was also instantly recognizable.     “Would you all shut up, so we hear what he’s saying!” interrupted Scott.

    “She, not he,” I corrected him.

    “Shh!” Scott answered without looking at me.

    We missed hearing the president’s first few remarks, but they all seemed to be pretty introductory. “By now, the whole world has undergone some big changes. Big changes, some might say the biggest change ever,” she said.

    “Typical,” I muttered to myself. Once again, I noticed, there was no change to the cadence, tone or word choice of the person speaking.

    The president continued, “I want to assure you that me and my administration are committed to leading America through this extraordinary period and we will continue to be the greatest country in the world.” I rolled my eyes. She didn’t seem to have changed much beyond her appearance.

    “Dinner time,” I said as I headed to the kitchen.
