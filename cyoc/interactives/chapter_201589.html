    Half his Age

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous A year ago AR S Kid Reality alteration

    Jeff's younger sister, Jaime, is 12. 4 years younger than Jeff. Jeff liked the idea of maintaining the same age gap to his sister is he were to become younger. Following a debate that lasted several minutes, Jeff settled on 8 years old, half his current age. So rather than being 4 years older than Jamie, he would be 4 years younger. He also chose to remove his shirt so that he could observe his body as it changed.

    Filled with anxiety and excitement, Jeff clicked ACCEPT.

    Seconds later, Jeff's body was overcome by a pins and needles feeling throughout his skin and bones. His body was covered by what reminded him of goosebumps. The feeling left him paralyzed as he descended through puberty in reverse. His height diminished, his face rounding out, and his teeth began to shift in his mouth.

    The whole process took less than a minute, and Jeff was soon able to move his body once again. He was alarmed after realizing his feet were dangling off the computer chair. He was preparing to spin the chair when he saw his shorts. Gone were his black cargo shorts and in their place were a pair of camo shorts. He never bothered to look underneath, figuring that he would have to go to the bathroom sooner or later.

    Save for his computer, his entire room changed. What used to resemble a normal teenage boy bedroom was now more childish, with bright colored walls and a smaller bed. The comforter on his bed were Marvel themed and posters of Marvel characters were on the walls. Being a Marvel fanboy as a kid, Jeff was not surprised to see that kind of change.

    Jeff then peeked into his dresser as he was not interested in spending the day in his underwear. From cartoon tees, matching pajamas, to even colored socks, Jeff's wardrobe matched his new age. He had also realized how tall the dresser looked compared to his height as he could barely reach the top while standing on his toes. It was at that moment that he noticed his missing traces of puberty.

    He searched his torso, limbs, and armpits for minutes to find even one strand of body hair. However, being 8 years old meant coming up short. His torso had also changed, with it being smoothed out and devoid of his hard work on the track team. This suddenly put Jeff in the mood for a run, which he could do while playing his "new" friends.

    Not being settled in his younger body yet, Jeff wasn't sure if the Marvel t-shirt and red and white socks would fit him (he picked them to match the shorts he was wearing), or if they would be too small. That perspective changed the second his head poked through the shirt easily. He darted to the bathroom once the rest of his clothes were on.

    Locking the door to the bathroom he shared with his siblings, Jeff froze after seeing the boy reflected at him in the mirror. This was the exact boy he used to be in 2013, with only four adult teeth on his bottom jaw and two budding front teeth on the top among the sea of baby teeth in his mouth. The mophead haircut that Jeff remembered from childhood photos made its return. With his clothes on, Jeff looked every bit like the 8-year-old he was transformed into. He jumped with joy upon realizing that he was really 8 years old again and looked forward to possibly spending the whole summer as a kid.

    Jeff took one look at the changes on the Chronivac before locking it:

    Name: Jeff Williams
    Age: 8 years old
    Birthday: 03/25/2013
    Gender: male
    Race: Caucasian
    Height: 4'3"
    Weight: 56 lbs.

    Satisfied with his change, and not interested in making another or turning himself back, Jeff locked the Chronivac. He then left his room and made his way downstairs to see if there was anything going on.
