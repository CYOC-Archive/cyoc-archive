    A scientist studies logic to understand the changes in this world.

    A computer scientist studies computer languages to alter the way something is done.

    A wizard studies spells so that his magic can alter the world..

    These three branches at first glance seem impossible to be related but one man decided to go further.

    — Magic uses spells, a series of steps, symbols, words and others that modify reality to our whim, that of the magicians.

    A man in a white coat said while he recorded himself with a camera in front of a blackboard with several strange symbols, of which a Pentateuch stood out.

    — Programmers learn how to use code to make computers create dialog windows, display a series of images, what we call animations, and so on...

    The man in the robe then left and showed a computer a series of codes that ended up enabling a pop-up window that allowed an animation of a jumping rabbit to open.

    — I have seen that both end up being the same, modifying a reality based on a series of codes, that is why I believe this thing.

    The man then turned on a projector showing a small logo with the name "Color your world".

    — This program was made by putting into practice a series of magical spells that ended up making an application that can modify reality.

    The man started laughing maniacally.

    — However, I have not yet been able to put it into practice right now, and letting it exist in an app store or with a download link would be dangerous.

    The scientist took a tablet and showed it to the camera.

    — That's why I installed it on this tablet that I protected so that no one can hack it, not even the company that manufactures them.

    The scientist turned it on and showed drawing software like those used by digital artists.

    — At first glance it looks like a normal drawing app, but in reality it is more than that.

    The doctor exited the app and re-entered.

    — Upon entering it has a writing interface and a drawing interface, both can modify reality. In both there are default options as is...

    The scientist opened a small sale inside the drawing version. This one said:

    "Name of the person to change"

    — This option, for example, allows you to change only one person, you can also make several clicks to, for example, change several people. And I know what you think!

    The scientist put the tablet aside, then opened his shirt and showed his body full of symbols and words written on his skin.

    — I filled my body with enough spells so that the changes do not affect me UNLESS I DECIDE TO CHANGE SOMETHING IN MYSELF! HAHAHAHA.

    The mad scientist laughed before suddenly falling silent.

    — My goal to put this to the test is to leave it in a second-hand house, there I will let someone buy it and start changing reality, if I don't like what I see I will try to get it back, but if everything goes well, maybe even I left it to him. At the end of the day this is just for demonstration.

    The man took the camera and approached her.

    - That me! That I achieved the impossible! Mix science and magic to achieve the impossible!

    The man put the camera back in his place.
