    Jennifer visibly relaxed "Trraa-ansforrrrm! Hurrry, plee-eease!"
    Liz laughs wickedly "You dont want to be inbetween one or the other so we might as well make you all one thing; Transform all the way into my sheep for 4-H! ...you know, I was wondering where I was going to get extra class credit, and you were sounding like you were going to be a spoilsport with this "T or D" thing. I may turn you "baaa-ck" when I'm done, maybe..."
    Jennifer bleats in horror as her clunky, hooflike hands rapidly progress toward hoofdom, fingers dwindling into thick stubs as they are encased in hoof. "Noo-oooo!" a look toward her wobbling legs shows them drawing up into complete sheep hind legs, her calves reducing to a small part of the middle of her legs. Terrified, she bleats as her chest and belly barrels out while the fluffy breasts shift down her body to stop at her lower belly. A soft crack announces the end of her bipedal stance, dropping her on all fours where she can feel her thighs and forearms merge to her sides.

    Liz giggles "I wonder how far "all the way" will take you? I mean it's more fun if you can remember being a girl, but if your mind goes all the way too, I won't even have to turn you back at all. After all, no one else will remember you existed, right?"
    She kneels and looks into Jennifer's panicked eyes in the final moments as her neck pops into place and her extra wool "hair" draws in to a normal length for head wool. Jennifer gives a plaintive bleat as her earrings turn into ear tags and the remainder of her clothes a collar. Liz continues to watch her eyes to see if they dim like some of the old stories her parents told her. The sheep's eye's blinked at the last second.
    Liz sighed and clicked the leash that appeared in her hand onto the collar "I'll find out soon enough if you're still in there or not Jen. there's no way you can fake it right? I dont' believe you're that bright to begin with. I mean come on, you got turned into a sheep girl for cryin' out loud. Tell you what, I'll find some dimbulb jock later to make a ram out of to keep you company and complete my project."
    Absently she dismissed the idea of Jennifer faking being mindless if it comes up. what could she do if she was?
