    Minerva was a young adult women, barely past 19, she was petit at 5'3 with long light blond hair and green eyes sparkling with lots and LOTS of hope. though she had an appealing hourglass figure, she had breast and bottocks on the smaller end of the scale. She had dimples in her cheeks whenever she smiled and had a unique and recognizable birthmark on her Clavical just above her boob in the distinct shape of a star, thats what made her think she was destined to become a star (Pretty cheesy right).

    She was waiting at the edge of the train station with all of her luggage from home, waiting for the next train to hollywood to arrive to go to her audition. The news was kinda abrupt for her parents, she was already starting packing when she announced to her mom and dad that she was catching the train to LA. Her parents tried to object to this Outrageous and Outlandish proposition, saying that she should stayed put. She was hoping to see them support her, but their dissapointment broke her heart, so when she fiished packing, she immediatly bolted out the door with all her luggage without looking back.

    Though with that setback, she was still waiting with youthful optimism for the next train, though on the edge of the platform lit by the full moon, raindrops started to fall. it was begining to rain hard on the train tracks, Minerva hoped this wouldnt delay the trains but the thought was quickly interupted by a ringtone. looking around she found that it was her phone that was ringing, so she quickly looked through her purse. when she pulled it out and put it next to her ear, all she heard was static, minerva was confused but a voice quickly cut through th silence. "Do you want to go Elsewhere?" said the voice. "Of course! Im heading straight to Hollywood to become famous!" replied Minerva with enthusiasm. Just as quickly as the call came, it ended. She was confused and curios about what was that about until she heard the distinct sound of a train pulling into the station.

    Though it was not the typical train or metro, instead was an old train made of black iron and coated in vibrant green colours, numerous cars were attached behind the engine with the Words "Elsewhere Express" written on its side. the train and cars were also adorned with butterfly and metamorphisis imagery. though she couldnt see what was in the cars through the tinted windows, she heard laughing, cheers and joy echoing through them. doors leading to the inside were opening before her, showing a very dark entrance to the cars, but no passengers of employes walked out of them.

    Being naive as she was, she thought this might be the train she was supposed to get on, thinking she got special treatment by the universe for becoming a star. she grabbed her suitcases and threw them through the door. and finally she walked through the doorframe ready to start her new adventure.

    the room she was in finally lit up, and the doors closed behind her, cutting her ties to her previous life, but she was ready for the future she was hoping for. the room she was in was small but neatly decorated with a locked door leading towards the inside of the cabin. on the butterfly themed wall paper was a small golden plaque with the words "What is your final destination?" written on it, below those words was a small slit where you can insert a small paper. looking downs she could see a small desk covered in a stack of paper with a cup full of pens in it. from the various context clues she got, she took a pen and paper and wrote in her cutesy handwritting "To an Audition to become famous!!!". after inserting the note into the slit, the door to the side quickly unlocked, Minerva reached her hand to turn the doorknob and pulled the door open to see...
