    Coach Sinclair cannot find Chad among the Speedos

    Previous chapter

    added by Drakkenfyre A year ago I

    Sinclair sighed after reviewing his Spring baseball roster. He needed Chad this season. He had been disappointed that Chad hadn't been furious with Coach Sinclair after spending the weekend as a Speedo. He wanted Chad angry. Anger redirected into sports made better athletes, right?

    Oh, sure he hadn't needed to transform Chad to win at football. But after his victory, the arrogant prick needed to be properly humiliated to make him more of a team player when it came to baseball.

    He had been tempted to leave Chad a Speedo, but without him, the team would have a losing season. He got up, and headed over to the laundry room to find Chad. Tyler had left the freshly washed Speedos neatly stackedI for Monday. Coach pulled put on his special monocle. Transformed humans glowed iridescent when viewed through the special lens. He looked at the stack of Speedos. None glowed. He began to sort them individually. Then he counted them. All 144 Speedos were there, but Chad was number 145.

    Furious at not finding Chad, Sinclair threw all the Speedos on the floor and stomped through them, as he searched the laundry room behind furniture and equipment.

    Coach then checked the washer, then the dryer. Then he went through all the towels. Where had Ty put Chad? Or had someone stolen that special Speedo? Coach was the one who was angry.

    He should have Chad in front of him when he transformed him back, but he might be able to do it remotely. He was thinking when he noticed the bottle of sanitizer with the Postit note stuck to it.

    "Coach, need more sanitizer. -Ty, " it read.

    "What? Ty used sanitizer on evert load? I only meant him to sanitize Chad once. Okay, this might be better if Chad's a blank slate, I can retrain him into just the sort of mindless jock I need." Sinclair smiled.

    His smile vanished, as he realized, he didn't have the Speedo. He growled. How long had the Speedo been missing. If Chad was stolen the first day, he would have only been sanitized anywhere from 1-4 times. If he had been stolen yesterday, he'd be totally sanitized. It might be a good thing if he was stolen early on, he'd still know how to play baseball. If he was stolen after fifty sanitizations, Coach would not only probably have to teach him how to play baseball, but what a ball was. He sighed again.

    He would deal with Ty on Monday, now he had to find Chad the Speedo.
