    The Three Teen Girls get to the Magic Shop

    It was mid-afternoon before Hannah, Claire and Leslie reach the Magic Shop. It wasn't a particularly big shop nor did it seem to be anything special about it.

    Hannah reached out and found the door unlocked.

    "Hello." The three girls came inside. While the area had all sort of items one would think of in a Magic Shop, the colorful play area in the corner stood out.

    "Hi Dears." Hannah saw the woman come out of the back followed by a man. She was in her mid-fifties with grey streaked hair while the white haired man appeared to be a bit older. "Can we help you?"

    Leslie and Claire decided to let Hannah talk. "We're here because of this Watch and what it did to us."

    The woman looked at the Watch and smiled knowingly. "The Regression Watch. So how old were you?"

    "In our forties."

    The man took a glance at the three girls. "This is an easy fix or you can buy the Reset Watch to do it yourselves." He named a price that none of the girls had on them at this time.

    "How much to make us our old age?"

    "That's complementary when someone gets here. I can do it in about an hour."

    The Gypsy Woman Lorelei looked at the Regression Watch. "There's the play area if you want to try being a child for the time. If you want to, I'll join you."

    "No thank you." All three teenagers said at the same time.

    "Are you sure? It's really fun."

    "Yes we are."

    The older man laughed. "You just like being younger."

    Lorelei giggled. "You know me brother. We almost never get any of the Regression Items back and even then I don't get to be young for more than an hour or two at a time."

    The older man thought for a moment. "I think I can give you a special on the Reset Watch and give you the manual on the Regression Watch."

    Hannah looked at bit weary. "What's the price?"

    "Babysit my sister for at least a couple of weeks and then drop her back off here when you all are ready."

    Hannah, Claire, and Leslie thought about it for a few minutes and Hannah answered for them.
