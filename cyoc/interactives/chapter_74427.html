    The entrance to the aquarium is sculptured to look like a cave in a rocky cliff face at the beach. It's dark when you and your parents go in, but turning a corner you find yourselves in another world.

    You and your parents find themselves in a labyrinth of glass tunnels, under what appears to be millions of gallons of water, both fresh and salt. Swimming in the water, right up close to you, are dolphins, turtles, sharks, lobsters, otters, alligators, jellyfish and every kind of fish you can imagine. You wander around the tunnels aimlessly, spellbound by the beauty of this other world. How you all long to be able to explore this magical relm, unhindered by the tunnels...

    "Ahoy there, me hearty!" comes a voice snapping you all back to reality. You all turn to see an elderly bearded man with a Western England accent that calls out 'Old sea dog'.

    "Yes, what do you want?" Mom asked.

    "I could see you were spellbound by the aquarium, and you look like you wants to experience it closer. Well I works here see, and if you come with me I can let any of you swim alongside them - for a price."

    "What, we can hire out frogman gear and dive in alongside them?"

    "You don't need gear!" chuckles the old man "I can make you literally able to swim like a fish!"
