    The thought continues to float in his mind, What is happening to me? Yet Aden has a good idea, but was not entirely sure. Aden could see from his buddy Guy, that Mr. Yuan could pick up on things, especially since he stressed the wolf in his name.

    The changes continued, giving him thicker and meatier muscles, as well as big, thick hands... or "man hands" as Aden would like to call them; thick and dense hair on his knuckles, hands, arms, legs, chest, and his now six pack abs. The pain stopped suddenly, allowing Aden to stand and take a look at himself. Excitement filled every cell of his being as he jumped for joy. Sure, he still didn't look like a wolfman, but he was a lot closer than he ever had been.

    Thankful to Mr. Yuan, Aden put on all his clothes, which were now slightly tighter, but he didn't care because they showed off his new muscular physique, and ran out of the room. Heading toward the desk, to thank Mr. Yuan for all that he did. Outside of the changing room, Aden noticed Mr. Yuan was walking out from a different changing room with Guy, back in his normal physical condition.

    As Guy and Aden saw each other, joy filled their hearts for the other persons physical characteristics. Aden was happy Mr. Yuan put Guy back to normal, Guy was happy Aden got what he always wanted, especially after Mr. Yuan told him of Aden's selfless heroics. Guy always knew that about Aden, how great a human being he was, that is why he went through his extreme changes in the first place.

    As the two happy men walked toward the exit, they both turned to speak to Mr. Yuan.

    "Mr. Yuan... I wanted to thank you deeply for giving me something I had always wanted. I never really could grow any hair on any place of my body other than the top of my head. The muscles were a nice touch as well! I also wanted to thank you for returning Guy to his normal self, I don't know what could have happened if he was left in that state after today or even tomorrow."

    "You are quite welcome my young friend! What I have done for you and your friend is a token of my appreciation for what you did for me today Aden. You are a selfless person and I knew that you should be justly rewarded. Unlike your friend Guy, who needed to be taught a lesson to show him that appearances aren't everything!"

    "Yes Mr. Yuan, I have learned that now and I know that I will be happy with who I am and what I can do on my own. What I did was in the hopes of impressing you Aden, to see if you would pay more attention to me because I looked that way. I just assumed that you were gay, or at least bi-sexual, since you were always ogling those body builders in those magazines."

    "Come now Guy, you can change anything about your physical appearance, especially if it is something like your friend Aden, who could not achieve it on his own, but you must always be certain of what you want before you ask for something. It is like the old warning Be Careful What You Wish For. Now go and enjoy your lives... if you need anything else sometime... I will be here waiting!

    While Mr. Yuan had been talking, Aden could not help but to look at his friend, with a new found affection for him. Aden did not see it before, but he could see now that Guy was a good man, even if he assumed too much and went about it in the wrong way.

    Both Aden and Guy bowed to Mr. Yuan, as was customary in his culture and Mr. Yuan returned the gesture. With that, both Guy and Aden turned, leaving the store, deciding it would be best to go home, as they felt there had been enough excitement for the time being.

    Back in Aden's car, the two hopped in, closing the doors. Once Aden started the car and placed it into gear, he grabbed the steering wheel, taking control of the car, heading back to their neighborhood. Aden began thinking about what had happened; him helping Guy, then him helping Mr. Yuan and all of the other selfless things he had done in the past. Aden wondered if Mr. Yuan could really have helped him to become a werewolf like he always wanted. Aden shrugged it off, knowing that if he wanted to truly see, he could always go back and find out... especially since Mr. Yuan had invited both of them to come back to the store if they needed something they could not acquire on their own.

    What neither Aden or Guy didn't notice was that in the short period of time since leaving Mr. Yuan's store, Aden's fingernail grew slightly longer and they were thick... like a werewolf's claws!
