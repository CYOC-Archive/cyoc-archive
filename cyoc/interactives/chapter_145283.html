    “What about the park? You know the one the large park with the kids playground and huge field, we could set up a nice little picnic and relax in the sun” you suggest.

    Your mother rubs her chin a little in thought before smiling “great idea” she says “I’ve been dying to use the picnic basket again, haven’t used it since your aunt Amanda was in town” she adds on.

    Breakfast continues with talks on food, something you made sure contained plenty of healthy foods like carrots, lettuce and other vegetables, you get up off your chair having finished your food.

    “Likewise, Whitney” Eric says with a nod from Luke.
