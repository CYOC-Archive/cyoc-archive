    "W-what do you mean?" Minako asked as she crawled towards the girl.
    "You got jealous of my other doll didn't you?" The girl asked as she looked at the pile of clothes that was her former possession.
    "Wh...why would I get jealous of her?" Minako asked.
    "She's my new doll, you can be too, if you do nice things for me." The girl explained.
    "I...I don't understand."
    "You are my new lovely slave aren't you? Now come here."

    "Tell me why you're here, you're not a courier or anything are you?"
    "N-No." Minako said. "I came here because I was told to come here, they said I could become famous."
    The girl looked down at the shrunken Minako and then back at her oversized and discarded clothes before speaking.
    "Well, it doesn't seem like you're famous, my slave." The girl said as she stroked the back of Minako's head.
    "I want to be famous, I want to be a singer!" Minako said in an attempt to make a connection with the girl.
    "Ah, music." The girl responded. "It is one of the few things that can distract me for very long, you should know that. If you're going to be my slave then you had best get good at it. In fact...retrieve my lute from the barn, it's in a place where it can be found easily, I'll need to practice."
    "As you wish Mistress." Minako said giving the girl a small bow as she made her way out of the room.
    The door shuts behind Minako as she begins to make her way down the hall.

A door was at the bottom of the stairs, it was then that she realized she was far to small to reach the doorknob.
    "Ah crap." Minako muttered. "How am I supposed to open that?"
