    Spencer thought for a few moments. His parents were right he could use some help on the farm. He smiled, and phoned Chad's cell phone. The box in the corner of the room rang. He would need to mute Chad's phone. He left a message on Chad's phone inviting him to work this week for $35/hr.

    A few minutes after he hung up, he listened to his message on Chad's phone. He waited another minute or two then texted his phone from Chad's saying sorry and that Chad had other plans.

    Then he phoned Bruno and offered him $15/hr, then phoned Rick and offered him $15/hr. He grinned after they both called back. He made an appointment for them to meet up at the farm at 8 am tomorrow. He grinned as he thought about Chad being fastened into the breeding harness by his buds Bruno and Rick. Chad might consider trampling Spencer, but he wouldn't hurt his buddies. He then took Chad's phone scheduled a text to be sent to him at 9:15 tomorrow. Bruno and Ryan would be his alibi if his name came up regarding Chad's disappearance.

    He typed up chores for Bruno and Rick. He went to the guest room. There were two twin beds pushed together to make one bed. He thought about separating them, but figured Bruno and Rick might prefer the current configuration. His purpose was to place hidden cameras and microphones. He would also put some in the barn and other places that they might talk to see if they suspected his 4H project breeding cow uses to be their buddy and MVP Chad. If he could fool these two, he would fool everyone. If they figured it out, there were enough nanobots to add a couple more cows to the herd.

    He headed out to see if Chad had stopped growing. It might be awkward if he added 50 or more pounds while Bruno or Rick were watching. He already had cameras in Chad's stall. The rest of the barn and the breeding pen needed to be done.
