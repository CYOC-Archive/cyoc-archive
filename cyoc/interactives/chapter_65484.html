    "Oh... I..." Jess stammered. She wasn't just hungry. She was starving. Famished. Her stomach audibly gurgled, as if to answer Mercedes's question.

    "I suppose that's a yes," Mercedes said with a soft smile. "Come on to the meeting room. I think you'll be quite pleased at the set-up I've arranged."

    "Arranged? But... but how..." Jess stammered, slowly getting to her feet.

    "Sorceress, remember?" Mercedes laughed.

    "Oh... okay, I guess..." Jess allowed herself to be guided to the door, a bit confused and befuddled. This meeting dropped a few bombshells on her, not only that she had accidentally thrown her own humanity away, but that her boss was actually a sorceress. If it wasn't for the obviously mystical calming influence Mercedes seemed to have on her, she'd probably be having a panic attack.

    Instead, she walked to the meeting room, with Mercedes behind her, trying to contain her eagerness. As she got closer, she could smell it. Oh, God, she could smell it. Finally, she threw open the doors and there, spread out over the table, was a feast. Foods of all kinds. Junk foods, vegetables, fruits, cakes, breads, potatoes, pizza, cheeses... there was so much food.

    "Here you go, Jessica. Dig in," Mercedes said as she closed the door behind them.

    Jess abandoned all hesitance, leaping upon the table like her life depended on getting that food inside her as soon as she could, and dropped her face into a chocolate cake, gorging herself immediately. The rush of endorphins that accompanied the transformation soon kicked in, and that wonderful feeling enveloped her. She was growing fatter, she knew it, but it felt so good that she didn't care. She welcomed it. Felt glad for it as her nose broadened and lifted into a snout, mouth enlargening to allow more food per bite.

    She didn't even pause as Mercedes's cellphone rang.
    "Hey, Clarissa, great timing. Yes, your premonition was right. It was absolutely right. Yes, she's changing right now. Sure, hold on, let me put it on video cam so you can see," Mercedes pulled the phone away from her face and tapped the screen a few times, training the phone on Jess as she grew more and more piglike with each intake of food. She tapped the little button on her bluetooth headset to activate it.

    "Yeah, I know, it's incredible. I wish you were here. I know, I know, but I haven't learned that level of teleportation yet. I know. Hey, you try running a tech support company while keeping your studies up! It is not easy, I tell you, and you told me I HAD to be here so we could... well, don't give me grief for doing what you asked of me, okay? Ooh, hold on, she's getting a bit too big for her clothes."

    Mercedes point her free hand in Jess's direction, waggling the fingers and muttering words in a language no non-mystic had ever uttered. With a yellowish glow, the clothes on Jess's back vanished, allowing her more corpulent porcine frame to hang free, her corkscrew tail bobbing with movement of her head, extra breasts hanging below her torso, hands and feet steadily becoming more and more hooflike.

    "Alright, I think I'd like to focus on this change right now, can I call you back? Alright, good. I'll see you later, Clarissa. Of course I love you. Bye!"
