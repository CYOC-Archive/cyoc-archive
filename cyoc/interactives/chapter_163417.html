One part of him was hesitant, after all even though it worked well for Max, it doesn’t mean the same thing will happen to him. What if there was some side effect that they didn’t know about or something like that? However, all those concerns were drowned out by the other part of him. The other part of him was envious of the muscular men he saw everywhere, envious that they possessed something he desired. He wanted to be strong and buff so badly he touched the statue.

    Instantly as he touched the statue Landon was thrown back due to an electric shock. The shock caused all the father’s muscles to pulsate at once. Briefly he thought that his concerns were true, and he made a grave error. However as soon as it started, the pulsing stopped, and it Landon with left with a more muscular body. It actually worked; he wasn’t a stick anymore! That being said, his body wasn’t anything special however, he looked like a person with good genetics that only worked out occasionally. That wasn’t enough for Landon though, he wanted more! So, he touched the statue again without hesitation.

    Unsurprisingly, the same thing happened once he touched the statue again. Again, he was hit with an electric shock, and once again all his muscles were pulsing at once, but this time Landon kept his eyes open to see his body slowly bulking up.

He felt great like the statue had promised and had the body of a fitness model, abate a body that was still smaller than his son, but Landon didn’t care about that right now. He was too busy being elated about his new muscles.

Not really, but now that he has them, he loved having that weight of them in the front. He cupped his pecs and gave them a squeeze, relishing in the feeling of the hard mounds, and then he flexed them making them dance.

One more touch could make him burst out of his trunks, and walking around naked wasn’t something he wanted, not to mention it would leave a bad impression for Max.
