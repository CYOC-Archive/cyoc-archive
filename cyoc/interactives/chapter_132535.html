Part of what made it amusing was Miss Birthday Girl's reluctance and generally being a wet blanket as her sisters led her toward the Meat Market. What made him different was that he had darker fur on his front in the general shape known as the "treasure trail" by most people. The thing that made it different was that his musculature spread it out enough that his regular pelt could be seen through the hair.

At that, one of the girls started singing 'Don't hold back now', but it was badly corrupted by lust, which seemed to show how much her mind was in the gutter.
