    Ty's Power

    Ty adjusts to life alone... But not for long!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by scriptboy 5 years ago BM

    Ty just grinned as his entire family just disappeared into thin air! They had become babies which were to be adopted and placed into foster care, the same way he had lived his entire life up to this point! He had gotten rid of his family, all thanks to his weird but cool magical powers!


    Ty spent the first two days playing ALL his video games, reading Dustin’s old comic books and just sleeping. It was like all the stress in the world had been lifted from his shoulders. He was now, finally, able to do the things he wanted, for the very first time in his life.


    But, on the third day, loneliness began to set in. Ty hated to admit this feeling. He started to miss his stepbrother, Dustin. Darn, even though he hated Dustin with passion, he also remembered still having plenty of fun times with him as well. Dustin only acted like a jerk when Mark was around. But, Dustin was great when Mark wasn’t home, and they had tons of fun together. The idea suddenly came to Ty’s mind to bring Dustin back.


    Ty thought about it long and hard. He had these cool powers, so he could wish for a bank account which had millions of Dollars in it so he would always have enough money to cover any expenses. He could basically do whatever he wanted and still go to school and be with his friends. He just felt so…. Alone. Lastly, Ty really wanted to feel loved. Having a boyfriend was just great right now.


    So, Ty got up and stood in the middle of his room and he wished for Dustin to be back, age 16, just like he was before Ty even got his powers. Then, when Ty snapped his fingers, there was a bright flash of light and then, in an instant, there was Dustin, standing in his bedroom with an incredible expression of surprise on his face.


    “What the HELL… Where am I? Ty? What happened?” Dustin shouted.


    “I sent you, June and Mark to foster care as babies to be reborn again but I got lonely and I decided to give you a second chance. So… I brought you back! I hope you won’t make me regret this…” Ty said calmly.


    Dustin just stared at his stepbrother. He remembered his Ty had gained this weird power which he had used to make him three years younger and how Ty had used this power to change Mark and June. Dustin quickly concluded that he needed to remain on Ty’s good side, otherwise Ty could easily change his life in a heartbeat!


    “Uhh… Thanks… I’m glad you decided not to make me have to live my life all over again…” Dustin sighed.


    “I’ll make a deal with you. You can stay here with me as long as you’re NICE to me! No more June and Mark! Besides, they weren’t great parents towards you, either!” Ty said sternly. “I’ll make sure you got everything you need as long as I’m the boss!”


    Dustin took a deep breath and held out his hands.


    “Alright, bro…. You’re the boss! I’m fine with that!”


    “Now… I want a boyfriend… I want to be loved… Hmmm… “Ty pondered out loud…. “I wanna find or create me a cool, sweet, hunky, athletic boyfriend…”

    Dustin’s jaw dropped wide open. What was Ty going to do, next?
