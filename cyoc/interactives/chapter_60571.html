    "Oh, good morning pup. How are you doing today?"

    "I'm in a cage." Nate shrieked, "Let me out."

    "Now why would I want to do that. You haven't been housebroken yet. You might have an accident."

    "What are you talking about?" Nate asked, "I'm human."

    "No, you are a werewolf. That has already been verified. You're an endangered species. So endangered that few people even know that you
    exist. But your capture means that we can finally prove the existence of your kind and get some protection for your species."

    "What species?" Nate shouted, "I became a werewolf because of drinking Citrus Moon Soda."

    "No, there are others of your kind. We haven't been able to prove it because what photos we have are blurry but we can prove it now. My name
    is Professor Billson. I'm preparing to exhibit you for the whole world to see. Then everyone will believe me that there really are such things as
    werewolves."

    Nate said, "But I'm naked. Can I at least have some clothes?"

    Professor Billson asked, "Whatever for? I know all about you werewolves, how you don't wear clothes and don't use toilets. You just pee on the
    floor wherever you're at and don't care who's watching."

    "Listen, Professor," Nate pleaded. "I'm just a human who changed into a werewolf last night. I don't even know if I'll change again."

    Professor Billson interrupted, "Oh I'm sure you will in about three hours. So I need to hurry to prepare the presentation. I want them to see you
    as fully human so they can watch you transform."

    Nate said, "They'll lock you in prison for if they see you keeping a naked man in a cage. Besides, I'm not violent and I do use a toilet. You don't
    need to keep me in a cage."

    Professor Billson said, "I know you're not violent. I know the truth about werewolves which is nothing like what Hollywood portrays. You
    werewolves are kind and gentle creatures. You make excellent pets once you've been trained. You just never seem to learn modesty no matter
    how trained you werewolves are. So until I can train you to pee outside, you're staying in the cage."
