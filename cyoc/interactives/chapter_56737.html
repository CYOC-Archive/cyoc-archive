    Amy (not Ami, that part of her has served it's purpose and returned to whence it came)
    walked groggily through the entry way to break, having spent the evening praying for the
    souls that how existed as the Gold Deer with limited forms of self expression and destine
    to be prey for her own kind and the leomans.

    She stopped right in front of Luna, Sunny, and Kira, and stared at them for three whole
    minutes with a calm almost serene look on her face.

    Luna and Sunny for their parts felt something shiver and shift inside them as the little white
    and red pup looked at them. She who had the power to restore those trapped as -things-
    back into Lupman just as Penny had the power to restore those trapped as Leoman.

    Finally, Amy placed a few minor offerings to the goddess statues and their consort. Shinto
    was, if nothing else, encompassing and polytheistic.

    As Amy left without a second look, Luna and Sunny felt an odd feeling spread through
    them after Amy had placed her minor offerings in a small offering bowel they were sure
    wasn't there a moment ago and they hadn't willed into existence either yet was very much
    a part of them.

    Kira meanwhile thought, 'Could I say that I'm part of the still world now so the code of
    honor doesn't apply to me? Ah who am I kidding? they'd say I was trying to have it both
    ways. There's only one honorable thing to do.' Kira then mentally spoke/thought/sent with
    all sublimity of a freight train to to the silver moon and golden sun goddesses he hadn't
    known for more than a few days now with tiny souls within them. 'Luna. Sunny. Will you
    marry me?'

    Luna and Sunny were distracted from the strange feeling triggered by Amy's offerings.
    Honor strictly dictated that should a she-wolf be left with a male's seed, they were
    expected to wed.

    'Are we supposed to wait six months? Or can we be wed as we are somehow?' Luna
    thought.

    Sunny meanwhile, likely many who fought for a goal for so long they had nearly forgotten
    the purpose and reason for the goal itself, it felt -alien- to be proposed to, let alone have
    the almighty binding power of honor to back up becoming a bride at long last. It was like
    climbing a long mountain, and now she was finally at the top and left with the confused
    thought of what to do now.

    Honor aside, common sense screamed that they had known each other for only a few days,
    and given Kira's life style choice, they were either going to be wed to a statue (which would
    leave most thinking them insane) or spend the centuries or eons to roll by as people
    around them LIVED their lives, growing up, growing old, and dying and being forgotten.

    And then there was their pups, would they be able to change 'back' when they would have
    been -born- as artwork with souls of their own opposed to that of the artist that made
    them?

    Luna sighed, letting the pack's basic laws of honor guide her, she knew the risks, she
    chose to play the game, and this was the consequences of her own choices. "I accept."
    Luna said/thought/sent first.

    Luna felt it strange, Sunny, the one who had always been in a blind rush to get married, to
    find a powerful lupman to be the trophy wife to and leech off him to live the good life, now
    found herself with a lupman who spend his life as a -decoration!- without a penny to his
    name. Not in the least what she had planned. But maybe, on the other hand, maybe it was
    what she had earned for herself. "Your sun goddess also accepts." Sunny
    said/thought/sent at last.

    Kira now wondered if Glendale herself marrying the three of them would be legally
    binding...

    And the three large statues assumed more regal poses for the day.
