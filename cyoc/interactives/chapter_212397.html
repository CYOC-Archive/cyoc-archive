    “...I have to transform you into a woman. I'm sorry for not warning you in advance, Ashley”

    You were so excited about finally moving into a new place, with a female roommate no less(!), that you didn't hear Paige's last condition, but Paige was impatient and didn't wait for your reaction. She wasted no time as she began to recite the binding incantation...

    “Khornath! Slaaneth! Nurgolth! Tzintchi! Gods of the great chaos that lies beyond, hear my prayer!”

    You were suddenly enveloped in unbreakable bands of mystic energy.

    “What the fu...” was all you could say as you stared at the red bands coiled around your body, red bands that had appeared out of thin air, before Paige continued the incantation, rendering you unable to speak.

    “Mmmm!! Mmmm!!!” you mumbled impotently, as if you had been gagged, yet nothing was covering your mouth.

    “Gods of chaos, release the anima of this man, his unconscious feminine form, and in return I will render unto you my blood...”

    Paige took a small knife out of her pocket and began to cut her wrist, releasing droplets of blood onto the floor.

    Drip.

    Drip.

    Drip.

    Looking closely, you could see a weird occult symbol where Paige was dropping her blood onto.

    You wanted to scream, not just because you were being held against your will by a crazy witch, but that this crazy witch was mutilating herself and praying to the “gods of chaos”, whoever the hell they were.

    “Gods of chaos, I pray, transform this man in body and soul into his anima, I plead you!” screamed Paige.

    Just below your feet, a chasm of red swirling light soon appeared out of thin air. Inside you were screaming. All you wanted was a roommate, and now you were being sent to Hell!?

    Lucky you, Hell was not what you were seeing. It was energy from beyond time and space, from places that no mortal man had ever stepped in. The red energy soon raised from the floor like an elevator and enveloped your entire body. Inside the field of mystical, eldritch power, you continued to hear Paige chant for a few more moments. Her voice sounded close to you, yet distant. Physically, you were no more than five feet away from Paige, but covered in this energy, you may as well have been entire dimensions away.

    Sooner than you had expected, the red energy simply evaporated like mist. You realized that the red bands were also gone, and you could move freely now. You were seriously pissed off at Paige and blurted out the first thing that crossed your mind.

    “What. The. Fuck. Was...wait, what's wrong with my voice!?” you asked yourself desperately. “What did you do to me you bitch!?” As you tried to run out the apartment door, frustated but not surprised to know that it was locked from the inside, you noticed that everything about your body was now different. Your clothes. You were wearing women's clothing. Your legs were encased in black yoga pants, and they looked much thinner and more toned than they had but moments ago. Your feet now had tiny looking black ballet flats on them instead of your trademark orange high tops.

    Frightened beyond belief that you had been tricked by a witch was now trapped in her apartment like this was some warped fairy tale, you ran to the large windows and tried to open them to scream for help, but you found you could not open them. You also took a glance at your thin, dainty arms. You had recently taken to going to the gym, and you had developed a respectable physique there, with arms that would place well in any gun show. That was all gone now, replaced by a body that couldn't harm a fly and was only good for showing off how sexy you looked. You were wearing a blue tank top with spaghetti straps, with a visible bra underneath. So you had been turned into a woman. Just great.

    Suddenly, the idea of calling 911 reached your mind, but you quickly discovered that you no longer had any pockets.

    You ran all around the apartment, driving yourself crazy looking for a device that had evaporated with Paige's magic. Speaking of Paige, the redheaded witch had just been standing there, holding onto her forehead the entire time.

    “Whoa that took a lot out of me” muttered Paige as she combed back her hair nervously, her entire body shaking.

    Giving up hope of finding your phone, you eventually found Paige's bathroom and stared at yourself in the mirror.

    “Oh my god, that bitch did turn me into a woman!”

    What do you do now?
