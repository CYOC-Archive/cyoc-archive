Daniel was leaning against the railing of the ferry as the pulled up the port, who would have thought that Pleasure Island was in view of the shoreline just off the entrance to the highway connecting several of the smaller towns together. With his excitement held just beneath the surface Daniel watched as the parent chaperons and the few teachers that had come with them form the students up and take roll, just to make sure that everyone was accounted for of course. A few moments later the Ferry docked and a ramp descended making a clear pathway for the students and facility to move into the park.

It also catered to all ages as there were several areas marked about with an eighteen or older sign along with what looked like security guards at their entrances.
