    As you continue to read through the Cosmo, which you have become completely engrossed in, another girl, this one a teenaged blonde, comes over, offering her services. "Hi my name's Sara, and my boss just told me to go ahead and start on your complementary pedicure."

    Thinking to yourself that it might be a good idea since you're on your feet all day, you mean to reply, "Thanks Sara, why not? Just try not to do anything girly," while what comes out is, "Like, great, I was totally looking for someone to start on my toes, cuz it's like summer, and no girl's set without a pedicure to show off in her sandles." Sara just smiles at your response, and sets to work filing on the nails and exfoliating the skin.

    "All done here," Kim says, leaving you to admire her job. Your hands and arms are much more slender than they used to be and the nails on them, painted in the pale and glittery pink, extend at least an inch beyond the fingertips.
