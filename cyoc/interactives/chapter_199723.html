    She tallied up the votes, with the answer coming out to one she personally thought could be interesting. "And the winner isss~... An Anthropomorphic Bunny!" She giggled briefly as the messages came in.

    There seemed to be an almost unanimous response of people excited, whether it be the possibility of how cute she'd look, the thought of seeing someone become an animal, or a variety of other things. There were those who were screaming to just transform, though, to cover herself with fur.

    There were a few giggles as she read some of the comments, becoming more at ease with the current state. "I know you want me to change now, Buuuuttt.... I'll set another poll up, to determine what type of bunny I'll become!"

    Temporarily, she froze the chat, so it'd be easier to count. She started a timer. "Aaand go!" As she finished the 'Go', she unfroze it, opening it back up for everyone as she read through it.

    She had grown surprisingly comfortable, being watched while naked as a child was still a bit off but she'd grown a bit into it. But she couldn't wait to be able to properly cover her lower bits.

    The timer went on, and she kept counting each one. She soon realized just how bad of an idea it was to count them manually; There was such a wide variety of answers, she almost definitely missed a bunch. And as the poll reached it's end, she froze the chat, going back to make sure she properly tallied up everything.

    "Aaand the winner iss...!"
