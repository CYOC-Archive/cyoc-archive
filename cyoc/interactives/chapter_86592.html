    The Nose Knows

    added by A 9 years ago A O

    You wake up bright and early, eager to see if your plan has worked. Despite the fact that your own nose remains that of a dog, you are oddly hopeful now that it is possible that many others maybe be in the same predicament. Misery loves company, after all, and you stand a better chance of getting help if you aren't the only odd one out. You bound down the stairs and flip on the television.

    "...where a form of mass hysteria seems to have occurred. We have received about a dozen reports of people behaving strangely, acting in ways which can only be described as dog-like," the reporter calmly reads from the teleprompter. "So far authorities are baffled by this outbreak, and the only explanation psychologists have been able to offer is that of some type of group madness. However, no commonalities have been found among the affected parties, other than their relative proximity. Aside from their strange actions, they seem completely normal and in perfect health..."

    You collapse into the nearest chair, flabbergasted. You have not only failed, but the BOD has done something completely unexpected. After all, it was only supposed to affect the body, right? Why would it make people think like dogs? Would it do the same to you eventually?

    You puzzle over the disparity between what has happened to you and the fate of your victims. Your only viable conclusion seems to be that perhaps the mixture of BOD and the scented candles resulted in a strange new symptom. You remember hearing once how the sense of smell is tied to memory.

    That would mean what someone smells affects their brain, in a sense. Maybe that's the answer. Because the BOD was mixed with a scent designed to trigger memory, it changed people's brains, and since it was diffused, it didn't affect their bodies like it did mine!

    You feel a bit sorry for the people you have altered, especially since you don't know if the change is permanent, but you are still focused on your own problem. The mass canine hysteria has now drawn attention to your area, and a dog nose like yours will only stand out all the more, especially since no one else has one. You need to come up with a way to change back, or at least to hide what has happened to you. Hesitantly, you decide to...
