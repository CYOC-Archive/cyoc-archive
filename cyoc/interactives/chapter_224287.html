    while the people who were in the parking garage only got a small amount the people in the mall weren't so lucky. many of those in the garage ran away. unknowingly spreading the virus through New York. on October 15, 2023, a news crew had gathered in front of the mall. the CDC cordoned off the area as a possible bio attack from a foreign government.

    men and women dressed in hazmat suits entered the mall via the garage as it had the least dust. once they entered the mall itself it depicted a darker setting. broken glass littered the floor. collapsed shoppers and staff were everywhere. soon they got to the very center of the mall.

    they saw the hole the meteor came from and the one it now resides in.     As it finished cooling down the dust began to clear. once the dust began to clear people began coughing as they woke up one by one. Everyone in the mall was quarantined and led to a nearby public gym. As there were so many they had to be kept there. after gathering a sample of the dust they found it to contain an unknown virus.

    this virus could alter the genetic level of its victims. the time would vary but soon the emergency bio center was filled with former humans. sadly that was not the end of it. Several of those who had been in the parking garage had unknowingly spread the contagion by touch. thankfully a vaccine was made to remove this problem.
