    "But I didn't do anything wrong. I just brought this ball to this beach," Pam told the nurse.

    The moment Pam said that, the two of them were teleported from the hospital to a nearby beach. On this day there were a lot of people all over the place not to mention it was quite hot. Pam noticed that both she and the nurse were no longer wearing their normal outfits, but were now wearing swim suits.

    "Okay but next time be careful where you throw your ball," the nurse told Pam before proceeding to walk away.

    Pam was relieved to learn that she would no longer be arrested and that the argument she had with the nurse was because she accidentally hit her with her beach ball.

    Now, what's the next thing she should do?
