    Rachel in Wonderland: A Bug's Life

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago S O Shrinking

    After taking as little of the potion as she could, Rachel places the bottle on the floor next to the package and waits for the results. Fortunately Rachel didn't have to wait too long as her body began to shrink, along with her Alice in Wonderland outfit.
    "Aaa, that feels better." Rachel said as she began stretching her shrinking body and limbs. As Rachel reached her original size, she quickly realized that she was still shrinking.
    "Ah crap, I was afraid of this." Rachel said to herself. The shrinking spell continued unchecked, taking poor Rachel down to half her old height and then down to half of that size. Without any way of stopping it, Rachel could only sit and watch as the world around her grew to colossal proportions. Finally the shrinking spell subsided, leaving Rachel at 1 centimeter tall. Rachel picks herself off the floor and straightens out her dress, thinking about the new problem she has fallen into.
    "Oh my god. I'm so small. So very tiny. I was trying so hard and being so careful not to over shrink myself, but the little potion that I drank was more powerful than I thought. And to think, just a monent ago I was as large as my room. Now look at me, I'm smaller than a bug and I don't know if I can reverse this. I'm gonna be tiny dressed as Alice in Wonderland forever. What'll happen to me now?"
    Rachel stood in place trying her best to come up with a plan, but then an extremely strong wind suddenly blows her windows open and starts wizzing around the tiny woman. Before Rachel can react, her skirt and petticoats suddenly puff open like a parachute and catch the wind, lifting Rachel's feet off the ground and sending to poor girl flying through the air.
    "AAAAHHHHHHHHH! HEEEELLLLPPPP! MAKE IT STOOOOPPPPP!" Rachel shouted her tiny little lungs off. The wind then pushes its way back out the window, taking Rachel with it. Now flying in the open outside air, Rachel was now at the mercy of a much harsher and far more massive world. But at the moment, all Rachel wanted was to land on something soft and put her feet back on solid ground.
