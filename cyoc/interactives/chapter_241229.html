Then, with an alien pressure, her nose pushes forward. “Whah…huff huff ah-BOORF.” Her voice is deepening now, sounding less like a sweet girl and more like the fat hairy beast she was becoming.

She tries to undo the buckle on her flats, but her fat paws are too stubby to do anything. Said buckle soon snapped under the pressure of her growing soon to be paws. The leather flats stretch as the socks conceal the claw and fur growth temporally.

    “Naaoourrr-ARKF !” Her spine cracks and she falls forward onto her hands and knees. Accompanied by some gut wrenching crunches, her legs adjust backward so her knee joints are closer to her body.

What remains of her shirt rips off her arms as long dense black fur pushes out. Her cute braided hair simply falls off of her head replaced by a comparatively short coat of fur.
