    "OKay, I'll put them back!" the white clad cleric shrieked. His hand glowed magcally and swung down to touch
    Maedel.

    Maedel wasn't falling for this. She'd finally turned back into a regular elf ranger. No wooden skin or other
    bizarre modifications. Sure she was naked but that could be solved. She wasn't going to get transformed by
    this idiot now. She batted his hand aside and the energy was redirected to the Kings magic shield which
    instantly shattered.

    "A dispel spell?" Maedel asked puzzled at why the guy had used that, "Oh right! I'm wearing a magic proof
    necklace! I think I've had enough of you though."

    She batted him in the side of the head with the butt of her sword and he was out cold on the floor.


    "Okay, who's next?" Maedel shouted as she started circling the room.

    "Dispel the necklace!" the Lady Cecilia in Arn's body was shouting.
    "I've got no magic in this body," the pixie turned bee-girl answered.

    The half orc in Dili's body lunged at Maedel but a swift kick from the elf sent the little gnome body across
    the room and out like a light.

    The elf in Morgana's body surveyed the room and all of her fallen comrades. "Is it too late to change sides?
    I'm elf too, you know? I'm only doing it for money anyway!"

    "You cannot switch sides! I paid good money for your services, I'll have you--" the Kings tirade was cut short
    with a zap from the elf.

    "Quiet you!" said the new Morgana.

    In an instant the Kings legs lifted off the ground and swung up begind him as his whole body shrank and
    thinned out at the same time, His arm flew out and stretched round to join with his legs which had merged into
    a wide pink triangle. His face slipped down his thinning pink body and he took on a silky shine. His mouth had
    completely disappered into the lacy pink fabric. The King float in the air for a moment as a pair of pink silk
    panties before dropping to the floor in a heap.

    "Ugh... I guess we'll call it a truce then?" said Maedel.
