    As they flew deeper into the forest, Ty knew he had much to take in, because not only was his real mom a fairy, and not only was he a fairy too, but he was also the son of the fairy queen, making him the prince!

    Now in the past, Ty and his foster family had gone camping in these woods, but he'd never seen this fairy city he was entering!

    His mom Queen Rose Bloom explained to him as they flew that the fairies and their city are invisible to human eyes.

    Seeing one fairy having a conversation with a squirrel, Rose Bloom also explained to her son that fairies can understand and speak with animals!

    Ty was amazed. With their huge eyes, they could see what humanity could not, and with their long pointed ears, they could hear the language of all living things!

    All the fairies and animals looked happy to see him, and some of the lady fey his age even blew kisses his way, which made him blush a deeper shade of amber!

    It wasn't long before they reached the fairy palace which was a massive tree.

    If he was still human, he would've only seen a big tree, but thanks to his big fairy eyes, he saw the massive structure which was his real home!

    Fey servants blew on empty snail shells to announce the arrival of the queen, and her son who had come home after 15 years.

    In the dinning hall, a great banquet of freash berries was held.

    Queen Rose Bloom sat at the head of the table, and her son Prince Ty sat next to her.

    The fairies were so small that to them, a single blueberry was the size of a softball!

    They drank nectar out of flower cups.

    A group of lovely fey girls happily performed a dance in the air for Prince Ty's entertainment, and afterward a pair of them went to work massaging his shoulders.

    He could feel years of stress being massaged away by these two lovely ladies on either side of him.

    One said to him "We're happy you're here!" and the other one added "And now we don't have to fear!" "Our future is now secure, thanks to our noble prince pure." "If you don't mind me to say, you make a cute prince of the fey!" "Your eyes are so dreamy, and your skin feels so creamy!"

    And when they were done, they both kissed him on either side of his face, and they flew away giggling.

    When dinner was over, his mom led him to his bedroom.

    It was huge....well.....by fairy standards at least.

    Rose Bloom told her son that as Fairy Prince, he had the right to pick any girl for himself, even if she wasn't a fairy!

    He thought about some girls in high school he liked, but then again, these fairy girls were pretty much throwing themselves at him!

    Rose Bloom also told him that he'd receive an instructor to help him learn how to use his magic.

    "You'll find that Daisy Pettel teaches well, for she knows every trick and spell!"

    And with that, Rose Bloom kissed Ty goodnight, and Ty drifted off to a blissful sleep, wondering what lay in store for him as Prince of the Fey!
