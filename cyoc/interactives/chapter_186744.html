    His Little Sister Finds The Chronivac

    Before Jeff could think of something however, he hears his mom call him, “JEFF, I NEED YOU TO PEAL SOME POTATOES!”

    The 16-year-old grumbles, 'every time when I am busy,' he thinks to himself, 'she is sooooo fucking annoying'. Jeff then shouts back, “COMING MOM,” he then turns back to his screen, “should I minimize the program,” he asks himself. The boy thinks about it, but then he decides to just leave the program open, “won't be a problem, no one ever comes into my room anyway,” he mutters to himself, standing up and leaving his room, “besides, I'll be back in five minutes,” he mumbles as he walks down the stairs.

    Just as Jeff arrives in the kitchen, the door to his little sister's room opens and a small girl steps out, heading straight for Jeff's room. Alison, who is 12 years of age, is always nosy and curious, so every time when Jeff leaves his room, she sneaks inside to see if she can find anything interesting. She notices that Jeff had left his door open and with a smirk the girl walks straight in and stops in the middle of the room, looking around, her eyes land on Jeff's laptop. “What's this? Did Jeff get a new game,” she asks herself, walking over and sitting down at her brother's desk, “it looks like a weird game, it has everything about Jeff on the screen.... eeew even his penis sizes.” The girl searches around the screen and finds a tab labled “manual”. Alison decides to read the manual, for her age the girl is pretty smart, but still has the mind of a mischievous brat, a very VERY dangerous combination. The more Alison reads the manual, the more her eyes widen in disbelief, 'th-this thing really can do all of... THAT,' she asks herself, 'let's see if it is true,' she closes the manual and opens the “scanned” page, where every stats of scanned people are stored. The girl is shocked as she sees her name right under Jeff's. Alison clicks on her name and the screen shows her her stats:

    Name: Alison Davis
    Gender: female
    Age: 12
    Height: 4'8''
    Weight: 90 lb
    Hair: blonde shoulder length, pony tail
    Body hair: none
    Eye color: bright green
    Bra size: 32B
    IQ: 170
    Libido: very low
    Sexual orientation: straight
    Sexual interest: athletic boys
    Wearing: blue denim skinny jeans, white t-shirt with a heart on the front, white socks, pink slippers

    'Let's try something small,' the girl thinks to herself, and decides to change the color of her slippers. Alison changes the color from pink to sky blue and confirms the change. As she looks down her eyes widen as she watches the color of her slippers slowly changing, soon she is wearing sky blue slippers instead of pink slippers, 'i-it actually works,' she thinks to herself, 'I got to have this game!' The girl hurries into her room and returns with an USB stick, she inserts the stick into her brother's computer and copies the Chronivac onto it, she then pulls the stick out and puts it in her pocket, then she opens Jeff's stats again, so that Jeff won't realize that she was in his room. Alison then hurries into her room and locks her door, then she walks over to her desk and inserts the USB into her own laptop and installs the Chronivac.

    Meanwhile, Jeff returns to his room after pealing the potatoes, 'took longer than expected,' he thinks to himself, 'man... how I hate helping in the kitchen'. The teen sits down at his desk with a sigh, “now... where was I,” he asks himself, “ah yes, I was trying to think of something to change,” he says and starts to check out the different transformation packages, unaware that his sister has copied and installed the “game” to her laptop.

    After the installation is complete, Alison opens up the folder containing the transformation packages and scrolls through them. “This game is amazing,” the girl says to herself, “I can transform myself, animals, objects or other people into anything I want.” As Alison continues to scroll through the packages, her eyes land on a folder labeled “unbirth package”. Curious she opens the package and starts to read the description: “Unbirth package! Ever thought of going through pregnancy again, but not wanting to wait nine months? Or do you and your lover roleplay as mother and child? Or are you simply curious? This package can make your fantasy reality,” the message reads.

    “I always wondered what pregnancy might feel like,” Alison mumbles.

    The girl continues to read, “simply chose yourself and the person who wishes to or should become your baby. You can also decide which gender your “baby” should be. You can even make the ones around you unaware of your pregnancy. Furthermore you can decide on for how long the person should stay your baby, you can choose between a hour to years, or even decide if he/she should age naturally. You also can decide if your baby should be your race or a total different race! Now, we hope you and your partner will enjoy this package.”

    “Sweet, now who should I choose to become MY child,” the girl asks herself with a grin.
