    The Magic Shop

    Fantasy Bath Beads

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 5 months ago O Mythological Magic Shower

    You look around, not knowing exactly where to start. There's just so many items! And so interesting to look at too! The fantasy-themed items to buy are odd, strange and yet their very originality is what makes them so enticing to start with. But to start knowing where to look, you decide to go and browse what you like best to buy as far as toiletries go. And that is bath pampering products. You adore baths. It is what you do on Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays, to relax. The rest of the time you have to make do with the faster, simplest and more practical showers. But that is what life is and it allows you to indulge and appreciate your baths even more. You have a whole collection of bath bombs and bath beads, as well as bath powders. You spend hours in the bathtub and you should, as those things are unfortunately only usable once. You are lucky that your family house possesses a state-of-the-art bathroom, even though it technically belongs to your parents. You hope to be able to afford an even grander bathroom once you can have your own place and money. Actually, you're thinking about going into studying bath and bathroom products as a marketer or in the field of business. Everyone loves to pamper themselves in a hot and warm and delicate and humid environment. With luscious scents too. So you see what this store has to offer.
    Quite a lot on fact. With the same fantasy themes and packaging. The names are just as strange and enticing yet the smells emanating from these products do not show that all the money went into the marketing. The quality is there too. You know that because with time and research and reading, you've become an expert in looking at traps for consumers with a single glance. And the names are just as tempting as the scents and pictures on the packages.

    What do you do now?
