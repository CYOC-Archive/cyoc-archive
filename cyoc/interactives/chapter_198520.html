    Entity #: E-3693

    Codename: Bio-Latex

    Containment Procedures: E-3693 is to be housed within an airtight, Psychic-Shielded vault at Site-03. No less than five armed guards are to be present at the vault entrance at any time. All access to the vault must be approved in advance by Class-2 personnel. Any personnel or entity attempting to access the vault without proper authorization is to be disabled immediately, using lethal force if required to prevent entry. Any attempted security breach or activity within the vault by E-3693 is to be reported to the Site Director immediately, and lockdown protocols are to be put into effect for the site.

    Samples of E-3683-DER must be verified as psychically inert before release from the Site-03 Manufacturing Complex. Samples removed from the complex are to be considered Classified Materials, and kept within airtight containment units when not in use. All personnel who interact on a regular basis with E-3693-DER samples must undergo monthly screening for cognitohazardous side effects. Any use of samples, including bonding to personnel, must be approved by Class-3 or higher personnel.

    Description: E-3693 is an inert humnaoid lifeform,1.84 meters tall, weighing 98.72 kilograms, with morphology similiar to that of a human being. The entity exhibits a combination of male and female sexual characteristics: while the build is primarily typical for a female, with slender arms, legs and waist as well as unusually large mammaries and hips, it possesses a phallus and associated testes of an unusual size matching the chest and posterior's proportions. The lifeform's exterior and skeletal structure is composed entirely of a black organic compound similiar in several ways to modern latex materials, with some exceptions.

    This compound, colliqually referred to as Bio-Latex, has a resistance to physical harm that far exceeds any conventional terrestrial metals. Ballistic, bladed, piercing, and blunt weaponry have all proved ineffective at causing serious harm, with only high-energy and incideary weapons capable of penetrating the material and causing notable damage. The Bio-Latex has demonstrated a capability to perform self-repairs, healing over minor damage and even repairing major penetrations over long periods of time. Production of new Bio-Latex is achieved through an extremely effeicient matter-to-energy conversion process, capable of directly transforming any provided matter into an energy source to compel new growth.

    Examination of E-3693 shows that the entity is alive, but currently inert. Latent psychic activity has led to theorization that the entity is capable of psychic communication with other members of its race, with the capability for long-range communication likely damaged when it arrived on Earth. While not councious, the entity's biological processes have continued to sustain it even after [REDACTED] years of isolation in a state of stasis. Examination of sexual organs has show an extreme ability to generate further Bio-Latex in a liquid form; samples of this liquid Bio-Latex aggressively convert any organic matter into more Bio-Latex through a process of cellualr assimilation.

    E-3693 Was discovered in [REDACTED] on [DATE REDACTED], following reports of a crashed object of unknown origin. E-6393 was found among what appeared to be the wreckage of a craft composed of Bio-Latex, and brought to Site-12 for examination. Upon determination of the entity's properties, a specialized facility at Site-03 was established, later followed by the construction of an onsite manufacturing facility for E-3693-DER. For more information, see Addendum 3963-1.

    Addendum 3693-1: Research in [DATE REDACTED] by Dr. Stacy Johnson, a Consulting Researcher from the Prometheus Initative, determined that the Bio-Latex material could be synthetically replicated through manufacturing processes available to the Foundation and the United States Government. Following approval by 1, a manufacturing complex was established at Site-03 in partnership with Prometheus in order to produce E-3693-DER, colloquially known as Synthetex.

    Synthetex exhibits many of the same capabilities as Bio-Latex, including extreme durability, self-repair, and the ability to bond with and convert organic material. A nullifcation process in the manufacturing renders the material psychically inert. The introduction of additional genetic instructions, and variable DNA markers able to accept new instructions, allows any bonded lifeform to alter the morphology of the synthetex, providing a limited capacity for shapeshifting.

    Deployment of Synthetex by the Foundation and partnered covert units has been extremely successful. The material is currently used in a variety of applications, providing a malleable and extremely secure form of protection, and is utilized in the containment of multiple other Entities. As of [DATE REDACTED], Synthetex was cleared for utilization by human military forces; the Prometheus Initative has spearheaded this development, with major successes with their bonded units, dubbed the Latex Corps.
