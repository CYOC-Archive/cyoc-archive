    As Tall As The Ceiling

    added by scriptboy 18 years ago BM S

    Ty just stood there, admiring his reflection in the mirror, while a wide grin formed on his face. He knew he had the unique ability to change others, so why would he not have some fun while changing his own, scrawny, 5ft 7 inch tall, 124 pound, blond-headed body?

    He gave it some careful thought, and he decided that he didn’t want to gain too much weight.

    “I want to grow to ten feet tall, without gaining any weight!” he blurted out.

    A tingling sensation came over his entire body, and then he felt the room grow smaller. He looked up and realized that the ceiling was slowly coming closer and closer to his head. He then looked around the room, and he gasped when the room seemed to be shrinking.

    “This is so sweet…” Ty whispered to himself. He loved the feeling of growing taller.

    He then tilted his head down, when he noticed that the distance between his face and his feet was slowly increasing as well, confirming the fact that he was indeed growing taller!

    Suddenly, he banged his head against something that was hanging over him!

    “OUCH!! What the heck? I hit my head against the ceiling!” he exclaimed, when he looked up and noted the wooden bars, which ran across the ceiling of his bedroom.

    He remembered that the ceiling in his room was only nine feet from the floor, so that’s why he banged his head against the ceiling, and had to stand slightly bent over, which was very uncomfortable, for the giant, ten-foot-tall teenager.

    “Uhh… I’m a bit too big….” Ty thought to himself. “I’d better shrink a foot…”

    Slowly and surely, Ty’s legs, torso, and arms shrunk down in proportion, so that he lost exactly one foot in height.

    Ty then looked straight in front of him, and realized that the six-foot tall mirror was just three feet too short! His reflection in the mirror only went up to his stomach!

    “I wish the mirror were as tall as my room….” Ty whispered to himself. Immediately, the mirror started to grow taller, until it covered the entire height of the room, so that Ty could see his reflection from head to toe.

    Ty was exactly as tall as the room itself, so he felt the top of his blond hairs rubbing against the ceiling, every time he slightly moved his head a little bit.

    “Wow… NBA, Here I come!” Ty chuckled, since he realized that he could make himself to be the tallest basketball player around.

    The only thing he didn’t like was the fact that he grew over three-and-a-half feet taller, but he kept the same weight! So, he was as skinny as a board!

    “Yuck… I’m way too skinny!” he thought, as he looked at his thin arms and his long, thin legs. “Where are all the muscles?”

    Ty turned sideways, and he then turned around, while trying not to let his gaze leave his reflection in the tall mirror.

    “I can change my body any way I want to…” he laughed to himself. “I love my powers, this is so VERY kewl!”

    Ty raised his arms until he touched the ceiling, realizing that he was indeed nine feet tall!

    “I’m as big as Goliath!” he chuckled, as he referred to the character from the Bible, who was also nine feet tall.

    He turned sideways one more time, and decided it was time to change his body again, for he got bored with standing nine feet tall, while he was as skinny as a board.

    “I wanna be a nine feet tall bodybuilder!” he cheered out loud. “Gimme four-hundred pounds of muscle, right now!”

    Ty started to change…
