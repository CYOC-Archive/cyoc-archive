My best guess is that you're worried about Janice and Snowflake and their health and to such a level that you've begun releasing this hormone, and given how much and how long you've worried about your younger family members that would be something that was triggered over time. It isn't a sudden stress that would potentially affect your pregnancies negatively. It's lengthened your pregnancies in this case... but in the course of this, I've never seen anything that would indicate that your children are not healthy..."
