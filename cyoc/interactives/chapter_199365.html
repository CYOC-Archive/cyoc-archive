    Jen turned around. She kept her head down, averting her eyes from what the enclosure contained. She really didn't want to know...

    "Jen, look! I didn't know these were so big!" Susan grabbed her hand, and Jen instinctively looked up. She looked at Susan, but in the corner of her eye she saw it. A bull moose, sporting an impressive set of antlers. As soon as she recognized it, it charged.

    It burst through the glass, barreling straight towards Jen. All of her friends screamed and ran out of the way of the massive herbivore, but Jen remained frozen in place. She wanted to scream, to cry, to run, but it was like her legs were nailed to the ground. She couldn't move a muscle.

    The moose stopped, turned around behind her, and began to lick at her genitals. She shuddered as the huge beast began to push its muzzle into her. It went farther and farther into her, stretching her body beyond comprehension. She felt no pain, but only a bit of pleasure. Then the antlers started.

    Jen screamed as they tore through her, the curse handling her wounds swiftly. They remained protruding from her abdomen for a while as the moose pushed deeper. Soon, they had dissolved, as had the moose's head. Jen's legs quickly atrophied, and the moose's neck lifted her torso. She rose, still frozen, higher and higher into the air. New feelings rushed through her body, and her head began to throb. Tears streamed down her face as her antlers grew in, her ears changing into those of a moose.

    Soon, her transformation was complete. She stood in the cold exhibit hall, towering over her classmates. Her torso swiveled frantically around as she ran her fingers through her coarse, brown fur. The weight of her massive antlers strained her neck muscles, and she had to be careful not to turn her head too hard. This couldn't be real, could it? She looked over at the exhibit housing the moose, where mere moments ago a huge bull moose had come barreling through the allegedly indestructible glass. It was as if nothing had happened. Jen couldn't believe it. All her classmates were watching the moose, and in the other corner of the zoo, the reindeer, oohing and ahing.

    She looked down at her friend Susan, now dwarfed by her moosetaur body. Jen guessed she had to be almost 10 feet tall, standing well over 6 feet where her human torso met her moose lower half. She then realized Susan was saying something.

    "...I don't even know why I'm telling you this, Jen, haha, I bet you know everything about moose, being one and all!"

    "Haha, yeah.." Jen trailed off. She still couldn't believe this was really happening! Just then her ears perked up as their teacher beckoned everyone back out of the ice age exhibit. Jen lumbered out of the building, careful not to hit her head on the door frame. She had to duck until her human torso was almost horizontal with the ground, and even then she bonked her antlers into the doorframe with a loud CLANG and an ow as she stumbled back into the building. After she got her bearings straight, she then went through the door one antler at a time.

    "Careful Jen, haha!" Susan laughed as Jen rubbed the base of her antlers. "Damn that hurt," she sighed. She followed her classmates out to a large clearing, where they sat around for lunch. Jen tucked her legs underneath her huge moose body and sat by Susan. They had signed a paper the day before as to which lunch option they wanted. Jen remembered that she had said she wanted a ham sandwich, but instead she got a special mix of leafy taiga leaves and berries. "For your special dietary needs," her teacher said, patting her bony, furry front knee.

    As her classmates began eating, Susan and Jen talked. However, Jen heard someone laughing, and her ears swiveled in response. It was Kyle, and he was clearly laughing at her! What did that little freshman twerp want?
