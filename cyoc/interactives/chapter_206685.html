    Morgan takes the Ring of Change

    Morgan grunted in pain, Jacks spell having launched the sorceress into an adjacent wall. Obviously Jack wasn't holding back, the warlocks eyes a murky green and black as she now approached. Morgan though was ready.

    "I'm disappointed grandpa," she sneered. "You're supposed to be the guardian of darkness. Surely you can do better!"

    Jack roared, the warlock now useing his magic to punish Morgan. The womans body now slamming into the walls and ceiling as he continued his onslaught. "Is that good enough," he roared, slamming Morgans prone body into a wall. Morgan winced, extracting herself from a sizable hole as she sneered at Jack.

    Morgan smirked, Jack was angry. The warlock consumed with rage at Morgan's attack on Amanda. Tbis was all part of her plan. In a one on one scenario Jack was more powerful, being a near God with unlimited access to the powers of darkness. Still, he had weakness. Namely those he looked out for whether they be demons, anthros, or even Vixi.

    Grunting Morgan now assumes lycan form. The sorceress's body growing in size, her muscles increasing as fur began to cover her body. As her mouth now extended into a muzzle of razor sharp teeth, she growled at Jack.

    Jack furrowed his brow as he gazed at the Lycan sorceress. So accustomed to Morgan's snow white werewolf form, he was slightly taken aback. The snow white fur was now streaked with black, the werewolves eyes now pools of inky darkness instead of the crystal blue he was accustomed to. Regardless he needed to finish this quickly. Morgan's strength nearly rivaled his own.

    Launching spell after spell Jack grimaced as Morgan deftly dodged his attacks. The spells now crashing against the walls, shakening the very foundation of the castle. Morgan was fast, Jack now beginning to worry as he listened to the sad cries of the pink kitten that was Amanda.

    Morgan smiled, watching as Jack glanced back at that pink cheetah kitten entangled in her discarded clothing. Already she could tell that Jack was summoning someone to take Amanda to safety. This is what Morgan wanted, once they appeared she would make her move.

    Jack now cast a spell, a series of chains now restraining Morgan as he glanced back at Amanda. He needed to get her out of her, keep her safe until he could reverse Morgan's spell. Glancing at the entrance to the throne room, he breathed a sigh of relief as Vixi now entered. "Vixi grab Amanda and go!"

    Vixi was at first puzzled by Jacks request. Looking down to where he pointed, she first fanned over the adorable cub before realizing it was Amanda. Rusbing to the kitten, the animatronic now scooped her up as she looked to make a quick escape.

    This was what Morgan was waiting for. The lycan sorceress now vanishing, appearing before Vixi with a malicious int in her eyes. Vixi was shocked, the animatronics eyes growing wide as Morgan looked to tear her to pieces.

    Instead, Vixi gasped watching Jack now appear in front of her. Morgan now tore through the warlocks chest. Vixi gasping in fright as a hairy arm now protruded from Jacks back. Morgan had torn through Jacks chest, critically wounding the warlock.

    Jack coughed, thick black blood now dripping from his chin as he collapsed to the ground. If he hadn't been immortal the wound have been fatal. Morgan had destroyed his heart, lungs, and a portion of his spine, leaving the warlock unable to move below his chest. Laying in a heap he now gazed up as the Lycan sorceress now grasped Vixi by the neck.

    "We can do this all day Jack," she sneered. "We both know each of us can battle for eons never tiring." Morgan now tightened her grip, Jack listening to the metal of Vixi's neck bend and break.

    "You may be willing to sacrifice yourself but are you willing to sacrifice theirs?"

    Jack growled, it would take sometime for him to heal. Morgan could kill most of the parks populace before he could fight again.

    "Give me the ring," Morgan stated. "And no one has to die."

    Jack narrowed his eyes, his face contorted in rage as he answered. "Done."

    Morgan smiled, the lycan sorceress watching as a small silver ring now floated towards her. Releasing Vixi she now plucked the ring from the air, giving Jack a sacrene smile.

    "Thank you grandpa," she smiled. "Tell grandma I said hi. I promise to visit again soon." A sinister chuckle now left Morgan's lips, the sorceress vanishing with her Ring of Change.
