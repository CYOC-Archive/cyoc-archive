    Holding Ben under the arms Don laughs and says, "That's better, isn't it? I guess you'll just have to wear that for a little bit while I take a break. Haven't been able to take one at all today with all the people who don't show any self control using the site. I'm sure you won't mind if I get something from the kitchen." Having said that he sets the now diapered baby boy down on the floor and turns toward the kitchen before Ben can voice his displeasure.
    Now sitting alone in the middle of the room within site of the computer that had been the cause of his unfortunate situation, Ben's thoughts are racing with thought's of how much he didn't want to wait any longer to return to his normal age. Determined to maintain at least some of his dignity and not allow, the man who was now rummaging through his kitchen see him cry he put all his focus on remaining calm.
    A few minutes later his focus was broken as Don returns to the room with a sandwich on a plate and a canned soda. Sitting down he smiles at the baby sitting on the floor and picking up the TV remote turns it on and begins flipping through stations while eating.
    Ben's attention was suddenly pulled away from the stranger ,who had seemingly made himself at home, by an uncomfortable feeling in his stomach that felt as if some weight was being pushed downward. Placing his chubby hands over his stomach Ben pushed back hoping that the strange feeling would go away. Before he realized what it was he felt the pressure quickly release into the back of his diaper as a warm mush that made it's way between his legs and filled up every space it could reach in the back of the absorbent garment. With the mass making him even more uncomfortable he found it increasingly difficult to maintain his composure.
    Only a few minutes later that seemed like an eternity while Ben was trying to decide what he should do to prevent the repairman from noticing his accident, Ben noticed a new pressure this time in front. Realizing what this must mean, Ben tries desperately to hold it but his weak infant bladder cannot contain the liquid and he begins to wet himself as well. The absorbent material soaks up his new accident becoming soggy and making him even more uncomfortable and worried about what would happen if Don noticed.
