    "Are you all right?" asked Lina.

    "What happened?" said Samantha curiously, "did yoo--oou take something in the store?"

    "I'm fine," said Amy, though the flicking of her ears and tail betrayed her tension, "and no, I didn't try anything new in the store today. Today was just for you two."

    "Then why did you just get small?" said Samantha.

    "The shake must have worn off," said Amy, more to herself than the other two girls. She turned, noticing Sam's confused look and explained, "I drank a protein shake from a vending machine back when I was find ways to be horsier. It gave me a mare's physique."

    "Geeighhuess it wasn't meant to last that long." she said, somewhat ruefully, plucking at her loose shirt with one hand while holding up her shorts with the other. "I wonder how long the other changes I have will last? I didn't check at each stop I made."

    "Well," said Lina, lightly punching the slender mare's arm, "eee-ee-eeven if a bunch of it fades, it's not like we can't go back and redo it!"

    "True enough," said Amy, looking a bit better as she adapted to her new frame, "let's hurry to your place though, Sam, it's gonna beeighh hard to walk while holding my pants up."
