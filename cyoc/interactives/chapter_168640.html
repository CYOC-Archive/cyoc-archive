    After his transformation, Billy was busy flexing at himself in the mirror. God, he felt so much bigger and stronger than he did before, and this was only after eight months! Eight months of workouts in only a few seconds! He could only imagine what he would look like after a few more years of workouts... Wait only in a few seconds? That gave him an idea.

    Billy pulled out his phone to text Luis:

    Hey Luis can u come over to my house? I have something to show u. Say that u accidentally took home my history textbook and we're going to do homework together to your Mom. Pls it's urgent.

    It didn't take long for him to get a response:

    K give me 10 mins.

    Alright, good. Now, all he had to do was wait, so in the meantime, Billy turned to his weight bench to test out his strength. Laying down on the bench, he pumped out a few reps before stopping. His usual benching weight was too light for him now, and Billy was ecstatic about that fact. He then got up and added another ten pounds and then continued his workout. Yeah, this was a much better weight for his new age and strength. After he finished his 24 reps, Billy got up from the bench to find Luis standing in his doorway, looking quite dumbfounded.

    "B-billy, what happened to you? You look..." Luis began before Billy interrupted.

    "Bigger? Stronger? Maybe even more mature?" Billy responded with a cocky smirk on his face. He then flexed his arm just to show Luis that he was indeed stronger.

    "Yeah... but how did this happen?" Luis asked while gawking at Billy's bigger muscles. That look of awe Luis gave was something that Billy quite enjoyed.

    "A cool man at the flea market gave me this cube over here that allows me to change people's ages, and I made myself 14." Probably best not to mention how shady Mr. Gaunt looked yet, nor should he mention that the cube leeched the years off of you either. Actually, now that he thought of it, didn't Luis' Uncle own that Printshop? Printing out a few fliers would more than return the favor to Mr. Gaunt.

    "Hey Luis, I know that you were always jealous about my build and wanted to be as buff as I am. Well, now you can with this cube, maybe even buffer. So, what do you say? You want to stay the same skinny kid, or do you want to become a buff stud like me? I can always change you back if for some reason you don't like it."

    Luis was taken aback. On the one hand, a cube that can change your age, and Billy was gifted it by a stranger from the flee market? What if something went wrong? What if it ages him into dust or something like that!? Yet, on the other hand, this was his chance to finally stop being so scrawny and become strong like Billy was... Billy did say that he could change him back, right?

    Sure about his choice, Luis looked Billy in the eye and gave him his answer. "Okay. I'll do it!"

    Billy smiled at the response, "Good choice. Now I can't use the cube to change you just yet. You don't work out, so you'll probably just end up scrawny even when you're older. But if I make you work out before I change you, then maybe we can trick the cube."

    And with that, the two of them headed towards the weights and Billy began coaching Luis how to lift. One grueling full-body workout later, and Luis was lying down on Billy's bed, exhausted and sweating profusely. Leeching Cube in hand, Billy approached his tired friend, finally ready to use the cube on him.

    "Alright, good job. Now, just imagine yourself doing that regularly, and let's see if this will work. Hmmm, how about I make you my age now. Age Luis two years!"
