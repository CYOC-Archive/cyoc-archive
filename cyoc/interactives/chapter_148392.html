    Lupo smiled confidently. "But don't let the ease at which I've tamed these beauties fool you, dear audience. These humanoid tigresses are much wilder than they appear. If she'd stood up for herself then--kicked him in the kneecap and scooted off--she'd have been spared being groped at the movies. Similar ideas played out in Joy's mind. The girls looked at each other, foreheads touching. They narrowed their eyes and smiling knowingly.

"You'd be lucky never to find yourself the subject of their ire, I can tell you that. They carry all the instincts of a savage beast and the cunning of human intellect in one alluring package. In the wilds of the urban jungle, they're the lords and everyone is their prey."

She'd been thinking about going for drinks after this, but images of prime ribs appeared next to her Mai Tais and whiskey sours. A punk invaded her daydream to steal her purse. She saw herself chase after him, tackle him to the ground and claw his eyes out. Onstage, she snarled in delight.
