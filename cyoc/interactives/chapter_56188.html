    You enter the tent, and can see the popcorn area of the tent. You walk up to the counter and ask for popcorn.
    "That'll be 5 dollars please." The man at the counter says.
    You pay him 5 dollars and walk away with a thank you.
    Yiu then find somewhere to sit. Not too close because you don't want your face to be burned. Not too far because you don't want people to block your view. So you sit in the middle part
    of the seating area of the circus. At last, the circus starts.
    "Welcome to this state of the art circus!" beams the man in a suit. You remember him. When you was outside, he appeared to ask you if you wanted to see the circus. "I'm Joe Andrews,
    and this is no ordinary circus. This is Dance Revolution!" The crowd cheers, and so do you. "We have five acts all performing on this solid ground, then we can get the circus going!"
    ~Joe Andrews~ you remember. ~He asked if I wanted to go to the circus. Now I know this is no ordinary circus. This must be the Grand Finale of Dance Revolution!~
    "First act performing 'air' by DJ Simon is The hot girls!" Joe announces, and the crowd cheers.
    The hot girls(Angie,, Jessica, kelly, Maria and Suzan) take their positions and the music begins to play.

    After 1 minute and 15 seconds, Joe returns with a letter in his hand.
    "We will get to the second act in a bit, but first, I have to read this letter." Joe announces. And Joe reads the letter, saying that one person from the crowd will be able to pick the
    winner. "We've got Jamie around here?" he asks. You throw your hand up, and so does a few other Jamies.
    "Jamie Jackson?" He asks again.
    "I am." You say.
    "The letter says that you, my friend will be able to pick a winner." Joe says.
    You accept, and decide to let the show go on.
    "Second act performing 'Tsugaru(Apple Mix)' by RevenG vs DeSire is J-man!" Joe then announces and the crowd cheers.
    J-man(Daryl, Charlie, Adam, George and Henry) take their positions and the music begins to play.

    After 1 minute and 38 seconds, Joe returns.
    "After two acts, The hot girls have 20 points(5 from Dan, 4 from James, 6 from Lisa and 5 from Craig) and J-man have 19 points(5 from Dan, 4 from James, 5 from Lisa and 5 from Craig)!"
    The crowd went wild for both acts.
    "Third act performing 'AM-3P(303 Bass Mix)' by Ktz Remixed by U1 is CASAP!" Joe announces. The crowd went wild, and so do you.
    CASAP(Colin Fouque, Andrew Stein, Sam Lothar, Alex Ivarov and Phillip Espimas) all took their positions and wait for the music to play. You feel nervous and sit down on your seat. At
    last, the music plays, and the boys began to dance.

    After 1 minute and 33 seconds, Sam and co. look tired, but they got maximum scores of 40(10 from Dan, 10 from James, 10 from Lisa and 10 from Craig).
    The crowd went wild. Sam and Alex couldn't believe their luck.
    "That was entertaining!" Cheered one of the boys.
    "We love CASAP!" The crowd chanted as Sam and co. leave the arena. Joe returns with two acts and the leaderboard.
    "Here's how it stands: 1.CASAP with 40 points
    2.The hot girls with 20 points
    3.J-man with 19 points."
    The crowd cheered in delight.
    "Fourth act performing 'CUTIE CHASER' by Club Spice is Spice!" The crowd went wild.
    Spice(Melanie B, Melanie C, Emma, Victoria and Geri) took their places and the music begins to play.

    After 1 minute and 27 seconds, CASAP still lead with 40 points, Spice pick up 27(9 from Dan, 5 from James, 9 from Lisa and 4 from Craig), The hot girls got 20 and J-man got 19. Joe
    returns.
    "Final act performing 'Do Me' by Moustache Men is Topeka!" Joe announces. The crowd went wild.
    Topeka(Lee, Mark, Julie, Alice and Brittany) took their places and the music begins to play.

    After 1 minute and 30 seconds, Joe returns with the leaderboard.
    "After all of our acts, we have: CASAP in the lead with 40 points, Spice with 27 points, Topeka with 24(5 from Dan, 4 from James, 8 from Lisa and 7 from Craig) points, The hot girls
    with 20 points and J-man with 19 points. Jamie Jackson, will you please come on down to announce the winner." Joe announces.
    You stroll down to the gate, but you've got the bad news: Theie's security guarding it. "Excuse, me." You say. "Are you Jamie Jackson?" The security asks.
    "Yes I am." you reply.
    The security opens the gate and you enter the arena. All five acts are waiting to hear who is the winner. "Jamie, who do you think has won the competition?" Joe asks.
    "Well," You say. "It's a difficult choice, The hot girls have been using the techniques with their mouths, J-man wasn't entertaining people, Spice as we well know are the Spice Girls,
    but dancewise, they picked up what J-man have missed, Topeka had an accident with Alice and Lee bumping heads, but they continued to pull off tjese amazing techniques. The act that
    blew me away was CASAP. Their flips were awesome, they timed their routine perfectly, and deserved to be the champions. n a whole, the winner is..."
