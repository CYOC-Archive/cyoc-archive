Aisha took a break from thinking about the medallion to look back at her friend. "Yeah? Is that bad or something?" Mary shrugged, sitting back. "Well, no. Not really. But we don't know if this whole thing works purely from touch or if wearing the clothes also works. We definitely know coming into contact with fabric which is being worn by someone else changes their body respective to where the clothing was worn, like that woman on the bus and her breasts."

    Aisha opened her mouth to speak, but closed it, thinking. "Yeah..... I guess-" Mary interrupted her. "Hey, let me try something." Taking the medallion off and paling it on the table, Mary ran upstairs and left Aisha alone to think for herself. What was all this? Was it just a very, VERY lucid dream? Was it a prank? Was she high, or-? No, not that last one. Aisha had never done drugs in her life. Well, except for that one time in college. But the smoke made her cough and she hated how paranoid the stuff had made her. So there was no was this whole thing was a hallucination. Though, at the same time, there was still a chance this was real life, which was even more alarming.....

    Mary came back down, a few pieces of clothes in her arms.
