She transformed into an anthro dragon. Her face reformed. She drank more than Mike at the first time so she had a similar appearance to Mike. But she had huge breasts, red scales and wings.

    Mike was jealous. His instincts kicked in and with a roar he tackled the dragoness with all his might. He was so fast that he and the surprised dragoness both landed in the lake.

    In the lake something strange happened. They both swallowed a lot of water. But this time the transformation was not as before. Mike felt a strange tugging at his body. At the same time strange thoughts flooded his mind. Suddenly he knew everything about this woman. He knew that her name was Jennifer and that she used to be a female bodybuilder in highschool. And he knew that she came here to visit her parents in a laboratory. This was strange. But he didn't feel the dragoness even more. He also couldn't see her. So he decided to go out of the lake.

So what happened. He couldn't see the woman anywhere so... That's it. He and the dragoness merged. That explains the strange thoughts. Luckily for Mike, he was in control. But now he had the lust for power of two animals.

Then he stopped and went out of the lake waiting for the change. It began with his legs. In a matter of seconds new thick veins popped up. His leg muscles started to enlarge. After five minutes he was twenty times bigger than before. Then his growth slowed down. Now standing at 300ft Every muscle was clearly visible through his thick scales. With a wingspan of 600ft combined with his unbelievable huge back and wing muscles he could easily release a storm that would destroy a whole city. And with his 300ft tail he could crush a building. The feeling of power flowed through his veins.

    "YESSS. PURE POWERRR. THISSS FEELSSS SSSO GOOD." Mike roared.
