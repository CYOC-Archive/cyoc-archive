    Gloria, Lisa, and the rest of the Moreland family were impressed with Spark's first time cooking.

    "Spark, you really outdid yourself, this is some of the best food I've had in a while." Connor complimented.

    "Hey, what about my cooking?" Nina asked.

    "Oh, you're cooking is really good too mom, but Spark's is just a little more....magical to me." Connor complimented as Spark blushed.

    "Ah, I see, you inherited a Moreland trait son. The way I knew I fell in love with your mother was with her cooking." Jacob said as Nina nodded thanking her husband knowing he was counting down the moments until sunset.

    "I guess I'll know how much I love Caleb when I cook for him." Gloria said as everyone including Caleb laughed when he gagged on his own dinner.

    After a while, it was nearly sunset and Connor, Spark, Nina, Lisa, and Jacob all went outside to watch it. Caleb and Gloria were about to follow when Lisa stopped them.

    "Hold on, you two were the last ones inside the house before dinner, that means you two are stuck with dishes." Lisa pointed out as Gloria stuck her tongue out at her young mother.

    Connor and Spark stood close to each other watching the sunset.

    "I never really thought how beautiful it is." Spark commented.

    "I guess you only really see the beauty of a sunset with someone special." Connor said.

    "Yeah, a shame this has to come to an end." Spark sighed.

    "Damn it, Connor, Spark, I...I suppose I was a little bit...unfair with you two before. Connor, I told you two that I didn't think you two should date because of your age but, I realize I was acting more like my own mother than like myself." Nina admitted.

    "I thought you said grandma always knew what she was talking about?" Connor asked.

    "She once told me I was too young to drive at 19, the woman didn't know everything! At any rate, Connor, Spark, if you two want to date each other, I'll be more than happy to let you two date." Nina said.

    "Yes!" Connor and Spark both cheered as they noticed the sun setting.

    "Oh! I better make this quick! I saw this from an old photo once." Connor said as he bent Spark down and gave her a passionate kiss on the lips which she returned.

    The kiss felt like it lasted an eternity before they both broke it off as they saw each other return to their true forms as well as Lisa and Nina become their normal ages. Spark then sat down on Connor's shoulder as they both walked back to the house.

    "You know it was fun pretending we were sisters, you mind if we schedule doing this for another time?" Nina asked Lisa.

    "Sure, I'd love to." Lisa replied.

    "Maybe not too soon honey, I want us to have a little quality time too." Jacob said as he wrapped his arm around his wife's shoulder as they made their way inside the Moreland home.
