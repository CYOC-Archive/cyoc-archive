    The Pleasure Island Coachman and his staff and crew, have grown bored and tired of turning bad kids into donkeys, and even if they didn’t the mainlanders just are not interested in donkeys anymore. The Pleasure Island people stopped turning bad kids into donkeys just in time because the customer demand for donkeys went from sky high to non-existent. Instead the Coachman wants to try something different.

    For some reason the Coachman feels like being in a Japanese spirit and he decides that Japanese spirits or Yokais are now the answer, Pleasure Island is now renovated to be more Japanese. Hotels are made to be Japanese Pagodas with hotel rooms in them, bathhouses and hot baths are now in Hot Springs, there are shrines and temples to Foxes and Raccoon dogs and rivers and bridges to cross them, and the food stalls now have things like sushi and rice balls on them.

    Yes the Coachman has decided to turn kids into Yokai and sell them to Japan or for those that might be of use to them, he will keep them.

    You are on a boat that is heading towards the new Pleasure Island that has been renovated to be Yokai Island, there are many different Japanese exclusive festivals, food, drinks, and other things happening around here, you couldn’t be anymore excited to be here.

    However little did you know that by being here you are in danger of being turned into a female Yokai.
