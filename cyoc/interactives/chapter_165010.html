    Andrew sat on the couch, wondering what had happened to him. First, he grew from being about 5 feet tall to a staggering 7 feet tall. That was amazing for a guy who was 17 years old! He used to be shorter than Ty (who is now 5-foot-4), but Ty had given him a growth spurt.

    “Gosh, this is kinda cool that I get to hang out with ya, Tyler! I never get to come over to your place. I mean… Your parents never used to let me come over and your brother was always acting kinda weird towards me…” Andrew sighed.

    “Don’t worry about Dustin now! He won’t mess with you anymore!” Ty grinned.

    “Are you sure? Last time I came over, he pushed me away! I don’t want any problems with your big bro!” Andrew replied.

    “Don’t worry, Andy! You’re my friend! Dustin won’t mess with you!”

    “But… But… Dustin’s bigger and stronger than me! He’ll kick me out!” Andrew cried out.

    “Ummm… I’ll make sure that won’t happen!” Ty assured him. “You’re bigger, taller AND stronger than Dustin!”

    Andrew stood up and stared at Ty. He was deeply concerned. He wanted to leave. Just as he was about to say goodbye, he was overcome by a strong, tingling sensation. Andrew was already 7 feet tall, so a foot taller than Dustin. Now, thanks to Ty, his muscles started to inflate all over his body, making the tall, lanky teen transform into a big, tall teenage bodybuilder, all within a matter of seconds!

    Andrew’s chest pushed outwards while his arms grew thicker. His shoulders became very wide as his abdominal muscles on his long torso became very defined. His long, thin legs suddenly grew into thick, solid pillars of muscle as they widened into big and powerful columns containing quads and calves which made his legs seem as thick as oak trees. Andrew suddenly seemed BIG and TALL!

    “TY! Who the hell is in your room?” Dustin shouted as he stormed into Ty’s room.

    “Uhhhhh….” Andrew stuttered, as he looked down at Dustin.

    Dustin’s jaw dropped to the floor when he saw the LARGE, TALL and VERY muscular teen standing before him.

    “Dustin, you know my friend Andrew, right?” Ty giggled.

    “Ohhh…. Oh, wowww….” Dustin gulped, when he stood face to face with the Hulking teenage boy, who was clearly much bigger, taller and stronger than him.

    “Do you have a problem with Andrew, Dustin?” Ty asked him innocently.

    “N-n-no, no… Not at all… It’s Okay, Ty… I-i-it’s c-c-cool…” Dustin stuttered as he meekly turned around and walked out of the room, closing the door behind him.

    “There! Now that we got THAT taken care of… What shall we do for fun, big guy?” Ty asked his big pal.
