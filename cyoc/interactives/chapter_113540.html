    Childish Fun

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Dragonman 6 years ago O

    "I want to play Mom, I want to play sooo badly right now!" Bloom said jumping around.

    "Well, it's good to see that you're full of energy since the transformation. Okay let's go." Flora said

    Flora, and Bloom exited the temple, and walked into a village where the children were playing. The parents were shocked to see Bloom, but she didn't even take notice until a woman asked her.

    "Your the Bloom from the legend right about rebirth, and reincarnation?" The woman asked

    "Rebirth, and reincarnation are what I represent besides childhood joy. I had an out of body experience for a long time, but now I'm all better. I'm here to make up for lost time, and do what I always like to do." Bloom said, and the woman just looked puzzled.

    "I think I should just explain what happened. Play nice Bloom." Flora said, and Bloom rushed off to play with the village kids.

    Bloom rushed over, and the kids began staring at her.

    "Your a dragon, who are you?" A boy asked

    "You probably have never heard of me, before because I haven't been able to do anything for a long time. I'm Bloom, I'm a nature dragoness, so no fire, and I represent rebirth, and child happiness overall. I'm just here to play with you, and see if I can make up for being gone for so long." Bloom said

    "How long were you gone?" A girl asked

    "I don't know, I don't get older so it's hard to keep track of time. Don't worry I won't bite, or anything, I just want to have fun after being gone all those years." Bloom said

    Most of the kids shrugged, and let Bloom join in, while the others were still shocked to see a dragon playing with humans. Bloom played games like tag, hide and seek, and even raced some of them around the center of the village. Eventually the time came to leave, and Bloom waved goodbye, and returned to the sanctuary with Flora.
