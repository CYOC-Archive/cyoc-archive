    Toni looked down at her crying little sister, Monica. Monica like her had been bought by the Goblin Club owner Wrar who now planned to earn on his investment by putting the two sisters together as a group price.
    “Monica…” said Toni as she ran her hands through her sisters now bright gold hair. “Let’s get you dressed.”
    “Alright…where are we anyhow? How did we get here and what happened to you?” inquired Monica “You have been gone for 6 months.”
    “I was taken, just like you.” Said Toni “Come on lets get you dressed.”
    The next few minutes was going into the group closet and getting something that would fit Monica. While the two of them where sisters and they looked like sisters there where some differences which prevented Monica from wearing her sister’s cloths. Toni was taller then her sister, while Monica had a bigger bust and was overall curve. Lucky one of Toni’s friends cloths fit and she just took them, knowing she could pay them back later. Finally, in a semi-normal top with a skirt Monica was able to sit down on the bed.
    “What…happened?” inquired Monica
    “Well turns out magical creatures are real and they kidnap people and transform them into new magical creatures that is what happened to us.” Stated Toni “I am a Drow…a dark elf basically and you are a sun elf.”
    “How…how do we get home?” inquired Monica
    “We can’t.” stated Toni “We are going to be like this for the rest of our lives.”
    “There has to be a way…” said Monica
    “Monica…there may be a way but first we have to get our freedom.” Stated Toni
    “Freedom?” inquired Monica as Toni waved to her leather top and skin tight black pants and high heel shoes.
    “Do you think I really want to wear this?” inquired Toni “I am the black sheep of the family but I am not this much of a black sheep. We where bought…we are basically slaves however unlike other places we can earn our freedom.”
    “Slaves…we are slaves? Like the Jews in the old testament?” inquired Monica
    “Well I don’t think those slaves had a chance to buy their own freedom like we do and…” started Toni as she prepared for another breakdown. “And…I don’t think those where used as strippers, dancers and whores.”
    “What?” inquired Monica her eyes widened as Toni took a breath, both of them came from a religious family. Monica was more religious then Toni, Monica had taken multiple mission trips and was active in the church.
    “We are whores now.” Said Toni with a sad look on her face. “Come tonight, that Goblin Mr. Wrar will put you and me in room 1 and sell us for the evening to the highest bidder and they will have their way with us.”
    Monica just looked at her with a look of pure terror on her face.
    “In the end, we will get paid and a large percentage of that money will go to Mr. Wrar for our food and board however we will get to keep a small part of it.” Explained Toni “And we can use that amount to buy our freedom and be able to go out and attempt to make our way in this world.”
    “No…I will not do that!” yelled Monica as she jumped to her feet in anger
    “YES YOU WILL!” snapped Toni as she jumped up as well and looked at her sister. “I don’t like it either…but if you don’t or if you hurt the buyer then Mr. Wrar will sell you to a far worse place.”
    “What could be worse then this!” yelled Monica
    “This…but with no way out.” Countered Toni “A lot of clubs like this buy their girls or boys for life, with no way out…here we have a chance to get out. Maybe the owner feels better for giving us a chance or maybe to give him better control over us but at least we have a chance to get out. So please…I know you will hate this, this will make your skin crawl and make you sick but we have to get through this.”
    Monica looked down at the floors with tears in her eyes.
    “How long do we have to do this?” inquired Monica
    “How much Gold did he spend on you?” inquired Toni
    “2,000 Gold.” Answered Monica
    “He got me for 1,700 and at my current rate I will be free in a decade.” Answered Toni “Since he will sell us together, we should get out sooner but I am not leaving you here, as long as I have a choice. You are my little sister; I will not abound you.”
    “Maybe Mom and Dad will help us.” Said Monica as Toni blinked. “They where taken too, that Goblin paid a higher price to split me off from them.”
    “Mom and Dad are here?” inquired Toni as Monica nodded.
    “We where taking part in a search of a woods for you when we where grabbed.” Explained Monica “Can they help us?”
    “Possible…we need to contact them, somehow.” Said Toni as a sad smile crossed her face. She was not alone anymore; her family was here. “They could help us, but that doesn’t change the fact of what will happen tonight.”
