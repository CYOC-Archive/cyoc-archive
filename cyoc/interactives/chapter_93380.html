    Hello, my name is Liam Kage, and ths is the story of how the world ended as we knew it.
    I was never a social person, n fact i would never go outside unless i had something to do outside, which given my sedentary life, was not much, anyway, i was nvited by my friends at college to work on our Completion of Course Work, we arranged to meet on the mall in the food court.
    I got there early, it was close to my house and i wanted to take a look at the new games on the store, after 10m I got a call from Steve, he brought his girlfriend and were waiting already, Steve was your avergae guy, average grades, average muscles, average height(1.75m), he had black hair and a trimmed beard , we were friends since I started enrolling college, his girlfrend, Samantha,was very interested in our studies and since she graduated last year and we helped her CCW she's here to return the favor, she's a bit tall for a girl standing just 5cm under Steve, brunette, 1.65m, never dared to ask her weight, C-Cup, overrall attractive and cheerful personality. I arrived at the food court and sat next to them, we had our usual talk, after 8m, Carol arrived, she's a blonde, green eyes, D-Cup, short(1.55m), got friendzoned by her, unfortunately.
    Our group composed of four people, we were at the mall, and talked about what to do, dfferent projects, but couldn't get the feeling that something was wrong off of me, excused myself for bathroom, and went to the parking lot, to take a breathing and calm myself down, I approached the border to see the city and the sky, looking up, i saw a purple mass hanging on the sky, looking closer i saw many hexagonal repeatng patterns on it, since i loved daydreaming about sci-fi i just admired the purple ship at the sky as it grew in size, as if nanomachines were transforming the matter into itself endlessly, when a shard launched down to the residential area neighboring the mall, I realized that wasn't daydream, i felt the shockwave going through me and engaging a few car alarms, shocked, but stll curious i stood and watched the shard take a square shape, with many silver-colored marks on the surface, at the top right of the square was a line of purple extending and growing, the process took only 15s and another square was in front of it, they, then startedto grow in all directions, never-moving just assimilating, looking down i saw people running, but at this moment many shards have fallen and were doing the same, cars on the parking lot were racing out of the building, at this point i runned back to my friend at the food court, the same thing here, the news reporting “nano-invasion on a global scale” were being broadcasted via speakers to the entire mall, i managed to find everyone and we all went to Steve's car to try to run, but all mall exits were those purple squares, the mall security tried shooting the squares, but the bullets were absorbed just as easily as anything else, people were desperate as we tried to run and find an exit without it,all ground level exits were sealed, and some people entered n contact with the nanomachines and were now in purple coffin structures with a small window, at head-level which showed a solid blue color inside, we kept running, then we decided to go the rooftop and spend our last moments together, I was curious about the coffins, and sprinted back to where I saw some of them previously, I noticed that the squares stopped a few centimeters from the coffin and were firing a green beam at the coffin top, and stopped reproducing, it continued like this for about 10s on everyone it locked, I always carried my backpack with me, and some emergency items, among them was a lab test tube, I wanted a sample of the machine, an inert one, after the laser finished, the coffin dematerialized and saw a woman inside the machine with dripping blue goo, she seemed unconscious, so I quickly dashed to her,the blue goo was slowly absorbing into her, but managed to scoop some on my test tube, looking around I saw the same thing, every single coffin revealed woman, all seemed young and attractive, looking at the woman's back right-hand, I saw written text, it read:
    “Name: William Frederick Jules;
    Age: 18;
    Profession: Baker;
    Template: 8HDYU9;
    Successfully converted.”

    Looking around I saw the purple squares resumed replicating, I sprinted to the stairs, after excruciatingly 8 floors I was at the rooftop, there I saw my friends, and some other people who just watched from the rooftop mouth agape, the purple mass assimilate everything in its path slowly going up, we all knew it would just be a matter of time before it got here, my friends were praying with the group on the rooftop, Despite not being very religious, joined the circle, after sometime, a shard fell on top of us, we wee knocked unconscious from the blast.
    When I came back to my self I was trapped inside a coffin and it started to fill up with the blue goo, limbs going numb as it touched it, I closed my eyes and waited for the my doom.
