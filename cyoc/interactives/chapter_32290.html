    Whatever Happened to the Big Bad Wolf?

    As the former Big Bad Wolf morphs into a half naked, muscled, young man short black hair, you find yourself licking your lips hungrily.

    You grin a toothy grin at the man, and growl, "You look good enough to eat!"

    "Wai-" before he can finish that word you lunge ripping the man's throat out before he can change back into a wolf.

    You sit on a boulder by the side of the road licking your paws and gnawing on a femur. You were famished, you note glancing at the gleaming white pile of bones that you. You sort through the former BBW's possessions: a wallet, a diary, a digital camera...

    The guy's driver's license photo is only three years old, so he can't have been the BBW too long. The digital camera was full of gory snapshots, and a couple videos of the BBW pouncing and devouring people.

    One video showed a young man and woman in ornate regalia. You crank up the volume and hear, "...so you're the Cinderella with the glass slipper and her Prince Charming?"

    "Yes, we're just going on our honeymoon," replied the prince suspiciously with his hand on the hilt of his sword.

    "Congratulations!" gushed the BBW, "That evil stepmother and mean stepsisters really did a number on poor Cindy. I'm glad her fairy godmother came through. You make such a perfect couple. Oh, say, do you think I could get a photograph with you. I mean real royalty, no one will believe I met you if I don't have a photo?"

    "Well, I don't know," begins the prince uncertainly.

    "Oh, come on. He's obviously friendly. Let's pose for the photo!" said Cindy.

    "All right," conceded the prince.

    "Let me just set the timer on the camera and aim it," said the wolf.

    The BBW moved toward the camera and the video stopped. Then a series of still photos followed. First, one with the BBW between Cinderella and her prince hugging them both. Second, the BBW's head is turned with his jaws around the prince's throat, blood stains his tunic, and his sword is half out of its sheath. Third, Cinderella struggles with the wolf with the prince's corpse crumpled at their feet.

    You grin, and pick up the diary. It goes back ten years, and is written in different handwriting. There have been several BBW's. You flip to the beginning of the last werewolf's entries, and read how he became the BBW.

    What do you do now?
