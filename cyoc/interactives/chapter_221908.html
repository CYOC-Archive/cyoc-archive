Clia screams as she tries to find you on the beach.

    You successfully get inside the cave, while walking inside you noticed that there are glowing crystals and see plants inside, the water has a slight blue glow as well, it helps you navigate in the dark cave, you suddenly felt pain on your hand and your reflexes were triggered to get it away, the object which hurt your hand was a spiky rock, there are more located inside the cave but also smooth one's, you decided to take a rest inside the cave while hiding behind a large smooth rock, after resting for a few minutes, you began to explore the cave and search for an exit out of this nightmare beach.....

    BOOM!! Your exploration was interrupted by a large explosion on the entrance of the cave! Clia must have tracked your footprints on the sand! You began to prepare yourself and picked up rocks and crystals to use as self defense and hid behind the rocks! Clia is searching for you and it seems like she is really pissed now, her eyes are red as crimson and she is definitely ready to either transform you to something horrible, enslave you or kill you off!

    "I know your in there, COME OUT NOW AND GIVE UP! YOU WILL NEVER ESCAPE THIS PLACE ANYWAY! I DESTROYED THE EXIT OF THIS CAVE, AND ONLY I CAN REOPEN IT! SO IT'S NO USE TRYING!" She keeps searching for you and looks behind the rocks that you previously hid, damn, this mermaid is insane! "Oh little human~ Come out now so I can get you and stop all this cat and mouse game!" BOOOM!! The rock you previously hid on exploded into pieces! Now you can tell that she is now hell bent on killing you off! "So? You want to play a game of hide and seek? Fine then, lets play! But we already know who the winner is! And that winner would only be me! SO STOP HIDING AND COME OUT NOW!" BOOOM!! Another rock exploded! The cave is shaking and might be destroyed if she keeps bombing everything inside! You do not want to be a mushed up pile of corpse now do you?

    "All I wanted was for you to accept my offer and be my mate! BUT YOU REJECTED ME! DO YOU HAVE ANY IDEA HOW MANY YEARS I HAVE WAITED JUST TO BE WITH YOU!? ONLY FOR YOU TO REJECT MY OFFER AND LEAVE MY HEART BROKEN!? WELL, IF THAT IS WHAT YOU WISH THEN MAYBE YOU SHOULDN'T EXIST! BOOOOOMM!! The cave's walls are beginning to grow cracks!
