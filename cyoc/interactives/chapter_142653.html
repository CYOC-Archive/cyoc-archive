    ”Hey babe,” she said in a cheery tone.

    ”Hi, Grace I'm going to need you to come to my lab. It's an emergency and I'll need you to help me get out.”

    ”Jon? Why does your voice sound different? Are you hurt?

    ”No, I'm not hurt it's just-”

    ”I'm on my way!”

    She hung up, not giving me a second to explain the situation.

    Knowing her, she was probably at home since she doesn't get job requests often. And my house is about 45 minutes away. So the only thing I can do is wait. But there is one problem. My work neighbor.

    He claimed to be back and probably would soon. I looked around and found an empty cabinet that I could sit in. But he could look in the cabinets since there is nowhere else I can go.

    Immediately, I reached on my tippy toes and opened the door, giving him the idea that I left. Then I ran to my cabinet and sat there quietly.
