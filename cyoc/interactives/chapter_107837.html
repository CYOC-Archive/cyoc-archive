    What had started as a perfectly ordinary day turned into one that will forever be engraved in human history. All too suddenly, every female on the planet, no matter their age or even species, were hit by a strange, nearly indescribable sensation. On the other hand, all males felt a surge of energy in their bodies - it felt as if they had, at least mentally, had gained a feeling of power and confidence.

    These signs, however, were only the beginning.

    Over the course of the next few hours, humanity could only watch as unbelievable changes happened to every living being on the planet. What came first, and was, to many men's dismay, was their sudden attraction towards other men... Even if they had once been attracted to women before, they no longer was aroused by the female frame. Rather, their desires were twisted into lusting over male anatomy.
    Women, though... Their bodies were, at this point, feeling extremely strange. And, not long after, they would find out why. As if straight from a fantasy movie, their bodies began to morph before their very eyes - curves evening out, voices deepening, muscles toning... It was clear - the were becoming males. Women who, originally, were interested in fellow women, much in the same manner as men, lost that interest.

    Within a couple of hours, everyone...everything had been twisted into a male rendition of its former self. Even then, that didn't seem to be enough... Men and pre-women alike seemed to burn away excess fat, their skin ridding itself of imperfections... It was as if the world was trying to make every person into the ideal male.

    Of course, images and the likes are solid proof that the female gender existed, so does the newly-formed males, still dressed in their feminine clothing.

    In this bewildered world, who will we follow, and when? Right as the change occurs, or later?
