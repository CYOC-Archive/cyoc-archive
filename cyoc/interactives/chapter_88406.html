    The next day at school was a breeze. Gym class was great. The rest of school was great. Untill, Kyle and Joshua came down the hall, with baseball bats they had stolen from gym. Of course, that was the time you and some of your friends had to take a leak. You, Alex, Jason, Kevin, Eddie, and Thomas went to take a leak, at the very same moment, Kyle and Joshua came into the bathroom. "Hey look it's the assholes who think they are so great in gym, eh? Well check this home run hit for size!" Kyle said. He slammed his baseball bat into nearest kid, which was of course you. As the bat collided with your left knee, the bone break was audible. Alex tried to slug Josh, but his arm was grabbed by Kyle and a baseball bat went down on it. The bone protruding from his arm was visible. Thomas, Eddie and Kevin's jumped to your aid, but one by one fell, and landed on the floor next to you.

    You got extremely mad, and pain seared your leg. Thomas gripped me with his good arm and said, "Hold it in!"

    It didn't matter, the changes had already begun. For your pack, they were all holding it in, but not for long, as Alex's teeth grew into fangs, and Thomas had a five o'clock shadow.

    All Josh and Kyle could do was watch in muted slack jawed horror as they saw your eyes flick into an animals and your broken bone healing itself. Then the changes accelerated. Muscle grew everywhere, and now you were on all fours. All that was left was the snout. The snout popped out with relative ease, and you grinned showing Josh and Kyle your set of fangs.

    Josh and Kyle backed across the bathroom untill they reached the end. Your pack encloses them and waits for your word to strike. "You know the cafeteria served us ham today again, and going on an empty stomach can make you quite grouchy." Alex said grinning devilishly

    "You almost killed me, now I'm almost going to kill you! You swing down and bite Kyles legs, as Jason wrestles Josh with ease. You bite down. Hard. He lets out a yelp of pain and passes out. Jacob is done with Josh too. " The lock and teach is now over" Suddenly you hear jasmine growling in the distance. She was in trouble!
    ----------------------------------------------------------------
    Jasmine started uncontrollably itching. It was science her least favorite period of the day. She had heard from the witch doctor that if the alpha is in trouble you can transform by just doing something.

    She let out a muffled curse as she looked in her shirt. The changes had began. With a raised hand, she said,"Teacher may I go to the bathroom?" She looked anxiously over at the teacher. Mr. Stienke frowned. "No jasmine, not right now." Jasmine pleaded, "But it's an emergency. Please?" She felt the makings of a tail started to grow. "No" She was going to have to reveal her secret. Slowly she closed her eyes and let out a grunt as she fell out of her chair.

    "Damn it!" She screamed in agony as her bones reshaped. Her classmates were watching, terrified and confused. Then she grew in height and fell on all fours, a tail ripping out of her skirt

    A voice called on the intercom "We will be now doing a lock-and-teach. Please remain seated in your classroom and the doors will lock for twenty five minutes. *click*. The automatic doors had shut, just as Jasmine was done with her transformation

    Jasmine let out a massive growl at the teacher, who was now huddling in the corner with Jasmine's classmates.

    It stayed that way for about what seemed like an hour. " The lock and teach is now over" the door didn't get through mid click without Alex and Jason busting through the door.
    ----------------------------------------------------------------
    Jasmine had the kids huddled up in a corner with the teacher in the back. "What a wuss" you say in your head. You point at him and scratch a line on the floor.

    "Hi" you said to the group of kids. "Like my Halloween costume?" Some of the kids laughed at that, and that releases some of the tension in the room. "So are you just going to stand there and gape at me? Don't worry, I won't kill you or anything."

    The group slowly dispersed and went back to their seats. "So you know as well as I do that the principle at this place blows" some of the kids nodded at that. "We have an assembly today right? "Talent show" one of the kids piped up. You lightened up at that.
