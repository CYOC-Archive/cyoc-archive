Jane is a 14 year-old girl, she has long smooth black hair, that reaches just bellow her shoulders and which she always styles in a pony tail. She has brown eyes and a perfect hourglass figure, she also has a nice pair of breasts, which are a big B-cup, which is average for a 14 year-old. She also has an athletic body, thanks to her cheerleading and other activities.

    Jane walks up to Jeff and looks at his Laptop, “you got a new game,” she asks, “why would you show me? You never let me play ANY of your games”.

    “It is not a game,” Jeff says, he then explains everything to Jane, by the end the girl’s eyes are wide in disbelief, “I don’t know if it really works, or if it is just some BS”.

    “Then why not test it out,” Jane asks, “you know, maybe change your hair colour?”

    “Hmm… yeah… that would be a safe way to test it out,” the boy says, he then looks at his sister, “listen, I know I haven’t always been nice to you”.

    Jane snorts, “oh, you think,” she says sarcastically.

    “Please, don’t start,” Jeff says, he then lets out a sigh, “I want to make a deal with you: IF this really works, I’d allow you to change something about me, or even transform me into something or someone”.

    Jane tilts her head, “so… you are saying: if this thing really works, I’m aloud to do anything to you?”

    Jeff nods, not thinking about what consequences it might come with, when giving his sister full control.

    Jane thinks for a while, ‘hmm… that actually sounds cool,’ she thinks to herself, ‘I could transform him into a puppy, a new set of clothing or a pretty dress… or I could transform him into a girl! I have a pen-pal from Japan, she sent me a picture of herself, so I know exactly how she looks like, I could transform Jeff into her for a week or two’.

    “Jane,” Jeff asks.

    Jane blinks, “oh, sorry,” she says, “I was just thinking”.

    “I see,” Jeff says, “so, would you be willing to help me with experimenting with this program?”

    Jane nods, “sure, you can trust me,” she says, “but under one condition: if it works and you let me transform you into something or someone, or change anything about you, I want to keep it a surprise, so I won’t let you in on what I will be doing!”

    Jeff thinks about it for a while, “hmm… sounds actually pretty exciting,” he says, grinning, “okay, deal!”

    Jane’s eyes light up, “sweet,” she says, “so, how do we know if it actually works?”

    “Let me change my hair colour,” Jeff says, “how about… blue?”

    Jane nods, “sure”.

    Jeff opens his file and changes his hair colour from black to blue, after confirming the change he feels a tingling run through his body, causing him to shiver.

    “What’s wrong,” Jane asks, noticing Jeff shiver.

    “Oh, I just felt some weird tingling all over my body,” he says, “is anything happening?”

    Jane looks at her brother, “n… oh, wait! Yes! Your hair is really changing colour!”

    “Seriously,” Jeff asks, he jumps to his feet and hurries to his mirror, his eyes widen and his jaw drops when he stares at his reflection, then a grin forms on his face, “I really have blue hair… haha… looks so weird!”

    “Unbelievable,” Jane breaths, “it actually works!”

    “Man… this is crazy,” Jeff says, “but, let’s see if it changes back! Change the colour back to black”.

    Jane does as told, once again Jeff feels the tingling, he stares at his reflection and smiles when his hair changes back to its original colour.

    “Wow,” they both exclaim, grinning at each other.

    “Okay, so now that we know it works,” Jane says, “may I…”.

    Jeff nods, “a deal is a deal,” he says, “go ahead, I am anxious to see what you have in mind”.

    Jane grins, “oh, I have something fun in mind,” she says.

    Seeing the look on his sister’s face, makes Jeff feel uneasy, but he did say that she is aloud to do anything, so he braces himself, ‘I hope she doesn’t do anything to weird,’ he thinks to himself as he watches his sister type away.
