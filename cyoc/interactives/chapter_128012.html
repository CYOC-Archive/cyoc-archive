    It was a fairly large cavern of dwarlocks ( think Morlocks x Dwarf)
    and it was pretty clear from their pointy teeth and the select cuts of
    arms and legs of normal cloud people that this swarm of
    dwarf monsters were cannibals.

    They hadn't noticed Ilvir yet and sensibly
    he backed away quietly.

    However he tripped over a rotting foot,still with a feminine boot
    enclosing it

    and the noise alerted the dwarlocks.

    99 pairs of glowing
    green dwarlock eyes turned away from Kitty
    and locked on the new source of bones and meat.

    Ilvir ran for it and Kitty quickly joined him as
    the howling swarm of dwarlocks,with spears,clubs and lances made
    of bone and skull,gave chase

    To be blunt about
    both Ilvir and Kitty didn't stand a chance and soon
    both had been pricked by little darts coated in dwarlock blood that would either kill
    its victim or turn he/she/it into
    another cannibal dwarlock.

    So facing death or a fate worse then death
    They briefly outdistanced their pursuers and Ilvir took
    a small side tunnel and ran down it.

    Kitty joined him
    and some distance down the side tunnel Ilvir tripped over the skeleton.

    It was that of a middle aged
    air dragon but which contained some peculiar
    features,like it might
    once have been a cloud female human who had
    transformed into a air dragon.

    It wasn't quite dragon,it wasn't quite
    human.

    It did however have a key and chain around its bony
    neck and the position of the skull suggested the late
    air dragon person had been staring at a particular spot on the opposite wall.

    Back behind them Ilvir could hear the howling dwarlocks getting closer
    so frantically he removed the key and chain and leapt to the wall
    where feeling around he felt a keyhole covered up in dust.

    He inseted the key,turned it and a massive dragon sized door swung
    open.
    Ilvir and Kitty dashed in and the door sealed shut again.

    Twenty second later the dwarlocks came and found
    only the skeleton of the air dragon person in the side tunnel.
