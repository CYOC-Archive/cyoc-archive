    Jeff was sitting in anticipation. The distance between the two homes weren't long. It shouldn't be much time until Jennifer arrived at his house. What was she planning with the Chronivac? All Jeff could do was wait and see what she had in store for him. Still he couldn't focus too much on speculating, since he was still chatting with Shannon, the person who had been Jennifer's mother before Jennifer was aged and became her sister.

    "When Jennifer told me about you, and how you were so much younger, I thought it was quite strange at first. However, I am now beginning to see why she likes you." Shannon expressed. "I can see the appeal of dating a young handsome man as yourself. It must be so much freer than dating a man my age. Older single men often come with a lot of baggage."

    "I am glad you support our relationship." Jeff smiled.

    "I support anything that makes Jennifer happy. She's been so down since the divorce." she divulged. "However, seeing her here today, it seems she's back to her happy self. She haven't been like this since before the divorce."

    "I do my best to make her happy. I enjoy our relationship, and want only the best for her."

    "Maybe I should do it too." she suggested. "Maybe I should try to find a young boyfriend like my sister did. Hey, it's even more socially acceptable, since I am younger than her.

    "With such a large age-difference, one year shouldn't matter much."

    "One year? Well the difference between Jennifer and me is more than just one year. She's my older sister by 12 years and I think that's significant, even if we might seem close in age from your perspective." She seemed a little bit insulted by Jeff's insinuation.

    Jeff was confused. He could swear he had made Jennifer 48 years old, making her one year older than her mother. Did he remember wrong, or had he typed something else? He looked at Shannon and realized what had happened. She was younger. Her red hair was thicker and a more lustrous red. Her face had less and fainter wrinkles. The skin of her arms were much tighter, instead of slightly sagging like they had done the moment before. Jeff understood what was happening. Jennifer had used the Chronivac to make Shannon younger.

    "Oh and Jeff, I wonder. Do you have any plans for the future? What do you think you're doing after high school?"

    "I am not sure actually. I think I might study to be an engineer."

    "Oh engineering! My college have a great engineering program. At least that's what I've heard from my friend who is studying it. What if we were to attend the same college? Like that would be so awesome."

    My college? Shannon a college student? Jeff had to have another proper look at her. She did look much younger now. Her skin was smooth without any wrinkles and wear. She was skinnier too. She must be in her early twenties at most. A moment passed, and as Jeff kept looking at her, he wondered if she might even be younger than twenty.

    "You know. My mom isn't home. We finally have some privacy." she purred, placing one hand on Jeff's chest.

    Shannon lightly pushed Jeff down, laying him down across the coach. She kept her advance, and crawled on top of him. Strands of curly red hair tickling his face. Her warm breath up close to him. She lowered her face, kissing him. Her lips had a taste of strawberry.

    Their kiss was interrupted by the sound of a car driving up the driveway.

    "Shit! She's home." She exclaimed. "Sit up! Pretend like everything is normal. Wait, you have lipstick on your face. Give me a second and I'll fix it." She hurriedly wiped his face with her sundress.

    They heard the sound of the front door being unlocked. Jennifer had returned.

    "Hey mom. Jeff and I were just, uhm. We were just working on some homework." Shannon explained in panic.

    "I see. How did you get lipstick all over your dress during homework?" Jennifer pointed out.

    Shannon turned completely pale. She tried to speak, but no words came out of her mouth.

    "Shannon, don't worry. I know what the two of you you were doing, but it's okay. Remember, I was young too one time. It's not like I am unaware of what hormonal teenagers like to do together. Just make sure you use protection if you ever have sex." she laughed.

    "Mom! Don't say such things in front of Jeff!" she protested, her face red in embarrassment.

    Jennifer smirked in response. Then when Shannon wasn't looking, she gave Jeff a sly wink.
