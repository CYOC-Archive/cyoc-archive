    Rick knew something must have gone wrong. He was back at his house lounging around when he decided to check in on his girlfriend Emily. He was about to call but was surprised when he couldn't find her name in his contacts list. He sighed in relief when he found a contact labeled "Baby" and figured he forgot that he changed it, but he quickly panicked again as a girl he never met before answered. He told her that he butt dialed her before swiftly hanging up.

    He got up and checked a picture in his room, and sure enough his prom picture had some cute asian girl in place of Emily. He didn't know if this was Emily transformed or not, but he definitely knew something happened with the camera. After driving over, he parked across the street and considered just knocking on the door and telling her parents what happened, but he knew if he did he'd probably get shipped off to some mental institution.

    After waiting a few minutes, he saw Emily's parent exit the front door, locking it behind them. Rick took not and noticed they looked more youthful than normal. "Maybe they changed as well?" He thought as he watched the drive away. After making sure they didn't come back for something, he made his way to the front porch. Luckily Emily had shown him where they kept their hidden key so he got in without a hitch.

    He searched around the house but couldn't find a trace of Emily anywhere. Not a single picture showed her. In fact, it looked like the Smith's never had kids. Rick was starting to worry that his girlfriend could be gone forever as he entered her old room to find it a bland guest room. He was about to break down when he noticed a couple of pictures lying next to the bed.

    He ran over and examined each of them. Weirdly enough, they seemed normal to him. There was one shot of a pool toy, one shot of Emily's neighbor Jacki, and another of someone's pregnant stomach. Rick figured that he was aware of the camera's changes, but only to a certain point. If he wasn't there for the photo's it's likely he wouldn't remember what these people used to be.

    "Well," He reasoned to himself, "One of these pictures must be Emily. Which also means that the camera is next door."

    Rick started to guess how he would sneak over there when he heard the sound of Jacki's voice out the window. He turned to see Jacki, a dog, and a pregnant woman who looked a bit like Jacki walk outside to the pool. Jacki was dressed in a nice dress but the pregnant woman was wearing a bikini. The most noticeable thing for Rick, however, was the Jacki was holding the camera!

    "Sweet, Jacki was practically an Aunt to Emily, I'll just walk over there and..." Rick started before realized that since Emily isn't herself, then Jacki wouldn't know who Rick is. "Dammit, I have to get that camera somehow, but how?" He wondered as he quickly ran downstairs and out the backdoor. He stood between the two houses at a gate in the fence and wondered how to go about this.
