    While you are contemplating what to do with the collar, the doorbell rings. You go to answer the door with the collar still in your hand.

    "Hello?" you say opening the door.

    It's that kid from down the street who has the dog-walking business. He graduated from high school last year and is working to raise money so he can go to college, or maybe he is in college and working to supplement it. He has two dogs on leashes standing behind him. You cannot help to notice that he has several other leashes on his belt including a yellow one which matches your new collar.

    "Here for the dog," he says curtly. He looks you up and down and stares for a moment at the collar in your hand.

    "You must have the wrong address, I don't have a dog," you explain.

    He shakes his head and pushes his way into your house with dogs trailing behind them. He closes the door.

    "Hey, wait a minute!" you shout as you fall backwards on your butt from the push.

    He grabs the collar out of your hand and fastens it around your neck. You suddenly feel a strange calmness with the collar around your neck. You seem to be overwhelmed with smells. The two dogs, the young man, even your own scent and so many others rush at you. You zone out for a few moments.

    You hear the young dog walker complaining, "I have to do everything myself," as he pulls your trousers off. You realize that you're already shirtless, and sockless. You don't remember him taking your shoes off either. He reaches for the waistband on your underwear. You react by snarling. You're having trouble finding words.

    "There, there, boy, maybe if I help you up on all fours, you'll realize you don't need those things anymore," he says as he tugs on the leash that he apparently fastened to your collar. You get up on all fours. Something is wrong, you're standing on your feet - all four of them without bending your knees. You look across the room to a mirror, some weird half-man, half dog thing stares back. Well, mostly dog as you look at the hairy beast you have become. The yellow collar is visible around your furry neck. The young man is petting your back, his finger catches in your waistband as his hand continues to rub you, he's stripping you of your last vestige of humanity...
