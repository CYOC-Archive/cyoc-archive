    While you are still upstairs, your sister takes a look at the empty package on the table. “It looks like someone got new socks” she thinks as she turns the package around. Which can change you into whoever shoes you put on afterwards?” she thinks and laughs a bit.
    Just then her little kid brother Tim moved towards the shoe cabinet by the front door. “Wait, Tim is over at my neighbors with his friend, I saw him there. And he has no keys to come in alone, and can´t be here faster than me” Susan thought. Another look at the sheet and she realizes that that wasn´t Tim, but someone else. “Most likely Tom, who is afraid of me finding out. That´s why he didn´t respond when I called.”

That way he couldn´t spit it out.

    While Ben´s eyes got wide in fear, not wanting to be his sisters second son, Susan returned Tim´s clothes to his room and took his shoes from Tom´s room to the cabinet alongside the one´s he had gotten from there before, and packed the socks back into the package. She would keep them, maybe she could use them herself later. They had grown back to normal size after she removed them from Tom´s feet.

    Susan smiled at her brother, now more like her son´s twin brother. She was sure she would miss Tom, but having two sons instead of one is far better than a brother.

Only a second Ken remained behind. And as Susan had said, she would call him Ben instead.

If someone asked, she would just say the second baby, Ben, had been left behind in the hospital, born shortly after her Ken, and she had agreed to raise him as her own son.
