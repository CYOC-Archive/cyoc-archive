    "I thought you liked your brother Cathy" Cathy's best friend Lisa said. "What could he have done to deserve that?"

    "Stay away Lisa!" Cathy shouted. "Save yourself!"

    "Save myself from what?" Lisa asked as she came into the room. She stopped short at the sight of Jim and Bobbi.

    Jim saw Lisa glance at Cathy's leg and the look of horror at what she saw was priceless. Cathy's leg had thinned and her foot had lengthened while the rest of her leg shortened. He looked back at Lisa and said "You'll be joining her Lisa."

    "You still don't have the power to beat me" Jim said with an evil laugh that made people's skins crawl.

    "Well you're right on that part" Lisa admitted. "For now at least. Though I'll save Cathy from your corrupting influence."

    "And how do you plan on doing that?" Jim asked.

    "Like this" Lisa said. She grabbed Bobbi's arm and she yelped in pain dropping Cathy. As she landed she stumbled and Lisa caught her. In a flash of light they were gone.

    Jim looked at the place where they had been. He smiled and said "You can't get away that easily." He stretched out his senses to locate Cathy but he couldn't find her anywhere on the planet. He frowned at this but then shrugged and said "Oh well, she'll return to me when her transformation is complete. For now we'll assume our human forms and go corrupt Mom."

    Somewhere in an underground cave...

    A flash of light and Lisa and Cathy materialized. Lisa lowered Cathy onto a bed and said "Don't worry Cathy. I'll stop the corruption." She twisted the ring she wore on her right middle finger around so the stone was on the underside. She placed her left hand on top of her right and a beam of blue light emitted from the stone.

She let out a bloodcurdling scream as it felt like her leg and groin were on fire. She clamped her mouth shut when she noticed that her leg was reverting back to her normal leg. She watched as it lengthened and her foot shortened. Her leg took on more muscle until it was the shape it had been before and color came flooding back into it. The claws on her toes shrank down and lost the ebony color until they were regular toenails again. As that last transformation took place the pain completely went away.

    The beam disappeared and Lisa slumped like she was exhausted which she probably was. Cathy looked at her friend and asked "Are you alright?"

    "I... will... be" Lisa said trying to catch her breath. "That... was... harder... than I... thought... it would... be."

    "What did you do?"

    "The Corrupter's power... is based on... pleasure. I turned that pleasure... into pain. The corruption withered... in the presence... of so much pain."

    "How do you know this?"

    "I've studied various forms of mysticism. One dealt with The Corrupter and what he did."

    "Where are we?"

    "We're in a pocket universe. We're safe here for now but I'll have to move us back to our own universe soon or it will collapse in on itself and kill us in the process."

    "Why did you bring us here then?"

    "I needed a place that The Corrupter couldn't get to. If I had taken us to another place on Earth he would have found us in seconds."

    "Won't he be able find us when we go back to Earth?"

    "We'll be going to a protected place. Without the corruption in you he won't be able to find us. I need to take a little nap. I'll wake back up in an hour and then we'll move back to our universe."

    Lisa laid down on the bed and was soon sound asleep. Cathy was feeling a little tired herself so she laid down to join her friend in her nap.
