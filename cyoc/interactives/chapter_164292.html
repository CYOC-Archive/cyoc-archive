    Oliver looks around desperately for something to wear. She spots an abandoned diaper bag nearby. She rushes over and digs through it. The only thing available is a thick diaper and a T-shirt with “Mommy’s Little Stinker” written on the front. Well, she has no other choice. Oliver clumsily tapes on the diaper and slips the T-shirt over her head.

    Now that she’s clothed, Oliver takes stock of the situation. She should pass the ring eventually, and once she does, she can use it to return to her proper self. It’s probably for the best if she wears diapers until then. Not just because she’s younger and might have lost some potty training, but also because it will be easier to find the ring once it comes out.

    She needs to grab some more diapers, then. She doesn’t have any diapers at her apartment, and she took the only one in this diaper bag. Oliver looks around, trying to decide where to get diapers.
