But once word got around; the new operating hours for the “club” became too big of a hit not to ignore. Still being too early to tell if it’s a boy or a girl; Lila still in the first trimester of her pregnancy, Lance knew he had to give serious thought to moving into a bigger house.

Mowing lawns and trimming hedges as well designing garden layouts; Nate was gob smacked at how much money there was to be had in yard work. While his job did keep him away from his girlfriend Jean Talbot, she was equally busy with her work. Juggling time with her course work at the college and helping her father out at the elder council building; both werewolf and were-lioness were lucky to meet up once or twice a week. With the rest of the council and city hall seeing the slippery politician being dragged away in cuffs; they knew that the new mayor is not someone you could try and manipulate or scheme against. With her briefing with Martin before he and Melissa swam off to Hawaii; she got a complete rundown on all of the other council members and their dirty tricks. She knew most of them and thanked Martin for the info; him handing over a file with the town’s financials as well as various account numbers gave her a better sense as to how Martin was able to put the town in the black. While they went over the issues that would soon need to be discussed; it was Frances and Melissa having another chat amongst themselves. Not about Julie, but regarding the son Alyssa Cooper had.

Martin vehemently denying it and Sheriff Cooper also stating to the fact; even showing the Stovall’s the birth certificate of Darrell. Seeing the name Rick Fernando as the father did not allay Melissa’s suspicions; even during their getaway to Hawaii. Demanding a paternity test and then having to deal with a pissed of were-grizzly after finding out both Martin and the sheriff were telling the truth. The ultrasound later determined that the fetuses were fine and Melissa and Martin would soon have twins. The two wer-mers were blown away from what they heard. Twins was a rarity for weres. The last twins being born were from the Carpenters seven years prior. Martin thrilled that he was going to be a father again, but with twins; hugs his wife who too was also happy with the news.

Waking up one day to see Joan in her were-tigress fursona snuggled up to him in bed, with his large grizzly paws wrapped around her; Douglas was at first worried he might inadvertently harm her. But hearing her purr as she buried herself deeper into his embrace, it wasn’t long afterwards that the two were getting to know each other on a more intimate level. After listening to the threatening message he left on the machine; it wasn’t long after that the lecherous were-otter was picked up by police, prowling around town. Sitting in a jail cell, George was furious that Andrew Curtis visited him and hand the man a scroll of the elder council. In it, it stated that he had to make restitution for his son and ex-wife; only then would they assist in having him being declared dead reversed. Fuming that he could not leave town and had payback what he did; George thumbs his nose at Andrew by ripping up the scroll and telling the retiring wer-mer where to shove it. He was soon transferred to the cell in the elder council to a wait having to explain his actions to the council its self. But Emily’s fortune’s have been looking better; after taking that Saturday shift in which she bagged the biggest tip of her waitressing career, she was able to pay off all the bills that were overdue and still have some money leftover to save. Not being underwater with a ridiculous mortgage thanks to George and the council helping out; Emily slept well for the first time in decade.

    Speaking of the Elder council; the matter of the Seattle chapter of the Luna brotherhood going rogue with its duties. It wasn’t long before the head of the organization from Romania stepped in to help resolve the situation. Stripping each werewolf of their position within the Luna brotherhood; some of the disgraced weres to ashamed of themselves attempted to take their own lives. Requesting Tachibana’s assistance in rebuilding the Seattle chapter; the head Alpha asked if Randolph the werewolf rep would like to lead the reformed chapter. Randolph laughing as he thought this was a jape; soon said no as he had his own family to look after. His mate giving birth to their first pup on Saturday during the snowstorm, his son; it was the happiest day of his life. Howling in celebration; not caring if it is still daytime as he ran through the woods in joy.

    Lastly; with Jack Crawford trying to oust Benton during the annual shareholder’s meeting, the young business man was shocked to see Anton Knight present and speaking on behalf of Benton. Thinking the old man retired; Jack was unaware that Anton still had a controlling interest in the company he founded. Acting as Benton’s proxy; Jack’s attempt to remove Benton failed. Not having enough of the votes and those content to let Benton run the ship, despite his recent medical issues coming to light; Jack was forced to concede this round to the Knights. Refusing to go quietly into the night; Jack Crawford has still vowed to regain his birthright. Finding out that Crawford Computers is now held in trust for Benton’s daughter Frieda; the scheming were-panther starts planning his next attack.

Each in their fursonas and purring upon seeing the other snuggled next to them; their green eyes flashing as they each sense the love from their special union. All five smile as they knew what each other did last night; with the waning days of summer break coming to an end, nearly the entire town is alive with the sounds of weres mating in the woods and indoors during the night.
