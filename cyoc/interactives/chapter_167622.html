    Jake woke up to an annoying alarm clock on his nightstand. He hit the snooze button, and lifted himself groggily rubbing his eyes. "What a night..." Jake said as he started scratching his neck from under his name. He opened his eyes to see Rajal sleeping on Nate's bed which he figured was now his. Jake wondered where Nate was since he wasn't in bed. He figured he'd sneak out of the room to look.

    Jake creaked open the door to see Nate from behind setting up the table with an assortment of food. Nate heard the door creak close as his cow ears wiggled and his tail seemed to wag. He turned around to see Jake and a smile sprawled over his snout. "Good morning master. I took the liberty of making breakfast." Jake noticed a full course of waffles and a bagel on the side toped off with a glass of milk, which he figured came from his udder.

    "Wow. Uh... Thanks... How did you sleep last night then?" Jake asked. Nate tilted his head with confusion. "Sleep? Me? Why would you assume that? I don't need sleep naturally. I thought you knew that." Nate explained finishing up a second batch of breakfast for Rajal. Jake was shocked to hear Nate didn't sleep last night. Had the Chronivac made him so that he didn't need it? "Oh... Well... It's just... I worry for you. You may be my maid, but I still want to treat you right." Jake said his tail swishing behind him. "Oh don't be so modest. I live to treat you master." Nate said happily as he walked past Jake to wake Rajal.

    "My, your cooking is top notch Nathan!" Rajal said patting his mouth with a napkin. "I'm glad you enjoyed it master Rajal." Nate said happily collecting the dishes. Jake was pleased with how well Nate was at cooking. Adjusting to this new personality of his would be a stretch, but he figured it work out. As for what's next, Jake had a new plan to change Rajal. "Nate, could you fetch my laptop, please?" Jake asked Nate. "Of course." Nate said walking to the coffee table and bringing Jake's laptop. "Thanks." Jake said as Nate set it down on the dining table.

    Jake decided he'd turn Rajal into an elephant man. Not merely because he was Indian, but to follow in the animal theme he had going on in the room. He started off by finding his profile and gave him elephant ears, a trunk with tusks, a tail, and he'd put some weight on. Jake clicked change and watched Rajal closely.

    The first thing Jake noticed is that Rajal's ears were turning from brown, to grey leathery skin. His lobs started flattening out and his ears overall started to stretch and grow. While his ears changed, his nose started pushing upward as his nostrils started flaring outward. The skin started turning the same leathery grey as his nose started pushing forward. Out of both sides of his upper lip, two white points appeared and grew into tusks to accommodate his new nose.

    "Hmm... I must've put on a few pounds..." Rajal says as his nose fully changed to a trunk as he uses it to feel his belly, which was growing in size. Rajal stood up and turned his back to Jake while pushing himself against the chair to support his new weight. Jake noticed a bulge from Rajal's lower back as it sprouted a grey leather bump frog his rear while growing strands of hair on the tip. Rajal's tail twitched trying to adjust to the new appendage. Over the course of his tail growth, Rajal turned around for Jake to see his gut fully in view as he struggled to keep his shirt on. "Oh... Stand back... It's going to burst..." Rajal warned Jake as his shirt finally gave out and tathers flew all over.

    Jake looked at Rajal's complete transformation into a half elephant, thinking it wasn't as bad looking as he'd imagined. Jake looked down at his laptop and enabled reality shift. "Well, at least I don't have to actually wear a shirt. But it was one of my favorite..." Rajal said picking up a piece of his shirt with his trunk. "I could probably sew it back together, and even make it bigger to accommodate your size." Nate suggested, looking to Jake. "If it's okay with you anyway..." Jake smiled at Nate's nervous face. "Of course, given you have access to a sewing machine." Jake said. "Of course. The arts department should have one I could use." Nate suggested. "That'd be wonderful. Thank you Nathan!" Rajal praised Nate.

    Jake could only laugh at what he was capable of now. He had turned his former roommate to his personal cow maid, and his new one to an elephant man. Jake was ready to play around with the Chronivac even more. But what to do next was the question...
