    "Do you always do that?" asked Nicola.

    "That would hardly befit a Dame" Alessa answered calmly "Or a noble lady."

    Nicola stamped her foot in frustration. "I'm not a noble lady! Until this morning I wasn't even female!"

    "What?!" Alessa was startled.

    "Goddess knows where you got this idea I was some kind of princess! I was a street thief! I robbed a witch and she turned me into a woman! That's when you found me." Nicola burst into tears and sat down on the bed. She covered her eyes. She knew that when she uncovered them, Alessa would be gone.

    Instead, she felt Alessa's arm around her shoulder. "Did you not return? Did not your kisses free me from this terrible curse? You are always a noble lady in my eyes."

    Nicola burrowed deeper into the comforting warmth of Alessa's embrace. When she raised her head again her eyes were red and swollen, but she was smiling radiantly. "OK" said the street thief "But from now on, I do the negotiating for both of us."
