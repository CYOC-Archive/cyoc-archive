    In another part of the country Rick was just getting home from work. When he opened the door to his tiny apartment he found someone already in there. He had tan skin and long white hair and beard. He was wearing what appeared to be a white robe and there was a certain air about him that screamed otherworldliness. "Zeus!" Rick exclaimed.

    "I'm glad that you remember me" Zeus said. "It's been a long time since we last saw each other. You look well."

    Rick snorted "As well as can be expected trapped in this body."

    "Well I'm here to change that" Zeus said.

    "Why now?" Rick asked folding his arms.

    "A seal that was placed on Amy's daughter has been prematurely broken" Zeus said. "She came to ask me to send someone to watch over her. She requested that it be you specifically."

    "Ok so who's Amy?" Rick asked.

    "She a demoness" Zeus said. "She's the mother of all incubi and succubi."

    "So why did she request me?" Rick asked.

    "She knows about your demon side" Zeus said.

    "How does she know that?" Rick asked.

    "Well since she's the mother of all incubi and succubi and since your demon half is a succubus..." Zeus said trailing off.

    "What a minute!" Rick said. "I'm a succubus?"

    "You are" Zeus said.

    "Where you going to keep this from me forever?" Rick asked throwing up his arms.

    "We thought it best for you not to know" Zeus explained. "Anyway I am here to turn you into a fifteen year old girl and to transport you to a church where Father Tobias will place you with the family. You don't have a choice. A prematurely broken seal can be disastrous."

    "Do it then" Rick said.

    Zeus nodded and placed a hand on Rick. In a flash of light Rick was transformed and they disappeared.
