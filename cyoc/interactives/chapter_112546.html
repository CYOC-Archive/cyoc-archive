    The Globe Dome

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by The Brilliant Dr. Garfield 6 years ago O

    Looking off to your right, you are shocked to see a large building with a humongous glass dome covering it. You think this strange, because you surely would have been able to see it from over the park wall. Thinking nothing more of it, you head towards it.

    The building sits on a hill, with the bright midmorning sun flashing over the dome. There's stairs cut into the hill with handrails, so you use those to climb up. When you reach the top, you stand in front of the building's main entrance. Hanging over the huge doors are flags of every conceivable country, in a variety of colors and designs, stretching all the way around the circumference of the building until they can't be seen anymore.

    Wondering how on earth they managed to get a North Korea flag, you open the huge glass doors and step inside.

    You enter into a giant three-story lobby, with pillars all around and balconies high above. In the center of the floor is an intricate globe made of bronze and copper. High above you, the glass ceiling of the dome glints in the sunlight.

    Looking straight ahead, you see a young woman with brown hair and a red suit sitting behind a large reception desk, reading a TIME magazine. You walk towards it and stand behind the counter, saying... "Uh... what is this place?"

    She looks up from the magazine and smiles. "Hello, Sir. Welcome to World Travel. I'm Bailey and I'm proud to inform you that you have stumbled upon the greatest company since Apple.

    "Well, that's great and all..." you say, "But what do you do?"

    "Oh, it's quite simple sir. If you simply go up those stairs on your right.." she points with a red pen towards a set of stairs that slopes up to the third floor, "...you'll find doors with various designs and placards on them. Step through and you'll find yourself immediately transported to that country."

    "Bullshit. I don't believe it." You say.

    Bailey smiles. "Our new customers always say that sir. Why don't you go upstairs and test it out fro yourself? The first trip is always free for new customers." She indicates the stairway again with her pen before going back to her magazine.

    Realizing you really don't have anything better to do. You decide to humor the girl. You start climbing the stairs, but every few steps you look back, expecting Bailey to burst out into laughter and proclaim the who thing as a joke. But she never does so. In fact, she barely looks up from her magazine.

    Sighing, you finally make your way to the top and are greeted by a Long hallway with gray tiles and dark green paint. The hallway is very long, and due to this, you can see many, many, doors of various sizes, shapes, and designs covering all the walls.

    You walk around the hallway, examining the doors, before deciding to just humor the lady and open one.

    What do you do now?
