    Kinda thought having an SCP-like story section could be an interesting place for people to make one off stories for beings/devices that could be expanded on. As this is cyoc I'd say the entries need not be horror based, although they can if you want.

    The G.E.T. was a shadow government organization formed to protect/abuse the world order from all the artifacts, devices, beings, and whatnot that can transform things willy nilly. The Illuminati and other shadow government organizations were able to hide or disappear those things and the events around them for a time, but after enough of these instances start pumping up the shadow government decided to make an organization that specialized in dealing with things that by their nature transformed things.

    The hierarchy is simple. 1 is top dog. 1 rules everything in the organization and only bends to those above 1 in the shadow government. No one in the G.E.T. actually knows anything about 1 but they know that 1 could make them and everyone they cared about 5 breasted firefly flamingos and no one would think anything was different. So if someone thought something would make 1 happy they did it.
    The 2's are top management below 1. If there were a face to the organization they would be the faces. Doing most of the management work while being subtly manipulated by 1. They also are the ones deciding how to use the Transformative Entities G.E.T. has "get".
    The 3's are the scientists. Hand selected to examine and deal with any transformative entities that G.E.T. gets their hands on. Usually very high up in the organization sometimes the scientists in charge of an entity are an unknowing citizen. In which case while the data they gather can be useful that scientist in particular is extremely expendable. Scientists tend to be the ones making reports.
    the 4's are the direct management. While the 2's do most of the broad and executive decisions the 4's are in charge of the day to day stuff. Making sure the cells are maintained, that agents are sent to the right locations, and most people don't particularly like them.
    the 5's are simple agents. While there are informal rankings between agents and the most senior of agents tend to be seen in a light similar to the 3's or even 2's they tend to be rather expendable. They do the real work, get their hands dirty, and resign themselves to coming into work as one thing and going out as something entirely different.
    Then there are the 6's. Essentially they are the civilians. A minority of 5's are scouted from 6's, but in general if you are a 6 and are working for G.E.T. you have no idea that you are. The two types of employment 6's get is either in one of the G.E.T. shell companies or as someone who gets a special computer program/virus... or who gets a special package... or who wakes up one day with the power to turn hair into pudding! Being a 6 also means that most 4's can just erase you from existence if they fill out a sheet of paperwork.

    Transformative Entities who are harmless enough and are at most urban legends usually are left alone in the wild, useful entities that are easily manipulated tend to be made tools for mostly agents are scientists depending on the utility, dangerous Entities tend to get contained somewhere in G.E.T, and Entities that can't be contained or endanger nearly everything get put in 1's special stash. No one really knows what or where the stash is much like 1 themselves, but everyone trusts that it'll keep them all safe. Otherwise there'd be no sense of safety or stability.

    When making a report a Scientist will generally use the format:

    Entity #:
    Codename:
    Known Abilities:
    Containment:
    Other:

    One important phenomena found in the G.E.T. is that certain transformative effects have priority over others. For instance let's say you have a doll house that transforms all living things within 5 feet of it into dolls, and a machine that transforms living things within 5 feet of it into robots. If one being is affected by both what'll happen is that instead of being transformed into a robot doll the living thing will be transformed into either a doll or a robot depending on transformation priority. How do you figure out something's priority? Well you kinda just have to figure it out through experimentation, but many agents find if they have a gadget with a high enough tf priority that put them in a form they are fine with they'll keep it around to get around any worse transformations.

    Now that you've learned about the workings of G.E.T. let's GET to work!
