    As the show got under way Jazzy, LoLo and Oly gathered up with their parents and the other clown families. When the ring master announced the clown act every clown bumbling into the center ring. They went about their way doing tricks and playing pranks. They even had a couple of tricks that required volunteers. L When LoLo and his brothers asked for volunteers. Jazzy pulled up a lanky 16 year old guy with long blond hair held back by backward hat. He was wearing a light blue long sleeved shirt and and pair of pink shorts. LoLo pulled up a good looking 15 year old girl with nice brown hair wearing a tie dye T shirt and a pair of shirt shorts. Oly picked a 13 year old boy with brown hair wearing a blue t shirt and black shorts. Next the three clowns summoned a long curtain that could that hid them and their volunteers.

    Jazzy looked the guy he picked he asked him his name and the boy said Jack. Jazzy said ooo that’s what my name used to be Confusing Jack. Jack was about to ask what was about to happen when he get a face full of whipped cream pie right to the face. Jazzy then grabbed the tin and moved it around his face and head Jazzy then conjured a bottle of seltzer and sprayed his clothes with it. After getting sprayed by the seltzer his shorts expanded to be as wide as a hula hoop with suspenders coming out of them and turning red with polka dots. Shirt shirt turned yellow with a big blue bow tie appearing around his neck all of his skin turned white and his shoes extended out and became red to match his hula hoop shorts. Grabbing the seltzer again he sprayed off the whipped cream from Jack’s head. From underneath the cream Jack now had a white face and a big red nose along with a big blue Afro. Jazzy was able to do all of this because he knows how to use the circus’s magic to change people. Any changes he made to people were not permanent as he would be able to go home and take the whole costume off and wash the makeup off.

    Oly was next since he didn’t use the magic of the circus he had is own ways for performing transformation tricks. Oly asked him his name and he said jimmy and when Oly heard that he said then jimbo let’s do this. Oly pulled from his giant pants a comically large controller pushing the big button a salon chair appeared dramatically bouncing the boy into the air. He landed in the chair as a bunch mechanical arms came out of it that started to paint Jimmy’s whole body white. One of the arms grabbed a green Afro and stuck it on his head along with a big red clown nose. Next thing he knew the arms grabbed jimmy and lifted him and took off his clothes it then went into a chest and grabbed a jumpsuit with vertical rainbow stripes on the legs with a light blue top and white sleeves and yellow suspenders. This costumes was also wide like a hula hoop around the hips. The chair also grabbed a pair of blue and yellow clown shoes and put them on jimbo’s feet.

    Lastly was LoLo and the girl he had pulled up. Lolo asked for her name and she said Liz. Lolo pushed a button when a salon chair similar to the one Oly used. Liz was sitting in the chair when it started to spin really fast. LoLo then pulled out a couple of spray can and started to spray Liz with the spray paint. As the chair slowed down Liz look down at her clothes and saw the her shirt and shorts had changed to a big frilly dress with all kinds of colors and polka dots all over it. LoLo held up a mirror and she started to laugh at her appearance. Her long brown hair had turned into a big red Afro with a bow in it. Her nose was a big red ball and she had red lips and and snow white skin. Standing up out of the chair Liz was acting really silly she even looked over at LoLo and gave him kiss on the cheek. LoLo’s cheeks turned bright red. And he smiled.

    Once all three clowns finished they moved the curtain away and showed the audience what everyone looked like now they were all laughing the butts off Jazzy instructed their volunteers to go back to their seats. Jack went back to sitting next to his girlfriend who was crying laughing at her boyfriend having to slide over some because of his hula hoop shorts. Jimbo went back and sat next to his friends who were also laughing at him. Then there was Liz who went and sat down with a guy she was on a date with how ever she blew LoLo a kiss. Jazzy brought everyone’s attention back to the center of the ring. Where the ring master brought out the whole circus and a table with a birthday cake on it with some candles. The ring master went on to talk about how the current generation is the 7th generation to perform at this circus. He said that at the age of 15 you become an adult in the circus’ eye, happy birthday LoLo he said. LoLo blew out the candles and then Honked his nose. After he did that he felt the rest of his skin turn Snow White and his feet grow into the rest of his shoes. The whole circus gave a bow and said good night.
