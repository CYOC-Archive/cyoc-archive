    You go back to the machine to look and see if there's a manual for it laying around. The search feels really awkward due to how unaccustomed you are with your new female body and after having searched everywhere including behind the machine and under the control panel you found nothing that appeared to be a manual. You decide to take a look at the control panel for yourself, maybe your new girlfriend missed something. Looking at the control panel you see the buttons for the different programs arranged in a neat line along the middle of the panel with the text "please initiate before choosing a program" written above them. Ignoring the programs for now you start looking for other buttons to press and spot one with an "i" on it.
    "Hey, I think I found something" you say as you press the i button.
    The girl looks up from the shelves she had started to search in hopes of finding a manual and looks at you. However instead of displaying an information page like you had hoped for pressing the button caused the doors to the tube to close and after a few seconds of nothing happening an error message appeared on the panel reading "Error. No subject detected. Press OK to cancel initiation and reset the test chamber.". Realizing you made the same mistake the girl must have made just a few moments ago you blush a bit as you press the OK button on the error message and the doors to the tube opens again. As you turn to tell the girl that you found nothing you spot yet another button in the lower corner of the panel marked with an o. You press it and the list of programs dissapear, replaced by a long list of settings or options. Most of it is just technological gibberish to you but near the top of the list you find options like "view current programs", "edit current programs" and "create new program".
    As the girl comes over to you you press the "view current programs" option and a list of the 15 different programs appears but this time they are labelled with short descriptions.
    "Seems like you found the manual" the girl comments as you scroll through the list of programs looking for a reset program. Like you had already guessed program 1 genderswaps the breasts and program 15 genderswaps the entire body. You also spotted programs like "genderswap genitalia", "genderswap reproducive organs", "genderswap muscle mass" and "genderswap vocal chords" amongst other things but no reset program.
