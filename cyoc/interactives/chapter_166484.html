    Ann and Jackie now watched as Kellie began her spell. The two of them watched both the grandmother and the granddaughter for any signs of regression or progression in either of them. Soon they began to notice a change in Penelope.

    As the elderly woman began to stir awake, she began to grow younger. Penelope took off her oversized sunhat as they now saw her silvery white stringy hair regain its color to that of an auburn tint.

    "Neat, I guess red hair runs in her family." Jackie noticed. "Well she is the grandmother, although she's look less like a grandmother with each moment. Just exactly how young are you making her?" Ann asked Kellie.

    "Just watch." Kellie said as Penelope had left her senior years behind her and was now going back into her late 40s as her muscles came back to her losing all sag and fatness that she previously had. The three continued to watch as Penelope's skin became smoother and her hair had regain its bright red color and had actually grown out more.

    As the three of them watched, Penelope's regression looked like it had stopped and she was now 25 and feeling more lively than she had been several minutes ago. The three of them watched as the now youthful Penelope began to play with Heather.

    "Aw, that's so sweet of you to make her younger." Ann complimented.

    "Not done yet." Kellie said as Jackie and Ann's attention went back to Penelope and Heather.

    Ann and Jackie now watched as young Heather began to change before their very eyes. Young Heather now began to develop into a preteen as it looked like her little swimsuit was now becoming too small for her. As Heather went into her teenage years, her red hair became curly and her body looked looked like someone who had played sports in her time. As she left her teenage years behind her, she almost now looked like a twin sister of Penelope. Kellie also made it so both of their swimsuits would now fit the two of them.

    "Wow, bravo Kellie. Those two look pretty happy now." Ann said.

    "Yeah, but they can be a little happier." Kellie said as she used her magic one last time on them.

    Ann and Jackie didn't notice anything unusual about Penelope and Heather now until Penelope began to play with Heather's curly hair making Heather giggle. Ann and Jackie were a little surprised to see that Heather and Penelope's lives had been rewritten for them to become a couple, but it sort of it works for them now as they didn't have too many other people in their lives before.

    Ann and Jackie now walked over to Penelope and Heather and began to talk.

    "Hey there, you two on a little beach outing?" Jackie asked.

    "Yeah, I wanted to go bowling, but she suggested the beach." Penelope said.

    "That's because you're inside so much Penny, I wanted to bring you outside so everyone can see my hot girlfriend." Heather complimented making Penelope blush.

    "Oh, you two are dating?" Ann asked trying to make polite conversation.

    "Yep. Six months and still going strong." Penelope said giving Heather a kiss on the cheek.

    "We've been dating for quite a while too. Say, how about we have a little double date?" Jackie asked excitedly and Heather cheered in excitement with her.
