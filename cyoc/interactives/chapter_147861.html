    You direct your attention to the mirror before you and are once again greeted by a beautiful young girl standing in front of you, dressed in the most wonderful clothing you’ve ever had the pleasure of looking. Your inner reflection is interrupted by your noticing of the shopkeeper. She still appears to have her attention riveted on you and you begin to find her presence unnerving.

    As if in response to your thoughts, the shopkeeper begins speaking again. “Since you look like a fairy princess right now, I’ll let you in on a little secret. I’m actually not a human, but an actual fairy. In fact, I happen to be a fairy queen.”

    You look at her confusedly; there was no way she was being serious, right? Maybe this is some sort of roleplay thing. “Do you have any proof to give me that you’re telling the truth?” you ask.

    She looks down at you with an almost disappointed look on her face. “Darling, I would’ve thought you would’ve pieced it together. How else have you taken this new form?”

    Your eyes widen in understanding. She was the one behind your transformation! Oddly, you weren’t quite sure how you feel about that. The burning question on your lip flies into the air: “why?”

    Her face returns to its typical joyful countenance as she responds. “ Well, as the queen, it is my duty to provide an heir suitable to lead my people.     Standing back up, she opens her arms, as if preparing for a hug. “Will you do it? Will you come with me and become my daughter. My lovely, little, fairy princess?”
