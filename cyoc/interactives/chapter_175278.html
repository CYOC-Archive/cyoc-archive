    The Magic Shop

    Laura Meets Someone With The Same Situation As Hers

    Laura waits until a family begins to enter the magic shop, then she joins the end, mixing with the rest of the family. She tries her best to minimize how dirty she gets from her filled diaper, but she can still feel it smearing all over her butt. It’s very unpleasant.

    There’s a line when she gets inside. With a soft sigh, Laura decides to wait in line to talk to the shop owner. But suddenly, she feels herself getting lifted into the air by someone.

    It was really a big surprise to Laura that one of the dice ended up accidentally falling out of her pocket and the spirit was instantly heard.

    "Three years you have walked this Earth, now this number is but two, each roll of the dice will restore more than you have lost."

    Laura begins to shrink a little and now her diaper and clothes have started to grow too big for her current age.

    "What's a little girl like you doing hanging around the store in those oversized clothes?" The woman asks Laura.

    Before Laura can respond, the woman begins to detect the terrible smell that is emerging from Laura's diaper.

    "And it also seems like you had an accident. Let’s go get you changed!”

    At that moment, the woman carries Laura to a bathroom. “You’re lucky, little girl. I forgot to put my daughter’s spare diapers away, so I still have them in my purse!” The woman lays Laura on a changing table. Laura is too confused to fight against her sudden diaper change. “There we go! All clean.” The woman takes Laura, who now only wears her diaper, back to the front of the store.

    “Excuse me, are you this little girl's parents?” the woman asks a couple waiting in line. The couple shakes their heads. “Huh.” The woman looks at Laura. “Who are your parents?”

    “She might be related to someone who works here,” says one of the people the woman spoke to. “There’s a playpen in the corner.”

    “Oh! That makes perfect sense!” The woman carries Laura over to the playpen and puts her in. “And even if you aren’t related to an employee, your mommy and daddy can find you fast here!” The woman walks away.

    Laura’s not pleased to be in a playpen, especially considering that she has just lost one of the dice. If that die is rolled again, she will get even younger to the point where she will not be able to talk or walk.

    But at that moment, the same woman who found Laura came back but this time she puts another toddler in that same playpen. It was a 2-year-old toddler girl wearing only a pink shirt and diaper. She also had a pink pacifier in her mouth and has pigtails in her hair.

    "Look my little one, here's someone you can play with while you wait for your parents." The woman told Laura before retiring again.

    Laura was not paying attention to the new toddler, as she had to focus on trying to find the other die before it was too late. The other toddler girl looks curiously at Laura and begins to approach her little by little. When she is close to Laura, she takes the pacifier out of her mouth and asked her a question that end up drawing Laura's attention.

    "Are you Laura Peters?"

    Laura's jaw dropped and her pacifier fell out of her mouth.
