    Slowly the computer ticked off until the transformation was complete. Looking at his hands, Mark was scared and immediatly began hitting the escape key. Immediatly a box popped up that explained the changes would be undone and asked him if this was okay. Hitting yes, Mark sat back as the his hands returned to normal.

    Bothered by this, he got up and began to pace about the room. "How much can this really change," Mark asked himself.

    Sitting down at the computer, he began to scroll though the different menus. After a bit and idea began to form. Browsing, he began to click on one thing and then another, subtly changing his genetic make-up, his body and finally his sex and gender.

    His main motivation for this was curiousity. That and the fact that he had been rejected by a girl just recently and wanted to know why females acted the way they did. A window popped up and reading awareness, Mark sat back, attempting to figure out what this meant. After a minute of thought, he left it at zero and closed it.

    Checking that everything was right, he began to carefully go through everything he had changed, scared because this was a lot bigger then just changing his hands and somewhat frightened because he didn't want to stuck like this forever.

    He choosen that he would still be the same age. His height would be five foot three and his weight eighty nine pounds. He had added that he was into ballet and gymnastics, thinking that they were the most girly things. He put in that he liked pink, purple, teal and white, thinking those to be soft feminine colors. His hair would be down just past his shoulders. His breasts would be a modest B cup and he would have a size four waist and wear size one shoes.

    A box popped up and without giving it much thought, clicked the X to close it, annoyed that he had lost his train of thought. Seeing no problems, he added as an after thought that he wore mostly skirts, button fly pants, thongs, boxers, blouses, tight t-shirts and canvas high-top shoes. His choice of swimwear was a low-cut, revealing bikini.

    Another box appeared and asked if he would wear pads of tampons for his monthly. Confused he clicked tampons.

    Another box asked him if he wanted to save the previous profile and irate, wanting this to be over, feeling like he should just shut the program down, he clicked on the no, thinking he would just hit the escape key again.

    Finally, a box appeared that asked if he wanted initiate now and he clicked yes. Immediatly several boxes appeared. Reading the first one, he saw that it was the progress for his transformation. The next one confused him, because it read like it was deleating his awarness of his previous life. The third one cause him panic as he read that a permancy in life and those around him was in effect.

    Scared, he hastily began to read what it said. He had just finished reading when suddenly he was over come by cramps, vertigo and a strange crushing feeling. Sliding to the floor, his last thoughts were, "Oh crap. This things turning me permantly into a girl. That is not what I wanted," and then he blacked out.
