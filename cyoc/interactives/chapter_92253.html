    My Brother, My Other Dog.

    Jeff's thoughts were interrupted as he heard his brother come in. He stepped out of his room to listen.
    "Hey Josh, how was school?" Called John. Josh sighed in frustration.
    "Fine, but I'm still a bench warmer. Doggone it! why can't I be part of the football team?"
    Jeff panicked. Josh had started John transformation! He dashed back to the Chronivac to stop the changes. The screen flashed a message across the screen; stating that the transformation couldn't be stopped until John finished his changes.
    "Hey dad" Jeff heard Josh say, "You might want to shave. you've got a pretty good amount of hair growing." Jeff quickly tried to find a way to stop anyone from noticing, but he couldn't really stop anything about his dad. If his jerk brother found out about the Chronivac, He'd torment Jeff forever! Suddenly, Jeff had an idea.
    "If I can't change Dad, maybe..." Jeff quickly pulled up Josh's profile. He set it so that Josh would change into a dog when John changed. Jeff also made sure that Josh would also change back when John did. Lastly, he set it so that Josh wouldn't notice any changes of himself or John, and he wouldn't remember his time as a dog.
    As Jeff decided what kind of dog to make his brother, he heard his brother again.
    "Dad? Did you get hairier???" Jeff quickly selected one of the dog options and started the changes.
    "What do you mean Josh? I'm the shhame ath alwayths." Jeff could tell John was quickly become his dog self.
    "Huh. Weird. Oh well. Never mind Dad." Jeff decided to go out to the hall, and see what was happening. As he got downstairs, He could see his dad had changed almost completely changed. The His face was pushing out and growing fur, as he barked happily at Josh.
    "Leths goo pray outshide!" Jeff saw Josh drop to all fours and hop about excitedly with John. Both had stripped out of their clothes, and Josh had almost caught up to his dad's transformation.
    "Yeahrrr! Outshide! Outshide!" John's transformation finished as his nose blackened. Josh's now furry face pushed out as he saw Jeff in the hall. "Jefff! Preeese helph. Want outshide! Wrreee woooaant owooooshieeede! Wroof! Wroof!"
    Josh and John ran to Jeff, and pushed each other out of the way to get attention. Jeff chuckled at the antics of his former relatives. Now two mere dogs with no other desire but to love and play with him.
    "I think I like you this way Josh. Maybe I'll keep you like this!" Josh barked happily at Jeff. He didn't know what his Boy said, but he knew it had to be good.
    Jeff opened the back door and let the two of them out. He watched while the dogs played and barked. John and Josh circled each other as they sniffed the other.
