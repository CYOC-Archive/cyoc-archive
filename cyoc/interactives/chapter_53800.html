He had decided to write down what happened when his memories started changing. The biggest memory change was that there was always Lupmans, Leomans, Felmans, Gargoyles, and Golden Deer. He still knew they hadn't always existed but that knowledge was fading fast. The other change he was aware of was in the new reality Samantha hadn't come onto the boat and forced them to go to the island. Instead a few of the passengers were going there to help record the experiment with the Golden Deer and they were caught up in Kurse's little war. He sighed and went back to writing. At least he knew that this would enable him to keep a record of what really happened even when he didn't remember it anymore. He was thankful that his mom and sister had been witches before they were killed. His mom had taught him about magic and the infinite chaos right along with his sister. She had said that he did posses the gift but it was dormant because he was a boy and very few boys who possess the gift are able to use it.

    Rick paused again and thought about the infinite chaos and Marie. Quite a few of the passengers and crew were afraid of Marie and that fear was justified. Marie had been immersed directly into the infinite chaos by Kurse. It's amazing that she didn't immediately go mad. That probably wouldn't last though. He thought about how he caught her crying in the mirror. He had seen her watching as he proposed to Maylene and then run off. He had followed and when he found her crying he guessed it was because she might not be able to get married.

    Rick shook his head and went back to writing since he could feel his memories slipping more. He had to get this done before he forgot. He paused again as he saw his writing was getting lower on the page. He looked at himself and sighed since he could see that he was getting younger. "So this is how it's going to be," he said shaking his head, "I'm going to be a kid again and won't be able to marry Maylene."

Reality couldn't change someone's feelings easily and since he did get on the boat in the original timeline he still had to have boarded in this timeline. But why a girl? As he sat there and pondered it he didn't notice the blond fur cover his body or his toes and balls of his feet turn into paws. He didn't notice his legs rearrange themselves to become digitigrade. He only noticed he was changing further when he saw a muzzle push out of his face.

It isn't logical unless..." He quit fighting the new memories and opened himself up to them. He immediately knew what had happened. He was Martin's twin brother in the new timeline which made sense since he would need different parents. What startled him though was the battle had gone a bit differently. Instead of Glass going into the mirror world with Abbey by themselves he had tagged along. He had hit his head and was knocked out the moment he entered. When Abby had knocked out Glass she had a moment of indecision of which to take out first. That moment of indecision is what sealed both of their fates as they didn't get out before Kurse was killed.

After a few seconds he said, "My name is Richelle now." She looked around at the now empty cabin and said, "I should get back to Mommy. She'll be worried about me like she is Marie." She climbed up onto the table and stepped through the mirror. Once on the other side she smiled as she saw her dress was red with black runes. That made sense since Martin and Rick liked to dress alike but opposite. Their favorite shirts were a yellow tee with a black smiley face for Rick and a black tee with a yellow smiley face for Martin.
