    "Here we are," Jane said as they pulled up to a small one story house. "Are you sure you're going to be okay here?"

    "Yeah..." Marie replied as she started getting her stuff out of the trunk. "Where will I sleep?"

    "Here, let me help you with that," Jane said and took two of the bags. "I have a guest bedroom down the hall, you can sleep there."

    "Sounds good," Marie said as they headed inside. Marie looked around, it was a pretty nice house all things considered. They walked down the hall to the guest bedroom, which was pretty simple, just had a bed and furniture.

    "I know its not much, but once you get more money we can go shopping so you can decorate it like you want," Jane said. "I put some of my old clothes in the dresser if you need any."

    "Thanks," Marie said, nervously looking around.

    "You get settled in, I'm going to go make a phone call," Jane said and left the room. In the living room she dialed Samantha Twist's number, after a minute she picked up.

    "Hey Jane, how's your friend settling in?" Samantha asked.

    "She's doing fine, thank you," Jane said. "She, umm, wants to know about the town secret, I was wondering if I could just go ahead and tell her."

    "Well..." Samantha said with a pause, that was a very unusual request. "Do you think she can be trusted?"

    "I've known her for years, if anyone can be trusted with the truth, its her," Jane said.

    "Okay, go ahead and tell her," Samantha said. "I trust your judgement Jane, just be careful, we don't need more problems."

    "Yes Mrs. Twist, say hi to Frances and Ben for me," Jane replied and hung up after a moment. She walked back to the bedroom door and took a deep breath. She didn't know the Town's Secret when she first moved in, but when she did learn it she had embraced it pretty readily. Now it was her turn to tell the secret, and she was nervous. She knocked on the door.

    "Come in," Marie said inside and Jane stepped inside, Marie looked up from her unpacking with a smile. "Hey Jane, so what did they say?"

    "You might want to sit down for this," Jane said and Marie nodded, taking a seat on the bed. "The secret I was talking about... well, the truth is that Moon Lake was founded and is partially inhabited by Werebeasts of different kinds."

    Marie blinked, she didn't expect that to be the secret. She had no idea what to say to be honest. "Why are you telling me this?"
