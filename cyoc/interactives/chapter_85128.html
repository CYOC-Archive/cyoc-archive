Of course you will have those that enjoy killing people because they can and I doubt you're one of those vampires so you are not a monster. By the way what name should we call you by now?"

    "I was thinking about using Rikki," Rick said without hesitation. "That's spelled r-i-k-k-i. It's what people have called me before just a different spelling. I won't have to learn to respond to a different name though I'll have to learn to write it."

    "Looks like there's news on it now," Dawn said nodding to the television.

    Indeed there was a woman on the screen though she looked like some type of alien. She had red and black mottled skin that looked like it might be a little rough. Her eyes were purple with vertical slits for pupils, well the eyes in her face anyway since she had about fifteen other eyes on stalks on her head that were different colors. Each of the eye stalks were turning and twisting in different directions. "As many of you know at 11:59 PM UTC an unexplained phenomenon occurred," she said her voice light with a sing song quality to it. "As far as officials and our own reporters can determine at this time this phenomenon has affected the majority of the world's population. As unbelievable as it may seem those affected have been transformed. The extent of the changes are unknown but they seem to run a wide gamut of possibilities, from small changes like eye color changing to major whole body changes like myself." She frowned and then raising her voice she said, "I'm still able to see you guys. Just because Debbie became a cake girl does not mean you can eat her."

Some of them seem to still be alive and aware because they are able to move and communicate. Others may or may not be alive or aware owing to the inability to communicate with them and their stillness. Perhaps in the coming days a way will be found to determine if they are still alive or not. In the meantime it is advised that you eat only what you personally know was food before this phenomenon occurred. Stay tuned for further updates."

    "I wonder when they'll declare martial law," Rikki said shaking her head. Seeing the looks on everyone else's face she said, "Well you know they will. Right now most people are in shock over their transformations. When that shock wears off law and order is going to go out the window. There will be those celebrating their transformation and there will be those that take advantage of their transformation. There will be fights and looting."

    "Well we'll cross that bridge when we get to it," Rikki's dad said running a hand through his hair. His wings fluttered and he rose into the air a little bit before settling back down on the floor. That pretty much summed it up. It wasn't a question of if but of when.
