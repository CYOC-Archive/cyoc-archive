    Sam's choice

    Sam crouched down, looking at Jenn. "Okay, I promised so. Transform or Dare?"

    Jenn barked, but something didn't feel right, the answer had always felt final before, now it felt wrong. Jenn barked again, and Sam just kept staring at her. 'Why isn't he responding.. he must know too. Damn, he can't understand me.' Jenn was in a panic she kept barking and barking, growling in different ways to try and sound the word, and finally, a terrible feeling came over her. A minute had passed.

    With Sam's New facilities He also seemed to recognize the problem, and possibly, it many potential benefits as well. With this language barrier, Sam had full control over what happens to Jenn. Jenn was always a little cold to Sam, but whether or not that will effect his decision is up in the air. A part of his old doggy self kinda likes Jenn as a bitch, it would be a shame to loose such a fine piece of tail even now that he is a human. Maybe keeping her as the NEW family pet would be best for everyone, hes sure she'd learn to like it, if she'd give it a chance. His new smooth appendage rises a little at that idea. He would just have to give her a few little tweaks. Or maybe turning her back into a human, but maker her his girlfreind for a change of pace. Or anything else that comes to mind...

    "Sorry, Jenn, but I couldn't understand your answer, so I guess you're stuck with both, I think I have an idea I want to try out..." The collie Girl whines as she relies her former dog is going off script...
