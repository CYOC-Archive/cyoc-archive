If they pick dare, you will dare them to try Transformation and Dare their friends, and give them the same T & D we're giving you until now. And if they pick both, you'll give them both."

He could still being funny as a happy hobo, but he'll be a hobo, still. If he choose dare, he'll have the same dare we said, and he'll no more drink booze, and he'll turn their drinking friends on hobos and daring them to no more drinking, like I'm daring you to do to him. Obviously, if he choose both, he'll get the full package. Was I clear? Had you any doubt?"
