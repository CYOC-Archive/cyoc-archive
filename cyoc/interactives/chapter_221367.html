Once then, Jeff lead the way walking slightly hunched as Kevin and Rose followed with Rose staying close almost clinging to her mate.

They felt the grass under there feet as if the world was brand new, they noticed animals moving by, swatting away Flys as they were getting close to the smoke. As they climbed up a small hill, Jeff wiped the sweat from hid brow as he looked up and the that the his hunch was correct. The smoke had come from a fire that was made by other cavepeople as he saw them near it. Looking closer he noticed that there were small straw huts out side of a collection of caves on the side of a hill. Some even higher than the others! The caveman then hollered to his friends that there were more like them. They hooted in response as they claimed over the hill to witness it too. They decided to fast run towards the tribe almost running on all four's. Once they made it there the Neanderthals were in aw of the people and sounds of it all. As they entered they noticed quickly that most of the tribe were close to the age they were. They looked around teenage to young adults with almost no elders. But they also noticed how friendly they were as some of the men and women greeted them with grunts and even offered jewelry and pieces of fruit. Rose was given a nice bone necklace from one of the women as the boys got ankle bracelets. One cavewoman came up to Jeff and even offered him a piece of cooked meat as she basically put it in his mouth. He excepted and ate the meat he was giving as he took a look at the woman. She had long red hair that was long and wild pinned in a ponytail from a bone. Her bikini fur was dark brown covering her breasts that were a bit larger than Rose's. He could feel his peines grow within his thong as the two had a small talk through grunts. But she abruptly left because some other person requested food as she went to get more. Jeff knew he had to keep an eye on her. As they continued through the tribe they noticed a small hut that had tools for hunting as Jeff and Kevin gave a look to each other as an idea. They happened to pass one cave that they could here moans and cries. Of course this sounded familiar to them as it was a cave for mateing, something Kevin and Rose were eager to do again as Kevin gave a suggestive grunt to Rose who smiled at the thought. Though the one thing that was on Jeff's mind was that the tibe did not have a set leader as random cavemen called for supper as people grabbed there food and sat by the fire. Maybe he could lead them? Through that didn't matter now as his stomach was calling him. The three grabbed there food and cowed down by the fire with the other cavepeople as they grunted and chewed. The three were eating and pondered what they were going to do in the stone age life.....
