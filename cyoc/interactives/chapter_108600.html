    Exploring a nearby forest.

    Well, it's not like your in a rush, might as well enjoy nature a bit, you are immortal after all.

    You shift into your fox form, enjoying the feeling of your weak human muscles turn powerful and strong. You go on all fours and start running towards the forest, becoming a black dash on the yellow green of the grass beneath your feet.

    You quickly enter the forest, going faster than any Olympic runner ever could. Your advance senses pick of everything from the tiny movements of from a ant walking on the forest floor to the tiny vibrations of a rabbit burrowing into it's underground nest. You even heard the calls of a couple of deer a few miles ahead.

    The forest around you reminds you of a certain place. That strange park with the three doors. The entire reason you had gotten your powers. Then you thought of that insane football player. You forgot his name. What it Jake? Or maybe it was Jack? You couldn't remember.

    What had happened to him? He was clearly alive albeit crazy when you last saw him. Did he go through one of the other two doors? If so which one? The Angelic or Nature Door? Was another person running around with supernatural abilities just like you? You would've been okay if it was just some random guy, but if someone like Jacob had magic, then that worried you immensely.

    You never really felt panicked about anything, except when it comes to Jace. That look of pure insanity he gave you, the look of pure murderous intent shivered you to the bone.

    Well, it's not good to worry about things too much, you should try and keep your mind off it you think to yourself.

    You keep running, enjoying the feeling of wind against your furred face. The way the leaves crunch against your paws as you run. You could get used to this.

    Then you pick up on something. You hear something deep in the woods to your right.

    What was it?
