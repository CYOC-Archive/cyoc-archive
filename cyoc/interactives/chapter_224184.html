    Make Babies, Not War

    added by Mad Min 16 days ago O Clothes

    Even if Sierra Cove was Sean before the transformation and kept his memory and opinions, the pre-select "HIPPIE" option on the Chronivac still gave Sierra the stereotypical hippie's viewpoint. So when Sierra finished with her transformation, she went ahead and made some more changes. But the changes that she made were for the house and her new wardrobe.
    Soon the tacky T-shirts and threadbare jeans were shifted to halter tops, flowing skirts, and the new addition of the midi dresses. Sierra also changed the tighty whities that Sean has to proper panties for her, but she didn't add bras for her breasts. "Bras are made to hold women back," Sierra said to herself, conforming to the program that the Chronivac left her.

    As for the house, Sierra has made it more psychedelic and colorful than before. A woven rug here and there, tie-dye sheets drape the walls, and the gaming couch that the boys played on has been replaced with beanbags and pillow cushions. Sierra reached over beside the computer and grabbed the bong (that had always been there) to get her started on her trip. After a quick huff and puff, Sierra put down her "medicinal drug" and started to think about what else she could do with this miraculous piece of tech.

    Sierra looked down at her lithe body, feeling how little she became with her much smaller hands. The changing of the clothes also changed the clothes she was wearing when she was Sean. The T-shirt and jeans combo that Sean was wearing became a tie-dye halter top and a long, patchwork skirt. The clothes that Sierra replaced really showed off her flat belly due to how high the halter top wore and how low the long skirt hung.

    Miracles. "little hands." Belly.

    The single thought that rang out from the weed-induced haze was that Sierra wanted to be full of life, to be able to have children to have and to own. Sierra glanced at her computer, the Chronivac was still running. "Maybe I can," slurred out Sierra, the drowsy side effect kicking in while she leaned forward to the computer. After a few misclicks in her attempt to get her file, with one of the misclicks making her unaware of her changes to go alongside everyone, she made it to her fertility tab.

    Sierra giggled at how many babies she could put in her and at how long the gestation could be. "So the lowest I can go is 1 baby in 1 minute to 999 babies in 999 years. I don't think I can live for that long or have that many babes either." Sierra nodded her head lower and lower before finally falling asleep, accidentally clicking "Enter" on the "RANDOM BABY NUMBER GENERATOR".
