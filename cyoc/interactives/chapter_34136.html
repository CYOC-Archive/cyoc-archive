    She couldn't get over how tiny she was now, it was weird...and kinda cool.

    She still wasn't sure why the girl hadn't just called the cops...but then it occured to her. Of

    her the awesome power to attract pretty girls.

    An ability she was now prepared to enjoy.

    The other girl hadn't bothered to get dressed. "Like I'm letting you out of my sight?
    Besides, it's nothing you haven't already seen, right?" The taller girl winked at that, and
    busied herself with breakfast.

    "So, what's your name? I'm Alex...short for Alexis, but if you call me that, I'll hurt you."

    Rachel believed that. "I'm Rachel."

    Alex sniffed. "Rachel...huh. Do you mind if I call you Rey?"

    "Ray? Well I guess..."

    Alex shook her head. "I said Rey. With an e."

    It sure sounded the same to Rachel. Then she remembered an anime she'd watched once.
    "You sure it's not Rei?"

    Alex paused. "You know, that's not bad. Ok, Rei it is!"

    "How could you tell the difference?" Rachel blurted out.

    "Very good hearing." She grinned.

    Rachel wasn't sure what to make of that.

    "So...Alex...um...do you usually invite strange girls into your home for breakfast?"

    Alex set down several plates of food, and poured 'Rei' a glass of orange juice before sitting
    down herself. "Dig in...and do you usually go around looking at naked girls in the
    morning?"

    "Uh..no...first time." Rachel reached for some toast. Dammit, she'd been so confident
    before, what was it about this girl that was making her so nervous?

    "Did you like what you saw?" Alex smiled suggestively.

    Rachel almost choked on her food, and reached for the juice to wash down the toast.

    "Gah..."

    "You need to be more careful, Rei. Not that I'd mind giving you CPR."

    Rachel took a few moments to collect herself. "Look, I'm sorry I cut through your yard and
    I'm sorry for staring at you. But you can stop now."

    Alex blinked. "Stop...?"

    Rachel glared balefully at the other girl. "There's no way any girl would be this forward.
    So you're just making fun of me."

    "What, no I'm not!"

    "Yeah right. You caught a girl checking you out, and you decided you just had to seduce
    her?"

    "Well...um..." Alex blinked a few times, then frowned. "That's exactly what I was going to
    do...and you're right, that is weird."

    Rachel stood up. "Well, whatever, I'm going now."

    Alex stood as well. "Please..don't?"

    Something about her tone of voice struck Rachel. She's...lonely. What the hell, why would
    a girl like her be lonely? It doesn't make any sense...it's got to be a trick. But looking into
    those eyes....Rachel knew she was trapped.

    "...ok."

    Alex beamed like a little girl at Disney World. Rachel felt her heart melt. She would never
    be able to deny this girl anything.

    "But I guess I should get dressed. Sorry...I don't know what came over me. Be right back,
    please, don't run!" And Alex dashed up the stairs.

    "What a strange girl. I think I'm in love."

    Rachel finished her breakfast, and realized Alex wasn't back yet. What to do now?
