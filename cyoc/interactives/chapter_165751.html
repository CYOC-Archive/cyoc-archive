    Sitting on the edge of Christy's desk Steve noted that he was about as big as her teddy bear that was now a nice decoration. While he sat all he could really do was watch her rummage through everything. Then she found what she wanted and as Christy turned Steve saw a plastic bin full of dolls and doll clothes.
    As she brought them over Christy asked "So I was thinking that we could play a game of house... So I got a maid, a butler, a dog, a nice lady, and a kind gentleman that should fit in this scenario."
    "So I'm not the d-" Steve blurted out before cutting himself off.
    Christy chuckled and said "No... I just wanted to play a fun game of house using my dolls and the new doll house I just got. Since we're best friends I wanted to give you the first pick." Steve looked at them and the maid looked nice and dignified victorian maid, the butler kinda reminded Steve of that guy on the cover of Black Butler, the dog was a golden retriever, the lady was a barbie doll in an elegant dress, and the man looked like a ken doll in formal attire. Steve thought that while the butler kinda looked cool he'd rather be the man in the house and as such chose the gentleman. Christy nodded and then went to the mansion doll house and seemed to be setting things up, and then things went black to Steve.

    Steve opened his eyes and found himself in a giant living room sitting on a really comfortable home. So spacious, there was a large TV, and as Steve turned to his side he found a giant barbie. He was only surprised for a second as Steve got up to examine the barbie who seemed life sized, but since he was wearing Ken's clothes... It was probably the case that he had inhabited Ken. Which was odd as his body was all smooth skin and anatomically correct... meanwhile you could still see this barbie's joints. Steve giving a testing touch to the Barbie doll and it was still hard plastic. Then it seemed to twitch for a moment though and suddenly the joints started smoothing out. Steve's hand was still on her arm as it immediately became soft and warm. Her eyes closed for a second and then they immediately opened.
    "Gah!" The barbie screamed a bit surprised at waking up to someone being so close "Why are you so close?!" and shoved Steve off of her.
    "Sorry, you were just a doll a second ago..." Steve noted that his voice was different and asked "Wait Christy is that you?"
