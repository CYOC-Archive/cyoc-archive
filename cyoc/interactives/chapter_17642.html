    "Revenge?! But what have I done? I thought we were friends!" Mary was starting to panic. She had found this wonderful magic in the vials. And had wanted to share it with her friend Jess, but now things were looking quite different.

    Mary thought only of changing back before she became a canary. She started for the box of vials but the tingling of the magic had now become a pickling all over her body. She surmised that her feathers had started growing. It was terribly itchy and Mary rubbed her hands all over her body, trying to satisfy it. Jess watched in apparent pleasure.

    "You might want to take off your clothes, you know." Jess offered in a friendly manner. Mary just glared at her, but took off her clothes. Her skin was still human looking, except that there were numerous bumps growing out all over it. Goose-flesh was Mary's first thought. It was like she was really cold. Soon, however, small translucent nibs where pushing their way through the skin. Mary quickly forgot about the need to get another vial and watched and felt her feathers grow in.

    The feathers sprouted first as a hairless tube. But soon thin filaments filled in. They were mostly yellow. Some were a darker brown to black. Most of those on her arms were coming in dark brown with yellow edges.

    It wasn't long before Mary was covered in feathers. Her body shape was still human, but covered in feathers. With the change complete, Mary remembered her goal of the vials. Mary started to walk toward the vials and Jess, but was suddenly disoriented. Everything was bigger! Oh, no! She was shrinking. Jess let out an evil laugh when she say Mary, about four feet high and shrinking with feathers.

    Mary felt a sinking feeling. She was not going to be able to change herself back. She panicked and started looking for an exit. But Jess had already closed the door and stood in front of it. Mary walked quickly away, her head starting to bob naturally. Soon Mary stopped walking and was hopping on two thin legs. Her feet grew into three long toes, with on coming out the back. A little black claw emerged at the ends.

    Next, Mary's arms changed, they molded themselves to her sides and the feathers grew long and firm. They were now canary wings. All this time, Mary was still shrinking. Smaller and smaller she became. From three feet to two, then to one foot. A tail seemed to poof out of Mary's back. It was unlike anything she had felt, it was thick and large, and now had long tail feathers growing out of it.

    Mary's face felt cool as her large beak slowly grew out. It was nearly the length of her face. Short and dark. Mary's size shrank down a little bit further until she was no more than four inches tall. She hopped around in dismay, not sure what to do.
