    After another commercial break, Miles did a brief story on a really surprising thing. Even he was visibly surprised about it.

    "When summer vacation ends, some people like to celebrate it with one final hoorah before the 'shackles' of the school year are 'clapped on', metaphorically speaking, and Ao Guang Island is doing this with their, and this is a rough translation of the name, 'Retreat to Beat the Heat'. Despite what some people on the internet may claim, this is only open to Chinese, Japanese, and Korean people between 30 and 60 years old." At that, there was a lot of complaining, mainly from internet "activists", but these seemed to show that such people have no idea what they're talking about. "What is also clear is that, judging from what Ao Guang Island has shown me, roughly 25-40% of the women are from bloodlines that have been, *ahem*, cursed by ushi-oni, for lack of a better term." At that, there were so many lewd comments on 'big-busted Asians' that these were eventually hit with 'horny jail/bonk' memes just to get them to shut up, with various forms of effectiveness. Of course, that was without Miles mentioning that the "Retreat" was also going to be Animalia's second official "conversion orgy".

    "Speaking of the school year, one of the most anticipated things to see is the rivalry between Ninja and Samurai Prep, since Ninja Prep is going to be officially opening this year." Reported Miles, much to the cliched responses in the comments. "But before you get your hopes up, this is most likely to be more like realistic rivalries between high schools, in sports events or otherwise, rather than what is portrayed in anime and manga." At that, several commentors clearly 'deflated' with disappointment.

    "Finally, be sure to visit the Animalia website to see the official comparisons between Olympic Athletes and Amateur Animalian Athletes." Said Miles, as the episode drew to a close. "And remember, even if you're really, really good at something, you can still mess up."
