She hasn't bothered to apply herself at all past graduating, and has spent most of the last year lounging around the house, pretending to look for a job. She has zero plans for college, and you're confident that she isn't really going anywhere in life, though somehow your parents are oblivious to this fact. Your parents have always treated her better than you, just because she's older. She got a free car before she even had her permit, and she never uses the thing, but also never lets you use it either. You aren't looking at her when you said all that, currently looking through the cabinets to find something to eat. Did you convince her to, or did she do it because you told her to? And why would she listen. Being allowed to do anything doesn't mean that others have to listen to you, does it?

Thankfully she was now wearing a bra or that could have looked much worse for her. So it does seem like she has to obey. "Why are you listening to me?" you ask, hopefully to get some answers about how this all works now.

    "Pretty sure yeah. I don't know why anybody wouldn't. We all listen to you." she says, as if it's incredibly normal.
    "Hey Caleb. Sneeze." You say to Caleb, who is now cleaning up his dishes after finishing eating. You say this as a test to see if you can get somebody to do something that would normally be difficult for them to force. Immediately after finishing the command, Caleb sneezes directly into the bowl he's cleaning. He continues cleaning as if nothing happened. They really listen to everything you say. You think of another test of this newer part of your power. Can they obey commands they don't hear? You whisper under your breath, quiet enough that Caleb definitely doesn't hear you, or at least he wouldn't be able to make out the words. "Hey Caleb. Sneeze again." Immediately after you say it Caleb sneezes again. That's so cool. You whisper something again. "Hey Kate. Come out here." There's no way she heard that from up in her room, nor did you want her to. But surely enough you hear her door unlock, and she grumpily stomps down the stairs and into the kitchen. "Why did you come back down?" you ask, curious if she knew you called her.
    "I just did, okay? Is that a good enough reason for you?" She says, still clearly annoyed with you from earlier. Interesting. So when you don't want them to realize they've been commanded, they don't seem to know why they're doing something, or at least they come up with a reason in their head to justify it, but they still follow through with the command. So intent does matter. You then think of two more tests that you can do in one command.

In a blink her hair has instantly turned bright blonde instead of her natural brown. You weren't sure whether her hair would change instantly, or whether she would go get it dyed. Turns out it was the former.
