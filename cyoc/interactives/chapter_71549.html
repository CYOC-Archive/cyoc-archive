    May had never considered herself lucky, she was nearing 95 and never married, dated but never loved, and while she did travel, she would usually be by herself.

    May had heard that Toon Town was not only a place to visit, one could also live there. She came to the visitor center and talked to the receptionist toon woman.

    "Where would I be able to go if I wanted to live here?" May asked.

    "Let me show you the way ma'am." The toon woman said as she stood up and May followed her to a door labeled 'MIRRORS'.

    "Just go through this door ma'am and you'll soon begin your life anew in toon town." The toon woman said as she left May by herself.

    May opened the door and it suddenly became dark, then a single light turned on revealing three mirrors with May at different stages in her life: Child, Teenager, Adult.

    "Oh dear, what am I supposed to do in here?" May turned to open the door but found there was no door entry anymore.

    'Don't worry May, you're doing fine.' Her Teenage reflection said.

    "You can talk?" May asked shocked.

    'Of course, we each tell you how your life was in three stages and then you jump into one of our mirrors and we continue from there.' May's adult self explained.

    "Oh, Alright."

    'If you choose me, you'll live all the excitement of childhood all over again and be able to play with kids your age. The drawback is that you won't be able to do a whole lot of other stuff until you're older.' Her child version explained.

    'If you choose me, you'll be able to hang out with the younger generation and experience a whole new world similar to how it was for you years ago: friends, dating, and having a good time. The drawback is your emotions might get the better of you sometimes, though you'll learn to live with some mistakes.' Her teen self explained.

    'And if you choose me, doing things that you could when you were much younger might be gone but you can still act like them at heart. The drawback is you might miss the things you could have done.' Her adult self explained.

    After thinking for a moment, May made her decision and jumped into the mirror with her...
