    A little too far back

    .........two baby girls who seemed much younger than 3 months old. They both looked like they were born not that long ago. Mark shook his head and turned to look at the dial for the age setter.

    When he saw it, he felt sheepish. “Oops, I turned it a little too far back girls, you two are now 18 months old according to this dial.” he said, followed by a suggestion “Do you both want try again or stay like this?”

    Luckily the girls retained their adult awareness and shook their heads no the best they could. They liked being this little. They giggled and were joyful to be babies again.

    “Well that’s good! You two are sooooooo CUTE!” Heather replied. She and Mark each picked up a baby and took them to the nursery to get a diaper on them, they took the clothes and diapers that fell off of them in the machine with them.

    Once they took care of everything and got to the nursery, they saw that the nursery had changed too following the machine’s magic that had altered their ages and surroundings. “It really does work!” They both said in amazement at the same time.

    The nursery had a crib that fit both babies inside of it, two changing tables, a dresser filled with onesies and footers for bedtime. One of the other drawers was filled with baby blankets to wrap them in also and cover them when they would be in their carriers. Speaking of, two of those were in the corner of the nursery for when they needed to go out in public. Finally, on the changing tables were acrylic jars filled with infant pacifiers. Mostly pink and magenta to show off their genders.

    Mark and Heather wasted no time and got them diapered up. Pretty soon, they would have to use it, especially after their bottles they were about to have soon. Once they were diapered they were each wrapped in a baby blanket and carried out to the kitchen area. Mark took both of them in his arms and rocked them softly, meanwhile Heather made their bottles .

    Mark was enjoying having two baby girls to take care of. He relished this moment, even if he knew they were really grown adults in an infant’s body. He wondered if they were aware of how cute they were and how tiny they seemed to the world.

    Those thoughts were interrupted when Heather came over with two of their bottles and gave one over to Mark, she took Paige and Mark took Sarah to feed. Once the bottle was introduced to their lips, they immediately started sucking the milk out and enjoyed every last drop. ‘This actually doesn’t taste half bad’ Sarah thought while she was drinking her bottle.

    Sarah still had an adult conscience in this body, she thought it was weird how she could think like an adult and only have the functions of an infant. Paige thought the same thing as well, but was enjoying being a baby too much to really care.

    Once they finished their bottles, they were burped properly by their new parents. Both of the parents then pondered what to do next.
