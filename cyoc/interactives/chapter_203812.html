    Blending In

    added by Maxxatom A year ago O Clothes

    As much as he wanted to spend the rest of his days caring for you, he knows that there would be an endless sea of questions and interrogations from neighbors, colleagues, and officers if he just treated you as a pet. With a determined look, he faced you and said, “Let’s get you ready for the world.” He grabbed a blue and purple dress for you to wear as you do a twirl in it, showing off. He gave you a thumbs up and said, “Now to get you some other outfits and get you into school. I’ll monitor you through this necklace, we’ll survey the school and I’ll make sure your safe in your first day. Ready, sweetie?” You nod your head with a twinkle in your eye, following his lead as he takes you outside for the first time since your transformation.

    The sun feels nice on your human skin, you drink it all in as Peter keeps his eye on you, trying his best to be a real father figure. You question him about a few things that normal five-year old girls usually do and say, but you can tell he’s struggling to give you an easy answer. Though your body is young m, your brain is still that of a 25 year old female, making blending in a bit more difficult. Not to mention you’re dragon form, which makes you more dexterous and sensitive than most adult humans you know. After a few weird looks, you and your “father” Peter make it to a clothing store that has clothing that he feels you might like. He grabs a few outfits that are your size and lays them out for you. “Well, what do you think?”

    Which outfit do you pick?
