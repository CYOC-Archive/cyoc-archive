    Nate stands where he had been after entering the room, looking the bear statue up and down, somewhat staring at it.

    "Hey Nate, whatcha' thinkin' there bro?"

    When I spoke to him, Nate came out of his stunned stupor. "Oh... what? I am sorry bro, I really was listening, but I am in awe of what we just created."

    "That's a good thing bro, but keep in mind that we can do what we want for the rest of our lives. If it's what you want, the both of us could be just like that bear!"

    "I didn't really think about it in that way! Do you want to be like that statue?"

    "Oh god yes! I would enjoy that more than anything else in the world.

    "Well then Nate, why don't you go ahead and say what you want. I'll caution you though, make sure you word your desire specifically. Even though we can change things until they are perfect for us, in every way, shape and form, it places an unknown amount of stress on the world around us. So, if you can get things right the first time for ourselves, it will be best. I know that once things are right for the both of us, we will more than likely make it so for the rest of the world."

    "Way to put the weight of the world on my shoulders!"

    "You wanna' know somethin' though? I know that you are up to the task, especially since you did such a nice job with that statue."

    "Thanx David, so when I do this, what kind of were-creature would you like to be?"

    "Hmmm... that is a good question, since I can be anything I want. I like how that bear came out, it would be very hot to be like him. I will have to say though, a black furred werewolf with features like that bear is appealing too. Oh, not to mention being an anthro dobie or german shepard. Jeeze bro, I have no clue! Why don't you choose for me and whatever you pick out, I'll agree with it."

    "Ok David, I'll see if I can figure out which one will suit you best."

    "Take your time Nate, I have all the confidence in the world in your decision making skills"

    As I stand and wait at the console, I am a bit curious to know what he was thinking toward what he would make me. I knew that his choice had already been made up in his mind. I decide to take a few steps toward him and I could see that the tenting in his pants was becoming more and more taught, almost ready to bust through the layers of clothing he had on. Nate was the older brother by a year and when we had taken a shower together in gym class while in high school, I saw that he had a much larger and thicker package than I had.

    "Alright, I know what you are going to be and I know you are going to like the way you turn out."

    "So then... what am I going to be?"

    "You are going to be what you mentioned, the black werewolf and I am going to be just like our statue friend here. Is there anything you want to add into our little request?"

    "Yup, just a couple. The first thing is to make it so we aren't family anymore, but we will know that we are brothers. The second is for us to either be in human form or anthro, having the ability to change at will. The third is during our first change, we will change as humans and once our bodies are in their newer human form, we will change into our anthro forms after five minutes of being in the new human form. The last thing is that if we enjoy both our new human forms as well as our anthro forms, we will remain what we have wanted to be changed into, one hour after our first transformation to anthro, all the people on the planet will become like us, either bear or wolf, fur color and type will depend on their current physical condition, everyone will be able to change at will after the first transformation, no one will age, or die, or get sick, all the people on the planet will be male, there will not be any births or deaths so population will remain the same, there will not be any family designation for one another, the planet will be self sustaining, once something is cut down or killed for food, it will be replaced automatically and instantly so there will never be a food shortage, the planet will always remain the same temperature at a warm 75 degrees, no matter your location, nothing in the world will go wrong, no matter what it is and all of the changes made once in place will be viewed by all to have been the way it always has been as no one will have a memory of how things were before we changed the world."

    "Ok, I think the machine got everything you wanted. You ready over there David?"

    Of course I was ready, I had always wanted life to be the way we've set it up to be. Now that everything was set, all of the parameters specified, I pressed the "Accept"toggle. The machine made it's hum again, this time a bit louder and I knew that things were going to be much more enjoyable, as well as for the better.
