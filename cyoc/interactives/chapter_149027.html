    "So?" Emile asked impatiently. "How'd it go?"

    "It's... crazy. I don't even think I was able to see most of them but everyone is different." She responded still staring at the crowd.

    "Well, like how?" He asked.

    "Um... see that chica over there?" She asked, to which Emile nodded. "Well, she used to be some fat nerdy white boy, but now she's a thick asian beauty."

    "No way that babe?!" Emile laughed.

    Emile kept looking around at the food court while Raquel looked at him puzzled. She still remembered him as her bff Emily, but she also had the new reality's memories of Emile as her boyfriend, and he would never say something like that. Raquel remembered a memory that never happened where Emile said a girl looked nice and Raquel almost murdered him.

    Emile noticed Raquel looking at him funny and quickly realized that they were dating now. He was still thinking of Raquel as his buddy Rick but Rick currently believed he was a hot latina. Emile coughed with an awkward laugh mumbling something along the lines of a "sorry babe" which was still weird enough for him to say. Raquel acted like the response was enough but something in the back of her head told her to pay more attention.

    "Well... uh... I guess it's my turn right?" Emile asked. "What should I do with it, another group shot?"

    "Yeah I guess so... it's not like we'll be able to turn everyone here back anyway so we might as well go all out." She said as she turned to face the group.

    Emile raised the camera back at the food court and took the pic, but what happens next?
