    You swam up to the reef of coral to check out the figure. It was some kind of mermaid like being. She (no doubt) had a shark's tail instead of legs, hands that were webbed with no nails, gills on her neck, a slight snout with sharp teeth, small boobs, fish-like eyes like yours, fins on her arms & one on her back, gray shark skin & no visible ears & no body hair. Also she was totally naked (like you). She noticed you & swam over to you. 'An incomplete.' she said to you. You're not sure how she spoke. 'What's an incomplete?' you asked puzzled; not sure how you spoke underwater too. 'You're an incomplete. I'm a complete.' she told you. 'A mermaid like you? Am I eventually going to turn into a mercreature like you?' you asked. 'Yes.' she answered nodding. 'What if I don't want to?' you asked her. She chuckled a bit & replied 'You do want to.' 'What makes you say that?' you asked puzzled. 'You feel relaxed here. It feels good being here.' she pointed out. It was true what she said. 'You are a seacreature like me. You will transformation totally into a shark merman in time.' she told you. 'No I'm not! I'm a human! I won't be like you!' you yelled upset by that. You quickly swam away from her. You swam back to the hole & went through it back into your living room. You sat on the floor dripping wet for awhile intill you calmed down. You got up & dried yourself off. For the next few days you couldn't get the Ocean out of your mind. You soon realize that the ocean is the only place you want to be. You for the last time stripped completely naked & drove into the pool. Your gills reopened & the webbing between your fingers and toes returned. You began swimming around calling out to the shark mermaid & soon found her in the same spot. 'Hi again.' she said nicely. 'You're right. I belong here.' you told her. 'I know.' she stated. 'How do I become a seacreature like you?' you asked her. 'To become a complete? A shark merman?' she asked. You nodded. She took your hands in hers & told you 'I'll help you.'
