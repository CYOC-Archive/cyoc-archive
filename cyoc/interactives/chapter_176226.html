She was the second to last interviewee and knew she had either position in the bag and was happy as she walked out until she saw Brittany. Her heart sank, her special moment of great triumph ruined. She knew she would have to work with Britt. Even though both roles are equal, Jeanne's mind slipped into that bitter place she tries to avoid. According to Jeanne, it seems like she can never have the spotlight to herself and with her history with Britt, Jeanne could single-handily save the company 12 billion dollars and the "golden girl" will still get credit and Jeanne will be cast into the shadows like always. No matter what she is in a never ending battle with this whore.
