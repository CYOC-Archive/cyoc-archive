    "TINA!" Jacob and Tina's Dad shouted startling both of them. "Sweet cakes come here please" he shouted to their Mom.

    After a few seconds they saw their Mom come in and ask, "What is it Darling?"

    "Tina just used some foul language" their Dad explained. "Do you know where she could have picked it up?"

    "No I don't" their Mom said. "What did she say?"

    Their Dad leaned over and whispered what Tina said into their Mom's ear. A look of total shock came over her face. "Our little angel said that?" she asked in a disbelieving voice.

    "I'm afraid so" their Dad said.

    "Where could she have learned such language?" their Mom asked.

    "That's what I'm wondering" their Dad said.

    "I have never heard that language around them while I've been with them" their Mom said. "I wonder if Tammy could have been exposing them to that type of language."

    "We should call her and find out" their Dad said.

    Their Mom picks up the bottle off the floor and says "Good idea Darling. I hope that she's home."

    Their Dad looks at first Jacob then Tina and said "I'll be back up to read your bedtime story in a bit. Now you two be good or I'll have no choice but to spank you."

    Both Jacob and Tina winced as their parents left the room. As soon as she was sure that they were well out of earshot Tina said "Now my sisser in twouble. Way to go Yake."

    "You're the potty mouth" Jacob said.

    "This wouldna happen if you didna ange me in fiwst pwace" Tina said.

    "I wa having fun at fiwst" Jacob said. "You were the meanny."

    "You wight" Tina admitted. "Wha do we do now do?"

    "We nee to get to puter" Jacob said.

    "The door cwosed do" Tina said.

    "I know" Jacob said.

    As they were thinking about how they were going to get to the computer the door opened and their Mom and Dad came in. There was a man with them that Jacob recognized as one of his Dad's friends from work.

    "I hope you can help us figure this out Rick" their Dad said.

    "Well I'll do my best" Rick said. He went over to Tina and said "Hello Tina."

    "Hewwo" Tina said.

    "I understand that you said some things you shouldn't have" Rick said kneeling down and looking Tina's eyes.

    "Uh huh" Tina said.

    "Do you want to tell me why you said those things?" Rick asked.

    "Me weally big boy" Tina said.

    "How come you're a three year old girl then?" Rick asked.

    "Yake used puter to ange me" Tina said.

    "Why did he do that?" Rick asked.

    "Cuz I ange him fiwst" Tina said.

    "What did you change him into?" Rick said.

    "I ange him to a wittle giwl" Tina said.

    "But he's a boy now" Rick said.

    "Dat cuz I ange him back to a boy" Tina said. "Den I spank him."

    "Why did you do that?" Rick asked.

    "Cuz I wanted to make him cwy" Tina said.

    "I see" Rick said as he got up. He then went over to Jacob and knelt down to look into his eyes. "Is Tina telling me the truth Jacob?" he asked.

    "Uh huh" Jacob said.

    "You're not telling me the truth" Rick said. "What else is there?"

    "I ange Tina fiwst" Jacob said. "I ange her fwom teen boy to baby boy. Den I made her wet herself."

    "And Tina changed you to get back at you for that?" Rick asked.

    "Uh huh" Jacob said.

    "Ok" Rick said as he got up. He turned to their parents and said "Neither of them are possessed."

    "Are they telling the truth?" their Dad asked.

    "Yes they are" Rick said. "At least so far as they're concerned."

    "What do you mean?" their Mom asked.

    "Have you seen the original Star Wars movies?" Rick asked.

    "Yes we have" their Dad answered.

    "Well in A New Hope Obi-Wan told Luke that Darth Vader had betrayed and killed his father" Rick said. "Then Luke found out that Darth Vader was his father. When Obi-Wan's spirit appeared to him he said that he had lied to him. Obi-Wan then said it was true from a certain point of view. This is like that. From their point of view they are telling the truth."

    "Well what do we do then?" their Mom asked.

    "Well it seems simple enough" their Dad said. "We go look at the computer and see if it could have changed them."

    "I think something did change them since they both have a strange energy around them" Rick said.

    "Could it be demonic?" their Dad asked.

    "I don't think so" Rick said. "I haven't seen this type of energy before."

    "Ok, let's take the kids and see if the computer changed them" their Dad said.
