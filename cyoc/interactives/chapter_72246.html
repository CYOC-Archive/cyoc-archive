    Meanwhile at the beach house, Tommy called up Randy.

    He said to him, “Hey Randy, how’s everything?”

    Randy said over his speaker phone, “Everything’s going pretty well. Alexandra and I are just hanging out, making out, and watching several
    seasons of Parks and Recreation. Also, my girlfriend says hi.“

    Alexandra replies, “Hi Tommy! Everything is going great, and we’re just chillaxing and being super cool as usual. How about you?”

    Tommy said, “Just finished some legal documents with a client of mine to finish the day. So, Randy, Is Josh around?”

    Randy said, “Nope. He went out with Hugh for the night.”

    Tommy replied, “Huh, wasn’t expecting that. Anyways, do you want to get a drink with me at the Party Club (Bar and Grill)? If yes, make sure
    to bring your girlfriend. It’d be cool to talk to you man to man, since we haven’t talked to each other too often.”

    Randy replied, “That’d be great! What time should we meet up?”

    Tommy said, “10:00 (p.m).”

    Randy responded, “Sweet! My girlfriend and I will be there.”

    Tommy replied, “Cool. Also, if you get ahold of Josh and Hugh, call them up so they arrive, too. This should be fun. Also, make sure to keep
    your phone on so I can send texts if I have arrived or not. I always tend to arrive a little late, but we should be in for a good time.”

    Randy replied, “Will do. Talk to you later.”

    Tommy replied, “Later.”


    (Back to 1st person POV with Josh Regence.)

    Nathan went up to me, and said, “They’re here. I don’t know how many, but we have to walk out of here. You’ve been spotted.”

    I said to him, “Nathan, you’re acting ridiculous. It’s a party. Let’s have a couple of drinks! I just met what’s her face, Sally? Brittany? Anyways,
    she gave me her number-“

    Nathan replies, “Josh, I don’t have time for that. You’ve had one too many. It’s time to go.”

    I replied back, “I’ve only had a couple of beers. And some shots. But I ain’t drunk! I’m here to paaaaaaaaaaartay!!!!!”

    Nathan said, “No, I don’t think so. I think we should go. I’ll catch up with you later.”

    I replied, “Are you sure? I just got here.”

    Nathan said, “Yup, I’m sure.”

    Damn. I just guess I have to call it a night. I have a number from that of Brittany Esterhoff. Checking my phone….oh, she’s pretty. B-E-A-
    utiful! Thank god I took a picture of her. One in fact. Hope she still remembers who I am.

    That’s not a bad idea that Randy had. We’re still best buds. He can be my best man, and us four can all hang out together in the same house.
    I like that idea.


    (Back to non-1st person POV)

    A man with a walkie talkie phones in to one of his bosses. He’s a bearded man with curly, dark brown hair, wearing a black suit with a white
    collared shirt, a black tie, pants, and is wearing a red bowtie. He’s about 6 foot 1, 180 pounds, and looks like he’s in his mid-20s.

    He says to his boss, “Sir, I spotted Mr. Regence. He’s walking out of the club. Should I pursue?”

    Looking away in a remote location, Mr. Dodson Jr. says in a suite hidden away, “No. This time you don’t, Mr. Cummings. He knows already,
    and his guard is up. I repeat, do not pursue.”

    Mr. Cummings asks, "So we are not chasing him? No chase scenes?"

    Mr. Dodson jr replies, "That is correct, sir. I repeat, do not pursue him."

    Mr. Cummings states, “I know his friends are staying at the beach house. I was tipped off at one of the parties that were being hosted a while
    ago. Should I crash their house?”

    Mr. Dodson Jr. replies, “Yes. Bring several of the men. If you see people, storm the castle, raid them, and hold them for ransom. If not, then I
    call it off.”

    Mr. Cummings responds, “But all of my men will think that you’re just calling a bluff on Mr. Regence. They already have never seen your face,
    so it’ll be harder for me to get them to keep placing their trust with you. So we’re just going to instill some fear in him, and not attack? That’s
    it?”

    Mr. Dodson Jr. replies, “That’s the point. To confuse the living fuck, out of Josh Regence, until I can take him when his guard is down. To get
    him to forget about us, and to live the good life. Do you follow?”

    Mr. Cummings responds sternly, “I do. Now let’s get out of here.”

    Afterwards, Mr. Cummings greets Nathan at the club, after Josh already walks out of the club.

    Nathan replies, “Hey, Aaron! How’ve you been! So how was the beach house party!”

    Aaron replies, “It was alright. Just talked to my boss. It’s a no go. Someone got to him, so we lost track of him.”

    Nathan replied, “Damn. Well, maybe we’ll get him next time. How’d we slip up like that?”

    Aaron replied, “No clue. I suppose maybe we have a rat tailing our organization.”

    Nathan responds, “That probably isn’t it. If we do have a snitch, we should smoke him or her out.”

    Aaron replied, “Well, he’s not in the building. Time to check his address.”
