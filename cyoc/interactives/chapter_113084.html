    The Next Successor

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 8 years ago O

    Before the universe and its inhabitance came into existence, there was originally a single immortal entity. Within the void of empty space was this immense source of sentient energy, which soon manipulated its own power into the elements that make up the physical world we inhabit today. Taking on the name "Origin" this god-like spirit proceeded to follow destiny down the costly path of realizing that vast potential hidden inside himself. After creating galaxy after galaxy, Origin finally understood that despite the expanse that was laid out before him, what was the point of having such space without anyone else to share it with. Despite finally beginning to grow weak, Origin gave up his immortality by using the majority of his remaining energy to create the first lifeforms, with the leftover emotions and memory becoming new sentient forms holding the fraction of the power their predecessor once held, each with their own limits. It is said among legends that apparently Origin's memory, the strongest and most unrestricted of the pieces, ruled over all the emotions as King, with their sole goal being to honor their father's last wish to protect and nurture the inhabitants of Earth. Just as Origin before them had, his descendants continued to pass on their powers and will, each taking on their own distinct form and job. Titania, the heir to both Origin's love and memory, is the next successor in line to rule over the cosmos. Unfortunately for both all lifeforms and gods, possessing the emotion of love means the next in line is a Fairy, and they can't even do their job of cupid without causing some mischief. Now that in Fairy years Titania is just turned 18, she has officially awakened to her broad skill set and many of the other gods have come to fear what chaos she may bring with her. Today is Titania first day on the job as a lowly love fairy. Sadly, due to her position as a princess, Titania has had no prior experience with love and how exactly it works besides textbook definitions. So...is Titania's first victim male or female.
