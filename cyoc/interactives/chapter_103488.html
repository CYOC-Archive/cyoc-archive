When she opened her eyes, she saw a mouse staring back at her.

Although her breasts were much smaller and covered by fur, they were still present on her chest. And she still had a slight hourglass shape, with a thin waist. She still had some dexterity in her hands, and she could hold objects. Likewise, she could still easily stand on the balls of her feet and walk like a human. And mercifully her shrinking had stopped at about 12 inches in height. In short, she looked liked some kind of anthropomorphic animal from a cartoon or something, like that little mouse from the Secret of NIMH or maybe closer to one of those mice from the Rescuers.

    "Oh, you cheated, " said Kelly above her. "Well, that's okay. I cheated a little, too. I guess that makes the game I have planned a little more fair."

Well, aside from her forked tongue and strange yellow eyes. But her lower body was monstrous. From her waist down, her body was covered in thick scales. She no longer had legs. They had merged into a long snakelike tail. Mary was immediately reminded of the snake women from legends .... the naga.

    "Kelly, what have you done to yourself?" asked Mary in shock. Her little mouse heart raced at the sight of Kelly's new body.

    "Do you like it? It took a little bit of planning to get it just right for our game," said Kelly.

    "Game? What game?" said Mary angrily.

    "Why, a game of hide and seek, silly. I made you into a mouse so you could hide, and I am going to find you. To do that I needed to be a little bit snake. Not too much, mind you. I didn't want to become feral or something. And I had to add a little bit of the 'human' and 'growth' vials so I wouldn't get too small or lose my hands. But enough talk - let's play," said Kelly.

    Mary shrank back. "I don't think I want to play this game."

    "Oh, relax," said Kelly. "I am not really going to hurt you. And we can change right back after I have finished having my fun."

    "No, " said Mary firmly. She tried to act brave, but her rapidly beating heart betrayed her fear.

    Kelly chuckled. "I don't think you have much choice. I am going to hunt you no matter what. So you probably better get going. Although, I must confess I think I might have a slight advantage. I can smell your fear, almost taste it" said Kelly as she flicked out her forked tongue.

    Hmm, better watch that. I need to learn to control my tail better, thought Kelly. Don't want it to get me into any trouble.
