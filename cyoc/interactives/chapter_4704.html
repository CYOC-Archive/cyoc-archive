Did someone harass you?” You shake your head even if Nancy can’t see it. “No, something worse…” Nancy’s voice sounds even more concerned now “Did someone… try to touch you, Joanne?” You let out a big sob. “No, something even worse…” You hear Nancy gasp at the other side of the line. “No! But I just have a little trouble to take it in. You mean a DOG reaped you? A dog as in woof woof?” You hear on Nancy’s voice that this sounds very bizarre to her ears. “Well… yes…”

    “Ok Joanne… so what happened exactly?”
