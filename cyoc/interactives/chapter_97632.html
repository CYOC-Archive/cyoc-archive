    When Ten Become Eleven

    added 7 years ago BM O

    It was amazing to think what ten teenage boys could make together. One by one, they had each become a new man/men. They now stood at about six feet tall, but they had grown wider with each merging. They now had two arms, two legs spread wide apart, and ten different heads in a row. Each of them now existed as a full-blood brother, each one conjoined to nine other brothers. Their clothes were merged into a different colored strip under their owners head, with a large 10 (to represent the people in the body) gleaming in the center of the wide fused shirt.

    "Hey, guys," Raph said to Donnie and Leo, "what if Mikey came over here?" These three were already brothers before this experience, but they were now closer than any brothers could ever hope to be. Mikey was another of their brothers. "It might be kinda cool." Donnie said. Leo agreed.

    "Well, as fate would have it..." Ty said, "...he just showed up here." The nearby window had blinds, but through it they could clearly see Mikey walking up towards the front door. Anticipating when Mikey would knock, Peter called out for him to come in. When Mikey did come in, he looked into the living room, right into the twenty eyes of the ten heads on the one wide body sitting on the even wider couch. He took particular notice of his three brothers.

    "I don't know what this is, but I want in!" Mikey cried with excitement. "I think having brothers this close would be freaking cool, and the same goes for friends!"

    "Then touch your side against Mason's." Dustin instructed. Mikey did this, and his right side disappeared into their left side. The long body grew wider, so that Mikey's head could sit on their shoulders and his left arm would be the body's new left arm. Their clothes also fused, incorporating another strip of color, so that it was now eleven different colors; their giant number shifted towards the new center, and changed from a 10 to a white 11.

    "OMG, this is awesome!" Mikey said to his three--and seven new--brothers.

    "Glad you like your new family." Raph said.

    "I'm glad our clothes are adjusting to this." Steven said, admiring the 11 on the shirt.

    "Who would believe that eleven different people would end up as one body?" Peter asked.

    "Anyone with no imagination, that's who." Mason answered.

    "Well, we sure appreciate this 'closeness'." Leo said.

    "I can't wait to see what will happen to us next!" Donnie cried.

    Jake, Allen, and Dustin agreed. As for Ty, he just wanted the guys to sit on the talk and enjoy their new body, which they did. They were as comfortable as any group of people could ever be. And they knew well that this was by no means over; any future brothers had yet to show up. They just hoped that they could make their "close circle of friends" even closer that it had ever been and ever would be again.
