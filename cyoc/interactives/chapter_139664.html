    Blending in

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Derpychicken 4 years ago BM

    Matt saw the ticket and decided to read what was on it. It said some normal things like the airport Matt was at and where he would arrive. Matt saw that he was apparently going to Rio de Janeiro in Brazil which interested him.

    Matt browsed the ticket further and he got really creeped out. The ticket had his name, a photo of him and his address. "How the fuck did this guy know my details. Was he trying to send me off to Brazil for some reason." Matt thought to himself.

    Matt got onto the flight with no issue and sat down in his designated seat. He looked out the window and watched as the plane passed by the fields in which the airport was located.

    Over the course of the flight, Matt felt very uneasy. He decides to pickup a brochure on the natives of Brazil. After reading it, he gets tired. So he decides to take a nap, but right before he closes his eyes, he feels tingling in his feet. He leans down to investigate, only to find that his sneakers are melting! The entire shoes just melt until they are a pair of cheap tennis shoes.

    He then notices his normally brown hair is now black! He stares at his hair curling and shortening until it was out of his sight. He then notices his skin is a tad darker, and it slowly tans right before his eyes! It continues tanning until its a dark brown, the exact same color as the natives in the brochure.

    Matt suddenly feels tingling on the soles of his feet, so he takes off his shoes to look. The soles of his feet are a peach color, but as he but his hand on it, he feels it thickening. Now he tries to dig his nails into his feet but feels nothing! Matt decides its all too weird and rushes into the bathroom.

    Matt looks into the mirror, only to have his vision blurred. After his vision returns, he notices he now has deep, brown eyes instead of his normal blue. He watches, mesmerized, as his nose broadens and his lips plump. His shoulders become wider and his eyes narrow. Matt's chest started to swell with muscle, but not too much, enough to be noticed, but not enough to actually see well. His eyebrows also get a lot thicker and bushier. In the mirror, he almost looks indian but with wider eyes.

    Matt reaches into his pocket to find his passport, and as he pulls it out its...an ID?
    It looks exactly like what he saw in the mirror. His eyes now wander down to the name, which now reads " Macalister Carvalho". He is the same age, but a little bit more muscular and heavier.

    Matt, now Macalister, walks out of the bathroom and takes a seat, picking up a Portuguese magazine. He reads through it effortlessly. Suddenly, the seatbelt sign turned on and you could hear the captain's voice. "We are landing in Rio de Janeiro, please buckle up." And then he repeats it in Portuguese. "Estamos pousando no Rio de Janeiro, por favor, apertem os cintos."

    Macalister sits in his seat, then eyes widening in surprise. He only understood the Portuguese part! He tries to speak English and say, "Good morning," but it comes out like, "Bom Dia." Macalister also notices his thoughts are all in Portuguese too! "O que diabos está acontecendo??" Macalister picks up an english magazine and looks at it. The characters on it look alien to him. He tries to pronounce some of the words but they come out like gibberish. Macalister then tries to think in English, but it was just like trying to think in a foreign language. He panics and then realizes that the plane has landed and he needs to get off.
