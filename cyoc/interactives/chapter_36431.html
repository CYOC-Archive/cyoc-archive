    'Dear Receiver of the pendant, greetings, I am Coyote, the Trickster, you may have heard of me. Due to the powerful influences of other forces both divine and wicked, I am no longer able to spread 'interesting times' among the mortal universe. A loop hole however allows me to grant certain gifts among humans. Thus, this pedant is my gift from me, to you. It will obey all commands as you intend them to be granted. It will not misinterpret any command given it! Due to your particular tastes, and to prevent you from conquering the world: I have given this pedant bearing my good looks with a particular set of spells. To change the age of anyone it is used on, along with changing their species while keeping them walking upright, and optionally changing their gender for kicks. and rewriting reality so it has been that way. I do hope you spread much 'interesting times' with these, as the divine and no so divine forces struggle to adjust their plans to keep pace. Please have fun, I know I will watching you. Coyote The Trickster.'


    Whoever is the owner of Coyote The Trickster's pendant may make certain alterations to reality within the guideline stated below.

    1) Any transformation of any (AR, AP, Animal, Statue) type may only be done on a person or place once. Inversely, you may also not perform a transformation on a person or place that cancels out a previous transformation. THINK BEFORE USING!

    2) If you do not use this pendant at least every seven days (the counter starting from the end of the last transformation) it'll vanish along with this letter and move onto a new owner.

    3) You may age regress any entity to a day after their conception or age progress them a day before their biological death. Unless you state otherwise, they'll receive memories, emotions, and a persona to match as if they had been that state naturally while losing their previous one.

    4) Any person whose gender is altered by this keep their attraction to either the opposite gender or the same gender. A straight man will become a straight woman and vice versa. Genderless entities will feel no physical attraction no their state. You may also not under any circumstances what so ever increase a person's sexual drive.

    5) You may alter any living being into a humanoid version of any species you desire, weather that species is mythical, robotic,fictional, copyrighted, extinct, plant, animal, plasmatic, etc. Only sentient non-humanoids may be selected as non-humanoid forms.

    6) Unless otherwise stated by the owner when used, the subject's identity along with their past will alter to fit their new existence.

    7) The owner shall remain aware of all changes but is otherwise is subject to all rules stated here. Even if the owner is altered by a new owner, she/he/it will remain aware of the changes they themselves made to reality.

    8) The pendant will never activate unintentionally and will always interpret the conscious designs of the owner. Commands may be made verbally or mentally.

    9) You may TF a subject as many times as desired as long as it does not negate a previous transformation.

    10) You may declare as many details of a person's new past or persona as you wish, conflicting elements shall cancel each other out. Cultural shock waves shall occur unless otherwise stated to support these details. THINK AHEAD!

    11) You may change a location into a new place, people and objects there will be automatically altered to fit this new location with the exception of yourself unless otherwise stated, BE CAREFUL!

    12) You may not contradict a previous owner's transformations.

    13) If you wish people or person into an inanimate object, the person's soul will remain as part of the object and the object will have self awareness, it's locomotion depends on the object choice. Giving an object locomotion does not count as negating a previous transformation.

    14) You remain the owner even if you are not touching the pendant or have it on your person. You remain the owner unless the seven days limit passes.

    15) You may willing give the pendant to a new owner at any time. If done in this way, you must conceded to any change done to you by the new owner for them to be effective.
