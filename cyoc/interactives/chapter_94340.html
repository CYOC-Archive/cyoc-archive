    Mark sat down in the dean's office. The Dean said, "You're a little late with your registration. Normally, I don't help with registration but I'm making an exception in your case. I hope you appreciate this."

    "Yes, sir." Mark said as he squirmed in his seat. He'd been trying to go to college but so far had received rejection notices. This was the only college that had accepted him and he was nervous. This college had a reputation. The colleges name was Dickering Mens University but people mocked the school by calling it Dickless U.

    It was a religious college although it didn't belong to any particular religion. Instead, the college prided itself on teaching objective morality that was not based on a single religious creed. The college taught evolution and taught that religion was an evolutionary adaptation: that by creating religions, ancient humans were able to impose morals that helped the community survive and therefore religious communities did better at survival of the fittest while more hedonistic communities did not do so well. With this as the founding principle of the college, Dickering University blended the moral traditions from several religions from Islam to Confucius and taught these morals as good for the survival of our species.

    Along with these moral teaching came a belief in good old fashioned corporal discipline. That is, the college believed in a liberal use of the paddle and other forms of indoctrination similar to what some militaries use to make good soldiers.

    As Mark sat there in his Sunday best, he fidgeted thinking about what he was agreeing to by going to this strict college but he was a good person and a strong person. He figured he could get through this college either by being good enough to not need discipline or strong enough to endure it.

    The dean continued, "As you know we expect the best behavior from all our students and the god or gods or whatever higher power there is has seen fit to provide us with a technology to ensure that.
