But as I stated before, as a supreme VIP, you have more control over mental awareness than most guests. This includes mental changes of your targets as they leave the resort, including yourself. Should you wish, you can even make it so your targets have no knowledge of the lives they are about to enter."

And oh boy, would he have some fun.
