Despite losing the eight years of age, Jennifer still managed to keep her bodily signs of being a mother to her twins.

She had just placed the order when she heard someone call her name out from across the restaurant. It took Jennifer a moment of looking at the boy and thinking she recognized him from somewhere before she remembered, and called out, "Trey? Trey Parker?"

    "One and the same," replied the man, which took Jennifer a quick moment to adjust looking to.

    "What are you doing here?" asked Jennifer, sliding over to make room for him to join her, and encouraging Tony and Emmy to make room for the boy accompanying him, which Jennifer now assumed was Trey's overly serious and stern father, James.

    "Well, my Dad heard from your parents that this was a great resort to relax and take your mind off of things. We got into the room and switched roles and little Jimmy over here suddenly was starving," said Trey.

    "It'd be a shame to separate them," added Trey.

    "Well, you know, you two can come to our room tonight... for a playdate," said Jennifer.

    "Sure, a play date. Jimmy can play at being big brother, that might be good for him. Make him a better Dad when he grows up again."

    "Kinda... but..."

    "But what?"

    "But but!" giggled Jimmy.

    "But what?" implored Trey.

    "But they're babies... stuck in diapers, and I'm a big boy."

    Trey deciding to test out if the Youthie was still in effect, said, "Yes, you're a big boy, but you still have accidents from time to time, don't you?"

    "Nuh huh!" protested Jimmy, but Trey could feel the boy starting to shrink a little in his lap, some more baby fat appearing on Jimmy's limbs and belly as he crossed the line back into toddlerhood.

    "What happened," asked Trey as he helped Jennifer up to her feet.

    "I was enjoying letting the twins breastfeed when the next thing I know they're newborns and suddenly I'm pregnant with them," said Jennifer in a rushed tone.

    "Can you make them younger so you can get around?" asked Trey, and Jennifer thought for a moment and her belly shrank like an ice cube on a hot sunlit patio.
