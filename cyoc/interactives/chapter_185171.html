Thinking it was probably Jake, she was ready to give him a good smack upside his snout. "Jake... If you wanted to wake me up..." She says while snarling. "You could probably do without- arf?" The snarling converted into a confused bark as Jake was no longer on the sofa with her. She looked back to see nobody behind her, but felt her tail like it was being yanked. "I don't like this... Did I pull a muscle or- *Whine*!" She made a whiny noise as she closed her eyes to the pressure her tail was receiving as it was starting to grow longer.

Not only that, but the overall thickness from inside his tail also seemed to be growing. The whole feeling had Jake panting as his tongue lay flat on the seat of his chair. "Heh... Was this the oversensitive reaction it meant? *ARF*" Jake felt a surge as his tail lengthened and thickened massively.

After the initial shock from the growth spurt in her tail, she couldn't hold back the emotions within and it started to wag. "Hah... It's... So big... Beautiful..." Naomi was overwhelmed by the feeling of the fur in her tail thickening all around it. It was so long that the black fur was tickling her back. She couldn't take it. She needed to hold it. She reached behind herself and pulled her giant tail over her left side to in curl and cuddle.

    With Jake now on the floor pulling his tail from between his legs, he stroked the soft, smooth fur that gave him shivers through his spine. Jake stuffed his face in the fur making all sorts of yips and barks of pleasure. Getting aroused, he lets it go as it swings back through his legs and curls up behind him as it used to be.

The two howled in unison as their tails finished growing. The two while exhausted, snapped out of their trances as Naomi peeked over the couch to look at Jake. "Jake, why... Why were we howling?" She asked panting hard. Jake himself was exhausted, but he seen that Naomi didn't take noticed that his tail was much bigger than it used to be. Uh... I don't... Know." Jake lied. He knew exactly why they did. But it seemed that the reality alteration worked, except Naomi felt everything how it was supposed to. She just didn't acknowledge the fact.

Jake began to stand up from his desk chair wagging his one giant tail that towered a few inches over his head. "Who needs blankets anyway?" Jake hopped over the couch to stroke Naomi's big bushy tail. Naomi did the same with Jake's as they feel asleep happily through the night, holding each others tails.
