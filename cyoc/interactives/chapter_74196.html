    Clara then made it to the meeting, and sat down in one of the chairs. Her friend Maria, also 3, was next to her. She had brown skin and eyes,
    and black hair. "Sowwy," Clara told her "but me had ta change my diapee." "It otay, Cwawa." she told her "It happens ta da best of us."

    The meeting started, and Beth, only 3, made her presentation to the group. It was a piechart, but it looked like a literal pie, and since she knew
    it might make everyone hungry, she brought enough apple pie to share with everyone. So they all ate a slice while they watched her. Clara,
    being the only one who remembered the old reality, had a hard time keeping a straight face listening to Beth explain, but she managed.

    When it was over, Clara was packing up to go home, when Maria came up to her.

    "Hey Cwawa. You busy tonite?" she asked.

    "Nu-uh." Clara replied "Why?"

    "Da giwws an me awe gonna go out ta din-din tonite." she explianed "Tewwi needs ouw hewp pwanning hew wedding with Bobby. You wanna
    come wid us?"

    "Suwe." Clara replied. She was glad to hear her friend Terri was getting married.

    "Dat's good." Maria said "Now dis isn't fancy, so jus weaw someting casuaw, otay?"

    "Otay." Clara replied.

    "Good." Maria said "See you dere!"

    Clara went there wearing a pink tee with a sunflower on it, a fresh diaper, pink socks, and pink shoes.

    Maria and the other girls were dressed similarly - it was typical for girls their age to dress like this when they were off work. Some of them even
