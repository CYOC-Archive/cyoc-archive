    A magical and mysterious mirror

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago AR

    For many years, a mysterious mirror has been known to exist that has the power to rejuvenate people. It can regress anyone, from a 90-year-old or a young 20-year-old. But for that to happen, the person has to look in the mirror for a long time and then the reflection in the mirror will start to distort and later that person will have to enter the mirror as if it were some kind of portal. And finally that person will come out of the mirror with his new age. And he can do it again as many times as he wants in order to become younger.

    Although there are some conditions:

    1. The mirror can randomly take 1-8 years from you for every time you enter the mirror. For example, if a 50-year-old person enters the mirror for the first time, the mirror will regress him at either the age of 42, 43, 44, 45, 46, 47, 48, or 49. And so on for each time he re-enters the mirror.

    2. Although you can enter the mirror as many times as you want, there is a limit. If by chance you regress at the age of 1, the mirror will no longer allow you to regress your age even further anymore.

    3. There are also some rumors that after entering the mirror, your reality may change. For example, if a 20-year-old returns to the age of 12, his reality might change a bit. Whether the rest of his family also become young, whether the place where he lives changes according to his new age, whether his younger brother is now the older brother, whether the clothes he is wearing at that moment also changes, etc.

    4. The changes are irreversible. You will no longer be able to return to your original age after being regressed by the mirror.

    After a long time, nothing has been heard from that strange mirror again. Or so it was believed when that mirror ended up in the hands of a strange guy who is dedicated to selling strange items and ancient relics to other people.
    And one day, someone was able to buy it without knowing that it was a magic mirror.

    But who would be the first to be transformed by that peculiar mirror?
