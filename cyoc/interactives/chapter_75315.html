    Ares should have known this would end badly. Up until then, he had
    been having a good day. He was told by Zeus that he could use his
    powers to transform the competitors of an event. Even better, that
    event was fencing. Ares was deciding between turning the loser into a
    set of weapons, or just turning both competitors into gorillas when
    his sister burst in.

    "Oh Ares! I've been looking all over for you. Did you hear Zeus's
    idea? It sounds marvelous don't you think?" She said.

    "Yes Aphrodite, it's great. Do you need something?" Ares asked.

    "Nothing much. I was just wondering if we could make a deal."
    Aphrodite said.

    "What kind of deal?" Ares asked.

    "Do you remember last week when I said I was looking forward to
    Women's Volleyball and you told me that women's sports weren't as
    entertaining as men's sports?" Aphrodite asked.

    "Sister, I don't know what you're planning, but you know that I meant
    entertainment value. They're both completely legitimate-" Ares began,
    trying to defend himself.

    "Yes yes, but I had an idea. Why not prove you're right. We switch our
    athletes' minds, and if your men win a medal in Volleyball, you win
    the bet." Aphrodite suggested.

    "And?" Ares asked, knowing Aphrodite wouldn't settle for simply
    "winning."

    "Whoever loses has to spend two weeks in the mortal world as the
    opposite sex, in a profession of the winner's choosing." Aphrodite
    said.

    "Deal. I hope you like shaving." Ares replied.

    "And I hope you like makeup." Aphrodite said as the two shook hands.

    That's when he noticed that he was substantially different. For one,
    he was nude, despite going to sleep in his boxers last night. Second,
    his room had a volleyball sitting on the dresser. Third, and most
    importantly, he was female! How did that happen. Andrew began grabbing
    his breasts and prodding at his vagina, both of which felt very good.
    He didn't have time for that now though, he needed to figure out what
    was going on. Andrew grabbed the wallet that was on the nightstand.
    According to the I.D. inside, he was now in the body of Fleur
    Xhenemont, a volleyball player from France. She was scheduled to play
    in two hours. Andrew had two hours to adapt to a female body, get to
    the Olympics, and remember how to play volleyball. He was in for a

    there was a sword on the bench next to a wallet. Yes, that would have
