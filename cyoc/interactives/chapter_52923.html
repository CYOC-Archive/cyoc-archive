    A few minutes after Gene and Mel, formerly Melissa, leave Lucy comes running up to you, proudly showing off her new teddy bear.

    "Look what I made!" she giggles delightedly, showing you a white teddy bear in a yellow tutu, "Her name is Dawn."

    You smile, "Real cute. I guess the party's over then?"

    Your little sister nods, "Yup! Now you have to buy me a pack of Pokemon cards!"

    "Alright, I guess you were well behaved enough," Lucy's interest in Pokemon seems to be more or less the only thing left from when she was Luke your little brother. Though changing her back would be easy enough, you have decided that you're going to keep her a little girl since she's so much better behaved. That and you find the idea of having an all female family interesting enough to keep it that way for a while long.

    On the way to the gaming store to get the cards, you think about what's happened since you bought the magical flash light. Both your parents are now women, and as far as you know no one finds that strange. You're a girl and your girlfriend, who was formerly straight and still thinks of herself as being mostly heterosexual is still your girlfriend. Then there's what you did to Gene and Melissa. What has to be the most amazing thing to you though, is how normal it feels for you to be a girl. When you first decided to change yourself you had been sure that it would feel strange and that as soon as you experimented with your girlfriend you would change yourself back. The fact that you've spent nearly a week as a girl while waiting for the chance to actually try what it's like to have lesbian sex is astounding when you give it some thought.

    Unless you're actively thinking about being a girl you forget that you're any different from how you've been. All of your memories are intact and you have no additional memories of life as a girl, you even still think of yourself as being a guy.

    Arriving at the store you buy two packs of Pokemon cards, one you give to Lucy immediately, the other you hold onto, telling her that if she behaves until you get back home you'll give her the second pack.

    Bribery works wonders, and the bus ride home is a breeze. Lucy sits quietly next to you, pretending to show her teddy bear the cards she has, and you look around at the other people on the bus. Just because you don't want to use the light on yourself or anyone in your family at the moment doesn't mean that you're not open to seeing what else it can do.

    Across the aisle from you there is a guy and his girlfriend, if the way they're making out is anything to judge their relationship by.

    You already know that changing the gender of one half of a relationship does nothing and that using the flashlight on its lowest setting for either gender renders someone more or less gender neutral and without any interest in sex. This makes you wonder what would happen to a couple if you were to use the lowest setting of the light on one or both of them.

    Setting the light to the lowest blue possible you shine it on the guy. Immediately he loses both height and muscle, his hair growing slightly longer as well. Interestingly enough he continues to make out with his girlfriend making you wish you could learn more about their relationship.

    Maybe after you see what it's like to have sex with your girlfriend as a girl you'll see what happens between the two of you when you use the light on its lowest setting on one of you. Odds are you'll end up using it on her, because though you've made yourself a girl, the idea of using the light on yourself creeps you out. Even the thought of changing yourself back is kind of creepy since you feel so natural the way you are. It's not that you don't want to go back to being a guy, because when you think about it, you do, but that you feel so normal the way you are that you don't really think of yourself as being changed.

    The more thought you give the matter, the more you can't wait to finally get the chance to experiment with your girlfriend. Experiencing sex as a woman is sure to be a different experience, and you can't help but wonder what it will feel like. You've heard that women are capable of having multiple orgasms and you wonder if you'll be able to experience something like that with your girlfriend. If sex as a woman feels really good, you think that you might even stay female for a bit longer afterwards. you don't plan on staying female forever, but you see no reason to be in a hurry to change back.

    By the time you get home your parents are already there and Lucy rushes to show them her new toy, "Mom, mommy! Look what I made."

    What do you do now?
