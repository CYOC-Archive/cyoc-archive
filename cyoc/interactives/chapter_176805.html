    What is this "gender dysphoria" nonsense?

    A couple days latter, Lieutenant Marie O'Conner had guided Sophie to a new secure location. (Which mean you are there too, as a prisoner inside a body you wrongfully believe is yours.) To your surprise, you didn't end in a lab but in a projection room. "Marie, what are we doing here?" ask Sophie, echoing your sentiments.

    "You expressed the desire to meet with whoever tried to continue ‘project Hecate’ while trapped behind enemy lines. The thing is that person is a bit… Odd. While hight commend decided to let you met her, they also decided that you needed to know her… shall we say history?" You can't help but notice how the Lieutenant emphasized the word ‘her’ to speak of Sophie's successor.

    Once Sophie sat down in one of the chairs, O'Conner switch off the light and start the video. The first recording showed a heavily bruised boy claiming that he was ‘born in the wrong body’, was ‘a girl on the inside’ and swearing that he would find a way to give himself his ‘rightful sex’ or die trying. "I don't know why their machine wasn't able to reprogram me the way it does all other girls, but if I find the reason, I'll work on making other girls immune too. …Unless it's because I was born in this disgusting body."

    After this Marie start other videos with the same boy. On some he was in better shape, on others he had been beaten again. Rapidly the boy grew into a thin man, but even beaten an inch from death, he never renounced. One of the thing he said struck you as odd, he claimed that the reason other men refused to kill him was because he was one of the last who was born as a man, most of the Mars army being former girls. "Mars High Command think nobody know that their alleged ‘men production farm’, is just propaganda. The truth is, while with a bit of science women can easily reproduce without help from men, the opposite isn't true. A woman's body contain a natural incubation chamber called an Uterus, which is far too complex to be made artificially. The alternative would be to use female members of another mammalian species, but all compatible animals were exterminated long ago."

    More videos, and the man aged forever more, to the point he was barely able to stand. "Finally! It needed me to work all my life on it, but I won't have to live my last days in this disgusting body!" Then the old man moved, revealing he wasn't speaking to a camera, but to a mirror. All this was extracted directly from the man's memories! You saw, from his own point of view, the man go inside a giant vat full of liquid and, from there, activate the machine connected to it. Pain make him scream in his breathing mask before blacking out. But the video doesn't end there. Slowly the old man wake up and look at his body, except his not a he anymore, or old for that matter. Instead, she now have the lithe but well muscled body of a young woman, one practicing some martial arts not a bodybuilder, with rather small breasts. Then the former man looked outside of her vat and saw an entire squadron from the Venus army pointing their TF rifle at her.

    "At first we believed her to be a spy training for an infiltration mission, which our inability in implementing one of our fake female personality suggested to be true. But then our test revealed she was also immune to masculinizating ray guns, both mentally and physically. Curious, our scientists decided to alter one of our mental conditioning helmet to instead copy memories, which is how we got those videos, among other things. So, what do you think, Dr. Sophie Charlotte Marshall?"

    You try to express how much you think this is bullshit that someone born a man could want to be a woman, how you know its just a ploy to convince you that you're just an artificial personality implanted into one of their scientist. Alas for you, ‘Dr. Marshall’ doesn't let you and make your mouth say something else instead. "Before the men captured me, I had read a book from long before the gender-schism that spoke of that poor girls condition, they called it ‘gender-dysphoria’. I thought it was eradicated a long time ago, but the men got a girl born in a boy's body, we may have boys born in girls body among our kids…"

    "Sophie, are you saying she's really a genuine girl?"

    "Yes, that's what I'm saying. If nobody believed her, why did did high command trust her, and how long did it take?"

    Lieutenant O'Conner sighed. "They don't. We just have our other scientists who figured they could use her work to help continue yours."
