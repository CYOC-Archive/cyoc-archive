    The weredragon after a full moon

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 11 years ago A BM S TG O

    Now the first night of the full moon brings out a weredragons huge body,the
    2nd night brings out the fire thats hotter than hades and the third night brings out the evil
    urges a weredragon needs to use that huge body and fire to stomp,smash and burn.

    So it was that the locals hid for 3 nights of the full moon as the mysterious new weredragon joined its cursed
    kinfolk in mayhem and fun.

    Then the full moon passed and the weredragon hordes dissappeared as swiftly as they appeared.

    The locals got out to clean the rubble and Tiama's dissappearance and ruined house was blamed on the
    new weredragons attack.

    Fafniina suspected differently,but kept her opinion to herself.For one thing,a fairy
    dragon has a footprint 1 foot across and the weredragon had left a trail of clawed twisting
    footprints nine feet across.

    Fafniina could not imagine a fair dragon becoming so huge under the full moon,no
    matter how much cursed Tiama may have been.

    Then came that night about two weeks later when Fafnirina heard the heavy footfalls
    outside her upper story window.The next mornong she peered down at the ground where a
    trail of 3 foot long footprints wandered about outside her window.

    And pasted on the wall with some kind of dragon slime was a note done in huge handwriting was
    a noted from a anthro dragoness claiming to be Tiama,and asking her friend to visit her
    at a secluded location that night.

    So Fafnirina went to the secret rendevous point,which was under a bridge over a stream.

    On the other side of the stream crouched a giant of a green scaled anthro dragoness,whose slitted
    eyes betrayed just a flicker of red within.

    The weredragon in her normal none full moon form stood up,and Fafnirina gasped as
    a anthro 15 feet tall stood there,and though there were traces of Tiama in that face,the monsters
    longer snout,pointier teeth and longer tail and other changes said this was a weredragon,not
    a fairy dragon or even a normal anthro mortal dragon for that matter.

    Tiama the weredragon was learning fast.She didn't remember much about being a male on the first two
    nights thanks to the Wish Note,but on that third night another weredragon had bitten the monster Tiama had turned into,and the Wish Note became irrelevant as the WereDragon curse infected his,and then her blood.

    When the full moon finally went below the horizon Tiama had found herself dealing with a entirely
    different sense of what a normal body now was.She was a giantess by the standards of her new anthro dragon mortal
    species,the better to defend herself from her enemies during the lull in the full moon.

    Her weredragon intuition told her that the fairy dragon was her enemy,yet she found herself unable
    to cross the running water to attack Fafnirina.The weredragoness was a mortal creature but bound by supernatural monster rules.

    So Tiama just stood there,wavering on her feet ,uncertain what to do next...
    on the other side of the stream the fairy dragon too did not know whether to call a
    greeting or run away.

    Then a seagull dived to catch a fish in the stream,and the spray of water struck the
    weredragon.

    The reaction was instantaneous.The water burnt and sizzled on Tiama's dark green
    scales,her eyes snapped wider and blazed red as she uttered
    not a scream of pain

    but the angry roar of the sleeping monster within her.

    Fafnirina looked in horror at Tiama's wide open jaws,with their lines of fairy dragon
    rending teeth.Just as quickly,hidden foot long talons hidden in her forefingers
    sprang out and with a swipe,the weredragon slashed out and sliced the seagull and the wiggling fish
    in its beak to ribbons,that sprayed down into the stream.

    It dawned on Fafnirina that weredragons might be able to transform into their monstrous
    selves evn in the absence of the full moon,and she turned and sprang into the air,flying away as
    Tiama's deep growly voice ordered her to come back...
