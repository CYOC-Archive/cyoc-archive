    There was no time to discuss what to do, Bong-cha used her sword to push Setepenre back and away from the opened apartment door. The older woman mindlessly entered the room and the door was promptly shut.
    The vampire wrestled with her friend, it was difficult to do so without touching her. Bong-cha jumped back to keep from her friend’s reach.

    “Help me pin her down!” She shouted to the werewolf.

    “I think we have bigger problems!” Luca yelled back.

    Bong-cha saw what he was talking about: all of the doors on the floor had opened and out walked bewitched people. They all started shuffling towards Bong-cha and Luca, even Setepenre joined the crowd as they got closer and closer. There was no room to maneuver so the two had no choice but to retreat back to the stairwell. Slamming the door behind them Luca was using his strength to keep the doors shut from the mindless horde that now pounded on the doors.

    “What the fuck are we going to do? Setepenre is cursed or something like the rest of the residents of this floor!” The black haired man asked.

    “I could call command? I doubt they could do anything but—“ she was interrupted by a series of three rising tones.

    Once the tune played out everyone stopped banging on the doors and shambled towards the apartments. The Korean girl tried to keep track of Setepenre but lost sight of her in the zombie-like crowd.

    “Shit I lost her, we’ve got to find out which door she went through.” She said.

    Luca and Bong-cha re entered floor 5 to a familiar emptiness. They tried shouting for their friend so they started checking doors when they got no response. Every door was locked and there was no way command would be able to use their sensors to locate her.
    Before they could decide what to do next the ground began to shake and a large figure emerged from the elevator.

    —————————

    Setepenre awoke to another lovely, sun filled day. She made sure her straightened hair was in its perfect ponytail with bumper bangs and her makeup was perfect. She adjusted her blouse and cardigan with matching petticoated skirt, her Mary janes and Bobby socks completed her ensemble. As she walked to the breakfast table where a mountain of pancakes were sitting she was greeted to the sight of her mother who was happily working in the stove wearing a polkadot dress with a tall beehive haircut.

    “Good morning mommy dearest! Gee willikers, it’s such a swell day!” Setepenre said with a big goofy smile.

    “Good morning my darling daughter!” The woman said with a matching wife smile like the girl’s.

    —————————

    Luca kept trying the lock of one of the doors as the large figure got closer, that’s when they saw it; a massive creature that looked to be made of multiple bodies melded together in a terrifying mass of agony. Just before it could reach them the door opened and the two rushed in, slamming it shut on their way in.

    “Fuck that was close!” Luca said with his back against the door.

    “Now we know why the doors were locked.” The girl said relieved.

    “But all the people on this floor are under some kind of spell, why would that thing be guarding the halls?” The man asked.

    Before she could respond a man and two little girls, all in their sleepwear, turned to face them.

    “Shit, almost forgot about these things.” The werewolf said.

    Bong-cha used her foot to kick back the man who stumbled over taking the two little girls with him to the ground.
    Using her sheathed sword she tried to knock out the three people but they were seemingly incapable of being knocked out.

    “Shit, I don’t think you can knock out someone who’s already knocked out!” She exclaimed and continued to keep them back with her sword.

    Bong-cha looked to Luca and then to her attackers, she had to think of something fast.
