In contrast to the slim, dorky Wyatt, Alex was growing into puberty with the sort of natural touch that left Wyatt feeling quite inadequate. Alex was also constantly showing off and making sure Wyatt knew in detail the dissonance between the two. When their parents were out, Wyatt took the opportunity to play video games, and Alex...Alex invited his girlfriend Lilah over, who was three years Alex's senior. Suffice it to say, Wyatt got no gaming in due to the racket going on.

    Right now, Alex was out at football camp, and just looking at the picture told Wyatt yet again how different they were. Alex had golden blond hair, bright blue eyes, and his countenance, while similarly pale, was slowly starting to develop a tan. His face was boyish and still entering puberty, but his body was toned and muscular, especially for his age group. Alex absolutely loved working out, and went as far as saying he hated the necessary rest days.

    The boy even maintained an instagram called "Alex_Hoffman_Fitness", where he posted pictures of himself working out and even going as far as giving fitness tips to other young would be gym goers.

    Looking at himself, and then looking at the picture of Alex, Wyatt decided he was going to go for it. He put the picture of Alex into the magical locket and shut it, immediately feeling a tingly sensation come over him. He started to move toward the couch to take a seat, as he was starting to feel distinctly dizzy. He didn't see how his face was changing, going from one phase of puberty to another. Or how his light brown hair was quickly changing, becoming golden blond.

    Wyatt groaned as he felt a sudden heat spreading through his body. His height slightly decreased to be more in line with Alex's height, as the thirteen year old was indeed two years younger. He felt his clothes getting tighter in some places as a well toned set of pecs grew in, his stomach gurgling and pulsing under his shirt. Wyatt pulled his shirt up just in time to watch his stomach quiver and then suddenly pulse inward, fat vanishing and his abs pushing out into a light but strong six pack.

    He raised his arms in time to watch as his biceps and triceps toned up and became more visible, a single flex showing him a well honed arm. Not exactly riven with mass, he was pretty young now, but Wyatt loved the tone he was seeing.

He felt a funny tingling feeling in his groin, but he put that out of sight and out of mind.

He slowly walked into the bathroom and looked a long time in the mirror, seeing a sweaty and shocked looking Alex standing there where he should be. Feeling his smooth, hairless face and flexing his arm again, Wyatt chuckled, "That...that did it! I'm Alex!"

    Now what to do with all this newfound energy and athleticism...?

    "I could go and try and hit the gym, but I don't really know how to do that yet..." Wyatt contemplated, looking over his options as he crossed his arms in front of his chest. "I could try and get with a girl from high school. That could be something." He then thought about something else, "Or I could just go out and hang out in town, see where it takes me."
