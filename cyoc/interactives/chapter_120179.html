    Not a Knight, but Destined for Something Else

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Dragonman 8 years ago A BM O

    "Sorry, but I'm not a knight at all." You said

    "Oh....well in that case..." the boy began and then there was a large crashing sound from behind you that shook the ground. The boy looked up at something behind you and his eyes were filled with pure terror. In response you slowly turned around to see what was behind you, and you saw a large 20 foot red scaled dragon standing almost directly behind you.

    You wanted to run, but were paralyzed with fear and you turned around to see the boy, but he was gone. The dragon then sniffed you and it's head darted back for a moment, and it looked almost shocked. The dragon then sniffed you again and then immediately grabbed with its front claw and took flight 50 feet into the sky whist clutching onto you.

    You wanted to struggle and see if you could get free, but deep down inside you knew that you would only fall to your death. As the dragon flew it looked down at you a few times to make sure that you were still in its grasp, and eventually the two of you ended up in a cave on the side of a cliff. Once inside the dragon landed and dropped you on the far wall of the cave and walked towards you. You stood there staring at the dragon as it kept inspecting you by various means thinking that you were being tested as it's food.

    The dragon then astonishingly layed down in a nest adjacent to you and layed its head down on the size of it. You immediately saw this as a chance to escape, but right as you bolted for the mouth of the cave a deep voice from behind you said,"I wouldn't do that if I were you."

    You turned around to see the dragon's head lifted and looking at you, and you saw it speak again and say,"You wouldn't be the first who've tried to escape that way. Most try to climb down, but all there is down there is a stream of lava. So all those who've tried have perished, and you of all things should know that I'm not keeping you as a prisoner."

    "What? You can speak?" You asked shocked

    "I understand how to speak human if that's what you were asking. You don't know what you're doing here don't you? Or why I need you alive, and your destiny?" The dragon asked

    "My....destiny? What are you talking about? You just kidnapped me after destroying a village! Why should I trust you at all?" You asked

    "You really are clueless then. *sigh* That 'village' as you call it was created for the sole purpose of hunting dragons, and it was a fortress for training humans. I destroyed it because I wish to live rather than die, and I spared the younger humans. As long as they know how to fend for themselves for the next two to three days they will probably have a group of soldiers take them somewhere else. I'm not a monster, and frankly humans are the true terrors for my species. Dragons are dwindling in numbers, but they thrive using theirs and are strengthened by it, so many eggs are lost to humans." The dragon said

    "Wait what? Are you seriously trying to blame me for everything that's happened?! I'm not even from this world, or at least I don't think I am. I know that the tradition of knights ended centuries ago from where I come from." You said

    "Indeed, I know that you are foreign to most things here in this land, and no, I do not blame you for what's happening. But you need to help end the suffering, and that's why I brought you here actually. Your apparel and scent are different from those here, and I know that you are the one for because it's your destiny." The dragon said

    "What do you mean by that? Seriously what do you want from me?" You asked

    "I'll explain everything to you, but before that we must perform a ritual. A ritual to make you draconic, but human. A ritual to turn you into a knight for dragons." The dragon said

    "A ritual? A knight for dragons? What do you mean by that?" You asked

    "Not much, most of the ritual records are lost to time, and humans except for how it's performed and who to perform it on." The dragon said

    "Well, how do I perform it?" You asked

    "You don't, myself and two other dragons will. Your changes will take place while you are asleep, so I'm going to use a spell to do that. So sleep now, and when you awaken you will be one of us in addition to being one of them." The dragon said and threw a bunch of dust from the cave at you. You suddenly began feeling drowsy and passed out onto the cave floor.

    As you slept you could see something, an outline of something similar to a dragon but anthropomorphic. The dragon outline looked at you then held out its hand for you to take, and you didn't know what was happening. You paused for a moment then took the dragon's hand hoping that you made the right choice.

    You then woke up breathing heavily for some reason, and you noticed a number of things were different now. For one, you were in a nest surrounded by little dragons about your size staring at you, and then you were naked and covered in scales. You looked at your hands and were shocked seeing as they had changed and looked like 4 digit claws with thumbs. Your feet were now 4 digit digitigrade claws, and you had large wings and a long lizard-like tail. You then looked in between your legs and realized that....

    What do you do now?
