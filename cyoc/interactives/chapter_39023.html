    You eat a blue one

    Blue has always been your favorite color, and you love blueberries. You see the blue ones and the different lables. You don't know what the letters stand for on each container, and you would ask but you don't see anyone to ask. So you just randomly grab one from the bin marked HaL.

    You look at the letters and laugh. Sounds like the name of a maniac robot or something. You pop it into your mouth. It does taste like blueberry - it's the best jellybean you've ever had!

    You feel a tickling on your neck, and scratch it, feeling something there. Absently, brushing whatever it is aside... then you notice something starting to hang down into your face. It looks like hair. "Hey," you say, annoyed, figuring one of your friends is messing with you holding something over your head. You brush it away, turning to see who's messing with you, but no one is there. And when you turn your head, you feel the stuff hanging softly about your head shift, flowing with your spin. You start to realize as it hangs longer and longer and you feel it on your neck and shoulders and face, that it's the same color as your hair - auburn. You reach for it, to pull whatever it is off your head, and are very surprised when you tug and feel a tugging on your scalp. It's your hair!

    It's past your shoulders now - and you hold the now long locks - YOUR locks of auburn hair - away from your face, looking at it in surprise and trying to understand what is happening. You see the reflection of a long haired dude in the glass of a framed picture of jelly beans... it's YOU!

    "Woe, this is sick!" You always liked long hair but your parents never let you grow yours out. Now you have wavy auburn locks hanging down to your waist. It feels so soft and fluffy like a girl's, but you're no chick. You look just like some of the skaters and surfers you've seen around.

    "Where'd you get the wig?"

    You turn and look at your friends. "Dudes, you're not going to believe this - it's no wig! Look." You tug at the locks. They don't believe you and come over, looking closer. "See, its my hair - you can look at the individual strands - they go right into my skin!"

    "Woe, no way."

    "Yes way!" You pick up a jelly bean from another container. This one is Purple, you missed it on your first inspection because it and a few others are over from the rest of the jelly beans. "I just ate one of these, and now look at me!" You shake your head, the hair flying around you.

    You offer the purple one to one of your friends. "Try it, see what it does - I think they all do soemthing different."

    What do you do now?
