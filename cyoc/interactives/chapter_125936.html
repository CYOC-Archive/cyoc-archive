    After Katie had her talk with Samantha, she finally chose an outfit and now Aunt Lisa was finally ready to check out. Lisa spent quite a lot of money on the four girls, but she was quite glad she got to help out her niece's friends. Lisa was especially pleased that Samantha happened to be a fan of her and began talking with her after they left the store.

    "All that shopping made me hungry, let's go get a bite." Mika said to no one in particular.

    "I know, I'm starving." Claudia said as she and Mika walked ahead of everyone else.

    Soon enough, the group came upon Kellie and her parents, Lee and Maggie. Katie was a little bit hesitant to talk to her worrying she wouldn't want to talk just yet. Slowly, Katie approached Kellie and couldn't think of what to say to her.

    "So...." That was the only word to come out of Katie's mouth as she couldn't think of anything else to say.

    Kellie rolled her eyes and embraced her girlfriend and kissed Katie on the lips.

    "Thanks for giving me some time with my parents, I really needed it." Kellie said breaking off the kiss.

    "Of course." Katie smiled.

    "Come on girls, you want something to eat? Maggie and I will buy." Lee offered the girls.

    "Sounds great." Mary Elise said as the others followed them to get a bite to eat.

    "So listen, can we use the ring just once more while we're here?" Katie asked Kellie.

    "Katie..." Kellie sighed shaking her head.

    "Look, I promise you: we won't use it on the girls, my aunt, your parents, or us. Just someone else, someone we don't know." Katie practically begged looking at her girlfriend with puppy eyes.

    "Oh, fine. But this is the very last time. Remember, just once and on no one we know." Kellie reminded her.

    "Deal." Katie said as she began to look around for someone to use the ring on one last time at the beach.
