    Two Wins and Love Assured

    Sitting on the bleachers in shorts and a t-shirt covering her new swimsuit Jessica Thompson cheered on Kate and the school at the sand volleyball practice. Being the only girl on the stands didn’t faze Jess she kept most of her attention on her girlfriend as all the boyfriends in the stands kept getting distracted between their own skimpily-dressed girlfriends and this attractive yelling girl around them in the bleachers. Soon Jessica had to leave for her own practice and being able to witness her love spike the ball hard to the ground on the opposite side of the net for Kate’s practice team and the win sent Jess into a state of ecstatic cheering. Giving a thumbs-up to Jessica in the stands Kate was returned with a copied thumbs-up as she also made out that from Jessica’s mouthing that she needed to head off to swim practice. Jessica blew Kate a kiss and then hoped down the bleachers to head for the gymnasium and the indoor pool. Blushing because of the kiss blown her way Kate felt a reinvigorated spirit churn within her. Kate knew she looked great in this outfit, and she knew that she was an important part of the team, and Kate knew above all else that Jessica loved her and so did she.

    The foretold event happened Jessica was swimming in quick strokes well ahead of her competition and she made it to the end of the lane to thunderous applause. Looking up to the timer board through her goggles Jessica met the screaming with her own and raised her left arm to the ceiling seeing that she had beaten the school record by several seconds. Looking about as people in the vicinity rushed to her to heap congratulations Jessica climbed out of the pool to receive them. Still the real reason she was found to be so unsure and fidgety in this moment of triumph was because she continued to look around for that special someone that she would have hoped made it to the event. Then there in the bleachers making her way over the last seat was the love of her life Kate still in her bikini but now wearing a pair of shorts over her bottom followed by flip-flops on her feet. As soon as she cleared the hurdle of the seat Kate made a dainty flip-flop dash towards Jessica as quickly as she could. Jessica continued to receive and hug her teammates and coaches but one eye never left this beautiful girl moving towards her at graceful speed.

    Butterflies erupted in Jessica’s stomach as Kate neared and both girls raised there arms to receive each other. Kate slammed into Jess at full speed as both girls yelled in congratulatory unison. Both girls embraced their hug for only several seconds before breaking it to go for the real reason they were there at that moment immediately both leaning in for the coveted kiss. All of Jessica’s teammates and coaches smiled at the sight as everyone knew of her relationship and their was no judgment there. Breaking the kiss Kate yelled “You were so fast, I knew you could do it!” Answering quickly but no less enthusiastically “I couldn’t have done it without you with me!” Kate heard this reply and her eyes sparkled even brighter then before “we really do belong together” she thought as both hugging girls again went back to kissing each other in both triumph and love.
