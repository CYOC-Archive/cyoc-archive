    She'd actually started off as a mere mortal woman ,born
    in 1923 as Laura Philadelphia Kent somewhere in Northern England.As a teen girl she'd shown a talent for numbers and
    codebreaking.

    Hence during WW2 she'd soon been snatched up by MI-9 and found herself in Bletchley Park working the Ultra and Bombe
    codebreaking machines.

    Which meant she first heard about the remains of the radioactive dragon not through the locals in England but from a
    code she'd broken that had been passed from Berlin
    to the Wilmhelmshaven Submarine pens.
    She wisely had kept the original German message and its
    deciphered English translation to herself,realizing that
    even though this war was producing strange new things
    on both sides
    presenting her boss Mr Beasely with a message about
    the location of a mythical radioactive beasty was probably
    going to can her career at Bletchly Park pretty quickly.
    So Laura P. Kent had stuck to the more mundane
    top secrets
    such as H2O2 powered submarines ,jetfighters and the like.
    However she kept her eye out for any more German codes mentioning radioactive mythical creatures,though at the time
    Laura the Codebreaker had been pretty clueless about why
    the Germans had taken a interest in radioactivity.
    There was one final message from the Germans on the matter,
    from the Kapitain of a German U Boat who had stated that
    although he had sighted the "Grey Spindlestane" along the
    English coast the minefield the enemy had laid down in the waters approaching it prevented him from investigating further.

    Then came the Americans dropping of the two A bombs on Japan and Laura finally got her answer to why the Germans had
    been interested in a dead radioactive dragon under the
    Grey Spindlestane.
    However with the end of the war and boys coming back home
    the ambitious codebreaker soon found herself drummed out of the secret service
    with not much of a future awaiting her beyond motherhood
    or perhaps service as a waitress.
    For the next while she drifted from dull job to dull job till she
    finally found employment at a cash strapped radio station where she acted as their field reporter looking for mysteries ,
    vanishings and weird sightings in the skies above England.
    What happened next was fairly straight forward.She checked around for wartime locations of minefields around
    northern English waters,found the one mentioned in
    the German report,and soon had located the Grey Spindlestane.
    Laura found the cave,went inside and found a green glowing
    skeleton of some kind of huge reptilian beast.
    Obsiously the radioactive dragon the Germans had been looking for.
    Unwisely Laura had stayed in that cave far too long for her
    own good and not too smartly had touched the skeleton,which had promptly collapsed into radioactive particles of dust that
    Laura had breathed in,thoroughly dooming her.
    She hadn't known it at the time but instead had left the cave and
    after climbing the cliff,returned to her 1949 Austin Royce automobile apparently without any proof of her discovery.
    That would change the next day when Laura was assaulted by
    by the muggers in the alleyway in Berwickshire.
    It started as a pretty conventional mugging and all seemed to be
    going the way of the muggers,till one of them mentioned Laura
    didn't have a shadow.
    And the muggers were right.Laura gasped as she frantically
    looked for her shadow,but her body cast now shadow.
    Both Laura and the muggers sensed something bad was going down and the muggers spun around and fled deeper into the
    alley.
    Then came their screams and as Laura watched her 'shadow'
    came into view behind them.
    Except it wasn't quite her shadow anymore since it was
    glowing green and growing and altering before her awed eyes.
    As Laura watched her shadow morphed into the shape of the
    green glowing shadow of a dragon,which promptly ripped her
    attacked to pieces in its radioactive shadowy claws.
    A sight that caused Laura to faint.
    When she recovered a while later her shadow was her
    own again,and appeared like any other shadow.
    But Laura knew differently.
    Shortly thereafter her personality began to change,become
    more twisted,monstrous,evil
    the personality of a emerging radioactive villainess
    with a bit too much dragon in her.....
