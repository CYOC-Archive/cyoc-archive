    LL Multiplying: One wrong answer

    added by Anonymous A year ago BM S O Multilimb

    Jen was furious, not only had her plan backfired, but now she had Kyle walking around with her face, she was distracted all day as she went through her classes, although she somehow got through it without lying. She found her life more difficult with all of these legs, she needed to pull all of them in to get through doors, which she barely cleared, and now she couldn't sit in any of the chairs. Instead she found her assigned seats were large stools, without a table, but instead a board she rested across her front most legs.

    Jen was in her last class of the day, still angry and distracted, her teacher going on with her lecture when she asked a question.

    "So, can anyone tell me what our state's capital is?" There was a pause, no one tried to answer, since everyone was ready to leave for the day. "Anyone know the capital of Virginia?" The teacher scanned the room. "Jen? Would you care to answer?"

    Jen blinked, and made a guess, having half heard the question about a capital. "Um... Washington D.C.?"

    "Sigh... Were you even paying attention Miss Smith?"

    "I'm sorry I've been distracted all day."

    "Richmond.. Richmond, Virginia." the teacher continued, as for Jen she started to feel a familiar feeling and let out a sigh, she felt another leg grow in, completing her second circle. She then saw another grow in, pushing her lap board against her stomach as it grew in, pointed upward in front of her at angled upward at forty five degrees, it was then that Jen realized what was happening, wide eyed she watched as more legs grew in, her wrong answer was considered a lie, not just to the teacher but all the students, and she was now getting all those legs. Jen wanted to scream as leg after leg grew in, her third row filling fast, she looked around her growing lower half, it finally stopped with her third row finishing it's circle with a total of sixteen legs, eight full sets of legs. Jen counted, trying to figure out if she had finished changing, and something was off, she had only grown seventeen legs, but there were twenty six people in the room including her, where were the other eight.

    It was with this thought that her next row began right in front of her, she nearly kneed herself in the chest as the next row started growing in, her new leg resting nearly straight up. She nearly screamed, her nightmare vision from this morning was coming true before her as her view began to be obstructed by leg after leg growing in front of her. When it finally stopped, her four new pairs crowded her, they were mere inches away from her face, she tried to push them down, but the angle of her asses pressed against each other kept them in a nearly straight up position. The bell rang and Jen sat there quietly crying to herself, wanting nothing more than for this to be a nightmare she finally brought herself to stand up, her inner legs faltering under the increased weight, causing her to spread her legs out, her inner circle not moving away from her by much, she grabbed her bag and tried to walk, her new legs kicked with the motion, and she nearly kicked herself in the face. Jen nearly screamed, how was she going to move like this? She finally grabbed the pair of legs in front of her and hooked her arms around them, trying to pull them behind her she finally found a way to see in front of her.

    Jen cautiously moved trough the door to the class room and finally out into the hall. Jen still crying made her way outside, where surely Kyle was waiting to mock her mercilessly. She finally made it down the stairs outside when she heard Kyle gasp.

    "Jen?"

    Jen now has 44 legs. 8 in her first row. 12 in her secound. 16 in her third. 8 in her fourth.
