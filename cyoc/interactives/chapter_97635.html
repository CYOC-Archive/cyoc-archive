"Due to circumstances beyond our control, Yvette will be unable to complete your transformation until after her arraignment hearing tomorrow morning, when proper arrangements can be made to insure that she will do what is right by you without attempting to escape again."

    "Harry!" said Jenny, wanting to run into his arms, but the orderly stood between them.

"I promised you I'd be by your side to help you in any way I can, and I meant that. And back in the tent, when at first I wasn't sure what was going on and feared I might lose you..." Then Harry got down on one knee before everyone present and asked, "Jennifer Harper, can I please have your hand and paw in marriage?"
