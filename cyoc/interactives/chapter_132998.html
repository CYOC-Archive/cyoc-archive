    John replied, "Yes! I'd love to be a werebear!" He perused through the photos and picked his 4 donors. He would have wavy blond hair, a darker skin tone, a squared jaw, and defined cheekbones. Then, Randy took out a syringe and removed two pints of John's blood. He took the blood of the 4 choices that he had in a fridge and injected it into John. John's appearance changed to what he wanted. He thanked Randy very much. For the next four weeks, Randy cooked lots of food to fatten John up for his first change. After the four weeks, John was quite fat. Then, Randy injected some of his own blood into John. John's changes then started. He lost a few inches in height. His nose turned black and leathery and it and his mouth pushed out into a muzzle. He grew a humongous beard. He gained more teeth and they all sharpened. His ears rounded and moved further up his head. His posture changed so his head hunched over a little. His eyes turned yellow. His arms grew longer. Pads appeared on his hands and feet. He still retained his thumb and was bipedal. His nails lengthened into claws. He grew wider, and fatter, breaking his belt and causing the button on his jeans to fly off. His whole body became more muscular, ripping his clothes to shreds. A pelt of thick fur appeared all over him. Lastly, he gained a small tail. He loved his new changes and roared in excitement. Then he and Randy made out and then he changed back. He was wider, stockier, had a thick beard, body hair, and an impressive package. What was left of his clothes clung to him. Randy offered to get him some new clothes.
