    Flight Over Height

    Mary wondered what other vials could give her an interesting experience at a small size. Spinning the racks, she saw vials with pictures of various small animals." Eehh... something a little closer to human shape." She thought to herself. As if on cue, she spotted just the vial. It had a picture of a female figure with long, pointed ears, butterfly-like wings and antennae. "A fairy; perfect!"

    Mary lifted the vial out, pulled the stopper and drank the whole thing. After a few seconds, the tingling returned and spread through her body; it was quicker this time, possibly due to her smaller size, but the familiar pulsations pooled in certain places.

    First, the ears. They started to turn outward and grow bigger, longer and sharper. Next, the forehead. It felt like a two, nozzled tube of toothpaste as a pair of long, thin antennae slowly squeezed out and curled into an elegant spiral at the end. Finally, the back. She felt a burrowing from within. It was painful, but at the same time, it also felt wonderful! At last, the surface of her skin pushed aside, allowing the tiny, clear sheets to push through. The pulsations intensified as the sheets grew larger and larger. Now the pain gave way to more pleasure; so much so, that had she not just orgasmed a few minutes ago, this would've certainly sent her over the edge.

    Soon, the prismatic, butterfly shaped wings reached their final size, rising high over your head, and dipping almost to the floor. A few test flaps showed how deceptively strong and powerful they felt for something looking so delicate, scattering the light shining through it into so many beautiful colors.

    Mary fluttered her wings, effortlessly lifting herself up into the air. "This is so amazing!" She squealed, not caring she was still naked as a jaybird. She giggled and laughed as she flew all over the house; diving, twirling dancing, even testing her strength by picking up various things. To her surprise, even her roommate, Jessica's 5lb. dumbbell was no match for her new fairy wings.

    Mary saw the empty fairy vial on the table next to the chest. Placing the vial back, she watched the pink liquid refill. After closing the chest, she hid it under a chair. It was then she heard the door open, and Jessica enter; back home from her morning jog. She got an idea.
