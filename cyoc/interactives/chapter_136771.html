    The next morning. Monica wakes up and finds her sleeping wererat boyfriend sleeping next to her.

    Instead of being mad, or surprise, Monica was happy to see Damon. She goes up to him and hugs him.

    "Damon I'm glad your here." Damon wakes up and finds Monica hugging him. Damon blush.

    "Monica morning." Monica gets out of bed as Damon does the same. "I am glad your here Damon. There's something I must tell you."

    Damon looks at Monica as she place her hands on her belly. "Damon I'm pregnant and your the father."

    Damon was shock. "Are you sure?" Damon ask her. "Yes it must have been a few days when we had sex." Said Monica.

    Damon was a bit shock. But he was happy as well. He was going to be a father. "Just think. We'll have a werekid."

    This made Damon concern. "Are you sure you want to have a were kid Monica? What will your parents think?" Damon ask her.

    "It will be fine Damon. We have to tell them sooner or later." Said Monica. "I guess. I just hope they don't tell anyone about me."

    Damon felt his stomach rumble. "I could do for some cheese right now." Said Damon.

    "Wait here. I'll go get some." Monica then left to get some cheese for Damon.
