From how they were talking, these would have to be transformation chambers, but they were too close together for that. Suddenly, she heard Charlotte cry out through the thin wall next to her causing her to pound on the door even harder than she already was. Next she heard a thunk, and the loud hum of an electric motor from above as a large bit of machinery mounted in the ceiling slid into place. So that was how they did it, but just what sort of experiments were they conducting here that called for this sort of setup?

Just as some sort of preliminary step, or...? Suddenly her legs gave way from under her, shrinking away to nothing even as the skin was starting to change into some kind of scales. Was she part fish, or part snake? Were these weird internal changes she was feeling just the result of no longer being a mammal, or had these monsters done the unthinkable and actually made her male on top of anything else?
