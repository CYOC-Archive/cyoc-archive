    Instantly Will's memories flooded back. What happened yesterday, she and her friends skating in the park, her and Sam's transformation, how
    everyone thought that they were always girls, their preparations for the party, how she met Zoe and Jess... Jess, the one who brought this upon
    them, her deal, the only chance for them to change back, the party... Most of her memories about the end of the evening were like a blur.
    Perhaps cause she drank a bit too much. Seems like her new body couldn't take as much alcohol like her male one. But at least she was sure that
    she and Mike didn't do anything she would regret. Not like Sam, who was all to eager to get into Joey's pants....Sam!

    Immediately Will took her phone and dialed Sam. Nervously waiting while the phone ringed, she was glad when she heard a hung-over Sam at the
    phone.

    "Do you know how late it is? I've gotta hell of an headache so call me in ten hours!", mumbled Sam sleepy.

    "Sam, listen, did you and Joey had sex last night?", asked Will with her heart beating wildly.

    "Well, yeah. But why is that so important? Wanna have some fun by yourself? I'll give you all the juicy details if you let me sleep some hours.",
    snickered Sam and was about to go back to bed.

    "Sam, listen, do you know that if you become pregnant than we might aren't able to change back? Remember our plan? Our deal with Jess? Two
    weeks and this torment is over!"

    "How dumb do you think I am? Joey used a condom! He cared about me and didn't want to become a teenage daddy. So if that's all I try to catch
    some sleep. See you you tomorrow." With this Sam hung up and left Will with her worries alone.

    Perhaps Sam was alright. She was a responsible guy, well, girl after all. Following Sam's advice she tried to catch some more sleep.
    It was after noon when she finally got out of her bed and came out of her room. At first she spent more than an hour in the bathroom. It took
    insanely long to wash and to dry her long hair. Also to she must wear some clothes today and so Will faced the full horror of her wardrobe. Well,
    must of things she had already seen yesterday when she and Sam got ready for the party, but now she also must put on a new pair of panties and
    after some further exploration she found a matching bra. Also she wore now jeans, a bit too tight for her taste, and a green top with not too
    much cleavage.

    Most of the day passed in a blur. Meal with her parents, some smalltalk about the party, an unnerving interrogation from her father if she and
    Mike had any sexual encounter, listening some music in her room, and also doing her homework last minute. Some things didn't even seem to
    change after a gender swap.

    When Will changed into her pajamas, in pink and very girl despite her image as a skater girl, she felt into sleep with very dissimilar thoughts. One
    the one hand she wanted to meet Jess as soon as possible, get her next challenge and get over with this hell after two weeks. On the other hand
    she feared what Jess might has planned for her and Sam, she didn't sound like it would be very pleasant. Also she didn't was very eager to
    experience her first school day as a girl. With this thoughts Will finally drifted into sleep.
