Although once he started going out more and more he seemed to be growing a bit more distant especially when he turned 11 and started noticing other girls.

Some guests came in, and Richard came to you with a girl with long straight black hair, and pretty sapphire like eyes. Richard said "Papo i'd like to introduce you to my friend Cathrine. Cathrine this is my childhood buddy Papo." You felt a little bit of jealousy over the girl's beauty.

    That said you put on a smile and said "Hello Cathrine."

    Cathrine put her hand out and said "I was hoping to see you. You were one of those butterfly beastmen right? I can't wait to see you once you've changed."

    Catherine getting a little closer to Richard said "I've heard you'll know what to do when the time comes. Although how long would it take for you to change once in the cocoon?"

    Richard said "I've heard it takes years. I think i'll feel rather lonely once you start to change Papo. We've known and had fun with each other for so long."

    Catherine getting a little more clingy said "Oh Richie, I can always come over when your lonely." Your heart started to throb a bit. You were never that close to Richard, but Catherine had in such a shorter amount of time. You started to scratch the back of your head, and Catherine asked "Papo, do you have bad dandruff?" All you could mutter was a huh before Catherine took your hand and said "I've had bad dandruff in the past, let me show you how I deal with it." then as she pulled her hand came off and she felt something odd and said "What is... huh?" She looked in her hand and saw some odd webbing.

    Richard said "Papo your skin... it's ... I think your making your cocoon." Alarmed you looked at your arms which were starting to get covered in the string and getting thicker. Scared you started to try to rip off the webbing but it just kept coming. Richard asked "Papo isn't this normal."

    It was covering your face and you ripping it off started curling up in terror and muttered "I don't want to die..." as you continued to try to deny your body doing it's thing. having curled up it felt oddly comforting, and after a minute of panicking your body calmed itself down as the webbing enveloped you. Everyone was watching as a girl had just turned herself into a cocoon.

    Richard asked "Are you alright in there?!?"

    Barely hearing him you muttered "Please don't leave me..."

    Richard said "I'm here Papo!" hearing the commotion your mother and Richard's parents came into the room to find everyone staring at a silk encasing. Your mother flew over in glee. The air in your cocoon was getting hot and more filled with carbon dioxide. Then something felt more odd as it felt like your stomach flipped inside out and your body started to feel like it was eating itself. You groaned in agony and Richard asked "Papo are you ok?!? Is this that dying thing?!?"

    your mother overhead said "I can see why you may think that, but it's just nature doing it's course. Her body is dissolving itself into a soup to where some of the most important parts are kept and the rest will turn itself into a majestic form." Richard didn't know this but felt scared for his old friend and he grabbed a knife to which his mother said "You open that cocoon now and she's dead. Considering she's screaming her body is halfway dissolved, opening the cocoon will let in all sorts of bacteria and will let her body leak out and she'll never be whole again." Richard put the knife back and your mother said "I'll take care of my daughter. Please enjoy your little party little master." your mother lifted your moaning cocoon which after a few minutes stopped moaning. You consciousness went dark, a long time had passed and in your heart the most important things you clutched was your love of your family, but especially Richard. And your Jealousy of Catherine.
