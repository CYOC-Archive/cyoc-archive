    The cool new phone on the street

    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous 5 years ago O

    Friday afternoon. The best time to the start of summer break. To make it even better, it was a half day at school, so everyone got to leave after lunch.

    On my way home, I found a phone box on the ground. It was probably empty trash, but I was still curious. I opened the box, and found that there was a phone, and it was still brand new. Hiding it in my bag, I decided to hurry home.

    Since it was a half day, I was home alone till 8. I dumped my bag on the couch and grabbed the phone box.

    Opening it up, it was a brand new GPhone XR. People who’ve heard of these know it costs at least $1000 just for a new camera feature. People who don’t have GPhones know that they just release the same phone for a new price every year.

    I turned on the phone and was met with the voice assistant on the phone, but she wasn’t normal.

    “Hello John! How can I help you today” it said. I stared at the phone, starting to get scared as to why it knew my name.

    “How did you know my name?” I asked her. It heard my question, and responded immediately, “With facial reconstruction and your finger print, I can identify anyone who touched or looks into this phone!”

    I stared in amazement. I just thought this phone only added two more cameras. Instead, I got an even better phone upgrade.

    “Let’s get you started with your new GPhone T John!”

    ‘GPhone T? I could have sworn it was a GPhone XR?’

    “Hey Yuri, I thought this was a GPhone XR?” I asked her. Again, she responded immediately, “It is! You have a limited edition of the new XR Phone!”

    “What does the new limited edition bring?”

    “It brings a new way to experience life!”

    “Ok then. So what does the T stand for?”

    “Transformation!”

    “Transformation? As in this phone can transform me into anything?”

    “Yes! Now before we can get started, let’s fill out your information!” A questionnaire pops up on the screen, and I filled out most of the information:

    Name: John Lopo
    Race: White
    Height: 5’ 10”
    Weight: 170
    Shoe size: 13
    Hair color: brown
    Eye color: blue
    Skin: tan
    Siblings: none
    Parents: Tom Lopo
    Parents: Jessica Lopo
    Gender: Male


    “Thank you. I will gather more information using the internet and medical records! Now please, choose a transformation”

    A new pop up appeared on screen. Three options were given to me: transformation, time limit, reality change. On those three options are even more.

    Transformation had the most. When I clicked on it, I was bombarded by a full row of options to scroll from. Age regression, Age Progression, Inanimate, Animal, Body shape, Gender, Size, Custom.

    Time Limit simply had different times. Scrolling through, the maximum I can transform would be 30 hours.

    Reality change had a few options. Looking at it, I can tell that it allowed those who were transformed to be remembered as their old selves, or forgotten and remembered as their new selves. It can even just affect just the transformer instead of everyone.

    “Hey Yuri, does this transformation thing work?”

    “Yes John, why don’t you try it out on yourself!”

    I decided to click options randomly and I pressed transform.
