The she was certain each one was placed carefully in their spots, they were her big sisters after all. They would sleep for now, until the magical girl army had organically grown large enough for them to turn back into real girls, for now Doll-Master Delilah would watch over them until they were ready. That day wouldn't be tomorrow, the day after that, or the day after that, or the day after that, but it would come eventually. She'd watch over her family until then.
