    Biggest bunny

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago A S

    Sam reaches behind her back and after pulling out a few useless items like a horn and chattering teeth, she pulls out an impossibly massive amount of sky blue fabric. This was enormous, the material filled the whole room and looked like it could fit over a skyscraper if given the chance. Dispite it's massive size, it was super soft, like sea of fluffy pillows.

    "Put this one" said Sam gesturing a paw at the massive mound of material.

    "Put it on?!" Tom remarked, incredulously.

    This was hilarious, what was he supposed to do with this circus tent of fabric? Tom decided to humor the cute bunny and crawled under it. Feeling around, Tom was encased in this sea of material, and with that he started to feel strange. Tom felt bloated, not exactly fat, just bigger somehow. Standing up, he felt something hit his head. Ouch! What could that be?

    Finding a hole, Tom poked his head out and was greeted with an incredible sight. Tom had hit is head on the ceiling, he was already 20 feet tall and growing quickly! His floppy ear fell over his face. They were large and fuzzy and the same sky blue as this giant costume that Tom was growing into.

    Sam looked pleased, "I hope you enjoy your new form, I'll meet you outside!"

    "Wait no!" Shouted Tom. There was no way for him to get outside, 30 feet tall, cramped into the room, no way to fit out the door.

    Moving his foot to reposition himself as he crouched, he looked down to see his foot had filled the massive costume's feet. Giving his toes a wiggle, the blue bunny paw was the size of an SUV, and still Tom continued to grow.

    He felt something behind him, but couldn't turn around to see it. Tom could only imagine that it was his fluffy tail.

    Tom was almost completely transformed into a blue bunny, and with no room left his the cramped hotel room he wondered what would happen next...
