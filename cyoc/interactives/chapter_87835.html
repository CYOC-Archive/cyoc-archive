    "WHAT DID YOU DO?!" A voice screamed across the static. Ty cringed as it seemed to emanate from within himself and bellow from the void around him. Female and male at the same time. Rage.

    "I... I..." Ty cried, tears finally falling from his face, and seemingly becoming weird geometric shapes as they fell away.

    "YOU FOOL! LOOK AT WHAT YOU'VE DONE!" The voice screamed again. "YOU'VE KILLED US ALL!"

    * * *

    Dustin couldn't breath. It seemed like there was no air. His giraffe body, despite his efforts to plunge on, was beginning to fall apart. He gasped for air and panicked as none would come, but that was to say nothing of the blood pouring from his eyes and nostrils, and the pressure inside his head. The static had become bizarre gray stripes, waving around in unfamiliar movements, and the titanic giraffe's body trembled as he felt another growth coming. Whatever he was in, wherever he was, the entire place was protesting, seemingly forcing against the animal's body to keep him from expanding. Little by little, though, Dustin began to press out and up. He screamed, a gut-wrenching giraffe scream, as pain violated his every fiber. He felt his chest began to expand in distension, his guts began to churn. His heart boiled and his blood burned through his veins. Finally, the tension became too much. Dustin managed one last look down at himself to see he had fattened up completely, his skin taut and floating over his muscle and bones.

    "I-I'm going to pop!" He screamed inside himself, feeling the blood from practically spewing from his face. He felt himself bloat more, the growth pushing him further, and he began to creak and gag, feeling his guts rising in his neck, being pushed out.

    * * *

    "DO SOMETHING! DO *SOMETHING* NOW!* The voice screamed. "HE'S GOING TO DESTROY US ALL!"

    Ty thought... it was impossible to think but for the static screaming through the anti-blackness. He tried... tried...

    * * *

    The explosion of Dustin was catastrophic. His expanded belly tore along the middle and with terrible force his guts burst forth. As soon as he came apart, electrical energy burst forth from his eye sockets and mouth, building to a lightning storm around him, singing him black in seconds. Dustin never even felt the death. Seconds more and his body had disintegrated totally, taking with it an unfathomable amount of the strange universe around him. A howling wind from nowhere and everywhere suddenly ripped through everything, sucking it into nothingness.

    * * *

    As this other higher universe collapsed around him, Ty heard nothing else from the voice. Only the tearing screaming howl of the wind, taking in everything and sucking it down into a swirl of black nothing. Ty screamed, unable to be hurt, unable to die, and tried to fly away, but the sheer power of the suction pulled him down along with everything else.

    Hours later, Ty opened his eyes. He saw nothing. He tried to move, and found he was all there, even touched his face, but it was infinitely black. He was in nothingness. No sound, no smells, no light. Just nothing. Infinite nothing.
