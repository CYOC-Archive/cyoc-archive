    Dani channel surfed but stopped when he came across a collegiate sports program featuring the national wrestling finals. Dani fluffed up his pillows and sat back to enjoy the show. As the young men locked each other in the their arms, Dani's eyes gleamed. It was exciting to watch the two guys in their tight wrestling singlets go at it. The blond kid looked like he was about to be pinned, when he did an acrobatic flip over his opponent and turned the tables pinning him. Dani was now leaning forward and sporting a boner. In a few minutes his skin was tingling.

    In the meantime, Gunther had discovered the bag in his possession belonged to Dani. He figured Dani had to have his bag and his nanobots. He went to the front desk and got Dani's room number, and then headed to get his bag before Dani opened it.

    Goosestepping rapidly down the hall toward Dani's room, Gunther thought what he would do if Dani had opened the bag and discovered Gunther's contraband contents. Most likely Dani wouldn't know what it was, but if he did, what would Gunther do. He recalled Dani was a lot smaller than him. He grinned.

    "Uh, uh, uh, uh," moaned Dani as his skin glistened like shiny lycra. Each gasp seemed to deflate him. He was shrinking getting flatter. It wasn't possible. He struggled to stand, but all he managed to accomplish was to send the little clothing that he had been wearing off his body and on to the floor. Then he fell backwards slowly as if he was floating back on to the bed.

    He continued to breathe harder and harder, "Uh, uh, uh, uh, uh, uh," deflating with each gasp. He finally let out a loud, "Aaaaaaaaaah!" his final sigh as he transformed into a wrestling singlet.

    Gunther knocked on Dani's door, but there was no answer. He noticed the housekeeping cart parked by the next room. He knocked on that open door, and the maid came out.

    "Excuse me, my name's Dani, and I locked myself out the room next door. Could you let me in?" Gunther asked with his sweetest expression.

    The maid looked on her clipboard and confirmed that the next room belonged to Dani. So she let Gunther into Dani's room with her passkey.

    Gunther thanked her and gave her a $5 tip. He closed the door and leaned back against it and sighed in relief. If Dani wasn't here, he probably hadn't even looked in the bag yet. Gunther stepped into the room and saw his bag lying open on the floor next to Dani's bed. The cannister of nanobots was on the nightstand next to the bed. Gunther checked the indicator on the cannister and sighed. It was half empty. He started to look around the room. He saw Dani's clothing on the floor. He picked it up and examined each article. The final garment he picked up was a wrestling singlet lying on the bed. The singlet was too large for tiny Dani.

    Gunther grinned as he realized what had happened to Dani.

    Gunther stripped out of his clothing and stepped into the singlet that was Dani. He walked over to admire himself in the mirror. Dani was a perfect fit, it molded to his muscular physique as if it was painted on. Gunther turned his attention to the television, and was soon acting out the moves he was watching on the screen.

    Amazingly, Dani was still conscious throughout the entire transformation. He lay there in horror until Gunther entered, and then he was excited that Gunther would rescue and restore him. That was until Gunther started stripping down. The Dani was both excited and terrified, as Gunther pulled him over the German giant's massive body.
