    Your heads all said in unison,"egg door." Without second thoughts You stumbled into it, "trying to move on fours with three brains is hard." You thought.

    "Cool." An unknown voice in your head said. "Who's there?" You thought

    "It's me the head to your right." The third head said Identifying herself,"I think we can communicate through our minds,"The second head followed up," and I think we're unconscious but we're waking up now."

    "Hello there miss." you heard a kid like voice that started, but you couldn't stand for some reason.

    You opened your eyes yo find a light blue male hydra kid with 4 heads standing before you however is the same size as you."what where how..." all heads said in unison but your voices sounded childish. "Wow mommy talking really did work, she's already asking questions." One of the males heads said calling out.

    "Wonderful," you hear a voice and look up in curiosity to see a red female hydra with orange scales on her underside and 6 heads continued to say,"Fear not little ones we are your mother." You all looked at yourself and were shocked to find that you were a kid."who am I, who are we," Your heads said in unison.
    "I see speaking to your egg expanded your vocabulary little ones," Mothers main head started,"Your main head will be named Cinder, your right Ember, and your left Sunny do you like that?"

    All of your heads looked at each ither and nodded in unison."Good this is you brothers." Mother said motioning to the blue hydra. Each of the blue hydra's heads said there name from left to right but finished with the main head,"Frost, Frigid, Cold, and Ice."

    "Say hello to your brother Cinder, Ember, and Sunny." Mother said. Your heads responded in order,"Hello, Hi, Hey."

    At that point a female hydra entered the cave. The female hydra wasn't much bigger than Ice, has yellow scales, and had 5 heads. "I see that my new sibling is a sister," The hydra said cheerfully each of her heads introduced themselves just like with Ice's heads," We are Tracy, Circuit, Voltrace, Thundres, and I'm Lighting the main head." You responded, "We're Sunny, Ember, and Cinder."

    "Wow she's bright she even introduced herselves correctly." Circuit said

    "THEY introduced THEMSELVES correctly, but yes your correct." Mother said correcting Circuit.

    "Right sorry mother," Circuit apologized.

    "You're forgiven." Mother said

    "Anyway we are your older sister you don't know how excited I was coming home from school to find a new sibling right brother." Lightning teased Ice

    "Shu....Be quiet, it's okay for me even though I wanted a brother I'm just as fine with a new sister too." Ice said holding his tongue.

    " School?" Ember questioned,"Brother?" Sunny followed up, "Sister?" You finished.

    "You will learn the meaning of those words when you start school is about 38 years my child, but don't worry now."Mother said

    "School?"You said in unison with the other heads,"don't worry your heads off over it, we have enough family members as is."Mother said.

    "What?" You asked remembering how hydra heads worked.

    "Don't worry Cinder your father is coming home any second, and he will be thrilled to see how smart you are." Mother said

    Moments later a Hydra with seven heads entered the cave and lightning, and Ice ran up to him and cuddled with him screaming,"Daddy?"

    "Daddy?" You said trying to process what is going on.

    "Is that a new voice I hear." The hydra said walking up to you.

    "This is our new daughter, Tell daddy your names now and say hi."Mother said

    "Hi I'm Sunny, Hello I'm Ember, Hi I'm Cinder, and we're your daughters."You said accepting your new life.

    "Wow that's the longest sentence she's ever said."Mother said

    "When did she hatch?" Father asked Mother

    "Only about a few minutes ago."Mother answered

    "That's a record for the family for sure."Father said,"anyway We're your daddy Cinder welcome to the family."

    (For those who are wondering how do hydras refer to one another
    when referring to someone as sigular use the name of the main head
    When referring to someone's heads use the name of make it plural
    Titles refer to all of the heads but aren't plural because it's referring to the body below the heads
    Finally when introducing heads go left to right but always end with tha main head.)
