    Ben looks at the computer and suddenly reminds himself of his friends. They would would just LOVE this program! He thinks to himself. He gets up and picks up the phone and starts calling his four friends, telling them to come over immediately. As he puts down the phone after the fourth and last call he stares at his paw hand on the phone. What if I would change myself into a complete dog and see what my friends will do. He thinks to himself and smiles. He rushes to the computer and starts going through the options. "Hm... change breed... no... change gender... no... ah! This must be it!: Increase change!" He clicks the option and looks at the percentage on the screen. "Hmm... apparently I'm 65% Canine... I think I'll increase it to 90%" Just as he types in the 9 in 90 he stops. "No wait, what if I won't look enough like a dog at 90%? Better go with 100% to be sure" He types in 100% and presses "Ok" A small message box appears on the screen which says: Change in progress and the 65% slowly starts raising.

    He looks down at his body with interest as it starts to shrink and change. He gets up on his feet to get a better view of the changes, but a sharp pain in his hips and back makes him fall forward. He lands hard on his hands and feet. "Man these Chrangrrres arrre prretty fruff!" He manage to bark out before his vocal chords quickly change. Maybe I shouldn't have changed 100%, now I can't surprise them with a talking dog... He thinks as he watches his body adept itself into a life on four legs. When his hind legs shortened into the same length as his forelegs he gets up on his four paws instead of his forepaws and knees. I have to correct the change so I at least can speak. He thinks. He jumps up on two legs and keeps up his balance with the help of his desk and looks at the computer screen. 96%...97% Now where's the cancel button? 98%...99% Suddenly he realizes that all of the number and words on the screen have somehow changed into small, strange black figures. What's happening? Ben whines in confusion, not really sure what to do.

    ...100% Ben the newfoundland looks confused in front of the screen, wondering that it is. He tries to sniff it, but as it has an totally uninteresting smell, he gets down on all fours again. He starts to sniff around the room and find, much to his joy that it smells like food in the kitchen.
