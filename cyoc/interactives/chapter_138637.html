    added by christopher 4 years ago A BM S O Aquatic

    Ron sighed a deep sigh. the 2 choices he had didn't seem to be ringing to him.,

    Somehow or other, he was beginning to think of something a bit more... fishy.

    he clicked on a arrow, and he saw more tf ideas:

    Aquatic
    Reptile
    part man, part animal

    Ron whistled.

    "Cool." he said. "I'd like to try something like..... Aquatic."

    He picked the Aquatic option.

    after that process went up, up came another question: 'What fish do you wanna be?'

    then it showed the options, like last time:

    shark
    cod
    barracuda
    whale
    octopus
    dolphin
    bass
    carp
    flounder
    jellyfish
    eel
    squid
    part-fish, part man

    THAT was where Ron had to think carefully. He placed a hand on his chin thoughtfully.

    The dolphin had lungs, but the others didn't. and he didn't want to have an instant tf; that mean he'd flop about on his chair, slowly suffocating due to lack of air. Then, there was the size of water needed for the fish to breathe. Running his bath deep could work for the smaller options, but the bigger ones would need more space, like the ocean.

    And he didn't need to worry about they type of water needed for certain fish forms, for luckily, the site would make the user's fish tf last in all water, be it fresh water or saltwater.

    he even made plans to discuss it with his son after school.

    so.

    what did Ron decide to do?
