Your old name is about to not matter. Your owner will decide your name for you. Perhaps your old memories will be less than a dream. You'll never be thrown away or destroyed, that part of the contract was very precise. But your time as a human being is about to end. What your gender was before is about to not matter anymore either.

    You signed three or five times that you were doing this of your own free will. You're about to be remade, who you were before remade from almost completely the ground up, with only the parts of your personality that'll serve your new purpose that will remain. You get the feeling that's what you wanted, that you feel this is a kind of freedom from who you were before. There's a great comfort in that, at least for YOU. There's no changing back once the change has been made. And you'll exist, alive and aware, virtually forever.

    Now there's only one choice left that your own.

You'll be a living love doll, your thoughts and ideas all focused around giving pleasure to your new owner. A living fake fur and fluff filled plushie, a living plastic and hollow baby doll, a toy puppet whose world and life changed according to the whims of the child who played with you, you'd be humanoid, but that was about the only rule.

You've already left your life as a human behind. Will you be one who exists to give pleasure to adults, or one who exists to give happiness to children? It's the last choice that belongs to you as a human.

    You take off your clothes, toss them down the trash chute, and make your choice.
