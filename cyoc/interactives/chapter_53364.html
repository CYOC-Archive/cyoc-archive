    There was breaking news on. A young model had just been kidnapped right in the middle of his photo shoot. The young model was doing a shoot for Abocromi & Finch. In short, he was taken from the shoot while wearing nothing but black briefs. The culprit had been a life sized wooden puppet. It had broken through the glass roof, right above the young man, and taken him. A second puppet had fallen from the same open roof top the previous puppet had just appeared from. It stared directly at the camera and said, in a mechanical voice:

    "To all heroes out there. This is a challenge from the great, and unstoppable Collector, 6th king of the Seven Fiends. He has issued out a challenge to all the young heroes within the country to try and stop him. As you can see, the young model we have taken is just the beginning. Every two days we will capture one citizen unless a hero decides to challenge the Great Collector. If a hero can defeat my master and his house, then the Collector will release every captive he has in his possession. However, if you should fail hero, you will join the captives in my master's vast collection.

    Are you brave enough to face the Collector's Fun House of 1000 Traps? Are you strong enough to defeat the great Collector? Are there even any heroes will to challenge him? We dare you to come heroes."

    With that, the puppet flew out of the studio, leaving the message for the whole country to here and heroes all over to hear the call. All over the country strapping young men put on their skin tight spandex suits. They were all willing to save the day, accepting the challenge set up by the mad man.

    Meanwhile, the young model, Jacob Maddison was taken to a house in an abandoned district. The house looked like some crazy fun house. He was taken through the back door and taken to the basement. There the puppets that belonged to the Collector got to work. They all came down on him, stripping him of his only remaining piece of clothing. Naked, Jacob was exposed to the groping puppets. They took him over to a pedestal. Once his feet made contact, he found that he couldn't move them. He was stuck to the piece of marble. Then the puppets started spraying him with some sort of gunk. He felt his body stiffen, getting rigid and hard. They posed his arms, making them flex. His tight muscles flexed and firmed as he was posed, made to stand with his arms flexing his powerful biceps. He was forced to put on a permanent smile. As the puppets finished spraying Jacob with the gunk, Jacob was locked in his pose. He looked like a plastic statue of himself.

    The Collector walked in to examine his newest prize. The supervillain didn't look like a villain. Rather he looked like a teenage boy with a fedora and wearing a black trench coat and a suit under.

    "Hmm. My Jacob you are quite handsome. I know you can feel the touch of my hand feeling your hard, smooth body. Your my prize now. And everyone I capture will lure in my ultimate prizes. The superheroes of the world. They'll all be a part of my ultimate collection."

    The villain laughed. His watch started beeping as a silent alarm was tripped.

    "Looks like the first of my master peices has arrived." he said, smirking.
