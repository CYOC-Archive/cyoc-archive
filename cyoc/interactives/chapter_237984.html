    A Lizard-Scientist And A Kid-Guard

    added by LittleBoy 20 days ago A AR BM S Reptilian

    Ivan looks around the room, his eyes first land on Case, ‘no, before I do THAT, I have to know if it really works,’ he thinks to himself, his eyes moving to the guards by the door, then to the scientist, ‘I think I’ll try it out on Yuri and Vlad,’ he thinks to himself.

    The man first opens the file of his guard Yuri and decides to have him regress to a ten-year-old, a smirk appearing on his face, which grows when he moves to his scientist’s file, deciding to transform HIM into an anthro lizard-man, once both changes are completed, Ivan confirms them and looks up to see what would happen.

    At first nothing seems to happen, Ivan frowns, but then he notices both his guard and scientist shiver, “you guys okay?”

    “Yeah, just a weird tingling swept through my body,” they both say at the same time, their eyes widen, “what, you felt it too,” they both say again together.

    Yuri then notices something, “what the… why does my uniform suddenly feel so baggy,” he asks.

    Ivan’s eyes widen in shock as he notices that Yuri is shrinking and growing younger, he then hears a surprised cry, “the hell,” Vlad yells, and when Ivan turns to look his eyes widen even more as he sees his scientist’s hand covered in green scales, his finger nails grown into long black claws.

    Both men look terrified, “the fuck is going on,” they both exclaimed, Yuri’s voice cracking, now a 12 year old and still getting younger, the boy struggling to keep his pants from falling to the floor.

    The sound of fabric tearing fills the room as a long lizard tail bursts from Vlad’s pants, “the FUCK,” the scientist yells, “how… what… am I transforming into some monster?”

    Ivan, Case and the other guard all look shocked, “no, way,” Case says, “it actually works?!”

    The mob boss is speechless, but then he grins, “it seems so,” he says as he watches both transformations come to an end, soon a 10-year-old boy wearing an oversized guard’s uniform and a lizard-man are standing in disbelief in the room.

    “What the fuck happened to us,” Yuri says, flinching at his high voice.

    “Un-unbelievable,” Vlad mumbles, staring down at himself, then back at HIS tail.

    Ivan grins wider, “you two look so much better than before, haha!”

    “Boss… please explain what has happened,” the two say.

    Ivan, continuing to grin, explains what had happened, Yuri is horrified, but Vlad is more curious than anything else, “boss… I’d love to study that app, please… send it to my computer”.

    Before Ivan can reply, Case says, “sorry to speak up, but,” he starts, all heads turn to him, “I found it on the app store, it is public, so anyone with access to the app-store and internet is able to download and install it, it’s free”. The agents eyes widen in horror, “why did I just tell you all that?!”

    Vlad grins, “because of the truth serum,” he explains, “it does not only cause you to answer questions truthfully, but also causes you to reply to certain phrases, it doesn’t have to be a question”.

    “Oh… shit,” Case says.

    Ivan grins, “thanks for bringing this amazing app to our attention,” he says, “with this, I’ll be able to rule the world, all thanks to you, Case”.

    “What have I done,” the man asks himself, fear raising in him.

    Ivan chuckles, “weren’t it for the serum, we might have never known,” he says, “since YOU won’t have need for this app anymore, I shall transfer it onto my phone”.

    “What do you mean,” Case asks, “you going to kill me?”

    Ivan smirks, “no, Case,” he says, “I have something else planned for you,” he then turns to the people in the room, “leave us alone,” he orders.

    “Sir, you can’t leave me like this,” Yuri pipes up.

    Ivan grins down at the boy, “and why not?”

    “Be-because I HATE kids,” Yuri says, “besides, how am supposed to defend this facility if I’m a stupid kid?!”

    “Oh come on, Yuri,” Ivan says, “you look so cute and being a kid again surely is not a bad thing, but let me fix that uniform of yours, you can still guard this place, even as a kid”.

    “You… you can’t be serious, boss,” the boy says, as his uniform adjusts to his new body and age.

    “I AM serious,” Ivan says, “now, if you don’t want me to transform you into a girl, you better get your cute little ass out of my office! Oh, and don’t worry, I am planning to transform ALL of my guards and agents into kids, because: no one dares killing children or harming them”.

    Yuri clenches his fists, but then he lets out a sigh, “okay, fine,” he says.

    “Good boy, now get lost,” Ivan says.

    Yuri nods, salutes and walks out of the office, followed by the other guard and Vlad, leaving Case alone with Ivan.

    “I’ll get him back for this,” Yuri grumbles, as he walks down a hallway with his partner.

    “I honestly am jealous,” his partner says, “but apparently he’s planning to transform all of us into kids”.

    “Kids are so annoying,” Yuri whines.

    “You’ll get used to it, you might hate it now, but I think you’ll be glad being a kid in a few hours,” his partner says, “come, let’s get back to our post, and if someone should laugh at you, just remember that he or she will soon be joining you as a kid. As for myself, I can’t WAIT to be transformed into a kid, heck, I might even get that app myself and do it myself!”

    Yuri is in disbelief, but he then lets out a sigh, “I guess you are right, being a kid isn’t that bad,” he says, “I’m faster and more flexible than I was as a adult”.

    His partner smiles, “see,” he says, smiling.

    Yuri can’t help and smiles back, “yeah… I guess I was just in shock,” he says.

    His partner nods, “understandable,” he says.

    Meanwhile, Vlad had arrived in his lab, his employees freaking out when they see their boss, but soon calming down and even nervously exclaiming how awesome he looks. Vlad grins, “thank you all,” he says, “I honestly feel great, now, let’s see what we can find out about this app,” he says, his crew nods and gets to work.

    Back in Ivan’s office, the man grins at Case, “what are you planning,” the agent asks.

    Ivan grin widens, “you’ll see soon enough,” he says, “one thing is for sure: you will NEVER be leaving this place,” his grin widening, “because you won’t be WANTING to leave!”
