    Scion Regal a boy on earth was off in his room going online looking for some sort of challenge. He was looking around the internet to find something that would test his skills where he found out about the security system. Looking around technically it wasn't illegal to attempt to or succeed into hacking into the security system. It was so unhackable that no one thought it was worth making it illegal, because it would never be illegal. He found the security system and started to try hacking into it as the ultimate puzzle. It took Scion all of his Saturday to break through the supposedly unhackable security system. He sighed at the realization that he probably wouldn't be getting any better challenges for his mind unless he wanted to solve an irrational puzzle. You looked out your window to see that the sun had set and thought to yourself you may try to hack the security system again at some point but try to finish it within an hour. First thing first though he would see what sort of prize he could get out from his efforts.

    to his non-surprise his ability to change any human throughout the universe was granted, and through his hack he was able to access the ability at any time really. He sighed and said to himself "Well I may as well go ahead and make an app to make this ability a little more useful." He did a bit of basic programming and gave himself another personal app he named "Change". It was a basic app allowing him to see everyone's stats and change the stats. It would be a mess to use this individual app to change a large amount of a population. In fact to even change someone he would have to have the potential for line of sight with the person he was changing. It would make it easier to tell that he had hacked the security system which he doubted anyone was really monitoring.

    *beep beep* an phone icon appeared in front of him that came from his mother. Scion answered and his mother scolded him immediately by saying "So you can hear your notifications in the virtual world, but not me yelling for you to go to dinner?!?"
    Scion sighed and said "I had my room sound proofed remember? There was no way for me to hear you even if you had been yelling at the top of your lungs right outside my door. Regardless I was in the middle of a tricky puzzle, I'll be down eating whatever cold leftover is now available." Scion's mother hung up and he walked downstairs to get something to eat. It was just half of a cold chicken breast on a plate. sighing he grabbed a soda from the fridge and then ate it as his mother came in.
    Scion's mother asked "So what puzzle was it that was so important?!?"
    "If you must know it was that unhackable security system protecting our nanomachines and implants from being abused. Although I suppose it wasn't unhackable all along."
    "So your admitting to becoming a criminal now?"
    "It's not against the law. Heck if the grand rulers were to have admitted that the system was hacked they would be the one's being killed, and as long as I don't abuse my powers I won't be seen as a demon."
    "you're kidding, there is no way someone who doesn't notice dinner could've broken that security."
    Sighing Scion said "Fine, i'll prove that I broke the security..." He thought for a second and then.
