    “Golly Ty, you need to quit picking on Dustin all the time!” Peter said to Ty.

    Poor Dustin had now shrunk to 18 inches tall! He closed the door and walked back into the living room where all the 6’9” guys were standing.

    “Alright, cousin… Why don’t you join Dustin as the world’s smallest basketball players?” Ty said to Peter with an evil grin.

    “You can’t shrink me anymore, Ty! I’ll be this big for a month, remember?” Peter said to Ty with a smirk.

    Ty thought about it for awhile.

    “I know! If I can’t shrink you two guys, then I’ll make you grow!” Ty said as he snapped his finger.

    Immediately Dustin began to grow back to his original size of about 6’1” feet. He then walked towards Peter to talk to him.

    Suddenly Dustin began to inflate like a balloon! The air pushed him off his feet, as his clothing now turned into his skin of rubber. His mouth was wide open and Ty wished for him to inflate like a giant helium balloon! Dustin’s arms became thick, along with his legs, while his torso and face became round like a giant balloon like you see in a float.

    Peter was laughing it up as Dustin inflated. Ty read his mind and realized that he also wanted to try that, so Ty repeated the wish for his cousin. Immediately Peter’s body began to inflate as well, ballooning up his torso and his chest. His face became round as his entire body became rubber. His mouth was opened wide as he began to suck in all the air, inflating himself like another helium balloon.

    Then Ty allowed both Dustin and Peter to float out the window.

    “Peter! Can’t you do anything? You also got the power! Do something!!” Dustin shouted at Peter as he floated right by him.

    “But why? I find this kinda cool!” Peter answered. Dustin, of course, didn’t expect that! “Let’s float around for awhile, and then I’ll make us deflate when we find a cool spot to land!”

    So Peter and Dustin floated off in the distance.

    “Over there! Let’s land over there!” Peter said.

    Peter then allowed himself and Dustin to float to this place he saw.

    “Are you having fun yet?” Peter wondered out loud, but Dustin kept quiet!
