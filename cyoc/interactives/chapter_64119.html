    sculpture.

    "This has gotta be a hoax," said Hank shaking his head.

    "Gentlemen, today's models are," the emcee said with a flourish, "On
    the big screen."

    Trav was already out of his seat and planning to leave when he saw
    Clay's image flash on the screen.

    "Whoa, that's Clay!" he said sitting down.

    Neil let out a nervous giggle.
