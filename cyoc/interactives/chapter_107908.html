    A not so giant tortoise

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 7 years ago A S

    After struggling haplessly for a few more moments, Christina eventually found her footing and flipped herself over onto her tortoise legs. However, her situation had barely improved, she was still a mute and slow moving reptile with absolutely no ability to fight against the twisted whims of her newly powerful 'friend'.

    Joe seemed pleased with the effects of his request, but Christina knew her troubles were only just beginning.

    "And to think you used to think you were too good for me" Joe laughed "Well now you're a fucking tortoise!"

    He considered Christina the tortoise for a few moments before seeming to settle on his next move.

    "You know, I think you make a pretty good tortoise. You'll probably want to stay like that for a while won't you?"

    Christina made a grunting noise.

    "Good, but I think you may be a little oversized for this place. Here, let me fix that for you!"

    Joe tapped on his phone again and Christina felt her body becoming smaller while mostly retaining it's shape. The changes were certainly less dramatic than before but just as definite as she felt the room becoming larger and the carpet moving closer to eye level. She guessed she couldn't have been much more than ten inches long by the time the changes stopped.

    "There, much more manageable" Joe smiled "But what now?"
