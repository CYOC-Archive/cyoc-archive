    Five years after Leticia and Jeremy's Wedding, Katie and Harry were not married, had no
    been married, and likely were not to be married for some time to come. The conflict had
    emerged from a simple truth, Harry loved being a husky, Katie while able to live as one,
    didn't want to give up her primate heritage just like that as many of the park guests
    apparently wanted to.

    They had managed to stay friends after Katie's contract was up, but Katie was surprised
    Harry chose to remain as a husky and was signing up for another contract, much as Katie
    was shocked at Harry signing another contract and staying an evolved doggie.

    Harry had grown to fully enjoy and even embrace the lack of dress the park guests and
    huskies enacted, to where clothing was more an effort than something normal. Katie on
    the other hand, was glade to be able to cover herself again (fur be damned).

    Like a double sided version of Chris and Krystal's relationship, both would regularly, and
    futilely attempt to entice the other to either lose the fur or find it again. Katie had no
    negative attitudes towards the evolved animals, not in the least, she understood these 'no
    longer humans' were still sentient people. But just wanted to stay the species she had
    spent her whole life getting used to, and didn't want her dresses to go to waste.

    What had stunned most was that the two had managed to -remain- friends! And still
    talked to each other as often as they could (some of the red ops style of management the
    park had used having decreased ever so slightly)

    With Katie having left the park, by order of seniority, Harry, a male, was now Naomi's
    second. Naomi was not amused by this, and Harry wasn't thrilled, but Jessica just didn't
    have the experience at the time to be Naomi's right arm.

    Jessica... it had been a shock to the pack to learn their petit superball had been in fact one
    of the Managers in another life. Most of all of course Jessica. When she had recovered her
    previous selves' memories a year later she described me as 'feeling like they belong to a
    different person.'

    All things considered, everyone was regretful to see the happy go lucky ping pong ball go
    north, taking a pack of new huskies with her for Transformation Park Canada.

    Zack attended her 'so long, good luck' party with The Twins. When it had been discovered
    that Zack been double dating them, The Twins couldn't have seemed to care less, and
    gladly accepted it. Meghan knew she was going to have a field day writing up a
    psychological report on those two!

    Harry and Katie meanwhile continued to simply wait on the other, fully convinced it was
    'just a phase' and they they'd come over to the other's side soon enough.
