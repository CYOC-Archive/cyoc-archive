    Emerald the Wonder Dragoness

    added 6 years ago A S TG O

    Now Otis 15 year old boy
    and his sister five year old Clara had recently lost their parents when
    the fishing dory their mom and dad had been in
    sank during a raging
    October storm.

    No bodies were recovered,nor was the sunken 40 foot dory ever located
    at the bottom of Mahones Bay.

    Now the previous Christmas her father had given Clara
    a chikdrens picture book about
    Emeral.Dragon Girl who Could Swallow
    all the Oceans..

    It was a picture book set in Arthurian times,and
    Emeral was the last dragon left in Ireland.\\Mainly because St Patrick couldn't
    figure out how to deal with a dragoness
    to whom mountains were mere pebbles.

    Emerald was one big girl.

    Naturally Otis had told his
    sister that if Emerald were around
    the Ocean wouldn't be drowning anyone else.

    Clara had given him a strange look,like
    she thought that was a good idea.

    Aunt Matilda had been awarded custody of the two kids and had
    promptly sold the family house to pay of the debts of her dead brother and
    sister in law.

    Otis could see that it had to happen
    but Clara had started daydreaming about Emerald the Dragon swallowing up the oceans so that
    she and Otis could run out there on the dry seabed and scoop up the gems from
    all those pirate ship wrecks.

    Let Aunt Matilda go trudging about in her
    rubber boots,oilskins and pail
    scooping up all those flopping fish.

    So it came pass that one day Clara was looking over her
    Emerald the Ocean Swallowing Dragon
    picture book and noticed that a stranger had replaced Merlin in all the
    pictures.

    The stranger was a old man with a grey beard and dressed up in some
    weird 1001 Nights
    outfit.

    The man in the book seemed to sneak little about in the picture
    everytime Clara looked away
    so Clara resolved to catch him in the act.

    She pretended she was getting sleepy and announced to the world
    and specifically that sneaky stranger in the book she'd been tired tired tired.

    So she pretended to go to sleep
    and in a flash openned her eyes
    and caught the old grey man in the act of hopping from one
    picture to them next.

    "Gottcha" she squealed delightedly

    And so she had

    The djinn sighed and asked her what
    her her five wishes were

    He reminded her bringing back the dead was not possible,so forget about the parents.

    1) a bag of candy
    2)new oilskins for Aunt Matilda
    3) a new Barbie doll
    4) The kid who'd bugged her last week in school would
    find a dozen toads in his desk on Monday

    Snap snap snap snap
    and four wishes were granted
    Now Otis was a bright brainy kid and used to play with his
    scientific calculator
    telling her how tall Emerald would need to be,weight,etc
    to do what the book claimed this wondrous dragon could
    do
    Swallow all the worlds oceans

    And then and there Clara realized her brother Otis
    was a natural to be Emerald.

    He'd practically measured the beaty inside and out,including
    her deepest innermost thoughts
    and desires.

    So wish 5 came

    Turn my brother Otis into Emeral the Ocean Swallowing
    Dragon Girl from this book

    The old grey guys beard paled somewhat
    at that 5th wish but all Clara said to the
    djinn's silence was
    "Wheres your Snap?"

    So the old man snaped
    his fingers
    and the 5th wish came to be

    Now the djinn was allowed a grace period of 14 days to follow
    through with a wish
    and he certainly applied
    that two weeks.

    Otis had immediately turned into a blue green
    dragoness and then she started getting bigger.

    But since we're talking a dragon who can swallow
    all the worlds oceans
    Otis-Emerald's idea of what
    "Big" meant wasn't nearly enough compared to how big
    she would become.

    So the djinn hastily rowed a dory in the middle of the harbour,where
    Otis-Emerald sat on her haunches,already towering a hundred
    feet above the water
    and after the djinn had got her attention
    he discussed matters
    with the worlds
    wonder of wonders

    Naturally
    Otis-Emerald wanted five wishes so she could undo
    Clara's fifth wish,but
    alas that wasn't possible.

    She could however get 5 wishes to make
    her new lifetyle more
    pleasant and less
    destructive.

    So by the end of the two week period Otis-Emerald
    could just barely manage to squeeze her
    draft horse sized
    body into Aunt Matilda's house.

    One thing Otis-Emerald couldn't
    shake away was the inner certainty
    that if she wanted
    to dry the Worlds Oceans dry
    she'd have no problem doing so.
