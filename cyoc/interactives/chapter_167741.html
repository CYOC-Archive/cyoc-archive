    Finally back to normal

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Dominoday 3 years ago TG Mental Reality alteration

    … toggled her own awareness to on before saving. As Tim watched, Jenny´s eyes went unfocused for a moment. Then, as soon as they went back to normal, she screamed in horror.

    “What have I done? Oh God, I´m so sorry Tim. That thing just let us fuck each other.” Her rant, mixed with apologies went on for another five minutes after she stopped screaming.

    Tim waited patiently. Now he would finally be released from this hell of a life. When Jenny was finally silent, breathing hard, he finally said something. “It´s okay, mistress. Finally, we can end this. I´m not angry at you, mistress.”

    Jenny looked up into Tim´s eyes, seeing the words were sincere. She let out a sigh in relieve. “Okay, thank you for not being angry with me. Now let´s see that all the changes made are undone and we can return to normal.” And with that, Jenny started to work on undoing them.

    First, Jenny turned herself back into Jacob, then went over all the changed settings, using the log to undo everything he had done to himself. When he was finished with that, he started on Tim´s profile. He turned the obedience bar off, changed his speech back and deleted his love interest in him. But then he stopped after saving.

    “Why did you stop, Jacob?” Tim asked, happy to not being forced to call him master anymore.

    “I wanted to ask if you wanted to keep anything that was changed for you, like the better cooking or something. I mean, I can understand if you don´t, but I must also say your cooking, even if it was forced, tasted heavenly.” Jacob explained.

    Tim thought about it a bit. “You know, when you changed that weeks ago, it was the only thing I thought was okay. And as much as I hated to work all these chores, at least the cooking wasn´t bad. Okay, let that stay as it is. And, as some have said I´m not as polite or truthful as they thought before, maybe set them lower again, but higher as they have been when this mess started. Maybe in the middle between old and new.”

    And so, Jacob did that. Soon, the only changes that remained behind were Tim´s raised politeness, truthfulness and his better cooking skills. Another peak Tim had wanted to keep when Jacob came to it was with the cleaning. While he hated cleaning and did normally just the bare minimum, he asked Jacob to let him keep that ability as well. Even if he didn´t like cleaning, maybe it would help being more efficient with it.

    “So, what do we do next?” Jacob asked, being eager still to use the program.
    “Please, just give me some time to get my life back to normal, Jacob. Even if only we remember it, the last two months were a pain to me. I will be honest, I had nearly no hope left of ever turning back. You hadn´t used the game anymore, and I couldn´t use it.” Tim explained with a sad look.

    Jacob took pity in that: “It´s okay, we can use it later. And I´m still sorry for all that shit I did to you. It´s no excuse I wasn´t aware of it. I ignored your protest before it started, and have only been an ass after that.”

    “No, it´s my fault as well. I made you a girl, and it was my fault you were unaware in the end. I realized I have been a prat when I was mostly trapped in my mind. I wasn´t angry at you from the first night of my predicament anymore.” Tim explained.

    After a few minutes in silence, Jacob closed the game and got up. “Then come, let´s go make something to have fun. After all, the last two months were no fun for you.” And with that, they left the room and house, deciding on going to watch a movie.
