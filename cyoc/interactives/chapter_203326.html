    "So what setting do you think they're going to use?" Timothy asked laying on his bed.

    Caitlyn shrugged from her bed across the room, "Not sure. I'd imagine not Mode 1. Going back to 17 wouldn't really make much of a difference."

    "Are you sure it was a good idea leaving it up to them?" he asked nervously.

    "We spent a month not being able to decide, so we don't really have much of a choice," she responded casually.

    Timothy and Caitlyn were always especially close even for twins. Which only made the end of high school extra bittersweet. The two were bound to be starting college on opposite sides of the country in the fall.

    And while some space was certainly not a bad thing it would be a big adjustment for the two who still shared a bedroom, though that was due more to the size of their parents' old apartment than anything else. When they moved last year they didn't feel like spliting into two bedrooms since they only had a year left before moving out. Besides their mom got more out of having a home office.

    Regardless with the big looming leap into adulthood at the end of the summer. The two wanted to take the summer in the opposite direction. Their parents' were dubious about spending money on the unbelievable device, but they also liked the idea of a "do over" summer.

    Strapped for cash when the twins were younger, they felt their kids missed out on a lot of activities they would have enjoyed. Now that they had the money for at least some of them, they were willing to give the device a shot.

    And so the night of graduation the two 18 year olds were laying in bed while their parents were waiting to set up the device. Fortunately the ceremony had been long enough that even Timothy's usual fretting couldn't keep the two awake for long.

    Once the two were asleep their parents entered and set the device to _____ before they slipped out quietly.
