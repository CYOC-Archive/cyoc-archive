    Immediately people came to investigate and after eye witness accounts confirmed what it was the military stepped in and formed a perimeter.

    At first, the military thought it was some secret Russian prototype till it was pointed out it had no obvious propulsion and was the size of an oil tanker, after it was determined that this was an alien ship of some sort was around the time Air Force 1 landed in LA.

    Both the President and the Secretary of State had arrived, the president wished to be there for first contact and brought the Secretary along on this potential first in the history of the earth; diplomatic relations with an alien species.

    An opening manifested on the underbelly of the vessel and a fire pole formed from this hole (and when I say manifested and formed I mean it, the video shows both coming from nothing) and down said pole was the first sight of these creatures.

    They were small, only around 3 feet tall with a spindly tube-like body a neon orange in color, their hands and feet each ending in three fingers/toes that end in suction cups, they stood on their toes, their legs being digitigrade, by contrast, their arms appeared to lack any joints at all, rather being tentacles with hands.

    Their head was teardrop-shaped with a rounded head with a small muzzle, their eyes were large proportional to their head and a solid blue lacking both pupils and sclera, on the top of their head was a pair of antenna, the creature had neither ears nor a nose. They had no neck, or one could say they had no shoulders.

    Aside from their mouth the only other opening they seemed to have was at the end of their tail, which connected seamlessly to their lube-like body which bent backward behind them as the only transition of the body to the tail, at the end of the tail was a small orifice.

    The creature was unarmed and also lacked any form of clothing (not that there was anything to hide) the creature introduced itself as Sen, and it’s species as the Kimasi, Sen explained that they were to represent the Kimasi race in the proceeding diplomacy that was to occur.

    The event was held later that day in a conference hall in LA, while the media wasn’t supposed to be there, the meeting was joined by several dozen reporters from every news station with the exception of FOX (who thought the whole thing was an elaborate hoax.)

    “We wish to share our advanced technology and our way of life with your people.” Said Sen at the conference, “With that in mind we have a proposal, you may study our ships and the data logs onboard, in exchange for letting the 50,000 Kimasi both on the ground and in orbit immigrate into your country, additionally, we wish for a patch of land upon which we may build a city and that this city be treated as an embassy, under your jurisdiction, but subject to Kimasi law.”

    After a bit of negotiation, it was decided that a chunk of federal land in the Nevada desert about twice the size of Vegas and LA combined would be handed over to the Kimasi to build their city, and the Secretary of State would provide all the necessary documents for the Kimasi immigration.

    After negotiations were over the media had many questions and Sen many answers, but one thing stuck out.

    “Yes, any human is open to join us in our city, so long as they are willing to follow our laws.” When asked how their laws differ Sen replied, “Our laws are pretty lax, in fact, we only have one, everyone must be biologically under 10 Kimasi years, or five of your Earth years.”

    Sen then explained that the Kimasi had advanced to the point of having absolute control over biology, using this power, they advanced at an unprecedented rate, till they found themselves with no jobs not already done by machines, since none of them needed to work, and they were all just playing games at this point anyway, they decided to regress themselves into children.

    When asked about violent crime, Sen explained that for them, death wasn’t a big deal, if you died then you would be resurrected at the hospital, death was more an annoyance then anything.

    After most realized they were essentially talking to a five-year-old, they decided against asking about sexual assault.

    That was two months ago, and Kimasi City has just finished construction and now open for both visitation and for permanent stay within its borders, although everyone who goes there as tourists generally decide on a permanent stay.

    Within the borders of Kimasi City, there is no concept of money, if you want something you just ask for it, the Kimasi themselves don’t have genders, as they explain it, they are all birthed from birthing pods and don’t require such things.

    All Kimasi go by very simple names: Di, Gi, Sen, Len, Fen, Ti, Len, etc, as it turns out, these are all single characters in the Kimasi language, as such, its not uncommon to meet two Kimasi with the same name, in these cases you identify them by their colors, such as Orange Sen and Green Sen.
