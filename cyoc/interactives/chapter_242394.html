The toy raccoon was again glad he turned it off. Matt carried the now turned off toy raccoon to his daughter's room & put it on the floor in a corner. He then left the room thinking yet again frustrated that Sophie yet again had left the toy raccoon on. And wondered again for a minute when & where did he get the toy raccoon.

The toy raccoon figured out only Sophie remembered it as human. And Sophie had learned more about her powers. She could turn herself into a living plastic toy animal too; & it worked just like it did with the toy raccoon. But Sophie could reverse it on herself & actually on others. Well not on the toy raccoon actually. Turns out after a month the transformation into a toy becomes permanent. The toy raccoon didn't care. He loved being a toy. So they when they neared the local playground with Sophie's father & saw many kids playing. Sophie placed a hand on her chest & stated "Rabbit toy." There was a poof & Sophie was gone. Now stood a 1 & half tall pink & white rabbit plastic anthromorphic toy with 3 toed paws & 4 digited hands. And nude of course; which was ok since it was NULL like all living plastic animal toys. Matt walked pass the playground leaving the now two toys behind. Matt again couldn't figure out why he got two living plastic anthromorphic animal toys. He didn't have any kids. So why he have these toys? Only the toy raccoon could remember Sophie now. Also strangely only the pink toy rabbit could make human vocalizations; speak in human. The toy raccoon guessed all that was unique to her being the fruit user. The 2 animal toys entered the playground & approached a group of kids. Theo a 6 years old boy begged "Please toy bunny make me toy lizard again." The pink rabbit placed a hand on Theo's chest asking "You want to not be Theo again?" "Yes. Make me not Theo again."
