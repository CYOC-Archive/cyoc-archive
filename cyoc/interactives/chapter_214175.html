    Bigger Baby Brother

    Instead of a photo of his brother sitting at his desk doing homework, the picture instead had his brother sitting on the floor, with Jake’s attention being drawn immediately to the fact that he was wearing nothing but a diaper. Jake’s eyes widened in shock, it was unmistakably his 17-year-old brother Luke wearing a diaper. “What the heck?” He muttered, looking closer at the image. Soon he noticed the rest of the photo containing his brother’s room, now resembling a nursery. The floor was a foam playmat, his bed was a large crib with a mobile, his band posters were gone, replaced by cute drawings of cartoons on the walls, and his anime figurines and books had vanished too. He could definitely see his brother on the floor playing with robot toys, like the ones he collected, though much more babyish, as well as a few plushies, miniature cars, and toy blocks he’d never seen before scattered on the mat.

    Jake couldn’t wrap his mind around it. There was no mistaking his brother there, but he had no clue how this photo could’ve turned out. Then he heard a “What do you want, squirt?” And looked up, suddenly even more surprised to see the exact image in the photograph. There Luke was, on his knees, playing with his toys on the carpet, with a thick, puffy white diaper around his waist and a confused expression on his face as he looked at him, then back to his toys. “W-What the heck?!” Jake yelped in confusion, “Luke, why are wearing a diaper?!”

    Luke seemed even more perplexed by this outburst, “What d’ya mean? You know I always wear ‘em.” He scratched his head with one of his toys. Jake couldn’t understand it. “What? But…” He watched as Luke continued banging his plastic robot toys together. Suddenly Luke let out a loud fart, and grunted, “Ugh, hang on a sec…” He scrunched his face up, sticking a thumb in his mouth as he got into a squatting position. Jake watched, increasingly freaked out, as Luke let out more farts, before finally the seat of Luke’s diaper began to tent and a foul smell filled the air. Jake couldn’t believe it, Luke was shitting himself in front of him, not even caring.

    The bottom of his diaper ballooned as he emptied his bowels, drooping heavily, before he finished with a relaxed sigh and another fart, followed by a soft hissing noise accompanying the front of his diaper becoming darker. With a grin, Luke sat back down, an audible squelch as he leaned into the mess he had made in his diaper, and casually picked up another toy and began to play with it as Jake stared, astonished. “Dude…” He said in bewilderment, “You just pooped your diaper!” Luke snorted, “Yeah, so? I always go poopie in my diaper.” Jake was completely confused. He’d just shit himself like it was normal. Jake then looked back down at the camera and photograph he held, examining it closer. Suddenly he noticed the inscription again. “Beware the camera…” He mumbled under his breath. Had the camera somehow done this to him? Was it really magic?

    “Look, dweeb, unless you’re here to change me, I’m kinda busy with something.” Luke said, snapping him out of his thoughts, as he became more enthusiastic as he picked up a teddy bear and rammed the other action figure he was holding against it. Jake couldn’t deny that something had happened and that this was extremely weird, but Luke didn’t seem phased by this, in fact he seemed to think that this really was normal for him. Jake turned his head to look at the family photos hanging outside his room along the wall, and saw every one of them featuring Luke had him in babyish clothing, shortalls, onesies, little sailor suits instead of formal wear, all with prominent diaper bulges, all despite him still being a teenager. It really had seemed that reality had been altered to make Luke an exceptionally immature 17-year-old.

    Then the last part registered in Jake’s mind, “Uhh, nope! Sorry for bothering you big bab-I mean bro.” He nervously said as he backed away from the room and walked down the hall, not wanting to have to change his older brother’s diaper. He could still smell it though, even walking back to his room. “Geez, Luke sure knows how to fill a diaper…” Jake muttered as he pinched his nose and walked back to his room, looking back down at the camera. Did it really have the power to change reality? He guessed so, he couldn’t deny what he just saw. The question was, if it could turn his big brother into a big baby, what else could it do?
