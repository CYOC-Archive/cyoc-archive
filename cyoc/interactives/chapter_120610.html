Inside, you find white ballet tights, pink pointe shoes, and a lacy pink strapless dress. The dress shimmers in the light, and looks very ornate, with bows and ribbons decorating the skirt. It even has a lacy design going up each side. Looking everything over, you wonder how you will squeeze into everything.

Your legs end in delicate little feet, which you reason are about size 6 or 7 in women's. You also notice that you have a severe lack of a bulge in between your legs. You run a hand in between your tights-clad legs, and find that it's completely smooth.

The dress fits very well, being snug enough, but not too tight. You see that your waist has slimmed down, and a pair of modest C-cups have sprouted on your chest. You see that you hips are slightly wider, and your arms and shoulders have slimmed down as well. Your hands have become smaller, and your fingers more slender and feminine.

Her blush is blended perfectly, and her plump lips are colored an attractive fuchsia color by lip gloss. She wears her perfect brunette hair in a bun. You then realize that this attractive woman that you're staring at is you. This makes you smile.

    Finally, you move on to the pointe shoes. You slip your feet in, and wrap the long ribbons all the way up to the middle of your thighs, before tying them up in delicate ribbons. You stand on the tips of your toes, finding that it doesn't actually hurt. You stand flat on your feet again, and leap into the air, twirl all the way around, and land on your toes.

You can't move your lips, and your eyes lock forward, meaning you now have to move your head to move your vision. Your hair becomes synthetic, and your makeup becomes painted on. You also shrink down to 5' 5".

    You blank out for a moment, and you look around. You gracefully stride out of the door on your toes, enjoying how it feels to walk on your toes. You look around, and find the cashier, and make your way over to her.

    "Well, you certainly look lovely." She comments.

    "Merci. I feel so very beautiful like this," You reply back in your french accent, "All that I want to do right now is dance."

    "Yes, everyone who chooses that costume says so, too. You can have it for free if you'd like."

    "Merci beaucoup. Now, I will go find somewhere that I can dance all that I want." You thank her before striding away on your toes to somewhere that you can dance at.
