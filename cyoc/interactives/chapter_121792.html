Her newer set was washing the lettuce while her original set was cutting the tomatoes. He was amazed that she’s adapting to the changes and never thought that she would accept it pretty quickly. It’s just the same as when she grew and became lanky, like it was all-natural to her.

    Jeff realized what he’s doing and tried to cover what he’s really doing. “It’s nothing mom. It’s just that you shouldn’t be cooking with your shirt off.”

    The mother smiled and said back, “Sorry about it. But my sweater wouldn’t fit me at all. I don’t know what I’m thinking wearing it. Having two sets of arms, you would think that there would be shirts with two sets of sleeves.”

    Hearing that, Jeff knew that her mind is affecting her as much as her body. He could tell she’s getting comfortable with the new changes that are happening on her and will continue to accept more of the changes as she continues to make dinner. Clearing his throat, he asked her, “You mind if I help you out?”

    His mother shrugged it off and said, “Naw… I’m fine. I can take care of all this myself. With all four of my arms, I can get things done quicker. Just continue to set the table. I got all of this taken care of.”

    Jeff sighed and returned to table, placing the napkins and silverware between the plates. He soon returns to get the cups from the cupboard. That gave him time to see his mother again to see any more changes.
