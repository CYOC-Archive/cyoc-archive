    It began when I made her clothes disappear

    Cathy reaches 17

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago O

    Years passed, with amusing consequences especially with a magic ring, I have chosen many wrong choices, but like Merlin I have found power can become distasteful especially when regarding love.
    When you can make anybody love you is it actually ever really love? A lesson well learnt especially in my teens when I made it through most celebrity A-listers, B- listers even Z- listers if they were pretty enough, hell news reporters, teachers, police officers, Librarians, prime ministers my libido knew no bounds.
    I have changed a whole bunch of strong men into young girls and teens ,reality was my curve ball and I knew how to throw it, but 10 years have passed, I was 17 now 27 years old. My former Aunt Cathy who had been 39 years old reduced to 7 years old was now 17. Her former life had faded within a year, I don't know exactly why, but I feel that our body's are much like funny shaped glass ,the mind moulds to the shape of the body, a 39 year old mind in a 7 year old body will essentially become an average 7 year old girl, she had been very bright at school and enjoyed her ballet and many other activities. Now at 17, she made her way through football team , the soccer team and the basket ball team, if you said she had a healthy appetite I might have to agree with you. She was gorgeous and her personality had been refined by her former husband becoming daddy, a new loving and caring step -mother and Sara had also become a loving big sister life was pretty great, my mom had becoming a loving Aunt too.
    My Mom had settled into a good relationship and I swear I hadn't used the ring to make it happen, the ring is still used especially for work, I considered wishing for riches and in a way I got it. I placed myself in a huge Corporate firm with an exceptional pay cheque and I didn't have to do much any day, but as I straighten my tie and comb my hair I eagerly await my usual fun and games, there is something amusing about putting, my colleagues into diapers and having them ask me to change them through thumb firmly planted in mouth when ever they need it. Bathroom breaks were mostly a thing of the past the women at work and with a magic ring at my disposal even the male workers spent their day female, before being returned to normal to go home to their wife and children. There was something amusing about turning a fat balding 50 year old male into a 20 year old women for my amusement and easier on the eye too.
    There have been times when I may have been cruel. I have made some women indulge their inner child while they were at home, I never see it but there is something strangely satisfying about making some one give up men and spending their evening watching toons and playing with dollies I occasionally buy them a new toy something I know they won't resist playing with, clothing taste had reverted to more juvenile types they would spend their free days in pinafores and dungarees and pink would loom large in their choices, I expect men would either find their choices unattractive or these women were somehow a bit simple either way a man with a ring is a powerful thing...
