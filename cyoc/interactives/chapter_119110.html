    ConjoinVerse--new meaning to "close friends"

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago BM O

    Ryan and Bradley had come into this universe from one where it was natural for a person to have only one head; it was amazing to think that in just a few hours, they had gone into a universe where family members are conjoined siblings, and that they had become conjoined with their dimensional counterparts. Now they looked like a four-headed boy, clothes and all. And to think that they were about to see their friends in this universe...

    It was weird for Ryan and Bradley to see their neighbors in this universe; each body had the same basic construct as any that could be found back in their own universe, but the torsos were significantly wider here, and each body had at least two heads on its broad shoulders. Sidewalks were wider here for wide bodies, and houses appeared significantly different as well, particularly doors and stairs. Cars, bikes, scooters, skateboards, trucks, motorcycles--any vehicle was wider as well, due to wider bodies.

    The four-headed body did not have to walk much. Ryan and Bradley lived near a park in their own universe, which is where they would often hang out with their friends; sure enough, that was the case here as well. There were ConjoinVerse Ryan and Bradley's friends, and--obviously--shared bodies with conjoined siblings. Ryan and Bradley could recognize several of their friends--Leo, Donnie, Ralph, Mikey, Will, Mark, Max, Zack, Ty, Luke and Kevin--while their ConjoinVerse counterparts pointed out who the other heads were. Leo, Donnie, Ralph, and Mikey were all brothers, and apparently quadruplets--they shared a body with their four heads on top. Will had a second head here--his name was Bill. Kevin had two extra heads--Allan in the middle and Dean. Ty had three heads as well--himself, Dustin, and Peter. Mark, Max, and Zack were brothers--here they were conjoined brothers. Luke had two new heads--Brian and Nick.

    Six bodies, eighteen heads--for Ryan and Bradley, that was an amazing sight. And these were the ConjoinVerse counterparts of their friends; imagine how THEY would feel upon seeing that THEIR Ryan and Bradley had become conjoined with dimensional counterparts! They certainly would be surprised to find that THEIR Ryan and Bradley now had a wider body, as well as four heads!

    Ryan and Bradley, along with their counterparts, walked up to ConjoinVerse Ryan and Bradley's friends.
