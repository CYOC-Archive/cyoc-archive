    Alibaba was genuinely surprised that worked. Not the three lionesses, but Leon himself, the only way they could have possibly worked was if it was what his master actually factually wanted. Daisy, April, and May, who were the three little lionesses, had now been Leon's friends since they were in diapers. Their old lives wiped away and now they were beginning anew.

    While Alibaba had turned Leon into a lion simply as a flexing of his magic, and had aged him to a older lionoid purely so he wouldn't have to put up with his childish panicking, the genie couldn't help but admit that this was indeed elevating his boredom.

    Most westerner didn't know that genies being trapped in bottles was to be an exciting and awe inspiring thing, beings of their power, confined to such a little container, and their power at the request of an unexceptional mortal... now they thought genies being trapped in bottles and serving mortals was a genie's natural state!

    Alibaba knew that if every genie granted the wishes of their master as intended the world would be overpopulated with all powerful rulers within a month. This culture want the west had built for itself had no other logical conclusion.

    That being said, there was no actual law on Alibaba not granting wishes that effected things on a global scale, as long as they were in line with what his master actually factually wanted, or a twisted skewering of the language they expressed their wish in.

    Little Leon and the lionesses skipped into the Fun Zone, his family having a account there (or they did now thanks to Alibaba), the little lion and his little entourage didn't nee to pay their way in.

    "Have a nice day Kerry," Leon waved the man behind the counter as the little pack went inside to have fun. Kerry waved, as he did golden fur sprouted all over his body, and his tail emerged, followed by hands and feet becoming digitgrated and thumbed paws, and his face extended into a lion's muzzle. Everything he was wearing vanished except for his employee ID that now hung around his neck. No one paying the naked lion man any mind for the four lion children playing inside.

    The ultimate irony was that genies were allowed to use their magic in ways outside of wishes as 'demos' and if they were indeed something their master actually factually genuinely wanted, rather than the genie twisting the language of the wish into something ironic and cruel, but since genies simply wanted to make whatever mortal held their bottle's life as miserable as possible, it didn't exactly turn up much.

    Alibaba also gave Kerry back several years of his life, and none found the idea of the little lion boy working there as odd, the lion boy himself simply seeing it as one more game to play.
