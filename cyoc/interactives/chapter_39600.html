    The four arm thing is not actually as good a deal as it sounds. Coordinating four arms in combat so that your moves don't interfere with each other takes training, and Roberta wasn't quite there yet.

    Which was good from Jasmine's point of view, as she was finding the Shiva vixen more formidable than she had first anticipated. The thing's regenerative capacity was frightening, given the combination of kitsune magic and the original Tommy's modern anatomical knowledge. An army of these things with modern weapons and kitsune powers would be a serious problem.

    But angels are formidable themselves, and Jasmine's blade wove a steel net of defense. Her focus was unwavering enough that she did not even divert energy to regenerate her ear.

It is not in the nature of the kitsune not to interfere.

Of course, there was the question of who to try to trip. Jasmine was really no fun at all, and Roberta was on some level a sister. Nonetheless Akane was on the same team as Jasmine, and Roberta would hopefully be saved from her current fate as Tamthis's slave if Jasmine defeated her. And if Roberta turned her head, she would see the unconscious body of Kyriel, and things could get very bad if that happened. (Akane noticed that Jasmine was trying to lead the vixen guard away from Kyriel.)
