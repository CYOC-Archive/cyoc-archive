    Max left his house, and walked across the street to Luke's house. The

    "Hi" Max responded. Luke motioned for Max to come in side.

    Luke shut the door behind Max. Max rubbed his hands together for
    warmth. "So..." Luke began.

    "Yeah..." Max replied. The two of them stood in awkward silence. They
    ran ideas in their heads on how to express their feelings, but neither
    came up with anything good. After several minutes, they both began
    speaking at the same time. "Luke Max I wanted you should know I..."

    nervously.

    said, "Luke...I have something to tell you...I...I love you."

    Luke returned the smile, as his face turned into another shade of red.
    "Max...I...love you too."

    Simultaneously, they wrapped their arms around each other in a tight
    embrace. Max and Luke just held onto each other. Neither one wanted to
    let go first. In the excitement, they fused with each other. Their
    hearts were now, literally, one.

    After several more minutes, Luke and Max partially unfused, so that
    just their chests were joined. They gazed into each other's eyes, so
    happy to be able to express their feelings.

    After that, they played around a little on the couch. But, they
    couldn't do it for too long. By now, it was 6:45. They had to leave
    for school now, or face detention.

    So, they quickly cleaned up, and headed out for Luke's car. As Luke
    pulled away from the curb, Max had the strangest feeling that he
