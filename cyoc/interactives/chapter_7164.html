    Ryan knew it would be best to get close to Bob. Two are always more than one! But in spite of that, he decided to go left, as every role player does. He said: "We have to take a risk and discover some more hexes, because our task is to find the map. And how can we find it in a short time, if we run in each others tracks. I go left."

    He tipped, and the display said: "You have to make a Perception role in this thick forest, to get not lost. Roll 4 or equal to."

    Bob looked at his roommate and said: "This was no good idea, pal, wasn´t it?" Ryan waved his hand. "No risk, no fun." He rolled a 4. "See, my old friend? Its done!" The display showed: "You found a secret place. There is a huge stone lying on the woods ground. A sword sticks in! What do you do? 1) Turn it out 2) Leave it alone."

    "Hm, it reminds me on the Artus legend. But am i blue-blooded?"

    Tobi gripped his cards, and wanted to play, but the display showed instantly: "Trapped players are not allowed to play cards." "What a pity...", said the hanging guy. But then, he had an idea: He placed a card in front of him, commenting: "I put out my changing card for this level." Ryan laughed: "Really clever, Tobi, thats cool. You tricked the game! I take it. He exchanged the card with one of his hand cards. It took instantly effect:

    He turned the card and it said: MULTIPLE a picture showed a prince on a horse, and the text said: Gain Blue Blood, Enhance Appearance, Arrogance, Add riding skills.

    Dina, who sat next to Ryan, touched his growing and shining hair with her webbed fingers. He was too beautiful. All the others, but especially the women, were looking at him. Ryans clothes became the wealthy ones of a prince in a fairytale, His hair now shoulder length, and his nice tan and winning smile made him gorgeous. He said with a distanced voice: "I take the sword." The board spit out a card. Lissy touched his strong arm with a dreaming look in her eyes. But Ryan answered: "Would you mind taking your unwashed fingers from my arm!"

    That was not his normal personality, he was a generous, witty guy, with respect of women. But he was much more adorable from his outer appearance now. Lissy gulped down the answer. She couldn´t tell why, but she had the need to serve him, and shut her mouth.

    She rolled, and it was a 3. She went to Tobis Token, and released him from the trap. The rope let him down from the lamp, and he thanked Lissy a lot. But she had still one move left.

    "I think, for the freeing, you could be so generous, Tobi, and play a Perception card on me!" Dina shouted: "Are you stupid? You want to be transformed without the need of it? You are totally nuts!" Lissy answered: It´s much better to gain perception now, than struggling in a mess, and suffer a lot more of unwilling transformations." Dina shook her head, the floppy rabbit ears shaking, and her look said: Lissy is mentally sick.

    Tobi looked at Lissys eyes, and said: "Ok, i choose a nice card to you. I think it´s the strongest i have. Are you ready?"

    Lissy gave him a trusting look, and Tobi played...
