    Making a False Love into True Love.

    I spotted a couple that held up their hands excitedly, Debbie and Sean. They were a sophomore couple both at 16 years old. Debbie is 5’7” tall, hazel eyes, hell of a great figure with surprisingly b-cup bust, wide hips, and brown hair in a pony tail. While Sean is 5’9” tall, green eyes, muscular torso, and short blonde hair. They make such a cute couple with Debbie dress up as The Greek goddess Artemis and Sean dress up a Viking; if it wasn’t for a fact that they were faking it.

    They grew up together, practically knew each other since they were in diapers and it was expected that they would fall in love and go out together. However, there was one snag in everyone’s perception of the two. Sean is gay while Debbie is a lesbian which was sad because everyone including me think they would make such a good couple if they were only attracted to each other. They just pretend to be a couple because everyone thinks that’s what’s expected of them, including their parents.

    Well thankfully, I have a trick that could fix that.

    “Sean and Debbie please come on stage.” I announce and the audience erupted into applause as the two people got up of their seats and headed to the stage. As they did, I made a couple silent wishes and snapped my fingers. Two boxes popped up on stage which was on their side they have three little fold up tabs on the front of them. They finally reached the stage.

    “Welcome on stage you two.” I said as I shook their hand.

    “Good to be here.” Said Sean his hand shaking me the longest.

    “So what is this ‘Open minded to walk a mile in each other shoes’?” Ask Debbie looking at me with curiosity. I just smiled as I went to the boxes and on the sides facing outwards, I opened up doors.

    “In all honesty I thought that what I said would’ve been a clue.” I said, earning the chuckle from the crowd. “ However, it seems in this case it’s best to show you please come here.” I said, pointing at Debbie in motion for her to come to me. She did as I ask, and when she got to me, I motion to step in the box.

    She did and I close the door behind her I then went over to the other box. I motion for Sean to follow and we repeat with Sean entering the box then me closing the door. Finally I move between the boxes to address the audience.

    “Now these three little bars are actually tabs that when unfolded will tell the gender, body type, and preference of the individual in box prefers. Let’s start with Debbie.” I explained as I went over to Debbie‘s box. I unfolded the top tab showing that it was pink with the symbol for female. I then unfolded the middle tab revealing the words Busty & Curvy. Finally I unfolded the bottom tab; but everyone gasped in shock as the top didn’t say Boys or Boys & Girls, instead it said Girls. I turned to acting confused then turn and addressed the audience.

    “Well that was unexpected. Huh, I wonder what Sean’s say.” I said, as I went over the Sean’s box. I unfolded the top tab showing that it was blue with the symbol for male. I then unfolded the middle tab revealing the words Tall and Muscular. Finally I unfolded the bottom tab; revealing that instead of Girls or Boys & Girls, it said Boys.

    “Well it seems we had a couple of gays pretending to be straight.” I said, causing the first out laughing. “Okay…okay…calm down. We can’t really blame the two on who they like so let’s switch things up with that.” Without the audience stop laughing as I got to work. I took off the top tabs of the boxes, switched them around, and placed them on the other boxes. I then proceeded to do the same with the middle tabs of the boxes. So now tabs on Sean’s box from top to bottom now said pink with the symbol for female, Busty & Curvy, and Boys; while Debbie’s box says blue with the symbol for male, Tall and Muscular, and Girls.

    “What?” I asked as I got between the boxes as if I was answering the question of why I didn’t move around the bottom taps. “Have to least keep one thing of theirs, the same. So let’s see what they look like now.” I said, as I open up the doors on the sides facing each other. Walking out from Sean’s box came a 5‘7“ tall woman, with green eyes, very busty and curvy figure, and brown hair; dress as Freya from the God of War series. Walking out of Debbie’s box came a 5‘9“tall man with hazel eyes dress as Kratos from God of War; granted he didn’t go all the way by making his upper body and head white as well as leaving his blonde hair but he got the tattoos accurate.

    The two men in the middle and they looked each other up 7 down, sporting the heavy blush no doubt admiring how they looked one another.

    “I take it you approve of your new looks?” I asked him and they turned to address me.

    “Yes.” Said the man formally known as Debbie giving their best Christopher Judge impression.

    “Well these could be permanent if you like?” I said both of them nodded to me as all three of us turned towards the audience.

    “Give it up for Devon and Selena.” I announced and audience erupted into applause. Devon and Selena went back into the audience towards their seats, holding hands. Unlike most times I’ve seen them these seem to be natural and not force. As the applause died down, I clap my hands together to address the audience.

    “Now is everyone having fun?” I asked, and the audience cheered. “Good because we’re only at the halfway point. Now who wants to volunteer for my next trick?”

    As soon as I ask that a lot of hands shut up.

    Who did I pick?
