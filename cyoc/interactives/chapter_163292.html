    Edwin and Mark made their way down through the lobby and out the back doors to the beach front, signs helpfully pointing them in the right direction. At the adult beach, there was a muscular man in a black tee sitting at the gate, eyeing people who passed by him. He stopped a few particular young looking twenty somethings, checking something before waving them through, but still Edwin felt his heart race a little. They were just sixteen, they were going to get turned away. But as they got closer, the man just looked them up and down and nodded.

    He sighed, this place must take age transformation into account, not caring how someone became an adult, just that they were. People lounged on the beach, some frolicked in the water and many congregated about the bar, but Edwin and Mark kept right on to a tall barrier that separated the nude beach from the adult beach. Another bouncer sat at the gate and this time he did stop them.

    "Room keys." He said gruffly, and both Edwin and Mark produced them. He gave them a quick scan with a pocket card reader he had with him. By now Edwin was sure they were going to get busted. "Mr. Dias, Mr. Wilson, enjoy your stay." He handed back the cards and waved them through.

    Mark was practically bouncing on his toes as he stepped onto the beach. It was the smallest of the three beaches, but still had all the amenities, rentable cabanas, a fully stocked bar, and plenty of sand and waves. The only difference was everyone but the newest arrivals or those on their way out were walking around completely nude. And it was a sight.

    A good portion of the visitors looked like normal people, though many were in ridiculously good shape. There were some who had some animal features mixed in with their human ones, in fact there were some animals lounging on the beach, from the animal wing no doubt. Others.. well.. they'd pass easily with clothes on, but apparently the gender wing was offering more than a swap between male and female. There were women with cocks, men with pussies, and both with.. well.. both. Edwin didn't know where to look, Mark seemed to be trying to look everywhere at once.

    "Hey! That cabana is open, let's snag it." Mark was already rushing over to the cloth roofed structure with its two lounge chairs, and Edwin shrugged and followed. Almost as soon as they arrived a hotel staff member was over in a blink. "Good morning! If you'd like to rent this spot, I just need your room number." She was clothed and wore a nametag, which made her stand out on this beach.

    "Room 453." The woman nodded. "Can I start you off with any cocktails? We have both standard varieties and transformation cocktails."

    "Transformation cocktails?" Mark echoed.. "What like what the rooms do?"

    The woman chuckled and shook her head. "The rooms are equipped to do more large scale changes, these cocktails just give small tweaks." She picked up a menu from the table and handed it to Mark. Edwin leaned in to read it over his shoulder, it listed the options and the effects. Edwin found himself reading some aloud. "Bronze Blast, gives you an instant tan. Love Crush, opens your mind to new romantic possibilities. Protein Shot, add a little muscle in a jiffy. Sandy Slim, lose that spare tire in a flash..." There were over twenty cocktails, and indeed, all were just little tweaks but could be fun to try. "Bartender's pick?"

    The woman smiled, "For those who want to live a little dangerously, our bartender will whip you up one of the drinks at random. Of course, guests are limited to two transformation cocktails a day, more can make things.. complicated."

    Edwin finally nodded and Mark seemed to make up his mind as well.
