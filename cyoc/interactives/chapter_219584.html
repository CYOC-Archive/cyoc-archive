    Trying an experiment together

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 months ago AR BM

    Bethany decided to call all of them because she believed that it was always better when the four of them work together as a team.

    So Bethany took out her cell phone and sent them the following message: "Maribeth, Macy, Betty, I need you to come to the lab urgently. It's something important."

    Some time passed, and the three girls finally arrived.

    "What happened, Bethany?" Maribeth asked.

    "Oh, just recently out of simple curiosity I was looking to see if there was something interesting to try. And in one of the cabinets, I found this thing", Bethany explained as she proceeded to take something out of a box.

    The three girls were confused when they saw what was inside the box. It seemed to be some kind of laser gun, almost like the ones they'd normally see in a sci-fi movie.

    "What is that?" Macy asked Bethany.

    "I don't remember very well. I'm sure it was one of the first experiments I created but ended up abandoning," Bethany told them.

    "I think I know what it is. No wait......forget it, I don't know either", Betty said. The rest of the girls simply rolled their eyes at Betty's response.

    "So, why are you showing it to us?" Macy asked Bethany again.

    "I thought it would be a good idea to find out what this thing does," Bethany told her.

    "But do you remember how it works?" Maribeth asked Bethany.

    "Sure. I just have to press the button where it says "On" and this will activate."

    Just then, Bethany proceeded to press the "On" button on the right side of the gun and it soon began to emit a strange pink glow. But before Bethany could pull the trigger, Betty finally remembered what the gun was for.

    "Wait Bethany, I finally remember what it was for!" Betty exclaimed trying to warn her but it was too late because Bethany ended up pulling the trigger.

    In that instant, the laser gun released an extremely fast pink beam that began to bounce all over the entire lab until it eventually ended up hitting the four girls.

    When the beam stopped, the four of them were no longer the same, now they had transformed into.....
