    She keeps you under mind control

    Ariella is decent enough to you, but she never releases you from her mind control. At first you're worried she'll treat you like an outright slave, but it's not so bad. She just makes you her boyfriend without even telling you about it. You go move in with her and start to live an everyday life, or as much as you can in this crazy new world.

    She's sweet and pretty cute, so all told it's not that bad a life. When walking with Ariella in public, you see men crawling on their knees on a leash like a dog, men physically branded by their owners like cattle, and you hear rumors of much worse.

    Ariella, on the other hand, is pretty nice. The biggest difference for you is that everything in your relationship is slightly skewed in her favor. You're pretty sure you're doing much more than 50% of the housework, and when she orders you to fuck her, you have to whether you're in the mood or not. And while she makes you go down on her every time, she rarely reciprocates.

    She also takes you to the secret meetings of the society to free men. Given how much more often the women speak than their men, you get the impression Ariella's not the only one whose views on male slavery don't quite line up with her actions. Nonetheless, they all seem to be sincerely plotting a revolution.
