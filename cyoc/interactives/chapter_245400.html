    Lisa reluctantly transformed. "Back to my dull, boring body," she said.

    "Your normal body is beautiful," Ty said. "I know you enjoy the transformations, but don't sell your normal form short."

    "Thank you," said Lisa. "I know you like my body; you had transformed your own body to be mostly a copy of mine when I first met you. But it does seem pretty mundane, compared to some of the other forms I've had. I'm eager to spend more time in my aquarium form, and I wish I could use that form even in public."

    "Well, maybe after Diego talks to Maria, you'll at least be able to use that form around your house, rather than just in our bedrooms."

    "I hope so. Say, Ty," Lisa asked. "Do you think your parents would be OK with my being in aquarium form in their house?"

    "I don't know," said Ty. "I'll have to ask. But they certainly have become much more accustomed to transformations, so they might be OK with it. By the way, I forgot to mention before. June is making chicken parmesan for dinner tonight, and as usual, you're invited. If you don't have other plans, we'll play board games too."

    "That sounds great," Lisa said. "Thank June for me, and I'll let my parents know." They finished preparing, and left for school.

    The school day passed uneventfully. They met in the cafeteria at lunchtime, and Lisa complained again to Ty about wanting her new aquarium form. "Geeze, Lisa, give it a rest already," Ty said, smiling. "I love that form, too, but not being able to be in our preferred forms in school, or public, is nothing new. In only a few more hours you'll be home, and then you can do that, at least in our bedrooms."

    "Well, at least I have something to look forward to," Lisa said. She kissed him as they left the cafeteria for their separate classes.

    When Dustin and Ty got home from school, June was in the living room. Dustin went to play video games. "So you're still ahead on work?" Ty asked June.

    "Yes, though I'll need to do the app thing again soon," June said. "Although I didn't have any issues last time, I'd still like to coordinate with you, just in case."

    "That's fine, Mom! I certainly wouldn't want anything to happen to you. Unless you feel that you want me closer, just text me when you're about to start. Let me know where you are, and how long you expect to stay in app form. Then text me again when you're done. If I don't get that second text by the time you expected to be done, I'll know to check up on you."

    "Thank you, Ty," June said.

    "Lisa plans to join us for dinner and games tonight, and thanks you for the invitation. She and I have a question, though. Mom, how would you and Dad feel about Lisa and I being in the house, in your, and Dustin's, view, but with noticeable transformations?"

    "I suppose that depends on the transformations," June said. "I mean, if they're overtly sexual, like... you know the ones..., then please don't."

    "No, not those transformations," Ty said. "We know better than that. But, for example, Lisa just asked me to do a new transformation to her this morning, and she really likes it. She knows she can't have that transformation in school, or in public, but she's hoping to at least be able to keep it in our houses, and not just our bedrooms. She's already shown Diego, and asked him to talk to Maria about being transformed in their house."

    "Well, I think you'd have to tell me the specifics, before I could offer a yes or no. And I'd probably have to discuss it with Mark."

    Ty explained Lisa's aquarium transformation to June. Her eyes grew wide. "That sounds absolutely amazing!" she said. "I don't have any issue with it, but I definitely will have to check with Mark. But I want to see her in that form, even if we ultimately decide that we don't want her that way outside your bedrooms. But if we do allow it, I think she'd better get used us staring at her."

    Ty said, "I think she might not mind the staring. The fact that she wants to be out of the bedroom with it makes me wonder if she has exhibitionist tendencies. But I'll warn her about that. Or better yet, why don't you warn her? Why don't we go see her now?"

    "Oh, I'd love to," said June, so the two of them went up to his bedroom.
