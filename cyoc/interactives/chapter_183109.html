    The next morning, John wakes up early and gets himself ready, he then heads straight for school. Arriving there he notices he is the first to arrive, he decides to wait at the gates for his friends to arrive.

    “Hey John,” he suddenly hears a voice, “you are early for once.”

    John turns his head and sees his nerd-friend Kiyoshi walking towards him. Kiyoshi is a slim 18-year-old boy who is crazy about aliens and anything paranormal. “Hey Kiyoshi,” John says, “any luck with your last project,” he asks with a grin.

    Kiyoshi hangs his head, “nah, it was a waste of time,” he says, “no matter how many crop circles I've made, no spaceship has appeared.”

    “Haha... maybe next time,” John says, “I just hope the farmer won't figure out who'd destroyed his crops.”

    “Booo,” Kiyoshi says, “it is all for science, so I don't have to worry about anything.”

    “If you say so,” John says with a grin.

    “KIYOSHIIIIIIIIIIII,” a voice suddenly shouts.

    John and Kiyoshi turn, Kiyoshi turns pale as he sees a small girl running towards him, “oh... HELL NO,” he says and he starts running at a fast pace.

    John just stands there, looking after his friend, laughing his head off, he then turns to the girl, “hello Zoey,” he says.

    “Hu,” she asks, blinking as if coming out from a daze, “oh, hi John. I am looking for Kiyoshi.” Zoey is a 18-year-old girl who is pretty small for her age, making her look as if she were 12-years-old. She has long blond hair, which is tied back into a ponytail, she has big green eyes and A-cup-sized breasts.

    “Yeah, you just missed him,” John says.

    Zoey sighs, “why does he always runs away,” she asks, her head low, “all I want is to talk to and get to know him,” she adds saddly.

    'I feel so sorry for Zoey,' John thinks to himself, 'Kiyoshi is such an idiot, he is always flirting with girls and wonders why he gets beaten up. Zoey is such a pretty girl, even a blind bat can tell that she is in love with the goof.' John looks at the girl, he wants to help her so badly, he then remembers the device and an idea pops into his head. “Zoey,” he starts, “I might be able to help you get close to Kiyoshi.”

    Zoey's head shoots up at this, “REALLY?”

    John nods, smiling, “come with me,” he says, “I want to show you something in private.”

    Zoey curiously follows John behind the school building where no body can see them, “what is it you want to show me,” Zoey then asks.

    John pulls out the device, “this,” he says.

    Zoey tilts her head, “what? Your phone,” she asks, “how do you think your phone will be able to help me getting closer to my crush?”

    John grins, “this is not a phone,” he says, “it is called the “Chronivac”, here let me show you.” The teen opens the camera-app and scans Zoey.

    “WHAT THE...,” Zoey shouts, jumping back as a red beam of light hits her, “wh-what are you doing to me?!”

    “Relax, I just scanned you to get your data,” John says, “here, have a look.”

    Zoey cautiously walks over and look at the screen, her eyes widen, “woah... it really shows... everything about me,” she says amazed. She looks up at John, “what else can this thing do,” she asks.

    “I have a way to give you the information without telling it to you, it would take ages,” the boy says. John points the device at himself and presses a button, he feels a slight tingling in his head, then he points the device at Zoey and presses a other button.

    Zoey blinks as she feels a tingling in her head, her eyes then widen, “woah... what did you do? I suddenly know everything what that device can do.”

    “I gave you my knowledge about the device,” John says, smiling, “that way we can both think of a plan how to get you closer to Kiyoshi.”

    “Hmmm...,” Zoey says, “I know, you could transform me into you, I could pretend to be you and get to know Kiyoshi better.”

    “But Zoey, there can't be two of me running around,” John says.

    “Well, you could transform into me,” Zoey says, “it would be only for a day,” she adds.

    John shakes his head, “no, I don't want to be a girl,” he says, “there has to be a better way.”

    “I think this is the best way,” Zoey argues, “you said, you'd help me.”

    “And I will,” John says, “but I don't want to be a girl.”

    “Well, then how do you suppose we should do it,” she asks.

    “I could alter your mind,” John says, “I'd give you knowledge on to how to talk to Kiyoshi in a way which would not scare him off.”

    “Hmmm... I don't like that idea,” Zoey says, “I don't find it cool if someone messes with my mind. I think us changing into each other is the best idea.”

    “I said no, Zoey,” John says, “we don't have to come up with an idea now. We can wai...,” John is unable to finish his sentence as Zoey suddenly dashes forward and snatches the device out of his hand. “ZOEY,” John shouts, “GIVE THAT BACK!”
