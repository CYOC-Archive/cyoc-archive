Though that didn't mean that snakes were completely discarded.

    ---

    Because of Chrissy and Marcella's little brouhaha, there was a new debate about distributing the Animalia Virus, though it was later determined that Marcy had obtained her sample of the virus through subterfuge while her mother was doing volunteer work with Animalia, so the only real solution for now was to better secure the samples.

However, there were some who took the phrase "new year, new me" a little too far and got their conversions on New Year's Eve/Day. This resulted in a few funny things such as new features (wings, tails, horns, etc.) bumping into things, getting caught in doors, or similar accidents.
