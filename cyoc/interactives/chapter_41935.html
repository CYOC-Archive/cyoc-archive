    Desert Foxes: Epilogue Akira's Surprise for Jen

    Jen looked at the necklace Akira had given her. She rejected becoming a fox boy because she wasn't sure she wanted to be a boy. Plus her mom and dad probably wouldn't like it as much. She also rejected becoming a Kitsune since she wanted to be more like her parents. That just left magical fox girl and the mystery purple facet. She should go with the safe option of the magical fox girl but she was curious about what the purple facet would turn her into. "You won't tell me what the purple side turns me into?" she asked.

    "I'm a Kitsune" Akira said. "I play games on people for fun. I have to play with you a little bit. If you weren't family I wouldn't have told you what each of the other facets did or that you wouldn't be able to change again."

    Jen giggled and said "I like games."

    "So which do you choose?" Akira asked.

    Jen looked into the purple facet and said "This is me." Immediately she felt her body tingling. She watched in fascination as fur grew on her arms. Her finger nails became short claws. Her feet lengthen and she stands on her toes which become paws with her toenails becoming short claws. She felt her face push out into a muzzle and her ears migrated to the top of her head. She felt a pressure building above her butt and two tails burst forth and lifted her dress up. She blushed a little bit when she realized that her panties were on display.

    "That's easy enough to get around" Akira said evidently guessing why Jen was blushing. A puff of smoke and Jen's dress had a hole for her tails. "You'll have to get dresses with tail holes now but that shouldn't be a problem. I've already re-tailored the rest of your dresses as well as your one piece swimsuits to accommodate your tails."

    "I turned into a fox girl" Jen said happy that she was like her parents now.

    "Not just a fox girl" Akira said with a smile.

    "What do you mean?" Jen asked.

    "Look at the necklace and say what comes to your mind" Akira said still smiling.

    Jen looked at the necklace wondering what Akira had meant. She did say the first thing that popped into her head. "Mystic Girl Power!"

    Jen underwent another transformation. Her muzzle disappeared as well as her fur. Her feet transformed back into human feet and the short claws she had became her finger and toenails again. There was another pressure building up above he butt and a third tail appeared. Her dress also underwent a transformation. It fused with her panties and changed to a pink color. Pink knee high boots appeared as well as pink elbow length gloves. The necklace had disappeared during the transformation but she didn't notice. She quickly looked into a mirror and was surprised by how she looked. She was indeed cuter than she had been but it was subtle.

    "How do you like it?" Akira asked.

    "I like it alot" Jen said.

    "Good" Akira said. "You're a magical girl now. Your fox girl form is what you will normally be and you won't have any powers in that form. In this form you do have power and it will grow as you grow. Remember what I said, you are not to go after robbers with guns. That would be a misuse of your power."

    "I'll remember" Jen said. "How do I change back?"

    "You know how already" Akira said.

    Jen nodded and said "Hidden Power!" The transformation reversed itself and Jen was soon standing in her fox girl form with the necklace around her neck. "Can I still recruit my friends to be magical girls?"

    "Of course" Akira said. "Though they'll remain what they were before. That means they'll remain human unless they transform if they were human to begin with. Of course you don't have to limit yourself to recruiting girls. You can recruit boys to though they'll be transformed into magical girls when they transform." Akira said this with a mischievous grin and twinkle in his eye.

    "That would be funny" Jen said. "Do I have to tell them what they'll become?"

    "Where would the fun be in that?" Akira asked with a smile. "The necklace will only transform your friends so you'll know who will stick by you if they're transformed."

    The door opened and Charlene came in saying "I thought I heard you Akira."

    "Well I had to come by to see you" Akira said. "Now that people know about the mystical world I figured I'd offer my niece the opportunity to be like you."

    "I see that" Charlene said. "Though what else have you done?"

    "Well Jen is a magical girl too" Akira said. "I also made it so she can recruit her friends if she wants."

    "A little chaos with your gift?" Charlene asked.

    "I am a Kitsune" Akira said. "If I didn't have a little chaos thrown in I'd go sane. Don't worry, it won't hurt anyone."

    "You still haven't answered my question from yesterday" Jen suddenly said. "How many kits are you going to have?"

    Akira looked at Charlene and asked "You're pregnant?"

    "Yes I am" Charlene said.

    "Congratulations!" Akira said sincerely. "So how many are you going to have?"

    "Well that's what we went to find out" Charlene said. "It's a little early to be sure but the doctor said it will probably be one or maybe two. He had said that with a natural pregnancy the number of nipples determines how many babies a mammal can have. Since I only have two nipples I shouldn't have more than three kits at a time."

    "Well you better tell me when you have your kit" Akira said.

    "You know I will" Charlene said.

    "I need to get back to my village now" Akira said. "Use your power wisely Jen and people may tell stories about you."

    "I will" Jen promised.

    Akira nodded and in a swirl of leaves vanished. Charlene looked at her daughter and said "Well I guess we should go out and celebrate the fact that I'm pregnant and your transformation." Charlene hugged her daughter. She loved her no matter what form she was in. She just wanted her to be happy and she seemed to be now that she was a fox girl too.
