    A Man To Become A Feral Black Footed Ferret For A Breeding Program

    Adam had replied to the online ad for people to become Feral Black Footed Ferrets to help repopulate the endangered species. He arrived at the building where he saw 4 other people. He was handed paperwork & told to read it through & then sign it if he agreed. The paperwork stated that he agreed to let them transform him into a Feral Black Footed Ferret. That he agreed to be bred as a Feral Black Footed Ferret. That he agreed to be the property of the zoo giving up all his human rights. That he won't be able to speak in human ever again, only make animal sounds. That he agreed to let them choose what gender he'd be as a Feral Black Footed Ferret. Adam gladly signed the paperwork. He gave it back to a staff member & they left with it. Adam while he waited decided to ask the others "You excited as me about becoming a Feral Black Footed Ferret?" No one answered. Soon Adam was called & he got up & went off with one of the staff members. He was lead into a plain room with only 3 things he saw in it. An open metal animal cage, a door on the wall marked 'Trash Chute' & an intercom on the wall. He was given a shot in the arm & told this would start his transformation into a Feral Black Footed Ferret in about 8 minutes. The staff then left shutting the door. Almost instantly a male voice came over the intercom instructing him to completely strip naked & place all clothing, shoes & personal items in the trash chute. They will go down it to chute to the incinerator to be completely incinerated. And lastly after he finishes becoming a feral black footed ferret he is to get in the cage. It will snap shut trapping him for transport. Then over intercom the voice began to say "Your gender as a feral black footed ferret will be..." Adam yelled out cutting the man off telling him "Don't tell me what gender I'm be as a feral black footed ferret. I don't want to know ahead of time. I want to discover that as I change." The man replied "Ok I won't tell you." Adam after that he began stripping naked. Soon he was naked & deposited all his clothes, shoes & personal items in the trash chute as instructed. It quickly struck him he'd never be wearing anything ever again the rest of his life. Adam now noticed the camera on the wall up near the ceiling. He would of covered up his nudity but didn't. He realized it didn't matter as he was going to be naked the rest of his life. And besides it wasn't like they were being pervy or anything. They were just monitoring his transformation into a feral black footed ferret. Soon Adam felt & heard his body starting to transform. Surprisingly it didn't hurt at all. "I'm transforming now." Adam announced happy.
