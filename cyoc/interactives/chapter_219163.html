    Meanwhile, back at the mall, Jessica generated a few more sentences to see how the first change was turning out.

    (This horror fan is busy making his passion project.)

    (This aspiring filmmaker hopes to get a better camera soon.)

    (This young man is losing track of time and may not make it to his girlfriend's fashion show.)

    Jessica raised an eyebrow. "Well, what did I expect? At least, he's got responsibilities now. But let's get him more in the thick of things."

    She generated another sentence.

    (During filming, this dedicated student director admires the movie's leading lady.)

    "That'll work. And admire THIS while you're at it, you two-timer."

    With a barely suppressed giggle, Jessica changed "admires" to "plays."

    (During filming, this dedicated student director plays the leading lady.)

    Will got close to Tanya, focusing on her face as defiance finally edged out some of the fear. Now backed into a corner, she had no choice but to fight, but is it too little, too late?

    Whipping the camera around to catch a glimpse of the slasher swinging the ax down, he turned back to-

    FLASH

    -grab the oncoming ax in both hands, letting out a girlish shriek as she stopped the blade from cutting her head in two. The attack still sent her falling backwards onto her shapely rear, and the two wrestled over the weapon for a while.

    Then, the slasher wrenched the ax out of her grip and brought the blade down in a wide arc, making sure to strike the ground far away from either side of her head as she pretended to dodge. They could always edit in a closeup shot of the blade "narrowly" missing her later. Then, the slasher lifted his ax one more time, preparing to give her the killing blow.

    The camera pushed itself close to her face as she gave it her best wide-eyed look of terror.

    "Aaaaand cut! Good work, Wren."

    Wren relaxed and smiled at Tanya as the girl lowered the camera. "Okay, let's see how that turned out."

    Tanya flipped the camera's screen to review the footage as both Wren and the "slasher" also huddled in to see.

    After a quick review, Wren nodded with satisfaction. "It's perfect! Let's wrap it up quick. I have to get over to the mall before the fashion show ends."

    At the mall, Jessica finished putting on her outfit for the next category. There was still a few minutes to go, so she checked the app again, generating another sentence.

    (Wren is done filming and is now hurrying to see Jessica in the fashion show.)

    Jessica arched an eyebrow. "So, we still have a connection somehow. I wonder what it is?"
