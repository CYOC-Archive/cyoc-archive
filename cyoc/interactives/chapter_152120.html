    Carded

    added by AnarchyNinetyNine 3 years ago I

    Mika waits patiently. His apprehension becoming clearer. Was this actually just a set up designed by a spiteful internet troll? He is shivering and terrified. He really can't go out in public like this, naked. He'd be arrested on the spot.

    Mika starts to notice a warm, burning sensation on his back. His hand automatically reaches behind only to feel nothing unusual about his skin. "Cold must be affecting my nerves," Mika reassures himself. A sense of nausea suddenly overcomes Mika, he feels like he's going to throw up his insides. Then it happens. Mika feels the acid run up his esophagus, into his mouth and through his lips. He screams in pain, this acid is unlike anything he's ever felt before. It feels like it's properly dissolving his body.

    Mika keeps retching for a few minutes. He is terrified and tired, no one should vomit this long continuously.

    A sharp pain hits Mika's spine. He screams in pain, causing the constant stream of vomit to bubble up and fly everywhere. Even onto his face. This pain then reverberates from his spine like a wave in all directions. Mika suddenly feels feint, and his vision goes blurry. There is another sudden pain which causes his body to convulse. Then his vision goes black and he passes out.

    Mika regains consciousness, looks down and groans. He attempts to move but finds himself unable to do so. He moves his head up to see a gigantic window of light above him. His face then involuntarily stiffens. Leaving it with a fearful expression.

    Mikas's eyes notice the white light give way into the scene of a factory. He looks ahead to find rows of trading cards in front of him. Mika then realised what's happened. He's been turned into a trading card.

    Mika is helpless as he is transported across the gigantic factory. He is placed onto a table. Suddenly he sees a now gigantic Asian man grap him, place him on top of a stack of cards. He then hears himself being placed into a box. His world goes pitch black.

    Now Mika is left wondering what card he is, and where he'll end up.
