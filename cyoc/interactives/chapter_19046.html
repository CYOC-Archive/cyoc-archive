And if he was lucky enough to not live his life as a beta.

    But being able to procreate with vulpine women, not to mention feline and even equine women? That sounded hot. Human sex might even be interesting.

    Tyler went to the Chronivac and typed in a command: "Please list species options under Sexual Orientation menu."

    It took a moment, as it appeared that the program was loading up new software, but then a list of species came up. His current one of LUPINE was already checked, but he clicked the mouse and added VULPINE, EQUINE, and FELINE, then clicked the "Mental and Physical" box.

    He typed in another request: Please check Alpha Male boxes, mental and physical, for appropriate menus.

    The Chronivac clicked and whirred, then reported: Operation complete. Other options?

    Tyler typed: Please add 'human' under 'sexual orientation' menu as well.

    Warning: Human mental/physical makeup differs from Furry. Estrus cycle more frequent but less pronounced, male pheremones similarly different. Admixture not fully researched. If proceeding, clients are requested to fill out consumer feedback forms. Continue Y/N?

    Tyler clicked Y, then went and clicked the APPLY CHANGES button.

    There was a whirr from the Chronivac and screens flashed up one after the other, most of them something called a CHIMERA.

    The first thing that Tyler noticed that he was growing, but then that made sense, since Equines were larger than almost every Furry, and Felines ranged from small Cats to huge Tigers and even the rare Sabretooth, like his friend Bob, who was terribly embarrassed about being a throwback and something of an outcast because of it. Plus alphas were bigger than other wolves as well.

    The next thing he noticed was that his hair was growing, both on his head and on his tail, turning red, as did all the fur on his body, save his arm which began striping black as his fingers grew tiger claws, while his his feet changed into hooves. And he grew larger and more powerful, tearing out of his clothes, while his shoulders broadened and his chest hair thinned until at last he stood there, a chimera.

    He inspected himself. The different parts were mostly blended and shaded. He'd gotten mostly the fur of a lupine, especially the tail with its white tip, while his legs and cock had become equine. His torso had apparently become human, especially the chest, though thankfully with some hair coverage, while his arms had become like those of a tiger, with lovely red bands and much sharper claws. But his face was still recognizably his same old lupine head, with only two changes, one attractive, one odd. The attractive one was that his fur had now become as red as the average fox, which was rare for a wolf, and the odd one was that the fur on the top of his head had grown into a mane somewhere between a lion and a human, over a foot of luxuriant blond mane, the same color as his original fur.

    However, Tyler felt incredibly handsome and virile, since he was an Alpha Chimera, producing pheremones that would send any female wolf, cat, fox, horse/donkey or human into heat after just a few hours exposure. And with the human blend, he was suddenly just becoming turned on by himself, because he was one sexy chimera.
