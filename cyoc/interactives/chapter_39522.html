    You looked at the sky when you opened your eyes that morning.

    There were still a couple of lost stars in the morning sky to be detected.

    Feeling the warm and wet fur against the soft waving grass you decided to just lay down a bit longer.

    As a human, the dew would have made you really sick but now it felt wonderful.

    You look besides you and see the genie, changed in a otter girl as well was sleeping innocently.

    Droplets of water dropping from his whiskers.

    You cuddled closer to the genie and tried to sleep again.

    It was not until noon you awoke again and noticed the genie had woken up and just was stretching his body.

    He was blocking the sunlight from you. The genie turned around and smiled.

    “Slept well miss?” the otter girl answered politely.

    “Wonderful” you answered as you stood up as well.

    You stretched your body and shook the dew from your body.

    Making the genie wet, which he didn’t mind that much at all.

    “So miss, what you want to do today?” he asked curiously at you.

    “Not sure, something fun” you say as you looked around you and saw the clearly magical looking forest.

    “Something fun eh?” he said cheerful.

    He looked over to the magical forest as well.

    “Ever wondered to be a forest nymph?” the genie asked.

    “A forest nymph?” you ask with big eyes. Heck, you heard of them but still.

    “Yes, a forest nymph. Beautiful creatures I must say”

    “Well, if you say so” you answer.

    “is that a wish?”

    “yeah” you reply and await his magic to occur.

    “excellent” the genie twirled around and directly your fur vanished, although your paws remained in the same shape.

    You ears were also still otterish.

    Your skin turned a very light green and your hair a light blue.

    That wasn’t everything. Gills appeared at the sides of your neck.

    For a moment you gasp for air but then you breath normally again.

    Your tail vanished, to your dismay as you seemed to have liked the tail.

    You look yourself over in wonder, you definitely looked like a gorgeous little nymph.

    What do you do now?
