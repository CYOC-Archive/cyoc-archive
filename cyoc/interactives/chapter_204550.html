    Rick spoke to them. ‘You have done really well girls. I’m pretty sure Mohammed was impressed. I know I was. I think my deal is going to happen. Jack couldn’t believe the sadness in Rick’s eyes as he said this. It must have been strong spirits he had been drinking.

    It wasn’t long before Mohammed and the dancing girl returned. Mohammed beckoned to Rick. ‘Come to my office. Let’s make this deal’ Rick left the room with him.

    The dancing girl was carrying a fancy decanter and glasses on a tray which she placed on the dining table and invited Jack and Jacky across. She poured drinks including one for herself. ‘This will make you….. recover’. The drinks were gratefully accepted. The white drink was very sweet. ‘It is Sobia’

    The girl said to Jack ‘I am very impressed. I could not do that dance in shoes like those’ Jack glanced down and realised she had been wearing the heels the whole time she had been dancing. That medallion was amazing.
    …

    In Mohammed’s office he opened a safe and extracted a wad of notes. ‘You have the girl’s passport?’ He queried.

    Rick handed over Sharon’s passport. ‘She will be looked after won’t she? We have our differences but she has been my companion for many years’

    ‘She will have an important role in my father’s household. She is exactly what he is after' Mohammed glanced at the passport '41 year’s old, talented, a natural blonde and as you have assured me submissive and obedient. She will do nicely’ He started counting wads of notes. 'Here you are $300000 US dollars as we agreed' He handed over to Rick a wad of notes.

    Rick accepted the money. Holding it in front of his eyes not believing them to be real but this was just pocket money to a guy like Mohammed. Sharon was effectively sold. He would miss the old girl but it was time for an upgrade.

    But Mohammed was still counting out notes. ‘Here is another half million. It is yours for the young girl. She is very attractive. I would take her for myself’

    Rick was stunned ‘No Jacky was not part of the deal’ but his eyes remained on the cash in Mohammed’s hands.

    ‘Perhaps if I make it an extra million for the girl. But that will be my final offer’

    ‘Er…’ Rick had been blindsided. A million dollars for Jacky? He had only known the girl for a week. He was not really emotionally attached to her. She was a stunner but she was rather forthright. Not as submissive as Sharon, she had lorded it over Sharon since she had arrived. No doubt soon she would be ordering Rick around soon. She could be Mohammed’s problem. With $1.3 million Rick wouldn’t have any problems finding himself another girl.

    ‘I don’t have her passport. I wasn’t expecting this. I don’t even know if she has a passport’

    ‘That is no matter, such things can be arranged. $1 million is my final offer, take it or leave it’

    Rick found himself accepting the deal, shaking hands with the Arab and accepting the huge wad of cash. The girls would no doubt be upset but money was money. And it was in Rick’s hands. With Mohammed’s money neither would ever want for anything in life.

    He had been looking forward to a farewell fuck with Sharon. That had been quite a performance in the cabin. No harm trying ‘Any chance I could say farewell to the girls?’

    Mohammed simply replied ‘No. I will have one of my guards escort you back to your vehicle’ With a click of his fingers Mohammed summoned a man carrying a machine gun.

    A final hand shake meant Rick was dismissed. He found himself walking back along the jetty accompanied by a guard rather than the two beautiful companions he had arrived with. At least he had a bag with $1.3 million in their place.
