His tail being so flexible that didn’t matter but it still made some sense.

He could feel the top his head hitting the very end of end of the tank while his tail actually had to bend a bit to fit in the tank. It was an extremely long tail. The view outside was horrible but good enough to see the figures come in, clean off the tank before it started to move. He couldn’t make out much beyond a certain point but he did see a few people getting into tanks that still looked mostly human.
