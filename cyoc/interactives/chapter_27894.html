    The angelic fox-girl Tabra raced through the hallways, desperately searching for Tammia before anything else could happen. Fortunately, the link between Tabra's body and Tammia had not COMPLETELY severed; as a result, Tammia could sense Tammia somewhat and, using the knowledge that only an ancient being like an angel would have, conceal her own from being detected by Tammia. The floor suddenly shook, and the abruptness of the vibration caused Tabra to lose her balance. "Damn" Janice said, "it's still difficult to move around in this body. " She looks up, noticing that where the hallway once was there was now a solid wall, "Not to mention that this evershifting castle is a pain in the..."

    "Agreed," Tabra interrupted, "But save that anger for Karther. We need to save Tammia as well; she just like us and the concubines are just victims in Karther's now-destroyed plan."

    "I wish it would be that easy..."

    The jolt that Tabra had was just enough for Tammia to sense her location for a second. "Ah, so that's where you were.... but what happened to your body? It feels different..." Tammia shrugs her shoulders. "Oh dear, we'll have to take a small detour my lord. I sense my other body nearby." She sways towards one of the walls and with a wave of her paw turns it into another hallway. She gestures to Karther to follow and Karther, still under the double-dose of magic that was hurled at him earlier, obediently followed. As she walked down the hallway, her keen ears picked up the sounds of the now-de-brainwashed concubines dashing around to find an exit. She smiled. While she was still around, those fools wouldn't find their way out ever. She would figure out what to do with them later. Until then...

    She gestured to two of the male fox-guards standing nearby, instantly altering them into four-tailed, female versions of themselves with light-golden-brown eyes. "Why don't you go keep those concubines company?" Tammia says playfully, and the new bodyguards silently dash off in the direction of the concubines. However, before Tammia could turn back around and continue her search, she heard a loud crashing from the wall to her left as Tabra shattered it and dashed at her. Before Tabra could land a strike, Tammia used the tiles in the floor to stop the angel-fox's momentum. Tabra jumped out of the way before the remains of the wall behind her could shackle her in place.

    "I'm so glad that you are still alive!" Tammia cheerfully says to the angelic fox-girl.

    "Tammia, this ends now. I will free you of the grasp that Karther..."

    "Tsk tsk tsk. What are you talking about? I have ALWAYS been subservient to my lord. Well, that does not matter."

    Tammia sended out a blast of magic at Tabra. In a second Tabra was back to being a clone of Tammia.... but her wings were still on her back. The wings shone with holy light and the transformation reverted. "That trick will not work twice on me!"

    Tammia frowned. "Well, if that would not work, then I will just have to find another source of power, won't I?" She quickly used the castle to bind Tabra's arms and legs in a flash. It would at least give Tammia enough time to do what she was thinking...

    The fox-queen strides up to Karther and gently places her hand on Karther's chest. Karther groans, his voice going up in octave. His body becomes more curvaceous and the chest starts to mold into one that was the same as Tammia. All that was left was the eyes and mind...

    Something in the fox-queen suddenly raised a red-flag. Karther was lucky; he had at least successfully melded a command into Tammia's mind to never try and intentionally harm him or his existence (although nothing was said about mind-manipulation). Tammia shook her head; what was she doing!? She was trying to turn her lord into her, essentially erasing his existence! She quickly reverted Karther back to the way he was. "Sorry for that my lord. However, I will need to borrow your power. I promise to give it back, ok?"

    Karther, who really had no say in the matter, nodded his head.

    Just as Tabra broke free from the bindings, she was temporarily blinded by a bright light. As her sight recovered, she saw Karther, weakened and exhausted, slumping on the opposite wall. Next to him was Tammia, who had gone through another, but not as significant transformation. Her body had become the epitome of femininity, being as realistically curvaceous without becoming extreme. Her eyes were now jade green, brimming with magic, and she was surrounded by a small aura of power. Her clothes had changed as well, not being that of a royal concubine as before, but that truly representing her as the Queen: A silk chest rap with silky-smooth, loose pants, and all of this partially covered by a beautiful, pearl-white shawl. "Well now," She says, power echoing in her voice, "now I should be able to defeat you. I WILL make you me again; you were too fun to pass up!"

    "We'll see about that" Janice/Tabra said, and got into her battle stance. The true battle now started....
