    Madeline pulled out the wand to look it closer. It was periwinkle and topped with white crystal. The crystal was clear and white, and shaped like wings of a bird. Madeline shook it unconsciously, not knowing what it was doing, and in an instant, numerous feathers appeared from its crystal and warped her like a cocoon.

    Feeling like standing in the wind, she changed to bird and wind themed magical girl. The clothes she was wearing was vanished. She got younger to look like a "girl", to age about late teens. Her new costume appeared to replace her vanished clothes. Soon the feathers disappeared and let her look herself.

    "Eh, what was it? Huh?" She said in little confusion, and noticed slight change of her voice. She felt something foreign, so rushed to the bathroom and looked at herself in the mirror. Most outstanding thing was a skirt looks like made with numerous white feathers. Also there was short shirt on her, with two big white feathered wings at behind. The wings were obviously on her short shirt, but they could be moved like parts of her body.

    Her height decreased to make her like a girl. She had long straight and periwinkle color hair. The color of her hair was very fit with the color of the wand, also her eyes were same color. Her skin got little darker to contrast with her white costume.
