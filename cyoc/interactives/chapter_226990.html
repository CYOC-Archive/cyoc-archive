    "This is incredible," Keiko gasped, admiring her youthful figure. She was in the prime of her life once more. "Lisa I can't believe this! Just look at me."

    "Oh trust me I know all about this kind of thing," she sighed. "I used to be 16 before this all started, now look at me." Keiko glanced at the sullen 8 year old, unable to fathom that this child was once a teenager. "Lisa I'm sorry that this happened to you but I don't think I can help you," she said. "I could get in a significant amount of trouble by having a personal relationship with a student."

    "I was afraid you were going to say that," Lisa sighed. Taking a step back from Keiko she now stared directly into her teachers eyes. "Look at your driver's license."

    Keiko raised an eyebrow, finding the child's question odd. Rummaging through her purse, she now extracted her license. It struck her as odd that the license had changed as she grew younger. The picture was of her as she currently was listing a birthday that made her 31 years 6 months and 25 days old. "My license changed," she said. "It says I'm 31 years old now."

    "Keep looking at it," Lisa stated. "You'll notice something else." Keiko now cast Lisa an odd look, her eyes growing wide in surprise as the day now changed. "Wait it's changing again!"

    "I know," Lisa stated. Keiko now studied the license carefully, slowly it dawned on her what was happening. "Wait I'm still getting younger?!"

    "Yes you are," Lisa stated. "So you see, you need to get the Reset Watch to help yourself. The longer you wait, the younger you become. Eventually you won't be old enough to teach, then you won't be old enough to drive, and on and on until you're sitting right next to me in this class. At least for awhile before even this class becomes too old for you."

    "You wouldn't," Keiko exclaimed. Lisa now scowled, glaring at the teacher with all the ferocity her 8 year old features could muster. "I would," she said. "I can't stay like this I need to be a teenager again."

    "And what if I get the watch and use it on myself?"

    "Do you really think I won't notice," Lisa asked. "And if you try to come after me then you'll find yourself getting alot younger, alot quicker." Lisa sighed, she didn't like going this route but her options were limited. She needed a Reset Watch and this was the only person who could help.

    "Look," Lisa exclaimed. "I won't make you any younger than eight but I really need your help. Please Mrs. Daniel's, for both of us. Find the Reset Watch."

    Keiko bit her lip, anxiously looking at her license before staring at the 8 year old who was blackmailing her. She could either find the watch or go back to third grade. What choice did she have?
