As she continued to wallow in despair she felt her phone vibrate. Curiously she pulled it out of her pocket and glanced at the text message smiling. “Katie good news,” she squealed. “Julia says they found the ring. She wants us to come back upstairs.” Katie sighed with relief. “That’s great who found it?” Claudia scrolled down the text message, her face turning pale as she now looked at Katie. “Ummmm your aunt.”

Lisa frowned giving Katie as stern a stare as her 10 year old features could muster.

    As the two now began to argue Claudia stepped forward sheepishly. “Ummm Ms. Lisa, Ms. Kumiko,” she muttered. “This wasn’t Katie’s fault. It was mine I kinda tackled Katie and took the ring from her.”

    Lisa frowned at Claudia as she folded her hands across her chest. “Well That was dumb,” she muttered. Lisa sighed as she now looked at Claudia. “Ugh sorry Claudia I’m kinda acting like a brat. Being this young you start acting like your this age for real, especially if your angry.”

    “So how long have you been able to do this Mika,” Kumiko asked as she raced past 11 and became 12. Mika fidgeted slightly as she looked at her preteen aunt. “I don’t know a little while.”

    Kumiko frowned as she glared at her niece. “Well I hope your careful,” she said. “If Lisa hadn’t found me I could of done some serious damage with that thing.” Lisa turned to Kumiko as they both became 13. “If I hadn’t found you I wouldn’t be reliving puberty....AGAIN.”

    “Wait Kumiko found the ring!” Kellie exclaimed as she watched the two girls reentered high school. They both nodded as they now became 16 like their nieces.

    “Yeah I was looking for you because I wanted to tell you something Mika,” Kumiko said. “I’ve been working on your parents and they may actually be willing to take you back in.”

    Mika nodded. “Oh,” she muttered. “That’s um great.”

    “But,” Kumiko stated becoming 17. “Here’s the thing. Even though they may be willing to take you back I was really hoping you would keep staying with me.”

    Mika’s eyes bulged as she looked at her teenage aunt. “Really!?!”

    Kumiko nodded shyly as she looked at her niece. “Well yeah,” she said. “I know it’s only been a couple of weeks but I really love having you around. I never realized how quiet things were at my place, you....you kind of bring a lot of joy into my world.”

    Mika stood speechless as she gaped at her aunt. Unable to answer she instead threw herself onto her aunt giving her a tight hug. “Thank you,” she whispered. Kumiko looked dumbfounded at her niece before smiling. “So I take it that’s a yes,” Mika nodded happily, hugging her aunt once again.

    As the three women continued to age they talked happily. Katie and her friends explained the ring to Kumiko, Julia, Cole and Derrick. The group listened attentively, understanding that while it was fun to use they needed to be very careful with it. As they got older Julia noticed her brother Cole staring at Kumiko.

    “Cole,” a now 19 year old Julia exclaimed. Cole jumped startled by his sister. “Geez Julia what!”

    Julia furrowed her brow as she looked at him critically. “I see you ogling Kumiko don’t tell me your crushing on her.”

    Cole shrugged as he looked at the 24 year old Kumiko. “I don’t know maybe,” he shrugged. Julia shook her head as she pushed her larger brother. “Well don’t just sit their like a lump go to talk to her.”

    Cole didn’t need any more prodding as he now began to strike up a conversation with Kumiko. As the group watched they could see that the two seemed to be hitting it off rather well as they exchanged pleasantries. Lisa and Katie smiled as they watched.

    “Well good for Kumi,” Lisa smiled. Katie looked at her 25 year old aunt in confusion. “What do you mean mom,” she asked. Lisa continued to smile as she looked at her niece. “Kumiko has been single for way too long and there are far worse things she could do than dating Captain America over there.”

    Katie nodded as 25 year old Cole began to flirt with a 26 year old Kumiko. “Hope the age difference isn’t any issue when Kumiko stops growing up.” Lisa looked at her niece laughing. “If there’s one thing I’ve learned recently it’s that age is just a number.”
