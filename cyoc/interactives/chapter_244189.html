    Faun Paunchus, Big Softy

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover Yesterday A AR TG O Mythological Magic Male to female

    As she ran her errands, Andi came across something that made her pause. It was Rycksyn, but she barely recognised him. She had heard he'd been pushed indeed but she had not paid attention to the details, what with the organisation of the wedding. She'd heard of a newcomer in the village too. She'd seen him from afar. He was a faun, meaning he had less animalistic characteristics than satyrs. It was a general rule. His name was Paunchus and he deserved his name. He had a big tubby belly, though his arms sported lots of muscles. He was considered a big softy, and was already vastly appreciated. He was looking for a male mate, rumors held. But at the moment, that big softy Paunchus held a baby in his arms. A baby dressed in blue and pink ands lots of frills, with a bonnet and bib. It looked more like a live doll than a true baby! And... Andi could not believe it! The baby doll was... Rycksyn! Andi gaped. He must have been regressed and offered to Paunchus. She approached the faun, not knowing what to really say.
    "Er... Um... Hello?" she said a bit timidly.
    "Hello!" smiled widely the faun.
    He noticed her belly and children and congratulated Andi for her stated and large family. Andi thanked him.
    "I wish I had the same," the faun sighed. "Hopefully I'll get a mate soon, a nice male too. We can adopt, like I did for. this one. Your council of elders were very nice, they allowed me to treat him like the perfect adorable baby. He did bad things recently. But it's so fun to have a baby at last! I hope it will show a male I can be a great mate."
    Andi wished him so and left. She saw that Rycksyn had human-like feet and sported less hair. He'd become a faun too! A baby faun! And he seemed unhappy at his fate, which was perfect in her eyes. And Paunchus would treat Rycksyn like a cutesy doll, which was great to humiliate the awful satyr. She felt happy at the thought, especially when Rycksyn cried as Paunchus forced a bottle into his mouth and told him he'd lull him to sleep in the rocking-chair afterwards, and threatening him with lots of tickles.
