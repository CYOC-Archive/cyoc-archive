    Jeff looked to the Age Time Travel option on the Chronivac and was curious as to what to do with it. His first thought was to go back in time in a younger age, but he had been through his past and the last thing he wanted was to make a mistake that would alter his future. Just then, he had an idea. “Hmm… The future… That’s it!” he said with excitement, “If I don’t want to go back in time, I can head down to the future to see what my life will be like. It will be perfect!” Going back to the Chronivac, he started to get to work on his trip to the future.

    First is what year he would be in. He didn’t want to go far ahead just to be an old man, he felt like he want to be at a time when he was still an adult with little aging look. So thinking of his options, he came up with one. Giving that the year is 2015 and he’s sixteen, he could go ahead fifteen years into the future, making it 2030 and him being thirty-one. That way, he won’t be too old or too young, it would be just right. He even checked on all of the other options as well. He decided on mentally aging himself as well, just so when he’s at the future, his mind would have the knowledge necessary in surviving it. Then he checked to see if he would return back and wanted to figure out how long the transformation will last. See an additional options, he checked the ‘Manual go back’, as he knew that it meant he could use the Chronivac again to return to his normal year and age incase he doesn’t like his future. Otherwise, if he liked it, he would stay the way he was and enjoys his new future life. Once he got all of the options together, he did a recheck and was pleased with the results. Clicking on the ‘begin’ button, a warning message popped up in front of him. It reads…

    ‘Warning: By going to the future, there is a chance that your computer containing the Chronivac will vanish if you are transported to a future location. If it disappears, you will be stuck in the future time period forever. Are you sure you want to continue.’

    Reading that, you know that it doesn’t sound too good that your computer will be gone with the Chronivac and you will never see it again. But the message still said that there’s a chance that the computer will be gone, after all, maybe his computer will alter into something more futuristic and the Chronivac will be there. So taking that risk, he clicked on the ‘yes’ icon as the Chronivac started to work it’s magic on Jeff and the world around him.

    As the changes started, he waited to see what happened. Looking to his clock, he noticed that the hour and minute was steadily increasing as day turned into night in a matter of seconds. Suddenly, the hands on the clock moved fast as the calendar next his desk started to X themselves out. Judging by how quick it’s getting, his guess is that hours are turning into seconds while months have now become minutes. Meaning in twelve minutes, a year would soon pass. He looked to his room and was amazed that things are appearing and disappearing in a fast pace. Turning to the window of his room, he could see that day and night whiz by so fast as people and cars moved in light speed. Yet for some reason, you start to move normally as you walk around while the world around you changes like a time-lapse video going in fast forward.

    Curious about yourself, you turned to the full-length mirror to see your clothes changing. They were flashing from his regular attire to his pajamas, to being naked for his private time. Jeff also saw himself growing as well as his body was aging through his teens. His height has increased a bit while his muscles increased by tone and definition. Jeff smirked to himself and was pleased that he looked a little buff then before. He also noticed that his hair kept on changing as styles shifted bit by bit and his hair grew and shorten from time to time during the many visits at the hair salon. During that time, he could feel his brain filled with knowledge and memories as the mental aging kicked in.

    Already, the year is 2017 as Jeff aged two years of his life. His room changed a little more as there are things that look a little futuristic. There was even a diploma on the shelf over his desk showing a high school diploma as a graduation gown flashed on him in front of the mirror. “This is so cool!” he said as his voice lowered down to make himself sounds like a young adult. He kept on watching as his body and world kept going through the years, wondering what he’s going to be in 2030.
