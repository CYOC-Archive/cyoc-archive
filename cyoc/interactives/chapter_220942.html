    Lef happily strode down the countryside of royal kingdom, his smile as bright as the day. Lef took another bite of a fresh local apple before turning to take a second mouthful of fresh honeycomb. After his recent adventures, Lef had made it big and his spirits were high - he might be able to finally retire as an adventurer, buy some nice countryside property around the kingdom or one of their sister states, and maybe start a family of his own. For adventurers like him, many never make it to retirement and suffer much worse fates.

    Lef has had a fortunate life. Still in his 20s, Lef had a lot to look forward to. Fairly tall, good physique, above average but nothing special. His short brown-blond hair helped to highlight his bright blue eyes. For a human, he was a pretty decent looking creature - definitely better looking than most. While these aspects of Lef's life helped shaped who he is, what really helped Lef find success as an adventurer were his keen skills in combat. Blessed with the ability to use both physical and magical techniques, Lef's skills in both hand to hand combat and other forms of battle were second to none. This helped establish Lef's reputation as the Lone Wolf - a man who would take on some of the most dangerous requests alone and succeed time and time again. Lef's twinblades, which he proudly named "Dawn" and "Dusk" were his crowning achievements as an adventurer - the two blades were personally forged by an ancient blacksmith and are of legendary quality with their magical properties. Combined with his skill in combat and adventuring, Lef had become the most powerful adventurer in the world...or so he thought.

    Bring the prodigious hero he thinks he is, Lef has grown a bit overconfident recently. With flawless bladework, focused magical techniques, his blades, and of course - his handsome charm, there was no challenge that could stop the hero. As proud as Lef was of his skills, such pride would lead to his downfall. As this final adventure Lef was about to embark on would be his last as a protagonist - fate had other plans for Lef.

    As Lef celebrating with the guild registrar staff about this being his final mission ever, Lef browsed the list of request, wanting to go out with a bang with this last request...
