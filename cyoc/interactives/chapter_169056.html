    Heading to his car as an invisible toddler, he decided to turn into an adult wearing the same clothes.

    Driving down the road, he looked back at his old life. Married, almost a father, a good job, plenty of friends. But if his wife kept him doing what he was doing, he wouldn’t have a life anymore.

    Edward drove for hours until he found a truck stop with no people. He reached into the back for his bag and went inside.

    Black hair to red
    Almost obese to totally ripped
    Light freckles on his face
    Brown eyes to blue
    Wide nose to thin

    Edward looked at himself and was ready to start a new life.
