    Why me?!?

    added by Darkclawsofdeath 10 years ago O

    Andrew woke up from his slumber, confused.
    "Sigh, what happened to me?" He wondered, pondering why he passed out. As he proped himself up, he saw that the door that he locked was busted open.

    "A burglar?" He was unsure, for there were claw marks all over the place.

    Finally regaining full consciousness, his senses went berserk. His vision became more blurry, not to the point of helplessness, but he can hear even droplets of water from his neighbor's kitchen sink. Following that was his sense of smell strong enough to sniff out... blood?!?

    Not minding his heightened senses, he went to the kitchen.

    "Oh my god!!!" He shouted.

    Bodies of dead people, young and old, all of them torn to bits with their blood scattered everywhere. To make things worse, he realized that he, also was covered in it.

    "How come I didn't smell the stains on my shirts?" Andrew wondered, but every question that popped in his ming must be answered later.

    ...

    And then he remembered.

    "THE VIDEOGAME!!!" It made sense, in a way. For he thought the wolves somehow escaped, which explains the wound on his finger. But why? Why did they put the bodies in the house? It made and didn't make sense. "Maybe I was being crazy".

    In the midst of thinking he felt a throbbing sensation on his chest, as he was about to reach it as a reaction, he felt something sharp. His fingernails were long and sharp, the pain grew and grew as black fur sprouted out of him enveloping him in it. The pain stopped, "Is...is it over?"

    Not yet. The pain returned, it was unforgiving, his bones rearranged themselves, his face stretched to a dog-like shape, teeth sprouted out in every corner of his mouth.

    He stood up in front of the mirror.

    "Is this me?" He said in a low wolfish voice.

    Suddenly, a man, covered in claw marks shouted "Officers it was this creature right here!!!" And the man passed out.

    Military officials then surrounded the area, but instead of the attacking, they explain that the game was a program to make werewolf soldiers, and that if how high a level you reach, how powerful your wolf-form will become. And the reason why you ate all those people was because you needed the energy to rewrite your genes into those of a wolf.

    "Since it's done, you have your free will back, we just needed to clear up the area, so that we can build out werewolf soldier den right here. No one will miss you right?"

    All was clear now, humanity is going to be used to fuel a world where all denizens are wolves

    "You're lucky we chose you!' One of them jokingly said.

    What will you do?
