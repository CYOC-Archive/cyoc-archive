Jen found her eyes drawn to her coach's legs, where she vaguely noticed that Hendricks' white ankle socks were slowly rising up out of her white athletic shoes. Their hems reached the bottoms of Hendricks' yoga pants. Like writhing snakes, the fabric of each hem split open, intertwining together. The blue color of the form fitting pants quickly infected the merging socks, shifting the fabric as in progressed down beneath her shoes.

    "Shape it up girls!" said the coach, idly twisting an ankle as she spoke. "Now that we have our pom-poms let's go through our routines."

    The girls quickly lined up in formation and for the rest of the period went over their flips, kicks, and spins. Jen thought the girls were looking rather good today, strong and agile, cute and toned, they were even managing to pull off tower drops with ease. Granted the cheerleading squad had been doing them since the beginning of the year, but today just seemed to flow better than last Friday's performance at the football game.

Much like during the warmup squats, Jen felt rushes of pleasure each time her toes rubbed against one another or her feet rubbed the fabric of her shoes when she changed her stance. She had to try hard not to moan a few times. Her eyes were increasingly admiring her teammates lithe forms, and once or twice she almost messed up parts of her routines. During the second half of practice, she could have sworn she was getting similar looks back from her teammates.

    At the end of the period, Miss Hendricks waved the girls in to form a circle around her, smoothing out the skirt of her short yellow skater dress with her other arm as she did so.

    "Girls I was SUPER happy with today's practice." their coach said, her hands clasped girlishly in front of her bosom in excitement.

    "You all look so cu...err great and I'm sure we will wow the judges big time when we head to our first Cheerleading competition in a few weeks. I couldn't keep my eyes off your excellent fee...footwork" she corrected herself, blushing slightly and avoiding their eyes as she twisted a white canvas sneaker idly.

    Miss Hendricks brushed a loose lock of hair behind her ear. "If we keep the same energy we had today and keep it going all year, I know we can take any competition with ease. Feet in." She said, extending a shapely leg. All the cheerleaders blinked in confusion for a second, but then quickly stepped forward and stuck a leg into the circle, feet bumping into and atop of each other.

    They put arms around each other in the circle. Jen found herself entranced by the circle of feet in front of her. Her eyes were unfocused and mouth open slightly. What she wouldn't do to get all those shoes off and rub those socked feet against one another. She began unconsciously squeezing and rubbing the shoulder of the girl to her right, her good friend Jade. Surprisingly, Jade made no move for her to stop and even leaned a bit harder into Jen.

    "One, two, three, Go Spartans!" said Miss Hendricks, a girly lilt sliding into her voice.

    "ONE, TWO, THREE, GO SPARTANS!" shouted the team in unison. They lifted their feet up in unison as they shouted, their energy strong and enthusiasm stronger than before.
