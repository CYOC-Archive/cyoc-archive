    Kat's Present

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 14 years ago A S

    "This can't be real." Dan says while looking over himself.

    He started feeling the pad in the palm of his once hands. Now a mix between a human hand and cat paw. With fear in his eyes Dan felt where his ears used to be but found nothing but fur. He moved his paws farther up his head and touch his new ears. His mouth dropped from the shock, like he had something to say but nothing came out. He quickly turned around, making his name tag jingle to see his tail again. It was blonde with two white stripes and a white tip.

    "You... You can't do this." Dan responds.
    "Yes I can I'm a witch now so I can do whatever I want." Kat reiterates.

    Dan reaches into his back pocket and pulls out his cell phone. "We'll see what mom and dad have to say about this." Dan replied.

    Before Dan can push a button Kat points her wand at him. "Don't even try it. I can cast another spell stopping you before you could press the call button. Put your hands or paws up." Kat orders.

    Dan ears flatten just before he raises both his paws.

    "Now hand over the phone." She says.

    Dan not wanting anything to make the situation worse than it is reluctantly hands Kat his cell phone.

    "Now then what were we doing... Oh yes we were going to get you fixed." She says with a smile.

    As Kat puts the cell phone in her pocket the house phone rings. Dan and Kat look at the caller id on the phone and see it is their mom. Since Kat is closer to the phone, Dan takes this chance while Kat slightly distracted to make a run for the front door. The phone rings for a second time but mixed in with the ring is a chant. Kat is casting another spell. Dan is reaching the door faster than he normally would. He didn't know if it was because of the fear in him or from his new body. But one thing wasn't right the door seem to be growing and his clothes were getting extremely baggy. By the time he reached the door the locks were out of reach. He looked at himself realizing what was happening.

    "I'm shrinking NO STOP PLEASE!" Dan begs.

    Kat picks up the phone and covers the mouth piece. "That's not all now shush while I talk to mom." Kat says, she then begin walking to another room taking the phone with her.

    When Dan body finishes shrinking his body starts to reshape itself again. His fingers shrink into his paws while his thumbs shrivel and move the side of his arm. His toes do the same thing as his fingers but his big toes completely vanish. Dan loses his balance as his spine realigns itself. He almost falls over but he catches himself on the door. Finally his muzzle fully extends to that of a normal cat.

    "KAT MAKE IT STOP MMMEEEOOOWWW." He cried as the change came to an end. Dan tried to speak but soon realized he couldn't muster up a single word.

    With no way to open the door and Kat in the next room Dan came to the decision that he must hide before Kat came back. There was no way he was going to let some vet neuter him. After looking around he decided that the couch would be best place to hide. He hopped down from the door and started a run. He doesn't get far before falling flat on his face. Kat giggles as she walks into the room holding a notepad paper slip.

    "I may have turned you into a cat but your more like a new born kitten, new to the world and no knowledge how your body works. Just like a human you need to crawl before you walk and walk before you run." Kat boasts.

    Kat walks up to Dan and kneels down. "As you probably know mom was on the phone. She enrolled me at magic school so I can become full-fledged witch. But before I go I need a familiar. Since you never given me a present for my birthday. I have chosen you to become my familiar as your present to me. Mom gave me two spells over the phone one to summon a familiar and one to bind the familiar to a witch. But since I'm using you I won't need the first spell." She claims.

    Kat balled up and tosses the slip of paper. She then stands up begins to chant the binding spell. Dan not waiting for her to finish leaped at Kat to attack her. Kat side-steps Dan and finish chanting her. She then kisses two fingers and touches Dan right back leg. Pain spreads all over Dan body from where Kat touched him.

    "There that wasn't so bad."

    Dan sprawls in agony on the floor, it felt like he was being burn to death. He was trying to resist the spell through the unbearable pain. But it prove futile as he couldn't take anymore and fainted from the pain.

    Kat picked up Dan after he stopped moving. "I better hurry I don't want to be late for orientation."
