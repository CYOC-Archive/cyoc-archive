    Don't keep unknown devices next to you while you're sleeping

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by green34 3 years ago A BM S O

    After spending all night looking online for any information on the device for hours, you, Chris, and a raccoon Jake fall asleep next to each other on the couch. You fall asleep with your laptop and the device sitting on the couch next to you. Eventually you feel a fuzzy mess come and cuddle with you in the middle of the night. At first its pretty comfortable, but eventually you get a bit sore from having to sit next to the raccoon, so you shift in your seat a little. You move your laptop out of the way and sprawl out a bit over the rest of the couch. Jake moves again to sit on your chest curled up. You sleep like this for a few hours before you wake up again a bit uncomfortable. A bit annoyed this time, you set him to the side of you as you roll over again to your side.

    You feel an lump beneath your arm as you roll over. At first you think its your phone, but then as you lift up your arm a little bit, you feel a bit sharper of a pain. Right below your arm is the device, still sitting on the couch. Now its lit up and there's something happening on the screen. You can see some DNA strands and a bunch of symbols flash across the screen. There's a lot of green light and then you feel an electric shock all across your body. The only light in the whole room is from the flashing device, that's now fallen on the ground beside the couch. Its hard to tell what color, but there's fur growing all over your body. You're only in your boxers and a t-shirt, but they begin to feel loose on your body. You stand up to go reach the lights across the room. Your whole face feels funny as you feel it stretch out into a muzzle. At this point your whole body is covered in fur. You can feel something stretch out near your tailbone and even in the dark you can see a large fluffy tail behind you. Right as you are about to reach the light switch, your legs give out under you and you fall down to all fours. Your hands and feet shift and become paws beneath you. Now much smaller then before, you are able to shimmy out of your clothes that are on you.

    Now on all fours, you run over to Chris and grab his hand with your mouth and pull him to wake him up. He abruptly wakes up all groggy. "Jake is that you? What do you need?" Now the room is pitch black as the device is no longer lighting up. Chris eventually walks over to the light and turns the lights on. You are immediately able to get a better look at yourself. You're covered almost completely in orange fur with the exception of black socks on your paws. "Huh where did this fox come from? Jake is this you?" He looks over to the couch where Jake the raccoon is still sleeping peacefully. "Wait is that really you? Did the device change you as too?" You nod as best as you can. You try to communicate back but all that comes out are some yips and barks. "How did this happen?" He then sees the device on the ground near the couch and he puts two and two together. "We need to be more careful with this, especially now." Chris picks up the device and sets it back on the table. Eventually you two settle down as reality of the situation sets in. Still very sleep deprived, you eventually both fall back asleep on the couch.
