    Next, the old couple grabbed the two wigs and positioned them over the heads of their respective recipients. Sally watched as the woman carefully measured the position of the ears on her skull, compared to where the wig would go if placed directly on top.

Seemingly pleased with her work, she then compared the cat ears and the wig again, and made another cut in the wig.
