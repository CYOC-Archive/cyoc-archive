    Marcus and Vito were in their car, waiting for the sun to set. Neither of them said a word as they sat there, their minds focused on the task that they had to do. Their mission was to go in, kill Moe and Nichole, get Karen, and get out as quickly as they could. Vito would focus on Moe and Nichole while Marcus would focus on getting Karen. As night had set on Moon Lake, Vito and Marcus got out of their car and headed towards the trunk. They opened the trunk and got out a couple of guns, some gas canisters, and a pair of gas masks.

    Unknown to them, their tail finally caught up to them and was watching them from a distance. "All Agents, be on alert," the man in the car said into his radio. "The Gambonis are out, and they're fully armed."
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    One of the first things that the Gamboni brothers noticed was how dark it was in the woods without the sunlight. It was dark, for certain, and perfect for an ambush. On the other hand, as Vito noticed, it would make it hard for them to get back to the car. He made mental notes of lights, features, and other such marks so that they could quickly get back to the car once their business was done.

    Guided by the lights in the house, Marcus and Vito approached quietly. The two of them quietly walked up one of the back windows, and Vito motioned to Marcus to move to a different window. Vito put on his gas mask and Marcus did the same. On the count of three, Marcus and Vito broke their respective windows, pulled the pin on their gas canisters before throwing them into the broken windows. Gas started to fill the rooms, and both Marcus and Vito heard voices coming from the broken windows.

    "We're under attack!"
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    "We're under attack!" Daniel Brooks heard from the radio. Daniel's eyes widened as he heard the news, and then said, "Everyone get out there. The Gamboni brothers are here!" He drew his firearm and led a small group of officers and agents out of his home to attack the Gamboni brothers."
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    Vito was at first he was satisfied when he heard the coughing. However, when people started to come out of the Charleston home and the next door Brooks home, his eyes widened.

    "Shit," he said to himself, "Marcus, it's a trap! RUN!" Vito called out as he pulled out his gun. Marcus ran in some direction while Vito ran towards the car. Vito looked behind him at the crowd had gathered after him. Vito fired his gun into the crowd, and he could see them scatter to avoid getting shot. Vito then started to run towards the car. He continued to turn and fire everyone once in a while to keep the officers chasing him at bay. He managed to reach the car, and then a small of squad cars surrounded him, their red and blue lights flashing in the night. All of the officers jumped out of their cars and drew their guns at Vito Gamboni.

    "Put your hands up!" one of the officers yelled out. Vito looked behind him and saw the crowd of agents and officers behind him and he realized that he was now surrounded. To fight now would only bring death. Vito slowly raised his hands as an agent came behind him, taking his gun and putting his hands behind his back.

    "Vito Gamboni," the agent said, "You are under arrest."
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    Marcus Gamboni was running wildly through the forest. Fortunately, most of the agents and officers were chasing Vito instead of Marcus, and there were very few that were chasing him. He fired his gun at those chasing him to slow them down as he ran deeper into the forest. Marcus was getting tired as he ran through the forest, but he kept pressing through anyway.
