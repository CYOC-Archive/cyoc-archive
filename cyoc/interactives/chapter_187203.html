    A Strange Magic Show: Olivia the diaper speaks

    Applause filled the tent as Sorceress Spectra presented Olivia’s new diaper body. Even as they clapped, many of the watching kids had stupefied expressions, wondering if they should be excited at what they saw.

    In the Sorceress’s hands, baby Caleb kicked his legs around in giggly laughter, not even caring that his own sister was wrapped around his tush.

    “Thank you, thank you!”, said the Sorceress, as the clapping died down. “Don’t fear, boys and girls; our little Olivia is still alive. In fact, she’s heard all of you clapping for her right now!”

    ““Now, I bet you’re all thinking: Oh no, Olivia’s a diaper! She must be so scared and embarrassed!” . The nude sorceress said this in a mock voice, as she tilted Caleb side to side. “Well, lets see what she has to say about that! Let’s open a little ‘connection’ to our fluffy volunteer…”

    Sorcere Spectra placed Caleb back onto the table, the weight of the baby plopping atop of Olivia. Everyone could see Olivia’s new body crunching up, as Caleb put his chubby legs together. With gravitas, the Sorceress gestured her hands around the changed girl, and then put her hands by the sides of her head. Her eyes closed in concentration.

    “Olivia….reach out to us,” the Sorceress recited, as a slight shimmering sound filled the tent. “Speak your mind……”

    A subtle light glowed around the flower patterned diaper. A light that gently pulsed. The audience waited, holding their breaths. And the shimmering stopped, leaving silence.

    “…..H-hello?”

    It was Olivia’s voice, loud enough for everyone to hear. The kids in the audience gasped all at once. “Olivia, can you hear us?”, the Sorceress spoke.

    “I-I’m a diaper!!”

    “How do you feel Olivia?”

    “This is so weird!!” Olivia cheerfully exclaimed. “I can still see! But I’m made out of feathers!!”

    Indeed, for the young girl it felt like her entire body was a squishy pillow, pushed down by the weight of her baby brother. Some of the watching kids, including Nate and Katie, wondered if this was just a trick of some kind. That maybe Olivia was hiding somewhere in the tent, just speaking into some microphone. Still, they were enraptured.

    “Can you feel this, Olivia?” With one of her red-nailed fingers, the Sorceress nudged the side of the diaper.

    “Y-yeah! Hee hee, it tickles!”, Olivia replied. “I-is Caleb going to poop on me?”

    The audience laughed, uncertainly. “Don’t worry. We don’t want to make things too stinky, wouldn’t we?," the Sorceress replied. "Let’s give you something a bit more mellow.”

    “Mellow?.....EEEK!”

    Caleb's baby face had an ‘uh-oh’ expression. A yellow stain growing beneath Olivia’s diaper form, right where her former mouth used to be.

    “H-h-he’s peeing in me!”

    The children in the stands let out a chorus of ‘ewws’ and other hollering. Fortunately for them, they couldn’t smell any of the stink. The stain grew bigger and bigger, spreading over the cartoon flowers that used to be Olivia’s face. The former little girl felt wet and pulpy, especially over her transformed chest and head. Above her, her baby brother gave a naughty grin and cackled, kicking his feet on the table.

    “Ah, you’re doing a wonderful job as a diaper already!”, the Sorceress proclaimed. “Do you like it, Olivia?”

    “I…I like it!”, Olivia replied, sounding a bit surprised too. “I feel warm!”

    “Oh? How does it taste?”

    “It’s like lemonade!”

    The audience erupted. It was a mix of cackling laughter, and icked-out groans from the audience. The Sorceress chuckled too, even as she motioned for the other children to quiet down.

    “Well of course!! You’re a diaper, after all.” said the Sorceress. “You have a different way of feeling things! Do you feel comfy?”

    “I do!”, Olivia exclaimed, with glee. “I wanna stay like this!!”

    “Well, I’m not sure your parents would approve,” the Sorceress replied, with a finger to her mouth. She grinned knowingly. “I’ll tell you what! Why don’t you be a diaper for a little while longer? Be good and quiet, and maybe, you’ll get to be a diaper even after our show! Or maybe something even better! Would you like that?”

    “Yeah!!”

    “Wonderful!! I’ll let you and Caleb rest on the side of our stage. And here’s a special treat: every time your brother uses you, you’ll magically clean yourself up, so he’ll get to use you all over again! Like this!”

    True to her word, the yellow stain that covered Olivia quickly evaporated, and she no longer felt soaked. A magical chime sounded as Olivia dried up.

    “Taa-daa! You’re a magic diaper!! Say bye to everyone, Caleb’s diaper!”

    “Bye everyone!!!”

    And with another hand gesture from the Sorceress, another magical shimmer sounded. And Caleb’s diaper went silent, happily accepting the new label the Sorceress had given her.

    “Let’s give another hand for our volunteer! Caleb’s diaper!!”

    As more clapping ensued, in the top row of the stands, Nate and Katie were glancing at each other, unsure of what to make of all this.

    “I…I think I want to go,” Katie whispered.

    “Go?”

    “I don’t want to watch anymore.”

    For Katie, it was bad enough, to anticipate being picked for a magic show. But this wasn’t any other magic show… what if she be turned into something like Olivia? Something weirder and grosser than a diaper? And from what the Sorceress said, she might not even have to turn her back!!

    Nate was looking uncomfortable too. “But if we go now, the Sorceress might see us!”, he whispered back. “What if she changes us if we try to run away?”

    Katie didn’t think of that. Uneasily, she and Nate turned their attention on the stage, waiting for a moment when Spectra might be too distracted to see both of them leave. For now, they could only watch as Sorceress Spectra began her next act…
