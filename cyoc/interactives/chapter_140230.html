    "Well," starts Lucy, "It's a bit of witchcraft."
    "You're a witch?" Replies Nick curious and shocked.
    "Yeah, and so is Marta."
    "Ok. Why did you transform Callum?"
    "He was too tardy," butts in Marta, "Miss Ewans had enough."
    "Guess that's Callum," sighs Nick.
    "Yeah, and he's also one of those guys who are easy to transform."
    "What do you mean?" Asks Nick.
    "Some guys are easier to transform than others. Callum is one of the easiest, and as such he would have ended up transformed anyway."
    "So Callum was destined to be transformed?"
    "Yeah. Although I was expecting Miss Ewans to turn him into the class hamster before anything."
    "You can turn people into animals?"
    "Yeah. But it's usually a fate reserved for failing students. Callum wasn't strictly failing, he was just tardy."
    "So the class pets," says Nick.
    "Most were once human," says Lucy, "But they don't have any memories of being so, and nor do their parents, friends etc."
    "Will that happen to Callum?" Asks Nick concerned.
    "Honestly, I have no idea," says Lucy.

    "So about me. How does shrinking and age regression work?" Asks Nick.
    "Well. 95% of all guys are transformable. You're mid range on the difficulty scale. Which most of us never get to that level."
    "So you made me younger?"
    "Nah. Technically Callum did. We just planned it out."
    "Oh okay," replies Nick a little scared. What could these girls do to him?

    They begin eating again. Nick had forgotten how much better everything tastes when younger.
    "So," says Lucy, "You need to live with me or Marta."
    "What?" Replies Nick.
    "You're only 15 and don't have a job."
    "But I have a roommate."
    "But for how long? I told you that reality may change to suit your transformation."
    "So Callum may become jeans permanently too and I'll forget about him?"
    "Possibly."

    Nick sigha. His 15 year old thoughts are telling him to risk it. But his adult thoughts tell him to live with one of them. He sighs and says

    What do you do now?
