    A Werewolf Game

    As Andrew sat down, the screen came on, illuminating the small room in the eerie blue glow before data was transmitted to the screen. Suddenly, the TV went black, causing the room to darken almost to pitch blackness as it once was. Then, just as suddenly, the words: Werewolf: The Video Game appeared. This was something that enthralled Andrew since there were several vampire based games out on video game consoles, he felt that it was exactly time for a werewolf one.

    The screen changed again, this time focusing on a realistic looking and dilapidated mansion room somewhere with the full moon as well as it's light shone through the dust covered window. As the video itself settled, smaller words appeared on the screen to say start aloud to begin and that is exactly what Andrew did. Suddenly, things began to change all around him as he felt a cold chill run up his spine. Shivering from the cold, Andrew had closed his eyes for a split second, but when he opened them, Andrew was shocked and bewildered at what happened. No longer was Andrew in the darkened room with a screen in front of him... he was inside the house with the moon shining brightly through the window.

    Not knowing what he should do exactly, Andrew thought that this was a holodeck like in Star Trek or something and tried shouting out commands. Unlike the television show, nothing happened but Andrew figured that was part of the experience. Needing to know the rules and what his objective was, he searched around the mansion bedroom for a document of any sort for help.

    As he searched farthest from the window, Andrew found a sheet of aged paper that looked incredibly brittle and could fall apart at the slightest touch. Andrew just sighed, leaving it where he found the paper and read what it had to say.

    Welcome to Werewolf: The Video Game

    You are now immersed in a world where you have two choices to begin your game. First and foremost, this world is one with humans and werewolves fighting one another to survive. The choice that you make here will determine the type of game you are going to play. You can choose to become a werewolf by standing in the light of the full moon shining through the window and embracing the gift to be bestowed upon you. If you choose this option, you will be the leader in the resistance against the humans and you will be the one to ensure the survival of the werewolf species or its destruction.

    You can also choose to remain human by walking through the door before standing in the light from the moon. If you choose this option, you will be fighting to extinguish the werewolf species and retain your species dominance as the top of the food chain. One thing to know, if this option is chosen you cannot come back to this room and you can become a werewolf at anytime if you are bitten or scratched, then you will have yet another choice which will not be revealed until that time comes.

    Make your choice wisely as the fate of two species lays in the balance!


    Andrew just stood there not knowing what to do. The option to become a werewolf was enthralling, even if he wouldn't really change into the werewolf. The other option was just as cool, since becoming part of the werewolf clan was a possibility later. Standing there thinking for a moment, Andrew made his choice and...

    What do you do now?
