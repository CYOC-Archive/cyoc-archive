    Lupe had a warm grin on her face throughout the whole trip. For years, she has dreams of being a mermaid underwater. It seemed like just a fantasy created by her mind but it wasn't anymore. This was real, the sea was her home now.

    Harmony was quite receptive to the attention from the sea life. The fish didn't look at her like some bimbo made to warm a bed but a beautiful creature of the sea, a nurse of the waters to be respected and nurtured.

With no worries of breaking any leg bones, Colton made his tail curl in ways a human can never replicate in a mermaid or merman costume. Colton swam in and out of various rocks in a speed that seemed to live up to the fiery imagery of his tail.

    "How could a baby turn people into mermaids?" She said.

    Cura turned around and answered. "All merbabies have magic specifically made to defend themselves. Your friends just so happened to be one of many humans who haved joined our kind due to an encounter with one."

    "I still can't believe I've lived my whole life not knowing about this until now. If merpeople are real, what about dragons and elves?" Lupe inquired.

    Cura giggled at that. "There's a lot of magic in the world that goes unnoticed by humans. For now, I'm more focused on reuniting you all with your friends."

    "Oh, how I would love to see the baby!" Harmony gushed. "I bet it's an adorable little sea angel."
