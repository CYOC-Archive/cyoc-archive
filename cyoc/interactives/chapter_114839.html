Ordering Grrrrrrr to shut down while Frederick Parker is in town was a necessary decision. The fact that it cost you the job you landed there is not lost on either of us.”

    “Samantha,” Frances placing a hand on the head of a tiny kitten that just jumped on her lap; “I understand you are under a lot of stress with the job as mayor. But you shouldn’t vent your frustrations or anger out on Ben. He was understandably upset when he lost his job before he could collect his first paycheck. You owe our son an apology for what you said last night.”
    “Ben owes me one as well,” Samantha grumbling.
    “I agree on that,” Frances answering; “so both of you better get this off your chest. No more letting this fester.”

    “I got shot this morning while trying to apprehend a dangerous man,” Samson motioning the bandage on his shoulder; “the man is dead and Frederick Parker by now was told he was harboring a wanted war criminal.”
    “What,” Alex holding onto another strip of beef; pausing from consuming it as well.
    “It’s not important,” Samson listening to his sister still speaking; “bit if you don’t want Frances to know about Ben’s latest hiding spot, you’ll pass that jerky over here.”
