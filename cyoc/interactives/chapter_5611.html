    Nancy licks at her nose feeling really confused as she watches the human and the two dogs. Seeing the other dogs getting treats and not herself makes her stomach growl with hunger.

    “Nancy? Come girl its time for us to get back to the kennel.” The zoo lady says while taking a collar out of her pocket.

    Joanne and Riley all ready wearing collars watch their trainer closely slightly confused why their pack mate did not follow them over to the mistress.

    Not sure what to do Nancy starts to find it harder to keep herself upright on hind legs. Will the funny looking dog give me food too she wonders licking her lips.

    The zoo lady looks at her watch then the two dogs watching her. Thinking enough time has passed for Nancy to have become canine she gives her dogs a hand signal to bring Nancy to her. Both dogs eye Nancy then begin padding over to her tails and stances neutral. Joanne moves to come toward one side of Nancy while Riley moves to come in another direction. The movements of the two dogs trying to cut off any escape routes of Nancy hint that they are either very well trained or smarter then most dogs.

    “Good doggies.” Nancy says watching herself be circled by the two. Her words being heard as a bark to the human both dogs stop and look at her intently.

    “Very funny Nancy.” The bitch who once was Joanne replies in canine.

    Riley glances to his mate as if it’s her that is the leader not him. “Should just knock her over and get it over with. Besides remember how much you resisted at first? Much better to do this fast so the finish is quick.”

    “You both can talk!” Nancy barks in surprise not seeming to think of herself as a canine just yet.

    “Nancy why are you doing tricks when our mistress is in a hurry to get back to the zoo?” Joanne asks her pack sister.

    “Tricks?” Nancy asks cocking her head to the side looking and smelling confused.

    “Standing on your hind legs to get more then one biscuit from the human.” Riley says padding over beside Nancy looking ready to pounce her.

    Before Nancy can reply the phone starts ringing causing all three dogs to look over at it as well as the zoo lady.
