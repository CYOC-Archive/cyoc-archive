    The Magic Shop

    A brother & sister came to the shop. The sister wants to become a rubber ball for her brother's dog

    added by Raven Cross A year ago I

    A man & woman entered the 'Inanimates R Us'. They walked up to the corner checking out all the objects on display as they went. An older looking man asked with a smile "How can I help you today?" The man answered "I'm Adam & this is my sister Sarah. Sarah here wants to become a a red rubber chew ball for a dog. A large dog." The shopkeeper asked "That so miss?" Sarah answered with an excited reply "Yes. Very much so." The shopkeeper warned "Transformations into objects are permanent." Sarah answered not deterred "I know that." The shopkeeper went to get the proper paperwork. Soon he returned explaining everything to Sarah. That by signing this paperwork she legally by her own desire becoming an object. That she would be an object for the rest of her life. That she would be legally property of the shop to be sold to someone. That this was permanent. Sarah understood all of it & signed the paperwork. The shopkeeper then said after looking it all over said that it was done. He went to instruct Sarah to follow him when Adam interrupted him stating "I will be buying her." The shopkeeper looked surprised at Adam. The shopkeeper thought a bit & replied "Ok. After she is done changing I will sell her to you for I guess...$8.00." Adam was pleased with that saying "See you soon my soon to be ex-sister new ball." The shopkeeper took Sarah to a backroom with a large steel door. He opened the door & instructed Sarah to enter explaining this where she will transform. He informed that she would still be aware as an object. That somehow she'd be able to see, hear & feel as an object. But won't feel any pain from anything. Sarah entered the room to see it had no windows, only the door she came in, a chute on the wall near the floor marked Disposal', & vents on the ceiling. Sarah was instructed to strip completely naked & toss everything into the chute marked 'Disposal' Clothes, shoes, items on her; everything. Then the gas will come in & start to transform her. She didn't have to breathe in the gas intentionally; just relax & breathe normally. Sarah nodded & began to take her shirt off. "Curious. You two planned to have your brother buy you as red rubber chew ball. Right?" The shopkeeper asked Sarah as she undid her bra. "It that wrong?" She asked wondering as she sat down to start to take off her shoes & socks. "Not at all. You will get sold immediately." As Sarah was pulling her pants down after undoing them she requested to the shopkeeper "Don't tell any of that. I don't want him knowing. I want to just be thought of as a lifeless object; no different than any other object." The shopkeeper replied "I wasn't going to anyway." He shut the door & it made a sealing sound as it sealed. By now Sarah was totally naked & she took everything & tossed them down the 'Disposal' chute. It sealed shut with the same sound as the door but not as loud. The gas started to pump into the room & soon felt herself starting to transform. [It's happening! I'm starting to become a rubber chew ball!] Sarah thought very excited.
