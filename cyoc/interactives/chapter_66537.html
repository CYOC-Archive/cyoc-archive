    Laura--Wonder Gal--felt an overwhelming desire to get on her hands and knees and let her dog make her pregnant. Somehow she knew her fertility now covered interspecies matings, and it didn't matter whether she had a baby, or puppies, or a litter of dog-people, the important thing was to get pregnant!

    But Wonder Gal also possessed an enormous strength of will, that had enabled her to resist mind control efforts in the past. She knew this wasn't right--she shouldn't be wanting to have sex with the family dog. Something was going on, something sort of like that initial rush of Mr. Irresistable's pheromones, and she needed to resist it. Thousands of little girls with Wonder Gal lunchboxes didn't need to know that their idol was into bestiality.

    The nanobots redoubled their efforts to force Laura to Science Master's will. Animus added his magical strength to theirs, but discovered that Wonder Gal's magic immunity was still at full strength. There was nothing he could do to influence her, he would just have to let the nanobots work their wonders.

    As the struggle between the nanobots and Laura's original personality intensified, both sides drew from the reserves of energy in her body. Given Wonder Gal's power, these were considerable, but the drain was increasing exponentially.
