    Transformation Resort: Josh uses his VIP power to make it that Anna was normally six inches tall

    As Josh marveled at his shrunken fiance's naked body, a thought came to his mind. 'What if Anna had been six inches before we arrived at the resort?' This thought made Josh curious. How would things be different if Anna had been this small while they dated? How would the two of them interact? And more interestingly, how would Anna react if she could become the same size as Josh?

    Wanting to see this, Josh focused on changing Anna. Immediately, he appeared in the white room, panels filling up with Anna's information. Before he could start working, Josh had some questions.

    "Hey voice!" Josh shouted out to the empty white space, hoping to reach the voice who had informed him of his VIP powers.

    "Wow, wow," The voice answered. "You don't need to shout."

    "Sorry bout that," Josh began. "I have some questions."

    "Sure, ask your questions," The voice answered. "That's why I am here for."

    "Can I use this system to make someone not remember what just happened? Like if I do a transformation on someone and change it later, can I make them not remember that transformation?"

    "Absolutely," the voice explained. "You can do that for everyone, not just the person you transformed. You can also change what they do remember during that time and you can even go through it as yourself."

    'That's good,' Josh thought. 'I can do this change to Anna and undo it without her remembering'.


    "I should also let you know about the saving system," the voice continued.

    "The saving system?" Josh repeated.

    "You can save the changes done to a person, like you would for a video game. You can use these saves to restore a person back to those transformations. You can even keep multiple saves for people at the same time."

    "Thank you for letting me know that," Josh replied, a little bothered the voice didn't bring this up earlier. "How do I make a save?"

    "Just think about saving and a panel to save to will appear."

    So Josh focused on saving Anna and a new panel emerged from the ground. The image on the panel was Anna's naked body, complete with her orange red hair, B cup breasts, and nice waist. Josh did notice that it still had Anna at 6 inches tall.

    "You can edit the save too," The voice informed Josh. "So you can undo the transformations you did to the person. "

    Josh reached out for the panel and saw the changes on Anna. So far it was just her new fantasy and the shrunken size. Josh removed the shrink and pressed a green indicator on the panel to save. With that, Josh moved back to the other panels.

    Josh opened Anna's profile and made her current height of six inches be what she was when she reached adulthood. Checking the changes, Josh saw this made Anna shorter in her youth too, just as he intended. He applied the changes and then returned to reality.
