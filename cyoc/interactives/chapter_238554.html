    Dainty Feet For A Demon

    Even weirder, you are not in the dressing room you are in. You see that you are within an ancient Chinese concubine's chambers. Large and full of luxurious items, from silk sheets and jade jewels to fine woodworks and oriental decorations galore. You cannot help marvelling at all these amazing works of art. You walk around, seeing that the bedroom includes living quarters and even a big washroom. There's lot of space here, even if no windows are present. Also, the door is locked. Damn... Now THAT appears ominous... And... It's hard to walk. You can only take tiny steps, and it's not the dress's fault. You stare down and see the problem at once. Your feet! They are tiny! Tinier than tiny! Yikes! You move them freely inside your little silk shoes, finely embroidered, and you feel no pain but... but it's so strange!... Sitting down, you remove with a frightened heart the shoes and see that your feet are not bound. That's the good news. The bad news is that your actual feet are indeed tiny. All dainty and minuscule, and white and smooth, with all the right curves and perfect nails. Perfect toes. Perfect feet. You wriggle your toes. Yep, they are yours. No doubt about it. Just then, the door opens. Hey, the person could knock! Though this would explain the tea set fuming on a table, smelling of green tea and peach fragrances. You slip back the little slipper in haste, rising up to see a man entering the room. He's got a fine moustache and goatee, but dressed in an attire that is most reminiscent of Japanese culture. And the man does speak Japanese, which to your utter amazement you are able to understand as fluently as a native speaker.
    "My day has been most tiring, my sweet lotus," he says.
    Not knowing what to do, you pour him tea. It must be the equivalent of getting a guy a beer when he comes home after work. But a lot of unknown things are occupying your mind at the moment too. What will the man expect next?

    Not much, apparently. He sips the tea and eats dainty little delicacies, while you offer him refills. You never liked green tea, or normal tea, but as you drink this one you come to decide it is your favorite drink ever. So yummy! But the man then goes behind a screen to undress and merely nods to you. A man of a few words. Though it suits you for now, in fact... But... But you can guess what he wants and that frightens you down to your very core. You're a guy! Not a concubine in a gilded cage! But if you do not undress or do something, he will get suspicious and might become violent and scary. So you take off your dress. Slowly.
    As a result, when he arrives in just some sort of traditional Japanese underwear, he frowns at seeing you.
    "I... Er... I thought...." you stammer, in perfect Chinese though.
    Which he seems to understand.
    "Allowing me to undress you? What a thoughtful gift," he smiles.
    He is a bit pudgy but wastes no time in removing your clothes. You keep yourself from pushing him away with all of your might. Yuck! You're not gay and do not want him so close to you and touching you! But he removes your shoes last and he does so with a sense of reverence which baffles you. He looks at your feet as if they were the greatest works of art in the whole world. He massages them, which makes you moan. Moan deeply under his ministrations. What an expert! He kisses your feet afterwards and as you giggle at the tickling sensations, he glides his nails and fingers, very much amused at your reactions. He says nothing but smiles and you glimpse in his eyes a wicked little gleam... A gleam that says he loves seeing you twitching and helpless under his ministrations. He adores your giggling, it seems, and as you twitch, you see his appearance discreetly changing. In no time, you have a Japanese demon, an oni, in front of you! That guy is a demon! A real one! There is a demon with a fetish for your feet that has the hots for you!
    "Enchanting," he smiles. "Utterly enchanting. I do not regret taking you from the mortal realm and making you even more beautiful than you were. Once my seed develops within you, I shall transform you fully into supernatural maiden. You shall ensure further my lineage."
    These words just terrify you. You don;'t want to be a concubine to a demon with a foot fetish! No way! You don't want to get pregnant! No!!!!! The mere idea... Ugh! You have to do something!
