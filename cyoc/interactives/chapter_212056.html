    It was awkward being a furry artist in this neighborhood. People who had seen that sensationalist news story, would give Laura her 'sympathy' at all those 'filthy degenerates' doing 'depraved' art of her. The idea most furries just like the idea of animal-people and weren't sexual predators was hard for them to grasp.

    But they did know Laura's parents, and knew they were good people, and so was she. She struggled to come up with art she could show her family. At least being a rabbit girl meant she could get away with showing self portraits at least. After all, it obviously was porn when she was doing art of herself of course!

    Given her body was covered in fur, most clothing just wasn't practical to her, and her family and neighbors accepted it. She was a person, but she wasn't human.

    Laura had never had any sense of 'not belonging' to her family. She'd just been born looking a little more different than most.

    The remote had done its absolute best to alter reality to accept Laura being a nudist anthro rabbit as always having been normal. It helped human language and human culture was FILLED with the little contradictions that made up civilization, humans were conditioned to accept them. That Laura was the only rabbit girl in existence should have made her a global news story... or vivisected in some government lab. But the mindless, unthinking, unconscious programming of the tablet connected to the 'network' (for lack of better term) of its creators, had gone for a more 'don't think about it too hard' approach. With Laura's state of being more of an oddity than a shocking impossibility. Laura's family had their fifteen minutes of fame and had moved on with their lives.

    Plus, the priority filters built into the programming had gone for the path of least resistance in this instance, as this iteration of reality was not likely to stick around for long the Creators of the remote knew.

    Though Laura did have the infamous honor of being among the few who had not only turned off the 'users aware' option (the creators would send out an update patch it so it wasn't easy to turn off by accident, not that Laura would know this, since it was forbidden for the creators to meddle with the options the user chose), but also one of the few to COMPLETELY IGNORE the tiny little question mark symbol in the bottom corner that was the remote tablet's MANUAL! She'd bought and used tablets before after all, she knew how they worked.

    The Creators figured Laura would fall into one of two types. She'd either splurge several changes at once, then use the tablet again, or she'd start out transforming subjects one at a time and build up from there. Or she'd fall somewhere in the middle. The more ambitious those with their products started out as, the less likely they were to complete their visions.

    Now Laura knew her neighbors personally, and knew none objected to being subjects of her art. She'd done so before. And she most DEFINITELY wanted to try out her new toy some more. Maybe even have some fun with the background option for her self model.

    BUT! Work before pleasure. Her commissioner had been waiting too long as was. Laura had been sick with the flu, and her commissioner had paid in advance. A good commissioner lived by their reputation, and she best follow though on obligations awaiting. On the bright side... this magic tablet was gonna make her job a whole lot easier!

    She'd been skeptical when the old man in the bathroom with glued-on glitter stars had said she'd never spotted his shop in the mall before because he was a wizard and he traveled between universes ... but given how amazing this device was, she wondered if he was a time traveler from the future giving her tech so advanced it might as well be magic? Naw. Why go to all that trouble to make it compatible with machines of the past to make some quick cash in money that might not even be viable in a hundred years? The tablet being made by a wizard actually made MORE sense.

    Laura shook her head and stopped procrastinating and got to work.

    This kinda art was borderline for the kind she'd agreed to make. But it hadn't been listed on the 'will not do' list of her artist profile, and she'd needed the money fast. Maybe that was WHY she was so eager to procrastinate. She'd indulged in 'kink' art from time to time, but 'kink' meant literally anything, maids, robots, genies, pool toys, pirates, brainwashed to the point of identity-death, petrification, cat-ears folded halfway, you name it, someone somewhere had interest in it. Though that also meant it was easy to miss as BEING a kink to the person who paid for the art. Usually.

    Laura didn't know anything about the commissioner in real life, not even their name, and that was fine by both of them. Commissioners preferred to not have their identities known most of the time.

    As a file name he wrote down the alias of the user. She was surprised when the default model was of a teenage boy. She wondered if the system had a sense of humor. She began modifying the picture. Aging up the model and changing it to a female, oh most definitely obviously female. Then came the option of species, and she scrolled straight to tigress. Like the machine was responding to her thoughts, the million opinions she'd had before narrowed down to simple things like hair color, eye colored, hair length. Then came musculature, it really was like the machine was anticipating what options she wanted next. She was still surprised when the option of 'occupation' came up. She opened up ... and typed in 'dominatrix.' Instantly the tigress was done up in leather, and the proper equipment, including the ever-present whip.

    The background option was next, and she was able to illustrate a 'dungeon' with eerie violet and blue lights, and more of the cliche dominatrix gear on the walls.

    She strongly considered putting in a blind folded and gagged skinny male fox like the commissioner had wanted but hasn't been able to come up with the money for as an apology for the delays... but it was best to keep things professional. A couple commissioners had thought they were automatically friends after she'd done art for them.

    She had her model, now it was time for the art.
