    The rest of Ashley’s party went off without any magical issues. As Katie looked at the clock she noticed it was now time to pick Gloria up.

    “Thanks for having us over Ash,” Katie said warmly as she hugg d her friend. “We had fun.”

    “Glad you could come,” Ashley smiled. “Just one thing. Can you turn Angie back before you leave?”

    “Oh come on Ashley,” Claudia groaned. “I like this version.”

    “Okay true,” Ashley grinned. “This version is a lot nicer but she’s supposed to help me out with some sorority business. If she’s not around I’m going to have to do it all myself and you know how much I hate doing extra work.”

    Claudia chuckled as she now looked at the Middle Ages thespian float amongst the crowd. “Ugh fine,” she sighed. “But she’s keeping the attitude and the glasses.”

    “Fair enough,” Ashley said. “Honestly she needed an attitude adjustment anyway. That whole snotty rich girl schtick was getting on everyone’s nerves anyway.”

    Claudia now concentrated on Angie, watching as the middle age woman began to grow younger once more. The fine lines around her eyes and mouth faded as she crept backwards through her 30s and 20s. As Angie once again became 20 the girls marveled at the her. Angie was now dressed in thrift shop clothing with cat eye glasses. The blonde girls hair fell to her waist and appeared somewhat wild. The girls previously well kept locks now appeared somewhat like horse hair, obviously Angie was no longer interested in looks alone, instead focusing on her acting and others.

    “Wow she’s got kind of a hippie look to her,” Mary Elise said. The girls nodded as memories of Angie now flooded their mind. Angie Hart was now the kindest girl they had ever met. The eccentric girl never had an unkind word for anyone, treating people with kindness. Angie’s humility made her one of the most likable girls in her sorority.

    “Not bad,” Mika mused. “Still theirs just one more thing.”

    The others gave Mika a curious look as she touched her pendant. The others watched as the leggy blond began to grow shorter. The others watched as the formerly 5’10” girl grew shorter. Before Angie stood out amongst the crowd as they watched her they could see her head begin to sink lower. As Mika released her pendant the girls now gaped at the now 5’2” girl in front of them.

    “That’s for the little lesbo comment,” Mika grinned. The others sighed, shrugging at the Mika. “Seriously Mika,” Kellie moaned. “Isn’t that a bit petty?”

    “What,” Mika grinned impishly. “I think she’s adorable like this. Besides her being tiny just makes her that much cuter.”

    Almost on que Angie now walked up to the girls, smiling warmly as she hugged Ashley. “Oh Ashley this was such a lovely party,” she sang. “Truely an inspiration, you truely know how to bring joy to the masses.”

    Ashley gave the shorter girl an odd look responding with an unsure grunt. Angie now turned to Mika, finding she was now two inches shorter than the Asian girl. “Mika,” Angie said warmly, looking upwards at Mika. “Truely it was an honor to meet you. The love you share with Claudia is the type only found in the greatest of plays, as the bard once said...parting is such sweet sorrow.”

    Angie hugged Mika and Claudia tightly before skipping away to join the rest of her sorority, her long skirt swaying as she seemed to bounce through the home. Katie and her friends watched as the girl bounded amongst the party guests.

    “Well this just got a whole lot more interesting,” Ashley mumbled. “Instead of a snob now she’s a freaking flower child. Remind me not to piss you two off.”

    Katie and the others said their goodbyes, inviting Ashley and her sisters to the party later that evening (especially Angie, the girl was such a ray of sunshine that they couldn’t help not wanting her present). As Katie pulled up to the mall she could see Gloria and her friends happily chatting with one another. As Katie waved to her sister she saw Gloria, Jackie, Ann and Sasha wish the others a good day as they piled into the car.

    “Hey Gloria how was shopping,” Katie asked as the teens tossed their packages into the trunk.

    “Awesome,” Gloria gushed. “Got most of my Christmas shopping done. Would of completed all of it if Jackie, Ann and Sasha here hadn’t spent so much time browsing Hot Topic.”

    “What,” Ann giggled. “Those clothes were so cute. Jackie and I found everything we could ever want to wear.”

    “Joys of being a teen with an adult bank account,” Jackie giggled, “You can make impulse purchases without emptying your bank account.”

    Katie grinned as the teens now laughed and joked with one another. “Glad you guys had a good time,” she said. “Now you ready to party?” The teens gave an enthusiastic cheer as Katie started the car, tonight was going to be a very interesting.
