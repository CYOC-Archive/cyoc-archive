Inside, they can see a large corral filled with donkeys, each one being inspected one at a time and then moved into an individual crate.

    The inspection process is fairly quick, they shine a small light into the donkey’s eye and then remove any remnants of humanity. Clothing, watches, earrings, hats, etc… Sam can see that the corral wraps around the side of the warehouse, almost to a large open door to the ferry. “Jake, if we sneak into the corral, we can hide among the donkeys and just make our way to the other end of the warehouse.”
