    Carrie filled out the papers, barely reading before signing them. Her mind was full of thoughts that were
    making it very hard to concentrate. Her mouth was dry, and her heart was beating hard, not just because of the

    The door opened, and the Doctor returned with a redhaired woman, her hair pulled up in a ponytail and dressed
    in white nurse's scrubs. "This is Jill," the doctor helpfully informed her. "She's been here two years, and a
    tier four for the past 6 months. You have NOTHING to worry about. She's bound by the same confidentiality
    anyone else here would be, and noone has EVER had a bad incident with her."

    "Great!" Carrie said, probably sounding too happy, she realized. "It's..umm..nice to meet you, Jill."

    Jill smiled, pulling her hair down out of the ponytail. "Ponytails are kind of a no-go if you know you're

    before nipping her earlobe lightly. "That's what the pheremones are for..just sniff me a few times..and you'll
    relax.."

    Carrie complied, burying her face into the hollow of Jill's collarbone and inhaling deeply several times. Her
    heart rate slowed, and she stared heavy-lidded into the woman's eyes. She'd been right, of course. Carrie
    wasn't nervous any more. She knew what she wanted. She wanted to bed this woman. Carrie was aware of a
    pinprick in her shoulder, and the doctor saying "I'll leave you two alone." The door opened once more and then
    shut again.
