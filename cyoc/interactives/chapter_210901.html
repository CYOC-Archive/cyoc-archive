    Nat noticed a couple of elegantly dressed women with a couple of shopping bags and thought it would be funny if they were teens again.

    "How about those two ladies into a couple of teens?" Nat suggested as Rei shrugged. Nat tried to use her ring on the women, but for some reason, the ring didn't seem to do anything to them and the two women stopped for a minute and looked around a little in confusion before noticing Rei and Nat and came over to the two of them.

    "By any chance would you two happen to know a little magic?" One of the women surprisingly asked them.

    "What?! Us? No, no way. We do not know magic, just the...magic of Christmas." Rei said trying to smile but it came off as a creepy grin.

    "It's alright girls, we know a little magic ourselves." The other woman said as she held the other woman's hand up to show a ring similar to Rei and Nat's ring.

    "You two are wizards?" Rei asked them.

    "Well, I am. My girlfriend just likes to tag along with me sometimes." The lady said.

    "Oh, uh, neat. I'm Rei and this is my friend, Nat. By any chance do you know a girl named Gloria?" Rei introduced herself.

    "I do in fact, she's one of my best friends, I'm Jackie by the way." The woman with the magic ring said.

    "And she's my niece, I"m Ann." The other woman said.

    "Wait a minute, she's one of your best friends? Does that mean..." Nat asked Jackie.

    "Don't let it get out but yeah, I'm 'younger' than I really look. I've been studying at Cornell at came down for a little bit to spend the holidays with my family and of course...my angel." Jackie said as she fluttered her eyes at Ann as she blushed back.

    "Anyway, I'm guessing you two are Christmas shopping while also doing a little 'magic' on the side?" Ann guessed.

    "Yeah, but we were thinking about maybe getting a bite to eat." Nat said as she looked towards the fast food court.

    "Why don't you two come with us? We're going to that new fancy restaurant they just put in here at the mall?" Jackie suggested.

    "I don't know if you noticed, but we're not exactly dressed for the occasion, and it would be a little weird to see a couple of teens sitting at a table in a restaurant and saying these ladies are relatives of a friend." Nat pointed out.

    "True, but, follow us...we're going to give you both a fancy makeover." Ann said as the girls followed Ann and Jackie into the women's restroom and made sure no one else was in there with them.

    Jackie used her ring on Rei first and aged her up into a woman that closely resembled her mother. Her clothes were transformed into a fancy Christmas dress with earrings to match. "Krasivaya..." Rei said looking at herself in the mirror.

    "Beg pardon?" Ann asked confused.

    "Oh, uh, beautiful. I'm part Russian, part Japanese." Rei informed them.

    Nat crossed her arms and looked away from the others.

    "Natty, come on, don't do this now. I've always thought you look beautiful. Come on, let's see what you look like as a grown woman." Rei asked holding her teenage girlfriend's hands.

    "Fine. But after we eat, we change back to teens." Nat said as Rei nodded in agreement.

    Nat had her eyes closed the entire time as she felt herself being aged up and her clothes transforming to look fancier. She almost didn't want to open her eyes until she felt Rei's arms around her encouraging her to slowly open her eyes. When she did, she almost couldn't believe what a beautiful woman she had turned out to be. She looked just as beautiful as her mom, when she saw Rei in the reflection, she turned to look at her.

    "So what do you...?" Rei began to ask before Nat immediately wrapped her arms around Rei and kissed her so passionately the two felt like Christmas had come early.

    "Let's give them a moment." Ann suggested.

    "Yeah, we have our own moment." Jackie smirked as the two of them also began kissing each other lovingly.

    The four of them soon stopped and exited the bathroom and headed for the mall's new fancy restaurant where they could talk more.
