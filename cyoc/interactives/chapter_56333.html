    The game would not, could not, dared not, even with only instinct, to touch, harm or
    interfere with Marie-Eliza, for it saw her as it's creator, it's master, it's MOTHER, for Marie-
    Eliza was after all, the idealized version of how the delusional Kurse had seen herself.

    And she felt every turn of the game used, and she knew a use of the cascade effect... which
    should have been impossible since the cascade effect and the game existed separately and
    independently from each other. She knew what Transform was gone, but figured Penny
    would sooner than later find out by fate and undo the change with her power... but then
    she felt the change happen to the punk.

    'This is not good at all.' Marie-Eliza thought. 'Best to make sure this doesn't result in a
    planet full of latex love dolls...' While she accepted the possibility the leoman cascade
    could result in a planet full of lion people being humane to each other, she knew no sane
    person would concede a world full of still love dolls would be a good thing!

    Creating an avatar of herself, transparent, only with a fraction of her real power, she
    continued to tell the tale without missing a beat of how Marie and her friends recovered
    while the witches created their fortress to protect their one remaining truly loved one and
    their small army of familiars.

    Her avatar left the world within mirrors, and became a mass of living solid glass. She
    looked at her naked body, now devoid of the black and red dress and blond fur, and now
    only indestructible transparent glass remained. She let her senses guide her to the bug
    within the game that HAD to be fixed before it resulted in another disaster. 'Not this time.
    No snowflake is going to cause an avalanche THIS time!'

    Madam Serenity sensed a turn of the game had been used. 'It never ends.' She sighed,
    there would always be someone. Which made the need to fix this mess of a game even
    more so! Just because she had been cursed to be never be effected by ANY transformation
    EVER didn't mean she was going to sit on her rear ever to let this curse run around like
    mad.

    And her sense was silently different now, it was like a veil was gone. He could sense a turn
    had been used. And she could almost -feel- where the game was... she couldn't pin down
    a street address, but she could swear she could almost feel where it was and almost who
    and what, -almost-. 'Is it because the mind of the game is gone? And it now can't shield
    itself from me? Maybe.'

    She excused herself from the now mutated half-ape men brothers along with Judas the
    descendant of a witch's familiar and took a few samplings of the cure along with her just to
    be on the safe side (and made sure to memorize the prayer to destroy the curse).

    It would be in the most rundown part of town. Thankfully she was a legal and registered
    gun owner and had two life times worth of experience in self defense.

    She found a naked humanoid glass wolf pup waiting outside a door leading to a one room
    apartment.

    "You're not any of Julie and Frank's children."

    "No I'm not. Your safe because your curse precisely says you CAN'T be changed by even the
    gods themselves. I'm safe because of how this is mixed with the game... and you could say
    I'm the last piece of sanity that existed within the mind of Kurse."

    "I'm too tired of all this to be paranoid... and you look like that because?"

    "When Samantha was made Kurse's daughter, and was freed, she needed a replacement, I
    was handy nearby... when Kurse was destroyed I was given my mind back... but we both
    have something we need to take care of because it becomes a plague."

    "The leoman cascade?"

    "That's benign. And it's no longer truly connected to the curse anyway so the curse
    wouldn't effect it. But since THIS is mixed in with the game, so perhaps the cure will work.
    You have some with you?"

    "Yes."

    "Good. I'm Marie-Eliza by the way."

    "Madam Serenity. Alchemist. PI. Fortune teller. And former cop."

    "Nice to meet you." Marie-Eliza gestured at the locks on the door and they became undone
    and with another gesture opened the door.
