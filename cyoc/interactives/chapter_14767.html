    “Time to try something silly…” Ty thought to himself. Ty used to be 124 pounds and he was 5 foot 7 inches tall. He added 400 pounds of muscle and he was now nine feet tall.

    “I wanna shrink back to my old height, but I want to keep the same weight in muscle…” he wished out loud.

    Immediately Ty started to feel the change, but he wasn’t just shrinking. For every inch he shrunk, his weight had to be redistributed in his body. He wanted to keep his 524 pounds of muscle, but he didn’t want to remain nine feet tall. So, his body started to grow wider and bigger, since he was losing height at the same time.

    “Hmmm… This is interesting….” He thought to himself, as he saw how his body was growing wider and deeper, while he was slowly growing shorter.

    His back and his torso continued to expand sideways, as his body gradually continued to shrink in height. His legs grew wider and even thicker, forcing him to adjust his stance several times. This time, he was slowly turning in to a short muscle freak!

    His shoulders were over 6 feet wide, as his legs grew to 2 feet in width as well. His torso was also 5 feet across, and his massive chest was sticking out by at least four feet, and it was growing! Ty was growing wider and thicker, as he slowly passed the 6 foot mark.

    “Whao! This is sooooo weird!” Ty chuckled, as he tried to examine his reflection in the tall mirror. But the mirror just wasn’t wide enough to show all of him, for his shoulders were now over six feet wide!

    “I look like the compressed muscle man!” he laughed, as he admired the results of his silly wish in the mirror.

    “Heck, I got the power, I might as well have some fun while changing myself…” the massive teen thought to himself, as he slowly turned sideways, while trying to keep his gaze on the reflection in the mirror.

    “My shoulders are almost as wide as the length of my bed…” he chuckled to himself, as he walked closer to the mirror.

    “Alright, I’m tired of being big and heavy…” he thought, as he decided that it was now time to do some shrinking.

    Ty stood still, as he wondered what to change next.
