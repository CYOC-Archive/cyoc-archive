    "All right, boys, let's get going!" Nate Lewis, a 19 year old boy, said, stepping out of his car, being sure to shut the door as he did so, breathing in the air of Las Vegas. As he got out, three other boys followed as well, not exactly sharing his enthusiasm. There was Benjamin Lincoln, an 18 year old that was a stickler for the rules, Edwin Dias, a smart 16 year old kid that enjoyed trying new things, and Jack Collins, a 19 year old who was basically just going along for the ride.

    "Nate, do you have any idea what we're doing here?" Benjamin asked tiredly, feeling fed up with Nate's antics, not really interested in this whole casino outing.

    "Come on, Ben, live a little!" Jack said, nudging his shoulder somewhat forcefully. "We're gonna walk out of here rich, just you wait! And then we'll be able to live like kings for the rest of our lives!"

    "If only every gambler that said that was right..." Edwin lamented, trailing off before glancing back at his phone. "Anyway, this casino has some pretty strange rumors surrounding it, rumors that involve trans-"

    "Edwin, can you please shut up about that transforming nonsense?" Benjamin said, tired with Edwin's antics as well. "It's all just a bunch of government hogwash designed to scare you. And when it is real, it's basically a bunch of freak shows on wheels."

    "Well, it doesn't scare me-" Edwin was about to say, but was cut off by Nick, as the 19 year old made a grand gesture towards the casino building in front of them. It was certainly big, perhaps one of the biggest casinos in Vegas, and it simply oozed out a rich feeling, yet also one that invited others to come and take as many chances as they wished. A large line was quickly forming around the building and extending for a few blocks, but luckily, Nate brought his fast pass.

    "As my buddy Jack said," Nate said, getting behind the other 3 boys as he pushed them towards the building, flashing the pass to one of the security guards as they skipped past the lengthy line, entering the casino. "Today is the day that we all get rich! If we start at 10 million and then work our way up, we'll be well off for the rest of our lives!"

    Upon entering the casino, all four of the boys couldn't help but let their jaws drop as the sights of the casino. It was way bigger than the outside let on, and even then, that was pretty big. The place was packed with both people and attractions, and even tons of waitresses were milling about the place. Of course, the losers were currently sporting new 'features', but the boys were so struck by the grandness of the casino, they barely even noticed.

    Pumped up to go and see what kind of transformations this place had to offer, Edwin made a move to head off. "All right, see you guys later! I'll be-"

    He was quickly pulled back by Benjamin, however, the older boy taking hold of his shirt as he was dragged back to the group. "You're still a minor, remember? Can't really let you go off by yourself, now can I?"

    "But?!" Edwin was about to say, before realizing that there was no point in arguing. Jack and Nate would simply back Benjamin anyway, to make sure that Benjamjn couldn't give them a hassle about all this. Sighing, Edwin simply figured that he'd get to see what transformations this place had as he went.

    Nate smirked to his friends as he took the lead, taking a few moments for him and his friends to get their chips that were worth 10 million dollars, already having a good idea of which game he'd try out with his friends first. Which game did he take them to?
