    Rachel rushed out of the house and over to Dawn's place. She felt funny as she went, but chalked it up to the alcohol.

    When she got to Dawn's front door, her friend looked at her and said--"Rachel, what took you so long--hurry up and come on in."

    She ushered Rachel on in to the house and then into her bedroom. She had shot glasses ready and before she knew it, she had poured a shot and downed it.

    "Oooh, that is really good," she told Rachel and then the two proceeded to down a couple more.

    About that time Dawn looked over at her friend and noticed she appeared to look different. She couldn't put her finger on why though.

    "Hey there Rachel ol gal," she said happily, "come with me."
