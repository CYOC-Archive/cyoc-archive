    Nathan was none too happy with that transformation into a rabbit, but surely couldn't do anything but take a glance at Bobby, holding the holy Chronivac in his hands.
    If only there was a way to reach out for it...
    But then, the boy decided that would be much funnier to experiment more and more with the device, until he did find the perfect pet for him and the house.

    Nathan simply scurried away, hiding under the bed, and behind some furniture buried within. Changing again was out of the question !
    Bobby did not care about the animal's feelings, he was just toying with what he though was a divine machine. The perfect Christmas present !
    Trying to spy his rabbit, he crouched right next to the bed, and waited for it to poke out it's muzzle.
    Nathan firmly stood his grounds. Was he that dumb to fall for that dim-witted trap ?
    Bobby grew annoyed (and disturbed by the animal's behavior), and simply began pressing the device back and forth.
    Waves and waves of transformation rays were shot out through the bed, but it proved to be useless, since there was no target in clear range.
    That is, until Nathan accidentally tripped when readjusting his position : his left paw went in contact with the ray, and already was swelling a bit.
    Bobby beamed when seeing this, took his seat, and simply waited for the changes to occur.

    Nathan was none too happy with the incident, but there was nothing else he was able to do, but bear with the situation and accept it for now...
    Holding his paw as much as he could as a rabbit, he watched as it hardened and wrinkled, keeping it's color shades, but solidifying and dulling out.
    His left front leg soon followed up, and contorted in a different way. It definitely had a much reptilian aspect when it reached the beginning of his hips.
    Nathan had a brief idea of what he was turning into, but needed to see it for himself.
    Once the right leg mirrored it's other counterpart, his hips were greatly modified : he felt his cotton-tail thinning out, as his animalistic penis shrinked and hid behind a tiny cloacca. At least, it seemed he'd keep his manhood intact.
    He retained a quadrupedal stance, but had difficulties raising up again. As if his back became heavier...
    He knew. Deep inside, what he suspected was happening before his eyes.
    He was becoming a bulky and slow turtle.
    Nathan only grew more frustrated from it, his front legs growing out and shifting the same as the rest.
    And it was time for his face, his lovely bunny face that he lacked time to fully appreciate...
    He hated it when he felt his ears simply vanishing into some holes, his snout growing into a much bigger one, while he gained even more more buck teeth from all sides of his jaws. Looks like salad was going to be his only meal by now.
    Finally, as his eyes moved from side to side, he sighed, asserting his new turtle-ish brown body. There was no way he'd have what it means to be back into his former human body, at least not maybe before a long time...

    Moments later, Bobby saw the pet going out of his bed.
    " Oh ! " he said, surprised. " A turtle ! "
    " Yeah, yeah, no need to point out the obvious... " mentally sighed Nathan, annoyed as hell.
    Bobby looked at the reptilian creature, and then at the Chronivac held in his hand, unsure.
