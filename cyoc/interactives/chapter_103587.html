    A Werewolf Party

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 7 years ago A BM S

    "How about a werewolf party?!?!?" Giggles shouts to the crowd of giggling teenagers. You just help yourself to the red cake, pretending to not care.You lift a spoonful to your mouth and it's suprisingly really good, tastes sort of like meat, you keep eating soon devouring the whole slice. You grab another, then another, soon your just sticking your hand in eating it chunk by chunk. "Liking the cake huh?" The fat clown asks you. You look up and the entire room is staring at you wide-eyed, scared expressions on their faces. "Glad you're getting into the spirit!" You try to ask what he means but you instantly see it on your hands, admist the cake chunks and frosting you see it, thick black fur, covering your body! You start feeling yourself grow, your muscles expand ripping the clothes off your monstrous body. A pair of massive clawed feet burst through your shoes. You feel a sharp pain as a short tail bursts out your rear. Finally your head morphs into that of a wolf's. Complete with sharp fangs, pointed ears and long snout. Drooling you look up at the clown. He's changing too, gripping a large chunk of cake in his hand. Soon a 8ft fat, brown werewolf towers over you, make up still stuck on his snouted wolf face. The two of you look at the crowd of screaming teenagers. They try to escape but somehow they can't, the doors won't unlock and the glass windows strangely resist any attempts of being broken. Unfortunately for the guests they are now trapped in your house with two hulking werewolves.
