She still recognized them, but it was still strange to see them like them are now. They had offered to come in with her, but she had refused, she wanted to face this challenge alone.
    “Mary Maxwell are you ready?” inquired the Doctor as Mary nodded her head and slowly undressed taking the time to even fold her cloths to buy her a few extra minutes as a human.
    “It will be painless.” Reassured a nurse as Mary gave a sad nod, she didn’t care if it was painful or not.
    “I am ready.” Said Mary as the doctor injected her. Her time swimming had taught her how to control her breathing but even she had trouble. For a moment nothing happened and then she felt it. Her shoulder length dirty blonde hair fell away in clumps. She opened her eyes and looked at a large mirror across from her. She was now totally bald.
    “You have got to be kidding me.” Said Mary as she ran her hand over her head, she felt multiple large bumps. “What the?”
    Then yanked her hand back as out of her head, multiple aqua blue scaled snakes began to grow, they had black diamond patterns on them.
    “My GOD!” yelled Mary as the snakes began to move around some of them snapping at each other showing teeth of fangs. Others rubbed against her cheek as the snake hair grew out until they reached her shoulder blades as her eyes turned a bright yellow and silted. She knew enough about monsters to know what she was becoming.
    “Don’t worry Medusas don’t turn people to stone.” Reassured the doctor as Mary looked on as her own face changed, her forehead became covered with blue and black scales in a semi beauty makeup style. Her only face elongated slightly as she felt two of her teeth fell out and two larger fangs grew in. Her skin shifted color to a neon aqua blue but no scales appeared outside of her forehead. She couldn’t decide if her breasts had increased in size or her frame narrowed either way it looked like her bust had grown.
    The skin tone shift happened across her whole body and then she felt it. Her legs being pulled together, she watched as her legs fussed as scales began to form around her hips and travel downward. The same pattern of scales which graced her new “hair” now graced her one limb legs.
    A few tears ran down her face, she had loved to swim and now she didn’t even have legs. Her new tail length out until it was nearly double the length they used to be.
    “A snake woman…I am a snake women.” Cried Mary as the doctors took a photo of the scales pattern.
    “Yes, but if this is correct the snake pattern you are based on a yellow-lipped sea krait.” Stated the doctor
    “A what?” inquired Mary
    “See a medusa or a naga is based around a real-world type of snake and it tell us a lot about what you are capable of.” Stated the doctor as he showed an image of a snake, the scales and pattern did match her. “You are based on a venomous sea snake.”
    “Sea snake?” inquired Mary
    “Yes, you are probably very fast in the water, but we have never had a sea snake medusa to my knowledge.” Explained the doctor, that was something at least for Mary, she could still swim.
    “Here…this top should do.” Said a nurse as Mary looked to see basically a black bikini top.
    “Don’t I need a bottom?” inquired Mary as she looked down to see scales covering her womanhood. “I guess that will not work will it?”
