    A Girlfriend's Surprise

    Dan looked up at the sign in confusion, and then back down at his phone. Nope, there wasn't a mistake; this was the place his girlfriend, Kat, had wanted him to meet her at this evening. She hadn't explained why; she'd just said it was urgent. But why did she want to meet him at a lesbian bar, of all places?

    Putting his phone away, he walked inside. The place seemed cozy enough, full of women - and a couple of men - laughing and chatting happily, and some of the women were making out with each other. Still, something about the place set the hair on the back of his neck on end, like he was somehow too masculine to be welcome here. Looking around, Dan didn't see Kat anywhere, and so he made his way over to the bar to ask.

    The bartender was just passing a drink over to a redheaded guy - one of the few other men in the place - when Dan cleared his throat. Turning to him, she introduced herself as Liz - *the* Liz, he guessed - and he told her who he was looking for. Liz smiled at him in a rather ... disconcertingly appraising way. "I thought I'd be seeing you tonight, hun," she said. "Kat's in one of our back rooms, just down that hall. Room Four. She's waiting for you. Have fun!"

    Still sure that something about this didn't add up, Dan thanked her and turned to head off in the direction she'd pointed. As he did, he brushed passed the well-endowed redhead next to him, then stopped and did a double-take. He'd been certain that person was a man earlier, but now they clearly looked like a woman, with shoulder-length red curls and a low-cut black top showing off some cleavage. Dan shook his head; clearly the place was getting to him. He needed to find Kat and figure out what was going on.

    He made his way down the hallway and stopped at Room Four, knocking once. Someone was giggling on the other side, and after a moment Dan took that as a yes and opened the door - and the sight that met his eyes shocked him. There was a semicircular couch around the room's far wall, and on it were two girls who were passionately making out, both of them stripped down to their underwear. And one of those girls - the one who currently had the other's hand down the front of her panties - was Kat! Dan could only stand there and stare, letting the door swing shut behind him. Kat had never shown any sign of being dissatisfied with their relationship, or any indication that she was anything but straight - and here she was, not only in the process of cheating on him, but with another woman!

    And the more Dan watched the scene, the more jealous he was, and the more he wished he could be part of that moment, that *he* could be the girl pressed up against Kat...

    He must have made some strangled noise, because the two girls pulled apart and turned to look at him, both smiling knowingly. "This must be Dan," the stranger said, grinning lazily.

    "You bet!" Kat said, getting to her feet and sauntering over to him. "I was wondering when you'd get here, babe!" She leaned in and planted a firm kiss on Dan's lips; he thought he could taste some sort of drink on her mouth as she pulled away, and suddenly felt a weird buzzing fill not only his head, but his whole body. "Now, then," she said. "I bet you're wondering what's going on here. Well, if you'll just sit down, we'll fill you in..."
