    Dan Is Back... But...

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy 3 months ago O Magic

    Sunday evening arrives quickly, Ken, who now has long smooth blond hair, blue eyes and pierced ears, is sitting in his room playing with a dolly, Ben, who now has paws for hands and feet and his spine “rearranged” to life on all fours, is curled up in his room on his dog bed having a nap. Kat casts a special spell, a spell that, when Kat is nowhere near the two boys, everything returns to normal, including their rooms, with the boys having no memory of the “game”, but as soon as Kat steps into their house, or is close to one or both of the boys, the boys will “slip” back into their “roles”: Ken becoming Kat’s “daughter Kenna” and Ben becoming their “pet dog”, and the “game” will continue where it left off. Even when it’s not official, like if Kat would to encounter one of the boys in public, they’d still take on their “role”, either as the “daughter” or the “pet”, and if Kat encounters both boys in public, well… you get the idea.

    When Kat returns home after the weekend “babysitting” Ken and Ben, the witch head straight for her room, her parents both aren’t there, and opens her secret vault and grabs the jar that contains the tiny blue orb, that is Dan’s soul.

    Kat opens the lid of the jar, she then grabs the soul of Dan, which at first tries to avoid being grabbed but fails, and places it in a pentagram she had drawn on the floor, and suddenly a naked Dan is standing in the center of the pentagram, looking around, then down at himself, then at his evil sister, a confused expression on his face, which quickly turns into a nervous expression when he notices the evil glint in the girl’s eyes and the smirk on her face.

    “Let me guess,” Dan says, “you just brought me back to have more “fun” with me?”

    Kat’s smirk grows into a grin, “I must say, Dan, you seem to have become smarter while in soul form,” she says, “you are so right!”

    Dan sighs, “so, what “life” have you chosen for me this time,” he asks.

    Kat seems surprised, “oh? No pleading? No protest?”

    Dan shrugs, “what’s the point,” he asks, “I know it’ll do me no good, there is nothing I can do to stop or prevent you from causing me to suffer. So, instead of wasting my energy on pleading and protesting, I decided to let you just do your thing”.

    Kat thinks for a while, she really hadn’t expected this from her brother, “answer me one question: how did it feel like being eaten?”

    “Honestly,” Dan says, “you KNOW exactly how I felt”.

    Kat smirks, “true, your cries of pain as me and our parents ate you, has been music to my ears,” she says, “but… I’d like to hear your thoughts”.

    “Well, apart of being eaten “alive”… the experience was kind of… interesting,” Dan says, “despite the pain and suffering I went through, being transformed into a turkey, then stuffed with filling, then finally cooked and eaten… all that has been a very interesting experience”.

    “I see,” Kat says, she then thinks for a while, “okay, let’s make a deal,” she then says.

    Dan tilts his head, “what kind of deal, and what will I get from it?”

    Kat grins, “if you willingly allow me to transform you into anything I come up with, I promise to make it less… unpleasant for you,” the girl says, “I shall give you the ability to respawn as your normal self whenever you get killed, damaged, eaten… you will respawn and I’ll transform you into something else”.

    “So, like a video game character,” Dan asks.

    Kat nods, “yeah,” she says, “I’ll even allow you to make a suggestion, if there is something you are curious about, I’d be willing to transform you into an animal or an object, or change your gender and/or age”.

    Dan thinks for a while, “you know, that does sound tempting,” he says, “but I assume, you don’t want me to enjoy it, right?”

    Kat thinks for a while, “you know… I got what I wanted from the “turkey feast” scenario,” she says, “I may be evil, but even I think it was more than enough suffering, and I feel at peace that I got my revenge on you for all those mean “pranks” you pulled on me”.

    “So… we’re even,” Dan asks.

    Kat smiles, a friendly smile, and nods, “yup,” she says, “but I still have so many ideas what to transform you into, but now, those curses or transformations, you will not suffer, instead I’ll make sure you’ll enjoy them”.

    “In other words: I am nothing but a toy for you,” Dan asks.

    “Well, yeah”.

    “What about mom,” Dan asks, “won’t she have something against you transforming me?”

    “Not if it’s willingly,” Kat says, “and by the sound of it, you’d be WILLING to be transformed or cursed by me”.

    “Right,” Dan says.

    “So, no, I won’t get into trouble”.

    Dan thinks for a while, “okay, Kat,” he then says, “I allow you to transform me into anything you come up with, with you giving me the ability to suggest being transformed into something, and your promise of not having me suffer”.

    “I accept the conditions and vow to uphold them,” Kat says.

    Suddenly the atmosphere in the room seems to tingle, “what was that,” Dan asks.

    Kat smiles, “by me accepting your conditions and vowing to uphold them,” she explains, “the deal is officially sealed, if I should go against any of the conditions I agreed to, I’ll forever lose my power or in the worst case: I’ll lose my life”.

    Dan’s eyes widen, “that serious?”

    Kat nods, “yup”.

    “Welp, guess there is no turning back now,” Dan says.

    “Nope”.

    “Okay,” Dan simply says, causing Kat to laugh.

    “So, ready to be transformed into something,” Kat asks, “or, do you by any chance have something of your own in mind?”

    What do you do now?
