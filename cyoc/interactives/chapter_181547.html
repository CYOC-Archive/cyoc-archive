This was it, one drink and she’d have the confidence to finally ask him out. Then they could be together forever, she’d get married to him, carry his babies, and build a life next to him. She shakily held that water bottle in her hands while Mason sat down and reached for the other one, bringing it to his lips and taking a big drink from it.

    She had to have a boyfriend, a chick that hot was dating someone and the last thing he wanted was to get rejected because of that. But wait, what did he care? You missed all the shots you didn’t take. There was a girl right beside him with all these luscious curves and a pretty face. Sure she could be dating but if she was she’d never shown any signs of it.
