    In the last three days, Aimee had regressed back in age until she finally stopped at the age of four. But the most confusing things of all - besides her sister Abby's transformation and the fact that their mother and babysitter acted like nothing was wrong - was the name Bobby.

And then, just as suddenly as the notion appeared, it vanished as Abby awoke.
