    Amy turned to her and asked, “Really?”

    “Sure thing. I don’t like it to see a lonely girl crying by herself. I’ll keep you company until they arrive.”

    Amy was quiet for a brief second until she decided to stay with her. “Okay….” She said to her, “You can stay until mommy and daddy get here.”
