    Lord Edward turns into a fearsome female dragon monster

    added 11 years ago A TG O

    Lord Edwards had always been a cowardly man,weak and weeping.

    So after the Coven of Warlocks had turned his niece into a dragon
    for stealing their Sacred Key,rather then do the manly thing
    and marching up to his scaly niece in the wilds and ending her
    cursed existence either with a kiss or a sword to her heart,
    he'd just sat in the Castle wine cellar and drank his trubles
    away whilst the scaly monster ravaged the lands.

    Then his brave son Edwin had come back with a fine norse
    ship and 33 brave but rowdy Vikings.

    His son the hero had dragged Edward into the boat and off they'd sailed
    with the rowdy boisterous Vikings singing raiding songs to their God Wotan.

    Lord Edward had remained cowering in the boat whilst the 33 brave crew and
    his son marched off into the watery dark woods to deal with niece Greta.

    They never came back.

    The Sun slowly slipped lower and a fearful weeping Edward heard the splash
    from the shore as a huge sinuous body slipped into the water and undulated
    towards the boat.

    Lord Edward knew his monstrous niece was coming to slay him with her toothy
    saliva laden blood dripping crocadillian jaws.

    So it was he was weeping and blubbering when his nieces head slowly rose
    out of the water on her scaly lengthy neck.

    Lord Edwards was mere seconds away from death by fright or her teeth.

    Now the nasty dragoness spirit who'd given the Coven of Warlocks the
    powerful magics they'd needed to turn the sweet innocent Princess Ann
    into a fiercesome proud monstrous dragoness,happened to be observing this
    encounter.

    The spirit always was up for a challenge,and nor did she want the pitiful
    human to go down snivelling and bawling.

    So she poured herself into Lord Edward and though he knew it not at
    that moment,in a manner of seconds Lord Edwards was no loner human,
    but a dragoness who was not yet aware of her nature.

    So it was that Edarignatious the Dragoness suddenly lost the ability to
    weep.

    Barely had this fact registered on the being then she noticed that the dragon
    striking at her with open jaws seemed to be slowing its strike.

    A suddenly fury boiled inside Edarignatious and as she leapt to
    avoid her nieces toothy jaws,her own body exploded into its proper dragon body
    and it was as a roaring 4 legged dragoness somersaulting in the air that the
    former Lord Edwards struck the waters surface.

    The 2 huge fierceome beasts,each weighing 10 tons apiece,stood up in the
    suddenly shallower waters,coiled their necks in attack stance,and
    roared at her opponent...
