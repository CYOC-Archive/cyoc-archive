    "Great mom." Alexis replied. "We're here on official business, by way of Snowy. Shi requested that we do more searching in their branch here. They caught the culprit, one of them at least, behind the outbreaks." Shi hugged hir mother.

    "Well, actually, seeing as we hadn't told you, me and Flake are pregnant."

    Justin chuckled. "I see, so me and your mother's going to be grandparents? Well, we both are pregnant as well, for a second time."

    After their visit, Flake and Alexis had made their journy to the facility and started to snoop. "Can you believe it's been six years since we've came to Paris?"

    "Flake, you know dad will do a good job of that already without our help." Shi giggled.

    "You know you're right."

    The two searched the place for clues and found a photo. Flake had stopped dead in hir tracks and found a photo of Li Sang plus another person, shaking hands. Shi swore shi's seen this man someplace before, but couldn't put hir finger on it, so shi grabbed the picture. "Um, you think, that could be Li Sang's partner, Flake?"

    Flake nodded. "That is why we're taking it back with us to investigate. I kinda figured he couldn't be working alone, but this could confirm it and have the guy that was the spy at the original facility. Let's see if we can't get a fax... ah here we go." Shi was glad that this place ran on it's own power, and was even further relieved to find that it was a working fax. So shi faxed the paper and called Snowy. "Did you get it?"

    "Yes, we got it, and that phone is on speaker." Snowy's said. "I know that man very well. He is... shit. MAURICE get your tail in here. Raul, we got a problem." Shi yelled, putting that phone on speaker. Raul walked in with Maurice.

    "What is it my love?" shi spoke up.

    "Li Sang was half of the mastermind behind the attacks." Snowy said.

    Raul looked. "Who's the other half?" he asked.

    Maurice almost flipped when he saw the picture. "Fuck. My brother. Why the hell.... I trusted him with our secrets."

    "Well, dear it seems that he's shared them with Li Sang. But it could just be coincidence." Snowy said. "But until we get him captured, we won't know."

"You two are incharged until we get some employees over there. Be careful and keep us up to date on everything."
