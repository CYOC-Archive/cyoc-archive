    They weren't sure what to do. Jake thought it would be a good idea to just put on their merged clothes again, but Kel argued that they had already worn them, and that they were starting to smell.

    "Well what are we suppose to wear?" Jake shifted their weight to his foot and looked at Kel. Kel stared at the ground and would have been fidgeting with his feet, but that would have made the brothers topple over. Jake took a breath, "We could wear mine. They're bigger than yours so they should fit our shared body better."

    "Ok!" Kel's head snapped up, sounding very eager. He had always secretly (or so he thought) tried on his older brother clothes when he wasn't home, and now he had an excuse. They entered Jake's room and headed for his dresser. Everything in the room was neat and organized, and Jake's dresser was no different. Kel was so excited that he let Jake pick whatever he wanted. They were his clothes after all, and Jake knew exactly which ones Kel liked to try on.

    They were wearing another pair of dark jeans with a belt, and because of their four arms, Jake had chosen a sleeveless shirt. Kel was thrilled with the choice, and Jake was happy that Kel liked it.

    Having dressed, the brothers headed downstairs just in time to hear the phone ring. It was the school, letting They're parents know that neither of them had shown up. Kel was a bit panicked, but Jake reassured him that everything would be fine, and proceeded to delete the message from the machine.

    Their stomach gave a low growl which made the pair smile. Jake rubbed their tummy, "Guess we're hungry body bro."

    "Ya, I guess we are!" Kel's smile grew and he held out his fist so Jake could bump it with his own, and then do their own special handshake before heading into the kitchen.
