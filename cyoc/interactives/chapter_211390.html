    Something changed, Nothing gained

    Anna looked speechless. Her pose looking less confident as she stared at the obviously powerful foe in front of her. The fusion of Terri and Niamh instinctively put her hands in front of her, as if ready to box. "Come on! You scared or something?"

    She could hear Bibi sigh inside her head. "Please try to stop this from getting out of hand." She nodded her head, and once again shot out a powerful beam of pink lightning. The energy crackling in the air as it lit up the atmosphere with electricity and magic. Aimed straight at the fusion girl, she expected a direct hit, but with what must've been an immense amount of speed, the girl quickly flipped back mid air and avoided the attack. The lightning shooting behind her and blasting through a nearby building, bimbofying any civilian nearby.

    Before Anna could react, her opponent was suddenly within striking distance of her. Without hesitation the fusion lunged forward and went for a powerful uppercut. It struck Anna right in the jaw, but surprisingly it didn't hurt all too much, leaving her to giggle a bit at the disappointing power.

    The Terri-Niamh fusion yelled ferociously as she unleashed a flurry of different attacks. Ranging from simple punches to flipping back and trying to kick Anna, before finally forcing her palm into the toned bimbo's stomach and shooting out her green lighting, sending the blonde flying back into a pile of rubble.

    Miraculously she still didn't feel much pain, just little stings here and there that quickly dissipated. "Wha-" She looked confusedly at her body, trying to figure out if she was just that strong, or if her assailant was just that weak. Before she could decides however, Bibi answered. "This is the gift of my power, you'll never have to worry about being harmed while you serve me."

    A devious smile spread across Anna's face as she finally started to realize just how strong this form has made her. "Cool..." She said under her breath, quickly standing up and launching herself back in the fight.

    The two stacked super-powered babes continued fighting. Anna retaliated fast and hard, unleashing her own flurry of attacks. She's had more time getting used to this than her opponent, and while her mental capacity has been reduced due to her new nature, she still was a fast learner.

    She giggled as blow after blow struck the fusion, before finally she started to charge something within her. A small pink glow could be seen growing brighter from the core of her chest. The fusion looked on in confusion and kept her guard up.

    Eventually the glow became bright enough that her opponent had to look away to avoid being blinded. Anna moaned at the intense pleasure this gave her, but nonetheless kept building the power within until Bibi gave her permission to unleash it.

    "Now..." The voice in her head said with a sultry tone.

    Anna giggled and threw her arms out to the side, releasing her hold over the light. Suddenly a bright pink aura along with that familiar pink lightning shot out of her in all directions. It quickly surged out, consuming every building and person in an increasingly expanding area.

    Civilians tried to hide from the explosion of energy, but even through walls of the buildings they were struck, all of them changing rapidly to fit Bibi's desires. Blonde bimbos popped up all over within the affected radius, along with the buildings changing a bit as well. Each boring old structure shifting to better fit the bimbo lifestyle.

    The fusion girl put her arms up to block the blast. She was forced back a bit but ultimately stood her ground enough to remain close to Anna. She clenched her teeth, trying to keep it together as the transformative blast threatened to permeate her once again. It even succeeded in the most trivial of ways, as tiny parts of her hair shifted from either red or black to platinum blonde, leaving her with overall the same hair color, just with a little speckle of blonde. She was obviously very resistant to the magic, and was able to fend it off for the whole duration of its effect.

    Anna was left speechless and out of breath. That was her ultimate attack, and it barely even did anything to the girl. If she looked closely she could see a couple blonde strands of hair... But at that rate, she'd have to hit her with that attack hundreds of more times before it made any real change to her.

    "Heya Bibi? She's not budging..." She said out loud.

    "Yes... I can see that... She's stolen my power and changed it into something else, something powered by her own natural state. This'll be tough to deal with."

    The fusion let's out a small chuckle, distinctively an antagonizing laugh rather than a ditzy giggle. "Is that really all you got?" Taking a moment to fix her clothes and retain some level of decency, the black and red haired girl cracks her neck and once again flies right at Anna with a warriors yell.
