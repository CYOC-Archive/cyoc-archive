    Thanksgiving 2021 was somewhat larger than it was last year, but it was still smaller than it normally was. Fortunately, there was plenty of things happening during the week to prevent things from being too boring, some more amusing than others.

    ---

    What was probably holding the most attention in terms of Animalians were the turkey hunts in both Animalia and Dinotopia. While a good deal of them were able to be relocated without too much trouble, there were a few who were just so "established in their territory" that the predator Animalians had no choice but to take them down. This wasn't too much of a problem because, while the turkeys had a lot more mass and ferocity, the predators had sharp teeth and claws, not to mention arms and hands which helped them either break the neck or slash them enough for them to bleed out. However, due to relative inexperience, this was causing all kinds of mixed results, from "critical successes" to "near failures".

    ---

    Mayor Tarr was both relieved and annoyed after his meeting with Miss Fabina's friend. While he was relieved that he wouldn't need to be in charge of "Dino Porn" as it was known on the website, he now had another annoyance to deal with. Asians/Asian Americans were moving into Dino City with intent on escaping all the hate/hate crimes that were being leveled at them, particularly since there were a few delusional people who were convinced that the pandemic had been created in a Chinese lab as a bioweapon.

    There were also quite a few homosexual couples moving into Dino City. While this in and of itself wasn't too bad, it was what they were choosing for their conversions that were annoying, due in no small part to the internet. One infamous example was a couple that had chosen to become an Ankylosaurus/Utahraptor couple. Fortunately, it wasn't just male/male couples that were moving to Dino City. There were a few lesbian couples moving as well, so the Homosexual Anonymous program wouldn't be having too much trouble.

    ---

    Miss Juliet was having a few headaches of her own, mainly because people were assuming that she and Juan were dating. There was also the case of the Animalia Colony that the French Government was planning, which had annoyed her quite a bit at first. While Belle's Bêtes was in the "wrapping up" stage, there were a few other things that were part of the colony infrastructure. There was also the equivalent of Claw Coliseum, which the French Government were a bit of a loss for a name, even though everything else was already accounted for.

    However, the French Government knew not to contact Miss Juliet during Thanksgiving week, so she, Juan, and her girls were able to enjoy their own Thanksgiving dinner, though I don't know if France has anything similar.

    ---

    Another headache was actually inflicted on Angelyn's daughters. After her conversion into a Yellowfin Tuna, she had started showing mild RHS symptoms, causing her to be drawn to the male Fish Animalians at Mieni Atoll. Another result of the mild RHS symptoms was that she had started doing advertising for her Bed and Breakfast with herself in said advertisements (yes, like that Angelyn). Fortunately, her daughters were less embarrassed than daughters otherwise were about their mothers and also became Yellowfin Tuna Animalians.

    There was a great laugh on the website after they had posted their 'results'. Someone commented that they not come within fifty miles of Zatch Bell, then included a clip from the Anime of the same name where a girl offers Zatch a piece of Yellowfin Tuna, but doing so caused him to go completely crazy and not only eat hers, but everyone else's as well.

    ---

    A final amusing thing was more focused on Heiya Island's new tanuki, Jin. While Jin had been focused on getting a new wardrobe, there was a lot more to hir makeover than just getting new clothes. Shi also got lessons on how to be a 'proper' geisha and admission into Heiya Island's Temple of the Body, which was their equivalent of Claw Coliseum. Shi was full of mixed feelings, especially since the "shrine maiden", Rose, was a pink mouse with enormous muscles, which often caused ill-timed erections, much to the amusement/confusion of witnesses.
