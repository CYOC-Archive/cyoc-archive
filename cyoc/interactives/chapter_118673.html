    "Yeah, seriously what are you doing?" Macy's right head asked

    Before Macy could answer he shoved the book into his mouth and began attempting to shove it down his throat. "Macy!" You yelled concerned

    - STOP!! YOU DON'T KNOW WHAT YOUR DOING! I'M USELESS IF I'M DESTROYED!

    Macy seemed to hear the book's voice, but neglected it as he continued trying to huff the book down. "Okay, at first I was okay with this, but now I'm not. Because I'm pretty sure that is you die the we die, so for the love of god stop!" Macy's left head yelled

    Macy didn't care and continued attempting to swallow it, and eventually Daryn watched as the book slid slowly down Macy's long neck. When the book finally hit Macy's stomach he let out a belch then began breathing heavily.

    "Well...*huff*....Now I know what a paper cuts in a throat feels like." Macy said

    "I wouldn't call that a plus, but...." Macy's right head began but suddenly there was a surge across Macy's entire body.

    "Ow!" All of Macy's heads said at the same time, and then all of them paused.

    "Wait, are we....we're synchronized again." All of Macy's heads said

    "Wait, how?" Daryn asked

    -Because allegedly since your thick skulled friend swallowed me, it reversed all of the curses within me. I imagine that he also had all of the powers within the book too, or at least until...

    Suddenly Macy's stomach gurgled, andhe let out a huge fart. Daryn refrained from opening her mouth considering that her tongue was now also her nose and remained silent. "Aw man, you must be bad for me. I am not going to sleep well with indignation like this, but I know how this will end for the book. Tell us though, if we digest you do we retain the abilities that you said we would?" Macy's heads asked

    The book gave no response, so Macy's heads all sighed. "Well I guess I'm going to probably be on the toilet for awhile...or behind some bushes. I don't think that I can fit in a stall." Macy's heads said smiling and looking towards Daryn.

    Daryn was still closing her mouth trying to avoid smelling the hydra gas that's Macy just let out. Macy then sniffed the air and grunted,"Oh it's not that bad Daryn, besides now I can make sure that curse doesn't come back by transforming you.
