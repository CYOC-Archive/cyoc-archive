    Spectra browsed her cards until a smile glided across her face.

    She picked her chosen card out of the air and it quickly floated in front of the audience and grew into banner size for all to see.

    Katie shuddered as she saw a scary octopus tentacle appear on the card. Can that even be a tail?

    Spectra warmly smiled at Katie's cute shudder. "Don't worry. One of the best ways to overcome a fear is to understand it." Spectra reassured.

    Before Katie could respond, the crowd watched in stunned silence as a slimy, muscley flesh started growing from Katie's tail bone.

    They watched as the blue tentacle grew in length and girth and as it's many suction cups developed. They watched as mucus coated the boneless tentacle so it wouldn't dry up in the air.

    Once the tentacle was heavy enough to balance Katie out on the balls of her T-Rex legs, it stopped growing. It was dragging all over the floor. It was three times longer then Katie's height. Katie could already feel some of her new suction cups attaching to the floor.

    Katie was afraid. The new tentacle tail felt so different then the rest of her body. Even her T-Rex legs felt more natural then her new tail. She was overwhelmed by the new muscles and sensations that she is suddenly controlling.

    Spectra watched in understanding. "Don't worry. The new tail may seem scary and unknown at first but once you learn how to control it, you'll be one step closer to understanding it."

    Five new cards floated in front of Katie.

    "If the octopus tentacle felt too alien to you, then feel free to pick a card that's more comfortable to you." Spectra offered.
