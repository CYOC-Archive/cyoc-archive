    Your sis had always been a pampered creature always getting everything she wanted and more on her Christmas list and even when it wasn’t Christmases she got what she wanted or she performs this same routine till she got her way. Just look at her she’s nearly in her mid-twenties still acting like a spoiled child wrecking the shed to pieces for not getting her way. She’s burned through the wallets of over five boyfriends already, your parents, and finally your account and she’s never even had a job in her life and yet fills her closets with the highest end of fashion wears. Even now she’s complaining about shoes while wearing Italian made velvet boots, black silk leggings, an Egyptian cotton striped sweater and a 24k gold necklaces. She’s always had a heart of stone when it came about the people around her maybe it was time to make her match her personality.
    It wasn’t until she demanded to know what you were hiding behind you when she got your attention again. “You want to know where the money went” you said sarcastically as you felt around in the bag for a good stone to throw. “YES, where is it” she shrieked again I spent it all you replied “on this as you pulled out the red silken bag of stones”. The look on her face was precious as it contorted in confusion trying to understand, like you had once been she hadn’t any idea of the existence of magic let alone the power that you possessed in this seemingly mundane bag. “Are you fucking kidding me is that some kind of dragons and dungeons shit you spent our money on THAT” she replied still having no idea as you grabbed one of the stones. She’s standing just a few feet across from you, it would be hard for you to miss but as you looked at her one last time and thought to yourself could you really do this would you turn your own sister into stone? Maybe family is off limits like think of all the good memories you and Katie had. But as you tried to think of some all you could remember were you two as kids when all she did was steal your toys, or when she plundered your allowance or framed you for things she did to get out of trouble. Your eyes squinted as a malicious smile stretch across your face you held one of the stones firm in your grasp and took aim “ hey sis catch” you said as you launched one of the gorgon stones in her direction. Your heart raced there’s no going back now as your magical story really starts going and finally giving your bratty sis some long overdue justice.
