    “So how are you with domestic animals?” Tom then asked. “Oh they’ve been my life for as long as I’ve known myself” Jill started “I cared for my neighbours pets growing up. I actually have a, maybe infinite, rapport of playfulness and friendship with animals of the domestic kinds” bringing her index finger to her lip “I’ve actually had dreams of being one” then clasped her mouth after realizing what she had said.

    “You don’t say” Tom responded in wonder just as his Smartphone (Only thing it seemed he had to match the current modern world) sent him an alert which he quickly checked like a pro “Actually I have something to show you that you might take interest in about that”. “IT WAS A SLIP OF THE TOUNGE” Jill insisted. “Relax. Heck a few housed dens for certain lizards of rodent-type pets. “Here are where those waiting for fosterers or adopters stay while they wait” Tom introduced “Depending on the coming outcome to what I’m about to show you you’ll probably get to know many of them well”. “Is this a job offer?” Jill questioned. “If you want it to be” Tom accepted as they continued into this cabin located right in the middle of the fields’ core area.

It’ll be quick” the girl in the circle followed unashamed.

    Before Jill knew it Tom had walked into this smaller circle nearby yet similar to the one Maxine was crouching, a simple line linking to two, and started making odd hand gestures while speaking words of a language Jill was unfamiliar in. But before she could ask Maxine’s mum what this was about he suddenly saw what this was all about...

Heck once Tom was done as opposed to a naked teenage girl a rather young Marine Coon cat was seated in the circle wearing the collar Maxine had been wearing only shrunk down to fit.

Your daughter is ready for her...interesting summer with your sister here” Tom assured while kindly stroking the held cat’s chin “And my apologises she had to be an example for a possible new experience-taker”. “It was her having issues I was talking about” Mrs Owens confessed while the kitten ran out of the circle and to her “Now let’s head on home you two”. “Y-y-you just...turned that girl...into a cat” Jill shuttered in fused shock and amazement. “At her request and mother’s approving” Tom assured before going into detail.

    “I do more than simple pet sales here. I also offer visitors something many like you confessed yourself to chance to be a domestic pet” Tom started. “So a number of the animals in the field outside are people you’re...selling?” Jill inquired. “Not without their consent and the right arrangements made beforehand I assured you” Tom continued “That said really such setup is very rare as I do revert many after either a set time or they and their chosen caretakers come back for that”. “Do tell?” Jill requested. “Well using Maxine and her aunt Lisa as examples usually someone coming to experience being a pet assigns someone they trust as their caretaker. In Maxine’s case I’m expecting her mother to come back with her towards the end of the current summer holidays” Tom continued “Her aunt’s a more complex case. Can’t go into too many details but she’s had a number of bad romantic experiences and it left her wanting to escape humanity for awhile and based on a review that same day with Maxine where I briefly revert her to talk I will either make her a cat again of allow her to leave her human. Her choice”.
