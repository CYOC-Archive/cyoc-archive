    Lawrence approached the teachers, and replied with a cheerful "Hello Mr. Johnston!". Mr. Johnston was Lawrence's favorite teacher, and Lawrence often stayed after his classes to discuss things with him. It was strange when Mr. Johnston didn't reply to him, just kind of staring off into space. Lawrence was weirded out, and said hello to Mrs. Tarn and Mr. Paxton, but they also failed to acknowledge him, rather staring off into the distance. Lawrence looked off to see what they were looking at, but couldn't find anything. He noticed the people in the background of his vision also weren't moving. Something weird was going on.

He rubbed it, trying to get the sweat off. Lawrence laughed, he hadn't had hair in years. But wasn't he a student here? The thoughts running rampant through Lawrence's head were hurting his brain.

He looked around the hallway, it wasn't just the teachers that were growing shorter, it was the entire building. Wait, was he getting taller? That didn't make sense. Lawrence looked down at the other teachers, he was a bit taller than them. But he'd always been tall growing up, standing at a solid 6'5 now he was definitely taller than your average person. He knew it was important to be fit if you were to teach kids the importance of fitness.

The fabric of his jeans shifted into a mesh, as the legs grew shorter, resting at just below his kneecaps. The color turned into a deep blue, nothing like the denim blue of the jeans they once were. His white briefs morphed and changed into a pair of black boxerjocks. A little silver whistle morphed out of the end of the sash and it hung from around his neck on the rope.
