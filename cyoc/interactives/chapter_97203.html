Nearly everyone is a were-beast and won't be old until they're closer to a hundred and seventy five years old if our lifespans are now between two hundred and three hundred years..."

I mean... we have a good car... If I need to commute for work, that wouldn't be too bad... Just to get out of the house and so on."
