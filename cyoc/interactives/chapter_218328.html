    Lead Scientist: Dr. Cassandra Shaw

    Project Goal: To continue the research started by RS-00 formerly Dr. Janice Smith. Studying the effects of impregnation by a Riastrad affected entity. To insure that RS-02 and RS-03 remain in containment, and remain healthy.

    Initial Notes: Well, this is a right mess, at the time of writing this RS-00 and RS-01 are still unaccounted for, and their current condition is unfortunately unknown. Previously I recommend that Project Riastrad be abandoned, I had assumed that it was a dead end. To my predecessor's credit (despite the recklessness of her methods) I can't deny her results. That said I shall be refraining from using myself as a test subject moving forward. As for RS-02, she has thankfully been cooperative, RS-02 and RS-03 appear to be in good health.

    Project Description: Riastrad was discovered on 05/23/2018, in Ireland. In its natural form Riastrad is a herb, typically found in subterranean environments. Ingesting the herb has shown to give a temporary increase in strength and vitality at the cost of cognitive functions. In higher concentrations is was discovered that in had regenerative and mutagenic qualities. However, in previous experiments subjects exhibited undesirable mutations such as, Muscular hypertrophy, Leg Deformations, Organ Displacement (notably in the eyes), Heir Calcification, Tumor Like Growths, and other deformities. I had assumed this research was a dead end, and moved to terminate the project. To my surprise an independent scientist had continued my research, and despite their I'll advised methods, they had managed to produce remarkable results. However, the situation quickly grew out of control and I was put back on the project, do to my previous experience with the subject.
