    The picker runs through the list of names, as it slows down the names become more visible until it stops. The girls all look surprised. "Callum's going to be Aaron Matthews shoes?" Says Chloe.
    "Yep. I added a few guys from other classes just to keep it fair, and I guess it's landed on him."

    Callum screens. He's not going to have a girl's foot inside him, he's going to have a guy's foot inside him. At least a girl's foot has the chance of being attractive, a guy's doesn't. Especially with the smell.

    "Go and hand them over to him," says Miss Ewans
    "But won't he find that weird?" Asks Kimberly, "Handing over a random pair of shoes?"
    "I've affected his mind so that as soon as he receives them he'll put them on. Nobody in class will have an issue with it."
    "Oh okay," says Kimberly picking up the red chucks. "Guy's minds aren't that hard to manipulate. Especially teenagers. I'll show you how once you come back."

    Kimberly walks out of the classroom. Callum is screaming as much as possible, trying to do anything to stop himself from being handed over to Aaron. He'll even be a girl's shoe, just not a guy's shoe. Kimberly walks into Aaron's class and heads to his table.
    "Here you go," she says.
    "Thank you," replies Aaron. Callum is put on the classroom floor as Kimberly walks off.

    Callum looks up at Aaron. He's not too bad looking, about average. He's a little smaller than average but not by much. He is wearing black jeans, a white t shirt and an Adidas zipper jumper. His blonde hair is rather thin but styled into a little mohawk. His blue eyes look down at Callum.

    Callum feels a socked foot enter his mouth. Followed by presure and tugging of his hair which is actually the laces. Callum's main point of view is looking upwards, he can slightly see ahead of him but only upside down. This nearly locked perspective really gives Callum a sense of how small he has become.
