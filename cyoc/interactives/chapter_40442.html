    Three makes it more fun

    Mary walked over to a concession stand and ordered two drinks as well as a small bottle of strawberry sauce.

    She knew that most would consider what she was doing perverse. Tails however, enjoyed it. That was enough for her.

    With the drinks and sauce in hand, she returned to Tails chair and handed him a drink.

    "What's in the jar?" Tails asked.

    "Strawberry sauce," Mary said with a blush.

    Tails sipped from the beverage and blinked.

    "You mean you want to rub that on my -- my feet?" Tails asked.

    Mary blushed and smiled.

    "You're weird," Tails said with a chuckle. "But I'm always willing to try new things. It might be fun."

    "Can I?" Mary asked. "I mean, I know it's weird. I've just never done it and want to try. You never know what might happen tomorrow."

    Tails sipped the drink, looked at his feet and then at Mary.

    "Okay, why not?"

    He put his feet on a foot rest. Mary smiled, put her drink down, undid the cap of the sauce and poured the contents on Tail's feet.

    He laughed as she started licking it off.

    "That tickles," Tails said. "But it also feel warm."

    The sound of someone clearing their throat distracted Mary. She turned to see a Pink Hedgehog in a pink bikini with hearts on it, sunglasses and a sun hat watching with her arms folded. She seemed to be about the same age as Tails.

    Mary recognized the hedgehog as Amy. Memories of time with Amy flooded into Mary's mind.

    "Amy is that you?" Mary asked.

    Amy pulled her shades up.

    "Mary, is that *you*?" Amy asked. "Where have you been and what are you doing to Tails?"

    Tails had a goofy smile on his face.

    "I lost a race with him," Mary said. "It was kind of a bet."

    Amy shook her head. "Are you out of your mind? I mean really!"

    "What," Mary asked.

    "You're licking his *feet*," Amy said. "That's just sick!"

    Mary looked at Tails feet and then at Amy.

    "I mean, come on, his face is way cuter," Amy said. "And those Tails of his are so huggable, soft and cute! Feet are filthy and smelly!"

    "They've just been in my socks and shoes! Those aren't that dirty," Tails said.

    "Just a minute Tails," Amy said.

    "I was just having a little fun," Mary said. "I mean, he's so cute and sweet."

    "He is isn't he?" Amy asked.

    "Seriously, my feet aren't toxic," Tails said. "I never thought of trying this but sometimes it's fun to try new things."

    "Why don't you try it? Maybe you'll like it," Mary said.

    "What? That's perverse," Amy said. "What if someone catches me? I'll never live it down!"

    "Even if it's *my* feet?" Tails asked, wiggling his toes.

    "What would you have me do, get on my hands and knees and suck on his toes?" Amy asked.

    Mary eyed the bottle of strawberry sauce.

    "I have an idea that might be fun," Mary said. "Maybe you could lick some of this off of my belly while Amy licks it off of your feet."

    Mary felt herself blush at the idea of it. Amy was looking shocked as ever.

    "You two are nuts," Amy said before walking away.

    Mary tried to give chase. "Come on, it'll be fun. You can stop if you don't like it."

    Amy looked at Mary and glared.

    "At least try it. It's not like I'm gonna take pictures or anything," Mary said.

    Amy looked at Mary and then at Tails, who winked and wiggled his toes at her.

    "Well, maybe just this once," Amy said. "I'll do it for you Tails. But first, let me get a blanket."

    Amy took off in a small dust cloud. When she returned, she had a large blanket that could easily hold all three of them. She put it down on the sand and petted it. Tails got up and lay down on his belly on the centre of it.

    "Don't be shy, it's fun," Tails said. "I won't bite."

    "Tell you what," Mary said. "He can lick your belly. That way, you don't have to lick anything."

    Amy blushed.

    "I'll be nice," Tails said.

    Moments later, Amy lay on her back with Tails over her belly. Mary was behind both of them and looking at two pairs of bare feet.

    Mary handed the bottle to Tails, who poured some of it onto Amy's belly. He handed it to Mary who put some more onto his feet.

    Tails went to work, as did Mary.

    Amy shuddered at first but then giggled and beamed. Tails was doing the same between licks as Mary skillfully licked between each of his toes. Even when the strawberry sauce was long gone, Mary kept licking. His feet tasted wonderful.

    But all things must end. Everybody eventually sat up.

    "See, what wasn't so bad," Mary said.

    "It was kinda fun," Amy said. "But what if someone saw us?"

    "No one saw us," Mary said.

    Tails put his arms around Mary and Amy. "You guys were fun."

    "So, what do we do now?" Mary asked.
