    "Hey, Thomas, how's it hanging?" James asked as he strode into his shared dorm room.

    Ten minutes later he left John drip-drying in the shower, and went to study at his dorm room desk. Tom stepped

    ago. You and your brother have made the short list. One of you may become a member, the only question is
    which one?"

    Which one? Turn me human now, I've been bicycle shorts for two years! What kind of initiation is that? Did
    you forget about me?

    "No, I didn't forget about you. The initiation isn't over yet. The test is would you rather remain bicycle
    shorts or would you rather be human knowing that your brother would become your bicycle shorts if you become
    human."

    What? You'd turn James into my bicycle shorts. James is right, you must be gay!

    "Hey, I'm not the one with a guys cock in my face and balls in my ass, am I?"

    Please, Tom, I won't tell anyone about your secret society, please change be back, but leave my brother human
    too.

    "Aw, that's nice, but like I said there is a price for admission to the society, and you haven't paid it yet."

    But? You cannot ask me to do this to my brother. He doesn't deserve it!

    "Who's the gay one? He walks around strutting his bulge in everyone's face, and it's always in your face when
    he struts. You must really like his manhood inside you, not to want to be human and show him how it feels to

    He doesn't even know I'mm his shorts. You did this! How could he know? He's not responsible.

    "Maybe, maybe not? He's psychically linked to you. He may not be able to read your mind, but he can sense
    you alright."

    Wha? No. You're just trying to get me to trade places with him. I won't do that.

    "Maybe you won't, but I wonder what he'll say? Should we find out?"

    Tom walked out of the bathroom with John in his hand.

    "Say, James, remember that conversation we had in the sauna yesterday at the Athletic Center?"

    "Oh, yeah, you told me about your secret Magical Society," James said, "Okay, you piqued my interest. So tell
    me more."

    "Magic really does exist, but it comes at a price. Are you willing to pay that price?"

    "Maybe? What is the price?"

    "Your price is to be your brother's bicycle shorts."

    "Funny! You know my brother John's been missing for 2 years now. He never came home for Christmas, and no

    "Ah, but with magic, I can bring him back. In fact, you can bring him back, but you have to will yourself to
    be his bicycle shorts. Just stand in this chalk circle," Thomas said as he drew on the floor, "And recite this
    incantation," he added handing a slip of paper to James.

    "You're serious?"

    "Are you? Would you give up your humanity to bring your brother back?"

    "For all I know he's sipping Pina Coladas in Mexico, and is quite happy."

    "What if I told you he's closer than you think? What if I told you that you've been wearing him for the past
    two years?" Tom said holding up the bicycle shorts.

    "John?" incredulity and hope echoed as he spoke his brother's name.

    "Read the incantation and find out!" Tom said triumphantly.
