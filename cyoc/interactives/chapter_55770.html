    Ty's Power

    A mysterious friend

    added by Sephikurene 12 years ago O

    Suddenly ty feels that he isn't handcuffed, he knows he isn't awake
    how he knows this well he doesn't know. He does know that this isn't a
    dream either, but that he doesn't really understand.

    He also knows that there is a voice here and it's speaking to him. The
    voice belongs to a boy with white hair dressed like some kind of
    gothic rockstar. The boy is cute, but not really his type he thinks
    absently.

    "I resent that!"
    "Huh? You knew what I was thinking?"
    "Oh yeah, kind of goes with semi-omnipotent you know semi-omniscient.
    The whole god gig thing."
    "Wait, you're a god?"
    "Yup!"
    "What the hell is going on!"
    "Okay the dealio is that I gave you the power. We call it the wishing
    gift. Something you should know though is that your power has ... well
    it's drawbacks."
    "What kind of drawbacks?"
    "Me" Suddenly ty can see the boy clearly, he has horns and red eyes
    and scars on his body. There are wings of flame behind him and he's
    carrying a scythe made of bones.
    "What no!"
    "Don't worry, I'm not all bad, but I am inevitable. You see your world
    is slowly crawling toward the end. That would be me by the way the god
    of ends and oblivion. You see when your world ends I'll be the one who
    collects it, call me the global grim reaper. So your power is one of
    those things that herald the end, the more you use it the faster that
    end will come. I hope you understand what I'm saying."
    "Fuck no!"
    "Sorry no changing it, I mean honestly I don't like it either. I hope
    you fight it with all your might and trust me I'm going to give you
    the tools to do so. Unfortunately the wishing power doesn't work on me
    so you can't just wish me away."
    "That sucks..."
    "Yeah I know, if it were that easy I would tell you to do just that."
    "So you're really not a bad guy?"
    "No, I think this sucks as much as you do, but I was created for
    this... I mean it's my nature I can't change who I am."
    "Wow that really ... that really fucking sucks..."
    "Yeah, but anyway I can't tell you how to stop it straight out, but I
    can give you clues and tools."
    "Okay what's the first clue?"
    "I have a couple actually, the first is elements and history, you're
    people stopped me once before long long long ago. You have to find the
    13 constructs the lighthouses that stopped me before and the 13
    elements and the okai tugra that can summon and light them."
    "Wait ... okai tugra?"
    "Yeah 13 people each of a different race."
    "Wait there are humans, vampires, werewolves, centaurs, elves, satyrs,
    merpeople... that's 7 not 13."
    "Don't forget catgirls, and winged humans. Also the elves are three
    different races and the werewolves two."
    "Oh yeah, but that's 14."
    "Yeah unfortunately I'm talking about the 13 original races that ruled
    your planet long ago."
    "Oh wait, shit you mean I already fragged it all up didn't I?"
    "Nope, luckily the okai tugra are immune well not completely, but
    partially from the wishing power as well. Although the races are gone
    they'll remain."
    "Oh thank god, what about the lighthouses?"
    "They aren't affected by the wishing."
    "Good, so what are the elements?"
    "Life, fire, shadow, earth, war, magic, light, wind, water, death,
    mind, love, and spirit."
    "Cool, anything else I need to know?"
    "Well a lot actually now you better listen I won't repeat this and
    you'll wake up when I'm done."
    "Shit okay I'm listening."
    "The art is the essence of life, it belonged to the race called the
    artions. The avatar of life is female and has red hair and green eyes
    you'll know her for her wisdom and leadership. The golden lighthouse
    of life is buried in sand and will answer only artias's call.
    The dance is the essence of fire, it belonged to the race called the
    infernai. The avatar of fire is female and has dark skin and flames
    for hair, you'll know her from her passion and temper. The burning
    lighthouse of fire is buried in a volcano and will answer only
    sparka's call.
    Stealth is the essence of shadow, it belonged to the race called the
    shades. The avatar of shadow is male and has black hair and pale skin,
    you'll know him from the scar that marks his face and the burning
    blood in his eyes. The hidden lighthouse of shadow is invisible in the
    land that is covered in eternal night and will answer only velia's
    call.
    Nature is the essence of earth, it belonged to the race called the bestial. The avatar of earth is male and has cat ears and tail, you'll
    know him from the secrets he keeps and his sharp tongue. The green
    lighthouse of earth is hidden within the world's biggest tree and will
    answer only gaia's call.
    Destruction is the essence of war, it belonged to the race called
    human. The avatar of war is male and is someone closer then you think,
    although he may not be human any more. The forged lighthouse of war is
    already kept by man, and will answer only arios's call.
    Knowledge is the essence of magic, it belonged to the race called
    dragons. The avatar of magic is male and is violet in hue and of royal
    descent, you will know him for the beauty and compassion he possesses.
    The ever-changing lighthouse of magic is right under your nose, and
    will answer only sorcere's call.
    Faith is the essence of light, it belonged to the race called the
    celestial. The avatars of light are both male and female one from hell
    and one from heaven, you will know them for both will give you aide
    when you need it most. The lawful lighthouse of light is found where
    it is brightest and will answer only fayth's call.
    Freedom is the essence of wind, it belonged to the race called the
    winged ones. The avatar of wind is male and flies with wings you gave
    him, you will know him for the knowledge he keeps. The storm channelling lighthouse of wind is on the tallest mountain and will
    answer only krozo's call.
    Purity is the essence of water, it belonged to the race called the
    vampires. The avater of water is male and is with you always, you will
    know him for you already do. The sea swept lighthouse of water is
    within a triangle your people fear and will answer only karakia's
    call.
    Respect is the essence of death, it belonged to the race called the
    lycan. The avatar of death is male and is found close to another
    avatar, face your fears and you will find him and you will know him
    for his open mind. The grave lighthouse of death is within the largest
    graveyard and will answer only necrios's call.
    Curiosity is the essence of the mind, it belonged to the race called
    the meta-humans. The avatar of the mind is one whose confidence will irritate you, but whose loyalty you will not take for granted. The
    crystal lighthouse of the mind is found in the deepest cave and will
    answer only luna's call.
    Truth is the essence of love, it belonged to the race called fay. The
    avatar of love is one you'll find most attractive in both masculine
    and feminine ways, you will know him and her by the love they'll give
    to you. The unseen lighthouse of love is found within the technology
    man has made and will answer only truth's call.
    We are the essence of spirit, it belonged to the race called the
    watchers. The avater of spirit is already found and changes the world
    with a wish. The great lighthouse of spirit is found in the ocean and
    only answer the great white whale's beautiful song.
