    "Okay, I'm sorry," you say. "Please turn me into a girl."

    "With pleasure" says Tamara.

    She points at you again, and your body is suddenly growing smaller, the acne disappearing from your face, the hair disappearing from your chest as your breasts once again become very feminine. The glasses disappear from your face which you can see is now the beautiful visage of Stacey Fox once again, an invisible hand painting it with makeup.

    Strangely, though, your black hair continued getting longer, reaching up to your waist.
    The B cups you have seem to be explode outwards, expanding to what must be at least an E cup

    Finally, and most surprisingly, you feel yourself getting taller and taller, until you reach an unbelievable stature.

    “What the hell have you done?” you ask the now much smaller looking Tamara, mad, but mostly confused.

    “Well, you see, I decided to not change you back in the same way, as a punishment” says Tamara
    “You have bigger breasts, which are complimenting your body even better than the B cups you had. They would have been around the larger size of an E cup if you were still six feet tall. But as beautiful as they are, they would still weigh you down at the sports” she explains
    “Compensating for the weight you now have on your chest, you are now much taller than before, helping you in the games. At the height of 9 feet, you tower over your competition and have little trouble scoring baskets.”
    “But” she continues, “while you don’t have the health problems most people of an extremely statue do, you still have a lot of inconveniences being the tallest person to ever live. Moving around closed spaces would be a problem, and you would always stand out in the crowd, wether you want to or not.”

    You are very shocked and confused about the explanation, but out of all the questions you have, you blurt out a fairly strange one.
    “What about my family? Didn’t you say you had to choose a different father for me to be a six foot tall girl? How exactly did you make me nine?”

    “Well, that was tough for me, but I basically had to change genes and relations up to your grandparents. Basically, your mother is now six foot ten and your father, which is still Richard Fox, is now a full seven foot five. Their statures are extreme, but their daughter still dwarfs them”
    “Thankfully, due to your family’s wealth, they have a spacious house with high ceilings, and they can afford the specially made clothes for its inhabitants.”
    “But at the end of it, the house was designed with 7 foot people in mind, so while you won’t be as crammed as you would be in most places, most of the furniture outside of the room would still be small for you.”

    Your mind has a lot of trouble processing everything Tamara said.
    “Any way you could change me back to the original Stacy? I’m sorry about demanding to return to be a boy.”

    “After all the work I had to do for the punishment, I’ll have to seriously consider that” Tamara replies. “I’ll probably keep you like that for a few days. If I feel like you’re really sorry enough. Maybe I’ll even change you back to your old self.”
    “But who knows? Maybe you’ll actually like your new life as a freakishly tall but still extremely hot version of Stacy Fox? Maybe you would even want to stay that way?” She says with a grin.
    “Well now I’ll leave you to yourself, bye!” She finishes before disappearing into thin air.

    “No, wait!” You say before ending up alone in your room.
