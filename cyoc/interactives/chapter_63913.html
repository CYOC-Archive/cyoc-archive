She marveled at how fantastic her curves looked in the low cut tops she'd always fought with her daughter about, and how the short skirts she hated displayed her long sensuous legs.

Elle glumly cast one long wistful gaze back at mirror. She'd had more fun today that she remembered having in months, but it was over now. But she promised herself she'd do it again soon. Tomorrow she'd have the house to herself again and she had not even begun to explore the possibilities. Walking back to the the heap of clothes on the floor, she picked up her shirt and, reluctantly, touched it to the medallion. Nothing happened. Elle kept holding the fabric against the medallion, but she felt nothing resembling the shock from last time. A cold feeling began forming in the pit of her stomach but she told herself to remain calm. She still had the box. Surely one of the other objects would fix this.

    Running back downstairs, she rushed over to the box and began rummaging through it, looking for some way to reverse her transformation, but she had never figured out how any of them worked. Finally she found the instruction manual and began flipping through it furiously until she found the entry on the medallion. Her spirits soared as she read, hoping to find the answer to her predicament, only to sink as she read the last lines of the entry:

    After the transformation is complete, at least twelve hours must elapse before the Medallion can be used to return the user to their original form or take on a new shape. It is prudent to remember this at all times.

    Twelve hours? She might not even have twelve minutes! Frantically she flipped through the manual looking for the something else that might help her. She dismissed the Test Tube and Amulets, along with the Gender Ring. The Wonder Watch was no use since she couldn't hypnotize herself. With increasing desperation, Elle grabbed the Universal Remote and pointed it at herself. But when she pressed "Select", the profile that appeared was showed her daughter's body. She came to the horrifying realization that the remote had no record of the changes, or of her old body. The Ring of Three had the same problem, her command of "back to normal" having no effect. Elle was now at her wits end as she moved to the last artifact left, the Master PC.

    Grabbing the disk she ran over to her computer and practically shoved the disk inside, pressing "yes" as soon as the window appeared. She fidgeted nervously as the progress bar on the installer filled, far to slowly. She jumped as she heard a bus pull up outside. Running to the window, she saw her daughter Alli step off the bus and start walking up to steps to the house. It was the worst of all possibilities. Elle cringed to imagine how her daughter would react when she found someone who looked exactly like her in her house.

    Now on the verge of sheer terror Elle ran back over to her computer...
