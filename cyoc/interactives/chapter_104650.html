She let out an ooh of wonder, "What a cool game, huh Odie? Let's see...who should I target." She stared at the dog for a long minute as he tilted his head to one side before she patted him on the furry head. "Maybe later buddy, I'm gonna play first." She found the scan button and very carefully turned the emitter around, pointing it at herself and pressing. "Name: Emily Towers", she read and then clapped her hands, "It knows my name Odie! It must be doing that face wrecker ignition dad told us about!" She flicked down through the list and then saw an array of buttons reading "Extra Options" she explored those for a while, a lot of words and phrases she didn't understand, getting bored with the game as she went. "Mental Changes...to what exactly?"

    She made a little raspberry, "This is boring Odie. There's all these things but none of them do anything." It was true, she had fiddled with several sliders to no effect. "Maybe it needs to be restarted! Ummmmm..." She found the revert button and the sliders snapped back to their original position. "Hmmm...still boring. There's not even any graphics! Ugh...." She paused, something catching her eye, "Wait...maybe it needs to be a different version! There's a slider here, Per Version. I guess they release one a day? It's only on 5 out of a hundred! That won't do..." She slid the slider all the way to the right and smiled, leaning back and waiting for the changes to take effect. Nothing. "Ugh! Stupid game for stupid babies like stupid Janet." She angrily moved the mouse up to the corner and pressed the close button.

    A slightly disdainful beep emitted from the computer and she blinked at the pop-up, "Close without saving...I guess I should save at least." She clicked the "Save and Close" button instead and then turned to Odysseus who was panting on the floor beside her, "Come on Odie, let's go do something really fun instead of this dumbness." She smiled and stood, slipping the emitter into the pocket of her shorts. As she turned and walked away, Odie following after, she started to get a funny lightheaded feeling. She shook it off and kept walking, heading into the hallway above the living room. Monique was on the sofa down below and Emily giggled a bit. The nurse was totally asleep and she needn't have worried about being caught. She leaned over the rail and stared down, catching sight of the older woman's cleavage and slowly a wide grin spread across her face as she stared at them, the spot where the shirt was falling open and you could see her bra.
