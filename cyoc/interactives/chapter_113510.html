    Well, no,not actually suddenly.

    What happened was that the Drakenarkenstone,which was consuming vast amounts of internal power
    keeping Nate in his dragonform,suffered
    a magickal short circuit recoil"

    Basically a item stretched to the limits like a tow cable under massive
    electromagical potential,snapped like a rubber
    band.

    What that meant was that Nate suddenly snapped back into
    his human shape(though nude)
    and in the spectactular light and smoke magic show the other four were witness to
    the Drakenarkenstone whipped off Nates shrinking tail,
    flew through the smoke and lightening filled air
    and plopped through Michaela (Mickey)'s open bag purse,where it
    lay undiscovered by her.

    Naturally Nate thought being a dragon would be cool,as did Sarah.

    So soon the five of them were crawling around the
    stone castle floor looking
    for the missing
    gemstone,without success.

    So they spent another half hour exploring the Castle,before they left to see
    that nights movies.

    So they all broke up and headed to their separate homes


    Mickey headed to her own bedroom ,tossed the big bag purse she had on the
    bed,and since it was already past midnight and she was tired,
    she flopped onto her bed and was soon asleep.

    Now Mickey had a Science Fair project she had to show off
    to hundreds of scientists the new day in the huge highschool
    gymnasium.

    Her topic of research was about how scientific thinking
    and logic had finally triumphed over primitive mythical thinking
    and folktales (such as about dragons and knights fighting it out for
    the maiden) in the 15th century,leading to the Age of Reason.

    However as she dreamed about Albert Einstein,Issac Newton and thomas
    Crookes shaking her hand and praising her on her Science Fair
    project,the shorted out Drakenarkenstone reset its circuit breakers
    and began to glow weakly,coating Mickey in a lower dose
    of drakenlight.

    Though it was weakly powered,that meant the Drakenarkenstone didn't
    trip any magical circuits this time and had the whole night and a few
    hours into the morning
    to turn Mickey into a bigger and more permanently dragon cursed
    dragoness then normally would have been possible with
    the "quik change" method the Drakenarkenstone
    normally did.

    Mickey was now a dragon,which mean

    -she naturally slept during the day
    -the bed had long since collapsed under the growing weight of the growing mythical creature
    -she had big black scaled forepaws,which since her new dragoness
    instincts dealt with mating,hunting,laying eggs and gathering her
    shiny coins beside and underneath her
    -meant that those huge muscled forelegs of hers had demolished the
    room as her claws had raked along the floor and swept all the nighttables,clothing and stuff beside her huge heaving body
    -the blast furnace within her had been at full flame hours before,which
    meant sparks and flames and smoke and brimstone shooting out of the
    nostrils and mouth of the sleeping scaly monster.

    That had caused her bedroom to catch fire whilst she dreamt about killing a handsome meaty stag with her powerful tail.

    Soon the whole house had been ablaze,and though the rest of the family
    had managed to flee the blazing inferno,to the sleeping dragoness she
    started dreaming about slithering about on a slightly warmed up rock.

    Sooner or later the monster had to come out of her dragoness
    dreams and so it
    was that her grieving parents were sobbing away,the firefighters
    were waiting for the fire to die out so they could look
    for their daughters charred corpse.

    Mickey woke up,kinda realized something wasn't quite right .

    So the shocked parents stared up in horror as something in
    the ruins shook off the burnt wood beams and stuff off its scaly hide as it got up to its
    full three story heigh.

    The monster swung its snouty reptillian head as it took in just
    how dragonnish and huge and unhuman it was

    then the monster bellowed away in despair as
    bits of burnt wood and stuff dribbled away from her huge bats wings.
