    "Hey, this looks fun," Tiana says, holding up the pamphlet for the Mythology area. "It says here that you can turn into a variety of fantastic creatures, why don't we go there?"

    The friends discuss it for a few minutes, they did figure they could change areas or even animals later, so what would it hurt to go there first? They turn back to Rebecca, who directs them to a tram area nearby that will take them to the Mythology area.

    "Okay, so what animals should we turn into when we get there?" Nate asks.

    "Well, lets go over the options," Jamie says, looking over the list provided in the pamphlet.

    Unicorn
    Dragon
    Centaur
    Cat Person
    Werewolf (Humanoid)
    Werewolf (Wolf)
    Nymph
    Ask the workers for other options.

    "So many options," Dylan says, thinking over the list for a moment. "You know we don't have to all turn into the same creature."

    "That is true," Nate says with a shrug.

    The tram arrives a few minutes later, and they are ushered into a small building where a woman with the same uniform as Rebecca is waiting with the name "Sandra" on her shirt.

    "Hello, welcome to the Mythology area," Sandra says. "What would you like to turn into?"
