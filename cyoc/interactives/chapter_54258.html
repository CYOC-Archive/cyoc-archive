    This is most irregular! A helicopter interrupts the cruise

    added by Anonymous 13 years ago I O

    The helicopter landing was quite a sight. There on the rear deck - in
    what usually served as the golf putting green stood a small
    helicopter. The captain went over to greet the woman and man who
    stepped out.

    "This is most irregular!" complained the captain.

    "Well, your cruise line owner Burke Devlin assured me that you would
    cooperate fully, Captain Hobbs," said the blonde woman in an overly
    short designer coat.

    "Yes, Mrs. Hawks, we will cooperate, but I assure you that your cousin
    and his friends never boarded this ship."

    "Oh, but they did. Tony, tell him!" Carolyn replied.

    "Hello, Captain, I'm Tony Petersen, the Collins family lawyer. And if
    we could go somewhere more private and quiet, I imagine the pilot
    would like to take off," he shouted.

    "Very well. We'll adjourn to the Alpha Lounge."

    Carolyn looked at Tony and rolled her eyes. Tony looked back at her
    devotedly. Tony had dated Carolyn years ago, but other then his being
    totally smitten with the heiress nothing more came of it other than
    being selected as the family's principal lawyer. He wondered what his
    great-grandfather Lamar Trask would have thought, if he had married
    Carolyn. His grandmother used to tell him that Lamar had been married
    to Judith Collins and had been master of all Collinwood. However, he
    had disappeared one day, and she suspected some man called Count
    Petofi. He had gotten to know a lot of the Collins family history in
    the last few years. If ever there was a family that was cursed, it
    was these people. Even Carolyn, first her father abandoned her and
    then he returned, and died. And she had been barely married to Heb
    Hawks, when she became a widow. Still he loved Carolyn, and if he
    couldn't have her, he wanted her to be happy.

    In the lounge, the captain escorted them to a table set back in an
    alcove in the corner. He gestured, and said, "Please be seated. Would
    you care for any drinks?"

    "A Manhattan for me," said Tony, "And for Mrs. Hawks, do you have any
    sherry?"

    "Not just any sherry," Carolyn snapped, "What kinds of sherry do you
    have? I'm particularly fond of Amontillado, if you have it."

    "Amontillado, Carolyn?" Tony said with an odd distaste in his mouth.
    It reminded him of mortar and brick dust whenever he thought of
    Amontillado.

    "Yes, didn't you know since Cousin Barnabas came it's the only type of
    sherry we stock at Collinwood. It is truly to die for - are you sure
    you won't have one?"

    "No amontillado, I'm afraid. Would port sherry do?"

    "No, instead get me a Cosmo - you know a Cosmopolitan?"

    The captain retreated.

    "Now, Carolyn, don't be so brusque. We need this man's help. Let me
    show him the evidence, and I'm sure he'll help us find out what
    happened to David," he said holding her hand. He could help but steal
    a whiff of her scent as he leaned in.

    "Oh, Tony, I know. It's just I'm so worried. How can a captain not
    even know who's on his ship?"

    "Well, these cruise ships carry thousands. They're like small cities.
    But he'll help us, and we will find David."

    "The steward will bring us our drinks shortly. Now what evidence do
    you have. I reviewed our ship's records and neither your cousin nor
    his friends boarded this ship."

    "Then how do you explain these photos?" Carolyn said.

    Apparently, you had used your iPhone to snap pictures of your boarding
    the ship and on the ship as you paused while following the steward to
    your fate. Oh, and if you haven't figured it out yet - you are David
    Collins.

    "Well, that is our gangway, and the pool and I assume these are David
    and his friends?"

    "Here's a picture of David and all of them. He used a mirror in their
    cabin to snap it."

    "None of our cabins look like that? Why that looks like the new
    window display for our Men's Resort Store. Damn, even the mannequins-"
    the Captain stared and paused. "Our drinks can wait. Come with me at
    once!"
