You said to her.
    "Firestorm said you broke him out of solitary and I admit when he told me I was interested, but I never expected you, my name is Chaos, and I know plenty about you Cal'kar plenty of things." Chaos said.
    "I don't know wether to feel flattered, attracted, or afraid of what you just said no offense." You said
    "None taken, come Firestorm was smart not to let you just come in you're responsible for plenty of massacres already." Chaos said. "How many beings did I kill?" You asked her,"Topaz told me about the incident at one of your temples, and so that brings your kill counter to 243."
    "Damn," you said and then thought," I don't even want to know what I did, but considering the dreams I've been having."
    "By the way I can read your mind, and you should know that I know that your memories are in fragments." Chaos whispered in your ear.
    "Lets be off, I'll have a place for you right away Cal." Chaos said to you
    "Cal?" You asked
    "You don't like it?" Chaos said
    "It's not that it's just odd, you can still call if you want to I'll get used to it." You said

    As you arrived at the camp everyone reacted exactly as you expected them to towards you at first, but Chaos assured them that you were an ally. You were moved into a hut with Firestorm, Topaz, and a dragon named Kri'mal who had silver scales and was male. He was uncomfortable with you at first but then you told him that you have no intention to harm, and that you would at most incapacitate any agresser from here.

Second, The Corrupted Darkness was obsessed with my demonic strength, and wanted to siphon my energy which is why I fight now, and that I want to redeem myself somehow. Finally I want to know your honest opinion about me, and don't worry I won't harm you at all I'm not that kind of person."
    "Well I'm afraid of you, everyone is which is why people fight you, your the only one who has influence over demonic magic, and if I could kill you I wouldn't because I'm certain that you didn't kill all of them on purpose, and as for the question for if I like you then I'm starting to come around, what do you remember by the way?" Kri'mal asked you
    You told him what you remembered from the past and that you would update everyone in the morning. Everyone nodded and you went to sleep seeing what you could find in your dreams, and so you could learn more about Cal'kar.
