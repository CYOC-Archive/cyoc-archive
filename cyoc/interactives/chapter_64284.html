    "Looks like it. Not much to the game I guess." Tim slouched in his chair as the others shrugged their shoulders.

    "Just feeling a little dizzy." Tim tried to stand but he immediately fell over onto the table. He wrapped his hands around his head as a heat overtook his face while Sara got up to try and see if he was ok.

    "Tim, what is it?" Sara asked trying to get him to sit back down. She turned to grab the chair to pull it closer when she noticed something poking in the back of his jeans. He started to panic as his body started to itch all over, scratching at himself wildly until tugs at his ears drew his hands upwards. He tried to stop them from moving but they moved higher on his head and head in general. Stopping at about ten inches, the floppy rabbit's ear fell down to either side of his face just as he himself fell back in his chair.
