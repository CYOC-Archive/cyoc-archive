    The strange little people having securely chained my neck and limbs to one one another had further secured the chains betwixt three stony out croppings. I find myself immobile and unable to free myself. The ropes and scaffolding I soon learnt were not intended to provide additional restraint, but were for the curious little folk's odd and embarrassing activities. Little men with scythes cut my clothing into large rectangles. Other little folk collected the rectangles of fabric into bundles. The bundles were lowered via the scaffolding's pulleys and ropes into waiting carts. It took several hours, but I soon found myself naked. While they had harvested the fabric from the front and sides of my garments, they had been thankfully unable to reach the fabric between me and the sand. It surely would have been most unpleasant to have the sand creeping into my crack. I soon discovered that the indignity of nakedness was the least of the indignities these foul little creatures were planning to render to me. The little fellows returned with freshly sharpened scythes, and began to whack the healthy thatching of hair that covered my body.
