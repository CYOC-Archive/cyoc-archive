    "Well, Mr. Evens." Ms. Helga said as he came back in "I trust Ms. Nelson straightened you out?"

    "Yeah, she did." he replied, still getting over what he'd seen.

    "Take your seat, please." She said.

    As soon as he did, she continued her lesson.

    Afterward, she wanted to talk with him for a few minuets.

    "You wanted to talk with me?" he asked.

    "Yes." she said, as she lost a bit of height "What you did in class today was inexcusable." she said as she shrunk down to his height "I'm not
    going to stand for it anymore." her skin turned white "Putting eggs in my chair is not funny." her nose became a golfball-sized blue ball "This
    isn't over," her hair turned purple, and went into ringlets with a blue bow on top "and you're not getting off easy," her lips became blue and
    plump, her eyebrows became black arches, and blue stars appeared on her cheeks, and her ears doubled in size with blue pearl earrings the
    same size as her nose "so I guess you could say that for you," she smiled as she now wore a purple, poofy dress, blue gloves, and blue clown
    shoes 2' long and 1' wide on feet that filled them just right "it won't be OVER EASY! Hee-hee-hee!" Her gold name tag went from saying "Ms
    Kelly Helga" to a pink, stick-on one that said "Hi, My Name Is: Kelly Ga-Ga" "Get it?" she asked in a helium voice.

    "Yeah," he said, smiling nervously "that's funny. I should go now. I'll miss my bus. Bye, Ms. Helga."

    "Please call me Miss Ga-Ga." she said, smiling "Everyone else does. Bye, Jimbo!"

    Jim left for the bus and as soon as he got in, Lily Summers, the cute girl he had a crush on, asked him to sit next to her, so he did.

    He was ready for a normal ride home, but he'd not get that either, as he'd soon find out!
