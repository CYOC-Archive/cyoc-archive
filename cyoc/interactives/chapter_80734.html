    Rogan smiled to himself as he tossed the flute up into the air before catching it. Catching up with the
    piglets he snatched them up and put them into his stolen satchel.

    "No wandering off again you two," he said to the piglets who squealed in shock at being plucked from the

    "S-sorry. I just kind of dozed off for a second." Connie said coyly.
