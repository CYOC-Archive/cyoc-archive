    In advance of the Venusian Landing

    If this had been all going on still on Earth with its heavy gravity
    that would have been one thing
    but what you and Dr Huang didn't know at the time
    was that after the lot of you had entered the TF Module-Capsule
    the World Space Authority had decided to get cracking and send
    a spaceship with a crew aboard to Venus .
    So the TF Module-Capsule had been quietly moved aboard
    the good telemotor spaceship Starburst and the ship had quietly
    lifted off the Launchpad and headed into deep space
    for Venus.
    Well that meant when Dr Huang's insides and internal organs
    had started transforming ,shifting and lengthening into those
    of a dragon his transformation had been going on in zero
    gravity.Which is a sensory more intense experience by a
    thousand fold then a similar transformation back in
    Earths heavy gravity.

    Basically Dr Huang had been intensely aware whilst he was
    still humn of his changing insides,including the horror of realizing
    that the squirms and spasms going on in his belly were most assuredly the formation of dragon ovries,egg tubes,and the
    various other squishy and yucky stuff a female dragon has
    inside her.
    So in his final rational moments Dr Huang had been aware
    of her changing sex and species and also the fct that she
    was in space,cheated by the World Space Authority.

    After the changes were complete the dragon no longer understood or cared about the complicated human stuff
    drifting aimlessly in her reptilian brain but she
    was well aware she was a female,laid eggs and really needed to
    eat something soon.
    That heartburn in her chest didn't make her too happy nor
    did her wings help any as they dried out after bursting wetly
    out of her back and into existence.
    She looked at the remaining humans in the lair she found
    herself in and realized they weren't changing anymore.

    In other words you and the other screaming humans were
    just so much dragon munchies to her now.

    Luckily for you humans the Starburst and the Transformation
    Module-Chamber contained a failsafe mechanism to stop
    and reverse the transformation in its early stages.

    In the case of the once-human dragon theres wasn't any human DNA left to built a reverse procedure on,just a big unhappy female dragon who the procedure would simply turn into another big unhappy female dragon if it was tried on her.
    So the automated failsafe system ignored the roaring dragon
    and instead doors slid open along the walls of the TF Module Chamber at the same time as needles built into the beds you and the
    others rested on injected the reverse TF solutions into you.
    Out the bed you rested on slid through that door and slammed shut just as the dragon leapt forward to strike at you with her
    toothy jaws.
    Instead she roared in pain as her snout slammed into the closed
    door where you had been seconds earlier and she let loose
    with her first firebreath.
    Then the TF Module Chamber shook as the dragon tried to smash her way out
    but to no avail.
    Meanwhile you and the other humans got up from your
    beds in the main part of the Starburst and groggily headed to the control room where the Strbursts 33 robot women were
    preparing to inject Starburst into Venusian orbit,

    The first landing of man,dragon and robot on Venus
    could wait a little while longer.....
