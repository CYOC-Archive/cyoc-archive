    The Sapphirus Kingdom was a great kingdom, but it was just one of four kingdoms on the continent of Duskhaven. The Sapphirus royal line is one of three that trace themselves back to the heroes that aided the benevolent demon queen Eda to save the world from a cabal of xenophobic elves and angels from the continent of Illuminala seeking to exterminate all other species on the planet, starting with the Duskhaven continent. Those heroes, Talia the Blue, Pytr the Red, and Haruka the Green, fought alongside Eda against the forces of Illuminala and stopped their plans to ethnically cleanse their realm of anything that wasn't elves or angels. For their valor, Eda blessed her friends with sigils that would prove their bloodlines forevermore; marks based on the sex of the bearer and cannot be forged or counterfeited by magic, and from the war-torn ashes help them set up three equal nations surrounding Eda's realm of Platina - the Sapphirus kingdom, in the southwest region of the continent, founded by Talia, the first queen of Sapphirus, the Rubiss kingdom, in the northern portion of the continent, founded by Pytr, the first king of Rubiss, and the Emerada kingdom, in the southeastern portion of Duskhaven, established by Haruka, Emerada's first empress.

    For ages, the three kingdoms lived in peace with each other and the demon nation of Platina in the center of Duskhaven, until recently, as tensions have increased following a few seasons of bad harvests across the continent leading to trade disputes among Sapphirus, Rubiss, and Emerada. Most believe the poor harvests just a series of misfortunes, but some intellectuals from all four kingdoms have a theory these "misfortunes" may not be bad luck but deliberate acts of sabotage, with some thinking one of the other nations are behind this, and some, namely Eda's descendent, the rarely seen and bashfully shy holy succubus Rita Platinus, current queen of Platina, worried this may be the lead-up to something from Illuminala once more, threatening to put the whole continent at danger again.

    What does this all have to do with our prince-turned-centaur mare? In a way, everything and nothing, as Damon was too young to be a player in the continent's political intrigue, but someone wanted to remove the only heir to the Sapphirus line, hiring the very powerful & talented yet ditzy & incompetent Ristania to deal with the prince discreetly, and she would have succeeded if not for her own clumsiness, a peasant's errant littering, and hubris leading her to not bother learning the counterspells to her own hexes. Because of this, she ended up making up most of the equine lower portion of the prince's new form, and her biomass was responsible for Damon switching sexes and sharing features with her now, like her big bustline, small waist, and long blue hair, the blue making up 70% of her new hair (the other 30% retaining her original rusty auburn tones in her hair and new tail). Damon and Ristania are merged together, but Damon is the one basically calling the shots, Ristania's consciousness largely a passenger in their merged forms.

    Inside of Damon, she could faintly hear the whiny high-pitched voice of her assailant Ristania inside of her, "Hey!! Take that back!! I'm not a bumbling buffoon!! I'm a great witch!! Apologize!! If you don't, I'll... I'll... I'll um... SWISH YOUR TAIL REALLY HARD!!"
