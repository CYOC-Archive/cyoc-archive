    Tiger tail except with blue fur and green stripes

    added by Drakkenfyre 6 years ago A BM I O

    Tag was really getting into this pet customizing thing. He had already added a striped tiger tail to Paul, and was selecting fur all over option with stripes. He grinned the colour options were amazing. He was tempted to go with pink and purple or blue and purple like Avatar, but instead decided to go with two shades of blue (darker on the back, sky blue on the belly) and neon green stripes to match Paul's new neon green Mohawk. He hit execute.

    "Wh-? N-n-n-o, no, no!" shouted Paul as he saw the fur rapidly spreading from his blue and green tail all over his body.

    He snarled and clawed at the chamber's invisible walls. Clawed. He pulled his right hand up to his face. He watched as he flexed his fingers and big menacing cat-claws extended and retracted beneath his furry fingertips.

    Tag was on a roll. He sprawled out on some pillows in front of the big screen, and edited like mad. Paul was taller, buffer, furrier, and had the cutest big glowing eyes. And best of all, Tag had set him to be totally obedient. Tag looked around the messy room, and decided what he would have his Paulpet do as soon as he was finished customizing it.
