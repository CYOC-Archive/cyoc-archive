    “I can’t believe your idea of a day out is to go for a zoo.” Stated a rather beautiful women as she stood in the zoo with her arms over her chest.

    “Our last date cost an arm and a leg.” Said her boyfriend who was standing beside her somewhat annoyed as well. “A nice day out at the zoo, a nice dinner afterwards is a good change. Besides you like the animals.”

    “True.” Said the women her arms over her chest, not wanting to admit the truth but couldn’t lie about it either. Still she couldn’t let it die. “Still, I expected…”

    “It is hard to top, a concert series, Katie.” stated her boyfriend as Katie wrinkled her nose a bit in annoyance. She was about to say something when her boyfriend laughed a bit.

    “What?” inquired Katie as her boyfriend kept laughing. “What is so funny Ash.”

    “You are just so cute when you’re angry.” admitted Ash as Katie got red in the face before getting right in his face.

    Katie and Ashton, Ash for short had been a couple for almost a decade now. The two of them had started dating each other when they where 15 and now both where 25. Saying that, some would call them High School sweethearts but others who knew them would wonder how or why the two of them where still together. They had broken up with each more time and anyone could count, sometimes it was for a few days, a week here, longest was a month, but they almost seamed to make it a habit of breaking up.

    Katie, was a beautiful young women, with a good size chest she liked to display and a nice ass as well. She was a little on the short size but not too much. However she had a temper which would make her a stereotype of an Irish red head, if it wasn’t for the fact that she wasn’t Irish and her hair was jet black. She kept her hair long enough to reach her shoulder blades but liked to style it with a braid. Today she was wearing a simple black tank top and a pair of jeans with flip flops and a handbag. Her sunglasses were resting on her forehead. There were times when she had to make herself the center of attention, and some would call her a backstabbing bitch but other times she seamed to be the most caring loving person in the world.

    Ashton, was a handsome young man, he had muscular but not over the top chest and body. He didn’t work out per say but his job as a professional welder kept him in shape moving heavy pieces of metal around. He sometimes gave off the vibe of a frat boy and he sometimes did things which would give frat boys a bad name. He wasn’t super tall, about average and he was clean shaven the only thing that really stood out was a couple of scars he had from workplace accidents on his arms. He was wearing a pair of cargo shorts and a T shirt. There where multiple times, where Ash was immature and he loved to poke at his girlfriend’s nerves to see how far he could push it, but yet like Katie he could be very kind, loving and caring.

    “There are times I really hate you.” Stated Katie with a playful yet pissed off glare

    “I know, sometimes I hate you too.” Stated Ash as he bent down and planted a kiss on her lips which she returned for a moment before pulling away, to say she was still angry with him.

    Both of them knew this ground and knew it well, maybe it was their flirting style or maybe it was something else. Both of them knew they had broken up multiple times, maybe that was in their future again but both also knew that the other had been with no one else. The also knew that the make up sex was amazing and maybe that was in their future tonight.

    Then the ground began to shake and before either of them could do much of anything a massive crystal rose from the center area of the zoo.

    “What the fuc…” started Katie before a flash of light shot out.

    It was blinding and lasted forever, before it finally started to fade.

    Little did they know, this flash would start the transformation process to change them into animal hybrids and whichever animal they where nearest too would be used as a template to transform them.

    Which animal are they near to?
