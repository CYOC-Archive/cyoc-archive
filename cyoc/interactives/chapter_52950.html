    Terry had enjoyed the babies that Frank had produced and it was nice
    that he was able to have Frank produce more and more of them for him.

    He kept one of the babies for his own and sold off the others to
    people that wanted a baby but couldn't have one of their own or didn't
    want to go through with becoming out of shape. And they paid well. The
    dozen babies Frank was carrying now would make Terry well over a
    million dollars. But he was expensive to take care of while he was in
    the process of being pregnant.

    Food, clothes, a place to stay, and a nurse to take care of him were
    expensive.

    Terry had a nurse friend that needed a job and he was a no questions
    asked sort of guy but it was expensive to have a live in nurse.

    Terry had to up his production. He needed all the men in his
    construction business. Well all except Don the new kid. He was barely
    out of high school and wasn't very knowledgeable about construction
    anyway. His football build would help with the babies but he was too
    smart Terry needed him as dumb or dumber than Frank and in moments he
    was.

    Terry had maxed out Franks capacity with a dozen babies but he was
    going to see if he could do more with Don. Frank was in his late 30's
    and he thought that maybe a young body could do more, plus his orders
    were vast. He now had a 5 year waiting list and he hoped that maybe
    with Don he could cut that in half or better. Terry had Don first
    impregnated with 15 babies. A mixture of boys and girls. He had so
    many different orders. He knew he had started Frank with 9 and now was
    up to a dozen but if he started the boy with 15 maybe he could push it
    to 2 dozen next. or at least 18.

    To increase his harem he went off to the gym and found some muscle
    boys that were easily led. He didn't have to reduce their IQ too much
    but lowering it made them so much more docile and controllable.

    And Terry expanded his production.

    Don went from beefy to fat in no time with his litter of babies and
    damn did he have some huge knockers.

    What do you do now?
