    'Some time ago'? How long have you been out here?

    Oh what a fool you are! You can cover up your nakedness, but not your green skin. Now he knows. Is the prince prone to gossip and soon everyone will know of your curse? Will he be disgusted and reject you forever?

    "Thank you."

    Derek starts to leave then stops. "What an enchanting perfume you are wearing..."

    Perfume? You aren't wearing any perfume! What is he talking about? Flowers smell sweetly to attract bees - is it something like that? (You are not familiar with plant pheromones.)

    The prince takes a few steps toward you, seemingly entranced.

    "You should go seek help" you say, but you realize your words are half-hearted.

    He slowly walks toward you. You stand there, clutching your dress to your body, but not backing away.

    Now the prince is within arm's reach.

    "You are a beautiful flower, Sara. May I kiss you?"

    Derek gently wraps his arms around you and pulls you into a tender kiss. Your grip weakens and your dress falls to the ground. You wrap your arms around Derek and press your naked body against him.
