    Since our classes for the day were completed we returned to my car. “See?” said Karyn. “It’s all in one
    piece and still here,” she said as she opened her unlocked door and entered.

    “Yea, yea,” I said as I got in the car. I turned on the car but did not immediately leave. “What did that
    feel like?” I asked her.

    “You know,” she said. “I really can’t fully describe it. The change felt good, but it was a little
    uncomfortable. But the running, the exploring…oh…just…” She paused as she closed her eyes and took a deep
    breath. “It was just so exhilarating.”

    “Where are we going?” I asked her. “It’s almost that time.”

    “Oh, right,” she said as she snapped out of her trance. “Let’s just go to your house.”

    We reached my house at about 3:30. I was grateful for the time to spare. We walked into the house and found
    my mother paying some bills in the kitchen. She looked up at us and asked, “How’d it go?”

    “Great,” I said. “Karyn volunteered us and…well, she’ll tell you.”

    Karyn proceeded to describe her experience. “Sounds like you enjoyed it,” said my mother. “Would you do it
    again if you had to?”

    “I think so,” Karyn answered. “The question is whether or not your daughter would be courageous enough to do
    it.”

    “Oh, shut it,” I said to Karyn. “I’d do it if I had to,” I insisted. “Especially since Karyn has shown me
    it’s safe.”

    “Oh so that was your game,” said Karyn. All of us laughed.

    Suddenly the front door burst open. I expected to see Leah, but I did not. A tall figure walked into the
    house and practically ran into the kitchen. “Diane! Diane! Diane!” called out a voice.

    My mother looked puzzled and said, “Bobbi, what are you doing home?”

    “Oh, Diane, I finally got it,” said the person who walked into the kitchen, who was obviously my father turned
    mother. She pretty much ignored Karyn and myself and sat down next to my mother. She was tall and had the
    same type of hair color as I did. Like everyone else we encountered, she had a very shapely figure with good
    size on her breasts and her cock. She also appeared to be in her late 20s.

    “You got the promotion?” my mother asked as her face slowly turned into a smile.

    “I got the promotion!” my other mother confirmed. My mother Diane screamed in happiness and threw her arms
    around mother Bobbi. I realized that’s how I’d have to describe them to other people now, so I figured I had
    better do it myself. “Finally we can do it!” At this point mother Bobbi noticed our presence. “Girls! I’m
    sorry, I barged right past you. How are you?” she asked.

    “Good,” Karyn said slowly.

    “Joey, this is a great day for me and your mother,” said Bobbi. “I finally got the promotion to branch
    manager.”

    “Great,” I said. “So what does that mean?”

    Bobbi scoffed. “It means a lot,” she said. “It means more income, more benefits. I can pick my own hours,
    so it means I’ll be home by 4:00 every day. But most of all it means we can finally expand the family!”

    This caught me completely off guard. In the old world, my parents said they were perfectly happy with two
    children, since they had been blessed with one son and one daughter. “Really?” I asked. “After all this
    time?”

    Bobbi and Diane looked at each other quizzically. Diane said, “Joey, you’ve been forgetting a lot of things
    lately. You know that we always intended to have another when your mother could. Now we can!”

    “That’s right,” agreed Bobbi. “Now that I’m going to be manager, I don’t have any fears about going out on
    maternity.”

    Karyn said, “Oh, so you’re…”

    Bobbi cut her off and said, “Yep, I’m going to be the one to give birth this time! I can’t wait!” I nearly
    fainted. I knew that my father was now going to be a mother, but I didn’t ever think this would happen.
    “Joey, are you OK? You look a little pale.”

    “No, no,” I said. “I’m fine. Just a little overwhelmed.”

    “Are you unhappy about this?” Diane asked.

    “Of course not,” I said. “Whatever makes you happy makes me happy. When are you going to start trying?”

    My mothers looked at each and smiled. “Right away,” said Diane.

    Karyn took me by the hand. “We’ll get out of your way then,” she said. “We have some things to discuss
    anyway.” Before anyone could say anything else, Karyn led me upstairs to my room.
