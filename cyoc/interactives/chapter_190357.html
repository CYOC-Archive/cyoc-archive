    While Bill was pondering on what to do with this, but found an something that really caught his attention. Out of all the options he scrolled through, this one intrigued him. "No way! I can turn anyone, or anything into an inanimate object? That sounds like an interesting option to test this out." Bill thought to himself as he click on the option. What appears is many options including clothing, foods, furniture, and much, much more. Down near the bottom are some parameters he can adjust. Such as sight, smell, mobility, taste, touch, and hearing. There is a setting of adjusting the duration, change reality on having the mind that the target always been an object, another option gives the the ability for the target to communicate telepathically or verbally to others around them, and lastly can make others unaware or aware of the target's presence. At the very bottom of the page there is a world map, by default this map is disabled. "Hmm, what's the map for?" Bill thought to himself...

    With so much awe, Bill thought about testing this thing on himself. But instead, he came up with the idea to try his little experiment on someone else. Being that he feels unsure about this, he really want's to try this to see if this is bullshit or not. (Which he thinks it is anyways.) He needs a worthy specimen to test his devilish inanimate object transformation on. Immediately he thought of his son Jeff, which seems logical enough to test it on him other than himself. But then he backpedaled starts to get afraid, about targeting this thing on his own son. It's his son overall, what if this machine actually works, and turns his son into something that isn't living? Bill is scared for the thought of his son not becoming human, or should say, something that's not really living nor his son. Will he be able to transform his son back at anytime? Before making his decision on what "thing" his son should be, he want's investigate more before committing, he scrolled back on top of the screen and, opened the description of this section.

    The state follows: "This option allows you to transform any living thing, completely into one of the objects listed below. The target will biologically down to the bare bones become the object of your choosing. All of the listed parameters shown, allows you to adjust some things before transforming, like reality and senses for example. There are options to set a teleportation on where the target should be before transforming, by using the map at the very bottom. After the transformations complete the target will be teleported to the area of choice. Examples can be, somewhere in your own kitchen, a random person's kitchen, your neighbor's bathroom, pretty much anywhere around the world. (This teleportation option was recently added during the release of the Chronivac Version 4.0.) Do not worry, the target won't be killed during this whole transformation process, if some sort of thing goes wrong during this process, the machine will scan the target's entire being before commencing the transformation, saving an exact bare bones backup of the target's whole body, also including a backup of the target's whole consciousness if needed. If anything goes wrong, the machine will trigger the target's backup and will be immediately transform back to it's original human self, and it's recommended that you to report a bug as soon as possible to TransDem Labs if such a thing happens. Also noting that target will never die or loose conscious, if the target is accidentally or intentionally digested, or destroyed like being stomped on for example. It won't matter what happens, the target's presence will still be there, whilst still behaving like any other object that's selected. An immunity trait will be implemented to the target's form at all times and cannot be altered through the Chronivac no matter what. We at TransDem Labs did this so people transformed into any state won't ever face death, or be forever stuck as the object of choice. Again if anything isn't working properly or if the Chronivac throws any errors, the target will immediately be transformed back. If in some cases the machine can't ever transform the target back, it will use the target's backup as mentioned before. If you set the target's time limit to 'infinite' The target will at all times have access to the Chronivac, if the target want's to change back. The target can also be changed back by someone else manually through the machine. If you set on time limit, The target will never be able to access the Chronivac in it's transformed state, having the target to wait to transform back. That includes attempting to change the target's time limit manually through the machine by someone else. Other than, that have fun!~"

    "Hmm, makes sense... Alright, I feel way less nervous trying this on my son." Bill said to himself.

    Bill seems to have his trust on this machine. It, in a way, sounds interesting. Maybe his son might like this experience as an object. The machine did say that his son has some sort of transformation fetish. His son can finally fulfill his fantasy! He thought about it more, and decided to bite the bullet and go for it! But first, he has to make a choice on what object his son should become. The question is, what will he be?
