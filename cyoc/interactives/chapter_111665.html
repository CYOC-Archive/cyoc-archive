    Due to the new reality of the curse two new species were created the Horsetyr and Cowtyr. Everyone in this reality had always thought these species were always there, but to Jennifer and Susan they weren't.

    Even though Jennifer and Susan were now late to school they still went. They had to stop by the school office because they were tardy they told the secretary at the office that they slept in however that's not a really good excuse and they got a afternoon detention.

    They completely missed 1st period and headed to 2nd period which they shared. 2nd period is biology. Coincidentally they would start a new section in their term known as Tyric species. This including the study of Horsetyrs and Cowtyrs.

    Typically each class the teacher Mr. Nemon would start with a lecture and give out a homework assignment.

    This lecture was beneficial to Susan and Jen, because they still had their old memories and wasn't quite sure how their bodies worked.

    Here is what they learned:
    Horsetyrs or Equine Sapiens cannot reproduce with humans or horses. They can only reproduce with other horsetyrs.
    Cowtyrs or Bovine Sapiens likewise cannot reproduce with humans or cows. They can only reproduce with other Cowtyrs.
    Tyric species are very rare. Less than 5 million of each species exists.
    Bovine Sapiens can only produce milk when a calf is born and not everyday like normal cows. Cow udders for Bovine Sapiens are much smaller than udders of a regular cow.
    Equine Sapiens are fast runners, naturally they are not as fast regular horses because they only have two legs instead of four.

    After the lesson Jen and Susan felt better knowing more about their kind and then parted ways when the bell rang because they have different classes.
