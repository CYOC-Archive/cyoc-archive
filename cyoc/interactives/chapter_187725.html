    Yvonne 03 was excited. After living inside a laboratory for 17 years, at last, she was going to meet her 'mother'.

    Of course, mother was just an affective nickname. She really didn't had a mother and it was well known that hybrids were unable to conceive. But she had donated a sample of her blood to create her. This made her the closest thing to a biological mother that she had. She had been created in a laboratory controlled by the Earth's Hive. A place managed by the exclusive cast of Hive's scientists. Part for a project that had the sole objective of create a perfect successor to the current Earth's queen.

    Now, she had been moved from her room in the underground laboratory and was being driven to the capital, inside one of shuttle built in the orbital shipyards. An oval sphere that appeared to be made of pure silver.

    Her silent companions were Ro and Oda. Two of the main scientific officers who ran the laboratory and had had an important role in her incubation and subsequent upbringing. Of course, not being part of the Hive, the bioengineered young human, had not been able to enjoy the connection that the Hive Mind provided, making her education slow, for exasperation of both hybrids.

    As they approached the palace, Yvonne was stunned by the majesty of that building taken from another world. A perfect black dome surrounded by ten huge towers that rose from the center of the megacity to the skies. Occasionally small objects, resembling the ship she was now, would pop off from their tips and disappear into the clouds above them.

    Her shuttle hopped on top of one of these towers and began its descent. Instead of crashing to the surface, the substance that covered the tower opened up to greet the newcomers.

    Once inside the structure, the walls of the shuttle merged with the walls. Leaving an opening.

    A solitary figure was to greet them and Yvonne was again gawking at the appearance of this new creature.

    A naked hybrid, her skin was beautiful marine blue. Round, she had two voluminous breasts and the a penis made of rubber was dangling between her slender legs. But what was really intimidating was her gaze: bright eyes watched the movements of the newcomers like a bird of prey. She was, without a doubt, the perfect example of what a hybrid should be. Everything in her was perfect except for one detail ... the left part of her oval face was marked by a series of healed burns.

    Yvonne had heard something about this: apparentlym the queen had been injured during the beginning of the Last War by a tactical nuclear weapon. She had emerged relatively unscathed thanks to the Hive's medical advances and her incredible regenerative abilities. Yet, radiation had left its mark on the young queen. Some, apparently, were deeper than others.

    With a slight movement of her hand, she gestured for her to come closer. Yvonne, blushing for her previous inappropriate behavior, advanced towards that figure of power and beauty.

    “I'm impressed, 311. She is without a doubt a perfect specimen.”

    “Thanks, your highness. I can assure you that she won't disappoint you.”

    "Really" With her thumb and index fingers she lifted Yvonne's face, her human skin shuddered at the alien touch of rubber. "She tends to inflate her personal achievements. Tell me, my child; Why are you here?"

    "My queen, I swear to serve you and The Hive in any way I can. And I assure you that I will be a perfect successor to you and Earth's Hive."

    "We'll see about that."
