    Fate of a Tattletail

    Daniel shakes his head and slowly backs away from Kat.

    "There is no way I'm going to let you get away with this." Daniel says.

    "I'll tell mom and dad."

    "They will do worse to you than what you're doing to me."

    Kat pauses for a second and she gives Daniel a alarming look.

    "I'm disappointed at you Daniel." Kat says.

    "I thought you would be grown up enough to not be such a tattle tale."

    "But if you going to act like a baby be a baby."

    Kat raises her wand and points it at Daniel. Daniel doesn't even have time to blink before that same feeling of before flows over him. This time the whole room and Kat started to grow. Daniel's bone structre was being reshaped, restricting his movement. And wiskers seemed to like weeds out of his face. Daniel was now 100% kitten in body.

    "Did I say baby I meant to say kitten." Kat said.

    Daniel tried talk to Kat but all he could get out was some meows and hisses. Then he tried to stand up on 2 legs only to fall his back. Kat giggled as Daniel struggled to get up on four legs.

    "What you said is true though mom and dad would do worse to me than what I did to you, but that is only if they find out." Kat says. With that said Kat conjoures up a carring case with air holes. Kat then picks Daniel up and puts him in, closing the case making sure Daniel couldn't get out.

    Daniel was freaking out. He attempted many tries to escape. He scratched at the door, kicked the door. He even headbunted the door a few times but nothing worked. Kat took the case outside and placed it in her car on the passenger side. As Kat got into the car she looked at Daniel who had a sad facial expression.

    "Aww such a sad face I just can't stand to look at you like that." Kat claims while reaching in the back. She grabs a jacket and covers the case. "That's much better." Kat starts the car and drives away.

    Kat has been driving for over an half-hour now but to Daniel it feels like hours have gone by.

    "Where can she be takeing me Daniel ponders. There is not much for a kitten to do cage up like this." Daniel meows.

    "Daniel I know you have to be bored by now but in a few minutes we will be at your destination." Kat says.

    "My destination?" Daniel meows.

    In anticapation Daniel almost relieve's himself in the case as the car stops. Kat begins to hum some song as she gets out of the her car. She reaches over and picks up the carring case. She removes the jacket from the case. Daniel looks around but doesn't have a good enough view to know his whereabouts. "Well we're here Daniel at your new home..."
