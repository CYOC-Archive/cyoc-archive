Lesily chuckled as Teen!Jane cringed with secondhand embarrassment.

    She headed back to Michelle and asked her, “So Michelle, how old are you?” Michelle’s answer in a clear less raspy voice said, “42 years old.” “And what do you think about your daughter?” Lesily continued. Michelle had a wide smile on her face and said, “Oh she’s doing great! Always full of energy and fun. And to think, she was once a young infant I was holding in my arms. Now look at her! She’s growing up oh so fast. I can tell that she’s going to have a big future ahead of her.” Lesily looked to the Janes after watching Little!Jane playing around with the mirrors. Tween!Jane was dancing to the rhythm to Teen!Jane's annoyance and College!Jane was struggling to wake herself up from her drunken stupor. If only they knew...

The doctor told me that I need to have a cane to support my back. But I’m pretty sure that the pain will go away in a few weeks.” Lesily gave out a sly smile and remembered Wendy in a walker back when she was really old. She would be in for a surprise.
