Whats worse is that if an ant or aphid finds you for the first time they will spray you with their pheromones, this will cause all the ants or aphids to come to you and make it impossible to hide, therefore it will become more difficult than ever to escape the anthill.

If it is an ant, you become an ant. If it is an aphid, you become an aphid. You also have only 24 hours to escape the Anthill or you will be trapped here forever and be doomed to be kidnapped and transformed by either the ants into an ant for all of eternity and even beyond that or kidnapped and transformed by the aphids into an aphid for all of eternity and even beyond that.
