    Sparks: Supergenius with tech powers and can transfer her memories onto any device to be watched like a video
    The Herd: Produces milk that turns others into copies of her, and has a hive mind with them.
    Violet: Shapeshifting and size changing powers
    The Witch: Knowledge of magic
    Thunder: Weather Control and extremely localized earthquakes. Can also predict the weather with perfect accuracy, and sense the structural integrity of anything made of stone or earth.
    Light: Light powers
    Belle: Flight, Beholder Eye Beams (Paralyse beam, Transform beam, Hypnotise beam etc), Super durability, Enhanced Speed (peak human), Super Senses
    Now on with the story:
    "...biggest earthquake to hit the Eastern seaboard in twenty years. Authorities are moving in carefully, wary of aftershocks, but not willing to leave people who are trapped. We need to hope for a miracle, folks, or this will be the last day on this earth for many of the citizens trapped within the city."
    Belle and the others were already preparing to go. Sparks's jet was being prepped to leave, and they were finishing suiting up before entering the jet. Taking off, they moved at supersonic speeds towards the location of the earthquake.
    They arrive to devastation. Traffic jams of wrecked and burning cars litter the roads out of the city, and huge cracks can be seen in buildings. Some have collapsed altogether. The team were staggered by the devastation that had happened to this city, so much so that none of them knew what to say for several moments. Then Sparks spoke first.
    "Our first directive should be to look for survivors. Splitting up would be the optimal way to do this. If anyone needs anything, radio to the others, and we'll be there as quickly as possible. If we get any aftershocks, hit the green button on your watch. It will teleport you back to the jet, which will hover above us," Sparks explained. The others nodded, as the jet moved down to a point that they could leap down, landing lightly on the ground and spreading out to search the city for any survivors. Who do we follow?
