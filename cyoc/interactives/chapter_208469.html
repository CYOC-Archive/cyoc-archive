    "Chro-knee-vack" sounded out Hannah as she and Joey looked at the screen.

    "Sounds weird," said Joey.

    "Yeah, but look, it looks like a Sims game."

    "Sims game?"

    "Make a character and then play their lives out. Let's see about these two over here..."

    Hannah selected two characters who were rather close to the edge of the scanning perimeters as far as the IPAD was concerned, located in a different house from the center of the screen. When Hannah clicked on them, information about "Alicia Fuentes" and "Zek Kartal" popped up.

    "That looks like our teacher, Ms. Fuentes!" exclaimed Joey.

    "Pregnant belly too... look there's even information on the baby inside her," said Hannah amazed that the baby inside of the Ms. Fuentes character was named "Cristobal Fuentes-Kartal".

    "I don't like that Ms. Fuentes will be leaving next week," said Joey as he did his characteristic stretch of his slightly big shirt over his knees to calm himself down. Ms. Fuentes had announced that she would be leaving for the remainder of the school year, which was only two months at this point, but it had caused Joey to nearly have a meltdown in anxiety over what the long-term substitute teacher might be like.

    "Well... if what we're looking at here is anything, she doesn't have to go, if she's already had the baby," said Hannah, figuring that perhaps she could help Joey through this fear by playing pretend with the game.

    "In fact, we could make Cristo... Cristo... Cris, a friend of ours by making him our age, then Ms. Fuentes won't have to go and we get a new friend... how does that sound?" asked Hannah.

    "Okay..." agreed Joey.

    Hannah immediately fudged around with the age of Cristobal, inputting that he should be 6 years old, just like Joey and her, and that he should be good friends with them both and the rest of their friends. The game asked a series of boring questions that didn't really make sense to Hannah, but she just clicked on the highlighted yes or no button like yes alter reality, yes don't age anyone else up but Cris, no don't make other users unaware of changes.

Hannah and Cristobal were especially close as they played soccer together, but Cristobal and Joey's friendship revolved more around their shared interest in dogs--and Cristobal was always coming over to play with Joey and Barney.
