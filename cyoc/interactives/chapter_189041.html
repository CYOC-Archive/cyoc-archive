He guessed the man with her was Amelia’s father. He looked nothing like Amelia. He was balding and stout. Amelia also seemed to have a younger sister. Or at least she looked to be younger maybe only 20 or 21. She had a face similar to Amelia’s but with the father’s teeth and shorter light brown hair.
