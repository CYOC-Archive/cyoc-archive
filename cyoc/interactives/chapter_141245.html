    Previously......

    "Our tf's are starting to reverse." Ron said. "That's a modification I forgot to tell you about."
    "What?" Nick asked, as the rhinolike horns shrunk into his body, turning his head to normal.
    "Well,"Ron said, as his skin started to gain normal skin colour splotches, "If we stay out of the house any longer than 2 hours, the tf will reverse."

    "Wow!" Nick said, shrinking at least 2 inches from his form. "We'd better get home before we turn human again!"

    with that, Ron and Nick sprinted through the forest, as more and more of their bodies began to revert back to their normal states.
    Ron and Nick dashed through the forest, as their monster transformations began to rapidly reverse.

    Almost halfway through, the reversing suddenly stopped, leaving dad and son as half human, half monster beings.

    Ron had his human head again, but he had some purple bits of skin left, and he still had the claws on his fingers and toes.
    Nick had his human head, like his dad, but his body still had some of its monstrous look and size. And he had a big splotch of human skin colour on his belly.
    Which, combined with the slight increase in size on their bodies, and on their hands and feet, made none of their clothes fit properly anymore.

    Ron and Nick stopped for a bit to rest. They could see their tf coming off as some sorta purple cloud.

    "What's gonna happen to that cloud." Nick asked his dad.
    "Well," Ron said, " when a tf on the tf site wears off, it becomes a cloud, and anyone it affects next, they get the previous tf setting."
    "Wow. Will it hit us again?" Nick asked. "Nope." Ron said. "once the tf is fully reversed, The cloud becomes invisible to the original owners."
    "Cool!" Nick said, amazed.

    Once they had their energy back, they kept on going.

    The cloud was busy, however; busy focusing on who it would tf once Ron and Nick's tf was fully 100% reversed.

    Ron and Nick's tf started reversing again, and Ron and Nick dashed outta the forest, and all the way home.

    Ron's new neighbour, a man called Leonard Gondman, a man with light brown skin, a small belly, a head of balding black hair, and wearing a purple sweater, black pants, and black shoes, was watering his garden.

    he didn't notice Ron and Nick dash by him.

    Once they got into their house, the last of the tf reversed, turning Ron and Nick back into their normal human selves again.
    The naked dad and son collapsed onto the sofa. "That was fun." Nick said. "I know." Ron said. "We should decide a tf for next time."

    by this time, The cloud flew toward Leno and covered him, and it affected him.

    the effects faded, and suddenly, Leno hunched over, groaning in pain and clutching his chest.
    Almost immediately, his ears pointed, and his nose as well.

    he gained 3 eyes on his face, and fangs, and his mouth stretched out a bit. he suddenly increased in size, making his shrunken sweater tear off of his body. bright pink stegosaurus spines grew on his back.

    "Whats happening to me!?" he yelled, and his voice was a guttural roar. His hands and feet (which tore through his shoes!) were huge, thick and beginning to take a green color, his nails turning black.

    the rest of his skin however, turned a purple colour, albeit a darker purple colour than Ron's monster form. his hair turned a bright custard yellow.

    When Leno could see above the first floor of his house he knew that he was turning into some kind of monster. The last of his clothes tore off his enormous frame. he roared and looked down. He was passing the house, standing at over thirty feet high. Things like bins and bushes became jammed on his toes. He was standing at least at 30 feet. He roared again and took one step, cracking the ground beneath his feet.
