    Elizabeth and I turned. A high-school-aged boy and girl cowered behind a couch with ripped upholstery. They wore some of the trappings of “Goth” culture, such as black clothing and dark makeup. The girl even had a choker around her neck that resembled a studded dog collar.

    The boy flipped through a book as I took a step toward him, only to be met by an apparent force field. Elizabeth tried the same, but could only bellow in frustration.

    The girl screamed.

    ”Don’t worry,” said the boy. “The pentagram is containing them.”

    I looked down to see that, indeed, there was a 5-pointed star inside a circle drawn in chalk on the concrete floor of the basement, and it was the circle that we were unable to travel beyond.

    The boy put the book down on the floor next to where he crouched, and slowly stood up. “Demons, we have summoned you here to do our bidding!” He winced, as if expecting us to do something.

    We did nothing, and he looked confused. “Uh, we have trapped you in the pentagram, and, uh...”

    Elizabeth interrupted. “So ask us for something specific,” she said in a tone that sounded more bored than menacing.

    The boy stared. The girl slowly stood up, keeping her eyes locked on us the entire time.

    I spoke up. “Or don’t ask us for anything. We can stay in this pentagram for eternity.” A grin crept onto my demonic face as I made a guess about the situation. “Of course, once your parents get home later tonight, it’ll be hard to explain the demons in the basement, right?”

    The expression on the boy’s face told me I was correct.

    ”You must have had something in the back of your mind when you summoned us,” Elizabeth said. “Perhaps a wish you would like us to use our powers to grant.”

    The boy remained tight-lipped. The girl had picked up the book and was flipping through it.

    In the girl’s direction, I asked, “Are you attempting to find out how to send us away? You would give up having our powers at your fingertips? Most people never get a chance like this -- to have at their disposal the mighty demons Azmodeus and Elizabeth!”

    ”Your given names!” Elizabeth demanded.

    ”Kevin and Jennifer!” the boy blurted.

    The girl -- Jennifer -- gasped. “Don’t names give them power over us?” she asked Kevin in a whisper.
