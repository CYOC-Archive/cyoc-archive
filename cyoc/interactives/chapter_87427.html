    Justin was a nerd. But he was also a genius. Tired of being pushed around by both the girls and the boys at East Metropolis High, he developed a muscle and strength enhancer. Placing the enhancer in different sports drinks of various strengths and mixtures, Justin was about to try the formula out on himself, or perhaps others in his school. Only there were a couple of major problems he did not foresee.

    The drink would work on boys, but only slightly, no more than a few good gym work outs even at the highest concentration. However, the boys then carry the potential for girls to gain the abilities of the drink at an even higher level through the boy’s semen. And to add to the strangeness, boys who drank the formula became very attractive to females close to them while the drink acted as a pumped up Viagra. The other issue was that girls who drank the formula straight away would gain the abilities Justin had intended, only at higher levels.

    Justin looked over the various sport drink bottles he had created. In order to keep things straight he decided to use different colors for different potencies of his enhancement formula. Of course he could always make more, or change the mix, but for now he was satisfied. If things worked as he expected, then he would be the biggest, strongest boy in school. And then the bullying would stop. At least that was the plan. He needed to decide if he just wanted to go for it, or possibly try it out on someone else to check for any side effects, which he may have calculated to be none. But that’s what field tests were for. Justin looked at his notes and back at the bottles.

    Yellow was moderate muscle gain and strength increase.
    Blue was large muscle gain and large strength increase
    Red was very large muscle gain and strength increase.

    Of course moderate, large and very large were all subjective at this point, but it did relate to the mix percentages and potencies.

    Justin glanced at the door of the chemistry lab he was in. The professor allowed him the extra time in the lab as Justin was clearly the best student in school. Now he was ready to see if what he had created really was everything he hoped.
