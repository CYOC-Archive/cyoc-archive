    You look at your reflection in the mirror your chocolate smiling face staring back at you. You put your finger and thumb on your chin and close your eyes and drift into thought.

    “Hmm, I wonder what other shapes you can make me...” You say to the baker pondering about all of the different forms your malleable body can take. “I know you put a lot of detail into me, but I don’t like my angelic shape…”

    The baker looks at you and eyes you from head to toe. “You don’t like being a cute little angel?” He says questioning you with a sadish tone.

    “No, not really…” You say opening your eyes, staring back at your reflection in the mirror. “I Kinda want to be shaped into something else. I just can’t think of what I want to be shaped as… So many different shapes I can take…”

    The baker puts his hand on your shoulder and says to you calmly “well we can start with what’s your favorite type of thing you’d want to be shaped as? Do you want to even be shaped like a human at all?” He says standing next to you.
    “I don’t know, I can just be reshaped if I don’t like it right?” You say turning your head to look at the baker.

    “Oh most certainly! I’ll keep your previous molds, just in case you don’t like your new shape and want to go back to your old one. So let’s find a type of object or creature you’d rather look like and work from there?” the baker says to you cheerfully.

    You have so many ideas of what you could be molded into, but you can only pick just one and have no idea of what to pick so starting off with a basic list to narrow things down to you sounds like a great place to start from. Its your body your changing after all...
