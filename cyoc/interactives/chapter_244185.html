Hoping he wouldn't have one of those female slips, as he'd come to call them, he was so lost in thought, he didn't see Denise as she entered the room, point a remote at him, then leave a few seconds afterward. Knowing Billy was hardly ever on time, he tried to tell himself, "No. Don't do anything to mess things up. Just sit here and wait for Billy."

There, he searched until he found three boxes filled with clothes which he guessed would fit. Hauling them down to his room one by one, he closed the door, shucked off his clothes and proceeded to go through them. Trying on one thing after another, he was suddenly drawn back to the here and now when a voice exclaimed, "What, the, fuck!" and turning, seeing Billy standing in the door, it took Jon a moment before realising he was wearing denim shortalls and a one-piece, whitish-pastel blue, long-sleeve swimsuit which zipped up in the front and had butterflies printed the front.

    The two stared open-mouth at each other for a moment, and as Jon became aware he'd tucked his junk between his legs, Billy screeched out, "Is this what you invited over here to talk to me about?! To show me?! If so, the truth is already known about how you act. So, I guess it's also no secret you not only act like a girl, know more about being a girl than a guy should, but you also dress like one."

But, before he could, Mr Parker, drawn by the yelling, came upstairs, started to demanded what was going on, but stopped upon seeing Jon. A mixture of emotions crossing his face before he finally managed, "Billy, go home, now," to which the teenager spat out, "Fine. I was going anyway," before he turned and stormed back downstairs.

Thinking about the Chronivac, wondering if what she'd inputted had anything to do with the way Jon was now dressed, she wanted to run upstairs, fetch the disk, and check it the programme. But, resisting the urge, she listened for a bit longer before she crept upstairs. Deciding to wait a bit to see how everything played out, she already had an idea what she'd change next on her foster brother.
