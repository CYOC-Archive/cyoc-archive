    The guys were already assembled when he arrived. Jimmy fiddling with the microphone, Zack mindlessly drumming out the odd beat, and Kevin keying out the first notes of their soon to be charting hit Wicked Brother, named after their band. A couple sparse greetings were given but Grant was eager to uncase Breanna and strum a couple chords on her. His band mates were impressed by her sound and complimented her. He basked in their praise for a moment before Jimmy called to start rehearsal.

Jimmy then turned to them, brushing a hand through his short cropped hair and frowned. “Alright, we’re going to make this song work this time. Kevin’s altered the tune slightly and Zack’s got his drum skin fixed. Let’s see what that new guitar can do.” “Aye, aye!” They all saluted before taking deep breaths, their future success before them. “One, two, three!” Zack shouted and they all began to play. He sported a wide smile as Jimmy drew a deep breath and launched into the lyrics of Wicked Brother. Everything had come together perfectly to that point, and yet those lyrics brought the whole song down. It was perfectly fine for a garage band, talent show, or weekend down at the pub, and Grant, Kevin, and Zack felt very satisfied by their playing at the end.
