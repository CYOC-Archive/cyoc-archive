    It as been several months and I had made progress in spells and making new races. The transform spell worked on a true dragon into a anthro one and he could change back to his old form. I now have several races living in the valley: Dragons, Kitsune, Nekos, Werewolves, Lamia, and the last group I rescued elves. I was some what surprised to find them but considering how wizards are with magic... 'Still less work for me to do.' As I thought of how I encountered them.

    Flashback three months ago

    I was collecting samples of plants and animals when a group of 38 elves appeared from the brush. "The hell?" I said as I looked at them. Suddenly 10 wizards came out as well. "Hello friend mind helping us with those things." One of them said as they ready to kill the elves. 'Oh hell no.' I thought as I stood up and readied lighting magic. Just as they were about to cast I made my move. I attacked the two closest to me and then chained the lighting hitting the others who tried to defend themselves but the lighting broke through their barriers.

    "Come with me. There will be more." I told them. They looked at me weary. I groan. "I swear on my magic I will not harm any of you unless attacked." I said as the magic sealed it. I used a light spell to show I still have it and they finally follow me.

    End flashback

    The elves were shock when they learned of what I have been doing but I pointed out that wizards could do many things plus for some reason what I'm doing feels like the right thing. Plus most of them aside from the Lamias are both male and female with a near equal ratio with the Lamias able to mate males of other races but give birth to female Lamias only Still progress never the less. I had also been keeping a eye on things and took a few books from the so called 'shop'. There is some trouble brewing word of a necromancer in the Far north which I'm thankful is a very long distance away but I improved the defenses for the valley in case things go bad. And if this turns into a lich king problem I'm getting the hell off this world after leave a few nastily surprises in the valley for him or if he is beaten the wizards.
