He walked to the edge of his cage, and decided he would be able to crawl up against the rail to see whats happening.

"Ww will take them to the vet and look if we can breed your mouse right? I just know Mike wasen't able to be the girl"

    Dan was astonished even after all HE had been through, they still had that crazy plan of making him a parent!.

    Dan couldn't hear anything. Jen was obviously happy with her pet and it seemed like she was not planning on turning her brother back. "I don't think Kat will be any different" Dan squeeked to himself. Even if he escaped, the world was not nice to a rodent, he probably would be killed within a day, If he only escaped when he was a dog.

    Dan shuffled around in the hay. If he could hide himself somewhere good within this cage, then maybe Kat woulden't find him. He started to burry himself.
