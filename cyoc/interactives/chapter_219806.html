    A Carrot For A Day Keeps The Doctors Away?

    added by RThevenard 2 months ago BM I O

    Penny took out a few carrots from the fridge. She wanted to keep her figure nice and lean, after all. She'd put on a little bit of weight recently, and she didn't want to lose her lithe figure because of some poor impulse control. As she ate the carrots, she rolled her eyes again at the old woman and what she'd said. There were a ton of weirdos out there in the world - this one had just happened to be a little weirder than usual.

    Finished with her snack, she turned towards the computer - she hoped to log on for a bit and waste some time browsing the internet before responding to some work e-mails.

    Except, as she turned around, her legs suddenly felt weak, and she stumbled forward. With a startled scream, she fell forward, smacking onto the linoleum.

    "What the hell?" she gasped, wincing in pain from the sudden fall.

    Her legs were feeling really weird now - and dazed, she lifted herself up by her hands and turned around slightly to look at what the hell was going on.

    To her horror, her legs were twisting - changing! They were turning into a dirty orange color, and...twisting together into a single appendage! Like some kind of mermaid, except, there was no fin at the end - it was just a dull, pointed tip where her feet had used to be. Screaming, eyes wide, she realized what it looked like - it looked like....it looked like a fucking carrot!

    "Are you fucking KIDDING me?!" she shouted.

    The change continued up her legs until there was nothing left by a distorted looking carrot - her ass was still there, sticking out of the carrot almost comically.

    "No, no, no, this can't be happening!" she wailed. She flipped herself over, looking down with a growing sense of horror and disbelief. "Noooooo!" she shouted. Her pants and panties had ripped off as her legs had merged into one - and the change continued up. She could feel her stomach becoming cold and hard. She tried crawling away from the bizarre sight - but since it was her own body that was changing shape, there wasn't really anything she could do to get away from it.

    She fell backwards with a shriek as her arms suddenly stiffened and stuck to the side of her body, melting into her orange skin. She could no longer feel her arms or hands and could only roll back and forth slightly on the floor. The changes continued up - her mouth sealed up mid-scream, and she shook her head. The rest of her head wasn't as swallowed up - her eyes were still there, thankfully - but her hair had turned a leafy green.

    And then it was over - she was a giant carrot girl on the floor of the kitchen!

    Fuck that old woman! she thought hysterically. What the fuck am I supposed to do now???
