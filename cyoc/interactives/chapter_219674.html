    Hey Butt, what time is it?

    "...11:54."

    It wouldn't be long before Sean's roommates got home from the party, then. He was surprised how quickly time flew, especially while being an anus the whole time. He was enjoying himself a lot more than he expected. Still, he had no intention of staying this way. As soon as his roommates got back and he learned just how capable the Chronivac was at making his puckered form seem normal to everyone, he would undo the transformation and change back to normal.

    Deciding he at least wanted a momento of the occasion, he asked his skunktaur body to take a picture of him. Without question, she grabbed his phone and once more held it in front of him. The selfie mode had been triggered, and Sean was forced to stare at his image as the snapshot was taken. It still was an ugly sight. He had become the nastiest part of a skunk's body, and here he was willingly allowing a snapshot of an anus to be saved onto his phone. Granted, he was freshly transformed into one, so he's probably the cleanest anus in the world, right now. He wondered if he'd be willing to get used. It'd be a dirty job, but he was made to do it. At the least, he had disabled his sense of taste (as far as he knew).

    It's a shame that it's getting so late. He was excited to try out more transformations, including on other people. He'd especially have some fun with his roommates, with them none the wiser. But as soon as he undoes the transformation, he'd be going straight to bed.

    Then Sean thought of something he hadn't considered: what'll happen to Butt? She's a life created by the Chronivac. Sean had expected her to be a mindless drone, whose sole purpose was to follow his commands. But while she was wholly subservient to him, she did have her own personality. He felt guilty at the idea of just erasing her.

    On the other hand, the only other option would be to save his changes, overwriting his human form. Sure, he could then have Butt recreate it later, either making her a part of him or giving her her own body. But with it getting late, that'd mean he'd have to stay in this form until tomorrow. And even then, he's not sure how easy it'd be to use the Chronivac with his roommates around. He figured that, at best, he could sneak into the dorm at lunchtime. That would mean going to his morning classes as a skunktaur's anus. And that's assuming the Chronivac's acceptance setting works as hoped.

    "I hear your rommates coming down the hall."

    Looks like he'll have to make his decision after this test is over. Alright, do me a favor and stand in front of the door, with me facing it. Hold your tail up so they can see me when they walk in.

    Butt got up and did as instructed. As she waddled to the door, her tail was lowered over Sean. Then, the world turned, and her tail was lifted up like a curtain revealing the stage of a new show. Sean could now hear his roommates nearing the door. In just a moment, the door will open, and the show will begin.
