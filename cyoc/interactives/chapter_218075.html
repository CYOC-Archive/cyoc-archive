    Well, her family will certainly like him... er, her

    added by Juttuj A year ago AR BM TG O Magic Mental Reality alteration

    "Alright alright." He chuckled. Ben held is hand over the well, then released his grip, thinking of his wish. He heard a plop as the water rippled, then felt... something odd. Like a wave of heart washed over him... as reality itself changed....

    All Ben put in was a single penny, and unfortunately for him, the well would not take kindly to his apparent stinginess.

    Ben felt odd, the strange warmth washing over him made him close his eyes. Dizzy was what he felt next, but as suddenly as it came it was gone, replaced by a strange sensation almost like he was... lying down? He opened his eyes, seeing a small structure above his head, while the wishing well seemed almost larger somehow?

    "Woah, what the hell?" Was what he wanted to say, but instead all that came out of his mouth was strangle gurgles, almost like an attempt at speech.

    He raised his hands, and his eyes went wide. Instead of his normal, hairy, fairly long arms, Ben was greeted by short, hairless, and chubby stumps with even smaller fingers. Below that, his eyes caught an even scarier sight. What he now wore was a frilly, pink shirt, which stretched slightly over a very prominent diaper.

    "Awa?!" He screamed, still unable to form words.

    "Aw, What's wrong Briana?" A sing song voice came, interrupting his freak out.

    Like an eclipse, hair swung down from above, casting a shadow over Ben's face. Before him was the upside down head of his girlfriend, Monica, but looked like a giant. She had a big smile on, and Ben noticed that the "roof" she passed over was the top of a stroller.

    "Briana?!" He tried to scream, but all that came out was "Bwuhwa?!"

    "Awww, yes baby that's right!" Monica cooed. "Briana, Bri-an-a."

    "This can't be happening." He tried to mutter, only now feeling that his manhood was now missing, instead replaced by something else in his freakin' DIAPER.

    "Wow you are so talkative today." Monica laughed. "Mom's gonna love that later! Lol, though she already loves you like everyone else in the family."

    The family loves him! That was it! THAT WAS HIS WISH. The well had done this! Somehow, someway, the well had transformed him into a baby girl, who was now part of his girlfriend's family. And, worst of all, nobody seemed to notice!

    Monica pulled her head back, and Ben felt the stroller he was trapped in begin to move.
    "No. Nonononono." Ben thought frantically. "I Need that thing to turn me back!"

    "Babe!" He tried to call out, despite his words forming nonsense. "Monica it's me, Ben! I'm not a baby or a girl I'm your boyfriend!" He managed to sit up ever so slightly, and point back towards the well. "Let me wish myself back!"

    "Monica, upon hearing a lot of babble and seeing her "sister" point towards the well, she began to form her own conclusions. "Aww, do you wanna make another wish? Well, I already gave you a penny, but I guess I could spare you another wish."

    To Ben's utter relief he felt the stroller do a 180. After a moment, he was face to face with the object that had done this to him. Ben began to contemplate exactly what he could do. Obviously when he wished for Monica's family to like him more, THIS wasn't what he meant. While Monica unbuckled him, he lost his train of thought briefly.

    As Monica held him, he had to take a moment to appreciate how... well endowed she was while at this size. He metaphorically hit himself. "Focus Ben, your relationship, life, and manhood are on the line!" He thought. "Okay, I guess I'm just gonna have to be specific this time."

    Monica then placed a coin in his tiny hand, which he firmly grasped like his life depended on it. Unfortunately for Ben, he was unaware of how the Greedy Wishing Well would alter the wishes effectiveness based on amount of money, so whatever coin Monica gave him would effect just how well this was about to work out.

    He thought for a moment, figured out his wish, and then tossed it into the well.
    "Yay!" Monica called out, bouncing Ben slightly. "Good job Briana, what did baby wish for-" She continued until she was interrupted by the splash of the coin hitting the water.

    Reality began to change again, but how well (pun intended) would it work out?
