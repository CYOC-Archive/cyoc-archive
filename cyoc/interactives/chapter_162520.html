    Sarah is a beautiful, slim girl with long brown hair and brown eyes.
    She is 35 years old and is one of the best employees in the company in which she works.
    But what most people do not know is that when she is not working in the company, she is involved in some of the most dangerous missions throughout the world.

    She works for a worldwide spy agency called S.S.O (Secret Spy Organization) in which she is considered as one of the most important agents.
    Every mission she is ordered to carry out, she manages to finish it as quickly as possible.
    For her, there is no mission that she cannot carry out but what she did not know is that her greatest challenge is about to happen.

    Gerald Connor, the leader of the spy agency, was about to inform Sarah about her new mission, which is to infiltrate a base of an evil organization who are creating a new weapon that may be able to eliminate the agency.
    Hearing this, Sarah thought it would be a piece of cake but Gerald still hasn't finished telling her about what she has to do.
    He also informed her that the organization decided to establish their base in a place that was difficult to find because most of their locations had already been discovered by Sarah.

    The only sighting that any of the main members of that organization was detected in the previous week was the one who was seen entering a place called Sunshine Daycare and who was then never seen leaving. This place is known as the biggest, largest and most secure daycare in the world. But that was not what ended up surprising Sarah, but exactly the way she has to infiltrate.

    The only way to infiltrate undetected is by posing as one of the workers or one of the local children.
    Sarah told Gerald that this would be impossible since the daycare only hires teenagers and only cares for children between the ages of 1-4.

    Gerald informed Sarah that this is why Isaac, who is the agency's scientist and also Sarah's best friend, was ordered to create a machine that was capable of altering Sarah's age so that she was able to infiltrate the nursery.

    At that moment, Isaac informed Sarah and Gerald that the machine had been completed.
    When they arrived at the laboratory, the first thing Sarah saw was a large booth with a computer and buttons inside it.

    Isaac began to inform Sarah about how the machine works.
    On the computer there are 4 options on the ages that she can choose:
    Option A will allow her to become a 15 year old teenager.
    Option B will allow her to become a 4 year old kid.
    Option C will allow her to become a 2 year old toddler.
    And option D will allow her to become a 1-year-old baby.

    By choosing an option, the booth will proceed to alter Sarah's age and reduce her to the age she has chosen.
    She will still keep her memories and be assigned clothing and equipment that is appropriate to her new age. That includes a watch on which she can communicate with them.

    But there are also certain consequences.
    For example, if she decides to become a 2-year-old toddler, her ability to walk, speak or read will also be affected.
    And if she decides to become a 1-year-old baby, not only will she have a lot of difficulty walking but she will also not be able to speak or read.
    But luckily, the watch also has a special system that will be able to translate her baby talking so that they are able to understand what she is saying.

    After all of Isaac's explanation, Sarah was totally overwhelmed. This mission really is different from any mission that she had done in the past. Not only the fact that she had to sneak into a daycare but it had to be done by becoming one of the children or the employees that are there.

    She was thinking of rejecting the mission but after thinking about it she realized that there really was no other way to infiltrate without being detected.
    So she proceeded to enter the strange booth and choose which of the 4 options would be the one she would choose.

    And that was ...
