    The Help Is Not Quite Helpful...

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 23 days ago O Magic

    Yes, the aisles are truly gigantic. The store, once you are inside, appears incredibly huge. How do all those costumes manage to fit in there? It truly seems impossible because the costume store appeared a whole lot smaller from the street. Nevertheless, you still feel lost. After all, even the men's section looks freakishly enormous. How will you find the perfect costume to impress your frat brothers in all this impossible selection? It sounds impossible.
    "Can I help you?"
    You jump, a little startled. You turn and see an employee of the store. You guess that from the question because nothing distinguishes him from any customers around. He had no uniform or anything. And you know he's a guy from his voice. Otherwise, his appearance is very androgynous. He really looks like a girl, especially with his long hair that reaches past his shoulders. However, here is the help you need and you're happy to receive it.
    "Yes, actually," you say with relief. "I am looking for a costume but I feel pretty overwhelmed by all the choices on display. Don't get me wrong, your costume store is awesome, but the choices are rather overwhelming."
    "Ah, I understand," smiles the employee with a soft voice. "Here, I'll show you around and guide you. I can give you recommendations too. Think about what you are looking for."
    "Gee, thanks," you say.
    This was great! This guy can help you in choosing a cool costume! And it looks like he knows the store like the back of his hand too! You smile, elated. The employee smiles too, but not at your relief. He's happy to incite you into a costume that will transform you and satisfy his master's desires. The director of the store is a powerful sorcerer who deals in transformed beings and humans are so malleable that it's easy for him to make costumes that have the magical power to transform them. And you're next...
