    much like our own, and many more, strange kinds of animals. There are four continents, Dinistria, Pelanksi, Verion, and Turph. Each of
    these continents have different, odd races of human. Dinistria has humans, much like our own. With standard humanoid bodys, they are
    the most well rounded, and have far less restrictions. They are Friendly on the subject of diplomacy, but do tend to work up a debt with
    other nations. Verion is lead by a race of Equestrian Humans, otherwise known as Centaurs. They're known for their speed, and, in
    some cases, their large genitelia. Although they have trouble reaching things on the ground, they have the strength where it counts, and
    live in Brick Homes. Neutral on the subject of diplomacy, because although they would rather not have any wars, they pride themselves
    with their strength in which they think they could take on the world. Pelanksi is lead by a race of Nagas, or for those who are not sure
    what a naga is, Snake People. Although they lack arms, their tails can accomplish many of the tasks that arms can, and in some cases,
    more. Through team work, they thrive, and they live in simple Wooden Huts, although those who are better off usually go in stone
    homes. They are friendly on the subject of diplomacy, and, despite their sharp fangs, will do anything in their power to avoid a fight.
    Watch out if you somehow make one angry enough to challenge you, though. The land of turph, a large desert wasteland, is lead by a
    race of trolls, who are usually very angry. They are very anti-social, and the term "Friend" is thrown around very loosely. Only few have
    broken the social boundries, but even then, the lifes of trolls are miserable. Being forced to work at the age of five, this is how a male
    troll will spend the rest of his life, unless he was born with the physical attribtes to be put into the military. Those who are in the force
    have more rights, and, are allowed some more freedom. These are the most likely to find a mate, due to less restrictions. The women of
    the troll society are forced to wear revealing clothing, or none at all. At birth, they are forced on a special diet of magical items and
    different foods to program their bodies into becoming "Beautifal" to attract a mate. Relation ships are non-existent - The government
    doesn't want a troll to become attached to another, so they out-right banned it. When a woman were to give birth, however, they give
    birth to several, and then the children are taken away by officals, and raised in special camps. They live in large, thick, steel bunkers
    where they sleep for Four Hours each night. The trolls are Aggressive on the status of diplomacy, and are currently at war with the rest
    of the countries. While the tides could eventually calm, who knows what they'll do next.

    It should be worth mentioning why we call this place the "Sensual Realms". In nature, their are species, plants, and items that have
    certain effects on people. These effects are usually of erotic variety - Such as a mushroom that when picked, turns you into a kushroom,

    these things. They are abundant in ALL of the nations, and are even sold at the local shops. All of the species find it socially acceptable
