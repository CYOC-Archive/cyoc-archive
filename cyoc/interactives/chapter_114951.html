    Jim becomes Princess Jen-Jen

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Anonymous 6 years ago BM TG

    Before he could get away, Angel picked Jim up and twirled him around before depositing him in front of a mirror.

    "Oh my oh my!" The new clown lady said. "Just look at me!"

    "Mom?" Jim said, it's the only thing he could get out.

    "Oh, this is going to be so much fun!"

    "This can't be happening. This can't be happening. This can't be happening" Jim said as he retreated into a corner. This was too much for anyone to take

    Then he noticed Britanny walk in from behind the couch. "Mommy? What happened?" She said more cofused than anything.

    Angel turned to see her daughter. "Hmmm." Angel said as she made a thoughtful pose, tapping an gloved finger against her alabaster chin. "I really don't know, sweetie." She then walked over to Brittany and crouched down to eye level. "But don't you worry, Beebee." She said as she beeped her daughter on the nose. "You just go upstairs and have a tea party or something. I'll send some special up to see you through it."

    "Umm..." Brittany said uncertainly "okay mom!" With that she made her way upstairs. Jim her nose began to inflate and turn pink and her skin began to loose it's color. It would not be long until she was fully changed as well.

    "Oh what a wonderful little girl i've got. Now where did... AH there you are!" Angel said chipper as ever. "Now don't be afraid." Her voice went soothing as one of her hands went behind her back "I've got something for you."

    "Wha?"

    "Why this Jen-Jen!" With a lightning quick motion, angel pulled something silvery from behind her back and put it on Jim's head. He felt something spikey on his head then went to the mirror. He was wearing a silver and gold plastic tiara with a single plastic pink diamond in it's center. He tried to take it off, but the stupid thing was stuck. Then he noticed that his skin had lost it's color and was gaining an unnatural sheen to it, turning as pale as milk and his short brown hair began to gain a blue hue and grew out a bit. Whatever it was that had changed his teachers, Lily, his little sister and his mom had got him as well.

    Even so, something else clicked together out of everything. Two little words "Jen-Jen?"

    "Yep, that's your name!" Angel said as she walked behind her in triumph, arms akimbo. "Princess Jen-Jen T. Clown to be precise, but that's a mouthful"

    Jim turned on her "What the!? Do I look like a Princess!?" He said as a lock of blue hair fell tosseled down in front of his eyes.

    Angel Giggled. "Well you sure got the hair of one!" She said as she turned Jim around back to the mirror and moved the stray lock aside. The thick curling cobalt blue locks had formed themselves into an shoulder length cut that flared out at the base. As this happens his T-shirt began to shift and tighten and his facial features began to shift and soften.

    Strangest of all, something in him liked the change. "Oh Golly Gosh!" He found himself saying. "This is, just bizarre. Even when everyone's turning into clowns. All the other clowns called me Jimbo, why did you want to make me into a girl clown?" With each word his voice became more and more feminine and part of him deep down began to feel that the masculine pronoun was entirely inappropriate to her. Her lower lip turned pink and glossy, a set red freckles popped across her cheek and upper nose while her eyelashes grew out, her eyes were shadowed in light blue and twisting spiraling lines grew out. He two front teeth grew out giving her a buck toothed grin.

    Angel shrugged "Well you and your sister have never gotten along that well. I figured that you two would get along better this way." As that happened, the T-shirt finished it's shift into a powder blue and white striped dress with a poofy skirt and a big bow in the backside. Her pants had become a set of bloomers and pink and white stocking. A pair of pink gloves formed on her hand while her runners became a set of pink pumps with blue bows on the end. A pair of pink gossamer wings sprouted from her back which she fluttered. The end of her nose became pink and grew slightly. As her final changes happened, Jen-Jen twirled to examine her new body head to foot. Giggling all the way.

    "Oh wow! I never dreamed i'd look so good! I hope Lulu doesn't mind."

    "I'm sure she'd love the new you. As will Beebee. Speaking of which..."

    "Yes mom!" She said as she went upstairs. She knocked on Beebee's door.

    "Come in!" A familiar if slightly higher pitched voice said. With that Jen-Jen entered the now clowny room. It was still a mess, even if it did have more polka dots, brighter colors and all the dolls being turned into clown girls. Beebee sat at a small table with a bunch of stuffed animals and the aforementioned clown dolls now fully a clown herself. She had a pink nose, pink hair in pigtails, hearts on her cheeks and is in a pink dress.

    "Hi! I'm Princess Jen-Jen." Jen-Jen said as she waved and fluttered her wings.

    "Don't be silly, I'd know my big sis anywhere!"

    "Me, Not be Silly?!" Jen-Jen said hurt. "What sort of a clown do you think that I am?"

    "One that wants a cup of tea?" She offered raising the pot.

    "That is very perceptive of you, I'd be honored." And with that the two sisters spent the rest of the afternoon having fun with each other.
