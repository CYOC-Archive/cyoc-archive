    Transformation Resort: Fixating on a Fixture

    Jenny and Ray were already transformed, and it was only a matter of time before Margaret would find herself transformed in the Animal Wing, so Josh decided to focus squarely on transforming the father, Steven.

    As Josh started looking through transformation options, the resort's computer chimed in. "As part of the welcoming process, you were made aware that transformations can happen in the resort at any time. While many of these transformations are obviously triggered by resort attractions, what you were not told is that some of these transformations can be performed by VIPs. Please note that we here at the Transformation Resort want to keep the VIP status fairly unknown. As such, please do not spread knowledge about VIPs outside your party."

    A cover-up? A very handy one for Josh, in that case. That means he can go as crazy as he wants with transformations, and guests will assume that it's just an effect of the resort.

    Josh decided not yet to dive into full craziness, though he did have a pretty fun idea for Steven. If the father just going to stare out into the pool (presumably to watch his daughter wearing his son-in-law), Josh is going to give him a more appropriate form. It was a straightforward transformation, so he didn't have any difficulty finding the option he wanted. Within moments, Steven had been turned into a statue, made of stone. Josh looked at the image of Steven, admiring his handiwork. Then, inspiration hit. Steven was standing at the edge of the pool, naked. So Josh made some furthur modifications, turning Steven into a fountain, with water pouring out of his penis.

    That was enough for the transformation. But why stop there? Josh decided to really test out his VIP powers. First, Josh changed Steven's name from "Steven Hartley" to "Steven the Fountain". He then removed his relationships to his wife, daughter, and son-in-law. Finally, as an extra twist, he set Steven's own status to "Property of the Transformation Resort". For now, he left Steven's awareness to 100%, as well as the family's. Let them all contend with the fact that Steven no longer belongs to them.

    At this, the resort's computer once again chimed in. "Please note that Steven the Fountain is still registered as a guest. This cannot be changed by VIPs. As with most guests, when Steven's visit is up, he will be given the options to remain in his new form or return to his old life, however altered it may be from his party's own decisions. However, if he chooses to remain, he will be allowed to keep his awareness, as per your current settings."

    Josh mulled over what he just heard. Steven will have the final say whether he remains property of the resort. He supposed he could add "being a fountain" to Steve's interests, make it more likely that he would remain. But it was enough to know that Steven would likely remain a fountain for the remainder of his visit, thanks to him now being affixed to the resort's plumbing.

    Thinking of nothing else to change, Josh hit the "exit" panel, returning to reality.

    Looking down to the pool area, Josh could immediately see his handiwork. Sure enough, a stone fountain resembling Steven now stood at the edge of the pool, water pouring out of its penis. Some of the immidate bystanders immediately took notice, walking up to the fountain and admiring it. One person even posed next to it for a picture.

    Then looking around the pool, Josh spotted Steven's former daughter, Jenny. She was swimming at the far end. While she was too far for Josh to admire her monkey features, he could just make out the blue swimsuit she was wearing, Ray. It didn't seem like she noticed her father's change, yet.

    Come to think of it, with Josh being on a third-floor balcony, he couldn't get a good look at his own handiwork, either. He decided to go into the pool area to get a better look. Though, why walk down when you can transform?
