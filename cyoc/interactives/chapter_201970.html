    *3:36 pm*

    Conner had just gotten home from school, the place he really hated. He lived in a poor town with a lot of crime, even at the school. Any attempts the school made to ban weapons fell on it's head. Conner was a stereotypical goth. He wore black and blue gloves that went up to his elbows, a black beanie, a watch he got from his grandfather, a black jacket, black t-shirt, jeans with a belt, and black shoes. He had black hair, brown eyes, skinny build, and a depressed look to him. Conner went to walk to the stairs, but he noticed something on the dining room table, a black choker with a blue jem in it. Under it was a note saying that it was from someone anonymous. Conner figured there wouldn't be any harm. If it manged to make it to the dining room table, his parents would've seen it and put it there. So they might have known who sent it. So he picked it up and put it on. Despite it being a choker it was comfy. Conner then went to his room to relax for a moment.

    *10:38 pm*

    As Conner slept he failed to notice his mom and dad walk into the room. They checked to see if he was wearing the necklace and left. On their way out his mom couldn't help, but say sorry to an unconscious Conner.

    *7:34 am*

    Conner had arrived at school when he noticed a kid named Jimmy and his second in command Tixtla. Jimmy was the son of a very powerful member of the mafia. The only reason Jimmy was in such a poor section of town was because he was recruiting people for his father's new generation of hit men. Tixtla was one of them. She was a normal girl until radioactive poisoning nearly killed her. Fortunately she was saved by Jimmy, who she pledge herself to after he saved her life. Now she acts like a bodyguard. Hurting anyone who tries to hurt Jimmy or anyone he wants hurt. Conner was trying to walk by them when he felt a baseball hit him in the back of the head. He fell to the ground as he heard a chuckle nearby him.

    Jimmy: How'd you like that goth?

    Jimmy: Oh where do you think you're going?
