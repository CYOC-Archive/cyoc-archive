    Caleb Sanchez sat in his room. One week ago, he had met a girl and things seemed to be looking up for him. Her name was Sarah, she lived in Caleb's neighbourhood and seemed to be taking in interest in him, which he thought was all in your head, until she approached him. She was beautiful, she could've been a model as she was drop-dead gorgeous. As a pretty scrawny boy, of average height, average intellect, and average personality, Caleb didn't know how he managed to get her. He was too thin and short to be considered a jock, yet too average in school to be grouped with the nerds, and he had no unique interests that defined him. Caleb was slightly suspicious though. As perfect as Sarah seemingly was, there was something weird about her. She was very vengeful, and was obsessive over every person she dated. Caleb wasn't the first, not by a mile. Caleb knew that Sarah had had at least six boyfriends before him, all living in the same neighbourhood. It was said that when someone would break up with Sarah, they would soon disappear, a new person moving into their house. Caleb had noticed this, but never paid any attention. The weirdest part however, was that there were never any moving trucks or anything of the sort. One day everything would be normal, and the next day, a new family would be there with new cars in the driveway, new yard decorations, and the people that visited the new families saw that they even had new furniture inside. Nothing ever happened, and yet everything changed. It was almost as if the new families were the old families transformed, but that was just a silly theory. All that mattered was that Caleb had a new girlfriend who loved him as much as he loved her, and he would never do anything to harm her.
