    "He is going to belong to Shannon," says the voice.
    "Who?" Asks Luke confused.
    "The last guy."
    "Can't he belong to one of his friends or even me?"
    "It doesn't work that way. When someone is permanently transformed they belong to their last owner. Unless circumstances prevented otherwise."
    Luke sighs and walks over to the shirt laying on the floor. He picks it up, sighs and says, "I'm so sorry about this." The shirt disappears into thin air. Luke quickly follows.

    Luke looks around. He's in his classroom with all his friends. He is dazed and shakes his head, he sighs and looks down at his schoolwork. It's algebra, not that he has trouble with it. He just finds it extremely repetitive and dull. He looks up at the clock, he's got 30 minutes before lunchtime. He plans to speak to Callum's friends.

    Lunchtime comes fairly soon. Luke heads out to try and find Braiden. He was given the knowledge of Callum's friends during the transition. He enters the library and quickly finds Braiden hanging out with his friends, including Max. Braiden looks at Luke and instantly recognizes him. "Luke?" He asks.
    "Yeah," Luke replies .
    "Good to see you made it out."
    "Yeah."
    "Do you know where Callum is by any chance?"
    "Uhm," stumbles Luke trying to soften the blow, "He's still clothing."
    "What? You wearing him?"
    "Unfortunately no. He's on Shannon, and he's permanent."
    "What?" Cries Braiden.
    "He sacrificed himself so you could be human again."
    "I may be able to fix him," sighs Braiden.
    "I don't think so."
    "What is he?"
    "A shirt."
    "Poor Callum," says Brodie solemnly, "Sacrificed himself for us."
    "Yeah," replies Braiden tearing up, "We've got to find Shannon."

    At that exact moment Shannon arrives to the group. He looks smug, "Guess I've got your friend forever."
    "You bastard," replies Maggie who is now back too.
    "Wasn't my choice. If it were up to me he'd be my underwear."
