    At the same time, Daniel Brooks made his way home. Now all the assignments were made and the agents were in their places, Daniel had to return home to let his family know what's going on. As he walked through the door, he could hear Francine saying, "Peter, you shouldn't eat so many cookies. It'll spoil your appetite." Daniel walked through to the front room before saying, "Son, Fran, we need to talk."

    Both Francine and Peter looked at each other. Judging by his body language and tone of voice, they both knew it was something serious. Francine walked from the kitchen and sat next to Peter on the couch. "What's up, dad?" Peter asked carefully.

    Daniel took a breath and started with, "The people that Moe has been running from...well...they found him." Both Peter and Francine were speechless as they heard the news. "We believe that they are on their way to Moon Lake right now."

    Francine asked, "Are Moe and Nikki ok? Have they moved away?"

    Daniel held up his hand to stop his wife before continuing. "We've moved the Charlestons to a safe location across town. The mobsters shouldn't find them." He took a deep breath before continuing. "We've set up a trap for them so we can take care of them for good. But there are two things I need you guys to do."

    "Yeah, dad?" Peter asked.

    "First, there will be agent coming in and out of this house. Don't get in their way and let them do their jobs. Second, under no circumstances are you to go to the Charleston house. We know the mobsters are coming, we just don't know when. And when they come, I want you guys to be safe."

    The Brooks took a moment to take this all in. Peter interrupted the silence by asking, "What about the were law? I know Karen's close to a decision but..."

    "We're working with the mayor's office for an exemption. My sense, though, is that they still want this over with, perhaps sooner rather than later. As far as I know, the Charlestons have not been turned over to Animal Control*." Daniel paused as he continued to think. "Karen and the other Charlestons can get visitors, but they need to be cleared by us first."
