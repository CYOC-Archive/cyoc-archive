    “Let me guess, Jeff,” Jane says, “you want to try and transform into some kind of anthropomorphic animal?”

    “Jeff’s” grin widens as he nods, “you are spot on,” he says, “so, what do you say?”

    “So, I assume you’d like to be a cat-girl,” the clone asks.

    Jane nods, “and what animal would you like to be,” she asks.

    “I was thinking, something like a Fox or Dragon,” “Jeff” says.

    Jane smiles, “both sound amazing,” she says, “Foxes look so fluffy, and Dragon’s look amazing, with their long strong and prehensile tails!”

    “Jeff” smiles, “I think I’m more driven to want to be an anthropomorphic Dragon-boy!”

    “Then, let’s do this,” Jane says in excitement.

    “Jeff” grins, he then opens the App and scans “his” girlfriend, he then opens her file, “so, what color do you want your fur to be?”

    “Complete black,” Jane says.

    “Jeff” nods, “okay, done,” he says, “now, do you want to have the transformation to be permanent? I am going to have my transformation to be permanent!”

    “Hm… sure, but wouldn’t that raise suspicions?”

    “Nah, I’ll just alter the reality, so everyone and everything will think we have always been this way,” the fake Jeff says.

    “Okay,” Jane says.

    “Jeff” smiles, he finishes Jane’s transformation and moves to his file, “hehe, I’m kind of curious to know what our kids will look like,” he mumbles.

    Jane raises an eye brow, “isn’t that a tad to early to think about?”

    “Jeff” blushes and widens his eyes, “I… um…,” he stutters.

    Jane giggles, “all good, I too can’t wait to have children,” she says, “and with a handsome Dragon-boy to be my husband… I’ll be the happiest kitty-girl in the whole universe!”

    “Jeff” chuckles, “well, I better not disappoint then,” he says, before confirming the changes.
