    After some trekking through the forest, I began to realize that it was rather boring. There were no more animals
    attacking me, no wayward travellers, not even a hill to climb. Then, a woman came running down the path.

    "Stop! Where are you going?" I asked.

    "Can't talk right now honey, I just screwed up a teleport spell. I didn't even think that thing could cross
    countries, let alone dimensions." She said.

    "Wait, you can teleport? You have magic powers too!" I said.

    "Yes, I have magic powers. Listen, I'm running a little late in getting back home, so unless you need something,
    kindly piss off." She said.

    "Bitch. Hey, I know, I wish you would show me your magic powers." I said. A dull pink glow came over the witch.

    "Alright then, but it's at your own risk." She said. The witch erupted in a sphere of light, knocking me back.
    When I looked up, she was gone, as well as a good chunk of the ground. Then I realized what else her spell had
    done.
