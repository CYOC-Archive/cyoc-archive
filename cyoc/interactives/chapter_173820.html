    She put the suit on, but nothing happened. She began to freak out a bit and stopped, defeated. She put the bikini back on and went back home. Luckily, her family was on vacation, and wouldn’t be back for a week. She decided to change into some of her sister’s clothes and think of other possibilities. She put on a small, white c-cup bra and matching panties, along with a white tank top and a blue skirt. She tried to think of any other possibility to change back into her hold self, but just couldn’t think of anything. Suddenly, she changed again. Samantha freaked out in excitement as her hair shrank, but she stopped celebrating when it stopped at the end of her neck and turned red. Her eyes then turned green and she grew slightly, aging up 2 years. Samantha didn’t know what to think, but her room began to change. A calendar appeared on her wall, which changed from brown to pink, and her clothes in her dresser changed to be female. She looked at her bed and saw a purse. She opened it and found a new ID for herself. She began to worry even more about how to explain this to her loved ones, when her phone rang...
