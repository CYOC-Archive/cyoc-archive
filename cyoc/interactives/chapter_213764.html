The dollhouse appeared to be fully functional, just sized for people only 6 inches tall. Rei and Miranda noticing real furniture, complete with cushions as well as functional lights that illuminated the interior. Glancing into a side room, the pair noticed a kitchen complete with running water. "Yeah he spared no expense when he made this place," Dee sighed. "If you didn't know any better you'd think we were regular size."

    "Four years," Dee sighed. "I used to own this place until that asshole showed up. Since then he's probably shrank close to 50 girls." Rei and Nat paused as they glanced around at the gathering of tinies. "Wait how come theirs only like 20 of you," Rei asked.
