    Jade then transformed, taking on a form similar to her daughter's. Except she had bright green eyes, her hair stayed black, the horns grew from above the forehead, and she had a second pair of wings below the first. “So you were a half demon weren't you” Azilith asked “it's the only explanation for why you remained a demon even after the curse was lifted.”

    Jade summoned a green energy sword before flying into the air and quickly closed the distance between them. Jade quickly swung her sword at Azilith but she was able to dodge every strike. Kira and Jen both sat there watching the battle unfold.

    “Miranda listen to me if you allow Azilith to control you she'll eventually suck your life force dry killing you and moving on to the next” Jade pleaded. “Why do you care your just a monster exactly like your daughter” Miranda replied. Hearing Miranda say those words made Jen's heart sink “I'm sorry Miranda” she whispered.

    Hearing Jen crying made Kira realize that she couldn't just sit and do nothing. She quickly stood up and engaged Azilith assisting Jen's mother. Kira was about to strike wen Azilith once again dodged causing her to hit the ground instead. This sent a massive shockwave through the floor reducing it to ruble.

    Azilith stood there with an annoyed expression on her face wen she said “it looks like I under estimated you pests.” “I'll have to deal with you later for now I'll have to wait until I regain my former strength” she added just then a crimson portal appears at her feet allowing her to escape.

    “Your Kylie aren't you” Jade asked looking at the strange looking demon. “Yeah I used to be anyway” Kira replied “did I really do that just now?” “You did” Jade replied “you formed a pact with Jennifer didn't you?” “Yes” she answered “Azilith forced me to do it using the curse.”

    Jade then turned her attention to her daughter who was still feeling very emotional. She began to approach her wen Jen suddenly warped her wings around herself. “I'm a monster” Jen said. “Jennifer I never wanted this for you” Jade said “I had hoped that you could have a normal life.” “It looks like you were wrong” Jen replied “why is this happening to us?”
