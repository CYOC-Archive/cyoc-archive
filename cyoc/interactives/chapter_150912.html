    Ty looked out over his newly transformed servants, watching them catch their breath. They looked at him with all manner of emotions: hatred, fear, confusion, worry, but were unable to do anything, being tired out as a side effect of their transformation. Ty waited a few moments before speaking.

    “All right, everybody up.” He commanded. Suddenly, the three “servants” found themselves standing up against their will. All three of them stood silently, still tired unable to control themselves fully, arms at their sides as Ty inspected them.

    Ty smirked as they all silently stared at him. “So, let me explain to you all what is going on. For 4 long years, I have suffered within this house. That changes today. From this day forward, I am in charge. Now, you all have seen what I am capable of, and what I can do to you. I have granted you all a gift, to become more than human. However, I also have the ability to make you, less than human as well…” He emphasized his point by generating a small humanoid figure within his palm, which quickly melted into a puddle of goo. “Now I will return control of your bodies back to you. All you need to do, is swear loyalty to me, and only me. Do you?”

    Suddenly the three new monster girls felt a certain warmth as their bodies became freed from Ty’s control. All three monster girls nodded quickly.

    “Excellent. Oh, and also, you are to refer to me as ‘Master Ty’ from now on. Do you think you can do that?”

    “Yes, Master Ty!” chorused June and M4R5H4 quickly, although reluctantly. Ty noticed that M4R5H4’s voice, although enthusiastic and full of human emotion, did seem to have a sort of robotic “filter” over it. Dustine, however, was silent. Ty walked up to her, glaring, and she glared back. “Well?” he said, raising his hand.

    A quick punch in the arm from M4R5H4 put Dustine in line. “Yes Master Ty.” she said without enthusiasm.

    “See, that wasn’t so hard. Now let’s get you all dressed.” With a snap of his fingers, all three girls were dressed in stereotypical “French maid” fetish gear. Due to Dustine’s claws and June’s feet becoming somewhere between human and bovine, both remained barefoot.
    “Now, let’s have a change in scenery. A prince of monsters can’t live in an ordinary suburban home, after all.” With yet another snap, everything began to change. A bright light filled the room and a powerful gust of wind began to pick up. Ty smirked as he saw his new maids try to keep their skirts from flying up, to no avail. As the light dissipated and the wind died down, the three new maids looked at their surroundings in shock.

    Indeed, as Ty had said, there was a change in scenery. The four of them found themselves not in a home in American suburbia, but in the foyer of a stone castle that looked like it had come out of a fantasy video game. Behind them were two tall wooden doors. In front of them, a large stone staircase that separate into two smaller staircases on either side, leading to an archway into the rest of the castle.

    “You gotta admit, it’s pretty cool right?” Ty said. “And don’t worry, this might LOOK like a medieval castle, but as you’ll soon, find, I’ve taken measure’s that will ensure that our new home will have all the conveniences of the 21st century. Please, make yourselves at home, for tomorrow, we have work to do.” The three girls looked at each other, worried at what “work might entail.”, but said nothing as they went off to explore the castle. Not knowing that Ty had "surveillance eyes" planted around the castle, in case his new maids planned something and needed to be put in their place.

    Ty grinned, knowing exactly what sort of work they would do. Ty wasn’t entirely fueled by lust, although it did admittedly play a large role. He would take in individuals forsaken by humanity and transform them into higher beings, creating a world that only those he deemed worthy would enter. He would create a kingdom of monsters, and he would be their ruler.
