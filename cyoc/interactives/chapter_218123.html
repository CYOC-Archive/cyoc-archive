They had taken everything but the anthro rabbit Animal Costume that you had on that was making her an anthro rabbit (naked of course) right now. She lost her house, her car, all her money, everything but the Animal Costume. She even was going to give him 90% of any money she makes forever to him. But none of that was going to matter soon. The car stopped just inside the forest & both got out. The man took off his shoes, pants & shirt to reveal he had an anaconda Animal Costume on all but the head. He put his clothes & shoes in the car & closed the door. The man flipped the costume's head over his own & almost instantly he became a flesh and blood feral male anaconda. The anthro rabbit looked over as the anaconda slithered over to her. The anaconda reared up & looked down at the much smaller anthro rabbit. The anaconda licked the anthro rabbit with his forked tongue getting a taste for her. The rabbit knew the snake was going to devour her; digest her, turn her into nothing but nutrients for his body. This is what she posted the ad online for. What was what they both wanted. The anaconda opened his mouth & began moving his head towards the anthro rabbit. "Remember no constricting." She reminded the snake. He remembered. The anthro rabbit was really happy. She had chosen a rabbit as her Animal Costume because she loved rabbits. And she had always intended to be devoured & digested by some animal as a rabbit eventually; but she still now couldn't get up the nerve to go through with it. She had had too much to live for. But since she's lost everything but her rabbit costume she currently wearing that was making an anthro rabbit she finally had no reason to not do it anymore. The anthro rabbit felt a strange feeling of peace knowing she was about to be devoured. She was going to be this snake's meal. That she would never see the light of day again. She was going to be digested inside the snake. She was dying. The snake opened his mouth. [This an amazing way to go.] The anthro rabbit thought the happiest she'd ever been in her life.
