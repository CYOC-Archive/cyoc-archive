    Lina groaned, every muscle in her body screamed with pain, the burning seeped deep into her muscle, her fur and her soul, her body felt alien, unfamiliar yet somehow the same.

    Then her eyes shot open, the light of the sun sent beams of light through the large glass window, the light was dancing around the room, Lina let out another groan as she stretched out her front paws, watching as they came into view.

    Lina was shocked by what she saw, Her paws had completely changed, her paws had become large claw like fingers, and her fur had become long and cream coloured along her front legs, her knuckles looked bulky and strong, like her paws were now designed to walk only on the knuckles, a large black V pattern covered her chest and wrapped around her back, her back legs didn't seem to have changed, the long cream fur seemed to fade into her normal short brown fur, and her tail had become long and covered in long black fur.

    "What's going on" Questioned Lina, before she let out an agonising scream, she could feel her cells being smashed together then pulled apart into something new, her new body was fighting with the parts of her old one, both trying to be in controll, her vision whent blurry and she could see the silhouettes of multiple people, their voices sounded distant and had an echo to them.

    Then Lina felt a warm glow in her underbelly, feeling her children move within her swolen womb, they were now her anchor, they were what kept her on the edge of consciousness, she refused to be pulled into that black abyss, her daughters needed her, Viki, Lucy and Sofia needed her. She would hold on for them, then pain once again shot through her veins, her body jolting like it had just been electrocuted, her cells that were once trying to destroy eachother were now fusing into one, one by one. Lina could feel her babies energyes, they seemed to be pulling her fragmented mind and body back together, Lina wanted so badly to rub all over her womb and tell her children just how much she loved them, how thankful she was to them and how much they now meant to her.

    Then the warmth from her womb began to grow, slowly, what was once a small plesent warmth, was now a powerful ball of heat, Linas rubber body began to sweat, which should have been impossible, yet still the heat grew, warmer and warmer, untill it reached the heat of a raging fire, Lina wanted to scream, she was on fire, she needed someone to put her out, and when she could not take it anymore, she opened her eyes and let out a primal scream.

    She saw nothing but white light, her throught burned like she had swallowed fire, the flames scorching her tongue on their way out. "Mama, what are you doing!" Came Ava's voice, the only voice that seemed clear, however Lina had no idea what she was doing, or how she was doing it, then she was swallowed once again into the black abyss of sleep.
