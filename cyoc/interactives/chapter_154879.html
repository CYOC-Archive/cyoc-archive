    Stephen Bulks Up

    Ty decided to practice using his cool, new powers at school, specifically, in the weight room. After all, one of the coolest things he wanted to do was grow the muscles on some of his buddies at school, especially those guys who were skinny, short and nerdy. They were always the targets of bullying by the bigger guys, and they never had much of a chance to grow any bigger. So, Ty wanted to see who he could change, and how easy it was to do so.

    Ty wandered around the small gym and spotted Stephen working out on one of the machines, all the way in the far back corner. Stephen was rather shy, so he tried to stay away from the bigger guys, who usually worked out around the free weights area. Stephen was just over 5 feet tall with black hair and brown eyes. He wore glasses and he just kept to himself. He started working out in the gym just because he wasn’t exercising at all, and his counselor advised him to do something in order to stay in shape. Stephen started gaining a few pounds during the first semester, so he wanted to work out just to keep the weight off.

    Ty quietly walked over to the lats pulldown machine as Stephen sat on the leg extension machine. He did about four reps, before he let his legs drop down. He was clearly tired. And, no wonder, his legs were hurting, even though he was only lifting 25 pounds. Stephen was wearing shorts and a tank top, which showed how skinny he was. His legs were short and thin. Ty almost felt sorry for him. Stephen just leaned back against the chair and rested, closing his eyes and breathing heavily. He was exhausted.

    “Time for Stephen to muscle up” Ty thought quietly. “He has suffered enough. He needs to become a teenage bodybuilder, right… NOW!”

    Something strange occurred in that weight room. Luckily, Ty was the only person who noticed anything unusual. Stephen was overcome by an odd, intense tingling sensation as he relaxed on the leg extension machine. The feeling felt good, in a way. While Stephen was overcome by these feelings, all his muscles started to grow, just as Ty had commanded!

    First, Stephen’s pectorals bulged from his chest, growing quickly into large mounds of muscle. This forced his shoulders to widen considerably. The boy’s arms inflated with thick, solid muscles as his biceps and triceps followed suit. Next, his flat stomach was suddenly covered with washboard abdominal muscles, making him look like a ripped athlete! Stephen instantly resembled a guy who had been lifting weights in the gym for years already!

    Stephen let out a loud moan as his muscles continued to grow. The kid’s quads grew into mighty, vertical pillars of thick, wide columns of muscle which ran from his waist to his knees. His calves inflated like helium balloons, exploding into soccer balls sticking out from the back of his legs. Stephen was suddenly so buff; he could barely sit on the leg extension machine anymore just because his legs had grown too wide!

    “Come on, one more set…” Stephen sighed as he did one more set of 30 reps, lifting 115 pounds on the machine, far more and much heavier weights than he was doing just three minutes ago. His legs were screaming with power, but he was loving it!

    Stephen finally stood up and flexed. His arms, his chest, and his legs were just enormous! He looked like a totally cool, young, teenage muscle man. Nobody ever dared bullying him anymore. Stephen was the nicest and the smartest guy in his class, and he had the biggest biceps of all the guys in his grade level. Plus, all the girls would gather around him and rub his massive biceps when he arrived at the schoolyard in the morning. They all asked him questions about their homework assignments, since the big, hunky, lovable nerd was happy to help anyone in need.

    Ty looked on and he was happy with his handiwork. As soon as Stephen got up from working out on the machine, he was surrounded by two boys and two girls, who all wanted to rub his massive muscles. Stephen remained that same, friendly guy who never taunted or bullied anyone.

    “Time to find my next victim” Ty grinned, as he scanned the weight room for another guy to grow…
