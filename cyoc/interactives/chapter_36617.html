"Magia Rojo shampoo," she typed for her second try, and hit what seemed to be the right listing -- "Magia Rojo Beauty Products," said the title, and the description was "Now conducting limited marketing tests." She clicked on the link.

It said chat was currently available, so she clicked on that link. It asked for her name, and she typed "Rachel Clark." Words started to appear in the text box.

    Jennifer: Yes, Magia Rojo products are innovative because they beautify you in a whole new way. Using our "Red Magic" technology, we are able to completely transform your life, giving you things you've dreamed of but never thought would be literally possible.
