The old man finds this situation a bit hilarious.

Is there anything you have that can fix this?" She asks. The owner shakes his head. "Sorry, but that device was the only way for you two to swap back. If it's destroyed as you say, then you're actually stuck like this. But, there may be something else here that might tickle your fancy." He suggests. Your mother smiles evilly and sets about browsing the shelves.
