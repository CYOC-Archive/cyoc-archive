    "I guess we'll just have to go with plan B" she sighed. She waved her hand, and I felt a tingling sensation over my body. My body began to shrink more rapidly than before, and I was lost inside my clothes. I felt my feet and arms become much smaller, and become limp. My head shrank into my chest and I lost all since of sight. I could feel warm hands grabbing my arm, and I felt myself being launched through the air. I could hear muffled sounds, but my hearing was horrible. Then I heard my girlfriend speak. I could barely make out what she was saying though because of my bad hearing.
    "I...could...easy...you...let me" she said. "Now....have...bra" she said. I heard muffled steps, and I could feel the breeze as she moved through the store. I was set down on a cold surface, and I waited for a few minutes. Then she picked me up, and I felt my back, which was now only a millimeter or so thick, being pressed against something warm. Two things actually, very warm, and something in the middle pressing into me. It felt nice, but I was scarred at the same time. I felt my right arm and my left arm being wrapped around something behind me, and then somehow they were clasped together. Try as I might I couldn't undue them. I felt her fingers let go of me, and I felt the two warm masses fall into me. It was odd, since my body was now pressed forward into an almost cup shape...two cup shapes. The masses of...of warmth seemed to fit perfectly into my cupped shape body, and I could feel the strain as I held these masses from falling into who knows where. I felt something soft being pulled over me, but I didn't mind it much since I lacked sight anyway. I heard the faint sound of my girlfriends heels hitting the floor, and the warm masses began bouncing up and down inside me. My body, which I lacked any control of, tried to constrain the movement of the warm things. This was a strain on me, it felt like I should be perspiring. But I wasn't. I was...wait what was that she had said earlier? Something about...about...a bra? NO! This is impossible...but it was happening. I was...a...a bra.
