    A box full of magic rings

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 19 days ago BM

    Elliott was surprised to see that inside the box there were no CDs, only rings. But they weren't just any rings, they were of different colors and had different symbols. For example, one had a bird symbol, another had a toy symbol, another had a crown symbol, and so on.

    While Elliott wasn't sure why they'd been delivered a package full of rings, she couldn't deny that they looked pretty good. Maybe it wouldn't be bad to try one of them. So Elliott took one of the rings at random and she proceeded to put it on her finger.

    But something that Elliott did not know is that these were not ordinary rings, but magic rings. And the symbols that each one carried indicated what the person in question would become. So when Elliott put the ring on, she immediately transformed into something totally different.

    Which was the ring she chose?
