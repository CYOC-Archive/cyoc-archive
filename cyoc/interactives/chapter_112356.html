Find out whatever you safely can, especially in regards to the Ashford/Smythe disguise or whatever it was. Contact me as soon as you have concrete, reliable information and, depending what my brother has to say on the subject, we'll compare notes and see if there's actually a story here," replied Lance.

On the other hand, Jack Crawford has a bad rep even without his dealings with my brother, so you have to wonder about any candidate he'd endorse, no matter what the potential family connections might be."
    "Agreed. I've been hearing some scuttlebutt about him and the delays in the new elementary school, but nothing I've been able to prove or print."
