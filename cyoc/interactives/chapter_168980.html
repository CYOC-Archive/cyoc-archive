    Jamie's pleas fell on deaf ears as the mirror flooded the room in a brilliant red light. More memories emerged, alongside new emotions and differences. As her room began to shift and change, an overpowering sense of giddiness began to swell in her. Covering her mouth, Jamie struggled to control her laughter but it proved increasingly more difficult. Unlike Nerd Jamie, Clown Jamie was an altogether different beast. Her will seemed much stronger and her lifestyle was significantly more...appealing.

    Here...Jamie was a star, a world-renowned performer with legions of fans. She traveled all across the globe to entertain people of all ages. Not only did her shows sell out but they were always critically acclaimed! Then again, good old genetics had a hand in her fortune. Not only were her parents' world-renown clowns themselves, but they were also brilliant investors and business people. When the Barnum and Bailey circuit was all but set to close its doors 3 years ago, her parents bought it out and restored it to its former glory. It was during that time Jamie joined the team full time alongside her boyfriend and now fiance, Dexter.


    Looking into the mirror, Jamie noticed several changes. Her acne riddled face was gone and her body was back to its original proportions. Her glasses and braces were also gone! The only remnant of her nerd self was her buck teeth though unbeknownst to her, Nerd Jamie was gradually being replaced by her clown persona. Uncovering her mouth, she got back up and smiled.

    She was so beautiful...

    As the giggles started leaving her mouth, she couldn't bring herself to stop admiring what she saw. The braids she wore but a moment ago were now gone, allowing her vibrant red hair to flow loose. She watched as her nose shifted into its trademark red ball and her cheeks slowly turn pink. Jamie was a clown. A beautiful, proud, wonderfully happy clown with a great future ahead of her...
