    "so, basically i'm stuck like this?" James replied angrily. "I mean, why should I be left like this just so some spoiled little girl could be taught a lesson. Besides, I had no confidence that her next spell would work any better than her last one." he was almost comforted that her father would turn him back to normal immediately if he wanted to. so why wouldn't he?

    "You see here my furry friend, our circus has been struggling recently that we need a star attraction to get us back on our feet financially." he explained "you see, normally I would never use magic to our advantage in a situation like this, but in this this case that the damage has already been done as it is completely unethical. by not using any further magic that we may be able to make use of you in your current form. Besides, as far as I am aware that it was you who said it was ok and give my daughter permission to cast her spells on you, even if you did undoubtedly misunderstand the nature of her request."

    "well, yeah. I did agree to her about it, but..." James protested

    "then I'm afraid that I will have no choice, but to leave as what you are. though obviously I will not be able to leave you with the mind you have now." the wizard answered bluntly. "so with that, I think it would be wise for all of us for you to agree on this offer. I give you my word if you do as I say."
