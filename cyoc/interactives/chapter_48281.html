    "Well I guess we should find a way out of here" Patrick said. "Maybe we'll be able to find a way off this ship too."

    "Sounds like a good plan" Lisa said looking around. "I'm sure we can find some way out."

    "If we can't get out on our own Force Man and Blue Victory will be here to rescue us" Patrick said.

    Lisa sighed and said "Shadowwolf will be expecting that though. Without the rest of Power Play they'll be at Shadowwolf's mercy and I don't think she has any."

    "You're right" Patrick said starting to look for a way out. "We have to get out of here ourselves. There has to be something she overlooked."

    Lisa knew that Shadowwolf could overlook something. She had done it before and she had probably done it again. She began running her hand over the door and frame looking for any spot that could be opened. Finally she found a panel that she could get off. Looking in she saw a bunch of wires and a circuit board. Sighing she said "This looks complicated."

    Patrick looked over her shoulder and said "I wish Kelly was here. She's a genius when it comes to this electronic stuff. Too bad she had to help set up the light show at the college."

    Suddenly a green stem appeared in the circuit board and sparks went everywhere. A soft click could be heard and Patrick forced the door open. Both of their jaws dropped when they saw Black Rose standing on the other side of the door. "Well come on" she said in an annoyed tone. "Don't you know a rescue when you see one."

    "Well lets go then" Patrick said shaking his head and grabbing Lisa dragging her along.

    After going down a couple corridors thick doors slammed shut in front of them. They turned around only to see doors slam shut there too. From a speaker overhead Shadowwolf's voice said "I can only imagine that Femsha is the one in my little trap. Though Power Play could be in there too. Well it doesn't matter as you're going to be dead in a few minutes." Green lights came on overhead. "I would like to see and hear your demise but the lights and sound would affect me too. You'll be happy to know that it doesn't affect regular humans but I'll be transforming them to serve me. Now die nice and slow and painfully." An evil laugh could be heard over the speaker.

    Lisa saw that Patrick was looking a little ill and she wasn't feeling well herself. "How did you get on the ship?" she asked turning to Black Rose who wasn't looking so good herself.

    "I used the shadows" Black Rose said. "Unfortunately there are no shadows in here."

    Lisa had a decision to make. Should she reveal herself as Femsha or wait and hope they get rescued again? While she was thinking she fingered the SmokeB she had hidden in the sash.
