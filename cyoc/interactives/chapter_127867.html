    Before Shiro had any time to leave, there suddenly came a knock at the door and Katie went to answer it. Ashley and Jennifer now felt even more sick when they saw it was their parents who were at the front door.

    "Oh Ashley, darling, just look at you, you're a complete mess." Candice Moore said as she came over to look over her daughter who was still looked sick.

    "Jennifer, are you okay?" Helen Bishop said to her daughter feeling her forehead.

    "Is she okay? Is she okay?! That's what you want to ask her after everything we learned earlier?" Alexander Bishop asked his wife as he looked furious.

    "But it was just tonight, she won't do it again? Right baby?" Helen said trying to fix her daughter's beautiful hair.

    Ashley and Jennifer now looked at each other with regret.

    "Uh....actually mommy, Jen and I have been sneaking into that nightclub for the last couple of weeks." Ashley admitted to her mother as she puked into her trash can again.

    "WHAT?!" Both Ashley and Jennifer's parents shouted at them.

    "You didn't tell us that." Candice said looking over at Lisa and Kumiko.

    "I...uh...I thought you should've probably heard it from them." Lisa said.

    "And where is this young man that's been hitting on them?" Candice demanded to know.

    "Uh...that would be me, I already..." Shiro admitted before he was slapped in the face by Candice.

    "I already told them I was sorry." Shiro finished before Helen slapped him on the face now.

    "Good, now get the hell out of our faces before we have a restraining order put on you two." Helen demanded as Shiro finally left the house.

    After things went a little quiet again, Ashley and Jennifer's parents talked among themselves for a little while before they stopped and faced Ashley an Jennifer.

    "Girls, we are still very mad at you." Phillip Moore said to his daughter and Jennifer.

    "And we are glad that you both didn't go very far with this." Alexander said looking at his own daughter and Ashley.

    "But, as your punishment, you two are not to speak or see each other for a week." Helen said to Jennifer and Ashley.

    "A week? But mom...." Jennifer tried to argue.

    "Do you want to make it two weeks young lady?" Helen threatened.

    Both Ashley and Jennifer were silent after that, except for Ashley puking into the trashcan again.

    "Um....by the way, Ashley, why do you have two different types of lipstick on your lips?" Candice asked her daughter.

    "Oh, that's uh...that's actually my lipstick. You see, she was feeling a little bit more tipsy at the nightclub and tried hitting on me." Kumiko admitted.

    "I did what? I don't....remember...." Ashley thought about it for a second before taking out her compact mirror and looking at her lips.

    "Oh my god, I can't believe I did that." Ashley said as she hung her head low.

    Ashley and Jennifer's parents now took them and began to leave the house.

    "You know mom, maybe their parents have forgotten what it's like to be young, maybe I could...." Katie whispered to her mom. "Katie, so help me god if you do anything right now with that ring I swear you are never going to prom." Lisa whispered back to her daughter who immediately dropped the subject.

    Ashley and Jennifer bid each other goodbye as they left separately and the girls couldn't help but feel sorry for them.
