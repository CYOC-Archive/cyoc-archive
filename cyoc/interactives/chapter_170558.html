    Tina had positioned Leah and carefully slid her thumb from her mouth and quickly inserted the warm bottle.

    Leah gave a small whine at the transition like most infants, but was soon back to suckling, now a bottle.

    Tina watched and smiled as the tiny bubbles rose through the liquid signified the little one’s success in nursing her baba.

    Leah made a sound like an intermittent “mmmmmm” as she enjoyed the suckling.

    Though one of the larger baby bottles, it had been designed for a baby. So as it was close to being emptied, Tina was ready with a second. This transition was much smoother.

    Soon, both bottles were emptied.

    “Mmmmm.” Leah sounded out with delight, even after the second bottle was pulled from her mouth.

    Tina lifted Leah to her shoulder, her hand immediately greeted by the warm wetness of yet another damp pair of training pants as she placed her hand onto Leah’s bottom. It was clear her baby had yet again wet herself. Tina began to gently rub and lightly pat her back.

    “Shhh! It’s okay baby. Mommy has you.”

    Tina carried Leah to the nursery as Leah whined and cried, but the movement quieted her down.

    Leah dozed while this was happening.

This time she was put into a short yellow and white skater dress that seemed a bit short and maybe a bit too young for a 15 year old, but it fit her small frame. As long as she didn’t bend over to much no one would see her undies (or in her case her nice thick training pants).

    Leah was too red faced to admit it, but other than spitting up it was a very soothing moment that she enjoyed very much. “It was otay”

    Tina nearly laughed at the response. “Just okay, huh.”

    If it could even be possible, Leah would have turned a brighter shade of red.

    “I thought so” Tina smile. “It’s alright to indulge in things like that and I don’t mind. As long as it doesn’t interfere with anything important, okay?

    “Otay.”

    “Sorry about your tooth, but fortunately it’s only a baby tooth.”

    Tina put Leah down, but this time held her hand and walked her out of the room.

    How about we just get out of here and take a walk?
