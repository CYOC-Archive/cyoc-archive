    Cassidy was hating her part of the plan at the moment, she didn't mind going to school, but the party was an entirely different matter all together. She had dressed up as Dorothy Gale so as to try and not raise any eyebrows, particularly from any one who used magic or were demon hunters. She texted her phone to see if Natalia's night was going any better and was sent a picture of Natalia dressed as a female genie standing next to Reiko who was dressed up as Catwoman. There was a text underneath the image from Natalia that read 'Wish you weren't here!'.

    Cassidy wasn't really up for mingling tonight with Naomi and Diane, especially since they would most likely be hanging out closely with their friends tonight. Cassidy was also hoping that none of the other high school students would come up and talk to her, but unfortunately for her, one did.

    "Uh...hi, hello, hey there. You're new around school, right? Just transferred?" The 17-year old boy who was obviously nervous asked her.

    "How did you know that?" Cassidy asked raising an eyebrow.

    "Well, I've seen a lot of people around school in the past few years. But you have been a new face to me. So what's your name?" The boy asked her.

    "I'm Cassie." Cassidy said giving him her false identity.

    "I'm Wyatt. I'm not really somebody that likes to talk to others, I'm more of a...social outcast." Wyatt said.

    An idea suddenly sparked in Cassidy's mind, she only needed to know one question.

    "Say, Wyatt, have you ever talked with those guys over there?" Cassidy asked nodding towards where Gloria and her friends were sitting.

    "No, I've seen them around school sometimes, but I've never really talked to them." Wyatt revealed.

    That was the advantage Cassidy was hoping for, she took a large sip of beer and grabbed Wyatt's hand.

    "You know Wyatt, I don't know if it's the beer talking right now, but you are looking very....handsome." Cassidy said whispering 'handsome' into his ears.

    "Really? Th-Thanks...I...uh...where are we going?" Wyatt asked.

    "You'll see." Cassidy replied with a wink.

    Once Cassidy saw that they were far enough from the campfire, she put Wyatt up against a tree and began kissing him on the lips. Wyatt found this crazy at first, but soon came to enjoy it.

    "Do you want to go...all the way?" Cassidy whispered into his ear.

    "Uh, uh-huh..." Wyatt replied as Cassidy grinned then turned her attention to Wyatt's neck and bit her teeth into them. She covered Wyatt's mouth so his scream wouldn't be heard so loudly. After a minute, she got off of his neck and looked into Wyatt's eyes.

    "What...what...what did you just do to me?" Wyatt asked with worry.

    "Congratulations little boy, you're now officially...a vampire."

    "I'm...I'm a vampire?" Wyatt asked frightened.

    "Yes, but don't be scared, just think and listen to me: vampires are a strong race, and have lived for thousands of years. You can now be part of something greater than what you once were. Tell me, did you have any family at home?" Cassidy asked him.

    "My...mother and grandmother. It was just the three of us. My mom, though is very overweight and hasn't had a date in years, and my grandmother might not have many years left." Wyatt told her.

    "Well listen, no one here will miss you if you go home now. But let me tell you what's so special about being a vampire: you can help others. Bite your mother and grandmother and they too will become vampires. But they're....weakness will vanish once they become like you and I are now. Your mother will emerge from her fat body like a butterfly emerging from a cocoon, whilst your grandmother shall regain her youth and beauty. Do you understand? You'll be doing them both a great service for them, but more importantly, you'll be a part of our lord's services. Lord Miro isn't one to be feared, he's one to be loved. He'll bring a good change for this world, and you and your family will be part of it. Understand?" Cassidy explained as Wyatt nodded his head.

    "Yes, I do, thank you." Wyatt said as he left to go home and do exactly as Cassidy had instructed him.

    Cassidy headed back to the party while also taking in some mouth wash.

    "I hate it when someone other than daddy's lips touch mine."
