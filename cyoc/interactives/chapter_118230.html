    *Carn's Perspective*

    You scampered along through the trees away from that male creature from before, but suddenly you heard him screaming out in pain and stopped. You were already so far away from the being but you could see the transformation starting, and his blood curdling screams could be heard echoing throughout the forest. You questioned yourself wether you should have left him alone or not, but the one thing that you saw was that the being fell out of the tree and onto the ground screaming.

    "D...Damn it! He's going to get caught at this rate, especially here. Alright whoever you are you're going to owe me for doing this." You thought then began making your way towards the being.

    ______________________________________________________________________________________________________________

    *Back to normal perspective*

    You fell out of the tree onto the ground beneath you screaming, and coughing up blood. You didn't know why Carn left you like this, but you hated every moment of your life right about now. Moments later you heard rustling in the bushes and Carn emerged again. All you could do is cry out in pain and cough up blood as your insides rearranged in what felt like the most violent ways possible. Carn then just stood there and watched you suffering for about 5 minutes until the distinct sound of others yelling could be heard in the distance. You started noticing fur sprouting on your body, but suddenly Carn opened his book and uttered some words that you couldn't understand. Suddenly your vision grew blurry, and you grew sleepy, and you passed out from both pain, and tiredness.

    When you woke up you were tied to a tree by your hands and feet, and you saw Carn standing in front of you. "Well, I would say that you passed, but I had to intervene in order to help you. Your pain tolerance is extremely low, and I'm surprised that you aren't something like a chicken or mouse right about now." Carn said

    "But you're a squirrel right?" You asked, and Carn punched you in the face.

    "I know what I am! But you need to grow a pair and suck it up, because pain is a natural thing that everyone needs to deal with! So unless you have built up a resistance during your transformation then shut up! You don't want me to be the one that toughens you up trust me, because if I have to then I can't guarantee that a softy like you would survive. So get out there, do what you need to do, and adapt to your new body!" Carn said then punched you in the stomach and knocked the wind out of you.

    "Stop that!" You yelled and it sounded like you barked like a dog.

    "It seems that you have built up some resistance to pain during your transformation. Heh, I was right to wait until the last minute before casting that spell. If you were wondering about two things one yes your transformation is complete, it has five days and you are a dog breed called a husky. The second is that I watched you suffer not because of my dislike of your initial lack of capability, but because I thought that it would help you get used to pain that you would soon feel as one of the four warriors...well five now. Good luck newbie, from this moment fourth your name is Scav, and you're in charge of the scavenging carnivores and those that pick off what's left for the rest. In other words, your job is lax compared to the rest of us. Also just a tip from me to you, read your spells before you use them." Carn said then jumped off into the trees.

    "Wait how do I get down?! You tied me up!" You barked

    "Your basically what is known as a domesticated wolf! You have claws, USE THEM!" Carn yelled back annoyed

    "Well I guess that makes sense." You thought to yourself, and looked down to see the book in your lap, and your clothes have changed to match your form. You then positioned you new body and were able to cut the ropes with your claws. Once you got down your looked at your new body intrigued, and it felt almost completely natural to you, "but why were you in charge of scavengers?" You thought to yourself then paused remembering that Carn was a squirrel in charge of carnivores.

    You then sighed and jumped down off of the tree, and you saw a note fall out of your book. You looked at the note and it read:

    To, Scav/New blood,

    I understand that you are new to this and all, but you are trying my patience for most situations. Then again the others comment that I have a "temper problem" and should find someone to talk to. I have already notified two of the other three of your existence and they said that they would help you. So go to either Herb(In a cave south of the city) or Avana(usually around a nest up the mountain next to the tree you were in) for help if you wish.

    P.S. Find someone who can toughen you up you wimp.
    -Carn

    The letter included two photos as well. One photo had a picture of a bobcat, and the other an eagle with the names of each written under them. So you now set a goal to find one of these two warriors and ask advice about your job now, but at the same time you wanted to get a look at what anthro life was like in this world.
