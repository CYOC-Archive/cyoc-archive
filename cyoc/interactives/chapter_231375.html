    Emma gets to be a monster

    Emma was a very attractive girl. Most boys lusted over, heck, even girls hated her for her amazing physique. But Emma herself hated it, she daily looked at herself many times in her bathroom mirror and was disgusted by what she saw as a reminder of her weak form. She hated being a human, a weak and uninteresting species that evolved to become even more lazy and boring. She wished nothing more than rejecting herself and fantasize about being something mighty, a mythological creature or even the Eldritch beings with cosmic abilities she had read.

    The Chronivac arrived at noon and it was Godsend to her. She could finally become what longed to be: a monster. Emma began to think the infinite variety of freaky creatures she could turn herself into, from something not so strange like a monster girl to an abominable and unstoppable creature.

    Emma closed and locked her bedroom door so no one could disturb her. She set the device up on her laptop on her desk. She stripped naked and tossed her clothes into a pile on the floor in a corner of her room. She went to the desk where her laptop was and sat in the chair in front of it. She scrolled through all the options untill she found 'Monster'. She was excited by the thought of becoming one for real, she could even customise the abilities and powers she could have. To be powerful. Intimidating. To be not a person but something stronger. It was so exciting to her.
