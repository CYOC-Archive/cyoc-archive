    Now you probably would have totally ignored everything and
    tried to get away from the strange room except for some
    puzzling observations you were making.

    A dozen years earlier when you were but 4 years old your
    greatgrandfather from the Old Country had passed away and the
    old guy had left you some faded photographs and relics from when he was
    young.
    Over the years you'd figured up the story behind those
    black and white photos.They'd been taken in the early 1940s
    when your great granddad had been a spy for the Scandinavian
    secret agents organization known as SI-6.

    In a nutshell the old gent had been spying on the Occupation Army.
    So you'd acquired a eye for 1940s and earlier furniture styles and
    the like.

    This weird room reminded you of a 1940s Scandinavian room
    you'd seen in one of those old photos.

    So you tried to act like nothing was amiss and gazed down on
    the highly reflective surface of the plaque set on the table.

    In its reflection you could see into the deep dark
    attic ceiling above you.
    Not surprisingly the font of the riddle had that kind
    of 1920s-1940s feel and as you acted like the riddle really
    interested you you could see in the reflective surface of the
    plaque a pair of glowing amber slitted reptilian eyes gazing down
    on you from the attic ceiling.

    Your host did not appear to have a solid body beyond those
    eyes but seemed to be a shifting ball of mist hovering in the
    heights above you.

    So as not to alarm him or her you next turned your attention to
    the bowls.Now that you'd been reminded of your greatgrandad again
    you also were reminded of the other relics you'd found amongst
    his effects,a extracted human tooth that a note in Olaf's shaky scrawl
    had informed you the tooth had once contained a drop of cyanide,a
    suicide pill all SI-6 captured agents could use in case the enemy
    pushed them to it.

    Poisoned bowl of oats ?
    Maybe

    But those apples looked too juicey and red.

    You felt under the table carefully and found what you were
    looking for
    the Secret Compartment.....
