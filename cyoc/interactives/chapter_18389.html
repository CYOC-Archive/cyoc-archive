    Allen and Ty were hanging out at the snack bar at school. It was the last week of classes and things were starting to wind down for the summer. The two students were discussing summer plans, when the discussion about girls came up.

    “My plans for the summer include getting a job, fixing up my car using the money earned at the job, and getting a girl friend.” Allen had mentioned to Ty. Milton, Jason, Jamie, Dustin, Rob, Tim and Peter had also joined the group. They all gathered around a large picnic table, which was located on the school’s property.

    “If you want a girl, then you need to make some changes, my friend…” Ty laughed, as he listened to Allen’s goals for the summer.

    “What kind of changes?” Allen wondered out loud.

    “You need to get in shape, first of all. You’ll need to muscle up.” Ty explained.

    “No! Having big muscles won’t do it!” Jason emphasized. “You gotta look good, too. And you need to grow some hair!”

    “What? No way! Prove it!” Allen countered.

    “What? Would you like me to change you?” Ty chuckled.

    “Not necessarily. Take some guy who hasn’t been out in awhile. Take… Ummm…. Take a guy like Jamie over here!” Allen said, as he pointed at Jamie.

    “Me? Why me? I got hair!” Jamie pointed out.

    Jamie was just less than 6ft tall and he had long straight hair that was hanging just above the shoulders.

    “Yeah, but you also need the muscles!” Ty laughed. “Stand up, Jamie!”

    Jamie stood up and took a step back, away from the table, so that everyone could see him. Suddenly, his chest began to inflate a little, as Ty caused his muscles to grow just enough for his growth to be noticeable. Within moments, his chest was considerably bigger, with his t-shirt now seeming to barely fit around his expanded body. His arms thickened slightly, and a set of abs popped up on his usually flat stomach.

    “Whoa! That is so cool!” Jamie cheered, as he looked down at his chest.

    Suddenly, one of the girls from class came walking around the corner, and spotted Jamie. Her name was Kim, and she had been keeping an eye on Jamie for awhile. She had finally worked up enough courage to ask him out, and she was even more motivated to do so now that Jamie had bulked up a little bit.

    “Hey Jamie” Kim asked him. “Would you like to take me to the disco?”

    “Sure!” Jamie cheered.

    Jamie and Kim walked across the screen, while holding hands.

    “Well, Jamie got his girl…” Allen noticed.

    “Now we need to find somebody to grow some hair…” Ty wondered, as he looked around the group. “I want to see if that has the same effect. Who can we change next?”
