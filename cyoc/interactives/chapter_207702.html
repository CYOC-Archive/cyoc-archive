    The first full week in October was bringing cool temperatures and windy weather to Lyre and Animalia, and summer was slowly but surely coming to an end. For most people, especially those in schools, one thing about fall to look forward to was Fall Break, even though some considered it one of the 'lesser breaks' in School. Though some would be taking advantage of a few days off from school.

    "Miles," said Mrs. Cox, who was one of the workers in the main office at WSA, "Make sure you get your permission passes stamped before you leave for Fall Break.

    "Why?" asked Miles, a little surprised at this.

    "You've been invited to Miss Renard-Argente's estate to do some coverage there, meet/interview the new students, and do some coverage of Belle's Bêtes." answered Mrs. Cox, "Just try not to lose all your money there."

    "...right." said Miles, shrugging and heading off to class.
