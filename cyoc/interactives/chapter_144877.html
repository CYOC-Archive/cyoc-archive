    An Old Couple

    Kellie, Jackie, and Ann looked to who they want to pick as their experiment for the ring’s magic. But as they were looking for someone, they heard a voice behind them.

    “Excuse me girls. Shouldn’t you three be in school today?”

    The three overheard this person and turned around to see who it is. Turning around, they could see a middle age couple standing there with their beach supplies in their hands.

    The first one is a male at 50-years-old with flab muscles and a paunch stomach with salt and pepper body hair on his arms, legs, chest, and stomach. His face was rough and rugged with creases around his eyes and mouth, a five o clock shadow on his face, and a baldhead with a gray ring around it. He was also wearing swim trucks and an opened button shirt that revealed his body and a straw hat on his head.

    The second one is a woman who is also fifty with droopy DD cup breast, a muffin tuff on her hips, and a slight gut on her stomach. Her face was puffy on the cheeks and a second chin with wrinkles around her eyes and mouth and stringy dull blonde hair that goes down to her upper back. She was wearing a black one piece bathing suit that covers up her body and scarf over her neck, completely with a sunhat on her head as well.

    The three girls looked to each other and knew what the man meant. It’s the weekday and all the kids are in school. So it would be strange to have three mid teens hanging around the beach when they should be in school. Kellie, who came up with the idea, stepped in an said, “Sorry, but it is our spring break today and we decided to spend some time at the beach.”

    The man looked to them and was a little confused about it. “Strange…” he said, “I thought that spring break is on mid March. It’s already April.”

    The three looked to each other and could tell that he’s putting the pieces together. So Kellie tried to cover it up by saying, “Yes, well…. Our school don’t do spring break on March. They believe April is a perfect spring weather, giving March has a chance of snow sometimes.”

    Before the man decided to talk them down, the woman approached his side and told him, “Sweetheart… Don’t talk down on those girls. They’re just here to have fun. We had that fun when we were their age, remember?”

    The man turned to the woman, who might be the wife, and said, “Sorry, just don’t want our birthday and anniversary to be ruined.”

    “It won’t be ruined, dear. I promise.” The two kissed each other in the lips and the man or husband decided to leave them be. The woman soon approached the three and said to them, ‘Sorry about my husband. He gets a little fickle at his age.”

    Kellie smiled and said, “It’s cool. We were just on a break is all. I’m Kellie by the way. These are my friends, Jackie and Ann.”

    The woman looked to the three and introduces herself as well. “Nice to meet you three. I’m Margaret, and this is my paranoid husband, Victor.”

    Victor looked to them and gave a bit of a wave, marking a hello to them. Kellie soon went on with the conversation and said, “Um… Your husband just said that it was your birthday and anniversary. Is that true?”

    Margaret heard her and smiled, saying, “But of course, Kellie. I was born on this day many years ago, I was engaged on this day many years later, and got married a few more. It’s a strange thing, yes, but that’s what makes it so special.”

    Kellie heard that and was intrigued by it all. “I see…” she said, “So you want this day to be something memorable for you and your husband.”

    “Indeed…” Margaret said to her.

    “And if you had one wish, will you relive these special moments again?” Kellie soon asked her.

    Both her and her husband looked to each other in confusion before the husband looked back to the young teen and said, “Well, if we could, we would relive those moments and many others. But those said wishes are just nothing but pipe dreams if you ask me.”

    Kellie heard that before turning back to both Jackie and Ann. Hearing that the couple would revisit moments in their past is a really good opportunity to try the ring out on that. Getting her hand up, she waved the ring and thought to herself, “Oh, those two are in for a real treat.”

    Turning back, she soon started to ask, “So if you guys wanted to go back to the past, what moment do you wish to relive?”

    The two gave it some thought and wondered what moment they want to relive. After much thought, Margaret came up with one and said, “Let’s see… If we would go back to a special moment between us, it would be…”
