    The odd store may have answers

    As soon as you step out from the shoe store, your eyes gaze upon what looks like an old fashioned witch's hut. Wood planks line the exterior walls and straw covers the entirety of the roof. It's a lot like what you will see on fantasy movies and TV shows. You find this quite odd to find such an odd looking building on a modern town. The sign above the door reads "Magic Galore" and items like brooms and books are laid neatly outside so passersby can browse. A thought suddenly hits you like a bolt of lightning. If you search around this place, you may be able to find a cure for the magic the genie placed on you. You rush inside.

    The store is jam packed with magical stock. Books are piled high and stacked in random places around the main store area. What looks like potions are neatly and alphabetically ordered placed in the glass cabinets. You glance over at the H section hoping for a human potion and you are in luck. There is a single potion with a 'Human' label in the locked cabinet. If this is what you need, then you need to find the store clerk and ask questions. You tread carefully around the piles of books and you finally make your way round to the front desk where a beautiful middle aged buxom woman sits reading a book on magical items.
