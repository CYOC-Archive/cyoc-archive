    After a second or two, his arms started to itch considerably. The man tried to ignore the feeling, but after a while, it just became too much for him to bear. Andrew began clawing at his arms, scratching them until his skin turned red, but nothing was helping. Worse yet, the scratching had spread to his chest. Fuck, did one of those wrappers he’d accidentally touched have peanuts in them?

    Unable to bear the itching any longer, Andrew threw off his vest and jumpsuit top, leaving him shirtless. To his surprise, however, the guards didn’t seem to notice him doing this. In fact, none of the other prisoners seemed to notice him doing it either. The man looked down at his chest, only to let out a scream of terror. His chest was now covered with a soft, yellowish fur, and it was also now beginning to sprout on his wrists and hands.

    “What the fuck?!” the convict yelled, dropping his trash picker and bag. “What the hell is going on?!” The man was so panicked by the fur growing in on his chest that he didn’t even notice how baggy his pants were getting, as well as how large the world around him was becoming. A crippling pain caused the man to double over, hyperventilating as his body practically shook with fear. It felt as if his torso was being stretched by an invisible force.

    The man looked down at his torso and realized that it had gotten much longer relative to his size, with its humanoid appearance rapidly changing. He was almost starting to look like the ferret he’d gotten for his tenth birthday almost twenty years ago. Oh God was that what was happening to him?! Was he turning into a ferret?!

    “Hey!” Andrew called out, trying to get the guard’s attention. “Hey! I know you can hear me! Please help me!” Much to Andrew’s dismay, the guards didn’t respond to his yelling. Neither did any of the other prisoners. Were they all in on this?! He didn’t even notice his fingernails growing into long, white claws as the warmth of fur covered his face. His arms and legs had shrunk to the point where they were short and stubby. Shit, he really was becoming a ferret!

    “Come on, this isn’t funny!” the man yelled, tears streaming down his face as his voice got more high-pitched. “Please, don’t do this to me! I promise I’ll be a better person! I pro-SQUEAK!” As Andrew’s voice turned into nothing more than chitters and squeaks, his face pressed out into a muzzle as his eyes turned completely black. His tailbone began to tingle, which led to the feeling of something brushing against his back legs and touching the ground.

    Andrew was forced down onto all fours as his neck realigned so his head would always be facing forward. As his ears began to round out and move to the top of his head, new instincts flooded his mind, suppressing his human thoughts. He was having thoughts of being docile and trained by humans, as any good ferret should. Wait, ferret? No, he was human…wasn’t he?

    The new ferret looked around, wondering who all these unfamiliar humans were before a pair of hands scooped him up.

    “God damn, these fucking people will throw just about anything out of their cars!” the prison guard said as he looked at the little ferret. “Poor little guy. Maybe I’ll bring you back home to my kids. They have always wanted a ferret…”

    Andrew squeaked in defiance. He was a cold-blooded killer! He was not going to become a pet to some snot-nosed kids! He had claws now! He could scratch the shit out of this man! But…why would he do that? This man was being nothing but nice to him. The ferret suddenly felt his entire body relax as the man began to gently rub between his ears. Fuck, that felt so…so good…

    “Let’s see, you’re going to need a name, aren’t you, little guy?” The guard said as he stroked the happy ferret some more. “How about something simple…like Peanut?”

    As the guard spoke, a tiny collar *popped* into existence around the 3-month old ferret’s neck.

    Peanut dook’d happily, and in a few more pleasant strokes and a scritch under his chin, Andrew was gently and pleasurably erased from reality.

    Thomas Burns was rather happy. Two months from today, he’d finally be out of prison and back on the streets. The twenty-year-old man had been arrested over six years ago for stabbing and killing a kid back when he was in his freshman year of high school. He would’ve gotten a much longer sentence had his parents not hired the best lawyer that money could buy and had the judge shorten his sentence under the fake pretense of “self-defense.” Served that queer-ass kid right. One less gay in the world the better.

    The man didn’t notice a cold shiver go up his spine or the taste of copper filling his mouth. He was too busy thinking of all the things he’d do once he got out of prison. Maybe he’d buy an assault rifle on the black market? Or maybe he’d…

    A sudden jolt caused Thomas to drop his trash stick. “Fuck, what the hell was that?” the man asked no one in particular. He bent down to pick up his trash stick, only to find that his arms had both gone completely numb.

    “Huh…that’s weird,” the man said. He tried to shake his arms or move them around, but they didn’t respond at all. It was almost like they’d lost all of their bones and muscles at once…

    Another jolt rocked Thomas’ body, and the man fell to the ground, landing flat on his face. He tried to get back up, only to find that his legs had also gone completely numb. He was unable to move his arms or his legs, and he was stuck on the ground. Surely things couldn’t get any worse for him…right?

    Suddenly, the man let out a loud cry of pain as his body began to stretch and contort into a completely inhuman shape. Turning his head, Thomas watched in horror as his arms slowly shrank and shriveled up, melting into his sides. His insides began to twist and change into completely new shapes as most of the bones in his body ceased to exist.

    “Wh-What the fuck is happening to me?!” Thomas cried as his legs melded into one long tail. As they did, brown scales began to rapidly cover the human’s body. It didn’t take long for Thomas to realize what was happening to him.

    “Oh God, I’m turning into a sssssnake!” The man closed his mouth in horror upon hearing himself hiss. He stuck his tongue out of his mouth and stared in horror at the forked appendage that now resided inside his mouth. As his hair rapidly fell out and his body began to shrink, his head began to flatten and take on a new shape. His ears disappeared completely, turning into nothing more than holes on the sides of his head. As his nose flattened and disappeared completely, Thomas’ eyes lost their human appearance, taking on a much more reptilian look. As the scales completely covered his body, a pair of sharp fangs grew in the new snake’s mouth, completing his transformation.

    Thomas was panicking. What the hell happened to him?! Why was he suddenly turned into a snake?! Did anyone else around him notice?! The new snake slithered out of his old clothes, not noticing them turn into a pile of trash and cardboard, and towards one of his fellow prisoners.

    “Hey! Hey! Can you hear me?!” Thomas tried to say. “It’ssss me, Thomasssss! I got turned into a sssssnake!”

    The other prisoner heard the hissing and turned towards Thomas, only to jump back in horror. “Oh fuck! It’s a snake!”

    “Stand back, I got this!” Another prisoner said, stepping forward and raising his trash spike.

    “No, you foolsssss! It’sssss me! Thomassss! You gotta help me! Pleasssse!” The snake’s cries for help fell on deaf ears, and the last thing he saw was a sharp trash spike rapidly approaching his head.

    Todd was breathing heavily, watching everything unfold before him. As the other prisoner drove his trash spike into the snake’s skull, the wolf watched intently as the snake thrashed and flailed about for a minute before going completely still. Xarxos stared at the wolf, a mixture of shock and awe on his face. He waited a second before handing Todd a stack of manila folders.
