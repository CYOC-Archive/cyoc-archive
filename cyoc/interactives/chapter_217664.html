    He couldn't stop laughing, looking down at the.... shell of a man the burglar once was.

    The intruder was banging on the door trying to get to the shitty kid who acted up, He'd teach that kid a lesson before he leaves. A sudden and striking thought occurred to him to submit to the kid, which he immediately dismissed knowing that would end with going to jail...

    The burglar collapsed to the floor and holding his head. His mind reeling as all personal desires were overridden with the solitary goal of "Serve Jeff, Obey Jeff, Please Jeff." Things like paying rent or outstanding doctors bills for his pregnant girlfriend just didn't compare to the new and sudden urge to satisfy any whim Jeff may have.

    It didn't make sense, he knew that, but that didn't make it any less real, any less overpowering, or any less all encompassing. The door swung open and the kid was standing above him with a smirk which became laughter. The burglar began to realize that it wasn't enough to give up the mundane aspects which gave order to his life, but his very life itself for Jeff! Self-Care, survival, feeding himself... yes all those things he would stop if directed to do so by Jeff... it was his one and only purpose, serve Jeff however he was called upon to do so.

    The Chronivac beeped.

    "Oh, you must be done." Jeff said casually.

    The burglar didn't know what happened so couldn't respond. Jeff turned to the man on the ground, "Are you my slave now?"

    "From this moment till my last I will serve you to the best of my ability!" The words fell out of the man's mouth. The man wanted to be disgusted, some fragment of his former belief system tried to say he should get away as fast as possible, but the anticipation of bringing Jeff joy and the terror at the thought of bringing Jeff sorrow was more tangible than any concept of masculinity, pride, or self-worth.

    "Good" Jeff smirked. "Let's figure out what to do with you."

    The burglar never knew such elation! Jeff was pleased by his answer! In that moment, any final doubts were erased and the burglar reaffirmed his vows to always serve Jeff because that pleases Jeff, and he wants Jeff to be happy.
