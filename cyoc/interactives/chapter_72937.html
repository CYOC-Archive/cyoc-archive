    In the end, the girls did end up having a movie night together. Sunny had had no other plans, Rain was too
    lost in thought about her new lot in life, and Daniele(and by extension, Misty) had no desire in going out to

    marathon. There was some initial protestation by Rain, who was in no mood for chick flicks, but Danni assured
    her that the two of them could just snark their way MST3K style through the whole ordeal. Daniele hated the
    Twilight series, but liked to sarcastically tease Misty and Sunny, who both had gaga eyes for the vampire
    Edward. And that they did. Rain had never watched the films, but had a good time pointing out the
    dysfunctional and even potentially abusive relationship between Bella and Edward.

    By the time "Breaking Dawn" ended, the four of them were long asleep, having drunken too much of the good
    booze they had retrieved from the freezer. When they all woke up the next morning, their arms and legs were
    interlinked with one another, and there were several moans of "Ow! Stop hitting me in the face!" as they all
    simultaneously try to stagger to the bathroom to vomit or take an early shower.

    The next day, Sunny confronted Rain about the unwanted changes she made to Dan and his drunken friend. Rain
    pulled a half truth out of her ass that the Chronivac had somehow broke in the middle of the night, a software
    problem perhaps. She had to order another one before she could do anything.

    "So the three of us are stuck like this until I get my new order" explained Rain.

    This excuse seemed to please Sunny, who then hugged Rain.

    Later that day, when she had some time alone, Rain decided to log on to her laptop once more.

    Rain wasn't sure if she wanted to change back into Sean. Daniele, Misty, and Sunny were much better company
    than Jay and Dan ever were. I mean, Jay and Dan weren't BAD guys, but neither had been anything more than
    cordial to Sean. Never did they seem particularly friendly with him in a genuine way. The four girls though
    all felt like sisters. Sisters bickered, of course, but they loved one another. Daniele and Misty were
    hilarious, quick witted, and incredibly sexy(which the male part of Rain's mind still enjoyed). Sunny was a
    total sweetheart, and while she did act most of the time like a ditzy blonde, she had other sides to her which
    made her interesting, and she CLEARLY had enough of a moral compass to be consistently worried about the
    changes Sean had made.

    Raid did want to know, however, why all references to the Chronivac 4.0 or Transdem Labs seemed to
    conveniently disappear overnight. One of the first stops Rain made on night Misty dropped water on the laptop
    was CYOC.net. None of the admins had had any knowledge or records of any ads for a "Chronivac 4.0", and had
    never heard of Transdem Labs, although Rain's bizarre tale did give one of them the inspiration to post a new
    Interactive Story imaginatively titled "Chronivac Version 4.0". Today, Rain was hunting down all the other
    sites that might have had the Chronivac ad. After three long hours, she was almost about to give up on her
    search when she found an obscure message board with a post about the Chronivac. It had been written under an
    hour ago.
