    Quentin takes a while to decide what to do next, ‘hmm… I could start working on my “dog”,’ he thinks to himself, ‘and I think I’ll “order” my motorcycle, I’d really need a transportation to get around, my feet are killing me’. The teen has been searching for a motorcycle for the last two months, but hasn’t been able to find one “cool enough” for him, and his busted old car has broken down a few days ago. Quentin smirks, ‘hehe… with the help of “Andy” all my troubles will soon be solved,’ he thinks to himself, ‘now, let’s start with Quinn, then move on to my “motorcycle” next,’ so he starts typing a command into “Andy”.

    When “Andy” receives the command of his “owner Quentin”, the “Android” looks over at Quinn out the corner of his eye, ‘sorry Quinn, but master Quentin wants a dog and you are perfect for the job,’ he thinks to himself, ‘you’ll make a fine guard dog, master Quentin will train and treat you well!’

    First, “Andy” adds the instincts of a dog to Quinn’s mind, he adds the age of an 8-week-old puppy to Quinn’s mind, next he causes Quinn to act like a dog whenever he is around Quentin and causes Quinn to not question why he is acting so weirdly, instead have Quinn think that acting like a dog in the presence of Quentin is totally normal. “Andy” then causes Quinn to think of Quentin as his owner and gives him the knowledge of how his “owner” looks and sounds like and where Quentin lives. For the last change, “Andy” causes Quinn’s hearing and sense of smell to increase to that of a dog, he also changes Quinn’s sense of taste, causing dog food and treats to taste delicious and causes Quinn’s digestive system and body to not act up when he eats dog food and treats.

    Quinn suddenly feels weird, the 14-year-old shakes his head, but the feeling vanishes as quickly as it came, the teen simply shrugs it off, ‘must be the heat getting to me,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I wonder when I’ll meet my master Quentin. I hope it’ll be soon, because I need to be trained and the sooner he starts training me the better’.

    Quentin is grinning to himself, ‘I can’t wait to start and train him,’ he thinks to himself, ‘hehe… when I’m done with him, he’ll be the perfect guard dog. Now, let’s focus on my motorcycle, I need to get him alone though, I hope an opportunity will come soon’.

    Just as he thinks that he hears the tune of a phone and watches as Tom pulls out and answers his phone, “okay mom,” he then hears the teen say, who then adds, “guys, my mom wants me to come home. I’ll see you around!”

    “Okay, Tom,” the others says, “see you in school!”

    Tom nods and starts to walk away, Quentin waits until Tom has reached the park entrance, then types a sentence into “Andy”, who turns to Quinn and says, “I need the boy’s-room again”.

    “Okay,” is all Quinn says.

    “Andy” first starts to walk towards the public restrooms, Quentin then causes “Andy” to make a B-line and pick up the pace, sending him in the direction of Tom.

    When the “Android” catches up to Tom, the teen is walking along the street, with a forest on his left and traffic on his right. Quentin causes “Andy” to shoot a knock-out needle and catch the boy, then drag him into the forest, without anyone noticing.

    Once deep enough in the forest, Quentin grins evilly, ‘luckily the forest isn’t to far away,’ he thinks to himself, ‘time to “manufacture” my dream motorcycle… hehe!’

    Without wasting any time, and not wanting Quinn to start asking why “Andy” is taking so long, Quentin opens “Andy’s” transformation option list and selects the category “vehicles”, he then scrolls through the list until he finds the “motorcycle” option.

    First, Quentin chooses the model, a 2024 Honda Hornet, he then selects the body paint to be a nice sky-blue color, with the models name written on the side in big silver letters. To not have Tom interfere, Quentin programs that “Andy” shall erase Tom’s memories of ever being a human, but otherwise keeping his mind intact. Quentin then grins as an idea pops into his head, ‘it’ll be fun to make him think that he’s some kind of brand new prototype AI-program with some human-like “personality”. That could be interesting,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I’ll give him the ability to tap into the computer of the motorcycle. He’ll only have the ability to turn the radio on/off and switch channels, and he’ll be able to control the navigation when I tell him to put a certain destination into the system. Other than that he’ll have no control over his body!’

    Happy with the settings, Quentin confirms the command and watches the “magic” take place, he watches as Tom’s clothes simply vanish and his skin taking on a metallic texture before changing into the desired color. Quentin’s ears lengthen and become the hand bars, while the rest of his face forms the front of the motorcycle, his eyes becoming the lights and his forehead the windshield. The teen’s hands and feet merge and become the front and rear tires while his arms and legs become the hydraulics. Tom’s back lengthens a little to make room for two people to sit, soon a brand-new Honda Hornet is lying on its side. Quentin then feels something suddenly appear in his jeans pocket, the teen smirks when he pulls out a brand-new set of motorcycle keys.

    Before sending “Andy” back to Quinn, Quentin has the “Android” pick up the heavy vehicle and stand it upright, then “Andy” without any emotions turns and starts to walk away.

    Once “Andy” has returned to Quinn, Quentin ends the connection he has with the “Android”, the teen then shuts off his Laptop and puts it into his backpack. Quentin gets to his feet and stretches, “I think I’ve done enough for one day,” he says and starts to head towards the park entrance, turning down the street towards his “motorcycle”, grinning to himself, “I can’t WAIT to drive IT! Hehe… this day has turned out to be a success after all,” he mumbles to himself, “I’ll head straight for the registration office and have it registered!”
