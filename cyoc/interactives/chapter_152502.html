Maybe if it was a regular video game she'd entertain the thought of "going darkside", but this world's people were real to her! She could never do a thing like that. Though as she pondered it, she was troubled to admit that maybe if she spent too much time transformed, she eventually COULD do it. Transformed Lillian certainly had had less qualms about devouring the souls of those bandits than regular Lillian would. The prospect of what kind of monster she could potentially become frightened her.

She hid most of her past, but she told him she wanted to be an adventurer and a sorceress, and that she was trying to get to level up before joining a party. She learned that he was the son of the finest weaponsmith in town, and then an information window told her that he'd give rare weapons if she gained enough relationship points with him. She'd already gained 9 points in 2 hours just by chatting with him. As they prepared to part ways she promised to meet him in the town square again tomorrow, and she watched the tenth relationship point pop. As it did, he blushed, and suddenly said "wait here." He ran off to a vendor and soon returned with a potion in hand. "For the lady adventurer, to start her journey with a good luck gift from a friend." he said as he handed it to her.

It was foolish, probably, wasting her money that way, but then again the dress probably didn't cost as much as the potion Luke had given her, and when he saw her in it tomorrow it might gain her even more relationship points, so it could pay for itself. And if it sent flutters through her heart to see Luke's eyes widen at the sight of her wearing it tomorrow, well, that was just a bonus.

This time she smiled as she scored the charisma boost from Unworldly Beauty again, and giggled as she felt her tits grow. A smile came to her face as she wondered if Luke would notice.

    She went to the forest and killed a few venom beetles and grimshrooms to get her fireball spell to level 3. She spent the rest of the day getting her Illusion spell to level 2 just to see if it could do anything useful. At level 2 she could basically create believable shadows at best, so not super useful, but she thought it might be useful at higher levels.

    After buying tonight's stay at the inn, she'd be just about broke, but she could sell some monster loot like rab-bat pelts to get her through maybe another day. She was ready though. She'd join a party first thing tomorrow. Well, first thing after meeting Luke, anyway...

    She returned to the inn and went to sleep.
