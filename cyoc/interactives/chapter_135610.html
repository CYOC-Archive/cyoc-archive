    "This whole other timeline thing is a lot to digest in one day, why don't we just try focusing on helping your dad?" Diane said looking at her girlfriend.

    Naomi nodded as she took a sip of her coffee.

    "So how exactly can we help him? There isn't a cure for cancer yet." Gloria asked.

    Naomi looked around for a second as if she were thinking and then looked up to Gloria before speaking.

    "Well....do you think...maybe you could make him a little younger?" Naomi asked.

    "What? I don't think that'll work." Gloria said.

    "I'm pretty sure he didn't have it a couple of years ago. All I'm asking is that you take a couple of years off from him." Naomi insisted.

    "Naomi, even if I did that, the cancer would probably come back in a couple of years and I can't just keep making him younger." Gloria explained.

    The girls all now sat in silence trying to think of another option to try.

    "Wait, what if we make him a lot younger than your mom? I've heard of plenty of couples where the woman is older than the man." Diane suggested.

    "I don't know, my mom might think it would be weird if he was much younger than her." Naomi said.

    Gloria then had a thought come to her.

    "Well....we could....no, maybe not. You probably wouldn't like it anyway." Gloria said before changing her mind.

    "What? What is it? Gloria, please tell me, if it could save my dad, I'll take any suggestion right now." Naomi begged.

    "Okay...well....what if we make him....your brother? I could rewrite reality so that your mother was married to a different guy but of the same descent and he could be your new older brother or younger brother." Gloria suggested.

    "I don't know Gloria, it sounds like an idea but....I don't know, maybe we can't really help them with this." Naomi said.

    "Help how?" Reiko's voice said as the girls turned to see Reiko and Hideo had just returned from the doctor's.

    "If you have someway to help my husband then please, help him." Reiko pleaded.

    "Mom, no, please, there's got to be something else we could do to help him. I don't want to lose my dad, but I don't want you to lose your husband either." Naomi began talking with her mother.

    "Naomi, you know how hard these last couple of days have been on me? I just....I want my Hideo to live longer, that's all. If you girls know something that can help, please tell us." Reiko begged as Hideo nodded in agreement and they listened to what the girls had suggested.
