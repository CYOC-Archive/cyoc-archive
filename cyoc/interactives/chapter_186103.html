"And we'll have more reasons to spend together too, exploiting our kid's love for ourselves. Don't go thinking we do this out of the goodness of our heart. I love you." Still holding Naomi's hand, she followed her mother's example on the best way to capture a speedster – by locking their lips together. She could feel her shuddering as they melted into each other.

    "So, when we scream each other's name in bed. Should I call you sis or Rachel?" Rachel didn't have it in her to send Naomi into the wall with a punch. I also offered to make her my sidekick." Naomi ruffled her hair with her free hand.

    It was Veronica's turn to be surprised, her eyes becoming large. So many plans she made with her newly super-powered daughter snatched away from her in an instant. "...I'm still teaching this young lady how to use her powers. Though I guess I have no choice." She sniffed, turning her nose up slightly. "Don't they grow up so fast, Anastasia? How long ago was it was Naomi stopping her first bank robbery and now taking her first super hero under her wing?" The mommying tone they both took was almost worst than imaging the sex they had.

    "We get it! Mom! Anastasia! But my super powers, my choice! Something like that, anyway."

    "I know." And she really did. She left her seat at the kitchen table and walked over to her daughter. "May I?" Looking meaningfully at where the two lovebirds still held hands. Once they released the grip, Veronica hugged Rachel. Their arms wrapping around each other. Rachel wasn't sure why, but tears were forming in her eyes and wouldn't stop falling. Veronica embraced her more tightly. "Don't think anyone of us could've imagined the way this day would go. I'm very proud of you." Rachel didn't say anything, focused entirely on hugging her mother as hard as she could.

    "So this is the way, I imagine of the rest of the day will go." Veronica continued. "First, I'll take your new measurements and run to the store with Anastasia to buy you something... casual... for when you and Naomi have your first date. We'll also replace the bed and the phone. And while we do that, I'll leave the car keys behind if you'd like to go out to the wilderness where it's safe to begin practicing your powers. We'll come up with a structured plan later. Just drive the car, no super speed, or flying, otherwise its your choice." Veronica tucked gently at her daughter's head so she could place chaste kiss on her forehead.

    "Clarks are wild. When you can see what they do up close." Superluminal chimed in. "But so are we. Knock your girlfriend off her feet, soldier."
