    Agreeing with Jason, they all walked back to Jordan and Jason's dorm room, holding hands. Trying to act like gentlemen, they opened the door for Jes, motioning for her to enter. Jes giggled as she walked in side.

    The movie they decided to watch was Van Helsing; Jes had never seen it before. During the tense scenes, Jordan pulled off the yawning technique, wrapping his arm around Jes. To both Jordan and his surprise, Jes snuggled closer.

    "That was a great movie." Jes said as she grabbed a slice of pizza. They were now sitting at the table with Jes directly across from them. "And how did you know Hawaiian style was my favorite."

    Jason struggled to hold back a laugh. "Oh, just a lucky guess." Jordan quickly replied.

    While Jordan and Jason finished their pizza, Jes started wandering around their room, looking at the displayed pictures. One in particular caught her eye. It was a picture of a young Jordan and Jason posing, but that wasn't what shocked her. In this picture, they looked like... ordinary twins.

    Jes had been staring so long, it attracted the attention of Jordan and Jason. "Guess you found that old picture of us." Jordan commented.

    Jes quickly turned around, embarrassed. "Sorry I didn't mean to stare at it like that. It's just... I though you two always were...

    Jordan gave her a half hearted smile. "No... we weren't always this close." Turning to Jason, the two nodded in agreement. "Since you told us about your story, it's only fair we explain ours."

    Jason continued. "That picture was taken on our twelfth birthday. It was the last time we would ever be... apart. Our dad was a well known detective, catching many dark witches and wizards. One such wizard had escaped earlier that year, and he wanted revenge on our dad for 'ruining his life.'"

    "Anyways," Jordan took over, "he broke into the house that night, to kidnap us. Our dad appeared just in time to save us, but during the struggle, a few curses were used. The wizard ended up getting mangled into an unrecognizable form, and we... ended up like this."

    "Needless to say, it took us a long time getting used to it. But, many many scrapes, bumps, and bruises later we got pretty good at working together." Jason finished up with a smile, trying to lighten the mood.

    Jes closed her eyes for a moment, collecting her thoughts. "Guess the three of us have a lot more in common then we thought." Jes said with a smile. "We're weird even among the weird."

    After that, they all realized it was getting late so Jordan and Jason volunteered to walk Jes back to her dorm. At the porch, Jes grudgingly walked the few steps up to the door, but instead of opening it, she quickly ran back and gave a quick kiss to Jordan and Jason.
