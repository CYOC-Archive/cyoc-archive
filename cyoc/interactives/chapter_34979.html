    Jenny nervously glanced up and down the deserted street before darting into one of the overgrown hedges that surrounded the school.

    “There is no way that I’m walking into school like this!” she said through gritted teeth as she pulled the outfit out of her school bag and quickly dressed. She smoothed the clothes down and with a satisfied sigh climbed through the hedge and onto the school running track.

    Walking confidently towards the school Jenny heard the first bell sound and quickened her pace, reaching a side door that led into the main building, the halls were quiet as classes had already begun.
    Her heels clicked as she walked as quickly as she could towards her first class of the day, maths.

    Jenny hated maths, and she hated her maths teacher; Mrs Gibson. She was a stuck up old bat with no patience and no sense of humour.
    She reached the door, took deep breath and pushed. Jenny started her mentally rehearsed apology for being late but quickly found herself stopping mid-sentence when she saw the looks on her classmate’s faces.

    A few started laughing most seemed to have stopped breathing, there was a random wolf whistle and she noticed that her friends were just staring with their mouths wide open.

    Mrs Gibson’s mouth was wide open; her eyes were fixed on Jenny, not on her face but her breasts!

    “WHAT ON EARTH ARE YOU DOING HERE DRESSED?!!” she hissed, “In all my years as a teacher I never thought one of my pupils would have the nerve to...to...” she gestured towards Jenny’s body. “...parade themselves around in public in such a way!”.

    Jenny backed away, this had been the final test, and Kyle’s curse had affected everyone and everything. It had changed her and the world around her and now she was at school being treated differently because she was trying to be the same.

The door swung open enough for some of her classmates to get a look at Jenny, standing naked in the hallway.

    They didn’t seem to care. Jenny saw her friend Susan mouth “what the hell were you thinking?” across the room but Jenny didn’t bother to respond. Mrs Gibson dropped Jenny’s clothes into the garbage and turned back to the class.

    “Now now, settle down. Miss Smith’s dilemma will be resolved with the principal while we continue our work on calculus”
    She pointed in the direction of the principal’s office and slammed the door in Jenny’s face.
