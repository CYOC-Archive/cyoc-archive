    The Enchanted Apple Of Transformations

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover A month ago O Magic Mental

    The person that had had Chad eat a magical apple though a ruse stepped out. Chad was dismayed to find out that it was Phil, the scrawny student he loved to bully. He had camouflaged his voice thanks to a device that he held into his hand. He pocketed the device, as its use was over. The boy with round glasses smiled wickedly at Chad.
    "Now that you have eaten the apple, I'll tell you more about the apple you've just eaten. It is a magical apple as once a person has eaten of it, he or she is subjected to transformations, whether physical, mental or both. Mine is a tad bit more special, as I still control its effects though you have eaten it."
    "Where... Where did you get it?" stammered Chad, forced to admit that this was no dream or indeed, no horrible nightmare.
    "Oh, a magic shop that randomly pops up," said Phil. "The guy who is the owner was nice. Told me he had the solution to solve the problems I had with bullies. He told me to give this fruit to the meanest bully of them all. Which I have. Now I can transform you into whatever I wish, throwing you into another reality to boot."
    Phil grinned evilly and Chad gulped nervously. Now that he was getting duly punished, the jock could only regret all the times he had acted so callously, towards Phil and countless other students at school. What did that asshole have in mind? Certainly nothing good, and Chad could only wait in anguish.
    "And actually, I have a few good ideas," said Phil with an even wider smile.
