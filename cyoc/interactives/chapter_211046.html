    Usually, bodily pains during winter, such as headaches, were the result of blood vessels constricting due to the cold. In Animalia, however, these headaches were more metaphorical, and often resulted from the trends of new members coming to Animalia and its colonies. The most recent trend, which just happened to take place during December was a prime example of this.

    ---

    "Daddy Deerest", one of the few non-predators of the BARA House, just got done with his latest "session" with Betti, which was about helping him adapt to his awakening bisexuality when he met Miss Fabina. Because of the most recent "immigrants" to Animalia, Miss Fabina seemed to be in a near constant foul mood, though this time she looked more... tired... than angry.

    "What is it, ma'am?" asked "Daddy", not really afraid of predator Animalians, but still feeling a slight shiver every now and then.

    "We just got a new group of bimbos for conversions," said Miss Fabina, still not happy about it but having 'bowed to the inevitable', "We've got three more to join Trixie, Dixie, and Vixi."

    "Don't forget Stacy and Lacey." reminded "Daddy", "They were dancing at the Slaughterhouse as goat daemons during Halloween."

    "Right." Growled Miss Fabina in frustration, "Anyway, partly because of Trixie's shot with your wolf colleague, one of those... bimbos... wants to become a Wolf Animalian for a 'role reversal' shot."

    "... thanks for that." said "Daddy", now also partly annoyed.

    ---

    Of course, it wasn't just Lyre/Animalia that was dealing with the influx of Bimbos. Dino City was also facing a similar influx, though this was mainly because of the Dino City "branch" of the BARA House that was opening in Dino City. Understandably, almost everyone was irritated by the bimbos, but they weren't just going to turn them away. Apparently, on their way to Dino City, the bimbos were relentlessly attacked (though not physically) by all kinds of Texans, mainly on their lack of intelligence and their "good looks", which seemingly made their lack of intelligence even worse, especially since at least one of them was incredibly wealthy.

    Fortunately, Ursula was able to lessen her reputation as a "Snobby-saurus" by offering them aid while they waited for their conversion orders. However, they were still attacked in various ways, from their car getting keyed or sprayed with negative graffiti. However, for some, this was just expected of Texans.

To her, they were just nice people who needed some extra help remembering things. Even though Harold explained what a 'bimbo' was, Kirana was still confused about it.
