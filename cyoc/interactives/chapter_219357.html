    Ethan felt Charlene feel a little lighter in his arms and then remembered he hadn't yet turned off the transformation. As swiftly he could without harming Charlene he rushed to the computer to stop the transformation of the increasingly newborn sized Charlene in his arms--however once he got there, she vanished out of his arms entirely as he hit the stop button on the transformation, a pop up appearing briefly before disappearing, and Ethan suddenly heard humming coming from the master bedroom bathroom.

    Curious, Ethan approached the master bedroom bathroom and heard the water stop and a few moments later a heavily pregnant woman who resembled Charlene step out, dressed in a nightie and robe that were larger to accommodate for her increased size.

    "Ahh! Ethan! You scared me!" scolded the woman, whom Ethan's memories were now telling him was Miranda--who apparently was Charlene's mother, and judging by the new weight on his left hand's ring finger, was his wife now... making him Charlene's father--and father to the four kids away at school.

    "Sorry 'Randa, I just... have to use the can," said Ethan, shuffling into the bathroom and closing the door--allowing himself the privacy to panic. Shit, shit, shit! This was not how he'd intended for this to go at all!

    Meanwhile outside in the master bathroom, Miranda positioned her eight month pregnant belly and herself onto the bed. This would be the last kid--after suffering through the two sets of twins, she was glad to hear that this one was just going to be one baby. There were days that she really loved her children, but sometimes she missed the sexy carefree days of her early marriage to Ethan, when they didn't have to worry about locking the bedroom door to make sure they weren't walked in on by a kid, or wake up in the middle of the night because she had a craving. That's when Miranda noticed Ethan's laptop left open and a program running on it.

    "Hmm, this looks like one of those Sims knock-offs..." said Miranda as with some difficulty she reached over, grabbed the computer and began to think about playing with what looked like characters based on her and Ethan.
