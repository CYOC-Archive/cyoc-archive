    Known Abilities:

Despite this, it is used as a pointer with a small pad at contact rather than injecting or suctioning and air pressure does not limit syringe range. When a button on the pressure gauge is pressed by an intelligent being, that being experiences a time stop phenomenon and may act arbitrarily upon others until pressed again to resume time.

    During time stop, a high priority mental change is in effect to use Injection Time for the sake of sexual preferences and react accordingly. Rape and other sexual harassment of anyone in proximity is standard but varies inconsistently, typically toward embarrassment and presentation of a smaller selection of figures deemed annoying by the user's normal personality. If a recording device is available, a recording of the events is normally taken where the user is shown to wear a pink nurse's hat unless the head is already occupied or removal of headwear is otherwise relevant. The user tends to gloat unnecessarily with grandiose physical gestures and strange dialog, evocative of machine translation of Japanese erotica.

It is typically used to either render victims more attractive to the user, or more easily embarrassed when time resumes. Users are typically very imaginative about qualities accessible to Injection Time, though certain customizations may require 'degree of stereotype' and multiple adjustments. Variation is typically within humanly possible ranges by default. However, setting explicit parameters for maximum, minimum, and rates of change can allow for extreme supernatural qualities. Heavily increased 'momentary arousal once time resumes' is near-universally applied, prompting immediate orgasm on resuming time (and typically with similarly increased volumes of ejaculate). The subject verbally ordering victims during timestop with some degree of intent will cause them to comply. This ability is not commonly used except to save effort posing and engage in sucking activities normally precluded by stopped time, though getting someone to admit something offensive or nonsensical on camera is known to occur.

    Transformation priority is low. However, the suspension of time means that most other transformations are not enforcing except for instantaneous area-wide conditionals. This means that even a persistent transformative effect may be overridden and have to be reinstantiated to continue. Transformation outside the physical body of the target (e.g. the other side of relationships, history-affecting, documentation-affecting, being perceived as normal or sexualized by others) not normalized beforehand occasionally 'snaps back' when a contradictory transformation occurs. This is due to being linked to Injection Time's collective transformation of that individual.

    A post-timestop mind-affecting process converts all trauma from mass sexual harassment and drastic transformation into mild embarrassment or significant arousal, with the occasional apathy/normalcy or desire to join in. Physical interference undoes damage to clothing, sensitive electronics, dropped items, bodily collapse, and other easily-stained or broken things that may occur in the wake of spontaneous orgasm and leftover force. Whether an individual is embarrassed, aroused, apathetic, or varying combinations thereof occurs depending on the user's intent when resuming time. This secondary influence generally persists and spreads until individuals dress or experience another transformation. Leaving someone stripped and embarrassed or performing ahegao will often cause authority figures to molest them uncharacteristically. Occasionally, everyone involved will decide to get naked 'for fairness' until leaving the area or some occasion that requires specific clothing. Dissipation of influence causes the overall event and related reactions to be treated as unremarkable or sexy and desired to happen again. Countermeasures or disgust are non-considerations unless independent mental influence protection is in play. Recordings of Injection Time activity reposted for pornographic purposes are considered a nonissue by those involved, or elaborate use of computer-generated imagery such as deepfakes.

Barring explicit fetishization of time stopping, medical treatment, or transformation, it is typically used only when encountering someone they already deem exceptionally attractive or when a specific use for its abilities presents itself.

It is demarcated and stored in the hosting site's medical area, alongside a list of approved users with a known sexual fetish for altruism or accepting others' desires. Altruism-fetish users are able to reliably create transformations on request or restore baseline form in the event of transformations with limiting side effects. A list of conditional users with oddly specific but timestop-applicable interests is maintained in the event their abilities prove useful for entity retrieval or containment: long-distance walking, construction of containment facilities, brainwashing or de-transformation of civilians, etc.

    Other: Non-language symbolic branding is shared with a number of devices and abnormal reality-affecting applications. Similar thaumaturgical actuation is detected, implying they use the same framework. Other instances of Injection Time may still be uncontained due to their mental interference reducing detectability.
