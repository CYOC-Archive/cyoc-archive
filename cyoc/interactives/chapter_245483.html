    The Madame Illusia

    Monica Of The Jungle

    added by Elflover 2 days ago BM O Mythological Magic

    Everyone cheered and clapped. How amazing! The Madame Illusia had transformed this rather plain youngster into Tarzan and clearly sent him of into a new life. A wild life. A wild life to be lived in the jungle. No doubt Damon would get to live a full life, replete with awesome and strange adventures. But one person quickly made everyone know how much she was NOT happy. Monica stomped onto the stage, furious at the turn of events.
    "Hey, you!" she said angrily while pointing at the woman with magical powers. "Bring my boyfriend back!"
    "I am afraid I cannot, my dear," calmly answered the witch. "He did not wish soi. He is gone to a better life. One that truly makes him happy and complete."
    "You've sent him to a horrible life to live as a savage!" countered Monica.
    "He wished for it," countered the elderly sorceress.
    "He did not know what he was getting into!"
    "Surely you trust your boyfriend to know what he desires?" said the witch with a twinkle in her eyes and a smile.
    People in the crowd snickered, angering Monica more.
    "He does not realize what he's doing, so bring him back, you hag with saggy tits!"
    "Well, if you want to see him back again so much, you can join him!" yelled Madame Illusia, enraged by the insult of the young woman.
    She cast her spell, which sent Monica straight to the jungle too. Madame Illusia laughed out throatily.
    "You'll get punished there all right!" she said while explaining to the audience what awaited Monica.
