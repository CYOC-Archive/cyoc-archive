He even had the enormous diamond on his left ring finger. He too was relieved to see the changes stopped at his wrist. There was a funny intermediate area at his wrist where his hairy arms narrowed down into Alicia’s hairless manicured hands.

The nail polish was what Alicia had applied to those toes yesterday afternoon. He had his own feet back! That was such a relief. His interpretation of the note had been correct.

    “Hello. This is Margo. How can I help you?”

    “All we got was one small note”

    “They are the directions”

    “Okay we stuffed up. We didn’t take a sachet on the second night. We changed heights and now I’m small. How do we fix that?”

    “By completing the process. Consuming all the doll”

    “Surely there is a quicker way. Is there an antidote?”

    “So I’m stuck like this until all the doll is gone?”
