    Jinn gives you a non-committal chuckle, and her lack of response piques your curiosity.

    "Hey, Jinn, I was wondering," you ask, looking up innocently. "Are you...a woman?" You're not trying to be a smart aleck, you are sincerely curious. It's not like you've had a lot of experience around genies before.

    "My form is usually selected with my master in mind," Jinn explains easily. "I take this form because it appears to be most acceptable to you, Master. In truth, a Jinn is a spirit of no fixed form. We merely appear as that which will be most convenient to our purpose at the moment."

    "So...can you become something else?" you inquire further. "Or would I have to wish for that?"

    "Although I am bound to the lamp, I can transform at will to a certain degree," Jinn replies, and before you can react, her body and clothes swim before your eyes, and suddenly, you are sitting on the knee of an extremely handsome man with a goatee and a ponytail, wearing a vest and, well, genie pants. If not for the bright blue skin, you might doubt it to be the same person.

    "...Jinn?" you can't help but ask, your eyes as round as saucers.

    "Does this form meet your approval, Master?" a deep, mellow voice causes you to involuntarily blush slightly as Jinn grins a bit too knowingly. You can't help but feel utterly self-conscious at the fact that you are practically nude and sitting on a handsome man's knee, even if you are in the form of a child. You're still mentally an adult, so you slide down to stand on the floor, trying not to look too awkward.

    "Can you turn into animals, too?" you try to change the mood. "But I mean, one that looks real, not blue." Jinn smiles, and as you watch, the smile expands and his teeth lengthen. His body again contorts before your eyes, and suddenly, sitting on its haunches on your desk chair is a beautiful black panther. You look on in awe as he lithely steps down to the ground.

    "Wow..." you can't help but say in wonder, and finally, you can't resist. You reach over and hug Jinn again, this time running your small hands through his ebony fur. "So pretty..."

    "As you can see, Master, I am rather versatile," Jinn the panther says with a toothy grin.
