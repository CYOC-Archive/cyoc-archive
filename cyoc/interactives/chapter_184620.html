Daniels quickly fixed himself up in a mirror seeing a Caucasian man with short dark hair, sunken eyes from little sleep and a less than health figure and meet the empress in the dining room soon after. He saw a long table wooden with eight chairs surrounding it in a candle lighted room with Ekyss sitting at the head of the table eating a gourmet plate of food with a glass of white wine to the side, at the opposite head was another plate of food with an empty wine glass. Daniels quickly sat down and commented “Are your ministers not joining us as usual”. “No I wish to have a private conversation with you” she calmly replied which unnerved Daniels to no end “I’m here to apologize for my behavior yesterday although I appear reptilian I am very hot blooded and when I heard you had returned so soon I thought you had brought me my acceptance. So to hear that it been delayed again you can understand my frustration”. “That’s completely understandable if I achieved great things like you I would want to be recognized as well” he responded cautiously “you must know that you conquered multiple nations to build your empire they aren’t going to take that lightly”. “Yet almost ever other nation in your union has been founded on conquest even some most recent members so why not me” Ekyss countered “But that isn’t the issue of why we need to talk”. “Why do we need to talk” the lonely man asked. “You have been here for 3 months now and I have seen some odd behaviors that are different from other ambassadors I’ve had here. You don’t really stand up for yourself whenever your confronted you alway take their assault almost as if you want them to. When ever members of my harem are around I alway either see fear or desire in the eyes of people you I see jealousy. Tell me Representative Daniels why did you take this assignment from what I heard you were the only volunteer” Ekyss coyly sip her wine as she finished “also don’t lie I’ll know if your lying”. Daniels found himself cornered so he told her everything. All his life he felt attracted to strong dominant personalities and you couldn’t find more than in politics which led him to into the field but his attraction was usually reject for his characteristics that turned off other politicians, so when he heard about Ekyss he jumped at the chance to see her to find the one person that could eclipse all his other crushes only to find she had tastes that were beyond human. “Oh baby you sweet child” Ekyss sweetly replied rising from her chair then going on to hug Daniels head into her breasts “you know I would let you join my harem if you do two things for me reside with me forever and a little mission that needs to be done at the UN”. Daniels soon elated with joy at the offer turned to his soon be mistress “anything what do you need me to do”. Ekyss smiled as she took a small bag filled with small stones from her hip and gave it to him. “Just give these stones to anyone who opposes my entry into the UN and then resign so you may return”. “What are they” Daniels asked to which Ekyss replied “Curses”.

    Over the next couple of week Daniels did as he was told returning to New York where he handed the stones to all the opposers of the Dracon Empire’s admission where after a few days they would dismissed for various reasons, corruption, elections, a few even died. Once they were dealt with the topic of admission became a simple one as the UN agreed to allow the empire entry and gave them a permanent seat on the security council. At that moment Daniels placed in his resignation and returned back to Havana. When Ekyss saw Daniels she happily greet him with open arms for he had achieved his mistress’s wishes. He was then escorted to the empress’ bedchamber now repaired after the marathon sex event a month ago. As Daniels prepared himself to get some, Ekyss stopped him then alluringly replied “Before we get to that you must choose your new form”. “What why I am okay with who I am” answered Daniels. “What you must understand my darling is that everyone in my harem lives in their ideal form, everyone has one, just tell me what it is and it shall be your” Ekyss lovingly explained then leaned in close to hear what form Daniels desired.
