    As you reflect about what to do next, whether to play more about or maybe find something to eat. Maybe if you climb up the vines you will find fruits or nuts. As you dart towards said vines, you see a bonobo couple nuzzling and mimicking having sex next to some bushes. They're both males and somehow... Somehow they look familiar... And... Wasn’t there a couple of male humans looking around the exhibit a little earlier and strolling around? They were naked but these bonobo chimps make you think of them intently. You frown, conflicted by these thoughts. Why are you so sure? And why are you thinking about humans to start with? They're weirds bipeds who give you treats if you're lucky. Their strangeness don't concern you unless there'd food involved. You have enough things to do in your monkey life to bother about these things, mainly napping, eating, playing, having fun and mating at some point. And looking at other apes and monkeys. That is fun and some are even playing together, regardless of species. Ah! If only humans were so smart! Then they would not have so many problems with wars and a myriad other stupid, violent or annoying things. However, your thoughts about wars and the human condition, last remnants of your human mind, are blown away as the bonobo chimps turn to you. Without realising it, you had been staring absent-mindedly at them. One cooed in curiosity and clapped his hands gently at you, uttering little grunts of affection. You approached tentatively. He didn't look dangerous and rather friendly. Then, as you approached, the bonobo chimp grabbed you and bounced you playfully on his knees. You were so stunned that you remained speechless. The other bonobo chimp, larger, looked on with amusement and fondness, letting his mate play and indulge in getting a baby. The bonobo chimp nuzzled and kissed you and as you protested and tried to get away from his grasp, he tutted in disapproval, exactly like chiding an unruly toddler. He didn't care that you did not want to be treated as a baby and to get you in a better mood he started to tickle you on the belly, ribs and the armpits. You absolutely chortled helplessly and could not resist the tickling treatment, stopping your gestures to escape the bonobo's grasp short. You could only giggle and shake, under the full control of your tormentor with motherly instincts. He and his parter really seemed to enjoy it, in fact. By the time you unwanted adoptive mother stopped, you were a giggling and sobbing mess, so breathless that you had no energy left in you. The bonobo chimp chuckled, knowing he had won and easily so. He knew you had become a ragdoll in his hands and he cradled you to his chest, rocking you. He them held your head so you could pretend suckling from him, to his utter delight. You obeyed, not wishing another assault of tickling. No milk came out, naturally, but for the bonobo chimps, all huddled together, it made a perfect picture of the domestic bliss they had wished for as humans. The other transformed humans found the sight just mildly interesting but for the workers of the island, it was just plain adorable, perfect for your time in the permanent exhibit, where you'd all be placed soon. They'd just have to adjust your own transformation just a bit more, that was all.
