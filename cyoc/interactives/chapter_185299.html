While Rachel was sure her mother would try to put things off as far as she could, she would train her eventually.     Totally busted. Although Rachel was glad it hadn't happened with her computer . Would her teacher accept superpower-related accidents as valid excuses as to why she couldn't turn in her assignments? As much as it hurt to admit, mom had probably made the right choice leaving her behind. She was wise, like her alter-ego's namesake, even if it sucked to admit it. While she waited, Rachel could at least figure out her superhero name. Mini-Athena was out. As was Lil'thena. The teen wracked her brain as she floated out of the basement. "Ouch!"
