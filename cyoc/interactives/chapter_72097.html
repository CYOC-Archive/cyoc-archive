Now this is a very difficult choice for him. He wants to live in an anime world but he doesn’t know how long he wants it to be. Leaving it forever could be a good option, but he won’t know if he would like it or not. So just to be on the safe side, he decided to let this last for a day. That way if he doesn’t like it, the day will end and everything would be back to normal.

He moved the mouse pointer to the ‘Start’ icon and clicked on it. The moment he did that, a window appeared and said, ‘Thanks for your decision. The transformation will start immediately. Enjoy your new world.’ Below it is a time counter that says five minutes and it was counting down. Jeff felt excited about it as he waited for the changes to begin. His shirt expanded and the color faded to while with a hard cloth texture. It then started to open up in the middle and wrapped around his body, making it look like a rope of some kind. His jeans puffed up as well and looked baggy, changing color and texture to the same as his shirt. A black belt appeared around him and tied itself up tight, fitting his new clothes. His shoes and socks disappeared, leaving him completely barefooted, which made Jeff feel a little uncomfortable but let it slide. Then other things appeared on his attire as well. Patches appeared on his shoulders and across his chest his Japanese markings and a karate logo. Jeff looked at all of it and realized what the clothes really were. He was wearing a karate uniform that looked really professional.

    Looking on, he remembered something. Last year, he enrolled into a karate class just to learn to defend himself. Now with the changes happening around him, it made him into a real third degree black belt, something Japanese karate masters have. It’s making his dream come true. But all this was only the beginning. The real changes are about to begin.

    Looking into the mirror, he noticed that something strange happened. Looking into the mirror, he saw his face was looking more animated. His eyes started to look really big and very wide. His nose started to shrink and looked like it was almost disappeared. But oddly enough, he was still breathing. His lips were completely vanished, leaving nothing but a mouth. He looked at the rest of his body and noticed that it was animated too. Looking more cartoonish then real life actually. When the changes are done, he looked exactly like an anime teenager.

    Looking back, he also saw that his room changed. He still has the desk, computer, and a TV with him, though it looked a little differently, but everything else has changed. His bed was replaced with a mattress like bed on the floor with a blanket on it, something Japanese people used to sleep. The room itself looked like a little dojo complete with a mat like floor, a punching back on the side, a few weights to lift his arms, and a wooden pole with sticks around it to do quick punches and blocks. With the timer finished counting down, Jeff knew that the changes were all done.
