    Linda turned around and blinked at what she saw. It was her sister, Caroline, but as she'd been almost ten years ago. Caroline was older than Linda by about 6 years. Now, Linda found herself staring at a little girl with wild red hair and freckled cheeks and sky blue eyes. She wore a simple winter coat, with a striped scarf and dark jeans, her feet tucked into bright red wellington boots.

    Caroline giggled and tiptoed up to her stall, "Such a pretty unicorn Linda! Im so happy to have you!"

    Linda tried to speak but all she could do was snort and whinnie. The little girl stepped back, "Woah, easy girl. Its me! Its Caroline. I raised you from a foal, remember?"

    Linda shook her head and felt her ears droop. Her sister didnt remember her. She felt her heart drop and her whole body sagged. Caroline frowned and came up, embracing her head. "Hey...hey, easy, its ok...goodness, something must have happened. Don't worry, I'm here with you."

    Something about the girl's touch soothed Linda. She felt at ease again...but she wondered what would happen next.
