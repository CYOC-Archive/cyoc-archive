    City of Screams

    The werebeast was picking itself up from the ground. Dave staggered backwards in fear as the anthro monster climbed up on its two feet and howled at the two of them. Before it could do anything else though, a loud bang filled the room alongside a bright light.

    The front of the werewolf seemed to rip off, skin and muscle exploded outwards, leaving behind a bloody mess on the floor.

    Dave cried out, looking down at his deceased girlfriend. The pool of blood expanding out. Jake gestures to Dave "Don't get near her, we still don't know this spreads"

    "Y-you killed her" Dave whimpered, falling to his knees. With his hands on his face.

    "She was gone, as far as I can tell...The longer they are transformed the more their minds decay. It was only a matter of time before she forget you and saw us as food".

    "So now what...." Dave tensed, looking up at Jake who was now reloading his double barrel.

    "We return to the lab where this all started, maybe try to find a cure" Jakes answer was less than comforting.

    "Come on lets go" Jake said, opening the lab door.

    "Goodbye Jean...." Dave sighed, picking himself up and following Jake.

    They didn't make it far. As soon as they left the building a loud screaming could be heard in the distance. In fact it seemed like the whole city was screaming. Alongisde that, they could hear howling as well as other strange creature noises in the night.

    "It's already broken out....dammit" Jake said, quickly running towards his car. Dave followed him but before either of them made it. A behemoth crashed onto the vehicle, crushing it. A desperate beep resounded from the vehicle before silence.

    In front of Jake and Dave was a enormous ape, going at 10 feet tall. It's muscles rippled across its incredible body. It also seemed more beastlike, with long claws protruding out of its hands and a demonic face with fangs. It growled at them angrily.

    "Sh-shit" Jake shouted, gesturing towards other creatures which were now running towards them. To Dave's surprise they weren't just werewolves, but werejaguar's, werelions and wereboar's too. Dave instinctively ran past the wrecked car and into a nearby alleyway. He could hear panting behind him, it sounded like Jake was following him.

    They reached a tall iron face, which was blocking the end of the alleyway.

    "Fuck!" Jake exclaimed. Looking behind them. The creatures were closing in on them, moaning desperately.

    "Join us...." they deeply said. Slowly advancing towards Jake and Dave. With not many options left, Jake and Dave have to make a quick decision.
