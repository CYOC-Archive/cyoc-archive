    Mr. Johnson's Class

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago AR

    Mr. Johnson was a strict and demanding teacher at a boys boarding school. He was a man in his 30s with sharp features, a pointed nose, and a stern look that seemed to always be etched onto his face. He was known for his tough grading policies and strict expectations for his students.

    One day, as the students in his class sat quietly at their desks, Mr. Johnson began to read from a mysterious book he had brought with him. The students soon found the reading to be dull and boring, and their minds started to wander.

    However, little did they know, the book was actually a spellbound, and as Mr. Johnson read from it, they all started to shrink and become younger. The students were shocked and angry, but they were helpless to stop the teacher.

    "What's happening to us?" one student cried out in terror.

    "Why is this happening?" another asked, panicking.

    As they became younger, their school clothes became too large and began to fall off their bodies. They clutched at their clothing in desperation, but their attempts to keep them on only made them shrink even more. They quickly abandoned their clothing, including their pants, shirts, underwear, watches, and phones, which littered the floor.

    As they shrunk, they started to resemble children again. Their faces became rounder and smoother, and their limbs became slender. They were now naked and exposed, with nothing to cover their shrinking bodies.

    The students quickly realized that the only way to stop the spell was to steal the book from Mr. Johnson. They banded together and overpowered him, snatching the book from his hands and making him read the spell that would make him younger.

    As the spell took hold, Mr. Johnson shrank before their very eyes. His features became softer, his body became smaller, and he soon found himself shrinking down to their size. He was now the youngest and smallest of all of them, completely vulnerable and at their mercy.

    "You did this to us!" one student shouted, pointing at Mr. Johnson. "Why would you do this?"

    "I wanted to punish you for not doing well in my class," Mr. Johnson admitted, his voice now higher pitched and smaller.

    The students stood triumphantly over their once-strict teacher, now naked and exposed like they were. They had taken control and claimed their power, leaving Mr. Johnson to face the consequences of his actions.

    At the end of the story, Mr. Johnson was now a younger version of himself, with rounded cheeks and dimpled chin. He had lost all his sharpness and severity, leaving behind a small and vulnerable boy who could only look up at the students in fear and regret.
