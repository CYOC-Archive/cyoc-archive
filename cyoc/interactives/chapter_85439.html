    Katherine and William are an elderly couple of seventy and seventy-one and they are going to celebrate their first year of retirement. After years of work, married life, kids, and grandkids, they decided to unwind and relax for the rest of their lives. The only problem is, where to go to begin their retirement. That’s when Will did some research and discovered a resort that could change their lives. Said that this resort has transformation abilities that could alter your age, turn you into an animal, and switch your gender. And the best part is, people who retire live better and happier lives. Feeling this is their ticket, he told his wife about this resort and she felt a happy they found someplace special. So they started to book the trip and choose the right wing for both of them.

    On the day of the trip, the two took a flight to the resort and were already at the entrance with the cab. Once they were at the resort. Katherine looks to the resort and was amazed by what she was seeing. The entrance to the hotel was extravagant. Golden pillars stood proudly with brownish roofs and golden metal arches on top of the pillars showed pictures of people turning into animals, genders, and ages in sequential form. One arch showed a woman regressing into a baby girl. Another was a man being turned into a horse. The third one was a woman turning into a man. The next one was a man aging to an elder. The list goes on and on and on. But in the middle of the entrance was a huge fountain that honors Circe, the goddess of transformation. “Wow… Look at this place!” she said in awe, “It’s beautiful.” Will got out of the cab with his luggage and responded back, “Of course it is sweetie. The website claim that first impressions are lovely. They certainly show it the moment you reach this place. Let’s go in and get our rooms.” Soon enough, they entered the lobby and were amazed by the inside then that of the outside. The lobby was all Mahogany wood and there was a crystal chandelier hanging in the middle. The lighting was soft and the whole atmosphere was warm and inviting.

    The two walked to the reception area as a female hotel clerk saw them and greeted them. “Hello sir and madam,” she said with a happy smile, “Welcome to the Transformation Resort. How can I help you?” Will approached the clerk and said, “Hi there. My name is William Jefferson and this is my wife Katherine. We’re celebrating our retirement and heard that this place will give us a real good time.” The clerk smiled while looking at the reservation list and said, “Oh of course, people who begin retirement always come here to have a fantasy experience. And the best part is that whatever they change to, would want to keep their changes to enjoy their retirement better then before. It’s more of a life changing experience. Now let’s see… Ah yes, you’re names are on the database. You’re rooms are already prepared for you.” Will blinked a little bit and thought that was a little unexpected. “Whoa! Hold up!” he said in surprise, “Did you just say we have rooms? I thought I order a single room.” The clerk smirked and said, “Oh no, this resort is different then the others. You see, our guests have three same rooms in three separate wings. All three wings are based on three different transformations. The Age Wing is where you can change your age, whether it is turning into a child or an old person. The Animal Wing is where you can turn yourself into any animal or inanimate object you choose. And in the Transgender Wing is where you can switch your gender whether you can to be male, female, or a shemale. All three wings have a variety of shops, restaurants, pools, and other entertainment that matches each wing’s ability. If you plan to switch rooms or visit the other wings, you are welcome to do that.” After hearing that, Katherine rubbed her chin and finds that to be a bit confusing. “Wow…” she responded, that’s a lot of info to sink in.” The clinic looked to her while getting the three keys for the rooms. “Well that’s what happens when we have first time guests. But give it a few days and you will get use to it. Here are your keys. Enjoy your stay at the Transformation Resort.”

    Will just took the keys and he and his wife headed to the main hub where the trolleys can take guests to their wings.
