As Marc was around 5.11 feet tall, Andre barelly touched 4 feet in size. But they were very close friends and it didn't bothered them to be that different in size. Andre sometimes complained to be so short, but Marc mostly managed to cheer him up again.

They went to swimming in a pond, played some baseball, fooled around at the playground and had a wonderfull day, so they thought it would be great to spent it outside and pretend to camp far far away in the wilderness.
