    Growing and Shrinking Mitch, Zack's Little Brother

    As Ty and Zack were talking, another boy walking through the doors of the large gym and came straight up to
    Zack. Of course, now that Zack was big and muscular, he didn't think anything of it, thanks to Ty's Powers.

    "Hey, bro! Are we ready to go?" the boy asked Zack. Ty looked on and learned that the youngster was Mitch,
    Zack's younger brother. Mitch was about 14 years old. He was an inch shorter than Zack, but very skinny.

    Ty scanned Mitch's mind and learned that Mitch was a little jealous of Zack, especially now that Zack was so
    big and muscular. So, he decided to even things out a little bit. The thing was, he wasn't quite sure how big
    to make him, since Zack was just an inch taller than his little brother. He needed to find a way to carefully
    make Mitch bulk up without making him too tall. So, he decided to do it in a different order: make him taller,
    bulk him up, and then shrink him down while making his muscles grow as his body compressed due to the
    shrinkage.

    Ty put his magic to use and Mitch suddenly felt a tingling sensation all over him. Then, he shot up, and began
    to grow taller. The boy grew from 5 foot 2 inches taller to over 6 foot 5 inches tall, all in a matter of
    seconds! Mitch couldn't believe it! He was the tallest fourteen-year-old boy in school!

    Next, Ty made Mitch bulk up just a little bit. He made all of his muscles inflate just a little bit,
    especially his arms and legs along with his pectorals. Mitch's biceps, triceps, abdominals and also his calves
    and quads inflated all at once, giving him a sudden buffed look.

    Finally, Ty decided that he didn't want Mitch to be taller than Zack since Zack was the older brother, so he
    decided to make Mitch shrink back everywhere except for his muscles. As Mitch's body started to dwindle, his
    muscles grew bigger while his legs, torso and arms continued to get shorter and shorter until Mitch was
    exactly an inch shorter than Zack but a bit more muscular!

    "Wow! Look at ME!" Mitch gasped.

    "Looking good, bro!" Zack grinned, as he rested his huge arm on his brother's shoulder.

    Ty just looked on with a smile on his face as the two muscle brothers walked off, towards the exit of the gym.

    Ty turned around, feeling very pleased with his work, hoping to find some more guys to grow...
