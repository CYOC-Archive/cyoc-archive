    "Hmm?" Tiffany knew that those threads meant that the creature was doing some sort of magic.

    Mitch was there, suspended five feet in the air, a long vine wrapped his mouth. His eyes were bugging out, practically oozing with fear.

    Tiffany began to flatter her "I'm so glad that you come back. I think you are an amazing creature! You're magic! I did a lot of thinking after you left me, and realized how foolish I had been."

    "See, originally, you were supposed to be transformed entirely into a donkey on the island. You would be sold off in the mortal realm to pay off expenses and damages to the island. Then, a new batch of folks are hunted and taken in, the are transformed and we repeat."

We need at least 10 years old kids for it" she said to Mitch, "Had I left you in the island, you would have still walked on two legs, your snout would have barely developed... you wouldn't even have foreleg hooves. Maybe your air would grow and your fingernails would darken, but that's it."

    She reeled back a bit from the thought, floating backwards, "Ugh. Hideous."

    "You mean he wouldn't become a cute little donkey like the ones we saw on the island?" Tiffany asked, surprising herself.

    "No." Adomm replied, "He would have become a humanoid donkey. Not nearly as desirable, I imagine."

    "MMM?" Mitch said in shock. He didn't want to believe it.

    "I have some work to do." Adomm sighed, "... but not before I finish with you two..."

    Tiffany turned back to her eyes wide. "Finish with US??? You mean...me too?" She asked trying to hide the hope in her voice.

    Adomm turned to Tiffany. "I can tell by your behavoir that you regret our past deal. On this new deal that I propose you, you will have whatever you want... and nothing in return."

    She looked up at Mitch, and then Tiffany. "I will grant you each one a last request." she said, "One form change, partial or complete."

    "Ah, you have thought about it." Adomm smiled, "Well, that keeps things simple."

    She turned to Mitch, "And how about you?"

    "MMMM MMMMPH MMM!" Mitch said, struggling.

    Tiffany rubbed his furry ankles with a giggle, and ran her fingers over his smooth hooves. "He's excited!"

    "Indeed." said Adomm. The vine gag did not recede.

    "Mmm? MMM MMMMMM MMMM MMMM!" Mitch struggled, unable to get a coherant word out.

    "I've been trying to explain to him how his donkey parts were a gift, how they made him unique..he just wanted to get back home...kind of like I did before i met you." said Tiffany.

    "Yes, I should probably mention that I do not CARE." Adomm said, cutting her off, "Your request now, please, Mitch."

    "Oh he surely wants to be a cute little jackass with me! I'm sure of it!"

    "MMMMPH?!?!" Mitch hummed in horror. If he was not scared of Adomm before, he was absolutely petrified now.

    "MMMM! MMMMM!" he protested.

    "Oh, you are not allowed to speak." Adomm pointed a finger at Mitch, "Accept it as punishment for not listening the first, second, and THIRD time..."

    Adomm's eyes narrowed as she looked at Tiffany, her white light pulsing brighter.

    "But not worry." she smiled, "I'm sure your beloved friend knows exactly what you want."

    She floated closer to Tiffany, and the air grew colder.

    "MMMMMMMMMMMPH!"
