Now here she was with three children, having to work full time from home, the stress that came with it, and a surprisingly generous alimony from a man trying to repent his transgressions.

A trench coat not unlike the kind children would use to pretend to be a grown up, a dressing robe that looked like really soft taffeta, and a long parka that looked like a dress. She was supposed to wear the trench coat first, so she did just that. It didn’t feel uncomfortable, but it was a tad stiff in the sleeves.

    “Uh, anyone want to tell me why I’m a bear?” the bear Coralline asked her clones.
