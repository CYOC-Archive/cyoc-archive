While the teacher is explaining some mathematical problems on the blackboard, “Zoey” looks around the classroom, ‘I need help to get my device back,’ she thinks to herself, ‘but who can I trust? I can’t approach my friends, they’ll think I’m laying, besides, if Kat would ever figure out the truth, I’d never hear the end of it,’ she thinks to herself.
