    Visiting His Girlfriend’s Mother

    “You sure you want to skip on your brother’s birthday just to have dinner with my mom?” Janette asked,

    “Absolutely,” Pete said, no hesitation,

    “Alright, whatever you say, babe,” Janette said, switching off the bedroom light, “goodnight,” she finished, crawling into bed and laying on her side. Pete was more than excited for the next day. Yeah sure, it was his brother’s birthday, but meeting with Janette’s mom was going to be the best thing he ever did.

    ———

    Pete, sat his in car beside his girlfriend, was driving to her mother’s home. He had met Janette’s mother before, that’s why he was so excited. He had so many ideas, and had been meaning to mess with Janette for a while now.

    Almost two years ago, Janette had slept with Pete’s former best friend, Nick. Pete and Nick don’t talk anymore (obviously), but Pete managed to forgive Janette. But she never really faced a punishment, and with this rock he had bought. It seemed like just the right time.

    Pete reached into his pocket and grabbed the rock, focussing on the road as he did. He lifted it up to his mouth and did as the old guy instructed, he bit down on it.
    Like magic, the rock turned to dust, then suddenly flew directly into his mouth. He expected to start choking, but he didn’t. He felt exactly the same, but he knew something was different.
    24 hours, he can change anything about anyone. So of course, he wanted to change Janette.
