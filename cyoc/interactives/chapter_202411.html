    After Jay had used his merge ring to absorb Brandon’s body inside of his own, he just walked around and tried to take the time to adjust to his new, bigger body. Brandon’s larger frame, height and bulging muscles were all added to Jay’s small stature, resulting in Jay ending up with a much bigger, taller and imposing physique. The coolest part of the body merge was, that everyone in the gym thought that the disappearance of the hulking Brandon was completely normal, and nobody said anything about Jay suddenly growing bigger and taller that day, not even his father.

    “Merging with other people is totally cool, but I don’t want to end up becoming too big!” Jay smiled, as he examined his new physique before the large mirror in the gym’s male locker room. He stood there and posed while he flexed, before he strolled back out into the large weight room.

    As Jay wandered around the large gym, he spotted a lonely young lady sitting on one of the lat-pulldown machines located in the far back corner of the weight room. She was practically crying to herself, quietly and all alone.

    “Excuse me, miss? Is everything okay?” he said quietly as he squatted down next to where she was sitting. He had to get used to his new height, which made him much taller than most of the people at the gym, so he had to bend over or squat down when talking to people who were seated at the machines.

    “Oh, I’m sorry… I just got some really bad news the other day and it’s starting to hit me now.” The young lady said to him. “You kind of reminded me of a young guy I knew on the gymnastics team who had recently passed away.”

    “Oh no! I’m so sorry to hear that!” Jay replied.

    “I’m a gymnast. We trained together until a week ago when he died in a car accident. This is my first time back in the gym and seeing you walking around just reminded me so much of him. I can’t believe he’s gone. You look so much like him.” She said as she grabbed a tissue out of her gym bag and wiped away her tears.

    “I’m so sorry!” was the only thing Jay could say to her. He felt terrible.

    “I feel so lonely right now. I wish I could be with a guy like you all the time, you know? A big muscle man. I don’t know if I can continue this on my own!” the girl sobbed.

    “Everything will be alright, I promise!” Jay said softly as he reached out and gently hugged the girl. “Here, give me a hug.”

    She reached over and hugged him with her muscular arm. She was short but she had a toned, muscular figure as well, just like a gymnast would. Her thighs were big and wide, and her arms and shoulders were strong. As soon as her body touched his, the ring activated, and her body became absorbed within Jay’s body.

    The cute, female gymnast found her body pulled towards Jay. When her skin touched his, her flesh merged with his. She got rapidly pulled into Jay’s bigger, bulkier frame, leaving her gym clothes behind on the lat-pulldown machine. Jay quickly collected the clothes and walked back to the locker room, where he saw his reflection in the mirror.

    Jay noticed that his hair had short and smooth. He already had a handsome face, but now he also had a square jawline and bigger, nicer muscles.

    “Poor girl. I didn’t even get to know her name!” he sighed, as he placed the girl’s belongings in his gym bag. He zipped up his bag before he walked out, where his dad was waiting for him.

    He had used the ring to merge with two people while at the gym. This was enough for now. He was big enough. Would he ever use the ring again?
