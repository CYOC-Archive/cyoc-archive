    Gary was walking down the hall on Monday, thinking about if he wanted to enact any more changes. Not paying much attention to who was around him, Gary was dragged from his musings by the sound of cruel laughter. Looking down the hall, Gary spied Devin picking up his things as Neil walked away laughing. Seeing this, Gary knew at least two people he would be changing.

    Walking into the locker room, Gary changed into some exercise clothes and headed into the weight room. Coach Eli was still acting like a team captain most of the time these days and was lifting weight with the rest of the team. That day, he was working with Hank, one of the defensive linemen that Gary had yet to transform.

    Hank was large like most defensive linemen, but he had a problem with extra weight he did not need to carry. Hank might have been just over three hundred pounds, the problem was he needed to lose the fat, but did not want to for fear of falling under the that mark. Finding his third subject, Gary started his own routine, out lifting the entire team.

    It was Thursday and Gary had yet to select his final subject for transformation. Any of his teammates would be excellent choices, but Gary just could not decide. Finally deciding to close at random, Gary’s eyes fell upon George.

    George was the center for the offensive line. Before the changes, he and Gary had been close in size. Now George was over a hundred pounds smaller. Liking the idea that the guys on the offensive line being close to the same size as the defensive line, Gary thought that George would be the best candidate to start on.

    Going home the evening, Gary got onto his computer and pulled up the Chronivac. Pulling up George’s profile first, Gary found that he was 284 pounds at 6 foot 4 inches with 15 percent body fat. Impressed, Gary decided to move the body fat down to 8 percent and add four pounds of muscle for the next two weeks. Adding an inch of height, Gary closed George’s profile and switched to Hank’s profile.

    Hank was sitting at 302 pounds at a height of 6 feet and 2 inches, but his body fat was sitting at 30 percent. Gary knew that he would have to lower Hank body fat while allowing him to increase his weight and add some additional muscle. Moving the body fat down to 10 percent, Gary searched the program until he was able to a way that Hank would end up gaining 2 pounds of additional muscle a day. This would leave him a 330 pounds. Smiling at the settings he had implemented, Gary closed Hank’s profile.

    Finally coming to the two most important changes for the next two weeks, Gary opened the profiles of Devin and Neil. Gary could see that Devin was your typical nerd. While he didn’t have much body fat, his weight was laughable. Standing at 5 feet and 4 inches, Devin weighed in at 112 pound. Neil on the other hand was well built for his age. Weighing 200 pounds and standing at 6 feet and 1 inch, Neil also impressively only had 7 percent body fat.

    Working the Chronivac, Gary found a way for Devin to gain 7 pounds of muscle a day while at the same time, Neil would gain 5 pounds of fat. Devin would be perfect for one of the sports team while Neil would be throw the football team. Finally Gary changed Devin’s height and up it to 6 feet and 2 inches. Gary could only think that with his improved body, Devin might want to join the football team after all.

    Sitting and congratulating himself, Gary could not help but pull up Coach Eli’s profile. Standing at 5 feet 10 inches and weighing 292 pounds, Gary knew that if he kept giving the coach more muscle with out some more height, Coach Eli would soon be unable to move properly. Thinking it over, Gary decided that he would give Eli an additional pound of muscle daily for the next two weeks, but he would also have the coach grow three inches, leaving him at 6 feet 1 inch. Finally, Gary was unable to resist and reduce the coach’s age an additional year. Gary knew how much Eli was enjoying his returned youth, but he wondered what he would think being a guy of 25 years.

    Hitting the submit button, Gary got ready for Lucy to come over. She wanted to tell him something important. He was glad for the opportunity because Gary wanted to tell her the he had gotten an offer to play for a professional team after he finished high school. Gary would be spending summer at a professional football team’s training camp.
