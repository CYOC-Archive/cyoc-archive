    Pleasure Island

    Luck Of The Lottery Cards

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 6 months ago O Mythological Magic

    Pleasure Island has a lot of things to try. So many colors, smells, stands, places and sights... It's so hard to choose! Should you try dice, roulette, blackjack, cards, or something other? You decide to have some beer and a few chocolate bar at a stand and stroll around so as to make your choice calmly. After all, no rush, right? You're on vacation here, so you have all the time in the world. And they didn't even check your age when you grabbed a beer. Sweet! Not only things are free here, but it's also not restricted at all. Cool! You have to be eighteen to enter Pleasure Island but once you're in, it's total freedom. You're twenty so it's fun not to show ID and potentially get drunk while you're underage. So you drink a bit of beer and eat chocolate while you look around, taking your time to choose. It's fun to sip on alcohol, but you don't want to get wasted so soon. Not like some groups of dudes or even girls doing so with mojitos or cocktails at different places outside that are loud and very visible. As you ponder on what to do first, you chance to see a little establishment. The front has bright colors like the other attractions but it says "Lottery Cards! Find Thew Right Beastie For You!". Intrigued, you go there and a youngish man, fairy tall and well-combed, smiles at your entrance. The place is empty and has numerous stands. Like for postcards but with lottery cards instead.
    "Hello, young man," the employee says jovially."Care to try our lottery cards? If you win, you get free cocktails and sweets, the rare types you'd have to pay for, unlike all the others on the island. You can take and try five cards per hour. Want to try? We have different themes. Lots. If you get three of the same animals in a row, you win!"
    You decide to try. Sounds like a bargain indeed. So you pick one "mythology" card, one "farm animal" card, one "wild animal" card, one "jungle animal" card and just for fun, you pick a "princess" card. You then start scratching, impatient to see the results.
