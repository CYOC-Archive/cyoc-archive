    "I've been waiting for you, Strasserman. Big Jon sent me, he says it's fucking judgement day for you" Rob's voice was the typical growl that Bill was more than familiar with. But before Bill could raise his fists, Rob broke out into a rumbling laugh that BIll felt just as much as he heard. On the table and in the sink, Willow, Tammy and Gloria all clutched at their ears in pain as the giant above them guffawed, ignorant of their pain.

    "Easy, Shitterman!" Rob said as he reined his laughter in, a handful of final chuckles fading away like thunder in the distance, "I'm not going to do anything! I'm here to help us solve a...mutual problem"

    "Or I can sit here and play with my new toys. Either way, I'm not going anywhere" Rob sounded bored but the look in his eyes was anything but. He made to attack the girls again, spurring Bill into action.
