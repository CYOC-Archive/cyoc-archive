    Going to the dogs

    Seeing his foster brother/dog eating out of the bowl gave Ty an idea.

    "On the Animation Station there was a show about a group of kids that had a talking dog." Ty thinks to himself. "What if I made it so that everyone would think that a talking dog was normal and they could understand what he was saying? But what if he tells everyone about my gift? I could wipe his memory but I want him to know that he is still a dog."

    "Dustin? I have a peace offering to let you know that I'm not totally without feelings." Ty said looking up from his bowl of corn flakes. "I will meet you half way with your request to be human again."

    Dustin raises his head from his bowl and looks soulfully into Ty's eyes. "Halfway?"

    "Yes, you will still be a dog in appearance but you will think and act almost human. But your dominate behavior will be canine." TY said with a evil grin. He let Dustin think about he just said before he continued talking. "With an exception, I can see in your mind that you are thinking about telling people who and what you really are. Anytime that you try to tell anyone you will lose your human intelligence and become a dog again, understand."

    Dustin hung his head in shame. "SHIT!"

    "Good boy, now here comes our father." TY tells Dustin.

    Dustin didn't need Ty to tell that his father was coming. He was still getting use to his canine senses but he knew who was coming before they stepped into the kitchen. "Good morning Sir."

    "Good morning Dusty." His once human father returned the greeting andpatting him on the head. "Has Ty taking you out for your run yet?"

    "No sir he has not because BARK, BARK, BARK, BARK!"

    "Now Dusty you know that I can't understand you when you talk that fast." His human father bendt down and starts to rub Dustin behind his ears. "Now slower, what are you trying to say?"

    Dustin looks over towards Ty still sitting at the table. "… Because he hasn't finished his breakfast yet, sir."

    "Very good, now I need to talk to Ty.” Ty's foster father stood up and approached the table where Ty was sitting. In a soft whisper he starts to talk to him. "I don't want Dusty to hear this but I set up an appointment at the vets to have him neutered. I don't what him getting that bitch next door pregnant. Our neighbor Tom told me that she is in heat again and he doesn't want anymore pups."

    Ty grin went even bigger when he heard the news. Of course he would not let it happen but why spoil all his fun.

    Dustin could not hear everything his human father and his foster brother saying. Even with his canine hearing he could only make out a few words like vet and neutered. Ether one struck fear in his canine mind.

    Ty's foster father left for work a few minutes later after which Ty was able to further torment his foster brother. "It seams like we have a busy day in store for us, but first let's get the walk out of the way."

    Ty got the leash and attached it to Dustin's collar. Dustin was reluctant to go in fear what was in store for him. When they got outside Dustin's senses were inundated with thousands of new scents. Out of all the thousands of scents there was one that stood out. It was a fragrance that stirred something deep inside him and he had to find it.

    With Ty in tow Dustin ran towards the sent. Him animal instincts took over driving him mad with desire. Even though he could talk like a human he could only bark wildly at what was behind a fence that blocked his path.

    "Oh I know what you want and you want it badly don't you." Ty struggled to hold onto Dustin's leash. "Good thing our neighbor isn't home now or you would be on your way to the vets. But since he's not and nobody else is around to tell I'll let you have your fun."

    Ty unlatches the wooden stockade fence and lets Dustin off his leash. As soon as the gate was open Dustin was off to find his bitch. From behind fence came the sounds of two dogs getting it on. Ty just sat in front of the closed gate waiting for Dustin to finish.

    After about a half an hour there was a whimper at the gate. Ty opened the gate and Dustin walked out with his head hung low. "What have I done Ty?

    "You did what dogs have done for centuries, continue the species." Ty said.

    Dustin answered back "But I'm not a dog, I'm a human."

    "It will be interesting to see what will be born in a few months." Ty says with a grin.
