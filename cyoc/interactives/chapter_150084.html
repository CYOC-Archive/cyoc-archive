Dr. Markle stared off into space, no doubt envisioning a futuristic disease free human race that ate concentrated algae pellets once a day and were able to hibernate during unfavorable weather.

    "Doctor?" I ventured. "You were telling us the procedure we would undergo?"

    "Huh? Oh yes. So... Sally discovered a way to make particles tinier than a cell that could control a cell and replace its DNA if needed. That warmth was her activating all the particles you consumed. Over the next few days there will be a fairly strict regimen of diet, exercise, as well as sleep. Sally will enforce this if necessary, she can directly control hormone synthesis. Tracking and commanding trillions of trillions of Nano particles in real time is the only thing that really gives her processors a workout..."

    "Well. Right now Sally is processing the data she got from both of you. Soon she will send the command to begin replacing your DNA with your son's and vice versa. Then phase 2 will begin and your physical features will change. On a molecular level you will become your son. The first indication of phase 2 is your hair. It will be disintegrated and the protein will be fed back into your body to allow your new hair to grow. We can't have a 14 year old with a salt and pepper beard, can we?" He slapped me on the shoulder with a warm smile. "Then there will be a number of things that will happen. For you, your bone marrow will begin making AB blood like your sons, and your body may react to that, we will need to watch you closely. You will be on a rapid weight loss program that'll make all your friends jealous. Huh Huh"
