    The next morning, Jeff meets up with Darren, who is back to normal, in the dining room for breakfast. “Good morning, Jeff,” the man says, looking up, he then tilts his head, “something wrong?”

    Jeff, who just yesterday, had been a relaxed teen, now seems to be somewhat tensed, of course, Darren knows the reason why, but he acts as though he didn’t, to not make Jeff suspicious of him.

    Jeff looks at Darren, “no, nothing’s wrong,” he says, “just… had a rough night with some weird dreams”.

    “Okay, but if there is anything bothering you,” Darren says, “I want you to tell me, okay?”

    “Yes, sir,” Jeff says.

    Darren nods, he turns back to continuing making breakfast, ‘man, I kind of feel sorry for Jeff,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I guess I went a little to far… oh well’.

    Jeff sits down at the table, the teen notices that the table is only set for two, he turns to looks at Darren, “isn’t… isn’t Jake joining us?”

    Darren turns around, acting surprised, “how do you know about Jake,” he asks.

    “We… we met yesterday,” Jeff says, “I was watching a movie in the cinema, that is where I met him. He acted… as though he runs the place… you know… extremely dominant and commanding.”

    Darren chuckles, “yeah, he can be a handful,” he says, “I told him not to go… to hard on you. I hope he didn’t do anything,” he asks, looking at Jeff in a stern way.

    Jeff quickly shakes his head, “n-no,” he says, “w-we just watched a movie together”.

    Darren, of course, knows that this is a lie, but he continues to play the “I don’t know anything” guy. The man nods, “good,” he says, “and no, he won’t be joining us for breakfast,” he adds, putting the food on the table.

    Jeff relaxes noticeably, he then grabs some food and starts to eat.

    Darren sits down on a chair opposite Jeff, ‘my next mission requires two people,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I already have an idea, but it is risky, Jeff might suspect something. I got to risk it though’. The man looks at Jeff, “I have something I need to tell you,” he says, Jeff looks up from his plate, “I will be going on a mission tomorrow, there is a problem: the mission requires two people. I was wondering if you’d be willing to join me”.

    Jeff thinks about it, ‘it could be dangerous and I have no idea what exactly my “job” would be,’ the teen thinks to himself, ‘but… if it will get me out of here… away from that… boy. I’ll do anything it takes’. The teen looks at Darren, “before I say “yes” or “no”… what is the mission about and what would be my “job”?”

    “It’s an undercover mission,” Darren says, „you’d need to either play my son or daughter in order for the mission to succeed”.

    “And how should that work,” Jeff asks, “we don’t look the same. People will defiantly realize that we are not related”.

    “Ever heard of “The Chronivac”,” Darren asks, even though he of course knows the answer.

    “Yes…,” Jeff says, “you have a Chronivac?”

    Darren nods, “it comes in handy on missions,” he says, “you know, if I need to disguise myself or such things”.

    “Let me guess,” Jeff says, “you plan on using the Chronivac on me to make me look as if I were you biological son?”

    Darren nods, “correct, but,” he says, “I’d prefer if you’d be my 12-year-old “daughter”,” he says.

    Jeff raises an eyebrow, “why? Why do I have to be a girl?”

    ‘Because,’ Darren thinks to himself, ‘I think it’ll suit your new submissive personality pretty well, and “Jake” would love that’. Darren then says, “because, a girl is easier to handle. Look at it this way: this would be a perfect opportunity to find out what it would be like as the other gender, or aren’t you curious what it would be like?”

    “I… I’m not sure,” Jeff says, “but… I guess I kinda am,” the teen thinks for a while, he then lets out a sigh, “okay, fine. I’ll be your “daughter”, but only under one condition: I don’t want to find myself suddenly in bed with some boy!”

    Darren chuckles, “don’t worry,” he says, “I was planning to set your sexual orientation to “lesbian”, that way you wouldn’t become attracted to a boy, if a boy should start hitting on you”.

    “Okay, that sounds fine,” Jeff says, “when would we do it? I mean, transforming me into your… “daughter”?”
