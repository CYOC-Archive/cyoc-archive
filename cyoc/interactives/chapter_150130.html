    Ty's Power

    Less school means more TF pranks and stuff by kids

    Well if the kids only went to school 3 days a week and goofed off the other four,that meant across all of the World there must have been a billion or so teens loafing off and getting into trouble,rather then being quiet and doing their homework.

    And since Ty's wish hadn't been too specific,it had been interpreted to mean schools had always operated on a
    three day week since schools had been invented,perhaps for the
    last 5 thousand or so years.

    The first inkling Ty had he had just destroyed civilization and possibly human existence was when the rock suddenly flew
    the livingroom window shattering the rug with glass and
    rock.
    Dustin immediately bounded down the stairs to join Ty and both were shocked to see that the rockthrower was a teenage werewolf fitted in ill dressed medieval garb.
    The two just stood there speechless as the werewolf punk delinquent drank some substance from a small vial and immediately changed into a human sized anthro dragon punk delinquent.
    "Lets rock and burn this Town " roared the werewolf turned dragon as he hightailed it out of Ty and Dustins sight.

    As the wail of the sirens grew louder and more frequent Dustin
    turned to his omnipotent brother,the explosions and gunfire
    caused by kids with too much time on their hands lighting up
    the noght with pinpricks of light.
    Pinpricks of light which showed the dark forms of the Town walls ,ramparts and Guard Towers of the Town ,all because
    there were more hooligans then schoolkids these days

    It looked like transformations and terror were being dished
    out in equal measure thanks to Ty's thoughtless wish come true.
