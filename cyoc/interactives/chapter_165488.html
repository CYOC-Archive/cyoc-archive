He never knew that the Chronivac would be able to do something like that. But here he was. Swimming around after instinctively eating some bird that had been flitting around like that. Also seemed the hair on his body and his eyebrows was also gone. Kinda left him looking a little odd. Jeff felt an inclination to get back into the pond...but for now he ignored it.

He blushed a little bit, realizing that he was only wearing a speedo at the present. However as she approached him, she didn't seem to find that all that unusual.

    "Ahh, thanks Jeffy," Lily said, and then the two started walking back toward Jeff's house, away from the pond, "Would you be up to bring in the Spring with a date today?"

    Fuck, Jeff thought, how would a date work with my new body? Just the idea of going out... would he be dressed or would it be normal for him to walk around in this speedo? And how would he handle a frog's need to be wet at certain intervals? Jeff decided to figure this out on his own, but he was really looking forward to dating Lily now with his new and improved body. Sure it was kind of odd, but now he actually looked muscular and strong. Probably the best swimmer in the state too!

As he entered he saw his mother was still fully human, and he guessed that the device just changed him. She said to him, "Remember to keep yourself hydrated, Jeff. I don't want to hear about you straining yourself out there."

He'd need to check and see if Lil Jeff still worked the same. He shrugged, "Sure thing mom. I'll keep it in mind. Thanks for the reminder." And with that Jeff headed to his room.
