    Ty's Power

    Mighty Morgan

    Ty felt he had spent enough time at this gym as a visitor, he may as well get a job there. So, he quietly wished he was working there instead. Instantly, he was wearing a polo shirt with the logo of the gym on his shirt along with a name tag pinned to the side. He was wearing black long pants and work out sneakers.

    “Well, Morgan, welcome to GainszWorld Gym! Let me give you a tour of the gym, alright?” Ty said to him.

    Ty showed Morgan around, taking him around to the men’s locker room and to the area where all the cardio equipment was until walking him past all the free weights and all the strength machines.

    “Now, this is where you’ll work those muscles, big guy! Since you decided to join our gym, we’ll give you a head-start. However, it’s up to you to maintain and grow your physique!” Ty explained to his classmate. Ty then waved his hand, changing Morgan’s shirt and jeans into a tank top and workout shorts.

    “HEY! What the--? A head-start? What do you mean--?” Morgan wondered out loud as his muscles started to tingle like crazy, all at once.

    “UHHHHH!!” he groaned loudly as his muscles started to grow instantly, thanks to Ty making a silent wish!

    Morgan’s flat chest suddenly bulged all over as his pectorals bulged forward and sideways, sticking out like big, meaty orbs of muscle from his chest! His shoulders grew much wider while his flat stomach instantly formed thick, mighty abdominal muscles which resembled a washboard! Not only that, but the teenager’s skinny arms inflated with amazing muscle as awe-inspiring biceps and triceps filled out his shirt sleeves, making him resemble a young teen bodybuilder who had spent the last three years lifting heavy weights in the gym!

    “WHOAA!!” Morgan gasped as he began to realize how his body was changing rapidly when he felt his arms and chest bulge with muscle and become heavier and harder, all at once.

    But the most dramatic changes were yet to come when Morgan’s thin, short chicken legs instantly exploded with fantastic muscle in the most dramatic way. His quads inflated like bread in the oven, filling out rapidly with thick, long cords of muscle, covered in long veins everywhere, running from his waist to his knees. His quads grew so wide, that they were even wider than the rest of his body. The surge in weight and strength that Morgan was feeling was insane! Lastly, his calves bulged to the size of footballs, blowing up to large, round mounds of muscle sticking out from the back of his legs. The poor teenage boy was now looking like a professional wrestler or a gymnast with an insanely muscular physique which made any bodybuilder squirm with envy.

    “HOLY CRAP! WHAT IS GOING ON HERE?” Morgan shouted as he examined his body in the large mirror on the wall. He was now a young, teenage bodybuilder!

    “Looking good, big guy!” Ty smiled as he examined his hulking classmate. He checked out his handiwork and he felt pleased with the results of the transformation. “If you know any other friends who might want to come here and sign up, then bring them over!”

    “I… Uhh…. I have some friends who would like to work out with me…” Morgan replied. “Can I bring them tomorrow?”

    “Of course! I’ll give them a tour of the gym and I’ll give them a head start! Now… Start your workout! Begin at the free-weights section over there!” Ty smiled as he pointed to the section where the large dumbbell rack was, right in front of the large mirror on the wall. Morgan walked over there and picked up a set of dumbbells before he started on his arm curls.

    “Another happy customer!” Ty chuckled quietly to himself. He was already looking forward to meeting the new guests whom Morgan was going to bring to his gym the following day.
