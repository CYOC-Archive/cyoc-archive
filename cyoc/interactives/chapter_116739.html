Jeff was pretty confident that his father wasn't potty trained and still in diapers at that point in time. He then selected his own age and changed it to the same and when it asked for "mental change" he clicked no as he wanted to be aware of his time as a baby. Finally it asked how long the transformation would last and he kept it the same still wanting to spend the week with his dad even though it would now be as babies. It would be the ultimate in father son bonding in his opinion.

The toddler was currently watching a little kids show while drinking from his tippi cup and didn't even notice him enter. And his shorts disappeared revealing his training pants puffed up into a pampers disposable diaper. Lastly the tippi cup he was drinking from morphed into a baby bottle which the baby boy happily began to suck on.

However Jeff was paying more attention to the state of his underwear as that was what he wanted to see changed the most. As he reached the single digits he felt his briefs change become more kid friendly and childishly looking. A few seconds later they changed again becoming slightly puffy turning into training pants. A few more seconds later they puffed up even more becoming a pair of pampers. Looking down at his new padding with a grin Jeff noticed that they had sesame street designs, and looking over at his dad's revealed he had the same. Also like his father he had a sesame street themed t-shirt except his had big bird on the front.

The floor was now covered with scattered baby toys and the staircase was now blocked by a baby gate. Not sure what else to do Jeff decided to hang out with his baby father on the couch and after a few tries managed to climb up. Sitting down next to him Jeff laid back and watched the show as the other continued to drink from his baby bottle.
