         Driving down the dark highway David couldn't help but think a bought what his friends had done to the police officer a mile back, the trio of foxes wiped the blood off the muzzles with thir paws climbed back in to the car & told David to drive. As David drove Scott lesurely licked the blood off his claws murring wildly content with his meal. David was begging to become very disturbed about what his friends had become. Just how safe was he? If this chest was so powerful who knows what other magic might greet them when they arrive? Suddenly Saul's words "We're here." brought him back to reality. Looking up the three foxes stared in awe of the size of the estate. "It's huge, wow this guy was loaded!" Scott yelled. Pulling up to a large cast iron gate Dave got out of the car & inspected the large padlock on the gate. " Ha, that's no problem." Scott said as he handed the bolt cutters in the back seat of the car to David. Placing the bolt cutters over the pad lock and chain David firmly cut through the chain & pushed the gate open. Quickly climbing back in to the car David began to drive up the long driveway up to the estate.

         Minutes later they pulled up along side of the building, "Whoa, this is it." Saul said. Stepping out of the car the quartet turned to face the huge house, huge statues lined the roof top. "Look statues of foxes, wolves, deer, tigers & wow look even a stature of the owner himself." Timothy said taking in the scenery, his sharp canine eyes seeing far more then any human could. Slowly the four walked up to the huge statue of the owner, a large plack underneath the stature read: "Douglas Haversome, Famous Science Fiction Director, Estate Established in 1953." " Well, let's get to it." Saul said. Walking up to the huge double doors crowbar in hand, "Here goes nothing." David grunted as he forced the large doors open breaking the look. Kicking the doors open the four men were greeted by a large main hall with a beautiful spiral staircase. "Let's go." Timothy said.
