    “Ok then. You win.” He said.

    Jen can’t help but feel a little confused. He said is so calmly. She kinda expected him to be a little more panicked… Oh well.

    Jen sat down on Kyle’s bed as she excitedly waited for Kyle to say the magic words.

    “Kyle… What’s going on?” Sophie asked.

    “Wait and see…” He simply replied.

    “Transform and Dare.” Kyle said.

    “Transform!” Jenifer shouted. She’s finally turning back to normal!

    Kyle took a deep breath…

    “Firstly, the cum of you and everyone who was intected by it is no lonfer contagious. None of you will be able to transform anyone else.”

    Jenifer immediately tried to get up and punch Kyle, but the curse prevented her from moving.

    “You bastard!” She screamed bloody murder.

    Kyle continued. “You and all those who were transformed by your contagion will now occasionally transform at random intervals. There is no telling when will the mutations will occur and how you will transform. In addition, the mutations won’t be restricted to just horses. The extent of the mutations will be random. You could gain human or inanimate transformations.”

    “I will kill you Kyle!” Jenifer screamed.

    “In addition, while everyone will act as if you born with your changes, they are also allowed to treat you however they like. If someone could treat you like a freak because of your appearance, they are allowed to do so. Anyone capable of mistreating, discriminate and bully you however they please depending on your current body. In addition, your life will change depending on your current body. For example, if your body is deemed unfit for the cheerleader position, your past will change to give you a different occupation or situation. It depends on your current body and how the mutations change you. This applies to everyone else who were transformed by your curse.”

    Fear started to overtake Jenifer. “NO! I just want to be normal again!”

    “You will forget ever considering taking my sister hostage. You and everyone else transformed by your curse will never consider using my sister against me again, nor will any of you be able to use the curse against me and my family.”


    “Why are you doing this?!” Jenifer is crying at this point.

    “You will now be teleported back to your current home and forget ever coming here.”

    And with that, Jenifer blipped out of Kyle’s room, back to were her current home would be.

    Sophie blinked at the exchange.

    “W-what was that?!”

    Kyle sighed. “Let me explain…”

    …

    And few minutes later, Kyle finished explaining the curse.

    “So it’s real…” Sophie said to herself.

    A thought occurred to her.

    “Wait, why are you telling me any of this? If the curse is that powerful, then I could easily use it against you.”

    Kyle sat back on his bed. “I know. I was debating wheather I should tell you or not. If I wanted to, I could have used the curse to erase your memories too. I know I’m taking a leap of faith by doing so… After all.. Power corrupts… But… nah. I trust you. I know that you’ve been bullied too.”

    Sophie looked down in thought. “I know… But I wouldn’t use the curse on them just to get revenge. Their human too.”

    Kyle looked up at the ceiling while he laid on his bed. “I originally wanted to teach Jenifer some humility. But I guess I wasn’t specific enough the first time. She ended up enjoying the curse. You probably saw her made out with her friends. I just kinda sat back and went with the flow.”

    A thought occurred to Sophie.

    "Wait, how did you get the curse in the first place? It goes from person to person, right."

    Kyle nodded. "Yeah. Jenifer cursed me before. I used to be taller then you and Jenifer. She transformed me to be as tall as you. She then pants me and posted a picture of my new body online. For some reason, she's acting as if she never cursed me. It's like she forgot. I just wanted to teach her humility, but things just kinda ran out of control."

    "I see... Well why didn't you turned yourself back?" Sophie asked.

    "I have no reason too. This height is fine. And it made me see things from your perspective."

    "Hmm... Well I would like you no matter how tall you are." Sophie said with conviction.

    "Thank you..." Kyle said. "Say... Do you want to use the curse?"

    "Um..." Sophie thought about what she could do with reality warping powers. What would she do with it?
