    Jennifer stopped in the doorway, shocked by the challenge issued by Beryl. She had forgotten that Beryl's brother Kyle had cursed her, and it was now her turn to curse someone else. She knew better than to avoid the challenge and get both parts of the curse. She slowly let the door close, and turned to face Beryl.

    "Now, Beryl. Let's not do anything rash. My beef is with your brother, not you. If anything, we should be working together. I mean, look what his curse did to you. Don't you want to get revenge on him? I can help, " pleaded Jennifer.

    Beryl just stared down at her, still grinning, and re-issued her challenge, "No, it's your turn now Jennifer. Transform or Dare? Or would you like both?"

    "Beryl, don't be hasty. If we work together, I am sure that we can get you back to normal. If you go easy on me, I promise I will use the curse to fix you. Do we have a deal?" asked Jennifer.

    Beryl continued to smile. "Time's almost up. Better make your choice. Transform or Dare?"

    Jennifer knew she was beat. "Fine. I choose Transform. But remember our deal. Nothing too weird."

    Beryl's smile grew even wider. "Oh, a bold choice. Hmmm, nothing too weird, you say. I know. I'm pretty average looking. How about I make you look more like me?"

    Jennifer turned pale. "You wouldn't ......"

    "Oh yes, I think I will, " said Beryl. "Jennifer, for your transformation, your body will be the same size as mine, regardless of what size I am."

    Jennifer cursed, as her clothes suddenly felt very tight.
