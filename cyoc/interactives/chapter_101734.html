    Karen arrived at the high school she taught at at exactly 7:30. It was quite a large school and had a lot of students almost 2000. Karen was quite new at the school having only taught their for just over a year after she quit teaching in a daycare.

    She opened the door and went to the classroom where she taught all of her lessons. It was quite a small room, this was because she taught relatively small classes of around 25 people. But she liked it, it had all she needed. She dumped her bag and paperwork on her desk. And left for the teachers lounge. She had never been this early before so she was eager to see who was at school this early. She hadn't seen any students yet other than the odd freshman. And she had only seen one teacher: Mr Yang, an elderly Asian gentleman who taught math. He looked surprised to see her so early but smiled and greeted her politely.

The only two staff there were Miss Rose and Mr Dennis both of whom, for whatever reason seriously disliked her. Miss Rose was a middle aged Science teacher, who was short, round and ugly to be frank. And Mr Dennis, who was also an English teacher. Frankly just didn't like her teaching style.
