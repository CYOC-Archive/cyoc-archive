    Getting your rooms

    Before the recepcionist can say anything to you or your family, a guy (or should we say a MAN!) comes to greet us, waving a hand to you.

    - "Greetings! My name is Alexandros. Before you say something, I know, you just want to get a room. If you follow me, I will show you the best suites of all the hotel. Now, if you please follow me..."

    The recepcionist sighs and tells Derrick and his family to go with him. After that, they go to a corridor where Alexandros is waiting. Not long after that, they follow him to a rather strange passage, where there's some dust and cobwebs up on the walls.

    - "Damn spiders... I told them to keep it clean!" - says Alexandros, who keeps a steady pace. He stops and faces one of the doors of the passage. "This room will be for the parents, John and Vanessa. The teens will go to the other rooms."

    After leaving their parents to settle in a rather strange room, with columns and an altar of sorts, Derrick and his sister keep following Alexandros to another door, where he gestures the girl to it.

    That room is strange too, nothing compared to the parents' room. There's a kind of pool in it, and it seems like vegetation has grown in it.

    - "I don't think you cleaned this up..." - says your sister, as Alexandros suddenly pushed her and closed the door.

    - "Hey! What was that for?" - says Derrick. Alexandros says nothing and keeps walking to the last door of the passage. He stops and opens the door, and inside, Derrick can see what seems a sort of gym, with treadmills and static bikes in it. Alexandros welcomes him in, and accompanies Derrick inside.

    - "Why my room seems like this?" - Derrick asks at Alexandros. That's when Derrick gets a closer look at him, watching his well-toned body, with his toned-up arms and strong legs. Seems like this room is perfect for a guy like him...

    - "Actually, this is my room. This is where I live and train. And I personally invited you in, so don't get cocky or anything. I would like to share it with you." - Derrick is stunned. Will he live with that man? And why does Alexandros stay with him?
