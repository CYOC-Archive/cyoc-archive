    pregnant but still a man

    John didn't know what to expect from these pills, they could be the key to having a new and better life or they could be something he regrets forever.

    Somewhat tentatively he took a pill at random, his uncle didn't say what changes each one would give him, he could send him a message but curiosity was already killing him.

    The pill he took was a brown one, it looked almost like chocolate. He was still a little hesitant but he took it anyway, all the way in.

    At first he didn't feel anything, maybe time passed slower because of his nervousness but he spent one, two minutes and nothing. He was starting to believe that maybe they were just some product related to self-motivation when he then started to feel a tingling sensation all over his abdomen.

    The tickling increased but he didn't see any external change, it was as if... Something was moving inside him. With more time that passed, the sensation became more intense, even becoming painful. He had to sit down a little to bear that sensation, with more and more doubt as to where he had gotten himself.

    Little by little the feeling of being squeezed inside ended, but his insides felt strange, empty...

    A while passed like this, he got up and went to look in a mirror to see if he could find any change that he had not been aware of but there was nothing. Disappointed, he was about to forget everything when he again felt a strange feeling in his abdomen, this time as if he were inflating like a balloon. He quickly looked at himself in the mirror again and sure enough, his abdomen looked slightly swollen. How could being fatter be a life change?

    Even so, he continued to see his changes, his belly felt increasingly hard, not soft like the belly of someone who is overweight usually is, that seemed strange to him but he let it be.

    It was also strange that only his belly was swelling, everything else, his arms, legs, everything remained the same.

    His abdomen continued to grow and grow, slowly but surely. John began to worry when he saw that he was taking on a too round shape, it even looked like that of a pregnant woman... But it couldn't be that, right?

    The weight of his new belly was getting heavier, so he returned to where he had left his pills, the living room, and sat down in front of them. There he didn't have a mirror but he could see his reflection on the black television screen.

    Growing and growing. If he weren't a man he would say she maybe looked about 4 months pregnant. Not too big, but definitely looked like a pregnancy. John told himself to calm himself that they were just his ideas, but he couldn't stop seeing his belly as that of someone who was pregnant.

    Everything was confirmed when he felt a little kick. It was an immense shock, even John remained static in his place. John didn't know what to do. A great wave of feelings came over him and he began to feel nauseous and wanted to cry.

    His belly began to grow even faster, as if he had already understood that John knew what it was and was no longer afraid of growing, he quickly reached what seemed like 7 months, John knew women who had given birth in this stage. They were few, but he knew that he already practically had a baby on his hands.

    He continued to grow, faster and faster. John held her belly tightly, not even he knew if to protect it or to prevent it from growing any more. But just the same he continued to grow until it looked like he was a formidable 9 months pregnant.

    John was worried that the change would continue until he gave birth, but he stopped. There was no longer any strange feeling. He waited for perhaps 15 minutes, during which time the "baby" had moved like a whirlwind, not letting him think that it was anything other than a baby.

    His shirt no longer fit, revealing a large, newly grown belly underneath. Still, John was a little afraid to take it off and face his new reality head on.

    John was static. It was then that...
