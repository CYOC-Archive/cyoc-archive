    The Witch's Baby Spell

    The witch threw a blast of magic at the two delinquents, grinning and with an evil glint in her eyes. Chris and John felt the effects immediately. They were of course extremely frightened and became even more so when they saw the world become out of proportion. It was getting bigger! The witch became a giant and from short was now looming over them both. And it wasn't all. As they darted scared glimpses at each other, Chris and John noticed the differences in their friend's face. It became rounder, the eyes bigger, the cheeks rounder and the nose smaller. The hair thinned out on their scalps and their facial hair disappeared utterly. The same happened to their body hair as their chest became non-existent, their limbs shorter and their fingers and toes similar to little sausages. Rolls of fat appeared and their muscled became akin to jelly, forcing them to plonk upon their buttocks, as they could not stand upright anymore. When the spell finally stopped, they realized that the world had not become bigger, they were the ones who had shrunk! And looking at their chubby digits, as well as their tummies and hairless bodies, and most of all the bewildered and bewildering appearance of the other, they understood they had become babies, of not even one year old. They could only babble, not speak properly even! The witch had even made their clothes disappear! They were naked, their tiny willies, useless and immature, as well as their minuscule scrotum, on full display! As well as their butts! And that awful witch was just cackling heartily, elated by her work.
    "Do not worry! Now, you're too young even for juvenile prison! But..." she started with an evil tone of voice, holding another bad surprise.
    John and Chris gulped and wondered what other worse spell would hit them.
    "... But I can make your transformation even better!"
    To their horror, the two burglars saw the witch blast them with another spell, wondering what other nasty surprise this witch had in store for them.
