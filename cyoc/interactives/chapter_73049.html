    The second one is in a pink diaper, blue dress, and a blue bow on top of her long, blonde hair. She has blue eyes, and tanned skin. Her name is
    Sue.

    The third one is in a white diaper, purple dress, and has her black hair done in buns by purple scrunchies. She has brown eyes, and you can tell
    she has Asian in her. Her name is Rin.

    And the last one is in a white diaper, yellow dress, and has a yellow flower in her brown hair. She has brown eyes, and you can tell she has Latin
    in her. Her name is Maria.

    All four girls happily walk over to you, and introduce themselves one after another.

    To your surprise (but not too much), they speak just as clearly as you do in their high voices, and they're just as smart as you are (or at least as
    smart as the age you were).

    "It's so nice of you to come up here." Beth says to you.

    "We always have room for one more." Sue says to you.

    "We were about to have some fun." Rin says to you.

    "Would you like to join us?" Maria asks you.

    "Sure." you say "Why not?"

    "Here you go, then." says Beth, smiling as she hands you a pacifier.

    "What do I do with this?" you ask.

    "You suck on it, silly!" Sue says before bursting into a fit of giggles.

    "We all have one each." Rin says.

    "It feels so good to suck on them." Maria says, smiling "Go ahead, try it."

    "Okay." you reply, and you do. You feel very happy "You're right. It DOES feel good, but it's kind of weird. Hee-hee."

    "I know it may feel odd to you," Beth says, "but give it some time, and it'll feel natural. We ARE babies after all."

    "But how come we can all walk, talk, and think like we're older?" you ask, for you'd been wondering for a bit now.

    "Because we're all around 18 like you are." Sue replies "This treehouse is magic - it turns anyone who goes in it into a cute baby."

    "We come here every day to play with each other as babies without a care in the world." Maria says "There's plenty of toys to play with, and
    when we're done, we take a nap. When we wake up, we're back to normal in our own beds like it never happened, but we can come back here
    anytime and be babies again by sucking our pacifiers. And if anyone wants to, they can come with us and be babies. There's even a way to
    leave this place, and have reality change for a bit so everyone's a baby like us, but we remember the real reality, and can go back here anytime."

    "We were going to play patty-cake," Beth says "but since you just joined us, we'll let you choose."

    "Really?" you ask, already exited.

    "Sure." Rin says with a smile.
