    When Ty walked into the computer lab, he noticed that there were only five students there, however only one of them seemed to be helping the other four. It was a short, skinny kid with glasses and sandy, short brown hair. His name was Milton, and Ty knew right away that he would become his next “victim”.

    “Hey, Milton. I have an assignment where I need to create a small webpage for my Information Sciences class, but I have no clue how to do that…” he said, which was the truth. Ty had seen how his cousin Peter was such a computer geek, while he never had the time to sit behind a pc.

    “No problem, Ty! I’ll help you! I’m the lab assistant here, and that’s my job!” Milton replied cheerfully, as he pulled up a chair and sat down right next to Ty.

    Within the next ninety minutes, Milton patiently instructed Ty how to write the HTML for his simple webpage in Notepad. He showed Ty the different tags, as he sketched a draft of Ty’s little page in his notebook. He asked Ty what he wanted on the page, and then Milton taught him the HTML tags which Ty needed to know. Then he instructed Ty how to load his page in the browser and display it. They edited the page several times before Ty was pleased with the results.

    “Thanks, Milton… Are you here every day?” Ty wondered, as he neatly placed the printout in his backpack.

    “Every day, after school. It’s my job to help everybody who comes in here!” he replied.

    As Ty walked out of the lab, he couldn’t help but feel impressed with Milton’s patience and enthusiasm about computers and writing HTML codes. It was his highest grade ever in that class, and he felt like Milton needed to be rewarded for his willingness to help!

    “Ty? What’s going on? I can’t move!” Milton exclaimed.

    “Relax, buddy! I’m using my magical powers on you! You will grow to 5’9” tall and you’ll gain 30 pounds of muscle right about NOW!” Ty said with a smile.
