As they made their way down the hall, flecks of light appeared in the blackness surrounding them. The points of light started rotating as they grew in number, quickly becoming a star field that swirled about them with growing intensity.

The light from the arcade machines created distinct rings around the room, broken up by the bodies that walked between them. Synth beats and spotlights helped strengthen the atmosphere, dark spaces contrasting with CRT and neon illumination.

Their youthful stubble, for what it was, faded. Skin became softer and unkempt hair grew bouncier and more vibrant.
