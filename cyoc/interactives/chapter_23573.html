    Letting out a sigh of contentment Katie wiggled her shoulders, "That feels nice."

To prevent herself from thinking inappropriate thoughts about her friend, something becoming increasingly difficult as Katie made small noises on relief, Tiff tried to start up a conversation, "You know, you should really get involved in sports. It was the level of fitness that didn't come without working out.

If she was she hid it very well, maybe she worked out in secret. That struck Tiff as a rather erotic thought and she found herself imagining Katie lifting weights, shirtless, her small breasts jiggling with every move.     "Tiff, I feel stronger," was all she could say to sum up the situation.
