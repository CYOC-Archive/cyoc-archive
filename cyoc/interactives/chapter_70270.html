         Just as Jennifer was about to make her choice, she heard Kyle sigh and say, "You know what? I've changed my mind. I was going to get back at you, but..."

         Since Jennifer didn't really believe in the curse, and she was running late, she became impatient when Kyle paused for too long. However, since her friend had taken the curse so seriously, she decided to hold back her usual tongue-lashing just in case.

         "So, can I go?" She asked, her voice strained with the effort that it took to be civil. "You're going to make me late."

         After a few seconds Kyle finally said, "...Okay, wait, I know what I want to do."

         Jennifer rolled her eyes and began to tap her bare foot. "And that would be...?"

         "Since you took more than a minute to make a choice," Kyle began, "I'll do you two favors instead of one. For your transformation, you'll always have your ideal body so you'll never be unhappy about your appearance. You'll always be in good health, heal quickly, never grow old, have a very good memory, and find it easy to learn whatever you want."

         Jennifer was shocked speechless. Even if the curse weren't real, she hadn't expected someone that she had mistreated and thought so lowly of to forward so many nice ideas, when they could have just as easily said something to try and upset her.

         "And for your dare," Kyle continued, "you'll live life to the fullest and never let anything get you down."

         Jennifer heard Kyle hang up immediately after he finished speaking, leaving her to wonder what to think about what he had done. On the one hand it was kind of gross, because she'd never fall for whatever someone like Kyle considered to be charms. On the other hand she would probably feel flattered by the words if she took their source (that being Kyle) out of the equation. However, in the end, all they were only words: it'd be so much better if there had been more to them than sound.

         That was when she began to feel peculiar, but not in a bad way. She looked down at herself, hoping against hope, and she gasped in pleasant surprise at what she saw. Barely having the presence of mind to hang up the phone, she ran from the kitchen and made her way to her bedroom in record time. With her heart racing with excitement, she stopped in front of her full-length mirror and removed what clothes she had on as quickly as possible.

         What she saw in the mirror was too good to be true, but her wandering hands confirmed what her eyes were telling her: she now had the idealized body that she had always envisioned whenever she had found some part of herself less than satisfactory. She'd always considered herself rather pretty when compared to most of the other girls at school, but she had been nothing compared to the goddess that she had wanted to be.

         Her skin was soft and smooth, free of blemish and glowing with health, and there was no hair to be seen where she never wished to shave again. Her face now held an ageless sort of beauty, something between the cuteness of youth and the sexiness of maturity, with eyes that sparkled and lips that glistened. Her hair was like a natural wonder, plentiful, lustrous, bouncy and full of life. And the proportions of her body...

         Jennifer ran her hands down her flanks, unable to resist the seduction of her own beauty. Then she cupped her breasts and shuddered in delight, as she assured herself that they were now wondrously symmetrical as well as responsive. It was amazing how little visual and tactical stimulation could send her into a heavenly state of arousal, making her feel more alive than ever.

         And some loser that she barely knew and wanted nothing to do with had given her this. Could she just ignore that fact and treat him the same? But she recalled that she now had the ability to "curse" (it didn't seem like one to her) someone, and didn't Kyle deserve something nice in return? It wasn't like anyone would know... And she'd be doing him a favor, besides. Of course, she could always use it on someone else for her own benefit. The best-looking girl should have the best-looking guy, right? And then there were the people that she wished to pay back for past transgressions...

         Decisions, decisions.
