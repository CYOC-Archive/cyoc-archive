    As Dan pressed the button on the blackberry and began to alter the girl's appearance and awareness, a man by the name of Henry just happened to be passing by. He had a job with the government and one of his favourite things was watching how the younger generation used the technology of today.

    Of course this had gotten him in trouble several times and his superiours had been forced to suspend him a couple of times. Now here he was, on his first real vacation since he'd joined the government bureau he worked for, "That was about twenty years ago," Henry reminded himself.

    A younger man, probably gay, was fooling around with a blackberry when he just happened to be passing by. Leaning ever so gently so as not to disturb the young man and what was obviously his significant other, "Couldn't those young girls see it," he asked himself, he watched as the young man pushed a button. Just then a tingling sensation went through the air and Henry watched as the one with the blackberry said something and then pointed at the girls.

    Before Henry's eyes, one of the girls turned into a young man and began to kiss the other one. "That's no ordinary blackberry," Henry thought as he rubbed his eyes, wondering also if the experimental drug that was suppose to up his awareness was messing with his mind.

    And then he heard a barking noise. Looking toward the noise, knowing the rule about pets on this beach, he saw two girls laughing and pointing at another, who was clearly barking instead of talking. And then, a stroke of luck happened as the two in front of him got up in a hurry and went to investigate, leaving the blackberry just lying on the towel they'd been sitting on.

    Looking over at the boy and how he was trying to get one of the laughing girls to calm down and tell him something, Henry carefully leaned forward and picked up the blackberry. Seeing that nobody was paying attention to him, he slipped it into his pocket and headed back to his hotel room.

    "If this can really do what I think it can, then I'm going to have some fun with those idiots at work. Who knows, maybe I can even use it on myself to make my own life a little better," Henry told himself as he got into a taxi he'd just hailed.
