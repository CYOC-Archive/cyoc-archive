    After we acquire the necessary six subjects for our hypothesis, the experiment may begin.

    We want to study the human mating responses to drastic and multiple biological changes. We want to determinate how much the biological aspect of a person impact on the relationships with other people. However, the subject are misinform of the true objective, they were told they were to test the long term effects of some drugs. This is false, the drugs used during the experiment have been completely studied and their effects on the subject would be the matter of the study, also all the effects have been shown to be reversible. No footage of the experiment will exist since the participants have not given permission for it, non the less they are under the pretence to just be secluded and only being able to communicate with us via the residence phone, in reality they are under the constant surveillance of our research team.

    For the remainder of the following 8 months, the subjects are to stay in a secluded property with a tropical climate, housing, food and entertainment systems in each room provided and have agreed to following guidelines:
    * First, since the climate is controlled, the subject must relinquish all clothing for the remainder of the experiment.
    * Each day must begin with them ingesting the provided drugs for the day.
    * Sexual relations are NOT discouraged, they could provided helpful data if engaged. However they're not compulsory.
    * No Outside contact or future disclosure of the contents of the experiment. All participants are to have their matters in order before the experiment.
    * Additional rules may become into effect if needed for the sake of the experiment, as long as the duration of the same is not altered.

    The six subjects have been selected given their different qualities both psychological and physical, (beside the lack of an allergic reaction to the drugs that are going to be used). The following is a brief detail of the participants (last names omitted as per agreement) and the proposed treatment for the first month:

    Subject #1
    Name: Matt
    Description: Caucasian, ectomorphic, heterosexual. Above average IQ.
    Treatment: Placebo. Part of the control group.

    Subject #2
    Name: Sofia
    Description: Mestizo, ectomorphic, heterosexual. Above average IQ.
    Treatment: Placebo. Part of the control group.

    Subject #3
    Name: Jodie
    Description: Nordic, ectomorphic, heterosexual. Average IQ.
    Treatment: Testosterone enhancers. Libido enhancer.

    Subject #4
    Name: Rex
    Description: Caucasian, mesomorphic, heterosexual. Average IQ.
    Treatment: Muscle reducer, Femenine traits enhancements. Libido enhancer.

    Subject #5
    Name: Darla
    Description: Mulato, mesomorphic, bisexual. Above average IQ.
    Treatment: Intelligence and inhibition suppressors, Femenine traits enhancements. Libido enhancer.

    Subject #6
    Name: Guy
    Description: Eurasian, mesomorphic, bisexual. Average.
    Treatment: Intelligence supressors, Male traits enhancents. Libido enhancer.

    We decided to start with the most normal of the treatments for the first weeks and see if the subject could handle the more altering drugs. Under this guidelines the experiment has begun.
