    It had been a few weeks since Sarah and Paige had made the transition to babyhood. They were both around 3 months old now and they were living a great life of being pampered.

    Mark and Heather had been adjusting to their new roles as parents. Heather had become more inclined to take care of two girls and start working remotely from home. Her company allowed her to do so after they found out that she had become a new mother to 2 beautiful baby girls. Mark, meanwhile, did not have the luxury of working from home like Heather and had to continue going to work as an Attorney-at-law. He had been very successful and was thinking about a run for District Attorney, one that he had to really think about how that he had 2 girls to worry about now. He loved being a girl dad, he absolutely loved coming home and seeing his two beautiful girls get excited to see him.

    Sarah and Paige both ended up in the right place at the right time. They got a chance to relive their childhood all over again. The Time Machine they had come across made their lives all for the better. Not only did it adjust their living conditions by changing Paige’s bedroom into a nursery, it had also adjusted their appearances too.

    Both of the girls had been given some features of both Mark and Heather, that way no one could tell if the girls were theirs or not. Paige had more features of mom, while Sarah looked just like her daddy. That was something exciting for Mark as he got to bond with Sarah while she was a toddler and now she gets to grow up as his identical daughter.

    One day, Mark came home from work and said hi to Heather before picking up his two girls from the playpen. They both giggled around their pink pacifiers as daddy bounced them in his arms. “Hello my two beautiful angels!” he greeted them with excitement, “were you both being daddy’s good girls today?” Both smiled and moved an arm up and down to say yes.

    He led them to the couch to watch tv with them while they rested on his lap. Mark would come home and watch some different shows after work. Sometimes the news, sometimes animated shows with the girls, sometimes sports. Sarah loved watching sports with daddy, but also loved watching her kids shows with Paige.

    Heather came by and talked about her day and wanted to ask about Marks. He talked about his long workday, but he didn’t go on too long, he wanted to enjoy time with his new family. He was really excited about being a dad now, Heather was also excited about being a mom. She was also given the gift of nutrients by the Time Machine as both of her breasts now were engorged with breastmilk for her two girls. Mark enjoyed the features a bit, but he knew that those were for the girls now.
