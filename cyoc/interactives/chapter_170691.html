John thought IKEA was a good name, and S-fee wasn't particularly hard to say. John told Stew that the idea was when they were unknown people would ask "What's the name supposed to mean?"
    Then an employee would say "Each Size Fits You, Good Sir/Madame!"
    "Oh do they?" the customer would then say and then maybe jokingly attempt to try on say a plus size and find out the store's gimmick. At which point all they'd have to do is either remove the clothes or buy them and spread the word of ESFY. However the issue would be that people could just look inside the store, see they were kind of a generic clothes store with a meh design, and the prices were pretty average for the market. They were open, but no one was really biting and coming in. The low traffic, boring design, and not particularly cheap prices wasn't really attracting anyone.
    Stew sighed, but supposed that as John set himself as restocker and he'd assist the disabled who couldn't use the self-checkout machines...
