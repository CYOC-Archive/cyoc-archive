    "Tina!" Leah called. Tina looked over. Her jaw dropped in shock. She walked over to Leah.

    "Leah, what- are you wearing a used diaper?"

    "It's a long story. I can explain once we get out of here," Leah said urgently. "They won't let me leave on my own. I don't know why you left me here in the first place, honestly."
