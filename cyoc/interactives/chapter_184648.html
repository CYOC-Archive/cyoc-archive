Grabbing your scaly muzzle and exploring it, you surprise yourself by blinking with only your inner eyelids, the ones you can partially see through. "Ph-what'sssss ghghghoing on-ngggg?" you stutter out, having trouble pronouncing English words because it's so easy to slip into hissing or gurgling sounds.
