    15 years later

    added by Anonymous 5 years ago O

    15 years pass. Braiden still remembers Callum. Brodie and Maggie are now 34 and 33 respectively. Braiden is exactly the same as before. Same personally, look and intelligence. The 15 years were just like a replay with some obvious alterations such as friends and teachers. Due to the advancement of time and education Braiden has had to relearn a sizable chunk of information. But one of the most dramatic changes was the abolishment of school uniform for the school. It made Braiden feel free, even if he wasn't.

    Braiden sits down with his friends. In the same spot as 15 years ago. He's sat here many times so it doesn't bother him too much, at times he does feel like bursting into tears though. It's the spot where he lost all the hard work of 15 years only to repeat it again.

    "So Braiden?" Asks Ryan, one of his friends with long blonde hair and light blue eyes.
    "Yeah?" Replies Braiden.
    "What do you think of that new kid?"
    "Yeah. He's a skater though so I have no idea what he's like."
    "Let's see if we can make friends with him. He looks lonely," says Riley.
    "What's his name?" Asks Braiden.
    "I think it's something like Lachlan or something."
    "Yeah. It's Lachlan. In one of my classes, and I'm no longer the shortest in the class," replies Nathan, another one of Braiden's friends. He is of Costa Rican descent but has never left Australia and has spoke English his whole life. He is thinly built and rather short. Lucky to be 170 cm.

    Riley gets up out of the chair and walks over to the skater. "Hi I'm Riley."
    "Lachlan," he replies.
    "Nice to meet you."
    "Nice to meet you too," he replies sounding nervous.
    "You want to hang out with us?"
    "I guess."

    Riley walks back to his friends and Lachlan follows. Braiden notices that Nathan wasn't lying, this guy is rather short. Looks to be 163cm tops.

    Braiden asks, "So where are you from?"
    "Down south," replies the boy.
    "I'm going to get a drink," says Riley, "Anyone want to come with me?"
    "Sure," replies Nathan. Riley looks at Braiden, "If we don't see each other again during lunch. Just remember to be at my house by 3:30."
    "I know," replies Braiden.

    Riley and Nathan walk off carrying their bags. Braiden notices Lachlan looks like he's go something to say. "What is it?" Asks Braiden.
    "Braiden. It's me," he replies.
    "Who in particular?"
    "Callum. From years ago."
    "Oh crap, you weren't living on the other side of the planet."
    "I wasn't. I've been trying to find you, Maggie and Brodie for ages but I didn't found you. I can't remember your last name."
    "How long?" Asks Braiden.
    "Since I was 10. I got spanked for having an account on Facebook."
    "Damm," replies Braiden.
    "Not to mention the amount of foster families I've went through."
    "What? Why?"
    "Because my reality adjusted parents died when I was 3, and you know how foster kids are treated."
    "So you in a foster family right now?"
    "Not a family. But an adoption centre."
    "Maybe Maggie and Brodie can adopt you."
    "Maybe. By the way. Can I hang out after school? The orphanage doesn't care until after 6."
    "Sure," replies Braiden.

    The end of lunchtime bell rings. "I've got to go," says Braiden.
    "Yeah. Me too."
