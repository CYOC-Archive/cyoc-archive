    You walk out the shop with Robin to finish your errands, but it started to bug your mind what it might feel like to be a Hobbit.

    "Rob, can I ask you something?" you ask. "Ask away my friend". "I was wondering what it would be like to be a little hobbit like that. Could you
    possibly do that for me?". "If I can do that to Adam, I can most certainly do that for you" he replies. "But I don't want to fall into any trap here... I
    want to be able to turn back to myself if I change my mind." You say.
    "Ahh, that ruins the fun of it. But if you insist, I can give you the ability to change back. I could even let you change between the forms at will,
    since I like you", Robin said with a friendly smile. "I'll make sure you're appropriately clothed too".

    At that moment, you begin to feel odd, cold, and slightly empty, as if you just threw up. Then, as it eased off, you noticed you were shrinking
    down. Down and down, you didn't quite realise you'd be this short at all, but you figure you would give it a try. As you were shrinking though, one
    part of you was growing, and that was your belly, becoming quite plump and filled out. Despite this, you didn't fatten up anywhere else.

    At that moment, it only just clicked in your head that you were naked. Most of your body hair, except for a slight snail trail and sparse pubes,
    receded into your tiny body. Overlooking your large belly, you can see that your penis and balls have also shrunk, but are still proportional to your
    new hobbit body.

    Your feet also began to tingle and feel funny. They started to grow a good few sizes larger. Normal for a hobbit, but almost comical for a human.
    As they grew, the tops became itchy as thick dark hair grew and curled from the tops of them, and the soles toughened up nicely. It feels like you
    wouldn't even need footwear with those.

    Finally, your face adjusted to be a little more hobbit-like. Your jawline rounded out, your ears became just slightly pointed, and your hair
    shortened a little, curled and darkened. The little facial hair you had was now nonexistant.

    "So how is it, short-stuff?" Robin asks. "Hmm, It's so strange, so different. Such a small body, such large feet!" You begin to walk... no... run
    around with excitement. This was real! It felt so strange, yet so good. These feet are also very useful. You're running around on stone and gravel,
    and it doesn't hurt at all.

    "Come on then, let's get that cheese for your aunt".
