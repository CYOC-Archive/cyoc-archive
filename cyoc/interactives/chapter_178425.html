You would have a voluptuous figure, wide hips, thick thighs, and a large soft rear were a must given the job title. You also decided that you would have a womb full of countless and infinite number of eggs as well as large lactating breasts, and that you would always appear to be carrying quadruplets and nine months pregnant by human standards. No mater how many times you would lay eggs, your belly would never get any smaller. Then in addition to that you could lay eggs whenever you wished, giving birth to a random species which would hatch from the egg, unless you previously took it upon yourself to breed with something prior.

    The age you decided to appear in your prime, and that despite the heavy load on your body that your magic would help support the weight of your belly, as well as reduce the pain of laying eggs by swapping it with pleasure. Your followers would most likely be reptilian in nature, or most of them would at least because of this. If they served you well you would bestow upon them blessings of bounty whether it came in the form of crops, eggs, or riches and make sure those wishes came true. Those who would defy you would be punished, regressed into an egg and reborn from your infinite womb and mothered by you so that they learn the error of their ways and went on.

    After all of this, you confirmed the goddess after giving her a name Beratha, before looking towards the modes of play as mentioned earlier. There was online Multiplayer Mode, which looked like a sort of survival mode against other gods and goddesses, where you would start with very little renown, glory, and wealth, but you would use you would use yourself and followers to spread your influence and gain power. There was a tutorial level it seemed like too about waking up in a poorly governed village and using your godly powers and influences to revolt against the corrupted mayor and claim the village as your own through the use of a lone follower. That seemed fun, and you could team up with other gods and goddesses, but what about the creative mode?

However there were no tutorials, no challenges, and no other gods or goddesses to interact with. Though you could most certainly create the most ideal world for yourself, and thrive off of that alone, who needs other gods or goddesses if you could just have peace with your very own world?
