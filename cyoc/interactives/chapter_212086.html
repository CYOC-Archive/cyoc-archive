    Meet the other kids

    Previous chapter

    added by MisterDriller 8 months ago O

    You push the door open and peer in to the dim room. There are walls of square white shelving lining the room. A few thick curtains on the far wall probably cover a window. You can see the flash of lightning on the other side of it, the boom of nearby thunder making you jump a little. Looking down you see carpet with large cartoon animals woven in to the bright forest pattern. In the center of the room on the floor sits a few more people, Sonia the ghost hovers quietly near them.

    You are suddenly struck with a sense of embarrassment, a sensation that there are strangers here and you feel so shy. Another artifact of the strange age regression you seem to be experiencing, and you push the feeling down. You are an adult man. Then you absent mindedly pull at the front of your big crackly diaper... ok an adult man in a diaper and a onesie in a haunted daycare listening to a story that is pretty horrible, and it's only half over.

    One of the people sitting in the circle turns and looks at you. You see his eyes, he looks tired, but unsurprised by your presence or what you are wearing. You take a few steps forward, still walking awkwardly in the bulk of your new outfit.

    "Come on over" you hear from Sonia, and she smiles and motions you to the circle. "Meet the other boys here."

    You waddle your way to the circle, the one who looked at you is still staring at you. There are two more, looking up at Sonia. An Asian guy, who isn't wearing anything but a blue diaper sits cross legged on a fuzzy looking blanket. He looks to be in his 20s like you. Between his legs is a scruffy looking teddy bear he has one hand on in a protective way. He glances up at you, sensing your scrutiny. He seems to not care at all about you, and looks away again. The other two are white boys, also about your age. They sit close together and each have on tshirts that are slightly too small, with big grinning dinosaurs on them. One is wearing a baseball cap with a cute puppy face looking over the brim, the other has thick blonde hair and a piercing blue eyes that meet yours and then look away quickly. Both are also diapered, looks like pull-ups with stretchy sides clearly visible as they lounge on a few pillows.

    "Come on in. Ok boys. I'm gonna pick up where I left off with our scary story ok?" She looks directly at you, and you nod, lowering yourself to the floor and gathering a blanket up to clutch in your hands. Shouldn't you feel cold? You realize you don't. The other three in the circle don't seem to care they are naked either. Well, mostly naked. "The rest of you have had your stories so you get to hear the end of his ok?" Sonia gestures at you, and the others nod. The Asian guy mumbles out "Ok sure." but the others just grunt their approval.

    "There are three boys left in the group and I was having a little bit of fun. So as you know I had to turn the last three in to diapers" and to illustrate her point the small pack of six diapers hovers it's way over to float next to her. "Now they were in the lobby and I wanted to split them up so I could take my time and make sure they learned a proper lesson about being bad guests in my workplace...."
