    Dinner and another game

    It wasn't long after the girls had dressed Alex that they heard the arrival of Maddie's father Henry and older brother James. James was two years older than the friends and Veronica secretly had a crush on him but alas for her he had a girlfriend of his own age.

    Almost immediately Molly called out; 'Girls! Dinner is ready come down while it's nice and hot'

    'I don't feel like eating. I'll just stay in here' suggested Alex

    'Don't lie Alex!' scolded Maddie 'You're always hungry. You heard what Mum just said. She thinks you are a girl and thinks you have always been a girl. I bet Dad thinks the same. The game must alter reality. Only those playing it are aware of the changes'

    'So you don't think James will notice that I'm flat?' asked Veronica.

    'He will think you and Sasha are just late developers'

    'Well I'll have dinner in that case. It certainly smells nice' Veronica just wanted the opportunity to perve at James.

    It did smell nice. Alex was hungry, in the end he trusted that Maddie was correct and that her family would not think anything odd about how Alex was dressed.

    They didn't. Maddie's Dad just said 'Hello girls' when they entered. Her Mum made the comment 'That top looks nice on you Alexandra. Why don't you take this seat next to me'

    Alex did get attention from James as he entered then took the seat Maddie's Mum had suggested. James said 'Hi there Alexandra' but ignored the girls. It was a bit weird. Alex didn't get it and felt very self conscious. James had never spoken to Alex in his life. James was looking at him but not making eye contact. It was if he was looking at Alex's chest rather than his face.

    Dinner was tasty. And it did seem that Maddie's parents and brother did think Alex was a girl and had always been a girl. He began to wonder whether that was the case with his own parents as well. Would they currently be thinking that their "daughter" was at Maddie's place?" This was so weird. Would he go home to find his room painted pink and with boy band posters on the wall?

    With dinner finished Molly said 'You go back to your games girls. James can help do the cleaning up tonight'

    It was a relief to all to get back to Maddie's room and the host shut her door and turned to her friends. 'That was so strange. They really thought Alex was a girl. Did you seem how James's eyes couldn't get enough of looking at Alex's chest? I think he has the hots for you Alexandra'

    'That is so weird' said Sasha. 'He didn't seem to care that Alex has whiskers, a boy's hair cut and his voice is cracking. All he seems to care about is that Alex has huge knockers'

    'He totally ignored me. It was as if I wasn't there' complained Veronica

    'That's because James is shallow and crass' said Maddie 'It doesn't help that currently you are flat and have no hips'

    'I need them back. We need to play another game. I'm calling it now "I curse you all to a body part bet"'

    Maddie was a bit upset that Veronica had usurped her right to be host. 'Hey that's my job!'

    'Does it matter? I think we should just go back to basics and bet the same body parts we started with. You and Sasha bet your hair. I'll bet my finger and Alex can bet his penis finger. And yes Alex if I win, I will ask for your penis shaped finger. And we just play dice again. Agreed?'

    'You have to say what the winner will get' pointed out Maddie

    'Okay the winner get's first choice of body part. Second gets second choice and third gets what's left. Okay?' No one immediately said anything so Maddie tossed. The dice landed with a double 6. 'Looks like I will be the winner'

    Maddie then tossed a 1 and a 2. Sasha tossed 4 and 5 then finally Alex tossed 4 and 2.

    'As I'm the winner and I'm true to my word. I claim Alex's penis shaped finger'

    'Ask for it to be on your groin' added Alex.

    'I don't want it on my groin. Your can add that when you win it back next game. Sasha you were second. Choose your prize'

    'I choose my hair' But the changes happened before Alex could claim his prize

    'That wasn't meant to happen!' exclaimed Veronica

    'Why what's happened? Oh!' Mid sentence Alex realised he now had long blonde hair 'That was Sasha's wish not mine' He looked at his hand. The penis finger was gone but it was now replaced by Veronica's finger. He still had 6 fingers on his left hand. They did a stock take of the most recent changes. Veronica had the penis finger, Sasha seemed unchange,d while Maddie now had Alex's short light brown hair.
