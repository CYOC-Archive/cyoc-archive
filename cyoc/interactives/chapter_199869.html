    added A year ago AR AP Toddler

    “Gina said that the potty was in here,” someone said. Leah looked over. A woman she didn’t know had entered with a toddler boy, wearing only training pants, who looked a bit older than Leah currently was. “Oh, you must be the little girl that Gina’s watching!” the woman said to Leah. Leah merely nodded silently. “Well, maybe Darren here can demonstrate how to potty like a big kid.”

    “Eileen,” whined the toddler boy. Eileen looked down at Darren, visibly frustrated.

    “Shh!”

    “She’s too little to blow our cover.”

    “Fine. What do you want?”

    “If you’re going to insist I use a toddler potty, could you at least give me some privacy?”

    “Fine,” Eileen sighed. “But you’ll show off your potty skills for the girl Gina’s watching.”

    “Fine,” Darren grumbled. Eileen left the room, closing the door behind her. Darren sprinted for the toddler training potty by the toy chest. He pulled his training pants down and sat on the potty, letting out a sigh of relief. As he peed, he looked over at Leah. “Stupid baby,” he muttered.

    “Don’t call that,” Leah snapped. “I s’pposed to be adult.” Darren’s eyes widened.

    “Wait. Are you like me? You put on some clothes, someone said you looked a different age, and then you turned that age?” he whispered. Leah nodded. Darren’s eyes widened further. “Are you still wearing the clothes?” Leah nodded again. “Cool. Then you look my age.”

    Leah felt a shiver spread across her body as she began to grow up again. When she stopped, her swim diaper had become much thinner.

    “Thanks,” Leah said gratefully.

    “No problem. Since I got you upgraded to training pants from diapers, maybe you could help me out.”

    “What do you want?”

    “I agreed to be a toddler for a weekend to try to convince my wife Eileen to have a kid with me. But it’s been a month and she’s refusing to turn me back. If I can convince her to have a playdate with you, think you could turn me back to an adult?”

    “Sure.”

    “Great!” Darren got off the potty, wiped himself with a nearby baby wipe, and then pulled his training pants back up. “I’ll be right back.” Darren left the room. Leah continued to sit on the bed nervously, waiting for him to return. Once Darren was back to normal, he’d make her normal too, so there was a lot riding on this.

    After what felt like ages, Darren returned. He was still wearing only training pants.

    “Why don’t you have any clothes?” Leah asked. Darren scowled.

    “Eileen won’t let me wear any. I think it’s a domination or power thing or something.” He shook his head. “Anyways, I talked to Eileen and Gina, and they agreed to a playdate. We can both get back to normal.”

    “Great!” Leah clapped her hands. “Where’s the playdate going to be?”
