    Fitting right in

    added 4 years ago A S

    A bright light covers and blinds you. You feel a warm tingling sensation all over your body.

    When you open your eyes you feel incredibly dizzy and strange. You are no longer in the water and are standing on a rock jutting out of the marsh. You open your eyes and see Keith the gator standing over you. He is much bigger than you. He is towering over him and your eyesight is at about his knee level.

    You hold out your hands and look at them. They are covered in wet fuzzy brown fur. Your new hands are dark brown clawed hands. You feel your face and feel a new nose and furry face. You look behind you to see a beaver tail.

    He turned you into a beaver. You try to call out to him to change you back but only strange animal noises come out. You can’t communicate.

    “Aww isn’t that cute, he doesn’t like being changed into a cute little beaver.” His tone is condescending, which only makes you more angry.

    There’s not a whole lot you can do. You’re fate is in his hands. You have to pray he’ll change you back. You don’t want to live as a beaver. You try to speak again, but still nothing gets across.

    “I’ll admit this isn’t what I was trying to do. I was hoping for a crocodile.” He gives you a pat on the head. “That’s why I’m going to test a bit on you before I change myself back.” He says with a sinister grin on his face.

    Without warning he takes a few steps back and fires the keys at you again
