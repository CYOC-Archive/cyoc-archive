    The Hypnotist's Coin: Horsing Around With Ally

    I had spent the next 30 minutes or so killing time watching online videos. During that time, I had thought to myself about what to do with this little coin.

    There was a knock on the door. “I finished mowing the lawn. You wanted me to see you again?”

    While Ally had apologized for her teasing, I still wanted to get just a little more payback. She been a pain for years, and one mowing wasn’t gonna make up for that on its own. Now what would be fitting?

    Oh, I know! She’d made fun of me for my secret hobbies, so making her act out one of her secret hobbies would get a good laugh.

    “Yea, sis, I’d like you to take a look at this again.” I wound up the coin and swung it in front of her.

    As soon as she saw it, her eyes glazed over again. “Yes Kyle.”

    “I know how much you love horses. You like to pretend it was just a phase, but we both know you still love them.”

    Ally nodded.

    “Well, I have some news for you! You’re not just gonna ride a horse, you’re gonna be a horse! You’re starting to turn into a horse right now! You’re gonna be stuck on all fours and not able to talk until I- Ahhh!” I gasped as I saw my sister’s fingers begin to actually merge together, the nail growing larger, wider, and thicker, becoming a genuine hoof! I stumbled back in shock, dropping the coin, but Ally remained entranced and got on all fours, even managing to keep her legs straight while doing it. I saw her body growing larger, her clothing stretching over an increasingly large frame.

    “Wait! Wait! You’re becoming human again!” I rushed to correct myself, before her clothing started to tear. I breathed a sigh of relief as she stood up again and her hands returned to normal, her torso shrinking back to normal size. I was freaked out. Who wouldn’t be? I just thought I’d make her act like a horse for a bit, I wasn’t expecting a real transformation.

    But, when I caught my breath, I thought about the new possibilities this opened up. Not only could I control people’s minds with this, but their bodies too! I’d had an interest in anthros for a while now. Maybe I could make some real ones with this. It was worth a shot.

    “When I snap my fingers, you’re going to start changing again, but this time, instead of becoming a normal horse, you’ll become a horse girl. You’ll want to take off your shoes when the change starts, because your feet will become hooves. Short fur will grow all over your body, a hairy tail will grow above your butt, your hair will become a mane, and your head and neck will change to a horse’s but you’ll still be able to walk on two legs and talk.” I paused, and thought of something else to keep her from drawing too much attention. “Also, you’ll find the fact that you’ve changed into a horse normal, and won’t freak out or want to go back to human until I reverse it.”

    I snapped my fingers.

    Ally removed her shoes, and her toes began to merge together, the nails fusing and thickening into a large, black hoof. The rest of her foot grew longer, the heel lifting far off the ground, becoming so thin it was hard to tell it used to be her foot, rather than an extension in her leg. Her thighs and calves each became a bit shorter, but still left her taller overall. Figures my first experiment here would make her even taller than me.

    I watched black fur grow all over her feet, traveling under her pants. I couldn’t see much changing clearly under her clothes, but I soon saw a short, black growth stick out from between her shirt and pants, which quickly grew thousands of long, black hairs. Which swished idly behind her.

    I saw more black fur travel up her arms, her nails getting a little thicker, but otherwise not changing much. Her neck was covered by the spread of fur as well, getting a little thicker, while her face began to stretch and swell. Her ears rose toward the top of her head becoming taller and thinner. Her jaws stretched forward dramatically, her nose getting pulled along with it, taking a dark color as it became far larger. Her teeth flattened and clustered toward the front of her mouth. Her eyes, to my surprise, weren’t changed that much.

    The change finished off with her hair growing subtly longer, and traveling down the back of her neck. Her head wasn’t quite as big as a real horse’s, and managed to look kinda proportional to her human-sized body. It seemed to have worked perfectly.

    “Say ‘I am deeply hypnotized,’” I told her.

    “I am deeply hypnotized,” she said, her voice sounding a little more like a whinney, but still recognizably hers.

    “Take two steps forward, then back.”

    She clopped forward easily on her new hooves, and stepped back just as effortlessly. So, it actually made her into a real, functional anthro. This was awesome! I’d need to keep her out of sight until I changed her back so people wouldn’t freak out, but as long as she was under my control, that wouldn’t be too hard. Now, what to do next?
