She recognized the grizzled animal keeper, but the woman was new. She wore a teacher's outfit with a worn brown scarf. Her outfit had seen better days. The teacher then happily clapped her hands as she looked over Hanna. Hanna noted that her left arm was damaged, marked with heavy scars. The hand had been replaced with some kind of mechanical substitute.

    "Hello, I’m Professor Crow. I’m the Mythical Animal instructor.”

One you must all accept if you chose this trade. I have no regrets. This wound is a reminder that you must treat all animals with care. And, of course, now I have this cool metal replacement Professor Henthral kindly made for me.”

    "Come on, girl. No one will hurt you. We just want to get a better look at you," said Professor Crow reassuringly.

They must eat to survive and kill to eat. Would you view a tiger or lion as 'evil' for killing a gazelle? Of course not! They are just obeying their natural instincts," said Professor Crow.
