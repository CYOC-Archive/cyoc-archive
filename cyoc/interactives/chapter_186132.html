    Suzan was awoken by her alarm, and something in her brain tried to flex something to smack the alarm button. Her brain however being sleepy and trying to unconsciously perform a somewhat similar action in her dragon form made her open her mouth towards the alarm...

    Shawna was up early making some breakfast that was Suzan's favorite. A lot was going on, and she figured something nice and familiar was something her daughter would need. The bacon was about ready so she took the heat off the pan and started putting the strips on some paper towels to drain the excess grease... There was an explosion from Suzan's room and Shawna immediately ran and barged into Suzan's room exclaiming "Are you alright Suzan!?!". Then she saw a mini-blue dragon yawning towards a smoking pile of slag. "Suzan?..." The small blue dragon stretched it's wings and then got a good look of the one it considered to be mom. Then it seemed to morph into a more humanoid form yet still covered in scales.
    As the dragon girl's scales started dissolving into flesh covered in soft pajamas she said "Morning... Sorry, didn't realize I could do that..."
    Shawna sighed and said "I'll clean it up, and send the bill to your mother... I made some bacon and eggs."
    "Ah! Thanks a lot Mom!" Suzan said as she jumped a bit hugging her mom. Shawna was expecting to be tackled hard, but as she realized it Suzan had shrunk herself considerably to hug her. Then she grew back to her normal height as she left for the kitchen to get her breakfast before going to school.

    Megan was waiting at the bus stop like usual, and then saw Suzan walking out of her front door with her backpack... but she was in her pajamas. Megan asked "Forget to dress yourself?"
    "Huh?" Suzan wondered and then looked at herself, "Oh, forgot they looked like this... one sec." and then Megan saw the pajamas morph into a cute pink shirt and a light blue skirt. Little did Megan know that Suzan left what was underneath the skirt all dragony for those who dared peak underneath. It's not like she was particularly hiding the fact that she was a dragon anymore.
    "You going all out with the transformation magic?"
    "Kinda... I was subconsciously hiding myself my whole life... I'm just going to use my natural dragon abilities like you'd use your lungs to breathe or hands to manipulate stuff."
    "You gonna fly to school?"
    "...going to wait till I've done some gym classes... I still need to get good with moving in that form."

    In the bus ride some of the students going just didn't care, and others just kinda looked and stared at the girl the rumors were saying was a full blown dragon. Walking to her first class Suzan got a few looks, but there wasn't enough time for anyone to really get a conversation going. However during classes whenever there were partners people were a lot more interested in trying to be her partner. Her issues really started when lunch started. Megan and Suzan were just eating their lunch like usual, and then some people came around asking if they could eat their lunch with the two... Then a bit of a crowd came here and there asking Suzan all sorts of questions, and whether or not she could change into her dragon form.
    Suzan smirked a bit and playing around a bit decided to change her clothes a bit. Her skirt into jeans, her whole ensemble into a dress, etc. Then decided to just change her body a bit into slightly draconic. The fake clothes were on, but there was no soft flesh visible. just blue scales, wings, and draconic features on a humanoid figure. Then a staff member came reminding Suzan there was a no magic policy. Since they were outside Suzan felt a little vindictive and said "fine, no magic." as she was undoing her transformation magic and became a relatively large dragon in the corner of the courtyard. Despite having accidentally breathed lightning she still hadn't intuited any telepathy though. That got her in a bit of trouble and sent to the principal's office where she got a warning since no one was harmed or damaged, and they settled on letting her use transformation magic for her clothes and human form... and if she were in danger she could use her humanoid dragon form. Then at her most important class of the day for now the Harpy Stacy had Suzan start to try to run in her dragon form... It was more of a gallop though.

    In general it was a good day and Suzan for sure felt more like herself... whatever that realistically meant as the little human known as Suzan who used to be so reserved and so out of tune with her actual self that she may as well have been a different person. The dragon in human flesh took out her phone and texted her mother "Will go to the spa"
