    "Oh come on!" she said "This can't be for real!" "It is intreiguing however." She put it down and went to straighten up, change her clothes, attempting to forget the crazy vials. Finally as she was passing with the laundry she set it down and said, "I have to know if this is for real!" She grabbed the mouse vial for no other reason than it was on the end. She looked it over and decided to take a light sip. In fact she even got a shot glass down so she could measure the size drink. "Just a shot of whisky." she told herself for courage as she downed the glass. "Not bad!" She waited a minute with no changes and said "Hrumph! Just as I thought, bogus!" So she went to finish the laundry. She was sorting the colors and whites, making piles when she noticed the washer seemed higher. She dropped in a load not giving it much thought and turned to start the washer, she had to step on her tippy toes to turn the knob. Her legs were lifting off the ground as she hunched on the filling machine. She jumped back in fright and ran to the bathroom where she attempted to see her reflection but the mirror was too high now. She climbed on the sink and looked at herself. She looked the same but she was shrinking! "Oh no!" she said, "That stuff does work!" "CRAP!" She jumped off the sink noticing it took along time to drop as she was now about 4 ft tall. She ran to the box of Vials hoping for something to cancel the effects. "I cant be a mouse!" she said. fumbling through she found the human vial and decided to take a shot of that. She did so but continued to shrink. She was losing the battle with her clothes, pulling and tying to keep them on. Soon she was 3 ft tall and running around looking in mirrors, waiting for this to stop and for her to get back to normal. Suddenly with no other plans coming to her tiny mind she opened the fridge and pulled out some cheese and nibbled a bit, by now she was 2' tall "Why cant I stop eating?" "Oh God!" she said and kealed over as a pain hit her insides, she began growing fur all over her dimishing body. Her clothes could no longer stay on when she reached a foot tall and soon she was covered with gray fur. "Oww, owww" she exclaimed as a tail grew from her spine. She looked it over and began licking it clean! This took a couple minutes, then she turned to the cheese and continued eating as her ears grew, then wiskers began poking out of her puffing lip. Sharp incisors poked through her lips and she grew tiny claws on her hands and feet. She shrunk to about 4 inches high but remained human in apperance because she had taken the dose from the Human vial. She was a very attractive 4 inch anthro female mouse with big ears, wiskers, a tail and full cover gray and white fur. She had a huge piece of cheese. She appears to have forgotten what had happened to her, just happy to have all that food and a warm place to live. I wonder if she is going to snap out of it, as she scampered under the couch with her prize.
