    Minutes passed as you lay on the bed, admiring your current half-snake form. While very appealing, your mind was already wandering towards what to do next.

    While calling a friend, one that you knew you’d be able to trust over was one option, the idea of heading outside as you were, and checking to see if you really could make anyone who happened to see you forget about it was something that was to be tested if you were to really make use of your new ability.

    Speaking of which, testing it out some more looked to be the most sensible option at this time. As well as checking to see how far you could go, to make certain that you could change back.

    Making the mental image in your mind, you soon feel the base of your spine extending, pushing down. Looking down, you see the reason for this, a tail is now growing from it, still quite thin and ending by the time it reaches your ankles. You can’t help but give a hiss of contentment at your control over your transformative abilities.

    For the testing purposes though, it’s not enough and already you are pushing it further, feeling your legs begin to shrivel and contract. Your slitted eyes see them exiting the boots as they become stumps before completely pulling into your body as the tail expands, thickens to match your torso, and grows considerably in length. Your penis too, has pulled into a genital slit as you rise off the bed, ditching the overcoat as you do so and drag yourself to the mirror.

    While the upper half of yourself is how it was before, the lower half it very much that of a snake, albeit a very large one, your internals having adjusted to accommodate this new body you now possess.

    It takes a bit of getting used to, but you find that the scales can be worked in a way to permit motion, though moving around like that is slow as you continue your practice. You try using your long tail to move by bunching up the rear of it and pushing with the forward part of it, while a jerkier means, it allows you to move much more quickly than before. Some more practice later and you feel you’ve gotten the hang of it.

    Now came the next big test, summoning your courage, you make the image and wait. It’s a short wait as the arms pull into your chest, which itself is becoming narrower as your neck widens to match your head which becomes longer, but shorter in height. You can feel yourself changing more and more, as the colours fade, and your eyes adjust to seeing things in the Infra-Red Spectrum. Not long after and your entire body is resting on the floor, now in the form of a Boa Constrictor.

    You are quick to discover that thanks to the earlier practice, getting around is simple, more so now that you have a lot more surface area on the ground to propel yourself with. But you knew you had to change back soon, if only so you could proceed with these tests.

    As before, you picture yourself in your mind, and the legs and arms return, allowing you to push yourself up to kneeling as your tail recedes into your spine as the scales are replaced by your old flesh, hair returning as do your nails.

    Now back to your usual human self, you find that you are kneeling naked in the lounge, getting up you return to your bedroom as you ponder the recent events. Having proven to yourself that you could change yourself into any form (well, just that one for now) and then back to normal, most of the objectives you’d set yourself had now been completed. All that was left to do, really, was to see if you could choose to make people forget it if they saw it, and it would be complete. The only question with regards to that being, just how to do it?
