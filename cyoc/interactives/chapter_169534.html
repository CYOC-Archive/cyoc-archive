    As Jaymee went about finding a new outfit, something new happened in the depths of her subconscious. What was once a black void shifted to that of an otherwise well-kept room, complete with furnishing and wallpaper. Situated at the center table were two young women, one bearing an athletic physique, the other a slightly more obese build. Upon seeing one another for the first time, both women were surprised...

    "Are you?!" Jamie sputtered out. Nerd Jamie just smiled.
    "I am thou...thou art I." Jamie was confused,
    "Um...what?" Nerd Jamie chuckled.
    "I've alwayth wanted to thay that, heh"
    "Whatever! Where are..." Jamie paused upon realizing she no longer had a lisp. Jumping up, she cheered. Her joy only intensified upon seeing her attire. A red T-shirt, A pair of FILA sweatpants, and regular sneakers. No more nerd clothes! Nerd Jamie, in contrast, was wearing the same blouse, slacks, and loafers from before. Once the excitement wore off, Jamie returned to her seat and asked.

    "Where are we anyway?" Nerd Jamie pointed to her right and in that direction were two great TV screens, both of which were showing what looked to be someone rummaging a closet for clothes. It didn't take a genius to figure out who it was.

    "Is that?" Nerd Jamie confirmed it. "Yeth, it'th the clown...Jaymee." She continued. "If I were to take a gueth I'd thay we're in a cognitive reality of her thubconcious. Thince we're not piloting the 'main character', we've been thent to the waiting lobby until we're either A.) Called upon to or B.) Wretht control of it from her."
    "So? What do we do now?" Nerd Jamie just shook her head.
    "At this point? Nothing. Leatht I can think anything. Our only hope at thith point is the mirror..."

    "BINGO!"

    Jaymee held up a custom sewn blue silk dress. Her vaunted "Clown Princess" number! It was a personal favorite of hers and fitting for the occasion since her "prince charming" was coming over! Grabbing the rest of the outfit, Jaymee rushed over to the mirror. The moment she matched eyes with her reflection, the room was engulfed in bright light.

    Nerd Jamie was staring at her reflection, back in her old room and in her own body. Did the mirror switch realities? Suffice to say, this was her chance! Grabbing a sheet from her bed, she threw it over the mirror. She had long surmised the thing was possibly sentient, but if it can't see what's coming to it, she might have a chance. Running to the closet, Nerd Jamie grabbed a hammer and was all but prepared to smash the damn thing to pieces. But before she could, an insidious ringing echoed in her mind...

    "hehehehehehe....hahahahahaha....AHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!!!!!!!"

    The heck? Focusing with all her might, Nerd Jamie was able to glimpse back into the cognitive room...

    And what she found shocked her.

    All the furnishing and the decor had changed to circus-like variations and normal Jamie was on the floor, laughing uncontrollably. Before her, was Jaymee, dancing and giggling like her usual loony self. Pulling something from her pocket, Jaymee showed it to Jamie. It was a red clown nose.

    "I can make you even funnier! *hyuck* *hyuck* Jutht put this baby on!"

    Popping the nose on her, Jamie instantly grew a one-piece star-patterned red jumpsuit with ruffled neck and wrist collars. A pair of big floppy shoes also sprouted from her feet. Squeezing her nose, several honks were heard, causing Jamie's laughter to increase tenfold. It got to the point that tears were coming from her eyes. Nerd Jamie knew she wouldn't be able to hold on much longer.

    "Being thilly is fun! *hyuck* Once you get a tathte you won't wanna thop!" Jaymee cackled.
