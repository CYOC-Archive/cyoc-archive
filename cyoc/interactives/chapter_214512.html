    “Life is like a box of chocolates. You never know what you’re gonna get.”
    — Forrest Gump

    It was Valentine’s Day, and you were panicking.

    You promised your girlfriend weeks ago you would get one big box of chocolates for the two of you to share. But like an idiot you waited until the absolute last minute. Now it was Valentine’s Day and everywhere was sold out. Every store was all out. Eventually you gave up the search.

    Not wanting to arrive home empty handed, you went to an odd antique store run by a very sexy woman in a black dress and witch’s hat in the hopes of finding a necklace or something she would like.

    However as fate would have it. The store happened to also have one heart shaped box of chocolates left. You immediately bought it and went back to you and your girlfriend’s shared apartment.

    However unknown to you, you had bought the Chocolate Box of Change. Every single chocolate inside having a different effect on the person who ate it. One chocolate might shrink you. While another gives you animal ears. Or changes your gender. The effects also stack.

    For example: If you ate one that turned you into an anthro cat, one that changed your gender, then another that changes your race, and one that shrank you. You would become a mini black anthro cat girl. Also if the box is empty and you put the lid back on then take it off it will magically refill. Some chocolates the same, some different. Some the same chocolate with different effects. You never know what you’re gonna get…

    Who eats the candy first, and what change do they get?
