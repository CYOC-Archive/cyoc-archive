    Author note:
    This interactive is an edited-together version of a really old roleplay between some friends and I a ways back. I've been meaning to put it up here for a long time, and will update it as regularly as I can... But that doesn't mean my way will be the only one.

    Six young men become the only survivors of a mysterious plane crash in the Caribbean Sea. They are already friends...some of them are family...and had been planning on a raucous spring break to distract one of their number from a bad breakup. They get a distraction, alright...but nothing like the one they were expecting.

    NICK
    Age: 22
    Build: Trim, well-built, but not buff
    Hair: Brown and curly.
    Eyes: Brown.
    Personality: Brooding, loyal, takes things fiercely to heart. Loves his friends.
    Clothes: Green tee-shirt, cargo shorts, black sneakers. No socks.
    Bio: Nick is recently coming off a bad break-up. This trip is an attempt by his frat brothers to get him out of his funk. He's mostly humoring them, and doesn't expect any bold revelations.
    ---
    JOEY
    Age: 25
    Build: Tall and thin, but thickening a little in the middle thanks to a sedentary lifestyle.
    Hair: Short and brown.
    Eyes: Brown. He wears glasses.
    Personality: Finnicky, stuffy, something of a hypochondriac. Loathes the culture of 'maleness'.
    Clothes: Blue polo shirt, black canvas shorts, black crew socks, white sneakers.
    Bio: Joey is Nick's older brother, and is dating the sister of one of his frat brothers. He's a freelance writer and usually can't be bothered with the college shenanigans of his brother and his friends, though he's been convinced to go on this vacation for Nick's sake. And, if nothing else, he loves his brother.
    ---
    MIKE
    Age: 22
    Build: Tall and lanky.
    Hair: Black and wavy.
    Eyes: Blue
    Personality: Sardonic, wry, sometimes insufferably intelligent. Aloof and high-minded.
    Clothes: Black tee-shirt. Jeans. Ankle boots.
    Bio: Mike is one of Nick's frat brothers. He's premed, and enjoys reminding people about it. While not necessarily full-of-himself, Mike IS a genius, and he's proud of it. He was eventually convinced to go on this vacation after discovering a bit of trivia about one of the islands in the wider archipelago, not that any of the others have paid his lecturing any mind.
    ---
    FRANKIE
    Age: 22
    Build: Short, but well-muscled.
    Hair: Black crewcut.
    Eyes: Hazel
    Personality: Fun-loving, "boy among boys", reckless, adventurous, something of a lech. Is attractive and knows it.
    Clothes: Some teeshirt for a band nobody's ever heard of. Black shorts. White crew socks. Black sneakers.
    Bio: Another of Nick's frat brothers. Frankie is a hard-partying, womanizing jackass who has nevertheless endeared himself to, well, MOST of the group. The vacation was his idea, and is paid for by his (well, his parents') money, so nobody's complaining.
    ---
    LUCAS
    Age: 22
    Build: Broad-shouldered and heavily muscled, the most of the group.
    Hair: Blond and wavy
    Eyes: Green
    Personality: Bright, friendly, a team player through and through.
    Clothes: Gray teeshirt. Red basketball shorts. White crew socks. White sneakers.
    Bio: Another of Nick's fraternity brothers. Star QB for their college's football team, Lucas is an upstanding guy and is easy to get along with. Nick's brother Joey has recently started dating HIS older sister, which he isn't exactly cool with (Joey is kind of lame), but he loves Nick like a brother, so it all works out.
    BEN
    Age: 21
    Build: Stocky. Slightly overweight.
    Hair: Sandy brown.
    Eyes: Brown
    Personality: Withdrawn, timid, wary of speaking his mind.
    Bio: The last of Nick's frat brothers, and the only one not graduating this year. Ben is a wallflower, and only joined the frat because Nick vouched for him at initiation. He thinks highly of Nick (though he's on his girlfriend's side when it comes to their breakup, not that he'll ever tell) and was taken aback to even be included on the trip in the first place.
    ---
