    The Sundew Trap

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover A month ago S Shrinking Magic

    "Let me go! Let me go and change me back!" you shout out in fright and anger.
    "Come on! Try to enjoy the moment! It'll be fun!" says the fairy, laughing as if nothing was wrong and as if what you're feeling is totally invalid.
    "NO! Just change me back! I don't want to go flying with you! I don’t want to be tiny! Or as short as you! Just put me back on the ground and return me to my original size! And give me my clothes back! Now!" you shout back.
    "You are SO ungrateful!" admonishes the fairy. "A lot of humans would love being in my company like that, flitting about as if on a ride with such a pretty fairy like me!"
    "I want to not be naked! So release me!"
    You are so angry that you struggle. It works as the fairy loses her grip on your wrists and you both stumble through the air. She shouts in frustration and the world spins around you, as much as you both spin, madly so, the fairy still above you, it seems, though top and bottom appear to be seriously off.
    SPLAT!!!
    You feel that you collide with something somewhat soft but sticky. Gooey. Ozzy. With lots of tendrils that secrete some sort of glue. You got stuck on your side and you realize that you've smacked into a giant sundew plant. And the more you try to get yourself out of it, the worse it gets, sticking yourself more and more.
    "You've hit a sundew plant! Good thing it wasn't me!" says the fairy, flapping at the periphery of your vision.
    "Thanks for your empathy. Now get me out! Help me!" you mutter, everything made worse by the fact that you are naked through this horrible experience.
    "I can't. The secretion is too sticky. I don't have the strength. Not on my own. But don't worry. It won't eat you. It prefers insects."
    "Great! So what am I supposed to do?" you shout in humiliation.
    "Well, these plants are used by fay trappers to capture fairies and sell them. They come by regularly to check what the sundews have trapped. They are disgusting ogres and gobelins that..."
    She turns her head as she hears stomps and vegetation moving outside of your field of vision.
    "Oh no! Here comes one now! I don't want to get caught!"
    She dashes away, leaving you to your fate. You see a goblin enter your eyesight, all smiles. He saw thje ball of light that was the fairy whisk away and he knew he could get in luck.
    "A transformed human! And the right size too! What luck!" he laughs throatily.
    He plucks you out of the sundew effortlessly and places you in a little cage, which he attaches to his carrying pack. You're still naked and now covered in awful sticky ooze, bouncing and on your way to a fate you're very, very afraid of.

    What do you do now?
