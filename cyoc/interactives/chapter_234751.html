    Lesily had a trick up her sleeves the women who had vanished into nothingness weren't completely gone. There was a special trick at this resort that would let them be brought back... It wasn't particularly cheap, but with the price of admission the resort wouldn't get THAT mad at her if a few people got "disappeared"... Although she was getting around the blurry line that would get the resort admins berating her a bit. An old man using a walking can with some assistance volunteered and... asked to be regressed into nothing.
    "To nothing? I think we've done that trick enough right crowd? How about we try something different like to the prime of your life?" Lesily asked
    "No, I'd like to go in the way of beginnings and not the end." said the somewhat poetic old man. So Lesily performed the trick again on the naked old man who put on a show of his own in a way as he had been an acrobatic in life... and then as the cells dwindled to nothing in her palm again a couple came up.
    "So maybe not to nothing, but can you regress us to unconception. Maybe our sperms will go to the other egg." The dude of the relationship said. Lesily didn't need to put them in a trance, and she was contractually obligated to do these so... She tried to make it seem like they were throwing away their lives and that they wouldn't just grow back like that, but they seemed happy seeing each other regress and making fun of their stuff shrinking. While disturbing it was cute in a way when the fetus' clung to each other when they turned into nothing.
    Lesily warned "Do not try it, without a womb they will not grow and die. Please, let us try a different trick. Any volunteers?" She knew she'd be getting some complaints from the heads upstairs. Gloria raised her hand as she went onstage and Lesily asked "So what is your age related wish?"
    Gloria said "I'd like to be regressed to nothing!" with a smirk on her face as she had noted Lesily's annoyance beneath her stage mask. There was something about the "nothing" trick that probably would keep them alive.
    "There is no trick you know." Lesily stated with a little more annoyance in her voice "They truly did become NOTHING... Well the couple are something, but they aren't alive..."
    "That's fine" Gloria said was a smirk in her tone
    "This is ASSISTED SUICIDE at this point"
    "Ok, I've had a good run. Die young and leave a pretty face or something like that. Not sure about the face but I don't see how you could die younger than going into the negative ages." Lesily gave up and Gloria dwindled and dwindled into "nothing" The crowd seemed to think this was funny and Lesily could tell she was going to get a giant earful from corporate...
