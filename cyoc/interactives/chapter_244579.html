The ten ethereal Heathers in the kitchen begun to clean the floor, wipe a counter with wet wipes, served her the sandwich she just made for herself and acted as Heather’s personal servants as she sat on the chair. She didn’t notice how her head grew in size again. The brain inside Heather’s skull inflated into an unbelievable size as it now begun to resemble the world's largest sultan’s turban. Heather’s gym ball sized cranium made her horny all the time. She took a bite of her sandwich and enjoyed how easy everything felt as she could move any object with her mind. After eating the sandwich she changed the scene by mentally teleported herself back to the kitchen while commanded her body to move to the sofa to sit down. Heather made a mental projection of John’s body in her mind. Then Heather used her telepathic linking to hijack and attach John’s brain waves to the replica body she had just made and soon John was invited to the fantasy land where Heather was the queen of the said land.
