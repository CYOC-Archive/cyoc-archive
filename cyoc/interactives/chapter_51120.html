    "Don't be silly Greta," Anna said putting a hand on Greta's shoulder. "You're a part of the family. I won't have you going anywhere."

    "Thank you," Greta said with tears streaming down her cheeks.

    "I need to see if you can read something," Rick said pulling out the spell book. He turned to a page with what looked like a harmless illusion spell. He showed them the page and asked, "Can you read this?"

    "I can't," Greta said shaking her head.

    "I can't either," Anna said looking puzzled.

    "What is it?" Kayla asked. "Can I see?"

    Rick lowered the book so Kayla could see and asked, "Do you understand anything written in it?"

    "No," Kayla said. "What is it?"

    "It's a spell book," Rick said flicking through the book. "I can understand the writing in it. I'm going to transform myself with one of the spells. How would you like a twin sister Kayla?"

    "I'd like a sister," Kayla said with a smile.

    "Now don't get her hopes up like that," Greta chided.

    After a few minutes Anna managed to say, "Well I guess you need a new name."

    "Of course Mommy," Rick said then giggled. Anna was his mom now so he might as well call her that.

    "Well I think I have the right to give you your new name since I'm your mommy now," Anna said then got a thoughtful look on her face. After a few minutes she said, "From now on your name is Evalynn Sue Shef. You will answer to that Eva."

    "Yes Mommy," Eva said giggling.

    "Now I wonder why you're naked," Anna said in a thoughtful voice.

    "That makes sense," Greta said nodding.

After that we'll go out to eat tonight. Fortunately we have a spare booster seat for you Eva. Now both of you get upstairs."
