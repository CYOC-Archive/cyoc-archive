    Months passed as did the seasons. You were taught some thing like how to write and some basic concepts of math. After your leaves started to fall off after turning completely red you tried fashioning some of your newly growing vines into some sort of coat. Still you ended up being exceptionally cold most of the time and was given a heated blanket (since regular blankets didn't really work since you didn't have any real body heat. Despite having lost most of your plumage a lot of Stella's bees warmed themselves up underneath your blanket. the problem of heat increased as white snow started to fall. While a lot more of the other kids were having fun throwing white ice at each other during recess and molding it you and Stella huddled together in the classroom during recess. Once the weather started getting warmer both you and Stella started perking up a little more and your own body started growing buds. Finally after the cold winter truly ended and the warm sun started coming out and your buds started opening as your blue flowers started to bloom. With your flowers out you couldn't keep Stella off of you as her bees were gathering nectar and spreading pollen around. That said Anna kept her distance since she had some allergies, but you didn't mind Stella's bees gathering around your flowers. The feeling of the little bugs gathering the nectar from your flowers was kind of enjoyable in a way, and made you start feeling tingly on the inside. That tingly feeling was your body gathering the pollen that Stella's bees had gathered and was starting to turn them into fruit which would contain some special seeds. You were also happy that your leaves had completely grown back with their greeny goodness. Regardless when your fruits started to grow you noticed quickly as the fruits were about the size of plums, and while the skin was clear the inner fruit shined a sky blue light. A little concerned you hid the fruits under your leaves and flowers.
    While you had your privacy Stella asked "So have you shown anyone else your fruits? They look nice and tasty, sure most people don't eat glow in the dark fruit, but what possible harm could those little things on you do?"
    you suggested "If you think they taste good why not just eat one?"
    Stella said "Your flowers are good enough, the nectar has been godly... I swear I may never leave you alone if the nectar stays that good."
    some footsteps sounded as your teacher Mrs. Stein came and asked "What are you talking about some sort of fruit?"
    You said "Yes Mrs. Stein. They are kind of odd and don't look like apples or oranges..."
    Mrs. Stein smiled and said "Don't worry too much, You are a good girl and doubt that your fruits would be harmful. Mind if I saw one?" You looked into you and extended a vine which had a few of your fruits on it. A bit surprised Mrs. Stein said "My those look interesting indeed. You may even be able to use them as nightlights. Mind if I tried a bite?"
    You shook your head and said "Take a fruit or two."
    Mrs. Stein said "Don't mind if I do." she plucked two fruits and then ate one of the fruits. With her first bite she struggled a little bit to open up the skin, but as it opened up the flesh of the fruit practically drained into Mrs. Stein. What exactly happened to Stein though?
