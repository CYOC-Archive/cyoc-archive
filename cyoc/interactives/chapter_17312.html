    "Genie," you say loudly, not knowing if he can hear you from where-ever he is. "Genie, I think I'm ready for my next wish...!"

    A tap on your shoulder catches your attention as, spinning around, you spot the grinning rabbit in his pantaloons. "I was hoping you'd call," he says, looking you over appreciatively. Gone is his cigar but you get the impression that he's still quite interested in anything even remotely pleasurable and ... hedonistic. "Got an idea, eh?"

    You nod, your doberman face breaking in to a shy grin at the rabbit-djinn's piercing, blue eyes. "You said you can grant animalistic wishes, right?"

    He nods, crossing his arms over his incredibly muscular chest. "You got that right... It's like a 'domain of power' thing. Anything that can be justified by the concept 'animal' or 'animalistic' I can grant..."

    You nod a bit and continue. "You see, I want to use my second wish wisely, but I'm not sure how to go about it. I'd like to be able to change Todd into a huge, fuckbeast like me, too. But, well, that might be kinda foolish..."

    The rabbit djinn smiles and puts his paws on his hips. "You want some advice?"

    "You'd do that?"

    The djinn nods, his long ears flopping softly. "Well, for the sexy ones, sure..."

    You blush, your ears reddening, as the rabbit continues.

    "Todd's your boyfriend?" he asks, to which you shake your head, "no."

    "Just sorta a -well- fuck-buddy," you admit, still blushing. "We screw around a lot and have some mutual friends like that, too... We have very similar fantasies."

    The genie nods, thinking. "Well, the thing is, you want to make the wishes count, right? Well, if you could -say- wish for some power of your own to make animalistic changes, perhaps you'd end up with just a little bit of leeway..."

    He shoots you a warning look, though, and says, "But most of us genies don't really appreciate greed, you know... Don't go overboard by wishing to have the powers I have or you could get in some trouble! Capiché?"

    You nod in agreement, your mind racing. "Ok, then, well..."

    You think for several minutes, wanting to hurry due to Todd's imminent arrival.

    Finally, you smile and chuckle. "Ok, Genie... What I want is to be able to change people back and forth; making animalistic fuck-beasts with all sorts of sexual proclivities and prowesses whenever I want..."

    The genie laughs out-loud and raises an eyebrow. "Well, I'm not sure that's the correct plural of 'prowess,' but that should seem to be in my ability to grant... But, you have to phrase it as a wish..."

    You nod, and -looking the hot rabbit in the eyes- say, "Genie, I wish that I could have something that would allow me to change people back-and-forth from human to fuck-beast of my choice with all sorts of controllable, sexual characteristics...!"

    The rabbit's buck-toothed grin widens to its fullest as he nods and vanished in a puff of purple smoke, his fading voice echoing as he vanishes. "Granted...!"

    With that, out of thin air, what looks like a necklace materializes and falls to the ground at your feet. Looking down, you see it has a tear-shaped diamond about the size of a walnut, set in its silver frame on a similar chain. Grinning, you pick it up.

    "This'll do it?" you ask the thin air.

    "Put it on; it will grant its wearer the power you asked for..." comes the djinn's echoing voice from everywhere around you.

    However, just then, there comes a knock at the door...
