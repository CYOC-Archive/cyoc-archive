Hearing became an intense sense and every move I made caused a sound, till soon the number and rush of sounds were making me angry.

Odd I thought as one tried to focus on things about but found a blockage. My nose was in the way somehow? Now those hooves touched my face, a face not flat and human. You will do fine and after say a year or so they will return you here for breeding. Once that experiance is done I will reinstate the humanity for the most part." he explained.
