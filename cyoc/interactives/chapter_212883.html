    Amory does not have to wait long, after just 10 minutes he hears rustling in the bushes and soon Eric is back, clutching his phone. The Jaguar sits up and smiles, “I see you got your phone,” he says, “let's get this done!”

    “Hehe, someone seems excited,” Eric chuckles, “so, before I do this: are you 100% sure you want this?”

    Amory nods, “yes,” he says.

    “Okay,” Eric says, he then opens Amory's file and starts typing, “okay, change body from feral to anthro: done! Change age from animal to human: done,” he looks at Amory, “it says here that your current human age would be 70... .”

    “That is way to old,” Amory says, “could you change it to your age?”

    Eric nods, “I can do that,” he says, “I am 15, would you like to be older or a little younger?”

    Amory thinks to himself, 'once I have my hands, I'll be able to change a few things later,' he then says, “make me 16.”

    Eric nods, “okay... done, set duration to permanent and reality alteration on... aaaaand we are ready to confirm the change,” he looks at Amory, “ready?”

    Amory nods, “ready,” he says, “um... will it be painful?”

    Eric shakes his head, “I set the transformation to be painless,” he says.

    Amory smiles, “okay... do it!”

    Eric nods and confirms the transformation.

    Amory suddenly feels his whole body tingling, “that felt weird,” he says.

    Eric nods, “the tingling? Yeah, it does, but at least it is painless,” he says.

    Amory then feels his body start to shift, his bones crack as they reposition themselves, “sounds painful, but it's not,” he says. The Jaguar watches in amazement as his body changes, his front paws slowly look more and more like hands, his front legs look more like arms, his back pops and he senses that he is able to stand upright. Amory then feels himself become younger, the years slipping from him, he smiles up at Eric, “I feel like a young cub again,” he says, “this is amazing!”

    Eric smiles, “I am glad you are happy,” he says.

    Soon the transformation end and Amory carefully gets to his feet, he wobbles a bit and Eric has to hold him.

    “You okay,” Eric asks.

    “Ye-yeah,” Amory says, his legs still shaking, “my body is not used to this new stance,” he adds.

    “Okay, let me know if you want to try walking, I'll stay nearby, in case you loose balance,” Eric says.

    Amory nods, “I think I'll risk it,” he says. Eric lets go of the now 16-year-old Jaguar-Boy and Amory cautiously takes his first step as an anthro, then a second, then a third... soon Amory is walking on two legs as if he always had been. Amory tries to walk faster, then jog and then run, he returns to Eric with a huge smile, “this feels amazing,” he says, he then takes Eric by surprise as he suddenly pulls the other Jaguar-Boy into a hug.

    Eric blushes when he feels their sheaths touch each other, he tries to ignore it as he hugs his friend back.

    Amory then gives Eric a lick on the nose, causing Eric to let out a surprised mrowl and wriggle his nose in a cute way, “wh-what was that for?”

    Amory chuckles, “just saying “thank you”,” he says with a grin.

    Eric blushes, “y-you are welcome,” he says.

    Amory lets go of Eric, “can I have a look at that App, please?”

    “Um... why,” Eric asks.

    “I am just curious,” Amory says, he then leans in closely, “or don't you trust me... cub,” he asks with a grin.

    “Pfff... you are a “cub” now yourself,” Eric says, grinning back and booping Amory's nows lightly with a claw.

    Amory mrowls and wriggles his nose, “no booping,” he says with a pout, they both then laugh.

    “Well,” Eric says, “I guess it'll be okay for you to have a look at the App. I do trust you, I know you wouldn't do anything without asking me first, right?”

    “Of course,” Amory says, “I'd never force you into anything.”

    “Good,” Eric says, smiling, “well, here you go,” he then says, and hands Amory his phone.
