    The longer Jamal had his muzzle, it actually didn't feel so bad. He could feel the saliva-like peanut butter and jelly slide down his throat. It was filling him up and satisfying his hunger. And it felt a bit tingly... Maybe Jamal can eat this way from now on! At least, until he can find a way to reverse this...
    The school lunch bell rang and it was time to go to class. Jamal looked around the hallways as the lunchtime rush left their classrooms to go get their food. The next class after lunch is gym. And since Jamal didn't want anyone seeing his new muzzle, he needed to figure out how to unfuse from the sandwich before that happens.
    Jamal needed someplace to hide where no one would be looking. His eyes fell onto a storage room that was used for P.E equipment.
    After making sure no one saw him, Jamal snuck inside the storage room and shut the door. With a little maneuvering, he turned the lights on. It seems no one is here. Good, the lights are a bit dim in this room so if anyone did see Jamal, they probably won't see what is going on in his mouth. Jamal set his backpack on the floor, and started thinking to himself.
    Just then, the door opened up. Jamal whirled around to see who came in. The last thing he wanted is someone finding out what happened to his face.
    As soon as the student opened the door, a P.E. Ball bounced in through the opening and rolled into a corner of the storage room. As the student came in after the ball, they tripped over something on the floor and stumbled forward. In an attempt to steady themselves, they grabbed a random object they were next to. That object, in this case, was the side of Jamal!
    "SCHWOOP!"
    That very familiar sound reverberated across the storage room. Jamal, his vision a bit fuzzy, rubbed his head with his hands. Once the dust cleared, Jamal could see who the student was that ran into him. Jamal was not prepared to see the familiar figure.
    It was Gen. Jamal had run into his long-time crush. Now... Gen's entire body is fused with a random P.E. ball that she dropped on her way in. Her features are flattened by the surface of the P.E ball, giving her the appearance of a normal P.E. ball with an animated face painted on it. Gen-ball looked around confused, wondering what had happened to her. As soon as Gen-ball noticed Jamal, she started rolling in his direction. "What the hell just happened Jamal!?" Gen-ball screamed as she started bouncing up and down.
    "Calm down Gen." Jamal responded as he caught Gen-ball. Gen-ball kept trying to bounce and wiggled in Jamal's grip. It's actually really cute seeing her get this worked up. She's basically a toy right now. Jamal placed his new toy down on the ground, and asked if he can explain what happened. Gen-ball finally calmed down, and agreed. Jamal explained that he's been cursed with the power to fuse things together. He showed off his sandwich muzzle and told Gen about fusing it with the sandwich by accident. He showed his glove that made the fusing happen.
    "Are you ok, Gen?" Jamal asked. Gen-ball rolled away from Jamal and bumped against the wall. "You're really freaked out by this, aren't you?" Jamal inquired as he bent down to Gen's level. "Look, I'll try my best to reverse this. I promise!" Jamal reassured Gen-ball as he raised his gloved hand to the P.E Ball.
    "SCHWOOP!"
    The suddenness of the fusion shocked Jamal, as well as the noise level. But when Jamal's eyes were back into focus, he was astonished by the new creature that stood before him. Gen-ball has fused with another random P.E. Ball and Gen-ball had become bigger! Not by a whole lot, but enough for Jamal to notice the difference.
    "Woah. It seems fusing more than one object makes them bigger. Cool..." Jamal mused to himself. But Jamal couldn't focus on this new discovery for long, because Gen-ball was already starting to freak out. "What the hell?" She said with an even more frightened expression then before.
    Jamal quickly grabbed onto the Gen-ball before she could run off again. Gen-ball, still not use to her new body, tried to flail her way out of his grip. Jamal tried to hold her steady. "You know," He said, "Fusing with the other ball actually helped me get a better grip on you!" He laughed a bit. But his amusement was not reciprocated, as Gen-ball still tried to flee Jamal's grip. "No!" She screamed. "This is awful! Let go!" She shouted in frustration as Jamal tightened his grip on her. "I promise I'll try to reverse this! You just gotta calm down." Jamal told Gen-ball as he tried to restrain her. "It's ok Gen, you just got bigger!" Jamal shouted in frustration.

    He didn't want her ratting him out. How could he keep her under control?
