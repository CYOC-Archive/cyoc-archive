    Getting out of the tunnels

    added 5 years ago O Mythological Magic

    “Let’s continue onwards, once my business is done here, we can arrange for another session...” Azaviel grinned.

    Exalt nodded, before taking them through the left intersection of the tunnel.

    Then, they simply walked.

    Without any form of transportation, the walk to the center had taken hours. And with time, the two could both tell they were getting closer. The walls of the tunnel were becoming... twisted. The darkness became infinently more so, and all three of the corrupted figures walking through the tunnels all felt a great heavy pressure push on them.

    There was no more talk. Even breathing was becoming an endeavor. So Exalt and Azaviel could only walk in painful silence, as they finally encountered a blink of light far away in the distance.

    Azaviel, Kitty, and Exalt all instinctively move faster, all of them wanting to be free from the darkness.

    To be reduced to such a state... Azaviel thought bitterly to himself as he ran.

    Eventually, upon reaching the light, they saw it came from a set of stairs going up, presumably to the surface.
    The three all felt a soft fresh wind blow downwards through the stairs, giving them some relief although the great pressure still remained.

    “This is it, this stairway should lead up to the central plaza of the city, the grand palace that resides in the center should only be an hour away from there.” Exalt panted, resting against a wall.

    “How do you know all this?” Azaviel asked.

    “I’m a scholar, plus I mainly use the tunnels to move throughout the city, it helps to memorize the routes to key places. “ the latex bat grinned

    “Well... shall we go? I’m expecting that we’ll be hit by the curse at full force once we go up?”

    “Correct, what we are feeling right now is merely a fraction of the curse’s power and unfortunatly there’s no way to defend ourselves from it, not for long anyway.”

    “Well then what can we do?”

    “Simple, we have to ignore it and push past it, I’ll be able to create some magical defense for us, though like I said, it won’t last long.”

    “And there is no other way to get to the center?”

    “Nope.”

    Azaviel sighed. Well, he supposed, this should at least be an experience.

    “Let’s go then...” Azaviel said, Kitty mewling in acceptance.

    Exalt nodded, and with no other choice, the three ascended upwards, all of them fully expecting to see an illusionary hell once they are above.
