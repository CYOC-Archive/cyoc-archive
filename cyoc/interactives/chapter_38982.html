    He was walking down 1st street one day and there was this new shop named "toys, hobbies, and more!". He was curious and walked in, "hi he said to the old shopkeeper, "looking for something youngster?" the guy replied back. "Well... now you mentioned it, do you got something that's interesting and, or fun to play with?". The shopkeeper shook his head and went into the back. He came back with a vary old looking board game with faded words on the box that read: "The Game of Changes, 1-5 players, not intended for ages 7 and below". "hmm... I'll take it how much?" Doug said expecting it to be a ripoff price, "It's nothing, free, the last people that haded only haded one day, then returned it" "ever since then no one would buy it, I put it in the back and forgot it for awhile" "it's soo.. old that I said if the next person that was willing to take it, could have it". Doug was shocked, "Are you sure I can have it for nothing?". "Yes zip..zilch..Nada" The old guy said. "Ok lets see" He dumped the box out onto the kitchen table. After just a few minutes he had set up the board just like in the instructions.
