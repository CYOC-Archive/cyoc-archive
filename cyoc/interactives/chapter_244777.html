    Faunus was happy with his triplet sons, but he could always use more. After all, he ran a farm, and more hands would be useful. So, not long after Will found herself a full woman, Faunus mounted her in bed. All Will could do was hope that her mate was unsuccessful at impregnating her.

    Will and the other frat boys gathered together for the final wish. Will saw that she was thankfully not the only one in a...difficult position. Mark had three eggs in a bag on his back, which he reluctantly confirmed he had laid. Jim was heavily pregnant and nursing a baby satyr from his pecs. His stepdaughter, he said. But Andy and David seemed to have escaped parenthood.

    When the genie appeared, she seemed thrilled by the three parents she saw. Mark used his third wish, to return them to their previous lives. The genie nodded. Andy and David disappeared. But Jim, Mark, and Will were still there.

    "Uh, what happened?" Will asked. The genie giggled.

    "Pregnancy override, sweetie. The three of you have little ones in your wombs, so I can't do any magic on you. It would affect both of you!"

    "What? I'm not-" Mark started. Suddenly, his face turned red. He bent into a squat.

    "Whoa, dude, are you shitting on the ground?" Will demanded. Jim covered the ears of the baby nursing from him.

    "Language," he snapped. Will crossed her arms, pissed. "Honestly, I'm glad that I'm staying here. Being a stay-at-home parent to my stepkids and, uh, future kids? Way better than the stressful life my parents had planned for me."

    "Well, I'm not happy," Will snarled. She clenched her hands into fists. "That stupid Faunus knocked me up again?! Ugh!" She turned on Mark, who was still grunting, his face bright red. "Are you pooping or not?"

    "Not," Mark gasped. He strained one final time, and a large frog egg plopped onto the ground below him. "That dumb gillman keeps making me lay these!"

    "Yes, and you had one in that you hadn't laid yet, which counted as a pregnancy," the genie said.

    "It's out of me now, so can I-"

    "Nope! The wish has already been used." The genie blew a kiss at Mark. "Enjoy parenthood, sweeties!"

    With that, she disappeared. The three former frat boys looked at each other.

    "Time to go back home," Jim said cheerfully. He turned around and began to head back to his house. Will sighed. She didn't have any choice now. She had to live life as a satyr's wife.
