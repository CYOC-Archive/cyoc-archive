Maybe if she took some pictures with it... "Uhm... Maybe I should... Emile, sweetie, say cheese!" said the middle aged woman as she pointed the camera at her son. After all, if she was going to take a picture of something, what could be better than her beloved son?

    "Cheese!" said the little boy with a smile as he saw his mother preparing to take a picture of him, unaware of what effects the camera would have on him. What none of them knew, however, was that while Rebecca was inspecting the camera, she changed it's mode to the Blendin mode with the third setting, which means that Emile would transform to fit with the one taking the picture, Rebecca!

    Rebecca had to blink a couple times to get the flash from the camera away from her eyes, that camera sure had a strong one! But still, the woman didn't took long to regain her fully visibility back and look back at her best friend, Emily. Emily was a woman of the same age as Rebecca with long brown hair and wearing a beautiful green dress that hide her figure amazingly. Rebecca and Emily had been friends since as long as they could remember, and that day, while they met up to go to the mall to have a girls day, they found a retro looking camera that they bought, and now Rebecca was testing it with her best friend; it worked perfectly.

    "You look amazing in this picture, Emily!" said Rebecca as she saw the picture she took, which showed her friend beautifully. "You are very photogenic, girl!"

    "Oh, shut it, it's not that great." replied Emily with a smile. "But I have to say, that camera does work very well! I was sure it would be broken or something, but the pictures it takes are great! Are you going to take a selfie with it now?" asked Emily.

    "Uhm... I don't think so." said Rebecca. Even if she couldn't put her finger on what, she still had a bit of an odd feeling about the camera, and something was telling her that she shouldn't take a picture of herself. "But when I get home, I can't wait to take a picture of Paul and the babies, they will look adorable!"

    "Hey, be sure to let me get the camera later as well, I also want to get some pictures of my family as well!" said Emily with a smile. "But let's not talk about that now, we are here to have a girls day all three! Speaking of... where do you think Olivia is? We weren't very clear when we said we would meet up at the mall..."

    Olivia was originally Emily's mother, and then a good friend of Rebecca after reality was changed to make her Emile's mother. Now, she is a close friend of Rebecca and Emily, but she has no familiar relations with the now middle aged women.

    "Uhm... I am not quite sure." said Rebecca as she looked around. "Knowing her, she is probably finding something cute to wear on a dress shop..."

    "Ah, good idea! Let's go to see if we catch her then!" said Emily with a smile as she began walking to the dress shop all three women frequented, with Rebecca following her closely. Rebecca looked at the camera in her hands as she walked though, the odd feeling about her not quite going away... she could just put the camera on her purse and forget about it, but she could also just take pictures of other random people in the mall...
