However, as a self-preservation and self-defense mechanism, human females have a sixth sense in which they can identify male threats on sight and if pressed by any such threat their strength and speed while triple until they are safe." He opened the page about natural female hair colors which said "...are blonde, black, red and various shades of brown...". He then rewrote the sentence to include "...hot pink, hot purple, bright and medium green, neon blue, cobalt blue, bright orange and rainbow." Then, he set down the pencil and put away the book, happy with his changes and ready to test them out.

    He checked the website picture and saw that where before, the three students had included a blonde and two brunettes, now the picture had a blonde, a brunette and a bright, hot purple. Malcolm closed his laptop, hid the book and went outside to check on the changes. The five women were still there. Before they'd been two blondes, two brunettes and a black-haired girl. Now, there was blonde, a brunette, a purple-hair, a bright blue and the black-haired girl. Malcolm smiled and decided to find someone to mate with.
