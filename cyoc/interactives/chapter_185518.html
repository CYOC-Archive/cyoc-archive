For the moment the two turned onto a nearly empty street, a bleat rang out. Frozen in fear at the satyr like sound, the pair turned behind them and saw the satyr that was once Jared's brother Rob.

Rob used his cloven feet to gain ground where the humans had to take time lest they injure themselves. Before too long, both Jared and Jack were cornered against a metal wired fence. It was too tall to simply climb. The only way they could get through it was, if they helped each other.

    Thinking quickly Jack spoke to Jared.

    "Give me a lift, once I'm on the other side I will lift you up!" He was rushed, the satyr was catching up to them.

    Jared looked at him with wide eyes, but then quickly nodded. Getting down on one knee, he braced himself and watched as Jack used their combined height to propel himself on top of the fence.

    They were stuck in a sort of stalemate. Or, they would've have been, if not for the horde of four another satyrs - including Tommy - approaching from behind Rob.

Jack knew he had to make a choice here and now; let go Jared and maybe he can escape, or both of them will be caught by the satyrs and in all probability transformed.
