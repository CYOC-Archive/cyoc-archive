    Kellie talks with Alt Lisa and Kumiko

    As Lisa, Kumiko, and Mary Elise now began to talk Kellie now turned to the other Lisa and Kumiko. Kellie could feel a lump form in her throat as she looked at her former little sister. “She’s doing so well for herself,” Kellie thought. “What could I have been thinking that night doing that to her. Lisa always looked up to me, I was her big sister. How could I have done this to her.” Wiping her eyes she now approached the two grown down adults who were smiling at their teenage counterparts.

    “Um Lisa, Kumi,” she started, feeling her throat close. “I...I want to thank you two for your help.”

    Lisa and Kumiko turned smiling warmly at Kellie. “Hey not a problem,” Lisa said. “Always willing to help Katie out.”

    “Even if it means becoming teenagers again,” Kumiko added with a smirk. Lisa grinned as she turned towards her friend. “Cmon Kumi It’s been awhile since you did this, I know you have just as much fun as I do when we use the ring.”

    Kumiko sighed as she gazed at her appearance. “Okay true,” she conceded, running her hands over her waist. “Geez hadn’t realized how much weight I put on since I had Tommy. Remind me to hit the gym when we get back.”

    Kellie watched as the two grown down adults began to chuckle softly, she realized that her time was limited. There was so much she wanted to say to Lisa but realized that she couldn’t say much without revealing things that would complicate everyone’s life. Taking a deep breath she decided to just keep things simple.

    “Lisa,” she sighed. “Thank you.....so much for your help. Whatever you two said to Lisa and Kumi looks like it helped.” Lisa smiled at Kellie placing a hand on the girls shoulder. “Don’t mention it,” she grinned. “We just had to cut through the teenage drama, had to show Lisa over there that she needs to get a hold of her anger.”

    “Sounds like you had it easy,” Kumiko smirked. “I had to show my teen self that there’s more to life than fame and money. Do you have any idea how obsessed I was with being famous at 16.”

    Kellie smiled sadly as she looked at the two grown down adults. “I’m....sorry,” she muttered. “I...I really appreciate everything you two have done. I...didn’t mean to involve you in everything that’s going on here. If you want....I can Age you two back up so you can go home.”

    “You have a Ring too,” Kumiko asked in surprise. Kellie smiled, holding out her own ring. “Yeah I’ve had mine for awhile,” she said. “I..I’m a little new to this reality, long story wont bore you with the details. Needless to say my skills are a little more refined.”

    Lisa and Kumiko rubbed their chins thoughtfully as they gazed out at the teenage versions of themselves. “What do you think Lisa,” Kumiko asked. “Ready to grow back up?”

    “I don’t know,” Lisa mused. “We could but why spoil the fun, we could stay like this for a bit. Maybe get a bit younger. I know we always had a ton of fun running around at the playground.”

    Kumiko laughed as she shook her head. “That was a years ago now Lisa,” she laughed. “Do you really want to go run barefoot through the grass that badly.”

    “Sort of,” Lisa smirked.

    Kumiko shook her head laughing. “Ugh you always were just a big kid,” she said. “Remember though our husbands are waiting for us and Gloria will be home from her date soon. You really want to miss out on finding out how it went?”

    Lisa sighed, smiling sadly. “No, no I don’t,” glancing at her watch she sighed. “Too bad we don’t have more time.”

    Kellie rubbed her chin thoughtfully as she looked at her former little sister. Casting a spell to give Lisa what she wanted would be simple enough but she needed it to be their decision. Letting them go home would be the easiest thing to do, but did she really want to do the easy thing? Sighing Kellie now turned to Lisa with an answer.
