    A Warlock Costume For Mark

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 9 days ago O Mythological Magic

    Mark emerged from the changing booth eventually and you saw at once he was in a warlock costume. The same sort a magician would see himself wear, but with more ominous symbols and hues. The dress was dark, with lots of black and burgundy colors. Yet there was gold too on the hems and esoteric macabre symbols etched upon the fabric. The top hat possessed a similar pattern and even had a devil-like goat head upon its front. Furthermore, Mark was holding a staff that was made of real wood and with a glass green gem, along with other devil sculptures on it. Far from any cheap plastic versions for sure! You whistled in admiration.
    "Mark! You look fantastic! Everyone at the party will love your costume!"
    "It IS cool," replies Mark, happily yet with a touch of smugness. "I really, really like the patterns. And I'm happy it looks serious and not simply with a bathing robe around my body. I can pretend to cast evil spells at the other people at the party. Look around for something. This place is huge and I'm sure you'll find something you see as suitable for yourself."
    "Will do!" you say enthusiastically.
    You salute Mark and go in search of something. Oh, seeing what Mark found, you can't wait to find something just a cool and realistic. You can impress everyone at the party too!
