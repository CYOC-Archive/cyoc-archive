    “Name’s Tania Steward. And I came as this was sent to me suggesting your advert in a paper” the young light-brown shoulder-length haired Asian-Caucasian woman wearing a red with gray sleeves bomber jacket over a green jumper with baggy blue jeans and red trainers that was the customer said while showing Crimson Titan a letter. “Really? Must say the adverts’ only very recent and only done so out of necessity with issues getting the web adverts setup” Crimson Titan remarked as he accepted the letter.

    However after a go over Crimson Titan’s expression shifted slightly “Ah… so you where scouted over my *cough* deeper practices”. “So are you able to fulfill my request?” Tania asked. “That depends. For I must insist full discretion yet honesty in this” Crimson Titan firmly insisted before pointing out for Tania to follow him further into the store.

    Once the pair were inside this office “Oh before we start. From here out just call me Crimson if you will… will save us a bit of time” Crimson instructed as he got seated at the user’s side of the desk. “In turn of course I’m Tania. For now at least” Tania followed as she got seated opposite Crimson.

    “Well then… based on the handwriting it was Lucas who found you” Crimson started after having another go over the letter. “And he made it clear this store is but the most recent of many used to cover these kind of activities that apparently you’ve done for centuries” Tania responded. “Oh I’m mortal and the most recent master. Plus for this and previous stores they were testers for those that chanced without help yet in their hearts needed the transformations and reality shifts” Crimson corrected “Also they themselves at heart are the keys if they change their minds”.

    “So you can transform me then? Make me what I’ve always dreamed?” Tania inquired somewhat pleadingly. “…Indeed but keep in mind for all my granted powers I cannot alter reality neither here nor upon your destination to shift everyone’s memories. At the other end you need to build from scratch and here I need to be sure your final whereabouts don’t lead back officially” Crimson instructed. “Maybe I didn’t make myself clear Crimson but I need this. At heart I am not human but merely by a nasty trick of fate forced into the shell of one and inhabit this realm where those like the real me are but a fantasy” Tania sternly stated. “…Indeed I can tell you speak from the heart… for now at least” Crimson noted while whispering the last parts “And by your statement, from here I can ascertain you’re a mythological creature at heart”. “I am” Tania agreed

    “Then let me be clear. You do this and it sticks as you believe as scouts like Lucas aren’t generated to fully know if I’m honest. I must do everything here to inhibit anyone that’ll come after you will figure out we ever met after you’ve left” Crimson warned “And if you do return your bound to being a scout for seekers yourself for generations if it reaches the lengths required”. “And how long will I be scouting?” Tania dared. “20 years. Don’t get me wrong besides requirements for the role your free to go about the rest of your life but anything that links to finding seekers like you are, or in the scenario we’re talking about were, you will be forced to make absolute top priority” Crimson warned. “Understood” Tania accepted. “Then let’s get looking in the mythological section before I take you to the prime portals hidden in the back then” Crimson instructed as he got up and led Tania out of the office.

    The area Crimson had led Tania to was unusually placed within the store, being an open semi-circle area with racks and shelves marking the circle and themselves carrying parts or the entire outfit of its themed creature. What with merfolk tails hanging from hangers at once section, Mannequins wearing the lower-halves of Centaur suits in another. Heck for some reason their where a collection of giant colourful flowers planted right in this pot at the heart of the area.

    “I warn you this might be your last chance to back out depending on the motives or methods of any who will seek you out?” Crimson announced. “Somehow I doubt it” Tania assured. “Fair enough.
