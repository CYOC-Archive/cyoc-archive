    “…Tomorrow!” The woman embraces Laura tightly. “So I have you for the whole day!” Laura is much less thrilled than the woman about this. She squirms. “Oh, don’t worry, I’ll take good care of you.” The woman pokes Laura’s nose. “Play with Dustin now, okay? I’m going to work on getting some breakfast ready for you two.”

    The woman sets Laura back in the playpen. The boy hands a toy over to her.

    “Pway?” he asks timidly. Laura stifles a sigh and decides to play along. She won’t get a chance to escape until night, when everyone has gone to bed, so she might as well be the best-behaved toddler in the world.

    Laura gets caught up in playing with Dustin and actually begins to enjoy herself. She’s startled when the woman interrupts their playing to give them each a bottle of milk. After they finish their milk, their diapers are checked. Laura is glad when the woman announces she’s dry, though she knows that it’s only a matter of time before she pees or worse in her diaper. She won’t be able to use the bathroom.

    After playing with Dustin for what feels like a short amount of time, the woman checks Laura and Dustin’s diapers again. Laura is somehow still dry, but Dustin needs to be changed. The woman whisks Dustin away, but not before mentioning to Laura that they’ll be having lunch and then taking a nap. Laura thinks that, since she managed to stay dry for so long, she might be able to stay dry through the nap. If she goes right now.

    Reluctantly, she squats in the playpen and pees. She only intends to wet herself – she hopes to hold in a bowel movement until after she’s escaped – but then she feels a shift in her stomach. She’s already in prime position, so a long log comes out without her meaning to. Laura is disgusted, but decides to finish the job and pushes as hard as she can, grunting as she does.

    She’s too distracted to notice that the woman has returned with Dustin, until Dustin is set in the playpen, suckling on another bottle of milk.

    “I was wondering when you’d make a poopy,” the woman says. Laura continues to grunt. “When I changed you this morning, you were just wet, not messy.” Laura’s eyes widen at hearing that the woman has already changed a diaper of hers. Finally, she finishes and stands.

    “I poopoo,” she says proudly, trying to act the part of a toddler. The woman beams.

    “Yes, you did!” She picks Laura up and carries her off to a nursery, where Laura is set on a changing table. Right before changing her, the woman pops a bottle of milk into Laura’s mouth. Suckling on the milk distracts her from the changing, and before she knows it, she’s in a clean diaper.

    “Ooh, you’re a hungry girl!” the woman coos. She takes the empty milk bottle from Laura. “I might as well put you down for your nap right now!” The woman places Laura in a crib. A few minutes later, she returns with Dustin and sets in the same crib as Laura. The warm milk in Laura’s stomach soothes her into falling asleep quickly.

    Laura wakes up as she is being changed yet again. Judging by the lack of smell, it was just a wet diaper, not a messy one. She looks up at the person changing her, an unfamiliar man. The man beams at her.

    “Jessica was right. You’re too cute,” he coos. He tickles Laura’s stomach, making her giggle. “I’m Charlie, Jessica’s husband. Sorry I didn’t get to spend the whole day with you like Jessica, but I’m glad to spend what little time I can.” Charlie finishes changing Laura’s diaper and then carries her out of the nursery and into the kitchen, where a highchair has been set up.

    “You get the seat of honor, Laura,” the woman – Jessica – says as Charlie places her in the highchair. Dustin is sitting on Jessica’s lap, being spoonfed from a jar of baby food. Laura feels dread build as she realizes that she is going to have to be fed the same thing. Her stomach rumbles, however, and she decides that baby food is better than nothing.

    Charlie gushes over Laura throughout dinner, then after picks her up and carries her to the bathroom, followed by Jessica carrying Dustin. Laura and Dustin are placed in the tub and given a bath together. Laura squirms with embarrassment the entire time, wishing she had her pacifier to comfort her. But she hasn’t seen it all day.

    Finally, Laura and Dustin are given one last bottle of milk, then brought to the nursery for bed, where Laura is finally reunited with her pacifier. Her eyes light up when she sees Jessica pull it out.

    “Aw, did someone miss her passy?” Jessica coos, popping the pacifier into Laura’s mouth. Laura sucks on it eagerly, glad for the comfort after an entire day of being treated like a toddler. Laura and Dustin are placed in the crib together. Jessica and Charlie say good night, turn off the light, and leave the room. Laura sucks on her pacifier eagerly, waiting for Dustin to fall asleep before she makes a break for it.
