    the story begins with a 15 year old boy looking out his bedroom window at night bored out of his mind. his parents gone on some trip said they wouldn't be back for days. a shooting zoomed across the sky and he decided to wish on it
    " I wish things would get exciting" he said. instantly after he felt dumb for wishing on a star "it was childish" he thought. then that's the moment his life changed forever. a flash of bright light blinded him and next thing he saw was that he was in a different room a different house. with a lit fireplace and books all around. a recliner chair was in the center of the room with a person sitting on it well reading. "hello Andrew" he said calmly without looking away from his book " I heard you wished for some excitement, well I am your man" spoke the man as he set down his book. Andrew had so many questions like how did he get here and how did he know his name, but all that didn't matter right now Andrew only cared for what this man may offer " if your not lying then do whatever you want as long as it makes my life exciting and doesn't kill me" Andrew said hoping he wouldn't regret this. the man stood up and walked over to Andrew " well then I got some plans for you" the man spoke with a smile then snapped and a paper appeared with a pen. " sign your name and it will be done" he held a serious tone in his voice as he handed Andrew the pen. Andrew grabbed the pen and signed without even bothering to read what was on it. in a instant Andrew disappeared back to his house. the man sat back down and laughed "I missed causing chaos and what lovely chaos this will cause" he said grabbing the contract that read " signing this will cause changing including very slowly becoming a skunk during the next few days but it shall not be just any skunk he will constantly stink and the more skunk he becomes the worse he will smell but all he will smell is a faint displeasing smell, signing this also will cause slow gender swap and will cause the subject to start producing eggs which infinity keep forming if managed to not lay the eggs will begin building filling them fill of eggs until they cant handle it and lay. the order of which effect happen first is random".

    Andrew back in his room unsure of what will come waits to see what happens.
