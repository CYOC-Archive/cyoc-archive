    “Hehe… yeah, and probably I’d look intimidating, at least people would finally show me some respect,” Andy says, grinning, “what about you? What animal would you transform into, if you had such power?”

I was thinking… your idea of having transformation powers, to be able to transform into an animal at will, does sound sweet. So, what would you say if I’d use the App to give us THAT power?”
