    “Sorry to bother you,” Brian says, “my mother texted me, but since I can’t wear glasses under this mask, I was wondering if you could read it to me,” he then holds his phone directly in front of Jordan’s face, the screen directly in line with Jordan’s eyes and before Jordan has a chance to reply, Brian activates the hypnosis.

    Jordan is taken aback as some guy suddenly holds up their phone to his eyes, the screen looking weird with some weird rainbow colored spiral which suddenly start to spin. The 10-year-old finds himself suddenly unable to tear his eyes away, his mind suddenly feeling numb and he just blankly stares at the screen.

    Brian smirks, ‘man the App is strong, she’s already under the influence,’ he thinks to himself. The teen then leans in to the “Raccoon-girl’s” ear and in a low voice, loud enough that Jordan is able to understand his words, but quiet enough that no one else hears what Brian is saying.

    “Listen closely, beautiful,” Brian starts, “you and me, we just met and started talking. You remember me saying that I am single, and you replied that you are single yourself. After talking for a while, I noticed that you started to take interest in my handsome and athletic body, you feel extremely attracted to me, even though you don’t know how I look like under this costume, but you don’t seem to care. When you come back to your senses, you will have forgotten about this incident, when I suggest to leave this boring party and go somewhere more private, you will happily agree and take me to a secluded and romantic location that only you know about,” Brian then stops the App and quickly lowers his phone, smiling when he sees the “girl” blink and look around a little dazed, “hey, are you alright,” he then asks.

    Jordan turns his head, “s-sorry… I… I kind of zoned out there for a minute,” he says, “wh-what was your name again?”

    “Brian,” Brian says, “I didn’t quite catch your name”.

    “Jordan,” Jordan says.

    “Jordana,” Brian repeats, the combination of the loud party and alcohol causing the teen to misunderstand Jordan, “such a beautiful name for a beautiful Raccoon-girl,” he says, smiling under his mask.

    “Wh-what,” Jordan asks, feeling confused, looking at the “Fox”, ‘this guy is weird,’ he thinks to himself, ‘but he’s really handsome! I just love his athletic appearance’.

It is… a big clearing with a lake that has clean water and the field is covered in all sorts of beautiful flowers… it’s rather romantic”.

    Brian smiles and takes Jordan’s hand, “sounds like the perfect place to get to know each other a little… better and… closer,” he says, “lead the way”.
