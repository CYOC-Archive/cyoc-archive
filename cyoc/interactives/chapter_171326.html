    Inner Goddess: Fur(r)y Breakout

    added by TransforMarv 2 years ago A BM I TG O Mythological Anthro

    You stood still in the middle of the temple. Nothing you tried seemed to work. And the more you thought about it, the angrier you got. These other goddesses had the gall to just throw you out immediately simply for your appearance. You would find a way out and gain enough power to make them pay. It reached the point where you felt yourself shaking from anger.

    Wait, shaking?

    You noticed that the more emotional you got, the more your statue started to vibrate. This could be your way out, even if it involved intentionally thinking about those stupid other goddesses. Your shaking got stronger and you started to hear the telltale sound of cracks appearing across your stone body. You really hoped this would help in some way and not just leave you in pieces on the ground, but as it quite literally was the only thing you could do you continued.

    More and more cracks appeared and soon the whole statue broke apart. Meanwhile you continued to stand in the middle of it all in your furry, naked glory. You took a deep breath. You were free from your stony shell. Carefully you started moving first your arms and then your legs to find you had full control. You also noticed you had no problems walking around with your new legs, despite technically never having used them yet.

    Inspecting your body, you quickly came to the conclusion that you looked exactly like the statue (B-cups included, sadly) just in colors. Your fur was mostly a beautiful crimson with a white chest. The lower arms and legs were black, but in patterns that made them look more like tribal tattoos at some points. Your tail neatly transitioned from your normal fur color to a golden yellow at its tip.

    You couldn't help but give the same smirk your statue-self did. Now you could easily take your revenge. You stuck out your hand-paw… and nothing happened.

    Okay, so breaking out of your shell sapped most of your powers, no big issue. You just had to wait till they recovered. Maybe even find and/or create some followers to speed things up. But first things first now, you had to get out of this temple. Unfortunately on foot...
