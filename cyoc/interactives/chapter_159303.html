He saw his roommate's image on the screen, and looking around closer, he realized this was a system for changing people arbitrarily, and even changing reality. This was insane! What could Jake have been trying to do in here?

For one thing, he was very fond of Jake, who had been his roommate for a year and a half now, and he secretly envied Jake a little bit. Leo liked his quiet but adventurous attitude, his tall and lanky frame, his punctuality, and his popularity. Leo also liked the way Jake always seemed happy to see him: Jake would smile and wag his tail whenever Leo came into the dorm. In fact, although Leo wouldn't admit it, he was actually kind of curious about Jake's tail. It looked more like something you would see on a dog rather than a person, specifically a shiba inu. It was strange in the sense that Leo couldn't think of anyone else who had a tail, but it was normal in that Jake had always been that way, as far as Leo knew. It didn't even occur to Leo that Jake's tail might have come from the Chronivac, but he did wonder if the machine could give him a tail like Jake's.

It didn't stop until it was a good two feet long and nearly as thick as his arm. The fur that had started with a few hairs now completely covered all of the skin. The first half was black on top and brown on the bottom, and the latter half was a brilliant perfect white. Curious, Leo soon discovered that he could raise it, lower it, and point it in different directions just as easily as he could flail his arms.
