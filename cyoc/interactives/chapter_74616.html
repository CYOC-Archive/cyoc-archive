    "Our principal might not recognize you, but I don't think he'd think twice about a room full of adults." Brenda said.
    "Just what is it you're asking me?" Jeanie asked a little confused.
    "I wish the entire class, including Ms. Chan, were adults but we all still have the same minds that we do now." Brenda asked.
    "Gotcha." Jeanie then crossed her arms and nodded as all the students in the classroom became adults including Brenda while Ms. Chan became an adult again. Jeanie had also been careful to mind that the clothes of everyone grew with them so they wouldn't shrink.

    It wasn't long before the whole class and Ms. Chan were all adults.
    "Oh my god, this is terrible, this is ridiculous, this is..." Ms. Chan was worrying when the door suddenly knocked and the principal came in. Jeanie quickly made her clothes more suitable so she wouldn't hurt the school's dress code.
    "Miss Chan, who're all these people?" The principal asked confused as to why there were adults on a school day.
    "Oh well they're uh..." Ms. Chan said trying to think of something when Jeanie interrupted.
    "These are some of the parents her students they're here to see what their kids go through during the day."
    "And who might you be?" The principal asked her.
    "I'm a teacher in training." The principal looked at the whole class again and decided everything was fine.
    "Very well, carry on." The principal said as he left the room.

    Everyone was relieved when he left, then Ms. Chan looked back at Jeanie.
    "This is all your fault you know, now I've got a classroom of adults that are supposed to be kids." Ms. Chan complained.
    "Relax, I can just use my magic to change them again."
    "Then do it." Ms. Chan demanded.
    "I'm afraid after what you said about Brenda and myself earlier, I'm not taking any demands from you."
    "Brenda, what do you and the rest of the class want to do now?" Jeanie asked.
    Brenda asked her classmates to huddle together and quietly discuss what they wanted to do next.
    Brenda then came back up to Jeanie and Ms. Chan and told them......
