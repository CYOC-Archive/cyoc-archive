    Venturing Outside

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Powor 5 years ago O


    Lisa waited for what seemed like forever but in reality it was only about an hour. She had climbed back down to the floor quite a while ago.

    Doesn't look like anyone's coming in here, Lisa silently thought to herself.

    This didn't surprise her too much since Danica - her roommate - was quite the party machine. She often didn't come to the room for days at a time. Lisa knew what this meant: if help wasn't going to come to her she would have to get help herself.

    I guess there's really no other choice, she thought as she approached her colossal door.

    Lisa stared at it for just a minute, hoping it would just open up and she would be saved. Of course that wasn't likely to happen anytime soon.

    So Lisa mustered up some courage and squeezed underneath the gap in the door. Surprisingly she was just small enough to accomplish such a feat. If she were even half an inch bigger she wouldn't have fit.

    Lisa was outside the door to her dorm room. The enormous halls were lined with dozens of rooms just like hers. Each housing students just like her. The only difference was they had not shrunk like she had.

    Now, where to go, thought Lisa.
