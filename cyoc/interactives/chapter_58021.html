Composing herself she picked it back up and activated it once more. Kyle did say that she was going to be pretty good with it. She looked around and saw a fruit in a tree and decided that she was going to knock it down with her whip.

    She flung the whip at it and with pin point accuracy she knocked it out of the tree. Proud with herself she walked over so that she could enjoy the spoils of her triumph. Getting close to it she heard a rustle in the bush next to it. Suddenly a small little fox came out and stole the fruit from her. Normally she loved animals and would not hurt one ever but. On impulse she grabbed her whip and with lightning fast speed she swung the whip around the fox’s neck forcing it to drop the fruit. She noticed the black fox was shaking. Walking up to the fox she grabbed the fruit from in front of it and undid the whip. The fox freed ran little bit away still scared and watched as jenny tried to eat the fruit. Failing she threw the fruit to the side in frustration. Still really hungry she heard her stomach start to growl at her. the fox noticing this ran over and pawed the fruit to be in front of jenny then started to yip this got jenny’s attention and then it showed her that the top may be hard to open but the bottom was soft and easy to open. Jenny looked at the fox as it started to leave her to eat the fruit. Jenny yelled at the fox to wait and it stopped and looked at jenny she grabbed her whip and slashed another fruit down and set it next to her. The fox took this as an act of kindness and ran over to sit next to jenny and eat. The two sat there for a little bit and became fast friends.
