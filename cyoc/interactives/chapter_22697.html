    SuperTy

    Ty smiled when he saw the opening scenes of the newest episode of the "Power Rangers" on TV.

    "These guys are so cool..." he chuckled to himself. "I wish I were a super hero, with super powers, muscles and super strength, just like Superman... But, without his weakness for Kryptonite!”

    Suddenly, Ty was overcome by a warm feeling which covered his entire body.

    A smooth, shiny layer of a rubber suit formed all over his skin, just like a wetsuit. His arms and torso were light blue, and he suddenly wore red trunks, red boots which ran up to just below his knees, and a long, red cape, which was draped over his shoulder, and billowed behind him whenever he made a move.

    Next, his muscles expanded a little, and he quickly gained a stunning, athletic look. He moaned when the super powers took effect, increasing his senses and his physical strength so that he was over 1,000 times stronger than an average human being!

    "Wow..." he whispered to himself, as he strolled to his mirror, in the bedroom. He grinned when he saw his long, wide, red cape as it billowed behind him while he walked.

    Ty then turned towards the door and ran over to the living room in a fraction of a second!

    "Whoa! I got super speed, too!"

    Then he lifted up, hovering six feet above the ground while maintaining his body in a horizontal position, as he flew back to his bedroom.

    "Awesome!" he laughed, now that he realized he could give himself as many super powers as he wanted to.

    "I should be like this permanently..."

    Suddenly, there was a bright glow, as very bright light surrounded his body!

    The glow disappeared, and Ty felt different...

    "Well, time to change back to my normal self..." he said.

    Nothing happened.

    "Uhh... I wish to change back to normal!"

    Still, nothing happened...

    He walked back to the mirror. He then turned and glanced at the candle, which was on his desk. Two pencil-wide beams of red light flashed from his eyes, striking the tip of the candle, lighting it instantly!

    Ty's mouth dropped wide open!

    "Oh wow! I'm a super hero... Permanently!"

    He tried to take off his costume, but he only ended up pinching his own skin.

    “This is cool! I wish I had the powers of every super hero in the comic books!”

    Again, there was a bright glow, as Ty filled up with every super power of every super hero which has ever appeared in any comic book that was ever made.

    “Wow, I’m STRONG and POWERFUL!” he gasped, as he willed his body to turn invisible for just a few seconds.

    Finally, he wished he could make his super-suit invisible, and that worked! The suit disappeared in his reflection in the mirror, although Ty could still see the suit covering his body.

    Ty had a red "T" logo, shaped as a red-and-yellow shield, embroidered on his uniform, and covering his chest from shoulder blade to shoulder blade.

    He couldn't help but grin, when he made a fist and flexed his arm, making a huge, thick bowling-ball-bicep rise up from his arm.

    "I may as well enjoy being a super hero, now!" Ty sighed out loud.

    As he turned around, his ears picked up music, sounds, and even telephone conversations from houses within ten miles of his home. He could see inside every house on his block, up to five blocks away. He could see what everyone was doing, for miles! Lastly, when he looked up at the sky, he could see several satellites, which were in space.

    Finally, Ty opened the window and leaped out the window with arms extended. He took off and flew across the sky, looping around the sky and making 360-degree turns.

    "Weeeeee!! This is fun!" Ty laughed to himself. “Whoooohoooooo!!!!”

    He started to enjoy being SuperTy, and he loved to fly above the city, with the strong wind blowing in his face, with his cape billowing behind him.

    SuperTy was here to stay!

    "I need to go visit somebody..." he wondered.

    Ty knew now, that he was going to LOVE being a super hero! Not only that, but Ty was now the most powerful super hero in history!

    Where would he go, to show off his new super powers, his super-body, and his cape and uniform?
