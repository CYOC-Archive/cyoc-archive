    Damon was just pulling into her empty garage when the sun started rising.

    Damon got out of the car and closed the garage as her now awake cub followed her out of the car. Damon walked into the house and noticed the clothes he had been wearing the day she met that Witch were clean, folded and placed on her dining room table, with her phone and wallet next to them, apparently Monica had broken into her house and put the stuff there and left, the only thing that seemed to be missing was about $50 from her wallet.

    Damon quickly realized that her cub was getting bored and hungry. So she went upstairs and grabbed some cat toys from her previous cat, Oscar, and gave it to her cub to play with, although he seemed more hungry than bored and kept looking hungrily at Damon, who then picks up the cub and lets the little lion drink her milk, when she realizes, out of the 5 days she has had her little one, she hasn’t given him a name yet! Damon begins racking her brain and comes up with a name, “Felix!” She exclaims and repeats herself “I’ll call you Felix” as she boops his nose. Before thinking, “wait I’m a girl now, so I should come up with a new name for myself.” As she began running through names.

    “Dana? Yeah Dana, I like that name.” Dana said to herself. After a few minutes of her son sucking on her breast she put him down to play as she went and grabbed some food to eat. She was done with the food she was fed at the zoo and moved on and made herself some cereal. Sat down and turned on the TV, she saw a news report that read “Local Lioness and Cub has Escaped” and laughed as she watched footage of herself escaping the Zoo.

    After she finished her cereal and turned off the TV Dana went upstairs and layed down to sleep. Dana woke up a few hours later and noticed her son had found her and was sucking on her boob. She shooed him off and tried to sit up but couldn’t because something was blocking her way, she looked down only to see she was now very pregnant. Apparently whatever magic Monica put on her, made her pregnancies last a shorter amount of time, because it was only yesterday that she found out. And it looked like she would be birthing 2-3 kids. Thats when she felt warm water run down her leg, her water just broke...

    The kids/cubs were on their way and she had to prepare for delivery, Dana got out of bed and waddled her way to the bath tub so she makes less of a mess and begins to push she felt one child being pushed out, then another, and another. She now had three kids/cubs in her bathtub but she had no clue if they were human tiger or a hybrid of the two...
