    The doctor eventually picks up a red vial from the cart, and tells you

    "Ok, for this test I need you cut your finger abit with this knife, right now I'm going to test if this formula will work on your cut"

    He then hands you a knife and goes in saying

    "If everything goes according plan it should heal the cut in a matter of minutes"

    Seeing how you already agreed in going along with this testing you use the knife and cut you index finger abit and then you hand the knife back to the doctor. You then stick out your now cut finger to the doctor, he then uses a eye dropper on the reddish liquid in the vial and drops a few drops on your cut.

    "now we wait, it will take a few minutes before anything will happen" says the doctor

    3 minutes later, you and the doctor stare at the cut on your finger. It seems to slowly get much smaller, and eventually it's completely disappears.

    "ha ha! no more cut!" says the doctor as he writes down somethings on clipboard "Now was that so bad?"

    "not really" you reply - you start thinking "Wow!" that sure was interesting.

    The doctor then walks over to the cart and pickups a slightly redder vial and tells you

    "This test will be slighty different, in this test you will have to drink this vial. And don't worry it's not poisonious in anyway and the formula basically have no taste to it - Just to warn you in advance, in this test I will be looking for side effects, so if you feel anything in anyway tell me"

    "Side effects?" you ask "you have done this test before? right?"

    "......uh..yes!" explains the doctor "we have done this test a few times under different settings and all those times the subjects experienced no serious side effects, I think the worst side effect was a young lady with a head-ache"

    "Alright then, let's give it a shot" you answer

    "Let's go!" proclaims the doctor

    The doctor then carefully hands you the vial. You take the vial and slowly drink the whole thing.....

    The doctor then stares you down in a strange manor and says "So how do you feel young man?"

    After a few moments of checking yourself you say "Actually I feel great! I feel like I have more energy, and I don't really feel like anything out of the normal"

    "Interesting...." says the doctor

    You stop for a second, you just had a bad feeling about this.....

    "What do you mean interesting? is something wrong?" you prompt

    "Ah.....It's nothing to worry about, you said yourself you feel fine..." explains the doctor

    "Worry about what?" you say

    The doctor then points to the ground, when you look down at the floor you see hair. You give a puzzled look to the doctor and he then explains

    "ha, I guess you haven't noticed but I believe your body hair has just fallen all out" 

    "What The?" you proclaim.

    You check your arms, your legs, your chest - the hair is all gone! - You even check you armpits and that hair is gone too. You check your head.....and with some relief think "good it's still there (that was a close one)".

    You then quickly ask the doctor "Have you ever seen such a side effect before?"

    "No, actually this a new one" he explains  "I'm not really surpised actually, this vial and you were kinda a bad combination to begin with, but if some hair loss is your only concern then don't worry about it"
