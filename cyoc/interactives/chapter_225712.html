    Movie night

    Today was a special day for Mindy Carter since she had recently invited her friends from college to a party she was hosting at her house. She would have everything: snacks, alcohol, music, and best of all, no parents nor boys. But there was still something she was missing and that was getting movies for the party.

    You see, not only was it a normal party, it was also going to be a movie night. But there was a small problem. Mindy had just had her Netflix account closed, and while she could borrow the account from someone else, she decided it was better to get the movies herself.

    So Mindy went to the video store near her house and started looking for movies she could take for the movie night. The first place she started looking was in the bargain bin as she wanted to see if there was anything interesting in there. And to her surprise, she found something that made her raise an eyebrow.

    It was a VHS, but its box was quite different from the rest of the VHS that were sold in the store. Not to mention that the title of the movie didn't sound like anything she had seen before in the past.

    What was the title of the movie?
