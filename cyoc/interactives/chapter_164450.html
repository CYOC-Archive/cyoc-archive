    “This thing must be a new prototype of a new VR system, I’m going to figure it out and have blast some alien scum!” Jake is super excited to play with device - even though he can’t understand the language on the device. “It must have some language settings so I can input English.” Looking through the device, he stumbles upon an avatar area. “Look at this Chris!”, Jake shouts out as he finds a menu that allows him to make a cool Donkey Man avatar. Jake selects a humanoid figure and selects what appears to be a Donkey’s Head. He selects a brownish fur with a dark blake mane going down it’s back. The donkey man even has a short tail. Further down it appears to have human features, including torso and feet. Unfortunately no matter what he presses, he cannot get the avatar to save.
    “ I told ya it was hopeless dude!”, exclaimed Chris in a taunting voice. Why don’t you leave it alone as Chris snatched the device from Jake. As he grabbed the device, he pressed a hidden button which shocked Chris and caused him to drop the device.
    The screen lit up with the same strange language, but now included a percentage of 1 % and starting counting up. Chris began to scream, “Oh WHat The Hell!! I’m on fire!” You watch in horror as Chris begins to rip his clothes off down to his underwear and starts convulsing - but then the crazy thing happens. Chris brings his hands up towards his face as they start to mold web together, going from five digits to a single knuckle - and then they start to turn dark like a hooved animal. “Jake - What did you do to me? - It hurts so bad!” - Chris screams again. Unfortunately for you and Chris, the changes continue up Chris’s torso as his skin is replaced with a dark brown fur - similar to a Donkey’s hide. Next up, Chris’s ears start to grow tall and pointed - as Jake realizes and says, “Chris, I think you are turning into the Donkey avatar in real life!”, to which Chris yells, “You must hawwwwwww giving me drugs - heeeHaaaaaw!” Chris’s head progressively is looking less human and more like a Donkey’s head. “ I haawwwwte yo heee hawww heeee hawww!” Chris says again as his head finishes it’s change into a donkey. In front of you is a half naked Donkey Man that looks just like the avatar, Donkey from the head down to the torso with Hooves instead of hands, but with mostly human legs and feet. “What are we going to do now? We got to get you and the device back home so I can figure out how to fix you.” says Jake. They quickly make their way back to to Jakes house where he calls up James in a panic recounting the story and asking him to come over. James is a bit skeptical but keeps hearing the donkey brays in the background and decides to come see what type of prank their friends are trying to pull off.
