With long arms, long legs, big feet the size of flippers and large hands with long fingers, he looked like he was ready for the Olympics.

He did that for about two hours until he was done, climbing out of the pool by way of the ladder by the side. Once he was done, he showered off and walked into the locker room where he took a shower.

    Once he dried off, Marco was overcome by that tingling sensation again. Unaware of his invisible visitor at the time, Ty was standing there, watching his handiwork as Marco started to shrink back.

    Marco’s body shrunk by a whole foot in height until he was a little bit on the short side, the same height as Ty. At the same time, his muscles started to blow up, inflating rapidly like helium balloon, quickly changing the wereswimmer back into a young, hulking muscle boy. This time, however, Marco would become even BIGGER than he was previously.

His arms were sticking out like an NFL linebacker! The only difference was, he wasn’t even wearing any pads or a harness! Mighty abdominal muscles formed over his stomach as his biceps inflated into large balls of muscle, even bigger than they were before!

    His legs became massive as his quads became giant pillars of thick muscle cords, wider than his body and his calves filled up until they were bigger than basketballs!

    “Holy shit!” Marco gasped, admiring his physique in the mirror. “Why do I look bigger every time I change?”

    “You’re a growing boy, dude!” Ty grinned, appearing before him with a smile on his face.

    “Ty! Why are my muscles so… HUGE!” Marco wondered, as he flexed again.

    “Dude… Because they are YOU! You look great with big muscles. They are so YOU!” Ty smiled. “Your body is made to hold huge muscles.”

    “But my shoulders are so… wide! I can barely fit through the door!!” Marco sighed.

    “Don’t worry… Just walk sideways! And, wear sporty clothes!” Ty grinned, snapping his fingers. Marco was blinded by a flash of light when he was suddenly wearing high tops, Lycra shorts, a tank top and a bandana over his forehead. “I’ll help you!”

Then, his classmates all fought over their turn to run their hands over Marco’s bulging muscles as Marco just stood there, maintaining his flex while Ty looked on and grinned as he admired his handiwork. Marco was the most popular kid in school…
