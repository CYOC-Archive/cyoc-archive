    As things went on, Alia, Pinkie, Luna, and Lia left for Paris. Alia's mother was happy that hir daughter is getting to see the sights of another country. Alia's mother was originally from Paris, but shi was just out of there a week before the outbreak had hit. Maurice was standing outside as news crews were out there filming and doing an interview. "Folks, listen, I know you all want to return to being human. However, I can only allow those under the age of sixty. The reason behind it is this. You currently have two hearts, those that are taurlike kats, and should you be over the age limit, the risks could be death. I am also not allowing children to do this either. Even with parental consent. Reason is the same effect on the children as the elders. When we did it in Fee City, even the Mayor agreed that it should be those between the ages sixteen and sixty. Considering the risks of heart attack as the two hearts become one."

    "Mrs. Chakat, we understand that, but what makes you think that the parents won't attempt to try it on their children?"

    "Simple. There are facilities in every capital city throughout the globe, the only place to get the stuff is at a Chakat Industries office. And since the outbreaks we've had security put up and with permission from the UN, each nation has their local national guardsmen guarding the building. This time should the outbreaks happen again, we'll know where to go instead of juggling the world searching each nation. And also it's a way we are keeping our tails out of the blender so to speak."

    "One more question. Do you think there will be another outbreak?"

    "I can't say there won't be another outbreak, I certainly hope there isn't. However if there is, they'll be picking a new team. The kats that took to stopping them all of us, have officially stepped down. Does that mean should an outbreak hit, and the world can't find someone else to do it, will we sit idly while it spreads? No. We will be ready but we are a last resort team now."

    "What about the two scientists you said you will hire that were involved in the outbreaks? What makes you think that they won't steal something and do this again?"

    "Simple. They themselves have became kats and know that it's not right to force people. The two only had the same vision me and Snowy had. Overrall their reason is not as bad as the others. Therefore, can I fault them for wanting peace? No I cannot. They were just blinded, and we helped open their eyes. Besides, the real culprits will be sentenced to prison time here in a prison built by the finest, and the security are scorpkats who chose to stay as they were. So, them escaping will not be easy, should they succeed in escape, they earned their freedom and that is the agreement with the governments, however, should they start the outbreaks over again, they will be shot on site. When they hit eighty, they will have a choice, to die naturally or become kats themselves to finish their 99 year sentience off."

    "My final question. Should they, if they change back to human, after near fifteen years, and say they decide to become kats this time, will that be possible?"

    "Yes, as we speak there is a development of a serum. The chakat virus is being completely eradicated. We are now making a Chakat Serum. And as for some of those changing back, as we speak some are changing back willingly. Majority of the kat population is declining however, we are still planning a trip to search for a new world soon, in about fifty years we'll have our ship. As said by the captain of the ship, humans and kats alike will be invited to join us."

    The reporters wrapped up and all over the world people were becoming human again, as stated it was those in that specified age range. It would prevent trouble. In Moscow the leader there wasn't forcing his citizens to return to normal. France now as half and half, kat and man. Cairo as well. Hong Kong even reverted back. Along the East Coast over half the population there. All of New Orleans changed back along with Houston. It would seem that seventy-five percent, those in the age range, became human again. The other 25% loved it so much that they didn't want anything else. The children and elderly were hardly complaining, for they've grown to love it or were born that way. So the kat population, after the census was taken was at 5.2 million to 15.4 billion total in population, counting women and children.
