    The Magic Shop

    Kenny shrinks but his muscles grow

    added by Anonymous 14 years ago BM S

    Anna just stared at Kenny with a serious look on her face.

    “I can do anything I want to, now!” she said to him.

    “Yeah, right...”

    “Serious!” Anna replied, as she folded her arms.

    Kenny hopped off the bed and stood before her.

    “Prove it!”

    “Alight!”

    Suddenly, Kenny was overcome by a tingling feeling as he saw his sister grow taller and his room grow larger... All at once!

    “Hey! What... What's going on?” he wondered as he looked around.

    “You're shrinking... Pipsqueek!” Anna grinned, as she saw Kenny dwindle down from 4 foot 8 inches tall to just over 4 feet tall.

    “Stop! STOP!” Kenny shouted. “STOP IT!”

    Kenny continued to shrink down, until he was just over 3 feet tall. He barely reached past Anne's waist.

    “There! You look so cute now!” Anne said, as she caused the shrinking to stop.

    “This sucks, being so small!” Kenny cried.

    “Oh, quit crying, will you?”

    “Can't you change me in a NICE way?” Kenny protested. “Why am I always smaller and younger than everyone else?”

    “Fine!” Anne replied. “How about this?”

    Suddenly, Kenny was overcome by that tingling feeling again. This time, he wasn't shrinking or growing. At first, he didn't see any change at all. Then, he noticed his biceps started to inflate! His arms grew thicker! His muscles were growing!

    “Hey! What are you doing?”

    “I'm making your muscles grow, little guy!” Anne laughed. “You're becoming a little muscle man!”

    Just as his bigger sister said, his arms, legs and chest bulged out with inflated muscle everywhere! Little Kenny now resembled a small gymnast!

    “Whoa!” he gasped, as he examined his body. He was ripped all over!
