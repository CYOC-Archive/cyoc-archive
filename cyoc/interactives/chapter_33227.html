You think that you would want to go to a carnival with your girlfriend, but instead I see you wandering around this place while I was passing by outside. You looked lost, so I thought I'd come in here and rescue you. Sometimes your so lost without me Ashley."

Besides, how could I insult my little cutie." And with that your girlfriend wrapped her arms around you and kissed you deeply. You wanted to protest or say something, but this felt nice enough to let it go for the moment.

    After the kiss, Karen looked around and saw some people looking at the two of you. "Huh, people look like they've never seen two girls kiss before. Or maybe it's that I'm eighteen and your fifteen. Maybe we should go somewhere else." "Wait" you start,"tell me something. Have you always remembered me as a girl? I mean seriously, because not five minutes ago I was your eighteen year old boy friend."

    At this Karen looked a little concerned. "Well of course you've always been a girl Ashley. Are you feeling alright? I mean, making up stories of you being a guy and the same age as me. Not that I wouldn't mind the age part, but you know I'm not into guys." Karen looked serious enough for you to believe her. This now led you to a conclusion about the carnival. The carnival had somehow altered reality so you have always been this way. Thinking quick, as Karen was still looking at you with concern, you start,"Haha, yeah, sorry. I am feeling okay now. I was just a little light headed and day dreaming a little before you found me. Really, I am okay now."

    "Are you sure? We can go back to my place so you can lay down." The offer was nice, but you wanted to find a way to change back. "Yeah, we could, but don't you want to try some of these rides and attractions first?" "Eh, not right now" Karen said, not showing interest in the carnival. "Besides, if I wanted to explore this place later, it will be here for about a week. Well, that's what the flyer said at least. But anyways, the offer still stands, you can come over to my place for a little rest "action" and then we could come back here later. Whadya say Ashley?" While your girlfriend asks this, she moves in close and wraps her arms around you.

    Your not sure why, but having her be the bigger, more responsible one make you feel comforted and happy while your in her arms. You know that the carnival will be here for the rest of the week, and it's not everyday you get to change genders. On the other hand, there is no telling how long it might take you to change back, or what kind of ordeals you might find yourself in. It was a hard choice, but you finally decided....
