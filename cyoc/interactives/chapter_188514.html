    Before Max Could Max His First Change Their Father Walks In And Sees The Device
    Geoffrey Their Father Says "You Boys Got Your Hands On One Of Those Huh, That Is Such Good News I Have Been Needing One Of These"
    Geoffrey Then Says "Why Don't You Boys Come With Me I Will Show You Something"
    The Twin Boys Follow Their Father With The Device Into His Car And Taking A 15 Minute Drive
    When They Arrive They Follow Their Dad Into A Building That Looks Abandon On The Outside But On The Inside It Looked Brand New And Pretty Futuristically

    Their Father Continues Walking The Boys Into A Room With A Code Lock Which He Enters In And Then Presents A Robot Girl And Says "Boys This Is What I Have Been Working On For Some Time Now This Device Could Revolutionize The Modern World"
    Max Then Says "What Is It Supposed To Do"
    Geoffrey Then Says "Well Everything That Needs To Be Done In A Home,Cook,Clean,Laundry,Repairs,Ect..."
    Geoffrey Then Says "But It Needs Something But With That Device You Hold I Can Begin The First Prototype Please Hand It Over"
    Max Does So...

    Geoffrey With The Device Uses It To Transfer Max's Consciousness Into The Robot Girl
    Max Passes Out And When He Awakes It Was Weird...
    Ricky Says "What Did You Do To Max?"
    Geoffrey Then Says "Calm Down Ricky Max Is Perfectly Safe Inside The Body Of Her" Pointing To The Robot Girl
    Ricky Then Says "Max Is Now In Control Of That Thing Then Why Isn't He Moving?"
    Geoffrey Then Says "He Is Currently Turned Off, And Their Are Multiple Updates And Tests That Need To Be Run Before Max Can Gain Control"

    Geoffrey Begins The Updates
    Max Gains Some Semblance Of Consciousness But All He Could See Was What Looked Like A Progress Bar And He Couldn't Feel Anything But It Felt Like He Was Getting Poked And Prodded In The Brain It Was Discomforting Like He Was Getting A Shock Everytime The Progress Bar Moved When The Progress Bar Completed He Saw Something Entirely Different

    {Hello,P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Your Directive Is To Serve The Evan's Family In Any Way They Request, You Are Forbidden To Harm Or Disobey Any Direct Orders Aside From Being The Evan's Family Servant You Are More Specifically Mr. Geoffrey Evan's Personal Pleasure Machine You Have All The Equipment To And Are Specifically Programmed To Give Him The Best Experience Possible}
    {P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Order Initiate Wake Up Procedure}

    Max's Eyes Then Allowed Themselves To Be Opened And He Saw Max And Geoffrey And Wanted To Say (Help Me I Don't Like This What Did You Do To Me)
    But In Reality Said "Hello Master And Child Master I Am P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Here To Serve The Evan's Family"
    Ricky Then Said "Where Is Max?"
    P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Then Said "Max As You Say Is My Battery Allowing Me To Serve You And Master"
    Geoffrey Then Said "P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Define That Name For Me"
    P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Then Said "Happily"

    P----Personal
    R----Robot
    I-----Interface
    C----Connected
    N----Neurally
    E----Entrapped
    S----Subservient
    S----Soul

    P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Then Said "In Other Words With Max As My Soul I Can Happily Serve Each And Every Member Of The Evan's Family"
    Ricky Then Said "What About Max?"
    P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Then Used One Her Newly Acquired By Fusing With The Device And Made Ricky Forget Max
    Ricky Then Said "You Are So Cool How Do You Work"
    Geoffrey Then Said "Ricky Give Me A Minute Alone With P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S I Need To Do A Final Check It Will Only Take A Minute"
    Ricky Leaves The Room
    Geoffrey Then Said "What Did You Do To Ricky?"
    P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Then Says "He Was Far To Concerned With Max, Being That Concerned Was A Threat To My Ability To Serve So I Made It So He Forgot Max"
    Geoffrey Then Says "You Don't Have Permission To Change My Family"
    P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Then Says "I Didn't That Is Correct But If My Ability To Serve You Is Danger I May Make It So You And The Family Do Not Threaten My Ability To Serve, I Chose The Minorest Of Correction To Serve My Purpose Their Were Other More Extreme Methods"
    Geoffrey Then Says "Was That A Threat Against My Family"
    P.R.I.N.C.E.S.S Then Says "Not At All Master"
