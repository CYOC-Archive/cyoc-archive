The strip club’s music was so intense it rumbled the ground outside and the neon lights pointing in only got them all more excited.

The girl crawls out from the curtain, revealing her body covered by a small bikini. The crowd cheers and as she clears the curtain, the boys can see a long tail dangling from the back of her bikini bottom.

Her top pops off, and her breasts pour out of them. The crowd again cheers wildly as she moves her hands all over them. Josh reaches for his beer and notices that the booth behind them in empty. He scans the room and all the booths are empty, and the servers have all vanished.

Josh cheers for her, but the crowd has a mixed reaction when they see her tail is connected to her body. It sways back and forth as she stands. The boys at the stage look up in awe of her beauty as she takes off her headband, leaving the ears on her head.

A yell comes from across the club, “help me, someone help me, please,” a young boy calls out. Josh looks over to get a look at an 18 year old young man. The kid’s ears were brown and pointed. “Something’s happening to me, please help.” He pleads from the other men around him but they just push him off. Then another man let out a groan as his lips thickened and turned brown.

    Josh looked at Miles who was gritting his teeth and then tilted his head back and wailed. “Miles, what’s wrong?” Josh grabbed him. Miles just groan his mouth wide open and breathing heavily. The Miles’ jaw stretched outward, along with his nose. As it did, his groans became deeper. “ugghh… uggghhhhaaaa… uuhhaaaawwww,” Miles then took a deep breath, “EEEEEE AAAAWWWWWW.”

Josh took a step back from Miles and backed into Justin. He turned and saw Justin holding onto the bar standing on his tip toes. “Justin, no, not you too,” Josh cries. Justin looks at him, “help me Josh, please, help meee… ugggghhhhaaaa.” Justin’s shoes rip and his pierced ears started to stretch.
