    Jeff smiles and nods, “so, tell me a little about yourself,” he then says.

    “What would you like to know,” Dennis asks, not looking up as he continues reading the manual, “man… this all sounds so crazy,” he mumbles.

    “Well, where do you live? Are you from around here? Do you have siblings? What do you enjoy, except horse riding and races, do you have other hobbies?”

    “Must be pretty frustrating, having 13-year-old twin brothers,” Jeff says, “I have a 12-year-old brother, and he alone is constantly getting on my nerves with his stupid pranks and is just plain annoying”.

    Dennis nods, “yeah, they can be little devils,” he says, then chuckles, “but weren’t we all little devils and demons in that age?”

    “I guess,” Jeff says, “still… I never was THAT… evil!”

    Dennis chuckles, “whatever you say, Jeff,” he says, “man… this app even is able to transform people into objects and even… food! This shit is crazy scary!”

    Jeff nods his head, “yeah, in the wrong hands… who knows what chaos it could cause,” he says, “by the way, I love playing video games myself, my favorites are Doom, Call of Duty, I also enjoy playing virtual novels”.

    “Cool,” Dennis says, still reading the manual, “you seem like a cool guy, would you like to hang out once you’re back to normal?”

    Jeff nods, “I’d love to, Dennis,” he says, “you seem like a cool dude yourself!”

    Dennis chuckles, “I try,” he says, finally he looks up at Jeff, “would you mind if I’d copy the app over onto my phone? I could have some fun with it… like transforming my brothers into something! I promise not to do anything evil!”

    Jeff tilts his head, “I goes it won’t be any harm,” he says, “just don’t copy my file over, even though I trust you, I’d feel less nervous”.
