    Scientists huddles around the window to get a closer view of the test. On the other side of the window was a naked man, pacing the room absentmindedly.

    "Are we ready" asked one of the scientists,
    "Yeah, test takes place in two minutes" muttered another

    In the room, the man paced and looked at wall length mirror, his body was well built, he had a handsome face that was dusted with stubble.

    "HEY" he called, "You're wasting my time is the photographer showing up or not"

    One of the Scientists frowned, "Photographer?"
    "He thinks he's at a porn photo shoot" muttered another scientist, "in a minute we'll ask him to pose and then we release our little virus."

    The man looked around impatiently, one of the scientists moved forwards and spoke into a microphone. "OK, we don't have a photographer but we have several camera's hidden around the room so if you just begin to pose, we'll begin the shoot."

    The man looked suspiciously around, shrugged and began to strike poses, showing off his muscles.

    "OK NOW" called a scientist, a switch was flicked and inside the room there came a quiet hissing sound as a gas containing the scientists mix slowly seeped into the room.

    The man suddenly felt funny, his hand began to stiffen, looking down he saw that his hand was turning grey, he tried to move it but it remained still and hard, it has turned to stone.

    "WHA, WHATS HAPPENING" he cried, stone began to form on his forearms, he ran to the door but it was locked. He growled and punched the door with his free hand, nothing happened. His fist suddenly became stone like his other hand and looking down, he saw that his chest was nearly completely covered in grey stone. He looked at the mirror. "This was no shoot" he thought, "they must be watching me behind the mirror."

    He began to move forwards, struggling as his legs and feet began to transform, running he pulled back his stone fist and rammed it forwards into the mirror.

    SMASH!

    There was pandemonium, scientists screamed and tried to run from the broken window. The man fell to his knees and watches as his legs feet and cock became stone, he felt it rising to his neck and gasped, the virus then completed its work, leaving a naked statue.

    The scientists screamed as their bodies to began to turn to stone, soon, the whole lab was filled with statues and the virus itself had left and was now spreading through the woods that surrounded the lab infecting the water, animals in close vicinity quickly turned to stone, the virus was free, who becomes infected next....
