    Bea Gets What She Wants

    Not far away from Bea’s home, which is located at the edge of a large field with a large forest surrounding it, a little fairy is sitting on one of the branches of a tree seeming to sulk, “I’m SO bored,” the little creature grumbles to herself, “I wish something interesting and fun would happen”.

    Just as she says that, her pointy ears twitch when she hears, what sounds like a young girl’s voice, saying, “I wish I had magical powers!”

    The little creature’s curiosity is awoken, “hm… I wonder why she’d like to have magical powers,” she says to herself, “let’s have a chat with her,” and with that the fairy spreads her wings and flies straight for the house she heard the voice come from.

    Once the fairy arrives at the house, she peeks in through a window and sees a young girl sitting on her bed, the fairy taps on the window with her small fist and grins when she sees the girl turn, her eyes wide and mouth hanging open in surprise.

    The fairy then sees the girl jump to her feet and soon the window is pulled open, “I must be dreaming,” the girl says, “a f-fairy?”

    The little fairy giggles as she flies into the room, “oh, you aren’t dreaming,” she says, “we fairies are real, we just don’t show ourselves to humans!”

    “But… But you are showing yourself to me,” the girl says, “my… my name is Beatrix… but I prefer Bea!”

    The fairy smiles, “you can call me Misty,” she says, “now, I’m here because I have heard that you wish to have magic powers. I’m curious to know the reason!”

    Bea’s eyes widen, “I… I have an older brother,” she says, “I hate him! He stole my parents from me! He gets all the coolest things and they treat him like a prince! I DESERVE all the attention! I’m better than him!”

    The fairy nods, “I know what you mean,” she says, “you want powers to get rid of that pesky boy?”

    Bea nods, “ya! I have so many ideas of how to get rid of him,” she says, “then I’ll finally have MY parents back!”

    Misty smiles, “well, since your reason makes sense,” she says, “I’ll give you that power!”

    Bea’s eyes widen, “really,” she asks, sounding surprised and excited.

    Misty nods, “yes! I promise there is no trick,” she says, “once I’m done, you have extremely powerful magic, you be able to shape shift and even become a fairy yourself, you will be able to hypnotize and manipulate other humans and transform anyone and anything into anything you want!”

    Bea grins, “what are you waiting for?”

    Misty giggles, “hold still, this won’t take long,” she says.

    Bea holds her breath, standing completely still, she then watches as the fairy hold out a tiny hand and a ball of white light starts to form, growing in size until it’s as bid as a tennis ball.

    Mist suddenly throws the orb directly at Bea, the orb hitting the girl in the chest, sinking into her body.

    Once the orb had sunken into the girl’s body, Bea suddenly feels a tingling sensation, which intensifies, the 12-year-old feels as though electricity is rushing through her body, becoming stronger and stronger, her blue eyes start to glow a little.

    Bea grins wide, “I feel so powerful,” she says, “thanks, Misty,” she then says.

    “My pleasure,” the fairy says, smiling.

    “How can I ever thank you,” Bea asks.

    “Well, if you allow me to be your companion, since I’m curious and would like to see what you’ll do with your power, I’ll promise to help you whenever you need it!”

    Bea grins, “of course, I’d be happy to have you as my companion,” she says, “hehe… now… let’s find that brother of mine,” with that, Bea and Misty, perching on Bea’s shoulder, head out of the room to search for Bea’s brother.
