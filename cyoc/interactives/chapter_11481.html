    "On the other hand it would be such a waste to have an intelligent and ...well perfect specimen get away! I have something for you that would make your life be on the wild side!" said Dr. Homen

    The Doctor walked about as if an animal caged and ready to strike.

    "Will you accept being part of my most worthy experiment?" asked the lady Doctor as her twinkling eye stared at Marcia.

    Marcia tensed up and was ready to put this mad man in her grave.

    Buzz...zap! Went the tasser as Marcia was lost both body and mind to a jolting electric charge sending to the floor and out cold.

    "Marcia, Marcia !" said a voice as she slowly opened her eyes.

    Suddenly fear took hold, she was inside one of those clear plastic tanks. Her eyes had goggles, her nose fitted with two plastic tubes, her mouth taped shut as teathers strung her suspended in the tank.

    She tried to move but the strong teathers allowed only short and little movements, she was in trouble!

    "I see we have awakened, you had me scared, I thought my best experiment might die before her time!" said the Doctor through a mic and speaker set into the tank.

    "Murah, maaauah!" tried Marcia to talk.

    "Easy, we don't want to break our lifelines or one might drown before I could retrieve her from the sealed tank!" said the Doctor with an evil grin.

    Marcia oggled the Doctor through the murky water which seemed the color of light tea water. She tried to move but found herself stuck and held tight.

    "Haat har hoo hooin hoo heee?" asked the muffled voice of Marcia.

    "What are you doing to me? Oh I have a wonderous plan for you my dear Marcia! Tommy was I might say a prototype and his treatment had some flaws. You on the other hand although not to become a wolf as he, you will help sponser my experiments all in due time! I have found that twp primary Chromosomes provide the pattern to physical growth. These two when repostioned begin a reconfiguration of the total organism. I maybe an easier term, the human body slowly and with a new prime growth priority slowly changes from one species to another. Now I know your next question, if I can make the change, can it be reverted? NO! It is easy to change the Chromosome's arranged pattern, but then as the human form grows it becomes a larger organism unto itself. Once you have grown larger I know of no way to make you shrink! In short my dear over the course of your next six weeks in that tank you will have a front row seat watching that sweet human female self be rerouted into a big strong Hereford Bull!" explained the Lady Doctor.

    Marcia screamed from within her tank. She no doubt knew that the Doctor had maybe some true ability, but worse yet poor Marcia might lose her female self and species to that of the Bovine way of life.
