    Mary rolled the dice and moved her token, carrying Bob, towards the river. Toby didn't want Mary to do this, but the curse stopped him from ordering her to move away. As Mary reached the river, the Uproot curse card disappeared from in front of Bob. Mary passed the dice over to Dina.
    Dina rolled the dice with some difficulty, considering her size, and moved her token towards Mary, Bob and Toby.
    Ryan decided to move Lissi as well. He struggled, trying to life Lissi's token, then realised that it wouldn't work unless Lisi physically was able to ride him.
    "Lissi, I need you to get on my back," he said. Seeing what Ryan wanted, Lissi walked around the table and climbed onto the horse-like portion of Ryan's body.
    "Are you comfortable there?" Ryan asked.
    "No," replied Lissi, still forced to honesty.
    "I can't do anything about that," said Ryan, watching Lissi's token moving onto his. He rolled the dice and moved in the same direction as Dina.
    It was now Toby's turn. He knew that he could stop the others from winning by moving the other way if he got off Mary, but he had told Mary specifically not to take him off. He was forced to ride Mary closer towards the others, then grudginly passed the dice to Lissi.
    Lissi couldn't do anything. She had used all her cards, and was riding Ryan. She passed the dice to Bob.
    Bob took the dice and realised that he couldn't move with his legs like that. Instead, he decided to play his last card on Dina. She looked at it and read.
    "BODY: Giant. This card will increase a person's size and strength twenty times." As she read, her body started to grow larger.
    Ryan reached over to Dina and said, "Let me put you on the ground."
    "Why?" asked Dina.
    "Because when you grow back to your usual size, your centaur body won't fit on the table."
    Dina let Ryan pick her up by her horse back. As Ryan set her down on the ground again, she was reaching waist height. In another few seconds, she was back to her usual size, except still in the form of a centaur. Bob passed the dice to Mary. Dina had two cards left, and Ryan had the other one. The players were getting close to each other, but was it too late to win?
