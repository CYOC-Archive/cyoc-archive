    Lisa couldn't believe this. the chandelier had become a thick fence that separated her from her family. She saw a door that wasn't there a second ago. She stepped into a hallway and walked. eventually, she found a staircase.

    about halfway down the staircase became a steep ramp. She slid down as a trapdoor opened up. she found herself on a pile of clothes. looking up she saw the door close. 'why did it even do that? I'm already separated from my family'.

    getting out of the pile she found herself in the basement. rows of boxes created a smaller maze in this room. they each said a couple of different holidays. She passed the dryer to see a note stuck within. opening it she was hit with a blast of heat.

    her hair turned from black to fiery red. it soon wrapped around her head. this gave her a lion's mane. She felt this and looked in the dryer's glass door. she then picked up the note.

    "beware of traps like the one you've experienced. it is clear your family did not know of the power of this game. do not worry you can change back. just get to the exit first and you'll choose what happens. if you like anything that is altered about your family you can keep it. I'll make sure to alter reality so you can" she read out loud.

    moving on she found the washing machine. she grabbed an umbrella and opened it from distance. a torrent of water shot out like a fire hose. checking inside she found a piece of metal. it had the image of a knight.

    She soon found a door with a plaque puzzle. on it were a cave and a village. she placed the knight on the right and it clicks. One spot remained empty. looking up she saw the heating pipes for the house.

    'if this game has a sense of humor then the other piece should be there'. Following the pipes led to a dead end. a gravestone decoration was in front of her. On it was an inscription. 'I have horns but I can’t beep. I like to bleat but I am not a sheep. name me'.

    "I guess a ram," she said. the letters glowed as did her head. She felt something on her head. feeling she now had a pair of ram horns on her head. "wait rams are sheep. I mean goats" She closed her eyes as the tomb glowed again.

    the Halloween decoration vanished. a new path was revealed. still carrying the umbrella she approached the furnace. opening it she found a red hot dragon plate. she grabbed it with the umbrella.

    once on the ground, the umbrella caught on fire. it burned her slightly. her hand soon became red. She saw scales covering it. her nails became claws. finally, it grew twice as big as his left hand.

    she then saw the fire poker from the living room hidden by some coals. 'guess I was supposed to use that huh'. she grabbed the dragon plate and went back to the door. placing it in revealed another hallway. this one seemed to look like a dungeon or something. she continued onward.
