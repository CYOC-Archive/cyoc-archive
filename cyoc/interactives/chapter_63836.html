    Revenge of Larry the Lizard

    added 12 years ago A I O

    Now the bikers name was Rudy,and all his biker friends laughed and called him
    names for a reason other than his shiny red nose.

    Whilst all the other bikers cruised around in Nortons,Indians and
    Harleys,Rudy had to make do with a Citroen 49cc moped.

    So it was that the gang got on their muscle machines and slammed down
    the kick starters,and the air was filled with the throaty rumble of their engines.

    Alas Rudy had to set for pedaling his Citron furiously to get its tiny
    washing machine motor going,and went onto the road looking like a adult on a
    kiddie bike that went "bzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzz"

    It wasn't long before Rudys biker buddies had pulled away and Rudy went
    puttering down the road at the legally mandated maximum speed limit of
    25 mph.

    He liked this however,since it gave him time to think,observe and smell the
    roses as the moped puttered along.

    As he was doing all this sightseeing,he noticed his bike had acquired a
    new set of paint detailing.Over its yellow frame coiled the green scaled paint decal
    of a lizard,namely the one he'd squashed.

    And the lizard looked like it was looking at him.

    Rudy puttered on a few more miles,then drove off to the side and shut off
    the engine and smoked a joint as he stared at the painted lizard decal staring back at them

    Then the decal moved,and Rudy nearly jumped out of his stormtrooper
    boots.

    He furiously puffed on that joint,and was about to considered it was all
    a illusion,a hallucination,when the tiny squeaky voice came from somewhere near the Citroens
    gas tank.

    "Hello Rudy,my names Larry'...
