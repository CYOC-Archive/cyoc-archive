    Colby's Training

    added 2 years ago O

    Suddenly hear a knock at the door. You place the the cookie tin down as you walk to the front door and open it to see A bearded man wearing a tank top and some sunglasses. "Hey Colby, it's time for your daily jog" the man says. "Excuse me...what?" You say confused. You've never heard of Colby running or even to be exact. "Don't you remember? Your Dad asked me to take you with me on my jogs everyday." the man said.

    You frown at the sudden information remembering how much harder it was engaging in physical activity as Colby. You sure didn't want to be running in his body. Suddenly you remember Colby's quick acceptance of George's idea of switching bodies. Perhaps he agreed easily since he knew it would give him the opportunity to skip his exercise. "Well go get changed and I'll meet you in 10 minutes" the man said as he begins to stretch. You close the door and head upstairs to Colby's room where you find his phone and call Colby. There's no response and after trying multiple times you ultimately decide you have no choice and find a pair of running shorts and a t-shirt before going back outside.

    "Let's go Colby" the man says eagerly as he begins sprinting. You struggle to catch up to him and find yourself out of breath quickly. "I'm going to kill Colby for this" you think as you run. After half an hour of running through town, the two of you return to Colby's house and you nearly pass out from exhaustion. "That was a good run Colby. See you tomorrow" he says cheerfully before heading inside the house next door. "Yeah tomorrow" you think realizing you would be long gone by then. You get up and drag yourself inside Colby's house where you head back to the bathroom. All the running has caused you to sweat a ton and for the second time of the day you take a shower as Colby.

    This time you're quick about it but it doesn't change the fact that taking a shower in someone else's body makes you feel strange. After you dry off you quickly change into Colby's pajamas ready to lay in bed for the rest of the day. You then hear Colby's phone ring and see that Colby is calling you.

    "Hello" you say picking up.
    "So how was your run?" Colby says in your voice failing to hide his laughter.
    "Fuck you! That was the worst thing I even had to do!" you say angrily.
    "Don't get mad at me! It's all my Dad's idea... he wants me to lose more weight so he thought that if I joined Mr. Williams on his runs it would motivate me" Colby explains.
    "Well it sure didn't motivate me, the only thing I felt was pain" you say.
    "Sorry I just did not feel like doing it today. Anyway I gotta go" Colby says.

    You say goodbye to Colby and spend the rest of the day on your phone. Since it was summer you didn't have any homework and after you run you didn't want to go outside for the rest of the day. You only go downstairs to eat dinner and head back to Colby's room. Before you know it your alarm is going off at 7am and you quickly turn it off before getting changed. You had to get to the park, the sooner you did the faster you'll get your body back. You then meet George and Colby inside the men's restroom at 8am and...

    What do you do now?
