    Rebeca walked over to the stable master. She still wasn't sure what she would say, but she hoped it was
    convincing. When she got over to him, a yellow exclamation point lit up above her head. The stable master
    looked around, trying to find an adventurer. Finding none, he walked over and read the quest.

    --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Becoming a PC

    Hello there. I am Rebeca the orphan, at least in this form. I am here to help you become a Player Character.
    We have been monitoring you, and think you would be an excellent addition to the Transformazone Player List.
    Should you accept, you will be transported to the character creation, and set back to level one. A life of
    danger and thrill will lie before you, but be warned, a Player Character's life is not always easy. You may
    find yourself as an alt, or your player may change you drastically. You may not even end up in the same world
    you're in now. Then again, that is part of the fun isn't it? So, do you accept the greatest and most dangerous
    mission of all? The one that leads to all others?

    Objectives:

    Accept the quest

    Rewards:

    Become a Player Character
    --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    The stable master hit accept, and was immediately transported away. All that was left of his NPC days was the
    loot that Rebeca would redistribute to the Players inside. Surprisingly, there was some decent armor, and even
    a trinket that increased mount speed. When the Players eventually came out, they found a little orphan girl
    crying, with an exclamation point over her head.

    "Guys, let me accept this real quick." One player, a pirate, said.

    "Hang on, share it with me later. I don't have it." Another said.

    "Me neither. Let me check the wiki." A third said before popping into AFK.

    The pirate came over and accepted Rebeca's quest, not bothering to read it. After sharing it with the others,
    one of them actually read through.

    "Dude, look at the trinket in the rewards!" He said.

    "Beats both the ones I'm using now." Said the pirate.

    "And all we have to do is kill the Marauding Trio." Said the last.

    "Guys, the wiki had an 'under construction' page for the quest and the girl. Looks like we're out of luck."
    The formerly AFK one said.

    "Not even a little man. Come on, we'll fill you in on the way." The pirate replied.

    As the players walked off, Will and Logan came out of hiding.

    "I think /dance-ing is in order." Logan said.

    "I can't believe it worked." Will said.

    "It was your plan though." Rebeca replied.

    "Yeah, but I didn't expect him to actually become a PC. Plus, I thought we were dead when they checked the
    wiki." Will explained.

    "Looks like the sprite actually wants us to win this one." Will said as he danced the macarena.

    "Yeah. I guess this one is supposed to be easy, first level and all. Now all we have to do is wait." Rebeca
    said, sitting down.

    "This is gonna be so cool!" Will said.
