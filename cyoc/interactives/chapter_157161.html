    John muttered "Chronos?"

    Chronos rang in John's mind "Sure I gave her time but I'm the god of time. How do you get soul from the Sun?"
    John asked "Silky? do you also feel scared that beings of unimaginable power are starting to become more involved in this world now?"
    Silky said "Duh, while I trust Amaterasu there are gods like Susano who can be abrasive at best. Then if Chronos is also out there then that implies there are probably also Zeus and other Greeks that are considerably flawed by nature... plus who knows what other pantheons?.. Hey! We've been friends for a long time why can't she?... Fine... Maybe not allowing her in your shrine is understandable."
