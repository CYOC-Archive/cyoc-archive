    Animalia Milestone: 1000

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Alphagor 6 years ago O

    It is both my pleasure and honor to announce that of this chapter, the Animalia thread is now 1000 chapters long. It has really been a rather interesting and exhilarating journey. When I first added the first part of this thread, I didn’t think it would ever go anywhere. But then makutamon started adding chapters to it and we’ve been able to progress further. But we might not have gone as far as we have if s1 hadn’t also added stability to this story. Between the three of us, we’ve created a captivating saga that’s provocative in more ways than one.

    But we weren’t the only writers of this story. Though we were the main three, there have been others who have contributed. A special mention should go to JosieHadly, who first introduced Heather as a dinosaur Animalian and thus got the Dinotopian part of this story started. And while most of the ideas attempted by AlanSmithee did not end up in the main continuity, the one idea that stuck was Mary Piper the rat queen, which created an interesting angle for the story. And while Bahamut626 and AnxiousLioness had written fewer chapters, they are no less appreciated.

    Overall, I want to thank everyone who contributed to getting us to this big milestone. This story has grown bigger than I ever would have imagined and it still isn’t anywhere close to being finished. I eagerly anticipate what future chapters of Animalia will tell.
