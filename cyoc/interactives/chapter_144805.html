    The Zoo

    Forever a fat T-rex

    added by Anonymous 4 years ago BM Fat

    You started freaking out at the fact that this would be permanent and you start clawing at where the flap to get in used to be.

    “It’s no use once it’s on there good there is now way in the world to get it off” Rose said.

    “Well how am I supposed to live at my house like this?” You ask pointing to your fat body.

    “You could work for the zoo. We already got some people doing it after something like this happened, and you get to live on site” Rose said.

    “Ok, but how am I going to explain this to my parents and my friends?” You ask.

    “Oh don’t worry the zoo will take care of that” Rose said.

    Rose walks you to a building close to the front of the zoo.

    “We have a customer who had some trouble with a costume and now he is requesting a job” Rose says to the lady in the front.

    “Ok, the manager will see you in a few minutes” the lady says.

    You squeeze yourself into a chair and you wait for a bit. You still felt hungry even after the 200 pounds of food you ate and you were only growing hungrier. After a bit of waiting you are greeted by a older looking fellow.

    “Nice to meet you, and sorry about the costume mishap” he said shaking your hand.

    “I’ve been looking to find a way to make them not do that but here we are, but anyway I hear that your looking for a job at the zoo?” He asks.

    “Uh, Yes, do you think there’s anything I can do?” You ask.

    “Why yes there is we always have room for more employees, and judgin by your looks you probably could be a mascot of sorts” he says.

    “So what will I do exactly?” You ask.

    “You would walk around, promote things we sell, that sort of thing” he says.

    “That sounds ok, but when do I work?” you ask.

    “Just when ur needed” he says, “well I’ll go fill all this out and what was ur name?” He asked.

    “Just call me scaly” you say.

    “Ok why don’t you go show scaly to his room” he says pointing to the receptionist.

    “Right away sir” she says grabbing a key. She leads you to another building which seemed a little weird, but this other one was so much bigger and was somewhat hidden from the public. She brings you to your room and the door had a number 8 engraved into the wood. “This will be your room” she said opening the door. The room was beyond what you thought it would be. The “room” was basically a full sized 2 story house with plenty of space. “Here is your key” she said giving you a key.

    The receptionist walks away before you could ask any questions. You easily manage to get into your room without any hassle, it was like the door was specially made for your almost 800 pound 9ft tall body. After the day you had all you could do was flop down onto your new bed and rest. While laying down you realize that you didn’t even get the managers name before drifting off into sleep. You wake up to your stomach begging for food.

    What do you do now?
