    “Oh, I will,” I muttered quietly to myself. “I most certainly will.”

    I closed the door behind me and half ran to the living room, placed the package on the coffee table, and with lightning speed ripped the brown paper from the box.

    Emblazoned upon the box’s side was the name: “Magicell® Transformation Industries”. I flipped open the box’s top and excitedly began rummaging through its contents. Among the items I retrieved from the box was an informational compact disc, an instruction manual, a USB and A/C adapter cable, and a rectangular device with a small LCD screen and multiple buttons covering its main surface. This was what I wanted. The advertisements for this little device boasted that it was capable of transforming a human being into whatever he or she desired. The device cost quite a bit of money, but if it truly worked, it would be well worth it.

    I set the device down and opened the instruction manual. On its first page, read a message: “Thank you for purchasing Magicell®’s newest and most efficient transformation device! This hand-held construction of science and sorcery lets you wield the power to transform yourself into anything you can imagine. This guide will help. . . Well, guide you toward making your transformations as safe and pleasurable as possible. Your ultimate dream transformation experience is only minutes away. Please read and follow the instructions in this manual to begin.”

After a moment, the screen lit up, and the Magicell® logo flashed across the screen. When the logo faded out, a menu popped up. The menu showed a number of options to choose from:

    1)Inanimate Transformation

    2)Plant Transformation

    3)Food Transformation

    4)Transgender

    5)Animal Transformation

    6)Mythical Creature Transformation

    7)Dinosaur Transformation

    8)Elemental Reconfiguration

    9)Size Modification

    10)Shape Modification

    11)Random Category

All in all, I didn’t really care which one I chose, so rolling a random category was definitely an option.
