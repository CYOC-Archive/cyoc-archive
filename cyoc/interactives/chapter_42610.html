    Resistance is Not Futile

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by loony28 14 years ago O

    You just laid on the ground stunned. How could you have missed the fact that you were a Terminator? Everything had a blue tint to it that you hadn't notice before. The blue reminds you of Cameron off of Terminator: The Sara Connor Chronicles. You notice something on your hud.

    CYBERDYNE SYSTEMS SERIES 900 MODEL 475 VERSION 1.0

    You had never heard of the T-900 before. Perhaps that was what Cameron was supposed to be. Another message comes up.

    MISSION OBJECTIVES UPDATE
    PRIMARY MISSION OBJECTIVE: TRAVEL TO JULY 4, 2008 AND TERMINATE KYLE REESE
    SECONDARY MISSION OBJECTIVE: NONE

    You were stunned again. You were to go back in time to 2008 and kill Kyle Reese when he was a boy. That could mean no John Connor to eventually lead the resistance. You find yourself on your feet before you know it and starting to walk off in one direction. You couldn't let this happen. You start fighting for control and your walk becomes irregular. Suddenly five messages pop up.

    WARNING: UNKNOWN SYSTEM ERROR
    CYBERDYNE NETWORK CONNECTION TERMINATED
    PRIMARY MISSION OBJECTIVE: NONE
    SECONDARY MISSION OBJECTIVE: NONE
    RESETTING

    Your vision suddenly went dark. You don't know how long you stayed like that but when it came back you were in a different place. Your series, model, and version number came up along with three messages.

    UNABLE TO CONNECT TO CYBERDYNE SYSTEMS: AUTONOMOUS FUNCTION ENABLED
    PRIMARY MISSION OBJECTIVE: OBEY JOHN CONNOR'S ORDERS
    SECONDARY MISSION OBJECTIVE: OBEY KATE CONNOR'S ORDERS

    You seem to have been reprogrammed. You look around to see where you're at. You're in some type of building laying one your back. In a moment a figure comes into your field of view. The files you have identify him as John Connor. Before you can really think you blurt out "John, Skynet is after Kyle now."

    John looks around and you hear several people leave. After he asks "Why did you tell me that?"

    "Because you need to know" you said. "They're targeting him in 2008. I was to go back to Terminate him."

    "I hadn't ask you anything yet" John said. "Why did you speak?"

    "I'm going to tell you something that you might find hard to believe" you said. "I'm from an alternate reality. I came to this reality using my watch to create a portal. I didn't know the scientists had programmed your reality into it and I as sure as hell didn't think I would be a Terminator. How's Mike by the way?"

    "He's a little battered but he'll be alright" someone says moving into your view. Your files indicate that she's Kate Connor. "Why are you concerned about him?"

    "I'm not the type of Terminator you're used to dealing with" you said. "How about the others in the base?" The look on their faces told you the answer. "How many?" you asked quietly.

    "Twenty-five resistance fighters and thirty civilians" John said.

    "May their souls rest in peace" you said.

    John and Kate looked surprised at that. Finally Kate said "You're right. You're not the type of Terminator we're used to dealing with. What do we now?"

    "We send someone back to protect Kyle" John said. "What's your series and model number?"

    "Cyberdyne Systems Series 900 Model 475" you say without hesitation.

    "They're getting closer to the T-1000" Kate said.

    "I know" John said. "We'll need to watch for Uncle Bob."

    "Who do we send back though?" Kate asked.

    John looks at you and says "I think it should be her."

    "I'll need to take my watch with me" you said.

    "Only living matter can go back" John said.

    "I can get around that easily" you said. "I'll put the watch inside me."

    "Well before we send you back I want to ask you something" John said. "Do you want to go back and protect Kyle?"

    What do you do now?
