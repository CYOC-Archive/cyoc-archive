    "That was interesting, wasn't it?" Mika studied Katie was she had gotten older at twice the speed as any of the other age changes.

    "What was?" Tricia climbed into the back of the van with the other kids as the 'adults' were up front. Or rather Mika shared a space with Tricia and Mary while Sammy was up in the second row with Jake. Kellie and Katie were in the driver and passenger seat.

    "Katie became eight years older in about a minute. That's a lot faster than any other age changes." The van started to go to the next street.

    "Make me your age quickly. We will be to the next stop in about a minute. "

    "You just want to be six again." Tricia's voice was as dry as a six year old could be.

    Mika didn't answer immediately. "That too, I. . ." Her voice cracked as the College Freshman had become a High School Junior. Two years in about ten seconds. Mika reached up to feel her quickly shrinking bust as she became sweet sixteen again. Inches and years melted off of the Asian girl quickly.

    Mary and Tricia watched in awe as their friend almost imploded back to childhood. At the slower speeds, the age changes weren't as visibly dramatic but this was incredible. And both of them thought for a moment that it might be fun to try in private, at home, and with clothes that don't change size.

    "This is so neat. Oh wow." Mika heard her whisper change from teen to child in that sentence. She felt years of puberty disappeared in seconds as her breasts disappeared.

    Her feet rose off the floor of the minivan as she quickly lost height to childhood. She turned to Tricia and Mary and was startled. Her friends were so big now.

    She was equally surprised as she got out of the van. It was easy to get in as an adult but a much bigger step to get out.
