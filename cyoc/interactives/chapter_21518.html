    No sooner had Kyle begun to curse his luck did he see a pair of headlights appear down the road. Rallying his battered body for one last bit of exertion, he charged the car with as much speed as his sprained ankle would allow.
    As the car, a small blue sedan, approached, the driver leaned on the horn before skidding to a stop right in front of Kyle.
    "Hey, get out of the road, puto!" came a slightly accented female voice from inside. Kyle recognized her as Mairsa, yet another "cool" girl from his school.
    He collapsed on her windshield. "Mairsa... I don't have time to explain. Please, just get me out of here!"
    "Hey, you're that nerd from school. Damn, yo, you look beat the fuck up," she observed.
    From above, Kyle heard two monstrous shrieks. He knew they'd pinpointed him.
    "Please!" he begged.
    Mairsa shook her head, "I'll call 911 for you, but you ain't bleeding all over my..."
    "Transform or dare?" Kyle cut her off.
    "Say what?"
    Kyle started counting to himself silently, waiting for the time to elapse before he could choose her fate for her. He doubted she'd choose one.
    "What the hell is wrong with you?" Mairsa speculated. "How do I know that's even your blood? You look like an axe murderer."
    She put her car into reverse and began to pull away.
    "DARE!" he exclaimed. Mairsa's time limit was up. "I DARE YOU TO GET ME THE FUCK OUT OF HERE!"
    Sure enough, the girl seemed to have a sudden change of heart. She stopped to car. "Okay, fine, get i... WHAT THE HELL IS THAT!?!?"
    Kyle turned just in time to see a red, wolf-headed demon divebombing straight at him. He hit the floor just in time, her talons raking his back but unable to get a grip on him.
    Grimacing in pain, he threw himself into the passenger's seat next to Mairsa. She wasted no time in flooring the gas, taking off along the rural streets at a dangerous speed.
    "What did you get me into?" Mairsa whimpered, her brown eyes wide with terror.
    "I can't explain now. Just drive! Try to get somewhere more populated!" Kyle yelled.
    The car suddenly lurched downward under a heavy impact from above. Black claws punctured the roof of the car, tearing away metal like it was cardboard. In a matter of seconds, the front half of car's roof was gone.
    Kyle looked up to see his sister's corrupted form leering down at him, vaginal mouth slobbering down on him in anticipation. Mairsa screamed, swerving the vehicle violently from side to side. Kim dug her toe claws into the remnants of the roof, remaining in a stable, standing position in spite of the violent motion. She cackled horrifically as she reached down for Kyle, grabbing him by his shirt.
    Out of the corner of his eye, he saw a container of unopened motor oil at his feet. He snatched it up with his good arm, fumbling with the cap while Kim raised him up out of the car.
    Kim roared in victory, preparing to take flight with her captive. Kyle thumbed open the cap and sloshed viscous oil into the creature's eyes. Kim reflexively dropped her prey, shrieking as she attempted to clear her burning eyes.
    With a sharp turn, Mairsa managed to finally shake the creature loose from her car.
    Kyle looked back for any sign of his sister, but all he saw was deserted road. After a moment he said, "I think we lost them."
