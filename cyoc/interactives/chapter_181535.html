    Tina tapped her feet onto the carpet, not noticing the toes of her feet break out of her heeled shoes. Sindy and Trish trotted around the store, every so often catching their balance, walking on two hooved legs, their outfits altered to be slightly more custom fit around the legs.

    A couple of minutes later the shoe shop clerk arrived back, with a small black rectangular box,

    "you know when you came in here asking for a shoe I wasn't sure we would have anything for you, but we did apparently," the clerk said scratching his head, opening up the box he presented a pair of metal horseshoes in perfect condition.

    "It's the best thing we have that will fit in afraid," he said handing the box to Tina

    The girls all gathered round looking at the horseshoe, taking turns discussing it while the clerk was obviously a bit distressed and wished to hurry this along, the site of these girls creeped him out.

    Erin meanwhile had been watching from afar, muffling her laughs so much she was surprised people weren't looking at her. she couldn't believe that everyone acted normally around them, looking more cow from the udder down than human. Generating a few more sentences, Eren bit her lip as she was hoping for a good prompt, comeone she said to herself as she waited for the bimbo trio to do something.

    "So like where do we want to go next? " Trish asked the group

    "I don't know but like I haven't got any jewelry in a while, how about we go to check out some out?" Tina suggested
