Every one of your posters had changed to either either a boy band girly cartoon character. Your clothes had each been replaced with female versions in brighter colors. Your work out set had been replaced with a heart shaped vanity mirror complete with make up and various pictures pinned around.

    You stumble out of bed to get a look at your new self only to fall on your face not used to your new center of gravity. You let out a groan and push your self up noticing how weak and dainty your new arms where, along with your long pink nails.

    In the mirror what you saw shocked you to your core. Instead of your normal reflection you instead saw a rather cute blond girl around your age with her hair done up in two long pig tails. Her ears where pierced with unicorn earrings. Lips full covered with red lip stick. She was wearing a pink t shirt that read “Daddy’s Princess” in the center of a red heart. You also noticed the shirt was a little tight on your chest which as a D cup at your best estimate, the shirt also left your smooth stomach exposed showing off a navel piercing. That was something you never considered before but you had to admit you kind of liked it. As you admired your self, and your hour glass body in the mirror doing various selfie posses, then you noticed something, the pictures along the sides. The girls looked almost like... oh no!

    You pulled out your phone which you instinctively knew was in the outer pocket of your purse and was also now bedazzled and scrolled through your library, as you did your heart sank. You weren’t the only one changed. All your pictures of your friends where replaced with them as females dressed in similarly trendy outfits to your own. You winced as you saw Tom (now Tina) wearing heals for the first time. Your trip to the lake now had all of you sunbathing rather than fishing like before, though you did half to admit Brian (now Bri) was rock’n that red bikini. You wondered if you could borrow it. Lastly Sam (now Sara)’s 19th birthday party. Instead of a black pick up truck she got a blue buggy. As you scrolled through new memories where added on top of your old ones. That time Tina laughed so hard fro yo shot out her nose. Bri needing your borrow one of your breath mints because she thought Thomas asked her on a date. You smiled, they where still your friends then shook your head. You REALLY hopped their memories changed otherwise things would be incredibly awkward the next time you all met.

    Just then you heard a knock on the door.

    Your sister entered in her pajamas.

    “You ready?”

    You tilted your head.

    ‘Ready for what?’ You then winced. You sounded just like an idiot blond. Your sister rolled her eyes.

    “Your sleep over!” She said. “Did you even remember to invite your friends?”

    You blinked as realization dawned on you ,YOUR sleep over?

    ‘Oh! Yea I remember.’ You lied. ‘It’s just... do you notice anything... different?’

    Your sister scanned your room. Then you then shrugged.

    “No everything’s the same.” She responded.

    ‘You suuuuuure?’ You pressed leanings forward trying to draw emphasis to your jiggling chest.

    “You feeling alright?” She asked taking a step back...
