Really had it not been for a rush to try and stop the trouble in a way that was hoped I was planning on preposing in a few years’ time…

But getting back to Sylia being a mermaid now you see when she was maybe 5, 6… at least 7 years old her folks died and by her bad luck her cousin who she was genetically incompatible with for organ transplanting needed a life saving transplant, said cousin’s mother cut a deal with a specialized lab to get the transplant underway but that required ‘selling’ Sylia to them to be among a selection of people who’s human rights were revoked to be turned into hybrid creatures, usually mythological. Naturally if you knew said aunt and Sylia’s late father you’d know how bad their sibling rivalry was that spilled over into an inner family feud (didn’t help this ‘selling’ acted as a loophole blacking said aunt getting a major inheritance Sylvia got that was otherwise blocked from the aunt’s family).

Now by now you might have realized Sylvia and I are childhood friends.
