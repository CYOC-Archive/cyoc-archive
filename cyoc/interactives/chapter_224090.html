    Animal Costumes Mark 2 - The end of the Predator & Prey Feral Freshwater Fish Year

    added by Raven Cross 18 days ago A Anthro Aquatic Vore

    You as a feral rainbow trout were swimming around as fast as you could avoiding all the aquatic predators in the tank with you. You caught sight of a few of the other feral fish being caught & devoured. The aquatic predators were being true predatory animals. Ripping with teeth & claws or just devouring fish whole. It was a truly amazing sight to see. You could smell the blood & all the predators. You knew all the fish were like you. You got so distracted by watching that you didn't notice the one who grabbed you. You were turned around & saw an anthro otter holding you. [Could this otter be Andrew?] You thought wondering. The anthromorphic otter took you to the surface. He paused almost as if thinking. He lifted you up a bit & opened his mouth.
    The anthromorphic male otter POV:
    I at first thought about revealing to this rainbow trout that I was Andrew. But I decided against it. I was sure he didn't want to know if I was by his look. And besides I was devouring him anyways. So it didn't matter if he knew. I reminded myself that all the feral fish Animal Costume Users understood that putting on the costumes meant becoming feral fish permanently; & being devoured at the end of the night. They would be just fish that eventually will be devoured by us. They all agreed to all of it happily. I bit into the fish & tore out a piece. God it tasted amazing.
    The feral rainbow trout POV:
    I looked at the anthromorphic otter & decided that this wasn't Andrew. The anthro otter bit into my flesh & tore out a piece. The pain was unimaginable. But I loved it because I was one of those people that loved pain. The anthro otter began to devour me as the animal he was. Before I died I mentally thanked Andrew for the experience of being a feral fish & being devoured by an animal. I just hoped I tasted good.
    The anthro otter POV:
    I finished eating the fish & dropped it's remains. I looked around at everyone else. I saw all the other aquatic predators; some anthromorphic like me but most feral. But all naked like me. They were eating the fish as the predators that we all were. We were all people before this. But right at this time we were just animals. I drived back down to find more prey. Soon enough we had eaten all the fish. Most of the feral & some of the anthromorphics removed their costumes becoming human again. I was still an anthromorphic male otter & I was on my back floating on top of the water. An anthromorphic female dolphin (of some kind) came up to me & said "Great party Andrew." I smiled (the best an otter can) stating "Glad you liked it." The anthro female dolphin patted her belly saying "And I'm glad you allowed Maxwell to be at this party to be one of the fish; be devoured by me " You answered "You're welcome." She then said again patting her belly "He tasted great by the way." This anthromorphic female dolphin named Sarah & her brother Maxwell had nearly begged to be at your party. Sarah revealed she already had an anthro female dolphin Animal Costume. And Maxwell told you he wanted nothing more to be a feral fish & for his sister to hunt & devour him while she was a dolphin. So you with them arranged for that to happen. Maxwell would be a feral goldfish & Sarah would be the only one allowed to eat him. You made it very clear to everything that "The Goldfish is off limits to everyone but Sarah. Sarah & the Goldfish made a special arrangement with me. Sarah is to devour the Goldfish herself. I. E. The Goldfish is the female anthro dolphin's prey; period." And everyone agreed to that leaving the Goldfish just for her; no questions. Sarah asked curious "So what's next year's Predator & Prey Party Theme?" You answered "I haven't decided yet my dear dolphin." She then replied "I want to formally request if my sister can be my prey next time." You looked at her & asked "Again you want to make a request to devour a family member as a predator while they are prey?" Sarah replied "Next year my sister will be old enough to attend one of your parties. And she wants nothing more than to be a feral prey I hunt & devour." You sighed "Sure...Deal." [I guess some people are predators & some people are prey that want to be devoured by predators. Who am I to judge? I love being a predator. I am one each year. I hunt & devour the prey each year as the predator I am that year. Most of the time anthromorphic but sometimes feral. And I do these yearly parties so we can hunt & devour each other as animals.] You thought.
    The End.
