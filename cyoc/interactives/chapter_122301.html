    When he openned them again seconds later he almost wished he hadn't
    because the shooting star had suddenly lurched off its path and was heading towards the orphanage now.

    As Matt watched in horror the sizzling shooting star thundered low just above his head then
    headed for the small window of the small room he shared
    with Luke and Brian.

    "smashhh" came the sound of the shattering glass as the shooting star
    shot into their room

    Matt put his fingers to his ears,expecting to hear a loud explosion or
    perhaps flames bursting out into the night again.

    But there was no
    "BBBOOMMM" or superheated fireball.

    Just the chirping of crickets

    Matt ran back to the orphanage and was surprised to find
    Mrs Millers frozen in midwalk in the hallway,as were some other orphans who

    sneak outside

    Matt pressed on the elevator button nothing happened.

    the door.
