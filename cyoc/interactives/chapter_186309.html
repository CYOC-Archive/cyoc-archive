    Nora and Molly's excellent day

    "I want to do...everything!" Nora answered.

    "You got it. First let's go get a mani/pedi." Molly replied as they headed off to the nail salon.

    Molly found that while she liked getting her toes done, she wasn't so much for her fingernails. Nora on the other hand wanted to do both, so Molly waited patiently while her friend finished. Nora had decided go with a bright pink color for her fingernails. Nora and Molly then headed over for their makeovers. Molly got her black hair trimmed to a nice bobcut, while Nora asked for something more retro in the style of a feathered hairstyle. All during this time, Molly couldn't help thinking that Nora was getting more cute and whenever Nora looked over at Molly, she would turn blushing.

    Nora and Molly then stopped over in the women's restroom for Nora to change her clothes.

    "Why did you want to change again?" Molly asked waiting near the faucets.

    "I just...I wanted to dress up to go see a movie together. You know, just you, me...together." Nora replied.

    "Okay, yeah. Fair. So are you almost done?" Molly asked.

    "Almost, but turn around first, I want you to be a little surprised." Nora asked.

    "Fine, I'm turning around." Molly said as she turned around and waited for the bathroom door stall to open.

    Molly turned around to see just what Nora had chosen and she was awestruck at the girl in front of her. Nora had picked out a blue bow blouse with a white skirt.

    "So...how do I look?" Nora asked.

    "You look....radiant." Molly replied.

    Nora blushed and gave a little smile.

    "So...you want to walk me over to the movie theater?" Nora asked holding out her arms.

    Molly wrapped her arm around Nora's as her hormones shot up like butterflies. Both still didn't realize that the regression was still affecting them. Molly liked dressing up, but now she didn't like dressing up so girly. Molly bought them tickets to a dramatic movie that was playing. As they sat and watched the film, Nora was slowly but surely forgetting at all that she had been older while Molly still retained some memory.

    "Molly? Molly? I need to tell you something." Nora whispered to her.

    "What is..." Molly began to ask before Nora gently took Molly's face and began kissing her on the lips. Molly kissed her back and couldn't remember the last time she had felt more in love than now. After their date was over, they exited the theater.

    "One last thing to do." Molly winked.

    "Oh, right." Nora winked back as they went to a local convenience store near Nora's house and slipped out with some alcohol.

    At Nora's house, the two partook in drinking their alcohol down and making out as well.
