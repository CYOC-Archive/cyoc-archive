    Realisations

    After a few minutes you’ve realised that running on your legs felt natural enough and you were happy to finally achieve something positive since your transformation. It was somehow surprising that even though you were a carnivorous dinosaur you didn’t only keep your mind, but also you didn’t acquire any animalistic instincts along with your new form. You had to learn how to walk again as if you were a baby learning how to walk because your legs bent differently and they were much more powerful… and dangerous. You were so absorbed in your thoughts that you almost missed the sight of a big round and yet square-shaped structure that was easily distinguishable from the wall you ran next to. It was made out of similar material as the walls of your enclosure – strong glass-like compound powered by robust pumps and stilts which were visible through the elevator’s transparent walls. The platform inside was repeatedly going up and down. You jogged in it’s direction to see if there’s anyone to eventually try and communicate with. You thought about the options you had when approaching someone in this form, hopeful they won’t shot you, at least not fatally. Approaching the building you spotted scientists transporting mysterious crates from outside to your enclosure. Just then you were noticed. One of the scientists immediately pulled out his telecommunicator and seemed pretty anxious that you showed up. Right after that they all rushed up the elevator and disappeared from your sight. As your sight wandered off to the top of the walls of your enclosure you noticed a few recognizable silhouettes. Your parents! They seemed horridly troubled while discussing something with the bossy looking scientist, next to them strolled your little sister. Even thought she was younger than you by 2 years you still loved her as much as your parents and you both had relatively good relations, except occasional brother and sister banter. Her sight cheered you up a little bit. But what’s that?! She seemed really upset when the scientist gestured at you standing bellow them. If you felt awful about your current situation, you can’t imagine what your close family was enduring right now. Your old life was suddenly turned around and you have no way of living as you did before while in this form. Your mother tried to comfort your sister with a hug, but she brushed her off and rushed off in the direction of the elevator. You could only watch as she carelessly turned the steering switch inside the elevator and started heading your way, while your parents screamed for her to come back. Your father dashed to the elevator gate and tried to budge them open as your sister began descending bellow. Your mother just weeped stunned at the loss not only of you at first, but now your sister, and she was unable to do anything. The bossy scientist long gone, already ordered rest of his squad to join him as fast as possible and get your sister back to safety. If only they knew you kept your humanity and only your form changed…! You needed to act fast, if your sister comes here with the reinforcements after her, they will think you wanted to attack her. You decided to try and communicate with them first. Luckily for you there was a dry bush next to you, so you opened your maw and took a bite of it, ripping a stick out. Good thing just in time too, the elevator landed on the ground few feet away from you and…
