    Spark and Connor were arguing with each other as they were walking toward the Moreland house one day after school. The haunted house attraction was almost ready, but Connor had been trying to get Spark to make peace with Amara to little result.

    "Spark, come on, it's been weeks now. Couldn't you just try and be friends with one another?" Connor asked as Spark held her head up high.

    "Friends? I don't know if you remembered what I told you, but she gave me nightmares....about you..." Spark said trying to hold back her tears.

    "I know, Spark I know. But we've already talked about this, and besides she seems to have made an impression on Miranda." Connor reminded her.

    "Yeah, well maybe she's just tricking her, so she can make a fool out of her later. I wouldn't put it past her, people can be devious. My sister, Star, once made me think another fairy was crushing on me. But she just made me look like a fool when I went to his house." Spark explained to Connor as they neared the front door.

    "As I remember it Spark, you fooled Star first into thinking her first crush had come back to her." Spark's mother, Nova said as she and her husband Arion came out from the Moreland household with Connor's parents following closely behind them as Nina clutched her very pregnant stomach.

    "Mom? Dad? What are you two doing here?" Spark asked as she gave her parents a hug.

    "Well, Spa'kalth..." Arion began.

    "Ah, it's just Spark now, remember dear?" Nova reminded her husband.

    "Right. Anyway, Spark, in case you forgot, it's our anniversary and your mother and I decided to spend our anniversary out in the human realm. We decided to stop by the Moreland home first to catch up with everyone." Arion explained.

    "Your anniversary? But it's not....oh shiiii....." Spark began to say as Nova glared at her daughter.

    "I...I....I...I can't believe I forgot, I've been so busy lately. The situation with Rei's aunt, the Halloween planning, the mara...." Spark said.

    "What?! You're dealing with a mara?" Jacob asked concern.

    "It's okay dad, we've got Amara under control." Connor said.

    "You're controlling a mara?"

    "No, Amara is under control." Connor corrected his father.

    "Wait...isn't that what I said?" Jacob asked confused.

    "No, you asked...."

    "Ugh, we found a literal mara called 'Amara' we know it's not the most subtle name, but it's what that little bitch calls herself." Spark explained.

    "Spa'kalth!" Nova shouted.

    "Mom, she made me and my friend Nat have...bad nightmares and I'm still reeling from it." Spark said rubbing her arm.

    "Well anyway, she's been better at the whole dream thing. Right? You haven't been getting any nightmares right?" Connor asked.

    "No, and the dream she gave me started off depressing but then it got....exciting with you and me." Spark said blushing.

    "Oh, Spark, was it a good dream? Did you and Connor.....fuck...!" Nina asked as she groaned her last word.

    "Mom! That's sort of...private business." Connor said blushing.

    "No! That's not it..." Nina said groaning holding her legs as Nova looked down to see water coming out.

    "Oh my! Her water, her water broke, the babies are coming! Nina, is that it?" Nova asked worriedly as Nina nodded in reply.

    "Crap, they can't come now, Connor and I were working on the truck, it's not ready to drive yet." Jacob said noting their loss of vehicle.

    "OH MY GOD, Jacob they're coming, for god's sake they're coming!!" Nina exclaimed.

    "Well, they're going to be Morelands, so they'll be born on the Moreland home." Nova said as Nina groaned.

    "Alright, alright, Nina, I'll help you out here. Spark, you come with me to help deliver these kids. Connor, Jacob, Arion, get some towels, a bowl, water, gloves..." Nova began to list as she grabbed Nina and headed inside alongside Spark and Nina began to howl.

    "Arion, just grab anything essential!" Nova said as she and Spark helped Nina into the living room where she was still screaming in pain.

    "But...but....mom, I don't think I can do this!" Spark said worriedly.

    "You're going to have to learn Spark. You know I did spend a little time in the human realm before helping deliver babies, now you get to help me." Nova said as Arion, Jacob, and Connor sprinted around the house grabbing whatever they could that would help.

    "Ohhhh.....JACOB MORELAND, YOU SON OF A BITCH, WHY AM I HAVING TWINS?!!!" Nova shouted in pain.

    Spark was still worried about how this situation would go, just half an hour ago, she and her boyfriend were having an argument. Now she was about to help deliver babies.
