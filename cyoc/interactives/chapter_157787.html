    A big second try

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 3 years ago S Reality alteration

    After quickly changing back due to panic, Ken actually though once again about why happened, and understood that he actually wanted to try that again, only this time actually prepared for what is going to happen.
    He once again wrote the height of 10 feet in the book, and he once again felt himself quickly rise up, this time already ducking in preparation.
    Before anything, he wrote in the book, being surprised at the larger hand that made the task harder “perfectly capable at writing even with his height”.
    He then scanned around his page and saw “Living in a fairly normal house”. After thinking about it, also fearing that the floor will collapse, he changed it (with far less struggles than the previous time) to “Living in a house with high ceilings, tall doors and plenty of space, as well as the furniture in his room being made for his height”. Ken felt his bed rising higher and looking up, he saw the ceiling doing the same for a few seconds.
    Remembering about the health problems the tallest people have, Ken then added “Doesn’t have any health problems even with his height”.
    Not seeing anything else important to change in order to not cause immediate chaos, he got up.
    The furniture around his room seemed to be about the same when compared to his height, though his bed appeared proportionally longer. Looking up at the ceiling, it appeared to be 12 feet tall, giving some distance away, though still looking much closer than he was used to. Lifting up his arm, he quickly reached it.
    He then decided to got to the bathroom to look at himself in the mirror.
    His door was tall at 8 feet, but still had to bend down significantly to get under it.
    Looking around his house, he saw how it was more spacious, assuring him that he will not feel crammed.
    As he reached the bathroom, one again bending down to get through, he saw how comically small everything looked, including the mirror. He only saw a part of his legs from his high vantage view, and they appeared largely the same, though much longer and proportionally skinnier (compared to their length, they were still wider than they originally originally) and two huge feet at the bottom.
    Bending down to his knees, Ken saw that the rest of his body was similarly elongated, though the same otherwise.
    It was strange, but very interesting.
