    Damon decided to accept the mermaid's offer as he takes the potion. The mermaid smile.

    "I'm glad you accept my offer. Drink the whole thing to get a greater result." Damon chugs the whole potion down.

    He then place the potion down and could feel a weird sensation go through his body.

    "I feel funny." Said Damon. "It means the potion is working then. You'll make a handsome merman."

    Damon felt two slits on his sides. He now had gills and can breath underwater. He then felt a weird sensation in his legs.

    He then fell and sees his two legs fuse into one limb and were stuck forever. He sees something else on his legs.

    Blue scales then appear on his legs as his toes changes into his new tail fins. His upper body then got stronger.

    Damon was now a merman as he immediately get's into the water. The mermaid swims up to him.

    "How do you feel?" She ask. "Weird and great at the same time." The mermaid then giggle.

    She place her hand on Damon's strong chest. "I'm Apolla. What's your name?" She ask.

    "Damon." He replied. "Damon I know you'll be a great mate for me." Apolla then kisses Damon.

    It was the best kiss Damon ever had as he return the favor. The two of them then dives into the ocean.
