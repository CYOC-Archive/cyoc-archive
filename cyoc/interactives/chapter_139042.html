    ...take another picture and hope for the best. He needed to change his dog back and the camera seemed to be random. He'd just have to keep trying until he got his dog back. He flipped the camera around and his dog bowser turned human began to whimper. Jake felt bad and asked what was the matter.

    "It's just, I'm scared Master. Everything feels weird and that thing might make me feel weird again." She said with a pout. "Why don't we just keep me like this? I could be your mate."

    Jake blushed redder than he ever had. "No no no no. There are so many things wrong with that from age difference to dog to you know... look I'm gonna try and change you back so hold still." He said.

    As he was aiming the camera, she jumped off the floor and spun the camera around in a second. The flash ended up striking both Jake and Bowser. Not only that, but Jake's had had slipped and turned reality change on. The world seemed to spin for a moment before Jake could focus in again.

    Jake immediately noticed he was on the floor. He tried to stand up but just fell back down. He looked at his hands, and saw hairy paws. He looked towards the mirror and saw a poodle puppy. He tried to speak but a yip only came out. He looked down and saw his member was also gone.

    Looking around, the room looked much different. Jake couldn't see the colors but if he could he'd see a pink nightmare. There were dolls and stuffed animals everywhere, with matching princess sheets to boot. Standing in the middle of the room was a 6 year old girl. She had curly blond hair and a poofy princess dress. She turned around it was a thankfully clothed and younger version of Bowser, the former dog.

    "Wow." She said. "This is my room. Jake did you hit the reality change on? Because, I remember my whole life as a human now. I remember birthdays and vacations, I even remember buying you Jane." she said, smiling at the new dog. Jake could remember his life as a dog too but he still held on to his old life with conviction that his old dog didn't seem to have, "And I also remember my annoying family. Now I know why you wanted to change them so much." She said laughing

    Jake waddled over to the camera and tried to flip it towards him but Bowser stopped him. She picked up the camera and put it on a shelf. She then carried Jake to a crate and looked him inside.

    "Sorry there "master" but I think I want to be human for a while. I'll turn you back, eventually, but right now I want to make the perfect family. Toodles! Barbie has got some work to do." She said evil like.

    Barbie walked off and Jane the poodle was panicking. Her former dog was now a human and might not have had any intention of turning her back into Jake. Jane needed to get that camera, and soon. Meanwhile, Barbie was thinking about who she wanted to change first.
