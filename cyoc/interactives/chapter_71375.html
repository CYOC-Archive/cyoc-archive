    immense irritation, she found herself being shaken awake.

    "Oh...uh...c'mon Jackie...what is it?"

    "Now Jade," said a deeper voice, "don't talk to yourself like
    that."

    twenty-six year old young woman. They had already met twice that
    year and little Jade loved the two meetings...even though the last
    one had seen her older self getting fired...and later rehired.

    kissed her on the forehead.

    Jade felt her face go white. Her stomach turned. "Is Uncle..."

    The Older Jade smiled, "Uncle and Jackie are going to be with us

    Jade nodded, she's spent a few hours as a forest monkey in

    "Yes." In that case, she had been scratched by a cursed statue
