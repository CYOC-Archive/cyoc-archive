    It was a cold summer evening in the basement as Roger and Collin sat in Roger's little mancave/room with a pool table between them. One of them had found the gun loaded with some sort of gender bender setting, and now that everything was set-
    "Hey dudes, think I could join in?" Roger's older sister asked. They looked as Vanessa walked down the staircase having been asked if she could lend one of them clothes when they were done. The nearly 16 year old boys had wanted a girlfriend, and after failing and finding this... They decided to make a gamble that whoever won this game of Russian roulette would get the loser as a girlfriend. "And Like I said anyways you set the conditions wrong. Traditionally the person whose shot is the winner... I think." Vanessa said

    "You can use it later with your friends or something. This is kinda important to us shoo." Roger tried saying
    "Well then whoever's a girl can just stay in boy's clothes or nude then." Vanessa spoke
    "Listen Vanny..." Collin tried saying. He used to have a crush on Vanessa and it was obvious he wasn't 100% over her and the idea of being in her clothes was slightly appealing "It's just we want this first one to be done right for what we want to do with it. We'll lend you the gun later. I guess you can watch?"
    Roger slightly groaned as he took out a coin and flipped it "Heads or tails?"
    "Heads" Collin said as Roger held his hand out and it was Heads. Collin took the gun and confirmed there was one magic bullet in the chamber and then spun the chambers. He put the gun to his head, pulled the trigger and- *click*
    "Aw... you know I'm not sure who I'd want to win more. May be nice to have a sister... but which one of yall would I prefer wearing my stuff?" Vanessa muttered
    "We're Trying to be serious here!" Roger said as he grabbed the gun. He took in a deep breath as he pulled the trigger *click* and passed the gun over
    Vanessa asked "Is being a girl that big of a deal? I do it all the time."
    Collin looked into the barrel of the gun trying to see if it was his time and saw that no light was in the barrel as he spoke "It's less about being a girl, and just changing so drastically.... and wardrobe budgets." He then pulled the trigger *click*
    As the gun passed to Roger Vanessa said "I mean it's not like this is THAT permanent. those are gender swap bullets so all you'd need to do is shoot yourself, even if you have to do it in this fashion statistically you can all change back to normal."
    Roger picked up the gun and the 1/3 chance of losing was starting to get to his nerves as he said "No! We're having a gentleman's agreement. Whoever gets shot becomes the other's girlfriend." Then he pulled the trigger *click* "And could you just stay silent for the last round Sis? This will determine our fate" Vanessa eyerolled her little brother's choice of words as the gun was passed back to Collin. 2 chambers left. 50/50 odds that either one would be a girl and fate would solidify with one more press of the gun. The trigger was pulled...
