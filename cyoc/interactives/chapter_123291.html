    In the backyard, Irene was pacing around the backyard in her evolving form looking impatient. With a body of an over six foot tall alien like T-Rex with four arms and six sharp claws. The head is the only thing left unchanged but it was showing off a slowly growing snout that looked to be like that of a T-Rex. But she was still showing some human traits and some semi intelligence along the way. But it would be a matter of time before the changes would be. She was still roaming the backyard, getting antsy on being confined in such a small space.

    “Must not stay…” she said with her diminishing vocabulary, “Need to leave. Need to survive.”

    Just then, her evolving stomach growled, as she didn’t make dinner since the start of the evolution. Rubbing one of her clawed hands and was a little hungry. “Stomach empty… Must feed…” As she was looking around for some food, she heard a mysterious chirp from above and saw a blue jay chirping on a nearby tree. She starred at it for a few seconds and muttered, “Pretty bird… Delicious bird… Must feast on bird…”

    By the time she said that, her next stage of evolution just kicked in. Her nose enlarged and grew further, leading to it slowly becoming a snout. That said snout let the mouth join in with gotten wider and her eyes drew further apart. Her nostrils inflared as she breathed deeper breathes then before and her sense of smell has increased dramatically. Her neck expanded and became the same size as her streamlined body, making it easier for her to digest even the biggest of foods. Her ears then changed as the lobes slowly faded away, leaving nothing but little holes for ears. That soon changed as her ear holes enlarged and gave her enhanced sound to hear just about everything around her. Most of her hair on her hair started to fall off in clumps until all that’s left is a small brown patch. That said patch started to extend down her neck and all the way down to the tip of her wavy tail, leaving what looks like a line of fur like hair on the top of her alien like body. Then her eyes were next to change as they grew out of her face and are as wide as circles. Her pupils soon spread out, covering her irises and the white surface, leaving her eyes completely black. That enhanced her sights and she could see incredibly better then before.

    When her mouth was quivering as she was starring at the bird, something very strange has happened. Her lips started to thin away, leaving nothing but a thin wide line that resembles her mouth. But suddenly, two more lines came from the tip of the snout and spread down the top and bottom of the snout. By the time they reached the ends near the eyes, the snout opened up in for separate directions and her original teeth shrunk but grew very sharp while new extra set of teeth appeared at the new ends of the folds. Her tongue then shifted and launched out of her new mouth, stretching and reaching the bird. It soon grabs hold of the bird with a quick whiplash and stuck to it good, preventing the bird to escape. The tongue soon retracted with the bird and withdrew into Irene’s new mouth, closing with a quick snap and swallowed the bird down her wide neck.

    By the time she ate the bird, she pranced around the backyard and opened her mouth again like that of a flytrap plant. But she didn’t say a word, as she could no longer form any words. Instead, she gave out an alien like shriek with a combination of a cackle. Even her mind grew small and simplified, erasing all of her human thoughts, memories, and emotions. All she could think about is three things; to explore, to hunt, and to survive. Her enhanced ears soon picked up another sound and turned to see a squirrel climbing up another tree. Seeing this, she opened her mouth and let her stretching tongue grab it in quick succession, swallowing it whole.

    In the house, Jeff saw the change of the face and was amazed that it evolved into something like that. Ever since evolving his mother slowly, he saw so many results since the change. Now all he saw in the backyard is a newly created creature with no trace of his mother at all. That both scared and fascinates him as he said, “Wow! My mom is now that?! That’s so cool! I just hope that the Chronivac finished with her evolution. Because the way it looks, I don’t think there’s anything else left to change.” So he kept watching, wondering if the changes have finished or if it’s going to continue.
