    Kazumi pulled the opening on the back between the spines wide open. Pushing her legs inside, she soon nestled them tightly into their individual and comfortable slots.

    How am I supposed to get it on, she thought. Lay down into it?

She calmed down when she remembered that the effects guys had told her that the suit had programming to help her put it on, as she wouldn't be able to do it herself.

    Kazumi realized the best way to help the suit fit itself to her was to indeed lie down, as Shin Gojira's first form didn't stand upright. She knelt down and lay flat on her belly and bust against the floor of the changing room. She then relaxed and let the suit do its thing.

    The material of the suit couldn't grab her, but it could move itself in a wavelike motion, gently pushing and sliding her limbs into the correct spots. Her arms were shifted behind her back, where they would be concealed by the suit's spines. The head and jaws of the suit fixed themselves in front of her head. And as the whole suit squeezed and settled against her body, Kazumi realized the suit's opening was sealing shut.

    This is it. For the next month, I will be in this suit, thought Kazumi with a mixture of fear and excitement. For the next month, I am no longer Kazumi Shimata.

    I am Gojira.

    Once the suit was sealed, Kazami felt a brief discomfort in her head as the suit synced up with her brain. Her vision, which was already dark since her head had been encased by the suit, now went completely black.

    After a few moments, her vision came back to her.
