    As Tim watched helplessly, the chamber began to fill with a white-colored liquid. "TIM!" Anna shouted, panicked, "DO SOMETHING!" Tim began to frantically hit buttons on the console, but the liquid continued to rise. "No no no no no!" Anna shouted, still struggling to escape the claw's steel grip. "Uh, uh, that one! Tim shouted as he slammed his fist down on a big red button.

    Abruptly, the liquid stopped filling the room and everything seemed still. Tim and Anna waited, glancing around nervously. Nothing in the room moved. "Whew..." Tim exhaled, wiping the sweat from his brow, "That could've been dangerous..." "Now that that's over with," Anna began, "Could you get me outta here-!"

    Suddenly, the claw came back sprung back to life. Before anyone had a chance to react, the claw dunked Anna into the liquid and began to spin extremely fast. "Whoa!" Tim yelped, turning back to the console and madly pressing buttons. "BLBBL FLP BLBBPLP!" Anna gurgled as she was thrashed around, struggling as hard as she could. "Stop you stupid machine!" Tim shouted as he slammed his fist down on the machine's console.

    With a loud, "GRRRRRNGH!", the claw ground to a halt and the liquid drained out of the room. "Anna, are you okay?!" Tim called, rushing over to the chamber's glass plane. At this point, the claw fell limp, allowing Anna to drop to the floor with a wet, "PLOP!" "Can we leave now...?" Anna groaned, staying where she was. "Let's get you outta there first," Tim said, glancing around for a weakness in the glass panel.

    "Tim... I feel weird..." Anna groaned. Tim looked over at Anna and frowned. She seemed paler than usual. Actually, her skin was practically white! "Uh, Anna?" Tim began. "You look really pale." Anna staggered to her feet and claimed, "I'll be okay. Let's just-"

    "PLOP!"

    Tim glanced down and gasped. There on the ground of the chamber lay a milky white, partially melted hand. Tim looked back up at Anna. Now her skin was pure white. "Tim?" Anna purred lazily, "I think that machine did something to me..." At this point, Anna seemed to be getting shorter and shorter, almost as if she was... melting. "Ooooo..." Anna slurred, her eyelids dropping a bit, "That feels sorta nice..." "Anna, focus!" Tim shouted nervously. " I-I'll get you out of the in just a moment, so hold on!"

    Anna wasn't even paying attention to Tim at this point. She just felt so... GOOD! It felt like someone was giving a full body massage and making love to her at the same time! "Ahehehe..." Anna giggled lazily as her form collapsed in on itself. Suddenly, a hole opened up beneath Anna's semi-liquid form. Immediately, what was left of Anna's body drained down into the hole. "Anna!" Tim shouted. All that was left in the chamber was Anna's clothes.

    Tim then heard a loud whirring noise and turned around. A small faucet had formed directly behind Tim, complete with a stack of plastic bowls and spoons. Tim cautiously grabbed a bowl and twisted on the faucet. What seemed to be ice cream poured out into the bowl. "Ice cream?" Tim murmured, grabbing as spoon and dipping it into the bowl. Suddenly, the ice cream began to jiggle. "Huh?" Tim muttered peering into the bowl. In front of Tim's eyes, a familiar face formed in the ice cream. "Don't stop..." the voice of Anna gurgled. "Anna?!" Tim shouted. The machine had turned Anna into a bowl of ice cream!

    "Tim... please..." Anna asked. Tim bit his lip nervously. It was time to make a decision.
