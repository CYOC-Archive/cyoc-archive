    "I-it's that my horn?! Am I using magic or something?!" Jen exclaimed.

    Suddenly, her vision became green and staticy as she can suddenly see the end of the hallway. And what she saw terified her.

    "Turn me back you monsters!" He shouted.

    He pulled back the chain that missed Ashely and then through it back with force. This time, the chain wasn't covered in flames.

    Jen's horn was busy holding back the other chain, so all she could do was stand there.

    But before the second chain could strike, the voice came back.

    ATTACK INCOMMING. INITIATING DEFENSE MANUVURES.

    But the man kept on charging.

    Jen tried using her magic to wrap up the man with his own chain, but she was having a hard time figuring out how to do that with how fast he is going.

    Right when the man was about to tackle Jen, a mirror like barrier apeared in front of her hands and stoped the scary man in his tracks. Did her gloves did this?!

    As the man punched away at the barrier, Jen noticed that he was holding back a little.

    "Wait, can we talk this out? Are you a transformed student? We can help you!" Jen said.

    Suprisingly, the man stoped. "Wait, you mean you are still yoursleves? You are not being controled by a monster lord or something?"

    The magic holding the first chain disipated as Jen lowered her arms in releif. "What did you get that idea? There is a virus turning everyone into their costumes. What's your name?"
