    The Zoo

    A plan is forming

    A lively discussion can be heard in one of the labs in the Science building. "This time he's gone too far, he's become a liability! We can't let him continue like this! First he lower the Science founding and now he steals test subjects for his own personal pleasure" says Dr. Bergström and gestures wildly. Arthur, his assistant nods in agreement. Dr. Dawson, Professor Jackson and their assistant Nathalie listens to the doctor with interest. When Dr. Bergström is done speaking there's a moment of thoughtful silence. Dr. Dawson breaks this silence "You're absolutely right, he's a danger to the research... but what can we do? We can't make him just disappear?" A devilish smile forms on Dr.Bergströms face. "Or can we?" The rest of the scientists seems to catch on. "Of course, we just make him a part of his own zoo" Jackson exclaims happily. But Dawson doesn't seem to be as positive about the idea. "But how should we do it? He will still own the zoo and be our boss"

    "I know exactly how" Says Dr.Bergström, still smiling. He explains his plan to them. "So everyone agree that this is what we should do?" Dr. Dawson and Professor Jackson nods. "Good. What about you Nathalie?" Nathalie looks up into Dr.Bergströms eyes. "I don't like this idea one bit... what if we get caught!?" Dr. Bergström grins. "Get caught? By who? No one knows about this and most of the park personnel dislike him and thinks that he doesn't do he job properly any more. So the only way we could get caught is if anyone mentions this to someone loyal to Sanchez. But that's not going to happen, since we'll make sure that anyone outside of the group who knows about this will loose their ability to speak, you know that right Nathalie?"

    "I still think it's a stupid idea, I'm not going to take part of it" She says pushing Dr.Bergström aside and starts walking out of the room. Dr. Bergström chuckles. "If you leave now, you won't be part of the group any longer, Nathalie" She answers by showing him a finger and the doctor's answer to this is pulling his trigger finger on the trigger of the dart gun he just pulled out of his lab coat. As Nathalie falls down to her knees because of the substance on the dart she suddenly realizes what the doctor really said. As she drifts of to sleep she finds herself wondering about what kind of species they're going to choose as her new shape.

    Doctor Bergström walks to Nathalie's motionless body and pokes her with his dart gun a couple of times. "What should we do with her?" Dr. Dawson asks as Dr.Bergström runs his hand through Nathalie's curly black hair. "I suggest the pig sty or maybe the snake house" Dawson and Jackson look at each other and then they look at their college once more. "Is that really necessary?" The look Dr.Bergström gave the one second later put a shiver down their spines and made them totally clear what his opinion about this is. "You want her to go around an tell everybody?" Dr. Bergström asks and the two scientist shake their heads. "Then we have to do this" He walks away and starts preparing a syringe with nanobots. As he starts injecting the girl with it Jackson starts pleading for Nathalie's sake. "But at least we can choose something that's not too disliked by people. We need to have a extra test subject to be sure that the treatment we gave our Tibetan spaniel test subject really works. We could use Nathalie" Dr.Bergström keeps staring at the two scientists without moving a muscle in his face. "She's been a very helpful assistant and we don't think we should be so hard on her. She would really hate being a pig or a snake. Let her be something more lovable, let her become a dog" Dawson adds. Jackson gives his college next to him a worried look, hoping that this comment won't make them the next targets of Dr. Bergströms dart gun.

    "She's a traitorous bitch and she needs to learn some loyalty so if any of you wants to keep her as a pet after the transformation, feel free to do so. Or just do whatever you want with her, I don't care just as long she won't utter a word about this to anyone" Relieved about the answer Jackson and Dawson let's out a sigh of relief. "Put her in a cage, gag her and tie her hands and feet. When you're done, we'll put our plan into work. You can start choose the animal she should become after we have taken care of Sanchez" Dr. Bergström continues.
