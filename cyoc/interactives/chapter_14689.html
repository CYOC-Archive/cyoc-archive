    "Oh...right," she replied, though still somehow seeming like she wasn't completely getting the idea.

    Then something happened that I didn't expect, truly a surprise.(Which by this point was rare. It's hard to surprise someone with godlike powers.) A couple more girls appeared from the back, apparently having hidden during the slaughter.

    "Veronica, Becky, Jessie...what the hell happened?" asked one of them, a redhead nearly as tall as Veronica, nearly 6 feet.

    "Actually, my dears, I can explain everything to you," I said, smiling.
