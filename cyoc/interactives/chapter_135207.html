    Some random time in the present, a creature from another reality seemingly found their way into our own, and this creature was but an anthropomorphic dragon. Instead of how one might expect things to turn out in this kind of situation, the dragon found their way into fame and became a popular celebrity for one who wasn't human.

    The dragon was traveling the world to see every kind of culture, as he was quite interested to learn about all the different life styles humans lived. He was currently traveling through the town of a young man named Damien. He won a mystery raffle for a ticket to go to a massive party the dragon was hosting in Damien's town, and although he wasn't that interested, he thought it would be a great experience to meet new people.

    On the day of the party, Damien woke up early and put his most fanciest outfit on, which was a new white tuxedo he bought for this kind of occasion. He walked to a mirror and looked at himself for a moment, slicking back his hair. "You're looking unusually snazzy today," He chuckled to himself. Damien finished tidying up and got into his car, leaving for the gathering. He was told it would be in a mansion the dragon rented just for the event, so he drove to the middle of town where the mansion was located. "Sure are a lot of cars here..." he mumbled to himself as he struggled to find a place to park.

    Once he did he left to go inside. The mansion's interior was heavily decorated with the most fancy of furniture, decked out with fancy sofas, tables, chairs, fireplaces, etc. Damien also noticed the absolutely massive amount of people who happened to be there. Many were the rich and wealthy people who came from out-of-town. Damien sighed. "On second thought, this wasn't the best of parties to come to... I just have to keep quiet and make sure I don't start any conversations with anyone..." As Damien continued to look around the mansion, he came across a familiar face. It was his best friend Kyle. Kyle stumbled over to Damien, drunk as can be. "Heeeyyy maaannn...I didn't know you were into reptiles..." Kyle blurted out in his drunk state. Damien's expression changed to that of confusion. "What?" "Oh come on maaan!! You totally came here to make out with that sexy dragon man yeeaah???" "No. No I didn't." "...Well, whatever man...See ya later...!!" And with that Kyle drunkily flopped back into the crowd of people.

    Damien sighed once again, and wandered up to a raffle box. He looked at it, and it was a mystery raffle just like the one he won the ticket from. "Eh, what the hell? Can't do any harm to try another one." Damien took part in the raffle, and then left for the main foyer, since the main event was starting soon enough.

    The foyer was absolutely massive for being a foyer, and it managed to fit everyone. They all soon went silent when the anthropomorphic dragon himself walked onto the stage on one side of the room. The dragon wore a white suit just like Damien, and he had shimmering white scales with a golden colored mane and horns. He smiled and took the mic on the stage and began to talk, talk, and talk some more. Damien found a lot of it boring, but his attention was grabbed when the dragon announced he was going to be announcing the raffle prize and winner. "So, the raffle prize for today is..." A drumroll from the band on stage followed his words. "...To get to spend an entire day with me!" Suddenly, the crowd all 'Ooh-ed,' and 'Ah-ed.' "And now for the winner..." The drumroll followed again.

    "...Damien Ferris!"

    Damien's head perked right up. "I won again?" He thought. The dragon smiled and pointed to him in the crowd, motioning a finger for him to come onto the stage. Everyone in the audience parted a path for him. Kyle suddenly shouted out, still drunk, "MAKE OUT WITH THAT DRAGON STU--" He was immediately slapped by one of the other guests, and the dragon silently chuckled and rolled his eyes.

    What should Damien do next?
