    Inside the large mansion at 1313 Forandring Street Dr Veranderen sat in his large lab typing away at his laptop computer:

    The first variance of Formula 525 has been prepared and had been added to the list of formulas ready to be tested.



    Eric, a almost 21-year-old college student, was in need of some extra cash for his first trip Vegas and after seeing the ad he found himself outside the mansion’s gate pushing the buzzer.

    Seconds later a voice came over the intercom “Yes, how can I help you?”

    Eric looked up into the camera and spoke “Hopefully this is the right place, I’m replying to the ad in the paper.”

    “Ah yes, I will open the gate, please come into the door to the right of the main entrance. You will find a clipboard with some paperwork to fill out and sign, I will be with you shortly.” The doctor replied.

    Eric pulled on the gate as it freely opened for him. Entering he closed the gate behind him hearing a large snap as the magnetic lock reinitiated.

    Entering the vacant waiting room Eric picked up a clipboard and a pin and sat down filling in the needed info; Name, Age, Weight, Height, and so on…

    The next page outlined the compensatory portion of the test:

    Compensation varies based on study(ies) completed and amount of time the study requires. Payment is made in cash at completion of the study. Minimum compensatory payment is $5000.

    The next 15 pages of small font text had this header:

    The following contract is to protect the organizations research. This must be signed in order to continue further. Main Clause is a non-disclosure agreement; if any mention of these tests are made outside of this research facility a suit in the amount of $10 million will be filed against you for damages.

    Not really wanting to read the legal mumbo jumbo Eric flipped to the last page and signed his name. If only he knew he was giving the Doctor complete control of his body and mind, he might have known what was soon to come.

    Just as he finished signing the middle age Dutch doctor walked in. Taking the clipboard from Eric he introduced himself, “Hi, I am Dr. Veranderen, if you follow me I will take you to one of the testing rooms for you to wait in while I double check your data.”

    Eric followed the doctor down the hall and into a door labeled ‘Testing Room 1’.

    “I will be back in just a few minutes, make yourself at home.”

    Eric was in awe looking at the large room, it was just like a dream studio apartment aside from the medical table in one corner and the two-way mirrors covering 2 of the 4 walls. It had a kitchen, a king sized bed, a TV with gaming system, a couch and a bathroom; much better than his own apartment on campus.
