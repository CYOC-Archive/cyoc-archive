    The Madame Illusia

    A Braying Donkey

    Previous chapter

    added by Elflover 5 days ago A Magic Equine

    Madame Illusia cast her spell at Monica. She wasted no time. That shameless hussy deserved her coming punishment and after a big puff of purple smoke, a donkey stood where Monica had stood. It was a female donkey but the witch was not going to let that stop her from humiliating her foe.
    "You acted like an ass, you can be one!" she exclaimed.
    The crowd laughed and cheered. The jenny-ass twitched her ears and looked around. She still had her human mind and seeing and hearing the audience jeer at her, she screamed her anger and fury. But as a donkey, only brays came out of her mouth. Her mouth with big ugly teeth. Looking at her new hooves and body, Monica wanted to cry but alas for her, donkey cannot cry. She was reduced to braying some more, while Madame Illusia revelled in the humiliation of her enemy. Furious, the jenny-ass turned toward the witch and sensing danger, she flicked her wand. By magic, Monica found herself tied to a pole with a bridle and rope. She shook her head furiously and pulled and tugged madly. She kicked wildly too, mad at the idea of being tied while her tormentor laughed as much as everyone in the public. The jerks! She brayed and kicked and Madame Illusia, seeing her audience starting to get bored, acted fast.
    "Oh, it seems Monica doesn't like her new body! Now that she has tired a bit that awful temper, well, maybe a new body would fit her better. Any suggestions? " she asked her audience.
    A lot shot from the public, and Monica froze, very frightened at what was going to befall her next. That horrible witch could not have her best intentions in mind, that was for sure...
