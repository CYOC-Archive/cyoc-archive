As you walk up to it, you see a sign; it reads, "Welcome to the Facepaint O'Matic, the first and only computerized face and body painting machine. This machine can paint any design you can imagine; it can do facepaint or a whole body painting! Choose from animals, movie and cartoon characters, superheros, famous figures, or anything else you can dream up. If you can think it, Facepaint O'Matic can paint it on you! For our unveiling, we are offering these services COMPLETELY FREE! Don't miss this once in a lifetime chance; step into the machine and become something else today!

By passing this point, you agree to indemnify and hold harmless ACME Corp LLC for any and all accidental bodily harm and any bodily changes that may occur while using this machine.
