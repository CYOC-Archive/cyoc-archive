    "So Peter, I am curious about something though."

    "What are you curious about?"

    "Well, I was wondering... what would happen if you decided to bring all the men together that ever drank this by using the nanites to bring them back?"

    "To be honest, I am not sure. I suppose that everyone would come back and meet in an area that I'd designate in the recall programming. ...Why do you ask?"

    "Well, you could always bring the people here and begin your new pack... couldn't you?"

    "Yeah, I suppose I could, but that would be unethical. I don't think I could take them away from their lives like that, not to mention force them to be the full werewolves they were all meant to be. Hell... I'd like some of them to come back because they were super hot before they got the nanites, but after... whooo man!"

    You smile your toothy smile seeing Peter's eyes light up like that and you knew that was what Peter really wanted.

    "Peter, couldn't you just write the routine to state that once they were a fully transformed werewolf that they would never know that they were never like that, not to mention that they would not think about being different because they were all naturally that way... just like you had the nanites make everyone think that they were always the way they were even when the comparatively small changes were happening?"

    "Wow, you know... you really are smart because I never thought to do that. The programming is there, I would just have to flip the switch on, more or less."

    "Well... then are you going to?"

    Peter grinned, then nodded and pulled out the receiver that was in the shredded pants pocket just below his wolven hind paws. With an even more mischevious grin, he turned on the PDA with the information on it. Peter then changed a couple of small details to make it as you brought Peter to think about. Once he was done, you watched as he tapped send.

    Peter wrapped his gigantically muscular wolf arm around your shoulders and gave you a decent strengthed squeeze, thanking you for your idea. Peter, wanting you to know how really happy he was, lapped at the side of your muzzle a couple of times in quick succession and had you been in your human form, Peter would have seen you blush.

    "Alright, it's done, but there is a few modifications to everything. The first is that the override will now call all of the men here to the convention hall. The override then will only transform all of the men, but only once all of the nanites are in close proximity. The programming knows how many men out there have drank the brew since I opened this facility and the nanites will communicate with everyone in the room to be sure that no one is outside of this place. Once everyone is all here, they will slowly go through the transformation. We will greet them in human form and we will change with them to make them all know that this place is their home and we will be their Alphas."

    "Sounds good, but how soon should we be expecting them?"

    "I am not real sure, it depends if they are all still on the island or not. I'd say though that everyone will probably have to make their way to the island because everyone, except you of course, drank their share of the full-moonshine some time ago and everyone does not stay here more than a day or two after drinking my brew."

    "Oh, why is that Peter?"

    "That's simple to be honest. Once the nanites are in their system, they prevent their host from gaining any of the other transformative abilities that are on this island. The only breeds here are werewolves like us, werebears and permanent donkeys but men only turn into donkeys here if they make an ass of themselves."

    "Well no wonder I saw a huge population of donkeys around here, I just thought the island liked the deal from Pinnochio or something, but it makes sense because most guys can't stop themselves from being an ass. So... shall we go get ready for our guests?"

    "I suppose, but all we need to wear is a pair of jean shorts or any other kind that is normally worn beyond under-garments. We could just stay in our skivvies too though, but I figured the best thing we can do is just make all those hotties like you will be comfortable, especially since they will learn they were 'natural werewolves' cause I am sure it will be a shock even though they will be accepting of the fact right away."

    You just nod while you both head to the conference room to change back into human form and don a couple pairs of denim shorts that were tailored to expand and contract with your changes, just to make owning clothes easier and less expensive overall.
