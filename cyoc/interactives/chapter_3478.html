    With each passing second time made them get younger by each month. Allan looked at a man who was already turning into a 2 month old.The baby wasnt able to stand anymore or crawl. Allan crawled to him and watched him thinking it was kind of interesting.Allan watched as the baby turned into a month old.Allan looked at himself and noticed he had turned into a 11 month old he watched as the baby's clothing changed. The baby now layed ontop of a blanket and his diaper had changed to one that all the other babies werent wearing. Allan had a second to realize. A newborn's. It had only balloons on it and said happy birthday. Allan then thought the baby would dissappear but his diaper had disappeared leaving a naked newborn. Allan thought about how old the baby must now be. A 9 month old fetus is what the baby would be. Allan looked at himself and noticed he was now a 9 month old baby.Allan looked at Kathy and giggled at how funny she looked.Allan looked down as the baby became younger to 8 monthes now. Allan looked at how much smaller the baby was but he thought it was odd that the baby didnt seem to be in pain or anything or having any difficulties like a baby born at that age would. Allan concluded the device was changing physical age appearance.Allan watched as the baby turned into a 7 month old.Allan thought for a sec and realized something, he was going to end up just like this baby and just like his grandparents, not being born. Allan looked at the baby and then Allan felt upset. He started to bawl like any other baby would. Kathy crawled over to Allan but she felt it was getting harder to do that because she was getting to young to crawl. In her hand was the device.She looked at the much smaller baby.The baby was now a 6 month old preemie.The baby's fat had all disappeared and his skin seemed translucent.Allan and Kathy were now 7 month olds. They cried even more now. Allan grabbed the device and started trying to get it to work but nothing happened. The baby beside them was now 5 monthes old. The baby was only 9 inches in length. Allan now focused on the device while Kathy focused on the baby next to her. The baby now entered the 4th month. Kathy found it harder to see the baby now because he was 6 inches long. Kathy looked an noticed she was 5 month old.The baby now seemed to disappear because they couldnt see him since it entered the 3rd month. Kathy and Allan were now 4 month old babies. Allan found it hard to sit up now and the same with Kathy. They started to cry harder now. They looked at each other for a minute and wondered what would happen soon. Allan looked around at how much bigger the world was. At that moment they both changed to being 3 monthes old. Allan looked down at the device now. It seemed so big now. It seemed almost as big as him. He now looked at anyone who was older than him but there werent many people. He now turned to being 2 monthes old. He now had to lay on his back because of his age. He was now one month old. He looked over at Kathy in tears. He noticed her change to newborn now. That meant he was a newborn too. He reached to the device and pressed a button one more time. Would it regress them more? Turn them back to normal? Or Make them disappear?
