    Feeling a bit of regret

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 months ago AR Baby

    Madison bit her lip, contemplating their options. The AR Patch was designed to steadily drain years from its wearer until removed, with warnings about potential consequences if left on for too long. While part of her wanted to see just how far Mr. Cameron's transformation could go, she also felt a twinge of guilt about pushing the prank too far.

    "We should probably take it off soon," Madison finally replied in a hushed tone. "He's already looking completely different. I don't want anything bad to happen to him."

    Pauline nodded thoughtfully. "You're right. We've had our fun. Let's figure out a way to remove it without him noticing."

    They watched Mr. Cameron for a few more moments, ensuring he was still preoccupied with his belongings. Then, with careful precision, Pauline approached him from behind and swiftly removed the AR Patch.

    Mr. Cameron instinctively rubbed the spot where the patch had been, momentarily puzzled by the sudden sensation of something missing. He turned to find Madison and Pauline wearing innocent expressions, though their eyes twinkled with suppressed laughter.

    "Is everything alright, Mr. Cameron?" Madison asked sweetly, feigning concern.

    Mr. Cameron blinked, noticing a subtle difference in how he felt. "Yeah... yeah, I think so. Just felt a little strange for a moment."

    Pauline chimed in smoothly, "Probably just the excitement of being here at the resort, right?"

    Mr. Cameron chuckled, feeling a renewed sense of energy and vitality. "Must be."

    "Let's hit the pool first," Madison suggested to Pauline.

    Pauline nodded eagerly. "Sounds perfect. But first, a quick shower to freshen up."

    They made their way to the luxurious bathroom in their shared suite, giggling like schoolgirls as they stripped down and turned on the water. Steam filled the room as they stood under the warm spray, washing away the remnants of their mischievous adventure.

    Madison and Pauline continued their shower, chatting and laughing about their successful prank on Mr. Cameron. Unbeknownst to them, the shampoo they had picked up was not just any ordinary shampoo—it was F.O.Y Shampoo, short for Fountain of Youth.

    As they lathered their hair with the golden liquid, they joked about Mr. Cameron's new look. But soon after rinsing, a strange tingling sensation swept over them. They exchanged confused glances as they noticed their skin becoming smoother, their hair more vibrant, and a renewed energy coursing through their bodies.

    Meanwhile, Mr. Cameron had just finished packing a few things in his suitcase when he heard the bathroom door creak open behind him. Expecting to see Madison and Pauline, his teenage charges, he turned around with a quizzical expression. However, what he saw made his heart skip a beat.

    Instead of the mischievous students, two 1.5-year-old baby girls were crawling out of the bathroom. They looked up at him with wide eyes, their faces still wet from the shower, and their tiny hands gripping the floor as they tried to steady themselves.

    Mr. Cameron's shock was palpable as he knelt down in front of them, his mind racing to comprehend what had happened. "Madison? Pauline?" he whispered, disbelief evident in his voice.
