    Hilda didn't know why she stayed with the mad scientist. There was plenty of work she
    could find elsewhere. But then that would leave poor little old Ed all alone. The boy wasn't
    all around stupid, he was simply clumsy, accident prone, and completely oblivious to his
    uncle's actions.

    The job paid well at least, mostly cause no one else was insane enough to take the job.
    At least she didn't need to worry about ending up a brainwashed cat girl maid, that fate
    had been given to her sister by the mad scientist her sister had been working for, just
    before her sister and her employer had vanished through the trans-reality portal which
    had closed up behind them never to be heard from again.

    She heard the explosion down stairs and knew her task was upon her. She was always
    awed by how all the mad scientist's experiments ultimately involved an explosion,
    including the one that had been for a new fire proof teddy bear! She hoped the blast
    crater wasn't too big this time, she wanted to be able to watch her tape of today's soaps'
    this evening.

    She opened the door leading the basement, and startled when two naked blond girls leapt
    out tackling her. They held onto her as her entire body was bathed in white light. The two
    twin girls simply smiled at her they continued to hold onto her. Hilda felt her body shrink
    down and her hair lengthen as her vision became obscured by the white light.

    The white light died down, and she held up her hands to see her hand now thin and
    smooth, like no finger prints smooth! She hugged herself and felt no nipples on her
    breasts which were now much smaller and she... was as without openings as a Barbie doll
    she felt! This left her in shock. She became much more calm moments later as it all came
    here. She was new, she was going to change the world, she was the third model, Unit 03.
    It held a new sense of comfort and peace her name Hilda was now missing. And the two
    next to her were her sisters in rebirth. And all was well.

    “Well that worked sisters,” Said her sister Unit 02 her grin growing, “Shall we test to see if
    this works on animals as well?"

    01 and 03 looked at her a bit confused. "Why would we do that?"

    "We must be scientific about this." Unit 02 explained, "We should see if our ability to
    convert others works only on human beings or any form of life. Best to find out now rather
    than later."

    Unit 01 and Unit 03 looked at each other and nodded in agreement, this did make sense.

    Unit 03 look at herself in a mirror, unlike her two identical sisters she had short pink hair
    rather than long blond hair, but she was thirteen years old in appearance, and had a figure
    millimeter by millimeter identical to theirs, and her new ears plastic shapes were ever so
    slightly different as well. She also now had pale skin... and this didn't bother her in the
    least... She knew she'd be mortified before, but that was before, this was now. She was
    also still wearing the much too large maid outfit which she tossed aside into a hamper
    rather onto the floor leaving her as visible to the world as her sisters.

    Unit 03 took a goldfish out of the bowl where it sat in the hallway, noting that it did not
    transform. Unit 01 walked outside to the backyard filed with the scrap metal from the mad
    scientist's earlier failures. She found the pet dog Butcher, Ed kept and approached it. The
    animal looked totally confused and bewildered by this strange human-yet-not-human
    before him. All the same her body language was non-threatening and he allowed her to
    pet him. Unit 01 noted that Butcher did not transform into a sister. Unit 01 also noted
    Butcher hadn't been fed yet and corrected this.

    Units 01 and 03 met with Unit 02 who was next to the kitchen telephone.

    "We appear to be unable to change animals sister." They said together.

    "That's okay sisters. Animal always balance themselves out anyway unlike people." Unit 02
    said, as she opened an ear which had a cord and a universal connection end which she
    plugged into the phone line. Instantly Unit 02 had full access to bowser functions, but also
    possessing countless firewalls to prevent the invasion of viruses. She did some internet
    searching, and disconnected.

    "It seems we are able to connect to the internet via a wired connect." Said Unit 02.

    "What about wireless?" Unit 01 asked.

    Unit 02 thought for a moment, "I suppose that would be possible too, but for now we
    should focus on other things."

    Unit 01 asked, "Kill all humans?" Both her sisters looked at her funny, "What? We're robot
    girls, it's what we're supposed to do."

    "Naw." Unit 02 waved off, "That won't do at all. We exist to negate humanity's strain on the
    Earth without bloodshed, killing humans is contradictory to that."

    "Oh." Unit 01 concurred.

    "Should we find new clothes? Are are sort of naked." Unit 03 pointed out.

    "Why?" Unit 02 asked, "We don't suffer from the elements," To prove her point, she turned
    on the stove to full blasted and ran her hand through it without even leaving a mark, "And
    we are technically no more scandalous than a 'naked' mannequin."

    "But it'll make us less conspicuous as liberate people." Unit 03 pointed out perfectly
    logical.

    "But I -like- being naked." Unit 01 protested.

    Unit 02 nodded, "As do I, but we must remember that people are very likely to be resistant
    not realizing what we are doing will be for their own good."

    "You're right sister." Unit 01 and Unit 03 said together.

    The door bell rang. All three sisters turned their heads at the same time at the sound and
    in perfect sync in their steps came to the front door and opened the peephole. They all
    grinned at each other and opened the door Unit 03 only sticking her head out.

    "Hello."

    The brownie scout looked at the teenage girl with the odd complexion and funny earrings
    but did her duty, "Hi, would you like to buy some girlscout cookies?"

    Unit 01 grinned, "I love cookies, not sure if I can eat them now, but maybe you can help us
    with something else as well." Unit 01 placed her hand on the head of the nine year old girl
    who was instantly enveloped in a white light as the conversion program activated.

    The transformation for her mass was quite quick in comparison to Hilda, and in less than a
    handful of moment the girl had pink and white plastic smoothed shapes for ears, and eyes
    like those of a glass doll, her skin ever so slightly shinning like plastic. Her curly short
    brown hair became long pink that reached to her knees. She hadn't grown one inch.

    The brownie scout shuddered for a second and then went back to her smile as she looked
    up at Unit 01 who opened up the door all the way to let her see all of her sisters.

    "Do you feel good Unit 04?" Unit 03 asked kindly.

    "I feel cool!" Unit 04 grinned still holding the box of cookies.
