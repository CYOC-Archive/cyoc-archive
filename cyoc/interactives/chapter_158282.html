    "I wish I could live under the sea." As if om cue. Damon heard a female voice. "Maybe I can help you."

    Damon looks and sees a women with dark green hair and light green skin with forest green octopus tentacles where her legs use to be.

    Damon was about to scream. But Alora cover his mouth with her tentacles. "Now now I'm not here to hurt you. I want to help you. I'm Alora." Said Alora.

    Damon felt excited. But he felt something was off. "This all sounds too good. What's the catch?" He ask.

    "Well aren't you smart. If you sign you'll become a merman. But in three days. You'll be drag into my cave and turn into a polyp." Said Alora.

    Damon wanted to live in the sea. But then he'll belong to Alora. Damon decided to take the risk and signs the contract.

    Damon then fell as his legs fuse together into one limb. Royal blue scales then appear on his legs.

    His toes change into his new tail fins as open slits appear on his torso as Damon was now a merman.

    "Whoa this is amazing." Damon goes into the water to enjoy his new life.
