    The moment if truth is here. My parents were quite upset, though they've kept their words to themselves at dinner, even Lucy and Kate could tell something was up. We walked towards the principal's office... They really weren't saying anything. Are they that upset or are they waiting until they hear more? I think it would be better if they talked. As we neared the principal's office, the door opened. A girl came out. Wait. This girl... Wasn't she the one who ignored me? Ms. Mason was with her. "Thank you again," was what the teacher had said. The girl left stealing a glance at me. "Come on in." Ms.Mason led us in. There was the principal. He was definitely scary looking. We sat down. My mother was the first to talk. "First off, we would like to apologize for all the trouble our son had caused." Right for the kill huh?" "About that..." the principal started up. "We would like to apologize." Huh? My parents reacted the same way. Ms.Mason continued. "You see one of our students Eliza had come up to me and told me that it was Jared who had instigated the fight two days ago. I was quite shocked. I was of the understanding that everyone had corroborated Jared's statement. And then others talked. They said the same thing. It would seem in my shortsightedness I had failed to noticed Jared and his friends had created a class of fear. Everyone was quite afraid to ever speak up to him. Even one of his friends, Clark, was now telling me the same thing" Clark? If I remember correctly, he was the one who had kept watch. He did seem out of place with those little shits. "Ms. Mason?" I asked. "Was Eliza the girl just passed me?" "Yes. Apparently she felt guilty for ignoring you and when James attacked Jared she felt the need to tell me the truth." "With all that said, we would like on behalf of the school, apologize for our rush to judgement," the principal added. I don't believe it. The others were like James too. They wanted to do something about it, but they were afraid of the consequences. But to think they would up an defend me. Amazing. There was just one thing I needed to confirm. "Um... About James?" "Naturally he is still suspended for the next week," Ms. Mason told me. I suppose that made sense. "But considering the circumstances , he will not suspended and depending on how he presents himself for the rest of the year we will scrub this incident from his permanent record." That's what I wanted to hear. "Thank you very much!"

    After that I left for class. My parents for work. They were quite relieved. I entered. There was no tripping today. Jared wasn't here and though his friends were there, they kept to themselves. Clark was at another table now. I sat in my seat. "Um.... Excuse me?" Some one called out to me. It was the girl Eliza. "Um... I'd like to apologize.... And maybe.... we could be friends?"
