    Chris was your average 16 year old boy. He had a love for video games, sports and wasn’t very fond of school. He was of decent build with shirt brown hair and blue eyes. He always knew he was different from everyone else at his school, something just felt like it made him different but he could never quite find out what. He had always been fascinated by the body swapping articles and chapters he had read, and the videos he had watched online. He dreamed of being able to possess this power one day. Little did he know what this next day held in store for him, and how it would change his life forever...

    It was a Sunday. This meant no school and Chris could just relax for the day. The previous day he had spent inside on the PS4 playing games with his friends online so today he decided to go outside and just walk around town. There had been a new high street built recently with a ton of new shops opened just the previous week so he thought “Why not go there.” He put on his Adidas trainers and set off out. He began the 15 minute walk down the pathway to reach the shop. It was very windy outside today and so it made it a tad more difficult to wall. After about 15 minutes he had finally reached the shops, but to his dismay they were all closed as it was a Sunday. “Great just what I wanted.” he sighed in displeasure and was about to walk home when he spotted one shop that was open. “The Magic Shop” it was called. “A range of mysterious and powerful items lie inside. Why not come take a look?” a sign below it read. Chris decided to enter the shop.

    Upon entering the shop he was greeted by an old man who was dressed rather peculiarly in a large blue robe. He had long locks of withered grey hair and walked with a walking stick. “Greeting Chris.” the man welcomed. Chris looked at the man in confusion. He had not told this man his name and had never met him before. “How is it you know my name?” he asked with curiosity
    “It is my duty to know all who seeks the possessions of my shop young one.” the man responded with in a rather mysterious tone.
    “It seems I may have an item that suits your needs. As you can tell I am a mind reader and I can see you have the desires of bodily possession in your mind an I not mistaken?” the man asked.
    Chris looked back at him in wild confusion once more. He had never shared this secret with anyone in his life but was curious to find out more.
    “What sort of item is it?” Chris asked the man.
    The man walked up behind his counter, followed closely by Chris and pulled out a very old box. This box was labelled the “Ring of Possession.” The man opened the bid to reveal a golden ring with an emerald green encrusted ring on it. He pulled the ring out of the box and gave it to Chris.
    “Put it on and try it out. See if it suits your desires or not.” the man told him
    Chris put the ring on and asked “How does it work?”
    “Simply think of someone to possess, click your fingers and you will become them.” the man stated rather excitedly
    Chris thought of who to possess.
