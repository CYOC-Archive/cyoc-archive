    “Do you want some help changing Quinn?” His Grandmother asked.

    “Yes, please” Quinn smiled.

    He unwrapped the gift, the gloves made it a little difficult but he managed. He pulled out a unicorn T-Shirt and some jeans, the outfit looked like it would fit him just fine.

    “Oh it looks like I got Lori!”
