    The girl who wants to be a humantaur

    added by Anonymous 2 days ago O Body merge

    Many decades passed after the great humantaur fusion wave and no other event like that has occurred again. But the wave caused some sequels, people who were fused into humantaurs were never separated again and a cure to reverse it was never found. At the same time, after the wave there has been cases of people who are born as humantaurs instead of two merged people, that happens when a humantaur breeds with another one or even with a regular person, but the cases were humantaurs are born are rare as one can also give birth to a normal human. To this day there has been more naturally born humantaurs comprared to the ones who were merged by the wave many decades ago.

    Looking at the present the humantaurs became something normal for people to see outside, even becoming a symbol of greatness and are viewed higher in society, commonly called a better version of “boring” human beings. They could travel faster thanks to their four legs, carry people on their lower body’s back like a horse and they had double the energy of a normal person. And taking a peek in their anatomy they were almost like a normal human as their upper body lacked organs more than a long tube that connected their mouth with their lower body’s stomach, but they were different to people that become a humantaur by the fusion wave having twice the organs, resulting into a greater version composted to those who were born as humantaurs.

    At the same time there was this 20 years old college girl called Amber who was born as a normal human, but admiring humantaurs since she was little. She sometimes even hated being a common person and seeing herself as inferior, seeing some people born as humantaurs sharing classes with her, envying their privileges. But she also hated her twin sister Kate who sadly lived together with her. Kate was also a normal human and wasn’t even a bad person but always reminded Amber that she was a human and nothing would change that, that she needed to forget about that childish fantasy about becoming a humantaur… that, always infuriated Amber.

    But nothing could stop Amber for pursuing her dreams, and she would do ANYTHING in order to make it real…
