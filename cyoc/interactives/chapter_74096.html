    Realizing that the changes weren't going to reverse themselves,Jennifer decided
    to seek some answers.

    First of all she got a crowbar and headed down to the family burial vaults
    with a lantern and moved through the line of crypts till she found the one
    she was looking for,that of 14 year old Alessa Smith-Lambe 1925-1939

    She pried open the lid and looked inside.

    Within lay a twistd skelton draped in dried folds of blue scaly
    hide.It wasn't human but was humanoid to some extent.A fireplace poker was shoved in the
    creatures chest and there were scratch marks on the inside of the tombs lid,as if death hadn't come easily.

    It was a aninmal not in a girls clothing,with a bony spiky tail annd its head was reptillian,with a lengthy
    snout and sharp pointy teeth like the dentition Jennifer now had.

    And visible through a gaping hole in its chest was its last meal
    a bony human hand with wrist bones.

    And other stuff.

    It seemed Alessa Smith-Lambe had been munching away on people when someone had
    driven the fireplace poker into the creature and forced it into its final resting
    place.

    Perhaps the dying girl-monster had bitten off one of her assailants hands as a last act of revenge before
    the coffin lid was shut on her

    Jennifer looked impassively at her grand aunt Alessa,many
    truths flooding her mind

    One being that the hand Alessa had bitten off had likely belonged
    to one of Kyle Perkins
    ancestors

    It was all starting to make sense now

    The "Transform or Dare" legend was actually a whitewash
    to cover up a real curse,one that turned her family line into some kind
    of reptillian monster,a curse stopped by Dragons Bane

    And Kyle Perkins,knowing that,had someone
    set off the family curse inside Jennifer
    as revenge for what Alessa had done decades earlier...
