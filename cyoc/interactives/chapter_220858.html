    You think twice about this. Genies are tricky no matter what, so even if you wished for something simple, you know it might backfire on you. With that in mind, you don't even bother thinking about wishing for world peace or to end world hunger. Nor do you think about a wish to get rid of her.

    ''You know what? Thanks but I'm good. I should get going now.'' you say and begin walking away.

    ''...What? You get the chance of a lifetime and you're just going to walk away, master? Not even going to wish for something as simple as money?'' she asks, certainly surprised.

    ''Yeah! Best of luck with your wishes though, I'm sure you'll find somebody soon enough though.'' you answer, trying to get as far away as you can from the genie.

    ''Now that won't do. Wish or we're going to have a little problem. Seriously, just wish for something simple, you won't get tricked then. Not wishing... is cheating, master. I don't like cheaters.'' she says teleports in front of you in a poof, clearly annoyed at you playing safe.

    No matter how much she tries or get annoyed, you don't budge. You don't even care if that's ''cheating'', you didn't agree to this in the first place. You can only hope she will give up eventually, but it doesn't come to that. Unfortunately for you, there is no rule stopping genies from rebelling against their masters.

    ''Ugh, been a long while since I got one like you. I suppose I'll get some use out of you regardless, my sister needs that place populated anyway.'' she says and crosses her arms.

    Your eyes widen in shock, you thought she'd just go back to her bottle eventually! You attempt to talk to her, but it's no use. She snaps her fingers and a purple portal opens up near you, it looks as if it's a rip on fabric of time, and its brightness hurts your eyes. The strange anomaly attempts to suck you in, but you dig your fingers into the dirt desperately and hold on. The genie watches as you struggle and smirks, the portal has no interest in her.

    She grabs your hands and removes them from the dirt, the portal still tries really hard to suck you in, but she holds your hands to stop that. The genie kisses your forehead before saying goodbye to her master and then simply let's you get sucked into the unknown. Thankfully, you don't spend a lot of time inside the great big mess of colours and flashes. When you open your eyes again, you're laying on the grass on what seems to be endless plains. Something is certainly different though.

    You desperately look down to find yourself covered in yellow, spotted fur. You scream in shock, but only a roar comes out which causes you to grab your new muzzle in surprise. You constantly shake your head and blink to see if you'll wake up, but you don't. You seem to have been turned into some sort of man cheetah. How funny. With the initial shock you get back on your feet. You're almost naked, the jeans you previously wore got torn apart and are now a pair of ripped shorts and they seem to be the only thing that survived your transformation. Not that it matters, your fur seems to provide you with good protection. A look behind you confirms that you do indeed have a curious tail.

    Spotting a river close by, you walk there and look at your reflection. You couldn't lie, you look quite handsome. You're tall, muscular and it seems you don't look as animal as you thought you looked. Your feet are plantigrade paws, you have kept the long hair you had as a human, and you can still speak. Taking some time to reflect, you also find out you have many new instincts and desires. You're still very lost though, no doubt about it.

    Figuring there's no use just standing around, you venture out into the endless plains. Finding out that you're quite the runner and that your new body is full of stamina.

    What do you do now?
