He turned to look at Josh, intending to blame him for what was going to happen, when the words died in his throat.

Josh was giving Chris the same puzzled and stupified look he was sure he was giving his brother. He quickly ran to the bathroom, looking into the mirror. Chris' eyes darkened, while Josh's got a little lighter, both becoming a dark hazel. They year gap between them continued to shrink, Josh looking older by the second, as the years slowly melted off of Chris.

There were even subtle changes in their features. As brothers there had been a similarity in their looks to begin with, but now nothing was different. Everything had been averaged out between them. Chris couldn't help but think it was a little spooky. Both opened their mouths to speak at the same time. Even their voices sounded like echoes of each other.
