He looks over at his sister who is scootched up right next to him on the love-seat, but luckily for him, she can't read. So he just opens the envelope, pulls out a letter and gives it a read.

You were going to become a 16-year-old girl, and with the body of one who had given birth to a child at that--even though no child of yours had (or was going to) come into existence.

    However, fortunately for you, the designer of this power wrote a fail-safe into its code for situations like this. It will be detected with the owner of the power obviously is confused about the logic of their wishes, and is trying desperately to reverse what they've done. This, clearly, was the situation you had put yourself in.

    When you wished to reverse your gender bending, your breastfeeding wish was reversed as well, in order to maintain the balance of logic. And then, naturally, this letter was fabricated and placed on your lap, so that you could come to an understanding of your mistake.

    So keep in mind, if you desire to continue making wishes, to take time to consider the logical ramifications of what you're wishing for, and more importantly, how the various wishes you make might interact with one another.

    Thank you for reading all of this."

This time, however, there was considerably more cabinet space than there usually was, which would make things much less cluttered, if it weren't for the addition of several large packages of diapers filling up so much space. Diapers, in addition to many other baby supplies like powder, cream, wipes, and various special needs paraphonalia.

    The longer he looked into this cabinet, he realized that it actually is much deeper than he thought; it's just hard to see all the way back there. The back of the cabinet is stuffed full with small pieces of bathroom furniture; an unused stool, a piece of glass that was probably going to be made into a mirror, ext.
    It would seem, even though Zack's family has become rich, they aren't any more organized or less messy. However much space has been afforded by this house, his mom has probably just bought way more stuff to fill all that space with, and they probably haven't ever hired a house cleaner, because Zack knows his mom would have a hard time trusting a stranger to wander around their house no matter what magical timeline they're living in.

    Hilariously, there was also a potty chair stowed away in the back of the cabinet. It was actually pretty big; way too tall for a toddler to sit on, implying that it was intended for an older child.
    "Hoh... I wonder what the story behind that is." Zack thought to himself.

    In this moment, he remembered what the letter had said; that his wishes could have all sorts of unintended side-effects. In this case, there was at least one glaring side-effect to all of these lifestyle-altering wishes Zack had been making; they had been altering the memories of both his mom and sister, but not him.

    Mulling this realization over, Zack considered that there may be a ton of things about his family history that just now retroactively came into existence, that he doesn't know anything about. It would be very awkward if, for example, his mom brought over friends from work (something he knows she tends to do), and none of them were the same people they were before, because his mom probably works somewhere totally different now, considering she makes so much more money.

    Susan and him often would often take a book or comic or manga home, take turns reading it over the course of a week, then have several long conversations about the thing they just read. These conversations became the foundation for a lot of the ideas and phrases the two of them shared; the start of a lot of their running jokes they'd crack at each other.
    But given that, thanks to his wish, Suzie has never learned to read, it would make sense if literally none of these conversations ever happened. Even talking to his sister might get confusing sometimes, since they would be talking to one another from two different timelines.

    "Okayyy...." Zack whispers to himself. "I wish I had all the memories of this altered reality, in addition to my memory of the original reality that existed before I made any wishes."
