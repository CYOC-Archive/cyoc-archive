    Us, humans ? What are you talking about ?

    added 5 years ago A O Mental

    The doggy quatuor, once relieved from their frights, stay silent for a while, thinking about what happened.
    Eventually, Maggie breaks out the deadly hush.
    " Maybe I should have pointed the phone at us instead of Callum and Braiden... "
    " Maggie- " starts the latter.
    " I mean, it makes sense : we turned into dogs because the phone thought being dogs was the norm here... "
    " Maggie " interrupts Brody, " It's no use. We're dogs now. And for how long, I don't even know... "
    The Ainu frowns a bit.
    " You think it's permanent ? I have my doubts... "
    She reaches for the phone, laying off on the floor since the event. But phones are not designed for dogs, let alone newly transformed ones.
    Maggie can't help but curse at those paws that replaced her dexterous fingers and thumbs.
    Meanwhile, Callum's attention was focusing on he outside world, staring at the garden of Brody's house. The Shepherd's face is showing severe signs of concentration.
    Braiden, who saw the whole scene, was getting a bit concerned at his friend.
    "Hey, Cal, are you alright ? "
    " Look at the tree, do you see anything ? " asks Callum.
    - See what ? replies Braiden.
    - I think there might be a squirrel on that tree.
    Braiden steps back, surprised.
    " Cal " he barks annoyed. " This is not the time to focus on a rodent ! We must find a way to go back to our former selves ! "
    " Former selves ? " mutters the Shepherd.
    - Yes, we are humans ! Not dogs !
    The Shepherd stops looking at the garden and stares sternly at Braiden.
    " Humans ? Us ? Are you alright ? I don't recall being a human in my entire life. "
    The rest of the group, which was focusing on the conversation shortly after, is shocked beyond words.

    " Oh no... " whimpers Maggie.
    " This is just great " adds Brodie on a lash. " Not only are we condemned to be canines, but we are going to lose ourselves one by one ! "
    " Cal " begs Braiden. " Tell me you're joking. You can't be serious... "
    The Shepherd looks confused.
    " Hey, hold on, I thought there was a squirrel, there's no need to be worried... "
    - But Cal, asks Brody, what about your parents ? Your family ? Don't you wanna see your brother and sister again ?
    - You must be having something weird, replies the Shepherd, I don't live with my family anymore... Unless you meant my masters then.
    The trio stays silent from what they heard. Except Braiden which saddens a bit because of the situation.
    But then, he stops, and stares angrily at Maggie.
    " This is your fault ! " he growls.
    " What ?! " replies the Ainu, surprised.
    - If you didn't had the brilliant idea of turning on the phone and using it, this whole thing wouldn't have happened !
    - Why are you saying it's my fault ?
    whimpers the Ainu. I don't even understand what you're talking about !
    Brody and Braiden look at each other in shock.
    " What do you mean ? " asks Brody, concerned.
    " Just because you're being angry for something doesn't mean you have to be mad at me, Braiden ! You're so mean ! "
    And just like that, she puts her muzzle between her forelegs, tail low from the discomfort of the situation.
    " Maggie ? " barks meekly Braiden.
    " I wish that you were nicer to me, and that I could see my masters right now... " mutters the Ainu.

    " This is no good " casually barks Brody.
    " It's just the two of us now, and I'm not being optimistic about this whole thing... "
    - What can we do ? Asks Brody, worried.
    - Well, nothing but maybe embrace this new reality. At least, it looks like we're still friends despite the changes...
    - But 30 minutes ago, we were still humans ! I was playing video games with Callum ! We... We were just iddling and... enjoying... ourselves...
    Braiden looks miserable now. The whole sum of events is making him more and more depressed.
    The Boxer looks at him in sadness. He can't stand seeing his doggy comrade in pain.
    Raising a paw, he pats him softly and comforts him as much as a doggy pat allows it.
    The two other dogs look at him the same, and get closer to cuddle him.
    " It's okay, Braiden " barks the Boxer. " We don't know what's making you worked up, but it'll be fine. Don't be sad. "
    - Our masters are probably looking for us, says the Ainu in a reassuring voice. It won't take long for them to find us and bring us home. And I'm not mad at you anymore.
    - I'm sure you'll be fine, remarks the Shepherd. Your master is waiting for you. You love him more than anything else but us of course. Brody and Maggie are right.
    Braiden looks up at his former friends, and can't help but grinning a bit.
    " Thank you so much " replies the Corgi. " I'm so glad you are my friends ! "
    The whole group, now reunited, spent their hours waiting fo their masters, iddling and playing joyfully, ignoring the phone laying on one side of the room.
