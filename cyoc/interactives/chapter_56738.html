    I looked around at all the people that had been on CYOC and been drawn into this
    story. Most of them had the same shocked expressions on their faces as I'm sure I
    had on mine. This couldn't really be happening could it? I saw three kids, two boys
    and a girl, that were definitely underage in this room. I hoped nothing would happen to
    them which could scar them for the rest of their lives. They were huddled into a group
    looking scared so I decided to go over to them. "Hello," I said with a smile. "I'm Rick.
    What are your names?"

    "Sara," the girl said quietly. I figured she was about eleven though it didn't look like she
    had undergone puberty yet. She had red hair, green eyes, and freckles. I wasn't really
    that good at guessing heights but I thought she was about four and a half foot tall.

    "I'm Michael," the boy who seemed to be the oldest said trying to be brave. I was
    guessing that he was fourteen and that he worked out since he seemed to have some
    muscle on him. His hair was dirty blond and he had brown eyes. He was close to my
    own height of five foot eight so I put him at about five foot five.

    "Bai," the second boy said looking around nervously. The other two kids were white
    while he was either Chinese or Japanese. He had black hair and brown eyes. I put his
    height at a bit under five foot and I thought he was about twelve.

    "What were you kids doing on CYOC?" I asked wanting to know. "No one under
    eighteen is supposed to be on there."

    "No one is going to tell me where I can go on the Internet," Michael said drawing
    himself up some. I hoped he wasn't about to start a fight.

    "My friend talked about it so I decided to see it for myself," Bai said blushing a bit.

    "I just passed Mommy's computer when a white light came out of it," Sara said looking
    up at me with pleading eyes. "I want to go home."

    I looked around the room and noticed that there weren't as many in here as before.
    They must have gone off to explore or maybe they had already been transformed. I
    had to wonder if Changes was sucked into this story as well. I looked at Sara and
    said, "I'll see if I can get you home. Michael, how much have you read on CYOC?"

    "Well enough to know that most transformations end up in..." Michael began.

    "That's enough," I interrupted. I didn't want Sara and Bai hearing the word sex and
    asking questions. I looked around again and sighed then said, "Well we won't find a
    way to get you guys back home just standing here. We'll have to explore a bit and
    hope that we don't get transformed to where we can't look for a way out. There's three
    doors out of this room. Which one should we take?"
