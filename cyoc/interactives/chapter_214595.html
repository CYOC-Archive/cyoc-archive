    Raven Cross of Transanimal approached the podium. She looked into the camera (obviously).
    "I am Raven Cross of the company Transanimal. I am here to introduce the newest Transanimal Costume. Fetus Costumes." She said with a smile into the camera. "Now what is a Fetus Costume? It's just as it's said. It's a costume that turns the wearer into a fetus like thing. How's it work you ask? It's simple. You put it on & it makes you into an undefined fetus thing ready to go into a female feral or anthropomorphic animal. You just approach the female you want to be your new mom & she'll present for you. You then put your soft rounded off top point of your head into her vagina. Your body will quickly start to shrink regressing losing your limbs, bones & organs, eyes, nose, ears as you are pulled into the female's body. You will quickly regress to a week old undefined fetus after entering the womb of the female. You will then start to develop into the young of whatever animal you entered. Later you'll be birthed as their young." Raven Cross explained. Then she got a really serious look. "We must tell you that once you regress into a week old undefined fetus you can't return to being human. You aren't a costume user anymore. You just a fetus. You will grow & develop into the creature's young. You'll just be an animal; be it feral or anthropomorphic. You will be birthed as that animal's young after you fully develop." There was a pause before she said more. "And if you became the fetus of another Animal Costume User that user as normal will be locked in their Animal Costume & form while pregnant with you intill they give birth to you; or lay you as an egg if they are not a mammal." Raven Cross then said with a smile "Hope you enjoy our new Fetus Costume. Intill next time." The public video then ended.
