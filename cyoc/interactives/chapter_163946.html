    I groaned as I slowly sat up in my rather small bed and turned to look at my clock. The clock read 9:00am meaning that I had once again overslept. "Crap..." I quickly pulled off my covers and groggily shuffled out of bed almost pulling the entire blanket off with me as I did. "The markets going to be full of them now." With a sigh I put on my slippers and started walking towards the bathroom.

    As I walked there I couldn't help but stop to glance out my window. Strangely enough the streets outside were completely empty. Usually there were at least a few of "them" wandering about, but all that seemed to reside there were cars and other junk. Either left behind by an uncaring person or one of "them". I shrugged and made my way into the bathroom quickly turning the faucet on and splashing some water on my face. I almost panicked when I looked up into the mirror and saw the pair of cat ears atop my head. I quickly put a hand to my head knocking off the pair of fake cat ears. The plastic band making a slight *clack* as it impacted the tile floor.

    After taking a moment to collect myself from my near heart attack I let out a nervous and relieved chuckle at my own stupidity. "Guess I forgot to take them off last night..."

    After mentally cursing at my past self for almost giving me a heart attack I proceeded to get ready for the day. Taking a shower, brushing my teeth, putting on deodorant, you know, the usual kind of things that you don't really need details about. I stepped out of the shower and proceeded to head back to my room to get dressed... Which had to be the most embarrassing part of the day.

    I opened up my closet and looked at the line of woman's clothing that sat against my old male clothes with an unamused look. To open up my closet to what mostly consisted of girls clothing was pretty...weird to say the least, but it was necessary... At least I think it is. I grabbed a long sleeve shirt, skirt, and leggings and quickly shut the closest door behind me tossing the clothes on to my bed as I did. I walked over to my dresser and grabbed the bottle of perfume.

    My nose wrinkled slightly at the smell. It was strong. Strong to the point of making me want to gag... But it too was a necessity. I sprayed some of it on me. The flowery aroma quickly sticking to me.

    After getting dressed I walked over to my kitchen already expecting the worst. I glanced over at the trashcan full of junk food wrappers, cans and other trash as I opened up my fridge. Sure enough, I was nearly out of food. The half a sandwich and half a liter of soda a clear indication of that. I frowned "Which means I need to go to the market...Great." I sighed and walked back to my bedroom. Making sure to grab the fake cat ears on my way there. I opened a drawer and pulled out my fake cat tail quickly snapping it to my pants. I then put the fake cat ears on my head and walked back to the bathroom. After a few minutes of makeup and brushing I glanced into the mirror to check my handy work.

    I normally would have loved to see what appeared to be a cute girl looking back at me, but given the fact that cute "girl" was actually me just made me really sad about where life has taken me. Though I suppose my rather girlish looks did contribute to my disguise. "Well, here goes nothing" I walked out of the bathroom and to the front door putting on my jacket and shoes as I did. I paused, realizing that I was forgetting two very important items. The first of which being my backpack. I walked over to my table and grabbed the bag so I could carry whatever supplies I could gather in it. The second item, while not necessarily important for my survival, was my journal. I walked over and chuckled picking up the small book labeled "C.A.T. Survival guide."

    Finally, I took a deep breath and stepped to the door. "Let's see what today has in store shall we?" With that I opened the apartment door prepared for whatever would happen.
