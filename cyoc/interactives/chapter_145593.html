    Alex Becomes a Man

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 4 years ago BM S

    John had a gym class next. This group of boys was very athletic, except for a freshman named Alex. Alex always ended up without a partner when it came to units like badminton or ignored by his team while playing flag football or basketball.

    Honestly, Alex had it rough all over. He only shone in academics. At 15, he stood 5'1 tall, weighed 90 pounds, and had a mop of brown hair that covered his eyes at times. He was so thin you could count his ribs and very plain in the face. Worst of all, he had a high thin voice and a severe stutter.

    John decided that Alex was in dire need of a change. He set it up in stages and so that only he and Alex would be aware of the changes.

    Alex was at his locker taking his clothes off when suddenly he felt a pain in his back. He groaned as his bones stretched and grew. He began to inch up, hitting 5'4", 5'6", 5'10", until finally he stood 6'2".

    Astonished, he looked around. Everything looked so different from up here! Unfortunately, his new height accentuated how skinny he was. But now his frame seemed to pack on weight in muscle, until his ribs had vanished and Alex had a lean but fairly muscular 140 pound frame.

    Soon his face began to pop and crack as he cried out in pain. Bones rearranged, his features became more handsome. Before long, his hair was short, wavy blond, his eyes were bright blue, his lips were fuller and his teeth straighter and whiter. His face was gorgeous! This Alex discovered for himself upon looking in the mirror.

    John announced to the class, finishing dressing, that they would be playing co-ed badminton. Out in the gym, Alex worked up the nerve to ask Josie, a really pretty girl, to be his partner.

    "Why of course, Alex," she told him, beaming up at him.

    While they were playing the next phase of changes began. Alex began to grow more muscular, thicker and bigger until he was 210 pounds. When he entered the locker room, he was shocked to see his amazing new body, and how tightly his PE clothes clung to him.

    Thus Alex began a new life as a jock, still only 15, and a hunk in demand around the school, a life far better than his previous one.
