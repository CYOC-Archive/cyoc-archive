    Emily hesitated a little. She thought it was a little odd that her twin was her first choice.

I've also been smoking way more too, but maybe that's just because I was addicted before the change and now it's just hugely amplified." Emily brainstormed.

    "I'll be back in a sec...I'm just gonna go...eat something now" Emily said awkwardly, feeling a little embarrassed.

    She clopped across the concrete and onto the grass, then got down on her knees and started to pull grass out of the ground with her teeth.
    Evie didn't say anything, just intently watching as she smoked.

    Each mouthful felt warm in her belly, as she mooed sometimes while chewing. Emily found her mind wandering.
    'it would be so nice to just do this all the time...live outdoors, completely naked, just spending the day eating grass, and being fucked...' she thought. Memories of friends and family slipped away temporarily as she fantasized about this new life, mooing contently to herself.

    "Em are you okay?" Evie asked.

    Emily snapped back to reality, realising she'd been wandering around the garden on all four hooves mooing into the air. She got back onto her hind legs and sat back down with Evie.

    "Can we try changing those guys we just had over?" Evie asked.

    "I guess so, did you have something in moond?"

    Evie laughed at her sisters moo-slip.

    "Shut up okay I can't help it, I feel like it's getting worse" Emily snapped. Evie stopped laughing and went back to her request.

    "Well if you're a cow, and cows are typically female, and Eli is a bull, which is a male cow, what would happen if you tried to change a guy into a cow?" Evie asked.

    "I dunno, let's find out. Lucas is a cow, Mike is a bull." She said at her phone.

    It updated again instantly.
    Mike Carson - Bull - 1%
    Lucas Pope - Cow - 1%

    "Huh, maybe it does." Evie said.

    "If it worked like we think it did, today probably isn't going to be the last day they're seeing each other naked" Emily joked. The pair laughed.

    "Do you think we're still gonna be part human when the changes are done?" Evie asked.

    "What, like physically or moontally?"

    "Either I guess. Will you still think like your current cow-you or will you just think like a cow? Or will you still look part human part cow, or will you just completely become a cow?" Evie explained.

    Emily instantly thought about where her mind went when she was just eating grass. It worried her a little. 'What if I am going to stop being me, and just become a cow?'

    "I think we'll still be us" Emily said, not really sure if she herself believed it right now.

    Evie didn't say anything. She finished her cigarette and stood up.

    "I'm gonna go lay down for a bit I think. This was all quite a lot to take in" she said, heading over to the door.

    "Yea I might do the same in a bit" Emily said, lighting up one more cigarette for herself.

    Her phone vibrated with a text message. It was from Nate.

    'Hey Em, theres a farm/fair/show thing on tomorrow, wanna go with me?'

    Emily couldn't help but laugh at his ridiculous suggestion, but it also kind of called out to her. She replied, agreeing to meet him at 10am.

    Emily then typed out another text.
    'Hey wanna come over? I think we've got a lot to catch up on'. She sent it to Kate.

    After a few minutes she replied.
    'ill be there in a little while, just at Eli's at the mo ;) ill be over after'.

    Emily stared at the message, feeling a little betrayed. Why was Eli not sending Kate away?!

    Emily finished her cigarette and stormed inside.
