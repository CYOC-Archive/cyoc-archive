    Now around this time there was a popular with the kiddies
    anime and comic book series going around called
    "Miss Prometheus"

    The premise of that series was that back around the
    time dinosaurs were facing extinction there'd been a race
    of super advanced dragons (imagine a dragonnish velociraptor
    with the IQ of Einstein ) who'd built a lizardy-dragonish looking
    robot named "Prometheus".The dragons had invited some of
    their more smarter ancestors the dfinosaurs to join them in this
    Ark which was meant to survive the arrival of the huge asteroid
    thundering towards Earth.

    Alas Prometheus was 15 feet tall upright,which meant its
    inhabitants had to minaturize themselves to about 1/25 of a inch tall
    to fit everyone aboard.

    They'd done so,and when the asteroid hit and wiped out
    everyone else on Earth the reptillian little people aboard the
    robot-lifeboat went into suspended animation.For about 70 million
    years.

    Then Miss Diana Cub ( lets say 6-10 years old ) was hiking with
    3 girlfriends when a earthquake hit and all went topsy turvy.

    Miss Cub had plunged into a cavern that had been sealed up for
    70 million years,where she'd found all sorts of weird science stuff,as
    well as a dormant Prometheus.

    The kid had foolishly touched some of the controls of
    a minaturization-transporter-dragonifier machine,got zapped and got rearranged
    as a 1/25 inch member of the robots crew.Alas after 70 million
    years some of the equipment had gone on the fritz and the vital data needed to return
    the newly created dragon little person to her human self had been lost.

    However the Prometheus's chameleon machines were fully functional
    and the huge robot had reshaped itself to look like the first sentient being
    it came across,which had been Miss Diana Cub prior to her playing with
    machinery she should have left alone.

    So in the anime the Prometheus ended up as Miss Diana Cub
    whilst Miss Diana Cub ended up being turned into one of the tiny dragon
    little people who inhabited and ran the Prometheus.

    Cally was looking at the mousy-bookish girl with the wireframe glasses and wondered
    how she'd fare as a member of the crew of the Prometheus.

    The two hungry werewolves had stopped mating and were looking at the two remaining
    human girls,the female werewolf was drooling and salivating,and the huge male began
    howling at the Full Moon.

    It didn't appear the 4rth girl was going to do much except quake in
    her Wonder Woman boots.Cally turned her attention back to the mousy girl
    put the ring into overdrive and made the tf happen.

    Alas Cally was dissappointed .The girl didn't suddenly shrink into a
    "Miss Prometheus" body,shriek or do much else but stand there.

    Cally glowered at the ring,thought sociopathic thoughts and tossed it to some
    sleeping biker perched on a Citroen 49 mped and stormed off.

    The evil werewolves suddenly leapt apon the defenseless Asian girl and were
    but seconds away from ripping out the screaming girls throat.

    Meanwhile aboard the Prometheus ...

    Miss Diana Cub was furiously furious,she was shriekingly ready
    to mouth off bad words at someone.

    Here she'd just become aware of her own existence,and she'd been
    shunted to the sidelines.

    The Captains Chair sat vacant ,she was surrounded by panicking
    dragon-lizard people who looked mousy,bookish and wore wirerimmed
    glasses,and the cause of it all was a curled up crewmember who mere
    microseconds earlier had been the third girl,and now had become
    Captain Cub ,the role Dianna Cub was supposed to play in this reallife
    anime.

    And the anime character who'd come to life found herself still
    herself,but only 1/25th inch tall.

    The violated anime girl looked at the robots view screens,which showed
    what Prometheus looked like from the outside
    a 5 foot tall bookish mousy 16 something 3rd girl,standing there frozen.

    Definitely not the "Miss Prometheus" of the anime series.

    Miss Diana Cub shrieked in frustration and threw away the script that had materialized
    in her hands,a script now useless and out of date.

    Miss Diana Cub still spoke japanese,whilst the Prometheus crew,including the dragonified
    character stealing former 3rd girl were frantically babbling in some 70 million year old
    language only they understood.

    The anime character remembered the radio and communications
    station had a universal translator,and ran over and pushed several buttons
    till the jabbing roars and hisses dimmed away and the speakers in the walls
    broadcast a more intelligible English translation.

    Well Miss Diana Cub had taken English as a 2nd language
