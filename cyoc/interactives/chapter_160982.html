    Mary wanted to understand the teleporter process better, so she decided to do some testings with the teleporter first. As she waked through the room, looking for something to test the teleporter, Mary felt something hitting her on the right leg with a loud kicking sound. Mary noticed, that a 2 inch big neodymium magnet, which are one of the strongest magnets in the world, pinned on her leg. Mary felt the magnetic field of the magnet with her metallic hairs and grabbed it. The magnetic field felt interesting in her hands, like it tried to pull her hand into it.

    Mary came up with an idea, as she saw the pallet of steel cubes.

    Mary grabbed 6 of the steel cubes and put them into the teleporter. She was still surprised of her strength and how light they felt to her. After Mary put 6 more into the teleporter, she grabbed a box of the neodymium magnet and put them also into the chamber. The 12 steel cubes moved a little by the force of the magnets. The feeling of the strong magnetic field was arousing Mary, as she felt the magnetic force all over her body, like she had a sixth sense.

    If the works out, I have to think about how to make this metal into gold.

    Mary went to the control console and hit the enter key. The door of the teleporter closed and the machine powered up. The known humming sound rose as bright light filled the chamber for the third time. And so was the steel and the box of magnets gone. Only seconds later, the receiver chamber also started to hum as light filled it.

    **** TELEPORTAION PORCESS INITIALIZED ****
    * Closing chamber... success
    * Emission of decomposing radiation initialized. Heating up laser... success
    * Decomposition initialized... success
    * Warning: multiple an-organic material detected. Current teleporting Mode=MERGING
    * merging initialized...
    * 2 an-organic materials detected
    * an-organic material recognized Type=Steel, Mass=720.030 lbs
    * an-organic material recognized Type=Steel, Mass=54.2 lbs
    * Result an-organic material 1: 92,99562145615644‬‬%
    * Result an-organic material 2: 7,00437854384356%
    * Processing merging... done
    * Merging success
    * 2 an-organic material merged, resulting Mass=774.230 lbs
    * Recomposition initialized... success
    * Recomposition finalized
    * Opening door
    **** TELEPORTAION PORCESS COMPLETED WITH SUCCESS ****

    Again, lightning bolts hit the metallic surface of the cubes as they started to materialize in the receiver chamber. Mary was a bit worried as her stomach felt odd. As the re-composition continued, Mary more and more felt the force pulling her towards the receiver. Mary thought, that the steel must have turned into magnets increasing the magnetic force thousandfold. All over the lab all kind of metallic stuff started to move and point towards the receiver. Thanks to her inhuman strength, Mary was able to resist the force of the magnetic field, but she saw that a lot of little metal things were already pinned to the chamber's outer walls. As the materialization continued, Mary had to use almost all of her newfound strength to escape the traction of the supercharged magnets. But when the teleportation finished and the door opened Mary could not longer push against it and was lifted off the ground and flew directly into the chamber... and with her a lot of small and large things were also flying around the room and some of them also landed in the teleporter. Mary shivered as the door closed and the machine started to hum again.

    The magnets were so strong that it was bending the decomposing radiation. Light was only not filling the chamber, but also running wild through the lab, hitting random objects and living beings... and with that, Mary, the magnets and a lot of stuff inside and outside the teleporter vanished...

    Unknown to her, the teleporter was bidirectional, which means both chambers can be used as sender and receiver.
