    Showing off the new you

    You figured that what you have on is enough so you decide to go the pretty cashier to buy the suit. She looks impressed upon seeing you.

    "Well you certainly wear that well "Mayday".." She said with a giggle.

    "So you know who Spidergirl is?" You asked.

    "Yeah you can say I'm a Marvel buff." She replied.

    "So am I!" You said feeling more excited to hear that this pretty lady is also a Marvel geek.

    She smiled as she gazes upon you with quite a lusty look. "I'm betting you'll have both guys and gals falling heels over you since you make quite a sexy spidergirl. Great bonus from that suit making you go from guy to girl "

    You now started to feel flustered at being flattered by her. Suddenly you remember you still need to buy the suit. "Say uh how much does this cost?" you inquire the cashier.

    "Oh today's the anniversary of this costume store being opened so a few chosen suits are free." She said. "So if you can give me the suit I'll see if it's among the free suits to have."

    "Uh yeah about that." You muttered also remembering another important factor. "I'm actually wearing the suit underneath my clothes. It just feels so good I couldn't fight against wearing it underneath."

    Rather then be annoyed the cashier looked amused. "Figured that'd be the case. I've seen this happened often with other folk. No worries just get your clothes off and I'll see it."

    You've wasted no time stripping your clothes off to only your spidergirl suit remaining. Your heart beated as the cashier inspected your suit scanning it for whatever it is to let her know that your suit is free. Finally she got up and nodded.

    "Yep your suit is free." She replied as she sat on the counter. "Should we get in touch more? There's something about you I like."

    "S-sure!" You said with a nod. "I feel likewise with you."

    "Thanks." She replied smiling once more. "My name's Lily Lang"

    After you told her your name and exchanged phone numbers and email addresses you said bye to her and walked out of the store feeling confidant about the new life you'll have as Spider-Girl.
