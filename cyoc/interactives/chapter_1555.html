    chance of that. No one would throw away a winning ticket. Even though you don't pay much attention to the lottery, you do happen to remember last night's

    A few years ago, people once quietly passed along tales of a place called The Body Shop. Rumored to be in Butte, Montana of all places, it was said that for
    several million dollars, they could redesign your body into anything you wanted. Male or female--any size, shape or color. Even exotic changes from the unusual to
    the truly bizarre could be easily accomplished. Most people dismissed it as an urban legend. And the few who did believe it seldom had the six to nine million it
    would have required, even if it was true.



    Then over time the rumors and stories died away. The only Body Shop that could be found in Butte was at the mall, selling fancy soap and candles. If there ever
    was a Body Shop and a Dr. Hashimoto, they weren't around anymore.



    But in the last few months, the whispers started again, this time about a place called The Newshape Shop. It's said that for a price of ten million, they offer the body
    design of your dreams. All the arrangements are handled by assistants. None of the doctors have ever been seen, but a few who remember the old Body Shop
    stories do believe that Laurel Hashimoto has returned to run this new service.



    As before, few people actually believe the stories, and fewer still could afford it if it was real. But at a nondescript little office complex in an unsuspecting town, The
    Newshape Shop is very real and redesigning bodies every day. And this morning, their next client has arrived. You.



    Having won the megalottery with the ticket you found, you became a multi-millionaire overnight. And a few weeks later, remembering the rumors you've heard of
    the Newshape Shop, you decided to check it out. Why not? You can afford to do most anything now. To your surprise, you discovered it exists. You can have
    your body redesigned into almost any shape you desire.
