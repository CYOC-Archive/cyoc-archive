After tucking her back in, Rita left the room and closed the door silently, deciding to make a nice breakfast to welcome the girl. She was almost done cooking when Leah joined her in the kitchen, rubbing the sleep away from her eyes.
    "Morning, Leah. How'd you sleep?"
    "Mmmmm...I don't think I've slept that well in years. That bed is amazing!" The teen replied with a half-asleep smile. She looked to have changed into more teenage appropriate PJ's, with her bottoms straining to cover her bulky diaper. It crinkled as she sat, gaining a look of discomfort on her face.
    "That smells really nice."
    "Bacon, pancakes, and eggs. The classic breakfast!" Rita shouted in excitement. Leah returned the smile briefly before it shifted to a frown.
    "Not that I'm not grateful or anything, but...do I really need to wear these things all the time. None of my clothes fit over it without leaving a bulge."
