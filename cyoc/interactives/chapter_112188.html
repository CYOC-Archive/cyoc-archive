    What a Mix...

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by PapayaJam 6 years ago A O

    You pause for a moment, thinking. "Okay," you say. "I want to be half wolf and half fox. I also want big wings and feathers on my head... oh, and I want to have a silver coat and black markings. Uhm, I want to be of authority, but not alot. As for other species; a mix of animals + mythological animals. As for lifespan, I choose when I die, and if I involuntarily die, I have 3 more lives. Got it?"
    The voice pauses for a moment as if thinking. "Okay. As for civilization...?" It asks. "Hmmm... Modern, but uh, not TOO modern." You say. "Is that all?" The voice asks. "Yep." You reply.
    As soon as you finish, you are launched into a swirling vortex. You close your eyes and suddenly feel it stop. You open them and feel like you just got smacked. The landscape around you is practically seizure inducing, as if the sunlight were strobe lights.
    You rub your eyes and realize you're not using hands. You're using paws! You glance down at the rest of your body. You are excatly as you described to the voice.
    Just then it whispers into your ear. "You are Kiiaerie, and your species is called Leira. You are the queen's second hand, and the strongest warrior." It says. "Speak of the devil." You mutter. "What?" The voice asks. You shake your head. "Nothing." You say.
    You get up and wobble, not used to being on 4 legs. "Fly." The voice whispers. You suddenly remember how to fly. You spread your wings and take off. You don't know where you're going, but your wings seem to. Within minutes, you spiral down and land in front a huge castle.
    "Woah." You breathe. You walk inside the castle and your jaw drops. Before your eyes lies an impressive room, complete with a chandelier and two beautifully crafted thrones. And sitting on top the thrones are two magestically huge replicas of yourself. You instantly recognize them as Queen Shiika and King Koelgate.
    You respectfully bow and Shiika waves her paw at you. "You know you don't have to do that, Kiiaerie, right?" She says, dismissing you. You nod. You hear a loud thud and turn your head. You recognize him as your personal messenger, Klaw. You tilt your head at him.
    "Kiiaerie! There is an invasion of harpies in the North Sector! We need your help!"

    What do you do now?
