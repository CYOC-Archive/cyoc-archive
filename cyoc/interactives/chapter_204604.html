    Shara was the same.

    “That’s what you get for pranking us!!!” Shara added.

    “How did you even do that?” Colin asked.
    “I borrowed some stuff from Uncle Gus.” Jamie smiled.
    “Uncle Gus!” Colin laughed “Good ol’ Uncle Gus….”

    Colin just stood there awkwardly.

    “Wait a minute … Jamie Benson… Uncle Gus… Your Uncle is GUS BENSON?!” Lucy asked.
    “Yeah, you JUST watched the movie he used that projection in!” Wendy smirked.
    “Wait, when Loni followed that shadow chick into the old ranger station…” Lucy said.
    “That was me!” Shara smiled.
    “Wait, so you actually took your top off for that scene?!” Lucy shouted.
    “I thought those shadow girls looked familiar…” Colin said.
    “Relax, Gus didn’t peek at my chest!” Shara yelled.
    “I saved that for Colin.”
    Shara pulled up a selfie of her bare breasts hanging out with the caption “A little behind the scenes just for you, babe!” with the winking emoji.
    “And send!” Shara said as she hit enter.

    Colin’s phone didn’t ding or light up.
    “Did you forget to charge your phone again?” Sam asked.
    “It’s at seventy percent…” Colin said.
    Shara proceeded to scream louder than when Lucy thought she was being attacked by shadow monsters.
    “What was that for?!” Wendy asked.
    Shara let out a pterodactyl screech as she held her phone up.
    In the name slot above her topless pic?
    One word.
    MOM.

    “Holy shit!” Wendy said as she chuckled into her hands.
    “Delete! Delete!” Shara frantically said as her fingers fumbled.
    “Wait wait wait, that’s not delete, that’s FaceTime!” Sam shouted.
    Too late, the camera opened as a tanned blonde popped up on screen.
    “Uhhhh… hiiiii mooooom!” Shara smiled with a nervous chuckle.
    “Who is it?” her mother asked.
    “Who’s… who?” Shara sweated.
    “You know… ‘babe’.” Shara’s mother replied with a furled brow.
    “And who are they?”
    “Some new friends of mine…” Shara chuckled.
    “I can explain!” Jamie interjected, “It’s like, not what you think Mrs Shara’s Mom!”
    “I think I can help you understand,” Sam said, “Mrs…”
    “Iris.” The tan blonde said.
    “Iris… you ever watch Valley of the Shadows?” Sam asked.
    “No I haven’t.” Iris said, “Please… enlighten me.”

    It took Shara explaining what found footage horror was, a plot rundown of Valley of the Shadows 2, and a demonstration of Gus’ projector to calm her mother down.
    “Alright… so who was gonna get that topless photo of my daughter?” Iris asked.
    “Oh my god, Mom, I’m 23!” Shara blushed.
    “It was me, Ma’am.” Colin said as he awkwardly walked into frame.
    “Colin, C-Colin Greys…”

    “I didn’t ask for that pic! I didn’t even know she was gonna be in that movie!” Colin said absolutely flustered, thinking about how this was the WORST way to meet your girlfriend’s mother.
