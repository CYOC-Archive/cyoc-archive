    Joey's inner monkey

    (First I must give some credit to ShadowDan because I'm using the name of the Anthro-drug from his story, some info based on other
    chapters by ShadowDan)

    Joey is almost 10. He was born on February 9th, 2050. Today is February 8th, 2060, his birthday is tomorrow. He always wanted to be
    a Furry, not the kind from the old days that would dress up and look weird, but an actual Furry. His parents are both human, but fully
    excepting of Anthros in general, especially because breathing in helium will reverse the effects, so it's not permanent. They tried to talk
    him out of it, not because they don't want him to, but because they don't want him to face prejudice because of it. This was America,
    Furries didn't face too much here, especially with the current president being a Furry himself. He pushes for more Furry-rights Acts.
    They usually want you to be over 18 before you take the drug, but there is no law against it, the law is you must be at least 10, which
    Joey will be tomorrow.

    The next morning he woke up and went downstairs for breakfast.
    "Happy Birthday!" his parents said when he came downstairs.
    "Thanks guys" Joey said. He opened his first present.
    "Oh my God!" Joey shouted because in the box was a bottle of Furcotin, the furry-drug made by VexCo.
    "well son, you are 10 now so it's legal." Mom said. Joey read the bottle and only half of it was needed, so he asked if he could give the
    rest to the family dog, Alex. His parents told him it was fine, their dog was only 1 in human years, so he should be about 7 in Anthro
    form. Joey took half and gave the other half to Alex.

    Joey's blond hair turned brown and brown fur covered his body. A tail popes out of his backside, his feet quickly took on the shape of
    hands. His ears stuck out a little. He was now an Anthro-monkey. Meanwhile, the dog Alex quickly grew thumbs and his muzzle got
    smaller. His vocal cords re-arranged so that he could talk and his brain got bigger so he could think like a human. Alex's age was
    starting to speed up until he was 7 human years old.
    "What the heck?" Alex said. Joey let out a monkey-like laugh and hugged Alex.

    Joey and Alex were registered as Furries. Alex stayed with them and was registered in school. He quickly became like a little brother for
    Joey. Joey and Alex got special Furry clothes with tail holes and made out of material that wouldn't bother their fur. Joey was allowed
    out of school for one week to get used to his Anthro form. Joey has been wearing his hair in a wild fashion because he likes it better
    that way now. Now it's time to go back and Joey can't help but wonder what his friends will think.

    What do you do now?
