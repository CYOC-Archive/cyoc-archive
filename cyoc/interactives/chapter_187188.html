    The girl had sweet, baby-blue eyes that radiated innocence and a head of pale golden curls just like Krystal wanted. She was short, ivory-skinned, and had almost no bust to speak of. She giggled girlishly at the sight of Krys.

    "Hello!" she chirped in a bubbly soprano that Krystal would have murdered for. "I love your tattoos. They're so pretty! I do so hope mine turn out half as nice."

    "Tats, huh?" Krystal laughed. "Fucking sweet! Wouldn't have ever pegged a cutie like you for the type, though."

    The cute girl giggled again. "I'm not. At least, not yet. I'm waiting for Morgan to give me the tomboy package."

    "Name's Krystal Brock, but you can call me Krys if you like. I'm here to get as girly and innocent as you are now."

    The girly girl giggled. "How funny! I'm Mary Jane Merriweather, but I think I'll change it when I'm done."

    "So," asked Krystal, "Why ya wanna be a tomboy?"

    Mary Jane sighed. "I never get to have any fun like this! All my friends get to party, but I'm too scared! I always wanted to take up smoking, and my friend Lexi offered to teach me how. I turned her down, Krys! I still don't know why. And then there's the worst part... I'm still a virgin! How old do you think I am, Krys?"

    Krystal shrugged. "I dunno. Twelve, I guess?"

    Mary Jane shook her pretty head. "I'm twenty! You see the problem? None of this feels right! It's like I'm being forced to play a role!"

    Krystal sighed. "Yeah. That feels shitty."

    Mary Jane instantly perked up and burst into peals of musical laughter. "I love it when you use naughty words! I'm never able to choke them out, myself."

    "No shit?" asked Krystal. "Say 'fuck.'"

    Mary Jane scrunched up her face in concentration. "F... fu... ff... fuuu... frick." Mary Jane sighed in defeat.

    "Okaaay..." Krystal responded. "Let's try something simple. Say 'crap.'"

    Mary Jane tried again. "Cr... cra... crud."

    Krystal raised a pierced eyebrow. "Wow, Jesus! You really can't swear?"

    Mary Jane shook her head sadly, causing her ornate curls to wave.

    "But you want to?" Krystal added. Mary Jane nodded

    "Fuck," said Krystal. "You need this as much as I do."

    Mary Jane nodded resolutely. "I absolutely can't wait, Krys!"
