    Ty decided that since Paul was the smallest and the shortest of the three boys, he would shrink Noah down some
    and transfer his height and muscles to Paul! That way, both boys would end up being about the same size again,
    without being giants!

    "Noah!" Ty said to him. "Quit reading! Look at me!"

    Noah put his book down and stood up, very slowly, as he realized that everything seemed a LOT smaller than
    before.

    "Whoa! Everything and everybody has shrunk!" Noah sighed as he looked around the room.

    "Not really... YOU have gotten bigger... a LOT bigger... A little TOO BIG... And it's my fault and now I gotta
    fix it!" Ty explained. "Stand next to Paul for a sec..."

    The giant boy stood next to Paul, who seemed to barely reach his waist.

    Then, Noah began to shrink in size and muscle while Paul began to grow! To make sure that nobody was butt
    naked, Ty made their clothes shrink or grow along with the rest of their bodies!

    Noah slowly dwindled down, with his arms shrinking and his legs becoming shorter. His head, his hands and his
    feet all shrunk down a bit as the giant boy slowly shrunk from 8 ft 9" to 6 ft 9", losing 2 feet in height. At
    the same time, he lost his muscles but he still was muscular for his size when the shrinking stopped.

    Paul, on the other hand, started to GROW! He shot UP and grew by two feet in height! In a matter of moments,
    Paul was now standing almost 7 feet tall. His skinny frame was now bulging with all the muscles that Noah had
    just a few moments ago! He was massive! Paul was now just a few inches taller than Noah!

    "There! You guys are about the same height!" Ty grinned, looking at the two giant boys. "Now, things are
    even!"
