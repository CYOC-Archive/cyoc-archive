    Janice walked to her bedroom, and started to get ready for work. She got dressed, grabbed her things, and headed to the institute.
    As Janice left her house and walked to the institute, she was met with stares from those around her. "How odd...maybe my skull looks worse than I think it does?" Janice thought to herself. As she continued to walk to work, she noticed that she was walking slower than normal.
    Janice arrived at the Institute and saw all eyes on her. "What's so fascinating about me?" Janice thought as she made her way to the elevator. She got inside and pressed the button for her boss' floor. As the doors closed, Janice could see the reflection of herself on the doors. "My head has grown bigger...how strange."
    Janice arrived on her boss' floor, and walked to his office. She knocked on the door, and heard her boss say "Come in!"
    She entered the office to see her boss standing by his desk, looking at her. "Janice! What a surprise to see you here today, I thought you called off."

    Janice was confused before she quickly replied. "You know that Superhuman Experiment I was asked to make?. Well this is the result."
    "That's your brain?" her boss exclaimed.
    "Yes. I figured out how to enhance the brain's cells. By combining the correct formula with a certain hormone in the body, the brain cells will grow and join together, making a person smarter." Janice explained.
    Her boss was shocked. He told Janice "You will not have to worry about that Superhuman Experiment anymore. You can go home."
    As Janice went past her lab she turned on her computer and searched through the database to find the research files for the Super Human Experiment. She found the folder and copied it to a flash drive. "Just in case." Janice thought as she put the flash drive in her bag.
    As Janice left the office, she felt another headache starting. She left the institute and decided to head to go back home.
    While walking back, Janice bumped into her neighbor, Mrs. Johnson. Mrs. Johnson asked, "Janice, dear, are you ok? You look like you're in pain."
    Janice replied, "Oh, I'm fine, Mrs. Johnson. I just have a bit of a headache. I'm going to take some medicine and lay down."
    Mrs. Johnson then said, "Oh, ok. If you need anything, let me know."
    As Janice arrived home, she went to the bathroom to inspect her head. She took off her clothes and stared into the mirror. Her skull was now twice the size as it was this morning. "This is strange." she said to herself.
    Janice went to her bedroom and laid down on the bed and went to sleep.
