    "You'll be pigs. It's a bit sad with two girls as lovely as you are. But the customer specifically asked for two real beauties, so that's where you're heading." With that he turned to the next girls, leaving them to their dull thoughts. Neither of them was able to say anything. Pig. That could only mean they were to be eaten. No other purpose was conceivable. Finally, Vicky found her voice: "At least we'll stay together." "Yes. But I'm really afraid.", Leonie replied. "Me, too." Than there was silence again until two new men came. They carried syringes and injected the girls in tehir sighs, according to the masters orders. All around girls began to change in what was not much more than an instant. Leonie felt almost nothing before she found herself in a pinkish pig body, next to her Vicky, equally pink but with a few dark plotches, reflecting her previous dark hair. Around them they saw anything from cats and dogs to cows and horses, also some freakish creatures half-human, half-animal. Then four men came, strapping them down one by one and putting them into cages of different size. Leonie and Vicky found themselves side by side, wheeled away in their cages to an unkown future. They struggled to keep on their pig legs and to adjust to their new field of vision. "Oh good.", the master commented when they passed him. "Bring them to the waiting room, the customer will come to collect them himself." With that, they were wheeled away and their cages finally rested in a room with comfortable looking seats, wooden panels and the aura of a very expensive hotel. Now they stood there. Two swine, sold for profit, nothing to expect but premature death.

    Then the door opened and an elderly man was lead in by the master. This had to be the buyer. He pointed at the two pigs, asking briskly: "Is that the stock?" Before the master could more but nod, the man was at their cages. He felt Leonie's ass and shoulders. When she tried to wriggle away, he just admonished her: "Stay calm, piggie. I'm just checking your fat to see whether you suit my needs." When he was satisfied, he turned to Vicky, before he focussed on the master. OK, I'll take them. Payment as usual and now please have them loaded."
