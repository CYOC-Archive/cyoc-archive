She looked down at the key ring and saw another key, with a sticker on it, that pointed her to the top floor. She gently pulled her legs inward, and found that she could stand with them at almost the normal angle for a human being, but unfortunately the mismatched sizes of her legs from front to back tilted her forward toward the door a little.

What would she say to explain the way she looked? could she afford to keep this apartment?
