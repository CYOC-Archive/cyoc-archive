    When June woke up she tried to get Dustin up, but she refused to get up so June left the room and let Dustin sleep. June found the St. Beloved had lifted off already. She left to have breakfast, watched some videos on her phone, and then Flint concerned asked for June to bring her "new little sister".

    Dustin had to admit that she didn't feel too homesick. Her old home was a pile of junk after all. However she did feel upset over feeling like she was kinda forced to come, and she really didn't like the pink frilly dress June shoved her in. Oh yeah Dustin was also a girl now. There were three faces at the dining table Captain Flint, June, and a guy in his twenties. Flint said "Well you know me and June, this man over here is Carl. We all kind of run this ship and explore the universe together. Being away from your home planet for the first time can lead to home sickness, but I thought you would be more excited about this. Is there anything wrong?"
    Dustin sighed and said "I mean I did want more freedom and to be away from the threat of being found and being turned into a prostitute." her hands going to her chest which was still smooth and continued "However... I kinda wish I could have brought or said by to my mom. She probably thinks I was abducted by a poacher."
    June was getting a little teary eyed and said "I kind of know the feeling like when my mom died when I was small."
    Carl nodded and said "She was a good woman, but we'll see each other again when we die."
    Dustin was looking confused and so Flint said "Well Dustin you see I started doing this space adventure by myself with the blessing of Ty, and then I met my wife who hadn't died yet. We explored together for awhile until we found Carl and adopted him, and then we had June as our biological daughter... However my wife died early in June's life. So we all here run the ship as a family till they die. Did that help any confusion?"
    Dustin asked "What's die?"
    The family thought for a second and then Flint said "I see... I guess you and most people on your planet were immortal. When your body deteriorates or takes too much damage usually it'll fail and then you are sent to heaven where you are given the blessing of Ty. On my ship though you are not allowed to try to kill yourself."
    Dustin was kind of getting it and said "So there is something... We can't get hurt but when we get hungry and can't eat we just... Stop until an Angel comes to fix them, but since we tried to stay away from Angels."
    Flint said "Well then, explore with us, die at some point, and then when you have the Blessing of Ty as an angel you can go back to the market and save everyone." Dustin nodded and then Flint continued "As you've just joined us I'll get you a list of planets and you can decide the next one we'll explore. Take your time."
