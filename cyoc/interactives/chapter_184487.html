    Suzan the unknowing dragon

    During the initial pulse a rich couple both from wealthy families ended up getting exposed to a little too much magic and morphed into their monstrous forms. Thankfully at the time they were outside so their new large bodies didn't break whatever. They were about the size of trucks so they had to change their residence to a small warehouse, and got special furniture that they both easily were able to afford. Thankfully for both of them they made most of their money by managing properties which they could do from the internet. There was something to be said about rich people being dragons, but what inevitably happened was that they had a kid together. An egg was laid, and no expenses were held to make sure it would be healthy. The egg was about as big as a small puppy, and was treated like a giant diamond their parents so protective of their little egg that they may have had to pay off some corrupt cops to say the charred corpse of a thief was a suicide.
    During the day of the hatching the mother dragon had one of her closest friends around. A human woman named Shawna. They trusted her enough to not burn her alive for being within a few yards of their egg which was good all around as when the egg started to hatch it had started wobbling a bit more than had been expected. The egg fell off it's podium, but Shawna was fast enough to catch the egg. It wasn't quite necessary as the little dragon inside the egg was fully formed and was just trying to get out. The impact of the catch and fall however made it easy for a small little dragon the size of a chihuahua to hatch it's way out. The little thing had blue scales like it's mother, and the first thing it was seeing was Shawna. The young dragon's parents watched being so big and strong they didn't dare try touching the relatively fragile little life. It seemed happy enough, and to their astonishment the young dragon fully attuned to her species abilities seemed to shift a little. As it stared at the human it liked who was nice and warm she tried to take on some of the traits of the woman. Everyone was astonished as the little dragon had shifted into what looked like a baby girl. Her dragon parents did some quick telepathy talking and then offered Shawna a deal. They'd give Shawna a decent amount of money for raising their child as their own. The dragons figuring that being raised up as a human would serve her better.

    Through the years the young dragon now known as Suzan would grow up like a normal girl... Kinda. Her growth was kind of rapid as she grew into a toddler in a month as she was trying to imitate the characters on TV she was watching. Afterwards as she had more interactions with humans she would subconsciously mirror the growth that her friend Megan went through. As this was reactionary she ended up being smaller than her friend most of the time. With how much she emulated and was surrounded by mostly humans Suzan would forget that she was actually a dragon. Shawna would occasionally send pictures and video to Suzan's actual parents. Those actual parents scared that they would scare Suzan as they had grown larger with age, and the two would work on trying to copy the shapeshifting magic that Suzan seemed to have an aptitude for.

    Suzan would end up being known for her talent with numbers, and how much of a hoarder she could end up being along with how possessive of her friend Megan she was. However soon her life would start to change.
