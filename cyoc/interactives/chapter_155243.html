    The mermaid looks up. "Oh! Hi! … Do I know you?"

    "No," says Anna, "no, you don't. I'm new here."

    "New?"

    "I was human a little while ago. I just got turned."

    Clia brightens up. "A human girl, turned into a mermaid!? Oh, that's so rare—you have to tell me all about it! Who changed you? How did it happen? I'm Clia, by the way—"

    "Anna," says Anna with a nervous laugh. "And, yeah, I kind of already knew who you were, because the merman who changed me—is Eric."

    Clia's expression darkens. "Eric? Hmph. He's so pushy, he—wait. He didn't *force* the change, on you did he? Because if that's what happened, I'll—"

    "No!" says Anna, throwing up her hands. "No, not at all! He didn't know that I'd change, and it happened by accident! But I'm happy that it did! I want to be a mermaid, and I want to be with him!"

    Clia pauses, considering. "Oh. Oh; well, in that case, I guess it's good that he finally found someone. Now maybe he'll leave me and my mate alone."

    "I don't think he'll just give up," says Anna. "He wants to kill Aaron."

    "Let him try," says Clia. "If he comes after us again, I'll give him more than a little bump on the head."

    Anna doesn't know quite what to say to that, so she pleads, "Don't hurt him! I'll try and keep Eric away from you two as best I can, but I need him. He's my mate."

    "Already!?" says Clia. "Wow. If you don't mind my asking, what do you see in him?"

    "Let's just say that I'm happy he's mine and I'm his, and leave it at that?"

    "Suit yourself." Clia looks Anna up and down, assessing her. "You must have a million questions. Aaron certainly did after I turned him."

    "Well that's actually why I'm here," says Anna. "I was hoping you could give me a little information, especially about this mating cycle thing. Since it is my first day as a mermaid, after all."

    Clia grins. "We could be here all day, if you want to know *everything*. How much time do you have?"

    "Not much. I need to get back to my nest to guard my eggs?"

    Clia's eyes go wide. "You and Eric have already *fertilized*!? Honey, you work faster than any mermaid I've ever met!"

    "Just a little good old-fashioned human gumption," says Anna with a laugh. "But I guess you already have some idea of what that's like."

    "Don't I ever," says Clia. "You land-mammals are just so sex-crazed! It's going to take some time for you and Arron both to adjust. Especially once the cycle ends and you can't do it anymore."

    Anna's voice becomes an emotionless deadpan. "Wait. What?"

    "Yup. After about a week, your sex drive is gonna go 'poof' and you won't be able to feel it again until the next full moon gets near."

    "Baby, we make a hundred eggs every month. If they don't hatch this time, there's always next cycle." Clia then kisses Anna, and Anna freezes.

But, what, now that she's a mermaid, she's no longer 100% straight? Is that how that worked? Or was she just being affected by the sight of all these horny mermaids playing with each other's tits around here, and going at each other like there's no tomorrow?
