    Feel the cleats merge

    added 13 years ago BM S

    Your friend's football cleats were sitting out in the locker room with his name and team
    number written on them, so you grab them and head back into the shower room. While
    headed back, you decide to grab yours from the locker as well.

    First, you loosen the laces of the cleats and then slide them onto his feet over the latex.
    Once you get the second one on him, the latex senses it and you can tell it's trying to do
    something inside them. You then sit down next to your friend and slip on your own cleats
    and tie the laces tight.

    There's an incredible pressure building up inside your cleats now, you can tell it's the same
    thing happening to your friend. It's almost like the latex on the both of you is linked. You
    both are on the team, unable to be separated.

    The pressure almost feels like it's going to break your foot when you notice the color the
    cleats is changing to the same bright blue color on the rest of your body. As the blue
    takes over the shoe, you can tell the Nike swooshes on yours is changing to purple and
    the Adidas three stripes on your friend's cleats are turning purple as well. At the same
    time, both of you feel the laces of your cleats disappear as the latex merges the tops of
    them together making them impossible to remove.

    You now begin to feel a weird sensation on the bottom of your feet. You can tell your
    friend feeling the same thing in his sleep as he grimaces a little. Then it hits you both. It
    feels like the metal spikes on the cleats are shooting up into you and your friend's feet.
    One at a time. It makes you want to scream as each spike extends itself into your body.
    As the spikes on the heel begin to extend, you can feel something extra down there. Like
    the spikes are an extension of your body. "Woah, I can feel the ground like I never have
    been able to before..." you say out loud to yourself.

    Now that the cleats have finished merging with you and your friend's bodies, your friend's
    latex finishes it's changes. Purple stripes begin to form just like your uniform up over the
    shoulders, with accents down the sides of his legs. Numbers begin to form as well, leaving
    behind number 23 boldly printed in purple on his chest and back.
