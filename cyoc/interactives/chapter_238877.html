As they were soon lead to a annex building Jennifer gasped at what she realized, she was one of those students, the kind that were held back for multiple years and needed remediation. Jennifer looked around, she was seven, which would normally place her in her second grade, but the material felt way below that.

Pulling out a large wide pencil she tried to write her name, only to lose all of her self esteem as her hand struggled to even form a clear J.
