    "....and then he proceeded to ask me to change his diaper." Selina was telling Felicia, an old friend of hers who was also a burglar cat like her, everything that had happened to her.

    "Ewwww, and you really did it?" Felicia asked Selina.

    "Of course not! I totally refused to do it. Luckily one of his butlers was the one who changed his diaper." Selina replied to Felicia

    "Oof, you were very lucky. And how did you spend the rest of the day in his mansion? Was it normal or was it just as horrible as that?"

    "Well, the rest was pretty normal. Yes, there were times when he was annoying but it wasn't all the time. And to be honest, it's kind of fun to play like a toddler and the food wasn't bad at all. The only thing I really didn't like was when I had to potty in my diaper. Not only did it leave a horrible smell, but the diaper changing part was also uncomfortable. But that was the only thing that bothered me, the rest wasn't that bad."

    "Wow, and how was your last day there?"

    "Well, the truth was quite normal. In the morning he already had the antidote so he proceeded to give it to me. After returning to normal, he gave me the briefcase that had all the money but before I could leave he wanted to give me this."

    At that moment, Selina proceeded to take something out of a shoebox that was under her bed.

    "And what is that supposed to be?" Felicia asked Selina.

    "It's a bottle with pills inside similar to the ones I had stolen. He told me he gave them to me as a little souvenir."

    "That's great. But do they work?" Felicia asked.

    "I don't know that yet. That's why I called you here. I was wondering if you wanted to try them out with me?" Selina asked Felicia curiously.
