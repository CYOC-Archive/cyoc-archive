    “You better hurry, or do you want something about you transformed,” Rick asks.

    Kevin stays silent, but the others notice the teen’s fill with fear, “it… it’s to embarrassing,” he finally mumbles.

    “Zero! Time’s up,” the voice chimes in, “sorry, Kevin, but rules are rules! As punishment for not answering the question, something about you will now change!”

    Kevin tenses, first nothing seems to happen, but then he feels a weird tingle run through his body, causing him to shiver, “that felt weird,” he mumbles.

    “What did,” the others ask.

    “I felt a tingle, almost like pins and needles, run through my body,” Kevin replies.

    “Usually that indicates that a change is about to happen,” Rick says, “do you or your body feel any different than before?”

    Kevin frowns, “not really,” he says, then his eyes widen, “wait! I do feel something different!”

    “What is it,” the others ask curiously.

    Kevin grimaces, “it’s… my tailbone,” he says, “I… I can feel it… growing! It… it’s pushing again my skin,” the 15-year-old then winces, “now it’s… pushing against… the waistband of my jeans! I… I think… I think I am growing a… t-tail!”

    “Whoa,” the others say, “you DO have a tail!”

    “Can… are you able to… you know… control it,” Rick asks.

    Kevin, staring in disbelief at his tail, “um… let… let me try,” he says, letting his tail flop out of his hand. The teen then concentrates and all eyes widen when the tail suddenly begins to wag back and forth, “whoa! That feels… weird,” Kevin exclaims, “I wonder as what animal I’ll end up as.
