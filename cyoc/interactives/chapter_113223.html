    Started to think about little boy clothes. I made sure the ring was on and started thinking.

    I got dizzy again and I blinked a few times.

    In the closet, all my original clothes were not there, but little boy clothes were.

    It was getting late so I looked for some pjs and I found a Thomas the Train footy pajamas. I put them on and then i went to sleep.

    I woke up wet in my bed, so I stripped from my clothes and started a shower.

    I found some little boy toys and objects in the shower, so I just had a bubble bath.

    When I got out, I dried myself and then I looked back in the closet. That is when I found something weird.

    There was a box of pull ups.

    When I looked even harder. I found every single one of my clothes and pull ups Thomas the Train themed.

    I have always wondered what a pull up felt like again, so I put one on.

    It felt a little weird, so I just grabbed a shirt and went on with my day.

    I opened the door and there was my parents

    They didn't notice me, but I noticed them. They looked 10 years younger. Back when I was 5 years old.

    I tried to turn back into my room, but they heard my footsteps.

    "Hey sport, whatcha doin" my dad said.

    "Honey I am about to go to the store to get something to eat, or would you like me to make pancakes." my mother said.

    "Um, may I have pancakes please" I said

    "Wow someone is well mannered, just for that, they will be chocolate chip with whip cream and spinkles" my mother said.

    "Thank you mom" I said. I then gave her a hug, and she did the same.

    Later that morning, while I was eating. My brother came downstairs. I forgot how old he was. He looked to be 17 or 18 years old. That is when my brother and dad started to talk

    "So when are you moving out son" my dad said.

    "I don't know, maybe when you guys give me money for a house and car" he said.

    "I GAVE YOU A HUGE LOAN AND WHAT DID YOU SPEND IT ON, DRUGS THAT IS WHAT!" my dad yelled.

    "Come on sweety let's go somewhere else, you can bring your pancakes." my mother said.

    She took me to the living room, but the screaming wasn't far behind.

    "Mommy, what are we doing today?" I asked.

    "Well, your father wants to go to a special place for his birthday. Maybe when we get there, you can give him your gift" she said.

    Right then, my brother slammed the door open and started to walk out of the house.

    "So you won't help me, but you will help the brat with potty training and home school on his schedule. He is already 5 and he doesn't know how to use a toilet. I am going some where I am welcomed." he said right before he slammed the door.

    "That is ok sweety, he didn't mean it" my mom said.

    "Yea he is just mad so he started to tell lies." my dad said.

    I finished my pancakes and I felt the need for the bathroom.

    "Can I go to the bathroom please?" I said.

    "Are you going to use the potty?" my dad asked.

    I shook my head in response and he got excited.

    "Janice get the camera, Danny is going to use the potty!" he said.

    I opened the door to the bathroom and I climed up the toilet seat and sat there. I saw my parents waiting for me to use it and record it. I pushed my poop out and got off the toilet. I wiped my but clean and pulled up my pull ups. My parents screamed in joy. They started to jump so I joined in.

    "Looks like the big boy doesn't need little boy pants does he" my dad said.

    I was confused till I found out what he ment.

    "Yea where are my big boy pants?" I asked.

    My father reached in the closet and got out some more Thomas the Train clothing. He found some underwear that were fluffy in the crotch area. They looked familiar. I remembered what they were. They were training underwear. They were for kids who just used the potty, I mean toilet. They have the same material of diapers to hold pee but not poop. I pulled it my pull up and I lifted one leg for my dad to put the training underwear on me, then I lifted the other.

    I looked down and I saw how puffy it was. That is when I noticed something, the ring was gone. I waited for my parents to leave the room, then I looked high and low for the ring. It was my only way back to my normal age. I saw a counter table and look on it. There was no ring but a necklace. It was golden with a sapphire amulet on it. The gold had covered it on the back, but it held the sapphire with small golden claws. I sighed in releaf.

    "Honey get changed, and bring daddy's present with you." my mom said.

    I looked for something that wasn't Thomas themed, till I found a dinosaur shirt. I put it on and then found some pants. I tried to look for a jacket but they were all Thomas themed.

    "Mommy what jacket do you want me to wear?" I asked.

    She came into my room.

    "Oh my you even got yourself dressed, you must be becoming a big boy." she said.

    She looked in the closet and picked out a jacket that looked like it was made for a hipster.

    She led me to the car outside and she buckeled me up in a car seat. I looked at the necklace wondering why it turned into it from a ring.
