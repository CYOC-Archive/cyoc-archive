She quickly went into her closet, reaching for the loose panel where she had hidden the Amulet and book before, only to find to her dismay that they were missing. That explained how Taylor was now her older sister. But it didn't explain how she had found it. Nor did it explain why she made herself an older girl when she could have changed back. Did Taylor like being a girl? That certainly wasn't supposed to happen. Holly began to think to herself how she could get the Amulet back...and what she could do to fix this huge mistake.
