    The more Jeff thinks of what to try out first, the more frustrated he gets, “why is it so fucking hard,” he asks himself. After a while, Jeff lets out a frustrated sigh, “I think I’ll go for a walk,” he says, “I might get some ideas”. Jeff gets up from his computer, he is the only one home so he doesn’t bother to turn off his computer, he even leaves the program running, “I’ll be back in an hour anyway,” he says.

    Jeff has been gone for 10 minutes, when the door to his room suddenly opens and his 10-year-old brother “Jared” enters. “Jeff, may I use…,” the boy starts, then realizes that his brother is not there, Jared looks around the room and notices Jeff’s computer is running. The boy shrugs, “he won’t notice that I used his computer for a few minutes,” with that Jared sits down in front of the computer. “Hm… what’s this,” he asks himself, “looks like Jeff got himself a new game”. As Jared looks over the screen he sees that the screen shows his brother’s name, age, gender, intelligence and lots of other stuff… even dark secrets and desires. “What a weird game,” the young boy mumbles, he then opens the manual and starts reading, his eyes growing wider and wider. “So, apparently this program is able to transform people,” he mutters to himself, “that impossible,” but the boy’s curiosity gets the better of him. “I always wanted a pet dog,” he says, “I am tired of Jeff bossing me around. He’ll be better as a dog, and I’LL be HIS boss”. The small boy grins evilly, “first, I’ll make him act like a dog, he’ll still think of himself as a human though, but he’ll obey every command, sniff the ground and do doggy stuff, he’ll be aware of what is going on, but he’ll be confused”. Jared starts typing, “I’ll increase his sense of smell and his hearing to that of a dog. Now, I want my puppy to be eight weeks old, so let’s add a playful-puppy-mind to his normal mind. Next, I’ll alter the reality so that everyone, including mom and dad, will treat and see him as a dog. They’ll remember Jeff but will assume he’s away with friends, only I will know the truth,” smirking evilly, the boy confirms the settings. Jared then closes the program and shuts off Jeff’s computer, he then heads to his room, “I can’t wait for my “dog” to arrive,” he says with a grin.
