    Suzi had you backed into a corner praying for your life. One wrong move and you knew you could wind up a stain on the bottom of her heels or worse. "Listen Suzi I'm sure you don't know what that is and I just don't want you getting hu...." before you can even finish she starts cackling. "Ha, you don't know a damn thing about me do you Mike? What you just see a pair of tits and some blonde hair and assume I'm a bimbo huh?"
    You're taken aback. "No...no" She shoots you a glare and cuts you off again. "I'm a fucking graduate student. How do you think I got this position? I wrote half of the script that came along with this gun I'll have you know."
    You're completely wide eyed at this point. "I always though you were just a secretary! I'm sorry, I didn't know."
    She scoff at you. "Of course you did that little parasite Dr. Mark couldn't wipe his own ass, much less do all the paperwork involved in his position. Not that that ever stopped him from Stealing all the credit, and for what? so the little pervert could use the gun to shrink himself and peek in the ladies dorm hall?"
    It's beginning to dawn on you just how dire you situation has become. "I'm sorry Suzi you've obviously been gravely wronged and I sympathize with you, so please I won't tell anyone about the gun or any of this just let me go."
    Suzi's expression softens for a bit and for a moment you think you'll be okay. "Sigh... Listen Mike I like you, you're a good kid, but this isn't just about you anymore." Suzi's face and tone grow darker as you feel a pit form in your stomach. "There are people after this gun Mike, dangerous people, and if they find out you know anything about it, and believe me they will; they will hurt you to get to me. You're a loose end understand?"
    You want to try and make a break for it there but before you can even muster the strength to run Suzi does something unexpected. She's pointing the gun at her own head. "Wait Stop!" you cry but it's too late Suzi pulls the trigger and a flash of light envelops the room.
    When you're eyes readjust you see Suzi standing there unchanged. Had the gun not worked? wait no where was the gun Suzi's hands were empty! "What did you do? Where is the gun?"
    Suzi smirks clearly amused by your confusion. "Oh nothing, I just unlocked the true power of the gun, a feature not even Dr. Mark was aware of."
    "What are you talking about?" Something about Suzi seems off, her confidence maybe? but whatever it is leaves you a bit unsettled.
    "Here let me demonstrate" Suzi raises her hand towards you and in an instant the same flash of light from the gun bursts from her palm and envelops you. When the light fades you realize what she's done...
