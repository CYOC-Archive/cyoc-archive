    Ty's Power

    A new start... for untold debauchery

    Ty waited a few minutes in bed, feeling the afterglow sour with the new revelation. He knew that with a thought, he could be privy to whatever was going on downstairs, whatever thoughts were crossing Lisa's mind, but he knew better. He'd already done enough meddling with her mental state. Slowly, he stretched and sat up, and took his time putting his clothes back on, giving Lisa as much time as he could as he made his way downstairs.

    When he got to the bottom, he found Lisa sitting on the couch, chin resting on her upper pair of hands and a deep frown creasing her brow. Furious, yet pensive. Ty wondered what that meant for his chances of escaping this conversation unscathed would be. Sucking in a deep breath, he decided to bite the bullet and ask, "Hey, Lisa. How are you feeling?"

    "What do you think?" Lisa snapped back, almost immediately, then sighed. She turned her dark gaze on him. "Fuck. I'm sorry, I guess. It's just... a lot to process."

    "Do you want to talk to me about it?" Ty hedged, and made a move to sit down next to her, but was quickly waved off. She gestured sharply with her lower hands to the seat across from her. Ty peacefully did as she wished.

    Lisa sighed, and hung her head. "God, Ty, do you know what sort of situation you've put me in? On one hand, I'm furious! Holy shit, you made me fall in love with you! You messed with my mind and fucked up my body, and it's seriously messed up that you thought it was okay to do so."

    Ty nodded. "I know. I'm really sorry, Lisa. I never meant to hurt you, but I know I have. Please, tell me what I can do to make this better."

    "See, that's just it!" she wailed. "I hate what you did to me! I hate you! And yet..." her voice dropped to a whisper. "And yet, you made me the happiest I have been in a long time. This-" she gestured to her body with all four of her arms, "is amazing. So, I hate you, but I want that happiness. What's a girl to do?" she remarked dryly.

    Ty was at a loss for words. He helplessly gave a shrug. Lisa snorted, and rolled her eyes at his non-answer. "See what I mean?" She laughed, though the sound was humourless. "I want to go back to that bliss. I want it more than anything in the world."

    She heaved in a sigh, and leaned back against the couch, spreading all her arms palm-up. "So, I want you to make me in love with you."

    "What?" Ty exclaimed, startled.

    "I know, it's a stupid, impulsive decision. Trust me, I don't like the thought of being with you all that nice, either. But, I remember that happiness, that pleasure so clearly. It overtakes every thought. I know it's a stupid decision, but I want it anyway. You better make it happen before I come to my senses."

    "Lisa, are you sure?"

    She groaned. "No, I'm not sure! That's the point! Just make me fall in love with you, so I don't have to deal with this mess of a situation you've created. But, I do have some caveats."

    Ty nodded, easily accepting of anything she could say.

    "I want you to take it further. I don't want to remember a time when I wasn't in love with you. I don't want you to remember you making me fall in love with you. I want the whole world to think that was always the way."

    Ty was prepared for anything, but that.

    "And, one more thing," Lisa said, voice turning quiet. "I want to fall so heavily into that bliss that I will be overjoyed at anything you do to me. Absolutely anything. You could make me an inanimate object, treat me like shit, and I'd enjoy every second of it. I don't ever want to feel anything but bliss."

    "Lisa, you understand how dangerous this is," Ty warned. "If I do this, you wouldn't be able to stop me from doing any number of horrendous things to you. You could seriously get hurt!"

    She laughed, jarringly loud and harsh. "Who cares! What's the point of worrying about shit like that? There's nothing left for the me who remembers, just a sad life trying to forget what you did. Why not just go all the way? Make me forget, and never let me feel pain again." Her gaze turned to Ty, wicked-sharp. "If you've got such a problem with that, why not throw your own scruples out too? Fuck it. Go the whole mile."

    Ty took a deep breath. Then another. "Are you sure?" he asked again. Lisa rolled her eyes.

    "Oh, for god's sake, just do it, or don't. Don't keep umm-ing and ahh-ing over it."

    "Fine. Okay. Sure." He took another deep, deep breath. "Let's do it."

    Concentrating hard, he focused on the wish in his mind. I wish that Lisa was completely and utterly in love with me. No one on this planet, including me, has ever known this to be otherwise. Lisa is fine with whatever is done to her, no matter how extreme. She will feel bliss no matter what. And I... he paused, biting his lip, so hesitant to cross the line. But, Lisa had said... I will have no moral, or scruples, and will never feel bad about what I do to her. He let the wish build, and grow, pushing his power out until his mind went white. It's done, he thought, and then the magic crashed over him.
