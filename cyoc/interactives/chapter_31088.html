    Down the bridle path

    Urges, need, and the sensation of something then grown so large as it begged for gaining its freedom; Josh stands there gawking at what is a real halter, reins, and what for him seems so righteous and proper.

    Letting fall his pants to the straw strewn floor, Josh reaches slowly to take a hold of the halter. Fingers tell of its strength and coarse texture, but again it lures him to try it on, giving him the first inkling he really wants to become as a male donkey.

    As the very first fibers of the halter come into contact with a face that Josh and his mother both loved; things began which, given due time, would become permanent.

    "You about to head down the bridle path too?" A gruff tone of voice asks a question, one poised at all in the rest room, whether those who were changing, or those stuck inside a stall and having changed so much, they cannot get out.

    Josh opens the door to his stall, stares at a goofy face, one with buck teeth and a jackass head. It eyes him as he does it, both seeing the other having changed enough to warrant the use of a halter, bit, and bridle to hold some brute beast in check and available for doing some burden of work.

    Just then, in walks Jesse, he stops when seeing what was still partially dressed to resemble Josh. He calls to his friend and gets in return a ragged bray that comes with such force, that the sound of it scares Josh to spray a squirt.

    Learned moral teaching has Josh grabbing at his elongated tool, trying to quell the squirting, and return the thing back into its sheath and home.

    The one Jackass youth begins to laugh at what Josh does to keep his maleness in check.

    Jesse affords a slight grin, but with trying to smile at what he sees, his own realization of change and self comes to roost. Feeling of his changing face, Jesse knows that he and Josh are soon to stand in a barn, in stalls, and live, work, and as well exist as jack donkey types for God only knows how long.

    Feeling suddenly quite faint, Jesse staggers a few steps; and from his left side the happy jackass gives the still changing young male into a stall and slams the door shut!
