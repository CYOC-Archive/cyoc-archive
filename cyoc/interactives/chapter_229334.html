    Jeff "Transfers" The "Power" To Cornelia

    Cornelia looks at her “clone”, “anything,” she asks.

    “That’s what I said,” Jeff replies, ‘damn it… me and my big mouth,’ he thinks to himself.

    “So, you’ll give me permission to transform you into anything I want for as long as I want?”

    Jeff nods, “y-yes,” he says, starting to REALLY regret of suggesting this.

    “Hm… okay,” Cornelia says, “but first, transform back into yourself, I don’t want you any longer looking like me… perv!”

    Jeff nods, he takes out his phone and reverts the transformation, both Valerie and Cornelia look in shock and amazement as Jeff transforms back into his normal self, his clothes becoming the male school uniform.

    “Holy… shit,” both girls say.

    “How is it even possible,” Cornelia asks, “I mean… stuff like this you only see in some boring sci-fi movie!”

    Jeff nods, “that’s what I thought myself,” he says.

    “Okay,” Cornelia says, “let’s make some conditions: you will hand me your phone, I’ll choose something you are supposed to become until I decide that you have learned your lesson, and if you ever protest or try to escape, I’ll make it worse for you!”

    Jeff gulps, “okay… I guess I have no choice but to agree to your terms,” he says.

    “Good,” Cornelia says, “now… hand me your phone and let’s get started with your punishment for being a perv”.

    Jeff reluctantly hands Cornelia his phone and watches as the girls look over the App, the boy assuming that they are reading the manual.

    Cornelia looks at Jeff, “wait right there,” she says, then she and Valerie walk a few feet away and then stick their heads together.

    Jeff is unable to make out any words, but he senses that he’ll be in for some humiliating punishment.

    After a while the girls walk back to Jeff, the boy looks anxiously at the two girls, “I must say,” Cornelia says, “there are so many things I’d love to do to you! It has not been easy to decide, but I think I found the perfect punishment for you,” the blond girl grins and starts to type into Jeff’s phone. The blond girl grins up at Jeff, “enjoy,” she simply says before confirming whatever she has programmed.

    Jeff shivers as he feels the tingle run through his body, the feeling of fear creeping up in him.
