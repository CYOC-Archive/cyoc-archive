    At her mercy

    You still can't believe what happened! This is impossible .... but you are standing on her palm!

    Shyanne doesn't hear your begging - it is an automatic reaction to this weird situation. But Shyanne is still the same and you are older so you should still have some kind of authority... you hope.

    Shyanne brings her hand up slowly. With you being the size of a bug, you would very easily die if you fell on the ground from the height you are at. Thank God she understands this!

    "What's going on, Shyanne???!!??" you yell out as soon as she has you closer to her head, by her breast area.

    "I TOLD YOU IT WOULD WORK!"

    Her voice is thundrous so you cover your ears to avoid having your ear drums shatter.

    "OH, SORRY, " she says in a quieter but still loud regular voice.

    You decide to go to something a little less obvious.

    "Why did you shrink me in the first place???"

    "WELL I HAD TO TEST THIS ON SOMEONE AND YOU SEEMED LIKE A GOOD CHOICE."

    "Do you at least have an antidote? I mean, I have to go meet with some friends soon!"

    Shyanne looks to the ground at her feet then sits down on a chair beside a table. She sets you down on the table.

    "THAT'S THE THING, JAMES. I WANT YOU TO STAY WITH ME."

    "W-w-what?!?"

    "YOU ARE ALWAYS WITH YOUR FRIENDS AND HARDLY EVER HERE AND IT'S MADE IT HARDER AND MORE COMPLICATED TO TELL YOU BUT... I LOVE YOU."

    You look up at Shyanne's eyes and see the little sparkle of emotion they have in them - she's serious!

    Shyanne likes me? you think. No, wait a second!

    "Shyanne! You and I both know I already have a girlfriend!" you yell up at her pointing.

    Shyanne then grabs you with her thumb and index finger. Just the two fingers alone cover your body leaving only your head sticking out and the grasp is pretty tight. You can't talk and you can hardly breathe. You try to squirm out of her grip but it's useless.

    "ARE YOU REALLY GOING TO HANG ON TO HER! SHE'S GONE FOR ABOUT A YEAR DOING WHO KNOWS WHAT BEHIND YOUR BACK! YOU KNOW WHAT - WHATEVER, I'LL SHOW YOU THAT I'M BETTER."

    You try to squirm more but she now tightens her grip. You never thought this usual annoying teenager you called Shyanne was this serious and ... crazy!

    She begins walking to...

    What do you do now?
