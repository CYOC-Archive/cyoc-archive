    “Now,” Sayaka says, “how do I want this to work,” the girl thinks for a while. After a while, Sayaka smiles, “I know,” she says, she looks at Jeff, “okay Jeffy-boy, just so you know: from now on, until I get bored with you, you are MY toy! You will do anything I say, first: you will be unable to use the App on me, you will not even THINK about using it on me!”

    Jeff feels a tingling in his head, he feels the thought of using the App on Sayaka being erased from his mind.

    Sayaka looks at Jeff, “now, tell me,” she says, “would you ever use the App on me?”

    Before Jeff can stop himself, he hears himself answering, “I'd never use the App on you, Sayaka. I have no reason to,” the boy looks in horror at the girl in front of him.

    Sayaka smirks, “good boy,” she says, “let's carry on, second: you will be unable to tell anyone about your situation. You will not be able to tell anyone the truth, the truth being, that you are my property. So, Jeffy-boy, WHAT are you?”

    Again Jeff feels the tingling in his head, “I am... your property,” he says, his voice sounding not to happy about it. He knows he is not Sayaka's toy nor property, but no matter how hard he tries to fight it, he feels that this is what he is.

    Sayaka tilts her head, “you don't sound very happy,” she says, “you should be happy about it. You know that I am not a evil person, I am, as a matter of fact, a very kind person. You are happy to belong to me, you are happy to be owned by a rich girl.”

    Again the tingling is back, this time, Jeff feels the feeling of happiness raise in him, he even smiles, “yes Sayaka,” he says, cheerfully, “I am really happy to be owned by you! I couldn't imagine anything else!”

    The girl smiles evilly, “I told you so,” she says, “I am glad you are happy, you were so miserable before, right? You hated your life, you always wanted to be owned by me, right?”

    Jeff finds himself nodding violently, “yes,” he says, “I was so miserable, but now I am so happy!”

    Sayaka smiles, “such a good toy,” she says, patting the boy's head, “now, since you are my property, you won't be needing this phone, it is trash, you deserve a much better phone. Tell you what, after school, we'll go shopping and we'll buy you a new phone, with your money of course. You'd love that, wouldn't you?”

    Again Jeff nods, even if he continues to try and break free of the mind control, “yes,” he says.

    “Good,” Sayaka says, “now, I want you to hand me all of your credit cards and loose change,” she holds out her hand and Jeff empties his pockets, handing everything to Sayaka.

    'This is hell,' the boy thinks to himself.

    Sayaka puts Jeff's belongings into her purse, “good,” she then says, “now, I will copy the App onto my phone, then I'll delete it from your phone. Once we have gotten you a new phone, I'll allow you to only have your mom's phone number and mine in your contacts, you don't need anyone else's.”

    “Of course Sayaka,” Jeff says, “you know whats best for me anyway.”

    Sayaka blinks, then she giggles, “hehe... and I have not even said that, but yes, I DO know what is best for you!” The girl ruffles the boy's head, after she had copied the App onto her phone, she deletes it from Jeff's phone.

    Because of Sayaka owning the exact copy of the App, this causes Jeff to still be under her control.

    Sayaka puts both phones into her purse, “now listen,” she says, “I want you to head back to class. After the school ends, I want you to wait until every student has left, then I want you to meet me at the front gates.”

    “Okay,” Jeff says, “but, what about my friends?”

    “Just tell them you need to study, and that you want to do it by yourself,” Sayaka says, rolling her eyes, “you are really one dumb toy. But I guess that is normal, don't worry Jeffy-boy, you have got me now. I will make the decisions from now on, just ask for my opinion, I know you will do that, right?”

    “Of course,” the boy says, screaming mentally.

    Sayaka smiles, “good,” she says, “now off you go and I'll see you after school, we have a busy day ahead of us.”

    Jeff nods and hurries out of the room and towards his next class, he can hear Sayaka laughing behind him, but the boy does not care, he only knows: he is happy to be owned by such a smart and kind girl.
