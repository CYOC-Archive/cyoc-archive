    With only nine days to go before Animalia's Birthday Celebration, Animalia and its colonies were getting ready for the Big Birthday Celebration. While the majority of the celebrations would be colony-based, there were still some constants, like Animalia's enormous birthday cake. However, there were still some surprises that could throw you for a loop if you didn't expect it.

    Lorenzo was getting his gym bag out of his locker in Claw Coliseum (he was still getting used to the idea of unisex locker rooms) when someone cleared their throat. Turning, he was very surprised to see Miss Fabina, and old (human) instincts caused him to move his hands to cover his sheathed manhood, and Miss Fabina smirked at that.

    "I'm sure that you've heard of Animalia's porn videos." said Miss Fabina.

    "... once or twice..." answered Lorenzo, not saying that he's got several stored on an external hard drive.

    "My newest production calls for a predator/prey relationship between a leopard seal and macaroni penguin." replied Miss Fabina.

    "What..." said Lorenzo, flatly, all awkwardness lost.

    "Don't worry," answered Miss Fabina. "Animalia is a lot more flexible in regard to things like love and lust, so you won't lose much."

Due to the reputation of Texans being stupid, conservative, etc., a lot of homosexuals, both male and female, were flocking to Dino City as a kind of refuge, due in no small part to Annie's speech. This, in and of itself, was causing an extension of the housing issue that was already going on, but fortunately, Dino City's own Construction Company was already working on expanding the apartment buildings that were already built or being built.

Because of Pride Month last month, there had been an increase in demand for gay and lesbian Dinotopian porn videos. While the initial videos had been well-patronized, there was ultimately a demand for new blood and new material.

    Probably the biggest news was from the two new colonies in France and Spain.

While she was glad that she'd only be receiving six new girls, she was a bit more conflicted that there would actually be twelve boys joining, which would require some new staff members. While she was glad that her school was expanding to meet the needs/demands of the new colony, she didn't like the idea (perpetrated by popular, furry, culture) of her students getting into... "relationships" with teachers for... various reasons.

It was his coverage of the Mictlan Cave that held the most attention, especially of Level Nine where there was, via the pareidolia effect on the cave wall, a giant skeletal man looking upwards, which came to be accepted as the Mesoamerican God of Death, as well as a crude altar at his feet, consisting of a large, triangular stone surrounded by a ring of smaller stones.
