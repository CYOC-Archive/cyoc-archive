    Dustin was walking down the paved path with his two dogs, when he came by the wooden park bench, where the beautiful, young lady was sitting at. He immediately felt attracted to the young lady, and he decided to tie the leashes of his two dogs to one of the wooden legs of the bench, before sitting down next to her. Tina immediately recognized him as Dustin. Of course, Dustin had no idea that Tina is actually Ty, in the body of a young lady.

    “Hey, cutie… Why your two dogs are so adorable…” she said to him, as he was busy tying the leashes.

    “Hi there…” Dustin said, as he sat down beside her. “Do you come to this park often?”

    “Often enough” she chuckled. “To meet a handsome, young man like you!”

    Her answer immediately made Dustin blush a little.

    “Hi… My name is Dustin. I live in a house that is just a block away from here” he explained, while pointing at the basketball court, which was several yards away. The house was on the opposite side of the street, facing the basketball court.

    “Well, Dustin. My name is Tina. I’m new in town!”

    Dustin and Tina talk for a few minutes. Tina knows exactly who Dustin is, but Dustin has no clue to who Tina is. He’s actually starting to like, the tall, slender girl with the jet black long hair and the big, blue eyes.

    After a few minutes, Dustin grabs the leashes of the two dogs.

    “I need to walk these two mutts home” he said, pointing at the two little dogs. “You’re more than welcome to join me, if you like!”

    “Sure!”

    Tina gets up, and holds Dustin’s free hand, while he’s holding the leashes in his other hand. Dustin and Tina walk home, where Dustin quickly leaves the dogs in the fenced back yard, before opening the front door so that he could let Tina in the house.

    “You got a gorgeous place here, Dustin!” she laughs, as she innocently wanders around the vast living room of the spacious house.

    “Wait till you see my bedroom!” he laughs, as he lead Tina up the stairs to the master bedroom, where Dustin had a large king size bed, a hot tub, and a weight room all to himself.

    Tina finally sat down on the edge of Dustin’s bed, when Dustin slipped into some more comfortable clothes. He wore just a pair of shorts now. Tina decided to remove her shoes.

    “I guess it’s time for us to get to know each other, huh?” Tina smiled, as she looked into Dustin’s love-struck eyes. Dustin was completely in love with the girl. Of course, Tina was absolutely gorgeous to begin with! But Dustin wasn’t aware of the fact that Tina still had the power to do several things that Ty used to do with him all the time!

    “Aaaah Dustin…” she whispered, as she ran her finger over Dustin’s chest. “I like a man who has a little muscle, you know what I mean?”

    “Oh yeah! Sure! I lift weights every day!”

    Tina just smiled, as she stared at his pecs.

    Suddenly, Dustin felt a tingling sensation. His pectorals began to increase in size! They were filling up like balloons, forcing his shoulders to become a bit wider!

    “Whoa!” Dustin shouted, as he felt his body changing a little bit. Somehow, the magic that had worked in him had a vaguely familiar feeling to it. It was as if he had felt this power work in his body before….

    Tina just grinned, as she pondered what to do to her new lover now.
