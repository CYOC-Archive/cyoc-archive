    Welcome to the Isolated Society of the Unknown!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Mr. Freak 6 years ago O

    "Now that you have properly been converted to your new identity, it's time for some proper introductions." The monotone voice continues, "You have came to the Isolated Society of the Unknown as a means of permanent escape from your previous life as...whatever it was that was troubling you in the past." You look up and see that the voice was coming from a camera attached to a loudspeaker mounted on a wall, looking straight at you.

    "I would probably personalize this message for any person who joins us, but there are two reasons why this message is pre-recorded. The first one, is the one I have already stated as our society is vastly growing and I cannot manage every single arrival of every newcomer that joins us. The second is because the minute you are encased in the rubbery skin that members of the Isolated Society wear, whatever identity that you had before joining will become part of the past and you shall begin life anew. A fresh new start with a new identity."

    A video plays on the TV next to the operating table that show a diverse selection of various rubber people, as faceless as they are, acting extremely happy while doing various tasks such as fishing from a rowboat, enjoying the sounds of one of them playing a guitar while at a campfire, hanging clothes on a rack to dry, snuggling in bed while reading a book, having a snowball fight when one of them falls over on the ground still happy, playing basketball with friends, painting a picture on a canvas, and overlooking the vista of the campgrounds that this Isolated Society is located. Just to name a few examples of the various things that the rubber people are doing, clearly enjoying their life in the community. The voice continues on...

    "Which brings us to the 'Unknown' part of the Isolated Society of the Unknown. With your new skin sealed behind rubber and your face and privates made non existant, whatever your life that was making you feel targeted for any underserved misery that you may have felt was undeserved will trouble you no further. You can begin again and mingle with a new set of friends who will love you and accept you for the person who is inside you, trapped under a skin of flesh who always had to put on a brave face just to live another day.

    Basically, we aren't trapping you in a rubber suit. You are being relased in a rubber skin that will serve as your new body for a soul that is looking for a chance to start fresh.

    'But Wait!' you say, 'Being sealed in rubber has to have some realistic ramifications! I will probably die of starvation! I may not be able to go to the bathroom! I am not buying into your sick fantasy of living the rest of my life as a Zentai suit!' Well, fear not because you will still be able to consume food without even the need for teeth! And drinking water should not be an issue! We will get into more details during the orientation, but for now, you will be released from these bindings that have strapped you to the table. We didn't want to release you yet until you understand that what we are doing is helping people like you end their suffering from the troubles of life outside. Now let's walk over to the next room to begin step two of your first day in the Isolated Society of the Unknown!"

    The video ends with a logo for "ISU" displayed, and the TV turns off. You are then released from your bindings and get off of the operating table, and a soft pink featureless being dressed in a white nurses outfit standing at the doorway gives a hand signal suggesting that you should follow it.
