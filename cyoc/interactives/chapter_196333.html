From her house, Mary Elise now sighed, pinching the bridge of her nose as Claudia and Samantha began to argue. "Girls," she said, her voice traveling to her friends who were miles away. "Can we focus on finding this nest of vampires please."

    "I am focused Mary," Claudia replied. "And whats with the cloak and dagger stuff? Aren't we trying to draw attention to ourselves?"

    "Because we need to be in position Claudia," Mary Elise stated. "Sammy and Katie are our heavy hitters. I want them to be near when thw vampires attack."

    "Heavy hitters," Claudia yelled indignantly "I can transform into an 8 foot flaming tiger avatar with super demon strength! How am I not a heavy hitter?"

    "Claudia," Mika sighed. "Let it go honey. Please." Claudia continued to grumble as Mary Elise now took a deep breath. "The more things change the more they stay the same."

    "Katie, Kellie," Mary Elise asked, trying to get her friends on task once more. "How are you two doing?"

    "All clear on this end," Katie replied. "We're heading towards the center to meet up with the others." Mary Elise sighed, telling the couple to proceed and to keep her posted.

    As Katie and Kellie now continued, Kellie paused as she scanned the graveyard. It was far too quiet, causing the young lawyer to feel a great deal of apprehension. If her wife hadn't been by her side she felt would have turned and sprinted from this place. Still, she needed to talk if for no other reason than to keep her mind off the fact she was hunting vampires. "So how do you think Morgan is doing?"

    "I'm sure Morgan is fine," Katie smiled. "Dont worry, Naomi and Diane are both really good babysitters plus you know what a sweetheart Morgan is."

    "Okay true," Kellie stated. "But they're watching ALL the kids tonight. Do you think it might be a bit too much for them?"

    Katie shook her head, giving her wife a reassuring smile. "Dont worry so much Kellie they got this. Trust me."
