    With Animalia ComicCon going on, there were plenty of funny things going on. Mainly this was from nerds getting the finer details of anime and magna mixed up, much to the amusement of many who witnessed it, especially when they were corrected by real fans. But there were a few things that were completely unexpected but absolutely hilarious.

    ---

    When Tsukuyomi arrived at Heiya Island, it was a little fair to assume that he and Ami would be mates. But on the journey, his nurse had fallen for him completely, and Ami (who already knew about it) greeted him as a brother. The knowledge that in Shintoism, Amaterasu and Tsukuyomi were brother and sister, had many recoiling in hilarious ways at what they had been suggesting.

    When later asked if there was any other plans, Ami answered that they were still looking for someone for Susanoo, but as she put it "There's not many people who would want to be someone who was born from Izanagi's nose." There was some brief trolling on the Animalia website after this, but another post, seemingly from Izanagi himself, stopped the trolling dead in its tracks by explaining how Amaterasu, Tsukuyomi, and Susanoo were born. In the end, the trolls got trolled themselves for knowing next to nothing about Japanese mythology.

    ---

    Another thing that was going on was actually going on in Dino City. The new influx of hippie immigrants was causing a few problems, but they were fortunately all minor and, in some cases, hilarious. Sgt. Slash was having his work cut out for keeping the newest members in line during their acclimation classes, but this was to be somewhat expected since Sgt. Bash had told him what to expect.

    Another thing that was happening at Dino city was seemingly a result of conversion. It would seem to be that, after conversion, many new Dinotopians were unintentionally "gifted" with the "babe/chick magnet" power. While some did like this side effect, there were those who weren't too happy about this.

    ---

    There were also some things that were happening that were actually minor things, but one such thing did keep up "with the times". Someone at the gate was checking people for COVID and accidentally sprayed one guest with disinfectant instead of checking her temperature. Fortunately, this was a forgivable mistake and the end result was only a minor fine.

    Another minor thing was at the child-friendly arcade they had set up. There was a Boxer Fire arcade game and someone tried to show off by kicking it. Unfortunately for him, this was a lot more difficult that movies and video games made it out to be. The end result was wild miss that ended with him on the floor.

    A similar thing happened at Golden Mountain Dojo. This case was a result of trying to kick a can off of a seated guy's head, but the blow was a little low, and we probably know what would happen. For those who don't, its enough to know that the seated guy ended up at the doctor's office.

    ---

    There was a really unusual one that was reported at Habitat Homes. Three guys and a camera were found on the ground alongside a broken tree branch and the guys weren't moving much. When APD was notified, the first officer on the scene got a strange, but funny answer.

    "Why are you three on the ground near a broken tree branch? And with a camera?" he had asked.

    "Some animals put on a display to attract attention of a suitable mate." said the second guy.

    "Ah, right." said the officer, realizing what was going on. Apparently these guys were attempting to get photos of Animalians 'in their natural environment' for an unknown reason. But their latest attempt (which also happened to be the one that got them caught) had them climbing a tree for one of their photos and the branch they were using abruptly decided that it wasn't up to the task, sending all three of them back to earth with a bang.

    ---

    Other than that, there wasn't too much going on, but just enough to keep everyone entertained.
