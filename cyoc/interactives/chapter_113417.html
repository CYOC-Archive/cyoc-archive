    late 1960's.,which coincidentally was when the TF craze first
    came to the attention of the startup Chronivac Corporation.

    She had a thing about dragons as well.

    And she had a thing about bringing back to life all the old
    TF machines her mother had developed when Fiorna was a
    little girl,especially "Mother',as the scientists in the community refered

    People had for decades though the machine had been smashed up in the 1960s and melted down for scrap,but when

    it had been built in the 1940's
    shed been shocked and pleased to discover the quonset hut and research base outbuildings
    still at their original location,a little buried under the 33 foot snow drifts,.

    According to her mother the Kanadraene had sat in a underground cavern
    parked right in the middle of a radium deposit.

    So she'd mushed on her huskies,and found the hatch
    leading down to the machine.

    The big black huge hulking Kanadraene ,transmutor coils and chrono-capacitors
    were still intact,and though mice and rats had chewed away at the wiring,it looked like Fiona had found a
    do-able restoration project.

    Now the Chronivac Corporation and her were no longer
    on speaking terms.

    That was bad,because the Canadian Secret Service,sometime in the
    intravening decades ,had removed the TF heart of the Kanadraene,
    its highly obscure and vitally necessary
    <What you wish is what you are" black box.

    had spent billions inventing and
    which the Chronivac Corporation had spent even more
    billions re-inventing in the 1960s.

    iron skillets worth of beans and bacon.
