    Like other big companies with questionable but profitable business practices, FarmazooCo had many networks and connections with rich business owners and other larger companies that were interested in their transformative work and even commission them for their "products" though most of them had very specialized requests: anthros.

    Whether it be personal or business reasons, these people wanted them and had all the money in the world to dispel FarmazooCo's reluctance to do so. The commissioners ranged from those wanting to have loyal animal people as bodyguards or entertainment for high profile individuals to the billionaires that simply wish to have their own private zoo of anthros that are quite adorable when they have no knowledge of their human origins despite their humanoid appearance.

    It's this reason why Amy and Taylor were soon approached by one of a few other FarmazooCo representatives to be taken early for hypnotic processing. "Hello gazelles!" the rep said cheerfully with little to no emotion of concern to the pair's changed states. "I'm excited to inform you two that you have been selected as winners of the mall's 'Super Secret Sweepstakes'!"

    "Really?" Taylor said in surprise as she moved her hair aside, it already being the same beige color as her main body fur.
    "That's amazing! I've never won anything like this since I was a fawn!" Amy said as the two held both each other's hands as they happily hopped in glee, their hooves clacking against the floor with every hop.

    "Well today's your day then, you lucky gazelles! All we need to do is to take you aside to make sure everything is in order so that you can enjoy your prize!" the rep said, already leading the transformed girls to one of the many large doors located in between some of the shops. Amy and Taylor eagerly followed, forgetting to even question what they had just won. What neither of them realized was that the representative's overly bubbly behavior is due to the fact that she is one of many subjected to FarmazooCo's hypnosis technology, made to ensure complete loyalty of those hired into the company.

    The rep lead the two anthro gazelles down what used to be the many maintenance halls now converted into a hypnotizing center. Amy and Taylor didn't even notice the rooms they passed were filled with many similar biome animals situated in front of large screens with varying hypnosis swirls and subliminal messages and images. As they went further down the halls, the rooms began to shrink in size being as small as classrooms with small groups of anthro animals like themselves being mentally oriented into their new roles in life as outlined by their special commissions. Soon the two were put into one of these classrooms with a few other girls who happened to be transformed in the same way. As Amy and Taylor took their seats, the two could hardly hold in their excitement as evident by their eagerly swishing tails mimicking the other gazelle girls in the room.

    "Oh this is so exciting! I can't wait to see what we've won!" Amy said, clopping her hooves on the ground repeatedly in anticipation.
    "Me too! I really hope it's something from back home on the savannah..." Taylor started before trailing off as she stared at the large screen the powered on in front of them with a large spiral entrancing the gazelles in the room.

    "You are and always have been gazelle girls" a voice from the screen stated, making sure to dispel any thoughts of doubt that may be lingering in the transformed girls.

    "We are and always have been gazelle girls" everyone in the room said in unison, the thought cementing itself as fact in their minds.
