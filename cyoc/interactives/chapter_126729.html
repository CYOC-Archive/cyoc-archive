    Making Marcus bigger

    added 5 years ago BM S

    It didn't take long for you to receive a new notification at the bottom of his page.

    You feel satisfied as you move your mouse to the notification and saw that it was, again, from Workout_Master. It seems whoever this was, they're generous enough to not push you too far. You click on the notification and read the new note:

    "Looks like the changes were a success, although your eyes were supposed to be blue. This site sometimes glitches, so we'll have to see what happens from here on out. Hopefully you're liking not having to pull so much weight around, although you're still a bit heavy. The problem is, you need some more meat on you. Let's add about 20 pounds of muscle to you and take out 50 pounds of fat. That should even you out nicely."

    50 pounds of fat?! The 30 pounds was pretty big before, but 50? That seems astronomical. You look down at your stomach somewhat wistfully. It's not that you enjoy being overweight, but you're used to it. You've stayed at this weight since high school and haven't lost or gained much more in that time.

    As your mind continues to process these changes, you feel that sensation of tingling come over you once more. This time, the tingling seems more powerful, as it comes with the removal and addition of even more weight. You're tempted to close your eyes so that you don't go crazy with all the sensations, but you manage to hold yourself together so you can watch the changes as they happen.

    Since Workout_Master said to add 20 pound of muscle first, that change happens first. Looking back at your stomach, you notice the slight rotundness grow a bit more refined, and some dents appear in the skin as new muscle grows underneath it. You feel somewhat bloated as your stomach pushes up slightly, giving room for the new mass.

    Your abdominal muscles aren't the only ones increasing with this change. You lean forward in your chair and watch as your legs slowly swell with added strength. Fortunately you wore shorts today, and your legs don't tear through any fabric as your thighs grow about three inches in diameter.

    Your vision of your legs becomes a bit more covered as your pectoral muscles also grow a bit, plumping up with new fibers and becoming more round, replacing the small man boobs you had before. You press your hands against them and feel resistance there, which you'd never had before. You smile to yourself as you realize how awesome this change is going to be.

    As the final parts of the first change come into reality, with your arms, neck, torso, feet and even groin grow with new and improved mass, you feel as if a giant vacuum cleaner was placed inside you. Your stomach churns, and you bend over just to avoid the cramp that suddenly came about.

    This change feels like it's going to be painful, you think to yourself. 30 pounds was one thing, but 50 pounds is going to be a doozy.

    And a doozy it is, which you see as the change commences. Because of the extent of this change, you feel the effects of it much more quickly. The slight bit of fat you had over your abs immediately disappears, making way for a chiseled and lean look. Your biceps become much more defined and you can't feel the flap of fat you were used to having as you move your arms up and down.

    You walk back over to the mirror to witness the rest of the changes, and your biggest surprise comes from looking at your face. While you were never dissatisfied with how your face looked, you always had a bit more baby fat than most and your struggled to make your expressions look like anything but an innocent child's.

    With this change, you realize, you'll never have that problem again. Your cheekbones are finally visible underneath your skin, and you are steadily losing that double chin as the fat recedes into your neck, now with very visible tendons and a prominent Adam's apple. You brush your hands against your face and feel that slight itch of stubble that was hiding underneath your fat, and your grin grows ever larger as you walk back to your computer.

    Before you can even sit down, you see another notification has appeared. You click on it to see what else is in store...
