    Will, a 34-year-old man with a well-trained body, short black hair, black eyes and a natural light brown tan, is in his kitchen drinking coffee before getting ready for work. The man is a state patrol officer, since his shift starts at four in the morning, so he is up at two in the morning. Will is at the moment drinking his coffee and checking his messages on his phone, a Samsung Galaxy A52. The man’s phone gives of a *ping* sound as he receives an add-message, “I hate these fucking adds,” he grumbles, “Chronivac? What the hell are those idiots trying to sell now,” he asks himself. Will moves his finger towards the “delete” button, to delete the message, but suddenly hesitates, ‘something tells me to at least read the message,’ he thinks to himself. The man shrugs, ‘I can still delete it,’ he thinks and opens the message, which reads:

    “Greetings,
    if you are reading this messages, you must be curious of what the “Chronivac” is about. The “Chronivac” is a new program for Smartphones and Computers/Notebooks designed and developed by a group of people, who wish to stay anonymous, in corporation with scientists. The individuals are a group of people who are bored of their lives, and wish to make it more interesting. Like what, you might ask, well: ever thought about being a child again? Or are you curious to know what it would be like being the opposite gender? Or have you even thought about what it would be like as a inanimate object, like a vehicle or a toy? Or even being an animal? Well, this program is able to grant you these wishes and secret desires.

    Don’t believe us? Well, why not test out the beta-version attached to this message? Don’t worry, the beta-version as well as the full version of the program is totally free and free of any hidden tricks.

    Since it is a new program, there might be some minor issues, which are harmless. And also if you are reading this message you’re one of only 4 lucky people worldwide to receive this program.”

    Will finishes reading the message, his eye brow raised, “a program that is able to transform people and objects,” the man asks out loud, “sounds more like a thing out of an sci-fi-movie”. The man scrolls back up to find the attached beta-version, he leans back in his chair, ‘should I give it a try? I am sceptic but extremely curious,’ he thinks to himself.

    The cop looks at his watch, “I still have over an hour left until I get picked up,” he says, then he sighs, “fuck it, I got to know”. He presses the attachment and a message pops up, “thank you for wanting to test out the beta-version. Do you wish to proceed,” it says. Will presses the “yes” button and a installation bar appears. After two minutes the man’s phone pings and the indicating, that the installation is complete. Will opens the app and looks through the options, “hm… most of the options are locked and only accessible via the full version,” he mutters to himself. He then notices a scan button and an info box next to it, he opens the infos and reads: “Scanning: you can either scan an object, person or animal manually or set the program to scan automatically”.

    Will clicks on the scan button and seconds later he sees a folder with his name next to it, he taps on the folder and his eyes widen. “Holy mother of Jesus,” he breaths, “this program knows everything about me, even… my darkest secret”. You see, Will’s darkest secret is: he is attracted to children, especially boys. After the shock died down, the man thinks for a while, ‘I hope this is no trick to get me locked up,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I never harmed a kid… and never will… but… if this thing really works, I might be able to live out my secret without having to worry about consequences’.

    Thinking about the possibility to finally become “lucky” and finally have an Relationship, without anyone thinking it is wrong, Will decides to test out the program. “Hm… what should I change,” he asks himself, then his eyes widen, and a smile appears on his face, as he decides to try to make himself younger.
