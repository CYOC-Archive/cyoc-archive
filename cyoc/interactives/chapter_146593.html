But these mirror would make her into the anthro animal she WANTED, or her subconscious wanted, not what she felt obliged to become... did she really want to be a naked rabbit person for an afternoon with her friends? While she knew what they said about jumping off a cliff if all your friends are doing it, all the anthro people had been reasonable and rational, and weren't under any sort of mind control magic. If it was just some harmless experience, why not share it with her friends?

And the more she looked at it, the more this was herself, not her other self. Her previous existence replaced with the one reflected in the mirror, only those like her friends knowing she'd ever been anything different.

She flew with total silence, a field mouse's sensitive ears wouldn't be able to hear anything. She flew out of the Cavern of Mirror, doing cartwheels and corkscrews in the air.
