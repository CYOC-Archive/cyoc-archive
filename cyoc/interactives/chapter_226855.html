    “Of course! I want to be an anthro animal more than anything else in the world!” Chris said excitedly as he looked at the anthros that surrounded the two of you.

    “Then I’ll be more than happy to take you to your transformer,” the lion girl says and grabbing Chris by the hand she takes him away.

    You only watch as the two disappear, leaving you alone in the crowd.

    “What the fuck! First that kid shows up out of nowhere and now he’s leaving to turn into an anthropomorphic animal?!” you shout in disbelief. This was starting to become even more ludicrous than you could imagine.

    “He was accidentally left locked in like you,” another anthro wolf says. He’s much larger than the one that spoke to you before and looks much more intimidating. As he walks closer to you, you back away slowly, before accidentally bumping into the friendly anthro wolf from before.

    “Sorry, dude!” the wolf says as he catches you from nearly falling.

    You feel panicked as you try to assess what’s happening. The wolf holds you against his body as the other wolf approaches. The other clothed anthros simply staring at you in curiosity and interest. It isn’t long before you pass out from the stress of the situation.

    ***

    You awaken to find yourself on a bench, covered in a blanket. Lifting your head, you see you’re completely alone. Maybe now you could escape?

    You get up from the bench as quietly as you can, tossing the blanket to the side. You sneak around the zoo, trying to find a way out. Every gate appears to be locked so what option do you have now? You try to decide what to do next.
