    ...Um..." Linda muttered.

    "Now what?" Ty asked.

    Sheepishly, Linda turned to Ty and said, "I forgot".

    "You...forgot?" Ty asked.

    "Well, with all this magic and sex, it kinda makes a girl forget her priorities, now doesn't it?"

    Ty sighed, "Well, we might as well keep on walking. We might just run into them".

    Linda nodded and both left the food court. Soon, they were walking amongst the other consumers through the mall.

    It had been roughly twenty minutes and neither of them had seen any signs of Linda's friends.

    "Where could they be?" Linda asked, worriedly.

    "We'll find them. Where do you think they would normally go?" Ty asked.

    "I don't...wait...Elane wanted to check out some new games that had come out. They might be by the Game Stop".

    "Right", Ty said, "That's just around the corner".

    They took the corner and indeed found Linda's friends. But they were not alone.

    "Hey gals", said a male figure. He and his friend were looking at the group of girls as if they were not but meat for their supper.

    "Didn't realize there was girls whom played games", said the other.

    "Proves cavemen really do exist these days", Elane remarked.

    She stood in front of the others, wearing her cheerleader uniform. The others were more casual, Cristin wearing a jean vest over her white shirt, her jeans covered in earth and green. Behind her was Joice, wearing a summer yellow sweater and a brown skirt. Beside her was both Ally and Tiffany, Ally wearing a dark pink shirt and short jeans, and Tiffany wearing a long sleeved black shirt, a blue skirt, black stockings and a blue beret.

    "Ooh, tough gal. Bet you're nothing but all talk" the other said again.

    "Cool it Fred. She's the captain of the cheer leading squad, and she's a black belt too, if I recall", The first said, rubbing his chin as if he had sustained a blow from her earlier.

    "Doesn't matter, Sam", Fred said, turning to the other girls, "Why don't you gals come with us. I'm sure we could have a real good time". He winked at Joice, whom backed away.

    "Why don't you guys leave us alone. We're waiting for someone anyway, so we won't be of much entertainment", Joice said.

    "Oh, I'm sure we can think of something", Sam said, suggestively, which put all the girls on guard and Elane raised her fists defensively.

    "This doesn't look good", Ty said.

    "We've got to do something", Linda replied.

    "Well, what do you think?"
