    corrupted and targeted people who didn't exist or weren't within range.

    Every now and again the emitter did happen to process a change for Jeff as his profile was loaded into the
    system and he slept close by. Several times throughout the night he had been transformed or modified though he
    didn't realize. He had been turned into a tomcat, an 11 year old girl, and a 36 year old midget who rides a
    unicycle at the fair, among others.

    The duration of the changes varied but were done by the time he woke up except that until 12:37 pm, if he eats
    rocky road ice cream with bacon, he will have an intense orgasm and be stoned for 16 hours.

    The reality changes effecting his parents now were unlike the changes that effected him throughout the night.

    all reality. Second, the data corruption that was spreading and causing more and more errors and failed
    transformation requests changed the duration from 139 seconds, into 9208 years, not that Jeff would care by
    the time the effect wears off.

    Though the transformation specifically targeted Jeff, additional modifications can be made freely when anti
    paradox is activated, the program tries to keep a sense of normality about the whole situation to give the
    user an optimal experience and allow things to flow more easily. Jeff's was the only profile ever entered into
    the device, all other transformation subjects that had been targeted truly didn't exist as the program
    essentially made the person up.

    The system then loaded the program into memory and based it off Jeff's most recent transformation request. to

    thousands of things that could seem out of the ordinary, and corrected them through recursive simulations each
    weighed against the last. The program actually stopped the device from doing anything else for countless hours

    The most reasonable answer was of course genetics, so Jeff's grandpa is now Italian (actually Mom had an
    affair and these two are half brothers but we don't talk about it). Even being 25% Italian would not

    mother with a 26 year old son does seem out of place, siblings seems much more reasonable.

    With that the device fired up and changed reality by sending a signal directly to Transdem Labs for the global
    perception and history modifications and zapping the sleeping Jeff who was only 12 feet away for the age
    enhancement. Then the Chronivac began calculating a similar transformation for a Sa$4ly Hopkir9Xt, who doesn't

    or body had changed, only that he was more tired than usual and was craving caffeine (he is soon to be a
    coffee drinker). The history modification enlarged the clothes in his closet so he didn't notice anything when

    When he spoke to his father and began to realize what happened two more things happened. First he ran to the
    mirror (within range of the emitter) and saw a picture of his 'brother'. Second, the broken and disconnected
