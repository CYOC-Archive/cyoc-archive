    "I think I want to watch the aerial races," you said smiling.

    "Ok," Nancy said grabbing your hand and dragging you off to where the races were to start. "Oh could I share your laptop since I don't have mine with me?"

    "Why do you need too?" you asked utterly perplexed.

    "It's because we'll be watching some of the race on it," Nancy said in an exasperated tone. "Most of the time the racers are either mere specks or can't be seen at all. There'll be a couple people following them and broadcasting it on the academy's own channel."

    "Oh ok," you said as you reached some bleachers. After sitting down you asked, "When does it start?"

    "In a few minutes," Nancy replied then pointed, "here they come now. There's Sara and Tara Mullen over there getting ready to shift. You'll want to watch them shift."

    You watched the sisters strip. After putting their clothes in cases they spread out a bit and began their transformation. They bent down as their hands and feet grew gray and elongated to meet forming some sort of rod. Their arms and legs took on the same color as their hands and feet. Their breasts morphed into something that looked like video cameras. Their hair melted into their bodies as their eyes grew to cover most of their faces. Their bodies and heads merged into what was becoming recognizable as helicopter bodies. The tail and tail rotor grew from where what was their butts while the main rotors grew from their backs. It took five minutes but in the end two helicopters had replaced the sisters.

    "They became helicopters," you said in awe as they took off.

    "Of course they can," Nancy said. "There are quite a few that can. Now could I see your laptop?"

    You nodded and Nancy soon showed you how to get the race and other school activities on it. You looked at the start line to see the racers stripping and shifting. You spotted Danny and Caitlin. They were fast shifters shifting almost instantly. One moment they were standing there and the next there were two white horses with wings and a golden horn from their forehead. They were definitely Pegasuses with Danny being smaller than Caitlin. You looked at the others and saw a few that looked like a cross between monkeys and bats. There were a few harpies, eagles, owls, angels, winged demons, and geese. There was one that was a naga coming up to the line but he grew wings so that he was a winged serpent. You didn't see a dragon in the mix though. Maybe once you know how to shift you'll go for the aerial races.

    With a shot from the starter pistol they flapped their wings and they were off. The winged serpent took an early lead right of the start but his wings shrank and he fell back to the ground with a sickening thud. Over the course of the race a few more shifted back and plummeted to the ground. One girl got a tree shoved up her pussy and out the top of her head. I kept a watch on the skies and the screen like most other people in the stands. As the race drew to a close an eagle was in the lead with a harpy in second and Danny in third. Caitlin was in fourth but she dug down deep and moved up along with Danny. It ended up being Caitlin that won by a nose with Danny coming in second, the eagle in third, and the harpy in fourth. As soon as they were all on the ground they shifted back panting and Danny collapsed.

    "Well we'll have to congratulate them tonight," Nancy said clapping and shouting their names. "We can't just swallow them tonight. No, this calls for something special."

    You looked at Nancy and wondered what she had in mind.
