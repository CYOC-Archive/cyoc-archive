    "This is stupid!" Jen said.

    "Time's up," said Kyle "you get BOTH now! Watch how you act from now on, because it'll determine what you become. For every good thing you
    say or do, you'll become more of an angel, but for every bad thing you do, these changes will reverse. If you say or do bad things when you're
    neutral, you'll become more of a succubus, but doing and saying good things will reverse this. No matter what, you'll still be beautiful, and you
    won't have any mental changes. You'll pretty much be going back and forth between having golden hair, blue eyes, a halo, fair skin, and white
    feathery wings, to having black hair, red eyes with slit pupils, pointy ears, horns, a long arrow-shaped tail, red skin, reverse-jointed legs
    ending in hooves, and bat wings. Everyone will see you, and they'll either come closer, or run away, depending on what you look like. Only your
    true love's kiss will make your form staple, and you'll be stuck as whatever he wants you to be - an angel, a succubus, in-between, normal, or
    another female humanoid creature like a nymph, fairy, or satyress, and once that happens, you can make him whatever YOU want him to be, as
    long as it's male, and humanoid. Oh, and one more thing (just to make things more interesting) - two evil factions have entered the school -
    Homunculus, and alien plant-girls (they have a proper name, but I can't even SAY it without barfing - they're THAT bad)!

    Homunculus are artificial human beings born when an idiot tries to create human life with alchemy and fails. They look human, but they have
    slit pupils, weird powers, weird tattoos, and are pure evil. They want to destroy human lives to sate their hunger for violence - and some are
    just plain hungry (for humans)!

    The alien plant-girls have green skin, leaf-like ears, colorful hair, breasts and hips way bigger than any female should have (not without
    surgery, anyway), and friendly faces. They infect humans, and make them part of their race with tentacle-like vine-fingers that stretch, or by
    throwing their seeds at their victims. They have no effect on Homunculus, and if they try to infect them, they'll be poisoned by the red stones
    that give the Homunculus their life. In turn, if they try to eat the green girls, they get sick off of them!

    Both disguise as humans, but you'll see through the disguises. You'll also be immune to plant-girl infection, and to give you an edge against
    the Homunculus, you'll also be good at using alchemy, which they can't (normally) use.

    Both have leaders (who are both beautiful-looking women). The Homunculus leader's name is Sloth, and the plant-girl leader's name is Althea.

    They both want the philosopher's stone, but for different reasons. Sloth wants to use it to open the gate to the other side, and let all those
    black, larval versions of her race run wild, while Althea wants it to amplify her powers and infect more at once on a grand scale, but isn't willing
    to make it herself since it requires live humans as the main ingredient!

    It's your job to stop them both!

    Whether you're a heroine, or anti-heroine is up to you.

    Your transformation will start by how you put down the phone. Have fun, and watch out for chimeras and walking suits of empty armor!"
