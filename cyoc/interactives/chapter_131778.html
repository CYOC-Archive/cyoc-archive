    Goodbye Bitch

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by HunterArrow 5 years ago A

    You realize that it is not right to let these poor people suffer for the rest of their lives as animals. Thus you decide to talk the animal control man out of taking them. “Look I think that… argh”, you yell out in pain as you step out of your car and the bitch bites your leg.

    “Oh my gosh, sir are you alright”, the man calls out as he puts the puppy down and rushes to your aid. The bitch continues digging her teeth into your leg and you begin to kick her with your free leg.

    The bitch continues to growl and she bites deeper and deeper, Keith barks at her angrily from inside the car, and the Rottweiler watches as the animal control man puts his hands around the fearsome bitch. “Let go you stupid bitch”, you yell in pain as you grab her muzzle and try to pry her jaw open. Finally the Rottweiler joins the fray, but shockingly helps you as he bites into the bitch’s leg. This causes the bitch you release your leg as she cries out in pain, and with one swift movement the animal control man muzzles her and holds her to the ground.

    “Sir are you okay”, the man asks you as he continues to put his full body weight onto the bitch.

    “Yeah I’ll be fine”, you tell the man as you glare at the bitch. At that moment you realize that the bitch has just made you change your mind. “Mister I was going to ask you not to take the dogs but after that little stunt that the bitch pulled feel free to take her”, you tell the man.

    The bitch continues to growl and starts to struggle more as the man informs you, “No problem although when we come across an animal that is this violent the only course of action is to put them down.”

    “I understand that, but could you please leave the Rottweiler and the puppy, because I doubt they will cause many problems once Princess here is gone”, you tell the man in the hopes of sparing the other two.

    The man looks over the other two dogs and agrees, “I guess I don’t see any reason to take them but you do need to get them on their leashes.”

    Grateful you thank the man, “Will do and thank you for being so understanding.”

    You watch as the man loads the bitch into a cage in the back of his truck, and she whines through the whole ordeal. Finally he slams the back door of his truck shut and you can no longer see or hear the bitch. As the man gets into the truck he stops and turns back to saying, “Oh and if you decide to sue the owners of this dog I will be happy to testify my name is James Line”, the man informs you.

    Finally you will no longer have to deal with the bitch but what will you do now?
