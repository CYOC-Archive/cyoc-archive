    Thomas Wolf meets Sofia Collie's parents

    After being transformed into a dog, as well as the vice-principal of the school, Sofia found herself engulfed in much work. But once the day's work was done, Sofia and Thomas made the most out of the end of their work day. It was a few days later that Sofia told Thomas she wanted to introduce him to her parents so she could also make them dogs as well.

    "It's a little strange, my family thinks it's always been normal for me to have been a dog and don't question it." Sofia said to Thomas as she rode in his car to her family home.

    "I know, it's how it was set up. The only people who remember you and I being human are you and I my love." Thomas said as Sofia gave him a kiss on the cheek.

    "It seemed when I arrived home that day after our.....first time, I found my parents had aged ten years. It makes sense since they would've been mere children if they had me at the age they had previously been." Sofia revealed as neither she nor Thomas knew that the chronivac could do something like that by itself.

    "So, do you want me to change them into dogs as well?" Thomas now asked.

    "Yes, but can you make it so they aren't aware of the transformation?" Sofia asked.

    "Of course." Thomas answered as they pulled up to the extravagant house.

    Thomas had been told by Sofia that her family lived a life of luxury, however he didn't expect this much luxury. The place looked like it could've been on one of those old shows that explored fancy houses. Making their way inside, Thomas was stunned that a woman like Sofia would ever want to have a job as vice principal.

    "You could have any kind of job in the world and I could give it to you gladly, but why did you want to keep your job as a vice principal with me?" Thomas asked.

    "You've already answered your own question. No other job has you." Sofia said batting her eyes as the two shared a passionate kiss.

    "Ahem!" Came a male voice as the two immediately broke off their kiss and were met by Sofia's parents.

    "Thomas, I'd like you to meet my parents. My mother, Simone," Sofia introduced as Thomas gently kissed Sofia's mother's hand out of respect. "And my father, Roman." Sofia said as Thomas shook Roman's strong hand.

    "So...you're the canine my Sofia has fallen for?" Roman asked Thomas sternly who looked very intimidated by him.

    "Uh....ye-yes sir. I have a....a great love for your daughter." Thomas said trying not to falter his words so much.

    The four of them walked down the long hallway of the Collie's rich home to see Sofia's life before becoming vice-principal of Thomas's school. Thomas decided to use the chronivac on the two parents one at a time, first by starting with Sofia's mother Simone.

    Thomas used the chronivac on her by changing her into a dog breed. Only Simone's breed was changed into a cocker spaniel with reddish brown hair. Once her transformation was complete, some of the pictures with Simone changed as well. Now she was a long time winner of many conformation shows against other dogs. Something she also started Sofia out with doing when she was a young pup.

    "You can see here, I thought my beautiful Sofia would follow in my paws and go on to win many shows like me. But she chose to work at a....lower class school. Where there are mongrels amuck." Simone said eyeing Thomas who turned red.

    "Mother, please, Thomas is a gentleman and he's also very good at his work." Sofia said privately to her mother when Thomas and her father went on ahead.

    "Oh my! Sofia that is scandalous, but...can you....give me some pointers?" Simone asked.

    "Mother!" Sofia said a little loudly before Simone shushed her daughter.

    "I mean it. Your father and I....haven't had it the best lately and I want to bring out that animal in him again." Simone said as Sofia gave a little smile and chuckle before talking quietly with her mother.

    Thomas was alone now and began changing Sofia's father, Roman, into a border collie like her. He noticed that as he was changing, Roman looked far more intimidating now as a dog than he ever did as a human. Once it was all over, this large dog man faced Thomas down with large eyes.

    "I don't know what my daughter sees in you. But I want my daughter to be happy, do you promise you'll keep her happy?" Roman asked.

    "Y-Y-Yes sir, she'll be as happy as a pup." Thomas promised as Roman held out his hand and gave Thomas a firm handshake.

    Soon enough, Simone and Sofia soon came back into the room and Thomas and Sofia saw a glint shimmer in the eyes of Roman and Simone, something that it seemed neither had seen in a long time.

    "Simone...I...I don't know why but....you're looking more radiant now than you have in years." Roman said as he held Simone closely.

    "Oh Roman, you're looking like the young collie that I fell in love with so long ago." Simone said to her husband in almost a whisper.

    "Oh, Sofia, why don't you show Thomas around the house? Your father and I are going to have a little....talk." Simone said the last word in a husky voice to her daughter.

    "Yes mother, come on Thomas, let's go see some of the servants around the home." Sofia said as she pushed Thomas out of the room leaving Roman and Simone alone and giving the two a moment of passion.
