    Tanning and Growing

    Ty just loved to grow the muscles on a guy like Samuel. It was just the best and the coolest thing he could do with his powers. So, he was always looking for other guys to grow. Luck had it that two other boys walked up, taking the two lounge chairs right next to Samuel.
    “What’s up?” one boy named Eric said.
    “Hey…” the other boy, Freddy, mumbled.
    “Let’s see if we can get a bit of a tan, first, before we get in the pool!” Eric suggested.
    Both boys were about 16 years old, about a year older than Ty, but Ty still recognized them from school although they were probably in a higher-grade level than him.
    Eric had black hair, but he was over 6 feet tall where Freddy was shorter and a little pudgy with blond hair. Neither boy was really all that muscular. They both looked at Samuel and they glanced at him with envy since Samuel was the biggest, most buff kid at the poolside at that moment.
    Freddy and Eric took turns rubbing sun tan lotion on their arms, legs, back and chest before they lay down on their lounge chairs. They put on their dark sunglasses and dozed off. Samuel was on the opposite end of the three and he had dozed off. Ty was laying next to Samuel. He looked up and glanced over, seeing the three other boys next to him. He made a silent wish for the muscles of all three boys to start growing by a pound for every minute they lay there.
    Very slowly, Eric and Freddy started to bulk up. Eric started to add muscle nicely to his long legs and arms, where Freddy began to add muscle rather quickly to his chest and his abs became very well-defined. His arms and legs became nice and tones as well. Of course, Samuel bulked up even more so, becoming bigger and stronger everywhere after Ty had made him Hulk out earlier. Samuel was now beginning to resemble a pro bodybuilder with his incredible physique.
    After thirty minutes had passed, Eric woke up, feeling stronger and heavier.
    “Fred! Freddy!” he said, waking up his pal.
    Both boys slowly got up and examined their bodies.
    “Dude! Were we this buff before?”
    “Where did all this muscle come from?”
    “I must have added like thirty pounds of muscle!”
    “Holy shit, I’m strong!”
    Both Eric and Freddy started to flex their arms and legs, waking up Samuel, who now got up and towered over the two boys with his size and bulk.
    “Damn! I’m massive!” Samuel grinned, flexing his gigantic arms.
    Ty just grinned since he was now surrounded by these three big, Hulking muscle dudes.
    “You guys wanna go for a dip in the pool?” he suggested before all three hunks dove in.
