    She looks at the door almost about to leave the house as a voice inside her compells her to call jennifer, she has barely any memories of her former life left but what little is left is doing all it can to get some form of controll over her.
    she walks up to the phone and calls up Jennifer, it rings for a few second before Jennifer picks up, H-hello ? she sounds slightly worried. Hi jennifer! Kylie here, we got alot to talk about, how about we met at the park in an hour ?
    Jennifer is confused by this request but she giggles she has to see this! sure kylie...i meet you at the park in one hour, Great! see you there then, bye bye jenny. they both giggle as they both hang up at the same time. This is going to be fun.

    An hour pass and Jennifer is sitting on a park bench checking her phone as she hears the sounds of heels closeby she looked up to see kylie in the same clothes she had transformed into. Jennifer looks at her, K-kylie ? she nods, Hi Jennifer, Jennifer grins you look alot better than i thought you would. Kylie shrugs and sits down next to her, I still remember my old life but honestly, i got so many new memories as Kylie now so i guess i should thank you ? Jennifer giggles thanks i guess ? they both smile as kylie grins and looks at her, Transform or dare, Jennifer! Jennifer looks at her shocked! you can't be serious ? oh my god...fine, but this will be the final transform or dare ever made! Kylie shrugs maybe ? who knows.
    Jennifer thinks about what to chose but unfortunately for her she runs out of time, Kylie giggles seems like you get both Jenny! Jenny sighs fine, let us first do your little transformation first then.
    Jenny nods slowly and looks at Kylie, Kylie ponders for a bit, Well you gave me this wonderfull life with new memories, she blushes slightly, how about.....Kylie thinks for a while untill she gives Jennifer a slightly evil look you made me like this so how about we make you the same ? Jennifer looks at her, W-what ?! Kylie smiles, it will be fun so it is all set i want jennifer here to turn into a Thirty year old woman, just like me except..she giggles, you will be black with long brown hair!
    Not before she is finish saying that Jennifer quickly gets of the bench and looks at Kylie, she looks furious but at the same time she looks confused, she looks at her hands as her clothes starts to change into the same type of buisness attire kylie is wearing, her skin changes from pale white to dark skinned, she starts to age and her chest size grows slightly, she pants heavily as the changes finally stops and she looks over at Kylie.
    Kylie smiles and walks up to her and smiles, Jennifer sighs Fine, what about the dare ? Kylie smiles for your dare...Well, i dare you to ask me transform or dare! Jennifer looks at her but then feels compelled to ask her so before she can ask her why she says it, Transform or dare Kylie, Kylie giggles Transform of course.
    Jennifer is now confused and looking at her, okay....kylie walks over to her and gently strokes her cheek, Jennifer looks at her W-what are you doing ? Kylie smiles, for my transformation i want to become a black woman just like you Jennifer.
    Jennifer gasps and looks at Kylie, K-kylie! Kylie smiles please ? Jennifer looks at her but gives in, i want Kylie to become like me.
    Kylie's skin goes from white to Black, her make-up changes slightly to not look to strange for her aswell. Kylie smiles and walks up to Jennifer and kisses her gently on the cheek, Jennifer looks at her as memories starts to change, she forgets her life as jennifer and new memories of Racheal comes to her, she grew up with Kylie, they went to same school, same high school liked the same stuff and were good friends, they went to the same college where they started to fall in love for real and as time went on they eventually become girlfriends and made a hugely succesfull Clothing-line.

    Three months has passed since that day, Kylie and Racheal are at home snuggling up in bed togheter, Racheal gently kisses Kylie on her lips and gives her a lovingly smile, next weeks is the wedding Dearie, Kylie smiles at her and kisses her back, mhm...looking forward to it ? she nods quickly, yes! love you forever! they both giggle as they cuddles up close togheter.
