    Sure enough the prying eyes turned out to be new invaders looking to take a claim to the land. Turns out it was a small army of Wild Boars much more fit and bigger than Porky. They were armed with weapons whereas Porky and Svelte were just two pigs living a quiet life. The wild boar were rugged black and brown pigs. Capturing pigs of another species would be ideal for their slaves and so forth. They were unarmed and naked and ready to be taken for ownership. The female would be ideal to breed a misraced or so forth. The invaders waited till the two pink pigs were sleeping and suddenly the two pigs were ambushed by the boars. Porky tried to put up a fight, but only got a lash by the whip and warned not to resist or else his mate would get it. Suddenly a collar and leash would put on both pink pigs and as svelte was refusing to go, a big slap with the whip from the boars across her fat behind convinced her, that these pigs were not messing around. They took both pigs on a pole and tied them to swing upside down, and then the pigs had apples shoved in their mouthes and they were carried away. On the way to the tribal ground, the male was tortured and beaten, while the female was given some kind of potion to intoxicate her and mess with her memory. Once they arrived in front of the Chief, the village celebrated. They captured two pink pigs. Both were sparred their lives, but it would be Porky who would be fattened up and used anyway he wanted, while Svelte would be fattened up and used to breed for the tribe to make litters of piglets along with the other wild sows.
