    invisible fairy circle into winged big firebreathing dragons.
    Plain and simple.
    So Nova started to turn around,to avoid this accursed place at all cost when one of the fairies,in a voice that sounded conversational
    told her to come closer and she'd tell Nova where her dad was.
    That stopped Nova in her tracks.
    Despite the 13 years that had passed since his apparent death Nova
    had never gotten over his fate and so
    Nova slowly edged back towards the fairies and after what she knew was a false promise not to transform her
