    Olivia On Days Off

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago AR TG Toddler Male to female

    “Actually, I want to be Olivia for a little while longer,” Olivia said.

    “How much longer?” Kimberly asked.

    “Well, I have tomorrow off work, so tomorrow at least.” Olivia shrugged. “Maybe my new tradition for days off can be that I turn into Olivia.”

    “I won’t be available every day you have off, you know,” Kimberly said.

    “I can always call a babysitter for those days.” Olivia stuck her thumb in her mouth, sucking on it happily. “I’ll turn back into Oliver tomorrow night before I go to bed.”

    “So you want to be Olivia overnight and all day tomorrow?”

    “Yep!”

    “Hmm.” Kimberly frowned thoughtfully. “I don’t have school tomorrow, so I can babysit you again.”

    “Can you babysit me overnight?”

    “Sure! But when you’re back to Oliver, you’ll have to pay me for it.”

    “No problem.” Olivia suddenly got a look of intense concentration. She began to grunt forcefully. A putrid smell filled the air. After a few moments, she let out a sigh of relief. A soft hiss soon followed. “I need another change.”

    “I can tell. You went number one and number two.”

    “Yep!” Olivia said proudly. Kimberly chuckled. She brought Olivia to the bathroom, where she began to change her diaper yet again. “Can you tuck me in when you’re done?”

    “Of course,” Kimberly said, tickling Olivia’s tummy. Olivia giggled. Kimberly finished changing Olivia’s diaper, then brought her to her bedroom, where she tucked the toddler into a bed that was far too big for her. Olivia promptly curled up and closed her eyes. Kimberly kissed her forehead. “Good night, Olivia.”

    Kimberly exited the bedroom, turning the light off on her way out. She pulled out her phone and texted her parents that she had an emergency overnight babysitting gig. Her parents texted back that they were proud of her work ethic. From there, Kimberly began to get ready for bed herself, thinking on how she wanted to spend tomorrow with Olivia.
