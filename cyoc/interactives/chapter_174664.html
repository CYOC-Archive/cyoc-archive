    The Zoo

    Vicky takes you on a road trip

    “Good morning Princess,” Vicky said in her typically upbeat voice as she walked over to you, “we have a very busy day ahead of us. Now come with me quickly, we are going on a little road trip.” You were slightly taken aback by this. Where was Vicky taking you? Was it the stud farm or somewhere worse?

    Upon noticing your anxiety, Vicky simply stroked your neck soothingly. “Now, now, Princess. You had better behave yourself or you’ll be in even more trouble. As promised, you will start making amends for all of the disruption that you caused yesterday. Unless I’m mistaken five of my colleagues were hospitalised, three horses needed to see the vet and, worst of all, we tragically lost one of our broodmares…”

    Your ears perked up when you realised that Vicky was talking about your girlfriend Jessica. You started to panic as you remembered that you hadn’t seen her since she was taken away by the attendants last night. You desperately hoped that she was alright, now that you had experienced the Zoo’s cruelty first hand.

    Upon noticing your reaction, Vicky simply smiled as she stroked your cheek soothingly. “Don’t worry Princess, Jessica left here safe and sound earlier this morning. I even took a video, just to put your mind at ease.” With that, Vicky whipped out her phone and showed you a short film. True to her word, the clip did show Jessica getting into her car and driving away. Seeing this made you sigh in relief; at least she was safe. Maybe she would even be able to free you and get this terrible place shut down. It was a long shot but you needed something to cling on to.

    “Of course,” Vicky continued after she noticed your spirits being uplifted, “Jessica’s freedom did come at a Price. She agreed that she would not do anything to undermine our business. That means no Police, no court cases and no questions asked.” You whinnied in dismay as your brief flicker of hope was snuffed out in an instant.

    “Moreover,” Vicky said with a grin, “she also agreed, after some… persuading, to help us make up for the broodmare and all the potential foals that she took from us. She didn’t want to at first, but when she realised that you would be sold to the glue factory if she refused... that was when she finally started to see things from our point of view.”

    You shifted about anxiously as Vicky’s words increasingly made you panic. What had they done to Jessica last night? What exactly had she told them?

    “Yes, your darling girlfriend was able to provide us with some very interesting information. Information about you Princess!” You backed away from Vicky as she said this with an evil smile. You dreaded to think what sadistic punishment this wicked woman had prepared for you.

    “Now,” Vicky said as she shifted into an angrier tone, “you are going to pay for humiliating me in front of my colleagues. Soon you will wish that you were just some stallion’s fucktoy at the local stud farm. You will see what happens when you cross me.”

    Hearing Vicky’s various threats made you whinny loudly and stomp your hooves. But rather than being intimidated, the sadistic woman simply smiled in her typically unnerving way. “Oh Princess, must I really come up with another load of punishments for you? Now that you’re a horse for good wouldn’t it be wise to behave yourself?”

    You bristled a bit upon being reminded that you were now permanently trapped as a dumb mare. However, you also sensed that Vicky wasn’t bluffing when she suggested that your life could get far worse if she wanted it to. With this in mind you snorted sadly as you lowered your head submissively.

    “That’s what I thought,” Vicky said as she patted your forehead, “now let’s get you loaded into the horsebox shall we?” You simply snorted in reluctant acceptance as you let Vicky fit you with your bridle. Then she proceeded to lead you out of your barn and over to where the Stables’ horseboxes were parked.

    Your anxiety continued to intensify as you climbed up the ramp and entered the small trailer. This fear of what was to come only intensified when the ramp was closed and the vehicle towing the trailer started to pull away.

    Before long you were heading down the road, nervously awaiting whatever torments Vicky had in store for you…
