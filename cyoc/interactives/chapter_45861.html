    After much thinking, Gloria came up with a decision and decided to go to the Age Wing. Sure the animal might be a fun experience, but it would be uncomfortable walking around in four legs. And the Transgender Wing, that would be a little disturbing. So she thought the Age Wing would be a much better option. So she waited for the tram to the Age Wing and entered it once it got there. The ride took five to ten minutes upon getting there and it was much soothing for the moment. Once the tram stopped, Gloria ext the cars and enter the main lobby of the Age Wing. When she did that, she was completely surprised at what she saw.

    The lobby of the Age Wing was really fantastic. It was a round circular room with marble flooring, white columns, and a huge hourglass fountain that drains of water on the top and drops into the bottom. Once the top is empty the hourglass turns over and starts the process again. She looked around the lobby and discovered many different people in different ages. Some were kids, most were teenagers, others were normal, and the rest were that of the elderly. Gloria guess that These are guest that have been regressed and progressed into different stages and there’s no telling who’s really a child, a teenager, a adult, or an old person. No matter, at least she would have a good time in this wing no matter what.

    She got her carryon luggage and approached the elevator to head up to the seventh floor. After leaving the elevator, she walked down one of the long hallways to find her appropriate room. Finally, after a long walk, she approached the door marked 728 and inserted the key into the slot. Once she opened the door and entered her new home, she looked around in the room so see what surprises in store for her. Sadly however, there wasn’t much.

    The room was just like any other hotel room, with a master bedroom, small kitchen, bathroom, closet for her clothes, a wide screen TV, the works. She sighed to herself, as she was a little disappointed at the room compared to the rest of the hotel. “I thought I was going to have a room so elegant.” Gloria said a little bummed, “Oh well, I guess this will do.” She approached the bed and placed her suitcase on the small hammock. As she was heading the bed, there was an envelope resting on her pillow with her name on it. Curious by this she picked up the envelope and opened it up to reveal a letter then reading it. It says…

    Dear Gloria,

    First of all, allow me to welcome you to the Transformation Resort. I hope this trip is worth it. Now I’m pretty aware that you have chosen the Age Wing of the resort. To that end let me give you some rules and advice.

    As you already know, this resort can transform their guests into anything and anyone they want. The Age Wing in particular can age or regress the guest into whatever age they want. But of course there are a couple of ways to do that. The room your in is one of them. It can help you change your age just by thinking about it. All you have to do is to picture the age you want to be and the room will do it for you. If you don’t want to change your age in your room, there are other things in the wing that can suit you better. All of our restaurants have a special ingredient on their food for appropriate ages, eat them and you will regress to that age. Our shopping center has different ways in changing your age. We got a pool and a spa for just regression, like a Fountain of Youth. And lastly we have some entertainment to help accommodate your age changes.

    Just a bit of a warning before you start. Age does have a limit from zero (newborn age) to a hundred. If you go past a hundred, you will instantly die from old age. If you go below ten-years-old a babysitter or a substitute mother will be assigned to the room to take care of you. Don’t worry if you regressed past newborn, you would be instantly inside the woman we would assign you with. But if you regressed past concievement, you will not die, only become just an egg and will need to wait to be reconcieve again. And for the clothing and other necessities in your luggage, don’t worry. When you change your age, you clothes will change to match that age.

    Once again, thank you for choosing the Transformation Resort and I hope you have yourself a safe and wonderful stay.

    From: The Chair of the Transformation Resort.

    Gloria read the letter fully and was quite impressed at what it says. From her standpoint, she can change her age in her room and have a babysitter or a sub mom incase she gets to young. Not only that, but she can also go to a restaurant, shop, pool, or entertaining venue and get changed in age there. She’s so pumped by this that she decided to start this experience now. What should she do first?
