    Shannon watched from the operating table as the doctor went about his business making phone calls and preparing for their trip. Werebears she could deal with, even werebirds, but now there were talk of even more mythical creatures, weredragons! She growled in anger, grabbing his arm before he could inject the boy.

    "Relax." Doctor Mark said calmly turning to look at her with reassuring eyes. "The Dragons are very secretive creatures, the boy is not a were, this will just be a simple injection that affects his short term memory, he won't be able to make connections between the faces he has seen and anyone he has met in the past."

    Shannon's relaxed her grip as Doctor Mark pressed the needle into Noah's upper arm and pressed the plunger in. "Does this mean if he sees me as a human, he won't recognize me from school? He wont recognize any of us? Anna? Kaida?"     "The weredragons are powerful, if they will it, they will remove it from his system, otherwise by Friday he won't be able to remember the faces of anyone that was in the van with."
