    Mad Science

    A different way to get rid of someone

    added by Tale Spinner 5 years ago AP BM S

    Yesterday was a fun day. A 5 year old girl bouncing around having fun in a park with a few friends. It was your birthday and you were looking forward to kindergarten, you were already able to read simple books! Your grandmother had given you the cutest stuffed dolphin, and after a nice bath and getting ready for bed you went to bed peacefully in warmth and plush. Regaining consciousness however wasn't quite as easy. Your whole body was in a dull aching sensation and everything smelt sterile. Opening your eyes you saw a lot of whiteness, and there was a beep occurring at regular intervals. You could hardly move a muscle and your head was only able to turn to the left and right a little bit. After awhile of waiting a nurse came inside ready to do some maintenance and then saw that you were awake. Her eyes wide she started walking quickly to get someone. A little while later having been left with your simple thoughts and wondering what had happened. Then a man in a white dr uniform came in and said "Ms. Clearwater your awake. Don't worry your safe, President Reece made sure you were in a more suite like hospital room. Once some of your wounds have healed a little more you'll be able to enjoy your stay a bit more. Is there anything I can do that would make you feel any better?"

    In pain, not understanding what's happening, and just scared muttered "where's mommy? I want to go home..." The Dr. was a little confused. muttering Ms. Clearwater? then said clearly "What's the last thing you remember?"

    You said "I was in the park playing with friends... there was this party, grandma gave me a stuffed dolphin..." your thoughts were getting a bit more lucid and you noticed your voice was a little more deeper than usual. The Dr. took out a notepad and started scribbling something down.

    He said "I'm going to examine some of your injuries... Most of them were mostly superficial... any other questions?"

    You asked "Are you going to give me any shots?" The Dr. scribbled down something else and then checking certain areas.

    The Dr. said "No, no shots. you've been asleep for awhile and most of your injuries have healed... i'll be slowly getting you out of most of this stuff, but you'll still be sore so just stay in bed... Can you tell me how old you are?"

    As the nice man was doing some stuff around you you said "I just turned five yesterday... I think."

    The Dr. said "Well... i'm not entirely sure how to explain things... however it's been over twenty years. You became the assistant of a very powerful woman. However something happened and now... well you think you are a five year old." You feel like you could move your body and it felt a lot heavier, then he removed the neck restraints. Looking down at your body there were scars around your arms or so, but you were a lot bigger and your chest were kind of like your mothers. You moved your long arms awkwardly and felt the squishy things for a second, before the aching feeling came back. The Dr continued "We'll do a brain scan when the aching goes away... In the mean time i'm going to have to make a few calls." The Dr. then left after giving you the television remote. After having the cartoons on for awhile and wondering where your favorites were...
