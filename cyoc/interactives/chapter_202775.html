    As Jane’s finger hits the mouse button to confirm, the door opens and she sees Jeff start to walk in. As he sees her, his eyes grow large in shock.

    Jeff hesitantly asks, “What are you doing in here?”

    “Um…” Jane knows she shouldn’t have been in her younger brother’s room. “I needed a charger.”

    “It’s on the counter downstairs.” Jeff responds with some caution in his voice. “Did you touch that?”

    Before she could respond, the machine, the Chronivac, begins to hum as a small needle springs out and quickly jabs Jane in the elbow.

    “Ow!” She screams as she spins back to the machine. “What the-“

    “What did you do!” Jeff interrupts as he pushes past his sister. Dread fills his voice as he says, “Oh no…”

    “What?” she asks tentatively as her body starts to itch all over. Reflexively she starts to scratch herself as she adds, “I’m really itchy.”

    “Sis…” Jeff says trailing off as he looks at the screen as he continues, “… you’re turning into a fox costume.”

    “Impossible!”

    The itching intensifies as she sees hair growing along her arms.

    “No…” Jane says as she stops her itching and rubs her hand over her arm. A comforting contentment fills her mind as her new fur is pet. With a start she adds, “It’s fur!”

    Having to see what’s happening, Jane pulls her shirt and pants off revealing her entire body is covered in fur just like the preview! Knowing her fur makes her bra and panties pointless, she removes them as well.

    As she straightens again, she sees Jeff who’s watching her transformation intently. With a glance she can see he also has a bulge in his pants.

    “Really?” She asks with disapproval.

    “You did this to yourself! And you’re the one getting naked in my room!”

    “Furry sisters get you off?”

    “Furry women, yes.” Jeff says with disbelief.

    What are you saying!? she mentally yells to herself. She never teases Jeff. Yet the idea of him getting off over her made her excited. She wanted…

    Numbness alerts Jane to her next change as she feels a lifeless weight developing at the base of her back. Turning around to show Jeff her back, they watch as a long furry tail grows from Jane’s back. As it nearly touches the ground it bulks out like a balloon being filled with air.

    “I have a tail!” Jane says with glee as she reaches around and picks it up. It’s soft to the tough and filled with squishy stuffing. As she tugs and squeezes it she can’t feel anything register with her mind.

    A stray thought crosses Jane’s mind as she examines the tail, I’m not complete…

    The same lack of feeling comes to her hands and feet as she watches her fingers grow fatter and her thumbs be absorbed into her hand. Looking up at Jeff who’s watching the same thing with mouth agape, she sees his eyes meet hers.

    “Does it hurt?” He asks.

    “Not really. Everything feels numb.”

    Reaching out a hand, Jeff takes a newly formed paw and sighs. “You’re becoming a costume sis. Your hands are just foam filled now.”

    “Will I be dead?”

    Shaking his head, Jeff says, “I don’t know. I don’t think-“

    Silence fills the room as her ears become numb. Trying to reach for her head reveals she can’t move anymore as her vision becomes blurry as she falls toward her brother.

    Another stray thought crosses Jane’s panic filled mind, I have to be worn!

    Gathering all her energy she tries to yell.

The only thing that has any density is the costumes head where his sister’s eyes are looking around then up at him. This is really freaky!. From the top of his vision he can see two pointy fox ears are growing from his sister’s head.
