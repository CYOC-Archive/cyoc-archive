    You start to move towards the female deer and you feel a sensation run through your new deer body. As you move closer the sensation increases. Then your antlers feel wierd. They feel like they are getting smaller and moving back into your head and not only that but you feel like you are smaller. You relize this is doing this as you get closer to the female deer, so you stop and you stop shrinking.

    You look at a nearby lake and see that you had become a younger deer. Your massive antlers have become short antlers and you are smaller than you were before. Then the urge to go towards the doe increases and you move more towards the doe. You look at your reflection again and notice your antlers are now small antlers that look like bumps and you realize that you are even younger.

    Worried about how young you cold become you stop.

    Man, it seems like everytime I get closer to her I get turn into younger deer. What if I walk away from her, maybe Ill get older.

    You try to walk away but you still have the urge to walk towards her more, so you continue to walk towards her and you feel yourself shrink more and your antlers finally sink back into your head.

    By the time you get to the doe, you realize that you are probably a baby dawn (a young deer) and you get an instinct to cuddle up with the doe.

    You realize you can still talk even though you are a baby deer.

    What happened to me?

    But why was I an adult before.
