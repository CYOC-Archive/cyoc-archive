    Inquire about your Faun Stepmother

    added by Anonymous A year ago A BM S O Mythological

    You just stand there stunned as you look at your stepmother who had changed before your eyes into a white fur deer like creature, a faun or satyr she called herself. Your brain was trying to process the information as she sat before you, studying you.

    “Why?” you finally ask as she looks at you. “Why are you here? If this isn’t some kind of trick, why are you here?”

    “Well first I am happy you aren’t screaming your head off or running away, they warned me that was not only possible but probably.” Stated Christian “As for why, long ago, long before I was born humans hunted fauns like me and other magical creatures to the point of near extension. We tried to hide among you humans, using spells to appear human. That is where the idea of werewolves comes from, they are in truth wolf like all the time but appeared human by magic. Still, it wasn’t working, so we made our own parallel world, a world of magic where we could live in peace.”

    “So why are you here then?” you inquire

    “Well, we found out that humans hunted us to the point where even if we where now safe from humans our numbers where too low we where doomed to die out from lack of numbers.” Stated Christian “There where now only 50 some Satyrs and Fauns where we used to number in the tens of thousands.”

    “DNA wise that wouldn’t be enough to support a population, too much inbreeding would cause a lot of problems.” You stated as Christian smiled and nodded her head.

    “Yes, well we didn’t know about DNA back then, but we knew it wasn’t enough, so a method was made to turn humans into mythical creatures like us to help repopulate.” explained Christian as she stood back up from the sitting position when she did you noticed more changes as she began to walk around you her hooves hitting the walkway.

    She was much taller Christian had been tall for a women being tall enough to look you in the eye and you are 6’2 but now you were near her bust line. She had to be at least seven foot tall now, you also notice the tip of a small tail which looked like a white tail deer tail only it was black. You noticed the same white fur which was on her legs was also on the back of her shoulder blades and you guess it ran down her whole back.

    “So we took humans, over the centuries we needed them less and less as we restored our numbers to a level which we could support ourselves with ease.” Explained Christian

    “So why are you here…you haven’t answered my question.” You inquire

    “Well…we are still worry about inbreeding because so much of our population came from such a small source. It is hard for some species to find mates which they aren’t related to in some way or another.” Explained Christian “So we come to the human world and…”

    “And sleep with people to get pregnant! Are you just using my father as a sperm bank!” you snapped “I thought you loved my father.”
    Christian’s ears lowered a bit as sadness washed over her face.

    “I do love him and you, which is why I am showing you my real form.” explained Christian “You don’t understand…you and your father is coming with me to my world, a world where I can live openly.”

    “You are going to turn me into a faun?” you inquire

    “We normally call males satyrs and females fauns.” explained Christian “I mean if you want to be a woman, I have heard they can arrange that when you change. I am not homophobic if your in the closet and our world if very open to that.”

    You didn’t really listen to the whole gender change information as realization hit you as you understand, she is going to take you away from everyone you know aside from your father and turn you into a faun/satyr.

    “How…how can you get away with something like this?” you inquire “People would notice when people go missing?”

    “From what I have been told the extraction teams our government uses have multiple different methods.” Stated Christian “Blood clones are the most used, they are copies of humans with no souls and then place them in car crashes, house fires and so on…”

    “And no one would be the wiser.” You finished as Christian nodded her head. The police would find this blood clone, a copy of whomever was taken, their DNA would match and that would be it. A tragic accident claimed someone, the community would morn and move on all the while the real people taken would be in this magical world.

    “Does…does dad know?” you inquired

    “Wouldn’t be fair to drop this on him before we tied the knot, he has known for a while.” Answered Christian as she finished walking around you and came back to where she started. “So what do you say Vinnie? What do you think? What do you think about becoming a Satyr”
