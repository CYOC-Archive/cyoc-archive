    Buttons T. Clown...Now THAT’S a name!

    As the transformation concludes, you start breaking into hysterics, a burst of overwhelming ecstasy coursing throughout your body. You just couldn't understand it, WHY IN THE WORLD DO YOU FEEL SO GREAT?! Normally one would be repulsed by such a forced metamorphosis but you?

    You couldn't get enough of it...

    Looking up with a smile you ask Mesmero for a mirror and he hands one to you without a second thought. Staring at your reflection, you couldn't help but admire your new self. You look so funny! You always hated the whole "monster clown" approach. No, you wanted to be a funny clown! One that would bring laughter and turn frowns upside down. Reaching up to your nose, you give it a good squeeze and loud honk is heard, prompting you to laugh.

    "You like?" Mesmero asks, with a hint of amusement.

    "Hyuk yeah!" You respond, your voice now sounding like a cross between Barney Rubble and Barney the Dinosaur. "Except for one thing..." You point toward the bald spot on your head. "Get rid of dat. I want a full top o’ blue!" Nodding, Mesmero waves his wand and poof! You're now sporting a full blue afro. The sensation makes you laugh and you continue looking into the mirror. It was perfect...you were perfect. By all accounts, you were ready to start your new life as a Bonafide Circus Clown...

    Except something was missing...

    A small portion of your former self still lingered, refusing to accept the new changes. As such you were privy to the many memory alterations, especially concerning your family. Your grizzled trucker of a father was now a clown, as was your managerial mother but these did not bother you. The intoxicating joy of your transformation made it near impossible to feel remorse.

    But your friends...

    You and your girlfriend Christine have been going steady for a year now and you couldn’t bear to separate from her. Shy she may be, she was still the best thing that ever happened to you after graduation and the thought of starting your new career without her was unacceptable! She simply had to experience this! You knew it in your heart, once the change took hold, she’d take to it like a bee to honey...

    A clown duo...you’d both be the best thing to ever hit the circus scene! The funniest jesters since Emmet Kelley!

    But why settle on a mere duo? Your thoughts drift to your buddy John. You and he have been pals since childhood and though you drifted apart briefly in high school, you managed to meet up again as adults. Not only was John hard worker but he was also engaged to his high school sweetheart Ruthanne. Though from the poor side of the tracks, Ruth was working her way through the managerial ladder of a business firm. The four of you were tight, always hitting up the bar Friday evenings...

    As you ponder more and more on the subject your smile grows wider. What was the fun of being a clown all by your lonesome self? Jeez, why not have a group? The thought causes you to break out in laughter. You look up at Mesmero and ask...

    “Hyuk! Think I can call a couple friends? Heh, they’d get a kick outta this!!”
    Mesmero just smiles and answers: “Why not? The more the merrier.”

    Smiling, you pull out your cell, a ridiculously oversized yellow toy phone, but just before you head for the contacts, you see Mesmero place his wand on it...

    “Who do you intend to call?” He asked.
    “My girlfriend Christine and my friends, John and Ruthanne!” You respond enthusiastically. “They’ll be GREAT clowns! Just you watch!” Mesmero just shakes his head. “Three? That’s a bit of an effort for yours truly.”

    He gives your phone a good tap with the wand before moving onto a bottle of seltzer and the back pocket of your trousers (in which you concealed a pie). Smiling devilishly, he elaborates.

    “Call one, pie one, or splash one. Either way, once you make em laugh, they’ll join the fold...”

    You nod understandings. Looking at your phone, you wonder who you should call first?

    What do you do now?
