    "Of course." the program wizard replied. "For it to work, though, I'll need the two of you to sit up straight in your chair, with your respective arms hanging off to the sides. Once you're both ready, we'll begin."

The crease deepened to the point that both sets of arms and shoulders were just barely connected by a thin layer of skin, the crease itself stopping just short of each twin's neck.     "Hehe...you got a point there." Jerry sheepishly replied back, running his front hand through his hair while his rear hand and forearm rested on the arm of the chair. "All the same, I'd say our new arms turned out great. What are your thoughts?"
