    Even More Shoe Magic!

    added by Doofy the Clown 3 years ago BM I S O

    "Hit me up with that magical shit, man" she giggled, her shoe body just soaking in all the sensations of being worn, something she loved now, enjoyning her form wrapped around his big wide clown feet.

    He giggled, enjoying taking her down this road of magical discovery, one he knew she might have a hard time coming back from, but it was fine by him. He took out the magical dust and sprinkled it on the shoes again and watched as they began to glow once more, and stretch outwards and wider, going from what was a pair of sneakers, to clearly costume clown shoes, flat and wide, with a big bulbous front, the face on the front stretching out longer and sillier, the teeth getting larger and the nose getting much bigger on the center of the clown face on each shoe.

    "WoAH!" the shoes said, sounding even more cartoonish, "I'm like totally wacky clown shoes now."

    "I tend to lean towards sneakers, but I figured since you wanted to try out the shoe life, we'd go full clown." Roly Poly said with a chuckle as he wiggled his toes inside the larger footware. He then willed his own feet to stretch out a bit, filling the shoes once more, which made them moan a bit.

    "More." they said, the voice echoing from both faces on his feet.

    "Well, I dunno if that's a good idea, we've already taken you pretty far." He stated.

    "Come on man."

    "Well, okay, how about something simple. How about I change the color you are." he said. "You are purple right now and we can play with that."

    "Sounds like a good time to me!" the shoes goofily agreed.
