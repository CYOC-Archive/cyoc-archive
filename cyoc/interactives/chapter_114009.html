    So Dr Kildare,expert on using medicines,surgery and 9
    other approved and some highly criminal techniques for stopping and
    reversing a homo-furry tf,showed up at Margarets cell hours later
    with his big box of medicines and stuff,

    He took her pulse and her temperature.They were within accepted
    norms for a homo-furry tf.

    He was getting ready to give her the usual anti-Elixer medicines
    and pastes,but first asked her why she'd decked
    herself as a Unnatural,a DRAGON,one of the Ichor Fyre Disease
    touched.

    Basically he wanted to know why she'd traipsed about the Homo Pure
    Co. hallways impersonating a monstrous entity who's
    nearest cousins were flesh eating werewolves,zombies,
    ghouls,blood sucking vampires and other Servants of
    Darkness.

    Well now that she thought about it,Margaret agreed it had been a pretty stupid and
    depraved stunt.

    She and Dr Kildare talked for the next hour,whilst he kept shooting her
    up with powerfully concentrated concoctions,that unknownst
    to the doomed homo-furry dragoness,should have knocked her out
    with the third injection.

    Margaret then explained the idea had all begun when she
    and her uncommunicative sister Alice had talked about Alice catching
    Ichor Fyre disease through Ira's dabbling with a
    century old Extract making machine.

    "Gasp" Margaret realized "is that why Alice isn't answering her
    phone?"

    Kildare didn't want to go into whether Alice now lay
    in some unnatural sleep in a cave,but he
    took his eyescope and checked Margarets eyes.

    No glowing yellow specks blossoming in them.

    Yet.

    No slitted reptillian pupil.

    Yet.

    No stilled human heart within her chest.

    Yet.

    However Kildare sensed the stubborn refusal of
    Margarets tf to stop and go away wasn't something medicine
    could cure.

    So he gave her a flask of Holy Oil,a Cross and saw to it that
    she was released from prison within 3 hours.

    ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
    So Margaret had signed out her large bag purse,the one
    she'd used to tote the Dragon Servant of Darkness
    accessories she'd used for her gorey impersonation.

    The tote bag was empty,the accessories now flushed down a toilet
    and presumably flowing through the sewers to the Black Sea waste pipe outlet.]
