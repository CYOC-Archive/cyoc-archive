    Alice wanted to test out the reality changing capabilities of this new machine. She hated being in her twenties. It felt everything was just too slow. She wasn't advancing in her career fast enough it seemed like her publisher didn't like her because of her age. Instead of writing great books as she hoped, she is writing some basic fiction work and gossip pieces for a big-time magazine. While she is grateful for a decent job, she wishes she could skip through the grind and become the great author she hoped for. She just wants to skip through her twenties and become something else. With this machine she could achieve that and write her own fate.
