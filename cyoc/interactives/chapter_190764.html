    I went to get Himari's phone and immediately started her friend Touka. A long wavy haired beauty that had been friends with Himari for a long time. Always supportive while using her wealth to help out Himari while getting her into odd situations. In fact, she was the one who helped Himari cope with her bisexuality and became her girlfriend because she was into her from the start. The two were lovely with each other and I get to be a part of it.

    "Hey Touka-chan! It's Himari. I just thought today is a good day to show you something," I said with my best Himari impression.

    "Ah! Himarin! I'm on my way!" said a cute soft voiced girl. Followed by the sounds of something revving up.

    After some time, I see the one and only appeared in my room. The girl wore the same school uniform as me with her long hair let loose down her soldiers. The girl had a really warm smile as she was really excited to meet me. Her eyes then darted at the possession mirror that contained the head of Himari or me in this case.

    "Whoa! Did you do that?" Touka asked.

    "Well um... actually I found this weird mirror and um... watch this," I said as I went in front of the mirror. "Touka Fujiwara!"

    The head changed as her hair grew longer and darken. The face shifted a little until it resembled Touka. Touka's disembodied head floated there and appeared as though she was bliss and dreaming. The actual Touka held onto my shoulders and looked excited.

    "Wow! That's cool but I want to see your face again," Touka said. "Himari Takahashi."

    Himari's disembodied head appeared once more. Making Touka blushed as if she was meeting someone really really special. The little girl then noticed the plaque below that said ‘Say a name and live their fame’ and the girl repeated it.

    "Is something wrong Touka-chan?" I asked.

    "Hmm... I feel like I can reach into the mirror," Touka said as she has her hands out to reach the head.

    "Wait wait wait, before you do anything... why don't I explain the real purpose of the mirror?" I suggested.

    "Ah! That would be great!" Touka said.

    "Well um... it lets you possess people if you say their names and enter the mirror," I said.

    "Wow! Just like the time we swapped bodies with everyone," Touka said.

    Oh right, that was a thing. Then again, it's a thing that can happen in a magical girl series.

    "So um... before we possess anyone at random, why don't we plan it out?" I asked.

    "Right... I wouldn't want to possess my loving friend without permission," Touka said while blushing. That's her way and the manga artist way to say that Himari is her girlfriend.

    "I was thinking about possessing you too... please..." I said.

    "Since you asked nicely... just make sure you take care of me," Touka said.

    I nodded. I won't do anything to her since she was a freaking kid. Though if I got a chance at someone older. Perhaps.

    "I also thought about sensei as well," I said.

    "Oh... sensei does have a beautiful body. But with you inside, you'll make her more lovely," Touka said.

    "Though I don't mind if you possessed me," I said.

    "Oh gee, what to do..." Touka asked.
