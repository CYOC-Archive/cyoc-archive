    “I want to try one too,” said Ben. He looked through the options before selecting Bacchus Brew, deciding it was the safe choice, since he liked grape soda.

    “All right, I guess I’ll try one too,” said Cary, deciding on the Satyr cream soda. The three then depart and head home to hang out and play video games.

    “Dude, what’s up with your ears?” asked Ben, taking another sip of his soda.

    “What?” asked Aaron, reaching up to his ears. To his horror, they were longer and came to rounded points. Aaron lept from the couch and went right for the bathroom.

    Ben followed, breaking Cary’s concentration from the game.

    “Guys?” asked Cary as he followed the other two into the next room, finishing his cream soda in a large gulp. “What’s going on?”

    “What?” asked Ben.

    “We drank the soda too. Which means I’m going to turn into a satyr and you’re going to turn into a centaur,” replied Cary.

    “This is all my fault,” whined Aaron as he sat down on the edge of the tub.

    “If it’s any consolation, you actually look good as a donkey,” said Cary.
