    When the light went away you were in the middle of a dense forest. You took a moment to recall what just happened,"Okay, my name is Twilight, and I am the legendary dragoness of darkness, and flames. I represent night, and day too. However, I'm female too, this is so weird, but why is it so familiar. This body is so odd, but I remember being this way my whole life, but I remember being human, and male just as fondly." You began to recollect what you thought you should do, and remembered that the voice said to go to it. "Okay, I just need to go to this temple, and talk to this person, or whatever it is, and figure out who I am, and what I'm doing here." You said to yourself. Suddenly you heard footsteps coming from behind you, and you looked back to see what appeared to be a blonde furred fox with five tails, a kitsune.

    "So Twilight, it's been awhile how have you been, being dead and everything?" The kitsune asked as she sat down. You cocked your head to the side, and asked the kitsune,"Do I know you from somewhere? I mean I feel like I do, but I don't have any of my former memories." The kitsune then paused in awe, and then shook off, and said,"Sorry, I forgot you just woke up from death, and I guess that you want me to take you to the temple of legend too right?" The kitsune said,"By the way you'll hear this later but I'm Flux, legendary kitsune of electricity, and spiritual aura." Flux said. You nodded, and Flux continued,"Follow me, and about before I just wanted to know how you were doing. You are the first of us to actually be reincarnated into a new life, and then revived from your previous life back into this one."

    "Oh, it's weird I feel like both memories from both this life, and that life are during the same period, and I'm fond of both of them. As for the change it was painful, and actually even mor odd considering I was a male human." You said.

    "Oh, my that would be incredibly odd, and considering you were living the pain must have been excruciating for you. I can only imagine how that must have felt, if you have any questions to help you spiritually just ask me." Flux said

    "Well there is one, but it doesn't really involve anything spiritually, but have any others of us died at all?" You asked

    "Oh, yes I just woke up a few hundred years ago from death, by the way if you already didn't know we are immortal, and can't die from natural causes. If we die unnaturally we just come back after a fer thousand years. However, something went wrong with you and you were reincarnated, so me and a few other of us beasts got together, and pulled you back through time, and space. Personally I still don't think we should messed with time, but that's what we needed to do, and I like you so there's that." Flux said.

    You and Flux walked to the temple, and you let Flux guide you to a set of doors. You opened the doors to find that there was another one of the beasts waiting for you inside.
