You felt your skeleton start to crack and shift as your arms shrank slightly, until they were sleek and scrawny. Your fingers fused together to become three as black claws grew from them. Your spinal cord began to crack, putting your back in a full arch.

    You screamed as you felt your neck grow in size and elongate slightly. Soon, your nose and mouth started to push outwards. Your teeth became razor-sharp as your jaw and skull had completely reshaped while all your hair fell out.
    Your ears shrank into small holes on the sides of your head as your tongue grew longer and sharper. Your eyes moved to the sides of your head, their whites turning yellow as your pupils widened into slits, the last of your humanity slipping away.

    Finally, leathery scales covered your entire body. You were about to let out one last scream, but instead all that came out was an ear-piercing roar.

    Your mind began to cloud. You couldn't understand what was going on. However, the tiny humans gathered around you in utter shock looked rather appetizing. However, Ms. Tomlin pulled out a small stick with a red gem on the end, which made your mind clear up and settle down.
