    Susan draws out her phone out of her left pocket. She feels the cold blade of a pocket knife as she draws out the phone. She shows Louis the picture one. The picture shows a stone statue of a muscular person who is holding a spear. They head is the head of a cow. "Wow..." Louis exclaimed "That's a minotaur statue...this old settlement must really be something, you've stumbled unto riches Susan". Susan beamed as she switched to the next picture.

    It shows a archway "This is at the entrance of the temple". On top of the arch, large blue symbols are drawn across it, like magical words or a secret hymn. "It's the Greek alphabet" Said Louis as he points to the letters, The first one was a circle with a line crossing down it "G", the second was a spiral with a line horizontally crossed through it "R". The third was a "O" simple symbol. After that it got complex the next symbol was an upside down y with a vertical line going through it. "Oh I knew this one it's y" said Susan. "Groy?" said Louis mocking Susan. Susan sighed and then shouted "Its T isn't it". The sound sent static through the headset. "Ow" Louis said holding his ears. "Sorry" Susan said quietly".

    The last letter was another O. "Groto" said Louis as he stroked his chin trying to figure it out. Susan looked around trying to think of ideas. The rain hammered on the windows as well as in the jungle.

    The jungle was thick and dark, the grass was high and like mud it stuck it's prey to be eaten by the predators. Loud hissing and croaking could be heard around the light. It felt like it was getting closer and closer. Ominously the guy turned around with his lantern and joined the rest of the crew who were standing on the settlement of a building. "Definitely alone" said the guy in a Jamaican accent. "Good job Williams, scout out the rest of the area with Jenkinson's here and report back to me pronto" the solider looked away at Williams and walked into the darkness. William's cracks a smile "Dick" he says softly and walks into the distance.

    Jenkinson's stands behind a pillar, smoke rises as he pulls a cigarette out of his mouth and breaths the smoke out. The ash crackles as it falls slowly to the ground, getting distorted by the heavy rain the cigarette is put out. Jenkinson's yells loudly as he drops the fag on the ground. "Oi, language" says a Jamaican accent. Williams turns the corner and laughs at Jenkinson takes out another fag. "You done" said Jenkinson's. "Oh come on Jenkins have a little laugh in you". Jenkinson's looks nervously at Williams. "I don't like this place, it gives me the creeps. It feels like something's gonna jump out at me any second".

    William's face suddenly fills with horror, he tilts his head behind Jenkinson's and walks backwards "Jenkins, behind you" Williams says slowly. Jenkinson turns his head cautiously around. The pillar goes out of view and he looks into the darkness. Then he sees it isn't darkness anymore it's plant's, trees and-. Suddenly Jenkinson's gets pushed forward by something, he screams out loudly and turns around. He then realises it was all a joke, Williams laugh's hysterically at Jenkinson as he stands up drenched. Jenkinson's looks at him angry. "You should have seen the look on your face, you were terrified". "Yeah well you'll be terrified in a minute". Jenkinson's advances to Williams. William's puts his hand In front of him and exclaims "Oh I'm sorry Jenkin's but your okay and so am I so lets just go do this job the Commander gave us". He puts his hand on Jenkinson's shoulder and they walk off into the darkness.
