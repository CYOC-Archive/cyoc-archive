    Ty lay among the willows roots, his mind racing to figure out what was going on, why was he here, who put him here, who was he, the last question took him off guard, but then he began to panic, his now small limbs kicking and flailing amongst the roots, Ty had forgotten who he was!.

    It was upon this realisation that Ty began to cry, his infant body now taking its toll on his mind, the magic that reduced his body's age was now seeping into his mind, making his mental state younger and younger.

    Ty's cries echoes through the forrest as the light of the sun was soon replaced by the ghostly glow of the moon, by this point Ty's tears had run dry and his throat was horse and dry, but the rational part of him that remained knew that crying would be his only chance at survival if anyone passed by.

    But Ty's cries did not fall upon deaf ears, as four black cloaked individuals, all four of them were bear foot and were all clutching something beneath ther robes as they aproched the willow tree, when one of them heard Ty's weak crys, the figure said not a word as they then walked over and parted the roots to see the babe nestled within the trees root cradle.

    The figure then lifted a lit lantern to get a look at ty, and Ty could see ritch dark skin and Rose red lips emerge from the shadows of this person's hood, and before Ty could feel at peace at being found another figure emerged into view.

    "What are you doing, the godess gave us exact instructions, if we stop and get caught I swear....." came a female voice until the dark figure turned her head down towards Ty, whose only response was to reach both of his hands up towards the figure.

    The woman let out a sigh "fine, fine take him, but with the gift of the godess within us and now a babe to care for, let's hope that the village can't catch us" she said before despairing, the woman with the lantern then reached down and scooped Ty from the roots and into the night air "come now my blessing from the godess, we have plans for you baby boy" came the soft voice of the woman that now cradled you as she and the other cloaked figured rand into the night......
