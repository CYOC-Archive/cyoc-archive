    Movie Night!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 8 years ago A

    Alex, Zach, and Victor all came together at Victor's apartment to watch a film and stay for the night. Oblivious to the rare lunar phase, and also oblivious to Victor's werewolf gene, that had been dormant, they were in for a surprising night.
    ----------
    "So Captain Phillips or The Fault in Our Stars?" Victor listed off the movie ideas. "I don't want to cry a river dude, captain Phillips!" Alex exclaimed. "So did you hear about the rare lunar something that's happening tonight?" Zack said
    "Sure, saw it on the news this morning. Maybe we could go out and look at it after the movie." Victor put in.

    The night had begun as the group settled in on the couch. Just as Captain Phillips was about to be rescued, Victor started getting really hot and itchy. "Dude I feel off, do you think I'm o-" Alex glared at him and said "Shut the fuck up and watch. Dude you also need to shave, you look like you haven't shaved in a month!"

    Victor started feeling a pain in his hands. He looked at them and gasped. They were reshaping into paws. Audible cracks were heard as his nails fell out and claws came in "Fuck! Guys look!"
    Zack, who was pretty mad about all the noise, stopped and gaped in horror. "Victor what the hell, we need a doctor, Ill call the ambulance" Just as Zack was dialing, Victor let out a huge growl that made him shriek and drop the phone.

    Victors changes accelerated, his body turning into a full on wolf. Victors beast instincts turned on. He looked at his friends, and uttered the word, "Run."

    Alex tried the door first, which was jammed. "This shitty door lock is busted again." Zack and Alex were trapped. Zack looked at Alex, while he was reciting a prayer. "...While I walk through the valley of the shado...."

    "Fight whatever it is Victor. Fight it!" Zack urged.
    Victor was stumbling towards them. "I--- can't, I need to sto-" then in a more evil bestial voice " No, kill them.... KILL THEM." Back to his normal voice. "No I won't! They have been my friends forever. The evil voice replied "SILENCE, if you don't want them dead, then I will make them just like you! Victor replied, "No!"

    Victor jumped on Alex, and bit into his neck, then reared on Zack and bit his forearm. They both cried out in pain as they dropped. Victor, who had fought off the evil, looked at what he did to his nearest friends. Alex, who had been cowering in the corner, held his neck. Blood seeped from the wound. Zack, on the other hand, was holding his forearm and gazing off with glazed over eyes. "Oh my god, what have I done? I'm a monster, I'm a fucking monster."
    Alex heard his friends voice, and backed into the corner even further. Zack did the same. "Can someone... explain to me... what the hell... just happened... now." Zack just nodded, as he wanted that explanation too. "Well for starters, I'm a wolf" Alex did another signature glare and said, "No shit Sherlock, tell me something I don't know while I bandage my arm" Zack said. "Fuck me if I know, I'm sorry for almost killing you guys."

    "We'll nothing could possibly be worse" Alex said

    *Riiipppp* Alex grew a tail. "Shit" was all he could say before the changes enveloped him, his black hair sprouting out, Zack actually started with him, both grunting and groaning in pain. Now instead of one wolf, there were suddenly three. But now, Alex's and Zack's evil side was starting to show. Kill the other two, KILL THEM NOW. "Zack, I'm sorry it has come to this." They both furiously clashed. Rolling spitting biting all out warfare. It was not till Alex and Zach were exhausted there evil sides were let loose.

    "Lets never do movie night again" Victor said
