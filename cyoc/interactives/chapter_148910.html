    Jamie and Jackie heard a loud crash in the entry hallway and rushed over. When they arrived, the two cousins saw a pile of more boys and a now currently open front door. As the new self-invited visitors groaned while getting back on their feet, Jamie and Jackie smiled to themselves as they witnessed the door slam and seal itself shut just like it had already done for Colin and his four, two of which had been Jamie and Jackie's pre-transformed selves, friends only a few minutes earlier.

    "Jerry, remind me why we came to this place again?" one of them prompted.

    "Because Dan, Colin and his four friends are here, and I want to know what they're up to!" Jerry snapped back in annoyance.

    "Seriously!? That's why you got us to come here!?" another one spoke.

    "Yes, Chad." Jerry responded before turning to the person closest to him and said, "Um, Evan, could you check the door?"

    "Sure," Evan said with a nod. Like Lucas had earlier discovered, Evan found the door to be shut.

    "Guys," Evan said, "It's locked!"

    "What do you mean it's locked!" one of them asked Evan.

    "I mean it's locked, Louis!" Evan repeated.

    "Great! Now we're all trapped in this place!" One of them complained.

    "Yeah, Nick. But we're trapped with two really hot chicks as well," another added, having noticed Jamie and Jackie standing nearby.

    Nick froze and then said, "What."

    The boy who noticed Jamie and Jackie pointed to them and said, "Look."

    "Heyyyy, boys~ I'm Jamie!" Jamie said.

    "And Ah'm her cousin Jackie!" Jackie said.

    "How long have you been standing there?" Jerry asked.

    "We've been here the whole time," Jamie said.

    "Would ya kindly care ta introduce da rest o' ya'll boys, cutie?" Jackie asked the boy who had noticed her and her cousin.

    "Sure, I'm Tony," he said.

    "I'm John," another one said. Then he used his thumbs to point to the two that he was sandwiched between. "The guy to my left is Maurice and the one to my right is Perry."

    Maurice and Perry waved a little, causing Jamie and Jackie to giggle.

    Another boy walked up to them and said, "Hi, I'm Layne."

    "Hiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiii~" the cousins chorused.

    Tony then spoke up again and pointed to someone who looked just like him and said, "That's my twin brother Tyrone."

    Tyrone sighed and then said, "And those three are Wendell, Mitchell, and Dakota." Pointing to each one as he said their names.

    Jamie smiled and said to Jerry, "If you're looking for Colin, he and a few other boys are exploring this place right now."

    "Thanks," Jerry said.
