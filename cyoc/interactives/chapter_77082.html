    All in all, the Las Vegas excursion had been great for everyone. Steve and Janet weren't fighting anymore,
    Wendy felt a rekindling of romance with her husband, and the two lovebirds would now be going on a bizarre
    three month odyssey into the lower levels of Hollyweird during one of Wendy's favorite TV program's. The night

    prize was a new Ferrari 458 Spider. On a stroke of daring whimsy, he put some coins into the machine, and
    immediately won it. He turned to Janet behind him, who started giggling, cutely shrugging her shoulders in
    mystery as to whether she had wished for it to happen. Meanwhile, Wendy and Carl(who had reverted back to
    being the blonde eye candy twin sister “Carly”) returned their Big Brother applications over to Jon Adams-
    Reilly, who was now a little buzzed, and he in turn returned them over to his assistant Lynne. She herself
    then left the casino, clearly a little uncomfortable being around all the decadent behavior.

    “Looks like you two are all set” he said. “Sit down, and I'll pass along a little wisdom from the gods.”

    Jon was closely involved in casting all the big Reality TV shows. Well, the competition based ones anyway. He
    had good information to share to Wendy and Carly on what to expect.

    “First, you're going to be taken to a large hotel in Los Angeles. Nice, but not the ritziest place in town
    like this hotel is. Once you meet the people from the network, who will establish contact with you both very
    soon, they will then sequester the both of you. This means that they will temporarily separate you two from
    each other in different rooms, and neither of you will be allowed to talk to one another or come into face to
    face contact.”

    “What?” yelled a terse Carly. “I can't talk to my...”, he was about to say 'wife', but he corrected himself in
    time, “...sister?”

    “Not until the vetting is over. One at a time, you will undergo a physical, which isn't too bad since it's Big
    Brother and not a physically taxing show like The Amazing Race, but a thorough physical nonetheless. Then a
    psych test. Then an interview, and that's where things get really personal.”

    “What, are they going to ask what my bra size is?” asked Carly, trying to stay in character.

    “Maybe. It's usually about what you are going to be like on the show and what you'll be willing to do. Since
    you're both attractive women, a lot sexual related questions will come up, including the obligatory Will you
    flirt or use your body to get ahead? type questions. Standard stuff, really. It does get really personal
    though, so fair warning.”

    “Then what do we do?” asked Wendy, holding onto Jon's every word, grinning like a fangirl fantasizing about
    joining her favorite band.

    “Once you're done, you'll be analyzed and compared against other people in your Group Type. This means you two
    will be ranked against other hot female twins applying for the show. Like I said, I have a lot of clout, and
    you two I have a feeling will bring a lot to the table, so I wouldn't get nervous. You two are going to be
    fine. While you're waiting for the results to see if you are on the shortlist to go on, you can pretty much go
    around the hotel and the surrounding shopping area. The room is on their dime, and so is the room service, but
    you have to pay your own way to L.A.”

    “Well...I guess that's OK. Then what?” asked Carly, slightly pissed that all the money Wendy won would have to
    be used to fund the flights. And Carl hated airports anyway. Oh boy...

    “After you two are picked, you'll do a little pre-show junk. Just a little...”
