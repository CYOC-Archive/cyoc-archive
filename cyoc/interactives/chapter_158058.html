    The weekend passed quickly, Gloria and her friends now trying to get to know Yuki as the teen now talked of her mission and what her godparents had told her. Sitting in Gloria’s room on a Sunday afternoon they began to make plans on how to handle whatever was coming.“I think we’re going to have to talk with Merlin at some point,” Gloria stated. “Maybe he can tell us exactly what’s going on.”

    “But if our Merlin is different from the Merlin that sent Yuki back will it help,” Jackie asked before sighing. “Ugh all this time travel crap is giving me a headache.”

    “Well I thInk right now we should see what Yuki can do,” Felicia stated. “I mean, if she’s going to be here for awhile we might as well see what she can do.”

    “I can pretty much do everything you guys can do,” Yuriko said. “I mean Lynne and Morgan are probably more powerful but I can hold my own.”

    Naomi now studied her future niece carefully, rubbing her chin as she now tried to think of a question. “You said you can do everything we can do but you also said you know mind control,” she said. “What exactly does that mean.”

    “Pretty much what it sounds like,” Yuriko shrugged. “I can control people’s minds.”

    “I kind of want to see that,” Diane said sheepishly. Slowly but surely Gloria and her friends nodded in agreement, they wanted to see what exactly mind control looked like. “Okay I guess I can try,” Yuriko shrugged. “Only question is who?” Without speaking Gloria and her friends now slowly turned, staring at Sasha.

    “Why the hell are you guys looking at me,” Sasha asked. As it spdawned on her what they were thinking she lept to her feet. “Oh hell no,” she exclaimed. “I’m not having future girl screw with my mind.”

    “Cmon Sasha,” Felicia pleaded. “Look your the strongest one here. I bet she won’t be able to control you at all.”

    “So then let her try it out on someone that she can control,” Sasha countered. “Let her mess around with you or Jackie. Why doesn’t it have to be my brain she messes with?”

    “Sasha it will be temporary,” Yuriko said. “And I promise to turn you back to normal. Besides I could use a challenge.” Sasha frowned, her shoulders slumping as she stared at her friends. “You guys owe me big time for this.”

    Gloria and her friends now anxiously watched as Yuriko stared at Sasha intently. The Asian teen had said she needed to concentrate to control someone’s mind. Considering Sashas incredible will power it was going to take an incredible amount of magic to control the rebellious teen. At first it seemed as if nothing was happening. Yuriko had furrowed her brow, beads of sweat forming on her brow as she seemed to try and impose her will. “Nothings happening,” Diane whispered.

    Gloria and the others said nothing, their silence hanging heavy until Sasha slowly got to her feet. “Sasha,” Gloria asked. “Are you Okay?”

    “Yeah I’m fine,” Sasha said. “Don’t think it worked. Now excuse me for a sec.” Gloria and the others said nothing as the rebellious teen now walked towards Gloria’s closet. As she opened the door she began to rub her chin thoughtfully before raising her ring finger. The others now gasped as Sasha’s clothing began to change.

    Sasha’s T-shirt And pants began to shift on her body, the material merging into a single piece of cloth. The sleeves of the T-shirt vanishing as the jeans unraveled, resting just above her knees. As that was happening the others watched as Sasha’s clothing began to become more colorful. The black shirt and blue jeans color was brightening, transforming into a Pastel pink, complete with white trimming. Soon Sasha was dressed in a very girly looking sundress, a sundress Gloria immediately recognized as her own.

    “Not bad but it’s missing something,” Sasha said quietly. Looking down at her feet Sasha now began to transform her boots. The black color faded away, becoming a pearly white as they seemed to unravel and reform. As the boots reformed Gloria and the others watched as Sasha’s socks unraveled, seeming merging with her shoes, which were transforming into a pair of strappy, open toed, cute white heels. As the changes completed themselves the teens were astounded to see Sasha dressed in a very girly outfit.

    “When the hell did you decide to dress like that Sasha,” Jackie exclaimed.

    “What,” Sasha said defensively, her voice sounding somewhat flighty. “I’ve like always thought Gloria’s clothes were like totes cute. I just like, needed to see them so I could copy them. Hope you don’t mind Glo but Ive like SOOOO wanted to borrow that dress.”

    Gloria and her friends now gaped at Yuriko, the teen smirking before snapping her fingers. Sasha now shook her head as of coming out of a daze. “What’s going on,” Sasha said groggily. As she looked herself over her eyes went wide as she saw what she was wearing. “WHY THE FUCK AM I WEARING THIS!”

    “Sorry Sasha,” Yuriko smirked. “Figured the best way to show off my power was to make you all girly.” Sasha and the others said nothing, marveling at the fact that just for a moment the rebellious teenage girl had become debutante.
