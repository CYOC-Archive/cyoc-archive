    When the transformation was finished, Mary was nothing more then a helpless rabbit running around, looking for a place to hide. To think, Mary was just a fourteen year old human girl wishing to be an animal. And after looking into the forums, decided to permanently become a rabbit to be eaten. Not to mention calling her arch enemy, Sarah, to come and be the predator, to which she happily oblige. With the Chronivac, Sarah used it to become a nine foot boa constrictor to hunt. And now the two of them were predator and prey, with Mary's changes permanent and Sarah's changes up to four months. A little bit long, but she would assume she wants to enjoy herself being a snake for a little while. Now she is being hunted by her enemy, and was looking for a way to run away and hide from her.

    Mary hopped into the other room, her heart racing, she was scared, bumps and noises could be heard as Sarah was etching closer. Instinctively Mary hopped into the corner, not not realizing how big her home has gotten due to her shrinking frame. Wiping her nose with her rabbit paw she braced for what was to come.

    Sarah continued to make her way to where Mary had run off too, she found by flexing her back muscles she could push herself forward quite a bit faster. Pushing her head round the corner, she could see a bit of a mess along the hallway. She knew that it was Mary and chuckled mentally, knowing that she was bad at running and hiding.
    Flicking her forked tongue, she followed the trail, inching closer to her prey.

    Running away from her predator, Mary knew that she can't get away from Sarah and had to get out of the house. Looking around while running, she saw a set of stairs and turned to jump down them and reached the bottom floor.

    Sarah slithered down the hall and saw her heading down the steps, wondering where she was going. So she slivered down the steps to see where she went to.

    Mary was running around the living room and approached the kitchen to find her escape path. That's when she saw the dog door that leads to the backyard. Satisfied, she hurried to the dog door and jumped through it, leaving the house and entering the backyard.

    Sarah slithered down the messy path Mary left behind and saw it lead to the kitchen and saw the doggy door swaying back and forth. Seeing that, she knew that Mary wants to make the hunt more interesting.

    "Looks like that foolish Mary decided to escape to the woods. Huh. No matter, I will find her. And when I do, I will enjoy devouring her." she thought in her head. So she slithered through the kitchen and out through the doggy door. Deep down, what was suppose to be a simple hunt turned into an adventure for the two.
