    Mad Science

    14 year old Bobby Brown finds the device

    added 4 years ago I O Reality alteration

    Bobby Brown was a short guy with wavy blond hair and a cute young looking face. He was always picked on by his family. His step mom always bitched to him about everything, his father was always out or drunk, his older sister never acknowledged him and his younger step sister always tattled on him. He hated staying at home so would try anything to leave the house.

    He had found an old metal detector in a dumpster near his families apartment and was willing to give it a go rather than stay at home. He walked around the beach for a while but couldn't find anything. He rode his bike around town for a bit to multiple parks but again couldn't find anything. He finally rode to the school in desperation and finally found something interesting near a tree.

    It was a strange remote looking device with a button that read "select" and another that read "swap". There were a couple smaller buttons near the bottom but Bobby was interested in the swap button for now. What did that do? Bobby looked around to see if anyone was near to claim the object but he saw no one. It was just him, a grassy field, and a tree. HE lost all guilt about someone missing it and he thought about how to test this find.
