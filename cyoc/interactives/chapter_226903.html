    A Short-Lived Fun

    added by Elflover A month ago AR Mythological Magic Kid

    You decide to remain a kid. After all, it's an opportunity that is unique, right? You can enjoy being a kid and have a slice of life free of responsibilities. You can be totally carefree! You just need to find more decent clothes than underoos, though. But getting to more populated places will help. You can pretend to be a real kid and pretend your clothes were stolen by a bunch of bullies. Then you can do silly kid things and not be judged for that. That sounds like a great plan. Maybe there are a few attractions and rides around. Those would be fun to try out, maybe win a few prizes. You grab the map and start your way towards the more populated areas of the property. You're about to have a good time indeed! Who could blame a kid that is four or five anyway? So you make you way to what you plan to try out. You feel totally free and at ease.
    You feel great at first, especially at the start. You snatch from a store a short, T-shirt and a pair of tiny sandals. Your guilt at stealing actually vanishes away fast. Because the sights are great! There are mythological and legendary creatures all around and it's awesome to watch them. Some are even humanoid. You see few babies, though, and the information put near the cages explains that creatures and beings of legends reproduce with great difficulty. You DO feel cheated when you see that the satyr section if for adults only. You don't dare try to explain that you're in fact an adult in disguise and that you stopped by the Fountain Of Youth. You even to get to try a few rides, like you wanted. But they get boring fast and loose their appeal incredibly quickly. Then, you feel caught by a large hand.
    "You," says a tall and stern employee. "Where are your parents?"
    You explain that you just drank from the fountain of youth and hearing your advanced vocabulary the employe believes. Nevertheless, he does point out that you were caught stealing and for that you have no good excuse. He drags you to an office building and locks you in a formal-looking room, explaining he's going to get his superiors. He leaves you, feeling more vulnerable than before. But in fact the employee is going to get a sorcerer to transform you. They weren't kidding about the animals and beings not reproducing well so a transformed thieving adult who's already a young kid is too much of an opportunity to pass. And you did steal, after all...
