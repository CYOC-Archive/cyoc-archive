    Days quickly passed. With Jeff's maturity gone, he was eager to play and do kids stuff, and Jenny even if she still retained her normal mind, also found it tons of fun. Jeff's enthusiasm was contagious, and overall being 8 was such a stressfree and fun experience.

    Jenny enjoyed the different angle of their relationship. Instead of being boyfriend and girlfriend, they were just friends. Of course, she didn't dislike being Jeff's girlfriend, but it was nice to have a break from sexual tension. She was just one of the boys, just as tall and strong as them if not more so. Way before puberty happened, leaving her behind as a short and weak girl.

    A full month passed with them living as 8 year olds.

    "Jeff?" Jenny said.

    The two kids were walking home together, their clothes and bodies dirty from playing.

    "Yeah?" Jeff replied.

    "Let us change back. Let us be big again."

    "Okay." Jeff chipperly replied.

    While Jenny wouldn't mind staying like this longer, it's already been a month. She'd asked Jeff if it was fine before, and he said yes, but he had the brain of a child. The normal Jeff would probably be less eager. She hoped he wouldn't be mad when he returned back to normal.

    Soon they were back to Jeff's room where the cheap-looking laptop housing the Chronivac was.

    Jenny found a setting to revert all their changes. She hesitated for a few seconds, and then she pressed apply.

    "Wow." Jeff immediately blurted.

    There he was. Back to his normal tall teenage self. And herself, looking at herself she was also back to her normal, but not tall, teenage self.

    "I can't believe we were kids for that long." Jeff said. "How long was it? A month?"

    "Yeah." Jenny sheepishly replied. "But it was fun wasn't it. I had a lot of fun, and it seemed like you did too."

    "It was I guess, but I am happy to be back to normal. Nice to see my normal girlfriend again too."

    He didn't seem mad. Jenny was relieved.

    "But now that we're not kids" Jeff started. "How about we have som adult fun?" he suggested. He looked at her, down her body, down her neckline.

    "No." Jenny flatly stated, her mood instantly turning sour. Here she was having all these sentimental thoughts about friendship, and as soon as Jeff's back he's all horny horny. She wasn't in the mood right now. "I'm tired. I want to go home."

    "Sure." Jeff said. "See you tommorrow baby.

    Jenny said her bye and left.
