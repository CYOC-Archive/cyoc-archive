Tyler leaned in close to Bill and lowered his voice to a playful whisper. "That means for as long as we feel like, slugger."

Delighted as Bill was to hear this, he couldn't help but notice - with deep curiosity - the huskier note Tyler ended that declaration on.     Tyler sighed as he looked down at Greg, who hadn't budged from his huddle in the corner.

    "It'll be a while before he's ready, I think. But you!" Tyler favors Bill's chubby tummy with a little tickle. "We can get you out there as soon as you do the deed. So what do you say, squirt?"
