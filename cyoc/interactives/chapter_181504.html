Instead, all James could now think about was growing the largest and bushiest squirrel tail he could manage as he curled the soles of his oversize pawfeet in the anticipation. “What the patient wants, the patient gets. Once more then. Heave, HO” cried Doctor Small as she braced herself against the gurney and pulled another couple of inches of tail from the convulsing boy.
