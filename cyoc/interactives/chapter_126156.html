He entered a room and saw it is a throne room and saw the lich at the end of it with a dragon laying in the middle of the hall. Chase looked at the dragon and said. "Lovey. Now I got to figure out how it will react to my spells." He groaned as he thought of different ways to take it down.

    Lord Yodbino stood up and spoke. "Well you sure are a interesting one." He told Chase. "Tell me. How did you become so strong?" He asked him. Chase looked at him before. "My world is dying." He said before explaining what happened to it and how he gaining his magic and it's affects on him. "I see." Lord Yodbino spoke to himself. He never thought he would end up facing some one like this man facing him. "Gingersnap leave.I see no sense in having you face him as skilled and powerful as him." He ordered her. "But Master!" She protested. "NOW!" He yelled. Gingersnap left the room. He cast a spell for all of his servants to watch the fight. "If I fall to him. He will be your new master." Yodbino told them. He looked at Chase. "Give me a good fight if it's to be my last. Don't hold back." Chase looked at him. "Word of warning. You won't last long if I do." He told Yodbino. "I don't care." He replied. Chase looked at him before finally understanding what is going on. "I will take care of them. You have my word." He spoke to the confusion of the servants. Chase put himself in a stance ready to fight as black magic leaked off him. One minute they were standing the next they had moved and stopped. Chase stood up as Yodbino fell to the ground.

    Chase turned and moved to Yodbino and made a crypt before sending it to a newly made level below. "WHERE DID YOU PUT HIM!" Gingersnap yelled as she entered the room. He pointed at a new door way and she entered going down the stairs after transforming into a anthro form to fit in it. Chase followed after her and saw her on the crypt crying. He stepped up beside her. "What do you want." She asked him. She may be loyal to him but she doesn't has to like him. "He wanted to die but there was one thing holding him back from killing himself." He told her as the others listened to what he says. "And what would that be?" Gingersnap asked him. "You. All of you." He told her. She looked at him in confusion as the rest had the same question she spoke in mind. "What do you mean?" She asked him. "He wanted you to live after his death. The problem was if he died then the you all would follow for his magic is what keeps this place intact and keeps your lives going. A flaw He didn't find out till he was ready to die and had no way of fixing that." He told her. "I may be able to but it would take years at least if not decades." He said before stopping for a few minutes. "I'll leave you to this. If any of you wish to join her you may." He told the rest before returning to the throne room and ended the feed.
