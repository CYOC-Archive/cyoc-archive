    "Oh my god... how young?" Emily asked.

I tried so hard, but there's only so many times a teenaged girl can ask to go to America before her parents just stop listening. We did actually visit once, but that was when I was 17. Not to mention we were on the wrong coast and I hadn't learned English yet." She sighed.

    "Wait, what?" Emily stammered. "English?"

    "Yeah, I knew Japanese. I could read it, speak it, perfectly understand it, but English was now some nonsense to me! I had to spend the last 5 years RE-learning a language. Even now, I'm trying my best to speak English and not shift back. At least I got my masters in communication, but Yuki and the others always teased me for being a "Westaboo" and wanting American dick." She sighed.

    "Who's Yuki?" Emily asked.

    "My bff... she's mad annoying but is still my friend... she didn't know it back then, but she really helped me adjust, get more comfortable with who I am now you know?" Rick said.

    "Rick..." Emily said, before stopping when Rick chuckled.

    "Sorry, I just, I'm so used to Rieko that Rick just sounds... weird." She giggled. "So, Emily, what's your life been like... clearly you used the camera more?" She asked, gesturing at the mansion.

    "Yeah... I just, when you were... well when I thought you were gone.... I tried to fill the whole, get rid of the guilt, but I guess I just made a wall to surround myself from thinking about it all." She sighed.

    "Oh Em." Rick sighed, placing her hand over Emily's "Don't worry, you don't have to feel guilty anymore... now, where's that damned camera?" She asked.

For a brief amount of time, she started to wonder if she had dreamed it all. Whenever she sleep over with Yuki or Akane, or whenever Akito tried to ask her out, she just felt like... herself.
