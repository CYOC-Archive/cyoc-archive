    He noticed an old friend of his coming near, her name was Brooke Venus. She had blond hair, a slight tan on her skin, an average figure. She was one of those people who you could tell were very intelligent but at the same time a little afraid to show it.

    Josh had known her since they were little, they're mothers were sorority sisters in college. But over the past most recent years they had grown apart.

    Josh could tell she had something on her mind but he couldn't tell if it was good or bad. She walked up to him, and said. "Hi Josh it's been a while."

    "Yeah, Its nice to see you again." There were a few moments of awkward silence between the two of them.

    "I can tell you have something you want to tell me so what is it?" Josh said in a curious tone.

    "Well I heard that you just heard you have the clown gene, and well I wanted to ask you if you wanted to join this thing called clown club?"

    "Hm. I'm guessing that a club for people who have the clown gene?"

    "Yup"

    "But, why are you in it, I don't see any clown features showing in you?"

    "Oh I do." She said as she rolled up her sleeves and her skin was becoming pure white. "It's been like this for a week, and it's been slowly creeping up my entire body. I hide it because a bunch of my friends have courlophobia, so I hide it underneath sweaters and pants. But this morning I noticed something that surprised me a bit." Brooke said that as she pulled her hair around and it could be clearly seen, the roots of her hair were turning bright daffodil yellow.

    "I personally don't mind changing. In fact clown club is mentally soothing as well as allowing me to act more like a clown." Brooke said as she set her hair back and smiled a little.

    "Well that does sound nice, when does it meet?"

    "Today actually. Guess I'll see you there." Brooke said with a hopeful face.

    "Hm. Why not. I don't have crew practice tonight so it works for me." Josh said with a smile on his face. Brooke walked away with a slight swaying but that captivated Josh a bit. He couldn't remember if that had already been there or if it was because of the clown gene. But he had his next class and he needed to got moving. So he smiled and walked towards his next classroom.
