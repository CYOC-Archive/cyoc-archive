    Veronica had liked Dilan for awhile now. They had been friends since kindergarten, and Veronica had developed a little crush on him. Course Dilan himself didn't see Veronica in that light, he still basically thought of most girls as weird creatures. Veronica's room was clean and she was setting up the ToD game, and Dilan was brought over as their play date commenced. Their mother's went to chat and have tea as they let their 8 year olds play a magical game.

    Dilan barging into her room said "Veronica what's up!?"

    Veronica startled got a little irritated and said "Don't just barge into my room!"

    Dilan with a smirk asked "So what's with that thing?"

    Veronica said "It's a game I got from the Store of Needs. A game called Transform or Dare it's simple and apparently everything goes back to normal once we're done."

    Dilan asked "How do you play?"

    Veronica took out the instruction manual and recited "Each player puts their hand on the machine then say Transform or Dare. The game will start and then you decide who goes first, you can forfeit the game any time you want, and last man standing wins."

    Dilan asked "So how do you play the game?"

    Veronica said "Well the player whose turn it is asks another player Transform or Dare. The other player chooses either Transform or Dare. If they choose Transform the player whose turn it is can choose to change the other player in any way they want. If they choose Dare the player whose turn it is can make the other player do anything they want. After the Transform or Dare is given it is now the Victim's turn."

    Dilan said "So we just make each other change or do things in humiliating ways to make the other want to give up first."

    Veronica said "Or we could just have fun, and not care about winning. I mean this is magic right?" She put her hand on the Transform or Dare game and said "Come on lets play!" Dilan shrugged and put his hand on the game as well and the two said "Transform or Dare!" Then a weird magic pulse flowed around them, but neither noticed anything weird. Sunlight still shown through the window and Veronica's room stayed as still as an inanimate room normally is.

    Dilan said "Well it's your game so why not you go first?"

    "Alright then, well Transform or Dare?"

    "DARE!"

    "Ok then, I dare you to... kiss me..."

    Dilan gave Veronica a curious look and asked "Why wou-" then his lips puckered up as his body was being pushed towards Veronica as his lips planted on hers and the game's magic gave his tongue lessons on how the french kiss. Veronica could feel Dilan's attempted to push himself off of her, but it only worked a few seconds later with both's faces flushed red. Both of their faces were red, but Dilan's heart was racing much faster as questions and emotions flooded his thoughts.

    Dilan asked "So then... Transform or Dare?"

    Veronica said "Transform." Curious what Dilan would want to turn her into now. Dilan decided to transform her into...
