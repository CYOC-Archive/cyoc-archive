    “I was at Colin’s store buying ingredients for lunch, and I couldn’t find the spices… so I headed down the aisles and you’ll never guess who I ran into!” Luan smiled.
    “Colin?” Lucy asked.

    “…. Yes…. Anyway he was talking to a friend and he cut the conversation short just for me!” The ginger smiled.
    “Uhhhhhh huh….” Lucy said with narrowed eyes.

    “Anyway, I left my sunglasses over here and Colin asked if we could move our date since he was hanging out with a friend.” Luan smiled, “Speaking of which, isn’t time?”
    “Oh geez, you’re right!” Colin said as he looked at his phone, “GOTTAGOBYE!”
    Colin planted two kisses and booked it out the door without a second thought.

    “Where’s he off to now?” Sam asked.
    “He’s meeting his new friend…” Lucy said.
    “Yeah, she seemed really nice!” Luan smiled.
    Sam and Lucy turned to face each other and looked back at Luan.
    “What?” Luan asked.
    The brunette tomboy sighed and put a hand on Luan’s knee.
    “Oh sweet, sweet, sweet naive Luan….”Lucy said as she shook her head, “That ‘friend’ of his…. is getting ready to suck his dick as we speak.”
    “What?!” Luan jumped.
    “Trust her, she’s the original “just a friend”…. Not that it ended up being a bad thing!” Sam nervously smiled.
    The blonde Amazon calmed down and added, “We’re following him.”

    The trio pulled up in a parking lot as Colin stepped out of his car.
    “I’m telling you, she’s not into him!” Luan said.

    “I’m telling you he’s way out of her league!” Luan snapped, “… That sounds pretty mean, actually.”
    “And completely wrong…” Sam said.

    If anyone was “in his league”, it was definitely her. The luscious lips coated in a glossy black, short fiery hair that perfectly framed her face, strolling next to him in Tennis gear.
    “If you look at her from behind and squint, she kinda looks like Wendy…” Sam thought aloud.

    “So how are we doing this?” Lucy asked “Switching cooking spray for sunscreen? Glue on the racket?”

    “I was saving these for Frenchy, but this time I’ll gladly make an exception.” Lucy smirked.
