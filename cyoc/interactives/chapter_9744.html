    Brian went directly the next morning to Pleasure Island and down the main street till he came to Madame' Illusia's theater. Slowly he opened the front door and peered inside. The auditorium was dankly lit by two small bulbs high in the ceiling.

    Walking inside he looked cautiously around as if someone might pounce on him at any moment. Up the stage steps he went and over the wooden stage to where voices were coming.

    "Stephen, Stephen I've told you time and time again not to toy with the girls I've changed to animals! Oh yes I know what you've said before about how they alure you and draw you to them in their new found time of bestial heat. My dear boy you can't toy with them or never again mount one less you join them in my curse and be as a male or maybe a gelding!" argured Madame.

    "I can't help myself Auntie, really those females want it so damn bad they would let any male fuck them silly!" said Stephen in his only defense.

    Slap! "Don't you use that gutter slang language again, fuck, I hate hearing that word!" grumbled Madame'

    "Well that's about what they want come Hell or..... what was that?" asked Stephen as they both turned to look at the dressing room's door.

    Slowly the door opened and Brian poked his head inside.

    "YOU !" said Madame' "Why aren't you trotting about feeling the Ass like self you should be right now!"

    "Hi, my brother drank the potion and he's now braying up a storm! Oh, but please I want to buy some more of the powder and maybe turn a friend named Troy to a Donkey too!" asked Brian with a dumb grin.

    Madame looked at Stephen and he at Madame'. Then she turned to beckon Brian enter her dressing room. "You gave the potion to your Brother?" asked Madame'

    "Well he was my older Brother and sorta just took it out of my hands!" replied Brian still sounding half scared to death.

    "You want to buy more?" asked Madame'.

    "Yes, can I please buy more?" asked Brian right back.

    Madame' motioned to Stephen sending him out of the room. Then with a sadistic smile she said to Brian, "If your going to act like a true Witch a change men to animals you'll need some protection. Stephen will get you three bags of potion making powder. I will mix you a special elixer which will protect you from comsuming any of the liquids per chance one of your victims finds out what's going on."

    Brian felt suddenly relaxed at all her willingness to help. As Stephen returned he handed the three pouches with powders to Brian and sat down where he was before.

    "Now young one I have a way for you to pay for this which I'm sure you will agree. I have mixed this elixer to full strength, I want you to call me here and tell me where Stephen can pick up those you've changed. The animals will be the price, I want them back here for work inside the Park!" explained Madame'.

    "Agreed!" said Brian as he looked at Stephen who was smiling at him.

    "Now take the elixer and go, call me at this number when you have the three corraled and ready to ship! Oh yes, one more thing, be very sure if any of them be girls not to toy or mount one less their bestialness becomes part in parted to you!" warned Madame'.

    Brian swallowed the black and thick elixer. A wipe of his mouth with a hand he offered a quick "Thank You!" and left on the run.

    "Madame'!" said a stunned Stephen.

    Laughing Madame' replies, "The elixer is one of olden times when young men and boys often found certain female animals ever so desireable. He's no exception, why I'd expect before he makes it out the Park's front gate he'll find a beastie he can't but lust to touch or mount!"
