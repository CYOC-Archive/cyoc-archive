    "Any chance you could do a werefox?" Carrie asked quietly.

    Dr. Thompson smiled. "Absolutely, of course, I should warn you that the women who choose this definitely find
    that they become vixens in every sense of the word."

    "Oh really?" Carrie asked, arching an eyebrow.

    The doctor nodded. "Yes. Of all the were-forms that we've documented, vixens consistently rate the highest in
    terms of sexual encounters, sexual partners, and sexual pleasure during the duration of their time."

    "Well.." Carrie said, clearly pondering that statement, "I can't exactly say I'm bothered by that little bit
    of trivia."

    "I do feel the need to highlight a couple things before we proceed further," Dr. Thompson cautioned. "There
    are only three tiers. We have been rather worried about going to a tier four with the vixen formula, because
    of the rather...profound level of heightened sexuality our clients experience. Let me ask you-are you here
    with a boyfriend, husband, or otherwise monogamous partner?"

    "Yes, my new husband," Carrie replied.

    "You need to understand that you WILL find yourself attracted to other people if you undergo this treatment,
    even at a tier one, and you might find that you don't distinguish between male or female, in terms of
    preference."

    Carrie paused. "Well, it wouldn't be the first time I had my tongue inside another woman."

    "It might not be only other women, Carrie. It might be other men as well. We've had solid documentation that a
    vixen almost has an aura of pure sexuality coming from her, and even if she has a strong will, she can't
    always resist the urge to have sex with someone."

    "Duly noted," Carrie said. "We could cross that bridge when we come to it, I think." Her tone of voice
    indicated she wasn't entirely sure on this, but her firm gaze and somewhat erect nipples showed that she
    wasn't entirely deterred. "Is there anything else?"

    "Due to the nature of the vixen formula, you'll find it utterly impossible to stay fully human during sex.
    While you might not always transform fully, you will always have at least some level of transformation. The
    majority of clients have said that they could resist a full morphing if they concentrated, but usually found
    themselves willing their transformations further, even if they had originally intended otherwise."

    "That's definitely not a problem for my husband. He's into women turning into animals. And honestly..I've seen
    some of the stuff he drew, and..it was pretty hot. So I'm pretty sure that's not going to bother me," Carrie
    said with a smirk.

    "We have also found that while a vixen's sexual encounter rate rises after dark, she is not tied to the moon
    in any way, and can change even in the daytime, though almost always requires sexual stimulation to achieve
    her change. Due to the heightened sexual nature of the formula, you might find yourself unable to control the
    urge to masturbate," she informed Carrie.

    "He likes watching me play with myself..and I've never had an issue scratching my own itch," Carrie retorted.
    It was becoming obvious that she'd become rather fond of the idea of the treatment.

    "And finally, we've found that a vixen has a rather..uncanny ability to force other werecreatures to change
    through sexual teasing, pleasuring, or her own transformation in front of them. We haven't exactly pinpointed
    what gives her this ability, though early documentation indicates that a vixen gains the ability to spray her
    pheromones on others, once her tail has popped out, and that her vulva produces the musk rather strongly as
    well."

    "Like I said..some of the stuff I've seen was pretty hot. And..though my hubby doesn't know, I've watched some
    other stuff online. So you're telling me that..say..if I was around a werewolf...I could force them to
    change?"

    The doctor nodded. "Yes. We're still not exactly sure how or why, as many of the werecreatures are a purely
    lunar phenomenon, or otherwise don't normally change during sex. The vixen seems to override this and almost
    draw it out of them. If..you're interested, we're currently doing a study on the vixen condition. If you're
    willing to report to us a minimum of once per day on your experiences...done by either phone interview or face
    to face..the treatment would be free, and there would be other perks."

    "Other perks?" Carrie asked.

    "Access to select locations in Toonworld that normally are either VIP-only, or VERY exclusive, requiring
    reservations, etc. All expenses paid, bar tabs comped...spending money..total pampering."

    "Well," Carrie said, knowing she was making a very important decision...
