    Suzy waited for the shoes to shrink her feet...and waited...and waited. The shoes didn't do anything though!

    "Mary, something is wrong. Your feet should have shrunk."

    "I don't care Suzy, I love my new self!"

    Suzy looked around the house. Where was that card the little man gave me? She found it and called the number.

    Like magic, the little man poofed into her house!

    "Ay lass you must have had quite a day!"

    "I called you about something else."

    "What would that be?"

    Suzy moved to the side. Now the leprechaun could see the person behind Suzy.

    "Great Caesar's ghost! What the hell happened?!?!?!!"

    Suzy told the whole story to Cornelius. Once finished, the little man shook his head.

    "The shoes were only supposed to be for you lass. I didn't even bother giving you that warning. I figured you would hide this from everyone. The shoes were designed with you in mind Suzy. THe warnings were true for you because you already had a spell cast on your feet. For this woman though, those rules don't apply."

    "What do you mean?" Mary steeped closer to him.

    "For someone who never had a previous spell cast, the effects are permanent! I'm afraid that this is your new body lass."

    Cornelius expected tears and shame from the balloon of a human in front of him. Instead, she smiled! Mary bent down and planted a huge kiss of the wee man's lips.

    "If this is permanent, then words cannot describe how thankful I am! I've been a shadow my whole life. Now everyone will notice me!!!"

    The leprechaun smiled. "well, perhaps we should get something for you to wear. Just like Suzy remembered, the little man poofed a small workshop in her home and got to work.

    While he was working, Mary felt a tingle in her lips. The tingle turned into a dull burning sensation as her lips pushed out and grew. Without saying a word, she brought an elongated finger to her lips and felt the growth. The last enlargement was complete. Mary now had a pair of the largest and softest lips that anyone had ever seen.

    The leprechaun finished his work and turned to Mary. "My, my your lips are massive! Here you go lass. I made you a bra, a top, a skirt, a pair of pants, and one pair of shoes. These are just ordinary clothes. You will want to befriend a tailor soon as no one will have anything in your size for the rest of your life."

    Mary nodded and put on the bra, top, and pants. She wondered if the pants would even fit into the washer. Last, she slipped off the magic heels and put her new shoes on.

    The leprechaun turned to Suzy and said...
