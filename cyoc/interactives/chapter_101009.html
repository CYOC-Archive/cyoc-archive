    Ty figured he should prove it to Max, but how?

    He wondered for a few seconds before he got an idea. Max did like anime after all, so he wouldn't mind it. He wished for Max to be a strong ninja kid in training, just like in the various anime Max was into.

    As soon as he thought this Max stood still and a shiver went through his entire body. He started to suddenly get slightly taller, though he wasn't getting any older. His muscles started to grow, stretching his clothes, not too big just the light lean muscles of a kid in training, needed to be quick and agile. Slightly square pecs forming and the beginning of a four pack as well as slight biceps that Max couldn't help but flex.

    He usually didn't care about looks, he was 7, who thinks about that at his age? Well he was now! His skin started to tan, like someone who spends all of his time outside. He wanted to scream but it seemed his voice was frozen even though his arms weren't. Made obvious by the fact he was flexing.

    He suddenly felt a lot more energetic and his core strengthens for him to have better balance and stamina. He felt like he could fight of run forever. He was confused by that thought, why would he want to? He knew he loved staying inside to read or study, or play video games and watch anime.

    His mind started to get foggy as his hair turned a dark red color, a very unnatural color, but of course it was his natural color wasn't it? It grew out and spiked out like what an anime character's might look like. His eyes grew a little bit bigger as his noise got a little crooked as if it had been broken as a bandage appeared over the bridge of his nose.

    His clothes started to rip and change into a white t-shirt with some Japanese letter on it and a long sleeve light red jacket with the sleeves rolled up so he could still show off. A black headband appeared on his forehead. It had metal on the forehead section to act like a piece of armor. The metal had no symbol on it as some ninjas did, but that's cause he was still in training. His pants turned into black shorts and his shoes turned into a pair of black high top style sneakers. To aid in his speed, he thought.

    His mind was the last thing to change as he got dumber, he had less book smarts. He forgot math and English and history. He only knew how to fight, and stay hidden. He was beat up from his training but all that was needed. A part of his mind accepted all the new memories as fact, another part was scared of losing himself, and another (his anime loving side) was loving this and wanted to thank Ty for this gift.

    Finally he could move. "Ty what the heck?" He shouted.

    "Told you I had powers" Ty said smiling "hope you like this"

    "I LOVE THIS!" Max shouted way more hyper then usual, more fitting his age actually. He tried to fight it but the ninja thoughts of fighting and running and having fun just seemed so tempting. Max couldn't help it as he started to lose himself in the new urges and energy. Max's mind fully shrunk away into a hyper ninja boy, he knew it wasn't true but he didn't care, he was gonna love this!

    On his waist formed a belt holding kunai knives and shuriken stars.

    Not wanting to give his little brother deadly weapons, Ty made them shadow weapons instead. They would pass right through anyone Max threw them at, but they would turn that person into a ninja kid just like him, so that Max could have more friends.

    Max looked up at Ty with a big dumb grin. "Thanks dude that was awesome!"

    "So now what are you gonna do with your new abilities?" Ty asked his brother.

    "Go outside and practice duh!" Max shouted as he grinned and ran outside.
