Bacon, eggs, buttery toast, hash browns, sausage, a whole pitcher of milk. It was enough to feed three people. There was a note, too.

    'Dad decided to let you sleep in since you had a rough day yesterday. When you're fed get dressed and meet me in the cow barn.

He moved his gaze down and with a start realized he had eaten everything. Even the pitcher was empty.
