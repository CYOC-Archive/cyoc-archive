    Shrinking Shannon

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by AnarchyNinetyNine 5 years ago S

    The bean hits Shannon in the middle of his face, between his eyes. He's dazed and stands blankly, He is about to Laugh at Maggie and the phone when nausea kicks in. He's that nauseous he falls to the floor. Shannon doesn't notice his clothes hanging off him.

    Shannon feels air hit his skin. He looks down and sees his shirt is extremely loose. "What the?" He says noticing a higher pitched voice. He looks around and sees that everything else is getting bigger each passing second. Shannon wants to scream but he doesn't want to attract attention to himself, especially with a voice like he has now.

    Shannon feels his clothes weighing him down. He pulls them off with great difficulty, they're double his size. Suddenly he is blinded. When his vision returns he notices that he's utterly tiny. His clothes look massive now, especially the shirt (Callum) which looks like a football field. He feels and hears the ground shake. Before he can react he's surrounded by darkness and warmth, he's inside someone's hand.

    After a few seconds the hand opens. Shannon sees Luke's now giant face. Luke smiles and says, "Not so big now. Are you?" Shannon lowers his head in fear. Luke speaks again, "Maggie. You got a jar or something to put him in?"
    "I don't have one on me. There is one is one in class though, and I'm here in the library for the last block."
    "Okay. So I could tape him under a table for the time being?"
    "Sure," replies Magic. Shannon wiggles and says, "Don't you dare."

    Shannon's screams are futile as he is wrapped in sticky tape and put under a table. "Nobody will notice him. Right?" Asks Luke.
    "He's only 6cm (2.3 inches). So probably not."
    "What do we do with Callum?"
