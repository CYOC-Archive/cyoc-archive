    "What the hell are you talking about" Jacobs said "I think that idea is real bad for a number of reasons."

    "OH and you can think of a better way for the three of you to spend the time it takes for us to find out what this thing can do" Mrs Cummings said as she took out her phone and took a picture of the medallion "Now take a picture with your phone, then we will turn it over to take pictures of the other side."

    "Why the hell do we need pictures of the medallion" Mrs Hastie asked.

    Mrs Cummings sat back "Well once we have the pictures I plain to lock the medallion in the safe until we know how it works so we can turn you back."

    "Why should we lock it up when we can use it once we know how it works" Jacobs said.

    Mrs Cummings smiled "And how mean of you girls will there be by that time, it is clear that think is dangerous, so it would be better to put it someplace safe until it is needed.

    At that moment Miss Robinson buzzed them to announce that Mrs Donaldson was there.

    Mrs Cummings told Ruby to go tell her mother about the plain while they take care of things here.

    After Ruby had left they talked thing over for a bit until they came to a decision and when the two came back in they had the medallion locked up.

    Mrs Donaldson was still a little in shock as she got a look at the other three girls that were now copies of her daughter but recovered fast "I still don't know what is totally going on but Ruby has made a good suggestion that she thinks will help you and I have no problems putting you three up for the weekend."
