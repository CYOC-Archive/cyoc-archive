    Ty decided the best place to start would be turning into an animal. Since childhood, he had had fantasies about being an animal. Seeing TV shows and movies or reading books where people turned into animals had always made him feel weirdly nice inside, and once he became a teen he realized those feelings were tied into his developing sexuality.

    He'd never quite been able to put his finger on what about it he liked and found turned him on so much. Imagining the physical sensation of his body contorting to a totally different form? Giving up his self-control, human intelligence and inhibitions to instinct? The unfamiliar sensations and things he could suddenly do and not do as an animal? In any case, whatever it was, he wanted to explore those feelings now he had the power to transform himself and the world however he wanted.

    He undressed, standing in front of his mirror naked, and wondered what kind of animal to change into...

    What did his mind settle on?
