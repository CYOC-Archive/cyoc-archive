    Why don't you slow down for a bit?

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago A BM

    This new tail was quite different from your original. It was the same length as your first tail but instead of having a dark green tip, this one had a grayish tip instead. It took you a few seconds to adjust to having another tail but as you stare at it you wonder what sin it represents. Your demonic knowledge didn't tell you about identifying a tails Sin. You assume lust would be a pink tail so that was one tail out of the way.

    There is really only one way to find out. You think as you snap your fingers as go back into your human form. You exit your apartment building and go out into the city, hopeing to find someone to test out the new powers you'be presumably gained.

    You notice that already newspapers and billboards have already spoken about your raid on Winterslam. You even hear it in the conversations of the people you walk past.

    "First some gang massacre, now some attack on a prison!"

    "You think some terrorist is doing all these crimes?"

    "Why would a terrorist target criminals and gang members?"

    "Who knows, this is a pretty crazy world after all."

    You hear this conversation as you walk past, grinning as you listen. You weren't a terrorist. You were more of a vigilante, a very violent vigilante if you were being honest.

    You kept walking, trying your best to ignore the intense city smell. You kept trying to use whatever your new powers were, but nothing seemed to be happening.

    You tried reading minds, levitating, even changing your gender but nothing seemed to work. Was your new tail completely useless?

    You were so distracted by your thoughts you failed to notice yourself going onto the busy street. You hear a loud honk as you turn around and see a giant truck about to hit you. Subconsciously you immediately transform into your fox form. Instinctually you tap front of the truck then realize it was too late. The truck was going to hit you. Your close your eyes and brace for the impact but... it never comes. The truck had stopped, or rather as you looked more closely had slowed down tremendously. Too slow to be possible.


    Then you realize what your power is. Though that wasn't important as you look around as see the shocked faces around you. Cars and pedestrians stopped as they all saw a large black fox man stand in the middle of a street. They all stared at you as your finally process what was happening.

    You snap your fingers and go invisible. As soon as you disappeared from sight, the people also reacted. Many of them screamed or ran as they realized what they saw.

    The mass hysteria nearly makes you go deaf as you run away from the area. You move agilely past people and jump over cars as you run away.

    You were scared that people have seen you... but also excited. As you ran past, everything you would touch would suddenly go into a state of slow motion. A can thrown midair would slow down to a near halt, barely moving closer to the ground. If you touched it again it would regain it'a regular speed and drop to the ground normally.

    The power to slow things down. The Sin of Sloth! You unleash a howl of joy, making everyone nearby you flinch in pain as they heard your ear bursting howl. You made your way invisibility through the city. You wanted to have a bit more fun.

    What do you do now?
