    A few days later, after receiving word that Case will be infiltrating his facility and after receiving notice that the agent is on his way and will arrive that evening, Ivan is sitting in his office, but he is not alone. A man wearing a white lab coat and another wearing a black suit are standing in front of the desk, “so, is everything prepared and ready for our… guest,” Ivan asks.

    “Yes sir,” the man in the lab coat says, “the truth serum is ready to go, everything is set for the questioning”.

    Ivan nods, “what about the “evidence”,” he asks, turning to the man in the black suit.

    “It’s all placed and prepared as you instructed,” the man says, “we’ll be ready for him”.

    “Good, good,” Ivan says, nodding, “remember, let him take pictures and send a message to his agency. Our mole will edit and alter everything in our favor, to buy us time, once he reaches the main “attraction” and snapped a few pictures and written his final message, that is when you will strike. Do not kill him, just knock him out or stun him, then bring him here so we can have a little “conversation”!”

    The man in the black suit nods, “will do sir,” he says.

    Ivan nods, “I’m counting on you, don’t mess this up,” he says, “have you located the traitor?”

    The man in the black suit shakes his head, “no, not yet, he or she seems to know how to stay hidden,” he says, “but we’ll find the person responsible!”

    “I hope you do,” Ivan says, “dismissed!”

    That evening Case arrives, the man pressing himself against the wall of the large building, slowly and quietly moving along the edge, making sure to stay in the shadows, his pistol ready to deal with any threat, unaware that his arrival is known.

    ‘This is weird,’ Case thinks to himself, ‘if this place really were so important and if it were to hold some kind of secret underground structure, surely it would be more heavily guarded, but so far I haven’t noticed any major activity’.

    Ivan is sitting in his office, watching Case sneak around HIS facility on the security system, “I must admit, this guy is good,” the man says to himself, “he is making sure to stay in the shadows and not be detected”.

    After a while Case finds a window that had been left open which leads to a dark hallway, unaware that it had been left open on purpose, the agent just assumes that some “idiot” had left it open and soon finds himself standing in the dark hallway.

    “He’s in,” Ivan says over the radio, informing his staff and security, “you all know what to do!”

    Case sneaks along the darkened hallway, his gun ready and his senses on high alert, the secret agent is confused as to why there is so little security around, ‘something does not feel right,’ he thinks to himself, but continues to press on, determined to find answers.

    “Sir, we have found and dealt with the traitor,” Ivan’s right hand man says as he enters the office, “he is locked in a secure room with two guards guarding him”.

    “Good, one issue less to deal with, I’ll deal with him after dealing with Case,” Ivan says, as he watches Case moving closer to the door leading into the underground tunnel system, “are your guys ready to stun Case when he arrives in the main chamber. Remember: only stun him once he has sent his findings and report to his agency!”

    “Understood, sir. My men are ready!”

    Case meanwhile finds the entrance and after cracking the code he slips through the door and begins following the tunnel, taking pictures and investigating every room, surprisingly not encountering any security, ‘something defiantly doesn’t feel right,’ he thinks to himself, ‘this feels like a trap, but that’s impossible, how would they know I’d be coming? Unless we have a mole in the agency…. Which I doubt’.

    When Case finally finds the main chamber, his eyes widen in horror as he stares up at a huge rocket or missile, “what the hell,” he says to himself, “if this thing is a missile, no matter if atomic or not, it’ll kill hundreds if not thousands! I got let the agency know, but… what is the destination? THAT is what I need to find out”.

    Case searches the chamber, finding clues that the target is the agency, he wastes no time and sends his findings, report and demand for back up to the director, unaware that the mole will intercept and change his message and request in the favor of Ivan.

    The agent looks around the room, “now, time to get the he…,” he is cut off when a sudden electrical surge causes him to suddenly collapse, a figure holding a stun-gun stepping out from the shadows, “time for you to meet the boss,” the figure says, picking the unconscious man up and carrying him out of the chamber.
