    The Vials

    Xander has a failing furry and tf convention at the MetraCon

    added by Anonymous 3 years ago A O

    Well Xander,Marys byfriend,was a furry and in his
    infinite wisdom he had got carried away and arranged
    to produce a furry and tf convention at the local
    MetraCon Centre.
    The MetraCon was state of the art and Xander had figured the
    crowds of furrys coming from around the world would easily
    generate enough receipts in advance bookings to pay for the
    MetraCons non refundable $33,000 deposit Xander had shelled
    out,thanks to telling his parents he was putting down a
    down payment for a fursuit manufacturing plant.

    Xander had figured the advance sales would repay the $33,000
    and the sales at the door would allow him to buy that fursuit
    factory and make dragon fursuits and
    live happily ever after.

    Alas the advance sales had been slow to come and it looked like
    Xander was going to be in serious financial difficulty.

    So knowing that Xanders ultimate goal was to manufacture dragon themed fursuits
    Mary figured what she needed to do was turn herself
    into a anthro dragoness to star as the center of attraction
    for her boyfriends furry and tf convention,haul in the
    money and serve as a walking advertisement for Xanders
    fursuit ambitions.

    So Mary looked through the box of vials for one labelled
    "anthro dragoness"
    but all she could find was one called "dragon"
    which left her with a worry.
    Would it turn her into a male dragon or a female dragon ?
    Would it make her a anthro or a feral dragon ?
    The instructions said nothing on the matter.

    Mary was pretty sure about one thing,if it turned her into a
    male dragon,she ( or more correctly he ) was going to be
    ending this relationship with Xander.

    So on a wing and a prayer Mary drank the contents of the
    'dragon ' vial.
    There was a sort of dizzy sensation and before Mary could
    say "wow ,I'm a human sized anthro dragoness " three times
    that's what she was.

    That done Mary the anthro dragoness went onto the next
    stage of her plan,to get Xanders furry and tf convention the
    publicity it desperately needed.
