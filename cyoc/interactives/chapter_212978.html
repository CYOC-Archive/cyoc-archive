While blind, Ruut couldn't help but think the man had sight, though perhaps not the same sight as himself.

    "The goddess, she is a gentle being. She does not kill,but transforms. Ruut and Rob are at once one in the same, and different through and through. Yes, you as Ruut are quite young and have much to learn about life, manhood and responsibility. I couldn't help but notice my pouch of mam’ya powder was missing this morning. Perhaps Rob wouldn't have taken it." He said, winking at the boy.

    "But Rob isn't a young boy, nor does he live in the jungle. If he did, however, I'm sure he would have made the same choice. You see, both of you share the same ọkàn , life essence.     The old man smiled "Was that dream so very different than your current dream of being the biggest, strongest warrior in the whole tribe?"

    Ruut thought about it, and a smile crossed his face "No"

    "While you may forget Rob, he'll never leave. Rob isn't dying, he's just becoming something new. He's becoming you!"

    Hearing these words, Ruut realized he was tensing his shoulders, the stress of his essential worries manifesting physically. Releasing them, it felt almost as if he was physically sinking into his new self. Embracing Ruut more fully.

    "Now as for your concerns about Maa’ti, do not worry. No matter what you do to speed or slow her changes, the decision is with the goddess. You may be her instrument at times, but that is all. Maa’ti and the goddess will share their own transformative journey."

    Ruut listened to the man. As he relaxed into his jungle boy self, he realized that each word this man said needed to be remembered. The medicine man was very old and it wouldn't be long before he would have to take the mantle. There was so much to learn and Chatok, as chief, would be counting on him to help lead the tribe through medicine, wisdom, and the magiks of the goddess.

    "You are lucky to be a part of such a journey and should celebrate the opportunity to act as her servant. Most never feel her hands on theirs so directly."

    Ruut nodded, excitement filling him as he realized just how special he was. He was working as the hand of the goddess!
