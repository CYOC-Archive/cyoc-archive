    Jennifer woke up the next day in a daze. Her room didn't seem to have changed despite her body growing again. Jennifer sat up on her bed. A nightlight glowing dimly as it cast a shadow over a pile of plushies and toys on the ground. Jennifer looked around as she pulled the lamp light on her nightstand. Her walls were plastered with posters of a show or film, maybe a video game she couldn't tell from the animated styled faces.

    Jennifer saw the report on her nightstand back when she snuck it onto her room. Curious she pulled it up again. The whole paper didn't make any sense, Jennifer tried to sound out each word, but gave up after a few. She just couldn't understand it. The graphs and other visuals didnt elucidate anything else nor the results. Even the number in bold couldn't even be understood. It started with a 4, but the number right next to it meant something, Jennifer felt like she knew that, but not what the value was.

    Scratching her head she put the paper back down. As her gaze tilted she saw she had finally hit puberty, or atleast a form of it. She wished she had her boobies back, heh, boobies. Maybe she could pester her dad or something. Jennifer giggled, also noticing her shirt matched the characters that donned her walls. She shuffled around the room, her pajamas drapped over her feet as she stepped over her toys. It didnt look like she got any older, infact maybe she got younger, how old was she? Jennifer tried to count on her fingers...1, 2, 3, 4, 5......Jennifer stared at her other hand for a minute before returning to her first hand, recounting back up to five. What was after it?

    Jennifer shook her head, she knew she remembered it, but it just felt like a complete void now, a empty space in her brain, and that void was growing.

    Her dad peaked his head around the corner, "Good morning sleepyhead, come on let's go out."

    Jennifer looked around confused, she was still in her pajamas shouldn't she atleast get dressed? Jennifer tried to speak but her dad had already left. Forcing her to trail out. Seeing her dad by the door he waved her over. "Come on, are you still tired this morning?"

    Jennifer nodded unsuredly, "Uh yea, I just still sleepy." Her brain secondguessed her wordplay, maybe she really was still groggy. She looked next to the open door, there weren't any shoes or even slippers for her. Noticing the growing impatience of her dad she continued shuffling out the door, the cool morning air freezing her feet. "It cold." Jennifer said as she shook her arms.

    "You'll be in the car soon, come on." Otto said as he led her down the pathway to the car, it was already running. Her dad opened the door for her as she stepped in. Fiddling with her hands she stuck them between her thighs as her dad entered on the other side. The car rolling out of the driveway.

    Jennifer was never one to get motion sickness, but now was a different story, the town around her blurring with their modest speed just made her stomach flip, she had a hard time focusing on every new house or passerby the car passed. Thankfully the ride wasnt long, as the car turned into a parking lot. "Jupiter Adult Care" Jennifer didnt know what the first word was but she new adult, thats what dad was. The door opened as Jennifer stumbled a little, still having her weight on the door when it opened.

    "Sorry sweety, you know you're supposed to not lean on the door. Come on." Otto reached over to unbuckle Jennifer. The two walking into the facility. It was brightly lit with clean sterile walls. Her dad was talking to someone through a glass wall until a door opened up across the room. A person in a light blue outfit waving over to her. "Goodmorning Jenny! Good to see you today!" Jennifer turned back in confusion as she pointed to her dad. The person nodded. "Yes hes busy, come with me ok?" Jennifer nodded without complaint. The other side of the wall was much more cheerful, with sections of books, toys, and private rooms all circling a center commons area. While Jennifer was taking in the new scenery, she did become much more interested in the small icon of a bear on the person's outfit, it connected to a badge or plastic card. Jennifer pointed to the person's chest. "Bear!" Excited that she knew the animal. The person smiled. "Yes thats my bear." She said leading Jennifer into the commons area, the door behind them locking with a feint click.
