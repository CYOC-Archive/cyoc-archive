He saw the pure predator that the dragon was. Max backed up against the log totally trapped. Max tried & discovered he could still speak, so he did. "Wait." Max said to Coal. "You are prey. Food. There is no backing out of this. You agreed to this." Coal told Max annoyed. "I am & I am not backing out. I just want to say something before you eat me." "I want to make a deal with you while I can." Max told him. "Deal?" Coal asked confused. "Every 3 months I become a new prey animal for you to hunt & eat." Max told him. Interested Coal said "Go on." "After I come back to life as a human I'll then transform into a new prey animal for you to hunt & eat. And every time after that." Max explained. "So what I get you're saying is you want to over & over become prey for me to hunt & eat?" Coal asked thinking he got that. Max nodded. "I realized I am prey through & through. I should be a prey animal period. It is what I should be." Max answered. "So after you come back as a human you going to immediately start your transformation into another animal for me to hunt & eat?" Coal asked thinking he got that. "Exactly. Though you get to choose what prey animal I will be next." Max answered nodding. "From now on you just want to be various prey animals I hunt & devour?" The dragon asked. Max nodded. "Deal. You will be whatever prey I want from now on." Coal said agreeing loving the idea. The dragon then pounced & sank his teeth into the rat's body. The rat cried out in serious pain & joy. The dragon quickly began to tear the rat apart devouring pieces of it. Max quickly died.

"So what prey animal you want me to be for you this time my dear dragon?" Max asked curious. I'll be a 2 foot tall anthromorphic rabbit in 10 minutes. Then we can fuck like animals." Max informed Coal. "I wouldn't be gentle." The dragon joked. "I know." Max said smiling.
