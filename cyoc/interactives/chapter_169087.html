    Gloria sighed and said "Didn't imagine I'd be babysitting today... making myself younger should've gotten rid of responsibilities you know?"
    Millie smirked and said "Well that's what you get for playing around with exp-" Reality seemed to flicker and now instead of the little ladies hanging out around a computer Gloria found herself laying on the carpet staring at a tv screen entranced by the young children programming that was being streamed.
    Gloria looked to a bit of a slack jawed in Millie in a trance and asked "Millie, do you still remember?" Gloria herself felt like there was a heavier fog on her mind. There was this thing on a computer, and that... She used to be a SUPER BIG GIRL... or something.
    Millie said "Umm... something is different... We were older right sis?" Gloria nodded although the Sis didn't quite feel right. She knew they were close, but were they identical twins before?
    The door to the room opened and out came momma Liz who said "Here my little angels, grand mommy's finished dinner." then she clapped her hands and Gloria and Millie's little bodies sprang into action automatically. Gloria paused the stream and both girls ran to their mother, and she carried them to the dinner table. There everyone was. Lizzie, her parents, and her twins Gloria and Millie. At the dinner table Lizzie's parents were trying to keep their granddaughter's attention as Lizzie was doing some college work on her laptop while taking a bite of dinner now and then.

    The Chronivac had grouped together Millie and Gloria, and then swapped their age with Lizzy's age. Lizzy got both of their 12 years turning her into a 24 year old woman, and Millie and Gloria had to split Lizzy's 5 years making them 2 but closer to 3. With such a change the Chronivac gave them a new backstory where Lizzy in the middle of college was dating, got pregnant, abandoned, and then her education got screwed up a bit. After a small break after giving birth to twins Lizzy changed to a public college near her parent's house, and took some classes while also doing a part time job to attempt to help take care of her kids. Her parents were a god send and helped her have a social life outside of school and work, and a lot of the time said she shouldn't worry about the money and to take her time in college. However Lizzy was stubborn and wanted to put money towards their college, get her life started, and give her kids a somewhat normal life.

    Lizzy was trying to finish a paper, and then went to the desktop for a second where she found a program shortcut she didn't recognize. She had been making good progress, and decided to first run a security check... It seemed a ok and maybe it was one of her kids who downloaded it somehow... Kids were more and more tech geniuses these days right? 2 and a half year olds can do this sorta thing right? Then opened it up and found the Chronivac program which had profiles for her parents, kids, and herself.

    Curious she opened up her profile and out of curiosity wondered what would happen if she changed her gender. she quickly noticed her hands were bigger and hairier, and that looking down she was now a he... and while the story was similar... the guy who fucked and abandoned her was now a girl who fucked, stayed with him till the babies were born, and then left him with them. Also his parents while happy he didn't try to abandon the girl and the kids were a lot less happy with Lizzy now Izzy for getting that girl knocked up in the first place. Still she was aware she had changed gender, but sadly the patch hadn't quite downloaded yet so everything was still unstable. What happened next?
