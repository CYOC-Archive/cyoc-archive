    9 months later,
    Ondine had gave birth to a beautiful Mermaid with Brunette hair and Dark brown eyes. As for the rest, she looks just like her Mother. Ondine Called her Allison.
    They quickly remembered about that stupid human that Allison used to be. But Allison doesn't remember being anyone but Ondine little Princess.

    (Allison POV)
    Couple years later.
    I am 13 years old. I could just swim here all day long. It's so peaceful and beautiful. The reef here is completely beautiful, just like Mum always said to me.

    "Allison, Come on sweetheart, time to go," Mum called to me.

    "Coming Mummy," I said and began to swim over to her, as soon as I reached her I swim straight for her and hugged her. "What's going on, Mummy," I said to her still hugging onto her tight around her neck.

    "Home sweetheart, I going to teach you some magic," Mummy said.
    Then Mummy began to swim towards our home with me still hugged onto her. I can't imagine life without my Mother. Or life without my Tail. My Aunts too.
    Once we got home mum took us to to where Mum had all of her Hair things. And got me to a seat and sat me on it and grabbed her hairbrush and started brushing my hair. Oh, it felt so good.
    "Everything all right my baby girl," Mum said.
    "Just great Mummy, I got you and my aunts with me forever," I said.
    "And you always will," Mum said.
    Once she finished my hair. She took me to my room and pulled out one of my Shells for my hair and put it on.
    "There we go sweetheart. All done. You like it," Mum said.
    "I love it Mummy," I said before I swim over to her and hugged her again. "Love you mum,"
    "Love you too, my Little Princess mermaid," Mum said. Which made me hug her a bit tighter for a sec or 2.

    Then we went and got so food, Lovely Prawns, tuna, crab. Then we went to get some sleep and went to our bed which we got into to and I cuddled into mum as she pulled up the cover for us to warm up a bit.
    "Night Mummy," I said.
    "Night my Beautiful Mermaid," Mum said. Then my eyes closed and went asleep.

    Over the next couple of days. Mum taught me everything what I could do with magic but one thing she said what we are going to do today.
    We swam over to this beautiful Lagoon. where Mum and I stayed behind a rock.
    "Right sweetheart, remember what I told you about humans," Mum said.
    "Of Course, Mummy. Not but selfish," I said.
    "Right, Now what do we do with ones we find alone," Mum said.
    "What?" I said.
    "We take them and Change them into one of us," Mum said.
    "But did them know?" I said.
    "No, we take their memories and plant new ones so the don't resist the change," Mum said.
    "Does that really work?" I said.
    "Yes, sweetheart, There is something I so tell you, baby," Mum said.
    "What is it? Mummy," I said.
    "You used to be one," Mum said.
    "What, but I remember being born and gave straight to see you, how could I be from those Selfish humans," I said.
    "Yes, you were born from me but before me and you human self were talking once which is how a knew about humans being all selfish. then I group of Mermaids from another pod came and push you onto me and made you kiss me," Mum said.
    "Wait, I kissed you," I said.
    "Allison what you need to remember that at the time before I had you, you used to be a boy. But Now your my Beautiful Mermaid which I would change for the world. You are my Pearl sweetheart," Mum said.
    "Wait then how?" I said.
    "When you kissed me, you started to shrink and was pulled into me. Then that version of you disappeared forever," Mum said.
    "I'm your Daughter, ain't I?" I said starting to get worried.
    Then Mum pulled me against her. "My Mermaid princess you are my Daughter. and always will be. I love you, my baby," She said.
    "Love you too, Mummy," I said.

    Then we heard like some one was crying and moved over enough to see a little girl crying.
    "Mummy why is she crying?" I asked.
    "No idea, but what if we set her free and you can make her a sister?" Mum said.
    "Let's do it Mummy," I said.
    "Come on, sweet heart," Mum said and we gently swam over to the crying girl.
    "Hi," I said. Making the girl look up to us.
    "Wow, you are Mermaid," The girl said.
    "Yeah, Names Allison," I said.
    "Sky, My Name is Sky," Sky said.
    "I'm Ondine, Allison's Mother," Mum said.
    "Wow!" Sky said.
    "So what was wrong?" I said.
    "What?" Sky said.
    "You were crying before," I said.
    "No one in my Family ever has time for me, They always busy doing something else," Sky said.
    "That's bad, Mum can't we do something about that," I said.
    "Well we could, if you really want it." Mum said.
    "Want what?" Sky said.
    "Want to join us?" Mum said.
    "What, As a mermaid?" Sky said.
    "and my Daughter," Mum said.
    "Which makes you my sister," I said.
    "um," Sky said.
