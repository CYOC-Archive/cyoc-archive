    You have earned +10 stats points.||

My base stats, the ones I had when everything started only summed up to 13 points in total and suddenly I had earned 10 more to allocate at my will. I also knew that training in different ways could help me improve my stats, as learning so many things from using Identify had helped me earn +4 Int.

    MP: 80/75

The place was massive and one could easily get lost, but I would use a rock to mark the paths and entrances I had used. As I kept on advancing and exploring the place, I fought a lot of other ratmen. It was quite scary at times and wished for my daddy to be there to protect me, but they were actually pretty easy to kill. Once they were out, they would just disappear on a trail of dust.

    Apparently, I also had an Inventory window from which I could add and remove items, as well as money. I didn’t know how to use the money, but I already had 850 coins, getting around 50 per rat. Through my adventure, I kept increasing my level, as well as the level of my Mana Strike. After what could have been easily been two or three hours, I felt like I needed to go back. I was getting really scared and the place was a mace, I didn’t want to get trapped. I decided I would get to level 5 and then I would go back home before anything happened.

    However, once I went through a turn, a little room was waiting for me with a chest right in the middle. Once I checked there was no monster around and I identified the chest with my ability, I opened it with increasing difficulty, as the wooden box was pretty big and my hand didn’t reach high enough for me to open it all the way up. I checked inside and I found something unexpected. Sitting on a small cushion was a purple scroll. Once I took it, a screen popped up again.

    ||You have acquired the Summon Familiar Scroll.
    Would you like to learn the spell right away?||

    I did not hesitate and learned the spell immediately. Once I had learned it, I knew I needed to get back home in order to use it. I was also running low on mana, so there was no point in continuing further. Besides, after the little room and behind the chest, I noticed the dungeon kept going, though the stone of which the hallway was meant to be looked… reddish. You could tell it was probably a whole different section of the maze.

    As decided, I went back all the way I came following the marks I had left. It took me a while but the running and exploring throughout this whole place increased my DEX by two points. Once I was able to get back to the entrance, I slowly went up the stairs until I reached the door. I slowly opened it thinking my family was back already. I was sure it was after dinner already, but I didn’t hear anything. I peeped a little bit and saw no one. Usually my dad and my brothers are really loud. They are very heavy men and you can hear how they stomp the floor when they are in their rooms. However, the house was dead silent. Once I got in, I noticed the clock again and it said that it was still 4:30 pm.
