That demons have developed means of mass tasty soul harvesting and corruption of their own (as had once said, the justice system in Hell was very straight forward, 'bring food, or BE food!'), and had also developed means of long range destruction. When's the last time you showed what severing yourself off from the love of everyone else REALLY made your spirit look like?"

    "GAH! You little-!" The hag snarled. "I haven't had to show my real material manifestation in centuries! I'm going to eat you and feel you squirm in my belly!" The hag grew and grew in size to tower over the little cherub more than before.

    It finally occurred to Hopel she might be in trouble. "Uh-oh." She looked at the bewildered James. "Hey! Hey you! Help! I know this might sound like I'm just trying to get you to join a DIFFERENT army, but I promise! What makes you 'you' is the one part that won't vanish! PLEEEASE say you volunteer before I get eaten!"

Silver claws replaced his fingers as he grew an EXTRA finger and toe on each limb, for a total of six claws on each! He felt a tale whip around as his spine grew, and felt his hair dark dark blue and trail along his back. Complete with whiskers and antler like horns growing from his head.

    James looked down at himself in awe he was... an angelic dragon, equal and opposite to the monstrous hell dragons he'd seen in the sky and on the news, turning entire landscape to burning wastelands. He was also now the size of a child.

    "It's okay," the angelic dragon said. "I gotcha. The name is James by the way."

    "Nice to meet you James... or is it Jamesel now. Either way, welcome to our little resistance army of two... We're kinda gonna need more recruits."
