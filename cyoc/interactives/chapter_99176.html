But although she could increase the time, she could not decrease it to less than 1 hour. She guessed that was the minimum time the device needed to complete her complex transformation.

She decided that the program was probably right. She didn't like the idea of being lost in an orgasm for 1 hour, helpless and in a daze. She left that as it was and continued on.

Without a second thought, she selected the start button, hoping that the program would begin despite the errors. Another red warning flashed. She tried to click past it, but it remained, demanding a percentage be input before it could proceed.

    Percentage? Percentage of what? she thought. She had clicked through the warning so quickly, she hadn't bothered to read what it was talking about. The only percentage she could think of was muscle. Is that what it wanted? To know how lean she wanted to be? What percentage of her body would be muscle?

The program could not calculate any measurements until she had specified how far she was willing to go. But now that all the input had been entered, the program whirred to life, starting to complete the request it was given.
