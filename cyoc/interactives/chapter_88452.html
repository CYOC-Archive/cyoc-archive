    Ty's Power

    Ty rewards Jake with huge muscles

    added by scriptboy 8 years ago BM S

    Ty was struggling to fight back the tears. His own family didn’t give him anything for his birthday and yet here stood Jake, a soccer player from school, with a $20 gift card from a video game store.

    “Jake… You… You really didn’t have to do this… My gosh… Thank you so much… I mean…” Ty said as he choked up. “This is the nicest thing anyone…”

    “It’s alright, Ty! You’re a nice guy! It’s your birthday, dude! Enjoy it!” Jake grinned.

    It took Ty about two minutes to stop crying. He was so overwhelmed by the gesture that he needed a moment to wipe the tears from his eyes.

    “You okay, dude?” Jake asked him, in his usual friendly manner. Jake had always been nice to Ty.

    “Jake… Uhmm… I gotta tell you something…” Ty said calmly. “Don’t worry… It’s nothing bad… It’s really cool, if you think about it…”

    “Oh, if it’s a cool thing then I want to hear it!” Jake smiled.

    “Okay… Uhmm…”Ty said in a slightly shaking voice. “I… I discovered this morning that… I can do things… Really cool things…”

    “What kind of things?”

    “I can change things… People, things, animals, objects… Anything! I can change anything, everything, anywhere and everywhere into anything I want!” Ty explained.

    “Oooh!” Jake pondered. “You can change things, animals, and… People?”

    “Yeah! I can change people, like me… and you!” Ty grinned, as he walked a little closer to Jake.

    “Uhh… Why would you change me? I mean…” Jake wondered out loud.

    “You’re a super-nice guy… But you get picked on now and then… So, I think it’s about time for you to Hulk out… I want all your muscles to become nice and BIG!” Ty grinned.

    “What do you m—“ Jake was about to ask, when his muscles began to inflate all over his body!

    Jake’s pectorals grew to the size of cantaloupes, sticking out from his big, broad shoulders! His biceps grew bigger and rounder until they were as hard as bowling balls which looked like they were implanted in his arms! His flat stomach was now covered with rows and columns of washboard abdominal muscles, forming a pattern which resembled a tiled kitchen floor.

    “Ohmygawd!!!” Jake gasped as he felt his muscles grow and his strength increase all over his body.

    Next, Jake’s quads grew thicker, deeper and wider, all at the same time, practically doubling in width in a matter of seconds. Thick columns of muscle formed above his knees, making his legs as thick and as wide as oak trees, covered with thick, red veins. And last but not least, his calves inflated like large balloons, growing bigger than soccer balls sticking out from the back of his legs.

    His back, shoulders and neck widened, while his lats stretched sideways like wings. His arms had become so thick that he couldn’t touch his sides anymore. His arms were now hanging from his shoulders at an angle, like a big bodybuilder. Thick delts formed over his shoulders as well.

    Lastly, his shirt and shorts grew in proportion to his muscles, changing into UnderArmour workout clothes which stuck to his massive body.

    “Holy frick! I’m STRONG!”

    Ty just smiled. Jake had not grown any taller, but he surely had grown several inches wider while packing on all that muscle! He now looked like a HUGE bodybuilder!

    “Wowww!” Jake shouted, as he looked down at his body while he flexed his gargantuan arms. Every movement he made resulted in some series of huge muscles flexing somewhere. His hands were enormous and his feet were incredibly large as well!

    “That’s my way of thanking you for being such a good friend to me, big guy!” Ty just smiled.

    “Well, I can join the weightlifting team with this body!” Jake grinned.

    Then Jake and Ty hugged each other, with Jake being really gentle, before they decided what to do next.
