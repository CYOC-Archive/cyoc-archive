    Of course you knew who you were going to use the remote on. Your sister-in-law, Sofia.

    Married to your younger brother, you always disliked her. She was bitchy, loved stirring drama and expressed the same dislike you had for her.

    Through the front door walked your brother and his wife,
    “Hey bro,” your brother Daniel said,
    “Hey,” you replied, patting him on the back. You look back at your sister-in-law, who gave you death glare. The two were staying over at your parents house since your sister was getting married. You didn’t really care, at end of the day you were just happy sitting in your lab all day, talking to no one. But family is family, so you pushed yourself a little harder than usual. Plus, thanks to your new development you knew it was going to be a good weekend.

    For the next few hours, you sat a distance away from each other. You sat with your sister, she sat with your brother. You were a close family, but she dragged him further and further away. You never liked her from the moment she set her evil eyes on you.

    After dinner, your family plus your sister’s fiance and your sister-in-law sat around the living room talking. As usual, you sat quietly at the back of the room minding your own business, occasionally speaking when your sweet little sister asked you a question. You could feel your sister-in-law’s gaze on you even if you were looking at the floor.

    Eventually, you excused yourself to use the ‘bathroom’. Instead, you grabbed your backpack and brought it into the bathroom. Once there, you calibrated the remote ready to use.
    ‘Sofia Murdock’
    With a smirk on your face, you opened the bathroom door ready to finally give that bitch what she deserved.

    But when you opened the bathroom door, you were far from greeted by a pleasant face, rather the unpleasant grimace of your sister-in-law. Your eyes went wide with surprise as she stood right outside, her face as uncomfortably close to yours as it always is,
    “Sorry,” she said, clearly not in any way sorry for being so rude. You quickly reacted,
    “Actually, give me a moment,” you said, stepping backwards, closing the door once again. Even behind the door you could hear her scoff.

    ‘Once I leave, Sofia will immediately wet herself’

    You typed in, knowing it would embarrass her. You finally unlocked the door and slid it open,
    “Here,” you said, allowing her in. As soon as her eyes landed on you, you watched as she suddenly let out a breath,
    “Oh no!” She said, looking down at herself, covering up as quickly as she could,
    “What’s wrong?” You asked with a false concern,
    “I-it’s nothing! Move!” She said, pushing you to the side, shutting the door and locking it as quick as she could.

    Looks like the remote worked exactly how you wanted it to. But just to be sure, you quickly entered,
    ‘Sofia will let out a moan’
    Pressing enter, Sofia immediately let out a very loud, very audible moan. You weren’t sure if anyone else could hear it, but you sure could, and Sofia was sure to be pretty embarrassed by it!

    Your next course of action was to find a place to be by yourself and analyse all of Sofia’s stats on the device. You decided to sneakily walk upstairs to your old bedroom. But before you could reach the top, you heard a voice call from downstairs,
    “Where are you going?” Your sister, Daphne asked,
    “Huh?” You replied, looking down. Your looked pretty suspicious,
    “C’mon,” she said, “I’m getting married! I know the whole marriage thing isn’t really for you but you gotta support me a little,”
    “I will, I will,” you reply, “I-I… I just need a few minutes to myself,”
    “Alright,” Daphne said, clearly suspicious, “five minutes maximum before I come up there and drag you out myself!” She said jokingly, but sternly,
    “I promise,” you reply. She smiles before walking back into the living room with her fiance, your brother, and your parents. Sofia was still pretty busy in the bathroom.

    Finally in your bedroom, you locked the door behind you, sitting with your back against your old bed.
    ‘Sofia Murdock, formerly Sofia Kettleman;
    Age: 27
    Marital Status: Married to Daniel Murdock
    Children: Currently Six Weeks Pregnant with Baby Boy’
    She was pregnant… you had no idea, and you weren’t sure if Daniel knew either. Maybe even Sofia didn’t know, but this gave you more opportunity to really mess with her.

    Weight: 200 Pounds
    Breasts: DD Cup with Implants, naturally B cup’

    Your first instinct,
    ‘Sofia’s implants make her nipples permanently tent her clothing’

    ‘Sofia can’t help herself from moaning anytime something touches her nipples’
