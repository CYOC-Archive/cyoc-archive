    Another boyfriend

    Billy picked up the phone, and remembered how he came in before: as a 40 year old man, with his dads William and Joel. Now he was stuck as a twelve year-old, and he was acting like one too. He generated a sentence on the phone, and as luck would have it the phone flashed "The 12 year-old has finished breastfeeding." He quickly changed 12 to 40 and suddenly his stature increased.

    He was once again an adult, sitting next two his two adult dads. Now that Billy was in full control of himself again, he realized that he was still in his stinky diaper, and he knew the smell bothered William. He set the phone down on the bench and quickly offered, "Hey Joel, can you give me the diaper bag? I'll change myself in the bathroom real quick." Joel looked relieved as he said "Sure, Billy," and handed the bag to his son, who went directly into the bathroom to change.

    Joel was still reeling from having changed William's pregnancy. He wondered what other things he could change with the app. "Once dear Billy gets back, let's go home, hun," William said between labored breaths. Joel replied with "Huh? Oh, yeah, sure." He was too distracted with the app. He generated another sentence: "William's and Joel's son has gone to change." Ah yes, this is something that has irked Joel for a while. Why did he and William adopt someone who was around their own age? Billy does help with the breastfeeding, and his incontinence is a bit infantile, but he's 40. Joel hit the word "son", and only a couple options came up. He looked at the word "friend". He would have hit that, but Billy's willingness to breastfeed stopped him. Joel needed all the help he could get. The only other choice was "boyfriend". What, like a three-way? Joel's curiosity got the better of him, and selected this option, and put the phone back into his pocket.

    He waited patiently as his new boyfriend cleaned himself up. Joel was eager to see how this changed the relationship between the three of them. Eventually, Billy came back, striding confidently with the diaper bag. When Joel saw him his eyes softened a bit. This was surprising to him: apparently the app made Joel feel like Billy was in fact his boyfriend. Joel stood up, only for Billy to come in with a quick peck on the lips. "Thanks for waiting, fellas," he said as he moved to William. Joel blushed as he turned and watched Billy help William to his feet, only to give William a kiss as well, which he returned. "No problem, babe," William replied, grinning back. Joel smiled at the two of them, realizing that he felt affection for them instead of jealousy. The app really did make the three of them an item. It felt rather natural. What an app.

    The three of them meandered to the car, keeping a reasonable pace for William's heft. Joel and Billy positioned him in between themselves for support, and they held hands as they walked. Once they got to the car, William said with a blush, "Hey, I think I'm gonna need some more draining. Do you mind if...?" Joel and Billy put the pieces together rather quickly, and agreed: Joel got into the front seat, while William and Billy got into the backseat together. Joel started to drive slowly and evenly back home as Billy delicately buried himself in a breast to relieve William's pressure.
