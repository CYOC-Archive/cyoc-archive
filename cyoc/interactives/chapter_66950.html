    It was about 10:30 when Kevin got back home. He kicked off his shoes and headed back upstairs. With all the laughter coming from Jake's room, it sounded like he was wide awake. He poked his head through the door and saw Kyle and Jake in bed, and reading a Captain Awesome comic. They didn't notice him standing in the doorway watching, until they dropped the book. "Oh, hey Kevin! Me and Kyle were just reading!" Jake had a huge smile on his face.

    "Well... I hate to break this up, but it's way passed your bed time Jake."

    "Aw, do I have to?" Jake wined.

    "Ya... it was just getting to the good part!" Kyle added with a grin.

    "Yes, I'm sorry, but you have to."

    Jake turned to Kyle with his big eyes, "Well, what do you think? Can't I stay up?"

    "I don't know, your brother sounds pretty sure."

    Jake pouted for a moment then said "Fine." He took the chain off of his neck, but instead of giving it to Kevin, he held onto it and put his hand under the pillow. He laid himself down, closed his eyes, and rested his head on his pillow on-top of his hand.

    "Uh.. Jake." Kyle had been made to lay down thanks to Jake.

    "Can't hear you, I'm asleep." Jake whispered and started making fake snoring sounds. Kyle looked to Kevin for some help, but he was too busy trying not to laugh. Kyle wasn't sure what to do, so he started poking Jake in his side of their chest. Jake ignored it and continued making his snoring sound. Kyle started to say Jake's name every-time he poked him and asked if he was annoying him yet. Jake responded by shifting sideways, away from Kyle. However Kyle moved with him every-time, and continued poking. Once Jake had reached the edge of the bed, he took all his strength and rolled over, so Kyle's half would fall out of bed. Kyle caught himself before the fall and shoved Jake's side. "Oh, sorry, I was sound asleep, and that's just something I do."

    "Ok you two, break it up." Kevin came over and helped them sit up in the bed.

    "Ya... me and him are kind of attached..." Kyle said sarcastically.

    "Ya, so we can't really break it up." Jake sounded identical to Kyle.

    Kevin sighed and shook his head. He took the chain from Jake, and put it on. In an instant, him and Kyle were fused. "Ok kid-o, off to sleep now." Jake groaned, but gave Kevin and Kyle a hug before turned off the light. Kevin gently closed the door behind them. They made their way downstairs and watched some t.v. At one of the first commercials, Kevin turned to Kyle, "So, I have to ask." He pointed at themselves, "Was this part of your plan too?" Kyle turned with a mischievous grin on his face and shrugged. Kevin smiled, and they gave each other a playful shove. "Great... back together again."
