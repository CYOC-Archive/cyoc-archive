    “Ah…I ran into you earlier today, you must be a former human, that tail doesn’t come naturally.” Said Alejandro with a chuckle as he ordered his drink. “I was a human myself…going on 10 years now.”
    For a while she did not say anything, it wasn’t until Alejandro’s drink arrived that she spoke up.
    “Yes…I have only been a mermaid for two days.” admitted Lupe as she wondered how to say this. “My name is Lupe.”
    “Lupe.” said Alejandro with a chuckle. “I actually knew a girl in middle school on the surface named that, she was a strange one…she believed in merfolk…kind of wish I could tell her she was right.”
    “You just did.” confirmed Lupe as Alejandro almost dropped his drink and he turned and looked at her. “I thought it was you when I ran into you earlier.”
    “Wow…what a strange world.” Said Alejandro with a chuckle. “So what is your story?”

Someone a mermaid was pulling a merman into the backroom where if he was right the back wall was covered with what looked to be a honeycomb like structure where he thought he saw mermaids and merman having sex.
