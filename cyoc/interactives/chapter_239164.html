    "You'll thank me," her reflection said. There was another static. Tina burst into the bathroom. At the sight of Leah on the floor, she did the worst possible thing. She cooed loudly.

    "You are just a cutie when you have a blowout!" Tina gushed. Her eyes watering from the smell, Leah finally finished with a salvo of urine. Tina picked her up. "All right, let's get you cleaned up. Then you can put on your diaper, since you're such a big girl!"

    "...I guess," Leah said reluctantly. "But I don't want to be a baby!"

    "Tick-tock. Tina will be back soon with a very cute outfit. Decide now. Do you accept your desires and thus take control of them? Or reject them and thus continue to spiral?"

    "I..." Leah swallowed. She could hear Tina's footsteps outside the bathroom. She had to make her choice now.
