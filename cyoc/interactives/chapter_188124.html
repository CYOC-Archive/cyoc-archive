    Daydreaming on the Bus

    Muttering under her breath, Jennifer rushed over to the laundry room -- the last place she could think to check for her missing clothing items. Fortunately, it was a very profitable last-minute decision, as Martha simply hadn't emptied the dryer out before taking her day off. Jen quickly pulled out a pair of panties and a different pair of socks, and immediately put them on. She then ran to the front door, slid on her shoes, grabbed her bag, and headed out.

    The Bus was in the middle of stopping when she made it outside, and she was within the drivers view. She gave a sigh of relief, knowing that she had made it on time. She walked down her front porch steps and got onto the bus, finding a seat towards the back, as the bus began its journey towards the school.

    Jen slumped down a bit in her seat. Being up this early in the morning was always rough, but today had an especially bad start. She started thinking about Kyle again, and how creepy he had been, telling her she's going to turn into a cat...when she gets horny? It was disgusting that his mind even thought like that. And how did he get her number, anyways?

    She decided that dwelling on Kyle's strange interests was a waste of time, as it'd just gross her out and make her angry. Instead, she let her mind drift towards other, more positive things, like how glad she was that she had made it to the bus on time. Her family certainly had the money to hire a chauffeur, but she decided early on in her high school career that she'd take the bus to be able to spend more time with her friends. It was days like these, where the threat of having to walk was nearly realized, that she regretted that decision; and to believe that a pair of panties could have forced that on her. Perhaps ditching the panties would have been worth it if she hadn't found them in the dryer.

    Jen grinned slightly, imagining once again what it would be like to show up to school with a skirt and no panties. It's not like she'd get in trouble, there's not a single guy there that wouldn't appreciate the show. Her grin got wider, her thoughts shifting towards how the boys would react, seeing her accidentally reveal herself, their pants tightening around the crotch area.

    The thoughts were beginning to excite her, as they got naughtier and naughtier, imagining situations where her skirt could get lifted and where men would crowd around her. She was lost in lust, and failed to notice her ears as they began to change, forming noticeable points at the tips and growing just slightly bigger and longer. The thoughts only got more extreme, as her hand started to travel down the front of her skirt.

    "Heya, Jen!"

    Jennifer gave a startled yelp. She had completely stopped paying attention to the world around her, and hadn't realized the bus had stopped again to pick up more students. It also just so happened that her friend, Lucy, had hopped on and spotted her in the back.

    "O-Oh! Hey Lucy! Have a seat!" Jen said, motioning to the spot next to her, while obviously blushing. Lucy happily took a seat, seemingly unaware of the fun Jen was having in her own headspace. Uncertain of this, Jen tried to start a conversation before it could be questioned.

    "Sooo umm, how's your morning been?"
