    You follow a squirrel, a new low.

    Well, it would seem that your choices involve either wandering aimlessly or following a squirrel that, could - for all you know - end up being just as aimless as you are. The squirrel runs ahead of you, obviously quite a bit faster than your own human legs can carry you. It occasionally gives a strange hiss sound to indicate the direction you should follow. The forest gets darker as you travel, the trees above letting less and less light through. You end up tripping over the occasional root of the giant old trees surrounding you. After some time you begin to re-think your decision, but the squirrel has taken you on so many different twists and turns that you're not sure you could find your way back to the clearing if you did decide to turn back. You're nearly ready to try anyway when you finally see something up ahead. At first it just looks like another clearing, albeit a smaller one. As you hike, however, you begin to see the color of well-tanned skin. Encouraged by this you continue along that path, the squirrel giving you encouraging noises as it heads off ahead. As you trudge the form begins to take a recognizable shape, though as you get even closer you realize it's not quite as recognizable as you might think. When you break through the forest to the tinier clearing you see her.

    She's a human, sort of. She reclines against a large old oak tree. Her skin shows the signs of a woman that has spent much time out doors. Her wavy, shoulder length light brown hair grows in a mop visible beneath a broad-brimmed green hat that's pulled over her eyes as she rests her hands behind her head and dozes. She wears a very short forest-green robe with loose sleeves. That, however, is where the similarities to a human woman end. Her feet, one crossed over her leg, have a coating of greyish brown fur and resemble paws more then human feet. Behind her she rests on a large tail of the same color. A pair of squirrel ears poke out from beneath the hat. You are astounded by the look of her.

    The squirrel you followed runs up to her and crawls up to her shoulder. She lifts her hat, revealing a pair of large brown eyes. She gives the squirrel a light peck on the forehead before turning her gaze towards you. She gives the squirrel a stroke along the back, revealing that her hands as being mostly human, but with a coating of fur along the backs of her hands. She sighs and stretches out her legs and stands.

    "Well, well, sweety, what is it that you've brought me? A lost traveler looking to find his way home? Perhaps a new devotee seeking to pay his fealty to Sciura the Squirrel Queen? Perhaps a bit of both?" she says and addresses you. You're reminded, upon seeing this lovely woman, that you are still very much under the influence of the root vegetable. She seems to notice as well "Hmm...perhaps you're looking for something else? To sooth the animalistic urge that even humans have." she says and runs her hand across your chest. "That could be arranged as well...for a price." She then turning around, brushing her tail against your face. She walks forward a bit, her tail sashaying behind her and turns around "Well explain yourself, human." she says.
