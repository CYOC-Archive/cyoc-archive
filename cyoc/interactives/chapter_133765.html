    The box of vials laid by the door of 72, Elenore Road, the home of the Jones family. The Jones' family, a family of five, are rather ordinary.

    Albert Jones, 43, is the husband and father of three. Cynical, strict and tired. He has a current job marketing for the mining corporation.
    Charley Jones, 41, is the wife and mother of three. Caring, clueless, and bubbly. She works as a gym instructor and is a secret alcoholic.
    Linda Jones, 19, is the first born child of Albert and Charley. Stingy, intelligent and ignorant. She is currently on her first gap year, and wants another, rather than going to college.
    Axal Jones, 14, is the second born child of Albert and Charley. Angsty, rebellious and loud. She once tried to start a riot at her school.
    Jamie Jones, 10, is the third born child of Albert and Charley. Curious, kind and interested in the weird and strange. He is usually bullied at school and at home, but his father and mother respect and praise him. This still doesn't give an ego, as he is unsure if his mother and father do actually care for him.
