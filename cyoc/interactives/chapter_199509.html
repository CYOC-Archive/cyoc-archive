    As Ty was walking the halls of his school, he passed one of the larger classrooms where he had math class. Thinking the classroom was empty, he stepped inside, only to notice that there was another boy named Jimmy sitting there, all alone, at a desk in the very back row. The boy looked lonely and sad. His eyes were red from what seemed like tears. He was also 15 years old, just like Ty.

    “Hey, man. What’s going on? What are you doing in here, all by yourself?” Ty asked him, as he sat down at the desk next to him.

    “All the other guys in class have brothers or cousins or nephews they get to hang out with. They get to watch football games, play basketball, or play soccer or do guy-stuff with. But, for me? I got nobody. I just have a younger sister, and she’s totally into her girly-stuff.” The boy sobbed. “I’m always all by myself!”

    “Hey, Jimmy! I have an older bro named Dustin and he never wants to hang out with me, either so I can kinda relate to you. I think it would be fun to have a sister!” Ty grinned, trying his hardest to cheer him up.

    “No, dude. I would kill to have a bigger, older bro right now, like Dustin, ya know?” Jimmy sighed.

    “Jimmy, Dustin can also be a real asshole sometimes, trust me!” Ty replied.

    “Kimberly is not interested in guy-things with me. She is in makeup and cosmetics and doing her hair and trying out women’s clothing and all that girl-stuff. I feel so alone, sometimes!”

    “Hey, your sister is very cute! But I see what you mean. Let me see what I can do for you, okay?” Ty said to him.

    Ty walked out of the room, and he slipped into the office. School was out so there was nobody there. He closes his eyes and concentrates.

    “Where can I find a good big bro for Jimmy?”

    Then, he had a vision. There was a homeless man sleeping on the bench just outside the school entrance!

    Ty snapped his fingers and the man appeared on the floor, in the office, right in front of him. The homeless guy’s name was Johnny, and he was about 24 years old. He was tall, handsome, and muscular. He worked for a grocery store, but he lost his job a few months earlier.

    “Hey, John. Johnny! Wake up!” Ty said to the lumbering man, as he slowly opened his eyes and got up from the floor.

    “Huh? How did I get in here?” Johnny wondered as he looked around the office. “I’m normally sleeping outside of this place!”

    “So, do you have a family, at all?” Ty asked him.

    “What? A family? No, I have nobody.” Johnny answered. “Who are you?”

    “I’m Ty and I have magical powers. I can give you a family and turn you into a big brother of a classmate of mine, okay?” Ty grinned.

    “Say what?”

    Ty snapped his fingers and made the adorable Kimberly – also known as Kimmy – appear in the office. She was wearing a pink dressed and she had on bright, pink leggings as well.

    “What… What am I doing here?” Kimmy wondered.

    “Okay, Johnny and Kimmy! I’m gonna merge the two of you together so you form a new, older, and bigger brother for Jimmy, who is in the classroom down the hall. You won’t remember anything about your lives anymore! You’ll become one big boy who is about 17 years old and who has a younger bro named Jimmy, who is sitting in a classroom across the hall, all by himself!” Ty explained.

    The strangest thing happened next, where Kimmy was drawn to Johnny’s larger, muscular body. As she got pulled closer to him, her arms and legs got absorbed into his body as soon as she touched him. This made Johnny grow even bigger and taller, while the man suddenly regressed by a few years. It all happened within a few seconds as Kimmy disappeared with Johnny, but the 25-year-old quickly lost a few years and became a tall, athletic 17-year-old high school junior, with big muscles, a square jawline and short, straight hair. The old, homeless man all but changed into a younger, handsome, and athletic high school student who was popular, well-liked and who was a teenage bodybuilder.

    “Whoa! What happened to me?” the new and improved Johnny gasped, as he flexed his big arms. “I gotta go check up on my bro!”

    Before Ty could even say another word, Johnny turned around, ran out of the office and he dashed into the classroom, which was just a few feet away.

    “Hey, bro! Whatcha doing in here all by yourself?” Johnny shouted at Jimmy, who looked up at the big, hulking boy in shock. “Come on! Aren’t you gonna shoot some hoops with your big bro?”

    “Oh, of course!” Jimmy grinned from ear to ear as he quickly packed his backpack and ran out the door behind his new, bigger brother as Ty looked on from a distance.

    “Last one on the basketball court is a rotten egg!” Johnny shouted as he ran out the door of the building, with Jimmy right behind him.

    “Hey, wait for me!” Jimmy hollered, trying to keep up with his big bro.

    “There, another happy customer!” Ty grinned as he stepped outside and smiled as he observed the two brothers on the basketball court, having fun. “Who can I change, next?”

    Ty looked around, searching the area for another kid to change…
