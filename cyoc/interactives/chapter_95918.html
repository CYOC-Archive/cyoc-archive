    A Useful New Power...

    You jump out of bed in a cold sweat, you look around your bedroom. You let out a sigh of releaf, "It was just some crazy Nightmare." you say to youself. You get out of bed, just as you begin to think about what your going to do today, a horrable smell enters your nose, a smell of Blood and Decay, and its coming from you!

    You look down at your clothes, they are soaked in Blood! "NO NO NO NO!" You begin to panic as your run to your bathroom. Your face is normal, but it and your clothes are covered in Blood! You look at your hands, they are also covered in blood, there's even blood under your finger nails. You feak out! "S-So I..... I REALLY JUST KILLED SOMEONE!?!" Suddenly, you hear a voice, "Ya, so what? Its not like she was Human or anything." You look up at the mirror, in it, you see the twisted version of you from earlier, "She was just some weird Skunk Girl thing that wanted to turn you into her Skunk Husband or something against your will! Is that what you wanted?" You look at your reflection, "Well, no, I didn't, but did you... did I HAVE to kill her?" you ask. "Ah, I see," Says your reflection, "You have a problem will killing people! I understand! Only a Psyco Killer would be Ok with killing someone. Well, how about this? I'll let you call the shots! You don't want me to kill someone, just tell me!" He says with a smile.

    You look up at that face in mirror, "What are you?" you ask. "Hmm? Me? Heh, just think of me as a new Power." He says with a laugh. "Power?" you ask. "Yes," he replys, "Your whole life, you've been treated like a push-over, been bossed around by everyone. Your sick of it! So in that moment, your mind finally snaped, and created me!" You think back on your life, and he's right! Peolpe have always treated you like you were nothing! With a power like this, noone will ever do that again! You smile as you think of what could do with your new "POWER".

    "Well," he says, "What do you think?" You look up at him, "I get to call the shots right?" He nods, "Of course! I won't kill a person or even so much as lift a finger unless you give me the Ok first!" He says. "Good," you say, "Then let's do this!"

    "Alright!" He says, "But first, let's take a shower and wash this blood off of us!" "Right" you say. You put your clothes in the washer a get in the shower. As you wash the blood off, you begin to wonder what you should use your new power for.

    What do you do now?
