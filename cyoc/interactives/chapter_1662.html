    Let's Go To The Zoo!

    Story index
    added 21 years ago

    It’s been over 6 months since the new zoo was open in town. For a town of your size it’s a big zoo. It has almost any animal you can think of including lions, tigers, bears, koalas, wolves, coyotes, elephants, a huge aquarium for the marine mammals, a reptile exhibit, and even a petting farm for the kids.

    It’s in the local paper every few days talking about new acquisitions and exchanges with other zoos. To your surprise the zoo already has an excellent breeding program with rare animals giving birth to offspring that other zoos have been trying for decades to do.

    Even more surprising to you is how many people flock to see the zoo. They come from all over the country to see it, they fly in, see the zoo and when they leave they plan their next visit. Could this zoo be THAT good?

    But the crowds were big and work has kept you busy.

    But yesterday you received some free passes from work to visit the zoo. With your free tickets in hand you decide to visit the new zoo!

    What do you do now?
