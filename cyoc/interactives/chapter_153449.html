    As you are sucked into the portal you feel your body changing. Bones snapping and reforming stronger, your skin getting smoother and stretching out. Muscles that weren’t there before suddenly coming alive with electricity. Organs shifting around and changing. Your face altering to become rounder and more amphibious. All your body hair was removed. You had no clue what was happening to your body. You felt your skin get smoother and rubber like, your bones get thinner cracking and reforming in new shapes, then they were over.

    You opened your eyes surprised to find yourself surrounded by a blue sky and swamp. However the swamp was several thousand feet below you, and getting closer at an alarming rate.

    You flailed your arms uselessly in the air in the vain hope your changes gave you wings of some kind. No use. And the ground was now heading towards your face at uncomfortable speed.

    You hit a body of water. Surprisingly though, you were unharmed and barely felt it more than a belly flop. At least you were sturdy.

    You swam to the surface then sat on the edge and rubbed your head and stood up, only to fall back down again on your chest, this time however you landed on something softer. You looked down and saw something squishy had broken your fall. You let out a sigh of relief only to fall on your face in the water for real. Your neck sack had expanded breaking your fall, then deflated when you opened your mouth.

    You looked down at your hands and saw four long green webbed fingers on each. Next your body which was lean but still muscular... and also green and slimy.

    You were a frog person.

    Every part of your body was amphibious, lean, muscular, and green. Your had a body of an Olympic swimmer, legs were muscular and your toes were also webbed. Your body now had a set of tight yellow abs on your stomach, and oddly enough a belly button. That was odd since last time you checked frogs hatched from eggs so they wouldn’t need a bellybutton. But they also didn’t have hands or were as big as you. Meaning you must be a very human like frog. That left an important question.

    What about your face?

    You found a relatively clean and reflective spot in the swamp and saw your reflection for the first time.

    You were a frog...
