    "Wow, a new Kingdom Hearts," Jared said to himself.

    This looked like a remake of the very first game. Jared was really good at this game, having beat it many times before. He proceeded to
    put the game in his system, and turned on the power. The game appeared to be like the original. Jared continued to play and was able to
    find a few glitches. Next thing he knew, it was 2AM. He was over a quarter through the game by now. He sees a new world to travel to in
    the game.

    It was one that hadn't been in the original. It was just called Earth. He selected it and continued to play the game. All of a sudden his game
    shut off. He figured it was another glitch. He tried to turn his system back on but the power wasn't working on it.

    Suddenly his TV lit up! It appeared as though something was coming through the screen. Whatever it was took a step out of his TV, then
    another. As it emerged, Jared's jaw dropped in awe. It appeared to be Sora from his game!

    "Who are you?" Sora spoke. "What have you done with my friends??"

    "I haven't idea what you're talking about," Jared replied. "One moment, I was playing the game you're in, the next moment you're in my
    house."

    "Oh then I must have made it out of this game finally!!" Sora said grinning.

    Jared went up to Sora and had to touch him to make sure he was real. The moment they touched, Jared felt a slight shock. Sora just
    continued grinning. It was as if he knew some thing Jared didn't.

    Jared felt the shock flow through his body and shuddered. He felt like Sora was getting taller! Sora's height seemed to shoot up as his
    baggy clothes began to get tight. Jared noticed his clothes were feeling looser on him! Sora wasn't just growing. Jared was shrinking!! He
    looked down at his arms and saw them get thinner, even though his hands appeared to stay the same size.

    He felt his facial hair suck back into his body, as the hair on his head grew, and spiked up tall. Sora grew some slight stubble that looked
    like Jared's. the hair on his head receding into his body a bit, and flattening down. Sora's arms and legs appeared to thicken as he
    reached 6'. Jared continued shrinking down to Sora's height, as Sora reached Jared's height. Sora's clothes had now become tight on his
    new frame, his jacket ripping slightly. Jared's clothes looked huge on his smaller frame.

    Jared's sweats slightly slid down, revealing his now super scrawny legs! Jared's feet grew bursting through his socks. His feet now
    appeared clown sized! Sora's feet shrunk making then slip around in his overly large shoes. Sora looked like Jared, only in his own
    clothes! Jared caught a glimpse of himself in his reflection on the window. He looked like Sora, only in his own clothes!! His clothes were
    practically falling off now, except his socks which were now way too small!

    Sora laughed, "I'm free! Finally! I've been waiting for someone like you to get me out of there."

    Jared couldn't believe it. Sora sounded just like him now!!

    "Now what are you going to do with me?" Jared noticed how kiddish his own voice sounded now. Just like Sora's!!

    Sora just laughed, and Jared could feel himself fainting from shock.

    Jared woke up and...
