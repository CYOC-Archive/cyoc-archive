    The various members of the park had been horrified, scared, and many crying at the sight. Vixi's murder had been silent and unseen... Liam and Lucas' murder had been in broad daylight. Lilith's message to Circe was clear, Lilith could do what she wanted, when she wanted, however she wanted, and none could ever hope to restrain her. Circe hoped their shared love for Jack would keep things from spiraling out of control even worse.

    "Attention everyone... for the tragedy of having lost three of our family today... all construction work is suspended for the rest of the day. I shall focus all my efforts on restoring those slain back to us, your goddess gives her word."


    +++


    Kellie marched defiantly into the Cavern of Mirrors, knowing exactly what she'd march out as. If Lilith wanted to play hard ball... so would she. She'd use the power Circe was offer, she'd use it to the hilt! She'd use it to protect those she loved. To protect those she loved, she'd give up anything, well, almost anything, she wouldn't become a mass murderer or torturer, Merlin had taught her better than that. If things were going to spiral out of control with Lilith losing it and becoming more murder happy and with no one able to stand up to her... Kellie would... even if Kellie couldn't kill her, she'd still find a way with her friends to push her back!

    Kellie looked angrily at the reflections. Herself as a dragon. herself a hydra. Herself as a Deep One. Herself as a kitsune. They reflections all KNELT before her, turning their heads and pointing. Kellie knew what she wanted. What she'd become. And nothing on Earth was going to stop her! She knew what she was risking. But Lilith had made clear that if they didn't prepare themselves with everything they had, Lilith would murder them. Jack might not. Maybe. He was raised by that monster after all. While he did insist that he was just going to champion the dark to keep it from being wiped away by the light.

    "So I'll make sure the light doesn't get wiped away by Lilith's darkness, like you don't want the dark wiped away by Merlin's light Jack." Kellie said.

    She found the mirror, standing proud, tall, defiantly, protectively. Kellie stood proudly and waited for the change to happen. Clothes dissolved into nothing. Pearly white fur grew on her body. He legs became digigrated. Her feet become cloven hooves. Her finger tips became incased in armor. A lion's tail grew from her spine. Even as her face pushed into an equine form, she was clearly not a horse. Her hair grew into a beautiful mane. She felt entropy's hands wretch away from her like she was a hot coal, the count down clock of her individual cells ceasing. And finally, the spiral horn grew from her forehead, and Jackie felt magical power on a level she'd never known surge through her. She was now practically radiated white magic. And... she understood.

    "All things in balance."

    Lilith at her core sought her freedom. And would reject any force that might restrain her to protect that freedom. Baga Yaga was nurturing and dangerous, both at the same time. And Circe sought to bring to the surface what was already there. They had their purpose in creation. Same way Jack now grew to fulfill the role originally filled by Morgonna (a burden the new Morgonna was free to never carry again).

    Good and evil were not the same thing as light and dark.

    "I understand." The anthro unicorn said.

    She left the fun house... Franchise actually broke down in tears at how beautiful the unicorn mare was.
