    As Emma strutted away from the house and Riley and Ashley rejoined the rest of their sorority sisters downstairs, the camera was left alone on the floor in the upstairs room. It stayed untouched for some time until it was discovered by two drunken jocks.

    “Hey bro, come check out this, like, ancient thing from the 1900s or something.” Said the first jock, Brad, as he picked up the Polaroid. “Bro, reckon you can catch it if I throw it to you from the balcony?”

    “Bro, that sounds dope! You’re so on, dude.” Said Chad, the second jock. He rushed down the stairs and into the house’s backyard as fast as he could. With liquid courage coursing through his veins, he called out to Brad on the balcony to throw it.

    “Go long, dude!” Brad yelled as he hurled the camera like a football towards his bro. Unfortunately, Chad misjudged his surroundings and tripped over a rouge deck chair, leaving the Polaroid to land harshly on the concrete patio below.

    “Whoops” Was the final word Chad muttered before the camera, battered and bruised, erupted into a large FLASHBANG! Signifying its destruction.

    Destroying a magical artifact has dire consequences as it can lead to the chaos magic housed inside to escape, transforming reality in a potentially permanent, chaotic way.

    With its setting stuck in “Twinning” mode, the camera decided it would rewrite reality one final time before it broke completely by transforming everyone into a twin of one random person. Reality will change so that humanity will have always been an identical species.

    That being said, who does the camera choose to transform everyone into a twin of?
