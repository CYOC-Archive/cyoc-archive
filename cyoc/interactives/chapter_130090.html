    Because Spring was considered a season of new beginnings, the number one problem with Animalians began to show itself. Because Spring was when many animals began seeking mates, that also began to show itself in Animalians as well. Fortunately, there were many solutions planned to keep it from getting out of control.

    While standard methods of birth control, such as the pill could work on humans. It was decided to include one that was completely revamped and safe for Animalians. The resulting Pill not only prevented conception, but also helped keep "heat and rut" down. Also there was the new Trojan X-Prime condoms that helped with keeping infection down alongside Animalian Virus Vaccinations, which were administered at half price in honor of "Spring Fever Season."

    Because of all the single Animalians that still hadn't found bondmates, a professional matchmaking service was started. This often involved many features found on dating sites, but there were other features included that were specific to Animalians. While many people were a little disappointed on their initial meeting, there was an allowance to up to three dates to get to know people before you could actually commit.

    There were also a number of Honeymoon Vacation packages available through the professional matchmaking service, mainly to Mieni Atoll or The Lost City of Vilcabamba. There was also a place on the website where new developments from the Colonies could be viewed. One of the more amusing ones of these was the addition of a "Muscle Beach" on Anka Islet. The reason for this was the old Tom and Jerry Episode "Muscle Beach Tom". It was rather amusing to see Animalians working out nearly naked (some modesty preserved for safety).

    Because of all the things that was going on, security in Animalia was at an all time high, but this was deemed necessary to keep accidental infections to a minimum.
