    "Transform" shaid exclaimed, not really believing the curse but wanting to get it out of the way as quickly as possible. She was curious to find out what the little pervert had planned. A thin bead of sweat had also begun to travel down the side of her face as she waited on the outcome of this sick game. Mostly in anticipation but also apprehension as she realized she could not move the phone away from her ear.
    "Okay that is what I was expecting. For your transformation you will become the vector for a new disease I like to call Jenfluenza. Anyone catching the disease will start changing into you a little bit at a time when they sneeze and won't start changing back until 48 hours after the transformation is complete. Everyone will transform at different speeds depending on how healthy they are or how drastic the change is and some people may not change all the way back once the disease is finished. Being patient zero though you are special. Instead of changing into yourself you'll change into the first person you infect. All the other rules of the disease apply even the possibility of not changing all the way back. Luckily once someone recovers from Jenfluenza they can't catch it again so the epidemic will eventually run its course but it is going to be a wild ride until then." Kyle smirked, his smugness almost palpable over the phone. "you can hang up now." he laughed before the line on his end went dead. Jen stared blankly at the phone before hanging up as well. The curse couldn't possibly be real could it?
