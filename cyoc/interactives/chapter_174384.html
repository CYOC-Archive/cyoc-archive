    Arriving at the Costume Store

    As we exited the arcade Sam still held on to my hand. “Which direction is the costume store?” Sam asked excitedly.

    Instead of responding to her question I just fixed my gaze on our intertwined hands. “Seriously which...” Sam cut herself off when she realized what I was staring at. She quickly yanked her hand away from mine as her face went red with embarrassment. “Shut up!” She said trying to sound tough.

    “I didn’t say anything.” I said with a grin. “Anyways the costume shop is down that way.” I said pointing to the end of the mall where the shop was located. “I can hold your hand on the way there if you are worried about getting lost.” I said in a teasing tone.

    “Ha Ha. Very funny.” Sam said rolling her eyes.

    On our way to the costume store my phone dinged with a text. It was a text from Ali at the coffee shop. She wanted to know if I was still up for bikini shopping with her and Sandy tonight. With as crazy as the day had been, I almost forgot about those two. I sent her a text back saying that I would meet up with them at 5pm, which was only a few hours from now. Luckily the place they wanted to meet at was here in the mall so I could just hang out with Sam until it was time to meet them. “Maybe I should bring Sam along.” I thought as I saw Ali’s smiley face response pop up on my phone.

    “Wait.” I said stopping before heading into the costume shop. “How is this going to work?” I asked Sam.

    “What do you mean? I get to pick a costume to try on. How is this that complicated?” Sam said.

    “Well I don’t want any accidental changes happening if I am shopping with you. We don’t know how this all works, so who knows what could happen....” My phone started ringing. Not recognizing the number I quickly silenced it. “Anyways, who knows what could happen in there.” My phone dinged signaling that I had a voicemail. “Ugh. Let me check this real quick.” I said as I pulled up the voicemail.

    The voicemail was from the old woman who had given me the ring. I listened as she explained how she showed up at my office, but I wasn’t there. Apparently my co-workers had explained the last minute day off, and she wanted to meet tomorrow. She ended the message by saying to call her back....

    “Sam, I have an idea. I need to call this person back ASAP. So why don’t you go in and find a costume to try on while I make his phone call. I’ll come in and find you once I’m done. Then you can try on your costume for me.” Before I finished speaking Sam was already heading into the store. “Sounds good!” Sam said as she hurried inside.

    I called the old woman back and after a bit of chatting we decided to meet up tomorrow morning for a Saturday brunch. As the call ended I was surprised to see that we had talked 15 minutes. “I bet Sam has a costume by now.” I thought as I dropped my phone into my pocket and headed in to see what Sam had found.
