    the pups... but they all involved the game, and the game in of itself required a level of

    effect of reproducing the Transform or Dare produced by Miss Harty BEFORE I would think

    sex was about the last thing on her priority list. While the pups were at the top of her list.

    Ameythst was a lot smarter than she ever let on, and Zack and Lawrence had both often
    questioned if such a 'bubble head' was really the best wolf to teach the next generation.
    but Martha had work along side her often enough, though Jensen's pups and Zackery's
    didn't interact as often with the gems as often as each other. None treated the two
    headed, three eyed, four breasted, four armed, two tailed shiva like she-wolf as anything
    other than beautiful (which artistically Martha knew she was), but Martha had wondered if
    Ameythst was simply under equipped for the job. Or maybe Martha felt what anyone felt
    at being one of a kind.

    And still the temptation bubbled up inside Martha, to ask Amethyst to accept a Transform
    or Dare to become a mutant like Martha was... She didn't want to FORCE it on, heavens
    no, she just prepared not to ask. But she knew once the game was gone, the door would
    close forever on that.

    Zelipe saw a woman middle aged, a loving wife, in love with the occult, but loving her
    husband more, of having a son who Zelipe knew was now his father, of having a mother
    who was not Mommy-Pearl. A woman who had been happy, a woman with a life all her
    own.

    A Zelipe remembered this had been his life... Her life. A life with a different mother and
    father who had given birth to her and loved her, of an elder sister who was now less than
    a stranger to him... If she even still existed at all.

    Zelipe was scared, knowing how much he'd forget, all those experiences, all those
    wonderful memories of being with her husband Randy... all just, gone? Never were?
    Zelipe cried. Not caring that was something only girls were supposed to do or what Bart
    would think of him for it.

    "It isn't fair."

    "No it isn't."

    Zelipe blinked at tears, "Alice?"

    "No... " Said the blond wolf girl wearing a black and red dress with funny shapes on it. "My
    name is Marie... I'm... I guess you could say I'm the Game Master now... "

    "Then fix it!" Zelipe snapped.

    Marie shook her head. Just yesterday she'd have responded in exactly the same tone, a
    little boy angry at the world for stealing things it had no right taking.

    "I'm sorry, it has to be this way. If reality doesn't heal itself, the chaos left in the world
    when Kurse tore the opening to the infinite chaos will eventually existence to fall apart on
    itself... Reality is fixing itself."

    The pale pink Zelipe blinked in confusion, "It's making sure it can't have kittens?"

    Marie blinked back and said, "No I mean it's working to compensate for the changes made
    due to chaos being introduced to the universe via the curse. Right now it's feeling the
    waters, patching up the obvious changes like the switches in parenthood between you and
    your father."

    "But I had a father!"

    "I'm sorry, now he's Zack's father... your sister is even worse off, she doesn't even have
    her parents in the previous reality as grandparents... "

    Zelipe started, he hadn't even thought about Amy. His sister had become so strange since
    their transformation into the pack... or had she always been a little bit off in her own
    world like Miss Amethyst?

    "Reality changes, as a rule, take the path of least resistance. This isn't some 'delusion' or
    'lie' where reality 'thinks' you're Zelipe of the Big Bad wolf pack. Reality knows you are
    Zelipe, and is doing it's best to compensate."

    "Then... then LET ME HAVE A TURN AT DA GAME! Let me be Zelda again.."

    "And could you leave your mother Pearl? You father Zack? Your sister Amy? The rest of
    the pack? Could you?"

    "Let's.. let's... LET'S CHANGE'EM ALL BACK!"

    ".... That's not possible anymore, maybe before Kurse died, maybe, but at this point trying
    to reverse the healing reality is doing would only make things worse... it could cause the
    wounds to tear open again and let the chaos back in... I'm sorry Zelipe... Any more major
    changes to reality with the game could be dangerous since reality is in flux... reality might
    just throw up it's hands and forsake us all. We'd be dead."

    Zelipe shivered. Still sniffing, "But, it's just so wrong."

    "My Other Mother wanted to make sure we'd lament for defeat when she fell.... If it's any
    consolation, you'll always be with your sister. Your father -does- love you. And if it
    means anything, this'll be the last misery in it's three hundred and ten years of life that
    the curse will cause."

    "... You're pretty..."

    "Thank you."

    She hugged Zelipe and stroked his head, "Dream of Harry Potter, dream of Wizard of Oz,
    dream of Dungeons and Dragons... let a past that no longer exists fade, let memories of
    things that never happened be gone..."

    Fall asleep in the dreamscape, Marie gently put the little boy down and kissed him on the
    forehead. "You're cute too."

    With that Marie left Zelipe's dreamscape, making way to Bart's, Kevin's, and Jenny's.
