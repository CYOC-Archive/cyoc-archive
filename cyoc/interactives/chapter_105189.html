    It was night now, days later after Justin had his experience with Mr. Green. Despite that short time Justin learned a lot about his new form.

    Doing research he had found out that the mask he found was of an old god that would curse certain men that Native Americans worshipped before the first American Colonies formed.

    The mask granted the user unlimited power.
    Teleportation and Summoning Demons were some of them, though Justin had yet to find out how to do that.

    It was early in the afternoon and Justin was in his room using his laptop when he suddenly got an email. Checking his email he found out it was his crush's boyfriend Jack but most of the kids called him Tiger since he was the star member of the school soccer team.

    The email said. "I don't know what you did to get on Mr. Green's good side but I don't like the idea of you being on the soccer team. GTFO and don't even think of joining. I don't care what muscles you have now, your still the same wimp.

    PS: Stay away from my girl."

    Justin could feel his anger rising. Justin had never done anything to Jack, sure he had a crush on Amy but that was hardly any reason to hate a guy.

    Days ago Justin wouldn't have been able to do anything but now he could. Getting out of his room he told his parents he was going out and that he would be back soon. They didn't suspect a thing and told him to be careful.

    He walked down the street his keen senses picking up every detail, his eyes growing purple slightly as he walked to "Tiger's" house

    Justin would show him a real animal. It didn't take long before he got to his destination. Jack's house was quite large having two stories and a massive backyard.

    Justin looked around and made sure nobody was watching when he easily jumped the five foot high fence and entered Jack's backyard.

    Justin knew Jack's parents weren't home due to Jack's grandmother being sick and his parents were taking care of her while Jack got on with his school work.

    Justin could hear Jack laugh inside watching tv with someone else on the second floor.

    Justin went out into the outside patio and snuck in Jack's house through an open window no one seeing him as he did it.

    Jacks home was fancy and rich. A large flatscreen took up most of a wall and several game consoles littered the floor. A couple of deer heads decorated the some of the walls and a mimi fridge stood next to the couch.
