When Cindy regained focus she noticed that the sight of her mother's feet under the stall door had disappeared, and a opening of said door revealed the stall was empty. Before she could contemplate the matter further Cindy heard a commotion.

    "Who are woo? What are woo doing ta me? What is going on?" a confused young child's voice rang out causing her to turn and see a woman tending to a young baby girl who couldn't be no older than 2. From what she was able to see the toddler was in the process of getting her diaper change d, lying back down on the fold down table with her dress pulled back to reveal a crinkly set of white padding. All too quickly a foul and pungent odor filled the restroom as Cindy covered her nose while the other lady appeared unfazed by the awful smell.

    "My what a big boom boom you made there Becky and we haven't even eaten lunch yet" she cooed down at the toddler. Meanwhile Cindy gave a grossed out look at the sight of the pile of brown solids nestled in the seat of the girl's padding. "Let's get you a fresh diaper shall we?" That last sentence seemed to snap the baby girl out of her confusion.

    "I don't need diapas, Imma a gown up" she indignantly shouted as the woman pulled the soiled diaper out from under her bottom, giving her little girl a stern disapproving look, "Now Becky what did I tell you about shouting?" she carefully warned the 2 year old as she fetched a packet of baby wipes.

    It was at this point in the conversation that Cindy figured out the toddler was her mother and the other lady was their "new" mommy, her plan to regress her over bearing mom with the strange phone having worked. And apparently she also remembered being an adult which was good cause now she'd understand what it feels like to be treated as younger than you are. And as a bonus her mom was no longer potty trained and have to suffer the humiliation of diapers.

    Returning her attention to scene before her Cindy watch with a small bit of satisfaction as her baby mom had started to put up more of a fuss, kicking her little legs at the strange woman who was attempting to clean her privates with a wipe. "Stop it Becky. Stay still so I can wipe you" the woman replied starting to get exasperated with her young charger's misbehavior. Instead of obeying her increased her struggles to the point where the lady got angry. "Okay Becky that's enough. If you don't settle down right now I'm going to have to spank you" she warned in a scary tone. That coupled with the fact that the other lady towered over her frightened her mom into calming down, the toddler allowing herself to be cleaned with the wipes. However the awfulness of the situation didn't leave her and her mother being unable to act out began to quietly cry at her unexpected misfortune, having gone from an independant adult to a baby girl in diapers. "That's a good girl, don't worry momma's gonna take care of you" she replied as she rushed to complete the change thinking Becky was upset at having a dirty dirty, which was sort of true. Disposing of the used wipes in the old padding and wrapping it in a bundle she quickly powdered and taped a clean one onto the toddler, readjusting her dress afterwards. She then picked up her lightly crying baby girl gently rocking and patting her back.

    "Cindy would you mind throwing this away for me while I put your sister in her stroller?" she asked offering her the wrapped up dirty diaper and also addressing Cindy for the first time since the woman magically appeared. Cindy almost didn't hear being shocked at seeing her former mommy actually crying like a baby. Pocketing the phone she walked over and hesitantly accepted the poop filled padding, sticking out her tongue as she quickly threw away the offending garment in the nearby trashcan. Turning around she saw her new mommy had succeed in getting her new little sister to stop crying and was in the middle of strapping her into the stroller.
