    "Thanks ladies. I can't tell you how hard it is moving to a town with me and my dad and not knowing anyone." Cassidy said giving a tale that was only half of a lie.

    "You don't have any other family in the area?" Naomi asked.

    "Well, there is me Aunt Nat, she's...something else. But for years it's just been me and my dad and it's been lovely." Cassidy said with a loving sigh.

    "Okay, so is your aunt on your dad's side or your mom's side?" Diane asked.

    "Maybe you could arrange a little date night for him and her and see if they connect?" Naomi suggested.

    "He is NOT interested in her." Cassidy said gritting her teeth keeping herself from yelling at them.

    "Besides, my aunt is....well, bisexual, and my dad just has never really gotten used to that side of her." Cassidy explained to them.

    "Oh, perfect. My dad won't be able to pick me up today, I've got to ask my aunt to come get me." Cassidy said as she began texting Natalia.

    "So do your parents pick you up as well?" Cassidy asked them.

    "My mom is actually picking us up today after school." Naomi said not really thinking anything of it.

    "Cool, your mom would like my aunt, they might have a lot to talk about. My aunt could always use a good friend." Cassidy said as she finished texting Natalia.

    'I've got a bisexual mom for you to seduce, pick me up after school and work your magic. She may know a lot about these wizards.'
