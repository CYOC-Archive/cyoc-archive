    Mike was stunned and thrilled to see that the now-giant crowd looked at themselves in confusion and simply filed silently onto the train. The woman's giant steps took Mike onto the train himself without too much incident, though the giant foot's swings up and down made his stomach feel a little woozy like on an amusement park ride. But he took care to wrap himself tightly in the ornamental bow on her shoe, so it wasn't that bad. Fortunately, the swinging eventually stopped, as the woman found a seat on the train and sat down. Mike remained in place for a few seconds, just to make sure, but once the doors shut and the train started moving, he felt relatively confident going out and wandering around. He slowly eased himself off the side of her shoe and took a look at your surroundings.

    Across the aisle was some guy in a business suit. It was just amazing to Mike to see giants acting with the casual indifference of everyday life, but he didn't have any interest in spying on or approaching a dude. He peeked out from under his seat, being careful not to move out into the aisle where he could be stepped on, and he saw in the seat behind him, across the aisle, an older woman, apparently in her 50s or 60s. He turned back and walked the few steps back over towards the foot of the woman whose shoe he rode into the train. He looked up at her, but at this point all he could see was up to her knees. He frowned.

    Giving his neck a rest from all that looking up, Mike noticed he could walk all the way under the seats. This was a big deal, because it let him walk as far back or forward as he wanted. As long as he didn't get caught underfoot or seen, there was really nowhere he couldn't go.

    As it turned out, though, he got lucky the first time. He had gone one seat behind his gracious hostess and discovered a truly hot girl. She was wearing a pink T-shirt and jeans, and though she had blonde hair and blue eyes, and one hell of a physique, he could see a lot of thought in her eyes. She seemed like she's in her mid-20s, and she was too caught up reading a book to notice him. Best of all, her handbag was just lying there on the floor. It wouldn't be too tough at all for Mike to climb on inside and see where she took him. She looked professional and fascinating. This might be fun.

    Then, he heard a bit of a thump behind him. He turned around, and on the ground he saw another purse, this one a little more drab and gray. Mike hadn't had much time on the platform for this stuff, but now that he thought about it, that woman whose shoe he jumped onto was pretty good-looking for a woman in her mid-30s. She seemed well put together, and he was kind of curious as to what her home life or work life was like. He could climb into her purse pretty easily too.
