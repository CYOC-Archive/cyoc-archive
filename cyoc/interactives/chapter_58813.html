    The start of something foxy

    added 12 years ago A BM

    They decided on the rabbit pie.

    They waited, and they each got one.

    "Enjoy!" the waiter said as he walked off.

    The two started eating.

    After the first few bites, Jamie looked up and saw that Carla's nose had turned black and
    became triangle-shaped. Her ears had also gotten pointy, and starting to move to the top
    of her head.

    "Carla," Jamie said "your face and ears!"

    She felt her ears on the top of her head - they were now big triangles, and her face had
    formed a small muzzle.

    "Wow," she said "I like this look. You should see yourself - the same wonderful thing is
    happening to you too!"

    Jamie now realized she was right - his ears were now big triangles resting on top of his
    head, and he had a bit of a muzzle with a black nose too.

    At first he was scared, but then he liked it.

    "Why is this happening to us?" he asked smiling.

    "It's the pie!" she said happily "I can't believe I didn't see it before - it's turning us into
    foxes, and making us smarter!"

    She took another bite, and her nails turned into black claws, and she got black pawpads on
    her fingers and palms.

    "See?" she said, barely able to contain her happiness "The more we eat, the foxier we get."

    "But I don't want to be a fox." Jamie said.

    "Don't worry." she assured him "I've already calculated, and if each of us finishes their pie,
    then we'll be anthro-foxes, with genius-level intellect! And we'll be so good-looking too!"

    "I guess it makes sense," Jamie said "afterall, foxes eat rabbits, right?"

    "Exactly!" Carla said "Now let's finish our pies so we can be super foxy!"

    In no time at all, they had finished eating and were now anthro foxes. they still had their
    hair, and they took their clothes off - their thick fur covered everything.

    They then kissed eachother passionately. As they kissed, both their tails exploded into nine
    new ones.

    They saw this, and were very happy.

    "Let's give the cook our thanks!" Carla said.

    The two ran hand -in-hand towards the kitchen and were greeted inside by more like them
    - antro kitsunes.

    They learned that it was all part of a plan to weed out the dumb from the wise - whoever
    picked anything else would become a mindless animal, but whoever was smart enough to
    pick the rabbit pie would become anthro kitsunes.

    They kitsunes had been few before, but were making a comeback in this way.

    Since they were together, Jamie and Carla were encouraged to make more kitsunes though
    giving birth to them.

    "What do you say, my foxy love?" Jamie asked smiling.
