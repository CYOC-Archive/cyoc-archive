He felt his face extend into a muzzle and convert to thick metal and his jaw hinge change into a simple flap that he could open and close, in addition to it being timed with his speech. It spread up the sides of his head. His ears, hair and nose replaced by smooth silver metal that covered everywhere but his eyes. His new cyber fox ears formed on the top of his head, and he could pivot them, too. Just like a robot.

    "Conversion 97% complete." He stated again.

    The last change was the most peculiar, to John at least. The silver metal that covered his head stopped at his "eyebrows", and on either side of his eyes and the top of his nose.
    Then, from the top, dark translucent plexiglass covered his eyes, like sunglasses. A grid of hundreds of LED's each, all blue, sprung up from the metallic skin around both eyes. Then, everything started to look very soft to him. Colors, shapes, everything. Then, his eyelids stiffened. His vision went monochrome. They fused open, as the edge of his eyes turned completely round as they changed into metal. The surface of John's eyes started to flatten and dry out as they turned into glass aperture lenses. The color returned to his vision, though everything seemed slightly pixilated as he looked from side to side. His eyes had been converted into digital video cameras.

    "Conversion 98% complete."

    John's head began to tingle. Since his new computer brain was in his chest, he no longer needed his old human brain. The only way he could describe it was to compare it to melting icicles. His mind slowly transferring downwards, like hot fudge traveling down his throat, his thoughts and memories uploaded to their new home inside his metal chest cavity. John's head buzzed as his old brain, now empty, was replaced by 3 gyroscopes, to replace his sense of balance.

    "Conversion 99% complete."

    Johns silver metallic body started to change color to resemble a foxes color scheme. His body became orange and white while his hands, feet, and the tips of his ears became a shiny black color.

    "Conversion complete. Reverting to user control mode C."

He paused for a moment, and stared at his body. Complete silence hung in the air, save for the quiet chirps of various birds, the hum of his electric motors and the sound of his cooling fan. He wasn't sure what to think, or do as he stood there in his new automated form.

    His robotic body, now feeling loose and, John had to admit, oddly comfortable, allowed him to move about again.
    "I'm glad it's finally over." He sighed.

    "Oh my god John! How did this happen to you?"

    "I don't really know. All I did was downloading something on my phone and... That's it!" John looked at his phone which was now fused to the back of his left hand, the text on its screen reading "Thank you for downloading this upgrade and conforming to this zones norm. Have a nice day!"

    "Great. I guess I'm stuck like this for the remainder of our visit.I guess you had better go up to the passengers area in my head and explore while you still can." John said as he opened up an elevator door in the heel of his right foot.
