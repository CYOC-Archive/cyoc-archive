    A Little Brotherly Coaching

    Cody Lightfoot has lived a considerably different life than others might have expected. He was one of the human kids in Lyre who didn't exposed during the Outbreak, even when his pet rabbit was uplifted. Stranger still is that he completed the rest of high school and started college without converting. It wasn't that he disliked Animalians. In fact, he was quite close to his new brother, Roger, and his girlfriend, Jessica. But as much as he liked Animalians, he was a martial arts student first. He had such a deep respect for martial arts that he chose not to convert as it would be 'cheating'. He would rather have earned his black belt on his own merit and not based on the athletic/muscular boost that becoming an Animalian would include. While Cody may not be as good as the legendary master such as Bruce Lee, he was rather respectable in his own right, particularly for being a human among Animalians.

    But that may change soon. After talking with Michiko Shikamitsu at Animalia's comic convention a couple of years ago, the two of them started dating. They were already acquainted with each other from Cody training at her father's dojo. The two of them actually made a pretty cute couple and got along on several common topics. But now it seemed things were getting a little more serious. Michiko seemed ready to take the next step with Cody, which would mean him converting into a rabbit Animalian at last. And as such, he wanted a little advice for how to make his first time with Michiko special.

    ==========

    "You're not asking for a live demonstration, are you?" asked Jessica dryly.

    "No, no, no, definitely not," said Cody hastily, "But any advice you can give would be appreciated."

    "Well, I can tell you something Shyra told me," said Roger, "If she's a virgin too, then it's going to be a little painful for her first time. As such, you should let her feel the pleasure she'll experience first before taking her virginity."

    "And, uh, how would I do that?" asked Cody, his face turning a little pink.

    For his sake, Roger whispered the instructions into Cody's ear. Though it was rather moot. For one thing, Jessica's ears could pick up every word Roger was saying. Also, she already knew what he was going to tell Cody. It was interesting to see Cody's face turn increasingly redder.

    "Another thing she'd greatly appreciate is you letting her take the lead," said Jessica, "That may also include letting her be on top first."

    "But don't overthink it," said Roger, "Your instincts will know what to do."

    "Once you have lapine instinct anyways," said Jessica.

    "I'm mostly...worried how I'd compare to other rabbits," said Cody.

    "If she hasn't been with other rabbits and you'll become one yourself anyways, then there's nothing to be worried about," said Jessica, "I know how nervous the first time may be. I was pretty nervous myself."

    "Could have fooled me," said Roger.

    "The first step's always the hardest, but it gets easier when you start getting into it," said Jessica. The other two gave her a look. "The first to remark upon seeing an innuendo gets kicked," said Jessica flatly.

    "Look, the most important thing is that you love each other, right?" asked Roger.

    "I...I think so," said Cody, "But would her family accept-"

    "What? Just because you're not full-blooded Japanese or trained in China?" asked Jessica, "Her parents are capable of making allowances for things you would be unfamiliar with in their culture. Besides, you're all going to be rabbits so does it matter what human ethnicity you were before?"

    "And if you're worried about her sister, I don't think you'll find uplifted pets very jealous. I'd be more concerned if Yumi took an interest in you," said Roger, "Not that she'd try to take you from Michiko."

    Cody nodded before asking, "Anything else?"

    "Not unless you want to get into technical detail," said Jessica, "Which I won't be demonstrating."
