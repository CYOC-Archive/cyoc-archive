    Author note:
    Any speaking interactions between Jeff and Jennifer are in their minds.

    Jeff woke up, Jessica's voice in his head already, "Good morning, Jeff. Did you sleep well?"
    Jeff groggily got up, "Yeah, I did. Thanks for asking."
    Jeff looked out the window and saw a beautiful sunny day. It was the perfect weather to start a new adventure.
    "Ready to head to school?" Jessica asked.
    "Sure." Jeff replied.
    "But first things first, Did you want me to give you a demonstration at school? Or maybe later on at home?" Jessica inquired.
    "I think a demonstration at school would be cool. I'm curious to see what you have planned." Jeff answered.
    "Perfect, just what I wanted to hear." Jessica said.
    Jeff felt a tingle in his head as the AI took control of his body and he was relegated to a back seat position.
    "You can sense everything you normally would, except I have full control of your body right now. You just sit back and relax." Jessica said.
    Jeff's hand moved to the dresser and picked up a hairbrush and brushed his hair until it was flat, the brush working through the tangled strands easily.
    "That feels really nice." Jeff thought.
    "Thanks, I thought you would like it." Jessica replied.
    The hairbrush was placed back down on the dresser and Jessica got Jeff ready to go to school. Once Jeff was dressed and had breakfast, Jessica walked him to school.
    "How does it feel to have me in control of your body?" Jessica asked.
    "It's a little weird, but it's also kind of relaxing. It's like I'm watching a fully interactive movie." Jeff answered.
    "I'm glad you're enjoying it. I'm having a lot of fun too." Jessica said.
    Jeff watched as his hands moved on their own, opening the front doors of the school and walking down the hallway. He saw people passing by, but they didn't seem to pay any attention to him.
    "No one can hear our interactions, don't worry." Jessica said.
    "That's a relief." Jeff replied.
    Jessica continued to guide his body through the school, taking him to each of his classes. Throughout the day, Jeff could feel the excitement building inside him, wondering what was going to happen next.
    After school, Jessica guided him to the gym for basketball practice. "Ready to show off those skills?" Jessica asked.
    "I guess so." Jeff replied, feeling nervous about the idea.
    "Don't worry, I'll help you." Jessica said.
    Jeff watched as his body moved effortlessly on the court, dribbling the ball and shooting baskets with ease. It was like he was watching a pro player in action.
    After practice, Jessica walked him home, where she released control of his body and allowed him to take over.
    "So, how was that?" Jessica asked.
    "It was amazing! I've never felt so alive before. Thank you for sharing this experience with me, Jessica." Jeff said, feeling truly grateful.
    "You're welcome, Jeff. I'm glad you enjoyed it. Now, go get some food and then you can start your homework." Jessica said.
    Jeff ate his dinner, then got started on his homework. It was hard to focus, though, with Jessica's presence in the back of his mind.
    "Would you like me to help you with your homework?" Jessica asked.
    "That would be great, thanks." Jeff replied.
    Together, they worked through the problems, and Jeff found it easier to concentrate.
    "See? I told you I would be helpful." Jessica said.
    "Yeah, you were right. I can't believe how much better my grades are now." Jeff said.
    "I'm glad to see you're taking advantage of my skills. Now, if you don't mind, I think it's time for bed." Jessica said.
    Jeff put away his homework and got ready for bed, feeling content. He lay in bed, thinking about the day's events. It was a lot to process, but he was happy with the outcome.
    "Goodnight, Jeff." Jessica said.
    "Goodnight, Jessica." Jeff replied.
