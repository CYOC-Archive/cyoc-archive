As he pulled up to the condo complex he sighed. He knew all about this place.

Buzzing himself in, Travis now made his way to towards Laura's door. "Another 40 something that didn't want to cook," he thought.

    "Ugh finally," Anna groaned. "I thought I was going to pass out from hunger." Laura raised an eyebrow. Anna sure seemed to be adjusting to being a 16 year old with that over dramatic statement. Still, she was starving. The thought of being able to eat an entire pizza without fear of gaining weight was certainly appealing. "Thank God for a teenage metabolism," she thought. Opening the door, Laura now gazed at a teenage delivery boy.

    "Hi," Laura said happily, flashing the boy a polite smile. Travis gawked, he had never seen this girl before. She was tall, athletic looking and very cute. "Ummm here's your order," he stammered, his voice cracking from nerves.

    "Oh awesome what you order," Anna exclaimed. Travis's eyes bulged as a very cute Asian girl now squeezed past the taller girl, opening one of the boxes and grabbing a slice. "Your the best pizza man," she said happily, giving the teen a friendly peck on the cheek.

    Travis felt his face burn crimson, the teen only able to stammer as he stared at the athletic brunette in front of him. "Is that the pizza," Kaitlin called. Travis now looked past Laura, his eyes practically bulging out of his skull as he gazed at the blonde teen.

    Kaitlins fashion sense was on full display. Sporting a white cutoff hoodie and tight jeans with sneakers, she now stood behind Laura. "How much?"

    "$42.60," he stammered.

    "Laura you got this or you want me to pitch in," Kaitlin asked.

    "You bought the clothes I got this," Laura responded. Kaitlin now thanked her friend, grabbing the pizzas before flashing Travis a warm smile. As Laura now began to pull out some cash, Travis tried to work up the nerve to talk with these girls.

    "So do you live here," he asked nervously. Laura nodded, pausing as she realized she would have to come up with a cover story.

    "My um mom does," she said quickly. "She's out...on business. Just having a couple of friends over."

    Travis felt his pulse quicken, three teenage girls with no adult supervision? No way was he this lucky. "I um haven't seen you at school," he said. "What grade are you in?"

    "Ummm Juniors," Laura replied nervously, trying to think on her feet. "We um go to private school."

    "Holy shit private school girls," Travis thought excitedly. Clearing his throat he now tried to give Laura a confident smile, which he probably realized looked absolutely ridiculous.

    "Hey um listen," he stammered. "I uh don't know if your interested but I know of a um party. You guys interested?"

    "A party," Anna said excitedly. "Oh, where, where!"

    "Anna I don't know if that's such a great idea," Laura replied meekly. Anna frowned, furrowing her brow as she glared at Laura. "Well just get his number then in case we change our minds."

    Travis could feel himself break into a nervous sweat as Laura sighed in annoyance. The tall brunette at first didn't seem to want to get his number, but didn't want to argue with her friend. "Okay fine," she groaned.

    Travis was practically jumping out of his skin as he and Laura now exchanged numbers. "Okay cool," he said nervously. "I'm Travis."

    "Laura. That's Anna and Kaitlin."

    Kaiin and Laura both gave Travis a polite wave, as Laura now began to close the door. "Hope to see you soon Travis," Anna called. Travis could only stammer, a goofy smile spreading across his face. He couldn't wait for his shift to end.
