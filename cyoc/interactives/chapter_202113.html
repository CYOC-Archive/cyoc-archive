    "Transforming Flynn was fun, but I think I can make things more fun..." Katy said to herself, looking back to the guys playing the card game and taking interest on doing something similar, since she didn't understand too much about the game, practically to her they were just saying random stuff and dropping any card on the table. That gave her the idea of creating her own card game.

    Katy started to write on the RealiTome making like the rules of the actual game rules never existed and wrote her new ones. As a really big fan of monsters and weird creatures Katy decided to make the game focused on that, the players would get transformed into a variety of monsters at their will based on their cards, the monsters would be mythical creatures, aliens, robots, fictional monsters, real animals and any type of monster that existed. Now that she decided the main idea, began to write the rules and how to play the game.

    The game would be about "Monster Points", all the players would get the same amounts of points at the start with the objective of lower the points of the rest of the players to 0. In order to lower the points the players would need to use two types of cards: the monster cards and the magic cards. The monster cards would transform the user into the creature the card displays, then after transforming the players would fight in their monstrous forms and every time they hit their opponent they earn points and lower the ones of the opponent they attacked, every fight would last a couple of minutes. The magic cards were spells the players could use to their advantage in the fight like magic attacks such as control water, fire breath (if the player is transformed into a creature that doesn't have fire breath), temporal hypnosis, magic traps and infinitely amount more of magic cards. However the players could select more monsters in one turn, turning into a fusion of the monsters they selected, this action would decrease their points but the result would be an even more powerful creature.

    The rules were:
    1. The players would need to undress before the game starts.
    2. The player with 0 points is eliminated.
    3. The first turn the players would get 1 monster card and 2 magic cards, the rest of the game they will receive a random card per turn, either a monster or magic card.
    4. The players would remain in the same form every turn until they decide to transform into a different monster.

    Katy wrote everything about the game, calling it "Monster Transformation Fight Card Game (MTFCG)", it sounded very literal but nothing more came to her mind, so she made some changes to the building as well, turning it from a boring room to a giant dome with the enough space for the monsters to fight, it was shaped like a coliseum so the people not playing could see the game from the distance. Katy made all the changes like everyone thought it was normal to see people transform into giant freak things and fighting in the campus.

    Then Katy brought all the geeks to the center of the coliseum.
    "Well guys, who will be my first opponent?" Katy smiled and said in a competitive tone, asking to some of them to play the first round of MTFCG with her.
