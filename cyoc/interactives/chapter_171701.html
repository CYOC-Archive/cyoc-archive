    The Magic Shop

    A smorgasbord of possibilities

    As Paula began to walk into the building, her mind was racing. It'd been a few hours since she had changed reality, and already she was loving every single second of it. The longer she stayed as an 18 year old, the more her mind adjusted. It was a little odd for her, remembering being only 6 just a few hours before, but it also felt... right.

    What's better, was that everyone treated he with respect for once. She had to continuously hold in her giggle of course whenever she saw little "Emily" around. The adorable little girl had been her formerly older brother, but was left as a cute girly girl without anyone else having any idea who she should be.

    Now, the real fun would begin. Paula glanced in her purse, looking with a smirk at the camera. She shut it quickly and continued to follow her family inside. They were all dressed up nice for a nice dinner with some work associates of her father. Her dad was dressed up in a nice suit, while Paula was dressed in a gorgeous long black dress.

    Her mother was dressed in a tight dress which really accented her auburn hair, and much to Paula's delight Emily was dressed in a frilly pinkish dress and adorably curled locks cascaded down her head. What Paula was really excited for, however, was the company get together. There were going to be several different nicely dressed families all across the restaurant, which left Paula with options.

    Now that her intelligence was rising, she really wanted to understand the scope of the camera's power. How many people could it change at once? How many transformation types or varieties could happen? Would anyone else be able to tell? She wanted to know everything, and changing the family's of random work associates seemed like it'd be harmless enough.

    "Hello, I'm here for the company family dinner." Oliver told the man standing outside by the podium that read "Giardino's".

    "Of course sir, head right in." The main replied, gesturing for him and the rest of them to follow.

    Once they were inside, Paula began to sweep the room for possibilities. The family sat down at a longer table, and Paula decided to focus her options towards the people at their table.

    The first group next to their family was a group of three, two parents and their younger daughter. They were hispanic, and it was clear the mother wore the pants in their family. She was dressed in a respectable pant suit which highlighted her curves, her hair straightened extensively and then tied it into a tight bun. She knew it was straightened based on how curly her roughly 10 year old daughter's hair was. The daughter seemed absent minded, while the man seemed a little out of shape, a little out of focus, and a little underdressed.

    Across from them was a larger group of four, another family but different. There was only the father, letting Paula know that the mother was either gone or divorced or something. The man was an older white guy with graying blonde hair, a scraggily beard and wore a suit that he clearly rushed to put on. Beside him was a cute 17-ish year old boy with wavy blonde hair and a dark suit. Next to him was a tween girl in a long dress, and then a small 5-ish year old boy who clearly did not want to be here as he was trying to steal his sister's phone.

    Finally, on the other end of the table opposite her family was just a couple there together. There was a strong, muscular African-American man with a shaved head and a bright suit next to a busty Irish looking woman wearing a bright red suit. There two hardly seemed to notice anything as they happily talked to one another. They both looked nice too, so Paula couldn't even tell which one worked for the company and which was the guest.

    Paula glanced around the room, and saw several other tables scattered about the restaurant. She could pick some of those if she wanted. She also noticed that a bit away was a non-company rented area where there were some empty tables alongside other people just having dinner there. She was relieved by that because if her changes made it so that they were no longer with the company, she could still observe them.

    Paula smiled.
    "This is gonna be a riot." She thought happily.

    The question is, who does she change first?
