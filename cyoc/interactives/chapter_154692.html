    Terrifying reality

    added by AnarchyNinetyNine 3 years ago S Reality alteration

    Callum crawls along the footpath. He's so humiliated, his face red. He is however a little relieved: this isn't his actual body. But it still doesn't remove the feeling of complete humiliation, and helplessness. Not with this rich kid treating him exactly like a dog. Right down to the "heel" command.

    There is also some respite considering the hands and knees of Bailey seem to be adapted to the position of being on all fours. That is due to Maggie's device creating the new terrifying reality. His hands are thicker than normal. This also has the side effect of fingers being less dexterous and useful. As for the knees, the extra padding makes it harder to stand in a bipedal position. Thus rendering it far more comfortable to lay closer to the ground. His neck also seems more inclined to be at a near 90 degree angle now.

    Callum crawls along. The jingle of the hanging tag as another constant reminder of his current situation. He feels something plastic covering 'his' privates and knows what it is. The rich kid tugs on the leash, choking him slightly every time. It's horrible being a pet human, and Callum knows it.

    As the walk continues. Callum watches as the surroundings become more affluent and the amount of human pets increases. It's already obvious that in this reality, the poor are pets and the rich are the owners. Talk about class division.

    Callum is led onto a polished marble driveway. It goes uphill and curves to the left. At the end is a giant, glass walled, white house. It has a veranda at the top and a large infinity pool overlooking the polished driveway. Callum is immediately intimidated by this sign of wealth.

    As he crawls through the front door. There is the immediate sight of a dark but modern concrete kitchen. It is empty, but it looks utterly massive.

    Callum is the led into a living room overlooking the city through a massive glass pane. The rich kid suddenly commands, "Sit." Callum immediately sits like the dog he is seen as. The rich kid smiles, ruffles 'Callum's' hair, and gives the other Snickers bar, "Good boy Bailey." Callum scoffs down the chocolate. The rich kid talks again, "You probably don't know my name. Just so you know. It's Keeden Davis. Not that it matters to you. I'm Master to you."

    Callum makes eye contact with Keeden Davis. Keeden clicks his fingers, "Oh Yeah. I've got to show/tell you something."

    An electric shock travels down 'Callum's' body and he is temperamentally blinded. Callum's vision returns to find himself looking at Keeden's knee. He looks up and Keeden laughs, "Just so you know pet. When you're in the house. You have to be shrunken down to the size of a Chihuahua. Not my choice, I would have kept you your original size, but parents."

    Keeden reaches down and picks Callum up. He laughs, "Clothes shrunk with you too. Guess I don't need to worry about getting you new clothes after all."

    Keeden places Callum back onto the soft grey carpet again and says, "If you want out. Just yap like a little dog. Realistically I've got to make you normal size again. Otherwise you're easy prey for eagles."

    Callum crawls out of the living room. Everything is so much bigger now, even more intimidating.
