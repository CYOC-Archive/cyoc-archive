    “-to be a toddler,” Kimberly said. “You had so much fun yesterday, I need to see what it’s like!” Olivia grinned.

    “I’ll give you a hint. It’s amazing.” Olivia began to rummage through a bag of clothes by the couch. “I’ve got the cutest dress for you.”

    “Actually, I want to be a boy,” Kimberly said. Olivia looked up at her. “You had so much fun being a girl yesterday that you want to do it again today. I want to see if I’ll enjoy being a boy.”

    “Sure thing! Unfortunately, I didn’t grab any toddler boy clothes. I figured if I wanted to be a toddler again today, I’d be a girl again. And I didn’t think you’d want to be a boy.” Olivia pulled out a package of diapers. “Luckily, I grabbed diapers that were meant for boys by accident. They should work.”

    “How do I do this?” Kimberly asked.

    “Put on the ring, then try to put on the diaper,” Olivia instructed, handing Kimberly the diaper.

    “Okay…” Kimberly went to the bathroom, where she removed her clothes, slipped the ring on, and then tried to put on the diaper. The moment it touched her skin, she began to shrink. Before she knew it, she was a toddler. And, since she was a naked toddler, she knew that she was a he now.

    “Everything all right in there?” Olivia called through the closed bathroom door.

    “Yeah! Can you come diaper me?” Kimberly requested.

    “You got it!” Olivia opened the door. She gasped at the sight of Kimberly. “Oh, you’re an adorable little boy!” Kimberly giggled. “Want me to call you Kim? It’s a gender-neutral name, after all.”

    “That’s great, actually,” Kim said. Olivia came over to Kim and lifted him onto the bathroom counter, where she proceeded to properly tape his diaper on.

    “There!” Olivia took the ring from Kim and slipped it into a pocket in her skirt. “I’ll give you the ring back later, I just want to make sure you don’t accidentally transform or something.”

    “No, I understand,” Kim chirped. There was a soft hiss. Kim felt warmth spread through his diaper. “Whoops.”

    “Wow, already peeing?”

    “Just a bit,” Kim replied. He looked down at his diaper. The cartoon cars and trucks that indicated when the diaper needed to be changed hadn’t faded at all. “I’ll be good for a while still.”

    “Good.” Olivia picked Kim up. “We need to get you some clothes so that you don’t spend the entire day in just a diaper.”

    “I agree.” Kim looked up at Olivia. “Where are we going to get the clothes from?”
