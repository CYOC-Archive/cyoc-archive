    Meanwhile outside, a small, cute, black haired, pale skinned girl reaches Jennifer's front door. This girl's name was Amy. Amy and Jennifer had been friends for almost 2 1/2 years, ever since Jennifer's parents first hired Amy to tutor her. Jennifer, being the chatty person she was, quickly warmed up to the girl and the two became good friends.

    However, Amy and Jennifer knew she would never get along with any of Jennifer's cheerleading group so they had been keeping their friendship a secret. It was very easy though, since Amy and Jennifer kept using the tutoring excuse to keep Amy coming over, even after Jennifer's grades had all improved to A's. In fact, for the past couple months Jennifer had been spending more time with Amy than the rest of her cheerleader groupies. Jennifer just felt she could be more natural around her.

    This morning Amy had come over to get her calculator she had left yesterday, and proceeded to knock on the door. "Uhg..COMING" Jennifer shouted, getting to the door fast. Opening the door quickly, Jennifer looked down to see Amy. "Hey Jenny, just came to get my calculator I left here. Mind if I go get it?" Amy asked, already coming in. "Sure" was all Jennifer could say before she got a sudden head rush. While Amy ran up the stairs to Jennifer's room, Jennifer's changes began.

    First Jennifer felt herself get shorter and shorter, until she had gone from 5'7" to 5'1". Next, Jennifer's long platinum blonde hair shortened into a shoulder length cut and changed to a sandy blonde. Then she felt her bra loosen as she went from a C almost D, to an A almost B. Her other bodily changes were rather minor though, as her face stayed almost the same, except for a few freckles, and her nails shortened back to her finger tips. After her body was done, her clothing made a quick change too. Jennifer had been wearing her cheerleading uniform with her hair in a ponytail, but as things rearranged themselves, she found herself wearing a white button-up blouse, tight blue jeans, and her hair was being held back by two white hair clips.

    With her changes done, Jennifer, stumbled into the downstairs bathroom to get a better look. In the mirror she saw a very cute, and kinda nerdy version of herself. She was amazed that not only was the curse real, but it really had changed her. No longer did she have her tan and fit body that any guy would kill to get with, but instead she looked small, thin, and innocent.

    While Jennifer was lost in her new look, Amy had found her calculator, and was now downstairs looking for her. "Jenny, where did you go? We can't be late to school." "I'm in the bathroom. I'll be out in a second" Jennifer shouted back, a little surprised that her voice sounded just a little higher than normal. Jennifer went to the front door where Amy was waiting for her. "Ah, well there you are" Amy said, walking up to her. "Yeah, sorry, just had something I needed to wash off of my...." Jennifer stopped. She had, up until now, forgotten about the first part of the curse, but looking at Amy she remembered. She now found Amy to not just be cute, but beautiful. "Your what?" Amy said snapping her out of her daze, "Your face? I can fix that." And with that Amy wrapped her arms around Jennifer and began to kiss her deeply.

    It was now, during the kiss, that the last bit of the curse took effect. Jennifer now began to remember a past where her and Amy were free to be friends without social constraints, and that at some point they had become a lot closer than just friends. Finally breaking the kiss, Amy, who was now looking at Jennifer eye to eye, said, "Ok, we don't have anymore time for that. We need to get to class. Let's get a move on." And with that her and Jennifer left for school.

    Jennifer's mind was swimming with new memories, and thoughts as she compared her situation to her old life. How much of her life had the curse really changed? And would she find a way to change everything back to the way things were? Did she even want to go back to her old life?
