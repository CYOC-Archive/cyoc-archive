    It was just a couple of minutes till it was October 31, 2010 on the east coast of the
    United States and the darkness was waiting to pounce. It had waited centuries for the
    chance to spread itself again and that chance was quickly approaching. It would
    transform the inhabitants of the nation's capital first to establish a stronghold and
    spread out from there. It emitted a low, evil laugh that would strike fear into the hearts
    of mortals. The number of creatures it could create from that one city was high it was
    sure. Many of it's inhabitants had the seed of darkness the darkness needed to
    transform them into creatures of darkness. Of course they also had the seed of light
    like all humans did but the seed of darkness was stronger.

    The light would be a problem. As soon as the darkness started spreading the light
    would seek to transform those whose seeds of light were stronger. The darkness had
    a plan though. It would create an endless night so the light would have a more difficult
    time transforming it's champions and it's champions would be weaker without the light
    of the sun to give them strength. The darkness didn't see any way it's plan could fail
    and laughed again.

    Midnight came and the darkness burst upon the city laughing as it transformed the
    inhabitants. As it did so it cast the spell to shroud the east coast in eternal night. It
    created werewolves, witches, goblins, headless horsemen, zombies, vampires, and
    any other creature it could think of to create fear in those it couldn't directly transform.

    Meanwhile the light sensed the darkness starting to spread. The only good thing is
    even though the darkness could shroud the east coast in eternal night it was slow
    transforming people so it would take some time for the inhabitants of the east coast to
    be transformed. The problem the light had now was how to transform it's champions.
    With it being night it would need an hour to prepare to transform a few champions.
    Also the light knew that there were not many who had a strong seed of light within
    them. The light knew that most of the population wouldn't be transformed directly by
    either it or the darkness.

    As the darkness transformed more people beyond the nation's capital the light
    prepared itself and chose those it would transform. It would take precise timing to
    transform it's champions before the darkness could cast it's eternal night spell on the
    next section.

    Midnight came to the central time zone and the light sent the transformation wave to
    it's chosen champions. The darkness was fast though and some of the transformations
    were interrupted causing some weird side effects. Those that did complete the
    transformation became elves, fairies, nature spirits, and others that would fight against
    the darkness. Those that had their transformation interrupted became mermaids,
    centaurs, white witches, pixies, ghosts, and others caught between the light and dark.
    This happened two more times in the mountain and pacific time zones leaving the
    continental United States shrouded in endless night.

    Who is transformed and for which side?

    (AN: For those adding branches to this story the darkness and the light don't transform
    people into inanimate objects. That's not to say those that were transformed into
    something with magic power can't transform people into inanimate objects. People can
    die gruesome deaths in this story and be resurrected if they're found. I do encourage
    you to intertwine some of the branches at least a bit. If you do please make sure to
    read the relevant branches to keep things in-character. If you do intertwine branches
    please put the chapter you're intertwining with at the top so others know
    the story and go back for a backstory on the other characters. For this I would say
    make an author's note and say something like "This branch is intertwined with..." and
    then the url of the chapter or chapters. Have fun with this story. Let's see if we can
    make a good story by Halloween.)
