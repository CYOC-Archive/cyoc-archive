He suspected that it may have been a bit of both. Still, he was spending the rainy Saturday afternoon at the local mall as he had recently gotten a sizeable bonus at work and it was burning a hole in his pocket, so to speak. He then caught sight of the Bodysuit store. It seemed like such a curious place, with racks and racks of what appeared to be different body types, races, genders and if he was seeing the racks in the back correctly even species. Intrigued, he decided to check it out.

    He did a double take at the sight of Marilyn Monroe greeting him as he walked into the store and then smiled and returned her greeting. As he walked through the store, he saw various different bodysuits of all different shapes and sizes. He couldn't help but feel a bit perplexed by all of it.

    "Hello," greeted another employee, a cute twentysomething guy wearing a simple dress shirt and slacks. "Are you finding everything alright?"

    "Honestly, I'm not sure what type of store this is." Nate looked around and then back to the young man when his eye caught the name tag that read, Sarah.

    "Our store offers you a chance to change anything about yourself that you want. We have suits that just alter your body to ones that give a full body options that allow you to become a completely different person as modeled by myself and my fellow employees."

    "I assure you it is. Normally, I'm a 32 year old woman, but here I am to you as a 22 year old man, using one of the may suits we offer. Both male and female, different races and if your interests lie elsewhere, even other species."
