    Pleasure Island

    Go for a hike

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 10 years ago O

    You remember your co-worker said when he helped set up this trip “The first thing you should do is to take her
    on a hike; I hear that Wolf Lake has great hiking. I guarantee you that you both will have a howling good
    time.” But still your surprised that he could afford such accommodations.
    “How about a hike?” you ask. Jane consults the brochure “Wolf Lake?” She asked “I hope there aren’t wolves
    there.” You say “Oh, come on. It’s just a name. Even if there are wolves there, I’m sure they’ll have them
    separated from the hiking trails.” Jane says “If you say so, ok let’s go.”

    You and Jane leave the hotel and made your ways to Wolf Lake, strangely as you enter the park it's suddenly
    night with a full moon and it’s only 2 o’clock in the afternoon. I swore I heard howling in the distance,
    must be the park's speaker system but it sounded so real but I also have a feeling I'm being watched. By what
    is the question. "Ooh," Jame says, "This place is certainly interesting. Must be roofed with a projection that
    looks like it's night. Looks romantic." You say "Err, yeah. Very romantic."

    We find the hiking path and on the side is a little kiosk, manned by young lady almost Jane's age with 2 mugs
    of cider "Welcome folks, care for our complimentary cider? It's our special blend." You take one of the mugs
    "Thanks, I am a little parched." Jane takes the other mug "Sure, why not? Looks delicious." You take a sip of
    the cider, it's amazingly delicious and herbal. You both take a long drink of the cider and suddenly you feel
    lightheaded. "Woo, that's quite a kick." Jane says "I'd say, it feels all my senses woke up all at once.
    What's in this stuff?" The gal says "Oh, just some special herbs that's keep anyone awake during the hike.
    Can't let anyone sleeping around here."

    I can tell she's lying, but whatever the case maybe, you both turn in the empty mugs "Thanks for the cider,
    now that our senses are awake, let's go for I hike Jane." Jane says "Let's go." You both start your hike as
    the lady looks at you enter the path "I hope you have a good time. A howling good time." She smiles with
    luminous yellow eyes.
