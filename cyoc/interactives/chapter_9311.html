    Ken had to think about which settings to use on his younger brother Jeff, who had accidentally been reduced to a droplet on the hardwood floor in the living room.

    Ken had a great relationship with his brother, so he didn’t want to change Jeff in a way that he would regret later on.

    Jeff’s original settings were age 16, weight 134, height 5’9”. Ken thought it was really cool, but he needed to think about how he would change his brother.

    Jeff’s age was now set to zero.

    If he were to set Jeff’s age at 5, then he would have a cool, little brother. But then he realized that he would have to baby sit him all the time. Not cool!

    If he set Jeff’s age at 10, then his little bro would be too annoying. He would want or need things all the time, and he would be so much younger, that they still wouldn’t have anything in common.

    He finally decided to put his age back at 16, the way it was. Besides, if he were to make him too young, he would only be a bother. But Ken pondered if he were to restore his brother the way he was? He thought about what it would be like to go to a party and to see his little brother there. He didn’t want to feel embarrassed about his brother either. So what to do?

    For weight he put 280 pounds and height he put 6’10”! Ken decided to make his little brother HUGE, like a 240 pound muscle titan! He would still be sixteen, but he would be his big little brother! He entered settings for hair and muscle; the way he figured his brother would love it for sure.

    Ken then pressed the Enter key on the keyboard and turned around to watch the little drop on the hardwood floor.

    The droplet increased in size as a light pink ball of flesh began to form. The little mass changed shape as it went from perfectly round to oval As it grew further, the head began to form on one end and legs began to form on the other. Within seconds, the small mass began to resemble the form of a human being.

    As the human grew further, noticeable details began to show the gender of the living form, and it was apparent that it was a male being. The skin was still hairless and the eyes were still closed, but from this moment on the growth seemed to speed up as the baby began to grow bigger, longer, and heavier.

    As the age progressed rapidly, the baby began to crawl on its arms and knees. Then it was able to stand up straight and walk its first steps. As he grew taller and taller, Ken began to recognize the familiar face, which was that of his little brother Jeff.

    Jeff continued to grow taller and taller, going from the height of 4 feet to 5 feet within a few seconds. Baby fat disappeared, and the first signs of muscle growth appeared as the boy slowly approached puberty.

    Within moments, the boy reached 6 feet in height and began to slow down. When the final height of 6 foot 10 inches had been reached, his muscles continued to grow in order to supply the requested weight which was chosen by the user.

    Jeff’s pectorals became large and round, shooting out a few inches in front of him. Large biceps popped up on his forearms, closely resembled bowling balls. His calves looked like muscular diamonds and his quads were as thick and as hard as tree trunks. Abdominal muscles dominated the landscape on his newly formed and expanded torso, and veins began to form everywhere, crawling over the quads and arms.

    When the growth had finally stopped, Ken stood up and walked towards his titanic younger brother, who was pleasantly occupied with admiring his new, muscular body and flexing as he glanced at his new form in the mirror.

    “Wow, bro! Thank you so much for this body! I’m huge!” Jeff said excitedly, while staring at himself in the mirror. Ken grabbed a pair of underwear out of the closet, and handed it to his much-bigger-than-him-younger-brother.

    “You want to do it to me?” Ken asked his giant little brother, “Or should we look for one of our little neighborhood kids and make him huge?”

    “Maybe we can call up some of our friends and neighbors, and invite them to come over?” Jeff wondered out loud, while looking down at Ken.
