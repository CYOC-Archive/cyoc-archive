    Time And the Fourth Wall Breaker SG.

    Branch Full view
    added by DianaGohan 7 years ago O

    Having returned back to where he was placed previously with the world around him unfrozen, Time looked to see the Magical Girl Deena and Familiar Milly playing video games as he waved his hand. "Well guess I should..." he paused, scratching his head. "Hmmm wait why was I brought back here exactly?"

    "Oh you'll find out soon enough" said a voice.

    Time groaned. "ughhhhhhhhhh didn't I just get done with dealing with faceless announcers? What am I going to have yet another part of my backstory revealed or something?"

    "No, and I'm far from a faceless." The voice was revealed to be a figure who warped into the middle of the room next to Time. The figure was an 11 year old boy with black spikey hair, wearing a light black jacket with a dark purple T-Shirt underneath, long black pants and brown steel tip boots with a long white cape on. He waved at time somewhat indifferently. "Hey" he said, sounding a bit annoyed.

    "Uh... hey" Time said scratching his head a bit confused. "How's it going....."

    "Son Gohan" The boy said, folding his arms.

    Time blinked a few times. "From Dragon Ball Z?"

    "Yes... and no" Son Gohan admitted. "I'm an idealized variation of a what if alternate reality storyline Diana had thought of, that ironically parallels your story in becoming an immortal being with incredible power who can travel to different dimensions."

    "Did it also involve having your family die horribly... more so then what actually happens in your series?"

    "Well that part wasn't exactly written up" Son grumbled. "Seriously you want to know how long that idea's been conceptualized? 14 years."

    ".... Uh that's not exactly impressive for me, no offense."

    "Well since you're meta aware that's 14 years AUTHOR TIME" Gohan clarified.

    Time blinked. "Damn, and she never wrote down your full backstory?"

    "A few pages here and there but yeah not near close ot the entire thing" Son sighed. "Seriously you think your former author had issues, nothing compared to DG."

    "DG" Time thought for a second. "ohhhhhhhhhhhhhhh, DianaGohan. So she has that name because of you then?"

    "Yeah that was also part of the rewrite" Son Gohan unfolded his hands. "But that's neither here nor there, unfouratnley" he stepped closer to Time. "I'm here to make sure you fufill your new job."

    "Oh right guess I won't be just hassling the two asking to stop changing people to magical girls if I'm under new managment" he then looked to see Deena and Milly still playing video games. "Hmmm shouldn't we of caught their attention by now or something."

    Son shook his head. "No, we're speaking in a 4th wall meta zone only. You're author referencing does not fit the meant to be more serious and character driven narrative originally intended for the tale."

    Time narrowed his eyes. "The serious narrative of two random teenagers being transformed into stock shoujo cliche tropes to battle demon aliens with armies of wannabe Sailor Moons?"

    "Well if you saw the other timeline that was building you'd know there is some actual serious discussions going on there" Son held out two fingers, holding them apart and then bringing them together. "But the two timelines is where you come in. See by creating you, BillBobeJoeGates created a splinter timeline parallel to the other ongoing narrative being told. And though these two timeline have been separated, they are going to need to come together at one particular point within the tale."

    "Why?"

    Son sighed. "Idea discussions that would of otherwise been impossible to impliment." he then pointed his fingers at Time. "And when that point comes, it's going to be up to you to bridge that gap so that reality can exist."

    "Oh well... I guess I'm glad I at least have a use instead of "being snarky about my character and then being tortured about it" or something."

    "Yeah DG got that out of her system like 16 years ago" Son admitted as a portal appeared behind them. "Still with how long it's taking DG to make the latest chaper, which isn't going to deal with that, you're going to be on the sidelines for a bit. Until that point comes though you can hang with the other fourth wall breakers."

    "Oooh, like Deadpool?"

    Son actually smirked a bit. "Yeah ironically enough he's also a new addition replacing a previous character we had on. I guess you can replace the other member we had who was ironically enough another OC created by an acquitance of Diana though we at least have permission to use you."

    "Oh well that's cool. Hanging out with Deadpool and... doing whatever you guys do."

    "Yeah mostly retaining the fourth wall, making storyline cameos and for awhile talking about movie trailers."

    "I... don't see how that really connects but okay."

    "Good" Son then raised an eyebrow. "I should point out though when you do appear next to interact with the main story you're origin is going to have to be tweaked some."

    "Uh, I think my author wrote out my origin above so DG wouldn't need to tweak it" Time pointed out.

    "Well again since the story you're going into is more serious and not going to have constant meta jokes, you can't really reference the fourth wall and also the limitations on your powers are going to have to be taken down even more."

    "Hmmm that kind of sucks" Time rubbed his chin. "Can I at least have it said that being in that particular dimension is messing with my memories and abilities some so I don't remember but when I complete my objective and fufill that role I do so that totally not cliched and incredibly well thought out backstory makes more sense."

    Son shrugged. "I don't see why not"

    "Cool" Time went to leave before looking back. "Wait what about this reality though? Is anything else going to be done with it?"

    Son shrugged. "Honestly DG didn't really have any plans and since you're coming to the main one now as DG's character, nothing else really should."

    "Well yeah but this is a CYOC story so you should at least give people options or something."

    "Fine whatever we'll think of something quick" Son said walking through the portal. Time quickly rushed through as it closed as Deena and Milly continued their game.
