However, you are not satisfied with your own answer, since this experiences really won't be needed in my regular human life. "Was I content with the transformation at the very start?", is your first worry as you lay on hay. It slightly disturbs you that this could be your final life. Your final way of life. A horse life. "It can't be, magic barber wouldn't lie to me, would he?" you, full of fear, ask. But you cant get an answer and you know it, so you calm down and try to make sense of it all. "He said he will visit in a month, so he might transform me back, then. Thats ok. I can live for a month as a horse." with a relaxed sigh you accept your fate. It wasn't too much but it made you much more content with this, horse life. Safe to say that, this and the thought that you actually love horses, made you happy.

But instead of panic, you start to consider the possibilites of your current reality. First is simple; a dream. Second is a bit more worrying; a nice trip due to drugged up picture and more than likely missing kidney. Third is esoteric; by using knowledge of the eldritch beings you are transported to a pocket dimension in which your soul is stuck for all eternity. To roam as a being that is under the influence of a shapeshifting manager, that prolongs your life by changing your form, every month. Whatever the answer is, you wont be the one to solve it, since you are going to try and enjoy life for awhile.
