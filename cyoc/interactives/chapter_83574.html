    Generous Twins

    The spirit wandered to a beach-side strip mall. People were getting into the spirit of the season buying gifts, she saw this with joy, but then she spotted a pair of people, he had pink shirt, collar popped, khaki board shorts, sandals, hat and sunglasses. The other wore a black top, shortpants, sunglasses and high heels. Unlike the other shoppers they simply stroll around the mall looking annoyed at all the shoppers, when they passed the charity raiser making disgruntling noises the spirit had enough. They look quite wealthy on their on right, but seemed unable to be happy even knowing some people had far less than they. The spirit saw how they dismissed the collection box and though that they were unhappy because they didn't know they could make other people happy by showing generosity toward them. The spirit was set to teach them just that.

    Vanessa and Pete Blake were indeed very rich. Their family owned most of the piers properties. And they sure were annoyed, their older brother was running late, as he was supposed to be there more than an hour ago, specially when he specifically told them to wait before choosing their parents presents. When they passed the charity drive they were simply too flustered to notice it, they were sleepy still from delivering those toys to the orphanage before the kids woke up. They had been pretty generous already, having been taught from little that the more privileged people is the ones who must do the most for others.

    So when the spirit messed inside their heads and kicked their generosity a notch, they went into overdrive. When the spirit left them the twins were giving all their cash into the charity with big smiles on their faces. The spirit left feeling she had done well, but had she stayed she would have seen the twins take different direction.

    Vanessa went to the nearest bank and practically emptied her trust fund in cold cash. She then went to the top of floor of the bank and simply made it rain creating quite a havoc. Her brother always more practical, simply wired all his money to different charities across the globe from his cellphone, when it had been done he just gave it to a passing teen.

    The siblings sold the apartment their parents bought for them for college, along with the furniture and cars and donate it to the local shelter. Then they went there personally and began to gave away their clothes to the homeless, even when in just three short days they themselves had become homeless. When their wardrobe ran out, they began to give up the very clothes they were wearing, Pete took off his shirt and gave it to a man which his own was torn long ago, Vanessa did the same and soon enough the pair of twins had gave up everything, they left the shelter naked with only a smile on their face, but then again no one noticed or seemed to care about the nude couple. It was a fortunate that both siblings suffered the same ailment, unable to hold even a piece of cloth to use as a blanket or even use the shelter they had donate all their worldly possessions, they GAVE each other heat during the nights they spent on the alleyways and gave each other the food they begged for the other. It was a miserable existence for the siblings, but they were content on their GIVING.
