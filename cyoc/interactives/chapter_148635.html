    Pulling yourself to your feet, you smooth your dress back down and rub your head, peering around on the floor for your lost tiara and wand. The wand landed in a corner of the room and seemed to be sparking. You tentatively reached for it, but briskly pulled your hand back as the sparks began to fly more furiously.

    All at once, the star at the top of the wand shattered in a mini explosion. To your surprise, what remained after the wand broke was a small cloud of darkness. You weren't sure what it was, but it didn't look like something to mess with. You turn to leave and tell the shopkeeper that you broke the wand when you felt a chill go up your spine as you feel a wave of... something wrong.

    You turn back and look at the wand and gasp as you watch the cloud of darkness stream towards you. It begins to surround you and you watch as your clothes disappear. You want to scream in agony as you begin to feel a burning sensation all along your body. You watch as your legs get longer and your breasts grow. You must be around 14 now, much older than before. Your antennae disappear as your wings begin to darken, becoming black at the ends. Your platinum hair grows rapidly to your waist and lightens to a silver. Your eyes also flash multiple colors before settling at a pale blue.

    The mist surrounds your body next and begins to form a black dress. The top layer is black with purple accents, just like your new wings, with white fabric underneath. The front of the lower dress was open, only barely covering your panties. The dress snugly held to your waist before cradling around your breasts, not covering a small area between them.
    The dress seemed to stop there, no longer progressing to cover your shoulders or arms.

    Finally, the mist surrounded your neck, forming a lacy, black collar with a black bow fastening it together. The mist finally begins to pool into the middle, creating a necklace that ends in a large ruby. The mist seems to pour into the gem before disappearing.

    Just as soon as your transformation ended, you feel your mind becoming assaulted by new thoughts. You cry out as you try to resist them, but one word usurps your control and demands your attention.
