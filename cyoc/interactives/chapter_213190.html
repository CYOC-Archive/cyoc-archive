    Neil got Clay to mold to his wishes. Next to him, Trav got Tom. Neil was considering the possibilities offered to him. So many! He could make Clay into a sexy woman, or into an animal. Or into an imaginary creature, even, if he wanted to! He pondered about making Clay into his pet, like a dog or a cat, when Trav elbowed him. This got him out of his thoughts in which he had been so deep.
    "Hey!" said Trav with a mischievous glint in his eyes. "Interested into a group project?"
    "Maybe," said Neil. "What do you have in mind?"
    "A horse. Or rather, a pony. Mingling both of these guys's bodies."
    As Neil looked a bit sceptical, Trav added another encouragement.
    "We could sell it afterwards. I'm sure a pony is worth quite a bit of cash. We split the money and have even more fun in this place. Sounds pretty good, uh?"
    Neil thought the young man had a point. And the more he thought about it, the more it sounded interesting. Interesting and profitable. And he could think of something to add to this lucrative plan. He talked to Trav about making the pony female and pregnant. She would reach an even higher price then and one of the models would still be alive, albeit as a foal foetus. They discussed which one would be the mare and who would be the foal. Neil wanted Clay to be the mare and Trav wanted Tom to be the mare, but after a little while, they reached an agreement. Tom would be the mare but to make it up, as a mean joke, Clay would be the foal, but a female one. Happy to know what to do and enthusiastic about their future sale, they went to work.

    And with a lot of work and practical help from the instructor, a more than decent result was achieved. Tom had been turned into a female pony and his bulging tummy now contained a female foal that used to be Clay. Yes, indeed, the result was most satisfying, especially as dividing the bodies to make a horse and its baby had been no simple task. But it was done and both boys had reasons to be proud. The instructor even congratulated them on their idea and good work. When the two young men said that they were thinking of selling their creation to the Island, perfect for rides, the instructor smiled and said that seemed an excellent idea and that it could be arranged. Trav and Neil grinned at each other. Their plan had worked! They were elated. However, they were completely unaware that they were being played for fools. The instructor was going to transform them, same as the others, and as these transformations depended upon what they had done to the models, chances were high that Neil and Trav would be on all fours upon equine legs and hooves.
