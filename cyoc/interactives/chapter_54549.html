    Trapped in a girly nightmare

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 13 years ago TG O

    Collin lay on the guest bed, staring at the ceiling, lost in thought. Most on her mind were the last two days, which in her opinion had been an utter nightmare.

    Because of some amulet she had found, Collin had turned into a seven year old female form of her former, male self. And what made all this even worse was the punishment that Stacy and her friends kept coming up with whenever Collin, or Lisa as they insisted on calling her, did something that was not feminine or appropriate for a girl.

    Two days ago, she had wet herself, unsure how a girl went to the bathroom, only knowing that it involved sitting down. The punishment, handed down by Stacy, was another day as a girl...who had never really told her how long Collin was to be a girl.

    The next morning, Collin had refused to wear a dress and answer to the name Lisa. Brie punished Collin with three more days as a girl.

    After that, Collin lost track of the days she was to remain a girl and all the really silly things Stacy and her friends came up with to punish Collin with. The one thing she was certain of is that she is probably looking at, at the least, one month of being a girl.

    "All though," Collin thinks. "The way Stacy, Brie, Marcie and Izzi are going, I'll never be male, ever again."

    Sighing, Collin shifts, and in the process picks up a plush mouse in ballet slipper, and hugging the creature close, unaware that she is cuddling the plush animal in the same fashion a little girl would, Collin starts to think on the plan she had been slowly formulating for the past couple of hours.

    After a moment, she slides out of bed and looking down at the tank-top with Hello Kitty on the front and the pyjama bottoms, also with Hello Kitty on them, she wonders if she'll ever be able to return to her male self.

    Shrugging, she wonders if she should go to the bathroom first, but not feeling any pressure on her young bladder, she hugs the plush mouse closer and creeping across the room, she opens the door.

    Peering out, she listens to the silent house and turning back, she peers at the alarm clock for a moment before figuring the time to be around seven am. Bothered that some things are harder then before, thinking it is Stacy's fault some how, Collin exits the room and slowly moves toward her babysitter's room.

    Seeing the door open, she pauses and listening, hearing nothing from within, Collin silently tells herself, "You can do this," as she moves closer to the doorway.

    In the doorway, she see immediately the sleeping forms of Brie and Izzi and looking toward the bed, Collin notes that Stacy is also still asleep. Knowing that Marcie went home for some reason last night and that the missing girl would probably not return until later that day, Collin sneaks into Stacy's room and toward her closet.

    "Find your clothes and find the amulet," she thinks as she slowly opens the door and peers inside, wondering what Stacy and her friends did with her clothes and the amulet after the transformation.

    Dropping the plush mouse soundlessly onto the carpet, Collin thinks about her plan, how she wants to punish Stacy and her friends, ponders if she will ever return to being a boy, and lets her mind drift to other important matters as she searches the closet, all the while she listens to the three sleeping behind her.

    When it becomes evident that neither her male clothes or the amulet is in the closet, Collin turns and peering about the room, wonders were to search next.

    Stealthily creeping across the room, the young girl starts with Stacy's nightstand and then under her bed. Slowly and meticulously she searches the room and it isn't until she finds a box that has been slipped carefully under the vanity that Collin makes her first big find...the amulet.

    Struggling not to jump about and whoop and holler with joy, Collin instead looks about the room one final time and figuring Stacy must have been serious when she told the other girl to dispose of Collin's male clothes, she moves across the room, picks up her stuffed mouse and exiting, she makes her way back to the guest room.

    Inside, she closes the door and looking down at the amulet, noting the androgynous figure and the hard to pronounce word, Collin wonders what she should do to Stacy and her friends.

    "Come to think of it," the girl thinks. "When would be a good time to exact revenge for what they did."

    Unsure, she looks back at the amulet, wondering if it could reverse what has happened to her, she decides to bide her time for the moment, and wrapping the amulet in a bit of clothe that might have been a piece of clothing for the plush mouse, careful not to touch anything but the chain, Collin slides the medallion into her pack, down toward the bottom and hidden under a piece of cardboard that the manufacturers placed inside to help the backpack retain its shape.

    Satisfied, she looks about the room and after a moment...her mind thinking on ways to punish Stacy and her friends, deciding that Stacy would be the last...Collin moves to the dresser and starts to take a set of clothes out, thinking of only wanting to look pretty.
