Still, my husband did promise, and I think I see a way to release the both of us from this agreement without giving you the satisfaction of winning." So you might as well stay here; you'd fit in wonderfully in the freak show, or you could work behind the scenes at one of the other attractions. It's up to you!"

    You dash out of the trailer before she can do anything else to you. "Toldja it was a bad idea," Sofi says. "Still, it'll be nice to have someone my age around."
