    The Princess of Hell turned, her full, perfect lips parting over her long fangs in a smile as the Imp joined her, and reaching out with a slender hand that ended in long, wicked claws, beckoned him closer to her. "Yes." She said after a moment, the tone a magnificent Soprano, "It's all over, finally." As the Imp arrived at her side, she gently trailed a clawed hand along the Imps horns, smiling as she quietly fed him power, and began to reform him into one of her Incubi, while her other hand reached out to the stone wall of the castle, which very slowly began to reshape itself into a new Palace more to her liking.

    "From now on, you will serve as groundsmaster in my Palace, there is a lot of work to be done reshaping my domain, so I will give you this new form to better assist me." Her hand pulled away as he continued to transform, and her attention shifted to the growing and changing Palace, but her thoughts drifted briefly to Jeffery, she had enjoyed his companionship, and suddenly she felt a pang of loneliness. However, Jeffery had made his choice, and she hoped he would be happy, there would be many Incubi to serve as consorts for her, so her needs would be met. A loud crack returned her attention to the palace as it finished reshaping into her new home, and the Princess swiftly entered, feeding power into the structure as the set up security throughout, and finally made her way into her Royal Chambers, where she slumped into an ornate, but very demonic throne she had created, sighing. It was a sight that any Gothic fantasy Artist would dream of painting, a Demonic Princess, her appearance such feminine perfection it would make a Goddess jealous, draped in the arms of her throne, embracing the calm, for at least a moment, knowing that her duties would give her little respite.

    Dana had been through more than any human would have been able to imagine since she had started as a human boy, oh so long ago, it was only fair she have a chance to consider it all. After what must have been hours, she finally sat back up, snapping her fingers as she summoned the new Incubus, gaining composure as she promptly commanded. "Summon the original class, except for Barry, if he is seen, I want him killed on sight. It is time to create nobility in my realm."
