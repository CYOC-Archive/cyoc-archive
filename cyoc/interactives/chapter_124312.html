    New Neighbors: Kate tags along

    added by Zeerox 5 years ago BM

    As you depart college to go grab something to eat from home, a familiar voice calls out behind you so you turn with a smile.

    “Hey Jay can I tag along? I haven’t got anything to do for lunch, Stacy and Tia are elsewhere right now” she says.

    You nod “sure thing babe, mum is home so you can say hi if you’d like” you say.

    Kate gives a small nod as you both begin heading back to yours, you chat about rather uninteresting things about college work and how they were doing in classes.

    “Oh yeah that reminds me with us getting the week off soon did you want to travel anywhere? We can stay with Stacy and Tia at their place it’s a little way off from the college and our places but it’s pretty quiet there” Kate says.

    “Anywhere else we could go?” you ask.

    “Well there is the lake, it’s outside the city, lots of wildlife and small cabins to rent, if we do decide that we need to book soon, I heard a couple of our classmates are heading out there I think for a party but who knows” she says.

    “That sounds like it might be fun” you say interested.

    “Then we have a special trip to Hogsville, it’s a town not far from here, places are limited as they’re only taking roughly fifteen people, the flyers for it say it’s a once in a lifetime trip they aren’t so lenient on accepting outsiders” Kate says as she hugs your arm while you walk.

    “So what would a trip to Hogsville offer us?” you ask still pondering on ideas.

    “A trip round a brewery along with free beer, all you can eat lunch buffet and visit various other locations like museums and a place they call the sty, possibly their prison called that to keep in theme with the towns name” Kate responds.

    “Let me think on it I’ll give you my answer after college if we need to plan this early then I’d rather not delay a week alone with you is what I really want, I’m sure mum would be OK with that” you say as you approach your house.

    You both step inside, Kate walks into the living room and strikes up a conversation with mum while you begin to sort out your lunch, you rummage through the fridge and remove a plate of left over chicken from the previous night’s dinner, you begin to make up a sandwich, your mind thinking about all the possible places you and Kate could visit.

    You could hear Kate and mum chatting and laughing, the occasional snort mixing in with the laughter which you don’t notice much, you continue to make your lunch when you notice mum enter the kitchen, she scratches her armpit, you could see a visible sweat stain on her shirt as she grabs one of the large bags of chips from the cupboard and walks past you letting forth a loud belch.

    You look at her and notice how big her ass looked, she looks like she has been stuffing cakes and chocolate into her belly and it all went to her now much fatter ass, she doesn’t really react to the belch she simply walks back into the living room and sits back on the couch.

    “Wow where the hell did her manners go?” you say to yourself quietly.

    You pack your lunch into your bag and step into the living room, Kate was munching on a couple of chips both of them watching some show, they must have stopped talking before mum grabbed the chips, you could see Kate was bored but mum was engrossed in it.

    “Hey Kate what kind of show is this?” you ask her.

    “Never seen it before, it doesn’t look brand new my mum knows all the current running shows as she watches a lot of them even when working, I honestly don’t know why all the actors and actresses are dressed like pigs just gives it some creepy vibe” Kate says.

    “Mum seems into it looks like something Butch and Trevor would watch” you mumble.

    You motion to Kate and you both head back to college, you both unsure on what you saw on the TV the trip back much quieter than the trip there, you decide to break the silence.

    “I’ve been thinking about our trip, it’s a tough choice I’m hoping to have an answer by the afternoon, so come see me after classes and I’ll tell you my decision I should be decided then” you say.

    Kate nods and you get back to college, you chow down on your food and get back to class, the classroom was buzzing with discussions on the week off and trips out of town and parties, you could feel the excitement.

    The girl next to you Molly turns and asks what your plans are for the week off, you shrug your shoulders as you were still unsure on where to go.

    “Not sure Molly, I’ve plans to go with Kate somewhere but I can’t decide on where to go, she mentioned the lake outside the city with the cabins” you say.

    “We’re heading there ourselves, Derek is hosting a massive party out there, we’re going to get totally shitfaced to the point we can’t even stand up” Molly says.

    You laugh and roll your eyes “I suppose getting drunk is a fun thing to do for party animals like you” you say jokingly.

    Molly playfully pushes you as Mrs Stillglass enters the class and everyone quietens down, you begin to daydream a little about where to take Kate, as the class drones on, you continue to do this for the rest of the afternoon and by the end of the college day you feel you have settled on a place, so you go meet with Kate and tell her where you want to go.
