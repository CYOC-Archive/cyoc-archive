    The snake blade twisted in their direction in mid-air even as they dodged it.

    'This is not good. Forgive me for saying this but we can't afford to burn off energy on her!
    Call the others! We can't tip our hand just yet! Like you said! They're expecting an army of
    djinn now not a small squad of angels! We need to use that to our advantage... here it
    comes again!' They twisted in midair, 'It's pointless to keep turning her creations to glass
    she just keeps making more, we to have do something a bit more drastic, or she'll just
    wear us down and hit us while we're down. Let's do it.'

    They blasted the sand snake sword into a mess of glass that fell downwards to the sandy
    ground. Lucy flew backwards and began to conjure a new weapon, Tabra/Janice then did a
    gamble and teleported behind her, and grabbed her from behind and began to pour
    intense head into her arms.

    Before Lucy's eyes... her hands... and arms... began to turn to glass, moving up from her
    wrists to her elbow and beyond that, "King Tamthis!!!" She screamed as the non-glass part

    fleeing until it had returned to Karthis City.

    Alexandra slowly peaked out from under her head from when the awful battle had started,
    and shook her head at own ineffectiveness, what was she or her master thinking that she
    could help him? Then again, there was no one else.

    Janice/Tabra looked at it all, it was all dead glass, nothing more, that included the two
    arms in her hands... 'Let me guess, she'll have new ones by the time we see her again?

    the glass arms as -far away- as possible, then blasted them to atoms.

    Alexandra looked up at them hopefully, "Can you, are you, are you the leader of the djinn

    Janice and Tabra replied, "Yes, I'm an Angel, but I'm not the one leading the dijnn, I'm
    really not sure if it's a good idea for you to meet him or not."

    command for me, I can't fail him."

    messenger than for the others. Something about the little cross made Alexandra
    confused.

    Janice? Why are you looking at me like that? No, Janice, please keep it away, it scars me..."

    'Janice. Yes? You don't know it, but your spirit and that of the others were all made into a
    uniform shape and structure when Karther made you his love dolls, you were all exactly

    automatically yes to both those questions. 'Obey Karther without question' is supposed to
