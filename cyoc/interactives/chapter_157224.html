    You honestly didn’t believe in Magic anyways but you figured this would at least make for a fun distraction. You really didn’t want to spend the extra money however as you decided to just buy the pink clothes against the advice of the strange shopkeeper.

    “Just make sure when you open it you read the tags carefully.” She said handing you the receipt as you nodded only half listening and taking the clothes home.

    When you got home you went into your room and opened up the bag and you were a bit surprised as inside the where 5 slightly smaller bags with number tags attached to them. Each bag had a number going from 1-5. There was also a small description under each number.

    Curious you grabbed the number one bag and read the description under the number.

    “The Butch, for the total masculine lady who wants to be seen as manly as possible.”

    You were a bit curious as you opened the bag and took out an outfit inside. There was a flannel button up shirt. Rough jeans that looked really worn with a big belt buckle. There was also a work pair of cowboy boots to go with it. It looked like the kind of outfit a butch lesbian chick would wear if she worked on a ranch.

    Setting the outfit down you grabbed the next bag seeing the number 2 and the tag below.

    “The rocker, for the girls who party like the guys. Not too feminine but still a lady.”

    You opened up this bag and found a different outfit inside. This one was a bit more feminine than the last. It had a pair of jeans that were more feminine looking and tighter around the waist and butt, they also had tears and holes in them which seemed intentional. It also had a black
    T-shirt that seemed like something you would get at a concert along with a pair of black flip flops.

    This outfit reminded you of what a girl would wear to a rock concert or a monster truck rally. The kind of girl smoking a cigarette and chucking a beer. While still a girl she definitely fits in more with the guys.

    You put that down and grabbed bag number 3 and read the tag.

    “The Sweetheart, for the girl who wants to be cute and sexy but not go over the top.”

    Opening that bag you discovered a definitive female outfit. It was a pair of tight yoga pants along with a blouse that showed just a bit of cleavage. Along with a pair of white flip flops that were more girly looking that the last set.

    This outfit reminded you of all those “basic girls.” You see everyday. The kind of girls who do hot yoga, go to Starbucks, and post on Instagram all day.

    Moving along you grabbed bag number 4 and read the label.

    “The Hot Chick, for the girl who wants to always be seen as sexy and wants others to know it.”

    Taking out this outfit you immediately saw a jump in the sexier nature of these clothes. You saw a mini skirt and low cut halter top. It was paired with pair of sandals that had a small heel to them and looked rather fancy.

    You grabbed the final bag number 5 and read it’s label.

    “The sex symbol, for the girl who HAS to be the sexiest girl in the room. Warning! Only for those willing to be their sexiest as possible at all times!”

    Opening the bag you found this outfit was actually rather simple. It was only a skin tight mini dress paired with set of very sexy high heeled sandals. The dress and shoes looked like something a model might wear.

    You stared at the 5 outfits below and realized pretty quick that they were labeled in order of femininity and sexiness. 1 being the lowest and 5 the most.

    These couldn’t really work could it? You began to think to yourself.
