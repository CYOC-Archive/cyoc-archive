    The Madame Illusia

    Madame Illusia's personal daycare

    "I think I'd rather go back to college", Damon said.

    "Excellent. But what do you say about your girlfriend? Does she also want to receive a better education?", Madame Illusia asked him.

    "I'm not sure but I think not", Damon replied.

    "Why don't you ask her to come on stage too?", Madame Illusia asked him. And that's when Damon's girlfriend, Monica, came on stage.

    "Tell me, young lady. Would you also like to go back to college?", Madame Illusia asked Monica.

    "Honestly no. But...."

    "What's the problem, darling?", Madame Illusia asked.

    "You see, I was never able to get into kindergarten, pre-K, preschool or even daycare. My parents thought they weren't that important so they never got me into them", Monica confessed to Madame Illusia.

    "So you want to be able to enter one of them so you can feel what you could never experience?"

    "Yes. Especially daycare. Oh, how I would have loved to have spent my days there as a toddler!", Monica answered her.

    "Awwww, don't worry dear. Thanks to my magic, I can make that happen. What's more, I know the right place for you", Madame Illusia told Monica.

    "Really? Where?", Monica asked, somewhat surprised by what she heard.

    "I just opened a girls-only daycare in my own home. I am in charge of taking care of many babies and toddlers. I even teach the older ones some magic tricks. And the best of all is that I do it for free. Would you be interested?"

    Monica couldn't believe what she had just heard. A daycare she could attend completely free? This sounded like a dream come true. But she immediately realized some things that might prevent her from entering there.

    "What you told me sounds pretty good and all, but isn't it going to be very strange for a woman like me to attend a daycare with a bunch of small children?", Monica asked her.

    "Don't worry about that, dear. Remember well that I am a witch. I can easily turn you into a toddler so you can fit in with the rest of the children", Madame Illusia replied to Monica.

    "Really? Thank you very much, Madame Illusia. But, if I become a toddler, who is going to take care of me when the day is over? I don't think I will be able to take care of myself as a toddler", Monica asked Madame Illusia again.

    "That's no problem. You can stay in my home," Madame Illusia replied.

    "Wait, really? But don't you think it can be a little annoying to take care of me all the time?", Monica asked, a bit worried.

    "Not at all, dear. In fact, it wouldn't be the first time I let other children stay in my house for a while", Madame Illusia told Monica, who was more than fascinated by what she had heard.

    "So what do you say dear? Would you like to come to my daycare?"

    "Yes.....yes I want", Monica told her very determinedly.

    "Very well, in that case..........it's magic time!"

    At that moment, Madame Illusia cast a spell on both Damon and Monica and in a short time the two immediately disappeared. The audience was left speechless when they saw this event but in a short time they began to applaud with excitement.

    After what felt like an eternal sleep, Monica finally began to wake up only to discover something very surprising.

    Monica saw that she was no longer an adult, but a 2-year-old toddler girl. Monica was now wearing a long-sleeved white sweater and a light orange overall dress that did little to cover her now diaper cover. She was also wearing little orange socks, her hair was tied into cute pigtails with white ribbons, and she now had an orange pacifier in her mouth.

    Finally, she found herself in what seemed to be a playroom. This place was quite big and colorful and had everything: boxes full of toys, small tables with activities, small shelves of books, a TV, etc. Not to mention the other toddlers and babies that were playing and running all over the place.

    "So this is Madame Illusia's home daycare? This is going to be a lot of fun!", Monica said to herself.
