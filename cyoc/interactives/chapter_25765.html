    Oh, lovely Nancy, Todd smiled deviously as he watched the restored brunette walk away, let’s try the other extreme.

    Todd aimed the remote, still set on change size, at Nancy’s turned back and clicked the minus button. The girl shrank completely smaller by one inch, now 5’8” tall. She must have felt something strange, because she stopped dead in her tracks and looked herself over with her mouth agape, eyes wide. She gasped suddenly and looked around nervously. Todd hid just out of view until Nancy broke into a quite fast walk, probably realizing that she was changing more.

    Todd followed her down the hallway leading out of the library. It looked like she was heading towards the elevator leading to the dorm rooms downstairs. Just out of view, Todd pointed the remote at Nancy again and pressed minus twice. Shrinking again, this left Nancy 5’6” tall, her running shoes loose on her feet, her already loose and ruined clothes sagging on her reduced frame. Todd’s jacket brushed against the floor. Nancy stopped again, and Todd noticed she was starting to cry in horror, holding her hands to her mouth. Todd took advantage of this and shrank Nancy down three times more, making her 5’3” tall. This time Nancy screamed and broke into a run, losing both her shoes as she dashed towards the elevator, holding up her pants with both hands, Todd’s coat dragging quite a bit behind her on the floor, all her clothes just swimming on her. A couple inches DID make a big difference after all.

    Todd giggled, watching the smaller lady’s panicked dash down the hall towards the elevator. Todd shrank her six times more as she ran down the hall and nearly bombed into students walking by. Still running, the much reduced little brunette was now just 4’9” tall. Todd followed her towards the elevator and hid at the end of the hall, watching her jam her finger into the elevator’s call button several times before standing looking around nervously waiting for the elevator to come, catching her breath and wrapping herself in the coat. Todd watched in delight as Nancy completely discarded her way too big pants in a pile beside her.

    While the elevator was delayed Todd decided to make her even smaller. He was just out of view around the corner and shrank Nancy down more and more, watching her horrified reaction as he made her just 4’ tall. She was quite tiny now, her eyes wide and teary, wondering how much more she was going to lose. At last, the elevator doors sprang open and Nancy dashed in, her shirt down to almost her ankles and the coat more like a cape now. With her back turned towards him Todd aimed the remote once more and minimized Nancy to three feet tall, hearing her squeal of dismay as she entered the elevator and realized how little she now was. No bigger than a child, Todd marveled. Poor Nancy was too shocked to move, staring at her little hands in comparison to the big coat surrounding her, shirt pooled at her feet. Todd thought he could see her panties lying on the floor with the bottom of the shirt. What an adorable little brunette, he grinned in hiding.

    He should go see her before somebody else did.
