    Terri's family vacation becomes a friend vacation

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 3 days ago AR AP BM S O Reality alteration

    Terri was about to choose one member of her family when an idea suddenly struck her, why did she only have to change one family member, why not change all of them? She had wanted to be spending time wit friends her age and now she was getting the chance to do just that. Terri then imagined herself back in the white void.

    "Back so soon?" The voice asked her sounding surprised.

    "Uh, yeah, I thought it over and decided to transform my whole family." Terri answered. "Interesting, I don't think anyone has ever tried to do more than two people on their first try, but it's very easy to do." The voice commented.

    Terri looked over the panel that showed the four remaining members of her family and began to make edits to each relative. She started with her dad, Dave who was 43 currently but edited his age down to hers at 19, made his brown hair completely brown eliminating any gray that was in it and made it so he was attending college to go into construction. She also saw that there was an option if the wanted to keep her family related to her.

    "Since, I'm the only one who's going to remember any of these changes, I think I'll make it so we're unrelated to each other." Terri said to herself but kept Dave's surname as Newman.

    Terri next went to edit her mother, Rose. Rose was 41, just a little younger than her father or current father. She changed Rose's age to 19 just as she had for her dad. This now made her red hair more vibrant and a little longer, but tied it up in one long ponytail. Now at 19, Rose's boobs didn't sag anymore and were now more risen like Terri's. She also made it so she wasn't married to David anymore and she now used her maiden name of Rose Matthews.

    Terri then moved on to her younger sister, Jessica, just as before made her younger, but decided to make it so Jessica was only 18 instead of 19 to change it up a little. Jessica's red hair also stood out from Rose's in that she didn't tie it up and let it all hang out very long past her shoulders. Terri now remembered she had to give her siblings and herself different last names. So she randomly thought of the name Hamilton and changed it to that, her little sister was now Jessica Hamilton.

    That left her youngest brother at 13, Terri aged Kevin up to 18 years old as well and made it so he kept at his physical activities giving him some good muscles when he got older. Terri also gave Kevin a goatee to distinguish him from David. The last change for Kevin would be his name and chose Williams to be Kevin's new last surname. So now he was Kevin Williams and to add a little bit of fun, she also added that Kevin was originally from England.

    "Okay, now my whole family is changed, except for me. So I'll still think of it as a family vacation, which will be weird. Hey uh...voice?" Terri asked the VIP voice.

    "Yes?" "Is it possible to change my self mentally to think I'm with friends, but be able to change myself back at the end of my vacation?" Terri asked.

    "You may, you can make it so you think you're on vacation with friends. Remember when you leave you decide which changes you made stay and which will revert, I don't make the decisions for you." The voice reminded her.

    "Okay, well, can you give me a...reminder at the end of my vacation to undo this mental change?" Terri asked. "I can, you won't remember the people you're with are your family, nor will you remember it once you leave this place after you change your mental state and reality altering."

    "Okay so now, one last change...myself. Terri...Vale. On vacation with friends....and once I press this button, I'm back to the foyer with my friends." Terri said as she pressed the button and returned to the foyer.

    "Terri? Terri, did you hear me?" Jessica asked her friend when she went back to the foyer.

    "I'm sorry, Jess, what did you say?" Terri asked her friend.

    "I said, do you think Kevin has a crush on me? I mean he was the one who invited me on the trip." Jessica asked her friend.

    Rose had asked them all about the trip and while Jessica had been a little hesitant, Kevin managed to finally convince Jessica to join them. Now the four friends would have a very memorable vacation. Terri thought so too as she still had the VIP upgrade but didn't divulge it to her friends, she just wanted them to enjoy their trip for now as they went to their rooms.
