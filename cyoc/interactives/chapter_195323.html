    Marcia in Wonderland: The Shrinking Bottle

    Upon exiting the room, you find yourself floating in a vast ocean. Still inside the glass bottle, you remain seated and ponder the origin of your new surroundings.
    "I wonder if I was responsible for creating this whole ocean with my tears? It would be rather unlikely, but I suppose anything's possible at this point. Especially considering that I'm literally sitting inside the very bottle I just drank out of a moment ago. Imagine if Rusty was here right now and ... wait a sec- RUSTY!" You suddenly blurt out as you remember your life long friend, who was probably worried sick about you, and pull yourself together.
    "What the hell is wrong with me? It's like I'm turning into a completely different person here. I need to find a way out of this dimension or whatever this place is before I forget who I-" Before you can finish talking, the bottle stops suddenly and you hit your head on the glass bottle's side, knocking you out.

    You awoke sometime later to the sound of vibrating glass. You sit up and find that your head buzzing wildly, causing you to become disoriented. Eventually the vibrations subside and you regain your senses. Looking around the now leaning bottle you've been traveling in, you realize that you were now on a sandy beach.
    "Oh my, what a lovely beach. Now to exit this bottle and find The White Rabbit." You said, once again speaking like a young proper british lady.
    You notice that the bottle's opening was leaning towards dry land and concluded that you might be able to climb out through there. You began your climb and, despite how smooth the glass was, were able to reach to the bottle's opening with limited difficulty.
    "Excellent. Now to just need to pull myself out and I'll be on my way." You said out loud.
    But as you start to pull yourself out, a strong wave appears and hits the bottle really hard. The sheer force of the wave is enough to push the bottle further inland and change its positioning, with the bottle now standing straight up. You try to hold on, but the impact from the wave causes you to lose your grip and fall back inside. Luckly your dress's skirt expands out like a parachute and you gently land on the glass bottom.
    "My goodness. This truly is such a wonderful dress. Now I shalt think nothing of falling from high places ever again."
    Once you get your bearings, you look around and realize that you were now trapped inside the bottle. Because you were currently less than half the size of the bottle itself and the glass inside was very smooth, you couldn't climb out like you did before. You try jumping for the opening, but you were too small to reach it. With no way out, you sit down with your legs crossed in defeat.
    Oh dear, how am I gonna get out of here now?"
    As you sit in place, you notice that the sun seems to be getting brighter as if it were shining directly at you and the bottle. Suddenly without warning, the bottle begins to vibrate and you along with it. As this happens, you start to hear a humming sound coming from the glass itself as the bottle continues to vibrate. You try to move your legs, but the vibrations keep them in place.
    "W-h-a-t-'s _ g-o-i-n-g _ o-n _ h-e-r-e?" you said as you vibrated.
    After a while, you look down at the ground outside the bottle and see the groove in the sand made by it actually move. You then see a little red crab walking by and noticed something off about it. As you stared at the crab, you realized that it seemed to be getting bigger as it moved passed you. After the crab leaves, you look around and discover that the entire beach had grown larger and was continuing to expand. I was then that you finally realized in your shock and horror what was really happening. The bottle itself was actually shrinking, with you inside shrinking along with it.
    "O-H _ N-O! _ N-O-T _ T-H-I-S!"
    Powerless to stop it, all you could do was sit there and watch as the world around your bottled prison continued to grow. Finally after about a minute, the vibrations ceased and the bottle had stopped shrinking. To the average person who might be walking along this beach, the bottle was now the same size to them that it was when you first discovered it. You get up and stared in dread at the now massive beach around the bottle and you.
    "Oh my, I'm even smaller than I was before. What's gonna happen to me now?"
