    The Kitsune and the Scientist

    added by Dragonman 6 years ago O

    (I am changing the perspective to second person from the kitsune's perspective)

    There was a man in a lab coat or a scientist to be exact, but you didn't know that. "A...human?" You thought and cocked your head to the side.

    "Don't worry now I will not harm you I promise." The scientist said

    "Who are you?!" You snarled

    "Whoa now, calm down I does it look like I'm going to hurt you?" The scientist asked

    You looked at the scientist puzzled and confused and you hated it, and it gave you a headache. "Yes...no...I don't know anymore, what's happening to my life?!" You said lowering your guard, and putting one your hands on your head and whimpering at the pain at your migrain.

    "Try not to overthink things, I can tell you the cause of your transformation, but I have to say that I'm surprised that you're still alive especially after you did it twice." The scientist said

    "I know how it happened, I drank some fucked up water from an even more fucked up river! I just don't know why I transformed, Arrgh! Fuck this goddamn pain!" You growled

    "Look I know why you transformed, and it's because that river you drank from was not water. You see a truck crashed and toxic waste, and that's what you drank from. Now normally drinking from toxic waste would kill a creature, but you seem to have have been affected differently. Now we don't know exactly how this happened entirely either, and that's why I'm here. You see as a scientist it's my job to learn, and right now I want to learn about you, and currently we are trying to determine exactly how you have been transformed. Others have already taken samples from and are currently trying to cleanse the area that you drank from, but I am here to acquire you for study. In other words I am going to take you somewhere, so we can help you figure out what happened and what you have become." The scientist said

    "What? What's a truck? What's a study? Arrgh! Damnit, what am I? Who are you?" You asked trying to contend with your headache.

    "My name is Dr. Carl, and all I wish is for you to cooperate with me, and come with me willingly. I promise that you won't be abused and that everything we will do is perfectly necessary. In return we will give you food, clean water, and shelter but only if you work with us. When I say "we" and "us" I am referring to my scientist team of course." The scientist, Dr. Carl said

    "Just...What am I? And how did you know about me when I transformed?" You asked once more

    "Well we can't quite determine what you are, especially after your second transformation. However, physically you resemble a humanoid kitsune, which is a mythical creature which is a fox with multiple tails. We saw you through what is called a camera, and there are lots of cameras set up all over the forest here, and that's how we were able to track you here. We also saw you are able to create and manipulate fire somehow, and we would want to know how you did that as well." Dr. Carl asked

    "Well, I don't know, it's really complicated...I think, it just kind of happens when I want it to. Arrgh! It's hard to think about it though with this blasted migrain." You said

    "So, since you answered my question does that mean you accept my offer?" Dr. Carl asked

    "Sure, if it helps with this stupid pain, and helps me with my life in general then I accept." You said

    "Excellent, and I would like to know, what's your name?" Dr. Carl asked

    "No, I don't let's go though, do you have anything to help me with this headache though." You asked

    "I'll see what we can about that, there's actually more ways than one." Dr. Carl said gesturing you to follow which you did, and he lead you to a car, but of course you didn't know what it was, and you just followed Dr. Carl's lead.

    What do you do now?
