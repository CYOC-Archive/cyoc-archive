    Jen got on the bus and looked for a seat, every seat was taken except for one, next to the most popular girl in school: Ella Smith. She asked to sit next to her.
    "Piss off you nerd" she said.
    "That's a no then" Jen replied.
    Jen got off the bus and decided she would walk to school, it was only 2 miles and Jen was rather fast. Jen got to school just after the bus, went inside, opened her locker to find a sheet waiting for her in there.
    Cheerleader tryouts today at lunch. Jen threw the sheet away and went off to her first lesson, chemistry.
    Jen's lessons went on as normal, she talked to no one, sat at the back, did all her work and pretended she did not exist. It was only when lunch came that she talked to someone. She got her lunch and sat down with her group of friends and began the normal conversation. However Jen couldn't help but feel as if she was being watched, she turned round to find a freshman staring at her. She got up to go and confront him, suddenly she felt something collide with her leg and the floor hit her, hard.
    "Oh I'm sorry, didn't see you there. My bad" it was Ella from earlier. All her friends laughed and made fun of her calling her a nerd amongst others. Jen stood up, she was used to it by now. She was only focused on this boy, she reached him.
    "Why are you staring at me?" She asked him. He only got up and beckoned her to follow him, she did. They arrived at the disabled bathroom, no one ever used it.
    "Remember that phone call from earlier?" He asked.
    "Um...yes".
    "Well that was me, I cursed you for making fun of me. Believe it or not that girl who just tripped you used to be your lap dog." Jen thought what he was saying was complete bull so she left and carried on with the rest of her day. But the thought was always on her mind that something was up. She ignored it and when she got home she did what she normally did: Do her homework, eat her dinner, play some games, read and go to bed. And she was about to when her phone rang. She picked it up.
    "Hello?" She said
    "Well since you seen keen on ignoring me Transform or dare"...
