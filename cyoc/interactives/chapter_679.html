    Sean was ecstatic! The UPS man finally delivered his new Chronivac 4.0! Ever since finding it online last week at an obscure metaphysical website, he was looking

    finally here!

    His roomates Jay and Dan were out for the rest of the day, so he had some time to experiment with his new toy. Sean had always been a transformation junkie,
    and he had a whole list of things he wanted to try. He placed the CD-ROM in the drive and installed the program. It took about 5 minutes to complete the install,
    and another 5 minutes to upload his personal information into the system. With the portable emitter attached to the computer, Sean sat it on top of his monitor and
    pointed it at himself. After everything was turned on and all programs enabled, he was ready to go.

    Sean decided he would begin slow and safe and decided he would start with a little weight gain. He went to the Change to text box adjacent to his current weight
    of 150, and typed 175 and hit enter. The Current weight box began to slowly increase as the green light on the emmitter began to blink. 152... 154... 156... 158...
    160... Sean began to feel his 501's begin to tighten. 162... 164... 166... 168... 170... 172... 175. The blinking green light stopped, and the Current weight box now
    matched the Change to box. 25 pounds wasn't that much, but Sean could definetly feel and see the difference! His heart was beating fast with uphoric excitement.
    It works!! the only thing was that the 25 pounds he had gained in the last 5 minutes was all fat as he could feel it in his gut. He lifted his T shirt up to expose a small
    gut that had formed beginning to spill over the waist of his now tight 501's. Not exactly the look he wanted to go for, but he was just overly satisfied that it worked
    so well. Sean looked at the results again and with a big smile on his face pressed save! Once he saved the results, the Change to box cleared and left his new
    weight visible in the Current weight box.

    Next, Sean went to the body tone area which was controlled by a scroll bar. Currently his tone factor was set at 1/8. Sean increased that to 3/4 and pressed enter.
    He again lifted his shirt to watch his new 25 pounds of fat transform into a solid 2 pack, and the rest of the wieght formed more defined muscle tone throughout the
    rest of his body as well. Nice! Sean pressed save again without any hesitation. In the first half an hour of receiveing his new Chronivac, Sean had gained 25 pounds
    of toned muscle, and this was just the beginning!
