    Alice left her friend to get what she needed from her stuff when she found herself in the living room alone with Sharon. Although before this situation would have completely freaked her out, now that Sharon had regained her youthfulness and lost her overbearing, unbearable air, it was quite different.

    "Glad you found what you need," Alice smiled.

    Sharon nodded in agreement, "Yeah, but as soon as Nicole's ready, we're going straight to the clothing store, because I've got so many outfit ideas to try out" said the young businesswoman enthusiastically.

    Alice sank into the sofa. "Yeah, let's do that, what better way to inaugurate a new life of youth?"

    Sharon gave Alice a questioning look. "Speaking of which, I think you should definitely throw another roll of the dice. I mean you're all very well but why stay in your forties when you can get back into the true bloom of age? Believe me, you won't regret it." The young blonde affirmed, handing her the dice.

    Alice thought for a moment. The temptation to join the two women was immense. It's true, all she needed was a roll of the dice to return to her prime, and it wouldn't cost her a thing, just a little attention to prevent the dice from rolling.
    Alice grabbed the dice, waited for them to bind to her and threw them onto the table.

    "forty one years you have walked this Earth. Now this number is but thirty. Each roll shall return more of what you have lost."

    Alice shivered as she heard the voice. Thirty was going to be the perfect age for her. Before Sharon's stunned eyes, the middle-aged woman began to slowly return to her thirties. Her long black hair regained its vitality and her hairstyle gradually changed to a square bobcut. Her face became livelier, wrinkles faded and her face matured, and her skin became softer. Her body, too, changed from that of a woman beginning to tire to that of a woman in great shape. Her breasts perked up and she lost a large chunk of cellulite.

    Sharon couldn't believe her eyes as, inching closer and closer to 30, Alice lost a large portion of fat, giving her a body that could be described as one of a "young woman".

    "Oh my god Alice you look fantastic!" Shouted Sharon, picking up a mirror "Look!"
    Even your hair has changed completely!"

    The new young woman looked at her reflection, never thinking she'd return to a stage like this. A perfect face, with a few faint wrinkles showing her experience, hair a sublime black, a body in perfect health.
    "It really is me...so young" Alice whispered, running her hands over her face.

    "Hey we should so go to a clothing store right now I can barely contain myself- Oh my god Alice you look amazing!"
    Nikki had just gotten dressed when she was stunned by her friend's appearance.

    Alice blushed. "Yeah thanks Nikki, I really feel like a different person now" Alice smiled.

    Sharon frowned, "Yeah, I feel the same, but seriously, do you think it's weird or is it just me?"

    Alice shrugged. "Frankly, after what we've just experienced, I don't think it's all that extraordinary." she giggles.

    Nikki laughs "Personally I don't see the problem with feeling good about yourself."


    Splitting the dice between Alice and Sharon, putting them in each other's pockets, the three women wasted no time and happily climbed into Sharon's Porsche Macan, the three friends making the rounds of all the top clothing boutiques and brands (Sharon insisting on paying for everything out of her own pocket). Sharon decided to wear a green sundress, Nikki jeans and a floral top revealing her shoulders and navel, while Alice opted for a denim jumpsuit.

    "Cheers!"

    As the sun set, the three young women gathered around a cocktail on a beach terrace. It was pure bliss for the three friends, who would now decide what to do next, or simply have a good time and chat.
