    Transformation into her new form

    Jess had this weird feeling resonating from her gut, it almost felt as if her guts were moving around on their own on the inside. It gave her this feeling of 'unease'.

    "What is going on...?" Jess said, holding onto her gut. She had to find a place to sit down, she couldn't handle this feeling. It was becoming to much for her. The suit was in no way helping her, becoming more and more of a burden than something that she just so happened to be wearing.

    "I have got to get this thing off of me, I cant stand it anymore!" She said, reaching behind herself. As she felt her back, something seemed to be off. It no longer felt like a suit, but was more like a kind of skin. As she felt her back something seemed to be missing.

    "Oh my god! The zipper!" Jess screamed. The zipper that was once on her back, holding together her outfit was gone. How could this have happened. The zipper cant, doesn't just disappear off of your back! Thats when she felt it. A sudden jolt of pressure felt upon Jess, something was going on in her gut and she had no idea what. Reaching down towards her gut she realized something, something different. The suit was slowly connecting to her skin. It felt as if her stomach was multiplying, growing in a way.

    "What is happening to me!" Jess said, holding onto her gut. Suddenly, some form of warm sensation was forming. Jess reached down and felt the udder that was connected to her suit, it felt almost real. The warm sensation began to fill into the udder, it was unreal. The feeling was incredible, it was special in a bad sort of way. It only continued to fill and fill. As her udder was filling her legs began to form more into that of a cow, they grew more in girth, seeming more as that of the cow. Her hips grew in size, forming that of a cow.

    "This is so unreal!" Jess said. Out of nowhere, Jess falls over, hands out in front of her with her legs, standing up just as a cow would. "What the!?" She said. All that she could do was watch as her body continued, slowly become more and more like that of a cow. Her body couldn't take any more changes, it was becoming more and more unbearable, every second that went by was like hell. Her body started to gain more and more weight, hundreds of pounds upon hundreds of pounds.

    "Ohhhh... This is unbearable!" She moaned. At her peak, Jess was around 1200 pounds in weight. The transformation only continued to grow and grow. Her insides shuffled around all over the place, growing and moving over and over again. Her hands began to form into a dark fist, then forming a hooves. Her arms began to transform into cow legs, growing in width. The transformation only continued to push on, moving closer and closer to her head.

    "No! No! I dont want to be a cow!" Jess yelled. It was to late. The transformation pushed up to her neck, almost making it impossible to breath. It continued moving upward, it began to change her ears, forcing them into a more triangle, floppy ear. It continued pushing its way around her entire face. Pushing a muzzle out of her nose, and pushing her eyes to the 2 sides of her face.

    After what must have felt like an eternity, of absolute pain. The transformation stopped. Leaving a cow simply sitting in a bunch of hay in a barn. 'What is going on!? Why do I have to be transformed into a cow!" Jess was now stuck, standing in the middle of a barn, now as a full cow.

    "Ahh, I see you drank some of the water!" The farmer said from behind her. Jess slowly tried to look around, seeing that the farmer was now located right behind her. "Well, don't worry to much, its only temporary. But you may be stuck like this for a while. So I suggest getting used to it." He said.

    Why did this have to happen to her, it could have been anyone, but it had to be her!
