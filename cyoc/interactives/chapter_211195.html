    The Great Pyrenees dog was originally bred to watch sheep and goats in mountainous areas. Part of the reason Ms. Fabina chose to become a Pyrenees dog Animalian was to be seen as a protector of those who came with her from Europe. Her opinion on sheep and goats was rather indifferent. But two of the bimbos who have moved into Lyre were starting to sour her view of sheep in general.

    Stacy and Lacey aren't really the type to do proper research into something. They had come in for conversions shortly before the Halloween season really started. That had majorly influenced them to do something 'demonic but sexy'. Obviously, they couldn't become real succubi for Halloween, but they were confident that they could make anything look sexy, even creepy goatman-type demons. And given how their bodies were shaped before conversion, they probably could. However, they ended up missing the point when they became Animalians that weren't actually goats.

    ==========

    "Ooh, we're gonna be the sexiest goat demons ever," said Stacy.

    "Tots, nobody's even gonna come close," said Lacey. There wasn't much to choose from between the two except that Stacy had dark hair and Lacey was blonde.

    "Uh, there is something you should know about the animals you picked," said Judy, looking at the pictures they attached to the forms.

    "You do have them, right?" asked Stacy.

    "Well, yes," said Judy.

    "Then what's the problem?" asked Lacey.

    "It's just that you keep calling them 'goats', but they're not actually goats," said Judy.

    "Mountain goats are totally goats," said Lacey, "That's why they're called mountain goats."

    "Well, they're a relative of goats, yes," said Judy, "Though it's a bit closer to antelopes than domesticated goats."

    "I'm hearing they're a kind of goat," said Lacey.

    "Not quite," said Judy.

    "But mine's totally a goat," said Stacy.

    "That's a sheep," said Judy.

    "But it's got four horns," said Stacy.

    "That's a Jacob sheep. It's a breed that has four horns," said Judy.

    "But I'd still make a cute ram, right?" asked Stacy.

    "Ewe," said Judy.

    "Me what?" asked Stacy.

    Judy resisted the urge to smack her forehead and said, "No, I said 'ewe', as in female sheep."

    "I'd totally be a cute me," said Stacy.

    Judy ground her teeth in frustration. These two couldn't really be that thick, could they? "Ok, fine, have you at least read about what comes with your new forms?" she asked.

    "Yep, pure veggie diet from now on. No problem," said Lacey.

    "Good enough," said Judy. They can find out the particulars of the new instincts, like cravings for salt, later. Judy only had to help with their conversions, it wasn't her job to guide them afterwards. She had enough of a headache already.

    Stacy and Lacey were at least right about becoming sexy Animalians, even if they weren't quite as goat-like as they thought they were. One thing they were both pleased with was how fluffy and warm they now felt. And with winter starting, their thick fleeces would be more necessary than ever.

    While the two of them did make an enticing performance as Baphomet demons, Ms. Fabina had to insist they perform as something else after Halloween was over. It wasn't that there was a problem for dressing provocatively at a strip club, but there are plenty of fundamentalists who would raise a big row about sexualizing demonic symbols in a 'house of sin'. It wouldn't be a serious threat, but it was an annoyance that Ms. Fabina would rather avoid.

    The annoyance she couldn't avoid was having to deal with all these new airheads until the next semester of WSA started. What Ms. Fabina really wanted for Christmas was some peace and quiet away from these bimbos. She'll have to look and see what kind of vacation she'll be able to have at this time of year.
