    Jeff gave some thought on what to become on Christmas. But the one thing that struck him the most is something he used to enjoy as a kid.

    Since he was young, he always love Santa. Every Christmas, Santa has also come to the living room to celebrate with him, his older brother, and younger sister. They spend time together, getting presents, and tell Christmas stories. It was the best day of his life. Now he was older and he knew that Santa doesn't exist and his dad was the one dressed as him. While his sister is young enough to believe it, he and his brother have to play along until she's older enough to understand. Still, he just wished that Santa did exist and would give out presents to good little boys and girls around the world.

    Just then, the idea has struck him. Why not become Santa for Christmas instead. Seems like a strange thing thinking about it, but if he was really Santa every year, it would mean that he can go around the world and deliver presents on Christmas Eve, only to stop by his house to spend time with his family on Christmas Day. And with the Chronivac, he can live that dream to the fullest.

    But the main problem was is how to do it. While completely changing into Santa would be fun. There will always be the problem of what will his family and friends will think of it. Not to mention that it is November 30th today and Christmas won't come on the 25th of December. So waiting would be such a pain. So he has a crazy idea. What if Jeff slowly turn into Santa throughout December until the 25th. And only he would be aware that he is changing both physically and mentally but know one else knows. And considering that he needed a place in the North Pole to begin giving out gifts throughout the world, why not alter reality so that delivering gifts, Santa, the elves, Rudolf, and even the workshop exist in this new world. And then then Christmas is over, he would change back on the 26th in the morning as if nothing happened during that time. In Jeff's head, it is a full proof plan.

    With a shrug of his shoulders, he said to himself, "Time deck the halls."

    Quickly, he started typing the information down to become Santa in the most elaborate and creative way possible. After writing it all down, he reviewed what he wrote.

    Name: Jeff Kendrick
    Age: 16
    Gender: Male
    Race: Caucasian
    IQ: 110
    Language: English
    Sex Drive: Low
    Build: Lean Athletic Teen
    Hair: Short/Black
    Eyes: Light Blue
    Occupation: High School Student
    Family: Father/Mother/Older Brother/Younger Sister
    Love Interest: Sharon (Girlfriend)

    Choice of Christmas Change: Santa Clause
    Mental Change: Yes
    Clothing Change: Yes
    Reality Change: Yes
    Self Awareness: On
    Start of transformation: December 1st
    Duration of Transformation: 25 Days
    How long will it last: 24 Hours
    Returning to Normal: December 26th

    After looking through his options, he nodded and knew that he was ready to take the slow journey to being Santa Clause.

    "Hey we go. Let's get jolly."

    He soon hit the 'Start Transformation Button' and a message started to appear.

    ‘Thank you for choosing the Chronivac 4.0. We hope you will enjoy your new transformed self. Have a Merry Christmas.’

    With that done, he couldn't wait to see what the results are going to be.

    "Now all I have to do is sit back and enjoy the changes." he said as he laid back and knew that everything is going to be interesting.

    So throughout the afternoon and evening, it's business as usual. Jeff did his homework, have dinner with his family, and enjoyed playing a few games while the Chronivac is still active. There is one more thing he does for a living at night, but it feels too soon for him and waited for the right moment to do his private business. Regardless, he didn't care for any of it. All he cares about is becoming Santa and spending time with his family as the jolly red man. He soon went back to sleep and lay on the bed, smiling and wondering how the changes will start. Soon he was dreaming of becoming Santa and spending time with his little sister as the clock struck 12:00 A.M. as the slow and steady changes starting to kick in.
