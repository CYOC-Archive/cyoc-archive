    The mad scientist took several steps back, he had not even programmed the machine yet!
    He had always considered his brother's child a waste of space even before the law had
    placed him in the mad scientist's care so he was not so much thinking about the boy he
    had intended to betray and turn into a thing as his precious machine! It coughed, it
    sputtered, the infinitely complex technology within screaming (power by a combination of
    hundreds of double-A's and pixie sticks).

    He heard his nephew scream from within with alarm and confusion, not pain of course.
    The mad scientist didn't consider himself evil, he didn't want the surplus masses of the
    human race to suffer after all.

    The pitch of Ed's voice got higher and higher as the machine started to collapse in on itself
    almost. Finally, lights that had turned red from green exploded sending fireworks
    everywhere like the stage set of a star trek movie. Then suddenly, Ed's screaming
    stopped. At the same time, the machine came to a smoking stop, the lights within looking
    to be sucked inward! All was silent and all was still around the smoking hissing piece of
    technology that the mad scientist had spend ten years building as his prototype.

    The mad scientist wanted to cry over the loss of his machine due to his idiot nephew, why
    did the mad scientist have to be the only intelligent person in his entire family?

    One of the metal plating of the machine fell over, revealing the transformation chamber.
    Wearing Ed's clothes (now totally ill fitting) was a thirteen year old girl. Or what looked like
    one at first glance. She long bright blond hair. Her skin shinned almost like plastic, in
    place of ears she had white and pink place of plastic in a smooth non-standard shape.
    And her eyes were reflective like glass marbles with a solid blue colored center looking an
    anime character possessed. Had the mad scientist seen something like her in the japanese
    international channel?

    She stumbled about, getting used to her arms and legs almost. After tripping over her own
    jeans for the third time she tossed them off, along with her underwear, shoes socks and
    shirt. The broken glass not even scratching her bare feet. The mad scientist saw she
    lacked one or two of the 'precise' details of the female body but still had the figure of a
    young and lovely thirteen year old lass.

    She stumbled about a few more times amist the ruined machine, she held her head as if
    she was trying to sort out her own thoughts. The mad scientist looked at her in absolute
    awe. Had he programmed this one into the machine? He wasn't sure. there had been so
    many.

    He was so distracted he didn't realize that she was finally getting her footing and senses
    and touched the mad scientist in curiosity. The mad scientist just had time to yelp in
    alarm when his body glowed white. Within a few moments, wearing a lab coat and lose
    slacks, with a couple of large glasses hanging on her nose, was another thirteen year old
    girl with plastic skin and strange ears.
