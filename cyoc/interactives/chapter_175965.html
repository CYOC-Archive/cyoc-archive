    Dude *facepalm*

    After reading through the chapters of various stories you finally happen across the chapter "Hannah becomes Human". Curious, you decide to read through it. As you complete the chapter you sigh, wishing you still drank.

    "Seriously," you groan. Glancing at the clock you realize it's getting really late. Still, a response us needed if for no other reason than to clarify.

    "I was going along with your wishes," you yell, pausing as you realize you probably should have said that softer. "I kept it going with the new thread. We'll go with Robo Hannah I was moving on to the next phase."

    You decide to read the chapter again, obviously unlike yourself, this author saves his writing. You curse yourself, maybe you should save your drafts. "Nah," you decide. "I do this for fun. Besides, I'm getting a kick out of this other guys writing. They seem to have an interesting grasp on Hannah. It's way different than what you originally thought she would be like."

    You rub your eyes, chances are this chapter gets deleted. It has nothing to do with change. Still, hopefully this anonymous author checks it out.

    "Hey listen," you say as you gaze up at the ceiling. "Look, dont know if you hear me. Hell, dont know if you'll even read this before the moderator delete it. But, I'm willing to work with you. Yeah, I had a different idea on where things were going but hell let's see what you got. All I wanted was you to stop being so antagonistic towards a group of characters I've been working on for three years."

    You take a deep breath, and then you chuckle softly to yourself. It's crazy how much you care about a series if characters created on this sight. Funny, you've thought up backstories, histories, relationships, an entire freaking universe about these characters. Hell if you ever had the time you'd probably write a damn compendium on their history.

    "But I digress," you whisper, realizing your vision is starting to get a bit blurry from lack if sleep. "Listen mystery partner keep moving forward with the visit to Circes. You might like it. If you check out the history you'll see theirs magic, anthros, demons, even an animatronic named Vixi who has a soul. We could have some fun with all that."

    You take a deep breath, hoping you spelled everything right but realizing your too damn tired. Chances are this chapter is gone when the moderators see it. That's fine because you realize it's meant simply as a long form text message to this anonymous author. Hopefully they continue off the last chapter you wrote. Otherwise, your writers block is setting in and your not sure where to go next. You now hit the send button, and wait.
