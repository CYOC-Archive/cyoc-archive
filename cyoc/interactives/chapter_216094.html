    The Magic Shop

    Rainforest Bloom Shampoo

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 5 months ago BM O Mythological Magic Mental

    As you took your time to decide what to pick precisely, you were attracted by one of the dainty bottles. All were shaped like precious phials, like the potion of a sorcerer. The shampoo inside was of the lightest green with golden hues. It had the silhouette of a sort of wood nymph, hiding her modesty amongst flowers and vines. Her hair looked gorgeous and lustrous. If your locks could look even half as good!... Of course, it was just a drawing but the smell of the product pushed you to purchase it. It was simply divine. It smelled of orchid, tonka bean, dragonfruit and other flowery scents that hinted at a tropical garden the likes of a paradise lost. You had to have it and since you could buy two, well, you didn't hesitate. The employee congratulated you at your choice and told you to enjoy your items. You sure would.

    Once back home, you get into the state-of-the-art bathroom of that your parents have and prepare for a few hours of lush pampering. You set the water just right, with foamy bubble bath, heat the radiator to have warm towels and the ready and strip naked. You have short blondish hair and you are a bot chubby but you have no severe blemish problems and your face is rather harmonious, truth be told. You slip into the water and start washing, scrubbing, and go through all your pampering routine. The sort done only once in a while to fully relax. Before washing your hair, you lie back and let yourself be engulfed by the delightful soapy water. What a paradise! The sort of simple pleasure that makes you happy to be alive and innocent in your indulgence. You then shower, thinking of a warm tropical waterfall, imagining being on a secluded island lush and warm and fertile. You them pop the false-looking ancient cork of the bottle and pour all of the shampoo upon your scalp. The smell doesn't vanish after a few minutes and it impregnates your entire body too. Awesome! It really smells like a tropical paradise, flowery and fruity. You scrub your scalp, humming and moaning in utter contentment. You imagine yourself on this fabled tropical isle from your imagination. And as you wash it off, you suddenly feel even better, as if your sense of self had to get out of your body, now too small. You lose yourself into the scent, and you are blissfully unaware of the changes assaulting your body. You become slimmer, your hair much lighter, the blond tips turning green. Your skin takes a light green tinge too, and your eyes as well, becoming not blue but light green, as green as summer leaves. Vegetal petals and shoots start coming out of your waist, eyebrows, ears and forehead. Long tendrils do the same. Soon, you'' want to bask in the sun, put your feet in rich fertile soil and eat fruits and vegetables galore. But for now, you revel in your own smell and warm water.

    When you wake, the water is getting cold. You empty the bathtub, feeling a bit woozy. You want to get to your room but on the other hand you wish to go outside and get fresh air and sunlight. To eat grains and green produce. To get the world green and lush. You look at your reflection in the mirror but your image does not startle you as much as it should. You are different but... rather prettier, in a alien and strange way. You can feel the tendrils and vine itching to grab humans and turn them into plant-like creatures. Yes... You should do that... The world would be a lot better... You could even punish the evil... You cannot live in this world and you have the power to change it now so... Why not? Yes... It is time to get some sunlight and help the planet be green and beautiful again...
