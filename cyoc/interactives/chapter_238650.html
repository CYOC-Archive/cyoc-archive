    The Life of a Swimsuit Model

    Author note:
    I got inspired by the title of another story but this story is nothing like that story. I only re-used the title, nothing else.

    Guy Fox hated his name. He wondered why his parents gave him that name. He got teased a lot because of his name but there was nothing he could do about it.

    However, Guy had a magnificent body. The perfection of his body often helped mitigate the pain of his name. Some teased that he was destined to become a porn star but even that teasing held an appreciation for his sculpted physique.

    As Guy was getting old enough to think about his future, he thought he ought to give modeling a chance. He could also explore other similar careers like acting or athletics. He started asking around about getting an agent.

    One of the agents, Alex Walton, told him about an immediate opening at Surf Style. Surf Style sold a variety of products but mostly specialized in swimwear. Alex said they needed someone new immediately and that this was a great opportunity to get started in the business instead of taking the slow route of building up a portfolio or attending modeling school.

    So Guy excitedly let Alex take him to meet Leon King, who would be Guy's boss.

    As soon as they entered, Leon asked, "You brought someone?"

    Alex said, "Here he is."

    Leon said, "Great, there's no time to lose. We're already behind schedule on several photo shoots and no one can find the guy that normally does this job. Quick, get undressed."

    Leon rushed Guy through the process of becoming a model. Guy had to fill out paperwork while he got undressed and listened to Leon's instructions. Leon and his staff helped Guy with all of it to the point that it all became a blur. Guy didn't understand half the paperwork he filled out before he was standing there naked in the middle of the room.

    "Uh, wear is the swimsuit I'm supposed to model?" Guy asked.

    "You weren't listening, were you?" Leon asked. "We have decided that it's a waste to model our products because once a model wears one of our products, it becomes useless to us. So we figured out a way to transform someone into a replica of our product. You can be a replica of one product and then transform into a replica of another product and another product. You can be transformed hundreds of times a day and then go through it all over again the next day, day after day for years."

    Guy felt perplexed but it was already too late. His skin had already started turning blue-black. He was going to be a swimsuit model, or was he going to be a model swimsuit?
