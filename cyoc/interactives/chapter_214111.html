    After a few minutes, the tingling in Jeff's body subsides, the teen looks at himself and then at Ken, who is sitting on Jeff's chair, smiling. “Is that it? Just a tingling sensation,” Jeff asks, sounding confused, “I thought I'd be transforming into something, but I do not feel any different.”

    Ken smiles, “just be patient, Jeff,” he says, “you'll see soon enough what I have done.” Suddenly they both hear the sound of a Van coming closer, they hear the Van stop right outside of Jeff's house and a loud honk. “That must be the Van,” Ken says, standing up and walking towards the window, “yep, it is.”

    “Wh-what Van,” Jeff asks.

    Ken turns, with a smile on his face, and says, “come and see for yourself.”

    Jeff walks over to the window, he looks out and raises an eyebrow, “why is there a Horse transportation Van standing outside my house,” he asks.

    Ken grins, “it is here to pick us up,” he says, his grin widens when Jeff gives him a confused look, “can you guess what I chose?”

    “Am I going to be a Horse,” Jeff asks.

    Ken nods, “yep,” he says, “you know that my family owns a ranch, right?”

    Jeff nods, “yeah,” he says, “I also remember that you told me, that you'd love to have a horse to attend Horse racing tournaments.”

    “That is correct,” Ken says, “there is a tournament coming up this coming weekend, so we'll have five days to train for it.”

    “B-but... I do not feel like I am transforming into a Horse,” Jeff says, sounding confused.

    “That is because I set the transformation to be slow,” Ken says, “you'll turn into a Horse bit by bit every day, until you are a full Horse by the time the tournament starts.”

    “Hmm... that does sound like fun,” Jeff says, he then grins, “I'll do my best.”

    Ken grins, what Jeff is unaware of is, that he will not be transforming into a Horse, Ken simply just altered the reality so that everyone, except him and Jeff, will think Jeff is a Horse, but Jeff will remain human. 'Hehe... this is gonna be so much fun,' Ken thinks to himself, however, Ken is unaware that the alteration has caused an error: the Chronivac simply made everyone see Jeff as a Horse, BUT Ken was not precise what kind of “Horse” Jeff is meant to be. This causes that Jeff could be seen as an male, or as a female Horse, another major part in Ken's mistake is, that he forgot that his family, especially his dad, is searching for a mare to breed with their price male American Quarter Horse “Lightning”. So, while Ken is focused on a Horse race and wants to train Jeff into winning the Horse race, Ken's dad, who is focused on wanting to breed Lightning with a female Quarter Horse, will think that the new “Horse” is the new “Mare” to breed Lightning with. The Van honks once again, the driver becoming impatient, Ken turns to Jeff, “we better hurry, the driver seems to be starting to lose his patience,” he says.

    “O-okay,” Jeff says, the teen turns and starts to head for the door.

    “Just a minute, Jeff!”

    Jeff turns around, he then gulps and blushes when he notices the look Ken is giving him, the boy shakes his head, “no... no effing way am I going to go outside naked!”

    Ken laughs, “well, you won't be needing clothes anyway,” he says, “better strip before you destroy them when you start to transform,” he adds, sticking to the lie that Jeff will be transforming into a horse, keeping his friend in the dark about the truth.

    Jeff grumbles, “fine,” he then says, he then quickly strips off all his clothes, blushing hard once he is fully naked, “this is so humiliating,” he mumbles.

    Ken chuckles, “you'll get used to it,” he says, “besides: since it is summer, you won't need to worry about freezing! Now, let's get you to the ranch.”

    Jeff reluctantly follows Ken out the house, luckily there aren't man people around and no one seems to pay any attention to Jeff, which actually causes Jeff to relax a little. The two teens walk towards the Van, they see the driver's door open and an elderly gentleman steps out, walking up towards them.

    The man has typical old-man-grumpy look on his face, when he stops in front of the two teens he first eyes Jeff. He lets out a huff and turns to Ken, “so, this is the Horse I am supposed to pick up,” he asks.

    Jeff looks at Ken, who grins, “sure is,” he says, he then looks at Jeff and whispers, “don't worry, even if you still look human, everyone, except you and me, think you are a normal Horse. They don't see a naked human teenager.”

    “Pfff... I have seen much more beautiful Horses than this one,” the old man says, “buuut, it is my job, so get the Horse into the trailer and let's get out of here.” He then walks to the back of the Van and opens the back doors, the man then lets down the ramp and steps aside.

    “Okay,” Ken says, he then leads Jeff to the trailer, “just play along and get in the trailer,” he whispers, Jeff nods and does as told.

    Once Jeff is in the trailer, the doors are shut, Jeff sits down on the straw and soon feels the trailer move, “man, this is going to be one crazy week,” he mumbles to himself.
