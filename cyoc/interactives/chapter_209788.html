    Now although Emily had never met her supposed adopted dragon mother face to snout in the physical mundane world she'd met her plenty of times in her
    dreams.

    The female dragon had said her name was
    Emrhysss Skylla
    and had made it clear to her human visitant that
    the dragon name had a bit of
    welsh and greek dragon in it as well as a sibilant "sssss" that signified
    the dragons serpentine heritage.

    Since questioning Emily wanted to know about her ancestors
    both biologically and mythically the dragon Emrhysss had seemed more then happy to fill her in on her adopted serpentine acsestry.

    One thing Emily had noticed in those face to snout dream talks was that both she and the dragon were about the same size to each another in the dreams whilst other human visitants appeared
    'realistic' as tiny mouse sized humans
    whereas Emily in her dream body appeared
    99 times bigger then what she aught to be.

    Emily herself had figured it was a dream distotion
    rather then a hint of something more complicated going on.

    So it was only a matter of time till that Pack Rat from Hell
    raided Mala Hexe's filing cabinet and brought Emily something the
    14 year old English schoolgirl something
    wicked.

    It was a file folder with Emily Smiths name typed on it
    and it was clear the Pack Rat from Hell had managed to lose
    a larger file,
    presumably on Emily herself,somewheres along the air ducts the Pack Rat used to get about
    St Chariots

    However in one of the pockets of the file was nestled a smaller file
    typed
    Emrhysss Skylla.

    At first Emily was excited to have physical photographic proof the female dragon actually was not a dream figment of her imagination but then the pleased and curious Emily
    realized something was off.

    Emrhyss Skylla shared many of the same details as Emily
    such as date of birth,village of birth,Education Board Test Scores,Immunization Record.

    Slowly it dawned on Emily Smith
    what was going on
    Emrhysss was NOT Emily's adoptive dragon mother but was actually Emily herself
    Emily the were-dragon
    or possibly
    Emrhysss the were-human

    Basically one being in two different states

    The Emrhyss Skylla photo and records contained a Dragon Identification Number
    which Emily knew likely contained a digital file of Emily transforming into
    Emrhysss and vice versa

    Did Emily was to see photos of her transforming into her
    dragon alter self ?

    She never got the chance to find out as
    unknownst to her
    the human girls heart began to race,grow and change and new
    muscles began to swell under her pale
    skin as her skin thickened,darkened and the racing scales on her back
    stretched as her leathery wings announced the arrival of a
    more mature dragon Emily turned Emrhysss


    It took all of 33 seconds from Emily to Emrhysss
    who was NOT pleased to find a file on her or Emily lying on the floor under her left foreclaws

    The Wicked Thing was there and not too happy about others knowing her secret

    Emrhysss felt her fire swelling out her ribcage as the rage built
