    Standing outside of the school, Nate watches as a familiar Shelby Cobra races towards the parking lot. Seeing it drift as it pulls into the lot; Nate cringes as the car nearly hits a redheaded girl as she made a last second leap to get out of the way. The redheaded girl glares at the driver and summarily flips them the bird as she shouts out her indignation. Nate shakes his head as he walks towards the classic car; opening the passenger door as he gets in, tossing his school bag in the spot between the two seats.

    “You nearly hit that girl,” Nate commenting as the driver rips out of the parking lot like a bat out of hell.
    Jean doesn’t answer as she works the stick shift; putting the car into second gear. Jean was thinking about what happened earlier as she was having a break between classes at Moon Lake College. Noticing Nate’s cell phone in her car after it started ringing. Picking it up and answering; Jean’s demeanor soured when the person who called was none other than Kenya, Nate’s ex-girlfriend.

    “Jean are you listening,” Nate trying to push himself farther back into the seat; his voice raised as he looks scared at her erratic driving. “Slow down!”

    “Kenya called,” Jean stopping the car at the side of the road; tossing Nate his cell phone after grabbing it from her purse.
    “What the hell did she want,” Nate grabbing the phone; checking to see if he missed any calls stemming from his landscaping business.
    “She wanted to hook up,” Jean growling; “apparently her husband was arrested this morning.”
    “And she felt that was cause to call me,” Nate frowning as he had a message to mow a lawn; “after I told her to never contact me again?”
    “Have you been in contact with her,” Jean looking directly at Nate; her face showing no hint of emotion as she waited for Nate’s answer.
    “Hell no,” Nate putting his phone away; “why would I after she strung me along and I found out she was still seeing clients? Besides,” Nate staring back at her; “you had my phone today and I’m certain you were looking to see if there was any call history between us.”

    Jean thought about that for the moment before sighing; knowing that Nate was correct, she did out of curiosity look through his phones call history. Seeing that the break up text he sent to Kenya last December was the last he ever contacted the were-panthress.
    “I’m sorry,” Jean’s expression softening; “I just had to be sure. Plus I’m a little upset. I called Liv today,” Jean putting the Shelby Cobra back into gear and driving towards Nate’s place; “I was hoping she would be willing to come over to have a little girl on girl fun but she can’t make it to Moon Lake for a few weeks.”
    “Ah,” Nate responding; “because I can’t shift for a few more days after getting attacked last Monday.”
    “Exactly,” Jean sighing; “so know this, when you can shift my brown furred werewolf, expect to be rutted raw and hard. Because I’m not going to stop until I am fully satisfied,” Jean leering at Nate as she arrives at Nate’s house; “and I do mean fully.”
