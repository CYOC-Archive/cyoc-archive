    "Mistress?" asked Ty as he dropped Vanessa's limp body, "you ceased to have a mistress the instant I decided you would make you a servant of the pack and its alpha."

    "Don't make me laugh, I'm immune to the disease that has changed you, and I will never betray my mistress."

    "You may be immune to the change," countered Ty as he gestured to the Draconian's hands and feet, "but that is not the only path open to me."

    Gildden looked down in horror as his claws reformed into soft globes which were then drawn together. As his hands and feet were melded into a single ball, he fell to the ground essentially hogtied. He then felt his tail being pulled up until its tip melded into his back. With a gesture Ty caused the Draconian's armour to fall to pieces.

    Meanwhile, Gildden struggled as he tried to escape. He couldn't polymorph, not that it was likely to work as whatever animal he became would likely also be bound as he was, same for shifting into a human form. Attempting to open a dimension door also failed. Finally he tried to dominate the creature that had done this to him, again failing.

    "Are you done fighting yet?" asked Ty, "I didn't know what other powers you might have, so I just blocked all of them." Bending down he took Gildden by the beard, pulling his head up to look him in the eye. "If you accept your fate quickly enough, and swear yourself to me as my eternal servant, I'll even make you my beta and leave you most of your free will. But if you insist on resisting, then...
