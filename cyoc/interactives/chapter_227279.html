    Transformation Resort: A Father Watches Over Newlyweds

    It was hard to say how many people down in the courtyard pool area were transformed. As far as Josh could tell, there were still plenty of people that appeared to be normal humans. And it's entirely possible that some guests arrived just for the resort activities, rather than undergo any transformations. Josh wondered if perhaps that's why Andrew's friends agreed to also come.

    Still, Josh was sure that the average visitor was bound to become curious enough to try a small transformation at some point. Maybe some visitors are here to shave off a few years. Some might even be adventurous enough to go through with a gender change. Hypothetically, it's also possible that some guests have a more interesting change hidden under their clothing. Really, it was hard to tell just by looking at them, especially from a third-floor balcony.

    They all had one thing in common, though: they could only transform while in the appropriate wings -- either the Age Wing, the Animal Wing, or the Gender Wing. However, with his supreme VIP status, Josh could transform any of them where they stand. Even if the guests he transformed wanted to immediately change back to normal, they would first have to make their way to the appropriate wing, ensuring they spent at least a little time transformed. Josh was excited at the thought. He could have some real fun, without worry of it being immediately undone.

    Looking toward the pool, one guest stood out. A nudist man standing standing near the pool's edge. Josh was pretty sure it wasn't his fiancé-turned-nudist brother. Josh found himself glad that he changed his sexual preference to "bi"; being in a clothing-optional resort would've been rather awkward for him, otherwise. Granted, he knew ahead of time that this place was clothing-optional, but that wasn't going to stop him from fulfilling his transformation fantasies.

    The nudist man seemed in no hurry to get in the pool, instead seeming content on just watching the swimmers. How boring. Josh decided to make things a little more interesting for the man.

    Focusing on transforming the man, he found himself in the white void, with the man's profile popping up. "Steven Hartley, Age 42." Finding an option to check his previous transformations, he found... none. So it *is* possible for someone to resist the transformations of the resort. Josh was gonna make sure he would change that.

    But first, was Steven here with anyone? It would certainly explain why he hasn't transformed, if he was here for someone else. Not finding a specific option to check that, he decided to go to the "relationships" option. Much to his surprise, only three names popped up.
    Margaret Hartley, Wife, Age 39
    Jenny Ray, Daughter, Age 18
    Kevin Ray, Son-in-Law, Age 21

    Were these really Steven's only relationships?

    Sensing his question, the resort's computer chimed in. "As a supreme VIP, you are able to change anyone in the resort, but *only* in the resort. While reality changes can affect relations outside the resort, you yourself have no control over this. As such, lists of relations will only show relations currently in the resort."

    Josh supposed that made sense. He didn't mind, though. He had a whole family he could play around with!

    He brought up the profiles of the other three, and checked on their transformations. To his disappointment, the wife was also not transformed. Things were looking up with the daughter, though -- she was an anthropomorphic monkey, complete with tail. And the son-in-law... was currently a one-piece swimsuit being worn by the daughter. Certainly an intimate way to spend time together. Josh will have to try something like that with Andrew.

    Given the transformations, it was safe that the daughter and son-in-law were at the pool. But where was Margaret? Was it possible to find guest locations? Yes, there she was, at a Yoga class in the Animal Wing. And she wasn't transformed, yet?

    Well, it was time that Josh mixed things up. Who would he transform, and how?
