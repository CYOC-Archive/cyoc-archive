    Jennifer-281s Mission

    added by Scarecrow131071 A month ago O Mental

    Jennifer-281 nodded and walked off, "Yes ma'am."
    She exited the temple and walked back towards the school.
    As ahe arrived at the cafeteria, she got a notification on her phone from her seniors. The message said that she was to report to the briefing room immediately. She quickly made her way to the briefing room, which was in a bunker under the school.
    As she entered the briefing room, she saw a group of students. They were all dressed in identical uniforms to hers and were all standing at attention. She took her place amongst them.
    The senior Jennifer and her assistant entered the room and began to speak, "Jennifer-281 you have been summoned here because you have a very special assignment. You are to become head of the German intelligence agency, the BND. This is an incredibly important job and it requires someone with your skills and experience. You will be leaving today for Germany and will begin your mission tomorrow. Understood?"
    Jennifer-281 nodded, "Yes ma'am."
    The senior Jennifer continued, "Now, you will be going undercover as a student at a university. Your cover story is that you are from a wealthy family who have sent you to study in Germany. You are fluent in German and you are familiar with their customs and culture. You will be given a set of documents which you will use to identify yourself and to gain access to various facilities. You will also be given a smartphone which will be used to communicate with us. All of the information you need is on this flash drive. Understood?"
    Jennifer-281 nodded, "Yes ma'am."
    The senior Jennifer finished, "Good. Then you are dismissed. You will be taken to Germany in an hour. Good luck."
    The senior Jennifer and her assistant left the room, leaving Jennifer-281 with the other students. They all looked at her and nodded in respect.
    She smiled and said, "Thank you. I will do my best to carry out the mission."
    She looked at them and said, "You will all do well too. We will be the future of Germany. We will show them that the Tanaka clan is not to be underestimated."
    They all nodded as Jennifer-281 got ready for her flight.
