The last images that came was me and another with a baby girl facing two gods then blackness before my dream. They had a dream like quality but with what I was told I was more inclined to believe that they were memories of who I was.

I wondered how this could be until I remembered that this was the Nexus and things didn't follow the normal rules. I made my way around the outside of the mall looking for Ansley figuring that he would be outside the mall if he looked like a homeless person. At the back I saw the docks but there was just a bit of land and no way for trucks to back up to them. Of course this was the Nexus so they probably just popped into place when they were needed. After looking around the outside of the mall and not finding anyone I decide to go into the mall. The first thing that I see is the food court and looking around I noticed that many of the people were in the process of transforming and by the looks on their faces I'd say many of them were unaware that they were transforming. I didn't see any sign of Ansley though so I continued my search.

    "Excuse me," a woman said tapping on my shoulder.

    "Yes," I said turning around to face her. She appeared to be in her late teens early twenties.

    "I was wondering if you could help me," she said with a smile.

    "Depends on what it is," I said with a shrug.

    "Well I was wondering if you would pretend to be my girlfriend so my boyfriend will get the hint that it's over," she said with a slight blush.

    "I don't think that will work," I said shaking my head. "I'm not old enough to be your girlfriend. Your boyfriend wouldn't believe it."

    "Well we can take care of that with a trip to one of the stores," the woman said holding out her hand.

    "Ok lead the way," I said gesturing for her to do just that.

    As she led me through the mall towards the stores I wondered at the wisdom of following this woman when I didn't know her name. I was about to ask her name when some guy shouted, "Begone, Jaundoco. She is not to become your pet or any part of your sick little game."

    Jaundoco grabbed me by the arm as Ansley said, "She made her decision long before she came here. Now let her go."

    "I don't think so," Jaundoco said narrowing her eyes and tightening her grip on my arm.

    "What's going on here?" I asked trying to wrench my arm free.

    "Jaundoco transforms people into animals to be sold as pets and livestock," Ansley said.

    "That doesn't go against the rules of our people but interfering does," Jaundoco said pulling me close.

    "I didn't agree to be transformed into an animal," I said looking daggers at Jaundoco.

    "You just have to agree to a transformation," Jaundoco said with a wicked smile. "Since you did you're mine."

    "She's already made her choice Jaundoco," Ansley said. "She has a different destiny."

    "Destinies can be changed by the decisions people make," Jaundoco said her voice starting to drip with venom. "I let the last one go but this one is mine. She said she was willing to be transformed and that is going to happen."

    "What were you going to transform me into?" I asked out of curiosity.

    "A female foal," Jaundoco said.

    "You will not be transforming her," Ansley said. "The deal isn't sealed until she is and thus she has time to back out."

    "Then I don't want to be transformed by you," I said to Jaundoco trying to pull my arm away again.
