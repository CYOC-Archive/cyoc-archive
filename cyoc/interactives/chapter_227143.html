    Sweets For Thought

    added 4 days ago O

    In the end you realize there’s not much you can do. Magic existed, you pissed off the wrong guy, and now you were stuck as a 12 years old with an older brother instead of a little sister. You’ve accidentally caused your life to permanently change since coming to The Magical Candy Factory and there was absolutely nothing you could do to fix it. Giving up, you’re about to leave when you notice the aisle sign again. You stare at it for a few seconds before getting an idea. Maybe there was something you could do after all.

    You go to the jelly bean section and grab one of the supplied bags and fill it up with as many different colored jellybeans as possible before taking it to the cash register where they weigh it and charge you. After paying you walk pass the greeter who gives you a smile and a wave before you exit the store and head back home.

    It’s an usual Friday afternoon for your household. Except it isn’t. The whole house was empty. Usually your Mom would be home taking care of your little sister but now that she was an 18 year old there was no need. Both of your parents are now probably at work and you remember that Brad had to go to his job at McDonald’s which meant… you had the whole house to yourself! Perfect.

    You find your muscle memory makes you enter Brad’s bedroom before you do a double take and enter what used to be your little sister’s bedroom. It’s the same room from before, the bedroom of a 12 year old boy which you unfortunately now were.

    Stopping at your desk, you put the bag of jellybeans on top and glance at them apprehensively. There was no reason for you not to take one. Nobody was home and you had plenty of time to try them, it being the weekend and all. Heck, reality even altered to whatever way you transformed. You just had to try them. However, you can’t bring yourself to eat one. At least not yet. You had no idea what any of them did nor what they were capable of. You got off easy last time by luckily eating two jellybeans that made you become younger by four years and giving you a pair of white briefs. But would you dare to try some more or even the different colored jellybeans?

    You take a few jellybeans out and toss them in the air with your right hand before catching them. This was a hard decision to make. Although there was nothing wrong with being a kid again, it definitely didn’t sound like a paradise to you with the lack of freedom. And the only way you were getting that back was by being 16 again. The jellybeans you bought are capable of turning you back to normal, all you had to do was eat the right ones. You think it over before you decide on what to do next.

    What do you do now?
