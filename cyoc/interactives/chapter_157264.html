    Messing With the Family A Bit

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by abc13 3 years ago A AP BM TG O Reality alteration

    Before his dad's boss would come over, Jeff wanted to fiddle around with the various choices he had available to him. Since Jeff basically had a little over an hour, there were no other options besides his family. He wanted to see a unique change that happened for each possible "Vector Type" but made extra sure that "Alter Reality" was selected so that his family wouldn't start freaking out. He'll save those changes for later. He also more or less alternated between selecting "Exposure" and "Trigger" for each of the changes.

    For the first one, he selected "Vector Sight" and "Exposure" in the left partition. He then turned to the right partition and selected "Species" and "Canine". A pop-up asked if he wanted a specific breed of dog or wanted it randomized per subject. Jeff selected to randomize it per subject. "Species Change: 25% [default]" and "Species Change Limit: 100% [default]" were now displayed. Jeff altered the first to 10% and the second to 50%. He didn't want his family to completely turn into dogs. Since "Exposure" was selected on the left, a switch was highlighted on the right to either have the change be instantaneous or to be gradual over a certain amount of time. Jeff selected it to be gradual over the course of ten minutes. He now read the selections in front of him:

    Change #: 1
    Vector Type: Vector Sight
    Vector Toggle: Exposure
    Alter Reality: Yes
    Species Change: Canine [Random Breed Per Subject]
    Species Change: 10%
    Species Change Limit: 50%
    Increase 10% over 10 minutes

    He continued on similarly with the other types. He set it so that whenever anyone looked at him, he set a gender-based change where the subjet would instantly become 5% more like the custom gender he had set; mostly male but slightly female as well. When he was within 5 meters of someone, they would gradually age one year every ten minutes. Whenever someone thought about him, utilizing the Awareness type, they would gradually gain 25% libido every five minutes of thinking about him. Finally, whenever someone came into contact with him, they would instantly gain 50 pounds of muscle. The whole process took five minutes to put together but he had everything ready to go:

    Change #: 2
    Vector Type: Subject Sight
    Vector Toggle: Trigger
    Alter Reality: Yes
    Gender Change: Custom
    Male: 100%
    Female: 50%
    Change by 5% each time

    Change #: 3
    Vector Type: Proximity [Distance: 3 meters]
    Vector Toggle: Exposure
    Increase age by 1 year per 3 minutes
    Alter Reality: Yes

    Change #: 4
    Vector Type: Awareness
    Vector Toggle: Exposure
    Alter Reality: Yes
    Mental Change: Libido
    Libido Change: +25%
    Libido Change Limit: 100%
    Increase 25% over 5 minutes


    Change #: 5
    Vector Type: Contact
    Vector Toggle: Trigger
    Alter Reality: Yes
    Body Change: Muscle
    Increase 50 pounds each time

    With everything set in place, he clicked the green "Apply" button. He waited for a little over two minutes, but felt like nothing happened. He decided to leave his room and see if it had worked.
