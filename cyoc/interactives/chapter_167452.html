    "I promise I'll play with you later, okay " Harriet said hoping to restore Kim's spirits.

    "Okay, but remember you promised." Kim said in her best commanding tone. Harriet chuckled and turned to enter the house. As Harriet walked away, Kim noticed something fall out of her bag and onto the grass. Before she could tell her sister Harriet closed the door behind her, leaving Kim alone outside.

    Kim went over and picked the thing up. "What is this?" She wondered aloud. It looked like the tv remote, but shaped a bit differently, and had different buttons. Kimmi noticed one that said 'me' and set it to that, wondering what it did.

She was in her 70s, with snow white hair, and a thin frail body. "How could your mother or sister just leave you out here alone? Don't they know how dangerous that is? Come here young lady. I'm going to take you to your mother and give her a piece of my mind."

    Kim panicked. She didn't want to get in trouble. Her mommy had told her she'd be fine if she stayed on her lawn. Hoping to somehow stop Mrs. Reed, Kim pointed the remote at her and pressed the button. She watched as Mrs. Reed began to look better.
