    Give you to neighbors daughter

    added A year ago AR I S

    "Ya know," Amanda smirks, seemingly made up her mind, "I think letting you suck on any woman's boobs, especially my own, is far too good for you."
    You breath a sigh of relief thinking maybe she's taken pity on you, but how wrong you were.
    "But thankfully, Claire next door isn't a woman yet, she's just a little girl, so I think letting her be your Mommy is a perfect punishment!"
    You couldn't believe what you were hearing. Before you could object Amanda grabbed a pacifier that she had been given for the assignment and shoved it into your mouth. Despite the fact the dollification had mosty worn off, other than the shrinking, you still felt the uncontrollable urge to suck on the pacifier, unable to pull it free.
    "Isn't that an interesting feature," Amanda said with a smile, "I'm sure Claire will have a lot of fun with that. Now, let's get going!"
    Unable to fight back, Amanda effortlessly snatches you in her arms before grabbing a nearby bag and cramming your body into it. She swings the bag playfully as she walks out of the house and takes a short walk down the street. Meanwhile in the bag you are dreading what's to come.
    Eventually you feel her stop and hear her knock on a door. After a few seconds you hear a voice.
    "Well isn't this a nice surprise," you hear the voice of Claire's mother, Pamala, say as the door opens, greeting your sister.
    "Hey Ms Jacobs," your sister says, "is Claire home? I have a little gift for her."
    "Oh isn't that sweet," Pamala says, oblivious to Amanda's motivation, "Of course, she's just upstairs in her room."
    "Thanks so much," Amanda says, happily stepping inside and rushing up to the young girls room.
    Each step she takes closer to the room your heart pounds in terror as you have no idea what this girl might have in store for you!
    "Hey Claire," Amanda finally says, stepping into the bedroom.
    "Amanda!" you hear the shrill voice of the young Claire exclaim in excitement.
    "I've got a little gift for you," Amanda says, raising up the bag you're in and giving it a playful shake.
    "Ohhh what is it?"
    "It's a new baby doll!" Amanda says as her hand snakes into the bag and yanks you out.
    Finally able to see you see the young girl that Amanda has decided will be your Mommy! Claire has long blonde hair in pony tails and wears a pair of jean shorts and a barbie t-shirt. At the sight of a new doll Claire's face lights up in excitement.
    "Oh my gosh really!" she says in excitement.
    "Really!" Amanda affirms, a wicked smile on her face as her plan seems to be taking effect, "But it's not any doll it's very special!"
    With you still in Amanda's arms she explains all of your 'features' to the young girl. At the sound of the sucking feature Claire's eyes go wide as she sees you sucking on the pacifier.
    "So I could make the dolly really drink a bottle?" Claire asks
    "Well yes, but" Amanda says with a smile, "This doll drinks from your chest. It's called breastfeeding! Not many dolls do that so it's very special!"
    "That's so cool!"
    Internally you are dreading what's to come as you are handed over.
    "Now you take very good care and be a good mommy to this doll," Amanda says as Claire hugs you tightly to her chest.
    "I promise I will!"
    Amanda starts to leave but stops just before leaving.
    "Oh and that's a very special surprise accessory in your dolly's diaper, but I'll let you figure out how that works," Amanda winks at you before taking off and leaving Claire with the information about her new doll.
    Claire holds you out before her and looks you over excited about what to do next.
    "Okay dolly, first things first, I wanna..."
