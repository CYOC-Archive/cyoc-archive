Every morning before class, Fredrika would open her locker to put away her backpack, only to find some red paint flecks had come off in her hand. Emma, being the concerned friend she is, offered to handle her locker-opening duties. "Don't worry, Fredrika!" she always said. "I would gladly risk paint poisoning in order to protect you!" Kind of dramatic, but Fredrika let her do it - secretly, she freaks out a little when her hands or anyplace else get dirty.

    One thing that was never a secret was Fredrika's tendency to think outside the box and act on ideas before anyone could tell her to consider the consequences. One day, after getting hustled by her lunch money by someone who obviously wasn't going to use it to buy lunch, she demonstrated her tendency by turning to her friend and saying, "We have to transfer schools, Emma. I literally can't survive in this dank hole for much longer."
    Emma ignored the improper usage of 'literally' and asked, "Great idea as usual, Fredrika, but where would we ever go? We have no wheels."
    Fredrika answered quickly. "They have a travel agency over to the strip mall that has a collection of alternate universe portals. We could go to school in an actual cartoon! Isn't that totes sweet?" Emma nodded her head yes, for it was indeed totes sweet. After school, they hustled it to ToonWorld so fast that I didn't have time to type the stuff they did in between.

Fredrika looked surprised when she saw that it was actually staring them in the face, with two giant googly eyes! She then re-encouraged herself and walked up to the booth to speak to the lady receptionist.
