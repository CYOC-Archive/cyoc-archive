    The 2018 Super Bowl, staring Canary Diamond and Aqua Marina

    While most people celebrate Super Bowl by going to a friend's house, watching the game, and stuffing themselves silly with party food, Animalia's Super Bowl Party was held in the Great Hall, and it was more like a giant, indoor tailgate party than a sports-watching party.

    ---

    One of the biggest things learned while there was that many Animalians could handle some breads and grains, as long as there was nothing artificial in them. This would lead to a new Pizza businesses being founded involving the use of all-natural ingredients, and their first big "break", so to speak, was providing custom vegan and/or "meat lovers" pizzas to Animalians. Another thing that was provided were burgers that used edible mushrooms that were as big as hamburger patties (I can't remember what they were called) for herbivore Animalians. Carnivores, on the other hand, would have burgers that didn't have any vegetable toppings that were sometimes placed on burgers. But fast food places weren't really complaining.

    Other common "Super Bowl" foods were present, but they had to be on a buffet table and labeled as to which ones were safe for herbivores/carnivores, which chicken wings had bones, etc.. One really creative new business that was started was one that provided cooked insects for the Animalians who had primarily insects in their diets, as well as new ways of eating them, as long as there was nothing that could potentially harm Animalians, such as chocolate.

    While alcohol wasn't as prominent as it would be at other Super Bowl Parties, there was many different flavors of Orangina available, including a new "experiment" known as "Orangina Hard" which was similar to Mike's Hard Lemonade, but still living up to Orangina's promise of no coloring agents or artificial flavors. In fact, one of the commercials shown during the Super Bowl was for Orangina Hard.

    ---

    There was a big screen having the Super Bowl projected on it, but most of the uplifted Animalians were more interested in the commercials that were played during the Super Bowl, especially those which starred Animalians. Two of those that had the most attention was the Advertisements for both Mieni Atoll and The Lost City of Vilcabamba as vacation spots. Another was how Heather and Jeremy was advertising a new Steak House that had started up in Animalia which specialized in "all the meat you can eat"; and whose advertising motto was "Feed the Beast Inside."

    The only other thing that was payed attention to more than the commercials was the Super Bowl Halftime Show, mainly because of Canary Diamond and Aqua Marina. Their duet performance, known later as "Earth, Sun, Sea, and Sky" was so successful that they were almost guaranteed fame and fortune for seventeen years and inspired a new motto for the new Animalia-Robinson School for the Performing Arts: "Real Fame, No Scandal".

    ---

    There was also a chance to meet some of the newborn members of Animalia, especially young Jake and his family. There was also a chance to meet others who were still expecting. One of the more surprising things to learn was that Iphis and Ianthe, Ty, and Lily were expecting 6 children each, and would be giving birth to them by next summer. Part of the reason why this was surprising was the fact that all three/four of them looked full term already, even though they were only in their second month. Ty was already finding the most to complain about being pregnant, mainly of the aches and pains that came with it, but Brittany, already with her two cubs Tiffany and Ace nursing, replied "You'll get used to it."

    ---

    All together, the Animalia Super Bowl Party was considered a great success, even though most Animalians weren't interested in the game proper.
