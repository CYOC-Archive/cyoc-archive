    "Monkey one!" Ruben called, as he picked up the diaper with a monkey print.

    It was a brown coloured diaper, with a small brown tail attached to the back. The picture on the front of the diaper looked like a cartoonish monkey, with brown fur and big monkey ears.

    Ruben giggled when he saw the cute monkey print. He wouldn't mind this! Ruben threw of his shirt in advance and proceeded to unfold the diaper.
    Excited, Ruben peed his underoos a little as the transforming diaper laid ready for him on the ground.
    Paying no attention to the fact he was peeing a little, Ruben threw off his underoos, and laid down on the diaper.
    But before he could continue to put it on, he lost all control over his bladder and proceeded to fully pee.
    Quickly pulling to front of the diaper over his crotch, Ruben wetted his diaper, before he managed to even put it on.
    Struggling to stop it, Ruben lost his balance, and fell on his back letting go of his diaper.
    Ruben wanted to quickly redo his diaper, but as soon as he managed to get back up, he noticed that the diaper had perfectly applied its own tapes!

    Now peeing inside his diaper, Ruben tried to stand up, but fell over, not being able to stand upright.
    After several more attempts, Ruben noticed it was easier to walk around with his hands as extra support, just like a monkey!
    Walking like this towards his room, Ruben wanted to stand infront of the mirror to see his changes.
    As soon as he looked at himself in the mirror, Ruben noticed his face. He was definitely older again, but he looked a bit different.
    His face had a more, monkey like look and he now rocked a unibrow.
    Feeling a pinch in his spine, Ruben turned around to see that the little tail that was first attached to the diaper, was no growing out of the base of his spine and it was growing.
    Ruben then noticed that his legs, had shrunk. They were now as long as his arms and he felt a strange sensation in his toes.
    He looked down and noticed that his feet were turning into that of a monkey, with his toes growing into little fingers.
    Ruben then noticed a tingling over his entire body, as fur started to grow all over.
    His ears were the last to change, as the grew in size, and turned outward.

    Ruben looked at himself in the mirror, pleased at the monkeyboy he had just become.
