    Prosthetic Bodysuits (Alternate)

    The doctor carefully examined each boy, taking careful notes as he did
    so. After some time examining his notes, the Doctor nodded. "Good,
    good. Now boys, we will be testing an experimental prototype on the
    both of you. The test will take a year, and you will be expected to
    come in once a week for a routine checkup and any adjustments that
    need to be made to the prototype. At the end, you will, of course,
    will be given a final production model to do with as you see fit. If
    you want to back out, now is the time. If not, we will proceed."

    A year? The boys hadn't expected a year. Giving each other nervous
    glances, the boys agreed. It wouldn't be that bad, and they'd make
    money. It's not like there were any other jobs for boys their ages.
    The two ten year olds nodded, and in response, the doctor retrieved
    two full-body suits. They certainly didn't look like much. "Strip down
    and put these on." The doctor ordered. Too far in to back out now, the
    boys did as ordered. Stripped down to their birthday suits, the boys
    both put on the full body suit and zipped up the back. The suit seemed
    kind of cheap. It was roughly skin color and felt like it was made out
    of cheap cloth.

    The Doctor, not wasting any time, pulled out his laptop and plugged
    each suit into it through a tiny port in the suit. Typing something
    into the laptop, the suits came to life.

    Rian had been having doubts about this. What kind of cheapo thing was
    this? Test it for a year? To him, this entire thing was starting to
    sound crazy. In an instant, though, it felt like the bodysuit began
    constricting around him. He felt crushing pain, but couldn't call out.
    Looking down at himself, Rian saw something he thought was impossible.
    The suit was sinking in to his skin. Rian never would have believed it
    unless he had seen it himself, but it was. Worse, beyond the pain, he
    felt it changing him. How, he couldn't tell. Not yet.

    With a final, intense, burst of pain, it was all over. No more
    crushing pain, no feeling of the suit on his skin. Nothing. On his
    hands and knees on the floor, the first sign that something was wrong
    was the long, red hair cascading down into his view as if attached to
    his head. It was also what he didn't feel, so much as what he did,
    that tipped him off. Cautiously, fearfully, looking down his now soft
    body, he saw, or didn't see, as it were, a sight that had him chilled.
    His boy parts were gone! Standing up wobbly, Rian looked around the
    room, only to spot another girl whom he had never seen before,
    standing naked where Bradley had been before. Turning towards the
    doctor, Rian panicky asked, "Wh-what did you do to us!?" Stumbling at
    the sound of his new voice.

    "Now now, don't panic. You agreed to the test. These bodysuits change
    the wearers in nearly any way. We need long term test subjects. You
    agreed. At this point there is no way you'll be able to take the suits
    off. That can only be done here, which, I assume, means you will be
    showing up for your appointments. Adjustments may even be made to the
    suit that you will prefer. Your parents are being informed as we speak
    and your wardrobe replaced to fit your current bodies. My suggestion:
    learn to enjoy being girls. You will be them until we end the test. A
    nurse will be in shortly with clothes to fit you. After which you will
    be free to go home. Have a good afternoon and happy testing." The
    doctor, who had disconnected the girls from the computer while talking
    to them, leaving the ports on their backs nearly impossible to find
    unless you knew what you were looking for, picked up his laptop and
    left the room. Standing in the room, two formerly male ten year old
    boys, now naked, ten year old girls, stood quietly in shock as the
    doctor left and the nurse entered with new clothes for them to wear.
