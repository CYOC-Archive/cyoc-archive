    Michael walked through the expansive estate admiring it's beauty as Sara headed to her pool. He was shocked at what Sara was talking about for multiple reasons. First, he knew Sara is intelligent probably one of the most intelligent people on the planet but being able to turn another human being into a clone of her, there is no way that can be real. But Sara isn't the type of person to talk unless she knows she can deliver on her promises. Okay, but turning someone turning into a clone of themselves has to be unethical right? Well then again, she isn't forcing Michael to become her it's still his choice besides becoming a clone Sara won't be a bad thing she's smart, a billionaire, and very attractive, plus Michael still gets to keep his memories and sense of his old-self. The more he thought about it the more he wanted to go through with this. He waited until Sara and him would have dinner again. Upon sitting down he said

    "Sara I want to accept your offer!"

    "Wow, Mikey are you sure? You still have three hours left."

    "Yes I am sure I want to be another you, I want to be the boss of two wildly successful companies!"

    "Okay if you say so, wake up at 7:00 A.M. tomorrow and meet me in by the pool. For now let's enjoy dinner.... Sara"

    With that it was now 7:00 A.M. and Michael was waiting by the pool.

    "Morning you excited?!"

    "Yes I am!"

    The two drove to a building near by one of the lakes on the estate.

    "What's this for?"

    "Oh this is just my at home laboratory follow me!", Sara said as she and Michael got out of her truck

    Michael was blown away by this lab and all of the equipment. He saw it once on one of the postcards Sara sent him but wow was it nice. They walked down to the basement and it looked like a futuristic operating room. There was a giant human sized-oval shaped tube in the room.

    "Sara I thought you were a dermatologist, not a surgeon what's with the operating room?"

    "This isn't an operating room it just looks like one, sit down on the chair over there."

    Sara took some samples of blood from Michael before putting it in a weird device and Michael wanted to kill the silence

    "Sara how exactly does this work?"

    "Basically you'll become a giant embryo and then get injected with my genetic material and you're me. Trust me you'll know how I made this possible at the end"

    "How long will this take?"

    "About a week!"

    "Wow that's long!"

    "I know but it is to ensure everything goes right".

    The machines went off

    "Okay we can begin, Mikey the drugs I am injecting you will make you feel hot, so you may want to strip."

    "Before or after?"

    "Your choice."

    Michael took off his clothes and Sara injected the drugs into his veins and he instantly felt the heat. Sara quickly escorted him into the tube and gave him a an oxygen mask connected to the tank he entered.

    "This will make sure you get enough oxygen while suspended, before I start this is your final chance, if you want out give me a thumbs down or forever hold your peace."

    "Okay let's do this then!"

    Sara locked Michael in the tank before a red fluid filled the tank before suspending Michael in it. Sara and Michael now had to wait a week.
