    Dean, Mike, Todd and Aaron had all been transformed by the cursed manor. Their history changed to a degree that, whilst all their actions in life had still occurred, people ignored that they all had physically, and for some, mentally changed completely. They soon graduated and moved on to college, as did the entire year level. Each of them would delve deeper into their new lifestyles, and soon learning to despise how they were before they went to that haunted mansion.

    All people ignored their change, that is, except for three fellow students, now at the same local college as the transformed four, who couldn’t put the change behind them. The three of them had heard about the changed fours dare to go into the closed off and cursed manor, and maybe thanks to destiny, their minds noticed the obvious change. The three of them, already close friends, met together and decided that they needed to find out what had happened to Dean, Mike, Todd and Aaron.

    They gathered at the foot of the old manor at night, just as it began to slowly rain.

    Seb was the leader of the ground, thanks to his confidence and his general smarts. He was tall with a slight belly, with short, black hair that was very knotted. He’d never felt the need to brush his hair, or dress well. He’d sleep in the clothes he’d wear out and likely never changed unless he had to. He’d never been all that popular in the dating scene with guys or gals, which hurt him a lot, but he’d never admit that.

    Keith had recently gotten into sports, but it had yet to show. He was skinny, with only slight areas of slim fat to give him some size. He’d been desperate to get into any sport team but lacked enough skill or confidence to join anything. Keith didn’t even have the confidence to admit his feelings for Seb, who’d been a close friend since middle school. He had short blond hair that was neatly combed. He wore a polo shirt and jeans.

    Julian was short and skinny and couldn’t grow facial hair to save himself. Julian was thin, short, and lanky. He’d, like Keith, was beginning to take interest in sports. He’d decided to focus on running, as the effort of bulking up with muscle seemed too strenuous for him. He’d been beginning to make progress, but after struggling to keep up with Keith on a run, was beginning to think he should give up.
    The three of them climbed clumsily over the closed, viny wall around the cursed manor, and quickly ran towards the front door as the rain became heavy. They entered the manor, and finding a light switch, turned it on.

    “Okay, let’s split up and figure out what’s up with his place,” Seb said.
    “Are we sure we should split up? Something happened to Mike and that…I mean, it seems to be cool, but like, we don’t know what happens here?” Said Keith.
    Julian kept quiet while looking up around the vast details of the room.
    “Come on, grow some balls Keith! Julian doesn’t care, right Julian?”
    Julian nodded in agreement. “Yeah, I mean, surely there must be an explanation. We’ll be fine. Let’s plan to meet up here in like, a couple hours.”

    Everyone nodded in a agreement, and went to explore a part of the manor.
