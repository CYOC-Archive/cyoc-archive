    "Sorry," she blushed. "Guess once a klutz always a klutz huh."

    Katie laughed inspite of herself as she shook her head. "Part of your charm Sammy," she said. "Thanks you two for coming I'm really worried about Gloria."

    Katie now explained to Mike and Samantha her concerns before telling her about Glorias date. Samantha nodded cheerfully as she looked at her longtime boyfriend.

    "So you want me and Mike to chaperone," she stated. "I think we can definitely do that."

    "Well," Katie said sheepishly. "That's....not exactly what I had in mind."

    Samantha gave her friend an odd look before realizing what she was insinuating. Samantha's eyes went wide eyed as she understood what Katie was saying. "Oh COME ON," she exclaimed. "What," Mike asked. "What are you two talking about?"

    Samantha sighed before looking up at her boyfriend. "Honey what would you say about reliving our first date?"

    Gloria nervously glanced out the window as she and Felicia anxiously awaited their dates arrival. The girls would be driven to a restaurant by Nates mom before going to a bowling alley afterwards. For Felicia and Gloria it sounded like a nice enoigh first date, yet they found themselves still nervous.

    "You feeling alright," Felicia asked nervously. Gloria turned towards her friend smiling. "Yeah I'm okay," she said. "Just nervous. By the way you look great."

    "Thanks," Felicia said shyly. "My mom picked the dress out for me and your mom did my hair. You don't think its too much do you?"

    "No," Gloria said. "I think Adam will be very impressed."

    "Gloria, Felicia?" Katie called. Gloria and Felicia curiously approached the steps, wondering what she wanted. As they approached they could see a nervous Katie standing at the top of the stairs.

    "Look," Katie sighed. "I...I know you two think I'm over reacting but Im really nervous about this. Thats why I want someone to tag along on this date."

    Before Gloria or Felicia could protest Katie asked for Samantha and Mike to step forward. Both teens went slack jawed as they saw Katie's friends. "No way," they gasped.

    Samantha and Mike were now physically 14 years old again. Samantha was a waifish girl with a youthful face while Mike appeared quite grumpy. The former athlete had relived a several growth spurts in reverse, now finding himself shorter than his childish looking girlfriend.

    "Sammy and Mike are going to come with you two," Katie stated. "I know you wouldnt want them as a chaperone so instead they're going to join you, just to keep an eye on things."

    "And what am I supposed to say to Nate and Adam," Gloria frowned.

    "Well this is your cousin Sammy and her boyfriend, " Katie said quickly.

    Gloria sighed as she looked at the rejuvenated Samantga and Mike. As annoyed as she was that Katie would go this far, she realized that it was better than having her sister spy on her date. "Okay Katie," Gloria grumbled. "I'll go along with this but when the date is done we're having a seriously long talk."

    Katie smiled as Samantha and Mike trudged down the stairs to join Gloria and Felicia. While she knew she was going to get an earful from Gloria later she realized this needed to be done. Hopefully she was just overreacting and Glorias datewould be a fond memory she would cherish for years to come.
