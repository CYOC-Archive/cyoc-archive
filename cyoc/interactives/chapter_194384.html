    Dad barely made it inside the door as he started hearing barking. He noticed multiple dogs barking as he looked down to see 3 puppies jumping up at his legs. Dad set the bag down and then started petting the dogs. Dad looked up to see Mom standing there, "Who else used the medallion?"

    Mom cracked a smile, "After you left, Tina and Brad were having so much fun watching Kimmy run around as a dog that they decided to try it too!"

    Dad bent down to pet all three dogs again smiling, "Who is who?"

    Mom pointed at the collars and looked confused., "Hmmmm... Kimmy has the pink collar, Tina has the yellow one and Brad has the blue one!.....I think..."

    Dad nodded, "Ok...I guess that's easy enough?"

    "Where did Andrew go?" Dad asked.

    Mom replied, "I think he went downstairs to watch TV."

    Dad poured three bowls of food for the dogs as Mom and Dad watched them eat.

    "We should let them outside to potty!" Mom said as she let the dogs outside.

    Mom and Dad watched as the three puppies all ran and played outside. One by one they all went to the bathroom and continued running around the backyard.

    "I think they are having fun!" Mom said smiling at Dad.

    Dad nodded, "It's great to see Shooter again running around...even if there are three of him!"

    Dad nodded as he smiled, "Hmmm....It's been 12 hours since we both used the medallion...do you want to try something else out?"
