    Jewels heads to open the door for her mistress only to find it locked, one of the researchers had gotten over his horror to run around and lock the door.
    "Jimmy, we can't let you out, you know that"
    "It's Jewels now. What are you guys afraid of?"
    "Your two turned in to clowns for fucks sake, you were turned into a girl and you’re a clown girl now. What the fuck do you think?" the two researchers stammered out
    "But I love being a clown girl, it feels sooo good, I feel funny and free. You'll love it and Mistress Gigi" Jewels lays her hands lovingly on Gigi. The look of horror returns to the faces of the researchers.
    "You know you want to become sillier *giggle*. Are you sure you don't want to join us?" Jewels leans in to give them a clear view of her new assets but is rebuffed by firm rejections from both of them.
    Gigi steps forward and announces "Let’s show them so of the new skills we have, a Magic Trick" she pulls out a purple sheet and hangs it in front of Jewels "I know shall now make Jewels disappear and reappear" despite the circumstances, both researchers laugh and shake their heads in disbelief "That's the sprit guys, Kazam!" Gigi pulls away the sheet to an empty space that Jewels previously occupied. Both researchers react with shock and start to peer through the glass looking for Jimmy. Both of them let out a loud shriek as Jewels comes up from behind them and embraces both of them and planting a kiss on each of their cheeks. Jumping back they hit the glass and upon seeing Jewels their shrieks find new levels and pitches. Based on what happened when Jimmy was kissed by Gigi they were about to become clowns and the thought terrified them but they laughed. The laughter suddenly stops as they look at each other with shock, they make a break for the door only to fall clutching their heads halfway there.
    "Please, no" crying both men plead for the changes to stop but go unheeded just like Jimmy's did.
    Their skin starts to change from dark brown and tan to a light pink, white forms around their mouths in ovals and around their eyes in arches, black forms on the lower lips, curls up and thickens in to a smile, blue blush forms around the white of their mouths, finally thick painted on blue eyebrows replace their old ones. Both men where different heights shrink down to the same short stature, their bodies filling out to a very chubby appearance-the bellies bouncing out to large round shapes extending far over their waists. Giggles fill the air and shake their plump bodies. The hair on top of their heads falls out and the remaining turns a dark blue and shortens to the same style on both of them. A tornado of color surrounds the two, when it vanishes both are wearing identical outfits-small black shoes, green pants, bright yellow shirt and white ruffs. With a pop small red noses appear and the two hop up and bow in thanks to Jewels.
    "Thank you" is repeated in unison from the twin clowns
    The three march around and open the door for Mistress Gigi who walks out and embraces Jewels.
    "Mistress Gigi, thank you" is heard again in unison from the twins kneeling before her.
    "Your welcome, Bim and Bam. Shall we head out and have some fun?"
