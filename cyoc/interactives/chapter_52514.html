    Suzy fumbled with her wallet, still not used to having paws for hands. She paid and was waiting for the fish when she felt a tingling in her mouth. It was a dull kind of tingle that Suzy tried to ignore. She walked over to the pickup side waiting for her fish. On an impulse, she ran her tongue over her teeth. Suzy gasped when she felt her teeth getting longer! Quickly they formed into feline-type teeth leaving Suzy bewildered. She had not had this happen before. It wasn't all of her teeth that got larger, just a few. Never before had the shoes actually changed something. They only enlarged her own body.

    "Here is your order Miss," the cashier said.

    Suzy thanked her and walked over to a table. Her mouth was still tingling as she took her first bite of the fish. Oh wow! The fish tasted heavenly! Quickly she devoured all the fish. Her mouth kept on tingling while she ate.

    Once she was done, Suzy opened her mouth to lick her paws and her tongue was able to reach her paw from quite a distance. Suzy stopped and looked around. No one seemed to be paying any attention to her. She opened her mouth again and stuck her tongue out. It had to be a foot long now! Her tongue tingled again and she watched her tongue grow to 18 inches long.

    "OK Suzy, don't freak out," she said to herself.

    Suzy got up and walked out of the food court thinking hard. She put the boots on and two things changed. Her teeth became more feline and her tongue grow longer. Why two things now? Unknown to her, the magics from the wizard and the leprechaun were both working inside her. When she put the boots on, both magics started up. When she broke the rules now, she would change a little more feline AND a body part would enlarge.

    Suzy wasn't sure where to go now. Two hours had passed since she put the cat suit on. She had 8 hours left on it. With no real direction, Suzy headed...
