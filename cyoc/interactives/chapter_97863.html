    I've seen better days

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 9 years ago O

    With Anna distracted and hopefully changing back in the other room, you lunge for Joy. Before you can get to her, she jumps partway through the door into Anna's former cell. However you are able to dive and grab her by the ankle. With a yank, you pull her back towards you, and with a punch you strike her on the side of her head.

    Unlike the movies, she doesn't pass out from a single hit. Instead she grunts and swings a punch at your eyes. A split second before contact, you see she has several fangs positioned between her fingers, like spiked brass knuckles. You flinch out of the way, but the teeth scratch down your cheek. You cry out in pain, and Joy uses the advantage to stand up and punch again and again - each hit tear flesh from your face. You try to kick her, but she sidesteps the hit and sends a knee to your crotch.

    That hurt, but after the first few seconds you decide the pain on your face is much worse.

    With one desperate attack, you lunge at her again. From behind, you feel a clipboard fly into the back of your head. It causes you to wince as you move forward, but it was enough of a distraction for Joy to take advantage again. She punches and drills the discarded fangs from Anna's previous transformation right into your neck. You cry out and stumble to the floor. The world doesn't seem quite as much in focus anymore.

    "Hold where you are!" You're able to see your blurred vision three guards near the door. Anna, human and nude, is standing near them, looking as angry as ever. All three have guns trained on her.

    "It's not my fault!" she shouts at them. "Joy! Tell them...."

    "She killed Danielle," Joy screams. "Take her out!" Several succession of bullets later, Anna falls to the floor.

    I wonder if those were silver bullets, you think to yourself. The fact you're dying on the floor seems lost on you now.

    "Get him in the cell. Do it, now!" she shouts.

    You pass out.

    What do you do now?
