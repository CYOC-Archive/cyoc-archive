    "Well, this is just great. This is the best day ever! Absolutely no way it could get worse!"

She cursed, realizing she'd have to be careful with her lies lest she become nothing more than a writhing pile of tentacles. She fished her phone out of her pocket, ready to call her friend Sarah, who drove a small truck to school every day - Jen didn't want her new slime-producing tentacles to coat anyone's seats, after all.

"God fucking damnit," she said, her slime-coated phone next to useless. She had to enter her security code with her nose, along with finding the phone app and calling up Sarah.     It took Jen around an hour to slither to school, and it was well underway by the time she got there. The local security guard, Mr. Fenman, was out on patrol. He had a tendency to quiz late students on their whereabouts, which Jen dreaded.

    "MS. SMITH! WHAT DO YOU THINK YOU'RE DOING?" he bellowed as she slowly slithered towards the school, her mass of tentacles twisting and leaving a trail behind her.

    "Oh, uh, nothing, sir!"
    "Likely story! Why are you late today, Ms. Smith? And quit hiding your head behind all of your tentacles!"
    "Well, I slept through my alarm, and then my parents couldn't drive me here. Mom was already at work and dad's car is broken, you see, so-"
    "So what! Cut the excuses!"
    "-s-so I tried to call my friend, but she refused, and now I'm here late."

    "Well, alright, in you go, but I'll have to mark this down! One more and you get detention for a month!"

    Jen slithered in past Mr. Fenman, and groaned as even more tingling began. "Oh, great. More." she thought, the 9 new tentacles growing in around her. A new, sharp tingling befell her back, however. Soon she realized the 2nd part of Kyle's curse had taken effect — she was now spineless, given her entire torso was covered in tentacles!
