    Entity #0047
    Codename: Cursed Tiaras
    Known Abilities: A set of seven tiaras set with an identifying gemstone, and an old book bound in thick leather. When Entity is worn, the subject is placed under a curse, believed to be the original curse the original owners possessed. The tiara Entities each universally make the wearers female and think of themselves as a princess. Should all seven be worn at the same time, the wearers will suddenly surge with energy and transform the planet to their tiara’s design, forming seven kingdoms for each princess to rule for centuries.

    0047-1: The book itself is mundane, with no magic properties of its own. Merely it describes the history of the tiaras and explains what each one does. Much of the information about the tiara Entities is derived from this text.

    0047-2: A red ruby tiara bearing the Curse of Lion. The wearer becomes a ten foot tall woman practically made of gigantic rippling muscles. It’s said that subjects of her kingdom would share her curse and be permanently hyper-muscular as well.

    0047-3: An orange jasper tiara bearing the Curse of Horse. The wearer becomes a woman who bears has hermaphroditism. Her penis would be said to be very thick, and long enough to reach her ankles. It’s said that subjects of her kingdom would share her curse and become exaggeratedly endowed hermaphrodites as well.

    0047-4 A yellow topaz tiara bearing the Curse of Swine. The wearer becomes a woman who is immobilized with obesity and possesses bottomless hunger. It’s noted that those suffering this curse no longer require food and drink to survive, however whenever they have not eaten for an hour their anxiety spikes. It’s said that subjects of her kingdom would share her curse and become morbidly obese and constantly hungry.

    0047-5: A green emerald tiara bearing the Curse of Skunk. The wearer becomes an obese slob of a woman with very little personal hygiene, and gigantic amounts of atrocious gas, especially farts. It’s said that subjects of her kingdom would share her curse and become obese gasbag slobs.

    0047-6: A blue sapphire tiara bearing the Curse of Blowfish. The wearer becomes a spherical woman with twiggy arms and legs and a fat face. Those suffering from the curse constantly feel bloated and are fearful of sharp edges. Popped victims eventually reform and re-inflate after a short period of time. It’s said that subjects of her kingdom would share her curse and become spherical balloon people.

    0047-7: A purple amethyst tiara bearing the Curse of Cow. The wearer becomes a woman with gigantic lactating breasts that are permanently leaking milk. The milk is believed to be highly nutritious and an aphrodisiac. It’s said that subjects of her kingdom would share her curse and become lactating women with gigantic breasts.

    0047-8: A diamond tiara bearing the Curse of Rabbit. The wearer becomes a woman who is perpetually pregnant. It’s believed that every nine months the victim will give birth to one to three children at once and immediately become pregnant again with no outside help. It’s said that subjects of her kingdom would share her curse and become perpetually pregnant women.

    Containment: All seven tiaras and the book should be locked in a leaded container and sealed away in GET’s underground storage facility.

    Other: The book seems to read like a fairy tale, but from what can be gleaned it seems to be some kind of true history. Perhaps it can lead to many more ancient magical treasures?
