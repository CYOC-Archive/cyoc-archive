    On the other side of the world, earlier that morning/day/night. the wolf Sasha had
    peacefully kept her distance while Ulysses and Mary in the most non-threatening and
    non-agressive manner possible had approached the poor passengers and crew. Sadly, due
    to all sharing the experience, and the pain being very real, and the animal people before
    them, they couldn't just shrug it off as a nightmare or a hallucination.

    One woman looked very desperate to know where her son was and if anyone had seen
    him. In fact this was shared with in fact two other sets of parents (all parents of three).
    Whose children were of course at the mansion, human, but very scared and confused who
    couldn't go through the mirror without being mutated by the glass pup's magic.

    Their boat was magically (of course) very much intact and ultimately perfectly ready to
    start up the engine on a moment's notice. But the fact seven children were missing, left
    people very divided on leaving or not.

    One middle aged woman with curly blond locks ran to the two cubs and knelt down and
    looked into both their eyes, she shook her head in transparent disappointment.

    Mary lowered her head, "I'm sorry I'm not your child... "

    "WHERE IS HE?!" She demanded, she had gone from winning a boat trip with her son to
    being turned into a pig monster with endless hunger driven only by base desire and then
    fighting a bunch of lion people and then being switched back.

    Sasha keeping her body low stepped foreword a few feet and said, "Kurse, I'm sure you all
    know who that is since you were all connected to her, sent most of the children after they
    were remade into monsters to kidnap parts of our family... but they failed to be as lethal
    as they should have been and we were able to subdue them... I promise none of them are
    hurt in anyway..."

    ALL the parents clamored at this along with the two children who had remained on the
    island and were human again... the fact most of them had been rendered naked by their
    clothes being ruined (and their precious cellphones and credit cards being barely
    recovered) left some amused, others delight, the majority humiliated or ashamed.

    The woman who had stepped foreword said, "Is my son there? His name is Dennis."

    Lion and wolf looked at each other, no one had a clue what glass or any of the children's
    names were.

    Abbey was with glass behind several out-croppings of rocks.

    'Why is she asleep? We don't sleep, not in the outside world... not ever...'

    But unconscious was precisely what Glass was. Or whatever her real name was. Abbey
    wondered if she'd be blindly loyal and loving to the pack and Abbey's father as the original
    transformation of Abbey's powers stated they would as Alice had explained.

    Sasha meanwhile knew they were trapped. There was no way in Hades any parent worth
    their DNA would leave this island without their child, and there was no safe way to bring
    their children back to them without mutating them into glass wolves in real time. And
    Glass... Sasha shuddered, knowing if it really was Abbey's powers and not an after effect of
    Kurse's power, then Glass would be like they had been originally, devotion and willing
    slaves to Jensen and the pack. Glass' mother would barely mean anything to her. Sasha
    remembered how she had thought of her own mother as simply a new sources for new
    wolves...

    Sasha had never seen one of the glass wolves asleep before, but she was honestly scared
    what would happen when Glass woke up... and none of this would change what had to be
    done. Technically, Glass' form wasn't locked because she had never been given the special
    turn, and neither would the children's as they passed through the mirrorverse... but Sasha
    wouldn't wish that kind of psychological trauma on -anyone!-

    All she wanted to do now was SLEEP, along with Mary and Ulysses... the sun was so sunny,
    who cared what the island had looked like half an hour ago?... But Sasha resisted the urge,
    she had to, Kurse had left this mess in their laps and they had to clean it up now.

    "Well we've gone from being male police in twenty four hours to lion people with four

    "If it didn't have to be from that beotch again... " Fox began.

    "Inaheartbeat!" Elise said, her muzzle lips quivering.

    Many science fiction writers had theorized that god had shattered itself into all souls in
    existence at the moment of realized... Fox wondered if this was such a hypothetical god
    felt, you being you, but at the same time, there were parts of you that were also you, and
    were a 'themself' that all their own as well.

    "You think that beotch knew we'd end up feeling this way?" The four souls of Moja / Mbili /

    only THEN does she realize this was his plan all along, and turning her back human had
    been the real punishment."

    "Happy the beotch is deader than dead?"

    Fox said with some humor, "Heck yeah, wanna throw a party?"

    Amethyst and the wolves who had been transformed into twins looked upon the island... it
    was so... plain. Like blank paper.

    Everyone was thinking the same thing and through no trick of magic.

    Caroline flashed back to the dance, to an outside observer it was pure and utter madness.
    Maybe it had been. But Caroline remembered, the pattern never repeated, the same path
    never taken twice, wasn't that in of itself a pattern? After all, perfect chaos meant there
    could be no pattern -at all-. But Caroline remembered, always being in two parts during
    the dance that had no beginning or ending, which simply was.

    "Sis... can't we can't some more?"

    Cobalt heard the voice of herself, the sound of herself as a little girl. Sounding innocent,
    sad, like she had watched her home burn down in front of her eyes.

    Amethyst finally stood up and uprooted a tree with her brute strength, and began to trace,
    not planning ahead or taking precise details, only doing what came naturally and letting it
    flow. She then threw the tree clear of the clearing and stood in the center, not stepping on
    any of the tracing lines.

    The gem wolves stepped into a circle around her, Betty, Lawrence, Robyn and Maine joined
    in too along with any others there. Then at an unspoken signal, they began to dance.
    Amethyst stayed in the center directing the movements ever so slight so they didn't bang
    into each other, Caroline carefully placed her paw on each 'tile' never stepping on the trace
    lines, or stepping in the same pattern.

    Infinite possibilities. Infinite paths to explore. All that could be taken, all that possible.
    And in her/her base gut, Caroline/Cobalt knew she/she should be in two place at the
    same time, not occupying the same space twice at once.

    So much she could do, she saw them in her mind's eye as she danced, she could build a
    hotel on the island with Opal... she could become a model and then the first of the pack to
    become an actress... she could become a dancer and eventually teach her talents on to
    others... she saw herself as an elderly gray gray wolf surrounded by olympic gold medals,
    then at middle aged holding a chain walking with a limp looking at oscars thinking if only
    not that for that one accident, she saw her son/daughter, named Cobalt/Coraline and --

    Caroline fell to her knees, the other wolves present soon after began to do much the same.
    Caroline realized she was sobbing, and she wasn't alone.

    'Was this... was this what she, they -really- wanted to bring to the world?' Caroline and
    Cobalt both thought.

    They saw a trio of young girls doing magic tricks to earn passage on a crowded ship, each
    of their parents having been taken by plagues their magic couldn't stop, to the 'New World'
    not having even won it's freedom from Great Britain yet... but their great-grandmother
    saying it would become 'the land of the free.'

    What they found was a nation almost as bound by laws and rules as the one they had
    risked their lives to travel from. They had grown older expanded on their craft, expanded
    their power, even learning a trick or two from the locals who were being crowded out by
    the new comers who didn't quite realize the people already here were people as well.

    Caroline and Cobalt saw a messiah complex bloom from selfless intention turning to blind
    ambition, they saw the 'us' and 'they' mentally form, 'they' being anyone still bound by
    rules (they loathed even the harsh rules magic had to follow).

    The twins saw their by now deluded goal of 'liberating' Glendale, tapping into the infinite
    chaos without a filter to direct or properly control it.

    Then the damage had started. Driving children insane believing they were 'liberating them'
    and encouraging the dissolving of every law on the books.

    No one expects the Inquisition, in particular in America. the Inquisition, born as an excuse
    by royalty to seize property (much as the attack on the Knight Templars had been
    centuries before), and historically known for it's ruthlessness... It also had the distinct
    honor of bringing 'innocent until proven guilty' into practical use. And officially considered
    witchcraft to be an absurdity ...

    Unofficially there were innocent people who needed protecting from those who were not
    so innocent. Five 'witch hunters' (though they never ever referred to themselves as such).

    The death of the witches, the curse, people realizing how powerful calling someone a
    witch was, spite turning towards land owners and the like, five voices of reason who knew
    what a witch actually did... drowned out.

    Cobalt and Caroline gasp.

    "... before revenge... before the chaos drove them insane... before they started seeing
    people with magic as mindless machines... before they saw themselves as better than
    everyone else... this was what they wanted... a world were all possibilities could be
    explored..."

    Cobalt/Caroline wanted to step away from each other then and there and continue the
    dance in their duet as they should... but they couldn't, they only had one body now...

    "Monster, witch, curse, thing of evil, it said it would make us want defeat... " Cobalt and
    Caroline whispered. Kurse had fulfilled her promise. Not by making the cost too high.

    'What is this? We were like that for what? A few hours? But... how long have been a pack
    member? A week? A few days?... We had a self enough to appreciate it for how long? A
    few minutes? I knew Opal for how long before I fell in love with them? ... What difference
    does time make at all? If love is for a moment or a century... '

    Kurse had no comprehension of love in of itself... sadly, this had proven untrue of her wolf
    twins...
