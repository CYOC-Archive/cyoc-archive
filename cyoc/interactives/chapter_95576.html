    You didn't feel like introducing yourself after being shrank down into a tiny kid so you just took an empty seat that was in a corner of the class. The teacher didn't seem to mind that much and merely said "Well class if you want to know our new student then just talk to her after class. In the mean time let's work on our multiplication tables. Now remember 2 times 2 is 4, 3 times 3 is 9, 4 times 4 is 16, etc." You drifted off, you had already mastered multiplication tables years ago and could do them in your sleep. You looked around and wondered what everyone else in this class was here for. She assumed some were 2nd or 3rd graders who were sent back or maybe a few kindergartners or pre-schoolers who were given a boost. The teacher had finished her lesson and given out some worksheets and once you finished them you would be able to go to recess. You took a look at the worksheet with it's 10 problems and just scribbled down some answers that you knew were right and then scribbled some numbers around in order to "show your work". After putting down your name and the date you rose out of your seat around the same time as the girl next to you did.

    The girl with long blond hair and twinkling blue eyes smiled and asked "These worksheets are a little too easy, right?"

    "Eh, they are probably good for this grade level..." you said kind of enthusiastically. You both turned in your worksheets and then you walked out of the question with the little blond hurrying after you after she grabbed her backpack.

    The girl held out her hand and said "I'm Clara, mind if I asked your name?"

    showing at least some courtesy you responded "I'm Jess... dont really feel like talking or anything right now... Was basically just shoved in a body against my will."

    Clara said "I think I know how you feel, I was recently put in this class too! I felt weird being put in a body so different, but it can be amazing!"

    Wondering if the girl was a little weird or not you asked "So what grade were you in before you came here?"

    She said "Well... I wasn't exactly in school before I cam here."

    "So were you home schooled or something?"

    "Not exactly... how to describe it?"

    "Well how old were you?"

    Clara stopped for a moment and said "Well... tomorrow i'll be... a month old I think..."

    Stunned you asked "You mean a month and 4 years or something?"

    Clara said "No a month... I was put here because I'm very intelligent or something. I was talking the day I was born I guess... my memories before I came here are a little fuzzy." You were silent for a bit just kinda wondering who would put a baby in this situation. "So you want to get some lunch or something? I brought my own but you dont even have a backpack with you."

    you asked "Where's the cafeteria i'll just buy my own..." you patted your school skirt's pockets and couldn't find your wallet. You continued to pat your pockets everywhere but couldn't find your wallet. You wondered where you wallet could've gone when the principal came along.

    The principal said "Why hello there Jess how are you enjoying your first day?"

    you said "I met a big baby and was really bored in class... I also lost my wallet."

    the principal chuckled at the big baby comment and said "that wasn't a very nice comment Jess... About the wallet, it isn't appropriate for a little girl to be carrying around so much money... So we sent your wallet back home with your mother. don't worry about money though, if you go to the cafeteria you can get a meal for free. Now carry along girls and behave."

    dumbfounded you said "well Clara let's just go to the cafeteria then..." you felt your stomach growl and when you got to the cafeteria (and being one of the first people to get there) you saw the selection of food was... predictable. Tater tots, hamburgers, pizza, and chicken. There was also a salad bar which looked like it hadn't been touched ever except for the bacon bits. You grabbed the chicken, put some lettuce on your tray, and then grabbed some low-fat milk. When you sat down to eat with Clara you saw that Clara had brought baby food with her. You asked "So why did you bring baby food with you?"

    Clara simply said "My parents still have it, and it doesn't taste that bad." she was chewing through 3 bottles of applesauce. You sighed thinking that it would be a long few school years.
