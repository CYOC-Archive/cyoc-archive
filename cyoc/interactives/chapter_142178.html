    Author note:
    Newfie Outport Fishernons Treasure Island Flints

    So Johnny leapt ( with his clothes on) into the ocean and began screaming for help as he realized he was drowning.

    No body seemed to be coming so after a few minutes Johnny Fishernon's head slipped beneath the waves of the cruel ocean,his heavy wet clothes dragging him down to his watery death.

    Now it just so happened that Johnny's eccentric Uncle Ezekial Flint and his cousin ( same age as him) Majory Flint were visiting the Fishernon family cottage on the Island outport ( I'm thinking Newfoundland here) to try to convince Mr and Mrs Fishernon to leave the Island and go partners with Captain E in a new smelt fishery and Processing Frozen Smelt Plant the Captain and his
    partners had invested in deep in the Mainland,somewheres along the North Shore of Lake Erie,the Great Lakes.

    Since Mr Fishernon wasn't much of a cod fisherperson but did understand mechanical things and was a bit
    of a inventive genius,the Offer was being seriously considered.

    So while the adults were discussing this Move from the Island Majory had gone out for a stroll on the beach with her pet parrot Long John Silver.

    Off in the distance the Captains two masted fishing schooner the Davey Jones bobbed peacefully in the outports harbour and the ships crew were spending some of their money in the Outports only resteraunt and telling the locals tales
    of bursting nets full of smelt and furry conventions on the other side of Lake Erie where weird American kids wished to be fox vixens and paid good money to make fur suits.

    The Head Witch of the Outports Witch Coven just happened to be the proprietor of that eatery and hence was greatly interested
    in these Mainland Furry Conventions where people paid good money to make fur suits.

    Since the Head Witch and her coven tf'd themselves all the time into various critters for free,as well as for friends,enemies and the odd Outport drunk
    Mother Gibbs was greatly interested in the tales the crew of the Davey Jones were telling.

    Meanwhile Majory Flint and her pet parrot Long John Silver had been heading in the general vicinity of where Johnny Fishernon had gone and drowned himself.

    So in between squawks of "Pieces of Eight" and "Dead Men Tell No Tales"
    both Majory and her very intelligent parrot were looking at the cast off fishing hamper her cousin Johnny had left on the beach before he'd gone and drowned himself a few minutes earlier.
    Majory of course knew the fishing hamper belonged to her cousin Johnny but where was he?
    She and Long John stared at the cruel waves and suddenly the parrot leapt into the air and began him aerial sweeps above the ocean below him.Suddenly he saw the body dimly at the ocean bottom and began circling over the area where drowned Johnny lay on the Ocean bottom
    constantly squawking
    "Dead Men Tell No Tales"

    Majory took the hint and leapt into the waves and swam along the surface till she reached the area where her pet parrot was circling above.She dived and pulled Johnny's body back to the surface where she began giving the drowning victim mouth to mouth whilst swimming them both back to the Beach where she continued breathing air into his lungs,giving him CPR and getting
    that ocean water out of him.

    Finally her cousin started sputtering nd coughing and breathing again
    and Majory glowered at him and screamed

    "Johnny Fishernon,YOU are and idiot!!"

    At which point Long John Silver the Parrot began squawking
    "Hit the Mutineers with a Belaying Pin,Hit the Mutineers with a Belaying Pin"

    and Majory thought that was pretty good advice coming from the bird so she punched Johnny with a good hard right.....
