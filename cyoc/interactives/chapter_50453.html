They worked together and stood up with some help. Matt beat Rex to the punch, "What happened to you guys?!"

    There in front of Matt and Rex, stood Jeff and John. Both of their heads sat beside the other on top of a single body. Jeff and john glanced at each other with halfhearted smiles before turning their attention back to their friends. "We went through the teleporter together."

    "And seeing how you're a living human subject," John took over explaining, "They opted for the same sort of test subject!"

    "Liability." John stated, "We work here, we get payed for this kind of stuff all the time." That didn't seem to help ease Matt or Rex, who continued to bombard them with questions.

    "Guy!" Jeff finally interrupted, "We did this to help you. Don't you get it?!" There was dead silence in the room followed that comment. Jeff glanced over at John and smiled, "If the only side effect is being stuck with this loser for a few days, then so be it!"

Matt and Rex got to their feet and couldn't help but smile. They exchanged high fives between all four of them and turned to leave.
