    "I personally prefer big strong girls," said Theseus, "The Meat Market isn't the same without you."

    "Well, it has been fun, but I don't really want to spend the rest of my life here," said Maddie. She flinched and said, "Oh, I'm not saying that I don't like this place, this just doesn't seem like a good place to have a long career."

    "I get it," said Theseus, "The reason you're going to college is for a respectable job that pays more. How much longer will that be now?"

    "Hard to say," said Maddie, "I mean, I still need to complete my courses and finding an agreeable job after that is not so easy. Especially with today's market."

    "I'm just glad to see you when you can come home," said Theseus.

    "Well, Valentine's Day is in the air," said Maddie before snuggling close to Theseus.
