    After much thinking, Gloria came up with a decision and decided to go to the Animal Wing. Sure the Age Wing can make you younger and older, but there wasn’t much fun in reliving your childhood. And the Transgender Wing, that would be a little disturbing. So she thought the Animal Wing would be a much better option. So she waited for the tram to the Animal Wing and entered it once it got there. The ride took five to ten minutes upon getting there and it was much soothing for the moment. Once the tram stopped, Gloria ext the cars and enter the main lobby of the Animal Wing. When she did that, she was completely surprised at what she saw.

    The lobby of the Animal Wing was really fantastic. It was a square room that had a lot of shrubs, trees, and vines on the walls, the pillars and in the middle was a fountain that has a variety of animal statues spitting out water from their mouths. Some are animals are alive even today, others were mythical beings that don’t even exist, and the rest were extinct for a long time ago. She looked around the lobby and discovered many different people in different animal forms. Some were complete animals with employees taking care of them, others were half-breeds, and the rest have parts of their body into different species. Upon inspecting all of the guests, Gloria knew that she was going to have a really good time in this place.

    She got her carryon luggage and approached the elevator to head up to the eleventh floor. After leaving the elevator, she walked down one of the long hallways to find her appropriate room. Finally, after a long walk, she approached the door marked 1152 and inserted the key into the slot. Once she opened the door and entered her new home, she looked around in the room so see what surprises in store for her. Sadly however, there wasn’t much.

    The room was just like any other hotel room, with a master bedroom, small kitchen, bathroom, closet for her clothes, a wide screen TV, the works. She sighed to herself, as she was a little disappointed at the room compared to the rest of the hotel. “I thought I was going to have a room so elegant.” Gloria said a little bummed, “Oh well, I guess this will do.” She approached the bed and placed her suitcase on the small hammock. As she was heading the bed, there was an envelope resting on her pillow with her name on it. Curious by this she picked up the envelope and opened it up to reveal a letter then reading it. It says…

    Dear Gloria,

    First of all, allow me to welcome you to the Transformation Resort. I hope this trip is worth it. Now I’m pretty aware that you have chosen the Animal Wing of the resort. To that end let me give you some rules and advice.

    As you have notice, the Animal Wing is where people transform themselves into animals of their choosing. However, there are ways you can change yourself into one. You can either turn a specific body part of yours into the animal of your choice. You can go do the hybrid approach, as in you can be half human and half animal. Or you can go all out and become a complete animal. Now if you decided in becoming a complete animal, our assistants will be called to come take care of you until your stay is over.

    There are a variety of animals you can choose from household pets, to jungle beasts, to mythical creatures, straight down to extinct dinosaurs. You can also transform yourself into an inanimate object, if someone else is with you at the time. If you are alone and are turned into an inanimate object, you would be taken to the lost and found section of the wing and someone will pick you up.

    The room you are in is also magical. If you turn yourself into an animal, the room and shift into that animal’s environment, just to make you comfortable. You can also go shop at our stores, go to the pool for some relaxation, enjoy a meal at our restaurants, or visit our entertainment venues. All those places can also change you into the animal you like.

    Once again, thank you for choosing the Transformation Resort and I hope you have yourself a safe and wonderful stay.

    From: The Chair of the Transformation Resort.

    Gloria read the letter fully and was quite impressed at what it says. From her standpoint, she can change herself in whatever animal she likes whether she has to pick a certain part of her body, a hybrid, or a complete animal. Not only that, but she can also go to a restaurant, shop, pool, or entertaining venue and get changed into the form she wants. She’s so pumped by this that she decided to start this experience now. What should she do first?
