    Snake pulled on his clothing and headed to the locker room. The football players had their own private weight room. All the walls were mirrored, so they could admire their bodies while they pumped iron. They had all the new equipment, while everyone else had to settle for the grungy old stuff that was in the regular weight room. Football Season was over, and he expected no one to be there. He hoped Ryan could see through the rubber to see where he was and what had become of him. This was the best way to rub it in. He got the locker room entrance. The door was locked, but it had electronic access keypad. Snake closed his eyes and concentrated. He could barely hear Ryan's thoughts through the rubber. They were like molasses in a jar. Stagnant. Snake concentrated on "locker room key code". Slowly the numbers formed in his mind. Good Ryan wasn't completely brain dead.

He stripped down to the jockstrap and hung his stuff in Ryan's open locker. Then he went to the private weight room, which could only be accessed through the private locker room, and keyed in Ryan's code. He flipped on the lights. The polished chrome gleamed in the LED light. The stuff was better than he had expected. He had only seen it through Ryan's eyes, and Ryan was jaded because he was used to this as his normal.     Best of all , no one seemed to notice that Ryan Standish was AWOL. Snake figured that Vinnie had whammied everyone to forget about Ryan or maybe it was a side effect of the rubberization.
