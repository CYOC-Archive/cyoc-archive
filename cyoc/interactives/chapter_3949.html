    The foam was lighting my body on fire, pains so intense I remember screaming till my voice could only make a whisper. As the feelings of what was attacking my body slowed the memories of how I got in this mess ran over and over in my mind.

    Thoughts of the Judge passing sentence on me for such a small infraction of any law. Now this torture and what yet to come. The memory of the two guards talking about a sentence of twenty to life, and for what?

    Then a voice,"William, Ahh number 711wp are you still with us in there, came the voice of a pretty woman?"

    Try at I might the voice would not work. I listened to hope for more from outside this plastic bubble from Hell.

    "Do you think he survived, or at least his mind might have survived what that fool judge ordered?"

    The sound on a man's voice, " Number 711wp is soon to find his old life and body gone! The Judge has some personal delights he wants to impose on Mister William Penny. Remember that the Judge when he first came here spent two years in Circe's House of Sensual Delights. As I remember he was kept as one of her special beasties' and she used him for her own pleasure almost weekly. I expect the Judge plans to have simular fun with number 711wp!"

    The woman asked, "will he have a sex change and a complete reformation process done too?"

    "Yup the foam purges the body's DNA and almost instantly replaces it with the new pattern. Then the plastic blister will be heat shrunk down to the total external body pattern. A minute or two in the Magnatomature and his complet form is permanantly reshaped. The mind, memories, and former life will still be there so that the Judge can inflict as much horror and terror as he always desires!"

    I was a willing person to serve my time even for a infraction of the law that never happened. It at first was easier to pay the fine and leave, but now I'm fodder for some ego ridden Judge. My life, all that I owned, friends, family, everything will be gone from me as I take up a life held here!

    The krinkle of the plastic blister is shifting the foam. I feel preasure as the foam and my body are one in the same. As the heat comes to be from warm to just plain hot a feeling of stiffening of my form is now a reality.

    The room I can see as the foam disappears, and then a black tunnel which the plastic covering me is moving down into. As the blackness blinds any views a pulling feeling as if my innards were being pulled around.

    Light, I see the light again and I feel so very alive. I can move my legs and arms! The sight of a pretty blonde haired woman smiling at me makes me feel all is going to be fine.

    "Easy 711wp easy, move slowly and let that new form stiffen, said the man next to her."

    "Why 711wp you came thru just fine, now just stand up and feel the weight of that pretty body on the new feet, said the woman."

    Strength, agility, and deisre to run like the wind!
