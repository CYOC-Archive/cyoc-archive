    "PROTECTOR! YOU ARE CALLED TO ACTION!"

    The voice was like a pickaxe driven into her skull - the sword's shrill tone caused Cassandra to wince, leaning forward and gripping her forehead as she let out a primal scream. "Motherfucker!" After all that exhausting pleasure, this was not at all what she needed. "Do you have to fucking yell?!"

    "Protector, I would be derelict in my duty to not ensure you were kept alert of all dangers!" The sword was already wriggling towards the redhead as she struggled to her feet, Elizabeth not far behind. All the demonic features faded, leaving behind a tired blonde trudging her way through collecting her lady's armor. "The forces of evil approach the holy fountain! You must take up arms, protector!"

    Ah. Right. The fighty bit of this whole... thing. Well, that was bound to come up sooner or later. She groaned, falling back to sit on the edge of the fountain. "Can they take a raincheck, or something? Kinda... tired here."

    "Evil does not take rainchecks, Protector! You must hurry, for battle will soon-"

    "Alright alright, fuckin' A!" She should've known - sex that great had to come with strings attached. This shrieking sentient blade, though, it was more like a goddamn rope. "Just shut up already."

    A bemused smile momentarily crossed Elizabeth's lips as she assisted Cassandra in donning the armor. "The last Protector endured similiar disagreements with her weapon, my lady," she explained, still somewhat groggy yet ever-attentive. "I ah... I do believe you'll need to go without the breastplate..."

    When the blonde held up the plate and its severed straps, Cassandra merely shrugged. "At least I'll be able to breathe." Her body was probably tough enough not to need it anyway. With one final grunt, she got back to her feet, grabbing up the sword as she marched back towards the exit. Elizabeth quickly followed, a hand upon her champion's bare back as the evening light streamed in through the columns and onto their faces.

    The demoness' expression grew tense as they spotted movement nearby, rustling in the thickest trees near the river. "I can sense them, my lady..." She took a step behind the amazon. "Wretched beings. The same that were trying to destroy my home when I made my way here."

    "Uh-huh." She didn't mean to sound so dismissive, but... she was tired, goddamnit. "So, what... a bunch of angry Frenchman, I'm guessing? Maybe some Celts?" It made the most sense, given her accent.

    Elizabeth, however, merely shook her head. "Nazis, my lady."

    "You what."

    The redhead's jaw hung open in amazement as she saw them stream in from the foliage. One, then two, then four, then dozens, shambling their way towards the temple. The tattered, dusty uniforms upon their bodies made their allegiance in life unmistakable - and the cacophony of hungry screams that now filled the air made it impossible for Cassandra to not correctly identify what these former soldiers and sailors of the Reich had now become.

    "NAZI FUCKING ZOMBIES?!"

    "My lady, I don't believe even they were so depraved as to lay with the undead willingly."

    A guttural scream emerged from Cassandra's throat as she charged. It was an effective war cry - but really, it was just an expression of frustration at how fucking weird this day was turning out to be. Shipwreck, amazon transformation, succubus sex, and now an army of undead nazis.

    "WHY IS MY LIFE SO FUCKING WEIRD?!"
