    Having reached Columbia Falls now, they navigated the streets to find Joseph’s house again, the Sun getting
    low in the sky as Jenny gave the directions while Clemens followed them, meaning that soon enough, they’d

    Getting out of the car, they’d just made it to the front of the garden when the door opened.

    “Clemens!” Joseph cried out as he approached them. “Great to see you again!” As they embraced each other, a
    friendly slap on each other’s backs.

    “You as well Jenny. Nice to see you again.” Joseph greeted her with a hug and kiss on her cheek. “Come on
    in.”

    Clemens quickly took the bags from the trunk before following his cousin inside, leaving them in the hallway
    for the time being.

    “Dinner’s almost ready now, so do you want to take care of your luggage now?”

    “Not right now.” Clemens answered cheerfully. “It can wait.”

    “I just need to use the bathroom a minute.” Jenny added in.

    “Upstairs, left, and second on the right.” Joseph told her where it was, as she left to head there.

    “Ssso.” Clemens asked Joseph while they were alone. “Jenny’sss been itching to sssee George and Molly for a
    while now. You know any good time to?”

    “Well.” Joseph took his time to think it through. “Molly’s on Maternity Leave right now, so she’ll be in
    their home tomorrow. So you could call her to see when George would be back and time the visit then.”

    “I think I’ll call and find out after dinner then.” Clemens decided.

    [+]

    A few minutes later and they were all seated, enjoying their meals. Though Clemens had a complete lack of
    vegetables, his digestive system only capable of handling meat these days, though at least he could still
    tolerate a select group of herbs and spices well enough to provide some flavour and variety.

    “So what’s happened here recently?” Jenny asked. “I mean besides George and Molly’s new kids?” Herself and
    Clemens not being as up to speed with developments in Columbia Falls.

    “Well another big piece was in November.” Joseph began. “Tabitha Choir managed to unseat Wayne Hireman to
    take over as School Superintendent for here.”

    “Yeah, I remember [i]him[/i].” Clemens remarked sourly. Despite only encountering him once, he had left a
    black mark with Clemens, so who knows what George and Molly must’ve gone through.

    “Still, that’s in the past now. The last of Beauregard’s influence removed.”

    “That’s good to hear.” Jenny noted.

    “So how are things in Jacksonville?” Joseph moved the conversation forwards.

    “They’re going well.” Clemens replied once he’d finished swallowing. “We might have another breeding pair
    there now.”

    “That’s good to hear. And you Jenny?”

    “Very steady right now. Nothing serious happening.”

    “Ah. That’s good news to hear, nothing bad happening.”

    The rest of dinner and its aftermath was taken up by this. Catching up on each other’s events, seeing how
    each other was doing these days. Until the big question arrived.

    “So when’s the day?”

    “Hm?” Clemens wondered what Joseph referred to.

    “Your marriage? And congratulations to the two of you as well!”

    “Oh! That’sss at the beginning of August!” Clemens answered, now that they were on the same page again.
    “You sure you can make it?”

    “I’ll try, but with Molly on Leave now, we’d be understaffed. I’ll do what I can, but no promises can be
    made.”

    “Well. As long as you try.” Jenny consoled him.

    “In any case, what are your honeymoon plans?” Joseph sought to move to a more cheerful topic.

    “Arranged an RV trek across the country. Travel the roads, see the sights, just Clemens and me.” Jenny
    replied, her smile widening at the thought of it.

    [+]

    It was now much later, and almost time to get some sleep now. Clemens and Jenny had just finished getting
    their stuff out, and Clemens had his shows and socks off, now pulling his sweater off, Jenny in simple
    nightwear beside him.

    “Ssso I asssked, and it looksss like we’ll be able to head over at about sixxx tomorrow.” Clemens let Jenny

    “Sssay. We have the RV hired, but where exxxactly should we head?” Clemens asked as his slid under the

    “Oh, I don’t know.” Jenny responded. “But we have time to work it all out. I’m sure we’ll think of
    somewhere to head.”

    “Yeah.” Clemens agreed. “And it’s something we can do while we’re here, when we’re not with Joseph, or
    George and Molly, or visiting the Glacier National Park.”

    “We won’t have a lot off time for that then.” Jenny stated the obvious.

    “I know, but it’s something to think about.” Clemens answered with a loving kiss. “Night then Jenny.”

    “Night Clemens.”
