    Sitting back in the stall, you twiddled your fingers as you wait for the crowd to clear. Naturally, you got bored, and you let your mind wander about the events during the day. Is this 'power' a direct cause of Senior Takeover? Why was he given this power before the actual Senior Takeover? Are there others like him?

    There were many questions left to be answered, but you decided that you should first understand the principles of your newfound power first. After gaining some insight while you were in Eliza, you had a better understanding, but you still don't know the basics, mainly when to initiate and stop it, how to specifically target someone, and what the limits were. Your head spun as the numerous options present themselves in front of you. You imagined that it will take you many tries to fully understand the mechanics. Since this semester was just beginning, classes were not difficult yet, so you luckily had ample time to experiment. Your parents both work late into the night, so you don't mind staying late at school today, if that means trying your power once more.

    You first try to focus on choosing your target host. Before, you remembered that you let your mind wander as you most likely chose your target subconsciously. You also realized that you were in different hosts on each of your previous attempts, making you wonder about if you can possess previous hosts.

    First focusing on your dad, you reminded yourself on dad's bulky features, as well as his hairy arms and long member. You had always seen your dad as a role model, despite numerous differences between you two. Your dad worked as a software engineer, your dream career, and even though he worked long hours, he still saved quality time for you and your mom. Apparently, he had also shown his capability as an adult responsible for his family, such as his handiness around the house or planning financial backups (although your mom also helped with the latter since she was more familiar in business and finances). As your mom would say to you from time to time, those were your dad's signature qualities of being an adult. However, you were legally an adult just by turning 18, so does replicating those same actions make you an adult? Some would take years to complete, and you had hoped that your maturing body alone would put you into this higher status. You guess that this power could allow you to experience non-physical qualities sooner. As embarrassed as you were, you had to admit that you had been focusing too much on the sensual qualities, and that you should try adopting more responsibilities, even being someone other than your dad, as long as time permits.

    Pondering this much, you were surprised you did not feel the same haziness you were expecting or that you should be in dad's office or something. You quickly assumed that perhaps distance was a limitation, as your dad worked far away from the school. (Now that you thought about it, that might also be a reason why you stayed in your dad's body for only several minutes.) Disappointed that your top choice was gone, you shifted your focus to your other two previous hosts. Coach Lucas would be the most appealing choice after your dad, sharing the same hulky appearance while having the same tender demeanor. You don't know much else about Coach Lucas, but owning those qualities should be a good indicator, right? On the other hand, Eliza's stature reminded you of yourself, slim, smooth, and not as imposing, which made you feel slightly better about yourself. Of course, she was a woman, and a young one at that, but students respected her all the same during the periods. She also acted mature and in control, features that certainly were not gender exclusive. Also, you still felt bad for leaving her in that state, potentially tarnishing her mature outlook.

    Stopping yourself from wandering too far off, you took a deep breath before your next move.

    What do you do now?
