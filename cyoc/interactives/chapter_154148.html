    Before she could look over herself, Officer Charley overheard the sound of an alarm. Making her way toward the source, she came upon broken glass on the sidewalk outside of a convenient store. Looking in, she saw a male, middle-aged clerk with a bloody rag to his head. Before she could say a word, the man called out, saying, “It was two of them, officer. Those hooligans snuck up on me and took the whole damn register!”

    “Did you see which way they headed?” asked Charley.

    “By the time I came to, the door was smashed through, and they were gone.”

    Looking around, Charley called out, “Did anyone see where they went?”

    “I did,” said a voice from behind. An elderly woman carrying a small dog in her arms stepped forward. “I saw them run into that alleyway. And they were big, too.”

    “Thank you,” said Charley. Reaching for her radio, Officer Charley called in for an ambulance and then notified dispatch about the robbery. Feeling uneasy once more, sweat began to form across Charley’s brow. The old woman walked up to Charley, asking, “Are you going to be okay? You look ill, officer.”

    “I’ll be fine. Thank you for asking,” replied Charley. Wiping the sweat away, she set out to chase after the suspects. Pushing past the crowded sidewalk, she made her way to the alley where the thieves are said to have gone. She saw the register on the ground, its tray broken open. Pulling out her side arm, Charley carefully tread down the alley, watching out for anyone. Looking above, there were no signs of people climbing the fire escapes. The lone garbage dumpster was her only guess as to where the suspects were. Slowly, Charley walked around the container only to find no one on the other side.

    As she approached the closed lid of the dumpster, Charley’s radio sounded off, prompting her to report in. During that moment, the lid flew open and two large hands grabbed her. Charley’s gun fired off, but she missed her intended target while being lifted into the air. Looking up, she was stunned to discover how big her intended quarry turned out to be. A young blonde woman’s huge face sneered down at her, what remained of her clothing torn away or hung on to her enlarged body. “Looks like we have ourselves a new toy, Suzie.”

    Glancing down, Charley watched as another young woman with short dark hair, slowly stretched out from the dumpster. Standing to her full height, she was not quite as large as her companion, but the did not make her any less intimidating to Officer Charley. “Don’t mess around for too long, Becky. We don’t know how long this can last or when the rest of the cops will show.”

    “I know, I know,” said Becky. “Let’s see how far I can toss a pig, then.”

    Charley knew she had to do something but was ultimately helpless in the grip of this giant thug. That is when she felt it; the strange feeling from earlier, only stronger. Much, much stronger as it poured out from her belly. With newfound strength, Charley slowly pried away the hands that held her, resulting is surprise from Becky. “What the fuck—”

    With a rush of adrenaline, Charley successfully pushed apart the hands and she dropped five feet to the ground. As she landed on her feet, the seams of her shoes blew apart. Tears began to form up her pants and shirt, and her vest and belt were losing a battle against Charley’s now growing form. As quickly as this sudden transformation began, the officer’s size increased in spurts, her uniform tearing away and falling off her body in the process. Within seconds, Charley reached the same height as Becky before shooting past her. Looking down, Charley guessed that she has five more feet over the tallest suspect. Ignoring the fact that she had become a giant, naked woman, Charley smirked and said, “My turn.”

    Already being in better physical shape, Officer Charley effortlessly subdued Becky, placing her into a hold and forcing her to the ground. Applying the right amount of pressure, she managed to render the young woman unconscious before letting go. Once finished, she looked down at the smaller Suzie with a paralyzing glare. Whimpering, the dark-haired girl said, “L-look, she didn’t mean to hit the old man so hard. We found out we was bigger than before and took a chance!” Slowly stepping out of the dumpster, she continued, “The money’s all there, ma’am. Not like we had any pockets left to shove ‘em into.”

    “Do you know what caused you to get bigger?” asked Charley.

    “N-no ma’am,” said Suzie. “I was kinda hoping you could tell me.”

    Before Charley said anything else, the sound of a police siren echoed from the street. Remembering her modesty, she crouched down to best hide her nude form. Looking around, she found while even sitting, her eye-line was higher than the lowest balcony of the nearby fire escape, which was sixteen feet above the ground. Charley’s attention lowered to the small men slowly approaching the scene, weapons drawn and looks of astonishment on their faces. She recognized them as Officers Daniel Bridges and Marcus Gonzales, both of whom are from the same precinct as her.

    “I think you’ll need some bigger cuffs, Danny,” Charley said, blushing while putting on a smile to hide her embarrassment.

    “I wish I can say this is the weirdest thing I’ve see today, but it’s not,” said Marcus, holstering his side arm.

    “Don’t tell me there’s more,” said Charley.

    “I wish I could,” replied Danny. “At least a dozen other folks in the same situation as you, though not quite as big.”

    Marcus looked up in awe before saying, “You didn’t happen to have the lunch special from the diner?”

    “Not only that, but I had seconds…and another plate,” Charlie said, a defeated tone in her voice. As if this could not be any more embarrassing for her. “But it was just too good, Marc. I couldn’t stop myself!”
