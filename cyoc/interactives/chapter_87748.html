    After the family finished unpacking they decided to check out the nature trail. They got a map from the front desk and began their hike. However, what seemed to be a simple hike in the woods turned into .. well, nobody knew how long. "John, I swear we've passed that tree a thousand times!" Sharon said. "No, I'm sure we're on the right path." John protested back. Julie and Derrick just rolled their eyes. Suddenly there was movement in the bushes. Picking up a nearby stick, Derrick approached the bushes. He raised the stick, ready to strike.... When a girl popped out. She appeared to be around Derrick's age, with brown hair, tanned skin, and green eyes. she wore an all green outfit of a T-shirt, shorts, and sneakers.Caught off guard, Derrick shrieked and stumbled backwards. "Hello. are you lost?" the girl asked. "Um... yes we are. Can you help us?" Julie said, smirking a bit at her brother's display of "bravery" "Sure!" the girl said "My name's June, by the way." "Are you a guide?" John asked. "In a manner of speaking." June replied. The family plus "guide" continued onward until they reached a clearing. "Um, no offense." Julie said, "but I think we're still going the wrong way. June shook her head and smiled. "You'll see."
