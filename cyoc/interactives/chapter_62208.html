    Jessica began thinking of all the possible things to turn one of them into.

"Wait, I'm just thinking" replied Jessica. A couple seconds later, Jessica begins growing hungry, which starts distracting her. "Ugh, I'm hungry, can you go get me something for lunch while I think?"     Jessica began scrolling through the options, but found it hard to concentrate; all she could think about was her growing hunger. She was just about to give up and go see how Nikki was doing when a particular option caught her eye: Food Transformation. Jessica realized that maybe this could be the solution to both her problems; Nikki wouldn't have to make her a lunch, she could be her lunch! Her hunger momentarily forgotten, Jessica opened the menu and began studying the options. There were quite a lot of them, but Jessica was searching for one specific one... ah, there it was: 'Auto-revert subject when timer expires, even if consumed'. Jessica made double-sure that that option was checked; she only wanted to have a little fun with Nikki, not permanently digest her or anything.

    Satisfied that Nikki would be safe, Jessica began selecting the rest of the options. Now, what was she hungry for...
