    There was a flashing light on the message recorder for the phone. Cat-3 listening to the message from Dr Stevens.
    "Good morning I think it would be over there. Anyway Dr Musa and I had made a interesting discovery that you should see. If you can get here quickly everything will be explained."
    The other Catalinas jolted awake at Cat-3 yell of glee as she quickly explained the message from Dr Stevens.
    Within an hour they were in their private jet on the way then a few hours later arriving at Nutrition-U.
    The Catalinas arrived in their limo and entered the building. They walked to Dr. Stevens's office.
    "Hello Catalina I am glad you could make it. We have toiled away since the incident and recently discovered how to tell the three of you apart. Well atleast the originalfrom the clones. Basically everyone has Carbon-14. Now when the clones were created,it reset their C-14 so they have less than the original Catalina."Dr Stevens said happily.

    "So how can you tell us apart?" asked Cat-1
    "Well we need a hair sample from each of you and we'll analyze it and we'll see who has more C-14." said Dr. Stevens.
    Cat-3 went first as she was the most eager. Dr. Stevens plucked a hair from her head. The scientist then placed the hair on a slide and analyzed it.
    They then repeated the procedure for the other 2, the Catalinas were anxious to hear the results.
    "I have the results here. Catalina #1 has the most carbon-14 out of all three. So she is the original Catalina. Catalina #2 and #3 have the least amount of C-14." said Dr. Stevens
    Cat-2 and Cat-3 were in shock. They were Catalina's best friends Emily and Sasha.
    "Oh my god, what the hell?! What will happen now?!" said Cat-2
