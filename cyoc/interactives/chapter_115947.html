    Animalia Ambassadoria: News and Previews

    "Hello, and welcome to a new episode of Animalia Ambassadoria." Said Abby, starting the newest episode.

    "Today we announce some new developments in Animalia, here and overseas, and we also answer your questions." Said Sarah, getting the laptop ready.

    "But before we do, let's all say hello to my new boyfriend, and newest member on the couch, Roland Luwrie." Said Sophie, introducing Roland for the first time.

    ---

    "First of all," said Malice, reading off the prepared list for today, "Animalia School of Modeling is getting several offers from European fashion dynasties, such as the Italian Fendi family, to model new clothing in what can be called Animalian style."

    "Apparently, having several decorative holes in clothing is evolving into its own fashion style." Said Jessica, "Also, several new students at the School are being approached by several manufacturers to do advertisements for their products." Said Abby, reading the announcements from the school.

    "I'm just glad that Maggie is getting some help from some of her old 'rivals' from her modeling days." Said Simon, "To go from just four students to forty in only two weeks must be overwhelming for just one person, even if they are experts at this."

    ---

    "Another thing we're going to talk about is how Animalia is planning to create a new "super school" for Animalians." Said Jessica, going down the list after a 10 minute question block.

    "The reason for this is because 'standardized' school buildings are actually somewhat cramped and crowded for certain Animalians, especially the megafauna." Said Abby.

    "It stopped being funny after Dean got his shoulders stuck in the doorway to his classes for the third time in a row." Said Sarah, "But it certainly wasn't funny when you bumped your head on the top of the doorframe."

    "I heard that ceilings at the 'super school' will be placed five feet higher than they currently are to help the Giraffe Animalians feel more comfortable without having to hunch over so much." Said Malice.

    ---

    "For Mieni Atoll developments," said Sarah, finishing another question block, "we've been told that there are plans to include a health spa at Animalia Reserve and Resort, because apparently an open air weight training area/muscle beach zone is turning out to be quite a popular idea on the website."

    "I hear that they will be using a series of buoys with fine netting to cordon off the area that would be safe for swimming without fear of being stung by jellyfish, the box jellyfish in particular." Said Jessica.

    "It also says that they created a low impact jungle trail that loops around the island for marathon runners, as well as a perfect place for beach sports, such as beach volleyball." Said Jessica, reading the announcements from Mieni.

    "I heard that they've found some test subjects for the Animalia virus. The ones they are doing first are: a moray eel, a sea snake, and a sea turtle. The only thing they've got so far is that all three of them are female, but they haven't been exposed to the virus yet." Read Roland, obviously nervous about being on the show for the first time.

    "Any new developments will be reported here on Animalia Ambassadoria, we promise." Said Sophie.

    ---

    "And that's all the time we have for today. See you all next time in the wilds of Animalia." Said Abby, closing the show after the final question block.
