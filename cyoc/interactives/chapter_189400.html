    origin: https://www.deviantart.com/robmartinson/art/They-can-t-see-you-here-891109312

    Greg looks up at his girlfriend confused and scared. Everything around turns almost black and white... No, it's his vision changing. "Oh, you came out so cute" Alice comments kneeling in front of him. He tilts his head in confusion.

    "Alice, everything okay? I've heard some..thing, can I come in?" her mom asks from behind the door. "Yeah, I'm fine... wait a moment..." tries to buy some time, hiding the rest of the mess they left. "Okay" finally answers when she was sure there were nothing suspicious now... except the dog.

    She knew she couldn't hold mother outside, it would just raise the suspicion. Playing open cards was the best strategy. "I thought I've heard a... what's this!?" asks surprised seeing a confused dog standing next to Alice.

    "Uh.. this is uh. Gr.. Goldy" she lies about the dog name, after his breed: Golden Retriever. "Hannah called me, she was... asked me to take care of him for few days, she's... sick and... can't, you know..." tries to convince her mother.

    "Oh.. okay... I hope he won't make a mess" mother comments. "N-no! He's well trained, look!" says excitedly, then stands in front of him. "Sit". Greg was terrified about it all, but... he had to play along. He sits, in quite a weird way. "Stand". He rolls his eyes, was he in the circus? Reluctantly he stands up, panting. "Sit! Give me a paw!" she orders. He again sits, then raises his paw, seeing a canine paw instead of a human paw was really surreal feeling for him.

    "Oh, isn't he a bit too excited?" her mom asks noticing his red rocket. Alice turns red and puts the paw back on the ground. "Uh you know... new place.. Maybe it's one of your perfumes?" tries to blame her. "Maybe... Now you better go sleep, you have to wake up early".

    He never felt more humiliated in his life, having to do silly commands, standing naked in front of his girlfriend and her mother, not knowing what's going on. He was a dog! He looks up at Alice and whines, wanting to know what's going on there. He didn't know how it happened, well, it was the collar but, how? And how long he will be like that? He was changed without any explanation and preparation!

    Seeing her mother being far enough, she closes the door and sits next to Greg. "Oh, boys... You couldn't... control it? Ugh, never mind. But now, you can stay the whole night!" she says excitedly. He looks at her and whines. "Don't worry, I'll try to sneak you out and change back tomorrow in the evening". She again starts scratching him behind his ear... Damn, it felt good!
