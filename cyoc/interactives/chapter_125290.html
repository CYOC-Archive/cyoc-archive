    It was Mr. Milhaud’s usually boring English class… The last class period of the day. Everybody was tired and all the students were just staring at the big, white clock on the wall, counting down the minutes to the final ring so they could go home.


    And, Mr. Milhaud was going on and on about poetry readings and about literature.


    “Darn it,” Ty thought. “How could I have some fun in this last class and use my powers right now, just to add some spice to this boring lecture?”


    Then, Mr. Milhaud said something interesting, noting that half the students in the class seemed to be bored out of their mind.


    “Alright, everyone. If anyone can come up here and give me a really great poetry reading, I will let you go early!”


    Ty perked up! Go early! Whoa! He didn’t need to say THAT twice. He had to think fast. So, Ty looked around the class and looked for someone he had been wanting to change for a long time, like, ever since he got his cool powers, which was just a few days ago. Then, his eyes fell on Jason, the cool, short guy with the short, black hair and the big brown eyes and the cute face. Ty had a bit of a crush on Jason, who was only 5-foot-5-inches tall but 15 years old in the High School freshman class. Jason was often teased for being short and skinny, so Ty could bulk him up right there in class!


    Ty immediately wished-for Jason’s muscle growth to be spelled out on a sheet of paper in his hands. And it happened. Ty was holding a sheet of letter paper with all the steps for growing Jason’s muscles. Then, he immediately raised his hand. He was the only person to volunteer.


    “Tyler? Really? Alright! Come to the front!” Mr. Milhaud said, as he sat down in his chair and made room for Ty to make his way to the front of the classroom.


    Ty came up and glanced over the classroom. Jason was sitting right in front of him, in the third row.


    “I want to talk about my friend,
    He is in this class.
    He is now short and sweet,
    But that will come to pass. “

    “I wish for him to grow,
    Bigger and taller he’ll be,
    for muscles and hair will grow,
    for everyone to see!”


    “I wish for pecs and abs,
    Bigger legs and muscles,
    Thick arms and big arms,
    So, nobody will give him hustles.”


    “I want him to be bigger and taller,
    Powerful, faster and stronger,
    With mighty legs, arms and shoulders,
    And legs that grow a foot longer!”


    “So, who is the young man,
    Is he Joe, Paul or Mason?
    None of them he shall be,
    But my best friend named Jason!”


    Just then, Ty bowed before the class as everyone clapped out loud when Jason started to groan loudly.


    “UHHHHHH!!!” he moaned as his arms swelled up with muscle. His biceps and triceps inflated like bowling balls and his forearms became as big as bowling pins covered with veins.


    Next, his pectorals increased to the size of large medicine balls, inflating to the point where he burst out of his t-shirt due to the enlarging muscle. His monstrous pecs were enormous!


    His stomach was covered with well-defined abdominal muscles which were like thick blocks of rock-hard cobblestones. They formed a layer of armor so thick, that it was a wall of muscle like a steel-plated armor.


    Finally, Jason’s quads grew so big, thick and wide that they burst out of his blue jeans! His quads were wider than his waist by now, due to the enormous volume of muscle he had gained. Ty quickly transformed his torn jeans into the super-stretchy Lycra shorts, so that he was not sitting naked in the classroom. His calves grew to the size of vein-covered soccer balls while his legs grew a foot longer, making the boy a whole 6-foot-6 inches tall, like a basketball player with nothing but muscle on his frame!


    “Alright! Thank you, Tyler, for your poem! Thanks to him, the class is dismissed!” Mr. Milhaud uttered.


    All the students quickly grabbed their bags and packed their books before leaving the classroom. Ty and Jason were the last ones to leave, while Dustin was waiting in the hallway.


    “Whoa…. Something seems…. Different….” Jason wondered as he looked around the classroom.


    “Yeah…. You’re a bit bigger and stronger, big guy!” Ty smiled as he walked up to him.


    “Huh? What?” Jason said, looking around the room. Of course, he didn’t SEE Ty, who only came up to his chest.


    “Down HERE, big guy!” Ty giggled. The poor kid wasn’t used to being so tall yet, that he had to look DOWN at his classmates when addressing them.


    “Oh! There you are! Gosh, Ty! You shrunk!” Jason smiled.


    “No, Jason! You GREW!” Ty grinned.


    Then, Ty and Jason walked out of the classroom together while Dustin met up with them in the hallway. There, they discussed what they would do after class. Ty wondered about inviting Jason over to his house afterwards….
