    Starting an Epidemic

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Maxxatom A year ago O

    Copious amounts of saliva and sexual fluids are spread throughout the town as the mutations ramp up. Soon everyone from police officer to office worker is catching a sudden shower of fluids and transforming. Most of the adults have already been transformed at this point, leaving only those inside of schools completely unscathed…

    Rudy stayed staring out of the window, a common habit of his as his class was in a slight panic as they had initiated a lockdown to keep everyone safe. One of the kid’s parents had decided to walk onto campus to pick up their child, but was unaware that their form could attract attention and transform others, so they kept some of the school locked down while others were escorted out as safely as possible. Rudy wonders of his mom and siblings were ok, he wanted to be anywhere but school right now. He was a good boy though, and his mom wanted him to stay that way, so he elected to listen to his teachers, making sure he wouldn’t give his mother something to worry about.

    Kurt and Allison were in the same school, Kurt being a freshman and Allison being a junior. Kurt did his best to stay as far away from his sister as possible, keeping up his edgy, troublemaker persona. His friends were a bunch of goths and delinquents, neither his siblings or his mother approved of. He was planning to skip school and head to a hangout spot that he and his friends would usually go to, but the school had enforced an odd lockdown and quarantine. He was going to attempt to use some black magic that he had picked up from his friends to try and escape, using surprisingly accurately drawn symbols and charms on white paper to do so, his classmates keeping their distance from him for various reasons.

    Allison was trying to get a hold of everyone as she was stuck in the locker rooms. The signal on her phone was absolutely terrible and her worries were rising. Apparently a massive, three-headed dog had broken in to campus and started to turn others. She was outside doing gym when the announcement was made, and it had been a good hour since then. Despite her fears and worries, she tried her best to keep a level head, used to being the responsible one out of all her family members.

    Right now, there are three transformed people at the front of Kurt and Allison’s school, and two wandering the halls of Rudy’s school. Everyone is about equal distance from them, but all the kids know they can’t stay there unless they want to be transformed.

    Should they wait for an announcement or try to sneak out?
