Yuriko had always been a bit embarrassed by the tanukis lack of modesty, useing their own genitals to forge items, however in this case she could not be more thankful. A phalanx of tanuki shield bearers now stood in front of Kiko, completely blocking the kitsune from fire.

    "Dont worry I brought backup with me,"

    Kari and Kiko now gave Kenta a bemused look as the tanuki pointed upwards. The kitsunes eyes going wide as close to a dozen dragons now swooped from the skies, their dragon fire incinerating the visible Normal. Kari immediately recognized the lead dragons as they landed. "Tricia! Matt!"

    Tricia, the Dragon Queen, and her husband Matt smiled, baring their huge teeth to the nine tailed kitsune. Tricia now began to shift into human form. The monstrous purple dragon shrinking down into human proportions while her scales became flesh and wings vanished. "Couldn't lose another friend Kari," she said.

    "As touching as this reunion is," Matt stated. "We still have work to do." Kari and Tricia nodded in agreement. Gazing out they could see that while the Dragons and Tanuki had turned the tide for the moment, the Normal were prepared.

    Yuriko and Hannah watched as the Normal retreated, regrouping, preparing a counter strike. Yuriko bit her lip nervously, she already had a bad feeling.

    Unfortunately it appeared the elderly wizards instincts were accurate as she watched a sniper fire at one of the dragons. Shocked, she watched as the blast tore through the chest of a young blue dragon, its death cries echoing through the city as it crashed to the ground.

    Yuriko roared in anger, she had seen too much death already. "Kitsunes, Dragons," she growled. "They've been family to me for as long as I can remember! I have had enough of this!"

    With a primal scream, the elderly wizard screamed reaching out with her psionic abilities. Hannah watched, unnerved as the wizards abilities crushed pavement, buildings, and bodies. Wincing she watched as Yuriko crushed soldiers useing her mind before tossing their mangled corpses away. "Hannah," she bellowed.

    Hannah shook herself out of her stupor, fearfully gazing at the enraged wizard. "Take out the sniper in that tower! I'll clear a path."

    Hannah nodded, the gynoid teen readying herself as Yuriko now systematically begin crushing soldiers with her mind. Hannah knew that the wizard was powerful, she just didn't know she could be so ruthless. Thinking back to all the times her and Steph had questioned her Hannah could appreciate the womans patience. For now though she had a job to do.

    Hannah was amazed at not only her speed but her agility. Climbing over walls, up buildings, and leaping between various objects, Hannah quickly made her way to the snipers position. Spitting the sniper she now lept at him, useing her foot to grab his face before slamming him to the ground and knocking him unconscious.

    Yuriko smirked, Hannah certainly knew how to take care of herself. With the sniper safely neutralized, Yuriko now went about providing support. Useing her powers to disarm and even freeze the bullets in midair, she turned towards Kari, Tricia, and Kiko. "Anytime now!"

    "Everybody get back," Kiko yelled. Thw others now withdrew behind Kiko as the seven tailed kitsune drew a series of symbols in the air. The ground now cracking open in front if them.

    "Kiko what is that," Kari asked.

    "Lets just say some old friends of our want to say hi to the Normal," Kiko smiled.

    The Normal paused as the chasm opened in front of them. Why would the kitsune open up a hole in the ground? That question was answered as an unearthly growl emanated from the hole. Sloqly but surely the Normal could see creatures pull themselves from the earth. Creatures the Normal though they had exterminated decades ago.

    "Werewolves!"

    Panic and fear spread like wildfire as the Normal tried to counter the undead lycans barreling down on their postion. The corpses of the Lycans, wiped out by the Normal, now found themselves seeking revenge. Their ghastly howls the only thing heard as they tore through the ranks of the Normal.

    "How," Kari asked.

    "Secret," Kiko smirked. "But they have to work fast. I can only keep them on this plane of existence for so long."
