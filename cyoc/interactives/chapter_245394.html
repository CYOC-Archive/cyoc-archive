    “You know, I’m not too sure…” she mused. “Let me try something.”

    Frowning to herself in concentration, Lisa tried to direct the fish around. It took a moment, but then the various fish started gathering into a simple circle pattern within her aquarium-torso. Gasping, she looked up at Ty.

    “I can kinda feel them! This is so cool!”

    “Wow, amazing!” Ty exclaimed, bending down to look through Lisa’s transparent skin at the fish she was commanding in increasingly complicated maneuvers. “I guess the mass had to come from somewhere and your body was the logical choice. Are you feeling alright?”

    “Absolutely!” Lisa smiled and nodded. “It kinda tickles, but it’s really fun and soothing!”

    “Glad to hear it!” Ty grinned back, standing up and embracing her again.

    “Although…” Lisa murmured slowly. “It does give me an idea…”

    “Oh?”

    "We've done all these changes, but we haven't done any animal-based transformations aside from my mouse episode," she mused. "I'm sure you've seen some of the furry art floating around the internet, right?"

    "Oh, yeah!" He nodded. "I've seen them, it's pretty interesting. Even came across some of the more, ah... sensitive pieces. I guess I could see the appeal, certainly not any weirder than most of the stuff we do on the regular!"

    Lisa chuckled along with him. "Yeah, you got that right. Well, anyways, I'm not sure I want to go right for the whole 'anthro' thing for the first attempt. But, I thought we could have some sexy fun along the same vein."

    "Why do I get the feeling this is going to get out of hand again…?” Ty groaned. Still, he couldn’t but admit to himself that the idea had a certain appeal, even if just for the novelty.

    “I had the same thought, but as long as we do it right, then it won’t be an issue. And anyways, I wasn’t thinking of becoming full animals right away. Just… parts,” Lisa explained.

    “Such as?” Ty questioned, still concerned.

    "Well," Lisa started, "Why don't we just add the tails and/or ears of our chosen species, along with the genitals? That should be pretty safe, right? No mental changes to speak of."

    "Oh, This is going to be so cute!" Lisa squealed, jumping a little and clapping her hands. "I love it! Yes, let's do it!"
