    “Well then, Jake,” Kyle said aloud on the other end of the phone, despite the fact that Jake wasn’t listening anymore. “The world I have decided changes regularly with a random order of a list of different worlds I have written down. Nobody except you and I will notice. But even when changes happen to you, Jake, you won’t notice at first. At the end of each month, the world will change drastically. But, rather than happen instantly, the changes will happen gradually, again unnoticed by everyone. The new world that comes in is going to creep in around you, Jake. And it is entirely independent of any of your actions, so you can’t stop it no matter how hard you may try. It will take about a week for the world to completely change. After two weeks in that world, the world will likewise gradually change back to what the world is currently right now over the course of that fourth week. Then the process starts all over again with a completely different kind of world. Are you with me so far?” Kyle had briefly forgotten that Jake had hung up.

    “Oh right, well, then in case the curse needs additional clarification I guess. For example, say that the first world is one where everybody is naked. As you go about your days, people will start to casually and nonchalantly take off some if not all of their clothing around you. Their clothing will gradually fade away from existence. After everybody in the world is naked for two weeks, people’s clothes will start returning. Once everyone is back to wearing clothes, the next world will follow a similar course. Suppose that world is one in which guys have massive dicks and girls have massive boobs. Their bodies would initially rip out of their clothing as their bodies changed. But then their clothing would appear on the ground next to them, repaired and changed so they can be clothed again. To reiterate, throughout all of this nobody, except the two of us, Jake Smith, would notice anything changing. But you won’t completely notice everything, especially when changes happen to you.”

    Kyle took a deep breath with a large smile on his face and added, “And that was all just your Transform. For your Dare, you will not be able to use the curse on me without my permission. The only way you can use the town curse is to make someone aware of the changes to reality, but neither you nor they can stop the changes to the world. Also, as we experience more worlds, you will begin to enjoy them more and more. One final thing, you will forget that it was me who cursed you until I say otherwise.” Kyle took another deep breath. “Time to get for the fun to start. Which world will be first on my list?"
