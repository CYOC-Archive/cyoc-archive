    Jenny, a 8 year old girl, had a very happy birthday. She had a little party that was a lot of fun with her friends, a boy she had a crush on noticed her and said they may be able to hang out later, and she was given her very first smart phone. After her bath she jumped onto her bed and took a look at her new phone and decided to set up a few different things. She set up her e-mail and certain internet accounts on the phone and then went to check her e-mail. Standard things from her friends however there was one mail that was kind of interesting.
    She read the text that said "Hello Jenny, I am a developer of an interesting game that should make your life a lot more interesting. I'll send you a link to the game in your phone's app store and you can download it there. Hope you have fun. From, The Developer."
    A little curious she downloaded the app really quickly and Jenny was impressed as she imagined that the phone wouldn't have downloaded the app instantly. She clicked the app to start it and after the app loaded up there was a little finger print icon and the words "put your finger here." She tapped the finger print icon and the app opened up with the title bursting out with confetti and trumpets "Life Adventure!" Kind of unimpressed Jenny continued to see what the game did.
    A little orb of light with wings started floating onto the screen and then said "Oh... Hello there Jenny! I'm Tutu! The guide fairy! Shall I show you how to interact with Life Adventure?"
    Jenny looking said to herself "Seriously? where are the Yes and No buttons?"
    Tutu chuckled and then said "There are none for most of these choices. Your gonna have to tell me whether or not you'll want some help."
    Jenny assumed that the game heard her through the phone part of the phone and said "Yes, tell me how to play the game."
    Tutu said "Life Adventure is one of the most amazing rpg's considering it takes place in your real life. Our game generates special missions for you to do and rewards you for completing these missions. Such rewards being things like money, magic items, or even special expansions for this game. You can even be given quests by other people who use this app. here's an interesting quest for you right now." the fairly snapped her... assumed fingers and a window opened saying "Quest: Understanding the Game, Rewards: 1 tf token and the ability body adaptation, description: tell us that you understood the tutorial"
    Jenny followed it as it was a simple rpg style sort of thing and said "I understand the tutorial." then there was trumpets and confetti as a "Quest Complete" message appeared and a small message appeared as telling her what she received. She closed the window.
    Tutu said "For points of reference I'll show you where you can see your quests, skills, and items. Just poke through these tabs and you'll find everything."
    Jenny tapped on the quest icon and saw she had no quests, she tapped on the item page to see that she had only 1 tf token, then finally she tapped on her skills tab where she found the "body adaptation" skill with the description "manipulate your body so you can attempt any challenge you are presented with."
    Tutu fluttered in and said "That's an interesting skill you've been given... Looks like your getting an interesting quest..."
