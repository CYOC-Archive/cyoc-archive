    A werewolf and a scarecrow

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 13 years ago A I

    Fredrika rushed into the cubicle with her monster costume. It was a werewolf suit. "Wolves are so cool!" Fredrika thought. She took off her clothes and slipped into the furry bodysuit. She then put on the clawed paw gloves and shoes. Finally, Fredrika donned the wolf head. Despite being unmistakably lupine, its features had a nearly human cast to them. It also had long, brunette hair on the top. After she put her her head on, Fredrika began to feel dizzy. After she felt better, she looked down. Her fur was real! Stunned, she touched he muzzle. She could feel it! As a final test she wagged her tail. She was a real toon werewolf! "Well, that settles it," thought Fredrika, "This is the best school ever!"

    She put her old clothes on her new body and exited the cubicle. From another cubicle came Emma, now a living scarecrow. Her body was a flannel shirt and overalls stuffed with straw. Her hands were a pair of old gloves filled with the same. Her head was a straw-stuffed burlap sack. For eyes the sack had a pair of shiny, blue buttons. The nose was a piece of orange, triangular fabric sewn onto the sack. Emma's mouth was a line sewn into her sack. Strangely, she could open and close it like a normal mouth. She wore a big straw hat on her head. Under it, long pieces of straw stuck out like hair. Emma spoke, still with her original voice,

    "Oh no! You too?" she asked. "Yeah! This is so cool! You look awesome." said Fredrika. "Y-you l-like it!?" said Emma. "You bet I do! You make one cool scarecrow." answered Fredrika. "I guess it is kind of cool to be a scarecrow. Fredrika, you're a werewolf, right? Werewolves can take human form. Try it." said Emma. "Okay, I'll give it a shot," said Fredrika. Fredrika concentrated as hard as she could on turning into a human. She slowly began to morph into a human. Her tail and claws disappeared, her ears and face returned to human shape, her fur vanished, and he muscles softened. "Wow!" she said, "I guess I can change my shape whenever I want." "Come on Fredrika," Emma said, "we'll be late for our first class!"
