    You went through the rest of the day using the wand to change people around you. Eventually, though, the time came to go home.

    You fished your house keys out of your pocket and unlocked the front door. Entering, you saw your 13-year-old brother, Ben, laying on the couch playing a video game. He was a skinny kid with messy brown hair and plain features, i.e. a younger version of you.

    Partly from the goodness of your heart, partly because you love this wand, you wave it at Ben. Instantly, he grows from 5’0 to 5’8”, his hair becomes a short styled blond, and muscles pack on everywhere until your brother is endowed with thick pecs, arms, and legs, along with a sculpted 6-pack.

    Ben grabs a football seemingly from nowhere and goes outside to play a shirtless game of catch.

    A loud whirring noise breaks your concentration. Looking outside, you see your neighbor, Mr. Finke, outside on his riding lawn mower. He’s about 65 years old, with no family, and quite fat and gray, resembling Jabba the Hut in his melted ice cream appearance. The 5’6” man with wisps of white hair on his head weighs about 300 pounds of fat, and due to the heat is unfortunately shirtless.

    You wave your wand at Mr. Finke. Instantly the years and fat begin to melt away; in fact he grows taller, to 6’2”, his weight quickly dropping. As he becomes younger, his re-growing hair turns black, covering his scalp and growing into a closely-shaved beard on his far more handsome face. With his fat gone, muscles pump up his frame dramatically, with massive pecs, washboard abs, and meaty biceps and legs, all adding up to about 250 pounds. Black hair sprouts thickly across his torso, where his small cross necklace rests, while a light dusting covers his limbs. Evidence of a massive penis is also apparent through his stretched athletic shorts. Finally, mirrored sunglasses appear on his face.

    You smile, thinking how you just transformed the ugly elderly Mr. Finke into the gorgeous 28-year-old hunk who prefers to be called by his first name, Ethan. What will you do now?
