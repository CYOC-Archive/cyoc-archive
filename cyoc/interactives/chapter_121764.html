But unlike any snake you have ever seen before it has two arms ending in very human looking hands, although hands covered in scales. Likewise, although the creature's chest is covered in scales you can see two bumps rising beneath them that can only be nipple-less breasts.     "Where ya going? Don't ya want your clothes?" asks the snake.

    "Please...please don't eat me," you say.

    The snake laughs loudly, loosening her grip a little.

    "If I wanted to eat ya, you would already be dead. Sides, I don't eat people. Plenty of better stuff to eat round here, " says the snake.

    "Then let me go, " you say, trying to use the distraction to slip free of her grasp.

    "Not yet. You still owe me," she says.

    "I thought you just wanted a kiss, " you say.

    The snake sighs.

    "Okay, you got me. I lied a little bit. I guess it is my nature - you know, snake and all. That was just part of my payment. But I wasn't lying when I said that I knew who took your clothes and where they are. And I can help you get them back and get out of here. If that is what ya want," says the snake.

    "Can you at least let go of me?" you ask, still trying to wriggle free of her tail. It seems like it is in constant motion, moving along your body and occasionally gently squeezing it.

" I am sorry about all the mysterious cloak and dagger stuff. I wasn't trying to scare ya or hurt ya. But most people - human or animal - get one look at me and head for the hills. This is really the only way I could get a chance to talk to you. And that's all I want to do is talk. If ya don't like what ya hear, then are free to try your luck on your own. But I wouldn't recommend it. That kangaroo was kid's stuff compared to some of the other things lurking around here."
