    Two days earlier once again at the prom, Loki waded into the crowd of students. They pleaded with him to continue his rampage and change them some more. But Loki had grown tired of doing a stage show for these people. He had changed a few more people but now he was willing to move on. With a little suggestion the revelers went back to dancing and having fun. Loki remained as a chaperone and found enjoyment where he dared but remained a memory to everyone he was not focusing on.

    And the Band played on.

    Bobby moved through the throng of people with two glasses in his hands. He made it to the table that his group had commandeered and sat down. "Has anyone seen Colleen?" He said shouting above the din. No one seemed to know exactly where she had slouched off to after Loki had his way.

    The other guys were quietly contemplating their new bodies. They were flexing new muscles or watching girlfriends dance on the floor. Summer was coming and the future lay open to them all. Thanks to Loki things looked better than they had. Bobby was happy but he knew it would be better if Colleen were around.

    Suddenly there was a person standing at their table. The five boys turned to look at the new comer. The background music and the crowds of people faded into obscurity around the little world of the table.

    "Gentlemen," Loki said acknowledging them all. He looked at each of them in turn; Arthur, called Art; Bobby; Colin; Derek; and Ernest. (Writer's confession: Yes, it is just ABCDE.) "How about we play a game?"

    The guys all looked at each other. The same thoughts must have been passing trough all of their minds. If they refused there was no telling what could happen. As one they agreed.

    From nowhere Loki produced five folded white cards in his hand. "The rules are simple. On each of these cards is written a change. If you read the change out loud you get that change. If you don't like the change you can switch it with someone else but you can not let them know what is written on your card. The game ends when everyone has a card they are happy with." Placing the cards in the centre of the table Loki backed away.

    The five guys each took one card and read it. Bobby smiled when he read his and decided to keep it. The four others decided to switch. The game was simple and straight forward. Art laughed at his card and switched with Colin who had scoffed at his. Derek and Ernest switched cards quickly then Ernst switched with Colin neither one looking at the second card they received. When everyone was done they decided to read them out loud one at a time.

    Bobby started, "Women withing twenty feet of your erection with become ever more aroused until they move out of range or your erection subsides. Bonus: For accepting what you are given, each woman's breasts will inflate temporarily while they get aroused. Enjoy!" Bobby and the rest laughed in glee at the ridiculousness of what they heard.

    The others then read their cards one at a time.
