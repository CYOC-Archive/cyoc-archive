    You stood there smirking along side the other goddesses in the room. However some didn’t seam to like how you turned out.

    (She looks absolutely hideous.) thought one to the others leaving you out of the conversation between statues.

    (I agree) replied another in the form of an ice angle (clearly she is not fit to join the rest of us.)

    (But what can we do?) thought the first.

    (I can move her.) said a golden statue of a thin woman. (But where?)

    A wooden elf like statue began giggling. (I know just the place.)

    You stood there ignorant to the statues conversation, till the golden statue glowed then shot a beam at you. You saw a black portal open, sucking you in.

    (‘What’s happening!?’) you thought to everyone.

    (Sorry.) said the first one without sounding sorry at all. She was a marble statue of a fat woman in a toga holding a cornucopia of various foods, a bird perched on her shoulder. (But this is a very civilized pantheon of goddesses, and We don’t have room for a wild goddess.)

    Then you were gone from the chamber. Your former pedestal empty.

    The question now is, Were did they send you?
