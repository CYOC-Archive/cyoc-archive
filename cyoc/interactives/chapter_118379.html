    An Interesting Situation Leads to a Bad Loophole

    Jeff looked over to see something standing behind her as she was throwing away the chronivac. Almost immediately Jeff felt nervous as she was now standing before another kyuubi, and thanks to her scent Jeff knew that it was male.

    "Hello there miss," The kyuubi said politely, and began slowly walking up to Jeff. The male kyuubi was larger than Jeff in all ways in comparison, with his more masculine form and features. The male kyuubi has fur that was midnight blue that darkened as it worked it's way from head to paw, and his eyes were deep green. As the kyuubi approached Jeff she back up nervously until she was cornered by a wall, and all she could do was stand now as the male kyuubi got closer by the second.

    "It's uncommon to find a fellow member of our species, and some say it's rare to see one over the lifetime of a planet." The male kyuubi said

    "H...how did you get in here? Who are you?!" You yipped and the kyuubi paused his approach only about half a foot in front of you.

    "Oh, apologies I suppose that I wasn't the most subtle with my appearance. My name is Kurak, and I have to say you certainly do have an exquisite scent. I got inside when I caught wind of your scent suddenly when I was wandering in the area. I saw an open window and made my way inside here, and I have to say that it's quite odd that your scent suddenly appeared like that. Actually I remember a human scent here, and both your and that scent share a few distinct similarities. Would you mind if I asked you to elaborate on why that is?" The make kyuubi, Kurak said

    "Well...uh, it's kind of uh..." Jeff began, but couldn't quite get the words out of her mouth.

    "Let me guess, you were transformed into a kyuubi like me. Am I right?" Kurak said

    Jeff paused, shocked and hesitantly responded,"H..How did y...you know?"

    "It's the obvious explanation really, that is unless you were switched with the previous occupant. Either way I find you quite the attractive female by scent, your golden-like fur compliments that smell perfectly, and your purple fur on your neck contrasts that golden color, but gives you character." Kurak said brushing his tails past Jeff's chin, and she blushed under her fur.

    Jeff then shook her head and snapped back into focus and said,"Well that's nice and all, but this isn't exactly permanent, and I'm a guy."

    "Well you certainly don't look male to me, but that's not necessarily bad at all. Besides, what do you mean by not permanent?" Kurak asked

    Jeff sighed, and said,"I used a device call the chronivac, and I accidentally made myself a female kyuubi, when I wanted to be male. Anyways I screwed that up and panicked, and because of that I'm stuck like this for 3 years."

    "Can I see this chronivac?" Kurak asked

    Jeff nodded despite her gut instinct, and lead Kurak to the chronivac. Jeff also saw that the screen hadn't changed from before, and Kurak saw that as well. "See it says three years, and I can't change that." Jeff said

    "Have you explored this device at all?" Kurak asked

    "It can only change others into other creatures and switch their genders too, and in my case both happened." Jeff said

    "Do you mind if I fiddle with this for a few moments? If I find a way to alter anything I could change you." Kurak said

    "I just want to be human, and male." Jeff said

    "Are you sure about that? I have lived longer than any human, and you're beautiful like you are." Kurak said

    Jeff blushed, and you saw Kurak backed out of the screen and was able to transform you again. "See you just needed to calm down and return to the screen. Now are you REALLY sure about becoming human again? The life of a kyuubi is quite free, and lax until you have to think about survival." Kurak said

    "You just want me to stay female don't you?" Jeff growled

    "No I don't, and actually I've been interested about becoming female myself. But seriously life is easy as a kyuubi, and I'd happily make you male if you so wish." Kurak said

    Jeff thought about Kurak's words, and she considered what she was doing. This was truly a once in a lifetime experience for her, and if she becomes human again she could lose it, or at least lose the chance to learn about being a kyuubi. Kurak then began typing something in, and...
