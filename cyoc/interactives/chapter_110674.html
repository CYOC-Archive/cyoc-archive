    Buffed Ben Nana Man

    added by Drakkenfyre 6 years ago BM I O

    Ben followed this routine daily for a week. Every morning, James harvested his banana. Then he'd be strung up in the backyard for an hour or two. Then he'd spend hours exercising, and then do whatever chores James had for him. One day it was washing the car in only his yellow jockstrap with the bananacup. Another day it was vacuuming the whole house. He was learning how to use banana oil in cooking too. He was pleased that he was lifting more weights in less time each day, but hadn't really noticed that between the exercise and photosynthesis, Ben had put on about 50 lbs of muscle. He was really tired when he went to bed. He vaguely thought about his friends and family from before he had used the Chronivac, he sort of thought he should let them know that he was okay- but it really didn't seem too important. He slept heavily.

    "Damn, sleepy head, why weren't you down at breakfast to give me my morning banana?" growled James angrily.

    Rubbing the sand from his eyes, Ben shooed some fruit flies away. He sat up in bed. James ripped the covers off him, and gasped.

    Between his thighs, Ben was sporting not one banana, but a whole bunch. James eyes widened, and he laughed.

    "I'm going to have to have a party to share all this fruit," he said as he snapped off the nearest ripest yellow banana from the bunch. He peeled it and started to chew. Then between bites he said, "Hey, Ben, you're going to break off that bunch and put it on the banana tree in the kitchen. I wonder how many bananas you can produce each day? Hm, definitely going to have to step up your workout routine and sun-time."

    "Uh?" Ben said staring at the bunch of bananas growing out of his crotch. He grabbed it roughly and twisted. SNAP. He sobbed slightly, as he carried the bunch downstairs to the kitchen to hang on the banana tree. It was after 8 am. He had slept late.

    He drank a big glass of water, and took his plant supplements with it. Then he headed out back to put on his harness.
