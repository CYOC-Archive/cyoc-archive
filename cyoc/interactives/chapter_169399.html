    To say that the last 72 hours had been a nightmare would be an understatement. Jennifer had hoped Regenesis would call back that day, yet that was not the case.

    Not wanting to panic, Jennifer tried her best to keep herself busy around her home. Jennifer tried to go about her day, trying to relax by the pool and working on her tan. Those things though quickly were becoming an anxiety inducing experience.

    It started the following morning when Jennifer had awoken. The teenager, trying to avoid the same embarrassment from yesterday, had simply thrown a long t shirt on. As she ran to her mirror she immediately noticed a problem.

    Jennifer's face looked just as youthful as yesterday. As she gazed at her appearance, she couldnt shake the feeling that something felt off. A feeling of dread now walked over the actress as she grabbed a tape measure. Standing with back against the wall, Jennifer placed a mark against the wall before unraveling her tape measure.

    "5'7"," she gasped. "I'm getting shorter!"

    The rest of the day wasnt much better for Jennifer. The teen realized she was still growing younger, remembering the growth spurt she had experienced between 15 and 16. Now she had just relived that growth spurt in reverse. Jennifer tried her best to forget the fact she was now too young to drive to little effect. The teens clothes were a little too baggy, the shoes a little too loose. Even laying by the pool Jrnnifer couldnt shake the feeling she was growing smaller just laying there.

    The following morning was no better. Jennifer gazed into the mirror, almost bursting into tears as she beheld a coltidh 14 yr old. Going through the same ritual as yesterday Jennifer found she had shrank another two inches leaving her 5'5". What was worse was the effect on her body.

    Jennifer's breasts had decreased to a modest set of B cups, while her legs were now far too skinny for her taste. Even Jennifer's once ample rear had been reduced to a flat butt with no curve to it. Jennifer now found herself sulking, kicking her self as she realized she looked every bit the moody teenager.

    As Jennifer awoke on the third day, she dreaded having to take a look at herself. As she slid out of bed, she was hit with a strange sense of vertigo. Looking over herself the girl was shocked to see how long and baggy her clothing was. Glancing down her nightshirt only made her wince as she beheld two barely B cups mounds.

    Sighing the teen measured herself, her eyes growing damp as she realized she was now only 5'1". If her short stature didnt sink her predicament home she could see her face was now rounder, more childish than yesterday.

    "I'm 13," she whispered, startled at the childish soprano of her voice. Sinking to the floor the girl buried her face in her hands. How was she supposed to work now? Jennifer was about to burst into tears when her cellphone rang. Glancing at the number she could see it was Regenesis. Hopefully they would finally have some good news for her.
