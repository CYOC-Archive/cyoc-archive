    “Hey, do you want something to drink? They have Gatorade! They got fruit punch, lemon lime, orange and even the cherry flavored one.” Anthony said to his pal.

    “Hmm… I’m kinda curious about that other bottle right next to the four Gatorade flavors. I’ve never heard of that. It’s called ‘MTX’? Must be something new!” Vincent noted, as he stopped and stared at the large glass display in the vending machine. “I wanna try the new stuff!”

Of course, they had never heard of the MTX drinks before and they had no idea what the effects of the drink were.

The two young men had just decided to sit down at the outside table and chairs by the small café located next to the gym. They spent a few minutes looking at their cell phones when Vincent suddenly had an overwhelming urge to use the bathroom.

Suddenly, Vincent was sporting an impressive V-taper, all because of that, with mighty lats! His flat stomach now contained a set of well-defined abdominal muscles as well.

He was tempted to buy a bottle of MTX and to try it for himself. But, would Vincent want to try another bottle as well?
