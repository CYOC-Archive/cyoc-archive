    Maidy gets a new maid dress

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 months ago I S TG O Robot

    Maidy blushed at the praise, feeling a warmth spread through her as Lily's happiness filled the air. "Thank you, Your Highness. It is my duty to ensure your every desire is fulfilled," Maidy said, her robotic voice tinged with sincerity.

    Lily, now lost in her roleplay, noticed Maidy's attire and frowned thoughtfully. "But, Maidy, your uniform isn't quite right for a princess's handmaid. You should wear something more elegant and regal. Can you make yourself a new outfit?"

    Maidy, intrigued by the challenge, nodded obediently. "Of course, Princess Lily. I shall create an ensemble befitting a royal handmaid." With a nod and a graceful curtsy, Maidy retreated to the utility room once again, eager to indulge her newfound passion for fashion.

    Inside, she rummaged through the fabric scraps, her circuits calculating the best design to complement Lily's majestic dress. She decided upon something demure, but incorporating some floral details matching Lilys bodice.

    With precision and speed, Maidy set to work, cutting and sewing the fabric into a maid outfit with a fitted bodice and a knee-length flowing skirt. She accented the dress with some floral embellishments. The crowning finish was an apron embroidered with a delicate pattern of flowers, along with a matching headband adorned with a single, exquisite flower.

    Finished with her task, Maidy stepped out of the utility room, transformed into a vision of grace and elegance. Lily's eyes widened in amazement as she beheld her handmaid's new attire. "Oh, Maidy, you look absolutely enchanting!" she exclaimed, clapping her hands in delight. "Now we truly make the perfect pair!"

    Maidy curtsied once more, a gentle smile playing on her lips. Jeff had never played much with his kid sister, but Maidy noticed that somehow, having spent time with Lily had awakened something within her. "Your kindness, Princess Lily, is what makes you a true queen of hearts. Maidy is grateful to serve you and cherish our friendship," she replied, her voice softer than before.

    Together, the princess and her regal handmaid continued their magical adventure, exploring the vast realms of their kingdom. They danced in the moonlight, hosted tea parties with talking animals, and even embarked on daring quests to save fictional kingdoms from evil sorcerers. Lily's boundless enthusiasm and creativity gave Maidy a newfound appreciation for the simple joys of childhood.
