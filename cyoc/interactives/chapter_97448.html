    Ty decided to teleport him and Jake home so they could have more fun there and so he could show off Jake’s new muscles when Dustin came home from the doctor’s office. That way, Dustin wouldn’t even dare bully them since Jake would be much bigger and stronger than him.

    Indeed, once they were back in Ty’s room, Dustin opened the door and just marched in the room since he never knocked.

    “Alright, Ty… Now, what is going on here… OH!” Dustin gasped when he saw Jake. Jake was much bigger than before, standing over 6 feet tall and ripped with muscle. He was no longer the short, skinny soccer kid from next door!

    “What the hell happened to YOU?” Dustin shouted when he saw Jake sitting on the bed.

    “Oh… I just had a little… growth spurt…” Jake smiled as he flexed his muscles.

    Just then, there was a knock on the door. It was another kid from the neighborhood who decided to come by and say hi to Ty since it was his birthday.

    It was Lidort, the short, chubby boy from down the street who always wanted to talk to Ty about food, computers, and roleplaying online but Ty never was allowed to thanks to Mark and June.

    “Hello… I came to see Ty?” the shy, blond boy said as he peered through the door opening. “Is he here?”

    “Come on in, Lidort!” Ty smiled. “I got a few friends here already so join the crowd!”

    “I got you a little card!” Lidort grinned as he walked in and handed Ty an envelope. Ty took it and opened it up, revealing a greeting card and a small gift card inside.

    “Oh cool! A gift card for the video game store! Thanks, dude!” Ty smiled as he got up and embraced his friend.

    “Alright, alright! Let’s get all these guys out of here!” Dustin grumbled. “Party’s over!”

    Ty became annoyed with Dustin and decided to show his brother that he could no longer push his friends out of his room.

    “Umm… Dustin… You shouldn’t pick fights with my friends… Especially since they’re all bigger than you, you know?” Ty said coolly.

    “What are you talking about?” Dustin sneered. “Only Jake is bigger than me! But Lidort is a fat little twerp! I can kick him out whenever I want!”

    “Who are you calling a fat, little twerp, Dustin?” Ty sighed.

    Lidort lowered his head. He had been in this situation before. Dustin had always been mean to him. But Lidort hated conflict and drama so he thought it was just better to avoid a fight with Dustin and to just leave.

    “Okay, well I better go…. See ya later, Ty… Happy Birthday!” Lidort said sadly as he turned around and he was about to walk out the door.

    “WAIT!” Ty shouted. “Nobody said you had to leave…”

    Then it happened. Lidort felt an intense tingling spread all over his body, specifically in every muscle! His arms then bulked up rapidly, growing into massive thick pythons of muscle! His pectorals pushed outwards and sideways, making his chest totally huge and his shoulders super wide! His biceps ballooned out from his arms and grew into large orbs of muscle! Within minutes, his flat stomach was all covered with mighty abs and his legs grew as thick and as wide as tree trunks!

    “Uuuuhhh!!!” Lidort groaned loudly as he ripped out of his shirt and he tore his shorts by the seams thanks to all that muscle.

    “Uhh… What the hell?” Dustin wondered out loud as he saw Lidort transform into a muscle guy right before his very eyes. Suddenly, Lidort was much bigger and stronger than him!

    “I think Lidort is staying here, Dustin!” Ty said calmly.

    “Hey! He’s almost as big as I am!” Jake grinned.

    “Whoa! Look at my muscles!” Lidort said as he flexed his arms.

    “Uhhh…. I think… I had better leave…” Dustin said as he left the room and closed the door.

    “Hah! Dustin couldn’t stand up to my muscle-friends!” Ty laughed out loud.

    Jake, Lidort and Ty then sat down and checked out some of his video games. Ty knew that as long as all his friends were big and buff, Dustin would leave them alone…
