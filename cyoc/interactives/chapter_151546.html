This is harder than Inthought it would be.” Mary Elise still had no idea what Mika was doing, gazing around the room her eyes grew wide as she gazed at her small stuffed cat.

At first horrified that Mika would try her magic on the last thing her father gave her she paused as she looked closer. The stuffed animals fur appeared to be filling in as the bald patches she had rubbed bare began to fill in. Soon enough the stuffed animal looked exactly as it had the day her father had given it to her.

It was almost like the animal was gazing a skeleton and muscles as it continued to change. Slowly approaching she watched as the cats dull plastic eyes began to change. The eyes of the animal began to look less like plastic and more life like. Unable to speak she continued watching as a small crack began to spread along the toys fAce. In seconds Mary Elise watched as a full and functioning mouth formed on her once lifeless doll.

    “Mika What are you doing,” Mary Elise whispered as she watched the toy blink.

The hard plastic nose began to lighten and become flesh. As the spell completed Mary Elise’s toy blinked before letting out a meow and rubbing against the teens outstretched hand, purring loudly.
