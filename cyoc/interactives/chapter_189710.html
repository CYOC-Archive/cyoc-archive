    "Hey there mister officer," Charlie slurred his words as he approached the other man. His legs didn't seem to be working right as he staggered across the room. But his focus was still on Liam.

    Liam took a step back. Charlie was always known for wild parties but this seemed too much. So many people were wandering around, and even more splayed out on top of one another. Sex was common for rich people like this. And with someone built like Charlie, it wasn't hard to see why either. He was a big dude. With all his free time and money he made sure to look like a bodybuilder and be as strong as an ox. Normally they had to send several officers over to make sure he wouldn't go hog wild.

    But this was different. He almost seemed out of it. "Hey Charlie. You're having a party?" Liam said. He needed to stall. Keep Charlie occupied until someone else were to show up.

    "I am," Charlie smiled. He took a cup off one of the shelves and drank it. Liam hoped it was water. "You here ta join us? We're having a pretty good time..."

    "Not quite. We received a couple of complaints. It's a bit too noisy. Think you can turn it down?" Liam shouted.

    "Anything for you mister officer," Charlie said. He waved his hand and the music turned down on it's own.

    Liam let go of his breath. "Thank you," he said. If Charlie was going to cooperate, then it wouldn't be too much of a problem. Still there was an air of discomfort in the room. Charlie's eyes never left Liam. If anything they looked almost trained on him. And maybe it was the low light, but Liam could have sworn they were different. Something about Charlie's pupils looked more like slits than the usual circle. And the blue color of his eyes seemed to be drifting further outside.

    "Not a problem," Charlie smiled. His smile broadened. "You look nice in that shirt. But I think you'd look better with out it."

    "Thank you," Liam forced a closed smile. "I appreciate that, but I'm kind of working right now."

    Charlie moved surprisingly fast to the other side of Liam. His hand quickly landed on the door, blocking the officer's escape. "Don't be such a prude. Have a little fun now and then." His smile broadened. Even though they were in a dark room, a flash of light bounced off Charlie's eyes.

    Liam felt his hand go to his gun. If something went south, he'd have to protect himself. But there was something else odd. Charlie had always been absolutely huge. A good 2 or 3 inches taller than Liam, and stacked with at least 60 or 70 pounds of muscle the officer wish he had. But right now they were looking eye to eye. And something about them were off. Almost like they weren't fully human. "Are--"

    The moment Liam opened his mouth, Charlie pounced. Their lips locked together and Charlie plunged his tongue deep inside the other man. With his strength, he easily held Liam against the wall as he continued to make out with the other man. Liam's eyes went wide. He tried to let out a scream, but nothing really came out. He readied his strength and used it all to push Charlie off him. "The fuck!?" Liam shouted.

    Charlie tumbled down to the ground. He almost looked proud of himself as he looked up at the officer. His eyes flashed again. "I'm just trying to have a little fun officer." He shifted his weight around on the ground, almost like he was trying to play. He flipped onto his back. His hands batted up to the sky as grinned at the officer.

    A deep foreboding grunt exited the officer. Liam stared down with all his anger at the other man. "Well, get your daddy's lawyer ready. Cause this is fucking bullshit." Liam left the house. "The fuck was he on? God damn it!" He quickly wiped his mouth. Then spit onto the concrete. He'd never kissed a guy before. And he needed to get that nasty taste out of his mouth.
