    Rachel paws at the ground with her new hoof a little nervously, she was still getting used to the idea of having four legs instead of two, but she was starting to get used to it. She had to admit, no matter how weird it was, she enjoyed it a lot, and she was going to have fun on this vacation.

    "Well hey there," a voice calls out, and she turns to see a golden furred unicorn watching her. "Are you lost?"

    "No, I'm just waiting for a friend," Rachel says, feeling her new body heating up a little, the Unicorn was one of the most beautiful things she had ever seen in her life. "Uh, hi, I'm Rachel."

    "I'm Wind Rider," Wind Rider says, catching Rachel a little off guard, she had expected him to have a human name. Wind Rider recognizes her confusion and chuckles a little. "I was born to transformed guests who didn't want to stay in this form and raised by a herd of Unicorns here on the Island."

    "Oh, wow," Rachel says, a little embarrassed. "What brings you here?"

    "I was in the area," Wind Rider says, circling around Rachel, looking her over curiously. "Have you decided how you're going to spend your vacation here?"

    "Well, not really," Rachel admits sheepishly. "I picked Unicorns at kind of the last minute and I don't really know what to do."

    "Well, maybe I can help you get more, acquainted with your new form," Wind Rider says, it takes Rachel a minute to realize what he really wants. "Or you can come and meet my herd, I'm sure you'd enjoy getting to know them."

    Rachel paused a moment, she wanted to wait for Cynthia, and something about this guy felt weird. "Are you suggesting that we, mate?"

    "Of course, we're animals silly, why should we resist what comes naturally to us?" Wind Rider points out.

    Rachel thought about that, she wouldn't mind trying sex as a Unicorn, but if she got pregnant she would be stuck in this form for awhile. What should she do?
