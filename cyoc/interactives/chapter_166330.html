    Sherman Stiff got herself turned into a giant centauress somehow in a blazing big barn

    added by Anonymous 3 years ago A S O

    It had been a wisp of smoke beyond the hill
    so I kept my eye on that direction for a minute or two and sure
    enough the smoke returned,but thicker and more permanent this
    time.

    I called down to Chris what I was observing and
    as I was talking to him I suddenly saw the flames shooting up and
    the tree I was in shook like there'd been a explosion or even worse
    a earthquake.

    Yet strangely I couldn't hear any sounds coming from that direction
    as you'd expect from smoke,flames and shaking trees.

    Chris called back up
    Hey didn't that mousy accountant Sherman Stiff head in that
    direction ?

    Who sez I

    You know
    Miss Sherman Stiff
    the Spinster who tore a strip off the Pilot before she left
    on her own six hours ago.

    Oh her
    sez I as I recalled the woman Chris was talking about

    She was the accountant for the Ladies Boarding and Grooming
    School in Melbourne
    as well as sole owner of the
    Miss Genteel Witch
    franchise chain.

    Sherman Stiff was certainly no lady
    and as I stared at the continuing smoke and flames
    Chris and I were going to have to go over the Hill
    to see if she were in danger.

    So a short while later Chris and I stood at the top of the Hill
    and could see down into the scene at the foot of the Hill.

    Basically a barn fit for giants lay in a clearing and it was
    blazing merrily away.

    Though I still couldn't hear any sounds I just knew
    Sherman Stiff was in that huge barn and in trouble.

    So Chris and I raced down to the barn where to our surprise
    although the barn clearly was being destroyed by a fire
    the fire had no heat nor were the clouds of smoke
    gushing from its giant sized door,giant livestock sized door
    and the ventilators along the top of the roof.

    Suddenly the swinging livestock doors swung open and a
    gust of wind swept me and Chris inside the interior of the
    barn then the doors behind us swung closed again with a crash.

    The inside of the giant barn was noisy.Over the crackling roaring
    flames
    which suddenly had heat and a acrid smell I could
    hear Sherman Stiff screaming for someone to
    cut her ropes.

    That puzzled me but Chris and I headed in the direction of her
    strangely loud,deep and somewhat beastial voice.
    till we came to the giant livetock stall where her voice was coming from.
    And there she stood
    a giant centauress,struggling to break the rope.....
