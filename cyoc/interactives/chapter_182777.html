    A special, yet problematic child is born

    As she was transfering your soul, the goddess made one heavy mistake. It rarely happens, but as this was her first time, she had a special connection with you and accidentally touched your soul directly. Not knowing what would become of this split second of contact, she continued on passing your soul into your new body. She choose to place you into a female's body, however, as she wanted to desperately grant your final wish, she slightly altered the baby's genetics, turning her into a hermaphrodite, that way you would for sure have the most ideal body for losing your virginity when it is time. She kept your general appearance as being completely female though, since from what she observed, a greater percentage of all females end up having sex tban males.

    As for your memories, she worried that if you remembered the scary moments from birth, it would harm your growth and ruin your childhood with unanswerable questions and traumas, so she just locked those bad ones away for a few years until you were better able to handle them, at which point they would unlock on their own.

    The family she choose to place you in is relatively well off financially, and not at the mercy of a terrible individual in a position of power. One of your grandmothers would even be a demon, so your ability to use magic would be exceptional, and given that she was a succubus, your physique and looks should be nothing shy of beautiful when you mature. In addition, your physical abilities as a dark elf would be equally as great. This truely was one of the best futures you could get, and it had one of the highest chances for you to live a full and happy life if everything went well.

    At least until you were born with pure white skin, hair, literally shimmering maroon colored eyes, and were completely weightless. The skin and hair color, along with the red eyes could be chalked up to you being born as an albino, which while very rare, is documented. However the shimmering effect on your eyes, and the total weightlessness were completely unexplainable.

    So they had you appraised by one of the mages to check your status for any birth defects involving a inherent skill. What they found was that you possessed a few incredible things. The top most being the title "Descendant of the Goddess of Life and Death". The other big shocks were you possessed the skill "Complete Immunity" and "Goddess' Walk". The first made you completely immune to all negative effects, and status debuffs. The other explained your weightlessness. It essentially meant that you can move your body in a 3D sphere of movement without needing to touch a surface or even take a step for that matter. You even could pass right through solid objects if you had a clear mental image of where you would end up. The last important point was your race. You were not just a dark elf. You also were a part of the Celestial, or the god race.

    It was quickly decided that no one would speak a word about this, and a high ranking disguise enchantment was placed upon your stats that would conceal them from others who were below the required rank. You would still be able to see them due to your "Complete Immunity" skill excluding you from the list of targeted people. Either way, your parents still were glad you were born happy, even if you had 1 more parent in addition to the two of them.
