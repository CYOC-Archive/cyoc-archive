With a smile, he said: “Thanks for that, Mark. I´m sure it will be easier for you to simply give me permission than to escort me everywhere. What do you want to do next?”

For my art project I need something from the uni, so I only get that tomorrow. I´m not interested in watching a movie, or tv at all.” Aidan sighed, still hoping Mark would text him, but that didn´t happen.
