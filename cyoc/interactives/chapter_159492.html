She had been a little disappointed earlier that she had to choose between making a transformation and attending the cocktail party held every afternoon out on the patio. Gloria’s room was right next to the patio and she was looking forward to attending, but her curiosity to transform had won out. Now she could do both.

    Although the vent was dark, Gloria had no problem flying towards the bright light down the tunnel that indicated the vent leading outside. She was getting better at flying, and she easily navigated her way through the vent and went outside.

    What she saw was exactly what she was looking for, and it was breathtaking. There were all sorts of giants out on the patio, men and women in cocktail attire, relaxed and having conversations in the warm afternoon breeze as they sipped their cocktails.

    Gloria flew at a low height, roughly three feet off the ground, to make sure she had a good view of all the giants towering over her. She was a little concerned that the giants would all recoil in horror as she flew past, but she discovered, to her surprise and arousal, that she meant so little to these giants that they didn’t notice or care if she was around.

In addition to giving Gloria plenty of landing area, the woman was in conversation with a circle of people at the edge of the party. If Gloria landed on her back, no one would even see her, so long as she avoided getting the giant woman’s attention.

She found zigzagging through the air like normal flies was easy to do given the subtle wind currents that normal people don’t see, making her flying look more natural. She swooped on in, roughly at thigh height, and then flew up right behind the woman, admiring her dress as she flew higher and higher. She picked the top of the giant woman’s left shoulder blade as her target for landing, and came in slowly. Fortunately, the woman was mostly standing in place, making the landing easier. She stared up at the woman’s neck and giant blonde hair. It was an incredible feeling being so close to someone so large without them even detecting you. She loved it. Gloria briefly considered seeing if she could land on top of the giant’s blonde hair without her or her companions noticing, but Gloria decided to go bolder.

She flew down past the woman’s back and past her ass, still looking pretty good for a woman her age, until she reached the bottom of the woman’s dress, just above her knees. Gloria then turned horizontally and flew under the woman’s dress.

Now she was a real fly.
