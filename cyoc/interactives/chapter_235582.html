    David and Mitch felt very small and vulnerable, something they were rather unaccustomed to as security guards. It had been a very long time since they'd been in the shoes of the ones that should feel intimidated by guards, holders of authority and plain bigger guys. They wre not the ones to wield power anymore. On the contrary, they were just young teens in oversized clothes that threatened to fall off their much altered frames. And many - too many- men were staring at them. They felt like prey. Suddenly the fat guys they secretly sneered at while being polite as they just did their job were no more objects of ridicule. On the contrary they were now frightening people that could do anything to them, looming and most impressive. Oh, how the tables had turned!
    Then one of these vile slobs approached them. He was holding in his arms a drowsy boy about the same age of Mitch and David - well, new age - who appeared to be taking a very restful nap, dressed in a baggy t-shirt, shorts and sandals.
    "Oh," the man said in a concerned voice. "It appears I have dragged inadvertently two men into the Daddy Dimension. Well, former men."
    "What?..." spluttered both boys.
    "My name is Winston Wydeloade and my new son here - he pointed at the boy in his arms at this - used to be like you not even a quarter of an hour ago. It was just bad luck that you accidentally were affected by my magic and got dragged into the dimension. It very rare, but it can happen."
    "You... You mean you're the one responsible for this?" screamed David in outrage. "Change us back!"
    "Well, now that you've seen the dimension..." waved Winston casually.
    "You gotta be kidding!" yelled David anew.
    But Winston had already grabbed both by their clothes in a wide grasp and was looking thoughtfully at them.
    "I suppose I COULD keep you... I've wanted a son for a long time, after all. But three at once, it might be too much indeed... And there are plenty of other daddies looking for cute little boys to adopt too..."
    Both Mitch and David squirmed, panic rising. This was not looking good...
