    Dean And Tom

    The two brothers Dean and Tom, Dean being 16 and Tom being 10, both having short black hair, brown eyes and an olive skin tone, both with an athletic built due to their favorite activity: parkour, are visiting the manufacturer as a “brother bonding” “expedition”, both being tech-nerds, head straight for the “Android” department.

    “Man, I can’t wait to check out those amazing Androids,” Tom says as they walk down a long corridor.

    “Yeah, though they are kinda creepy,” Dean replies, “you know… because of they appear like real humans. You can’t tell if they are androids or actual humans!”

    Tom giggles, “yeah, but the thought of being an android is kinda cool,” he says.

    Dean nods, “living forever, never aging,” he says.

    “That would be so cool,” Tom says, “we’d be stronger, faster, smarter than normal humans!”

    “Yeah, though I guess it would depend on our programming,” Dean says.

    Tom nods, “true, still, it would be cool”.

    The two boys finally arrive at the “Android” room, no other person seems to be around, “we seem to be the only ones here,” Tom says as they enter.

    “Yeah, probably they all are in other departments, wanting to check out what interests them the most, then head to the next, like we have planned,” Dean replies.

    “Makes sense,” Tom says.

    As the two walk further into the room and closer to the conveyor belt, their eyes widen, “just look at all those robots and androids,” Tom says, “the robots look more like they are meant for jobs, you know, like technician stuff, the androids all look like real people, adults, teens and children! Some are even wearing clothes”. He then turns to his brother, “do… do you think they have also got genitals?”

    Dean chuckles, “probably,” he says, “after all, androids ARE supposed to resemble real humans, so I do believe they even have real genitals”.

    “Really, but how… I mean… would they be able to… you know…,” Tom blushes.

    Dean raises an eyebrow at his brother, he then lets out a snort as he smirks, “you and your sexual fantasies,” he says, “but I believe androids are able to make babies, who knows what the nanobots inside of them are able to do”.

    What the two boys don’t notice, as they stepped into the room, they had been picked up by the AI’s sensors, due to the virus, which had infested the AI, it doesn’t recognize the two boys as humans, but as “lose androids”. The boys don’t notice as two hatches on the high ceiling open right above them and two large grabbing-claws start to extend from the ceiling, aiming direct for Dean and Tom.

    The boys suddenly feel something grabbing them around the waist, “what the…,” they both exclaim, looking down to see large metal rings around their waists before they feel themselves been hosted suddenly into the air, “HEY! LET US GO,” they then shout, the claws however do not let them go and the boys find themselves being transported to a large pole in one corner of the large room. As they come closer, they notice that at least 20 strange coffin-like glass boxes surround it, a sign that reads “fixing area”, hangs above the “coffins” on the pole.

    The boys widen their eyes in horror and begin to struggle, “HELP! SOMEONE, PLEASE HELP US,” they shout as loud as they can, though due to being the only ones in that part of the large manufacturer, no hears their cries, soon the boys find themselves dangling right above two of the coffin-like boxes, the lids hiss open and a second claw can be seen inside the “coffins”.

    Tom and Dean struggle and even begin to cry out of fear as the find themselves being turned vertical, causing them to stare at the ceiling as they feel themselves being slowly lowered. The boys try grabbing the cord of the claw which is holding them, but they are unable to reach and soon they feel themselves being lowered into the coffins.

    The first claw lets them go, but before Tom and Dean are able to jump out of the coffins, the claw inside the coffin immediately closes around their waists and holds them in place as the lid hiss shut, trapping both in their coffins.

    Tom turns his head to look at his brother, Dean meets his gaze, tears of fear roll down both of their faces, “wh-what is going to happen to us,” Tom asks.

    Due to the thick glass, Dean is unable to hear his brother, but he is able to read his lips, “I don’t know,” he mouths back, “I can’t reach the effing claw, and I doubt I could open it!”

    “We are doomed,” Tom mouths, “I… I don’t wanna die!”

    “I think I know what is going on,” Dean says, “and to be honest, if my suspicion is correct, I’d honestly prefer to die!”

    Tom’s eyes widen, “Wha… oh,” he then says as he understands what his brother meant, “y-you know I was kidding when I said I’d like to be an android, right?”

    Dean nods, letting out a weak chuckle, “well, I guess we have no option than to see what will happen”.
