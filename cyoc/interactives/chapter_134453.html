    Celestia and Oakmoss wandered together the streets of the kingdom, while the two of them were walking, the air started getting colder and colder, "Just as i expected." sighed Celestia, as she put on an adorable white fur coat, specially made for her by Willhelm, meanwhile Oakmoss chuckled, dragons could easily survive cold temperatures due to their metabolism, and for Oakmoss this was no exception, most of the time the teal green drake lived in cold forests, almost near tundras.

    Oakmoss walked proudly in the streets of the kingdom, meanwhile Celestia was on his head, sitting like a lady with her legs crossed, making herself sure that she couldn't block Oakmoss' view, and with her soft delicate hands on her hips, from time to time the two of them would look at eachother smiling, somtimes even grabbing the attention of multiple citizens, some hens would stare at Oakmoss' muscular form.

    Eventually Celestia and Oakmoss reached the marketplace of the kingdom, the area wasn't so crowded since it was barely the morning, Oakmoss looked around in a stunned manner, around him were multiple markets containing diffrent things, jewlery, ingredients for chemistry, rare metals and so on.

    The teal green drake and the young chemist walked around, searching for the fish market, while there, Celestia from time to time would visit other markets, getting other essentials for her chemistry experiments and other materials her father needs, Oakmoss from time to time would look around at the citizens wandering around the marketplace, most of them weared the same outfits, leather tunics and dresses, but there were two individuals that stood out.

    The first individual was a man, wearing a beautiful trench coat, leather pants and shoes, the man also weared a tophat, making him stand out.
    The other individual was a beautiful anthropomorphic dragoness, with white scales and fur, this was the first time Oakmoss saw an anthropomorphic dragon in his life, he never understood why a dragon would get a bipedal form instead of keeping the strong quadrupedal form, the dragoness wasn't like any ordinary dragon, the dragoness was an angelic dragon, she weared a beautiful medieval blouse with a black long skirt.

    "Come on Dragon, let's go to the fish market." Said Celestia as she held a bag, containing most of the things she bought, Oakmoss quickly nodded as he followed his Rider, heading towards the fish market nearby.
    Celestia bought six mackerels, "That will be thirty coins my dear." said the vendor, Celestia nodded and quickly gave thirty coins to the vendor, "Thank you, have a nice day!" said the vendor as Celestia left, accidently bumping into Anija in the process.
    "Oh gosh i'm so sorry!" Said Celestia as she looked at Anija, attempting to hide the stunned look of her eyes, just like Oakmoss, she never saw an anthropomorphic dragon before.
    "Don't worry sweetie, everything's alright, i shouldn't have been behind you in the first place." Said Anija with a smile, Celestia was speechless, she didn't know what to say, she quickly nodded and left the marketplace, heading home with her beautiful drake.

    "Just who was she?" Asked Celestia as she cooked one of the mackerels, the smell filling the air.
    "She's was an anthropomorphic angelic dragon." Replied Oakmoss, as he ate one of the mackerels, Celestia remained there confused, she never heard of 'anthropomorphic' dragons before, nor 'angelic' dragons.
    "Angelic dragons?" Asked Celestia as she finished the mackerel gently putting it on the dinning table and starting to eat it.
    "They're a rare type of species of dragons, commonly associated with healing and restoration." Said Oakmoss, "Those type of dragons are almost immortal, they can't die due to age or from diseases, anyone that holds them is also considered immortal." finished Oakmoss as he laid his snout on Celestia's lap.
    "And why did she look like that?" Asked Celestia, "Why did she have a bipedal form and not a quadrupedal form?" she asked again, before taking another bite from the mackerel.
    "Every dragon has the ability to switch from a quadrupedal form into a bipedal one." Replied Oakmoss, "She held quite a powerful scent from the man beside her, most likely her Rider." said Oakmoss, "So maybe, she mated with him." finished Oakmoss.
    Celestia heard of unions between humans and dragons, at first when she heard them for the first time she thought that it was some form of sick taboo, eventually she found out that in reality, they were the ultimate bonding between Rider and dragon, at first she was afraid that her father was gonna do the same with his dragoness, and if that wasn't enough, she was afraid that in the future when she was older, her father would force her to mate with a drake and have Kilin children with it.

    Celestia finished eating the mackerel, she looked at Oakmoss who laid his snout on her lap, a smile appeared on her lips as she ran her hand down the teal green scales of her dragon.
