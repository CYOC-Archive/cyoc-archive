    “Oh you can’t go to the dance wearing this! It’s too old and cheap. Let me get you a better dress! Bippity boppity boo!” Godmother Amy said, waving her wand at Past Amy.

    I groaned in annoyance. I did not care to watch Amy act insecure, and I had no idea why she dragged me to watch this. She could’ve done this by herself.

    As Godmother Amy transformed Past Amy’s dress into an expensive gown, I could hear a car pull into the driveway.

    “Well, you can’t go to the dance in just any old car. Let’s fix that!” Godmother Amy said. She snapped her fingers and we all teleported outside.

    The car that pulled in was my car. I watched my past self and my girlfriend get out of the car, turn to the Amys, and give a weird look.

    “A Princess like you should be given a proper ride to the dance! But a simple car won’t do! Let’s fix this! Boppity Boo!” Godmother Amy shouted. She waved her wand at my car, and sparkles flew out. As the sparkles touched my car, I watched my car slowly contract, change shape, then expand into a coach!

    “Wait, what did you just do!” Past me and I shouted.

    “What the? What’s happening?” My girlfriend asked, completely in shock.

    “Wow it’s beautiful, Godmother. But how is it going to work? There are no horses, and there are no drivers!” Past Amy pointed out.

    “Oh don’t worry, we can fix that!” Godmother Amy said. She turned to my girlfriend and waved her wand. The sparkles flew from the wand onto her, and I saw her body change. She started to grow older, her clothes changed, and she grew. It also seemed like she went from a “she” to a “he”.

    “What did you just do to her!” Past me shouted.

    I stood there speechless. I mean, this was just an illusion, so none of this actually happened. But watching my girlfriend turn into a guy felt wrong.

    “There is our coachman! But ah horses! We need 2! But there is only one person here! Or is there?” Godmother Amy pointed out.

    The coachman, both Amys, and past me turned to look directly at me.

    “Wha? How did he just get here?” Past me asked, bewildered.

    I realized I was a part of the story now. And what did she just say? That we needed horses? Oh no.

    “Bippity! Boppity! Boo!”

    I stood still as I saw sparkles flying from Godmother Amy’s wand land on both me and my past self. I couldn’t tell what was happening to him, because I felt the changes start immediately.

    I felt a strong pressure form on my nose. It was as if it were being tugged on, harshly. It stretched out, pushing my eyes further apart. I could see from a wider point of view, but it was hard to focus right in front of me. A muzzle formed.

    I felt my spine start to crack. A sharp pain stuck me throughout my skeleton, reshaping itself and forcing me to fall forward.

    When my hands landed on the ground, they felt hard. I struggled to move my head down to see what happened, but I could tell that I just grew hooves. I also knew that I was stuck on all fours as my legs began to reshape.

    I felt a sharp pain from my tailbone. Suddenly, I felt movement on that spot. I must have grown a tail.

    My body became much more hairy and muscular as this transformation continued. Soon enough, my entire body was covered in fur. Some extra hair grew on the back of my neck, forming a mane.

    The transformation was complete, and I was in utter shock. Unable to adapt to my body, I felt myself jumping around on accident, acting uncontrollably.

    I felt a whip strike the air behind me, and I froze.

    Another whip stuck behind me, but this time, a harness formed from it, and wrapped itself around me.

    I realized then, that I was standing right next to another horse, presumably my past self, and right in front of the carriage.

    “Alright, you should be ready for the dance! And remember, win your true love’s heart with a kiss!” Fairy Amy shouted, then disappeared.
