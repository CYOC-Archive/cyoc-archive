    As the dear slept, the chemicals in her stomach moved into her bloodstream and
    changes began to take affect on the dears form.
    Her skin became stretchier, near unbreakable, as her heart and other muscles grew
    stronger without actually increasing in size. Her bones grew thicker and harder to
    break. And then the chemicals started kicking in to a higher gear.
    Her fat deposits, while few, were quickly multiplied and spread throughout her body.
    In moments every inch of her, under her skin, was covered with a thin layer of fat.
    Then the fat deposits started to grow.
    it was at this point the doe awoke.
    Standing a little unsteadily, due to her shifting center of weight she started to walk
    back to the stream, feeling thirsty again. As she dipped her head in the stream and
    started to drink more of the contaminated water, the chemicals began to act faster.
    Her belly bulged between her legs down to he knees, pushing her legs further apart.
    She hardly noticed, so great was here thirst now. She simple readjusted her legs and
    continued to drink huge mouthfuls of water.
    It wasn't until she felt he face slowly moving out of the water that she felt something
    was wrong. Looking at herself it was amazing to see that she hadn't noticed all four
    hooves of her's were now suspended in the air due to her huge gut. But the rest of
    here had grow as well, Her legs now looked more like elephant legs, they were so
    thick and nearly covered her hooves. Here tail had been swallowed by her growing
    butt, each cheek now the size of a beach ball. Her neck had reached the size of a car
    tire and here cheeks were the size of softballs.
    There was no way she'd ever be able to move again, with this weight.
    Amazingly, this hardly registered to the doe, who by now was feeling a, beyond
    urgent, need for more water. But she had no way of reaching it.
    What's she to do?
