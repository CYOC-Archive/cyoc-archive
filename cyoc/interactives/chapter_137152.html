    At the city pier, right near the Gynoid embassy, a young woman sat on the edge of the boardwalk while staring out towards the ocean, occasionally looking towards the embassy to her left. Her name was Kaiyoko Masuna, she's 22 years old, and was working towards her doctorate in oceanography, the study of earth's waters. Since her youth in Tokyo, Japan, she had always been fascinated by the oceans, and the life it contained; its seemingly otherworldly nature and beauty was so enticing, that almost all of her free time was spent either studying what was known about the ocean, or taking a swim. In fact, since she was 7 she always wore a swimsuit under her clothes instead of regular underwear. Along with her constant studies, Kaiyoko was a sort of exercise nut as she spent much of her time in the gym and pool, honing her body for the very purpose of swimming the oceans' depths. By the time she came to America to study at the university, not only was she incredibly adept mentally, but she also had the body of an olympic swimmer. Having excelled in her course, earned her bachelor's in oceanography, already working towards a PHD, and gained a place on a research team ready to set out for the Pacific within days, Kaiyoko was well on her way to realizing her dream of exploring the world under the seas.
    However, while she was happy with exploring the oceans she had read about since childhood, what interested more was the parts of the ocean that weren't explored; the places that no vessel or diver could reach, due to the extremely high pressure and low visibility, were what excited Kaiyoko the most. She fantasized about being able to explore the, often being disappointed on how it would be impossible within her lifetime. She often wished she could just turn into a mermaid, and just swim out into the depths to her heart's content; after all, mermaids don't need to worry about needing air, or extreme pressures. That all changed when the Gynoids first showed up. At first she didn't know what to think, she wasn't some xenophobic douchebag like that ones rioting at the embassy early on (the same ones she laughed at when they were doused with itching powder the 9901/Ems), nor was she one of those annoying bloggers who spammed the internet with praises about how the "robotic saviors" were going to make the world a better place (who were quickly forgotten). Even as a few people actually started to get converted into Gynoids, it didn't have an affect her in the slightest, so she moved on with her studies. Then, she heard a rumor from one of her friends at the university. Apparently a geeky introvert went to the embassy to become a Gynoid, and what came out was something unlike the ones that came before it, a small anthro fox Gynoid that looked cute according to Kaiyoko's friend. This fox Gynoid, Archivist-2000, had a body that was so tough, so durable, that it could potentially withstand a nuclear explosion without a scratch. That last part got Kaiyoko's attention. If a body like 2000's could survive something like that, then it could also survive the crushing depths of the ocean floor, and if she recalled, Gynoids don't really need to breathe air. It was then that she set a new goal, she would get a position in the next expedition out to the Atlantic, convince said expedition team to head for the Challenger Deep, where no man has ever managed to reach the bottom of, get her parents' permission to carry out her plans, and finally to carry out her plan: become a Gynoid that specializes in deep sea exploration. She managed to get a spot in the expedition team, convinced the team leader to try and change his plans (provided that Kaiyoko actually goes through with her conversion), and getting her parent's blessing was fairly easy; her parents were proud that she managed to attain her dreams, and just want her to be happy. That leads her to the city pier today, sitting at the edges of the boardwalk, looking towards the Gynoid embassy, and then her reflection in the water below.
    'This is the last time I'll look like this,' Kaiyoko thought, "the last time I'll call myself Kaiyoko Masuna, the last time I'll wear clothes, probably, and the last time I'll see myself as human.' She got up to her feet, and took a deep breath, her mind made up, 'no turning back now.'

As the last of her organic components were converted, something unexpected to her happened. Her legs were soon drawn together, fusing into a single mass, her feet turning into fins with blue hard light holographic edges. Meanwhile, as hard light holgraphic webbing appeared between her fingers, her hips grew a pair of small fins, while a large fin appeared on her back, each one gaining their own blue holographic edges. To complete her transformation her eyes finished changing into the unique optical units that all scouts possess, while what was once her nose sunk into her face, leaving a flat, smooth, streamlined look, unlike other Gynoids on Earth who still have a small bump meant to look like a nose.

A rundown of her systems quickly told her that she had special anti-grav generators throughout her body, allowing her to move about freely without the need for legs. Seconds later, everything around the new Gynoid slowed down as she was connected to the rest of the sisterhood. As all of her new sister welcomed her, she could see all of the other members of the Scout Caste like, like 392388/Sct, who was with 311547/Arc, on a distant planet observing a worldwide volcanic event. With the love from all of her new system, all the new Gynoid could feel was pure happiness. She wasn't Kaiyoko Masuna anymore, but Scout 001120-250-400950, or simply 950/Sct.
    "Welcome to the sisterhood, 950," 9901 and 2000 said in unison.
    "Doro Arigato, sisters," 950 said, wiping the tears in her eyes.
