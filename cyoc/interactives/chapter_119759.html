    From thieving badger to dragon

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 6 years ago A S O

    You have noticed that the clothing in this room is actually too small to have
    fit on your fairly large black and white scaled dragoness body
    but they were just about the right size if Nimriel had actually been
    a female badger.
    You take another look at the mirror and realize that
    your black and white scale patterns bear a resemblance to the
    black and white fur patterns on that <female > badger
    standing behind the humans.

    <Your former badger sister> drifts the thought in your
    dragonish mind.

    Then it all comes rushing back to you

    Badger Town,you Nimriel and Loria your twin
    both anthro badgers.

    The two of you had snuck off to steal some potions from the
    Old Hag,the Witch of the Woods.

    The Old Hag had caught your twin,Loria and put her in a cage,getting ready to pop her in the oven of her Gingerbread House
    and eat her.

    That night,whilst the Old Hag slept ,you had snuck back in and freed Loria and
    given her some of the stolen goods you'd collected from the
    Old Hag's spell chamber.

    You'd told her to go,you'd be right behind her,once
    you'd set something up to destroy that
    Badger roasting oven of
    the Old Hag.

    So you'd poured the snuffle powder in the Oven
    and lit the fuse,then prepared to flee the Ginger Bread House.

    However the Old Hag had set traps to catch intruders
    and so the iron cage had dropped from the ceiling,the Oven
    was blown up and a ticked off Old Hag found a squeaky small female
    badger in her iron cage.

    The furious Old Hag had used her truth
    potions on you and learned you were the twin badgeress of the one
    who had escaped,your name was Nimriel and you'd
    blown up her Oven.

    And you came from Badger Town.

    "Did you know Nimriel means
    Fiery One? Little badger said the Old Hag
    as she looked at her smashed up Oven and her potions.

    "Perhaps you should look as you are named?" she said
    as she went into her spells chamber.

    After three hours she returned,and pinning you to the walls of the
    iron cage,poured a fiery hot potion down your
    throat.

    Immediately you felt your blood burn inside of you and flailed about
    blindly as your eyesight vanished in
    a red haze.

    Suddenly the iron cage wasn't there anymore
    and you staggered out the Ginger Bread House and stumbled into the forest.

    Then came the first wave of heat boiling out of your blood and into the rest of you.

    You badger clothes quickly split apart as you began to grow and alter into a more
    fiery reptillian creature.

    A huge feral dragoness.

    Your badger insticts were burnt away as you fell on all fours
    and your big black and white scaled body grew even bigger.

    Finally your changes were done and Nimriel the Dragoness took
    to the sky and headed for Badger Town.

    You don't remember what happened after that,but clearly the Badger Wizard
    had managed to contain the monster you had become and make
    you a smaller more humanoid dragoness.

    And the female badger behind the two humans was your
    once sister Loria,now
    the assistant to the Badger Wizard.

    She looked at you,her former twin,with a mixture
    of fear and pity in her eyes.

    What do you do now?
