    Welcome to A-Game-Of-Change: Online:

    This room is welcome to all users.

    — — —

    A Game of Change is a niche party game that features a number of Change-Related punishments. This game is perfect to play with FRIENDS or even with complete strangers.

    The online version of A Game of Change has been optimised to play with a group of at least 2 players. It’s suggested that the game is played with around 3-5 players for the best experience.

    Different from most other experiences on CHAT-ROOM-2006, we have decided to make two versions of A Game of Change.

    The game, like the board game, allows the winner of the game to make a single WISH. The wish has absolutely no limits, and will not be reversed alongside the rest of the changes at the end of the game. It is completely up to the user, but the game with not end until the WISH has been made.

    — — —

    Once the user has confirmed their CONSENT by pressing:

    CONSENT

    They will be given the choice to join two different versions of A-Game-Of-Change: Online:

    FRIEND-MODE

    or

    RANDOM-MODE

    —

    Friend Mode allows users to enter a chosen code to allow up to 8 of their friends to join. Once all players press:

    READY-TO-PLAY

    The game will begin.

    Once all users have begun the game, there is no leaving until the game is complete. If a player tries to leave, they will be punished and suffer consequences.

    — — —

    The board had 35 tiles, which means that were will be at least 5 rounds, however it is possible that there could be more.
    Each player rolls a single 6 sided die that determines how far their figure will move. Once the player has rolled, a single card will appear on their screen alone. They can choose to read the card aloud, or keep it to themself. Of course, all players will be able to see the change take place, especially if it is physical.

    — — —

    The game has essentially no limit, but must be played in it’s entirety, otherwise it will not end. The game is potentially infinite in length, but in real time it will never last more than three hours at a time.

    The rules remain the same as the original, and cheating will result in serious punishment.

    It is encouraged that players work together, not against one another.

    — — —

    If a user is not satisfied with what happened in their game, they must refer back to the rules. All users are CONSENTing to whatever happens during their session. This is why we suggest getting to know the users you are playing with before pressing READY-TO-PLAY.

    — — —

    Please make sure to read the rules very carefully as to understand the Do’s, Don’t’s, and Risk’s that follow consenting.

    All changes, aside from any that may result from the final WISH, will be reversed as long as rules are followed.
