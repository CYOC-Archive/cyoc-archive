    The rest of the day goes by pretty quiet, Dean has locked himself in the men’s restroom and wouldn’t come out, the 16-year-old is crying and feels so humiliated. When “Amy” hears about this, she does feel a little bad, ‘maybe I went to far,’ she asks herself, ‘nah, I think I was actually to easy going, I should have given him a pig tail too! He really is a “pig” and perv, I never really noticed how bad he is, but as “Amy” I really felt disgusted and creeped out by the way he stared at me! Well, tomorrow everything will be as it was before and no one will remember it, for Dean, it’ll feel as though it all had been nothing but a realistic nightmare.’

    While “Amy” is in her thoughts, Sarah feels the urge to use the lady’s restroom, ‘this would be my chance to get that App,’ she thinks to herself. The girl raises her hand, “miss, I’d need to use the lady’s room,” she says, “may I be excused?”

    “Sure,” the teacher, Miss Williams, says, “but make it quick!”

    Sarah gets to her feet, “yes, miss,” she says and heads out the classroom.

    When Sarah enters the lady’s restroom, she hurries into a stall and takes out her phone, which she always stows in her bra. After searching the App-store, she finds the App version of the Chronivac, smiling to herself, the girl downloads and installs it, ‘now all I need to do is to scan Jeff,’ she thinks to herself, ‘should I scan him while he’s “Amy” or should I wait until he’s back to his normal self? Hm… I think I’ll do both, I just hope that when I scan him as “Amy”, that it will somehow be recognizable as a “fake”, so that I don’t accidently do something to the original, because I’m planning to scan the original too.’

    After Sarah returned to her class and sat down next to “Amy”, she looks over at the smaller “girl”, that is when she notices that “Amy” seems to be bothered by something, she decides to ask her later after school.

    “Amy”, unaware that Sarah now has the App herself, is still deep in thought, but this time she is thinking about something else. ‘Man, I wish I were so wealthy like Sarah and the real Amy,’ the little “girl” thinks to herself, ‘then I wouldn’t always have to buy second hand clothes, and my phone is also falling apart,’ she seems to not understand, that with the App she could make herself wealthy.

    As the two are heading to the mall a few hours later, they don’t talk much until Sarah can’t take it anymore, “what’s the matter, Jeff,” she asks the “girl” by her real name, which doesn’t matter since there is no one nearby.

    “Amy” looks up at Sarah, “what do you mean,” she asks.

    Sarah scowls, “don’t give me that BS, Jeff,” she says, “I can tell that something is bothering you. So, what’s up?”

    “Oh,” the “girl” says, “it’s nothing, really. I… I just feel that I’m out of my league.”

    “Come again,” Sarah says.

    “Amy” sighs, “you, your friends… Amy,” she says, “all of you are wealthy. You all are able to live in luxury, always able to afford the newest tech, expensive clothes, jewellery… anything that you desire you are able to get. Me… I have nothing, my family is not really swimming in money, like yours or Amy’s. All I can afford are second hand and outdated clothes, and my phone is starting to fall apart,” the “girl” lets out a sigh and hangs her head, “I wish I had such an amazing life, not having to worry about stuff.”

    “You can easily change that,” Sarah says, “you have the App! I mean, if it can transform you into a girl, surely it can make you and your family rich!”

    “Yes,” the “girl” says, “but everyone would ask where all the money suddenly came from.”

    “Reality alteration, might be a way to make it seem that you have always been rich,” Sarah says.

    “True,” “Amy” replies, “but… I don’t know… it feels wrong to do.”

    “Transforming into a girl isn’t? Or giving your friend the snout of a Pig?”

    “Transforming myself into a girl was only to cheer you up, I did it for you, not for me,” “Amy” replies, “as for Dean… he deserved it. But if I were to use it, just to gain wealth… it just feels like I’m “cheating”. I’d rather work hard to EARN the money, than to magically become rich.”

    “That makes sense,” Sarah says, she then sighs, “look, Jeff. I AM actually enjoying my day with you, you really did cheer me up. So, do NOT ruin it with your “down phase”, just enjoy the day and be happy that your plan worked.”

    “Amy” smiles up at Sarah, “you are right, Sarah,” she says, “I’m actually really happy myself, because of me transforming into Amy, I actually got to know you better. Yeah, you are a mean bitch, BUT you are also caring… what I want to say is: I am starting to really like you.”

    Sarah smiles, “I care about people I like, so before you ask: yes, Jeff, I honestly like you too,” she says, “but THAT is just between you and me, just know: if you are back to normal, and I start being mean to you again, please do not take it personal!”

    “Amy” nods, not seeming to notice that Sarah instead of saying “when” had said “if”, “I won’t,” she says, “I understand: when I’m back being “Jeff”, we’d need to continue as we did before today, so I get it, otherwise people would question why we are suddenly good with each other.”

    Sarah nods, “smart girl,” she says, both smile at each other, “now, enough of this, let’s get to the mall,” the little “girl” nods and they continue on their way.

    “Thanks, Sarah,” “Amy” says, smiling up at the tall girl, Sarah simply smiles back and winks at her.
