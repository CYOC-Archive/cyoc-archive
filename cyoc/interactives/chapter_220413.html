The displays prevented them from seeing too far back into the store, but they couldn't see any other customers. They stood just inside the entrance looking for anyone who worked there, a service desk, or anything. As they looked around, a voice spoke up behind them.

    "Yes, we did have some unexpected fun, but..." John looked toward Katie to finish her story.

Esme reached over and examined Katie's hands as she continued the story. "So I took the gloves off to adjust something and then put them back on. After I took the gloves off again a couple hours later, my arms and hands didn't change back when we expected. It's been well over 12 hours now, but I only wore the gloves for a little while. Is there a way I can get my old arms and hands back?"

    Esme pulled out another copy of the instructions from within the counter. She muttered to herself for a moment as she read the sheet. "Ah, here it is. 'After removing the items, effects of the magic will remain for as long as the item was worn at which time the wearer will revert to their original form.'"

    The woman nodded. "Yes, yes. I can see how you would think that. We really should get these instructions updated and clarified. When you took off the gloves, it started the countdown until your arms would revert to how they originally were. However, you interrupted that countdown by putting the gloves back on. This created a... A 'short circuit' if you will. The process of reverting your arms was underway, but you had put the gloves back on which conflicted with that process."

    "A short circuit?" repeated John.

    "Yes. It's hard to explain the mechanics behind the magic to those unfamiliar with the art, but imagine you were driving down the road at highway speeds and then shoved your car into reverse. It's a crude analogy, but that's essentially what you've done." Esme looked under the countertop for a moment. "Now let me see here..."

    John and Katie looked at each other wondering what Esme was doing or if she got distracted.

    Esme stood up and held a mitten shaped like a cat's paw which was stuffed to appear somewhat puffy and cartoon-like. "This will do.
    Here, let me show you." She handed the glove to John. "Please put this on."

    John glanced at Katie and took the paw. He pulled it on his right hand.

    "Okay, now we wait a moment," instructed Esme. The three of them sat silently looking at John's hand inside the mitten. They could see the paw move a bit as John moved his fingers slightly. After about 20 seconds, Esme broke the silence. "Okay, that should be long enough. Please take off the paw."

    John did as instructed. As he removed the mitten, it appeared as if he was only removing an outer covering for the mitten and it no longer contained any stuffing. When the mitten was removed, it revealed that John's right hand had become an actual cat's paw very similar to the original appearance of the mitten. As the empty mitten sat on the counter, it slowly started to puff up again and returned to its original appearance.

    John wiggled his puffy claws. "Woah!" He felt the paw with his left hand. "I can feel this. This is actually me!"
