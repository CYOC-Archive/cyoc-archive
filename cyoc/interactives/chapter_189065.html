    Manny Calvo, the manager of the largest garbage dump in the state, returned home from his doctors after his check up.
    Manny was a 45 year old man with brown hair, blue eyes and a small brown moustache. He wore a white shirt under a brown Jacket, a striped tie, brown suit pants, and black shoes.

    His son, Andrew, an 11 year old boy with ginger brown hair, blue eyes, wearing a white t shirt and blue shorts and red socks, saw him coming in.
    "Dad,' he said, excitedly. "How was your check up?"

    "It went very well," Manny said. "However..."

    Manny sat down in his armchair, and sighed.

    "Is it serious, darling?" Asked his wife, Margaret, a 45 year old woman with blonde hair, glasses, and green eyes, wearing a blue dress, white apron, and brown house shoes.
    "Truth is, " Manny said, "I have been diagnosed with 'Morphosis H.D.'"

    "What's 'Morphosis H.D.', dad?" Asked Andrew.

    "Well," said Manny, "It's a very rare DNA ailment that allows the one who has it to be able to morph their body in any way they fancy, like changing into animals, monsters, and the like."

    "Wow!" Andrew said, excitedly. "Does this mean that you can really transform, dad?"
    "Yes, yes I can." Said Manny. "It is now my super power."

    "So go on, honey," said Margaret, just as excited as her son, "please show us a morphing!"
    "Well, since you said please, I will!" Manny said, just as excited as his wife and son.
