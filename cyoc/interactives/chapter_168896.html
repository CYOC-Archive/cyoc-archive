    Jake turns into an adult too

    added 3 years ago AP BM Muscle

    And there Jake was, alone in the hotel room, being the only one in his family that hasn't transformed. It was kind of ironic, Jake was looking forward to this vacation for months, and now he's getting cold feet?

    "Calm down, you're just overthinking this," the boy said to himself as he shook his head. There was no reason to suddenly be self-conscious when everyone here was transforming. All he really needed to think about was how he should transform himself now.

    Since he was in the mix room, there were so many options for him to choose, but no matter what, his mind wandered back to his now bigger and older brother's transformation. Yeah, being an adult sounded quite nice. Jake closed his eyes and imagined being able to be an adult and being able to do whatever he wanted.

    Jake wanted to imagine more, but he suddenly had to open his eyes as he felt his body stretching. Oops, it seems like Jake accidentally activated the room's transformation power. The young teen stumbled over to the mirror with his rapidly growing legs to watch himself grow into a young man. His whole body continued to stretch until it grew past the six-foot mark, around his brother's new height. His face lost its baby fat revealing a square jaw and some sharp-looking cheekbones. He even grew a small goatee on his chin and now looked exactly like he imagined himself being, a tall but thin man around 25.

    "Sweet," Jake said to himself in a now deeper voice. He was quite happy about his new appearance, but it needed one more change, a change that Jake was going to have fun with. The now young man took off his shirt to see a trim and toned body, that won't be that for much longer. Imitating the poses his Elliot needed to learn, Jake raised his arms up and flex them, and as he flexed, his biceps grew bigger.

    "Nice, but these pythons need to be bigger." So, on that cue, Jake flexed his arms harder, causing his biceps to grow until they were at least 17 inches. Satisfied with how big his biceps were, Jake turned to his side and flexed his triceps until they were proportionate to the rest of his arms. Next came the pecs, and he flexed and grew them, perhaps a bit too big, but he loved how round and strong they looked. After that, the man faced forward again and did the lat spread pose, watching as his back strengthened and his lats forming into strong-looking wings. Jake then put his hands over his head and flexed his abs, watching as eight defined tabs pushed out of his once flat stomach. Finally, Jake performed the final pose, the most muscular pose, and the muscles that he didn't grow before grew and completed his transformation.

    "Haha! Damn, that was fun. I may not be as big as Elliot, but I'm at least ten times hotter. Now, what should I do next now that I'm an adult?" Choices, choices. Well, there was a beach at the resort, plenty of fun to have there and show off his bod. Oh, he could also go to the bar now that he was old enough, they probably had some weird transformation related drinks.

    Not only that, but he could always meet up with Elliot or his parents. He had to wonder if they would even recognize him like this. And of course, he can always enter that contest Elliot entered.

    Ah, so many things to do, but he had plenty of vacation time to do it.
