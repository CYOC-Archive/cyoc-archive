    Paul decided the best test subjects were the nerds sitting on the bleachers. He took a look at them through the camera and he recognized them. One was this dork named Robbie who was always talking about Pokemon or some other lame video game. He was a 15 year old latino kid with greasy, wavy black hair and pimples all over. He always wore a vest and stupid dress pants. The kid next to him was his friend Danny who was nerdier than him. Danny was a blond, slightly overweight kid who always wore t-shirts slightly too small for him and gross jeans.

    Paul thought back to his time in school and at first he didn't really want to change them. After all he never really hated them or anything, they were just dorks. Then Paul thought harder and realized another gift from the camera. He could now pull up any memories he wanted from "Paulette's" life. After a quick look down changing room memory lane, Paul thought about how the nerds interacted with Paul. He now remembered they would never leave Paulette alone as they would fantasize about her boobs constantly.

    Even if it wasn't necessarily his life, Paul still felt irked by their pervy nature. Paul, ignoring the hypocrisy, re-aimed the camera at the two boys and took their picture. The changes happened so fast that Paul almost couldn't see them. Robbie's hair became softer as it sprouted past his shoulders. His facial features grew more delicate as his face became covered in makeup. His hips wildly expanded as his butt grew tremendously. Decent breasts formed from his chest as his clothes morphed into a dress as his man parts clearly vanished.

    Meanwhile, Danny's changes were far more drastic. His body shrunk to half of that of the new girly Robbie. His blond hair grew started to sprout out across his body as his face extended. His clothes vanished as a collar appeared around his neck and a tail sprouted from his back. In mere seconds he had become a yellow lab sitting next to a teenaged girl. Paul snickered as he listened in to something Robbie was saying.

    "I can't wait to be a cheerleader Daisy. Just imagine it, Rosita the head cheerleader! Of course, after Paulette leaves the school." She happily said to the dog, who was not paying attention but rather licking itself.

    Paul silently chuckled at the power he now had. He still remembered Robbie and Danny, but he could also remember the younger Rosita and her cute puppy Daisy. Paul stopped laughing as he realized what "Rosita" had said.
    "Oh shit." He said. "I'm the head cheerleader."

    Suddenly, from behind, a girl's voice surprised him.
    "Like duh girl! Where have you been?" She joked.

    Paul turned to see a cute African American girl. She was taller than "Paulette" and had a skinny killer body to boot. Her curly black hair was tied up in a bun and her makeup made her look great in her cheerleading uniform. Paul remembered her as the head cheerleader Brandy, but looking into Paulette's memories revealed that in this new reality, Paulette was head cheerleader and Brandy was her bff. It made Paul sick just thinking about being head cheerleader, let alone being friends with the entire squad.

    "Hey P? Whatcha got there?" She asked pointing at the camera.

    Paul panicked realizing he was still holding it, and after what he had seen of it he couldn't risk people knowing about it at all. Paul knew he had to act fast.
