    Fight the Road Rashers

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by loony28 13 years ago A BM I

    "I don't want to be taken as a trophy," Ariel said shivering a bit, "so I say we go with
    the Road Rashers."

    "I add my vote to battle the Road Rashers," Alex said shaking her head.

    "I vote we accept the Shadow Cutters' challenge," Huang said with a wicked smile.

    "I also say that we accept the Shadow Cutters' challenge," Tim said taking out a circuit
    board from the radio.

    "Well it looks like your's is the deciding vote sis," Ariel said looking daggers at Tim and
    Huang.

    "I don't like it when we put our club in jeopardy so I'm voting we accept the Road
    Rashers' challenge," Karen said turning around and looking at each of them. "We'll be
    meeting them at the city park in an hour." She turned around and sent the confirmation
    reply.

    An hour later they were assembled in the city park and they looked across at the Road
    Rashers. They had fifteen members and since it was a club challenge they would all
    be fighting at the same time. At least the odds were better than they would have been
    if they had accepted the Shadow Cutters' challenge.

    "Well we might as well get this over with," Karen said with a sigh pulling out one of her
    stones from her pouch. She placed it in her ring and a cloud of dust obscured her for a
    dew moments. When it had cleared Karen had transformed into a Diamond Golem.

    A cloud obscured Alex and she transformed into a Harpy.

    Fire erupted around Huang and a Fire Boy emerged.

    Ariel was submerged in a column of water and when it lowered a Water Girl, who was
    water in the shape of a girl, was standing in her place.

    Tim transformed into a vampire in a flash of light.

    On the opposite side there now stood a Stone Golem, a Sand Golem, a Metal Golem,
    a Wind Dragon, a Cloud Boy, a Giant Eagle, a Fire Wolf, a Fire Girl, a Fire Man, a
    Mermaid, a Merman, a Water Sprite, a Zombie Girl, a Zombie Man, and a Zombie
    Ghost.

    The Zombie Man came forward and said, "Let's battle."

    Karen nodded then the battle started.
