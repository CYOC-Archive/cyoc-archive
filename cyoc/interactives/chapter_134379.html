    Daniel glanced around at his options and frowned slightly. Although all of the food girls looked delectable, none of them seemed to appeal to him. Then it hit him: why didn't he just do something different? Daniel turned to Laura and asked, "Is it possible to do a donut?"

    Laura paused for a moment, then nodded as she reached into her lab coat's pocket. "Absolutely," she responded, pulling out what seemed to be a small remote and showing it to Daniel. "If you'll just step into the center of the room, we can begin," Laura continued. Daniel repositioned himself to the center of the room and asked, "Now what?" Laura smiled, a slightly mischievous glint in her eyes, and announced, "Now we begin." Laura pressed a button on the remote.

    "THOOM!"

    Abruptly, four pure white walls slammed down around Daniel. "Woah!" Daniel yelped, "What the heck?!" "Don't worry; this is just a part of the process!" came the voice of Laura. Daniel glanced up to see Laura waving at him from a window. "This is also a part of the process!" Laura disclaimed, holding up the remote and pressing another button.

    "SCHUNK SCHUNK!"

    With that sound, tens of small pipes emerged from the walls of Daniel's chamber. "Uh.... What are those?" Daniel asked cautiously. "Part of the process," Laura stated distractedly as she hit another button.

    "POOMF POOMF!"

    Out of nowhere, three giant globs of a doughy substance smacked directly into his chest and stomach. "Oof!" Daniel blurted as he took a few steps back. "POOMF!" Another doughy glob slammed into Daniel's face, knocking him flat onto the ground. "All part of the process!" Laura called again, that mischievous smile now widening. "Mmmm?!" Daniel murmured from beneath the glob. "POOMF!" A glob slammed straight between Daniel's legs, causing him to bolt upright and squeal, "MMMMMM!" "Ooooo..." Laura groaned, "Ouch."

    Daniel stumbled forward a few steps, then collapsed face-first to the ground. "POOMF POOMF!" Two more globs promptly slammed into Daniel's butt, pushing him a few inches forward. This went on for a few minutes, and by the end of it, Daniel was buried under a pile of doughy globs. "Now for the next part," Laura said, hitting yet another button on the remote.

    "PCHUNK!"

    The floor beneath Daniel suddenly slid away, revealing what seemed to be a gigantic deep fryer. "Mmmmm?!" Daniel murmured, suddenly feeling oddly weightless. "All a part of the process!" Laura shouted as the doughy bundle that was Daniel tumbled into the pit. "SPLOOSH!"

    The first thing that Daniel felt was the heat; that extreme, broiling heat that surrounded him completely. It felt like he was being cooked! Then the sensation dulled, and Daniel became acutely aware of a sort of swelling sensation. It wasn't unpleasant of painful; it simply felt odd, yet relaxing in a way. His body was changing, swelling, shaping itself into a form completely different to its predecessor. And then, it abruptly ended.

    Out of the white walls of the room emerged a singular mechanical claw. The claw reached into the fryer and fished out a vauguely humanoid figure, a feminine one at that. "Urgh...." the figure groaned in a familiar voice, "What was that?" "A part of the process, Daniel!" Laura called once more, pressing yet another button on her remote. Another mechanical arm burst out of the wall, grabbing ahold to the Daniel's arm while the other mechanical claw shifted its grip to the other arm.

    "FWOOMP!"

    Suddenly, a hose burst out of the wall, shoving itself straight into Daniel's mouth. "Mmm?!" Daniel muttered. "Process!" Laura exclaimed giddily as she hit three more buttons. The hose began to pump a sweet-tasting fluid into Daniel's mouth, forcing his body to swell even more. At the same time, two more mechanical arms popped out of the walls, each armed with various toppings, and descended upon Daniel's body. "Mrrgh!" Daniel gurgled, panicking slightly. The hose pumped gallons of the liquid into Daniel's mouth while the arms whipped around his form so fast that he was barely visible.

    Abruptly, the mechanical arms and the hose sucked themselves back into the walls, allowing the confused Daniel to flop to the ground. "Urgh..." Daniel gurgled, slowly rising to his feet. "Now for the finishing touch!" Laura exclaimed, hitting one last button. "PCHUNK!" A reflective wall of glass burst out of the ground directly in front of Daniel. "Mrgh!" Daniel burbled, stumbling a few steps back. What Daniel saw was... disconcerting, to say the least.

    The figure in the mirror was quite obviously female, with her huge breasts, thick thighs, and bubbly behind, but she looked pretty chubby. Her skin was a deep tan, like fried dough, and it seemed sorta doughy. Her shoulders and face seemed to be dusted with powdered sugar and her stomach and head had a coat of glaze with sprinkles. Daniel peered at the image for a moment, then gurgled. That woman was him, straight down to the sweet substance dripping out of his mouth.
