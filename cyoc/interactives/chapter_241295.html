    You stop eating the fruit for a bit, and turn to see another person inside the tent. And what a person! It's a woman, very much attractive and dressed in an oriental set of clothes that really, really become her. Decked in Middle-eastern veils, smacking of exoticism, the dark-skinned and black-haired young woman is staring at you with dark eyes, just as beautiful as the rest of her body and possessing long lashes. All her jewels glint in the light and jingle softly. And her cleavage is just too much not to notice. She is so beautiful... You can only stutter and gape. Oh no... You really hope you are not in any sort of trouble... Will she be angry at you eating the fruit? Luckily, she looks at you and smiles wildly.
    "Well, someone is hungry," she says in a sweet voice and a little laugh. "Are you enjoying the fruits?"
    "Yes..." she answer slowly, relief enveloping you as you understand that the gorgeous woman is not at all furious with you. "They are... good... Very good..."
    You can feel juice from your meal still dripping along your chin. You wipe yourself with your sleeve, not really caring. You just want to look at the oriental woman.
    "I am very happy you like them!" exclaims the woman with enthusiasm. "Eat them! Eat more! Eat all of them! They are here for that very purpose!"
    What luck! You start eating more and munching them with happy grunts.
    "Oh, and what big feet you have!" she says in amazement.
    You stare at you feet, still unsure if they are the ones you had before. Your thoughts appear fuzzy to you anyway. You move your big feet and long toes a little. You just worry whether the beautiful lady will be disgusted by them.
    "How funny! Are you... ticklish?" the woman says mischievously.
    Without waiting for an answer, she scratches lightly with her long painted nails under your toes, creating a loud laughter from you.
    "You like that?" she says without departing from her wide smile.
    You nod fervently. Yes, yes, yes! Being tickled is funny! Especially by her!
    "Eat all the fruits you want and I'll tickle you more," says the woman. "How would you like that? You can even stay with me!"
    You nod again, overjoyed. What a great thing to do! Eating fruits and being fussed over by this beautiful woman! Eagerly, you gorge yourself on more fruits, unaware in your altered state that more transformations are hitting you, all under the gaze of the woman. This woman so beautiful and so happy at having a new pet.
