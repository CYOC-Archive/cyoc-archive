    Spark felt a little uncomfortable at first after she had gotten dressed and gone to the court house. It was one thing to meet a few people, but it was entirely different thing to meet an entire family.

    "I'm a little nervous to be meeting everyone." Spark said to Connor as they approached the court house.

    "Now you know how I felt with your family." Connor said as they went inside. Connor had opted to wear a white buttoned shirt collar with a tie while Spark wore a green informal dress. Miles and Miranda had decided to go home after their trip while Rei and Nat accompanied Connor and Spark to the court house.

    "Oh, hold on, you didn't straighten your tie right." Spark said looking at Connor's tie. Rei and Nat thought this was a cute moment between the two of them and watched the situation play out more.

    "There, now how's my hair? Do you think I should have done more? How about the dress? Should I have dressed a little more formal? Should I have worn more make up or less?" Spark continuously asked as Connor held his girlfriend's hands tightly.

    "You look amazing, and they'll love you." Connor reassured her. They found Nina and Jacob sitting on a bench with Nina patting her stomach and slowly swaying back and forth.

    "How are the twins mom?" Connor asked.

    "They're quiet for now. They were a little active while you were gone. Speaking of, I can't wait to see you in a wedding dress Spark." Nina said with a smirk as Spark blushed red thinking about the dance.

    "So, where's Dr. Harris's family?" Spark finally asked.

    "With Dr. Harris, guess I should start calling her Julia, she is going to be my sister in-law." Jacob said.

    "So why did your brother and Dr. Harris want a court house wedding and not a traditional wedding?" Rei asked as the Moreland couple.

    "You could say I've been a bit of influence in that department." Rick Moreland said coming up to the group. "We'd only been dating each other for half a year, finally I decided to propose to her. She said yes, but then she admitted that she didn't really want a whole extravagant wedding. With how much 'excitement' is in both of our lives with some of our relatives, we decided to just have a court house wedding." Rick explained.

    "That's really sweet. My parents had a court house wedding too." Nat revealed.

    "Really? You're mom is gorgeous and she didn't want to be seen in a wedding dress?" Rei asked.

    "Nah, my mom doesn't really like to parade her beauty." Nat answered.

    "Where's Caleb? Is he here yet?" Connor asked.

    "Your brother is running late, Gloria got all ready fast to come over, but had to wait on him." Nina said as she slowly stood up with Jacob trying to help her stand to his best ability.

    "I can't wait to see these little Harris kids. They'll be happy to have a new Aunt, though they might have trouble with the new last name." Rei said excitedly.

    "Oh yeah, that's the other thing. We're not taking one name, we're actually taking each other's names. So soon we'll be Mr. and Mrs. Harris-Moreland or Moreland-Harris, we haven't really quite settled on it yet." Rick admitted.

    "Come on, it's time you meet the rest of the soon to be family." Rick said as they went to meet up with the Harris family.

    Rei and Nat went ahead, helping Nina along the way while Connor and Spark lagged behind.

    "You still a little nervous?" Connor asked.

    "Y-Yeah, it's a little much all in just a few days." Spark said as they slowly made their way forward.

    "I think there's a lady married to one of Julia's brother's named Morgan who you could talk to about being part of a big family." Connor suggested.

    "I think I heard Gloria talk about her, she sounds nice." Spark said as she began to rub her neck.

    "Do you have a favorite number?" Spark asked randomly.

    "Number? Uh, no, why?"

    "For some reason, I like the number 3. I don't know why, it just sounds like a good number. Maybe I'll call my mom and ask her about it later." Spark said as they proceeded further into the court house.
