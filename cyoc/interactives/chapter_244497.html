    Damon saw everything start to change. Streets, lights & signs disappeared. More trees appeared. More grass & other fauna appeared. Buildings altered to caves or burrows or simply vanished. Then he felt himself start to change. He began shrinking as his body started changing. His feet shifted to those of a mouse. His hands became paw-like. His privates became rodent. He grew a long pink tail. His ears shifted becoming rounder & moved on his head. His nose shifted & his face pushed out to a snout as his teeth & tongue changed. His eyes shifted as greyish fur covered his body all for his feet, hands & tail. Then his body shifted in other ways completing his transformation. Damon was now anthromorphic grey mouse which was about 6 inches tail. Damon the now nameless anthromorphic grey mouse looked around himself sniffing & looking at himself. The anthro mouse was nameless sense his mom never gave him a name he knows of. She died before naming him as far as he knows. He noticed his house was gone only now being a small hole in the ground. The anthro mouse pleased decided to head out to explore the world he had created with his wishes; despite the danger to himself. He was a prey animal after all. He headed into what was once the city to find it was very primitive. It was not much more than a few crudely made buildings of mud, sticks & whatnot. He saw a few anthromorphics roaming around all naked as he. Also all of them were bigger than he. Being prey he felt uneasy around a lot of the bigger anthros. Eventually he saw a snake swallowing a rabbit & no one was really reacting to it. [So that part of wish works too.] The anthro mouse thought pleased. "Hey mouse."
