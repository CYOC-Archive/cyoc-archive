    You tell Lottie you're going to explore the latex factory.

    "Good idea!" Lottie beams at you. "Let's go! I bet I'll beat you to the Hivemind's main body!" Lottie flies off and flies through a window before you can say anything.

    As you trot to the large concrete building, you notice the pile of eggs from last night. The eggs have grown since then. Each egg was originally the size of a pear, now each one is the size of a large soccer ball. You make a mental note to move them to somewhere with more space as you enter the facility.

    Your body speaks up again. "Just so you know, you're what's known to the hive as a "resistor". Resistors are uncommon people who can resist the transformation, but when changed, they make valuable assets to us as a whole. Their final stage of growth is stronger than most others, and any eggs a resistor bears will already be at the final stage of growth, as well as genetically sharing your strength."

    After your body finishes telling you about your abilities, you ask it if there's anything you need to know about the Biolatex factory.

    "I know everything about the factory, but I'll only guide you to the important areas."

    You lose control of your body as it starts dashing back and forth through the hallways, past many other latex creatures, before stopping at some changing rooms. Peering inside the Ladies' locker room you see many pregnant latex animals and giant piles of latex eggs, all of various sizes and colors. Taking a glimpse inside the men's locker room, you see lots of latex animals having sex with each other. "These are the dens. We usually impregnate ourselves and lay eggs in here. You laid your eggs outside, but that's okay. Just lay them in here next time."

    Your body takes over again as it dashes down the halls, until you enter a giant room with two similarly-sized vats. Liquid latex is leaking out from cracks in the sides and into a small indent in the floor. A few latex animals are currently slurping up the latex as it flows into the hole. "Here's the feeding room. The vats are still almost completely full, despite leaking for a long time now. Moving on."

    Finally, you stop in front of a test chamber-looking thing. There are lots of latex animals around here for some reason, and out of the crowd, you spot Lottie, waiting for you. As she approaches, your body tells you a few more things. "And here is the Hivemind's main body. It's in the test chamber, the large pool of black latex. This is currently our only body, but another is being generated in the town we captured yesterday. Now, looks like your girlfriend has something to say."

    The voice disappears in your head as Lottie speaks up. "Heya. I guess you've taken the tour of the important parts of the facility, now?"

    You nod.

    "Hehe, good. Well, I beat you to the hivemind and now I'm bored again. Want to go into the forest and find a human so you can take that last stage of growth?"

    What do you do now?
