    It was embarrassingly easy to get a new girlfriend. You just walked up to Tiffany, the hot redheaded cheerleader you had always lusted over, and wished that she was in love with you. Tiffany turned out to be a little simple and conversations with her were a little dull. It was not like the intelligent conversation you previously had with Lauren. Lauren had been a sophisticated older woman, a profession newscaster before you changed her into your teenage girlfriend. You kind of missed her. But the fantastic sex with Tiffany really made up for her lackluster personality. You could always wish she was smarter later. But not too smart. You didn't want to lose control like you did with Lauren.

    After some particularly rousing storeroom sex, you and Tiffany walked hand in hand down the hallway to your next class. You passed by some of Lauren's teammates and crane your neck up to look at their faces. It was still a little shocking just how big they had become. You noticed that Tiffany looked down and avoided their gaze.

    "What's the matter?" you asked her.

    "Those basketball girls kinda scare me. They run the school, you know. Everyone wants to be on the team, but only the team captain Lauren can let you join, " she said.

    You rolled your eyes. What in the hell had Lauren done? Did she wish that the basketball team girls were the most popular girls in school? Or maybe that the team simply ran the school? You really were going to have to do something about her before she really got out of control.

    At the end of the hallway, the two of Lauren's teammates were staring at you and whispering. They didn't look happy and you doubted they were saying nice things. But when the bell rang they went back to class and left you alone.

    After school it was a different story. Lauren and her crew ambushed you and Tiffany in a deserted hallway.

    "What the hell are you doing with this little tramp?" she said, pointing at Tiffany.

    "I have moved on. So should you. Enjoy what I have given you and your friends but leave us alone," you said.

    "Leave you alone? Move on? I love you. We all do," she said motioning to the girls around her. "And we are never going to let you leave us."

    You immediately regret your wish to make Lauren love you, and the other girls to lust after you. It was kind of like that story - 'The chaser' - where that guy couldn't get rid of the girl after he gave her a love potion. You desperately wished that you could reverse at least one wish. But you knew it was impossible to reverse wishes. Instead you needed to think of something new to wish or it looked like Lauren was going to kill someone.

    What do you do now?
