    Another Victim is a Loki's Best Friend

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 16 years ago A BM

    The Loki walked down the sidewalk again wondering who to play with next. As he walked he passed a young girl playing tea with her dolls. The Loki paused mid step and walked back wards back to the little girl.
    "Excuse me little girl, who is that young lad standing there by the lawn mower"
    "Oh thats David, he lives next door to me." she said the Loki thought for a moment and made his decision as he sat down on one of the little girls toy chairs pushing a doll out of the way.
    "Only child?" the Loki asked picking up a tiny cup and sipped at the imaginary tea inside.
    "Ya, isn't he cute though?" she stated
    "Certainly reminds me of a puppy. Hold my tea I'll be back in a second." he said getting up leaving the girl to play with her dolls.

    The Loki walked up to the young teen desperately trying to get the lawn mower started again.
    "Hey, mower problems?" The Loki said as he walked to the mower and leaned over
    "Ya, it was working fine and then just stopped! Fucking piece of shit!" David said kicking the machine almost kicking the Loki in the face.
    "Hmmm, I think I see the problem..." the Loki says reaching under the mower, watching the teens ears begin to stretch and become pointed and his nails become dark and pointed.
    "What?" he said as hair grew connecting his brows together
    "theres this big stick stuck in here..." The Loki says as a big stick immediately appears stuck in the mower. "Fetch!" the Loki shouts pulling the stick out from under the machine and tossing it into the air above David's head.

    David's eyes went wide as he saw the stick fly over his head in almost slow motion. Turning around he ran for the airborne stick, halfway there giving up the two legged approach. As he ran Grey hairs began to grown down his arms and legs. As he leaped into the air catching the stick in his mouth a tail suddenly grew from his backside poking out from his pants curling up wards in a crescent. David walked back on two legs with the stick still in his mouth, before sitting with his tail wagging in front of the Loki. Then suddenly a look of realization went across his face as his tail stopped wagging and he spit out the stick. He opened his mouth to speak his tongue swelled and hung out of his mouth. He tried to speak, but his new tongue prevented him from forming words, so he barked.

    David was horror struck he was confused, terrified, and shuddered at the thought of a bath. As he stared and whined at Loki trying his best to come up with a sad puppy dog face, he felt something around his neck. Reaching to his neck he found his shirt gone now replaced by a thick ring of fluffy fur that extended from in between his pectorals to the back of his shoulders, but surprisingly the rest of him seemed almost hairless compared to it. His face felt vaguely the same except the big fluffy sideburns and the tiny nose. Reaching for his neck his now clawed hands felt a spiked leather collar wrapped around his neck and feeling the tag he could just barely make out the word 'DAVID'.

    Reaching over the Loki attached a leash to David's neck.
    "Don't worry boy, I'll give you a good home. And don't worry about how much of a freak you look like to everyone else you will just look like a rare breed of humanoid dog. Come on time for a walk boy." Loki said tugging at the collar.
