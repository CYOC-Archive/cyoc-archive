    “Planning a vacation is so much stress that you will need a vacation just from planning your vacation so let us at Magical Vacation plan the perfect vacation for you and your family.”

    That was the simple tag line for Magical Vacations the most top-notch vacation planning company in the world, they plan everything for any time of vacation. They do simple day trips to amusement parks or the beach to months long trip around the world and they have yet to get anything below a 4 out of 5 star review. They will plan for any size of trip be it a single person to whole families and church/business retreats. The best part is that they are rather cheap considering the work they do and some of wondered how they are able to make money but with such good word of mouth they have to do almost no advertising and somehow they are able to handle massive amounts of work on a small staff. The only thing odd about them was that part of the questionnaire they make people fill out before they plan the perfect vacation is incredibly detailed, things like height and weight for example and multiple likes and dislikes. They claim this to make sure everyone fits on certain rides or passes height limits for certain places. With that answer people shrug their shoulders and move on to enjoy their perfect vacation.

    What is not known is that Magical Vacations is a front, but not a front for organize crime or a shady government group but for a hidden world of magical creatures who stay hidden from the human world. They pass the information of those who sign up for magical vacations to their own people and every so often when a candidate or group that they like sign up for a vacation they are taken. They are taken to the magical world to become said creatures and because it was the Magical Vacation team who planned out the whole trips it was easy for them to make the disappearance appear to be random accidents. Getting lost in the woods, killed when a yacht sank at sea or a simple and common car crash all these methods and more where used.

    Now it was time for another person or people to be taken who just learned that their “Magical” vacation just turned into a new Magical life as a magical creature?
