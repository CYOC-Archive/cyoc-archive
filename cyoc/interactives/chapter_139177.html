    Enough messing around

    added 4 years ago A BM I O Reality alteration

    Kyle had seen enough. The actor Lena Headey was sitting on his couch, believing she was a male college student named Alex. Her former life was now occupied by a sentient purple dinosaur. Meanwhile, the former fictional character Barney's role was now being filled by Sarah Michelle Gellar, who was now nothing more than a costume. The character Supergirl was now always considered Korean, while Melissa Benoist was no longer an actor known for her role as Supergirl, but know from her child acting days on the the show Buffy the vampire slayer. And of course, his former suite mate was now seen as a female Korean actress.

    This thing was clearly powerful, and Kyle was ready to use it. He needed to get out of his shitty life, and live the life of someone else. The question was who. Kyle kept thinking "Maybe a celebrity? No too much drama. Someone rich? I guess but I don't want to be seen as some fart. Plus I don't want to do that much work."

    Kyle realized his best bet was to just randomly try someone's life and if he liked it, then he'd stay in it. Kyle looked out the window and saw plenty of candidates. There was a muscular joke talking with a ditzy blond. He'd kill to get that much attention from any girl, even if she was a bimbo.

    Then he saw a Spanish looking musician playing on his guitar near some high school kids touring campus. Kyle didn't know how he'd feel being seen as a different race, but if those skills were still with him, being a musician would be awesome. Or maybe even the high schoolers could be an option. Starting over could be fun.

    The he saw an older, but fit couple jogging around. He felt it'd be nice to be out of school and have a wife to boot. So many choices, and as some started to walk off, Kyle had to make a decision, and fast.

    He aimed at himself and then out the window.He wasn't the best long distance aim so he hoped he'd hit his mark. Who did Kyle hit, and inhabit the life of?
