As his hands were completing their transformation he looked into a reflective window, seeing his face starting to push out into a muzzle right before his eyes. The ears on the side of his head started moving up toward the top of his head, his nose slowly became black and wet, like that of a common dog. All the while the fur on his body filled out to a nice even coat.

His feet started the same transformation, becoming much shorter and harder to balance on, as well as his shifting legs, causing him to fall onto his hands (now paws), but it became easier for him, his legs shifted in favor of walking on all fours, which came naturally for him to just stand there as such.

He felt an odd sensation at the end of his spine, like something was pushing it's way through, he turned his head, which made most of his body shift, to look at his now fur covered tail, this entire situation was making him go nuts, he had no idea what he should do next, all he could do was watch it all change in front of him.

His size as oppose to length and basic mass, shrunk a good deal, stretching his neck in a classic dog format, he was now completely a dog. He let out a slight whine in dislike.

His tail suddenly shifted it's position to between his legs, he wasn't sure what to do now. That was when he noticed some more changes, the backyard shifted, there was a dog house in the distance and a few chew toys spread across the yard. A red collar had appeared on his neck and throughout the house, all of Will's belongings disappeared, his clothes, even his favorite cereal had vanished.
