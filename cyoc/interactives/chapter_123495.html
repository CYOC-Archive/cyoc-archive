    Josephine Bakes

    added 5 years ago BM

    As Jeff and his mom broke their hug she reached and squeezed her sons nose making it honk. Her name was Josephine (just picking one to make writing easier), and she couldn't help but smile even wider with the one on her face.

    BBBRRRRIIINNNGGGG!!!

    A sound went off in her dress pocket. She reached in and found a kitchen timer, but this one was clownified, looking like a children's toy timer that was red and yellow, the main colors shared between the family.

    Josephine giggled, "I guess my pot roast is done, i'll be downstairs, come down soon for dinner." Josephine thn tried to walk like she was used to, but found that she had no choice but to waddle. Her new large and comical feet wouldn't let her do anything else. When she got to the stairs, she had an idea, she hadnt done it since she was a little girl, but she couldn't resist. She sat on the had rail that guided the stair and a small balcony upstairs, and slid down it on her rump like Mary Poppins. When she got to the bottom she jumped off, dusted herself off, and put her hands to her large head of hair.

    She saw herself in the mirror for the first time and she thought she looked exctly like a goofy clown mom would look like. Jospehine put her hands to her head again checking that her red afro was just the right size and position, it felt like she was wearing a furry ball on her head, and then went off into the kitchen.

    Right behind her the house was changin as she walked through it. The walls seemed to follow her as they changed from their original colors to those of a clown family. The walls orinaly were a dull base color, but the soon became white with red and yellow polka dots. The photos on the walls changed into photos of famous clowns, even the recent family portrait changed until it was Josephine, Jeff and Amy, but all as clowns.

    Josephine went into the kitchen and took out a pair of oven mittens, she slipped them over her hands, but as soon as she did the mittens morphed into gloves, gloves that looked like the ones the Minnie and Mickey Mouse wore. She opened the oven door, saw her hands in the large cartoon gloves, and let out a laugh as she took the pot rost out of the oven. It looked the same, and smelled the same. Josephine thought though it wasn't quite right to just have this for the occasion. She put her large gloved fingers to her chin in a thinking pose, in the gloves the fingers were as large are german sausages, and inspiration struck. She got our a few bowls, flour, baking powder, and some other things, and began making a cake for Jeff, Amy, and her to all eat to celebrate their new clown lives.

    Back to Jeff, he and Amy were sitting down at his computer, what he had said was true they did not want to change back, they wanted to see what else the chronoviac could do.
