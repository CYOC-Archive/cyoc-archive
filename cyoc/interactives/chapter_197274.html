    Since Elephants wherr his favorite animal. Damon decided to go see the elephants as he heads to the enclosure.

    In a few minuets. He makes it to the elephant enclosure as a person walks by him. "Going to see the elephants?" He ask.

    "Yeah there my favortie animals." Said Damon. "Well you'll be wasting your time. They only have one elephant right now. A female. Said they'll get more soon." The person then left.

    "Well they'll have two Elephants soon." Damon comes to the Elephant enclosure. Indeed there was only one and she look lonely.

    "Poor girl. She'll have a mate soon." Damon goes into the cage. The door, to the enclosure, was unlock. Damon looks at the elephant as it look back.

    He then felt strange as the effects, of his wish, begins to work as Damon took off his clothes.

    Lucky there was no one around. His nose change first as it grew long and turn into his new trunk.

    Two tusk form below his mouth. He then grew, in size, as he was now the size of an elephant. His skin turn grey.

    He fell on four legs as his hands, and feet, become his new elephant feet. His ears grew large and became round.

    A tail then came out, from his butt, finishing his changes. Damon wrap his trunk, around the female elephant, as he told her to trust him.

    People then gather, after Damon's transformation, and wonder where the second elephant came from. The zoo staff begins to wonder as well.
