    Samantha's hellish Christmas

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 10 days ago BM O Magic

    Even that she saw all her options Samantha already knew what to pick. Being always a fan of dark magic, devils, and even the hell sounding like a place she would like to visit she was the complete opposite of her annoying and religious family and now she could be their worst nightmare, easily taking the black book with the devil symbol.

    She saw how the rest of the books disappeared in a pair of sparks magically after holding the book, then some strange noises and symbols came from it, emitting red and black particles, and not just that, the room started to shake. Seconds later a pentagram painted on the floor below her, shooting a beam of light that covered her but didn't last more than 5 seconds, after fading away she noticed her new appearance in front of the mirror.

    Her clothes were replaced by a way long dress that touched the floor, with a V-neckline that reached her belly, it seemed like it was made from red and black scales. But not only her clothes changed, her physical changed way more, her breasts grew to a pair of DD cups, a pair of black horns here on top of her head as her nails were replaced by small but really sharp black claws, lastly her hair grew almost covering her back, she loved her new witchy but demonic self.

    But then the room shook again, Samantha excitedly waited for what was left for her. Another but smaller pentagram appeared on the floor in front of her feet, this time it slowly pulled out a black and red sceptre with a devil skull on the top of it, she grabbed it as her new book was floating close to her and felt how she channeled her all mighty magic through the sceptre, during the process her lips turned black and her eyes turned the same colour with red pupils, she finished her transformation as a hell witch.

    Samantha couldn't believe one of her fantasies came true, how powerful she felt and none could stop her now, she was above everyone now. Begining to use her book she read through the pages in a really strange language she now could understand, she saw many spells such as curses, invocations and many pages left for the use of her powers, but there was one that caught her attention, the "army summoning".

    She recited the spell and shoot a red lighting on the floor with her sceptre. The spell made a crack on the floor that opened showing a orange glow with strange noises like the book made, like it was a direct door from hell. Samantha saw in excitement how many monsters and devilish creatures came from it and she could recognise the majority of them, demons like Baal, Baphomet or Beelzebub, creatures like succubus, hydras and cerberus were there too, even monsters representing the seven deadly sins, and way more creatures. All of them gathered around her and greeted her.

    "Hello, everyone, you must be the servants I just summoned", she casted a spell to make her room expand infinitely inside but looking normal from outside, also used her magic so none could hear her and her army.

    "Yessss, mistressssss" a hydra said.

    "Exciting... So I can summon all of you at once or individually in addition to my magic and spells"

    "Yes mistress" a black strong beast, actually a hell hound spoke, "you can also absorb us through the orb in your chest, this will allow you to transform yourself into us and even use our powers, you can also absorb more than one to combine your form and powers you acquire from us."

    "And you can release us back with the orb too" the sin of Envy added.

    "Ooooor... You can unleash all of us and set the chaos through all of this place!!" An imp laughed.

    "Interesting..." Samantha told herself, learning more and more about not only her powers but her whole army of devilish subjects. "Thanks to all of you, you'll certainly be useful for my master plan and take revenge to all the ones that rejected us!" The creatures cheered, going back through the crack until she was the only one left in the room, using her magic to seal the crack so none couldn't notice something weird there, planning to see her subjects again later.

    "I know what to do now, this Christmas will be unforgettable!" She said out loud, grabbing her sceptre to use her magic for her plan.

    What will Samantha do now?
