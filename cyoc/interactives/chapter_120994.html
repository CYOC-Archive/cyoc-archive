    A sweet chocolate bunny

    Suddenly, a Tim heard a voice from above again.

    ADDITIONAL MATERIALS DETECTED. BEGINNING SPECIES CONVERSION PROCESS.

    A large laser beam shot down from the roof and hit Tim right in the chest. A horrible tingle spread through his body and he dropped Anna-Egg in shock. Dazed and confused, he picked himself off the ground.

    "ugh...what was that..." he said, rubbing his head. However, his hand brushed against something atop his head. Long, furry, floppy. He poked and prodded and felt up the new appendages sticking out of his head before suddenly realizing what they were: Rabbit Ears. He now understood what the voice meant, he was going to turn into a bunny.

    "No! Please! I don't want to be a bunny!" He said as his nose began to twitch, turning pink and small. His two front teeth grew longer in his mouth, and his face pushed out into a tiny rabbit muzzle. He began to feel up his face, before his fingers started to twitch. The grew smaller and smaller into tiny, furless rabbit paws. Tims clothes began to feel loose on his body as he began to shrink smaller and smaller.

    He fell to the ground, now unable to stand on two feet. "I'm sorry, I'll leave! I won't tell anyone about the machine. You can keep Anna too, just don't make me a little bunny!" He cried. His feet began to hurt, before growing long and puffy, bursting out of his shoes. His legs reshaped, becoming the proper limbs of a rabbit. Tim cried as he began to shrink more and more, a fluffy bunny tail bursts out behind him as his changed complete. There is a rustling in the former boys clothes, and a small white rabbit hops out.

    "This is bad" Tim thought "That thing turned me into a bunny, whats going to happen to me now"

    He didnt need to wait long for his answer. A mechanical claw descended from the ceiling, picking up the new bunny by his ears. The bunny struggled, kicking his furry feet in the air. The claw brought him over to a vat of liquid chocolate, and it dropped the bunny inside. Tim freaked out as he sunk into the pool of chocolate. A few minutes later, the claw returned and picked out Tim, who was now a bunny coated in liquid chocolate. The claw brought him back onto the floor where he dropped Anna. Another claw propped the egg up, and the other lifted Tims paw so that it rested on the girl turned egg. Tim was surprised to feel his chocolate paw merge with Annas hard chocolate shell. The fans began to spin again, and Tim grew harder and harder. The chocolate, which once only coated him, now merged with his bunny body. He was now made of chocolate, and the cold air hardened his skin into a solid chocolate shell.

    There he was: a solid chocolate bunny with his paw resting on a small, beautifully decorated chocolate egg. Surprisingly, he could still see and hear and feel. He wondered if Anna was also still there inside her small, eggy body. Tim and Anna were now one object, a tasty chocolate easter treat. A ribbon was tied onto Tims neck, and the chocolate treat was packaged in a small cardboard box, ready to be shipped off to a candy store.
