    It's tomorrow after school. Jeremy and Ryan had avoided to touch anyone or anything during the whole day. It wasn't an easy task but they made a promise. And now, they're in their room facing each other and expecting a weird experience.

    "Ok, here we are." Jeremy told Ryan before he sighed. "Who's first?"

    "Me, please." Ryan answered. "But what are going to do?"

    "Touching people I guess. Although we're alone now." Jeremy said. "We need to find someone to touch."

    "Mom and dad are downstairs, old Jenkins is across the street, Danny must be coming from his soccer practice and Kaden hasn't gone to the gym yet." Ryan said. "We can touch them."

    "I had another idea." Jeremy said before he came out of their room. A brief moment later he came back with a dog, a male golden retriever.

    "Lucky? I was expecting to become a human being not an animal."

    "We are experimenting. You can become another person later."

    Ryan sighed and nodded. He put a hand on Lucky's head and he felt some sparkles going through him. It happened quickly, starting from his hand, his arm, his chest and then the rest of his body. He turned into an exact copy of Lucky, a golden retriever, except for one thing, his clothes.

    "Let me help you with that!" Jeremy told his dog brother as he saw him struggling with his bigger baggy clothes. He took his shirt and pants out, leaving a comical sight of a dog wearing boxers. Jeremy giggled a little but Ryan barked at him. "Ok, ok. I'm sorry." Jeremy pulled Ryan's boxers down and immediately his dog tail jumped out. "Wow, you two are identical!" Jeremy said as he watched both dogs side to side. If it wasn't for Lucky's collar he couldn't know who was his brother. He kneeled to pet his brother's head when he heard a knock on his door.

    "Jeremy, Ryan!! May I come in?" It was their dad who didn't wait for an answer and opened the door. "Hey, Jeremy. Where's your brother?"

    "He's buying some chips. He will be coming back in a moment."

    "Ok. I see you're with Lucky. Don't touch him too much, he has some fleas. I'm going to take him to the vet tomorrow."

    Jeremy noticed that Lucky started to scratch himself and Ryan, who was hiding behind the door was doing the same. Jeremy's dad heard the noises that Ryan was making and freaked out very little when he found out another dog.

    "Why there's another dog here?"

    "It's a friend's pet. Its name is... ehem... Ray."

    "Well, it wasn't a good idea to bring another dog. Tell your friend that Ray could need a check." Jeremy's dad said. "Anyway. Your mom and I have decided to go to a restaurant tonight with you. Your mom has been working so hard in the hospital these days that I think it'll be good for her. We're going in a hour!"

    "Ok, dad. I'll tell Ryan."

    "And please, keep the dogs out. I don't want you or your bother get some fleas." Jeremy's dad said before he left the room. Jeremy sighed in relief when the door closed. Ryan didn't seem very happy as he started biting his own skin.

    "I'm sorry Ryan. I didn't know Lucky has fleas." Ryan was scratching his ears with a leg when Jeremy touched him and he started to turn back to human. All his fur vanished, his long ears became shorter, his snout retracted and his dick came out from his doggy sheath. Jeremy giggled again when he saw his naked brother with his feet behind his ear.

    "What happened? Did I turn into Lucky?" Ryan asked as he stood up.

    "Don't you remember anything? Turning into a dog? Dad's coming? The fleas?"

    "Fleas?" Ryan repeated before he felt an itch on his body and started to scratch himself. "Oh damn! Jeremy! I got fleas!!"

    "It seems that when we turned into someone else we got their minds. You're acting like a normal dog a moment ago." Jeremy said as he took Lucky out of their room. "That's dangerous, Ryan. We could end up forgetting who we are."

    "Yeah, yeah... Do something about the fleas!!" Jeremy looked around and noticed the fishbowl next to his bed.

    "Fishes don't get fleas, right?"
