    added by christopher 3 years ago A BM S O Aquatic Swimming

    Next day, Pete decided to try out his frog tf again.

    He stripped off his clothes and glasses, turned into a frog, then he hopped right outside into the back garden, to his garden pond.

    He jumped into the pond.

    Suddenly, Pete the frog suddenly travelled from his garden pond all the way to the river.

    "How did that happen?" he said.

    all the same, he hopped off the lily pad he had appeared on, and onto the river bank. He saw he was in a forest.

    "However did I travel here?"
    all day long, he hopped along the river bank, caught and ate a few flies, and dove and swam under the water.

    Pete sat on the edge of the bank and relaxed.
    It was then Pete suddenly discovered some huge human hands pick him up.

    He looked, and saw the human hands led to a man with tan skin, and short curly black hair, wearing a cream wool sweater under a thick green coat, blue jeans, and Brown shoes.

    "Well well well." The man spoke. Pete knew that voice; it was his good friend Thomas Fletcher, a forest animal protector, a part time vet, and also, a very funny jokester.

    "This is a very strange new species of frog. It's got a human face, with hair, and everything!" He looked again at the frog. "I have a nagging feeling i've seen that face somewhere before."

    Of course, he didn't know the frog was actually Pete.
    "Of course!" He said. "I'll call it the 'Mimic Frog', a frog that can mimic the face shape of any human or animal!"
    He took a picture of the 'Mimic Frog' with his phone. And he uploaded it to Facebook.
    He stroked the frog with Pete's face. He felt a slimy feeling on his fingers. Henoticed he'd got some slime on his fingers. He tasted it. It strangely tasted like apple jelly.

    "I sure wish I could be a Mimic Frog like you." He said.
    He carefully put the 'Mimic Frog' that resembled his friend Pete back into the river.
    "Time to go home. Ribbit." He said, staring at the setting sun.
    He stood up, and then, to Pete's surprise, Thomas hopped all the way back to his car.
    he started up his car and drove off.
    "That was odd." Pete said to himself, watching Thomas drive off in his car. "I didn't know that Thomas could hop that well."

    Of course, Pete didn't know it, but he'd actually given Thomas Fletcher a taste of his froggy DNA, which was gonna cause Thomas Fletcher to turn into a frog as well!

    Pete looked up at the setting sun, and he decided to go home. he dove into the river, and suddenly travelled all the way back to his garden pond.

    he hopped outta the pond, and looked at his reflection in the water. he dipped the finger on his hand in the water, and the reflection changed to his naked human self.

    "Ribbit!" He croaked. He hopped back into his house.

    "How do I change back to me?" Pete said to himself. "I suppose I've just got to think the reverse of transforming into a FROG and back into myself."

    SUDDENLY!
    then, he turned back to human;
    His skin turned form green to flesh, the toes lost the webbing, and his body thinned.
    There was a sudden flash of green smoke, and there was a naked Pete, crouched in a froggy position.

    "Ribbit!" He croaked, then coughed. "Good," he said, standing up. "I'm human again!" A long frog tongue shot outta his mouth and got a fly. he wriggled his toes.

    he said out aloud "Well, those voices in my head were not wrong! I have the gift of transformation. It's amazing."
    It was true, the voices in his head were true to their word. He had the power to transform. And he enjoyed every moment of it!
