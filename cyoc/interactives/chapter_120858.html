    Max headed home with Zach and slept over his house for the night. His parents were very nice and the boys slept in Zach's gigantic basement. Zach and Max stayed up until 2:00 and then felt the weight of exhaustion on them and passed out.

    They awoke at 9:00 and walked upstairs to be treated to bacon and waffles.
    "So what do you think this place will be like?" Max asked eating his waffles.
    "I have no idea. Something with clowns I'm guessing."

    The boys finished breakfast, got dressed and headed into the car to pick up Katrina and head to ClownLand.
    Katrina hopped in he car wearing jorts and a blue top with a jean jacket.
    "Hey babe," Zach greeted Katrina kissing her on he cheek.
    He pulled out of Katrina's drive away and drove to the amusement park.

    Pulling up into ClownLand, a whiteface clown with over-exaggerated makeup, blue hair the curled on the back of his head, a bulbous clown nose and red smile greeted them. He wore a yellow vest with a red and white polka dot bow tie. He had purple and orange striped pants with rainbow suspenders and wore orange bubble toed shoes.

    "Welcome to ClownLand! I'm the valet may I park your car sir?" The clown asked.
    The teens jumped out of the car and Zach tossed the keys to the clown.
    "Be careful," Zach warned the clown.
    He nodded and drove off.

    The teens were greeted at the entrance with a giant clown head smiling at the holding a sign that said "ClownLand."

    They walked forward and were greeted again by another clown, this time he was an auguste clown. He wore the same outfit as the clown at valet.

    "Tickets please!"

    The kids handed the clown their tickets and he inserted them in the redbox beside him.
    "Enjoy your time!" He honked his nose and waved at the kids entering.
    "He looked so...real." Max called out.
    "I know it's so weird." Katrina agreed

    "I say we split up and investigate this place and meet back later." Zach said smiling.

    "I don't know. That sounds like a bad idea," Max said nervously.
    He looked around and saw that everyone there was a clown. All different clowns too. Whitefaces, hobos, and auguste's stared at the three non-clowns.
    "Well I'm doing it regardless. Catch you later."
    Zach waved goodbye and ran off.
    "Guess I'm doing the same thing. Take care." Katrina ran the opposite direction of Zach and also waved to Max.

    Max was alone standing in the middle of park. He found a sign that showed directions and decided to go to....
