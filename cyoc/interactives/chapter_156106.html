    “W-what... it’s midnight, can we—“ William said, taken aback,
    “No!!” Lila demanded, “we do this now, then you go back to sleep,” she said sternly,
    “Okay...” William sighed, “what do you want, I can wish for the world to be back to normal, I can wish us back to yesterday? What do you want,”
    “Neither of those, I’ll do them myself,”
    “How?” William giggled to himself, “I’m the only one with a daily wish,”
    “Exactly! I want you to wish that I have a daily wish as well!!”
    “You want me to... I’m not sure if that—“
    “Well we’ll never know if you keep sitting there doing nothing, get up and make the damn wish,”
    “Okay, okay.” William lifted himself up to his feet, he wobbled around a little, still not fully awake yet,

    “I wish,” William began speaking, Lila’s face lit up with excitement, “that Lila Parkin, will get wishes. She can make wishes,”

    “There, you happy?” William said, yawning, “can I go back to sleep now?” He complained,
    “Wait! We have to see if it worked or not,” Lila said, she grabbed Williams arm and began to speak,

    “I wish that me and William are back to yesterday, before he made the wish to change everyone. Me and him are changed in no way physically. All the wishes made are the same except for the one with the bigger boobs and dicks,”

    Both of them stood there for a moment, nothing.

    “So I didn’t work... I’ll make the wish then, I wish—“ as William was talking, he felt his body flitter, he looked down and saw his legs turn to a blue glittery mist, as did Lila, “what the hell do you do!?” His body turned into glittery mist. He could only feel the feeling of the glitter, but could not see or hear anything.

    He opened his eyes. He was back in school, in the hallway. He looked around, everything seemed normal. Normal sized boobs and normal sized dicks, he sighed in relief. He was still holding Lila’s hand, who was doing exactly the same as him, looking around. She looks at a group of jackass bullies walking in their direction. They laughed at Lila and William, one of them whistled at them.

    Neither of them cared, all they cared was that everything was back to normal. They quickly let go of each other’s hands, “so do I still have my daily wish?”
    “The way you phrased it. If you have a daily wish, it’s used up already, so nope. I think I’m gonna call the butler to pick me up, I still wanna sleep,”

    William walked a small bit away from Lila and pulled out his phone. He began calling his butler.

    Lila stood there for a moment, she thought about how she phrased that wish. She wouldn’t have a wish if she had a daily wish, then she thought back to how William made the wish to give her a daily wish. A grin appeared on her face.

    She had infinite wishes.
