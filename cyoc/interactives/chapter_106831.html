    It's the Beadles just in the nick of time

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Drakkenfyre 7 years ago I O

    "STOP IN THE NAME OF THE CONVOCATION!" shouted a bearded man in a military style long coat who appeared after the lightning flash. He is wielding a staff with a glowing ball on top.

    Another flash of light, and another similar man appears, he shouts, "STOP IN THE NAME OF THE CONVOC-" He stops when he sees the other beadle, "Oh, you already said that, I suppose."

    "You suppose correctly," said the first Beadle, "Cannibalism?" he said distastefully looking down at Liam.

    "Puh-lease, first, these are vegetables. I wouldn't eat people! And second, they weren't wizards before they were vegetables anyway, they were just human," Liam explains. From the expressions on the beadles faces, he may be making things worse.

    Inside his mind, Liam can hear his brother Merlin's telepathic thought, "SHUT UP, LAMO! You're making it worse!"

    Liam's eyes shift over to where Merlin was standing, and see he's now spread eagle on the floor between four beadles.

    Liam thinks back at Merlin, "What's with all these beadles? You'd think they'd have something better to do. I, mean, it's not like these veggies were anyone important?"

    You, Zach and Callum can hear every telepathic word. You all shout, "What do you mean we aren't anybody?"

    "You're veggies, deal with it," thought Liam at them.

    "Wait, Liam, you've been able to hear us all along!" you roar.

    "Noisy, carrot. I told you, Lamo, not to do it," Merlin intoned.

    "NO TELEPATHY!" said the head beadle clanking his staff on the floor.

    "Noisy carrot!" you shout.

    "Now, you've done it, you animated my dinner!" Liam whined.

    Indeed he had, cooked pumpkin had sprouted legs and run to the far side of the table's edge.

    "Don't jump, Callum! You'll be squashed!" you shout.

    "Pumpkin! Squash!" snorts Liam laughing.

    Well, it is sort of funny, if the pumpkin wasn't a transformed human.

    Zach had sprouted legs and arms, and was standing thoughtfully with his hand on what you think probably was where his chin should be. He is silent.

    What do you do now?
