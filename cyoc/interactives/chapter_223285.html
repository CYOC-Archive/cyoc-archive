    Baking... shoes?

    added by Anonymous A year ago BM O Magic Mental Reality alteration

    "Okay, let's try this..." Jenna murmured as she changed establishment to bakery, causing her to stifle a giggle. "This'll definitely be obvious. I don't even know how this'll work." She thought before pressing enter.

    She looked up, yet was left baffled again. Jenna was starting to worry, why hadn't it worked? She was standing in the local bakery. The owners were happily fiddling with dough behind the counter. The younger one with the afro then walked over and handed them to the Asian girl who had just walked in.

    "Oh thank you I love them!" She squealed. "I hope they at least last till next week."

    Jenna glanced to see she was holding a wonderfully baked pair of shoes. They were made of bread, with pink and purple icing. The girl took off her old, starting to crust and peel apart, and slid on the freshly baked pair. Jenna looked down at her own shoes. She was wearing a high heel pair made from cheese. She always preferred to get her shoes form a deli, it was just more convenient. Though bakery shoes are the most popular.

    "Wait.... why did that feel... weird?" She mumbled, realizing that while the app seemingly didn't work, she did feel like something was off.

    She looked around, and saw everyone walking in their food made shoes... but, it's always been like that. Why did it feel odd? There had to be something happening with the app! Maybe it had changed? She needed to be sure.

    She decided to try one more crazy one. This time to definitely see if the app still worked. She loaded the sentence, and it read "This shop sells shoes made of bread."

    She then typed and replaced _________ unaware of how reality was only getting more and more strange.
