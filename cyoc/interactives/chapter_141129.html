    Chronivac Version 4.0

    Jeff is restored, but when and where

    Branch Full view
    added 4 years ago BM I O

    Jeff hung in Nicci's wardrobe for years. He was used as a bra by Nicci for a few months but she had an unexpected growth spurt and thus Jeff was too small for her. This resulted in being hung at the back of the wardrobe collecting dust and mold.

    Jeff's mental state was destroyed at this point. The insanity of loneliness and the pain from being abandoned by his sister and mother meant Jeff just wallowed in misery in silence.

    Or that's how it was for many years but something happened eventually. Jeff's body was restored and his body burst out the wardrobe. He looked up and saw an adult Nicci, possibly 30 or something, on a laptop seeing Jeff on the floor.

    "I'm, free. I'm not a bra. Yessssss!" Jeff was ecstatic

    "Jeff? What the hell happened. You disappeared years ago, but your family tries to cover it up." Nicci questioned

    "My family? Oh they're scum. Those f*ckers turned me into a bra and made me a bra forever, or atleast that's what they said."

    "How?"

    "The chronivac, a device able to change reality. I don't know if you will believe what I said though."

    "Well, you fell out my wardrobe when you weren't there a second ago. And it explains your 20 year disappearance and how you haven't aged a day."

    "Thanks Nicci. Anyway, I'd like to pay my family a visit. It's been 20 years you say and they'd be 'glad' if I payed a visit."

    "So your probably gonna confront them about your change."

    "Oh yes, can you drive me to where they are."

    "Yes, your sister and mother are still in the same house, but they've changed."

    "By change what do you mean."

    "They grown distrustful of others. They erected 5 metre high fences around the house to keep others out and many people in the neighbouring houses disappear."

    "They don't want others to know about the chronivac and are using it cruelly. That will be it, and that means I need to get it to stop there power."

    "Ok, I'll drive you there. Good luck."

    "Thanks"

    Nicci drives Jeff to his old home. As Jeff saw the house, he could tell that the chronivac corrupted them. He packed a knife with him and hid it in his hoodie which he used to cover his face.

    *knocks on the door* "anyone there. I have a parcel for you."

    No answer

    "It's an urgent delivery."

    This time a tall woman answers, who Jeff instantly recognises as his sister Stephanie.

    "What's the parcel" Stephanie asks

    "This" Jeff pulls out the knife and instantly slashes at Stephanie.

    The attack cause his hood to fall off which exposes his face. Stephanies eyes go wide in shock but the slash knocks Stephanie to floor causing Jeff to run inside to try and locate the chronivac.

    What do you do now?
