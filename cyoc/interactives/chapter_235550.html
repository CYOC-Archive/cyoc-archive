    The Magic Shop

    Lisa become smaller than an atom

    At this point Lisa was drifting in the sea of atoms and quarks as her parents were still affected by the involuntary regression. Mary, now 19-years-old, were desperately chasing the high while Gary, age 21, was lying on the ground snoring.

    But something unexpected happened. In the sentient atomic state the regression had stopped and at this point Lisa found herself back at her 16-year-old body inside a white void. She was able to see, sense and hear again. But for some reason she didn't feel as disturbed or stressed as she should had been. Lisa had no idea what was happening in this weird room.

    Suddenly a man appeared in front of her and the room changed into a living room. The man stood there looking at Lisa as if he wasn't expecting her to be there. The young man greeted Lisa and asked for her name. Lisa felt that the man was familiar and introduced herself to him. As the man invited Lisa to sit down on the couch she couldn't put her finger on the room why it looked so familiar. So she asked the man's name.

    "Oh, my name's Gary." The man smiled.

    Lisa was a bit flabbergasted by the name. There was no way a guy older than her would have the same name as her father. So she laughed it off but soon noticed something. The young man in this weird living room was getting younger.

    "How old are you Gary?" Lisa asked.

    "Twenty. How about you?" Gary laughed as he felt that it was a weird question. "Actually scratch that... I'm... uhm... nineteen years old..."

    Lisa was somewhat puzzled as the man didn't know how old he was and said so confidentally that he was younger...

    Younger... There was something that Lisa had almost forgotten. The clock was still ticking in his room. As she sprung from the sofa she noticed that the stairs leading to her room were not there. Instead there was more white void filling the place.

    "Gary? This might sound strange but I need you to go upstairs quickly and shutdown a stopwatch immediately! It's making you younger by a second." Lisa explained in hasteful manner to Gary.

    "Huh? Younger?" Gary smiled. "That's..."

    Suddenly the man vanished in front of Lisa and the room returned back to it's white void colors with nothing in it.

    Gary, now 18-years-old, opened his eyes and noticed himself on the floor. He somehow remembered what the girl had said to him and tried his best to go to the second floor. As he was turning himself his nose picked up a very nasty stench that filled the living room. The damp mat under him made a crusty sound as he lifted a slimy hand.

    He could only shook his head and walk to the second floor. Before he could do anything a hand grabbed his foot. As he turned his head he noticed that it was a 16-year-old girl on ground. Gary lost his balance as it spooked the hell of out him. This panting girl was like a ghost that crawled slowly towards him. He then sprinted to the 2nd room and saw a stopwatch in the middle of the room. Without hesitating he took the whole thing in his palm and smashed it in to pieces.

    "What the hell is going on here..." Gary said as he finally noticed his dick flopping freely.

    He was too scared to go downstairs to confront the ghost girl.

    Inside Gary's brain a small sentient quark of Lisa begun to feel something happening to her. Was the regression ending or what was going to happen as the stopwatch was destroyed?
