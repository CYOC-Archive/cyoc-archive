    Jessica and The Crystal Ball of Change

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 10 years ago A AR BM S

    Jessica was 16, and in highschool. She was a green-eyed red-head. She found the crystal ball in in locker - it was only the size of a golf ball.

    "How'd this get here?" she thought to herself. She took it in her hands, and it glowed. It then sank into her hands, and was absorbed into her.

    "That was weird." she thought to herself, and started walking to class.

    She sat listening to her teacher, Miss Wells. She was 30 with blonde hair and blue eyes.

    Jessica always had an overactive imagination, and she imagined Miss Wells becoming a part cat.

    When Miss Wells had her back turned, her ears moved up her head and became triangle-shaped, and a long tail grew out the back of her dress.

    When she turned around, she had slit pupils, a pink cat nose, a split upper lip, and whiskers. She also had pink paw-pads on her hands and
    feet, and retractable claws.

    "Now class," she said, not noticing the changes "remember to read pages 304-310 in your science books tonight. There will be a test on
    Thurrrrsday that will count for 10% of yourrrr grade this semesterrrrr" Miss Wells didn't even seem to notice her own odd speech.

    As the class left, Julie went up to her.

    "Miss Wells." she said.

    "Yes, Jessica?" she asked.

    "Have you noticed anything different about yourself lately?" Jessica asked.

    "I can't say that I have." she replied "Why do you ask?"

    "Look at your hands." Jessica replied.

    Miss Wells did, and looked back at Jessica a little confused.

    "What am I supposed to see?" She asked her.

    "You mean you don't think you're weird in any way?" Jessica asked.

    "No," Ms Wells answered "I'm fine."

    Then Jessica decided to test something. She imagined Miss wells only 20, wearing only a red collar with a bell on it, and a red bikini top and
    bottom.

    "How about now?" Jessica asked.

    "No." Ms Wells answered "Still fine."

    Jessica then imagined Miss Wells shrinking down to only 2 feet tall, with her clothes still fitting her.

    "Now?" Jessica asked.

    "No." Ms Wells answered "Don't you have somewhere else to be?"

    "Sorry," Jessica said "I'll go." and as she left, she saw Ms Wells climb up into her chair and begin to lick herself clean.

    Then Jessica realized she could change people just by thinking about it, and the best part was only she would notice.

    Her head filled with possibilities of who she could transform, so who does she transform next?
