    As Josh and his fiance made their way into their room, the possibilities of his VIP powers swam through his mind. But how much was really possible? To what extent could reality be altered? Also, to what extent can he make mental changes?

    "Hey, are you listening?" Anna's question brought Josh out of his train of thought.

    "Huh? What?"

    "I asked you what you wanted to do first. I'm letting you have first dibs on a transformation."

    "Oh." Now focused on the current situation, Josh looked around the room they would be staying in for the next 3 days. The room appeared to be your typical hotel room. A bed they would be sharing. A TV on a cabinet, with itself had a built-in mini-fridge. A small closet. A bathroom. And a small balcony overlooking the central courtyard and swimming pool. The one unique object in the hotel room was the floor-length mirror, so guests can admire their changes. And of course, the room itself would change to accomodate any form guests acquire.

    This room would be for him and Anna. Anna's friends would be sharing two other rooms. He was surprised that they had agreed to join Anna. As far as he knew, they didn't have any interests in transformation.

    "Hey. Something on your mind?" Anna looked at Josh questioningly.

    "Uh? Just taking things in." Suddenly, Josh rembered the VIP treatment he had received, and excitement started bubbling up. "Hey, I gotta tell you about what happened! Oh, but don't tell your friends. This'll be more fun if they don't know."

    Anna looked more puzzled. "O... kay. Alright, I promise."

    "Okay, so! After we signed in, I saw... I guess like a vision? I was in a white room, and I was told that I now had VIP access, with pretty much unlimited transformation potential! You know how we expected to only be able to transform ourselves? Well, I can transform anyone, in any way I want! I can even alter their memories, make 'em think they were always transformed. And then I can have reality change so it'd be like they really were!"

    Anna was shocked. "Thats... are you sure? That sounds awfully overpowered. Would this place really allow that?"

    Josh's excitement diminished a little. Anna was right, that did sound too good to be true. "I don't know. The experience was so real, I'd like to imagine it's true."

    "Well, why don't you try it out on me? Transform me into something. Change reality."

    "Wait, are you sure?"

    "Of course. I'm here to experience some transformations too, you know. I'm not as interested in transformations as you, but I thought it'd be fun to play around a little. Just don't tell me what you're gonna turn me into."

    "Don't you want to choose? I don't wanna choose something you don't like."

    "I trust you. Besides, if I picked, then how would we know it was you who transformed me? If we wanna prove this VIP thing is real, you need to show that you're in full control."

    Josh nodded his head in agreement. He focused his mind on changing Anna. Suddenly, the world went white again. Panels showing info about Anna started popping up. So far, this much seemed real. Now, how to change Anna?
