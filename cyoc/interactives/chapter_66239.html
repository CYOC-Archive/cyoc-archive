"I look amazing!" she said"
    "What's that button on the wall?" Fredrika asked. She walked over to it and read the label, "Toon Life Generator. If you are a new toon, press this button to gain a backstory and a place in Toon World. Backstories may be specific to the location of the button."
    "Should we press it?" asked Emma.
    "Sure," said Fredrika, "We want a life in Toon World don't we?" Fredrika pressed the button. For a moment, the two poodles felt dizzy. When they recovered, they weren't Fredrika Pryce and Emma Hiller anymore. They were Fifi and Yvette de Roucy, the daughters of a French wine tycoon. Their family had just bought a home in the American part of Toon World, and this was their first day in their new high school.
    "Why are we standing een front of zees button?" asked Yvette.
    "I suppose zat we were humans from ze real world once," said Fifi, "But we are zem no longer; we are toons. Let us enjoy our new lives."
    "If we were once real people, our transformation ees complete," said Yvette, "Let's put on our clothes and get out of ze changing room." They put on their clothes (which was now designer) and left.

    As they were walking through the hallway, Yvette and Fifi ran into another poodle. She had no accent, so she must have been born in America. "Hello," the poodle said, "I'm Giselle Chenard. I sort of run this school. You must be those rich French kids, Fifi and Yvette. I could use some sidekicks. You in?"
    "Sure," said Fifi, "Why not?" The three walked down the hall together and bumped into an elephant girl who didn't get out of the way fast enough. She way pretty fat, and obviously working class.
    "Cute necklace, Becky," Giselle said, "I have one just like it... only it's real."
