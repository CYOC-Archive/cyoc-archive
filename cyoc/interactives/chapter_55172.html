    Amethyst led the pups into a living room area, partially with the hopes of letting them "veg
    out" in front of the television for a while. She had left Bart, Jenny, Kevin, and Marcy in the
    arms of their mother, and Abbey and Alice remained in the mirrorverse. The purple she
    wolf, with some sparkling luster in her fur guessed they were searching for their sister.
    Amy had departed for her shrine area with Zelipe tagging along. Amethyst didn't worry
    about that, though it could be argued she didn't worry about anything. If they truly needed
    supervision, one of the Omegas would take up the task. Amethyst's main concern was the
    gem twins. She'd help the gem teens whenever possible (much to Garnett's annoyance
    when she became "trapped" in Amethyst's bosom).

    As they made their way in, they found the present Omegas dancing for nine very depressed
    looking cubs.

    "There has to be something we can do to make them happy," Jay said weakly.

    Amethyst sighed at the sight of the Pride's cubs. While little could shake Amethyst's cheery
    demeanor, the sadness radiating off the cubs came close. She then noticed how the cubs
    were fascinated by the Omega's dancing. It did give Amethyst a slight idea.

    "Maybe we can entertain them too," Amethyst said with a smile and took Martha and
    Whitney's paws and joined in. The pups were close behind her.

    Mary looked up slightly as stroked the fur on Moja's back to see Amethyst and all of the
    gem twins joining in.

    Amethyst began her dance, and the gem twins followed close behind. Amethyst began at
    the center of the living room without even thinking about it, miracle or miracles avoiding
    walking into the TV. The gem twins danced in a pattern that never repeated itself, twisting
    about the omegas like wind.

    Amethyst let her body do what came naturally as did the twins. The dance of chaos, the
    dance of absolute freedom, not as living crystal as they had been, but they danced all the
    same. The left the grieving cubs entranced at the hypnotic movements, forgetting their
    sadness for just a little while, forgetting their loss for just a little while, leaving only the
    dance to look upon.

    Martha and her fellow omegas found the dance Amethyst and her charges were doing was
    proving greater than the ones the Omegas were doing. Even her own daughters Giselle
    and Gretchen moved so naturally, so enveloping, that Martha found herself following
    THEIR steps.

    The nine cubs eyes were all glued to the dancing gem wolves.

    When Kaylee came in with her pups a short time later planning on playing the Wii console...
    The moment she and her pups saw the dance they forgot all about that. And almost like
    zombies they sat down on the floor the coach having been taken and just watched the
    lovely dance, their own worries and problems forgotten for the time being.

    As much as the gem twins themselves wished it, this dance couldn't last forever, they were
    flesh and blood now, their muscled burned from use and now knew tiredness. But they
    would dance as long as they could.

    'Was Kurse evil?' Amethyst thought, 'Is it good that the witches' shadow is gone? Is the
    world a better place without them? Yes. Yes. Yes.' But like the yacht victims who had been
    turned into beasts free to express their vices without limit, Amethyst secretly admitted to
    herself, if the circumstances had been different, she wouldn't have minded the beautiful
    dance lasting forever.
