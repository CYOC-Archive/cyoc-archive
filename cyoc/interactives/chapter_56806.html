    "Trying to persuade her and her husband to let it go has proved impossible," Penny commented, "Grandma tried to explain that it wasn't necessary. I tried to explain that it wasn't necessary. They are apparently driven to duty and honor."

    "I see," Serenity spoke, silently thankful that this was not some twisted joke created by the game.

    "Anyway," Penny spoke, "If I am to free them, I suppose we had best get started."

    Serenity nodded. And handed the first love doll, a female, to Penny. What followed was a fairly predicable outcome. Penny slowly touched each love doll and said, "you are of the Pride. You have always been and always will be Leomens of the Glendale Pride". After each repeating of the phrase, rubber or latex and the other inanimate materials softened into flesh, and fur the same color as the love doll's material grew. On the males, the fur around their necks grew into a full mane. The image of teeth separated into individual teeth and hardened into bone, as all teeth were. The male's members softened and were "pulled" into a protective sheath.

    "Are you all okay?" Penny asked as the last transformation finished.

    "I'm me again!" one of the females announced, and then hugged one of Penny's legs, "I'M ME AGAIN!"

    "Easy with the legs, Pride Sister," Penny urged.

    "Sorry, Penny," the female answered and then noticed Kirk standing beside Penny's wheelchair, Umeko behind it, and Madame Serenity standing nearby, "I trust this wasn't too much trouble?"

    "Not too much," Serenity spoke, "I generally specialized in investigating the game... I go under the "name" of Madame Serenity."

    "What is your first name, Ms. Serenity?"

    That made Serenity pause for a moment. As a male, her first name had been Patrick, but over the years she had gone by gypsy/fortune teller type name of Madame Serenity. And she had created the alias of Serena when she first met Anne in the church to mimic the stage name she commonly used, instead of Patricia, which would be the feminine version of her original name. She sighed and decided to stick with the stage name.

    "Serena, though I usually prefer my surname," Serenity answered.

    "I see," the female responded, "My name is Leona Wye, and fine male is my Prime Mate Leon Wye."

    "We have the same first name!" the other female announced and then hugged Leona Wye, "I'm Leona Wayne. My Prime Mate is Lionel Wayne."

    "A pleasure to meet you all," Serenity said politely.

    "Thank you for helping us," Lionel told her.
