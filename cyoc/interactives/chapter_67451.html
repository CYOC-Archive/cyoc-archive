Her height is 4’9 with a teenage feminine body with peach skin. She had long flowing blonde hair with pink purplish tips at the bottom of each strand and brown eyes. Her clothes consist of a green strap tank top, brown kaki pants that go down to below her knees, and a pair of blue slippers. Her powers in morphing are summarized as a doughish human with the tools and abilities to execute a variety of transformations.

    Before joining the academy, Kyoko was once an interdemnsional travel going through different dimensions. While exploring our dimension, she was founded by the Academy and was used as a prime experiment to prove that if a person were immediately killed, a morpher would absorb the body and recreate the person. The experiment was a complete success and Kyoko was reborn as a human morpher. Getting use to her new powers, she was enrolled into the Academy and made some new friends such as Ally, Kate, Talichan, Fasen, Sophia, and Rai. But months later, things have taken a turn for the worse for her.

    A few months after enrollment, she was fighting with Freida over an argument and was caught by Nony. After hearing about the fight and her actions, Kyoko was sentenced to imprisonment in solitary confinement. She then later escaped and left the Academy, never to see her piers ever again. Lost and alone she discovered curiosity shop in town and the owner was looking for new employees. Kyoko gladly excepted the owner’s offer and worked in the shop while making new friends who worked there as well. Her new friends include Eckleston, Pheobe Moraity, Denise, Monoko, and Melody. During that time, she started to train herself, getting use to her new abilities and learning how to control it.

    When the dust settles, Kyoko went back to the academy and redeemed herself. She was amazed at how much the school has changed and shocked that most of her friends have forgotten about her. But there were still few who remembered her and managed to find her over time. Now she got a second chance at the Academy and her life was better then ever. Though she encountered Freida a couple of times, she tends to avoid her in order to prevent a repeat of what happened the last time.
