    "Who would be next?" wondered Sean while he entered the bathtub. He already had revenge on Jay, Mark and Dan, he thought. He left the Chronivac on the bathtub corner, and started shampooing his hair.
    "Mark, the new guy, is very rude. He needs some 'education through pain'." he said not thinking that he'd become the same kind of person he'd always hated: vindictive, rude and cruel.

    A grin showed up on his face while he created a plan. He thought he had everything under control, but destiny's will is treacherous, and Sean didn't have a plan B.

    Dan (the new skinny and stupid Dan) entered the bathroom crying, moving his arms like a wind mill and shouting something like "WANGE ME WACK!". Sean suffered a panic attack and tried to take the Chronivac, but it fell into the bathtub full of water!. Scared (he didn't want to end his life in a bathtub, fried like a chicken thanks to a short-circuit), he ran out of the bathtub.

    "Welcome back, Dan... how's weather?" a VERY scared Sean said.
    "Don't be silly, Sean. You attacked me you WILL be punished for that. I don't know what will I transform you to, but I'll start with the something evil." Dan said while he clicked all over Sean stats.
    "Listen, Dan, I was jealous, but I'm sure we can solve this"

    And Dan clicked SUBMIT

    "Take a look at your new stats while you shrink, Sean" Dan said.

    Development state Prepubescent (Adult)
    IQ 85 (107)
    Penis length 0" (7")

    And he started crying.
