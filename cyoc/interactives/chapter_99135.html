    Jeff looked up from his locker, and winced as he saw the Terrible Trio stalking the hallways.

    I guess some other things didn't change, either, he thought sadly.

    The Terrible Trio were the bane of everyone. Although they were the most popular girls at school, they ran it like their own personal kingdom. Woe to the person who crossed them. They were notorious for making life a living hell for kids who they didn't like.

    The Trio consisted of three girls, all cheerleaders, of course. Brittany was the leader, and the brains of the outfit. She was a tall blonde, with a thin model's figure and long wavy blonde hair. She always seemed to be wearing the coolest clothes with the hippest accessories. She looked like a princess, but acted like the evil Queen of Hearts from Alice in Wonderland. Her furious temper was infamous, and he could easily imagine her screaming "Off with their heads! Off with their heads" as she passed judgment on her court.

    Leslie was the brawn of the group. She was not tall, barely over five feet tall, but what she lacked in height she made up for in strength. She was built like a female bodybuilder, with dense muscle packed over her small frame, and seemed to spend all of her free time in the gym, lifting weights. She still had a nice feminine body, but her breasts were very small. There were rumors that she had used steroids to get bigger, and that it had stunted her breast development. There were other rumors, too - that she might be interested in girls rather than boys. But woe to the person who referred to her by the nickname sometimes used behind her back - Les the Lesbian.

    And then there was Cindy. Cindy was the beauty. Although all the girls were attractive, in Jeff's opinion, Cindy blew them all away. She was the tallest of the three, measuring well over six feet. She was not thin like Brittany, but rather had a nice, curvy figure that reminded Jeff of Marilyn Monroe. Her features seemed exaggerated, with humungous DD breasts, a tiny little waist, and a huge bubble butt. She looked like a Barbie doll come to life. On the surface Cindy seemed perfect - she was easily the nicest of the three - but she did have a problem. She was dumb. Really dumb. There were rumors that her IQ was less than 90, but her parents threatened to sue the school if they put her in Special Education classes.

    Jeff ducked behind his locker door, trying to stay out of their line of sight. He was pretty sure he was an outcast, and he had no desire to find out what the Terrible Trio thought of him in this reality. He doubted it would be anything good.

    When he heard the bell ring, signaling time to return to class, he thought he was safe. He shut his locker door, and was surprised to see all three girls standing behind it....... and staring directly at him.
