    Question #1- How obedient are you?

    Looking at the last sentence on the instruction page, you decide to take the Canine Personality Test and see just what kind of dog you really are. You put the headphones over your ears and then plug the headphone socket into a port on the side of the computer. The moment you put the headphones on and connect them to the computer, you smile because it is playing some very relaxing music. The kind of music that makes you want to just relax and take a nap and have pleasant dreams with. You try to identify the piece of music being pumped into your ears through the headphones. But the title of this musical piece escapes you for the moment.

    You then click on the yes button and watch as the instruction page is replaced with a new page of the test. And you see your first question.

    Question #1- As Man's best friend, how obedient are you to your friend's wishes? (Answer One)

    You smile again, this time at the nature of the question being asked of you. And then you chuckle as you see the choices the question is offering to you.

    The choices being offered to you by this question are of the following:
    (A) "I don't pay attention to everything that he or she wants me to do. There are too many things around me that are more interesting than hearing them flapping their gums at me. Besides I am training them to be obedient to my wishes."

    (B) "Sometimes I pay attention to my friend but I am easily distracted by what's around me. Perhaps someone out there could give me an incentive to pay attention?"

    (C) "I try my very best to pay attention to what my friend wants me to do. I want to please them very much."

    (D) "I am very good at paying attention to what my friend's wishes. Have to make them think that they are in charge when it is really the other way around."

    You look at each of the choices being offered to you. And as you are doing so you are trying to not to crack up at the silliness of the choices. You lean back in your chair for a moment and take some deep breaths to ease yourself.

    You go back to looking at the question again, this time with a straight face. You look over each choice and ask yourself if you fit any of them. You ask yourself as to how obedient you were when you were growing up at home. And you ask yourself if really listened and followed the desires and wants of your parents and elders.

    A minute passes by as you ask yourself about these choices. You then eliminate the choices that you believe don't fit you. Guiding the mouse over to the choices presented on this page of the test, you press the one choice that fits you. The computer then speaks to you in a beautiful woman's voice if that choice is the one you want to submit as your answer for the test. You click on the yes button that appears next to your choice. The computer then tells you that you have picked...
