    As… uh, ‘tempting’ as it is to check out the erogenous options, you think that you’ll stick with something more socially acceptable on the off-chance it leads to anything unfortunate. This store is already weird enough what with it being completely barren and all, but on the off chance someone else does come in then like hell you wanna be caught looking at something as awful as that.

    You decide to click on the ‘Tail’ category, having somewhat of an affinity for them. The feeling from watching a dog’s tail wag as a sign of happiness is super cute. The screen transitions to a long wall of text that you almost mistake as some kind of ‘Terms and Conditions’ page before you see the word ‘Tail’ being mentioned over and over again throughout the passage. This is some sort of article describing tails in a very formal and scientific way. Scrolling down, you see more tabs for ‘Customization’ and ‘Species’. Clicking on the ‘Species’ tab, you see a large selection of creatures along with detailed photographs for each and every one of them. Upon closer inspection, however, the images all appear to be extremely accurate computer renders instead of actual photos.

    You take one more look behind you, sure enough you don’t see or hear anyone. In fact, It’s kind of eerie how quiet it’s gotten since you’ve gotten on this thing… maybe that’s just you though. Hopefully.

    You sigh and start scrolling. “Fuck it.” you mutter, looking for any animals you like or just stand out in particular…
