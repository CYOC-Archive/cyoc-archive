    Courtney realized the dress and heels she was now wearing wasn’t the best choice to run in and she had to slow her pace out of the house in order to avoid trips and rips of the embarrassing kind. Courtney made a break for her Tacoma which she saw that thankfully hadn’t disappeared but had begrudgingly been painted pink. As she got closer she heard whimpers and cries behind her and looked back to the open front door were her sisters were crouched around not daring to cross the threshold crying and consoling each other. “Courtney don’t leave please! Were your Sisters we need to stay together! We can’t live without each other please we love you!” Courtney was a bit touched by this outpouring but she wasn’t a harem girl and she needed to get away from the area to think up a plan “No matter what I am not their sister and I am not a harem girl!” exclaimed Courtney as she stepped into her truck put the lamp on the passenger seat and pulled the started truck out of the driveway and down the road. Looking back in the rear-view mirror Courtney was surprised to see the girls sobbing and crying even harder into complete hysterics. Most of the girls where hugging and crying into their shoulders, one girl had fainted, and another had her face pushed into the carpet and was shaking in big frantic moans. “Jeez what a bunch of dramatists” Courtney thought as she looked back to the road. Courtney was perhaps a half mile away from her townhouse before the first sign something was wrong hit her. For no apparent reason Courtney began to choke up and her breathing rapidly increased, soon after Courtney’s eyes began to water and she began silently sobbing to herself with no control of her emotions. She had to pull to the side of the road and cry her eyes out in racking sobs as she had began burying her face into the steering wheel making driving dangerous.

    While still crying Courtney knew what she had to do she rubbed the lamp and as Genie poured out and came to rest in the passenger seat with herself holding the lamp Courtney blurted out “what is happening to me!” Genie wanted so much to help her distraught mistress “Mistress I tried to warn you that you couldn’t leave the house or most especially your sisters as you are bound to the building as it is your harem chambers and you couldn’t leave your sisters as you are bound together more then family but almost as one being.” Courtney didn’t know what the heck was being told to her so she asked Genie to explain “Mistress you are feeling the effects of sisterly separation, each of your sisters and you are bonded together for life you can’t be separated as it would be like chopping off body parts without stopping the bleeding, it just can’t be done and expect the patient to survive. Bad enough that you serve as the emotional support and control for the group, each of your sisters has a job and purpose in the family that helps everyone stay on task and be happy. Remember Mistress before you were turned how the girls always fought with each other? Well now they don’t fight and don’t let there emotions get the better of them thanks to your presence among them. What your feeling right now is extreme separation anxiety mixed in with your power among the group. You need to return Mistress.

    Courtney couldn’t believe what she was hearing but feeling the evidence inside her she knew it had to be the truth “I wish I was bound to them and the property” Courtney sobbed but Genie knew this wouldn’t go anywhere “I’m sorry mistress but I cannot grant that wish as it is in violation of your original wish that made you a harem girl and that included your bonding with your sisters and the property. “I wish there was a bond between the harem girls the property and me” Sorry again Mistress but again that violates the parameters of the original wish quipped Genie. Defeated and completely racked with uncontrollable crying and tears Courtney buried her head into the steering wheel “I am a harem girl, I am their sister!” sobbed Courtney as she turned the car around and sped back to her property. “I’m glad you made this choice Mistress as if you had gone another half mile down the road you would have been quite frightened as the property bond would come into effect as the vehicle and us two would be disintegrated and re-materialized back in your driveway” said Genie. Courtney could see her sisters still crying in the door-frame as they came into view. They all looked up in surprise and excitement as Courtney raced back to them. Courtney roared into the driveway halted the truck grabbed the lamp and climbed out of the window as she got her bearings and began running as fast as she could in heels towards the front door. Both groups were crying in happiness and were very giddy as Courtney flung herself onto the group of her sisters flinging the lamp softly onto the floor in the process. The huge pile of crying and sobbing girls along with many hugs and laughter must have been a strange sight with the front door open “I’m so sorry, I’ll never leave any of you ever again” cried Courtney as she hugged tightly each of her newfound family. “We love you so much Courtney we belong together forever” the girls cried, “ I love you all so much” cried Courtney.
