Leaving the lab to conduct field work would expose Randall to the possibility of being changed.

I understand that you're an engineer, and I'm impressed with your work. You would make a valuable asset to the company, so I'm here to discuss your career options." Randall was able to access to all the information that the chronivac had recorded about scanned individuals, allowing him to tailor a lie to grant Rajesh's trust.

Even with Rajesh sitting on the opposite edge, his wide form was well within Randall's personal space. "So, Trans Dem Labs? I don't recall hearing of this company."
