    Beckys a weredragon

    Now what Becky hadn't known was that the dragongirl whom the blood had
    been taken from to make those microscope slides that Becky had examined
    in the library's microscope had performed the Dance of the Weredragon
    in a dank cavern far away from prying dragonpeople eyes,and had succeeded in getting
    herself turned into a weredragon.

    A item which had been passed along to Becky when she'd cut herself with the
    dragongirls blood.

    So besides all the other new issues Becky was dealing with,she was a weredragon
    yet didn't know it.

    The group had been going down that dark dank tunnel into the Harpy and
    Owl hold from the old ship.

    Becky had been feeling her human teeth in the dark every few seconds,and
    suddenly noticed her teeth were loose.wobbly and were being pushed
    out of her gums.

    She silently slowly came to stop in the dark tunnel and had let the rest of the
    group keep on walking towards the tiny pinhole of light ahead.

    It had only taken a few more seconds for the shocked girls teeth to fall
    out "pitter patter " on the tunnel floor and when Becky felt along her gums she'd felt the
    sharp pointing teeth that had pushed out her human teeth start to lengthen and get pointier in her stretching gums.

    Quite sensibly she'd not screamed and instead had tried to run back outside the tunnel to the
    outerworld.

    Suddenly she was yanked to a halt.It was as if her hips and tailbone had been yanked backward.

    She turned around and gasped as she saw the stretched out spiny dragons tail that led from her
    rump back to a stone pillar in the tunnel behind her,a pillar her dragons tail had
    coiled itself around,to keep her from avoiding a transformation into a
    weredragon.

    It was the dark dank tunnel that had trigger the weredragons transformation.

    Thus trapped,Becky soon found it hard to think abbout anything besides making her
    body much bigger,much scalier and more weredragonish.A urge that her body was eager to
    satisfy as Becky turned into a huge 4 foot wild dragoness with evil dragon urges.

    The weredragon squeezed its way out of the Harpy cave,took to wing and flew towards
    the dragonman tribe,eager to infect them with her weredragonism and evil desires

    As Becky the huge dragoness flew along,she gave not a glance to the mile long
    iron hulled wreck that she passed over,a sea going Sea Serpente miles inland,
    cracked in half,wedged between two mountains...

    **********************************************
    Now it had been a dark cloudy day when the dark dank tunnel had triggered Beckys
    weredragonic changes.

    So the big scaly reptile had gotten part ways over the mountain lake the old iron
    wrecl lay partially submerged in when the clouds parted and the Sun shone
    full force on Becky.

    Who began to shrink and change as the Sunlight turned her back into a naked
    human girl hundreds of feet in the air.

    Reflexively she flapped her arms,but plunged into the icy
    cold mountain lake,which shook her awake.

    She breaststroked her way to shore,the nearest shore.

    She lay there shivering and gasping for breath,puzzled what she was doing
    plunging nude out of the sky into a lake.

    The last thing she remembered was just entering the dark tunnel,reaching up to feel
    her human teeth for the umpteenth time.

    After that it was all a blank till she'd found herself flapping her arms above the clouds.

    She gasped as she felt her bald scalp.All her thick luxerious hair was gone,except for a
    tiny fuzz of new hairs poking out from her scalp.

    She then gasped as she realized her teeth were in a similar state,with tiny baby like molars
    and canines barely poking out of her gums.

    The hair on her arms also seemed to be mostly gone,replaced by tiny hair growth.

    The clueless weredragon felt herself all over,and could feel little changes here and there,
    like traces of a transformation she didn't remember going through.

    Becky got up and went looking for something to cover herself in.As she headed around a bay
    the trees gave way,and she found herself staring at a huge snarling metal
    dragonshead,rusted and battered in spots.

    She was looking at a gigantic ships prow ,shaped like a dragon,and the
    glint of sunlight fathoms below told her the hull it was attached to lay down deep
    on the lake bottom.

    Becky slowly walked around the next bend of trees,and saw the rest of the broken wreck
    coming out of the lake and going for hundreds of feet into a mountain forest...
