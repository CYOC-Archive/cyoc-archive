    My briefly deceased obnoxious aunt turned horse turned anthro unicorn pondered what she really wanted to become for a few moments and then blushed a deep, deep shade of red on her cheeks.

    "Welllll..." She began, clearly embarrassed. "There is this one thing I always wanted to be, but... it's a little weird. Frankly, I think it'll turn you off, so!" She kissed me on the cheek quickly. "Let's still do that sex thing, huh?"

    I laughed and pushed her away. "Hold on, back up! Tell me what you want me to make you!"

    "Oh, James..." Cathy looked at her hooves sadly. "You said such lovely things about me, but... I don't think you'll say the same if I... if I ask you to make me a dinosaur."

    My eyes went wide for a moment. Was she serious? She was! "That's awesome!" I blurted out. "That'a brilliant! Nobody ever wants to be a dinosaur in my stories."

    Cathy looked confused and flicked her tail in annoyance. "What stories?"

    "Nevermind!" I announced. "The point is, that's amazing! Why would you want to be a dinosaur? What made you pick that?"

    "Well... I thought they were so big and strong. So... sexy." She grinned. "I... I actually got turned on by that Walking With Dinosaurs program one time, and..." She seemed lost for a moment and then snapped back to reality. "Well, you can probably guess!"

    "So... what'll it be, then?" I smiled mischievously.
