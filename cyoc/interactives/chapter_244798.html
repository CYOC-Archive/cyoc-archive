    The genie was happy to have managed Damon's wish so well. The lad would enjoy his new life. Sure, he'd be a mare instead of stallion, but it would be a lot easier. No competition, no races, just breeding. Which had its fair share of dangers but the genie had made Damon's body so that giving birth once he was an adult as easy as possible. Besides, he'd be seen as an extraordinary horse to be bred. He'd be the perfect mare and mother. All would gush at how Beauty was deserving of her name and how a great unicorn she would be if only a horn was sprouting from her forehead. So all in all it would be a simple, joyful life for Damon. One that would be without human concerns, the very ones he had sought to escape. Damon had been a good lad so his wish deserved to be what he wanted. He had deserved to be happy. The genie loved tricking jerks with their silly wishes, but for good people, she made sure that they gathered nice outcomes regarding their wishes. The mortals that were stupid had similar wishes, mainly sex and riches. Girls wanted beauty and big boobs, guys to be chick magnets and all wanted to be rich. Typical. Then again, punishing jerks was so much fun! Hopefully she would get a massive cad or meanie as her next master and she'd have great fun. Seeing their faces once they saw they'd been swindled had always been her greatest joy in life. In fact, it was better than simply watching series in the giant penthouse that was her lamp. No exterior views but as much space and entertaining things as she wanted. Oh, she had to serve a master once in a while, but it was mainly a fun and interesting job, once she could twist the magic like she wanted to screw with these idiotic mortals. Thus the genie waited for her next customer, while watching series and munching on snacks.
