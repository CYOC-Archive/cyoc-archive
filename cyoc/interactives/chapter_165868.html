    "What was that?" The woman said, suspicion in her voice.

    "Nothing, just thought the name was kinda dumb." Bree replies.

    "Listen, if you want to leave you can go, we aren't forcing you to work here you know." The woman says irritated.

    Bree decides to act a bit nicer to the lady, she doesn't want to ruin her mission. "okay okay, here I'll drink it." Bree grabs the vial and examines it for a moment before bringing it up to her lips. In the brief glimpse she had of the vial, she noticed this serum was a darker pink than the other in her pocket.

    Bree drinks the serum and returns the vial to the lady waiting in front of her, this one tasted like grapes. "I hope that pill will help against this" she thinks.

    "Alright, now that that's in order, time to go meet your boss, he has asked to speak with you personally." The woman said.

    You can't tell from the outside but knowing she's about to be face to face with Mr. Clark in a moment is stressing Bree. "Why would he want to talk to me so fast? This might make the mission harder."

    After a quick walk up to the door and inside the mansion, Bree is standing in front of the man himself. "So... Bree is it? I've heard you're coming here to be our new maid. Is that correct?"

    "Uh yes sir." Bree hesitantly replies, feeling intimidated.

    "Good... good... now tell me something, why is it that you have decided to keep that vial in your back pocket?" Mr. Clark asks, looking angry.

    "Umm... uhh" Bree doesn't know what to say, she's been caught. Her fight or flight response kicks in. Bree grabs the vial from her pocket and throws it at the woman who brought her inside, the vial shatters and the pink serum splashes all over the woman. Bree then dashes toward the door. She grabs the handle and realizes it's locked. Before she can turn around two pairs of hands grab her and hold her.

    "Let me go!" Bree screams as she thrashes about.

    The two men holding Bree turn her around to face Mr Clark. "Bree, we do have cameras you know, now tell me, what do you know about this place. Who sent you?" Mr Clark raises his voice a bit. "You've wasted my time and some of that perfectly good serum!" He gestures over to the woman Bree threw the vial at. She looks like she's about halfway through turning into some bimbo.

    "I'm not telling you anything." Bree says with a look of hatred on her face, trying to look tough even though on the inside she's terrified.

    Mr Clark reaches into his back pocket and pulls out what looks like a remote. "Bree... you are going to tell me, or you will face the consequences. You see that serum you drank on your way up here hasn't done anything because I haven't activated it yet. That serum was full of millions of nano-bots ready to change your body in any way I choose. They can even change your mind if it comes to that. So tell me everything." Mr Clark says in an uncomfortably calm voice.

    Bree thinks for a moment before speaking. "Do your worst."
