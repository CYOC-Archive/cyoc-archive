    Crimson Titan... The Sumo Wrestler?

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Blue the Sumo Wolf 3 days ago O Magic Clothes Sumo

    You are taken aback! You were expecting to see that same Fat man in a Red Ringleader suit again. But this time, it looked like it’s just a random fat man in a red bathrobe!

    You came closer to him, and you realized that this man looks more like a Sumo Wrestler. He wears nothing but a Red, and white Yukata, sandals, and his red hair is tied in a Chonmage (Samurai hairstyle)...

    Wait... Red hair?

    "Crimson Titan? Is that you?" You asked the sumo man...

    "Hai!" He says with a thick Japanese accent. "I am Korimasun Tieton." You tilt your head. like 'What?'.

    The Sumo shook his head, and tried again. "I'm Crimson Titan. But this time I am experiencing a life as a Rikishi! Or a Sumo Wrestler as you can already tell." He is smiling. He's supposed to be a Japanese Wrestler, But he still has his facial features, from your last visit. His Red hair, and Mustache-Sideburns combo with his chin, and neck is shaved.

    "I thought you have to be Japanese to be a sumo?" you say without thinking. And he Laughs. "Oh Wally-san. Being Japanese is not a requirement to be a rikishi. Although, If you wanted to be a sumo, the opiton is there with a race change, and Language transfer. Nevertheless, while there are other elements to be a sumo like repecting the culture, and learning the language. But you don't have to change your race if you don't want to."

    You scratch the back of your head. "I had no idea." You feel guilty for assuming what a sumo is suposed to be... Still you wonder... "Crimson?... What is it that you're wearing inside your robe?" And he smiles on that subject.

    "Oh this?" He opens his Yukata to reveal a crimson red mawashi, though you don't know what a 'Mawashi' is? So to you it looked like a diaper.

    He says that. "This is called a Mawashi. A sumo belt to be worn at all times, even in the ring, to show their massive size in all its glory while covering their private parts, and of course... It is the most fashionable and comfortable silky smooth undergarments a fat Man can wear without shame!" He gives his mawashi a rub. While you remarked without thinking... "It looks like a Diaper." you bit your lip at that.

    Crimson glares at you. "No Wally-san, This is not a diaper..." He covers himself, then said. "This is!" He opens up his Yukata again, and you cringe at the sight of him. Revealing that he now wears an adult sized diaper, With a crimson red banner on the front. It somehow in someway it fits around his fat body, despite it looking like a thick diaper. He smlies at your reaction... You don't want to keep seeing this!

    "Okay Sir! I'm sorry, I shouldn't have thought that out loud! Please change it back!" You are blushing at the thought of seeing him in a diaper, and not only that. He apparently can do a tricks like that. He would have costumes that centers on baby clothes in someway. But…

    "Good!" He covers himself again. "As a Sumo I prefer to wear my belt, more then my diaper." He looks himself over, he now wears his red mawashi again. As if nothings change. "... Ah! Much better! Though if you want to give that Adult Baby, or Diaper Lover lifestyle a try. I do have costumes that you can try! Ones that will make your life like a baby, or your life altered in a way that you've always wore diapers. Rather your life is just the same, but you're a Diaper Lover, or you always needed them..." He gave a suggestion.

    You just look at him weird. "I just... I want to forget what I just saw." He nods, as he rubs his Sumo belt. "Of course! Just so you know. I do have options on the Sumo Wrestler lifestyle. Ones where you are competing in America, or in Japan! Ones where your body alters to fit the role of a sumo, or like me where you only change slightly to fit your new role." He says as he finishes tying a knot on his Yukata. "So Wally-san… What do you say?... Want to give it a try?"

    You did sort of, indirectly insult his new clothes, it's only fair to give it a chance... "Maybe... Let me think about it." With your silence he continues.


    "Where was I?... Oh yes! Your new life! Are you ready for me to show you more Lion variants we have? Fair warning, I do also have an anthro lion variant that is a Sumo Wrestler! Or do you have something else in mind?"
