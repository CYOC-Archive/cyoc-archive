    It rested on it's belly patting at a water filled mat as It chortled watching the funny fish move around. It had tried the best It could to regress itself and it was extremely successful. It no longer remembered or cared about the children It once loved. All of those thoughts had long been stripped away from It. It only had the mental capacity of a 3 month old. It's nurse watched as something squishy pushed its way out into the white thing It had on. It's nurse moved it's patient lift over It so that she could get the white thing off It and into a new one.

    In six-year-old Rhianna's room, She was patiently waiting for her meal of stage one baby food and sippy cup. The girl had gotten gender confirming surgery and attenuation therapy. She was buckled in her pink special needs stroller with her knee abductor in place. She excitedly kicked her foot board as her nurse snapped her feeding tray in and placed a pink silicone kitty bib with crumb catcher around her neck. Her pig tails stuck out of her hard pink special helmet. Finally, Rhianna heard the pop of the lid of her food and her nurse dipped a pink silicone spoon into the banana baby food and made airplane noises as she guided it into the toothless tot's mouth. The food was very tasty, but was soon followed by Rhianna's infant formula in a pink baby bottle with two handles. She grasped the two handles of her baby bottle and started to suck on the tasty drink.

    In Another part of the building in a shared room, two nine year old girls were lying on their backs in nothing but thick white slimy wet diapers and thick yellow nursery print mittens in their sound proof dimly lit incubators, but couldn't see each other. All the two babies heard were the sounds of a heart beat like they would hear if they were in the womb that was piped into speakers that were embedded into their incubators. Their nurse was sitting in a corner of the dark room quietly taking notes. Ventilators were piping in oxygen through their noses while feeding pumps fed a white substance through a tube that went through their tummies and into their stomach. It contained everything they needed for nourishment, water and loose bowel movements. Much like Monica, Dana was rendered blind, growth attenuated, and devoid of breast buds and ovaries. Both babies didn't have a speck of hair on their bodies due to laser hair removal and electrolysis. All they could do was wiggle and shake as their their muscles randomly spasmed.

    The director was pleased with each student's regression. He still had work to do on Rhrianna and It. But It in particular provided the most data of all his students. Dana and Monica were also interesting because they had regressed down to the mind of premies. He didn't have time to dawdle because he had to get a set of 11 year old twins sorted out in their rooms. The government had started to send orphans to his school much to his delight. He wondered how many would join his school.

    In another part of town, Madame Illusia cackled into the night.
