    Dakota and Harper were heading down to a designated "cool-off zone" after their latest "raptor hunt" in Dino City when they were met by a PR-MD agent with an unusual request.

    "This has to be the most unusual result of the pandemic." said the agent, still shaking his head at what had been requested.

    "Why? What?" asked Dakota.

    "We know that, with more people staying home, Animals were moving in." said the PR-MD agent, "However, the problem we got contacted for is turkeys."

    "Excuse me?" asked Harper, both confused and incredulous.

    "You heard me right, turkeys." confirmed the PR-MD agent, "They're causing quite a bit of trouble in a few towns. However, they're inviting predator Animalians and/or Dinotopians to participate in a 'turkey hunt'."

    "Excuse me?" asked Harper again, now more incredulous than confused.

    "Well, Thanksgiving's coming up, and Animalia has a fine way of having predators 'indulge their instincts', so to speak."

    "... alright." said Dakota, before a notification sounded on the table to get ready for their next 'attack'.
