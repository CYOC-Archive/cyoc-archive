    Michelle Wong stepped out onto the porch of the cabin and started to smoke her cigarette. As she looked through the woods, she noticed something moving not far away in the bushes. She thought about telling someone about it, but she didn't want to ruin the good mood everyone was in. She tossed her cigarette down as she quietly made her way over to the rustling bush and prayed that it was just a lost animal. Michelle looked over to see something bent over looking like it was having a meal and saw that it was rather large and wanted to take a step closer to it, she stepped on a branch breaking it make a sound and the creature turned around to show it was a skin walker.

    Michelle wanted to scream, but she couldn't, fear took her as she crawled backward on the ground as the creature crept closer to her with it's mouth drooling and it raised it's hand and was about to strike when she heard another strike come. Michelle looked down at herself to see she was still alive. Michelle looked to see the skin walker was beheaded and looked behind the creature to see two women standing behind the creature. One was a teenage girl dressed in a Victorian funeral gown complete with pillbox hat and veil while the other wore a blue Victorian gown and had a parasol with a hidden blade at the bottom that had sliced the creature's neck and looked a little older.

    "How was that Mistress?" The one in blue had asked. "Exquisite darling. I see you even lured a human here for a little meal." The younger one said as she produced vampire fangs.

    "If you bite me, I'll call my magic friends for help." Michelle warned the woman. The woman stopped before she could go any closer and looked back to the cabin she had come from.

    "Does anyone in that cabin go by the name 'Moreland'?" The young girl asked. "Uh...yes. Two in fact." Michelle replied. "Damn it, I would stumble on someone who knows those bastards. Come along Duchess, we'll hunt more skin walkers elsewhere." The young girl said as she turned to walk away, the older woman almost following her.

    "Duchess....Jenkins?" Michelle asked as the two stopped to turn and look at her.

    "I'm...I'm with the DHS. I had read about you and you vanished after turning in your partner for corruption. Are you....a vampire now?" Michelle asked.

    "Indeed I am. Let me tell you, I have never felt more alive than when Mistress Cassandra turned me into a vampire." Duchess Jenkins said.

    "The world isn't black and white, agent, it's always gray. That's what my beloved father, used to tell me." Cassandra said taking out a locket and looking at a picture of her father. "Vampires aren't all evil, we're just misunderstood. The lord of the vampires has been making great changes to the world to better our kind and one day, if you're interested, call us." Cassandra said as she took out a card with a phone number on it and slipped it into Michelle's breast.

Cassandra said as Duchess stood up. "Yes, of course my mistress." Duchess Jenkins said as she wrapped her arm around Cassandra's arm and they walked off together.

She decided to head back to the cabin and not speak a word of this to the others. Instead she put the card back into her breast and went back to the porch and got out another cigarette.
