    Your attention goes to the clothing vials.

    You've been into the idea of becoming clothing for some time now. You love imagining how nice it must feel like to be worn so close to someone. You've thought about being shirts and sticking to your wearer's torso, about being pants and hugging their legs and crotch. You've even given thought to the fact that you'd be absorbing their body's sweat and smell, and that has never made it seem less appealing at all.

    You look through the vast array of vials, trying not to freak out. So many options! There's the basic ones: underwear, pants, footwear, sportswear, shirts, dresses and such, but there's also stuff like gloves, hoodies, swimwear, pajamas, maid uniforms... After thinking long and hard, and checking all of your options, you reach towards a vial.
