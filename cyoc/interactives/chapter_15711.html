Curious, he scroll over "Heroic Proportions" and an information box popped up, explaining: THE PROPORTIONS OF A HERO, TAKEN FROM THE COMMON TERMS FROM CLASSIC STATUARY. TALLER AND BROADER THAN AN AVERAGE PERSON, WITH ATTRIBUTES MAGNIFIED.

    That sounded good, so Bryan checked it, as well as Mythic Proportions, which was likely just more of the same. Classical Proportions sounded good too, so he gave it a check.

    There was a button that said "Adult Options," so Brian clicked it, finding one of the first items that seemed clickable was "Dick Size" (nexted to a greyed out "Breast Size") He chuckled. "Kewl!" This was rad.

He slid the two over so that the rarity for the size to be one in 500 million. The explanation showed that this would make him 19.54" long, with proportional girth, at his current height. (WARNING: Other options and modifications selected can and may affect this. CLICK FOR EXPLANATION.)

Both of his read average, so he slid the testosterone over to 100% increase, then did the HGH for good measure. He then clicked on the Masculinity Index, which was on a new panel labeled "Sexual Dimorphism." It appeared to go on a scale from 1 to 30. Bryan found that it listed his score currently at 10, but mentioned "After applied changes, combined Masculinity index will average 24." He clicked the box for explanation, finding that a whole range of secondary sex characteristics: genital size, body hair, muscle distribution, fat distribution, shoulder to hip ratio, jaw size, brow size and so forth were used to calculate the index, and his extreme penis size and additional testosterion selections were raising the base average.

He popped that up just for the hell of it, increased his character's IQ by 50 points, then clicked a box that said "Normalized Outlook" which sounded better than going down to all the sexual preferences, check boxes for fetishes, and everything else.

    WARNING! the Chronivac flashed. CLASSICAL PROPORTIONS OPTION IN CONFLICT WITH OTHER OPTIONS. DESELECT OR APPLY CHANGES IN STAGES?

His penis increased massively, tenting out the front of his pants as his balls grew to match. Not that he could see that well because his nose was suddenly dividing his field of vision.

    Bryan's dick, balls, hands, feet, adam's apple and nose all continued to grow, becoming not just the largest to be seen in 500 million average men, but 500 million heroically proportioned freaks!

    His hands were nearly the size of his head, except for the fact that a third of his head was his nose, and his adam's apple stuck out like a second chin.

    APPLYING HORMONAL INCREASE. DOING SO SIMULTANEOUSLY.

    HORMONAL LEVELS, BODY HAIR AND INTELLIGENCE ALL FREAKISH. INCREASING.
