    Immediately embraced Cass as she walked towards the table she was sitting at. The spread there looked just as big to the one she had for breakfast. There were multiple stacks of sandwiches of all different types, each at least 10 sandwiches tall, there was a fruit salad in a bowl the size of a trash can, and there were dips, yogurt and sauces on one side of the lunch room. On the other side, there were all different kinds of meat arranged from raw to medium rare, burgers, potatoes, and sides like nachos and fried pickles. Jen took off her hoodie and shirt before letting her stomach take over. She went hard on both sides of the table with reckless abandon, shoving all the morsels of food into her mouth. Her body starting bloating out more and more as she kept shoving food into her mouth. The whole student body worked to clear the table of food in about 30 minutes. Some were greedily patting their stomachs, begging for more, while others were laid out on the floor, belching and rubbing their distended bellies as they groaned, having no control until they hit their limit. Some bloated chefs brought out another table loaded with even more food as those still hungry didn’t even let them park the table and move out of the way before digging in. Jen looked at Cass, she was still going strong, so Jen kept on eating. As the students got even fatter, Jen started to feel full, but she kept eating anyway. A lot more students ended up tapping out this time, to the point where the only ones left were the larger students, Cass, Jen, and a miserable looking rat that looked like he couldn’t move or eat anymore still trying to shove a loaf of bread in his mouth. The chefs came back with one final table, and Jen’s jaw dropped. This table was loaded with several multi-layered cakes, ice cream the size of shrubs, a giant chocolate fountain, a giant fondue bowl, s’mores, donut towers, churros, cinnamon rolls, and all sorts of parties and desserts piled high and spread out wide along this table. Jen looked back at Cass, she didn’t look like she was gonna move at all, but one unruly rumble of her stomach later, she seemed to lose herself as she dived into some of the cakes. Jen had the exact same thing happen to her, as her consciousness blacked out.

    Waking up on the floor of the cafeteria, Jen felt like she was being held to the floor by chains. She felt like she had been filled with sand, everything felt like it wanted to stay on the ground. She looked down at her body and could only see her massive belly, but she did notice that there was a ribbon on her tummy just above her bellybutton. She tried to move with some difficulty, but managed to at least sit up as her belly bounced on the floor between her legs. She rubbed her stomach as she picked up the ribbon and looked at it. It said, “Finish the lunch buffet!” Jen couldn’t believe she ate all the food on all three tables, but her stomach’s taut and bloated nature didn’t lie. Belching loudly, Jen looked around to find Cass, who fell asleep rubbing her stomach. Rolling over to her, she kissed her and fell asleep beside her.
