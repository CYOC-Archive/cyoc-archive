    Brenda the zodiac witch

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago A BM S Magic

    A guard was monitoring the pile of comics, but for a moment he noticed something a little off, like one of the comics was getting out of the pile, so he decided to open a little the glass and take that comic, only reading zodiac witches without paying attention to the rest of the cover. Suddenly a magic breeze appeared even if it was a closed room, the comic flew away to under the door of the room, the comic kept flying until it went through many doors and finally scaped the containment prison.

    Many agents alarmed and searched through all the prison, even sending people outside to search for the comic. Meanwhile the comic was flying just getting carried away by the wind, arriving at the top of a city and falling into a girl's house through the window.

    The girl was writing on her desk making her homework. Her name was Brenda and was a 18 year old studying in school, but she always found school boring, so she spent many of her time reading comics as a hobby, because of her addiction to reading she didn't have any friends and was seen as the nerdy weird girl, but she didn't care, just wanted school or even her life to be entertaining as her comics.

    But that night seemed that Brenda's thoughts were listened, seeing how a comic fell at her side on her desk.

    "Zodiac Witches #15? I don't even know this existed" Brenda said holding the comic. She decided to do a quick research on internet but found nothing about it, making her curiosity grow.

    "Well, just see what's this", she opened the comic and a big shine came from the pages, almost leaving her blind.

    Brenda opened her eyes again cleaning her glasses, seeming to be in a different city, looking attractive 12 women in front of her, the women looked exactly to the ones in the comic book cover, each one with clothes and features from different animals, the animals being a rat, ox, tiger, rabbit, pig, dog, horse, goat, snake, rooster, monkey and a woman in front of all of them being the dragon.

    Brenda approached them nervously, tapping the shoulder of one of them.

    "Excuse m-me..." Brenda blushed, "can you explain me who are you, and where I am?"

    All the women slowly moved their gazes to Brenda.

    "OH OH! You're new, right?" The rabbit themed girl said enthusiastically.

    "Yeah, I haven't see you here, could she be...?" The snake woman looked at the dragon one.

    "You... Opened the comic... Right?" The dragon woman asked her.

    "Y-yep! I found you on my desk and decided to give it a chance" Brenda explained.

    "That's awesome, we finally have a new host!" The dragon woman smiled looking at everyone.

    "Wh-what do you mean by that?" Brenda confusedly asked.

    "Well as you see, we are part of this comic, we are witches and each one is specialized in a magic of a different animal, we can control, transform into feral or anthro forms of our animals, even fully of partially and controlling our size" the dragon explained.

    "Yes! And it's really cool" the dog woman said, then she got bigger and her clothes ripped, turning into a giant naked anthro dog woman, "and we are the queens of this place, no one can stop us!" She began to pee on a car nearby.

    "Gross, why you always have to do that?" The monkey woman asked.

    "Well I think I get it, but what do you mean with a new host" Brenda asked.

    The dragon woman stood in front of her, "as you see, we're trapped here, it's fine and all, but we want more! So we can give you all of our powers and send you to your world and use them there, we would feel it every time you use us, isn't that cool?" The dragon said.

    Brenda didn't think too much about it, with this she could make her life more interesting, having fun with the girls at her school taking revenge of what they did to her, and transform into a giant animal and walk through the city doing what she wants. "Deal!" Brenda stretched her arm.

    "Let's unleash the chaos" the dragon woman shaked her hand, then all the 12 witches dissolved in magical smokes of different colours, the smokes being absorbed by Brenda as her eyes shone, looking the big shine again and appearing in her room.

    Brenda felt powerful, and for testing her powers she changed her hand into a tiger claw, meaning everything was real. Brenda was ready to use her new abilities.
