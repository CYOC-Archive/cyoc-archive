    “Father, where does mom keep my were-birth control pills?” Frieda asks as both leave the kitchen.
    “In the medicine cabinet in the bathroom,” Benton answers.
    “Good,” Frieda answers marching to her mother’s bathroom; “This is one morning tradition that ends here and now.” Frieda grabs her pills and takes one; Frieda marches back to her room with pill bottle in hand.
    Benton looks slightly bemused as he proceeds to get ready to visit his wife in the hospital and for his mid afternoon press conference.

    Frieda is back in her room placing her were- birth control pills on her bedside table; she heads to her private washroom to shower and get ready for her day. After a an hour in the bathroom she gets back out and puts on a matching green skirt and blouse, with black stockings and black shoes. Finishing getting dressed she comments; "Eric and Spencer aren't the only one's that can dress seductively."
    Frieda about put on a bracelet when she hears the front door bell ring.
    “Frieda get that it’s probably the junk removal people!” Benton shouts.

    Frieda opens the door to see Mike Lawson.
    “Mr. Lawson what are you doing here?”
    “Came to collect my car,” He answers.
    “You didn’t need to ring the bell,” Frieda answers; “If my father opened the door, there is no telling what he would do.”
    “Yet I figured if I just drove off without anyone knowing,” Mike grins; “then Benton would be freaking out thinking he is about to be charged with auto theft.” Mike starts his car up and drives off.

    Frieda is about to close the door when the junk truck arrives. Three people climb out of the cab and the driver is the first to speak.
    “Hello, would Benton Knight be home?”
    “My father is here,” Frieda states; “but he is busy.” She presses a button to open the garage door; “He asked me to show you that the trash is in the garage. But I must warn you, scratch his car and I can’t be responsible for what happens.”

    The driver eyes Frieda questioningly but when he sees Benton’s Lamborghini Veneno he lets out a whistle, “That’s some car.”
    “Just remember,” Frieda warns; “One scratch and there will be hell to pay.”

    The driver nods and instructs his helpers to start collecting the bags. With the three people working, the large mound where the town car usually sits is quickly removed. Benton shortly comes out to view the work being done. Despite most of his suits being ripped and torn, Benton still had a few suits left and was able to look like the respectable business man. Impressed with the quick work he pays them and adds a small bonus. They thank him and leave.

    As Benton sees the junk removal truck leaves he notices Mike Lawson car missing.
    “Did they take that car as well?” Benton looking alarmed.
    “No,” Frieda answers; “Mike Lawson came by a few minutes ago to collect it.
    “Did you witness that?” Benton asking
    “Yes,” Frieda answering; “Now can we go see mom so we can both do what we want after today?”
    “Alright,” Benton answers; “But first we need to stop at a gas station. My Veneno needs gas.”
