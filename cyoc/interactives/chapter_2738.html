He rubbed the lamp again and said, "I wish to return to my natural human form instantaneously(he didnt want to have to go through another slow change)." "Ow" Drake laughed a bit and said, "Dont tell ANYONE about this Ethan" "Dont worry Drake, I'm not stupid, they wouldnt believe me anyway."
