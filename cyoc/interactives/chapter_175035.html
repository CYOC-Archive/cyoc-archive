    “You got it, big guy! You deserve to be big and strong because you’re NICE to me!” Ty responded firmly.

    “TY! You’re not allowed to have guests over and the kid needs to leave right n--…” Dustin said as he marched into the room.

    Finn gave Dustin an angry look as he got up from the bed. Finn stood up, towering over Dustin by 2 inches, with more than 30 pounds of muscle.

    “What are you talking about, little man?” Finn growled at Dustin.

    Ty decided to help Finn out some more by giving him another growth spurt. Dustin needed to be taught a lesson!

His legs grew longer, wider and thicker as his quads and calves exploded with even MORE muscle! The boy suddenly shot up, growing to 6-foot-8 and tipping the scale at 280 pounds of solid muscle!

Finn’s pecs stuck out almost a foot from his frame while his arms were as thick as pythons. Not to mention, the boy had the legs of an Olympian! Dustin quickly realized that he was just no match for the Hulking kid, who was clearly determined to protect and defend Ty no matter what!

    A huge arm extended from the Hulking kid ending in a large fist grabbing hold of Dustin’s shirt, finally lifting Dustin off the ground like a rag doll.

    “I can come and visit ty anytime I want to and you can’t stop me! And, if you ever hurt Ty, I will come over here and I will with you in person, do you hear me?” Finn’s voice boomed in Dustin’s head before Finn tossed the older brother out of Ty’s bedroom through the open door like a piece of paper.

    Then, Finn turned towards Ty and picked him up, holding him in his huge arms like a baby.

    “Are you okay, sweetie?” Finn asked him.

    “I’m doing great, big guy! As long as you are here, I’m doing just wonderful!” Ty smiled, looking up at the large muscle boy.

    Finn raised him up and kissed Ty on his cheeks. Being with Ty made him so incredibly happy.
