    Jake uses his new powers at the Rec Center

    When Jake walked in the recreation center, he knew something was different about him. He felt like he knew everything about everybody who was there. He knew their feelings, emotions, their problems and their desires. He knew their struggles and the things they experienced that day. As he strolled into the locker room, he saw Lucas, one of the teenage boys, sitting on the bench.

    Right away, he understood that Lucas, who was age 14, was frustrated with another boy at school who had been bullying him. Lucas was short and skinny and he had started working out but he hadn’t seen any gains yet.

    “Hey dude, how’s going?” Jake said to the boy.

    “It’s going…” Lucas sighed, as he got dressed to start his work-out.

    “Is that big Jonathan-dude giving you a hard time again?” Jake asked him.

    “”Yeah… he’s a jerk… Wait! How do you know about that?” Lucas wondered out loud.

    “I… Uhh… I just know… That’s all… You should be stronger, Lucas…” Jake chuckled.

    “Well… I am working out… But I’m not making any gains!” Lucas grumbled.

    “Oh… Maybe I can help you?” Jake responded. “You can use some more muscle in your arms, chest, and legs!”

    “Yeah, but I don’t--- HEY!” Lucas replied when he felt that weird cramp in all his muscles like he was having a huge growth spurt. Then his muscles began to inflate, just as Jake commanded!

    Within seconds, Lucas’ arms bulged, his biceps became huge, his pectorals enlarged dramatically, his quads and calves increased and his stomach was covered with well-defined abs! His shoulders widened as his lats spread out like wings! His quads were as wide as oak trees as the boy just stood there, in the locker room.

    “HOLY SHIT!” Lucas exclaimed as he went from scrawny to a muscle kid in seconds. “JAKE! How… Did you… do that?”

    Jake just smiled. That was incredibly easy. He just spoke a few words and Lucas saw his muscles expand like crazy!

    Lucas ran over to the mirror and examined his new body. He couldn’t believe it! Then he walked back to where Jake was standing.

    “What the hell? Did you make my muscles grow?”

    Jake just nodded. Lucas’ pecs were almost ripping through his tank top. His legs were so thick and wide that his shorts could barely contain them.

    “Let me help you with your clothes, big guy!” Jake grinned.

    Then, Lucas’ clothes grew as well, and they fit him perfectly now. Lucas and Jake walked out of the locker room and back into the recreation center where the weight room was. But Jake didn’t go into the weight room right away. He decided to walk around and see who else he could help with his cool, new powers…
