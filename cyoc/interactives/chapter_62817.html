    "Man, this is a really heavy costume" Ellan said, contemplating the huge mass of fur he was holding. All of a
    sudden he found the still opened flop on the backside of the costume. Without noticing, his right arm made
    it's way to the kangaroo's right arm, and somehow his hand was locked in place inside. "What the fuck!!" Ellan
    said, trying to pull his hand outside the costume, but he didn't notice a chair on his way and he tripped,
    falling face-first inside the costume. All of a sudden, the costume started envolving his whole body, and the
    zipper misteriously made it's way back up, encasing him inside and dissolving his clothes; even the hands of
    the costume reached the Kangaroo head an placed it on top of Ellan's. When Ellan realized, he was completely
    trapped inside the kangaroo fursuit, just like you were just moments ago!

    inside the suit he started to forgot everything that happened because of it. He doesn't notice how his legs
    start to feel more powerful, his new tail swinging around on it's own, his arms and shoulders and chest
    starting to look very ripped - just as you wearing the costume looked before...

    He runs to your room to admire himself in the mirror, and begins to grin broadly, running his paws over his
    muzzle and ears. His body feels so good inside the suit, it's almost as if the suit -is- his body. He start to
    get scared - It's happening again! But it feels so good, his body couldn't deny the pleasure he was getting
    now. His mind is paralyzed but his body responds to the pleasure running through him, just as you return to
