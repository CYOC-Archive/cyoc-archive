If and when I do decide to assume the mantle leadership with Knight Industries, it will be when I am ready.”

    Lance just sighs as he pulls up to the Anderson house hold, while he was hoping to talk more; the rest he had to say did was for Frieda's ears only. Lance just sighs as he decides to discuss the remainder of what he wanted tomorrow in private with his niece. Looking at the three as they are about to leave.

Eric, Frieda, you may be excused from school tomorrow, but that in no way means you should stay up late. You need to be at the council building tomorrow at roughly the same time school starts.”
    “Will you be picking us up uncle,” Frieda asking; “also will we be required to stay there for the entire duration?”
    “Of course I’ll pick you two up,” Lance answering; “just remember to need to be in your fursona when in front of the council. Don’t worry about needing robes, they will be provided for you tommorow. As for having to stay for the entire session, I’ll talk to Tachibana about that tomorrow morning.”
    “Why not tonight,” Eric asks?
    “I have my bi-polar testing to do,” Lance answering; “plus I have to head over to the gazette and deal with tomorrow’s edition.”
    “Will we wake up to hearing DJ Woof on the radio,” Frieda asking?
    “No,” Lance answering; “just so you know, I had to spend my entire morning at KPXX dealing with him and a few other issues that I wasn’t made aware of. DJ Woof will not be on the air anymore. I had him fired.”
