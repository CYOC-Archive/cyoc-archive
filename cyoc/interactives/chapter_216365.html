    Jennifer was still trying to process the first half of the first sentence, let alone the stream of pure nerd that came after. Her arms immediately pinned up in a loose T-shaped pose. She tried to wrestle her arms free but her muscles were locked into place. She could slightly rotate her wrists. She found she could look around; but the void didn't show much.

    Suddenly, Jennifer heard Kyle's voice: "Ah, there you are! Took a second to load. I guess it's a little longer when there's an entire human consciousness behind it." Jennifer spat, "What kind of sick joke is this you needledick nerd? You better hope I never get fre-" her audio cut off. She was still "saying" the words but she no longer heard her voice issuing out. "That's enough of that."
