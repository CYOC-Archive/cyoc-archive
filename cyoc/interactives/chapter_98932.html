    Mary Lou cursed as she ran through the zoo looking for Adam. She had tried using her nose to track Adam, like she had for Evelyn earlier, but after the last transformation all of the scents were less potent, and everything smelled too similar.

    That stupid fool! He'll surely get us caught, she thought. Suddenly, she saw movement on the path ahead. She hid in the shadows of a nearby building, waiting to see who it was from a hidden position. Has the staff arrived already? she thought. I may have to just leave and take a chance of surviving on my own.

He had broad shoulders, and thick biceps. He abdomen was ripped with a well-developed six-pack. His thighs were equally large, nearly as thick as tree trunks. When he stood beneath a nearby lamppost, Mary Lou was shocked to see a somewhat familiar face. It was Adam!

    So, this is what the 'shampoo' did to him, thought Mary Lou. Nice, very nice.

    As he stood beneath the lamppost, she saw that he was covered by a thin layer of scales, that glimmered slightly in the light. He turned, and she saw a thick short tail, covered by a sprinkling of short round spines. He lifted his tail a little as he turned again, and Mary Lou saw a firm, tight ass. Yes, very nice indeed, she thought. She was a little surprised to find that she was getting sexually excited. She was not in season, so she was confused why this male was affecting her in this way.

When she was smaller, and not quite as large as her final length of 19 feet, the zoo staff had introduced some bull alligators in her pen, in attempt to get her to breed. She tolerated the first few attempts, but the experience was not pleasant. The males just laid on her back like a lead weight, clumsily shoving their organs around her cloaca until they released their seed. She felt no pleasure from the process. Later, when she was larger, she fought any male that the zookeepers placed in her pen (she actually killed a few of the more aggressive and stupid ones), and the zoo staff eventually stopped adding new males to her enclosure.

The zoo staff sometimes made love in the hidden paths the employees used to get around the zoo unseen by the tourists. She guessed making love near the animals was some kind of thrill for them. Like the annoying primates, they made loud caterwauling noises, moans that sounded like it was enjoyable. Occasionally, she actually saw them making love, and she was perplexed by the strange positions they assumed. They didn't seem to make sense, but by the smiles on their faces, she assumed that the positions somehow increased the pleasure of mating. Mary Lou had to admit, that even as an alligator she envied them a little bit, and wished she had the flexibility to try mating in a more enjoyable way. She looked down at her body, stretching and flexing her arms and legs. She experimented with how far she could bend, mimicking some of the strange positions she remembered.

She was a monster to him, not in the least bit appealing to him. What had he called her - hideous? He would probably laugh at the creature's attempts to imitate the humans she despised so much. He called again, and this time Mary Lou could hear what he was saying. "Evelyn, Evelyn, Is that I you?"

    Mary Lou smiled. This might be just the opportunity she had been hoping for.

    She left her hidden position in the shadow of the building, and walked towards the light of the lamppost with Adam.
