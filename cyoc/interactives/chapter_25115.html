    Pleasure Island

    Arrival

    You step off of the ferry and onto the sands of Pleasure Island. The sun is beating down hard and a large group of passengers make their way off of the ferry pushing past you.

    Your name is Max and you are 19. You have lightly tanned skin, spiked up dirty blonde hair and stand at 5'6" tall. You're not a muscular guy but you're not chubby either, you have an average build. You're wearing a white t-shirt, navy blue shorts and brown sandles.

    You walk across the sand and make your way off the beach to go and see what you can do on Pleasure Island. As you look around you spot a balloon salesman. Since you were little you've never been able to resist the urge to inhale the helium and make your voice all squeaky. You head over to the salesman and get your wallet out in antisipation of paying.
    "You're an eager one aren't you?" States the salesman as you approach him.
    "I guess. So how much are they?" You ask.
    "First time to the island?"
    "Yeah sure."
    "Then this one's free. Choose whichever you like." The salesman brings his balloons down closer to you so that you can easily see them all. You don't take long to pull one out as you're only going to suck the helium out anyway.
    "A fine choice, goodbye." Says the salesman as he waves you off.
    You walk away and begin to untie the knot in the balloon. You raise the balloon to your lips and inhale a small amount of the gas. You quickly re-tie the knot and begin to speak but notice that your voice hasn't changed. A little disappointed you accidentally let go of the balloon and it begins to float away. The balloon floats quickly but you don't want to lose it so you reach, grabbing for it.
    You stop suddenly, letting the balloon fly off, as you realise you can reach further than you could before. It might not have been helium after all. You reach a little further stretching out your arm to a good 4 feet in length and your arm shows no signs off having a limit. You look to your other arm and stretch that one out to the same length. You're a little freaked out and even a little turned on by the changes but it doesn't stop you from exploring a bit more. You try to make your arms thicker and find that they seem to inflate as they grow in size.
    "Cool!" You think to yourself. "It's time to have a little fun and see what my new body can do."
    You return your arms to normal and then stretch out your torso to make yourself tall enough to survey your surroundings and choose your next destination. So, where do you head off to?
