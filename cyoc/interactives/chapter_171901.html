    A Game of Change

    Turn 1: Joy

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Jack 3 years ago A BM Aquatic

    "Hey Joy, how about you go first?" Diane blurts out. In truth, Diane was scared of taking the first turn herself. Joy looked up in surprise. "Me? Uh... okay..." she says sheepishly as she looks down at the board. The board itself wasn't all too intricate. It resembled somewhat of a multi biome map, with lagoons, trees, deserts and mountains. Different squares had different colours, and in the center of the board was a small convex piece of glass. It didn't seem to have a function... the creepy part of the game was that each board piece looked exactly like their real life counter part. They also seemed to be in the player's favourite color (Diane had purple, Cyndi had green, Joy had teal, Sue had red, and Jerri had Yellow). Joy cautiously picked up the two 6 sided dice, and cast them onto the board. She rolled a 7. She reached for her piece, but was shocked to find that it started moving on it's own... "What the fuck..." and "Woah..." escaped the Cyndi and Jerri's lips. As Joy's piece came to halt upon a red square in the Lagoon, text appeared on the glass slab in the middle of the board. "Both on land and in water, you will start your journey as Poseidon's daughter" Joy looked up at the others, confused. "So is this a riddle game then?" She said, the rest of the girls chuckling. Joy's sly comment was soon followed by an exclamation. "Oh my go- OH!" She shouted, almost moaning. Joy felt immense pressure building up in her body, centering at her hands. She brought them in front of her, as she and the rest of the girls started witnessing them change. As she spread her fingers, blue tinted webbing started to grow in between the digits. Their eyes widened as it spread up all the way to her fingertips. She panted, looking at her webbed hands in disbelief. "Joy... your hands..." Sue said, near speechless. That wasn't the end of her turn however, as a new jolt of energy shocked her. "Ah! No... what now..?!" She said as she felt the pressure build up in her feet this time. Quickly getting her socks off, she soon realized that the same thing was happening to her feet. Webbing, tinted blue, grew in between her toes. As she watched on, Joy also noticed her toenails getting longer, more curved, and what seemed to be sharper too! As her change came to an end, the girls all looked at her, mouths wide open. "Joy... are you okay..?! Your feet! Your hands!" Diane exclaimed. They all got closer to Joy, supporting their girlfriend if need be. Joy simply stared at her webbed hands and feet, astonished at how this game has proven itself to be real. "Who's next?" Joy asks, holding out the dice in her webbed hands.
