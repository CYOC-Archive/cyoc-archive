    added by ratX 2 years ago A Insect

    as he enter the ride he sees a hexagonal door where a constant waterfall of a golden sticky honey like substance dropping trough, a screen says to pass one person at a time, he didnt want to be all sticky from passing trough the honey waterfall but his daughter had already passed so he didnt have an option, as he passed trough the substance covered all of him once on the other side he cleaned his face and saw he was now in an open prairie with a giant beehive in the distance and giant flowers all around, then he noticed a strange feeling as the substance that was covering the majority of his body started to dry up, turning black on his arms and legs with and yellow with black stripes on his torso it was becoming something like a bee costume...
    - hey dad look i can fly, the voice of his daughter from above
    looking up he sees his daughter in a similar bees costume flying by moving the wings of the costume
    - come on dad, just think of moving your wings really really fast
    he looked at the wing that had grown out of the costume with every doubt in his mind but if his daugther could he had to try it,
    he focused on the wings thinking of moving them fast and they started to respond slow at first but after a few second they started to generate lift allowing him to fly at the same high as his daughter was
    - yay daddy can fly too
    - yeah this is pretty amazing, now where do we go, what bee thing do you want to do?
