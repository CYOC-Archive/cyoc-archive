    "I'll take care of you."

    The giant lowered his hand and extended one of his enormous fingers and brushed it across the front of Bryan's chest.

    He laughed, not angrily, as Bryan retreated a few steps.

    "You'll get used to that," Martin thought to himself. "You'll get used to that and a lot more."
