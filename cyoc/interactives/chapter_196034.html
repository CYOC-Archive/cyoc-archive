    Cindy's jaw dropped. That sounded exactly like something Ron would do. How was this app doing this? She was about to select Jim when she noticed that there was a little extra sentence in a speech bubble next to Ron's sentence. It read "Change one word or refresh for a new sentence!"

    "What does all of this mean?" She thought to herself. Could a silly little app really do anything?

    She decided to try and find out once and for all. She selected the word "procrastinates" and thought of things that could have been better.
    "Rich?" No, that didn't make sense.
    "Sing?" Useless.

She felt herself get her bearings as she heard a bestial grunt in front of her. Cindy's eyes drifted in shock as she watched Ron heft a metal dumbbell, sitting across from her in a gym shirt and shorts, his dirty blonde hair had been cut down a tad while his once chubby face had been reduced to where his jawline could be seen.

    "93... 94...95..." Ron growled, music had been playing in his newly minted sport earphones, scrolling with his free hand as the bicep bulged.

    Cindy gasped as she saw Ron's form and he turned to her, before winking and pausing his scrolling.

    "See something you like, baby?" Ron said. Even his voice had changed, deepening slightly to where she could stand to hear it more often.

    "Oh, of course..." Cindy started, before noticing Ron's lustful gaze drifting down to her bosom. She blushed, before Ron put down his phone and switched the dumbbell into his other hand.

    "...And the job..?" Cindy questioned, wondering what was going on. It was almost like it was a new dream altogether as Ron looked back up and shook himself out of his stupor and smiled.

    "Yeah, Xane's covering me at the gym today so I came earlier, wanted uh," Ron took a moment to lick his lips,
    "...To have some quality time."

In this new reality, she was slowly realizing, Ron had a job and was possibly a much better lover. She looked over to the television and saw the smaller tv she once had was replaced with a newer modeled flat screen and a PS4 that Jim possibly had due to the wealth influx.

    The living room had lost its messy appearance, the dust on the floor and coach cushions strewn over the area had become a modernized aesthetic, with touches that Cindy had once dreamed to have rather than wistfully decorating in her mind.

    She could still hear Jim playing his video games, but could clearly hear him speaking as if to new friends he hadn't had before and she sighed, this was the life... Before hitching her breath as Ron slipped a greedy hand into her chest and groped her pliable breast.

    "Mm, I love when you call me that," Ron spoke, licking his lips, before leaning in to whisper into Cindy's ear.
    "I wanna hear you scream it."

    Cindy whined as she got up to Ron's amusement.

    "We can't, not here, I, what if Jim comes?" She hastily buttoned her dress shirt.

    Ron chuckled, ripping off his weightlifting gloves and picking up a phone in his hands and putting it into his pocket.

    "Then we'll do a... Private workplace meeting, yeah?" He said, coming towards his wife and placing a kiss that Cindy melted, before taking her into his arms and lifting her easily into his arms.

    "Ron!" Cindy squealed, "You're so strong!"

    Her husband laughed, easily carrying her up the stairs.

    "And I'll only get stronger from here baby, all those workouts are good for something right?"

    Ron quickly took her into their room and sat her down onto the bed, he turned and walked and peeked his head outside to see if Jim had seen them... Before gleefully locking the door. He tore off his shirt as Cindy threw off hers and was about to crawl onto his wife when he stopped.

    "Something wrong, Ron..?" Cindy asked, out of breath.

    Ron felt an annoying vibration in his pocket and recognized a call, swiping his phone from his pocket in one swoop.

    "One sec, I think work's calling, just gimme a minute and we'll be back to cardio." He said confidently, turning away and opening the door and stepping out of the room.

    "Hurry on back, stud!" Cindy called out to him, to which Ron smirked as he answered the phone call.
