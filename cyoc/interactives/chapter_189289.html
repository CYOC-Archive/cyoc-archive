    Ty's Power

    A family of dogs

    “What have we ever done to you?” Helen asked between her sobs before Ty made a decision.
    Ty grinned at her. “You did nothing. I just have this power, and have fun using it on others. And maybe I wanted to get out something of it in the end.” With that, he raised the phone of her husband.
    “You… did this for your fun? And for my husbands’ phone? You monster” Helen screamed, horrified by the boy before her.
    Ty just shook his head at her. “Yeah, I did. But now I have to deal with you, and by extension, your unborn family inside you. They will need a father after all.”
    She looked like he was crazy, which he probably was. “Don´t come near me. Before you are their father, I will take our dog as that.” Without knowing it, she just sealed their fate.
    Ty shuddered but then grinned at her. “I didn´t think of me as their father or your husband. That is gross, even if you are younger now, you still are a couple of years older than me. No, I hadn´t decided yet, but you just gave me the solution. Say cheese.”
    Before Helen could respond, as it dawned to her what the boy could mean, the camera flashed again. And all of the sudden, she found herself down on all fours, far smaller than before, but still with a belly to show. Her eyes widened as she saw her front-paws instead of hands over her grown out muzzle. She yowled loudly in horror.
    This new turn of events finally got the attention of their dog Buster, who until now had just sat there ignoring what was happening to his owners. But the yowling of another dog, a female one at that, right next to him, let him get active. Before Helen´s yowls could get worse, Buster started to sniff her, making her rather uncomfortable, but distracting her of her situation.
    Inside of Helen, her four kids and husband had become puppies, and as it looked like on the outside, she would soon give birth to them. They remained unaware of this, as like before they were only semi-consciousness.
    “There, see, now you can take your dog as their father. Just as you asked for.” Ty said laughing as Buster continued to sniff Helen. But the fear on her face needed to be dealt with in some way as well. “Now for the finishing touch. We can´t have you or your puppies act like humans, after all.”
    Helen looked at the boy in absolute panic now. What would he do to her and her family? But she couldn´t run, as Buster had just now tried to mount her, leaving her shell-shocked and frozen in place.
    Another flash came from the camera, as Ty shot his final picture of her, this time including Buster. As the light cleared, Helen´s face wasn´t fearful, but satisfied, as Buster slobbered her up, all mating forgotten. And there was also love on both their faces, for each other, as well as for their master, who stood in front of them.
    Ty grinned as he lowered the phone. Now both Buster and Helen would see him as their master, and by extension would her puppies later. He hadn´t erased their minds and memories, but repressed them for now. For now, they were all dogs, with no active memories about being human left. But he wasn´t sure if he wouldn´t change that later.
    Ty grabbed the leashes of the ground, together with a collar for Helen, which had before been her dress, quickly fastening it around her neck. “Let´s go home no, shall we?” Ty asked them, and both barked an affirmative. What intelligent pets he had now. And with that, he left the area, taking his dogs with him.
