    "What are you two carpet munchers doing here!" A voice sneered. Katie and Kellie turned and groaned. Behind them Ashley and Jennifer sneered at the two teens. "Look Ashley," Jennifer sneered. "These two are combing the beach looking for girlfriends." Jennifer laughed and glared at Katie and Kellie. "That fish smell isn't what you think losers." Both girls now giggled at their own joke.

    "Hey leave them alone," Samantha said meekly. Jennifer and Ashley stopped laughing and glared at where Samantha sat on her towel. "Well well well," Ashley said menacingly. "What a surprise. The wallflower finally has something to say. Tell me Sammy? You identify with these two lesbos."

    Samantha blushed as Ashley and Jennifer turned their venom on her. "Well it makes sense if you ask me," Jennifer said. "Athlete, doesn't talk to guys, hell I don't think she's ever had a boyfriend. Tell me Sammy you ever kiss a guy?" Ashley smirked. "Come on Sammy," she said with a sick smile. "Apparently our school is fine with that sort of thing. Come on out the closet." Samantha lowered her head as her eyes began to dampen with tears.

    "Leave Sammy alone," Mary Elise protested. "Oh shut up brainiac!" Ashley exclaimed. "If I want the answers to next weeks test I'll talk to you." Mary Elise flushed and gritted her teeth. "Sammy, Katie and Kellie did nothing wrong just leave them alone."

    Ashley and Jennifer paused and glared down at Mary Elise. "You must think your something huh Mary," Jennifer said. "Just because Mika and Claudia need you around to get them through their classes doesn't mean they actually care about you. You don't mean shit in this school, your nothing so remember your place!"

    Now it was Mika turn to confront Ashley and Jennifer. "Hey Ash, Jen that's enough of that shit!" She exclaimed. "Mary and Sammy did nothing wrong and Katie and Kellie haven't done anything to you either. Knock of this stupid shit before I beat you down myself bitch!" Ashley and Jennifer paused giving Mika a befuddled look.

    Katie and Kellie had watched these interactions with great interest. In this reality both Mary Elise and Samantha were obviously lower on the high school popularity scale than both Ashley and Jennifer. The two bullies had no problem belittling and intimidating them. When Mika had stood up to them though the two bullies paused, obviously Mika was on the same level popularity wise as these two.

    "What's your deal Mika?" Jennifer asked. "Since when do you care about a pair of freaks, a geek and a butch tomboy." Mika glared directly into Jennifer's eyes. "I've known Sammy and Mary forever," she growled. "There my friends and there off limits. As far as Katie and Kellie go I don't know them but they do so just back off."

    Ashley smirked as she looked down on the shorter Mika. "Or what!" She snarled. "Please Mika, like your so tough. Your not going to do shit and you know it!"

    "She may not but I will," Claudia said coolly. Ashley and Jennifer paused as Claudia stood up. Although Claudia, like Mika, was a couple of inches shorter than the two girls they obviously had a great deal of respect for her. Either that or it was fear. "I've heard enough from you two," she said coldly. "You little bitches come over here, talk a bunch of shit and try to run down my friends. If you think I'm just going to sit here and let you two whores fuck up my day anymore you got another thing coming."

    Ashley and Jennifer said nothing and just glared at Claudia. Katie and Kellie could see that Claudia was obviously the most popular girl at the school and someone not to be messed with. "You don't scare us," Ashley boasted. Although outwardly she seemed confident Katie could see a concerned look in her eyes. Claudia nodded and smirked. "Keep telling yourself that sweety," she said calmly. Ashley and Jennifer glowered at the group before shrugging their shoulders. "Let's get out of here Ash," Jen said. "Leave these losers and their baggy swimsuits to do what they want."

    "I still hear you flapping your lips," Claudia exclaimed. "Remember I can destroy you two without breaking a sweat. Get out of here or I'll make your last few years of school the worst of your lives. It's a promise." Ashley and Jennifer turned and quickly started in the opposite direction. Claudia sighed as she looked over the group. "Sorry about those two," she said glumly. "They've gotten way too full of themselves lately. Going to have to knock them down a peg or two. You guys okay?"

    The girls nodded and smiled. Samantha wiped away a stray tear while Mary Elise smiled up at her friend. "Your the best Claud," she said as she gave her friend a tight hug. Claudia smiled as she returned her friends hug. "Well Mary can't have anyone saying those kind of things about my best friend."

    Katie and Kellie stared slack jawed at the group of girls in front of them. They had been fortunate to find that not only did two of them were friends of theirs but the other two were popular and protective of the girls. Now as the former women stood up and began to adjust their ill fitting clothing the girls now decided what to do with the rest of their day.
