    "Jen, c'mon! Wake up!" yelled Link.
    "I don't think yelling at her will work." said Yusuke. "I've seen this kind of transformation before, well, not seen, but experienced. It's not exactly like what I went through but it's similar somehow. We just need to let this run it's course."
    "Uhhh... What're you guys staring at nya?" aske Jen.
    "Good, you're awake! Now let's get that mask off you. It should never be worn over a prolonged period." said Link. He tied to pull the mask off but it didn't budge. "Great, it's stuck."
    "What nya?!" asked Jen. She got up and went over to the river to look at her reflection. "Oh, thank God nya. It's not one of your ugly masks nya."
    "Oh Jen, I finished your gundam." said Rain.
    "Right, I should go have my fight with Gaara." said Jen. She flew off towards the border.
    "Hey Gaara nya! You ready for that fight?!" yelled Jen.
    "Yes, let's begin." said Gaara. Jen immediately threw a kunai. Some sand blocked it. Jen began punching at him from all different directions. Sand went flying at Jen but she dodged by flying upwards.
    "Ushi, tora, uma, i, ne, tori! Fire Style! Fireball Jutsu nya!" yelled Jen. A large fireball appeared and was headed straight for Gaara. It was blocked by sand. Jen slipped through the barrier of sand using the fireball jutsu as a cover. "Here's something I picked up from a friend nya; Kachû Tenshin Amaguriken!" yelled Jen. She threw a long series of incredibly fast punches. "Now for the finish nya! Tatsu, saru, tora! Fire Style! Burning Finger Jutsu!" She punched him and he went flying. "Oh, c'mon. Is that all you've got?!"
    "Sand Coffin!" said Gaara. Some sand tried to crush Jen's leg. She dodged by taking flight. Kunai made of sand shot up from the ground. Jen flew in between them with ease, until one hit her wing.
    "Ugh!" Jen fell out of the sky. It didn't take long for her to recover. "Ne, mi, tora. Multi Shadow Clone Jutsu nya!" yelled Jen. 100 Jen clones appeared. "Ushi, u, uma, saru, uma! Chidori!" yelled all the Jens. "Kachû Tenshin Amaguriken!" All the Jens combined the Chestnut Fist(Kachû Tenshin Amaguriken) with Chidori to attack all the sand with lightning, turning it all into glass. The Jen clones disappeared. "Now I've got you nya." said Jen. She began gathering chakra in her hand. "Rasengan!" She slammed it into his stomach. Gaara turned into a pile of sand. "A sand clone nya?!"
    "Sand Funeral!" yelled Gaara. Sand surrounded Jen and crushed her, but Jen vanished in a puff of smoke. "A shadow clone." said Gaara. Sam and the others approached the battlefield.
    "Whoa! They're evenly matched!" said Sam.
    "No they're not." said Link. "Jen is wearing the Fierce Deity's Mask. She might not realize it yet but, the difference in their power is almost immeasurable. The question is, can she tap it?" said Link.
    "Ne, mi, tora. Multi Shadow Clone Jutsu nya!" Hundreds of Jen clones appeared. Some threw kunai with wires on them. They landed in a circle around Gaara. "Mi, tatsu, u, tora! Fire Style! Dragon Flame Jutsu nya!" yelled the Jen clones that threw the wired kunai. Flames followed the wires down, surrounding Gaara. The remaining Jen clones threw kunai with paper bombs attached into the circle around Gaara. They didn't go off. "Tatsu, saru, tora! Fire Style! Burning Finger Jutsu nya!" yelled the real Jen. Her hand turned red while she gathered chakra in it. The Rasengan formed and absorbed the heat, turning red. Jen held the Rasengan in both hands, held it above her head, lit up her wings and went into a spiraling dive bomb, heading straight down into the ring of fire. "Volcanic Impact!" she yelled. The Rasengan crashed into the ground at Gaara's feet and Jen used her incredible speed to escape. The paper bombs formed a massive explosion. When the smoke cleared, there was only a human shaped sand cocoon. Gaara stepped out of it.
    "I concede." said Gaara.
    "You fought well Gaara nya. I had a lot of fun! Let's do it again sometime. What do ya say? Friends?" said Jen. She held out her hand to shake his hand. Gaara hesitated, but shook Jen's hand.
    "F-friends." said Gaara.
    "Cool. I'll write ya buddy nya. Stop by the Leaf Village sometime nya! We can spar. See you later nya!" said Jen. They all headed towards the border.
