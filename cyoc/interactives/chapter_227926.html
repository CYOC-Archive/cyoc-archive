There's my ten year old fairy of a son, Ashley, who's always playing with his five year old sister, Morgan. I can't keep the damn boy from acting like a total sissy baby. He's always playing with his sister's Barbies and pretending to be a ballerina with her.", said the man. Next Madame turned to Mark's wife, Margot. "What seems to be wrong with your family, Margot", asked Madame Margot replied, "My husband is a total drunk and asshole. I want to divorce him and find a new man, but I'm stuck. Mary Anne has had to take my place as mother because my husband is too lazy to get a job or clean house. If he were a baby, he'd be much easier to take care of, but he's not. Still, it'd be nice if he were a maid or a cook or a nanny or preferably all three to help around the house. Unfortunately, he's none of those things. Mary Anne needs the pressure taken off of her so that she can just be a kid. I'm worried about my son, Ashley. I think he may be transgender and I want him to be happy, but his father gets in the way of his joy. He's adorable and a big help to me in that he looks after and plays with his little sister. I wish they could share a room together and have more time together. It would be nice. As for my youngest, Morgan.....well, I wish she was my little girl forever. I don't really want her to grow up." Madame then moved on to Mary Anne. Mary Anne said, "Madame, I hate high school and have no desire to go to college. I wish my life was a lot simpler. I love my mom, but my dad's a total authoritarian. My younger siblings are adorable and they deserve better than what they currently have. As for me, I just wish school was a lot simpler and that my family and teachers would treat me special." Madame then turned to Ashley. "I have a secret.", replied Ashley. "I want to be a girl like my sister, Morgan. I love being in her room and playing with her toys and pretending to be a ballerina or pretending to have a tea party. Daddy's really mean and yells at me. Can you make him nicer? Can you make him understand that I'm a girl?" Madame simply smiled at the boy. "I'll try my best.", she said. Finally Madame turned to Morgan. "Daddy's a meanie and deserves to be punished for being bad. He yells at Ashley for playing with me. I want Ashley to play with me all the time, he's nice. Can he share my room with me so that we can play together forever?" Madame didn't say anything as a plan was formulating in her mind.
