    "I vote for Ashley and Jen's plan." Claudia said raising her hand.

    Ashley and Jennifer were surprised at this as they never thought Claudia would be the first person to agree to something from them based on their history.

    "Really?" Ashley asked stunned.

    "Yeah, I mean don't get me wrong Katie and Kellie are both really good friends of mine, but I prefer you plan." Claudia explained.

    "If you're in on this, so am I." Mika said raising her hand as well.

    "In all honesty, I have never been the biggest fans of you two. But from what I hear, your parents are a lot harder to deal with. I'm willing to give this a shot if you two are so willing to change." Samantha said to them raising her hand now as well.

    "Ashley, Jennifer, don't take this the wrong way but your parents kind of suck. You two on the other hand, rock right now." Mary Elise agreed now raising her own hand.

    Now it was just left up to Katie and Kellie, the two of them had sort of known Ashley and Jennifer the longest. They didn't like all the stuff they had gone through with them before they came out but in a way it was also thanks to them that they even got together in the first place and they sort of owed the two that.

    "I'm in on your idea." Kellie said.

    "Really Kellie, you're in on their deal?" Katie asked surprised.

    "Yeah, don't you think we kind of need to repay them for admitting our feelings to each other?" Kellie pointed out.

    Katie looked at Ashley and Jennifer, then at Kellie and realized that her girlfriend was right.

    "I almost can't believe I'm saying this: Ashley and Jennifer, it's your plan by unanimous vote." Katie said raising her own hand now.

    "Thanks girls, we promise you won't regret this." Ashley said with Jennifer nodding.
