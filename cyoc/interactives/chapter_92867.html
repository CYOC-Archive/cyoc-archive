    Tyler was your average nerd, living with his mother at 20 years and virgin, he never succeded in finding a girlfriend, academicaly he aspires to be a scientist and currently enrolls computer science, is currently unemployed and enjoy passing whole days at home playing games in his computer.

    The story starts on a Monday, coming back home from college(he studied at night), he found a package on his bed, it was small with your usual UPS signs all over it, being late at night and having just arrived home, he put the package away and went to his computer to play until 12PM. The next morning, his mom already left for work, even though he himself didn't work he had an habit of waking up in early morning, took his breakfast and coming back to his room he saw the package, almost being swallowed by his bed sheets. The package didn't had any information on who sent it, just his own address, opening the package, he found a small ring box and a scroll, "I guess a typical manual was off the question" said Tyler, being a loner he developed the habit of talking to himself, at the bottom of the package was a letter, picking it up, it read:

    "To our most beloved Tyler Highrain, my name is Cooper Highrain, although your mother negated your contact with your dad's side with the family we still managed to send our good will to you, being a Highrain and born from my dear brother, I just could not stand that woman, keeping you out of our family's business. You might be confused now, allow me to explain, We the Highrain's family have been known sorcerers for the longest time, more specifically in the art of alchemical constructs, your mother, refused to show you, your heritage, afraid you would leave her, just like my brother did, believe me, he had a good reason to, the rest of the family discussed and agreed to send you one of our magical items, to show you the power of your heritage and present you, to our art. On the scroll you'll find detailed instructions on how to use it, and how to contact us when you are ready to know more.
    From your uncle, Cooper Highrain"

    Tyler was surprised by the letter, his mother indeed never told much about his dad, much less from his family, he left him when he was only 1 year old, still shocked with such information, he picked the scroll, and analysed the engravings on the top, it portrayed an owl on the top of a tree looking down at a 3-headed dog eating the carcass of a knight, the engravings were beautiful, yet they did not fail to capture the moment, under the illustration was a banner like form with "The Watcher never sleeps" written in it. A little shocked by the realism of the etching, Tyler pulled the string to open, the paper was surprisingly strong despite being in such an antiquate form of container, the scroll extended to about 2M, Tyler quickly rolled it again to see the start of the scroll:

    "The Trinity Word Ring.

    Fellow Tyler, my name is Rose Highrain and this is my creation, for you, our farthest sibling. This ring has the power to make your wills come true, but be careful, powerful magic's require much more energy than you may have. The ring draws power from you, your own force, everyone is born with some, and everyday when we are not using it, it recharges, you as a Highrain have a inherent capability of channelling your force to cast spells and use magical items.
    The Trinity Word ring has the power to apply 3 Words to anything, this means, that these 3-words will change the being to make themselves true. This is one of our novice constructs as such it can be used by non-sorcerers with some difficulty. If you wish to know more about this, check the 'Channelling' page. To use the ring put it in your index finger, it adjusts to the person, then use your thumb to press the base of the ring, this will allow your force to channel into the ring, then say the 3 words, the ring is capable of transforming, non-animate and animals, and since it uses your force, it knows your intentions and your target."

    Tyler then goes for the ring box, opening it he finds a metal rings with detailed engravings and three gems at the top. "Index finger huh?" he puts it on, a bit sceptical, but willing to try, "This thing is supposed to transform anything I want to fit into three words, and is only limited to my mana?", he then say his old computer, still on his e-mail from the night before, puts his thumb on the ring, he immediately feel something flowing to the ring from him, a little shocked, he decides to shrug off and continue, he raises his right hand with the ring and says:"Expensive Powerful Fast", a green bolt shoots from his hand and he instantly feel his legs weak, he crumbles on his own weight, shocked he looks at his computer to see, instead of his old white box now stood a shining black computer, with 2 graphics cards, LEDs detailing the rig, stumbling, feeling very tired he manages to drag himself to his chair and checks the computer specs,

    his computer went from a normal machine to an overclockers wet dream . Surprised by this, he debates against himself to try this on people or perhaps himself, he was always amazed by TG sites and was very curious about female pleasure, but should he use the ring to know more about his dad?
