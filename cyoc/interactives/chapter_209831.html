    A short while after the maids left, the vase started shaking and fell over, before the top started slowly pushing out,
    until, finally, there was a loud *POP* and a woman crawled out of the vase.
    She tried to stand up, but stumbled, unable to manage anything more than a slight crouch, with her ass jutting out.
    The woman waddled around the room for a moment, not understanding why she couldn't stand up,
    until she turned her head back and saw that her rear-end seemed to be stuck in somthing.
    She grabbed it and tried to pull it off, but it seemed to be firmly attached, and her failed attempts only served to frustrate her.
    Several failed attempts later, she finally decided to give up, waddeling over to a mirror to get a better look at how it was attached.
    It seemed like a bowl at first glance, but a closer look revealed a large hole in the middle.
    Guessing the depth of the hole wasn't possible without more light, but the woman assumed that there couldn't be much space between it and her behind, as it seemed to be fairly closely stuck to it.
    She thought for a moment, before opening a nearby drawer, containing a number of precious, giant pearls,
    that had been recently presented as a tribute to the queen.
    Her idea was to put as many of the pearls into the bowl as possible, to weigh it down, thereby assisting in removing it.
    She wondered the pearls would fit, considering their size, but decided to give it a try, inserting the first into the hole.
    It was easier to insert the pearl that she had expected, the hole apparently having a larger circumference than she thought,
    but the expected contact with her body didn't come, the space seemingly being larger than she assumed,
    She didn't dwell on it, as that simply meant that more pearls would fit inside, allowing her to add more weight, and kept putting in more.
    The drawer was soon empty, but the bowl still didn't feel like it was any closer to coming loose.
    The woman didn't stop to consider how so many objects of such size could fit into a space that should have been fairly small,
    instead looking around the room for things she could use to further weigh the bowl down.
    She began waddling over to another set of drawers, when the added weight made her lose her balance, causing her to fall forwad.
    She managed to stretch out her arms, keeping her face from impacting the floor, but the sudden movement flung the pearls forwards,
    catapulting the directly into an area that the woman could identify as somewhere roughly behind her collar bone.
    The feeling confused her, and she decided to think that she had been mistaken; her collar bone had simply hit the ground,
    giving her the impression that something had impacted it from the inside.
    She was beginning to feel panicked, randomly grabbing nearby items and putting them in the hole, desperate to remove it at this point.
    Magical items, jewelry, a small vase, various precious trinkets, and even a beautiful fur blanket, made from the hide of a monster,
    went into the hole, adding more and more weight, until the woman had to crawl through the room, but the bowl was still firmly attached.
    Eventually, most of the loose objects in the room had been shoved through the hole, and it finally reached it's capacity,
    when she forced one of the pillows from the bed through it.
    She sat down, exhausted from hauling a massive amount of weight around the room.
    The bowl still showed no signs of coming loose, and the massive weight of everything she'd put inside stopped her from getting up again.
    Removing the objects wasn't possible, as the large pillow blocked the hole from the inside, effectively trapping her where she sat for now.
    As the woman slowly calmed down, her eyes eventually drifted down, and she noticed for the first time that she seemed somewhat bloated.
    It looked like she had become wider and her belly larger, but she didn't seem fat, instead it rather looked like she had been expanded,
    somewhat like a balloon filled with air.
    Now that she had time to think, everything slowly started fitting together, leading her to a chilling realization.
    What she had assumed to be the space between the bowl and her was actually the interior of her own body,
    which had somehow been hollowed out, and she had filled it with random things she found, up to a point where she couldn't even move.
