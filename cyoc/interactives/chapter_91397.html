This time you had enough thought to back away from the sound quickly.
    You asked "Mind if I waited out here?"
    Emily asked "Something wrong Kitty?"
    "I think Stan or someone is playing more of that whistle... I can't go in there."
    "Alright, just wait out here and i'll go get us some food." you nodded and Emily dashed inside and you started to wait for her until some guys started to walk outside with a whistle in his hand.
    Kind of leaning at a wall you heard one of the kids say "Yeah Stan had a bunch of these things. You blow into it and it makes a soft noise, some people like the sound and apparently it keeps animals away. Listen" the kid blew into the whistle and you put your hands on your ears and whimpered trying to outlast the noise. The kids saw you overreact and whispered something to each other and the kid without a whistle went into the cafeteria. You sighed relief until about a dozen kids came out with whistles. Eyes widening you scampered off as the kids walked towards you with flutes drawn.
