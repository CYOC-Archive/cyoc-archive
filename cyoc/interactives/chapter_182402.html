    One day they just appeared. Nobody knew if they were the result of a failed experiment or some kind of magical ritual. Mysterious fruits that could grant people supernatural abilities. While usually based on normal fruits they usually came in unnatural colors with weird patterns. Of course people quickly turned to a certain popular manga series and even after it was proven the author had nothing to do with them, the general public still decided to use a similar naming scheme and call them Lilith Fruits.

    The reason for that name was the often highly sexualized nature of these abilities, which often seemed to be based on a certain kink. Progress in figuring out how they worked was slow, but they still made some important discoveries:

    -Each fruit and by extension each ability was unique. The first person to take one bite or eat one piece of these surprisingly sweet fruits would immediately bestow all its powers on that person. Even if another person were to then eat the rest nothing would happen to them. Lilith Fruit powers would stay with that person until their death in which case all the powers would be automatically transferred to the nearest compatible fruit for someone new to eat.

    -Each person can only have one ability, though there are rumors of people that are able to use more than one ability.

    -There are currently no known weaknesses that apply to all Lilith Fruit users. At least none that are known to the general public…

    -Lilith Fruit powers can manifest in many different ways, some give people supernatural powers, some might allow people to create certain materials or substances out of their bodies and some might permanently change a person's body, some might even be a combination of the above.

    -The body changes that fruits with transforming properties have also seem to be unique to each person. Out of three known users of the Milk Fruit among its ability to control your own milk production, one had her boobs expand in size, another grew an additional pair of breasts and the third one even grew an udder.

    This is the story of one of those people, who ate a Lilith Fruit and had their life change because of it. But who is this protagonist? And, more importantly, which fruit did they eat?
