    An Anthropomorphic Lion:

    The Red Circus Leader spoke again. "I grab the average build because that's what you are currently. But if you want a different build. I have one that's muscular build, Or a FATTER build..." On the word 'fatter' he pats his own belly for emphasis. As he smiles "...Just to name a few."

They're not animal eyes they look... almost human. And that's not all. the front paws have poseable thumbs. Well that's not really ‘that’ unusual, But then you look at it's groin and... Okay it's too freaky! Do they really make those!

    "If you have the time..." Crimson Titan spoke snapping you out of gazing eyes of the mask. "... You can even take off all your clothes when you change, and put them over it. They will morph to fit your new skin! although I can't guarantee on the other forms... So if you want to keep your clothes... Believe that they will fit you before you zip it up competely, and they will be fine."

    And now he's testing your patience... Thinking to yourself. 'This is not practical! My clothes don't just morph to fit at all! I know he's trying to play a character for his store. But this too much!' "Sir, I don't want to be rude but..." Then he snatches the costume.

    "Say no more! I have a another skin you can try... The one you can be happy with!" And what he gave you (although this one is just has a red mane, and yellow body fur)... is a very colorful suit.

    The Cartoon Lion:

    "Rather be it from a Disney cartoon, or Loony Tunes show... You can be 'your own' Character in your own Saturday Morning Cartoon World! You can be naked without shame because there won't be anything there! Or if you just want to wear clothes? Or maybe you want to wear just a shirt with no pants on? You can do just that!" He describe it, with such hype you'd think he's trying too hard!

    What you are actually seeing is what you have seen in a Sports Mascot. All it needs is a jersey.

    This is similar to the... Anthro Lion from eariler... But it's less Realistic, and more Stylized! With a Cartoony head, define colors on this suit, It's created with such simplicity... there is nothing on the groin! And, somehow weirdly it feels light. Not heavy at all!

    And Crimson finishes with. "And Wally boy, I only showed you a few of what I actually have!"

    You stare at him... He also points at the doors telling you where the dressing rooms are. You can't tell how long you been here, and he's giving you too many choices. You just want to either get this over with, or get back to work before you're late. You have set an alarm on your phone before you got here, in case you lost track of time.

    "Sir... It's been fun but..." You handed him the costume. "I need to think this over..."

    He then pulled a random Lion mask from the shelf, and stuffs a piece of paper in it. "Wally, If you don't have the money in hand, then I would like to give this mask to you for free. Call it a constellation, to make up for your lost time here."

    'Who is this guy?' You wondered, he won't let you leave empty handed. Should you entertain the idea of a chance to change your life, as absurd as that sounds... What should you pick to try on?

    If you still have time, what would you try on first?
