    The guard was lading you down a fairly deserted street, when you suddenly hear an alarm bell nearby.
    "Damn, this is probably important." The guard turned towards the boy. "Can you keep an eye on her for a while? I'll be back soon."
    "Sure thing!" "Thanks kid." He gave the boy a thumbs up and ran off, leaving you two alone.
    The boy waited for a while, before eventually getting bored and making you walk up and down the street a few times.

    A man approached you, after you walked down the street for what felt like the tenth time, blocking the way.
    "I'll give you twenty coins for her." You realized what he meant, but it seemed the boy hadn't. "What are you talking about?"
    "I'm saying that i'll give you twenty coins if you give her to me." He pointed at you. "I don't know, i don't think i'm supposed to sell her."
    "Alright, i'll give you twenty five, but i'm not going any higher." "Hmm... alright!"
    The boy agreed with a smile and received the coins from the man, before leaning down to you.
    "You belong to him now, okay? Make sure you do everything he tells you to."
    The boy got off of you and ran away, stopping a little further down the street.
    "Byeee! Please take good care of her!" He disappeared around a corner shortly after.
    The man turned to you after the boy was gone. "Come along bitch, you've got a long day ahead of you."
    Your body followed him the moment he started walking, but you had already stopped trying to resist,
    knowing that there was little you could do right now.
    You only hoped that your guard would find you soon.
