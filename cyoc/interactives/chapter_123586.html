    Jen flew to her school, changing back to herself. She walked to see Elizabeth Deras, a strong girl by most boys, holding up Daniel Fruse's shirt, a boy, known for his short size, while being punched. She wanted justice and what was the way then to turn into someone. She was thinking really fast, before thinking of becoming one the the school councils, Ms. Freder. She was a 32 year old, with long, blond hair and bright, blue eyes. She chose her because she actually stop bullies and they look a lot in common, thus having the transformation faster. So she started thinking of her in a green dress, since it was a common thing of her to wear. She was starting to transform, with her hair growing longer and her eyes glowing brighter. Her faces grew some wrinkles, and grew make-up on spots. Then her breast grew bigger, to a CC. Then green cloth grew around her body below to form a green dress. She got to the kid and shoved her off. "You know what I think about bullying, Ms. Deras," said Jen, sounding like Ms. Freder. "I'm sorry, I'll stop," said Elizabeth. She put him down, and ran. "Thank you, Ms. Freder," said Daniel. "You're welcome," said Jen. She walked away, before changing into herself and walking to get breakfast.

    The bus came to the front door, holding Kylie with her backpack. She walked in, feeling really scared from her form, She walked in the cafeteria to see Jen at a table. She got her food and sat by her. "Hey Jen, how are you going?" said Kylie. "Good, but who are you?" said Jen. "Oh yeah, I haven't showed you, I'm Kylie, or Kyle on the phone," said Kylie. "Oh! You look beautiful! What an honor to meet you!" said Jen. They shook hands. "So how is the morning?" said Kylie. "Amazing, I showed Elizabeth who's the boss!" said Jen, "How about you?" "Fine, nothing interes-" said Kylie, as she let out a fart. "Uh, Kylie, are yo-" said Jen, before being cut off by Kylie saying an ah loud and seeing a happy face. A few minutes later, and Kylie said, "Oh I'm sorry, I just peed myself. It felt so good. Now it's a good day." "Hey Kylie?" said Jen. "Yeah?" said Kylie. "Can I change you?" said Jen. "Actually yes, I would," said Kylie. "Great, let's go to the bathroom," said Jen.

    They walked to the nearest girl's bathroom, and found no one in it, so Jen and Kylie walked to one of the big spaced stalls. Jen put Kylie on the ground, took her backpack off, and unstrapped the diaper. She slid the diaper off, with Jen asking, "Where's the baby wipes?" "It should be in the backpack," said Kylie. She looked in her backpack, and found it, and pulled it out, with baby powder and a fresh pair. She wiped the pee off, with Kylie giggling, then putting on the powder, making her laugh a little more. She slid one back on, and strapped it. The bell rang, and Jen turned into a fly to get her stuff fast. They have finished their homework, and got 100s on 5 tests they had without studying.
