    Jake clearly hadn't though his plan fully through when he forgot to set reality. Naomi was already in too deep to think the change was normal. Jake was hesitant, but he knew he had to come clean. He held the panicked Naomi's shoulder. "Calm down, Naomi. Please." Jake said trying to calm her. Naomi was shaken, but did so. "Okay... Why are you so okay with this?" Naomi asked. Jake put his pawed hand on top of her head and ran his hand to the back pulling Naomi's ears in the process. It seemed to calm her even more through her canine tendencies. "Because..." Jake paused. "I... I did this..." Jake stuttered. Naomi tilted her head in confusion. "You... You did 'this'?" Naomi questioned feeling her furless muzzle.

    Jake took his laptop from the table and opened it. "You see... I got this program today... And it changes things. I thought it was too good to be true, but it really works. I made it so that whenever we show signs of affection, we turn more into dogs... Or partial. Not all the way." Jake looked at Naomi looking at her face, hands, and feet. "And judging by how we look, I'd say we reached the maximum." Jake said grinning through his muzzle. Naomi still in slight shock, giggled at his smile through his face. "I'll admit, we do look kinda cute." Naomi reassured Jake.

    "But why do this? What's the point of this prospect?" Naomi asked. "That's the thing. I'm still experimenting with this thing. I gave everyone tails with this- oh damn." Jake realized that he gave away the first thing he did with the Chronivac. "Wait... So we didn't have tails to begin with... I thought we were born with tails." Naomi questioned. "That... Was apart of an alter reality setting. You think you had it the whole time, but really you just got it today." Jake explained. "So you lied to me... About your lineage." Jake blushed putting his tail between his legs. "I... I didn't want to cause any suspicion. I'm sorry."

    Naomi stood up off the couch to face Jake. Her expression turned from unimpressed to amused. She took her one pawed hand and scratched the back of Jake's ear. "Oh! Naomi, what... Oh... Yeah, right there..." Jake said rolling his big tongue out once again. "Judging by your reaction, I'd say you have us dog like reactions of some sort?" She asked still scratching his ear. Jake nodded his head panting heavily with his tail wagging. "I see the appeal now." Naomi admitted.

    Naomi stopped scratching Jake's ear and Jake turned to face her. Jake than turned to his laptop to use the alter reality button."There. If anybody sees us like this, they won't think any different. But if you're worried about looking like that, I set it so that you change back after a few hours. As long as we don't do anything to affectionate, we could turn back." Jake explained. "Is that so? Well I guess we're gonna be like this for a while." Naomi said putting his arms around Jake's neck. "One question. We won't loose our tails, right?" Naomi asked raising her tail. "Of course not." Jake confirmed. "Good. Now give me that cute face of yours." Naomi pushed Jake back down to the couch and both started making out (licking) again.
