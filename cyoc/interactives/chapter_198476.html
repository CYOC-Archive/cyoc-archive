    added by AzureMist A year ago TG Mental Male to female

    As you are pondering this just then your head starts to throb with what feels like a migraine headache and you think you are going to pass out, but you manage to stay awake. As this is happening you look around and notice that your room is changing where your once messy bed with blue sheets laid is a neatly made lavish bed with pink sheets and blanket. You look at the walls and watch as they shift from blue to pink and all your furniture follows suit making a room that matches and just screams femininity. Just then the headache you are experiencing starts getting worse and you squeeze your eyes shut as the memories of your past life fade away. New memories then begin flooding your head, you recall always being a woman since birth and that you, your sister, and your parent's all moved here from Japan when you were verry young.

    At the same time your whole family undergoes their own transformations too as they all turn into a traditional Japanese family and their memories also change recalling moving here from Japan many years ago. Your parents also remember only having two daughters, you and your sister and the rest of your house also changes to be decorated with Japanese decorations and various other things that is indicative of your upbringing. Your headache then finally subsides and you think to yourself "that was a strange headache, I should probably take some medicine" you then swallow down a couple of pain pills and then decide to take a nap.

    Moments later you are awoken to the sound of your sister calling for you and you blink as you are awoken and your sister then opens the door to your room and smiles at you "c'mon sis! my slumber party is going to start soon and it would be a shame if my own sister was late to her own sister's slumber party!" You then sit up in your bed and smile at your sister and say "sure thing sis ill be there in a jiff!" She then jumps up and down giggling and clapping her hands and then runs off to her room. You then realize that you are still wearing your school clothes and instantly get changed into proper pajamas and head to your sister's room for the slumber party.
