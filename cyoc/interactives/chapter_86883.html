    Tim walked over and asked Gregory, "Hey, its only fair if I get to grow up too, I mean, you look pretty amazing as a thirty year old and all."

    Jen smirked as she watched the boys. Good - more eye-candy - and she leaned back on a couch, "Yeah, I figure its only fair."

    Gregory decided, eh, what could it hurt, and he turned over the remote to Tim, who excitedly watched himself form on the TV as he used the remote. He started to play with the numbers - he was a rather scrawny boy, so he wanted to first of all Age Well. He then considered his current prospects. Considering, despite or perhaps because of his thin frame, he was currently on the track team, he wondered what aging would do to him.

    He looked over at a very self-satisfied Gregory examining his muscles, looking down at his manhood, and running a hand through his hair in appreciation of his more mature look, and he decided to become a bit more mature than Gregory. He outlined that he wanted a Reality Change, but he wanted to remain Aware of who and what age he really was, going through menus and options in that regard.

    He set the final change to be taking him to the age of 35, and it would happen in thirty minutes.

    After okaying the change and getting it done, Tim sat back and decided to see what he could do until then. Maybe he could have some fun, one way or another. Or content himself watching the change in the mirror. Or maybe just for fun, he could run while changing.

    Though it did look like Jen was very interested in what he might do, all of a sudden. Seemed she had rather liked seeing Gregory grow, and he wondered what that meant...
