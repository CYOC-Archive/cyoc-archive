    "So, that quick huh? Let's see it." said Jiraiya.
    "Right nya." said Jen. She dropped the ball and formed a Rasengan in her hand. Then she slammed it into the ground. A huge crater formed with a swirl design in it. The Rasengan held together.
    "Well, you've learned the Rasengan so all that's left is for me to know what you plan to do in the future."
    "Well nya, I won't fit in at my home town anymore so I'll be staying in the Leaf Village nya. I thought maybe, I could succeed you as 7th Hokage Naruto Sensei nya." said Jen.
    "Well, wanting to be Hokage isn't enough, you need a reason." said Naruto.
    "I want to be Hokage because, well nya, when I first arrived, everybody was so nice to me nya, they helped me even though I'm a freak nya. I want to help them by being a great shinobi nya, and someday, a great Hokage nya." said Jen.
    "Good reason. Now, you graduate!" said Naruto.
    "Congradulations. Now that you have an arsenal of powerful jutsu, It's time to develope your own style. This includes what jutsu elements you'll use, the steps you try to go through to win a fight, and a few other things. Your style should be second nature to you. Why don't we start with a signature jutsu. Your signature jutsu needs to be powerful and have some significance to your personality or past. For example, Naruto's Shadow Clone Jutsu represents how lonely he felt throughout his childhood. Your signature jutsu should also be second nature." said Jiraiya.
    "What's going on?" asked Sam, she just woke up and came out of the tent.
    "Hmm... I got it nya! C'mon!" Jen flew away from the camp and Naruto, Sam and Jiraiya followed her. "Ne, mi, tora. Shadow Clone Jutsu!" said Jen. Another Jen appeared. "Tatsu, saru, tora! Fire Style! Burning Finger Jutsu!" yelled the real Jen. Her glowed red. Then she gathered chakra in that hand, forming a Rasengan. The Rasengan ate up all the fiery heat in Jen's hand and turned red. The real Jen used her free hand to draw her sword. "Ushi, u, uma, saru, uma!" said the Jen clone. "Chidori!" The Jen clone channeled the Chidori into the real Jen's sword, then the clone vanished. Then Jen went into a nose dive. "Meteor Storm!" yelled Jen With split second timing she slammed the red hot Rasengan into the ground and slashed at it once with her Lightning Blade. "AHHHH!" scremed Jen. An explosion occured with several flashes of fire and lightning coming from the epicenter of the explosion. Jen was blown out of the smoke and landed half conscious at Naruto's feet. She tried to get up. "Ugh! I think... I need to... practice that one nya. Heh heh." said Jen, barely able to talk.
    "Oh, not another false alarm!" yelled a voice from above. A girl with blue hair and a pink kimono was floating on an oar.
    "Botan of the River Sticks?!" yelled Sam.
    "Bingo!" said Botan.
