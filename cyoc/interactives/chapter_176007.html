    “And that isn’t your fault.” Said a new voice as Barbara walked up and knelt down as well. “Hello, you must be Luke and Sarah, your Aunt has told me a lot about you two but I can’t wait to get to know you better.”

    Christina thanked God that Barbara had come up even as the kids looked up at her confused. She actually hadn’t talked about Luke and Sarah all that much, they had been 4 and a half when she transformed and at that age they were toddlers.

    “Who are you?” inquired Luke

    “My name is Barbara.” Said Barbara as she extended her hand which Luke took. “I am your Aunt’s…”

    “Girlfriend.” Answered Christina deciding to just rip the bandage off.

About how it was wrong and it was against God, Barbara and Christina just took but they took heart that neither kid pulled away. Maybe it was their need to be close to someone when they face the future of monster hood but it still made both of them smile.

The process was much smoother than it had been for Christina and while the kids where tired once the process was done they where free to leave the hospital.
