    Chronivac Version 4.0

    The two fugitive dragons shrink the police to the size of dolls

    added by Anonymous 5 years ago S O

    So the cops lobbed in a stun grenade,which landed right on the
    Chronivac
    and with a huge "BBOOOMMMM"
    the Chronivac lay in little pieces all over Clementine Fergusons
    store floor.

    The dragoness stared in shock at the wrecked Chronivac
    getting it in her dragonish mind that she was stuck as a
    dragoness now.

    Meanwhile Jamilla,who remembered the cops treating him
    badly when he had been a 15 year old human waif
    was determined to take as many fuzz with him as possible.

    However there were hundreds of cops out there with all
    manner of weapons,shields and armoured transport.

    Without the Chronivac the dragon couldn't realistically stand
    a chance against the fuzz with just fire breath,fangs,claw and brute force.
    So he turned to Clementine the Dragoness and asked her
    if there were any explosives,bazookas or Bofors Cannon
    in the comic book and rock album store.

    The dragoness gave him a look but then suddenly
    had a flash of inspiration.
    As we all know Rock Bands use Temporary Shrinking Powder
    in their acts.

    One of the rockers had left a few sacks of the stuff
    in trade for a rare Cultus Force Quantum Issue 1
    comic book .
    So the dragoness went to the back room and hauled the sacks
    of shrinking dust to the front.
    She explained the stuff was activated by fire,clearly not a issue
    for her and Jamilla's new species.
    So whilst Clementine Ferguson got to work on one
    batch of shrinking powder sacks she explained the stuff was
    active for 90 seconds,so whatever Jamilla had in mind it
    had to be quick.

    So on the count of three Jamilla smashed out the
    store window and flung a box of burning comic books and
    shrinking powder as hard as he could in the direction of the
    fuzz.
    There was a huge cloud of pink smoke billowing outside
    and the coughing cops shrank to the size of Ken and Barbie dolls.
    Meanwhile the dragoness ignited the other sacks of shrinking powder and she and Jamilla were soon the size of Great Danes.
    Being dragons,there was a limit to what the shrinking
    dust could do to shrink the two fugitives from the law.
    So the two flamebreathers leapt out the smashed out front
    store window and made a dash for it,with Clementine
    trying to avoid stepping on all the 8 inch police persons
    coughing and running in circles and Jamilla looking for fuzz to squash......
