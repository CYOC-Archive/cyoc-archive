    Grabbing her things, Teri went outside, and got in the car. The driver was a tall, slender brunette with shoulder length hair and long, beautiful legs. She had a healthy tan, and wore a pair of black sunglasses. She had a black halter top that showed off a taut, flat belly, and a red miniskirt. She was wearing a pair of calf-high red leather boots. She was very pretty, and she leaned over to kiss Teri. 'Oh wow, she's a fantastic kisser!'

    "um...wow...", was all Teri could say.

    The girl laughed. "Oh Teri, you're so adorable!" She lowered her sunglasses, showing off a beautiful pair of green eyes. "And that outfit is sexy, girlfriend. I almost don't want to take it off of you...almost."

    Teri smiled. "Maybe later? Right now, I want to see Daddy."

    "Sure thing, Angel." The girl shifted into reverse, effortlessly backed out of the driveway, and got on the road. She put her hand on the stick shift, and Teri put her hand on top of the girl's, giving it a gentle squeeze. The girl smiled at Teri.

    'Oh she's gorgeous! But I have no idea what her name is!', Teri realized. "Say, I had a weird thought earlier."

    "You always have weird thoughts, Angel! What is it this time? You know I don't mind your questions."
