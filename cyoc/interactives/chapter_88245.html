    A Satyr

    "Genie, I wish that over the course of the next few days Nate would slowly transform into a Satyr, with pointed ears, furry legs and ripped muscles. As he transforms, no one will notice anything out of the ordinary and every guy he fancies will find him irresistible." Jamie flashed Nate a smile.

    "Your wish is granted," replied Xander. "Looks like things will be getting interesting for you, Nate."

    "Yeah," replied Nate. He walked over to Jamie. "What made you make that wish?"

    "Well, I know you always like mythological creatures and I've seen how you pine for guys who don't give you the time of day."

    Nate blushed a little. "Thanks."

    "So, what should we do now?"

    "Well, we still need to clean up this room," replied Nate, gesturing around the room.

    "Um, guys, did you forget I'm a genie?" interrupted Xander.

    "Oh, right, you can make this all neat and organized in seconds."

    "Just need you to wish for it buddy," replied Xander.

    "Wait, you're only giving me one, I don't want to waste it on that."

    "It'll be a freebie, ok? Because I like you."

    "Aww, ok. I wish this room was perfectly clean and organized."

    "Your wish is granted." In a flash, the room was spotless, clean and extremely well organized. "Now, then, how about we go out and test out your new found irresistability."

    Nate suddenly felt very shy. "Oh, I don't know..."

    "Come on. Let's go." Jamie motioned for them to leave.

    "Well, what are we going to do with Xander? I think wandering around with a 6 foot bunny is going to attract attention," replied Nate.

    "Ah, I got you there, mate." Both Nate and Jamie watched as Xander's fur disappeared, his ears shrank down and his body transformed until he was a fully formed human.

    "Cool," whispered Nate.

    "Um, gonna need some clothes there, buddy," added Jamie.

    "Right." Xander clicked his fingers and was suddenly fully clothed in blue jeans, sneakers and a t-shirt, with his same vest over the t-shirt. He decided to leave the Fez behind though.

    "No Fez?" asked Nate.

    "Nope. Might attract too much attention."

    "But Fez's are cool," replied Nate as all three of them walked out of the room.

    "Now then, where to go?" asked Jamie.

    "Yes, where indeed?" Nate felt an itch on his left leg and scratched it. Curious, he lifted his pant legs and was surprised to find both legs were increasingly hairier. He smiled and dropped them. It was really happening.
