Finally giving up, Conner opened a random room and found himself in an art room. There wasn't much there except a giant block of clay and a light in the corner. He also noticed there was a spray bottle on the floor next to the lamp. Confused, he looked around. It was then that he noticed there was a sign in the front of the door.

    "Art Studio. Models needed. Huh." he walked into the room, looking around it. There wasn't much there. Just a lamp, baby oil in a bottle, block of clay, and...

Well, why complain right? Conner aimed the camera in the direction of the lamp and set the timer. He smiled and walked into the frame, holding a pose. There was a flash and Conner made another pose. He repeated this for a few more shots.

    Then Conner took off his shirt. He threw it onto the floor and then picked up the spray bottle. He sprayed the oil onto his muscles. He began rubbing the oil over his six pack and toned chest. He felt a tingling feeling that made him giggle. He rubbed it mroe on his shoulders, neck, and face, making sure he got his body nice and shiny. It's features changed, going from very masculine to very round and baby-ish. Conner felt his own face starting to change. He felt his muscles and bone change very smoothly. His face became rounder and younger. The statue's arms moved, falling to his sides and Conner felt his own arms move in the same way. He suddenly realized that his body was changing according to the clay statue! what ever happened to it happened to him!
