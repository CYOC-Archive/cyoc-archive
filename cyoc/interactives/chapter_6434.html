    Luke and his best friend Ike were into their second year of college. The dorms being a real drag they moved into a small apartment. As parents argued with both of them about addtional costs they soon found themselves looking for work.

    Ike found the leaflet tacked to the bulletin board outside the college student lounge.

    ......................Natas Research & Study Corporation.............

    Be a part of medical history and earn big buck wages!

    Apply at: 1172 N. Easy Street or phone for appointment.

    Ages 18-55 are welcomed to apply. Males only need apply for our study into male preferance and endowment. Testing and studies are
    done on weekly, monthly, and year long endurances. Qualified males
    will be paid for their service while given room and board during all terms of each study.

    Natas Research makes no claims of personal safety during or after each study. Payment is equal to the risk and all funds distributed to the recipient's according to their needs. The Doctor
    in charge may offer lifetime employment with full benefits and a
    retirement package to those who give their all to science and the
    betterment of our company.

    ....................................................................

    This looked great to Ike and Luke as they were now between semesters.

    Ike called for their two appointments and was greeted with one warm welcoming attitude. The Doctor in charge took down weights, age, height, and many questions pertaining to strength ability.

    Luke asked the big question, "What are we taking part in, what kind of test?"

    "The Stud Process Study!" came the answer.

    "Doing what?" asked Luke.

    "According to weight and some tests of personal body strength you each shall be injected with new drugs made by our company. These drugs will enrich your desire for sexual need while endowing you with more enhancements to accomplish the tasks. This test has an elapse time requirement of 60 days. During which time we shall pay your rent, board you and the reseach facility, and escrow your wages for final dispursement according to your needs." said the Doctor.

    Luke was quiet for a moment and then asked one thing more, "Those enhancements, will they make us look or be different on a permanant basis?"

    Now the Doctor was silent and Luke took it to mean he was less than happy with the question.

    "Let me say that all endowments are reversable, and at the request of each benifactor of the study your final wishes will be taken under advice." said the Doctor.

    Luke wasn't sure but the explaination sounded a little like doubletalk.

    At last all seemed in agreement and both Luke and Ike were to report the next morning.
