    Then, to really decide what you will do, you take another glimpse at the soldier squad. Ten of them, it's really too much. Escape would be the preferred solution.

    So, decided to get out of this situation, you jump in the air and begin to fly. Then, to make escape a little bit easier by creating confusion, you dive at them and slash two f them with your feet talon before rising again in the sky. You instinctually knew this should not infect them(after all, they need to have their blood come in contact with your saliva to do so), but you hoped that THEY didn’t knew that. And it apparently worked as, as soon that the captain came aware of the two men wound, he shot them.
    But after the confusion got cleared in their rank, you were indeed far away going to an hiding place.

    As you searched for a place to hide and that the day came, you began to think about your old boring existence as an human. You would have probably amazed to find that therianthrope(that's the proper term, lycanthrope mean werewolf) existed. And as the biologist you were, you would have been happy to study them. But now, as one of them, it didn’t concern you much as your point of view on life had changed as your shape changed. And now, seeing what had happened this night, you don’t think you could trust humans now. Anyway, you stopped thinking about it and searched an hiding place now.

    Finally, after a good number of hour, you finally found a little clearing in an big forest. Then you decide to land. It is only at this moment that you noticed that you were hungry. You then decide to hunt for food to stop your hunger. After some hour of searching for a prey you found a deer that you killed with your razor sharp talons and beak. But then, as you began to eat it, you suddenly notice that night is coming again.
    Shortly after that an incredible pain shoot through your whole body. You had no idea of what it was.
    Well, until you saw your feather began to recede in your skin and your bones changing of shape with cracking and popping sound.
    « Nroooooarck », did you shrieked as you grew aware that you were turning back into an human

    Finally after the last feather shrunk back into your skin you looked at yourself again.
    « Finally »,did you whispered to yourself, »I’m back again now, I’m human »
    What an experience it was! It was like you were someone else! But no matter how much your personality did changed then, you remembered all that had happened, especially the part with those « mercenary » that were searching you. Too bad that YOU were the therianthrope, you would just have liked to study it and publish your studies. But since YOU are it and that some people are wanting to capture you, this isn’t possible. Then, looking at the moon you suddenly remembered that were-eagle took their bestial shape during the day. That mean that only during the night could you act as your old self. Because of this, you now got a lot of thing to change in your habits. One of them being your house. You can’t simply afford being a were-eagle in a city. By luck, you got quite a sum of money that you spared in case of crisis.
    And if being a were-creature isn’t a dire problem, then there something wrong. But first, you had to get out of this forest now. This could take some day. And, looking at yourself, you will need new clothing also.
    Your old one got ripped in many place during your change. So, putting your clothing in a semblance of dignity, you decide to try to find a way out of this forest.

    One hour later, when you were walking on a forest track you stumbled upon so minutes ago, you hear the sound of a car coming by.
