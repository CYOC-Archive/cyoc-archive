    Due to the time of year--near to Christmas--costumes were not to be expected at the boys' high school. None of these nine were showing up in a costume, but no one knew the truth. They were not just dressed as conjoined triplets; each of them actually were conjoined triplets now. None of them wanted this secret blown, however, so they had made custom clothing to make it seem as though each group was just three separate teens in a really good costume--giant shoes, wide pants, and a wide shirt with three neck holes.

    Surprisingly, the school allowed the guys to stay in their "costumes", as long as none of them were distracted from their classes. Fortunately, each group had had the same classes, and there were no quizzes that day that would have made them separate to avoid cheating. In some cases, the classes would split into group discussions; being conjoined triplets, the guys could be their own groups. Lunch was even more interesting; each group would get one lunch--one shared body, one shared stomach, one shared meal--and they would all sit "together" at a table. Each body's three heads would take on one of three tasks during the meal--eating, drinking, and talking--and would alternate so that each body bro was able to get sustenance, refreshment, and a word in edgewise. The bathroom situation was a little difficult, since someone would be there to see them go into a stall, and everyone at school thought that they were only pretending to be conjoined twins. If someone had to go, it would be awkward--for the person outside the stall if they went while still conjoined, and for the guys if they had to unfuse there, especially since only Max and Luke had the ability to separate and merge any of them and had to do so with direct contact. None of the guys had to go while at school, however, and could take care of their shared bladders and colons once they got home.

    In the end, the guys enjoyed their first school day as conjoined siblings. Mark, Max, and Zack looked forward to some video games; Brian, Luke, and Nick wanted to do some swimming; Kevin, Allan, and Dean had some new comics to read. For all nine guys, it was time to head back to Luke's and Mark's/Max's/Zack's houses, where Ethan and Scott--Luke and the trio's conjoined dads were waiting.
