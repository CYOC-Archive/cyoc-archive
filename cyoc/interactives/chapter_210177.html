Hardly anything that would stand up in court... or the principal's office. Nonetheless, Haylee and I are enjoying all the chaos." She grinned sadistically as she picked up a green scale and rubbed it between her finger and thumb. "Fuck the circus, this is the greatest show on earth! On that note, I wonder if the girls in the Clown Club have been affected yet. I never did understand why a girl would actually want twenty to a hundred pounds of extra weight, frizzy and loudly colored hair, and a big red nose."

    "You mean other clubs are being affected?!" Guadalupe gasped in horror.

    "Just came from the Anthro Club," Diana smirked. "The only fur I saw was on the floor. The room was packed with naked human girls just like you. It seems to be all the clubs that offer the most extreme magical transformations. Futa Club, Anthro Club, Fairy Club, Clown Club, Werecreature Club, Mermaid Club, that kind of thing. Robot Club's home free 'cause those chrome-plated nerds are using science, not magic. The Genderswap Club's safe, too, since it's a less radical change."

    "I was wondering why you hadn't turned back into a guy, Diana," said Rochelle. "I was almost hoping you'd have to start calling yourself Dylan again soon, puta. Why is it that las perras have all the luck?"

    "Fortune favors the bold, Ro," Diana chuckled coldly. "By the way, Cheer Squad's having tryouts right now," she added with false sweetness. "Anyone looking for a new extracurricular's welcome to come." She winked at the mermaidless Mermaid Club before strutting out the door as if she owned the place.

    "Fuck that pompom-waving whore!" Brianna spat. "Mark my words, it was her and Haylee Irons who did this to us!"

    "Maybe, but still..." an ex-mermaid of Middle Eastern descent named Fatima said as she reluctently stepped out of her mound of cast-off orange tail scales. "I really do need an extracurricular activity, and I can't count on the Mermaid Club being saved. I... I think I'll... go to the Cheer Squad tryouts."

    "What the hell, Fatima?!" gasped Brianna. "You're just abandoning the Mermaid Club?! Traitor!"

    "Look around!" Fatima barked, gesturing at the countless scales of lost tails littering the indoor beach. "There IS no Mermaid Club! I'm joining a club that still has a reason to exist!"

    Fatima found some clothes and stormed out of the Mermaid Club, followed by six or seven other ex-mermaids. Brianna sighed defeatedly. "Some club vice president I am," she groaned. "On my own in a crisis for five minutes, and I'm already dealing with a motherfucking mutiny! What'll Tiff say?!"

I should know; I used to be on Cheer Squad. Hey, I bet those girls'll come crawling back after suffering under Haylee Irons for five fucking minutes."

    "You think so?" asked Brianna.

    "Sure," said Tori. "They're just panicking 'cause they've lost their magic. Oh shit, I hope my cousin Nikki hasn't been affected yet! She's the vice president of the Futanari Club, and she's covered her entire body with cocks! Can you imagine what a goddamn blow it'll be to her if they ALL fall off of her like lint?!"

    "I guess we just have to hope that 'Chelle, Tiff, and their friends get to the bottom of this," said Brianna. "I've got a pretty good idea who did it, but I wanna know how. That's the only way we'll get our tails back."
