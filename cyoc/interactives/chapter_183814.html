    He brings you into a darkly lit room. Your hear some shuffling until someone lights a candle lighting up the room in the process. “Here it is bro.” The skunk says. “Our secret society!” “So will you join us?” Before you can say anything the skunk says “I’m pretty sure you’ll say yes. Let’s get going!” He brings you into a another room there are red robes everywhere folded nicely. “Now stand still I need to get more members to do this.” The skunk leaves only to come back with 2 more skunks. He takes off his clothes. And begins to put the robe on. Once the skunk is changed he begins to start chanting with all the other skunks in the room. “Momomomo…shemeushemu…fimifimiooo….TRANSFORM!” They all say in unison. And a sensation begins to flow all throughout your body. Black fur covers all of your body. Then above your butt a tail bursts out with a white stripe forming on it going from the tail to your back to your newly formed muzzle. Then you gain some weight near your belly. And also gain a white underbelly. Finally your feet and hands convert to paws with claws. “Ah…It’s all over bro.” The skunk says. Knelling down the other skunk in the room hands you a robe. You begin to put it on but the Jock Skunk interrupts you. “Strip down man.” He says. “Embrace your Skunk…Embrace your Musk.” “We're all animals here y’know.” So you strip down and after that put on the robe. He then escorts you out the room and puts you in a certain spot in the room your were in earlier. Now other members join in creating a circle. A skunk in a Blue robe . Yells “Open up your robes let me give you the marking of the Holy One!” You open your robe exposing your belly. “Ooo..Nice one man!” One of the skunks says before he closes his eyes receiving a marking on his belly. Then it’s your turn you do the same as him you bow your head and feel something like paint being put on your belly. After everyone receives the marking. You open your eyes and join hands with everyone then begin to chant which surprisingly comes naturally to you. “Omomomo….kememekeme….Ahhhhhhh!” You all day. Then the skunk in blue returns and begins to glow you all go towards him. Everyone who goes to him is absorbed by him. You then go to him only to be absorbed.

    You are now one with the Holy One. You now receive all his thoughts and will be able to speak in the Holy Tongue as you pray with everyone else who is a part of him.

    The End. PRAISE THE HOLY ONE!
