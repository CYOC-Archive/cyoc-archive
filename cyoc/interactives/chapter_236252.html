    "Lose the clothing, keep the skills." The man said with a too wide smile.
    "Huh? Oh, no. I want neither."
    "Too late, the change is permanent."
    The statement took a good few seconds to fully sink in, and Leah's stomach began to sink.
    "Uh, what?"
    "Gaga bo?" Leah blinked in surprise at the words. With a look of horror, Leah realized the 'skills' remained. If there were any doubt, the front of her jeans suddenly turned wet, causing tears to build up in her eyes. Didn't she wet herself enough? How is she still peeing?
    "Um, sir? That's...that's ridiculous. I never wanted her to-"
    "-have this behavior that would last forever? Your words." The man said with a robotic chuckle.
    Tina's face started to drain of color as she, too, realized this wasn't some simple misunderstanding.
    "I-I didn't mean that, I was tease- please, I take it back, turn her back to normal!"
    "No can do! Once a purchase is made, it's final! Have a nice day!" The man said, and with a wave goodbye prompted Tina and her cart to propelled rapidly forward, out of the entrance at a blurring speed. Before they even realized it, the two were outside, and the doors shut behind them. The two stood there for a few moments, the events too great and too fast to process. Finally, Tina scampered back towards the doors, stumbling slightly in her new stilettoes. The doors did not automatically open, but she didn't care as she started banging her fist against it loudly.
    "Excuse me? Excuse me?! You just left my step-daughter physically and mentally impaired! Hello!?"

    "Oh shit." Tina whispered. Leah couldn't agree more. What could they even do next?
