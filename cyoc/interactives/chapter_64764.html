    "Sure why the hell not?" They walked up to the blackboard and wrote their name under the other contestants'
    names.

    "I assume we just go back stage." The right Ty said. They walked into a door that said back stage and a ten
    armed man holding all sorts of things looked at them strangely.

    "Are you our newest contestants?" He said and both Ty's nodded.

    "Ok. Wait until I give you the go." He said and went onto other activities. Then, the show started. People
    went out looking relatively normal and came back with various transformations. No one had done a multiple head
    routine yet and that's when the Ty's got an idea.

    "I've got a great routine idea."

    "Way ahead of you." They smashed their heads back together. It was finally their turn and walked out to a
    spotlight following their every move.

    "Hey, folks. So, I was at the mall one da- Hold on, I've got a-" The single headed Ty said and as he said his
    next word. His head divided smoothly. His clothes modified themselves as he wished he was wearing transforming
    clothes as well.

    "-splitting headache." They both said together. This gave them a round of laughs.

    "So, like we were saying-"

    "We were at the mall one day and we went up to a counter and asked for cheeseburger." The other Ty continued.

    "Just cheeseburger. Didn't tell her how much."

    "So she asked and we told her-" After this comment, the left Ty's head divided and gave them an extra set of
    both legs and arms. The legs were right behind their first set.

    "Three" All three heads responded.

    "Hey, you guys. Let's get on with this." The new Ty said. They nodded at him and knew what he was going to do.

    "Well, be-" The left Ty said and the center Ty's head divided and gave them a total of four heads, four new
    legs, giving them four in front and four in back and eight arms.

    "-fore we went back home-"

    "-we decided we would-"

    "-ride the-"

    "TRAIN!!!!" All four yelled and their heads divided back and gave them a separate row of four heads behind
    their first. Their arms divided too giving them eight arms for the back set of Ty's They already had legs back
    there so it didn't matter. Then, the back set divided too and they had a third set of four Ty's adding all the
    extra additions as the first and second ones. Then, another and another. They ended up with 20 heads, 40
    arms, 40 legs and a house full of applause. They marched off still in their Ty train form and waited for the
    results of the contest.
