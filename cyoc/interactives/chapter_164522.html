    Knowing your role.

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Todd M Maccarone 3 years ago A

    As you are female, you go to the right. Walking along, you hear an automated voice speaking. "As a female animal, your job is to serve as the creator of new generations. Be plentiful and bring many new lives into this world".
    Rolling your eyes at this, you can't believe how insultingly chauvinistic this place is. Females have more rights than to just be breeders, after all! Granted, these were female animals, but still...
    Eventually, you reach a console that depicts various animal species. Plenty of them are real, but some of them... they're clearly mythological. They have to be, right? After all, you're pretty sure dragons, unicorns and Bigfoot (among other tall tales) weren't part of the ark! The automated voice perks up again, saying "Please select one of the following species". Uncertain, you think a moment before selecting...
