    When Dan woke up and realized he had a fig leaf for genitals, he immediately went looking for his parents. He found his mom and Kat talking.

    Dan asked, "Mom, I'm not fully turned back to normal. Help me."

    Mom said, "I wish I could. Kat needs to learn how to control her magic."

    "I thought mom you were going to take Kat's wand away." Dan said.

    Mom said, "It's not that simple. I have had a stern talk with your sister and she knows now that she can't get away with trying to kill you but I can't completely take her power away either."

    "Why can't you restore me?" Dan asked.

    Mom said, "If she had changed your genitals directly with magic, I could have but she used an indirect magic that isn't as easy to reverse. She is the only one who can give you your genitals back. If you'll excuse me, I've got some things to attend to but your brother better have his genitals back when I get back."

    After Mom left, Kat said, "There is only one way to restore you. I need to change you back into chocolate. Then I need to sculpt your genitals back into shape."

    Kat didn't wait for Dan's approval. She waved her wand forcing Dan to open his mouth. A bowl of chocolate appeared above his head and poured chocolate straight down his throat. Dan tried to fight it and didn't notice a few other ingredients added to the chocolate. When the spell released him, he saw that he was made of chocolate again but he could move.

    Dan asked, "Why'd you force me to drink chocolate?"

    Kat said, "For the fun of it. Now get over here so I can sculpt your genitals."

    Dan nervously walked over to Kat. Kat heated her wand to melt Dan's fig leaf. Then she got to work sculpting it into genitalia. "Might as well make it bigger while I'm at it." Dan gritted his teeth the entire time Kat handled his genitals but Kat gave Dan his genitals back and bigger than before. Then Kat froze her wand and touched Dan's genitals to cool them off.

    Dan asked, "Why did you have to be so rough, forcing me to drink chocolate and everything?"

    Kat said, "I learned my lesson. Mom can't reverse indirect magic. Your chocolate permanently now."

    Dan cried, "Mom" and went looking for her.

    After Mom learned what Kat had done, Mom explained to Dan, "She's right. You're permanently chocolate now and I can't fix that. I will make sure that Kat is severely punished so she will stop messing with you but you're going to have to learn to live as a chocolate boy. A few tips: You can melt. It would have to be very hot for you to start melting and even then you won't melt quickly but you'll want to be careful to have a bowl nearby that you can melt into if it's too hot. You won't have to worry about death. Even if Kat ate you, I can bring you back but I'd have to stick you in the oven first, shape you into a human shape again, and chill you."
