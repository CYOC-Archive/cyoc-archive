    What Raijin Wants Most

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 12 years ago O

    After some time, Mr Yaun decided Raijin wasn't such a bad guy. He was kind, chrarismatic, and strong - but still dumber than a rock and had lackluster mannerisms. He figured he would make something to keep Raijin distracted, so he wouldn't be interested in Lily any more... As apposed to destroying him.

    "Great work. Here, have some of this tea. It will rejuvenate you and chase the weariness away." He said with a smile, pushing a tea cup towards Raijin.

    "Thanks!" Raijin blurted, quickly taking up the cup of tea and glugging it down in a barbaric manner. "Hmm... That went down pretty good."

    "I'm glad you like it, it's my own special brand of herbs. Tell me boy, what do you want more than anything in the world?" Mr Yaun asked, which would normally surprise anyone since it was such a personal question.

    Raijin suddenly felt strange, and felt like he needed to answer truthfully.

    "Lily." He bluntly replied, which caused Mr Yaun to clench his fist. "Wait uh... Who's Lily again...?"

    "Hah! It worked." Mr Yaun cheered.

    "How'd I get here?" The obviously confused Raijin queried as he scratched his head. "Who are you?"

    "I am just a friend, you could say. You came here to tell me what you wanted most, remember?" Mr Yaun lied. He figured he'd reward Raijin and send him on his way for the help earlier, and then he'd never have to see him again. Mr Yaun also liked to make changing mixtures, so it was a big win-win for everyone in his eyes.

    "Oh yeah, I did, didn't I?" Raijin said, as if he knew the whole time. "This is a tough one, I want so much... Oh I got it!"
