    A Bovine Fling

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 12 years ago A TG

    You settle in sadly to your life as a cow.

    The nymphs use you as little more than a mobile milk station, sucking your teats as they desire. At first,
    Diane talks with you now and then, but since you can't speak the conversations are very one sided, and eventually they stop all together. They treat you as little more than a dumb animal.

    You spend your days grazing from one end of the island to the other. You spot a few other animals now and
    then, there's a hen that stays near the clearing and you've spotted a mare who keeps to herself, but there is
    little else to worry about.

    You realize after a time that three of the nymphs that you had sex with become pregannt, but what happens to
    the children is a mystery. You never see any child-like nymphs, and even the pregnant ones seem to vanish
    once they get far along. You're sure something is happening in the trees, but cows are not exactly noted
    climbers.

    You also get a chance to see two other men come though to meet their fates. The first one you try to push
    back to the door, but he ignores you and ends up a sow the next morning. That's when you discover that the
    nymphs do actually have some tools, since they slice his throat and cook him in a pit. The smell of pork hangs
    over the island for what seems like weeks.

    The second man is turned into a rabbit, and while it looked like the nymphs were ready to cook him up, he
    managed to escape into the underbrush and vanish.

    The next appearance took you off guard, as you smell it before you see it. You don't even know what you're
    smelling, but some part of your new animal brain does and you feel an unfamiliar itch develop. Curious, you
    start to follow the smell, only slightly suspecting what it is.

    You arrive near the clearing to discover a massive bull hanging around. It's only then that you realize
    what's happening.

    And you allow it to happen.

    You turn and feel your tail move out of the way, which is all the invitation the bull needs to mount you, and
    mount you several times over the next few hours. You can't beleive that you're allowing this to happen, but
    you do, and it feels wonderful.

    The bull only stops at dusk, when he is suddenly turned into a cow himself.

    What do you do now?
