    The group all stopped when they heard shouting from above and the sound of running footsteps.

    “Is there any way we can bring Leonard back real quick?” Luca asked.

    They all looked to Jolie who looked upon the man’s half eaten body.

    “I don’t think that’s going to work.” The white haired girl replied.

    “Fuck it, let’s go kill the rest of the Serpent Society and hope not too many hunters get in our way.” Bong-cha stated.

    The four prepared the guns they brought but were stopped when Matt transformed back into his dragon form.

    “Let me handle this one.” He said.

    The golden dragon crawled over to the lift shaft and shot a fury of fire, practically disintegrating the descending hunters. When he looked back he saw the other look in awe.

    “Fuck yea! Burn the entire island woo!” Jolie giggled.

    She looked to the others and shrugged her shoulders when she saw their disapproving faces.

    “…or you know, burn the serpent society and not the island we’re currently on!” Jolie added.

    “The rest of the dragon hunters will be at the main keep, take the car towards the volcano.” Matt said.

    Once they were in the car Bong-cha leaned out the window and shouted to the dragon king,

    “Okay, but how are we going to get out of this cave? You torched the lift!” The red haired vampire yelled.

    Her question was answered when the golden dragon grabbed the car and threw them out onto the surface. When they were out Matt changed back to his human form and got on the car.

    “I thought you were gonna set these assholes on fire?” Jolie asked.

    “Believe me I’d love nothing more, but this whole island is built to defend against dragons. I’m strong but there’s no way I can take this place out on my own.” He explained.

    The vampire nodded and hit the accelerator, they dodged bullets and even the stray missile on the way towards the main keep. They passed rouge hunters and the whole island seemed to be in chaos, most of the hunters had fled but some remained, most likely to kill the intruders. When they ran into a blockade Jolie used hee magic to rip up the earth, dislodging the barricades and scattering the soldiers. Eventually they arrived at the walls to the keep, turrets and soldiers lined the walls, Bong-cha parked behind a small outcrop of trees where they got out to survey the keep.

    “The car could make it but there’d be no room to maneuver after we’re in.” The vampire said.

    “What if we snuck in as rouge hunters again?” Setepenre asked.

    “I don’t think that’s a good idea, they’ll be on high alert, they probably won’t let anybody who’s not Serpent Society in.” Matt replied.

    “We have to do something, maybe sneak inside?” Luca added.
