    The Magic Shop

    A new caretaker for her

    added 5 days ago O

    Laura took a deep breath, trying to process her new reality. "I want everyone else to be like me," she said finally, looking up at the wizard with a determined gaze. "I want everyone in the world to wear diapers and act in a babyish way"
    The wizard's expression turned sympathetic, but firm. "I'm afraid that's not possible, Laura," he said gently. "I can't just change the entire world to suit your desires."
    Laura felt a pang of disappointment, but she refused to give up. "Then what can you do for me?" she asked, trying to keep her tone calm.
    The wizard smiled kindly. "I can find someone to take care of you," he said. "Someone who can help you adjust to your new reality, and make sure you're safe and happy."
    Laura thought about it for a moment, then nodded slowly. "Okay," she said softly.
    The wizard nodded, and raised his hand. Laura felt a strange sensation wash over her, like she was being lifted out of her body. When she opened her eyes again, she found herself in a cozy, dimly lit room. A warm, comforting smell filled the air, like baking cookies.
    A gentle voice spoke from behind her. "Welcome, Laura. I'm so glad you're here."
    Laura turned to see a kind-faced woman, with long, curly brown hair and a warm smile. She was dressed in a soft, flowing robe, and her eyes twinkled with kindness.
    "Who are you?" Laura asked, feeling a little shy.
    "I'm Mrs. Emma," the woman replied. "I'm a wizard, just like the one who brought you here. And I'm here to take care of you, and help you adjust to your new reality."
    Laura felt a wave of relief wash over her. Maybe this new reality wouldn't be so bad after all.
    Mrs. Emma smiled, and opened her arms. "Come here, dear," she said. "Let me give you a hug. You must be feeling so overwhelmed."
    Laura nodded, feeling a lump form in her throat. She walked into her arms, and felt herself enveloped in a warm, comforting hug.
    As she hugged Mrs. Emma back, Laura felt a strange sensation wash over her. It was like her body was relaxing, letting go of all its tension and stress.
    When Mrs. Emma finally pulled back, Laura felt like a new person. She smiled up at her, feeling a sense of gratitude and wonder.
    "Thank you," she said softly. "I feel so much better now."
    Mrs. Emma smiled, and stroked Laura's hair. "You're welcome, dear," she said. "Now, let's get you changed into something comfortable. And then we'll have some lunch, and talk about your new routine."
    Laura nodded, feeling a sense of excitement and anticipation. She had no idea what the future held, but with Mrs. Emma by her side, she felt like she could face anything.
    As Mrs. Emma led her to the changing table, Laura felt a sense of wonder and curiosity. What would her new life be like? And how would she adjust to being an adult baby?
