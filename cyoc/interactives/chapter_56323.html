One boy was clearly a few years older than the other. As Ty and Cody stepped outside and watched, both boys came riding down the ramp and they rolled off to the side, stopping right in front of them.

    The older boy’s name was Ryan. He was about 17 years old, and he was clearly and expert skater. The younger boy was about 11 years old, and his name was Eric. Ryan was busy teaching Eric a few tricks about skateboarding on the ramp.

    “Hey, Ryan! This is my friend Ty!” Cody said. “Ty, This is Ryan and his little bro, Eric…”

    “Hi, Ty! Nice to meet ya!” Ryan said, shaking Ty’s hand.

    Ty was clearly impressed. Ryan was skinny and tall. He was wearing skate shoes with knee pads, elbow pads and wrist guards, along with a black helmet on his head. Eric was completely dressed up in the same protective gear. Other than that, both boys wore t-shirts, shorts, and the same skate-shoes.

    “So… Do you guys ever work out in the gym?” Ty asked Ryan.

    “Naw… I can’t seem to gain any muscle… I tried, but… I dunno… I wish I could…” Ryan sighed. “And, of course, Eric’s too young to gain muscle!”

    “Oh, yeah?” Ty laughed.

    “We just skate… It’s the only thing we know how to do!” Ryan said, as he turned around and faced the ramp, where Eric started to run a few laps up and down the ramp.

Ryan felt his calves harden and then fill up a bit. As he bent down to scratch them with his hand, he felt them stretch out even further. His calves then hardened and stiffened up for a little bit, swelling up until they were as big as baseballs, giving that amazing diamond-look that athletes love.

    “What’s this?” Ryan wondered, as he bent down and saw what was happening.

    Next, his quads began to swell and thicken while widen at the same time, becoming huge pillars of thick, powerful pillars of power. His legs became like huge oak trees, big and thick and round, which were formed like layers upon layers of muscle from the knee to the waist.

    “Man! What is going on?” Ryan gasped, looking down at his hulking 44-inch legs.

At the same time, his pectorals pushed outwards and sideways, filling out like two huge spheres of muscle the size of half-basketballs, large and round and as hard as granite, protecting his chest like a layer of armor.

    “TY! What’s going on!!??”

    “Well… You said you couldn’t gain any muscle… so here you go!” Ty laughed.

    “Oh man… I’m huge!” Ryan gasped, as he admired his muscles while flexing them one by one.

    Ryan jumped on his board and realized how easy it was to skate, now that he possessed such amazing strength. He could skate around and not grow tired.

    Ty and Cody just looked on and Ryan just glided his board over the ramp, from one edge to the other, without getting tired, even with little Eric looking on at his big, muscular brother…
