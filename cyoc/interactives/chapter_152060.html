    You exited the magic shop with a box in your hands. You have no memory of the other items, just entering the store, what you bought and leaving the store. You tried to go back in. But the Magic Shop was gone, in it’s place was an empty lot. You open the box and see your purchase.

    Inside was an ordinary Rubix cube with a list of instructions that read as follows:

    —————

    “Thank you for your purchase of THE RUBIX CUBE OF BODY!

    How To Use:

    Step 01: Select a target for the Rubix Cube to effect the body or form of. This can be: a single person, your self, your self and 1-6 other people(one person representing each side) an object, or an animal.

    Step 02: Once you have selected: yourself, the person, animal, object etc. Start mixing up the RUBIX CUBE OF BODY. The traits of all those selected will be shuffled around similar to the sides of the rubix cube. The more mixed up the rubix cube, the more the body parts will shift around.

    How to select:

    Step 01: Touch one of the RUBIX CUBE OF BODY’s sides To: your self, the person or animal etc. you want it to effect and wait for that side to start glowing signifying the selection in made.

    Note: For more detail in mixing you can touch more than one side to the same object. For example: if you and a friend are swapping, it is recommended you each touch 3 sides to get the best experience.

    How to undo:

    Step 01: Solve the rubix cube after it is mixed up. EASY RIGHT?”

    —————

    After rereading the instructions for a third time, your lift the rubix cube out of the box and look for your first target to test it out on in the street.
