    You and your friends enter the mysterious house just outside of Manitou Springs Colorado. It's a lot like the Adam's Family House of the West, full of Metaphysical and occult-ish objects. No one's wanted to go in here for awhile, even through dares.

    There are four of you. There is yourself, obviously. Anthony acts somewhat as the leader of the group, being the tallest. He is notable for smoking, but fortunately for you and your friend Rita, he's not a chain-smoker, and will actually go around the corner when he needs a smoke.

    Rita is a girl of Spanish (From Spain) origin. Around the town, there are rumours that she's dating Anthony, but you haven't seen any proof that she may have at all.

    And there's Les, well...he's Les. He's only eleven and is basically just following you three around because he thinks it's cool. It's actually pretty nice that you allow him to do that, and that you don't try to teach him anything that he'll go say to his parents. (This is a small town after all...)



    Anthony's pointing to an open window. "Hey look," He said, "The window's open...You want to go in here?"

    You look at Rita and Les. It's all going to go down to Rita's choice, Les will do whatever you and Anthony say, and you don't want to have the choice made on you.

    "It's empty, isn't it?" Rita asks.

    "Of course it's empty," Anthony responds as he pushes back the old-fashioned window frame, "Look at all that dust, it's like a tomb in there!"

    Les runs over to the window and climbs in.

    "I guess it's decided! We're going in!" Anthony says.

    "It's awesome in here!" You hear Les saying from the other side.

    You, Rita, and Anthony are following each other through the open window. Les is standing at a doorway and he takes off.

    "LES!" You say to him.

    You and Anthony follow Les, sure that Rita is behind you. You find an open door and walk in. Les is looking at a book, seemingly like a library full of stuff.

    "Look at this!" Les says hyperactively. He opens a page.

    "Les! Put that down!" You say.

    Anthony is distracted. "This is cool, look at all this stuff here!" Anthony's looking around. You're too busy trying to convince Les to put the book down that you don't see that Rita isn't following you.

    Les puts the book back down. You see him touching something that looks like it's got powder in it.

    "Have you never been taught not to enter someone else's house?" A voice asks.

    You and Anthony turn around. There is a shadowy figure, probably Native American.

    "W-We didn't know you lived here!" You say, trying not to get in trouble.

    "You also don't want to break into a Shaman's house...Haven't you ever been told that breaking into Shaman and Wizards' houses are the most dangerous kind?"

    Neither you nor Anthony can move. You have no idea where Rita and Les are.

    The man turns to his left and opens up a chest. He pulls out something, an animal skin!

    But wait, what's he going to do with an animal skin?

    "Can you guess what this is?" He said, taking the skin over to Anthony. He begins to wrap the skin around Anthony. He gasps.

    What kind of skin is it?

    What kind of skin is it?
