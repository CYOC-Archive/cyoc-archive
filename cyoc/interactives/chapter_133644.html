    Mika decided, "We might as well trust him and see where this goes. It's not like we can't fight him off, after all." Audrey looked suspicious but nodded.

    "Yes! Thank you" the boy exclaimed, "Follow me!" He ran off through the unpaved streets and Mika and Audrey followed behind, eventually coming to a small derelict barn on the outskirts of the village. "In here, the other victims of the stone can help us."

    Audrey grabbed her sword, just in case, and she and Mika entered the unlit and unfurnished building... only to find the innkeeper waiting inside for them, smiling evilly. In his hand was a blue stone. Mika tried to cast a spell, while Audrey yelled and charged at the man with her sword, but before either of them could do anything the innkeeper took out a stone, pointed it at them and blasted two blue beams that squarely hit both targets. Mika and Audrey collapsed to the ground, instantly unconscious. There forms started shrinking in their clothes and armor, after they diapered from view entirely the innkeeper waved the stone over the two bundles of cloth and metal vanishing then and revealing the two identical little blond girls underneath.

    The newly made identical twins of about four or five years old wore dirty, naked, and rather homily. The innkeeper sneered, when he found the stone and discovered its powers he had aspirations of creating a slave harem for himself, but in the last decade of ownership had yet to allow him to transfigure someone into an attractive adult woman. The other ‘sows’ of his own creation lay strewn about the dirt and mud of the abandoned barn he keeps them in.

    The boy that led the adventurers was his only, rather disastrous, attempt at creating a pretty, girly boy. Speaking of the boy, the innkeeper turns to point the stone at him.

    The boy had only a moment of panic and outrage, protesting, "What?! But I brought them to you; I did just as you asked me too!”

    “Yah, and that’s why I’m not turning you into a hog and making you fuck my pigs, but I’m still wiping your memory.”

    The Stone glowed, his eyes dulled, and he fell silent and looked around in confusion, not seeming to notice all the women and young girls lying immobile on the floor. "Uh, what'm I doin' here?"

    "You helped me with an errand," the innkeeper explained condescendingly, and then held out a small hunk of bread. "Now take this for your troubles."

    A meager prize though it was, the Boy's eyes lit up and he grinned as happily as though he'd been given a sack of candy. "Wow, thanks, Master Robin!" He grabbed the bread and munched it greedily.

    “Up you two lazy shits, Master Robin want’s you two up before sun-up or he’ll turn you into piglets”
    Mika felt a un-shoed foot kick her in the side alongside the shouting of her eldest sister. Something about that last thought felt strangely untrue to Mika.

    Standing above her was a young filthy and ugly Airborn woman with blond hair, small dishevelled white-feathered wings, a slightly misshapen head and pregnant belly.

    Air-born are a magical slave-race that Master created out of air magic using the power of the stone. They were blond haired and blue eyed and had white-fathered wings that did not allow them to fly. Mika suddenly remembers that she is also an Airborn when she stretched her stubby, vestige wings.

    Master created her and her twin sister Audrey last night to help around the inn, mostly doing the dirtiest jobs, you are going to have to work hard or the Master will think you are useless and turn you into something else.

    Mika hear her twin stand up and say in a way that is so polite that she is shocked that it came from Audrey “Greetings eldest sister, my name is Audrey” Mika jumps up and adds “and I’m Mika, what’s your name?”

    “It’s Loral and you don’t have time for chit-chat, if you don’t get the cleaning done you don’t eat. Now if you need to pee, use that corner over there and if you want a drink the watering trough is..”

    “Big sister, the Waterborn went to sleep in the trough again.” Said the other older Airborn sister as she lifts a black haired fishy looking girl of seven or eight out of the water. Other than the four Airborn sisters and the Waterborn girl the Master has created three Earthborn women and a single Fireborn boy.

    Loral let out a long suffering sigh. “We don’t have time to change the water right now Harry, we are just going to have to put up with drinking Mona’s bath water, Again.”

    Loral moved to the center of the barn “All right every one, line up by age and I’ll hand out this morning’s job’s then we will get dressed in the clothes the Master graciously lets us use, and get to work, we will have our morning feeding after sunrise.”

    “Harry, you have to pick-up some sack cloth from the store house and get to work on clothing for our new youngest sisters.” Sister Harry nods happily “I have a great idea for the cutest dresses for them.”

    “Moon, your lute is fixed; you can find it in the main room of the inn, your back on Minstrel duty today.” Loral said while pointing to the eldest of the three Earthborn sisters, Earthborns have mud-brown skin and hair, and have a few leaves sprouting on their shoulders.

    “Lewy that means you’re off entertainment, so I’ll need you back in the kitchen with me.” The next eldest Earthborn looked disappointed with the news.

    “Hunter, you’re on hunting again.” Loral told the youngest Earthborn

    “Ash, you’re on lookout, Master Robin want to know if any more Adventurers come to town.” She tells the only boy among you, the Fireborn nods. Fireborn are red haired and pale skinned, the also have red gems on their foreheads, but Master cut Ash’s off and sold it when he was created, Ash still has the scar.

    “Mona, Master has gifted us a fresh straw to try and patch the leak in the roof, do you think you can handle that while we are out?” Mona nods slowly, she is the least human looking and the Master says she’s not allowed in the inn

    “As for you two, your job is to clean out the inn’s chamber pots and wash the wiping rags, the toilet rooms are the in the north-east corner on every floor, use the sewer grate and water pump in the alleyway on the left side of the inn for the cleaning.” Loral did not hesitate to give the dirtiest job to her two youngest sisters.
