    Meeting an old friend

    added 9 months ago AR Baby

    At first, Selina was happy to see that Derek was in this place, but as she started to approach the desk, her happiness soon began to fade. Looking closer, she realized that this person wasn't Derek. Her friend had no mustache, no green eyes, much less brown hair. So, to her bad luck, she would still have to find a way to escape.

    Soon, the daycare workers proceeded to take Selina and some children to the nap room. Inside the room, the children were placed on cots since they were the oldest in the daycare. Selina simply proceeded to lie down and pretended to fall asleep. After making sure all the children were asleep, the worker proceeded to turn off the lights and leave the room.

    As the worker left the room, Selina proceeded to get up and quickly started heading towards the door. Unfortunately, the daycare worker had closed it completely, so Selina had to find another way out. And to her surprise, she found something that might help her. In the room there was a small vent that might be able to take her out of the daycare.

    Although there was a small problem. The vent was very small, so Selina would have to regress a few years if she wanted to go in there. Selina didn't like the idea of regressing even more to enter the vent but she really didn't want to get trapped in this place so she had no choice but to take another 3 pills to escape. By swallowing the pills, Selina began to regress to the point that now all that was left of her was a 1-year-old baby girl.

    Although she was already the right size to fit into the vent, her clothes were now too big for her, so she started looking for something she could wear and something where she could store the pills and the flash drive. Luckily, near her was a toy chest containing a white onesie, a diaper, and a stuffed dog attached to a pacifier.

    So Selina proceeded to remove all her old clothes, she took the diaper that was inside the toy chest and with some difficulty she was able to put it on. Then she proceeded to put on the white onesie. And finally she proceeded to place both the pills and the flash drive inside the stuffed animal. And although she didn't think of using the pacifier at first, she eventually proceeded to place the pacifier in her mouth so she wouldn't have to go around holding it in her hand.

    Now that she was finally ready, she proceeded to head towards the vent. She proceeded to climb inside the vent and began to crawl inside as she continued to suck on her pacifier.

    She kept on crawling for a long time until she finally reached the end of the vent. Coming out of that small duct, Selina was shocked. It turns out that the vent didn't take her outside of the daycare but only took her to the playroom. And right now the room was being occupied by the youngest children in the daycare (who were mostly babies).

    Although Selina at first believed that there would be no way to escape from here, she soon realized something important. One of the workers who took care of the babies in the room was Clover, a friend of hers. Maybe she could help her though there was a problem. Since she was now a baby, all that came out of her mouth was baby babble so she had to find a way to convince Clover that she was really Selina. And the first thing that occurred to Selina is...
