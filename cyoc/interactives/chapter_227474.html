    Hannah, felt good and thought she had pressed the stop button and left the stopwatch on the seat as she decided to go up and socialize a bit... Maybe people would mistake her as Bianca who was late to prep for the ceremony. The woman mostly stayed around the same size as she had gotten the bar area in 3 minutes she said "Can I get some whisky on the rocks" while going for her wallet in her purse the bartender gave her a look.
    "Can I please see some ID?" The woman losing more and more years every quarter of a minute spent another year grabbing her ID from her wallet and after she showed it... "...Wait... I can believe the old picture a bit, but the date's all wrong. At least make it believable shrimp."
    "Shrimp!?!" Hannah sputtered she hadn't been called shrimp since her teens.
    "Yeah, and that oversized business suit your mom loaned you isn't doing you any favors for the whole age thing either." The bartender had been through giving any shits to underage clientele for awhile. He'd turn a blind eye if a family member gave someone 17-18+ a little beer or wine, but serving them was a whole other story. The girl went for her bag and she brought out a compact and was walking away. from the bar, the Bartender wondered if she had tried some makeup to make herself look older and wondered if it blew off or something... He didn't wonder long as someone else came up for a drink.

    Hannah saw she was getting too young, the youth in her face was too much as she was a teen and every couple of seconds she had a shrink spurt! An inch or two, her breast sizes, everything was shrinking away! Her socks were baggy and she had abandoned her shoes as she tried to get to the stopwatch which she OBVIOUSLY hadn't stopped! Somewhere when she was 14 someone stopped her and asked "Young miss? Where are your parents?" and grabbed her wrist. She didn't have TIME for this! Obviously not as she tried to think and as they asked "Is this girl anyone's child?" as they wondered who let their daughter around in pants that were quickly losing the hips to stay attached to, and in half a minute Hannah saw one good thing about getting younger and pulled down as her thinning wrist and hands managed to slip through the grip. She made a mad dash to the stopwatch only being hampered as she had to disrobe her suit pants which didn't matter much as her top was large enough to cover her bottom. People were trying to catch her and confused as she was visibly shrinking till she got to the stopwatch and made sure it stopped! How young was she exactly? She wasn't sure, but puberty hadn't set in! People concerned came to look at the once tween playing dress up turned little girl playing dress up unsure what was happening. Hannah's parents now greyed and aging into their seniority recognized the little girl they had raised before Bianca was... Well the concept of a younger sibling had been around back then... still how did she get so young?
