Starting with expulsion of contagious #8108 gases from both belches and flatulence, followed by excessive weight gain across the whole body, typically in the stomach, chest, head, and rear with the limbs typically being engulfed by the after mentioned reigns. This is further offset by the instance's new tendency to 'laziness', sometimes not moving for days during their infection. #8108-1 instances also expel a contagus gas that when inhaled by unaffected individuals reproduces the effect of direct contact with #8108 sources. This gas also warps the instances mind. Individuals who encounter a #8108 for the first time are typically repulsed by such and individual and will in some way reach out to inform someone. Rumors of this nature have led to numerous positive cases of a #8108-1 instance. [research into similarities between #8108 and #28 are underway: results still pending]

Meaning so long as a instance of #8108-1 is exposed to their own gasses, or the gasses of another #8108-1 instance symptoms will continue. This realization was uncovered when Mary W[REDACTED] a suspected #8108-1 instance under surveillance was caught in a house fire in [REDACTED] Georgia was rescued by firefighters. Within hours symptoms had started to regress.
