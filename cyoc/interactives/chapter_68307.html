    "Hi mom!" LJ said to his mom.

    "Hey mom!" RJ said out loud.

    "EEEEEKK!" their mother screamed as she saw her ten-year-old son standing before her, slightly more muscular,
    and with two heads on his shoulders that were completely identical!

    "JAKE!" she shouted.

    "What mom?"

    "Yes, mom?"

    Their mother gasped out loud since she got TWO responses, at the same time!

    "How... How on earth is this possible? How did you get two heads?" their mom said as tears began to roll from
    her eyes.

    "I was walking past the river in the woods when I stopped and took a drink from the water! The next thing I
    knew, was that some of my muscles started to grow! Right after that, my head started to split and I grew
    another head on my shoulders!" Jake's head on the left explained.

    "Yeah! That's when I popped up!" RJ added.

    "This... This is crazy!" their mom said, as she began to tremble.

    Just then, the bedroom door opened and Jake's dad walked in.

    "What is going on, here? Why aren't you downstairs? Why... Oh!"

    "Jerry!" mom said to her husband. "Look at Jake! He has two heads!"

    His dad walked over to his son and took a good look at him.

    "What? How did you do that?"

    "I drank some water from the river and my muscles started to gr--... Uh oh!" LJ explained, just as he was
    overcome by that weird feeling again.

    "What? What's wrong?" dad asked them.

    "Uhhhh!" LJ and RJ groaned in unison.

    Suddenly, Jake's arms began to thicken as his biceps bulged with more muscle! Their quads widened and grew
    even more while their pectorals bulged a bit as well! The two parents saw their sons' muscles grow before
    their very eyes!

    "Ohmygosh... Not only has he become a FREAK... He's getting stronger as well!" their dad gasped.

    Jerry and Carla looked on as the two Jakes gained twenty pounds of muscles on their body!
