    “That's...a very unique name.”

Part of her charm included a form of subtle hypnotism, and at first, Terry had resisted it- as if she either had very strong willpower, or someone else had used a similar power on her. But her desire and greed had overcome whatever it was, and now Meridiana had the woman in the palm of her hand.
