    "Down on the Farm!" Molly says excitedly, she had always loved farms and farm animals ever since her parents had taken her to visit one.

    "Okay then, here are your passes to the area," Natalie says, taking out three passes and giving it to the family. "There's a bus outside that will take you there, have fun here at the Animal Resort."

    "Come on!" Molly says, running for the door, but stopping to wait for her parents. Jane was starting to be more relaxed about this whole thing, after all what harm could you get to on a farm? She follows Carl to where Molly was standing, and they board the bus a few minutes later.

    Molly watches out the window while the sights of the island go past. "Wow, look at that one," she says, pointing as the Prehistoric area goes past with a herd of dinosaurs. This was going to be the best vacation ever!

    When they reach Down on the Farm, they're ushered into a waiting area where another female receptionist is waiting with a smile. "Welcome to Down on the Farm, here's a list of the animals you can turn into," she says, giving them a list.

    "Its your decision, Molly," Carl says, giving the list to her.

    Chicken
    Duck
    Goose
    Cat
    Cow
    Dog
    Goat
    Horse (Draught)
    Horse (Riding)
    Pig
    Sheep

    Molly's eyes go wide, all the options sounded pretty fun to her (except maybe Pig). The receptionist leaned over the counter to look down at her.

    "Don't forget, any animal you change into's fur will match your normal hair color," she says, and Molly nods in understanding. "Well, what would you like to turn into young lady?"

    Molly thinks before answering.
