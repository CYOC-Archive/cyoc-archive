    Transform or Dare?

    Jen the Cerberus Centaur

    The bus flung from side to side as it travelled down the gravel path. Jen kept her head down, buried in her lap, as she pretended to nap for lack of wanting to explain the curse to anyone else, lest they mock her too. The bus suddenly shifted particularly hard over a metal grate, and Jen bounced up. For a split second, she looked up. She saw a three-headed guard dog, a German shephard!

    "Oh, no," she sighed, aghast. The dog began to run alongside the bus, much to the amusement of the other students. It followed by Jen's window as the bus bounded down the path to the farmhouse, letting Jen take in what was about to happen.

    "But... if I become a taur version of you, what happens to your other heads?" she wondered to herself. The bus began to slow. "This'll be interesting," Jen groaned, scared for her coming change.

    The bus came to a stop. "Alright, students, now partner up!" her teacher called from the front. Jen looked over at her friend Claire, and she nodded in silent agreement. The two got up, the last pair of students to exit the bus. Jen looked around, dazed, worried about her change.

    BOOM! The dog ran into Jen at full force, shoving its center head up Jen's skirt. Jen felt it merge with her, and began to cry. She called out, but none of the other students responded, or even registered what was taking place.

    As Jen felt her body merge, an intense tingling spread over her new lower body and arms. Her arms and legs shriveled up as the dogs' body began to grow. "No no no no!" Jen cried. She looked over as her arms completely disappeared, her lower body continuing to grow. And grow. And grow.

    Soon enough, the tingling ceased. A flash filled Jen's vision and she was suddenly seeing from two more heads! Her sense of smell increased dramatically too, as did her hearing. She now had control of the other two heads! Speaking of heads, they now towered on either side of her, immense, far larger than any other dog's heads. They panted in the summer heat. Jen felt immense power in her new body, being twice the size of a shire horse, while also having two massive dog's head's, had its advantages! Sure, Jen's human body didn't have any arms, but her new dog heads could work. She saw Kyle and began to bark madly, her anger taking over her until she felt someone pat at her stomach below her.

    "Jen, Jen, calm down, girl," she heard Claire say. The pats soothed her fiery rage, and she began to wag her massive tail as Claire continued to rub her belly. She felt her two heads begin to pant happily, a calm rushing over her.

    "Now, class, it's time to tour!" she heard her teacher say...
