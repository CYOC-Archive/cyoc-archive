    As Todd was leaving the library, he still felt like the girl needed at least one more change. He fiddled with the remote until he got the desired setting, aimed, and fired.

    Suddenly the girl's udders started to shift to the left, sloshing noises audible from where Todd was standing, while another, just as pink growth shifted just next to it. It expanded and filled until it reached the same size as her udders, and 4 teats of the same size grew in on it, too. She now had two 30 gallon udders! They sprawled out over her legs and lap and onto the ground, carrying a mind boggling 140 lbs and 60 gallons of milk. The girl conitnued to read, thinking nothing of her now inconcievably huge udders. She reached again to scratch the new one, pressing her torso flat against it and reaching as far as she could to scratch an itch between her far teats. "Why does she keep scratching them?" Todd whispered to himself, to which she replied "They're still growing, dipshit. Duh, didn't you learn anything in Anatomy?" Todd had forgotten that he had made her ears extra sensitive too.

    He heard her grunt and try to get up. Her two udders shifted onto the ground as she moved to the edge of the couch, causing her to momentarily lose balance as they fell to the ground, sloshing violently. She sighed and heaved her udders up, feeling the copious amount of liquid in it slosh and move, walking about three steps every minute to catch her breath from heaving 140 lbs of milk up so she could move her feet. She heaved up, walked two or three steps, and then let her udders fall, slapping the ground. In the entire process, all 8 of her teats continued to drag on the ground, and her thick tail slapped the ground periodically. She continued to walk like this for what seemed like hours, after about 30 minutes eventually leaving the library and going wherever she was going.
