What would drive your girlfriend further was an 11 which in fact did get her closer to her goals of the age spaces but landed her in Jail... Just visiting though. Second non-double 8 landed you on your Pennsylvania avenue, and then your girlfriend rolled a 9 and hit New York Avenue or in other words the sister of the one she got prior "Physical" for the "Aging" leylines. The Monopoly money she had couldn't go flying fast enough as she concentrated as she aged quickly back to her regular age. Then she thought for a second and regressed back into an 8 year old, then further into her early thirties. Reality adjusted so a grown woman wasn't in the sundress and panties of a little kid, but in a mature dress which showed off a decent amount of cleavage. You had a slight feeling in the back of your head that the local Cougar had invited you for a board game and maybe... a little something extra. Then she regressed back to her regular age and chuckled a bit as reality went back to normal... proper this time aside from a few changes like her bunny ears. "This feels so good~" Your girlfriend nearly moaned "Shame we'll probably lose these powers when the game's over."
    "Well we can enjoy the game as it is now~" You said ready to accept what came after. 6... which landed you on Body Mod, Merge/Remove and you could see your girlfriend deciding what to add or remove. "Those Wings, they are kinda stupid." and then the wings you had given yourself popped off. You weren't THAT attached to them, but this felt roughly equivalent to when she "accidentally" threw away some of your clothes in the trash... and then cut them up after you fished them out. Anyways it was her turn.
