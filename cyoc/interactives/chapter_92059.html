    The doorbell rang. Cy heard the door open. The person walked in. Cy could tell it was Jason's girlfriend, just by the way they talked. He could see something beside her on a leash it was . . . a naked young man.

    "Xerxes?" Cyrus asked and was zapped. He had tried to get the shock collar off and failed. He was still fighting and trying to get away but the Youngs had attached additional collars, bracelets and anklets so that even if Cyrus managed to get free from one, he'd be zapped by the others. All these shock collars were programmed to shock if only one of them was removed and since he was monitored at all times, any collar he removed would quickly be put back on him.

    Xerxes sat silently like an obedient pet. He barely acknowledged Cyrus.

    Mrs. Young said, "Jennifer, thank you for coming over. Our new pet needs trained quickly. We only have a week before his father comes back.

    Jennifer said, "Yes, Mrs. Young. Are we going to try to turn his father into a pet as well? I never expected Xerxes father and brother to move into our community but if we can turn all three of them into pets then there will be no one left to ask questions."

    Mrs. Young said, "Ideally, it would be great to get the father as well and complete the set. However, it isn't going to be as easy to get him as it was to get his boys. You got Xerxes by seduction and Jason got Cyrus by pretending to be his friend but I'm not sure either will work to get the father. That's why we should train Cyrus quickly. If we can get him to help us get his father..."

    Cyrus yelled, "I'll never help you. Aaarrrggghh." He rolled on the floor in pain from the shock collars.

    Jennifer said, "He's still fighting. I don't know if we can get him trained in a week."

    Jason said, "That's why I asked you to bring his brother over. Your new pet X can help us train my new pet Cy."

    Jennifer asked, "What do you have in mind?"
