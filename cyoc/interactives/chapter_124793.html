    DAY 0 OF BIMBOPOCALYPSE

    Steve and James are twin brothers who just turned off the t.v. after hearing the reports. They were denying anything like that is possible but soon hear screaming outside.

    They are both 13 and their parents are out of town while they get stuck at home. They knew everything about the house and quickly run into the underground tunnel their superstitious dad built in case of a zombie apocalypse.

    Steve and James ran through the tunnel, carrying guns, food and essentials (as well as a family photo) to keep themselves alive and happy.

    DAY 2 OF BIMBOPOCALYPSE

    Steve and James had been walking for a day in the tunnel. Near the end of it, they took a nap too gain some energy for the walk a head. They knew what to do and where to go since they decided to bring an old radio and managed to tap into a news station. The news station reported the massacre of people across America and then told all survivors to head to California for the next 2 months to get a ride over to Hawaii, Alaska or Japan.

    Steve and James set out on their adventure to get to california. They lived in Maine so they are going to need a car or truck to get their on time. The two brothers didn't care what fucked up place America had become in just a day. They knew to just kill the bimbos and bring any survivors that were in need with them.
