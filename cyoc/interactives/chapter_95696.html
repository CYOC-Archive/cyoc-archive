Inside were several pairs of woman's shoes of various sizes, along with a small, black notebook. Rani, and especially you, were perplexed at the offering. "You remember the talk we had about the socks a few days ago?". He went on to explain, mainly for your sake, that he and Rani discussed the power of the socks while at the look-out point. She used to be hesitant about the socks, not really sure if she'd like becoming another person. She even thought they would be a great way to help her to create relatable characters with realistic quirks and emotions. But she still wasn't enthusiastic about becoming a man again.
