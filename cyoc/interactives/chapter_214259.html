For the dhamphir she felt as if she was following a bloodhound, the mara seeming to hone in on her girlfriend. The teens now paused as they stood outside a small shop. "Is this it," Nat asked. The shelves adorned with figurines and other small trinkets. Unsure of what could be wrong, she blushed as she saw a rather confused middle aged man exit the back. "Can I help you," he asked on confusion.
