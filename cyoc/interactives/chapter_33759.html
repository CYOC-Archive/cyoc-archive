    Robert sat in his cluttered living room, packed with papers, boxes and books, and opened the strange brown package he had found on the doorstep of his house, inside he found a series of plastic wrapped CD's and a odd futuristic remote along with an instruction booklet.

    Robert picked up the instruction book as he rubbed the sleep from his eyes (even though the sun had just set) The plain white paperback displayed the words TransDem Labs in bold, official print.
    Robert stared at the title, scarcely daring to breath. Was this it? What he had been waiting for? He quickly opened the book and found a quick hand-written message on the first page:

    Dear sir or Madam,
    The package you have just received is the newest version of TransDem's revolutionary new morphing software. The Chronivac 4.0 can not only alter the physical bounds of any individual, but can bend and twist the very fabric of reality.
    You may notice that this booklet is blank, Chronivac is a new piece of technology that you will be testing, it's limits are not yet known therefore feel free to fill the pages with any notes you want to take.
    Remember, be safe, be responsible.

    Robert shook, this was it, this was TransDem's brake-through he had followed for months on the internet, this was the launch point from which he had created his own 'pet project'.

    Giddy with excitement Robert grabbed the package and it's contents and practically flew down the stairs to the basement, hooking up the discs and emitter to his computer he began unraveling the programs source code.
    Robert lost all track of time as he rifled through the mysteries the Chronivac had behind it's numerous firewalls and barriers. Tough eventually he had it cracked, the entire code that made the Chronivac what it was, he was finally ready to use it for the completion of his own project.

    Several days passed but eventually Robert through down his tools and marveled at his genius, before him on the table sat an antique style ray gun fused to bits of the Chronivacs emitter as well as a small headset device that looked like bluetooth.

    Robert smiled, with the ray gun and the headset, he had harnessed the Chronivac's transformative power a hundred fold. All one had too do was wear the head set and your thoughts would be transmitted to the gun.
    Robert smiled, all his dreams were about to come true.
