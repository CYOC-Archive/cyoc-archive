    "Hey, everyone, it's the Quasi-Raccoon Cuties here to show you how the Winter Weight Watcher's Party is winding down," said Date.

    "Yeah, it's been fun, but everything has to come to an end as spring begins," said Naya, who was wearing a green, gold, and purple bikini and many bead necklaces of the same colors.

    "Naya, what are you wearing?" asked Date.

    "It's Mardi Gras, so this is a perfect to finish up the last of the debauchery," said Naya.

    "What's Mardi Gras?" asked Date.

    "I'll explain later," said Naya, "Right now, let's look at some of the people who have been partying here."

    ==========

    Here, they cut to Tani standing in front of a bamboo grove, wearing a loosely-tied kimono. "Hey, everyone, this is Tani standing outside the Silent Grove. This is as close as I can get to it with the camera working. For reasons that aren't entirely clear, electronic devices tend to stop working within that area. Scientists believe that it may be due to a magnetically-charged deposit of iron ore in the area while occultists believe that spirit presence within the grove stifles the disruption caused by unnatural machines. In any case, the Silent Grove has become an ideal location for those who want full privacy. Whether it's to escape the modern world for some tranquility or to have uninterrupted passions, the inn within the grove and its adjacent hot spring is a perfect retreat. Of course, since cameras don't work there, pictures have to be taken the old-fashioned way."

    Tani indicated a set of paintings, done in the oriental style of a small red inn, like something that could be found in the rural areas of feudal Japan. One of the paintings showed the hot spring behind the inn, which made quite a large pool. "Besides the Secret Grotto, this is certainly going to be a favorite spot for couples, particularly newlyweds," said Tani, "But why don't we go to where some of the action is? Naya?"

    ==========

    "Thanks, Tani. The beach has been a popular place for most of the Winter Weight Watcher's Party. Not only have most of the conversions taken place here, but plenty of guests have stayed here for more fun and enjoying their new bodies."

    Here, there are several shots of new Animalians enjoying the beach, from swimming in the surf, playing beach volleyball, or just soaking up the sun. As with many Animalians, their bodies hue closely to sexual perfection, though one may notice that they seem more muscular than average.

    "It seems to be a rough rule of thumb that the older a person is before their double-dosing, the more impressive their Animalian form will be. Of course, no one was elderly at this event. But another rule of thumb is that fat serves as fuel for muscles and bigger bodies. And that's certainly the case for our next interviewee. Over to you, Date."

    ==========

    The view switched over to Date, who was sitting in a lifeguard's chair. This was somewhat necessary to keep hir in shot as shi was currently talking with Koji Tomoshita, the herm Mamenchisaurus. Koji was wearing a pair of trunks and a swimsuit top that was close to a sports bra in design. Though hir top did nothing to hide the size of hir titanic breasts.

    "So, Koji, may I assume that is not your original name?" asked Date.

    "No, but I was trans before my conversion," said Koji, "Of course, I couldn't openly identify myself as such with my parents. They wouldn't understand. And the frustration from that led to...bad eating habits."

    "That resulted in you being just a little under 600 pounds?" asked Date, "It's rather incredible that you were able to stay alive for so long."

    "I have a surprisingly good heart," said Koji, "But I probably would have had a shorter life if I hadn't converted."

    "So you've gone from the world's fattest woman to the world's largest herm. How do you feel about that?" asked Date.

    "I'm not sure if I was the fattest woman," said Koji, "But I was more concerned about becoming male, or at least half-male. I chose Mamenchisaurus because I thought that it would be able to process most of my mass, which I think it did."

    "So, now that you've achieved the transition you wanted," said Date.

    "More or less," said Koji.

    "What do you intend to do with your life?" asked Date.

    "Well, I can still continue my job. I already worked from home considering my previous condition. Now I just need a larger keyboard. Ishinowa isn't a bad place to live if you're not a city lover. Also, I want to say that Animalia doesn't have the perfect answer for trans men the way it has for trans women. But I can say that it's better than nothing. I feel more like the real me now than I ever have before. There's a lot to adjust for this, but now I feel right."

    "I also share your sentiments regarding herm conversion," said Date, "I might not have the same reasons as you, but being herm feels right to me."

    "I just hope that other trans men will hear this and consider herm conversion," said Koji.

    "Of course, it all comes down to if they truly want it," said Date, "Anyways, thanks for being here."

    "I'm glad to be here," said Koji.

    "So, the Winter Weight Watcher's Party may be over, but it's only a matter of time before spring break comes to Ao Guang Island," said Date, "We'll be sure to tell you about it when it comes."
