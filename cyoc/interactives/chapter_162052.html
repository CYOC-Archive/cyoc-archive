    "What's going-*blaargh!*" Anthony puked green slime before he could end his sentence.

    "Anthony! I can expl-*blaargh!*" David started to puke as well followed by Leon and Ryan. All of them were uncontrollably puking green slime until they realized that their mouths were full of slime and they can't properly talk anymore. Then, they started to feel uncomfortable with their remaining clothes, their shirts, and one by one started to take them off just to realize in horror that their last part of humanity, their torsos, were covered in a greenish slimy substance.

    They tried to scream but their mouth could only produce weird gurgling groans. They were freaking out and slithered their fat slug through the room uncontrollably. They didn't know it but their mind were shrinking and devolving quickly into a primitive state until they can't recognized each other nor themselves. Their brains can only understand the most basic needs like feeding and mating. That last one has an interesting variation, because they had a strong need to turn every other living form into slugs like them.

    After trying to eat several pieces of furniture, the slug men slithered out of Ryan's dorm. They started to invade other dorms turning any other living creature inside there into slug people. Mostly college guys, but several girls and even some pets ended becoming slug monsters that immediately started to behave like Ryan and the others. In a few hours half of the campus had been transformed.

    Meanwhile, on a house near the campus, Hunter was giving an extraordinary party to all his friends. A big pool, loud music and tons of beer cans were delighting everybody in there.

    "Hey Hunter! Give another beer!" Hunter's best friend, Brad, asked him. Hunter took a can from the fridge and threw it to his friend who was flirting in the pool. He smiled and kept drinking his own beer. While he walked through the kitchen another guy who was looking at his phone told him. "Hey Hunter, I got a weird message from my brother. He says that there's an outbreak in the campus."

    "Outbreak?" Hunter repeated. "What does it mean?"

    "I'm not sure. I asked him but he hadn't answered me in while despite it says he's writing. Could it be that coronavirus thing that everybody is talking about?"

    "Maybe, I'm going to call Anthony." Hunter said. "He and David are still in the campus. Maybe he knows what's happening."

    Anthony's cellphone started to ring and the near slug people got attracted by its sound. They circled around it and tried to hold it in their slimy hands but it was like holding a slippery bar of soap.

    "He doesn't answer me." Hunter said. "I'm going to call David." He called but he got no answer as well. "What's going on?"

    "I've tried to call my brother too, but he didn't answer me. It's like everybody is missing."

    "This is super weird." Hunter said. "Hey! Brad!! We're going to the campus!!"

    "Why?!!" Brad didn't sound pleased with that idea.

    "Something is happening in there."

    "So? I'm hooking up with these babes!" Brad shouted before he started kissing the girl next to him.

    "I'm going with you." The guy told Hunter.

    "No, I'm going alone." Hunter replaid. "Stay here and wait for my call. If something bad is happening I will tell you, but. If you don't get my call... Please, call the police."
