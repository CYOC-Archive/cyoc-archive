    "David!" Bryan yelled. "Don't freak out..."

    Stupid comment, Bryan could not help but think. For the past hour all he had been able to
    do was fight the urge to freak out.

    But, for some odd reason, his friend, now looking huge like some storybook giant, didn't
    freak out.

    Instead, David stared down at his friend's tiny, naked body in the passenger seat of the
    car. "Bryan! My God! Is that you?"

    "Yeah," Bryan said. "Just stay calm, David."

    "I am calm," David said. He could not believe his eyes...or his luck.

    His friend looked like one of his kid brother's action figures. "You must only be a foot tall!"

    "I don't know how it happened," Bryan said. "It can't be possible, but..."

    David controlled his urge to reach out and touch his small friend.

    "What should we do?" David asked.

    "I don't know," Bryan said, shaking his head in confusion. "You've got to help me."

    "Sure," David said. "You know I will. Do you want me to take you home?"

    Bryan thought of being seen at his ridiculous size by his father and brothers. "No, I don't
    want anyone else to see me like this."

    David hid his joy at Bryan's answer. "We can go back to my home. You can have some
    privacy until we figure this out."

    "Thanks," Bryan said.

    "Are you cold?"

    Bryan nodded. "Yeah, it is chilly."

    David didn't understand his feelings. He found himself fascinated at looking at his friends
    tiny and naked body but he didn't understand why. He wasn't gay. But he couldn't stop
    staring.

    "I don't have anything you could wrap yourself in. The only thing I can think of is putting
    you in my pocket."

    Bryan was unaware of what that meant when he said, "Okay, I guess I would be as snug as

    David reached out and picked Bryan up and Bryan became aware of the skin to skin contact
    all over his body but he was too embarrassed to say anything. Then he found himself

    "I guess I should take you to my house for now." David said, starting the car.

    Bryan then began to realize what being in David's pocket meant. He could feel the
    vibrations as the car moved. He could feel the warmth of David's leg through the pocket
    material. He couldn't be sure but at one point he felt something else and he realized just
    how close he was to David's equipment at that moment. Embarrassment made Bryan want
    to pretend it all away.

    David was also pretending some thoughts away. He realized that he had his best friend
    naked, in his pocket, near his manhood and he didn't want to think about it. So as he
    drove, he forgot about Bryan. He got home and started the shower. He stripped off his
    pants and hopped in without a single thought about Bryan.

    crawling out of the pocket to see what was happening and looked up to see his friend
    naked and stepping into the shower. David looked enormous and Bryan was frozen in
    shock.

    Bryan didn't understand his feelings. He wasn't gay either. But the sight of his giant and
    naked friend was fascinating and he couldn't stop staring.
