    New Surfer

    Shane had just transferred to a new high school. He was a senior and just about to graduate so he was worried if he would be able to make new friends this late in his high school life. He wasn't very popular at his old school but he had a nice, close group of friends. As he walked up to the school for his first day he saw lots of jocks and surfers hanging out and having fun, but no one like him. He also so a group of skaters wearing Elements shirts, all around 16. They seemed to be welcoming someone... Shane wished he had a group. After going all the day without anyone talking to him Shane decided to try to fit in. The surfers seemed nice, better than being a dumb jock or skater. He got a button up shirt with a floral pattern, some boarding shorts and some sandals and decided to dress like this from now on and see what happens.

    The very next day the surfers came up to him and started talking to him! He felt pretty weird wearing these clothes still but he fit in almost too perfectly. He found himself talking with them easily like he had always been one of them. They even talked about surfing stuff like tides and stuff that he didn't even think he knew. This went on for a few days and Shane was happy but Friday when he got home he felt really weird. His skin looked really tan like he had spent his life outside and his complextion had cleared up, he had a perfect smooth face like a model. He also felt really uncomfortable in all this clothing. He pulled open his shirt and immediately felt better. His chest grew bigger so that his pecs would show through his shirt and he had a great 4 pack. His arms and legs also got nice and toned.

    "Woah what's happening?" He cried out in a cool surfer voice. But he started to love it. He got dumb and blond like the other surfers and his hair felt down past his shoulders. He looked like a real big surfer dude. He also regressed back down to 16. These next few years would be better than the first time!

    "Yeah dude time to surf! I love this!" Shane went back out to reintroduce himself to his surfer friends. He finally fit in. But now who tries to fit in next?
