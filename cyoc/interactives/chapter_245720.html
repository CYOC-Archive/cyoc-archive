    Becoming An Apple

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover Yesterday BM I O Magic

    The person that had stepped out was Magalia, a fat girl that Chad loved to bully and bad-mouth. Him being handsome and popular, all followed his example and he simply adored the power it brought him. Considering he was the football star at school, no one told him off or blamed him. The coach adored his skills with a football so what other teachers said - or students for that matter - had no importance. But for Chad it was normal. He was viewed as handsome and good at sports. He had much going for him so he was cocky. Furthermore, he WAS good and popular so he thought that over time he DID deserve his privileges, not realizing how much was just owed to chance and how not everyone could be like him for many, many reasons. Besides, he never thought that he should be kinder to people in general. But now, things were about to change.
    "Ha! It's you, ugly?" Chad sneered as he saw the girl he loved to despise. "Is this a joke? Are you really THAT pathetic?"
    "I got the apple from a magic store in a rundown town. It's a wishing apple."
    "You truly believe in this shit? Of course you do, since you're such a loser."
    "And you still wished. You wished for more wishes, right?"
    Chad did not say anything, because Magalia was correct.
    "You thought yourself sp smart. But it's so common. But as you did wish for more wishes, the twist comes in the wording. You'll soon become an apple. A wishing apple. But not just any wishing apple. You'll be a baby wishing apple. Anyone biting into you will get you as a baby, but as the perfect baby the woman wished. Woman, or animal. Because yes, you might be reborn as an animal. But then, yoiu will bring more joy to your new parents. You might be a much better person for once."
    Chad was about to scoff again when suddenly he felt himself shrink. His arms and legs and head retreated into his body. At once, he was on the ground, unable to move. Although with no eyes, he could see. And with no ears, he could hear still too. But he could not move. Magalia towered over him. But he could not speak. He couldn't believe it. An apple! He was an apple! And he would be eaten, only to become a baby in a womb! With a life decided by someone else! Ugh! This was horrible! Magalia picked him up, smirking wickedly. Chad was terrified. Who would she give him to? A woman? An animal?
