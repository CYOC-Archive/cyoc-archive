    When the Lycothans first invaded, some humans chose to side with them in their attack on Earth. Most of these humans were rewarded with being transformed early into the Lycothans rule of Earth. Many of them became Alphas, but a few were given a chance to remain human for awhile while they were preparing to join the others.

    Jennifer Cale was one of these privileged humans who was given access to the home of the Lycothan Alpha in charge of the City of Chicago. An elaborate mansion that was built to cater to the Lycothans living there. Jennifer was a beautiful human woman with long brown hair, she didn't bother to wear clothing, after all most of the Lycothans didn't, showing off her medium sized breasts and neatly shaved pussy. While this wasn't required among the privileged humans, Jennifer enjoyed doing it because it showed the Lycothans that she was more than willing to live like they did even as a Human.

    "Your dinner, mistress Jennifer," a female Lycothan of the Servant Caste says, bowing to the brunette human who was seated at a table before setting down her food.

    "Thank you, Chaira," Jennifer says, giving the Servant a smile. She had become close to Chaira, and if she was to become an Alpha, then she had already decided to make Chaira her personal servant, though she still had yet to be inducted into any caste. "Is the Alpha going to join us today?"

    "The Alpha is running late," Chaira says regretfully. "They were in a meeting that ran late with the High Alpha."

    "Of course," Jennifer says, smiling a little. The Alpha had assured her that today would be the day she would be transformed, and she was looking forward to it. She hated being human, she would've rather been transformed right away like her parents and Sister, but she could wait if she had to.

    As she started eating, she hears the door open. She turns to see who it is.
