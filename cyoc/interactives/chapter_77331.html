    Through your fog, you fought to think of a way out. You had to become part of the dragon and he was letting you choose. You thought
    of becoming his powerful arms, his flowing tail, or his massive hemipenes, but you knew that wouldn't help you at all. Then, you got an
    idea. If the magic forced you to become part of him, and whatever part you chose he had to accept...

    "I want to be your brain!"

    The dragon sits up quickly, the smug look dropping from his face. He realizes that you have bested him, and he's not happy. He gets up
    and pounces on you, forcing you into the ground and glaring into your eyes.

    "Insolent mortal!" he roars, his golden eyes flashing with rage. "Do you know what you have done?! You have condemned me to be a
    prisoner of my own body and you have condemned yourself to hold power you cannot hope to control! You will kill us both!"

    "I guess you should have let me be a full dragon so I would only kill one of us!" you yell back.

    "I won't let this happen! I will eat you first!"

    The dragon lunges his head forward, jaw open wide, dagger-like teeth closing in. Suddenly, he stops. As soon as his tooth touched your
    neck, the two of you began glowing with a golden light. The dragon appears in a trance as he instead lifts you up and places you on his
    head and snout. Against your own will, you thrust your pelvis forward, as if you were humping his head. Your penis, forcibly erect,
    plunges into the dragon's skull.

    You suddenly feel as though you are both yourself and the dragon at once. You hear him raging mentally, but know that no sound is
    escaping his mouth. You feel your human body attach itself to the dragon's head and slowly disappear into it's skull. You feel a surge of
    unimaginable power as you become one with the dragon. The strength of the energy coursing through his, now your, body is almost too
    much. You fight to stay conscious, but after what seems like an eternity, it becomes too much and you black out.

    • • • • • • • • • • • • • • • • • • • •

    Much later, you awake in a fog. You remember having a dream about finding a dragon, being forced to merge with it, and outsmarting it
    by becoming its brain. You laugh mentally at the strange creations of your mind and start to get up. But when you realize that there are
    no covers, you aren't in your bed, and the hands rubbing your eyes are scaly and clawed, you are jerked from your sleepiness and enter
    panic mode. You can't stop yourself from letting out a scream, which turns into a deafening roar from your dragon vocal chords.

    "How did this happen?" you ask aloud and find that you have the dragon's deep, powerful voice.

    «You took my body, remember?» That voice in your head, you remember it. «That's right, human, I'm still in here. I may not have
    control, but I still have a voice. It would appear that by merging with my brain, you actually saved yourself. Our combined mental
    strength can control our immense power. I was wrong, and you lucked out.»

    "Look, I'm sorry for stealing your body," you apologize, "but you did lead me on and try to trick me."

    «It is a dragon's nature to mistrust humans. I was only trying to protect myself and my hoard by making you harmless. Now you have
    my body, power, and treasure. I guess my plan backfired.»

    "Let's make a truce," you suggest. "I'll do whatever you say so you can try to live out your life normally if you help me find a way to get
    us both back to our old bodies."

    «You mean you'll give me control and look for a way to give me back my body? I don't see how that would benefit you, but who am I to
    argue? I accept your terms.»

    "Okay, so what do dragons normally do?"

    «Well, after that roar you gave out, my mate will probably be flying in any minute now to see what's wrong.»
