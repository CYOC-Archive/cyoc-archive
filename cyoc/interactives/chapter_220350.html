    TRANSFORMATION FAIR:
    A place where you can be anything you wanna be! Even in your Wildest, and Weirdest Dreams!


    Dalton should have known that, if there is one thing that’s wild, and weird. Is the fact that… For a skinny person at least… Would fantasize about being Obese temporarily.

    Even still, what made you wonder is what Fat Larry said about your newfound weight gain:

    ‘If it were me, though, I'd stick with it for a little while. You may find that having a little extra ain't so bad.’

    “Ain’t so bad?” You whisper. Is there really a benefit of being this massive? So far… you’ve been getting exhausted, and weary… worrying you would tip over if your not careful! And let’s not forget… You were panting, and sweating, trying to undo this weight gain…

    Granted… Maybe that Larry guy might have a point… As far as you can see he’s not wearing any suit that would make him skinny. Perhaps he’s always like this, or he has that weight gain moment in his life, and decides to embrace it…

    Dalton not wanting to just stand there, and do nothing decides to move on to others attractions…

    “Well, Let’s see what else this Transformation Fair has in store?…” you say, realizing just now your voice is a lot fuller, and more richer…

    And so you walk-… Or more accurately waddle, to see what else a fat person can do…


    \\\\\30 Minutes Later/////


    As Dalton kept looking around not knowing where to go next. You sat a near by bench… Eating a funnel cake, you asked to make sure there’s nothing that would change you… And they say that ‘it’s just normal food’… You took their word for it… And it IS just food.

    Dalton just sat there thinking about what he saw earlier…

    ————————————————————————

    While you were waddling… Trying to get used to this body… Initially you were distraught, ‘Why would anybody enjoy this?’

    Then you see a group of skinny people shaking, and shivering… ‘Oh yeah it’s Fall, It would be cold at this time!’ That’s when it occurred to you!

    ‘Wait a minute! Shouldn’t I be cold as well?!… So how am I…’ Your wearing nothing but a skin tight suit, with no insulation… then that means….

    ‘MY FAT IS KEEPING ME FROM FREEZING!’

    Dalton just discovered the benefits of being fat! Maybe that’s what that guy from the truck… Larry?… Meant by it!

    You hugged yourself, never even considering that there’s even something TO LIKE about being fat!… although…

    You remind yourself that this is only temporary.

    Once you leave you’ll be your skinny self again… ‘Ah well, “life is short”, as they say!’ Thinking to yourself.

    ‘Maybe I won’t have this chance to be fat, or anything while I’m alive! Something’s can’t last… I better make the most of it…’

    And as you traveled you still hadn’t decided on where you wanted to go… there are some areas that would suit you fancy considering you are a fat person now… Like…

    The Sumo Wrestling attraction:
    The area is covered with what you could guess is Japanese architecture, and there’s even a thick rope tied to the top… Dose this have anything to do with Sumo? Anyway here’s their description:
    A place where any man can simply come forward and compete in a dohyo (sumo wrestling ring) the only condition:
    Only Males, you have to be at the appropriate weight to participate, and lastly you’ll be suited with both a Mawashi, and a Race change to make you fit in… besides all that, enjoy both the atmosphere, and the show!

    You only saw one of those that would work with your weight, though you don’t know what they mean by ‘suited with a race change’ but that’s something to consider… if only there are others that’s fat friendly… Maybe you should talked to Larry about this, and see if there are others like this…
    Or maybe make this fat thing permanent…
    You shake your head… ‘I’m getting ahead of myself! I’m not ready to go just yet!’

    And then there are some odd ones like…

    Unleash your Fursona!:
    Where you can be any animal! From Furries, to Scalies… From Anthro, to Feral, you can be anything! Your Fursona doesn’t have to have the same body type as you do… You can alter yourself to fit your ideal body! So what are you waiting for? Go try everything!
    Warning: This attraction dose not allow you to change species base on Copyright Characters… So be Creative, Imaginative, and Original!

    You don’t know what to say about that? If you can’t change into made up species like… A Pokémon, or a Xenomorph, then that would explain the lack of a big line…

    And as far as you could tell… you don’t know of anyone who is even a Furry…

    And then there’s a wild card…

    The Cavemen Walk Around:
    Embark on a journey to the past… before the age of Technology, before the age of Knowledge… walkthrough this self contained walk around where you not only get to see cave paintings, but also get to experience the mindset of a Devolved Caveman… Enjoy this temporary brain drain of a lifetime…
    Disclaimer: Individuals who part take this attraction, must go though it all to the end! To be able to have their intelligence back! Otherwise these changes could lead to brain damage.
    SO ENTER WITH CAUTION!

    Well that’s a new one, an area where you lose your intelligence, this may not be a good idea, unless you have someone with you…
    A Designated Person, whose willing to help you if you become a vegetable, “And I’m not talking about Food!” You say to no one in particular…
    Unless you want to take the risk… You better stay away from it… For now…

    ————————————————————————

    And so you finish your funnel cake, not only liking taste of it, But also noticing your sense of enjoying… Well… Just eating without even caring about your weight… You’re already fat anyway so why worry?

    And so you got up…

    Dalton dose his own set of stretches, to get himself moving again… “This body is just something I have to get used to…” you say to yourself…

    You also hope that… Perhaps at some point, to find someone who is as fat as you, and also who is not too bothered about their weight…
    Maybe a new fat friend…

    Anyhow… Dalton is contemplating on where to go… Out of all the ideas of where, or what to do…

    What dose he decide to do?
