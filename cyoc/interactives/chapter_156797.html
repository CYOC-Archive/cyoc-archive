    “I do kinda wish we weren’t over concrete. Plenty of softer places.”

    “You can, Suga’.” Evan smiled back, slowly taking a step. Once his side seemed set, his body mate followed suit. Joy smiled, blushing a little as they finally figured out how to work together walking.

    “So.... Your place or mine?” Evan thought for a moment.

    “Depends how far we have to walk.” Joy chuckled a little.

    “Yeah. Ain’t too far though.” Evan began to think, realizing one downside of being conjoined. You had to agree and decide on absolutely everything.
