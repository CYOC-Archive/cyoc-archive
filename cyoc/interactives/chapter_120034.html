    Finished with his morning stretches, he walked onto the natural trail that lead to a nearby river. He would always go out and take a fresh drink from the rushing waters before starting his day. He had missed his alarm clock yesterday, though and had to skip it to make up for time. But now, he could enjoy it once again, bringing a large handful of clear water to his mouth, unaware of the strange contents that had been thoroughly mixed into the water since yesterday.
