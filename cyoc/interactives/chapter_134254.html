He was somewhat top heavy. His stomach got a little fatter and he gained some abs. His feet and hands grew in size. These additional changes were now complete. Jeff wondered why the program said earlier that his changes were done, then he looked at the computer and it said that there were a few software updates for the splicing program. Jeff had put on his shirt, underwear, and pants, earlier, which ripped and tore because his body grew. Then they repaired themselves and changed to accommodate his hugeness along with all his other clothes, though he left his shirt unbuttoned to show off his handsome abs and cute fat belly. He was still half human, half gorilla. He still had socks and shoes, but they wouldn't fit and he didn't want to wear them anyway, since he wouldn't be able to grab things with his feet. He now weighed about 300 pounds, as opposed to his 170 pound full human self. Jeff walked down to the living room and saw his gorilla dad there. They had breakfast which was pancakes with bananas and blueberries. Jeff ate about 20 pancakes, while his dad, named Franklin, ate about 60. They devoured the pancakes and several gallons of orange juice messily and so their fur got stained. Franklin said, "This is no big deal. We're apes, we can groom each other." So they took turns licking each other's fur. After a while they were done.

He had an even better sense of smell and stronger bite than Jeff. His body was about 10 times thicker than a human's and four times as wide. He still retained his human hairstyle with brown hair. He could lift about 20 tons of something over his head, while Jeff could lift about 15 tons. When Jeff first made the changes, Franklin's clothes tore to shreds before suddenly they fixed themselves and with the rest of his wardrobe changed to fit him. He normally stood on two legs but a little hunched over. If either were to run, they could do so on all fours. Both father and son were wearing shorts, button down shirts except unbuttoned to show off their abs and stomachs and also they'd be tight otherwise, and no socks or shoes because it would feel wrong.
