This all served as a reminder that this wasn't likely the result of a couple of lovers with odd tastes wanting to create a new era of peace. Whoever was behind the Virus here, had not only either spied on Maurice and Snowy while Chakat Industries still existed or had gotten the formula from the US government when the government confiscated Chakat Industries after the Fee City outbreak, but he/she or more likely shi had some plan that was most certainly NOT peaceful.

On a gold plated emblem near the gate was the letters BdM with two fleurs de lis on it. The BdM stood for Bourbon de Moltitier and represented a small portion of the old Bourbon royal line that had survived the various revolutions and foreign invasions of France. The fleurs de lis was the traditional emblem of the French monarchy, officially dropped for good by the French government when the Third Republic won the support of the French people, but that didn't mean that the old noble families didn't drop their personal coats of arms. Jess knew that and that was sort of where hir father, then an American civil servant in the US Embassy in Paris had come into play. His assignment to Paris had come after Jess and Sally were able to live on their own, but they had stayed in relatively close touch. It was through letters and phone calls that their father had informed them of his meeting a French noblewoman, apparently Alexis's mother, and when he retired from the US State Department, that noblewoman had hired him as a personal servant. From the way Jess and heard it, it reminded her of the stories of Queen Victoria and her manservant John Brown after Prince Albert's death with the rumors of a secret romantic affair, but Jess never got enough to know for sure.

    " Bonjour. C'est Alexis Bourbon de Moltitier avec vos amis. Je suis ici pour voir ma mère. N'ayez pas peur, car je suis maintenant un peu ... différente ... mais je ne vais pas vous blesser ou infecter n'importe qui. Pas plus que mes amis," Alexis then spoke into the intercom.

    Alexis sighed, "J'ai été infecté par le virus dans la partie nord de Paris. Il a fait de moi un ... dans un Skunkat. Un de mes amis est un Chakat et l'autre est un Foxkat."

    Jess wondered if that was a good idea. Given some to the fears and responses to the Virus, especially if the family had seen the news, they might not even be allowed in.

    "Nous ne serons pas infecter n'importe qui et n'ont aucun désir de. Nous avons même été vaccinés pour s'assurer de cela," Alexis continued.

    There was a silence after a moment, but then the gate clicked and began to open.

    "Vous pouvez entrer. Vos amis parlent français?" the voice asked back.

    "My French is poor, but if Justin Cain is there, he can act as an interpreter," Jess told Alexis, "tell them that Jessica Cain is here and requests her father be present for the meeting."

It gave Jess some relief that the infected Kats had not come this way, yet, but it also created some nervousness. They were greeted at the door by a well dressed servant. He looked Alexis and gave a short sigh and then to Flake and Jess gave a short snort and turned.

    "Votre mère est en attente dans la salle à manger principale avec son valet de chambre. Vous pouvez vous-mêmes et votre visite à les expliquer ... après avoir correctement vous habillé," he spoke as he began to lead them into the chateau.

    Alexis then turned to Jess, "He wants us to get dressed first."

    "Most cloth irritates our fur," Jess answered, "And none has been made for our taur bodies. If we have to wear anything... it must fit loosely."

    Jess had actually become quite comfortable with being naked, as all the Chakats and Foxkats commonly were, with Juan and any Chakat that was based off of a hairless domestic breed of cat as an obvious exception as they didn't have the fur that would rub and be irritated by cloth. About ten minutes later, dressed in what amounted to a very fancy version of the "little black dress" the three then followed the servant back down the stairs. It still itched their fur tremendously, but was loose enough that it wasn't too bad. The greatest problem was in the length. The dresses had been cut to hang practically down to the floor, which their taur-like bodies couldn't allow for. The back of the dress hit their first waist where their torsos med the bodies and essentially held the dresses there. The front of the dresses hung down a little and nearly did make it to the floor, and forced the three of them to be very careful as they walked.

    They were lead to a long room with a long table in it with several chairs and several exquisite glass chandeliers hanging from the ceiling. At the far end of the table sat an older but middle aged woman who was dressed rather well, though it looked more like an expensive woman's business suit than the sort of ball gown expected of royalty, but then, France WAS a republic. Perhaps the woman had decided to run a business since political rule was not an option for her. She sat quietly with a well dressed manservant standing beside her. Her face did not appear judgmental or even disapproving, though there was a bit of a detachment, like she was reserving her judgement for the moment.

    Alexis walked up to the woman and got down onto her forelegs' "elbows" and then bowed her torso so that she could nuzzle her black nose into the back of the woman's hand.

    "Bonjour, ma mère. Je m'excuse d'être si différent," Alexis said slowly.

    With that Alexis backed away. The woman slowly nodded and said, "Votre nez est froid et humide, Alexis.Comment est-ce arrivé à vous?"

    Jess and Flake stood quietly as they listened to Alexis essentially tell her mother that it was a long story and would be best told after the introductions. Jess was particularly focused on the manservant, and he was indeed Justin Cain. He looked between Jess and Flake fairly intently and Jess wondered her father recognized her. The outbreak had been so fast and sudden that she had not told him about what had happened to her and Sally or that they had both taken mates, Sally with Charlie, her long time boyfriend, and Jess, herself, with Nikole, Charlie's mother, who she had fallen in love with during the outbreak in Fee City.     "My name is Kelsey, but please, call me Flake," Flake commented.

    Alexis's mother said something that Flake believed sounded like she looked dainty like a snowflake, which actually made her smile.

    "A lot has happened to us, Daddy," Jess told him, feeling somewhat like a little girl again, "Sally's still in Paris as we only just got here a few hours ago to help fight the spread of the Virus... and what has happened is a long story..."
