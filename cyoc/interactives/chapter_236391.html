    Breaking Through

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 17 hours ago AR O Mental Toddler Baby

    Leah seethed as she sat in the playpen. She was furious. It was one thing for her to have fallen to this state on her own. It was an entirely different thing for it to be forced upon her. After just one night forced to be a toddler or baby or whatever age she was supposed to be, she was pissed.

    Speaking of pissed, Leah felt warmth spread through her diaper. Rita, busy in the kitchen making breakfast, didn't notice the hiss. But she did notice the fart that followed.

    Leah clenched her hands into fists. She was determined to fight every single baby behavior that she had been hypnotized with. So far, the only luck she'd had was at the beginning, with calling Tina by her proper name instead of mommy. But she felt certain that she could overcome more.

    So Leah also clenched her abdominal muscles, desperately attempting to stop the poop that was soon to come. After her very poopy night, she'd settle for just slowing it down. But it was useless. Her tummy rumbled, and immediately, a log began to enter her diaper.

    "Let me know when you're done, Leah," Rita called. "I'll change you and we can have breakfast!"

    Leah felt a bit guilty for how she was treating Rita. She was taking her frustration out on her aunt, becoming a nightmare child in her attempts to overcome the hypnosis. Rita was innocent in all this, after all.

    Some part of Leah was scared that Tina would punish her with more hypnosis after finding out how much she misbehaved. But if Leah could overcome the hypnosis, then that wouldn't matter.

    Leah let out a loud fart as her bowels finished emptying. She waited for another round of pee, then when that was over, stood. Her diaper sagged under her.

    "Done!" she called around her pacifier. Rita set down what she was doing and rushed over. Leah laid back and just closed her eyes. Rita sighed in relief.

    "There, that's the angel your mom said you were!" Leah felt guilt again. She hadn't been making diaper changes easy. But maybe she should start doing so.

    Once in a fresh diaper, Leah was brought over to the kitchen and placed into a high chair. She clumsily grabbed at her breakfast: plain Cheerios. As she ate, a realization slowly dawned over her. She had broken through the hypnosis, though only partially. One of the things that Tina had taken away was back!
