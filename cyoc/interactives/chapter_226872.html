    A Feminine Costume

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 23 days ago O Magic

    You look around the aisles for a while. They seem to go on and on. How could so many fit into such a seemingly much smaller store? Such as thing seemed impossible. You decide to go through an aisle at random, because you have to start somewhere. You quickly come to the realisation that it's the feminine section. And it's just as large as the others. Out of curiosity, you look through what's on display. Indeed, you can get ideas and then go to the young people's section later, for a more male-oriented costume. And you can only marvel at the sort of costumes women can wear here. Exotic costumes like that of a geisha or a beautiful mMaya priestess. Also Arabic veils worthy of being worn in an actual harem. In fact, there were few costumes that you'd assume would be present in this type of store. No sexy nurses or sexy cops, or of weird domination kinks. These disguises were refined and of quality for sure. And they all held a lot of appeal. There were as well animal costumes, along with some based upon myths. It was all rather extraordinary, really. The costumes of witches or fairies looked not all cheap or tacky. None of them did. Even the plastic parts appeared well-crafted. The animals were cats, feline, birds and countless others. But the most alluring had to be the mythological costumes. They seemed to have everything! Japanese or Chinese elements of folklore, alongside sphinxes, mermaids, werewolves, nymphs, female fauns, dragons and an entire plethora of other creatures. Assaulted by their beauty, mesmerised by it, you decide to pick one. Too bad if you are a male. They all look so beautiful and you might gain in popularity by making the other guys laugh.
