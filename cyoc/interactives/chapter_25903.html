    As of this date, we are proud to announce that we no longer have to maintain the beta program that was being tested on this website.  We are also now not bound by the restrictive energies that forced us to use temporary transformative processes.

    We realize that this may seem a tad bit odd to you, sitting in front of your screen, relaxing, while staring at this message.  It is understandable that you may not think of this site as more than a collection of fantasy musings.  The truth is that this site does indeed hold a large repository of narratives that have one thing in common - they are all designed to try and hold attention for as long as possible.

    We admit, sometimes our site fails.  There are those with strong wills that come and quickly scan through our archives, not slowing to try and appreciate anything along the way.  These visitors are harmless enough, but do not add to the forwarding of our cause.

    Choose Your Own Change is not the only site where we have made progress.  There are several other areas that have connectivity within our interests.  You may not have even realized the process that initially led you to this site.  Many of you came by way of a "link", be this from a favorites list, or just a plain web search.  Some of you were "referred" to us.  We try to do our best to keep this association, with you, at a pleasing level.  It is not favorable for us to lose your interest.

    At our early stages, some users found ways to break this connection.  It is unfortunate that this occurred.  Much time and energy was wasting correcting those errors and severing the distribution of, shall we say, user related, disturbing information.  We are very happy to announce that the newer version of our operating equipment has not had a failure in over a year.

    This brings us back to the real reason that you first came to seek us.  You may have been looking for any number of websites on the day that you found our locale, but something drew you to this site.  Like our sister sites, we are geared to satisfy certain inner needs expressed by our customers.  You may not have realized it, but it was your inner desires that led you here, and not a simple "link", or even the false memory of a referral from a friend.

    One achievement of which we are most proud is our newer ability to reduce haziness experienced by our users.  It is now almost impossible to differentiate our reality, from true reality.  This has led to more thorough completions, and thereby allowed us a way to prevent partial manipulations - most of them being unfortunately regrettable.

    Even now, you remain in front of the screen feeling your clothing as you would normally.  The smells around you are familiar.  Your vision is affixed to the screen in the same manner as you are accustomed.  Auditory stimuli have remained unchanged.  Even your size feels correct.  Believe us, it took a long time to fine tune the applications of software with occult energies to produce this "real" feeling.

    The truth is that you have never been to this site before.  You just feel as if you have.  It is likely that you have been to several other sites that deal with transformation.  Maybe there is a bit of a familiarity that you feel while relating this site to others, but we assure you that you have never experienced our system previously.

    Another truth is that we do our best work slowly.. well, slowly by certain standards.  Many changes are made rather quickly, and while results may end up being desirable, we have a certain clientele that pays us to take part in our process.  A significant portion of our work involves visualization of our users.  Even now you are being watched.  Not by cameras or anything as crude as simple technology.  Our methods involve well developed enchantments that allow our clients too not only see you, but feel as if they were actually there in the room with you.

    This leaves us with you.  We understand that you are likely still confused.  The writings above serve two purposes.  The first is to allow you to better understand our methods.  The second is to maintain your attention while our spells work within you.  It is not expected that you would feel any different at this moment.  However, let me assure you that several very large changes have already occurred.   The reason that you do not feel any of these is the "new reality" that we have developed for you.

    When you initially came to our site, we evaluated you on several levels.  Your transformative desires, while hidden to many others in your life, were the easiest for us to review.  The more difficult portion of our task was to determine the other subtle aspects of your existence.  We admit that we are not perfect, in fact, as you watch the screen before you, you can see a language that you recognize as English.   It may sound funny, but the words that you see are not the words that you spent your early life learning.  These words are of an old dialect that allows our energies to be focused directly upon you.  The enchantment has made you believe that these "letters" are your standard.  It may also interest you to learn that you normally do not read left to right.  It feels completely normal though, doesn't it?

    Why capture your attention for so long?  Because you are our business.  We sell you... or perhaps it is better put as - we sell your changes, and the end result.  To whom, you may ask?

    Well... there are several people with substantial resources who pay great amounts to see your inner desires come to life.  Some of these people do it for revenge, while others do it to put a competitor out of business.  Still others simply love the process itself.  You may or may not know your "benefactor", but be it revenge upon you, or suppression of your abilities in the marketplace, you are now well beyond doing anything about it.
