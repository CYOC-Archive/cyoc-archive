    After changing into gym clothes and and putting shoes back on, you headed back out to the gym to wait for further instructions. After fifteen minutes, the gym teacher came out carrying a blue bag hung over his left shoulders. Then as he got near the class, he called everyone into a group including you. So you walk over along with everyone else to find out that you and the rest of the class are going out to the back field to play soccer. But before the class leaves the gym, the teacher added that everyone must partner. So you find your friend Wolfey and the two of you become partners. After everyone partner up, the teacher and your class headed out to the back field for soccer. By the time your class reach the back door, the teacher told everyone to stop and wait a few minutes as he only went out to the field. At the edge of the field where everyone from the school enters, the teacher pulled out a ray gun and blasted a yellow fumed layer over the grass. The fumed cause the grass to be a sneaker transform type of grass. Which means when the PE class steps on to the grass, their sneakers will turn into soccer cleats. After the fumed settle on the grass and placed the blue bag down, he came back to get you and the class who were waiting inside by the backdoors. When he open up the doors he told you and everyone else to get with their partners and line up on the white line with their partners when you get on the soccer field. So you get with your friend who is your partner and the two of you walk out with everyone else and line up on the white line facing the back of the soccer net. As everyone line up facing the soccer net, nobody except you and Wolfey noticed that their sneakers slowly turn into soccer cleats. The upper part of the sneakers turn into the collar section while the bottom of the sneakers grew studs (part of soccer clets that help control on soccer field). But your sneakers now soccer cleats still kept the blue and orange strips colors while Wolfey's sneakers-soccer cleats color change from green and yellow to red and orange strips after the transformation. After waiting for all the sneakers turn into soccer cleats, everyone was ready to play soccer. So the gym teacher now soccer coach handed each partners team a soccer ball and had them practice. After you, Wolfey and the rest of the class finished practicing, the coach called everyone back and explained that you and the class were practicing for a game that was going to talk place soon as the other team showed up and dismissed the class back to continuing
    practice while waiting for the other team. Just then your coach gets a phone call from the opposite coach so he step away to talk to the opssing team's coach saying they are in the gym finishing with a transformation. Back in the gym, the opposite team was getting ready for the game that would be starting at 5pm. After the opposite team change, the only thing that needed to be done was the transformation the team into animals. So the coach pulled out an animal ray gun and basted a player into a tiger, another into a wolf and the rest into animals. The player's feet turn into animals feet and their sneakers transform to fit their animal's feet. After the transformations were done the team headed out to the soccer field to practice from 4:30-5pm.
