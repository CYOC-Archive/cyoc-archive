    When Jake was younger he had a bit of an obsession with dinosaurs. Even now he won't skip a dime to watch the new Jurassic Park movies. He couldn't stop thinking about the t-tex scenes in the movies, so it would only be appropriate to change into one of his big interests. Although he didn't want to go crazy just yet. Become a full sized t-rex in a two story dorm didn't seem like the best idea at the time.

    Jake thought about it and decided to give himself a t-rex snout. It would certainly be awkward to take a visit to the dentist, but if he had that strong of teeth, would there be any need to worry about it? Jake went ahead and typed in t-rex snout into the box. A reference picture showed up with a human model to show how the change would look. He saw that the snout was huge! It was bigger than his head! It must take reference from a life-sized that usually stand about 50 feet tall.

    Jake decided he would scale it down to a place he felt appropriate. He got it to a size where it was still big, but would likely be light enough to hold his own weight. Once he was happy with the reference picture shown, Jake clicked "Change" on the screen.

    Jake stood awhile looking at the screen with a progress bar. He was getting anxious. Was this thing really going to work or did he just waste his time on a silly little joke... Then he felt itching on his nose. He scratched the bridge trying to releve himself. That's when he actually saw... His nose was shrinking. He's nose was actually being pushed back into his face. "What the hell?" Was all Jake could say before he felt his nostrils reshape upward. At the same time, his jawbone started cracking. "Gah!" Caught off guard, Jake held his hands over his mouth. They weren't exactly hurting, but he felt as if his jaw was falling out. But it was actually extending. His under-bite had gained density in the back, and along with his upper jaw started pushing out. Jake's newly flattened nostrils pushed out along with it, receding from his face to his upper jaw. Last was his teeth. Jake couldn't help himself due to his shifting jaw, so he opened his mouth wide. His lips had spread further away from the center of his face when he tried opening. "Ah...AHH!" Jake fell head first into his desk as his voice nearly transition to a growl. With his jaws wide open, his teeth started reforming into sharp razor blades.

    Jake had been though quit alot of pressure (luckily not a lot of pain) throughout the experience. Jake had finally opened his eyes to the monitor. "Change complete. Profile saved." Jake lifted himself from his desk panting in exhaustion. He opened his eyes to see his vision was a bit obscured when looking forward. He could actually see his nostrils in front of him. Looking up at his monitor he saw his webcam had turned on. The program brought up a screen that showed Jake in display with a text box saying "See your new look".

    Jake studied himself in his camera. "Waoh..." Jake had saw his lips still moved like a humans. But his jaw was anything but human. He knew it was real too just from his perspective. It wasn't one of those cool live face trackers that changed the way you look. It was all real. His face had extended to a t-rex snout with human skin.

    "Now that's something new." Jake's voice was the same, but a bit gruffy, as if he snarled while talking. Even hearing his voice coming out of his monstrous maw was satisfying. Jake could help but use a toothy smile. It looked menising and frightening, which was all he was hoping for.

    After closing the webcam window Jake saw another text box. "Alter reality to accept changes?" For some reason, it didn't seem to hit Jake until that message popped up. He clicked yes. Thought it be best to stay safe and keep anybody from freaking out... For now.

    Jake proceeded into a bathroom to look at himself again to ensure himself this was real. "I'm such a beast! Like, Damn! That's fucking awesome! What else can this thing do?!"
