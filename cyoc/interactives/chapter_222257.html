    Leah's Return to Daycare

    Leah wakes up the next morning excited for her first day of daycare. She’s in the crib of the spare room slash daycare that has become her bedroom. Tina says that it’s only temporary, but that’s because Tina thinks Leah will give up on her plans. Leah feels certain that this room will become her bedroom permanently, once Tina realizes Leah wants this diaper life for good.

    Speaking of her diaper… Leah squeezes her diaper. Tina didn’t put her in any pajamas, having her sleep in only the diaper. Apparently, it will take some time to override her potty training. The diaper is completely clean. Tina thinks Leah has completely lost her potty training, so a clean diaper will not be appropriate.

    Leah is able to wet her diaper while laying down, but to mess it, she has to get into a small squat. She shivers in anticipation, excited for when she can mess herself laying down. After a few minutes of pushing and grunting, she fills her diaper up with poop. The teenager then lays back down and rolls over a few times, spreading the mess over her butt. Hopefully, the mess won’t seem as fresh by the time Tina comes to get her.

    Sure enough, when Tina walks into the room, Leah’s diaper has gone cold and clammy. Tina sighs at the state of Leah’s diaper, but changes her without any comment. She then dresses Leah in a dress that doesn’t even begin to cover her diaper. She brings Leah downstairs, puts her in a highchair, and gives her a bowl of dry cereal for breakfast.

    After breakfast, Tina buckles Leah into the carseat, puts a bottle of milk into her mouth, and the two set off for daycare. Leah’s so excited, she can barely finish her bottle. But she wants to prime her diaper as much as possible, so she manages to drink every last drop by the time they arrive. Tina takes Leah out of the car and brings her into the daycare.

    Leah’s excitement suddenly turns to nervousness, as she wonders how Tina has enrolled her. Some of Tina’s coworkers know about her teenage stepdaughter, after all. Her plan to fully fit in will be ruined if people know her true age and identity. What did Tina put on the paperwork?
