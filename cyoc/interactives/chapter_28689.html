    "Okay you affed for thif! I'm telling the aforities!" you say, trying to talk around a mouth full of bigger teeth. You storm out of the exhibit and look around for someone in charge. It isn't long before you find a fairly young woman in a zoo uniform.

    "Excuve me..." you say to her. She turns to face you and is slightly startled at first, but then realization makes itself known on her face.

    "You've been in the tiger exhibit, huh?"

    "Yef! Can you help me..me..." you say, having a little trouble with the word "Me".

    "You bet, I'm so sorry sir, we'll just give that mean ol' tiger a severe dressing down first." she takes you by the arm and takes you back to the tiger cage. The tiger is now sitting relaxedly on a rock, but approaches you both when you come into it's view.

    "I warned you." You feel a sense of victory both for getting the tiger in trouble and for finally finding something you can say with your mouth full of teeth.

    "Now, you KNOW you're not suppose to be doing this anymore for ANY reason." The woman says. The tiger makes an odd noise that you never heard come out of a tiger before.

    "It's not funny. Now you KNOW we're going to have to complete the transformation!"

    "Yeah! wait, what?" you say. For answer the woman presses a remote cotnrol, and a steel door drops down blocking the only exit to the room.

    "I'm really sorry about this, sir, but we really really can't have you running around telling people the problems we've been having here, but don't worry, you'll receive our finest tiger accomodations."

    "Hey, I won't tell anyone I promise!" you say.

    "I know you won't." She said and pushes in on your nose, which you can suddenly feel getting wider and flatter. Your face also pushes out allowing room for the rest of your teeth to finish changing. Your new ears fall into place as your head changes and your old ears get absorbed into the mass of orange and black fur that grows on your head.

    The zoo employee grabs you by the wrists and squeezes them with a playful smile. You feel your fingers curl up involentarily and you see claws start growing out of your knuckles. The plams of your hand start to change too, turning into dark brown pads. Fur grows on to your newly formed paws and continues up your arms, whose muscle structure changes as it does.

    "You should feel lucky, the first couple of times we did this it hurt the victims like nobody's business. You wouldn't believe what I had to go through when they turned me into a human." she says and rolls her eyes. Then she gives your arms a firm yank and you fall onto the ground. The moment your front paws hit the ground your back legs and spine rearrange with an uncomfortable snap. You try to get back up, the transformation has already gone too far. You watch with a mixture of sadness and panic as your body fills out the last few changes.

    "Boy that makes me homesick." the zoo employee says. "How about you?" she turns to someone behind you. You turn around to see a human male buttoning up a zoo-employee's shirt.

    "I can believe you made me a human." the man says.

    "Serves you right for messing with the guests." She says and the both grab you and push you into the cage. The door slams behind you. You can't believe what has just happened. Part of you wants to tear up the enclosure with your bare claws, and the other part just wants to lie down, resigned to your fate.
