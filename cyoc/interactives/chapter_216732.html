Then he entered in and saw a nice well lit bar with nice wooden tables and chairs, the bar table seemed nicely polished with a handsome man behind the counter with a distinguished vest and moustache who was polishing a beer mug.

The bartender started chuckling a little at Jeff's inexperience/distaste and Jeff left out with a huff. He'd be back... with his girlfriend... and maybe with knowledge of some cocktails or something...
