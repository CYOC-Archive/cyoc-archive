    Every day across the world people disappear, be it some homeless men in the streets of a major city to a college girl on school’s ground. Sometimes whole families disappear without a trace or only their car is found. People know this as a fact, sometimes the disappearance of some people are noticed and in other cases it isn’t. Either way people assume the person or people who disappeared became the victim of some crime done on them by their fellow men or maybe they ran away from their lives. Both of these are true but isn’t know by many is that there is a 3rd option.

    Unknown to the humans who walked the planet was that just a magically portal spell away from their world is a world of fantasy and magic, where humans do no exist but the creatures that humans considered mythical do. A place where Dragons fly in the sky, centaurs galloped across the fields, fairies lived among the trees, where elves build impossible thin towers reaching toward the heavens, where merpeople swim through the sea and where dozens of other species lived together free of humans.

    But they where not totally free, every so often the races needed new blood, new members to make sure their numbers stay high and to stop interbreeding from killing them off. So a council of the all the races was held and it was decided that every so often specially trained teams would travel to the human world and take a certain number of humans. They could grab individuals or small groups, whatever they felt was needed by the races. Those people would be brought back to the mythical world and through magical transformation made new members of the races.
