    "I would love to be a plane! A fast, dangerous fighter jet like an air force might have," you say with a wide grin on your face.

    "I see. You want the size, the speed, the engines, the weaponry ..."

    "That's the idea," you confirm gladly.

    "You do realize you'll need to be quite a bit bigger, and you'll have to live on a ship or an airfield instead of a house. And you won't have much control of your body, because that's what pilots are for."

    "I know it's weird, but I've always wondered what it would be like to fly through the air at potentially supersonic speeds and do the kind of maneuvers you see in movies. Maybe even get to launch missiles! I used to think it would be fun to be a pilot, but at some point I started wondering what it would be like to be the plane."

    "Well, if you're sure you want to find out, then I guess it's worth a try."

    You're not sure you like the uncertain tone in her voice.

    "This ... This isn't too hard for you, is it?"

    She laughs. "No, not for me, it isn't. I was just thinking it might be kind of challenging for you. You're got a lot of pretty big changes to go through. And the first one is a change of location."

    Your house fades out of your vision, and then you see an airstrip in front of you. There is snow on the ground, and a light wind is blowing. You're suddenly very cold! You suddenly shiver and realize you've been teleported outside.

    "I hope the weather isn't too much of a problem," said the genie. "This is a place where you might see some use by an actual air force. I know it's uncomfortable now, but planes have to be out in many different climates, and I'm sure you'll be sturdy enough to handle that after I'm done with you." She claps her hands together and starts to glow. Then you start to glow a little bit as well. You feel the magic filling your inner being. Suddenly you don't feel quite as cold any more, but you can tell something is about to happen. You can feel things happening inside you, but you can't really be sure what they are. One thing's for certain: you won't be a human much longer.
