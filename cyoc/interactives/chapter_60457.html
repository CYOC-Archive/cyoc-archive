    Certain governments had banded together to make a military take over of Pleasure Island
    and although they suffered many casualties, they had succeeded at shutting down Pleasure
    Island and killing most of the magical beings that caused all the mischief. Even Circe
    herself had been wounded although she couldn't be killed. Then negotiations ensued and
    finally a peaceable agreement was reached. The end result was Pleasure Cruise, a luxury
    boat as large as an aircraft carrier or floating city where people could go and enjoy
    vacations.

    The agreement with Circe and her crew, all transformations were temporary unless the
    guest gave into temptation. Guests had to be informed clearly of what temptations they
    were not allowed to give into and what they could indulge in. If the guest could prove that
    he or she was not clearly informed, Circe had to change the guest back. But Circe and her
    crew were free to do as much tempting as they wished.

    Therefore, Circe designed the cruise by dividing it into sections but allowing transition
    zones between the sections. This way, a guest could travel freely from one section to
    another but the rules in each section would be different and in that way, it would be more
    easy to catch guests off guard. For example, someone from the Mythology zone could walk
    through the transition zone and into the Dinosaur zone and if he forgot to read the sign
    explaining that centaurs weren't allowed in the Dinosaur zone, then he would stay a
    centaur permanently and it wouldn't be Circe's fault that he forgot to read the sign.

    There were one-hundred-and-twenty different sections, plus the transition zones. In the
    transition zones, mostly long hallways running the length of the ship plus a few lounge
    areas and a pool, centaurs and dinosaurs can meet for conversation. But in each of the
    one-hundred-and-twenty sections, there was to be no mixing.

    About one third of these sections are male only, about a third are female only, and about a
    third are coed. If you are male and wish to visit a female only section, you first must
    undergo transformation into a female.

    Nearly all of the sections are naked only, but that is because certain kinds of
    transformations don't mix well with clothes, but there are sections where even the
    transformed must wear clothes at all times and to take one's clothes off is a temptation
    that could lead to a permanent transformation as a penalty for breaking the rules.
    Likewise, attempting to wear clothes in one of the naked only sections could also cause a
    permanent transformation. All guests must be very careful to follow all the rules. Breaking
    a rule while drunk, asleep, or experiencing any form of altered mental state would not be
    an excuse. (Guests can choose whether to experience an altered mental state or not.)

    This is just a sample of some of the different zones that a guest might choose from:

    Zone 2 Myth CN zone: Here you can be any mythological creature such as centaur,
    minotaur, mer, or satyr. It's co-ed and clothing is not allowed and the rule is that you may
    not have sex with a stranger or your transformation becomes permanent.

    Zone 5 Dinosaur CN zone: Here you can be any prehistoric creature, not just dinosaurs
    although the dinosaurs are the most popular option. It's co-ed and clothing is not allowed
    and the rule is that the predators must eat at least one other guest and the prey must let
    themselves be eaten at least once during the cruise to avoid becoming permanently
    transformed.

    Zone 13 Prehistoric MN zone: The prehistoric zones are similar to the dinosaur zones
    except that the emphasis is on primitive humans like Neanderthals. Guests have to get
    permission from Circe to become non-humans but Circe may allow it so the Neanderthals
    have hunting dogs or mammoths to hunt. MN means men only and naked only.

    There are other myth zones, dinosaur zones and prehistoric zones with slight differences
    in the rules. There are also underwater zones, desert zones, African zones, jungle zones,
    forest zones, marsupial zones and snow zones. Due to their popularity, there are zones
    totally dedicated to dogs and zones totally dedicated to horses. There are also barn and
    ranch zones. Some of these zones allow anthro forms while others don't.

    There are zones where everyone is human, either naked or clothed, and these zones are
    specifically for guests who want to try a transgender vacation. Nearby are other zones for
    various body modifications such as those who want to be ultra-masculine or ultra-
    feminine (or those who want to be less than masculine men or less than feminine men). Or
    there are zones for those who want to try changes to their age or other bodily changes
    while staying human.

    There are also zones for those who want to try being a different size although there are
    limits because too big and a guest might sink the ship. But a guest can try being at least
    seventy feet tall or one inch small.

    There are also inanimate zones for guests who want to try that. There are very few of these
    zones because they are among the least popular but the zones are there if you want.

    ONE VERY IMPORTANT NOTE: Although there are slight differences in the rules for each
    zone, there is an overarching theme. Sex is the biggest temptation so naturally Circe is
    going to attempt to tempt people to have too much sex or sex that breaks the rules. The
    best way to stay safe on the Pleasure Cruise is to be extremely careful who you have sex
    with, how you have sex and how often. NO ORGIES.
