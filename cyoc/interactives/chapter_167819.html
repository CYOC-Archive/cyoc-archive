    Lynn and Shelby ran after them as fast as they could, trying not to trip on Colin’s huge mess.
    “Look out!” The twins yelled at the same time. Colin slammed gut first into a rail and dropped something over the edge. Shelby and Lynn looked over to see Harmony at the farmer’s market. A notebook fell right into her bag as she zipped it up and walked off. Colin saw it as well; he looked like he was about to have a heart attack as he realized the notebook was there.
    “Colin are you okay?!” A brunette yelled as she ran up and hugged the chubby guy. “Who the hell are you two?! Why are you after my man?!”
    “He dropped his game.” Lily said.
    “Oh...” The brunette replied as she grabbed it.

    Shelby and Lynn came over and sat on a bench as Colin did.
    “Are you alright?” the brunette girl asked as she pecked his cheek.
    “I lost my notebook, Lucy.” Colin said.
    “Notebook? .... you don’t mean THAT notebook?!” The brunette yelled.
    “It fell out and ended up with her!” Colin said as he showed a picture of Harmony to her.

    “Harmony?” The twins asked as Shelby and Lynn listened behind a billboard.
    “You know her?” Lucy asked.
    “Yeah, she’s at our Sorority.” Lily smiled.
    “She’s super understanding, she’ll give it back.” Kaia added. “What’s in it anyway?”
    “Oh, my uhhhh... bank stuff!” Colin replied, sweating nervously.
    “Where is she?!” Lucy yelled.
    “She’ll be with her husband at the Morning Glory hotel.” Lily said, “It’s their anniversary.”
    “Nice, nice..... where is it?!” Lucy yelled.
    “She left the address here.” Lily said as she gave them a slip of paper.
    “Are you two watching us?” Colin asked as he saw Shelby and Lynn fall out.
    “Weeeee.... came to give them this!” Lynn lied as she held out the schedule.

    The twins ran off back to class as Colin confronted them.
    “I saw you two in the window at GameStop... why are you really here?” He asked.
    “Look, we can’t tell you why, but we need to keep Harmony from sleeping with her husband.” Shelby said.
    “And why does this matter to us?” Lucy asked.
    “Okay then, we’ll stop her ourselves and see what’s REALLY in your notebook.” Lynn said with a puffed up chest.
    The pair sweated nervously.
    “Okay, fine.” Colin said.
