Doctor Jones really didn't judge her, or at least not in a way that would contradict the oaths he'd taken to be a good therapist and it often struck Evelyn that when he did do something more "judgmental," it was more to get her to react and state a point that she'd felt she'd burred. His humor made it a bit of a dirty trick at times, but since it was just the two of them and he always included a question on what she felt about those things, Evelyn ultimately accepted it.

After all she was be lead toward a back door to one of the buildings Lyre that she didn't recognize... of course much of that was also the fact that prior to now she had never been in Lyre or Animalia and thus didn't know either town's full layout or anything of that nature. She had some memory of things that had been reported about a year or more back regarding a "Heather Wilcox" and how Animalia published a different method of dealing with crime. In this, while she didn't know where she was going, she suspected that something wasn't going to be fully pleasant. She didn't expect torture, but potentially something embarrassing.

But that meant she had go somewhere to get through this punishment, but the room was relatively small, like it would fit over the area just before a flight of stairs and looked like a elevator, complete with polished steal. Evelyn thought she could even see her own reflection in the surface, but she wasn't sure as the lighting wasn't that great.

    However, there wasn't something right about the platform. The material looked metallic and shiny, but it didn't feel that way on Evelyn's scales. In fact it felt more like paper, and as she moved against it, Evelyn had brought her full weight onto a section of the platform that was near the corner. Given the political climate... that probably wouldn't be welcomed."

    Evelyn didn't immediately answer as her clothes were tossed onto the concrete with an audible "splat." Though she had seen enough on the news to give some indication of that. America's present government had certainly been trying to legislate Animalia out of existence, only to run into either miswording the laws that left them "unconstitutional" or tripping over other biases that made keeping Animalia more desirable, and then since 2019 the division between the House and Senate. However with some of the other things going on, Evelyn also expected that the present administration would regain full control over the government following the upcoming elections and triggering some kind of excuse to punish Animalia by spreading the Animalia Virus was the last thing Evelyn wanted.

    "Who are you...?" Evelyn asked after a moment.

    "You may call me, Sabine," the other cobra answered as she returned with a folding step ladder with a series of brushes and soaps on the top step, "and we will start with getting clean..."

And some times the sides two, as that will help you push off against points of friction. Particularly for those of us that are cobras. We aren't like an un-uplifted Puff Adder that can just pull their full mass forward in a straight line. Which means that even if you don't catch a flea or a tick or some other parasite... you might still come into something that might not be good for your health. That's why keeping clean is a must."
