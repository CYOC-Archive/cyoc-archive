You pull yourself out of the bathtub but you fell right back in the bathtub, you try a second and successfully you did but you fell flat on your face, you then noticed that for some reason you can breath air again, you check your sides, the gills were still their however they seem to be closed, it looks like you gained the ability to breath both air and water, what confuses you though is the fact that your still a merman despite being able to breath on land, you struggle to dragged yourself to your bedroom, you rest your arms for a while and then continue too find where your phone is, luckily you found it on top of your mini drawer next to your bed, you then pull yourself up to the bed and lie down, you don't feel dry for some reason, you check yourself, it seems like your tail is still their despite being on land, looks like it won't turn back into legs in an instant unlike those mermaid tv shows that you've seen on television.

    You kept using your phone and binge watching on the internet, this is what boredom does especially when you don't have anything to do on land as a merman, you then search in the internet on how to get rid of something like this, if it even EXIST on the internet.
    Nothing.. there were no results about the situation your currently in, guess your stuck like this for a while or maybe for a long time, who knows.
    You kept using your phone for minutes, you check your tail again... Still no changes whatsoever, and how on earth does it not feel dry despite being on land? Maybe your tail magically self lubricates it's insides to prevent being dry or maybe it can handle being dry.. now the question that's been bugging your mind is how long will this tail even last?
    You eventually got tired so you took your charger from a nearby drawer in your bed and charged your phone next to it, you then think for a while, why did this happen to you? First you saw mermaids, then I fell in a pool of water, grew a tail and losing your legs, almost died from not having H2O in your body then gaining back the ability to breath, and now your a land and water breathing merman you hasn't changed back for almost an hour... Well this is such a strange day..
    You think and think, you got so tired of thinking that you felt sleepy, eventually you fell in the depths of sleep.
