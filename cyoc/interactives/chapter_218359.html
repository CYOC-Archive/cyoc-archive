He set the microwave to "defrost", as his socks began to unravel as well, disappearing thread by thread, freeing his green, long-clawed feet. At this point, it clicked in his mind and he slightly panicked. He had better open the window before the smoke chokes him! He wasted no time freeing up some air for himself.

This ruin was of course attached to a modern home which still had not felt the effects. Trevor spit the roast onto a big metal fork and took it back inside to serve to his folks. If he hadn't mistakenly set "Victims Aware of Change" to "Off", he would certainly not be so casual about the whole ordeal right now.
