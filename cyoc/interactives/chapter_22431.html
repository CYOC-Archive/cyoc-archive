Here, this is my pride and joy," He draws out an old wooden box, and opens it, revealing another box labeled the Game of Change. "The Game of Change! Only 9.95 for today! I guarantee it will entertain you and all the players, or I'll pay you DOUBLE what you pay the next day, provided you finish the game. To judge it fairly I mean."
