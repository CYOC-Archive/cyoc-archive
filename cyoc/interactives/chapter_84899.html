    Since that time Emily's life has adopted the new routine. Every day after college Emily went to the transformation shop, changed into goat girl, donned her uniform and started her work. Her job was to advise clients pre-made spells and potions and lead them to Winifred if these weren't meeting their needs.
    Her goat girl form was adjusted to suit her new job and communicating with human clients. Her muzzle was reverted to almost human face, save for the cute flat nose, big flappy goat ears and wool. Her eyes returned to mostly human with slightly rectangular pupils.
    Her digestive system was returned to almost human, but it was more suited to fruits and vegetables now, instead of grass. As a goat girl, Emily was a vegetarian, so she happily munched on carrots and cabbages.
    Her mind though was a subject of a big discussion between her and Winifred. On the farm Emily found out that not only she wasn't able to speak, but she couldn't read or write properly as well. Using her human memories, the goat girl was barely able to write her own name, carefully drawing each letter line by line, but even this simple task gave her a headache, so she decided to stop. Reading was simply out of question, as Emily couldn't make out any letters, let alone words, which she found pretty amusing. This obviously had to change, but there was something else. Winifred was worried that the less "book smart" mind would hinder her ability to consult clients, but Emily really didn't want to give up the simplicity of a goat's mind. Eventually, after a small examination, the sorceress decided not to eliminate goat consciousness in the girl completely and just enable her speech, reading and writing, though Emily still bleated on habit once in a while. Counting was still difficult (in goat form Emily could count to about twenty, but this was it, complex decimal fractions were out of question), but neither the woman nor the girl worried, since Winifred would be the one charging clients anyway.
    Since Emily's wool was thick and warm, she was against usual store uniforms like blouses and skirts. Winifred made her a white top, which looked like a sports bra with wide straps, and boxers-like panties. They were comfortable enough on the girl's wool and covered all the "X-rated" bits. The top even had her name sewn on it in nice dark green letters.
    Emily was seeing a lot of new people, and a lot of exotic, strange, even bisarre things. She has seen three girls entering the shop, and a three headed dog girl leaving. She has seen boys turning into girls, girls transforming into men, women becoming younger, and even a fifteen-year-old girl cautiously waddling out of the shop with a belly big enough to carry triplets.
    Over time, Emily grew accustomed to her job. She made some new friends, and her simple mind allowed her to relax and work away the stresses of the college. She wasn't growing exactly bored, but she wasn't as excited as she was before, especially when the work become slow. She was yet unaware of another surprise that Winifred prepared for her...
