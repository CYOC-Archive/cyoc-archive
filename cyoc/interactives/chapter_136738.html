We’re playing capture the flag soon.” Ann just grunted in response as she closed the door to her office. “That was surreal,” she thought. “And I’m pretty sure I’m the oldest in the office right now, just not sure for how much longer.”

They share common traits like hair color, eyes, skin color, etc. mine and Katie’s magic is the same way. The magic we use is related and it turns out the magic affecting you is related to us as well.”

    “How,” Ann said curiously. “I mean Lisa and my folks don’t have magic. You two are the only ones with magic in the family.”

    “Apparently they’re not the only ones,” Lisa sighed as she pointed to her stomach. Ann’s eyes went wide as she now gazed first at Lisa’s stomach before gaping at Katie and Gloria. “You’ve got to be kidding me!”

    “Nope no joke,” Lisa groaned. “This one right here apparently felt a little bad about that you couldn’t relive your teen years. So she decided to make your job a little more fun.”

    “Lisa,” Ann exclaimed. “How is that even freaking possible! Your like what a month pregnant!? How does she have that much magic already?”

    “We could,” Katie answered. “But I’m pretty sure she would just do it again. And next time it could be at a very inopportune time. She seems to think that you need this so you may just want to roll with it.”
