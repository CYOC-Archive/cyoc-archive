    with quick thinking, James did everything he could for Mr. Spinoza to back off in an convincing act. as Kayla gave a smile that Mr. Spinoza was almost convinced. she gently walked to his side as he felt fer hand on his back that gave him the cue to calm down that she looked up to him.

    "I see, so he is smart as a human. really well trained indeed, for a reptile." he coldly said as James heard all of this that all he wanted to was to rip him apart, but didn't with her hand still on his back.

    "sir, if I may. we all see the rabbit trick being used with the magician all the time, but they will never hear of a magician using an alligator before. Mr. Spinoza, we can do this." Kayla said with a Convincing grin.

    hearing her case while stroking his beard as he then looked down on the alligator that James made a low below sound, telling him to not think about doing it again. he wasn't sure what the alligator was saying, but he knew that if he tried to go after her like he did before again is where he would be attack.
