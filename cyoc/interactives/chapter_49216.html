    "So what will we do with all this stuff?" Kaylee asked as she returned with Scott's shredded clothing and lost police equipment that Scott lost when he became the current dead pig on Jennifer's shoulder.

    Jennifer sighed heavily. The weapons and handcuffs they could keep, but there wasn't much that they needed. The blood still on Kaylee's paws from when she had killed Scott in pig form proved that they didn't need the gun. And at different points, both of them had proved that their own strength was enough to make the handcuffs needless for them, but if they left the handcuffs behind anywhere, they would eventually be traced back to either their presence or something else. The clothes could burned eventually.

    "I'm not sure entirely," Jennifer sighed, "We may have to burn it eventually, but for now, we'll have to keep it with us."

    Jennifer began to head out the back door he had initially come through with Kaylee following.

    "Where will we go?" Kaylee asked as she followed.

    "There are some woods outside of town, which will be a start," Jennifer told her mate as they went, "which we can start with. We can finish eating our meal there, and from there, we can start our pack from."

    From there, Jennifer leaped over the fence with Scott's pig body still over her shoulders into the yard that separated the home that Kaylee had lived in before her present transformation her present form. Kaylee followed with ease. One onto the next line of sidewalks, the two of them began their next line of walking toward the next line of streets toward the line of woods toward around the western part of the town.

    "What will we have to do to regain the 'transform or dare' power?" Kaylee asked after they made a turn onto another street.

    Jennifer sighed again as they went. Regaining that power was a goal for him as they continued on their way. He had originally hoped that Kaylee would refrain from using it until he had the opportunity to give the command to actually enlarge the pack he had started earlier with Kaylee and to insure that the followers would remain loyal to him. But, Frank was now the only one had that power. Scott had it as well, but with Kaylee killing him after his transformation into the the now dead pig that Jennifer carried, only the other officer that Jennifer carried had that power.

    "I'll have to track down the other officer and find out what he does with it after he arrives back at his station," Jennifer sighed, "while you establish a den for us in the woods."

    Kaylee nodded as she followed her mate.
