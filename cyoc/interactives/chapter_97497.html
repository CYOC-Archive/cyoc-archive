    Scott had finally arrive to his girlfriend, but on his way here he could not stop being so curious about the medallion that he held in his hand. A moment ago he felt something happen when he wore it, it even glow just a mere moment ago. He wanted to first show this thing to his girlfriend to discuss about it, but his curiosity had began to reach there limits, he couldn't fight this strange urge to wear this thing again, even for just a second notices.

    "I...I think I should try this thing out, just so nothing bad happen to Brittany show something does happen." He slowly place the medallion around his neck once more, and already it started to glow once more. However, the once strange sensation he had got did not come back at all, and the glow soon began to dyed once more. Feeling that this was a rather anti-climatic event, Scoot felt silly for letting this thing get to him like a fool and felt now was the time to throw it away. Scott place his hand slowly on the medallion, but out of no where a voice got Scoot by surprise.

    "AT LAST! SOMETHING HAS FOUND THE MEDALLION OF POWER ONCE MORE. THANK YOU MY MASTER FOR FREEING ME!' The voice Scott hear was almost feminine, but it had a loud, booming voice that nearly scare the living shit out of him. He look around the from all direction of his view, but there was no one around him or close to him to be found. Once more the voice quickly return.

    "WHERE ARE YOU LOOKING FOR? I'M RIGHT AROUND YOUR NECK." Scott complete froze, slowly staring down at his medallion as he his lifted it up to his face. There was a face on the medallion, it face was cover in strange flames that doesn't burn at all, along with it eyes and mouth. It did look like a woman, only with the lack of hair and just cover in flame.

    "Wh...What the hell is this!" Scott was shock by this disturbing event, but the flaming face was not paying attention to the comment and continue speaking.

    "AS A REWARD FOR FREEING ME I, THE SPIRIT OF THE MEDALLION, WILL NOW GRANT YOU POWER THAT YOU DESIRE!"

    "Power? What power?" Scott was indeed scared by this, but the way the medallion spoke about this reward got him curious and decided to pay action to it.
