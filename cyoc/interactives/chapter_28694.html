    "It's like I could either spend the next thirty days fighting the change and being miserable, or I could just go with it and see what happens. Just seeing how well you're doing as a man--it feels very natural right now being who I am."

    "If you and I go somewhere away from everyone else, I just might end up having sex with you." She could feel Keith's heart beat faster through his chest.

"No, you're right."

    Keith began again. "You notice, though, that there aren't a lot of kittens or cubs or fawns around, and I bet someone's having sex. Maybe there's a contraceptive that people use."

    Jackie replied. "Hmmm, I bet there is--the people running the park can do all kinds of tricks with biology. I bet Hannah would know."

    "Do you want me to ask her?"

    "No." Jackie began to disentangle herself from Keith. "I'm going to ask her." She smiled. "It's a girl thing."
