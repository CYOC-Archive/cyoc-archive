    "There are no rules in the circus, many are killed!" said the Shiek to Juda Ben Hur.

    You sit up in your seat as a section of the movie changes and a scene you've never seen before begins to play tricks on the audience.

    "Come Juda and see the mircle to my being the owner of great and powerful horse flesh." said the Shiek as he puts his arm over Juda's shoulder.

    Juda (Charlton Heston) looks back at the camera yet you feel he's looking somehow directly at you.

    A drape seperates and the two men walk into another section of this great tent of the Shiek.

    "LaBella my sweet and how are you this lovely evening?" asked the Shiek as the camera pans to a black mare of some size. The mare's rump is facing the camera and it turns to greet her lord and master.

    Now your one the edge of the seat as the view of this horse brings a sweating shudder. The sleek black mare is all horse except for her face which is as a human face were photomanipulated into replace the actual horse's face.

    "No, no this is all wrong, who the hell fucked up the movie?" you scream and standing up get people shouting for you to sit back down.

    Again Juda looks back at the camrea and you. Yet too now the Shiek gets a seductive look and casts his view your way too.

    "You there, you do not like my sweet daughter here? She was and is my most precious of things! She like you had the stubborness to think she was always right. It took a wizard name Mothesus to show her what she wished most. A right and gorgous mare my daughter does make and a good breeder too! Ah but did she enjoy the first mouning of my best stallion. She moaned with true satisfaction as his maleness bore in deep and she did gasp with each thrust. She's known a life now of absolute female pleasure that as a woman she would never have allowed a man's touch." says the Shiek as Juda is wide eyed and looking scared.

    The Shiek smiles and then walks to the side curtain of the tent. Hanging on a tent pole is a leather purse which the Shiek reaches his fist into and turns to smile at you again.

    "You've seen and believe not! My daughter needs a companion for the coming cold Arabian nights to come, you, yeas you there the upset and questioning one I grant you to come a find or give companionship to my daughter." says the Shiek as he then tossed a handful of sparkled dust out at the audience.

    People laugh, some moan but then you notice this fine dust of silver sparkles settling down all around you.

    In seconds its very hard to hold your balance sitting there in the seat. You reach to grab the chair in front of you only to see and scream.

    The Shiek turns and gives a look your way.

    Juda turns and looks your way then he looks at the Shiek. "I guess you'll have that companion for your daughter now!"

    The Shiek has an evil grin over his unshaven face. He steps closer to the camera and looking down from his lofty screen he says, "The time is right, please have the usher escort my Daughter's companion to her tent!"

    The normal movie continues on as if nothing more was odd about what you just saw. A sound to your right sees a red coated usher motioning for you to come to the isle side and talk.

    In a very uneasy movment you work your way to the isle. Looking up into the wrinkled man's face he's wearing an Arab getup and has in his hand a small halter made for some pony.

    Quickly the halter is slipped over your face and cinched tight to hold about you head. A tug on the rein urges you to step out into the isle.
    The walk is toward the screen and not back up to the front and exit. Up the steps you plod making a bunch of noise as hoofs beat a pattern of movement onto the wooden stage.

    Your led behind the screen and into what looks like a very dark night with thousands upon thousands of stars twinkling in the black sky.

    "Is this the one?" asked a man who came out of nowhere.

    The Usher gives him the reins to your halter and turns quietly and walks away. This new man looks down at you and with dark orbed eyes makes you feel a bit uneasy.

    "You are blessed I've been told to be unto the Shiek's daughter a companion for these long winters nights. Be this true enough I can see you will do just fine but remember one thing well, no sexual touching of her is allowed, understand?" the guard asked.

    You knod your head in silence and then your led to that tent and the human faced mare.

    What do you do now?
