    “Oh, no you won’t!” a woman’s voice called out.

    Yukiko turned around to see an all too familiar sight. A fairy with shocking pink hair tied in a high ponytail, a long, rainbow colored gown and a highly ornate wand stood before her. Two other fairies accompanied her; one with shoulder length lavender hair and a blue gown; and one with green hair that reached her ankles in a pink gown. Both of them had equally ornate wands as the first. “M-Magistrate Celeste!” Yukiko said nervously. “What brings you…?”

    “Silence!” Celeste shouted. “You know damn well why we’re here!”

    “We’re appalled by your actions, Yukiko!” said Flora, the green haired fairy. “Not only did you manipulate a vaguely worded wish for your own personal gain, AGAIN; but you did so in a way unnatural to our kind!”

    “And, to top it all off…” Spoke Star, the lavender haired fairy. “You tried to rob a loving couple of their children!”

    “C’mon, it’s not like our kind hasn’t done that before.”

    “You know we abolished such things centuries ago!” Celeste spoke. “But, since you seem to enjoy your current state so much…” All three fairies pointed their wands at Yukiko and loosed a bright beam of light that struck her in the chest. A wave of light spread out from Yukiko in all directions. Yukiko’s wand vanished as she began to grow, her body quickly bursting out of her dress. Her breasts shrank to B-cups, her wings shriveled up and vanished and her belly shrank down to normal size.

    When everything ended Yukiko looked down at herself and gasped. “What have you done to me? Where are Kimiko and Naomi?”

    “JIRO and Naomi are still inside of you.” Flora stated.

    “We just reverted your pregnancy to it’s first days.” Added Star.

    “You are now the sole adopted daughter of Naomi’s parents.” Celeste said. “You are the same age as Naomi and you must carry your children for the full 9 months of human pregnancy.”

    “Nine months? That’s how long it takes?” Yukiko shouted.

    “Yes.” Celeste said. “Once you’ve carried and delivered the children, everything will change to the way it would have been, had you never met Naomi.”

    The fairies turned around, Celeste raised her wand and conjured a portal. “Oh, one more thing.” Celeste added. “Don’t even THINK about terminating the pregnancy. You KNOW the penalty for a fairy who takes a human life.”

    With that the fairies stepped through the portal leaving Yukiko by herself. “Nine months of pregnancy,” Yukiko said to herself. “I can handle that e…” Yukiko’s stomach started to bother her. “What’s… ugh…” Yukiko groaned before throwing up on the floor.
