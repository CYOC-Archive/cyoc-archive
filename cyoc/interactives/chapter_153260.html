    "You know you two should join us," Sarah stated as she looked between Jessie and Duke. "I dont know," Jessie frowned. "Hanging out in a bathing suit all day. Not exactly my idea of a fun time."

    "Look guys," Sarah exclaimed. "Whether we like it or not were stuck like this for the next few days. The way I see we can either sit here and put or try to have fun." Duke and Jessie looked between eachother, shrugging before nodding in acceptance. If they were going to be kids, might as well enjoy it.

    Getting into the park proved to not be a problem for the group. After purchasing some bathing suits the group mixed in with the other kids and adults, entering without even being noticed. As they headed towards one of the pools Jessie and Sarah immediately found a pair of beach chairs to settle down in.

    "What are you two doing," Terry asked as the two girls now played out in the sun.

    "Working on my tan what else," Jessie stated as she smoothed out her orange tankini. Terry could only chuckle as he looked at the two 12 year old girls. "In case you two hadn't noticed your the youngest ones over here. It kinda looks weird." Jessie and Sarah paused as they looked over the row of beach chairs. Terry had a point, they were easily the youngest there. Most of the women looked like mothers, all between in their late 20s to early 40s. Jessie in her tankini and Sarah in her one piece looked out of place amongst the adults. Worse for the girls was the fact they were lined up with women who possessed adult curves. Among 12 year olds they might seem mature, amongst adults; it only made the two realize how young they had become.

    "Terry might have a point," Sarah said sheepishly as she now crossed her arms over her chest in embarrassment. Jessie could only sigh and nod in agreement. "So what do we do instead," Jessie asked.

    Terry smirked, motioning towards the wave pool and water slide. "I say we have some fun," he smirked. "From the looks of things most of the older kids are hanging out over there. Why dont we just mingle in and enjoy ourselves."

    Duke and the girls shrugged, agreeing while not exactly excited about the idea. Their lack of excitement though changed quickly.

    At first embarrassed to be hanging out with children, they quickly began to enjoy themselves. Duke and Terry playfully splashed their respective girlfriends who responded in kind. From their the group participated in chicken fights before breaking off into Paris. Duke and Terry now playfully flirted with their respective dates, dunking them in the pool and laughing all the while. It would be hours before hunger finally overcame them as they made their way towards the snack bar.

    "I haven't had that much fun in years," Jessie laughed. "I mean look at me. I'm soaked and my fingers are all pruned. O usually hate going in the water."

    "It was fun wasn't it," Duke said happily. "Especially watching you shriek when I dunked you." Jessie playfully stuck out her tongue as she shoved Duke. While the group maintained their adult emotions, something about the innocence and playfulness of the actions made things quite refreshing.

    Terry gazed over at Sarah. The brunette girls hair hung in wet strands as she giggled. "How are you feeling," he asked. Sarah grinned ear to ear as she nuzzled into him. "You know," she said quietly. "This really was alot of fun. It's strange, no pressure or worry about making anything romantic. Were just having fun and cutting loose. Havent enjoyed a date like this in forever."

    "So was this our second date then," Terry asked anxiously, his voice cracking nervously.

    Sarah giggled as she gave Terry a gentle peck on the cheek. "Call it what you want Muscles," she smiled. "But next time you try dunking me in the pool I'm kicking your ass."

    "Hey you had fun," Terry laughed. "Besides you dunked me too."

    "Yeah I did," Sarah laughed. "But I look like a drowned rat now." Looking herself over Sarah stared down at her feet. "Which reminds me, gonna have to ask Jessie if she can do my nails later. I'm in desperate need of a pedicure."

    Terry could only chuckle as he draped an arm over Sarah's shoulder. While stuck as kids for a few days they could at least enjoy themselves.
