The chemical smell of chlorine fills the air in a intoxicating way. As the last ones leave, the equipement is left alone in this empty and dark place.

    What might be prepared in this secluded facility, all the way onto Antartica? According to the records and digital diaries of the employees, it seems like this builiding as been assigned to the "Synthetize" Project, an idea completely revoked during most scientific groupings, due to his unethical and questionable goal. Indeed, the project as for priority to manage to synthesize life out of artificial matter, with absolutely nothing remotely close to the usual reproduction techniques. The goal is to push the boundaries of the creation of life. It is understandable that most scientifics would not even approach this project, as it would "contradict nature and god".

    This group of hard working independent minds though, decided to take the project under their arms, financed by some unknown organisation. They are not numerous, barely hitting ten members, but their efforts does not look like it has been in vain! There are a lot of incubators decorating the walls, the multiple tries and errors getinng monitored day and night by the computers to try to make a breakthrough. It seems like none of them woke up yet... maybe this project is not in the best shape right now... Let's take a look!
