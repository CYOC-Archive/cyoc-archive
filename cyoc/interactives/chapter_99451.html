    You aren't the kind of robot who obeys orders.

    added 7 years ago TG

    You don't remember anything else, until you feel a switch being hit in the back of your head again. Involuntarily you say "Operational."

    Your feet are your first view when you "wake up". You quickly conclude that you must have bent down when you deactivated yourself, like a wind-up doll with no power. "How stupid I am, luckily enough someone has reactivated me". You get up and look around, and realize you are in some kind of store, full of computers and various electronic devices.

    "Turn around, robot girl" You hear a voice behind you. You do so, and you see two young guys, in their twenties. One of them is holding a remote. "Did they build a remote for me!?" You wonder.

    "Listen, I'm not a girl, and definitely not a robot either, this is just a costume, you have to believe me!" You say, hoping they´ll help you.

    "Well, you look like a girl to me, and you have a control panel with an on/off switch and everything so yeah, you´re a robot. Tough luck, I guess it´s not fun being shut down with the press of a button or being reprogrammed..." The first guy said.

    "Actually, we have news for you. We built this remote for you, so you´re now remote-controlled, congratulations. And we, as the owners of this store, have decided to sell you, as a brand new robotic companion, the newest technology in the world. You should be proud of yourself! By the way, what are you programmed for? We´ll probably change that anyway, but I´m curious" Asked the guy holding the remote.

    "I told you, I´m not a robot, and I´m not programmed, I have free will! And I won´t let you program me, who the hell do you think you are? I´m leaving and I´ll definitely call the police!" You state in anger.

    "Yeah, I´m sure they´ll help a crazy robot girl... You know they´ll disable and then dismantle you to find out what kind of short circuit you´re experiencing, right?" Said the first guy, quite sarcastically.

    "You know what?" Said the other guy, now talking to his friend and aiming his remote at you, causing you to start panicking. "It's getting late, let´s shut her down and deal with this tomorrow".

    "No! Please wai-" You try to say, but he presses the off button, and your world goes black.

    What do you do now?
