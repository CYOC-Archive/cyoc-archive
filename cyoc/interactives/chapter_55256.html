    Nate walked over to Susan, looking a little apprehensive. "So, what're we going to do first?"

    "Well, the assignment is to do a series of photos and paintings of a mythological, or several different ones if we want, in a natural setting," replied Susan.

    "Well, that leaves things pretty open," replied Nate. "I mean there's a lot of different types of mythological creatures."

    "I figure since you two are the ones being changed, it should be your choice," said Susan.

    "You two...you mean, me and Patrick?" asked Nate, Susan and Kat nodded. "Oh, that's going to be so cute."

    "Yeah, we thought so too," replied Kat.

    Nate looked around and saw a couple boulders next to the pond and a thought sparked into his head.

    "So, I think for the first picture, Patrick and I should be fauns sitting on those boulders over there. I think that'd be a nice shot," he said.

    "Ooh, that's a good idea," said Susan. "Nate, you get to go first..."

    "All right, one second," he replied, barely containing his excitement as he quickly pulled off his t-shirt and slipped off his shoes. "Okay, go."

    Susan gave a quick chant and wave of her wand and the transformation started. All four watched as coarse brown fur started to grow in over Nate's legs.

    He cried out and grabbed his head as two horns started to grow out. Nate fell to his knees as his feet stretched and reformed, each ending in a hard hoof. His horns continued to grow, curving around either side of his head as his ears stretched to a point. A small nubbin of a tail grew out. A fine amount of fur grew over his forearms as well.

    As the transformation ended, Nate checked himself out and was pleased at what he saw. His legs and waist were covered in a thick, dense fur all the way down to the ends of his legs, which each now ended in a hard hoof. Nate slowly pulled off his shorts, revealing more thick fur, effectively hiding his penis, no doubt to keep the pictures family friendly. He carefully touched his horns, discovering them to be firmly attached. He also caught sight of his torso, which had become significantly more defined now.

    "Holy crap! This is awesome!" exclaimed Nate.

    "That was so cool! Do me now!" exclaimed Patrick. Kat performed the spell on her brother and watched as he went through the same changes. When it was done, Patrick looked like an exact match to Nate, except his horns weren't as long, no doubt an age thing.

    With the transformations complete, the group made their way to the boulders. Nate and Patrick climbed up, their hard hooves scraping on the rocks. Susan's only instruction as she began snapping pictures were for them to ignore the camera. Susan snapped away as Nate and Patrick joked around and talked. She couldn't believe how natural the two looked as a pair of fauns, almost as if they had always been that way.

    After about an hour, the spell faded and Patrick and Nate returned to normal. Susan put down her camera and they tried to decide what they wanted to do next.
