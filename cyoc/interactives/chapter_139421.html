    added by higeman 4 years ago A BM O Anthro Equine Reality alteration

    As the two left the more populated area of the town near the forest, the second phase of magic washed over them, adding an odd new reality to the mentally changed duo. The two began to physically change, both with similar yet different changes.

    Both ladies' ears began to stretch out slowly, flicking in the cool air as a light coat of fur began to cover each pair, with Annie's ears being covered with a soft chestnut coloration to contrast with her blonde hair and Lucy's becoming furred with a golden yellow despite her own blonde hair due to the fact that her natural hair color is indeed a darker shade.

    The magic had also appeared to affect their perception of their changes as even though their hearing abilities where increased to the point of being able to make out every sound in the forested area, neither of them could hear the ripping sounds coming from behind them as long haired tails broke free from the seats of their designer pants, swishing absentmindedly above their rears. The hair colors correlated to their natural colors with Annie's being a silky blonde while Lucy's was a deep black.

    This is where the changes stop being identical and begin having slight differences from each other. Starting with their hands, Annie's hands began to harden with every step, causing them to uncurl from fists to holding them straight out as her fingers merged into a single large hoof on each. Lucy's hands instead merged only some of her fingers and the tips to harden, leaving her with two large hoofed tipped fingers and a thumb on each hand. Though both their feet merged similarly into large singular hooves on each foot, the difference showed Annie's feet and legs becoming much thicker and stronger to be able to hold up more weight, though they were still somewhat humanoid in appearance and function should she ever find the rare situation to stand up on her now 'hind' legs. Lucy's feet and legs retained most of their bipedal appearance and function, though it was still very digitigrade enough to be identified as horse-like.

    The pair's faces were next as both their mouths began to stretch outwards, pulling their faces with them. Lucy's stop shortly after, leaving her with a half-snout still with flared nostrils and rubbery lips, but not as pronounced as a real horse. Annie's, on the other hoof, was much longer and closer resembling a horse's muzzle, even having a distinctive white horse mark on the bridge of her nose, travelling up between her eyes, which had now slightly readjusted to look towards the sides rather than forward. Even her eyes began to dilate, making them appear darker though still retaining some of her humanly blue iris.

    And lastly, Annie's body shape started to change unlike Lucy's with her chest and abdomen becoming slightly larger and barreled, giving her the extra core muscles to carry her friend on her back while still being able to briskly walk in her increasingly horse-like gait.

    Nonetheless, the pair had now changed into anthro horses in respect to the new memories and reality that the crystal orb had bestowed onto them.
