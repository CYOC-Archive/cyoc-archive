    Sarah knew the of a bake sale going on down the street to raise funds for the gay community.

    There was a section of the bear, chub community that was under represented and she had always thought that Alex needed to learn to be a little more tolerant to gay men.

    As she dropped off her donation of the glass jar with cookies and Alex she stood by and watched a young man, furry from head to toe. Completely unlike Alex. He was a bit chubby but more muscle chub.

    He picked up the Alex jar and looked at it. Alex did his best to hide hoping that the man didn't see him in there but his efforts were thwarted. He did see Alex but wouldn't let on that he saw him in there.

    I think I will take this one Damion said.

    Damion slipped the jar in his bag and headed home.

    When he took the jar home he went to his room and got out some leather gear.

    As he returned he opened the jar and as Alex tumbled out onto the counter a change happened.

    Have you ever seen a jar of marshmallows opened that had the air pumped into it compressing the marshmallows?

    That is what happened to Alex, It was like he was a soft pliable person that inflated like a balloon. And I do mean like a balloon. He didn't just grow taller to his old height he also grew rounder. His belly jutted out. Not terribly large but a bit larger. He now had chubby sausage like fingers. His face rounded out giving him a double chin. His legs became like tree trunks rubbing together. If he had jiggled his arms they would have wiggled. And his once proud pecs were now beginning to become man boobs.

    Before Alex was able to come to his senses Damion locked on a leather collar around Alex's neck and said good boy.

    What are you doing to me?

    I didn't do anything to you. Whoever put this spell on you did it I just know how to take care of you.

    But how did I get so fat?

    Well that is the compression spell. You see every time you are compressed and re-expanded you will round out more.

    Re-expanded? You mean I am going to be compressed again?

    Oh yes Damian replied. I like my boys round. I am what the community calls an encourager. I like to fatten up boys but that take so long. You are going to be much more fun. Oh I am going to fatten you up as I have you out but as you grow I am going to put you back in the jar and every time you come out you are going to be rounder and rounder. Soon you wont be able to go through a door without buttering up your sides.

    But I don't want to be fat.

    Well boy you see I bought you so now you are my property.

    But people can't be property.

    I didn't by a person I bought a jar with you in it. You are mine.

    Damian linked the collar to a chain and hooked that chain to a bolt in the floor keeping Alex from running away. He handed Alex the cookies and told him to eat up. And started dinner. A big banquet just for his boy.
