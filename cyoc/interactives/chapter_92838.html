    Sure enough, Patrick got younger. His facial and pubic hair receaded and he began to thin, loosing his muscle and regaining baby fat. Patrick started to shrink until he was ten. Patrick looked in the mirror and grinned. He was ten again, he was so excited he couldn't contain himself. Patrick jumped around, happy as could be.

    But Patrick was lonly too, he was the oldest child in his family and never had a older sibling. Then an Idea struck him, Patrick went to the room's phone and called the main desk.

    "Transformation Resort Lobby Desk, how can we help you?" The attendent asked. "This is Patrick Anderson, I regressed to ten and was wondering if I could get someone to come take care of me?" Patrick asked. "Of course Mr. Anderson, anyone can request this. Do you have a particular type of care giver you want? We have big sister's, big brother's, mom's, dad's, baby sitter's." The attendant said.

    Patrick thought for a bit. "Big sister for now, please." Patrick said. "She'll be up in a moment." The attendent said. "Thank you." Patrick said as he hung up.


    Patrick waited in excitement as he heard a knock. Patrick opened up and saw her.

    There stood a beautiful 18 year old Caucasian girl with blond hair running down to her shoulders. She had a sweet angelic face with a warm smile, thin nose, small chin, green eyes and a athletic and sexy body.

    "Hi, I'm Patrick Anderson, who are you?" Patrick asked as he introduced himself. The young lady smiled. "My name's Sirena, you called for a big sis?" She asked. Patrick nodded, hugging Sirena. "Yes, I was lonely and I wanted someone to take care of me." Patrick said as Sirena hugged him back before releasing him. "That's..why..I'm..here." She replied, playfully tapping Patrick's nose between each word. This made Patrick grin. "You'll stay even if I become younger?" Patrick asked as Sirena nodded. "So my new little brother, what do you want to do first?" She asked sweetly.

    "I kinda wish I could explore the other wings too." Patrick said. "I can arrangr for you to get the all access package." Sirena smiled. Patrick vigorously nodded. Sirena giggled at this and called it in. After giving the resort Patrick's room and bank card number, the room started shifting to include access to the other wings. "Cool!" Patrick said. Sirena smiled, seeing him happy made her happy. "Ok little brother, what do you wanna do?" Sirena asked as Patrick thought.
