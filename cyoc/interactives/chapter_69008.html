    I saw that Rebecca wasn't afraid, but she does look a little annoyed "Bonnie,” she said, “I knew you for 7
    years and we shared our secrets, but you never told me you were a werewolf. How could you keep this from me?"
    Bonnie growled, “Sorry Rebbeca, I didn’t know how you reacted if I showed you. You were scared enough when
    that muscle head Rick took you.” Rebecca said “I understand but why did you involve Chad into the pack?” I
    growled “Rebecca, honey, let me explain. Bonnie planned all this with me and Ronald, and with Wendy and
    Sandy’s help. I was turned.” Wendy interrupted, “I prefer the term reborn.” I continued “Reborn into the
    pack, to help liberate you from Rick’s clutches.” Rebecca said “You’re not going to kill him, are you?” I
    shook my head “No, nothing like that.” I growled “Just going to help you stand up to him. If you’re up to it.”
    Rebecca says “You mean you all are going to make me a werewolf?” Bonnie growls “Not all of us.” Rebecca faces
    Ronald “Hey,” he growls, “I’m in it to forfill my fantasy.” Rebecca just faces me, “Yes Rebbeca, me.” I
    growled “What better way to help then the ma..uhhh wolf you meant to be with?” I can see the other initiates
    completing their progress as one of the humans invited joined the pack with a howl of approval. “You’re not
    just going to be a werewolf.” Rebecca says “I don’t know I’m a little nervous.” Bonnie gently places a clawed
    hand on Rebecca’s shoulder, “Think of it, you’ll be stronger then Rick, and think of the benefits of being a
    werewolf. Never be sick again and injuries suffered will heal instantly.” Rebecca just smiled and said “Thanks
    Bonnie, I’ll do it. I’m up for anything that’ll help me get away from that self-proclaimed beast of a man.”
    She comes up to me and rubs my chest fur “I hope I didn’t offend you. With that comment.” I just comfort her
    by combing my claws into her hair “An understandable mistake.” I growled. The Mayor growls “Chad, Rebecca.
    You’re up next.” I nodded to the Mayor and escorted Rebecca to the stage. Ace came up to me “Good luck Chad.”
    He growled “show her how wolves do it.” He give me a thumbs up and got out of the way. “How are you going to
    transform me?” Rebecca said “I’m sure you’ll like it,” I growled, “It take more than a bite to make a
    werewolf.” Rebecca grins “Chad, I never made it with a werewolf before, but I wouldn’t mind trying it.”
