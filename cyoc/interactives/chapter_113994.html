Normally he’d avoid this guy like the plague, such an obvious stoner was not the sort of person Luke would try to start a conversation with but there was something about him that was just captivating. Luke sat on the bed next to Mike and put the blunt in his mouth, lighting it he inhaled and suddenly his whole life seemed so… serene, calm, he’d been missing this shit his whole life. With each puff of the blunt he took his short brown hair snaked out of his head, lightening as it went along into a very dirty blonde until it hit his shoulders. His chubby frame began to lessen as he looked down on the bed and saw his beanie lying next to him, he grabbed it and placed it on the mop of hair atop his head as his height settled in at 5’9”.

His plain brown eyes became a similar washed out Blue to Cain’s.

I mean it helped that he knew a good client when he saw one. The blunt came back around to him again and he took a long toke, blowing out smoke rings as his lips thickened, his own cheekbones pushing out as his face grew more gaunt, he was probably 10-15 pounds underweight but he was a good looking dude and at 18 being a good looking guy with access to weed will get you many places he thought to himself as his 5” cock grew in to 7”, losing some girth along the way.

He liked his tattered Nikes, sure they weren’t the best looking but they did their job and they were comfy. His stomach pulled in, his abs faintly visible, his t-shirt’s sleeves grew longer, the neck billowing out at the back as the material got thicker until he was wearing a baggy hoody with nothing else on underneath.
