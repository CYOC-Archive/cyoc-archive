He secretly wished from time to time that he could become a little kid again and just sit at his mother's feet in his pajamas as she told him cute stories about talking animals. Maybe this little baking adventure was his mother's way of making up for it.

    “So what do you want to do after this?” asked Molly. “I thought that we could play some board games. Or maybe we could watch a movie.”
