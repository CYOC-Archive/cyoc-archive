    Wyatt and Kyra had come back from another day at school, the two of them had internally been counting down the days until their grand plan was to enact and for all intents and purposes, their enemies were unaware of their plans.

    "You know it's still unbelievable that I'm the adopted son of an entire vampire nation." Wyatt said as he and Kyra roamed through their location.

    "Yes, and with me at your side as your hand and wife, I feel like we can help progress the vampire community more than ever before. You are after all, the heir to Lord Miro." Kyra said smiling.

    "Not by blood though, that title belongs....to someone else. I promised myself I wouldn't lose control again over it." Wyatt said taking in a deep breath.

    "Who said that you can't be related by blood?" Kyra looked at Wyatt smiling while Wyatt had a confused look on his face as the two of them opened a door into a large room with many vampires inside of it, many of them dressed in robes.

    Towards the front of the room were the five with Duncan, Cassidy, Ken, Natalia, and little Ann who stood closest to Miro dressed in robes with unique colors while Lord Miro stood out among them with the symbol of his grandfather on the front of it.

    "For over 600 years, I've wanted the legacy of my grandfather, Dracula, to carry on. Fate gave me a son who path a road less traveled, but fate also brought me a son who is on the road to becoming what I always wanted. Wyatt, before you took a blood bond with me as an adopted son. Now thanks to Selene and Kyra we can make the bond be of my blood." Miro said as he and Kyra watch Selene and bring out two tables for them both to lay on.

    Selene drew some blood from Miro whilst Kyra drew blood from Wyatt. The two began their enchantment spell and all watched as the blood mixed together making new blood all together and then began finishing their enchantment so the new blood would flow back into Miro and Wyatt. Both of their bodies shook for a moment before they calmed down. Both of them slowly got up from their tables and Miro walked over to Wyatt as he put his hands on Wyatt's shoulders.

    "Welcome....at last to the true bloodline of Dracula, my son." Miro said as Wyatt smiled and began to chuckle with Miro joining him. Everyone in the room smiled at this ceremony, though in the back of Natalia's mind, she seemed somewhat uncomfortable now with these events.
