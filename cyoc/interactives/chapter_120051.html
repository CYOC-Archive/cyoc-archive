    "I'm tired of hiding, I want to show somebody and I felt that you would understand. Since I'm going off to college soon I feel like I should have some fun as a clown."

    "Well I was thinking we could go to school and stuff, and while I doubt you will, maybe even become a changing clown girl like me?" Cassie said slightly hopeful.

    Rachael couldn't think for a moment. She did it know what to think, unfortunately she didn't get a chance.

    "Well too late, I want to share the fun with the mod people. Transform or Dare Rachael!"

    "Uh...well...I don't know?"

    "Well then you get both! Alright my then, you will turn into a transitioning clown like me. You will be able to freely change forms of clown of your human self at will but you will enjoy your clown form more so you will want to be in that form as much as possible. Additionally you will enjoy being a clown like me so much that you will want to share the joy of it with other people. For For you're Dare you will T or D them and make them into clowns that can change form too and give them the same T or D that I'm giving you or just one of them.
