All this made for quite the looker.

Today was a sunny day with moderate cloud cover and a temperature hovering in the upper 70s. In essence, the perfect day for a swim in the mansion's large outdoor pool. As she pulled herself out of the pool after a few laps, Nicole noticed something metal glinting in the hedge that lined that side of the back yard. Toweling off and making sure her pink zebra-stripe bikini was secure, she walked over to the hedge and looked at the foliage. There was something wedged in there, something that she didn't think had been there before. Reaching into the thick vegetation, she pulled out an odd looking device.

    Taking the strange item back to the patio, Nicole turned it over in her hands, careful not to touch anything that might be dangerous. In its most basic form, the device looked like a gun. A very weird-looking gun, mind you, but a gun none the less. It looked like a black squirt-gun pistol with silver trim. The barrel seemed to have a ray-gun attachment with a small sphere on the end and three discs of sorts inbetween the it and the body of the gun. Finally, where the hammer would be on a normal pistol, there was a small LCD screen. Nicole tapped the screen and jumped slightly when it lit up. First, the word "Reducer(tm)" flashed up on the screen. Then, after that vanished, up came a menu. It was a simple menu, with the options for "Grow" and "Shrink" being the primary features. There was also an "Options" and a "Help" options as well, but Nicole ignored those for the time being.

    Nicole wasn't stupid. She wasn't going to risk testing this thing on herself yet. Instead, she started browsing through the menus. A half-hour later, she thought she had a handle on how the thing worked and was ready to test it. Standing back, she selected "shrink" and targeted one of the deck chairs. The gun had a surprisingly complex targeting system that seemed easy to use and with enough failsafes to avoid accidentally shooting oneself. With the chair targeted, she fired. The chair rapidly shrunk to dollhouse size. "Okay," Nicole said to herself, "it worked." She quickly returned the chair to its original size.

    She still had a few days left of house-sitting here. She mostly house-sat alone. However, with this particular house, she was often allowed to have a couple vetted close friends over. She didn't like to abuse the privilege, so she didn't do it very often. However, today was an excellent candidate. She had been getting a little bored anyway, and she knew the two friends she had in mind would love to test out her new toy. This would be so much fun. Nicole couldn't wait to see what this little gun could do.
