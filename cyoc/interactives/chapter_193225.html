    Connor was visiting his parents in the hospital again, only this time, he was wearing a red vest with a necktie for his dance and first date (or second) with Spark.

    "So where's your date son?" Jacob Moreland asked while lying down in his hospital bed.

    "Well, Caleb and Gloria said they were going to help Spark get ready tonight." Connor explained to his parents as there was a knock on the hospital door and Gloria came in first.

    "Your date is ready for you Connor." Gloria smiled as she let Spark come in next with Caleb right behind her.

    "Wow Spark, you look...so....so...radiant." That was the only word that Connor could think of to say as Spark blushed at the compliment.

    "So you'll take her to the dance and come home, no horse playing around with her, right?" Jacob Moreland said with a chuckle.

    "Jacob Moreland." Nina said as she slapped her husband on his good shoulder.

    "We're just going to the dance, dad, that's all." Connor said as Gloria and Spark looked at each other and chuckled.

    "What?" Connor asked them.

    "Oh, I'll tell you in the car. Come on, Caleb's driving us there." Spark said as Caleb led the two to his jeep where Connor and Spark sat in the backseat.

    "Check it out, Gloria let me borrow this tonight." Spark said showing Connor Gloria's ring.

    "Is it okay for you to use that?" Connor asked.

    "She said if things get out of hand, use the ring to undo whatever we do." Spark told Connor.

    "Well, okay."

    "Oh, and one more thing...." Spark said as she kissed Connor on the cheek.

    "You do look really handsome tonight." Spark said as she and Connor both blushed.
