    Consequences Of A Rebellion

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 10 days ago AR O Magic Kid

    The powerful and mighty Cyber Space Warriors were deeply asleep. The Sleepover King watched them, highly amused by what had occurred. To have little boys drop onto him like that, well, it had been a most welcome surprise! Such luck should come to him more often. He would not complain at all. It happened so little! Normally, he would have to coax little boys or girls from their dreams or bedrooms in order to attract them to his land of eternal sleepovers. Going to the human world was never easy and took much, much magical energy. Another way to get children to come was through the help of the fay realm. Fairies and fauns and other creatures were always willing to get him children if they could spare a few from the ones they caught in the intent of making them their own progeny or pets. Their magic allowed them to cross to the human kingdom a lot more easily. In his mind, he found the situation rather unfair. Why would HE, the Sleepover King, have less access to children coming into his own land? HE had a much better time to offer these children than the fae! In his world children could play all day and all night and never grow and have an eternity of fun! What was bad about it? It was the most entertaining. The fay realm possessed nothing of what HE had! Though he did have to admit that when a boy or girl ended up being rebellious and bratty and demanding to grow up - the ridiculous notion and want! - then pawning the ungrateful brat off to the fae was extremely practical.
    And the Sleepover King precisely thought that Alex might be a bundle of trouble. He had been rendered calm thanks to his magic but who knew when he would act up again? He was bound to rebel at some point, and sooner rather than later. Maybe he should just get rid of the problem now, for the sake of sensibility. And then the fae would owe him a favor, right? The Sleepover King smiled and took his decision. Yes, he was indeed right in acting now.

    A few hours later, Alex opened his eyes. He saw at once that he was in a forest drenched in sunlight. That had woken him up. He groaned, completely at a loss. But then his memories flooded back. Luke! Carl! The crazy dimension! He was still a little kid! And in pyjamas, no less!!! Alex looked around. Where was he? Why was he in a forest now? Where were the other two Cyber Space Warriors? Why was he all alone? Where was the fat idiot that he had tried beating? Little did Alex know that he better worry about his more direct surroundings...
