    The Wrong Kind of Random?

    "T-the Chronivac must've totally switched my mindset with Dawn's!" Mr. Dias said aloud, putting one hand to his head as the other remained to hold the Chronivac. "This is so weird, to be honest.... but it could be fun, right?"

    Looking through the Chronivac, Mr. Dias quickly surveyed the changes made by the last randomization session. The changes were somewhat weird, but Mr. Dias supposed that for a device like the Chronivac, weird was the norm. Eva definitely had some semblance of a penis back, while Shae was experiencing having an asshole on both sides of her.

    With his new mindset, Mr. Dias found himself almost disappointed that he got a simple mind change. “The others are getting changes more fun than mine….” He pouted, the expression itself strange on his face. “It’s so unfair…”

    Suddenly, he felt a tapping on his shoulder, the child-minded man turning around to see his daughter Dawn with a mischievous grin on her face. “So, you wanted a better ‘change’, right?” The blue-haired girl asked, a grin spreading across her face. “Well then, ‘Dad’, how about you try that randomizer again?”

    Unbeknownst to Mr. Dias, the latest change had actually Taken away Dawn’s “normalcy” setting and discarded it. Her mischievous nature hadn’t exactly gone away, but now she had the mindset to pull off many more of her schemes.

    Mr. Dias hesitated, before nodding, pressing the randomizer once more. “Okay! How about we go wait out the changes with Eva and Shae, alright?”

    “That would be fine…” Dawn said as the two went up the stairs.

    -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Shae was beginning to hyperventilate in front of the mirror, entirely done with this weirdness and wanting out of anything having to do with the Chronivac. Despite their previous argument, Eva was currently trying to calm down her girlfriend, with little to no success.

    “H-Hey, Shae…” Eva said, trying to make her voice seem soothing as she cuddled up against her girlfriend. “It’s gonna be okay, you’ll see…”

    Just then, however, Mr. Dias and Dawn burst in, the former practically jumping up and down with joy.

    “OMG, guys, we’re all gonna get another change!” He said, grinning from ear to ear, Dawn behind him, her clothes still ill-fitting on her sexier body currently being borrowed from Shae.

    “Really now,” Eva said dryly as Shae began to start panicking again. “What are we swapping this time?”

    “....swap?” Mr. Dias asked his daughter, leading to the other girls in the room to begin to panic, Shae doing so even more.

    “Wait, did you even do a swap?” Dawn asked her father, her eyes wide with worry.

    “I’m not sure if I did…” Mr. Dias said, rubbing the back of his head sheepishly. “I just pressed the closest button that said random….”

    All 3 of the girls looked at each other, a sinking feeling in the pits of their stomachs as the tingling of the Chronivac began to pass through all of them...
