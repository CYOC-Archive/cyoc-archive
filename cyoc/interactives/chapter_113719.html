    Jimmy awoke later, dazed, but still fully cognizant of what had happened. He was determined to get quick retribution against this challenger boar and reclaim his sows. He looked up, Petula, Jacquie, and Aja were in the mud, rooting around. while Stephanie and the boar were in the little farm house loudly mating.

    Jimmy walked up to the three sows. "How long have I been out?"

    The sows looked at Jimmy for a moment, a clear annoyed look on all their snouts, but went back to rooting for food in the mud.

    "Hey! Answer mrrnt-me!" She snorted.

    Petula spoke up: "It on-lee be frrnt-few hour, Jim-mee! Now lrnt-leave alone! We need grrnt-get big and fat for boar." It was clear that even a few hours under the boar's influence had done damage and reverted the girls' intellects to what were apparently natural pig levels.

    "Come again?" Jimmy said.

    "Steph-anie fattest of grrnk all of us, that why al-pha mrrr-mate her!" Said Aja.

    "OINK! If wrrnt-we eat and fat, then we brrreee-come boar mate!" Added Jacquie.

    A loud pair of squeals from the barn indicated Stephanie and the boar had finished their latest round of mating. Ironically the girl least enthused with being a pig had quickly become the most popular sow.

    Jimmy didn't want to concern himself with these dumb squabbles, he only wanted to reclaim what he felt was his. He put his thoughts to work, he imagined himself becoming a larger, more powerful pig. After a few moments he opened his eyes and saw, nothing. He hadn't changed at all. Something was wrong with his powers.

    Stephanie trotted over with a dumb smile on her snout. "Oooiiink hello Jim-mee!" She sneered. "How yrrnt-yuu enjoy nap?"

    Jimmy ignored Stephanie's passive aggressive insults. If he couldn't mate with his sows, then no one else was going to either. He imagined himself and the four girls transforming back into humans. But after a few moments it was clear there would be no change.

    "Srr-something's wrong with my powers! I can't change us back!" He squealed.

    "So? GRRNK why we need be huu-man?" Aja snorted.

    "GRRROINK! Yeah! Sows Get be frr-fat, mrnt-mate, eat, smelly." Petula added, punctuating her point with a loud fart.

    "Me drrn't mind me is sow. OIINK! Me is hrrnt-happy way!" Jacquie cheered.

    "Frrr-ace it Jim-ee, yuu only one who not want be pig bree-cause yuu no get mate fat sow butts!" Stephanie added with venom.

    "Hrrnt! Why yuu near sows! Grrt oo-way!" Grunted the boar in a sudden appearance. Jimmy instinctively fled away from him, prompting the sows to join in a snorting laughter with their new boar.

    Jimmy cried to himself in the corner, he would need to find another way of beating the boar and reclaiming his power.
