    Animalia's Funniest Videos: Scared Silly/Stupid Edition

    While things were calming down from Spookyville, there was one last event, Animalia's Funniest Videos. Most of them were from funny reactions to sinister manifestations or similar events. But what was also showed was how such things were achieved. One such funny thing was Liao's panicking as she and the Five Elements entered the Buena Vista Community Hospital which triggered sudden flickering of the lights.

    Another such thing was a guy trying a "mad scientist's concoction" that his kid made in Franken Castle and then letting out a big "rip", while another got "Nailed" with one of the famous/infamous Do not Open chests, which was programmed to let loose a spine-chilling scream.

    ---

    There were also some funny hunting videos that showed how "macho man hunters" could end up looking like idiots. The funniest one was one guy tried to use a homemade turkey call to attract a turkey, but the only problem was that it worked too well. The call was that of a male, and a larger male came to chase the rival out of his territory. The attack, that had been caught on camera, showed how funny it was, mainly because the hunter's friends were too busy laughing at the turkey's attack on him. Though it did inspire Duluth Trading Company to use the fire hose canvas that they use in their pants in new jean jackets marketed as "stronger than a territorial turkey's beak".

    Other funny hunting videos often involved "epic firing fails", which involved either hunters failing by either falling prey to the massive recoil into either their shoulder, face, etc., or causing the firearm to slip out of the hands; or the most amusing/embarrassing things ever to happen to someone wielding a firearm, the gun's magazine falling out before any round gets out or the gun temporarily jams and fires when your guard is down, missing you by inches.

    One such thing of this kind was one guy using an ultra customized AR-15 "hunting" rifle, but the magazine fell out before he could take a shot at a bull moose, causing it to become aware of him.

    ---

    There were also several incidents of "Karmic Irony" from the colonies, where someone tries to prank an Animalian and ends up the butt of the joke. Occasionally there were the "Macho big mouths" that seem to hog (pig Animalian: "I resent that.") almost everything about their "spot" on the beach. These were seemingly the most vulnerable to one beachside scam: fake suntan lotion that provided no protection from the sun and leaving them redder than cooked lobster.

    ---

    The overall winners of the AFV Halloween Special was, from Third to First Place: "Accidental Discharge, Reload Please." (the epic AR-15 fail); "Are you there, Duluth? It's me, Joe." (The attack on one overconfident hunter by a territorial turkey); and the Grand $10,000 Prize went to Liao and the Five Elements with their video "Flicker Lights, White Frights" (Liao's reaction to the flickering lights in the entrance of Buena Vista Community Hospital during Spookyville).
