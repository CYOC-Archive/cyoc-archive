    After a while, Jeff decides to become a block of modeling clay, “the thought is kinda interesting, I wonder what it'll feel like to be made of clay,” he says to himself, “I don't have to worry about someone coming in, I am all alone at the house for at least four hours, so, I'll set the duration to last that long”.

    So Jeff selects the Clay transformation and selects himself as the target, he sets the transformation to take 30 minutes and the duration to last 4 hours, forgetting to subtract the 30 minutes from the four hours. Jeff leaves his awareness on, wanting to keep his mind have all his senses intact.

    Before confirming the settings, Jeff strips out of his clothes and lies them on his bed, butt naked he walks back to his Laptop. The teen clears his desk and sits down on it, “better to be on my desk than on the floor,” he says to himself, then he confirms the transformation.

    Jeff feels a tingling run through his whole body, he then feels himself shrink, while at the same time his skin starts to feel much more softer and his skin tone also darkens into a light brown color. The teens legs, arms and head all start to shrink into his body, while his torso take on a square shape.

    After 30 minutes, a normal looking block of brown modeling clay is sitting on Jeff's desk, next to his Laptop. Even the “block” does not have Jeff's face, Jeff is still able to see, hear and smell, “man, this feels to weird,” the teen tries to say, but notices that no voice comes out, he then realizes that he can't feel his mouth, 'shit, I thought I forgot something,' he thinks to himself, 'oh well'.

    Jeff the clay block sits motionless on the desk for two hours, 'man... I guess it wasn't a bright idea becoming a block of clay,' he thinks to himself, 'it's super boring if I can't move or do anything. Man, why does my curiosity always make me decide wrongly?'

    As Jeff mentally whines about how bored and stupid he is, he does not hear nor notice the door to his room slowly and quietly open and someone stepping in.
