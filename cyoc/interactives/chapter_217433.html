    Mr Rosaro the school caretaker finally had time to clean out the storage room that the students kept using as a make-out area for some time and if he made it fast he had time to caught the trash pickup for the day.

    When he got to the room he immediately started filling some garbage bags with everything that looked to be old, broken or just simply no good. There was a towel, used condoms, and a lot of other rubbish which he made short work of.

    After clearing out all the rubbish he started to organise the room again when he heard something hit the floor. Taking a good look he seen some kind of medallion which he picked up and put in his pocket for the moment.

    When he finally got the room cleaned up he spent a little time fixing the lock again, this time putting a stronger lock on the door so it might keep the students out.

    When that was done he took the bags of trash to the dumpster and throw them out.

    He was about to leave when he felt the medallion in his pocket so he took it out to look at it again.
