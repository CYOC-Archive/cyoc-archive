    Getting a mobile emitter

    He looks at Alice. "You told me, that you would wear me even when you leave the house. What did you mean by that? You know that we have to stay within the range of the Transform-O-Matic otherwise the transformation will be unstable." Alice smiles. "I didn't think about that. I just meant that I would love to wear you as my regular gym clothes, not just in the house." She giggles. "But I guess that's not possible. Unless we buy a mobile emitter for the Transform-O-Matic. Do you think we should do that?"

    "Yes, absolutely! I have a feeling that the range limit would limit us pretty quickly." He seems quite excited. "Have you looked at the different emitters that are offered?" Alice nods. "Yes, I've looked at them. There are a few different options, but they all seem to be pretty affordable. And they have good reviews." She smiles. "I think we should definitely get one. That way we can explore more possibilities with the Transform-O-Matic."

    "I completely agree with you. But let's go take a shower first. Somehow my crotch is still sticky after the transformation. And I don't think I'm the only one who needs to wash up." Jake grins at her. Alice smiles. "Yeah, maybe we should have a quick shower first." She stands up and holds out her hand to Jake. "Come on, let's shower, and then we'll buy the emitter and explore the Transform-O-Matic some more." Jake takes her hand and gets up. "I can't wait to explore the possibilities together with you."

    While showering together, Jake makes a proposal. "Do you remember that I said that I'd want to be your one-piece swimsuit? What if we do this with the smallest emitter? I think there is a waterproof version. You could wear me and have the emitter on your wrist." Alice thinks for a moment. "That sounds promising. But I have to admit, I'm insecure to wear such an emitter openly. Others will see it and some might recognize it. Then they will know that the only item I wear apart of the emitter, my swimsuit, is someone who is transformed into this swimsuit." Jake hugs her. "I understand. And I think that only people who are into using the Transform-O-Matic will know what this thing on your wrist is. Others will think that it was just some kind of smartwatch."

    "You're right. Would you be OK with being recognized as my husband who is transformed to be my swimsuit?" Alice asks him. Jake nods. "I'd be your swimsuit. I'm sure I won't be the only one transformed into clothes or accessories. And the Transform-O-Matic is sold openly. It's legal and I'm really sure that it will become more and more common publicly. It's the wave of the future. We can do this. And I'm really excited to feel you wearing me in the public pool. I'll be your swimsuit. And maybe there will be others doing exactly the same?" Alice smiles and nods back. "Yes, maybe. And then I would see them and recognize them, too. OK, let's do it." She hugs him again and they continue showering together, laughing and talking. Then they get dressed and head out to the store. They are both excited and happy and looking forward to exploring the possibilities of the Transform-O-Matic together.

    They ask one of the salespeople which model she would recommend and what the differences are between the mobile emitters. Alice asks her if all the watch-sized emitters are waterproof and Jake is curious how long a battery charge lasts. The woman explains to them that there are different emitters for different purposes and prices, but the waterproof version is her favorite. It can be attached to a person's wrist and it will have a battery life of about four to five hours. It also allows for a range that is sufficient for about six meters. "What I really like is that the latest model of these wristband emitters also supports Mental Link. However, this comes at the expense of range and battery life. With Mental Link activated, you'll have roughly three to four hours of use at a range of not much more than three meters. But if that's enough for you, then it's ideal for going out and having transformation fun. And you're right, all the emitters are waterproof." The woman adds that she has a some customers who have bought one of these emitters and use them for transformations as clothing or jewelry. "I'm sure it will become more common to do this openly in public."

    Alice didn't mention that she and Jake talked about transforming him into a one-piece swimsuit but the words of the saleswoman have them both very excited about using the Transform-O-Matic in public. "Are there any downsides to the wristband emitter?" Jake wants to know. The saleswoman shakes her head. "Nope. Beside the loss of range and battery life with Mental Link, it has no other downsides. It will be a good investment if you plan to have fun in public. And I'm sure many will use it for transformations. We are seeing a rise in the use of the Transform-O-Matic and it will become more common publicly."

    Alice looks at Jake. "What do you say? Do you think the price is reasonable? After all, we still have to pay the installments for the Transform-O-Matic. The emitter is not that cheap." Jake nods. "Yes, I think it's worth it. It is a great addition to the Transform-O-Matic. And 75 bucks doesn't really make much difference when you consider the price of the Transform-O-Matic." Alice smiles. "I agree. Let's get one and explore what else we could do with the Transform-O-Matic." She pays with her credit card and thanks the woman. The saleswoman smiles. "You're welcome. I'm sure you'll have a lot of fun with it. And if you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact us." Alice and Jake leave the store, both excited to try out the new emitter.

    "I can't wait to transform you into a one-piece swimsuit and wear you to the pool," Alice says, smiling at Jake. "Oh, I can't wait either," Jake replies, smiling back at her. "I'm excited to feel you wearing me and to be able to communicate with you through the Mental Link." They continue walking, talking about the possibilities of the Transform-O-Matic and the emitter. They both feel like they are on the verge of a new and exciting adventure together. And they can't wait to see what the future holds for them.
