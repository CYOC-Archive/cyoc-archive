    Now the lizardwoman had been getting increasingly ticked off
    with the general unfairness of life and ridiculously high and getting higher lizard mechanics bills for space-tractos.

    So having to burn off antimatter fluid to get her ship back in the
    air in order to start the telemotor engine generators that
    drove the Transporter she needed online in order to beam aboard the hairless ape that had run away from her rather then
    run towards her was costing her and it had ticked her off.
    So quite sensibly sitting in that chair she popped a self relaxation tape into the space tractors tape deck in order to
    calm herself down.

    She was worldly wise enough by now to know that the
    She-Drulk metamorphosis in her didn't start on the visual
    appearance level but down in her lizardwoman DNA.
    Basically her DNA metamorphosized first without her knowing
    it.Then came the new surge of hormones that caused 1001
    minor but visible cosmetic changes,before the heavy duty stuff kicked in.

    So yes,the lizardwomans DNA was metamorphosizing right that
    moment and it was a good thing she had that self relaxation tape
    playing.
    Sometimes the relaxing mood reversed matters,sometimes it
    slowed down the onset of the hormonal rush.

    So the lizardwoman gave you the Keep it Simple and Stupid
    version of gouging space tractor mechanics,space tractors and
    the Slave Auctions on Sirius V.
    Then because she didn't need you gawking at her whilst she
    tried to think,she told you to make yourself scarce and go look at the Moon the space-tractor was currently orbiting.

    So a worried you went to the Observation Deck and looked through the windows at the Moon below the ship.
    Just like the lizardwoman had her problems you had yours,
    trying to avoid being sold off as a lizardwomans sex toy at
    Sirius V.

    Now perhaps it was all that leaking antimatter fluid dripping
    off the lines to the telemotor,or some other thing,but the
    lizardwoman missed the hormonal rush inside her that
    brought about the 1001 cosmetic changes in her body.
    She woke up with a drowsy start and immediately realized something was wrong.The chair gripping at her seemed a little
    tighter.She immediately felt at her scales,which were now a little
    more raised and thicker.She felt at her nostrils,which were
    a little more larger and raised.
    Next her trembling slender,still slender
    fingers brushed against her scalp and felt the slight rises
    under her scaly scalp or the forming horn buds all over up there.
    Her eyes were still blue,but her pupils had narrowed a
    fraction.
    The more advanced metamorphosis always caused her lizard
    teeth to go wobbly in her gums,a prelude to them getting pushed out by a more dangerous dentition of a dragoness.
    And yes,some of her normal teeth were a little looser then before.

    The lizardwoman turned on the self relaxation tape decks
    volume even louder,not to reverse the metamorphosis but to
    mask the sounds of the transformation if it did occur.
    Having done all this sort of stuff before the lizardwoman stripped out of her lizardwoman skin suit ,which revealed even more of the 1001 cosmetic changes caused by her hit of
    dragoness hormones such as shifting natural camaflouge patterns in her scales.
    As a lizardwoman her species had evolved in one enrivonment
    the hot brownish sunny desert,while her dragon granpa's
    species had evolved more in a green swampy and greyish alpine
    environment.
    So her body's natural brown blotches were altering to
    something more greyish in colour.
    The slowly metamorphosizing lizrd woman,whose own
    lizard females would have picked out plenty of other subtle
    changes in her
    skinny dipped out of the control cabin and sneaked down the
    hallway past the Observation Deck where you
    still stood and headed to a specially designed and secretize room where she kept her strong anti-metamorphosis items
    such as relaxation puffers,tooth braces and anti-hormonal shots.

    This time she had managed to catch the onset of her metamorphosis in time and if you had
    walked in on her a half hour later you would have found
    her rubbing at one of her lizard feet,which was pretty sore
    after threatening to reaarange itself into a dragonesses
    digitigrade foot without her permission.
    All around the couch where she sat wincing,on the floor
    lays plenty of puffers,dripping spilled bottles of clear anti hormones and spiny wire tooth braces.

    Frankly she looked like death warmed over but she
    was still a lizardwoman
    albeit one with aching everything.
