    It was late in the night before the doorbell rang and Luigi the young adult who lives in the apartment where it rang stood up to answer the door questioning himself "who would come by at this time?" He answered the door and waited as the delivery guy went to hand him the package, but before Luigi could say or ask anything, the delivery guy just left leaving the package by him.

    Luigi had walked inside with the package and was wondering what might be inside it. As he opened it he found a CD and a note that said "this is the Chronivac 4.0" he turned the note hoping it would say more, but it didn't so he just sat there for a few minutes looking at the CD.

    When he got bored of just sitting there he had decided to give it a try and went to install what ever was on that CD. And when he did he started to install the Chronivac and left his room to get something to drink.

    When he returned the program was already running and he nearly choked at his drink when he saw the options it showed him, his eyes where fixated at a few options took him by surprise, and they were....

    Inanimate
    Species
    AI
    Reality altering

    He was very curious if any of these options would work but he knew that some of them might be risky since he lived alone and probably no one would even notice if he would be gone.
