    she was surprised at how many flavors there were. "you do want ice cream right?" the man said. "ah... yes... um, one sea salt double ice cream. then I would like a Banana ripple. pride of Savannah... and hunky mint" she asked. "thinking of a man are you" he grinned as he grabbed the ice cream from the cart. "usually these are popular with men. I assume it's a boyfriend".

"well I'm sorry for your loss. hopefully these will help" he said as he handed them to her. looking down she reached for her purse. "okay how much is..." looking up she was shocked. the man was gone.
