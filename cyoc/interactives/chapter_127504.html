    For Rachel it is already clear that she wants to spend her time on the island as a farm animal. However, the resort constantly organizes special events and also offers special deals. Despite from the financial aspect, these events and deals allow you a much more intense experience.
    For example with a 20% discount you can spend your time as a chicken in a common coop together with the ordinary poultry during the “poultry power week”. Or you can take part at the monthly livestock auction. You can set the minimum bid yourself, but if someone win the bidding you have to stay as an animal and you will become the property of your new owner. The very thought sends a tingling sensation through her whole body.
    But this event is not the only possibility to join the livestock auction on stage. In the resort brochure Rachel read that all guests who stay longer than booked will be sold at that livestock auction at some point. So if she can't change back or if she decided for a permanent transformation she's gonna spend the rest of her life as a simple animal on an ordinary farm. That idea excites and frightened her at the same time.
    “Looks like a hard decision” a fat man in the seat next to her said.
    “Yes, definitely” she said distantly, still feeling a bit of apprehension welling up in her gut. Rachel hoped her nervousness about the fact that she’d be changing into an animal soon didn’t show too much, but the man still picked up on it.
    “I guess this is your first time here?” he asked gently.
    A thin smile crept across Rachel’s lips. “Yeah. You?”
    The man smiled back and bit his lips for a second as he slowly shook his head. “Not exactly. I am here to check out the livestock for the next auction”
    A shocked look jolted over Rachel’s face. “You are here to bid for new livestock?” she exclaimed. She glanced off to the side before she asked, “So, have you also bought former humans?”
    The man let out a soft laugh and looked back at Rachel as if she should know better. “Of course, most animals sold on the auction are former humans. I've met many women here I acquired later as simple animals for my farm."
    "I think you'd also fit well to my farm." he added with a grin. "According my experience attractive women like you become excellent breeding sows." He said and gave her a lascivious look.
    Rachel doesn't like the fat farmer, but becoming someone else's property provided her with a panicked and secretive excitement. "Be that as it may, but I'm not planning to end up as anyone's livestock." She replied with cool tone.
    With a loud laugh the farmer answered. "Many women said that before. Poor ones who decided to become a prime beef cattle and get pregnant during their stay. You can imagine they regret that they didn't choose a dog or cat when they were taken away to be fatten up."
    With no answer Rachel returns to the counter. The farmer had frightened her but nevertheless she stick to her decision and books...
