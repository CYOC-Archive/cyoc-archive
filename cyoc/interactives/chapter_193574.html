    Gloria's third delayed date night

    Caleb Moreland had not been having it easy against the vampires recently, he had still felt guilty about not being able to stop save his dad from losing an arm, and now he felt like he could've done more in assisting his mother with getting his dad out of the hospital. Jacob and Nina Moreland both agreed that their son needed his spirit lifted up, so they asked Gloria to call him up and ask for a real date night, or at least their third attempt for a real date night.

    Caleb had come over in his jeep to Gloria's house to see Gloria waiting outside just as he drove up.

    "Hey....you look great." Caleb complimented as Gloria thanked him and they were about to get into his jeep when another car pulled up.

    "Is this trouble?" Caleb asked.

    "No, that's my sister and sister in-law with my niece." Gloria said as Katie and Kellie came up holding little Morgan while Katie held her daughter's bag of belongings like diapers and bottles in another hand.

    "Hey you two, Gloria, did you forget what night it is?" Katie asked.

    "Uh....date night?" Gloria answered innocently.

    "Correct. Our date night, we called you a week ago and you said you were free." Kellie said as she held up a note from Gloria in her own handwriting which read 'I, Gloria, agree to babysit Morgan on this date...' and the date following was now the night in question.

    "Anyway, Caleb and I...." Gloria began before Katie interrupted and shook Caleb's hand.

    "Caleb Moreland, right? Nice to properly meet you. I know we've been having that vampire business but we can't forget our family. I believe you met the love of my life, my beautiful wife, Kellie. And this of course is our precious daughter, Morgan Carlson." Katie introduced with Kellie holding up little Morgan and handing her to Caleb.

    "But we need..." Gloria interrupted again.

    "All of her things? Correct, that is why you're a great babysitter. Here are her diapers, her bottles, her stuffed toys, the little unicorn is her personal favorite, her blanket, don't let her stay up late, bed time is 8:30 sharp, not after, and one more thing: Caleb since this is your first time babysitting our adorable little girl let us make one thing perfectly clear: if you make our daughter unhappy in anyway, we have ways of making you unhappy. And you don't want to see me when my daughter is upset." Kellie threatened.

    "Bye bye our sweet little bundle of magic and joy! Mommy loves you!" Katie said as she kissed Morgan on the forehead as she giggled.

    "Momma loves you too honey. Be a good girl for Gloria and mister Caleb now." Kellie said as she kissed Morgan on the cheek and waved goodbye as Morgan smiled and waved goodbye to her mothers.

    "What just happened?" Caleb asked standing there holding Morgan.

    "My sister has bad timing and our third date night out has taken another turn again. Come on, let's bring her into the house. Be careful with her, she is a magic baby after all." Gloria said.

    "What do you mean by....where did she get that cookie?" Caleb said surprised.

    "No Morgan, no cookies until after dinner." Gloria said taking the cookie from little Morgan as she pouted.
