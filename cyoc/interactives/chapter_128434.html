He walked over to the director of the facility (a frail looking man with short blonde hair, blue eyes, and wearing a white lab coat).

    But before the director could get a word out he was grabbed by the collar of his shirt and lifted off his feet by the man in black. "Director Wells what the hell is going on down here" the man screamed obviously annoyed. Slowly being strangled Wells gasped for air before saying "Test Subject #36 escaped, we were able to capture It but not before It contaminated the water supply."

    "THE WATER SUPPLY THAT FILTERS BACK UP TO THE LAKE" the man yelled as he through the director across the room "HOW COULD YOU LET THIS HAPPEN!" Wells coughed trying to catch his breath before saying "Mr. Van Kleiss were working as fast as possible but it will take some time before the virus is fully purged from the lake." "Your lucky that there are no rivers running out of this lake" Van Kleiss said beginning to calm down.

    "How did Subject #36 escape" Van Kleiss asked. "Were still looking into it sir" Wells replied "however we must keep in mind that before the "incident" She was the director of this futility before me." "it's likely that It still has knowledge of the facility's layout despite the perverted state of It's mind.

"The "Heart of Darkness" is that what your calling it" Van Kleiss interrupted looking up at a monitor displaying an image of a massive black crystal being held within a containment field.

    "As I was saying" Wells said "do to the previous director's "enthusiasm" she exposed herself to the virus and went on a rampage throughout the facility." "fortunately she was stopped all infected personnel were eradicated and Subject #36 was captured for further study."

    "As for the virus itself" Wells added "it seems to have a gestation period of 24 hours." "after which time it will begin to affect the mind of the individual resulting in an exponential increase in their sex drive." "Soon after they will begin to transform into what can only be described as a demon." "Interestingly subjects always seem to take on feminine traits" Wells added.

"We have also discovered that animals react differently to the virus, becoming more aggressive and eventually becoming more monstrous in appearance" he added. "In that case we'll need to eradicate all possible vectors through which the virus can spread" Van Kleiss said.

    "Send all available personnel to the surface and begin exterminating anything that could potentially be infected" Van Kleiss demanded as he began to leave the room. "But sir what if there are people in the area" Wells asked. Van Kleiss stopped and glared at the director before saying "eliminate all potential vectors do I make myself clear Mr. Wells?"

    "Y..yes sir" Wells said reluctantly. "Clean up this mess" Van Kleiss said as he left the room "or you'll find that you have more in common with Subject #36 then simply being this facility's former director."
