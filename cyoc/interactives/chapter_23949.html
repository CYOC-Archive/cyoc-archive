    Dressed for success

    You really find your eyes drawn to the red polka dotted dresses for some reason. The thought of meeting Minnie Mouse make a bit excited.

    "Well, your clothes are all wet..."

    Yes, they are, you agree with yourself. The thought of putting on such damp clothes again depresses you.

    "Maybe just a pair of clean underwear..."

    Yes, why not, you open up a nearby dresser and pull out a pair of cotton panties. The satiny fringe along the bottom is a bit girly, but at least they are clean and dry. You pull them up your legs, and and snap the waistband when they are on tight.

    "Don't they fit nice? But how embarrassing to be seen wearing them..."

    You glance around the room feeling alarmed. You really don't want to be caught wearing girly panties, the thought of someone seeing them is mortifying. Finding the red polka dotted dress in your hands you quickly slip it over your head, and pull it down. It barely covers the bottom edge of the panties, but it does cover them.

    "Now lets finished getting dressed......"

    Getting dressed, yeah, almost dressed for the day. Your feeling a bit confused, but your only partly dressed, and its going to be a long day. You smile when you think about meeting Mickey Mouse. You slip on a hair bow, a pair of the large yellow heeled shoes, and finally a pair of the white gloves.

    You go look at your reflection in the mirror. Your eyes are growing rounder and blacker. In fact all your skin is turning a nice shade of black, except for your pink face. "I'm Minnie?", you ask in the cute voice that has been speaking to your for the last couple of minutes. You can't help but giggle as the transformation completes, and you skip out into the main part of the house.
