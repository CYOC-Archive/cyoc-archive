    This could be bad...

    added 10 years ago A I O

    "Don't worry, Aaron," Alice began, leaning down to look at the plushified Aaron at eye level. "I'm not going
    to sell you, or throw you away, or give you to my dog or anything like that!" She picked him up and made her
    way over to the living room. "Trust me! You'll be with me the whole time."

    *Still don't feel too good about this,* Aaron thought to himself.

    "You never were a fan of teddy bears and dolls and stuff like that, so I thought I'd turn you into one so
    you'd know what it feels like for a change. And don't worry, I won't leave you like that for very long. Maybe
    a few days, tops."

    The very second she stopped talking, her body began to glow, before turning semi-transparent. Before she had
    time to react, she was gone. She had disappeared right before Aaron' unmoving eyes, as he felt himself fall
    onto the couch.

    This, of course, made Aaron nervous. *Alice? Alice, where have you gone?*

    *Aaron, is that you?*

    Aaron would have jumped, had he been able to, at the sound of the voice that randomly popped into his head.
    *Alice! You can hear me?*

    *Apparently...* She sounded like she was struggling. *Why can't I move?*

    *What are you talking about...?*

    She continued to struggle, making lots of annoying grunts and tiny squeals. *And why am I looking up at the
    ceiling...?*

    *Funny,* Aaron replied, *I'm looking at the ceiling, too... Wait, you don't think...*

    *I think we're both sharing the same body...*

    Aaron began to shout. *You mean we're both stuck in here now?! H-how long did you setthe timer?*

    *Timer?* Alice was beginning to sound worried. *I didn't know you were supposed to set the timer. I figured
    you could turn back whenever you wanted...*

    With the both of them trapped in the same plushie body, and no-one knowing of what happened to them, Aaron was
    beginning to wonder if he truly was stuck like this for good. And if he was, he'd be going through it with his
    girlfriend occupying the same body, constantly nagging and whining and talking. *I hope I lose my sanity
    soon.*
