    Jane watches “Mayumi” leave, ‘something tells me, that something is not right, but I can’t seem to figure out what. Did something go wrong with the transformation? I hope not!     “Mayumi” blinks, and looks up at the boy, who is half a head taller than her, “s-sorry… what was the question?”

    Raúl chuckles, “I asked, what are you doing out here,” he repeats his question, “didn’t you want to spend the evening with my sister and her friends, and especially Jane?”

    The “girl” nervously rubs the back of her neck, ‘wh-why am I feeling so nervous in his presence all of a sudden,’ she asks herself, “I… this is all new to me. I feel a little overwhelmed, so I decided to take a little walk and catch a breather,” she then says.

    “I see,” Raúl says, “would you mind if I join you? Especially since you don’t know your way around, and it is starting to get dark.”

    Jeff/”Mayumi” is about to decline and tell Raúl that she would like to be by herself, but the more she thinks about it the more she feels as if it wouldn’t be such a bad idea, ‘after all,’ she thinks to herself, ‘he is right. I shouldn’t go out by myself, what if I get lost or something even worse happens?’

    The girl looks at the boy, “I… I guess it wouldn’t be a bad idea,” she says.

    Raúl smiles, “great,” he says, “let me just get dressed and grab my jacket,” he then hurries into his room. The boy soon reappears, now wearing a plain blue t-shirt and a jeans jacket, the two of them then head out of the house. “I know a nice spot,” Raúl says, “it is a place I always go to when I need some alone time and to relax. It’s not far from here.”

    Jeff/”Mayumi” thinks for a while, “okay,” she then simply says, “lead the way”.

    Raúl smiles, “follow me then,” he says and they head down the street.

    As they walk, Jeff/”Mayumi” can’t help but feel her confusion grow, ‘why am I all of a sudden feeling attracted to Raúl,’ she asks herself, ‘he is a handsome boy and seems nice, but I can’t shake off the feeling that I shouldn’t be feeling this way. What is going on?’
