    Spider-Cop?

    added by ALionetti 3 years ago A BM

    Officer Brody decided based on the fact he was a fan of spider-themed superheroes to go ahead and make himself 20% Spider, see what effect that had on the picture in the little device. He was immediately confronted with a length and duration screen, making Brody pause. He wondered if this was also a component in some kind of online game. He didn't know how good he was with that. He decided to still go through with it. He put in for length of the transformation 3 hours, deciding maybe that'd be represented in game time rather than real time or something...

    ...And seven days in duration. He figured he wanted some time to explore whatever this meant. He was looking forward to figuring out whatever it was that this thing did. He was really feeling pretty excited! Grinning, the cop waited to see what would come of this. The loading screen was annoying, and he saw the screen shift into a rotating model of himself, no changes yet. He scratched his head a little and wondered when this was gonna start.

    It displayed a ticking down clock of three hours, stating in a machine monotone, "THREE HOURS TO TRANSFORMATION COMPLETION".

    Again Officer Brody was left curious if that was in game hours or in real hours. But he was starting to wonder - was this a game at all? He was not seeing any more screens. What was going on here---?

    He grunted as he felt something like a punch in the gut, though nowhere near the worst thing he ever felt. He decided he needed to head home before anything worse happened - just in case he got sick for some reason. He felt an itchy sensation in a few spots on his sides, and his head felt kinda achy, but at the same time his groin and ass were feeling tighter for some reason. He wondered what it was...

    ...but decided he could figure it out in the upstairs bathroom at home, which was not too far away. He winced and felt something on his head shift a little. He groaned and quickly headed inside, being thankful he lived the bachelor life and hurried up to his bathroom. He needed to get this figured out.

    Tearing off his clothes in the bathroom, Officer Brody saw the results of his decision.
