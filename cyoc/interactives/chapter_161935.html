    Only a few days ago she had been a beautiful girl, whose only work she had to do was learning in school. Now she was a scared gray donkey, who was forced to perform hard labor for the rest of her life and was treated as if she was expendable.

    Tomorrow she and the other jennies would be working even deeper underground and were likely not to see the sun for a couple of days.

    "YOU REFUSE TO WORK, SO I WHIP YOU GOOD!!" Emil angrily shouted at the helpless donkey.
