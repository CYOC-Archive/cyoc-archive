    Still hungry

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago O

    It is a little dark, and you kind of want to sleep, but even after eating this wolf, you are still hungry. You figure out that it must be your new spider body that craves for food, and you have no other way but satiate its hunger. There is no way that something is going to fall into your web just now, you think. So how to get lots of food so quickly at night?
    You suddenly figure it out. It was a wolf what attacked you right now, and wolves never go out alone. Its pack must be near somewhere.
    It was time for a hunt.
    You begin to walk in the direction from which the first wolf came from. You pay much attention to what you see, and what you hear, but there are no signs of any wolves close. Then an idea strikes you. You climb onto the nearest tree, and look down, scouting the area around you. You then see it. An entire pack of wolves in between the bushes. You get off the tree, and start walking towards them.
    You walk inside a bush and start looking at the pack. There are five wolves. You can't fight them all at once. There has to be a way to isolate each one from another. You look closely, and notice that three of them are really close to themselves. Suddenly, a plan forms in your head.
    You turn around, and aim your butt at the trio of wolves. You then peek out of the bush, and shoot a giant mass of sticky web at them. You are lucky enough to immobilize them all with one shot. You smirk, and then you jump out of your hiding spot. The two remaining wolves instantly rush at you. You turn around again, and you shoot another mass of your web, hitting one of the wolves right at the front paws, pinning it to the ground. You then use your six arms to stop the second one. It growls and barks at you, but you block its paws with your spider arms, and his muzzle with your human arms. You then move your mouth closer to the wolf's neck, and then you bite it, injecting your venom. Your deadly toxin kills the wolf in seconds. You then come to the one that you have immobilized last. You squat over it, and open its muzzle with your strong hands. You then open your mouth, and you move it just above the wolf's open muzzle. You stick out your poisonous canines, and you release your toxin down the wolf's throat. You watch it die, and then you come over the trio of wolves, still stuck in your clingy web. You kill each one with a bite and a dose of your lethal venom. You won.
    Now it was the time for the feast.
