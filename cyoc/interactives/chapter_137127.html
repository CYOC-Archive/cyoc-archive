    Almost 4 years ago, the owner of the magic shop threw out some stock in the back that wasn’t of any use. He threw out a dusty old broom, an old magic toaster, and a small locked box. The stock was thrown out and sent to the garbage. The stock was all crushed and taken to the dump, except that box was crushed open, out fell a semi-transparent stone. The stone fell onto the road where it was run over by cars and travelled maybe a mile over the course of a month. It flipped onto a sidewalk near a school where it was left in the dirt of a nearby house.

    Now four years later in the winter, a young boy of the name Carter was on his way home from the nearby school when he noticed something just in the reflection of the sun. His curious nature led him to look at what it was, he saw a semi-transparent stone. He picked it up in his winter gloves and walked home. As he got home he greeted his mother, father, younger sister, and older brother. He went into his bedroom, took off his gloves, then took his cell phon rout of his pocket, looking in both pockets he put his hands in them at the same time. He grabbed his phone while in the other pocket he grabbed the stone. He looked at it in his bare hand where upon he heard an unfamiliar voice. He turned around in surprise and saw a woman in her twenties with bright blue hair, very pink lips, black eyes, and a very average figure. He was surprised to find a twenty year old in his room but it wasn’t the most unusual person he’d ever found in his room, he was just not used to having someone he didn’t know in his room and even so a moderately attractive one with bright blue hair.

    “Hello Carter, I’m.. well you haven’t given me a name yet. What’ll it be??” She introduced,
    “Oh, well..” the startled 14 year old said, “what do you mean ‘given me a name’??”
    “Well I’m your personal.. well as I’ve been called, Genie, but I don’t work like that,”
    “Uh huh, go on,” still in mild shock,
    “Am I scaring you, let me pick someone more of your style,” her body started to morph, becoming younger, a 14 year old with bright blue hair, very pink lips, and an average figure, for her age that is.
    “I guess that’s better..” now a bit more on edge, “what were you saying about yourself??” Trying to move away from what she just did,
    “Ah yes, so as I was saying,” in a slightly less matured voice, “I am not a genie in that sense, but I will do as you command me, I can make anything you want happen in any way you want, even change physical objects and people, including changing their mind and personality. I can even create a person out of nothing, just to let you know,”

    Carter was a bit flustered by the sudden fact that he could change anything. He wondered what he could ask her to change, “Could you change my pen??” He picked it up and put it in front of her,
    “How would you like me to??” Her face changed to confused,
    “Oh well.. you can choose,” he said with slight stutter in his voice,
    “Yes of course,” the pen suddenly grew to the same size as Carter, it caused his arm to lose grip of it and it hit the ground with a large bang. His mother yelled from downstairs, “Carter?? You okay??”
    “Yes I’m fine, I just dropped something,” he yelled back,
    “Okay, be careful!!” His mother finished,
    “Can you by chance sound proof the room,” he said to make sure his mother, or father, couldn’t hear if anything and happened,
    “Yes of course, but are you sure since I foresee this ending badly,”
    “Oh, well then, never mind,” he stood their aimlessly for a minute or so, making awkward eye contact with the girl, she responded with positive facial expressions,
    “Would you like to try some of my uses??” She said to Carter to break the silence,
    “Yeah, sure, wait what’s your name??” he said back,
    “You still haven’t chosen it,” she said, “feel free to choose any,”
    “Well, I’ll all you.. uhm.. Quinn,”
    “Great, have you any ideas of what I can do for you, I can suggest some if you’d like,”
