    "Don't know yet". You reply as you go back the instructions. "I hadn't really thought about it. But it won't be a bird. Not yet anyways".

    You both sit in silence for the next few minutes. When you finish reading, you close the instruction booklet and put it on the desk next to your laptop. Claire was, it is pretty simple really. It's then that you have an idea. You decide to take a chance and let her decide what your first transformation should be.

    "How about you decide for me"? You say with a mischievous grin. "Keep it something simple though".

    Claire initially looks surprised. Then she returns your mischievous grin.

    "You trust me"? She asks. "Wow, I'm honoured".

    "Anything? No matter what it is"? She asks.

    "YES! You're making me nervous now. Just do it". You say irritably".

    "Ok". She replies "What should I do with you? I know, I've got just the thing".

    "Who is the subject to be transformed? My brother Jeff. When it does it's scan, it'll identify you as Jeff. I'm not sure how, but it will".

    "What're you turning me into"? You ask nervously.

    "You'll see". She replies with a grin. "It's a surprise.

    You watch over the next few minutes as Claire goes through various questions and instructions. You want to go and see what she's doing, but you manage to resist the urge. Despite being nervous, you really want this to be a surprise. At one point, the emitter clicks and lets out a soft beep. A pale blue light fills the room which you realise is the scan. After about a minute, the light dissipates and Claire says;

    "When do you want the transformation to begin? Erm...thirty seconds. Hit F5 to confirm. Confirmed".

    Claire spins the chair and looks at you with a grin.

    "There". She says "All done. In a few seconds, you'll be something other than a boy".

    You nervously smile at her but stay quiet. On the screen, you can see a clock that's counting down. It's now down to fifteen seconds. You start to feel a little scared, but resist the urge to cancel the transformation. There's no point having this thing if you back out when you try to use it. You watch the clock as it counts from five to zero, then the emitter emits the pale blue light again. Almost immediately, you feel a strange tingling throughout your body, similar to pins and needles. Then your vision blurs and you hear Claire gasp and then giggle. You're not sure how long it takes exactly, no more than ten seconds you guess. but you find yourself in darkness. You feel as though you're wrapped in something soft and warm, with a little bit of weight pressing down on you. You try to move, but you're completely paralysed, something that initially makes you panic. You manage to calm yourself down and realise that you've turned into something inanimate, though you don't know what yet.

    A bright light appears as whatever was covering you is removed. Within seconds you can see that it was the t-shirt you had been wearing, which has now grown to gigantic. You quickly realise that you've got it wrong. The t-shirt hasn't grown, you've shrunk. Without knowing what you are though, you have no way of knowing how big you are now. A shadow appears over you and you hear Claire giggle again.

    "Wow, that is EXACTLY what I had in mind". She says. "Now this might freak you out, but don't worry, you'll return to normal in two hours".

    "Ok". She says. "Are you ready to see what you are now. Here we go".
