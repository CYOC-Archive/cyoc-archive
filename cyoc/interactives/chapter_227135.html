    A Swamp Monster Costume

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 9 days ago A BM O Mythological Magic

    When Mark came out, you barely recognized him. He was dressed as a swamp monster and that costume did not look like it was made with just plastic and a pair of cheap flippers on the feet that made onlookers just feel pity for the wearer. It was made of latex and you just made out Mark's mouth and eyes from the elaborate costume. It had a greenish and dark blue tinge and this swamp monster resembled the creature from the Black Lagoon movie from the 50's. It had sharp claws, a fish tail and fangs. The effect was awesome and rather realistic. This thing was a work of art and you couldn't believe it was not on a movie set instead.
    "Wow Mark!" you exclaimed. "This costume is absolutely amazing! You look great!"
    Mark smiled from behind the fake fangs.
    "I thought it looked pretty good too. The realism on this costume is just great and I'm sure to get rewarded for my efforts."
    You wished you could have a costume as cool because Mark is going to get many congratulations for sure. Luckily, Mark makes you feel better as it must show on your face.
    "You know, there are loads of awesome costumes in here! Try to grab one with an underwater theme too, like that we can spend the party together as a themed pair! Take your time and join me there!"
    You nod and thank him. Actually, Mark's plan is great! You'll feel much more confident that way! Mark leaves and you search for a costume with an aquatic theme that would match his.
