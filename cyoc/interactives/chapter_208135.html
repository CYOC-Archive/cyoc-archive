    It didn't take you long to get your body back, due to regaining the use of your magic.
    You were finally back where you were supposed to be, and gave your body a good stretch, before looking down at the pig.
    It was only an empty body now, with it's soul mixed into your own and it's personality surpressed.
    The body disappeared in a flash of smoke after you snapped your fingers.
    The cooks would be surprised about the carcass lying in front of the kitchen's back door, but there was no other use for such an inferior specimen, other than food. How dare that beast even dream of mounting you?
    Only the best man in the kingdom had the right to breed the queen, and it was far from that standard.
    You had an idea as that thought went trough your mind and called for an assistant. 'I will hold a contest to find the best man!'
    It only took moments for someone to arrive. You looked at him coldly and spoke with authority in your voice.
    "I have decided to hold a contest to find the best pig in the kingdom." 'Wait why did i say pig?'
    The assistant bowed deeply. "Your majesty is truly wise. Such a competition is sure to spark competition and encourage the economy."
    He then asked carefully. "Your majesty, may i ask what kind of prize you intend to present to the victor?"
    You shook your head and stopped questioning yourself to focus on the conversation.
    "The winning pig shall be presented to me and kept in the castle, while the owner will be presented a large prize of some sort."
    You thought for a moment before continuing. "I will leave the exact prize up to you or the organizers of the contest."
    You crossed your arms in front of you. "But keep in mind that i said 'best', not 'fattest'." He bowed deeper. "Of course your majesty."
    It only took a dismissive wave of your hand, and the assistant ran off to start organizing the contest.
    "They will likely need at least a few days to gather the participants, perhaps even weeks... i suppose i can wait that long to meet my mate."
    You blinked, mildly confused. "Mate?" 'Only the best may breed the queen.' "I suppose the winner should be the best..."
