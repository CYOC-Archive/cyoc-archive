    Something other then sulphur and brimstone was stinking up
    the air here.

    So in strolled the Devil and his daughter Margaret Cloverseed,who
    though they looked perfectly human,were actually older then the
    Universe by a few Eternities.

    The board room was a fairly huge cavern with the board room table
    and human sized chairs in its center and all the newbie monsters
    coiled all around it on ledges .

    Some of them had already done two or three tours-of-duty
    in Hell,and hence knew all about being bug eyed Hellspawn
    wading through pools of human excrement ,mating and all the rest during
    their tours,and wisely kept the tour-of-duty stuff to themselves.

    Let Old Nick and Margaret Cloverseed sell THAT to the
    suckers bellowing away,spitting fire and flapping their wings in
    bravado and bluster.

    multiple Earth's shone on the cavern wall.

    Underneath each Earth was a Year number and a Population
    number,starting about 5 million BC and ending with 1999AD,
    current times.

    Margaret shone her laser pointer at the various Earths
    and made sure everyone got the message.
