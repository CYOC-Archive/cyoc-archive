    While “Jess” and Mason are getting ready to watch the movie, Jeff’s little sister Mandy sneaks into Jeff’s room.

    When she enters she notices that the Laptop is on and that a weird looking game is running, curiously, Mandy climbs onto the chair to get a closer look at the game.

    On the screen, there is a 3D image of a house with the roof missing, but she is able to see each floor and room of the house, “weird, looks exactly like our house,” the little girl says, she then notices two characters in the living room of the model with names stating “Jess” and “Mason” written over the heads of the figures, each with a green diamond floating above them. Mandy then notices a third figure on the second floor, also with a green diamond above it’s head and the name “Mandy” written below it. The girl tilts her head, “what a weird game,” she says, “why do the characters have my sister’s, her friend’s and my name? Also… it kinda reminds me of the game “Sims”… maybe it’s some kind of new version?”

    Curiously, Mandy moves the mouse over the character of Mason, when she clicks on “it”, a window with selectable options, like “kiss”, “hug”, “cuddle”, “undress”,... slides open. Mandy for “Mason” to lie his arm around “Jess”, she then sees a window appear next to “Jess” with two options: “accept” and “decline”. Mandy selects “accept”, she then decides it’ll look cuter if “Jess” would lie her head on “Mason”.

    Mandy giggles, “this game is super fun,” she says, she then moves the mouse back to “Mason” and opens the action window, “time to make it a little bit more interesting,” she says and selects “kiss”, she then watches as the “Mason” figure leans forward and plants his lips on “Jess”, then the same “reaction” window next to “Jess” reappears, this time Mandy selects “decline” and watches as the “Jess” on the screen suddenly pushes “Mason” away and leaping to her feet.

    Mandy’s eyes widen and she gasps, covering her mouth with her hands, when she watches “Jess” kick “Mason” in the groin and hearing the painful wince echoing through the house.

    ‘Shit, shit, shit,’ Mandy thinks to herself, ‘I… I got to hide!’
