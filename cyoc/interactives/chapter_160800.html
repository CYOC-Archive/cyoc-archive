She needed to escape before Tony and she joined the other donkeys.

She could see some of her friends there but now with long ears, tails and hooves. Pam and Tony decided to join the crowd as that seemed like their best option for escape, so she grabbed Tony’s hand to keep them from getting separated, but Pam quickly pulled her hand back as she felt a hard, bony mass instead of his fingers. Looking back at her boyfriend, she recognized a pair of black donkey hooves getting out of his arms.

They did their best to push through the changing crowd as they approached the front gate, but their hopes of escape were dashed as they found the gate locked. Without much time to think, Pam pulled Tony along as they tried to find another escape route. But as they began to run, a panic-stricken guy, who boasted a donkey head like many others, bumped into them, knocking them to the ground. Pam quickly got up and dusted herself off, but her heart sank as she looked down to see Tony struggling.

    “Oh Pam! I can’t get up! Help me!”, he shouted at her. Determined not to give up, she helped Tony to his feet, but after wobbling on his feet, trying to stand upright, he fell forward onto his hooves with a loud clop. “Noooooooooo! I can’t stand up! I’m stuck! I’m HEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEE HAWWWWWWWWWWW!”

Looking around the crowd, there were now more donkeys than people as the transformations continued to run their course. More and more donkeys were being wrangled up as well, which added another wrinkle to their careful escape. Not only did she have to keep Tony upright, she had to do so without alerting the guards that were capturing both the donkeys and the changing people.

    After a while, as they kept in the shadows to avoid detection, she felt a prickling sensation on the back of her neck. Looking back, out of the corner of her eye, she saw brown fur growing on Tony’s arms, which now looked more like donkey legs than human arms. Unfortunately, her momentary lapse of concentration cost them as a guard jumped at them from around the corner, knocking Tony out of her grasp and onto all fours.

Unfortunately, since she was looking back at Tony, she didn’t see the baton of another guard come across and hit her square in the head. In an instant, she fell to the ground and everything went black.
