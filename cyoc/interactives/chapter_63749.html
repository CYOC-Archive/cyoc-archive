    After pondering about it and shooting down several ideas in her head that at first sounded kinky and funny but
    would draw too much attention to her apartment, Rain decided upon something relatively unique. She wanted to
    see what would happen if she "split" Chris off into multiple people. 'Does the Chronivac even allow that?' she
    wondered. After a few minutes of searching, she did indeed discover that there was a sub-program allowing for
    multi-task transformations. Perfect. Rain would be killing off two birds with one stone this way. The first
    split would be "Jayne", the woman that she had made up to Sunny to get her off the scent. Jayne's implanted
    memories would be of Rain's friend and co-worker and they would instruct her to leave the apartment
    immediately to return to wherever her home was in the new reality. Next, Rain would turn Chris himself into
    the little sister of Sunny, named Moon. She would be staying in the dorms just to get a feel of college
    life(she would be 16 and attending college early). The third split would be of Moon's pet kitty cat.

    But Rain decided to take a detour first and looked at her own profile, specifically her IQ. As Sean he had
    always had an inferiority complex towards those who were more intelligent than him. He looked up to the
    various geniuses that made history what it was, tactical geniuses like Hannibal Barca especially, but even
    merely getting less than a perfect score on an exam, any exam, made Sean wince. Not that he was a nerd or
    anything, he just felt a need to be the smartest guy in the room. Sean WAS smart though, and obviously by
    extension so was Rain, but it wasn't nearly enough for either of them. Rain clicked on the bar and raised her
    IQ to the 300+ range. Then she clicked to confirm.

    "Maybe if this works, I'll be able to take apart the Chronivac and understand how it really works. Maybe I can
    even improve upon it" exclaimed Rain out loud. She quickly turned around to make sure no one heard her, not
    that it mattered, because everyone was either asleep or too drunk to tell their shirts from their shoelaces.

    As the task bar reached 100%, Rain felt a sudden influx of new feelings and thoughts which she assumed to be
    her new intellect kicking in. Excited and almost sexually aroused by the feeling, she quickly set up the three
    multi-task forms all targeted on Chris that would split Chris into different different people. Once they were
    all done, she clicked to confirm that the changes were permanent(or least until Rain changed them again).

    Walking into the living room, Rain was slightly disgusted to see Chris, former handsome young college student,
    now a big blob of sentient flesh. Rain thought that it looked like something out of a Clive Barker story.
    Thankfully, the process was quick, and the ball of flesh soon fell apart onto the floor into three distinct
    shapes. The first shape morphed into that of a young woman who was casually dressed and quickly walked out of
    the room as if in a trance. The second morphed into a pretty young lady a couple of years younger than Rain
    and was obviously related to Sunny, right down to her demeanor. The third ball of flesh was smaller than the
    other two and turned into a small grey kitten that started nicking Moon's exposed ankles.

    Rain smiled.
