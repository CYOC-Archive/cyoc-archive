    The Purifier was in New York. He had done a lot of small work in all sorts of areas, undoing teen pregnancies, having some frisky young couples get less frisky, the lord's work really. However there was only so much he could do at once on that low scale. With the power of god he had nothing to fear from some Protectors who saw his work like he was some criminal! Maybe under certain laws of the state, but under the law of GOD he has done nothing wrong. Some people seemed to even enjoy the experience, not his family though no. When he performed his miracle on his daughter neither her or his wife were pleased... Which led to this... extended vacation... Now he is in New York, so many people doing all sorts of things the lord would frown on. It could just be one continuous stream of purifications one right after another. With the lord on his side the Purifier would be able to do this for a long time without any too powerful Super coming around and stopping him. His first stop, one of New York's giant shopping malls right before school ends. Enough time to get in position, and maybe take care of a few students who skipped class.

    Red 6 however was also at the scene. Not as part of the Magical Girl Squad with her other members, Not as part of some Protector force, but as the early bloomer who may have put off getting new bra's for a little too long. the 12 year old had started growing a little before she turned 11 and now had rather large B-cups... Maybe it had something to do with being a Super but regardless the red head with a Tee and Jeans needed to get some new Bras. Then the Purifier came onto the scene in his white hooded garb and went to work. A pregnant teen there, some teens being a little too rambunctious. The feeling on the excess estrogen and testosterone leave people was just amazing. Oh looks like some bodybuilder abusing steroids was trying to stop him... And for sure The Purifier was in a hold and then the bodybuilder's puberty, and excess hormone abuse was taken away. Then The Purifier easily overpowered the once bodybuilder and continued on his way, and then checked out the Bra shop Red 6 was in.

    People could pick several mistake The Purifier had made that led to his downfall. It could be his attack of the Mall, or going to New York without researching most of the minor heroes, or going to New York at all, or not changing his name and choosing a white garb for his "vigilante" costume, or ever going on this "Purifier" business, but most go straight for his last victim. The one he thought "Was so young, but filled with so many hormones her chest was full to bursting. Who knows how her mind is coping with the cocktail of hormones, it would be cruel of me not to relief her of those sinful urges."

    To her credit while Red 6 and her Magical Girl Squad were relatively well known and in the papers and web articles now and then their faces weren't that remarkable. Unless they were all grouped together it was hard to point them out in a crowd. Red 6 knowing this attempted to try to ignore everything, and go about her business as her Civilian self and praying that another one of her outfits would not be plastered on the news... But when The Purifier grabs your shoulder and all of a sudden your once overflowing Bra goes completely limp...

    When the Police came to apprehend The Purifier who was wrapped up like a mummy in ornate magical red ribbons reporters were already at the scene hounding Red 6 for questions... Such as "What did you think about the fight with KKK/The Purifier/That Racist?" or "What were you doing at that Store?" or "You make Ribbons right? How did the entire store get so trashed?"

    Red faced Red 6 only replied with "No comment..." and started using her Ribbons to zip away to help get out of the crowds. That day the magical girl had saved the world from some nut job given powers by who knows what entity? But at what cost?
