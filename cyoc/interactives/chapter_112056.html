    Primitive Harmony

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Dragonman 6 years ago A O

    "I wish for the world to return to what it was like before humans started building civilizations, but I want the intelligence of everyone to remain the same as the modern age today. Everyone will then have to adapt to this, and rebuild, but change their minds to think environmentally, and help concerve resources. I also want people to respect, and accept each other for what they are, and refuse to hunt anthro animals, and maintain unity." You said. "Yes master, clothes are to still remain nonexistent, but everyone will remember transforming, and you will be transported to a cave where everyone will accept us as mates, and live with me, your wish is my command." The genie said snapping her fingers. You saw a bright light, and you appeared inside a cave alongside a hill, and you witnessed the genie grow legs and become male. "Your parents, and siblings will most definitely come here to see what happened, and we need to act as if we were victims to, and my name is Hunter." The former genie said. Minutes later three anthr wolves ran into the cave, two were female, and one male. "Alex, Haley do you know what happened to......." The male wolf started then paused looking at you. "Oh dear is this the wrong cave again?" The younger female asked. "No," Hunter said,"It's us, what happened, one minute everything's normal then we become anthro wolves and switch genders!" "At least your safe I guess I'm your mother now." The older female wolf said. "If that's the case then I'm your dad now this is so weird." The older male said. "It's......I feel.......I feel so different in so many ways, but why does this feel so right?" You said. "I know what you feel like I never wanted this, but I want to stay like this, and for the meantime please call me Chelsy." Your sister said,"What are your new names?" "Well if that's the case call me Alexis." You said and turned towards Hunter. "Then I'll be Hunter...... Alexis if this is too weird for you we can put our relationship on hold." Hunter said,"No, no of course not," you said looking outside,"What happened to well everything!?"
    "I don't know, but it seems like all forms of technology have been....... reset, like the whole world has been returned to a primitive age, and if that's the case then we won't be able to recover from this." Chelsy said. "This may be just about the weirdest question I will ever ask, but do any of you feel like you don't want to go back?" You asked. "You too I thought it was just me, what about the rest of you?" Hunter said, and everyone nodded. "If that's the case then, you two can get back to your business, and I'll ask around seeing what others think." Chelsy said, and your family left.
