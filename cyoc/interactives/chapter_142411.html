    The female and male researchers that assisted Ivan made their way across the hallways of the facility.

    The male gave a sigh of relief and said, "That sure was exciting, wasn't it Eva?" giving a friendly pat on the shoulder of his fellow researcher and sister, Eva.

    Eva only mustered a smirk as she fixed her glasses, "I can't deny it that when I saw that look on Ivan's face I couldn't be more than happy." she said, removing the hand of her brother, Adam.

    Adam laughed in return, "Dad was always like that." he said, as he along with Eva reached their rooms, which allowed them to survey Alina and Christy, "Alright, let's see what happened during the changes." he said as Adam started to watch the internal changes of the females during the procedure.

    Meanwhile, Eva watched the extraction team bring the subjects to their new enclosure, she giggled as she watched the members of the extraction team laugh at the bodies of the two females, only to then leave. After that, Eva shut the doors of the enclosure, and reinforced the walls so that Alina and Christy had no way of escaping.

    "Woah..." started Adam, as he was left with his mouth open watching the progressed changes of the bones and organs of the two reptillian girls. "Eva you need to take a look at this." he said, looking at his dear sister.

    Eva went to Adam giving an interested look and asked, "What did you find?"

    Adam pointed at the organs and bones of Christy and Alina, "It seems that Subject B used to smoke a lot." he noted, receiving a nod from Eva, "But during the transformation, the side effects suddenly disappeared!" he said.

    "The transformation must've fully regenerated her lungs."

    "Not only that, but it seems like the organs and the bones have been reinforced, and the generation of blood cells has increased." said Adam, "And it seems that the side effects of aging have disappeared completely."

    "So that means..." started Eva, giving a shocked look at Adam.

    Adam nodded, "This is the closest thing we have to immortality." he said with a smile.

    Eva stared at him, "Well what are you waiting for?! Go tell daddy Ivan about your discovery!" she said, in response, Adam quickly took the USB drive that contained the information and went in search for Ivan, to show his discovery.

    Meanwhile, Eva watched the two dinosaur girls that barely woke up.

    "Hng... My head hurts..." grunted Alina, as she looked at her friend Christy, now an anthropomorphic Triceratops, she gave a shocked look, only to then quickly look at her new body, "Oh god.. Christy we're freaks!" she shouted.

    Christy quickly gave an annoyed growl, "Glad you realized that Sherlock!" she said sarcastically, as she looked around and angrily said, "I can't believe they placed us in a spot like this." looking at the artificial enclosure they are.

    "We need to get out of here, I don't want to be tested by that freak again!"

    "Calm down!" shouted Christy, silencing Alina, "Try to see the positive! Atleast we are still girls and our physique is much better than before." she said, inspecting her new body, "Plus, didn't you hear him? Escape isn't an option, we can only stay here and hope that someone will rescue us." said Christy as she looked at Alina.

    Alina quickly shook her head and said, "No way! If you won't help me, then i'll find a way to escape myself!"

    "Are you dumb? Someone might be watching and listening to us! What if that old timer Ivan heard of your ideas?!" said Christy, "Who knows what cruel experiments he will do to you!" continued Christy.

    Eva giggled as she eavesdropped the entire discussion of Christy and Alina, she recorded their entire conversation on another USB drive and went to her father, Ivan, to tell him about the informations she received.

    Eva reached the office of Ivan, right after Adam finished telling his discovery.

    Ivan smiled and asked, "What knowledge does my second Prometheus bring to me?"

    Eva smiled, "I'm happy to announce that Subject B and T have fully awakened." she said receiving a small sinister laugh from Adam and her father. "However!" she started as she inserted the USB drive, presenting the audio log to Ivan, "It would seem that Subject B is already having plans for a... Rebellion." she said.

    After Ivan listened to the entire connversation of Christy and Alina, he gave a menacing smile, "Disciplinary actions must be taken." he announced, he then looked at one of the commanders of the extraction team, "Bring Subject B to the operating table, I must use her for an experiment." he said, receiving a salute from the commander, who quickly went with his fellow soldiers to recover Alina.

    Adam looked at Ivan, "Father? May I ask you for what is this experiment?" he asked.

    "I always wondered what will happen if a female is exposed to a serum meant for a male." replied Ivan, making Eva gulp after hearing this.

    After a few preparations, Alina suddenly woke up again in the same room in which she was turned into an anthropomorphic brontosaurus, however she found out that she was human again and completely naked.

    "Why am I here again!?" shouted Alina, demanding for an answer.

    "It would seem that you my dear, don't know that every interaction has a reaction!" said Ivan, over the intercoms.

    Alina suddenly groaned as she felt the machinary stab a syringe on the back of her neck, injecting a slightly grayish substance. The machine suddenly took off the syringe and disappeared again.

    Ivan looked at Eva and nodded, in response, Eva entered the codes and started to release a large amount of nuclear powered radiation in the chamber.

    Alina shutted her eyes tightly as she screamed in pain, feeling the forced chemicals change her in a...
