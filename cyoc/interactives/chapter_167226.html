    While you where examining your black latex wolf costume their was a sudden flash of light and suddenly you where no longer in your bathroom.

    Instead you now stood in the middle of a dense humid jungle. Naturally confused you begin to panic looking around for any landmarks that would lead you back to civilization finding nothing. Despite it supposed to being Christmas, the jungle felt like it was summer making you sweat inside the suit and it sticking to you. You could probably take it off but for some strange reason you felt you shouldn’t. As you wondered, the suit clung more and more to your skin getting tighter and tighter. Eventually you swore you could feel the forest leaves and branches brushing against you like it was your real skin. At several points you where forced to crawl on all fours to get under trees, despite this the motion felt oddly natural for you. Wondering through the jungle soon relaxed you to. Despite your new situation and outfit you felt at piece with nature, though it wouldn’t hurt if you had something besides a full body black latex wolf suit on. Along the way you started panting inside the mask to calm down.
