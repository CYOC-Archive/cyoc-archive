    Eric knew that he couldn't just steal that ring from Jon. He probably never let that ring out of his sight. So instead, Eric decided to try and figure out where Jon got that ring in the first place. It couldn't have just appeared out of thin air, right? So, Jon did everything he could to track down where that ring came from. He tailed Jon, asked around school and town, things like that. It took a few weeks, but eventually, he managed to narrow down the location to one place, and Eric was shocked when he figured out that location.

    The next day, Eric went to his Mother's office to deliver her dinner, as his Mom working late again. Then, he took this opportunity to look around the place. Eric doubted that he could steal a ring from this place, but maybe he could find out where to purchase it or something. Eric didn't have much time to look around as he was quickly approached by a female employee.

    "Excuse me, but you wouldn't happen to be a tester, would you?" she asked.

    "Uh, sorry. I'm not," Eric replied truthfully.

    "W-well, would you like to be one? Oh, I'm sorry for asking that out of the blue. It's just that a couple of the testers never showed up, and we have a deadline to meet, and combining magic and science is hard and..." The woman was talking too frantically for Eric to completely understand what she was saying. However, he knew he needed to calm her down.

    "Hey, hey, calm down. Just breath in and out, okay?" Eric brought the woman to a place where she could sit down and catch her breath. After she calmed down, Eric decided to ask her something. "So, may I ask what you're testing, exactly?"

    "Oh, it's a ring that you can use to change your body and muscle mass-" that all Eric needed to hear, so he cut her off.

    "Say no more. I'll be happy to help test your product."

Eric wondered how the engineers could make something like this, however judging from that woman's reaction earlier, it couldn't have been easy.
