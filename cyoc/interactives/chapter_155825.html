    The Vials

    Mary Doll and vial case repossessed.

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LuisJM 3 years ago O

    Many months have passed since Mary became a doll. But the plush doll that was once Mary, time has no meaning to a toy. All she can care about is for some girl to come and pick her up and play with her. So she lay on the floor, waiting for someone to come get her. But as she was waiting, everything was changing.

    Since she drank the vial, she didn't know that drinking the whole thing would lead to the changes be permanent. Because of that, she is stuck as an inanimate toy for the rest of her life. Yet around her, her previous life was deteriorating. It starts out with mail piling at her door, mostly involving bills that were slowly starting to overdue. Slowly, water and electricity was cut off and then an eviction notice was on the door. Not only that, but her family and friends were starting to worry as there were no phone calls from her and where was no answer from the door. What went from worry turn into fear as they have deemed missing. Then the rumors spread that she was either kidnapped or killed by crooks or murderers. Then the rumors stopped as everything thinks that Mary has been gone, not realizing that she was a doll the whole time.

    After all that went down, Repo men came in and started to take all of Mary's possessions to the truck to be taken to a warehouse. The landlord looked to all of them moving all the things and was still confused about what happened to Mary.

    "I just don't understand." he said with confusion, "Mary was a kind woman. Always pays her bills, spends time with family and friends, has a job. She seems like a perfect woman. And now she vanished without a trace and there's no sign of her. I just don't get it."

    The head of the repo men shook his head and signed the waver. "What can I say. With Mary hasn't paid her bills nor visit those close to her for months on end. Doesn't matter if she's missing or dead, once you're gone, you're gone."

    In the bedroom, people were moving furniture from Mary as the one in charge told them to be careful.

    "Don't damage any of this! They need to be in mint condition for the family, since the owner hasn't been found in a long time."

    As he looked around, he noticed two things that were in the room. The first is a plush doll all dressed up in a blue sundress and Mary Janes. Course that was Mary as she remained motionless for a long time. The second is a strange case that's filled with a variety of vials. Looking at the two, he asked, "Now what are these things doing here. Doesn't look like they belonged here. So he picked up the doll and looked at the vials, wondering what to take.
