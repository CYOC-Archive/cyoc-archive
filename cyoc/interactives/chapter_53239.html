    Nate grabs his wallet and changes clothes before he goes to buy the clothes. Just as he grabs the key to his motobike, his Dad stirs and asks him what he is doing.
    Nate responds, "Just headed to the mall to pick up a few clothes."
    He notices how much older and wrinkle-er his Dad now is, and is slightly grossed out by all the extra hair growing from his ears and nose.. and the bushier crazy old man eyebrows. His chin has begun to form a double chin, and the neck is much has loosened and drooped.
    "I'm only 38, but that doesn't mean I have the memory of a 70 year old man", ironically says his Dad.
    Nate quickly points the calculator at his Dad and enters + 25. He watches in amazement his Dad begins to age much faster. All of his remaining hair has turned white, and he only has little hair left on top of his head as it has completelu receeded back. He loses another 2 inches of height, and appears rounder because of the lose of muscle mass and fat replacing it.
    "What were we argueing about? My show is coming on soon and it's almost time for my afternoon nap", said Nate's 75 year old Father.
    "It doesn't matter", responds Nate. "I don't want to interrupt your afternoon."
    Nate lets out a long sigh of relief as he heads out the door.
