    "Perhaps the two of you are wondering about the details of this little experiment. Very well, you have the right to know the course we anticipate your lives will be taking over the next few years. Based on the information you submitted, our personality tests of you, and years of scientific research, the two of you have been selected as two particular test subjects in our initial human study group. By the way, let me introduce you two, as i imagine you will be spending quite a bit of time together." As the scientist continued speaking, I felt my stomach churning. I felt hot all over, as if my skin were on fire.. "Baxter," he said turning to me, "Let me introduce you to your partner in this endeavor, Luke. Luke, this is Baxter." I looked over the young man. He had medium-length black hair and stood about 6-1 with a cut military physique. Like myself, he seemed to be rubbing himself nervously, as if already under the effects of whatever serum we had been administered. He had the look of the kind of man I always imagined myself loving if I had been born female. The scientist continued speaking, "Anyway, as I was saying, you are part of a cutting edge research experiment that could change the face of humanity and civilization as we now know and understand it. We have discerned in both of you a fascination in the concept of personal physical transformation. Luke, you will be undergoing a remarkable transformation of your entire body and being.. Soon, in addition to most of your own mental capacities, you will have many of the physical attributes previously only seen in the species we commonly call 'foxes.' The ability to take on characteristics of different species as needed could prove a valuable asset to mankind. Such changes could serve profitable recreational or even lifestyle purposes as well. In a sense, you, Baxter, will be testing the feasibility of more drastic changes. As I am sure you are aware, and as your tests indicate, your interest in transformation includes a desire to be female. In addition to sprouting red fox fur, growing a tail, gaining increased speed, agility, and the heightend senses of a fox, you will soon and quickly be undergoing a gender change. You shall become a vixen, in physical form."
    Questions were mounting in my mind as I listened to his speech.. A vixen! Female! Furry! Is that REALLY what I've always wanted? And I could feel changes already.. Holy shit! Am I supposed to be this Luke guy's fox mate?? And I can't believe the scientist said all that stuff with him right there!! He's probably going to think I'm a freak.. Looking over at him, he didn't look disgusted though.. just thoughtful.. and quite interested in what was happening to his body.
    "Now as I said, you will be a vixen in physical form. We cannot say much else. This topic is too new, and there just isn't any way of knowing what will happen to you mentally.. To what extent you will feel dominated by instinct, to what extent your male impulses and tendencies will be modified, or how you will adapt to the changes. After your transformations are complete, we will outfit the two of you with sensor collars to track vital information. You will be let loose to live as you will, and you are to return in 1 year upon the completion of this study.. Of course, at that time you may elect to return to your former lives, to select a different study group, or remain in the form you will soon take. Essentially, we want to know if this is feasible for general sale and use. The answers to our questions we hope to discover over the course of the experiment... I will leave you now to your future."
