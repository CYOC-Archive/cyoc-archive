    After a moment of thought, Jason made it so that he and all the other diapered subs went to the college daycare. He submitted the change.

    The words saying only Sufjan could change him faded from his diaper. Sufjan turned on his turn signal. Jason looked at him, confused.

    "Where are we going?" he asked.

    "The daycare, babe. Remember? All the subs go there." Sufjan sighed. "I wish you could live in the frat house with me, but the last dom who let his sub live in the house got made into a sub. I love it for you, but not for me."

    They drove up to the college daycare. Students and professors with children brought their children there, where they were supervised by students and professors in childcare degree programs. The daycare was different from the last time Jason walked by it on the way to class. It was bigger, and seemed to be split in half. One half had the playground Jason remembered, while the other half had a similar playground, but with much larger, adult-sized toys.

    As Jason and Sufjan walked up to the building, new information about the reality came to Jason. Subs were now used as practice by childcare students, instead of actual children. The daycare was split in two to keep the two groups separate. Subs still had to take classes, but now did so remotely from the daycare. Once the subs got closer to graduation, the daycare workers gradually potty trained them and weaned them from their sub behaviors, so they could join society like a normal person after graduation.

    "Here we are!" Sufjan said, coming to a stop in front of a closed door with stickers all over it. He kissed Jason. "I'll see you tomorrow, babe. Don't fill too many diapers without me!"

    Jason watched Sufjan walk away, upset. This wasn't what he wanted! He wanted to get out of class entirely, not still need to take classes and be separated from his boyfriend! Quickly, he grabbed his phone and generated a new sentence. He had an idea of how to fix it.
