    A Coffee Date and Evil Plans

    Branch Full view
    added 5 years ago O

    Samantha soon arrived back at Katie's house and the girls asked her how the date went. She was a little shy about telling anything that happened, but eventually confessed that she would have another date with Mike in the future. Now the rest of the girls and Aunt Lisa went to sleep and morning came almost too quickly. Lisa got up before any of the girls and was getting ready to go meet with Mr. Jason Turner for a cup of coffee. Katie happened to wake up to see her aunt having finished getting ready and was now pacing back and forth, clearly worried about her date.

    "Aunt Lisa? Are you okay?" Katie asked her aunt.

    "I'm....I'm a little bit nervous is all. I haven't been on a date in....I don't know how many years." Aunt Lisa said trying to take it all in.

    "Hey, come on, it'll be alright, it's just one date. You're going to do great." Katie told her aunt with confidence.

    Lisa happily smiled at her niece, wondering if Katie got her good confidence from her mother, she remembered how her sister would sometimes do this when she went on teenage dates in the past.

    "Thanks, honey." Lisa said thanking Katie as the doorbell rang and Lisa went up to it and answered it.

    "Uh...hi there, Lisa. Are you ready? I mean, you can take your own car if you want. I just had to drop Gloria off with the girls." Jason said as Gloria came into the house with some of her stuff.

    "Oh yeah, it's fine." Lisa said as they both stood there awkwardly for a moment.

    "Well, you two don't want to be late, do you?" Katie asked them with a smirk as Lisa and Jason both decided they had lingered on for quite enough and went to their own cars to go to the coffee shop.

    The other girls had been getting up just as Lisa and Jason were leaving and were all now in charge of watching Gloria. As Kellie and the rest of the girls were having fun with Gloria, Kellie noticed her girlfriend was looking out the window a little nervously.

    "Katie, honey, are you feeling okay?" Kellie asked her.

    "Yeah, I just hope her date is going well. She hasn't been on one in years, and I know she always acts confident, but I'm just a little nervous that her date won't go well." Katie explained.

    "If you want..." Kellie said looking back towards where Gloria and the other girls were before speaking privately with her.

    "We could go spy on them, to see how her date's going?" Kellie suggested.

    Katie thought hard about this, she wanted her aunt's date to go well, but then again she was going to feel a little bad about leaving Gloria.

    "What about Gloria?" Katie now asked her.

    "We could progress her to 16 until we get back then turn her back to 10?" Kellie suggested.

    "I don't know how I feel about turning her older so quickly. Maybe we can explain to her we want to see how her dad's date is going?" Katie suggested.

    "Yeah, we could do that." Kellie agreed.

    "What are you guys talking about?" The two of them suddenly heard a voice ask and they turned to see Gloria standing there.

    "Oh! Gloria, we were just talking about how your dad and my aunt's date might be going. We were thinking about going to spy on them and just to see if it was going well." Katie explained to the little blonde girl.

    "Oh, you want to be spies? That sounds so cool, I want to be a spy too so I can see how my dad's date is going." Gloria said excitedly.

    "Uh, no. We need you to....stay here and keep an eye on the others, because we don't want them knowing anything just yet, okay?" Kellie asked her.

    "Oh yeah, I get it." Gloria said winking at them.

    "Good. Now remember, if they ask you, just tell them we went to go get breakfast from a fast food place and will be back soon." Kellie instructed her.

    Gloria nodded as Katie and Kellie got out of the house and went as fast as they could to go see how Lisa's date was going. They both arrived at the coffee shop and made sure that Lisa and Jason didn't spot them. As they sat in their booth, not far away they both heard two familiar voices.

    "So, you said you have a plan?" A female voice asked. Katie and Kellie both recognized it as Ashley's voice.

    "Oh yeah. I have the perfect plan to get back at those assholes for talking back to us the way they did at the beach." Jennifer's voice now replied.

    "Well, let me hear it." Ashley insisted.

    "It's simple, I'll spread a rumor that one of them is secretly having an affair with a teacher at the school. It'll take a little time, but I know some other people I can pay off so they can sell it good." Jennifer said.

    "That is so devious. How did you think of something like that so quickly?" Ashley asked intrigued.

    "I just...I just did, okay?" Jennifer said almost sounding like she was hiding something.

    "Okay, so who do we say is doing the dirty deed?"

    "Mika, obviously. That bitch is going to pay for threatening to beat us up." Jennifer said.

    "Yeah. I can't wait to see how this plays out." Ashley said as she and Jennifer both left their booth and headed out of the coffee shop.

    "We've got to do something about those two." Kellie said.

    "Yeah, but what?" Katie asked.

    "We could go back and tell the girls, or we could follow one of them and maybe get some dirt on them instead." Kellie suggested.
