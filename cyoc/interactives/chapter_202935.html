    Two days later, Officer Cooper was at his desk doing daily paperwork when Detective Warner came over to his desk looking disappointed.

    "Morning detective, something I can help you with?" Officer Cooper asked.

    "Come into my office Cooper, now." Detective Warner said as Officer Cooper followed behind him and sat down in the chair opposite his desk.

    "Cooper, I've always believed you were a good cop, but lately you've had....moments where you seem to let the rules slide a little. A small brutality here, giving rough warnings to a few minor misdemeanors at most. But what I can't believe is that you had the gall to tamper with a crime scene." Warner said as Cooper began to look a little worried.

    "Wh-what? Warner, come on, you know I would never do that. I've been giving my best to this force all these years and I would never in my life...." Cooper began to say before being interrupted by Warner.

    "We have confirmation from Officer Jenkins that she witnessed you deliberately tampering with evidence at the Pendragon location." Warner explained with Cooper looking shocked at what he had just said.

    "No, I did nothing of the sort, what we found there was completely real!"

    "Was threatening a fellow officer's life for her silence real?!" Detective Warner angrily asked as he stood up.

    "Officer Jenkins came in here yesterday to tell me that she not only witnessed you tampering with the evidence, but that when you saw her, you threatened her to keep her mouth shut about the whole thing. Now tell me, did you tamper with police evidence?" Warner asked.

    "I did, but Jenkins..."

    "Has decided to quit the force altogether." Warner explained as Cooper looked shocked by all this revelation.

    "Now turn in your badge and prepare for five years or more." Warner said as Cooper got out his badge slowly, but then went for his gun as Detective Warner tackled him to the ground.

    "Backup! Get me backup in here for this traitor!" Warner shouted as two officers came in and grabbed him and hauled him away to a cell.

    "Jenkins double crossed me! That bitch Jenkins double crossed me!" Cooper shouted as he was taken away to a cell.

    Lord Wyatt Tepes stood outside a backyard in the mansion that Miro had left to him. Wyatt looked around at all his surviving subjects and a few new additions that had been added such as Cassidy or Cassandra as she preferred to be called now, showing Jenkins around their new location. Wyatt smiled as he could hardly believe it, months ago he was only a lonely teenager at high school with barely any friends, now he was the lord of the vampires and had an entire legacy and vampires to lead.

    "Vampires old and new!" Wyatt exclaimed getting everyone's attention.

    "My father Lord Miro's plans, unfortunately failed. But we had enough vampires survive the extermination from the wizards that we are going to be able to rebuild and build a better vampire future!" Wyatt exclaimed as the other vampires cheered.

    "However, we cannot ignore the events of the past few days. Wizards are dangerous and as of now, no vampire is to go after them for any reason. If I so much as hear a rumor that one of you is going after a wizard or any of their kids, you are dust." Wyatt threatened.

    "Why shouldn't we? Those wizards ruined our legacy and killed your father....maybe you aren't the leader we deserve." A middle age vampire man said as Wyatt nodded with a smile and nod then grabbed the vampire by his shirt collar and punch him hard in the nose before slamming the vampire's face into the pavement.

    "Any other objections?!" Wyatt angrily asked.

    "You're....you're nothing but a....worthless little imposter....you weren't born into the Tepes line, you just got his blood in your body through some magic shit. You're nothing but a...." The beaten up vampire suddenly felt a sharp pain in his back as he looked down to see something push through his chest and come out the other side and saw what looked to be a child's hand.
