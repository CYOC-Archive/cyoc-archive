    There was a 17-year-old athletic, teenage boy named Cody watching her act and he had a request. He had seen how Madame Illusia had transformed Damon into a cow, and he also wanted something like that for himself. He loved to drink milk in the morning and before his workouts in the gym, but he couldn’t always carry a jug of milk with him. So, he raised his hand, and the Madame called him onto the stage.

    The shy, short but muscular young man with short, blond hair and blue eyes stepped on the stage when Stephan directed him to the Madame.

    “What can I do for you, young man?” the Madame asked him.

    “Umm… Well… “the shy boy began, as he looked down and shuffled his feet. “I wanna make my own milk. But… I wanna still be a human. Kinda like a hybrid or something, ya know?”

    “Oh, absolutely! I think I understand! You love to drink milk! Milk is good for your bones and for the muscles. You would like to produce milk so you can share, without becoming a full cow?” the Madame said to the extremely shy teen.

    “Uhh… Yeah… Am I… Am I asking too much? I’m so sorry… I didn’t mean to—” the boy started to apologize.

    “Oh, no, my boy! You’re fine. You’re such a sweetheart! Come here, my boy!” the Madame said, as she spread apart her arms.

    The Madame was a thin but tall woman. Cody walked up to her, she closed her long, thin arms and she wrapped her arms around him, hugging him tight.

    “You’re such a sweet boy. I will grant you all your wishes!” the Madame whispered.

    While people looked on, they saw his clothes vanish while she hugged Cody. Then, a long, white cow’s tail began to come from the base of his spine, growing all the way to his ankles, much to the loud gasps of the audience.

    “Now and then you’ll have the urge to go out on the lawn in the back yard and to get down on your knees and to eat some grass, okay?” the Madame said to him. “It will taste delicious to you!”

    “Oh… Okay!”

    “And your milk will be of the utmost best quality. Anyone who drinks your milk will experience some degree of muscular growth and increased physical strength! Everyone will like the taste of your milk, which you will produce abundantly!” the Madame said loudly for all to hear. “Now, Cody, turn around and face the audience for me, okay?”

    The Madame had turned Cody into a human cow hybrid. Madame Illusia spoke a few magical verses in a strange language and the audience witnessed the changes happening to Cody, as he stood on stage completely naked.

    Two long udders grew from his nipples on his chest while his pectorals bulged and grew into large orbs of muscle.

    Finally, Madame Illusia came with an empty glass, holding it under one of Cody's udders, and she gently pulled on the end of Cody's right udder which now poured a few ounces of fresh milk into the glass.

    When Cody drank this milk, which he had produced himself, it caused his muscles to grow a little bit. Cody raised up his right arm and flexed, showing off a bigger bicep!

    Cody was very happy, and he thanked the Madame for the changes she had made to his body, before stepping off the stage to roaring applause from the entire audience.
