    You carefully select the figurine of the anthropomorphic cat robot, captivated by its unique design. Clad in a white plastic armor that blends elements of science fiction and the Japanese Edo period, it exudes an intriguing charm. Its almost spherical head boasts a tan mouth and nose, situated beneath a pair of large, expressive eyes. A white helmet frames its visage, while a red plate resembling a visor, secured by four yellow screws and adorned with a circular blue insert, rests upon its forehead. Atop its head, a pair of tetrahedral ears, outlined in gold and adorned with blue interiors and a red dot in the center, add a touch of whimsy. The chest armor features a combination of white and blue accents, complete with a cat bell positioned prominently on the chest. Shoulder pads offer protection to its shoulders, while blue arms extend through white gauntlets, terminating in nimble blue hands. The lower portion mirrors the aesthetic, with armored boots safeguarding its feet. A tail adorns its back, and a red and gold holster, situated on its left side, safeguards a sword.

    Following the instructions in the manual, you locate the button positioned between the figurine's legs. Intrigued and excited, you decide to give it a try, placing the figurine carefully on the table before you. Fixing your gaze upon its eyes, you press the button.

    In an instant, a brilliant flash of light engulfs your vision, and you find yourself momentarily blacked out. Slowly, your systems begin to boot up, and a sense of awareness washes over you. You realize, with a curious mix of awe and delight, that your consciousness now resides within the figurine itself. It's part of your programming, and although it isn't mandatory, an instinct compels you to strike a pose, as if posing for an imaginary camera. Something about it feels oddly satisfying, and a surge of pride courses through you as you sheathe your sword, mentally acknowledging your inventory of systems.

    However, amidst your newfound realization and excitement, a sudden alarm from your battery management system jolts you into a state of panic. The battery level is critically low, threatening to shut down your current form. Frantically, you attempt to activate the restore function, hoping to regain your former living body. Yet, the subsystem responsible for the restoration refuses to work due to the depleted battery. Desperation sets in as you scramble to locate a phone charger, but with each movement, more and more systems begin to glitch out until you are rendered immobile and darkness descends upon your consciousness once more.
