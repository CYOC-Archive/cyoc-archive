    Gabriel was finishing his work organizing his boss's files, when he heard one of his coworkers who was staying for some over time complaining to the janitor that he recently went for the mandatory weekly checkup and had apparently got the monster flu and the worst thing is he said, he was going to turn into a fairy. Gabriel could only feel sorry for the guy as he over heard his complaints, but it was now time for him to leave and make his way for his weekly checkup, hopefully he didn't have the monster flu.
    Gabriel left work and made his way to the bus stop, he waited for an roughly 7 minutes before the bus came, it was a hour before he got to where the clinic was and hopped off.
    Gabriel went through the usual checkup procedures, then waited for the doctor to come back with his results. it took longer the he would have liked before the doctor came back with a pamphlet, Gabriel knew what that pamphlet meant, it meant that he had gotten the monster flu. the doctor sat down and said "Gabriel i have gotten the results from the tests and you have gotten the monster flu, now before we discuss what you will be turning into you know of the new rules and regulations regarding changed people, Yes?" Gabriel nodded in affirmative, "good" the doctor said before continuing "but i still have to go over some of them with you nonetheless, as you know monsterized people need to move into new turns and housing designed for them in order to keep the peace between everyone so you will need to call you banks and sell any housing you have, you will have a week to organize your belongings before your move. you will the move into a transformation center that will act as your home and as a school for what your changes will bring, after that the staff and the center will provide you with a permanent residence in the monster city, as well as help find you a job according to what you want and what you have changed into."
    Gabriel nodded along to what the doctor said, already knowing all this was sent up in order to protect the people who had changed and keep a sense of normalcy for those who hated them. "I know you are well aware of all this given it has been 3 years since the flu became a pandemic, so i won't say any more then what i have told you, the rest you can find on this pamphlet." Gabriel grabbed the pamphlet from the doctors hand.
    "so want am i going to be turning into? doc" asked Gabriel, the doctor replied "from what the tests have shown you are going to be turning female for one, and well it looks like you are going to turn into a hybrid, fusion of two different monsters"
    Gabriel's face whitened as he heard he was going to be a women, he the asked "so what monster hybrid am i becoming?"
