    After a couple of hours of walking, Arianna and Arran struggling uselessly every step of the way, the goblins finally reached their destination. It was a ramshackle village full of decrepit wooden huts and animal hide tents, and full of goblins, orcs, trolls, elves, and various other non-human races. Which wasn't to say there were no humans - it was just that they were all locked in cages or tied to posts, all of them wearing rags or outright naked, and all wearing looks of utter hopelessness.

    For Arianna, it was shocking to see this in a human-dominated kingdom. For Arran, it was shocking to see something out of a fantasy movie come to life in front of him. And for both, they simply couldn't believe they were in this situation.

    "Hey, Garmock!" the lead goblin called out to a passing orc, "There any open sale slots? I got me a couple of humies to put out."

    "Yah, there's one over here," the orc pointed to a nearby patch of muddy ground with several posts sticking out of it. The goblin nodded in thanks and directed his minions to drag Arianna and Arran over to the posts, tying each of them to one of them. Once they were secured, the lead goblin spread his arms and started speaking loudly to catch the attention of anyone passing by.

    "Listen up, all of ya! I got me a couple of grade-A humies for sale! They're in their prime, young and strong, good looking if yer into that kinda thing, though they ain't no virgins," he said, snickering at that last line, "Ya can a lot of work out 'em, ya can fuck 'em, ya can fatten 'em up and eat 'em! So many options! Just make me an offer, no less than five gold coins each!"

    This continued for some time, numerous people listening to the goblin's speech, inspecting the squirming humans' bare forms like cattle on display, and negotiating firmly with the goblin. Finally, however, an agreement was reached, and the pair was sold to their new fate.
