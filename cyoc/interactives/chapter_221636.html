    Tyler discovers the program on his computer?!

    added by theguestfb A month ago A Bovine Reality alteration

    "Ehh, Toby can figure it out." He sighed before going back to day dreaming, as he hopped into his car and drove to his shitty apartment, stopping only to pick up some takeout. He sat in front of his computer eating and surfing the net when suddenly he noticed a strange icon on his computer.

    "Chronovac? I don't remember installing anything by that name...some kind of fancy time clock?" He clicked it and it instantly began to load. "Previous program completed, do you wish to review summary of results?" He read. "Program objective...Human and cow swap roles. Cows now only intelligent species on Earth."

    "Now that's science-fiction. Must be some kind of game like the Sims." He minimized the tab and looked at the program. There was a scan button, and he clicked it. It generated a view of him and his apartment, showing his name, vital statistics, and a last modified date of about 4 months ago. There was a link next to Last Modified to show previous revisions. He clicked it, and an option for a side by side view.

    It showed an image of a naked massively obese Tyler on one side, and his current image on the other side. The other Tyler was listed as four years old, with a nonexistent IQ. "Shit," Tyler cursed. the more he scrolled, the more detail he saw...this was too detailed to be a game, wasn't it?
