    Changing a friend

    added by Xander6981 3 years ago A BM

    Dillon immediately thought of his friend Nate and knew he had to be his next test. Nate had confided in him a love of human to animal transformation got excited at the thought of his friend's actual reaction as he transformed into something else. He pulled out his cellphone and sent his friend a text to come over immediately and that he had something very exciting to show him. Curious, Nate texted back that he'd be right over.

    A short time later, the buzzer for the front door went off and Dillon pressed the button to let Nate in. He waited patiently for his friend to make his way to his apartment. He could only imagine his friend's reaction at his rather startling changes. Rather than wish for this to be normal like he did with Phil, Dillon wanted Nate's full reaction. Soon enough, there was a knock on his apartment door as Nate let himself in. He closed the door behind him and then turned around coming face to face with Dillon and his long chameleon tail. In a state a shock, Nate staggered into the living room as he took in his friend's shocking transformation.

    "Wha...I...how?" Nate could barely contain his shock and excitement. He walked over and took in his friend's new tail. It sank in for him that it was really real, not some clever effect.

    "It's this ring I got. It gives me the power to transform whatever I want." Dillon was grinning from ear to ear.

    "Really?" Nate couldn't contain his excitement any longer. "You gotta turn me into something. Something cool, though. Promise?"

    "Sure. But what though?" Dillon stood there pondering as Nate waited to see what he would be turned into. Finally, after a few excruciating moments, Dillon made his decision. Nate would turn into...
