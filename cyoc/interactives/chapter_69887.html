    "Cody Rayne, stoner?" Craig said, confused. He looked around, looking for someone. "This has to be a joke" he thinks to himself. However he once again checked that no one is around and he looked puzzled at the uniform.

    Who would steal his uniform and why would they leave someone else's uniform for him to wear? Was it this Cody Rayne who did this? This was all just too confusing. He needed to find Cody Rayne, whoever he was, and ask him about all this mess. In the meanwhile Craig would just have to borrow these clothes. After all he couldn't walk around naked in the middle of the school.

    The uniform itself was just like any other in his school. black trousers, white shirt and, red sweatshirt with the school's logo and black footwear. However he noticed that these clothes were slightly smaller than his own and they had a faint but distinct smell of pot.

    "Maybe he switched uniforms so that no one would notice the smell... but why would he fold the uniform and leave a note? It's like I was being targeted all along!" He thought annoyed as he put on the shirt and started buttoning it. "These clothes will do for now until I straight everything out"

    Indeed the clothes were slightly smaller, as were the shoes, but they didn't bug him very much. He was focused on finding Cody Rayne and get his uniform back.

    After he put on the uniform he left the changing room and was greeted by a typical grey day in England.

    His stomach growled just like every time he finished P.E. He considered ignoring it and just go search for Cody but he faced the truth that he didn't even know where to begin.

    He didn't know anyone in this school and his average grades, his below average physical performance and shyness didn't really put him on the spotlight.

    Walking to the cafeteria he sat by himself. There weren't many students around. Over here and over there he noticed students from his class, some groups of boys and girls that really didn't care much for Craig and he didn't really care much for them as well. His eyes went left and right, up and down always looking for someone with a slightly bigger uniform, someone whose eyes would betray them and avoid Craig's eyes but no one remotely suspect appeared.

    He quickly finished all his food. He didn't even care much for the food that was served but when Craig was hungry he just couldn't refuse any food. Still no matter how much he ate no food could make him gain any weight.

    Craig sighed as he wandered outside alone, again. He had no clue where to go. But then he saw Samuel.

    Samuel was a boy from his class. He was slightly taller than Craig and had medium size straight red hair. The two of them had never really talked to each other but Craig saw from his classes that although Samuel didn't hang around much with others (just like Craig), he had a carefree attitude and was generally liked by all. But Craig wasn't thinking about that. He was thinking that sometimes during class he noticed that Samuel's clothes smelled like pot as he walked passed him. So maybe Samuel could know something about this Cody guy since they both seemed to like to get stoned, right?
