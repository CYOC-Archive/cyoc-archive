    Ty's Power

    Helping a boy who was too big!

    added by scriptboy 11 years ago BM S

    Ty was just getting the hang of his awesome new powers on his first Monday at school following the Sunday
    after he had first discovered his Godly powers which allowed him to change just about anything about anyone
    any way he wanted just by simply wishing for it! How cool was that? He could make anything happen by making a
    simple wish with a thought.

    Ty wandered around the halls of his high school and saw the long row of lockers as he turned the corner.
    There, he came upon two of his best friends in school, Grant and Scott. Grant was short and skinny while Scott
    was much taller but he was severely overweight. It was funny to see them side-by-side because one was so much
    bigger than the other, but the boys were still great friends. Now, Ty was pretty much an average guy, only 5
    ft 6 inches tall and he had an average build, before he made his changes, so he was about in the middle
    between the two extremes.

    "Hey, you guys want to go to the library?" Scott had asked Ty and Grant.

    "Sure, I still need to finish up on a homework assignment!" Grant added.

    Ty grabbed his backpack and walked behind the two other boys when an idea came to him: Since one of his
    friends was just way too small and the other was way too big, why not make them the same height as him? Ty was
    now 6 feet tall, since he made himself taller and more muscular the previous day, and he wanted to reward his
    friends using his cool new powers.

    Ty silently wished for Grant to grow from 5 feet to 6 feet tall, and to gain about a 100 pounds of muscle, so
    that would make him about the same height as him. Next, he wished for Scott to lose all his fat and for him to
    shrink down from 6 foot 7 inches to 6 foot even, but with 300 pounds of muscle rather than all that fat! That
    way, all three boys would look about the same in height and in muscle!

    Just as Ty, Grant and Scott had exited the main building where all the classrooms were and they were heading
    out towards the campus library, the changes began to take place. Grant started to become taller and taller
    while his muscles began to inflate at the same time. His arms and legs grew longer, making him taller at the
    same time. When Grant looked down and began to notice his increased height, he began to feel his muscle
    increase in size and weight as well. His pecs pushed outward and his triceps and biceps bulged, while his
    calves and quads filled out his jeans. All his clothes began to feel incredibly tight as his muscles came
    close to making his shirt sleeves and his jeans rip at the seams!

    For Scott, it was exactly the other way around! The large boy began to shrink in his large clothes as
    everything began to shrink! His arms, legs and torso first dwindled by several inches as his fat in his limbs
    and torso melted away! When every ounce of fat had disappeared, and Scott was now about 6 feet tall, muscle
    began to inflate in all the spots where fat was there before. The big, chubby guy slowly transformed into a
    shorter, muscular athlete! Unfortunately, his clothes were still rather big on him, but his muscles made up
    the difference!

    "Grant..." Scott said to his friend, as the two were walking towards the library, with Ty still walking behind
    them. Of course, Ty had seen them transform all the way.

    "Yeah?"

    "Do you feel different?"

    "Yeah... I'm taller... I feel stronger as well!" Grant responded.

    "Dude!" Scott replied. "I don't know what happened but... I think I've shrunk! All my fat has gone but I
    gained all this muscle instead!"

    But boys realized that they were now about the same height. They flexed their arms together, revealing their
    huge biceps. Then they just continued walking until they reached the library. Ty just grinned as he saw how
    happy Grant and Scott were with their new bodies, and he was already looking forward to changing other guys
    using his cool new powers...
