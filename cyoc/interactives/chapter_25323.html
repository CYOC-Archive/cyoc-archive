    Amy led the others down an aisle of candies. Lina skimmed the labels as they passed, then told the others to stop. "I think something in here can help." The three searched for a candy that sounded like it would change Lina into a monkey.

    "Chihuahua Chews, Candy Cows, Gummy Grasshoppers..." Sam read aloud in amazement, still trying to believe this store was for real.

    Amy danced over to Lina on her bare paws. "Mrow! I found it!" She gave Amy a box with gummy bananas on the front. It was labeled "Going Bananas: Candy that takes you back to your wild origins."

    "This looks as good as anything," Lina smiled. The banana smell was already noticeable before she even opened the box. She pried open the flap and dumped several small banana-shaped gummies into her hand. Sam and Amy watched her intently, as if she was about to eat a handful of live worms. Lina chuckled at their stares and popped the candies into her mouth.

The odd feeling spread over her entire face, and she saw her friends looking at her wide-eyed. "Wush happenin?" she asked with the bananas still in her mouth.

    "Your face is changing," said Sam incredulously. "Just like a monkey."

    "Your hair, too!" Amy added. She looked up and down the aisle. "Come on, hurry!"

    Amy pulled Lina by the arm down the aisle. Lina didn't know where they were headed, but she went along anyway. She threw a few more candies into her mouth as she felt her scalp itch like crazy. Soon her arms were feeling weird too, and she saw black hair thickening on them. Her feet felt more and more cramped every step she took, and finally put her hand on Amy's shoulder to get her to stop walking. Lina pulled off her shoes and nearly choked on the candy as her eyes beheld hands. They were bigger than normal hands, and much furrier, but they were definitely not feet anymore. She wiggled her toes...or fingers...and they responded just like a normal hand would. Her thumbs were dexterous, and she tried griping one hairy ankle with the other hand/foot. It was a little hard to maneuver at first, but she managed to grip it fine. Soon she was twiddling her new fingers as if she had been using the hands her entire life.

It stopped at three feet, but she popped another few candies and watched it grow beyond four.

She shimmied her pants back on, pulling them up as much as she could with her tail in the way. "Ooo-oo...Um, Amy?" she asked through thick teeth. She was surprised to be making chimp-like sounds. "Cooo-oooh...could you...?" she turned to indicate her tail while holding the front of her pants up at the waist.

    Amy's eyes twinkled to show that she understood. She flexed a finger and a sharp claw jutted out of it. Carefully, she cut a hole in Lina's pants large enough for her monkey tail. Once it was done, she helped Lina guide the tail through. With her pants back on, Amy once again grabbed Lina's arm.

    Her normal hair was gone, replaced with a long mane of straight black hair that was thicker and wilder than before. Her cheeks also had fur on them, and her ears were huge. Her nose was pulled into her face more, revealing two prominent nostrils. Her eyes were a tad larger, and her jaw was very pronounced, like a chimp's. Her entire body was covered in long, coarse black hair. Her hands were a little larger, and her arms were a few inches longer. Her long tail was waving behind her. She looked closely, and realized that her clothes were a little baggier than before. She must have shrunk at least six inches.

    "This is great!" she said. I even feel more energetic! Like I could hang from the rafters of this musty old place!" She did a few cartwheels down the aisle.

    "Meow! Glad you like it," Amy said. "Now what's next?" She gave a playful glance at Sam, who looked amazed at Lina's incredible change.
