"This is a Chimney lighter, you toss some newspaper into it underneath and some charcoal briquets on the vents up top..     Moonlight trickles through the air as we all laugh and sit around burgers, brats, and even some more items like steaks and little quiches and some more comfort foods for the tamer delights.. Little bagel pizzas, shishkabobed chicken skewers with purple, white, and red onions and three colors of peppers, piles of barbeque sauce, and teriyaki, and exotic hoisin sauces and hot chili oil and hot sauce are piled around.

The juicy meat slides down and gives a wonderful meaty feeling, it's fresh, juicy, and some of the ground beef flavors seem accentuated with a little extra beefy flavor of some extra beef bouillon mixed in to give a richer flavor.. The char of the burgers with the mesquite smoke adds a nice and juicy flavor hinted with smoke.. You can't pick out quite what makes smoke smoke and so delicious on a burger.. But it's like the difference that makes a rich and meaty soy sauce completely differentiated from bland tofu.. Even though they're still technically the same thing..
