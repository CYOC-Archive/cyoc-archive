    Ocean sometimes bemoaned the hippy name that his folks had given him.
    This was especially so after his kid brother John was named. He was
    happy his folks had given John his "normal" name, but sometimes he was
    jealous of him too. Still there were advantages to having a semi-
    unique name - Ocean had only met one other kid with his name though
    Google said their were actually thousands.

    Anyway, today his brother was being particularly annoying.

    "John, I need to take a shower. I've got a date in 30 minutes," Ocean
    said entering the bathroom.

    John was kneeling by the half full tub wearing rubber gloves with a
    plastic box next to him.

    "You can't be cleaning the tub, so what are you doing?" Ocean
    continued adjusting his towel which he could feel coming loose around
    his waist.

    "I'm priming my new Terrarium Transformation Stone," John answered.

    "Priming?"

    "Yes, the instructions said to activate it, I needed to put in a bath
    of hot water."

    Ocean laughed, "Well, I think they just meant to soak it in hot water.
    You could've used the sink. Do you know how many gallons are in that
    tub? Not very enviromentally sound thinking, bro. Let's see that
    rock taking the rock out of the tub."

    "The instructions said to wear rubber gloves to avoid getting damaging
    oils from hands on my terrarium components," John scolded.

    "Dude, this is just a rock. Besides I'll wash it off, while I take my
    bath. No need wasting good water," Ocean said stepping into the tub
    still wearing his towel. Standing in the water, he dropped the rock
    into water by his feet, and pushed John away from the tub. "Now I've
    only got 25 minutes to get bathed and get ready for my date. I can't
    believe she's going as mermaid. She wants me to go as her human
    prince and wear purple tights! Maybe I can think up an appropriate
    alternate costume while I soak in privacy in the tub. Now scoot, and
    hang my towel up as you leave," he added. Ocean whipped the towel from
    around his waist and threw it in John's face.

    John said, "Ew!" He put the towel on the rack and scooped up his
    terrarium and bolted from the bathroom, as Ocean sat down into the hot
    water.

    As he closed the door behind him, John heard Ocean say, "Maybe I'll
    just wear a swimsuit."

    John muttered, "Big brothers." He shook his head.

    He continued to assemble his terrarium. He glanced at the clock 20
    minutes had elapsed. His terrarium was ready except for the
    transformation stone. Ocean couldn't still be in the tub. John
    chuckled as he remembered that Ocean tended to fall asleep in the tub
    while he was soaking. Still Ocean would probably blame John if he was
    late, and Ocean really wanted to complete his terrarium and to do that
    he needed the rock.

    He headed to the bathroom door, and tried the knob it was still
    unlocked. He pushed the door open, and stepped inside...
