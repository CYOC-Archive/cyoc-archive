    Calum went to his room and got dressed. The room felt strangely small and he bumped his desk while moving around the space. The monitor blinked on and something about the program that popped up made some part of him cry out to give it attention.

    Painfully slowly dots connected. He isn't naturally a super masculine 13 year old, his father was abusing and drugging him, he was... he was... trying to figure out how tall he wanted to become to offset the forced steroid abuse. That's why he's built like an 18 year old.

He found where he had accidentally messed up his perception of reality, or maybe it was intentional, or just to a greater extent than intended... He dialed it back and then remembered what he did to his dad and step-mom...

Glancing beyond the bruising, it seemed he was lacking body hair and beard shadow... Calum recalled wanting to steal his masculinity and succeeding, and couldn't help but rest his cheek on his hand feeling his stubble as his father was more and more emasculated... Then Calum saw his dad's breakfast. "Dad! What are you eating?"

While the rest of the family had healthy, nutritious, filling meals, his dad was eating a can of sweetened condensed milk, half a cup of olive oil to garnish. "Dad, that will spike your insulin and you'll not only be hungry in an hour but blow up like a blimp, if your intestines don't kill you first."
