    Ty turns a geek into a muscle-geek

    Ty went out into the weight room and spotted a geeky-looking kid just sitting on a plastic chair, playing with
    his cell phone. The kid was obviously just playing Angry Birds on his iPhone, and he really didn't come to the
    gym to work out.

    A few minutes had passed and a taller, older boy walked up. This was his older brother. Ty knew right there
    that the kid was waiting for his big brother to finish his workout in the gym.

    Ty concentrated his powers on the geeky boy and learned that his name was Alex. He was about 16 years old and
    he wore a t-shirt, shorts and he had a pair of glasses on. He was very smart. Ty saw that Alex was a whiz in
    math and science as well, plus he had won awards in school for math and history projects.

    "What a shame that this geeky kid doesn't work out in the gym..." Ty sighed. "Which explains why he is so
    skinny! Why can't there be a geek who is muscular? Hmm..."

    Dustin overheard what Ty was mumbling to himself.

    "Ty! What are you gonna do? This kid is a geek! Don't mess with him! You'll ruin his life!" Dustin warned him.

    "WHO says geeks have to be thin and scrawny all the time?" Ty wondered. "Geeks can also be muscular!"

    Ty then stared at Alex and wished for the geeky boy to become a muscle kid, right then and there!

    Suddenly, Alex felt a warm, tingling sensation in his muscles! His calves in his legs began to grow hard as
    they inflated like balloons! His quads above the knees grew thicker and wider and deeper, making his legs
    bulge with raw, thick muscle! Long, thick veins suddenly popped up over the boy's legs, as thick as ropes!
    Alex's flat stomach suddenly re-formed into a valley of hills and buttons as mighty abdominal muscles formed
    in rows and columns as a six-pack! And, right above that, his pectorals pushed outwards and sideways, blowing
    up like inflated balloons! His mighty pectorals made his shoulders wider while broad lats formed ust below his
    arms! Thick, powerful biceps, triceps and traps formed over the arms and shoulders as well, giving the boy
    that V-taper look that bodybuilders had. Within moments, Alex was ripped with muscle!

    "Hey, bro... Are you ready... WHOA!?" his brother gasped, as Alex stood up, almost ripping out of his t-shirt!

    Alex looked stunning! The boy was just huge with amazing muscles everywhere!

    "Heyyyy... I'm strong!" Alex grinned as he flexed his arms, allowing big, round biceps to fill his arms.

    "What the heck!!!" his brother gasped in amazement as Ty and Dustin grinned while watching from a distance.

    "There! A geek with muscle, and he is still just as smart as before!!!" Ty laughed. "I should do that again!"

    Ty looked around the gym and spotted someone else...
