    An hour passes and Will starts to regain consciousness, when he hears a knock on the front door, “yo, Will,” he hears the familiar voice of his partner calling, “get your ass out here”.

    “Shit,” the 10-year-old boy whispers, starting to panic, “I gotta think fast,” he looks around, his eyes land on his uniform, which is still lying on the kitchen floor. Suddenly he gets an idea, he grabs his phone and opens the app, ‘I don’t want to return to being a grown up… at least not yet,’ he thinks to himself, ‘I still want to have some fun’. The “boy” scans the uniform, and is amazed to see a folder pop up labeling “uniform of Will Williams”, he opens the file and sees the following:

    One blue uniform jumper
    One plain white button shirt
    One blue uniform jacket
    One blue uniform pants
    One pair of white socks
    One pair of black police boots
    One black boxer shorts

    The boy quickly types in the following, while still hearing the knocks and yelling of his partner:

    One blue pajama top with the Spongebob Squarepants print (former uniform jumper)
    One blue pajama pants with Spongebob Squarepants print (former uniform pants)

    Lastly he changes the age setting from “male adult” to “10-year-old boy”, and hits confirm. The “boy” watches in amazement as his clothes start to wriggle, shrinking in size as they change their appearances. Soon Will’s new pajamas are done and the boy quickly puts them on, ‘perfect fit,’ he thinks to himself, he quickly hides the unchanged parts of his uniform under the couch and slowly walks to the door, and putting on his best “sleepy face” he opens the door. The boy lets out a yawn and rubs his eye, “uh? Wh-who are you,” he asks.

    Will’s partner raises an eyebrow, “um… I could ask the same thing,” he says.

    Will thinks fast and comes up with a story, “I… um… I’m Will’s nephew,” he says, trying his best to act shy and nervous, hoping that his partner buys it.

    “Oh really,” his partner asks, “what is your name?”.

    “Um… m-my name is Dr-Draco,” Will says, remembering the Harry Potter stories and movies, “um… Dr-Draco Smith, s-sir”.

    “Hm… Will never mentioned he had a nephew,” the police officer says, “my name is Todd, Todd Davis”.

    “It… it’s nice meeting… you,” Will/Draco replies, “um… yeah… my un-uncle doesn’t talk a lot about me,” he continues.

    “And why is that,” Todd asks.

    “I… um… I am pretty… um… well… mischievous,” the “boy” replies, “un-uncle Will is n-not that pr-proud of me”.

    The cop nods, “yeah… I understand why he never mentioned you,” he says, “do you know where he is,” he then asks.

    Will laughs mentally, ‘man, my partner is easy to convince,’ he thinks to himself, before replying, “um… n-no sir, he left his phone here. M-maybe he already went to work?”.

    Todd thinks about it, “that is not like him,” he mutters, “he hates driving that busted up Beetle”.

    ‘Yeah… good thing I got rid of it a few days ago, I am still looking for a replacement,’ he thinks to himself, ‘with the Chronivac I might not even have to search long,’ the boy grins inwardly as he imagines transforming a sexy young boy into his dream car.

    Todd sighs, “well, if he shows up, tell him to call me ASAP,” he says, “I’ll tell the Chief, that Will suddenly fell ill. I still owe him a favor”.

    The “boy” nods, “I will, sir,” he says.

    Todd smiles, “please, call me Todd,” he says.

    Will nods, “um… I’d like to continue my sleep,” he says, “I still have a few hours until school starts”.

    “Oh… of course,” Todd says, “sorry to have woken you”.

    Will smiles, “no problem, sir… I mean Todd,” he says. Todd smiles back and takes his leave, Will closes the door and walks into the living room. He pulls the curtains back a little and peeks through the slit. Will watches as his partner gets into the police car and drives off, once the car is out of sight Will leans against the wall, sliding down to sit on the floor, “that was close," he mumbles to himself, "I really dodged a bullet there". After Will had calmed down, he thought of what to do next, after a long thought he decides to...
