Oh, wait, did Ryan finally do something to get Vinnie in trouble? Is that why Vinnie's in the VP's office, I just figured it was to discuss his Valedictory Speech. Maybe I'll get to give it after all!" There was glee in her voice as she walked toward her next class without waiting for slack-jawed Horst's reply. She assumed he didn't know anything.

    "Have him wait. I have another appointment. I'll see him in about 20 minutes."

    He disconnected the intercom, and ducked out the side door from his office. He was holding Carter's class schedule in his hand.

    Ten minutes later, Horst was told to go into the VP's office. He was surprised to see Carter already sitting in the chair facing the VP's desk. No one else was visible in the office. The door closed shut behind him. He turned and saw the VP.
