    "Well, depends, what's your item?" She asked.

    Emily pulled out the polaroid and handed it to the woman. She looked it over and handed it back.
    "From the look of it I'd say it's a random type. Probably changes the person in the camera randomly right?" She asked.

    "Yeah, I think so, though I've only tried it twice." Emily replied.

    "Well sorry but your only option is to keep trying. Eventually you'll get a transformation that'll bring him back or almost back. Also, be carful cause there are a few switches on that thing for awareness and other stuff. You and him are the only ones who will notice reality changing, but that could alter if you play with that so be carful." She warned.

    "Ok... I guess we'll just have to keep trying. Thank you os much... um..." Emily continued before she realized she didn't even know this woman's name.

    "Mary. And here's my number. I'll put you in a group chat with other victims like us. Some still have their items and see it as a responsibility, while others are like me who completely screwed up and are just needing someone to talk to." Mary explained.
