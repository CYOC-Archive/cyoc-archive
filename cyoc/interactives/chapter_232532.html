    “What about jewellery?” you asked

    “Nothing planned. Tracy doesn’t even want us to wear rings. She wants to keep everything very simple”

    “So these are my choices? I can’t even be a bra again?”

    “Actually, I don’t think I want to be a bra anyway. And definitely not your panties. I’d effectively be blind again”

I was thinking you should be my dress and we could put the sticker high up on my bodice, so if the sticker does become your eyes your view would be pretty close to normal. I initially thought of the ribbon thinking I could wear it my hair but a hairdresser is waiting for me at the motel and may not want me wearing a ribbon”
