He looked at you confused. You then questioned "I never worn any shoes or socks. Right?" "Why would you with those pads on your paws?" He asked you. "Silly me." You fake laughed. "Get off to school son." He said with a smile. As you were walking you thought sighing [So no more wearing shoes or socks for me.] Later at school... You were eating lunch you were thinking [How long will I be enough human to be considered human? How long will I be at all human?] Soon your friend Max walked up & sat at the table with you. "Remember what we were talking about yesterday?" You asked him. "What was that?" He asked. "About me turning into a fox." You reminded him. "You turning into a fox?" Max asked seeming puzzled. You sighed & asked him curious "What fox parts have I always had?" "Fox ears, a fox tail, fox paws & your fox fur." "Have I always had those?" You asked. "Yep. Sense I've known you." He answered. "Have I ever worn anything on my paws?" You asked. "Never seen you do that. You always been barefoot. Barepawed." He answered. You sighed because apparently he doesn't remember the conversation you two had yesterday. "Why you asking these odd questions?" Max asked confused. "Forget I said anything." You told him. [It appears Max totally forgot our conversation about me becoming more & more fox & less & less human by the day. And of course everyone remembers me always having fox paws. And never wearing anything on my paws.] You thought sighing sad. You finished your lunch & got up from the table. Before you walked away you told Max sadly "The day I wake up as a full fox I have a strong feeling you won't know me." "What you talking about [your name]?" He asked seeming confused. "Somehow I got a strong almost certain feeling I'll be a wild fox living in a forest by the end of this. And we would have never met. Or ever will." You told him strongly sensing that's what is going to happen. That's how this is going to end. "Why you saying this weird crazy stuff man?" He asked puzzled. "Nevermind. I know you wouldn't remember this conversation tomorrow anyway." You told him sighing. You heard Max say "Weird stuff to say." You were sure he didn't realize you heard him. And what did it matter if he did realize you heard him? You somehow knew he wouldn't remember any of what you said today. He wouldn't remember any of the conversation you two had. You now knew no one can help you. No one will believe you when you tell them you are turning more & more into a fox by the day. And they wouldn't remember you telling them the next day either. You headed up to your room & laid down on your bed. You looked yourself over & wondered what part of you will change next. You wondered how long you'll still be even part human. You now knew in your soul you were going to in the end become nothing but a wild feral fox living somewhere in the wild; & will always have been. There was nothing you could do to stop it. You just wondered how long it going to be before that reality change happens. You knew your life as a human was ending. That after reality shifted so you've always been a wild feral fox no human would know you; because they never met you. That your dad wouldn't remember you because you were never human & had never of been his son. [Why is this happening to me?!] You thought in confused sorrow. You have no idea why you are becoming more & more fox by the day. And you somehow know you'll never ever know why it's happening.
