    Andrew.

    Chills ran down his spine when he saw Andrew’s image on the screen! How was this possible? He looked to the right of the image and saw a list of personal attributes; each one had a drop-down menu or text box in which he could change each attribute.

    Ty chuckled a bit, finding humor in Andrew’s high-pitched voice. “Dude… it’s not that… have you looked in the mirror?”

    Ty looked back at his computer and noticed a new option next to Andrew’s picture: “Undo”. So he could go back to how he was before.
