    “This fine specimen is Jen, the world’s first, and only, living dragoness. Jen arrived just yesterday, but I can happily report that she is already settling in well.” Patterson paused again as she studied Jen closely, looking for a reaction. “She and her partner Max, who you will meet in a minute, have already... ‘gotten to know each other’ if you know what I mean!”

    “And,” Patterson resumed, “you will then get to watch Jen and Max performing together. Hopefully this will include some courting behaviour and, if we’re really lucky, the world’s first dragon egg laying!”
