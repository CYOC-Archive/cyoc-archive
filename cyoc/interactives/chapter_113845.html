    Blood of the Hunter, and Predator to the Weak (redux)

    added by Dragonman 6 years ago O

    You're name is Daxon (yes fantasy name I know) your mother only wanted to live a normal life without poverty, and partially with you. However from the day you were told that you were Demonblood you really thought you were doomed to hell, and didn't want to embrace it any more than you had to. Despite this you feel as if the wild calls to you for some reason, and you knew this was the day that you would meet your 'father' of sorts. Your mother knew this, and told you,"If you choose to stay in the mortal realm then do so, if you don't then please do visit. But if you do keep me out of this life of yours."

    Your mothers words were fresh in your mind, but you ultimately knew you couldn't forgive her for giving into temptation to a demon, to your father you would meet today. As you wandered towards the woods thinking you would probably meet your father there a portal of flames appeared before you. A voice from the portal echoed through and said,"Daxon you know it is time, now please step through the portal, and know your fate." You knew that this moment would come so you sighed, and said,"Why not? Let's get this over with." You then stepped through the portal into what appeared to be Hell itself, but at the same time you were inside a cave, and your body seemed to be oddly adapted to the heat, and dryness. You then saw a black furred wolf with orange, and red cinders all over his body. The wolf demon had no apparel, nor weapons, and stood on two legs like an anthropomorphic creature.

    "Hello Daxon, I am Fenris demon of predation, and nature, the hunt is all I represent in my existence." The wolf demon, Fenris said.

    "So your my dad huh? So what happened to my human father? Don't play dumb mom told me everything about what happened that night." You said.

    "I see you're angered by me. I understand, and I shall answer your question to the best of my abilities. The human who would have been your father died two months after your mother was pregnant, and I saw this, and oddly enough I for once pitied the prey, and offered her my assistance. In reality the child that I shared my soul with wasn't you, for I destroyed that soul, and replaced it with a shard of my own." Fenris said.

    "So your not just a killer, but you kill a mere fetus too." You said angrily

    "Not exactly, reincarnation exists, and the soul in the infant that I destroyed was already on it's 87th cycle through. In my case I killed a soul that was rather quite old, and wouldn't have lasted another cycle. However human emotions are quite odd to me, so I ask why do you hate me?" Fenris asked

    "I don't....." you began, and sighed,"I don't hate you exactly, it's just once mom told me everything I didn't expect you to be a demon, and on top of that I'm half of one because of you. So in the end I guess my destiny is to I don't know slaughter millions, enslave the human race!? You tell me!" You yelled.

    "You know so much yet so little," Fenris sighed,"I don't slaughter sentient life, except for the demons below me, and those who rebel. My only duty is to maintain that predators remain on top by any means necessary, as well as hunt myself, and create new predators to combat the increasing number of prey. All thanks with the help of Anubis of course, but that's besides the point. I only help maintain the cycle of natural selection, and the food chain. Without me no being would know their own power, their own pride, or their own instincts for hunting. There would be rabbits challenging wolves, and rabbits succeeding, humans becoming slaves to non sentient animals, or even worse. Daxon, I may be the only demon lord who understands the meaning of order, and lives to withhold the rights, and enforces order." Fenris explained

    You looked at Fenris in shock, everything he said wasn't exactly wrong at all, and made sense. "I guess I see your point, but why am I here exactly? What do you want from me dad?" You asked

    Fenris gave you a subtle look of compassion, and said,"Well son I was hoping you would join me in the hunt, and assist me as omega until it was my time for you to step up. Then we would support the chains, and order as alphas, and equals. However, that isn't the only thing I called you here for my son. You see there is something I had to do to my soul to convert it into a human fetus. I had to alter my DNA, and like all demonic DNA mine is infectious to all parts of the body. This means your human DNA will be overtaken by demon DNA rather quickly by the time I am seeing you now, and I have no way to stop it. On top of this your DNA grew unstable, you may be genetically, or demonically altered in other ways such as gender, age, body functions, mental intentions, or lust."

    "So your saying that I will become like you, but genetically mutated beyond what even demons may consider normal?" You asked

    "Potentially yes, but by joining with me can help that. You see I have created a special pack rune that when engraved on you will help control this, however due to the safeguard you would become fully demon. I know how you must feel towards me, and your mother, but ultimately we only want what you want. You can take the pact, and join me, gain immortality, hunt with me, maintain order, and possibly even visit your mother back in the human realm; However, at the cost of your human life, friends, and other things. Or you could return to your human life, but once your body starts transforming you could become something of an abomination even by demonic standards, and you would have no choice but to give up your humanity. Ultimately though it is your choice, but consider wisely there is no turning back." Fenris said.

    You thought to yourself,"He seems genuine, what if he's lying? Should I trust him? He's a demon after all, who destroyed a children's soul to create me.....However he stands for order, he's warning me of a change that could ruin my life, he seems to somehow care about me, and he's giving me a choice instead of forcing me."

    What do you chose?
