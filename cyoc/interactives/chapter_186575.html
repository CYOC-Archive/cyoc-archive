    But this is going to be their biggest (or smallest) prank yet.

    While they were playing football in the park, Billy accidentally kicked the ball into the far side of the park. When he went to go get it he stumbled upon a large fountain tucked behind some trees. On the plaque at the bottom it read.

    “Drink from the large bowl and you will grow to the size of a skyscraper. Drink from the small bowl and you will shrink to the size of a mouse”

    Billy had heard about this fountain online, it allowed people to change sizes just by drinking the water. He couldn’t believe he found it. Billy called Joey over and he too was amazed.
