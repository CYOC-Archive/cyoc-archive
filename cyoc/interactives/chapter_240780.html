Please just tell me if something is wrong!"

    Alice wasn't happy with that answer, she stomped her foot down. "What are you not telling me? Are you cheating on me? You're cheating on me, aren't you?"

    James exhaled sharply, planting his feet on the ground to try to calm his anger. "Oh my fucking God Alice, no I'm not cheating on you! Please just give me space for a bit, okay!"

    Alice wasn't happy with that either as she picked up a nearby glass and threw it at James. James managed to dodge last second, shattering at the wall behind him. Somehow, that was the sound that woke up the baby in the nearby room, James' three-month-old sister Carly. This was so terrible! He had to do something that would end this fight!! His parents are hiding something from each other, if only they were more honest. Oh wait, maybe that's it! James opened the book again, thinking about human instincts once more.

Jordon looks towards his wife with very wide eyes. "Alice, I'll take care of Carly this time. Please go to our bedroom, we can talk about this from there." Alice nodded and turned around and brisked off to the master bedroom while Jordon goes the opposite direction to Carly's room.
