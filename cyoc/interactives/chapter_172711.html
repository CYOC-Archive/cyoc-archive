    Now as usually happens in these transformations Ricky
    had neglected to read the fine print at the bottom of the
    spell in that book.
    Basically the fine print at the bottom of the page dealt with the
    finer alrterations to the spell
    such as gender,duration,size and subspecies amongst
    Western dragons.

    Had Ricky just waddled off and breathed fire,roared and gobbled
    up a nearby sheep the main spell would have ended in a hour or
    three and Ricky would have found himself being 4'3" 125 pound
    human Ricky again.

    Alas he didn't and hung around the cursed pool with the bird blood
    and the other stuff that had changed him.
    And repeated exposure to that cursed pool,bird blood and the like
    was what was mentioned in the fine print of the spell.

    So dragonified Ricky waded back into the pool,soaked up that spilt blood from the dead beheaded female bird ,and afterwards rubbed his wings and aching back against that nearby tree infecting his cut sores with all manner of tree sap,bark and other linden leaves.

    Nothing happened at that moment beyond relieving some of the
    skin irritation he was feeling but Ricky had mucked up a safe
    simple three hour dragon transformation somewhat.
    ***********************
    So Ricky's three hour stint as a dragon came and went and somehow he had managed to avoid drawing attention to himself with all those roars.
    The nude wimpy younf man returned to the pool where he'd left
    his clothing,put them on and got in his dad's old cargo van and
    headed back to the garage/warehouse/apartment building his
    dad owned.

    As Ricky got out of the parked cargo van that size wise
    the silent dark warehouse was big enough to carry
    out his dragon transformations in.

    So now that he had a sense of what to do the landlords son
    assembled a 'artificial' transformation centre he could try out in that warehouse.
    15 foot diameter inflatable pool to serve as a 'lake'
    Check
    Tree ?
    He went to a nursery and bought a linden tree sampling already in a 33 gallon pot
    ( for some strange reason Ricky nowe had a hunch the spell would
    only work with a 33 gallon planter,not a 25 or a 50 gallon pot )
    so Check
    the warehouse already had a North facing window so Check on that item
    Even back then there were already signs Ricky should have taken
    heed of that he'd somehow 'broken' the safe spell and was going into dangerous spell casting.
    As an example when he went to examine that spell book he'd kept in his bedroom although the rest of the pages looked unaltered when he went to the section dealing with 'Dragons' it was a little
    hard not to notice that the number of pages dealing with dragon spells had increased nine fold and and pages and ink seemed to
    glow with a inner fiery orange,red and white glow now.
    ***************
    So into the artificial pool sank a nude Ricky the
    next night and vanished into its suddenly bottomless depths.
    Since this was a 15 foot inflatable pool that at most was 3 feet deep
    a panicked Ricky knew something bad was going down.
    Then the transformation took hold of both Ricky's mind and body
    and a while later the young powerful female sea dragon
    a sort of fish faced sea lizard monster with flippers for
    arms and legs and a long finned tail was pushing herself up from
    the depths,not in the warehouse Ricky had set the artificial
    'lake' in but the open cold deep ocean near a beach where human
    swimmers in the shallows.
    The female sea dragon that Ricky had turned himself into certainly
    wasn't wimpy human male thoughts anymore and with her
    belly full of sea dragon eggs that needed laying she was
    more interested in assimilating the nearby humans into her own
    already huge sea dragon body.
    The deed was done,the humans vanished,Ricky laid her sea dragon
    eggs in the shallows near a wreck and then returned to the bottomless depths she had risen from.
    Slowly human intelligence returned as well as a human shape
    and wimpy Ricky found himself,with flashes of distorted beastial
    memories of being a female sea dragon,lying nude and half sprawled out of the inflatable pool in the warehouse.
