    The car of her dreams.

    Branch Full view
    added 7 months ago BM O

    Julia Kyte was a 37-year-old woman who seemed to have a normal life. But there was something she wanted for a while, a car of her own.

    Since she was young, she always wanted to have a car with which she could drive and travel fast, but due to certain problems, she could never get it. And since she didn't have a car, she almost simply had to travel by public transport or even had to ask one of her friends for help.

    She thought it would take a long time for her to finally get her own car, but that would change soon.

    On her way to work, Julia was walking calmly until she suddenly came across a small coin on the sidewalk. When picking it up, she noticed that this coin looked somewhat old and that it had some strange symbols and said some words like "Arzu" or "Wish".

    Noticing what it said on the coin, Julia got a little curious and proceeded to flip the coin:

    "I wish I had my own car," Julia said as she flipped it over.

    On which side did the coin land?
