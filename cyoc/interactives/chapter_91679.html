    As your senses started kicking back in you felt smaller. You looked down and saw that your body had shrank down to the size of a child, and was happy that your clothes had shrank down into a small white dress made from down feathers. Quickly though you had gained some new sensations that were kind of funny and tingly. You looked at Scion and Lisa and the tingling got a bit stronger. Thinking to yourself you put your hands to your ears and confirmed that you were now and elf and that tingling was probably your body being more in tune with magic.
    Scion saying "As you see one human now is an elf, don't see many around here so I figure one more should be good for diversity. Now then for a bit of a stronger trick i'll ask for a little magical help from our more magical breed of beings here. Simply clasp your hands together and think of home."
    Walking up to Lisa you felt like you could tell how much more mature Lisa was than you were despite how humans may have thought you two were roughly the same age. Lisa said "don't worry i'm not going to hurt you."
    You locked hands with her and then tried to think of your home back at the palace, but what you could only really think of was some grand tree in the middle of a bunch of wood and cobble buildings. you thought to yourself that this may be an ingrained feeling elves have about home, and that what you could be seeing is the elf holy land's capital. As you opened your eyes when Scion commanded you saw that there was a tree that had sprouted near you that reminded you of the tree in your little vision.
    Snapping his fingers Scion said "What good is a beautiful illusion tree if it does not bear fruit right?" in the branches little pink bulbs started to grow and then drop from the tree into the crowd slowly. Catching the treats the crowd was enjoying the little show. The show continued and somewhere in the middle you and Lisa were brought back stage. When you had some time you asked Lisa "Was that tree and buildings I saw th-"
    Lisa explained "No, it's merely some self projected city with a tree of the soul. It's hard to explain to an elf who was only and elf for a few minutes. It took me a decade to really understand it."
    sighing a little you guess "So do you think me being an elf right now is a little bit of an insult?"
    Lisa asked "Do you want me to be honest?"
    "Sure, i'm all for transparency!"
    "I think the insult was your magic, trapping the soul of a majestic creature for power... It's just despicable."
    "What do you mean? I was born with my magic. I don't even think my family has ever trapped any magic creatures."
    Lisa shook her head and said "I wonder if it would be any easier for you to see if you stayed an elf for a little longer."
    "Dawn Where Are You!?!" you heard a somewhat voice call to you. Turning your head you saw it was one of your bodyguards.
    you asked "What are you doing here?"
    the bodyguard turned to Lisa and said "Not a word or your forest could get burned down..." then he continued "Your cover has been nearly blown. You are coming back right now." Your bodyguard picked you up like a kid and you thrashed a bit.
    "Put me down!" you stated defiantly
    the bodyguard sighed and murmured "easier to deal with when she was this small..." then brought you to the castle where you...
