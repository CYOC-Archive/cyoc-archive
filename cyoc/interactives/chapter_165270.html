    Hunky Sam to Sam the Hulking Bodybuilder

    I looked at Sam. Wow, what a hunk of a guy! He looked awesome. I was eager to go up to him and meet the kid just when a truck pulled up in our driveway. It was a friend of my dad’s with the bed of the truck filled with large boxes which were all taped shut.

    “Hey there! I got a dozen boxes filled with books for your dad!” the man said as he got out of the truck. “I need some strong arms to unload them and carry them into the house! These boxes are about sixty pounds each!”

    “Oh… Okay!” I sighed as I walked into the house. My dad had just finished setting up three large empty bookcases and these were his books. I just didn’t feel like breaking my back over lifting all these heavy boxes. Then, I remembered Sam! Couldn’t I just have the muscle kid do all the heavy work for me?

    I walked back outside, and I saw him water the plants. Sure, he was buff, but could he handle lifting and carrying all these heavy boxes? Then, I just figured the heck with it. I already gave him some muscle. I might as well make him change from a hunk into something bigger, like a bodybuilder, right?

    I held the ring and twisted it as I uttered my wish, very quietly.

    “I wish Sam’s muscles would grow even more until he were to become a pro teen bodybuilder with a strong desire to always lift weights in the gym and to continually pose and show off his gigantic muscles everywhere.” I say quietly.

    “Uhhmmm!” Sam groans as he drops the garden hose. The hunky teen let out a mighty roar as his muscles inflated even more, for the second time, transforming him from a hunk into a massive bodybuilder Hulk! His legs grew thick and wide, showing mighty vertical columns of muscle above his knees. His calves grew as big as soccer balls, fully covered in a web of thick veins everywhere. His stomach was now covered in the most well-defined area of abdominal muscles on the planet, showing thick, rectangular tiles of muscle which resembled a brick wall.

    Sam’s pectorals inflated into large orbs of solid muscle, becoming big and round and solid. This forced his shoulders to become even wider, so that the V-taper was obvious from the front and the back. His wide shoulders also supported the impressive delts, lats and traps. His thick arms sported huge football-sized biceps and horseshoe triceps on each side and he now had forearms the size of bowling pins. Truly, the teen had muscles which were huge, putting him in the bodybuilder category. He was tanned and solid, and his arms and thick legs were completely covered in veins everywhere. The teenage boy now resembled a Junior Mr. Olympia who could have entered and possibly won a bodybuilding competition hands down. The teen hunk was now a teenage Hulk!

    So, I strolled up to him to say hi.

    “Hey there! I’m the guy next door! I just saw you out here and I was wondering if you could do me a favor?” I asked him.

    “Sure! What can I do for you?” he said in a friendly tone of voice.

    He walked next to me as I led him to the back of the truck which was parked in our driveway where I pointed out all the boxes which were in the bed of the truck.

    “They just delivered all these heavy boxes filled with books. They all need to be carried into the house and since you look like a pretty strong guy…” I said to him as I flexed my thin, weak arm.

    “Oh, not a problem. No problem at all!” Sam grinned, happily flexing both arms for me, as this was his chance to show off his massive biceps.

    Of course, Sam grabbed each box and carried it into the house like it weighed nothing at all. For him, this was nothing more than a light workout. He was done in just ten minutes, doing what would have taken me an hour, using his massive muscles! When he finished, he waved goodbye and walked back into his house next door. What a hunk!

    I strolled into the house, enjoying the fact that the books were inside, and I didn’t need to lift a finger. The weather was so nice, I decided to ride my bike on the street for a few minutes. Maybe I could use my ring and change a few more people along the way?
