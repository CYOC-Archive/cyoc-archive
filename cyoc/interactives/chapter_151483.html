    Well Kyle had watched a few horror movies involving
    Bee Girls,Wasp Women and the like
    and kind of liked the notion but not the corny f/x makeup of seeing
    aticked off woman infected with Wasp Queen Rejuvenation Jelly
    getting angry and suddenly buzzing as their teeth fused
    into a Wasp Queens beak and then chopped up ex lovers and the
    like.They usually got gunned down in a hail of police bullets
    and lay there dead in the final closing scene of the movie
    all freaky and deformed.

    No what Kyle wanted was a true Wasp Queen,a giant mean
    nasty wasp that no hail of gunfire could take out so
    easily.
    Obviously for such a monster to survive for any
    period of time it needed some survival
    skills to allow it to blend into human society till its
    rage brought up the killer wasp in it again.
    And Kyle wanted the monster he planned to turn
    Jennifer into to obey his commands and then blend
    back into human camaflouge till the next time.

    So Kyle gave Jennifer some pretty detailed information ,including of course forgetting she was the
    Monster.
    He hung up the phone and for all intents and purposes
    Jennifer was just her bitchy cheerleader self again.
    Yet for some reason she remembered she had a appointment with that
    jerk Kyle in the Town Park at 7 PM.

    The day progressed and Jennifer finished cheerleading
    practice at the school and headed to the Town Park
    to meet Kyle.
    He was standing there nervously near the old
    bunker.
    They chatted and though Jennifer didn't know it Kyle
    was checking her to see if she would transform without the
    Trigger he had wired into her.
    She certainly hated him,and if Jennifer had just
    tf'd in front of him he planned to head for that bunker beside them.
    It was crunch time and Kyle drew in a deep breath as he
    uttered
    the phrase that would Trigger the transformation.
    It was GIANT WASP QUEEN
    in a archaic language he was pretty sure neither Jennifer
    or anyone else but him knew.
    Jennifer just stood there sweating for a few seconds before
    the BUZZ became pronounced.
    Her eyeballs seemed to swell in her eyesockets
    and become the compound eyes of a insect.
    Her dress began to tear as her body began to grow and
    somewheres around the seven foot high at the shoulders
    mark she bent over on all fours and shifted.
    One second she had been freakishly giant Jennifer and seconds
    later a giant female wasp was shaking the shredded human
    clothes off of its thorax and abdomen.
    The Monster buzzed angrily at Kyle and he
    dashed for the bunker and closed the door just in time as the
    Monster slammed against the bunker angrily.
    Jennifer made a pretty good Monster.
    Three minutes later the buzzing and banging abruptly died
    away and cautiously Kyle opened the bunkers door
    and looked around.There seemed to be no sight of her but as Kyle backed away from the bunker he spotted her
    nude and human again and totally unconscious lying
    on the bunker roof fourteen feet above him.
    Not a climb human Jennifer was capable of but the Monster
    had had no problem flying up to the top of the bunker before
    it had changed back into its human form.
