    mind. So...he remembered Nate being this skinny, awkward guy. But Nate
    didn't seem to remember that. Was he going mad?

    He kept torturing himself with these thoughts over and over. He barely
    slept that night, and the next morning when Nate went to work he was
    still too shocked to really do anything about it. However, he decided
    to try to recreate what he'd done last night, have a look at the
    website again. So Sam again found Nate's laptop, minimized some video
    of two dudes fucking, and found the One Click-a-Day website in the
    background. There was Nate's figure, with his occupation and a
    revolving model of his body. And it gave various other options which
    Sam hadn't seen the night before, giving details about every aspect of
    Nate's life.

    "Is this some kind of bio he's built for porn?" Sam wondered. But
    there was something about this site that didn't add up, something that
    led him to think it was more than that.

    On a whim, he decided to change Nate's body mass. That'd be nice and
    easy to notice, and not something he could change easily on his own,
    like hair color. So he bumped it up from 170 up to 200, but made this
    extra be fat so that Nate would be a bit of a bear cub. Sure enough,
    his model changed, with a decent gut but also you could see there was
    some power in there still from the muscle he'd had. Sam again selected
    'Save (Unaware)' (this being the default option from when Nate had
    made all the changes to Sam), and anxiously waited for him to come
    home. Sam was still trying to convince himself that the site was just
    a profile for his porn work but somehow that didn't feel right to him.

    Finally, after what seemed like hours, he heard Nate's keys turning in
    the door. And there, walking into the apartment was a solid, bulky
    bear cub, who Sam instantly recognized as his roommate Nate. Sam
    struggled to contain his excitement - it had worked! He could change
    anything with this site!

    "Hey," Nate said as he wandered to the fridge to make himself a few
    sandwiches to snack on before dinner, "How was your day?"

    Sam could just stare, unable just yet to string a sentence together.

    "You OK dude?" Nate mumbled through a mouthful of food.

    "Ye-yeah, just got a headache..." Sam murmured.

    "Well feel better. I am bushed after the great fuck I had today, fuck
    I love being a porn star! I'm gonna hit the sack, have a good evening
    bro," Nate said as he went into his room.

    Sam was shocked - he could change anything about Nate now! And by
    extension, about anyone he wanted too! So he went back to Nate's
    laptop, and tried to change something else, only to find an error:

    "You have already used your Change for today. Please come back
    tomorrow"

    But, Sam saw, there was a History tab. So in the meantime, he could
    still see who'd been changed by this computer before.

    "I wonder if he's changed anyone I know...." Sam wondered.
