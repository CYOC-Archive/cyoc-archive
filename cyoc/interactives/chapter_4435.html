    got into the passenger side. His boots were flopping on his legs and he was
    already starting to turn 15. If he had been wearing pants and a belt they
    would have started falling off by now.

    Carolyn kept nagging him as we drove the last bit towards the lab, sounding
    more and more like an upset older sister than an old wife yelling at her old
    husband.

    “My god, just look at me!” said Stevc, looking at his own hands. “I…I’m a
    young’un again…just a kid.” At this point his rustic, way oversized clothing
    over his 13-year-old body made him look like Huck Finn dressing in his dad's
    clothes.

    “See, you melonhead!” said Carolyn. “You always do what your heart tells
    you, though; that’s why I love ya.” Steve blushed visibly. “Don’t worry, if worse
    comes t’ worse I’ll take care of you.”

    “So who’ll take care of you?” laughed Steve.

    We finally pulled into the parking lot of the research center and Steve and
    Carolyn had both lost some more years...he was now 11 and she was 18
    herself, looking quite pretty even though her dress was several sizes too large
    for her now.

    "Let's get to the lab!" said Cindy and we hopped out of the car, Cindy and I
    running like excited kids towards Disneyland. Only then I noticed that there
    were men wearing black suits, sunglasses and gloves waiting for us at the
    door...

    "Subjects confirmed!" one of them said into a walkie-talkie. They grabbed us
    and put handcuffs on us. Over by the car some more men in black grabbed
    Carolyn and Steve, who tried to fight back by kicking and punching, to no
    avail. Then everything went black as a hood went over my head...

    When it was removed I found myself tied to a little kid's chair with Steve and
    Carolyn tied to some reguar-sized chairs in the cell with me. Actually, it
    looked more like an observation chamber, the kind where the cops watch the
    suspect talking to the detective through the one-way mirror. Cindy was tied
    up as well outside the chamber, in a room full of electronic controls,
    monitoring devices, and test equipment. One of the burly guards was in our
    room, and another in the lab room guarding Cindy.

    A middle-aged looking woman with hawkish features wearing a lab coat over
    her white business suit strolled into the room where Cindy was.

    "So, Cindy...we meet again!" she said, sneering. "My, you were quite the cutie
    back then, weren't you? Have you reconsidered my offer?"

    "You!" spat Cindy. "So it *was* you who burnt down the lab and stole my
    equipment! Dr. Ellen Hardy!"

    "Yes, you didn't want to share your research with my company despite our
    generous grant offer," said Dr. Hardy. "Well, it seems that you and a few of
    your friends got quite overexposed to the chemical you were developing in
    that lab of yours. I don't recognize the other two older ones, but that little one"
    - she must have meant me - "is the one that lived close to your lab, correct?"

    Cindy said nothing.

    "Well, I suppose it doesn't matter," said Dr. Hardy. "As you can see, we've
    already taken over your urban facility as well."

    "So why are we still talking?" asked Cindy. She looked like she was stalling
    for time...but why?
