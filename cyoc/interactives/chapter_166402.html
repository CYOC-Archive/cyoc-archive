    The Magic Shop

    Reality altering spell

    Josh breathed heavily as he waddled into the magic shop and made his way towards the spell section. He browsed for a bit but couldn’t find anything that could give him what he wanted. Sighing in disappointment he made his way to the front of the store to ask the lady for some assistance. “ Excuse me?” Josh said as he waddled up to the counter.

    The lady turned around and smiled when she saw Josh. “Well I didn’t expect to see you back here so soon and I see you used to spell.” The lady said smiling.

    “Yeah I have and... wait you still remember me?” Josh said with a bit of confusion in his voice.

    “Of course I remember you.” Replied the lady.

    “But I thought it was supposed to wipe everyone’s memories as well?” Josh said to the lady with a now visibly confused look on his face.

    “Well yes the spell does have that affect however it doesn’t work on masters of magic which I happen to be.” The lady stated.

    Josh nodded in partial understanding but was still a little confused. “If that’s the case then how come the persons body I’m currently in still has memories of me.”

    “Ah yes well the memory part of the spell only works on those whose body you’re not inhabiting. So this person and their body isn’t affected.” The lady replied with a smile.

    “Oh ok I understand.” Said Josh.

    “Good now that we got that out of the way is there something I can help you with?” asked the lady.

    “Well yeah... it’s gonna sound weird but I have grown to liking having a body like this and I was wondering if you have a reality spell of some kind that can help with that?” Josh asked while blushing and giving Jacobs belly a soft pat.

    “I see...” the lady responded.

    “So can you help?” Josh asked again.

    “Well yeah I do have one spell but it’s a one way trip and I don’t give it away lightly.” The lady responded. “So are you positively sure this is something you want?” The lady asked with a sincere tone in her voice.

    “I’m positive.” Josh said with a smile.

    The lady nodded And walked into the back and returned a few moments later with the spell and a lighter. “Here is everything you need please read to spell carefully.” The lady instructed.

    “Thank you so how much for the spell?” Josh asked.

    “It’s on the house.” The lady replied.

    “Really? Thanks!” Josh said surprised as he grabbed the spell and lighter.

    “Don’t mention it and good luck.” The lady replied in a happy tone.

    Josh waddled out of the store as fast as he could and made his way to the back of the store eager to use the spell. Once there he pulled out spell and later and read the instructions.

    Instructions:
    •To activate the spell use a lighter or match to light this paper on fire.
    •Once done recite the following phrase. “ Play burning this spell and reciting this phrase I give up my former life to start a new life in a reality where... (speak the reality you desire to create.) and spell will take affect and be complete

    Warning: Once the spell is complete there is no going back you will be trapped in your new reality forever and new memories will replace your old ones. You will not remember using this spell and any previous spells used will be removed. Please think carefully before casting.

    Once Josh was done reading he began to think long and hard about what to do and decided...
