    "Oh, sorry. Sometimes when I greet someone new, I just love saying that to them. It's from Romeo and Juliet, if you remember your high school days." Angie explained.

    "Right, I guess I forgot about that. Shakespeare was never really my thing or really any of our things." Claudia said pointing to herself and her friends.

    "It kind of became my own thing after college. I saw the movie 'Hamlet' and was actually blown away by it. When I discovered all the other plays he wrote, I looked up a lot of them and fell in love with the bard himself." Angie explained.

    "Well these days I'm kind of a painter. All those Shakespeare stories sparked my passion projects." Angie said showing them some of her paintings on her phone which were very impressive.

    "That's....cool. So I guess you don't have much of a personal life because of all that?" Claudia asked.

    "No actually, I am dating this attractive guy, he's about 8 years younger than me, but still, you know what the bard said: love looks not with the eyes, but with the mind. Been a pleasure talking to you girls, bye." Angie Hart happily smiled as she walked away.

    "That.....didn't go the way I thought." Claudia said still stunned that a former bully had turned her life around so much and had so much going for her.
