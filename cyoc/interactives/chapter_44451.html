    Jason bumps into a 250 lb. black male bodybuilder. He rubs the medallion across the bodybuilder's muscle tee as he falls to the floor and quickly slips it into his pocket. "Are you okay man?" says the friendly bodybuilder as he lends a hand to Jason lifting him up. "Yeah, I'm fine, thanks though." Jason says shyly. He walks away and the bodybuilder looks at Jason inquisitively wondering what he rubbed against his body.

    Jason walks further into the park when he comes by a kid being bullied by much older teenagers. He notices the hell the guy is being put through and walks up to the guy and quickly swipes it through the guy's hand and whispers "Get your revenge." Jason strolls by and is looked at menacingly by the bullies then turn their attention back to the wimp or so they assumed.

    The wimpy guy was clawing his way behind the dumpster and began to convulse. His body was going through a magnificent transformation from sickly loser to musclebound jock. He watched in horror as his puny fame was being swallowed under massive muscles, his testicles grew and shot testosterone throughout his body changing his fear into a state of empowerment and his demeanor from timid to cocky.

    "Come on out you little shit!" The bullies hear movement and instead of witnessing a shrimp crawl from behind the dumpster, they witnessed the rise of a 250lb., 5'7", musclebound god. There the once victim stands proud and turns to his harassers busting out a double bicep pose, and with a deep intimidating voice "Let's have some real fun, boys."
