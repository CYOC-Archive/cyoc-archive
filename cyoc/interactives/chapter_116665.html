He looked through the refrigerated case as he almost salivating at the products of display. Pork, Beef, Chicken. He picked up a large, marbled rib roast, sniffing it, completely oblivious to the other shoppers and then dropping it into his cart.

    it was the same with a couple packages of chicken drumsticks and a couple packages of pork chops. He then wheeled the cart to the front of the store, selecting the self checkout. He scanned each item and then slid his credit card through paying for his purchases. It was only as he was waiting for the transaction to process that he noticed someone else looking at him, a young woman overseeing the self checkout stations.

    “What?” asked Nate.

    “It’s nothing,” she replied.

    “No, really, what?” Nate was now curious.

    “It’s just that I've never seen anyone with pointy ears before." she replied. Nate quickly reached up and discovered his ears had grown and become pointier. He tried not to panic and just smiled at the woman. The machine spit out his receipt and he grabbed it while jamming it into his plastic bag. He dashed to the bathroom, which was thankfully empty and checked himself out in the mirror. He was shocked to see that both of his ears came to rounded lupine points and the backs of them were decidedly fuzzy.

    The scraggly beard he had before was much thicker now and covering his chin and entire neck. He looked at his arms and noticed the hair cover on them had gotten decidedly thicker and if he wasn’t mistaken his nails were a bit longer and thicker as well. He opened his mouth and saw that his canines were noticeably longer and sharper now as well.

He knew this would be the last time he could go out without being watched strangely.
