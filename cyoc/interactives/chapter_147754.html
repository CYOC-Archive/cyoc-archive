    Ann and Jackie woke up at roughly the same time and headed downstairs for breakfast. The two said nothing to each other but simply smiled as they ate their breakfast. Jackie blew a kiss over at Ann who turned her head slightly and giggled.

    "I think I'll take a shower." Ann said as she stood up.

    "Sounds good, I'll go put some clothes on." Jackie said as she headed towards the bedroom again.

    "What? But you didn't bring clothes with you." Ann said.

    "Remember Angel, we're married for the moment, so that means we share a closet. Did you notice the new pictures in the house?" Jackie pointed out to her as she headed towards the bedroom.

    Ann looked around and saw several pictures of the life that she and Jackie had and knew that it would change back to normal by the end of the weekend but she still couldn't help but smile at all the new pictures she saw. There was a picture of the two of them celebrating a birthday together, the two of them in fancy dresses, but the one she loved most of all was their wedding picture. Ann held the picture in her hand and knew that when it really happened, it would be one of the best days of her life.

    Ann then got in the shower and after several minutes, she emerged and got dressed, she also saw that Jackie was also dressed and ready to head out wearing a lovely sun hat.

    "So where should we go?" Ann asked her wife.

    "I was thinking the park, it's a rather beautiful day." Jackie answered.

    "Whatever you say darling." Ann said as the two of them got into their car and drove to the park.

    Ann and Jackie strolled through the park shoulder to shoulder with Ann sometimes laying her head down on Jackie's shoulder as they walked along. They soon purchased some ice cream and sat down at a bench to watch some people pass by.

    "So, do you want to have a little fun?" Jackie said holding up her ring.

    "Well....okay, but let's use it on the ones we think really need it, not just the ones we think it would be amusing on." Ann suggested.

    "Okay Angel." Jackie said as she and Ann glanced around at the people in the park.

    There was a 9 year-old boy being babysat by an 18 year-old girl and the boy was obviously crushing on her. There was a 70 year-old Asian woman who couldn't fathom the way her two granddaughter's dressed. A 50 year-old African American woman observing her son and his geeky friends cosplaying as fantasy characters. Finally there was a classmate of Jackie's she recognized as a slightly overweight 14 year-old girl who was being tutored by a teacher she had a major crush on. Ann and Jackie now considered who they could use the ring on.
