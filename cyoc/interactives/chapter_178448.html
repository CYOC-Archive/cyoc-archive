    Honestly, the answer was simple when he really thought about it. Why choose only one beneficiary when he could have everyone benefit from Duncan's workouts. Yeah, he could see it now, Duncan helplessly getting weaker while everyone he used to torment got bigger. Yes, now that was vindicating. Jeff typed up the changes that he prepared for everyone. He adjusted the numbers so that everyone other than himself would get 150% of Duncan's workout benefits for the first two weeks just to give them some time to catch up to him. Jeff was already quite fit and muscular after all.

    Then with one last button press, the transformation was locked in. Say goodbye to your body and social standing, Duncan, since it's going to go away soon. Oh, Jeff couldn't wait to see the changes when he went back to sch- Oh wait, he was thirty now, he didn't go to school anymore. Hmm, maybe he could use the Chronovic to get him a job at the school then. School Librarian, maybe. Yeah, that sounds like an easy job and something to allow him easy observations.

    Once that was done, Jeff suddenly felt a weird sensation come over him. He was suddenly feeling healthy and energized. The man quickly theorized what was going and went back to observing Duncan. There he saw that his hypothesis was right; Duncan was working out. Heh, looks like the siphoning was going according to plan.

    Meanwhile, in various parts of the city, the men that Jeff had chosen to siphon Duncan's gains were feeling the effects of Duncan's workout, as well. All of them were confused at first but decided to use that energy productively. Not knowing that all their lives were about to take quite the turn.

    -----------

    The next day, Jeff arrived at his old school. It's going to be strange to be working here instead of being a student, but he'll get used to it. Near the entrance, Jeff spotted Jacques getting some practice in before class. The student was tall and lean, he didn't work out much, but his body was still better than Marshall's.

    "Wow, what elegant music you're playing. You seem very talented for someone your age," Jeff said to Jacques to strike up a conversation.

    "Ah, Merci Monsieur. Thank you so much for the kind words," Jacques replied. Jeff chuckled as he went on his way. Jacq could speak perfect English, but he chooses to sprinkle in some french words to seem more charming.

    As he entered school, it seemed like he came across the aftermath of one of Duncan's bulling routine on poor Marshall. The skinny nerd that was Marshall was on the floor, papers were flying everywhere, and Duncan was walking away. Jeff helped Marshall up and pick up some of his things before going on his way. Hopefully, this will be the last bullying attempt from Duncan once the transformation really takes off.

    Finally, he got to his destination, the teacher's lounge. There he ran into his last two targets, Mr. Resendez and Coach Davis. Both of them had bodies that could simply be described as dad bods, but Coach had remnants of a once fit body. Speaking of the Coach, he was the first to notice Jeff enter and went to greet him.

    "Ah, you must be the new librarian. I'm Coach Davis; I coach the football team, but please call me Charles," the coach greeted him with a firm handshake.

    "It's nice to meet you, Charles. You can call me Jeffrey. You're the coach football team, you say? Make sure you whip those students into shape." Coach Davis chuckled at the response. Jeff was smiling too, but for different reasons. The coach will whip his own body into shape if he trains Duncan hard, after all. After that Coach, Davis had to leave, and that's when Mr. Resendez came to greet him.

    "It's nice to meet you too, Jeffery. I'm Greg Resendez. Uh, are you sure you're a librarian? Most of the ones I know aren't as... buff like you." Jeff couldn't help but chuckle at that statement.

    "Well, what can I say; I like working out. You want to join me sometime? I'm sure you'll look great with a little bit of work." Saying this had caused Greg to blush. It might be weird flirting with his former teacher, but they were around the same age now. Besides, Jeff was sure that Greg will look great after a couple of weeks of siphoning from Duncan.

    "U-Uh, yeah. I should get to class now, but I'll get back to you on the working out part." Mr. Resendez then left, still a bit flustered. The next couple of weeks were going to be interesting, to say the least.
