    Across town, Mitch had just settled down at his desk when an uneasy
    feeling swept through him. He felt completely out of place…like he
    needed to be out running in a field. His body was pulsing with energy,
    and he couldn’t keep his mind focused on his work. Instead of his
    presentation, he thought about how stuffy it felt in the office, and
    how he longed to be outside making a few goals for his team. What?
    Mitch thought, a bit uncertain. I don’t have a team…But he couldn’t
    shake the uneasy feeling that he was out of place in the office…That
    this wasn’t where he belonged. He got up to head toward the
    conference room, but found his body moving in a different direction,
    toward the elevator that led to the main lobby and outside. He was
    able to get control of himself before he reached the elevator, and ran
    into the bathroom nearby.

    He looked at himself in the mirror. He was young! Not ridiculously
    young, but definitely not his face. He looked like a teenager!
    What’s going on? He thought. He splashed some water in his face,
    thinking that he must be in some sort of dream, but looking up in the
    mirror again, the young teenage face stared back at him. His hair
    grew longer and darker, turning from a light brown to a dark black.
    His skin began to tan, from the hours that he had spent playing in the
    hot sun. He looked strange: a young teenage guy in a business suit.
    His hair stopped growing in the front right above his eyebrows, and
    grew shaggy in the back covering the top of his collar. His formerly
    straight hair was now wavy and a bit messy, as if he had purposely not
    combed it when he had awakened that morning.

    He started to back up, trying to think of some excuse that he could
    pass off for his total change of body. Before he could continue out
    the door, however, he was shocked to see his suit jacket and tie
    completely disappear. His dress shirt was changing…the collar
    shrinking into the neck of the shirt while the sleeves pulled
    themselves up his arms, stopping about halfway up his upper arm. He
    could see that his arms matched his face in the dark tan, but they had
    lost quite a bit of their hairiness. The shirt started to change
    color from white to a lime green color, except for a brilliant blue
    streak that started at the bottom of the sleeves and worked its way
    down on both sides of his stomach. The fabric changed to a polyester
    blend, perfect for wicking the sweat away during an intense game. The
    buttons on the shirt merged as the lime green color finished in the
    center of his chest, with the Adidas name and logo appearing where his
    top button had been. The word “Knights” appeared on the fabric across
    his chest, and the number “6” appeared underneath it. On the back, a
    larger number “6” had appeared, with “Zanden” written across the back
    between his shoulders. His nice white button down shirt was now a
    green and blue soccer jersey.

    A soccer player?! Mitch though to himself. I have no idea how to play
    soccer! But the more he thought about it, the more it seemed
    perfectly natural. Of course he was a great soccer player. He had
    been playing for years, since he was a little kid. He was the star
    forward for his high school soccer team! Wait, that wasn’t right. He
    was a businessman, wasn’t he? He couldn’t get a hold of his thoughts…
    they kept running back and forth between his old and new
    personalities.

    While Mitch struggled to get a hold on himself, his pants started to
    change. The slacks worked their way up his legs, revealing tanned
    legs to match the rest of his body. His leg hair had lessened a bit,
    but there was still quite a bit, much darker than it had been before.
    The legs of his pants stopped about a quarter of the way up his
    calves, where the fabric loosened slightly, changing to the matching
    polyester blend as his shirt. The button and zipper disappeared
    completely, as the waist changed to an elastic drawstring. The color
    changed from black to the same lime green as his jersey, except for
    the blue stripe, which continued down the side of the shorts. The
    Adidas name logo appeared halfway down the stripe on his right calf.
    He felt a heat expanding from his boxers, and pulled back elastic
    waistband to see that his boxers where changing into black compression
    shorts. The fabric tightened around his groin, and continued down his
    legs stopping above his knees. The compression shorts showed off his
    fairly hung package pretty well, which hadn’t seemed to shrink at all
    in the change. The Adidas name and logo once again appeared in the
    compression shorts, halfway up his left thigh. Damn, he thought to
    himself. Our school must have gotten a pretty good deal from Adidas!
    But then, as he thought about it, he realized that he had always been
    a huge Adidas fan. After all, no one knew soccer better than Adidas.
    They were the ones who provided all the stuff for the major leagues…

    Mitch let go of the waistband of his shorts and stared at himself in
    the mirror. The old Mitch was almost completely gone, but he was
    fighting as hard as he could to stay in control. He was, however,
    fighting a losing battle. The last bit of the transformation began,
    as his socks started to change to cotton, turning a blue color and
    making their way up his legs, stopping just below the knee. As the
    socks changed fabric, they also changed color, much to the old Mitch’s
    dismay, into a green color similar to the same color as his jersey.
    Three white stripes appeared across the top, and of course, the Adidas
    name and logo appeared halfway up the leg of the sock, pushed outward
    by the newly formed shin guards underneath his sock. He could feel
    the strap around the back of his calf, keeping the shin guards in
    place. The final transformation began as his leather shoes began to
    change to microfiber. The color changed to match the same color as
    the blue on his jersey, except for a black flap which grew across the
    laces and had the Adidas name and logo on it. Pairs of white ovals
    began to appear on the front of the shoe, and made their way to the
    outer sides. One pair changed to a red color about halfway down the
    side of his shoe. The back of his shoe had three white lines across
    the top half, where the new sole appeared see-through on the bottom
    half. Four black studs grew out of the back bottom of each of his
    shoes, whereas six more studs pushed their way out of the front bottom
    of each shoe, three on each side. Three stripes appeared on the
    opposite side of the ovals, with the words “F50” appearing in black
    above them. The words “Sprint Skin” appeared in tiny letters in the
    front of the shoes, and the word “Tunit” appeared on the tongues of
    both shoes. Mitch’s expensive leather dress shoes were now a blue
    pair of Adidas F50i Tunit cleats.

    Mitch looked around the bathroom. Where was he? This definitely
    wasn’t East High…He had some vague memory of a presentation or
    something…Maybe it was for a big soccer match later? That seemed to
    be right. After all, Mitch Zanden was the star soccer player of East
    High School.
