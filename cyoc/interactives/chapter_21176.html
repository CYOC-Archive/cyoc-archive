    You become aware of a bright light.
    Sunlight has managed to find a way through the leaves and is dazing you. You open your eyes to see that it is late evening.
    Still a Centaur, you stand shakilly. The exhaustion has diminished and you feel fine. You feel as if youve had this body all your life.
    You look around you and begin to panic, reallising that you have no idea which way you came from.
    You begin to gallop through the forrest, looking for familiar sights. But there are nothing but trees.
    You stumble upon a clearing. There, you find the dark figure who you beleive cast the spell on you. He, or she, is plucking flowers.
    "Hey," you stutter.
    The figure stands and turns. You become aware that it is in fact wearing a long, black cloak. It drops it's hood.
    It is a strange creature. You could describe it as a unicorn, with a horses head, pure white fur and a long spiral patterned horn protruding from it's forehead. Yet it stands on it's hand legs like a human.
    "Hello," he smiles.
    "What did you do to me?" you ask, although you aren't entirely uncomfortable about the change. You rephrase the question: "Why?"
    "I haven't done anything to you. I don't know how it happened. I can guess why"
    "Why?"
    "This forrest is full of magical plants and herbs that nobody in the scientific world understand or even know about. I can guess it's something you ate, touched or even inhaled"
    In a swift action, he drops the cloak to the ground, leaving him naked. He is stunningly beautiful. His muscles are adept and boyish. His tail swishes from side to side in an alluring manner.
    "I could cure it if you'd like"
    He smiles. Evidently he's noticed the throbbing organ between your hind legs.
    "My home is not far from here. I could teach you plenty about magic"

    What do you do now?
