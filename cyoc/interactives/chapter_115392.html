    Melissa Jenkins pulled Trevor towards their first class together, unaware of the phone call that Megumi made to her grandmother. A smile was plastered on her face, revealing her excitement for her first date with Trevor.

    "So, where do you think we should go?" Melissa asked Trevor.

    "Um," Trevor said as he thought for a moment. "Well, what do you want to see? I mean, I can think of a few things, but your ideas are as good as mine."

    "Well, I really want to see this town a bit. I mean, we just got here a few days ago, but I want to see more of this place. I don't think we had much like it where we lived in Michigan."

    "I see," he said slowly as he continued to think. "I do know of this one place. It's sorta secluded, but it's on top of a hill where we can see the lay of the town. We can see the lake and the leaves look really nice this time of year."

    "Oooh, that does sound nice," Melissa said as she leaned towards him. "It'll be like a picnic, with just the two of us. Can we go tonight?"

    Trevor laughed as he said, "You're an eager one. How about tomorrow? After school? That way, we don't have things like homework or tests to scare us." The two continued to chat as they went in the classroom. Alice Weiss was close behind them, smiling as she heard the two of them talk.

And besides, women tend to not make her pregnant, so they weren't worth it to her. And with the extra police scrutiny, the accident method probably wouldn't work. She couldn't get close enough to Melissa for that to work.

    Alice made one last check of her appearance before heading out to her shop. Turning Melissa would be a challenge, but it'd be worth it. No one rejects her and gets in her way. No one.
