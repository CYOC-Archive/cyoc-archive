    The Ointment Store

    Reliving your childhood in the cinema

    Allison was a 28-year-old girl who had recently bought baby cream from the ointment store. And the reason she had bought it was for a special occasion.

    Recently, a kid’s movie had been released at the cinema, which is based on a series that Allison used to watch when she was a baby. But while she wanted to see this movie, she didn't like the idea of being an adult going to see a kid’s movie since it could be a bit embarrassing.

    But luckily, Allison had come up with an idea that would allow her to go to the theater to see that movie without having to feel ashamed. And that idea was: go see that movie but as a toddler.

    While that seemed somewhat impossible, with the baby cream she had in her possession that could come true. Although she still had a small question to solve: who could be the person to take her to the movies?

    The movie theater employees wouldn't allow a toddler to go see a movie on her own, so she had to think of someone who could take her.

    The first person she resorted to calling was her friend, Abigail. After Allison finished explaining the whole thing to her friend, she asked Abigail if she could take her to the movies.
