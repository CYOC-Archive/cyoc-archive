    Brian raised an eyebrow and said, "Well, that sounds pretty interesting. What does it do?"

    Feeling threatened by Brian's curiosity, Jeff decided to play along. He explained, "It's a program that allows me to become... well, not a girl exactly, but you know... change my genitals."

    The smile on Brian's face widened as he replied, "Oh wow! That sounds kinky. And how did it feel, becoming a chick?"

    "Well, it was different," Jeff said hesitantly. "But I must say that I really like my new vagina."

    Brian's eyes lit up with excitement as he pressed further, "Really? So you're into guys then?"

    Jeff rolled his eyes at the naive statement and clarified, "No! Not at all. It's just an entirely different sensation that feels amazing. And it's a secret, so please don't tell anyone."

    Brian chuckled slyly and said, "Hey, I'm cool. I won't say anything... for now." He then walked away leaving Jeff to contemplate the consequences of his actions.
