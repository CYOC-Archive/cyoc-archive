    Animal Costumes Mark 2 - Feral velociraptors

    added by Raven Cross 14 days ago A BM S Reptilian

    Dan's parents have never been pleased about their son having a son that is a velociraptor dinosaur. A straight up velociraptor dinosaur; not a person in an Animal Costume. Natural born feral velociraptor dinosaur. Dan was very surprised himself when the egg was mailed to him by Sarah. You see Sarah, Dan & two other people had been getting together each year for the 14 years as feral velociraptors; to hunt & just be animals in a forest. And about 11 years ago the egg arrived in the mail. It was in a package & had a letter with it explaining it that it was Dan who had knocked her up & this was the result. This was his. Dan was of course surprised. He did fuck her while they were dinosaurs but he didn't know anything had come from it. But he wasn't the only one that had. Dan wasn't a 100% sure this was his; more than likely it was though. But regardless Dan took full responsibility for the egg & the velociraptor that hatched from it. Of course Dan's parents weren't pleased by this & Dan telling them he was keeping the dino. He told them the dino is his son & he won't get rid of him. Needless to say Sarah did not return to the group after this. The other 2 in the group were surprised to learn of Dan's dino son; but accepted him into the pack regardless.
    Dan arrived at the forest in his van & saw Adam & Max's vehicles there & a new velociraptor there. The new velociraptor seemed a bit young by its size. Dan changed into his feral velociraptor dinosaur Animal Costume; not flipping the head on yet. He got & then let his son Fangs out. Max & Adam soon got out of their cars also in their feral velociraptor dinosaur Animal Costumes; all but the heads. Max informed Dan & Adam "This is my daughter Claw. She's a natural born velociraptor dinosaur like Fangs. She is joining our pack this year." Dan said with a smile "Ok. You are our alpha so who am I to disagree." "Natural born or not. Soon we will just be dinosaurs. No different than each other." Adam stated. He then flipped his costume head over his own & almost immediately became a velociraptor dinosaur. Max followed suit. Dan turned to Fangs & reminded Fangs "I won't be the alpha. Max is. We will be betas together. We will all be velociraptors period." Fangs nodded understanding; this had been explained the things multiple times over the years. Frankly Fangs was kind of getting sick of Dan his dad explaining this to him. Fangs knew the deal by now; his dad didn't need to keep explaining it to him every year. Dan flipped his costume head over his own. Dan became a feral velociraptor dinosaur too. The alpha (Max) let out a kind of chirp. The other 3 feral velociraptor dinosaurs let out a confirming chirp that he was there alpha; leader. The alpha darted into the forest followed by the 3 others.
    So this started their annual 1 month of being a pack of velociraptor dinosaurs hunting and living as such. They would teach their new packmate Claw obviously.
