    "Hello, is your mom home?" she asked.
    "No" Lilly answered

    "Oh, I was hoping to talk to her about an object I let her borrow, as well as some good news I have"

    "The Medallion?" Lilly said
    "Yes, so she told you two about it, may I come in"

    "Sure, Mrs, um...." Lilly said as she let her in and closed the door

    "Call me Patty" Patty said

    The group moved from the front door to the family room

    "So, what did you want to tell my mom?" Robby asked

    "I convinced my boss to give me time off, so I can join you in your vacation as well as how to use the different settings on the medallion" Patty responded

    "How did you get time off?" Lilly asked
    "My family is very wealthy, I just have that job to keep me busy" said Patty, "Why did Michelle go to the mall?"

    "She used the medallion to turn herself into a 25 year old and-" Lilly said

    "She left to have fun shopping" Patty interrupted, "I sometimes do that myself"

    "Really, what else do-"

    "What are the different settings on the medallion?" Robby interrupted

    "I rather tell when everybody's here, so I don't have to repeat myself" said Patty

    "What do we do now?" Lilly asked

    "Well how about we go to my vacation home" said Patty

    "What about our Mom" asked Lilly

    "Just call her cell phone, and ask her to come home" Patty responded

    Lilly left to the kitchen to call mom on the house phone

    "Good point" Robby answered

    "She said that she's on her way home" said Lilly, "How far away is it and what is it?"

    Patty replied, "I'ts a large ranch house with a farm, 4 hours away!"

    Lilly and Robby smiled, "Wow! we could really have some fun on a ranch with the medallion!"

    Patty nodded, "There are clothes for all shapes and sizes in the bedroooms....not to mention the animals if you want to really see life from a different point of view!"
