    Mr. Mendes and the Book of Transformation

    Francisco Mendes was the coolest counselor in the Jr. High. Plus he was a very good looking Latin man. At age 32 he had a slim body...not skinny, but thin with
    just enough definition to have a nice shape. No body builder but toned.
    His facial features were sharp and angular and very handsome...Aztec in his look. He always dressed cool like the kids in school and had thick beautiful black
    hair...with the sides buzzed and top hanging thick and long, parted down the middle.

    Ric and Josh were two of his students at the school. They had no father and Mr. Mendes was a mentor to them as well as a good friend. Ric was thirteen and just
    in the early stages of puberty while younger brother josh was a 12 year old late bloomer, showing no signs of change from boy to man. Their mother was relieved
    to have Mr. Mendes around to help explain the changes they were just finding out about.

    The boy's mother had to go out of town for the summer with her job and Francisco had agreed to keep the boys at his home. That morning the boys had walked
    up the street to "ritzy" neighborhood to check out the estate sale of an old man. It was always reported that the man who owned the house was much older than he
    looked (though he looked old to the boys when they saw him on the porch) and that he had used his fortune to travel the world looking for oddities. The boys
    decided that it might be fun to check out the neat stuff in the sale.

    By the time they got there, an antique dealer had bought just about everything. They went though a box of books, nothing much there but Reader's Digest
    Condensed...except for a very old and tattered book with a leather cover titled "Body Transformations...A Complete Guide". Ric thought this was great. He might
    learn something about his recent transformations.

    When the two Latin boys got home, Francisco had just come in from his morning jog. "Hey guys, I'm going to shower and dress, then we'll plan the day."

    Ric opened the book, he suddenly realized that it wasn't a scientific book, but a book of transformation spells. "Look at this Josh!", he said. Josh took a look at the
    book, read the table of contents and said, "Cool, are you gonna try it?" "Sure", said Ric, "How would you like to be turned into something?" "Hey not me!", Josh
    protested, "I think it should be you." Ric looked at Josh and rolled his eyes, "Like it would really work." At that moment Francisco came out of his room dressed in
    his shorts and tee shirt ready to go out for the day. Josh leaned over to Ric and whispered, "Okay, then try it on Mr. Mendes if it won't work." Ric uttered the
    words of the first spell he turned to concentrating on Mr. Mendes.

    "So what's on the agenda for today guys?" Francisco asked, just as he felt a wave of energy...actually very pleasurable, hit his body. "What the hell was that?" their
    friend and counselor said aloud. Then the boys noticed a change starting to take place to Mr. Mendes...Francisco was lost in the pleasure of feeling he was having.
