    “Ah…ah…I am Lisa.” Introduced Lisa “Well…welcome.”

    “Yes.” Answered the RA “So is the second desk, closet and the mini fridge by the desk. Lisa you will have to clean off your stuff, you can’t have them both.”

    “But…but.” started Lisa not wanting to give up anything. She was a spoiled roaten kid used to getting everything she wanted and she used to have a push over for a roommate. She didn’t have a push over for a roommate now.
    “You have to make room for Grace, here she has just as much right to this room as you do.” Explained the RA “Now do this or I have no choose but to write you up.”
