    David Remembers

    As David Woods left the auditorium, he looked around at all the other boys with worry. He knew what was happening. Just a few days ago, he had lost track of where his Chronivac device was, and now the principal had the power to change the entire student body? Yeah, obviously Principal James (who David also noticed looked a lot younger, no doubt because of the Chronivac) had used the device to make that happen. The only reason David even noticed that anything was different was because of the fail-safe he inputted into the device, to keep him from forgetting who he was, if he accidentally changed himself too much.

    He looked down at his strange attire - a white short-sleeved button-down shirt with a silver necktie, a pair of tight shiny silver short-shorts, silver knee-high socks, and penny-loafers - now the official school uniform. He couldn't help but think of the outfit as being kind of "gay". Was the principal gay? Was that why he turned every girl into a boy? David didn't know, but one thing was clear - he was going to have to get the Chronivac back, somehow. He couldn't let Principal James change even more stuff with it.

    Walking back to his locker, David looked around at all the grinning content boys, a lot of who were girls just minutes ago, but they didn't seem to remember that. It was a really disturbing sight. "I love to learn so much!" said a boy, opening his locker. David remembered that specific locker belonging to a girl named Ashley. This boy did kind of look like her, like he could have been her brother. But no, David knew the truth. This was what Ashley would look like if she had been born male. On the inside of Ashley's locker door was a photo of two boys kissing, with the words Ashton (Heart) Michael written in marker in the corner. Michael Jones, who went to a different school, was Ashley's boyfriend. But now Michael was gay? And dating Ashton, who was apparently Ashley's new boy name?

    David really needed to get that Chronivac back and get things back to normal!
