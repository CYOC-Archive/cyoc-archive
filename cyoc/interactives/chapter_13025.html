    I shouted back, "I'll explain after you come out. Don't wear the bikini?"

    Amanda nodded. "That's what was supposed to do, right? All I remember was trying to breath underwater. What happened after that?"

    "Yes, the bikini did what was supposed to do. You became a mermaid, a driven one, if you catch my drift," I hinted to her.

    Amanda gave me a thoughtful, one-squinted eye look. "You mean, it made me," she paused as her face reddened. I nodded in agreement. Her face became bright red. "Did I, ahhh, try to..." she stumbled for the right words.

    "You tried to kiss me and turn me into a merman. The ring saved me. I had to get you into the changing room on the promise I'd let you kiss me. Once you took the bikini off, you were no longer a mermaid. You never did anything wrong, I promise you," I told her.

    I turned around in surprise to find a no longer 16 year old Amanda standing there, the one-piece at her feet. But instead of naked, she was wearing the blue bikini! I had a moment to realize that Amanda had never taken off the bikini and had simply put on the one-piece on top of it. And that meant she was still a mermaid. And that meant we had kissed while she as a mermaid. And then, before I could do anything about it, she pushed me into the pool.
