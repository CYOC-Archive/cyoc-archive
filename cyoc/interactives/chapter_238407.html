The virus would later be known as the furry virus and it would in time ravage the entire world but it started in this simple dorm room.

He was thin, lanky and had the type of face which would disappear into the background of any movie scene. He wasn’t a party goer and outside of maybe a half dozen parties his whole college social life was his D&D group and some video game parties he went to.

    Right now partying was the last thing on his mind as he tossed and turned in his sleep.

    His breathing became rapid and shallow as his body began to grow and change. His back arched off the bed as his hands gripped into the mattress. When that happened his hands started to bleed as his finger nails where forced out as new razor sharp claws came bursting out of each of his fingers cutting deep into the foam of the bed. His whole body began to rapidly grow, not just in height but in muscles. He had never had visible muscles but now he had more strength than the strongest human who ever lived.

    Even with his new muscles he didn’t look like a body builder, he was still thin but now seamed to have the body of a swimmer but his muscles where now stronger and dancer then any other creature on earth.

    His eyes shot open as he rolled out of bed and began to cough, as he cough blood flowed from his mouth as his human teeth fell out as new fangs came in. He arched his back again as his pants ripped apart as a long thin tail formed. The tail was long enough to touch the flood even when he was standing as orange, tan fur with black spots grew in.     Orange/tan fur along with black spots began to spread over most of his body with lighter white fur on his chest. His short brown hair remained his face began to take on a more feline look, a short muzzle with whiskers growing out. His eyes became more focused as rounded ears came out the top of his head.
