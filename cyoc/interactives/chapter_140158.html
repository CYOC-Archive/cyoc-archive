    You wake up on your boyfriend’s chest. You felt so safe and warm, being near him. You almost didn’t want to wake up. Almost mind you, the stomach, on the other hand, wanted something else. You carefully get up, while not trying to disturb Richard while making your way to the walk-in closet.

    Since you came home last night, a few things had been moving around the apartment. While your oldself had little use of a walk-in closet before. Now that you are a girl, the entire closet was transformed to house all of your clothes and even had a door mirror hanging off it now.

    You still can’t get over how much your body has changed. Your oldself, before Richard made you his female clone. You used to be a tall man, albeit slightly overweight man with short brown hair. And now here you are, you were a short petite girl at 4’11” weighing 95 pounds, and with long flowing blonde hair so long that it reaches your waist. And to top it off, you were currently wearing a rather fetching pink Victorian style nightgown.

    You start checking out the clothes you bought yesterday and trying to decide which one to wear. You eventually see the Re:Zero cosplay maid outfit, he bought. “Well, he did say he wanted to see me in that.” You then proceed to change into the maid outfit. After changing, you decided to make two alterations that you knew he like, first you have your cat ears positioned to poke out of the headpiece, and you tied your long blonde hair into pigtails.

    You giggle at the sight of how adorable and cute you look like a catgirl maid. “Oh, he is going to love this.” You then proceed to put on an apron, and start cooking some grub.

    You made some French Toast with blueberry, your guys’ favorite. Once done, you sneak back into the room and start to cheerfully say in your best maid voice, “You breakfast is ready, Master.”

    Richard darts of, and takes a few seconds to get his bearings. It takes a few seconds for him to remember everything from yesterday. “Oh gosh, Kitty. I haven’t had Blueberry French Toast in a long long time.” He starts heading off to the table and joins you in having breakfast.

    “You know, I like the outfit you have on. It looks really good on you,” he said.

    “Oh stop, you say that to every girl in cute clothes.” You respond, smiling back at that compliment.

    “Maybe, though sometimes I do wish girls these days wore more cute clothes. You just don’t see enough women in dresses these days,“ He said and proceeds to finish the rest of his food. “Hey Kitty, I like to do a little experiment if you will. I want to see how far, this whole ordering you could possibly go.”

    “Alright, but don’t do anything permanent that I wouldn’t like.”

    He stops for a minute, “Ok, Kitty. Every weekend while you are wearing Cat Ears, where it is just you and I in the house. You must end all sentience, with a nya.”

    With those words, you felt a sensation from every word he said as the command with buried into your head. “Phew. “ You take a second to recollect yourself. “Did it work, nya?” You said. “Oh, wait I didn’t mean to say nya. It just comes out naturally, nya. “ You keep saying, looking slightly embarrassed.

    He laughed. “Ok, now that was for a bit of fun, but I will get back to that command in a second. Now for a more interesting one. Katherine, I command that your hair is to start growing so that it reaches the top of your butt, and that it must grow within a matter of 15 minutes.”

    I don’t think you can command things that my body is not capable of doing, nya. But it was worth a – a – nya“ You try to keep talking, but you feel a sharp pain coming from your head that it feels like a migraine. You fall to the floor while holding your head. You can feel it, the hair from your head shifting and lengthening.

    For the next 15 minutes, your head was in so much pain. You didn’t even notice Richard holding on to you after you fell. But after 15 minutes, it stopped. And what came out of your head was nearly another foot of hair, long enough for your hair to reach the bit of your butt. Once you had taken a breather, you dry off your new hair and readjust your pigtails to the new length. “Please, don’t do that again. Especially without telling me first, nya. I don’t want to go through with that, nya. Now what else did you want to try, nya”

    “Um, ya. I wanted to see if I could override my own orders. Katherine, you no longer need to keep saying nya.”
