    Punishment Game

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Tale Spinner 6 years ago AR S

    Veronica was in the isle which had all sorts of things then felt something tug at her as she saw an odd board game. Well not really as she would soon find out as she looked a box and the front box had a black smiling face behind a smile. It gave Veronica a little bit of a chill and on the back she saw the instructions and items. They were a bunch of matching one size fits all bracelets and an odd orb. The words on the box says that you come up with a punishment depending on a game and then if someone with a bracelet on deserves a punishment they'll get one. Then with it comes a few games prepackaged, but you can use it for any game.

    Suggested punishments: mild pain, youthening/aging, changing gender, or an animal change.

    games included: Uno, Monopoly, Twister, Life, and also includes a pack of playing cards.

    Veronica wasn't the most violent or twisted girl, but something about this thing spoke to her. A mage materialized behind her and said "Ah that ole thing. Do you want it?" Veronica nodded and the mage said "Go ahead and take it, it seems to see something in you... here let me put it in a bag." The mage then took out a small pouch that was just a little bigger than Veronica's fist and then stretched it and put the somewhat big Punishment game into it. the mage continued "Bag of holding, very useful and we generally give one or two out now and then." Veronica took the bag and it didn't weigh very much.

    A hand tapped Veronica on the shoulder and a teen girl's voice said "Got you're game kiddo? Time to go." Veronica looked and saw her 17 year old sister Kira who said "Yeah so I was asked to come when one of the younger mages used too much of the fountain of youth make up on mom... She's currently waiting in the car with a bunch of that product. Need to take a picture of the two of you together."

    Veronica said "Well I got my game and the mage let me have it."

    Kira looked at the mage and said "did he? Well I suppose we should go home." then she muttered to herself "keep an eye on that thing." Then the two of you went to Kira's car and got into the backseat where your mother was waiting... as a ten year old in a frilly black dress and had a rather red embarrassed face. Kira was grinning and brought out her camera and said "Come on you two, why not take a picture and show how much you look like each other."

    their mother tried saying something but Veronica nodded and said "Yeah let's take a picture!" and went to her mother. A quick photo latter and they were going on the road. Back at home...
