    Punished for love

    It was a completely horrible situation. Rachel and Hannah had kept their relationship a secret since they were fifteen - then, at the age of 19, they were discovered in bed together by Rachel's father home early from work. It was not a flattering position that could easily be explained away - both girls were nude and Hannah had her head in a place most platonic friends didn't care to see.

    Needless to say, Rachel's dad ratted her out, and both girls were quickly arrested and sent to the Court of Moral Contraventions. Both girls knew that the juries of these things absolutely hated homosexual behaviour, and they hated gay women even more. They would not be handed a light sentence - there was no hope or being made into cats or dogs today.

    What was even more certain was that they would be made into different species so they were no longer attracted to one another.

    Rachel and Hannah stood in court in their official overalls. Rachel had light skin, freckles, dirty blonde hair and green eyes. Hannah was blonde with clear skin, blue eyes and a charming smile.

    But by the end of this case, neither would be sporting any of those features. The two girls held hands, and they were booed, the jury shaking their heads and muttering to each other. They only hoped there would be a chance for one final kiss before the sentence was carried out.

    As the charges were laid out against them, the jurors shuffled out. They took several hours to concur on a sentence. The jury reemerged, and the head juror read the sentence.
