    Jeff looked at me, his eyes wide like silver dollars and grin forming on his face. He turned back to Dan.

    "Dan, the movie isn't going to start for another hour." He said. Just as he finished I noticed the times on the screen change accordingly. "So Sam and I are gonna go out for a second. You and Kent just wait in the theatre and make sure no takes our seats.

    "Will Do!" said Kent and Dan in unison as we turned and headed into the lobby.

    I looked at Jeff incredulously. "Dude, why did you set the movie for an hour later?"

    He had an 'are you serious?' plastered on his face. "C'mon, Sam. So we'd have time to practice our new powers."

    Frustration filled me as he said that. Despite both of us being 20, he acted like he was still in middle school. I placed my ace into my hands and screamed a little.

    "What's wrong?" Jeff asked. I honestly wasn't sure if was actually that oblivious or was just pretending. Neither one would surprise me.

    "Jeff." I said through near gritted teeth. "She did this to us to teach us a lesson, not so we could play around!"

    Jeff rolled his eyes. "Really, you're still on about it?"

    "YES!" I shouted. "We did an awful thing to the woman and she punished us! We shouldn't take advantage! Who knows what could happen!"

    As I said this, I failed to take notice of the mischievous look in Jeff's eye.

    "Okay, Sam" Jeff said firmly.. "You are my best friend. You were on my side. You wanted me to get the purse and you are just as miffed as I was. You WANT to test out our powers and see what we can do."

    There was a ringing in my ears. I shook my head. Why was I upset? Jeff was right, I thought it was cool and I did want to try out my powers. Why am I so worked up over that lady? She was mean!

    "Man, I don't even know what I'm on about." I said, chuckling. "You're right, Jeff. Let's test it out."

    Jeff nodded. "Now you're speaking my language. So what do you want to do first?"

    I looked around the lobby, scanning all the people. My eyes settled on two men at the entrance way. Both were very big and muscular.

    "What is it dude?" Jeff asked. I pointed towards the men. "What about them?"

    "Look at them. I say we make ourselves just like them." I said, still pointing.

    "Alright, okay." Jeff said. "You and I are just like those two men you are pointing at."

    Another ringing hit my ears as a tingling sensation hit my body. I closed my eyes. When I opened them a second later, I saw Jeff checking out his new muscular physique.

    "It worked, Dude." He said, showing off his now pearly white teeth as he flexed. His black hair was styled and he even had a bit of stubble.

    "Awesome." I said. Checking my reflection in the door near by, I saw I was just as muscular and my blonde hair was style just like Jeff's. I also noticed a huge bulge in both our groin areas.

    "We look pretty hot, babe." I said.

    "We do." said Jeff. He pulled me into a kiss.

    It was pure euphoric bliss until we opened our eyes and realized what just happened.

    Jeff broke the kiss first. "Were gay."

    "Yeah." I nodded.

    "Oh crud." was Jeff's reaction.
