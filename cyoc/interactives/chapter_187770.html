    Invoking an Elder God

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 2 years ago BM S O Mythological Mental

    Kennedy found herself in the middle of a room surrounded by a pentagram painted in red. A group of hooded figures were chanting in some creepy speech that she can't understand.

    With the use of old enchantments, found inside a book made of human skin and printed in blood, the fleet had found a new solution for the latex aliens... The Elder gods: A race of extra-dimensional beings from another dimension with powers that surpassed everything seen until now by the group of vagabonds.

    With their help, the Fleet would be able of defend itself against The Hive´s armada. They only needed a host for the creature that it was going to be invoked to this Universe.


    She could felt something inside her mind, myriads of unknown, alien voices that chanted in an ancient language that her primitive brain was incapable of understand. Her head was filled with Images of strange places: Undersea cities abandoned before man’s arrival, estrange worlds where lifeforms fed themselves from the void of Space ... Then she saw it: A black void in the middle of a starry sky. She knew that the voices were coming from there. Then, a giant eyeball came from inside and looked directly at her.

    "Nuz didan anuhn annah"

    A female scream and the smell of blood made her return to the real world.

    She returned to the room. But now, everything was in chaos. The people that were participating on the ritual were now fighting each other. The pentagram painted on the ground was shining with a bloody red light. Shali was nowhere to be seen.

    Her body had changed. Her human skin has been replaced by a translucent substance that let her look how her insides were. All of Her internal organs have been removed and substituted by tiny sacks that hanged on top of each other. She tried to move her legs, or at least she tried. What have been her old muscular legs were now half a dozen of tentacles that allowed the stunned human to move and grasp objects. Her head was bald and completely rounded. Her vision and sense of smell were drastically altered; not just because she was now capable of smelling new odors and see estrange colors that were invisible to the human eyes. Now she was capable of seeing from multiple directions thanks to the dozens of eyes that she now possessed.

    The ancient human tried to scream but only a pitiful, animal screech came out from a mouth full of sharp teeth.
