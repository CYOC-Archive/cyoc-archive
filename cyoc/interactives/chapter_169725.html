He says to Rachel, who has scooched herself to the far corner of the room. "Let's start over. I'm Alex. we've met before but you probably don't recognize me. I asked you to senior prom but you and your friends laughed at me. Said something about 'not being man enough for you.' Now I'm here to make up for lost time."

    "What am I?" Flash! Alex moved to the middle of the room, closing the distance and making a Captain Morgan pose, "why, I'm the Big Bad Wolf, the manliest of men around!" And as though on queue, Rachel finally notices the huge bulge between Alex's legs. That sack probably handles like a bag of potatoes, not to mention the log of meat. "And I am here to cash in on that date you own me!"

She flings the door open only to find Alex there waiting for her. "Where do you think you're going, beautiful?" She slams the door and runs the other way down the hall towards the front door. She opens it and again he had gotten there first. "Come here often?"

    Rachel slams the door again and goes to run in another direction, but she stays running in place instead. Looking around confused, she sees that she somehow has been surrounded by a human sized mouse wheel. Stunned, the door behind her creaks open and Alex saunters inside. "Are we having fun yet? That was a nice game of Wolf and mouse, but now it's my turn to pick the game." Alex puts a hand on the wheel and gives it a twirl. Rachel stumbles but catches herself, and is back to running in the exercise contraption. Strangely, the details in the wheel looked to be losing complexity, and the lighting is hitting it differently than it was a moment ago. Alex gives another whirl and Rachel is back to an all out sprint. The wheel was merely a blur now and Rachel struggled to keep up. With one last push, the wheel sped up one last time causing Rachel to lose her footing and tumble. She is instantly picked up and carried along with the blur of motion the contraption has become, losing all track of position as she merges with and is lost inside the twister.

    As the wheel and Rachel spin, the cartoonish blur begins to shrink. Down and down it goes until it's a size fit for, as you may have guessed, a mouse. Alex lifts his soft foot paw up and halts the wheel with his toe pads, and instantly a small brown blur is ejected out, tumbling across the floor. It crashes into a set of bowling pins that were inexplicably set up at the end of the hall. Sitting on the floor in a daze, eyes still spinning, is a tiny toon mouse girl. Big plate like ears, brown coat with white underbelly, spherical nose at the end of a thin snout, pink hands and feet at the ends of stubby limbs, and a long naked tail. In fact, she's entirely naked, but there does not appear to be any private parts to hide, only the outline of childbearing hips, a modest bust, and long eye lashes to suggest her female sex.

Well, Rachel is getting the opposite feeling; the doorknob is a mile high, the stair case may as well be Mount Everest, and the ceiling is just as in reach as the moon or stars.

    Busy marveling at her surrounding, she only just notices her pink hands, brown coloring and black inky outline separating herself from the 3-D world when she's suddenly lifted into the air, her tail pinched between two claws. She's brought to eye level with the big bad wolf, dangling just above his black nose. "Oh please Mr Big Bad! Don't eat me! I'll do anything you want, just let me go!" she pleads with her hands together. Her voice is much higher pitch and squeaky than before.

    The Wolf ponders for a moment. "Eat you? the thought hadn't crossed my mind. But now that you mention it, I could go for a second course. I'll have to think on it..." What happens next?
