    "Welcome to Game of Change!"

    added by The 1 5 years ago O Mythological

    Nat got a good look of the room they are currently in.

    They were in some kind of modern looking game show like area. In the center of the room was a stage with spot lights shining on it. On the stage was a incubus dressed in a formal business suit holding a microphone. It's obvious to anyone who knows modern culture that he is the show's spokesman/host.

    Lining around the stage were seats filled with a variety of demons and creatures of all kind of shapes and sizes. All of them seems to be wearing modern cloths for humans. Some cloths were modified to match their body types.

    Obviously, since Isaac and Nat never encountered modern society before, they didn't know what to think of this bizarre and alien room.

    Before they can do anything, a spot light from the main stage shone on them.

    "Aaaand, coming to you live to all the people of basementtopia... is our next contestants! Give it up to... Armored monster guy and Human looking cloud!!!"

    The crowd was cheering for Isaac for looking like a corrupt creature and booing at Nat for looking like a human.

    "Come on up on the stage! Don't worry. We won't hurt you. Are you new to this show?" The incubus host encourages.

    Nat and Isaac reluctantly walked up the stage. They weren't used to having a spot light shone on them.

    "Um, yes." Isaac answered. "Where's the exit to this room?' Isaac asked.

    The Host looked confused for a moment. "Didn't you see the sign on the contestant door? It says any customers who walked through that door can't leave the room unless they are transformed at least once. Sorry, but those are the rules. Try to leave if you want, but the magic binding this floor prevents the contestant from leaving. Our sponsors told us to leave seals that prevents contestants from second guessing themselves. Why go through the trouble of entering the show if you're not going to play?" The Game Show Host explained.

    This is outrageous! "That's unfair! It was pitch black back there! How do you expect us to see the sign on the door!" Isaac exclaimed.

    The Host looked genuinely surprised and then understanding. "You came from the back room? No one used that floor in years! My apologizes but that door still counts as a constant door. No mater how old the door is. I'm sorry that you two didn't willingly chosen to be contestants but rules are rules. The seal will prevent you from leaving this floor unless you play the game."

    Isaac and Nat looked at each other. The game show host could see the hesitance on their faces.

    "Sorry, but you both can't avoid a transformation. You're already entered into the show." The incubus showed compassion. "Hey, if it makes you feel better, some people from the audience are former contestants. They can tell you how fun this game is!" He waved his clawed hand to the large group of miss-configured creatures in the audience. My name is Terry Thue, and this is... GAME OF CHANGE!!!" He introduced.

    The crowd cheered! A neon sign showing the game show's logo was lowered in front of the duo.

    "Don't worry! Depending on the game you pick, you will only have to go through one transformation." The Host reassured.

    'I'm already liking this game!' Dark Isaac commented.

    'Hmp! Back in my day, the games to play were games to the death!' Excalibur commented in disgust.

    Both Nat and Isaac didn't respond to their mental companions.

    A monitor was lowered from the ceiling. It stopped in front of Nat and Isaac's faces.

    The screen showed a number of different game logos. None of them they were familiar of.

    "Go on! Just name a game you wanna play!" Terry encouraged.

    Isaac looked at Nat in concern. He can deal with being transformed again but he doesn't want Nat to experience the same fate. Yet, it looks like that have no choice.

    The Duo examined the games on the screen.
