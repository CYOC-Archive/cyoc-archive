    "Well, I found this weird remote on my way here." She said holding up the device. "It has these weird buttons, and says it can do all sorts of crazy stuff."

    "You're such a nerd Linda. Why the hell would we care about a remote?" Sarah scolded the plump girl.

    Linda hung her head, but was thankful when Mark spoke up to defend her. "It's not like we have much better to do. We can at least check it out."

    "I've got first dibs!" Anthony yelled as he snatched the remote out of Linda's hands. He looked over it, and saw Linda was right. It had menus that said it was capable of all sorts of weird shit.

    He was trying to figure out what it was when the front door opened, and he heard an angry woman shout "What are you doing in my house?" He fumbled with the remote as he turned to see a seething Mrs. Taylor in the doorway. He also saw an odd light seem to hit Mrs. Taylor, and she began changing.

Anthony's body followed on auto-pilot as he took in what had happened. The remote said something about changing people, and it worked! He spent the night going through the menus and finding out everything the remote could do. He decided school would be a lot more fun tomorrow.
