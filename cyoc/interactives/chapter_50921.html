    Slowly consciousness swam back to you and blinking, you asked yourself, "What happened?" and then, standing, you suddenly remember the events that had transpired to bring you here.

    A couple hours ago, you had returned from the kitchen, slowly eating an apple. As you did so, you went to the closet, curious about something. Taking from the back a costume of a vixen, you set it down on the bed and then after a moment, after you have finished your apple, you go to work.

    Quickly you removed the clothes off the four year old girl costume and then taking the skin of the girl, you gently place it inside the fox costume, making sure that everything lined up in place.

    Afterward, you take the clothes...a pair of pink panties, a dress with a blue printed pattern and a short-sleeved shirt with pink along the edge of the sleeves and along the hem...and place them onto the fox costume. Afterward, you sit back and wonder if you really should try wearing another animal costume and if you do how it is exactly you are going to get it on.

    After a bit, you stand and figuring you might want to try it to see what would happen, you take off your clothes and carefully begin to pull on the two different costumes at once, being extra careful to keep the clothes you placed on the fox in place.

    The moment you have the two costumes on, a shot of pain ripples down your back and you cry out as your back immediately arches. As this occurs, you feel your shoulders, hips and waist shift to something more appropriate for a four legged animal. Hissing in pain, unaware that you are forcing your tail to twitch back in forth, you cry out and then comment aloud, "Not like the last time," and then black out from the amount of pain you are in.

    Now, you find yourself still in your room, but it’s different. Where your dresser would be are storage bins containing clothes. Shifting about, you realised that you are lying in Scot's dog bed, but it doesn't smell like it has ever been used by him...in fact, when you realised that your sense of smell is so much better, you also realised that you can't even smell your German Sheppard any more.

    Confused, you stand and taking a clumsy step…clumsy on account that you are wearing a dress and the fact that you are walking on all fours again…you proceeded to explore your surroundings.

    In the bathroom you find a litter box and suddenly you remember that this is for you. In the kitchen you find two bowls...one for food and the other for water. Taking this all in, you stroll into the living room, finding your sister sitting on the couch, watching some programme.

    Hopping up onto the couch, your lay down next to your sister, interested in what’s playing. As you do so, your sister asked, "Did you have a nice nap Eleanor?"

    Unsure, and without giving it a second thought, you automatically replied in a human voice, "Uh-huh...Is mom home?"

    Your sister shook her head and returned her attention to her programme, leaving you alone with your thoughts. Curious, you notice that for some reason your sister has fox ears and closer inspection you notice that she also has a fox tail. Bothered by this, you let your mind wander, trying to remember as much as possible...and within moments you have your answer.

    It had happened about a year and a half ago. You mother, who is the head of a coven of witches, had had some friends over...and one of them had left a book of spells lying about.

    Curious you...still human then and clearly a little girl about a year younger than your sister...had taken the book and when your sister asked about it, you gladly showed it to her. After a bit of trying to make sense of the book, your sister was ready to take it back, but you still wanted to look at it.

    In a desperate measure, you flipped the pages and then hoping it would keep your sister from taking away the book, you read aloud one of the spells.

    Immediately you felt yourself change. But what was even stranger was when you watched your sister first grow taller and then have her ears melt into the side of her head...and then to be replaced by a set of fox ears on top of her head.

    And it didn't take you long to figure out that it wasn't your sister growing, it was you shrinking. Crying out, you suddenly doubled over in pain as your body began to morph into something else. Within moments, you had changed into a fox that had the mind of a human and the ability to talk like a human, but was unable to communicate with other foxes.

    The end result of your experimenting with magick was that both you and your sister were in big trouble and you having to spend a year and half as a fox, with so far no hope of returning to human form on account that you misread the spell, causing you to be trapped somewhere between an animal and a human...and your sister to take on select bestial traits; a tail, ears and later on, teats. And what made this all the more unnerving was that you continued to age like a human, not like an animal.

    Startled by this and all that you are remembering, you briefly think about taking off the costumes, but decide against it, wondering where this new life would go. Twitching your ears, you suddenly realised that you need to use the litter box and jumping down off the couch, you automatically ask your sister, "Can you assist me? I need to use the litter box."

    Your sister nodded and sliding off the couch, the two of you walked toward the bathroom. As you do so, you realised that there is somebody else in the house with you...and the moment you became aware of this fact, your memories told you it was the nice woman from next door, who not only babysat for you and your sister, but was also a member of your mother's coven.

    What do you do now?
