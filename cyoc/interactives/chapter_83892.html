    "Look up something else on that book. Might have been a warm up spell or something." Jolted Dale.

    "Well, lets seeeeee........there's......Uhhhh!!!!" said Mitch, as he was suddenly cut off guard by the following spurred event. The tome had immediately upon the wolf paw contact shoot out an all consuming dark pitched flame all over Mitch's wolf form. Immersing rapidly inside his body like water to an empty sponge; just all within fraction of a second. Blink of an eye even.

    "WHOLLY SHIATTTHHH MITCH!!!!! WHOLY MOFO CRUD!!" yelped Alan as he fell over his back in fear.

"I dunno wuts going on......I think my body is tingling....Its burning up fast like a fever or something." he replied between breaths. Then immediately he stood still on all fours canine legs lowering slowing his wolf head till it drop still facing down. Tongue sticking out.

Mitch's body contorted in odd directions making his head swing a bit side to side in painful grunts. His Long canine neck grew somewhat thicker with more muscle. "Guys....." Mitch struggled to say between canine moans and whines. The body was disproportionate in growth. Just his back grew large in muscle mass rather oddly, same with his front legs....then in almost in half a trance, like a sleep walker, Mitch look over to Alan as if asking for help. Alan locked eyes with wolf form Mitch with empathy to a trusting friend. He walked over on all fours, quite slowly towards Mitch's odd shaped still transforming body. The hell was that spell?!
