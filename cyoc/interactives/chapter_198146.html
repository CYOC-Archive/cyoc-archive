Your girlfriend looks surprised for just a split second, before sporting a sly smirk and wagging her new tail saucily. You didn't like how how that tail was moving. When dogs' tails wagged they were happy, but when cats' tails wagged, they were either upset, or schemi-

"Penny, what did you do?" you nervously call out, only to realize no one outside could hear you. That was when the window went opaque as the lasers zapped off your clothes, including a limited print Justice-Man t-shirt. "Aw, I liked that shirt."

    "Brian." Penny answers.

    "Right, is Brian goin to mad we did this?"

    "Psssshhh, nahhhhh." Penny waves her hand jokingly, tail still wagging coyly. "Besides, it was his fault for not hitting 'Confirm' and forgetting theere's like a gazillion Arachnid-Girls out there, so I picked one for him."

"So, I'm still becoming Arachnid-Girl, just Arachnid-Girl of one of those alternate earth reboot series." You began to think which one you were becoming.

    Was it:

    - The Arachnid-Girl of Universe 8994, a girl named Mina Morales, a teenaged barricuo (half-black, half-Puerto Rican) girl bit by a mutant spider and became a drider with electrical powers.

    - The Arachnid-Girl of Universe 51178, "Webby-Chan," a regular spider from a universe that has toon physics that got attacked by a horde of radioactive magical girls, and is now a human going by the name of Madoka Hoshinori, who can turn into a spider-themed magical girl known as "Webby-Chan."

    - The Arachnid-Girl of Universe 98, a 30-year old mother of one named Parker Maeby that by chance took an offer to bond with a peculiar alien arachid symbiote when her and her child was put in danger by a street gang when her car broke down, giving her superhuman spider-based powers, the ability to grow and retract four extra arms, grow and fire hairy barbs much like a tarantula all over her body, inject venom from her teeth, and coat herself in a suit with her symbiote that also augments her defense, she became known as the superhero "Girl-Arachnid."

    - The Arachnid-Girl of Universe 69420, also known as Bambi Winters, the "Snowy Widow." This blonde, massively-mammaried, midriff-baring, miniskirt-clad iteration of Arachnid-Girl comes from a reality that was hit with the devastating "Ditz Virus," turning everyone on the planet, except 69 people, most of them women & children, into dull-witted blonde bombshell babes. The Snowy Widow not only has spider-based powers, but ice powers as well. Her super strength is also at least five times greater than that of Phoebe Parker, the OG Arachnid-Girl. Bambi however... is bad about forgetting what her power set is at times.

    - The Arachnid-Girl of Universe 4, a young girl no older than 8 but possessing a super-genius intellect from the 42nd century, Ami Yamamoto, who after being bit by a genetically modified cybernetic spider was rendered comatose and near-death; her father Kei decided to transfer her mind into an experimental bio-android body he made, using her genetic material as a base, not knowing the spider's bite had mutated her genes. Now she not only has spider powers, but can remotely hack and control various computers and machines, and has her own little mini-mech she calls "Strider."
