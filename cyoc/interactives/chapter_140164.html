    Matt saw the ticker and decided to read what was on it. It said some normal things like the airport Matt was at and where he would arrive. Matt saw that he was apparently going to Seoul in Korea which interested him. He always wanted to go so now he got his wish.

    Matt browsed the ticket further and he got really creeped out. The ticket had his name, a photo of him and his address. "How the fuck did this guy know my details. Was he trying to send me off to korea for some reason." Matt thought to himself.

    Matt got onto the flight with no issue and sat down in his designated seat. He looked out the window and watched as the plane passed by the fields in which the airport was located.

    Over the course of the flight, Matt felt very uneasy. It felt like he shouldn't be on this flight and he just can't dismiss the uneasiness. He went to the in-flight toilet and sat down in it.

    Matt felt slightly better in here away from the other passengers and decided to write in his stress relief journal. Matt would write mini stories to keep his mind at peace and did just that.

    After 30 minutes or so, Matt went to get out of the toilet. As he opened to door, he looked around the deck and saw things that just didn't add up. There were a lot more Korean people on the flight then he remembered. He chalked it down to altitude hallucination but if people did spontaneously turn into another ethnicity, it would explain his uneasiness about this flight.

    Matt sat down back in his chair and stayed still just watching the flight deck. Matt was about to take a nap but just before he did, he saw people changing into Korean people.

    "That's it, I'm hiding in the toilet." Was what he whispered to himself.

    Matt walked into the toilet and locked himself in it for the rest of the flight. He hid in their until the end of the flight and thought to himself on what to do next. Matt assumed that everyone besides him got turned into Korean people and questioned if things like that are real.
