    "My boyfriend is going to kick your boyfriends ASS," they replied in unison.

    "Nope," Caleb replied, the teens muscles straining under Dukes supernatural strength. "Adam dumped this on me at the last second just like you."

    Duke sighed, his muscles increasing in size slightly as Caleb tried to gain an advantage. "Sasha is going to be pissed."

    "Yeah Gloria is not gonna be happy either," Caleb said. "Remind me to get back at Adam for this later."

    "So what do we do," Duke asked. "I can't lose in front of Sasha!"

No one seemed to notice the two teens creeping closer and closer to Adam. With a nod the two teens now launched themselves at the burly teen, effectively tackling him to the ground with a sickening thud.

    "I think so," Duke answered, pretending to nurse an injured shoulder.

    "You three okay," the coach asked as he studied his three wrestlers. The teens nodded, wincing at their perceived injuries. "Well I guess we can call this a tie then," the coach answered. "Hit the showers everyone and you two, put some ice on those injuries when you get home. And Haskins, wear a cup from now on."
