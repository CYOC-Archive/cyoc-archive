    Nothing happened.

    "Huh." Pete said, staring at his reflection in the mirror. "I don't look any different."

    Feeling tired, Pete turned in, and went to sleep.

    The very next day, he was up and about, doing his job.
    all the while, he kept wondering if the app was just a fun photo editing site.

    it wasn't too long till the end of his shift.
    but on his drive back home, the changes began.
    Pete began to feel his stomach gurgle, and he felt a tingle in his toes.

    Pete didn't know that he'd set his tf to happen whenever he finished his shift at work.
    and now, he was about to find out what was happening to him.

    first, his hair changed colour, turning from blonde to light blue. Even his goatee.
    Gradually, his skin turned lime green, starting with splotches till it spread.
    His ears pointed, then became as big as those on the average vampire bat.
    then, he gained an extra eye, and his canine teeth became bigger, giving him a underbite.

    and when he got home, the rest of the changes went underway - His legs shortened, he grew a horn on his head, he had 3 fingers on his hands, and a short stumpy tail popped out from his pants.

    He got his phone out, and then, his clothes vanished, and then he saw he had claws on his toes.
    He was a monster.
    In fact, Pete had become the monster as seen on the app.

    and, he had lost his penis.
    he'd found out on the site that a person's private parts disappeared when they turned into monsters.

    what now?
