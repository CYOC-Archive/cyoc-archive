    “I told you not to do that, you guys,” Trisha scolded, “He was clearly super upset if he phoned our captain because of it!”

    “What was his name again?” Samantha asked, “Korey or something?”

    “Kyle I think,” Wendy clarified, “The weird, scrawny, hairless ape boy with a ginger afro that covers his eyes.”

    “Yeah that’s the guy!” Jennifer nodded, “Umm... except I think I used to be a scrawny hairless ape before now... probably all of us?”

    “You’re kidding,” Amanda balked, “You’re telling me that there’s a naked ape version of me who isn’t buff as fuck?”

    “Or a me who isn’t fat?” Trisha asked, “That would be horrible!”

    “So that weird freshman got you to believe in multiverse theory?” Susan inquired, “That’s the dumbest revenge plot I’ve ever heard of!”

    “Probably?” Jennifer cocked her head, “Get this though, he tried using the town curse on me... maybe I really was a naked skinny ape and he used it to alter my perception of reality?”

    “That’d probably make more sense,” Susan nodded, “Of course, I definitely don’t remember any of us being apes, let alone scrawny and hairless. As long as I’ve known you, you’ve always been a fat golden retriever, and I’ve always been your fat squirrelly bestie.”

    “That... I think I remember it a little like both?” Jennifer shrugged, “It’s probably nothing to worry about. Can’t believe I’m saying this, but I don’t think I want to skip too much of our classes today. What’s our schedules again?”
