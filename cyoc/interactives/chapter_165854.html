    Claudia grinned, an almost feral look crossed her face. Stepping out from behind her hiding spot, she now stared down Malak. "Alright let's see what we can do."

    Malak glared at the petite blond sorceress, a curious look on his demonic face. Claudia smirked before a raging inferno consumed her. Puzzled the demon now watched as the towering flames began to take shape.

    Slowly but surely the fire morphed, thickly muscled arms and digitgrade legs forming from the flames. The flames becoming almost solid, as a thickly muscled torso took shape, topped by an anthro tigress face. The flaming anthro tigress bared its teeth as two white hot eyes glared at the demon. "Let's see if you can deal with this," she growled.

    Malak was momentarily stunned as the flame tigress kept at him. Swiping with her claws, Claudia raked the demon across the chest, leaving jagged slash Mark's across his chest. From Mikas spot she could smell the stench of burned flesh as the demon howled in pain.

    Malak roared, clenching his fist as he looked to strike Claudia down. The enchanted demon now swung at Claudia, his fist looking to crush the flame vessel. Claudia now raised one of her claws, shocking herself as she caught the demons hand.

    For a moment Malak and Claudia simply stared at Claudia's flaming hand, neither believing that the sorceress possessed the strength to deflect Malaks blow. A small laugh escaped Claudia flaming mouth as she reared back with her other hand, punching Malak with enough force to knock him backwards.

    Mika had been watching her fiance with interest, shocked to see Claudia generate this much strength into her flaming avatar. "Wow I didnt think we would be THIS strong."

    "Would you like to see what you can do?"

    Mika jumped, startled at the voice that seemed to echo inside her head. "Mika is that you?"

    "Yes Mika," the kitsune Mika responded. "I exist inside you now. Living within your subconscious."

    "How is that even possible," Mika asked.

    "Your sister could probably explain it. It's kinda like when Piccolo absorbed Kami in Dragonball."

    Mika could only sigh in annoyance, Naomi's encyclopedic memory of anime had always caused her to roll her eyes. Now it appeared her doppleganger had access to everything her sister had ever told her.

    "We can talk about that later," she sighed. "Right now I need to know how to help Claudia."

    "Conjure your chains," the other Mika said. "And when you do, make sure they're wrapped around your forearms." Mika did as she was told, watching as the solid water chains wrapped around her forearms. "Now what?"

    "Make a fist I'll tale care of the rest."

    Mika complied, wondering what her doppleganger had in store. The question was answered quickly as she watched a two foot long blade begin to form. The blade itself looked familiar, having a single edge and attaching itself to a solid handle. As soon as the blade finished, Mika watched as the chains on her arms snaked their way towards the handle before atta hing themselves to it.

    "Whoa," Mika whispered. "What the hell are these?"

    "Blades of Chaos," kitsune Mika responded. "I got the idea from your sister. I remember her showing you the game God of War. Did you know shes a really good gamer?"

    "Alright stop poking around in my memories of my sister," Mika sighed. "Its kinda freaking me out."

    Kitsune Mika apologized as Mika now stepped forward, her grip tightening on the chairs blades. Glancing at Claudia and Malak she could see the two engaged in a vicious battle. The two seemed to be equals in strength. As Claudia now slammed him to the ground Mika hollered to her fiance.

    "Stand back!"

    Claudia looked up in surprise, stepping backwards as she curiously gazed at Mika. Mika now swung hands over her head, releasing the blades so as to gain momentum, when she had built enough speed she whipped her hands forward, watching as the blades slashed outwards, leaving a massive slash across Malaks chest.

    Malak howled, kneeling over as his black blood now stained the ground. The wound was serious but the demon was still in the fight. Grasping his wound he snarled at Mika as she now flipped her wrist, allowing the blades to return to her hand.

    "Hey watch it!"

    Mika looked towards Claudia, gasping in terror as she saw Claudia's arm fall from her body. The flaming appendage disappearing as it hit the ground, leaving a one armed and grumpy flaming tigress.

    "Oh my god Claudia are you okay?!"

    Claudia gazed at her severed arm, closing her eyes as she repaired the damage. Slowly a new flame emerged from the severed end growing into a new arm in seconds. As Claudia now flexed the repaired arm she narrowed her eyes at her fiance.

    "Yeah I'm fine," she grumbled. "But watch where you swing those things!"

    Mika offered a meek apology as she now stood next to her towering fiance. The two staring at the angry and injured Malak.

    "What sorcery is this," he growled as he got unsteadily to his feet. "No creature is stronger than I."

    "Now they are," Mika grinned.

    "And the two," Claudia added with a smile. "Are about to end you."
