It wasn't a sharp incline or a fast pace, but Katie soon started feeling a rush of energy. "This is like what Mommy does at the gym," she said as she kept walking. She felt proud to be doing something like a big girl. As she walked, she watched the virtual forest pass before her.

    Katie was so fascinated by the moving forest she didn't pay attention to the age reading. As such, she didn't notice her age rate was speeding up. The seconds, minutes, hours, and days quickly became an unreadable blur. Only the months and years were still readable as they moved faster in time. Quite soon, Katie reached her 8th birthday before passing it and continuing towards her 9th.
