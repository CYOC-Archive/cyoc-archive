They also had Erika, a level 3 Paladin devoted to Ellnesis, goddess of warriors, and Venrie, a level 4 Cleric from the Order of Arkenos, which was a holy order devoted to a nature god. In short, they were a pretty tanky party, perfect to protect her while she cranked out the DPS, which was something she could now do fairly well with her leveled up fireball and her power enhancing items.
