    "So how long have you to been together?"

    Felicia and Adam looked at each other, a small smile on their faces. They had decided to keep things a honest as they could with Jackie's parents without revealing their true ages.

    "Since high school," Felicia stated. "We actually started dating freshman year."

    "That's so cute," Hannah smiled. "High school sweethearts, you dont see that you often."

    "Wouldnt exactly call us sweethearts," Adam chuckled. "I want exactly the nicest guy in the world. Felicia actually punched me in the nose the first time we met."

    "And with good reason," Felicia said. "You were acting like a jerk." Adam nodded, agreeing with his girlfriend before smiling. "Shes right," he smiled. "But over time I've worked on that. Felicia here made me a much better person than I was when we first met."

    Hannah and Frank smiled warmly as the couple took each others hands, placing a tender kiss on each others lips. "Why aren't you that sweet Frank," Hannah smirked.

    Frank, shocked that Hannah would try to use the warm relationship between the couple as a tool to criticize him, stammered a response. Adam and Felicia chuckled softly as Frank now blushed.

    "Didnt mean to get you in trouble," Adam apologized.

    "Dont worry about," Frank said. "You know wives get sometimes."

    "Wish I could agree but Felicia is within elbowing distance of me."

    The couples now laughed softly as they continued to talk. Frank and Hannah seemed impressed by the warm and tender relationship Adam and Felica had for one another. Any concerns they had about a troubled home were quickly being dismissed. Still, Frank had a few more questions about their relationship with Ann.

    "So I do have a question," Frank said. "When did Ann come out to you and how did you feel about it?"

    Adam and Felicia looked at each other trying to figure out exactly when it occurred. It hadn't been that long ago in Felicias mind. Jackie and Ann had started hanging out just after Halloween. The relationship between them though only became common knowledge around Christmas. "You know I think it was just this winter," Felicia whispered.

    "How did you handle it," Hannah asked.

    "We were surprised," Adam stated. "But after talking with Ann we realized how happy she was. As long as they aren't doing anything dangerous I dont see the harm."

    Frank and Hannah nodded, smiling at the response. "Were still trying to wrap our minds around it," Hannah stated. "But Ann seems like such a sweet and responsible girl. You should be very proud."

    Adam and Felicia smiled, nodding as they gazed at one another. They appeared to have made a good first impression. Hopefully it would mean the Ann and Jackie could continue their relationship without any major concerns.
