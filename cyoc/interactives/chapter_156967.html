    Before any of the goddesses could decide to go reassure their master, Pam sent an e-mail to Skyla titled "about your mother". Curious, the Head Maid opened it. It said "I saw Tomoyo-kami on the security feed. She came in walking and, once in the dining room, found a toy-gun, seemed to observe it, and then teleported out with it. Since you asked me to, I quote, “let you know if she is sighted, so we can prevent her pranks from disturbing the mansion's peace” and do it in written form, here it is." This e-mail had a video attached, in which a 9-tailed vixen who would seem to be burning if it wasn't for the strange colors said fire had in some place, did just what Pam had described.

    — – - – —

    While all three goddesses were glad Skyla had learned what really happened, at least in the changed reality, Amaterasu and Issun felt the situation still needed to be defused, and manifested themselves in Skyla's office, in front of Evenson-dono and his current entourage.
    …Then realized they were thinking of him as ‘Evenson-dono’ rather than ‘master’. Sure, it mean the same thing, but apparently Tomoyo's ‘Japanese-kick’ in changing/creating herself and the 2 other goddesses had caused some of Japan's usages to become universal.

    "Hello Skyla-buchō! Tomoyo-kami is sorry to have scared you like that, but she saw that Evenson-dono's favorite toy seemed to be damaged and wanted to see if she would be able to repair it once she took it back to the divine realm." said Amaterasu.

    Then turning toward Curt, she said "Which make me remember we haven't introduced ourselves to you yet, at least not in your current incarnation. My name is Shiranui Amaterasu, goddess of the sun, life and friendship" she said, before indicating Issun "and this is my personal assistant, body guard, best friend and wife, Shiranui Issun, goddess of paperwork, darkness, protection and death. Now, if Everson-dono grant us leave, we would like to return to our godly duties."

    "O-of course!" Immediately answered Curt, embarrassed by how even goddesses seemed to worship him, instead of the other way around, in this new reality.

    As soon as the Shiranui couple disappeared, Skyla whispered to Anna, Ashley, Misty and Curt. "You realise those two were changed by ‘esteemed mother’, Tomoyo, right?" Curt nodded dumbly, the rest seemed more surprised by this declaration.

    — – - – —

    As soon as Amaterasu and Issun departed for the mortal plane, Tomoyo couldn't help but wonder about one of her master's memory, the one about how even after restoring Marceline's awareness of who she used to be she, mentally, had stayed the same Marceline the new world always had, how when Everson-dono took back said awareness (at Marceline's own demand) she forgot her old life again, thinking it was a dream.

    So in the gun's parameters she selected herself, Editing and replaced Change Awareness by Reality Distortion Field before validating. In an instant, her memories of her old life as Thomas became vague, dream like memory, while millennia of memories as Tomoyo bombarded her. But again, being a goddess, it wasn't what overwhelmed her. No, it was the emotional part that affected her, prompting her to reverse what she had just done to herself.

    Even then none of her new memories really disappeared, but it felt more like they were from someone else, from a fellow goddess. It also restored her old memories as they were once she had first zapped herself.

    What no one noticed, was that when Tomoyo ‘erased’ the memories she inherited from Thomas, she somehow also turned some of Japan's usages, like the use of honorifics after a name, or the fact a family name should be said before one's given name.

    That said, thanks to the memories from her ‘unaware’ self, she realized how sad it was she hadn't been there when Kasai started her new cycle. After all, what kind of child isn't there for their mother in her time of need? She would have to go see her as soon as she could. The only question was: Should she make more goddesses loyal to Curt-dono first? Or was it more important to bond with her reborn mother?
