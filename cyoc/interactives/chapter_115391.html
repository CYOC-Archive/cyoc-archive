    You began falling, and fast too due to your immense size. As you fell you wanted to scream, but instead roared along the way. You looked down to see large jagged rocks covering your landing zone, and you thought you were done for. However suddenly something grabbed you by the waist, and back and you noticed you were somehow gliding over the sharp rocks, and towards the ground.

    You thought you were doing something somehow, until you looked up to see a black dragon carrying you by his talons. The dragon seemed to loose his grip after about a minute, and ended up dropping you close to the ground, and you rolled a few times before stopping in place. {{You idiot! Why didn't you fly?!}} you heard the voice again, and tried asking,"Who are you?" But you only roared again, and followed it up with a groan from your brief fall.

    {{And can you please stop trying to pretend to be something that verbally talks. Use your damn thoughts dumbass, or else I'll just burn your tail!}} the voice said again, and you saw the black dragon flick it's tail at you annoyed. {{Wait thoughts, does that mean you can hear me right now?}} you thought, and the black dragon responded,{{Yes, and when I saved your tail from most-likely death I didn't know I would be saving a damn cave-head dragon.}}

    {{Cave-head dragon? What does that mean?}} you thought

    {{Are you seriously that stupid? What kind of dragon are you exactly? Did you fall off a small ledge unsupervised as a child? Or did you literally sleep for a millennia inside that cave for your entire existence? It's bad enough I had to instruct you on how to empty your fire bladder so you wouldn't get stuck in a collapsing cave, or burn up a forest to expose yourself.}} the black dragon said

    {{Technically no I never fell from a ledge, nor have I lived in a cave at all. Or is that exaggeration for you, because I haven't been a dragon for ten god damn minutes, and already I'm being criticized about being what I am even though I've been human my entire life, and this for only a few minutes.}} you said then roared

    {{Wait you weren't dragon before?}} the dragon asked

    {{Yes, and the last time I checked I had no damn wings, no damn tail, and certainly couldn't shoot fire from my mouth!}} you said and roared

    {{Don't FUCKING ROAR!!!}} the black dragon said and roared at the top of its lungs, and you felt your ears ringing.

    You covered your ears, or the places they were at before, and thought,{{Okay I see your point, but you have to believe me I wasn't a dragon until just minutes ago I don't know anything about being one.}}

    {{Considered everything that just happened I'm willing to believe you if...you answer my question. What the hell is a human?}} the dragon asked, almost growling

    {{Wait humans aren't around here, or anywhere?}} you thought, and the dragon nodded, so you continued,{{Alright then, think of humans kind of like apes accept without fur with only hair on their heads, and uh private region. Anyways they walk on their two hind legs, and overall are intelligent and communicate verbally. That's just a rough description.}}

    {{Considering that everything you just said is complete and udder bovine dung I'll just do this.}} the dragon said and placed a claw on your forehead.

    Suddenly you saw what seemed to be your entire life flash before your eyes, and then you backed up dazed,{{W...what was that?}} you asked

    {{Me reading your mind, well more like me reading your memories. Technically we're reading each other's minds at this moment. I suggest you follow me if you want any chance at survival, we can share territory once you get used to your new life.}} the dragon said

    {{New life? Wait you mean I can't change back?}} you asked

    {{If you could we both would know by your memories, besides the only clues would be back through the cave, and it collapsed because of your little struggle to escape. By the way the name's Stalker, and no that's not a hobby, it's just a name that I am forced to bear unfortunately.}} the dragon, Stalker said

    {{Well I guess that I really don't have a choice, wait if humans don't exist does that mean that I'm in the past or something like that?}} you asked

    {{I don't know what you mean by past, but I can say that you basically crossed dimensions, and basically can't go back. Nonetheless I think I saw humans before, it's just we dragons don't have a name for the species yet, here I saw a settlement on my way here inside my territory so I'll show you.}} Stalker said taking to the air

    {{I don't know how to fly remember?}} you said

    {{Just follow your instincts, and don't think about it too much.}} Stalker said


    You paused for a second and began flapping your wings to get used the the feel, and then flapped them once hard to gain some height. However when you did you didn't expect to go as high as you did and fell to the ground. You shook your head and tried again and seemed to be getting the hang of it, or at least you did on your 4th attempt, and hover stationary above the ground.

    {{Good, now tilt your wings in the direction you want to go. Landing is another story so just basically getting closer to the ground and landing on your feet is all you need to know. Slowing down is basically opening your wings in the opposite direction of where you're traveling. Just don't go too fast, or you could end up in a worse situation that you were going to be in than earlier with those rocks}} Stalker said

    {{Too soon}} you said

    {{What do you mean by that?}} Stalker asked

    {{It's just....Never mind I live in a world that finds humor in odd things. Anyways I'm going to have to practice this for some time, but let's see your humans.}} you said and Stalker began flying in a direction, and you followed him.

    Once you got to the area Stalker landed, and you crashed into the ground. {{Ooowww,}} you said dazed

    {{You definitely need practice flying, good thing we're not too close to the settlement.}} Stalker said,{{ this way,}}

    You followed Stalker to the settlement and were shocked to see that the humans were nothing like the ones you knew you were, and were...
