    Jeff's New Wardrobe

    added by Zoran 8 months ago TG O Mental

    Jeff unwillingly follows Sayaka into a clothing store, Jeff notices that something about the store feels off. First, there was the name “Poppy’s Princess Store”, and second, when stepping inside, the teen notices that the place is a “female-only” store! Jeff turns to Sayaka, looking at the pink haired girl in confusion, “um… why have you brought me to a “female-only” store,” he asks, “if you have forgotten: I am a MALE!”

    “Oh hush, Jeff,” Sayaka says, without looking at the boy, “what does it matter? Clothes are clothes, besides I think you will look good in some of the outfits. You do want to catch attention and want to look good, right?”

    “I… I guess,” Jeff says.

    Sayaka smiles, “of course you do,” she says, “I will make sure that you will have heads turned, and people think, “man, what a good looking and stylish boy”!”

    Jeff feels his mind seemingly to divide into two, one part tells him to run, while the other part tells him to trust Sayaka, that the pink haired girl knows what is best for him, that she only wants to help him make his life better and happier. Jeff clutches his head as he feels a headache start up, he lets out a groan and tries to get his thoughts sorted.

    Sayaka, noticing this, smirks, “having trouble, there,” she asks, “why are you trying to convince yourself that I am messing with you?”

    “Be-because you ARE,” Jeff says, “y-you are controlling me… and my thoughts…!”

    Sayaka pretends to be hurt, “I am disappointed that you don’t trust me, even though I only want your best,” she says, she then sighs, “I guess I have to win your trust. I hope by the end of the day I will have your trust.”

    “Dream on,” Jeff says through gritted teeth.

    Sayaka grins, “oh come on, Jeff,” she says, “at least try on some clothes, you might find them comfortable.”

    Jeff hesitates, he then says, “f-fine… I guess I could give it a try. BUT if I don’t like it, promise to leave me alone.”

    Sayaka smile, “of course, I promise,” she says, “now, come on, let’s find you some clothes.”


    Jeff follows Sayaka through the aisles towards the teen girl section, the boy blushes, ‘I can’t believe this is happening to me,’ he thinks to himself.

    First Sayaka guides Jeff to a rack with jeans, she takes a pair of plain blue jeans, a jeans with flowers printed on them, a jeans with glittery glass stones and a jeans with manufactured tears. She then continues on to the T-shirts and starts picking a few out, next were the sweaters and again Sayaka randomly grabs a few. The girl hands Jeff the clothes and continues on, Sayaka walks straight for the aisle of skirts and dresses.

    “Sa-Sayaka,” Jeff says, “wh-why are we in this section?”

    “To get some skirts and dresses, of course,” the girl says, rolling her eyes, “don’t worry, Jeff. You’ll find them comfortable.”

    Jeff is shocked, “I can’t run around in dresses and skirts! What will the people think?”

    Sayaka turns to look at Jeff, she grins, “oh, don’t worry,” she says, “they won’t think it is weird.” The girl then turns and starts to pick a few skirts and dresses, after that they head towards the shoes, Sayaka takes some sandals, sneakers and heels, she then turns to Jeff. “Let’s head to the changing area,” she says, “time for you to try on your new clothes.” On the way to the Changing Area, Sayaka grabs a few bras and panties out of a crate.
