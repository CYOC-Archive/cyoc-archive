    Mad Science

    The game is a weird one

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 8 years ago BM

    Mike and Kevin both got dressed which was also an interesting situation. They fell over while trying to put their pants on because they still had not got the coordination of being two halves sewn together. The shirt was not so bad. Oddly enough, it had two neck holes already. It's a good thing they were twins already otherwise their halves probably wouldn't have come together very well. They were identical on either side. Unfortunately for them, so were the two doors. One lead to the outside world where they would have to cope with being conjoined twin brothers now or they could continue to try and fix this problem of theirs.

    "Go ahead. Make your choice." The computer voice said. It was kind of rude, to be honest.

    "What do you think?" Kevin said, to his newly joined brother.

    "Well, the way I see it. Either choice is just as likely. We have no idea which door is which but each way we go would have equal results, so to speak. On one hand, we get out but we deal with everyone's questions about how we became conjoined and possible experimentation on us. On the other, we keep playing and possibly fix this or maybe make it worse. We're really better off just picking one at random. It doesn't matter either way, but that's my side. Your the other now." He said, jokingly. Mike thought about what his brother said and he agreed. The chances of either happening were exactly the same. There really wasn't a point to choosing one or the other.

    "Let's just go right. Like you said, it doesn't matter." Mike and Kevin nodded to one another and stepped through the right door. It was a hallway that didn't really seem like an exit. Just a deeper tunnel.

    "I'm sorry, subjects. You chose to continue the game. That means you get a reward of sorts." The computer voice said. The twins had no idea what the voice was talking about. They didn't feel rewarded really. Suddenly, a shock went through their body. They fell to their knees and placed their hands on the ground, although they did stumble to do this even. They couldn't freaking figure out why their entire body hurt. Suddenly, the pain concentrated entirely at just behind their shoulders. They could feel a bump forming.

    "What the fuck is happening?" Mike said as the bump moved closer and closer to the space between their necks. Their heads moved farther apart even to allow the bump more room. The bump grew bigger and bigger. Eventually, it formed a full head that looked identical to Mike and Kevin's. The shirt had somehow changed to have three holes.

    "What the fuck?" The third head said. The original twins were just as confused. They had now become conjoined triplets. Unfortunately, the changes weren't over.

    "To quote the late Mr. Mays, 'But wait there's more." The computer voice said. They could feel the pain concentrating to their arms now. Each of their fingers seemed to split in half, or rather simply replicate itself. They had ten fingers on each hand which was definitely weird. However, another set of arms simply set itself off from the original.

    "Every time you choose to stay and play the game, this is what will happen. There's another little surprise too." The voice said, as the triplets tried to coordinate their limbs. The middle head wanted control of the legs but their new configuration somehow wrapped up all three of their nervous systems so they all had some part in moving the legs. They eventually got to the next little intersection and saw there was now three doors instead of two.

    "Two of these doors lead to more games while the third leads to freedom. It's up to you to decide."
