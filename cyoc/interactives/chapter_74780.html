Small knots grew at their bases as their now fully canine tongues lolled out of their mouths. “We arrREe CHangiING INrrnrto doRRRGS” Scotty attempted to mouth but his now completely canine vocal cords cut him short.

The changes moved to their heads where hair shortened and changed to become matching fur while ears flopped over. The only human part of their physical forms were their young faces which started to push out into canine muzzles and their transformation was complete.

Stephen come get a picture of us,” the Madame ordered. Her son came bearing a large camera and took 3 or 4 pictures. He then came and showed the group what they looked like. In the picture was Riley and Eric two lion cubs almost identical except for fur color and next to them sat tongues lolled out Scotty and Jack to golden retriever pups.

And also have the ability to become your animal forms in cub form like you are now and in adult form like you would be at your normal ages. Go ahead and try to change back now, just imagine yourselves in your original forms.” The four young animals suddenly started to lose hair at an alarming rate and one at a time stood up as their changing musculature allowed them. Stephen managed to get one last picture of the four 19 year olds as they stood their on stage 2 with lion tails and lion hoods and two with doggy hoods and long bushy tails before they receded back into their now completely normal male bodies.

You four are a proud bunch.”

    She turned around and yelled, “NOW, who is next?!”
