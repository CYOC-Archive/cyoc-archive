One step at a time." She gave Xiao the best reassuring smile she could muster before the woman reluctantly nodded, turning to take a seat. Madeline quickly headed back into the front part of the lab, grabbing a broom and dustpan to clean up the glass shards scattered about.

    In short order, the mess had been dealt with, Madeline offering the doctor one final reassuring hug before heading back out the door and down towards the parking lot. She did her best to focus on what had to be done next as she walked the empty lot, but her mind couldn't help but wander back to that kiss. Even with all of Jennifer's changes... it hadn't felt so bad. Kind of nice, even. Maybe... maybe when things had stabilized, and she had moved from being mere student to a partner in developing this incredible genetic breakthrough... maybe she could be honest about how she'd been feeling.

And then... growing further still, not stopping even once she had turned the key and started the car up.
