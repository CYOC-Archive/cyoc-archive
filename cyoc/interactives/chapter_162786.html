    Erotos is a kingdom of immense power and wealth. The lands of Erotos are vast in their expanse and as such, it's people have evolved very varied subcultures and traditions. Some have even developed secret traits and abilities over the centuries isolated from the others. But no matter what, every Lord in Erotos submits to the Great King Edmond: champion of the Unity Wars and the single greatest and most respected man in the entire kingdom. His prowess on the battlefield and amongst his fellow men made him undeniably the perfect choice to lead the peoples of Erotos into a new golden age of peace. Which he did.

    But every great king must produce heirs. And the legendary Edmond was no exception. Once peace had been guaranteed, Edmond returned to a tiny village he campaigned through and made the virginal but divinely stunning milkmaid Veronica his Queen. The assembled court at first did not want to except the low born, insignificant girl as a viable bride to their ruler instead of one of their own daughters. But Great King Edmond sensed great potential in their union and threatened another war if they denied him. Terrified of the repercussions, the Court blessed their union. And fruitful their union was because, together, they went about providing Erotos with a battalion of princes, all eager to serve their father and Erotos. Each of his fifteen sons is a clear image of their father; tall, strong, and incredibly handsome.

    The eldest of these sons, William, is the true successor to his father's glory. Undefeated in all manners of combat, he is the perfect warrior. Charming and well-spoken, he is a promising politician. Especially magnetic to the ladies, he managed to snag a perfect wife from the faraway, exotic lands of Merdin. Another silent beauty, the crown prince's wife was selected over the millions of eager and fetching young maidens of the realm to be the perfectly satisfying choice. Each of the Great King's sons' wives were chosen this way. A prince's wife need only provide him with more children and be another jewel on his crown.

Many prestigious families have submitted their sons for his Majesty's selection, but who will he pick for his Princess Eve?
