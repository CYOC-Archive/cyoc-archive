    “Only if you slap me.” Countered Kelly “Can…can I touch it?”

    “My God…it is real.” Stated Kelly as Kenny reached over and touched Amanda’s tail as well.
    “You can have one to.” Said a new voice as Kelly and Kenny looked out toward the water to see multiple mermaids and merman surfacing. “You can live for years under the sea and be free of everything on the surface.”
    Liz went on to explain how they would gain longer lives and a bit about merpeople society like how love was accepted between almost everyone.
    “The rest of our friends will get the same choice.” Said Amanda as Kelly and Kenny shared a lot between each other. Amanda knew somewhat was going through Kelly’s mind, Kenny’s folks where racists who did not like their only son being with a minority and Kenny didn’t want to deal with his crazy family.
    “What do you think?” inquired Kenny
    “I want to say yes.” admitted Kelly
    “So do I.” admitted Kenny
    “So fuck it…lets do it.” Stated Kelly as Kenny nodded his head in agreement.
    “Great.” Answered Liz as she went on to explain the process.
    Within ten minutes Kelly and Kenny where standing by the edge of the same pool which had transformed Amanda only a few hours ago.
    “So in a few hours we will be merpeople?” inquired Kenny

    “It feels amazing.” confirmed Amanda
    “Well lets get this started.” confirmed Kelly as both of them got into the water to begin their transformation into merpeople.
