    "I have you now! Minion! bring them closer!" Dark Isaac commanded.

    Nat and the baby dragon struggle as they were back up the steps.

    As Dark Isaac went to remove Nat's cloths, Nat's sword hand suddenly moved on it's own and knocked away Dark Isaac's hand before he can touch her.

    Dark Isaac yelled in rage as he took back his hand. It was bleeding purple blood.

    "Y-you bitch! Alright, I'm trying to make you my slave! You're better off dead!" Dark Isaac screamed.

    Dark Isaac held out his hand towards the baby air dragon.

    "Kill the cloud girl my spawn!" Dark Isaac commanded.

    But the dragon didn't move. It just blinked in confusion. Despite being the lust demon's spawn, it seems it has no connection to Dark Isaac.

    This revelation confused Dark Isaac. He thought his soul had a part Azaviel! Maybe the baby can recognize that Dark Isaac is different from Azaviel.

    Dark Isaac growled in frustration.

    Looks like he has to do this the old fashioned way.

    Dark Isaac commanded the converted mummy to let Dark Isaac take care of this fight. He held out his hand and spawned a glowing purple crystal sword.

    Dark Isaac wasted no time and started swinging his sword at Nat.

    Nat gasped as the sword in her sword hand moved on it's own and blocked Dark Isaac's powerful swing.

    Soon, the fight evolved into a full blown sword fight with Nat dodging and parrying.

    With how fast the battle was moving, it took Nat a moment to realize that her body is moving on it's own!

    'No it's not. You're controlling you're body just find. You have the latent reflexes of a swordsman. I'm just guiding your movements in accordance to your inherited muscle memory.'

    "W-who are you?" Nat tried to speak, but the clashing of swords drowned out her words.

    'My name is Excalibur. I'm the deity of Clarity. I am currently speaking to you through your mind so no one other then you can hear me. Do you see the yellow gem embedded to the hilt of tour sword?' The strange voice asked.

    It was hard to see her hand moving at lighting fast speeds to counter Dark Isaac's strikes. But eventually, see was able to see he hand wrapped around the yellow gem embedded on the hilt.

    'That yellow gem is were I reside. I am the sword you are holding now. I was willingly sealed in the yellow gem many years ago. When you touched me, you and I formed a connection. You and I will be able to communicate as as long we have that connection.'

    'Why are you helping me?' Nat mentally asked.

    'Because I have chosen you. You are compatible enough to weld my power. You are one of the prophesied heroes who will purge the multiverse of corruption.' Excalibur explained.

    'Whoa. How could I be a hero if I STOLE you?' Nat asked.

    The sword let out a mental sigh. 'I wouldn't be able to take me if you were not chosen. I CHOSE to come with you.'

    Nat was confused. Why would a former town librarian like her be chosen for some crazy prophesy?

    Excalibur decided to change subjects. 'See that floating metal ring up there? You must strike me against the red gem embedded on the ring!'

    Nat nodded in understanding. Movement started feeling more natural as she willing moved with her sword fighting body.

    But Dark Isaac must have noticed Nat was after the Halo because he started menuvering himself between the Halo and Nat.

    Try as she might, not even the combined stregth of Nat and Excalibur could out menuver Dark Isaac's blocks.
