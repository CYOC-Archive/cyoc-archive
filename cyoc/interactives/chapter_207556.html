    Catching the attention of the public after springing up out of nowhere, the Conversion Store had a following of all sorts of individuals, humans and furries alike, who all shared an affinity for Zentai, Spandex, and adjacent kinks and fetishes. Zentai are defined as a one-piece bodysuit that covers the individual from head to toe in a smooth tight spandex-esque material, obscuring all physical features and identifying features.

    The store that was supplying these suits that are now made as drones was known as Minimal You. Utilizing a nanobot infused Zentai that allowed for specialized programming and personalization, they gained notoriety after producing a line of people being converted into Zentai Drones. Their ranks grew as more and more people strangely volunteered to undergo the process to become faceless, featureless Zentai Drones.

    After a steady growth, the company eventually announced construction of a greater Drone Conversion Convention Center and started franchising stores out all over the country, offering their conversion services to any who desired to become Zentai Drones.
    The primary goal of the company is to make Zentai Drones, and their primary targets are fetishists and zentai enthusiasts. This was started due to issues in overpopulation and economical disasters, by specifically attempting to reduce the population and help stabilize and reduce the overall national debt. In addition to those deeply in debt, this program was offered to individuals who were serving extensive sentences (predominantly life sentences) in penitentiary centers and prisons. Those who had particularly egregious sentences were even subjected to the erasure of their memories and personalities after being converted to drones.

    Zentai Drones are humans and furries who sacrificed their physical identities and varying amounts of their free will to become Zentai Drones. They typically converted ownership to family and loved ones, though sometimes, Drones surrendered themselves to the Conversion Center and subsidiary stores, while others gave themselves to strangers, and others still offered themselves up as freelance drones.

    Drones make excellent servants and living toys, and the nanobot Zentai comes installed with a full-fledged programming which allows Zentai Drones to be a part of the workforce, home companions, and therapeutic tools immediately after conversion. In addition, the programming can allow for individuals to have varying degrees of mental erasure, from having their personalities fully intact, to erasing every aspect of their past life and exist purely as Zentai Drones. Of course, all personality and memory is archived into their drone mindset vault which is highly secured.

    Zentai Drones all sport featureless bodies and faces, with very subtle hints as to what they were prior to their conversion. For instance, human females can maintain their breast and body curvature, though it's obscured and streamlined to further obscure identity. In addition, males can have visible pectorals and their genitalia can be outfitted to swell and become bulges upon erection, or permanently. For furries, they tend to retain any exterior features that were present, such as wings and tails, and their muzzles retain the shape of the species they started as, in addition to accommodating for any difference in posture, such as quadrupedal creatures or creatures without legs.

    Of course, features are altered. In addition to the features mentioned above, Zentai Drones have their hands converted to useless mitts, and their feet become like socks by default, unless specified. Any blemishes are smoothened and removed, as designated prior to conversion. And body weight is significantly reduced to make transportation and care easier.

    They come in a variety of colors and patterns, and these are all programmable through our specialized Tablet, which will be detailed later in the document. They are also granted special designations, typically located on the rear, above the pec/breast, or on the forehead. Most of their designations are mostly located on the left pec. Though some former features can be worked out, Zentai drones have most identifying traits obscured beneath the spandex skin. Their original names are etched into the soles of their feet or faded into the color below their designation, for the purpose of tracking and verifying identities for legal purposes.

    There are three types of drones in the world: Freelance, Companions, and Workers; while they have different arrays of conversion methods and newer ones that are still in the prototype stages. The mostly known conversions are- Masks, nanite gas exposure, injection, and the public prototype ring station. Out of those three, they are portable as well for someone to take it home, but only used for freelance or companion drone conversions (along with some papers to sign too). In order for a person to be a zentai drone they must be 18-21 or older to apply; even if curious, those below 18-21 must not be allowed in the premise at all times.

    If a person is becoming a companion drone but doesn't have an owner, they are taken to the M-Y's compound's public conversion shopping area, along with the conversion kits and wait to be bought. Following conversion, Zentai Drones are given a barcode to encode them into the system, and given a new designation, consisting of a letter of their first name and Two or More Digits (such as A-01). This designation is etched in using laser scanners to keep track of the drones. Pricing ranges from $50-$100 for baseline models, with specialty models costing extra. In addition, conversion devices can be purchased as a cheaper alternative.

    Drones that have had a full identity wipe can be customized and programmed with new personalities in addition to their color/pattern and their Drone Type.

    When a drone is purchased, the new Owner is also sent the Minimal You Tablet which will be included with the zentai drone..
