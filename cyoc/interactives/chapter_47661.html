    Matt undid their seat belt and opened the door with their arms, while Rex used their legs to step out of the car. Rex walked to the back door so Matt could open it and grab their backpack and throw it on. This cooperative maneuver of theirs only took a minute, but it didn't take long for them to be spotted, and people began to stare. Matt and Rex glanced at each other and sighed before Rex walked right by everyone and into the school.

    They had talked in the car on the way over and decided they were just going to ignore people gawking and just go about their business. The first thing they had to do was figure out who's classes they should go to. During another round of rock-paper-scissors they worked out a solution. They would alternate classes starting with Matt, then Rex, then Matt again, and the next day they would start with Rex, then Matt.

    First up was History. Rex walked them through the door and down the stairs before Matt grabbed onto one of the desks near the back and pulled them to it. Rex sat them down while Matt pulled his books out of their bag. After a few minutes the teacher came in and took a look around, stopping his gaze when he spotted the two headed student. "I'm sorry, but you must be new to my class, who are you?"

    The entire class turned and stared at Rex and Matt. Rex took control of his hand and covered his face, but Matt remained unfazed, "Umm, I'm Matthew. I've been in your class since..."

    "Not you. I know who you are. I'm talking to that other head growing out of your shoulder!" The teacher crossed his arms and stared at the pair with a straight face. "Care to answer that? And why you're in my class?"

    "I'm Rex, and well..." Rex said raising an eyebrow then gestured at Matt, "Me and him were fused together... So, I... kinda... have to be here?"

    "Rrriiight.... Well, just stay quiet." He turned to the chalkboard and started writing page numbers for the class to read for their next test. Matt chuckled to himself as Rex rolled his eyes. Matt began reading the first page of one of his text books while Rex sat bored out of his mind. Rex started twitching his legs and lightly tapping his foot.

    "Shh! Stop that!" Matt whispered and shoved Rex while glaring at him. "I'm trying to study!"

    "Sorry!" Rex whispered back, "This is just REALLY boring..."

    "Well, you're the one that lost the game." Matt grinned and opened his second book to another page. "Maybe you could help! I'll read the pages from this book, you read the ones for that book."

    "Oh joy!" Rex whispered sarcastically.

    "Hey! As long as we're stuck like this..." Matt quickly ran their hands across their chest, "We need to work together. Just remember, it's your class next... so it's up to you" Matt grinned wickedly as Rex turned away and sighed, then pulled the book closer and began reading it for his friend.
