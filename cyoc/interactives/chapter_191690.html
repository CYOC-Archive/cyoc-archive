    Entity #4232
    Codename: Alraune Flower

    Know powers:
    Part 1: At first glance a series of giant variety of simple beautiful flowers like Roses or Lilies. But if made in contact with those of female hearts (E.G. Natural born females and men that identify as female regardless or pre or post-op transition) can entice entry then fusion with contact. End result of fusion is green skinned version of person fused from waist-up to the core of the flower (reports also confirm further from ideal female form prior to fusion are inverted in end result). All results are reported as fully nude.
    Part 2: All known found victims and test subjects have displayed high connection to earth connected to roots and seem to draw nutrients and sustainability like normal flowers mixed in with wilful forceful suction of surrounding nature bar fellow Alraune. Extension: Alraune can if feeling threatened use roots by extending and breaching the earth grab targets and then drain plasma and blood from them in place of prior noted feeding.
    Part 3: Human-part of Alraune are able to separate from flower part for tasks but this is not a result of fusion being undone for they retain green skin and expelling of flower pheromones. Range of distance seems limited to reports of at max since last recordings to 10 feet away.
    Part 4: de-fusion is possible but risky, needing either consent of Alraune or specialized defensive equipment around support members for task. Classified Alchemy recipe is to be applied to body of human half of Alraune while separated from flower. Flower must also not be destroyed until process is finished for lifeforce between two is shared and as such premature destruction will result in human death (E.G. depending on mental state of restored victims or subjects, one can either fully recover or become more depressed depending on their acceptance and will power/noted male to female transformations often fall into later as they return to genetically male)

    Containment: Only males and females identifying as male (Pre and post op) can approached Alraune Flowers without issue, otherwise one must wear full body dense plastic minimum armour around Flowers. Otherwise unless labelled for termination (Bylaws prohibit complete extension of Entities) Flowers bonded or not as to be stored upon classified areas off grid of mainland under robotic guard yet maintains with usual plant care methods (only boosted in amount).

    Others: Currently records on hand say first find was 1874 in Germany or Greece (Notes of Number 2s of era involved in find contain some contradictions). Is believed to have involvement in same Novel that shares Entity name, Number 1 reports of time in question suggest Author was involved in an incident unrecorded in public records concerning the death of a friend’s daughter, supposedly from botched result to separate said daughter from Alraune flower (accuracy unconfirmed)
