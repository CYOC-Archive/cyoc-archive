    She Becomes Round and Red!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 4 years ago BM I S

    "Round and Red" she repeated. "That'd be the traditional clown nose?"

    "As classic as it gets." Roly Poly said. "Just imagine it. A nice round clown nose who can smell everything it's little body can desire."

    She giggled at the thought, and that's all that magic needed. She felt a sudden wave of goosebumps forming all over her body as her head began to throb and inflate. It was losing definition as a human head as her hair feel out and drifted to the bench she was sitting on. She felt it growing larger and rounder as the magic pounded in her mind. "Woah. Feels good." she said as her face became flat on the rounded surface, her own nose vanishing, leaving eyes and a mouth.

    Her body began to reduce and suck up into the mass that was her head, taking her limbs with it. As these changes happened, her arms and feet were all that was left after a few moments of her body turning basically into a liquid as it flowed away. There sat an orb with a simple face, hands, and feet that was quickly turning a shade of red. Soon it was shiny and red as the nose on Roly Poly's face.

    "Oh my goodness, I feel so strange." she said.

    "You should see how you look!" he laughed. "On second thought, let's finish the transformation for you" he said. "You still have a face, and look at my clown nose...does it have a face?"

    "No. It doesn't" she said.

    "So do you think you should have one?" he asked. Her magic addled mind thought, and she let out a giggle. "No! I gotta be a good nose to smell all the smells."

    "Bingo" he said as he began to rub his hands on her face and watched as her eyes vanished. "We'll leave the mouth for a bit" he said.

    "Uh okay" she said, unable to see now. She then felt him rubbing on her hands. "Can't use those as a nose, so they gotta go." he added.

    "Okie dokie!" she said dumbly. He did the same with her feet, which shrank and melted into her, leaving just a large red orb with a mouth.

    "Now we gotta cut you down to size. Noses aren't often this big."

    "Well, I wanna be perfect!" she said with a chuckle.

    He picked up the orb and began to squeeze her on the sides, which caused her entire orb body to compress, smaller and smaller, until it was about the size of a softball. "Not quite there." he said as he did it some more, making it the size of a ping pong ball, holding it aloft.

    "There, a perfect orb for a perfect nose." he said. "The next step, however, might make requesting to turn back hard. You okay with that?"

    "Yes please. I want to smell everything. I want it so bad." she replied.

    He giggled and began rubbing his fingers over her lips as she said "Thank You!" and the lips turned from human lips into a simple slot for a nose to be placed in the rubber looking orb. Silent, perfect, and waiting to be attached to an unsuspecting face.

    "What a good nose for my first try" he said.

    He then pondered what to do with it.
