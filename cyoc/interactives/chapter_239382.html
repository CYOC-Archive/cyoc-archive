I also got word from Casseus that Mrs. Hoti also has a daughter,” Effie seemed to lean forward in her chair at this mention. “And what’s more, she has a roommate with a daughter of her own around the same age. So it seems like you’ll have some playmates on this one.” Henry said getting her daughter excited again as she spun around in the chair.

    “Fuck Trisha, why do you have to be the thorn in my father’s side? Fine, what’s the fucking catch with the brats?” Effie sighed as she leaned back in the chair.

    “According to Mr. Casseus as well as the Dark Garden, Eliana Hoti’s roommate as well as her daughter have powers related to a quote ‘Japanese Magical Girl Show shit’ as Dark Garden put it.” Trisha explained.
