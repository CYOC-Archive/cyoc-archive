    Jenny quickly got dressed to go meet master's.....er Jeff's parents. She couldn't help but giggle at how fast the Chronovac was working. This was going to be a fun week at school. Once dressed, Jenny ran down the stairs to greet the new arrivals. "Hey! Hey!" She called as she the hall as she excitedly ran. "Hi." She nearly shouted when she saw them.

    Jeff's parents smiled at her abundant enthusiasm. "Well hi there." Jeff's dad responded.

    His mom smiled knowingly. "I'm guessing someone already started her transformation."

    "Me!" Jenny responded. "Its me." She couldn't help but jump up and down a little.

    Jeff's mom laughed. "Well, I'm glad you are excited for your new role. I know Jeff always wanted a dog, but real dogs are so messy and hard to train. We are so lucky he found a girl willing to become a Permute-Dog for him. They are so popular these days."

    Jenny squirmed with excitement. "Honestly, I've always wanted something like this. It feels like I was just waiting for the right person to give myself to."

    "Speaking of," Jeff's dad interrupted, "we have bought all sorts of dog stuff for you." He reached in a bag and pulled out a dog bowl with Jenny's name on it. "I promised your parents we would take care of you and I plan on making you the most pampered pup in town."

    "Remember Craig." His wife chastised him. "Jenny is Jeff's dog. He needs to take care of her."

    "I know, I know."

    Jenny ignored the whole conversation and stared at the dog bowl. It didn't matter that she was still a human, the already considered her an animal. She couldn't wait for that to be a reality.

    Jeff came downstairs and helped unload the groceries. Finally his mom suggested taking Jenny for a walk. Jenny squealed with excitement. As they were leaving she called out to them.

    "Jeff! What are you thinking? Where's Jenny's leash?"

    Jeff was confused for a moment. "I mean, she's still human so..."

    His mother sighed. "Jeff, you need to watch the video from permuted pets again. Jenny only looks human. Her senses and instincts are already changing. If she catches an interesting scent she's bound to run off without you. Maybe even into traffic. "

    Jenny waited patiently while Jeff attached the leash to her collar. As he opened the door, she dashed out in front of him and pulled him along. Jenny barely heard Jeff's mom call out I told you so as they left.

    Eventually Jeff got her to calm down enough and found the video his mom talked about on his phone. Jenny had to force herself from ignoring the mysterious of the great outdoors to see what the video said. She was curious about how the world had changed to fit her wish.

    While transformation was Jenny's kink, she found the first part hard to focus on. All about why permutted pets were better. Easier to train and blah blah blah.

    Finally it got to the interesting part where the spokesman had his daughter come out. "Do demonstrate, Sophie has volunteered to become a cat." The busty Goth girl was distracted by a cat toy in her dad's hands. She batted the toy away as he kept bringing it closer.

    "Within ten minutes, her mindset has already started to change to be that of a common house cat. Withing just five days the permutations will have completely changed her species. "

    It then showed a series of photo, each one labeled a different day. Each day the girl was less human and more cat like. Finally a black cat was all that remained of the former humans.

    "Anyone can make a good permute pet. Friends, siblings, children, or even whoever you are dating. The closer the relationship, the better the pet will be."

    "Looks like the reality alteration made this an actual business." Jeff commented. "I wonder if he turns Sophie back, it doesn't say."

    "Its okay." Jenny told him. "I'm sure she will go back if you ever decide to undo my change." Jenny stressed the if. It was important that he knew it was up to him if she was restored. He was her master after all.

    Once they got back from the walk, Jenny had dinner with her new family. She was allowed to eat at the table, but was told not to get used to it. Once she was a bit more dog like she would be eating on the floor like a normal pet.

    Everyone had tacos, while she ate dog food from her new bowl with a spoon. It was almost like eating weird smelling cereal. She never cared for the smell of dog food, but it was actually kind of appetizing now. The taste was still odd though.

    Jenny slept on the couch, another temporary luxury. Tomorrow she would start her last week of school, and see her first change. She could barely sleep due to all of the excitement.
