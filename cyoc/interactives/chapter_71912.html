    The floor rumbled and rose for a few feet. The four boys woke up in a shock. Slits appeared on the walls and wall suddenly poured out.

    "Wake up! Wake up! Time for a shower!" the doctor's voice boomed loudly from the speakers, "Grab the soap and shampoo from the drawers. We have a busy day ahead."

    "What does this psycho have in plan now?" grumbled Justin.

    "Whatever it is. It's not pretty," responded James. The two Jims gave each other a kiss and then gave did a three way kiss with James. Justin saw this. He winced slightly, but he was intrigued.

    The four boys showered and dried themselves with the towels that suddenly appeared. The floor rose up again and another drawer revealed 4 black speedos.

    "Each of you take one and wear it. We're gonna do some physical exercises today," shouted the doctor. After the four boys got dressed, the floor rumbled and rose again. This time they were out of the hole. Four giant robot arms quickly grabbed one brother and placed him an steep ledge. The doctor appeared on a hologram in front of the boys.

    "Today we are gonna test some physical skills you may have. Just finish this obstacle by reaching the goal." then the doctor disappeared.

    The floor quickly grew sharp spikes. One fall could kill any of them. In order to get to the goal, one would have to walk across this flimsy bridge that suddenly appeared in front of the ledge. The course was a straight line to the goal. However, what dangers lurk as the brothers walk across it?

    Since they didn't really have a choice, the brothers attempted to cross the bridge. The bridge was slippery and shaky. With each step, it shook and vibrated. It got narrower as it got closer to the goal. The floor rumbled and it sank deeper. Now the fall is even greater now. Each brother was careful not to slip. One by one they crossed to reach the goal. Justin was first, followed by James, and Jim. John was close behind but before he could step into the goal, a robot arm grabbed John and slammed him into the floor.

    "Oh god!!" cried Jim. Justin and James were stunned and horrifying. John's lifeless body was impaled by several spikes and broken at several places.

    "That one was slow," the doctor said through speakers, " can't have slow folks here."

    Other robot arms grabbed the remaining three brothers and gently placed them in a new hole.
