    husband, "Carl, honey." Carl noticed that for the first time since he'd been transformed she called him by his

    Isn't the idea of that great."

    "The idea of Wendy and her husband Carl going on might get me interested. Not Wendy and her big boobed twin
    sister Carly."

    "But you know that's the only way we would ever get on the show. They never put happily married people on
    there, it's not interesting. I don't like it either but it's the only way we can do this." Carl really had a
    feeling she was not telling the whole truth about not liking that but chose to skip that argument for now.

    "But what about the kids? We can't just go off and leave them."

    "Reality contestants do it all the time. I can just wish them to be living happily with my parents. Just think
    about what half a million could do for them. They could be set for life." Wendy said now going with the guilt

    "Then why don't you just wish for a half million!" Said Carl stating the obvious.

    "Because that would cheapen it. I want to earn that money on the show. Please, let's do this together. It's a
    dream come true for me."

    Carl looked at his wife in the eyes. He knew for certain the last part was true, he knew it was her dream to
    do this. So finally defeated, Carl reluctantly said. "On two conditions."

    Wendy looked about ready to burst with happiness, "Name it."

    "First, in between the time we accept the deal to the time we are about to enter the house, I get to be Carl
    again. And two, when we get there, please don't mess with me mentally or personality wise. If I'm going to do
    this, I want to retain as much of myself as possible."

    "Deal." Wendy said, all the while thinking she'd avoid it the best she could, but if an opportunity popped up
