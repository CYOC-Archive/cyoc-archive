    Jeff groans, rubbing his temples, ‘why is it so hard to decide,’ he asks himself, he then lets out a sigh, ‘I think I’ll take a break,’ getting to his feet and exiting his room, heading downstairs to make himself a snack, leaving his laptop and program running, not worried about someone just walking into his room.

    However, as soon as Jeff has entered the kitchen and begins preparing a snack, he doesn’t notice the door to his 6-year-old brother Colin’s room open and the young boy stepping out, heading straight for Jeff’s room.

    ‘I hope Jeff doesn’t mind me borrowing his coloring pens,’ Colin thinks to himself as he enters Jeff’s room, ‘I’ll be quick, he won’t even notice I was here’. Colin walks towards Jeff’s desk, opening drawers and rummages around for the coloring pen set of his brother. As he rummages around, his gaze lands on the laptop screen, Colin tilts his head when he notices the weird program running, ‘did Jeff get himself a new game,’ he asks himself, ‘it looks interesting, I’d like to check it out. I should better copy it onto my phone, I don’t want to risk making Jeff mad by fiddling with his game’.

    Colin takes out his phone and activates the Bluetooth on both devices, he then begins copying the “game” over onto his phone. As he starts the copying, he is asked if he’d like to link to copy with the original, or have it be it’s own and independent program. Colin decides to create an independent program, to not risk ending up messing up Jeff’s “game”.

    Once the program is copied over, Colin heads back into his room, the coloring pen set forgotten, as he jumps onto his bed and opens the app, curiosity and excitement in his eyes as he opens the manual and begins to read.

    Colin’s eyes widen the more he reads, his surprised expression turns into a mischievous grin, ‘ooooh, I’ll have so much fun with this,’ he thinks to himself, unlike Jeff, immediately believing what the manual claims, ‘I could do so many awesome pranks, and also, finally, satisfy my curiosity! I’m kinda curious what it would feel and how I’d look like as a girl! And I could also finally understand why mommy acts so strange around dad, besides: I’m curious what it would feel like being a big girl! Hehe… I could prank Jeff! Maybe swap bodies with him… hehe… would be cool being the big brother for once! Hm… what shall I do first?’

    As Colin thinks this, he hears Jeff call out, “COLIN, I AM HEADING TO MY GIRLFRIEND’S! I WON’T BE BACK UNTIL SUNDAY EVENING!”

    “WHAT?! YOU’RE LEAVING ME HERE ALL BY MYSELF!?”

    “DON’T WORRY, SQUIRT,” Jeff calls back, “DAD’S GONNA BE HOME SOON,” and before Colin is able to respond, the front door slams shut.

    “Well… guess I won’t be pranking Jeff,” Colin mumbles to himself, “no biggie! I could simply call a friend over. Or I could head out and prank some neighbor, or I could transform myself into a girl or… mommy! Hm… what should I do?”
