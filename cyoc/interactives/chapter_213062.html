    The Narrator: A Change In Management

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 7 months ago A I O Superpowers

    "I don't like the way you speak to me sometimes" I decide that, armed with my new found powers I am willing to have it out with my boss at last. It's not like she gave me the raise of her own free will after all.

    "You don't?" Christina folded her arms and looked annoyed "I'm arranging for you to get a raise and a bonus and that is how you thank me?"

    "Yeah" I shrug "You can be pretty sarcastic and contemptuous sometimes and it pisses me off. Besides, it's not like the bonus or raise were your idea."

    "Really? I'm pretty sure they were" she looks like she genuinely believes this "And you will get them. But I can't always be super polite with you when you keep messing up or not following simple instructions. I guess I try and use humour to try and take the edge off"

    "You use humour like anyone does to make people laugh" I reply, feeling my annoyance levels rising again "And at my expense. It's like you think I am beneath you, less than human"

    "Less than human? Really?" Christina replies cynically.

    "Yes" I reiterate "Do you have any idea what that feels like?"

    "Well, no. Probably because I'm human" she responds with a smirk.

    "Oh really?" I smile back as I prepare to once again enter Narrator mode "To her great surprise Christina found herself completely transforming from my obnoxious human boss into .."
