    Fulfilling a Fantasy

    added by LilLilly96 7 months ago AR AP BM S TG Mythological Magic

    “Actually,” you said to Bianca, “I was wondering if you’ve got any good fantasy books in stock? I’m a huge fan of fantasy stories and-“

    “Ah, say no more,” Bianca said with a smile, “I completely get it… Sometimes you just need to lose yourself in tales of knights, dragons, goblins and princesses… We have plenty of books in stock that would tickle your fancy, sweetie!”

    The petite goth girl leads you through the aisles to a section marked FANTASY… As you passed, you saw a few other library patrons passing with their arms full of books… They seemed to be excited with their choices but… You could have sworn you saw someone at one of the tables disappear as he was reading?

    Ah, probably your imagination.

    You began to peruse the books in the shelves, finding a few options that interested you…

    -“The Lost Princess”: The Kingdom of Kiba has been missing its Princess, the lovely Amelia, since she had been stolen by raiders many moons ago… But all hope returns when a young village girl is found with the same locket that the princess had long ago… A tale of rags to royalty that will entice any fantasy lover!

    -“Fairy’s Folly”: Sugar Snap is a playful little fairy… One who causes mischief everywhere she goes! Fed up with the humans trying to take her mushroom home away, she decides to enact some good old fashioned mischief

    -“The College of Magic”: On her 18th birthday, Penny Blotter discovers that she’s more than just some poor orphan girl… She’s a witch! Join Penny and her friends in her first year at a magic college as she and her friends learn the ropes of magecraft and magicry!

    -“Goblin Girls are Easy”: Two virgin peasants looking to be considered men decide to finally get some action… And they decide to do it in the best way possible… With a goblin girl! After all, goblin girls are notoriously easy and willing to hook up… And Gays, the new goblin bartender at the local tavern, may just be the one to help.

    What book will you choose?
