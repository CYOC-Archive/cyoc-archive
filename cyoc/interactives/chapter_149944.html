    An Angry Transformation

    added 4 years ago AP BM S

    "He had to leave" Jake lied. "Have you seen Jenny?" He asked. "She's over there" Jason says and Jake walks over. "Hey I'm feeling a little sick. I'll see you tomorrow" Jake says. Jenny just nods nonchalantly. "Man everyone here has been drinking too much" Jake says and heads back to Scott's pickup truck. Using the medallion he transforms back into Scott and drives back to his house.

    However, instead of Dave meeting Jake on the porch he sees two other people. They look like... Sean and Jessica! And hey both look extremely angry at Jake. "Hey why didn't you pick me up from the airport! I had to hail a taxi!" Sean yells. "And you said you would back soon not the entire day! You left me alone with Mat- I mean Andy for the entire day" Jessica exclaims. They continue to yell at Jake as Dave stands off to the side.

    "Listen Jessica I'll meet you at your place as long as you leave now" Jake whispers to her. Jessica still glares at him. "I'll also give you $200" Jake says. Jessica shrugs and walks to her car and drives away. Now Jake would have to deal with Dave. "Look I gotta talk to my bro but if you want I can meet you tomorrow" Jake says with a wink. Dave nods and gives him a quick kiss before driving away. Meanwhile Sean is still glaring at Jake.

    "I thought you had to cancel or get together?" Jake asks. "Dude I sent that letter weeks ago remember" Sean says. "Shit!" Jake thought. He had only found the letter in Scott's locker but had not bothered to check the date. Now if he left Scott would wake up and find Sean angry at him. Then it wouldn't be long before the two would find out something fishy was going on. "Listen Sean...what would it take for you to forgive me?" Jake asks. Sean gives a mischievous smile.

    Jake leaves Scott's house feeling rather sore and begins walking over to Jessica's house. He knocks and she opens the door carrying Matt. "It's a good thing my parents aren't home right now or they would have noticed him" Jessica says. Matt glares at Jake. "Here give him some of these" He says passing her a sleeping pill. "Why?" She asks. "Just do it. And don't worry they're safe for him" Jake says.

    Jessica gives the pill to Matt and they soon find him sleeping like a baby. "OK now we need to change him back" Jake says. Using the medallion they transform Matt back into himself but only after they drop him off at the party. "This way it doesn't look so weird" Jake explains as they placed him on the couch inside still asleep. "OK but why is he still wearing the Power Ranger underwear?" Jessica asks. "I thought it was funny besides there was no way I was going to give him back his pants and go home in my boxers" Jake says.

    After paying Jessica $200 she drops Jake off at his house. Clutching the medallion he cautiously peers inside and sees his whole family watching a movie in the living room. He enters through the back door and walk upstairs where he transforms back into himself. "Man today was hectic but I can't wait to use this again" Jake says as he crawls into bed.

    Jake would soon be grounded by his parents for skipping school while Matt would awaken a few hours later to laughing and rush back home where he would believe the whole experience to be a dream and would later be berated along with Scott for skipping wrestling practice. Sean would never mention the dilemma with the airport and everything just seemed to work out.

    In the end it was a few weeks before Jake had a chance to use the medallion again. By then it was April and spring break was approaching. Jake had lots of plans for the medallion but who would he change into and where?
