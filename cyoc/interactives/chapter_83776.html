    "Crap. Come on, we need to get to him first." Cal said, bursting out of the room.

    "I need to go do pray stuff." He shouted, tearing past the battle nuns.

    "Sorry." Logan said, also running past the nuns.

    "Where's he being held?" Cal asked once Logan caught up.

    "Follow me." Logan said, kicking open the door for a stairwell. Mere minutes later, the pair had arrived at the detention cell.

    "Hold it." The guard said.

    "I'm security officer Whiting, I've been working with you guys to solve this case the whole time." Logan said.

    "Funny that you didn't catch the Magos then." The guard smirked.

    "We were just about to. Now let us in or I'll use my big mech hands to rip your anal cavity open." Cal said.

    "Aren't you a nun?" The guard said, stepping aside.

    "Bloody Roses punk, now piss off." Cal said, punching the door in.

    "She's upset over... prayers or something. I'll keep an eye on her." Logan said, ducking in behind Cal as the guard ran away.

    "Holy cow, am I glad to see you guys." Gabe said from behind a transparent blue wall.

    "Let me guess, laser/forcefield." Logan said.

    "Hmm." Cal said, poking the wall with his finger, producing a bit of smoke.

    "It'll melt right through your armor." Gabe said.

    "Great. Gabe, we know you killed the guy, so we're gonna accuse you and kill you now." Cal said, walking over to the cell's controls.

    "What?" Gabe shouted.

    "Well, you're the only one besides Rebeca who isn't on our team, and Rebeca hasn't done anything at all, so it probably wasn't her." Logan said.

    "You're on a team? That's cool! Can I join?" Gabe asked.

    "Nope." Cal said, scanning the controls.

    "But I have all the evidence of the killer." Gabe said.

    "What?" Logan and Cal said in unison.

    "Yeah, it was on a floppy disk. I sent my savant to get something that could read the disk, but when he hacked the lifeboats, they arrested me." Gabe said.

    "The captain's eyes were gouged out." Logan said.

    "So you would have just left instead of sending your savant if you were the killer." Cal said.

    "Yes, now get me out of here." Gabe said.

    "Where's the disk, first?" Logan asked.

    "Off the ship, but I can take you to it." Gabe said.

    "Sounds like a trap." Cal said.

    "But if we're wrong, then Rebeca or Will wins." Logan said.

    "And if we're right?" Cal asked.

    "It's Battle Royale, only one of us can win." Logan said.

    "This is bad." Gabe said.

    "We get screwed no matter what happens." Cal said.

    "Pretty much." Logan said.
