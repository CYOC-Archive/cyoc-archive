    It all began when a lonely spaceship arrived in earths orbit. The captain turned on the cloaking device and then left the bridge to report to his queen. What made everyone on the ship special was that they were an alien race of anthropomorphic felines known as the Gatolites. Each one varied in both feline species and their fur color was very diverse, the captain for example was a green furred male lion. The only similarities among the Gatolites were that everyone aboard were wearing uniforms that were very revealing due to them having higher than average libidos. The males had large muscular builds while the females had curvy hourglass shaped bodies while still being agile.

    The captain entered a chamber that was repurposed into a throne room where a blue furred yellow striped tigress anthro was lying on a bed in a very seductive pose wearing very revealing underwear laced with jewelry appearing to be royal attire by Gatolite standards. But what stood out the most was she was pregnant, in fact about half of the female Gatolites onboard were expecting cubs. This is because due to the reckless actions of space pirates, an asteroid drifted and crashed onto the Gatolites home planet destroying it and resulting in the anthro space felines becoming an endangered species.

    Fortunately for them, the galactic council gave them permission to claim a new planet as their home since the asteroid crash was not caused naturally, and the planet that the council chose to give them was Earth. “My queen,” the green lion said while taking a knee. “We have arrived in the orbit of our new home, have you and the pride houses decided on how we’re going to deal with the planet’s primitive humans?” “We have,” the tigress replied rubbing her pregnant tummy.
