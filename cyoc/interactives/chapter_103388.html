    "I wish I was a kirin," Amy said.

    "A kirin?" Mary asked, unsure of what she meant.

    "Yeah they're like unicorns, only a little bit." The child said. She pulled out a book from her pack and showed her a picture of what appeared to be a unicorn and a dragon mix with the horn, legs and hooves of a unicorn, but the back has a little bit of scales of a dragon with other features. "This is a kirin."

    Mary studied it carefully. "Why do you want to be that?"

    The girl pouted, "Cause they're my favorite! plus, it's my wish."

    Mary chuckled to herself as She didn't know if she had that kind of power. before she could even think about it though, she felt something trickling through her fingers. Looking down, she saw golden energy pouring from her fingers and forming a sort of magic dust that began to float around Amy.

    Almost as soon as it hit Amy, Mary felt a strange but wonderful sensation, as if living sunlight was radiating from her body. It manifested around Amy more as the little fairy watch the child began to transform.

    Amy was too lost in the magic herself to know or answer if her imagination gave her some indication. To Mary, she saw the girl's hair start to kink as Her hair turned bright yellow and fell long down her back, as her face and features softened even more as scales start to form. Her ears grew to points as Her clothes swirled with magic and fell to all fours, fur and scales started to covering her swiftly growing body as her hands and feet changed to little cloven hooves.

    Her tail grew out and swishing behind her as her face pushed into a horse's muzzle. The last change was a horn, which as it grew from her skull to sprouted a smaller horn from it as it turned bright brown, as her hooves were coated gold.

    The magic swirled around the new creature as it faded and Amy fell on her side with a whinnied, standing back up yet feeling tall now as a beautiful kirin. Amy was At first confused, but then smiled with delight as she saw the changes and bowed her head to Mary.

    "I'm a kirin! Mary....thank you."

    "Amy?" Mary asked.

    "The child that you transformed has been remade into me, but I am still Amy...
