    The truck eventually made its way to the African Savanah exhibit. This was a popular attraction filled with amazing elephants, lions, giraffes and many other classic safari animals. The guests particularly enjoyed the drive-thru safari which allowed them to get up close and personal with their favourite animals.

    However, Dr Jenkins was not enjoying this particular visit. He was scared as the two men from the truck pushed him inside a small office hut before throwing him on to his knees.

    Inside he was greeted by the Taylor twins, Lily and Serena. The two sisters had been running the African exhibit like clockwork for nearly a decade. Both were feisty and took no prisoners, which left the doctor with little hope of a merciful pardon.

    “Hello doctor” Lily began “welcome to the African Savanah. I suggest you get comfortable because from now on this will be your new home.”

    “Well unless you escape that is...or we decide to turn you back!” Serena’s interjection made both of them burst into laughter. Meanwhile Jenkins was visibly shaking; these two bitches were clearly deranged!

    “As I was saying before I was so rudely interrupted,” Lily said as she glanced at her sister, “we will take care of you during your stay, but misbehave in any way and don’t think we won’t cart you off to become some rich hunter’s future trophy.”

    “Yes that would be terrible,” Serena added sarcastically, “I would much rather keep you as my little pet. Besides, if you leave then you won’t be around for our award-winning breeding programme. Most of our new arrivals are reluctant at first, but once they try it for themselves they tend to enjoy it. Just imagine, soon there will be adorable babies running around; and some of them could be yours!”

    The doctor didn’t say anything, not wanting to show his internal terror to the two crazy women.

    “Well you’d better change your attitude mister,” Serena continued with a mocking tone of pretend disappointment, “you won’t be able to enjoy yourself until you accept your fate.”

    Again Dr Jenkins said nothing.

    “Well sister” Lily said “I think it’s time we give the doctor here what he deserves.”

    Jenkins put up a pathetic struggle as he finally cracked, begging his captors to show mercy. Sadly it was too little too late; soon the doctor was being injected with a black-coloured transformation serum.
