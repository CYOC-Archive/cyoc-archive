    listened in on a conversation that her parents obviously hadn't wanted her to hear.

    Serenity felt like she was an audience of a disaster movie unable to do anything but what

    "Now, listen to mommy very carefully sweat heart, we're all depending on you," Felicity

    curse.. of course Julie would tell that information to Jensen... 'This Jensen is much much

    teenager around Ulysses' age! That... that if she waited any longer, she'd likely be trading

    "Mommy, daddy? Truth, I mean, Transform or Dare?"

    Trevor saw the cracks around the door connected with other cracks. They would break

    'We need more time.' Trevor thought. 'Are you watching Queen?'

    "TREVOR Don't kill yourself now!" Serenity screamed in horror.

    "GETOFFGETOFFGETOFF!" The beast's claws shadowed downward on Trevor.

    Trevor clawed onto the top of the wolf's head, and he felt claws of his bandaged leg dig

    "MYEYE!!!" The wolf man screamed.

    "That was for Queen! Tell the black gold ugly one I'm coming for him!"

    The claws were on Trevor's back in a second, he felt their bone knives slice along his back,

    The wolf man screamed even more now one eye closed forever and a stream of blood

    Trevor off, Trevor bit into nerves and bone not letting go, he did however lose a fang that
    remained imbedded in the wolf's tail as the law of motion overruled Trevor's
    determination and he went flying, hitting the hallway wall -just- missing a glass portrait
    that would have cut up his inside, the impact was enough as he suffered a second impact
    as he hit the floor a moment later, all the world upside down and inside out as his brain
    and legs seemed to have opposing ideas about how to stand up.

    One of the other wolves snarled while the other two tried to stop the bleeding of their
    teammate, "Someone tie up or the break legs up or SOMETHING that little devil! I don't
    care if we were to bring him back unharmed! I'm sure Master won't mind if the cat a
    couple more broken legs after this ****ING STUNT!"

    gave, but it didn't go inside, it went outward!

    Roger was caught off guard as the door and door frame (themselves still not broken,
    opened, or damaged seriously in any way whatsoever) went into the hallway right into him,
    smashing into the opposite side leaving an impression.

    Trevor's blurred eyes saw a reddish gold and brownish gold humanoid shapes riding the
    inside of the doorway as it came into the hallway on of the wolf. Trevor smiled lion very
    similar to Mary's scent.

    He heard an adult lioness snarl, no, that was putting it too lightly, he heard an adult
    lioness -roar- in english, "This is our home! You're trespassing! GET-OUT!"

    "THE HELL!" Zack had time to swear before he got a double pawed kick to the stomach that
    made him grunt.

    The two yellow shaped attacked in full force, and the wolves, simply not being as limber or
    as fast as them, began to edge back.

    Trevor felt wetness all over his back, he saw redness all over his gray and black fur...
    'Dang... am I gonna bleed to death? How much blood does a cat have in it?'

    A smaller yellow shape ran out of the room the larger wolves distracted that gently picked
    him up off the floor and spoke in Mary's voice, "It's okay Trevor! I'm here! You're gonna be
    okay! Promise!"

    That was when a purple shaped crashed into Mary, and the two rolled around, Trevor
    hitting the floor again.

    "I don't wanna fight your Ulee!"

    "I don't wanna fight you Mary! But you all gotta see Master and join da pack!"

    "I'm part of a pride already! Please Trevor! I don't wanna hurt ya!"

    "I don't either!"

    Needless to say, both voices sounded like this was the last thing in the universe any of
    them wanted to be doing.

    Trevor felt elderly hands grab him and pull him into the study, and he felt a soft but firm
    shape go against his back stemming the bleeding. "Don't you die yet understand?" Madam
    Serenity's voice told him sternly.

    A second later one of the wolf men screamed as one of the yellow shapes used leverage to
    dislocate bones.

    That was when they heard a great ape snarl. It was followed by a female wolf screaming
    the words, loathing every syllable, "RETREAT!!"
