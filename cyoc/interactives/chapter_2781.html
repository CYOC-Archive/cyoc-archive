    Off in the distance Stella Flycatcher could see a dust cloud rapidly approaching the livery.

    "Daddy,who are those men in black riding black horses?"

    Mr Flycatcher didn't answer his daughter immediately.He watched while A tall gaunt woman dressed in black from her boots to her bonnet dismounted and tied her horse.

    Afterwards 6 women and 7 men dressed in similar attire dismounted and tied their horses to the posts.Then they formed a line,with the tall woman and her husband in the middle,and marched as one towards the livery.

    You could hear the thump of their boots as they all stepped forward in unison.

    The tall woman and her husband entered the building while the rest of the gang stood guard outside.

    Stella whispered"MY Gawd,its Doc Fenton and her Sterilizer gang."

    "Jeb,its come to our attention that youse got a non sterile mule.It aint natural"

    "we're giving you till noon to bring the mule in for treatment,after that we're comin to get him"

    Doc Fenton put her trembling hands back in the pockets of her petticoat and exited.
