    The Sorcerer's Cards of Transformation

    “Alright, we’re going to play a game. Our brave volunteer Katie is going to change. But not just into anything. We’re going to turn out Katie into a brand new animal, that no one in this world has ever seen before! But to do that, we’ll have the help of these.”

    With the swirl of a cape, the Sorcerer suddenly had a deck of large cards in her hand, with each card big enough to fill the page of a kid’s chapter book. The backsides were decorated with the same mystic green as her magic, along with pictures of a magician’s mask.

    “This is a deck of cards, but not normal playing cards. Every card describes a certain part of an animal! Or maybe a body part that’s strange for a person. FOR EXAMPLE!”

    The Sorcerer drew out a card from the deck, and threw it out into the air. Once she let it go, there was a flash of light, and the card was suddenly hovering over the stage as becoming as big as a large banner. The audience of kids let out a chorus of impressed ‘whoa’s ‘. At least until they saw what was on the giganticized card.

    The card had a cartoonish but detailed drawing of a horse’s penis and testicles, with the words ‘A Horse’s Penis’ written on the bottom in comic sans. The children broke into hysterical laughter.

    “Something like that,” the Sorcerer quipped, as Katie looked at the card’s picture with a shiver. The young girl felt her fingers, which were shielding her privates, lightly squeeze on her little opening as she imagined what it was like to have that thing on her body.

    “Don’t worry, that card doesn’t count. Unless I pick it for you,” the Sorcerer teased. “Or…unless you want to pick it.”

    That last bit made Katie do a double take. Unless she picked it?

    The gigantic hovering card began to rapidly shrink like a quickly deflating giant balloon, and the now normal-sized card fluttered back onto the Sorcerer’s deck on the proper side.

    “Here’s how it’s going to go. I , Sorcerer Spectra, will take out five cards from this deck.” As she said this, the Sorcerer drew five cards from the deck in her hand, and showed the backsides of the deck to the audience. “Each time that I do so, I’ll pick a single card that will show a new animal part that Katie will get. And then, our brave volunteer will change according to what’s on that card.”

    “But, here’s the interesting part: I’m not the only one who gets to pick!”

    The Sorcerer placed her cards back into the deck, and shuffled it up. Then, with her powers, the cards glowed, and five cards floated into the air from the top of the deck.

    “Katie will get to do the same thing as I did! I will pull out a new set of cards, and she will pick one card from what I pull out. Then, she’ll get to change according to what she picked. Sounds fun, right?”

    The Sorcerer gave Katie a wink. Katie wasn’t sure how to react. At least she wasn’t at the complete mercy of the Sorcerer, but what can Katie choose? What if everything she could pick was going to be embarrassing?

    Her mind was still trying to grasp that she was going to be an animal. It made her anxious, and admittedly a little bit excited. But there was also a fear that she’d be stuck in whatever new body the Sorcerer changes her to. What if the Sorcerer was lying when she said she’ll turn back to normal?

    “Now, we’ll both take turns in taking cards, and we won’t stop until Katie is completely animal, with no normal parts of her left!”, the Sorcerer called out.

    “And, here’s the even fun part! Katie, you get to pick first.”

    The five floating cards flew over towards Katie, and her choices floating right in front of her face. As the audience waited in anticipation, Katie looked at each card, and decided on her choice...
