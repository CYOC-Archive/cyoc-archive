You get a large burger with some fries and Rose gets a salad. The food takes about 30 minutes to get to your table and when it does you dig in. You finish your burger and you chat with Rose for a while. You pay for the food and you walk around the rest of the zoo. You grab a snack at almost every booth you saw. After about 2 hours you decide to go home.

You just get some water and you go and get some food. You load you plate full of fried chicken and mashed potatoes. you end up eating 5 plates of food. After eating you pay for your food and you go home. You go into your bathroom and you see your fat gut in the mirror. Curious about your weight you step onto the scale, you end up weighting 253 pounds and growing.

    You wake up very late, like at around dinner late. Your stomach is begging for food like it was for the past few days. You go to your local fast food place and you start binging on whatever food you could order. By the time you were done feasting you had a pile of 10 receipts with a total of 83 burgers ordered. You go home, take a shower and go to sleep.

You could hear your bed creaking under your weight. Curious, you go yo your bathroom to see if you can weigh yourself. When you step on the scale you couldn’t even see the number or much else because your gut hung down to your knees. Your stomach keeps growing and the hunger was to hard to ignore. You squeeze your body into your car and drive to the buffet in town.

    You wake up with your fat hanging off of your bed. You manage to stand up and you notice that your a foot taller than you were when you went to sleep. You think that it must be the “wearer will always be mobile” thing that the paper said. Getting through doors was more of a challenge, not only did you have to duck more you also had to push harder to get your gut through.

By the end of the day you hit up the nearest 7 fast food places with your orders getting much larger with each one visited. You finally get home and you are unable to fit inside your house.
