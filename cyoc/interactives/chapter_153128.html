    5 Monsters to-be

    A group a five friends, Quinton, Michelle, Charlie, Sam, and Rosa gathered around in Quinton's living room. They had all been called together by Quinton to play with this new website he had found.

    Quinton, Charlie, Sam, & Rosa where all knew each other from work.

    Michelle was Sam girlfriend of over a year. Michelle was possessive of Sam and would rarely let him go anywhere with out her. Sam was fine with this as he genuinely enjoyed her company and understood why Michelle never wanted to leave his side.

    Charlie had invited Rosa from his workplace in order to get to know her better before he asked her out for real. Rosa thought that Charlie's careful actions were unnecessary as she already found him attractive. But she liked him enough to play along.

    Charlie and the host Quinton had know each other the longest. They had been friends since high school and knew each other's interests.

    Up until today's gathering, Quinton had been coy when asked questions about what the website he had found actually did. But, he assured his friends that they would have a fun time playing with it.

    Quinton pulled up the 'MonsterMe.com' site on his smartphone. He showed the rest of the group the picture of himself he had given to the website's algorithm.

    "This website is so cool," Quinton explained, "It can transform pictures of yourself into cartoon monsters like you would find in Monster's Inc. Watch this."

    Quinton pressed the preview button next to the picture. His body in the picture twisted and morphed into a new, monster form. The picture of monster-Quinton grew taller and had dry, teal-collored skin with a bright, yellow underbelly. His feet had changed into hooves. His hands had lost a finger and gained an extra digit. His neck had grow longer and changed into an arm-like appendage with an elbow joint. The hair on his head was longer and bright red. His face had morphed as his mouth had changed into an alligator's jaw with large, human lips affixed to the end. Two, muscular tails grew out from his tailbone. Each tail was 5-feet-long and half-a-foot wide at its base and was capped with a large eye.

    "Hey, that's really cool!" Rosa exclaimed.

    "But that's not all it can do." Quinto cut her off. "Check this out." He pressed his finger on the MonsterizeMe button.

    Quinton real body began to morph into the body that was displayed on his phone. He grew taller as new vertebra where magically added to his spine. His feet turned into hooves and his pinky fingers disappeared into his hands. His neck grew longer and the bones in his neck became copies of the bones in his arms. His hair became red as his mouth morphed into a long maw with his human mouth on its tip. The skin on his arms, legs, and face shifted to an unnatural shade of teal as his belly, neck, and chin changed to a putrid shade of yellow fit for a monster. Two, thick appendages grew from his back and twisted around each other as they became his new, ocular, twin tails.

    His transformation completed, Quinton turned his body to show off to his friends. His friends were accepting and curious of Quinton's monster body. They asked several questions about his new body as he shoved the eyes on the ends on his tails into the faces. All four friends wanted to be turned into monsters too.
