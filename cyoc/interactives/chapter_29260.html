Before it would let the changes to occur though, it asked for her to input a name for the new boy that was about to grow older. Anna thought for a moment before typing in the name Edmund. The name Anna recalled had been her grandfather's name who had died in Vietnam, and Anna recalled of how her mother said that if she had ever had a boy, she would have named him after her father. Anna watched the screen then as the representation of Sarah's belly ballooned out to seven months pregnant. Motherly instincts taking over, Anna cooed at her two young children before finding a crib next to the computer. She put both of her babies down to nap, and placed bottles within their reach.
