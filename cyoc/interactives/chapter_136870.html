    Now Mary had noticed that Bank Wow's drummer

    Rango
    who was also the bands money man
    hadn't given her the customary wad of 20s
    so that she could go down to the corner and get the guys their weekend supply of cocaine and cannabis.

    Instead Rango had spent the last three hours looking about the living room for something
    and Mary
    had that Eureka moment

    Rango was looking for the bottle of dragon candies.

    Even before he confirmed it,Mary realized that
    of course
    the band had sent over the bottle of candy from one of the world cities they'd played in
    as a gift to either Jenny or the Smith family in general.

    So Mary fished the packaging from the kitchen wastebasket and asked Rango if he was looking for this and the bottle of candies.

    Bottle of candies asked Rango a confused look in his eyes

    Then the horror dawned in his eyes and he told her they needed to find that bottle immediately.

    So the drummer and Mary rushed upstairs and finally found the empty bottle lying on the Study's rug where Hubert had dropped it after he'd eaten the final five dragon candies.

    Rango picked up the empty bottle and he and Mary rushed to the nearby bathroom where obese Hubert was bent over the toilet
    trying unsuccessfully to empty his heaving insides of the contents of that bottle.

    Rango and Mary led Hubert back to the Study's sofa where after a quite literally green faced Hubert settled himself down
    Rango explained that in Dhubai,Arabian Sands
    the band had found a little known Arabian wheeler and dealer in
    dragoness djinn summoning tablets.

    Hubert had just consumed a whole bottle of summoning tablets meant to summon
    a dragoness shaped wish granting genie named
    Ssliver Al O'Donnel

    They definitely were not meant for human consumption and certainlty
    were not candies.

    Rango explained,as he peeled away the outer white label on the empty bottle that
    there was a prohibition on exporting the country's genii

    Rango showed the sickly green Hubert and the horrified Mary
    the real label that lay underneath the false white label he had put over it to fool
    the Arabian Export and Border Customs Control Police
    of Arabian Sands.
