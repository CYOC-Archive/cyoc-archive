That made sense sadly since her boyfriend had likely been the one to infect her and he was now turning into a Zebra stallion, so of course she was a Zebra mare.

    She sighed a little and got up, she looked in her closet briefly and shrugged a little and shut the door again. What did it matter if people were going to see her naked? She wasn't going to care much longer anyway if she even cared now so she didn't bother to get dressed as she opened the door and headed down to the living room where her friends were already waiting.

    Heather was already in stage 4 with the fur of a spotted Appaloosa mare. She was stretched out over the couch naked and gave Sarah a smile when she came in.

    Katie was an odd one out of the group, she was only in stage 3 like Sarah was but she had a pair of wings starting to grow out of her back. She was apparently a Pegasus given that she was one of the rare mythical strain infected. She was trying to get used to her new wings, though they didn't seem to do anything yet, still.

    Jamie was her last roommate, she was still in stage 2 and was down to just her bra and thong. She gave them all a nervous smile and blushed a little when Sarah entered naked.

    "Look at that, we've got a sexy Zebra babe," Heather said with a laugh as she looked at Sarah and let out a whistle. "You're looking fine girl, though I keep telling you, spots are in right now not stripes."

    "Do you have to go naked..." Jamie said softly as she looked at Sarah who took a seat. "I mean, I know you can't help it but..."

    "Come on girl, being naked is liberating!" Heather giggled as she shook her head. "Besides, we're not really human anymore are we? Only humans wear clothes, so what's the point of being dressed? You don't see horses wearing clothes do you?"

    "It still feels weird," Jamie said with a shake of her head. "And I still haven't even entered stage 3 yet, I don't even know what I'm going to be right now."

    Sarah frowned a little, Jamie had been a rare case of being forced to be infected. In stage 5 infected people would do everything they could to infect people, including some less than good ways. She had apparently been the victim of one who had snuck the virus into a punch bowl at a party she had been at and no one knew how many had been infected.

    Given that everyone in stage 5 at the time was fully transformed by now, they'd probably never know who had done it.

    "Come on, we're stuck in this town with a bunch of transforming people, lets have some fun," Heather said with a smile as she sat up. "Besides, I'll bet a certain stud would love to see what's happened to his future mate."

    Sarah blushed deeply and shook her head trying not to think about that. Though she didn't mind the idea of being mounted by that big handsome stallion, right now she was not in any position to do so. She blushed a little as there was a knock at the door and got up to answer it.
