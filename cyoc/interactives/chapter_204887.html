    The group finally arrived at Spark's family home, Spark wasn't impressed by it as she was well familiar with it, but her friends were very impressed by it. Out of all the other houses on their street that looked modern, theirs looked the most historic with vines, a flower bed and a mural on the wall out front of their family.

    "Wow Spark, your home is amazing." Connor complimented smiling at it.

    "Oh, thanks...it's...it's nice I guess." Spark said blushing a little bit.

    "That's a lovely mural of your family, who did that?" Miranda asked.

    "That would be Cosmo, my youngest, he's always been the artist of the family. He painted that as a going away gift for Spark before she left to go live with the Moreland family saying that even if she's away from us, some part of her will always still be here." Nova said as Spark went up to the wall and put her hand on it, still remembering the day she had left.

    Spark had said goodbye to everyone and given them all a hug, including Star, she had felt a little nervous that day, but her family had been encouraging in her decision. The group made their way inside where they could hear violin music being played and came into the living room to see one light blue fairy boy playing the violin while a dark yellow fairy boy was painting on an easel. They looked to see the group arrive in and were overjoyed to see Spark step through the door.

    "Spark!" Both of the boy fairies cheered joyously when they saw their sister and went to giver her a hug.

    "You're back!" Zee said with a smile. "You look younger than me now." Cosmo said as Spark chuckled.

    "I know, I know, I look younger now. But it's so great to see my little brothers again, even if you do look older than me now." Spark said as she let go from them and pulled Connor up front beside her.

    "Zephyr, Cosmo, this is Connor Moreland, my boyfriend." Spark introduced.

    "Oh, so you're Spark's betrothed. You don't look too bad." Zephyr said.

    "Yeah, I'm sure you and Spark can have at least seven kids altogether for a nice family painting." Cosmo said.

    "Cosmo!" Spark said to her brother as she and Connor blushed at the mention of kids.

    "Anyway, these must be your other friends. Any of you humans free? Maybe you like music?" Zephyr asked looking at the rest of the group.

    "Uh yeah no, I'm a damphir, and I'm already taken." Nat said holding Rei's hand.

    "Yeah and I'm not interested in guys." Miles said.

    "I'm....uh....not interested either....I have a....certain type." Miranda said. Nat and Rei looked at Miranda for a moment.

    "Miranda, don't be shy, are you.....lesbian? bi-curious, maybe?" Rei asked.

    "No! I'm just....into people....a little....older than me. But really I'd say the gender doesn't really matter to me." Miranda said.

    "Oh....! Miranda, you're....a Pan!" Rei said excitedly.

    "What? like Peter Pan?" Miranda asked.

    "No, you're Pansexual, oh my god, oh my god, we're so proud of you." Nat said hugging her friend.

    "Uh, thanks, I think." Miranda said.

    "Right, so girls, these are my youngest ones. Zephyr, or Zee is our family musician, he's a big fan of classical music. I showed him that movie, 'Amadeus', and he just couldn't get enough of it. Then of course there's Cosmo, he's painted for almost everyone in the kingdom. He even got to paint Queen Glitter." Nova said showing them a picture of their queen. The group was enthralled by her beauty, Miranda seemed to almost stare into her painted eyes as if hypnotized.

    "Wow, she looks kind of like that actress from those older films my mom likes. Uh...Lauren Bacall, I think." Nat commented. Miranda took her gaze away from the painting as she looked around the house.

    "So Spark, I've been meaning to ask you...where's your dad? Is he joining us?" Miranda asked. The group of fairies grew a little quiet for a moment.

    "I don't think he'll join us for dinner." Nova sighed.

    "Oh my god, I'm so sorry, is he dead?" Miranda asked covering her mouth.

    "What?! Oh no, dear. He's just part of army of the queen and is usually busy." Nova said giving the humans a sigh of relief.

    "But he did stop by earlier at Midna's shop to say he'd be home to see Spark and her betrothed." Zephyr said.

    They all then heard the door open and saw a crimson fairy come into the door.

    "Daddy!" Spark said happily as she flew over to hug her father.

    "There's my Spa'kalth. I was so happy to hear you were coming home." Spark's father said.

    "Arion, glad you could make it dear." Nova said to her husband.

    "I wouldn't miss seeing our little girl." Arion said.

    "Ahem, Daddy, there's someone I'd like you to meet: this is Connor Moreland, my boyfriend...and my betrothed as mom puts it." Spark said as she went to Connor and pulled him up to her father holding his hand.
