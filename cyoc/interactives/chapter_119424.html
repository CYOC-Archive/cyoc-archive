    Mr. White hated mythics.

    He was a history teacher, who often worked- no, slaved away at teaching ungrateful kids and grading tests. When he first started he tried to be that fun nice teacher that everyone would like, but he quickly fell apart after a few years. Five years of doing this and he had yet to truly enjoy it.
    Part of that problem was a mythic by the name of Dawn. He had been one of her main targets for her constant pranks targeted at teachers. Just yesterday, without him ever noticing it until today, she had somehow glued together all of his test forms.
    Worst of all, since she was a mythic, he could never be too harsh with his punishments. The first time he had tried, the PTA had accused of racism and he was forced to withdraw lest he lose his position.
    And so Adam White the lowly history teacher was stuck. Unable to leave his job and unable to sufficiently punish the torture happening to him.
    So when he had been walking to the teacher's lounge to see if he could salvage the tests and passed a thick book just lying there, his head was filled with rage. A book about mythics. As if they could taunt him even more.
    He grabbed the book and began to flip through it before pausing on a beautiful image of a young woman with strange wings. He reached out and touched the image.
    The book began to glow.
    Mr. White withdrew his hand quickly but the glow had just spread to his hand and was already crawling up his arm. In a panic, he waved his arms as the light slowly but surely enveloped his entire body. Just before the light covered his ears, he could've sworn another mythic tell something out.
    He felt his hair frowning longer and his chest and ass extending outward. His face tingled as it was rearranged. Next, he felt fur springing up on his arms and legs, his fingers and toes suddenly feeling more sharp. As he lost all feeling in his groin, Adam felt something growing out of his back like wings, along some sort of bulb growing out of a point just above his butt. At that point he passed out.
