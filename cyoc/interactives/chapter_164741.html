    Where is Jeff's Chronivac?

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Unnamed 3 years ago O

    In her sleep, Jenny may have found why the Chronivac had disappeared. Jeff had received the thing at home, but because in the reality she created with said Chronivac he hadn't been home, there had been nobody to install and use it. It's even possible that there was no one to reception it when it arrived.

    Waking up, it didn't take her long to notice that Jeff wasn't in the bed with her. Looking around, she saw Lorelei reading some file, a files-cabinet she never saw before near her. "Doctor?!"

    "Oh, hello princess. Did you sleep well?"

    Jenny used her link with Jeff to try to find him, and noticed it pointed her to Lorelei. "Oh, right. My doctor is Jeff's other self, now" she remembered. "What are you reading?"

    "The king's medical files. It is particularly bulky with how long he lived." The blond succubus closed the file in her hands, put it down and looked at the teenage one. "How do you feel?"

    "Still digesting yesterday's event."

    "Understandable. Do you need me to turn back into my main-self?"

    "No no, I… I'll just go take a walk, see if I can find Alice…"

    "As you wish princess. If you ever change your mind you know how to find me."

    "Lor, —Can I call you Lor?— I want to know why you are so formal with me. I mean, I could understand it before you became Jeff's other self, when you were just my doctor. But now that you're a part of him, you're just as married to me as he is. So why?"

    "I was born and raised here in hell, raised by parents that drilled in me to always call our royalty by their titles and act with great deference toward them. It was instilled so profoundly I probably never will be able to speak like a lover should, sorry."

    "S'not your fault." And with that, Jenny exited the room.
    — – - – —
    As soon as she was out, the teen princess was assaulted by her angel friend. "JENNY!!!" Before she could understand what was happening, the young succubus was in a life-crushing hug. "I heard about what transpired yesterday after my departure. Are you okay?"

    "I… Honestly, I don't know. I'm still digesting everything."

    "Yeah, your mother said it was probably wrong for your dad and her to shelter you from how much royals tended to gain other selves."

    "Wait, WHAT?!?"

    Alice simply nodded. "Since she became hell's queen, your mom gained an impressive collection of other selves, and your father as so much of them that even he only remember a tiny part of them. And the only reason you don't have at least a couple yourself is because they thought it would be better to wait until you reached majority before letting you gain any."

    "I'm not sure I would have taken it any better…"

    "…We'll never know Jenny, never."
