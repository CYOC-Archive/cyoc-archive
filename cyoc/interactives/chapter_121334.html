    "Well... I'm not sure exactly how this works but hear me out..." You say nervously.

    The woman smiles compassionately at you, giving a soft giggle before saying "Of course Master, tell me your deepest desires and if possible I will do my best to make them reality."

    "Okay then... Genie, are you familiar with MMORPGs? " You ask, looking up at the beautiful floating woman.

    "Of course my Master, I pride myself on knowing everything there is to know about humans. I am familiar with the term." She says, a hint of curiosity in her eyes as she wonders what your wish will be.

    "Well I was wondering if I could wish to become my main character on the MMORPG called Planebreak. With all of his powers and abilities." You ask.

    Planebreak is one of the most popular MMORPGs out there in existence with incredibly customizable and in-depth character creation and gameplay. Pretty much no two players were alike with over twenty races and a unique skill system where instead of classes you got to fully customize your character 's abilities head to toe. Making the possibilities endless.

    You've heard of several characters such as a Minotaur Demon Druid to a Werewolf Barbarian Angel, each with their own unique strengths.

    You spent most of your time playing Planebreak to the point where your main character was in the top hundred player list.

    "Hmm... an interesting wish indeed Master. I do know my fair share of knowledge concerning the Planebreak game so I will be able to grant your wish. However if your character is high level I would have to use all three of your wishes at once in order to properly execute the wish. I'm afraid casting such a wish would also cause my existence to end, though after millennia of staying in that lamp, a final rest doesn't sound so bad." The genie says, a hint of sadness in her voice at the last part.

    "Really? You don't mind at all?" You ask. You knew that your wish might be pushing it but you didn't expect it to kill her as well. Suddenly you weren't so sure about this. The genie sensed your hesitation and immedialty responded to it in a almost mother like fashion.
    "Oh please my Master, it would be an honor to make your wish my final act. Besides, my essence would only spur on the creation of another genie so in a way I would be reborn. So please Master, do not feel guilt over my death." She says soothingly, gently moving to caress your face with her smooth feminine hands.

    "So tell me my master, what is your character, describe them to me so I can ensure I cast your wish properly." She says.

    You nod and open your mouth to describe
    your character.
