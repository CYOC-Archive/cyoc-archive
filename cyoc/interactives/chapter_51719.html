    Looking down, he gasped in amazement as steam began to spew forth from his body. Confused, he watched it as one of the jocks called out, "This is too weird for me."

    Looking up, Dwight watched as the jocks slowly backed up and then as the captain nodded his head and commented, "Yeah. Definitely too weird," the trio turned and took off down the street, not saying a word or even looking back.

    Had they looked back, they would have seen the steam pouring forth from Dwight was gathering in intensity until finally it just suddenly stopped. Curious, Dwight looked down, unsure of what just happened and then even more confused, asking himself, "How can steam suddenly exit from a person's body," he shrugged and the shifting his backpack, he continued home.
    ------

    That night, Dwight tossed back and forth, his sleep troubled by dreams of a shadow creature giving chase, but he was powerless to turn and actually fight it.

    As he shifted about in his bed, slowly more steam began to exit his body until his room resembled a sauna, only without the heat. As the steam built in density, Dwight continued to twitch back and forth until he started to first glow and then to shrink.

    Shrinking down to a height of three foot three, Dwight twisted himself straight out of his pyjamas, which was lucky for him as everything from the waist down began to shift in proportion until Dwight looked like a miniature centaur, only with more human qualities and nothing horse.

    Groaning in his sleep, Dwight shifted so that his new backside was in a better position as he was now lying on his side. Making a moaning noise in his sleep, Dwight would have been shocked if he could see what happened next as his penis and testicles shrank down, turned a bright pink and folded back up into his body, while red and white fur sprouted all over his body.

    Within moments, Dwight's transformation was complete and were once Dwight rested a female fox-taur now lay. Shifting, Dwight was suddenly awake, but unaware that he had changed at all - almost as if she was sleep-walking, but with full consciousness.

    Looking about the room, Dwight moved easily across the room and then opening the window, and accidentally ripping it out of the frame, she climbed out onto the roof. Scanning the suburban neighbourhood, she thought, "More crime exists in the downtown area. And where there is crime, I shall find my nemesis, Charger," and then leaping down, she took off across the lawn and out into the night.
    ------

    Dwight slowly came to, a cool breeze blowing across his naked body. Blinking, he stared blankly at the window, which was all in one piece, but clearly removed from the frame.

    Shifting, Dwight stood and yawning, running a hand across his human skin as he scratched a couple areas, he thought, "To many comic books. Now I am starting to actually dream about a fox-taur named the Dodge Avenger." and crossing the room, he tried to pick up the window and fit it back into the frame, all before his step-father woke and seeing the mess, blew a gasket.
