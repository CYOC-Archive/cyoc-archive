    Just at that moment Jake's friend Justin walked in. Justin was about 5 foot 4 inches tall, and he had black hair and he was skinny to the bone.

    "Sorry man, I was looking for Jake" he said, not recognizing Jake of course.

    "It's me, dude!" Jake replied, now in Mark's body. "I changed into Mark's body..."

    "What?" Justin gasped, as he walked closer to him. "You must be joking!"

    "No, dude, it's me! That medallion over there is magic. As soon as I touched it, I began to change!" Mark explained to his friend.

    Justin turned and stared at the medallion at the table.

    "Gosh, I would love to change..." he thought to himself. "I would love to be tall and muscular like him..."

    "Give me that!" Justin shouted, as he grabbed the medallion and stared at it intently.

    "NO!" Mark shouted. "Don't touch that thing! You don't know what's going to happen to you!"





    "I wanna grow..." Justin said to him. "I wanna get big..."

    "Justin! You have no idea what that medallion will do to you!" Mark said to him.

    "If it can change you, then it will also change me!" Justin growled in reply.

    "Yes, but it could make you older, or younger... You never know!"

    "He's just jealous..." Justin thought to himself. "He wants to keep the medallion to himself..."

    "I'm taking the medallion with me... It's mine now!" Justin shouted, before he grabbed the medallion and ran out the door towards the beach.

    "Justin!" Mark shouted. But it was too late. He was already long gone.

    In the meantime, Justin continued to run towards the water, when he was suddenly overcome by an odd tingling feeling in his legs.

    "What's going on?" he wondered. "I'm feeling kinda weird..."

    Justin suddenly became very dizzy, and stumbled to the ground. There, he was overcome by the strangest feelings in his body, especially in his muscles! It started in his chest. His flat chest started to balloon at a rapid pace. His pectorals slowly filled out, like they were being pushed out by some invisble force. They grew harder and bigger, turning into solid spheres of muscle!

    "Ooooh man.... What... What's going on? I'm changing?????" Justin gasped, as he looked down at his expanding chest.





    Next, the surface of his flat stomach changed dramatically, as rows of abdominals surfaced, quickly forming a six-pack of muscles like tiles on the kitchen floor.

    Following that, his hands and arms started to grow thicker and longer. His arms became thick pythons of muscular steel, with biceps and triceps forming on them, making him seem very strong and muscular as well. As his biceps increased, he felt an incredible surge of strength in his arms. Immediately he had the urge to flex, and he was very pleased when he realized the size of his new guns.

    "Wow... Are these my biceps? On my arms?" he chuckled. "All my life I've been skinny... I have never imagined being so muscular! This is great..."

    Finally, Justin's quads widened and grew thick layers of muscle, while they were covered with veins. Lastly, his calves inflated until they were as thick and as round as soccer balls, giving his leg an enormous boost of power and strength, so that he had the endurance of a runner!

    The dizzy feelings finally went away, and Justin slowly got up. He stumbled over to the water, since he had to get used to his new weight. Not only that, but he also felt like he had gained a few inches in height. When he reached the water and looked down, he saw his reflection and realized that he was taller than before!

    "Holy crap!" he thought to himself. "I look like I've become 22 or 23 years old for sure!"

    Justin then stared at the medallion and grinned.

    "I should leave the medallion at someone's bag, or perhaps place it at the door of someone's room or something..." he whispered to himself.

    He looked around and saw several hotel rooms where people were staying. There were families where couples were staying with children, but there were also several singles hanging around. Then he looked around the beach, and he saw numerous lounge chairs and umbrellas set up everywhere. There were many families that were enjoying the beach, but there were also several couples and many singles, who were wandering around, looking to meet new friends. As they were walking around, they left behind their bags with their chairs.

    "Let me find some room or some bag to drop the medallion in..." Justin thought to himself. "That person who finds it and touches it, will be in for a surprise..."

    Where would he leave the medallion?
