    Harry and Becky: Preschool again

    Jeff decided he wanted to examine the behavior of Harry and Rebecca; to see if being promoted to high school was going well for them. He would see both of them in first period and would observe them there. If they seemed to be thriving Jeff would leave it alone, if not that's what the Chronivac and Mrs. Perris's pre-school class is for. After dropping of his charges at their respective classrooms Jeff hurried to first period.
    "Shut up Becky" scoffed Harry "I don't care about your stupid gossip. It has nothing to do with me".
    "You don't have to be such a jerk about" replied Becky "You got into a fight with that other boy and people are talking, I just tried to divert their attention by making something up is all".
    "You are ruining both of our reputations" replied Harry.
    Jeff was taken aback by all of this. Fighting and gossiping? Clearly high school was not the best environment for Becky and Harry. Back to pre-school for both of them than. Jeff got out the Chronivac and put in the information.

    Jeff pressed start on the machine and watched the results on the freshman. The teenagers quickly shrunk into their clothes with their clothes quickly changing to suit their new stature and status. Within a few seconds Harry is clad in a baby blue t-shirt with a cute little dinosaur on the front. His jeans transformed into a brown corduroy pants. Harry's hair changed too, going from a somewhat stylish hairstyle to a bowl cut. Becky's transformation was more dramatic. Her jeans shorts expanded into purple overalls with a white shirt. Her hair, previously cut short, grew and changed into a pigtails. The two ex-teens looked around confused before their eyes glazed over, losing their knowledge and their memories changing to fit their new age. Harry and Becky were once again preschoolers.
    "Hey what are you little guys doing here", the teacher Mr. Fuller exclaimed at the new four year old's. "Hey Mr. Fuller I know these two I can take them back to Mrs. Perris if you want" said Jeff. "Thank you Jeff that would be great" Mr. Fuller preceded to give a pass to Jeff to do just that. "Hey kids let's get you to pre-school" Jeff told the new preschoolers. "Kay" lisped Becky. Harry was quite but like Becky ultimately took Jeff's hand.
    Jeff took his reduced charges back to the preschool section of school, explained the situation to Mrs. Perris and let Becky and Harry into the pre-school class. As he was leaving Jeff glanced back at the now young children and was happy to note that they were now playing happily. Satisfied with his decision Jeff went back to thinking about what he should do next with the Chronivac.
