    Ty's Power

    A Family of Conjoined Siblings

    Previous chapter

    added 7 years ago BM O

    Once Ty finished his wish, he could feel his power flowing the air of the house. It felt as though some other-worldy or divine power was affecting the house and its inhabitants. Even Hunter, the family dog, seemed to be affected by the magic. Ty definitely felt something happening to him as well, because his left-hand side felt a little strange. Ty closed his eyes at that point--he wanted the results to be a surprise. As he watched the darkness of his closed eyelids, Ty could hear Hunter, and something was clearly happening. After a while, Ty heard the voice of not one dog, but three. At that same time, Ty felt his left side growing wider, although his head remained in place. It also felt as though something was growing out of his left shoulder, right where his body had grown wider. Soon after this, the feelings in his body, along with Hunter's squealing, disappeared as though none of them had even happened. Still, Ty knew that something was different--when he opened his eyes, there was definitely something different.

    The first thing Ty say was Hunter, especially his new appearance. Hunter was no longer an ordinary dog: he now had three heads! Each head appeared identical, each with identical black collars. The family dog now appeared to be the three-headed dog "Fluffy" from the first Harry Potter movie. Despite his new appearance, Hunter seemed completely unaware of any change. As far as Hunter was concerned--if he even was--he had always been a three-headed dog, and Ty had always been...

    Ty! Ty suddenly realized that his wish would have affected him as well, and what if that was what those growing feeling were? Ty immediately turned his head to his left and saw...his head! Ty's body had become one shared by two conjoined identical siblings! There were still two arms and two legs, but there were now two heads on the widened torso: Ky on their left-hand side, and Ty on their right-hand side.

    "That was amazing, wasn't it?" Ky said.

    "You know about it?" Ty asked.

    "Of course. You made it so that only you, Dustin, and our conjoined siblings would be the only ones to know about this."

    "Oh, yeah," Ty said.

    "Ty! Ky! Come here!" called three female voices in succession.

    "That sounds like June and her conjoined siblings," Ky said.

    "Do you think Mark has a conjoined brother now?" Ty asked. "I mean, do you think he has one new brother, or two, or..."

    "Well, we won't find out just sitting here," Ky answered.

    "You're right," Ty said. "If we're going to be a conjoined family, we'll have to see our new family members."

    With that, Ty and Ky lifted up their shared body and walked to the door, showing no signs of falling or stumbling. As they walked down the hallway to the stairs, it was if they had been conjoined twins for their entire lives--granted, Ky had not even existed until very recently, but it seemed as though he had existed with Ty ever since birth.

    As they began walking down the stairs, they thought over what they would find: Dustin and Justin were in the foyer, June and her two conjoined sisters were in the kitchen, and Mark was in the library with his conjoined brother(s). Who would they see first?
