    Jeff smiled as he watched Jenny examine her body and surroundings. Her eyes were bright and curious, innocent and sweet. There was no indication she had ever been his girlfriend, she was far too young physically, and even mentally she seemed to have adjusted in a way. He ruffled her hair. "Lets make a couple more changes kiddo."

    "Yay! Whatcha gonna do?" She asked, hopping up and down excitedly while Jeff brought up her information. "Its top secret, so no peeking!" He said.

    The girl giggled and turned around, covering her hands over her eyes as Jeff made some alterations.

    Age: 10
    Species: Werewolf
    Gender: Female
    Height: 4'5"
    Hair Colour/Style: Red head/long
    Eye Colour: Green
    Weight: 70.5
    Build: Slender
    Relationship Status: Kid crush on Jeff Hansen, her babysitter
    Social Status: Popular
    Sexual Preference: Straight
    Bust Size: None
    Occupation: Fourth grader, soccer player, kid.
    Education: Elementary school student
    Reality Change: Yes
    Subject Awareness: No
    Lycanthropy: Subject changes in subtle ways with emotions, or at will, not just at the full moon. Will have transformed before in private, but has kept it a secret until its revealed to Jeff, who she will trust with her secret.

    He clicked confirm and turned just in time to see Jenny's ears twitch and shift, red fur growing over them and they tapered to points and swiveled atop her head like little dog ears. He closed and locked the program so she couldnt alter or know about the changes and played a knowing smile.

    "Jenny?" Those ears twitched as she uncovered her eyes and turned. Jeff felt his breath get taken away as he stared into bright amber orbs staring back at him. Her nose had darkened slightly, her eyebrows were thicker and small fangs poked from darker lips. "Yeah?"

    "Your ears...Jenny!" He feigned suprise.

    The girl blinked again, reached up to feel them and gasped, trotting over to a mirror. "oh noooo!" She whined, a sudden rush of emotions bringing more fur along her innocent features as her nose and mouth began to push out. "No no, not now.." She desperately tried to pull her hoodie over her head and tuck herself down, even as nails pushed out into claws and fur grew on the back of her hands. Jeff padded over and knelt down beside her. "Jenny?" What's going on?"

    The girl shook her head, tucking her knees up against her coltish body, fur peeked out from everywhere as the fronts of her sneakers creaked open as claws grew from her pawlike feet. "I...Im a werewolf...a real one..." She whimpered. Turning bright, tear filled eyes up at him. "Please...dont hate me, Im not a monster!"

    Jeff smiled sympathetically and reached to wipe away a tear from that snout. He gingerly lifted her under her arms onto the bed and unlaced her shoes, letting them drop and freeing her paws. "I don't see a monster, I just see Jenny." He said.

    The kid sniffed and wrapped her arms tigjtly around his neck, smiling as a tail pushed out behind her, waggling emphatically. "I...I knew it! I knew you would be the only one who understood!"

    Jeff patted her back, chuckling to himself. Why not let her enjoy this for a while, just be a kid albeit, a werewolf kid? She was too young to be his girlfriend for now, and he wasn't some creep who wanted anything out of a kid other than to see them happy and grow up safe. but maybe he could join her.
