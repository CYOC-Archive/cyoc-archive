Kai was thankful that Jayla was with her at least, not that she would or could be of much help in this house or world. One of the servants announced their arrival.

    “Master, Lady Annabelle Dickson has arrived.” He spoke.

    Kai once again cringed at the use of their female name but quickly forgot when they saw the man of the house. Damian Preston descended the grand staircase and Kai could barely believe it was the same man. He was much taller, around 6’11” with golden blonde hair, an impeccably groomed mustache and an impossibly sharp jawline, he was nothing like the scrawny creep they knew before. He wore an old fashioned three piece suit but none of his new appearance or impressive status could hide the same perverted glare Kai knew all too well. Even with Kai’s painfully modest clothing they still wished they could cover up more to hide from his lust filled gaze.

    “Do not worry m’lady, I’m sure a cold may be too much for a delicate woman, but my grandfather was a giant you know. It will take more than a little cold to affect myself.” Damien said.
