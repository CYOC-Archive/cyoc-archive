"Youz's mad?" he spat. "It'z a dragon! It's-" "SILENCE!" roared his master. Change him back, now! Or can't you?" The shaman didn't reply, but instead turned to Joe and began incanting. The clearing became eerily silent for a moment, and then Joe burst outwards, scaled skin ripping out of his human flesh, his old body being pulled apart. Christine screamed as she was showered with parts of Joe's human body and a massive dragon loomed over them all.

"You're welcome" replied the hulking Brian, and almost lazily swept his axe out behind him without even looking. The head of one of the guards flew across the clearing and landed in the scrub. And then Joe was roaring and the shaman was screaming at this betrayal. The other guard ran towards Brian and struck at him with his axe; Brian didn't even bother to use his own, but swung his arm around, smashing though the axe's handle and sent the guard flying through the air and into a tree trunk, where he dropped to the ground, motionless. It was then a shriek from Christine gained their attention. The shaman had gotten to her, and was holding her with one hand, around which a black aura had formed. Christine was gasping. "Weapons down now, or she dies" The others stood, frozen, while his grip tightened. "I mean i-" And he slumped to the ground, a dart sticking out of his back. Shira stepped out of the bushes, a dart bow in her hand.

This band, whilst a constant annoyance, is not our target. We need to get further north, however, before we can rest in relative safety. I take it you managed to turn this creature to your side?"
