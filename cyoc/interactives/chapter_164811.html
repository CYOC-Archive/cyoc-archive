    Local Adventure

    added by ALionetti 3 years ago O

    ...And apparently there was something closeby home as well. A sort of a "Local Adventure" so it was called. While Andy was more apprehensive about this due to the risk of Lucy discovering what he was up to and trying to come along or disrupt it entirely, this was much more in his wheelhouse when it came to comfort and the amount of time needed to go somewhere. If this was really as enjoyable as the other "adventure" resort locations, he was just going to try this out. Maybe later he could try out the other, further off Adventures.

    Andy thus started looking into the sorts of adventures that were available on there, and was rather surprised by the sheer variety. He looked over his shoulder to check and see if he was being looked in on, and was pleasantly surprised to see he had some time by himself now. Adjusting his seat and leaning back, Andy started reading...

    First of all of the experiences that were available, there was some weird kind of "relive your high school days through a different experience." Andy did admit he was not entirely fond of how he had experienced high school. He mostly just kept to himself and occasionally hanged out and did some of the student body stuff. The dances and the outings were all a little too crowded and involved too much in the way of gathering for Andy's tastes at the time. Now on the other hand he was feeling more bold and regretted his decisions back then.

    Apparently there were all kinds of "experiences" to explore. Though Andy wondered how they would affect a high school like atmosphere. The advertisement was remarkably less than helpful in that regard. But it did have his interest.

    Next was an experience saying that they would be able to give him a "Freaky Friday" experience in a local resort - wherein everyone who came in would come as one sex and leave as the opposite. Andy did find that kind of curious, wondering how they would handle such a thing. It was not what he was used to when he thought of these sorts of vacations. Cupping his chin, Andy looked at the information about it. Apparently there would be an "adjustment period" lasting about five days where the "full flip" would be complete on the fifth day, then a full week long experience.

    Interesting...very interesting... Seemed kind of kooky, but it definitely had Andy's attention.

    And then Andy saw another prospective vacation. Something called the "Walk A Mile" Resort experience. Very close by, and offered to give people an insight into a different peoples' experiences, culture and race. Seemed like a weird and potentially...iffy thing, but the pictures showing outtakes from it all showed people having fun. Andy grinned at the sights of the people frollicking. But still, there were mostly men there and few women. How odd. He did start to wonder again if this was some sort of mostly gay themed resort thing.

    Well, even if it was, it'd be better than staying here looking over his shoulder and checking to see if Lucy was spying on him again. Andy crossed his arms and made his decision about where he would be going...
