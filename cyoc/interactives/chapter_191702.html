    Caleb was a little disappointed that his dad had asked him to go out with him tonight on a hunt.

    "Usually you like doing these kinds of things." Jacob said as he was checking his ammunition.

    "Yeah, but I....told Gloria I would make up for our 'first date' by going on a date like she wanted." Caleb said as he finished checking all of his equipment.

    Jacob drove up to what was an abandoned pharmacy store. He didn't know why but he felt a little uneasy about this one.

    "Hey, uh...if this gets too hot, we're bailing the moment I say so." Jacob said to Caleb.

    "You sure? You don't usually say that."

    "Yeah, I've just....got a really bad feeling about this place." Jacob said as they made their way to the back. Caleb sent Spark in ahead, and waited a little bit for her to get back.

    "There's about five in there, they were talking to someone, but I couldn't see who." Spark informed them.

    They set a demolition charge on the door as the door blew open and they carefully made their way inside. They could hear voices coming from the front of the pharmacy.

    "So we won't be trying to turn them?" One of the other vampires asked.

    "No, no, as good as that would be for us, they'd both die before they would ever become a vampire." A voice answered that sounded like an older male voice.

    "Then why are the rest of us here? It seems like you'd do fine on your own." A different vampire asked.

    "I would, but I figured a few more extra hands wouldn't hurt. It's good to have some backup. Isn't that right, Mr. Moreland?" The older vampire voice asked aloud.

    "Yes, Mr. Moreland, I'm well away that you and your son are here, so please don't bother with the theatrics, just come out front." The vampire asked as Jacob and Caleb looked confused at each other before Jacob took a sigh and nodded for the two of them to proceed out.

    Jacob and Caleb stood in front of four vampires with an older looking one in the back.

    "I figured you had figured out one of our many hideouts and decided that I'd pay a personal visit to you myself." The vampire said as she stepped forward holding a sword and showing the two of them his eyes were clearly blind.

    "Ken, I presume." Jacob said surprised.

    "Hmm, surprised it sounds. Yes, my reputation must proceed to you as it likely has your son. I'm sure your son is more than capable of battling these other vampires, why not you and myself have a little sword duel? I'm quite interested in your swordsmanship." Ken suggested.

    "Fine, you bastard, I agree. Hand me a sword and I'll show you my skill." Jacob said with a spit towards Ken as he threw him a sword.

    "Dad...." Caleb said trying to argue.

    "Don't argue boy. Just take out those other assholes." Jacob said as Caleb listened to his father and proceeded to shoot at the other vampires who managed to dodge him at first.

    Jacob and Ken began their sword duel, with Jacob managing to hold his own. After a few minutes, Jacob came to realize that Ken was much more skilled in the art of the blades than he was.

    "Got to do things the old fashioned way, why can't you just use guns like the rest of the world?!" Jacob said as he was fighting Ken.

    "There's no art to it. You can kill enemies from a distance, but they'd never see you up close, you don't get a sense of their fighting ability, their strength, or even their mind." Ken replied as he came near Jacob's skin with his sword.

    Meanwhile Caleb was fighting the other vampires with superior ease. He had wondered why exactly his dad had gotten a bad feeling earlier, this 'Ken' vampire didn't seem much of a challenge and the other vampires were almost regular cannon fodder.

    "Why don't you just eat some of these bullets you assholes!" Caleb taunted as the other vampires looked a little frightened.

    "What? Gotten afraid of me now?" Caleb asked as he faced two in front of him with one standing behind him. The two in front of him looked frightened at Caleb it seemed, but Caleb realized that they were more afraid of something behind him.

    Caleb turned to see the third vampire standing there with his heart outside of his chest and then he disintegrated completely as Caleb saw a little girl who looked no more than 8 years old eat the dead vampire's heart like a snack before tossing it aside and ran towards Caleb knocking him down hard in his stomach. Caleb could almost feel a cracked rib as he began to crawl fast as he could away from the girl.

    Caleb managed to stand up with all his might and pain to go over to where his dad was and see that he was struggling against the swordsman, Ken.

    "Dad! It's that girl! Ann, she's here too!" Caleb said he coughed up a little blood then felt a hard push from behind him as he fell on the floor. He also felt his hat being taken off his head.

    Caleb looked up to see his dad get pushed down against a wall and Ken's sword pushed into his shoulder blade making him stick to the wall as the little girl made her way over to Ken whilst tearing up Caleb's hat.

    "Go on and finish me then you bastards." Jacob said as he spat his blood at them.

    "Ann, should we honor Mr. Moreland's request and finish him?" Ken asked Ann who stomped twice for 'No'.

    "No? Then what should we do now?" Ken asked as little Ann made her way over to Jacob and pulled Jacob's right arm off completely with all her might.

    "Dad!" Caleb cried out as he got out tear gas and tossed it towards the three of them. It took Caleb all his remaining strength, but he managed to carry his dad out of the pharmacy from one of their biggest losses in a long time.

    "That was a warning Morelands! Keep meddling in Miro's affairs and it'll be much worse for you both!" Ken shouted to them.

    Ken heard nibbling next to him from Ann.

    "Ann, are you chewing on that Moreland man's severed arm?" Ken asked as Ann stomped once for yes.
