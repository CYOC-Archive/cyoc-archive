    Nobody could explain why, but they just dug each other.

    Which is how Jennifer found herself, on a lazy Saturday afternoon, fighting Nancy's dad in an empty soccer field.

    Jennifer desperately tried to remember some of the spells that Aziza had taught her.

    She manifested a force field around herself to block Savannah's pursuit, but Nancy's dad passed right through it as if it had been nothing.

    She then turned the grass Savannah was standing on into quicksand, but Savannah instantly nullified Jennifer's spell with a snap of her fingers.

    Panicking, Jennifer was about to blast Savannah with a gale of arctic air, but the beautiful woman vanished from sight.

    Jennifer tried her best to not get triggered. This was reminding her a lot of Kylie. Her heart began to pound in her chest.

    Jennifer suddenly felt someone behind her.

    Before she could react, Jennifer felt Savannah strike her in the face, knocking her to the ground.

    "Oww!" Jennifer yelled in a surprising amount of pain, holding the side of her face.

    "Goddess, that fucking hurt. Ms. Tullio, I thought that you were teaching me to be a sorceress, not to be a boxer."

    "If you're going to be the Sorceress Supreme, you need to learn how to fight. For all of their spells and curses, I've never known another sorceress who could take a punch" Savannah said.

    "Fair enough, I guess" Jennifer said as she got back onto her feet.

    "They have all the power in the universe. They could turn the moon into soup, but most of them are sheltered, ditzy idiots. Any sniper could kill most of them. Shit, any random big guy with a wrench could sneak up behind most of them them and hit them over the head."

    Nancy, who had been watching from the bleachers, ran up to Jennifer and hugged her.

    "You did a great job for your first time!"

    Jennifer and Savannah practiced for another two hours, by which point Jennifer was totally exhausted.

    She had no idea just how physically and mentally taxing sorcery actually was, or at least Savannah's version of it.

    Savannah was a really hard taskmaster, way more than Aziza seemed to be.

    No wonder Aziza thought that she was a bitch.

    "Hey" Nancy said, grabbing Jennifer's shoulders in the changing room.

    "How about my Dad and I show you our version of Earth? I told you, it's got nothing but sexy lesbians like us!"

    "Now you're talking" a sweaty Jennifer said with a smile.
