    "Anne, what do you have in your hand?" she asked.

    "What? Oh, nothing," Anne said nervously.

    "Oh really?"

    "Now Anne - show the class what you've got, " Ms. Flores demanded.

    "Show the class. Now, " Ms. Flores said with a hint of anger.

    Anne reached into her left pocket and pulled out the tiny teenage girl.

    "What is that? A doll?" mocked one student named Jane.

    "Haha, Anne plays with dolls!" mocked another named Kim.

    The classroom burst into laughter as Anne hid her face in shame.

    "It's not a doll! It's a shrunken girl!" yelled Anne furiously.

    The class was silent for a moment. Then the laughter erupted once again.

    "Have a seat, Anne," said Ms. Flores, pitying the girl. Anne walked with her head down back to her seat. She stopped paying attention to Kelly throughout the rest of the period.

    After the bell rang Anne met up with Megan in the hallway.

    "Hey Anne, how are you enjoying my sister?" asked Megan with a smile.

    Anne didn't answer the question.

    "Hey, come talk to me outside," said Anne.

    "Alright, I guess," responded Megan.

    Once they got outside, they sat under a tree and begin to chat.

    "Hey, Megan. Would you let me borrow your toy gun for a while?" asked Anne.

    "Uh...NO. It's kind of an important trinket. I can't just go letting anyone use it," replied Megan.

    "But ... I need to get revenge on a few girls in my class ... please?"

    "Hmm ... alright, but only until tonight. You can take Kelly with you. Bring her home by 6," said Megan.

    "Oh, thank you Megan!" said Anne.

    Inside Anne's pocket, Kelly popped her head out.

    "Woah! What? Megan, you need to bring me home and return me to normal! NOW!" she yelled.

    "Kelly, Anne is your new big sister for the rest of the day. Behave for her and I might make you normal again," replied Megan.

    Kelly crossed her arms and muttered to herself.

    Anne took Kelly and the Reducer back into school and to her next period. Once school was over Anne decided to enact her plan for revenge.

    "Make fun of me, will you!?" Anne said out loud.

    First, she intended to shrink Kim. Kim was an Asian girl with long black hair in a ponytail. She has been held back three years so she was 5' tall.

    Second, she wanted to show her teacher who was boss. Ms. Jessica Flores was a 22 year old woman who had just recently gotten out of college. She had short brown hair and was drop dead gorgeous. She was 5'8" (with high heels).

Jane was quite pretty for her age, had long brown hair that was kept in a ponytail, and was short for her age at only 4' tall.
