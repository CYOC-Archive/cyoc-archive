    Just as Ty and Dustin were about to walk away, one of the bigger boys had pushed a skinny, geeky kid out of
    the door of the weight room. It was Etan, a short kid with sandy brown hair and blue eyes and glasses.

    "Man... All I wanted to do was lift weights just to gain some muscle! Give me a break!" the boy shouted back
    at the big jocks.

    The kock had pushed him out the door so hard, that his glasses fell off. Etan was looking all over the floor
    for them, and Ty had spotted them at his feet.

    "My glasses! I'm blind without my glasses!"

    "Here you are, Etan..." Ty said, as he picked them up and handed them to the boy. "But, you won't need them
    anymore!"

    Ty quietly wished for Etan to have perfect vision in his eyes from now on.

    "Hey! I can see... Oh! Ty! Dustin!" Etan gasped, as he looked up at the two brothers standing before him.

    "Stand up, Etan!" Ty ordered.

    Etan got up, wondering what Ty was going to do to him.

    "You're going to bulk up right now so that nobody will deny you entrance to the gym ever again! You'll be
    bigger than all those jerks who are in the weight room now!" Ty stated.

    "Say what?" Etan wondered, as he was overcome by an intense tingling sensation in every muscle in his body.

    Suddenly, Etan just shot UP! He GREW bigger and BIGGER! He went from 5 ft 4 inches to almost 7 feet tall,
    towering over Ty and Dustin! But his growth spurt didn't end there!

    Next, his torso grew twice as wide while his limbs exploded with muscle! His arms and his legs filled out with
    mighty calves, hamstrings, glutes, biceps and triceps, all at once! He packed on massive amounts of muscle all
    within seconds!

    Then, his pectorals exploded into large spheres of giant muscle, filling out his huge chest, and his stomach
    now contained giant rows of abdominal muscles. The boy burst out of his shirt and shorts, ripping everything
    to shreads!

    "ARRGGHH!!!" he screamed as his body exploded with POWER!

    Finally, when the growth spurt was over, Ty fixed his ripped clothes and made everything bigger. Etan was
    wearing a large tanktop and a giant pair of shorts, covering his monster quads and his enormous pecs and abs!

    "Ty! My body! I'm a giant! Look at me!" the giant boy gasped.

    "There you go, Etan! You're one of the biggest muscle dudes in school!" Ty grinned. "Now, go work out in the
    gym!"

    Etan turned around and entered the weight room again. This time, the jocks trembled when they recognized him.
    They didn't DARE throw him out again!

    Ty and Dustin just looked on as Etan lifted the heaviest weights in the weight room, while the jocks looked on
    in fear and astonishment...
