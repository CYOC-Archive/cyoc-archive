    After class finished, Olivia was packing up her stuff when she heard Rachel, one of Jeff's friends, ask Jenny, "Hey, have you seen Jeff anywhere today?"

    Shifting uncomfortably, Jenny responded, "Uhh... No, actually. Maybe he slept in or something?"

    Olivia scooched a bit closer. *She's acting way too suspicious, she must be hiding something!* the eavesdropper thought to herself.

    Meanwhile, Rachel sarcastically replied, "Riiiight, the guy always complaining about his mom never letting him sleep in managed to sneak a whole extra hour." Shrugging, she continued, "Eh, I won't worry about it yet, just hope he's not sick." Shouldering her backpack, she asked, "So, wanna walk with me to the next class? Perhaps we'll run into him on the way."

    "Oh, sorry, I would but..." Jenny replied hesitantly, "I gotta head to the restroom first. You go ahead without me."

    Sensing an opportunity to snoop around further, Olivia quickly put on her backpack and left the classroom, hoping to reach the restroom first and hide in one of the stalls. Maybe, just maybe, she might be able to figure out the secret behind Jenny's infuriatingly enlarged chest. As soon as she got there, Olivia locked herself in one of the stalls before stepping onto the toilet and crouching down so that her feet wouldn't show under the door.

    Right on cue, Jenny entered the bathroom, then looked around furtively to make sure nobody else was around. On her end, Olivia leaned forward, peeking through the crack between the walls and door of the stall, seeing the other girl reflected in the mirror.

    "What should we do? It seems like Rachel was looking for you," Jenny said in a hushed voice. There was a pause, then she continued, "I mean probably... That said, I should have thought about the more important detail of your absence rather than how noticeable my boob size would be or not. What if she starts getting suspicious?"

    Olivia's brow furrowed as the pieces started falling in place. And while some might have assumed that Jenny was just talking on a cellphone or something, she could tell that very much wasn't the case. A few moments later, the other girl said something that all but confirmed her theory.

    "Let you out? I'm in the women's room! What if someone saw you walk out? At most I could just return you to the pendant for now."

    That was all she needed to hear. Unlocking the door, Olivia burst out with a cry, "Aha! I knew something was up with you and that pendant!" Continuing her tirade, she pointed accusingly at Jenny's chest, "And those outrageous breasts! Who ever heard of someone growing multiple cup sizes overnight?" Now she was up in the other girl's face, poking one of her tits roughly while saying, "You're responsible for that Jeff boy going missing too, aren't you? I'm going to make sure the whole school knows about it." Sneering haughtily, she finished, "Your life's over, bitch."

    Now, we encounter a classic problem, that of the overly pretentious and popular young adult getting in way over their head. Confronting Jenny head on and announcing her malicious intent, whilst knowing with reasonable certainty that she was able to seemingly erase someone, is not something most would consider to be sound judgment.

    "Wait, I can explain!" Jenny exclaimed, instinctively grabbing Olivia's wrist so that she'd stop prodding her chest.

    "What's there to explain?" the slim girl retorted, "You know as well as I do that I've never cared for you, and now you've made it all too easy for me to show everyone who you truly are."

    In that moment, Jenny had a desperate, stray thought as she contemplated the damage Olivia could cause. *If only there was some way to get rid of her,* she thought. Without a warning, the pendant began to glow, and Olivia instantly knew something was horribly wrong.

    "Let go of me, you freak!" Olivia exclaimed, but it was too late. Within moments, she got sucked into the pendant, leaving not a single trace.

    "Oh no..." Jenny lamented, realizing what she just did. Suddenly, she could hear Olivia's voice in her head.

    *What the hell did you do to me?!* the other girl screamed, practically giving Jenny a headache. *You better let me out right this instant, you conniving bi-* Suddenly the voice cut off as Jenny tried to block her out.

    *Well, she's certainly not happy,* Jeff commented mentally, glad he didn't have to keep listening to her.

    "Yeah, but what do I do now?" his girlfriend asked worriedly. "I can't leave her trapped forever, but I can't let her ruin my life either."

    *One thing at a time,* Jeff calmly advised, *We'll figure it out later, just try not to draw any more attention to yourself for now.*

    "You're right, sorry... So, should I return my breasts to normal for now, then?"

    *Maybe not, I think it wouldn't be a good idea for people to see your bust size fluctuating in the middle of the day,* came her boyfriend's reply. *I'll be fine, don't worry. I actually kinda like the feel of being your breasts, anyway.*
