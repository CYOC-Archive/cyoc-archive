The clown who was running the party introduced herself as Petunia the clown. She started off the party by giving the four boys big red clown noses with them being at the circus and all. The party went on with the group playing with each other. While the boys were playing Petunia went over to Alfie’s parents and told them about this program that doing at the circus as a part of their birthday party program. It’s was called join the act. She explained that as the name suggested they would allow the Alfie, Jack , Sam, and Harry to join the clowns on staged as a part of their performance this was the “special seats” that were apart of the party. As the four boys ate their ways out of the pies they continued laughing Jack throwing some cream at the others. After the food fight the boys all walked over to Petunia and Alfie’s parents, large Cherries still stuck to their heads covered in whipped cream. Petunia brought the four boys over to a large shower where they lined up as they got sprayed by the water, washing all the whipped cream off.
