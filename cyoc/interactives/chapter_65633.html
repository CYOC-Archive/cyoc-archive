    As Jen continued to look for her sock, she heard her pet cat, Sasha. She was a calico with
    yellow eyes, and a pink bell-collor.

    "I don't suppose YOU'VE seen it, have you?" she asked sarcastically. Then, Jen shrank into
    her clothes, and came out as an anthro calico cat-girl half her original height, and she saw
    that Sasha was now her twin in how she looked. The 2 calico girls stared at eachother for a
    few seconds.

    "Is Jen okay?" Sasha asked, reaching for her. "No touch Jen!" Jen screamed, and Sasha
    withdrew her arm.

    "Sasha sorry." Sasha said "Sasha only want know if Jen okay."

    "Jen NOT okay!" Jen said "If Sasha touches Jen, it bad!"

    "Oh, right." Sasha said "Mean-sounding boy say that, right?"

    "Sasha remembers that?" Jen asked. "Of course," she answers "Sasha in room when Kyle
    call, and heard everything." Sasha's ears drooped "Sorry, Jen. Sasha just wanted to see what
    being human like." Then Jen smiled "Jen have idea!" "What?" asked Sasha smiling.

    "Transform or Dare!" Jen said. "Ooh!" Sasha said smiling, feeling she knew the plan "Sasha
    pick both!" "Good!" Jen said, smiling "For transformation, we both become anime-style cat-
    girls - still covered in fur, but we both be Jen's old height, be twice as smart as Jen was,
    have anime eyes, and it not matter if we touch eachother, or anything else - we always be
    cat-girls, and we have magic powers to fight evil, have fun, and other things! And for dare,
    Jen Dares Sasha to help Jen find Kyle, and use new magic to get back at him!"

    When she finished, they both became as Jen described.

    "Wow," Jen said "what a rush!" "I know!" said Sasha "You didn't have to dare me, Jen - I
    wanted to help you get back at Kyle anyway!" "I wasn't thinking clearly," Jen said "but now
    that I am, I realize I dared us to always be cat-girls - what if Mom sees us?" "Then we'll
    make her one too!" Sasha said smiling "We've got magic, remember?" "Oh, right." Jen said,
    somewhat relieved.
