    "What is this...?" he grumbled with a frown.

The diaper escaped his hands and wrapped itself around his waist and crotch. It attached itself with pins. The diaper was old-fashioned too, made of cloth and hugging his privates snugly. And he could not pull it off! And as he was occupied with this devilish diaper, the bonnet wrapped itself on his head! It tied itself below his chin and remained there in spite of his best efforts.
    While he struggled helplessly, he did not notice his body changing. Shrinking. Becoming rounder. Weaker. Hairless. Before he knew what was happening, Chad was lying on his back, now a helpless baby. He wanted to cry but then a shadow loomed over him.
    "Hello, Chad. Enjoying your new youth?" said the voice.
    It was Morgan, a new hot girl that Chad hated because she had refused his advances.
    "Yep, it's me, Morgan. I'm a fairy, actually. I go through schools to punish evil-doers. Jerks like you."
    Chad started protesting but only baby babble came out of his mouth.
    "Don't bother. You're a baby now. And to complete your punishment, I'll take you to the Troll Market, in the fay realm. They love babies and adopting them. So you'll get new parents soon. Hopefully they'll raise you to be a better person. Unless they find you boring and annoying and hypnotise or enchant you to become an actual baby in thought."
    With a snap of her fingers, Morgan tied a pacifier to Chad's mouth, slipped booties on his feet and had a blanket wrapped itself around him, before he was placed in a wicker basket."
    "There!" she clapped. "Adorable! Now off to the Troll Market we go!"
    She poked Chad's belly, who giggled due to his newfound ticklishness and squirmed helplessly. She then picked him up and crossed a portal to the craziest place Chad had ever seen. She gave Chad to a creature putting humans and other monsters on auction and left with a pouch containing for certain valuables, leaving Chad to await his turn. He was too feeble to escape or really move and he wondered with apprehension who was going to bid for him. All of the buyers looked terrifying...
