    Rachel in Wonderland: The 50 Foot Alice

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago S

    "Ow." Rachel said as her head hit the ceiling of her room, causing her to lean forward as she grew larger and larger.
    Soon Rachel's increasing size forced her into bending her knees and finally into sitting down on the floor with her legs stretched out.
    "Okay, I think this is large enough. Can I stop growing now? Please?" Rachel said to her body as she continued to gain height and her legs now long enough for her shoe-covered feet to touch the opposite wall, all while moving the furniture out of their positions with her expanding mass.
    Eventually Rachel's head touched the ceiling again and her knees had to be bend upwards as she found herself continuing to grow, breaking the furniture around her.
    "Ah crap, why didn't I wait and eat the cupcake outside? At this rate I'll destroy the whole house." Rachel said.
    At that moment Rachel heard her mother knocking on her bedroom door, wondering what was going on.
    "Rachel. Are you okay in there? I heard something break." Rachel's mom said.
    "Mom, I'm growing larger. You need to get out of the house." Rachel said, now nearly cramped in her own room.
    "Your what? Rachel, open this door right now." Rachel's mom said.
    Suddenly the floor and walls beneath and around Rachel started to crack from her increasing weight.
    "MOM, THERE'S NO TIME LEFT! YOU NEED TO GET OUT OF HERE, NOW!" Rachel shouted to her mother.
    Rachel's mom saw the cracks forming on her half of the wall and immediately started heading downstairs and running for the front door. At this point the flooring and walls gave way, causing Rachel to brake out of her room and continue growing inside the rest of her house. Rachel's mom makes it outside just in time to see a large black mary jane shoe bursting out of the front door and moves out of the way. Rachel's right leg then burst out of the dining room window, with Rachel's mom standing between both legs, then both her arms punch out holes on the upper half of the house, and finally her head bursts out through the roof. And yet after all that, Rachel found herself still growing larger.
    "ARE YOU KIDDING ME? I'M STILL GROWING?" Rachel shouted.
    Soon cracking noises could be heard throughout the whole house, indicating that it was about to completely break apart. Rachel's mom knew this and began running for cover. Eventually the strain became too much and the entire house broke apart around Rachel's growing body. As the dust settled from the aftermath, Rachel discovered that she had finally stopped growing altogether.
    "Oh thank goodness, I wasn't sure it would actually stop. Alright then, let's see how big I've gotten."
    After gently wiping the debris off her entire person, Rachel slowly moved her legs inwards and lift herself off of what remains of the foundations of her house. Rachel then stands straight up and looks around at her neighborhood that she was now towering over.
    "Wow, everything's so much smaller now. It's like I'm standing in one of those miniture toy landscapes, only this one's real. I must be like 50 feet tall or something. Well, now that I'm basically Alice in Wonderland at this point, I wonder what I should do now."
