    Ethan opens the inanimated file and scrolls through the various objects, “cool, I can become anything,” the boy says, “a gaming console, a toy, clothing and even… a vehicle”. The boy’s thumb hovers over the different inanimated files, finding it surprisingly difficult to decide, “why is it so hard to decide,” he whines.
