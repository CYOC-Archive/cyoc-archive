    The benefits of diapers

    added A year ago AR Toddler

    "Wow I can't believe I'm really a toddler whose back in diapers. This so awesome" Jenny exclaimed with joy as she gripped and tugged on the waistband of her disposable padding. Giving her diapered rear a shake she giggled at the crinkling rustle. "And we're both in Pampers, that's a really good brand."

    "I wouldn't know" Jeff replied but trusting his girlfriend's word. "You mind telling why you're so excited to be back in these things" he gestured to his own diaper for emphasis.

    "Because when I was really a toddler in them the first time around, I was too young to remember much of anything from those early years" she admitted, "And I bet it was the same for you right?"

    Jeff nodded, "Pretty much. All I have is a large photo album full of pictures."

    "Me too" Jenny replied, "Though my mom also shot lots of video of me as a baby and showed it to me. Turns out I was quite the rambunctious 2 year old. I was so full of energy and always on the move. I hardly ever sat still, not even when I was getting my diaper changed."

    "Sounds like you were a handful" Jeff chuckled at learning something new about his girlfriend.

    "So basically you wanted to become a baby to re-experience a time in your life that you couldn't remember?" Jeff pieced together.

    "You got it" she giggled before stick her thumb in her mouth and sucking on it.

    "Still you had to pick your diaper days to go back to of all things?"

    Jenny frowned as she removed her thumb.

    "Okay why do you keep on dissing diapers?" she asked.

    Jeff shrugged his shoulders, "I just don't get what's so great about going to the bathroom in your pants that's all."

    The other toddler looked shocked at the response. "Are you serious? That's like the number one best thing about them. With a diaper we can go potty anywhere and anytime we want, no matter what we're doing. Not only that but we don't even clean up after ourselves, our parents will change us into fresh ones then we can do it all over again. Every single need of ours is taken care of for us, we'll literally be pampered in our Pampers."

    Hearing the strong case Jenny made, Jeff felt his view on the matter shifting.

    "Well when you put it that way..."

    He then shook his head.

    "But this still just feels too weird for me."

    Sensing she found the root of the matter and unwilling to let go when she had her boyfriend on the line Jenny pressed on.

    "I see what the problem is, you're still thinking like a teenager. News flash we're not teenagers any more Jeff, we're babies. Bay-bees. And babies need and use diapers so don't feel ashamed or embarrassed to embrace your inner 2 year old. As for retaining our 16 year old minds, remember that's just so we could be aware of and enjoy all this" Jenny finished by spreading her arms wide with a flourish.

    "Maybe you're right" Jeff said slowly coming around.

    "Of course I'm right" she smirked while placing her hands on her hips. Her expression then turned thoughtful.

    "I just realized there's another cool thing about diapers I forgot to mention."

    "Oh yeah and what's that?" Jeff asked with intrigue.

    "How soft, thick and comfy they are" Jenny grinned as she let herself fall backward, her padded butt landing on the floor with series of crinkles from her Pamper. "See that? That hardly hurt thanks to my diaper. Wearing these things is like having a pillow for your behind" she giggled at the discovery while Jeff grinned a little at her silliness.
