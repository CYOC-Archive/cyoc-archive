    The Magic Shop

    A change in her appearance might help.

    Jennie, still not giving up, questioned Karly again.

    "Okay Karly, so tell me. What do you like the most in the world?" Jennie asked Karly, who seemed somewhat distracted.

    "What? Well, I like to drink." Karly answered him.

    "Apart from that, what else do you like?" Jennie asked, still trying to find out more about Karly.

    "I don't know, what any normal person likes I guess." Karly told him very naively

    "Like what?" Jennie asked with a raised eyebrow.

    "You know: sleep, eat, drink, talk to other people, wear nice clothes, watch cute animals on Youtube. Those kinds of things that one normally likes to do."

    "And what do you usually hate? Is there anything you dislike in particular?" Jennie asked Karly again.

    "Nothing really. At most it would be work, but the thing is, I almost don't work." Karly answered him very calmly

    "And what about your appearance? Is there anything in particular you'd like to change?" Jennie asked Karly one last time.

    "I guess I'd like to be a little rounder, but I think I'm fine with my appearance. By the way, don't you have any alcohol by any chance? I think I could use a drink." Karly asked very casually.

    "Okay Jennie, let's go over everything we know about Karly so far. Although she says she doesn't need anything and is fine with her life, that may not be entirely true. Maybe there is something that she deeply desires." Jennie thought.

    "Let's see, well, she seems to be a very calm and naive girl. And from what she's told me so far, she seems to have a pretty ordinary life. Something I've noticed is that she doesn't seem to like working very much, so I assume she's someone a bit lazy. Not to mention, she likes to drink and suck.......the other thing. But what catches my attention the most is her little obsession with looking pretty. According to her, she would look prettier if she were a little rounder."

    In that instant, Jennie had finally come up with the ideal wish for Karly.

    "Karly Loh, do you wish to change your appearance?" Jennie asked Karly out loud

    "I guess so." Karly responded to Jennie.

    "Then that wish will be granted immediately."

    In that instant, Jennie proceeded to grant Karly her wish and he did so with a single snap.

    After the snap occurred, Karly had been magically transported to a large and strange room which had absolutely nothing.

    Before Karly could question where she was, she began to feel a strange sensation all over her body. Although at first she didn't seem to be affected much, she later began to feel intense pain and dizziness.

    Then she began to notice that the world around her was getting bigger and bigger while she was starting to shrink. But what she didn't know is that she wasn't actually shrinking, but rather she was getting younger and younger.

    Her body was regressing more and more, her hair became shorter and both her clothes and the room were starting to change.

    And after a while, the transformation was over. And now Karly had been reduced to a chubby 1-year-old baby girl who seemed to be wearing a pink onesie and diaper. Not to mention that she also had a pacifier in her mouth, which she seemed to be calmly sucking on. And finally, the entire room had been transformed into a pink nursery.

    After seeing all the changes....
