Remembering the other part of the curse she quickly looked at her self and saw that her clothing had changed accordingly: her hands covered in what appeared to be pink silk glovers and she was wearing a typical fairy tail princess dress just like Kyle had said knowing that that bit was true she also assumed she was wearing some heels and that assumption was proven correct when she got out and heard a loud clack on the stone floor.

Just for fun she tried to remove the crown but that thing was practically glued to her head so she did not bother much. Moving forward was now much easier but hard since the hallway she was in was quite dark and she had no idea just how large this damnable castle was at all.

Not having any other options she pushed the doors opened and was greeted by what looked like the throne room: as there was a large pink chair at the beginning and a carped going all the way down from it ending to another set of double door and lot's of small doors all around them.

"great I still got time to get the heck out of here and get help" was her thought but the moment she tried to step out into the sun she fainted.
