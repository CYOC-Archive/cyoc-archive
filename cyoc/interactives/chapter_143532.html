    Joanna found the many vials curious, but kept looking.

    The next aisle surprised her because it was lined with shoes. Many seemed more like costume props than normal footwear. Ruby slippers, hooves, flippers.

    She continued to the next aisle and full of clothes. Hats, gloves, coats. Some looked almost rubbery. Some of the hats were decorated with birds. She was particularly intrigued by the fur coats and touched several, feeling amazed how soft they felt. She imagined wearing one out for a night on the town - how rich and fashionable she would look in one. Then as she felt the fur more she thought about wearing one with nothing else.

    The old woman at the counter merely watched Joanna with a smile.
