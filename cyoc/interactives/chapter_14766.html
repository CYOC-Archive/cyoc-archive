    Slowly but surely, Ty felt his muscles on his huge, nine-foot body inflate. The calves and the quads on his long, skinny legs began to grow, which gave the legs a more “filled up” look. They were no longer skinny bones, but giant pillars of muscle, strength, and power!

    His calves inflated like soccer balls, until they were huge spheres of muscle, which were adorned with many thick veins, which had the job of supplying the much needed blood to his overgrown body!

    “WHOOOHOOO!!” Ty cheered, as he witnessed his muscles grow, while feeling the rapid increase in strength at the same time.

    The quadriceps inflated to the point where they were as thick as an oak tree, with a web of long, thick veins covering them from top to bottom. Layer upon layer of muscle formed over his legs, forcing Ty to continually adjust his stance by spreading the legs further apart, since they kept on rubbing against each other while they were growing ever thicker and bigger.

    “Ohhh mama!” Ty cheered out loud, while he admired his titanic legs in the mirror!

    Ty stood on his toes and grinned at the sight of his calves, which seemed powerful enough, to allow him to jump over ten feet straight in the air!

    “I can run in the Olympics with these babies….” He chuckled to himself, as he turned sideways before the tall mirror.

    Next, his abdominals and his pectorals began to inflate. He allowed his legs to grow muscular at first, so that he could grow his upper body, without having to worry about his legs not being able to hold up the increase in weight. He understood that muscles were heavy, and he needed his legs to be strong enough to support his upper body before he allowed that part of him to grow massive.

    “Ahhh Tyler, don’t you just LOVE being big?” he grinned, as he watched his twelve pack of abdominals form over his stomach, like the bricks on the side of a wall.

    “Come on, muscles! Grow bigger… BIGGER!” he cheered, as he allowed the rest of his muscles to grow.

    His pectorals grow to the size of two inflatable beach balls, which were hanging side by side. Massive lats gave him the V-shape on his back, while offering the needed support for the large, expanded pectorals, which made up his incredible chest. He started to look like a massive bodybuilder, as the shoulders, arms, triceps and biceps expanded as well, completing his desired muscle growth.

    Ty just stared at his new body in amazement, until he had the incredible urge to pose before the tall mirror. He made two fists and raised his elbows, allowing two massive biceps to pop up from the top of his massive arms. The biceps were like bowling balls, and they gave him that Hercules look that he had always wanted ever since he was a little kid.

    “Try to bully Ty, and surely you will die!” the blond, overgrown teen sang to himself, as he did all the familiar poses that bodybuilders do when they are competing in a muscle contest.

    Ty walked around the room for awhile, for he had to get used to his increased body weight. He had never made himself this big before, and he understood that he was probably one of the heaviest men on the planet, especially for his age.

    “I’m… too sexy for my room, too sexy for my school, too sexy for my house…” he started to sing to himself, as he marched around the room in a big circle, while striking different poses every time he came close to the mirror.

    Finally, after spending ten minutes marching around his bedroom while having the size and the body of a massive muscle man, he once again got bored with his incredible physique, and decided that it was time to shrink back.

    “Hmmm… I like feeling this heavy, but maybe I should be a little shorter?” he wondered out loud. He knew that he could always grow back to nine feet if and when he wanted to, and he just loved the idea of experimenting with his powers just a little more.
