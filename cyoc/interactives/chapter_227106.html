    Not content with waiting around as a human sized tomato maya heaved herself forward, the sloshing of her juicy interior to pealing her forward.

    "Whoops!" She cried as she rolled onto her belly and then her head, to her back and but and colliding into a wall right side up again.

    "Haha, that was pretty fun! Didn't hurt at all!" She then tossed herself sideways along the wall bouncing into furniture and other walls like a pinball in her mansion all the while screaming "wheeeeee!".

    Somehow she ended back in the kitchen with only one decretive vase broken. Oddly enough she now faced the juice bottle though in her round form she couldn't get close enough to read anymore of the label.

    "This juice stuff is great! I wana try more veggie forms though." She pondered. All her other food was in the refrigerator behind the counter. Big and round she could never get to it.

    "Ah! The garden!" She exclaimed pivoting on one foot and managing to grab the bottle from the counter in her stubby hand.

    "Look out world here I come!" She cheered hurling herself forward and toward her French doors leading to the garden outback.

    Maya had become the veggie queen from her love of gardening, and even as a powerful CEO she still took time to maintain her personal garden filled with vegetables modified to grow in any season.

    She crashed through the doors out into her huge multi level patio and launched of the first steps whirling through the air and bouncing onto the next level below. "Ouf, ah! Ouch!" She moaned as she spun and bounced down into the garden. As she twirls and bounces she lifts her thumb from the bottle and in her ricochet moment douces the garden in the juice. Somewhere in the frenzy though the bottle slips from her grip.

    That's of little concern to her though as her though as she's reached her goal. Still rolling uncontrolled in the garden and in able to feed herself with her stuck limbs she simply faces upward with her mouth open and biting anything that lands there.
