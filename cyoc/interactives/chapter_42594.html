    Carol thought about it and nodded. "I'll probably have to sit on a book, but I should be able to."

    Laura nodded. "Grab anything you need from the locker room, Carol, the keys to my van are in my desk, top right drawer. Go get it, bring it around the back doors. We'll slip out of the gym, then go to my house. I have money there, we can use that to get clothes and food.
