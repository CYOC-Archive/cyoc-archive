    The door opened and a lady entered the bar; she was a young woman who had clear brown eyes, long brown hair that hung just above her shoulders and a curvy body with decent-sized breasts that looked through her shirt quite well

    "Hey Tina!" The young woman greeted the bar jovially.

    Tina nodded to the young woman, "Oh, hello Mary. You're going to ask for the usual, aren't you?" Tina asked.

    "Yes please." the young girl answered

    "Very well, I'll get to you in a second. I still have a couple of orders to attend."

    "Don't worry," said Mary casually, "Take as much time as you need."

    The young woman sat next to Jason, who was trying to look inconspicuous to the new arrival. Impossible given the fact that he was a three-year-old boy who wore nothing but a diaper sitting in a bar full of adults.

    Mary looked at Jason and gave him a friendly smile. "Excuse me Tina, and who is this child?"

    "He's a newbie," explained a nearby patron. "He tried the Youthenizer and I guess he must have drunk too much"

    Apparently, Mary thought it was a little funny because then she giggled a little bit, "Well, what a nice diaper you are wearing, little one! "

    Hearing that Jason pulled the pacifier out of his mouth and angrily stuck out his tongue.

    At that moment, Mary held up her hands, "Hey there, I wasn't trying to sound rude or anything like that. I've also tried to go that way. Right, Tina?"

    Tina looked up hearing her name. "Yes, in fact, I think you asked for a bit last month. You never told me how it felt after taking it. "

    "At first it was fun, but it turns out there isn't much you can do around the city when you're just a 13-year-old girl."

    Mary turned her attention to the boy. "So, what do you think, kid? How is the drink?"

    "It's, uh ... different." Jason wasn't used to how childish his voice was now.

    "I know what you are trying to say. Next time be more careful not to drink too much and you won't have to spend all day in diapers again."

    "Actually this is the age I wanted to reach to be able to wear diapers" Jason admitted blushing a little in the process.

    When he drank the youthenizer he had drunk enough to be young enough for diapers, but not young enough to be practically helpless, so he decided to become a toddler.

    "Seriously?" Mary gave him a thoughtful look as if she was contemplating something, "Well, I must tell you that you are very adorable at this age! By the way, what is your name?"

    "Jason"

    "Well, it was a great pleasure meeting you Jason," she said giving the toddler's a friendly hug which he eagerly returned. Even though he just met her, Jason could tell she really seemed like a very nice person.

    At that precise moment, Tina returned bringing a glass full of wine for Mary. However, before she could grab the glass, Mary pushed it away with her hand.

    "Yeah know what? After thinking about it a bit, I think I'd rather like to try a little Youthenizer."

    At that moment, both Jason and Tina were surprised to hear what Mary said.
    "Wait ... What? Do you want to try the Youthenizer again? I thought you didn't want to try it again" Tina exclaimed

    "Well, at the beginning I wasn't going to do it, but after meeting Jason, it gave me a certain desire to be a baby again so that I could be able to wear diapers again. Besides, I think I could use a little break after all the work I've had in the office."

    "So, you want to be a baby again?" Jason asked who still couldn't believe what she had just said.

    "I do actually", Mary replied.

    "Besides, that way I could be able to spend more time with you. Also, I've always wanted to see Tina's friend's daycare. Surely it will be more fun to visit it as a baby than as an adult. And it's only for one day."

    "Sure, it's fine that you want to go back to a baby. I have no problem at all. In fact, I think it would be a lot of fun to have someone else to play with me while I am still at this age," Jason said.

    Mary also replied very excited, "Thank you very much, Jason"

    "Well, I guess my friend will have another baby to take care of," Tina said with a shrug.

    Tina took away her drink and brought out a small bottle of youthenizer, which Mary quickly grabbed. Popping the top, Mary sighed deeply before taking a long drink of the cinnamon-flavored liquid. Mary set her bottle down and waited patiently for the effects to begin. About half a minute later, it was when Mary's body began to regress and became younger and younger with each passing second. All the regression was happening right before Jason's eyes.

    Jason thought that this is what he must have looked like to the people in the bar when he regressed earlier.
    He watched closely as how Mary's appearance began to change as she regressed. From her hair to her arms and legs that began to get smaller and smaller. And finally, her clothes were beginning to fall off completely.
