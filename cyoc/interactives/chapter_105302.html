    Robo-Kate wakes up.

    You're laying on a bed as you wake up, and get an extremely weird feeling. You still feel somewhat human... until you look at the tall mirror that was placed close to the bed. You stare at your new reflection in a state of shock as you realize that you only felt somewhat human because you still stand like one, but you are now a robotic anthro dragon that is slightly taller than your former human self.

    You look around and see that your new body has transparent glass-like skin with a bumpy scale pattern and below your skin you can see that green copper colored gears and other machinery have replaced all visible muscles, while new golden chest scales travel down your whole body until they stop at the under-tip of your new long tail. You still have four fingers and a thumb on each scaley hand but your new digitigrade feet only have three large toes with golden claws on all of your digits. your new head is pointed and beautiful with two snake-eye shaped monitors in place of eyes, solar panel shaped fins, sharp golden teeth, and exhaust ports instead of nostrils to give the look of you exhaling smoke like an actual dragon.

    Your new wings are large and powerful and similar in style to the wings of a bat, and now that you think about it you no longer feel feminine. In fact you notice that your whole body is bigger in multiple ways. You have the appearance of having thick muscles everywhere and and you're thin but not too thin. You also notice that you no longer have anything that look like breasts and have a very male looking chest instead. "Does this mean I'm not a lesbian anymore?" you think with a concerned expression, "but I guess I did choose the random transformation option so I can't complain."

    Soon after that thought, a woman entered the room from a side door. The woman stepped in front of you and said, "Hello dear, I'm Dr. Sanson. Well, you chose random, and this is the body you'll have to live with but you probably know this by now. You're actually rather lucky. You didn't put ANY limits at all on what you could have become. You could have ended up something much, much stranger, believe me. But I'm sure you read the disclaimer you signed. I hope. Well anyway, there are a few things you should know about this body. Your skin may look like glass, but I assure you, you're quite indestructible. Also, when you feel tired you are actually running low on power and will need to recharge in some way. You can do this by standing under sunlight, eating anything, or resting on a large charging station. You can connect to the internet at any time since your head is a wifi hotspot, but be careful as malware can make you feel ill, but if that happens just visit any specialized doctor. We already gave your parents the phone numbers of various clinics for your kind. Now, do you have any questions, or would you like to see your family now? They're waiting outside to meet you.

    What do you do now?
