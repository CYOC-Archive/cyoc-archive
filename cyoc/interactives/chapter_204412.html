    Spark took in every word her mother had just said to her and repeated it back to herself. The Queen of the fairies invited Spark and Connor to her court in front of a delegation of other fairies, to her home land that she hadn't been back to in a very long time.

    "The Queen wants to meet us....the queen wants to meet us....?" Spark repeated aloud.

    "Spark? You okay?" Connor asked placing his hand on Spark's hand.

    "Never better Connor poo, your Sparky-Warky just needs to go into her room for a minute...to think...out loud..." Spark said as she stood up perfectly straight from her chair with a large grin and proceeded into her bedroom.

    "Well she seems to be taking it perfectly well." Nova said.

    "Connor poo?" Jacob asked looking to Connor who shrugged.

    "Wait for it mom." Star said to her mother as everyone waited in silence before they heard Spark let out a loud scream.

    "I should go see her." Connor said as he began to get up.

    "Actually, Connor, Midna and I are going to handle this one. Come on Midna." Star said to Midna motioning for her sister to get up from her chair.

    "Can't I have a little pie first?" Midna asked as Star before Star pulled her up from her chair.

    Midna and Star looked into the various rooms before finding the one that was Spark's room. The two sisters opened the door to find her room decorated to half filled things for when she was fairy size and the other half for when she's human sized. They noticed her sitting by the window sill in fairy form looking outside. Minda and Star shrunk down to their fairy forms and flew over next to Spark.

    "Hey Spark, feeling a little nervous?" Midna asked her little sister.

    "What gave it away? The way I left the table, or my scream a minute ago?" Spark sarcastically asked.

    "Come on, it's not that bad. The Queen's really nice and she doesn't always invite humans to her kingdom so you and your betrothed should..." Star began to say before Spark stopped her.

    "IT'S NOT THAT EASY! It was never that easy. I never cared to try and get a position in the queen's court and I never wanted to stick around to run a shop or anything like that. I wanted to be different from everyone else. Mom told me so much about her time with Connor's granddad and the stuff they did together that I wanted to carry on after her and it's been great. Working with Jacob, then working Caleb gave me purpose. Working alongside Connor and being with Connor, it's....god it's amazing. He's amazing. I never thought I'd fall in love with a human, but there's no human like Connor." Spark sighed as she looked outside the window.

    "Wow, you've really got it bad for him. So what's making you so nervous?" Midna asked.

    Spark's wings drooped at this question as she tried to hold back tears.

    "Connor and I are the first union of a fairy and a human in a long time. There weren't any unions like this since before mom was younger than us and I'm afraid....I'm afraid she'll reject him....and us together...." Spark said as she couldn't hold back her tears anymore and began to cry.

    Midna and Star sat on one side of their sister's shoulders and held her close.

    "From what I've seen Spark, Connor is lucky to have you." Star said to Spark wiping a tear off her sister's face.

    "Yeah, and you're lucky to have him. Star and I noticed that you were holding hands under the dinner table." Midna said as Star and Spark chuckled while Spark also blushed.

    "Was that his hand she was holding? I could have sworn she was reaching for something else?" Star smirked as Spark gave her a little push.

    "As much as I do like the exciting life, it's always nice for me and Connor to come home to a view like this on the farm. It always makes me feel happy inside." Spark said as she took a deep breath and looked at her sisters and gave them a hug.

    "Thanks, you two are the most annoyingly lovable sisters I've got." Spark said.

    They then heard a knock at Spark's bedroom door.

    "Spark? You doing okay?" Connor asked as he opened the door. Spark flew up and zipped over to Connor turning back into her human form and giving Connor a great kiss on the lips.

    "I'm doing better." Spark said as she took his hand. "Just promise you'll stand by me as we meet the queen."

    "I'll always stand by your side." Connor said smiling with Spark smiling back at him.

    Nova now made her way to Spark's bedroom door as well.

    "If you're feeling better dear, we do have something else we should probably discuss. Exactly how to explain to some of your friends parents that they're going to the land of the fairies to meet the queen." Nova said.
