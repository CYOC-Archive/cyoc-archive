Gianna has to walk across the room and passed the kitchen island to see the two of them. The girl was on top of Dan as they continued to fight. "You guys have some spunk I'll give you that."     "Woah! I didn't sign up for this. You want Dan not me, I didn't even know he had a girlfriend." She says instantly getting up from him and backing away.     "It's Hannah. I'm sorry, I promise you'll never see me again if you change me back!" She says squirming inside the giant grip.

    "Let me go you giant bitch!" Dan finally chimes in shouting frustratedly. His fists pounded against her finger, but they might as well been little taps.

    "No, I don't think I will." She smirks scooping Dan up and walking towards the large cage. She opened the front door latch and dropped them in onto the woodchips. They both groaned standing up and trying to get the chips out of their hair. The black metal bars rattled around them as Gianna snapped the plastic latch shut, sealing them in. "D'aww aren't you two such a cute couple now."

    "What are we supposed to do in here? You're just gonna leave us?!" He says holding his arms out looking around the cage. There was a large water bottle attached to the bars and a small bowl with some food still in it.

    "Well, now you two have all the time in the world for your favorite hobby. In fact how about this... I wish Hannah will always get pregnant when you two fuck." She says smiling. Dan and Hannah look at each other confused not knowing how Gianna's powers work. She watches as Hannah shifts away from Dan nervously. "I'll leave you two to it though. Oh, I almost forgot I had this installed." She reaches behind the cage and presses a button on a small device. The gadget beeps coming to life. As soon as Dan makes a step a camera lens pops up and starts following his motions. "I had a monitor set up for speedy when I was at work. Gotta keep an eye on you two." She winks before walking off.
