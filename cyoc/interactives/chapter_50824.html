    At first you are a little dubious about letting your younger brother take you for a walk on a leash, but you figure since you look like a dog you might as well act like a dog to get the whole experience. After he clips the least on you practically drag Ken toward the elevator in your haste to get outside.

    "Wow," Ken said pushing the button to send the elevator to the ground floor, "you must really need to go for a walk. How long have you been cooped up in that apartment?"

    You finally manage to get out the front door of your building and the sights and smells of the outside world seen from this new point of view are almost more than you can take all at once. You start running for the park across the street barely remembering to stop at the curb long enough to look both ways for cars, Ken rushing to keep up the entire time.

    As you enter the park you feel the need to stop and examine a few interesting smells coming from the base of many of the trees. Before you realize you are even doing it you find yourself peeing on a nearby tree. For a second you are embarrassed before remembering that you are currently a dog and this is acceptable behavior.

    You pee on a few more trees before settling into a steady quick pace that has Ken jogging along side you. When you reach the other end of the jogging trail you start to turn around to head for home before Ken pulls on your leash guiding you to follow him onto the nearby street. You follow Ken's lead since you don't really care where you are going as long as you get to stay outside.

    You hesitate when Ken stops pulling you to a halt. He is holding a door open and seems to expect you to go through. You stare up at him with a questioning look. "It's okay boy you can come in here."

    Not really feeling any better about it you reluctantly enter the building realizing as the door swings closed that this is the vets office that you always bring Scot to.

    You see Ken lean across the front desk and talk quietly enough to the nurse sitting there that you can't hear what they are saying. You see Ken reach into his back pocket and pull out his wallet, handing a credit card to the nurse who quickly swipes it through a card reader. After signing the slip of paper that had printed out Ken looks down at you with a big grin on his face saying, "Good news Boy, they have an opening and are going to take you right now."

    The nurse comes around the desk and pets your head while taking hold of your leash from Ken. "Come on boy, lets get you all ready for the doctor shall we?" She leads you to a back room that is set up as an operating room. You feel a sharp pinch as the nurse injects you with something that starts to make you drowsy almost immediately.

    You give the nurse a sad look as your eyes start to lose focus. "Don't worry boy," she says, "the doctor is very good at what he does and it won't hurt too much. And I promise you won't even miss your testicles, once we're through with you it will be just like you never had them to begin with."

    You last conscious thought is one of panic as the realization that they mean to neuter you sets in. You try to say something in protest but it's too late that blackness has already taken you.
