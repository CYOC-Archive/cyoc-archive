    As Rachel wondered about looking out for either Cynthia or hoping to learn where the Centaurs gathered she stopped as some rustling came from some nearby bushes. “Who’s there?” Rachel called firmly in response.

    Right after the calling two child Centaurs, both 8 years old with one a boy and the other a girl, albeit both with chin-length hair, emerged from the bushes. “Oh. Hi their new one” the girl called out joyfully to Rachel. “What’s your name?” the boy followed while Rachel quickly tried using her hands to cover her bare chest.

    “Corice, Sebeus. There you are” this female centaur, mid-30s by Rachel’s guessing with long swept back Red hair that matched the boy’s, called as she emerged with this equally aged yet heathly human-half male centaur adult with chin-length blond hair. “Oh, Our apologises miss. You must be new” the adult male centaur responded upon seeing Rachel. “Rachel Sinclair” Rachel greeted. “First timer I take it. Well it’s alright, nudity is purely accepted among us centaurs” the female centaur assured which Rachel easily agreeing as she was just as nude.

    “She a false walker or an awakened father?” the girl then asked. “That has yet to be determined Corice” the adult male kindly answered. “Oh their your kids” Rachel realized. “Perele Vorl and my husband Alano” the mother introduced. “And our kids, Daughter Corice and Son Sebeus” Alano followed. “Interesting...names” Rachel nervously noted as otherwise like her this centaur family was Caucasian in human background. “Me and my wife are awakened as Corice asked over you” Alano started “Or as you’d probably heard about the resort. Those that chose to remain forever following rule 5”. “But us even getting pregnant to begin with was over us realizing we preferred being centaurs over humanity” Perele followed. “Oh I see” Rachel accepted.

    “She going to follow us to the fields mother?” Sebeus then asked. “I would gather so as she is new Sebeus” Perele assured. “Oh thank you. I admit being my first ever time at the resort I do need a guide... or four” Rachel admitted “That said I was hoping to regroup a bit with a girl I met on the bus”. “Another Centaur hopeful?” Alano asked. “Actually she said she was planning to be a unicorn” Rachel admitted. “Ah and their woodlands neighbour the centaur fields as it happens” Alano happily assured “anyhow follow us”.

    “Oh I do believe you showed signs of interest over our names” Perele noted a bit later while Alano went on ahead a bit with the kids. “Well yes. I did” Rachel confessed. “Between signing away our forsaken human lives and the birth of the twins we underwent the resort version of deed polling” Perele started “That said many centaurs do go by more...centaurish names based on the old stories and legends even without doing so. Regardless of being false walkers and awakened”. “So I see” Rachel accepted as the fields came into sight:
