    Liz spent the next few classes thoroughly enjoying her transformation. Her left hand was stroking the fur on her right arm as she took notes in math. The fine hairs were soft and smooth, giving Liz a warmth that, while not very welcome on such a hot day, would under the right conditions be quite pleasurable.

    Nobody spoke one word about her strange appearance. She didn't even get one remark about her tail. The only time it seemed anyone was acknowledging its existence was when it dragged along the floor behind her when she moved from class to class. She noticed that people politely took care not to step on it. One of her acquaintances even shouted "Looking good today, Liz!" as she passed by.

    She made sure to keep checking her hair as classes continued, noting that its growth was progressing nicely. Just like she wanted, it was growing about a foot every hour. When it started, it had hung loosely to her shoulder blades, but within three classes' time, it grew into a beautiful curtain of red-brown that reached her waist.

    "I wonder how Jen is doing with her own hair?" Liz thought, amused. She pictured a sheep-like Jennifer so engulfed by wool that it turned her body into a giant ball of black fluff, her fingers and toes wiggling futilely at its edges and her head comically poking out of the top. She couldn't help but chuckle to herself.

    * * *

    Little did she know that Jennifer was forming much the same image in her own mind as she contemplated her future. She had to find Liz after school and make her pay. But how? Liz had the power of Transform or Dare on her side, and would probably only make Jennifer suffer even more. Meanwhile her wool was growing thicker, straining the confines of her once-baggy blouse and causing the straps of her bra underneath it to tighten irritably. If this continued, she would be forced to strip completely.

    Then a notion crept into her mind. There was one person who she could go to for help, though the very thought of it made Jennifer believe it would be better to just throw herself off the nearest cliff. But, she realized, it was the only way she could ensure her body was returned to normal.

    She had to join forces with Kyle Perkins.
