Though Neil did still feel rather curious about Brock Medina, and how he had been looking today. As well as, now, why Matt it seemed had stayed home or otherwise just not been around. Maybe it was his inner sense for patterns, but seeing this particular pattern broken...well. It just wasn't normal.

Neil saw Max doing some push ups on the floor, and walked in - chucking his school bags on the chair nearby before joining Max for some push ups.
