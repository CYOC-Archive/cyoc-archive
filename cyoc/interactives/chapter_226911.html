    New Memories and New Family

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 16 days ago A

    You figure that your first task should be to explore your home to know where everything is, if your spending the rest of your life here might as well get to know it. It was awkward walking with your body parallel with the ground rather then perpendicular making balancing difficult, your sight was so much sharper blurring your mind for how bright and detailed everything was and found the resting position for one of your toes to be pointed upward causing it to feel weird standing on them but your instincts were starting to kick in your balance was restored, your mind could now process your enhanced senses and your foot placement was not bothering you anymore.

    As you approach the pond of the oasis you see a mirror still surface that reflects your feathered figure more fully you are already fully aware of the black feather body, you also see your long slender neck leading to your long angular snout filled with sharp teeth and large amber eyes surrounded violet facial feathers that lead into your twin stripes, your thin nimble limbs each ended in a trio of razor like claws especially your feet that held your signature sickle claws perfect for cutting into prey but all so easily hidden by the fans of black feathers the rim blazing into bright violet matching your body, to finish your form a long full tail in equal length to your body an aurora of violet color waving out in beautiful spectacle. You find your visage majestic and regal a desert queen admiring her reflection. You are caught off guard by that thought just a moment ago you were horrified by your transformation now you are admiring your form. Why shouldn’t you, you are a smart fast hunter who thrives in an environment that kills most others a soon to be pack leader. Pack?

    Just as the thought crossed your mind you see five feathered figures move pass a sand dune whereupon they start to approach you. Your human mind starts to panic as to who these creatures are but your raptor instinct seem to take control as you soon recognize them as your family. The five figures were your pack. In the lead a beautiful older larger female velociraptor with similar violet markings as your own maybe a little duller, you remember her as your mother. The female raptor standing next to her is slightly smaller with the same feather pattern only with blue coloration rather then violet, this you are reminded is your aunt. The three younger males in the back with their multi-color plumage and high feathered head crests are your mischievous brothers. Although this is your first meeting your mind is filled with memories of these individuals. Hatching to see your mother’s face, playing with your clutch mates, learning to hunt with your aunt, avoiding the dangers of the desert unlike so many of your siblings and most importantly how to become a self assured leader for when you and your brothers eventually leave your mother’s pack. It was troubling at first having these memories but if this is your life now they could be useful also they make you out as a badass. As you reacquainted yourself with your family your mother motion everyone to move out. What for, you will soon learn.
