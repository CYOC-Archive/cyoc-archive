    The Magic Shop

    The plan: fail but not fail

    Previous chapter

    added by Liquid O 2 months ago A BM Anthro Mental



    —————————

    After Setepenre had finished her breakfast she kissed her mother goodbye and skipped her way down the clean sidewalk to catch the bright yellow school bus. On the way to school every student in the bus, including Setepenre, sang in perfect harmony, each with the same big smile.

    —————————

    Bong-cha had a plan, she used one of her guns to shoot a ceiling fan and pin the zombie-like residents, allowing them to focus on the creature in the hall.
    Luca then grew larger, transforming into his werewolf form; his limbs grew bigger with large claws, black fur lined his skin and his face became very wolf like. Once he was finished he bursted through the door and tackled the monster outside. This allowed Bong-cha to pull out Setepenre’s shotgun she dropped and shoved its barrel into the creature’s mouth. She pulled the trigger until she heard the click when it was empty, the dragons breathe silver rounds burned through the creature.
    Luca returned to his human form and they both took a moment to breathe. This didn’t last long as another creature had emerged from the elevator and stalked towards them.

    “Shit, let’s get in one of these rooms!” She shouted and reached for the nearest door.

    “No, let’s hide in this one.” Luca said and guided Bong-cha to an apartment three doors down.

    When they got inside they saw an older man with a little girl they assumed was his daughter along with their friend, Setepenre.

    “I caught her sent coming from this room, now we just have to snap her out of this and get out of this floor.” Luca explained.

    This minor victory was spoiled when a powerful banging came from the doors, the werewolf kept the door shut with his body but it was clear he couldn’t hold on much longer. To make matters worse the three mesmerized people started creeping towards them.
    Bong-cha didn’t know what to do, that’s when she had an idea.

    “I think I know how to get out of this, but you have to trust me.” She said.

    Luca nodded and she took out a prototype magical field disrupter and set it on a timer.
    Then she motioned for Luca to follow her and the two walked towards the enchanted people and touched them, causing the two to also be affected by the strange spell.
    Both of them gained the same glass eyed look and their bodies slouched. Bong-cha’s assumption paid off and once the monster from the halls busted open the apartment door it stopped, seeing the intruders now under the same spell as the others on the 5th floor it left for the elevator shaft.
    The two agents now slipped into the hypnotic dreamland.

    —————————

    Bong-cha woke up with a bright smile on her face, she breathed in and smelled the sweet scent of pancakes and orange juice.
    After getting dressed for school in a pretty pink, lace collared drop waist dress with poofy short sleeves. Her makeup brought out her girlish features as did the big bows in her ringlet pigtails, on her feet were frilled socks with brown, low heeled, character shoes.
    Once she was finished getting ready she bounced down the stairs and kissed her father good morning,

    “Good morning daddy dearest!” She said, never losing her wide smile.

    She got to the kitchen and greeted her mother before sitting down to breakfast.

    “Good morning mommy dearest!” She said.

    After eating breakfast and kissing her parents goodbye she hopped into the yellow school bus where she joined in with the other students’ cheery song.

    ——

    Luca awoke to the smell of his wife’s cooking, he got dressed in a three piece suit complete with fedora and greeted his family,

    “Good morning my dearest sons, beloved wife!” He said with a grin.

    After eating his wife’s breakfast he kissed her and left for work, taking note of the sunny day.

    —————————

    As Bong-cha and Luca were entranced the magical field disruptor she set went off and the two were freed from the spell, as well as Setepenre, the man and his daughter.

    “What the fuck was that?” Bong-cha shuttered.

    “It was like a dream.” Setepenre said still in a daze.

    “More like a nightmare.” The Asian girl corrected.

    “Ugh…see? It worked!” Bong-cha said, still shaking off the mental fog still lingering in her head.

    “Hell…you’re lucky that actually worked, but those things only work once, we won’t be able to do that again if either of us get bewitched again.” Luca said checking the hall.

    “W-what?… what just happened? I don’t remember…” the black girl stammered confusingly.

    Bong-cha just hugged her and told her what had happened.

    “So how do we get past that creature of it shows up again?” Setepenre asked.

    “Let’s just get to the other stairwell, before that thing comes back.” The red haired girl said.

    Bong-cha, Setepenre, and Luca cautiously peaked out the door and when they confirmed that the monster was gone they made a break for the stairwell next to the elevator. The elevator opened and another monster came out but before it could attack they had already made it past the doors to the stairs and were climbing them as fast as they could towards the next floor.
