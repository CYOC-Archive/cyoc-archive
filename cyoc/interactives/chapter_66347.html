    Ty's Power

    SuperTy's First Heroic Deed

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 11 years ago O

    Ty was having a blast as SuperTy. He loved being this powerful. He was currently invisible because he did not want people to be surprised. It is not every day they see a superhero flying in the sky. Also, Ty wanted some time alone to enjoy his new powers. After a while of flying he landed and started to walk down the street, wondering what to do next with his powers. Walking past a bank Ty heard faint whispers of fright. Turning towards the building he turn on his x-ray vision and saw that the bank was being rob. Ty was worried and wondered what to do. Then he remembered that he was a superhero now. Before he went in he wished that he would not be seen as a weirdo. Instead, he would be seen as a genuine superhero, but this is the first time he has appeared. Ty made himself visible again as he raced into the bank. “Leave this people alone,” he demands. Everyone looked relieved except for the robber who fired his gun while saying, “Hit hot lead!” The robber fired the gun but the bullets bounced off Ty’s chest. The man was panicking because his weapon was having no effect while Ty had a smirk on his face. Before the robber could think Ty used his super speed to race to him and punch him in the stomach which knocked him out. The people cheered. Ty felt great to have his amount of admiration landing at his feet.

    The police came and took the criminal away, thanking Ty for the help. Along with the police the press also came. Ty was surrounded by reporters and cameras, asking questions about him. All this attention was new to Ty and he loved it.

    “Who are you?” one reporter asked. Ty put his fists on his hips and said, proudly, “I’m SuperTy!” Cameras clicked to take his picture. As Ty hit a few more heroic poses and flexed his muscles for the camera in his head he was yelling, “I love this!”
