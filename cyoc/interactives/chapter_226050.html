Damon wonders as to who they were, yet he had a strange feeling of who they might of been.     "Sleep well, brother." That aside as Damon realize that she was going to do the same, to him. So why, why would she do this to her very own brother?

Who knows, within a few years or so that my family will think you might be dead. All because you were born without magic. Well, have fun kid. I'm sure you'll have fun with you're new friends that I've made over the years by now."

The other foxes you saw earlier from the bar were once human too as they were his friends, team mates from school, not to mention how many girlfriends he had over the years now, and also some random drunk who wished to be part of the act when I told them that it was the real thing."
