    Dawn Sneaks In.

    added by Burst Jammer 4 years ago A BM TG O Mental Male to female

    Edwin knew that he had to think long and hard about this. The body he was choosing now, although the changes could be reversed, would be the body he would have for a good while. He'd definitely want to be comfortable in it without having to struggle too much to adjust, that went without saying. But he also wanted to know what feminine urges felt like.

    Knowing this would be a big decision, Edwin became lost in his own thoughts, not even noticing Dawn sneak into his room, creeping through the ajar door.

    She, unlike Edwin, had filled out her survey before going to bed. She had chosen to become part snow leopard, since that was her favorite animal. She now sported cat like ears coming out of the top of her head, a leopard's tail coming out of her skirt, and cat like feet with claws and pads. Her body and spine were also a lot more flexible, and she had gotten a few animalistic instincts.

    Smirking, the blue haired girl tapped Edwin on the shoulder while he was still in his thoughts. Startled, Edwin gave a yelp, nearly jumping out of the chair he was sitting on.

    "Heehee, I surprised you, didn't I?" Dawn said, playfully sticking out her tongue as Edwin gathered himself. "By the way, why aren't you changed yet?"

    "Well, I'm trying to choose my options now," Edwin said, showing her the survey, as well as the multitude of options he had been given. Dawn swiped the surgery out of his hands, however, looking it over herself.

    "Hey, Dawn, give that back!" Edwin said, reaching out to take the survey back. Unfortunately for him, though, Dawn's changes made her more nimble, the cat girl taking quick steps to distance herself from her brother.

    The blue haired girl then gave her brother a smirk, grabbing a pen to check off the last two boxes, tossing the survey down a built in mail chute much to Edwin's display.

    "Hey!" Edwin exclaimed, much to his sister's amusement, before calming himself down. It wasn't like his sister would actively give himself a body that he'd hate... right?

    "Trust me, I did you a favor!" Dawn said, giving her brother a reassuring pat on his shoulder. "You probably would've spent FOREVER on that survey. Now, you don't have to!"

    Edwin sighed, seeing her point. Yet, he was still curious as to what Dawn had put down on the survey. "I guess I owe you some thanks... but what did you put on the survey?"

    "Well, you're gonna be a Party Girl, with a 50% Mental Change!" Dawn said with a smile, before taking some time to lick her side. Seeing this, Edwin couldn't help but think that it was pretty weird to watch, but he also smiled at the fact that Dawn was comfortable in her new form.

    "I guess that'll work out..." Edwin said, interrupting Dawn as he voiced his thoughts about her selection. "Now, I guess I just have to wait, huh?"

    "Yep!" Dawn said, springing back up to her feet. "Ooh, but I wanna watch you change!"
