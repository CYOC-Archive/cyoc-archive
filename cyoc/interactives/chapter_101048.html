    Day of the Baby Furs: She's Not Here...

    Maxi brought her plane in for a soft and easy landing at the Jackie Ville airport and Sammy couldn't help but smile and kick his legs a little as the plane taxied, even while he could hear Maxi making airplane noises to simulate the sound an airplane's engine might make. Though, the sound she made was more like what a propeller driven plane would sound like than a jet powered one even though the plane sort of looked like a little kid's version of a plastic Boeing 747. None of the baby furs on it, including Sammy, really minded though. It got them to their destination.

    He actually had a great deal of fun with the flight. He'd never hated flying before but neither had he really enjoyed flying before, but as a baby fur, he had a great time. It was like he was a kid again on a new adventure. He giggled when he remembered he was a baby fur, a furry toddler wearing nothing but a diaper, and was on an adventure to find the first fur and see if there would be a way they could make it so that they would be able to talk to humans and assure them that they only wished to make friends and that they wouldn't lose anything by becoming baby furs, or if they didn't wish to become baby furs, they could at least get along with the baby furs that lived with them without fear and so on.

    "Did you have fun?" Sammy asked to Jessy as the plane came to a stop.

    "I liked the flight," Jessy answered, "everything seemed so small while flying..."

    "That's because we were high up and far away from everything," Maxi commented to them as she came out from the area where the plane's cockpit was.

    Jessy and Sammy both nodded and stepped aside as the other passengers began to disembark. Verri lead the others out while Sammy and Jessy paused to talk with the pilot. They smiled and waved to all the other passengers as they left. They were, after all, Sammy and Jessy's friends as fellow baby furs, and Sammy in a strange way felt like they had all been best friends for ages. It let him feel good about letting Jessy transform him.

    "So... what brings you to Jackie Ville?" Maxi asked as the three of them then began to follow the others out of the plane and into the Jackie Ville airport, which looked pretty much like the airport that Sammy and Jessy had flown to Jackie Ville from.

    "The first fur," Sammy answered, "I'm kinda curious over all the powers and stuff that we can do... what made her the first fur."

    Maxi tilted her head for a moment. Then came the inevitable question. "Why?"

    "To learn, I suppose," Sammy answered honestly, "maybe think of a song to play that would talk about her and stuff like that..."

    "Like Old McDonald?" Maxi asked, "and his farm?"

    "Yeah," Sammy answered, "I figured it'd help us let non-friends know we don't mean them harm... that we want to be friends... without them being afraid of us. Maybe then, they'll ask to be friends."

    "That doesn't sound bad," Maxi commented.

    Jessy hugged him, "Sammy's very smart."

    Maxi nodded in response to that, while Sammy patted Jessy's furry back.

    "Have you met the first fur?" Sammy asked.

    "No... I'm ah-fray-duh not," Maxi said slowly, "but I've met lots of friends who have met her. She lives down here in Florida... though not in Jackie Ville."

    Sammy sighed, and then figured that not everything would be easy. He remembered that the first fur had appeared in Florida on a beach, but now he couldn't exactly remember WHICH beach in Florida. He supposed he and Jessy would just have to travel around Florida's coastal cities first, if only to find the first fur at some point. It'd certainly give them plenty of time to have fun, make new friends with other baby furs, and maybe build on their bestest-friends status.

    "Do any of them live in Jackie Ville?" Jessy asked.

    "I believe so," Maxi nodded, "There's a tiger baby fur that runs a gym here that's met her."

    "Then let's go!" Sammy declared, eager for the first clue that would lead to the first fur and help with his mission and just as important, to figure what a baby fur gym looked like and see what fun could be had there.
