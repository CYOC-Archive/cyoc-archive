    James nodded his head knowing that this was going to be better for the both of you in a way. "Splendid, and don't worry, your life as a dragon will be lived to the fullest, trust me." Kayla said, and casted a spell on James. James suddenly noticed that everything was getting bigger, or he was shrinking, and the next thing he noticed was that he didn't just shrink he was younger. Suddenly James let out a cough blowing fire out of his mouth as he did.

    "Ehh, so you basically made me younger, as a means to force me to trust you. Am I correct?" James said in a childish voice.

    "You aren't too far off, allow me to explain. When I turned you into a dragon, I made you older as to accommodate for your age, and put a seal making you an adult; However, I just undid that seal making you a hatchling due to the lifespan of dragons. Late 30s for a dragon is about.... 7 months for a dragon so your really young, and I mean REALLY young." Kayla said picking James up off of the ground.

    "Not to worry though," Kayla continued,"I know someone who owes me a favor in another world that I found, and is where I learned my magic from. That person wants to have a child, but is worried about said child's future, you follow me." Kayla said looking at you.

    "And you want me to be said child, quick question should I be at all worried about who I get sent too, and are they qualified overall to raise a child?" James asked.

    "Correct, and not to worry they actually have multiple children, and in this case they are willing to raise you like one of them, and your advantage is that these 'parents' of yours are also dragons. In addition to that they asked me to do this for them, and in your case you set me up in the perfect position to convert you to assist me. So are you on board?" Kayla asked.

    James had to think again about his decision. On one hand, or claw in this case, she turned James into a dragon against his will, and turned him into a kid as well, and is handing him off to some random parents. On the other however she gave James a fresh start at life, and did so in an unexpected way, in addition him personally think dragons are cool, and feel young, and childish again giving him a second childhood.

    What do you do now?
