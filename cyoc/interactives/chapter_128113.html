    Mad Science

    60 Foot Toddler: The Reducer meets a big diaper

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by PrincessKooh 6 years ago S


    Bigger and bigger Sara continued to grow. Her growth wasn't super fast nor super slow, yet visible enough to be noticeable were anyone present. What seemed to be happening fast was actually taking a fair amount of time to transpire.

    Sara sat in her playpen rocking back and forth giggling at the feelings and sensations of growing as well her changing perspective. In her child's mind it was a game.

    At 4 feet tall and growing the toddler was running out of space in the playpen.

    Minutes later, at 6 feet tall wood and plastic started to bend and creak. Sara's legs slid between the bars. Suddenly wood splintered and plastic snapped. The growing girl fell a few barely noticeable inches at her size, the combination crib/playpen in ruins.

    The 7 foot tot took a happy moment of notice.

    "Oooo!"

    For Sara this was a rare experience. She was now free of her confines. Fortunately her nursery was an unusually exceptionally large room. Still, it was a good thing the tyke decided to go for a crawl!

    As Sara crawled her way across the carpet little did she know her body was creating static electricity that was mixing with the mysterious energy her growth generated.

    As she crawled across the room (still growing) a coffee table and a few toys met their end. Sara was 9 feet tall and still growing. She stopped a moment after her hand flattened another toy. Sara, who was not unlike some other children who had a habit of putting things in their mouth, picked up the busted and shrinking (by her perspective) toy.

    "Mmm candy. Yumm yum snacky, " said the unaware toddler.

    With that the slightly hungry growing girl tossed the toy in her mouth and swallowed what once would have been a choking hazard.

    10 feet tall and satisfied for the moment, the growing tyke resumed her slow crawl across the large room. Every so often Sara would flatten a toy or another piece of furniture with her growing body. She stopped at a bookshelf once bigger than her and knocked it over.

    Downstairs 2 floors below Megan, who was still messing with Kelly, was as blissfully ignorant to the situation above as Kelly. Neither had a clue what had transpired in the last 25 minutes. And was still going on.

    The 11 foot and still growing toddler soon found the diaper bag left by her parents. Inside she found a box of treats for kids her age and ate it. Next she found a sippy cup full of formula. She chugged it then let out a burp. Tossing aside the bag she resumed her crawl.

    Once more toys met their end under the growing toddler as Sara slowly crawled across the room. Bigger and bigger Sara swelled.

    A few minutes later her growth was finally starting to slow. The growing toddler had reached the other side of the room now 13 ft tall and counting.

    Sara opened the door with ease but noticed a problem... sort of.

    "Aww dwer too small."

    Her growth was slowing but it was too little too late. The 14 ft tall girl would not fit. Sara closed the door then noticed the dresser next to it. She was now bigger than it as her growth finally stopped leaving her 15 ft tall!

    What caught the tykes attention though was the Reducer. Or as Sara saw it:

    "Oooo! Shiny toy!"

    Sara grabbed it off the dresser. The moment she did the static electricity and mysterious growth energy combination that had built up around her was channeled into the device which had been slowly self recharging. The device was instantly fully recharged and then some. The toddler then began to play with the 'toy' for a while.

    Suddenly Sara felt strange again without having used the Reducer yet.

    Sara began growing again.

    Worried about her new 'toy' the growing toddler slipped the Reducer into her diaper, unaware she had also fired a small dose at herself.

    "Safe toy."

    Finally Megan and Kelly decided it was time to check on cousin Sara. They went upstairs. Once they got there Megan tried to open the nursery door.

    "Hey, it won't open."

    "What do you mean 'it won't open'? You must be doing it wrong, Megan."

    "I know how to open a door, sis! This one is supposed to push open. But it's like it's a brick wall!"

    Suddenly they heard a loud giggle.

    "Is it just me or does Sara sound a bit loud?" asked Kelly.

    Suddenly Kelly noticed cracks forming in the door and wall.

    "Uhh, Megan ... something tells me we should move."

    They did so. Just then the door and wall were pushed away, busted to pieces and revealing a nightmare.

    The giant and growing face and form of Sara.

    "Peek-A-Boo," said Sara, adding a giggle.

    "Oh shit! Not good! Megan, don't tell me you left the Reducer with Sara unattended!"

    "I didn't do it on purpose! Besides, how could I expect she'd get to it? She was in her crib and normal size!"

    "Megan you idiot! Did you forget you used Sara as your damned guinea pig number 2!"

    "Maybe now isn't the best time to fight or point blame, sis," said Megan, drawing attention back to the growing Sara, starting to grow out into the hallway.

    "Right, let's get outta here!"

    "But what about that gun thingy of yours?"

    "We can't worry about it now. Even if we wanted to we can't exactly get into the nursery. Plus we've no clue how big Sara is going to get! If you'd rather stay here to get hurt or dead though be my guest."

    Megan didn't need telling twice. They quickly got outta the house. Once outside various creaking, banging and other noises could be heard. Soon the house began to shake and the walls and roof started to bend and deform as Sara's growing form burst out.

    "Oh my god Sara."

    "Teehee. House get little go boom," giggled the growing tyke.

    Sara grew until she was 60 feet tall.
