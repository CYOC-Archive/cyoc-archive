    Jeff was stunned as he watched everything around him grow larger. He then realized that he was being
    transformed into a doll. A Barbie like doll if the big mounds growing on his chest were any indication.
    He quickly glanced at the computer screen and was shocked to see that the original him was to be deleted
    from reality and his being self aware was disabled. As his body and mind transformed into plastic, his
    contemplation on why his mom chose this for him slowed until he completed his transformation into a
    lifeless, thoughtless plastic Barbie doll.

    Jeff’s mom smiled as she looked over the computer program. She quickly realized that she could use it
    to make reality to her specifications. She never really wanted a son and she now had a way to correct that
    minor problem. She debated on changing Jeff into a teenage daughter but he wanted to be something
    Inanimate and she wasn’t looking forward to instantly having a teenage daughter to raise. She would
    rather help her eight year old daughter gradually grow into a teenager under her guidance. She also
    debated on using the program to change Jeff into an eight year old twin of Jenny but then she would also
    end up with more then she really wanted. One child was more then enough for anybody. She then
    remembered that she promised Jenny a new Barbie doll for her birthday.
    She smiled as she reviewed her birthday plans at the Pizza parlor scheduled for Saturday. She quickly
    entered the specs for a gift wrapped Barbie doll and then debated on whether she really wanted to torture
    Jeff by allowing him to keep his mind as a doll. She finally decided that it was acceptable for him to cease
    to exist as their reality is changed but not if his consciousness still existed in the doll. She quickly toggled
    the self aware to off before starting the transformation. She was actually surprised as she watched Jeff
    start shrinking. A part of her didn’t really believe that this was possible. She was intrigued as she watched
    her no-longer son transform into a beautiful Barbie doll in mere seconds.
    She then giggled as a factory box grew around the doll. She was quite pleased with the ‘Happy Birthday’
    wrapping paper that now covered the box. She was then stunned as Jeff’s room changed into a home
    office around her. The computer even changed into a desktop model. She happily picked up the
    wrapped doll and quickly filled out the attached birthday card before placing the gift into a desk drawer.
    She then sighed as she debated on having the computer program remove her memory of her no longer son.
    Her decision made, she quickly had her memories adjusted to remove Jeff from them.
    After a moment of confusion, she smiled as she contemplated on how useful this new program was. After
    all, didn’t she just have it create a new doll for her only child. She was also interested in the other features
    that would allow it to modify anything into anything else. She was actually debating on using it to turn
    back the clock so to speak on herself. Her hair was starting to gray and certain parts were starting to wrinkle
    and sag.
