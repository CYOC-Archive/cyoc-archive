    Crawling to Safety

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added A year ago AR Baby

    As much as Marcia tried to move her bag, it was only slowing her down. Also, she was beginning to have serious problems walking so she had no choice but to abandon her bag and try to crawl as fast as possible to a safe place.

    She finally made it into a room and with what little strength she had she was able to close the door. And after a while, she no longer felt that presence near her. Marcia felt very relieved but she still had one matter to attend to. And that was looking new clothes for herself. Surveying the room, she realized that she was now in a nursery.

    Marcia crawled over to a dresser and began searching the nearest drawers for something she could wear. But to her bad luck, none of the clothes fit her. She then proceeded to search through some toy chests and at the bottom of them were clothes that fit her well but, to her annoyance, were very girly and babyish. But that wasn't the worst part, the worst part was that the only underwear available was a few packs of disposable diapers. Considering that she didn't have many options at this time, Marcia had no choice but to use what was available in the nursery.

    Marcia took one of the diapers and clumsily taped it on. She then proceeded to put on a pink footed onesie adorned with unicorns. Now that she was fully dressed, she proceeded to crawl over to a mirror so she could look at her new appearance. Looking at her reflection, Marcia was left in complete shock. Instead of seeing the serious adult she used to be, she saw a little baby barely 1 year old. Although she didn't like what she had become, she couldn't deny that she looked really adorable. Now that she was finally ready, Marcia saw that it was time to leave the nursery.
