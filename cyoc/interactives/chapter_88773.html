    "Dun' care, ya big ssshhhhtallion. Get over to my shide," he heard Suzanne say, and he stumbled his way over to her, hard hooved feet thumping clumsily with each step in the grass. If his arms were a bit longer, he thought, he could walk right, and it wouldn't be this hard.
