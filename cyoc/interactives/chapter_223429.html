    An unexpected opportunity

    added A year ago AR Baby

    Lizzy thought that the best thing would be to try to find some way out that could help her escape, but to do so she had to find a way to escape from this baby walker. So Lizzy immediately started rocking back and forth so that the baby walker would fall off on one side.

    Although the baby walker caused some noise, Lizzy managed to make the baby walker fall from the front and finally managed to get out of there.

    Although Lizzy was very happy to have managed to escape from the walker, her happiness did not last long as she soon heard someone starting to open the door. At first Lizzy thought it was the witch but when the door opened she got a big surprise.

    The person who had opened the door was a 30-year-old red-haired woman carrying a camping backpack. And not only that, the witch was also next to her but she was now wearing casual clothes and her hair was now more colorful than before.

    "Awwwwww, what a cute baby!", the woman exclaimed.

    "She's very cute, right? She just turned 1 year old", the witch told her.

    "Really? Then she's definitely going to look beautiful when she grows up", the woman told the witch.

    "I'm sure so. By the way Olivia, could you do me a favor? Could you put Lizzy in one of the highchairs? I need to feed her."

    "Sure", Olivia replied.

    So while the witch proceeded to leave, Olivia began to walk towards Lizzy and began to lift her out of her baby walker. Lizzy wasn't sure who this person was and why she was here. Although one thing she was sure of was that she certainly didn't like her babytalking.

    "Who's a cute baby? Yes, you are. Yes, you are", Olivia said as she tickled Lizzy. Lizzy tried to resist but she ended up laughing out loud. Olivia continued pampering Lizzy until she finally decided to put her on one of the highchairs that was near the closet.

    "Your mother is really lucky to have a beautiful baby. Although I'm surprised that only you are here. I wonder where her other daughter is?", Olivia commented.

    Lizzy was surprised to hear what the lady said. What would she have meant by another daughter? But before she could figure it out, the witch had returned and she came with some baby bottles and some plates that contained small french toasts, yogurt, raspberries and some cereal.

    Lizzy noticed that she was carrying two bottles and two plates. Did she plan to feed her twice? Or is it.....oh no. Lizzy knew very well what the witch wanted to do with the woman.

    "Thank you for placing her in the highchair", the witch told her as she proceeded to place the food on a small table.

    "No problem. By the way, speaking of my room..."

    "Oh yeah, I forgot. But before that, could you feed Lizzy? I still need a few things to take care of"

    "Oh, yes. No problem"

    Lizzy knew this was her only chance. If she managed to communicate with the lady, perhaps she could escape from here and at the same time prevent this woman from being transformed into a baby. But how?
