    Rescue Fenrir

    "I'm sorry sir, your friends must leave. You are going to stay, because you're wearing one." The gatekeeper sounded upset. She poofed a diaper on the Wolf.

    Fenrir started to shrink, his Clothes becoming looser. The three other adventurers watched in shock as they saw what was happening. "Wha's happonin' to me?" Fenrir asked, sounding very confused and childish. Soon his legs felt heavy and gave out, making him land on his butt, surrounded in a pile of clothes. The gatekeeper summoned some guards and had him taken to the dungeon. Fenrir looked very confused.

    "Fenrir!" Galax yelled, he attempted to run after him, but was stopped by Lex and John. "Don't you idiot! They'll just turn YOU into a baby!" John yelled. "We'll find another way in to rescue him!" Lex attempted to calm him down. Galax hated that he was stuck in a weak Kobold body, but he did feel better since they're going to get Fenrir back.

    The three walked off. "What do you know? Maybe he likes being a baby! He did com-" John tried to joke before getting cut off. "SHUT UP!" Galax yelled, "Who cares if he's a babyfur?! We don't even know if he is or not! And even if he is, I'm still going to love him!" "Just... stop for now, John. He lost Wolfgang, he's going to be a bit emotional." "I was just kidding..." John mumbled under his breath. The three began circling the castle, looking for an Entrance way. "Guys! Found this!" Galax yelled. "Shh! Do you want us to get caught?!" John whisper yelled. "Just look!" The two others looked at the wall, it looked like someone could climb it easily with how the bricks are positioned. "I'll scout it out." Galax pressed a button on his left Gauntlet, turning him invisible. "Looks clear, come on!" Galax whispered, getting the other two to follow. They snuck into the Castle and located the dungeon... but it didn't look like a dungeon, it was more like a Nursery than anything else. "I see Fenrir!" Galax rushed over to him, "Don't worry, Fenrir! We're going to get you out!" James rolled for Fenrir's intelligence.
