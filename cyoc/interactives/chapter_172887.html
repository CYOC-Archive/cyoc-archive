    Nami said "No! Let's keep playing!" acting a little bratty for once.
    I said "This was the highlight of the day, but we need to get ready for bed."
    "Easy for you to say! You get to play video games all the time!"
    "Come on Nami, We need to go to sleep. If we wake up early enough maybe we can continue before scho-" Nami still looked upset with a red face, but she seemed quiet. "... Nami, do you want to talk about something?"
    "...its not fair..." she squeaked out
    "what isn't?"
    "you all get to make the rules... get the most food... get to play all the games you want..." I chuckled a little "What? What's so funny Meanie?!"
    "It must seem so simple to you but it really isn-"
    "Yes it is! You get to make all the rules!"
    "Well we do most of the wo-"
    "I do work too!"
    A third more ominous voice said "Ah, this is looking like a neat jealousy wish. I bet you wish you were in John's roll aren't you Nami?"
    Nami looked around and said "Yeah, that would be amazing!"
    I looked around for the voice and kind of saw an ethereal somewhat feminine form and asked "Who are you and how do you know our names?"
    The voice said "Just a mystical being looking for... Shall we say entertainment. I have one bet going already, but hey how about two? Nami wants more power, and John looks like you want a break of sorts. How about I switch you two around a little?"

John gets to be the youngest daughter of the Yami family, and oh let's say Nami gets to be the youngest daughter of the Mavis family? I looked back to where that Ethereal thing was, but couldn't see anything. I then looked at myself and...
