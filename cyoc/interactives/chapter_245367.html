    Max Gets Chipped!

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by LittleBoy Yesterday A Anthro Canine

    After a while Trevor slowly smiles, “you know,” he starts, “I believe you are right. I know he is not “Orion”, however… he reminds me so much of him: his personality, the way he controlled the suspect… it’s as if Orion came back to life, of course that is impossible, but… they are so much alike”.

    Mandy smiles, “I think he’d do you good,” she says.

    Trevor nods, smiling wider, “yeah,” he says before looking at the nurse, “do it… chip him!”

    The nurse beams, “right away,” she says, walking over to a cabinet and takes out a syringe, then returns to the table, and a second later the dog is chipped, “now we only need to register him, we can do that right now”.

    Trevor smiles, “that would be great,” he says.

    “Have you thought of a name,” Mandy asks.

    Trevor thinks for a while, he then smiles, “how about “Atlas”,” he asks, “I think that is a strong and powerful name, which suits him well!”

    Mandy and the nurse smile, “I agree,” Mandy says, the nurse nods.

    Trevor looks at the dog, “do you like that name, Atlas?”

    The dog turns its head and lets out a bark, wagging its tail.

    All three chuckle, “I think he approves,” Mandy and the nurse say.

    Trevor grins, “looks that way,” he says, “Atlas it is then!”

    The nurse nods and in just a matter of minutes, Trevor is the legal and official owner of the dog, unaware that the dog is in reality an 11-year-old boy who had been transformed into an Malinois-boy, then transformed into a real dog without his knowledge and that he is being controlled by some stranger.

    Back at the hotel, Brian smirks, ‘hehe… I don’t mind Trevor being the owner of the “dog”, with this app, I can always make myself a pet,’ he thinks to himself, ‘let’s let Trevor take his new pet home, I’ll cause Max to memorize the address. I’ll then have Max return to the hotel and convention center, he’ll think that everything was a dream, however, he’ll recognize Trevor whenever he sees him again, and he’ll also feel the need to return to Trevor’s address’.

    An hour later, Trevor arrives home, “welcome to your new home, Atlas,” he says, smiling when the dog starts sniffing and exploring the place. Trevor’s home is a simple four room apartment, one room is used as an office, one as a bedroom, the biggest as the living room and the fourth as a guest room. The apartment also has an open kitchen with a medium sized dining table, and a small bathroom.

    “Let me show you where you’ll be sleeping,” Trevor says, leading the dog into the bedroom.

    Brian can see a large dog bed and some toys in one of the corners.

    “Tomorrow, we’ll get you your official tag with your name and my contacts, in case you decide to runaway again,” Trevor then says.

    This gives Brian an idea, ‘I think I’ll keep Max as a dog for at least the weekend,’ he thinks to himself, ‘that way his mind will cause him to remember exactly where Trevor lives and cause him to bond to the cop. Once he’s back to normal, he’ll feel the strong need to seek out Trevor, hehe… this should be fun!’

    So for the next two days, Brian controls Max, making sure the “dog” memorizes everything, and when the opportunity arrives, Brian causes “Atlas” to sneak away and return to the hotel.

    Once Brian had transformed Max back into his normal anthro self, he uses the app to conjure some pants around the Malinois-boy’s waist. He then gives Max the full control back.

    Max blinks, scratching his head, “what the… hell happened,” he mumbles to himself before shrugging and simply enters the hotel.
