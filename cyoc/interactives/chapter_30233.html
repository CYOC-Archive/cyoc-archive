He slipped it on over his own head, it too seemed lined with the silky, pleasure inducing substance. John found that he could see and breathe perfectly fine. When he opened his mouth, the suit's opened with it. 'This is so awesome, but gah, I feel so horny!' John thought, rubbing at his crotch. 'I wonder if it's the suit or if it's just me.'
