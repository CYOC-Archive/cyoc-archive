The vulture then said "I propose an agreement. A deal." Curious the fox asked "What?" "We keep the secret that we seen each other at the same school. That we might have an idea of who each other are. "It's very clear that we are both animals regardless of whether or not we are wearing our Animal Costumes. That we prefer to be animals. And I want to keep our relationship that way." You're just a fox to me. And frankly I don't even care where you get these animals we're both eating from." The fox was pleased by that he didn't want to explain anything. The vulture then went to say their name but the fox grabbed her beak holding it shut and telling her almost in a growl "I don't want to know your name. You were just a vulture to me. As I am just a fox to you. I don't want us to use stupid names for ourselves. I am a fox through & through. We animals don't have names nor should we." The fox let go of the vulture's beak and she chuckled a bit saying "You're right. We're animals. Names are irrelevant to us." "Good. We understand each other then." The fox said pleased. The vulture then stated "I'm going to feral form now so I can eat this." The fox told her being very stern "I don't want to ever talk to you again. We're animals. We shouldn't be communicating like humans." The vulture chuckled at this saying "No problem. I prefer to be feral when I'm a vulture. You will never see me again as an anthro." The fox told her being very serious about that "You and I are not going to be living together or anything. I don't want company. I don't want a friend so we won't be. And I sure as hell don't want you perched or anything around my house." She told the fox with a chuckle "I have my own place to roost. I don't hang around your place. And as I stated a minute ago you will not ever see me in anthro form again and we will not be talking again. Also I won't tell anybody I saw you at the school or you're an Animal Costume User like me. I will just treat it like we never met in the school." The vulture nodded agreeing to that. The fox then shifted back to his feral form and walked off. As he stated he doesn't care who the hell the vulture might be. She's just an animal as much as he is. And of course for the vulture it's the same.
