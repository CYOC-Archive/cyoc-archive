    Why should a god play by the rules of a bitch?

    Sitting on the steps in the little tube in the play area, the realization of my situation suddenly hits me: I've been played. There's no way in hell I'll be able to hide from a werewolf's hightened senses for half an hour in a park! Kelly knew this when she got the idea for this little "game" of hers. She just wants the ring, but she can't have it. It's mine! This is a ring that grants wishes, with seemingly no limit, and now she's going to win this game, and I'll have to- oh wait, I don't have to do anything. I'm a god, I gave her this form and I can take it away. Yes... "'No cheating' she says..." I mutter under my breath. "Who's going to stop me? Who CAN stop me? Not Kelly, that's for sure." I make a few wishes while I have the time, and crawl out of the tube.

    She doesn't keep me waiting for very long, barely a minute after my head-start has ended, I hear her rustling through some nearby bushes. She's big, she's strong, she's fast, but stealthy she is not. I look the other way, pretend I don't know she's there, take a few steps backwards, moving closer to her. A few seconds later, I can feel her breath on my neck, I step backwards, bumping into her. Feigning surprise, I whirl around and look into her yellow eyes. "Found you!" she pratically yells, placing her powerful paws on my shoulders. I breathe out, why act scared? I'm the one in control, I may as well show it. "It seems you did." I say in a calm tone, Kelly's still smiling, showing off her canine teeth. She holds out a hand. "I win, and now for my prize." I keeping looking into her eyes, she's actually expecting me to comply? "No." She looks at me in a mix of surprise and anger. "What do you mean 'no'? We both agreed to this. Give me the ring!" I gave her a cruel smile. "Why don't you make me?"

    The taunting tone in my voice is just enough to spark her anger. Good. She raises her arm to strike me, not with her claws, she probably just wants to show how much stronger than me she is, send me flying a little bit. When the strike hits, nothing happens. All I feel is a light tap on my cheek, even though she clearly put some force behind it. That wish worked, good. "What?" She looks at me, very confused, then it hits her. "You used the ring!" My smile grows wider. "Yes. I wished that you would never be able to harm me, no matter how hard you tried. You know what else I wished? My Little Human Bitch?" The moment I finish my sentence, Kelly doubles over in pain, as her transformation rapidly reverting. Her bones snap and crackele as they rapidly shrink, her great muscles pulsating as they too wither away, her fur falls off her body, leaving a pile around her, a testament to what she had been moments earlier. Kelly screams in pain as the now fur-less tail forces it's way back into her body, leaving her naked and crying in a pile of wolf fur.

    "As you can see, I made a wish that whenever I refer to you as "My Little Human Bitch", your werewolf transformation would immediately revert itself, turning you back into a human. Oh, and I made sure to emphasize just how painful it would be. I bet you felt that part." Kelly is still crying, she isn't moving at all, aside from her shaking shoulders. I crouch down next to her. "Now you've felt first hand what I can do with this ring. I can make your wildest dreams come true, and I'll gladly do that, as long you do as I say. But I can also make every moment of your life a living hell, which I'll do the moment you step out of line. I am a god now, Kelly. YOUR god! I hope you understand that." With that, I get up and wish for some clothes to appear piled up neatly next to her, and wait.

    This ring has extraordinary power... It seems I might really be a god. Perhaps I should test it out some more?
