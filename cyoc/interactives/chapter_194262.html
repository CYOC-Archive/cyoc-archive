    It is mounted on a shelf and is the only one of its kind that you only of. You grab the wooden rod and take it down for a closer look. The wood has been dyed a much blacker color and treated with something to make it shiny, and instead of the normal bristles at the business end, a collection of feathers are there instead. You wonder if this is some new kind of invention as it reminds you of a feather duster, only broom sized.

    "My, I see you have an eye for the interesting. Very well, I shall gift her to you, seeing as how she seems to have chosen you already."

    You turn around to see a hooded person standing behind you. There eyes are fully covered by their hood, but their figure and the sound of their voice at least lets you know that they are a woman.

    "Excuse me? Who are you, and I can keep this for free?" You say confused and a bit startled. The woman nods at your response and smiles. "Who am I indeed. I am no one of importance to you, and you need not worry about that anyways, since this should be our one and only time ever meeting. How about this, if we see each other again, I will at least tell you my name at that time" She says with a smile.

    "Ok..." You look back down at the weird broom again and hold it in front of you as if you are handing it to her. She pushes it back to you and shakes her head. "Keep her, she's all yours. Treat her well and she will return it back to you. Abuse her and she will curse you further."

    You give the woman another weird look of confusion. "I'm sorry, who are we talking about?"

    "Her name is Odette and you are holding her right now. Once one of the greatest witches I knew, but she turned herself into a magical device in order to escape her own boredom. And now she is this magical feathered broom of yours. She may be a bit on the eccentric side, but she usually is a nice lady. But you must treat her right, as she tends to not let insults and misuse slide without punishment."

    You shake your head at this answer. "I see, well then please take her back. I don't want to add anymore unnecessary difficulties into my life." Your hands begins to shake at this and you find yourself incapable of releasing the feathered broom. Somehow you feel as though you have just offended it, which seems weird to you since how can an object be offended?

    "Hmm, well, at least go easy on him this time Odette, he is still new to all of this after all" Says the hooded woman who then turns to leave. You feel as if the feathered broom has somehow calmed down at this, and the vibrating stops as well. But you still feel as though it is somehow glaring daggers at you. It's very off putting. You attempt to put it back when you suddenly find yourself sinking into the floor, which has turned all shimmery in a circle around your feet. You also cannot drop the feathered broom either.

    Once you pass through the floor, you find yourself standing in a large grand hall. It looks like the foyer of some large mansion. The other main concern you find yourself with, is that you are now no longer holding the feathered broom, but instead are being glared at by a young woman who looks like she is in her early twenties. She is minimally dressed, as just a few draped bits of thin, practically see through fabric cover her most intimate areas. A few of the same feathers from the feathered broom now are poking out of her hair as they are being used to style it. Her figure is very good too, and the fabric leaves almost nothing to the imagination.

    "So, I am just an added difficulty huh? So be it, I can make your life difficult easily." The woman then hits you in the stomach with a weird blue orb that she makes appear in her free hand. You double over and hold your stomach whike the woman let's go of you and begins floating in front of you in the air, while taking a more relaxed pose. It's like she is laying down in a lounge chair as she watches you try to catch your breath from having the wind knocked out of you. The concerning part of all of this though, is the grin she now has. It brings chills to you over and over again. They radiate throughout your entire body too. But then you notice something about yourself. You are changing...

    What are you changing into?
