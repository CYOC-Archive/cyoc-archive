Under the Blue, Pink, and Yellow, you see the letters Cl. Under Blue-Cl you see the letters; S(shirts), P(pants), Sh(shorts), B(boxers), U(underwear), SS(shoes). Under Pink-Cl you see the letters; S(shirts), P(pants), Sh(shorts), U(Underwear), T(Thong), SK(skirt), B(bikini), BR(Bra).
