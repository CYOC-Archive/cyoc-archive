    After taking as little of the potion as she could, Rachel places the bottle on the floor next to the package and waits for the results. Fortunately Rachel didn't have to wait too long as her body began to shrink, along with her Alice in Wonderland outfit.
    "Aaa, that feels better." Rachel said as she began stretching her shrinking body and limbs. As Rachel reached her original size, she immediately stopped shrinking. Rachel then picks herself off the floor and straightens out her dress.
    "Glad that's over with, though it's gonna be a pain to move all my furniture back into place." Rachel then looks over her outfit to make sure everything was as it should be.
    "This really is an amazing costume, definitely worth keep- OH CRAP." As Rachel admired her new attire, she accidentally knocks over the Drink Me Bottle and spilling onto the package. Rachel picks up the bottle and was about to clean up the mess when the liquid suddenly evaporates and the package instantly shrinks to nothingness.
    "Well that stinks. I just lost my magic package on top of wasting the only shrinking potion in the world." Rachel said depressingly. Rachel then locates the bottle's cork and proceeds to push it back into place. As soon as Rachel had finished, the bottle instantly began to refill itself with liquid and was soon back to being full.
    "Woah, that's pretty useful. Now I never have to worry about running out ever again." Rachel said looking at the bottle in amazement and placing it in her right apron's pocket for safekeeping.
    "But wait a minute. If the bottle can instanly refill, then that means..." Rachel then climbs over her bed and grabs the box she left on the nightstand. Rachel opens the box and find another cupcake inside.
    "Aha, I knew it. Hmm, I should find a save place for this. It's a shame I can't carry it with me like the bottle. ... Or can I?" Rachel take out the bottle, pulls the cork out and lets a tiny drop fall onto the box. Almost instantly, the box shrunk to the same size as the bottle. Rachel then opened the box and sees the cupcake completely unharmed, aside from being smaller. Satisfied, Rachel closed both items and placed them into her apron's pockets. After getting off the bed, Rachel started planning her next move.
    "Now that I can change whenever I want, what am I gonna do next?"
