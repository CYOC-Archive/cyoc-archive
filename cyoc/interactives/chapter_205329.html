    "What's your favorite food?"

    Ruse knew she should be thinking of a way to get away but instead, that more cardinal side of her found a way to speak for her.

    "You.~"

    Serena's surprised holographic expression on her smooth manikin face was almost too scrumptious. But more so was her smile.

    "You know what? That's not a bad idea. If I fuse with you... I should still be human enough to move around and pretend to be you. And you will get your favorite food! How does that sound my darling dumb sandwich?~"

    'INITIATING DIFFUSION PROCESS'

    With a hearty moan, Ruse felt her beloved melons separate from her as the mechanical arms pull them away. But just as quickly, the arms moved Ruse's body and position her right in front of Serena's motionless robotic manikin body and gently press the two bodies against each other.

    Ruse can practically feel Serena's grin behind her. "Of course, there is a risk that I could loose myself in our merged mind, but having access to your memories could have the benefit of confirming what you said about the current state of the world to be true."

    'INITIATING FUSION AND TRANSFERRAL PROCESS'.

    . . .

    She does a few stretches as she experiments with her new self. Luckily, she still looks very much human from the outside. But only from a distance. Upon closer inspection, her skin gives off a glossy fake look. And she can feel her internal organs being structured like that of a android but with organic material.

    Now... who is she? Ruse? Serena? What does it matter? For now, she'll just call herself Ruse, and try to complete both of her objectives, to complete Ruse's job and get Serena's revenge.

    But before "Ruse" leaves the storage room that she spent literal decades living in, she gotta check something first.

    A compartment door opens up in her torso. From her personal storage pocket dimension, she pulls out the watermelon and peanut butter coated bread that was once part of her. She placed the bread on top of the watermelon.

    'INITIATING FUSION PROCESS'.

    As if automatically, Ruse spoke the command words. And in a flesh of light, it was done. At first glance, it looks like nothing has changed, but when she pulled at the bread, she finds that it's firmly attached too the watermelon, as if it's part of it.

    Smirking her lips as if it been years since she done so, she places the weird hybrid food back into her storage pocket dimension compartment and pulls out something out. It's a glove made out of bread. Ruse puts on the glove and begins the experiment.

    'INITIATING FUSION PROCESS'.

    And just like that, her hand is now made of bread. After a few experimental flexes, Ruse let out a satisfied grin.

    'INITIATING DIFFUSION PROCESS'

    Ruse places the now separated bread glove back into her dimensional compartment.

    And with that, she approaches the exit. If feels weird. Having memories of both freedom and isolation. But now she enact her plan and get some mercenary money on the side! Regardless of who she is, both sides of her are brimming with excitement and slight bit of arousal!
