    “Oh yes, Master!” Peter replied immediately. “It’s such a major rush…”

    “Would you be willing to help him begin his training as a Transformation Wizard?”

    “Absolutely, Master! I’ll do anything for Allen!” Peter exclaimed.

    Prince frowned as he walked towards Peter, showing a very serious expression on his face.

    “Would you be willing to give up something, so that Allen can begin his training as a Wizard?” Prince asked him. “Would you make a sacrifice for him?”

    “Yes! What would I need to sacrifice?”

    “Would you give up your powers for Allen?” Prince asked him in all seriousness.

    “Huh?” Allen wondered out loud.

    “What do you mean, Master?” Peter asked him.

    “I will remove your powers and transfer them to Allen!” Prince replied calmly, as he stared at both young men.

    “Oh wow!” Allen cheered. “That means I’d be just like Ty? And, would I become just as strong as him?”

    “Yes and no.” Prince answered. “You’ll be even stronger than Ty… But, your powers will still be restricted to only Peter and Tim!”

    “But… Why?” Allen asked him.

    “You’ll learn your power, but you’ll also learn the responsibility that comes with it. You’ll be their guardian and their protector. You can make them shrink and grow, but no change will be permanent. You’ll watch over them and protect them! In the meantime, you’ll use your powers on them and only on those two!”

    “Sure! I will watch over them! I’ll watch over my men all the time!” Allen replied with a smile.

    “What about you, Peter? Are you willing to make this sacrifice?” Prince asked him.

    Peter bowed his head and pondered for a moment. Then he raised up his head and turned to face his Master.

    “Yes, I’ll give up my powers for Allen!” he replied. “He’s my best friend and I trust him!”

    “But… Will I be able to see where Peter is? Could I see what he’s doing?”

    “Yes! You’ll be with him, or inside of him, 24-7” Prince responded. “You’ll be their bodyguard from now on! I’ll talk to Ty myself, and I’ll ask him to help you with your training!”

    Then prince took a step back, and looked on at the two youngsters.

    “Alright! It will be done! Your training begins now!” Prince shouted, as he raised his arms up high above his head. Suddenly, lightning bolts flashed from the palms of his hands and struck Peter and Allen. There was a loud explosion, and then Prince disappeared, leaving the two young men in a cloud of thick smoke.

    Peter blinked. Then he realized that everything seemed different to him. He was normal; he no longer had his power! Then, he turned towards Allen, who had a wide grin on his face.

    “I see you; I can feel you; I hear your thoughts and know your feelings… This is gonna be awesome!” Allen cheered.

    Peter and Allen just stared at each other for a moment.
