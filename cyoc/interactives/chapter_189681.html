    Todd quickly scrolled through the remote's options, knowing he had little time. With so many alluring options though, it was hard to pick, especially if he wanted to keep Nancy's transformations on the down-low. The long coat gave him some leeway though, and after a few moments of considering his options he settled on his choice.
    The remote had a duplication control, and it took only a moment to set it to something that would probably be quite fun: arms. With the remote set, he aimed it at Nancy and activated it.
    Nancy slowed as she felt the transformation begin and a pressure seemed to form on either side of her chest under her arms. Her eyes widened as she realized that something new was happening. She reached her right hand to her left side, and sure enough she could feel something firm protruding from her torso, growing longer with each second. As it quickly took up space, stretching her already tattered shirt, she realized she could move it and pressed it against her side. The motion instantly felt familiar, cluing her in to what was happening. With the realization, she could only watch in horrified fascination as her new arms formed.
    When they finished, Nancy could make out their outline underneath her shirt, glad that Todd's coat otherwise concealed them. Less than eager for someone else to see her new additions though, she pulled the jacket closed across her front and then looked back. "Todd?" she called, noticing him still by the library entrance.
    Todd straightened his posture as he feigned surprise. "Yeah?" he asked, beginning to walk towards her.
    "Something, uh... something else happened," she said, her voice quieting as he got nearer.
    "What?" he asked. He eyed the way she was holding his jacket closed in front of her. "What happened?"
    "I got new arms!" Nancy hissed. "I can't go back to my apartment! My roommates will freak the hell out!"
    "New arms?" Todd asked, putting on an alarmed look.
    Nancy nodded and then looked around briefly, making sure no one else was near to see before opening the jacket so he could see. She moved them under her shirt, their forms clearly visible under the fabric and through the holes torn into it. She brought one up through her cleavage to peek out from the collar of shirt and give him a timid yet playful wave.
    "Oh, whoa," Todd said, pulling his head back.
