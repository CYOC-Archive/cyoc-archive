A similarly colored tail was growing from the base of her spine, and she grabbed it with her clearly feline hands. "No way!" she exclaimed. Kicking her new catty feet out of her shorts, Tricia bounded the rest of the way to her mirror.

Her eyes had turned yellow, with black vertical slits for pupils. She smiled with a mouthful of little sharp teeth.

She jumped around on the bed and even tried walking on all fours. Her loose panties fell off during the activities, and she ended up removing her bra because it was swinging uncomfortably against her fur. Thinking for a moment, she crouched down and pushed off the floor with all her strength. She soared up high, landing with a dull thump on top of the dresser. She knocked a few picture frames and a case of lipstick to the floor, but at least she had the remote back in her young little paws. It was bigger to her now, and heavier. She couldn't believe that she had changed into an eight-year-old catgirl in just a matter of minutes!
