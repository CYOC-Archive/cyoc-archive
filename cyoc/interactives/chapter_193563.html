    A fluid that changes everything

    added A year ago O

    In the box, she found a vial filled with a strange fluid. As Megan looked at the fluid, she was entranced by the mottling of the different colors. Megan snapped out of her trance long enough to see that even though she liked what it was, she had no idea who it was for.

    That is until she saw a letter at the bottom of the box. Megan read out loud what the letter said. "To the one who opened the package, the contents of this vial will give you the most pleasant dream. In said dream, you will become your favorite fantasy creature or the animal of your dreams. The choice will be yours after you drink it. Afterwards, you will wake up as whatever it is you dreamed of. Courtesy of Life Changers "

    It was then Megan remembered she had signed up for that in college. She knew what she wanted to become...
