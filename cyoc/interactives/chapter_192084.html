    Natalia and Reiko had decided to take a stroll through the moonlight. Reiko had stated she knew of a beautiful spot that would give them a lovely view of the night sky. "Demons," Natalia exclaimed. The two teens stumbled, quaking in fear as the group of vampires now advanced slowly on their meal.

    A crack like thunder now rang out, Natalia covering her ears as her eyes grew wide with fear. Something had crashed to the ground in front of them. As the dust settled, Natalia now beheld the image of a winged woman.

    "An archangel," Natalia exclaimed excitedly. "Like St. Michael! Reiko look God has sent an archangel to protect us!"

She had at first decided to call Katie's sister Ann but after discovering the two teens had wandered off, she called Katie instead. You two stay behind me," Katie said.

    "BEGONE!"

The vampires screamed in agony, burned by the holy light before bursting into flame. As Katie now sheathed Excalibur, all that was left was ash that floated in the wind. "You two alright?"

    The teens nodded, Natalia reaching a hand up timidly towards Katie. "Are you an archangel like St. Michael?"

    "Um no," Katie replied. "I'm Katie."
