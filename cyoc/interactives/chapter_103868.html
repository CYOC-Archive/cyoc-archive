    The Vials

    Fluupering your way back to King Arthurs Court

    added by AnonyMouse 7 years ago A O

    Well it was going to take a lot more then pixie dust to get rid of the evil in
    Sarah's dragon soul.

    This was a flame breathing western dragon after all.

    However it just so happened that the towns resident crackpot inventor
    Professor MacGee,had gotten over trying to make Model T Fords fly with
    fluuper,and was currently working out the machinery and the physics
    equations for time travel.

    What MacGee had discovered was that to go into the past or future,you
    needed to be holding not just something from that year,but something
    that had been "fractalized" as the physicists put it,or
    "magicked" as fairies might put it.

    There weren't too many fractalized things lying around town.

    All had seemed lost,but MacGee and his off again on again assistant
    Biff the Football star had been marching closer to Sarah's ruined farm
    with the Fractal-o-meter when the red light on top began blinking and the various needles began redlining.

    Clearly they'd hit paydirt.

    So in due course they came upon Sarah's egg and the Professor
    solemnly announce the Fractal-o-meter
    readings indicated it was a object originating from around the year
    470 AD around the Northeast area of England.

    Biff was pretty impressed with that deep knowledge until MacGee pointed
    to the tag taped to the object with fairly delicate handwriting
    stating the object was a dragon's egg from 470AD from Camelot,
    ruling seat of Uther Pendragon.

    So the two carried the surprisingly heavy dragons egg to the
    Professor's mini-van and headed back into town with their
    discovery.

    Biff wasted no time telling his high school friends that he'd
    helped the Professor find the key ingredient for MacGee's
    machine,and soon a excited crowd of kids had
    gathered around the Professors flying saucer shaped
    conveyance,which could float and move about when fluuper was put
    in its various Motivator engines.

    They all were eager for a trip to King Arthurs Court.
