    Before the next ones in line could go forth, the Goddess Eris raised up a large palm, and ordered things to halt.

    "What's this?", Eris spoke, in a loud booming whisper. The goddess tilted her head, with an ear out into the crowd. "Are those the moans of sadness I hear?"

    David heard it too. Somewhere in the watching crowd, there was a little girl crying. He was confused. Shouldn't his wish cause everyone on the beach to be reverent to the Goddess? Or maybe the crying was for some other reason?

    " Well, that will not do!", Eris boomed. With a booming snap of Eris's fingers, two small figures were suddenly conjured onto the altar.

    Two little girls now stood before the Goddess, and were looking around, startled. They were both blonde and fair skinned, with the oldest one looked around 11 years old, and the youngest at 8 years. The two were in swimsuits, with the smallest girl wearing a frilly pink leotard, and the oldest girl wearing a child's yellow two-piece. And it seems the oldest was trying to comfort her youngest sister, who was still sobbing loudly.

    "Why hello there, little ones!" said Eris. "Why are you crying? Are you not pleased to be in my presence?"

    "G...Goddess Eris...." the youngest girl stammered, her face still wet with tears. The older sister held her close, and looked up at the Goddess.

    "Sorry, Ms. Goddess," said the older girl. "We're trying to look for our mom."

    "Oh dear," said Eris, with feigned concern. "And why would you be separated from your mother? Isn't she here?"

    "She was, but -"

    "Penny! Sally!", shouted a woman from the crowd. The woman made her way through between the lined-up watchers, and up the steps of the altar.

    "Mom!" "Mommy!" shouted the two sisters. The smallest girl ran up to the her mother, and they hugged tightly. There was a relieved applause amongst the goddess's followers. She sighed relief, while wiping the last bits of tears off her youngest's face.

    "Thank goodness you're both alright.", said the woman. The mother was blonde like her daughters, and looked around her late 30s. Her bikini revealed a slim figure, with a pair of sizable D-cup breasts. She bowed towards the towering Eris, with deepest of reverence.

    "My biggest apologies, my goddess! We got separated during the moving of the crowd. I hope you can forgive us!"

    Eris smiled. "Ah yes, I'm glad you reunited with your children. Now, what are your names?"

    "Debra Klein," said the woman. "This is my youngest daughter Sally, and Penny."

    Sally shyly hid behind her mother's legs, while Penny waved nervously towards the goddess. Eris smiled. "Such a pretty family, with such love for one another. Tell me, woman...what does your children mean to you?"

    Debra Klein gently patted her youngest's head, and touched Penny's shoulder. "They're everything to me. If I ever lost them, I'd be heartbroken."

    "How beautiful." said Eris. "And children...what do you think of your mother? Do you love her most greatly?"

    "Yes, Ms. Goddess!", said Penny, beaming.

    "And you, little Sally?" Eris bent her head forth, and gave a teasing smile at the shiest daughter. "You can speak up, little one. Don't be afraid. Can you say that you love your mother?"

    Sally slowly came out from her mother's legs, and looked up at the giant goddess. The little girl gave a sheepish smile. "Mhm....I love Mommy."

    There were loud adoring 'awww's around the crowd. Debra blushed, and smiled lovingly at her daughters.

    "Such love for one another!" Eris praised. "Yes...I believe I have a most appropriate gift to bestow upon you!"

    "Ooo," the older daughter, Penny, raised her hand. "Are...are we going to change too?"

    Eris laughed. "Yes, child, you shall be changed. I will ensure all of you will stay together, and will never lose one another again! Now, if you three are ready?"

    "Yes my goddess! We're happy to accept your gift!", said Debra. Both Sally and Penny nodded eagerly.

    "Wonderful!", said Eris. The Goddess snapped her fingers.

    Instantly, the family's swimsuits dissolved into dust, revealing their nude backsides to the goddess's watching worshippers. Debra and her kids didn't pay too much mind, though their cheeks did turn a shade of pink.

    Debra held her girls close, and both Penny and Sally hugged her sides, with excited smiles. The three awaited whatever change the Goddess had in store.
