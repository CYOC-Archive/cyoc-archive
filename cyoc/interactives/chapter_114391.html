    So it was that the Quee-Priestess and 33 of her dragon ladies
    trotted over to the cave.

    It was fairly obvious that they weren't human,since though they were human
    looking above the shoulders
    everything south of those shoulders was that of a pear shaped dragoness
    walking about on two legs and with strong gripping forearms with scaly hands with opposable claws.

    Their allegiance was also easy enough to figure out,since the Queen-Priestess wore on her head a iron helm with a image of Lord Arumbis
    rivited on.

    So,doubting that it work anyways,you greeted the Quuen-Priestess
    rather then trying to tf her into a complete feral dragoness or some other such foolishness.

    You invited the thirty four dragon ladies into the cave,and soon the cave was
    crowded.,partially because they were a lot bigger then they looked at a distance.

    Lord Arumbis had of course contacted them about your arrival,and so it
    was that the 34 dragon ladies explained the political situation
    on planet Dracos.

    Basically the dragon ladies had once been human,and had worshipped Arumbis
    along with the guys,the Knights of the Draconic Order.

    They'd all started as telepathic human dragon riders,and used to go flying about in the Temple
    with teleportation and telepathic capable feral wild
    dragons.(Dragon Riders of Pern anyone?)

    So the lot of them and their Temple and those dragons had been engaged
    in a awfully long teleport jump from one star system to another.

    Somebody had botched up the navigation and what should have been a 33 minute jump in the freezing cold of
    'tween had turned into a three day horror.

    The wild dragons had all died by the sixth day from exposure so it was a awfully
    good time for someone to call apon Lord Arumbis for help.

    The guys had called him up first and made a deal.

    So when the Queen-Priestess had called up Lord Arumbis and asked her God to give her and the other ninety nine ladies the power of teleportation and telepathy the dead wild
    dragons had once had,she was glad to have Lord Arumbis grant the powers
    to her and thirty three other ladies.

    So the chosen 34 had teleported the Temple and themselves and everyone else from
    'tween point to 'tween point.

    Till they all popped up back into existence high up in the atmosphere of planet
    Dracos.

    The Queen-Priestess and her 33 trusted followers came in for a nasty surprise as their bodies started getting bigger and stprouting scales
    the second the Temple arrive in Dracos.

    The 34 were too busy dealing with their own
    rearranging bodies to keep track of the Temple,
    and it had plunged to the surface far below and got smashed to pieces.

    The guys meanwhile,seemed to have acquired teleportation powers,which they hadn't told the Queen-Priestess
    about,and had teleported themselves and their lovers to safety.

    Which left thirty one women who simply died when the Temple crashed to bits on the ground.

    The thirty four Knights of the Order had made a earlier deal to give them the power.

    The price Lord rumbis had demanded was that an equal number
    of women would be turned into feral wild dragonesses
    to balance the books.

    So here was the still transforming Queen-Priestess and her thirty three cursed
    dragon ladies with a mission for you.
