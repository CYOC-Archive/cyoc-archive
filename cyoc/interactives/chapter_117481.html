    Matt was surprised at how easily Alexis agreed, but she suspected that Alexis might not completely believe Matt about the Chronivac yet.

    "Okay, first let's set up the model," Matt said, and clicked on "Load 3-D Design." Two models then popped up on the screen, one of Matt in her female form and one of Alexis. Having learned from her earlier mistake, Matt closed her own model, then quickly saved a copy of Alexis' current model.

    "Wow...now that's...surreal..." Alexis gaped, stunned by the detail and accuracy of the model of her body. Her eyes widened when Matt hid the layer with with her clothes. Even though Alexis still might not completely believe Matt's explanation of the Chronivac, she was definitely convinced about its scanning abilities.

    Having gone through most of the options before when she created her new form, Matt quickly flew through the initial selection. Selecting "other" under the title of "gender," Matt was rewarded with a model that, while having Alexis' basic dimensions, was completely androgynous. Using other editing tools, Matt quickly gave the model female breasts but male genitals.

    "How does that look?" Matt asked, pushing back from the desk.

    "That's all?" Alexis sputtered. "What a waste! What is this, pin the penis on the woman? C'mon, put at least some effort into it!" Matt gave Alexis an odd look, but finally shook her head.

    "It's your body, you know..." Matt started.

    "That's right, so why not make it into a real work of art?" Alexis stated. While Alexis had no love for computers, there was no denying her eye for beauty. Without missing a beat, Alexis began to give detailed orders while scrutinizing the model, and Matt struggled to keep up. As the changes to the model began to pile up, Matt couldn't help but acknowledge that Alexis was right. Matt's version of her new lover had been rather crude and slapdash, but under Alexis' direction the model was quickly turning into a creature which could easily bewitch any person, regardless of gender or sexual orientation. Finally, Alexis stepped back, and giving a final squint, said, "There, that should do it."

    Matt exhaled and looked in wonderment at the model. It was far beyond anything he could have imagined, and he was becoming more and more curious as to what it would appear like in real life. Matt quickly saved a new copy under a different filename. Having explored much of the program by this point, Matt pulled up an option she wished she had found yesterday, which said, "Apply Temporarily" with a timer. Setting the timer for 168 hours, Matt paused and looked back at Alexis.

    "You sure about this?" Matt asked one last time.

    "Let's see what this Chronivac can do!" Alexis grinned and nodded. Matt still wasn't sure if Alexis believed her, but she was about to get proof. Taking a deep breath, Matt returned to the timer window and hit "Confirm: Start Timer."
