    headband, she noticed what looked like small earmuffs attached to the sides of the headband. This must be for
    covering her ears so that the cat ears would stand out more. What really impressed her were the ears
    themselves since they appeared identical to actual cat ears. Taking the headband, she stood in front of her
    full length mirror mounted on her closet door, and put it on.

    Immediately, she noticed a problem: there was no "fur" on the hairband itself or the earmuff, which managed to
    cover her ears without the sides of her head look ridiculous. Because of this, people who see her wearing this
    will immediately know she's wearing a headband. sighing in disappointment, she was about to take off the
    headband when her head started to hurt suddenly. the aching was mostly centered at her ears as it seemed like
    everything went silent to the point that she couldn't even her own moaning. looking at the mirror again,
    Jessica's eyes widened as it appeared that her hair was growing over the headband, as well as the earmuffs,
    but on a closer look, it appeared that her very skin was growing and spreading over it, leaving only the cat
    ears exposed. then suddenly, Jessica felt a sharp pain as she could suddenly hear again, only the sound of her
    room's fan seemed louder then before. Recovering from what just happened, Jessica decided to take a look her
    head. Other than the cat ears, there appeared to be no trace of the head band. She felt the sides of her head
    for her ears, only to feel nothing but her own hair, and smooth skin underneath. Starting to feel scared, she
    put a hand on one of the cat ears resting at the top of her head, and when she touch one of them, she not only
    felt it, but the ear was twitching.

    "OH MY GOOOOOOD!" Jessica screamed, "Oh god, what's happened to me? Why do I have cat ears? Why oh why?"

    Desperate for answers, She looked in the box that the costume was in, and found a note at the bottom of this.
    According to this note, this "full body costume" is imbued with a magic that turns the wearer into whatever
    the costume is supposed to represent, both anthro and non-morphic versions if it's an animal. The note also
    states that costume is seperated into multiple pieces in order to ease the wearer through the transformation
    without too much stress, so it is ill advised to put it all on at once. It also says that in order to revert
    the transformation and take off the costume, the wearer must simply concentrate on his or her human form.

    After reading the note, Jessica sat down at her desk, and thought about what just happened to her. This
    costume was meant to turn her into some sort of cat creature. that news alone would freak her out, but knowing
    that she could turn back easily managed to put her mind at ease. Still, she couldn't believe it. She heard
    some of the stories about the store she bought this costume from, but she didn't think she would actually turn
    into some cat girl. Then it hit her. The moment Jessica thought of the words "cat girl" a smile had spread
    across her face. Going back into the mirror, she stared at her new cat ears as they twitch on the tap of her
    head, and thought how adorable they looked, and now that she knows what to expect, she decides to put on
    another part of the costume, which would be...
