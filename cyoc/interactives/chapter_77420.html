    Jaffar finished reading the manual and looked for the mental change vial. He found more than one. There was one for almost every aspect of the personality. One vial was for
    obedience, and another one was for changing the mind to fit the body, which was called "Mental Match". He grabbed these and went over to Aden. He filled the dropper from the
    obedience dropper and held it over his father's muzzle. Jaffar emptied the dropper and put it down.
    "Hey pops wake up!"
    Aden woke with a start. He tried to stand, but lost this wasn't possible. he slipped off the bed and landed on all fours. This is when he noticed his changes. His cries for help
    were in vain, as he could only bark.
    "Down pops!"
    Aden stopped barking and dropped to the ground. He tried his best to get up, but his body wouldn't move.
    "Good boy Aden. Now come over here and sit."
    Aden did as he was told.
    "Well dad, I've always wanted a dog. Thanks for helping me get one! I decided I'd let you keep your mind, that way I can have the smartest dog ever!"
    Aden growled furiously at his son.
    "Quiet!" Aden instantly stopped. "Looks like you need some training. Well from now on, you'll never try to hurt me, got it?" Aden felt his body restrain him. Dread and fear washed
    over him. He was entirely at his son's mercy.
    "Furthermore..." said Jaffar, "Your gonna take a drop of mental change now. We wouldn't want you feeling uncomfortable with your new body. Open up!"
    Aden reluctantly opened his mouth. Jaffar put a drop of the mental match. Aden's felt parts of his human mind melt away; only to be replaced canine instinct. He twitched as his
    brain was remapped to work his new body. Aden knew he needed to get away, he made a mad dash for the door.
    "Stay."
    Aden skidded to a stop.
    "You a little more training I guess let's see... Oh, I know. You're my dog, understand? I'm your master, and you're gonna love me with all your heart! Finally, you are to forget
    that you were ever human, until I tell you to remember."
    Aden Howled mournfully. He could feel it happening. Memories of his life became blurry and unrecognizable. His friends, coworkers, everything was going away. Except for his son.
    His every thought of his son soon became that of undying love and admiration. The final human memory disappeared, and Aden was gone. "Aden" shook his tail violently as he stood
    there looking at his master. His one and only master. He whined pitifully. He couldn't move unless told so.
    "Okay boy, come 'ere!" Said Jaffar.
    Aden tackled his master and licked him all over.
    "Hmmm... You're rather old by dog standards. I think I'll fix that."
    With a few drops of another vial, Aden was now at the peak of his virility.
    Jaffar took a few dropps of the Mental Match vial for himself. He twitched his head before looking at Aden.
    "Hey boy, you'll be a great dog, but first I'd like you to be something else for a bit..."
