    His answer was that he was pretty Neko girl with soft white fur covering her ears and white furry tail extending down from her tail bone. This was all complemented by her strawberry blonde hair.

    A strange sensation could be felt at Samantha's ears and tail bone, but she choose to ignore it, having too much fun with the magic letter. Before her eyes the words disappeared and the next question formed.

    Question 11: Missus Zeiss....Are you a female Neko or a male Neko?

    Silently giggling because of what had just happened to her husband, she took the pen and wrote down that she was a male Neko. He had light brown coloured fur on his ears and tail; which went well with his light brown hair.

    Question 12: Samantha, honey, who are your favourite childhood icons?

    Taking the pen, Samantha began to right that she played with her dollies, mostly Barbie and Ken. She also liked Rainbow Bright, Strawberry Shortcake, Looney Tunes and My Little Pony.

    Question 13: Zach, who are your favourite childhood icons?

    Zach answered that he liked He-Man, Ghostbusters, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Looney Tunes and Thunder Cats. He once and awhile played with G.I. Joe.

    Question 14: Zach, what are you wearing?

    Zach thought about it and then answered that he was wearing a T.M.N.T t-shirt, a pair of underwear with spiderman on it, a pair of shorts and his favourite sneakers.

    Question 15: Samantha, what are you wearing.

    Taking the pen, she wrote down that she was wearing a pink skirt, a t-shirt that had a picture of a pony, Rainbow Bright panties and a pair of pink, white and teal sneakers.

    Question 16: Are the both of you housebroken?

    Samantha answered this by saying that she had been since the age of three and Zach had been sometime around the age of five. Pushing her light brown hair out of her eyes, she saw the next question form.

    Question 17: Are you two related to Elizabeth Zeiss? If so, how?

    Zach took the pen and wrote down that they were cousins on their mother's side.

    Question 18: Are you two related and how?

    Giggling some more, liking the Questionnaire, Samantha wrote down that they were fraternal Twins in a childish scrawl; though she did misspell the word Fraternal.

    New memories began to flood them and both brother and sister had to briefly close their eyes. Opening their eyes, they saw the next question form on the paper. As it form, Zach snatched the pen from his sister and said, "It's my turn to answer."

    Question 19: Were are you right now?

    Thinking about it, Zach wrote down that they were at home and mom had told them to go outside and play.

    Suddenly the Questionnaire went blank and after a bit, new words began to form onto the blank note. Zach and Samantha looked on, finding it harder and harder to read, seeing as they had never attended school yet, but would start in the fall. The last thing they read before it all slipped away was:

    You two rascals are supposed to be outside playing. You'll have an interesting, but happy life. Try to stay out of trouble. Oh, Zach. You look like the adventures type. Hide me were no one is sure to find me for good long time.

    Giggling like his sister, Zach had no idea what these words were suppose to mean. He understood what some of them meant, but not all of them. The message as a whole was lost. Scooping up the note, he watched as it went blank and then an idea came to him. Running through the house, he heard his mother telling him to stop it. Darting outside, Samantha right behind him, he ran to the sand box.

    Inside the sandbox, he began to dig. When he had good size hole, he placed the note inside and whispering conspiratorially to his sister, told her, "This is the map of a pirate. We will hide it here until we are ready to dig up the chest."

    Nodding, Samantha watched as her brother buried the piece of paper. Afterward, the two ran off to play. Within an hour the note was forgotten.
