    After a while an idea pops into Jeff’s mind, his twin brother Ben, who is only 10 minutes younger than him, even though they are twins, Jeff and Ben do not look alike, while Jeff has a more slender figure and smooth black hair, Ben has a more athletic figure and he has blond highlights in his black hair, Ben is also half a head taller than Jeff.

    Ben has just received his full driver's license a month ago and is searching for a car, but not just any car, Ben doesn’t want a rust-bucket, the teen wants a car to impress girls, so Jeff decides to help his brother out a little, Jeff’s smile widens as he imagines himself becoming his brother’s car for a while.

    “Ben, can you come here for a minute,” Jeff calls out, “there is something I’d like to show and discuss with you!”

    The door to Jeff’s room opens a minute later and Ben walks in, “what’s up Jeff,” he asks, “I need to get back to my studies, I have this math’s exam I can’t fuck up!”

    “I promise it won’t take long,” Jeff says, smiling, “take a seat,” he then says, pointing to a spare chair.

    Once Ben has sat down, Jeff begins to explain his idea to his brother, Ben’s eyes widen when Jeff demonstrates the power of the program to his brother, by changing his age from 16 to 8 then back to 16.

    “So, Ben,” Jeff says, once back to 16, “what do you say?”

    Ben grins, “let’s do it! It’ll be so cool! Finally I’ll be able to get around Boise so much easier,” he says, “plus, I’ll have a car to impress girls, and finally manage to hook up with a hot chick!”

    Jeff chuckles and rolls his eyes, “you really are desperate in getting a girl,” he says, “alright, what car do you want me to be for the weekend? You can chose the paintjob and design. I’ll fill out a fake registration form which would add your “car”, me, to the official car registration system, without messing with my mind”.

    “Meaning, you’ll still be yourself, even though I’ll be your “owner”?”

    Jeff nods, “yup,” he says.

    “Alright, however I’d like to make a deal with you, Jeff,” Ben says.

    “Which would be?”

    Ben grins, “whenever I need my “car”, I expect you to transform into it, no matter what you are currently doing, or where you currently are. Furthermore, while you are my car, I don’t want you to talk, unless I talk to you directly,” he says, “and I won’t only have the right to decide how you look like, I’ll also have the right to decide what your license plates should look like and what they say”.

    “Okay, but I need to at least have the ability to at least be able to drive on my own,” Jeff says, “how should I get to you if I’m on the other side of town, hanging out with some friends, when you decide you need your “car”?”

    “I don’t want you to have control over your body, Jeff,” Ben says, “however, once you have transformed into the “car”, your GPS will guide you to my location, it would feel to you as though you’d be moving by yourself”.

    Jeff thinks for a while, he then nods, “alright, Ben,” he says, “we have a deal!”

    Ben grins, “awesome,” he says, Jeff is to excited to realize the glint in Ben’s eyes, ‘he… Jeff is such a lazy and useless person, a dumb too, he doesn’t seem to notice that he gave me the total control over HIM,’ he thinks to himself.

    “So, what car do you want me to become,” Jeff asks.
