    As Justin continued talking to Dustin, Ty wondered what else he could do. As he stared at his brothers, he began to get an idea: Dustin and Justin were not just brothers, but conjoined twins--two heads on one body with a broad waist and a broad pair of shoulders. If Ty could turn Dustin into that, why not himself?

    "Hey, guys?" Ty said. Justin stopped talking about dying their hair, and he and Dustin turned their attention--and heads--to Ty.

    "Yeah?" asked Dustin.

    "Wazzup?" asked Justin.

    Ty said, "If you guys are comfortable with sharing a body with someone else, would mind if I did the same thing?"

    Naturally, the body bros misunderstood Ty--something that occurs often with ambiguous language. Dustin figured that Ty wanted to be conjoined with a friend of theirs, and Justin assumed that Ty wanted to conjoined with himself and Dustin. When the two asked Ty whose interpretation was correct, Ty told them that neither of them was correct.

    "I meant, how would you feel if I gave myself another brother/head?" Ty said.

    "Oh. Alright, sure." Justin said.

    "Yeah, do it!" Dustin exclaimed. Up until today, Dustin had not been that happy with Ty, but this had changed after Ty got his power, and especially after Ty had given him the brother he had always wanted.

    "Alright," Ty said as he closed his eyes. "I wish I had two heads with my new head having its own mind." Ty was excited as he opened his eyes. The first thing he saw was obviously Dustin and Justin, who stared at him with simple expressions. Both began to jerk their eyes to Ty's left, nonverbally telling Ty to look there. As Ty turned his head to his right, his eyes met his own eyes--he now had two heads! As both heads looked down, they saw a broad pair of shoulders, two arms, a wide torso, a wide waist, and two legs. Ty's wish had come true just like Dustin's: two identical heads on one wide body!

    "Isn't this great?" Ty asked his new head.

    The new head responded, "Yeah! How about I be called Ky?"

    "Sure thing!" Ty responded.

    "So freaking cool!" Dustin said.

    "Totally!" Justin said.

    As the four brothers sat and chatted, they could not stop admiring themselves and their bodies. Only five minutes ago, they were just two brothers--just two bodies with one head each. Thanks to Ty's new power, they had gained two new brothers--two bodies with TWO heads each! On top of that, Ty had expected the world to be surprised at two teenagers suddenly gaining extra heads--Ty had wished that everyone who had met or seen them remembered that they had always been a pair of two-headed conjoined siblings. On top of that, the day was not over yet--they began to wonder what else Ty's power could do and what could happen.
