His privates were now encased in a layer of solid rubber, thick treads running across them, and the center morphed into a metallic rim. It was surreal and unlike anything he had ever felt. He was awestruck by the level of detail, the way it perfectly mimicked the grooves and patterns found on a motorcycle rear wheel.

    Meanwhile, Jake stood as if glued to the spot, every instinct telling him to intervene, but pure incredulity rooting him to the spot. "Aidan! This... This isn't what I meant!"

After a few false starts, Aidan found his equilibrium and began to actually roll around the room - his laughter bouncing off the walls along with barks of delight, "Yes, I'm doing it! Jake, I'm doing it!"
