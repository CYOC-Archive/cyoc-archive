    An abduction like no other

    Dillon had enjoyed his new body, and was eager to test if he could do what he thought he could. Dillon had not only grew a tail for amusement, but also made it a a reproductive organ, and an infectious one at that. If Dillon had stuck his tail in someone internally, they would change to aliens themselves. Dillon decided to take that test and test it in a friend. He reverted to his human form, changed his clothes, and headed out.

    Dillon stopped by Leo's apartment door and knocked. "He Leo, can I come in? Got something to tell you." Dillon said as he knocked. Dillon could change Leo to accept his alien form if he wanted, but he wanted to see the shock value for himself. The door opened and Leo was on the other side. "What could it possibly be this time?" Leo shrugged. Dillon rolled his eyes. "Just let me in and I'll tell you. It's kinda private." Leo raised an eyebrow. This just got interesting for him. "Alright. Come on." He said beckoning Dillon in.

    Inside his living area, Dillon made sure to lock the door just in case he tried to run. "Well, what is it then?" Leo asked. Dillon walked Leo to the center of the room and put his shoulder around Leo. "Okay... This... This might be alarming to you, but I can't keep this to myself anymore." Leo raised an eyebrow as he pulled away from Dillon. "Did, what's up? Are you some secret agent, or criminal mastermind or something?" Leo asked despite no believing either. "Well, it's a bit more extreme." Dillon said. "What could be more extreme than-" Leo was suddenly cut off by Dillon. "I'm... I'm an... Alien." Dillon whispered acting as morbid as possible.

    Leo looked at Dillon for a few seconds. "You on some new meds?" Leo asked. "Leo... It's true. I am a space alien. I came to this planet to study you humans, but my ship had a failure. I've been stranded here for many years, and your the closet one I could relate to. That's why I wanted to tell you." Leo rolled his eyes. "Okay... So then 'mister alien', why tell me this now? And what's so special about you anyway?" Leo questions Dillon. Dillon smiles before he spoke. "Because you and I share a bond like no human I've met yet. I want you to help me grow our ranks. Turn this planet to a place I can truly be myself." Leo looked at Dillon unimpressed, until he saw something growing from his forehead. "And, as for the second part of the question..." Dillon said as his third eye grew and his stalks popped out of his head. The all pointed upward, looking at Leo's shocked expression. "Holy fuck..." Leo whimpered with his eyes wide open.

    Dillon's muscles swollen up, his ears arms, antenna, and tail grew, ripping up his wardrobe, and his tentacle tongue lashed to the floor while his skin took on a reddish tone. "Leo, I don't want to force anything on you, but you know what I am now. I'm hopeful you'll accept my offer and join me. If not, you'll be more compliant in your new form anyway. So, will you struggle hopelessly to get help, or will you make this easy?" Dillon asked still keeping his act up. He would almost believe he'd been an alien all this time if he were in Leo's position.

    While Leo looked at Dillon in a slight trance of horror, the thought of him becoming an alien himself sounds amusing. But at the same time, he might not be so committed to this much change in his life. Leo's back was against the door as he tried to make a decision.
