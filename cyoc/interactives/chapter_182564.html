    "I suppose you also have the power to make the impossible, possible by changing a person into something else. Both reality and imaginary... can you do that?" You propose inquisitively to the genie while being shyly. The genie smiles.

    "Master, I've changed almost every aspect of people's lives in my time. I've changed a poor man into a prince. A young peasant girl into a queen. Most people into plants, though you probably can't guess which ones. Adults into children, kids into birds, even grownups. Heck, i even turned a few Murderers into mice. Just remember, I'm a genie for I can do wonders. Of course, I have much power over everyone and everything here in this human world. You can even make anyone and anything into something that they're not."

    "What if I wanted to BE something that's not real and almost impossible to see?" You suggest. The genie then smiles at your questionable idea, yet didn't seem to be surprised about it though.

    "Would you like to be something a bit more, magical yourself?" I did say I could for you can be anything you want, even something that doesn't exist."

    Your heart nearly skipped a beat as it picked up speed. Could it be true? But if it weren't, how could a beautiful woman have simply materialized in your room like this? You grit your teeth, build up your courage as you unloaded what was been bottling up for years now.

    "Genie, I don't really care about money, power, fame, or justice right about now. Sure, I'd take 'em if you gave 'em to me! I mean, who wouldn't for that's human stuff to get you to the top of human society. why bother with all the limitations of a mortal body when you have a genie of the possible and impossible? That is why I'm done with what little human society for we have spent our whole lives stressing ourselves out on stuff, which won't matter once were gone. We think we're too good to live in nature, but we're not good enough for anything else is stronger than we humans are. Don't get me wrong for I know that it's like a kid's movie or a fairy tale story, not to mention someone's D&D game, but I really would rather live my own life to be something which could not be explain by mortal science. Don't get me wrong, its just that it seems like it wouldbe so much better. Not to have worries of all the things humans worry about."

    "Ah, ha! I see. Well, it's certainly not something I've heard before in my thousands of years. Although, I did have maybe one or two masters who wished to become mythical creatures both big and small of sorts. it's just as I said before, I've done it this."

    It's hard to stop yourself from bouncing up and down with joy that you reflect on the past history of technology and the scifi speculations of the future. it was hard to decide what you wanted to be. you added questions about how the genie can make the impossible, possible.

    "You can really do the impossible?"

    "Yep."

    "Even if it's really crazy or weird?"

    "There's no limit to what your wish can turn you into."

    "Even if I might not be sentient in the usual sense?"

    "Master, I can guarantee you that everyone will continue to have life and consciousness of some sort. It's also guaranteed to be a positive outcome that you'll enjoy being this while people will think you've been one. Master, is that what you want?"

    It's surely hard to believe, but you do believe in her because she's obviously a genie from what you've already seen. you were so excited that you could barely contain yourself from thinking. You could finally get what you've always wanted, better yet, who you want to be as.

    "Genie, I wish to turn me into a mythology creature."

    She raises an eyebrow as she smiles. "Wow, Master, that is a tall order. Still, it's always amazing to see what people would want when there are really no limits that I guess some people would rather not even be people at all. Very well, then. if that is what you want, but you have to be more specific about your wish on what you want to be. Think carefully, master."

    You've always been able to envision yourself as one particular myth after the others. However, only one sticksout above all others. What kind of myth do you want to be as?
