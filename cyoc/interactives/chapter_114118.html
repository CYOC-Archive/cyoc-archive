    The smile that you wore on your face started to twist and turn into that of an evil sneer and the genie's eyes started to widen as ideas started to come to mind as to what would be your first action as a god. The evil sneer then turned into an evil grin as you slowly started to approach her and she started to back up, then look to her lamp and finally back to you. Before you knew it, she began to turn into a cloud of vapor to reenter her lamp to try and get away from you, but you knew she had nowhere to run. As you kept walking toward her, you brought your right hand out in front of your face and clenched your fingers together while balling them into a tight fist as you turned your forearm underside up. Instantly you were able to see the terror on her face as she tried, in vain, to enter the lamp while also noticing she wasn't able to move into it. Grinning still, you made a pulling motion toward your left arm to your left pectoral as she was flung out of her lamp, smacking the wall to your left as she screamed.

    Hitting the wall with a loud thud, especially for something and someone who was used to being in a somewhat ephemeral state of being, she gasped and yelled out in pain. "Wh-what are you?!" She bellowed.

    "Me...? I am just a god that you created. What else could I be?" You said in an evil, condescending, sinister and toying tone.

    "You're no god... you're a monster! An evil god that I should never have created!" She shouted out in anger.

    "Well... be that as it may, you really didn't have a choice... now did you? You were bound to make my wish come true and that you did. Also... there isn't anything you can do about it now either, considering your magic is bound to wishes and you, yourself, said I'd have no wishes once you made me what I am. Speaking of... undoing... I'm going to make sure no one, ever, can undo what you've done." You said sinisterly.

    A look of fear came over her face as she tried to back away from you, even though there wasn't anywhere for her to back away to. "N-no... wh-what are you going to do to me? I've lived for countless millennia..." She said frightened and incredibly scared by you.

    You said nothing as the lamp that was her source of power and home floated in the air from where you placed it earlier and hung there for a moment. You then envisioned the lamp crumpling in on itself, imploding and destroying itself. Just as you saw it in your mind, it happened right before you and you couldn't have been more pleased with yourself than right now. You watched as the lamp slowly crumpled into itself as she tried desperately to do anything to stop you, but even as she did, she began to fade in and out of existence.

    "N-no... you can't!" She screamed as the lamp finally imploded into nothingness as she exploded and burst into billions of tiny glowing orbs like the fizzling type of fireworks launched high into the air during special events or occasions.

    "Looks like I just did..." You said to nothing, thinking that it was possible that some vestige of her could hear your words, "... and now it is time for your fellow genie to meet the same fate in the universe and the multiverse!" You finished saying, knowing everything about everything in existence.

    Closing your eyes, you focused on the particular energy signature that they gave off, no matter where they were in all of existence. You saw all of them, everywhere, like tiny glowing orbs of white light dotted around in darkness and even found several evil ones that gave off crimson light. You smiled thinking that they could serve your greater dark purpose, in one way, but then thought against it immediately. What if they turned against you, wanting to undo what you have become or usurp you at some point even though your power was vast and limitless compared to theirs. No... you couldn't let anything be a possible threat to stop you. Focusing on all of them, all at once, regardless of what they were doing at the moment and thought about them undergoing the same fate as their recently departed sister. Grinning and even chuckling sinisterly to yourself, you watched all of the lights flickering, struggling to stay alight, but failing miserably as each one... one by one... dissipated into nothingness. As the millions turned into maybe dozens, to even just a handful, you couldn't help to feel an immense amount of satisfaction with yourself as you managed to wipe out an entire race of beings in mere seconds... and that was just a taste of your power. There, with your eyes closed, you watched that dozen fade into nothingness until there was just one left and just like with all of them you could hear the genie's screams as it was destroyed. Now... with them all gone... you had all of creation to play with, especially since with the knowledge of god... you knew that God didn't even exist and was a fairytale of the human mind and conceptualization that something had to be out there that existed to keep things in check. However, now there was a god and you had things you wanted to see come to reality. Knowing just how many worlds there were in every inch within the vast amount of multiverses, you were going to see that each and every world played to the many kinks you had when you were just a human mortal.

    What do you do now?
