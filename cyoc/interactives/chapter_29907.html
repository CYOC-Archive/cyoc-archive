    The living room looked like it had been hit by a tornado! A tornado made of salad and milk, as well as some other fluids Mary didn't want to acknowledge. It looked (and smelled) like a bunch of cows had stampeded in, had an milk orgy-fest and stampeded out again. This was beyond bizarre. The Joneses always had the cleanest house she had ever seen. What could have possibly caused this horror?!?

    As she stepped into the room she could hear the wet rug squelch beneather her sneakers."Jen?" Mary called out tentatively now feeling genuinely scared!

    As she turned to survey the other end of the room a figure caught her attention. It was draped, stark naked (except for some odd looking shoes...), facedown over the coffee table near the sofa. Mary recognized her friend instantly. Something seemed a bit strange, but she couldn't dwell on that, Jen needed her help!

    "JEN!" she yelled as she rushed to her friends side.
    "Jen Wake Up! Jen it's me Mary! Wake Up Jen!!!"

    When her friend didn't respond she grabbed her cell phone out of her purse and dialed 911 faster than she had dialed anything in her entire life!

    "911. Please state you're emergency."

    But just then Mary heard a moan come from behind her. She whipped around, phone completely forgotten, and could see Jen starting to move! She was shakily pushing herself up with her arms, her hair covering most of her face, but soon lost the battle to exhaustion and collapsed back down onto the table, mumbling incoherently.

    "Jen you're alive! Here let me help you up." Mary said as she dropped the phone and took one of Jens arms in her hands (the skin felt oddly dry...). Mary assisted her drowsy friend into a sitting position on the soggy rug. (She seemed a bit heavier than in cheerleading practice...) Suddenly she released her grip in shock causing Jen to fall back into the seat of the sofa! What Mary saw shocked her to the core!

    All the weird things began to become clear, screwing with her sense of reality! Jen's skin wasn't dry, it was leathery! Not only that but it had grown a slight fuzz of coarse white hair! She wasn't wearing any shoe, her toes had fused together while thick black nails had engulfed what was left of her 2 toes to form into some sort of hoof-feet combination! She Had felt heavier because Jens breasts had at least doubled in size and were topped with four perky teats each! And not only that but another set of udder-breasts had sprouted beneath the first pair! As Mary scanned her friend's body she noticed other changes that she had overlooked before. Jen's hands had grown thicker... no... her fingers had fused together into 2 large fingers with thick black nails, while her thumbs had... disappeared! Mary could see the black tuft of a tail lying between Jen's "feet" and as her eyes followed it up she was met with the most shocking development yet! Sitting between Jen;s Thighs was a modest cow's udder! It looked mostly empty as the four teats hung limply against its pink fleshy mass. Jen's face had been altered as well. Her nose had turned black and flattened slightly and topped a slight muzzle, while two small round black bumps emerged from her forehead. Jen's ears had moved up a bit on her head and hung to the sides, displaying Jen's exhaustion.

    "Jen... What... what happened to you?"
