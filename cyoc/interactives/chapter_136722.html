    "Ann I promise it wasnt me," Katie said quickly. "Look let me call Gloria. I'll get to the bottom of this and get right back to you." Ann sighed in response before saying her goodbyes. Waiting anxiously Ann drummed her fingers nervously on the sink.

    "Maybe I'm overreacting," she thought. "Maybe everyone is okay? I'll just peak outside and see how everyones doing."

Turning she could see another group of teenage boys running races through the cubicles.
