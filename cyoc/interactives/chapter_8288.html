    The sunlight blinds you for a moment when you step outside. You see a big park with a pond and some trees. There several other “patients” in the park, and everyone of the are more or less a dog. Some have tails, some have furs, some have muzzles and everyone except the guards are on their way of becoming dogs. There’s also a fence at the end of the park, with a obedience field for dogs on the other side. You see several dogs of different breeds getting obedience training on the other side of the fence. “Ok, you’ve got twenty minutes to stretch your legs and do your stuff. Here take this” Your guard hands you something that’s made of leather. You see that it’s a dog collar. “Why do I need that fo... no wait... I’m a dog, I need to get used to a collar” The guard snickers. “You’re absolutely right dog. Another reason is because one of our females is in phase four, and we don’t want you to ‘flirt’ with her, that would be very inappropriate” You pull your ears back in confusion, before a peculiar smell reaches your nose. Making you sniff the air curiously “I don’t *sniff* follow you” The guard grabs the collar in your hand and attaches it around your neck, then a leash is attached to the collar with a silent click. “She’s in heat. Which means that every male within smelling range would love to get their paws on her. You’ve actually met her before”

    “Mmmhmm” Is all you answer him, as you have a hard time to concentrate with that smell filling your nose. A pull from the leash wakes you up from you dream-state. “C’mon, you’ve only got twenty minutes” As you almost gets pulled away by your guard you spot the dog girl that you saw before, you can still recognize her, even if her human characteristics has started to fade away. She looks very unhappy and almost embarrassed as she squat down and relieves herself on the grass. Suddenly a large German Shepard starts barking and panting. He tries to pull away from his guard, but the leash keeps him from running away. He starts to whimper and whine softly, as he’s obviously is attracted to the bitch in heat but can’t get to her. The dog’s guard starts laughing. “You’ve got your own girls, she’s somebody else’s bitch. Calm down and I’ll let you go back to your ‘girlfriends’” The red furred fox like bitch pulls her ears back, you smell fear from her. It’s an unpleasant smell. Suddenly she sees you and her ears perk forward. She lets out a very strange bark and her tail starts wagging slowly. You give the German Shepard one last look as he’s dragged away by his guard, and you don’t feel surprised when you notice his hand-like paws. You turn your head toward the bitch once more. She keeps looking at you and tilts her head. “You want to say hallo to her? I can let you do that, as long as you don’t try to claim her” You sniff the air and you can tell both by her smell and body-language that she’s friendly.
