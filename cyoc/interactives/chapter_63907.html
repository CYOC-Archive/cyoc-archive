    All of a sudden there was a knock at the door. Cally started to ask who it was, but before she had even gotten the first syllable out the door opened and in flew the blond haired girl from earlier. "Jeez, Vick don't you ever knock?" Cally had responded automatically. Whether she was prompted by instinct or her Master PC commands she didn't know. But either way Vicky accepted it. She smiled at Cally before saying "Wellll, when someone is being a downer and locks herself in her room all night...."

    Cally smiled at this. It did kind of sound like her. But not anymore. "I told you I'll be down in a second." A look from Vicky. "Promise." Cally gave her her best sincere look.

    "Well okay but you better not be lying or Anna and I are coming back up here to drag you downstairs." With this last parting shot, Vicky left the room with a flourish and one last accusing stare. Alone again Cally took a quick look around her room. Walking over to her computer, she made sure to password protect it before leaving. She was tempted to make a few more changes with the Master PC, but she stopped herself. She'd done all she needed to make sure she fit in. Now it was time to enjoy herself. She turned to go downstairs and join the party. But before she did she slipped an object into her pocket. She intended to make this a night none of them forgot (unless of course it went badly in which case she might need to make a run for Master PC).

    Walking down the stairs, she found seven girls scattered around the living room, all of them with plastic cups in their hands. As she looked, she found her brain automatically connecting faces with names as the Master PC worked its magic on her life.

    First there was Vicky, the girl she'd already met. She was a bit shorter than Cally, with bright blond hair and a slim build. Perhaps not as voluptuous as some of the others, but very fit. Cally somehow remembered that she was champion swimmer. She smiled at Cally as she came down the stairs and pointed the empty seat next to her on the couch. Cally sat down next to her and accepted the drink when it was offered.

    She sipped her drink as she continued her scan of the room. Sitting on the other side of Vicky was her best friend Anna. A tall leggy brunette, Cally somehow knew that the thin wireframe glasses she usually wore were her way of trying to keep people from only seeing her body. With her elegant legs, tanned skin and impressive bust, that was usually wishful thinking though. Even among this company she stood out.

    Then there was Sarah, standing over by the TV. That wasn't her name, but her real name was usually too difficult to pronounce so she'd decided to just drop it a long time ago. A wiry asian girl with sleek jet black hair, Sarah was short, but people said at that at their own risk. She may not have been as well endowed as some of the other girls, but she made up for a lot of her physical deficiencies with her atleticism.

    Sitting on the couch in front of Sarah were Heidi and Stephanie. Heidi was the exact opposite of Sarah. With her imposing 6'3" height, she would have stood out of a crowd even without her looks, which were in a word stunning. Her vibrant blue eyes were set in a face that looked like it could have been sculpted, framed by wavy golden hair on either side. That alone would have been cause envy even if that face wasn't attached to the most gorgeous body Cally had ever seen. All the girls in the sorority envied her, but she was too nice and cheerful to stay mad at for too long.

    Stephanie, on the other hand, was a lively redhead with pale skin and smattering of freckles underneath each of her vivid green eyes. She was about average in height, with a voluptuous figure that she was very proud of. Perhaps she wasn't quite as toned as some of the others, but most people overlooked this since she carried most of that weight around her hips and on her chest.

    The last two girls were sitting on the floor, chatting with each other. Alexandra, or Alex as she preferred to be called, was a tall hispaniac beauty. Looking at her, Cally really did not see any one feature that seemed to set her apart. She did not have the largest bust or the widest hips of the group, and Heidi had her beat by several inches in height. Rather her distinguishing characteristic seemed to be that every part of her seemed to fit her perfectly. Legs, hips, waist, everything perfectly proportioned and balanced.

    The last girl, Kelly, was barely taller than Sarah but not nearly as thin. A dancer, Kelly had shapely muscular legs that attached to rounded hips and a cute backside that she made sure to show off whenever she could. Cally knew she was a tad insecure about her looks, mostly because of her comparatively flat chest. Still, they were firm and perky and her grace and flexibility definitely worked to her advantage, as did her perfect smile and auburn hair.
