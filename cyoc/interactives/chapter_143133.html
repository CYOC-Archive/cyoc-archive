If there was a form of animation, here at the entrance-depo between the human world and the toon world... they were here. Though Vixi noted that toons of different age-ratings and different styles seemed to instinctively segregate from each other.

She saw an obese young man walk into the costume store... and then a bright red haired cat girl came out in a blue stripped swim suit, her eyes golden as her skin flawless.     "What happened?" Vixi asked.

    "I'm pretty sure we just went through a scene transition."

    "Oh right, silly me," Vixi giggled.

    Looking up and up, the hotel floors reached up into the clouds and into outerspace. Vixi hoped their hotel room had a nice view. This was their honeymoon after all, just imagine having a nice view of the moon!

    "Do you think we can get some moon honey from moon bees while we're here?" Vixi asked excited like it was the most natural thing in the world.

    "Anything is possible!" Raynar said proudly as they stepped inside.

    And inside they found a beautifully painted hotel background, with every style from classic toons to CGI coming and going from the hotel.

    Behind the desk was a shapely pink skunk woman with flowers in her hair with a name tag that read 'Rosey Skunkette' right on her fur.

    "Why hello! Welcome to the Hotel! How may I help you?" She asked warmly.

    "We're here for our honey moon, can you give us a room with a view?"

    Vixi then write her own name, 'Vixi Fox' rather than her maiden name, 'Vixi Fox.'

    Rosey Skunkette clapped her hands. "Mikey-The-Monkey? Please show our guests to their room!"

    A G-Rated toon monkey hopped into frame. He had large black 'button eyes', and wore nothing below the waist, instead wearing a cute hat and vest. "Follow me! Ook-ook!"

    The two rubber foxes followed the bellhop, as he hopped along.

    A rubber neon rainbow ram was waiting for the elevator clasping hands with a rubber green lizard woman with a yellow front and a blue stripe down her back.

    "Hi!" waved the lizard. "Nice to see we're not the only rubbers here! I'm Ashley! Ashley the Lizard! This is Casper The Ram!"

    "Nice to meet you," Casper said as they waited for the elevator to reach them, which Vixi figure would be just long enough for them to have brief introductions.

    "I'm Vixi, and this my husband Raynar, we're on our honeymoon."

    "What a coincidence! So are we!" Ashley said excitedly.

    There was a dig and the elevator opened, and the four rubbers and the monkey stepped inside. The doors closed with a ding, and up they zoomed, the inside stretching up, and squeezing inward, making the monkey in the middle be smooshed as the rubbers around him had their heads and feet expand as the air inside was pressed out of the middle.

    After zooming up countless floors, the elevator car stopped, reshaped perfectly normal again with another ding, and opened up, all five stepping out of the elevator.

    "This is our floor too," Ashley said. "We're in room Infinity-Plus-Twenty-Two-C if you want to visit!"

    "We might, no promises though," Raynard said.

    "Have a nice honeymoon you two," Vixi said.

    The couples parted ways.

    Walking along, Vixi felt a sense of oddness, with how her head and feet had tripled in size inside the elevator, while her waist and arms were made hose thin. She... she knew it was because the air had been pressed out... but wasn't there supposed to be something inside besides air?

When she happily bounced on the bed (hitting the ceiling), it became a cell-painting instead however.
