    The Little Lady

    added 6 years ago S

    You just had to wear your more revealing clothes today, you thought to yourself as you struggle to keep your chest and crotch concealed. "Why are you doing this to me?!" you shout.

    "To prevent you from running off while I take you to the Queen." The fairy answered." After all, you wouldn't last 5 minutes at your size; but don't worry, I'll be here to fend off anything looking for a light snack." She giggled.

    "Queen?"

    "Yes, she's going to judge you to see if you can be trusted to keep our secret. If she deems you trustworthy, you'll be free to go!"

    "And, if not?"

    The fairy's face took a somber expression. "Well, that means you'll be imprisoned for life." Your face turned to horror at that bit of information. "Don't worry, it's rarely come to that, though; mainly because most people take the third option."

    "Which is?"

    "Become a fairy, like us!" She said, her usual chipper voice returning.

    You thought about the last thing the fairy said as you finished shrinking down to roughly the fairy's own height. Would you really want to be a fairy, even if in meant avoiding a lifetime of imprisonment? Meanwhile, the fairy flew around, gathering flower petals, leaves and other material; and, waving her wand in intricate patterns, wove the material into a botanical recreation of her old clothing.

    "Okay, that looks nice, but why not simply shrink my old clothing with me?" You ask as you put the clothing on.

    "My magic isn't strong enough to work with manmade materials."

    "Fair enough."

    "Okay, now let's get going." The fairy said as she lead you along.
