    Leaving the Herd

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Z21 7 years ago A

    The sun rolled upon the world as Jennifer awoke, she stood up on her four legs and looked back at herself. She was still a cow, and had to think of a way to get out of here.

    "What am I going to do, I don't want to say a cow forever, I just cant!" Jennifer said. As the morning continued more and more cows started to awake, it got to the point where Jennifer was no longer able to move freely and was stuck pressed against two to 3 other cows. "Well this is just great, just great!" Jennifer said mockingly.

    Eventually the farmer arrived, "Morning there, I hope you are ready for another day!" The farmer exclaimed as he opened the gate to the barn to let out the cows. Jennifer followed suit as she walked with the crowd to what felt like the middle of the field.

    "I cant spend the rest of my days like a cow, I just have to get out of here!" Jennifer said. She was eventually able to break free from the crowd and wander out on her own, luckily the farmer wasn't out on the pasture to notice that one was walking out on their own.

    Jennifer eventually made it to the end of the fence, It was a good thing that they lived in the middle of nowhere, because then she may have never been able to find a way out! Jennifer continued to follow the fence until she found a door, Jennifer examined the lock, looking at any way that she could somehow get it open. It looked hopeless, for all she knew she would be stuck in this body forever.

    Eventually she figured out how to get it open, she picked up her hoof and smashed it into the look more than once. Eventually it broke, freeing her from her con-finds.

    "Finally I can get out of here!" Jennifer exclaimed with joy. It was a good thing she knew her way around, or she might have gotten herself lost. She walked over to her friends house and walked around, praying that she wouldn't notice. She found a paint can laying on the ground, she picked up the brush with her mouth and started spelling what she needed to say on the wall. Eventually Jennifer's friend went out to see a cow sitting in their backyard.

    "What are you doing here? Hmmm... Whats this?" Susan looked up at the wall to see the words Transform or Dare posted on the wall, next to it read Dare, "What does this mean..." Susan questioned.

    It then read You will ask me to transform or dare, I will then say transform and you will turn me into a human. Immediately Susan went into a trans and started the cycle. "Transformation or Dare, Transformation! You will turn into a human." Susan said as she snapped out of her trans.

    Jennifer was ecstatic as she started to reform back into a human! "Thank you so much Susan. You saved my life!" Jennifer exclaimed. "Your welcome I guess?" Susan said.

    "Listen, if there is anything you want me to do for you, anything.... You can let me know!" Jennifer said.
