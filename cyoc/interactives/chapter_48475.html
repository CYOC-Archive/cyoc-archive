    "We'll figure out what to do with her later." Ty grinned, and turned to face Mark and Dustin, who were cowering in fear in front of what remained of their home. The crowd, so interested in the randomness moments before, had witnessed June's change and shrinkage in stunned disbelief. The onlookers had either fled or stood way back.

    Wendy, meanwhile, was interested in the shrunken woman in the little French maid outfit. While Ty advanced on the elephant and mouse, Wendy laid down in front of June.

    "W-What are you doing?!" June stared at the giant girl. "And who are you, anyway?"

    "Just a friend of Ty's." Wendy grinned, and reached out with a giant hand to stroke June's back. The tiny woman began trembling in response, terrified.

    "Hey, don't worry." Wendy reassured her. "I won't hurt you. You're too cute to hurt."

    With that, Wendy grasped June carefully in both hands and stood up with her kicking and screaming.

    "Put me down!" June cried. "Put me down this instant!"

    "No, I think I'll hang onto you so you don't run away on us." Wendy giggled at her new toy, and joined Ty's side.

    "So? What next?" She asked.

    Ty looked toward Wendy's hands, where she held a still violently protesting little June, then back to the horrified mouse and elephant.

    "Well, let's see. First of all, Mark."

    The mouse's gaze shot way up to Ty's and his eyes widened.

    "Yes, you." Ty bent down for a better look. "I think you've always been more of a rat than a mouse."

    The cute little brown mouse squeaked as he suddenly grew shaggier, larger, and more ugly. In moments Mark was now a large vile sewer rat. He sneered and twitched his nose.

    "Oh, Mark!" June cried sadly, looking away from her husband's fate.

    Wendy shot Ty a sympathetic look, and he nodded.

    "All right. I guess that's a little too cruel, even after all you've done to me. I guess you can go back on two legs, but stay a rat."

    The rat-Mark's eyes widened as suddenly his hunched back snapped and crooked oddly. He bent up, startled, and found his back legs growing longer, his front legs turning back into arms. His torso shifted balance, his tail stuck out behind him, and he grew larger, growing from rat sized to human sized.

    As the transformation completed, Mark was now a 6'2" tall humanoid rat, blended between animal and human. Mark stared at himself, marveled as his restored size, and then stared at Ty.

    Mark's eyes flashed and his anger boiled. "You little bastard!" He screamed, and his claws suddenly flew toward the boy's face.

    But too late. He was already shrinking by the time he made his move, and already child sized by the time he recovered from his attack. His eyes widened and his heart fell as he once again found himself a very tiny thing, just as large as June if smaller.

    "That wasn't very nice." Wendy scolded the rat, and set June down beside him.

    Mark gulped and stared up at an angry June, finding that she was easily double his size, which would place him at about 6" tall. "You stupid man. Who knows what he'll do to us now!" June scolded. "Maybe you deserve to be a little rat."

    "B-But honey!" Mark cowered.

    "No!" June screamed. "Look at us! We're freaks! And it's thanks to you!"
