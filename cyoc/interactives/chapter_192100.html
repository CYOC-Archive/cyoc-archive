    Karen shook her head no as Maddy said "I kinda may have made a few wishes, but I... had no idea they'd come true this way..."

    "So anyway I'm Maddy, and... I had just wished that things would get a little more simple."
    Ryoka chuckled and said "Well being a kid is pretty simple... Let's see if wishes are on the table I wish your hair was pink!"
    Maddy grabbed at her hair and looked and saw it was still dark brown and gave Ryoka a pout "Please don't tease me like that!"
    "But we're so cute... But what about that... jellybird thing? Is he your mascot?"
    Madoka thought and said "Last night I did wish for... not feeling like a little girl-" she stopped as Ryoka couldn't stop laughing. "It's not that funny!"
    "But it's so Ironic!"
    "Hey, Maddy's trying to be serious here." Karen said making her presence known again.
    Maddy said "I did go through a growth spurt, I also wished for a friend, and to get over hating anime..."
    "But your name is-" Ryoka thought through the rest of her sentence and then just stopped that train of thought. "Well... I guess you got a friend."

    Ryoka thought and asked "Say... could you wish for us to be living in our own apartment?"

There was a lot of reality alterations going on, but the three little girls giggled at the unlimited power Maddy had.
    "Holy Shilackles! You're Godoka!"
    Maddy had to wonder "Shilackles?"
    "Must be the age" Ryoka said "Dad would say the Poopy word and then changed it, but yeah Godoka! How about solving all Despair in the world?!?"
    "I am not Godoka! Who also knows what the world would even look like?"
    "Well... If not world peace... How about we have some fun?"
    Maddy was about to ask Ryoka why she should help her out... or who Ryoka was, but Karen hovered and put one wing on the shoulder of each girl "Yes! Come on imagine all the games you can play and no responsibility!"
    Maddy realized Ryoka didn't seem bad at least, and if she had unlimited wishes... Well there would be plenty of wishes to share, and it was kind of her fault that Ryoka was caught up in this to begin with.
