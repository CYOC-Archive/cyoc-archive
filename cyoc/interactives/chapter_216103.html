    The Magic Shop

    The Sorceress of Beauty

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Elflover 5 months ago O Mythological Magic

    You feel a bit lost, truth be told. You are very much tempted by all that you see but how can you choose? Everything looks so enticing! The packaging is so original too! Who thought all these themes linked to mythology and fantasy would mean such great items? There are mascots based upon imaginary creatures everywhere! Wow! You can't wait to try out the products and ardently hope that not all the money went into the packaging and nothing into the quality of the actual product. However, the delicate smells issuing from the various items seem to indicate that it is not the case. This would make the price of the products very much reasonable, if not the cheapest, clearly. Yet you browse absent-mindedly, wondering what would suit you best, your eyes full of images of unicorns, fairies, mermaids, fauns, dragons, devils and whatnot.
    "May I help you?" says a voice in your ear.
    It is the woman in the yellow dress. She's got a kind voice, amiable eyes and a necklace with weird symbols on it. She definitely has the look of someone working in such a store. She possesses a friendly attitude.
    "Yes..." you stammer. "I... Well... I'm looking for something, but I don't really know what... Everything looks super cool. And original. So... What would you recommend for me? I... I just need some suggestions, really..."
    The woman smiles and her answer is not really what you would have expected.
    "Well, you have nothing really disharmonious about you. You would just need a few everyday products, like everyone else, I'd say. But if you want more... unique products, let me ask you a question of my own. If you could be any imaginary creature, what would you like to be? This will allow me to peer more closely art your personality."
    You answer her after a minute of thinking about such a strange question.

    What do you do now?
