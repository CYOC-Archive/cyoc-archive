    You looked up into the sky, and it turned almost completely blood red, and you saw a giant shadow figure form in the center of the area the sun was. Suddenly you saw what appeared to be winged imps, and assorted demons rain down from the sky, and you were both shocked in awe, and paralyzed with fear. People were screaming, and panicking as these demons ran around causing carnage. You wanted to help the people, but you weren't sure that you could all things considered.

    Suddenly you felt something tugging on your tail, and you looked back to see a winged imp pulling it. "Hey!" You growled at the imp, and it stumbled over and said,"Hey yourself idiot, master didn't go this far just to have you just die. Use the chaos as a cloak to escape before the more knights show up!" While the imp was much smaller than you, he was right, so you began running, and trying to find a gate or something. Eventually you got to what appeared to be a gate, but then a small platoon of soldiers blocked your escape.

    Suddenly a surge of anger coursed through your body, and you felt something building up inside your mouth. You eventually got to the point where you couldn't control it, and you shot a giant plume of fire as if by instinct from your mouth. The soldiers were burning, and on fire, but despite this you needed to escape now, so you made a mad dash for the main gate, and escaped from the village. You wanted to see what you needed to do next, but your only instinct was to run, and you did so until you were exhausted, and you ended up in the center of a thick woods.

    You saw a pond nearby, and you walked up to it, and began drinking from it. When you finished drinking you stuck your head inside the water, and shook it around hoping that you were just having a dream. However, when you withdrew your head from the water you were still a dragon, and this time you could look at yourself without any sort of danger nearby, or so you thought. You began shaking your head side to side saying,"No, no this isn't real, this can't be real, how!?" You said and paused recalling your escape.

    "Ah, DAMNIT! I KILLED THOSE PEOPLE, I ACTUALLY KILLED SOMEONE! GOD DAMNIT!" You roared into the sky above you, and you ended up spewing flames from your mouth again.

    "Impressive rage there, you're going to need it before too long there dragon." A dark, and deep voice said from behind you

    You turned around quickly, but to only see nothing was there. "Who's there? Where are you?" You called out into the forest. "Why look to your reflection dragon, and you will see me." The voice said again, and you turned around, and looked into the pond again. You were shocked that instead of your reflection, the pond showed what appeared to be a phantom wraith, that seemed to be possessing a suit of black stained armor, and surrounded by a black mist. You stepped back, and then approached the pond again and asked,"How? Who are you? And how are you doing that?" You asked

    "I see you know not of me, but I know much about you my friend. You were not originally dragon, but human, but due to your curiosity you found our world, and I was the one who pulled you through the fabrics of dimension and brought you here. I have to admit, I intended to lead you to me, and turn you monstrous myself, but your body seemed to choose it's own form, and I have to say it suits you perfectly. Anyways my name is Nitrosinous, and I am the demonic lord of this world, and I brought you here for a task that I'm sure you will be perfect for." The demonic figure, Nitrosinous said

    "May I ask what that task is exactly?" You asked, and Nitrosinous nodded
