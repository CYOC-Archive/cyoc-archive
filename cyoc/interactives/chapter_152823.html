As they walked down paths of endless caverns, the crystals of violet began to stab outwards from the granite ground and walls, yet no other elementals formed from them. The dragonman's silver scales began to grow colder with each crystal they passed, their divine luster growing dull and dim.

A large circular chamber, a mass of giant, jagged glowing prisms adorning the dome-like ceiling. The ornate architecture was quite a departure from the unnatural, and cavernous paths they had traversed. Far on the other side lay a large pair of stone double doors, runic inscription carved into them.

It stood tall and proud, an axe heaved across muscular shoulders while one hand lay clasped across it's armored chest, a prism of purple piercing through the back of its hand and revealing itself to the pair. It's fangs were parted in a psychotic grin, revealing fanged teeth.

He groaned in annoyance, bringing a claw up to his face as he realized Ilvir had been glancing at the demons loins in a dream-like luster. Sighing his disappointment, the dragon's claws clicked against the stone floor as he trekked to the human's side.

With a final pulse, the crystal all but vanished as it became a singular mass of energy, a large hole taking its place in the ceiling.

    "Get back!" Isaac roared, his claws grasping for Ilvir as the human stared aimlessly at the purple mass. The dragon's road became a gasp as his claws smashed against a large barrier, runes of purple growing and blocking him off from the entranced man, who now remained trapped with the swirling mass and demonic statue.

    The draconic paladin's jaw clenched shut in dread as the violet mass of energy trickled into the demonic statue, what he presumed to be the skin of the statue becoming pure energy and formless, while the black armor adorning it shimmered with a dark luster alongside the monstrous axe. The stone pedestal it stood on crumbled to dust as limbs barely resembling feet met the stone ground, the human below at eye level with its armored chest.
