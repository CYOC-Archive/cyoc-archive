Darren was looking around when he saw a shop with a big sign above saying "Spells, Potions and all things Magic". Like most, Darren didn't believe in spells and magical potions but he decided to go inside and take a look anyway because there wasn't anything better to do.

    Once inside, there was a nice aroma that you would normally only smell at a plant shop. Darren had a noticed that the shop had three aisles. The first one had potions, the seconds had spells and the third other magical items. Darren looked down the potion aisle first and he stopped when he saw a muscle growth potion and he probably might have bought it if I weren't ridiculously overpriced. He then walked down the spells aisle and saw so many different varieties of spells but the one that caught his eye was a body swap spell. At first he wasn't going the buy it because he thought that it would probably be a trick the then he saw that it was super cheap so Darren decided to wing it and he picked the spell up off the shelf.

    Darren was so engolfed in the body swap spell that he completely forgot about the third aisle and went straight to the register were a smiling man and woman were waiting. Darren gave the spell to the woman and she scanned the price tag. Darren then handed the money to the woman who then passed it to the man who put in the register and then gave the change back to Darren. The man then gave Darren the spell back in a small carrier bag.

    Darren left the magic shop as soon as he had the carrier bag in his hand. Darren was so excited to read the spell that he forgot about his walk around town and went straight home. When he got home, Darren's wife, Penny was sitting on the sofa in the living. Darren greeted her and gave her a kiss before heading upstairs to check out the spell. Then just wait for the change to happen." Darren began to get really really excited which was a little strange for his age.
