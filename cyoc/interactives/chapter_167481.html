    Nathan entered the store because he heard that he could find help for his world here, and the first thing he saw was Goodwill walking near the door. Nathan was about fifteen years old with average appearance and red hair. Seeing Goodwill, who as old as the world for a moment he didn't know what to do.
    -Welcome to my store. - Goodwill said
    -G-Good mor-morning. - Nathan stammered. - I…
    -You came looking for a skinsuit. - He interrupted him.
    -S-skinsuit?
    -Yes.
    -I heard that I will find someone here who will save my world from the demon lord.
    -Which world?
    -Asvartia.
    -Ah! The world of magic and sword. It will be easier for you to explain it all. You see, my suits are magical, if one of them has been enchanted to give some powers then he is able to give you those. Of course, they can do more, they can change your gender, race, sexual orientation, memories, give you skills, melt you with another user, or turn you into a beast.
    Goodwill took Nathan by the hand and led him to the section titled Worlds of Magic and Sword.
    -Here is what I can recommend to you. He said, pointing to the suits and beginning to describe them:
    Amalia Everest - Blond hair knight with a pale complexion, straight, with the magic of light. Proud but caring for others.
    Bezzeria - Succubus, witch, bisexual. Energized but obligatory, she prefers to be with humans rather than demons. She rules the minds.
    Cindi Kallishia - A human sorceress with jet black hair and huge breasts, homosexual, enormous magical power. She loves sweets and magic experiments.
    Dennaria - Half-orc girl, barbarian, vague sexuality, brutal but loyal. Has the strength of a troll.
    Evvellyn Lightfoot - An Elven Huntress with beautiful green hair, heterosexual, loves nature and animals. She rules over animals and plants.
    Friderik Hander - A human paladin with brown hair, heterosexual, with a great sense of duty. He have the magic of light.
    Ghazer - A rebellious bisexual demon seeks revenge on his former ruler. Summon demonic beasts.
    Hubert Uksyn - Half-elf hunter, black-haired, unspecified sexuality. He loves loneliness, wields spiritual magic.
    Irrun Jektus - A human sorcerer, blonde, power seeking, homosexual. He has great power.
    Ekaterina - A human priestess, platinum-haired, bisexual, dedicated to her duties, knows how to resurrect people.

    -There are other characters too, and these I can customize. Who will you choose?
