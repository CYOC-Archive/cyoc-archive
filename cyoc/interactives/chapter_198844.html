    Jake slowly walks up behind Nadia and puts both hands on her now wider shoulders. As a man she was now taller than before, though still shorter than Jake. I actually think you look better in a tux than you did in a dress, you know? Who knew such a beautiful girl would turn out to look even better as a man eh?"
    Nadia smiled bitterly and touched her masculinized face and then her newly shaved head. "Jake, do you really think that? You think I'm better as a man? I'll be honest I don't agree and I don't like this, I don't want to be a guy but of course I will do whatever it takes to please you as is my duty... but I do want you to know I'm very uncomfortable with this. I am still a woman and that can't really change, no matter how you make me look and sound."
    "I see. Well could you just pretend do be a man?" Jake asks, a bit coldly.
    "I...I guess I could pretend, yes... You don't care about how I feel at all? Jake are you aware I'm a real person, not some kind of android? I have feelings and an identity, you know I was MADE into a CONSTRUCT and I didn't as for it. I had a life before that, I had family, a boyfriend..."
    Jake rolls his eyes. "Ok I'll humor you, tell me about your old life, but please be brief."
    "O-okay... Well for starters my real appearance is exactly how I looked before you changed me, that's my real, actual looks. Nadia White is my real name. I grew up in a small town and moved to the big city after college to find a better life. I struggled to find work and ended up as a stripper. I have a boyfriend, Dean, who has no idea where I am now. A few months ago after work I got kidnapped in a back alley by two men in trench coats and they took me to some strange lab where they experimented on me, injected me with those nano-machines that respond to remote control with the power to alter my DNA. They made me into a CONSTRUCT, a personal and customizable doll, waiting to be sold to someone... and then you bought me and here I am. Well, that's my story Jake. I'm not a doll, I'm not an object, I'm a real person, a woman."
    Jake nods, looking bored. "Fascinating, real touching story Nadia. Don't think that makes any difference though. So you were a stripper eh? That explains a lot. Well I don't want you to be a stripper anymore, I want you to forget about all that. No more flirting, no more slutty clothes, no more boyfriend, understood? First and most important of all, from now on you will be a MAN, that is your new gender because I think it suits you much better. No buts, I've seen you in your natural female form and I used the remote to JUST change your gender to male resulting in your current form. I made NO other changes, this is exactly what you would have looked like if you had only been born male and in my personal opinion you make a better man. As for your job, from now on you will be my personal server, understood? You will be cordial, serious and non sexual, you will dress smart and elegant every day. You can choose to wear full on formal suits or just less formal but still dressy shirts, vests, waistcoats and either neckties or bowties. Oh and your name will no longer be Nadia, you are Oscar from now on. Got all that? You need to take notes?"
    Nadia nods weakly, feeling her heart sink. "No it's alright, I can remember all your instructions, Jake... I have to do as you say so I'll do my best to be as you want me to be..." She turns back to the mirror and sighs sadly as she sits up straight, controlling her posture to be more confident and masculine. She tries a few facial expressions to get accustomed to her new face and she pulls her bowtie tighter to try and feel more like a guy. She still didn't want to be a man and she didn't think she ever could, but she could at least do her best to try and play the role of a man. After all she came to the city not to be a stripper, she was an aspiring actress, so in a way now she gets to put her acting skills to use. "Okay... I can do this... Ahem! Hello Sir, my name is Oscar, nice to meet you." She says, in a professional tone and giving him a firm handshake. Jake smiles, pleasantly surprised at the masculine firmness of the handshake, not to mention the satisfaction of hearing her call herself Oscar instead of Nadia and calling him "Sir". "Well well, very pleased to meet you Oscar!! Tell me, what happened to Nadia, eh? I thought your name was Nadia heh heh!"
    Nadia struggled to keep smiling. "Nadia WAS my name sir. What happened to me? Well you gave me a full gender reassignment as you can see and I am now a man so yeah... Bye bye Nadia, bye bye femininity... I will embrace manhood and my name will be Oscar from now on." She cringed internally but kept a straight face.
    "Damn right. Bye bye Nadia indeed and good riddance, we will never call you that again. Good boy, Oscar!" Jake slaps Nadia/oscar's shoulder. "Now Oscar, go make some coffee, my girlfriend Rebecca will be here in a bit."
    "Yes sir!" Oscar/nadia chirps, giving her/HIS bowtie a quick tug and rushing to the kitchen.
    Jake sat back down on the sofa and watched the bald man in a tuxedo work silently to make the coffee. Hard to think this formally dressed man used to be a slim, sexy brunette girl just awhile ago and she was on his lap making out with him, his hands fondling her perfect boobs. What a difference a sex change and new outfit can do to someone! Jake did like Nadia and he enjoyed her kisses and lap dance, sure, and if he didn't have a girlfriend he would not have done this to her, but given the situation it's probably best to keep her as a man named Oscar. If not forever, at least for the time being.
