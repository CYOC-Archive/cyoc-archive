    If you truly do hold the power to do anything, no questions asked, than what exactly are you supposed to do with it? what can you do with it? Could you skip class? Could you skip school? Could you break the law with no consequences at all? There has to be a way to test it without getting yourself in too much trouble if it fails, but what should you do? You decide it would be best to think about it in an environment with minimal distractions, and something tells you that the loud classroom is the worst place to think, not to mention that just walking out is the perfect way to test it. Hesitantly you brace your hands on the desk and slowly stand up. Normally being the only person standing in the class would garner attention, or at least a glance, but not this time. You were expecting so many things, you were expecting to be stared at, you were expecting to be told to sit down by the teacher, or at least asked what you need, and you were expecting to look down and find the emblem gone from your hand. But no, no stares, no questions, and that emblem was still very much embedded in your skin.

    You begin walking towards the door, maybe the teacher was just ignoring you, but this would prove it. Ms. Welsh was usually very strict with hall passes due to several incidents of students skipping in the class before. But apparently not today as she didn't even bat an eye at your abrupt exit from the room.
    "Really? You're really not going to say anything about me leaving the room without a pass Ms. Welsh?" You ask as you reach the doorway.
    "No? Why would I? You're allowed to do anything you want." Ms. Welsh says.
    "Anything, huh? Even call you Catherine?" Oooh, she hated it so much when someone call her Catherine or worse yet Cathy, she had to say no to this.
    "I'm not going to say I like it if that is what you're saying."
    Nothing. She really didn't care. Or rather it seems like she just knows you can. Everyone in the class knows it.

    You finally exit the classroom after being dumbfounded by the teachers unusually lax attitude. You keep walking further out of the school, through the commons, through the cafeteria, and right by the front office, without even checking out. Every rational part of your mind was telling you this wasn't happening, but here you were breaking several rules right in front of teachers and office staff, and nobody seemed to be stopping you. And at last you push open the front door and walk out of the school with no resistance. It was a nice day today, slightly cloudy, but nothing to block the sun, nice spring breeze, and here you would've been in the school in social studies learning nothing. But now you're here, with this power, and you are gonna use it. You still don't know how you're gonna use it, but you'll get thinking on that as soon as the rush of having magic powers wares off.
