    She reached out her hand again. “Does any one want to see him as a real baby?”

    “YEAH!” shouted several kids in the audience.

    “Alright then. Get ready for one little baby!”

    She snapped her fingers. Immediately, Caleb started to rapidly shrink in size, with his head deflating like a balloon and sinking into the neck hole of shirt. His shoes fell off his feet, and all his clothes collapsed all over him like a miniature tent. The kids in the audience watched in fascination.

    Eventually, Caleb shrunk so much he had completely vanished into the clothes pile, and the child audience could only see a bit of wiggling inside the pile. The Sorcerer went up to the clothes, and reached her hands in.

    “Ah, there we go…” She moved the neck hole of Caleb’s shirt a bit, and eventually the head of a little infant boy poked its way out. Little Caleb smiled and laughed as more saliva dribbled from his mouth.

    The audience gasped. Nate had finally stopped grinning while Katie’s mouth was wide open in horror. For a moment, he had actually forgotten the Sorcerer could change bodies into something else.

    “Ladies and gentlemen, CALEB! OUR BRAVE VOLUNTEER!”

    The Sorcerer pulled him out. The crowd of kids soon broke into hysterical laughter when they realized Caleb was now completely naked. The hollering became even louder, as right as the Sorcerer hoisted Caleb up for the audience to see, a stream of yellow liquid started spouting from little Caleb’s wiener, splattering right in front of the Sorcerer’s feet.

Katie did glance over to the laughing audience, and saw that Olivia was gawking silently at the sight of her brother’s transformation. She didn’t look entirely upset.

    The magical woman only acted mildly surprised as Caleb urinated. “Well…looks like our volunteer needed to go to the bathroom after all!” she quipped, as she watched Caleb doing his business.

    Caleb didn’t seem any concerned about what had happened to him. Instead, he cooed and giggled at the sight of the big kids around him, and he playfully wiggled his limbs around as he let loose his bladder onto the stage below.

    Eventually, Caleb’s bladder trickled away as the audience continued to let out grossed-out laughs. The Sorcerer gently placed Caleb atop of his now oversized clothes.

    “There, all cleaned up. Again, give a round of applause for our little volunteer Caleb!!”

    In the stands, Katie and Nate kept quiet together while the rest of the audience clapped. Oddly, Joey, the one boy sitting next to them was acting silent as well.

    “Now, for my next act of magic, perhaps we can change little Caleb again here! Or maybe we can another volunteer onto the stage…”
