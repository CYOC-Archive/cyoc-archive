    The Witches' Body Swap Meet - Losing Yourself at Sea

    Branch Full view

    You've never felt anything like the adrenaline surging through this latest, most heavily tattooed body. Your heart is racing, your head is swimming. You try to think about some of what these witches have told you, body alterations or something but it's hard to focus. Before you know it, you're leaping a few fences headed straight for the ocean. You figure a good swim might finally wear you out, full body workout and all, so you just dive right in, doing the breaststroke. This still all feels just amazing. The tide should be causing you problems but it just feels like doing laps in a pool. Makes you curious just how far out you can go before turning around.

    ***
    Pretty far, it turns out. By the time you think to turn around, the sun has set, the stars are out, and turning around, you can't see any sign of land at all. You swim for another hour, and still nothing. Did you get turned around? You start glancing around, trying to look to the stars to help navigate, but astronomy was never your thing. Eventually you just pick a direction and go, figuring you'll hit land eventually.

    ***
    You do, but it's an island. Probably uncharted from the look of it, definitely inhabited. As you pull yourself onto the shore, two men come wandering down from the treeline, spears in hand. You can't help but notice a lot of superficial similarities. You're tattooed with stripes from head to toe, they're wearing some sort of white paint with a similar end result. You're probably in the body of the fittest woman to ever walk the planet, and these guys are both totally ripped. You're naked as the day you were born, and not that you look down, they don't even have loinclothes. And they're both really packing down there. Two big ol' slabs of meat just waiting to pound you. Your mind starts drifting towards some fantasies that really bring back the memory of some former occupant of this body telling you it wasn't into women, but never followed that thought to its conclusion until now.

    Then of course you realize that you've just been staring at their crotches for a good minute.
