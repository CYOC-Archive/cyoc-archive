    “Yo! Jeff,” a voice suddenly calls out, causing Jeff to jump and crash to the floor.

    Turning his head he notices the head of his best friend Ken, is peeking in through the open window, “Ken! What the hell, man,” Jeff barks, getting to his feet.

    Ken chuckles as he climbs in through the window, “sorry dude,” he says, “didn’t mean to startle and surprise you like that! What are you up to?”

    “Nothing really,” Jeff says, “other than trying out a new… game I just got,” he then says, nodding towards his laptop.

    “What kind of game,” Ken asks, turning to look at the screen.

    Jeff thinks if it really would be a great idea to tell Ken about the program, after a minute Jeff decides to tell Ken everything, deciding it would be more fun to try the program with a friend, so he starts explaining the “game” to his friend.

    Ken’s eyes are wide as saucers by the time Jeff finishes, “no shit,” he asks.

    Jeff chuckles, “no shit,” he says.

    Ken looks at the laptop screen, a grin appears on his face, “this weekend just got more interesting,” he says.

    “Sounds like you’d want to try it out with me,” Jeff says.

    “Hell yeah I do,” Ken response, “I already have some neat ideas in my head”.

    “Oh? What kind of ideas,” Jeff asks.
