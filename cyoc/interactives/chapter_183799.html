    You get a good running start aim aim directly at the lock holding the doors together. With the speed and force of your kick, the lock breaks apart and rips the door clean off the hinges. The skunk man is startled by the sudden loud crash and turns around to find you, an angry, wild look in your eye as you prepare to face him. He’s clearly taller than you, but looking at all you had to do today, you seem to be the same strength wise, feeling pretty muscular under your jumpsuit. You run up to him, prepared to separate him from your girlfriend, but he uses her as a meat shield, walking towards the changing room. You decide to follow him and make sure he doesn’t do anything else.

    Following him past the doors leads you into an empty zoo enclosure. There are a few decorations of plants growing and some rocks, but you can’t tell what animal is supposed to be there. The skunk man skunks your girlfriend’s face as she then passes out. He then starts talking, “I appreciate your help bro, I wouldn’t have gotten such good help without you. I’ll take good care of her here, if you wanna see her, you can stay too. This zoo has life in it bro, the animals feel better than the humans do. I can let you meet some of my buddies, if your willing to stay with me.”

    You couldn’t tell whether he was asking you out or trying to be evil, but one thing is sure, he was not gonna pull any more tricks on you or your girlfriend. You’ve played enough video games to memorize some moves you can use against this guy, and you’ve been waiting to try them out. He tries to skunk you, but you roll out of the way in time. Using a costume as a shield, you close in on him, the assault of spray not even phasing you as it splashed against the fur of the suit. You throw your leg out to trip him, and it works. You lay the suit on him and zip him up in it before he gain regain his footing. Using whatever adrenaline you have left you carry your new heavy bear anthro girlfriend out of reach of the skunk man. You watch him struggle to get out of the suit as you drag her away from the costume shop.

    You both end up inside an odd section of the zoo, where it looks like there are anthros just like you, walking around, talking, having a good time. You need a place to hide from the skunk man, so you head to…
