I was calling with a question about 'person' changes." you started. "Ah yes, room 325. I have you and your girlfriend on the list for the room. Is everything to your liking?" Mark asked. "Oh, yes." you respond, "The room is great. I just have a question about the actual changes the room makes. You see, my girlfriend has made a change already, and she is now experiencing a headache. Also, she seems to act a lot more like that person, than herself. Is this normal?"

    "And what if we choose someone strong in spirit? Is there any damage to the person's mind?" you ask, knowing your aunt's persona is much stronger than your girlfriend's. "Ah, that situation" Mark begins again. "No need to worry. The only thing that results from this is while the person is changed, they will have no memories or recollection of who they originally were. Instead, they will completely believe that they are that person, and that all the altered memories are true. No worry though, as once the person changes back, they gain all their old memories and personality back without any problems. I must warn you though, if this is your girlfriend's first change, do not keep her changed longer than 3 days in any one form. We seemed to have that problems in the past with these first time transformations and time limits. I don't have any specifics on what happens, but our engineers have told us to relay the message to the customers."

    "I see", you begin again, trying to take in all the information. "Um, thank you for all the information. Just wanted to know there were no problems." "It was my pleasure sir. And if you have anymore questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to call the front desk. Goodbye." And with that, the conversation was over. You put down your phone and look into the room through the glass door. The woman laying on the bed isn't just your girlfriend in your aunt's body anymore. The woman on your bed IS really your aunt, first physically, and now mentally. However, if Mark was right, she also has memories of being in love with you, and maybe more.

You sit down next to her and ask,"Is your head feeling much better?" she just smiles and responds,"Yeah, I guess I just needed a little rest from the plane ride, but I am okay now, my sweet boy." "That's good to hear. I don't want you feeling bad during this vacation. After all, it's not very often we get to spend time like this together." your finish while moving very close to her.

    She laughs a little and then pulls your face very close to her's and says softly,"Well it's not very often any older woman, especially your auntie here gets to spend time like this together with my beautiful, handsome nephew. And since this is so rare, I would never miss this over some little headache." And before you can think of anything else to say, she pulls you on top of her, and begins to kiss you deeply.
