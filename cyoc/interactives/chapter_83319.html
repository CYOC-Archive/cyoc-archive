    Ty just smiled as he looked down at the cute, tiny baby which he was holding in his arms. It was just hard to believe that this little baby boy had been a strong, athletic, fourteen-year-old teenager just a few minutes earlier. Even more amazing was the fact that Ty was the one who had the amazing power to change the boy's age at will!

    "You're so cute as a baby!" Ty whispered to the little baby boy.

    "Googooooo!" the baby cried back, raising his short, chubby arms.

    "Aww... You're too young to talk, Jeremy!" Ty laughed. "And it's all because of me! I made you into a baby again! So, what should I do to you, huh? Keep you as a baby for awhile?"

    "Goooooohh!" the baby boy responded.

    "Well, I can also make you a few years older..." Ty pondered. "The cool thing is... I can always make you younger later on! I can turn you back into a baby any time I want!"

    Jeremy just looked up at Ty with a smile.

    "Okay Jeremy, I'll lay you down on my bed so you don't get hurt..."

    Ty lay the baby on his soft bed.

    Ty then got up and turned around, facing the baby on the bed before him.

    "Okay, Jeremy, time for you to grow older again... Until I tell you to stop growing!"

    Ty just loved how he had total control over Jeremy's body. He controlled the boy's age, his size, his muscles... Everything!

    Jeremy suddenly began to age right before his eyes! His short, chubby legs began to stretch out. They grow longer and thicker, as well as the boy's arms. His fingers, his hands, along with his toes and his feet all started to grow in proportion as well.

    Within moments, the baby's torso started to stretch and widen at the same time, growing longer and wider as the boy began to age.

    Soon, the boy seemed like he had reached the height of a five-year-old, as his head and face started to change and lose baby fat. Hair sprouted on his head as well, and he grew taller along the way.

    His legs and arms grew longer and the hands and feet increased in size at the same time, with the boy's face now changing to match the age. His face became more serious as his head grew longer and more narrow. Hair grew longer and the boy gained slightly more muscle at the same time.

    When Ty saw that Jeremy was now about 9 years old, he made the aging process stop.

    "Okay! Stop growing, Jeremy!" Ty commanded.

    Jeremy looked around and got off the bed. He was about 4 foot 6 inches tall, which made him about a foot shorter than Ty.

    "Hey, Ty! Holy smoke! I was a baby... Now I'm a kid again!" Jeremy shouted as he looked down at his naked body. He was still skinny and short, and his voice was clearly that of a little kid.

    "Now, I'm gonna make you have the body of a gymnast!" Ty commanded.

    Then, Jeremy's arms suddenly bulged, as well as his thighs and his calves in his legs. His flat chest suddenly bulged with two small pectorals popping out, making his shoulders wider. And six-pack abdominals formed under his pecs. For a 9-year-old boy, Jeremy was suddenly very muscular!

    "Whoa! I got a lot of muscle!" Jeremy grinned while flexing his arms.

    "Like I said, you have the body of a young gymnast now! And you're nine years-old!" Ty said, while still towering over his friend by a foot.

    But there was no question about who was the strongest kid in the room right now...
