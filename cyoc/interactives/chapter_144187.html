Still you've gone from absolutely panicked to just kind of bored. It has to have been a least an hour since Brianna left saying she'd "be back".

Was she lying when she said she'd be back? If so why leave the cash box? Why trust a complete stranger that you've turned into a cat girl with this much cash in the first place? Is she insane?
