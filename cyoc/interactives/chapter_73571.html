    "No, I can still 'sense' him," Bobby said. "Don't ask me to explain it."

    "I'm still feeling really maternal," Ashley said. "Can you cause him to begin to age at a normal rate again? Then we can see him in nine months."

If it takes too long, I can always make myself young again and keep earning."
