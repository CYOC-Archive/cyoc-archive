    Jen finished getting dressed, or at least to the best of her ability. Her skirt would now fall to the floor whenever she tried to put it on thanks to her new hind legs. "all this and first period hasn't even started" Jen muttered to herself while deciding to leave the skirt where it was.After falling a few times, jen managed to find out how to walk again and headed to her first period. Her first period was math, home of her least favorite teacher, Or more accurately, the teacher she hated the most. Her teacher, Mr.donot, was known for being manipulative, stubborn, and inconsiderate. Today would not be a good day to deal with him.

    Jen slowly walked into the classroom and awkwardly took her seat. Mr.donot didn't seem to pay attention to the weird dog-like girl sitting in his classroom. At least this is what Jen had thought up until the teacher called her up to the board to answer a question. He whistled and yelled to her, saying "come here jenny, come on girl" in the most degrading way possible. With Jen's new dog-like instincts, she found herself running over to him, hearing laughter to each side of her. She finally reached him on all fours. Mr.donot petted her head and said, "good girl" as would be said to female dogs at times. The worst part about this all was that Jen actually enjoyed being pet but forgot soon after Mr.donot stopped and told her to finish the equation on the board. Jen let out a small whimper while everyone else was still laughing at her previous actions. She stood next to the board and tried to figure out how to complete the equation. Suddenly her and began to itch. Jen knew what that meant. She whimpered again hand begin to change into paws. Her hand morphed while she looked at them waiting for it to all be over. Her nails lengthened and sharpened, she grew pads on her palms, her thumb moved higher on her wrist, and her fingers became shorter and stubbier. Her arms had changed as well, taking on the form of a dogs arm. Jen quickly fell against the board after this had happened. She had trouble standing again. Everyone burst out laughing, even the teacher started laughing a little. The sight was hilarious to them, seeing Jen up against the wall, tail swaying from side to side, ears down, and slowly falling to the ground.

    After about a minute, the teacher told Jen to take her seat. She then fell down on all fours and crawled back to her seat. She had trouble getting back on, but eventually took her seat sitting like a dog would. A few kids were still laughing at her and calling her a dog. she put her head on her desk with her paws right next to it. "I won't survive the rest of today." she thought as the bell rang and everyone packed up their things. Jen walked off her seat on all fours and made her way into the hall to get to next period.
