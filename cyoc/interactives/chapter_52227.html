    You accept the unexpected gift, feeling a little odd. “O-ok”. You put it into your mouth and inhale; you are not habituated to smoking, and a moment later you cringe in pain, your lungs burning. The unpleasant sensation, however, lasts only for a few seconds: the pain disappear, and you let out the cigar smoke; you then try to breathe some fresh air, but suddenly realize you cannot do it anymore: in fact, your lungs seem to have disappeared. Now your penis and your balls are deflating and being sucked into a pouch, and the fur all over your body is becoming synthetic but still soft and warm…”.

But you don’t want it to be anymore: and so your skull begins to inflate while your face becomes more cartoony and caricatural, and your brain dissolves into cotton. The finishing touches are your eyes becoming semi-transparent and your beautiful buck teeth turning into hardened white plastic.

    “One more thing?”, you repeat, your voice sounding perfectly normal (you were expecting something more muffled or goofy).

    You smile in response: you are happy, but that’s not all. You suddenly feel like you’ve been destined to become what you are now since the day of your birth.
    For the first time in your life, you feel actually complete.
