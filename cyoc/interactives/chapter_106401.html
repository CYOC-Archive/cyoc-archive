I scanned over my options, ranging from bright reds and yellows to more neutral flesh tones, and decided on a package that gave her a smoky look. Dark blue bordering on black with a slight shimmer for the eyeliner and a slightly lighter shade of the same applied as a bit of eye-shadow. Matching dark lips finished the look, and the contrast was great on her pale new skin. With that selected options for nails and nail-polish came up. I chose to give her 1/8 inch fingernails painted in the same shade as her lips, with her toenails being shorter but a match for colour.

Still... the question had merit, and what harm was there in answering it. "Yeah," I said "it's been fun changing her like this and that sex was incredible, but I love her for who she is not how she looks."

    "Great, she'll be thrilled to hear that!" The machine beeped as the lab-tech said that, signaling that Laura was done being processed. "Now let's go see how she turned out."

    "Go see?" I questioned, figuring Laura could show herself off, but I followed the girl anyway. Once I reached Laura I realized why I had to go to her: the 'permanent preservation' really was permanent. Laura was now a solid silicone sex-doll, complete with the makeup options I'd chosen for her. Before I could voice my astonishment at the situation the lab-tech started speaking.

    "Congratulations Quinn on finding someone willing to become your perfect lover permanently. Like this Laura will never age, and will always be ready for a good love-making session, either as the inanimate toy she is now or as an active, but still silicone, participant in the act. I'm sure she'll enjoy both by the way, as she knew exactly what she was getting into before this happened. Anyway with the way you answered that last question she's given you control over when she changes, so if you want her like this again just whisper 'be my love-toy' in her ear. To change her back you'll need to satisfy her enough to make her want to change back, then tell her 'be my lover'."

    I just stared at the girl with a dumbfounded expression frozen on my face.

    "Oh you lucid dreamer types always get confused here. Look, when you wake up this will make a lot more sense. Anyway I hope you enjoyed your time here at the Perfect Lover's Retreat, and that you both enjoy the fruits of your visit for many years to come! Now have a fun Saturday you two! Good Morning!"
