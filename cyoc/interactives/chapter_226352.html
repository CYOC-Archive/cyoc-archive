Next up was the smell - it was unlike any scent he had ever experienced. The must of an expensive cologne but with an distinctive but not unpleasant animal aroma. It was a primal smell that fueled his reps as he continued his lifts. This burst in energy was interrupted though as he was just curious enough to look up to see what this stranger was doing next to him.

    Catching sight of John the beastly dog man, the asian lifter gasped in shock. Thanks to the reality-warping capabilities of the Chronivac, the presence of a German shepard anthro casually walking around in public was not foreign to him, but it was still quite rare for most humans (in this world anthros represented less than 0.1% of the population). Combined with John's sheer size it was enough for him to lose his concentration entirely and drop his weights.

    "I got you bro," John said, easily reacting by catching the heavy bar with one hand.

    "Thanks, man," the hunky human said. He was barely even angry he had been interrupted by John. "I guess you can go ahead, I'm done now."

    "Sounds good, what's your name?" John asked.

    "I'm Adrian," he replied. He picked up his water and moved aside, watching keenly as John prepared his weights.

That had to be close to the world record, right? Nevertheless, John breezed through his set without much issue. Adrian could barely take his eyes off John, appreciating how his fur and sweat combined into a slick density that showed off his perfect muscles.

    "Hey, I said, do you want to get changed now? We both seem to be done." Adrian quickly accepted the offer, keen to get back to the locker room with this friendly beast of a man he just met.
