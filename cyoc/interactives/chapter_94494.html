    Spend the eternity as a display model.

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 8 years ago I O

    ...but it was a girl who wanted another figure. You'd yell at the sky if you could. An incredible anger overcomes you, and you attempt with all the will you've got, to try to move... but nothing happens. You can't force even the slightest of movements. You're an inanimate object, and that's all you will ever be...

    Years pass. You've seen countless customers buying items or being turned into them. From what you've gathered, the store was a huge sucess, and they're soon going to become a big industry. And, apparently, the employees are inmortal and eternally young... somehow. But, luckily for you, they aren't a greedy and soulless company, and they take care of their items.

    You heard one time, due to the enormous sucess, they're going to move to a big city to set up the major store of the company. Two days after, Ashley comes to you, grabs you with her delicate and smooth hands, and tells you:

    "I'm sorry, dear. I know how much you'd like to have an owner, but since nobody has bought you in years, we're not going to sell you, and we're going to use you as an exposition model and leave you in the shelves. It's not on my reach... i've tried to sell you, but i've failed. But there's one thing I can do before we move to the city. I can use a spell on you which will change your mind so you won't want an owner and will love being in a shelf instead."

    You're surprised by how nice Ashley is. You try to scream "Yes! Yes, please! Do that!" but no sound comes out of your inmobile mouth. But Ashley seems to feel your desires.

    "Well, i'll do that. I hope you enjoy an eternity on a shelf. And don't worry about how we treat you, we'll take you with us every time it's needed... and it doesn't looks like we're going to go bankrupt, ever."

    She places you in a desk and picks up a weird book that was on a table near her, and spells a weird incantation. Moments later, a strange violet beam comes out of her finger and hits you. And then... you feel different. Like your whole being has changed. Why are you on a desk? You need to be in a shelf, for everyone to admire your cuteness! You try to beg Ashley to put you on display, but again, no words are heard.

    "Looks like the spell was a sucess." she says. "Now, in a few days we'll arrive at the new store, and your new eternal home." These words feel like heaven for you, and you can't wait.

    The time that passed between that and until you arrived to the store felt like years, but you already lost count of time. You can't remember how much time has passed since you asked Ashley to transform you... you can't even remember your own name, or if you were a guy or a girl. All you know is that you are a cute, tiny plastic mermaid. They wrapped you carefully, just in case, and packed you in a box, put in the plane which took you all to your destiny.

    Ashley offered to take you with her, so she picked you up when the plane arrived and saved you in her purse. She then took you out and carried you in her delicate hands when you arrived at the new store. You'd open your mouth wide if you could, this place is huge! Looks good for your new home...

    Days later, everything is done and the store is up and running. You've been sitting at the desk for a while until everything was set up. And then, you feel Ashley picking you up and putting you into the place you've desired to be in for so long.

    You're in a shelf. You're in display, for everyone to see, and it feels wonderful. You can't move, your destiny is at mercy of your goddess Ashley who sits at the desk. You can't talk, but a plastic figure doesn't needs to. You're soon lost in an ecstatic bliss which comes from being an inanimate object. You can feel dozens of customers looking at you, which feel like sweet caresses over your smooth and sensitive plastic surface. You're a model, just to be admired through the ages...

    You can still vaguely remember things from your old life as a human... until Ashley decides it was time to put a calendar. Luckily, she put it on your sight, and you can spot today's date:

    "26th August, 2089."

    You remember... you've been transformed in 2015, which means 74 years have passed. And with that thought, you let all your memories from your human life vanish away, and realize again, that you are eternal, a cute, tiny, smooth, carefully painted anime mermaid figurine, designed just to be looked at, and admired for countless centuries to come...

    The End.
