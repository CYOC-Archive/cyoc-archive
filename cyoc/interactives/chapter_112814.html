    You decided to follow the tracks to see where they lead. You followed them for about 2 minutes when you notice that the tracks seemed different in a way, like they were changing. You continue to follow the tracks and as you did the tracks seemed to change to resemble what seemed like large claws. You stopped to wonder if you should continue, and ultimately decided that you had to considering that you were basically lost in the woods.

    As you followed the tracks they seemed odd in positioning, as if the creature was dazed, or confused. You continue to follow them, and eventually they lead to a small ledge, and below the ledge you saw what appeared to be a large dragon of sorts. You knew it wasn't dead, because it was breathing, but as you motioned to turn back it started moving. You immediately panicked tripping over backwards with your back to the ledge. The dragon slowly stood, shook its head, and then stared at your breathing heavily at the sight of you.

    You motioned to run but then you heard the dragon speak, and it said,"Please, Henry if you are my friend please stay. I won't harm you in any way, shape, or form."

    You turned back towards the dragon and said,"M...Michael?"

    The dragon looked at you surprised,"Y...you do know me." The dragon, Michael, said to you as he walked up and sat down.

    "What happened to you?" You asked Michael, and he said,"I can't really answer that, but listen. I don't think that there is a monster killing people, but what I do know is that there is a being that turned me into this. Now I don't expect you to tell the world, not that they would believe you, but you need to get out of the woods here before it's too late." Michael said grabbed your arm.

    "Okay but what about.....?" You began, but were interrupted some sort of smoke bomb, and were knocked out. When you awoke you saw Michael staring at you shocked.
