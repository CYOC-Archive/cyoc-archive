    Jeff's mom had made her decision very quickly. Without saying anything to her son, she reached for the Chronivac. She looked through the options until she settled on what she was looking for. Looking through Jeff's profile she had changed a few things. First, she reduced Jeff's table manners, knowledge of food and ability to digest food all the way down to newborn. She'd be providing him all of his meals for the next few days. Next, she had reduced Jeff's knowledge of clothing, potty training, urinary and bowel system, and how to dress himself down to newborn. She had changed all of her bras to nursing bras and changed Jeff's shoes from his Nike brand to light up baby walking shoes with a light blue puppy motif to fit him. She changed all of his socks to short blue baby socks in his size. She then changed all of his underwear into disposable size 5 diapers in his size. She then made the rest of his clothes disappear. She then opted to let the chronivac change anything else she needed to let Jeff experience wearing and messing his diaper and being breast fed like a baby. A fully stocked changing table and fully stocked diaper bag appeared in Jeff's room while bibs appeared in the kitchen along with newborn baby bottles in his size and a breast pump. She then opted to make Jeff unaware of the changes until the end when he changes back to normal. She then set reality to change just a bit so that Jeff wouldn't be shamed when out in public. She then made herself and her daughter unaware of the changes she did until the timer had expired when things would go back to normal and she, Jeff, and her daughter would remember everything. Finally, she set the time it took for the changes to take hold for five minutes while setting the timer for the changes to last until things go back to normal for seven days. Jeff may have only wanted a weekend, but she wanted to have a full week of it to see how everyone reacts to the changes. Once she was finished with everything, she clicked the "Yes for Changes" button, logged off of the Chronivac, and called for her ten year old daughter, Katie to get ready to go to the mall so that they could do some light shopping and eat at the food court......once all of the changes has taken hold.
