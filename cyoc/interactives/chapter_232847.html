She was practically adored by everyone… And here you were, wanted by no one. She may have been a little girl… But being Karen sounded pretty good!

    Wait… Did she just call you… Karen?!

    Your eyes shot open almost instantly… You were clearly in a little girl’s room… No, not just any little girl’s room. This was Karen’s room! You recognized everything in here… The Disney Princess bedspread and sheets, the rows of Barbies and stuffed animals, the little pink TV, the white dresser… But it was what you saw in the massive closet mirror that made you scream.

    Staring back at you was the spitting image of your niece Karen, eyes wide with fear and confusion as she sat in bed, wearing a pink nightgown with the smiling face of Hello Kitty on the front… And… Even weirder, she seemed to copy your moves. Every little blink, every little movement… You were afraid to look down but… Well, you had to. And, as pretty long chestnut hair entered your view, you saw the fabric of the nightgown attached to you… You couldn’t help but scream again!
