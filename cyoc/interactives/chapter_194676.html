    Getting used to it

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added by Bookworm A year ago TG

    Ethan was panicked. It seemed like something out of fantasy or science fiction. He looked at himself, now herself, in the mirror. It was clear that he was now a she. The cute face, wavy brunette hair, and other changes made it clear. Her voice was now unmistakably feminine.

    Ethan went to his room and pulled out his wallet and looked at the learner license. It now said Evelyn Sunderguard. It seemed reality had changed.

    Evelyn looked around her room. It was not the pink she expected. The roof had glow in the dark stars. The room had a desk with a laptop. There were red heart decals on the back of the laptop. One shelf in the room had books, and another had trophies and medals.

    Evelyn approached the shelf with the trophies and medals. They seemed to be the same as the ones she had previously, only now they said Evelyn. It appeared that she was still active in baseball.

    Still feeling the shock of the change, Evelyn heard a knock on the front door. Evelyn walked upstairs from her basement room and looked through the peephole. She saw Ashley holding a box.

    Evelyn opened the door. “Come in Ashley”. Evelyn welcomed Ashley in, sounding cross about what just happened. “You said you would take care of the problem?” Evelyn asked, raising an eyebrow.

    “Yes” Ashley replied. She appeared a bit upset as well. “Although you can see how exciting this was. I am happy to finally have a female friend”.

    Evelyn nodded. “Yes. This doesn’t happen frequently. But I’m in shock. You turned me into a girl”.

    The two stared at each other for a moment. Evelyn broke the silence. “Well, come on in. Take off your shoes. What did you bring?”

    Ashley took her shoes off and the two walked over to the kitchen. Ashley placed the box on the kitchen table and started to unpack it.

    Inside the box was a sleeveless pink turtleneck sweater with darker pink stripes, a light yellow pleated skirt, and long black socks.

    “I brought you something to wear”.

    Evelyn was flabbergasted. She looked down and saw the baggy clothes she was wearing. She was about to protest, but Ashley looked at Evelyn and said “please”.

    “Fine” Evelyn gave up. She took the clothes and walked to the bathroom. She took off her male clothes and changed into the female clothes. She also found a hairband and made a pony tail.

    As Evelyn walked out she looked very feminine. She was short, around 5’3”. She had a long brunette pony tail and a cute face. She even had on a sleeveless pink turtleneck sweater with darker pink stripes, a light yellow pleated skirt, and knee length black socks.

    Seeing Evelyn like this, Ashley looked excited. Ashley came over and hugged Evelyn. The hug was reciprocated.

    “You said you would fix the problem. Now I’m dressed like a girl too. Are you able to change me back?”

    Ashley looked down. “I’m not sure. I can try if you’d like. But could we hang out first?”

    Evelyn sighed. “Yes. Ok. What would you like to do?”

    Ashley was a fan of rpg’s and lord of the rings. “Can we play World of Warcraft?” Evelyn was surprised but agreed. Together they played for about two hours. While they played, Ashley put classical music on. Evelyn found that she strangely enjoyed it.

    “That was fun” Evelyn revealed. Ashley smiled. “Would you like to watch the Lord of the Rings?” Evelyn didn’t see why not. The Fellow of the Ring was almost 3 hours long though.

    After the film they had lunch. They made plans for the afternoon. Evelyn was enjoying herself. Evelyn agreed to stay like this for the day. They could look into changing back tomorrow.
