    Camilla was a cow girl. She was quite a beautiful young lady, with her silky black hair, crimson eyes, and of course, her large udder and breasts. Her lightly tanned skin was littered with bruises, however.

    A year ago she had been captured by humans, who forced her to live on a "monster girl farm". There weren't very many of these farms, as monsters were incredibly hard to capture, but the ones that did exist were managed by very strong humans.

    Most of the monsters in these farms had been captured when they were in a state of weakness. Camilla herself was dehydrated and on the verge of death when she was captured. They took care of her hydration by feeding her a special liquid to increase her milk input.

    She was loaded onto a truck before being abused by the burly farmers, and milked almost nonstop. She was injected with a purification shot so that her milk would be safe for human consumption.

    When she wasn't being milked, she was chained to the ground and forced to lay in the hot sun. She had a miserable life, her mind was always on vengeance. Everytime she was milked, all she felt was hate. Hate for the human race.

    Right now she was chained down outside, her backside was incredibly hot. The heat stung her skin. A few fellow monsters were also chained in various ways. It was mostly other cow girls, but there were also a few chicken harpies; their wings chained shut. There were a few of the rare sheep girls and goat girls, and some centaurs locked in the stables.
