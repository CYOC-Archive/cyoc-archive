If this App can really devolve you, there has to be some proof then just saying it. This is the reason why I’m here. See, today, I’m going to devolve myself and all of you are going to witness it. If it’s real, then I doubted those that try it. If it isn’t, well, then this was one huge joke. Let us begin.”

    “Interesting…” she said, “Looking into the rules, they say how to use this app. The first is to find a target to Devolve, most like myself, or someone else. Then there’s this time period, as in to select a geological age to De-Evolve the person of interest into its equivalent from said age. Most likely selecting the time period and the target will look like a person from that said time. Then there’s this ‘Evolutionary Tree’, displaying the person’s evolutionary tree, allowing to De-Evolve this person into a selected stage. Then there’s the timer option to set the time to the person devolving along with when they will return to normal. There is an option for the changes to be permanent, but I would avoid it, as I don’t want to be a monkey forever. Not like I think this app is actually real. There are even setting on this phone where you can decide to mentally change, make yourself or others aware, or alter reality. That’s a strange choice for a simple app. And then there’s the De-Evolve, as in devolving the target you need. Seems simple enough. Let’s begin.”

She soon notices a 3D image of herself with her attire on her and was amazed. “Wow!” she said, “The app must’ve created a clone avatar of me. Looks really life like.” She soon saw a slider before with the arrow pointing to the ‘Now’ option and the other side that said, ‘Beginning’. Curious, she used the tip of her finger to slowly slide the bar to the right and was amazed at what she was seeing. Her avatar’s hairstyles and outfits continuously change through past looks, sometimes with her body and face morphing differently. By the time she past the prehistoric years, there were no clothes on her avatar and her body devolving through Neanderthal, big gorilla like creature, a small monkey, a prehistoric rodent, a lizard creature, a fish, and finally a single cell organism. She found it very amusing as she said, “Got to admit, the details of the avatar is quite fascinating. I never thought that I would be looking at myself as just a small piece of slime in the beginning. Wonder what time period I would devolve into…”
