    You feel a tug on your left side and then a sharp pain.

    "Ow!" You say. You look over and give a nervous laugh at Jason. "Hehe, sorry. I kind of zoned out there." Jason looked at you and gave you a look saying, 'You're awnry, you know that?' You realize Jason is somewhat of a mother hen to you.

    "Well, come on. You don't want to miss our favorite lunch, do you?" Jason said. You both stand up and you start to walk as you had always been fused to Jason. Your amazed by the magic the note has made. You could feel Jason's leg move and his arm move too. It felt really great.

    "So, like I said, English. You are taking notes." Jason commanded you. You gave another nervous laugh and he gave another 'You're awnry look'.

    "Yeah, so whats for lunch, again?" You ask. He rolled his eyes and tilted his head back.

    "Gosh, you're forgetful today. Its our favorite? You know? Cheeseburgers?" Jason said. You remembered you love cheeseburgers a lot. I guess Jason loves them too, probably because you share a stomach. You walk to your locker and Jason starts to put in your combination. He pulls his hand away, quickly.

    "I'm sorry. Did you want to put it in, this time?" He asked you. You were confused. You didn't know Jason was so cooperative about being conjoined to you.

    "No, its fine. Hurry up though, we'll be late getting to lunch." You said. He stuck his tongue out and entered their combo in 10 seconds flat. You stick your backpack and he sticks your books in your locker and you head off. You reach the lunch room and see that many people had already gotten their lunch so you didn't need to wait long for sweet cheeseburgers. Jason got the tray and loaded some stuff on while you loaded some other stuff. You both only got enough for one person and you realized it was because of your single stomach. You found a spot at a familiar table and sat down.

    At this table, sat 3 people. One kid had pale skin, bright green eyes and eye length black hair. He had a look of depression on his face. The girl had pink hair, blue eyes, and wore a tear drop shaped necklace. She looked immensely happy to see you. The third was a smaller boy who had yellow eyes, sandy gold hair, and small whisker like markings on his face. He wore a cap that said, "Meowch!" You had just begun to notice that many of the students seemed to look weirder and weirder.

    "Hey Jason? I seem to have slight amnesia today. Where are we?" You asked, lying about amnesia. He scoffed at this then, had a look of laughter on his face.

    "Were at Elysian High, duh! You know? Supernatural school for any magical peeps?" He said, as if completely normal. You thought about this and sat your selves down. You reached into your pocket and felt a long stick and realized it was a wand. Hm? A school of magic? This could be fun.
