    "Stop this at once!" shouts out a man in a dull grey short trench coat w/ a tan shirt underneath and two swords in their sheaths worn back. The blades w/ a gold circle hilt that goes from the top of the hilt to the pommel w/ a small blade sticking out. His face is scarred w/ light blue scales and some on his exposed hands and feet, the feet wrapped w/ some sort of medical bandages and wearing gauntlets. The emblem showing on his right sleeve of his coat shows his ranking and role which shows a human and a dragon face facing opposite directions looking up adorned w/ a blade between them. The stars represent the rank he is right now (from three to one is lowest to highest) and wears tan cargo shorts w/ knee pads on and a knife pouch. His eyes are sky blue and his hair is long and swept back that reaches to the nape of his neck and the fingers and toes are tipped w/ snow-white claws as he comes closer to you.

    He then stops a few feet away from your forearms and look at the guards that stepped back. "It's okay, I will handle this. Continue your businesses everyone!" he orders them. As the captain of the knight groups disbanded their positions and sheaths/lowers their weapons, the paladin then turns to you and cocks a brow, "You don't look like you're from here. What is your name?"

    "David. Thanks for saving me Mr...." you don't know his name and then the paladin, introduces himself, "My name is Daze. Daze Louros of the Dragon City of Palmeros. Nice to meet you David." He then jumps at inhuman speed to climb onto your mane back (you then realize you can't go on two legs and go on four instead). "We'll head to my quarters north of here. I'll show you the way as you tell me how you started to turn into your dragon form." he says as you nod and followed his direction. You then tell the story he thinks it is crazy, but still listens. From the beginning as you walk from street to street w/ some people and knights look at you in awe. Even some of the kids were happy to see you (despite the fact you started turning into a dragon when you entered the city.), you tell Daze how you were walking around the park, and then followed up a stone path that says Castrumiel and as you follow along the stone path to this, "Castrumiel" you then notice that there is a castle city w/ people wearing medieval outfits left and right as it soon take hold of reality. And then once you arrived, you felt lightheaded and dizzy, and that's when you told Daze you turned into a dragon and then met Daze when he was being cornered by the knights.

    Daze then crosses his arms over his chest and ponders the wild story and then you disturbed his thinking by thanking him, "Oh, and thank you for saving me Mr. Louros."
    "It's Daze, you can save me the respects for later. And you are welcome my dragon kin."
    (Dragon kin? I wasn't even a dragon to begin with, I was human!) you then think to your head w/ an annoying look on your face. You then notice a group of flying dragons heading north where Daze said he'd take you as your annoying look turned to awe as a tower that looms over a fraction of Castrumiel below by its shadow as dragons flying around the tower. And from human to feral dragons, there were humanoid dragons from almost full human, to anthromorphic and feral dragons in groups talking to each other as you walk by. Daze grins wide, showing his sharp human teeth and greets you to the town, "Welcome to Castrumiel's Dragon Town, David!"

    Near the base of the tower, the population of hybrid and feral dragon increases and there were not only dragons as knights, there are dragons that act as merchants, bar/inn keepers along w/ humans, or just like any other people you saw in Castrumiel. You then say to your amazement to Daze, "There's so many dragons here!" Daze then replies, "That's why it's called Dragon Town my friend." He then suggests on where to go next. "We can continue to go to my quarters which is not too far from here, or go find my wizard friend, Kalren who should be at the barracks teaching the younglings the way of magic. He might know why or where this anomaly happened." He then made the last suggestion as you stopped at the gate of the dragon tower, "Or do you want to do some exploring w/ or w/o me?"
