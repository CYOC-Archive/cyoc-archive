    {{Whoa whoa, Stalker, it's okay I think, I can just try hunting later I'm fine I swear.}} you said

    {{How's your fire bladder? Have you eaten anything to replenish it?}} Stalker asked

    {{No}} you responded

    {{Then you need to hunt something, fire is our main defense, and offense as dragons.}} Stalker said

    {{Okay then, it's basically sneak, and pounce right?}} you guessed

    {{Then there's speed, strength, accuracy of your strike, and willingness to kill. Basically you should suspenct the run, fly, or have fast reflexes. I guess fishing is the easiest thing we can do for now anyways.}} Stalker said, and flew off so you followed him.

    Stalker landed near a pond, and you came in a bit too fast, and tripped while landing. {{You seem to have grasped the concept of flying swiftly. So let's see how well you are with fishing, this pond has been here for a while now, I'd say maybe 1... no 2 centuries old. Fishing here is great for me, basically you need to have fast reflexes, and do exactly what I'm doing now.}}

    Stalker began slowly creeping across the coastline and you saw him swiftly reach down and grab a fish by using his claw. {{Like that, so basically just focus, and if your instincts helped you like they did flight then we should be fine. That is unless those same instincts are what help you land.}} Stalker said

    {{You know I'm getting a bit tired of you always criticizing my when I don't know how to do something.}} you said, and then a thought popped into your head,{{Wait, Stalker if you read my memories back when we first met couldn't you see everything and know my capabilities already?}}

    {{I mean yeah if my interests were true to my name. However, I see privacy essential in most cases, plus trying to see an entire memory bank inside a mind is a bit daunting. Hatchlings always end up with a huge headache after attempting the first time too, that's why they stopped teaching memories and reading them to hatchling adolescents.}} Stalker said

    {{Oh, I guess that makes sense, which of my memories did you see?}} you asked

    {{Just your transformation, should I criticize your intelligence considering you drank from that chalice that was how many years old?}} Stalker said

    {{Okay, I'll allow that one, now let me focus.}} you said closing your eyes for a moment, and then opening them to try to regain some composure.

    You looked at the water, which was oddly clear, and saw a small group of fish. You took a deep breath and focused on your objective, and swiftly made your execution. To your surprise you caught a fish on your first try,{{Holy shit! I did it first try! So now.....}} you began, and then saw Stalker swallowing his fish by taking one huge bite, and chewing it raw.

    {{What?}} Stalker said seeing you staring at him

    {{You just ate a raw fish! A raw fish you literally just caught by claw.}} you said

    {{So what?}} Stalker asked

    You let out a dragon sigh, and said,{{Oh nothing I guess. Humans can't eat raw meats so I guess this is a first for me.}} you said looking at the fish in your claw, and it was now lifeless. You then did the same as Stalker and ate your fish whole, and it was definitely a new taste, especially with scales.

    {{See was that so hard?}} Stalker asked grinning

    {{It wasn't so bad, but the damn fiah scales are stuck in my teeth. Humans on my world had a way to remove them, but it's not really relevant now I guess.}} you said picking your teeth with your claws

    {{What is it with humans and raw meats? Why can't they devour them? Personally I'm curious and now I can ask someone who isn't utterly terrified by my very existence.}} Stalker said

    {{If humans eat raw meat they basically contract a disease that sucks really bad, and can potentially kill them. The disease is in animal blood, but our bodies,I guess, are immune to the disease.}} you said

    {{Ah, I get it now, humans are weird.}} Stalker said

    {{Well is there anything else we should do before you comment aside from either how stupid I am for being from another world, or how odd humans are?}} you asked

    {{Well...}} Stalker began
