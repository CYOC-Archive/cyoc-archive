    Getting the Tail End of the Joke

    Previous chapter
    Branch Full view
    added 5 years ago A BM S O


    Without further warning, another blast of skunk spray hits you dead in the face. The voice inside slowly grows fainter and you realize you're about to black out. The skunk-girl laughs as you try to struggle, but to no avail.
    Your world darkens as you drop to the ground. You try to stay awake, but the smell is over powering. Eventually you can hold out no longer. The skunk-girl's eyes are the last thing you see as she raises her horrible tail once more.
    Yet another blast of spray hits you and you finally fall unconscious...

    ...

    ...

    ...

    Hours have passed since you passed out, and this seemly endless rain forest is truly at its darkest hour.
    You rub your head, wondering if you'll ever get the skunky stench out of your...fur... You also wonder if the transformation is fully complete. You sit up, then slump back down, your poor head spinning.

    "Finally," a voice out of nowhere surprises you. And it's Not the skunk-girl from earlier's voice! At first you just fell relief. But then you look up and practically scream!
    A hugemongus bear/man is staring down upon you! Is he planning to eat you or something weird like that!?

    "Well, I can officially say I have never seen anyone else look so terrified in my life." the bear-man says dryly, "You just had a run in with a skunk and you're freaked out by seeing a bear, now where's the logic in that?" He sighs and sits down next to you, clearly without a fork or knife.

    "I apologize," you say, not sure how to handle this, "But, um, speaking of skunks, where is she now?"

    "Don't tell me you want to meet her again after I saved you from being her slave!!" the bear-man roars.
    Instantly, you arch your tail. Then shake your head and drop it thinking,
    'I guess I have skunk instincts now too. Great.'

    "Hey now," he says kind of gruffly, "Don't you dare go spraying my cave. I ain't gonna have it stunk up more than it already is."

    You nod, and wonder what in the world comes next...
