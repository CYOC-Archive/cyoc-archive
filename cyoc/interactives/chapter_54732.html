    Growing a Fat Kid in the Hallway

    Since Ty had a free hour in-between classes, he decided to hang out in the hallway, and play with his new Android cell phone, while leaning against the wall next to a row of lockers. generally, the teachers didn't mind it, as long as he remained quiet.

    Ty looked up and saw another boy walking towards him. The boy was short and overweight. It was Carlos, a classmate who had come from Puerto Rico a few years ago. Carlos was a nice guy; he was short, he had short black hair and brown eyes, but he was just thirty pounds overweight.

    As Carlos came walking towards Ty, another boy passed him and gave him a slight shove while he walked past him, almost pushing Carlos into Ty.

    "Move over, fathead!" the other kid sneered, as he laughed while he walked on.

    Indeed, Carlos got picked on a lot, due to his excessive weight, and Ty had seen that. Carlos' legs were thick and meaty, his arms had a lot of flab on him, and his belly was big and round.

    "Hey Carlos... Are you alright?" Ty said to the boy.

    "Yeah... Thanks Ty... This is nothing, dude... Trust me... I'm used to it..." Carlos replied sadly.

    Ty just stared at the boy with a smile on his face.

    "Hey! Don't be sad. You don't have to put up with this crap anymore..." Ty smiled. "I'll fix you up..."

    "Oh please!" Carlos sighed as he rolled his eyes. "Don't tell me you're gonna put me on some exercise program or put me on a diet!"

    "Wouldn't you like to be lean and muscular?" Ty asked him.

    "Yeah... But that ain't gonna happen..."

    "Watch this! I'm gonna change your fat into muscle!" Ty said with a smile on his face.

    "What are you talking ab---" Carlos replied, when he was overcome by a strange feeling throughout his entire body.

    Carlos' belly suddenly began to shrink rapidly, while his arms, his legs, and his abdominal muscles hardened and inflated at the same time. While every ounce of fat melted away from his body, every muscle suddenly began to swell at the same time. For every pound of fat that he had, he now gained a pound of muscle! As his belly continued to shrink away, and his legs got thinner, while every ounce of flab melted away from his arms, his shoulders, his back, his neck, and even his face, his muscles started to swell and increase in size at the same time.

    Carlos' pectorals continued to harden and push outward and sideways at the same time as his belly continued to shrink, until his entire upper body had now taken on a reverse form: his chest barreled out further while his stomach now sported an impressive six-pack of abs.

    His biceps and triceps bulged out while all the flab under his arms disappeared, making his arms solid like a rock. The same happened for his legs. Every ounce of fat, which was hanging from his quads, now melted away as his calves and quads inflated into large pillars of rock-solid muscle.

    "Heyyyyy! I-I... I'm not fat anymore! I.... w-w-wWhat did you ddddo to m-m-m-me?????" Carlos gasped, as he looked down and stared at his new body in complete disbelief.

    "I told you!" Ty said calmly. "I took all your fat, and turned it into muscle!"

    "B-b-but... I got a lot of muscle!" Carlos exclaimed, as he made a fist and flexed, allowing a large bicep to pop up on his arm. "I-I mean... Look at me! I'm huge!

    "You had a lot of fat!" Ty replied.

    "H-h-how... How did you do that....?" Carlos asked him.

    Ty silently wished for Carlos to forget the transformation and for everyone to find it completely normal for him to be buff.

    "Do what?"

    "Uhh... I forgot what we were talking about... Well, see ya in class, Ty!"

    "You bet, big guy!" Ty grinned.

    Carlos turned around and swaggered down the hall. Ty just looked on, proud of his handiwork, looking forward to his next transformation...
