But this time, you're alone, and instead of being in a force field cage, you seem to be floating, semi-fetal position, in midair. Some kind of anti-gravity? You're not sure, but...

    Wait, you're not in a cage! You instinctively try to struggle, as though you were swimming in the air, the possibility of escape seeming so close you can almost taste it... but you go nowhere. It feels less like the gravity has been turned off, and more like the gravity for you alone has been centered on one specific point. There's nothing holding you up, as far as you can tell you're not suspended in anything other than air - something a part of you is grateful for, at least these aliens seem to breathe the same air you do - and it's not like the solid force field that made up the walls of your enclosure earlier. You're able to move your arms and legs, but there seems to be a gentle but irresistible force that keeps you in approximately the same position. Like an intangible human-shaped bubble - if you try to push against your invisible restraints, you can't get far, and the second you relax, your limbs drift back into that same semi-fetal position. There's nothing within reach to try and grab onto, either. Above you is a bright light and what look like several mechanical arms suspended from the ceiling, all too far to grasp, and below you is what looks like a grate with a grid of circular holes. You're as trapped as you ever were.

    Resigning yourself, at least for the moment, to the futility of your situation, you finally take a better look around at the rest of the room, and at the aliens that are watching and seem to be discussing you. Most of them, you can barely tell what they look like at all. You get the impression that they're scientists or maybe doctors, as they're covered head to toe (well, assuming they have toes) in spotlessly clean, white protective gear, with masks over their faces that remind you of a combination of a rebreather and goggles that look like an optometrist's equipment, they have so many complicated-looking lenses. All of the scientist-looking guys appear to be taking notes, rapidly typing on handheld devices, while listening intently to another, different creature. And if the scientists looked intimidating...

    While all of the aliens here look taller than you, the last one is absolutely enormous. Its appearance most closely resembles a huge, fat slug, or maybe a giant elephant seal, with the upper part of its body resting heavily on a broad, distended belly. It looks like it's at least 10 feet tall, and the part of its body that's sprawled out on the floor is even longer. Its eyes are sitting on two short stalks, like a slug, and instead of anything identifiable as a nose or mouth, it has a long tentacle or elephant-like trunk coming out of its face. And starting about halfway down its front, and continuing underneath the rest of its body, are dozens of other short, rounded protuberances. You assume they must be the equivalent to its feet, as they undulate obscenely as it moves around the floor, but you can't shake the thought that they have an uncanny resemblance to rows and rows of huge, disgusting tits.

    Worst of all, as Slug Lady (not that you can really know it's female, there's no way to tell for certain) exits the room and the scientists start busying themselves with other instruments and machinery, you can only assume that that huge, disgusting slug-elephant-blob-monster is your new owner, and is going to be the deciding factor behind what you're about to become.
