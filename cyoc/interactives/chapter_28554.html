She couldn’t hide out underneath the couch forever and took a deep breath as she decided what to do. That mosquito was going to be even bigger in no time and apparently wanted to drink her blood. She should make a run for it, hide out somewhere in the house, and let her body grow fully before she did anything else.

She looked back and saw the mosquito had gained several more inches during this time and was looking even more intimidating to her. Four feet long perhaps?
