    You are becoming a Furry

    “You are becoming a Furry.” Said the doctor as Chris just looked at this werewolf doctor and blinked.
    “A Furry?” inquired Chris, he knew what a Furry was but he expected more information then just that.
    “Yes a furry is a half human half animal hybrid, a werewolf is a type of furry or a cat person is also a furry.” Stated the Doctor “I understand you want to know more and expected a lot more detail but there is just no way we currently know to tell what type of furry you will become. You could be another werewolf or a kitsune, you could be a naga or a lizardman, you could be a mix of almost any type of animal.”
    “Why not, you know when a person is becoming a Centaur or a Elf or something else, why not this?” inquired Chris near the top of his lungs. A few people turned and looked at him but being upset was common enough, so they looked away quickly enough.
    “Because the amount of change is just too great, for a centaur or an elf we where able to isolate their DNA make up and when we find those within someone we know that is what a person is becoming.” Explained the Doctor “The issue is with Furry and other types of monsters is that we can only determine the type of monster…we just have so many monsters’ types that we can’t isolate each of them. Sorry, we can’t even tell if you are going to remain a man or if you will age down or the like.”
    “I hadn’t even thought of that…so thanks for reminding me.” complained Chris
    “Sorry but it is something you need to be prepared for.” explained the Doctor “However you may be lucky, you may end up with only small changes like an animal tail or some fur on your body. You may not be a full half human half animal like me.”
    “That would be good.” Admitted Chris
    “Right now, you have two choices before you, either we can get your transformation done today or we can let it proceed slowly and naturally.”
    “What?” inquired Chris
    “We have a drug which will push your transformation forward so that you could be done with it in as little as 10 minutes or we can let it go slowly.” explained the Doctor “If you want to go quickly we will take you to a transformation chamber and under our watch you will be transformed and then we begin your orientation for your new life. If slowly we will take you to a medical wing where you will be monitored and watched as you transform, once done we will begin your orientation. In the end the results will be the same but the quick way gets you out of here sooner but it is a harder mental change to deal with. So which do you want?”
