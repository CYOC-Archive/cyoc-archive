    “Um… are you sure this method works? Can those rottweilers really train our puppy?” Dave asks and gives the two rottweiler bitches next to Sarah a worried look. His wife holds the leonberger puppy close to her body and tries to calm the puppy, who is obviously not very fond of being lifted up. “Let me down! I’m your alpha! I’m in charge you big furless bitch!” the puppy yips as he tries to squirm out of her grip. Lisa and Ashley first looks at the puppy and then at each other, knowing that soon the puppy will regret that he even dared calling himself a alpha.

    “Well these bitches are specially train since birth. They’ve helped training countless of disobedient puppies” Sarah assures the couple. “Wait a minute… didn’t you say over the phone that this never happened before?” Dave asks and gives Sarah a questioning look. “Well it has never happened to any of the pups that have been born in this kennel before, but we have treated puppies from other kennels” Sarah quickly answers, as this question have been asked a couple of times before. “C’mon follow me” Sarah says and wave her hand to signal them to follow. She walks away to an empty kennel which she opens. “In her girls” Ashley and Lisa walks obediently into the kennel and waits for their “patient” to follow. Dave and his wife look almost jealously at how obedient the two dogs are. “Now take the puppy into the kennel and my dogs will take care of the rest” Sarah instructs and the puppy is taking into the kennel. “Hey! What are you doing!?” He barks as he tries to escape from the kennel, only to be pushed back in.

    The kennel door closes and the puppy begins to realise that he won’t be able to escape. He lets out a whimper and turns around and looks at the two dogs he shares this kennel with. “So… You said you’re an alpha?” Ashley asks with a grin on her face. “Well my sister and I are her to show you who the real alpha is…”

    The couple watches as their puppy gives clear signals of submission to the two adult dogs. “Are you absolutely sure this will work?” Dave’s wife asks. “Yes, in about half an hour your puppy will have no problem with obedience, he’ll learn that you’re his alpha”

    “Wait a minute… Where’s Robert? I bet he have explored the whole kennel by now… I told him to not to go too far away” Dave’s wife says. “Should I go and look for him?” Dave asks his wife. But as she’s about to answer Sarah interrupts her. “I can go and look for him. You just stay here and watch your puppy. Just call my name if there is any trouble”

    Sarah quickly walks down the kennel corridor and give each kennel a quick look, just to see if the boy have gone into one of them. But as she reaches the end of the corridors, without even catching a glimpse of Robert she begins to get worried. Her instincts tell her something just isn’t right as she notice that the door to the lab is opened and her premonition proves to be correct when she peek into the room and sees clothes spread across the room. “Oh no…” She whimpers as the puddle of formula on the floor gives her a clear hint of what has happened. But what makes her even more distressed is that the clothes that are scattered around the lab belong to the couple’s son Robert. She walks into the room and closes the door behind her. Suddenly she can hear a whispering voice from the other side of the room. Sarah walks as quietly as she can towards the voice and finds that it belongs to Victoria. She’s crouched in front of a table with lots of boxes under it. It seems like she’s trying to lure something out that has hidden itself between the boxes.
