    Sean had one more change to make. Focusing the
    handheld unit at himself, he sat on the edge of the bed
    and programmed it to shrink his entire body to eight
    inches tall. Taking a deep breath out of nervousness
    and anticipation, he activated the unit and felt the
    "bugbite" of the beam hit him. A moment later, the world
    exploded around him as everything both grew dozens
    of times larger as well as receded into the distance.
    Even his clothes ballooned and puffed out as his body
    dwindled. The Chronivac unit, suddenly much too large
    for him to hold, bounced onto the bed and then onto the
    floor.

    Within seconds, it was over, and Sean stood,
    disoriented and naked, on the bed. He was intimidated
    by the size of everything around him, and further up the
    bed, he could sense the presence of the two sleeping
    giants. Whereas before he had dwarfed them, they
    were now impossibly huge gods. Thank god they're
    sleeping, he thought. He had always wanted to
    experiment with being tiny, to see the world from this
    perspective, but now that his wish had come true, he
    didn't think he'd ever be able to face or interact with a
    giant. This was just too weird.

    He'd programmed the unit by only activing the "save"
    command once, so he knew he had only an hour at this
    size before it wore off. While some small part of his
    mind thought that even an hour was too long, that he
    should find the unit and restore his size immediately,
    for the most part, he was up for the adventure. Taking
    another deep breath, he began to walk up the bed
    toward his giant friends.

    As he approached their feet, he thought, Man, they're
    skinny. He liked pumped guys himself, like he'd
    become, like Jay and Mark used to be. He'd deflated
    their bodies to teach them a lesson, but since he would
    have rather explored their bodies the way they normally
    were, he guessed the joke was on him, as well. Maybe
    one day he'd figure out how to get what he really
    wanted, but right now, he'd make the best of it.

    approaching the level of their now minuscule penises

    somewhere else in the apartment. To his tiny ears, it
    sounded like a sonic boom and earthquake combined,
    but his rational mind thought it must just be Dan (now
    also a giant) getting home.

    Dan was his other roomate, and a beautiful man in
    every way except temperment. He was friendly enough,
    but often very wary around Sean, who he was convinced
    was gay and lusting after him, which was in fact the
    case. Sometimes, he was mean to Sean for no reason
    other than this. While gorgeous, he was no one Sean
    would like to face from his new height. In fact, now that
    he thought about it, eight inches tall might be a good
    height for Dan to explore for a while.

    back to where the Chronivac unit had fallen. He'd heard
    it thump on the floor, but he was sure he'd be able to
    climb down a blanket to retrieve it. Wouldn't he?
